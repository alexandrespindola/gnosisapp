Delfus windo jinaabu beeri gaŋa kambeere boŋ, dabari taaga tammaase gaŋa: "MA NAA ASAARU NII BOYA". Maa ni booya ni booya koyne, ni ga adduunya nda Irikoyey bay.

Fahamay beeri ganje, gaaba taji yaŋ gaaba beeri gaŋa ciine no haya beeri nooya GREKIYAN jinaabu dumi kaa gaa.

To ka day hari cimi nda laakal hayyaŋ, iri ga hina iri windo gaaba zama ganje kaŋ hagu iri naŋ ciine, a ga hagu iri ma iri booya iri fundey zaama kulu boŋ.

Iri windo ganje gaaba iri te, a ga ciine day iri ma dogu haray, fundu-beeriyaŋ, humbu, ciika nda gundayaŋ gaa, doole funey beeriyaŋ nda hayyaŋ kase yaŋ gaa, dsb.

A bayray borey kulu se kaŋ hay kulu saba kaŋ iri te GANJE ZAAMA KULU gaa ganje se, iri fundu ga to da kaŋ a ga kay jii nda fundu beerijaŋ kaŋ ga zina.

Laakal baaru boŋ, a ga haŋ kaŋ iri na gonda se kaŋ iri ma laakal hayyaŋ, iri ma iri booya iri fundey kulu boŋ.

A ga ti himma kaŋ iri ma faham laakal hayyaŋ kulu, nda iri funey zaama kulu boŋ, iri teendi kulu kaŋ ga fun iri fundu boŋ, iri baakasinay kulu, iri fongandey kulu, iri taabu beeriyaŋ kulu, dsb.

A bayray borey kulu se kaŋ ganje zaama kulu boŋ, iri taabu beeriyaŋ kulu kaŋ iri ga booya, iri farhantey nda iri bine sarrey kulu, fongandey beeriyaŋ, iri ga fun adduunya banda, wala iri fundey zaama banda, iri baakasina beeriyaŋ, fundu beeriyaŋ beeriyaŋ, bine sarrey beeriyaŋ, ciika nda gundayaŋ kulu, dsb.

Borey kaŋ ga ba iri windo ganje gaaba iri te, iri naŋ ciine, a ga himma iri ma hangay hayyaŋ boŋ da kaŋ iri faham a ma hagu iri fundey zaama kulu boŋ. Iri ma faham a ma hagu iri laakal boŋ, zama fundu ga gonda zaama beeriyaŋ.

Kalaam kulu kaŋ iri ga faham, a ti iri ma iri booya iri fundey zaama kulu boŋ.

Filimu kulu kaŋ iri fundu ga hangay, a gonda funyaŋ nda banandi. Waati kaŋ fonda, baakasina, fundu beeriyaŋ, doguyaŋ, fongandey ga ban, iri fundu ga to da kaŋ a ga kay jii nda fundu beerijaŋ kaŋ ga zina nda laakal fundey kulu.

Borey kaŋ ga hangay laakal fundey se, iri ga gonda kaŋ iri ma laakal fundu beerijaŋ booya. Laakal fundu kaŋ a ga fun iri fundu boŋ, a ga ciine iri ma faham nda bi, hay kulu kaŋ ga cindi, a ti laakal hayyaŋ.

Iri ma fayda fundu kaŋ ga kay nda fundu kaŋ ga kay hansa.

Iri ma fayda fundu kaŋ ga kay jii nda fundu kaŋ iri na a kay da gundayaŋ.

Laakal baaru boŋ, iri ga hagu iri ma faham kaŋ waati kaŋ iri na fundu kay da gundayaŋ, a ga tugu wala zaama kaŋ a ga ba a ma huru.

Laakal baaru boŋ, iri ga hagu iri ma faham kaŋ waati kaŋ iri fundu na kay da gundayaŋ, a ga tugu wala a na kay jii, a ga jinde nda bine sarra beeriyaŋ.

Kayyaŋ cimi nda jii kaŋ ga fun, iri ga siibi nda barka, waati kaŋ iri film beeriyaŋ ban iri boŋ fundu zaama gaŋa.

Waati kaŋ fundu ga kay jii, waati kaŋ fundu ga to da jii, iri ga siibi laakal fundu beerijaŋ.

Iri na si hagu iri ma laakal fundu sifa. Iri na si hagu iri ma laakal fundu sifa, zama iri zandi te hagu iri ma zandi nda laakal boŋ nda binda.

Iri ga haŋ iri ma tammaase laakal fundu boŋ zandi boŋ adduunya zaama boŋ.

«LAAKAL FUNDU YA DIN NA DUMU».

"FONDA NA SANDI NDAGA LAAKAL FUNDU NDA LAAKAL FUNDU NA SANDI NDAGA FONDA".

"FONDA TI LAAKAL FUNDU NDA LAAKAL FUNDU TI FONDA, A TI LAAKAL FUNDU YA KA HAUYAŊ GA GORO".

"LAAKAL FUNDU NDA GOROYAŊ GA CIYA, A NA ASAARU TAHAN". LAAKAL FUNDU NDA GOROYAŊ GA HURYA.

"WAATI KAŊ BOREY KAŊ GA GORO, I GA HAY BOYA, I GA A GANJE YA GORO, I NA A LAAKAL FUNDU BOYA".

"Borey kaŋ ya boori, i ga hagu i ga hay booya a goro nda a laakal fundu boŋ".

"LAAKAL FUNDU TI ZANDI KAŊ GA ALMAALI TAHAN BOREY MAALUM NDAGA MAALUM KA TAHAN YA YA GORO".

Alfa-Alfa dumi nda Alfa-Alfa jamano, i ga hagu i ma iri fahamay beeri ganje hangay da kaŋ i ga koyiri iri talibey se fonda kaŋ ga zeeri laakal hayyaŋ se.

A ga ciine iri ma to se kaŋ IRI MA BOOYA HAYYAŊ WAATI KAŊ FONGU BAN.

LAAKAL FUNDU GA CIINE IRI SE IRI MA SINA HAKIKA GANJE BOORIYAŊ.

LAAKAL BAYYAŊ BOORIYAŊ, ALMAALI KAŊ GA SANDI, A TI HAKIKA BOORIYAŊ.

NI FOHAMA KAŊ A GANJE TI LAAKAL FUNDU KAŊ NI NAA BOYA NDAGA NATA, A TI FOHAMA FUNDU GORU, ADDUUNIYA BOORIYAŊ A TI MAALUM, BUDDHA ADDUUNIYA BOORIYAŊ.

NI FUNDU KAŊ A GORO, FOHAMA KAŊ A BORU KAŊ IRI NAA FATAN A GAA ZAAMA. A BAA A GA BONA A SI HAYYAŊ CINE.

NI FUNDU KAŊ A BORU NDA LAAKAL BEERI YA, A GONDANDI YA SI HAYYAŊ, A SI HAYYAŊ CINE, A TI BONA AMITARA BUDDHA.

Fahamay han. Ni MA BOOYA NI FOHAMA NDAGA BUDDHA YA GORO, GANJE YA NI FUNDU, A TI BUDDHA YA FUNDU.

Ni FOHAMA HAN, HALI A MA GANJE ZAMA GAA GANJE ZAMA GAA, Ni MA FONGU NI GORO GAA, NI MA FONGU NI MEDITATION TI, ZAMA WAATI KAŊ NI FONGU NI MEDITATION TI, ALMAALI GA HALI KA NI MEDITATION TUURA. NI FUNDU GAA TO DA GANJE ZAMA NI GA HAYYAŊ BOOYA.
