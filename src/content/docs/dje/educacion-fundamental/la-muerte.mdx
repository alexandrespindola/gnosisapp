Guna tilasta ka bayray ka faham da fondo nda bine zama koyne, halatte maate ka buuya woone yaamaray, yaadin banda no a gonda yeele ka faham da gonda kay a ma koyne ka halitta ga hay.

Ka adam-ize hinna di koyne nwaaray kusa bande, manti a gonda hinna ka faham da buuya gundukoy.

Cimiriya waati kulu mana bay kulle. Cimiriya buuya gonda, sanda manti koyne taale.

Ay bine ga koyne, haŋ ka gonda tilasta, gonda l’amuya ka buuya, ka tabatar zaa ka tilasta, gonda jamba koyne haŋ ka gaaba da yeele nda baaray ga yeele haykulay din banda.

Ay bine ga koyne, nanga na ga buu. Ay bine ga koyne, nanga a hunku. Ay bine ga koyne, gonda humu ka buuya.

Cimiriya manti haŋ ka naŋ ga tum nda zanga ga tum. Cimiriya manti gonda alaka tumanteyandi ga, wala ga tum ga zanga ga tum. Cimiriya manti haŋ ka hayyarri, walla ka al’ada, walla ka anniya, walla ka tilasta anniya. Cimiriya buuya gonda, sanda manti koyne taale.

Cimiriya buuya gonda, hima ni taamu beeri te taamukoy.

A saba ga, ka cimiri taamu ka buuya ka gaaba da borey haŋ ka mana a bay.

Baykoone koyne ga hantum dabu cimu hari ka ganji, ama a saba ga ka cimiriya gaaba ka cimu hari ga borey haŋ ka mana a taamu beeri te, day sanda cine mi ga gaaba ka cimiriya buuya ga borey haŋ ka mana a taamu beeri te.

Borey haŋ ka koyne ka cimiriya buuya bay, hima a ceeci, a taamu beeri te a boŋ, a ceeci koyne sanda ya gonda tilasta, yaadin bande ni ga yeele ka faham da hinna ka gonda ka buuya.

Ka ceeci ka gonda nda taamukoy ka gonda zama koyne ka mi ka faham, borey nanga ga koyne ka faham da hinna ka gonda ka buuya, borey koyne haŋ ka koyne ga tilasta a ma hunku gonda haŋ banda yaadin no.

Borey bari bari koyne ga hunku ga yeele nda arzaka, ka gonda daraja, ka yeele nda banda, ka tumanteyandi, ka anniya, ka zanka, d.s.b, nda waati ka mi a faham taamu ka koyne ga koyne ga halitta, waati ka ga huula, waati ka waati zaa ka a si tabata, yaadin bande mi a tin, a humu, ka hagaray.

I nanga ga koyne ka faham borey yaabu, i nanga ga koyne ka faham taamu ka hunku waati bande.

I nanga ga koyne ka faham borey yaabu taamu ka hunku ga baani waati bande.

I nanga ga koyne ka faham borey yaabu taamu ka hunku ga keyri te, ka hunku ga ceebe koyri.

Gonda tilasta, gonda ga hinna, gonda ga gaabu, ma te hina ga bay kullu da hinna ka gonda ka buuya, yaadin bande humu ga taŋ ma hunku.

Ka ceeci ka gonda nda Adamize, mi ga gonda, ka bina ga keyri ga mana bay kullu, ka koyne taamu ka gonda ga hunku haray haykulay din banda.

Bina haŋ ka keyri ga mana bay kullu, zaa nanga ga yeele taamu ka mana bay kullu, cimiriya.

Day ka keyri taamu ka gonda waati bande, ka tammahilla taamukoy, mi ga yeele ka taamu ka halitta, waati zaa ka a si hunku.

Borey haŋ ka koyne ga hunku, gonda humu ka buuya, i tumanteyandi nda al’ada ga tumanteyandi a ma te i ga al’ada.

Buuya day a boŋ mana gonda humu, koyne ga ganji, koyne ga daraja, koyne ga gaabu, ama bina haŋ ka keyri ga mana bay kullu, ga keyri ga waati zaa a hunku.

Waati ka gonda ga cimiriya ga bay kullu nda hinna ka gonda ka buuya, mi ga yeele ka taamu ka halitta, ka hunku nda buuya gonda ga kulle integre, uni-total.

Buuya te ka neebe ka hunku. Lala ka hunku gonda, ka kambe taabi ka buuya.

Hunku te ka neebe ka tilasta. Zama jiire zaa buuya mi ga hunku ga Adamize banda ka neebe fo bari bari.

Neebe fo no haŋ ka Adamize si hinna, a yaaray, a te buuya. Ka buuya yaaray gonda barja bari. Adamize si hinna a barja.

Sanda sanda ka neebe ga taabi tuuri, sanda cine koyne ka buuya yaaray ga hunku ga Adamize banda, a ga halaci kulle.

Buuya yaaray ga gaaba ka buuya, ka gonda hunku.

Buuya yaaray ga neebe ka bari bari yeele, nda gundekoy haŋ ka gonda, a ga te ga te ka bine keyri ga kulle.

Buuya yaaray ga Adamize ga waati zama koyne a gonda tilasta.

Ay Bine, ay neebe ga, ga hunku zanka banda woy.

Haŋ te Cimiriya buuya gonda, waati te buuya nda bine keyri ga koyne te haŋ mana waati gonda, day yeele ka tammahilla bande mi ga yeele ka taamu beeri te.

Mastaray nda Mastarayzey ka Eskulu banda, hima i ga gaaba i talibey ga lala ka gaaba ka taamu ka halitta ka cimiriya ga.
