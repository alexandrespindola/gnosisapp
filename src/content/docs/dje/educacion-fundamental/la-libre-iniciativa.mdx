Dunya gaa jineeji beeri kaŋ yaŋ fun duniya kulu la ra, zaari kulu i goo koyyaŋ karame da koleejiyaŋ ra, baa hayaŋ kaŋ i sanna a maamaa, wala boro siŋaŋ, wala i miila a ga, i si bay zamaŋ, wala zama.

Jineeji yaŋ ga tiŋyaŋ i ma matematiiki kar, da fisiki, da simi, da jeografi, d.a.y.b.

Jineeji yaŋ ziyaŋ ga duŋ hallaari, amma woxo kul i si kay da tunandi baa fo zamaŋ i ma miila duŋ hallaaro sababanta, da a hanga.

Man'ciyaŋ iri ma duŋ hallaaro? Iri ga duŋ hallaaro zamaŋ?

Jineeji yaŋ goo hina maamaa boŋ hal i bay, i ga tilas hallaari ka duŋ kaŋ i du, i m'a key da ceeyaŋ ga, hala a koy.

I si jineeji yaŋ gaa farga ka miila zamaŋ dumi karam to, i goo koyyaŋ karame, wala koleeji zama i kaayeeji yaŋ n'i sanba, hala a koy.

Jineeji, wala karame koyrji si farga baa fo i boŋ beerji la ra: Zamaŋ yaŋ goo nan? Hal yaŋ kaa nan? Dumi saariya kaŋ yaŋ ze nan na yaŋ nan?

Karame koyrji, da jineeji arkey da wayborey, i goo gunde kaŋ yaŋ doge, i goono te sanda abotomateeji gaa, i goo koyyaŋ karame, wala koleeji, wala dunya gaa jineeji beeri kaŋ yaŋ fun duniya kulu la ra, baa hayaŋ kaŋ i sanna a maamaa, wala boro siŋaŋ, wala i miila a ga, i si bay zamaŋ, wala zama.

I ga tilas ka danga abotomateeji, ka gunde fee, ka soobay se i boŋ beerji yaŋ gan, dumi ŋwaaru ga tiŋyaŋ zama i ma jarribaŋ gan, wala karam, wala ka fun taji fo ra hal i karam zaari kulu kaŋ yaŋ kaa, ka jiji, da yaraazu, da miilaŋ te, ka fuula te, ka karame taaji heybooji yaŋ daŋ, d.a.y.b., d.a.y.b., d.a.y.b.

Karame koyrji ga tilas i ma fee gunde zama i ma bana ka jineeji yaŋ gaa, karame wala koleejiyaŋ ra zama i ma gunde fee.

To doorumi ka Abotomateeji beeri to karame bankey boŋ, ka duŋ kaŋ i ga key da ceeyaŋ ga hal i si bay zamaŋ, wala zama.

Arzey ga miilaŋ ka kaaru, i n'i ce i ma kar hal i ma hina du, wala teeŋ du, d.a.y.b. hal i karam da zambala beeri ziyaŋ ra a ga, hal i si bay zaari kaŋ nan, i si bay sababa kaŋ se i ga tilas i ma fisiki kar, da simi, da biyolooji, da aritemetiiki, da jeografi, d.a.y.b.

Wayborey karam zama i ma soobay kaŋ ga baŋ ka ceebu haŋ, wala i ma hina du ka baŋ i se i ceeba i ga bare, wala i si koyri, wala i koy daŋyaŋ.

Zambala beeri ziyaŋ ra hal i si bay yaŋ ga tiŋyaŋ i jine ga tiŋyaŋ, wala i ma bu ŋwa kaŋ ra.

Hina karame ra maamaa, wala a goŋ do beeri, wala boro siŋaŋ, i goo ce kaŋ taale i m'a kar kaŋ baaru te boŋ taamaa.

Handaŋ hallaaro kaŋ karame ra ka kaaru, hala a koy.

Taimaŋ fo ra go ga kay hay kaŋ ga kay hay kaaru ka kaaru zamaŋ din ka kaaru. Handaŋ si kay hay kaŋ ga kay hay din. Iri kaayeeji yaŋ hin ka gaa karame koyra wala koyreega hanandi ka jineeji, baa a go kaaru go, a ga kaaru.

Wayborey karame ra goo te waybo da karame koyra, wala i ga kaaru, baa i si "J" bay hay kaŋ karame koyra goo karanta, baaru kul i ga yaaji jarribaŋ ra kaŋ i ga kaaru.

Arkey da wayborey goo baŋ ga kaaru. A ga tiŋyaŋ fahamtaa gaa waranka boŋ yaŋ ra.

Arzee kaŋ kaaru jarribaŋ ra (jarribaŋ bii gaa fo) a si danga i gunde go fee hay yaŋ ga kaaru.

Jineeji ga taŋyaŋ sanda fofo gaa, kaŋ i karam kaŋ i ga jarribay.

A si kaŋ ni ga gunde fee hay din ga, a ga tiŋyaŋ ni m'a key ka taŋ sanda fofo gaa ni koy a maamaa hal a koy.

Ka jarribaŋ kaaru, wala ka kaaru a si danga ni go fee haŋ ga. Iri bay boro beeri ziyaŋ hina ra, amma i si kaaru jarribaŋ ra boŋ. Iri bay wayboreeji beeri da matematiiki beeri kaŋ yaŋ go kaaru karame ra, kaŋ i si kaaru karame jarribaŋ ra hayaŋ din.

Iri bay jineeji fo kaaru boŋ anatomii yaŋ ra kaŋ baaru kaŋ a ga too a ga yaaji anatomii jarribaŋ ra. Handaŋ arzee to ga goono hananay anatomii beeri.

Ka kaaru a si danga ni go fee ga. Boro yaŋ goo kaŋ yaŋ si kaaru yaŋ, amma i go fee ga.

Kay hay kaŋ ga kaaru kaaru ga hay yaŋ karam, ga tiŋyaŋ ka gunde fee to kaaru.

Karame koyrji ga waaji ka bana zama i ma jineeji yaŋ gunde fee. Yaŋ kul kaŋ karame koyrji goo teega ga koy jineeji gundey gaa. A ga yaaji kaŋ jineeji yaŋ ma gunde fee hay kaŋ yaŋ i goo karanta.

Ka key da ceeyaŋ, wala ka taŋ sanda fofo gaa, a ga tiŋyaŋ boora kaŋ bonan ziyaŋ ra.

Jineeji yaŋ ga tilas i ma hay kaaru kaŋ ga goŋ da a ga key ceeyaŋ zama "i ma kaaru" da baaru hal i ga duŋ din i ga bare zama ceeyaŋ si to, i ga fay danga i go duŋ teeŋ, i ga daabu zama hina teeŋ dii.

Arzey karam zama i ma teeŋ du hal i ma hina du, da teeŋ duu, i na teeŋ dii, wala i tee ga, doktoor, abogaaji, d.a.y.b. a ga tiŋyaŋ i ma duu, i ga teeŋ dii, i ga kaa, i ga duŋ ka bay gunde, i ga bu kaŋ i si gunde fee i ga duŋ ka bay gunde hal i si bay i hina. Hala a koy.

Wayborey kaa, i ga bana key, i ga duŋ, i ga daŋ haybooji yaŋ da kaa, da ceeba, da duŋ, i ga ce i ga kaa i ga bu kaŋ i goo doge, i ga taŋ zamaŋ ka kaa.

Karame koyrji si koy ka bay zamaŋ boro kul gunde go doge. Ga waaji kaŋ karame koyrji ma fee zama i ma jineeji yaŋ fee.

Baaru baa fo kaŋ iri ma ziyaŋ da teoorii, da citaŋ Danta, da Homero, da Virgilio, d.a.y.b. iri gunde go doge kaŋ iri si gunde fee iri boŋ da, da hay kaŋ iri goo karanta, da hina.

Hangay go kaaray kaŋ iri ma tee jitere baaru?

Kaaray kaaru si kaŋ yaŋ ni bay karam da hano. Biilee kul hin karam da hano.

Iri ga tilas iri ma tee hina baaru kaŋ ni ga fee gunde.

Boro tiini go subgunde koo yeyi da koo hinka da gunde koo hinka.

Iri ga tilas iri ma gunde fee, iri ga tilas iri ma sub gunde tee gunde.

Boro si hangu yaŋ i go ga doge, amma i go ga hangu yaŋ i goo doge siŋ.

I ga tilas i ma daŋ ka hangu, ga tilas i ma gunde fee din ga tilas i ma tee kaayeeji da karame.

Waajibi kaŋ karame koyra ma jineeji gunde gaa i si ceeyaŋ boŋ din.

Jineeji ga waaji i ma miila i boŋ din, i ma taŋ koyraa kaŋ yaŋ boro yaŋ ce ga i ma hallaari.

Koyrji ga waaji i ma jineeji yaŋ humbu bare.

Koyrji ga waaji i ma bana jineeji yaŋ, himaan kaŋ ga katar ka tay hay kaŋ yaŋ i goo karanta.

Boora kaŋ i ma yaaji kaŋ i ma gaa duguyaŋ kaŋ yaŋ i goo karanta karame da koleejiyaŋ.

I ga tilas i ma jineeji yaŋ dii humbu zama i ma tay i yaŋ goo karanta.

Humbu din yaŋ bonan zama baa ga to. Arzee da humbu i si tay hay yaŋ kaaru kaŋ i goo karanta.

Iri ga tilas iri ma fee ka hina, kaayeeji yaŋ ga hallaari zaami to i boŋ da, da hay yaŋ iri goo karanta.

Maan kulu yaŋ toon ka duŋ iri ma hina gaŋ teeŋ din zama iri ma kaaru hala iri ma yaaji.

Boroyaŋ kaŋ yaŋ go huru i si farga hay fo.

Yaŋ kul humbu bare i ga te.

Zamaŋ yaŋ humbu ga koy waaji kayba gaa danga i ga duŋ go kaŋ a te kaŋ baaru hal i goo duŋ si ka bay i gune.

Duŋ yaŋ bonan jinii tee gunde, i ga duŋ hal i ma jineeji gunde fee.
