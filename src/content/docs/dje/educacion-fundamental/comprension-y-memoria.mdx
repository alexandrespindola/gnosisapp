Tuma koyne, yaŋ ga ti, kaŋ ga ti iri n'a di, kaŋ iri maa, kaŋ iri kar, kaŋ borey d'iri se, kaŋ yaŋ gonda iri, dkd.

Farmaasey ga koyne, ngey talibey ma ngey sanno, ngey jumlanta, kaŋ i hantum takarda-zabandey ra, hay ga hay, karga-hayyaŋ, d'a pointu-komay kulu dkd.

Jarriba taŋ koyne, kaŋ iri n'a ci, kaŋ iri kar masinniyaŋ, sanni furu, kaŋ iri naŋ ga batayyaŋ, wala bangu-fe, sanda kaŋ iri gonda tuma ra kulu.

Goyko taji beeri ma faamu kaŋ batay sanda radioconsola teebu goy, kaŋ i n'a to tuma ra, sika ti faamu-bam gaa. Tuma si ti faamu, tuma jaŋ na faamu, tuma go waati kaŋ bisa, hay kaŋ bu, hay kaŋ jaŋ gonda fundi.

Ya tilas, ya urgenta nda waati zaamani, talibi kulu nda taliba boro kulu fuula ra, kolejiyaŋ nda jammiyaŋ ra, ma faamu sifa beeri kaŋ ga ti faamu-bam.

FAAMU, hay kusa-kusa ne, sanda kaŋ iri gonda fundi se, hay kaŋ iri n'a ta dambara gaa, hay kaŋ iri n'a jiri gumo gaa, kaŋ ga yee ka ciya goy-bayra fonda.

Tuma, tuma koyne hay kaŋ bu ne, nga ya waati kaŋ bisa nda alhakku, danga yee ka ciya ideal, danga alama, danga miila, danga idealisme, kaŋ iri koyne ka sara masinniyaŋ nda koyne ka sara laakal-laakale.

FAAMU HAKIIKA, faamu-bam ra, faamu gumo ra, laakal-laakale faamu fundo ra ya teendi laakal, teendi daima kaŋ fun za-ra, kaŋ iri gonda iri fundi ra.

Faamu gonda bayra danga goy kanay, sanda haŋniyaŋ, sanda sassey taŋ, sanda zaba kaŋ ga dagay, sanda zanka koyne. FAAMU kaŋ yee ka ciya goy-bambay, hay ga boori, hay ga cimi, hay ga taagadandi nda fundi gaandi.

Goy kaŋ sinji tuma kaŋ iri kar gaa, ideal kaŋ iri ga naŋ, al'ada kaŋ i n'iri koyne, tuma kaŋ i n'a tum tuma ra, dkd., goy kuuku ne, nga ya sinji zaba-zaba gaa, nga ya dagay, nga ya to zaba-zaba gaa nda danga nga ga yee ka dira batilla nda waase ga.

Hay kaŋ ma haandi goy tuma se, hay kaŋ ma koyne ka goy kanay nda tuma kaŋ i n'a tum tuma ra, hay ga baani, hay jaŋ gonda kanay kaŋ ga dira batilla nda waase ga.

Hay kaŋ ma jarriba taŋ, hay kaŋ ma ciya jiiri, boro laakal-kusa kulu ga hin ka tum a, kaŋ gonda tuma gumo.

Ma faamu hayyaŋ kaŋ i n'a kar kaŋ i ga jarriba, hay ga to hay fo, hay jaŋ gonda hay tuma gaa, nga ga to laakal-bam gaa kaŋ jaŋ na to laakal kuuku gaa.

Borey kaŋ ga naŋ ngey goy kulu ngey fundi ra gaa, teyorey nda tuma sifa kulu kaŋ i n'a tum tuma ra, borey ga dira kokora hay-dumi ra, nda kokora hay-dumi ga ti, handu ga ciya. Borey ga kokora ngey boro, ngey zuru, ngey izey nda hay-dumi izey, nda hay-dumi borey. Ngey ga kokora ngey windo, ngey alkatima, ngey libay sifa kulu nda hay-dumi hayyaŋ. Ngey ga kokora ngey miile, ngey izey laakal nda borey miila, nda borey laakal nda handu yee, kaŋ ga yee ka ciya goy-bambay.

Waati kaŋ ga dagay dunya se, al'ada sifa kulu ga sinji handu gaa nda suuban-laakal. Boro kulu ga handu boro kulu. Iri ga handu miile, hayyaŋ, borey, iri ga naŋ ka suuban dirhamu nda dirhamu boro, teyorey taaji, miile taaji kaŋ iri n'a tum tuma ra, hayyaŋ taaji ka iri ma borey dambara, dkd.

FAAMU HAKIIKA, cimiyaŋ, kanayaŋ ra, a gonda baaru cimi nda sanni furu jaŋ.

Hayyaŋ kaŋ iri ga tuma, kaŋ hay kaŋ iri ga naŋ tuma se, duuru ga yee ka kaŋ, zama tuma si cimi. Talibey ga te tuma hayyaŋ ra, idealey, teyorey, hantum cindey, kaŋ jaŋ na goy borey se, zama a ga kaŋ tuma ra ka jaŋ na hay fo daŋ.

Borey kaŋ yaŋ ga fundi kar kar masinniyaŋ, borey kaŋ yaŋ ga yadde ka tum teyorey, i ga laakal baani, i ga laakal jaabi.

Iri jaŋ taba da karandi cimi faamu-bam gaa. Iri ga jabu koyne al'ada kaŋ bisa waati gaa. Iri ga jabu karandi sifa kulu, tuma koyne kulu dkd. Tuma ga gaamu, kaŋ a gonda faamu cimi.

Iri ga naŋ ka karandi, iri ga naŋ takarda kanayaŋ, iri ga naŋ farmaasee, fuula ra, kolejiyaŋ nda jammiyaŋ ra. Iri ga naŋ GURU, haniyaŋ, mahatmas dkd., ammaa goy kaŋ ma faamu koyne koyne nda kaŋ jaŋ na te tuma ra tuma.

Kaŋ iri ga ciya kanayaŋ zama iri ga kokora iri nda tuma kaŋ iri n'a tum, nda ideal, nda kaŋ iri ga naŋ ma ciya, kaŋ iri jaŋ ciya, dkd.

Waati kaŋ iri ga faamu koyne koyne karandi kaŋ iri ta, iri jaŋ na tuma koyne, iri ga ma ciya idealey.

Waati kaŋ a gonda kokora kaŋ iri ciya ne da waati kaŋ iri ga naŋ ma ciya waati kaŋ ga yee, waati kaŋ a gonda kokora iri fundi nda ideal nda model, kaŋ iri ga naŋ ma haandi, jaŋ na baaru cimi ciya.

Kokora kulu ga jaabu, kokora kulu ga boori, handu ga fun danga iri ga ciya kulu, nga ga ti, ma fun zeero nda ma iri fundi nda kanay, sanda kaŋ iri ciya, kaŋ iri ga naŋ ma ciya.

A jaŋ ciya goy kaŋ ma zay MI, MUNDA SE, kaŋ iri jaŋ na karandi ma kokora kaŋ iri ga faamu kaŋ iri ciya ne koyne.

Kaŋ iri ga naŋ ma faamu, iri ga naŋ ma maa iri farmaasey, iri laamu, laamu se dkd.

Zanka beybey gonda bayra kaŋ ma yaazi iri baaba se nda iri farmaasee, nda iri laamu se dkd.

A jaŋ ciya goy kaŋ ma faamu karandi waati kaŋ iri jaŋ na taazim iri baaba se nda iri farmaasee se.

Tuma kaŋ iri n'a kar masinniyaŋ jaŋ na faamu, a ga laakal yaaru, a ga handu fundu, dkd.

Waati kaŋ iri bay maa koyne, a ga fun iri fundi ra hagu kaani fo, faamu ga boori fo, sanda a ga kanay, a ga boori, zama iri ga koyne, a ga kanay kaŋ jaŋ gonda goy, jaŋ gonda faamu koyne.

Kaŋ i n'a zay zanka laakal kaŋ ma tuma gumo, kaŋ a ga goy, a ga boori ma karandi fundu hay sifa kulu, taliba kaŋ diri na fundi se, kaŋ a ma faamu relation se.

Sanda kaŋ iri ga saba farmaasee ra i ga handu al'ada kaŋ bisa waati gaa. I ga naŋ zanka ga karandi koyne hay kulu kaŋ a jaŋ na faamu.

Waati ga yee ka yadde kaŋ a ga boori ma faamu koyne tuma ammaa i ga ganja kaŋ forme sifa kulu kaŋ i n'a tum fundu ra.

A ga bara kaŋ alhakku si cimi zama waati kaŋ fundu sifa nda faamu nda laakal dkd. A jaŋ ciya goy kaŋ ma ciya tuma kaŋ a ga yee ka ciya iri fundi ra waati kaŋ goy ga gonda se.

Tuma INTEGRO ga iri ta, iri ma sifa kulu hagu kaani nda bayraa.

Fundi faamu fondo kulu se, a ga ciya hay goy kokora laakal-laakale ga.
