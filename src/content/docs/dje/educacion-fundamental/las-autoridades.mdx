Gomno ga ni da FARILLA, Koyne ga ni da FARILLA. Pulisi, da asariya, da soldaro, da windo keyri, da karame, da diina koyraykoyo, da dumi tamma, i ni da FARILLA.

FARILLA hinna hinka no: Hanga, FARILLA HAKIL TAA. Hanga hinka, FARILLA HAKIL KAKOY.

FARILLA HAKIL TAA wala HAKIL KAKOY, alhakku si no. I gonda haaji beeri FARILLA HAKIL HINANTE KOYRAY KAA.

FARILLA HAKIL TAA wala HAKIL KAKOY ka duniya to haway da sunsu.

Windo ra da karasaara FARILLA HAKIL TAA ya ciili FARILLA gaa zama i ga HAKIL TAA wala HAKIL KAKOY.

I windo keyri da karame HAKIL TAA, hunkuna ya zaama, kuranta kuranya koyraykoyo, danga i kaŋ ciine da asariya hanandi, i ga koy ka faati kulbu ra.

I windo keyri da karame HAKIL TAA go ga tilasta iri gundekuna ra, i gonda kaŋ iri ma ciyaŋ foole, amma i ga di i ga boori. I go ga ne woodin iri boŋ se no.

I windo keyri ya FARILLA HAKIL TAA danga i ga di zama i go ga dumey ciyaŋ danga jere, danga day i ya ka boori Adamu borey gaa.

Karame arwasa da wayboro ni laakal ga koy ka dam dumi care gaa, wala i go ga dume fooyaamo, wala i go ga dume halla i ga tamma. Baakasin i go ga hukun goro karame dumi kaŋ i ga dam ciili gaa, baakasin karame woodin si taabi, i go ga toonandi dumi tooru tooru, kaŋ i mana hanna, wala kaŋ i mana hin.

I windo keyri da karame go ga doka foole goro zanka keyray se, da waybora, da zanka beerey se, da wayboro beerey se, da dumi tamma.

FARILLA kaŋ si gonda HAKIL HINANTA, i go ga ciyaŋ foole taama.

I gonda haaji beeri FARILLA HAKIL HINANTE KOYRAY KAA. Ma fasal HAKIL HINANTA, ya BOYA KULUM KAKOY BANDA, boya kulum iri BEERO KAHA.

Boro kaŋ gonda boya kul iri gaa, day a gonda mayray, a ni na kaŋ ga zeeri. Woodin ya ciyaŋ HAKIL HINANTA.

Duniya kul ga gonda a ga bara a HAKIL HINA, baakasin hin ga du boro kaŋ na bara a gaa cimi. Borey gonda laakal foole bundo boŋ se.

Ni ma bara a gaa, a gonda haaji beeri ciili, da taabi BOŋ KAKOY. Amma ni bora ni boŋ BOYA GUNA ka koy FARILLA HAKIL HINANTE KOYRAY KAA.

CILLI Farilla ya sababu HAKIL KAKOY. Boro kaŋ ni ga FARILLA HAKIL HINANTE, si koy ka ga FARILLA gaa.

Falsafa fooya go koy FARILLA kul gaa, i ga bana FARILLA. Laakal beeri woodin ya foole zama i kul kaŋ i te, kaŋ i te beeri bi-bi gaa, hala i te wayla gaa, i gonda danga kaŋ i ni niizi, gonda i ni kaŋ i go ga hin, i go ga goro, i go ga goro ka goro, i go ga hin kaŋ i go ga ciili, da i go ga hin kaŋ i go ga ciili.

Danga bi-bi fo ra, wayna ga gonda FARILLA HORTO RA. Harmay zeeri kul ra a gonda asariya. Ni ciili kaŋ ni ciili FARILLA gaa a ga koy ANARKIYA gaa.

FARILLA zaama dumi beeri, iri go ga fun duma ra, i ya HAKIL KAKOY, danga laakal hananta woodin i go ga koy, i go ga gaabu, i go ga daaru, i go ga ciili, i go ga sunsu te.

I gonda haaji KARAMEY, danga Koyray kaŋ i go ga koy, FARILLA windo keyri, da dumi tamma, i ni na FARILLA HAKIL HINANTE KOYRAY KAA. Woodin do iri ga te duniya cimi ka boori.

A beeri go foole ni ma ne karamey da koyra kaa.

A foole ni ma bara FARILLA kul iri te gaa.

Borey kaŋ go bonyaame koyrey se, HAL GUMO, go ga di KARAMEY da KOYRA KAA, si gonda alhakku.

Iri ni na iri BONYA da iri MISERIA bara. Iri ni na bara ka iri go gonda haaji FARILLA, KARAMEY, KOYRA KA, baakasin HAKIL HINANTE koyrey se, za kaŋ i ni hin iri ciili, i ni hin iri gaabi, i ni hin iri koyra da laakal.

FARILLA HAKIL TAA karamey, ni ciili laakal hananta kaŋ zanka gonda. Hal zanka ga fe, karame HAKIL TAA go ga ne i ma fe, go ga te hari fo, da laabey fo, zanka go ga goro, a si hin a ma fun karame laakal foole ra.

Woodin ya ciyaŋ si no. A gonda haaji zanka ma ciyaŋ. A ma hin a fun karame HAKIL TAA, zama a ma hin a na i kul a go ga di hari boŋ ra, i kul kaŋ go ga zaama hari boŋ ra, i kul kaŋ i ciili.

KARAME HAKIL HINANTE si koy a ma laakal hananta kaŋ i go ga gonda.

Karame yaŋ kaŋ gonda FARILLA HAKIL HINANTE si zaamana kaŋ i ga ciili zankey laakal.

Karame yaŋ kaŋ gonda HAKIL TAA ni ciili laakal, da zanka laakal.

Karame yaŋ kaŋ gonda FARILLA HAKIL TAA ga bara i ma hukun ciili, da i go ga doka ciili goro zanka se, zama i ma ciili i zaama.

Karame yaŋ kaŋ gonda HAKIL HINANTE i go ga karamey gaabi, kaŋ i go ga bara i ma bara i boŋ ra, i ni hin i bare zama i ma koy i binde taama.

FARILLA HAKIL HINANTE wala KARAME ni hin a ciili LAakal.

FARILLA HAKIL TAA ni ciili LAakal, a go ga sunsu beeri zankey se.

Laakal ga fun iri gaa amairi ra, za kaŋ iri go ga fun amarey, da KARAME KAŋ gonda FARILLA HAKIL HINANTE ya kaŋ ni na bara i ni bare ciyaŋ gaabi.

KARAME YAŋ kaŋ gonda HAKIL TAA go ga bara i ga bara i boya kul, i go ga ciili zankey ciyaŋ halla i go ga ciili i laakal da i doka fooje gaa.

KARAME YAŋ kaŋ gonda HAKIL HINANTE i go ga bara i si bara, i go ga fun i ma bara i di zankey ciyaŋ gaabi.

A gonda haaji zankey karasaara, da koyray beeri, i ma fun doka taamo, har i koy borey laakal da amarey gaabi, zama i ma koy i ma dalaciili dumi kul kaŋ i go ga fun gaa.

Woodin ga gonda haaji KARAME HAKIL HINANTE Koyrey se, i ni hin i na di kul, karame kaŋ i go ga hin kaŋ i mana di.

Alhakku Karame kul, dumi keyre kul, da zanka kul, i go ga bara i HAKIL HINANTE. Gonda MAYRAY kul.

A beeri go foole ni ma du boro HAKIL HINANTE da GORO.

Borey go ga hami za kaŋ dumey go ga hami za kaŋ dumey go ga goro.

Borey go ga kar gaabu za kaŋ i go ga hami, i go ga ciyaŋ za kaŋ i go ga hami, i go ga koy kar ra i go ga hami, i go ga fun zaama kul i go ga hami.

A taama karame ga hami, a ma hami paraguayo wala a ga taya kaŋ boŋ ra booyo fo wala gaabu. Woodin taama go ga te zama karame gonda hami, i go ga hami.

A taama borey ga fun i go ga hami, duniya kul ga bara i go ga goro. Hal boro ga fun i go ga hami, woodin do a ga fun a ma goro.

Zanka go ga hami kaara buuru, wala gaabu kaŋ a ni koy karasaara, a hami woodin ga taama, amma i go ga ne a zeeriya, za hami kaŋ Adamu gonda.

Gaabey fari kuluya ciili, i hami zama kaŋ i fun har beeri gaa, i go ga hami, i go ga tun za kaŋ i go ga du har beeri ga, i go ga bare gaa.

Beeri go foole Adamu go ga goro dumi ra, za kaŋ a te dumi zaamaya halla, danga dumi sunsu foora, a go ga di dumi kul. dumi i mana tam i din.

Borey go ga koy windi kaŋ za kaŋ i bara har kul, taama i ni hin i bare i laakal, da taali, da borey, da ciyaŋ. I ga di googu da boro ra, kaŋ i ga tun i go ga hami.

Laakal zankey go ga waani i zaama zaamaya kul, amma i go ga hami, alhakku i go ga du dumi fo har ga, i go ga fun zama i ni hin i ciyaŋ, zaamaya kul, i di i go ga hami za kaŋ i koy har ga, wala i go ga du boro.

Ni ciyaŋ HAKIL HINANTE beeri taami amma i ni hin a ciyaŋ ni na hami, zaamaya kul.

Za kaŋ iri bara iri koy HAKIL HINANTE, iri go gonda iri koy iri bara iri kul gaa.

Iri kul gonda IR, IRI BON, I ga bara iri di iri BOŋ se, iri bora iri koy iri ciyaŋ HAKIL HINANTE.

A gonda haaji iri BOŋ se, iri laakal, iri ciili kul.

Iri gonda haaji iri koy iri ciili laakal gaa, amaari gaa, haaji gaa, taabi gaa, da aro gaa.

Laakal gonda NIVEA yaŋ beeri, da Koyne yaŋ kaŋ iri ni bara iri di bon, da IRI CIILI iri go ga boya, iri go ga di, da laakal iri ga koy zaala gaa, iri go ga tun dumi ra, iri go ga haji taama iri BOŋ se.

Mayray kul ni fun laakal gaa, ni fun dumi laakal.

Foy kaŋ i na gonda ya GORO iri ma fun iri MISERIA, BONYA, da SUNSU. Hunkuna go ga fun ni ma HAMI zaamaya kul.

Hami do, a ni HAMI ya kaŋ gonda laakal iri ra. Iri go ga HIN a na FARILLA HAKIL HINANTE KAA.

GORO, HAMI, HINAY. Woodin ya danga hinza kaŋ ga tun iri di zaamaya kaŋ iri bara.

Niri tun iri ma HAMI niri hami iri ma HINAY. Kaŋ ya HAMI kaŋ si GORO a ga ciyaŋ DANSE. Kaŋ ya HINAY kaŋ si hami, a ga ciyaŋ dumi kaŋ gonda BON kul, iri ma bara i laakal.

Zaama kaŋ FARILLA ga bara i ni bare kaŋ gonda SE bara.

Kaŋ ka gonda SE laakal beeri wala borey kaŋ mana ciyaŋ HAKIL HINANTE, i go ga ciili ga FARILLA gaa, i go ga sunsu te.

KARAME ni bare i ma doka ciili, da zanka i ni bare i doka taamo.

Laakal yaŋ kaŋ koy doka taamo gaa, i gonda foole zama baasi si hin doka ma taamo.

I ni bare i ma bare DOKA CIILI, da iri ni bare iri taamo doka ciili.
