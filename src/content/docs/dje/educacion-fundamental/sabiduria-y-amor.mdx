Baykaliɲe da baakasinay ga hima ka hinka sinda da jiney. Baykaliɲe baakasinay laala, a ga halakal tammaasa no. Baakasinay baykaliɲe laala, a ga hin ka iri ndaŋ halli zuru “BAAKASINAY YAASU AMAA HAKILAY GUMEY BAASAKASINAY”.

Goe ka duumi da zantey binde, amaa a ga laakal ka iri woyna laakalhaya te iri ra.
