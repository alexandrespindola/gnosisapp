Borro adamize fundu be sanda halabu jirkaciŋ gonda, danga mate kaŋ ga ti, fundu beere waati gaadi kaŋ sinda dumi.

Hayyana, dumo, hayni, a ga ti alhaalika da gundo beeri kaŋ bora fu goy te danga borey kulu.

A ga fee gunde ka bay kaŋ waati sintina iri gonda, iri ga goro a cire mo boŋ, ka bare borey kulu danga halabu jirkaciŋ mikroskopu.

Iri sintin ga goro danga halabu kaŋ sinda darza, iri ma goro iri fundu haybara, kuray zeeri, da beene taale tammaase.

Ayyaŋa, beene taale ya. Kuray zeeri beere maay ga goro waati halla, kuray zeeri beere maay ga goro waati beene taale. Haybara kulu sanda dumbe da dayba ya. Kuray zeeri kulu ya beene taale da zaamani ya.

Hayyana borey ga sintin waati gaadi kaŋ sinda dumi, amma dumo goyo ga cindi funfun waati beere goy ga cindi ka cindi ka zay.

Boro beere ga bendi ya gonda ya ka bare waati. Halabu jirkaciŋ kaŋ gonda naŋkanya saaharay hinka taale kuukuy kaŋ iri ga goro, bora beere goro nda jiiri tamanji, ama naŋkanya saaharay hinka taale ga goro gaadi kaŋ bora tamanji goro, bora tamanji ga goro jiiri tamanji yaŋ kulu kaŋ planeta goro jiiri miliyoŋ yaŋ.

Waati Zoospermo ga margu nda hamni dumo ga sintin. Halabu kaŋ bora fundu ga sintin a gonda cuarentay halabu tacinji hinka.

Halabu tacinji ga bare gene, gene daŋsa ndaŋsa wala koyne kaŋ te halabu tacinji gonda.

Gene yaŋ ga taji ka du kaŋ zama borey ga te, zama borey beeri kulu ga te molekulu beere ndaŋ ndaŋ kaŋ ga zeere gaadi kaŋ sinda bayyan.

Alhaalika gene beere ya goro jiri hinka gooruyaŋ da jiri taata gooruyaŋ cire.

Geneyaŋ gonda alama boreyaŋ. Ayyaŋa psikolojia naŋ ka bare hamni kaŋ hayyana yaŋ.

Waati elektrotik da aatom sayaŋ, baasi sinda gonda ka gonda kaŋ duusa kaŋ a haybara kulu kaŋ duusa gene nda halabu tacinji nda hayyana nda kaana yaŋ.

A ga faham kaŋ fundu gonda duusa haro faasi nda.

Dumo goyo ra, dumo beeri yaŋ beere ga yaara borey wanka; dumo beeri kulu gonda goyo sayaŋ, dumo beeri ga bayyan a waati da a hara yaŋ.

Hanji goyo iri gonda dumo hinka taale kaana, dumo taata goro goyo iri gonda goyo beeri kaŋ gonda goyo dumo da goyo hanji dumo.

A ga alhaalika sayaŋ faham kaŋ boro ga hayni da kaŋ borey ga bu.

Sayaŋ beeri maay ga gonda kaŋ a ga ti alhaalika kaŋ boro ga hayni nda borey ga hayni nda alamize asaman ra.

Dumo taata taale boro ga hayni, dumo wayta a ga cindi dumo taale gonda a alhaalika sayaŋ da dumo sayaŋ ka te.

Waati Fontanela boro hayni ga deeri jiiri hinka wala jiiri taata a ga ti alama kaŋ dumo cerebro spinal goyo ga cindi.

Sayaŋ beeri maay ga gonda kaŋ bora teene gonda tunyaŋ da tunyaŋ wo ga te borey fundu kaŋ goyaŋ, borey goyaŋ kaŋ borey maay ga te.

Borize beeri maay ga dari tunyaŋ da borey maay tunyaŋ "Borey Kayan".

Za tunyaŋ, borize ga bare nda boro beeri maay ga bare tunyaŋ nda FANTASIA.

Sayaŋ beeri maay ga gonda kaŋ tunyaŋ hinka yaŋ. Daŋ taale borey tunyaŋ mekanika da dumo taale borey tunyaŋ zama goy yaŋ.

Danga nda borey men taale dumo mekanika wala jindi morboza infraconsciente wala subjekta yaŋ.

Dumo tunyaŋ automatika ga goy cire borey bayray gooru.

Hotone alhaalika yaŋ, sine morboza yaŋ, taale morboza nda dumo hinka yaŋ, borize morboza yaŋ, ala, ga te ka goy dumo mekanika ra.

Analisizin gooru borey men ala borey faham kaŋ hotone alhaalika yaŋ da toono tiŋyaŋ ga te dumo mekanika yaŋ.

CASTIDAD ABSOLUTA sinda darza halla dumo mekanika gonda.

A ga bayyan zama tunyaŋ gooru dumo ga bare gaadi kaŋ boro ga tunyaŋ mekanika, subjekta, infraconsciente, subconsciente.

Takara kulu kaŋ boro men darza AUTO ga tee borey darza, amma tunyaŋ sub, dumo mekanika yaŋ, infraconsciente, subconsciente ga cindi goyo automatika nda alama alhaalika yaŋ, sumergidas.

Iri ga bendi CASTIDAD INTEGRA uni tolal, borey ga tunyaŋ gooru, bora ga dumo mekanika yaŋ da dumo INCONSCÍENTE AUTOMÁTICA, SUBCONSCIENTE, SUMERGIDA.

Bora sinda tunyaŋ gooru borey daŋ da SEKSO da TUNYAŊ.

Danga Meditazione beeri bora ga bare tunyaŋ mekanika yaŋ da SUB-IMAGINACION da INFRA-IMAGINACION AUTOMÁTICA kulu, ga tee tunyaŋ gooru, objetiva.

Tunyan OBJETIVA ga tee zama tunyaŋ beeri gonda goy, bora sinda tee kaŋ fundi nda telefone, radio, jirgin sama yaŋ.

Tunyan WAYBO waati jiriyaŋyaŋ ga fee dumo zama tee zama fundu. Borey bayyan kaŋ waybora kulu bora ga tee ka bare dumo psiqis ra.

A ga bendi Waybo waati jiriyaŋ ga guna jiri alhaalika yaŋ, gundo alhaalika yaŋ, da bora ga haŋ kaŋ kaana yaŋ, a ga koy ka goy dumo psiqis ra kaŋ a te.

Waybo waati jiriyaŋ sinda tunyaŋ borey haŋ alkol wala sumfu wala guna alhaalika nga, nda nga zama wo kulu ga boora tee yaŋ zama fundu.

Borey ga bay ka yaafa waybo preñada beeri darza yaŋ da borey yaŋ.

Borize beeri maay kaŋ sinda bora yaafa da faham, borey tunya ka dira waybo preñada. Waybo jeeriyaŋ da bora janga yaŋ beeri saabu, a ga cindi dumo ra, borey men a ga tee zama fundu.

Gonda ga bay tunyaŋ gooru yaŋ, bora ga tunyaŋ waybo waati jiriyaŋ, sinda tunyaŋ borey guna ala alhaalika nga.

A du waati kaŋ direzza ga bay ka tee borey ka bay borey janga beeri kaŋ gonda borey hayyana yaŋ.

A ga konguru kaŋ dumo kaŋ ga tee kirista nda democrazia, bora sinda bay ka day borey yaŋ ka bora hayyana yaŋ. A ga alkaaci guna millare waybo preñada kaŋ sinda baani wala boro halla, bora ga baani borey direzza da dumo ga borey dumo, bora ga yafe borey taala taala zama ka goro fundu nda kaŋ borey taasiyaŋ.

Jindi infrahumane yaŋ kaŋ ga tee jama kaŋ iri goro kaŋ a ra, baani nga ya ga koyne ka bayyan kaŋ demokrasia sinda.

Spitali nda sale borey bay sinda bare nga janga, zama borey te spitali nga waybo yaŋ ga du kaŋ borey ga yafe.

Borey yafe borey hayra, borey tee garza, borey tee ginde zama borey yaŋ.

Borey ga hayra ga tee waybo talibiyaŋ zama waati jiriyaŋ, jindi kala, alhaalika, borey saabu kulu bare borey janga alhaalika.

Iri ga bendi ka faham kaŋ dumo ka tee gooru beere da kaŋ janga sinda zama janga kulu ga cindi dumo yaŋ. A ga boora borey ka borey ga dirwayborey yaŋ zama borey tee talibiyaŋ, a ga bonkooni borey ga boora wala borey dumo kaŋ.

Dumo goyo ra iri goro kaŋ ra sinda haybo nda dumbo, zama iri borey dumi yaŋ da iri gonda hukuŋ sayaŋ.

Iri ga bendi demokrasia dumo, so Irin sinda bendi ka kaŋ komunista.
