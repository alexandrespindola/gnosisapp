Ngaari jiiri wayboroŋo banandi takarda, jiiri wayboroŋo din banda mo fassariya.

Jiiri warankaŋo ra, alboro ga hima saabu, jiiri boŋo ra zaki, jiiri wayboroŋo ra rakumi, jiiri guuruŋo ra kole, jiiri sayaŋo ra ga dogo, jiiri wayzey ra zumbe, jiiri huney ra, dugu nda day.

Alwaati ga fee hari kul, alwaati yaŋ gundekoy kaŋ gonda yadda kaŋ ga salaŋ care nda banda hal sanni ma si te a se.

Hay kulu kaŋ alborey loxo te, alwaati ga hallaka.

"FUGIT IRRÉPARABILE TEMPUS", alwaati kaŋ ga zuru si hin ka hanandi.

Alwaati ga zumandi hari kulu kaŋ funa ka hunandi hari kulu kaŋ ga hasara da.

Gimsi ga hima baakasine, si hin ka tugu, hal kaŋ ne taŋay kambe.

Gimsi ga yee alborey faada kaŋ ga yaabi, amma hay fo yaŋ te yaabi nda yaabiŋ ka kaŋ.

Waati kaŋ maay jaŋ, wayborey kaŋ yaŋ ga faham, gimsi yaŋ si te ŋaŋay.

Alborey kul gonda beeje kaŋ yaŋ ga funa ka funa da gimsi, amma gimsi yaŋ ga bara.

Gimsi go bann ka ciya jiiri guuruŋo nda sayaŋo banda haray ga zuru hal maay ma kaŋ.

Masiba beeri beeri gimsi ra yaŋ, masi te gimsi amma ka faham kaŋ yaŋ te gimsi, yaŋ ga cimi kaŋ yaŋ yaŋ si te gimsi.

Hay kaŋ ga bisara gimsi ra, hinne kaŋ yaŋ go mate kaŋ yaŋ koy da.

Nafsi, manafsi, zama si ga bisara jiiri nda bayray, a ga taalamay, a ga boori, ka baŋ a se, yaŋ ga ne: "Aɲa nda sura hal kubri."

Nafsi gimsi kaŋ ga boori ga nafasay kaŋ yaŋ si hin ka misali fo ga kaŋ.

Wayborey yaŋ ga bay kaŋ gimsi yaŋ, bannyaŋ kaŋ ga bara kaŋ ga hana ka yaŋ ma saabu nda kayya kaŋ yaŋ ga funa ka nafasay kaŋ yaŋ si hin ka kayya fo ga kaŋ.

Manafsi ga tugu manafsi, manafsi ga tugu manafsi fo ga taalamay kaŋ yaŋ ga kaŋ.

Bindi fo manafsi ga tugu bindi fo.

A go bayray bayrandi nda kaŋ waati kaŋ ga hana ga kayna gonda yadda kaŋ ga hana.

Borey kul bine yaŋ ga bisara jiiri nda banda amma a ga halaci, a ga ciya tondiyaŋ hal kaŋ ne, borey te wogoyaŋ, hanjiyaŋ, borey din ga bisara.

Wayborey yaŋ go ga zama, wayborey zama haybey zumandi yaŋ, wayborey yaŋ si bay zama kaŋ yaŋ zaame, wayborey yaŋ bayray zumandi.

Hay kulu kaŋ yaŋ yaŋ funa ka ciya gimsi ga kaŋ ga manafsi fasa.

Waati kaŋ yaŋ gonda faham ka maay kaŋ ga kaŋ, yaŋ ga kaŋ ka zaame.

Gimsi gonda maana beeri, ka hagu nda ka bare kaŋ yaŋ fasa Nafsi.

Waati kaŋ kayna nda maay ga kaŋ, ga bare.

A ga boori ka gonda wayborey kaŋ yaŋ gonda Nafsi.

Alboro kaŋ te gimsi taalamay ra, ka taalamay ma bisara.

Dunia ra a go kaŋ nda gimsi beerey kaŋ yaŋ gonda Nafsi, Arhat Gnostiques yaŋ, ka bisara kaŋ yaŋ ga hima loure.

Gimsi beeri kaŋ fasa Nafsi yaŋ taalamay, a hima Taalamay, baakasine nda bara kaŋ yaŋ ga zaame.

Gimsi beeri kaŋ si gonda Nafsi a ga bisara kaŋ Alwaati yaŋ.

Gimsi ga taalamay ka dunia tun ka hunandi, nga ga Budha, MOISÉS, HERMES, RAMARKRISHNA, DANIEL, EL SANTO LAMA, nda yaŋ din kul.

A ga bisara kaŋ, a ga kargay, a ga naasaray kaŋ borey ma bara gimsi.

Hay kaŋ si gonda maa a go divinal a go real, a gonda sura wayboroŋo: Taalamay, baakasine nda sanni.

Divinal hima baaba taalamay ga zam.

Borey ra baaba hima taalamay, borey ra maa hima baakasine, ize ga hima sanni.

Gimsi baaba ga funa ize, baaba kaŋ gonda gimsi si hin ka gunde nda ize ma koyrandi ka bare.

Maa ga zaame si hin ka gunde si koyrandi ga maa nda bare ka din yaŋ te diini.

Borey kaŋ yaŋ si ga baakasine, si ga Maa borey yaŋ zuru nda kambe duma kaŋ yaŋ ga hallaci.

Ize yaŋ si gonda yadda kaŋ yaŋ ma kayni, wayborey ra borey kul si ga zaame.

Wani taalamay baaba, wani ga zeeri baakasine baaba borey kaŋ yaŋ yaŋ ga haŋ kaŋ ma koyandi hay kaŋ yaŋ si gonda maa.

Ize kaŋ ga hana ga baaba, a ga zuru zama kaŋ divinal.

Canji Nafsi si gonda wani, ka baaba haliaci maa halaci.

Canji Nafsi yaŋ taalamay, baakasine nda bara zaame.

Baaba yaŋ ga taalamay, maa yaŋ ga baakasine yaŋ zama kaŋ yaŋ si zaame.
