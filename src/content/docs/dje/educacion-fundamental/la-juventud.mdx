Way dumey zanka kayri hinka ga, jiiri iyye-iyye kulu. Kayri sintina fu jiiri 21 gaa, ka ban jiiri 28 gaa. Kayri hinka fu jiiri 28 gaa, ka ban jiiri 35 gaa.

Zanka koyrayyaŋ dake fu fuɲe ra, daŋ-duu ra, nda laabu ra. Zanka kaŋ g'a kayandi FUNDAMENTAL HIMMA n'a boŋ, sanda kaŋ g'a tee ALHERI nda BOONDO.

Zanka kaŋ g'a kayandi koyrayyaŋ tilley boŋ, a koyra tilley no.

Boru dumi beeri goy ga ngey fundi sintina kunda gonda, zama ngey ma fundi naŋ ka koy gonda bana gonda.

Zanka-ize takamba dumi tilley se, ngey ga kaŋ tabbatanda-wayboraŋ kambe ra.

Zanka-ize ceecendiyaŋ ga te gundey kaŋ gonda daynandi yagay zama arkuseŋ, jiiri waranza gaa, daa beeri gonda.

FUNDAMENTAL HIMMA si, zanka-ize ga te borrey daa gumo: a go ga hima kambe dumo, daa nda dumi bine gonda.

Hay kulu kaŋ bora ga tee nga fundi ra, a go ga bara sanda saawara gumo jiiri waranza kaŋ gonda banda.

Goy beeri kulu kaŋ adduɲa bora go ga bay, dumi kaŋ gonda, goy dumi beeri ga sintin jiiri waranza kaŋ gonda sintina.

Bora kaŋ kaa jiiri waranza gaa, a go ga hima sanda yaaru beeri kaŋ gonda kaa ra, kaŋ gonda di ngey bandirey kaŋ te ka kul sanda kul.

Jiiri waranza gaa, boru nda wayboraŋ kul, ngey go ga di ngey gundo nda bine-gondo. D'i kayri sintina kayra taaga, i go ga tee bine-gondo kaŋ gonda gumo, ka ngey naŋ te.

Bine-gondo taakey g'a may bine-gondo taakey kaŋ gonda zanka-ize gonda. FUNDAMENTAL HIMMA si, arkuseŋ ga tee bine-gondo gumo.

Zanka-ize hima zaari kaŋ go ga kaaga. Halle wayboraŋ gaabo no ga hima zanka-ize gonda, amma tilley no, a si hanse.

Zanka-ize gonda basarri kaŋ go ga fundi, da gundey si gaabandi. Dumi boriyaŋ zanka-ize kaŋ gonda gundey gaabandi nda basarri kaŋ go ga fundi.

FUNDAMENTAL HIMMA si, zanka-ize ga tee bine-taakey, borrey, goy-ga-laamey, harey-sigaamey, bine-maysinkaarey, bine-koytawey, duumi-boroŋ, naaci-boroŋ, bine-biyaamey, bine-ceey, goy-ga-boroŋ, guuru-boroŋ, guuru-boroŋ, guruba-boroŋ, alkaali-boroŋ, daŋyaŋ, dumi.

Mocedad no ga hima zaari kaŋ gonda kaaga, kaŋ takarda ga ba ka di ngey gaabo kaŋ gonda mocedad tilley.

Arkuseŋ ga goy zanka-ize ma gaabo donda ka koy gaaru.

Boru gaabo ga way ga tee ka farga adduɲa, d'i kayri koyra kaŋ gonda FUNDAMENTAL HIMMA.

Zanka-ize gonda, i go ga di bine-gondo tilley, kaŋ go ga kand'ey kayra diɲaɲaŋ.

YON ga di zanka-ize kayri gonda ka gaabandandi ka tee gaabo.

YON ga ba zama hay ka tee a se kaŋ gonda bine gonda, dumi kaŋ gonda beeri, wala hal a ma tee arkuseŋ kaŋ si ga ba gumo.

Arkuseŋ go ga ba zama ngey ma di ngey gaabu kaŋ te ga kaŋ, daa nda gaabu dumi kaŋ gonda.

Zanka-ize si di kaŋ tee ngey maa, dumi boru maa, amma tilley wayboraŋ go ga tee ka farga a boro.

Daa kaŋ tee a go ga hanse, nda daa go ga tee ANIMALES INTELECTUALES diɲaɲaŋ gumo.

Hay si ka tee ga hanse gumo. HAY GUNDO ZAMA FUDI.
