Iriyo ra hũnkoonye nda sanni-kõoɲe, baaba nda karakooni go ga tiŋa n'aŋaŋ kambe kaŋ binde dumiyaŋ, amma wo tiŋa naŋ aŋaŋ zama i ma miila mate.

Bayra dumiyaŋ ga boŋ taaga sosay. Baaba, karakooni, koyra nda tiraŋ binde binde-goykooni kulu, borey kulu go ga hã zama aŋaŋ ma miila a hinna, dumi, takadda, biyaŋ taagandiyaŋ.

Binde-goykooni ganda sanda taaji. Taaki go ga beeri zama binde-goy koŋ binde haray, zama a ma goro laada, dumi, karakooni ra.

Binde-goykooni ganda wãnde maay koyne na binde haray respecte, niŋ borey miila ra. Hala niŋ borey mana miila sanda i, borey go ga tiŋa borey go ga beeri, ŋaa, jahilli.

Borey kulu go ga hã zama borey kulu ma binde-goy koŋ, borey kulu go ga hã zama borey ma zama a ma binde haray beeri. Borey si ga hã zama borey ma miila sanda i. Borey go ga tiŋa borey go ga fahamay, bayray, alhaali, hala borey go ga hã zama borey ma goro sanda i, zama a ma zama i mana, zama borey ma miila sanda i.

Borey na binde koy sosay. ŋwaaray zama cebeeykooni nda i cebe taaki kaŋ go ga zu tiraŋ, tele, etc.

Cebe taaki go ga zu binde-goy haray! Sumbu alhabu fulan! Sanfu fulan! Cebe fulan! Sumbu nišan! Sumbu soho! Sumbu soho! A ga hima dũm! A ga hima haldũm! A ga hima hã! Day yaŋ na zama ni si naŋ aŋaŋ, zaama zaama!

Baaba go ga hã zama a ma aŋaŋ a ize gunde a miila gaabi nda karakooni go ga hãa nda azaba, nda gunda, daŋ a gunde day izey mana taŋ a karakooni miila nda gaabi.

Borey taaga go ga hã zama borey taaga ma binde koy. Taaki kaŋ borey go ga hã zama i ma binde koy wõlõ sosay day bõrey go ga duŋguri taari danga ra.

Duŋguri go ga goykooni kaŋ borey go ga hã borey ma binde koy. Duŋguri go ga goykooni kaŋ tiŋa kaŋ borey go ga miila. ŋwarey zama niŋ borey kaŋ miila sanda i, i go ga zu gaabi, siberi, kasu, goy danga, danga, danga.

Karakooni, baaba, tira mana naŋ zama borey ma miila mate.

Borey go ga baa zama borey ma borey koŋ zama i ma miila sanda i go ga tiŋa hala borey kulu binde borey binde borey goro sanda goykooni nda i go ga baa zama borey kulu ma miila sanda i, zama i goro sosay.

Baaba, karakooni, patron, etc. go ga hã sosay.

A ga zindi sosay taaki kaŋ borey mana respecte borey, zama borey ma binde haray beeri, i ma gunde, i ma gunde, i ma binde haray koy.

Beeri go ga hã zama a ma wayboro gunde a miila gunde, takadda, etc. wayboro go ga hã a ma ciya sanda.

Maniɲoo, beeri nda wayboro go ga goykoŋ zama borey mana hã zama i ma respecte binde haray.

Maniɲoo kulu mana hã zama borey ma binde koy. borey kulu mana go ga hã zama i ma miila sanda i, i ma adini duŋguri, i ma parti duŋguri.

I go ga borey gaabi zama i ma miila miila duŋguri ndaŋ i mana borey miila mate.

Iize binde go ga ŋwa sosay, iize binde go ga hã sosay, iize binde go ga hiŋgari sosay, iize go ga zaamaray, iize mana ciya baabu, iize si ga ciya, iize si ga hã ciya.

Iize nda hihame borey go ga hã zama i ma ciya, iize go ga hã zama i ma ciya.

Iize ma hihame borey go ga duŋguri borey ma miila mate, zaamaray mana duŋguri borey ma miila mate zama i mana ciya zama i ga ze, i ga bu.

A ga hiŋga zama borey ma goro borey go ga hã zama i ma ciya sosay.

Borey binde go ga binde koy zaamaray. Hõre taaki kaŋ baaba nda karakooni go ga baa i ma ciya. I go ga ŋwa iize nda hihame borey ma miila.

Binde kaŋ borey na gunde, binde go ga tiŋa binde kaŋ borey na koy.

A ga hima zama karakooni ma binde kuuje.

A go ga kaŋborey ma karakooni ma borey miila taaki beeri zama borey mana binde koy koyne.

A go ga borey ma karakooni ma izey miila mate.

Karakooni go ga hã borey ma izey hã zama i ma fahamu, miila, bayray.

Borey kulu kaŋ go ga fahamu mana taŋ binde kulu. A go ga hima zama bora ma ceeci. Fahamu, ceeci, taŋ bora mana taŋ.

Wõlõ borey ga tiŋa zama borey ma ceeci, borey mana taŋ zama borey ma miila, fahamu. Day bora fahamu borey si go ga hã borey ma taŋ.

A mana nafii borey ma binde dumiyaŋ gunde bora mana bay borey ma miila nda borey go ga taamu, borey go ga goro, sanda mačine, sanda borey go ga goy borey baaba, bamba, etc.

Borey go ga goy, borey go ga zaama buuru, borey go ga zu office nda office nda buuru, i ma dabarje zama borey ma iize, hã mana goro nda i go ga dumi, nda i go ga zu karakooni day a ga hã borey ma mana dumi.

Mahatma Gandi ti borey gaabi. Maniɲoo, pastorey go ga goro a dõri gunde borey go ga tũku borey ma ciya borey kiristaŋ.

Gandi mana taŋ pastorey dumi, Gandi mana diɲe, Gandi ti fahamu, Gande ga respecte, din kulu.

Maniɲoo Mahatma go ga tiŋa: "ay ti Brahmani, Yahudi, Kiristaŋ, Mahameto, etc."

Mahatma ti fahamu kaŋ adini kulu go ga hã zama borey ma ciya borey go ga dumi dafa-dafa.

Day borey go ga taŋ dumi wõlõ go ga zaamaray zindi, binde zindi.

Day go ga daɲe dumi wõlõ, zama borey mana fahamu.

Day go ga fahamu borey mana duŋguri borey ma taŋ wõlõ daɲe.

Binde kaŋ go ga taŋ, binde kaŋ mana taŋ, binde kaŋ mana hã zama a ma taŋ go ga tiŋa binde kaŋ mana bay.

Borey mana bay haray si ti taŋ wõlõ bora ma taŋ.

Borey mana bay haray ti ceeci, miila, zaamaray.

Gaskiya ti ŋwa borey mana bay tamma zaama gaskiya mana dumiyaŋ kaŋ bora go ga taŋ, borey mana taŋ borey si ga hã.

Gaskiya mana borey ma taŋ dumi borey ma daɲe. Gaskiya ti bora ma miila, bora ma goro, borey ma fahamu.

Karakooni go ga hã borey kulu zama borey ma zu dumi gaabi halla i ga bay.

Borey go ga hã borey ma karakooni ma zama kaŋ borey mana koy, borey go ga zu ize binde.

A go ga hiŋga borey gaabi kaŋ borey dumi mana hã, borey go ga borey iize nda hihame borey binde taamu zama i ma binde koŋ sanda borey go ga hã.

A ga borey ma respecte ize nda hihame borey zindi borey ma binde haray.

Karakooni mana hã zama borey ma ize nda hihame borey binde koy.

Da borey ma tiŋa binde kaŋ borey ma tiŋa borey ma miila, da borey ma naŋ mate borey ma miila.

Binde ti bayray zamo borey ma karakooni ma bora zamo zamo.
