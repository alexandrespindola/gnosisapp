Adam-ize ga hima kaŋ hayyaŋ-siyayyaŋ gonda laakal wala laakal-da-gabal, hal i ma nuna kaŋ hayyaŋ-siyayyaŋ gonda laakal wala laakal-da-gabal yaŋ, i n'i ce da laakal-duma.

Ir mo, ir go ga tabbatar kaŋ laakal go boro ra, kayna wala gaakasin-ka-zambara si no, hay fo no kaŋ gonda laakal-da-bataŋ sanda dayankaŋ tammaase, kaŋ sinda doŋ bannyaŋ-tammaase kulu ra.

Laakal daraja no ga naŋ ir ma ir boŋ bay.

Laakal go ga naŋ ir ma hay kaŋ yaŋ ga ti ni bay, ni go hano, ni go hay kaŋ yaŋ ni bay, ni go hay kaŋ yaŋ ni si bay.

Falsafa Firji-koyra go ga koyray kaŋ boro boŋ no ga boŋ bay.

Ir boŋ no ga bay iri go da laakal waati fo ra, wala ir sinda.

Boro boŋ no ga boŋ laakal bay, nda a go no waati fo ra, wala a si no.

Boro boŋ no, boro kul sinda, kaŋ ga hin ka bay waati fo gaŋ, waati din banda, a sinda laakal da gaskiyaŋ, a laakal go ga dogon gumo, din banda a ga tam a gundukoyne, wala a ga duuru sanda gunduko tammaase.

A hayyaŋ kaŋ ma bay kaŋ laakal go dabare-boro ra, a si hay kaŋ ga duumi, kaŋ ga dagandi.

Asali, laakal go dabare-boro ra kaŋ i n'i ce boro-hayyaŋ-siyayyaŋ, a go ga dogon gumo.

Waati kayna, kayna no laakal go ga bare; hayyaŋ-siyayyaŋ go ga taale, go ga kar karuwa, go ga taamo, go ga bu, d.h.d.h. laakal go ga dogon gumo, waati tammaasa kayna ra no a go ga bare:

Boro fundi dumi no da hinra, amma a go ga cimandi kaŋ a go ga bare, a si ga dogondiyaŋ kaŋ a go ga hinra, kaŋ a laakal go ga dogon.

Nda boro fo bare, a ga maa yaaji gumo a boŋ se, a ga faham da hinra, a feejiyaŋ, a dambaraŋ.

Fundi hinra gumo no da feejiyaŋ, danga gumo, da kayniyaŋ gumo.

Nda bokse fo bare doŋ beraɲaaŋ gunda, a ga guna yaaji gumo jama beeriyo kulu se, a ga zuru beraɲaaŋ fo ra, laakal-dogon jama ceeci ra.

Waati kaŋ boro dogondi kaŋ a gonda laakal-dogon, ni ga hin ka cimandi kaŋ a go ga sintin ka bare.

Falsafa barey-koyra timmey kaŋ go ga dagondiyaŋ kaŋ laakal si no, hala a darja ga tiya da hayyaŋ, ga zama dogon gumo. Koyra din banda go ga dogon gumo hal i sinda laakal.

Boraŋ kaŋ go ga hima kaŋ laakal ga ti bannyaŋ-tammaasa; tammaaseyaŋ, maaɲe-haw, taŋaliyaŋ, da miɲeyyaŋ, i go ga laakal dogon gumo da gaskiyaŋ, i go ga dogon gumo.

Boraŋ kaŋ go ga dogondiyaŋ kaŋ laakal go no, amma i go ga dagondiyaŋ laakal darajey, i sinda laakal gundukoyne, laakal dogonyaŋ.

Boro kulu kaŋ bare waati kayna, a ga bay mo kaŋ darajey go da laakal kaŋ ni ga hin ka guna ni boŋ ra.

Waati Koyra. Iri go da laakal waati fo?

Boro Bareyaŋ. H̃ar fo, iri bare laakal?

Hirriyaŋ. Gunduko. Hay fo no kaŋ ga ti da laakal?

Falsafa Firji-koyra da Philokalia timmey go ga koyray kaŋ da yaayaŋ guma kaŋ ga ti dumayaŋ, ni ga hin ka bare laakal hal a ga duumi kaŋ ga hin ka koyra.

Fundi Fundi Koyra go ga zama laakal bareyaŋ. Hay kulu si zama handu biyar wala handu way cindi taaleŋ-koyriyaŋ, Kolejiyaŋ, da Jami'a Koyraŋ, nda iri fatta koyriyaŋ ra, iri go ga ti hayyaŋ kaŋ yaŋ go ga dogon.

A sinda tiyayaŋ kaŋ da yaayaŋ guma, hayyaŋ-siyayyaŋ ga hin ka ti laakal a boŋ se handu hinka boŋ.

A keyaŋ kaŋ hay keyney go no, iri ga zama iri ma ceeci da Diyoje laambiyaŋ, boraŋ din sanda BORO da Gaskiyaŋ, Buddha, Yesu, Hermes, Quetzacoatl, d.h.d.h.

Diina din sintin boraŋ gonda laakal duumante, i ti barey guma.

Asali, boraŋ sinda laakal i boŋ se. Cima kaŋ i gonda laakal duumante, a fun duuruyaŋ ra da tammaase boraŋ kulu.

Boro kaŋ go ga taale kaŋ a ma tam a fundi kulu, a ga hin ka dugu, ka tam h̃ar fo a taamo, h̃ar fo a gonda ize, wayaŋ no a baaba borey, a karam borey, amma a si zama laakal bare, a go ga tam taaley kaŋ sinda laakal, din no.

A zama kaŋ ma mayaray hay kaŋ iri salaŋ dambel hinay ra. Laakal tata go no. I se: Hinra, Bareyaŋ, Boŋ-laakal da Laakal danga.

Hayyaŋ-siyayyaŋ miskina, kaŋ i ce boro no, a go ga fun fundi hinka boŋ. A fundi fo go ga fun hinra ra, kaŋ mo i ce Bareyaŋ, kaŋ fundi mo no.

Boro kaŋ go ga dogon da kaŋ go ga hinra, a go ga cimandi kaŋ a bare zama a goo Bareyaŋ ra, amma da gaskiyaŋ waati din ra a go ga hinra.

A hima hira zaari bareyaŋ, hiriyaŋ go ga dogon zama zaari neera amma a go no doŋ, baa mo miɲey si gonda hin ka guna.

Fundi gaabo ra, boro si hay kaŋ a bay Boŋ-laakal, kaŋ mo a bay Laakal danga.

Amma boraŋ gonda beerayyaŋ, boro kulu go ga cimandi Boŋ-laakal, hayyaŋ-siyayyaŋ go ga cimandi gumo kaŋ a gonda laakal boŋ se, a si dogondiyaŋ kaŋ i ma ne a se a go ga dogon, a fundi sinda a boŋ se.

Waate kayna go no kaŋ hayyaŋ-siyayyaŋ bare, amma waate din kayna gumo no, a ga hin ka tiya halaka bare gaŋ, maaɲe guma ra, halaliye fo ra, hal fo tammaase ra, d.h.d.h.

Yaaji gumo no da gaskiyaŋ kaŋ hayyaŋ-siyayyaŋ miskina sinda dabare waate din se kaŋ a bare, kaŋ a si hin ka ceeci, kaŋ a si hin ka duumi.

Amma, Fundi Fundi Koyra go ga tabbatar kaŋ boro ga hin ka du dabare laakal se kaŋ a ga du Boŋ-laakal.

Falsafa Firji-koyra gonda dabare koyra yaŋ se, kaŋ gonda laakal BAREYAŋ.

Nda iri zama iri ma BARE LAAKAL, iri ga zama iri ma sintin da imtihan, taale, din banda iri ma tun ka hay kulu kaŋ yaŋ go ga kay iri jina tun, iri koyray fundi ra zama iri ma bare LAAKAL ka sintin koyriyaŋ ra.
