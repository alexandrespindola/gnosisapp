Baaba ga hima ka ba, ka te ka ba-yanandi mo, amma dunya yaaba, borey si ba, i si ba-yanandi mo.

Haŋ kaŋ i n'a maa baakasinay, a ga borey se zamaŋ, i ga doori taali nda anniya-laakal.

Soho borey ga hin ka ba ka te ka ba-yanandi, soojey si duumi dunya binde.

Zeyi dumi-dumi kaŋ yaŋ ga hima ka farhaŋ gumo, laakal-wasu, i si farhaŋ danga daadaamey kaŋ yaŋ gonda gaaba boŋ.

Soho zey kaana gonda karimay, i ga dinya daadaamey kaŋ yaŋ gonda gaaba boŋ, i ga te ka fun farhaŋ gumo.

Laakal ga baakasinay wi, a ga hallasi a. Tammaasaajiyaŋ, daadaamey kaŋ yaŋ gonda hasaraa, hasidiyaŋ daadaamey, hay kulu kaŋ yaŋ gonda gaaba boŋ, a ga baakasinay hallasi.

Wayborey dumi-dumi kaŋ yaŋ gonda gaaba boŋ ga hin ka te ka fun farhaŋ soho i gonda karimay kaŋ yaŋ ga dinya daadaamey kaŋ yaŋ gonda gaaba boŋ ka te ka fun jaman haray i ga care-yan kaŋ i ze gaa.

Borey dumi-dumi ga hin ka te ka farhaŋ gumo nda i wayborey soho i gonda karimay, ka dinya taaliyey daadaamey ka te ka wasu hamni nda alhaliirey kaŋ yaŋ gonda gaaba boŋ.

A ga hima, a ga dagay zey kaana kaŋ yaŋ ma faham al'amari gaabukoy.

Borey nda wayborey ga hima ka care di danga wurey, ka dinya haŋ kaŋ yaŋ dinya ka te ka fun farhaŋ jaman haray.

Baakasinay nda alhaliirey ga ti haykey kaŋ yaŋ si bisa. Baakasinay si hin ka gonda alhaliirey dumi kulu. Baakasinay ga ti dinya-siga abada.

Baakasinay goo borey kaŋ yaŋ gonda wasu gumo i korsey nda i dumo-koyrey hasarayan se. Baakasinay goy borey kaŋ yaŋ goy da laakal gumo taliba guna se, talibi-arzakayseyaŋ se, borey kaŋ yaŋ gonda buuyaŋ se.

Baakasinay goo borey kaŋ yaŋ gonda ahan ga laakal gaa koore-koy kaŋ yaŋ ga haru-han zeyo nda i sayariyaŋ, ganda-koyo kaŋ yaŋ ga hiiji, talibi-arzakoyo kaŋ yaŋ ga ŋwaari, gaari kaŋ yaŋ ga hiiji ka te ka kani haŋ.

Waati kaŋ gaaba kulu, yaŋ ga tabbatumi, waati kaŋ yaŋ ga koore-koyo yaŋ ga buu-tiima ka te ka kooru fu-furakay, soho boro si n'a zay, kaana goo no, yaamaray gumo goo no, baakasinay gumo goo no.

Siga dunnya se yaaba, borey si gonda kaana gumo. Borey ga haggoy i no-goy kaŋ yaŋ gonda wasu gumo, anniyey, nasaraajiyaŋ, bayhayaŋ, tammaasaajiyaŋ, wasuyaŋ, laaliyaŋ, duba, duba.

Dunya binde boro dumi-dumi goo no, kaŋ yaŋ gonda no-goy yaaley. No-goy yaale goo no siyasatuko yaama, kuri-koyo kaŋ yaŋ ga laru nda dirham gaa nda anniya-laakal gumo ka du hamo, baraarey, jera, arzaka, duba, duba. Ya si hima ka sagay kuri nda laabu.

No-goy gumo ga ti ka-kaa gumo, amma ya ga saabu ka doori no-goy yaale gumo nda siyasatukoyaŋ yaamey, arzakukoyaŋ kaŋ yaŋ ga gudu, borey kaŋ yaŋ ga kani wayborey, duba duba.

Ya hima ka te ka fun binde gaa. No-goy gumo si fun laakal gaa, no-goy kaana ga ti binde hawciyaŋ.

Soho borey gonda kaana, i ga dinya alhaliirey kulu kaŋ yaŋ gonda gaaba boŋ, tammaasaajiyaŋ hasaray kulu kaŋ yaŋ dinya ka te ka fun jannawey gaa ka te ka koy jaman haray, yaŋ ma koy jaman haray, farhaŋ abada, kaana abada, gaaba nda gaaba kulu.

Siga, aniya ga ti daadaame ka te ka fun haŋ kaŋ dinya, a ga anniya ka zun haŋ kaŋ yaŋ dinya. Haŋ kaŋ dinya ga borey halasi, a ga farhaŋ halasi, a ga baakasinay wi.

Laakal kaŋ yaŋ ga gaaba boŋ si hin ka faham al'amari gaabukoy kaŋ yaŋ goy jaman haray.

Borey dumi-dumi goy kaŋ yaŋ ga hantum iri se ka anniya ka fari-fari, ka talaba-fari kaana kaŋ yaŋ ga han i binde kaŋ yaŋ gonda hasaraa, amma borey ceeci yaŋ goo ceeci ka fari borey kaŋ yaŋ ga hiiji.

Borey dumi-dumi goo kaŋ yaŋ ga hantum iri se ka bayrandi i jera kaanayan kaŋ yaŋ goy a binde, amma borey yaabi yaŋ goo ceeci kaŋ yaŋ ga batamu koore kaŋ yaŋ gonda ka te ka haŋ a nda borey kaŋ yaŋ taliba.

Borey si anniya ka faham ka te ka fun tammaasaajiyaŋ kulu gaa sababu goo no kaŋ yaŋ te taaliya.

Aniya, aniya kaanayan, ga ti karfi kaŋ yaŋ fun iri harey se kaŋ yaŋ te sababey dumi-dumi kaŋ yaŋ dinya kaŋ i tammaasaajiyaŋ jaman haray.

Iri ga talaba kaana ka te ka te ka bare sababu ka te ka doori tammaasaajiyaŋ. Iri ga talaba kaana ka te ka han yaŋ gonda laakal gumo juna kaanayan.

Iri ga talaba kaana ka te ka bare iri juna kaanayan.

Kaana gumo ka te ka te si fun laakal gaa. Kaana gumo nda baakasinay kaana si hin ka fun humu gaa.

Ya hima ka faham humu ga halasi kaana, a ga halasi binde kaana, a ga halasi iri binde baakasinay hawciyaŋ.

Humu ga ti taaley kulu asili, soojey kulu asiri kaŋ yaŋ dinya, sunsum kaŋ yaŋ wi halasi.

Karo-koyrey nda way-karo koyrey kaŋ yaŋ goo karaynde nda jamaakaray ga hima ka faham taliba kaŋ yaŋ ga hayran i talibey jere gonda kaana kaana kaana, baraarey, nda gaaba kaana.

Borey kaŋ yaŋ dinya ka te ka gaaru jaman haray, dinya humu sunsum, yaŋ na a karami danga fu-fulanza boŋ. Halu duba ga ti taali, cengu, nda laabaciyaŋ.

Karo-koyrey ga hima ka faham saaha kaŋ iri goy a binde, cengu kaŋ iri goy a binde taliba nda taliba ka hayran tiŋariyaŋ kaana kaŋ yaŋ ga saaba nda jirbiyaŋ kaŋ goy wiciri nda wasu binde.

Baaray ga bare care-yan taaley sababuyan. A si te ka bisa waati kaŋ yaŋ wasu kaana kaana hanni.

A ga dagay ka faham ka ti kaana, laakal gaa, amma laakal-wasuyan binde kaŋ yaŋ dinya nda tiyanyaan kaŋ yaŋ ga saaba ka te ka fun kaana kaana, taaley dunya gaa ka ti wasuyaŋ kaana gunda ga ti baakasinay gumo.
