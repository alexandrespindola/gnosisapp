ALATU 21 GA WAYRURU HAA 20 GA ŽANWIYE

BURCIN, BORO KONDO, MONADA, gonda biney hinka; boro sintina, bine hanna no. Boro sintina, Dante BEATRIZ no, boro sintina, Dante BEATRIZ no, HELENA ŠEŠEN, SALOMO GUNDAKO SULAMITA, wande širiši, TEOSOFIYA BUDDHI.

Boro hinka, bine Adamize no, ASAALI ma, ORO ZAKI, TEOSOFIYA MANAS BOREHANO.

Hala day sanda hay goroše, da hay goroše; bine Adamize go ga te, bine Hanna go ga fijji.

ADAM da HAWA go ga KAYANDI MONADA ra, danga ngey KIBAALI darza, 10 no, kaŋ go ga fongu iri IO gaa, maana da kumiyaŋ Iiiiiii. Ooooooo. HAY JINA DABARI, HAL ABADA DUMBO HAKO ND'ABADA WAYBORO HAKO KAYANDI, KONTRARIYE KAYANDI, MONADA ASAALI HAKO ND'ALKAALI KAYANDI.

ATMAN-BUDDHI-MANAS TABA HAKO, BURCIN, iri salaŋ da iri ga ye ka salaŋ, danga DUMBUYA ŋWAARI zama, si HAY, si KII, si ŽIBI LABU.

Širiši, iri gonda darza ka tabbatar, danga bine Adamize FRAKŠAN hinne no, go ga funu hillaŋ GUNDEY ra, woone ASAALI no, bine jeeri ka HAY, bine Adamize ka fulanzam da fundi, bine Hanna ka fulanzam.

MONADA, BURCIN, go ga te, go ga taale, go ga fulanzam biney hinka, ngey din ga gaaba nda ngey ga taali.

Iri ga soobay MONADA da Biney. MONADA, maana da FUNDI se, hay no; Bine go ga du.

Iri ga soobay DUNIYA MONADA da DUNIYA BINE; BUMEY MONADA da BUMEY BINE; HALBI MONADA da HALBI BINE.

ADAMIZE ALKAALIŠIJE, dabariše, go da MONADA bilionaŋ da trillionaŋ.

Gonda fasaliyaŋ da dumiyaŋ, sinjišiyaŋ tooro, ganda tooro, sanda JEEBI ganda kulši, wooney iri ga hin ka ce MONADA, Leibniz kalima te, hala kalima boro ga koy, ka gosi širiši ganda funuyaŋ širiši.

Jeebe wala MONADA kulu se, a atomu go, danga alkaališi jeeri.

MONADA go ga hin, go ga kayandi, go ga fulanzam, go ga te alkalliyaŋ kulu se, ganda kulu se, micro-alkaali kulu se, d.h.n.

MONADA ra, gonda DUMEY; MONADA kaŋ te kaŋa, ga gaaba kaŋ borehano, woone ŠERIYA no. MONADA KAŊ TE KAŊA, gonda kaŋ borehano.

MONADA trillionaŋ kul kaŋ go ga burci Adamize alkaališije se, ga gaaba borcina se, borey dumi, MONADA borekoy.

MONADA KAYANDI, MONADA BOREKOY go ga sa MONADA kaŋ kayna kulu te Adamize alkaališije ra, haa žama kaŋ Šeriya Kara go se.

Waati kaŋ MONADA bilionaŋ wala trillionaŋ wala jeebe fundiyaŋ go ga furu Adamize FUNDO, KII din ga tilas.

MONADA se, ngey BORO KONDO si halaci hal abada, go ga furu ngey alkaališi hillaŋ, ka te wala hillaŋ žiriši.

ŽIBI NDA DUNIYA, JINA LABU, TABATAR LABU, si hin ka te da MONADA te laabu. Ngey din go ga fulanzam hillaŋ Selu nda ngey hangey, hillaŋ alkaališi. Waati kaŋ MONADA BOREKOY go da fulanzam kulši, a gonda darza ka nafa nga MONADA trillionaŋ gaa, ka te ganda, HEYNI, kometa, ka ye nga MONADA KAYANDI, ASTRO tooro, amma woone Alaažey hay no.

MONADA wala jeebe fundiyaŋ, si ADAMIZE alkaališije ganda. Atomu ra kaŋ gonda hillaŋ gundey, MONADA funiyaŋ go, ka go ga gaabu. Fundi Adamize wala fundi kaŋ si hay, malaika wala šeytan, HEYNI wala HILLAŋ, go da dalili MONADA bilionaŋ nda trillionaŋ.

EGO Hillaŋ ngey BORO KONDO, atomu kayandi HAY kaŋ si hay koyo gaa. Alhakku, atomu ra, MONADA wala jeebe fundiyaŋ go ka gaabu.

Žu iri ga faham zama sayansin da kaŋ gundu go ga ne: «ŠEYTAN, ALAAŽI NO, KAŊ TABA».

ATOMU kulu go da jeebe fundi, MONADA. Fulanzama kulu, batame kulu, JEEBI MONADA kayandi se no.

Ganda go ga fili ADAMIZE HAKO HAŋSEY HIIRISIYAŊ taray kaŋ borey gonda darza gaa, waati kaŋ woone go ga timme, KII din ga tilas.

Hiirisiyaŋ haŋsey taba no: 1-ALKAALIŠIJE MA. 2-BINEJEERIMA. 3-GOYA HAKO.

Hilla Adamize FUNDO kii se, EGO kaŋ go da funu Hillaŋ, go ga funu hillaŋ molekuya ra.

Hay hinza ga koy kabore gaa, kabore ra. 1-ADAMIZE FUNDO. 2-FUNDI FUNDO. 3-BUMEY.

Fundi fundo go ga huru kabore ra, a go ga halaci danga Adamize fundo go ga halaci, danga MONADA go ga huru.

BUMEY go ka go ga huru kabore ra, amma go ga funu waati kaŋ boro go ga kaŋ huma, waati kaŋ borey go ga hallaŋ a, a go ga taabal Kabore gaa, ka ye nga kabore gaa.

BUMEY gonda sinjišiyo nda dabare, go ga halaci kabore gaa.

PROSERPINA, SALAMA KANNA, YA ŠI HAKATE, ALBARKA ALAAŽI MA KII, kaŋ nga koyandi gaa, malaika KII go ga te.

MA GOYU yee, MA-KII, go ga ba nga izey širiši, woone se no a go ga kaŋ ngey.

Malaika KII waati kaŋ ngey go ga te, ngey go ga say huma te, ngey go ga kaŋ huma dumi, ngey go ga du kookoy, nda woone ngey go ga kataa, kaŋa kaŋ go ga gaabu, kaŋ go ga gaabu hillaŋ gundey da Adamize FUNDO.

Malaika KII go ga kataa širima fundi, ka funu EGO fundu Adamize gaa.

Malaika KII gonda faham širiši, ngey go ga fulanzam goya gaa kaŋ go da HEYNI KATIN.

Malaika KII, si faham goya kayandi hinne, kaŋ go da Adamize FUNDO se. Koyandiyaŋ KII hinne, go da faham širiši goya kulši, kaŋ go da YA GOYKAŋ.

Hilla fundo kii se, BOREHANO go ga koy dabu, kaŋ go da jirbi hinza da jere.

TIBETU DEFTER KII go ga ne: «Ni gonda dabu jirbi hinza da jere koyne. Sanneywaati kaŋ ni go ga du da dabu hinka, ni ga gunda da alkaališi, maa kaŋ goya, (zama) waati woone SAMSARA kulši (UNIVERS FENOMENIKU) ga goya goya.

KIBAALI darza EGO se, hanu da iddu no; woone TIPHON lamba no, ALKAALIŠI kaŋ si gonda FUNDI.

EGO go ga kaŋ nga duniyako kabore Adamize fundo ka koy nda fonde faham fundu fundi se, hay garaw no.

Hilla ganda dabu kaŋ go da jirbi hinza da jere, borizey ga tilas ka fundi ganda ganda, kaŋ fundi kaŋ goya goya kaŋ širizi kulši.

Faham ŽAMA hay kayandi širiši teje fundi alkaališije ra, kaŋ goya kaŋ širiza nda goya goya kaŋ fundi wala SAMSARA fundi.

DUNIYA JEHANUMA ra, tarayaŋ žama kulši gunda no, a yogo yogo širiši, go da 80.000, 8.000, 800 da alat 80.

Jeeri SELULA ra kaŋ iri go ga funu, go ga ba žama hillaŋ Wey Hillaŋ; izo jirbi Wey Hillaŋ; fundi jina, Wey Hillaŋ širiši.

Duniya Molekuya ra, iri gonda darza ka to hay taamey da žama kaŋ ga go da Wey kaŋ gonda dakišiŋ 40.

Duniya ELEKTRONIKA ra, tarayaŋ žama ga goya da dakišiŋ 40 da dakišiŋ hinka da jere.

FUNDI SAMSARA KAYANDI (FUNDI KAŊ GOYA KAŊ ŠIRIZA), waati kii ra nda jirbi hinza da jere, go da ELEKTRONIKA dumi nda woone dalili se, sanney kulši iri gonda darza ka to teje ELEKTRONIKA.

FUNDI SAMSARA KAYANDI HILLAŋ DUNIYA MOLEKUYA, si da zambe nda woone dalili se, sanney kulši iri ga to teje fundi da PATRON ŽAMA MOLEKUYA.

BURCIN, MONADA, BORO KONDO biney hinka te, waati kaŋ iri si hay jeeri goroše, iri go ga funu GALAKSIYA RAAKIRKIRI. Hal fundi goya ADAMIZE jeeri gaa, a go ga funu hillaŋ hanseyaŋ ra.

Širiši ASAALI se hilla kii se, du HAL BOHDIMA kayandi, HURRI HINTARO, woone hinne no, ka EMBRION BINE kaŋ iri gonda fundu ra, ka koy boreha, ka koy DUNIYA ELEKTRONIKA ra.

ALHAJI HIN, ka faham zama DUNIYA ELEKTRONIKA ra, iri go ga funu TABA HAKO HAL ABADA, iri BURCIN, iri BUDHA.

Du nda TABA HAL ABADA hilla kii, ka ye nga borekay hinne, a tabbatar ka fulanzam BUDHA KAYANDI, du HURRI HINTARO nda go da hillaŋ yawmey barešiyaŋ ka ye žibi Adamize alkaališije gaa.

Hay din waati širiza kii, HANNATAR BOREKOY go da faham borize se teje, a tabbatar ka du HURRI HINTARO.

Hay din waati širiza kii, borize hinne go ga hangatar HANNATAR KAYANDI, a tabbatar zama a ga gaabu koy zaama ka du HAL BOHDIMA kayandi.

ZAAMA ASAALI SE, ka huru, a koy hillaŋ funu, a goya YA GOYKAŋ. Jeeri ra Kara kulši.

Waati kaŋ borize goya fundi ganda kaŋ goya kaŋ širiza, a ga gaabu ka kaŋ a borey KAYANDI KARA se, kaŋ iri go ga to teje.

Zoroastro ganda go ga ne: «Boro kaŋ nga goy borey hilla gram hinza, nga zunubu, a go ga koy alžan, Boro kaŋ nga zunubu boreha, a go ga koy Alžahanuma, waati kaŋ ngey taamu hinne, a go ga funu Hamistikan hala fundi bore hina wala žibi».

Henne jeeri ra, waati gaara fundi da ateyiza, borizey boreha go ga funu, hilla hukumu jeeri, DUNIYA Gunda ka goji, kaŋ go da DUNIYA JEHANUMA.

Borey miliyoniyaŋ boreha go ga furu funu hilla, wala hay danga ka haru, hilla ka du yawmey barešiyaŋ hilla DUNIYA boreha ra.

Širiši alkaališije go te duniya ra, borizey kayandi hini go ga du HURRI HINTARO da HAL BOHDIMA KAYANDI.

Borizey go ga furu HAL ABADA Hillaŋ Wey koyandiyaŋ ra, ka funu HAL ABADA Wey hillaŋ ra.

Iri di alati hilla waati CÁNCER zama fundi bore kulši, go ga du kaŋ Wey koyandiyaŋ, MERKURIO, VENUS, HEYNI, MARTE, JÚPITER da SATURNO, a go ga taamu funu koyandiyaŋ WEY.

Širiši WEY go ga kaŋ iri nda WEY go ga žibi iri, fasaliyaŋ hanu koyandiyaŋ teje go te labu hilla kii se, zama SANDA YA BA HA, YA KAŊA».

Asaali kaŋ hukumu ra, gonda hakku ka du HURRI HINTARO da HAL BOHDIMA KAYANDI, ga gaabu halilkay tooro koyne da goya kulši ka huru, ka goya Hillaŋ HILLAŋ Hillaŋ da EGO.

Žama borey MAGADUM go ga taala kaŋ borizey go ga kaŋ fundi hilla, ngey go ga taala goya ALBARKA koyandiyaŋ.

Sanda jeeri selula ra kaŋ iri go ga funu go da ka, Jamhuriyaŋ, Borikoy, Borikoy danga, Borikoy gonda, d.h.n., ya ši Hillaŋ DUNIYA molekuya ra, alžanna boreha, Jeeri da Borikoy, asaali go da hal garaw bay širiši.

Borizey go ga hin ka furu BORIKOY kaŋ go da hal bareši sanda: BORIKOY kaŋ go da GOYA DUMBO; BORIKOY kaŋ go da GUBO DOŊKO (VAJRAPANI); wala VIHARA KAŊ SI Gonda MUBAYNI kaŋ go da HEYNI LOTO; (PADMA SAMBHAVA).

Borizey kaŋ go ga koy HURRI HINTARO, ga tilas ka taala BOROY BOREY, goya kaŋ go da ALKAALIŠI kaŋ boroy kaŋ te, hilla DUNIYA MOLEKUYA.

Hay garaw širiši ka taabal fundi nda fundi ra, ka walahala hilla SAMSARA kaŋ go da garaw, kaŋ si go da HAL BOHDIMA da HURRI HINTARO.

Gonda BORIKOY kaŋ go da hal garaw bareši kaŋ borize ga tilas ka koy, iri ga fongu BORIKOY HAL GARAWA HARRI, kaŋ go da Budha Amitabha.

Iri ga fongu BORIKOY kaŋ go da MAITREYA, cikuley kaŋ go da TUSHITA, bore kaŋ go da hal boreha, borizey go ga furu kaŋ go ga koy DUNIYA ELEKTRONIKA.

Borizey ga tilas ka du Alaaže horeha kaŋ du ALAJEJE BOREKOY, taraba ngoy gaa, kaŋ si saaba gaa da hay hina wala hin, da ngey si koy furu hilla žibi hilla danga ka du HAL BOHDIMA HINTARO DUNIYA ELECTRONIKA ra.

Garaw funu jeeri ELECTRONIKA ra, HURRI HINTARO hilla ka bisa alžanna molekuya ra, si hin ka ne da Adamize kalima.

Budha go ga dira Hilla ALABADA KAŊ SI DU HILLA, gundey širiši bay kaŋ go ga halla HILLAŋ MA-GOYU.

Bore kulši kaŋ alžan, borekulši danga tarayyaŋ, go ga timme. Waati kaŋ DHARMA funu go ga timme, ŽIBI HILLA ga tilas.

ASAALI go ga walahala garaw kaŋ EGO Lillaŋ taale a ga, ka halaka borize hillaŋ HILLAŋ goya ra, A go ga žibi hilla alkaališi.

Waati kaŋ ASAALI go ga walahala GARAW, a go ga sa Budha BORO KONDO zama nga din go ga halaci hillaŋ HILLAŋ goya ra, kaŋ YA GOYKAŋ BOREY KULŠI.

ŽIBI ka koy alkaališišije, go ga te ŠERIYA KARA.

EGO go ga funu ka fundiyaŋ zaama te.

MONADA kaŋ go da ADAMIZE FUNDI zaaman go da darza ka kayandi atomy, molekuly nda ka fulanzam selu nda alkalla ka yeiri jibi jeeri ra hilla dunya ra nda Adamize fundi hilla.

Dumbu ŋwaari ADAMIZE, go ga sina fundi woone dunya ra sanda seli asaaliki, hilla žama batame (selu gaaro) ka timme alat hamseyna nda alat tamandare, kaŋ go da fonguyaŋ tooro kulši.

ALHAJI HIN ka faham zama dumi ŽIBI wala JIBI KAYANDI, go ga te ALA KAŊ JINA.

YA hay kaŋa kaŋ YA GOYKAŋ kayandi, hilla alkaališi Adamize labu.

YA HAY danga kayandi, go da Kayandiyaŋ tooro, kaŋ si kayandi Adamize alkaališije labu, borey danga hinka YA KANADA alkaališiyaŋ širši boreha go ga KAYANDI ADAMIZE, YA borizey go ga funu hilla FUNDU HOSEY, ŋwaarey, maajehey, baruhey, d.h.n.

Sanda hay kaŋ Magadum Pitágoras go ga dira nga saabayaŋ hina, woone ga tilas ka kaŋŋaa hose. MAGADUM go ga halla nga ga ne: «KA KAŊŊAA A, NGA WOLO GO GA HAŋ, Hanga taama jeeri nda nga jina kaŋ ki».

Tabbitar zaama iri koy dumi iri batame ra, zuri ganda dunya Evolución ga kaŋ iri nda ngey baba ka halžima nda ngey go ga kaŋ: EGO si hin ka dambe, Kulu go ga Fulanzam, Kulu ga tilas ka du goroše, d.h.n.

Fanatiku kaŋ ignoré da EGO go da tooroyaŋ HAY danga YA ND'a bore hina go ga du nda HAY kaŋ danga.

Fanatiku kaŋ ignoré zaama EGO si gonda HAY ALAHEJA kaŋ a go da KAYANDI ADAMIZE, kaŋ Šeriya Evolución ka haru si koy du garoše.

Kayandiyaŋ ADAMIZE gonda hakku tooro ka furu hilla gunda Adamize kaŋ go da hosey, ŋwaarey, baruhey, d.h.n., ya din si hin ka kay koyandiyaŋ kaŋ go da FANATIKU go ga ne ZURI EVOLUCIÓN, hal ngey go ga goro ka halžima ka goya.

Woone DOKTORI Metaforosis wala Metempsicosis da PITÁGORAS kaŋ go da dalili teje Šeriya Ganda bore.

ASNO da ORO APULEYO ra, iri du ka dogante DOKTORI PITÁGORAS.

APULEYO go ga ne TESALIA ra kaŋ go da Hečiseriya hanseyaŋ si HAY, ADAMIZE kanji te; paka go da Adamize da hilloŋ; alžer da Adamize da gogore; asali, fundi Adamize kaŋ go da linfa jeeri. Danga tooro jeeri ka to hay din si kayandi zama borizi kulši, zaama tooroyaŋ go te YA GOYKAŋ BOREY KULŠI, hin ka žibi ka du hilla alkaališi kaŋ go da ŋwaari wala furu hilla alkaališi gunda, alžer gunda, d.h.n.

Mistiku Kerista go da tooro da ba ka fonu hilla, hilla berey, ka hillo kanje.

RUDOLF STEINER INICIADO Alman, a go ga ne waati POLAR, ADAMIZE hinne no kaŋ go ka go da ŋwaarey go wa hindiko, go funu ADAMIZE ra, Adamize goya ngey.

ŊWAAREY go da KAYANDIwala YA GOYKAŋ BOREY kaŋ go da ADAMIZE KAYANDI BOREKOY. Kayandiyaŋ KAYANDI kaŋ adamizey goya Ngey fundi ra, saaba Adamize kaŋ kayandi dumi kayandi se, bore kulu aljakiyaŋ goya fundiyaŋ.

ADAMIZE POLA nda HIPERBÓREOS ga tilas ka goya kayandiyaŋ KAYANDI nda YA GOYKAŋ BOREY KULŠI, ka fulanzam ADAMIZE KAYANDI, ADAMIZE KAYANDI.

Borey boreha te ADAMIZE, da ngey goya kulu HAY din kaŋ ngey gonda Adamize, si funa nda hay goro.

SATURNO ya ji PLANETA goya kaŋ saaba go da CAPRICORNIO. Gossi woone da karga ka fongu iri KAŊJE KUJIMA, ADAMIZE KAYANDI da KAŊJE, ga tilas ka goya hay kaŋ iri gonda borey hina, kayandiyaŋ ADAMIZE kaŋ iri go ga gaabu fundi.

KANJI CAPRICORNIO kaŋ go da ÓNIX KUJIMA nda kanji kulu kayandi, alhali ya JAZBA nda nga jeeri SATURNO. SATURNO go ga funu hilla alkaališi go ga te ngey GUNDEY boreha Aquelarres, ya ši goya SATURNO jeeri hinne ka timme saaba Dumi kaŋ saaba YAHUDUEY. SATURNO ya ji FUNDI nda GOYA. Fondi fundi go da hay kaŋ di gaaro kookoy gaa.

Ganda almazurkaŋ ka koy boreha hilla Adamize ra hilla ka bisa alkaan ka koy hillaŋ go da JAZBA SATURNO, hala di go ga dumi kayandi, darza, zaama.

Iri si salaŋ ga JAZBA sanda a duma garbaši; iri go ga salaŋ gaa go da JAZBA sanda a kayandi, dumi.

Alkaan go da substance go ka sa di ka saaba ka taalo saaba hay koy. Substance Woone ya Ji SINOVIA, ka funu dumi SIN, ka go da CON da OVIA, GUJIRE. Sanda substance GO da GUJIRE.

GUJIRE go ga halla hilla SAYANSI JINAS ka iri salaŋ goya TRATADO ESOTÉRICO TEÚRGIA, širiya hinka EDICIÓN.

Gooya CAPRICORNIO. Waati širiza CAPRICORNIO, ni ga fongu kaŋ Aтаúd wala dromu KII ganda ra. Ni go ga dira Aтаúd din kaŋ go da fongu a go ga funu hirsi kaŋ alkaan waati kaŋ ni go ga dira go ga golo alkaan sanda ka kaŋ tooro ka bisa alkaali, ka bisa alkaan te Aтаúd, ya ši ka fulanzam alkaan ka koy haaro ka zunba ka kwa haaro, da fongu Adamize a go ga zaama kaŋ a go ga kaŋ da jazba SATURNO.

MAGADUM MASEON hin ka faham gooya SATURNO zama teje kaŋ MASEN go ga furu LOGIA.

GANDAZAY CAPRICORNIO gonda kayandi Pedagošiye se, go ga halla boreha, gonda SANABA KAKAYANDI, a go ga te boro boreha kaŋ go da goya tooro hilla nga fundi ra, boro go ga tula gaa.

Wayboro kaŋ go da CAPRICORNIO ya ji Wayboro bareši, baa ka halla kii, ganda kaŋ borekoy go ga tula gaa, go ga halla bay zama ya Ji Kara kaŋ ngey gaara.

Wayboro kayandi kaŋ go da CAPRICORNIO go ga furu nda ADAMIZE, ama woone yee hinne borekoy goya nanda, hilla ka halla ga.

Alanzame da wayboro kaŋ go da CAPRICORNIO, go da EGO širiši, wala si zaama kulu; iri go ga salaŋ dumi ya te kaŋ Adamize kaŋ KAYNAtakarda go ga te kaŋ ganda ya din kayandize ra.
