**Juyyo Bi 22 Za Too Juyyo Bi 23 Ga**

ANNIE BESANT na ci NANA-GANJA Biiro kaŋ ga hima ka hantum.

"Jumaare hano no, waati kaŋ adduwa hano kaa, koyra borey da jina bora key Koyra-windo. Waati kaŋ KARI (IZUULUMU ALKAALI) sintin adduwa, NABABU da nga jama fun dugu, sanda Izulumu HAKIKA (RITO) na n'a ce. NANA-GANJA kay, dugu si, hasara si. Adduwa ban, NABABU ze da zanka, a sanja a ce: "Izuulumu HAKIKA n'a ce, zama mayra kaŋ ga hima. Yaa ŋwa, yaa ciyaŋ. Amana ma kaa ne ka kay sanda kurmu".

NANA-GANJA NAA CE:

"Araŋ fun dugu, ama araŋ miilaŋ go beene, araŋ go ga miila farasiŋ kaa daŋ KANDAR, adduwa miilaŋ si. A go ga tee, Izuulumu-bora go ga du ga dugu ka te, ama a miila go ga koy ka nga farka jarey faaba kaŋ hay kulbaŋyaŋ. Maayaŋ ya du ka adduwa araŋ banda, borey kaŋ go ga dugu ka te kaŋ yaŋ adduwa borey sanda bunbun?"

"NABABU ciya kaŋ a go ga miila farasiŋ kaaŋ tooru. KARI sanni tiŋ ka ce a ba sanka ciyaŋ, a sanja zanka sanni ya."

A go ga honga day ka bay sanda ya hima ka adduwa taale, boro kaŋ ga du ka adduwa da miila-sinnay taale tee, a ga du asiri.

Ama a go ga honga ka bay kaŋ adduwey go, n'i zama go.

Adduwey go da sargaray, ama adduwa kul kaŋ go ga sargaray si.

Adduwey go kaŋ kulbaŋyaŋ, n'i gaskiya gunde ya CIYA-HAKIKA jeeriŋyaŋ, m'a ga du n'i kul, danga m'a miila, zama sanni kul, zama jarey kul, da laakal ka sanda ya ce.

Jine Bi Baabo no teekay-yaraamu da honga sanda ya boori, ama a go ga honga ka bay n'a bay n'a kul kaŋ zama sanni kul maana, zama jarey kul maana, zama sargaray kul maana.

Jine Bi Baabo adduwa sargaray no, adduwa kaŋ i ga Baabo sanni te kaŋ go sirri. Jine Bi Baabo da miila-sinnay taale tee, a ga du asiri.

GNOSTIQUE hakika kaŋ go, DIINI jeeriŋyaŋ, n'i go ka taale-bayra jeeriŋyaŋ, boro kaŋ go ga bay ka miila, borey kaŋ go ga bay n'i da gunde ya.

Boro kaŋ ba ka Koyra-hakkilo fonda tee, a ga hima ka PRANA da LIFE da FUERZA SEXUAL zama n'i ze binde-cindi da hakkilo gunde.

A go ga honga ka bay ka hakkilo miila da gunde ya, ka hakkilo dan Gunde Windo. INITIATION-kuruusa ga du alwaati kul Gunde windo honga.

NANA-GANJA, Biiro kaŋ zama SIKH DIINI tee Berede kaŋ ya boori, kaŋ go ga VEDA-borey koyra, n'a koyra Gunde fonda.

NANA-GANJA koyra zama day ka day n'a tee day Diiney kul game, Day-zaykul, day-jarey, da hakaŋ.

Waati kaŋ m'a daga Diiney kul gaa, wala DIINI fo, m'a tee gunde HAKIKA ciyaŋ.

Gunde Windo go da danji Diiney kul, Day-zaykul, Day-jarey, da hakaŋ.

Diiney kul marjeeriŋyaŋ no, kaŋ go ga haabu day gunda kaŋ go.

GNÓSTICO ciyaŋ tiŋ da borey kaŋ go Diiney kul, Day-zaykul, Day-jarey, Dumi-taaley kul, da hakaŋ.

Gunde-Windo go da danji Diiney kul, Diiney kul zama. JIZU na ce: "Araŋ amay araŋ banda, araŋ ya du ka bay araŋ ga ne ay Almuurikoy no".

SIKHIA sanni-cindiyaŋ, sanda DIINI kul, ya teene.

SIKHIA game, OMKARA no biiti kaŋ garga ka beene tee, ka Berede tee, ka ji tee, ka haŋkul, kaŋ go kul.

OMKARA no BIITI ALZIINJE, kaŋ yala no, kaŋ yala ka tee, kaŋ go za hano kul ka tee, kaŋ go za hano kul ka ban, kaŋ Hawa ga fee CATORCE GUNDEY, bayray bore kul, karga ciyaŋ bore kul.

"Wando no araŋ haney. HANGA da ALLU araŋ laamu. Handu-gangey gariwano araŋ marjeeriŋyaŋ. Ya Baabo! HIMALA-toori ga gaaru araŋ tufkey. Hawa go ga ore araŋ. Jiiro go ga araŋ foole jeeri, ya hawa. Zama araŋ adduwey koyra, ya halakkarey! ANATAL SHABDHA (HANKEYYA-GARGARI) go ga ci sanda araŋ tambarey. Araŋ kooni si, ama araŋ go da zangu yaŋ. Araŋ ce si, ama araŋ go da zangu yaŋ. Araŋ hine si, ama araŋ go da zangu yaŋ. Araŋ tammaasa tammaase ga tee n'a danji. Araŋ Hawa, ya darza, go ga tee kul. Borey kul game Hawa no kaŋ go kaŋ goo araŋ Hawa. Ka Biiro koyrey, Hawa no ga fee. Ya ARATI no".

Biiro Nana-Ganji honga, kaŋ za UPANISHADA game, g'a bay kaŋ BRAHAMA (Baabo) fo no, kaŋ DISEY teene, n'i gafee no ka fee, ABSOLUTA gaabiyaŋ fee no.

GURÚ-DEVA ga tee day kaŋ go fo da BAABO (BRAHAMA). Darza gaa boro kaŋ go da GURÚ-DEVA zama koyra bore. Darza gaa boro kaŋ du TAALE BIIRO.

Fonda go ga jabu, ga hanse, ga zeme. A go ga honga GURÚ-DEVA, koyra bora.

Gunde-windo game m'a du HARI kaŋ ga tee. Gunde-windo game m'a du GURÚ-DEVA.

Sohõ m'a ci CIKIA koondey da GURÚ-DEVA honga.

"Ya NANA-GANJA! Bay n'a zama GURÚ kaŋ go, kaŋ amay araŋ nda haŋkul..."

"Zangu fo hano kul game ma tee sarji zama GURÚ kaŋ ha tee ya ka tee DIOS za kulbaŋyaŋ".

"Da ALLu zangu da Manga wuli, i ga koyra da GURÚ zeme".

"Hawa gaa no GURÚ kaŋ teene, kaŋ bay HARI (Kaŋ ga tee) kaŋ koyra m'a tee da gaa aminey da jayney".

"!Ya koyro! Araŋ te ka GURÚ-DEVA aminey, zama araŋ da a, day araŋ borey kaŋ go ga bata, m'a tee day dugu ga".

"GURÚ-DEVA, GURÚ kaŋ go, ya PARABRAHMAN koyro kaŋ go. NANA-GANJA fun dugu Gurú DEVA HARI jine".

INDUSTÁN SAMYASIN hakkilo kaŋ tee, ya boro kaŋ go ga koyra GURÚ-DEVA, boro kaŋ du a gunde game, boro kaŋ go ga tee EGO LUNAR halakkirey.

Boro kaŋ ba ka tee da EGO, da YO, a ga tee ga filla zuuru da daara, da ŋwaala, da hasidiyaŋ, da tagay-boreyaŋ, da guluŋ, da gurme ya. Ama tee da taaley kul da hunda kul Binde game ya te, yo ga bu da honga, da jaban kul.

MIILA da HARI (Kaŋ ga tee) zamba, m'a dan ka du Gaskiya, kaŋ gaa gaskiya.

A go ga honga ka bay da Baabo adduwa, ka bay ka BRAHAMA (BAABO) sanni te kaŋ go sirri.

Baabo fo adduwa kaŋ boori kaŋ taale tee da miila-sinnay, ya taale-gaabi yaamu taale no.

Baabo fo adduwa kaŋ boori a tee da hora fo wala hora fo ga tee.

Adduwa ban game a ga hima ka bay ka Baabo wanga haaro, m'a tee ka miila bay, ka bayra da dugu tee, ka hakkilo yaale nda miila si, go ga haaro Baabo wanga.

Waati kaŋ HAKKILO go ga dugu yaale gaa nda yaale si, waati kaŋ HAKKILO go ga dugu honga gaa nda honga si, waati kaŋ hakkilo du ka farga day DUALISMO gaa, toho kaay ka baaru.

A go ga honga ka HAKKILO yaale zama miiley kul, ka ce kul, ka ba kul, ka bafe kul, ka honga kul, ka gaabi kul, da hakaŋ, zama a dan ka du Gaskiya danjey.

YAALEY ka tee, Hawa FEEY kaŋ go HAWWAA fee, a ga du day waati kaŋ ESENCIA, da ALMA, da BUDHATA, du ka farga da taaley honga game.

ESENCIA go ga danji da zama hunda game da haray da humay, da ce da daga, ay da si, day boori da banna, da zama boori da banna si.

Waati kaŋ HAKKILO go ga dugu, waati kaŋ HAKKILO go ga honga, tohõ ESENCIA du ka farga ka Gaskiya danjey ka HAWWAA FEE GAME.

Araŋ adduwa, almuuriko kaŋ boori, toho da hakkilo kaŋ go ga dugu nda honga, yaale zama miiley kul, araŋ haaro ka Baabo wanga: "Araŋ ce m'i daŋ araŋ gaa, araŋ ze m'i wanga araŋ gaa".

Adduwa ya sanni te ka DIOS banda nda gaskiya a ga hima ka bay ka sanni te da BAABO, da BRAHAMA.

Gunde-windo no adduwa windo. Gunde-windo game m'a du zama kaŋ go fee beene ga mi zama kaŋ go fee gaa ka tee SALOMÓN ciyaŋ.

A go ga honga ka adduwa nda miila kulbaŋyaŋ te. A go ga honga ka tee ka bare deeyi zama MIILA boori.

GNOSTIQUE-almuuriko go ga kase DECIÚBITO DORSAL go gaa, ya gaa tee, araŋ bare taale gaa wala dugu gaa, gaŋkuwa da hanu foo, ka tee Handu foo.
