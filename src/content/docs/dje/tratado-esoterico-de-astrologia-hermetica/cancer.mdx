22 ZUWE ŋWAARAY KOI 23 YULIY KAA

"Jismi daŋ, kaŋ ga taaji fonda, han ziyaara, jasi-koyraa kaŋ yaŋu to, da beene-hayaŋ kulbu fonda, BRAHAMA bayraykoy kulu ga koy BRAHAMA do." (Batayyaŋ 24, Shaawara 8-Bhagavad-Gita).

"YOGI kaŋ, waati kaŋ a ga bu, a ga koy duruku fonda gaa, jasi-koyraa kaŋ yaŋu si, da beene-hayaŋ kulbu fonda, a ga too Koyraa dugu, woodin banda a ga ye teewo." (Batayyaŋ 25, Shaawara 8-Bhagavad-Gita).

"Fonda hinka din, kaŋ yaŋu to da kaŋ yaŋu si, i ga tiŋandi. Kaŋ yaŋu to, a ga franta, kaŋ yaŋu si, a ga ye teewo." (Batayyaŋ 26, Shaawara 8-Bhagavad-Gita).

"SERI si dumu, wala a si bu, wala a si tun teewo; a sinda asili; a yaama, a si barmay, a ga bisa kulu, a si bu waati kaŋ i ga jismi wi." (Batayyaŋ 20, Shaawara 8-Bhagavad-Gita).

EGO ga dumu, EGO ga bu. Ganji EGO da SERI game. SERI si DUMU wala a si BU wala a si TUN TEEWO.

"Tuma hayyey yaŋu hinza: naŋ-ga-baanaa, ga-baanaa da gandi. Hayyey din ga nina sombu, buuyaŋ banda, borey kaŋ mana han, amma, halaŋkoyo se araŋ." (Batayyaŋ 12, Shaawara XVIII-Bhagavad-Gita).

"Faham ay gaa, ya nin bora kaŋ gonda kambe gaabikoy! Ize sanda daliley gu, kaŋ ga sombu tuma hayyaŋ kunda, sanda bayray kaŋ ga bisa kulu, kaŋ ti tuma kulu banandi." (Batayyaŋ 13, Shaawara XVIII-Bhagavad-Gita).

"Jismi, EGO, meyyan, tuma hayyaŋ da IZEW (PLANETEY) kaŋ yaŋu ga bisa meyyan, woodin ya daliley gu." (Batayyaŋ 14, Shaawara 18-Bhagavad-Gita).

"Tuma kulu kaŋ yaŋu to wala kaŋ yaŋu si to, a boŋ, dumi wala laakal, a gonda daliley gu din." (Batayyaŋ 15, Shaawara 18, Bhagavad-Gita).

"Hay kulu sanda din, bora kaŋ ga bayray laaliyaŋ bay ATMAN (SERI), hal ABSOLUTE, sanda tuma teere, wowo din si di SAHAA." (Batayyaŋ 16-Shaawara 81-Bhagavad Gita).

BHAGAVAD GITA ga taaliyaŋ te, EGO (AY), da SERI (ATMAN) game.

Dabba baakasinciyaŋ kaŋ i ga ce HINEY, ti sombo kaŋ gonda JISMI, EGO (AY), MEYYAN da tuma hayyaŋ. Mashin kaŋ IZEW ga saray, wala sanda PLANETEY.

Waati boobo catakalmi kuukumi kulu gonda dogu, hala zama daari kaŋ ga too laabu gaa, i ga mashiney din kaŋ yaŋu funay da funay, koyroone gaa. Mashiney funaykoy miliyoŋey, da mashiney funaykoy miliyoŋey gaa.

KOYRAA ga EGOY kaa jineyaŋ gaa, Koyraa ga koy dumi da koy." Max Heindel ga ci CONCEPTION ga te alwaati kulu kaŋ KOYRAA go CÁNCER ra. Koyraa si CONCEPTION si waasey.

Jiiley iyyaŋ sintin ka koyraa ga may. Jiiley iyyaŋ hinka MERCUREY wano ti, hala zanka ga koy KARANTA, a si fo, a go saray koyne.

Septeniyoŋ hinza jiiley ra, zanka kaŋ yaŋu soola, kaŋ gonda jiiley waycindi da waranka teere, VENUS ga may, hananyaŋ kuru; woodin ti baakasin zaama, hananyaŋ zaama, zaama kaŋ iri ga di fundu sanda koore boŋ.

Jiiley 21 (WARANKA WO-HINKA) hala jiiley 42 (WARANKA TAACI-HINKA) iri ga ganda falkasina iri TIREEYO nda iri ga faham iri fundu. Zamana din ka KOOREY ga may.

SEPTENIO kaŋ gonda waranka taaci-hinka da waranka taaci-wo-kaaray jiiley ra, MARS wano ti da fundu ga bare koyroone gaa, zama MARS ti hay-jasey.

Zaman kaŋ gonda waranka taaci-wo-kaaray da waranka gu-cindi-wo-gu jiiley ra, JUPITER ti; borey kaŋ gonda JÚPITER nda boŋ gonda zaama toora ra, a bayray koyne waati kaŋ i ga naanay fundu ra, borey kulu ga day i se da daari kaŋ gonda GAAYI TILASA YAAMANKA DUNIYA, i gonda halaŋ nda i ga naanay to.

Baa, bora kaŋ gonda JÚPITER laaliyaŋ zaama toora ra; borey din ga taabi waani, i sinda mbu, wala kabu, wala ganda hayni, borey ga te boori i se, etc., etc., etc.

Zaman kaŋ gonda waranka gu-cindi-wo-gu da waranka gu-cindi-wo-tatari jiiley ra, koy-kuma kaŋ ti beene, gumo Saturno ga may.

Sahaa gumo ga sintin jiiley waranka gu-cindi-wo-gu ra. Waati kaŋ a bisa Saturno, Koyraa ga ye, a ga EGOY kaa, ZUMI gaa da a ga koy dumi da koy.

Da iri go di borey kaŋ yaŋu gonda jiiley ga bisa kul, iri ga saajaw jeeri koyne, a bayray borey din ga ye jiiley zanka, wayborey da wayzanka ga ye kaaryey da muɲey gaa. Borey kaŋ jiiley ga bisa waranka gu-cindi-wo-tatari da zanka kaŋ jiiley si bisa iyyaŋ KOYRAA ga may.

"Borey miliyoŋey ra, woy ga miila a ga too TILAS; borey kaŋ miila yaasey koyne woy ga jeeri tilas, da borey kaŋ gonda tilas ra, woy ga di AY feeri." (Batayyaŋ 3, Capitulo VII-Bhagavad-Gita.)

EGO KOYRAA wano ti ka jiismi daŋ, a ga koy duruku fonda gaa, jasi-koyraa kaŋ yaŋu si da BEENEY-HAYAN KULBU fonda da a ga ye jineyaŋ teewo. Koyraa ga koy da Koyraa ga kaa da koy, woodin ti SARRI.

EGO go kabu da JISMI KOYRAA. Kaari kaŋ yaŋu gonda dugu kaŋ TEOSOFÍA ga darsu, gonda nattara KOYRAA.

Jainoy tira hanna ka ci: "DUNIYA GO BAABU DA HAYKOY KAŋ YAŋU GO SAMSARA RA, KAŋ YAŋU DUMU DOWEY DA TOOREY GAMEY RA ZAAMA KAŋ I GA TUMA LAALI KOI BOOBO DA SANDA YA TE SANDA YA KOY A ZEEYAŋ DOWEY DUNIYA RA, Wala JAHANNAMA RA DA ZAAM BOOBO I GA BARE SANDA ASURA (BORI BIJIYAŊ). SANDA YA SI HAŊJI SAMSARA HAYKOY KAŋ YAŋU SI HAKUM DA DUMU DA TUN TEEWO ZAAMA KAŋ I GA TUMA LAALI".

KOYRAA ga EGOY kulu koy dumi da koy, amma si borey kulu kaa koyne. Waati hinka din ra, boobey ga koy DUNIYA-JAHANNAMA, yataŋ kaŋ go Koyraa gaa, ARZAKA-HAYNI kaŋ yaŋu sombu, ziyaara kaynandi ra, boro ga hangan gaa da niŋ-duuru.

Boobo yaŋu ga tun teewo sanda koyyaŋ kaŋ I go daaba da koy ka KOYRAA ga koy dumi, daari kaŋ i ga ye naaney Dowey DUNIYA.

TILASKOY, kaŋ i ga suuban, borey kaŋ i ga FURU EGO; I GA FABRIQUE KOOREY SEYYAŊ DA I GA HANBO DUNIYA, i gonda albarka, waati kaŋ i ga jismi daŋ da buuyaŋ, i ga taaji fonda gaa, kaŋ ti ziyaara, han, han, jasi-koyraa kaŋ yaŋu to da beene-hayaŋ kulbu, i go ENCARNADO da SERI, i ga BRAHAMA bay (BAABA KAŋ GO SIRRI RA) da a bayray i ga koy BRAHAMA (BAABA).

JAINISMO ga ci zama GRAN HAN din ra BRAHAMA ga zaba taaji ra DUMI KOY BOBO kaŋ i ga too TILAS.

TIRA GNÓSTICOS ka ci gonda SALVADOR WO-HINKA, a go kaari: AVATARA WO-HINKA; Amma da iri go miila JUAN BAUTISTA sanda bora zama JESÚS sanda AVATARA, zama PISCIS zaama kaŋ a gonda pasa, waati din ra iri ga di zama zaama zoodiyakoy wo-hinka kulu se gonda bora da AVATARA koyne, borey BOOBE taaji kaŋ gonda bora.

MAHAVIRA go BORA se BUDHA da JUAN BAUTISTA se JESÚS.

HANNA RASKOARNO (BUUYAŊ), BAABU DA HANANYAŊ KAŋ GO BOREY RA. Boro kaŋ ga BAY haakika Buuyaŋ boŋ, borey kaŋ I go EXPERIMENTADO sanda bora, a ga jeeri tiŋandi.

KOYRAA ga koy da koy da borey kaŋ yaŋu bu. Yataŋ ga gaaba taabiyan. Buuyaŋ da CONCEPTION ga sombu. FOND kaŋ ti FUNDU gonda jeeri da kaaŋkamey HARSI BUUYAŊ.

FURRAY kulu kaŋ ga jismi sombu, a ga tondi boori ga bisa kaŋ ga pasa da saray ziyaara gaa.

Sanda daari kaŋ ga bora bisa ZABARA TELEVISIÓN zama i ga di, ga tondi saraykoy kaŋ borey kaŋ yaŋu bu. Hay kaŋ ti écran gaa ONDAS zama zabarey, a ti embriyo gaa ONDAS zama buuyaŋ.

ONDAS VIBRATORIAS ZAMA BUUYAŊ go tondo IMAGEN bora kaŋ yaŋu bu. Imagen din ga tigi jineyaŋ kaŋ yaŋu yee harandi gaa.

Koyraa MAYRA, ZOOSPERMO ga hun toore gaa zama jineyaŋ, a ga han banda ka taabiyan nda koyra ga koy. Hani ga ganda sara boori, a ga dugu dugu ga too hariyaŋ koyra kaŋ go hangan gaa.

Waati kaŋ NÚCLEOS CAPITALES hinka din ga to hinka hin go gaa, CROMOSOMAS ga sintin zarfu caarey kaŋ ga bora, i ga sombu da I ga sombu haldi ra. Sanda din ti DISEÑO kaŋ boro agonizado da bu, ga too kristalisar embriyo gaa.

CÉLULA waaydin kulu kaŋ go bora ganje gaa, gonda sari waranka taaci-wo-kaaray kaŋ go duniya kaŋ iri go ra.

Células reproductivas kaŋ go ganje gaa gonda CROMOSOMA woy kulu baka ra, da boŋkoy zama taabiyan zaama waranka taaci-wo-kaaray kaŋ ga te embriyo kulu hin ti da ganji.

Dumi bora kulu, ganje kulu, ti mashin kaŋ gonda dogu. CROMOSOMA kulu go tondo dugu zama tuma, wala boori wala ciri kaŋ ga dugu, baka ga faham se, zama baka ga te HAYEY.

Haykoy kaŋ bu Onda, onda kaŋ yaŋu bu, ga te GENES boŋ ka naaney jineyaŋ kaŋ yaŋu ga ziyaara da koy. Sanda i ga jismi hinka kaŋ i bu ga di hinka koy embriyo gaa.

Zaman kaŋ ti CÁNCER, iri DISCÍPULOS GNÓSTICOS ga tilas I go PRACTICAR jina ka funay da fulanzam, saray RETROSPECTIVO tuma borey boŋ sanda boro kaŋ go di film sanda bonde ka too jirbi.

Hayaŋ RETROSPECTIVO din zama tuma iri go boŋ, ti AUTO-BAY, AUTO-FURU.

RECONOCER tuma tooyaŋ da kaŋ laala, iri ga zarma iri EGO KOYRAA, iri ga te CONSCIENTE le SUB-CONSCIENTE.

A ga tilas a ga too RETROSPECTIVA hal ZUMI da a ga taaley, taarey boori ga dugu ga bora kañje zama tuma ka taabi da BUUYAŋ zama jiismi kaŋ pasa. FUNAY da SOMBIY, da EXERCICIO RETROSPECTIVO, ga bora waasi ka taaley iri fundu ka koyda ka taaley.

EXERCICIO RETROSPECTIVO ga bora waasi I ga te haniyaŋ iri EGO KOYRAA boŋ, iri laali tumey boŋ. Iri ga taaley EGO ga ti MANOJO gaa, harriyaŋ, baakasin zaama, kuun, laakumi, hananyaŋ, yaawo, haŋkam da gonda bine-fongu da sooyay, etc.

Da iri go ba EGO FURU, iri ga tilas ka I ga darasi jina. EGO ti asil laaliyaŋ da bine-fongu.

SERI kulu, ATMAN, ti TILAS, amma A si DUMU wala A si BU wala A si TUN TEEWO; sanda din ti KRISHNA ci BHAGAVAD GITA ra.

Da karantekoyo go fulanzam EXERCICIO RETROSPECTIVO din, a ga boori hala zama INTERNO DUNIA araŋ ga AUTO-BAY, taaley fundu da funda kaŋ pasey.

Sanda MEDICO CIRUJANO ga tilas I ga darasi catakali cancere jina hala zama I ga furu, sanda GNÓSTICO ga tilas a ga darasi a EGO jina hala EXTIRPARLO.

Zaman kaŋ ti CÁNCER, gaabo kaŋ borey sombu BRONQUIOS da PULMONES kaŋ GÉMINIS, a ga tilas I ga pasa zaman din ra CÁNCER hal GLÁNDULA TIMO.

FUERZAS CÓSMICAS kaŋ yaŋu ga zaba iri ganje gaa a ga sombu GLÁNDULA TIMO ra da gaabo kaŋ yaŋu zama zaba da i ga bara triangle hinka kaŋ i ga sombu, sigil SALOMÓN.

DISCÍPULO ga tilas a ga miila han kulu sigil SALOMÓN hinka kaŋ go GLÁNDULA TIMO gaa.

Iri ci iri se GLÁNDULA TIMO ga naaney zanka kura boŋ. A ga boori GLÁNDULAS MAMARIAS kaŋ ga MAMA, go ga sombu GLÁNDULA TIMO. Woodin se ti hananyaŋ kaŋ mama si waasey ka ci kulu halda se zanka.

Borey kaŋ i duumi CÁNCER gonda jiri ga barmay sanda zaarey kaŋ KOYRAA go.

Borey kaŋ i duumi CÁNCER gonda salama nattara, amma waati kaŋ I ga kuun I ti kabbi.

Borey kaŋ i duumi CÁNCER gonda waasi kambe-arjeji, arjeji kaŋ ga te.

Borey kaŋ i duumi CÁNCER gonda IMAGINACIÓN fundu, amma I ga tilas ka dugu FANTASÍA gaa.

A ga han ka IMAGINACIÓN CONSCIENTE. A si waasey ka miila mashincina kaŋ i ga ce FANTASÍA.

CANCERINOY gonda nattara ka soola, ka kaynandi ka daabi.

CÁNCER ra iri ga di zaam boobo borey kaŋ yaŋu yaa boobo, i go taabiyan.

BOREY KAŋ YAŋU DUUMI CÁNCER ga ba takarda, film, etc.

Azakar kaŋ ti CÁNCER, TI PLATA. Tondo, PERLA; koore, BLANCO.

CÁNCER sigil CANGREJO wala ESCARABAJO HANNA, ti windo KOYRAA.
