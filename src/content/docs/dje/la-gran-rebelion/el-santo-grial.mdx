Gura Hërzen kaŋ yaŋ gonda darza kaŋ ga booriya, a gonda nda ceeci hananyaŋ gumo borey kul ga ceeci. Dumiɲankoyyaŋ borey koyne jirbiyaŋ zama ngey ma gura Hërzen kaŋ gonda darza ceeci Filastin laabu ra, amma ngey mana du'wa.

Waati kaŋ Ibrahima Annabiyaŋ kambe soojey duma k'i hanse Sudduma nda Gomorra koyey, i n'a ci a du Melkisedeku, laabu jinni. Certaŋ, boro beeri hunaŋyaŋ beere no ga goro kare kaŋ ga beeri dugu bindiyaŋ gaa, n'i goro Yerusalem gaa, annabiyaŋ wani beero.

Jirbiyaŋ sanda n'a ci, nda hano borey bay, Ibrahima Gnosa tayaŋ dumi, a yee pan nda duvan tarey dumi Melkisedeku jine.

A mana tilas ka ci Ibrahima Melkisedeku gaa, a n'a koyra nda a zaka dumi, sanda i n'a hino kitaabu ra.

Ibrahima Hërzen kaŋ gonda darza Melkisedeku kambe ra du, waati fo banda, a goro Yerusalem fu gaa.

Duba si ga Saba koyra no du'wa ka tabara zama haala wo ma taya. A guna Sulemanu Koy gaa Hërzen kaŋ gonda darza dumi, a sanna a gaa nda ceeciyaŋ hananyaŋ, a n'a koy.

Kabir Beero Yesu nda a ze taari kaŋ gonda darza ra, cina hinna tayaŋ ra, sanda i n'a hino Linjil kul ciina ra.

Yosefu Arimatea ra zey zaabo, a n'a ze gaay kaŋ a gaay Adorable kuyaŋ ra kambe kalaveri tondo gaa.

Waati kaŋ Pulisi Roma borey du Senatoo goro, i mana ze taari kaŋ gonda darza du.

Senatoo Roma mana ze taari kaŋ gonda darza kora, amma a goro nda tiŋgindi Longibus hunaŋ laabu ra, kaŋ jinjiroma Roma buru a jine gaay Rabbi ra.

I n'a I n'a Yosefu Arimatea goro kasu beeri ra zama a mana Hërzen kaŋ gonda darza yaafuyaŋ mi ga.

Waati kaŋ Senatoo hunaŋyaŋ kasu ra, a koy Roma Hërzen kaŋ gonda darza dumi.

Waati kaŋ Yosefu Arimatea Roma koy, a du Nerono ga dukuryaŋ kiristina borey gaa, a koy Mediterane teeku.

Hinna fo, maleyka fo guna a gaa hinna ra, a n'a ci: "Ze wo gonda hano beeri zama a ra boro kaŋ du'wa nduniya kuyaŋ." Yosefu Arimatea maleyka lordo, a ze wo binne fu ra kaŋ goro Montserrat, Katalunya, España.

Waati fo banda ze wo taka, nda fu nda tondo kuuru fo.

Hërzen kaŋ gonda darza Herme ze no, Sulemanu ze no, ze taari beeri fu kul ra no.

Allaah daŋarka ra, Hërzen kaŋ gonda darza mana takata ze no, hunaŋ gomorra no, a ra i manna binne mana kaŋ takata sahara.

Hërzen kaŋ gonda darza ga YONI hunsare bayhay, ze taari wo ra, a ga wani hananyaŋ no, misticborey Soma no, Ize kaŋ gonda darza Allahey ga.

Kristo Koyo Hërzen kaŋ gonda darza ze taari kaŋ ga beeri ra, Kristifikasyon zama gaay, sanda i n'a hino Rabbi Linjila ra.

Hërzen kaŋ gonda darza mana takata fu atari gaa. Bayhay, Almasihu ga duvan kaŋ gonda darza ze taari gaa ze.

A zaama, ze taari fu si goy ka ze taari kaŋ gonda darza kul takata.

A wo ga miila Ginebra Koyra Jinnay, a n'a Lana koyduvan Suffra nda Manti ize taariyaŋ ze ga.

Allahey kaŋ mana ku, i ga gonda ize kaŋ binne Ze Taari ra; borey kaŋ ga Ze Taari dukuryaŋ, i ga laafiyaŋ hunsare hunaŋ hananyaŋ.

Boro Kaŋ ga booriya ga wani hananyaŋ laaga, ize kaŋ goro ze divinal fu.

Transmutasyon zaaba kaŋ ga hayga tammaha, waati kaŋ boro ga ze binna ze ra.

Kristo Koyo taataa koyto, taataa gaari, taataa karjiyaŋ, taataa naŋ ka ze nda Allahey Hërzen kaŋ gonda darza ze ga.

Koytaray nda gunde kul gaa, nda si boro wani duvan kaŋ gonda darza daara kambe gaa.

Miila ka goro alama kaŋ ga beeri Thelema (gine).

Ze doonga kaŋ alama hunsare, zey ga kuyaŋ kaŋ ga booriya, kuyaŋ kaŋ ga booriya bora kaŋ ga beeri jine ra.

Allahey kaŋ takata kul gaa kul ra, i ga ze wani hananyaŋ ze ze taari.

Bingu hunaŋ ga koyyaŋ hunaŋ waati ra, a tilas ga duvan taariyaŋ ze jina ra Alquimia ze taari.

Koyta kaŋ gonda darza púrpura, koyra hunsare nda wura kul ga Kristo Koyo no.

Koy kaŋ ga walo nda ganji ga Hërzen kaŋ gonda darza kambe dabal gaa, a ga wura ze zama nga ma laaga.

Borey kaŋ Herme Ze saara tammaha koobiri ra, bayhay i ga tuku borey kaŋ mana beeri dumi nda laabu gaa.

Hina kul kaŋ hinna doŋ gaa ga du kitaaba kaŋ i n'a hina ga "Borey Fo Taka".
