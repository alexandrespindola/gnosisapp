Fundiɲe ra, sifa-laadaŋ kambeŋ, daŋ-goy kambeŋ, daŋ-goy kambeŋ hal a koy ka ban, nda din fo kulu sinda nafa.

Haŋ kaŋ gundaŋ ga te taray borey no, waati kaŋ iri ga ba sifa-laadaŋ din.

Iri si ba haŋ kaŋ sinda nafa, kaŋ yaŋandiyaŋ, kaŋ taabu sinda.

Iri muna ka iri ma gonda dangaŋ gaabu nda daŋ suuban bindeŋ goy boŋ kaŋ ga yaka nda taabu sinda.

A ga hima ka iri ma gonda dangaŋ gaabu, a ga hima ka iri ma hayyane sifa-laadaŋ, zama iri ma si te barankaŋ goy din ra.

Borey kaŋ si bay haŋ, borey kaŋ yaŋandiyaŋ, borey kaŋ ceeci taabu sinda, zama a ma tun borey-nuna kulu nda din ga hagu ga ti kuru-kayno kuray fundiɲe ra.

Taali kaŋ a guna zama a ma goy fundiɲe ra, a ga hima a ma faham a gaabu, n'a guna haŋ goy din.

Bayrandiɲe alhabaru zama haŋ, a goy jiney wandeŋ ra, zaari kulu fundiɲe ra, din ga ti cimi.

Borey ga laami halla alhabaru sinda soobo boŋ, i si bay nafa kaŋ alhabaru din gonda.

Iri na te yaami nda alhabaru sinda soobo din, iri muna ka iri ma tun din, iri ma hay nafa zama iri ma beeri fundiɲe ra.

Haŋ kaŋ a gaabu nda sifa-laadaŋ soobo boŋ kaŋ na tamma nda taabu sinda, a ga naŋ iri ma bay nuna din ra handi, hara.

A ga hima ka iri ma te yantarey gubu fundiɲe boŋ handi kaŋ goy ga te nda handi kaŋ fundiɲe ga te.

Hara fo kulu ra, zama a ma handi din goy fundiɲe ra nuna din ra, a ga hima a ma lamba goy-goy boŋ kaŋ borey ga naŋ waati kulu fundiɲe ra.

Borey fo kulu si tun handi din kaŋ goy ga te, a ga te taali zama a ma saba nda alhabaru dumi kulu.

Cimi, sabarey ga gaabu sifa-laadaŋ kaŋ hima alhabaru boŋ.

Waati kaŋ borey ga saba nda alhabaru fo, a si tun a ma hay din nafa zama a ma boori borey-nuna nda beeri fundiɲe ra.

Goy-borey kaŋ tun sabarey do, a naŋ a bayra si, a tun a ma handi din kaŋ fundiɲe ga te, a si tun handi kaŋ goy ga te.

Din ga naŋ goy-goy boŋ kaŋ borey ga naŋ a beeri a koy ta sabarey do.

Alhabaru sinda soobo fo kulu a ga hima a ma garga bayra nda hayyaney hay, haŋ goy din.

Garga alhabaru dumi kulu ga naŋ iri ma guna nuna din ra nda kambeŋ nda yo-nuna kaŋ goy alhabaru din ra.

Misale: Alhabaru kaŋ bine kanandi ga te; Yo-nuna kaŋ ga te bine kanandi, ga te bine kanandi danga bine kanandi.

A ma faham yo-nuna kulu, danga taari kaŋ ga te danga din, a ga hima tun sifa-laadaŋ gaabu, dangaŋ, haŋ.

Sifa-laadaŋ beeri zama a ma taali goy kaŋ borey gonda.

Haci, a ga te goy kaŋ ga gaabu iri ma tun taali goy kaŋ iri gonda.

Iri ga ci cimi, iri ga ci iri ga ti taali borey zama alhabaru sinda soobo kaŋ fundiɲe ga te.

Alhabaru soobo kulu nda naafa kulu, a go iri do wala iri do, a go ga gonda zaari kulu, a ga te taabu sinda.

Waati kaŋ iri tun alhabaru din do, hala taali fo kulu si koy haray.

Taali din ga ti fundiɲe ra, hala a ga gonda haray din, fundiɲe si te fundiɲe, a ga te mutu.

Hala a ga gonda sifa-laadaŋ alhabaru din ra, amma a si naŋ a ma gonda haray, a ga tun a ma tun a si naŋ a ma gonda haray.

Fundiɲe ga ti guuru kaŋ go ga tun taabu sinda nda alhabaru dumi nda dumi, a ga koy zaari kulu.

Iri si tun iri ma gaabay guuru din, alhabaru soobo wala alhabaru laalo, a ga goy zaari kulu, amma iri ga tun iri ma yantarey iri koy din do fundiɲe ra.

Waati kaŋ iri koy zama iri ma hay sifa-laadaŋ goy din ra, iri ga boori borey-nuna.

Fundiɲe fo kulu, alhabaru fo kulu, yo-nuna dumi dumi go din, a ga hima a ma faham nda haŋ goy din.

Din ga naŋ yo-nuna kulu kaŋ a ga goy goy fundiɲe ra, a ga faham nda haŋ goy din, a ga hima a ma koy nda yaamarey kaŋ babu Musa gonda.

Waati kaŋ iri go ga goy nda sifa-laadaŋ, a ga beeri gaabu. Hala iri ga tun iri ma ceeci fundiɲe ra yo-nuna din kaŋ a goy, amma waati kulu kaŋ goy din ga te.

Waati kaŋ yo-nuna din ga te koyya nda tun, iri ga handi kaŋ ga gaabu, hariya kaŋ ga gaabu.
