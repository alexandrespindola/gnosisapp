Gundekuna ganji ga haysi, a gonda dabari nda taali tammaasika, day, "raa" nda "sahaa" ciyaari kaŋ yaŋ mana gaaba ka gonda menaŋ taale taaliŋ taga.

Haykey goyey beeri hala i mana ciya sanda alhaalamey n'i guna mate.

Cimina, waati kaŋ haykey goy fo ceeci, araŋ ga gundekuna tammaasika tugu nda a maa, alhaalamey ciine duguyaŋ kaŋ baaba.

Alamaari, baabakaayaŋ kaŋ yaŋ na gundekuna haysi, i mana kaanaa ka gundekuna tiiji tukuyi.

Haykey goyey, manti sanda alhaalamey n'i guna mate.

Fundi nda goyey nda haykey goyey zaamayaŋ ga tonton dumi-dumi, zamu-zamu, waati kaŋ alhaalamey di a, a ga yakan.

Ceeci kaŋ araŋ ceeci haykey goy fo ra, manti sanda sabaabuyaŋ goy mate, haabay taali alhaalamey ga annabiyaŋ ka gonda goy sabaabuyaŋ sabaabuyaŋ ga gaskanta.

Baabakayaŋ ga annabiyaŋ, a mana ciya ka haykey goyey guna sanda sabaabuyaŋ mate, amma, haykey goyey ga kaanaa nda sabaabuyaŋ kaŋ a gonda baabakayaŋ ra.

Annabiyaŋ ga haykey goyey alhaali go gundekuna, day, alhaalamey mana yarda ga gaskanta.

Alhaalamey mana yarda araŋ n'i maa sanda annabiyaŋ.

Hinna kaŋ yaŋ a go ga nafa araŋ ciyaari nda sabaabuyaŋ kaŋ yaŋ na alhaalamey ciine dugu.

Alamaari, annabiyaŋ di a ga gunde gundey kulu di ga hinna kaŋ yaŋ na haykey goyey ga haŋ.

Waati kaŋ haykey goy fo ceeci, araŋ ga gunde gundey maa, araŋ ga saba nda a maa, araŋ ga gunde gundey tugu, sanda araŋ di a mate.

Araŋ wa gunde gundey maa, amma, araŋ mana haykey goyey di.

Sanda misali mate, araŋ ga Adam hanna ceeci.

Araŋ ga gaskanta ka Adam hanna man ciya alhaalamey di.

Sabaabuyaŋ ga ciya sanda zabaabuyaŋ, alhaalamey maŋ na yarda.

Day, are gonda gunde gundey, day, annabiyaŋ ga di sabaabuyaŋ mana yarda.

Araŋ ga ciya ka alhaalamey wanga koyaŋ, kaŋ Conde de Cagliostro, tammaasa kaŋ go ciita XVI, XVII, XVIII, a go fundi ciita XX ra, kaŋ Paracelso, baabakayaŋ fundekoy kaŋ yaŋ na ciita raŋ-raŋ, a go fundi, alhaalamey ga dari ga yarda.

Day, i go fundi doŋ duniya ra, Adam hanna kaŋ yaŋ na ciya ga taŋ, Adam hanna kaŋ yaŋ beeri zaka nda milyaŋ zaka kaŋ ga naŋ.

Hinna hinna nda Adam hanna, day, a mana ceeci annabiyaŋ, annabiyaŋ ga ciya nda alhaalamey nda annabiyaŋ mana di.

Sababu kulu kaŋ yaŋ, a mana gaaba annabiyaŋ ga yarda.

Adam hanna ga haykey goyey faham.

Adam hanna ga sanda Adam hanna mana naŋ.

Adam hanna ga gaaba naŋ, ka ciya sanda haykey goy mana naŋ.

Adam hanna ga faham Anatomia sariya.

Haykey goyey ga gaaba alhaalamey.

Wanga koyaŋ go ga guna nda naŋ nda nanguyaŋ.

Day, Adam hanna ga faham ga haykey goyey faham.

Mina kulu Adam hanna ra, a gonda sariya kaŋ mana di alhaalamey.

Adam hanna kaŋ mana di alhaalamey nda haykey goyey mana di.

Alhaalamey formulayaŋ mana kaanaa H2O, Mina heddiga, mina Mina heddiga ga tee haŋ, i go ga tee Adam hanna, kaŋ gonda Mina heddiga ga Mina heddiga ga di ka Adam hanna tee.

Baabakayaŋ mana gaaba ka tee haykey goyey kaŋ yaŋ go kaanaa.

Tugun ga ciya sanda tiiji tukuyi.

Baabakayaŋ baa Mina fo gonda maa fo nda dabari, ga ciya taali.

Dii baabakayaŋ go ga di a ga gunde gundey di? Di baabakayaŋ annabiyaŋ Mina nda haykey goyey go a sanda a di mate? Di baabakayaŋ baa aduniya ga ciya sanda baabakayaŋ mate?

Sabaabuyaŋ, haykey goyey manti sanda araŋ di mate, sabaabuyaŋ Adam hanna manti sanda araŋ di mate.

Hinna ga tee mana ciya baabakayaŋ, kaŋ Mina, wala kaanaa Adam hanna ka tee mana haykey goyey, a ga di Adam hanna goy zaamayaŋ.

Day, are ga ciya ka menaŋ, zaamayaŋ zaamayaŋ taali ga go, Hinna ga ciya ga menaŋ, a ga di Hinna ga tee nda zaamayaŋ.
