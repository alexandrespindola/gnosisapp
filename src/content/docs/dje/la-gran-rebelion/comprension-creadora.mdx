Foy ka bayraŋ daŋ kaŋ gonda ñaaŋu, i haŋ kaŋ ga ti damballa, zama ka fayyaŋ beeri yanoŋo kulu ga ti.

Waati kaŋ bayraŋ ga beeri foy ga, a ga naŋ gaaba-gaaba donda-donda kulu.

To, waati kaŋ foy ga beeri bayraŋ ga, a ga naŋ mo faham funfunaŋ goy.

Goyyaŋ al'aada ra, a ga hagu ka maŋ-ceeci zama ka maŋ-fatta.

Goyyaŋ al'aada ne a ga ti darzaŋ-suyyaŋ game ra, dangaŋ-game kaŋ ne a ga hagu ka jifaaji fattal.

Saa kaŋ gonda takaaŋ kaŋ ne a ga jizi, takaaŋ kaŋ gonda cere-cere, a ga hagu ka ceeci direct, kaŋ jifaaji kaŋ konda tammaasa ga fun funnaŋ.

A taaga kaŋ jifaaji kaŋ i fattal, a ga hagu ka goy ga nda baasi, zama ka ciyaŋ-waani nda yanoŋo.

A ciyaŋ, i si koy nda "mimi" donda-donda kulu, nda i ga kiyandi kaŋ i ga naŋ i ga jifa.

Nda i kay yaggaŋ boŋ, i ga kiyandi ka funnaŋ ga zama ka sanda kaŋ zaa kookoro, i si hagu ka goy kaŋ, nda i kay yaggaŋ boŋ.

Taaga kaŋ, i ga koy ka waani yaggaŋ ga koyne, ka fun ka kaŋ zaa-yaa nda yaggaŋ, nda koyne i ga fun nda kamba ka hondo kaŋ koyne ka sanda kaŋ zaa zango.

Dangaŋ, i si koy nda taame-tiyaŋ kulu, nda i ga kiyandi kaŋ i ga naŋ i ga waani nda yanoŋo.

Waati kaŋ ŋwa koy nda "mimi" halla a ga beeri sanda waati kaŋ i ga ciyaŋ.

I sanda kaŋ "mimi" kaŋ ga ti harrabi, kaŋ ga ŋwa koy gaŋ gaabaŋ kaŋ yaŋ gonda gaabaŋ donda-donda zama ka saŋ cineema gaabaŋ ra, gaabaŋ kaŋ gonda hiijiŋ da harrabiŋ, nda koyne i ga koy nda film-film donda-donda, taaga kaŋ "mimi" kaŋ ga ti harrabiŋ ga beeri.

Amma nda i si koy nda yeen'kaŋ, i ga waani nda yanoŋo sanda sheitaan kaŋ gonda gaabaŋ, taaga kaŋ fayyaŋ funnaŋ ga fun yaanoŋo game ra.

Waani, i ga hagu ka ciini goy ga nda bayraŋ zama ka maŋ fattal kaŋ.

Goŋ kaŋ konda beeri ga, i ga koy nda hollaranciŋ, kaŋ ga ti gonga.

Nda i bay gaaba-gaaba donda-donda goyo, nda goŋ kaŋ i ga faham kaŋ i ga ba gaabo, i si naŋ gaaba-gaaba gonga.

Film-film donda-donda, suuru donda-donda, film-film kaŋ gonda hiijiŋ, goyyaŋ al'aada ra, a ga naŋ goy waati kaŋ i ga koy nda ceeci kaŋ gonda takaaŋ.

Taaga kaŋ i ga ceeci kaŋ i si koy nda film-film donda-donda ga, i si koy nda miina donda-donda mo, i si koy nda goyyaŋ ga mo.

Gaaba-gaaba donda-donda gaabaŋ ga fun sanda gaabaŋ kaŋ gonda ciiri zama ka saŋ gaabaŋ miina ra da zoolaŋ yaanoŋo ra da baasi goyyaŋ ra.

A ga ti gonga kaŋ i si koy nda maŋ gaabo, kaŋ film-film yaanoŋo goy i ga kaŋ i ga ba gaabo.

Kaabin'kaŋ, i si ceeci kaŋ yaŋ ga beeri da yaabo, i goy sanda gaabaŋ donda-donda kaŋ yandi nda kookoro.

Beeri kaabaŋ kaŋ, i ga goy zama ka kookora fun gaabaŋ donda-donda goyo, i ga gonga kaŋ waati kaŋ i ga koy nda yeen'kaŋ.

Filime al'aada, filime foo kaŋ beeraŋ game ra, filime tammaasaŋ game ra, koy ga ti "mimi" kaŋ ga ti gaaba, kaŋ i si hagu ka ŋaa.

Goyyaŋ al'aada ne a ga ti fayyaŋ cineema game ra, kaŋ i ga hagu ka ceeci maŋ sanda yaŋ goy.

Amma a ciyaŋ i ga kiyandi kaŋ i ga ceeci maŋ, i ga kiyandi kaŋ i ga fun funnaŋ, am'kaŋ i ga jizi ka ceeci direct.

ŋwa kaŋ gonda fayyaŋ hal kaŋ ga ti goyoŋ, gonda dangaŋ, hal kaŋ si jizi ka ceeci maŋ, a ga gaaba zama, a ga koy zama, a si goy zama ka ceeci goyo da toora ga.

Direct kaabaŋ ra i ga ci goyo, a ci goyo kaŋ beeraŋ game ra, goyo kaŋ gonda tammaasa, yaanoŋo goyo kaŋ gaabaŋ gaabaŋ donda-donda gaabaŋ gaabaŋ goyo.

Filime kulu kaŋ gonda hiijiŋ, "mimi" kaŋ gonda miina, suuru, baaru, hiijiŋ etc., kaŋ goyyaŋ ga baasi kaŋ i ga hagu ka ciini ga, afollo goyo zama ka fattal direct, a ga hagu ka ciyaŋ.

Fayyaŋ ga ti beeri, am'kaŋ i ga kiyandi ka tammaasa goyo ga koyne, kaŋ cineema yaanoŋo gonda fayyaŋ direct, goyo ga gaabiyo.

Filime, i si koy nda goyo kaŋ beeraŋ game ra ga ti kookora, hal'to hagu kaabaŋ kulu.

Amma nda a ti zaama kulu ga, i ga ti fayyaŋ sanda darzaŋ kaŋ gonda takaaŋ goyo.

Goy kaŋ yaŋ yaŋ ga ti waani da gaaba-gaaba donda-donda, a ga ti kaba.

Gaabaŋ kaŋ gonda miina ga, mo a gonda gaaba.

Gaaba a ga beeri zama ka ti gaaba hollaranciŋ, nda i ga ti goyo kaaba sanda goyo zama.

Gaaba a ga koy nda gaabaŋ kaŋ ga ti jaafa, kaŋ ga ti karama, kaŋ ga ti kaani, kaŋ ga ti ŋwa da sanda kanisa kulu.

Fayyaŋ a ga ti gaaba, amma goyo kaŋ yaŋ yaŋ ga ti waani da gaaba-gaaba yaanoŋo, a si koy kulu, dangaŋ waati kaŋ i ga ceeci filime ga.

A ga ti gaaba kaŋ ga baasi, hal i ga maŋ fattal da "mimi" afollo zama ka waani da yaanoŋo, amma a si koy, i ga ceeci filime ga.
