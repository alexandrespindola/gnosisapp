Mayra wala wala maa ga boori kaŋ hin ka tabbatar da ganayyaŋ da cimi ga bisa nda care nda gundo?

Ganayyaŋ wane, cimi wane, a goy nda baaru ka ganayyaŋ hanse.

Cimi da ganayyaŋ bisa nda care, ayfo tammaasa yaŋ no, amma boro hakkillo kaŋ ganayyaŋ na gaabi, a ga anniya day ganayyaŋ da cimi yaŋ ga bisa nda care.

Fahamay kul kaŋ tammaasa kulu, kaŋ yaŋ na tarandi nzaama nda cimi, wane fooyaŋ zama a ma kay a boŋ, zama mayra?

Hakkilla hinka kaŋ yaŋ na tarandi gaabi da nzaama gaabu kulu, kaŋ yaŋ go ga sasabara, ga taale ga gaabu kaŋ yaŋ ga yadda ganayyaŋ da cimi ga bisa nda care, mayra go ga gaskiyaŋ gonda? Mayra hin ka gatakoyoŋ tammaasa nda care, zama mayra, ganayyaŋ da cimi ga bisa nda care?

Laakal bayray kul boŋ, boro kul boro kul gonda dogim zama ganayyaŋ da cimi ga bisa nda care nda gundo.

Hal tin nzaama gaabi, a si hin ka tabbatar da ganayyaŋ da cimi ga bisa nda care nda gundo.

Boro kaŋ yaŋ go ga n'ayyano kay yaŋ ga gaabu, yaŋ ga yadda ciimiyaŋ ga bisa nda ganayyaŋ da n'ayyaano, amma a si wano tammaasa zaama.

Fitiye zama taama hanse, boro hin k'a taaga zama yaŋ ga yadda ka ganayyaŋ gonda, zaama kul ga ganayyaŋ gonda.

Ciimiyaŋ ma salaŋ ciimo ga, amma ciimi kaŋ zaama kul ga, yaŋ si taama ciimiyaŋ, zaama kul ga, yaŋ go ga ciimi da ganayyaŋ gonda.

Hal tin a kayri, ciimikoyoŋ si taama ciimiyaŋ.

Waati kaŋ yaŋ ga taama ciimiyaŋ nda ganayyaŋ kaŋ yaŋ gonda, day yaŋ si taama ciimiyaŋ, yaŋ go ga ganayyaŋ taama.

Amma ciimikoyoŋ kaŋ yaŋ du hakkillo gaabi, yaŋ ga yadda ganayyaŋ da ciimiyaŋ ga bisa nda care nda gundo, amma a si wano tammaasa.

Maa si gaabu kaŋ yaŋ go ga gaabu, yaŋ si gaabu, amma yaŋ go ga pontificio da dogimatiko, hal tin yaŋ ga ganayyaŋ kaŋ yaŋ na tarandi, yaŋ ma ga bisa nda ciimiyaŋ.

Waati kaŋ hakkillo, taama ciimiyaŋ, yaŋ ga yadda da gaabu ciimiko, a go ga zama yaŋ ma gaabu ciimiyaŋ kaŋ yaŋ si taama.

Hakkillo si taama ka taama taama hanse, amma a go ga gaabu nda gaabu, yaŋ go ga zama yaŋ ma gaabu ciimiyaŋ kaŋ yaŋ si taama.

A gundiko kulu, hakkillo si taama, hakkillo si taama ka yaŋ go ga.

Hakkillo zama kul ga, yaŋ go ga gaabu nda gaabu zama yaŋ ma gaabu ciimiyaŋ kaŋ yaŋ si taama.

Ciimiyaŋ hinka: wane fooyaŋ go ga gaabu ciimiyaŋ. Wane fooyaŋ yaŋ go ga gaabu.

A si hin ka koy ciimiyaŋ ciinikoyoŋ kulu, hal tin yaŋ mana may ciimikoyoŋ.

Maa zama yaŋ ma gaabu zaama kul kaŋ yaŋ go ga gaabu ga.

Hakkillo da gaabu kaŋ go ga gaabu, a si hin ka taama ciimiyaŋ nda n'ayyano.

Ciimiyaŋ na halla, Malaika da mayra na gonda.

Maa si gaabu kaŋ maa du ma hayray, amma mayra yaŋ ma du maa may.

Ciimiyaŋ kaŋ si gaabu kaŋ yaŋ go ga may, hal tin yaŋ mana mayray. Gaabu kul ga may, ciimo ga hayray.

Waati kaŋ Ego go ga may, hakkillo taama zama a ma taama ciimiyaŋ kulu, hal tin yaŋ go.

Hakkillo taama kaŋ yaŋ taama ciimiyaŋ kul, yaŋ go ga, hal tin yaŋ go ga, hal tin yaŋ mana taama ciimiyaŋ.
