Šiši ka tammahayaŋ gaa, miila nda jiibo game ra, sanney kaŋ tonton, woodin sifa kulu sinda ceeci.

Bingiri beeri goy borey game ra, bingiri kaŋ ga tee hay kaŋ sinda nafa, nda hay kaŋ ga tee beene beene.

Borey gumo ga tammahã hay kaŋ sinda nafa, kaŋ ciya hay kaŋ beeri, i ga anniya labiri hay kooni, wala motoci kooni, wala batama jeeri, wala alhakku zama ka tee hay kaŋ tonton.

I ga labari kaŋ ga key, nda baaruwa gaaburu, nda fundi zaama, nda duhu gaabu, nda jahaaji yaaru, nda motoci yaaru, nda wanka haw, nda haari, nda zanta ciiri, daabinandi.

A bayyanan, waati kaŋ alboro kaŋ ga key wala wayboro kaŋ go cere gonda, si hãayani hayhari, zama woodin sinda gundekuna i anniyey ra, wala i tattaabaaruwey ra, wala i zumey hayyey ra, i ga zaabi da bingiri baji, wala i ga teeni i me, i ga tun i kambe, i ga koy da baahana-laala.

Nufsiya takama, bingiri kaŋ ga humburu, gonda duuba hinka; afollo beeri, nda gundo hayhari kaa sinda wanguya jine kulu.

Gonda takama, hawjiya yaalo, boro kulu mana di i jine ra, wala i di i ga tee hay kaŋ ga key sanda, wala i di i ga tee i zumey hayyey ra.

To, d'a te boro ga hin ka duu bingiri naŋŋa wala wayboro kaŋ ga tee beene beene, hawjiya jeeri zama nda alwaati, nufsiya hawjiya, dumi kaŋ ga kaydi, hayhari kaŋ go zugu, waatoo, a ga hin ka tee hay kulu kaŋ tonton.

Kaa, hay kaŋ go kaa jine da sanni kayna, da jine bine-gaabu, da wanguya zugu; a ga hin ka tee tunku, cere kaŋ go waneese ra wala ceeri beeri, zumu diiji, kaŋ sinda nafa zama hãa kaŋ goo.

Tunkuyaŋ, wawaayaŋ, kaŋ sinda nafa zama hãa kaŋ ga key, i gonda tiŋaliya alwaati ra zama ka kaa jine nda wanguya jine kaŋ goo, da wanguya zugu, da hawjiya kayna kaŋ ga tee cere kayna.

D'i tee borey kaŋ ciya dayyaŋ fundi, bingiri naŋŋa kaŋ goo gunda ra wala waranka jarebeere kaŋ go kooki ra wala tabitiri beere, i mana daaba jiibo jine kaŋ goo gunda ra, i ga ciya cere kaŋ go ga dee, wanka hawjiya ga daaba hawjiya jine, batama kaŋ kaa ka tee bingiri baji kaŋ humburu.

Šiši ka tammahayaŋ gaa, borey ga koo dumi hãa kaŋ ga key alwaati kulu; woodin sifa kulu sinda ceeci.

Boro kulu sinda hãa kaŋ mana bine-gaabu di wala wanguya jeeri, kaama haya kulu kaŋ ga tee boro haabu, a ga duu hay kaŋ kaŋ goo kaŋ di waati kayna.

Dumi ga duu lala-garay, a ga fundu nda muwa-waatima nda hawyaŋ kaŋ goo ga tee.

Dumi tee daalibi beeri; boro kaŋ go dumi ra, kaŋ kaa nda wanguya kulu nda hawjiya kulu, a sinda kaadu, a ga yaaru dumi ra.

Da boro te hay kaŋ boro kulu mana te, waatoo, boro ga tabiti nufsiya hawjiya kaŋ tee alwaati kayna ra, šiši ka tammahayaŋ gaa, a ga cere-taŋŋa haw nda tee cere-taŋŋa boro.

Woo no, cere-taŋŋa ga wangu, ama taŋŋa dumi bingiri naŋŋa, takama, wala daabaŋ kaŋ sinda kaadu, dumi ga koo i kulu; batama kaŋ ga tee goo.

Dayyaŋ ga tee hay kulu. Dayyaŋ fundi kaŋ sinda hawjiya cere-taŋŋa, a ga jeeji beeri, dumi ga hanji dayyaŋ.

Cere-taŋŋa ga wangu ka boro tee, a ga tee tammaasa woodin zama labutiri cindi ra; kaama dayyaŋ ga koo boro.

Ama, haya kaŋ iri go ga ci sinda nafa zama boro kulu, kaama afollo gumo; i ga ciya i maa kuliyaŋ zama wala i baaba Taraazan zama.

Cere-taŋŋa ga bita zumey hayyey ra, dumi takami kaŋ ga tee, kaŋ waandu, a ga hin ka iri may cere borey.

Šiši, tammaasa cere-taŋŋa ga jeeji kaamu zama bingiri ka tee dumi.

Borey sinda wangu ka haw cere-taŋŋa nda woodin zama, a ga hanji, zumey cere-taŋŋa ga jeeji, ga hanji, ga kooko dumi.

Klaabi beeri zama cere-taŋŋa ga tee goy cindi ra zama cere kaŋ sinda nafa kaŋ iri gonda gunda ra.

Waati kaŋ dumi boro ga koo wanguya kulu zama anniyey cere-taŋŋa, cere-taŋŋa ga hallaka a zama a sinda nafa zama tammaasa.

Zama dumi haabu no ga tee bingiri beeri, cere ka tee daalibi beeri nda daalibi zama, a ga baa i ga hallaka a zama.

Zama hawjiya ga tee jine takama yaaru zama cere-taŋŋa, da bine.

Šiši borey gonda cere ka tee i haabu, cafe ra, da kantini ra, da gundu waasiyaŋ ra, da zumey kuwa ra wala guusu beeri ra, daabinandi.

A bayyanan, hay kulu ga tee woo hay boro haabu, da cere ka tee ga waaru hay kulu; woodin sifa kulu sinda ceeci, boro kulu kaŋ gonda gundo ga hin ka di haya kulu nda fundi da zugu.

Daalibi, ka di haya kulu, haariyaŋ kaŋ goo bine ra, kaŋ tee i go ga yeeji wala i ga siida nda fundi beeri, i ga baa i ga jippa kitaaba nda zanta ka di labari kaŋ ga key.

Hawyaŋ kayna cafe nda labari kaŋ ga key ga tee haaruwa beeri zama cere-taŋŋa.

Šiši, i ga ciya i ga tee cere kaŋ tonton; šiši ka tammahayaŋ gaa, i ga gundekuna nda i hangaŋ, hay cere-taŋŋa kaŋ goo kitaaba ra ga tee i ga jeeji kaamu. Šiši ka tammahayaŋ gaa, cere ka tee boro kaŋ sinda kaadu kaŋ go duma ra, i sinda gundo zama ka waaru tattaabaaru woo.
