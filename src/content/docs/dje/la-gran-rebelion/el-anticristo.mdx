Faham-ize gaa baani d'alhakki yaŋ, sifa kaŋ bangay taali šilima n'zey, sanda bine šilima hagu, šir'walaa, nga no Almasihu-koy-šii.

Nda borey kaŋ yaŋ ti Almasihu-koy-šii ga ti boro fo to, kaŋ hayandi laabu foo ra wala kaŋ kaa gallu foo ra, borey din dogu gumo.

Ir nee da garaw, Almasihu-koy-šii si boro fo to, amma borey kul.

Širi, Almasihu-koy-šii go boro kulu bine koondey ra ka bangay jina šiyara.

Faham kaŋ go Bi šilima ra, a ga nafii, amma faham kaŋ kay Bi si ra, a si nafii.

Faham-ize kaŋ sinda Bi, nzaaw kaŋ Almasihu-koy-šii bangay kaŋ gaama, a ga fun din.

Širi, nzaaw bumbum nga no Almasihu-koy-šii. Hasiyaši, ndunya wo, da taabi nda čičeeji kul kaŋ go a ra, Almasihu-koy-šii no g'a may.

Laabu wo kaŋ funyaŋ a go, širi, Almasihu-koy-šii no sabu.

Taali-boro kaŋ Pawulu Tarso salaŋ a koyne, a ga bangay nda gaskiya šilima zamana wo ra.

Taali-boro kaa koyne, a go bangay nda hay kul, širi, a gonda hanney kaŋ a go bendi hay kul.

A go ceeci cafeey ra, a go dayray nda Ž.N, a go beežay Ginevra ra da laakal, a go te šilima-žibu labratori ra, a go soobay bombu-kuuku, roketi-kanandi, haw šingarey, bombu-beeri, dsb, dsb, dsb.

Almasihu-koy-šii ga yadda da faham-izetaray, baani d'alhakki yaŋ borey šilima, a go yadda a bay alaamu kul kaŋ go nateera ra.

Almasihu-koy-šii go yadda a bumbum bay hay kul, a go hundu nga teoriya kul haybey ra, a go gaaba da hay kul kaŋ hane Irikoy wala kaŋ borey go na ŋwaaray.

Almasihu-koy-šii bumbum nafii dumi, borey gaaba da ka širwa nda borey gonda, a go jaŋ gumo.

Almasihu-koy-šii go hallak yadda-ize šilima, muñay nda yase.

Borey kul go gurmu Almasihu-koy-šii jine. Širi, a soobay jiržii-hanney, kaaru-garžiyaŋ, moto-ganda šiyaraw, šeriyyaa kaŋ gonda haaw, dsb.

Hal šiyara wo, waati kaŋ borey ga dogu Almasihu-koy-šii ra? Boro kaŋ ga gaaba da hari kul din, kaŋ ga kaa da haaw nda hagu koyne, a go bumbum hallak da borey haara, da borey gaaba, da borey šilima, da borey ne a ga laaloo nda jahil.

A go gaafaa nda borey kaŋ ga himandi nda borey kaŋ gonda bayri, amma borey din ga gaaba, borey go muɲa.

A bay kaŋ dabba-ize faham kaŋ borey nee boro, a go ti robotu kaŋ borey dayandi da kinder, primary, secondary, preparatoria, yunifasitee, dsb.

Boro kul si hin ka gaaba da robotu kaŋ borey dayandi, a go goy sanda dayray, a si hin ka goy dayray si.

Almasihu-koy-šii soobay dayray kaŋ borey go dayandi robotu din dumi zamana wo ra.

Ka širšira šiyara din, ka garwandi hay kaŋ ni go ceeci, a go jaŋ gumo, zama a go dayray se, robotu fo kul si hin ka yadda da hay kaŋ go dayray se, zama borey ga a dayandi sanda dayray, hal borey si ga dogu dayray ra, zama a ga himandi nda heeri, hay kaŋ sinda dumo nda laakal sinda.

Robotu fo dogu nga dayray ra a go ti laafayo, hay kaŋ si hin hal, zama nga širima go dayray se.

Hasiyaši, hari si sanda robotu go mi, bayru fo go, hakko fo go, kaŋ robotu si hin ka yadda.

Robotu go gaaba nda, a gonda hakku ka gaaba, zama borey si a dayandi bayru wala hagu fo se, wala hay fo kaŋ si sanda a dayray se.

Almasihu-koy-šii soobay robotu dayray, robotu go gurmu nga mayra jine nda yase. Dumo be robotu dogu nga mayra hihama ra?

A hay hay-ize kaŋ ga yase nda ka širii; Bi kaŋ go bangay hay kulu ra a ga gaamu gumo.

Šir'walaa, nateera go daŋ bayzi kul ra kaŋ hay hayandi dumi, bayzi kul din ga gaamu gumo, alaamu din gaandi kul kaŋ nateera ra, kaŋ hin ka dugu hal, a go ti hay kaŋ si hin ka gaaba da gaskiyey kaŋ go alaamu kul kaŋ borey hin ka dugu kaŋ go nateera ra, alaamu kul din ra.

A naŋ kaŋ hay-ize kaŋ hay hayandi hin ka bumbum dugu alaamu kul kaŋ go nateera ra, hasiyaši, Almasihu-koy-šii dayray go muɲa nda hay-ize kaŋ go hina šiyaraw kaŋ nateera daŋ hay-ize kaŋ hay hayandi bayzi kul ra ga hallak wažiri.

Almasihu-koy-šii go gaaba nda borey mi šiyara; hay kulu kaŋ hayandi, da Almasihu-koy-šii odiri borey ga dayandi a.

Žirba si kaŋ Almasihu-koy-šii go hallak yadda-ize šilima hagu, kaŋ borey bay sanda "hinna dugu gaskiyey alaamu kaŋ fun kayra".

Bayru kaŋ širii, si sanda teoriya kul kaŋ go yunifasitee ra, kaŋ go soolay nda ze, a si yadda da robotu dumi kaŋ go Almasihu-koy-šii se.

Gera, waybu nda hanan go gunda kaŋ Almasihu-koy-šii farga laabu kulu ra, žirba si kaŋ a ga yaŋ sooba hala čeri gaa.

Hasiyaši, waati kaa ka bare gaamu kaŋ Nabiyaŋ kul salaŋ ka koyne, boro kul si ga gaaba da Almasihu-koy-šii.
