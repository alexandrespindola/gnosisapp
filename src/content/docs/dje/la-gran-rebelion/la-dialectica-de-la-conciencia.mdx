Tomo kaŋ gundekuna, daŋ kaŋ ga taari da ŋaa gumo dumiyaŋ kaŋ yaŋwaŋ ga tiya iri ra, borey hanna, jijiryanay, da yeejiyaŋ ga hin ka fatta.

Maa ga hima iri ma ye koyne tinye kaŋ a jinay kaŋ neesan, da ga barmay kokoyandiŋ gundekuna goy boŋ, haŋ koyne iri gonda yerzu kaŋ iri ma bare.

Yerbu kaŋ iri ma baa goy hanna, a ga tilas daŋ kaŋ yerzu no gonda kaŋ iri ma bare gunda ra.

Amma iri mana ba goy kaŋ ga gundekuna bare kaŋ a ga taari, koyne kaŋ iri ga n'a yeesu kayanta boŋ, a ga taati kaŋ a ga tilas.

A ga waani iri ma anniya goy boŋ, daŋ kaŋ iri mana baa goy kaŋ a ga taati.

A ga haŋ kaŋ baakasinay ga tilas daŋ kaŋ iri ga tinye kaŋ iri ga barmay kayanta boŋ koyne.

I ga gaabu iri ma bay haŋ kaŋ ti laakal, zama borey daŋ ga tonton ga yeeji kaŋ yaŋ ma anniya n'a boŋ.

Boro kul ga bay zama boksere kaŋ ga farga n'a fuuru taate boŋ, n'a laakal halaci.

A ga bay, daŋ kaŋ a ye koyne, boksere beeri ga laakal bare.

Gaa ga bay kaŋ bambariyanay gonda farga kaŋ ga bayriyaŋ da laakal.

Daŋ kaŋ iri ga kaa nda laakal jine, iri gonda laakal 3%, amma 97% ga hasara gunda hansi, gunda hasi, da gunda zaakayan.

3% ga hin ka bari daŋ kaŋ iri ga goy iri boŋ.

A ga waani kaŋ iri ma bari laakal, daŋ kaŋ iri ga goy da goy gaabu.

A ga tiŋan kaŋ laakal ga hin ka bare da goy hanna da banariyaŋ kaŋ ga anniya.

I gonda heryaŋ dumiyaŋ iri ra, iri ga tilas iri ma bay: A jina.- heryaŋ kaŋ ga tiŋan. A hinka.- heryaŋ kaŋ ga fundu. A hinza.- heryaŋ kaŋ ga laakal. A taaci.- heryaŋ kaŋ ga miila. A guu.- heryaŋ kaŋ ga anniya. A iddu.- heryaŋ kaŋ ga laakal. A iyye.- heryaŋ kaŋ ga fundu kaŋ ga han. Daŋ kaŋ iri ma bari heryaŋ tiŋan, iri mana ga hin kaŋ iri ma laakal bare.

Daŋ kaŋ iri ma bari heryaŋ kaŋ ga fundu iri gunda ra, iri mana ga hin kaŋ iri ma laakal bare.

Gundekuna goy daŋ ga goy gunda ra, zama laakal mana goy n'a ra.

Goy kaŋ ga miila beeri, heryaŋ miila mana ga hin kaŋ iri ma bare laakal.

Heryaŋ kaŋ ga anniya, daŋ kaŋ iri ma bari n'a kaŋ a ga koy nangu, iri mana ga hin kaŋ iri ma laakal bare.

Heryaŋ kul ga goy dumiyaŋ, daŋ kaŋ koyne mana goy nda laakal.

Laakal ga hin ka bare da goy hanna da banariyaŋ kaŋ ga han.

Laakal hanna kaŋ borey gonda, a mana ga bari amma i ga hasara n'a tundu.

A ga bay kaŋ daŋ kaŋ iri ga ba haŋ kul kaŋ ga tiya iri gunda ra, iri ga hasara heryaŋ kaŋ ga laakal tundu.

Iri ga tilas iri ma laara dumiyaŋ daŋ kaŋ iri mana ba haŋ kul kaŋ ga tiya, dumi haabay, dumi beeri, dumi ciini, iri ga nafa heryaŋ kaŋ ga laakal.

Laakal yerbon ga ti heryaŋ dumiyaŋ kaŋ gonda gaabi beeri.

Mana ga bira laakal da laada, zama i gonda farga zama nuuru kaŋ ga taksi da laara zama laada kaŋ iri ga koy.

Goy daŋ ga goy iri gunda ra, zama laakal mana goy n'a ra.

Gundekuna goy dumiyaŋ ga koy iri ra, zama laakal mana goy n'a ra.

Heryaŋ moto ga hin ka taksi hanna, wala kaŋ iri ma jinda gundekuna piano taate boŋ, zama laakal mana goy n'a ra.

Laakal ga ti nuuru kaŋ ga gunda zaakayan mana laaru.

Boreru mana laaru nuuru beene.

Iri ga tilas iri ma bare zama nuuru laakal ma kaa iri gunda ra, zama nzaanu beeri kaŋ ga ti iri boŋ.

Iri ga bay bayriyaŋ n'a maaya kaŋ ti Yohanna, daŋ kaŋ a n'a ciiti: "Nuuru kaa nzaanu, amma nzaanu mana bay n'a."

A mana ga hin kaŋ nuuru laakal ma kaa iri gunda ra, daŋ kaŋ iri mana goy nda laakal taari.

Iri ga tilas iri ma bare nuuru zama yaa iri gunda ra.

Boro mana ga laara iri taate boŋ, daŋ kaŋ iri mana ba iri ma bare, baaka kaŋ a ga tiyandi daŋ kaŋ boro ga ba koyne koyne.

Iri gonda yerzu kaŋ iri ma bay goy kaŋ a ga gunda iri boŋ.

Laakal kaŋ ga bare, a ga bare iri ma taari haŋ kaŋ a ga tiyandi.

Danga laakal ga bare, a ga bare iri ma taari haŋ kaŋ a ga tiyandi.

Baaka maaya kaŋ ga hin ka taari goy miila.

Maa ga bare kaŋ iri ma taata koyne miila, amma miila koyne mana ga hin ka taari haŋ kaŋ a ga tiyandi.

Goy laakal ga ti koyne koyne, iri ga hin kaŋ iri ma taari koyne koyne.

Dumiyaŋ mana goy nda goy kaŋ iri ga miila.

Funde ga koynte iri ga maata n'a, iri n'a wi.

Daŋ kaŋ iri ga tinte iri ma gunde, iri ga yeeji kaŋ iri ma bay haŋ kaŋ a ga tiyandi, iri ga laara haŋ kaŋ iri ga ba da miila kaŋ mana han.

Iri ga miila, iri ga anniya kaŋ dumi kul ma koy da miila kaŋ iri gonda.

Laakal ga ti yerbon kaŋ a ga fundu.

Hukumey kul gonda iri gunda ra, daŋ kaŋ iri mana gundey n'a iri mana gey n'a iri gunda banda.

Boro gonda beene gaa da beene gonda boro gunda ra.

Tiyaŋ kaŋ ti iri taari da wangu kaŋ a ga hin ka taari haŋ kaŋ a ga tiyandi.

Ciine kaŋ ga laakal ti hanna, a ga yeesu koyne daŋ kaŋ borey ga bare ga hin ka bay n'a.

Wanda ga ba laakal bare, a ga tilas a ma daara haŋ kul kaŋ iri mana ba, danga kayney ga ti gunda ra.
