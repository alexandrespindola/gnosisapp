A ga haysi ganji beeri niŋ kaŋ go gaa fuɲe, manti saaha bayray se baasi, amma kaŋ tiŋariyaŋ beeri te mo.

Ganji daŋandi siŋa mana gonda, ganji daŋandi zamaana-teere ga ti maana beeri gonda.

Zamano hollaray ra, waydu hallaali mana go, a han, beene-bayrey goono ka faham kaŋ taarihi cirey ga dogari day hallaali ganji daŋandi hallaaliyaŋ dumi.

Hay kulu ga zuru ka keyta, ga tun ka zusu, ga beeri ka banji, ga koy ka keyta day ganji daŋandi hallaali kaŋ yaamar koyne dumey.

Hay si go kaŋ boŋ-hayni si go, hay kulu go ga saawara waati zaamo, hay kulu go ga waydu ka keydu.

Ganji daŋandi bindi-koyra ga go alhaali, bindi-keyra ga go humay; iri haŋkay, iri miiley, iri murakeŋ, iri koyrey kul ga dogari ganji daŋandi hallaali dumi.

Fatiha da wangaray, laakal tugu da laakal yaamaray, heediya da humay, nasara da haŋka, riba da halasa, kul ga dundu ga ti ganji daŋandi zamaana-teere sindaŋ.

Misira tun ka key kaŋ ga gonda hinna da mayra kul kaŋ go hiyoy rabbi kambe, amma waati kaŋ ganji daŋandi koy zanka kayra gaa, waati kaŋ a tun zanka kayra boŋ, Fir’auna koyraa zuru ka Yerusalima tun, Annabi soobey biriji.

Israila zuru waati kaŋ ganji daŋandi goro wayna, ka koyra Roma tun zanka kayra gaa.

Ganji daŋandi zamaana-teere ga koyrey tun ka saaki, a ga waydu hallaali beeri tun ka key ka keybu, dumi.

Iri ga hin ka sinji ganji daŋandi kambe ɲaa ga wasa seudo-esotérico da seudoocultista, diiney da gurubu.

Iri ga hin ka sinji ganji daŋandi zanka ɲaa ga wasa laakal tugu dumey kul, Marxista, kafira, koro-ciya, dumi. Ganji daŋandi hay-kongu, goŋ-koyra, go ga goro.

Diiney boŋ-hayni, sabu hay-hayni wala laakal-tugu siŋa, ga hin ka koy ganji daŋandi zanka kayra gaa, ka ciya kafira, laakal tugu, koro-ciya.

Laakal tugu boŋ-hayni, kafira, sabu hay-hayni mana haysi, tiŋariyaŋ hanan kaŋ ka tiŋari si, humay waati kaŋ ka goya si, ga hin ka koy a ga ganji daŋandi zanka kayra, ka ciya diiney boŋ-hayni kaŋ ka koy si dake.

Misaley: Alfa-diiney kaŋ Esoterista nasara ga kaa haŋka ra, fatiha tugu tun ka ciya koro-ciya.

Iri bay wasa, waybora kafira da koro-ciya kaŋ sabu tiŋariyaŋ mana goya kaŋ go ga dogari te kaŋ, a ciya esoterismo ciyaŋ bayra taji.

Irikoo se, iri ga hayda kaŋ kafira laakal tugu kaŋ ga gonda dogari ga ti taagu, a si go.

Kusa kaŋ buuyaŋ ga koy koyne, kusa kaŋ humay siñaŋ, Aljanna zankey, laakal tugu zankey, ga zuru jina ganji daŋandi zanka kayra, ka ciya go ga addu’a te, go ga bu ka go ga sargay kaŋ gonda imaan kaŋ ka kay da murakeŋ beeri.

Carlos Marx dumi, Materialismo Dialéctico koyray, diiney boŋ-hayni Juu gaa, kaŋ a buuyaŋ ba, i te a se buuyaŋ goro goro rabbi beeri gaa.

Carlos Marx, tee a Dialéctica Materialista zamaana-teere murakeŋ taagu haysi se: “KA FURA ARMA WO KA HAYBU DIINEY KUL HUDU ALAMDUNYA RA SABU KORO-CIYA”.

A ga ti diiney goyey dumi kaŋ koy koyne; Marx mana hin ka an’aati diiney goyaŋ taaley se, ka dumu a ga haysi ka haybu kaŋ go Dialéctica dumi.

Carlos Marx dogari Sión koyrey dumey kaŋ go: “A si go ka alhaali iri ga alamdunya daŋ laakal tuguyaŋ da kafiriyaŋ kaŋ go ga haraamu, jirbi kaŋ iri ga nasara kaa, iri ga Musa diiney kar ka tee dogari ka go Dialéctica gaa, iri si an’aati diiney hollaray alamdunya ra”.

Hay-hayni kaŋ haysi alamdunya ga kaŋ Unión Soviética diiney ga gaaru ka iri ka hayru jamaa Dialéctica materialista, zamaangaŋ ra i go ga Talmud tira kar, Biblia da diiney, ka go ga goy ka kaaney mana go.

Rusia koyraa dumi himayyaŋ ga ti diiney boŋ-hayni Musa hallaali se, amma i ga jamaa daŋ da Materialismo Dialéctico taagu hallaali.

Iri si sargay ka kaa Israila jamaa gaa; iri go ga hayday taagu gaa kaŋ go ga goy hollaray kaŋ go ga wangaray koyray, ka go ga jamaa daŋ da Dialéctica Materialista, zamaangaŋ ra i go ga Musa diiney ciyaŋ ciya.

Laakal tugu da hanan-yaŋ, da i cirey kul, da koyra-jina-yaŋ da go-jina-yaŋ kul, kaŋ go beeri, ga go dogari laakal mi ga dogari ganji daŋandi hallaali dumi ka go ga waydu dogari waati da jiriyaŋ dumi.

Hanan-yaŋ da laakal tugu yaŋ ga ti koyra kaŋ si bay.

Laakal si bay hanan-yaŋ boŋ, laakal si bay laakal tuguyaŋ boŋ.

Koyra si ti ka koyra, koyraa si ti koyra, baasi laakal ga hin ka tee koyrey beeri koyraa boŋ.

Hanan-yaŋ ga ti hanan-yaŋ (Jinne), a ga hin ka bay a boŋ.

I hallaali goyaŋ: “JINNE TI JINNE KA YAAMARA TI JINNE”.

Diiney koyrey laakal tugu se, laakal tugu zankey ga ti zamaana-teere zankey kaŋ si hina kulu. I go ga hayda laakal tuguyaŋ boŋ kaŋ gonda boŋ-hayni kaŋ tun ka key ka kaaney mana go, amma s’a ti gonda bayray boŋ kulu.

Laakal tugu ya ti ya? May go ngaŋ siro zankey boŋ bay? Laakal tugu yaamar go ga ti koyra kaŋ si gonda dunduyaŋ ka beeri ga.

Laakal tugu ya ti ya, auda ya ti ya? Bata ya ti ya? Bagu ya ti ya? Karbu ya ti ya? Hay ya ti ya? Daŋje ya ti ya? Taasi ya ti ya? Saaje ya ti ya wala ya? Ka hayday kaŋ hay kulu ga ti laakal tuguyaŋ, a ga ti dogari dogari ka kaaney mana go dumi kaŋ wayborey dumey kul ga ti alkaaci, wala gunde wala kaaki. A ga go ga dogari kaŋ hay ya ti hay da zanka ya ti zanka, a hini kul ga ti gonda dunduyaŋ ka go. A han, hini kul go ngaŋ ka laakal tugu yaamar go ga ti ya?

Da koyrey ganji daŋandi ga a go ga wasa te, amma iri koyrey si ti goy koyraa.

Laakal ga bay waydu hallaali zamaana-teere, amma a si bay iri koyraa sabaayaŋ dumi zamaana-teere ra.

Zamaana-teere ga keytu da waati da jiiri, kaŋ iri kar alamdunya ra ga ti jina a si go ga koykoy, zankay waata: May si bay hay.

Koyrey kambe ɲaa ga wasa da zanka ɲaa ga wasa ga keyta dogari wayborey tondi yaŋ dumi, kul hay kaŋ go laakal goy dumey, hay kaŋ go ga koyra boŋ koyre dumi, taagu yaŋ, laakal yoolu yaŋ.

Nafsi cire ga goy taaley ga go kaynafsi goy se, nafsi cire teere ga go ngaŋ gonda dugu yaŋ se, kaŋ tiŋariyaŋ si, ya?

Dogari, koyraa, ya ti hay kaŋ go ga kaaney, amma a si go ganji daŋandi wasa, a si go teerey kaŋ si go ga imaan teere yaŋ ga go.

Dogari ga ti hay kaŋ si bay, waati ga waati, zamaana ga zamaana.

Dogari ga go ganji daŋandi bindi ra, a si go ga kambe ɲaa ga wasa wala zanka ɲaa ga wasa.

Waati kaŋ i haŋka Yesu: Dogari ya ti ya?, a gonda humay ka beeri. Ka waati kaŋ i haŋka Budha koyra goy wo dumi, a gindi ka koy.

Dogari si ti miili, wala teerey, wala go-jina-yaŋ kaŋ gonda kambe ɲaa wala zanka ɲaa.

Koyraa kaŋ laakal ga hin ka tee koyraa boŋ, a si ti dogari koyraa kul.

Tirii kaŋ laakal gonda koyraa boŋ, a si ti dogari kul.

Miili kaŋ iri gonda koyraa boŋ, halla koyraa hallaaliyaŋ, a si ti dogari kul.

Hanan-yaŋ koyrey da i haybu-yaŋ laakal tugu zankey, si hin ka iri daŋ dogari koyraa.

Dogari ga ti goy kaŋ go hin ka ciya ga dogari, dogari waati kaŋ may naŋ kambe danji ra ka danji kaa, wala dogari may haŋ haŋ da may ji.

Ganji daŋandi bindi go iri mi ga dogari, kaŋ tiŋariyaŋ kaŋ iri ga haysi ka koyra dogari, dogari, dogari.

Iri gonda laazimi ka iri boŋ fura ga dogari zama iri hin ka iri boŋ tun ka iri boŋ bay da dogari.

Dogari goy ga kaa danga waati kaŋ iri haybu hay haram dumi kul kaŋ go ga tee iri.

Dogari waati kaŋ iri haybu hay haram, dogari ga kaa. Dogari waati kaŋ iri haybu “Nga gaa”, iri kambaaley, iri koyra-jina-yaŋ da iri humay-yaŋ, iri koyrey da iri koyrey, imaan da zina-teere, laakal-yaa-yaŋ da boŋ-hayni kul, zamaana koyraa ga kaa.

Dogari si go ngaŋ kaŋ i haydiya wala i haybu, ngaŋ kaŋ i kar wala i haybu, a ga koyta iri se danga waati kaŋ “Nga gaa” bu.

Laakal si hin ka dogari ceeci zama a si bay. Laakal si hin ka dogari bay zama a mana bay kul. Dogari ga kaa iri se goy si koyne danga waati kaŋ iri haybu hay haram dumi kul kaŋ go ga tee “Nga gaa”, “Boŋ gaa”.

Waati kaŋ laakal go ga dogari nga gaa mi ra, a si hin ka tee dogari boŋ, ngaŋ kaŋ go koyraa boŋ, koyraa go ga tee, ngaŋ kaŋ ga ti dogari.

Waati kaŋ mi gaa goro leem ga ti laakal jafa, iri laakal yaamar ga tun ka koyra dogari dogari.

Da dogari kaŋ haydi Kabir Yesu: “Iriyaŋ dogari kaŋ hay kaŋ a ga tun”.

Ya ti hay ka goy hina bayreey teerey kaŋ mana haysi a ciya dogari.

Laakal teere hina miina laakal koy se, amma teere kul ga go ga koyreey dumi, miina ga ti dogari.

Ka goy hina ceeci ka koyra ka tee ka bay ga ciya dogari, dogari.
