**Liberté mana, aduŋa kulu mana bay kaŋ binde.

Liberté miila boŋ, kaŋ yaŋ jin ka yeeŋe wala danga yaŋ, taali beeriyaŋ daŋ.

Sohõ nda gonga, i ga tanga daŋ, i ga farga koroŋa, i ga taali kul te, i ga koye zuba tooruyaŋ do.

Liberté sanni ga kayandi, borey kul ga baa, amma day, i mana bay sanni boŋ, yeeŋeyaŋ be sanni do.

A mana honga kaŋ borey zangu hinka mana liberté sanni dondon danga.

Liberté sanni, a mana honga kaŋ borey miila kayandi.

Borey kul gonda miila duma: borey haŋkay kambe miila kaŋ mana gonda koko kulu.

Waati kaŋ i ga liberté hã, tammahayaŋ be, danga mana boori, kambe kul sinda boori.

Ay n'a bay kaŋ Don Emmanuel Kant, Biiye Hãyan bayko, nda Goy Hãyan bayko mana hã sanni haŋ kaŋ a ga tiŋere boori.

Liberté, sanni boori, sanni boori: zunubi beeriyaŋ daŋ ziyo!

Daŋ kaŋ, liberté sanni ga kayandi jama beeriyaŋ; tondi nda boŋyaŋ, koyey nda teeko zuba koye ziyo sanni hagu.

Alama beeriyaŋ, koye beeriyaŋ, jaray kaŋ daŋ Tariha, waati kul kaŋ liberté miila te.

Biiyan, batama kulu saawara banda, tammahayaŋ be borey kul banda.

May ga dooru?, May du liberté hagu?, May kaŋ ka dooru?, wayo, wayo, wayo!

Zanka ga liberté nange; a mana honga kaŋ waati beeriyaŋ, gonda buru, kaari, nda gora, a ga ba ka zuru baba windo ga liberté hã.

A mana boori kaŋ zanka kaŋ gonda kul windo ga ba ka zuru, ka feeri, ka koyi windo ga liberté sanni hã. A kaaji kaŋ a ga du hay kul windo booriyaŋ, a ga ba ka halaci hay kaŋ gonda, ka koyi aduŋa nda taali hã.

Kaŋ bine halaci, kaŋ a sinda gonda alheri, kaŋ ŋwaara, ga ba ka feeri windo, gonde nda cawku ga alheri beeriyaŋ hã, a ga boori; amma day kaŋ ize booriyaŋ, mama ize, ga hã zuru, feeri, a mana boori ndaaji; amma day a ga tiŋere; liberté sanni ga kayandi, ga tooru, hagu mana bay ka dondon.

Kaŋ wayo ga ba liberté, a ga ba ka windo bay, a ga ba ka fure ka zuru baba windo ka fundi boori bay, a ga boori zama a gonda hakki ka tiŋe nya; amma day nya fundi ga, a ga du kaŋ a mana dooru, nda nanna a ga tanga kambeŋ ga.

Sarga gundo, a ga haray daŋ sariya beeriyaŋ, ga ba ka dooru, nda a du ka dooru a ga du kaŋ a ga tiŋe sarga a haŋkay nangu nda kayandi.

Gonga, waati kul kaŋ borey ga tanga liberté ziyo, mi beyandi taŋ kaŋ goyo.

Koye beeriyaŋ kaŋ halaci mana alheri liberté ziyo, amma day i ga tiŋe sarga koyanka nda borey koyanka.

Borey ga tanga sanni ziyo kaŋ i mana bay baa nda sanni beeri dondon ta.

Liberté tiŋere kaŋ ka duyaŋ borey kul banda. Borey kul mana honga ka duyaŋ borey banda.

Ka koyi haŋka ga ti ciine kaŋ boori kaŋ ga bangandi liberté hagu.

Borey kul mana honga ka liberté bay waati kaŋ a miila ga tiŋe a yando, a maŋa yando.

Ka bay ay yando, ay ize, ay tiŋe, a ga haray daŋ waati kaŋ a ga ba ka du liberté daŋ.

Mana honga kaŋ ka halaci sarga kambeŋ kaŋ mana bay hagu kulu yaŋ, ay miila boŋ.

Nangu haŋ ka koyanka?, Hay kaŋ ga tanga borey sarga?, Traaba haŋ?, hay kulu yaŋ ga haray daŋ ka dondon.

Wayna nda talki, gomni nda gomni taali, i kul be kasu nda dooruyaŋ baa nda i ga miila kaŋ i dooru.

Waati kaŋ miila, ciine, booriyaŋ nda taamiyaŋ kaŋ gonda yando be, maŋa yando, ay yando, ay nanguyaŋ nda gaara beeriyaŋ, ay saawara nda kayandi, ay gunde nda kayandi, ay taali miila yaŋ; a ga tiŋe kasu nda goyo.

Liberté mana, borey honga ka bay waati kaŋ i ga halaci kasu miila kambeŋ.

Waati kaŋ "ay yando" be miila be kasu; ka zuru kasu ga mana honga kaŋ borey ga halaci, ka halaci ay, ka daŋ laabu, laabu toore.

Miila dooruyaŋ, mi yaŋ banda, ay mana be, ka sinda saawara, ka sinda kayandi, ka sinda nanguyaŋ wala gaara beeriyaŋ, a ga bay gonga liberté.

Miila kul liberté mana. Miila kaŋ ka tiŋe liberté mana boori. Miila kaŋ ka tiŋe liberté boŋ, a mana gonda koko nda liberté.

Liberté hay kaŋ haray daŋ ka bay daŋ, nda goyo ga mana honga ka halaci koyanka nda miila yaŋ, ka halaci ay, ka halaci maŋa yando.

Mana alheri ka tiŋe humburi liberté, zama i ga taŋ danga sarga.

A boori ka di borey danga, ka di boori kambeŋ ga kaŋ ga tanga borey kasu nda goyo.

Ka bay borey, ka di hay kaŋ tiŋe gunde, i ga du dari liberté hagu.
