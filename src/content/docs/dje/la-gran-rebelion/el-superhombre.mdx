Anahuac Code fo kaa: "Irikoy yaŋ g'adamizey te takarda ra, kaŋ binde i n'i haw nda Irikoytara"; amma a ye ka tonton: "Adamizey kul s'i boŋ haw nda cimi".

Guna, binde, haŋ kaŋ yaŋ ga hima, adamize te no, hal a boŋ haw da cimi.

Dumi-dumi ganji-feeranta kaŋ i n'a ce adamize ga to, dumi-dumi adamize kulu si no.

To, da iri n'adamize da ganji-feeranta sandi, iri ga hin ka cimi bay kaŋ ganji-feeranta, hala ni a goy da adamize goy gogo, feeranta ra, a si goy da adamize kulu.

Hanaŋkoyyan, borey kul ga miila da halliyaŋ, i ga anniyaŋ kaŋ i adamize, i ga seeda nda woodin.

Waati kulu iri ga cimandi kaŋ adamize no binde ga koytaray teeri; hala ganji-feeranta maŋ na seeda kaŋ a si koytaray i boŋ, da a si koytaray a taalamizey, da a si hin i koyra nda i anniyaŋ, a ga hin ka namma laabu koyra.

Iri si hin ka adamize koyta kaŋ a zama tam, kaŋ a si hin a boŋ koyra, kaŋ a zama jogo laabu hammadareyaŋ se.

Waati kaŋ ni zama koytaray alamiyya ra wala si; al'adaa goy koyne, a ga bayyan kaŋ ni si too adamizetaray hala.

Ganji-feeranta jinde me ga, wayna n'adamize alwaati hangan gusam.

Bayyan, alwaati hanganey ga hin ka beeri wala i halaci gogo.

Da iri ga anniyaŋ alwaati hanganey ma beeri, a ga tilas iri ma goy da wayna ceeci kaŋ a ga adamize te.

Adamize cimi ga goy da kariyaŋ da anniyaŋ kaŋ a boŋ daŋyaŋ kaŋ go ga goro iri ra halaci.

Da adamize cimi si daŋyaŋ din halaci a ra, a ga halaci da laalaŋ; a ga zama al'adaa halaci, halaci.

Adamize kaŋ ga goy nda cimi a boŋ ga anniyaŋ kaŋ a ma hankuri farka, a ga hin ka i boŋ haw nda Irikoytara.

Bayyan, adamize wayna kaŋ i boŋ haw nda Irikoytara, a ga zama da cimi da hakki ALADUMIZE.

A si hẽ ka too ALADUMIZE. Siiba, fonda kaŋ ga dira Aladumize do, a ga koy da barka da laala yaŋ.

Hay fo ga barka waati kaŋ a ga iri nafaw, a ga laala waati kaŋ a si iri nafaw. Daŋayzey sinji ga, jina go ga goro. Barka boobo go ga goro laalakatara ra, laala boobo go ga goro barkataray ra.

Fonda kaŋ ga dira Aladumize do, a ga zama Kariya-hanji Fondatay; fonda din ga to da hay gaŋyaŋ beene da ganda.

Laala ga gaŋ, barka koyne ga gaŋ; fonda gaŋ din ga koy da barka da laala yaŋ, a ga gaŋ koyne.

Code kul, gomni kul ga hin ka iri daŋ iri dira ra Aladumize do. Gunde da goy taabo wala goy jare ga hin ka iri daŋ iri dira ra ka dira Aladumize do.

Iri tamma heyyaŋ, iri goy teereyaŋ, koyne da waybora, da i go ga hangan i miila fo ra, wala cimi fo ra ga hin ka iri gaŋ iri dira ra Aladumize do.

Aladumize ga bay barka da laala, a ga bay laala da barka; a ga yadda nda adalcitaray takuba kaŋ ga koy da barka da laala.

Aladumize kaŋ a n'a barka da laala kul halaci a ra, a zama hay kaŋ boro kul si faham, a zama haraw, a zama hankuri jilla alamiyya kaŋ ga taŋada Musa ce ra.

Daada kulu ra, sanda borey ga humburkumey i yadda kaŋ i ma alwaati hanganey yadda Aladumize se, amma Aladumize go ga dira hala alwaati hangan din daada yaŋ koyne.

Hay kaŋ borey cihiyandi tamplu kaayan da, a ga gaasa, amma Aladumize go ga dira hala borey ciyaŋ.

Aladumize ga zama haraw, a sanni ga zama raadu kaŋ ga halaci koytaray kaŋ go da barka da laala.

Aladumize ga taŋada zibi ra, amma zibi ga Aladumize hã.

Borey boobo ga Aladumize ce laala binde kaŋ a si cindi miila kaŋ yaŋ ga hẽ ra, wala sanni yaŋ kaŋ ga gaasa ra, wala borey kaayan gomni ra.

Borey ga Aladumize hã, i ga gurjina a se da laala borey zama i si faham a, zama i ga sara a, i ga guna a taalamize laakal ganji ra, miila kaŋ i cimandi barka waati kaŋ a zama laala.

Aladumize ga to da yulwa kaŋ ga kusa laala borey boŋ, wala taŋada kaŋ boro kul si faham, kaŋ a halaci binde zibi ra.

Aladumize si barka wala laala, a go ga dira hala barka da laala; amma borey ga a ce barka wala laala.

Aladumize ga taŋada waati feye zibiyaŋ beeri din ra kaŋ go beeri nda alamiyya, a halaci gogo.

Aladumize ra, Yesu Bõõ ga taŋada da zafi. Yesu wayboreyaŋ, Koytaray kaŋ Beeri Taalam ta.
