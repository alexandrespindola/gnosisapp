Iri jaman haw go ga ganda, ay ga soola Kundalini sanney koye, iri dabari hollarzey hakyika, beeri-beeri hima din woyborey se, kaŋ dumo fun borey funday beeri din gaa.

Kundalini ga takada daŋ, sanda hari beeri, a ga hima ka cini a gaa.

Funday cinari takadey ra, Kundalini ga ti cinari beene danji hollarje se, STELLA MARIS, teeku waybora, kaŋ go ga tugu tammaasin tammaasin teekoyze goy borey se.

Aztek borey do a ga ti TONANTZIN, Girk borey do a ga ti CASTA DIANA, Misraŋ do a ga ti ISIS, Iya Cebe kaŋ al'umma boro kulu mana faaru a fu gaa.

Almasihu funday mana fay ka kundalini hollarje cebe, a ga ti MARAH, wala Ram-io MARIA.

Din waazu taagandi mana bayrandi a zeeri ka ISIS hanga boro meena.

Kala sirri ra no i koyne ka iya Cebe din boro kulu ra.

A mana taale ka taŋ ka bayrandi kaŋ Irikoy Iya, REA, CIBELES, ADONÍA wala mate kaŋ iri ga mi, a ga ti iri Ser meena iri boro kulu ga ti ne sanji hinka.

Iri ga gaabu ka ne iri boro kulu gonda a Iya Cebe meena.

Iya beeri fooyaŋ no beene sanda al'umma beeri kaŋ go duniya gaa.

Kundalini ga ti dabari dogari kaŋ go ka duniya te, Brahma meena.

A funday sifa ra kaŋ beyandi boro haw hollarje gaa, KUNDALINI go ga kookoro waranka hinza nda afa gaa takarje day do kaŋ go dumaaji kuuku taŋ ra.

A go gonda bare sanda hoono boro boŋ.

Takarje din ra a gonda waranka wayboro wala YONI kaŋ ra a gonda LINGAM day.

LINGAM din ra kaŋ ga ti Brahma dabari teekoyze, KUNDALINI hoono cebe go ga kookoro.

Danjari wayboro beere sanda hoono, ga farka da sirri beeri cinari kaŋ ay koyne ka koyrandi ay tira ra: «Wura Firka».

Haŋka kaŋ hollarje din farka, a ga keya sanda may donda ga donda haw titi ka dabari keyandi.

A keyandi sanda dabari keyandi, hoono cebe mo ga funna ka zeeri alaada hollarje ga, a go sanda ay Ser meena.

Ay mana soola ka koyrandi mate ka hoono farka.

Ay ga soola ka haŋ ka gaabi ka ga bayrandi kaŋ iri ga soola ka ga bayrandi.

Funday boro boŋ mana hin ka taale taagandi.

Funday boro boŋ hin ka taale to, ka fun donda fo ka koy donda fo, ka hun a boro boŋ wala borey gaa, ka ga yaafe, amma a mana hun gandi kulu.

Fahamti ga ti donda beeri, amma a mana kulu gaabu, iri ga soola ka hun gandi.

Taale beeri kaŋ iri diiri ga soola ka cinari nda fahamti ka ganda.

Iri ga soola ka dabari kaŋ zeeri funday, kaŋ ga hin ka taale to.

Haŋka dabari din go ga cebe haw gaa, sanda iri koyne.

Halle kaŋ iri fahamtindi taale fo, iri ga soola ka koy funday beeri ra, ka tu ka cebe iri Iya Cebe ga taale din to.

Koyne kaŋ go ga soola ka hun gaandi ga ti.

Iya Cebe Kundalini gonda dabari ka taale kulu to.

Laara din si, dabari din si, goy kulu kayan taale ga fay, a ga boori ka hun gaandi.
