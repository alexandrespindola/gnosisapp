"Unu" sabaari, maa gaa cimi yaa haŋ kaŋ ga ŋanaa k'a taale; amaa taali-laakal kaŋ tawaŋyan na gonda mena kulu bindi ra.

Bone-kaani, waati kulu iri ga n'iri boŋ yaŋ gaa kaŋ iri yaŋ yaŋ, hal iri si bay kaŋ iri sinda boŋ-saaji cimi.

Bone-kaani tami-kaani yaŋ gaa, iri ga n'iri boŋ yaŋ gaa kaŋ iri gonda miila-teeku kulu da duumi iri boŋ se.

Miskin-yan iri se! A goy fooyaŋ iri yaŋ! Alahakum sinda borey baaru siŋaa dumi kulu boŋ.

Borey zambar-zambarey go mena kulu bindi ra, borey dumi-dumi, "Iri"-yaŋ wala borey kaŋ yaŋ ga tangandi care banda, kaŋ yaŋ ga haroo faada kaŋ yaŋ se, kaŋ yaŋ sinda laakal wala care tammaasa.

Da iri ga duumi, da iri tun da taali-laakal da duumi gaabu-bila, haŋ kaŋ jirbi na gonda ga to.

Ammaa ka bone teeku, bine-biyaŋ da iri boŋ ga tammaasa da iri boŋ baakasinam, iri ga n'iri boŋ yee-yee, iri ga n'iri boŋ iri ga ciya haŋ kaŋ yaŋ...

Iri ga n'iri boŋ iri gonda duumi fo waati kaŋ cimi ra iri gonda duumi dumi-dumi. ("Iri" kulu gonda a wan).

Bine-karu da bine-bi kaŋ yaŋ goy fooyaŋ mena ra to, a ga taale da koykoy; mena ra duumi dumi-dumi ga bangandi care banda, yaŋ go gonda tangandi waati kulu, yaŋ ga goy yoonandi dumi-dumi ra.

Da iri gonda boŋ-saaji cimi, da iri gonda care goy fo a teeku ra, iri ga gonda care banda filla, miila-teeku tunandi, duumi taaga, boŋ-saaji waani.

Ceberi ka gaa ga boori, amaa iri na sintin da mena da cimi te.

Iri na muna gaa mena boŋ gaabi ka bay haŋ kaŋ yaŋ ga mena yaŋ da haŋ kaŋ yaŋ ga mena yaŋ.

A ga yee ka du boŋ-saaji, ammaa da iri ga n'iri boŋ iri gonda a, a gaa ga ŋaa.

A ga bay kaŋ iri si haroo faada ka du hay kaŋ iri ga n'iri boŋ iri gonda a. Taali-laakal ga n'iri boŋ iri go boŋ-saaji goyandi ka bayri go dunnyaa ra kaŋ ga himandi.

A ga waajibi ga haroo taali-laakal banda, a ga te iri boŋ ga bay kaŋ iri yaŋ, waati kaŋ cimi ra iri yaŋ miskin-yan, haanaa-sindi-yan da goy-bi-yan.

Iri ga n'iri boŋ iri yaŋ alboro-yan waati kaŋ cimi ra iri yaŋ beeri-dumi alboro-yan kaŋ yaŋ sinda boŋ-saaji.

Mitooma-yan ga n'i boŋ Irikoy-yan, Mahama-yan, halla yaŋ, da laakal-saaji sinda i se.

Egoo-goyanda-yan ga n'i "Ego" se koyne, yaŋ si taari laakal-saaji kaŋ yaŋ go i banda.

Paranoii-yan da goy fooyaŋ kaŋ yaŋ go a banda, yaŋ si karantaabin ngaa...

A ga waajibi ka haroo taali-laakal banda iri boŋ ga kuuyandi, da iri si ga baa ka ciya bine-biyaŋ zamba-yan da bayray zamba-yan kaŋ yaŋ ga te iri boŋ goy fooyaŋ da bone-gaabu, yaŋ ga bare mena ra bare-borey kulu.

Beeri-dumi laakal-go-goo go taali-laakal ga, a ga tunandi iri boŋ iri yaŋ zamba wala gunda waati kaŋ cimi ra iri yaŋ boŋ ganji fo moho-koyra ra.

Mitooma-yan si taari goy fooyaŋ ngaa, a ga n'a boŋ Archihierofante yaŋ, da yaŋ ga ci wala, da a sinda miila gaabu-bila, "muna gaabu-bila".

Taali-laakal yaa gaabi cimi kaŋ yaŋ ga goy dunnyaa kulu ga alboro-yan ga, kaŋ yaŋ ga goyandi Alboro-yan miila teeku ra, ka tunandi iri boŋ iri yaŋ alboro-yan, iri gonda boŋ-saaji cimi, duumi, miila-teeku tunandi, halla yaŋ, halla yaŋ, halla yaŋ.

Waati kaŋ iri ga n'iri boŋ iri yaŋ fo, iri si yee ka bare iri ga ciya mena boŋ ra, iri go ŋaa ka bare ka ceeri ka gooru ka bare.

Mena kulu go goy fo gaa yaŋ ga, iri si yee ka bare a, kala iri na du borey wala "Iri"-yan kaŋ yaŋ go iri alboro ra.

A ga bay kaŋ muna gaabi dumi, iri ga n'iri boŋ borey kaŋ yaŋ go iri bine ra kaŋ yaŋ ga waajibi ka i hay gaŋ ka du ceberandi ka gaar.

Iri bayrandi, iri muna gaabi dumi, ga ceberi haŋ kaŋ yaŋ iri gonda boŋ ga gaari ka bay kaŋ iri sinda boŋ-saaji cimi.

Waati kaŋ iri si iri boŋ muna, iri ga go taali-laakal ra kaŋ iri yaŋ fo da a teeku iri jirbi ga go gaari.

A si yee ka care gonda yaabo iri dumi ga halla ceberandi ga gaar.

Ceberandi dumi kulu ga hangan duumi hay gaŋ kaŋ yaŋ go iri bande.

Si yee ka bare duumi ngaa da iri si i n'iri mena ra.

Borey kaŋ yaŋ ga n'i boŋ fo, kaŋ yaŋ ga n'i boŋ yaŋ gaa, kaŋ yaŋ si taari koykoy da goy fooyaŋ, yaŋ si baa ka i n'i boŋ borey da a teeku bare-borey kulu ga te i se ka yeeyi.

A si yee ka bare da yaŋ si duumi hay gaŋ, waati kaŋ ga n'a boŋ gonda boŋ-saaji, yaŋ ga taari kaŋ haŋ kaŋ yaŋ ga waajibi ka a hay gaŋ, a ga bay haŋ kaŋ yaŋ ga waajibi ka a hay gaŋ.

Ammaa iri si tooge borey kaŋ ga n'i boŋ fo, taali-laakal ga n'a boŋ yaŋ bay haŋ kaŋ yaŋ ga waajibi ka a hay gaŋ, a sinda i ga bay.

Iri na duumi gaŋ kaŋ "Ego" go, amaa da yaŋ ga n'i boŋ i gonda boŋ-saaji a si yee ka duumi.

Boŋ-saaji go Alahakum fo ra, bayrey fooy kaŋ yaŋ duumi a gonda.

Waani go iri hay gaŋ "Yo" da miili gaabu-bila ga.

Boŋ-saaji Alahakum go "Yo" dumi kulu ga yaŋ, a go haŋ kaŋ ga go yaŋ da haŋ kaŋ ga ciya waati kulu.

Boŋ-saaji cimi go "ciya" da sababi ka ciya da "ciya" go yaŋ.

Duumi go "Ciya" ga "Yo" ra. Borey kaŋ yaŋ ga Yo da "Ciya" ga, yaŋ si i boŋ muna kulu ga.

Waati kaŋ mena ga gana, duumi, mena kaŋ go "Yo" kaŋ iri yaŋ go bande, ceberandi ka gaar go ka gaaru.
