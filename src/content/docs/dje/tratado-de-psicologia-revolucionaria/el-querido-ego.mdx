Woy ta sama nda ganda, i boro hẽne folloŋ hinka no, a ma to kaŋ hã kaŋ a kaŋ a ce ta hayyaŋ kaŋ gaa: "AY SAMAYO, AY GANDAYO" boŋ hinna hinay gaibi nda yagayyaŋ himma hinka no.

Arzaka kaŋ i n'a maa "HINNA HANNA" wala "AY SAMAYO", "BORO FOLLOŋ", wala haŋ kaŋ a dumi, cimi nda cimi "AY BOBO" go ga tangam taray no, boŋ faham taŋ taray. Waati kaŋ AY ga ba ka dõo ne nda laahira ra, a ga boŋ faham taŋ nda AY Hanno fundi-si-kaaŋo sifa beeri.

Ir boro kulu sinda "Ay" cimo, fundi-si-kaaŋo, kaŋ mana gõo, fundi-si-kaaŋo, kaŋ s'a taale, danga din danga din danga din. Ir boro kulu sinda cimi-cimi boŋ toorey hẽne; baa kaŋ ir sinda boro boŋtoorey kaŋ ga yõo.

Hinay, baa a ga dõo buuyaŋ banda, a gonda sinna nda banandi. Hinay, AY, mana hã kaŋ boro hẽne, boŋ hẽne, boŋ toorey. Danga kaŋ ay, "AYEY" no.

Tibet Irhankoŋ ra, "AYEY" ga maa "GANJI ZIYAŋEY" wala "Alhãdey", baa i hanney ga gaabuyi wala i mana gaabuyi. To waati kaŋ ir gaŋ AY kulu danga boro fo no, ir ga hin ka faham daŋ ndaŋ kaŋ: "Boro kulu kaŋ ga dõo dũuniya ra, boro yagayyaŋ go a banda".

Laakalikulu, ir kulu banda boro yagayyaŋ go, afolloŋ ga boori, afolloŋ ga laala... Ay kulu wo, boro kulu wo ga haraɲɲa kaŋ yaŋ ga ciya beeri, a ga ba ka ciya boŋ hẽne, a ga may laakal faaro wala dondonko nda soobayzey, waati kaŋ a ga hin, waati kaŋ boro fo kaŋ go a ga sasaba.

AY yagayyaŋ koyne koyne dõocto koyne koyne ga koyre Tibet Irhankoŋ ra nda boro kaŋ gonda doole, nda boro kaŋ ga tara hanno... Ir laakal laaleri kulu takara Ay fo ra. Waati kaŋ ir gonda laakal laaleri zangu yagayyaŋ wala miliyoŋey, a ga bayyan kaŋ boro yagayyaŋ go ir ra.

Laakal dondonandiyan ra, ir ga bayyan kaŋ boro kaŋ ga waani boŋ ra, kaŋ ga boŋ hanay nda boŋ hanay, baa zama hay folloŋ i mana ga koyre hinay kaŋ i ga ba. Laakalikulu boro din ga konnaji nda buuyaŋ ga Ay yagayyaŋ dõocto koyne koyne.

Waati kaŋ boro ga ba ka boŋ bay cimi nda cimi, a ga boŋ guna nda boŋ guna kaŋ a ga ba ka bay AYEY kaŋ go boŋra ra. To ir tira kariney ra boro fo sinda dõocto koyne koyne din faham baa hõ, a gaa sabu ga boŋ guna taabõ taŋ no.

Waati kaŋ boro ga dondon boŋ guna ra, a ga bay baaba nda boŋyaŋ nda AY yagayyaŋ kaŋ ga dõo ir boŋra ra. Boro kaŋ ga tanga AY yagayyaŋ dõocto koyne koyne, boro kaŋ ga hanna AY ga gumayyaŋ, cimi sinda laakal kan bay kaŋ a ga boŋ guna. Ga saba daara, ir ga ne boro din sinda bayrandi, baa kaŋ i ga bay kaŋ i sinda bayrandi.

Cimi nda cimi ir mana hin ka boŋ bay nda boŋ guna taabõ nda boŋ fundi. Saa kulu kaŋ boro fo ga boŋ ganji danga boŋ hẽne, a ga bayyan kaŋ sasabay kulu kaŋ ga taari boŋ ra ga ciya haŋ kaŋ mana hin.
