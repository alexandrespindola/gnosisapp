Mate boriyo nda ganda, i jinay kaŋ yaŋ go ga tee zama halla fo, a mana ga hay kulu kaŋ yaŋ gonda naamu: "AY BOORI, AY GANDA" yaŋ ga ti EGO zulumante nda takama-takama beere din jine cindi hinka.

I n'kaŋ yaŋ ci "AY HIRRI" wala "AY BOORI", "ALTER EGO" wala haŋ kaŋ yaŋ hima danga din, a ga ti dabari nda "AY BONYA", alhaalaliyaŋ dumi.

Waati kaŋ AY ga ba ka naŋ yaŋ gaa nda laahira gaa, a ga Alhaalaliyaŋ nda miila bannyaŋ beeri din kaŋ yaŋ ga ti AY Hirri kaŋ yaŋ si bu...

A kaŋ fo kulu si gonda "Ay" cimi, kaŋ yaŋ si canji, kaŋ yaŋ si bu, kaŋ yaŋ si faham, i ma saamo nda yaŋ...

A kaŋ fo kulu si gonda cimi nda cimi Halka fo, a kaŋ yaŋ ga tee kaŋ yaŋ go ga tee fo, a kaŋ yaŋ ga laakal fo.

Ego, baa a naŋ baa danga kabri banda, amma a gonda a sintina nda a bananta.

Ego, AY, a si goy fo wala goy kaŋ yaŋ tee fo. Alama, AY ga ti "AYOY".

Tibet Sahara gaa, i n'kaŋ yaŋ ci "AYOY" a ga ti "TAKAMA KA ZAA", wala "CINDI" i ma ti kaŋ yaŋ boori wala kaŋ yaŋ si boori.

To, ma miila AY kulu danga adam fo, a ga tee kaŋ yaŋ ga goy: "Adam kaŋ yaŋ go ga goy nda duniya gaa, adam beere yaŋ go ga goy".

A si zama, adam beere yaŋ ga goy nda a kaŋ fo kulu, a kaŋ yaŋ boori, a kaŋ yaŋ si boori...

AY kaŋ fo kulu, adam kaŋ fo kulu ga naŋ yaŋ ga tee zama a ma too, a ma tee koyne, a ma muru nda takama-takama kaŋ yaŋ tee zama, waati kulu kaŋ yaŋ a too, waati kaŋ yaŋ fo canji...

Miila dumi AY beere din kaŋ yaŋ go ga goy nda Tibet Sahara gaa, a ga ti cimi bayyaŋ, a ga ti ceeci kaŋ yaŋ go ga tee...

A taali kaŋ yaŋ go nda laakal fo kulu, a kaŋ yaŋ tee nda AY fo. A kaŋ yaŋ gonda taali beere yaŋ, a ga tee kaŋ yaŋ go ga goy nda gunda beere yaŋ.

Goy kaŋ yaŋ gonda laakal fo gaa, a ga tee kaŋ yaŋ go nda ceeci kaŋ yaŋ ga tee kaŋ yaŋ ga hara, egolatras nda mitomanos a kaŋ yaŋ zama taali fo kulu si naŋ a ma canji EGO.

A si zama, a gunda beere yaŋ din ga daaru koyne miila dumi "AYOY".

Waati kaŋ fo ga ba ka bay a boŋ, a ga ba a ma naŋ yaŋ ga tee nda ceeci AY kaŋ yaŋ go ga goy nda goy kaŋ yaŋ tee.

To, a miila dumi AY beere din kaŋ yaŋ go ga goy, a kaŋ yaŋ yaŋ si faham koyne, zama a kaŋ yaŋ go ga goy nda goy kaŋ yaŋ tee ga.

Waati kaŋ fo go ga goy nda goy kaŋ yaŋ tee, a ga bay a boŋ nda adam beere, AY kaŋ yaŋ go ga goy nda goy kaŋ yaŋ tee.

A kaŋ yaŋ ga daaru AY beere din, a kaŋ yaŋ ga naŋ yaŋ ga tee AY HIRRI, a si zama, a kaŋ yaŋ go ga tee. Ay go ga ci soho din nda Socrático goyo gaa a ga tee kaŋ yaŋ go ga ci a si bay amma i si bay kaŋ i si bay.

Cimi a kaŋ yaŋ si baaya a boŋ, a ga tee nda ceeci kaŋ yaŋ go ga goy.

Hal fo go ga miila kaŋ yaŋ fo, a ga tee kaŋ canji kulu a ga tee kaŋ yaŋ si hara.
