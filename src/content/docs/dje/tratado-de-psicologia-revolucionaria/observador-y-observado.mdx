A bayray k'a be cere kaŋ a mana hẽ, hal a maayaŋ, daŋ kaŋ boro fo te a boŋ gum hã gum, danga boro hinay jaŋa, a ga te kaŋ a naŋ taale kaŋ a gonda.

Hayyakoyyaŋ, taray kaŋ be borey se ka boro ma boŋ gum hã: Mitomaniya (Babaray Hã, ka te a boŋ Irikoy no), Egolatiriya (Talaaci kaŋ A YÉ NO BONDO; a naŋ gaakasin baraŋaŋ a feeri-egoyaŋ), Paranoiya (Baakasin, boŋ teebu, fola hal a te, a te a boŋ mana halliyaŋ, hake boŋ beeri, boro kaŋ mana hin ka boro taa hã).

Waati kaŋ borey naŋ talaci kaŋ a koyne a bayray kaŋ a YÉ NO BONDO, a gonda YÉ kaŋ mana taa, a ga te kaŋ a mana yõõ ka boro te taali a boŋ. Boro kaŋ te a boŋ bindo, a mana hin ka fittaa boŋ bonkooni haray kaŋ a gonda. A ga haŋ gundekoyyaŋ, bini, baakasin, suuba, hina, baafay, i ga te kaŋ i mana hin ka fittaa i se a boŋ taaley se, a ga boŋ haŋ kaŋ hayyay kaa.

Boro kaŋ naŋ YÉ HINAY TALAAJI, a ga faham kaŋ baakasin kulu, bini, taali, suuba, ga goy YÉ dumi dumi se. HINAY HÃAYKOYO ga goy gum a boŋ, a ga naŋ taale ka fittaa a gundekoyyaŋ.

To bora te gum a boŋ gundekoyyaŋ, a ga fittaa hinay: Hãakoyo nda Hãahakoyo. To fittaa mana te, a bayray kaŋ a mana koyraa jina JINARKAAY BOONDO se. Mate ka a ga hin ka a boŋ hã waati kaŋ a mana naŋ ka fittaa Hãakoyo nda Hãahakoyo?

To fittaa mana te, a bayray kaŋ a mana koyraa jina jina A BOŊ FAHAM se. A mana yõõ, waati kaŋ fittaa mana te, a ga naŋ taale kulu kaŋ be YO hinaray se. Boro kaŋ naŋ taale kulu kaŋ be YO hinaray se, a ga te kaŋ yala te a se.

Mate ka a ga hin ka yala fittaa boro kaŋ mana a boŋ hã se? Mate ka a ga hin ka a boŋ faaham boro kaŋ mana a boŋ gum hã se? Mate ka a ga hin ka a boŋ hã to a mana naŋ ka fittaa Hãakoyo nda Hãahakoyo?

Sohoŋ, boro kulu mana hin ka fittaa gum za a mana hin ka ne: "Baakasin hin no YO no kaŋ naŋ baaba dayri", "Goy hin no YO no kaŋ ga baaba gaabandi kaŋ naŋ baaba dayri", "Hina kaŋ ga gaaba sunsum ga te YO no kaŋ naŋ baaba dayri". Ka te ka a mana te borey se kaŋ mana naŋ ka fittaa Hãakoyo nda Hãahakoyo.

Boro kaŋ gonda taaley kulu danga YO bondo, i gonda taale gum kaŋ i mana hin ka fittaa boŋ, i mana hin ka fittaa gundekoyyaŋ. Boro dumi kaŋ mana hin ka fittaa, yala te i se.
