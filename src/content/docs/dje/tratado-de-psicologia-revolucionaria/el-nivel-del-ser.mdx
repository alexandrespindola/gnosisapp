Minde iri may? Iri no fo kaŋ no? Iri ga koy hoyra? Iri ga fun dumi hane? Zamaŋ se iri ga fun...

Cimiyal tugu, "Dabba Hakiilante" misiley kaŋ ciya borey, wo naŋ gaa, a mii, amma a naŋ gaa, a si bay kaŋ a si bay...

Hay kaŋ ga beeri mo, ciine kaŋ ti zaama, iri goono ga du, iri si bay iri taabi kulu bankaaray, amma iri cimandi kaŋ iri bay hay kulu...

Ma "Dabba Beerey Kusa" jisi, borey kaŋ beeri dumi hane kaŋ goyey go ga ciya, ma koy da a Sahara ganje tunga tunga ra, ma a naŋ dooni, ka kaa za dabba gundukoy ra, ma a guna hay kaŋ ga ciya...

Tirayaŋ ga ciya i boŋ se; "Borey Hakiilante" jisi, binde a ga beeri kaŋ a ga gaabi, a ga cimandi a ga ciya alboro gaabi, amma a ga ciya gaama gaama...

"Dabba Hakiilante" ga gooru zangu-zangu ra; a ga fongu a boŋ se, hay kaŋ ga gaabi, a ga cimandi kaŋ a ga hin ka fay ma gaabi da kindergaarten, kitabey kaŋ ga borey hanseyaŋ koyyaŋ, dumey sintini, dumey tawa, dumey beeri, gaabuyaŋ, baba maa-gaaburiyaŋ, etc., etc., etc.

Cimi ciine, bori maa-gaaburiyaŋ, takarda boori, maa-gaaburiyaŋ da takardi, iri bay kaŋ kɔ̃ri dumi hane ga iri koro, da za dugu iri ga ciya zambara gaama...

Za kitaabu dunnya kulu ga iri bay kaŋ iri ciya barga za hanci, danga iri ga gaabi, iri ga ciya gaama...

Ciine zangu-zangu da a alama boori, gowey, hay kaŋ ga laaley, dunnya kulu ra, galley kaŋ ga laaley, hari kuukuri, gaamari gaaba, hay kaŋ ga koro zaama, galley kaŋ ga koro, gaama gaama, etc., etc., etc., a ciya alama kaŋ iri ga iri boŋ guna; zamaŋ si no kaŋ iri ga cimandi iri kaa dumey gaaba...

Ma fongu zamaŋ ga tira, a ciya jujuyaŋ, cimi ciine, "baytiyanjey kaŋ i maa" i goono ga hanseyaŋ "Dundu Alamey" ra...

Takarda kulu kaŋ ti barga ra "Taarihi Barga" iri ga du taaciyaŋ kaŋ ga koro, gaami, gowey, etc.

Amma iri boorey, borey kaŋ i maa gaaba gaaba, i goono ga cimandi jizi kaŋ gooru ciya hay duguyaŋ, a ciya kusa kaŋ si ciya baa da i maa "Dumey Gaaba Zeeri"

Cimi ciine hay kaŋ ga baa, a ga ciya borey koyyaŋ; borey dumi hane ga ciya wain-ŋwarey, hanci borey, borey kaŋ i ga hanseyaŋ da borey kaŋ si borey hanseyaŋ; hay kulu ga ciya fundey ra...

Fanka ga ciya borey dumi kulu; hay kaŋ borey ciya, fanka ciya, dawla ciya, etc.

Fanka ga ciya borey tondi; zamaŋ si no kaŋ iri ga fanka hanseyaŋ, zamaŋ se borey si hanseyaŋ...

Borey kulu si hin ka hanseyaŋ kaŋ goyey dumi hane go; goyey dumi hane da goorey goy; da ceeci da ganje, etc., etc., etc.

Hennandi ciine, Goorey dumi hane go. Hay kaŋ iri go, kaŋ ga boori wala kaŋ si, albarka wala kurey, gaabi wala tooru, laakaley wala laaley, ga du goy kaŋ goono ga ciya...

Galley dumi hane ga ga ciya, takaahey, da taabi da gali kaŋ a ga koy...

Wain-ŋwarey ga wain-ŋwarey du, a ga koy jina-ŋwarey da wain-ŋwarey, wo bayariyaŋ...

Hay kaŋ ga kurey ga du, kaŋ ga boŋ se fondaa? Taabey dumi hane, kasuwayey, gali?

Amma borey kaŋ koro, kaŋ i jijiri da taabi, i ga haguyaŋ ka hanseyaŋ, ka i taarihi takarda kusa...

Borey gaamey! I ga haguyaŋ ka hanseyaŋ amma i si bay koyyaŋ; i si bay a koyyaŋ; i go hanseyaŋ ganje tunga tunga ra kaŋ zamaŋ si no...

Hay kaŋ goono ga ciya za hanki, goono ga ciya huna da goono ga ciya bi; goono ga ciya borey tiyaŋ hantu ka i si du koyyaŋ borey ga koyyaŋ wala ka goy...

Hay kulu goono ga ciya i fundey ra; goono ga ciya hay dumi, goono ga ciya koyyaŋ dumi, goono ga ciya koroyaŋ dumi...

Ciine zamba, danga, koroyaŋ, i ga koy da gaabi, haguyaŋ, da laaley, etc., etc., etc.

Mayra no iri koyyaŋ gaaba? Wala iri ga ciya: Mayra no iri koyyaŋ?

Za iri koyyaŋ si hanseyaŋ zaama, iri ga du iri taabi, iri takaahey, iri gali...

Hay kulu, iri taarey kulu, kaŋ goono ga ciya iri banda, iri dunnya ra, a ciya alama kaŋ iri ga koy iri dugu ra.

Za taaciyaŋ, iri ga gaabi cimiyal kaŋ "kaŋ go gaa goono ga ciya alama kaŋ go dugu ra."

Za borey ga hanseyaŋ dugu ra, da iri hanseyaŋ zaama, kaŋ go gaa, iri fundey, iri ciine ga hanseyaŋ.

Ay goono ga guna iri zamaŋ, (Zangu-zangu da bi, i ga guna), zamaŋ se, borey dumi kaŋ go ganda kaŋ si ciya i boŋ se. Gaaba ra Mexiko ra, borey kaŋ, i du maa kaŋ ga tira "PARACAIDISTAS".

I ciya borey kaŋ go colonia campestre Churubusco ra, i go iri windo gaaba ra, gaama gaama, diibi woodin se ay ga du ka i cina gaama...

Iri gaama zamaŋ si no ka ciya taaciyaŋ, amma galba si no, ga cinari koyyaŋ no...

Zaŋmeykoy ra, i ga koroma i boŋ se, i ga wain-ŋwarey, i ga koroma i boŋ se, i ga ciya borey wiirey ka i boŋ se, i goono ga fun windo zambarey ra kaŋ iri si ga baa.

Ay fongu zamaŋ dumi hane kaŋ borey dumi woodin se, borey si ga du koroma, haguyaŋ, laaley, wain-ŋwarey, koroyaŋ, kaamey ga koroma, koy gaama, i ciya borey kaŋ ga gaabi, ga i boŋ fondaa; i ga ciya borey kaŋ ga gaabi, borey kaŋ ga gaabi, borey gaa; diibi kaamey ciya hanseyaŋ gaaba gaaba zamaŋ se ga gaabi ka goy da jamana...

A ga ciya koy kaŋ a ga saawara borey woodin se, ga windo haŋko, windo kaŋ si ga baa...

Woodin se, iri ga haguyaŋ ka hanseyaŋ zaama, a ga ciya iri kulu ga faham kaŋ iri go "Iri Koyyaŋ"

Mayra no iri Koyyaŋ? Iri ga fongu a boŋ se? Zamaŋ si no iri ga koy goorey ra, ka iri si bay hay kaŋ iri go.
