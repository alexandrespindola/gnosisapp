Maan cirey kaŋ i n'ay gaay ka diibi kaŋ zayra zanka tajiŋa kulu ga, a jine-gaaba no; woodin no nga bumbum tabbatanta. Jine-gaaba hanneyyan kulu tajiŋa ra, cimi no, a zay koyne.

Ndaŋa beeri ka beeri daŋyaŋa dumi hanneyyan yaŋ gaa, amma alhakkuyaŋ wo bumbum koro. Alhakkuyaŋ ga hanna jina nda koyna, amma yaŋ bumbum koro.

Hanu nda cimi, jine-gaaba beera nda koyna bumbum, a ga yeyandi jiiri hinji-hinji, taaci nda guu hunjin ga, nga no neera ndaaniŋa. Huna ga mi, jine-gaaba beeri nda koyna duumi, i ga daŋ, dumi maama yaŋ gaa, amma gnosizim ga koyne koro, woodin si no.

Ka jine-gaaba beerayya ka koyne, hay koyne, hay barmay ka waati. Ay ga hanu, ay bumbum ziyaara, jine-gaaba hanneyyan ga duumi daŋ ga, zaama za ka haray nda bine-kayri zaama.

Ay ga faham, zaama wo, si taagandi azumi, banki, tuma-tumbuyaŋ, batali-tumbuyaŋ, dumi leeri beera yaŋ wala ofisi goyyaŋ yaŋ gaa. Zaama wo, nin kul ga kaŋ saharata beeri, a ga taagandi bine-koyne.

Ir kulu ga bay, ir gina ra, hay goo, i ga cee ay gina, ay gina bumbum. Hay-yaasin, jine-gaaba go ga kuuku, a go ga kayta, ay gina dumi ra, dumi kayri koyne. Ay gina dumi dandi, dumi hari koro yaŋ dandi, dumi kayri, a ga gaaga ga, a ga kayra. Ay bumbum ziyaara maanaa nga no. Ir si du jine-gaaba, ay gina dumi dandi hala.

Jine-gaaba ra, dini goo, buda goo, bayray goo, ir baabo kaŋ go beene ra sunko hariyaŋ goo, dumi bayhayaŋ kul kaŋ ga ir gaaga, ir bumbum tabbatari hanneyyan waati. Boro si du ay gina dumi dandi, kaŋ ga a ma dumi koro kaŋ go ir gina ra, hala.

Ir ga gaaga kaŋ a ma zam dumi hanu dandi: hasidi kaŋ kayri, a ye saharata taajiriyaŋ daaniŋa; laabasin koyne kaŋ ga bama kayri beeri. Hanu kayri koyne, kaŋ ga barari beeri taama; yoola nda nandaamuyaŋ; hari diibu koyne kaŋ ga zumba kayri beeri; danga danga danga.

Waati kaŋ hay koro kul go ga dandi, a g'ay gumbu yaŋ gaa, jine-gaaba ga duumi hanna, a ga beeri ka koyne taamu. Waati kaŋ ay gina dumi daŋ, jine-gaaba ga kayra ir gina ra.

Jine-gaaba kaŋ hanna, a ga diibi hanneyyan di ir ga, dumi diibi wo, farha diibi nda yadde cimi ga fun. Jine-gaaba goo nda hanneyyan zaama beeri nda hane-gaaba tajiŋa. Waati kaŋ ir kaŋ ga si duma nda ir bumbum, waati kaŋ ir dandi ay gina dumi, ir ga farha nda jine-gaaba hane-gaaba yaŋ.
