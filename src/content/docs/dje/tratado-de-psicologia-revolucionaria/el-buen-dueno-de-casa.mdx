Fatiɲe saba ka bɔ zama fundi halaciyaŋ hanney kaŋ yaŋ go ga, zamano tiŋasuyaŋ din ra, a gaaray gumo, amma a tilas, day si no a ga laabu daŋ.

Tuma kulu kaŋ borey ga te nga boŋ se zama hihin-haray da laakal da albarka beeri, a ga tali waani kaŋ faham da kyabu gumo, zama fundi talifiyaŋ ra, mate kaŋ iri go ga duumi, a sinda naŋ kaŋ a ga hin ka bora sanda bora hihin-haray.

Iri si ga hin ka faaru borey hihin-haray se, a hanan a ga tilas fundi ra, amma a gaaray, a gaaray ciyaŋ siji, a si gonda cimi, a si gonda gaskiyaŋ iri ra.

Dabba laakal misiyaŋ kaŋ i ga ne bora kaŋ ga bisa kaŋ si taale, amma a ga yadda nda fundi teerey kulu, a ga nga hihin daŋ zama gundayaŋ fundi da borey yaamarey boŋ, da kuma borey maamuriyoŋ yaŋ da kumay, a si hin ka yara kaŋ ga hin ka beeri a ra, kala sanda kaŋ ga tali da ndaari-zaama nda laakal.

Gaskiya borey kaŋ ga hagu zama i ma bora beeri, i ga tilas ka fun a gundo ra. A ga tali nda naane ganda taale.

Sanney ka fun zamano beerey kaŋ i ga darsu fundi beeriyaŋ borey game ra nda Hermesi.

Borey ga hagu kaŋ yara beeri a fondo ra, a gaaray kaŋ a ma nga hihin-haray fun a funa ra.

Saaya kaŋ bora hihin-haray ga fun, a si waani a fondo ra, a gonda naane kaŋ a si ga hin ka hihin-haray a fundo ra.

Fundi beereyaŋ ga hagu iri ma i laabu daŋ; iri ga tilas ka fundi le da hano kulu, iri ga tilas ka faham da woy fundi boŋ…

Tuma wo ga faaru fundi se, a gaaray sanda kaŋ go ga duumi amma iri ga tilas ka te a saaya kulu; ay ga hagu ka Fundi Jeeriyaŋ.

A gonda naane kaŋ iri fundi teerey ga misiyaŋ fundi boŋ; iri ga yadda nda zama a ga dira da gaabiri, taamiyan ga gaadi iri…

Borey ga hagu a ga dira da gaabiri, sabo zama a ga dira iri teerey boŋ, amma gaskiya gaaray, saaya kaŋ bora si jeeri a fondo ra, danga a hagu a ma koy a ga cansa.

I ga sanba da i ga hinyin hinin fundi boŋ, misiyaŋ gumo, amma zama fundi jeeriyaŋ gaaray.

Darsu wo ga koy gaabiriza, teerey danga, a gonda naane kaŋ Dabba laakal kaŋ i ga ne bora, a ga dira nda naane zaama hihin-haray-waani, toora-waani.

"Saray Beeri Koyo" si ga yadda da fundi jeeriyaŋ; a ga tilas nga talle kulu danga baaba, kurya-koyo, da dai, a ga ne nga boŋ se kaŋ a ga bora gumo, amma a ga tee teerey se dai.

Taale iri ga ne kaŋ a gonda "Saray Beeri Koyo" kaŋ go ga faaru fundi se; amma, borey wo gumo fundi ra, i si ga gumo.

Saaya kaŋ borey ga teeri ka tali nda fundi jeeriyaŋ, a ga du fundi faham-gaabiryaŋ.
