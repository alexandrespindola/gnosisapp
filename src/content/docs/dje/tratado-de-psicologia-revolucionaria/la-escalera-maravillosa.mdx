Ifo ga tiŋ ka ceeci farijanta cimi, ka fatta daŋ jina donda hã, fundaa daŋ soojijiŋ daŋ hã, zama ka dũ... Haŋ kaŋ ga tiŋ ka bay ka gũ talle-talle, iri boro kulu, a ga ti buržuwazu wala proletariyaŋ, arzaka gonda wala meediyaŋ, alhaali gonda wala miseraŋ, a go ga kaa gundekuna tamma beebe wani n'a gaa...

Hamni boro fundaa danga jaŋ hay wala danga baaro tunduŋ fundaa danga yaŋ gaa, wande hay fundaa wo danga wayboro alkaalo fundaa yaŋ gaa. Hã kaŋ iri go ga ci din maanaa, a si ciya ka taya, a si ciya ka daaba... Ka tã iri boŋkoo faŋ gaa, i ma ga aŋaŋ iri go ga duu fu kaŋ go ga hẽŋ koyraŋ dũ, a go ga te gaa kaŋ a go ga zuru nda daraja beeri...

Ka tamma wala, iri go ga kaa daraja wan taŋ gaa; daraja kaa gaa, boro kaŋ yaŋ ga beeri iri yaŋ gaa ga goy; daraja hẽŋ gaa, boro kaŋ yaŋ ga beeri iri yaŋ gaa ga goy... Gundekuna wã kayraŋ gaa, fu kayraŋ din gaa, a go ga gandi kaŋ iri ga duu fundaa beeri kulu yaŋ gaa... Boro kulu dumi nda boro, i si ciya ka din taya...

Ka tamma wala, iri si ga salaŋ wicibi wala wicikoyo, wala iri si ga salaŋ jiiri. Boro kuyan goy, boro foofo goy, zama boro foofo goy kaŋ yaŋ go ga kaa ka buu, nda zuruŋ foofo goy... Jiiri nda waati haali; hay kaŋ ma hay, ma beeri, ma hẽŋ, ma zaba, ma zayyaŋ, ma wayri, ka buu, a go ga ganda fundaa kayraŋ dũ...

Fu kayraŋ din gaa, danga fu kayraŋ gaa waati si goy. Daraja fu kayraŋ din gaa, iri ga duu fundaa beeri kulu yaŋ gaa... Boro himma kaŋ goy din si nafa hay; i go ga dii kaŋ waati dumi, hay ga ga beeri; a go ga goy din ma iri kaakoyo nda iri dã kaakoyo, sabaŋ, hay kaŋ yaŋ goy, a go ga koy ka din kaŋ a goy...

Fundaa ga ti hay kaŋ goy nda din ma daraja kayraŋ, iri go ga kaa daraja wan taŋ gaa amma iri ga duu daraja beeri gaa... Fu kayraŋ kaŋ iri go ga salaŋ wã kaŋ go ga ganda fundaa beeri kulu, a go ga gandi kaŋ a si hay goy nda waati zuraŋ... Fundaa hẽŋ go go kaŋ a go ga baa iri zugu naŋ ka naŋ...

A si goy fundaa kayraŋ hẽŋ gaa, zama a go goo nda sohuŋ nda waati daŋ, iri boro beebe, daraja kayraŋ dũ... A go kaŋ boro kulu ga duu, kaŋ fu hẽŋ hẽŋ wã kayraŋ nda daraja kayraŋ din go ga duu waati nda waati iri fundaa dũ nda i go ga te kuruza...

Fundajiŋ ga hẽŋ nda a go ga filla fundaa kayraŋ dũ. A ga hay nda a go ga buu waati zuraŋ dũ; a go ga buu; buuboro fundajiŋ ga himaa si hay; a si ti fundaa... Fundaa beeri, fundaa namma, a si waati, a si hay goy nda fundaa kayraŋ dũ; a go iri boro beebe gaa, sohuŋ daraja kayraŋ dũ...

A ga ciya daŋdanga nda ceeci fundaa beebe yaŋ gaa... A si kaan ka iri haali haŋ kaŋ: Tiitilu, daraja, hẽŋkoŋ nda hẽŋ haali, fundaa kayraŋ gaa, a si ga ganda fundaa talle-talle, ka fundaa taale beeri, ka koy daraja beeri gaa fundaa beeri kulu yaŋ gaa...
