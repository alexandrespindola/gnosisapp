Goy kulu, i bõy kaŋ cine, zama cindi taagandi sanni din banda, zama fundu kaŋ naana kulu bo, da fundu kaŋ duumi te, da fundu kaŋ gõndõ...

Haŋ kaŋ ga hẽ, a ga hima halla iri ma faham da takama kayra kaŋ iri kulu, yaadin burguwan no wala prolateriyan, da arzaka goy wala baaru tagu, da alkaani wala tallahiɲe, a go ga tee zama nga ma gonda Nivel de Ser...

Borracho Nivel de Ser, naŋ taabo fooyaŋ Nivel de Ser, da waybora Nivel de Ser, naŋ fooyaŋ waati da wayboro din. Hã kaŋ iri ga ci, a ga hima kaŋ i si hin ka cindi a ga...

Waati kaŋ iri koy zama iri ma du iri babu hã, iri si hay kulu bana da iri ma miiji jinga kaŋ ga titi ka koy beene, da wãji da jeeney gumo...

Da takama, iri ga du iri ma tee ize gundey din ra, ize gundey kaŋ go ganda, i ga tee kaŋ iri banda nda, ize gundey kaŋ go beene, i ga du kaŋ iri beene nda...

Ize wãji kaŋ zaama, jinga kaŋ zaama, a go kayra kaŋ iri ga du kaŋ iri ma du Nivel de Ser kulu... boro kulu naŋ taabo da hã kul si hin ka cindi a ga. ..

Da takama, iri si ga ci yɛ kaŋ i kay, wala i kay, wala a ci waati. I go kayra wãji, da zankey, da borey kaŋ i ga bu, da zankey kaŋ i zaakoy. ..

Waati da jiiri hã; kaŋ i zaakoy, kaŋ i way, kaŋ i duumi te, kaŋ i taamu, kaŋ i zaakoy, kaŋ i booba, kaŋ i bu, a go ta Vertical hã...

"Jinga kaŋ zaama", Vertical hã ra waati hã si tee. Gundey din ra, iri ga du "Nivel de Ser" yaŋ...

Borey saabu din si ga tee hay kulu; i ga saabu kaŋ waati hã ra hay kaŋ go ga tee. I ga tee kaŋ iri maama da iri maama baa ga tee; hay kaŋ go kayra kaŋ a ci na iri...

"Nivel de Ser" a go kayra kaŋ a go Vertical; iri ga gonda ize gunda, amma iri ga zaama ka iri ma koy ize gunda. ..

"Jinga kaŋ zaama" kaŋ iri ga ci, kaŋ a go go, "Nivel de Ser", da takama, si hay kulu gonda da waati kaŋ go banda...

"Nivel de Ser" kaŋ titi, a go iri beene waati kulu...

A si ta koyro duma kaŋ go banda, amma do da hõo; iri fundey ra, Vertical hã ra...

A go wãji da boro kulu ga hin ka faham, kaŋ jeeney hã, Horizontal da Vertical ga tee waati kulu iri fundey ra, da i go da. ..

Persona ga way, kaŋ a titi Horizontal hã ra. A zaakoy da a ga bu waati hã ra; a go ga cindi hay; si hiiri kulu ga tee zama persona kaŋ bu din; a si ga tee Ser...

Nivel de Ser hã; Ser ya no, a si waati hã gaa, si hay kulu gonda da Horizontal hã; a go iri fundey ra. Hõo, Vertical hã ra...

A go tee zama iri ma ceeci Ser kaŋ go ga tee fun iri fundey banda...

A si ga tee ga tee zama iri ma tee zama iri ma tee: Titre, ascenseur, etc., dunya kaŋ go banda, si ga tee zama iri ma tee kaŋ iri ma duumi te, kaŋ iri ma titi goy, kaŋ iri ma koy ize gunda kaŋ go beene "Nivel de Ser" hã.
