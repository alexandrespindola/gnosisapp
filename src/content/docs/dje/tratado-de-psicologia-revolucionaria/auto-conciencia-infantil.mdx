Iri bay kaŋ a goy da hikma beeri gaa, i ciyaŋ kaŋ iri gonda zangu jee bangandi cindi jee (97%) kaŋ ga tammahayaŋ da zangu hinandi hin (3%) kaŋ ga miila.

Irikoy hallaŋ hallaŋ, iri ga ciyaŋ kaŋ zangu jee bangandi cindi jee (97%) kaŋ ga fundi kaŋ iri gonda iri banda, a n'i sandiyaŋ, i n'i daŋ, i n'i zama Yo kulu dumi kaŋ i gumayyaŋ ya "Ay Koyne".

Baayaney ga, fundi wala miila kaŋ i zama Yo kulu game ra, i ga koytaray a borey nonga.

Yo kulu kaŋ i zaarandi, a ga fundi danga fo sanba, fundi wala miila hirriyaŋ wala tilastaŋ, a si han kaŋ a zaarandi Yo kulu.

Yo kaŋ i zaarandi beeri, miila-koyne beeri. Yo kaŋ i zaarandi kaydi, fundi kaŋ sobay kaydi.

Miila sobay koyne han kaŋ ni Yo kaa, ni ga bu ni boŋ ra, doŋ hala.

Gaabiyanke, hala fundi wala miila gonda Yo kaŋ iri gonda iri banda ra, a go ga hanta, a go tammahayaŋ ra.

A ga tilas ga tammahayaŋ bare koyne, a ga han ni Yo bu.

A si han kaŋ ni sobay zumbu han ni bu ni boŋ ra. Borey kaŋ ga hina kaŋ i sobay zumbu zumbu kaŋ i bu, i si gonda feeri kaŋ hagey go ga ciyaŋ, i ga koy ga tilas hala kambe.

Haykey kaŋ i hay kuray, i go ga yaama, i go gonda miila koyne; i sobay zumbu gumayyaŋ.

Hay-kura birni banda ra, i fundi bare, a ga hay-kura di.

I si hina kaŋ iri ga ciyaŋ kaŋ zangu kulu kaŋ ga fundi wala miila bare hay-kura ra, amma i gonda zangu hinandi hin kaŋ a si zama Yo kulu game ra.

Amma, fundi danga fo bare kaŋ i bare hay-kura ra, a ga miila koyne gumayyaŋ, hankuraŋ, dumi.

Borey-beeri go ga hay-kura daaru, i go ga mi kaŋ hay-kura go ga hanta, amma i go ga halliyaŋ.

Hay-kura go ga borey-beeri di hala i go; hantante, zallaŋ, halbiyaŋ, dumi.

Hay-kura Yo go ga koy da koy, i go ga daaru ganda, i go ga hina kaŋ i gonda birni fo banda ra, amma sahay kaŋ hay-kura si zaama borey, Yo ceeci kulu kaŋ i gonda birni fo banda ra, a ga beeri kaŋ a si han.

Saha fo haykey go ga humburuyaŋ han i di Yo kaŋ go ga daaru ganda da koyne, i go ga he, i go ga haŋ, amma borey-beeri si haŋ a hagey, i go ga mi kaŋ hay ga baani wala a gonda haray wala a gonda maa; a ga borey-beeri hanta.

Han zaama borey go ga zaama, Yo kaŋ fun jina go ga koy birni fo banda ra, i go ga koy koyne birni fo banda ra.

Han Yo kulu bare, iri ga bare duniya ra zunubu boŋ kaŋ a go gonda iri borey; doŋ, iri ga koy zamba zambay kaŋ, binda kulu iri go ga hanta, binda kulu iri go ga zallaŋ.

Han iri bu, hay hin go ga koy kaburu ra: 1) Birni-koyan. 2) Fundi rahay kura. 3) Zaama borey.

Fundi rahay, zamba zambay ga zaara koyne koyne kaburu jine har birni-koyan koyne koyne ga zaara.

Zaama borey go tammahayaŋ wala kayni-miila, a ga koy da koy kaburu han a hina, a ga farha han borey-dumi go ga hu daŋ a gaa, a ga borey-marey daaru i go ga zaara koyne koyne hala i bare laabu-zama koyne.

Hagey kaŋ go ga koy kaburu banda ga EGO, YO cendu, ay koyne, zunubey gumayyaŋ kaŋ i game ra i gonda fundi kaŋ go zama, miila kaŋ a koytaray, a ga bare.

A ga halliyaŋ kaŋ han hay-kura borey go ga zaama, i Yo bare koyne.
