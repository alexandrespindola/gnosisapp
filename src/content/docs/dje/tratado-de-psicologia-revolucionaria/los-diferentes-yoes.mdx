Boroŋkoŋ harijirbiŋkoŋ kaŋ iŋa daŋ alboroŋ, mana kodo ga yaffe kodo goy. Cikasoŋ, hay ciyaŋ koyne kaŋ mana takayya alboroŋ ra, woodin n'a ciyaŋ taabu da laakaley.

Bandu ga ti kodo kaŋ iŋa ka to, a go ga te goy boŋ tammaase dumi, fooyaŋ kaŋ a ma dukurwa. Amma alboroŋ boroŋ gunda, a mana ciya kodo ga yaffe kodo goy sam. Haŋ kaŋ ga baray kul, baasi nda takaddama kaŋ i go ga ne seedo-hakkillaŋ da seedo-koyra-tuŋ koyne, ga ti cikasoŋ koyne, a mana ciyaŋ te koyne, a goy se albora kul gunda.

Tiŋ ga gonda, warjani hiyarey ga te goy ga yaffe dumi ga te goy boroŋ gunda. Alboroŋ boroŋ boriyaŋ gunda, a ga ti taŋkiyyaŋ kaŋ iŋa tun "ay-yaŋ".

Himayyaŋ kaŋ ga faham kaŋ ga kaba kaŋ zaaman ga gonda, i ga "ay-yaŋ" bato, iŋa daŋ hinna-dumo, iŋa daŋ a te hinna-dumo gaaboŋ, iŋa daŋ a te "ALTER EGO", "AY GAAWOŊ", "AY HINNA" yaŋ, etc., etc., etc. I naŋ ga bay "Goy-bay-boroŋ" kaŋ i go ga gonda, kaŋ "AY GAAWOŊ" wala "AY KULU" ga ti ŋga-tun-jare, a go daŋ boŋ hayyaŋ taŋkiyyaŋ tun...

Alboroŋ mana gonda "AY DUUMAY" mana tun "AY-YAŊ" dumi hiyarey kaŋ ga baray da kaŋ mana ti cimi. Boroŋ gaabu gaabuŋ, mana ti boroŋ kaŋ iŋa daŋ alboroŋ ga man daŋ baaru, a mana ti ciiri-safaŋ kaŋ a gonda mooroŋ gunda, mooroŋ boŋ harijekoŋ, i go ga koy ga maraŋ kaŋ i go ga daŋ goy kaŋ iŋa tun koyne...

Boroŋkoŋ harijirbiŋkoŋ gaawoŋ, boroŋ boŋ jare, a go ga canji kaŋ iŋa tun... Hakkillaŋ dumo kaŋ boroŋ ga ciya Luis, a go ga ciya Luis, a go ga ne a go ga ciya fohõ koŋkõ... Boroŋ kaŋ i go ga ciya Luis, a ga gonda "Ay-yaŋ" koyne, ŋga-yaŋ koyne, kaŋ i go ga daŋ zama a nza, a go daŋ zama a boŋ koyne, ga te goy zama waato koyne, ba Luis mana boŋ boŋ jamba, "Ay" koyne a gunda - yaŋ ga daŋ a te Pepe - a go ga boŋ boŋ jamba, nda yaŋ koyne...

Boroŋ kul mana ciyaŋ koŋkõ, a ciima mana ga gonda zama war ma ga bay kulu, canjiyaŋ da baŋ-taa-yaŋ kul kaŋ i go da gonda kul... Daŋ boroŋ ga gonda "AY DUUMAY" mana ciyaŋ koŋkõ, a go ga baraŋ zama gaaboroŋ ma te koyne zama war gaaboŋ koyne...

Boroŋ kul gunda, boroŋ barey go ga fun, "Ay-yaŋ" barey, haŋ woo a ga tiŋga bay zama war gaaboŋ, boroŋ kaŋ go ga fun ga yaffa.
