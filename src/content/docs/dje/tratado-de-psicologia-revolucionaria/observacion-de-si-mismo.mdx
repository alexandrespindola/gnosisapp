Boŋŋantaŋ gundekuna beere, nga boŋ gundekuna beere, ya hanse ga baaru kaŋ yaŋ cindi-cindi tilay.

Bay kaŋ bay da hanga, wo differente. Boro boobo ga tangam hanga zunubu boŋ bay se. I ga bay kaŋ iri goro-goro tooruyaŋ gaa fu seŋ, amma woodin si halla iri go tooruyaŋ gaa.

Iri ga bay kaŋ haliyo fo ra iri go halay laaley seŋ, zama afollo gonda iri, wala iri ga laralu baafollo se wala iri go baa bine, wala iri ga fahamyaŋ seŋ, d.h.h., amma woodin si halla iri go a gaa.

Halla ni n' ga gunde-laalaŋ gonda boro fo se? Ni gunde si naŋ boro fo? Man' talhaŋ? Ni g'a ciya kaŋ ni bay boro din... Saabu ni ma hanga-hanga a se, bay sika go hanga; ma si tangam bay da hanga...

Boŋ-hanga kaŋ yaŋ ga dira teema fo gaa, ya hanse kaŋ ga boŋ tilay, waati kaŋ bay kaŋ yaŋ ga dira baa si, si danga woodin.

Cimariya bay si ga tiŋyaŋ ganay. Tiŋyaŋ kaŋ yaŋ ga dira boŋ gundekuna, kaŋ yaŋ ga dira kaŋ yaŋ go diranta iri boŋ gundekuna ra, nga ya hanse, dira.

Da boro fo go gunde-laalaŋ gonda a se danga woodin zama iri go haw gundey kaŋ boobo ga tondo iri bine ra, jinde kunda kaŋ yaŋ ga salaŋ da jinde da fiyay iri boŋ gundekuna ra, kaŋ yaŋ go salaŋ, bine-laalaŋ kaŋ yaŋ ga tun iri boŋ gundekuna ra, duuru laalaŋ kaŋ yaŋ yaŋ go koy iri zeeniyaŋ ra, d.h.h., d.h.h.

Taare, halay din ra iri g'a bay mo kaŋ iri go diranta laalaŋ tammaasa borey se kaŋ yaŋ iri go gunde-laalaŋ gonda a se.

Amma zama ka woodin kul yaŋ n' dii, tiŋyaŋ ga hima kaŋ yaŋ ga dira dambara boŋ gundekuna ra; si tiŋyaŋ baa.

Tiŋyaŋ dira kaŋ yaŋ ga tun dira kaŋ yaŋ go hanga boŋ, waati kaŋ laralaŋ da bine ga gan gan hanga boŋ.

Woodin kul ga iri faham kaŋ bay yaŋ ga dira baa da gandar kul, takadda ga dira da tiŋyaŋ kaŋ go bine kul.

Iri si koy yaŋ kul si go boŋ-hanga dira, amma hala hangu danga woodin kul gonda baaru koyo da boŋ-hanga zeeniyaŋ se kaŋ yaŋ iri go a gaa.

Hinna da hanga mo wo differente. Boro kul du ka boŋ hinna gonda boŋ se danga kaŋ yaŋ haw, amma woodin si koy yaŋ i go dira kaŋ yaŋ go hanga.

Iri ga hima ka "Boŋyaŋ" differenteyaŋ dii diranta, ma duu a gaa iri zeeniyaŋ ra, ma faham kaŋ iri kul ra zeeniyaŋ diri-diri go iri laakal porsey gaa, ma tubu iri diina, d.h.h.

Gaa no iri g' a ciya. "Amma man' talhaŋ go Boŋ hinna go diranta?" "Man' talhaŋ go a ga salaŋ?" "Man' talhaŋ go a haw?" "Man' talhaŋ go a goono ga tiŋgandiiri gunde?", "Da a dambara?", d.h.h., d.h.h.

Gaa no iri n' dii iri boŋ gundekuna ra, laralu, bine, haw, laakal, takadda, waaso boŋ, darza boŋ, sariya bareyaŋ, zeybo, yadda, laakal teema, alhaali taasa, gunda-kaarata, d.h.h., d.h.h.

Alwaati boobo iri go bora, haliyo seŋ kaŋ go cendi diranta da suubo iri go ga diya iri bine ra, jinde differenteyaŋ kaŋ yaŋ ga salaŋ zeeniyaŋ gaa, Boŋyaŋ differenteyaŋ din yaŋ ga hima ka yaŋ bare kul toora da zeenayaŋ maasin differenteyaŋ kul zama ka yaŋ tun zeenayaŋ zeeyaŋ ra, "Hanjiŋ gu Basaŋ".
