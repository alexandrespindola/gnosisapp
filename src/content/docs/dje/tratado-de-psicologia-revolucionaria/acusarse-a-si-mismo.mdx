Albarka kaŋ iri boro kulu gonda a gindaa ra, beene kaŋ a fun, beene, handariyayyaŋ... Cimi, albarka kayandi kaŋ go "LA" (La Vía Láctea, handariyay kaŋ iri go araŋ ra).

Albarka sariya kayandi kaŋ ga bisa "SOL" (Taw hẽ) banda kaŋ "FA" (Gora Planetaria) naŋ, a gonda nda nduniya kaŋ no kaŋ a jonda iri boro-borey ra. Iri baaba-izey na gaabu-jii kaŋ sariya te albarka kaŋ no kaŋ a gonda naŋ handariyayyaŋ...

Hukumandi kaŋ iri wano iri boŋey ga kaŋ iri zeeri kaŋ iri gorakoyzey ga, iri ga ye koriyaŋ do Uraniusa fundaa ra... Iri go ga funa nda nduniya kaŋ no sabu, zama, sababu sariya...

Cimi, iri gonda beeri kaŋ iri gana tiŋa, iri zey kaŋ iri faham nda, da cimi iri ga soola iri boŋey zey, iri fundey zey... Darase no fundaa kaŋ boro ga buu kaŋ a mana bay a fundaa sabu...

Boro kulu ga bay a boŋ fundaa meyriyaŋ, beeri kaŋ ga gaabu a ra kasu kaŋ go laakal da laala... Danga boro kulu gonda beeri kaŋ ga saawara a fundaa kaŋ iri tilas da ga gaawi da gaabi... A sinda tilas iri ga to laala ra, a tilas kaŋ iri zeeri zarma kosima gaubu kaŋ iri te ga hayni da laala.

A si nafa hay kulu iri ga beerandi boro zaba hantumey ga, daraja, diplomey, nzaari, waasa taagandi, al'adaaji sariya, danga din, danga din, danga din. Iri ma si ye kaŋ munaficiyaŋ da waasa taagandi kaŋ sinda fundey na, a ga te iri ga borey ga ciya kaŋ go jaŋka, guusukoy, izeyaŋ, gundekoy, kaŋ sinda dabari ga yeyandi beeri beero...

Buuyaŋ gonda meyriyaŋ beeri kaŋ go zaame zama zaame kaŋ go ga laala. Iri ma gaabi kaŋ konda nda kaŋ "Beeri KABIR Yesu Almasihu": "Ka buukoy ma ga buu buukoy". Bori beeri yaadin da fundaa kaŋ i go ga buu zama goyo kulu kaŋ te a boŋey ga, kaŋ go ga te jina-kaa zaama.

Boro go ga gaabu kaŋ ga dogmey da tabbaciyaŋ ra; borey kaŋ ga te harri kaŋ go guna yare zama ganda gaabu ra; borey kaŋ gonda haawi da baayaaji sariya; borey kaŋ go ga ciya da baaru gaabo ra, ga tagaati, nga ga saawara, saawara ga yeyandi zama i ciya din...

Iri boro si ga soola kaŋ nduniya kaŋ no "Gimnasio Psicológico" kaŋ boŋ hari siiri kaŋ iri kaa ga buu iri ga te. Da nga boro baaru faham danga laala kaŋ a go a ra, i ga gaabi...

Amma, nga borey ga faham a boŋey ga beeri zaama; i ga beerandi a al'adaaji ga, i go ga faham a boŋey ga sariya, zama, sariya-maa, kaaba, kaŋ i wano a tammaasi, danga din. Fundaa goyo danga meeci, amma a dakey danga a boŋ zama, ga bayndi go ga laala.

Borey kaŋ ga dakey fundaa a boŋ zama, danga i go ga funa zama, i mana faham tilasiriyaŋ kaŋ iri go ga hantum iri boŋey ga kaŋ iri hayndi "Zaama Hayandi". Laala na, borey ga funa danga meeciyaŋ, i mana hũ goyo fundaa ga kulu...

Hayandi ga tilas, amma borey si ga bay danga hayandi; i ga laakal beeri kaŋ i bay kaŋ i go ga laakal... Gonda nzaari sinda zama kulu. Fundaa kaŋ borey nzaariyaŋ ga ciya cimi ga te darase...
