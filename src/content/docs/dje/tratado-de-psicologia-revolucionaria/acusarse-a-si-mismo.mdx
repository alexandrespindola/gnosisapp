Gunduma kaŋ borey kulu gonda nga bindi ra, a funandi beene, beene, handariyayey...

Cimiriyaŋ kul si, gunduma cimi kaŋ taya "LA" (La Voix Lactée, galaksi kaŋ gonda iri) funandi.

Gunduma sifa harayey kaŋ taya "SOL" (Tijja) daŋ, d'a banda taya "FA" (Gundumey yerkoy) daŋ ka koy dunya wo ra ka koy iri bindi ra.

Iri kaayey na bandi kaŋ yaŋ gunduma kaŋ funandi handariyayey kar...

Iri boŋey ga teeti ga hagu iri gorokoy se, iri ga ye ka goro Urania bindi ra da nasara.

Iri goy dunya wo ra sabu kaŋ gonda dalili, sabu kaŋ gonda goy, sabu kaŋ gonda jine.

Cimiriyaŋ, iri gonda hayyaŋ beeri kaŋ iri ga di, iri ga izé d'iri ga baaru, hal iri ga faham iri boŋey gaa, iri fundey gaa.

Fundey baraafiyaŋ ga ti boro kaŋ ga bu ka na bay nga fundey dalili.

Borey kulu ga bay nga boŋ fundey maana, hay kaŋ ga zumandi nga azaba kasu ra.

Koyney, borey kulu gonda hay kaŋ ga fundey muru d'iri ga hagu nga se.

Hala si iri ma ciiti azabayaŋ ra, goŋgey ci si iri ma muru hay kaŋ ga iri zeeri laakalaaley koontey ra.

Naamu kul si iri ma taaga titrewey da albarka da takarda da arzaka da miila bareyakoy da goyey kaŋ borey bay, d.h.d.h.d.

Iri ma barmay jiiŋ kaŋ munaficiriyaŋ da borey boŋ dambarey kaŋ yaŋ ci gundekoy, borey goŋkokoy, borey kaŋ goono ga too, borey kaŋ jaabay koyne borey taji ka di hari taaga...

Buuyaŋ gonda maanay beeri, sifa da baaraafiyaŋ.

Iri ma dondore bayray beeri kaŋ fun "Gran KABIR Jesús el Cristo" gaa. "Borey kaŋ bu, i ma bu burey". Borey beeri baa kaŋ i goy, i bu hal goy kulu kaŋ iri ga teeti iri boŋey gaa d'a banda, danga ciiri kulu kaŋ ga taaga bindi ra.

Borey kaŋ i dumo dogmey da ciite ra; borey kaŋ i birjin gundey kaŋ yaŋ hanse; borey kaŋ i cimo al'aada ganji ra; borey kaŋ yaŋ may cii, borey kaŋ yaŋ naŋ ga humburandi, borey kaŋ yaŋ naŋ ga farga, waati fo borey kaŋ yaŋ bay hay bayrayey kaŋ cimiriyaŋ a ciité sabu i ceeci i se d.h.d.h.d.

Borey wo si baaba kaŋ dunya wo ti "Sportey kaŋ ga ciité" kaŋ dooley hara kaŋ iri kulu gonda bindi ra daŋ muru.

Da borey wo gonda faham goy kaŋ yaŋ i ga goro ra, i ga humburandi humburku...

Amma, borey wo ga miila a se zaama kaŋ taaga; i ga taaga goyey kaŋ yaŋ gonda sifa, i ga miila i da zaama, i gonda barka, i goy jama se, i goy borey se, i gonda laakal, i ga goyey kaŋ i gonda ga te d.h.

Fundey kaŋ gonda goy ti lekool, i ga ciiti kaŋ yaŋ, i ga di a ti ciiti bare, a cimiriyaŋ ti naamu kul si.

Borey kaŋ ga ciiti fundey gaa kaŋ yaŋ a goy, i si faham na goy kaŋ iri ga teeti iri boŋey gaa hal iri ga taaga "Ciiri gunde".

Baaraafiyaŋ, borey ga goy miila yaŋ, i si hantum ayyaŋ kuuna goy kaŋ iri ga teeti bindi ra.

Taage yaŋ ga ciiti amma borey si bay yaŋ taage yaŋ ga te, i ga azaba beeri amma i si bay yaŋ azaba yaŋ ga te.

Arzaka gonda si ciiti kul si. Fundey kaŋ yaŋ borey arzaka ga beeri, a ciiti ga ti baraafiyaŋ.
