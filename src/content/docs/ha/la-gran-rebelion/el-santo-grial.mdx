Santo Greal yana haskakawa a cikin zurfin dare na dukkan zamanai. Jaruman zamanin tsakiya a zamanin yakin Crusade sun nemi Santo Greal a cikin kasa mai tsarki amma ba su same shi ba.

Lokacin da Ibrahim Annabi ya dawo daga yakin da sarakunan Saduma da Gwamrata, an ce ya sadu da Melchizedek Geniu na Duniya. Tabbas wannan Babban Halitta ya zauna a cikin kagara da ke daidai a wurin da aka gina Urushalima a nan gaba, birnin da Annabawa ke so.

Tarihin karni ya ce, kuma wadannan abubuwa alloli da mutane sun san su, cewa Ibrahim ya yi bikin Unction Gnostic tare da raba gurasa da giya a gaban Melchizedek.

Ba a buƙatar a tabbatar da cewa Ibrahim ya ba Melchizedek zakka da farkon 'ya'yan itace kamar yadda aka rubuta a cikin Littafin Dokar.

Ibrahim ya karbi Santo Greal daga hannun Melchizedek; daga baya a cikin lokaci wannan kofin ya tafi haikalin Urushalima.

Babu shakka Sarauniyar Sheba ta yi aiki a matsayin mai shiga tsakani don wannan aikin. Ta bayyana a gaban Sulemanu Sarki tare da Santo Greal kuma bayan ta yi masa tsauraran gwaje-gwaje, sai ta mika masa wannan lu'u-lu'u mai daraja.

Babban Kabir Yesu ya sha a wannan kofin a cikin al'adar tsarkakakkiyar cin abinci ta karshe kamar yadda aka rubuta a cikin Linjila hudu.

Yusufu na Arimatiya ya cika kofin da jinin da ke fitowa daga raunukan Mai Adawata a Dutsen Kwanyar.

Lokacin da 'yan sandan Roman suka kai samame a gidan Sanata da aka ambata, ba su sami wannan lu'u-lu'u mai daraja ba.

Sanatan Roman bai boye wannan lu'u-lu'u mai daraja kawai ba, amma kuma, tare da shi, ya binne mashi Longibus wanda jami'in Roman ya ji rauni a gefen Ubangiji.

An daure Yusufu na Arimatiya a wani kurkuku mai ban tsoro saboda rashin son mika Santo Greal.

Lokacin da Sanatan da aka ambata ya fito daga kurkuku, ya tafi Rome dauke da Santo Greal.

Lokacin da ya isa Rome, Yusufu na Arimatiya ya sami tsananta Neron ga Kiristoci kuma ya tafi gefen Bahar Rum.

Wata dare a cikin mafarki mala'ika ya bayyana a gare ku ya ce: "Wannan kofin yana da iko mai girma saboda jinin Mai Ceton Duniya yana cikinsa." Yusufu na Arimatiya ya binne irin wannan kofin a cikin haikali da ke Montserrat, Catalonia, Spain, yana bin umarnin mala'ika.

A kan lokaci irin wannan kofin ya zama ganuwa tare da haikalin da wani bangare na dutsen.

Santo Greal shine jirgin Hermes, kofin Sulemanu, urn mai daraja na duk haikalin asirai.

A cikin akwatin alkawari, Santo Greal ba ya rasa a cikin siffar kofi ko gomor, a cikinsa aka ajiye manna daga hamada.

Santo Greal yana nuna YONI na mace a cikin tsari mai karfi, a cikin wannan kofin mai tsarki akwai nectar na rashin mutuwa, Soma na sufaye, babban abin sha na Alloli masu tsarki.

Red Kristus yana shan Santo Greal a lokaci mafi girma na Kristification, haka aka rubuta a cikin Linjilar Ubangiji.

Santo Greal ba ya rasa a kan bagadin haikalin. A bayyane yake, firist ɗin dole ne ya sha ruwan inabin haske a cikin Kofin Mai Tsarki.

Zai zama abin ban mamaki a zaci haikalin asirai a cikinsa ya rasa albarkar kofin dukkan zamanai.

Wannan yana tunatar da mu Ginebra Sarauniyar Jinas wacce ta zuba wa Lanzarote giya a cikin kofuna masu dadi na SUFRA da MANTI.

Alloli marasa mutuwa suna ciyar da abin sha da ke cikin Kofin Mai Tsarki; wadanda suka ƙi Albarkar Kofin, suna yin sabo ga Ruhu Mai Tsarki.

Dole ne babban mutum ya ciyar da nectar na rashin mutuwa da ke cikin kofin Allah na haikalin.

Canja wurin makamashi mai halitta yana da mahimmanci lokacin da kake son sha a cikin Vaso Santo.

Red Kristus koyaushe mai juyin juya hali, koyaushe mai tawaye, koyaushe jarumi, koyaushe mai nasara, yana toast ga Alloli da shan kofin zinariya.

Daga da kofin ku sosai kuma ku kula kada ku zubar da ko da digo daya na giya mai daraja.

Ka tuna cewa jigon mu shine THELEMA (son rai).

Daga zurfin kofin - alama ce ta gabobin jima'i na mata -, harshen wuta ya fito wanda ke haskakawa a fuskar haske ta Babban Mutum.

Allolin da ba za a iya fassarawa ba na dukkan taurari koyaushe suna shan abin sha na rashin mutuwa a cikin kofin madawwami.

Sanyi na wata yana haifar da juyawa a cikin lokaci; yana da mahimmanci a sha giya mai tsarki na haske a cikin jirgin tsarkakakku na Alkimiyya.

Purple na sarakuna masu tsarki, kambin sarauta da zinariya mai haske kawai don Red Kristus ne.

Ubangiji na tsawa da tsawa yana rike da Santo Greal a hannun damansa kuma yana shan ruwan inabin zinariya don ciyar da kansa.

Wadanda suka zubar da Vaso de Hermes a lokacin haɗuwa ta sinadarai, a zahiri sun zama halittu marasa ɗabi'a na ƙaramin duniya.

Duk abin da muka rubuta a nan yana samun cikakken takardu a cikin littafina mai suna "Auren Cikakke".
