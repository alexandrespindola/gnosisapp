Imwe Mitemo yeAnahuac yakati: "Vamwari vakasika vanhu nematanda uye mushure mekuvasika vakavabatanidza nehumwari"; zvino inowedzera kuti: "Havasi vanhu vose vanokwanisa kubatanidzwa nehumwari".

Pasina mubvunzo chinhu chekutanga chinodiwa ndechekuti munhu asikwe asati akwanisa kubatanidzwa nechokwadi.

Mhuka inofunga inonzi munhu zvisizvo haisi munhu zvachose.

Kana tikafananidza munhu nemhuka inofunga, tinogona kuzvionera pachedu kuti mhuka inofunga kunyange ikafanana nemunhu mumuviri, mupfungwa yakasiyana zvachose.

Zvinosuruvarisa kuti munhu wose anofunga zvisizvo, anofunga kuti munhu, anozvidaidza kudaro.

Isu takagara tichitenda kuti munhu ndiye mambo wezvakasikwa; mhuka inofunga kusvika nhasi haisati yarati kumbozova mambo wayo; kana asiri mambo wezvinhu zvayo pachayo, zvepfungwa, kana asingakwanisi kuzvitungamirira pakuda kwake, ndoda kutonga zvakasikwa.

Hatingambogamuchira munhu akashandurwa kuita muranda, asingakwanisi kuzvitonga uye akashandurwa kuita chidhori chesimba remhuka rehunhu.

Kana uri mambo wepasirese kana kuti hausi; mumamiriro ezvinhu ekupedzisira aya pasina mubvunzo inoratidza chokwadi chekusvika kune chimiro chemunhu.

Mukati memarasha ezvepabonde emhuka inofunga zuva rakaisa mbeu dzemunhu.

Zviri pachena kuti mbeu dzakadaro dzinogona kukura kana kurasika zvachose.

Kana tichida kuti mbeu dzakadaro dzikure, zvinova zvakakosha kushanda nesimba riri kuitwa nezuva kuti risike vanhu.

Munhu akakodzera anofanira kushanda zvakasimba nechinangwa chekubvisa kubva maari zvinhu zvisingadiwi zvatakatakura mukati medu.

Kana munhu chaiye asingabvise zvinhu zvakadaro maari, aizokundikana zvinosiririsa; aizova nhumbu yakaenda isina nguva yeAmai Vekudenga, kukundikana.

Munhu anoshanda zvakanyanya paari pachake nechinangwa chekumutsa kuziva, anogona kubatanidzwa nehumwari.

Zviri pachena kuti munhu wezuva akabatanidzwa nehumwari, anova chaizvo uye nekodzero yake pachake SUPER-MUNHU.

Hazvisi nyore kusvika kuSUPER-MUNHU. Pasina mubvunzo nzira inoenda kuSUPER-MUNHU iri kupfuura zvakanaka nezvakaipa.

Chimwe chinhu chakanaka kana chichitibatsira uye chakaipa kana chisingatibatsiri. Pakati pemazinga evhesi panovigwawo mhosva. Pane hunhu hwakawanda mumunhu akaipa uye huipi hwakawanda mumunhu akanaka.

Nzira inoenda kuSUPER-MUNHU iNzira yeEdge yeRazor; nzira iyi izere nenjodzi mukati nekunze.

Zvakaipa zvine njodzi, zvakanakawo zvine njodzi; nzira inotyisa iri kupfuura zvakanaka nezvakaipa, inotyisa zvikuru.

Chero mutemo wetsika unogona kutimisa munzira yedu yekuenda kuSUPER-MUNHU. Kunamatira kune zvakapfuura zvakadaro, kune zviitiko zvakadaro kunogona kutitadzisa munzira inoenda kuSUPER-MUNHU.

Mitemo, maitiro, zvisinei nekuchenjera kwavo, kana vakanyudzwa mukufungira zvakadaro, mukufungira zvakadaro, mune pfungwa yakadaro inogona kutitadzisa kufambira mberi kuenda kuSUPER-MUNHU.

SUPER-MUNHU anoziva zvakanaka zvezvakaipa uye zvakaipa zvezvakanaka; anobata bakatwa rekururamisira kwepasirese uye ari kupfuura zvakanaka nezvakaipa.

SUPER-MUNHU abvisa maari maitiro ese akanaka neakaipa, ave chinhu chisina munhu anonzwisisa, ijekerere, irimi remweya wepasirese wehupenyu hunopenya pachiso chaMosesi.

Muchitoro chega chega munzira imwe anacorete anopa zvipo zvake kuSUPER-MUNHU asi anopfuurira nenzira yake kupfuura zvinangwa zvakanaka zvevane anacorete.

Zvakarehwa nevanhu pasi peveranda dzvene rematemberi zvine runako rukuru, asi SUPER-MUNHU ari kupfuura matauriro matsvene evanhu.

SUPER-MUNHU ijekerere uye shoko rake ibhanan'ana rinoparadza simba rezvakanaka nezvakaipa.

SUPER-MUNHU anopenya murima, asi rima rinovenga SUPER-MUNHU.

Mapoka evanhu anotora SUPER-MUNHU seanonyengera nekuda kwechokwadi chekuti haakodzeri mukati memitemo isingakundiki, kana mukati memitsara mitsvene, kana mukati metsika dzakanaka dzevarume vakakomba.

Vanhu vanovenga SUPER-MUNHU uye vanomurovera pamuchinjikwa pakati pematsotsi nekuti havamunzwisise, nekuti vanomutongera, vachimutarisa nepahwindo repfungwa dzezvavanotenda kuti zvitsvene kunyange zviri zvakaipa.

SUPER-MUNHU akaita semheni inowira pavakaipa kana kupenya kwechimwe chinhu chisinganzwisisike chinobva chanyangarika mushure mezvo muchakavanzika.

SUPER-MUNHU haasi mutsvene kana kuti munyengeri, ari kupfuura hutsvene nehunyengeri; asi vanhu vanomutora semutsvene kana munyengeri.

SUPER-MUNHU anopenya kwechinguva pakati perima renyika ino obva anyangarika zvachose.

Mukati meSUPER-MUNHU munopenya zvinotyisa Kristu Tsvuku. Kristu anopandukira, Ishe weKumukira Kukuru.
