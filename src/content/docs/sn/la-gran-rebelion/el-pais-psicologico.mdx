Pasina mubvunzo, sezvo kune Nyika Yokunze yatiri kurarama, saizvozvowo mukati medu mune nyika yetsika.

Vanhu havamboziva guta kana dunhu ravangararama, zvinosuruvarisa zvinowanzoitika kuti havazive nzvimbo yetsika kwavanowanikwa.

Panguva yakati, munhu wose anoziva kuti ndeipi nharaunda kana koloni yaanenge ari, asi munyika yetsika zvinhu hazvina kumira saizvozvo, kazhinji vanhu havambofungiri panguva yakati nzvimbo yenyika yavo yetsika kwavanenge vapinda.

Sezvo munyika ino mune makoloni evanhu vakakodzera uye vakadzidza, saizvozvowo zvinoitika mudunhu retsika remumwe nomumwe wedu; hapana mubvunzo kuti kune makoloni akaisvonaka uye akanaka.

Sezvo munyika ino mune makoloni kana nharaunda dzine nzira dzine ngozi zvikuru, dzakazara nemakororo, saizvozvowo zvinoitika mudunhu retsika renharaunda yedu yemukati.

Zvose zvinoenderana nerudzi rwevanhu vanotiperekedza; kana tine shamwari dzakadhakwa tichaenda kubhawa, uye kana idzi dziri zvipenga, hapana mubvunzo kuti magumo edu achava mumahure.

Mukati menyika yedu yetsika mumwe nomumwe ane vaperekedzi vake, iwo MAI AKE, ivava achamuendesa kunofanira kuenda maererano netsika dzake.

Mukadzi akachena uye anoremekedzwa, mudzimai akanaka kwazvo, ane mufambiro wakanaka, anogara mumuzinda wakanaka munyika ino, nemhaka yeMAI AKE ane ruchiva anogona kuva mumahure mukati menyika yake yetsika.

Murume anoremekedzwa, akatendeseka, mugari akanaka kwazvo, angangowanikwa mubako remakororo mukati medunhu rake retsika, nemhaka yevamwe vake vakashata, MAI ehutsotsi, akanyudzwa zvikuru mukati mekusaziva.

Munhu anogara ari ega uye anotendeuka, pamweya mamongi akadaro anogara asingazvikudzi mukati mesero rake, mune imwe monastery, anogona kuva mutsika munharaunda yevapambi, vapambi, varwi, vanhu vanodhaka, chaizvoizvo nemhaka yeMAI asina kuziva zvakakwana kana kusaziva, akanyudzwa zvikuru mukati menzvimbo dzakaoma zvikuru dzemurangariro wake.

Pane chikonzero chatiudzwa kuti kune hunhu hwakawanda muvakaipa uye kuti kune uipi hwakawanda muvakanaka.

Vatsvene vakawanda vakarongeka vachiri kurarama mukati memahure etsika ehutsotsi kana mudzimba dzehure.

Izvi zvatiri kusimbisa nenzira yakasimba zvingagumbura vanozvinyima, vapietists, mapenzi akadzidza, vanhu vakachenjera, asi kwete vatsvakurudzi vepfungwa vechokwadi.

Kunyange zvazvingaita sezvisingatendi, pakati pezvinonhuwira zvemunamato kunovandawo hutsotsi, pakati pezvikamu zvenhetembo kunovandawo hutsotsi, pasi pedenga dzvene renzvimbo tsvene dzoumwari zvikuru hutsotsi hunopfeka nguo youtsvene neshoko rorusununguko.

Pakati pemafundo akadzama evatsvene vanoremekedzwa zvikuru, MAI emumba yehure, hutsotsi, kuuraya, nezvimwewo anorarama.

Vaperekedzi vasiri vanhu vakavanzwa pakati pehudzamu husinganzwisisiki hwekusaziva.

Vatsvene vakasiyana-siyana venhoroondo vakatambura zvikuru nokuda kwechikonzero ichocho; ngatiyeukei miedzo yaSan Antonio, zvinonyangadza zvese zvaaifanira kurwisa mukoma wedu Francisco de Asís.

Zvisinei, havana kutaura zvose avo vatsvene, uye vazhinji vavanhu vanogara vari voga vakaramba vakanyarara.

Munhu anoshamiswa nokufunga kuti vamwe vanhu vanogara vari voga vanotendeuka uye vatsvene vanogara mumakoloni etsika ehure uye hutsotsi.

Asi ivo vatsvene, uye kana vasati vawana zvinhu izvi zvinotyisa zvepfungwa dzavo, pavanongo zviwana vanozoshandisa masilinda panyama yavo, vachazvinyima zvokudya, pamwe vachazvirova, uye vachakumbira mai vavo voutsvene KUNDALINI kuti vabvise MAI avo vakaipa avo vane zvibereko zvinotyisa zvenyika yavo yetsika.

Zvitendero zvakasiyana-siyana zvakataura zvakawanda pamusoro poupenyu pashure porufu noupenyu hwapashure.

Ngavarege kuzviomesa misoro yavo vanhu varombo pamusoro pezviri kune rumwe rutivi, kupfuura guva.

Pasina mubvunzo pashure porufu mumwe nomumwe anoramba achirarama mukoloni yetsika yenguva dzose.

Mbavha mumahure embavha icharamba iripo; ane ruchiva mumahure acharamba aripo sechiratidzo cheshura; ane hasha, ane hasha acharamba achirarama mumigwagwa ine ngozi yetsika uye nehasha, uyewo uko banga rinopenya uye kupfurwa kwepfuti kunonzwika.

Hwaro pachahwo hwakanaka kwazvo, hwakabva kumusoro, kubva kunyeredzi uye zvinosuruvarisa kuti hwakapinzwa mukati mezvibereko zvose izvi zvatakatakura mukati.

Nokupesana, hwaro hunogona kufamba nzira yacho, kudzokera kunharaunda yokutanga, kudzokera kunyeredzi, asi hunofanira kutanga hwazosunungurwa kubva kuvaperekedzi vayo vakaipa vanoita kuti huve munzvimbo dzokurashikirwa.

Apo Francisco de Asís naAntonio de Padua, vadzidzisi vakatanhamara vakaitwa vaKristu, vakawana mukati mavo zvibereko zvokuparadzwa, vakatambura zvisingatauriki uye hapana mubvunzo kuti pahwaro hwemabasa okuziva uye kutambura kwokuzvidira vakakwanisa kuderedza guruva rezvinhu zvisiri zvounhu zvose izvo zvaigara mukati mavo. Pasina mubvunzo Vatsvene ivavo vakaitwa vaKristu ndokudzokera kunharaunda yokutanga pashure pokunge vatambura zvikuru.

Chinhu chekutanga chinodiwa, chinokosha, chekurumidza, ndechekuti nzvimbo yemagineti yatakatanga zvisina kujairika muhunhu hwedu hwenhema, iendeswe kuHwaro, nenzira iyi murume akakwana anogona kutanga rwendo rwake kubva muhunhu kuenda kunyeredzi, achikwira nenzira yekudzidzisa inofambira mberi, nhanho nhanho negomo REKUVAPO.

Chero bedzi nzvimbo yemagineti ichiramba yakatangwa muhunhu hwedu hwokunyengera tichararama mumahure etsika anonyangadza zvikuru, kunyange zvazvo muupenyu hunobatsira tiri vagari vakanaka kwazvo.

Mumwe nomumwe ane nzvimbo yemagineti inomuzivisa; mutengesi ane nzvimbo yemagineti yezvokutengeserana uye nokuda kwechikonzero ichocho anozviita mumisika uye anokwezva zvinhu zvaanofarira, vatengi navatengesi.

Munhu wezvesayenzi ane muhunhu hwake nzvimbo yemagineti yezvesayenzi uye nokuda kwechikonzero ichocho anokwezva kwaari zvinhu zvose zvesayenzi, mabhuku, marabhoritari, nezvimwewo.

Munhu anoziva zvakavanzika ane maari nzvimbo yemagineti yezvakavanzika, uye sezvo rudzi urwu rwemagineti ruchiva rwakasiyana nenyaya dzehunhu, pasina mubvunzo shanduko inoitika nokuda kwechikonzero ichi.

Apo nzvimbo yemagineti inotangwa mukuziva, ndiko kuti, muhwaro, ipapo kudzoka kwomurume wose kunyeredzi kunotanga.
