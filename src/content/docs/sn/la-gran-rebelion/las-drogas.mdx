Kuzviparadzanisa kwepfungwa dzemunhu kunotibvumidza kuratidza uchokwadi hwakakomba hwedanho repamusoro mukati memumwe nemumwe wedu.

Kana munhu akwanisa kuzvionera pachake zviri pachena chiitiko chevarume vaviri mumunhu mumwe, wezasi padanho renguva dzose, wepamusoro pane imwe octave yakakwirira, zvino zvese zvinoshanduka uye tinotsvaga mune ino kesi kuita muhupenyu zvinoenderana nenheyo huru dzaakabata mukati meHUNGWARU hwake.

Sezvo hupenyu hwekunze huripo, saizvozvowo hupenyu hwemukati huripo.

Munhu wekunze haasi zvese, kuzviparadzanisa kwepfungwa kunotidzidzisa chokwadi chemunhu wemukati.

Munhu wekunze ane maitiro ake, chinhu chine maitiro mazhinji uye maitiro akajairika muhupenyu, karamu kanofambiswa netambo dzisingaonekwi.

Munhu wemukati ndiye HUNGWARU chaihwo, zvinotariswa mumimwe mitemo yakasiyana kwazvo, haambokwanise kuchinjwa kuita robhoti.

Munhu wekunze haavhariri pasina mutanda wekusonera, anonzwa kuti haana kubhadharwa zvakanaka, anozvinzwira tsitsi, anozvitarisa zvakanyanya, kana ari musoja anoshuvira kuva mukuru, kana ari mushandi mufekitari anoramba kana asingasimudzirwi, anoda kuti zvaakawana zvizivikanwe nemazvo, nezvimwewo.

Hapana angagona kusvika pakuzvarwa KECHIPIRI, kuzvarwa patsva sezvinotaura Evhangeri yaIshe, chero achiri kurarama nepfungwa dzemunhu wezasi akajairika.

Kana munhu achiziva kusakosha kwake pachake uye urombo hwemukati, kana ane ushingi hwekunzvera hupenyu hwake, hapana mubvunzo kuti anoziva pachake kuti haana kana hunhu hwemhando ipi zvayo.

"Vakaropafadzwa varombo mumweya nekuti vachagamuchira umambo hwematenga."

Varombo mumweya kana kuti vanoshayiwa mumweya, ndivo chaivo vanoziva kusakosha kwavo pachavo, kusanyara uye urombo hwemukati. Rudzi irworwo rwezvisikwa zvinogamuchira chiedza pasina mubvunzo.

"Zviri nyore kuti ngamera ipfuure nomunjodzi wetsono, kupfuura mupfumi kupinda muumambo hwokumatenga."

Zviri pachena kuti pfungwa dzakapfuma nezvakawanda zvazvakawana, mawararano nemenduru, hunhu hwevanhu vakakurumbira uye dzidziso dzakaoma dzemasayenzi, haazvirambi mumweya uye nekudaro haambogona kupinda muumambo hwematenga.

Kuti apinde muUmambo, pfuma yekutenda inokurumidza. Chero bedzi kuzviparadzanisa kwepfungwa kusingazviratidzi mumwe nomumwe wedu, KUTENDA kunoita chimwe chinhu chinopfuura zvisingabviri.

KUTENDA ruzivo rwakachena, huchenjeri hwakananga.

KUTENDA kwagara kuchivhiringidzwa nezvitendero zvisina maturo, maGnostics hatifanirwe kumbokanganisa zvakakomba.

KUTENDA chiitiko chakananga chezvechokwadi; chiitiko chikuru chemunhu wemukati; kuziva kwoumwari kwechokwadi.

Munhu wemukati, paanoziva kuburikidza neruzivo rwemashiripiti akananga nyika dzake dzemukati, zviri pachena kuti anozivawo nyika dzemukati dzevanhu vose vanogara panyika.

Hapana angagona kuziva nyika dzemukati dzepasi pano, yegadziriro yezuva uye neboka renyeredzi ratinogara, kana asati aziva nyika dzake dzemukati. Izvi zvakafanana nemunhu anozviuraya anotiza hupenyu nepamusiwo wenhema.

Kunzwa kunoshamisa kwemushonga wezvinodhaka kune midzi yawo chaiyo munhengo inotyisa yeKUNDARTIGUADOR (nyoka inoedza yeEdheni).

Kuziva kwakavharirwa pakati pezvinhu zvakawanda zvinoumba Ego kunotariswa nekuda kwekuvharirwa kwayo pachayo.

Kuziva kweego kunova, mune mamiriro ekoma, nezviroto zvinokonzerwa nehope zvakafanana nezvemunhu chero upi zvake ari pasi pesimba remushonga wakadaro kana wakadaro.

Tinogona kuronga nyaya iyi nenzira inotevera: zviroto zvekuziva kweego zvakaenzana nezviroto zvinokonzerwa nemishonga.

Sezviri pachena aya marudzi maviri ezviroto ane zvawo zvekutanga zvikonzero munhengo inotyisa yeKUNDARTIGUADOR. (Ona chitsauko XVI chebhuku rino).

Pasina mubvunzo mishonga inoparadza matanda ealpha, saka pasina mubvunzo kubatana kwechokwadi pakati pepfungwa nehuropi kunorasika; izvi zvinoguma nekukundikana kwakazara.

Mutorerwa wezvinodhaka anoshandura tsika kuita chitendero uye anofunga nenzira yakatsauka kuti aone zviri zvechokwadi ari pasi pesimba remishonga, asingazivi kuti kunzwa kwakawedzerwa kunokonzerwa neweti, L.S.D., morphine, howa hunokonzeresa zviroto, cocaine, heroin, hashishi, mapiritsi ekudzikamisa akawandisa, amphetamines, barbiturates, nezvimwewo, nezvimwewo, zviroto zvinongogadzirwa nenhengo inotyisa yeKUNDARTIGUADOR.

Vanotora zvinodhaka vachidzokera shure, vachishata nekufamba kwenguva, pakupedzisira vanonyura zvachose munyika dzegehena.
