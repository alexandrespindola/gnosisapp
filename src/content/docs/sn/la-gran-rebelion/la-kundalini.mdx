Pasina mubvunzo Kundalini ine zvinyorwa zvakawanda uye chinhu chakakosha kuongorora.

Mumagwaro eMedieval Alchemy, Kundalini isayinatura yeastral yemvura yechirume tsvene, STELLA MARIS, MUVIRGO WEGUNGWA, anotungamira vashandi veBasa Guru neuchenjeri.

Izvo zvisina kutaurwa nezvitendero zvakajairika, kanenge maererano nedenderedzwa reexoteric kana reruzhinji, chitarisiko cheISIS mune yayo yega yega chimiro chemunhu.
