Mubasa wakadzama wemukati, mukati mezvivakwa zvekutarisa kwepfungwa kwakasimba, tinofanira kurarama zvese zviri zviviri nenhoroondo huru yezvose zviri mukati.

Kristu Wemukati anofanira kubvisa zvese zvinhu zvisingadiwe zvatakatakura mukati medu.

Kuwedzera kwepfungwa kwakawanda mukati mezvinhu zvedu zvekudzika kwepfungwa kunoshevedzera kukumbira kurovererwa Ishe wemukati.

Hazvirambidzike kuti mumwe nomumwe wedu ane vatengesi vatatu mupfungwa dzake.

Judasi, dhimoni rechishuwo; Pirato dhimoni repfungwa; Kaifasi, dhimoni rekuvenga.

Ava vatengesi vatatu vakaroverera Ishe wekukwana mukati chaiko memweya yedu.

Aya marudzi matatu chaiwo ezvinhu zvisiri zvevanhu zvakakosha munhoroondo huru yezvose zviri mukati.

Pasina mubvunzo nhoroondo yambotaurwa yagara ichiraramiwa muchivande mukati mezvinhu zvekudzika kwekuziva kukuru kwechokwadi.

Nokudaro, nhoroondo huru yezvose zviri mukati haisi yechokwadi chaicho yaKabiri Mukuru Jesu sezvinogara zvichifungidzirwa nezvitumirano zvinodzidziswa.

Vanotanga vezera rose, Madzishe emakore ose, vakafanira kurarama nhoroondo huru yezvose zviri mukati, mukati mavo, pano uye ikozvino.

Zvisinei, Jesu Kabiri Mukuru aiva noushingi hwokumirira nhoroondo yakadaro yemukati pachena, mumugwagwa uye muchiedza chezuva, kuzarura kunzwisisa kwokutanga kuvazhinji vose, pasina rusaruro rweganda, murume kana mukadzi, boka kana ruvara.

Zvinoshamisa kuti pane mumwe munhu anofanira kudzidzisa nhoroondo yemukati kumadzinza ose epasi pachena.

Kristu Wemukati asiri mhombwe anofanira kubvisa maari zvinhu zvepfungwa zvemhombwe.

Kristu Wemukati ari rugare norudo anofanira kubvisa kubva maari zvinhu zvisingadiwe zvehasha.

Kristu Wemukati asiri munhu anokara anofanira kubvisa kubva maari zvinhu zvisingadiwe zvemakaro.

Kristu Wemukati asiri munhu ane godo anofanira kubvisa zvinhu zvepfungwa zvegodo maari.

Kristu Wemukati achiva kuzvininipisa kwakakwana, kuzvininipisa kusingagumi, nyore chose, anofanira kubvisa kubva maari zvinhu zvinonyangadza zvekudada, kuzvitutumadza, kuzvikudza.

Kristu Wemukati, shoko, Logos Musiki anogara achirarama mumabasa anoramba achienderera mberi anofanira kubvisa mukati medu, maari amene uye naye amene zvinhu zvisingadiwe zvekusaita basa, usimbe, kusaenda mberi.

Ishe weKukwana akajaira kutsanya kwose, akaenzana, asingambovi shamwari yokubatwa uye madyiro makuru anofanira kubvisa maari zvinhu zvinonyangadza zvemakaro.

Kubatana kusinganzwisisike kwaKristu-Jesu; Kristu-Munhu; musanganiswa usina kujairika weuhwo hunoyera neuhwo hunhu; muedzo nguva dzose kune Logos.

Chinonyanya kufadza pane zvose izvi ndechekuti Kristu akavanzika anogara ari mukundi; mumwe munhu anogara achikunda rima; mumwe munhu anobvisa rima mukati make, pano uye ikozvino.

Kristu Akavanzika ndiye ishe weKumukira Kukuru, akarambwa nevaprista, vakuru navanyori vetemberi.

Vaprista vanomuvenga; ndiko kuti, havamunzwisisi, vanoda kuti Ishe weKukwana ararame kwenguva yakareba maererano nemitemo yavo isingachinjiki.

Vakuru, ndiko kuti, vagari vepasi, varidzi vemba vakanaka, vanhu vane njere, vanhu vane ruzivo vanovenga Logos, Kristu Mutsvuku, Kristu weKumukira Kukuru, nokuti uyu anobva munyika yetsika dzavo dzakaoma, dzakare, dzinopesana, dzakaoma mumazuva akawanda apfuura.

Vanyori vetemberi, matsotsi emupfungwa vanovenga Kristu Wemukati nokuti uyu ndiye anopesana naAntikristu, muvengi akaziviswa wezvese zviri kurara kwedzidziso dze yunivhesiti izvo zvakawanda mumisika yemiviri nemweya.

Vatengesi vatatu vanovenga Kristu Akavanzika zvakanyanya uye vanomuendesa kurufu mukati medu uye munzvimbo yedu yepfungwa.

Judasi dhimoni rechido rinogara richichinja ishe kwemazana matatu esirivheri, ndiko kuti, zvinwiwa, mari, mukurumbira, kusazvibata, upombwe, nezvimwe.

Pirato dhimoni repfungwa rinogara richigeza maoko aro, rinogara richizvizivisa risingaiti mhosva, harimbovi nemhosva, rinogara richizviruramisa kwariri pacharo uye kune vamwe, rinotsvaga kunzvenga, nzira dzekutiza kuti risarase mabasa aro, nezvimwe.

Kaifasi dhimoni rekuvenga rinogara richitengesa ishe mukati medu; mukati anodiwa anomupa tsvimbo yekufudza makwai ake, zvisinei, mutengesi ane shungu anoshandura aritari kuita mubhedha wekunakidzwa, anoita ufeve nguva dzose, anoita upombwe, anotengesa masacramenti, nezvimwe.

Ava vatengesi vatatu vanotambudza muchivande ishe anodiwa wemukati vasina tsitsi.

Pirato anomuita kuti aise korona yeminzwa patembere dzake, vanhu vakaipa vanomurova, vanomutuka, vanomutuka munzvimbo yepfungwa yepedyo pasina tsitsi dzemhando ipi zvayo.
