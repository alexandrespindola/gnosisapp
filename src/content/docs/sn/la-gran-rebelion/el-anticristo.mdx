Uchenjeri hwekuzvikudza sehumbowo hunoratidzwa hwe Ego yepfungwa, pasina mubvunzo ndiYE ANTICHRIST.

Avo vanofunga kuti ANTICHRIST munhu asina kujairika akazvarwa mune imwe nzvimbo yepasi kana kubva kune imwe nyika, zvirokwazvo vakarasika zvachose.

Takataura neushingi kuti ANTICHRIST haasi munhu akajeka, asi vanhu vese.

Zviri pachena kuti ANTICHRIST ari mumoyo memunhu wese uye anoratidzirwa nenzira dzakawanda.

Uchenjeri hwakashandiswa kushumira mweya hunobatsira; uchenjeri hwakaparadzaniswa nemweya hahubatsiri.

Kubva mukuchenjeri kusina umwari kunobuda matsotsi, kuratidzwa kwakajeka kweANTICHRIST.

Zviri pachena kuti tsotsi pacharo ndiye ANTICHRIST. Zvinosuruvarisa kuti nyika yanhasi nenjodzi dzayo dzese nenhamo zvinotongwa naANTICHRIST.

Mamiriro enyonganiso ayo vanhu varipo zviri pasina mubvunzo nekuda kweANTICHRIST.

Hapana angaramba kuti robhoti rakagadzirirwa rinoshanda maererano nechirongwa, harigone kushanda kana rikabviswa muchirongwa.

ANTICHRIST agadzira chirongwa chinogadzirisa marobhoti e humanoid enguva dzino dzekuderera.

Kuita tsananguro idzi, kusimbisa zvandiri kutaura, zvinoita senge zvinotyisa nekuti zviri kunze kwechirongwa, hapana humanoid robhoti inogona kubvuma zvinhu zviri kunze kwechirongwa.

Nyaya iyi yakakomba zvekuti, uye kunamatira kwepfungwa kukuru, zvekuti, nerumwe rudzi, chero humanoid robhoti hainga fungire kana zvishoma kuti chirongwa hachishande, nekuti iye agadziriswa maererano nechirongwa, uye kupokana nacho kwaizoita kunge hweswa, chimwe chinhu chisingawirirane uye chisina musoro.

Kuti robhoti riparadze chirongwa chayo chinhu chakaipa, chimwe chinhu chisingabviri zvachose nekuti kuvapo kwayo kunobva muchirongwa.

Zvinosuruvarisa, zvinhu hazvina kumira sezvinofungwa nerobhoti re humanoid; kune imwe sainzi, humwe uchenjeri, husingagamuchirike ku humanoid robhoti.

Iyo humanoid robhoti inopindura uye ine chikonzero chekupindura nekuti haina kurongedzerwa imwe sainzi kana imwe tsika, kana chero chinhu chakasiyana nechirongwa chayo chinozivikanwa.
