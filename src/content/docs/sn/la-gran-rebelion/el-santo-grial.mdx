Chirongo Chitsvene chinopenya muusiku hwakadzika hwemazera ese. Varwi vechidiki veMiddle Ages munguva dzeHondo dzeMuchinjikwa vakatsvaga zvisina maturo Chirongo Chitsvene muNyika Tsvene asi havana kuwana.

Abrahamu Muporofita paakange achidzoka kubva kuhondo ne madzimambo eSodoma ne Gomora, zvinonzi akasangana naMelkizedeki nyanzvi yepasi. Zvirokwazvo munhu mukuru uyu aigara mune nhare yakange iri panzvimbo chaipo payakazovakwa Jerusarema, guta rinodiwa revaporofita.

Ngano dzemazana emakore dzinoti uye izvi zvinozivikanwa nevanhu nevamwari, kuti Abrahamu akaita kuzodzwa kweGnostic nekugovana chingwa newaini pamberi paMelkizedeki.

Hazvina kukodzera kutaura kuti Abrahamu akapa Melkizedeki chegumi ne zvibereko zvekutanga sezvazvakanyorwa muBhuku reMutemo.

Abrahamu akagamuchira Chirongo Chitsvene kubva kumaoko aMelkizedeki; gare gare munguva mukombe uyu wakaenda kutembere yeJerusarema.

Hapana mubvunzo kuti Mambokadzi weSheba akashanda semurevereri wechiitiko ichi. Akazviratidza pamberi paSoromoni Mambo aine Chirongo Chitsvene uye mushure mekumisa kune miedzo yakaoma akamuendesa gem rakakosha kudaro.

Kabir Mukuru Jesu akanwa mumukombe uyu mumutambo mutsvene wechirairo chekupedzisira sezvazvakanyorwa muEvhangeri ina.

Josefa weArimatiya akazadza girazi neropa rakabuda mumavanga eAnodiwa paGomo reDehenya.

Mapurisa eRoma paakapinda munharaunda yemutungamiriri wenyika ataurwa havana kuwana gem iri rinokosha.

Seneta weRoma haana kungoviga gem raikosha kudaro asi, pamusoro pezvo, pamwe chete naro akaviga pasi pevhu pfumo reLongibus iro zana reRoma rakakuvadza divi raShe.

Josefa weArimatiya akapinzwa mujeri rinotyisa nekusada kuendesa Chirongo Chitsvene.

Seneta ataurwa paakabuda mujeri akaenda kuRoma akatakura Chirongo Chitsvene.

Paakasvika kuRoma Josefa weArimatiya akawana kutambudzwa kwaNero kwevaKristu ndokuenda kumahombekombe eMediterranean.

Husiku humwe muzviroto ngirozi yakamuonekera ikati: "Mukombe uyu une simba guru nekuti mauri mune ropa reMudzikinuri weNyika." Josefa weArimatiya achiteerera mirairo yengirozi akaviga mukombe wakadaro mutembere yaive kuMontserrat, Catalonia, Spain.

Nekufamba kwenguva mukombe wakadaro wakazova usingaonekwe pamwe chete netembere uye chikamu chegomo.

Chirongo Chitsvene ichigadziko chaHermes, mukombe waSoromoni, urn inokosha yematembere ese ezvakavanzika.

MuAreka yesungano pakanga pasingatongoshayikwi Chirongo Chitsvene muchimiro chemukombe kana gomor, mukati mayo maive manna werenje.

Chirongo Chitsvene chinotsanangura zvakajeka YONI yechikadzi, mukati memukombe mutsvene uyu mune nectar yekusafa, iyo Soma yevanhu vese, chinwiwa chepamusoro-soro chevamwari Vatsvene.

Kristu Dzvuku anomiririra Chirongo Chitsvene muawa yepamusoro-soro yeChristification, sezvazvakanyorwa muEvhangeri yaShe.

Chirongo Chitsvene hachingashayikwe paatari yetembere. Zviripachena kuti muPristi anofanira kunwa waini yechiedza muMukombe Mutsvene.

Zvingave zvisina musoro kufunga nezvetembere yezvakavanzika mukati mayo mukombe wakaropafadzwa wemazera ese wanga usipo.

Izvi zvinotiyeuchidza Geneva Queen weMajinn avo vakanyengedza Lanzarote waini mumakapu ekunakidzwa kweSUFRA neMANTI.

Vamwari vasingafi vanodya nechikafu chiri muMukombe Mutsvene; avo vanovenga Mukombe Wakaropafadzwa, vanomhura pamweya mutsvene.

Munhu Mukuru anofanira kuzvidya nechikafu chinonwiwa chekusafa chiri mugirazi rutsvene retembere.

Kuchinja kwesimba rekugadzira kwakakosha kana munhu achida kunwa muGirazi Ritsvene.

Kristu Dzvuku agara ari mushanduri, agara ari mupanduki, agara ari gamba, agara achikunda, anokwazisa Vamwari nekunwa mugirazi regoridhe.

Simudzai mukombe wenyu zvakanaka muve nechokwadi chekurarama kunyangwe donho rimwe chete rewaini inokosha.

Rangarirai kuti mutsara wedu wedenga ndiTHELEMA (kuda).

Kubva pasi pegirazi -chiratidzo chemukadzi- moto unopisa unopenya pachiso chakatsvuka cheSuper-Munhu.

Vamwari vasingatauriki vemagalaxi ese vanogara vachinwa chinwiwa chekusafa mugirazi risingaperi.

Kutonhora kwemwedzi kunoburitsa kushanduka munguva; zvinodikanwa kunwa waini tsvene yechiedza mugirazi ritsvene reAlkimia.

Puru yevamambo vatsvene, korona yehumambo uye goridhe rinopfuta nderimwe chete raKristu Dzvuku.

Ishe weMheni neKutinhira anobata Chirongo Chitsvene muruoko rwake rwerudyi ndokunwa waini yegoridhe kuti azvidye.

Avo vanodurura Girazi raHermes panguva yekuvhiya kwemakemikari, chaizvo vanova zvisikwa zvisiri zvevanhu zvepasi-nyika.

Zvese zvatanyora pano zvinowanikwa magwaro azere mubhuku rangu rinonzi "Kuroora Kwakakwana".
