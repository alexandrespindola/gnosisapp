Kristo iMoto yeMoto, Murazvo woMurazvo, Chiratidzo cheNyeredzi cheMoto.

Pamuchinjikwa woMutendi weKarivhari panotsanangurwa Chakavanzika chaKristo neshoko rimwe chete rine mavara mana: INRI. Ignis Natura Renovatur Integram - Moto Unogara Uchivandudza Zvisikwa.

Kuuya kwaKristo mumwoyo womunhu kunotishandura zvachose.

Kristo iLOGOS ZUVA, Kubatana Kwakakwana Kwakawanda. Kristo ndihwo upenyu hunobvunda muchisiko chose, ndihwo huri, hwagara huripo, uye hucharamba huripo.

Zvawanda zvataurwa pamusoro peMutambo weCosmic; hapana mubvunzo kuti Mutambo uyu unoumbwa neevhangeri ina.

Taudzwa kuti Mutambo weCosmic wakaunzwa panyika nevaElohim; Ishe Mukuru weAtlantis akamiririra mutambo uyu muNyama neRopa.

Ishe Mukuru KABIR JESU aifanirawo kumiririra Mutambo iwoyo pachena muNyika Tsvene.

Kunyange Kristo akaberekwa kane chiuru muBhetemu, hazvina basa kana asina kuberekwa mumwoyo yeduwo.

Kunyange akange afa akamuka nezuva rechitatu kubva kuvakafa, hazvina basa kana asina kufa akamuka matiriwo.

Kuedza kuwana chimiro uye zvinoreva moto kuedza kuwana Mwari, uyo kuvapo chaiko kwagara kuchiratidzwa pamauri ezvinoita semoto.

Gwenzi rinopfuta (Eksodo, III, 2) uye moto weSinai mushure mekupihwa kweMirairo Gumi (Eksodo, XIX, 18): zviratidzo zviviri izvo Mwari akazviratidza kuna Mosesi.

Mumufananidzo wemunhu weJaspa neSardoniki ane ruvara rwemurazvo, akagara pachigaro choushe chinopenya uye chinobwinya, Johane Mutsvene anotsanangura muridzi weChisiko chose. (Zvakazarurwa, IV, 3,5). "Mwari wedu iMoto Unoparadza", anonyora Pauro Mutsvene muTsamba yake kuvaHebheru.

Kristo wepamoyo, Moto weKudenga, anofanira kuberekwa matiri uye anoberekwa chaizvo patinenge tafambira mberi zvakakwana mubasa repfungwa.

Kristo wepamoyo anofanira kubvisa muZvisikwa zvedu zvepfungwa, zvikonzero zvimwe chetezvo zvekukanganisa; MAI CAUSAS.

Kunyungudika kwezvikonzero zveEGO hakungaite kusvikira Kristo wepedyo aberekwa matiri.

Moto mupenyu uye weUchenjeri, Kristo wepamoyo, iMoto yeMoto, yakachena yezvakachena.

Moto unotikomba nekutishambidza kwose kwose, unouya kwatiri nemhepo, nemvura uye nepasi pacharo izvo zvinochengetedza nemotokari dzaro dzakasiyana-siyana.

Moto weKudenga unofanira kuiswa matiri, ndiKristo wepedyo, Muponesi wedu wemukati akadzama.

Ishe wepedyo anofanira kutora mhosva yezvose zvedu zvepfungwa zveMasirinda mashanu emuchina weOrganic; yenzira dzedu dzose dzePfunganidzo, Manzwiro, Injini, Zvinongoitika zvekuroorana.
