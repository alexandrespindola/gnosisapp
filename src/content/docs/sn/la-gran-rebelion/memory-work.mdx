Hapana mubvunzo kuti munhu wese ane Psychology yake, izvi hazvirambidzike, hazvipokanidzike, hazvirambike.

Zvinosuwisa kuti vanhu havatombofungi nezve izvi uye vazhinji havatombobvumi nekuti vakabatwa mupfungwa dzemuviri.

Munhu wese anobvuma chokwadi chemuviri nekuti anogona kuuona nekubata, asi Psychology inyaya yakasiyana, haionekwi nezvinhu zvishanu, saka maitiro makuru ekuramba kana kungoizvidza nekushaya basa ichiita seinokosha.

Pasina mubvunzo kana munhu achitanga kuzvitarisa ichiratidza kuti abvuma chokwadi chinotyisa chePsychology yake.

Zviri pachena kuti hapana munhu angazama kuzvitarisa kunze kwekunge atanga awana chikonzero chakakosha.

Zviripachena kuti anovamba kuzvitarisa anova munhu akasiyana nevamwe, muchokwadi zvinoratidza mukana wekushanduka.

Zvinosuwisa kuti vanhu havadi kuchinja, vanogutsikana nemamiriro avanogara.

Zvinorwadza kuona vanhu vachiberekwa, vachikura, vachibereka semhuka, vachitambura zvisingaite uye vachifa vasingazive kuti sei.

Kuchinja chinhu chakakosha, asi hazvigoneke kana kuzvitarisa kusingatange.

Zvakakosha kutanga kuzviona nechinangwa chekuzviziva, nekuti muchokwadi munhu ane pfungwa haazvizive.

Kana munhu akawana mhosva yepfungwa, muchokwadi atora nhanho huru nekuti izvi zvichamubvumira kuifunda uye kunyange kuibvisa zvachose.

Chokwadi, mhosva dzedu dzepfungwa hadziverengeki, kunyangwe taiva nendimi chiuru dzekutaura uye muromo wesimbi taisagona kudziverenga dzose zvakanaka.

Chinhu chakakomba pane zvose izvi ndechekuti hatizivi kuyera chokwadi chinotyisa chekukanganisa kupi zvako; tinogara tichikuona zvisina basa pasina kunyatsochiteerera; tinochiona sechinhu chisina basa.

Kana tikagamuchira dzidziso yevazhinji uye tikanzwisisa chokwadi chakaipa chemadhimoni manomwe ayo Jesu Kristu akabudisa mumuviri waMaria Magadalena, zviri pachena kuti mafungiro edu pamusoro pemhosva dzepfungwa anochinja zvakanyanya.

Hazvina kuipa kutaura zvakasimba kuti dzidziso yevazhinji inobva kuTibet uye Gnostic zana muzana.

Chokwadi, hazvitombofadzi kuziva kuti mukati medu mune mazana ezviuru zvevanhu vepfungwa.

Mhosva yega yega yepfungwa munhu akasiyana aripo mukati medu pano nepo.

Madhimoni manomwe ayo Mudzidzisi Mukuru Jesu Kristu akadzinga mumuviri waMaria Magadalena zvivi zvinouraya zvinomwe: Hasha, Makaro, Ruchiva, Godo, Kudada, Usimbe, Makaro.

Sezvingatarisirwa, rimwe nerimwe remadhimoni aya rakasiyana musoro wemauto.

MuIjipita rekare reMafarao, anotangwa aifanira kubvisa madhimoni matsvuku aSETH kubva mumamiriro ake emukati kana aida kuwana kumuka kwehana.

Tichiona chokwadi chemhosva dzepfungwa, anotsvaga anoda kuchinja, haadi kuramba ari mumamiriro aanogara nevanhu vakawanda kudaro mukati mepfungwa dzake, uye anotanga kuzvitarisa.

Sezvatinofambira mberi mukati mebasa, tinogona kuzvionera pachedu kurongeka kunonakidza kwazvo muhurongwa hwekubvisa.

Munhu anoshamisika paanowana kurongeka mubasa rine chekuita nekubviswa kwemapoka akasiyana-siyana epfungwa anoratidza zvikanganiso zvedu.

Chinonakidza pane zvose izvi ndechekuti kurongeka kwakadaro mukubviswa kwezvikanganiso kunoitwa zvishoma nezvishoma uye kunogadziriswa maererano neDialectic yeHana.

Hapana kana imwe nguva dialectic yekufunga ingagona kudarika basa guru re dialectic yehana.

Zvinhu zviri kutiratidza kuti kurongeka kwepfungwa mubasa rekubvisa zvikanganiso kunoongororwa nekuzvibata kwedu kwemukati.

Tinofanira kujekesa kuti pane musiyano mukuru pakati peEgo neSer. Ini handaigona kumisikidza kurongeka munyaya dzepfungwa, nekuti pachayo mugumisiro wekusarongeka.

Ser chete ndiyo ine simba rekugadzira kurongeka mupfungwa dzedu. Iyo Ser iSer. Chikonzero chekuvepo kweSer ndicho Ser pachayo.

Kurongeka mubasa rekuzvitarisa, kutongwa uye kubviswa kwemapoka edu epfungwa, kuri kuratidzwa nekuziva kwekuzvitarisa kwepfungwa.

Muzvisikwa zvose zvevanhu pane pfungwa yekuzvitarisa kwepfungwa iri mumamiriro ekunonoka, asi inokura zvishoma nezvishoma sezvatinoramba tichiishandisa.

Pfungwa yakadaro inotibvumira kunzwisisa zvakananga uye kwete kuburikidza nekubatana kwepfungwa, iwo akasiyana ini anogara mukati mepfungwa dzedu.

Nyaya iyi ye extra-perceptions yemanzwiro inotanga kufundwa munzvimbo yeParapsychology, uye muchokwadi yakaratidzwa muzviitiko zvakawanda zvakaitwa nekuchenjera nekufamba kwenguva uye pane magwaro mazhinji pamusoro payo.

Avo vanoramba chokwadi chekuwedzera-kunzwisisa kwemanzwiro vanoziva muzana zana, matsotsi epfungwa akavharirwa mupfungwa inotapira.

Nekudaro, pfungwa yekuzvitarisa kwepfungwa chinhu chakadzika, inoenda kure kupfuura zvirevo zvakapusa zveparapsychological, inotibvumira kuzvitarisa kwepedyo uye kusimbiswa kwakazara kwechokwadi chinotyisa chemapoka edu akasiyana.

Kurongeka kwakatevedzana kwezvikamu zvakasiyana zvebasa rine chekuita nenyaya ino yakakomba yekubviswa kwemapoka epfungwa, inotibvumira kufunga "ndangariro yebasa" inonakidza kwazvo uye inobatsira kwazvo munyaya yekukura kwemukati.

Iyi ndangariro-yebasa, kunyangwe ichokwadi kuti inogona kutipa mifananidzo yakasiyana-siyana yepfungwa yematanho akasiyana-siyana ehupenyu hwekare, akaunganidzwa pamwe chete angada kubvisa mumufananidzo wedu mufananidzo mupenyu uye unosemesa zvekuti taivei tisati tatanga psycho-transformist radical basa.

Hapana mubvunzo kuti hatimbode kudzokera kumufananidzo iwoyo unotyisa, mumiriri mupenyu wezvatakanga tiri.

Kubva panguva ino, mufananidzo wepfungwa waizobatsira senzira yekusangana pakati pezvazvino zvachinja uye zvakapfuura zvinodzokera shure, zvekare, zvine hunyanzvi uye zvinosuruvarisa.

Ndongariro-yebasa inogara ichinyorwa pahwaro hwezviitiko zvepfungwa zvakarekodhwa nenzvimbo yekuzvitarisa kwepfungwa.

Mupfungwa dzedu mune zvinhu zvisingadiwi zvatinotombofungira.

Kuti murume akatendeseka, asingakwanisi kutora chero chinhu chisingamufaniri, ane rukudzo uye akakodzera rukudzo rwese, awane zvisingaite akatevedzana neni mbavha dziri munzvimbo dzakadzika-dzika dzepfungwa dzake, chinhu chinotyisa, asi hachisi chisingabviri.

Kuti mudzimai akanaka azere hunhu hukuru kana musikana ane hunyanzvi hwepamusoro hwepfungwa uye dzidzo yakanaka kwazvo, kuburikidza nepfungwa yekuzvitarisa kwepfungwa anowana nenzira isina kujairika kuti mupfungwa dzake dzepedyo mune boka rema prostitute, zvinosemesa uye zvinotomboshayisa kukodzera nzvimbo yepfungwa kana tsika yemunhu wese anonzwisisa, asi zvese izvo zvinogoneka mukati menzvimbo chaiyo yekuzvitarisa kwepfungwa.
