Hapana mubvunzo pakati pekufunga nekunzwa pane musiyano mukuru, izvi hazvirambidziki.

Kune kutonhora kukuru pakati pevanhu, kutonhora kweizvo zvisina basa, kwezvisina maturo.

Mapoka evanhu anotenda kuti chakakosha ndechekuti chisiri chakakosha, vanofunga kuti fashoni yazvino uno, kana mota yazvino uno, kana nyaya iyi yemari yevashandi yakakosha ndicho chinhu chakakomba chete.

Vanoti kwakakomba nhau dzanhasi, nyaya yerudo, hupenyu hwekugara, kapu yedoro, mujaho wemabhiza, mujaho wemotokari, kurwa kwenzombe, makuhwa, kunyomba, nezvimwewo.

Sezviri pachena, kana murume wezuva kana mukadzi wekuimba yekunaka akanzwa chimwe chinhu pamusoro peesotericism, sezvo izvi zvisiri muzvirongwa zvavo, kana mukukurukurirana kwavo, kana mumafaro avo epabonde, vanopindura nekusaziva kutonhora kunotyisa, kana kungo kamura miromo yavo, vosimudza mapfudzi avo, uye vanobva nekusava nehanya.

Kuneta ikoko kwepfungwa, kutonhora kunotyisa, kune nheyo mbiri; chekutanga kusaziva kwakanyanya, chechipiri kusavapo zvachose kwekunetseka kwemweya.

Pane kushayikwa kwekubatana, kuvhunduka kwemagetsi, hapana akakupa muchitoro, kana pakati peizvo zvaifungidzirwa kuti zvakakomba, kunyanya mumafaro emubhedha.

Dai mumwe munhu aikwanisa kupa benzi rinotonhora kana mukadzi asina hanya kubata kwemagetsi kwechinguvana, kupenya kwemoyo, kumwe kuyeuka kusinganzwisisike, kusaziva chimwe chinhu chiri pedyo, pamwe panguva iyoyo zvese zvingave zvakasiyana.

Asi chimwe chinhu chinotsiva inzwi diki rakavanzika, kunzwa kwekutanga, chishuwo chepedyo; pamwe upenzi, ngowani yakanaka yewindo kana kabati, inotapira yakanakisa yeresitorendi, kusangana kweshamwari iyo gare gare isina kukosha kwatiri, nezvimwewo.

Upenzi, ufuza izvo zvisiri zvekuchinja, asi zvinogona kuve nesimba munguva yakatarwa sekudzimisa kunetseka kwemweya kwekutanga, chishuwo chepedyo, spark diki yechiedza, kunzwa kwatakashatirwa nacho kwekanguva tisingazive kuti sei.

Dai avo nhasi zvitunha zvipenyu, vanofamba husiku vanotonhora vekirabhu kana kungori vatengesi emambure muchitoro chemugwagwa mukuru, vasina kudzipa kunetseka kwepedyo kwekutanga, vangadai vari panguva ino magetsi emweya, nyanzvi dzechiedza, varume vechokwadi mupfungwa yakazara yezwi.

Kupenya, kunzwa, kugomera kusinganzwisisike, kusaziva chimwe chinhu, kwakanzwiwa rimwe zuva nemutengesi wenyama pakona, nemugadziri weshangu kana nachiremba wekutanga hukuru, asi zvese zvaive pasina, upenzi hwehunhu hunogara huchidzima kupenya kwekutanga kwechiedza; ipapo kutonhora kwekusava nehanya kunotyisa kunotevera.
