Nyamavhuvhu 22 KUSVIKIRA GUNYERE 23

PRAKRITI ndimai Mwari, chinhu chepakutanga chezvisikwa.

Munyika yose mune zvinhu zvakasiyana-siyana, zvinhu zvakasiyana-siyana uye zvinhu zviduku, asi izvozvo zvose zvakasiyana-siyana zvekushandurwa kweCHINHU CHIMWE CHEGA.

MATERIA PRIMORDIAL ndiyo AKASA PURO inowanikwa munzvimbo yose, AMAI VAKURU, PRAKRITI.

MAHANVANTARA naPRALAYA mazwi maviri eSANSKRIT akakosha zvikuru ayo vadzidzi veGNÓSTIC vanofanira kujairana nawo.

MAHANVANTARA ndiro ZUVA GURU RENYIKA. PRALAYA ndiyo HOUSIKU HUKURU HWENYIKA. Munguva yeZUVA GURU, pasi pose iripo. Kana HOUSIKU HUKURU hwazosvika, pasi pose hunorega kuvapo, hunonyunguduka mukati memukono wePRAKRITI.

Nzvimbo isingagumi isingagumi yakazadzwa neMISHANDIRO YEZUVA ine MAHANVANTARAS ayo nePRALAYAS.

Nepo vamwe vari muMAHANVANTARA, vamwe vari muPRALAYA.

Mamiriyoni nemabhiriyoni ePASI ROSE anozvarwa uye anofa mukati memukono wePRAKRITI.

COSMOS yose inozvarwa kubva kuPRAKRITI uye inonyungudika muPRAKRITI. Nyika yose ibhora remoto rinobatidzwa rodzima mukati memukono wePRAKRITI.

Zvose zvinobva kuPRAKRITI, zvose zvinodzokera kuPRAKRITI. Iye ndimai VAKURU.
