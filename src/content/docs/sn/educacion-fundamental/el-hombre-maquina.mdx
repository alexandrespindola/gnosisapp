MURUME-MUSHINA ndiye chikara chisingafare kupfuura zvose chiripo mumupata uyu wemisodzi, asi ane, KUZVIITA uye kunyange DZIMUNYANYADZIRO yekUZVITUMIDZA MAMBO WEZVOKUSIKWA.

"ZIVA IWE MUMENE" "MURUME ZVIZIVE IWE MUMENE". Iyi ndiyo MHWARA yekare yeGORIDHE yakanyorwa pamadziro asinga kundike etemberi yeDelfos muGREECE YEKARE.

Murume, mhuka iyoyo isina urombo yeCHINYAMWE chepfungwa inozvitumidza zvisirizvo kuti MURUME, akagadzira zviuru zve muchina yakaoma zvikuru uye yakaoma uye anoziva zvakanaka kuti kuti akwanise kushandisa MASHINI, dzimwe nguva anoda makore akawanda ekudzidza nekudzidziswa, asi kana zvasvika PAARI AMENE anokanganwa zvachose chokwadi ichi, kunyangwe iye amene ari muchina wakaoma kupfuura yose yaakagadzira.

Hakuna murume asina kuzadzwa, nemafungiro enhema pamusoro pake amene, chakanyanya kunetsa ndechekuti haadi kuziva kuti iye muchina chaizvo.

Muchina wevanhu hauna rusununguko rwekusimuka, unoshanda chete nekuda kwesimba rakawanda uye rakasiyana-siyana remukati uye kukanganisa kwekunze.

Mafambiro ose, mabasa, mashoko, pfungwa, manzwiro, mirangariro, zvishuvo, zvemuchina wevanhu zvinokonzerwa nekukanganisa kwekunze uye nezvikonzero zvakawanda zvemukati zvisinganzwisisiki uye zvakaoma.
