Kuponda munhu pachena, uye pasina mubvunzo, ndicho chiito chinoparadza uye chine huwori hukuru kupfuura zvose zvinozivikanwa panyika.

Nzira yakaipisisa yokuuraya nayo ndeyokuparadza upenyu hwevamwe vedu.
