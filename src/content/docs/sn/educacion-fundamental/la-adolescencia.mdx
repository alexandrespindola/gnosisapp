Yave yava nguva yekurega zvachose manyawi enhema uye kusarura kune chekuita nedambudziko rezvebonde.

Zvakakosha kunzwisisa zvakajeka uye nemazvo dambudziko rezvebonde revachiri kuyaruka veese mativi.

Pazera remakore gumi nemana simba rezvebonde rinoonekwa mumuviri wemuyaruka, iro rinoyerera zvinotyisa nepakati peiyo neuro-sympathetic system.

Rudzi urwu rwakakosha rwesimba runoshandura muviri wemunhu, ruchichinja izwi remurume uye ruchitanga basa reovarian mumukadzi.

Muviri wemunhu ifekitari chaiyo inoshandura zvinhu zvakakasharara kuita zvinhu zvakanaka zvehupenyu.

Zvekudya zvatinoisa mudumbu zvinopfuura nemushanduko dzakawanda uye nekunatsiridzwa kusvika pakupedzisira nekugadzira chinhu ichocho chakasimba-semi, chakamisikidzwa-semi chakataurwa naParacelsus neshoko rekuti Ens.-Seminis (Entity of Semen)

Iyo girazi remvura, rinofekeseka, rinoteedzera, urume ihwohwo, rine mukati mayo, nenzira inogona kuitika majerimani ese ehupenyu.

Gnosticism inoziva muurume iyo CAOS iyo hupenyu hunobuda nayo nehasha.

MaAlchemist ekare epakati nepakati akadai saParacelsus, Sendivogius, Nicol√°s Flamel, Raymundo Lulio, nezvimwewo, vakadzidza neruremekedzo rukuru ENS-SEMINIS kana mercury yefilosofi yakavanzika.

Iyi VITRIOL, mushonga wechokwadi wakagadzirwa nehungwaru, nechisikigo mukati memavesicles embeu.

Mune iyi mercury yeuchenjeri hwekare, mune urume uhu, zvese zvinogoneka zvehupenyu zvirimo chaizvo.

Zvinosuruvarisa kuti vechidiki vazhinji nekushaikwa kwekurongeka kwepfungwa kwechokwadi vanozvipira kune tsika yekubatana kana kuti vanotsauka zvinosiririsa nenzira yepasi-bonde yehu-bonde.

Vana nevechidiki vanopihwa ruzivo rwehungwaru pamusoro penyaya zhinji uye vanokurudzirwa munzira yemutambo wevadzidzi, kushungurudzwa kwacho kunopfupisa hupenyu zvinosiririsa, asi zvinosiririsa kuti kana simba rezvebonde rabuda iro rinotanga kuyaruka, vese vabereki vemhuri nevadzidzisi vechikoro, zvichibva pane huchenu hwenhema uye tsika dzoupenzi, vanosarudza kunyarara zvinotyisa.

Kune kunyarara kwemhosva uye kune mazwi anonyadzisa. Kunyarara pamusoro pedambudziko rezvebonde imhosva. Kutaura zvisirizvo pamusoro pedambudziko rezvebonde kunoumbawo imwe mhosva.

Kana vabereki nevadzidzisi vakanyarara, vakarasika zvepabonde vanotaura uye vanobatwa vanova vechidiki vasina ruzivo.

Kana muyaruka asingakwanise kubvunza vabereki kana vadzidzisi, achabva abvunza vake vechikoro vangangove vatotsauka nenzira isiriyo. Mugumisiro hauregi kumirira kwenguva yakareba uye muyaruka mutsva achitevera mazano enhema achazvipira kune tsika yekubatana kana kuti achatsauka nenzira yehu-bonde.

Tsika yekubatana inoparadza zvachose simba rehuropi. Zvakakosha kuziva kuti kune hukama hwepedyo pakati pembeu nehuropi. Zvakakosha cerebrizar urume. Zvakakosha seminizar huropi.

Huropi hunozadzikiswa nekushandura simba rezvebonde, nekurikwirisa, nekurishandura kuita simba rehuropi.

Nenzira iyi urume hunosiiwa hwaiswa uropi uye huropi hwaiswa urume.

Sainzi yeGnostic inodzidza endocrinology zvizere uye inodzidzisa nzira nemaitiro ekushandura masimba ezvebonde, asi iyi inyaya isingakodzere mukati mebhuku rino.

Kana muverengi achida ruzivo nezve Gnosticism anofanira kudzidza mabhuku edu eGnostic ndokupinda muzvidzidzo zvedu.

Vechidiki vanofanira kukwidza masimba ezvebonde nekurima pfungwa yekunaka, kudzidza mimhanzi, chivezwa, kupenda, kuenda kunozorora kumakomo marefu, nezvimwe.

Zviso zvakawanda sei zvingadai zvakanga zvakanaka zvinosvava!

Huropi hwakawanda sei hunoderera! Zvese nekuda kwekushaikwa kwokuchema kwenyevero panguva yakafanira.

Tsika yekubatana, mune vese vechidiki nevasikana yave yakajairika kupfuura kugeza maoko.

Nzvimbo dzepfungwa dzakazara nevarume nevakadzi vakaparadza huropi hwavo mutsika inonyangadza yekubatana. Magumo evanobatana inzvimbo yezvepfungwa.

Tsika yehu-bonde ine midzi yakaora yerudzi urwu runopera uye rwakaipa.

Zvinoita sekusatendeka kuti munyika dzakaita seEngland dzinoti dzakadzidza uye dzakanyatsobudirira, kune mafirimu anoratidza mafirimu ehunhu hwehu-bonde.

Zvinoita sekusatendeka kuti ndizvo chaizvo muEngland uko kuedza kutoitwa kuti pamutemo michato yehunhu hwehu-bonde.

Mumaguta makuru enyika kune dzimba dzoufeve nemakirabhu erudzi rwehu-bonde.

Hama dzakasviba dzevavengi vevakadzi, nhasi dzine masangano akarasika anoshamisa nekuda kwehukama hwavo hwakaderera.

Vaverengi vazhinji vangashamiswa zvakanyanya neizvi zve "hukama hwakaderera" asi hatifanirwe kukanganwa kuti munguva dzese dzemwenje dzakagara dziripo hama dzakasiyana-siyana dzemhosva.

Hama inorwara yevavengi vevakadzi, pasina kupokana hama yemhosva.

Vavengi vevakadzi vanogara vachitora kana kuti vanenge vachitora nzvimbo dzakakosha mukati meurukato hwebazi.

Kana munhu ane hu-bonde akaenda kujeri, anokurumidza kusunungurwa nekuda pesimba rakakodzera revarume vakakosha vesangano remhosva.

Kana munhu akafemera akawira munyasha, anokurumidza kugamuchira rubatsiro rwemari kubva kune vese vatambi vanotyisa veSangano remhosva.

Nhengo dzakasviba dzehu-bonde dzinozviziva nekufanana nemifananidzo yavanoratidza.

Zvinoshamisa kuziva kuti vanhukadzi vanopfeka yunifomu, asi ndizvo zvazviri. Yunifomu yevanhu vane hu-bonde inoenderana nemafashoni ese anotanga. Vanhu vane hunhu vanotanga mafashoni matsva. Kana fashoni ikava yakajairika, vanobva vatanga imwe. Nenzira iyi yunifomu yesangano remhosva inogara iri itsva.

Maguta makuru ese enyika, nhasi ane mamirioni evanhu vane hunhu.

Tsika yehu-bonde inotanga kufamba kwayo kunonyadzisa panguva yekuyaruka.

Zvikoro zvakawanda zvevechidiki vechirume nevasikana inzvimbo chaidzo dzehufeve dzerudzi rwehu-bonde.

Mamirioni evasikana vechidiki vanofamba vakatsunga nenzira ine rima yevavengi vemurume.

Mamirioni evechidiki vechikadzi vane hu-bonde. Sangano remhosva pakati pehu-bonde hwechikadzi rakasimba, sesangano remhosva pakati pechikadzi.

Zvinokurumidza kusiya zvachose manyawi enhema uye kuratidza vechidiki veese mativi, pachena zvakavanzika zvese zvepabonde.
