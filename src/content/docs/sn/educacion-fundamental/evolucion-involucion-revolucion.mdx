Mukuita kwatakaita, takakwanisa kuona kuti MAZIKORO EMISANGANISIRANO uye MAZIKORO EMWE ZVINOFUNGA zvakadzika mumutemo weEVOLUTION.

Mafungiro emazuva ano pamusoro pekwakatangira munhu uye EVOLUTION yake yapfuura, kutaura zvazviri unyanzvi hwakachipa, haahwire padanho rakadzama rekutsoropodza.

Pasinei nekushaikwa kwedzidziso dzese dzaDARWIN dzinogamuchirwa sechinyorwa cheKUTENDA kusina musoro naCARLOS MARX uye NYAYA yake yakawanda yeMATERIALISM DIALECTIC, masayendisiti emazuva ano hapana chavanoziva nezve kwakatangira munhu, hapana chavanogona kusimbisa, hapana chavakamboita zvakananga uye havana humbowo hwakananga, hwakajeka, pamusoro peHUMAN EVOLUTION.

Mukusiyana, kana tikatora rudzi rwevanhu rwekare, ndiko kuti, makore zviuru makumi maviri kana makumi matatu apfuura Kristu, tinowana humbowo hwakajeka, zviratidzo zvisingakanganwiki zvemurume wepamusoro, zvisinganzwisisike kuvanhu vemazuva ano, uye kuvapo kwavo kunogona kuratidzwa nehumboo hwakawanda, maJeroglyph ekare, maPyramids ekare kwazvo, monoliti isinganzwisisike, mapapyrus asinganzwisisike uye zvivakwa zvakasiyana-siyana zvekare.

Kana iri reMUNHU APFUURA KARE, kuzvisikwa izvo zvisinganzwisisike uye zvisinganzwisisike zvinoita sezvakafanana neANIMAL INTELECTUAL uye zvisinei zvakasiyana, zvakasiyana zvakadaro, zvisinganzwisisike uye mapfupa azvo anopenya akavigwa zvakadzika dzimwe nguva munzvimbo dzekare dzeGlacial kana Preglacial nguva, masayendisiti emazuva ano hapana chavanoziva nenzira chaiyo uye kuburikidza neruzivo rwakananga.

SCIENCE YEGNOSTIC inodzidzisa kuti ANIMAL RACIONAL sezvatinoziva, haisi SER PERFECT, haisati iri MUNHU mupfungwa yakazara yezwi; zvakasikwa zvinomuita kusvika pamwero wakati uye zvobva zvamusiya akasununguka zvachose kuti aenderere mberi nekukura kwake kana kurasikirwa nemikana yake yese ndokukanganisa.

MITEMO yeEVOLUTION neINVOLUTION ndiyo axis yemichina yezvisikwa zvese uye haina chekuita neAUTOREALIZATION yepamoyo yeSER.

Mukati meANIMAL INTELECTUAL mune mikana mikuru inogona kukura kana kurasika, haisi mutemo kuti ikure. The EVOLUTIVE mechanics haigone kuikudziridza.

Kukura kwemikana yakavanzika yakadaro, kunongogoneka mumamiriro akanyatsotsanangurwa uye izvi zvinoda SUPER-KUSHANDA kukuru kwemunhu uye rubatsiro runobudirira kubva kuna MASTERS avo vakatogadzira basa iroro munguva yakapfuura.

Ani nani anoda kukudziridza mikana yake yese yakavanzika kuti ave munhu, anofanira kupinda munzira yeREVOLUTION YEKUZIVA.

ANIMAL INTELECTUAL IYO GRAIN, MBEU; kubva kumbeu iyoyo MUTI WOUpenyu, MUNHU WECHOKWADI anogona kuzvarwa, MUNHU iyeye aitsvakwa naDIOGENES nerambi rakabatidzwa mumigwagwa yeATHENS uye masikati machena uye zvinosuruvarisa kuti aisakwanisa kumuwana.

HAISI MUTEMO kuti tsanga iyi, kuti mbeu iyi yakakosha kudaro igone kukura, zvakajairika, zvakasikwa ndezvekuti irasike.

MUNHU WECHOKWADI akasiyana neANIMAL INTELECTUAL, sezvinoita MWENJE kugore.

Kana tsanga ikasafa mbeu haimeri, zvinodikanwa, zviri kukurumidza kuti EGO, I, IYENI IFARE, kuti MUNHU azvarwe.

Varairidzi neVarairidzi veZvikoro, Makoreji neYunivhesiti, vanofanira kudzidzisa vadzidzi vavo nzira yeETHICS YEMAkurumidza, ndiyo chete nzira yekuwana kufa kweEGO.

Tichisimbisa tinogona kutaura kuti REVOLUTION YEKUZIVA haisi yega munyika ino, asi iri kuramba ichiwedzera kuwanda.

REVOLUTION YEKUZIVA ine zvinhu zvitatu zvakanyatsotsanangurwa: Chekutanga, Kufa; Chechipiri, Kuzvarwa; Chechitatu, Kuzvipira kuvanhu. Hurongwa hwezvinhu hahushandure chigadzirwa.
