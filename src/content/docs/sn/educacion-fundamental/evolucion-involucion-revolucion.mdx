Mukuita, takakwanisa kuona kuti ZVIKORO ZVEMATERIALIST pamwe neZVIKORO ZVEMWEZIRO zvakavharirwa zvachose muDOGMA yeEVOLUTION.

Mafungiro emazuva ano pamusoro pekwakatangira munhu uye EVOLUTION yake yekare, muchokwadi, unyengeri hwakachipa, haumiri pachinzvimbo chakadzama chekutsoropodza.

Pasinei nemadzidziso ese aDARWIN anogamuchirwa sechinyorwa cheKUTENDA kwemapofu naCARLOS MARX uye MATERIALISM yake yeDIALECTICAL inotaurwa zvakanyanya, masayendisiti emazuva ano hapana chaanoziva pamusoro pekwakatangira munhu, hapana chavanotoziva, hapana chavakaratidza zvakananga uye havana humbowo chaihwo, hwakananga, pamusoro peHUMAN EVOLUTION.

Mukupesana, kana tikatora rudzi rwevanhu rwenhoroondo, kureva, makore zviuru makumi maviri kana makumi matatu apfuura Kristu asati avapo, tinowana humbowo chaihwo, zviratidzo zvisingarasikirwe zverudzi rwepamusoro rwevanhu, rusinganzwisisike kuvanhu vemazuva ano, uye kuvepo kwaro kunogona kuratidzwa neuchapupu hwakawanda, maJeroglyph ekare, mapiramidi ekare kwazvo, matombo anoshamisa, mapepa emanyoro asinganzwisisike uye zvivakwa zvakasiyana siyana zvekare.

Kana ari MUNHU WEMAZUVA EKARE, kuzvisikwa zvisingazivikanwe uye zvinoshamisa zvakafanana neMHUKA INE NJERE asi zvakasiyana, zvakasiyana, zvisinganzwisisike uye mapfupa azvo ane mukurumbira akavigwa zvakadzika dzimwe nguva munzvimbo dzekare dzenguva yeGlacial kana Preglaciar, masayendisiti emazuva ano hapana chaanoziva zvakananga uye kuburikidza neruzivo rwakananga.

SAYENZI yeGNOSTIC inodzidzisa kuti MHUKA YAKANAKA sezvatinoziva, haisi SERI YAKAKWANA, haisati iri MUNHU mupfungwa yakazara yeshoko; hunhu hunoikudziridza kusvika pamwero wakati wobva waisiya yakasununguka zvachose kuti ienderere mberi nekukura kwayo kana kurasikirwa nemikana yayo yese uye kuparara.

MIrawo yeEVOLUTION neINVOLUTION ndiyo axis yemuchina wezvose zvakasikwa uye haina chekuita neAUTOREALIZATION YEPEDYODYO yeSERI.

Mukati meMHUKA INE NJERE mune mikana mikuru inogona kukura kana kurasika, hazvisi mutemo kuti ikure. Magetsi eEVOLUTION haakwanise kuakudziridza.

Kukura kwemikana yakadaro yakavanzika, kunobvira chete mumamiriro ezvinhu akatsanangurwa zvakanaka uye izvi zvinoda KUSIMBISWA kwemunhu mumwe nemwe uye rubatsiro runobudirira kubva kuVadzidzisi vakatoita basa iroro munguva yakapfuura.

Ani nani anoda kukudziridza mikana yake yese yakavanzika kuti ave munhu, anofanira kupinda munzira yeREVOLUTION YEKUZIVA.

MHUKA INE NJERE itsanga, MBEU; kubva pambeu iyoyo panogona kuzvarwa MUTI WOUpenyu, MUNHU WECHOKWADI, MUNHU iyeye aitsvakwa naDIOGENES nerambi rakabatidzwa mumigwagwa yeATHENS uye masikati machena uye zvinosuruvarisa kuti akatadza kuwana.

HAISI MUTEMO kuti tsanga iyi, kuti mbeu iyi yakakosha yakadaro ikure, zvakajairika, zvakasikwa ndezvekuti irasike.

MUNHU WECHOKWADI akasiyana neMHUKA INE NJERE, sezvinoita MUNYAI kugore.

Kana tsanga ikasafa mbeu haimeri, zvinodikanwa, zvinokurumidza kuti EGO ife, I, IYE MWENE, paca kuti MUNHU azvarwe.

Varairidzi neVarairidzi veZvikoro, Makoreji neMayunivhesiti, vanofanirwa kudzidzisa vadzidzi vavo NZIRA yeETHICS YOKUPANDUKA, chete ndipo pazvinobvira kuwana kufa kweEGO.

Tichisimbisa tinogona kutaura kuti REVOLUTION YEKUZIVA haisi yega munyika ino, asi inowedzera kushomeka uye kushomeka.

KUFA inyaya yeETHICS YOKUPANDUKA uye KUDZIVIRIRA kweYO PSYCHOLOGICAL.

KUZVARWA inyaya yeTRANSMUTATION YEPABONDO, nyaya iyi inoenderana neTRANSCENDENTAL SEXOLOGY, ani nani anoda kudzidza nyaya iyi, anofanira kutinyorera uye kuziva mabhuku edu eGnostic.
