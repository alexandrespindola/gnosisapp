"Dzidzo yeMusimboti" isayenzi inotibvumira kuziva hukama hwedu nevanhu, nezvisikwa, nezvinhu zvese. Kuburikidza nesayenzi iyi, tinoziva mashandiro epfungwa nekuti pfungwa chishandiso che ruzivo uye tinofanirwa kudzidza kushandisa chishandiso ichocho, icho chiri hwaro hwe psychological yangu.

Mubhuku rino, tinodzidziswa nzira yekufunga nayo nenzira yakatwasuka, kuburikidza nekutsvaga, kuongorora, kunzwisisa nekufungisisa.

Inotizivisa, maitiro ekuvandudza ndangariro dze memory nguva dzose tichishandisa zvinhu zvitatu: munhu, chinhu nenzvimbo; memory inofambiswa nekufarira, saka unofanira kuisa kufarira mune zvaunodzidza kuti zvinyorwe mu memory. Memory inovandudzwa kuburikidza ne maitiro e alchemy transmutation iyo vadzidzi vanofarira kuvandudza hunhu hwavo vachazoziva zvishoma nezvishoma.

Kune vekuMadokero, kudzidza kunotanga pazera re 6 kana kuti pavanofungidzirwa kuti vanonzwisisa; kune vekuMabvazuva, kunyanya maHindu, dzidzo inotanga kubva pakuzvara; kune maGnostic kubva pakudanana, kureva kuti, kusati kwabata pamuviri.

Dzidzo yeramangwana ichabatanidza nhanho mbiri: imwe inotungamirirwa nevabereki uye imwe inotungamirirwa nevadzidzisi. Dzidzo yeramangwana ichaisa vadzidzi muzivo yeHumwari yekudzidza kuva vanababa nanaamai. Chinhu chinodiwa nemukadzi ndechekudzivirirwa, kuchengetwa, ndosaka musikana achinyanya kunamatira kubaba paanenge ari musikana nekuti anomuona akasimba uye ane simba; mukomana anoda rudo, kutarisirwa, kurerukirwa, ndosaka mukomana achinyanya kunamatira amai nekuda kwechimiro. Gare gare, pfungwa dze vese dzinoshatiswa, mukadzi anotarisira murume akanaka kana murume anomuda, apo iye ndiye anofanira kupa rudo, uye, murume anotarisira mukadzi ane nzira dzekurarama nadzo kana kuti ane basa; kune vamwe kumeso nemiviri zvinonyanya kukosha kune pfungwa dzavo.

Zvinoshamisa kuona mabhuku ezvikoro, basa rega rega rine zviuru zve mibvunzo, iyo munyori anopindura akanyora kuti vadzidzi vagodzidza nazvo nemusoro, memory isina kuvimbika ndiyo inochengetera ruzivo runodzidzwa nevese vechidiki nesimba rakakura, dzidzo iyoyo yekunyama chete inovakwanisa kuzviwanira raramo pavanopedza zvidzidzo zvavo, asi havazivi chinhu nezve hupenyu hwavari kuzorarama, vanopinda mahuri vakapofumadzwa, havana kana kudzidziswa kubereka mhando yacho nenzira yakakudzwa, dzidziso iyoyo inotungamirirwa nematsotsi mumumvuri wehutsinye.

Zvinodiwa kuti jaya rinzwisise kuti mbeu inobudisa muviri wemunhu, ndicho chinhu chakakosha zvikuru kuhupenyu hwemunhu (mhando), yakakomborerwa uye naizvozvo kushandiswa kwayo kusina kunaka kuchakanganisa vana vayo. Paatari dzeChechi yeKatorike, host inochengetwa mutabernake rakaropafadzwa nekuremekedzwa kukuru se inomiririra muviri waKristu, chifananidzo Chitsvene ichocho; inoumbwa nembewu yegorosi. Paatari mhenyu, kureva muviri wedu wepanyama, mbeu yedu inotora nzvimbo ye host tsvene yechiKristu inotevera Kristu Wenhoroondo; mumbewu yedu pachedu tinochengeta Kristu mune zvese avo vanotevera Kristu mupenyu anorarama uye anorova pasi pembeu yedu pachedu.

Nekufarira kukuru tinoona kuti varimi vanoona nezvezivo yezvirimwa zvinoshandira vanhu, vanodzidzisa varimi kuremekedza mbeu dzavanodiridza muminda, tinoona kuti vavandudza kunaka kwembewu kubudisa goho rakanaka, vachichengeta mukati masilos makuru zvavanazvo zvezviyo, kuti mbeu dzavakatamburira kugadzira dzirege kurasika. Tinoona maitiro anoita vanachiremba vemhuka, vane basa rekutarisira hupenyu hwemhuka, vakakwanisa kuburitsa mhuka dzinobereka kana zviyero zvinodhura zana kupfuura chigadzirwa chenyama, chinoratidza kuti imbeu yavanogadzira, ndicho chikonzero chemutengo wakakura kudaro. Mushonga wepamutemo chete, une hanya nemarudzi evanhu, hautitauri chinhu nezve kuvandudzwa kwembeu; isu tinochema kunonoka uku uye tinozivisa vaverengi vedu kuti mbeu yevanhu iri nyore kuvandudza, nekushandisa nguva dzose zvekudya zvitatu zvekutanga: kuburikidza nezvatinofunga, zvatino fema uye zvatiri kudya. Kana tikafunga chete nezvezvinhu zvisina kujeka, zvinhu zvisina maturo, zvisina basa, mbeu yatinoburitsa ichadaro nekuti kufunga kwakakosha pakugadzirwa ikoko. Jaya rinodzidza rakasiyana neaya asingadzidziswi muchitarisiko nekuvapo, pane shanduko muhunhu; Chokwadi chekufema doro rakagayiwa mumabhawa nemakantina, chinotarisa hupenyu hwevatengi vanowanzoenda kunzvimbo idzodzo: Vanhu vanodya makeke, nyama yenguruve, doro, zvinonhuwirira, doro uye chikafu chinonyandura, vanorarama hupenyu hwekushuvira hunovatungamirira kuhupombwe.

Mhuka yese yeupombwe inonhuwa: madhongi, nguruve, mbudzi uye kunyangwe huku kunyangwe dziri shiri, semukono wepamba. Musiyano uripo pakati pevanhu vanoita upombwe neavo vanoitwa vanotoora nevanhu kuti vazvishandise unogona kuonekwa zviri nyore, ona magonads ebhiza remujaho kune ebhiza remutoro, pakati penzombe dzekurwisa nenhombe dzinobuda mumapepanhau zuva nezuva, berraco kana nguruve hombe, kunyangwe mumhuka diki sen'amba inonyanya kuchiva uye chitarisiko chayo chinogara chichinyangadza, chinhu chakafanana chinoitika kumurume anoita upombwe anovhara kunhuwa kwake nezvinonhuwirira nezvinonhuwirira. Kana munhu akazviita akachena, akachena uye mutsvene, mukufunga, kutaura uye kuita, anodzosa hudiki hwakarasika, anozvinatsa mumuviri nemweya uye muviri wake hauburitse kunhuwa.

Dzidzo yepamuviri inowanikwa sei? Izvi zvinoitika pakati pemiviri inotevedzera kuchena, kureva, kuti haimbofi yakarasikirwa nembewu yayo mukuzvidza nekunakidzwa kwechinguvana, saka: Varoora vanoda kupa muviri kune chisikwa chitsva, vanobvumirana uye vanokumbira Denga kuti vatungamirirwe kuchitiko che kubereka, ipapo mumafungiro erudo rwechigarire vanogara pamwe chete vachifara uye vakafara, vanotora mukana wenguva iyo zvisikwa zvinonyanya kupa, sezvinoita varimi pakudyara, vanoshandisa maitiro e alchemy transmutation vachibatana pamwe chete semurume nemukadzi, iyo inobvumira kutiza kwespermatozoa yakasimba uye ine simba, yakavandudzwa nemaitiro anozivikanwa kare uye nekudaro chiitiko chekubata pamuviri kwoumwari chinoitika, kana mukadzi angonzwa kuti ane pamuviri, anobva kumurume, kureva kuti, hupenyu hwewanano hunopera, izvi zvinofanira kuitwa nyore nyore nemurume akachena nekuti azere nenyasha nemasimba makuru evanhu, nenzira dzose anofarira hupenyu kumukadzi wake kuti arege kushandisa kunetsa kana zvinhu zvakafanana nekuti zvese izvi zvinokanganisa fetus iri kukura, kana izvi zvichikonzera kukuvadzwa, chii chisingave kumusangano wemuviri unobatwa nenzira yevashuvi nevanhu vasina kumbobvira vagamuchira zano mupfungwa iyi? Izvo zvinopa zvikonzero zvekuti vana vazhinji vanzwe zvido zvinotyisa kubva pazera diki uye vanonyadzisa amai vavo nenzira inonyadzisa.

Amai vanoziva kuti vari kupa hupenyu kune chisikwa chitsva chavanochengeta muTembere yavo Mhenyu, seguyo rinokosha, vachipa neminyengetero yavo nekufunga mafomu akanaka achakudza chisikwa chitsva, kozouya chiitiko chekusununguka chisingarwadzi; nenzira yakapfava uye yechisikigo kukudzwa kwevabereki vayo. Vaviri vacho vanodya chikafu chinowanzova chemazuva makumi mana kusvika dumbu rakashandiswa sechigaro chechisikwa chitsva radzokera panzvimbo yaro, murume anoziva kuti mukadzi ari kurera mwana anofanira kuva dzimimai nokucherechedzwa, nokubata kwakanaka nokuti chero nzira yechido inokuvadza mazamu amai uye inounza kuvharika mumachannel kunoyerera mvura inokosha ichapa hupenyu, kumwana we dumbu rake, mukadzi anoda kuisa murairo uyu muchiito achaona kunyara kwekushanda mazamu kunyangarika nekuda kwekuvhara kusingaperi. Apo pane kuchena pane rudo nokuteerera, vana vanomuka nenzira yomuzvarirwo uye uipi hwose hunonyangarika, saizvozvo hunotanga idzodzo dzidzo huru nokuda kwokugadzirira unhu hwechiumbwa chitsva icho chatoenda kuchikoro chakagadzirirwa kutevera dzidzo ichamubvumira kugarisana uye gare gare kuzviwanira chingwa chezuva nezuva.
