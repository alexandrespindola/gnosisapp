Huwandu hwevechidiki hunokamurwa kuita nguva mbiri dzemakore manomwe imwe neimwe. Nguva yekutanga inotanga pazera ramakore makumi maviri nerimwe uye inoguma pamakumi maviri nemasere. Nguva yechipiri inotanga pamakumi maviri nemasere uye inopera pamakumi matatu nemashanu.

Nheyo dzehuwandu hwevechidiki dzinowanikwa mumusha, chikoro nemumugwagwa. Huwandu hwevechidiki hwakavakirwa panheyo yeDZIDZO YEKUTANGA zvirokwazvo hunovaka uye hunokosha mukukudza.

Huwandu hwevechidiki hwakavakirwa panheyo dzenhema, semhedzisiro inonzwisisika, inzira isiriyo.

Varume vazhinji vanoshandisa chikamu chekutanga chehupenyu kuita kuti chikamu chasara chive chinoshungurudza.

Vechidiki nekuda kwemafungiro asiriyo ehurume hwenhema vanowanzowira mumaoko evahure.

Kudarika kwehuwandu hwevechidiki itsamba dzinotenderedzwa pamusoro peuchembere dzinobhadharwa nemari inodhura zvikuru mumakore makumi matatu.

Pasina DZIDZO YEKUTANGA huwandu hwevechidiki hunova hudhakwa husingaperi: imhando yemafivha ekukanganisa, doro uye chishuwo chechipfuwo.

Zvese izvo murume anofanira kuve muhupenyu hwake zvinowanikwa mugwara rekukura kwemakore makumi matatu ekutanga ehupenyu.

Pazviito zvikuru zvevanhu zvese zvatinazvo ruzivo nazvo, munguva dzekare sedzedu, ruzhinji rwezviito izvozvo zvakatangwa zvisati zvaitwa makore makumi matatu.

Murume asvika makore makumi matatu dzimwe nguva anonzwa sekunge ari kubuda muhondo huru umo akaona vazhinji vake vachidonha mumwe mushure memumwe.

Pamakore makumi matatu varume nevakadzi vatotorerwa simba ravo rese uye shungu dzavo, uye kana vakatadza mumabhizinesi avo ekutanga, vanozadzwa nekushushikana uye vanosiya mutambo.

Zvinofungidzirwa zvekukura zvinotevera zvinofungidzirwa zvehuduku. Pasina Dzidzo Yekutanga nhaka yeuchembere inowanzova kuora mwoyo.

Huwandu hwevechidiki hunokurumidza kupfuura. Runako ndirwo rupo rwehuwandu hwevechidiki, asi ihwo hunonyengera, hahugare.

Huwandu hwevechidiki hune Njere mhenyu uye Kutonga kusina simba. Vashoma muhupenyu vechidiki veKutonga kwakasimba uye Njere mhenyu.

Pasina DZIDZO YEKUTANGA vechidiki vanova vanoshuva, vakadhakwa, matsotsi, vanotsvinya, vane ruchiva, vane tsvina, makororo, vanochiva, vane godo, vanovenga, magangster, mbavha, vanozvitutumadza, vane usimbe, nezvimwewo.

Ujana izuva rezhizha rinokurumidza kuvanda. Vechidiki vanoda kupambadza hunhu hwakakosha hweujana.

Vabereki vazhinji vemhuri vane godo nevanasikana zvotyisa uye havadi kuti vave nevarume. Maitiro akadaro haana musoro zana muzana nekuti vasikana vanoda kuva nemurume uye kuroora.

Mhedzisiro yekushaikwa kwekunzwisisa kwakadaro varume muchivande, mumugwagwa, nengozi yekuwira mumaoko emudzingwi anonyengera.

Vasikana vanofanira kugara vakasununguka kuva nemurume wavo, asi nekuti havati vapedza IYE, zvinokurudzirwa kusavasiya vari voga nemurume.

Vechidiki nevasikana vanofanira kuva nerusununguko rwekuita mapati avo kumba. Zvinovaraidza zvakanaka hazvikuvadzi munhu uye Ujana hunoda kuva nezvinovaraidza.
