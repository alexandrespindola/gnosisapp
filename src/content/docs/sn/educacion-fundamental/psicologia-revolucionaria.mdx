Vadzidzisi veZvikoro, Makoroji, neYunivhesiti, vanofanira kunyatso dzidza DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU INOSHANDUKA, inodzidziswa ne SANGANO RAPASI ROSE REGNÓSTICO.

Dzidziso ye Pfungwa ye CHIMURENGA chiri kuitika yakasiyana zvakanyanya nezvese zvazvino zivikanwa nezita iri.

Pasina mubvunzo, tinogona kutaura tisingatyi kukanganisa kuti mumazana emakore atakatangira, kubva murima rakadzika remakore ese, DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU haina kumbobvira yawa kusvika pazasi sezvazviri munguva ino ye "MUKOMANA ANO PANDUKIRA PASINA CHIKONZERO" nemagamba eROCK.

Dzidziso ye Pfungwa inovhiringidza uye inopesana yenguva dzazvino ino yarasikirwa zvinosiririsa nechinangwa chayo chekuvepo, uye nekuonana kwakananga nekwakabva kwayo kwechokwadi.

Munguva dzino dze Kuora kwetsika dzezvepabonde uye nekukanganiswa kwakazara kwepfungwa, hazviiti chete kutsanangura nemazvo zita rekuti DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU asiwo nyaya dzinokosha dze DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU hadzizivikanwi zvechokwadi.

Avo vanofunga zvisirizvo kuti DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU isayenzi yezuva ranhasi, vakavhiringidzika zvechokwadi nekuti DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU isayenzi yekare kwazvo ine mavambo ayo muzvikoro zvekare zve ZVAKAVANZIKA ZVEKARE.

Kumunhu werudzi rwe SNOB, imbavha yazvino uno, inovhiringidza, hazvibviri kutsanangura izvo zvinozivikanwa seDZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU nekuti kunze kwenguva ino yazvino, zviri pachena kuti DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU haina kumbobvira yavapo pasi pezita rayo pachayo nekuda kwezvikonzero zvakadaro kana zvakadaro, yaimbogara ichifungirwa nezve maitiro ekupandukira ehunhu hwezvematongerwo enyika kana Zvechitendero uye nokudaro yakazviona iri mukudiwa kwekuzvivanza nenguo dzakawanda.

Kubva panguva dzekare, munzvimbo dzakasiyana-siyana dzenhandare yehupenyu, DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU yakagara ichimiririra basa rayo, yakavanzwa neuchenjeri nenguo dzeuzivi.

Kumahombekombe eGanges, muIndia Tsvene yeMAVEDAS, kubva usiku hunotyisa hwemazana emakore, kune nzira dzeYOGA idzo, mukati, dzinongova DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU INOSHINGA, yezviroto zvikuru.

MaYOGA manomwe agara achitsanangurwa senzira, maitiro, kana masisitimu ehuzivi.

Munyika yeArab, dzidziso dzinoyera dzeMASUFI, dzimwe metafiziki, dzimwe dzezvitendero, chaizvoizvo dzechimiro cheDZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU zvachose.

MuEurope yekare yakaozwa kusvika mumapfupa nezvehondo zhinji, rusarura, zvitendero, zvematongerwo enyika, nezvimwewo. kusvika pakupera kwezana ramakore rapfuura, DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU yakazvivanza nenguvo yeUchenjeri kuti isazivikanwa.

Uchenjeri kunyange zvazvo hune migove yose nemigove, zvakadai sa Logic, dzidziso yezivo, tsika, Aesthetics, etc., pasina mubvunzo pachayo, KUZVIONA ZVIRI PACENA, KUZIVA KWAKA DZAMA KWE CHIRIPO, MASHANDIRO EKUZIVA EKUMUKA KWEpfungwa.

Kukanganisa kweZVIKORO ZVIZHINJI ZVEUZIVI ndiko kufunga nezve dzidziso yepfungwa sechimwe chinhu chakaderera kune UCHENJERI, sechimwe chinhu chine chekuita chete nezvinhu zvakaderera uye kunyange zvisina basa zvechimiro chevanhu.

Kufunda kwakasanganisirwa kweZvitendero kunotibvumira kusvika pamhedziso ine musoro yekuti SAYENZI YEDZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU yagara ichibatanidzwa zvakanyanya kune DZIDZISO DZESE DZechitendero. Chero kudzidza kwakasanganisirwa kweZvitendero kunoratidza kuti mubhuku dzvene rinonyanya Orthodox renyika dzakasiyana uye nguva dzakasiyana, kune hupfumi hunoshamisa hwesayenzi yeDZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU.

Tsvakurudzo yakadzama munharaunda yeGNOSTICISM inotitendera kuti tiwaneunganidzwa unoshamisa wevanyori vakasiyana veGnostic unobva kumazuva ekutanga eChikristu uye unozivikanwa pasi pezita rekuti PHILOKALIA, ichiri kushandiswa nhasi muCHECHI YEKUMABVIRIRA, kunyanya kudzidzisa mamongi.

Pasina mubvunzo uye pasina kutya kudonha mukunyengedzwa, tinogona kusimbisa zvakasimba kuti PHILOKALIA inonyanya kunakidza DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU.

MuZVIKORO ZVEKARE ZVEZVAKAVANZIKA zveGreece, Egypt, Rome, India, Persia, Mexico, Peru, Assyria, Caldea, etc. Etc. nezvimwe, DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU yakagara ichibatanidzwa neuzivi, unyanzvi hwechokwadi, sainzi uye Chitendero.

Munguva dzekare DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU yaivigwa neuchenjeri pakati pemhando dzakanaka dzeVatambi Vatsvene, kana pakati pezvakavanzika zveHieroglyph yekunze kana zvivezwa zvakanaka, kana munhetembo, kana munjodzi uye kunyange mumimhanzi inonakidza yetemberi.

Sayenzi, Uchenjeri, Unyanzvi, uye Chitendero zvisati zvaparadzana kuti zvitende zvakazvimirira, DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU yakatonga zvikuru muZVIKORO ZVESE ZVEKARE ZVEZVAKAVANZIKA.

Pakavharwa Makoroji Ekutanga nekuda kweKALIYUGA, kana ZERA RITEMA ratiri mazviri, DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU yakapona pakati pemifananidzo yeZVIKORO zvakasiyana-siyana zveESOTERIC uye SEUDO-ESOTERIC zveNyika Yazvino uno uye kunyanya pakati peGNÓSTICO ESOTERICISM.

Ongororo dzakadzama uye tsvakurudzo dzakadzama, dzinotitendera kuti tinzwisise zvakajeka kwazvo kuti masisitimu akasiyana-siyana uye dzidziso dze DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU dzakamboitika kare uye dziripo nhasi, dzinogona kukamurwa kuva mapoka maviri.

Rokutanga.- Dzidziso sezvinofungidzirwa nevanhu vakawanda vakadzidza. Dzidziso yepfungwa yevanhu yemazuva ano ndeyechokwadi yeboka iri.

Rechipiri.- Dzidziso dzinodzidza munhu maererano ne KUSHANDUKA KWEpfungwa.

Dzidziso dzekupedzisira idzi ndedzechokwadi dzidziso dzekutanga, dzekare kwazvo, ivo chete vanotitendera kuti tinzwisise mavambo ehupenyu hwe DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU uye kukosha kwayo kukuru.

Patinenge tasvika pakunzwisisa zvizere uye mumatanho ese epfungwa, kukosha kwakadini kwekudzidza munhu kubva pakuona kutsva kweKUSHANDUKA KWEpfungwa, tichazonzwisisa kuti DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU ndiyo dzidzo yezvirevo, mitemo uye chokwadi chinobatana zvakanyanya neKUSHANDUKA kukuru uye kwekupedzisira kwemunhu.

Zvinokosha kuti Vadzidzisi veZvikoro, Makoroji, neYunivhesiti, vanzwisise zvizere nguva yakakomba yatiri kurarama uye mamiriro anotyisa ekusaziva zve DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU umo chizvarwa chitsva chiri.

Zvakakosha kutungamirira "GUNGWA DZVA" munzira yeCHIMURENGA CHE PFUNGWA uye izvi zvinogoneka chete kuburikidza ne DZIDZISO YE PFUNGWA YEVANHU INOSHANDUKA yeDZIDZO YEPASI.
