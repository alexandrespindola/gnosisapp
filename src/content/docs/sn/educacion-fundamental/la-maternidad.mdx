Hupenyu hwemunhu hunotanga serussel imwe chete riri pasi, sezvazviri, kukurumidza kwazvo kwesero dzinorarama.

Kuzvara, nhumbu, kuzvarwa, nguva dzose ndivo vatatu vanoshamisa uye vanotyisa avo hupenyu hwechisikwa chipi nechipi hunotanga navo.

Zvinoshamisa zvechokwadi kuziva kuti nguva dzedu dzekutanga dzekuvapo tinofanira kurarama muzvisingaite zvidiki, tichishandurwa, mumwe nomumwe wedu kuita sero rimwe chete duku.

Tinotanga kuvapo muchimiro cheselo risingakoshi uye tinopedza hupenyu hwakwegura, vakuru uye vakaremerwa nendangariro.

IYE ndangariro. Vakwegura vakawanda havamborarami muzvino, vakwegura vakawanda vanongorarama vachiyeuka zvakapfuura. Murume mukuru wose inzwi nemumvuri chete. Munhu mukuru wose chipoko chezvakapfuura, ndangariro yakawanda uye ndiyo inoenderera mberi muGenes yevana vedu.

Kuzvarwa kwevanhu kunotanga nenguva dzinokurumidza zvisingaite, asi kuburikidza nemaitiro akasiyana-siyana ehupenyu anoenda achiwedzera kunonoka uye kunonoka.

Vaverengi vazhinji vanofanira kuyeuka hukama hwenguva. Chipembenene chiduku chinongorarama maawa mashomanana masikati echirimo, chinoita sokunge hachina kumborarama, asi chinorarama zvose zvinorarama munhu mumakore makumi masere, chinoitika ndechekuti chinorarama nokukurumidza, munhu anorarama mumakore makumi masere zvose zvinorarama nyika mumamiriyoni emakore.

Apo Zoospermo inosangana nezai, nhumbu inotanga. Selo rinotanga naro hupenyu hwomunhu, rine makromosomes makumi mana nemasere.

Makromosomes anokamurwa kuva majini, zana emakromosomes okutanga kana kuti chimwe chinhu zvirokwazvo zvinoumba izvo zviri Kromosome.

Majini akaoma kwazvo kudzidza nokuti rimwe nerimwe rinoumbwa nemamorekuru mashomanana anodedera nokukurumidza zvisingafungidziriki.

Nyika inoshamisa yeGenes inoumba nzvimbo iri pakati penyika ine mativi matatu nenyika yechina.

MuGenes mune maatomu enhaka. IYO PFUNGWA YEDU yevatateguru vedu, inouya kuzopararira zai rakaberekwa.

Munguva ino yeElectro-teknikhi nesainzi yeatomiki, hazvina kunyanya kunzwisisa kutaura kuti tsoka yemagetsi-magineti yakasiiwa nemadzitateguru akabudisa mweya wake wokupedzisira yakasvika kuzodhinda muGenes uye chromosomes yezai rakaberekwa nemuzukuru.

Nzira youpenyu yakavambwa netsoka dzematende ehondo yebhiza rorufu.

Mukati meupenyu, marudzi akasiyana-siyana esimba anoyerera mumuviri womunhu; rudzi rumwe norumwe rwesimba rune nzira yarwo yokuita nayo, rudzi rumwe norumwe rwesimba runozviratidza panguva yaro neawa.

Pashure pemwedzi miviri yokubata pamuviri tine basa rokugaya zvokudya uye pashure pemwedzi mina yokubata pamuviri simba rinofambisa rinobatanidzwa zvikuru nemaitiro okufema nemhasuru rinotanga kushanda.

Chiratidziro chesainzi chokuzvarwa nokufa kwezvinhu zvose chinoshamisa.

Nyanzvi dzakawanda dzinoti pane kufanana kwepedyo pakati pokuzvarwa kwechisikwa chomunhu nokuzvarwa kwenyika muzvikamu zvemuchadenga.

Pashure pemwedzi mipfumbamwe mwana anozvarwa, pashure pemwedzi gumi kukura kunotanga nezvose zvaanodya zvinoshamisa uye kukura kwakaringana uye kwakakwana kwemagadzirirwo emuviri.

Apo Fontanela yepamberi yevana vacheche inovhara pashure pemakore maviri kana matatu okukura, chiratidzo chokuti sisitimu youropi-musana yapera zvakakwana.

Vanasayenzi vakawanda vakati zvisikwa zvine fungidziro uye kuti fungidziro iyi inopa chimiro chipenyu kune zvose zviri, kune zvose zvave zviri kune zvose zvichava.

Vanhu vazhinji vanoseka fungidziro uye vamwe vanotoiisa "BENZI REMBA".

Pane kuvhiringidzika kukuru pamusoro peshoko rokuti FUNGIDZIRO uye pane vazhinji vanovhiringidza FUNGIDZIRO neFUNGIDZIRO.

Nyanzvi dzakati dzimwe dzinoti pane mafungiro maviri. Yokutanga vanoidana kuti FUNGIDZIRO YEMICHINA uye yechipiri vanoidana kuti FUNGIDZIRO YOKUDANIRA: Yokutanga inoumbwa nemarara epfungwa uye yechipiri inoenderana nezvatinazvo zvinokudzwa uye zvakanaka mukati.

Kuburikidza nekucherechedza uye ruzivo takakwanisa kuona kuti pane rudzi rweSUB-FUNGIDZIRO YEMICHINA INOSHUMISA INFRACONSCIOUS uye SUBJECTIVE.

Rudzi irworwo rweSUB-FUNGIDZIRO YEAUTOMATIC inoshanda pasi peNZVIMBO YEPFUNGA.

Mifananidzo yechingochani, cinema inoshungurudza, nhau dzinonaka dzinoreva zvakawanda, majee anoshungurudza, zvichingodaro, zvinowanzotanga kushandisa SUB-FUNGIDZIRO YEMICHINA zvisingazivi.

Ongororo yakadzama yakatiunzira kumhedziso ine musoro yokuti zviroto zvechingochani nokusvibiswa kwousiku zvinokonzerwa neSUB-FUNGIDZIRO YEMICHINA.

UCHENA HWAKAKWANA hahugoneki apo SUB-FUNGIDZIRO YEMICHINA ichiripo.

Zviri pachena zvakakwana kuti FUNGIDZIRO YEPFUNGA yakasiyana zvachose neizvo zvinonzi FUNGIDZIRO YEMICHINA, INOKANGANISA, ISINGARANGARIWI, INOKANGANISA. ZVINODZIKIRA.

Mumiririri chero upi zvawo unogona kuonekwa nenzira INOZVIKUZA uye inokudzwa, asi SUB-FUNGIDZIRO yerudzi rwemichina, isingarangarirwi, yakavanzika, isingarangarirwi inogona kutitengesa tichishanda otomatiki nemaitiro nemifananidzo ine mutserendende, inoshuva, yakanyudzwa.

Kana tichida UCHENA HWAZVOKUTANGA, hwakazara, hwakadzama, tinofanira kungwarira kwete FUNGIDZIRO YEPFUNGA chete, asiwo FUNGIDZIRO YEMICHINA neSUB-FUNGIDZIRO YISINGARANGARIWI, YEVANHU, YAKAVANZIKA, YAKANYUDZWA.

Hatifaniri kumbokanganwa hukama hwepedyo huripo pakati peBONDE NEFUNGIDZIRO.

Kuburikidza nokufungisisa kwakadzama tinofanira kushandura rudzi rwose rwefungidziro yemichina uye chimiro chiri chose cheSUB-FUNGIDZIRO neINFRA-FUNGIDZIRO YEAUTOMATIC, kuva FUNGIDZIRO YEPFUNGA, isinganetsi.

FUNGIDZIRO ISINGANETSI pachayo ndeyokutanga, pasina iyo muvambi angadai asina kukwanisa kufunga nezverunhare, redhiyo, ndege, zvichingodaro.

FUNGIDZIRO YEMUKADZI akazvitakura yakakosha pakukura kwemwana ari mudumbu. Zvinoratidzirwa kuti amai vose vanogona neFUNGIDZIRO yavo kuchinja pfungwa dzemwana ari mudumbu.

Zvinokosha kuti mukadzi akazvitakura aone mifananidzo yakanaka, nzvimbo dzakakwirira, uye ateerere mimhanzi yakanaka nemashoko ane mwero, nokudaro anogona kushanda pamusoro pepingwa yeuipi hwaanotakura mudumbu rake nenzira inowirirana.

Mukadzi akazvitakura haafaniri kunwa doro, kana kusvuta, kana kuona zvisina kunaka, zvisingafadzi nokuti izvi zvose zvinokuvadza kukura kwakanaka kwechisikwa.

Unofanira kuziva kukanganwira zvido zvose nezvikanganiso zvomukadzi akazvitakura.

Varume vazhinji vasingashiviriri uye vasina kunzwisisa kwechokwadi, vanotsamwa uye vanotuka mukadzi akazvitakura. Kusuruvara kwake, kushungurudzika kunokonzerwa nomurume asina kunaka, kunokanganisa fetus iri mudumbu, kwete chete mumuviri asiwo mupfungwa.

Tichifunga nezvesimba refungidziro yekusika, zvine musoro kutaura kuti mukadzi akazvitakura haafaniri kutarisa zvisina kunaka, zvisingafadzi, zvisingawirirani, zvinonyangadza, zvichingodaro.

Nguva yasvika yokuti hurumende dzinofanira kunetseka nokugadzirisa matambudziko makuru ane chokuita noumai.

Hazvienderani kuti munzanga inozvidaidzira kuti yechiKristu nedemocracy, hazvizivikanwi kuremekedza nokuremekedza kunzwisisa kworudzidziso rweuamai. Zvinotyisa kuona zviuru zvevakadzi vakazvitakura vasina rubatsiro rwupi zvarwo, vakasiiwa nomurume nenzanga, vachipemha chimedu chezvingwa kana kuti basa uye vachiita kazhinji kazhinji mabasa akaoma omuviri, kuitira kuti vapone nechisikwa chavanotakura mudumbu mavo.

Mamiriro aya asina kukodzera munhu enharaunda yazvino, hutsinye uhwu nekushomeka kwemutoro kwevatungamiriri nevanhu zviri kutiratidza zvakajeka kuti democracy haisati iripo.

Zvipatara nemakamuri avo okuberekera hazvisati zvagadzirisa dambudziko, nokuti vakadzi vanogona kungosvika kuzvipatara izvozvo apo kuzvara kwava pedyo.

Pane kudikanwa kwedzimba dzokugara pamwe chete nokukurumidza, maguta chaiwoiwo eminda akashongedzerwa nemahoro nemisha yevakadzi vari munhamu vakazvitakura, makiriniki nemaquindes evana vavo.

Dzimba dzokugara pamwe chete idzi dziri kugara vakadzi vari munhamo vakazvitakura, vakazadzwa nemhando dzose dzounhu, maruva, mimhanzi, kuwirirana, runako, zvichingodaro, zvingagadzirisa zvachose dambudziko guru roumai.

Tinofanira kunzwisisa kuti nzanga yavanhu imhuri huru uye kuti hapana dambudziko rekunze nokuti dambudziko rose mune imwe nzira kana imwe rinokanganisa nhengo dzose dzenzanga mukati medenderedzwa ravo. Hazvina maturo kusarura vakadzi vakazvitakura nemhaka yokuti vari munhamo. Zvinotyisa kuvazvidza, kuvazvidza kana kuti kuvapinza mumusha wevarombo.

Munjanga iyi matinorarama hatigoni kuva navanakomana navanasikana vokurera, nokuti tose tiri vanhu uye tinemirairo yakafanana.

Tinofanira kusika democracy yechokwadi, kana tisingadi kumedzwa neCommunism zvechokwadi.
