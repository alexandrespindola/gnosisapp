Kuzvara, nhumbu, kuberekwa, inogara iri nzira inoshamisa uye inotyisa iyo hupenyu hwechisikwa chipi nechipi hunotanga nayo.

Zvinoshamisa chaizvo kuziva kuti nguva dzedu dzekutanga dzekuvapo dzinofanirwa kugara mukati mediki risingaperi, tichishandurwa, mumwe nemumwe wedu kuita sero rimwe chete diki.

Tinotanga kuvapo muchimiro chesero risingakoshi uye tinopedza hupenyu tave vacheche, vakwegura uye takatakurwa nemarangaridzo.

Nyika inoshamisa yeGenes inoumba nzvimbo iri pakati penyika ine mativi matatu nenyika yechina.

Nzira yehupenyu yakagadzirwa netsoka dzetsoka dzabhiza rerufu.

Mukati mehupenyu, mhando dzakasiyana dzesimba dzinoyerera nemumuviri wemunhu; rudzi rwesimba rwega rwega rune nzira yarwo yekuita, rudzi rwesimba rwega rwega runozviratidza munguva yaro nenguva yaro.

Pashure pemwedzi miviri yekuzvarwa tine basa rekugaya uye pashure pemwedzi mina yekuzvarwa simba rinofambisa rinobatana zvakanyanya nemaitiro ekufema nemamhasuru rinoita.

Zvinoshamisa kuona sainzi yekuberekwa nekufa kwezvinhu zvese.

Nyanzvi zhinji dzinoti pane kufanana kwepedyo pakati pekuzvarwa kwechisikwa chemunhu nekuzvarwa kwenyika munzvimbo yekunze.

Pashure pemwedzi mipfumbamwe mwana anoberekwa, pashure pemwedzi gumi kukura kunotanga nemetabolism yayo yose inoshamisa uye kukura kwakafanana uye kwakakwana kwemamashu.

Apo Fontanel yepamberi yevacheche inovhara pashure pemakore maviri kana matatu, chiratidzo chokuti tsika yehuropi-musana yakapedzwa zvakakwana.

Masayendisiti mazhinji akataura kuti hunhu hune fungidziro uye kuti iyi fungidziro inopa chimiro chipenyu kune zvese zviri, kune zvese zvave zviripo kune zvese zvichazovapo.

Vanhu vazhinji vanoseka fungidziro uye vamwe vanotoiti "MAD WOMAN OF THE HOUSE".

Pane kuvhiringika kwakawanda pamusoro peshoko rokuti FUNGIDZIRO uye vazhinji vanovhiringidza FUNGIDZIRO NE FANTASY.

Vamwe vanhu vakachenjera vanoti kune fungidziro mbiri. Yekutanga inonzi MECHANICAL IMAGINATION uye yechipiri inonzi INTENTIONAL IMAGINATION: Yekutanga inoumbwa nemarara epfungwa uye yechipiri inoenderana nezvinhu zvakakodzera uye zvine hunhu zvatiri mukati.

Kuburikidza nekucherechedza uye ruzivo takakwanisa kuona kuti kune zvakare rudzi rweSUB-MECHANICAL MORBID INFRA-CONSCIOUS uye SUBJECTIVE IMAGINATION.

Iyo mhando yeSUB-AUTOMATIC IMAGINATION inoshanda pazasi peINTELLECTUAL ZONE.

Mifananidzo yeerotic, mafirimu ane hutsinye, nyaya dzinonaka dzine revo mbiri, majee ane hutsinye, nezvimwewo, zvinowanzoisa SUB-MECHANICAL IMAGINATION kushanda zvisingaite.

Ongororo yakadzama yakatiunzira kumhedziso ine musoro yekuti kurota kweerotic uye kusvibiswa kwehusiku kunokonzerwa neSUB-MECHANICAL IMAGINATION.

TSVENE YAKAKWANA haigoneke chero bedzi SUB-MECHANICAL IMAGINATION iripo.

Zvakajeka zvachose kuti CONSCIOUS IMAGINATION yakasiyana zvachose neiyo inonzi MECHANICAL, SUBJECTIVE, INFRA-CONSCIOUS IMAGINATION. SUBCONSCIOUS.

Chero kumiririrwa kunogona kuonekwa muAUTO-ENHANCING uye nzira inokudza, asi SUB-IMAGINATION yerudzi rwemichina, infraconscious, subconscious, isingazivi inogona kutinyengera nekushanda otomatiki nemamwe uye mifananidzo inonakidza, ine chido, yakanyudzwa.

Kana tichida INTEGRA CASTITY, uni-total, of background, tinoda kutarisa kwete chete CONSCIOUS IMAGINATION, asiwo MECHANICAL IMAGINATION uye INCONSCIOUS, AUTOMATIC, SUBCONSCIOUS, SUBMERGED SUB-IMAGINATION.

Hatifaniri kukanganwa hukama hwepedyo huripo pakati peBONDE NE FUNGIDZIRO.

Kuburikidza nekufungisisa kwakadzama tinofanira kushandura marudzi ese ekufungidzira kwemichina uye nzira yese yeSUB-IMAGINATION uye INFRA-IMAGINATION AUTOMATIC, kuita CONSCIOUS IMAGINATION, objective.

OBJECTIVE IMAGINATION pachayo inonyanya kugadzira, pasina iyo muvambi angadai asina kukwanisa kufunga nezvefoni, redhiyo, ndege, nezvimwe.

FUNGIDZIRO yEMUKADZI ari mumamiriro ekuzvitakura yakakosha pakukura kwemwana ari mudumbu. Zvinoratidzwa kuti amai vese vanogona nekufungidzira kwavo kuchinja psyche yemwana ari mudumbu.

Zvinokurumidza kuti mukadzi ari mumamiriro ezvinhu epamuviri atarise mifananidzo yakaisvonaka, nzvimbo dzepamusoro, uye ateerere mimhanzi yepamusoro-soro nemashoko anowirirana, nenzira iyo anogona kushanda pane psyche yechisikwa chaakatakura mudumbu rake nenzira inowirirana.

Mukadzi ari mumamiriro ezvinhu epamuviri haafaniri kunwa doro, kana kusvuta, kana kufungisisa nezvezvakaipa, zvisingafadzi nokuti izvi zvose zvinokuvadza kukura kunowirirana kwechisikwa.

Zvakakosha kuziva kukanganwira zvose zvisingawanzoitiki nezvikanganiso zvomukadzi ane pamuviri.

Varume vazhinji vasingashiviriri uye vasinganzwisisi zvechokwadi, vanotsamwa ndokutuka mukadzi ari mumamiriro ezvinhu epamuviri. Kuvava kwaizvozvi, kutambudzika kunokonzerwa nomurume asina unhu, kunoparira pambudzi pamwana ari mudumbu, kwete bedzi mumuviri asiwo mupfungwa.

Tichifunga nezvesimba rekufungidzira rekugadzira, zvine musoro kutaura kuti mukadzi ari mumamiriro ezvinhu epamuviri, haafaniri kufungisisa nezvezvakaipa, zvisingafadzi, zvisingaenderani, zvinosemesa, nezvimwe.

Yave nguva yokuti hurumende dzizvidye mwoyo nokugadzirisa zvinetso zvikuru zvine chokuita naamai.

Hazvina musoro kuti munzanga inozviti ndeyechiKristu uye yedemokrasi, munhu haazive kuremekedza nokukudza revo yorudzidziso youmai. Zvinotyisa kuona zviuru zvevakadzi vari mumamiriro ezvinhu epamuviri vasina dziviriro ipi neipi, vakarambwa nomurume nomunharaunda, vachipemha chimedu chechingwa kana kuti basa uye vachiita kazhinji kazhinji mabasa akaoma, kuti vagone kurarama nechisikwa chavanotakura pakati pemimba yavo.

Aya mamiriro ezvinhu asiri evanhu munzanga yanhasi, hutsinye uhu uye kushaya mutoro kwevatongi nevanhu kuri kutiratidza zvakajeka kuti democracy haisati yavapo.

Zvipatara nemawadhi avo oumai hazvisati zvagadzirisa chinetso, nokuti vakadzi vanogona kusvika muzvipatara izvozvo apo kuberekwa kwava pedyo.

Panodiwa nokukurumidza dzimba dzokugara dzomunharaunda, maguta echokwadi emaruva akashongedzerwa namahoro nemahombekombe evakadzi vari mumamiriro ezvinhu epamuviri avo varombo zvikuru, makiriniki namagarden ezvokudya nokuda kwavana ava.

Aya madzimba ekugaramo ndeenzvimbo dzekugara dzevakadzi varombo zvakanyanya vari mumamiriro ekuzvitakura, azere nemhando dzese dzezvivakwa, maruva, mimhanzi, kuwirirana, runako, nezvimwe, zvingapedza zvachose dambudziko guru rekuve amai.

Tinofanira kunzwisisa kuti nzanga yavanhu imhuri huru uye kuti hakuna dambudziko rekunze nokuti dambudziko rose neimwe nzira rinokanganisa nhengo dzose dzomunharaunda mukati medenderedzwa yazvo. Hazvina maturo kusarura vakadzi vane pamuviri nokuda kwokuti varombo zvikuru. Zvinotyisa kuvazvidza, kuvazvidza kana kuvapinza munzvimbo yokupotera yevanorara.

Munzanga ino yatiri kurarama, hakugoni kuva navana navanakomana vokurera, nokuti tose tiri vanhu uye tine maruramiro mamwe chete.

Tinofanira kusika democracy yechokwadi, kana tisingadi chaizvo kubvurwa neCommunist.
