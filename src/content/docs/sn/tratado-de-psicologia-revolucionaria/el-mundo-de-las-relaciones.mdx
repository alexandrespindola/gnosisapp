Nyika yehukama ine mativi matatu akasiyana zvikuru atinoda kujekesa zvakanaka.

Chekutanga: Takabatana nemuviri wepasi. Kureva kuti nemuviri wenyama.

Chechipiri: Tinogara panyika yePasi uye nechikonzero chinonzwisisika takabatana nenyika yekunze uye nenyaya dzinotitambudza, mhuri, mabhizinesi, mari, nyaya dzehunyanzvi, basa, zvematongerwo enyika, nezvimwe, nezvimwe, nezvimwe.

Chechitatu: Hukama hwemunhu naye pachake. Kune vanhu vazhinji rudzi urwu rwehukama haruna kana basa kadiki.

Zvinosuruvarisa kuti vanhu vanongofarira marudzi maviri ekutanga ehukama, vachitarisa neruvengo rukuru rudzi rwechitatu.

Chikafu, hutano, mari, mabhizinesi, zvirokwazvo ndizvo zvinonyanya kunetsa "Mhuka Yeungwaru" inonzi "munhu" zvisizvo.

Zvino: Zvinonzwisisika kuti muviri wenyama pamwe nezvinhu zvenyika zvese zviri kunze kwedu.

Muviri wePasi (muviri wenyama), dzimwe nguva unorwara, dzimwe nguva une hutano uye zvichingodaro.

Tinogara tichifunga kuti tine rumwe ruzivo nezve muviri wedu wenyama, asi chokwadi kunyangwe masayendisiti epamusoro pasirese haazive zvakawanda nezve muviri wenyama neropa.

Hapana mubvunzo kuti muviri wenyama nekuda kwegadziriro yayo huru uye yakaoma, zvirokwazvo iri kure nekunzwisisa kwedu.

Kana zviri zverudzi rwechipiri rwehukama, tinogara tichibatwa nemamiriro ezvinhu; zvinosuruvarisa kuti hatisati tadzidza kutanga mamiriro ezvinhu tichiziva.

Kune vanhu vazhinji vasingakwanise kuchinjika kune chero chinhu kana chero munhu kana kuva nekubudirira kwechokwadi muhupenyu.

Paunofunga nezvako kubva kumakona ebasa reesoteric reGnostic, zvinokurumidza kuziva kuti ndeupi wemarudzi matatu ehukama atiri kukanganisa.

Zvinogona kuitika kuti takabatana zvisizvo nemuviri wenyama uye semugumisiro tiri kurwara.

Zvinogona kuitika kuti takabatana zvisina kunaka nenyika yekunze uye semugumisiro tine gakava, matambudziko emari neezvemagariro, nezvimwe, nezvimwe, nezvimwe.

Zvinogona kuitika kuti takabatana zvisina kunaka nesu pachedu uye kuti tiri kutambura zvakanyanya nekushayikwa, kwekuvhenekerwa kwemukati.

Zviri pachena kuti kana rambi rekamuri yedu risina kubatana nemagetsi, imba yedu ichava murima.

Avo vanotambura nekushayikwa kwekuvhenekerwa kwemukati, vanofanira kubatanidza pfungwa dzavo neNzvimbo dzepamusoro dzeSer yavo.

Pasina mubvunzo tinoda kumisa hukama hwakanaka kwete chete neMuviri wedu wePasi (muviri wenyama) uye nenyika yekunze, asiwo nechikamu chega chega cheSer yedu pachedu.

Varwere vanoora mwoyo vakaneta navanachiremba vakawanda nemishonga, havachadi kupora uye varwere vane tariro vanorwira kurarama.

MuCasino yeMonte Carlo mamirioni evanhu akarasikirwa nepfuma yavo mukubhejera, vakazviuraya. Mamirioni amai varombo vanoshanda kuti vatsigire vana vavo.

Hakuna vanhu vangaverengeka vanoshuvira vakaora mwoyo avo nekushayikwa kwemasimba epfungwa uye nekuvhenekerwa kwepedyo, vakarega basa reesoteric pavari. Vashoma ndivo vanoziva kushandisa matambudziko.

Munguva yekuedzwa kwakakomba, kuora mwoyo uye kuparadzwa, munhu anofanira kukwidza kunyeuka kwepedyo kwake pachake.

Pasi pemumwe neumwe wedu pane TONANZIN Aztec, STELLA MARIS, ISIS Egyptian, Mwari Amai, vakatimirira kuti tipore moyo wedu unorwadza.

Kana mumwe wacho achizvipa kurohwa kwe "Kuyeuka kweIye", shanduko inoshamisa inoburitswa mumabasa ese emuviri, kuitira kuti masero agadzire chikafu chakasiyana.
