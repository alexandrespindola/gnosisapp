Chiremba chinoyamwisa chine pfungwa chiri kunzi munhu zvisizvo, hachina hunhu hwakasimba hwakajeka. Zvechokwadi, kushaikwa kwekubatana kwepfungwa uku muMunhu ndiko kunokonzera matambudziko akawanda nekuvadza.

Muviri wenyama chinhu chakakwana uye unoshanda sezvese zviri pamwechete, kunze kwekunge uchirwara. Zvisinei, hupenyu hwemukati hweMunhu hausi hwose hwakabatana mupfungwa. Chinhu chakakomba kupfuura zvose, pasinei nezvinotaurwa nezvikoro zvakasiyana-siyana zveSeudo-Esoteric neSeudo-Occultist, kushayikwa kwesangano repfungwa mukati-kati memunhu mumwe nomumwe.

Chokwadi, mumamiriro ezvinhu akadaro hapana basa rinowirirana sehwaro hwese muhupenyu hwemukati hwevanhu. Munhu, maererano nemamiriro ake emukati, kuwanda kwepfungwa, huwandu hwe "Ini".

Mapenzi akadzidza enguva ino yerima anokudza "Ini", vanoimisa, vanoiisa paatari, vanoidana kuti "ALTER EGO", "INI REPAMUSORO", "INI DZVENE", nezvimwewo, nezvimwewo, nezvimwewo. Havafari kuziva "Vazivi" veiyi zera dema ratiri kurarama, kuti "Ini Repamusoro" kana "Ini Rezasi" zvikamu zviviri zveEgo imwechete yakawanda...

Munhu zvechokwadi haana "INI Inogara" asi kuwanda kwe "Ini" dzakasiyana-siyana dziri pasi pevanhu uye dzisina musoro. Mhuka ine pfungwa inosiririsa inonzi munhu zvisizvo yakafanana neimba yakarashika apo panzvimbo yemuridzi mumwe, pane varanda vakawanda vanogara vachida kutonga uye kuita zvavanoda...

Chikanganiso chikuru cheSeudo-Esotericism neSeudo-Occultism yakachipa kufunga kuti vamwe vane kana kuti munhu ane "INI Inogara uye Isingachinji" isina mavambo uye isina magumo... Kana avo vanofunga saizvozvo vamuka kuziva kunyange kwechinguvana, vanogona kuratidza zvakajeka ivo pachavo kuti Munhu ane pfungwa haatombove mumwechete kwenguva yakareba...

Chiremba chinoyamwisa ane pfungwa, kubva pamaonero epfungwa, ari kuchinja nguva dzose... Kufunga kuti kana munhu achinzi Luis anogara ari Luis, zvinoita sechinhu chakadai sejoko rinorwadza kwazvo... Munhu iyeye anonzi Luis ane mamwe "Ini" mukati make, mamwe maegos, anozviratidza kuburikidza nehunhu hwake panguva dzakasiyana-siyana uye kunyange Luis asingafariri makaro, imwe "Ini" maari - ngatichidanei kuti Pepe - anofarira makaro zvichingoenda zvakadaro...

Hapana munhu akafanana nenzira inoenderera mberi; hazvidi zvechokwadi kuti uve akachenjera kwazvo kuti uzive zvizere shanduko dzisingaverengeki uye kupesana kwemunhu mumwe nomumwe... Kufunga kuti mumwe munhu ane "INI Inogara uye Isingachinji" zvirokwazvo zvakaenzana nekushungurudza muvakidzani wako uye iwe pachako...

Mukati memunhu mumwe nomumwe mune vanhu vakawanda vanogara, ma "Ini" akawanda, izvi zvinogona kusimbiswa iwe pachako uye zvakananga nemunhu wese akamuka, anoziva...
