Hutsvene hunotakura mumwe nemumwe wedu mukati make hunobva kumusoro, kudenga, kubva kunyeredzi... Hapana mubvunzo kuti Hutsvene hunoshamisa hunobva kunoti "LA" (Nzira yeMukaka, Gwara ratinogara).

Hutsvene hunokosha hunopfuura nepakati penoti "SOL" (Zuva) uye mushure mezvo noti "FA" (Nzvimbo yePlanet) inopinda munyika ino uye inopinda mukati medu pachedu. Vabereki vedu vakagadzira muviri wakakodzera kugamuchira Hutsvene uhu hunobva kuNyeredzi...

Nokushanda nesimba patiri pachedu uye kuzvipira nokuda kwevamwe vedu, tichadzokera takunda mudumbu rakadzika reUrania... Isu tiri kurarama munyika ino nokuda kwechimwe chikonzero, nokuda kwechimwe chinhu, nokuda kwechimwe chinhu chakakosha...

Zviri pachena kuti matiri mune zvakawanda zvatinofanira kuona, kudzidza uye kunzwisisa, kana tichinyatsoshuva kuziva chimwe chinhu pamusoro pedu pachedu, pamusoro poupenyu hwedu pachedu... Zvinosiririsa kuvapo kwouyo anofa asina kuziva chinangwa choupenyu hwake...

Mumwe nomumwe wedu anofanira kuzvionera oga revo youpenyu hwake, icho chinomuchengeta akasungwa mutirongo yorwadzo... Zviri pachena kuti mumwe nomumwe wedu mune chimwe chinhu chinovava upenyu hwedu uye chatinoda kurwisa nesimba... Hazvina basa kuti tirambe tiri mumamiriro ezvinhu akaipa, zvinoda kukurumidza kuderedza kuita guruva remuchadenga icho chinoita kuti tinete uye tisafare zvakadaro.

Hazvina maturo kuzvikudza nemazita, rukudzo, madhipuroma, mari, pfungwa dzisina maturo dzepfungwa, hunhu hunozivikanwa, nezvimwewo, nezvimwewo, nezvimwewo. Hatifaniri kumbokanganwa kuti unyengeri nokuzvikudza kwoupenzi hwehuzivi hwenhema zvinotiita vanhu vasina maturo, vakasakara, vanonoka, vanopesana, vasingakwanisi kuona chinhu chitsva...

Rufu rune revo zhinji dzose dziri mbiri dzakanaka nedzisina kunaka. Ngatitarii uyu mufungo mukuru wa"KABIR Mukuru Jesu Kristu": "Ngavashire avo vakafa avo vakafa vavo". Vanhu vazhinji kunyange zvavo vari kurarama vakafa chaizvo nokuda kwebasa rinobvira rose pavari pachedu uye naizvozvo, nokuda kwechimwe chinjo yomukati.

Vanhu vakavharirwa pakati pemitemo yavo nezvavanodavira; vanhu vakaomeswa mumatombo mundangariro dzezuro dzakawanda; vanhu vakazara mafungiro akaipa emadzitateguru; vanhu vari varanda vokuti vamwe vachati kudini, vanodziya zvinotyisa, vasingafariri, dzimwe nguva "vanoziva" vachitenda kuti vari muzvokwadi nokuti vakaudzwa kudaro, nezvimwewo, nezvimwewo, nezvimwewo.

Vanhu ivavo havadi kunzwisisa kuti nyika ino i"Gimnasium yePfungwa" iyo kwainogona kubvira kuparadza kushatirwa kwakavanzika kwatinotakura mukati... Dai vanhu ivavo varombo vainzwisisa mamiriro ezvinhu anosiririsa avari, vaidedera nokutya...

Zvisinei, vanhu vakadaro vanogara vachifunga zvakanakisisa nezvavo pachedu; vanozvikudza nokuda kwounhu hwavo, vanonzwa vakakwana, vane mutsa, vanobatsira, vane unhu, vane rudo, vakangwara, vanozadzisa mabasa avo, nezvimwewo. Upenyu hunoshanda sechikoro hwakanaka kwazvo, asi kuhutora semugumo muzvarwo, hazvina maturo zviri pachena.

Avo vanotora upenyu muzvarwo, sezvahunoraramiwa zuva nezuva, havana kunzwisisa kudikanwa kwokushanda pavari pachedu kuti vazadzise "Chinjo Yokunyanyisa". Zvinosiririsa kuti vanhu vanorarama nenzira yomuuyo, havana kumbonzwa chinhu pamusoro pebasa romukati...

Kuchinja kunokosha, asi vanhu havazivi nzira yokuchinja nayo; vanotambura zvikuru uye havatozivi chikonzero chokutambura... Kuva nemari hakusi zvose. Upenyu hwevanhu vakawanda vakapfuma hunowanzova hunosiririsa chaizvo...
