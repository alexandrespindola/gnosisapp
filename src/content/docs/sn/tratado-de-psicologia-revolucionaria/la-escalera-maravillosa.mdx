Tinofanira kushuva shanduko yechokwadi, kubuda mumaitiro aya anofinha, ehupenyu uhu hunongova hwemuchina, hunonetsa... Chinhu chekutanga chatinofanira kunzwisisa zvakajeka ndechekuti mumwe nemumwe wedu, angave murwi kana mushandi, akasununguka kana wepakati, mupfumi kana murombo, ari muchokwadi pane imwe kana imwe Level of Being...

Level of Being yechidhakwa yakasiyana neye asingamwi uye ye pfambi yakasiyana zvikuru neyemhandara. Izvi zvatiri kutaura hazvirambiki, hazvipokanidzwi... Pakusvika pachikamu chino chechitsauko chedu, hapana chatinorasikirwa nacho nekufungidzira manera anotambanuka kubva pasi kuenda kumusoro, akatwasuka uye aine zvikwiriso zvakawanda...

Zvisingapanikirwi pane chimwe chezvikwiriso izvi ndipo patinowanikwa; zvikwiriso zviri pasi pane vanhu vakatinyanya; zvikwiriso zviri pamusoro pane vanhu vakatipfuura... MuVertical inoshamisa iyi, mumamera anoshamisa aya, zviri pachena kuti tinogona kuwana ese Levels of Being... munhu wese akasiyana uye hapana anogona kuramba izvi...

Pasina kupokana hatisi kutaura nezvezviso zvakaipa kana zvakanaka, uye hazvisi nyaya yezera. Kune vanhu vadiki nevakuru, vakwegura vanenge vachifa uye vana vacheche... Nyaya yenguva nemakore; izvo zvekuzvarwa, kukura, kukura, kuroora, kubereka, kukwegura nekufa, zvakasarudzika zveHorizontal...

Mu"Escalera Maravillosa", muVertical pfungwa yenguva haikwanise. Pazvikwiriso zvechiyero chakadai tinogona kungowana "Levels of Being"... Tariro yemuchina yevanhu haibatsiri chinhu; vanotenda kuti nekufamba kwenguva zvinhu zvichava nani; ndizvo zvakafunga madzitateguru edu; zvakaitika zvangoratidza zvinopesana...

"Level of Being" ndiyo inokosha uye iyi iVertical; tinowanikwa pachikwiriso asi tinogona kukwira kune chimwe chikwiriso... "Escalera Maravillosa" yatinotaura nezvayo uye inoreva "Levels of Being" dzakasiyana, zvirokwazvo, haina chekuita nenguva yakatwasuka... "Level of Being" yakakwirira iri pamusoro pedu pakarepo kubva pachinguvana kuenda pachinguvana...

Haisi mune ramangwana riri kure, asi pano uye zvino; mukati medu; muVertical... Zviri pachena uye chero munhu anogona kunzwisisa, kuti mitsetse miviri - Horizontal neVertical - inosangana nguva nenguva mukati medu Psychological uye inoita Muchinjikwa...

Unhu hunokura uye hunozaruka mumutsara weHorizontal yeHupenyu. Hunozvarwa nekufa mukati menguva yayo yakatwasuka; hunoparara; hapana mangwana ehunhu hwemunhu akafa; haisi iyo Being... Iwo Levels of the Being; iyo Being pachayo, haisi yenguva, haina chekuita neHorizontal Line; iri mukati medu. Ikozvino, muVertical...

Zvingave zvisina musoro kutsvaga Being yedu kunze kwedu... Hazvina basa kuisa zvinotevera semhedziso: Mazita, madhigirii, kukwidziridzwa, nezvimwewo, munyika yekunze, hazviite kuti pave nekukudzwa kwechokwadi, kuongororwa patsva kweBeing, kuenda kuchikwiriso chepamusoro mu"Levels of Being"...
