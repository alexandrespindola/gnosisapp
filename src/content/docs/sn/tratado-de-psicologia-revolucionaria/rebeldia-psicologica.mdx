Hazvina kuipa kuyeuchidza vaverengi vedu kuti pane nzvimbo yemasvomhu mukati medu... Pasina mubvunzo nzvimbo yakadaro haimbozowanikwi munguva yakapfuura, kana mune ramangwana...

Ani nani anoda kuwana nzvimbo iyoyo yakavanzika, anofanira kuitsvaga pano uye zvino, mukati make, panguva ino chaiyo, kwete sekondi imwe chete kumberi, kana sekondi imwe chete kumashure... Matanda maviri Akatwasuka uye Akarara eMuchinjikwa Mutsvene, anowanikwa panzvimbo ino...

Saka tinozviwana nguva nenguva tiri pamberi penzira mbiri: iyo Yakarara neYakatwasuka... Zviri pachena kuti iyo Yakarara "yakaderera" kwazvo, "Vicente nevanhu vese" vanofamba nayo, "Villegas nemunhu wese anosvika", "Don Raimundo nemunhu wese"...

Zviri pachena kuti iyo Yakatwasuka yakasiyana; inzira yevapanduki vakangwara, iyo yevaRevolutionaries... Kana munhu akazvirangarira, kana achishanda paari, kana asingazivikanwi nematambudziko ese nekutambudzika kwehupenyu, muchokwadi ari kufamba neNzira Yakatwasuka...

Chokwadi hazvisi nyore kubvisa manzwiro asina kunaka; kurasikirwa nekuzivikanwa kwese nehupenyu hwedu; matambudziko emhando dzese, mabhizinesi, zvikwereti, kubhadhara mabhiri, maremendi, runhare, mvura, magetsi, nezvimwewo, nezvimwewo, nezvimwewo. Vanhu vasingashandi, avo nekuda kwechimwe chikonzero vakarasikirwa nebasa ravo, basa, zviri pachena kuti vanotambura nekushaiwa mari uye kukanganwa nyaya yavo, kusazvidya moyo, kana kuzvizivisa nedambudziko ravo, zvinonyanya kuoma.

Avo vanotambura, avo vanochema, avo vakabatwa nekutengesa, kubhadharwa kwakashata muhupenyu, kusatenda, kunyomba kana hutsotsi, vanozvikanganwa, Kune Wavo chaiye wemukati, vanozviona zvizere nenhamo yavo yetsika...

Basa pamusoro pako ndicho chinhu chikuru cheNzira Yakatwasuka. Hapana aigona kutsika Nzira yeKupandukira Kukuru, kana asina kumboshanda paari... Basa ratiri kutaura nezvaro ndererudzi rwePfungwa; rinobata nekushandurwa kwenguva ino yatiri. Tinoda kudzidza kurarama nguva nenguva...

Semuyenzaniso, munhu anoshaiwa tariro nekuda kwedambudziko remanzwiro, rezvemari kana rezvematongerwo enyika zviri pachena kuti akazvikanganwa... Munhu akadaro kana akambomira, kana achitarisa mamiriro ezvinhu uye achiedza kuzvirangarira uye ozvishingirira kunzwisisa zvinoreva maitiro ake... Kana akafungisisa zvishoma, kana akafunga kuti zvese zvinopfuura; kuti hupenyu huchinyengeri, hunokurumidza kupfuura uye kuti rufu runoderedza manyawi ese enyika kuita madota...

Kana akanzwisisa kuti dambudziko rake pachiri harisi chinhu chinopfuura "moto wemarara", moto unonyengera unodzima nokukurumidza, achangoona achishamisika kuti zvose zvachinja... Kushandura maitiro emuchina kunogoneka kuburikidza nekusangana kwemanzwiro uye Kuzvirangarira Kwepedyo kweSeri...

Zviri pachena kuti vanhu vanoita zvemuchina kune mamiriro akasiyana ehupenyu... Vanhu varombo! Vanowanzo shanduka kuita vakabatwa. Kana mumwe munhu akavabata vanonyemwerera; pavanonyadziswa, vanotambura. Vanotuka kana vakatukwa; vanorwadza kana varwadziswa; havambosununguki; vanhu vavo vane simba rokuvatungamirira kubva kumufaro kuenda kunhamo, kubva kutariro kuenda mukushaiwa tariro.

Munhu mumwe nemumwe anofamba neNzira Yakarara akafanana nechiridzwa, apo mumwe nomumwe wavanhu vavo anoridza zvaanoda... Ani nani anodzidza kushandura hukama hwemuchina, muchokwadi anopinda mu "Nzira Yakatwasuka". Izvi zvinomiririra shanduko yakakosha mu "Nhanho yeSeri" mhedzisiro inoshamisa ye "Kupandukira Kwepfungwa".
