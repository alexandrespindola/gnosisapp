Ndanga ndichicherechedza panguva ino (Gore ra1974), boka revanhu vakapinda munharaunda yemumwe munhu. Pano muMexico vanhu vakadaro vanogamuchira zita rinoshamisa rekuti "PARACAIDISTAS".
