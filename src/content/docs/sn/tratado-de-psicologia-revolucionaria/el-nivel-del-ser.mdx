Chero bedzi Nhanho yokuva isingachinji zvachose, kudzokororwa kwourombo hwedu hwose, zviitiko, nhamo uye nhamo zvichapfuurira...

Zvinhu zvose, mamiriro ezvinhu ose, anoitika kunze kwedu, pachikuva chenyika ino, zvinongova kuratidza kwezvatinotakura mukati.

Nemhaka yakarurama tinogona kutaura nomutoo woumwari kuti “kunze kunoratidza mukati”.

Apo munhu anochinja mukati uye shanduko yakadaro iri huru, kunze, mamiriro ezvinhu, upenyu, zvinoshandukawo.

Ndanga ndichicherechedza kwenguva ino, (Gore ra1974), boka revanhu vakapinda munzvimbo yomumwe munhu. Pano muMexico vanhu vakadaro vanogamuchira zita rinoshamisa rokuti "PARACAIDISTAS".

Vavakidzani vomunyika yeChurubusco, vari pedyo zvikuru neimba yangu, ichi chiri chikonzero nei ndakakwanisa kuvadzidza zvikuru...
