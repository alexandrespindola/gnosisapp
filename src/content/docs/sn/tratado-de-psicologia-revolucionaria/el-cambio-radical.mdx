Kana munhu achiri kuramba achikanganisa nekuzviti iye Mumwe, Asina mumwe, Asingakamurikike, zviri pachena kuti shanduko huru ichange iri chinhu chinopfuura zvisingabviri. Chokwadi chekuti basa reesoteric rinotanga nekuongorora kwakadzama kwemunhu pachake, riri kutiratidza huwandu hwezvinhu zvepfungwa, Iwo kana zvinhu zvisingadiwe izvo zvinokurumidza kubviswa, kubviswa mukati medu.

Pasina mubvunzo, hazvingakwanisike kubvisa zvikanganiso zvisingazivikanwe; zvinokurumidza kutarisa zvatiri kuda kuparadzanisa nepfungwa dzedu. Rudzi urwu rwebasa harusi rwekunze asi rwemukati, uye avo vanofunga kuti chero bhuku retsika kana hurongwa hwetsika hwekunze uye hwekunze hunogona kuvatungamirira kubudiriro, vachange vakarasika zvachose.

Chokwadi chaicho uye chakajeka chekuti basa repedyo rinotanga nekutarisa kwakanyanya pakuongorora kuzere kwemunhu pachake, chikonzero chinopfuura chakakwana kuratidza kuti izvi zvinoda kuedza kwemunhu mumwe nemumwe wedu. Kutaura pachena uye pasina kurova kumativi, tinotsinhira zvakasimba zvinotevera: Hakuna munhu angaita basa iri kwatiri.

Hazvibviri kuita shanduko chero ipi zvayo mupfungwa dzedu pasina kuongorora kwakananga kweiyo seti yese yezvinhu zvinonzwisisika zvatakatakura mukati. Kugamuchira huwandu hwezvikanganiso, kuregeredza kudiwa kwekudzidza uye kuongorora kwakananga kwazvo, zvinoreva chaizvo kunzvenga kana kutiza, kutiza kubva kwauri pachako, nzira yekuzvinyengera.

Kungozvishandira nesimba rekuongorora kwakaringana kwemunhu pachake, pasina kutiza kwechero rudzi, ndipo patinogona kuratidza chaizvo kuti hatisi "Mumwe" asi "Vazhinji". Kubvuma huwandu hweIYO uye kuratidza kuburikidza nekuongorora kwakadzama zvinhu zviviri zvakasiyana.

Mumwe munhu anogona kugamuchira Dzidziso yeIyo zhinji asati amboparatidza; izvi zvinongogoneka nekuzvitarisa zvakanyatsonaka. Kutiza basa rekuongorora kwepedyo, kutsvaga nzira dzekunzvenga, chiratidzo chisina musoro chekuperevedza. Chero bedzi munhu achiramba achivimba nokuti iye nguva dzose munhu mumwe chete, haagoni kuchinja, uye zviri pachena kuti chinangwa chebasa iri ndechokuti tiwane kuchinja zvishoma nezvishoma muupenyu hwedu hwomukati.

Kushanduka kukuru itsananguro yakajeka iyo inowanzo rasika kana munhu asingashande pamusoro pake pachake. Nzvimbo yekutanga yeshanduko huru inoramba yakavanzika chero munhu akaramba achizvitenda Mumwe. Avo vanoramba Dzidziso yeIyo zhinji vanoratidza zvakajeka kuti havana kumbobvira vazviongorora zvakakomba.

Kuongorora kwakasimba kwemunhu pachake pasina kutiza kwechero rudzi kunotibvumira kuzviongorora pachedu chokwadi chakaoma chekuti hatisi "Mumwe" asi "Vazhinji". Munyika yemaonero ezvinhu, dzidziso dzakasiyana-siyana dzenhema-esoteric kana dzenhema-occultist dzinogara dzichishanda senzira yekutiza kubva kwauri pachako... Pasina mubvunzo, kunyengera kwekuti unogara uri munhu mumwe chete kunoshanda sechipingamupinyi pakuongorora kwemunhu pachake...

Mumwe munhu angati: "Ndinoziva kuti handisi Mumwe asi Vazhinji, Gnosis yakandidzidzisa izvozvo". Kutaura kwakadaro, kunyange kwaizova kwakavimbika zvikuru, pasina kuvapo kwechiitiko chakazara pamusoro peiyi nzira yedzidziso, zviri pachena kuti kutaura kwakadaro kwaizova chimwe chinhu chekunze uye chepamusoro. Kuratidza, kuwana, uye kunzwisisa ndizvo zvakakosha; ikoku ndiko chete kunogoneka kushanda zvine mufungo kuti tiwane shanduko huru.

Kusimbisa chinhu chimwe uye kunzwisisa chimwe. Kana mumwe munhu akati: "Ndinonzwisisa kuti handisi Mumwe asi Vazhinji", kana kunzwisisa kwake kuri kwechokwadi uye kwete kungotaura kusina maturo kwekutaura kusina kujeka, izvi zvinoratidza, zvinoratidza, zvinopomera, kuratidzwa kwakazara kweDzidziso yeIyo zhinji. Ruzivo uye Kunzwisisa zvakasiyana. Yokutanga yezvinhu izvi ndeye pfungwa, yechipiri yemwoyo.

Ruzivo chete rweDzidziso yeIyo zhinji harubatsiri; zvinosuruvarisa, munguva dzatinozvirarama, ruzivo rwakaenda kure kupfuura kunzwisisa, nokuti mhuka ine njere inonzi zvisina kururama munhu yakagadzirisa rutivi roruzivo bedzi ichikanganwa zvinosuruvarisa rutivi rwakafanira rweUri. Kuziva Dzidziso yeIyo zhinji uye kuinzwisisa kwakakosha pakushanduka kukuru kwechokwadi.

Kana munhu anotanga kuzvitarisa zvakadzama kubva pakuona kwokuti haasi Mumwe asi Vazhinji, zviri pachena kuti atanga basa rakakomba pamusoro pechimiro chake chomukati.
