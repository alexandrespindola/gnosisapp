Kufunga zvakadzama uye nekunyatso chenjera kunoita sechinhu chisinganzwisisike munguva ino yekuderera nekuparara.

Kubva muNzvimbo yeUchenjeri kunobuda pfungwa dzakasiyana, kwete kubva kune Ini usingachinji sezvinofungidzirwa nemajaya asina ruzivo, asi kubva kune "Ini" dzakasiyana mune mumwe neumwe wedu.

Kana murume ari kufunga, anotenda zvakasimba kuti iye pachake uye ega ari kufunga.

Haadi kuziva mhuka ine musoro inonzwisisa kuti pfungwa dzakawanda dzinopfuura nekunzwisisa kwake, dzinobva kune dzakasiyana "Ini" dzatinotakura mukati.

Izvi zvinoreva kuti hatisi vanhu vanofunga vechokwadi; hatisati tine pfungwa dzemunhu mumwe.

Nekudaro, imwe neimwe ye "Ini" dzakasiyana dzatinotakura mukati, inoshandisa Nzvimbo yedu yeUchenjeri, inoishandisa pese painokwanisa kufunga.

Zvingave zvisina musoro, saka, kuzvizivisa nepfungwa yakaipa kana inokuvadza, tichitenda kuti ndeye yedu pachedu.

Zviri pachena kuti iyi kana iyo pfungwa yakaipa inobva kune chero "Ini" iyo pane imwe nguva yakashandisa zvisina kunaka Nzvimbo yedu yeUchenjeri.

Pfungwa dzakaipa dziripo dzemhando dzakasiyana: kufungira, kusavimba, kuda zvakaipa kune mumwe munhu, godo rekushuva, godo rechitendero, godo rezvematongerwo enyika, godo reushamwari kana remhuri, ruchiva, kusazvibata pabonde, kutsiva, hasha, kudada, shanje, ruvengo, chigumbu, kuba, hupombwe, usimbe, makaro, nezvimwe, nezvimwe, nezvimwe.

Chaizvoizvo kukanganisa kwepfungwa kwatinako kwakawanda zvekuti kunyangwe dai taive nemukanwa wedare nezviuru zverurimi kuti titaure, hatingakwanise kuzvinyora zvizere.

Semhedzisiro kana mhedziso yezvataurwa pamusoro, zvinova zvisina musoro kuzvizivisa nepfungwa dzakaipa.

Sezvo zvisingakwanisike kuti pave nemhedzisiro isina chikonzero, tinotaura zvine mutsindo kuti hapambogona kuve nepfungwa pachayo, kuburikidza nekuzvarwa kwega...

Hukama huri pakati pemufungi nepfungwa hunoonekwa; pfungwa yega yega yakaipa ine mavambo ayo mumufungi akasiyana.

Mumwe neumwe wedu mune vafungi vakaipa vakawanda, sezvo paine pfungwa dzemhando imwechete.

Nyaya iyi ikatariswa kubva kudivi rezvizhinji re "Vafungi nePfungwa," zvinoitika kuti imwe neimwe ye "Ini dzatinotakura muPfungwa dzedu, zvirokwazvo mufungi akasiyana.

Zvisingabvumirwi mukati memumwe neumwe wedu, mune vafungi vakawanda; zvisinei, mumwe neumwe weava kunyangwe ari chikamu chete, anozvitenda kuti ndiye zvese, pane imwe nguva...

Vanhu vanonyepa, vanozvida, vanozvifarira, vanofungira, havangatongogamuchira dzidziso ye "Uwandu hweVafungi" nekuti vanozvida zvakanyanya, vanonzwa se "baba vaTarzan" kana "amai vehuku"...

Vanhu vakadai vasina kujairika vangagamuchira sei pfungwa yekuti havana pfungwa dzemunhu mumwe, dzakangwara, dzinoshamisa?...

Nekudaro vanhu vakadaro vane ruzivo vanofunga zvakanakisisa nezvavo uye vanotopfeka nguo yaAristippus kuratidza uchenjeri nekuzvininipisa...

Ngano yezana remakore inotaura kuti Aristippus, achida kuratidza uchenjeri nekuzvininipisa, akapfeka nguo yekare izere nezvigamba nemakomba; akabata netsvimbo yerudyi Tsvimbo yeFalsafa ndokuenda nemumigwagwa yeAtene...

Vanoti Socrates paakamuona achiuya, akadanidzira nenzwi guru: "Haiwa Aristippus, kuzvitutumadza kwako kunoonekwa nemumakomba enguo yako!".

Uyo asingararami nguva dzose ari muMamiriro Ekuchenjera Itsva, Kuchenjera Kwekuona, achifunga kuti ari kufunga, anozvizivisa nyore nyore nechero pfungwa yakaipa.

Somugumisiro weizvi, zvinosimbisa zvinosuruvarisa simba rakaipa re "Ini Yakaipa," munyori wepfungwa inotaurwa.

Patinonyanya kuzvizivisa nepfungwa yakaipa, ndipo patinowedzera kuva varanda ve "Ini" inoienzanisa.

Nezve Gnosis, nzira yakavanzika, basa pamunhu pachake, miedzo yedu yega yega iri chaizvo mu "Ini" dzinovenga Gnosis, basa risinganzwisisike, nekuti havazive kuti kuvapo kwavo mukati mepfungwa dzedu kunotyisidzirwa nerufu neGnosis uye nebasa.

"Ini Dzakaipa" idzi dzinopesana dzinobva dzatora nyore nyore mamwe marori epfungwa akachengetwa muNzvimbo yedu yeUchenjeri uye anotanga nhevedzano yemafungu epfungwa anokuvadza uye anokuvadza.

Kana tikagamuchira pfungwa idzo, "Ini Dzakaipa" dzinoti pane imwe nguva dzinodzora Nzvimbo yedu yeUchenjeri, saka hatizokwanise kuzvisunungura kubva kumigumisiro yavo.

Hatifanirwe kumbokanganwa kuti yega yega "Ini Yakaipa" "Inozvinyengedza-Yega" uye "Inonyengedza", mhedziso: Inoreva nhema.

Pese patinonzwa kurasikirwa kwesimba kamwe kamwe, apo munhu anotsvaka anoodzwa mwoyo, neGnosis, nebasa risinganzwisisike, paanorasikirwa nechido uye achisiya zvakanakisa, zviri pachena kuti anyengerwa neImwe Ini Yakaipa.

Iyo "Ini Yakaipa yeHupombwe", inoparadza misha inoremekedzwa uye inoita kuti vana vave vanoshungurudzika.

Iyo "Ini Yakaipa yeGodo", inonyengedza vanhu vanonamatirana uye inoparadza mufaro wavo.

Iyo "Ini Yakaipa yeKudada kweMistic" inonyengedza vanozvipira veNzira uye ava, vachinzwa vakachenjera, vanovenga Mudzidzisi wavo kana kumutengesa...

Ini Yakaipa inokwezva kune zvatinorarama nazvo, kune ndangariro dzedu, kune zvatinoshuva zvikuru, kune kutendeseka kwedu, uye, kuburikidza nekusarudzwa kwakakwana kwezvose izvi, inopa chimwe chinhu muchiedza chenhema, chimwe chinhu chinofadza uye kutadza kunouya...

Zvisinei, apo munhu anowana "Ini" muchiito, paakadzidza kurarama ari munzvimbo yekuchenjera, kunyengera kwakadaro kunova kusingabviri...
