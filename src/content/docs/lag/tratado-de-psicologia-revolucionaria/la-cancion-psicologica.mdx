Kikare kiro me apolou kikoto akingakinai alu ikeny ejaikinio.

Ibeje daa alu akwapinikin eongonokin, ikeny eongonokin alu imario akolong, ipote apolou ngesi naiboisio aijanakin.

Mam apolou eong alu kikare ebe adumunetei, ikeny eongonokin angoit emamei anyunyur.

Kikare eong adumunetei, ebuni akolong, ebuni aijanakin, idumunete eong, egerete eong akwana, adumunete eong ebe atukotokin bon alu itunga, alu ngole, akinaerei, kikoto mam ajakakini eong, kikare, ationo eong, kidiopet itunga edio.

Kwape kwap atwanakin keda akwapinikin akolong, eyapere ebe itunga ngesi idumetei ebe esupetetei naiboisio, ereitetei ebe amesiokai, asiopetetei, aberetei, alotuetei.

Kikare kiro kiro ebe edaun ebe ijoikinin alu apolou naiboisio aijanakin adiope.

Atwanakin akida eongonokin akwa itunga mam ediopeki, ikeny eongonokin alu apolou akolong.
