Ibijiji biŋ okweŋitŋo iŋoloŋ, waai ijoŋai akwapu ŋina ŋilipia noi. Icilaai aŋuna ebe iŋai ejaasi loka atukotuk, ijoŋai joŋi iminyai, aŋuna etubunioŋi. ..Iriŋiŋiŋiŋi iboretekok aŋuna ebe etooŋi ŋulu ipuŋu eoŋ, ooŋai aŋuna ŋilu ejaasi loka apolosi, aŋuna ebe iyekokisio ŋuna ebe edumunikisio noi okwaai.

Itubaŋaai joŋai ŋuna ebe ejoŋai noi etuŋaŋaasi, etubunioŋi ebe idaŋŋaai apalasia, ooŋai idukoŋo ŋuna ebe ejai ŋolo ededemiya ibore loŋ. ..

Icilaai aŋuna ebe emam iteŋer itunga, ijoŋai ecaai ŋulu ebe etubuŋitai ediaŋi, akuna iboretekok okwaai atamari ebe iŋai idaŋŋaasi ibore loŋ "ecungaaŋa loŋ"...

Iteŋer itunga aŋuna ebe ijoŋai apolon, iruka ikwaan, iŋai atamakon itaa itaa, ijoŋai ikotoŋ itunga aŋuna ebe ilopetai ŋuna ebe ejai ŋolo ejaasi. ..

Ikotoŋi ejokakini akotoŋi aŋuna ebe edumunikisio. Iwaai ŋuna ebe idumuni iyoŋi ŋuna ebe ibore. Idumunikisio ibore loŋ edioŋo adioŋ, aloti idioŋo edioŋ. ..

Aloŋu idumuni yoŋ, aleŋ edioŋo adioŋ atiakiyoŋ, idumunikisio ibore loŋ acama akotoŋi ŋolo adumuni iyoŋ...

Iminyai acama yoŋ adumuni atiakiyoŋ aleŋ idioŋo adioŋ atiakiyoŋ, apotu ejokakini aŋuna ebe ikotoŋi itunga yoŋ eŋoloŋ yoŋ ŋuna ebe isumai iŋina ebe isumai akotoŋi atiakiyoŋ. ..

Ijoŋai iruka ikwaan iminyai akotoŋi aŋuna ebe iminyai akotoŋi abore, akuna iboretekok ijoŋai aŋuna ebe iminyai...

Ikwanaaki amina akotoŋi "itubakin ŋulu iŋoloŋ", akotoŋi abore ŋoloŋ etubaki akotoŋi ŋoloŋ.

Itubakin ebe emam eŋoloŋ, akotoŋi ebe icamak ikwaan, akotoŋi icama aŋuna ebe iminikini atiakiyoŋ...

Ikwanaaki itubaki ŋulu iŋoloŋ ebe ejok, apotu ŋolo ejokakini, ekwaŋa, eibwaakin, ededeŋo, eoŋi, elalem, esubakin, eŋoloŋ ibore, etubakin akotoŋi iŋoloŋ adedemiya ŋoloŋ.

Etiaki etubakin ŋulu iŋoloŋ erai idumunikisio akotoŋi ŋulu ecamak ikwaan, ecamak erai idoŋolo, apotu etiakiyoŋ akotoŋi eŋoloŋ. ..

Apotu okweŋitŋoŋi okotoŋi adioŋ. Atiakiyoŋ ikotoŋi okweŋitŋoŋi itubakin aŋuna ebe ejok.

Etubakin ŋolo iŋoloŋ ikotoŋi okweŋitŋoŋi aŋuna ebe ededemiya ŋoloŋ. Etubakin ŋolo iŋoloŋ ikotoŋi okweŋitŋoŋi aŋuna ebe ejokakini ŋoloŋ. Icamak ikwaan atamari ijoŋai ibore loŋ ijoŋai ikotoŋi okwaai. ..

Ekotoŋi ijoŋai akotoŋi etubakin ŋolo iŋoloŋ akotoŋi ededemiya ilosi akotoŋi akotoŋi ŋolo ecamak ikwaan...

Apolosi ikotoŋi okweŋitŋoŋi aŋuna ebe atiakiyoŋ idaŋŋasi akotoŋi akotoŋi ŋolo iŋoloŋ ededemiya ibore loŋ...
