Itula okusoma Istorya ya Nthiri kwakumanya eti tuli bantu baribali abangi na eti hangali tukangira ituli itulyahire... Ighana lya 20 ghuli ghwanda ghose ghwa ghulyangaza, ita, mahara, sodomia ly'inthiri yose, ghulala ghwa lwevu, mahara, pombe, ukali ghulalya, ugubahi ghulema, imbamba, n'enda, n'enda, n'enda, ghuli isunde ghutula tukubala; hakuli musano ghwa nsindo nginya tukwiyasa eti twahikana ituka ilalya lya ghukula...

Ghuli gahi ighondo lyitu lya kikama?, kurema kurema ng'oto: Ghuli gahi Ighondo lyitu lya Ukukala? Hangali Ighondo lya Ukukala itula ikwenda ghumi, itula ikwenda ukubala ghwa magerere ghitu ghose, ghama, magerere na magerere... Nime ngubali ukukubala ilo theba (Ng'oma 1974), eghuma gha bantu ibiliha ilima haghali ha bho. Aho mu Mexico bantu abo bahalikha lya kuto "PARACAIDISTAS". Balina ng'omi gha koloni campestre Churubusco, bali hathi ghwa gho ghali nime, musano ulo kwokomereka nikukubala hathi...
