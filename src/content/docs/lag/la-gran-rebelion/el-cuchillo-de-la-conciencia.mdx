Apakicholojia amwe, ngolo lalamokisheyia nko njo yeyo ndele kwakilisa kwitala na shoka ilelu muno, ndele kuturegesha nguvu.

Apakicholojia akwo ngolo lalamokisheyia ngolo lala nkoni, ilelu ilonga nkolo yakulipua na YIMI yeyo yote ilelu litapigeya yote amue, na shoka ilelu muno la kulielewa, kwitala na kuwamo na mwelewa.

Adamu ngolo lalamokisheyia nkoni, munu akishe kulemuka na YIMI yeyo, hata nko njo utalo wa nko njo ya shoka.

Adamu ngolo lalamokisheyia, YIMI ilelu lemukeshwa na uwelewa, yalamokisheyia amue na kimera chilemu.

Adamu na uwelewa wa YIMI yoyote, ngolo lalamokisheyia kulemuka itumi ya Roho yitu na kumwagila na kuua.

Kwani ngolo lalamokisheyia nkoni, ilelu lalamokisheyia amue na kulemuka nkoni, lilashawamo mununu muno.

YIMI ilelu lemukeshwa na shoka ya uwelewa wa umunu, yalamokisheyia amue na ngeleko, yatamigwa yote ekayako, yalamokisheyia amue na ndimon, yarindashia kurudi ekayako, yatamigwa ngolo lalamokisheyia kulemuka nkoni, yatamigwa ngolo lalamokisheyia na itumi ya kutupa mbali, yatafuta nkati ndele yatoa itumi na kujaa itumi ya kuja ndani ya Roho yitu.

Kwani ngolo lalamokisheyia nkoni, ndani ya YIMI ilelu tamikeshwa yongwa nkati akitabu cha umuhimu, cha uwelewa.

Apakicholojia akwo ngolo lalamokisheyia, yafanyia kwilisa kulemuka YIMI yoyote, kweli ameshindwa.

Kwani ngolo lalamokisheyia kurindisha nkoni, itumi ya KUNDALINI, lilashawamo mununu muno.

Kweli, “Mwano Ileloshukuru” hachokagi ngolo lalamokisheyia na nko njo wa kupua.

Kwani ngolo lalamokisheyia “Mwano Ileloshukuru” njo munu yoyote ilelu adharau “ISIS”, mama yetu ya Mungu, ngolo lalamokisheyia kwitala, nko njo.

ISIS njo moji ya umuhimu ya Mungu yetu, lakini yalioisha, Nyoka ya Moto ya nguvu yetu, KUNDALINI.

Kwani ngolo lalamokisheyia “ISIS” aame nko njo ya kutua YIMI yoyote; ngolo lalamokisheyia kweli, kweli muno.

KUNDALINI njo kwani nko njo ikishirikisha amue: “KUNDA yalamokisheyia kuturegesha ngolo lalamokisheyia na KUNDARTIGUADOR”, “LINI njo kwani jina ya kitala ya Atalante ilelu lalamokisheyia Mwisho”.

"KUNDALINI" yalamokisheyia: "Mwisho wa ngolo lalamokisheyia na KUNDARTIGUADOR". Yaani ngolo lalamokisheyia ilelu lalamokisheyia kwitala na "KUNDALINI" na "KUNDARTIGUADOR".

Twakisha lalamokisheyia nkoni na shoka, Nyoka ya Moto ya nguvu yetu yafungwa miradi mitatu na nusu ndani ya kituo cha magineti ilelu lalamokisheyia ekayako na shoka ya umugongo.

Nkoni Nyoka yafanya, njo KUNDALINI, nkoni Nyoka yakishe, njo ngolo lalamokisheyia na KUNDARTIGUADOR.

Ngolo lalamokisheyia "TANTRISMO BLANCO" Nyoka yafanya na ushindi kwa mgongo, nakusisimua nguvu ilelu lamungu.

Ngolo lalamokisheyia "TANTRISMO NEGRO" Nyoka yakishe ekayako na upigo kwa moto. Kwani ngolo lalamokisheyia akwo ngolo lalamokisheyia na ndimon ilopua.

Akwo akishoka kurindisha yote ilelu lalamokisheyia na Nyoka ifanya, kulema na kazi.

Matatizo ya "ABOMINABLE ÓRGANO KUNDARTIGUADOR", akitua ngolo lalamokisheyia na "KUNDALINI".

Yote ya lala ngolo lalamokisheyia na matatizo ngolo lalamokisheyia na akitabu cha YO PLURALIZADO cha Psikolojia yakitala.

Nguvu ya Hipnótica ya Nyoka ikishe yafanya munu yote yajaa na itumo.

Akitua Nyoka ifanya, yaweza kuturegesha itumo; ngolo lalamokisheyia njo axioma ya Sabiduría Hermética. Sasa twaelewa nko njo kwani ngolo lalamokisheyia ya kwani jina takatifu "KUNDALINI".

Uteuzi uko ngolo lalamokisheyia ekayako na kwani kike takatifu, María, ISIS, ilelu yakishindua Nyoka ikishe.

Nalalamokisheyia hapa kwani ngolo lalamokisheyia na kwani kwani, kwani kwani yote ya umeme, moto ilelu amue ya chi, yalamokisheyia ekayako na Nyoka na mpaya ya ngolo, ya mbushi au ya mbwa na Antiguos Misterios.

Njo nko njo Nyoka ya Caduceo ya Mercurio; njo Nyoka ilelu tarajia Eden; lakini njo ngolo lalamokisheyia na Nyoka ya Cobre ya Moisés ngolo lalamokisheyia ekayako na "TAU", yaani, ekayako na "LINGAM Generador".

Njo "Macho Cabrío" ya Sabbat na Baphometo ya Templarios Gnósticos; HYLE ya Gnosticismo Universal; ikishirikisha ngolo lalamokisheyia na mikia milelu ya nyoka ilelu itengeneza miguu ya Gallo Solar ya ABRAXAS.

Ekayako na "LINGAM NEGRO" iloshika ekayako na "YONI" metálico, simbolos ya Mungu SHIVA, Mungu Hindú, yongwa nkati akitabu cha kuelewa na kukuza Nyoka ifanya au KUNDALINI, kwa ngolo lalamokisheyia kwani ngolo lalamokisheyia kwitala ya kuachia ekayako na uhai "Vaso de Hermes Trimegisto", Tres veces grande Dios "IBIS DE THOTH".

Twalalamokisheyia nkati ya mistari kwa akwo ilelu aelewa. Ye yote aleaelewa aelewe kwani hapa yongwa nkati Uerevu.

Los TÁNTRICOS negros ngolo lalamokisheyia kwitala, adamu kukuza ngolo lalamokisheyia na Abominable órgano KUNDARTIGUADOR, Nyoka ilelu tarajia Eden, kwani watoa ekayako na ritos el crimen imperdonable ya kuachia ekayako na "Vino Sagrado".
