Lawi lokomeke te yenyo me wiye me doko iwic maber jami mogo ma waneno dok wiwinyo, jami mawasomo, jami mogo ma dano mukene owaco botwa, jami mogo ma otimere botwa, etc. etc. etc.

Lupwonnyi gimito ni lutino kwan giwek iwicgi lokgi, kitabu gi, jami ma gicoyo i kitabu me kwan, wiye lok tere ducu, tic mucalo opong, ki poto ducu, etc.

Diyo jami me kwan tyen loke me wiyo jami ma giwaco botwa, jami ma wasomo calo macini, lok me wiye, dwoko calo akuru, nyunyi, onyo onyo kit jami ducu ma wakano iwicwa.

Weke likwaye manyen gunge ni dwoko calo disiki i redioconsola lok ducu ma gicoyo iwiye, tyen loke ni niang iye maber. Wiya poko me niang, tero peke niwiyo ka peya niang iye, wiyo tero i kare ma okato, en gin mo muto, gin mo ma tye kwo bene pe tye.

Ting me cobo, yot dok itere me timo ni lutino kwan ducu me cukul, koleji, dok univasiti gunge gin ma lokeke i niang ma tek.

NIANG en gin mo ma kelo piyo piyo, atira, gin ma wanongo maber adada, gin mo ma waneno adada dok maber ma lokke bedo me atima ma ngeyo iye maber.

Wiyo, lokomeke en gin mo muto, tero i kare ma okato dok rac lokke bedo gin macalo, i tam, i gin ma mito dwoko calo macini dok lubo ma wangeyo peke.

I NIANG MA ATIRA, I niang ma tek, i niang me iye, tye iye diyo me iye me cwiny, diyo ma medde ma nywal i gin ma watiyo i iye dok meno aye ducu.

Niang ma atira nyutu lokke calo tim ma piny piny, macalo, yot, lire me timo jami ma myero watiye; leng labongo tam moro keken. NIANG ma oloke bedo ATIMA MUTIMME i ngene keni en gin maber adada, me uru, me yubo dok gin ma pire tek adada.

Tim ma odok wiye i wiyo jami ma wasomo, gin ma mito bedo botwa, cik, kit me timo gin maber ma gipwonjwa, jami ma wakano i wiye, etc., en gin ma kikwan, odok wiye i tamo jami me yot peke, en gin me apiny apiny, odok wiye i tamo jami dok kelo tero dok lit keni.

Meno me yubo tim me wiye, meno me temo loko tim weke opong gin ma wakano i wiye, en gin mo ma kidiyo, gin ma tye ada labongo tim ma piny piny dok kelo tero keni dok lit.

Meno me diyo jami me kwan, meno me diyo mwaka, ditimo dano ma wiye tye yot dok itere me timo calo.

Niang jami ma gikwano dok ka mito penyo, en gin mo mapat, tero peke ki wiye, tero me ryeko atira ma myero kilok wiye ki ryeko.

Dano mogo ma gimito doko wiye gin ducu ma gitimo i kwogi i gin ma mito bedo, lok me wiye mogo ducu ma gikano i wiye, gimedde ki poko dok ka poko tye iye tye iye bene kwir. Lubele poko kwo, luot, litinogi ki litino me wabedo, ki dano ma wabedo kede. Gipoko odegi, gamuk, bolegi, gin ducu kede gin, kede jami me wabedo gin kede onyo me wabedo kede onyo lacan. Gipoko tamogi, ryeko me litinogi kede tamo me dano mukene, ki ryeko me dano mukene dok kwir kella dok lokke bedo atima mutimme.

Me rac me piny ducu, yubu ducu me society odok wiye i kwir dok kit me keno. Dano ducu kwiro dano ducu. Wakwiro tamo, jami, dano dok mito keno sente dok sente mangi, lok manyen, tamo manyen ma wakano iwiye, jami manyen me uru me luwotwa, etc.

I NIANG MA ATIRA, MABEBA, MA ATIRA, tye iye mar atira labongo lok me wiye keni.

Jami ma giwiyo, gin ma kimio wiye, woto piyo piyo i wilobo pien wiye pe gen. Lutino kwan gicoyo i gamuk me wiye, jami ma mito bedo, lok me wiye, gin ducu ma tero peke i kwo pien giwilobo iwiye labongo weko gin mo.

Dano ma kwo kede somo kede somo calo macini, dano ma maro kano lok me wiye i gamuk me wiye balo tam, gibalo rac.

Wan pe waloko i kom kwan atira ma tek ma odok wiye i niang ma tek. Wan wakwero kede kit me pwony ma con ma okato. Wakwero yubu ducu me kwan macini, wiyo ducu, etc. Wiyo yot ka niang atira tye iye.

Wamito kwan, kitabu maber mitere, lupwonnyi me cukul mitere, koleji, univasiti. Mito GURU, luketela me cwiny, mahatmas, etc. ento mito niang pwony ducu ma atima dok pe kikano gamuk me wiye mapetegen.

Pe wabi bedo lire ada kace wadwong i komwa kede tamo ma wakano i wiye, kede gin ma mito bedo, kede gin ma wamito doko bene pe wabedo, etc. etc.

Ka waniang pwony ma wamiyo ada, pe wamito wiyo i wiye, onyo dwoko bedo jami ma mito bedo.

Ka poko tye iye me gin ma wabedo kany dok kany kede gin ma wamito doko ikare me anyim, ka poko tye iye me kwo ma watimo kede gin ma mito bedo onyo labongo ma wamito yubo, mar atira pe bedo iye.

Poko ducu rac, poko ducu kelo lworo, kwir, dumo, etc. lworo me timo gin ma wamito, kwir pi tim maber me wabedo, dumo pien wabedo malo kuluwotwa. Gin ma pire tek i kwo ma watimo, wabedo rac, wakwiro, wamarre kenu, wabedo lakeno, etc., en me bedo maleng, poko zero, dok niang ngengena maber, calo wabedo dok pe calo wamito doko onyo calo wamito bedo.

Pe yot me weko YO, MI MISMO, ka pe wapwonye me neno, me winyo pi niang gin ma wabedo ada kany dok kany i kit maber dok matira.

Ka wamito niang ada myero winy lupwonnyi, luketela me cwiny, etc., etc.

Awobi, dok anyira me likwaye manyen giwillo niang me woro, me miyo woro bot wonwa, lupwonnyi, luketela me cwiny, gurus, mahatmas, etc.

Pe yot me niang pwony kace pe wangeyo woro dok miyo woro bot wonwa, lupwonywa, luketela me cwiny.

Wiyo keni ma wakwano wiye labongo niang, balo tam dok cwiny dok kelo kwir, lworo, dumo, etc.

Ka wangeyo winyo ada i kit maber dok matut, lokke kella i iyewa tek me uru, niang maber, macalo, yot, lire me timo jami macalo macini, lire me tamo jami, lire me wiyo.

Ka kimiyo tam me lakwan lire me tic matek me wiyo ma myero otim, bi bedo yot me pwonyo yubu me adaka dok tebul me jami me lutino me kwan me aryo dok timo niang kom ma tye kuluwotwa.

Calo waloko kede lupwonnyi mogo me cukul, wangeyo ni gi lworo ada yubu ma con. Gimito ni lutino kwan gupwonye wiye ducu kace pe giniang iye.

Ikare mogo giye ni ber me niang kulu me wiyo ento gimedde ki lok ni cik me physics, chemistry, mathematics, etc. Myero gicoyo i wiye.

Tyenyeke ni lok man lokke abeda pien cik me physics, chemistry, mathematics, etc, giniang iye maber i tam keni, bene i tero mukene me tam macalo tamo, tam ma iye tye yot, etc. etc. etc. pe wamito coyo i wiye, lokke bedo i cwinywa dok nyutu lokke calo ryeko ma iye tye yot ka timo kwo mito timo.

Ryeko MANKELLO miniyo kit me OMNICIENCIA, kit me nyutu ngeyo iye.

Niang ma tek dok i tero ducu me tam yot, Meditational.
