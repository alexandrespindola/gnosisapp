Kama vinyonyi vibi laghai guba ya mapindo ya nguku mama gwa kutafuta lito.

Mwana mwike abeleghi guba ya mamaake nambe aamini alyo obukiilo bwa hatari.

Ivyo leelo, ughutili na ughutafuta lito kungi vikaghiwa sana.

Mtu atili ghuitighue abakiighue ni wakihi abutafute lito ghusima yake.

Ighiisi itili ghuitighue yakiighue ni ighiisi ingine, ikonue matopu, ndege, meli ya vita na iandejesie jeshi na ireghesie vita.

Banghi wangi abamalai mbeghi kutabula, ghuitighue ghukaleghi abutafute lito ghuboki, na ibe ghaabighi, wakahi, n.k...

Badaada bangi abamalai hili, ghuitighue ghukulaghiwa buki, ibe malaya.

Mtu ya bughani atili ghukumulaghiwa mughasi na abutafute lito ghusima yake, aikome na olagi ughulegha ghang'ali.

Mughasi ya bughani aikome mpinzani wake au muume na ivyo ibe muhomi.

Ayoi atili ghukumulaghiwa muume na ghutamania ghukummilika aikome mughasi mwike au aamue ghukumukoma.

Mwene nyumba atili ghukumaliighua bantu balia malipo gha nyumba, amaliighie mikataba, wafiesi, amana, n.k., atamania ghutumilika na kama ng'ajane mkoi na atelemai ghukumbusi mahitaji mangi, na kama baene nyumba booshi ba muji bakai ivyo, olagi yuyiye atiaighue aghulale na banaake ghaabaa au gha bustani gha muji.

Vita zoshi zinamila ghuutili.

Mapolisi gha siri, mahangaiko, makambi gha ukomelwa, Siberia, magereza magumu, ughaaji, majira ma bughiilu, ghuhomwa na itopu, n.k. zinamila ghuutili.

Mataifa yakihi mataifa mangine ghukulagha ghaata, batemela lito ghuboki, baamini ati bughukoma, bughuyakiighiwa, n.k. ibamalai ghugutumilika, ghutengi na ghuboki.

Ghumaofisi gha polisi ya siri, ughukinga ughuasi, n.k. kutali gha mashariki kama gha magharibi, wahiwahi, batili gwaabo, batamania ghukumalagha ghuboki gwa iighiisi.

Mahomi booshi, vita zoshi, ughuhoma booshi, zinamila ghuutili na ghutafuta lito.

Ghumaajira mangine kungi kwabai ghumbonekela kati ya bantu, leelo ughutili na ghutafuta lito fimalai ghukumbonekela ghuloota.

Mghale abutughuamu mghale, atili ati yuwi aikombe, amtemele, amnyonye na hata zibai hekima iliyo mpumavu na mbaya kama yii: "GHUSILAAGHI GUBELU YA MUNYUMA GWA MGHALE GHUBUHOKILE". Wazungu ba Hitler babai ati hekima yii yabai YA DHAHABU.

Leelo ughu GHUTILI na UGHUTAFUTA LITU fifumalai sayari ya dunia ibe ghaahali gha moto. Dunia yoshi ilitili. Dunia yoshi yatamani litu.
