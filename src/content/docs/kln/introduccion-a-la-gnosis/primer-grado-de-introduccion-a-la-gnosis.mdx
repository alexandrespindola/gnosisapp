Este es el grado de introducción a los Estudios Filosóficos Gnósticos o grados externos de la gnosis. Ni kekimache grado ne nyoneet engʼ kianyanet engʼ Filosofico ne Gnóstico koboot engʼ grados che mengeet che kakwaut engʼ gnosis. Ne leel kobun students ko kamache kiitwaan engʼ grado ak kemache koitee ko uu grado netai, abeer, somok, alakwakin. Kamache koiit ne tai engʼ ngʼaleek che mengeet ko ma grados che esotérico ne Gnóstico. Grados che esotérico ne Gnóstico kobwa student amu kageergeer. Grados che esotérico, che iyoni grados che Gnóstico che tuntunindet, ma toi kotes kole kogeer komie; koitun. Ye kikii: “ani kain grados somok, initiaciones somok”, kobwa koyai ngʼaleet ne ma tuntunindet.

Amun mutuurotindo engineer, abogado ak mutuuch, alakwakin, kamache kageergeer engʼ kianyanet. Ye uuneni engʼ somanet ak kianyegei. Ko uu komie kogasei kianyanet ne leel engʼ branch ne kianyei, ko tee koitee koyai kiy ne kaanyei. Yaitet nebo kokwaut perfection. Mutaiyok che boo, Mutaiyok che profit, Mutaiyok che scientist, alakwakin, maakote kogasei kianyanet ne leel engʼ branches che uuneni, ago kageeree kiyai engʼ kiy che kaanyei. Kianyanet kekuru ma koitei ngʼo (ibak kokwaut kayabinet nebo kwo engʼ boisiek). Kamache koyai.

Uuneni course nebo lecciones ak uunenchi yeityo engʼ incentivo ne tuntunindet, engʼ kogon engʼ kianyanet, engʼ kogon engʼ koya kianyanet nebo Wisdom ne boiboiyet. Ago kergeech ole kamache koyai engʼ kiy ne kaaanyei, amun mutuurotindo kokwaut perfection nebo Obra.
