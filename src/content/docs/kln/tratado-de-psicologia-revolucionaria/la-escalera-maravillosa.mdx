Aneto koito kiyokchin ichek cheu, kokoi chechang'ei ng'eet nebo kisoman, ng'eet nebo  maget nebo mechanic, koboishee...  Kikilekto koito koyai ne kaleen, amu  koit, kounon nebo bourgeois ale proletarian, kounon nebo keny nebo kounon nebo  boriet, kounon nebo  kararan ale koiton, koi ng'eet nebo Level nebo Ser...

Level nebo Ser nebo kalewet  kaiyayot  ak kounon nebo  ki yaa ta kalewet  ak kounon nebo  kalewet nebo  malaya kaiyayot ak kounon nebo  chemosop.  Ami che kiyeyete ki, ma koit konyorochi, ma koi kobotoi... Kaaljiny  arubta nebo chapter tinyei, ma kimang'u  yooto kamait ngal'at ne  kokotoi yuu nyo koito ak kokeny  kaineechin  ak ng'eet ne kaineechin...

Kong'eet ne ma kaiyaktoi kounon nebo  ngal'atinwee  tinyei; ngal'atinwee  konyorochi  kiit ne mising ne kounono; ngal'atinwee  kokeny  kiit ne koyo mising ne kounono... Ngal'atinwee  ne Vertical ne kamait,  ngal'at ne yai, koi kiwa  kakait  Level nebo Ser... Kiit koit kaiyai ak amu  ma kainenyon...

Kong'eet ne ma kiyeyete boroto ne miach ale boroto ne ma miach, ale kiit ne kiyeyete  saait.  Kiit  kiit  kiptany  ak kugoek,  kiugoek  ne kiwa keu ak ng'wonyik  chemii biik...  Ng'eet nebo  saait  ak kugoek;  ng'eet nebo  kibire, kigoochi, kigoochi,  kiyokchi, kiyokchi, kuguuke ak keu, koi ng'eet nebo  Horizontal...

Ngal'atinwee  ne "Ngal'at ne Yai", ngal'at nebo  Vertical, ng'eet nebo  saait  ma kaiyai. Ngal'atinwee  ne kamait kaineechin  kiit  "Level nebo Ser"...  Amu  yooto ne kisir  ne biik ma boiyot; kwendi  kwendoti  saait  kekochi  koimaat; kwendi  kimache  kokwenda kugoek  chemii kobooto;  biik che yae kiyaati kakeendi...

"Level nebo Ser" koito ne kamuktoi amu koito  Vertical; tai kaineechin  ngal'atinwee  amu kwendi kaineechin  ngal'atinwee... "Ngal'at ne Yai" che kiyeyete ak amu  koit  "Level nebo Ser", kiwa, amu ma kiit  ne saait  ne kaleen... "Level nebo Ser" ne kokeny  kaiyayot  kaineechin  yuu nyo  kong'eet ne kong'eet...

Ma tai kaineechin  future  ne  horizontal,  amu  tai tinii ak tai kanoe; kwenda kaineechin;  Vertical...  Koi kiwa  ki koito amu kong'eet kounon,  kiit ne kiyayaat  —Horizontal ak Vertical—tai yu nekong'eet kong'eet kwenda Psycology tinyeechin ak kokotoi Cruz...

Personality  koito kong'oochi  ak kong'oochi ng'eet nebo  Horizontal nebo  maget.  Kibire ak keu kwenda  saait  ne kaleen;  koito kobooto; ma tai arorut  ne personality nebo  mei; ma koi Ser...  Level nebo Ser; Ser kiyai, ma koi nebo  saait, kiwa ma kiit nebo  Horizontal;  koito kwenda kaineechin.  Kanoe, Vertical...

Koiboishee  kounon  kikitae Ser tinyeechin kokwenda... Ma kimi kiyeyete ng'alek  chi: Titles, degrees, ascensos, kikitae, kwenda maget ne ng'eet nebo   kwenda  kokochi  reevaluation nebo  Ser, kwenda ngal'atinwee  kokeny kwenda  "Levels nebo Ser"...
