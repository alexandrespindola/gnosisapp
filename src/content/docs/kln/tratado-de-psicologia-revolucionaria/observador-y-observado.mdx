Ko uu ole ng'alek che mengeista ak konyoru kuskus kobun, ko ki koek cheitei ko kachin komwa ole amu ki amu kounen ko nebo che mengeista kora.

Ng'a kitokchin, ng'a kotiok ole, ng'a kokoi nebo ketekchin kotoret Auto-observación Íntima, kiyok chekiyoksei: Mitomanía (Kebeechen nebo Teset, kowakchini kounen kilewenin), Egolatría (Kebeechen nebo Anai Chemenyei; kochiito anyun Alter-Ego), Paranoia (Kibagenge, Kotiok Kounen, kebeechen, kowakchini kobun ng'a kobokolech bik che chang).

Komwa ole kachil komie kokakiminei kilewenin ko Anai Chemenyei, kimei kometinyei kotoret kokasun cheitei kora. Ole kowakchini kilewenin, ma koyai komie katamokchin bik che ng'atet. Kotoi kilewenin ng'alek, ng'alek che yaachei, koboisyo, chemii, kotesta, ng'alek che boi, ng'alek che tebi, etc., etc., etc., kilewenin bik che machei, ma komie katamokchin, kotei ko katuiyee kora komwa ole bik che chang kamwa ole koboisyo ko ng'alek chebo kokwet...

Ole kowakchini Doctrina nebo Bik Chechang, ko kometinyei komie ole ng'alek chebo kowakchini koboisyo, ng'alek nebo akat, kilewenin chechang... Atleta nebo Auto-observación íntima, koboisyo kaskasun ak konere bik che ng'atet.

Komwa ole kachil ko yae ko ketinee ko kelii, kotei kaskasun: Ketinyei ak Ketinge. Komwa ole maketee kele, kometinyei kole matinee kesubkei ko mising nebo Auto-Conocimiento. Komeyai ketingeti kesubkei komwa ole mamatinee kele ketinyei ak Ketinge?

Komwa ole maketee kele, kometinyei kole matinee kesubkei kotinyei komie. Koo ole matinee, kometinyei kole konetingei kiyok Bik Chechang... Ole konetingei kiyok Bik Chechang, komei ko kiuwerei nebo ng'alek che boi.

Komeyai koyaole bik chebo kokwet konyoru bik chetei kora? Komeyai koeti ketinyei bik chetei kora ole ma ketinge kaskasun? Komeyai ketingeti kaskasun komwa ole mamatinee kele ketinyei ak Ketinge?

Amu anyun, ma koyai kiworu konyoru, kole ma kowakchini kole: "Ng'alek chemii kolewenin ainek kobun". "Ng'alek chemii che ma yae kilewenin ainek kobun". "Ng'alek chemii cheboi kipsengerei kilewenin ainek kobun". etc., etc., etc. Yaany kole, ma koyai ole matinee kele ketinyei ak Ketinge.

Ole kowakchini ng'alek chemii kilewenin Chemenyei, Melei, ak Chemenyei, konetingei bik chebo kokwet, kora kanyoru amun, ma koyai kora katamokchin bik chebo kokwet. Koi anyun, bik che ma koyai konyoru, bik che yaai.
