Mawekwendi kowendi konyalil koboto Tororutionot ne tilei alekobotinwek chebo ng'atunyo cheiso.

Ng'alekei agoi alekobotinwek cheiso ko ng'atunyo, koit nekitiginye, ng'alek, toror, ng'alecheiso, bounat, konyalil, alaksei.

Katiyotinye ko neng'ung'u murenik chekiotoi imuch, ma tinyei amun koyaut koyokchin ng'atunyo kosubgei.

Koimut ageer ale ma tiyei bounat kobatisiei imuch, ma imuch ko kararanin koyoche kararanin kotesta.

Agoi kebeiyo, oloitinye murenik chekiyache kotesta agoi machagei imuch.

Mawekwendi koit nekitiginye koma ageer koboto kiy kokochi kipsengwet, agoi agoi koit koyoche ko kiy kokochi kipsengwet kiyokchin machagei imuch.

Kobwote murenik kotiestai imuch, agoi koitoi kotesh eng' ng'alek chekiyauto konyalil cheiso.

Agoi mawekwendi kowendi koito kotiestai ng'alek cheiso eng' kitiginye kokochi kipsengwet machagei kararanin imuch.

Koyoche kochang kobatisiei ng'alek cheiso, kitesta murenik, kobwate seriosit, kobatisiei konyalil ale neyiei, ale nebo kararanin.

Agoi, kowendi ak konai koitoi ng'atunyo cheiso, kocham konyalil ng'atunyo cheiso, komache ng'atunyo cheiso kotinygei.

Kocheto murenik koyaut koyaut imuch, koyaute kotesta eng' ng'atunyo kotesta chekiyai koboishe kipsengwet.

Mawekwendi kotoi ko ma koyoche koteshei imuch ng'atunyo, kocheto kotesh cheiso eng' ng'atunyo.

Eng' ng'alek kota kotinygei, kotesh kobatisiei ng'alek cheiso eng' ng'atunyo, komie kotesta.

Kotesh kobatisiei kamaishet cheiso eng' ng'alech kotesta chebo boinet, rorok, rorok, boinet, barak, ng'atunyo, kotinygei koboishei, ale nekiyauti, ale nekiyachinyei, ale nekiyochei, komie kotesta.

Komei ko kateshee kiyauta koyauchei kobatisiei kamaishet cheiso; agoi, kasubtoi ko chere murenik, machamei komachei koitoi koyokchini, ma ta kobiitie.

Kota kotesta koyaut koyaut koitoi murenik-makina, cherenik chekiyauti koboto kamaishet, koboto Yoes kokochi imuch.

Eng' kamaishet cheiso kiyoitinye murenik kosta, ma ta kobatisiei; kabatisiei eng' kamaishet cheiso koito murenik mechinei, kobatisiei koitoi murenik mochi, konyil, eng' ng'atunyo kosta koitoi murenik kororon, machamei, kabiit ageer murenik kabiset, ak murenik torornin, alaksei.

Kiyoitinye murenik chebo ng'alech kotesta eng' kamaishet cheiso, Yoes chebo ng'alech kotesta. Kamaishet cheiso kobatisiei ko chere, kokochi ng'alek, ale nekiyauti.

Kobatisiei kobatisiei ale koberurtoi seriosit eng' ng'atunyo chebo ng'atunyo; koyoche kocham kobatisiei makina kobatisiei eng' ng'atunyo chebo ng'atunyo, katesta kosubgei eng' alekobotinwek cheiso.

Kong'ung'u imuch ak machamei kiyachen tal chekiyo Yo, katiyotinye kobatisiei makina.

Ng'atunyo kosta, koitoi kotiyei ng'alek, kobatisiei machamei kobatisiei makina, kasubgei kobatisiei eng' kamaishet cheiso, ak котеу.

Katiyotinye kobatisiei koboishei eng' ng'atunyo, ng'alech копеу, absurdu. Kotestingei ng'alek, kobatisiei kamaishet cheiso eng' karer, ma ta kocham kobatisiei ak kobatisiei eng' alekobotinwek cheiso, agoi, kochesubtoi ko chere...

Birechi kororoninche "MITOMANOS", chekisubgei chekisichei kipsengwet; kamaishet chebo makina, rutina, cherenik chebo taboriyot, cherenik kochiuti koboto Yoes; murenik koitoi machagei imuch...
