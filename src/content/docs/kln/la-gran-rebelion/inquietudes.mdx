Ame yale kokristo ne boit kiit ne ng'eet, ng'eet ne ng'etei.

Kongoiit ne boit maget chebo binadamu, ko ng'eteet ne yaani ole sikororchin, ole ma ng'etei.

Kongoiit kosubai eng' kwanit ole yaani ole ma yaani, kongoiit kobel oleng'u ne bo ng'eet, kobel motokaa ne bo ng'eet, kobel kobo kalyet ne yaani ko ole yaani.

Kongoiit kobo yaani koroit ne bo chi, mauneti ne bo betut, iyokisiet ne bo ng'eet, betut ne ketes, kikombe chebo biik, kondoiet chebo farasi, kondoiet chebo motokaa, korokto ne bo ng'ombe, kimong'et, ites, ak eng'alala.

Kamet, ng'etindet ne bo chi ak ng'etindet ne bo salon yaam ole kagosikin kobo esoterismo, amu ma o eng' kimnatetabchi, amu ma eng' iyokisietabchi, amu ma eng' maunetabchi, kagosukin ak ng'etindet ne bo yaan ole ng'eteet, ak kong'etei imiis, ak kong'ol ng'ombeet, ak kong'egei ak betut.

Apatia ne bo ng'eet, ng'eteet ne itegei, ko yaani kasubatin; karon, ng'eteet ne yaani, kakei, ng'eteet ne yaani kobo kiit.

Koboru kiit, borunet ne bo betut, amu ma koboru eng' tiende, amu ma koboru eng' ole kosubai kobo yaani, ak amu ma koboru eng' maunet chebo taani.

Ama neng'ung'u koyai chi ne bo ng'eet ne yaani, kobo ng'etindet ne bo yaani keborunet ne bo betut, kobo kimeng'et ne bo ng'eet, kobo kimnatet ne bo yaani, talas ko eng' amu ne bo ng'eet.

Amu o, yaani ole ing'aa chi ne bo ng'eet, kobo kiit ne yaani, kobo korokto ne bo tiende, kobo maunet ne bo yaani; talas tonet, sukari ne bo ng'eteet ne bo restaurante, ketunet ne bo ng'etindet ne ma yaani eng' ng'eet.

Maunet, eng'alala che ma koboru, ko yaani eng' betut ne yaani kobo ing'aa kiit ne bo kiit, kobo kimnatet ne bo kiit, kobo kimeng'et ne bo kiit, kobo korokto ne ma nganas.

Ama ale yaani kokristo ne kogasei kokwaan, kokristo ne bo ng'eteet chebo club, kokristo ne bo tich ne bo sambarini, kogoing'aa kiit ne bo kiit, ko kogo kosubai kowanendet chebo ng'eet, koboru chebo betut, kokristo ne bo kiit eng' ng'eet.

Kimeng'et, korokto, kimais ne bo ng'eet, ma nganas ne bo ng'eet, ko kogoing'aa chi ne bo taai, ko kogoing'aa chi ne bo mii, ko kogoing'aa doctor ne bo ng'eet, amu o, maunet chebo personality ko koging'aa kimeng'et ne bo betut; amu o, kokristo ne bo ng'eteet ne yaani.

Ame yale, kokristo ko kookoitei betut, ko yaani.

Maai chi ne ma kogoing'aa korokto, maai kiit ne bo ng'eet, amu o, yaani ole bo personality, yaani ole eng'alala, ko ing'aa chi ne yait amu kogoing'aa chi ne eng' ng'eteet ne ma nganas.

Betut ko kogingi, ko kobaru ak imbiet chebo biik.

Betut ko yaani kigakoni; chi ne bo betut, ole ma boru betut, ko ma yaani ak koigai eng' kwanit chebo taani.

Ama neng'ung'u koyai chi ne ma konai, kobo ing'aa kiit ne koboru talas eng' betut ne yaani, ame yale ko kosubai kowanendet chebo betut ak ko kogai chi ne bo betut.

Amu o, ole yaani chi, amu ale chi chebo betut ak chi chebo ng'eteet che yaani, ko kokristo ko kookoitei betut; amu o, koboru itet ne bo kwan.

Itet ko kogi ng'aang'a. Chi kogi ng'aang'a ole ma boru betut, ko kong'etei amu kokwaan amu betut ko kogitoi.

Betut ko kogingi chi, eng' kwanitetabchi, ole yaani eng' kwanitetabchi; amu o, chi ko kookoitei betut.

Amu ale kogasei, amu o, ma kotun kiit, ak ko kongoiit amu yai eng' laburet ne bo ng'eet; amu o, ma kotoi amu eng'alala, betut ko kogitoi.

Amu o, ale ma kotun chi, amu ma kotun chi, ko amu chi ko kongoiit eng' glandeet chebo taai chebo chi ne bo ng'eet, kimii chebo betut che neng'ung'u kosubai kosubai chi ne bo kiit.

Amu o, chi chebo betut ko yaani eng'alala amu ng'eteet ne bo betut.

Kokristo ma kokoyok chi ak amu ale yaani, kimii chebo betut ko kokwa eng' eng'alala ak ko kokosei.

Clavicula maestra ne bo tich chebo betut ko yai che yaani che ma kokoyan ole kaitet.

Yamun rais ne bo binadamu kogasei kobo kiit chebo betut, betut ko kogitoi amu ma kotun eng' kimnatetabchi.

Amu ale rais ne kasubai kogai betut, kogai yaani ak kogai chi ne maunet, amu o, ko kogitoi.

Kobo kobaru ing'aa kiit ne bo ng'eet ko koboru ing'aa maget ne bo kiit eng' esencia.

Desafortunadamente, kokristo ko kongoiit ing'aa maget eng' personalidad, eng' kafe, eng' cantina, eng' negocios chebo banco, eng' nyumba ne bo maunet, ak eng' plaza ne bo tiende.

Kamet, ame yaani, che yaani chebo personality ak ing'aa maget chebo amu yaani ko kogi amu yaani; ko yaani ak chi ne bo kwan ko koboru ak koyai.

Desgraciadamente, ole kogasei ame yaani, chi che ma kotos kotun ko kongoiit ak kokwa ak iginunet ne yaani, talas kong'ol kitabu ak kogasei gazeti.

Kongot chebo kafe ak maunet chebo betut ko amu chi chebo ng'eteet.

Amu o, kongoiit kobel ole yaani; amu o, kokwan ko kokwa chi, ak ame yaani chebo betut ko kokweti. Ame yale ko ma ko kosobai chi ne bo rani kotun.
