KOKWANEET

Ne: V.M. GARGHA KUICHINES

“KOGEREN PEET” chebo Ketorosiek Maestro Samael Aun Weor ko kiimei kotesta eng’ ng’alek chebo muut eng’ ng’wony.

Kong’ete ng’alek chebo kotes keguurech kotesta eng’ ng’wony.

Agoi agoot kowendi koot konomi bik chetiendo koyai koger eng’ batalle ne keguurech.

Ng’alek tugul chebo rani koleenji konyookto yoik chetyo koropkei esensiya ne kowendi eng’ komieget.

Yook ko magete kareta agoi ko maiyos eng’ keer ne imuch.

Eng’ ng’wony komie bik che magete ak kakerian kotesta tugul.

“MAITEI NG’ALEK CHEMAGETE, AGOI KO MIE BIK CHEMAGE KARETA”.

# KOKWANEET 1

Bik tugul magete keguurech eng’ koret; ng’alechepso kotesta tugul. Keyai piyet magete. Kerit koit tuguk. Maitei loloto amu bik kokonjere ak kobekse.

Ng’alek chebo bik che konjeron kaguurech bik chetooru tugul.

# KOKWANEET 2

Maa ak kobekse kotesta eng’ tukuk ak tuguk ak ng’alek chemang’u kosoba atimosfer chebo ng’wony, agoi koit kokwet kobun ng’alek chetooru kowendi: “Kasitat ne Sayans” amei koineti ng’alek chemang’u kobelchi energi eng’ laboratoriet chetyo ak ko kiimei eng’ Lee ak Moto eng’ betusiek che kotamete komang’unji ng’alek sisit ne kakimong’ eng’ kongoi: 1. Kareta chetyo. 2. Yai miondo chebo Yoo eng’ komieget. 3. Kinyooku Biiyot (Bik).

Taai, piich, ak mulul, kosobu amu kabisa ne bo yoo; maikiit kupa kobel kobun ng’alek chetooru; kikweny ng’alek chemang’u kobelchi, ng’alek chemang’u eng’ ng’wony, kotamnete komang’unji eng’ koi komang’unji, agoi kobelji bik ak kinyooku eng’ kongoi ne kakimong’.

Kokoiyai Askerisiet chebo Serikali chebo Ng’wony koleenji bik tugul chetiendo che konomjii ak Avatara nebo Akwariyus, koorokchini Dotirina chebo Kristofikasyon ne kobun keguurech eng’ ng’alek chetooru tugul.

Ama kotes kotesta ng’wony.

# KOKWANEET 3

Eng’ bik konomunji kokwet, maitei eng’ ng’aleche chetyo eng’ chetyo kotesta, kotesta ak ng’alek chemang’u, Simiente chetyo.

Betusiek che kwendete kobendete ko konamon, agoi ko maikiit tukuk tugul; ama kemiut kaguurech tililet chetyo, kon’geer eng’ tililet chetyo, amu ko mago konamon eng’ ng’alek tugul. Ko miit eng’ ng’wony agoi ko mongeer eng’ tililet chetyo.

# KOKWANEET 4 KOLEENJI KOGUURECH KOIYOKTINDA

Koioktinda kobeu, kwendete koteiyoktu, agoi koteiyai ng’aleche korok bich ak konyekse ak komi kon’geer ak komi bik chemang’u.

Yu ne kirorchi ng’alek chemang’u, ko kiimei bik chemang’u ne kotiit, amu yuu koma bik che tiit, kwendete keyai amu tuiyet, ak kotooru, yu ne kirorchi kayai koleenji komie bik chemang’u ne tuguurech. “Agiitee Ng’alek che Miotin ak koioktu”. Agoi amuunei kobendi kobun Ng’alek che Miotin bik che mirireti? Eng’ betusiek chebo ko magete kaguurech.

Ng’alek che Miotin ko ng’alek chebo Tata ne bendete, kobun ak Yai. Amuunei kobendi kobun Yai bik che mirireti, ama kiiti Ng’alek chebo Tata? Tiisiek chebo Tata maget kobun bik che plon ng’alek, yoik, ak konjeksei kobun eng’ imuch ak konjeksei. Kon’geer eng’ ng’alek chetyo; koropchi bik che kimong’u eng’ keeti ng’aleche che kimong’u eng’; yu kayai kolenji ng’alek eng’ komieet koteiyai, ko koit kokupa ak ng’alek tugul.

“Amuunei koioktu? Amuunei koi keguurech koteiyai? Eng’ amun kochooktoi kobendi? Ay!, Ay!, Ay!”, (Samael). Bik che mirireti magete kobendi eng’ koioktin amu komeei eng’ tata ne kiimei koleenji ng’alek che miotin.

# KOKWANEET 5 KOLEENJI KOGUURECH KONG’ET NEBO PEMBET

Ngalek tugul kobel ak koebel, koteu ak kootin, konya ak konya; agoi kogoo bik kobel amu bendi nebo kokwet ne kimie amu bendi nebo kokwet ne kimie koyai eng’ kokosik ne bo kimieet, koineti ng’alek chemang’u ko kagoo bendi nebo kokwet, agoi amuunei eng’? Betusiek che bo bik komai yoik keguurech kobel, agoi koteiyoktu eng’ betusiek tugul.

Kokwet tugul ko konam eng’ betusiek tugul, agoi kwendete kobel agoot.

Ng’alek chebo kutesta eng’ mwaat kobun ng’alek ne keguurech ak konamei eng’ betusiek. “Kogerenyo ng’alek chetooru eng’ bendi nebo Ng’alek che miotin” (Samael).

# KOKWANEET 6 KOIMEI NG’ALEK AK BETUSIEK

Koiyai kobelchi bik konomi bik konyookto kobun ng’alek che bo ng’alek che bo ng’alek chetooru; beta komei yook, yoik, amu kotesta eng’ ng’alek tugul, yuu eng’ ng’alek ne: “Kayai amu kwachooti eng’”, ko eng’ ne bo yook, ng’alek tugul ko mie arisiet, ak ng’alek che tuguurech eng’, betusiek kobel bik che maiyoseng’ komiotineng’, kobo ng’alek koleenji ng’alek koleenji bik.

# KOKWANEET 7 NG’ALEK CHEBO KONG’OI

Kotamete ak kotesta ng’aleche, kobun kimong’u kobel eng’ kimong’u.

Bik chetuguurech kosobi energi chebo ng’alek che bo kimong’u eng’ beta konye ng’alek chetiendo koleenji betusiek chetiendo.

Maestro ne tamete, kitesta eng’ ng’alek, kaye kobelchi ng’alek, kimii koleenji bik eng’ cirko ne bo ng’wony; yuu eng’ cine, bik kobel komiotineng’ eng’ koleenji amei koleenji. Maestro eng’ ng’wony ko yu kolenji bik ng’alek chemie ak konyookto, kayai kotesta ng’alek eng’ chemie, Mam chebo teiyet koot, ak bik kowendi ak koret.

“Kong’oi ko lee, agoi ko bik chemagon eng’ komiotineet” (Samael Aun Weor) bik che komagon kokwanje ak lee ne bo kong’oi, amei kobelchi bik che meeut ak lee ne bo Cheptalephut.

Betusiek che kotes kong’oi eng’, koiit kobelchi eng’.

# KOKWANEET 8 NG’ALEK CHEBO SAYANS

Bik eng’ betusiek chebo tetutiet eng’ teiyet kobekse ak komirire betusiek. Sayans kotesta ak koi ng’alek chemagei kobel bik che magete kareta.

Kimie millionesiek che tamete eng’ ng’alek chebo kimieet eng’, agoi maitei betusiek chebo kareta.

Bik kotamete ng’aleche chebo tiendo eng’, agoi maitei komang’unji ng’aleche chetiendo: miondo che kiinji ng’wony eng’ betusiek; bik amu komang’unji, koleenji laboratorium ak kokwet eng’, agoi koleenji ng’alek ko kotes, kamai yook, yoik, amu kotesta eng’, agoi maiko, koleenji kabatiset koiito.

# KOKWANEET 9 KOBETET

Komei eng’ keguurech. Mayai koma kobendi kobun Tata ne bendete. Agoi betusiek che mang’unji eng’ eng’ ng’alek tugul.

Kobetet kotooti ng’alek chebo koret chebo Yai, Kobekse, Koboot, amu kotesta eng’. “Bik” kobendi koleenji sayans eng’ ak kotesta.

# KOKWANEET 10 YOO CHEBO SAIKOLIJIA

Kwende kobelchi eng’ betusiek tugul, kotakamei ama ng’alek che iyete eng’ kotes, amu koger eng’ bik kokwet kobun bik chetuguurech. Kinyookto ko koleenji koger eng’ bik che magete kareta, agoi kobelji betusiek chebo koger eng’ eng’ keguurech betusiek chemang’u, agoi kotes kokwa kaguurech eng’ Kristo ne kimii eng’ keguurech komiotineet kobel kotes bik.

Kobelchi kotooru, bik tugul kotamete, agoi kobelchi koret, ko ko maa.

Belchi eng’ komiotineet bik konomi bik, ama kwendete kobun keman.

# KOKWANEET 11 KOOI KOKWANEET 20

Bik konamon koleenji ng’alek, kobel bik yuu eng’ ng’alek che kogeer, agoi maitei bik kote amete eng’, ko ko yu kowendi eng’ bendi nebo Kristofikasyon.

Yu kokweet miriret kotaa; Lee eng’ kong’oi ak betusiek che kotaa eng’ eng’ keguurech, koleenji kenyook bik. “Ng’alek chemiee ko koot” ko kaa Yesu Kristo.

Koma kowendi ng’alek chekayai. !!!Nostik… kimong’!!!

Bik chebo tililet amei kong’oi komiotin eng’ kayai amei ng’alek chebo tililet amei ng’oi. Yu bik eng’ bik koot ng’alek chebotesto, kimirire amei kobelji bik ak ng’alek ne koleenji Mirire ne komie eng’.

Beta komi lee komi kong’oi. Mire ko ng’alek chemang’u, maitei eng’ lee.

Eng’ kokwaneet 12 koleenji keguurech tililet siiset: Tilet ne tuguurech eng’ ak bik, Tilet ne bo kimie; yuu eng’ betusiek tugul ak koot eng’ koleenji amei koleenji; beta kochob kong’oi eng’, koiit konyookto, koyai bik konomi eng’.

Yu kimii yai kobendi, kimirireti; mirire magete kobendi kobun yai, amu koiti ng’alek chebo Tata ak betusiek chebo ng’wony; ak tilet che miotin, kobelji eng’ betusiek che kokwa kareta eng’ bik chetiendo.

Bik che tamete kareta eng’ yoik, kobel ak kororo ak ko kiimei kokwet ak maminyu RAM-IO, kobun kogeer eng’ eng’ yoo tugul.

Yoo, ego, ko ng’alek chemaget eng’ keguurech; Yu ne bendete kolenji kotes eng’ keguurech, eng’ Psiquis chetyo.

Kogeren ne kotes ko kitoor eng’ bik ne bik. Beta kobelchi kwakwa kareta kobelchi koleenji ng’alek chemang’u eng’ bik.

Yu kobelchi keguurech, kobiit kotooru eng’ bik.

Yuu ak tamete, kobelji konyookto; kobun eng’ tililet. Kobel, beta kotonje bik, ko bik.

“Kimeyet kotesta ak koleenji bik, amei kobelchi bik che meeut, amei kobelchi komie ak maminyu” (Samael).

Mam chebo teiyet chebo Marah, Mariam amei RAM-IO kokolentei eng’ gnostik, ko Yu ne bo Tata ne bendete ak keguurech, amei ng’alek chebo teiyet ak mamu; kochob eng’ ak kooteng’, teiyet chebo teiyet kotesta. Ko Deva, koleenji ngalek chebo teiyet ak miondo chebo tik.

Beta kogerei ak Saserdotisa, ko bik kobeu ak bik, eng’ Mam RAM-IO ko yu kokwa kareta eng’ yoik. Ak ng’alek chetuguurech eng’ magete kote eng’ ngalek chebo Koo, amu kobelchi amei betusiek, vicios, bik chebo tililet, bik chebo kimio, amu kobelchi eng’ tililet, ngalek tugul.

Eng’ kokwaneet 18, eng’ bendi ne bo koleenji ngalek koleenji eng’ teiyet chemang’u kobelji keguurech. Belchi eng’ kobelchi kolenji amuunei kolenji amuunei eng’ karanet che kogeer eng’.

Betusiek che kotamete maminyu RAM-IO kokwa yoik kotesta ak koteiyoktu eng’ ng’alek chebo betusiek. Ngaleche nekotesta kobun tililet chetyo.

Bik che mirireti kotou eng’ Tata ak bik che kotou eng’ Roho Muyetu; eng’ tililet, ng’alek ak koot.

Kimie bik kotin ak eng’, bik konomi bik, amei kobelchi eng’ kikwaanjet, eng’ kwakwa ko bik kogerei, kolenji koger.

Eng’ kokwaneet 19, eng’ komiotinet chebo bik kotit eng’ miondo chebo Avatara; ne bo kayai ngalek chemie kokwenje.

Yai ko ngalek che miotin, eng’ betusiek tugul chebo ego keeti ng’alek (Samael).

Eng’ kokwaneet 20 kobel kokwa kokwet eng’ kiir chemwaii ak kobel.

# KOKWANEET 21 KOOI KOKWANEET 29

Eng’ 21 koleenji keguurech kobelchi betusiek che kobel kokwa kaguurech eng’.

Eng’ 22 koleenji “KOYONG’U AK KOREEK”. Kometamete eng’ bendi nebo kororo; ama magete koyaeng’ koleenji betusiek, kobelji kobai yoik. Kote kobelji kotes komiutiisiek chemie.

Kristo ko moto nebo moto; eng’ kogeer ak kobel ko miondo chebo moto nebo Kristo. Konyorokchini moto nebo Kristo eng’ bik tugul. Eng’ moto nebo komiotinet tugul ak kokwete komiotinet.

“Bik chetuguurech kobelchi kote tiendo eng’, kowendi kotestai”.

Eng’ kokwaneet 25, Maestro koleenji keguurech koleenji ngalek chebo keguurech, ngalek chebo sine.

Ng’alek tugul kobel bik chemamwaati, bik che mwaati; kowendi kotesta ng’alek tugul kobel bik tugul ak kobel ng’alek tugul beta kokwa kareta.

Eng’ kokwaneet 26 koleenji ng’alek sisit, kotuutii Kristo, ngalek chemang’u: 1.- Tilet 2.- Mang’unji Bik 3.- Konjeksei

Beta kimii eng’ Psiquis ngalek sisit.

Kolenji Kristo eng’ eng’, kobel kote bik. Eng’ kokwaneet koleenji Kristo eng’ bik, kobel kobun bich.

Eng’ kokwaneet 28, koleenji ng’alek keguurech Bik Ne kotes.

Kokwa konjeksei koleenji Bik Ne kotes kayai eng’ keguurech, amu kotesta eng’ bik chemisio.

Eng’ kokwaneet 29, eng’ kobate, koleenji keguurech Kobu, Kobu nebo Solomon; kobu ng’aleche chebo Yoni.

Kobu magete eng’ Kobu tugul, amei eng’ Kobu ne bo teiyet.

Betusiek chebo gnostik kokwat ng’alek tugul kobel kaguurech eng’ ng’wony.

Ama loloto eng’ tililet chebo yoo.

GARGHA KUICHINES
