Achicha ketes kotinye koba, anin koyai eng’ kergeiyot neng’o Kundalini, iyook che po mwaiguk cheiso, chekiyokchini eng’ ndererek chebo geer nebo Sabati nebo Asia.

Kong’etegei, Kundalini inee eng’ lakwet che mengelei ak keng’etegei koboishee.

Eng’ ndererek nebo Alquimia nebo katamweet, Kundalini inee leek nebo asterisko nebo itik nebo ng’eet, STELLA MARIS, MWAET NEBO PARAK, keyaarei boisionik chebo Boisioni Neng’o.

Eng’ kakeeti nebo aztecs inee TONANTZIN, eng’ kakeeti nebo Greeks inee CASTA DIANA, ak eng’ Egypt inee ISIS, MAMAI NEBO TAWEET, ng’aalet agei ma kotupchi lakwet.

Ameiyo ko ma ng’aleek chebo Kristiano nebo Esoteric ma ko magat Mama nebo Taweet nebo Kundalini; kiyookchini inee MARAH, andoi ko RAM-IO, MARIA.

Ng’aleek chema kibwote religianik chebo ortodoxa, andoi eng’ kergeiyot nebo exotérico andei buren, inee kergeiyot nebo ISIS eng’ buren nebo kokoch nebo binadamu.

Kiyookchini, eng’ siriet kigochin bik chemiit neng’waan Mama nebo Taweet eng’ kokoch nebo binadamu agei agei.

Ma miach kotonon eng’ ng’alek chemengelei ko ng’aleek nebo Nyamnyam-Mama, REA, CIBELES, ADONÍA andei ko kityo koboishee, inee agenge nebo Serik cheiso agei agei agoi.

Eng’ bwotet, kotes ko agei agei chemie Mama nebo Taweet nebo chito agei agei, individual.

Mi Mama che mengelei eng’ yatebik andoi kakeeti chemiit eng’ baro nebo ng’wony.

Kundalini inee barak nebo siriet nekeer ng’wony, kergeiyot nebo BRAHMA.

Eng’ kergeiyot nebo psicolojia neng’et eng’ anatomia nebo siriet nebo binadamu, KUNDALINI inee kireren eng’ borusiek somok ak kimnat eng’ barto nebo magnetik nebo it nebo koktaret.

Agoi kereek chemiit kokong’eten andoi iyoot kererek nebo Taweet.

Eng’ barto nebo Chakra andei rani, mi triangolo nebo korgoret andei YONI andei LINGAM nebo korg’wet.

Eng’ LINGAM nebo atomiki andei mwaiguk nekeer barak nebo BRAHMA, kererek nebo sublime nebo KUNDALINI.

Rani nebo mwaik eng’ kergeiyot nebo iyoot, keyai ak siriet nebo siriet nebo artificio nebo alquimista nekeerchi eng’ boisionik, kotinygei: «Siriet nebo Aureo Florecer».

Kong’etegei, ngo keyai barak nebo taweet, koboot eng’ kornata nebo medula espinal koborjin eng’ kergesiek che ko taweeshee.

Eng’ kergeiyot nebo trascendental nebo divinal nebo subliminal, iyoot nebo siriet nebo taweet kotes fisiolojia, anatomi, eng’ state nebo etnico, inee kotes Serik cheiso, andoi ko kibwote.

Ma inee boroto ang’at eng’ tratat neba ng’aleek nebo keyai iyoot nebo siriet nebo taweet.

Anin koyai ng’etundit eng’ boroto nebo Ego ak urgencia nebo ng’etundit eng’ karareng’anet nebo elementos nebo inhumanos.

Mente sisyai ma kotees karareng’anet agei nebo psicolojia.

Mente sisyai kolotet ng’aleek, kotes ko boroto agei, kotoshe ng’aleek ak bik alak, komong’u andei kotoshe lakwet.

Ng’etundit inee barto nebo fundamental, andoi ko ma kokwer agei, kobwotei.

Ng’aleek ne kiit komong’u kobwotei eng’ bwotet andei komwaii.

Kobwotei barak che teret mente, barak nebwa koten karene eng’ atomiko yo-defecto nekeerchi kotup ak kitupchin.

Teng’wong’woo, barak koot subyace eng’ boroto nebo kokwet, nebo afectos ak mente, agoi mi exponentes eng’ it nebo koktaret, andei konegee eng’ párrafos chebo katunisia nebo capítulo nebo yongoi.

Kikotupchin yo-defecto, ko kiitet eng’ meditación, kosup, komwoe, kong’etegei Mama nebo Taweet nebo binadamu yo-defecto eng’ bwotet.

Ineee ng’aleek nebo precisa ne kibwotei eng’ karareng’anet nebo elementos che ma ko chito nebo yongoi.

Mama nebo Taweet nebo Kundalini inee barak nebo kutup agregados nebo psíquico nebo subjetivo, inhumanos.

Sisyai ng’aleek, sisyai procedura, eng’ bwotet eng’ karareng’anet nebo Ego kibwotei yoob, ma ng’wan.
