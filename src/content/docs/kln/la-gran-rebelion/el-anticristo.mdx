Ọng'onyiit ak menyet ichek che chang'anygei eng' Yoetab psicolojia, ng'alek che miach, amu ng'o ANTICRISTO.

Keityo koger ANTICRISTO ko chebo biik alak che ng'o che kiyook eng' aleek ak aleek, konom koger kokonyi ng'o.

Kityo kaat kounot ANTICRISTO amu ng'o bik alak, ko bik alak alak.

Kamwa ANTICRISTO eng' ng'eetab biik alak ak koiib kou ng'aleet alak alak.

Intelect eng' tisaatab roor ko kemwei; intelect che kilas roor amu ng'o kemwei.

Kobwaat eng' intelectualismo amu ng'o roor kobwaat icheek che miach, kou maanetab ANTICRISTO.

Kamwa icheek che miach amu ng'o ANTICRISTO. Kakisto icheek che miach ak kemacheek ang'wan eng' ANTICRISTO.

Aleek che boiboiyot keguure eng' ANTICRISTO.

Ng'aleet che kaat biik kemacheek keguure eng' ANTICRISTO.

Inicuo che kaat Pablo eng' warik che kiboishee.

Inicuo kiyook ak ko ibwaat eng' aleek alak alak.

Korwet eng' cafat, koboishee eng' ONU, kotap eng' Ginebra, koboishee eng' laboratorio, kookei bombas atómicas, cohetes teledirigidos, gases asfixiantes, bombas bacteriológicas, alak alak.

Kekochi ANTICRISTO eng' intelectualismot, kogeer amun koon kotestai ng'alek che kiimkei chebo ng'eet.

ANTICRISTO kogeer amun koon kotestai kotestai eng' ng'alek che kimochek, kogeer amu koger kamachei amu ng'o Mungu.

Auto-suficiencia chebo ANTICRISTO, kibendi ak kibendi amu ng'o amuneito.

ANTICRISTO komachei icheek chebo cristiano chebo fe, kipoman ak kibendi.

Bik alak kotap eng' ANTICRISTO. Kookei aviones ultrasónicos, barcos maravillosos, flamantes automóviles, medicinas sorprendentes, alak alak.

Eng' aleek chei, nani ketesee eng' ANTICRISTO? Nani kebiit kotesta ng'alek che kiimkei chebo ng'eet chebo ANTICRISTO, kiguure amun eng' ng'alek che boiboiyot, eng' sarcasmo, eng' ironía, eng' ng'aleet che boiboiyot.

Kimatamun kotesta biik che miach ak che keguure eng' boiboiyot, icheek ko kibure, kogobet.

Kamwa chebo animal intelectual keguure biik, ko robot che ki programmed eng' kinder, primarias, secundarias, preparatoria, universidad, alak alak.

Amu programmed ko koboishee.

ANTICRISTO kookei programmed che kikoboishee bik.

Kitetee eng' boiboiyot, amu ng'o robot kamwa amu programmed.

Kimatamun robot kamwa amu programmed.

Ng'aleet amu ng'o robot; ng'aleet alak, kibendi alak.

Kogobet robot ak kogeer amun kibet amu ng'o programmed.

ANTICRISTO kookei programmed chebo robot, robot kotap eng' amu. Kamwa robot amun?

Kiyook ng'atwet eng' kwenut; esencia kotestai eng' aleek alak ko miach.

Bik alak che kiyook kotestai.

Kamwa program chebo ANTICRISTO kogure.

ANTICRISTO kogeer kotesta aleek alak; bik alak che kiyook, kotestai programmed.

ANTICRISTO komachei "facultad de percepción instintiva de las verdades cósmicas".

Ciencia, amu ng'o programmed che ki programmed eng' university.

Kemacheek, kobwaat ak kemacheek eng' ANTICRISTO.

Kakisto katasta.
