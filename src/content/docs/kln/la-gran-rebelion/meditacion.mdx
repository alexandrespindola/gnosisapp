Kotyennyi ko uu ipej ak kamanut che mengech, kokochi ak kobendi; ng'alek alak komie amu amu.

Kametitiosiek kaimet konyor kolenji kikiyokchin kele kitok koirotyi.

Ma kiteechen kametitiosiek chebendi ng'alek, chebendi kogoito ak chemengech.

Komei kole kitoitu ak kibendi ng'alek chechutok chemengech cheitita barabara cheitei seudoesoterismo ak seudo-ocultismo chepochok.

Komei kole kitoitu, komei kole kibendi inee katinyi kele ma kitoor kokochi ng'alek cheitei esoteriko.

Nee ma kitooru kametitiosiek, nee ki menyeitit, nee ki metukulyo, ma koyai kokochi Ego; koito koyai kochepak chebendi ng'alek kokochi koito.

Kochepak chemiat kokochi kobendi ng'alek kokochi kametitiosiek.

Tingeigei itetitit ng'alek cheitei kokochi metet, matee kotelel kele ng'alek cheitita kaimet.
