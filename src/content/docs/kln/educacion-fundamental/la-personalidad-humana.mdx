Neet koito, kobu kesis ne arawetab sukut ne kobendi.  Akanie kobendi kwong'u ne kimwaita 1900 ak ako kobendi kasarta ne kimwaita 1965? Sayansi ne kiitet kora mait nebo aeng' konetik.  Amait koboishee ng'alekab koi koito ak koi kobendi.

Kwondokto kachil: "AENG' OBENDI AMET NE BOISHE CASARTANEI, MAKITO KASEI TORORINNO NE OBOENDI".

Arawani koit ne bo casarta, ng'eeti koito ne bo casarta, kenyit koito ne bo casarta ak ng'eetiik che mengeet kora koi koboishee AENG' NE BO KOITO.

Casarta koit ne bo kelyek ak koito nebo AENG' ne bo NG'ALEK koit ne bo kelyek ne kimong'unji.

Koito nebo AENG' nebo NG'ALEK koit ne bo casartanei, kobu casartanei ak kobendi casartanei, mataito kebendi koigaset casartanei.

Amait nebo casarta koit ne kibichu kotinyei bik che mengeet.  Eng' kimnget, casarta koit ne bo KAVANIS NEBO ANAN.

Geometria nebo EUCLIDES koboishee konyoru kotinyei ng'atunyi che bo KAKONIS KONAN ne kakei ng'atunyi koit ab tisabune ak KAVANIS KOIT CASARTA.

Mindi nebo aeng' koboishee KILEK casartane koing'atunyi chemengeet ng'alek, makito kwondoktoi eng' amait ne bo amu amu KILEK koit NEET NEBO ANAN.

Kasa ne bo koito kogashee casartanei ak konyoru kogashee KILEK.

Kobendi ak KOITO koitab nganaset che mengeet.  Koito kobendi tororini ne kobendi, konoru koi kobu ak konoru koi kobunji, kobendi koi kimongu eng' KILEK NE KIMONG'UNJI NETA.

Amu amu kiboishee konyoru, koiboishee kogashee konyoru konyoru koito amu amu DRAMANE nebo koito, imuchi si, NG'ALEK nebo AENG' korirwok eng' kobendi, ak konoru koit?

Koboishee kararan kobendi kobendi amuu, konoru koit che bo KORIRWOK, konoru koit kogashee ng'ony ne bo chokotinyei.

Koboishee kararan mamane kotinyei bik che somok kolejin NEBO KORIRWOK ak mising' NEBO REENCARNACIÓN ne kigaskei eng' TEOSOFÍA NE BO LEEL.

Kolejin REENCARNACIÓN kigobu eng' itet nebo KRISHNA ne koitab RELIGIÓN nebo INDOSTAN ne bo Védico, ng'alek che mengeet kogasakei ak kobek bik che korirente.

Eng' itet ne kwondok ne bo Krishna, kagasakei KORIRWOK, Bik Che Kitegen, bik che koi tetut, bik che keguire INDIVIDUALIDAD ne BO YAIK kagasakei kogashee.

AENG' NE KITINYEI NE BO KORIRWOK, kogashee asiche REENCARNACIÓN.  Masanii bik, muren KORIRWOK, ak asiche REENCARNACIÓN.

Kolejin ne bo KORIRWOK nebo ng'alek ak imuchik, kolejin ne bo kogashee KILEK mataito yotok ak konoru eng' SABIDURÍA NE BO PITAGÓRICA ak eng' cosmogonía ne bo INDOSTAN.

KILEK NEBO KORIRWOK nebo ARAWETIK AK NG'EETIK nebo BRAHAMA, kogashee imuchik nebo KALPAS, ak kigasak kogashee kotinyei SABIDURÍA NE BO PITAGÓRICA ak kolejin ne bo RECURRENCIA ne bo KILEK NEBO KORIRWOK.

Gautama, BUDHA kigasakei tetut NEBO DOCTRINA NE BO KILEK NEBO KORIRWOK ak kelyek nebo kasatet che mengeet, ne kakei DOCTRINANEI kigasakei bik che somok.

KORIRWOK koboishee kotetut kobu NG'ALEK NEBO AENG', amait kibune arawetik sobeng' kwong'u nebo kimet.

Aeng' ne bo familia, koito nebo barabara ak somok, kobu NG'ALEK NEBO AENG', kiitet nebo kwong'uno kararan.

NGETUNO NEBO BIK CHENGEET koboishee tetut ne bo ngechek nebo kimet.

Ngetuno nebo kimet keguire amu amu ne bo kara.  Koi ne kitet nebo koito, ngetuno ne kiguire, miache ne bo bik che mengeet kobu NG'ALEK nebo kimet, ng'eeti ne botete ak koi koboishee kasarta ne kobendeni.

Kasartet ne kobendeni adulterio kogasakei amu amu ne bo mbata ak tomatiak ak koi koboishee amu amu ng'etundo eng' betutik.

Amu amu koitoshek che koboishee koyai bik che chep koboishee kotinyei, bikab kaguure ak miache, kobendi ng'orotet ak itik nebo itita ne bo papa o nebo mama.  Kagere eng' koi koboishee NG'ALEK nebo kimet kogeere eng' kaguure, kimangat ak kimiat.

Koboitoshee ng'alek: "Ng'wanen nebo kimet kokobendi ng'oleek che miache kogashee".  Kagere amuu, ng'alek che mengeet kaguire amuu ak ng'alek che mengeet koit.

Kimangatet eng' papa ak mama eng' amu amu che bo kimiat, keeyai ak kogere eng' kimangatet o ng'eeti ne kimangatet o kimait, kochob NG'ALEK nebo kimet ng'eeti che miachet ak kotoret amu amu ne kibendi koito.

Eng' betutik che bo leel mamaik che kitegen kobek ng'aleek che chang eng' bikap korotinyei eng' salón de belleza o kokorok.  Ng'eeti nebo mamaik kotoret amu amu.

Kimet ne keguure ng'alek che bo amu amu koboishee keeyai amu amu eng' betuni ne bo mama o kotinyei ng'aleek ne bo bikap korotinyei ak ng'etundo ne bo katastrófico ne bo NG'ALEK NEBO KINET.

Amu amu kiibune televisión kiparei aeng' nebo familia.  Kasatet che mengeet aeng' kogashee eng' barabara ak kosome mamaik kotinyei keeyai.  Leel mamaik maibute kosom aeng' eng' katibit amu amu koboishee keguure televisión.

Eng' betutik che kobendeni papa, mama, ng'alek, koineng' ng'eeti ne kobendi eng' pantalla nebo televisión.

Leel aeng' mataito konete mamaik eng' ng'alek che bo arawani, koboishee, ak keng'eet amu amu koboishee keguure película nebo kesiran, ng'alek che bo Al Capone, rukuruk nebo leel, ak keng'eet.

Koineng' ng'aleek kobune kogashee eng' betutik che kobendeni kiboishee kwong'u ng'alek, machootik, matukche, kogashee kogeere ng'alek che bo amu amu eng' pantalla nebo televisión.

Kiyai koboishee televisión kogeere koboishee eng' imuchik che miache.  Imuchi bik koboishee kogeere koboishee eng' amu amu, kogashee kotinyei sayansi chebo ng'ony, kogashee kiguure koito nebo YAIK NEBO NATURA, kogashee kigasakei bik, ng'eeti koboishee kararan keguure ng'alek nebo aeng'.

Koboishee kiutet koboishee NG'ALEK NEBO KINET eng' musica ne miache, imuchik nebo kararan. Koboishee kopan NG'ALEK nebo ng'alek, eng' ng'alek che bo ng'alek ak polisiak, amu amu ne kitoret ak konyoru, dramane nebo adulterio, pornografía.

Kogure eng' kiitet koboishee kogeere eng' bik che kobendi amu amu, bik che komait kogashee.

Kiyai koboishee mamaik kosob ng'aleek nebo chebotinyei, kogashee kogeere eng' ng'alek che kitoret ak kobendi.  Kogure eng' kiitet koboishee kimangat, kimiat, kotor eng'.

Eng' boishee kikeguure bik che kibune eng' ng'alek, chebotinyei ak kogasakei eng' bik che kotoret eng' ng'alek ak mataito kotinyei keeyai.

Kobendi kogue komashee eng' bikap betutik.

Koboishee koboishee kikoguure eng' kaguegure koboishee kotinyei.

Papa koing'eete amuu, mama koing'eete keeyai.  Papa kotore SABIDURÍA.  Mama kotore AMOR.

SABIDURÍA ak AMOR, koboishee keeyai eng' kaguegure.

Papaik ak mamaik koboishee koboishee koboishee kotoret bikap betutik.

Koboishee kogue koboishee bikap papaik ak mamaik komashee ng'aleek eng' mindi nebo ng'aleek koboishee kasartet NEBO YAIK NEBO KILEK.

Kiyai koboishee ng'aleek che kobendeni maiboishee itet nebo VENERACIÓN, amu amu ng'aleek che bo vaqueros ladrones ak polisiak, televisión, cine.

PSICOLOGÍA NE KITORENTE nebo MOVIMIENTO GNÓSTICO, eng' ng'alek che kararan boishee tetut eng' EGO ak ESENCIA.

Koboishee kenyisek sobeng' kwong'u nebo kimet, kogashee eng' ng'alek ng'aleek ne miachet nebo ESENCIA, ng'alek koito, miachet, murenek eng' aspecto Psicológicos.

Casarta EGO koboishee koguure tetut nebo kimet ng'alek che bo ESENCIA koboishee koror ak konoru ng'aleek che miachet Psicológicos nebo bik che korirente.

Amu amu koboishee kikogurei eng' EGO ak ESENCIA koboishee koboishee kikogurei eng' NG'ALEK ak ESENCIA.

Aeng' kobune kotinyei ESENCIA ne kakei kabune kotinyei NG'ALEK, amu amu koboishee kibuune.

NG'ALEK ak ESENCIA koboishee kogeere eng' kararan ak kaguegure.

Eng' boishee kikeguure casarta NG'ALEK koboishee kogeere kotinyei ESENCIA, kogure koit BRIBÓN.

Ngetuno ak kiboishee bik che mengeet kekomashee koboishee casarta ESENCIA koboishee kogeere eng' NG'ALEK, kogure koit místico mataito intelecto, mataito ng'alek, ng'eeti ne murenek ne mataito kobendi.

Koboishee eng' NG'ALEK ak ESENCIA kogeere bik che kibendi amu amu.

Eng' ESENCIA kotinyei imuchik che bo ng'ony, eng' NG'ALEK kotinyei ngetuno nebo bik che mengeet, amu amu kikoguure eng' bikap betutik, eng' somok.

Koboishee kogue koboishee ng'aleek koboishee amu amu ne bo ESENCIA ak amu amu ne bo NG'ALEK.

ESENCIA koboishee kotinyei ng'alek che miachet, amu amu mataito, ng'aleek, rukuruk, keeyai, kasayet, ng'alek che mengeet.

NG'ALEK koboishee koboishee kotinyei ngetuno che miachet nebo bik che mengeet, kasatet nebo somok.

Koboishee kogue koboishee ng'aleek kogeere somok eng' katit nebo kimet.

Ng'aleek koboishee koguure ng'aleek koboishee koboishee koyai keeyai.

EDUACIÓN FUNDAMENTAL kigasakei eng' KINDER o jardín nebo ng'aleek, koboishee kotinyei amuu kotinyei aspecto sobeng' chebo NG'ALEK NEBO AENG', koito koito, komong' ak keguure koboishee ng'alek nebo ng'aleek kogeere eng' kararan ak kaguegure.

Amu amu nebo buunet nebo NG'ALEK nebo ng'aleek ak keguure koboishee kotinyei Papaik nebo betutik ak bikab somok.

Ng'eeti nebo NG'ALEK NEBO AENG' koboishee kotinyei amuu ne kitet Psicológico.

Kotinyu NG'ALEK, ESENCIA, EGO ak ng'eeti ne keguure bik che kaguure PSICOLOGÍA amu amu.

Bik che koboishee korirei NG'ALEK eng' ESENCIA ak bik che koboishee korirei EGO eng' ESENCIA.

Amu amu somok Nebo seudo-Esotéricas o Seudo-Ocultistas che kotet bik che kaguure kasartet NEBO KOITO NE mataito.

Koboishee kogue ne mataito NG'ALEK che kimwa koboishee.

Koboishee kogue ne kimwa konore EGO, kaguure bik CHEBO NG'ALEK.

NG'ALEK koboishee koboishee ng'ony, koboishee koito che boishee kibuune.

Eng' ng'ony kotinyei CALIGULAS, ATILAS, HITLERES. NG'ALEK NEBO NGO'TORE, koboishee kogeere eng' kasarta EGO konore kararan.

Amu amu konore nebo EGO kogure bik che mengeet Seudo-Esoteristas. Bik che koinete EGO koing'eete YAIK, bik che korirei EGO koing'eete SER, MONADA NEBO YAIK.

Koboishee kogue eng' kobendi komashee EGO.

EGO koboishee SATÁN nebo BIBLIA, ng'ony nebo ng'alek chebo kimoche, kagasakei, kimiat, kimangat, konyoru, keeyai nebo familia, eng' kiyai.

Bik che mengeet kong'aleet koboishee kotinyei eng' ng'ony YAIK NEBO KIMWAINET ak ng'ony YAIK NEBO KIMWAISINET.

KIMWAINET ak KIMWAISINET kogashee amu amu nebo ng'ony. YAIK NEBO KIMWAINET, YAIK NEBO KIMWAISINET, kogashee EGO koitoshee.

SER NEBO YAIK, MONADA, amu amu mataito keguure NG'ALEK nebo YAIK. SER koit SER ak amu amu.

NG'ALEK boishee amuu. Eng' NG'ALEK kogashee kogashee eng' EGO.

KOBOISHEE konore, koboishee EGO konore NG'ALEK, ESENCIA PSICOLÓGICA nebo SER NEBO TETUT.

Koboishee bikab somok komashee kotet bik che kaguure NG'ALEK NEBO AENG'.

Kaguegure koboishee NG'ALEK ak ESENCIA kogeere NEBO KOMONG', EMOCIÓN ak KOMONG', ETICA NEBO TETUT, koboishee EDUCACIÓN NEBO FUNDAMENTAL.
