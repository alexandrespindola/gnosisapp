Ng'aleu ak Pipa maibatoi. Kityo kilewenishei ko yaunee, ak kilemaunenishei ko ma yaunee. Ng'aleu ak Pipa ye ngan'at ne yaunee ak iteset chebo mutyo.

Chepkondeny ole ne tilil ng'aleu cheu cheu ng'aleu chebo Pipa ye Atlante ne boitit MAKARI KRONVERNKZYON ne murenik chebo kamet ne boitit AKALDAN, ne tinyei kotokchin Atlantida.

Ma koyaei kuguuguni olech kongoi tiliil oleng' ta ne yaunee kobun kowo iman, ak kobaatii ng'aleu cheu cheu.

Kogasei murenik chebo Atlante ko iman tiliil eng' ng'eet chebo EVOLUTIVAS, INVOLUTIVAS ak NEUTRAS chebo leel, ago kuguuguni olech koitit ng'aleu chebo iman ne koitit chebo iman che boitit BIEN ak MAL. Kobaatii ng'eet chebo EVOLUTIVO ko iman che yaunee ak ng'eet chebo INVOLUTTVO ko iman che Pipa. Ng'eet chebo neutras ma ko baatii ng'aleu.

Ng'eet cheu chito ko ng'etindoi eng' iman ak eng' leel ak ng'eet ne bo neutra ye kamet ne yaunee ko yauni ak kabaor.

Miondonyin che po iman ko tokchin ATLANTIDA ak POISEDONIS ne imanda, ne kowol Platón eng' Ripabliki, kigai eng' ng'eetyo ne boitit TIKLYAMISHAYANA, osotiyot ne kolewenit ne kiyautin bik che yaanee ko baatii ng'aleu chebo BIEN ak MAL ko yaanii kobate ng'alek cheu kobaanii ng'etindet. Chepkondeny ole ne yaanii ko ARMANATOORA.

Eng' ng'etindet chebo istoria eng' lakwet kot koma ng'alek cheu chito ak kobaatii kobaanii temel eng' ng'etindet chebo moral. Raanit ng'alek cheu ko ng'etindet.

Lekiniet eng'in REFORMADORES che kwendi korestoro moral ago ko yaunii kobate, kobeiii ng'aleu chebo BIEN ak MAL.

Moral tiliil ye kobate eng' ng'alek chebo BIEN ak MAL amun REFORMADOR MORAL tiliil ye REACCIONARIO.

Ng'aleu chebo BIEN ak MAL koboatee kotiirei ak koleel ng'etindet cheitoi.

Ole ne kotiirei ak koleel ma kongelel. Ko yaunee kokongel kong'etindet chebo ng'eet chebo EVOLUTIVAS ago ma ko yaunee kotiirei eng' ng'aleu che yaunee. Ko yaunee kokongel ng'eet chebo involutivas ago ko kokoleei kongol eng' ng'aleu chebo Pipa.

Ng'eet tiliil chebo centífuga ko yaunee kogee eng' ng'eet chebo centrípeta. Ng'eet tiliil chebo involutiva ko yaunee kolewen eng' EVOLUTIVA.

Eng' ng'etindet chebo ng'eet eng' EVOLUTIVO ng'etindet chebo ng'eet eng' INVOLUTIVO.

Eng' chepkondeny raanit tinyei ng'etindet chebo ng'eet che EVOLUCIONAN, INVOLUCIONAN ak kolewen.

Kotiirei ng'eet ak koleel ng'eet, ma kongel. Ne ng'aleu ye kokongel.

Kongel eng' NEOTO eng' iman ko yaunee, ko yaunee ng'aleu chebo BIEN ak MAL.

PSICOLOGÍA REVOLUCIONARIA chebo MOVIMIENTO GNÓSTICO ye kobate eng' gaa chebo ng'eet chebo iman eng' iman ak leel.

MOVIMIENTO GNÓSTICO tinyei ETICA REVOLUCIONARIA ne ma tinyei bo moral chebo reaccionarios ak ng'aleu chebo BIEN ak MAL.

Eng' laboratorio Psico-Fisiológico chebo iman tinyei ng'eet evolutivas, involutivas ak neutras che yaunee kogasei ak kokongel.

Ng'aleu chebo BIEN kokober kongel eng'eet chebo EVOLUTIVAS kabaatii kotir.

Ng'aleu chebo MAL kokober kongel ng'eet chebo INVOLUTIVAS kabaatii koleel.

Kotiirei ak koleel ma kongel. Ole ne kwendi kotoi ng'etindet ma yaunee kotiirei ak koleel. Ye ng'aleu ye kokongel ng'etindet cheitoi.

Kokongel ITENG' eng' ng'etindet chebo mutyo ye ng'aleu ko yaunee kobun kowo kora ak meei.

Kokongel KOROS eng' ng'etindet chebo mutyo ye ng'aleu ko yaunee kobun kowo kiruet ak aroriat.

Kokongel KILILIET eng' ng'etindet chebo mutyo ye ng'aleu ko yaunee kobun kowo kasarta.

Kokongel KUTIT eng' tereno chebo mutyo ko yaunee kobun kowo tirien ak maget eng' ng'etindet chebo iman.

Kokongel KUTIT eng' ng'etindet ak grada ko yaunee kobun kowo ng'aleu chebo tumbo.

Kokongel kilewenishei ne batii temet ko pereza, ma ko kimwaita kobatee eng' iman ak ko kimwaita kobatee eng' ng'eet chebo subyiles ye yaunee kobun kowo ng'aleu chebo wendi.

Kokongel kilewenishei chebo KIREET ak glotonería ko kitoe bieg chebo kamet ak bangi, kobaatii iman ak ng'eet chebo imanda.

Maestros chebo skul, koleji ak univesiti kogas bikabun ko karei ko kakareitu ak kobaatii virtud ko kikobatee ak virtud.

Ye ng'aleu kokongel ne ma kakarei, ne ma kasartaa ak ole ne kwendi virtud ko kokoroste YO.

KASARTA TILIIL kobun kowo ko kitoe YO. Virtud kobun kowo engelei ak kasartaa ak kokongel ng'etindet cheitoi eng' ng'aleu tiliil ak eng' ng'etindet chebo subconsciente.

Kwendi karei ko temet, kwendi kasarta ye kutit, kwendi virtud ko kokoroste YO eng' toek chebo kutit.

Ko yaunee imut YO ma ng'aleu tiliil tiliil eng' tereno chebo mutyo. Ko kititoe tiliil ko kitinyo ESO ne kasartaa. ESO ne kaitirr eng' virtud, ESO ne yaunee eng' SER INTIMO, ESO ne ma yaunee eng' iman.

Kokongel kokongel ng'etindet chebo ng'eet evolutivas che kore eng' iman. Kokongel kokongel ng'etindet chebo ng'eet INVOLUTIVAS che kore eng' iman, ko yamete kokitoe YO.

Ng'aleu chebo BIEN ak MAL koboatee kotir ak koleel ago ma kongel.

Ng'etindet tinyei kut, fondos ak profundidades. Kongel ng'etindet eng' ng'aleu tiliil ma kingel eng' ng'etindet chebo subconscientes.

Ng'etindet tiliil ko yaunee kitoe eng' ng'aleu tiliil ak kore eng' tereno chebo mutyo.

ITENG' kokoitee eng' toga chebo Juez. Koros bik kokoroste koros, tinyei bik che makoros koros, tinyei bik che makoros pesa ago kokoroste poderes Psíquicos, virtud, amores felicidad.

Bik koyaei kobaatii kobaatii kobaatii eng' ng'aleu kobatee chebo LUJURIA.

Bik kokutit eng' kasarta eng' SANTOS ak koite ng'etindet.

Bik kokutit bik chekitoe iman ak kobatee ko kabeshei eng' bik che kokutit.

Tinyei bik che kosirei eng' kandanda ak pesa ak tinyei bik che kosirei eng' kandi.

Tinyei Diógenes kogire eng' tonel ne korore ak kogai eng' sokrates koyache ng'aleu: "Koiirei siretat sokrates koiirei siretat". "Ee, Diógenes, eng' siretat kotiirei siretat". Ko chei.

Chepyoso koteng' kolewen ng'aleu, kolewen ng'aleu kobaatii kutit chebo bik eng' ng'aleu tiliil.

Ko kaat Arístipo. Kosireieng'.

Bik kosireieng eng' temet, kosae ak koititeng ko kakongel.

Tinyei bik chekitoe KIREET ak Gula ago ko kabaatii kore.

Ng'etindet tiliil ye kosae.

Eng' gaa chebo imanda koiitee, eng' ng'etindet.

Ng'aleu chebo BIEN ak MAL koiitee.
