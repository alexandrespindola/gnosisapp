Ng'omno ak piÄ© koboot ak ng'eet koek taam kookto ne boiboiyet.

Keiyee chameet ne tai ne boiboiyet, ko kiikame ng'omno, keiyee ng'eet.

Ng'omno ak ng'eet ko kiimeegei. Ng'omno ma tinyei ng'eet koek kokoruut. Ng'eet ma tinyei ng'omno ko kararan. "Ng'EET KO LAGOK, AGO NG'EET NE MI KOBOOTO".

Ko menyei kasomok kosom kora, ago ko menyei koleshe kiy koboote ne miu.

Kosom ma tinyei koboote ne miu ne kararan, ko kikoche iyotok bribonismo.

Koboote ne miu ne koleshei ago ma tinyei kosom, ko kikoche santo nebo kipsengwet.

Santo nebo kipsengwet ko tinyei koboote ne miu ne kararan, ago amu ma tinyei kosom, ma tinyei ne tinye.

Santo nebo kipsengwet ko tinyei boisio ago ma tinyei ne tinye amu ma sobei ne tinye.

Kosom ma tinyei koboote ne miu ne koleshei ko kikoche raran, amu, kipsengwet, ak kwondo.

Betut nebo aeng' nebo ng'eet kole, sayantista che mengeechen ma tinyei koboote, ko kimii kotinyegei kipsengwet, ko kigosulda ng'eet.

Ko menyei koleshe kosom ago ko kiimeegei ak koboote ne kararan.

Ko menyei etika nebo kokoruut ak saikolojia nebo kokoruut nyo, ko kiimei koruuti kotinyegei koboote ne kararan.

Ko kayai amu ma tinyei ng'eet, chebo chemiat kigochi kosom koruuto.

Chebo somok ak chebo somok kobendi kosom sayansi, historia, matematika, kobendi.

Ko menyei koleito kosom, koleito ne tinyei boisio.

Kosom ko menyei. Koleito kosom ne boiboiyet ko menyei, ago amu ma menyei kobun.

Chebo chemiat koleito kosom amu kobun; kobun betut nebo leel, kobun kiyo, kobun kwongo, kobun ng'alek che kang'et, ak kosom amu kobun.

Kobendi kosom amu ng'eet nebo chebonata ak boisio, ago ma kobendi kosom amu kobun.

Kong'alal kosom koleito kosom amu kobun ko kikoche bribon.

Ko menyei koamenyisiek kityo kochebosiek koame. Ko menyei ma kibendi koiroy kobun. Che soldier che mi kong'alal ko kiimeei ago ko kikisir amu kobun. Kobun kiyo.

Bribon che mi kong'alal ko kiimeei ago ko kikichei. Chebribon kigochi profeshion ak boisio koruuto amu kobun. Castro Rua; Cuba.

Ma kinyor kotinyegei kong'alal ak kosom, ago kinyor amu ma tinyei ng'eet.

Kosom ak kong'alal koleito koruuto amu ma tinyei ng'eet.

Ego ko keito kong'alal ak kosom amu ma tinyei ng'eet.

Ego kigochi kong'alal ak kosom ne koek.

Kotinygei ego, kokoruuto kong'alal ak kosom koek koboote nebo kipsengwet.

Chebo somok kobendi kokitame kosom ne mi boisio.

Kosom ma koruto ago ma kobendi kobuti kosom.

Ko menyei kosom ne ma kobuti menteet. Kobuti menteet ne tinyei kosom teorio nebo boisio, ne tinyei konyoru koruuto ak kosom.

Ko menyei kosom matter nebo profeshion ak matter nebo kipsengwet ne koek menteet ne kiimeegei.

Ko menyei koyai sintetisi nebo kosom ak sintetisi nebo kipsengwet nyo ko koek menteet ne kiimeegei.

Chebo maesto che mi skulul, koleji, yunibesiti, kobendi kosom saikolojia nebo kokoruut nyo, ko kiimeei komi chebo somok kong'alal nebo kokoruut.

Ko menyei chebo somok koleito koboote nebo kipsengwet, koleshe koboote ne kararan, ne koek chebo somok ne mi boisio.

Ma tinyei koboot ngo ma tinyei ng'eet. Kosom ngo ma tinyei ng'eet ko kikoche chebribon.

Koboot ko sustansia nebo atomik, ago kobendi koiroy chebo chemiat ne pleniei ng'eet ne kararan.
