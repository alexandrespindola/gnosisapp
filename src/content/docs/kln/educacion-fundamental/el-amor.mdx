Kutok kobaab koleegi, koito kaalenjini ak kaalenjinet eng' kompreni kesi kityo kwo kotinyei AMOR.

MIIYET ak MUTEI ketinyei konyol AMOR aeng' ago maoyai AMOR.

Kaalenjini ak kaalenjinet mumitei kobatenyi mamaak ak tisaak ago keisomin kenyishek ak keemyeishek alaktoi.

Chepkoswek ak chepkosich, tororok ak chetororyok mumitei kobatenyi mamaak kobatenyi ng'alek chebo ng'eet, komiet, kobatenyi mbesa, kobatenyi seureet, ak kakilenjin kenyishek kosich kaseur, koito kaalenjini koyai mumitei kobatenyi mamaak ak kakeisomin ak kamyeishek, ago maoyai AMOR.

Koboishe ng'alek chetiyei koito, kenyoru kenyishek ak kaalenjinet, tororok ak chetororyok kotinyei isirik chebo koleegi kwong'u kobatenyi mamaak.

Tisaik chepkoswek, chepkosich, tororok ak chetororyok kwong'u kobatenyi koleegisyek ng'alek chebo chito kole maarei kobatenyi mamaak.

Koito keguureti maitei isirik chebo batenyi ak mamaak, maitei AMOR chebo tout.

Koito urete kompreni koito kaitet ak ng'ale kenyori ak kakeisoman ak kamyeishek, kamutei ak kamiyet.

Koito urete kesom mamaak ak tisaak ago maitei konyol isirik ak AMOR.

ISIRIK ak AMOR ketinyei keguureti ago maitei konyol aeng'.

Mamaak kemyei kobatenyi ichami, kesubishek seurr kityo, boisio nelei, keguureti, kokaseur, ak ketinyei konyol kamiyet ak AMOR chebo tout.

Koito urete maitei AMOR chebo tout koito maitei mamaak ak tisaak keeguureti isich chebo kenyishek kora kainek kounyei kora koraitei subishek chemitei ine.

Aeng' chechang' koitebe abisimo kotun chebwa boisio nebo subishek.

Kenyori ng'ale chebo ng'wong'w nebo "MITEET YEISEI BOISIO". Koito kowokisiek nebo atebe eng' ng'wong'w kole. Motwet nebo "ICHAMI CHETIEYE," keguurti komam, keboich, kasir, kaalebo boisio, kekong'ut ak ketut chemiito, keguurti, keeng'et, koiyo eng' kibwong'u keguureti ng'wong'u, kekot mamaak ak tisaak, ak mamaak chebo boma, ak kora kora kora.

"MITEET YEISEI BOISIO" koyai kobaibai AMOR chebo tout.

Koteshei AMOR chebo tout maarei keguureti "MITEET YEISEI BOISIO".

Asi mamaak kemiteet ichami chebo tout ketinyei keeguureti kainek kotinyei mai koimaarei keguureti "MITEET YEISEI BOISIO".

Miteet yeisei boisio koyai kogureti kainek.

Mamaak mamiteet AMOR kityo kwo koboishe kobatenyi ichami kainek keguureti.

Mamaak chebo koigengei kwong'u kobatenyi mbesa ak kaat ichami mbesa chechang', ak ng'alechebo kainek nelel, ak ng'alek chebo ng'eet chechang', ago mamiteet chebo tout maarei ketinyei keguureti "miteet yeisei boisio".

Keitoisyet nebo koigengei kotun kobaibai AMOR chebo tout.

Boisioni nebo koigengei kotun kobilbil chebo koigong', mamitei ndaani.

Eng' bilbil nebo ndaani nebo boisioni, ketinyei boisioni chechang', ketinyei susich chechang', ago bilbil kwong'u eng' berur nebo ng'wong'w, koiotei eng' letiet nebo cheptilet ak kora king'eeti king'eeti kityo, kokas, koretet.

Maarei keguureti koretet nebo boisioni eng' ng'wong'u, asi mamitei ketiyei keisomo AMOR.

Ting'ik ketinyei konyol isirik ak kamiyet ak ng'aleny koyai keguureti AMOR.

Kesom kesirisyek ak kemyeishek ago keguureti komit.

Chepkoswek kemyei mamaak ak tisaak ak kakeisomin ak keguureti komit.

Kemyei chepkoswo chebwai, keferula, keboitio nebo kainek, kemwaani eng' boma ak koleegi, ak keguureti komit mamaak ak tisaak ago eng' tout kemyei kityo.

Mumitei kobatenyi boisio, kobatenyi patron, kemyei kobatenyi kora, keterei boisio ak keguureti komit patron ak kotun keret boisio nebo, kemet boisio nebo ng'alek chebo, ago maoyai AMOR, koito miyet.

Ting'ik chechang' ketinyei miyet kobatenyi atebe kuguureti eng' koigengei nebo boisioni ak komiet, miyet kora, keguureti, kuguureti, kompreni, kubatiso, ak kora keguureti, kwo ak keguureti AMIT KOSUBISHE, AK KITO KITYO!

Keguureti komit KOSUBISHE ago eng' tout mamitei komit, kemyei.

Eng' sabab chebo ng'eet, chepto ineti kotinyei kesom chebong'wun ago kemuch kole ago eng' tout mamitei komit, kemyei koterei kowendi boisioni, kokaseur, ak kora kora kora.

Kamutei nebo atebe eng' atebe nebo tisaik, kamitei kobatenyi chi, maoyai AMOR. Koyai kityo MIYET ak kito kityo.

Chepkoswo eng' batik kobatenyi TISA ama KOKULEET ak kenyishek kemyei ENG' NG'WENY, eng' boisio nebo kainek ak chechang' kenyishek keguureti komit ago chebo kwo eng' miyet.

Ata chepto inet eng' betut nebo komutun ama eng' tat nebo komiet eng' tai, chepong'un keguureti komitet chechang' kobooti ako chebo kwo eng' kemyei, eng' boisioni chechang', kotun komiet, konet ng'eet, konet kotong', kasekseet, ak kemyei koteret. Koito maoyai AMOR.

Ting'ik chebo ng'wong'w keguureti komit ting'ik chebo ng'wong'w ago maarei eng' boisioni nelel kobai kenyishek ketiyei keisomo AMOR chebo tout.

Asi mamaak kemiteet ichami chebo tout kobatenyi ichami, asi ichami kemiteet chebo tout kobatenyi mamaak, asi tisaak kemiteet chebo tout kobatenyi kaalenjini ak kaalenjinet koimaarei keguureti ng'eet. Ngetet ketinyei boisio eng' ciento por ciento.

Chebo kwo eng' ting'ik mamiteet kompreni kwo koyai komiyet, ak kamiyet ak kamutei nebo atebe eng' atebe nebo tisaik, ak kamaiywet, ak kora ketinyei konyol kamiyet.

Ting'ik maarei keisomo AMOR, asi ting'ik keisomo komiyet boisioni ketinyei kowendi paradiso.

ENG'AMORINTI keguureti keamiteet ak chechang' kotoret keguureti kwong'u kamiteet. Ago KWONG'U KWAIYWET. Kamusati KWAIYWET, kiburech nebo maiywet kowendi eng' ng'wong'u.

KWAIYWET ketinyei kong'et ATEBE ak NET. Ting'ik chebo ng'wong'w keguureti KEENG'AMORINTI.

Maarei eng' boisioni kora keguureti kobotenyi ENG'AMORINTI. Keguureti kopotenyi KWAIYWET ago kotiyei kora keguureti kobotenyi ENG'AMORINTI.

Ting'ik chebo ng'wong'w chemiit eng' kamiyet ago maarei kong'et kwo koyai komiyet ak keguureti konyol KWAIYWET ak kamiyet.

Asi kora ndaani eng' boisioni koito maitei konyol KWAIYWET ak KAMİYET.

KWAIYWET koyai asista nelei ak kosir kora katun, katun koi eng' kainek nebo ya.

KWAIYWET koyai ng'alek chebo LALANEET ciento por ciento, KWAIYWET koyai katun ago chechang' kekaiywe ak kosir. Ketinyei konyol kamiyet.

Tisaak ak kokuleet ketinyei ketub chebosiek, Tororok ak chetororyok, kobotenyi eng' kaitet nebo kamiyet ak KWAIYWET. Koyai kityo keeguureti tat chechang' eng' boisioni.

Tisaak ak kokuleet koyai koboishe kainek chebo kaalenjini ak kaalenjinet ak kotun keeguureti kobai kobwa tai chetrach eng' boisioni.

Koito urete kwo koyai KAMİYET, kwo maarei keguureti ak kanang'iet, kamaiywet, kamitet, kamiyet, kamuteet nebo atebe, kora kora kora.

KAMİYET koguurti kuguureti eng' ting'ik, ago maoyai nelel maarei kokoboot koteshe, kowendi chepto chebo cheret, kora kora kora.

KAMİYET ketinyei KOBOT eng' kityo ak kotinyei KOBOT asi maitei kompreni ndaani kwo koyai OSODIT nebo ketiyei ndaani, kwo koyai KAMİYET NEBO NG'ALEK CHEBO RARA, kamiyet, kobotei atebe nebo atebe, ak kamutei nebo atebe, kora kora kora.

Ketinyei kompreni kwo koyai chebo atebe nebo atebe, ketinyei kompreni kainek kesir eng' kityo eng' kobetenyi atebe, ago ak kesir eng' kaitet cheleel ak maarei kong'et nebo KWAIYWET.

Koito urete kaeng'et eng' kibwatet nebo atebe atebe. Koi kityo kobot eng' kityo eng' koraa ak neel kwo koyai kamiyet.

Maarei keguureti kutindoi ng'wong'w asi maarei kobaa ng'aal nebo KAMİYET. Kong'u KAMİYET kokutindoite ng'wong'w.
