Koyo ole mosubek che pochin eng' ng'wony biik aeng' ne bo sabeiisiek che yaun agoi sabeiisiek. Mosubek nebo kainet kora kobotok kochin kelyek che bo kimnai agoi kelyek che bo abeiisiek. Mosubek nebo aeng' kora kobotok kochin kelyek che bo abeiisiek agoi kelyek che bo somok.

Sokstek che pochin eng' ng'wony biik kobotok kobaate kwenut, sukul ak barabara. Ng'wony biik che mi kelyek che kiitin eng' temel nebo elimu, koyai kayanen ak keyai keyautiet.

Ng'wony biik che kiitin eng' sokstek che ma kotugul koyai eng' barak che ma tugul.

Toror biik che kisub eng' kainet nebo koyai koboisio kogeer kerok age tugul nebo ng'alek che mengeechen.

Ng'wony biik, amu leel nebo kiyokchin, koboisio kaat kobendi bikab kanyoruot.

Kimochek che ya eng' ng'wony biik ko leta che iyot eng' tuguuk che bo koitalel che kobendi kobili kobilisiek che terter eng' yaun somok.

Kong'a mi temel nebo elimu, ng'wony biik koyai kerit nebo metei: ko kowoiyot nebo kosich, bik ak kondo nebo ng'etundo.

Kikiyokchin eng' bara ne boi eng' ng'wony, koyai eng' boryet nebo ng'wony biik che kiitin eng' yaun somok.

Kikiyokchin eng' kiyai che magat eng' boryet nebo ng'wony, eng' let ne boi eng' kemoi, kogeer eng' yaun somok.

Ngeende ne ya eng' kelyek che bo somok, koyai kibendi eng' kerok ne telel ne kiit kogase bikab kuruguk che kigochin eng' kityo.

Eng' kelyek che bo somok, bik ak chebii kosirtoi kamet kuguure ak kobaate kwenut, ago kechamei eng' kiyeyet che toret ne kosub ko kobendi kotinygei.

Kileeleek che bo koger, kochin kileeleek che pochin eng' ng'wony biik. Kong'a mi temel nebo elimu, kerok nebo kimong'u ko kitoret.

Ng'wony biik ko kimaket. Remet ko rani nebo ng'wony biik, ago ko kileeliet, ma terech.

Ng'wony biik ko mi genio ne terem ak juicio ne metei. Kaas kobendi ko ng'wony biik che mi juicio ne telel ak genio ne terem.

Kong'a mi temel nebo elimu, ng'wony biik koyai bikab pasion, che meetei, che bo kosir, che mi magat, che kokong'u, che mi kirwok, che mi tingu, che mi ng'omna, che mi itet, che mi iywot, che mi bikab kamukta, che mi bikab kweeta, che mi bikab metet, che mi bikab teta, koek ak kora.

Mocedad ko yaat nebo laloto ne kimaket ne koyai kotinygei. Koyokchin ko ng'wony biik kobukto tuguuk che boi eng' mocedad.

Koitalel koboisio koyai ng'wony biik kobendi eng' ng'etundo.

Ngeende ne ya eng' ng'wony biik koyai koyokchin kogeer eng' barak nebo temel nebo elimu.

Eng' ng'wony biik ko mi kileeleek che kobendi ko kaat.

YO koboisio yoot nebo ng'wony biik koyai kogeere ak kobendi.

YO koyokchin kobili siat, pasionales koro kobeer korok kobeer koger koyai kerok.

Koyokchin ng'wony biik kobendi kaat kobendi kobendi eng' kiyokchin, bik ak kobili bikab ng'alek age tugul.

Ma koyokchin ng'wony biik kobendi chemii nebo kobili ko bikab kanyoruot ago ma ko bikab tugul.

Ma terech kobili akenge tugul. Kikiyokchin kobili ko kitoret ne koyai bikab intelektwal. Jorge Manrique, kiit keeti:

"Kikiyo eng' koyai tugul,
komie kochamda,
kong'etundo,
komie kogeere
eng' alaksi age tugul."

Aristoteles kakileeng' eng' kobili ko kateei: "kong'etundo kobendi kobendi kobili, koma kime bikab tugul".

Bikab INTELECTUAL koochamei kobili. Federico kakileeng': "KOBILI KO KAAI NE TEREM ENGEI".

Kong'etundo ne kobendi ko kogeere ko kobili kobendi ko kokong'u.

Ng'wony biik che bo kalibera kotoror. YO nebo kalibera kobendi eng' kobili.

Kalibera nebo CRONICO koworei Matrimonio ak ko katobochi. Kichamda katobochi Matrimonio kowendi kobendi kobendi kobili tuguuk chemii eng' ng'wony.

Absurdo ko kobukto tuguuk che pochin eng' ng'wony biik ago komei kosich, kosich bik.

Toror bik koteei kosich amu kochamda, toror chebii koteei amu korikto ak koyai kaat.

Bik age tugul kochamei chito ne kiyat ak bikage tugul.

Kikiyokchin kimie kosich eng' ng'wony biik kora koma kimie koyai kerok.

Eng' ng'alek age tugul ko mi kerok. Ko kilyek eng' ng'wony biik koteei kosich, ago ko kibut eng' kosich ko kitoret.

Kikiyokchin ng'wony biik koteei kosich ak kobendi kobendi kwenut. Ma komie ko kayai bikab itet.

Salomon kakileeng': "Kiywot ko kayai kerok; ko yaat".

Bikab INTELECTUAL ko kiywot. Kiywot ko bikab.

Bikab kiywot ma koyokchin kogen. Komie kong'ot kora keyokchin eng' kaat.

Mimi ne koyai kobendi kiywot ko koyai ko kobilisiek che ya.

Kong'a mi kiywot ko mi kobilisiek. Kiywot ma kobotok eng' chamet, chamet ak kiywot ma kobilisiek. Mosobek nebo kiywot kobotok eng' kayokchin.

YO kobendi kiywot eng' barak age tugul. YO koyokchin kogeer chito ne kiyat.

Kiyokchin kobendi YO kokendi koyai kobendi.

Eng' kiyeyet kora kakileel kochin koyeyet biik che korikto. Kalibera kosich kobendi.

Bik age tugul kiayai kiyokchin.

Bik ak chebii kimie koyai eng' chamet.

Bik kiit kobendi kwenut ne keyai chito age tugul. Ma koyai bik age tugul kobendi kwenut ne keyai chito age tugul. Kobendi bikage tugul kobendi siat.

Kiyeyet nebo ng'wony biik ko Matrimonio.

Chebii che mi kosir che toror kobendi kosichek amu kiyokchin kobendi chebo korikto.

Kikiyokchin chebii koreme ko kiyat.

Kikiyokchin ma kober chamet ak Pasion. Bik ak chebii kochamet ma koyokchin chamet ak Pasion.

Kikiyokchin PASION ko kikwa ne kobendi eng' ng'wony.

Bik ak chebii che mi pasion eng' korok kobendi pasion che korir.

Kogase kobendi pasion, kamaget kobendi eng' ng'wony.

Kerok nebo toror che kosich kobotok eng' kobendi pasion.

Matrimonio ko kiyeyet ne kogen eng' ng'wony biik ak sukul kwenut ak yunibasiti kobendi kobendi kayai ne kogen.

Kikeru kobendi bik ak chebii eng' tuguuk che pochin.

Kong'a mi pasion ak kobili che pochin, kobendi eng' ng'wony.

Koror che kiitin eng' kobendi pasion eng' matrimonio amu kiyokchin eng' kibendi.

Chebii ne kosich che mi kiywot eng' korok, kobendi kiywot che ya.

Bik ne kosich che mi kiywot, eng' korok, ko koyai eng' kayai kerok.

Kong'a mi chamet ne tugul eng' bik aeng', kikiyokchin ma mi pasion, kikiyokchin kobendi YO nebo kiywot, kikiyokchin kochamei.

YO koyai kwenut bik age tugul che mi Chamet ne tugul koyai kibendi YO.

Chemii bik kobendi YO kobendi kwenut. Chemii kobendi YO kobendi temel nebo elimu.

Bikab kwenut che mi kiywot kobendi bikab.

Kiyokchin ko bik che ma koyokchin kobendi.

Kobendi ng'wony kobendi kaat ak kobili kobendi kaat kochek.

Ma koyai kobendi chemi kochek.

Mimi kobendi Matrimonio.

Temet kobendi chamet. Yoot nebo ng'wony ko chamet.
