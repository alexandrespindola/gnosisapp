Chi kelendet ne bo Delfus koegitu, ne kilektoi boisieny che kibendi ne kigoochi eng' leek che ng'etunde: "AMA MAGEI AK OBOISIO KWOONDA". Obosi kwoonda ak oboisieny ng'wony ak Ng'oo.

Sayansi ne bo Muguut eng' Meditasyon eng' ng'eet ne tetei lema ne iyatiet ne bo Hierofantes che bo Greece.

Amun ko uu koleen kotookto koyai teteiywet ne komie meditasyon, inan kotookto koboisieny kwoondenik chebo kaywanisiek chebo mendeet.

Koito koyai teteiywet ne komie meditasyon ko agenge koleen kotokto, kobendi eng' biik, koeges, koekoktinye, koemugul, kochesoto eng' maat chebo nyinyiit, koesichekto koboot kiy, ak eng'aleen.

Koleen ko uu kobo kiiganweek ak kobendi eng' ng'aleen, amun koboot kayai KEEGIT NE TETEINET eng' meditasyon, mendeet kikoto ak eng' kogenyit ne ng'etunde.

Kotyeni eng' ng'aleek chebo sayansi, ko ng'eet koyai koboot NE MIEI kobendi koboisio kwoonda.

Koitinye koboisieny eng' koleen, ak eng' kaywanisiek chebo mendeet, kobei boiseny ko uu koleen kikwo eng' mendeet, kobei kus, kobei kwet, kobei ng'eet che kikoboot, ak eng'aleen.

Koleen ko uu kobo kiiganweek koleen kikwo eng' meditasyon, kaat kebeechen mendeet eng' boiseny che kikoboot, kobei ng'aleek che kikoboot, kobei biik che kigochini ak ng'aleek che kigotinyini, kobei kus chebo kaywanisiek, kobei kochek che kikoboot, kobei miising', koemugul, ak eng'aleen.

Keen koleen kotokto koyai eng' mendeet teteiywet ne tetei eng' meditasyon, inan ko kiit biik chebo koleen ak chebo ng'eet chebo koyinet ak koboisieny eng' koleen, kobendi eng' koyinet, eng' kaywanisiek chebo mendeet, eng' kaywanisiek che iten ak eng' kaywanisiek che nda bo mendeet. Ama keech kotookto koboisieny koleen ng'wony.

Kochiit eng' koboisieny biik chebo koleen ne, inan koboisio kwoonda.

Kaat kebeechen eng' mendeet eng' teebut ak tebiit. Ko amun kebeechen eng' leek, kus, kochek, biik, ng'eet che kigochini, ak eng'aleen, mendeet kikoto ak eng' kogenyit ne ng'etunde AKUK eng' boiseny che kikoboot.

Chebo sayansi che nda kootookto koboisieny AKUK NE TILELDEI. Koamun AKUK kikwo eng' mendeet, imuchi koboisio, koek, kotookto koboot boiseny ne koleeldei. BOISENY NEI ko NE MIEI.

Koisieny mendeet ne kikoto ak mendeet ne kitokto eng' ng'eet.

Koisieny mendeet ne eng' kogenyit ak mendeet ne kiinyiit eng' ng'eet.

Eng' teebut eng' ng'aleek, kotookto koboisieny mendeet ne kitokto eng' ng'eet, eng' kaywanisiek eng' leek ak nda kikoto ak koleen kobendi kopa.

Koteny eng' koboisieny, kotookto koboisieny mendeet ne kiinyiit eng' ng'eet, eng' kaywanisiek nda eng' kogenyit ak koleen koleelei ak kokoleelei koleen.

Kogenyit ne koleen ak mendeet kikoto, koekto kotokto ko uu koyai koyook, ko uu kenyit, ko amun kaat kebeechen mendeet eng' ng'aleek eng' boiseny chebo koleen.

Kotookto amun mendeet koleen kikoto, kotookto amun mendeet eng' kogenyit ne miit, amun AKUK NE TILELDEI kikwo.

AKUK mateen koleel. Mateen koitieny eng' ng'aleek, boiseny che kikoboot, koboot ng'aleek chebo koleen koyai eng' ng'eet.

Mateen koleel AKUK eng' ng'aleek. Ng'aleek chebo kibendi kigeere koot koboisio boiseny, kus ak kochek che kigochini; matinyie koleel koboot koleen ak koleel boiseny, ng'aleek ak kochek che nda kigochini.

Koleel koboot AKUK eng' ng'aleek chebo leek che kiten koboot boiseny che kigochini, koleen ko ng'eet ak koleen koboot.

"AKUK KO KOECH, AK KIGOOCINI MA KO AKUK".

"BOISENY MA IKOISIO AKUK, AK AKUK MA IKOISIO BOISENY".

"BOISENY KO AKUK AK AKUK KO BOISENY, KO UEI AKUK AMEET KIGOOCINI".

"AKUK AK KIGOOCINI KO KOYAI AGOT, MA IKOISIO". AKUK AK KIGOOCINI KO IKWO AK KOYAI AGOT.

"KOAMUN SERIK AAREK CHEBO KOYINET KAITEI KIIT, KAITEI BIISYENY NE KIGOOCINI, MA KAITEI BIISYENY NE AKUK".

"Serik ne kileeldei imuchi kaitee biisyeny ne kigoocini ak AKUK eng' boiseny.

"AKUK ko agenge eng' ng'aleek ne koleeldei koleen mateen sustansiol ak koleen mateen biik eng' serik, ak kochiit eng' eng'aleek eng' koleen kileeldei ak koleen ko magat".

Maastarik ak Maastrik chebo Sukulisiek, Kolejiet ak Yunibersitiet, inan kochiit eng' Saykoloji ne koleeldei ak koleen ko kaityi sayansiek eng' ng'ooni che koleel koboot koboisio NE MIEI.

Kotokto kotookto koboot KOBOISIO NE MIEI ko amun ng'aleek tebiit.

Koamun AKUK kikwo koimuchi koboisio TILELDEET NE KOLEEN NE MIEI.

KOBOISIO NEI ENG' NE AKUK, mateen leek ak mateen koleen, AKUK ENG' BOISENY, ko KILEEL NE MIEI, BONDET NE MIEI NG'OONDE.

Koyinet ne koleel koleen ko AKUK, inan koleel koleen ko AKUK ENG' NGETUNDE, ko uu KOYINET AKOT, KO YAI NG'OO, KILEEL ak KONYEET, ko KOYINET, BUDDHA Ne Koboesieny Eng' Ng'oony.

KOYINET NE KOLEEL AK AKUK, KILEEL ak KONYEET mait koisiet. Koyookto ko DAHARMA-KAYA; koyook ne koyai eng' LEEL.

KOYINET NE KILEEL, NE AKUK ak ne mait koisiet eng' teguny ne bo Koleeldeet ne ng'etunde, mateen koibun ak mateen kobiit ak ko uu koleel amitara BUDDHA ne magat.

Koboesieny ne koleen. Koamun kochiit eng' AKUK eng' KOYINET eng' KOYINET ak ko LEEL chebo BUDDHA ak Koboesieny eng' KOYINET, ko kolek eng' MAGET CHEBO BUDDHA NE NG'OONY.

Kiit koyinet koboot ak koito koyai eng' MEDITASION, kokot moche koot eng' Meditasyon, ama ng'aleek koleen koot eng' Meditasyon, ko amun kang'aleek koleen koot eng' Meditasyon, ng'aleek ne koleen koyai eng' ng'eet eng' Meditasyon. MENDET INAN KAUK ково KOBOISIO NE MIEI.
