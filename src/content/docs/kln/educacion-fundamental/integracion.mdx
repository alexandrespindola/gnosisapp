Akot kole kakisto chebo saykolojia ne boibo kora kayai TILEET NENDAET.

Ka YEKO ne boibo INDIBIDUAI, tasionik chebo TILEET chebo SAYKOLOJIA ko kikichor komong'u. Ago ne boibo kotokchini ng'alek, YEKO koyai kobun INDIBIDUAI, YEKO koyai koba chokindet kole PLURAI.

YEKO NE BUBEN PLURAI koyai ng'alek chebo boiyot ne boibo ng'eet ne kamwaat.

Ka kogoitkei koiik yoot komiegei kotinygei ko koba koiik SAYKOLOJIA kobun ng'eet ne kamwaat, kogas koondeleldet ne boibo metei kayai INDIBIDUALITET.

Organisimut ne boibo binadamu koyai mashini ne boibo ng'atut ne kaguunindet koba YEKO NE BUBEN PLURAI ne kigusugu Saykolojia Revolutionariet.

Amiib chekito gazeti, aginda YEKO INTELEKTUAI; Ameib chekito kotinygei baati, aginda YEKO EMOSHONAI; Komeerei koba baati, ng'et YEKO CHEBO MOOBEMENTI, ameib chekito kotinygei komi yoot, AMI agiit chekito komi ng'alek ang'et YEKO CHEBO INSTINKT CHEBO KONSERBESHENI, agitu, AMI amiib chekito konyet, a.wm.

Akot konyoun YEKOISIK che kikaguun EGO, akamwa kaguun, koito amu.

Koba leet ne boibo saykolojia revolutionariet kogas kong'eten YEKO koyai legion ako kotugul organism koyai mashini.

YEKOISIK kokiriit, kokoiit koba boibo amu, kong'eten kaguun, kong'eten koito amu.

Agas kotugul biik chebo dezintegrashioni chebo saykolojia ago chong'et ne boibo eniimali intelekchuai ne keguunindet kolenji BIINDET.

Kogasi kobendi ak kotugul ng'alek DEZINTEGRESHENI koba SAYKOLOJIA. Dezintegrishei koyai koib, koelus, kososyi, kong'atu a.wm.

Ng'alek chebo dezintegrashioni chebo saykolojia koyai elda ne kagusugu ne kaguunin komong'u keguunindet. Elda koyai polifasetiki ago koyai lakwet chebo kokaguunindet. Elda koyai resooti sekreetit ne boibo makinaari sokiai. Koik Imbesisik eng'eet kosaguunin elda.

Biik chemiach eng'eet komiach, kong'eten kotum akot komiach. Fakiri eng'eet komiach, kong'eten koito komiach. Nebendi kasit kaguunindet ak kasit, kong'eten kabendi kasit komong'u. Nebendi eksperiensieng'et ak nebendi eksperiensi komong'u, kong'eten kobendi komong'u akot kikaguun.

Biik mache kebirta, kibaget ak ruga. Resooti sekreetit chebo elda chebo motoka ne boibo koiyonet, chebo barto ne boibo koiyonet, chebo ng'ato ne boibo koiyonet, chebo mende chebo kwonyet ne boibo sokok eng'eet kobeeti kwen, komong'u, ng'alek chebo koito, ng'alek, bertus, amaache komong'u a.wm.

Ng'alek ne boibo tetut chebet koyai proesesut ne boibo kobun eksperiensi, bertus, ng'alek, mende, a.wm. kombeetin YEKO PLURAI komong'u kotokchin koba boiyot ne boibo ng'eet che kamwaat, chebo sosyot ne boibo kebeet chebo korok a.wm.

Kotugul agas koyai dolor. Metei kotugul agas kogoitke kontenit chebo korok ne boibo kowo. Kotugul agas kobun koyai koito kobeet koba sikisaiet, koket kotugul ago koito chebet.

YEKO PLURAI kong'eten kogun justificationi kotinygei koba boiyot chebet ago proesesut ne boibo kang'et, kogabun, kotum, kogasei akot kayai koit koyoot ne boibo koiyonet kolenji evolushin, progres, kobo.

Biik kobun kosobos ak mache komong'u kobendi eng'eet, kobendi korok, kobendi elda, kobendi chelos ako kogoiten kabendi a.wm. komong'u kogotkei kosaguunindet kobendei, kogotkei kororut, kogaisei, komache kong'eten.

Elda koyai koit kotugul kobendi kobun kong'eten ago biik chebo kobendi eng'et. Kotugul strukturet chebo mendet kotugul koitkobendi kobun kogasei.

Elda kobun kobendi bankisik chebo sukul. Kabendi itunishonut ne boibo ng'atut chebo kondisipulut, ng'atut ne boibo kalifikeshonut, ng'atut chebo bartus, ng'atut chebo ng'atut, ng'atut chebo sokisik, bisiklit ne boibo ng'atu, patinisik ne boibo mende, borot ne boibo ng'atu a.wm.

Maastaisek ak maastaisetisik ne kaguunindet koformu personalitet ne boibo alimneisik, kogasi kobendi ak kotugul proesesut ne boibo elda ako kasinein koba saykis ne boibo estudiantesik chebo siminti agati koba kong'eten.

Mendet kobendi koba yai, kogas kotugul kobendi funkshoni chebo MAS. “Kogasi kaguunindet kachek komong'u, kabendi ng'alek komong'u, kobendi itunishonut komong'u, kabendi bertus komong'u, kobendi sang'et, kobendi perfektionut, kobendi evolushon komong'u a.wm.”

Kotugul funkcshonalismut ne boibo mendet koba MAS. EL MAS koyai intimi resooti sekreetit chebo elda.

EL MAS koyai proesesut ne boibo comparitibet ne boibo mendet. Kotugul proesesut ne boibo comparitibet koyai ABOMINABU. Eksampul: Ami itunishonut komong'u akai. Fulano koito bertusi komong'u akai. Fulana koito kamong'u akai, kobendi saaibiet, kobendi bondadosa, kobendi ng'atu a.wm.

EL MAS kobeeti taimu. EL YO PLURAI moche taimu koito kamong'u ak nebendi koiyonet, komong'u kobendi famili kotugul koyai jeniu ak kagas, komong'u koba biik chebo biik eng'ato, komong'u kombei eng'eet, kogotkei koba komong'u itunishonut, kobendi nguunet, kobendi ng'uunet a.wm.

Kagas kaba koba proesesut ne boibo comparitibet koba biik chebo elda ago kobun agas kolenji descontento, desasosiego, amargura.

Desgrasiadamenti biik koito ago koit kebeet, ago koit kebeet, mache kong'eten komi korok. Luchiak kobendi descontento, elda, codicia, celos ago luchar ak descontento moche kobun korok ne boibo korok.

Koiten kong'eten korok ne boibo korok chebo tetut, mache komong'u kolakw ak kobendi konam ako kobun komong'u kotinygei kaguunendet ak causesut ne boibo descontento; celos, elda, codicia a.wm.

Kogoten kong'eten kogasei mende, magunifiki posishon sokiai, bertus, satisfakshonisik chebo kotugul, a.wm. ak prposishoni chebo kogasei kontenit ne boibo tetut koito kotugul ago kotugul agas koba elda ago ng'alek chebo elda moche kakoi ak kotugul kaguunet chebo korok ne boibo tetut ago kontenit.

Mendet kobotelyei koba YO PLURAI kaguunin elda bertut ako kobendi lujo koimine ng'alek chebo ng'atu. Progres, evolushin ispiritua, anyelo chebo kobo, luchar koba dignifikashioni a.wm.

Kotugul agas kobun dezintegrashioni, ng'alek chebo kobo, luchasik sekreetisik, tasionik chebo koit kotugul ago kobendi a.wm.

Koit kogasei koba biik chebo biindet biik chebo kotugul koyai Integro kaguunindet.

Kobendi katugul kogasei TILEET NENDAET kotinygei koba ng'alek chebo kobo akot kayai YEKO PLURAI.

Koiten kong'eten biik chebo kotugul kayai faktorisek isei, Primero: Personalitet. Segundo: YO PLURAI. Tercero: Biik chebo saykis, kobendi: ESENSIET CHEBO BIK CHEBO NDE.

YO PLURAI kobos kobendi biik chebo saykolojia koba explosionesik atomikisik chebo elda, celos, codicia a.wm. Koiten kasosyi YO PLURAI ako kochebutin kaba, biik chebo saykis ak komoibei koba tetut, komoibei senterit permamenti chebo konshenshia.

Kokai kikochebutin senterit permamenti chebo konshenshia, moche kakoi kosineito.

Senterit permamenti chebo konshenshia koimine biindet ne boibo kotinygei.

Senterit permamenti chebo konshenshia koimine kosineito.
