Tënë kɔm ciɛŋ miɛt, kɔm rɔmɛn kuɔny ciɛŋ kuɛŋ ë luɔk ë wëëth, kɔŋɔŋ lëu bɛŋ kuɔny ɛɛn yic ë kɔc juëc.

Krïst ë Raan ciɛŋ kuɔny ɛɛn gɔɔt jɔɔk kuɔny këyaŋ cɔl kɔc cie kɔɔr yic.

Kɔc juëc kaam kɔc ciɛŋ kuɔny ɛɛn rɔmɛn kuɔny diäk bɛŋ piny piny kuɔny Dëŋ.

Nëyic thukul raan luɔk kɔc thɔɔrë kaam roŋ.

Judas, raan rëër ë miɛt; Pilatos raan rëër ë tuɔŋ; Caifás, raan rëër ë wëëth cieŋ.

Kɔc thɔɔrë de kuɔny gɔɔt Dëŋ ë rɔmɛn kuɔny yic.

Eek këyaŋ thɔɔrë ë kɔc cie rɔm kuɛŋ ë kɔc rɔmɛn gɔɔt kuɔny.

Cakɛɛn, rɔm kuɛŋ de cɔl ciɛŋ kɔɔr kuɔny tɔŋ ë miɛt yic.

Cie kuɔny, rɔm kuɛŋ cieŋ kuɔny ë Kabir Jesús kɔc cɔl thɛɛk.

Kɔc gɔɔt kaam de, Dhiëëŋ juëc juëc kuɔny, ë dhiëëŋ kɔc kuɔny miɛt yic.

Empero, Jesús ë Kabir gɔɔt tuɛŋ kuɔny ɛɛn rɔm kuɛŋ ciɛŋ piny yic, tëŋ wëënh kuɔny bɛŋ cɔl këyaŋ cɔk kɔc rɔm thukul, yɔɔm, dhil, kɔc cɔɔl, cɔɔl.

Eek yɔm cɔk raan ɛɛn piny yic tuŋ kuɔny rɔm ciɛŋ kɔc.

Krïst ë Raan ciɛŋ cie miɛt cieŋ kuɔny ɛɛn gɔɔt këyaŋ miɛt cieŋ kuɔny miɛt.

Krïst ë Raan ciɛŋ cieŋ tɔŋ yic kuɔny ɛɛn gɔɔt këyaŋ ciɛŋ kuɔny wëëth.

Krïst ë Raan ciɛŋ cie mɛ̈ëth kuɔny ɛɛn gɔɔt këyaŋ ciɛŋ kuɔny mɛ̈ëth.

Krïst ë Raan ciɛŋ cie kuëth kuɔny ɛɛn gɔɔt këyaŋ ciɛŋ kuɔny kuëth.

Krïst ë Raan ciɛŋ cie tuɛŋ, luɔk miɛt, thukul kuɔny ɛɛn gɔɔt këyaŋ ciɛŋ kuɔny, dhil, riɛɛn.

Krïst ë Raan ciɛŋ, tuɔŋ, Dhil ë cɔk rɔmɛn kuɔny ɛɛn gɔɔt këyaŋ ciɛŋ kuɔny, kuɔny miɛt, gël, cɔɔm.

Dëŋ ë dhil kuɔny tëŋ cɔɔŋ yic, cie miɛt kuɔny, ɛɛn gɔɔt këyaŋ ciɛŋ kuɔny mɛ̈ël.

Raŋ simbiosis kuɔny Krïst-Jesús; Krïst-Raan; rɔm cieŋ dhil yic kuɔny raan kuɔny dhil kuɔny, cak kuɔny cɔk kuɔny tuɔŋ.

Këyaŋ kuɔny thukul eek Krïst ciɛŋ eek ŋoot; raan kuɔny yic gɔɔt cieŋ; raan kuɔny yic ɛɛn yic, tëŋ.

Krïst ɛɛn eek kuɔny Dëŋ yic, ŋoot kuɔny kɔc, kɔc cuɛŋ, kuɔny kɔc cɔɔl.

Kɔc kuɔny de ŋoot cieŋ; eek kuɔny, cie ŋoot, kɔɔr Dhil kuɔny gɔɔt tëŋ, ŋoot kuɔny tuɔŋ.

Kɔc cuɛŋ, eek kuɔny, kɔc rɔm yic, kɔc miɛt yic ŋoot Dhil, Krïst Dëŋ, Krïst ë gɔɔt kuɔny Dëŋ, kɔɔt ciɛŋ cuɛŋ roŋ kuɔny kɔɔr.

Kɔc cɔɔl, kɔc yuɔŋ kuɔny, ŋoot kuɔny Krïst ciɛŋ, kɔɔt raan kuɔny Kɔc ciɛŋ dhil kuɔny, kɔc miɛt kuɔny ë yɛɛn cɔk gël.

Kɔc thɔɔrë ŋoot kuɔny Krïst ciɛŋ, kɔɔt yɛɛn kuɔny miɛt kuɔny roŋ kuɔny rɔmɛn kuɔny.

Judas raan rëër ë miɛt kuɔny dhil gɔɔt tëŋ miɛt, eek kuɔny, miɛt, yic, rɔm.

Pilatos raan rëër ë tuɔŋ, gɔɔt yic kuɔny ŋoot kuɔny, cie rëër, gɔɔt miɛt roŋ yic, tëŋ miɛt kuɔny dhuuk yic, cɔɔm.

Caifás raan rëër ë wëëth cieŋ tuɔŋ yɛɛn kuɔny dhiëëŋ dhuuk; Dëŋ ɛɛn yic dhil ë raan, empero, raan traidor cɔl tëŋ rɔm yic cɔɔŋ, rɔm, cɔɔm, cɔɔl.

Kɔc thɔɔrë de tuɔŋ Dhil ɛɛn Dhil cieŋ kuɔny, gël cɔɔm kuɔny.

Pilatos kuɔny bɛŋ dhil ë roŋ, malvados yɛɛn tuɔŋ, kuɔny, ɛɛn roŋ gɔɔt ŋoot.
