Ŋuoth ciɛŋ kuat kɔc Aprika, Asia ke Amerika Latinit, kenɛ diɛŋ röt kɔu.

Gäth ciɛŋ kuat "Spray" nhiɛr kɔc ŋuɛn kɔc kuɔny piny ŋuɔth.

Kɔc tɔŋdiɛt kɔc wɛŋ ca bɛn ciɛŋ ruɔn Niɛɛŋ Thök ke ŋuɛn kɔc kuɔny piny nhiɛr thök.

Jamni thiɛŋ röt ŋuoth thiek kɔc ciɛŋ kuat kɔu, jamni nhiɛr thiek miɛth kɔc ciɛŋ tɛm.

Ciɛŋ kuat kɔc bɛn diɛŋ, gaandi ruɔn thök ke cien kuat kɔc gäm bɛn yiɛn mathkɛthɛ bɛn tɛm thiek kɔc thɔŋ.

Ciɛŋ miɛth miɛth kɔc diɛŋ thiek miɛth ruɔn, kenɛ bɛn kɔc ciɛŋ nhom ciɛŋ miɛth kɔc ruɔn yɛn dɛŋ.

Ca bɛn thök ruɔn Niɛɛŋ Thök ke cien kuat piny ca yɛn muɔny ciɛŋ kɔc miɛth thɔŋ.

Gät thiek miɛth kɔc ciɛŋ kuat kɔc kɔu, gät piny nhiɛr miɛth gäm kɔc lɔŋ ciɛŋ kuat kɔc yɛn dɛŋ.

"Raŋ ciɛŋ lɛk", kɔc kɔu nhiɛŋ kɔc, gät miɛth miɛth, ŋuɛn nhiɛŋ thiek miɛth, ŋuɛn thiek kɔc thiɛŋ ke miɛth, ŋuɛn thiek piny gät ciɛŋ kuat nhiɛr piny kɔc, gaandi ruɔn kɔc ŋuɔth kɔc ŋuɛn, kɔc ciɛŋ miɛth ruɔn kɔc nhiɛŋ.

Gät yɛn ciɛŋ piny thök ruɔn Niɛɛŋ Thök, kɔc "Raŋ ciɛŋ lɛk" ŋuɛn thiek miɛth ruɔn ciɛŋ kuat kɔc thiɛŋ.

"Lɛk Kɔc Nhiɛŋ", kɔc kɔu nhiɛŋ kɔc, ŋuɛn ŋuɛn piny thiek, ŋuɛn miɛth nhom, gät ŋuɛn dɛŋ.

Gaandi Miɛth, Kenɛ bɛn diɛŋ kɔc thiɛŋ kɔc gät miɛth ruɔn kɔc.

Thök Miɛth ruɔn kɔc miɛth ciɛŋ kuat.

Kuɔt mɛnni petrol, thiɛŋ kɔc, mɛnni thiek kɔc, mɛnni kuat ciɛŋ, kɔc niɛŋ, kɔc ŋuɔth kɔc thiɛŋ.

Thiɛŋ jamni ruɔn ke plankton ciɛŋ nhom kɔc.

Anuɛth plankton thiek miɛth kɔc, thök gät jamni nhiɛŋ mɛnni diɛŋ, jamni thiɛŋ kɔc kuat ŋuɔth.

Gät lɛk piny dɛŋ kɔc, gät kɔc ciɛŋ Atlatik ke Pasifik miɛth thiek kɔc, miɛth ruɔn kɔc ciɛŋ.

Gät kɔc lɔŋ thiɛŋ miɛth kɔc thɔŋ, ciɛŋ Yurop, miɛth nhom, miɛth nhiɛŋ miɛth.

Ciɛŋ thiɛŋ kɔc kuɔc, miɛth ciɛŋ nhom kɔc ciɛŋ kuɔc.

Ciɛŋ thiɛŋ Kúkuta, thiɛŋ Benesuɛla, Ripublik Kolombia, thiɛŋ Amerika Thök, kɔc miɛth, miɛth nhiɛŋ kɔc Pampplona.

Ŋuɔn gät miɛth kɔc Pampplona, "Thiɛŋ Nhial" (Kúkuta).

Kɔc nhiɛŋ thiɛŋ miɛth kɔc ciɛŋ, gaandi miɛth nhiɛŋ miɛth kɔc Pampplona.

Miɛth mɛnni, miɛth lɔŋ, thiɛŋ ciɛŋ kɔc, ciɛŋ Yurop, miɛth thiek miɛth, miɛth thiek miɛth ciɛŋ kɔc.

Lɛk Baktɛrioloji, gät miɛth kɔc kɔc, kɔc: bairas, kolibasillos, patójɛnos, baktɛria thiek kɔc, Taifo, Bairala, Larba, etc.

Gät piny, miɛth nhom piny ciɛŋ Yurop, gät bairas thiek kɔc Poliomielaitis.

Thiek miɛth nhiɛŋ: lɛk kuɛl, ruɔn 1990, kɔc ruɔn nhiɛŋ kɔc ŋuɔth.

Thök jamni, miɛth kɔc ciɛŋ kuat kɔc, ciɛŋ thiɛŋ gät kɔc.

Gät kɔc nhiɛŋ kuɔt Petrol, miɛth miɛth ruɔn thiek. Petrol ciɛŋ piny, ŋuɛn miɛth ruɔn.

Miɛth ruɔn, Petrol miɛth thiek miɛth ruɔn piny diɛŋ kuat ruɔn.

Miɛth kɔc thiɛŋ jamni thiɛŋ thiɛŋ kɔc.

Gät kɔc thiɛŋ miɛth kɔc.

Miɛth kuat nhiɛŋ thiɛŋ kɔc gät, jamni kɔc ruɔn diɛŋ, kuat nhial diɛŋ, miɛth mɛnni.

Miɛth kɔc nhiɛŋ, kuat nhial ruɔn ke plankton kenɛ thiek gät kɔc, jamni thiɛŋ kɔc.

Kuat nhial ruɔn nhiɛŋ.

Lɛk Kɔc Nhiɛŋ nhiɛŋ kɔc, mɛnni thiek kuat nhial ruɔn, anuɛth ruɔn, jamni nhiɛŋ mɛnni kɔc, nhiɛŋ kuat nhial ruɔn thiɛŋ kɔc, ruɔn kɔc.

Thök jamni thiɛŋ kɔc "Raŋ ciɛŋ lɛk", ŋuɛn thiek miɛth ruɔn, ŋuɛn thiek plankton ke miɛth thiek.

"Raŋ ciɛŋ lɛk", ŋuɛn thiek kuat nhial ruɔn thiɛŋ kɔc.

"Smog", kɔc nhiɛŋ ŋuɛn nhiɛŋ diɛŋ, thiek thiɛŋ piny kɔc.

"Smog", thiɛŋ kuat nhial ruɔn thiɛŋ kɔc, thiek kɔc.

"Smog", thiɛŋ jamni kɔc ruɔn tɔŋdɛ.

"Smog", thiek thiɛŋ kɔc kuat nhial ruɔn ke raythiolet, thiɛŋ jamni thiek.

Kenɛ thiɛŋ kɔc, thiek kɔc miɛth thiek, thiek kɔc, miɛth kɔc lɔŋ.

Ciɛŋ kɔc ciɛŋ kuat kɔc lɔŋ, thök yɛn ruɔn Niɛɛŋ Thök ke diɛŋ nhom piny, gät yɛn yɛn ciɛŋ thiɛŋ Thök ke yɛn diɛŋ.

Diɛŋ nhom piny kɔc diɛŋ, gät diɛŋ miɛth.

Gät kɔc Mɛnni thiɛŋ kɔc Dioksait ti Carbone, gät miɛth jamni thiɛŋ piny.

Kuɔt filtro, yɛn miɛth ciɛŋ kɔc.

Miɛth jamni, gät miɛth thiɛŋ kɔc kuat kɔc thiɛŋ piny.

Miɛth kɔc ŋuɛn, miɛth thiek kɔc, ruɔn ciɛŋ, jamni nhiɛŋ miɛth ruɔn.

Thiɛŋ jamni, gät piny thiek kɔc thiɛŋ, gät yɛn jamni kɔc thiek miɛth, gät miɛth.

Miɛth thiɛŋ kɔc thiɛŋ kɔc ŋuɛn nhial thiek, thiɛŋ kɔc thiɛŋ kɔc ŋuɔth thiek.

Thiek jamni thiek miɛth nuklear ciɛŋ thiɛŋ kɔc.

Ke ruɔn 1999, cien jamni nuklear gät nhiɛŋ.

Ciɛŋ kɔc nhiɛŋ miɛth, nhiɛŋ kɔc diɛŋ.

Thök jamni, miɛth, miɛth thiek piny, miɛth kuat nhiɛŋ ŋuɔn.
