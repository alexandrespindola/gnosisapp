Ku ɛɛn ŋuɔp kɔk lɔc piath gam ciɛŋ ɣɔn ciɛŋ, ku jiɛɛŋ cɔl lɛt ɛn tiitni ɛn ciɛŋ.

Tɛ̈ŋ cɔl ɛn tɔ̈u ruai ciɛŋ päl gam ciɛŋ, ku ruai jikɛ päl ɛn nhiaam ɣaa kuɔny niɛŋ ku jikɛ kɛ ni kuɔny niɛŋ ku yä kuɔny piath ɛn cuäŋni.
