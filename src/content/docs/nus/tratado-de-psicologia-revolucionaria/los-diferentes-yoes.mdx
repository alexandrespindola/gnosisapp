Raan mi̠th ci̠ kuɛlɛ luŋ kɔ̱ɔ̱rɛ man, ɛ gɔ̱aaŋ ca mi̠th cakɛ thɛɛth.

Kɛ ji̠ŋɛ ci̠ kuɛlɛ kɔ̱k mi̠th ca nhiaar, ɛ ci̠ kuɛlɛ kɔ̱k thɛth ku thɛɛth.

Ri̠ɛŋkɛ ci̠ kuɛlɛ thooŋ ku baai̠ de̠ŋ, ku baai̠ ca de̠ŋ ɛn thɔɔŋ, kɔ̱kɛn ci̠ laal.

Kɛnɛ ci̠ nhi̠aam mi̠th ca kuɛlɛ ke̠ de̠ŋ.

Yɛŋ ci̠ ɛ nhiaarɛ ci̠ thɔɔŋ, kɔ̱kɛn ci̠ kɔ̱ɔ̱rɛ kɛ kuɛlɛ ca jɛi̠ se̠e̠t-ɛsɔʊtɛri̠k ku se̠e̠t-ɔkɔʊlti̠st, ɛ ca ke̠ di̠it ci̠ nhi̠aam mi̠th ca kuɛlɛ piny.

Ɛn de̠ŋ kɛnɛ, ɛ gɔ̱aaŋ ca baai̠ mi̠th ca kuɛlɛ piny.

Kuɛlɛ luŋ kɔ̱ɔ̱rɛ mi̠th ca nhi̠aam, kuɛlɛ mi̠th ci̠ ji̠ŋɛ, kuɛlɛ gɔ̱aaŋ ci̠ "Yoes".

Kɔ̱cɛ di̠tni̠ thɔɔŋ kɛnɛ, ɛ ca ke̠ nhi̠im "YO", ɛ ca ke̠ lɛ̠ɛ̠r, ɛ ca ke̠ thii̠kɛn, ɛ ca ke̠ lɔɔc "ALTER EGO", "YO SUPERIOR", "YO DIVINO", ect., ect., ect.

Ɛ ca ke̠ jɛi̠ kɔ̱cɛ thɔɔŋ kɛnɛ ca ke̠ dɛŋ, ci̠ "Yo Superior" ko̠ ci̠ "Yo Inferior", kuɛlɛ thi̠th ci̠ Ego ɛ ji̠ŋɛ.

Kuɛlɛ ca ji̠ŋɛn "YO Permanente" kɔ̱k ci̠ gɔ̱aaŋ ca "Yoes" Infrahumanos ku ca ji̠ŋɛ.

Mi̠thɛ kuɛlɛ ca thɔɔŋ kɛnɛ, kuɛlɛ ru̠a̠i̠ ku ca ke̠ ri̠ɛŋkɛ ca mɛɛt, kuɛlɛ mi̠th ca ke̠ caath, kuɛlɛ kɔ̱c ɛ luɔkɛn ku ca ke̠ tɛɛm.

Yɛŋɛ ci̠ nhiaar kɛ se̠e̠t-ɛsɔʊtɛri̠zm ku se̠e̠t-ɔkɔʊlti̠zmɛ, ɛ ŋu̠tɛn kuɛlɛ kɔ̱cɛdi̠t ku ca ke̠ luɔk "YO Permanente e Inmutable" kɔ̱kɛn ci̠ lɔɔc ku kɔ̱kɛn ci̠ thɔɔŋ.

Ci̠ kɔ̱cɛ ci̠ ca ke̠ di̠it kɛnɛ ku ca ke̠ di̠it, ɛ ca ke̠ pɛɛth kɛnɛ kuɛlɛ luŋ mi̠th ca di̠t jɛi̠ ke̠ nhi̠i̠m.

Raan mi̠th ca thɔɔŋ kɛnɛ, ɛ ci̠ guuŋ.

Di̠it kɛnɛ, ci̠ guuŋɛn.

Di̠it kuɛlɛ gɔ̱aaŋ kuɛlɛ Luis kuɛlɛ Luis, ɛ kuɛlɛ gɔ̱aaŋ.

Kuɛlɛ tɛ di̠it kuɛlɛ Luis ɛ ca ke̠ thi̠th ci̠ "Yoes", kuɛlɛ egos, ku ca ke̠ di̠it kɛnɛ tɛ ji̠ŋɛn ku gɔ̱aaŋ ca ke̠ di̠itɛn.

Kɔ̱kɛn ci̠ ji̠ŋɛ ca raan mi̠th ca baai̠ɛn, ɛn de̠ŋ kuɛlɛ di̠it kɔ̱cɛ ke̠ di̠it.

Suponer kuɛlɛ ca raan luɔk "Yo Permanente e Inmutable" ɛ ca ke̠ nhi̠aam kɛ ke̠ cu̠ŋ.

Piny ca raan ɛ ca ke̠ thi̠th kuɛlɛ kɔ̱c, kuɛlɛ "Yoes", kɔ̱c ku ca ke̠ di̠it kɛnɛ, kuɛlɛ raan di̠it ku ca ke̠ di̠itɛn.
