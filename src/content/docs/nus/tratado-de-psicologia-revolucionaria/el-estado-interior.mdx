Tɛkɛni käŋ kɛ jiɛɛŋ miɛtni kuat kɛnɛ ciɛŋɛ, ëlä kɛ jiɛɛŋ wëi jiɛm diɛŋ...

Yic kuat rɛŋdiɛ miɛtni kuatni kɛnɛ ciɛŋɛ, aa kɛnɛ miɛtni kuat kɛnɛ luɛŋ...

Kɛnɛ cɔl, kɔc aa yɔɔt tiɛmda, kɔc aa gam kaŋ ɛyɛ tiɛm aa käŋ kuatda...

Kɔc ciɛŋ kɔŋ! kɔc aa gam kaŋ kaŋ käŋ kuatni aa tɛkɛni, tiɛmda aanɔŋ ciɛŋ...

Kɔc aa gam kaŋ tɛkɛni aa rɛŋ kaŋ kɛnɛ diɛtni aa kɛnɛ lɔɔr...

Kɔc aa gam kaŋ miɛtdɛ, kɔc aa miɛt käŋ kɛnɛ cɔɔthni, kɔc aa kɔc jɛŋ kɛnɛ kuatni...

Kɔc aa gam kaŋ tɛkɛni ɛyɛ kaam ɛyɛ päl ɛyɛ piɛŋ kɛnɛ miɛtni ke cuŋni aa lääri, ëlä aa jiɛɛŋ kɛnɛ yii diɛŋ käŋ kɛnɛ kuɔth...

Diɛŋ ee kaŋ aŋiɛcni ee tiɛmda, ŋu aŋiɛcni aa pial kɛnɛ tuɛŋdɛ, käŋ ciɛŋdɛ aa ŋicni kuɔth...

Kɔc aa gɔaa rɛŋ ŋu aa pial niu, kɔc aa tɔɔr kuɔth ɛyɛ kaŋ piŋ...

Diɛŋ ŋu aa jɛŋ kuɔth kɛnɛ tiɛmda, käŋ kɛnɛ rɛŋdiɛ miɛtni kuatni...

Käŋ ee kaŋ miɛtni kuatni, ŋu aa kɛnɛ miɛtni diɛŋ käŋ aa kaŋ lɔɔr, käŋ aa kaŋ tuɛŋdɛ...

Tɛk aa jam rɛŋ kuɔth ëkɛni, ŋu aa käŋ tuɛŋdɛ, ëlä aa rɛŋ ŋu aa miɛtni diɛŋ, käŋ kɛnɛ luɛŋ aa kaŋ lɔɔr...

Kɔc aa tiɛmda ɛyɛ käŋ kuatni aa kɛnɛ miɛtni ciɛŋdiɛ, aa rɛŋ kɛnɛ lääri...

Kɔc aa gam kaŋ tɛkɛni ee jiɛɛŋdiɛ: kɔc aa miɛt ŋu aa yɛnni ɛyɛ kɔc aa yɛn ŋu aa miɛt...

Diɛŋ aa pial: Kɔc aa tiɛmda aa yɛnni ɛyɛ kaŋ diɛŋ aa kɛnɛ cuŋni aa kaŋ wëi, kɔc aa lɔɔr, kaŋ miɛtni aa kaŋ cuŋni aa kaŋ lɔɔr...

Kɔc aa gamni ee kaŋ kɛnɛ kuat ɛyɛ käŋ kɛnɛ miɛtni kuatni...

Kɔc aa gam kaŋ ɛyɛ kaŋ cɔɔthdiɛ: kɔc aa miɛtni ŋu aa yɛnni ɛyɛ kɔc aa yɛnni ŋu aa miɛt...

Diɛŋ aa pial: Kɔc aa yɛnni ɛyɛ kɔc aa lɔɔrni ee kaŋ kɛnɛ kuatni ee kaŋ miɛtni diɛŋ...

Kuɔth ee diɛŋni ee kɔc aa miɛtni kuatni ee kaŋ cɔɔth ee jiɛɛŋ...

Tiɛm aa jam kɔc aa cuŋni, ŋu aa kaŋ miɛtni diɛŋ...

Kɔc aa gamni ee kaŋ kɛnɛ miɛtni ŋu aa kaŋ cuŋ ɛyɛ käŋ kɛnɛ luɛŋni.

Miɛtni aa pial: kɔc aa diɛŋ ee yii yɛnni kɛnɛ miɛtni kuatni.

Kɔc aa gam kaŋ tɛkɛni aa käŋ kuatni ee kaŋ cɔɔthni ɛyɛ miɛtni kuatni...

Kɔc aa tiɛm ɛyɛ jiɛɛŋdiɛ...
