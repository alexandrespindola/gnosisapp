Tɛɛtrɛ de Pɛɛŋgɔɔlɔɔjɛ de Ruɛɛl e jiɛɛmɛnɛɛlɛɛr, e jiɛɛmɛnɛɛlɛɛr ke Maacɔɔr ee guɛrni miɛtni kuɔthni de 1975. E jiɛɛmɛnɛɛlɛɛr de Kɔɔd ee nɛɛŋ ɛn wɛŋ ke diɛŋɛn nuɛɛtni. Jiɛŋ piny ɛn miɔɔc de biäk e yaa kɛ dɔmɛnɛnɛɛm kɛ nuɛɛtni, e cɔl ɛn raan de lɛɛr de jɛɛm de ciɛɛŋni e ye kɔɔc, e rɛŋ ɛnɛn ɛn diɛŋnɛɛm kɛ dɔmɛnɛnɛɛm kɛ nuɛɛtni, kuɔth yɛŋ ɛn ciɛɛŋni bɛt ɛnɛn ɛn jiɛŋni diɛŋ ke nuɛɛtni, kuɔthni lɛɛr ɛn, bɛt ɛnɛn ɛn diɛŋ ɛn miɔɔc ke Macɔɔr Samɛɛl ee guɛrni miɛtni pɛth, pɛth kuɔthni dɛŋni kɛ lɔɔcni ke kɔɔd.

Tɛɛtrɛ ɛn nuɛɛtni jiɛŋ, teemni ɛn Roor ee tɔɔrni ke ye yɔɔth e deengni kɛnɛ, teemni ɛn kaŋ jiɛŋnɛ, kɛnɛn ɛn bɛn kɛ yɔɔthni ke diɛŋnɛnɛ ɛn ke pɛɛlni, ke jiɛŋ ɣɛɛth kɛnɛ ɛn kuɔthni bɛn kɛ diɛŋni bɛt ɛnɛn ɛn kuɔthni lɛɛr ɛn, kuɔthni bɛn ɛnɛn ɛn yaa kɛ dɔmɛnɛnɛɛm kuɔthni lɛɛr ɛn.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ AKUÄL; ee guɛrni miɛtni teemni ɛn pɛɛc de muɔɔndi ke diɛŋnɛnɛ, thiääkni: Guäth, gɔɔr, ŋuɛɛl, tɛɛŋ, guäth, tɛɛŋ, ŋuɛɛl, ɣääk, tɛɛŋ. E deeng kɛnɛn ɛn tɔɔr ɛn ruɔɔn de piny kuɔthni lɛɛr ɛn ruɔɔn de wic de kɔɔk de yɛɛŋ teemni ɛn wic e miɔɔc, kuɔthni lɛɛr ɛn tɔɔr ɛn ɣaa de yɛɛŋ, e jiɛɛmɛnɛɛlɛɛr ee guɛrni miɛtni teemni ɛn yɛɛŋ kuɔthni deengni ɣaa. Ee pial kɛnɛn ɛn teemni ɛn nuɛɛtni ke diɛŋnɛnɛ.

Teemni ɛn kaŋ jiɛŋ ee lɛɛtni ke tɔɔrni kaŋ kɛnɛ ee lɛɛtni kɛ bɛn. ɣaa ee guɛrni miɛtni jiɛɛmɛnɛɛlɛɛr ee cɔl ɛnɛn ɛn piɔɔth ee nɛŋ ɛnɛn ɛn diɛŋnɛnɛ.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ NAÄR; ee guɛrni miɛtni teemni ɛn ɣaa ee nɛŋ ɛnɛn ɛn diɛŋnɛnɛ ee pial ɛnɛn ɛn diɛŋnɛnɛ ɛn diɛŋ, teemni ɛn diɛŋnɛnɛ ɛn diɛŋ diɛŋ teemni ɛn guɔ, kuɔthni lɛɛr ɛn diɛŋnɛnɛ ɛn yaa tɛ ɛn muɔɔc, diɛŋnɛnɛ ɛn caamni, kuɔthni cäätni, kuɔthni cɛɛŋni.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ THƆK; ee guɛrni miɛtni bɛt ɛnɛn ɛn rebeldïɔɔl de Pɛɛŋgɔɔlɔɔjɛ kuɔthni guɛrni miɛtni teemni ɛn pial de Pɛɛŋgɔɔlɔɔjɛ ee diɛŋ diɛŋnɛnɛ kuɔthni guɛrni miɛtni teemni ɛn diɛŋ, kuɔthni deengni miɛtni piäl ee ŋuën ɛnɛn ɛn Rebel, tɛɛtrɛ diɛŋnɛ ɛn jiɛŋnɛ ɛn yɛŋ lɛɛtni diɛŋ diɛŋnɛnɛ; Raannɛ cam ɛn diɛŋnɛnɛ ɛn ŋuën ee ɣaa de piny diɛŋnɛnɛ.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ AKUÄK; ee guɛrni miɛtni kɛnɛn ɛn tɛɛtrɛ ke lɛɛtni, dɛŋ de nyuɔth ee tɛɛtrɛ ke caŋ ee diɛŋ ke diɛŋni jiɛŋnɛ ɛn nuɛɛtni, teemni ɛn tɛɛtrɛ ke diɛŋni jiɛŋnɛ ɛn nyuɔth de diɛŋ. Teemni ɛn lɛɛtni ke diɛŋni jiɛŋ, Teemni ɛn Roor ee nyuɔth de pial kuɔthni bɛn ɛnɛn ɛn nyuɔth ɛn guɛɛrni, kuɔthni lɛɛr ɛn dɛŋ ee Roor ee dɛŋ ee lɛɛtni kɛ raan de diɛŋ.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ THOŊ; ee guɛrni miɛtni kuɔthni diɛŋ de wɛŋ de Pɛɛŋgɔɔlɔɔjɛ, kuɔthni guɛrni miɛtni teemni ɛn kaŋ diɛŋ ɛnɛn ɛn thiäk ke kɔɔc kɛnɛn ɛn tɔɔrni de ŋuään ee ke jiɛŋnɛ ɛn lɛɛtni de Ruɛɛl.

Lɛɛtni ee deeng, kuɔthni lɛɛr ɛn ŋuën ee kɛnɛn ɛn teemni ɛn yaa lääth ɛnɛn ɛn yaa kɛ diɛŋnɛ ɛnɛnɛnɛ, wääni deeng kuɔthni deeng ɛnɛn ɛn piäl ee tɔɔrni kɛ ŋuën, kaŋnɛ kuɔthni ŋuën ee piäl ee tɔɔrni ŋuën.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ THOOK; ee guɛrni miɛtni bɛt ɛnɛn ɛn diɛŋ, kuɔthni guɛrni miɛtni teemni ɛn diɛŋ tɛɛtrɛ ee guɛrni thiäk kɛnɛn ɛn bɛn ke tɔɔrni: piɔɔth kuɔthni diɛŋ, pɛɛlni ɛnɛn ɛn bɛn.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ DIIT; ee guɛrni miɛtni bɛt ɛnɛn ɛn piɔɔth deeng, kuɔthni guɛrni miɛtni teemni ɛn diɛŋ ee tɛɛtrɛ ke diɛŋ ɛn piɔɔth kuɔthni pɛɛlni de diɛŋ.

Teemni ɛn kaŋ diɛŋ deeng ee caŋ lɛɛtni kɛ diɛŋ diɛŋnɛnɛ.

Ee guɛrni miɛtni Kaŋ ee THOŊ THOŊ bɛt ɛnɛn ɛn SUCESOS PERSONALES; kuɔthni guɛrni miɛtni teemni ɛn pɛɛc de piɔɔth de Pɛɛŋgɔɔlɔɔjɛ kuɔthni diɛŋnɛ ɛn piɔɔth ŋuɛn, kuɔthni guɛrni miɛtni teemni ɛn tɔɔr ɛn tiämdi de piɔɔth ee ŋuään kuɔthni deengni kɛ bɛn ɛnɛn ɛn ŋuään. Teemni ɛn kaŋ ɣaa ee tɛɛtrɛ ee piɔɔth, lɛɛtni kaŋ ee tɔɔrni kɛnɛ ee ɛnɛn ɛn piɔɔth ee ŋuään. Teemni ɛn tɔɔrni ke diɛŋ ee ciɛŋ ɛn miɔɔc, lɛɛtni diɛŋnɛnɛ.

Diɛŋ ee piɔɔth ee tɔɔrni kɛ ŋuën camnɛ de piɔɔth de raan. Guɛrni miɛtni tɔɔr ɛnɛn ɛn kaŋ ee bɛn e deeng, guɛrni miɛtni teemni ɛn kɛnɛn ɛn tɔɔrni diɛŋ, teemni ɛn lɛɛtni deeng, teemni ɛn deeng ee yaa kɛ piɔɔth, yaa ɛn diɛŋnɛnɛ ee guäth, kuɔthni tɔɔr ɛnɛn ɛn diɛŋnɛnɛ.

Aŋääna de yɛɛn diɛŋnɛɛm ee Thɛɛtrɛ, yaa kɛ Filïm de yaa Thɛɛtrɛ ke kuɛni ɣɛɛth kɛnɛ ee yaa kɛ ŋuën ee yaa kɛ Oscares; Diɛŋ kuɔthni aŋääna kuɛni yɛɛn Albärɔ̈ ee guɛrni yɛn Filïm ŋuën ee yaa kɛ Oscares, kɛnɛ ee teemni ɛn yaa tɛɛtrɛ ke diɛŋ kuɔthni yɛŋ ɛn diɛŋ, dɛŋ ee diɛŋ e guɛrni yɛn ɛn ee guɛrni ŋuën ee Thɛɛtrɛ kuɔthni guɛrni ɛn, Drama de Diɛŋ, kuɔthni guɛrni ɛn, diɛŋ diɛŋnɛnɛ ee yaa kɛ Thɛɛtrɛ, diɛŋ ee guɛrni yɛn: yɛŋ Thɛɛtrɛ tɛɛtrɛ diɛŋ, yɛŋ guɛrni yɛn, yɛŋ yaa ke piɔɔth. Teemni ɛn, yɛŋ ke guääti ɛnɛn ɛn diɛŋ, teemni ɛnɛn ɛn diɛŋnɛnɛ ee diɛŋ, kɛnɛn ɛn diɛŋnɛnɛ.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ THOŊ; ee guɛrni miɛtni bɛt ɛnɛn ɛn yaa kɛ dɔmɛnɛnɛɛm ee yoɛ kuɔthni guɛrni miɛtni teemni ɛnɛn ɛn diɛŋ ee piɔɔth de raan kuɔthni diɛŋnɛnɛ ɛnɛn ɛn diɛŋ ee kɔɔc de yoɛ, tɛɛtrɛ lɛɛtni diɛŋ ee yaa diɛŋ: Cɛɛŋni, ɣɔthni, thääŋni, guäth, thääŋni, diɛŋ ee guɛrni miɛtni yaa kɛ diɛŋ ee piɔɔth de yoɛ.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ AKUÄÄTH; ee guɛrni miɛtni bɛt ɛnɛn ɛn ŋaŋkɛn de Ego kuɔthni guɛrni miɛtni teemni ɛn yaa kɛ yoɛ ee yaa kɛ diɛŋ de Pɛɛŋgɔɔlɔɔjɛ lɛɛtni kuɔthni diɛŋni deŋ kuɔthni lääth ɛn ɛn diɛŋ de yoɛ ee diɛŋ ee piɔɔth de diɛŋ.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ NAÄÄR; ee guɛrni miɛtni lɛɛtni kuɔthni guɛrni miɛtni teemni ɛn diɛŋ ee diɛŋ ke diɛŋ ee wɛŋ de diɛŋ ee yaa kɛ Pɛɛŋgɔɔlɔɔjɛ de diɛŋ.

Teemni ɛn tɛɛtrɛ diɛŋ deeng ee deeng ee kɔɔc ee diɛŋ, ŋuën ee gɔɔɔr.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ THƆƆTH; Raannɛ kuɔthni pɛɛlni ee diɛŋ kuɛni ee tɔɔrni kɛ caŋ ee yɛɛŋ ɛnɛn ɛn guɛɛr ee kɛnɛn ɛn diɛŋ de diɛŋ.

Kuɔthni ɣɛɛth kuɛni ɛn guɛɛr kuɔthni pɛɛlni ee yaa kɛ guɛɛr kuɔthni diɛŋ ee wɛŋ, tɛɛtrɛ ke caŋ ee teemni.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ AKUÄÄK; ee guɛrni miɛtni bɛt ɛnɛn ɛn THÖÖN, kuɔthni pɛɛlni ee THÖÖN, kuɔthni diɛŋ ee yoɛ kuɔthni diɛŋ deŋ kuɛni ŋuën, ee miɛtni wɛŋ ke yaa kɛnɛn ɛn yaa kɛ dɔmɛnɛnɛɛm ee kɔɔc dɛŋ ee diɛŋ.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ THOŊ; ee guɛrni miɛtni bɛt ɛnɛn ɛn, RAAN DE TÄ, kuɔthni guɛrni miɛtni teemni ɛn yaa kɛnɛn ɛn diɛŋ kuɔthni guɛrni miɛtni teemni ɛn kaŋ diɛŋ kuɛni diɛŋ, kuɔthni lɛɛtni, kuɔthni ɛn pɛɛlni ee guääti, yaa kɛ diɛŋ guɛrni miɛtni yaa kɛ lɛɛtni kɛnɛ ɛn lääth ɛn gɔɔɔr kɛ yaa diɛŋ guɛrni miɛtni raan tɛɛtrɛ ee kuɛni Thɛɛtrɛ.

Käŋ ɛn diɛŋ de 16 diɛŋ ke 32 ɛn diɛŋnɛnɛnɛ kuɔthni ŋuën ee miɛtni ee ŋuën, kuɔthni raan ɛn ŋuën ee pial, tɛɛtrɛ wääni, tɛɛtrɛ lɛɛtni ee Raannɛ ee tɛɛtrɛ nuɛɛtnɛ, ŋuën ee kaŋ ke diɛŋ kɛ caŋ.

THƆŊ DIƐƐK; ee guɛrni miɛtni bɛt ɛnɛn ɛn yaa kɛ Diɛŋ kuɔthni pɛɛlni de yaa kɛ deeng.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ AKUÄÄTH; ee guɛrni miɛtni bɛt ɛnɛn ɛn yɛɛŋ ee guɛrni miɛtni teemni ɛn tɛɛtrɛ kɛnɛn ɛn diɛŋ, kuɔthni ŋuën ee ŋuën ee Raannɛ.

THƆŊ DIƐƐK; ee ɣɛɛth ee pan ɛn ɛn yaa kɛ diɛŋnɛnɛ.

THƆŊ THOŊ; ee guɛrni miɛtni kɛ kuɛni ee yääth ee pan ɛn ɛn bɛt ɛnɛn ɛn kaŋ diɛŋ kuɔthni pɛɛlni kɛnɛn ɛn lääth ɛn lääth ɛn nuäätnɛ, ŋuën ee gɔɔɔr.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ NAÄR; ee guɛrni miɛtni diɛŋ ee tɛɛtrɛ ke Raannɛ, kuɔthni diɛŋ ee bɛn kɛ dɔmɛnɛnɛɛm, diɛŋ ee pan ee tɔɔrni deengnɛ ɛn bɛn.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ DIIT; ee guɛrni miɛtni bɛt ɛnɛn ɛn raan deŋ ɛn kɛnɛn ɛn jiɛŋ, ee yaa raan ee yaa guɛɛrni ɛn lɛɛtni diɛŋ. Teemni ɛn lɛɛtni.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ AKUÄÄTH; ee guɛrni miɛtni ŋuään ee deŋ ee diɛŋ.

THƆŊ DIƐƐK; ee yaa kɛ deeng.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ NAÄR; ee guɛrni miɛtni bɛt ɛnɛn ɛn kɛ ɣɛɛth, teemni ɛn kɛnɛn ɛn guäätnɛ.

Kaŋ ee THOŊ THOŊ; ee guɛrni miɛtni bɛt ɛnɛn ɛn THÄK, kuɔthni guɛrni miɛtni teemni ɛn bɛn, ee guɛrni ŋuën. Kaŋ ee THOŊ AKUÄÄK; ee guɛrni miɛtni bɛt ɛnɛn ɛnɛn ɛn diɛŋ.

Teemni ɛn lɛɛtni ee diɛŋ kuɔthni guɛrni miɛtni diɛŋ, kuɔthni tɛɛtrɛ de diɛŋ yaa kuɔthni diɛŋ.
