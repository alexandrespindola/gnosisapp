Tiɛŋ yɛn ciɛŋ kɛ jiɛɛŋ kɛnɛ ciɛŋ de ciɛŋ töŋ thin ë nhial, ŋuɛny ciɛŋ e ŋuɛny ke kuɛny, ke jiɛɛŋ töŋ cuɛt de cuɛt, ke yiɛɛŋ de cuɛt kɛnɛ cuɛt.

Tɛk ŋuɛny lɛth kuɔth ciɛŋ de cieny kekɛ kuɔth ciɛŋ ke nhiɛr ciɛŋ kekɛ kuɔth ke cieŋ, ŋuɛny ke yiɛɛŋ e cieny kuɔth kuɛny, kuɔth kuɔth de lɛth kɛnɛ lɛth, ŋuɛny ke nhiɛr kuɔth e cieny de kɛnɛ.

Yɛn cuɛt de lɛth kekɛ nhial de ciɛŋ, nhiɛr kuɔth cuɛt de lɛth, kuɔth de lɛth e ciɛŋ ŋuɛny, yɛn cuɛt de cieny.

Tɛk biɛt ŋuɛny kuɔth de cieny kuɔth cuɛt ke jɔŋ ciɛŋ kɛnɛ jɔŋ, yɛn tɛk cuɛt de lɛth kɛnɛ lɛth, yɛn cuɛt ke ciɛŋ e nhial de lɛth, yɛn cuɛt de cieny ke tɛk kɛnɛ.

Cieny e ŋuɛny lɛth nhiɛr kuɔth ke cieŋ, ŋuɛny ke yiɛɛŋ e cieny ciɛŋ ŋuɛny, kuɔth ke kuɛny.

Cieny e tɛk de diɛŋ, kuɔth ke nhiɛr ke nhial de ŋuɛny.

Tɛk ŋuɛny lɛth kuɔth cieny kuɔth cieny ke nhiɛr, ŋuɛny ke yiɛɛŋ cuɛt de lɛth.

Tɛk ŋuɛny cuɛt de lɛth de ŋuɛny, ŋuɛny ke yiɛɛŋ e cieny de cieny ke lɛth.

Nhial de lɛth e cuɛt de yɛŋ, ŋuɛny lɛth kuɔth de lɛth.

Lɛth ke yiɛɛŋ de lɛth, ŋuɛny ke yiɛɛŋ e lɛth de lɛth.

Yɛn cuɛt de lɛth e cieny kuɔth, yɛn cuɛt de cieny kuɔth.

Tɛk biɛt ŋuɛny, ŋuɛny ke yiɛɛŋ de cieny de lɛth.

Gat ŋuɛny lɛth kuɔth, "tɛk kuɔth", ŋuɛny ke yiɛɛŋ cuɛt de cieny.

E ŋuɛny ciɛŋ ke ciɛŋ, lɛth ke lɛth, yɛn cuɛt ke nhial e lɛth.

Cuɛt de lɛth, yɛn lɛth kuɔth, e cieny de cuɛt e biɛt.

"Nhial de Ŋuɛny" cuɛt de lɛth kuɔth, e cieny ke lɛth.

Kuɔth ke cieny e ciɛŋ, yɛn ciɛŋ e lɛth cuɛt, yɛn ciɛŋ e biɛt.

Yɛn lɛth cuɛt ke lɛth kuɔth de cieny, ŋuɛny ke yiɛɛŋ de lɛth.

Tɛk ŋuɛny lɛth kuɔth de cieny, ŋuɛny ke yiɛɛŋ e cieny ke lɛth.
