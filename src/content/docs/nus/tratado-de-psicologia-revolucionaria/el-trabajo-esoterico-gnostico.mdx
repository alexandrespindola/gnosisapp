Kɛ yin bi̱ŋ di̱t Gnosis ku kuɔny ɛ ji̱icni tɛ dicni kɛ ji̱i̱k kɛ di̱ɛ̱tni kɛnɛ ci̱ɛ̱ŋni ku yɛn.

Ti gɔaa̱ti di̱ɛ̱tnɛ ji̱i̱kni kɛ kuɔny kɛ ji̱icni ku lɔc thɔŋ mi̱th wɛy bɛn kɛnɛ kuɔny wɛy, ti ci̱ɛ̱ŋni di̱ɛ̱tnɛ yɛn dɛŋ.

Di̱ɛ̱tnɛ ji̱i̱kni cuɔw kɛnɛ rɛy dɔk ɛ ji̱i̱kni.

Mi̱th kɛnɛ kɛ ciɛŋni gɔaa̱ piny ɛ buɔth, ɛ cien ɛ cuat ɛ ji̱i̱k.

Ti gɔaa̱ di̱ɛ̱tnɛ mi̱th wɛy yɛn cuŋ, kɛnɛ cuŋni ɛ ru̱ɔ̱tni jɛ. Gɔaa̱ni piny ɛ cien ɛ cuat ɛ ji̱i̱k ɛ ru̱ɔ̱tni.

Lɔcni ɛ ji̱ŋkɛni, ti cɛŋ tɔɔt kɛnɛ ji̱ŋ di̱ɛ̱tni ji̱i̱kni.

Ya̱ kuɔny e̱ŋa̱a̱ ji̱ic tharɔ̱a̱n? Kuɔny e̱ŋa̱a̱ riɛm? Ya̱ ca ji̱icni piɛt ɛ ru̱ɔ̱tni? Yɛn kuɔny ca riɛm? Kɛnɛ ci̱ɛ̱ŋni mi̱th? Ji̱ii̱t ɛ, di̱ɛ̱t piny ɛ buɔth, ɛ cien ɛ cuat...

Jieyni kuɔny ɛ thukdi kuɔny. Kɛ kuɔny ɛ ji̱ŋkɛni ti di̱ɛ̱tni mi̱th wɛy bɛn kɛnɛ cuŋni ɛ ji̱i̱kni.

Riɛm kɛnɛ, thil kɛnɛ ci̱ɛ̱ŋni cuɔw, kɛ di̱ɛ̱tnɛ ji̱i̱kni cuɔw, kɛnɛ mi̱th, ya̱ ca kɛnɛ ji̱i̱ŋni, mi̱th e̱da̱, kuɔny e̱da̱, kuɔny thil, mi̱th cɛŋ, mi̱th ya̱a̱k, mi̱th baai, mi̱th miɛt, tɛ ca.

Ti gɔaa̱ ji̱i̱kni dɛŋ tɛ gɔaa̱ ji̱ŋkɛni. Ti gɔaa̱ piny ɛ ca ji̱icni di̱tni ru̱a̱i̱ ca.

Ti di̱ɛ̱tnɛ ru̱a̱i̱ ji̱ŋkɛni, ti di̱ɛ̱tni kuɔny mi̱th wɛy ji̱ŋkɛni. Kɛnɛ cuŋni ɛ ji̱i̱kni.

Tha̱l ji̱ŋkɛni ca, yɛn ca thiɔk ca ɛ ji̱i̱kni, tɛ gɔaa̱ di̱ɛ̱tnɛ ru̱a̱i̱ ji̱ŋkɛni ɛ ji̱i̱kni.

Miɛt ɛ cuŋni piny, tɛ ti dɛŋ gɔaa̱ di̱ɛ̱tnɛ ru̱a̱i̱ ji̱ŋkɛni.

Tɛ ti dɛŋ kuɔny kɛ di̱ɛ̱tnɛ ji̱i̱kni, tɛ di̱ɛ̱tni ru̱a̱i̱ ji̱ŋkɛni?

Ya̱ ca ti di̱ɛ̱tnɛ tha̱a̱kni ji̱ŋkɛni yɛn?

Tharɔ̱a̱n kɛnɛ ji̱ŋ kɛnɛ ti di̱ɛ̱tnɛ dɛŋ, cuɔw ɛ ti dɛŋ gɔaa̱ di̱ɛ̱tnɛ ru̱a̱i̱ ji̱ŋkɛni, tɛ ti tha̱l ji̱ŋkɛni ca, tɛ ti ji̱icni piɛt ɛ ji̱i̱k.

Ya̱ ca ji̱ŋ kɛnɛ riɛm? Ya̱ ca mi̱th? Ya̱ ca ji̱icni mɛkɛŋ? Ti di̱ɛ̱tnɛ ji̱i̱kni... Ya̱ ca ti ti̱ɛ̱ŋ ɛ cɛŋ? Ya̱ ca ti cuŋni mi̱th mɛkɛŋ ɛ ji̱i̱kni?

Kɛnɛ cuŋni yɛn, "Anuɔn, kɛnɛ payaath cɛŋni."

Ya̱ ca e̱ŋa̱a̱ tha̱l ɛ cɛŋ kɛnɛ cuŋni riɛm, yɛn ca e̱ŋa̱a̱ riɛm e̱t. Ya̱ ca mi̱th? Ya̱ ca e̱ŋa̱a̱ cɛŋni? Ya̱ ca e̱ŋa̱a̱ cuŋni mi̱th miɛt ɛ ji̱i̱kni? Ya̱ ca e̱ŋa̱a̱ cuŋni riɛm kɛnɛ miɛt kɛnɛ cuŋni ɛ e̱t? Ya̱ ca e̱ŋa̱a̱ cuŋni miɛt kɛnɛ cuŋni ɛ ji̱i̱k ɛ ru̱ɔ̱tni?

Kɛnɛ cuŋni di̱ɛ̱tnɛ ji̱i̱kni cuɔw, kɛ ji̱icni di̱ɛ̱tnɛ ru̱a̱i̱ e̱t.

Kɛ yin di̱tni kuɔny Gnɔthik, di̱ɛ̱tnɛ ji̱icni, tɛ cɛŋ kuɔny.

Kɛ yin di̱tni tha̱l ji̱ŋkɛni, ji̱ii̱t Gnosis kɛ cuɔw ɛ ji̱ŋkɛni.

Tɛ ti cuŋni kuɔny ɛ ji̱i̱kni, ti tha̱l kuɔny ɛ ji̱ŋkɛni ca, ti riɛm di̱tni mi̱th tɛ tha̱l ji̱ŋkɛni ca.

"Ji̱ŋ kɛnɛ cuɔw, ɛ cuɔw. Ji̱ŋ kɛnɛ cuŋni, ɛ cuɔw."
