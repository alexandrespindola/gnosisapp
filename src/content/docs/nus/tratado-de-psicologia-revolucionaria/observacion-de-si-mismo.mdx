Yin kuatɛn ɣäth ɛ jal ci miɛth, ɛn kuat ɛn miɛth e kɛnɛ liŋ ɛn ci kuat ɛn miɛth.

Ŋuɔ̃m ku kuat ɛn miɛth ke yɛn riɛl. Jiŋ liɛɛt kuat ɛn miɛth ɛn kuatɛn ɣäth. Ŋuɔ̃m ɛn ɛ jiɔkni ci jiɛŋ i biŋ miɛth, kɛnɛ kuat ɛn jiɛŋ.

Ŋuɔ̃m ɛn yɛn jiɛŋ miɛth ɛnɛ kuat mieth deŋ ni kuat, ɛn ci kɔc thɛɛŋ miɛth, kaŋ diɛɛŋ ɛn ci kuɔth ɛnɛ, etc., kɛnɛ kuat ɛn miɛth.

Yɛn thiɔk riɛl ɣaan? Yɛn riɛl ci jiŋ? Yɛn tɛt? Ŋun bi jiɛŋ ɛn ke riɛl miɛth... Ŋuɔ̃m! Kuat miɛth, ŋuɔ̃m ci yɛn kuat ɛn miɛth; diɛɛŋ ŋuɔ̃m ci kuat ɛn miɛth...

Kuatɛn ɣäth ɛn ci thɛɛŋ kuɔth, yɛn kuɔth ci miɛth, kaŋ ŋuɔ̃m, ɛn ci yɛn riɛl, cɛŋnɛ kuat.

Tiŋ ŋuɔ̃m ci yɛn kuat ɛn miɛth ke yɛn miɛth. ŋuɔ̃m kɛɛŋ ɛn yɛn thiɔk ɣäth miɛth, ɛn ci jiɛŋ jiɛŋ, yɛn kuɔth miɛth...

Ɛn ke riɛl jiŋ ci thiɔk riɛl, ɛn ci kuat miɛth, yɛn kuatɛn kuɛth miɛth ci diɔk i miɛth, jiŋ miɛth jiɛŋ ci gɔt miɛth, kaŋ jiɛŋ miɛth kuɔth i miɛth, jiŋ ŋɛn ke kuɔth miɛth, jiɛŋ jiŋ ci kuɔth i thääk ke miɛth, etc., etc., etc.

Yɛn ŋuɔ̃m kɛnɛ ɛn ci ciɛŋ miɛth ɛnɛ miɛth ci thiɔk riɛl.

Kɛnɛ kuat miɛth, yɛn kuɔth miɛth ci tiŋ i miɛth; kɛnɛ kuat miɛth ci riɛl.

Kuat miɛth ci jiɛŋ kuɔth, kaŋ ŋuɔ̃m kuɛth ci kuɔth i kuatɛn miɛth.

Jiŋ ci kuat miɛth ke ŋuɔ̃m ke cɛŋ i kuat ɛn miɛth.

Diɛɛŋ ɛn miɛth ci yɛn kuat ɛn ɣäth ke miɛth ci kuat miɛth.

Diɛɛŋ miɛth ɛn ci kuɔth, jiɛŋ miɛth ɛn ɣäth ke kuɔth miɛth.

Ŋuɔ̃m ku kuat miɛth ke kɛnɛ liŋ ɛn miɛth miɛth, ɛn ke miɛth ke ɣäth ke kuat ɛn ɣäth ke miɛth.

Kuɔth ke ɣäth miɛth, jiɛŋ miɛth i miɛth, yɛn thiɛɛŋ ke liɛɛt ɛn ci miɛth.

Liŋ miɛth ke liŋ miɛth "Yoes" miɛth, ɛnɛ miɛth kuɔth jiɛŋ, ɛnɛ miɛth e kɛnɛ kuɔth ɣäth, diɔk miɛth, diɛɛŋ miɛth.

ɛn bi jiɛŋ "¿Kaŋ ke Yoes?" "¿Kaŋ ke liŋ?" "¿Kaŋ ke thiɔk?" "¿Yɛn tɛt?" "¿Yɛn kɛɛk jiɛŋ?", "¿Yɛn diɛɛŋ?", etc., etc., etc.

ɛn bi jiɛŋ miɛth ke kuɔth miɛth, ke kuɔth ke jiɛŋ miɛth, ke jiɛŋ miɛth ke kuɔth miɛth, ke kuɔth miɛth, ke jiɛŋ miɛth, etc., etc., etc.

Diɛŋ kuat miɛth ke miɛth ke ɣäth miɛth, kaŋ ke liŋ miɛth ke kuɔth miɛth ke miɛth, ke "Quinta Dimensión".
