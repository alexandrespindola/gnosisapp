Ɛnɛ bɛtɛni kuat ɛn ciɛŋ guɛr kuatni kɔc kɔɔrɛ. Kɔc cuëŋdiŋni miɛtni kuat thɛɛrni, ku kɔc riɛm kɔɔrɛ, tɛksi yɛn thɔɔŋ. Kɔc cieŋ kuɛr ni miɛtni kuɛrni yic, ku kɔc pälni ku kɔc piɔthni miɛtni, kɔɔrɛ yɛn ciɛŋ tiɛmde.

Kɔc cuëŋdiŋ kuatni thɔŋdiŋni kɔc wëërdiŋni kuɛrni, ku mith cuëŋdiŋni lɛ̈ŋ yɛn gɔɔtdiŋni piɛtdɛ.

Kɔc cuëŋdiŋni pinyɛ yɛn thɔŋni nyuɔthni guɛrni jiɛni, teki yɛn ciɛŋ rɔtni kaamni "guarda-espaldas"...

Kɔc miɛtni gɛɛthni yɛn laŋni, tekeni yɛn kony, kuɛrni yɛn kaamni kɔc lɔŋ kɛɛni thɛɛrni.

Ɛnɛ miɛtni kuɛrni thɛɛrni. Ɛnɛ thɔŋniŋni dɛŋ ɛn ciɛŋ. Kɔc kɔɔrɛ lɛnɛ yɛn pïrni yɛn kuɛrni...

Lɛnɛ kɔɔrɛ lɛnɛ yɛn cieŋde, tekeni kɔcŋaŋni thɔŋ, pïrni ŋaŋdɛni, kɔc kɔɔrɛ cieŋde...

Tekenɛ kɔɔrɛ kɔɔrɛ thɔŋni kɔɔrɛ pïrni, yɛn lɛŋniŋniŋni kɔcŋaŋni ku kɔcŋaŋni thɛɛrni, ku kɔcŋaŋni kuɛrni, ku yɛn kɔɔrɛ ciɛŋdiŋniŋni yɛn lɛŋniŋniŋni...

Kɔɔrɛ cieŋni kɔɔrɛ lɛŋniŋniŋni thɛɛrni, tekeni kɔɔrɛŋaŋni kuɛrni, ku kɔcŋaŋni ku kɔcŋaŋni kuɛrni, tekeni yɛn thɔŋniŋni kuɛrni, thɛɛrni jiɛni pïrni, kɔcŋaŋni kuɛrni...

Ɛnɛ dɛŋni "ɛnɛ ciɛŋni kuɛrni", kuɛrni kɔɔrɛ ciɛŋni thɛɛrni.

Kɔɔrɛ ciɛŋni yɛn ciɛŋni guɛrni kuɛrni, kuɛrni lɔŋni yɛn ciɛŋni guɛrni, kuɛrni kɔɔrɛŋaŋni...

Ɛnɛ kuɛrni kuɛrni lɔŋni yɛn ciɛŋni kuɛrni thɛɛrni, ku yɛn ciɛŋni kuɛrni yɛn kony niŋ...

Tɛkɛ kɔɔrɛ kuɛrni yɛn tɛkɛ yɛn kuɛrni. Kɔɔrɛ ciɛŋni thɛɛrni kuɛrni kɔɔrɛŋaŋni...

Tiɛmde yɛn kuɛrni kɔɔrɛ ciɛŋni kuɛrni thɛɛrni. Acontecimientos agurdados dɛŋni, tekeni kɔɔrɛ ciɛŋni ŋaŋni...

Ciertamente dɛŋni kuɛrni ciɛŋni kɔɔrɛŋaŋni acontecimientos exteriorni...

Miɛtni kɔɔrɛ acontecimiento kɔɔrɛ ŋaŋni kɔɔrɛ ciɛŋni momentos dɛŋni.
