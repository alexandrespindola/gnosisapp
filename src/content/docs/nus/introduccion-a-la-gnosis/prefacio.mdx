**Acuɔrda jiic kɛɛrɛ, ɛn doktrinɛ kɛ kɔc ë jɔk, acuɔrda ɛn deet kɛɛr, ku deet kɛnɛŋ, acuɔrda gɔap kɛnɛŋ ku acuɔrda rɛŋ kɛnɛŋ. Deet gɔap kɛnɛŋ lɔc kɔc ku tiit kɔc ë piŋ. Deet rɛŋ kɛnɛŋ ku kɔc yïn rɛŋ kɛnɛŋ tɔ ɛ jiɛɛŋ Nhialic, nɔŋ ŋuɔt, kɔc deet acaŋ rɛŋ.

Dhɔɔŋ jiic kɛɛr thɔŋ kɔc piŋ deet kɛɛr, ku dhɔɔŋ dhɔŋ dhiɛl kɔc piŋ deet bɛɛn, dhɔɔŋ dhɔŋ dhɔk ee: rɔt nhiaar, rɔt thɔŋ, rɔt wɛɛc, yaam bɛɛn, cuai, kuɔth kuɔth ku rɔt laau thɔŋ. Guop ee: pineal, hipófisis (glandulas in the brain), tiroides (la manzana del cuello), jɔk ku plexo solar o epigastrio (arriba del ombligo); yïn kɔc piŋ dhɔɔŋ kɔc (7) guop ë kɔc: Físico, vital, astral, mental, jɔk acuɔrda dhɔɔŋ guop ë bï käŋ dɔɔk, jɔk akuɔrdä lunares protoplasmáticos ku dhɔɔŋ kɛɛr jɔk ee guop ë miɛt, ë yiɛn ku ë thar, kɔc thiɔk deet kɛnɛŋ, deet acaŋ rɛŋ, deet acaŋ rɛŋ tɔ ɛ cuaiŋ kuɔth, kɔc yiɛn kuɔth cuaiŋ, kuɔth kuɔth kɔc lɛɛu jɔk ee yiɛn.

Ciɛɛŋ lɔc jiic cuaiŋ jiic kɔc piŋ deet. Jiic cuaiŋ lɔc yiɛn kɔc rɔt dɔɔk käŋ, yïn kɔc lɔc miɛt, kuɛth jiic kɔc kɔc lɔc miɛt, yïn kɔc bribones, kuɛth jiic kɔc kuɛth lɔc miɛt.

Ciɛɛŋ kɔc ŋɛɛn, tɔ ɛ bɛɛn miɛt kɔc piŋ kɔc piŋ cuaiŋ jiic kɔc dhɔɔŋ. Kɔc yïn ɛɛn Gnóstica kɔc lɔc miɛt piŋ kuɔth ŋuɔt miɛt tɔ caŋ kuɔth miɛt miɛt.

JULIO MEDINA V.
