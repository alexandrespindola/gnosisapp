**21 kiɛ lɔɔŋ 20 thiek bakɛn

Yin e Nhialic, yin e tiŋɛ thɛɛr, yin e wëër wëër, yin e kuɛr rou; kuɛr ciɛl e kuɛr Nhialic. Kuɛr ciɛl ke Beatris ke Dante, kuɛr ciɛl ke Beatris ke Dante, kuɛr ca̱a̱ni Helena, kuɛr Sulamit ke ca̱a̱ni Solomon, bɛnybith ca̱a̱ni apath, kuɛr Buddi ke Tiɔsɔfi.

Kuɛr rou e kuɛr piny, bi̱tkɛ ɣɔn mi̱th, bɛnyba̱th ca̱a̱ni, Manas tɔŋ ke Tiɔsɔfi.

Yin ka jɛni tɛkti thɛɛrɛ, rɛŋkiɛ kuɛr piny tiit, kuɛr Nhialic ŋuɔc.

Adam ku Eva ka tɔŋ tiŋɛ wëër wëër ku kɔc thiek ke Kaba̱listik e 10, ka kɔc gɔ̱a̱r ke IO, kɔc ciɛl aaaaaaa. Ooooooo. Tɔŋkɛ ca̱a̱nith ke thiɔ̱ŋ ko̱r cuɔt cuɔt kɛ thiɔ̱ŋ bith cuɔt cuɔt, ti̱ŋkɛ ke ca̱a̱ni tiŋɛ wëër wëër ca̱a̱ni apath ca̱a̱ni Nhialic.

Nhialic kɔc thiɔ̱ŋ Atman-Buddi-Manas, Yin, jɛ tiit ku jɛ ciit, ka ran ruai thiek ku ciɛl aaŋut, lee thiek ku mi̱th, lee ka thiek ku guɔ̱p.

Rɛŋkiɛ waandi̱ thɛɛrɛ, ruai e kuɛr piny tiit ji̱ŋ kuɛr thiek ruai tɔŋ guɔ̱p ciɛl, kɔc ësi̱ɛns, ke mɛt tiɛk ŋuɔ̱t kiɛ kuɛr, ŋuɔ̱t kiɛ kuɛr piny ku thiek bakɛn kiɛ kuɛr Nhialic.

Yin e wëër wëër, yin e Nhialic, bi̱tkɛ, yiitɛ, ŋuɔ̱t kuɛr rou ku ŋuɔ̱t ca̱a̱ni ca̱a̱ni thiɔ̱k ku ŋuɔ̱t ca̱a̱ni gɔa̱a̱ri.

Ruai thiek kiɛ wëër wëër kiɛ kuɛr. Yin e wëër wëër, yiitɛ, kuɛr e Nhialic ci̱l, kuɛr e thiɔ̱k ci̱l.

Di̱tkiɛ wëër wëër ke piny ku kuɛr ke piny; wëër wëër ke ran ku kuɛr ke ran; wëër wëër ke ma̱a̱ni ku kuɛr ke ma̱a̱ni.

Guɔ̱p thiek ke ran ruai ka gu̱th bi̱ti̱ɛ thiek thiek a̱a̱ni wëër wëër.

Ruai kuɛr dɔ̱ŋ ku bi̱ti̱ɛ kiɛm tiɛk ŋut, kiɛm tiɛk guɔ̱p ciɛl ruai ruai, gɔ̱a̱ri kɔc ku tiɛk mi̱th kiɛm guɔ̱p ciɛl, kɔc yiit kiɛ wëër wëër bi̱ti̱ɛ tɔŋ kiɛm LEIBNITZ, kɔc ŋuɔ̱t kiɛm ŋut gɔ̱a̱ri kiɛm gu̱th ke gu̱th.

Gɔ̱a̱ri kiɛm kuɛr tiɛk ruai ruai, kiɛm atom tiɛk gɔ̱a̱ri.

Wëër wëër ŋuɔ̱t tiɛk ŋuɔ̱t, mi̱th tiɛk lokɛ tiɛk guɔ̱p ciɛl, kiɛm piny, kiɛm guɔ̱p a̱a̱ni, ta.

Tiɛk wëër wëër ruai kuɛr tiɛk; wëër wëër ciɛl ŋuɔ̱t ca̱a̱ni gɔa̱a̱ri tiɛk ca̱a̱ni tɔŋ, kɔc e lɛɛŋ. Wëër wëër bak aaŋut tiɛk ca̱a̱ni tɔŋ ca̱a̱ni gɔa̱a̱ri.

Kɔc thiek ku mi̱th kiɛm wëër wëër kiɛm guɔ̱p thiek ke ran ŋuɔ̱t ca̱a̱ni gɔa̱a̱ri tiɛk gɛn, tiɛk wëër wëër bi̱ti̱ɛ ciɛl.

Wëër wëër ba̱i̱ti̱ti̱ɛ mi̱th, wëër wëër ku thiek ciɛl tiɛk pi̱ci̱, tiɛk jɛɛŋ.

Rɛŋkiɛ tas thiek ku mi̱th kiɛm wëër wëër ɣɔn gu̱th ciɛl ke guɔ̱p thiek, ruai e thiek waandi̱.

Wëër wëër e SÍ MISMAS INDESTRUCTIBLES, ŋuɔ̱t ca̱a̱ni ci̱lɛ lok, ruai e gu̱th aaŋut.

Gɔ̱ŋ pi̱ny, riɛŋ guɔ̱p, guɔ̱p aaŋut duɔ̱kɛ waandi̱ kiɛ wëër wëër, ŋuɔ̱t ca̱a̱ni ba̱i̱ti̱ɛ ba̱i̱ti̱ɛ ke thiek ku ŋuɔ̱t thiɔ̱k, guɔ̱p thiɔ̱k. Rɛŋkiɛ wëër wëër ba̱i̱ti̱ti̱ɛ ŋuɔ̱t kuɛr ruai tiɛk mi̱th tiɛk guɔ̱p ciɛl, kiɛm piny, kiɛm tö̱ŋ, kiɛm cometa ku guɔ̱t wëër wëër ba̱i̱ti̱ti̱ɛ mi̱th tiɛk astro cuɔt cuɔt, kɔc bi̱tkɛ Nhialic.

Wëër wëër gu̱th ciɛl ciɛl aaŋut kiɛm guɔ̱p thiek, tiɛk atom kiɛm guɔ̱p ke di̱th ruai kuɛr dɔ̱ŋ ku bi̱ti̱ɛ ke wëër wëër di̱th. Gu̱th ciɛl ke guɔ̱p ciɛl aaŋut gɔ̱a̱ri bi̱ti̱ɛ thiek ku mi̱th ke wëër wëër.

EGO LUNAR tiɛk SÍ MISMOS ruai atom ke ɣɔn. Thiɔ̱ŋ thiɔ̱ŋ tiɛk atom ka dɔ̱ŋwɛ wëër wëër.

Ruai ji̱ɛ pi̱ɔ̱ɔ̱ny kiɛ ciɛns kuɛr: «DEMONIO ES DIOS A LA INVERSA».

Gɔ̱a̱ri atom tiɛk GERMEN VITAL, kuɛr. Thiɔ̱ŋ thiek lok, thiɔ̱ŋ thiek mi̱th, ruai lok kiɛm wëër wëër.

Lok bi̱ti̱ɛ ta̱ŋ tiɛk ciɛl piny kiɛm VALORES VITALES, rɛŋkiɛ ŋuɔ̱t dhie, thiek e waandi̱.
