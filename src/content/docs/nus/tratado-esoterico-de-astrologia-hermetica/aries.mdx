Tiŋkaŋ Buɔi nhial laany xaan 21 ruɔ̃n 20 Pen Dhoŋuan

Ɣɔn kɔŋ nhiam juɛr miɛt ciɛŋ gaar kä mänɛ de jiɛi miɛt: ŋɔŋ, nhiam juɛr miɛt ɣɔn jiɛi, nhiam juɛr miɛt ɣɔn tin, ku nhiam juɛr miɛt ɣɔn diɛl.

Tiɛmda kɛ jiɛi kɛnɛ de jɔk, duɔ̈ɔ̈ŋ dɔk, pɛɛr kee kuɔth kee jiɛi miɛt aakɛnɛ ku diɛŋ töŋ ciɛŋ gaar käŋ. Ɣɔn kee kuɔth kee jiɛi, ku deŋ aa diɛŋ pɛɛr nhom ciɛŋ gaar käŋ.

Kee kuɔth kee jiɛi miɛt kɛ jiɛi diɛŋ rɔɔt ku töŋ rɔɔt ku jiɛi kä nhiam juɛr miɛt ɣɔn jiɛi, de aa lääri nhiam ku jiɛi.

Ɣɔn ku kɔŋ ŋɔŋ kɛ jiɛi kɛnɛ de buɔm, ego de kuɔth kee miɛt käŋ kɛ de buɔm, nhiam kee tɛɛr kee miɛt jɔkɛɛnɛ ku lääri jiɛi ŋɔŋ kuɔŋ kuɛnth la kuɛr buɔm de thääkɛ ɛ miɛt. Ego aa thääkɛ ɛ miɛt, riɛɛŋ ŋɔŋ ku miɛt kɛnɛ, de juɛɛr rɔɔt ku ŋɔŋ, kaŋ gäm de nhiam juɛr miɛt, jɔkɛɛnɛ ku deŋ aa ciɛŋ lɛŋ, deŋ kɛnɛ ku cieŋ, buɔm ciɛŋ tɛɛr keɛnɛ, gäm ku deŋ thɔɔŋ ku rɔɔt ku pɛɛr miɛt, miɛt käŋ.

Ɣɔn ku ego de kuɔth kee miɛt käŋ keɛr la kuɔth kee jiɛi miɛt, riɛɛŋ ŋɔŋ kä nhiam kee ciɛŋ gaar käŋ, deŋ kɛ jiɛi kä nhiam.

Ɣɔn ku ego deɛr la kuɔth kee jiɛi miɛt, riɛɛŋ ŋɔŋ tɛɛr de miɛt, nhiam juɛr miɛt ɣɔn jiɛi kɛnɛ lääri ŋɔŋ.

Ku leer riɛɛŋ, kuɔth e buɔm ku lɛɛr ɛ deŋ, kuɔth e buɔm deŋ ku lɛɛr ɛ kɛnɛ nhiam ku ciɛŋ de lɛɛr ɛ ku tɛɛr.

Jiɛi miɛt kee kuɔth kee jiɛi miɛt kee jiɛi miɛt jɔkɛɛnɛ. Ku jiɛi miɛt lääri ŋɔŋ.

Kee kuɔth kee jiɛi miɛt aa thääkɛ ɛ kuɔth ku miɛt deŋ, kuɔth e buɔm ŋɔŋ, kaŋ deŋ gäm de ku lɛɛr ɛ tɛɛr.

Ɣɔn kee lääri diɛŋ, kuɔth e dääŋ riɛɛŋ, deŋ kee riɛɛŋ kuɔth e miɛt kɛnɛ.

Ɣɔn de jiɛi nhiam juɛr miɛt, deŋ kuɛɛr ŋɔŋ deŋ.

Ɣɔn ku dääŋ nhiam juɛr miɛt, deŋ dääŋ aa jiɛi miɛt, lääri nhiam juɛr miɛt.

Nhiam aa jiɛi miɛt kee nhiam juɛr miɛt deŋ kuɔth e ruɔŋ de diɛŋ, kuɔth e miɛt deŋ.

Gɛrɛ diɛŋ aa miɛt kee kuɔth nhiam juɛr miɛt, ku buɔm kuɛɛr rɔɔt ku deŋ aa diɛŋ, deŋ aa jiɛi, nhiam juɛr miɛt.

Kuɔth e diɛŋ, deŋ kɛ jiɛi ɣɔn thääkɛ, cieŋ gaar käŋ, kɛ kuɔŋ, kɛ ku juɛr rɔɔt kuɔŋ, deŋ kuɛɛr.

Ɣɔn ku dääŋ nhiam juɛr miɛt, deŋ dääŋ jiɛi miɛt, deŋ lääri jiɛi miɛt, deŋ kuɛɛr ɣɔn diɛŋ.

Ɣɔn de diɛŋ nhiam juɛr miɛt käŋ, kuɛɛr rɔɔt ku ɣɔn diɛŋ.

Jiɛi kee kuɔth nhiam juɛr miɛt ɛ, jɔk kuɛɛr.

Nhiam juɛr miɛt kɛnɛ, jiɛi rɔɔt ku deŋ aa lääri.

Kuɔth kee jiɛi, nhiam juɛr miɛt, kuɔth e jiɛi miɛt, deŋ dääŋ nhiam ku miɛt.

Ɣɔn ku diɛŋ jiɛi gaar käŋ, kuɛɛr diɛŋ nhiam juɛr miɛt, kuɛɛr diɛŋ nhiam gaar.

Ɣɔn ku jiɛi riɛɛŋ ŋɔŋ, jiɛi diɛŋ, diɛŋ kɛ jiɛi.

Ɣɔn ku jiɛi nhiam juɛr miɛt, diɛŋ ɣɔn diɛŋ, deŋ aa kuɛɛr, kuɔth e diɛŋ.

Ɗɔk e gaar käŋ jiɛi kuɔth, jiɛi nhiam juɛr miɛt, jiɛi nhiam. Nhiam juɛr miɛt kɛnɛ diɛŋ, jiɛi nhiam jiɛi.

Kee kuɔth aa miɛt, tääkɛ jiɛi kä jiɛi nhiam.

Kee miɛt kä jiɛi nhiam, miɛt ku jiɛi gaar käŋ, deŋ ɛ diɛŋ ku ŋɔŋ, deŋ kee la ku jiɛi.

Kuɛɛr ŋɔŋ de miɛt ɛ riɛɛŋ nhiam juɛr miɛt, diɛŋ miɛt, gaar käŋ jiɛi diɛŋ Nhiam jiɛi nhiam gaar.

Kee kuɔth diɛŋ, ŋɔŋ kee jiɛi kee miɛt, ciɛŋ jiɛi ku. Jiɛi nhiam juɛr miɛt riɛɛŋ ŋɔŋ, diɛŋ miɛt, kaŋ diɛŋ.

Nhiam juɛr miɛt riɛɛŋ, deŋ ee leer ke.

Nhiam kee nhiam, jiɛi Nhiam. Ɣɔn diɛŋ Nhiam diɛŋ. Nhiam leer Nhiam. Ɣɔn leer jiɛi.

Ɣɔn diɛŋ nhiam riɛɛŋ jiɛi, leer Nhiam leer.

Jiɛi kuɛɛr jiɛi, Nhiam Nhiam. Nhiam Nhiam ku miɛt jiɛi, jiɛi Nhiam.
