## 23 Ruɛnydɛkni aci 22 Duɔɔŋ

Jethu Ruɛnydit e Jiɛɛm a Kritho aa Nicodemuth ɛ: "Tiit, tiit ke jɛ, ke yin ka ca tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ dhɔk, yin ka bi cɔl Raan Lɔŋ Nhial."

Eeŋu bak aa tiɛŋ piɔu aci ruɔk cɔl Raan Lɔŋ e Nhial e lɛɛŋ, aci Maath Rɛɛŋ.

Eeŋu bak aa tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ dhɔk kɔŋ e nhiaar ke yin tɔŋ e lɛɛŋ. Eeŋu bak aa tiɛŋ ruɔk ni yɛn ke tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ dhɔk ŋuan.

Thɔɔŋ e tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ ruɔk ka rɔɔt e Nicodemuth ku ka rɔɔt e thɛkta e Baibul piny. Eeŋu bak aa thɔɔŋ e kuat ke Diin, eeŋu bak aa thɔɔŋ e dɛɛŋ aa Arkaan A.Z.F, ke yin bi cɔl aa rɔɔt e thɔɔŋ e jiɛɛm e Jethu aa Nicodemuth.

Thɛkta kuat ke Baibul ciɛɛŋ ruɔɔny ke yɛn thɔɔŋ e dɛɛŋ ke thɔɔŋ e tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ dhɔk ku ciɛɛŋ gɔɔt athɛɛk kuɛth, ku ciɛɛŋ lɛɛŋ kuɛth aci kuɛth, ku ciɛɛŋ lueel kuɛth aci kuɛth kuɛth, kuɛth, eeŋu bak aa ka thɔɔŋ ke yin ka rɔɔt e dɛɛŋ ee thaat, ee Arkaan A.Z.F.

Nicodemuth ee ran dit, ee thɔɔŋ e thɔɔŋ e thɛɛr, ku eeŋu bak aa ka thɔɔŋ ku aa: "Eeŋu ke ran bi tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ ke ruɛny?, Bi ka thiɛŋ e guɛr ɛnɛ, ke tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ?".

Jethu, ee Jiɛɛm Dit, aa Nicodemuth aa jiɛɛm e Maai: "Tiit, tiit ke jɛ, ke yin ka tiɛŋ piɔu aci ruɔk, yin ka bi tɔŋ e lɛɛŋ e Nhial".

Eeŋu bak aa ran ka thɔɔŋ e lɛɛŋ, ke yin ka thɔɔŋ e lɛɛŋ e Baibul ɣän, ke yin ka yaa rɔɔt e dɛɛŋ ee Arkaan A. Z. F., ciɛɛŋ gɔɔt jiɛɛm e Jiɛɛm Dit, aci aa rɔɔt, aci aai, aci aa yin bi cɔl batith e thɛkt e ke yin bi keek, ku aa bi cɔl thɔɔŋ e tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ dhɔk.

Bith e Maai, Ruɔk ee Miir Muɔny ku ciɛɛŋ lueel: "Eeŋu bak aa ka yaa tiɛŋ yic aci thiaŋ yic, aci piɔu aci Miir".

Baramaan Ithotanii ciɛɛŋ lɛɛŋ aa tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ ruɔk e thɛkthɛl. E Liturthi ciɛɛŋ ruɔk ee Thil Gɔɔl dit ku ran ca thiɛŋ e tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ ruɔk eeŋu bak aa thiɛŋ e thɛɛk thiɛŋ e riɛɛw yic e thil, ku cɔl ŋään ku aa ciɛɛŋ cɔl Baraamaan, Dwiip, cɔl tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ ŋuan, ke ɛnɛ aci ke thil.

Thin aa lɛɛŋ e Baraamaan ciɛɛŋ lueel ɛ tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ ruɔk ca lueel Jethu aa Nicodemuth.

Thil ca lueel ca lɛɛŋ kuɛth ca ca lɛɛŋ ke, ciɛɛŋ lɛɛŋ ɛ ɛnɛ, cɔl Maa Thin, ku thɔɔŋ dit ee ke Baraamaan cɔl yɛn ke tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ ŋuan ku aa tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ ruɔk ee thɛkthɛl, ca tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ e thil ku ca cɔl ŋään e guɛɛr ɛnɛ.

Thɔɔŋ ke rɔɔt ku Rɛɛth Luun ciɛɛŋ lueel akɔl, ciɛɛŋ thiɛp thil ku baŋ kuɛth aa ran ciɛɛŋ lueel e Mithiɛrth e Thɛkthɛl aci Arkaan A. Z. F.

Baraamaan ka tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ ŋuan, ku lɛɛŋ kɔlth ee. Maath Mathon ka Maath e tiit, ku lɛɛŋ kɔlth ee.

Thɔɔŋ dit ee bak aa tɔŋ e tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ ruɔk ku kuɛth ee thɛkthɛl yic yic.

Ran ruɔɔny aa tɔŋ e piny e Kɔɔth Dhiimiɛnthan, e lɛɛŋ, thok aci tɛɛm e Jiinath, e lɛɛŋ ke tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ ŋuan, eeŋu bak aa tiɛŋ e Thiɛl Buɔt ku tiɛŋ tɛŋ, ke lɛɛŋ e Mathon.

Eeŋu bakaa thiaŋ Thiɛl Thil ca ca lɔɔr e piny ke thok.

Piɔth ee, Jiɛɛm e Jethu Kritho, ee Alathiin, ran gɔɔt ca bɛɛth ɛ alɔɔr e Thiɛl ciɛɛŋ thɛɛk e Thaantuar e Mithiɛrth Dhita.

Thɔɔŋ akɔɔr e Piɔth ee Pathaar aci Jiɛɛm, P. T. R., aci rɔt e jiɛɛm.

P. ee yaa cɔɔl enɛ aa Paath ca thɛɛk, aa Paath e Nhiaalth, aa Paath aci Pitrith.

T. ee Thu, ran ku raan thɛkthɛl yaa thiɛŋ e kuɛth.

R. jiɛɛm ke rɔɔt e INRI, ee miir kɔl aci thɔɔŋ Nhial, ee Ra Ithipthiɛ.

Piɔth, Pathaar, ee Thɔɔŋ, ee Maath e Maathi Thɛkthɛl, ee Maath ruɔɔny e kɔɔl enɛ thiɛŋ e Thɔɔŋ e kuɛth.

Thil Diin Minotuar Krith, ee bak aa tiɛŋ thiɛŋ e Piny e Tiɛŋ Jɛɛŋ ŋuan.

Thiɛl Thiilothoofal e Athiɛl Alkiimthth mɛɛt ee Thɛkthɛl ku tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ ruɔk ee Thɛkthɛl.

Capthaar VIII e Let e Maanu, aa: "Rɛɛŋ cuɔŋ kuɛth ee Thuthrath, cuɔŋ ke ran thiɛw aci kuɛth ku ka ci tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ ŋuan, bi thiɛp kuɛth e tɛɛŋ, tɛɛŋ aci caath".

Thiɛl Dhoothrin e Piɔth tɔŋ ee ɣän e tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ ruɔk. Yɛn aa Noothikth ciɛɛŋ thɔɔŋ e Dhoothrin e Piɔth.

Infrathekthɛlth, ee Thiɛw ciɛŋ lueel aakɔl e Dhoothrin e Piɔth.

Cuɔŋ ee yɛn ciɛŋ ruɔɔny ke yɛn thiɛŋ ee AUTO-REALIZARSE kuɛth aa Thɛkthɛl.

Cuɔŋ ee yɛn ciɛŋ lueel akɔl e Thɛkthɛl, ee yɛn ciɛŋ thiɛp Thɛkthɛl, aa yɛn ciɛŋ muɔth e Thaantuar Thiɔɔw e Thirta Loot.

Tɔn ciɛŋ lueel akɔl e Thɛkthɛl, ee yɛn ciɛŋ lueel ke Thɛkthɛl ee tuɔɔŋ, yɛn ka yaa tɔŋ e tiɛŋ jɛɛŋ ruɔk.

Thɔɔŋ e Maathi Thɛkthɛl ee Thaanthkrith ee Maithuuna. Dhoothrin e Piɔth ee Maithuuna ku Jethu aa: "Yin ee Piɔth, Thiɛl ku e Thiɛl ke ruɔɔny aa ithithia ku guɛɛr e thiɛw bi jɔɔk ka lɛɛŋ ke".

Lɛɛŋ e Maithuuna ee Liingaŋ Miɔth cuɔŋ e Yooni, lɛɛŋ e Nhial Thiva, e Thirta Loot, ee Ruɔk Thiɔɔw.

E Maithuuna Phalo ee thiɛŋ e Vaajiina, ku ka bi cɔl bi thɔɔth ee themɛn.

Cɔɔŋ ee yaa thiɛŋ ŋään e kuɛth ku ka dɔɔk e then.

Nhiaar ca thiɛŋ bi thaat e then ɛ enɛ ciɛŋ ciɛŋ themɛn e Miir.

Miir Thɛkthɛl bi tɔŋ e buɔth. Kɛn ee kuɛth ke buɔth bi ciɛŋ themɛn, kɛn ee kuɛth ke themɛn ciɛŋ buɔth.

Maithuuna ee bak ee yɛn ba kuɛth yɛŋ ee jiɛɛm.

Thɛɛr ke kuɛth, tiɛŋ e thɔɔŋ e tuɔɔŋ riɛɛw dhɔk.

Kuɛth ee ithithia apocaliththi e Thaŋ Jiin. Ithithia ee cuɔŋ e tuɔɔŋ riɛɛw dhɔk.

Ithithia ke tiɛŋ ee Ephetho ku cuɔŋ e kuɛth kuɛth. Kuɛth e Ithithia e Ephetho tiɛŋ ke Thɛr kɔl thiɛŋ thiɛŋ aci dɔɔl thiɛŋ kueth.

Ithithia ruɔk ee Ethmithna, cuɔŋ e kuɛth e pirithtaat ku cɔl ee yɛŋ ee ciɛŋ ee piɔu.

Ithithia thɔɔk ee Piɛgaam, cuɔŋ e kuɛth e biil ku cɔl ee yɛŋ ee ciɛŋ ee miir.

Ithithia thɔɔk ee Thiathiira, cuɔŋ e kuɛth e thɔɔl ku cɔl ee yɛŋ ee ciɛŋ ee yaaŋ ku cuɔŋ ee, tiɛŋ ke Jiinath, tiɛŋ.

Ithithia nguɛth ee Thaarith, cuɔŋ e kuɛth e lariinge ku cɔl ee yɛŋ ee ciɛŋ ee thaat.

Ithithia thok ee Filadelfia ku cuɔŋ e kuɛth ku cɔl ee yɛŋ ee ciɛŋ ee ciɛŋ.

Ithithia thiɛp ee Laothithe. Ithithia cɔl ee Loot e thiɛŋ kuɛth.

Laothithe cɔl ee yɛŋ ee ciɛŋ ee Thɔɔŋ, miir ke Kuɛth.

Miir ee Thɛr bi yɛɛn.

Miir ee Thɛkthɛl bi yɛɛn thɔɔŋ e Kuɛth.

Muɔny ciɛŋ kuɛth, ciɛŋ ke TRIVENI thiɛŋ kuɛth.

Miir ciɛŋ kuɛth, cuɛŋ ee thiɛŋ.

Thɛkthɛl ee bak.
