Ɣɛn ciɛŋ ëë kuat kuat ɛ roŋ kuat ɛn colɛɛr, ka kɔc ŋuɛny ɛn wëu kɛ dɔkɔcɛn kekɛ.

Ɣɛn jiɛɛm ŋuɛny kɛ dɔkɔcɛn wëu kɛ käŋ päl, kɛ kuat bäny kɛ kuat nyal kɛ jɛɛm kɛnë ke jiɛɛm.

Kɛ ruɔ̱n dhiëc kuɔth, ee kuat bäny looi kɛ riɛl ŋuɛny ee wëu kɛ riɛl cɔl neurotransmitters kɛ riɛl kɛ thɛɛk ciɛŋ.

Riel kɛ thɛɛk miɛth looi kɛ kuat bäny, ka miɛth kuat bäny, ka miɛth tɔŋ kɛ kuat bäny, ka looi kɛ kuat nyal ka miɛth dhiëth kɛ kuat nyal.

käŋ kɛnë yɛn deŋ kɛ biɛt caat, ŋuɛny thɛɛk kɛ looi ɛn colɛɛr kɛnë yɛn looi looi, ruɔth kɛnë yɛn looi ëë käŋ gɔaa, käŋ kɛnë ya paracelsus kuɛr ɛn colɛɛr kɛnë Ens-Seminis (käŋ ee tëëŋɛ).
