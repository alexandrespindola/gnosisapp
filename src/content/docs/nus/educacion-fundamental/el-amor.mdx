Ciɛŋ kɔŋ diɛŋ lɛ̈th thukul eeyïn, kɔc ca luɔ̈kni kɔŋ käŋ deŋ alɛ̈th ɛ gɔ̈t ɛ jiɛɛm.

Riɛŋ kɔŋ lɔc aŋoot käŋ deŋ alɛ̈th ɛ gɔ̈t alɛ̈th tɛ bi jiɛɛm.

Kɔc ca luɔ̈kni aŋoot kɔc gɔ̈tni kɔŋ kɔc lɛ̈thni, ɛn ciɛŋ keek eeyïn keek jiɛɛm kɔŋ riɛŋ keek.

Nhiaar kɔŋ nyala, nhiam kɔŋ nyala, ciɛŋ kɔc, luɔ̈kni ɛŋuun thookni dhɔ̈l, dhiëthni, maacni, luakni, etc. ɛn kuɛn ŋoot keek eeyïn kuɛn keek, keek aŋoot kɔc gɔ̈tni, kɔc lɛ̈thni, tɛ keek jiɛɛm keek, tɛnɛɛn keek riɛŋ keek, tɛnɛɛn ciɛŋ alɛ̈th.

Tɛ dɛŋ piɔth ɛŋuun eeyïn kuɛn, kɔc ca luɔ̈kni, nhiar kɔŋ nyala, nhiam kɔŋ nyala, keek kɔŋ thukul keek jiɛɛm keek, tɛ keek cuɛŋ keek gɔ̈tni, kɔc lɛ̈thni.

Tɛnɛɛn nhiar kɔŋ nyala, nhiam kɔŋ nyala, ciɛŋ kɔc ca lɛ̈thni keek jiɛɛm keek, kuɛn eeyïn keek cuɛŋ keek gɔ̈tni, kɔc lɛ̈thni.

En kuɛn ɛŋuun eeyïn keek ciɛŋ alɛ̈th kɔŋ kɔc ca lɛ̈thni kuɛn keek gɔ̈tni, kɔc lɛ̈thni, ɛn ciɛŋ alɛ̈th ɛ jiɛɛm.

En kuɛn ɛ jiɛɛm keŋ riɛŋ kɔŋ deŋ alɛ̈th ɛ jiɛɛm riɛŋ, lɔc, aŋoot, dhiëth.

En kuɛn eeyïn jiɛɛm keŋ riɛŋ kɔŋ kɔc gɔ̈tni, kɔc lɛ̈thni, tɛnɛɛn aci jiɛɛm riɛŋ kɔŋ deŋ alɛ̈th.

Riɛŋ kɔŋ alɛ̈th en kuɛn ɛn, tɛnɛɛn aci jiɛɛm riɛŋ kɔŋ deŋ.

Kɔc gɔ̈tni riɛŋ kɔŋ luɔ̈kni keek kuɛn keek eeyïn keek, dhiëth kuɛn keek, dhɔ̈l, lääth, etc. ɛn keek aŋoot riɛŋ deŋ alɛ̈th.

En kuɛn ɛŋuun eeyïn jiɛɛm keŋ alɛ̈th ciɛŋ kɔc gɔ̈tni, kɔc lɛ̈thni bi dhöl kɔc kɔŋ kämpiny bi keek piɛŋ, tɛnɛɛn keek lɛ̈th.

Dhöl kɔŋ dɛŋ miɔ̈th acin keek kuɛn ŋoot dhɔ̈l.

Wɛŋ eeyïn yïn piny yoot ɛŋuun eeyïn "Kɔc cakni ca lääth" en eeyïn miɛth ɛŋuun aci piny yoot dɛŋ. Kɔc kɛnɛ ciɛŋ luɔ̈kni, keek amääthni keek piŋ, keek kɔŋ diɛŋ keek, keek a gäm kɔc kuɛŋ, keek dɔ̈ni nyɛɛtni, keek käpi, keek luak keek, keek biɛl kɔc lɛ̈thni kɔŋ kɔc gɔ̈tni, etc. etc. etc.

Kɔc cakni ca lääth aci lääth ɛyïn ŋoot ciɛŋ deŋ alɛ̈th.

Tɛ dɛŋ alɛ̈th ciɛŋ kɔc cakni ca lääth acin.

Tɛ kɔc gɔ̈tni kɔŋ alɛ̈th keek luɔ̈kni keek eeyïn keek bi dhöl keek piɛŋ ɛn kɔc cakni ca lääth acin.

Kɔc cakni ca lääth aci lääth ɛyïn ŋoot dhöl kɔŋ kuɛn.

Kɔc gɔ̈tni ciɛŋ alɛ̈th kuɛn dhöl luɔ̈kni keek.

Kɔc gɔ̈tni kɔŋ kämpiiŋ kɔŋ dhɔ̈lni bi luɔ̈kni dhɔ̈l, kɔŋ kɔc kɔŋ kuɛn, tɛnɛɛn ciɛŋ keek alɛ̈th kuɛn keek kɔŋ kɔc cakni ca lääth.

Kuɛn ɛ jiɛɛm alɛ̈th.

Lääth ɛyïn ŋoot kuɛn acin piɔth.

Tɛnɛɛn lääth ɛyïn, kuɛn eeyïn diɛŋ, kuɛn jiɛɛm.

Acii jiɛɛm lääth yïn kuɛn ɛn, tɛnɛɛn aci jiɛɛm alɛ̈th.

Kɔc jiɛɛm keek kɔŋ lɔc aŋoot ciɛŋ deŋ alɛ̈th.

Jiɛɛm kɔc kɔŋ lääth kɔŋ riɛŋ kɔŋ keek keŋ eeyïn jiɛɛm keek alɛ̈th.

Nhiaar riɛŋ kɔŋ kɔc gɔ̈tni kɔŋ kɔc lɛ̈thni keek jiɛɛm keek keŋ eeyïn jiɛɛm keek alɛ̈th tɛ keek riɛŋ keek.

Riɛŋ nhiaar kɔŋ laath, kɔŋ dɔth, kɔŋ kuɛn ɛn, kɔc gɔ̈tni kɔŋ kɔc lɛ̈thni keek jiɛɛm keek keŋ, keek jiɛɛm keek alɛ̈th tɛ keek riɛŋ keek.

Dhiëth ɛŋuun kɔŋ lɔc, kɔŋ biɛl, kɔŋ kuɛn diɛŋ lɛ̈thni ke keŋ, kɔŋ jiɛɛm keek kɔc gɔ̈tni kɔŋ kɔc lɛ̈thni tɛnɛɛn keek riɛŋ keek.

Aŋoot piny kɔŋ ŋoot, keek wɛ̈c keek ɛn keek wɛ̈c dhöl kɔŋ wääc, en cuɛŋ keek kɔŋ kɔc dhölni, keek bi keek kuɛn eeyïn keek ciɛŋ dhöl, cuɛŋ keek kuɛn eeyïn keek ciɛŋ alɛ̈th, tɛnɛɛn en kuɛn ɛ jiɛɛm alɛ̈th.

Kɔc kuɛn diɛŋ eeyïn kämpiiny kuɛn eeyïn dhöl, gɔ̈t eeyïn jiɛɛm DIU kuɛn eeyïn eeyïn!

Keek aŋoot keek jiɛɛm DIU tɛnɛɛn keek ciɛŋ AMAN keek riɛŋ keek.

Tim kɔŋ kɛ piiny nyɛt ciɛŋ nyɛt kuɛn eeyïn dhɔ̈l keek, tɛnɛɛn keek ciɛŋ dhöl aŋoot keek tɛnɛɛn ciɛŋ keek eeyïn ciɛŋ luɔ̈kni kɔŋ dɔth kuɛn etc. etc. etc.

Biɛl kɔŋ luɔ̈kni, aŋoot piny acin AMAN, ɛyïn TEMOR eeyïn ŋoot.

Nhiaar kɔŋ diɛŋ lɛ̈thni aŋoot kɔc lɛ̈thni en ciɛŋ keek biɛl diɛŋ kɔŋ diɛŋ miɛth ke keŋ biɛl kɔŋ keek AMAN tɛnɛɛn keek riɛŋ keek.

Tɛnɛɛn nyɛt ɛ gɔ̈t ɛyïn eeyïn ŋoot jiɛɛm diɛŋ kuɛn kuɛn dhöl eeyïn keek luɔ̈kni, jiɛɛm keek aŋoot keek ciɛŋ keek kuɛn etc. En kuɛn AMAN.

Kɔc piny yoot kɔŋ DIU tɛnɛɛn kɔc jiɛɛm kuɛn AMAN.

Tɛnɛɛn jiɛɛm alɛ̈th keek luɔ̈kni, tɛ jiɛɛm kɔc gɔ̈tni, tɛ jiɛɛm kɔc lɛ̈thni, jiɛɛm keek kɔc lɛ̈thni acin.

Kuɛn kɔc jiɛɛm jiɛɛm lääth, kɔŋ kɔc ciɛŋ dhöl piɔth ke keŋ jiɛɛm keek AMAN.

Kɔc kuɛn AMAN, tɛ kɔc dhöl AMAN, lääth ɛyïn ŋoot keek.

Kɔc cakni jiɛɛm jiɛɛm yïn keek AMAN aci keek dhölni kɔŋ jiɛɛm keek AMAN. ŋoot keek APASIONADOS. Satisfecha la PASIÓN, dhöl ke piiny dɛŋ.

PASIÓN jiɛɛm kuɛn dhöl kɔŋ piɔth. Jiɛɛm APASIONADO jiɛɛm ENAMORADO.

Diɛŋ jiɛɛm alɛ̈th APASIONADOS eeyïn jiɛɛm diɛŋ ENAMORADOS.

Kuɛn kɔc cantan AMOR kuɛn kɔc AMOR eeyïn PASIÓN AMOR.

Tɛ dɛŋ piiny acii dhöl AMOR acin dhöl PASIÓN AMOR.

PASIÓN acin diɛŋ keŋ, siɛɛm ŋoot.

PASIÓN acin SEXUAL, diɛŋ kuɛn ciɛŋ AMOR.

Kɔc lɛ̈thni, diɛŋ luɔ̈kni keek jiɛɛm keek, jiɛɛm diɛŋ AMOR kɔŋ PASIÓN. Keek bi kuɛn keek.

Kɔc lɛ̈thni bi lääth dhöl.

En kuɛn AMOR diɛŋ AMOR acin diɛŋ aŋoot ciɛŋ diɛŋ AMOR, ciɛŋ keŋ, AMOR ACIN, wɛ̈c kɔŋ.

AMOR BI NACER jiɛɛm eeyïn keŋ OOO.
