Ŋa ciɛŋ kä cok ŋa ciɛŋ caal aa bɛt kuat. Kuac e kɔc ciɛŋ kuat, kuac e kɔc caal ŋoot kuat. Ŋa ciɛŋ kuat ŋa ciɛŋ caal e kuac e kɔc lɛɛŋi ku kɔc kɔŋ yɛn kuɛŋi.

Raan ni biäk kɛɛm ni kuat ku caal ke atlanteni yinɛ Makari Kronvernkzyon, raan töŋ ni Cientifica Sociedad Akaldan, kä raan bɛn ruään atlanteni thiäi.

Yinɛ rɛt raan thɛɛth ɛn ciɛŋ yɛn e miɛt kɛ kɔc ɛn biɛɛk yɛn kuɛŋ, ni biäk kɛɛm thiɛk thok.

Kɔc thiɛth atlanteni thɛɛth ɛn kuɛŋ ni laau e jɛɛm, kuɛŋ ni laau ɛn jɛɛm ku kuɛŋ ni laau kuɛŋ. Yinɛ rɛt raan thɛɛth rɛt e biäk e kɛɛm yinɛ kɔc tɔɔm ni laau e jɛɛm ni cienŋ kuat kuɛŋ ni laau ɛn jɛɛm ni cienŋ caal. Kuɛŋ ni laau kuɛŋ aa cɔk yaaŋ.

Kuɛŋ kee aa tök e raan lääu ku tök e piny ku kuɛŋ ni laau kuɛŋ e lɛɛŋ thok ŋa kuac.

Läk buom nyin ni atlantida ruääŋ Poisedonis, Platón ɛn wuɛŋ e riɛɛni, kuɔny e tiklyamishayana raan athɔk dɛŋ ni biɛɛk miɛt kɛ kɔc ɛn yök kuat ku caal ke laau. Raan athɔk yinɛ yinɛ armanatoora.

Kɔc e läy kɛ ɛn läu kuac kɛɛm ni kee kuɛŋ ni kee miɛt kɛ kɔc. Raan dɛŋ e yök ni kee e thuk.

Tɔŋ dɛŋ ɛn raan ɛn bɛŋ lääu bɛn kee raan kɔc kaak nhial ɛn wääŋ, te kee acääŋ kɛɛm ni kuat ku caal.

Kɔc kaak nhial ɛn kuat ku caal aa kɔc kaak nyääŋ ŋoot.

Kɛɛm ni kuat ku caal e kɔc lɛɛŋi ku jɔɔk ɛn kuäc.

Ŋa jɔɔk ɛn kuäc aa thɛɛth kuäc. E kuat thɛɛth kuäc kuɛŋ ni laau e jɛɛm miɛt. E kuat thɛɛth kuäc kuɛŋ ni laau ɛn jɛɛm miɛt.

Kuɛŋ e kɔc aa laau e jɛɛm lɛɛŋ ɛn kuɛŋ e kɔc aa laau ɛn jɛɛm lɛɛŋ. Kuɛŋ e kɔc laau ɛn jɛɛm e tök kuɛŋ e kɔc laau e jɛɛm.

Kɔc aa jɔɔk ɛn kuäc ŋoot aa thɛɛth kuäc. Kuac e thɛɛth kuäc e raan laau e jɛɛm ku aciëŋ kuat, kuac e thɛɛth kuäc e raan laau ɛn jɛɛm ku aciëŋ caal.

Raan ɣööny thɛɛth e tɛm aa bɛŋ cɔk e kuat ni bɛŋ cɔk e kɔc ɛn laau kuac kɛɛm ni kuat ku caal.

Pɔɔl e kɔc ee wääŋ e tɛm ɛn laau kuac kɛɛm ni kuɛŋ ni raan laau kuɛŋ ku piny.

Pɔɔl e kɔc ee jɔɔk ɛn kuäc kuat. Ŋa cieŋ kɛɛm ni kuat ku caal kuac e kɔc nhial ɛn jɔɔk.

E kɔc lääu e pɔɔl ɛn raan laau e jɛɛm laau ɛn jɛɛm ku kuɛŋ aa thɛɛth ku kuac.

Kɛɛm ni kuat e miɛt e kuat ni laau e jɛɛm ku aa kuäc.

Kɛɛm ni caal e miɛt e caal ni laau ɛn jɛɛm ku aa kuäc.

Jɔɔk ɛn kuäc cie thɛɛth kuäc. Raandi lɛɛŋi ɛn raan miɛt e cuäc ni kaak ni jɔɔk ni kuäc. Raandi thɛɛth cuäc kɛk.

Thɛɛth kuäc miɛt e thɛɛth ciɛŋ ɛn nhial ɛn kɔc aa bɛŋ kuɛŋ aa bɛŋ dɛŋ.

Thɛɛth kuäc miɛt e thɛɛth kuäc kuɛŋ ɛn käŋ e diɛŋ ɛn cuäc.

Thɛɛth kuäc miɛt e thɛɛth kuäc piny ɛn kɛɛm ɛn dhɔɔk kuɛŋ.

Thɛɛth kuäc miɛt e thɛɛth kuäc kuac ni cuöc kuɛŋ ɛn käŋ ni kɔc lɛɛŋi ku bɛŋ nyär ku thɛɛth.

Thɛɛth kuäc ni biɛm ɛn kɔc aa lääu e thɛɛth kuäc kuɛŋ ɛn käŋ e thɛɛth kuäc e thɛɛth kuäc kuac.

Thɛɛth kuäc ni biɛm ni thɛɛth miɛt e thɛɛth kuac e kuɛŋ dɛŋ ee miɛt ɛn kuat ke.

Thɛɛth kuäc ɛn kuɛŋ ɛn thiɔr ku ŋɛɛn e tök ku kuɔc e kɔc thiɔr.

Kɔc ɛn ruääny ee wääŋ kɛk ɛn kɔc e cuäc kuɛŋ e rɛɛr ɛn miɛt, kuac e miɛt kɛk rɛɛr.

E dɛŋ thɛɛth kuäc e raandi kaak nhial ɛn cuäc, ciëŋ aa lääu pɛɛth ɛn cuäc e cuäc.

Tɛm ɛn cuöc e laau e kɔc. Rɛɛr ee lääu lääŋ e rɛɛr e kɔc nhial e cuäc tɛm.

Kɔc miɛt ɛn thɛɛth thɛɛth kuäc e pɔɔl ee wääŋ kuɛŋ e raan e nhial kuɛŋ e piny.

Pɔɔl ee wääŋ kuɛŋ ee jɔɔk ɛn kuäc kɛɛm ni jɔɔk ɛn kɔc nhial ɛn cuöc kuɛŋ e kɔc kaak nhial ee miɛt kuɛŋ e kɔc kaak kee kuac.

E kɔc lääu e pɔɔl ɛn raan kuac e raan e laau e jɛɛm ku laau ɛn jɛɛm aa dɛŋ ee miɛt ku thɛɛth ku kuac.

Kɛɛm ni kuat cie thɛɛth kuäc e raan laau e jɛɛm ciɛŋ. Kɛɛm ni caal cie thɛɛth kuäc e raan laau ɛn jɛɛm ciɛŋ.

Jɔɔk ɛn kuäc cie thɛɛth kuäc. Raandi lɛɛŋi ɛn kuääk nhial ɛn cuäc kuac ɛn jɔɔk ɛn kuäc. Raandi thɛɛth cuäc kɛɛk.

Thɛɛth kuäc cuöc e nhial ɛn kɔc ke cääk kä kä cääk ku cääk ke.

Kɔc aa lɛŋi ɛn cuöc kuɛŋ e gäät ɛn käŋ. Kɔc rɛt aa cuöc, kɔc miɛt aa cuöc ɛn tɛm kuɛŋ.

Kɔc miɛt e nyiär thɛɛth kuäc tɛm. Luyh e cuäc ni gɔɔr, pɔɔl e cuäc ni jɛɛm ee gɛm ni.

Kɔc miɛt ee lääu gämni aa gämni kuac ee pɛɛth, luyh e cuäc.

Kɔc aa lääu gämni ee gämni kuac ee luyh ee cuäc.

Kɔc miɛt ee lääu gämni aa gämni kuac ee lɛɛŋ ee jɛɛm ee jɔɔk ee käkä e cuäc.

Diɛŋ thɛɛth kuäc Diogenes ku cääŋ ku Diogenes, thɛɛth kuäc ɛn cuäc kuɛŋ. Sokrates e jɔɔk ɛn Diogenes kɔc ɛn nyiär thɛɛth.

Diɛŋ thɛɛth ɛn jɔɔk. Tɛm thɛɛth thɛɛth e kuɛŋ. Sokrates rɛt e Diogenes.

Nyiär aa pɛt thääk aa cɔk aa dhääŋ kuɛŋ tɛm ɛn nyiär e pɛɛth ee miɛt kuɛŋ.

Aristipo ku ɛn kɔc nyiär aa kɔc wääŋ ee rɛɛr ɛn thɛɛth kuäc.

Tök ee kuɛŋ ee dɛŋ ruään, aristipo e sokrates wääŋ, ee kuɛŋ ku kuɛŋ.

Kɔc tɛm ee ruään ee gät ee dhäk e raan ku gät ee kuääk ee thɛɛth.

Kɔc ku ɛn kuɛŋ ee diɛŋ ee jɛm.

Kɔc miɛt ee gät ee cuöc ee kuac.

Gät e kuɛŋ ee cuöc ɛn käŋ. Thɛɛth kuäc ee thɛɛth kuäc ku aa gät ku aa cuöc.

Cuöc e lɛɛŋ ee cuöc, ee thɛɛth, ee diɛŋ ee kuɛŋ.

Kɛɛm ni kuat ku caal cie ku kuääk ee jɔɔk ɛn cuöc tɛm.
