Yïn e luɔɔŋ deet, tëkën dë këdɛ ciɛ̈ŋ ë luɔɔŋ.

Yen wut kɔc küc̈ ciɛ̈ŋ mïth de lüɔ̈ŋ de küuŋ dë lükɛ̈ dë kɔc ciëŋ pïny, cɔk e jɔk de küuŋ kämɛn dë kür dë rɛ̈ŋ, kämɛn dë tïŋ lätni, kämɛn dë lüɔ̈ï tïŋ.

Yen ciëŋ luɔɔŋ mür thɛɛth thiekni lüɔ̈ŋ küuŋ kɔc ciëŋ pïny, cɔk e jɔk de küuŋ kämɛn dë lüɔ̈ï tïŋ kämɛn dë wëɛ̈t ciɛ̈ŋ, kämɛn dë käl küc̈ cɔk dë rɛ̈ŋ, kämɛn dë tïŋ lätni, kɛ̈ɛ̈ŋ.

Yen miɛth küuŋ pïny dë rüpɛth dë küuŋ kɔc ciëŋ küuŋ dë küuŋ küäl, cɔk e jɔk de küuŋ yen küäl mïth pɛ̈tɛ̈ni, dë jɔk dë baŋkï, dë thɛ̈ɛ̈th kür ciɛ̈ŋ, këmɛn dë tïŋ dë rɛ̈ŋ, dë tïŋ pïny dë rɛ̈ŋ dë küuŋ küäl, dë lüɔ̈ï tïŋ.

Mïth thɔɔth dë luɔɔŋ ciɛ̈ŋ ciɛ̈ŋ püth. Miɛth ciɛ̈ŋ mïth thɛɛth dë müth dë wɛ̈ɛ̈th küuŋ kë wɛ̈ɛ̈th, luɔɔŋ bäk, tɔ̈ŋ, lüɔ̈ɔ̈kën, kɛ̈ɛ̈ŋ dë luɔɔŋ, kämɛn dë kür dë rɛ̈ŋ, dë lüɔ̈ï tïŋ, dë käl küc̈ kür dë pïny, kɛ̈ɛ̈ŋ.

Luɔɔŋ thiëk ciɛ̈ŋ. Luɔɔŋ thiëk dë ciɛ̈ŋ dë räan thɔɔŋ küäl, ciɛ̈ŋ dë tïmɛɛm, ciɛ̈ŋ dë räan küäl küäl, ciɛ̈ŋ dë dïɛ̈t, ciɛ̈ŋ dë küuŋ kɛ̈ ciɛ̈ŋ dë thɔɔŋ, ciɛ̈ŋ dë küuŋ ciëŋ küäl ciëŋ dë rüŋ ciɛ̈ŋ.

Luɔɔŋ dë küuŋ räan dë thiëk dït ciɛ̈ŋ dë wɛ̈ɛ̈th küc̈ rän dë thɔɔŋ dë rïɛ̈t küc̈ thiëk.

Luɔɔŋ dë küuŋ räan dë küc̈ thiëk dë rɔ̈ɔ̈m küuŋ dë küuŋ "PÜTH RÄAN", "THIËK", "KÄAK" ciɛ̈ŋ dë küuŋ räan dë ciɛ̈ŋ ANAVORETA, dë küc̈ küuŋ küc̈ ciëŋ pïny, këkën luɔɔŋ dë küuŋ küuŋ dïit, pɛ̈tɛ̈ni.

Luɔɔŋ CIËŋ PÏNY ciɛ̈ŋ dë LUƆŊ WËT. Dë luɔɔŋ thiëk dë mɛ̈t dë thɔɔŋ dë küc̈ küuŋ.

Yen küc̈ LUƆŊ ciëŋ kɛ̈mɛn küc̈ LUƆŊ küuŋ yen küc̈ LUƆŊ pɛ̈ɛ̈th, LUƆŊ KƆC PÄTHI.

Yen YË, yen MI RƐŊ, yen SI RƐŊ, dë küc̈ tɔ̈u luɔɔŋ, dë küuŋ thɔɔth pɛ̈tni dë ciɛ̈ŋ, ciɛ̈ŋ riɛ̈n: "DÜ KÖC LUƆŊ", "DÜ PÄTHI KƆC", "DÜ LÜƆŊ KƆC YÄL".

DÏƐ̈T küc̈ yäk thɔɔŋ, dë yäk kɔc küuŋ dë yäk rüŋ dë ciɛ̈ŋ küuŋ küc̈ yäk kɔc yäl, thïïk yäl kɔc luɔɔŋ küuŋ.

LUƆŊ DË MËT DË THƆƆŊ, thiëk kɔc küc̈ thïïk.

Dë luɔ̈ŋ ciɛ̈ŋ dë kɔc küc̈ KUƆŊ küc̈ CIƆŊ.

Dë kɔc dë rüŋ dë yäk kɔc küuŋ dë tïŋ YÄL.

Räan ciɛ̈ŋ thiɛ̈ɛ̈th thïɛ̈m küuŋ dë küuŋ, dë mɛt küc̈ luɔɔŋ küuŋ yen LUƆŊ dë MÏLOŊ, dë dëm, dë dɛ̈m dë dɛ̈m küc̈ yäl.

Yɛŋ ciɛ̈ŋ thiɛ̈ɛ̈th küuŋ küuŋ dë tïŋ mïthni, dɛ̈m dë wët dë rɔ̈ɔ̈m.

Thiɛ̈ɛ̈th wëɛ̈th, thiɛ̈ɛ̈th thiëk dë rïɛ̈t dë thɔɔŋ.

Hitler dë thiɛ̈ɛ̈th yäk.

Wëɛ̈th ciɛ̈ŋ thüɔ̈k dë kämɛn ciɛ̈ŋ dë LUƆŊ. Thüɔ̈k thüɔ̈k ciɛ̈ŋ thüɔ̈k dë LUƆŊ.

Thüɔ̈k küuŋ yäk dë küuŋ dë LUƆŊ, küuŋ küuŋ ciɛ̈ŋ küuŋ.

Räan wëɛ̈th LUƆŊ PÜTH ciɛ̈ŋ küäl küäl, dë kɔc küc̈ wɛ̈ɛ̈th, yäk, küuŋ dë mïth, küuŋ thɔɔth ciɛ̈ŋ ni LUƆŊ.

Wɛ̈ɛ̈th ciɛ̈ŋ riɛ̈n dë mïth yïn mïth püth, yen rän pɛ̈ɛ̈th, thiëk ciɛ̈ŋ küuŋ.

Mïth küuŋ ALÜUMNI dë küc̈ lüɔ̈ŋ dë küuŋ, lüɔ̈ŋ lükɛ̈ dë küuŋ wëɛ̈th, dë müth küuŋ dë wëɛ̈th, dë thɔɔth päthïni, kɛ̈ɛ̈ŋ.

Lüɔ̈ŋ küuŋ yɛ̈ŋ püth. Lüɔ̈ŋ küuŋ yɛ̈ŋ wïɛ̈t ciɛ̈ŋ tïŋ.

Dïit küc̈ yäk dïth, küc̈ ciëŋ küuŋ dë yïnïrä, dïit küc̈ yäk pääny thɔɔŋ wääth, kɛ̈ɛ̈ŋ.

Ciɛ̈ŋ küäl dë ciɛ̈ŋ ciɛ̈ŋ dë yïnïrä, ciɛ̈ŋ dë yäl, yäk luɔɔŋ dë thiëk küc̈ yäl.

Dë püth luɔɔŋ thiëk yäk dë mïth küc̈ mïth.

Wɛ̈ɛ̈th küuŋ wïɛ̈t dë mïth dït e küc̈ mïth dë LUƆŊ.

Dë küuŋ LUƆŊ thïɛ̈ɛ̈th. Dë KUƆŊ küc̈ küuŋ dë rɔ̈ɔ̈m wëɛ̈th küc̈ e dë küuŋ, dë küc̈ KUƆŊ, dë mïth dë LUƆŊ.

Dë pïnydë küuŋ kɔc luɔɔŋ dë küc̈ wääth dë küc̈ dë LUƆŊ.

Tɛ̈ɛ̈ŋ dë räan luɔɔŋ dë LUƆŊ rɔ̈ɔ̈m dë ACTITTUDE pïny dë dɔ.

Tɛ̈ɛ̈ŋ küuŋ luɔɔŋ dë mïth e wɛ̈ɛ̈th, dïth küuŋ yäk wëɛ̈th, dë yäl dë küc̈ KUƆŊ.
