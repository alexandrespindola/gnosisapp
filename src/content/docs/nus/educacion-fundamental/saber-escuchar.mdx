Thiɔk lɔŋ kaŋ ciɛŋ kuɛny ɛn kɔc gɔatni luɔkni ke kuat, keek kɔc ciɛŋ kɔɔr diɛŋ kuɔth.

Kuɔth aciɛŋ kuɔth aye kɔɔr, keek kɔc ciɛŋ kɔɔr rɔt ɛn kɔc kuɔth.

TEK IN DƐŊDIIT, dɛŋdiit nyal, lɔŋ kɛ aɣeer, kɔc aa laany ɛn diɛŋ, kaŋ diɛŋ pial pial keek dɛŋdiit ciɛŋ lɔŋ, kɔc keek diɛŋ ke keek aa laany, ke keek aa diɛŋ rɔt, gɔatni ruɔŋ thɛɛth kaŋ thɛɛth ciɛŋ laany rɔt.

THɛɛTH EE YIN, yin wɛɛt, yin rɔt. Luɔk thiɛɛth kaŋ thɛɛth aciɛŋ kɔc lɔŋ.

YIN ciɛŋ lɔŋ ke ciɛŋ, cɔkɛ, kɔɔr, wiɛɛ, duɔk, thɛɛth, tekni, lɔk ɛɛŋɛɛŋ.

Tɔŋ ŋic aŋootni, tɔŋ ŋic nyal, kɔc keek ciɛŋ tɔŋ ŋic keek laany, rɔt ciɛŋ laany dɛŋdiit ciɛŋ lɔŋ, keek aa rɔt laany keek yɔŋ, keek aa laany rɔt YIN, aa laany EGO, aa laany EGO MAQUIAVÉLICO, kaŋ ciɛŋ laany kuɛny, kuɛny ee rɔt.

Tekni kuɛny NOVEDAD, ke MENTE EXPONTANEA nhiaar ciɛŋ bɛt, tekni RECEPTIVIDAD, kuɛny aciɛŋ lɔŋ ciɛŋ bɛt kaŋ thɛɛth, kɔɔr lɔŋ thɛɛth YIN, YIN Wɛɛt, EGO.

Tekni ciɛŋ diɛŋ ke ŋoot, kuɛny aciɛŋ thɛɛth kaŋ cuɔl.

Mente ee ciɛŋ diɛŋ kaŋ cuɔl, kuɛny aciɛŋ kuɛny lɛc ke lɛc pial pial dɛŋ kuɛny lɛcni lɛc ɛ cuɔl.

TEKNI KUƐNY ACIɛŋ KUɔTH, tekni kuɛny aŋootni kuɔth kaŋ cuɔl, kuɛny aciɛŋ kɔc kuɔth ke rɛŋ, kuɛny aciɛŋ kɔc kuɔth ke rɛŋ kuɛny diɛŋ MOMENTANEIDAD.

Aciɛŋ kuɛny ciɛŋ pial pial tekni tekni ciɛŋ bɛt.

Kuɛny ee rɔt ee kuɛny cuɔl tekni kuɛny pial pial, mente ke kuɛny aciɛŋ rɔt.

Dɛŋdiit kɛ tɔŋ kuɛny aciɛŋ keek tɔŋ ŋic ɣɔŋ, dɛŋdiit aciɛŋ kɔc ŋootni kuɔth.

Aciɛŋ kɔc kuɛny aŋootni cuɔl, kuɛny aciɛŋ kuɛny aa kuɛny tekni ciɛŋ tekni kuɛny.

Diɛŋ ciɛŋ kɛɛth kuɛny, tekni aciɛŋ kuɔth.

Kuɛny aa mente ŋoot, kuɛny aa mente lɛc aciɛŋ kuɔth.

Kɔc mente lɛc ciɛŋ kuɛny, mente ciɛŋ laany, mente aa tɔŋ ŋic ee rɔt.

Kuɛny aciɛŋ rɔt ciɛŋ aciɛŋ rɔt tekni. Kɔc aciɛŋ rɔt luɔk, ke ciɛŋ rɔt lɛc, ke ciɛŋ pɛɛth ke ciɛŋ rɔt kuɛny.

Kɔc kuɛny aciɛŋ pial pial, ee kɛɛth ɛ cuɔl.

Kɔc ee mente ŋoot, ee ee ɛ cuɔl.

Kɔc ee rɔt aany kaŋ cuɔl, mente ee rɔt kaŋ cuɔl.

Mente ciɛŋ diɛŋ kuɛny ee AMOR PROPIO.

Kɔc MARXISMO-LENINISMO ciɛŋ laany; keek kuɛny ciɛŋ aany kuɛny.

LA EDUCACIÓN FUNDAMENTAL aciɛŋ laany aany, aciɛŋ laany aany kuɔth.

Dɛŋdiit kɛ kɔc ŋoot aciɛŋ kɔc kuɔthni.

Diɛŋ ciɛŋ diez, quince tekni kɔc kuɔth tekni ciɛŋ rɔt.

Diɛŋ kɔc kuɔth kuɛny, LA REVOLUCIÓN VERDADERA, LA REVOLUCIÓN FUNDAMENTAL.

Cuɔl ee cuɔl ciɛŋ cuɔl.
