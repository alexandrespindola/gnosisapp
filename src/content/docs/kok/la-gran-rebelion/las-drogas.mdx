Mon'xachea monobod'dhik vibhajonan amkam amchea zannvayechea unch pamvddeache krur vostavta dakhounk melltta.

Jea vellar ekan aplo apunnuch don monxam ekuch mon'xant asat mhunn sid'dh kelea, ek sokoilea pamvddeacher sadharan monis ani dusro ek unch pamvddeacher, ten'na sogllem bodolta ani ami hea velar jivitak mukh'y totvanchea adharan cholpaak proitn kortat je tanchim atmant asat.

Bhaili jinn asta toxich bhitrelli jinnui asta.

Bhailo monis sogllo nhoi, monobod'dhik vibhajan bhitrolea mon'xachem vostav dakhaita.

Bhailo monis koso cholta, to zaite torechen astai ani sadharan jinnent tachi pratikria asta, adrush dhaageani halovpi eka putlli bhaxen.

Bhitrello monis khoree atmachem astai, tem vegvellea totvancher adharit astai, taka robot korunk xokona.

Bhailo monis koslich vostu viksa korun ghena, taka dishtta ki taka vaitt poryan dila, apunnach dukhi zata, apunnuch chodd mandta, to soinik asa zalear general zanvk sodta, to ek karkhanent kamgar asa zalear promoxon mellona tedna virodh korta, taka dista ki tachea gunnank yogy man mellonk zai, ani her.

Konnakuch dusrem zolm mellonk xokona, punorjivont zanvk zai zoxem Som'xachem susmvad sangta, torui sadharan mon'xachea sokoilea monopod'dhant jiyet ravonk zai.

Jea vellar ek apli nadannai ani bhitrelli dusmani vollkhota, jea vellar taka apli jinn sodhunk dhiry asta, ten'na taka khorench kollta ki taka koslich gunnvan'n nastana asa.

"Dhany te atmik garib, kiteak tankam sorgachem raj melltolem".

Atmik garib vo atmik bhikar mhonnje khorench te je apli nadannai, beixelponn ani bhitrelli dusmani vollkhotat. Tea torechea pranneank nischit rupan uzvadd mellta.

"Sorgachea rajant dhon'yanchi bhitor sorop eka suiyechea nakentlean utta bhaxen ason vadd".

Hem dista ki mon zaitench gunnvata, manpatram ani medaleamni bhorlelem, manadik samajik gunn ani avghodd axaik siddhantamni, atmik garib nhoi ani tenna tenka sorgachea rajant bhitor soronk xokona.

Rajant bhitor sorpak vixvasacho khazno mhunnje ut'tam. Tor jokren amchea dogam'modim monobod'dhik vibhajan zait ten'na jokren vixvas anikui asom'bhavit zata.

Vixvas mhonnje khoree gyan, prayogik bud'dh.

Vixvasak hem vattea andhvixvassamni bhuglait, dnyanim kaddinch itle avghodd chuki korunk favona.

Vixvas mhonnje vostavik anubhav, bhitrolea mon'xacho sundor anubhav, promannik divi gyan.

Bhitrello monis, gupt prayogik anubhavan aplem bhitrolea sonvsarant vollkhota, ten'na dishta ki to sogllea mon'xanchem bhitrolen sonvsar vollkhota je prithvecher jieye asat.

Konnakuch prithvi gireachem, surya manddlachem ani amhi jieye asat tea akaxe gangachem bhitrole sonvsar vollkhunk xokona, tor to aple bhitrole sonvsar vollkhona. Hem eka atmaghatache bhaxen asa, to jivitacho darvajo pordvo korun bhair soronk sodta.

Drug ghetlelea mon'xachem atirikt gyanachem karan, gardhest kundartigudar organacher adharit asta (edenachea bagentlea naktta rupi sorop).

Ahnkarachea zaitea tatvani bhanddlilli bud'dh apli bhanddlileachea totvan cholta.

Ahnkari bud'dh eka komachea sthitin utponn zata, nipnotik bhramache bhaxen, kosloi monis eka drugachea probhavant asa.

Ami hem prashn ashem uktem korunk xoktat: ahnkari bud'dhache bhram drugachea bhramachem samani astat.

Spasht rupan donhi toreche bhram kundartigudar organachea mulakarananche bhaxen astat.

Nirdharit rupan drug alfa kirnanchak nash kortai, ten'na mon ani mendu hankam aslole sambandh sampun vellta, khorench hem purnn faskarponn zata.

Drug ghetlolea monis tacho vyasan dhorm korta ani vengddo zata ani chint'ta ki to vostav drugachea probhavan anubhavtat, punn taka kollona ki marihuana, el.es.di., morfina, bhramit fondde, kokaina, heroin, hxis, chodd xant korpi gulleo, enfetamin, barbituretik, adi, adi, adi-n utponn kel'lim extra anubhavam fakt bhram kundartigudar organan kel'lea bhaxen astat.

Drug ghetlele monis ultti vatt choltat, kalant viroop zata ani tenna tankam nischit rupan narakachea sonvsarant buddonk poddtta.
