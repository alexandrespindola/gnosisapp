Vevharik jivitachea moidanacher dor eklea monxak tacho tacho nirdhar, taka taka dusrea poros komi zanno, ani novea vostuk to kednach ugto zaina; hem nirvivad, asaty, ani badolpak zaina.

Buddhivant monxachem mon kharab zalam, kamzor zalam, ani portun khala denvta.

Khorench sangpak zait zalear, aizchea monxak bore torein kollop ek pornea, bekar ani beudd'dea mashin sarkhem asa, zantunt apnnachak khorench bodol korpachi xokti na.

Mon dhil'leponnan cholona, tem rigidd ani kallbai vostunchea niyomanchea zallant aspaitalam.

Dor ekleacho tacho tacho nirdhar asta ani tharavik rigidd niyom asta zanche bhitor to nirontor kam korta ani protikria dita.

Hea sogllea vixoyantli khup gombhir vost mhunllear koddok niyomanchea adharan lokancho nirdhar lokhanchea kuxil'lea ani beudd'dea niyomancho orth dita.

Kiteak tori lokank chukichem bhognna zainam, dor ekachem matem ek sonvsar ani kiteach dubav na ki itlea monachea kovlancher dusrem vengovpi ani tras divpi murkhapan asta.

Punn lokanchem unch nirdhar tanchem budhichem bandpan kollona.

Sarke tilacharanche matenchim aizkalchim lok apnnachem borem chint'tat, tankam vhodd monachin ani vhodd budhicheancho bhom asta, tankam dista ki tanchem nirdhar khup ruchik asa.

Uzvadd kel'le agone vhodd tankalik aileanim asat, kiteak khorench sokratik zannvai dakhoun ami sangtele: "tankam fuddarakuch zannvai na, punn tankam mahit na ki tankam zannvai na".

Tachea monachea bandpanchea adharan pornea kallache niyomanchea add aslele budhivant chokmacheak tankam tankanch zulum zata ani tankam tankam zannvai aili tori tankam manunk naka zankam tanchea metalchea niyomanchea bhitor asunk zainam.

Uzvadd kel'le budhivant lok chint'tat ki ek chokmak lagon va dusrea karnnak lagon tankam tankanchea rigidd torencho marg soddunk poddla tor tem ek xekddo beudd'deancho orth dita. Oxem hem ritinn dilolo nirdhar aslele gorib lok apnnakuch dukh divpi zhuzpech dhorun astat.

Aizkalea kallache budhivant lok zanniv divpi asat ani zankam kallbailim tanche niyom soddunk tankam dhir asta tankam tim unch dishtin polletat, heachem vhodd vaitt mhunllear tankam apnnachea beudd'deancho khorench ghans disona.

Zannvayen kamzorki aslolea monanchem mon itlem kitim lagta ki tem khorench tem dista zacher tem monachem na teacher pradarshan dakhoupacho sukh dita.

Uzvadd kel'le gorib ani asahisk monxank mandunk naka ki monachem vokhddeponn ego nastana mhonnuch bhognna zata.

Dubav nastana jivitachea ani mornachea rahosyo zanna zaunk kiteak tori amchea bhitorchem mon ugtop zaunk zai.

Hea prakronnan portun sangpak zai ki fakt ek boreantlem borem astitvacho bodh khorench zanno zata.

Bhitorlem mon fakt brahmandacho bodh ailea dattancho vapor korun kam korum zata.

Vixoyik buddhi, tanchea tarkik vadvadant, tankam tanchea adhikaranchea bhair vochun zankam mahit zaina.

Ami zanno asa ki tarkik vadvadanchea vixoyanchem sankalpna bayle percepçãochya dattancho vapor korun toyar kortat.

Zankam tankanche budhichea torenchea ani sthir niyomanchea bhitor bandpan asa tankam hem krantikari vicharam zanna zatat.

Fakt ego radikal ani nitichea toren dissolve korun bodh ugtaupak zata ani bhitorlem mon khorench ugtop zata.

Torui punn hem krantikari ghoxnnam nirmal tarkant vo dialektik tarkant vochunk zainam, denvlelea monachea vixoyik protikriyak zulum zata.

Budhichea gorib lokanchea maten tankam doryacho udok ek kalchea glasachea bhitor ghalunk zai, tankam dista ki kolej hem sonvsarantlea sogllea zannvaichem niyontron korum zata ani brahmandachea niyoman tankam tankanchea pornea akadmik niyomanchem adhin zaunk poddta.

Zannvayen borem aslele unneatlokank tankanchi denvleli sthiti disona.

Kaim vellak him lok udd'dea velar borem dishtin dishttai dita jevha tim guptatmik sonvsarant ietat, punn tim thoddea vellak vattechea ujvadachi tarancho sarkhe dhumortat, atmik chintnanchea drishyantlean tim pois zatat, buddhi tankam pirantak gelta ani tim drishyantlean sodanchak pois zatat.

Budhichem nirmollponn kadinch astitvachea bhitor vochunk zainam, punn vicharachea vixoyik zhuzpantlem beudd'deank khup ujjwall punn beudd'dea nirnneachem karonn zata.

Tarkik sankalpnancho utponnk vokhddeponnak khorench anubhavachi goroz na.

Tarkik vadvadancho asvadan, vicharanim vicharanim khorench monxeak dhong diun monxeak sadhin ani sasnacho dhong dita.

Uzvall vicharanchem utponn budhichea chokmakeak dhong dita ani taka svata-purai dita zaka tankam pustak ani kolejiche dondd'dhar dondd dita.

Alkolika dhong asloli “delirium tremens” ghoddun haddtat, punn budhichea bhognnak dhong divpak sompem asa.

Amchea prokronnanchea hea bhagan ienva, ami sangtele kiteak tori dhong asa kitlea budhiche bhagneak ani kitlea monachem khoreponn suru zata.

Jevha ami budhichea riga ani kalbaili niyomanchea bhitor zatat, tankam tem mindantle mhonn sangpak bhov kotthin, zaka tem vegllench vo velache mhonn sangpak zainam, tem khorem mhonn sangpak kotthin asa.
