Konn disonant, amchea bhitrlle ek kallokachem rup assa jem amkam zannem na va amkam mandun gheunk zaina; tem kallokachea rupa zannvayecho uzvadd vhorunk zai.

Amchea Gnostik adhyanacho puro hetu mhunnttlear atm-zannvikodden odik savadh ravat.

Jevha tumchea sovem khub vostu assat je tumkam zannam na va tumkam mandun gheunk zaina, tedna ti vostu amchem jivit trasdayak kortat ani khorench soglle torecheo paristhiti utponn kortat jem atm-zannvayen talunk zata.

Sogleant vaitt mhunnttlear ami te agyat ani atm-zannvayen nastik aslelem rup dusrea lokank dakhoitat ani tedna ami te lokam modim polletoch.

Udahornnak: ami tankam fottkiro, be-imaan, lhan monisk porim pollentoch, je ami amchea bhitr gheun firtoch.

Gnosis hea vixeyen sangta ki, ami amchea lhan bhaga bhitor jietat.

Tacho orth asa ki amchi zannvay ami amchea lhan bhaga porient vistarit kortat.

Gnostic esoterik kamacho vichar mhunnttlear amchea zannvayek nirmoll korunk zata.

Nisondhe amchi svatah sovem bori vollkhi asli tor ami dusrea kodden bori vollkhi dovrunk xokonant ani tacho parinnam sogle toreche zhogddim zatat.

Svatah-zannvayecho sidho nirikshonn korunk zai ani atm-zannvayen odik savadh ravat.

Esoterik Gnostic kamachea kamant ek sadharan niyom mhunnttlear jevha tumkam konnak sovem bori vollkhi naste tedna tumchi atm-vixleshonn korunk zata ki tumi konache virudh kam kortat.

Dusrea vixeyen jem ami aitar kortat tem amchea kallokachea rupak rauta jem amkam zannem na va amkam mandun gheunk zaina.

Jevha ami ti paristhitint asta tedna amchea kallokachem rup khub vhodd asta punn jevha atm-nirikshonnacho uzvadd amchea kallokachea rupak uzvadd dita tedna atm-zannvayena zannvay vaddta.

Hi Sura-Katrikichi vatt, hille peksha odik khatt; khub lok suruvat kortat, khub thorode lok lakxya purnn kortat.

Zoso Chandrak ek dhaklollo bhag asta zo dista nastana, tosli stithi manasik Chandrak zata jem ami amchea bhitrant gheun firtoch.

Nisondhe aslolea manasik Chandrak Ahankar, Svatah, Mhunntllear, Atmo asta.

Hea manasik Chandrak ami monxak naslele ghatok bhirant dakhoitat, tras dita, ani kosle-i toren te gheunk mandun gheunk xokonant.

Atma-Sakarachi khor vatt, Kiteak khondd? Kiteak kitinch kashtache pauti? Kiteak kitliche bhirantak ullhonn?

Kedna kedna bhitloli vatt khub ghoddnea ani firnea uprant, bhirantak ujea utron ani opayachem torn utron uprant retachea rejistanam modim harauta ani ti vatt khoinchean fuddem veta mhunn kitem zannam na ani uzvaddacho kirnn tumkam uzvaddit korina.

Bhitor ani bhair opayan bhorlolli vatt; agyatach marag sangunk zaina, zoim fakt mriticho suskarta.

He bhitlule vatter jevha tumkam dison ami borem choltat tedna khorem mhunnttlear tumi vaitt choltat.

He bhitlule vatter jevha tumkam dison ami vaitt choltat, tedna khorem mhunnttlear tumi borem choltat.

He gupt vatter aslelea kxannak ami borem va vaitt mhunn zannem na.

Jem sadharanponan bondh kortalem tem kedna kedna n'yayik zata; toxem bhitlule vatt.

Bhitlule vattem sogle netik niyom asarpant; ek sundor uchit va ek sobit netik niyom, sthiti zannun gheunk Atmo-Sakarak bhari addvattavonn zata.

Bhagyam'modi amchea Atmyicho Krist amchea Atmyichea kholayen kam kortat, tras bhogta, ruddan dukh bhogta, opayachem ghatok vinax kortat jem ami amchea bhitr gheun firtoch.

Krist monxachea kallzak ek bhurgeaponnan zolm gheta punn te ghatok vokalta jem ami amchea bhitr gheun firtoch, poddkumponnan to ek purnn monis zata.
