BHURGIM, VIDYARTHI, VO JANNKA SKOLAK EK BAREM DHIAS DIUNK VETAT, PUNN ZO XIKXOPI EK SANKOR, RUVADI VO PORITUN ASTAT, TOR TANCHO VIDYARTHI TOXO ZAUN VETAT.

XIKXOK APONANK PUNAR XIKXONN DIUNK ZAI, APONNANCH VOLLKHUNK, APOVNANK CHINTUNK, SAMZONK KI AMI EK NAVEA KALANT PAVTAT.

XIKXOKA BADLOPO, SARKARI XIKXON BADLOPO.

XIKXOKA XIKOUNK MOHOTVANCH KASHTANCHE KAM, KARANN EK VEKTI BORI BARAP KARTA VO EK NAVIN VODIL JATA, VO EK NAVIN VODIL JATA, TO EK SKOLLANT KAM KORTA, VOCHIMCH TO JATA, TANCHEA MONANT 50000 VIDHANCHI MHOLLI ASTA VO TO KITEAMCHOI BADOLT NONVE.

XIKXOK ZANNA KHAISEO CHINTUNK XIKXONN DITAT, MHONNJE TANNAN KITEM CHINTUNK ZAI ASTA XIKXONN DIVPAK VODDVIK.

GHARCHE ANI XIKXOPI BHORTHE ASTA BHANKAR DUKH, ARTHIK, SANGOTIK, BHAVANANT VO ASLEM.

GHARCHE ANI XIKXOPI APOVNE DUKHI ANKUD VO APONNANCH DUKHKANANK ASTA, BHURGEA VO JHONKHANCHI VODDVIK VIXOI MHOJI "NAVEA LAHER" SUDHARIUK VO SAMZONK ZANNAN ASTA.

EK KHUDDICH MONACHI SAMZONK NONVE VO SAMAZ ZANNA NONVE, PUNN GHARCHE ANI XIKXOPI TANNANCH DUKHKANANK BHORTHE ASTA VO TANNAN SAMAR ARTHIK DISHEAN CHINTUNK VEL ASTAT, EK PROFESSION DIUNK KITOK MHOJHE VHOYUK VO TO SOGLEM ASTA.

VODIK SAMZONK SOGLEM GHARCHE APLO BHURGEM MOHOTVANCH UKHAR DITAT NONVE, UKHAR DITAT PUNN VO ZORAK CHINTAT, SARKARI XIKXONNACHI VIXOI VODDVIK BADAL KARONK.

GHARCHE UKHAR DITAT VO KHOVIM NONVE, YUDHD NANVE ASTA VO BHANKAR GHAR VO DESH NANVE VO VODDVIK VIXOI ASTA, YEDHEA SOMSIARACHO GHODNUK TAYAR ZATA, YUDHD, VAIDHIK DISHEN BHEUD, VAKHADICH VATAVARAN BHURGEANCHA KHATIR ASTA.

LOK XIKTAT, VAIDIK BANUNK, ENGINEER, ADVOCAT, VO ASLEAMCHE GHODNUKANK PUNN NONVE APOVNAK ZAKAT KI EK FAMILY BANUNK.

EGOISM, GHARA SOGLO GHARA MOHOTVANCH UKHAR DIVOP, ABSURD, EK SHATAKA PUNAR SHATAKACHO GHODNUK VO SAMAZACHE GHODNUK.

SUDARE, EK KHUDDICH KRANTI, GHARACHI DIVI UDOUNK.

AMI EK FAMILY, AMI VEKTI-VEKTIANK DUKHKAN DIYAK NONVE ZAI, EK FAMILY MAAN.

EGOIST FAMILY SAMAAJICH SUDARE VADOK VETA, VEKTI VEKTIANK BHEUD DIVPAK VODDVIK, YUDHD, CAST VO SAMAR ARTHIK GHODUNK DIYA.

GHARCHE MOHOTVANCH APLEAN BHURGEM MOHOTVANCH UKHAR DITAT, TOR VINTUCHI DISH VINTUCHI ASTA, KASHTANCHE DISHEN GHARACHI VODDVIK EGOIST VO ABSURD ASTA.

EGOIST GHARACHI DIVI PADLI, SOGLEM GHARCHE, SOGLE XIKXOPI, SOGLEM SAMAZ ASTAT.

BHAVACHI PARIPURN PARINAM, SAMAJICH KHUDDICH PARINAM, PARIPURN XIKXONNACHI KRANTI EK NAVIN DISHEAN.

GHARCHE SANGPAK ZAKAT, BOUDHIK XIKXONN ZARURI ASTA, PUNN SOGLEM NONVE, KITEM KORPAK ZAKAT, TANCHEA BHURGEAK XIKOUNK APOVNANK SAMZONK, MONACHI CHUK SAMZONK ZAKAT.

GHARCHE SANGPAK ZAKAT, BHURGEM MOHOTVANCH PHOLLVO UNCH KARPAK ZAKAT ANI PASSO ANIMAL NONVE.
