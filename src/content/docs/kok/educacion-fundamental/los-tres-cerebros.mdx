Poilem mendu kopreanchea pettent band kela. Dusro mendu khaxa zannkammerdandaak tachi kendriy marji ani tachim sogllim nervochi shakhaanchea sangatak aasa. Tisro mendu nirantar sthanant ravoona vo to tharavik avayav vo organ vo zannkaa naa. Khorem mhonnlear tisro mendu sahanubhuti nerveche plett ani sadharonnpan mon'xachea angik jivitache soglle vishesh kendrantlean toiar zata.

Poilo mendu vichar korpacho kendra aasa. Dusro mendu challan korpacho kendra, sadharonnpan motor kendra mhonn vollkhotat. Tisro mendu bhavnanchak kendra aasa.

Vichar korpache mendvachem atirik vapar kel'lean buddhimattechi shakti atirekhtaa hem vyavharan sid'dh zalam. Dhaddoxina darrun sanshay nastana sangunk zata ki mental hospitall buddhimanchea mornnanchem khorem kabristan aasa.

Sampurna ani santulit khell challan korpache mendvak upyogi aasa, punn khellacho atirik vapar mhonnlear challan korpachea shaktinchem atirekhtaa ani tachem parinam bhayanak zata. Challan korpachea mendvachem morn zaunk xakta mhonn sangopak sarkhem naa. Toxe mornn hemiplejia, paraplejia, progressiv paralysis, vagere rogi mhonn vollkhotat.

Saundaryabod, rahasyavaad, extasi, uchhuch sangit, bhavnanchak kendrak posunk zait, punn tachem mendvachem atirik vapar kel'lean nirarthak kharch ani bhavnanchak shaktinchem vinash zata. Bhavnanchak mendvacho atirik vapar "novea lahrak" eksistenxiyalista, rockache fanatik, adhuniktom kalantunche sensuel pseudu-kalakar, sensualityche morbido apasionaria vagere, vagere kortat.

Avishvasneem dista torui mornn dar ek mon'xan kel'lem teen bhaganim korta. Dar ekeo duenticho adhar tin mendvachean korta mhonn puray sid'dh kelam.

Motta kaydean buddhimanchea praniche dar ek tin mendvant jivitache MOL yatren dharlya. Tanchim sambhall mhonnlear jinn lambaunk, tanchim vinash mhonnlear morn zata.

Aizkalichea gasolin ani celluloidchea yugant, eksistenxiyalisma ani rock rebelanchea yugant, suraksha kampaninchea hisobaani mon'xaachem jivit fokot pannaas vorsham aasa.

AXIANCHE madhyant eke dharmik ekvott aasa, jea vhoddlyank aplea tarnekaaichi yaad na. Tea vhoddlyanchem jivit charxim ani panchxim vorsham bhonvton aasa.

Ek kosmik kaydo aasa jeo "ZORI ZAI TEA SANKHYANCHEM SAMAN VIBRATION" mhonn vollkhotat. Tea mathachea monkak tea kaydeacho vapar tin mendvan kel'lean zanna.

Asamayik shikxonn xikpek ani xikpeamni vichar korpache mendvacho atirik vapar korpak dharta, jeachem parinam psikhiatria zanna.

Tin mendvachem buddhimanchem posonn KHOREM SHIKXONN aasa. Babilonia, Grecia, India, Persia, Ijipt, vagere puraton rahasya shalleanim xikpek ani xikpeank aplea tin mendvanche puray mahiti direct dita, shikxonn, nach, sangit, vagere, buddhimanchem ekvott korunk.

Puraton vellanchea nattak shallechem bhag aasilem. Natak, komedi, trasdi, khasa mimica sangata, sangit, tondanche shikxonn vagere, dar eke mon'xachea tin mendvank mahiti divpak sadon aasilem.

Tedna vidhyarthi vichar korpachea mendvacho atirik vapar korinasle ani tannim buddhimanin ani santulan rupant aplea tin mendvacho vapar korunk zannasle.

Greciaant elefasischea rahasyache nacht, babiloniant nattak, greciaant escultura hanka sodanch xikpeank mahiti divpak vapartat.

Aiz rockachea bigoddlelea yugant xikpeo ani xikpeamni gollmulun ani dishtibhrasht zaun mannshik atirik vaprachea kallkhichea vatten choltat.

Aizkal tin mendvanchem sangatiche posonn divpak khoream ritinchem utpadak nizam naat.

Shalleche, kolejiche ani unibersidadiche xikxok ani xikxokni fokot borena zal'lea vidhyarthyanchi visravnneache yaadashiche vatter choltat, jeo shallechea bhair sorunchea vellaak asturen raakta.

Vidhyarthyanchi mahiti tin mendvank divpak buddhi, challani ani bhavnanim ektaar korun diunk urgent aasa, zori zai aa.

Ekech mendvache mahiti divpak sarkhem na. Poilo mendu vollkhun ghevopacho ekuch na. Xikpeanchea vichar korpachea mendvacho atirik vapar korop aparadh aasa.

KHOREA SHIKXONNan vidhyarthiank sangatiche udhegaache vatten dhartali.

Krantikarak manashastram porzollit rupant xikoita ki tin mendvant tin torechim sangatam swatantra ani bhinnam aasa. Him tin sangatam vostunchea vegvegllea toreanchea vegamni utponn korta.

Hem fakt amkam tin bhinnyo moniskain dita jeo tanche svabhavanim vo prakasanim kainch samanaank dhorina.

Novea yugaachi krantikarak manashastram xikoita ki dar eke monxan teen bhinnam psishik tore aasa. Psishik vostunchea ek bhagaan ek vostu zai, dusrea bhagaan kainch bhinnam zai ani tisrea bhagaan virodhi vostu kortat.

Vhodd dukhanchea kxannant, kednaakai ek daddichem morn vo kainch bhitrulem tras jaata, bhavnanchik moniskai nirash poriant veta jen'na mannshik moniskai tea dukhanchea vichar korpak dharta ani challanche moniskai fokot sceanache bhair soruncho prayatn korta.

Hyo tin bhinnyo moniskain buddhimanin posunche ani vishesh ritimni ani nizamani dar eke shalleant, kolejint ani unibersidadiant shikoune zai.

Psishik dishten fokot buddhimanchea moniskaik xikoupak sarkhem naa. Monxan tin moniskain aasa jeka urgent KHOREM SHIKXONN zai.
