Sogllya deshanthle lakhon vidhyarthi doryek shalet ani kolejik vochont, kiteak mhonn tankam khobor na, ani tankam mhonnje kiteak mhonnui khobor na.

Vidhyarthink ganith, bhoutikshastr, rosainshastr, bhugol, adi shikonk lagta.

Vidhyarthinchem mon doryek mahiti gheta punn aple jivitant te hi mahiti kiteak ghetat, hacho hetu kitem mhonn vichar korunk ek kshanbhair thambonam.

Ami kiteak hi mahiti bhorun ghetat? Ami kiteak hi mahiti bhorun ghetat?

Vidhyarthi khorench ek mekhkanist jivonn jiyetat ani tankam fokot bhoutik mahiti gheunk ani tivishixt memori bhitor sambhallun dovrunk zata, itlench.

Vidhyarthink hem shixonn khorench kitem mhonn vichar korunkui disonam, te shalet, kolejik vo vidhyapithik vetat kiteak tankam apnnaim-bapain dhaddtat ani itlench.

Vidhyarthi, shikoipam vo shikoipanim apleakuch kednanch punn vichar korunk disonam: hanv hanga kiteak asa? Hanv hanga kitem korunk ailo? Mhaka hanga haddpacho khoreponnim gupt hetu kitem?

Shikshok, shikoipanni, purush vidhyarthi ani stri vidhyarthi, nhid-bhojit bhovtonn jiyetat, te khoreponnim ottometa bhaxen kortat, te bexuddhi bhaxen shalet, kolejik ani vidhyapithik vochont, kiteak vochonk mhonn tankam khobor na, vo kiteak mhonnui khobor na.

Ottometa zaunk soddunk zai, jaagrutayek jaagi zaunk zai, porixa uttin korpacheak, shikunk, doryek zagear doryek divas shikunk ravunk ani vors utrin korpacheak ani kallji, tras, chinta bhogunk, khell korunk, xallachea sangatheank zogra korunk, adi, adi, adi kiteak itlo bhiranticho zhuz mhonn tankamuch sodhun kaddunk zai.

Vidhyarthink jaagrutayek jaagrut korunk shalet, kolejik vo vidhyapithik maddot korun shikxokanim odhik jaagrut zaunk zai.

Xallanchim, kolejinchim ani vidhyapithikanchim benchir ottometa bhusun asa mhonn polleunk dukh bhogta, tankam memori bhitor sambhallun dovrunk zata mhonn mahiti ghetat punn kiteak ani mhonnje kiteak tankam khobor na.

Zannttim bhurgim vors uttin korpacheak kallji kortat; tankam apli jiviki kamounk zata, nokri mellounk zata, adi mhonn sanglam ani bhavixyak lagun monant uzvaddaun zata, punn te vertoman khorench vollkhonam, fizik, kemi, jivshastr, ankganith, bhugol, adi kiteak shikunk zai hachi khobor na.

Adhunik bhurgeok borea potik mellounk tankam koxall zaunk zai vo tanchea potin tankam soddun dilear, vo te vidhva zatat vo aiek vo te doryek boreponnim toyar zaunk zai mhonn shikunk zai.

Monant puray uzvaddo kiteak khorench tankam aplo bhavixo kitem zatolo vo kitlhea pirayek mortale mhonn khobor na.

Shalet jivit kitem tori dhund, kitem tori vikri, kitem tori subjetiv asa, bhurgeak kedna tori kitem tori vixoy shikounk lagta jem vastavik jivitank kiteakui upayogi na.

Ajea kalak shalet vors uttin korop mhottvachem ani itlench.

Dusrea kallant vors uttin korpachea vixoyant kitem tori nitimatta asli. Atam nitimatta na. Bhurgeanche pai-maim chiktan shikshok vo shikoipachem dhond mellounk zatat ani bhurgo vo bhurgen bore zannkar naxlearui, vors uttin kortat.

Xallancheo bhurgeak vors uttin korpachea hetun shikshokak tel diitat ani hem vhoddleponn bhokte, tankam shikshok kitem shikouta tachem "J" mhonn zannkar naxlearui, te porixent bore zaun vors uttin kortat.

Vors uttin korpacheak zannttim bhurgimui asa. Hem kitli borobor korun zata mhonn khobor.

Ek bhurgo zokhon bore porixent uttin zata (kitem tori bevkuf porixa) mhonn taka khoreponnim vishoyi bodh asa mhonn nhoi, jem porixent vicharilem.

Vidhyarthi popot, kotoro vo papugai bhaxen ani mekhkanik bhaxen shikta ani jim porixent vicharillem tankam shikounk zata.

Tachem vishoyi svatah-bhodh nhoi, tem popot vo kotoro bhaxen memori korun gheun kiteak shiklim mhonn sangunk zata ani itlench.

Porixa uttin korpacheak, vors uttin korpacheak, borim zannttim zaunk laginam. Vastavik jivitant ami zannttim bhurgim polleleam jim shalet porixent bore zannkar naxle. Ami borim lekhpi ani vhodd ganitikar vollkhotam jim shalet bure vidhyarthi asle ani veakaran ani ganitant bore zannkar naxle.

Ami ek bure "ANATOMIA" vidhyarthi vollkhotam ani totras bhognnemnantor "ANATOMIA" porixent bore zaunk favo zale. Ajea kalak to "ANATOMIA" vixoyan bore lekhak zala.

Vors uttin korop mhonnje chotur zaunk zaite mhonn nhoi. Asle lokui asa jim kednach vors uttin korunk nasli punn te chotur asa.

Vors uttin korpachea porosui odhik mhottvachem asa, kaim vixoy shikpachea porosui odhik mhottvachem asa ani vishoyi, ujvalla ani nirmoll bodh kiteak shikta tache vishoyar asa.

Vidhyarthink bodh zago korunk maddot korun shikxokanim toras korun gheunk zai; shikxokanchem puray toras vidhyarthinchea bodhek dhaddunk zai. Vidhyarthi jim vixoy shiktat tacho svatah-bhodh purayponnim gheunk zai.

Memori korun ghetlear, popot bhaxen shiklear, tem purayponnim bevkuf asa.

Vidhyarthink bore torasache vixoy shikunk lagta ani "VORS UTTIN" korpacheak tanchea memorint sambhallun dovrunk lagta ani magir vastavik jivitant te fokot nirupayogi mhonn disonam punn te visortat kiteak memori imanadari nhoi.

Zannttim bhurgim nokri mellounk ani apli jiviki kamounk shikta ani magir tankam nokri mell'lear boreponnim zata, te profesonal zaleat, doktor, vakil, adi zaleat tor tankam fokot pornea kallachi kahanni sangunk mellta, te logn zatat, toras bhogtat, tankam bhurgim zatat ani bodh jaago zanna mortat, tankam apli jivitant bodh naxlear mortat. Itlench.

Bhurgeo logn zatat, apnneaim-bapain ghor toyar kortat, tankam bhurgim zatat, te shezarea bhurgeank ani potik zogra kortat, te soddun divonk zatat ani porot logn zatat, te vidhva zatat, tem vhoddleo pirayecho zatat, adi ani magir nhidilamnantor mortat, bexuddhi bhaxen, doryek kallamni dukhacho jivita dukh dhorun.

Shikxokani vo shikoipanim vollkhonam kiteak puray monis nhidilam bhaxen asa. Vidyarthi jaago korun haddpacheak shikxok jaago zaunk zai.

Teoriya ani teorie bhorun gheunk kiteak upayogi nhoi ani dante, homer; virjilio, adi kiteak dil'lem, zoxon ami svatah-bhodh aslolo zago zannkar nanvlear ami shiktolim vixoyam zago zannkar nanvlear, vastavik jivit.

Shixonn kiteak upayogi jar ami zannkar, bodhi, khorench zannttim nanvlear?

Khorem shixonn vapunk ani borounk zannam mhonn nhoi. Koni bevkuf, koni murkh vapunk ani borounk zannam.

Ami zannttim zaunk zai ani zannttim ami bodh jaago kelearuch jaago zata.

Zannttim monxak xekdda nabbai ani sat subconsciencia asa ani tin consciencia asa.

Ami CONCIENCIA jaago korun haddunk zai, ami SUBCONSCIENTE CONSCIENTE korunk zai. Ami xekdda consciencia zaunk zai.

Zannttim monis sopnan fokot nhido korun nhoi punn taka svopnam zatat zokhon to nhido korinam, zokhon to jaago asta.

Sopnam polleunk soddunk lagta, bodh jaago korunk lagta ani jaago korun haddpachi prakria ghara ani xallant suru korunk zai.

Shikxokanchem toras vidhyarthinchea CONCIENCIA dhaddunk zai ani fokot memorik nhoi.

Vidhyarthink apleakuch vichar korunk shikunk zai ani fokot popot vo kotoro bhaxen dusreachim teori porot sangunk nhoi.

Vidhyarthinchea bhirant uttin korpacheak shikxokanim zhuzunk zai.

Shikxokanim vidhyarthink sotya uttin korpachi svotontrata ani boreponnim ani rchnatmak sobhavachea vixoyachim sab teoreancher totra korunk zai.

Xallant, kolejik vo vidhyapithik shikoutat tem sab teorem dogma bhaxen manun gheunk lagta mhonn sangop murkh asa.

Vidhyarthink bhirant soddunk zai kiteak tankam apleakuch vichar korunk mellta. Vidhyarthink bhirant soddunk zai kiteak tankam jim teori shiktat tem totran korunk mellta.

Bhirant buddhi asa tacho ek addvo asa. Bhirant aslolo vidhyarthi sotya uttin korunk tho zor zatta ani andhlo vishvasachem vasto bhaxen, soglle lekhpi sangta tem gheun dhorun dovorta.

Shikxok himmaticher ulouta mhonn kiteak upyogi tankamuch bhirant aslolo aslear. Shikxok bhirant uttin zaunk zai. Jinchi shikxok torekechea bhirant dhorta, kiteak sangteleachem, adi, khorench zannkar zaunk tho zonam.

Shixonnachem vastavik hetu bhirant uttin korunk zai ani bodh jaago zaunk zai.

Ami bhirant aslolo tori porixa uttin korun kiteak upayogi?

Jivitank bore zaunk favo zatach mhonn xallanchem benchicher vidhyarthink maddot korun haddpachem shikxokachem kartov asat, punn bhirant aslear konnim jivitank bore zaunk tho zona.

Torasan bhorlolo zon dusreachim vichar soddunk tho zonam.

Torasan bhorlolo zonak bore suruvat zaunk tho zonam.

Doryek shikxokachem kartov evident bhaxen, apnneaichem xallachea doryek vidhyarthink bhirant uttin asa mhonn madrut korunk zai, te svatah zaunk kortat kiteak tankam sangonk vo dhaddnk laginam.

Vidhyarthink svatah svotontr ani rchnatmak zaunk bore suruvat melloun bhirant soddunk zai.

Zokhon vidhyarthi svatah bore ani svotontr suruvatin jim teori shiktat team boreponnim totran ani totra zatat, te merkaniko, subir ani bevkuf asa tem choddonk zatat.

Vidhyarthink rchnatmak buddhi suru zata tacheak bore suruvat asa mhonn disunk zai.

Sab vidhyarthink svatah utpon korunche svotontrata tankam bodh zaunk asa tem shikunk dita tanka bore svabhavachea kiteakim nam zalea tor.

Khoreponn utpon korunche boreponn torasachea bhirant asleanv konnacheanui nirmonn zaunk tho zonam, shikxokachem porxem, niyamam adi.

Zannttim monxanchem mon bhirant ani dharmatmatan pad zalela asa ani bure suruvatin svatah boreponnim ani bhirant uttin zaun nirmall zait mhonn asta.

Amchea svatah jivitacher ami bodh zaunk zai ani jaago korpachem proses xallache benchicher suru zatale.

Ami jaaga zanka nastlear amkam thoddi upayogi xallachea uttin kel'lea asle.

Bhirant uttin korun ani bore suruvatin boreponnachem kartov utponn zata.

Bore suruvatin sab vidhyarthink hakk mellounk favo doryek xallant sab teoreancher jim shiktat tem ektthaim vichar korunk.

Fokot oso toreken torekechea uttin kel'lea ani boreponnachem vichar korunk svotontrata, totran korunk, DHIAN DIUNK ani boreponnim kritikarok zaunk ani ami kitem shikounk asa mhonn ami bodhi zaunk zatat ani na fokot popot vo kotoro jim memorith astem tem porot sangta.
