Morn'n khollaiek ani monantlea dor ek bhagant mritu mhonnge khoremponnank kiteak tem somzop khub gorjechem asa, tevui tumak khoreponnan omortvachi vollokh yetli.

Tujea mogachea monxachem kallez petient dharun pollounk mritu somzunk mellona.

Satya hea kxannak oxir asa. Mrituvixinchea sotyank hem laagu zata.

‘Hanv’ mhunnpi monxak apnnachea mrituvixim ek surekxittai zai asta, ek garantti zai asta, kosle tori dhormik lok zai astat jea mritu uprant ek bori suvat melloun ditat ani omortvachi garantti dita.

‘Hanv’ hea monxak mortale zait ravonk khuxek dista. ‘Hanv’ mhunnpi monxak ravonk zai asta. ‘Hanv’ mhunnpi monxak mrituchi bhiti asta.

Satya hea monxank mandun ghevnk vo dubav ghevnk poddona. Sotyacho dhyas vo dubhavo ghevnk kainch sambandh na. Satya vichar, te’ori, abhiprai, kalpona, purvakalpona, suchovnneo, purvagrah, tharav, nivednam asl’le vixinchi vost na. Mrituchea govkire vixinchea sotyak hem laagu zata.

Mrituchea govkire vixinchem satya tumchea anubhavanuch khobor poddta.

Jea monxank mritucho anubhav na taka sangop xoky na.

Konkllui kovita boroitolli, punn mogacho khore anubhav sangop khub avghodd asa. Mritucho khore anubhav tumink sangop xoky na.

Zaka mrituvixinchem satya zai taka sodunk poddta, anubhav ghevnk poddta, tevhunch tumka mritucho orth kollto.

Ami zannllean, lokank mrituchea ortachi vollokh mellovpachi ruchi na, lokank dusrea jevnnant ravonk zai asta, hea sarki dusri vost na.

Zaite lok voitu, kirtichem jivit, famil, bhavnna, vichar, bhurgim hanchea vorvim ravonk sodta, punn jenna tankam kollta ki manasikponnan hem soponn asar, nixfell, ani komi asta, tevun tankam bhiti dista, ani tenka tras zatat.

Tim somzunk raji nant, he lok hea vellar ravunk sodtat.

Tim somzunk raji nant, hem zaka kitem tori lagta tem kallam vorvim komi zata.

Tim somzunk raji nant, hem zaka kitem tori lagta tem mashini sarkhem zata, bejar zata ani thakaita.

Mritucho orth somzopachi khub goroz asa, tevui mortana tumchi bhiti pois zatli.

Zomnit amkam dixtti poddtta monachem bodol zal’lem asa ani tumkam khobor asta, tem mritu uprant ravonk zai asta.

Mon bodol zal’lean tumcho anubhav bodlonk xokona, khoremponn bodlonk xokona.

Kallachi bottli toddun tumi dhyan korun sasnnik, omort ani sotichem jivit jiyeta.

Zanka jiyonk zai tankam mrituchi bhiti asta ani tim narkotik sarkhem kam korta.

Mrituk bhionk kainch na, tem sunder, unch ani varnnon korunk avghodda, punn tumchem mon bodol zal’lean tumi bhavnnechea ani dubhavachea golleantlean bhonvtat.

Jenna tumkam khorench mritucho orth kollta, tevun tumkam vollokh yeta tumchea anubhavan jivit ani moronn ek uchch ani ekuch vostu.

Moronn jivitantleo vostu samballta. Jivitacho rosto mrituchea payamni bhortat.

Jivit mhunnge niradharit ani nirdharit urza. Zolmachea mritulegem manvi bhagant thoddi urza vhanvnk lagta.

Manvi angak ekuch urza bhogunk zainam, moronnachi kiran. Hea kirnank vhodd elektrik voltaje asta. Manvi angak voltaje bhogunk zainam.

Zoxtti zaddo moddta, ani mrituchi kiran manvi angantlean vhanvta ani nasttta.

Mrituchi kiran zolmachea ghoddnek mellta.

Mrituchea kirnnam vorvim elektrik ghoddneo ghoddtat ani jen’na genes eksarkhem melltta, tevun hem dhyanant yeta.

Mrituchi kiran manvi anga vorvim mulbhut vostunk komi korta.

EGO, energitik hanv mhunnpi amchea putan ravta.

Mrituvixinchem satya, moronn ani garbhadanacho vel kallacho bhag nhoi ani hem dhyanachea xastran tumi anubhavun zannun gheta.

Xikxonnik, kolejiche ani universitive zannteanim tankam xikounk zai sotyacho rosto khoinchea vostunche vorvim tumkam mellonk xakta.
