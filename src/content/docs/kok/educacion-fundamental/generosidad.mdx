Mog korunk ani mog zoddunk gorjechem, punn dukhachem vishay mhonnlear lok mog korinant ani tankam mog mellona.

Taka mog mhonnant tem lokank oddkhichem asa ani tem utsuktai ani bhiti kodden chuktat.

Zonn lokank mog korunk ani mog zoddunk mellttalo zalear, zhuz hem prithvecher asompoornn zatale.

Zaiti logn jinn khoree khuxalkayechem asunk xaktat, punn puranne dvesh monant sambhallun dovorlelean tem khuxal naxint.

Zonn zonnavrank udar mon axilo zalear, tem dukhest bhutkal visrun khorea khuxalkayen jiyet.

Mon mogak marta, tem nest korunk xakta. Anubhav, puranne dvesh, puranne bhirant, hem sogllem monant sambhallun dovorlelean, mog nest zata.

Zaiti patnnink dvesh axil'lean tem khuxal zatat zonnavrank pastvisrun hea vellak jivit apnna zonnnak bhagoun.

Zonn zonank aple patnnik khuxal korunk xaktat tem udar mon dakhoun aplem pastvisrun monachi kudd'ddi bhair uddoun.

Lognak hea vellachi goroz ani mahatv zonnnank zannvonk zai.

Zonn ani patnnink hem novech lagin zal'lean bhaxen disunk zai ani pastvisrun hea vellak khuxalkayen jiyet.

Mog ani dvesh hem utpatti-vidravok vostu asamantaram zatat. Mogan koslich dvesh astam mhonn naxim. Mog hem sasnnik map asat.

Tanchim ixttam ani dusmananchea dukham pasun zattech dukhan bhogtat tankam mog asa. Khorea monan goribam khatir vavurtat tankam khorea mog asa, asahayam khatir.

Konnu tor spontan zagear zata ani tachi sahanubhooti aikun veta tea shetkareachea pasun je sheta pavsanta tacha ghama pasun, tea zonnsan pasun je dukhta, tea bhikekarak pasun je rupiya magta ani tea dukhest ani bimart kukaryachea pasun je bhuke pasun rattechea bazun morta, tankam mog asa.

Khorea monan konnak madat kortat, naitor svabhavik toran gaddam samballtat ani udak dita gaddachea phulant tea vellar asli genuinati, khorea sahanubhooti, khorea mog asat.

Sukhach dukach mhonnlear, lokank genuinati nasli. Lok aple khaxa atme dhaktech korta, khaxa dhaktech kamna, jeety, ginan, anubhav, dukha, khushi, anink zaitim.

Duniaan zaitim zonnas asat je fottkiregenuinati dakoitat. Fottkiregenuinati asat hushar rajkarnean, junnu nivadnukikar je dhab dhab dhuddu udoytat khaxa atme dhaktech taka utrikat zoddunk, dhaktech yash, dhaktech sthaan, dhaktech girestkay, anink zaiti. Lokank sangonak zai mhunn gaddiak daddyo gaddyo korunk naste.

Khari genuinati hem poorn panan khaxa sovarth mhunn naxim punn hem sopee zatam mhunn bhulunk zata fottkiregenuinati kodden je rajkarnachea foxam pasun, girestkayachea nirdoy lokan pasun, je streek dhaktech pisa zatat, anink zaiti.

Zonn khorea monan udar zatam mhonn naxim, khari genuinati hem dhaktech monan pasun naxim, khari genuinati hem dhaktech kallzak sugandhita zaun asta.

Zonn udar zalear tem monachea dvesh varchantani, zonn dukhest anubhava varchantani visrum zatat ani hea vellak bhagoun zaitim bhagim zatat, sodanch khuxal, sodanch udar, khorea satyachn bhartam.

Sukhach dukach mhonnlear, "hanv" hem mon ani tem pastvella jita, sodanch pastvella cholon veta. Pastvelak lokanchi duki bhorta, khuxalkai nest korta, mog marta.

Mogachi vrutt pastvellan asun kadi punn somzun gheunk xaki na hea vellachi mahatv jeta mhunn.

Zaita lok amkam boroint dukhim visoun gheunk, uttam balsamo magunk tanchi dukhi kallzam bhare korunk punn tho'dem lok dukham pasun dukhi bhare karunk chintatat.

Zaiti lok amkam boroint aple dukhest sthiti detat je tem jiyetat punn tho'dech zonn bhage kartat ekuch pao jeka tem jivnk astam tea asahayama sangati bhage kartat.

Lokank soddi na somzunk je fattar eka karyam pasun ek hetu asta ani hetu porigartun karya bodli zatam.

"Hanv" amchea pyarya "hanv" tem shakt amchea ancestorsan jivlo ani je hetu sambhaltat zanchi karya vartman sthiti kartat amchea astitvat.

Genuinati ami hetu porigartunk ani karyam bodli korunk zai. Genuinati ami amchea jivitachea vahanak hushari panan cholovunk zai.

Genuinati ami amchea jivank porigartunk zai.

Kharya genuinati hem monache pasun naxim. Kharya sahanubhooti ani kharya khara mog hem khadi punn bhitichean nirmann zalem asta.

Hem somzunk gorjechem je bhiti sahanubhooti nest korta, kallzachi genuinati khatam ani amchea bhitorcha mogachi sugandhita nest korta.

Bhiti hem khatea vinash, sodanch zhuz utpon karta, hem dukhachem shiri je nest karta ani marta.

Shikshak ani shikshika shallelache, kolejiche ani univesitetiche zonnank zannvounk zai je tankanche shisyam ani shishikank genuinati utpon karunk, himmat korunk ani kallzak khar zatam mhunn shikounk zai.

Mugadd ani nirbal lok je agil yuga asle je bhitichea shiri somzunk zai tum mhunn, tem ek gadd bhag zaun utpon kelem. Hacho porinnam bharti, nirankush ani nirdal aslo.

Shikshak ani shikshika zannvounk zai je vell ami jiyetale, sanghatik sthiti jeka ami astale ani nave utpadnak varchivunk ek kraantika neetichea baza pasun je atmik yugachea sangatin asa je hea dukhin ani dukhan shakti panan chalunk asa.

Purnn shiksha ek kraantika psikologi ani kraantika neeticher sthayik zata je nave shaktechna sangatan asa.

Sahakarachea arth tumk poorn toran bhrasht karunk zai bhrasht zabardasti ani khaxa jitam. Sahakar hem asompoorn zatam asli genuinati ani krantikarinikarthik ani sovarth vargun zaitam.

Hem sodan zaun zata somzunk purnn toran monachea stharani, zonn dvesh karchantani nirdakshkallza monachi ani dvesh genuinati ani bhayanak sovarth. Khalee zaun atm-jagrukhem amchea bhitorche sovarth ani genuinati pasun amchea kallzan utpon zata je uttam sugandhita KHARYA MOGACHI ani EFETIVA GENUINATI je monache pasun naxim.
