Ami soddun dilem ki sanskrutik itihasantlea zaitea xikxokamni Bud'dh, Confucius, Mohammed, Hermes, Quetzacoatl, Moses, Krishna ani heram vixim koslim udgar kaddle.

Khorea vicharancher adarit asli tori ami teo porinnam' poriant pavonk xoklim ki zaitelea xikxokamni puraton dhormam vixim, devan vixim, mitthaxran vixim kosleo poristhiticho ubheo korpa sangtaleo, ani he tumche buddhench eksarkhem na.

Xallamni, kolejant ani vixvovidealoyani dharmik vishayancher odhik sommanan ani unch bhaktibhavanen, khorea buddhimottein vichar koronk zai.

Dhormik rupi aplem sasvot mol samballtat ani dor eka lokachem, dor ek zatichem monoxastr ani aitihasik gorje pormannem teo vevosthit kel'leo astat.

Sogllea dhormanche ekuch sid'dhant astat, ekuch sasvot mol astat ani tankam fokot rupantuch forok asta.

Ek Kristanvan Bud'dhachea dhormachi vo Hebrev vo Hindudhormachi thorai korop buddhimotechem nhoi, kiteak soglle dhorm ekuch buniyadicher adarit astat.

Zaitea bud'dhivantamni dhormam ani tanchea sansthapakancher kel'lea vinod marxist vixachea lagun zata, jem sokllea komzor monank zerixt korunk laglam.

Xallam, kolejam ani vixvovidealoyamni xikxokamni aplea vidhyarthi ani vidhyarthinimk amchea sovkateank khorea sommanachea margan margdorxon korunk zai.

Tem soglle ritimni virodhi ani lajekhorem zata ki eka vekten kosleai totvantlean kosleo dhormik mandiram, ponth, xalla vo adhyatmik somazanche thorai korchi.

Abhyasachea vargonk soddun dilea uprant vidhyartheank sogllea dhormanchea, xallanchea, ponthanchea lokam kodden zata ani tancham modle konnak mandirant somyok ritein ravonkui zaina tem buddhimotechem nhoi.

Don vo pondhra vorsanchea abhyasan uprant vargonk soddun dilea uprant tornatte ani tornatteo her monxam bhaxench susth ani nidristh zata, xallant bhitor sortona koxe rikame ani buddhimotechin nasle tache bhaxench.

Zoruri asa ki vidhyarthinimni her vostunchea bhitor bhavnatmik kendracho vikas korcho, kiteak sogllem buddhimota nhoi. Jivitachea vhodd avadhanank, ekanteakuch rukhachem sobhitayek, ranantlea bhurgeachem gayonak, sobhit sompoddeantlea songit ani ronganchea sinfoniak anubhavonk xikchichi goroz asa.

Hemui zobardastichi goroz asa jibontlea khorea virodhank zanna zaunk, koslem samjik nirdoy ani khor torichem samjik rit asa jea kallant ami jietat, roadak dukhichim main bhurgeanchem pot bhukelea bhurgeam sangata eksurponnantlean khanachem tunkddem magunk vetat, baim' kuddam zantun hozaramni gorib famil jietat, nirlajj raostam zatun hozaramni gaddio bhonkunk vetat dadea jivatak hanik pavtayta.

Varg soddun dilea uprant vidhyartheank fokot aplea svorthapanank ani problemank mukhekar zaunk zata, tori poriant sogllea lokanchea svorthapanank ani samjik problemank zanna zaunk zata.

Hem soglleam poros vhodd sankxt ki varg soddun dilea uprant vidhyarthi bud'dhimota asun tori, buddhimota nastana zata, tacho zankar nidristh astat, jibonak mukhar zaunk xikxonn dhoreponnantlean dila.

Vell pavla asa sodhunk ani sodunk tem koshem buddhimota mantat. Sabd'dakx, enciklopedia, tem purai zaunk xokta budhdimoteak sangonk.

Buddhimatexinvai moddan haddunk vo khorea khuxalaink zaina ani jibonak tem vhodd vikrell astat khoreponnantlean buddhimonta lokan mellpak.

Jibonant tem mahatvachem na budhdimota mhunn soddunk, punn amche bhitor tacho orth zanna zaunk.

Zaite zaana zann buddhimotecho gajar martat, dadeak konn vo konn budhdimotecho gajar marta tori, ani Karl Marx aapnnachem buddhimota mandun aplem kallpnik vastavad udgarlem jeachem molo sansarak sasvott molachea apnnaiponnache hanik, hozaramni dhormgurunko marunk, nanvanchi dhormancher bollatkar korun, bud'dhistor, kristanvan, her mandiram nas korun, hozaramni ani milianmni lokan tras korun poriant asa.

Konnui budhdimotecho gajar marunk xokta, punn khorea budhdimotechea asun cholonk kholleponnantlean durlabh zata.

Tem odhik pustokanchi mahiti gheun ani odhik gyan, anubhav gheun lokan dhyan diunk, odhik poiso nyayadisham ani pulisank viktem gheunk, ani hem zata mhunn doddun buddhimota manta.

Tem zala ani buddhimota dhoreponnant pavunk xokta mhunn chukun choltat. Te fottak poddle konn buddhimota odhik prokreien jito zavo zata mhunn vichar kortat.

Soglleamni manka ani mannasvaddhinechea sogllea bhagamni soglleamni zanna zaunk tem piddem diita prokreis koxi asa, kiteak dolleant gopon zata atma, mhaka, mhaka odhik odhik favo ani vaddo zavo kornk xiktat.

Mefistofeles je ami bhitor gheun choltat, satan, aham, sangtat ki mhaka odhik poiso asa, odhik sobitai, odhik buddhimota taka, odhik pratista, odhik huxarkai, ani heram modle.

Zann konn buddhimota zanna zaunk xokta, taka tem sonvaunk zai, taka jiyonk zai ani tacho anubhav meditasanchea margan zanna zaunk zai.

Soglle zann akumulum korun choltat kabrent jhorant, buddhimota, jibonent anubhav akumulumn zata ani tem dukhichi vostu odhik ani odhik akumulum korun choltat. Hachi tor soddun diunchi te kadi zanna zaunk.

Zaite zann pustok vachun ani tem memoriant diunk khuxal zata anik mahiti melloun, punn kedna zann vicharta pustokant sonkolem zanna zaunk, to te pustokachea zankaran apnnak zanna zainak, punn mhaka odhik pustokam favo tori apnnak konn vo konn te pustokachea orthak zanna zainak.

Buddhimota pustokacho dyanank, vo anubhavank, vo poisan pavonakai, punn buddhimota pavonaka, punn buddhimota amchea bhitor fulonk pavta tumi janna zata aham koxi asa, kedna tumi samjik automazm samjikodichem zanna zaunk xoktat.

Hem mehatvak zata tumi mon koso asa. Khorem mhunn tor torim, tem aham samjik astanachi goroz asa ani mon tebuniyadichi asa.

Zaka budhdimont asa zaka mortak zai soddunk tor manasik purai purai purai purai moddun zata mhunn.

Kedna aham morta kedna aham purai vortuta soklla zata dogdo tumcho bhavo zata tumcho bhavo zata tem sothyatmik zata.

Lok manta ki mon vaddo zata, punn lok sukho zata.

Bud'dhichea zalla asa kiteak ami devacho ek gunn assa ami konfus korunk zaina monacho ani buddhimotecho

Sukho zata ki buddhimotecho vikas korcho kasheli utpadan ani viktem ghetolponan.

Hem zobordastichi goroz asa tumi manasik prosesechem sakxaat zanna zaunk, sobodichem riaxan ani odhik samjik akumulom.

Poddi asa ki amchea bhitor zalla asa swatha swatha sotha swatha.

Tumchebhitor je mefistoffeles asta tem soklla zata, devacho uzo vaddo zata.

Amchi sachhi utor asa ani tem prem asa ani te utoran bud'dhichem utor favo zata ki kall favo zainak
