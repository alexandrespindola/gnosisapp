Kitlem ani taleanche donuy bhag ekach vostuche astat dekhun, ek vichar manddop sarkem asa: "Uchch Aham, Nich Aham" ekach andhkarit ani anekvant Ahamache don ruup asat.

Zaka "Devik Aham" va "Uchch Aham", "Badlacho Aham" va toxem kitem tori mhontat, tem khoreponnan "Mhojem Aapan", "Aapan-Fattounk" ek ruch asa.

Zon Aham hanga ani mojerui chalu dovrunk zata ten'na, ti ek Devik Amrut Ahamachea khotteam vicharan fattounk zata...

Amchea konnakui ek khorem, sthir, bodolnashem, sasnnachem, varnann korunk nokllo, adi, adi, adi Aham nasta.

Amchea konnakui khoreponnan ek khorem ani pramanik Ekvott Naslo; durbhagyean amchea lagim ek pramanik vyaktigatvui naslo.

Aham kobresthanachea pulank ximerui chalu astana, tachem mat ek survat ani ek sonn asa.

Aham, Aham, kednach ek vyaktigat, ekvotti, ekunttit nhõi. Spashtten, Aham mhollear "Aham Zale".

Pudvett Tibetant "Aham Zaleank" "Manasik Ekvott" va fokot "Molyeo" mhonn vollkhotat, tim molyeo sakaratmak asot va nakaratmak.

Zon ami dor ek "Aham" ek veglli vyakti mhunn chintunk xakta, ami uttejit rupan khanchi-khamchen manddunk xakta: "Sonsarant jieupi dor vyaktichea bhitor, zaitim lok asat".

Nirviklvopen amchea dor eka bhitor zaiti veglli vyaktim jieupi asat, thoddim bore, thoddim vatt...

Dor ek Aham, dor ek vyakti udhdhestpanekhatir zhogddi kortat, tem fokot aponnuch asunk sodta, bud'dhivat buddhi va bhavanik ani motor kendrank tem zata ten'na niyontrann korta, zalear dusri vyakti tanka paltaita...

Zaitea Ahamanchi Dhormopodesh pudentt Tibettant khorea Dristtant, khorea Uzelitank shikounn dili...

Amchea dor eka manasik doshan ek adim va dusrea Ahamant rupantor kelam. Ami hazaram ani millionan dosh asat dekhun, spashtten zaiti lok amchea bhitor jietat.

Manasxastriye vixeancher ami spashtten siddh korunk pavle ki paranoid vishoy, egolatran ani mitomanan konnacheai jivnant Ahamache pujan fattim poddchinant.

Nirviklvopen toslea lok zaitea "Ahamanchi" dhormopodeshak mortalik dves kortat.

Zon konnui aponnk vollkhunk sodta ten'na, tannem aponn-nirikxonn korunk zai ani vyaktitvant guspachea vegvegllea "Ahamank" vollkhunk praytn korunk zai.

Zor amchea konnakui vachpikeank zaitea "Ahamanchi" dhormopodesh azun kollna, tem fokot Aaponn-Nirikxonnachea prakrtint aslolea kamichea adharak lagun asa.

Zon konnui bhitlo Aaponn-Nirikxonnachi praktis korta, ten'na to aponnuch zaitea lokank, zaitea "Ahamank" sodun kaddta, je amchea apleach vyaktitvant jietat.

Konn zaitea Ahamanchi dhormopodesh nankar kortat, konn Devik Ahamak pujtat, tankam udhdheshtit rupan kednach gambhirtayen Aaponn-Nirikxonn kel'lem na. Sodkretik stailant uloitollean ki ti lok fokot agyank vochonant, punn tanka agyanacho agyanoi asa.

Nischiten ami aponnank gambhir ani khol aaponn-nirikxonnacher nastana kednach vollkhunk pavchonant.

Konnui vishoy sodankall aponnank ek mhunn mantat, te bhitlem bodol kitem purnnponnan asombhov astelem mhunn spasht asa.
