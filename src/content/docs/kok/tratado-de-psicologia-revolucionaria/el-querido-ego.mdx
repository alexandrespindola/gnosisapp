Kitlem ani sokoil ani voir ekach vostuche don bhag aslolean, fuddlem siddhant manddpak harkat na: "Uchch Aham, Nich Aham" ekach kallokh ani veg-vegllea Ahamache don rup asat.

"Devik Aham" vo "Uchch Aham", "ALTER EGO" vo oxench kitem tori mhonnllelem, tem khorench "Hanvuch", "Apleakuch dhokho divpacho" ek prokar. Zannam hanv hanga ani paralokant cholounk zata, tednam to devik immortal hachya khotya vicharanni apleakuch dhokho dita...

Konnakuch amchea bhitor "Hanv" soko, sthir, bodolnaso, sasnnacho, avarnnio, itleo vostu, vostu, vostu nahim. konnakuch amchea bhitor soko ani kharo Ekvott na; durbhagyan amchea kodden manyatai dil'li vyaktigotai-i na.

Aham, moronnachea uprantui cholta punn taka survat ani ant asta. Aham, Hanv, kednach vyaktik, ekvotti, ektthaim aslolo nasta. Sopeakuch hanv "Hanvponn" asa.

Udent tibant "Hanvponnak" "Manasik Ekvott" vo fokot "Mol" mhonn vollkhotat, he nimnne sakaratmak vo nakaratmak asot. Dor eka "Hanv" hanka veglli vyakti mhonn chintun ami dhrudhtaen sangonk xokta: "Sonvsarant jieupi dor eka vyaktichea bhitor, zaiti monxam astat".

Nisondhehn dor eka amchea bhitor zaiti veg-vegllea monxam jiyeutat, kaim bore, kaim vaitt... Dor ek Hanvponn, dor ek vyakti uchch ponnasthi astolea khatir zhuzta, ekuch zaunk zata, buddhi-xaktichi talleachi, manasik ani halchalanachea kendrachim niyontronn zata zate tedna, dusro taka pois korta...

Zaitichya Hanvponnachi totv Udent Tibant khorea durdishti lokanni, khorea uzvadd lokanni xikoileli... Amcho dor ek monasik dosh to Hanvponnan dakhoytat. Zannan amchea kodden hazaramni ani koddinimni dosh astat, dor eka amchea bhitor zaiti monxam jiyeutat.

Monasik vixayanchea vorvim ami spoxtt zannonk xokta ki je monis pisolleponnache, apleakuch vhodd mantat ani khoti uloupi astolea lok, aplea jivitant Ahamache pujek soddna. Nisondhehn toxem zannam zatim tankam zaitichya "Hanvponnachi" totv vaitt dista.

Zannan konn apunn khorench apleak vollkhonk xota, tannem svata:-nireekxonn korunk ani veg-vegllea "Hanvponn" vollkhonk prayatn korunk zai, je vegtikat bhitor bhitor asta. Zannan amchea vachpeank hi zaitichya "Hanvponnachi" totv azun somzona, tankam swata:-nireekxonnachi kami asa.

Zannan monis bhitor swata:-nireekxonn korta, to aapnnach bhitor, zaiti monxam, zaiti "Hanvponn" shodhta, je amchea vegtikat bhitor jietat. Konn zaitichya Hanvponnak manina, konn Devik Hanvachi puja korta, tannem kednach gambhirtayen swata:-nireekxonn korunahim. Socratic shelli ani ulounk ami sangtaun ki tem zannam fokot zannanonam punn zannanonam tem zannana.

Nisondhehn ami kednach apleak vollkhonk xokona, gambhir ani khol swata:-nireekxonn nastana. Zori konnaka taka ektto mhonn manta, tantun kitem badol korunk asak tem kitem tori asaky asat.
