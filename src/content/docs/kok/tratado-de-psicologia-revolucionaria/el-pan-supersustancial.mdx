Amchea jivitachim disachim disam zoddlean pollelear, amkam dista ki amkam zannvayen jieunk khobor na.

Amchem jivit cholchea trenak bhaxen dista, niyall habitanchea, sthir, vana ani dhaktulea astityanchea rastyancher cholta.

Ajeeb vat mhunnllear amkam habit bodluncheak kednach suchona, dista amkam sodanch temuch korunk bore zata.

Habitan amkam zodd korun dovorlea, punn amkam lagta ami svatantra asat; ami bhayanak dispi asat punn amkam Apollo bhaxen lagta...

Ami yantrik lok, hem purn karonn asa jivant ami karta tache vishayant soglea khorea bhavanache udentik nasunk.

Ami niyall habitanchea purannea rastyachea bhitor disacho choltat ani tech karnnan amkam khorem jivit na; jieunche porim, ami dainyavaste zata ani nove imprexanv gheunat.

Zonn eke vyaktin aplo dis zannvayen suru keli zalear, tem uktem asta ki to dis dusrea disank vegllachem astalo.

Zonnan ekle vyakti aplo purn jivit gheunk gheta, tea disachech bhaxen jiyeunk lagta, zonnan to udheak korunk zai tem azuch karta, tedna taka khorench kollta ki svatahcher koxem kam korchem mhunn.

Kednach ek dis mahatv nasun na; zonn ami khorench bodlonk chahata, ami polleunk zai, niyall karunk zai ani disacho samazunk zai.

Pun, lokank apnnakuch polleunchem na, konn apnacher kam korunk ichchitan, tankam apli nirdharanna hea utranche bhaxen sidh korunk bore lagta: "Office-achem kam svatahcher kam korunk favo daina". He utram bina-arthachem, khalle, vana, murkh, je keval susthi, kashtik nasun, Devachea Mahan Karanank mog nasun dhakun dovorunk upyogik podtta.

Hea bhaxecho lok, tanche bhov adhyatmik chinta aslear, uktem asa ki te kednach bodalche nant.

Apnnak niyall korunk urgent asa, der korunk favo na, rokddich korunk zai. Antrik Atma-Niyall khorea bodlank moolik asa.

Uthlea vellar tumchi mansik stithi koxi asta? Nasteachem vellar tumcho mood koso asta? Tum waiter-acher nirantarak ravonche nastalo?, bhayecher? Kiteak tum nirantarak ravonche nastalo? Tuka kiteak sodanch tras zata?, ityadi.

Dhumravpan korop vo unne jeunk hem soglem bodal mhunn na, punn hem ek adharachi sanketa dita. Ami bore bhaxen zannat ki veasan ani bhokepann he amanavi ani dhoranchi vat asa.

Borem na ki konn ek Gupt Rastyank bhovtalo, tache lagin ek bhautik sharir, bhov fattevasta ani ek mottem pot bhair asun ani purnataik asun. Tem bhokepann, lalch ani susthik dhakto.

Disachi vat, vevsaay, kamgari, khorench jivanik juree, punn te zannvayichea sopnat upastit asat.

Jivit sopan mhunn zannun gheunchen mhanje hem samazun gheunk na. Samajun gheunchen hem atma-niyallan ani svatahcher ghat kam korunk bore mellta.

Svatahcher kam korunk, disachea jivitacher kam korunk zai, azuch, ani tedna tuka kollta ki Devachea Prarthanecho arth koso asa: "Amkam Amchem Disachem Povacho di".

"Dor Dis" mhunnchen "Pan supersubstancial" greek bhaxen vo "Uchch Pan".

Gnosis jivantacho Povacho vicharanchea ani taktinchea donhi arthan dita jem amkam manasik chukank nash korunk adhar karta.

Dor pavtim ami ek "Ami" cosmix dhulichem karta, manasik anubhav gheunk mellta, "Zannvaichem Pan" khaunk mellta, ami ek novem zannvem gheunk mellta.

Gnosis amkam "Pan Supersustancial", "Zannvaichem Pan" dita ani apnnakuch nov jivit kashim suru korta to nirdhar dita, apnnakuch bhitor, hangach ani azuch.

Pun, konnachean aplo jivit bodlunk vo je konn astityachea yantrik pratikriyakodden sobhondit asa tem konnacheankui bodlunk bore mellchena, zonne poriant tankam nove vicharanchem adhar na ani Devachea Adharak mellchena.

Gnosis tem nove vichar dita ani "modus operandi" kashim eke vyaktik manasik taktik chodd fojan madad mellta tem shikxaita.

Amchea sharirache unnea kendrak kendrank uttam vichar ani taktik Devachem asta tem gheunk toyar korunk zai.

Svatahcher kam korunchen, konnuch tori hakkat nasun na. Konnuch vichar kitlem tori lagir aslear, polleunk favo asa. Konnuch virodhi bhav, pratikriya, ityadi, polleunk favo asa.
