Khool dolok ani purnn laksh divun chintop hi eka unnoti naslilya ani poddttalea kallant eksarki vostu asa.

Boudhdik kendrantlean vegvegllea toreche vichar yetat, te ek dhruddyo "hanv" asa mhunn murkhani samzonk zaina, punn amche bhitor aslolea dor ekant vegvegllea "hanv"chea vicharanchi te utponn zatat.

Zonn ek monis chinta ten'na, to dhruddyen visvas dovorta ki to svota ani svotakhatir chintta.

Taka disonam ki to durbhagicho boudhdik zonvar ki tache budh'dhenchean khub vichar bhonvtan, te amche bhitor aslolea vegvegllea "hanv"chean utponn zatat.

Hacho orth asa ki ami khorech vichar korpi vyokti nhui; khorench amkam ajun vyoktichem mon nam.

Punn, amche bhitor bhorlolea dor ek vegvegllea "hanv" amchem boudhdik kendr vaprota, dor pavtim vapurta chintpakhatir.

Tor, ami toxem sarkhem nishedatmok ani nuksan korpi vicharankodden ekvottit zatam mhunn murkhachem zatolem, taka amchi khasgi sampot'ti mhunn mandun gheta.

Ukttoch asa ki hem vo tem nishedatmok chintop koslea-i "hanv"chea utponn zal'lem asa zannem ek pavtim amchea boudhdik kendracho durupyog kel'lo asa.

Nishedatmok vichar vegvegllea zatiche astat: Dusreacher dubav, visvas nasop, dusrea mon'xakodden vaitt mon, ut'tavollicheo dvesh, dhormik dvesh, rajkornniche dvesh, ixttagotiche vo ghuttallantole dvesh, lobh, kam'vasna, fattir lagop, rag, obhiman, dvesh, dukhestponn, chorop, vyabhichar, ajgarponn, khan'nasan, ityadi, ityadi, ityadi.

Khorench amkam itle mon'yache dosh astat, ki amkam zati pallni asleari ani ulovochchim hojar jibhi asleari, ami tankam nit'toll korun sangonk pavonam.

Mhunnuch, mhunnche adim sanglam tem vachun, ami nishedatmok vicharankodden ekvottit zatam mhunn anapekshit asa.

Karonn karonn nastana porinnam sambhovona, ami bhoktiponnan sangtam ki vichar svotach svata-utponn korun koxech utponn zaunk shokona.

Vichar korpi ani vicharanchim zhogddim ujwaddak yetat; dor ek nishedatmok vichar ek vegvegllea vichar korpeachean utponn zata.

Dor ekant nishedatmok vichar korpi itle astat kiteak nishedatmok vichar itlech astat.

"Vichar korpi ani vicharanchi" hi vostu vegvegllea angonamni dekhun gheun, dor ek "hanv je amche psykhisant bhorun asat, tem khorench ek vegvegllo vichar korpi asa.

Khorench dor eka amche bhitor, zaitench vichar korpi astat; punn, dor ek zonn thoddeach bhag asleari, ek pavtim to soglle mhunn somzota.

Mithyant visvas dovornare, ahonkari, narsist, udvegnare kadinch "Vicharanche anekponanchem" totv many korchonant kiteak te apleak khub mog kortat, tankam "tarzanacho bapui" vo "pillyanchem auy" mhunn dista.

Kosle ashakt lok koshem hi vegllicheni budhd asa mhunn chinttolle?

Punn te apnnak vishesh mhunn chintun vishvasani ani vinmraponn dakhoin Aristippochem angrakhem ghalun bhonvtat.

Shetkantlo kallo sangta ki Aristippon viswasni ani vinmraponn dakhoipache aslelem mhunn tanne fattlem, purna angrakhem bhonvdak ghalun athant darshanachi bott dhorun Athenachea rostoilean bhonvle.

Socratesan taka yetalo dekhun, tanne zorn darun ulo marun mhunllem "oh Aristippa, tuje udvegnni tuka torantlean dista!"

Zonn sodanch nove lokshat, nove samjonnent jiyena ani chintun ami chinttam mhunn somzota, tor tor tor to koslyai nishedatmok vicharankodden ekvottit zata.

Hache vorvim, to "Nishedatmok Hanv"che shaktek boll dita, toch tea vicharacho lekhok asa.

Kitle ami ek nishedatmok vicharankodden ekvottit zata, title ami "Hanv" che golam zatam.

Gnosisank, gupit margank, apleacher kam korpak, amchea khasgi uttejonnanchi thoddi "Hanv" zann Gnosisank, esoterik kamak dvesh kortat, kiteak tankam mahit asa ki amchea psykhisant tankam Gnosisani marunk sambhovta ani kam korun.

Tech "Nishedatmok Hanv" amchea boudhdik kendrant bhorlolea vicharancher kabzo kortat ani nuksan korpi vichar utponn kortat.

Zatle ami te vichar, te "Nishedatmok Hanv" zonn ek pavtim amchem boudhdik kendr niyontrit kortat, many kortat tor ami tankam soddunk somorth zaunche na.

Kadinch visronacho nhoi ki dor ek "Nishedatmok Hanv" "Svata-Thokaita" ani "Thokaita", nimanno uloup: Khorem ulouna.

Dor pavtim amkam sakshik bollachi khobrakhobit kami disonam ten'na, zonn shiknnar Nirash zata, Gnosisancho, esoterik kamacho, zonna tannem ut'sah haraita ani borem asa te soddta, punn koslo-i nishedatmok Hanv taka thokaita.

"Vyabhicharacho Nishedatmok Hanv", povitr ghara utponn korta ani bhurgeank durbhagiche kortat.

"Dveshacho Nishedatmok Hanv", mog dhorpeank thokaita ani tankanchim boremponn nash korta.

"Guhy Obhimanacho Nishedatmok Hanv" margachea bhoktank thokaita ani te zannkar zaun Guruchench dvesh kortat vo taka dukh dita.

Nishedatmok Hanv amchea svata anubhovank, amchea yadink, amchea ut'tom ichtank, amchea nisthanchean ulo korta, ani, heachem nit'tollch niyojon korun, koslich vostu fottkire ujwaddant dakhaita, ten'na ti mohonnak dita ani apnnak aplo nash dista.

Punn, zonna "Hanv"chea kamachea vellar zanna zata, ten'na to sadan loksh dhorun jiyetat ten'na thokaitponn asambhov zata.
