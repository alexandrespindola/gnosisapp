Ek monis aplo jivit koxem asta toxo asta, eka monxan apnache bhitor kainch bodol korina zalear, tannem aplo jivit mulant bodol korina zalear, tannem apnacher kam korina zalear, to aplo vell dukhdasponnan piraaita.

Moron mhunnttlear apla jivitantlea survatecher portun ieun to portun korunk melltta.

Pustokamni psevdo-esoterik ani psevdo-okultist vixoyancher bhov sanglam, punn portun portun ieun jivita vixoyancher amchea dhyan divop chodd borem.

Dor eka monxachem jivit, tem kitlichem-i assona, tem dor ek jivitacher portun portun yetat, anant kallam poriant.

Khoinchoi sondeho nastana ami amchea bhurgeanchea bijant choltut ravtat; hem sid'dh zalam.

Dor eka monxachem jivit, ek cholchitr asa, je morna vellar ami anantak gheun vetat.

Dor ek monis aplem cholchitr gheun veta ani tem portun novea jivitachea podenner dakhoupa khatir haddtta.

Dukham, vinodi ani trasdeik ghoddnneo portun portun ghoddop hem punaravruti niyamachem ek mukhel tatv asa.

Dor novea jivitant, teoch poristhiti portun portun yetat. Te scene korun dakhoupi teoch monxam zatat je amchea bhitor ravtat, te "hanv".

Zoxim natakant kam korpi monxam astana scene ghoddttat toxem, punn zannuch je natakant kam korpi monxam vo "hanv" asat tanchi ami asktakai keli zalear, dor jivitant ghoddnneo portun portun ghoddop xokyoch na.

Natakant kam korpi monis nastana scene ghoddona; hem khorem asa, hem khonddon korunk zaina.

Tech porim ami portun portun ieupachea ani punaravruti niyamancher azad zata; oxem ami khorem azad zata.

Khoinchoi dubav nastana dor ek monis (hanv) je amchea bhitor asat, te dor eka jivitant apaplem kam korun dakhouitat; punn zannuch ami tea natakant kam korpeank marle zalear, tench scene thambta.

Punaravruti niyamacher chintun vo dor jivitant ghoddnneo portun portun yetat teamcher ami dhyan dilear, ami aplea bhitor sodun kaddttat ki hem koxem ghoddtta.

Fattim gel'lea jivitant ami pondravis vorsanche astana, eka mogachi ghoddnni ghoddloli aslear, khoinchoi dubav nastana "hanv" tea sangatik novea jivitant pondravis vorsanche astana sodtat.

Tea bailak fattim pandra vorsam asloli zalear, ti bail aplea mogiak novea jivitant tech vellar sodtat.

Tech porim "hanv" aniekachea tonddan aikun portun mellttat ani fattim ghoddlolem tech portun korun mogachem jivit jieitat...

Don dusman je fattim mornachea vellar zhogddle, te novea jivitant aplem dukh portun korun dakhoupa khatir tech vellar portun mellttat.

Fattim don monxamni zomnichea babtint challis vorsanche astana zhogddim kel'lim zalear, te novea jivitant tech vellar portun mellttat ani tech portun korun dakhouitat.

Dor eka monxachea bhitor bhov monxam asat zanchem jivit poripurnn zall bhandhun asta; hem khorem asa.

Ek chor aplea bhitor bhov choranchea sangatak aplem chorichem kam choloiita. Ek khuni aplea bhitor khuniancho sangat gheun cholta ani ek lobhi aplea monan asktakechi "bhettochi kudd" gheun cholta.

Hantunt dusra vixeanchi garai mhunnttlear amchi bud'dh amchea bhitor asloli hi monxam "hanv" ani teamchea kamank vollkhonach na.

Hi soglli monxam amchea kallzak khalter kam kortat.

Hi vostu ami vollkhonach na, ti amchea sangata ghoddtta, hem subconsciente ani inconsciente bhitor ghoddtta.

Khorench ami aikolam ki hem sogllem ami kartat toxem vo pavs poddtta toxem vo viz poddtta toxem zata.

Khorench ami kartat mhunn dissta, punn ami kainch korunach, hem ghoddtta, hem mechani asa...

Amchi vakyaktitv mhunnttlear ek sadhon asa jechea udexim tea monxank (hanv) aplea kamank korun zatta.

Amchea bud'dhichea khaltar bhov vostu ghoddttat, punn durbhagyan amchi bud'dh vollkhonach na.

Ami khub xanne mhunn dakhounn kortat punn ami kitench zonchonam mhunn vollkhonach na.

Ami dukhi lakddache tukdde asat, je jivitantlea dolyanchea udkan vhallon vetat.

Hea durbhagya thavn bhair ieunk, hea agyani, dukhdasponnachea stithint ami asat titli thavn bhair ieunk, fakt ami bhitor moropachem poddtta...

Amchem bhitor moropachem nastana ami koxem zagrut zatle? Mornanuch noveponn yeta! Bij mortach zadd zata.

Kon konn khorench zagrut zata taka aplea monacher niyantronn asta, borea uzvaddan ani sontosachea jivitant jiyeunk zata...
