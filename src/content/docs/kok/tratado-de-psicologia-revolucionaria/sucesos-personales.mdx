Svatah-antarmukhi purnn nirikshann, chukiche mansik sthiti sodun kaddpache aslear telna thavn mhunn zaupachi garaz asa.

Nisandeh chukiche antrik sthiti yogya paddhtin sudharunk zatat.

Antrik jivit he bhayli ghoddnuka akarshit kortat, amkam chukiche mansik sthiti amche psique thavn kadpachi urgent garaz asa.

chukiche mansik sthiti sudharop hem mahatvachem jeva tum certain anist ghoddnuka fundamental rupan bodlopak sodtat.

certain ghoddnuka sangat amchea sombond bodlopak shokya zatat tum amchea bhitor certain mansik sthiti kadlekar.

nashak bhayli paristhiti nirupadravi zatat vo constructive zatat chaturai-en chukiche antrik sthiti sudharlekar.

tum ghoddnuka svabhav bodlunkat je tumka ghoddta jeva tum antarangan shudh zatat. konn kadinch mansik sthiti sudharina, bhav martan apon bhov zoracho asa mhunn ani paristhiticho bali zaupa zata.

amchea antrik ghara utar korop hem mahatvachem jeva tum nirdai jivitachi dik bodlpak dakoita.

lok soglle vixayant dukhat, raddtat, virodh kortat, jivit bodlpak sodtat, durbhagyan tench svata sangata kam korina.

lokanka dista ki antarangan bhayli paristhiti akarshit kortat ani ti dukhdi asllyan chukiche antarangan asata.

bhayli hem fakt antrikachem pratibimb, konn antarangan bodolta tench nov utar karant.

bhayli ghoddnuka kadinch mahatvache asonk nant, toxem pratikriya ditanant.

tum santi asle tumka apman kellyan? tum svagat kele tumchea bhavananche manfestação nashtikar?

tum koxem pratikriya dile jeva zann sangatan infidelidade keli? tum zallichicho vis ghetlo? tum marle? tum bandkhannent asat?

hospital, kabrastan vo panteon, bandkhano, hem bhole-bhadke lokani bhorlele jeani absurd rupant pratikriya dile bhayleo ghoddnukank.

soglleant boro hatyar zo dadlyak jivit dhorunk zata ti yogya mansik sthiti.

Konak pichai kadunk zata ani dusmanacho bogo udghadunk zatam yogya antarangan estado dhorlekar.

chukiche antarangan estado amkam asurkhit victim kortat manxi dusmanponna sangata.

jivitachye sagleant nashtikar ghoddnukank yogya antarangan attitude dhorun bhetunk xika...

konache sangata ektach zau naka; yaad dovrat ki sogllem veta; jivit filma rupan polleunk xika ani tumka labh melltole...

visrunaka nirarthak ghoddnuka tumka dukha votauk zatat, tum chukiche antarangan estado tumcha psique thavn kadina zalear.

dor ek bhayli ghoddnukaka yogya ticketachi garaz asa; mhunnje yogya mansik sthiti.
