Nirikshonn, vincharnn ani am'mllo, hem visržonachem tin mull ghottok asat.

Poilem: nirikshonn korchem. Dusrem: vincharnn korchem. Tisrem: am'mllo korchem.

Zhuzant guptcharancher poilim nirikshonn kortat; dusrem tancho vincharnn kortat; tisrem tankam gullyam marun jivexim martat.

Ontor-som'bondhant svota-sod ani svota-probhavo aasa. Zo konn aplench so-manxann sovem jiyevpak soddtta, to svota-soddkuy soddtta.

Jinnent ghodpi kitemuy ghodnem kitlench nirlakshachem dislem tori, khuim tori amchea bhitor ek gupt nattki aasa, ek mon'achea adharacho, ek "Mi".

Svota-sod tea vellar xokye aasa jevna ami xatrek zavun bosun astam, xatrek nothankayent.

"Mi", chori kortana zoddlelem, tem amchea tonddkant, kallzant ani lingant kalljikayen polleunk zai.

Kamsuktechem Mi kallzant mog mhonnun, tonddkant ek ut'tom vichar mhonnun ugddapem zaunk xokta, punn jevna ami lingak lokx diunk lagtam, tanka khuimsulem ut'tejon chukun disunk pavta.

Konnachea Mi-chekui vincharnn nixchit zaunk zai. Ami taka gunhegara zagear bosoun nirdayeponnan vincharnn korunk zai.

Kitemuy bachavo, nyay divpachi, vijechnn, durlokx korunk zai, jevna ami khorench zann zaunk sodhtam "Mi" je amkam amchea monantlean kaddunk asa.

Am'mllo veglllo aasa; kiteak tor ek "Mi"-chek kitemui am'mllo korunk xokye naslelem, tem adim nirikshonn ani vincharnn kortam.

Monoxastrachea kamant magnnem visržonnakhatir khub mhotvachem. Amkam monapek unch bollachi goroz aasa, jevna ami khorench hem vo tem "Mi" nirmull korunk ut'suk aasam.

Mon aplench konnachea Mi-chekui nirmull korunk xokona, hem sandean bher naslelem, tharavicho aasa.

Magnnem mhollear Devakodden uloyp. Ami amchea Dev-Maimchea bhitortekhatir ulo korunk zai, jevna ami khorench "Mi" nirmull korunk ut'suk aasam, zo konn aplea Maiek mog korina, putravo naslelo pul, aplench kamant hartelea.

Amchea zann ekleachea Dev-Mai particular aasa, individual, ti aplench Ek Seracha ek bhag aasa, punn dervhaichem.

Puranne lokanche zann Dev-Maiek bhitortekhatir bhajtalem. Sasnachea bailechea mullak ISIS, MARÍA, TONANZIN, CIBELES, REA, ADONIA, INSOBERTA, adi, adi, adi mhontat.

Fokot fyzikek amkam Pai ani Mai aasa, zagear amchea bhitoruk amkam amcho Pai je guptant asa ani amchi Dev-Mai KUNDALINI aasa.

Sorgar itlen Pai aasa itlen monx zemeler aasa. Dev-Mai amchea bhitortekhatir amchea Paiachi bailechi disnni je guptant aasa.

To ani Ti khorench amchea apovachea Bhitortekachea don voir bhag aasat. Sandean bher naslelem To ani Ti amchem Ser Real aasa "Mi" pek monoxastrachem.

To Ti-nt ugddapem zata ani hukum dita, cholta, xikxon dita. Ti avanchi vortunk naslele ank amchea bhitoruk vhorun kaddta, kamachea lagunk aplea voir.

Jevna ami murkh zalelim, jevna sogle vortunk naslele ank kaddle uprant khub kamam kortoch ani dukhk kortoch ami "PAI-MAI" ekvottunk ani melltolim, tednam ami Dev zatolim bhayanok Deivachem, voitichem ani nollechem bhair.

Amchi Dev-Mai particular, individual, aplea bollachem uzo lavun konnachea"Mi" kitemui dhumallo korun poddounk xokta, je adim nirikshonn ani vincharnn kelea.

Ek khas formulo amchea bhitortele Dev-Maiek magnnem korunk goroz na. Ami khub sovik ani sadhem zaunk zai Tichek ulo dita jevna. Ek pul aplea maiek ulo dita, tachekodden konnicheo khas formulo nastam, tachea kallzantlean kitem bhair yeta tem sangta ani titlench.

Konnchem Mi velvelikayen dison vochina; amchea Dev-Maiek kam korunk zai ani dukhk kortoch konnachea Mi-che nasti korunk.

Apleak bhitor korun, magnnem bhitruk dakoit, amchea bhitoruk amche Divi Srimtichea sodhant asa ani vinovnint tum Tichekodden ulo korunk xokteleat. Tichek magnne korum taka visrong korteleak je tuvem adim nirikshonn ani vincharnn kela.

Bhitrukea nirikshonnachem motem, tumchi kamak vaddo ditam tuji kamache proghrese vicharak gheun.

Somzonni, bodh hi fundamemtal, punn titli gorjechi aasa je ami "MI-CHEK" nasti korunk sodhtat.

Monachem konnchech khuxalikarachem durbegun kortele, eke bhagintlem dusrea bhagint gheun veche, dakounche, lipounche, punn taka khorelponn badolunk xokona.

Ek "khas boll" gorjechem monepek voir, ek uzovachem boll konnchech khuxalikarachem rakh zatolem.

STELLA MARIS, amchi Divi Mai, tichekodden tem boll aasa, tichi konnchea monachea doshichekui poddi korunk xokta.

Amchi Divi Mai amchea bhitoruk jieti, dehpud, mogpud ani mon pud. Tichi svatan eke agneyeche boll monepe voir aasa.

Amchi Partikular Kosmik Mai, individal, Sabdhanai, Mog ani Boll dharta. Tint apovachek perfection aasa.

Bore udhes ani sodanch ti punn korpeant kitem dhenk na, kitem nashtele na.

Kitem dhenk nasteli mhonnun mhonnun "hanv kamsuktek zavchem na"; lasciviyache mi konnem punn ashtoli amchea monache talent.

Kitem dhenk nasteli mhonnun sadanichek punn punn "hanv bhiranto ghevchem na". Bhiranteche "Mi" ashtoli amchea maneche taleant.

Kitem dhenk nasteli mhonnun sadanichek punn punn "hanv lobhi zavchem na". Lobheche "Mi" ashtoli amchea monache taleant.

Kitem dhenk nasteli amkam svatan korun pormaneant lipunk vo konnem ghuvem ghevun jiete; "Mi" amchea bhitoruk ashtolem.

Konnche Anakarite guvent rigourus disapline divun eksatek zatele santachem ani svargeleache bhair ghetele, thoim tannim pollelim ani aikilim vostu manis lokan somzounk pavnachem na; tari punn "Mi" ashtolem amchea bhitoruk.

Bhesankh sandean mi d'esens aple mi-than eksapek divun rigourus disapline ani eksatekachi khuxi gheunka, punn punn khushi uprant, "MI" bhitruk yetolem.

Zankan eksatekache savek aavele, ego nasti kortam, sodhanch divle ami nivronn zadle amkam, svatan thukche amka mestri mhonnun ani sankat ghevnk pavtole.

Kodhich amchi bhashon zaiche miyastik arrobatament voroddh, eksatek voroddh ani sukh atmachem ek mi nastam.

Amchea tankache stresa dhakounk gorozasa ami minchea visranche nimane nivronne khater.

D'ensens konnachech disapline varti, savkavelem mi-than, ti punn punn tem korteli dhakoun uprant fizikachem mharan, eksatek korteli thodde vellakani firi yetolem jeeniachem lambiche bhitoruk, egochek.

Taka punn punn eks fisike vocho gorzechi voroddhi, techi jinni punn punn vortunk.

Zanne mistik je gheuvam Himalaya ghuveant, asiache moddh, punn sadhea lok zaleat dhartrek ani vortuk, punn tannanche potti pujta ani ghevta punn.

Soglichech praytnachi nivronne voroddh jevni vhodd dista, je dhenk dinavtam ami eego visronchi gorozichi aasa, ami mar zatelim.
