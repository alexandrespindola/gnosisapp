Ekdam navjat bhurgeak sundar ani mogall korta ti mhanje tacho Sar; to svatahach tachi khari vastavikata... Dor ek pranneant Sarachem samanya vikas chodd unnem aani survatichem asta...

Monxachem dor biologique niyamapramann vhodd zatat ani viksit zatat, punn asli sambhavitam Sarache khatir khub komi astat... Nibandit Sar konachi madad nastana khub komi vikasit zanv yeta...

Ughddasponan ani nirdharponan ammi sangtat ki Sarachem svatahunn ani nisargik vikas fokot poilea tin, char ani panch vorsank zanv yeta, mhonnge jivitachea poilea panthiek... Lokank dista ki Sarachem vikas ani vikasit sodanch ekmekank mellun zata, evolutionache paddtichim, punn Universal Gnosticism spastponan xikoiita ki he ashem ghoddona...

Sarachem vikas ani vikasit zanvche khatir, kainch khas ghoddunk zai, kainch novem korunk zai. Hanv svatahacher kamak ud'des korunk sodtam. Sarachem vikas fokot zannvik kamam ani khuxik dukham sangata sambhav asta...

Hem somzunk gorjechem ki him kamam dhandeache prosnank, bankank, voizukamank, ghara bandkamank, relve linicho sudharna vo officechea kamank ud'des korina... Hem kam dor eka monxa khatir asa zannem vyaktimatv vikasit kela; hem kainch manasik asa...

Ami soglleank mahit asa ki amchea bhitor asat tem mhanje EGO, HANV, MHAKA, SVATAH... Durbhagyach Sar EGOchea bhitor band zala, gherlela ani hem dukhdik. Manasik HANV virghallunk, tachim anavshyak ghatkam virghallunk jaldichem asa, thambunk zonam, nivaarunk zonam... ashem svatahacher kamacho arth asa. Ami manasik HANV virghallaitona Sar koxench mukt korunk shakona...

Sarant Dhorm asa, BUDDHA, Zannvai, amchea Bapak sorgar asta tache dukhache kon ani soglli mahiti ji amkam SVATAH-SAKSHATKARACHE khatir ZAI. Konnui manasik HANV virghallunk shakona bhitorlea amanus ghatkam virghallaitona...

Amkam eka rakhak he kallchea krur rakshasponak dovrunk zai: irsha ji durbhagyach karvai zaunk pavlea; asahya lobh zannen jivit khub koddok kela: tiraskarachi ninda; ninda ji khub trasanchem mul karan zata; pirekai; ghanerdi kamukta ji itli vaitt vas marita; vagera vagera vagera.

Jashin him soglli ghennani vostu dhuliche zata, Sar mukt zata ani vikasit zata ekdam shantitayen... Nibandit zankai manasik HANV morta tedna Sar amchea bhitor ujed ditat...

Mukt Sar amkam bhitorli sundorta dita; asle sundortekhun purn sukh ani kharem mog yetat... Sarak khub purnachi dik ani asadharan nisargik xakti astat... Jedna ami "Svatah mortat", jedna ami manasik HANV virghallttat, tedna ami moladik dik ani Sarache xakti bhogtat...
