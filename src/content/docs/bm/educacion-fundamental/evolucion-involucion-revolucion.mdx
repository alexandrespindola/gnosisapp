An ka kan ka a lajɛ, kalanso minnu bɛ Fɛnnafiliya dege, ani kalanso minnu bɛ Hakilifiliya dege, olu bɛɛ jɔnjɔnnen don Tɛmɛsirali kama dogo ma.

Hakili kura minnu bɛ mɔgɔ sɔrɔli ni a Tɛmɛsirali ɲɛmɛntɔ kan, olu bɛɛ dɔrɔn ye Hakilintiɲɛ ye min man gbɛlɛn, a tɛ se ka jɔ gɛlɛyaw kan.

Hali Darwin ka miiriya bɛɛ n’a ta, minnu sɔnnen don i n’a fɔ laadalakow ye Karl Marx fɛ ani a ka Fɛnnafiliya-kalan min bɛ tɔgɔla kosɔbɛ, alimami kura si tɛ foyi dɔn mɔgɔ sɔrɔli kan, foyi tɛ ɲɛnajɛ olu ye, olu si ma foyi ɲɛnajɛ a yɛrɛ fɛ, ani olu tɛ se ka kunnafoni tigitigi sɔrɔ Mɔgɔya tɛmɛsirali kan.

Kɔrɔbɔra, n’an ye mɔgɔya tariku ta, o ye ko kɛlenw ye minnu kɛra san kɛmɛ mugan walima san kɛmɛ saba Jeesu Krisiti ɲɛ, an bɛ kunnafoni tigitigi sɔrɔ, taamasiyɛn minnu tɛ se ka ɲɛnɛma ɲɛmɔgɔya sifa la, minnu tɛ se ka faamu mɔgɔ kura fɛ, ani minnu bɛ se ka yira seereya caman fɛ, Hieroglifu kɔrɔw, Piramidi kɔrɔw, monɔliti jumanw, papiru gundo minnu ani yɛrɛmahɔrɔnyaw tariku kɔrɔw.

Mɔgɔ ɲɛmɛntɔ ka ko la, gundo danfaralen minnu bɛ ɲɛnamalaya hakiliman cogoya la, nka olu danfaralen don kosɔbɛ, gundo kosɔbɛ, ani a jaati minnu dafalen don gundo la, olu bɛ dafalen don yɔrɔ kɔrɔw la a tɛmɛɲɛgɛn tuma la walima a ɲɛmɛɲɛgɛn tuma la, alimami kura si tɛ foyi dɔn a yɛrɛ fɛ ani a ɲɛnajɛ fɛ.

Gnositiya Sɛnsɛnniya bɛ a dege ko Hakili Bagantɛ minnu dɔnnen don an fɛ, olu tɛ Ko Dafalenw ye, olu tɛ Mɔgɔ ye fɔlɔ kuma dafalen na; ɲɛnamalaya b’a bɔ ɲɛ, ka a to ɲɛnamalaya bɛɛ la walisa ka taa a ɲɛ walima ka a seko bɛɛ tɔɔrɔ ka bɔ a la.

Tɛmɛsirali ni ŋaniya sariyaw ye ɲɛnamalaya bɛɛ taamasiyɛn ye ani olu tɛ foyi ye ani An yɛrɛ sɔrɔli.

Hakili Bagantɛ kɔnɔ, seko gɛlɛnw bɛ yen minnu bɛ se ka bɔ walima ka tɔɔrɔ, a tɛ sariya ye ko a bɛ bɔ. Tɛmɛsirali tɛ se ka olu bɔ.

Seko ɲininenw bɔli, a bɛ se ka kɛ cogoya dɔw dɔrɔn na minnu tigitigi don ani o bɛ kan ka Bange-gɛlɛya gɛlɛnw kɛ yɛrɛkunnata minnu kan ani dɛmɛ ɲuman min bɔra Karamɔgɔ minnu fɛ minnu y’o baara kɛ tɛmɛnnen na.

Mɔgɔ min bɛ a seko ɲininenw bɛɛ bɔ walisa ka kɛ mɔgɔ ye, o bɛ kan ka don Hakili ɲɛnamali sira kan.

Hakili Bagantɛ ye Kaba ye, Bin ye; o bin fɛ, ɲɛnamaya jiri bɛ se ka sɔrɔ, Mɔgɔ Tidɛ, Mɔgɔ min Diogenes tun bɛ ɲini lamini min bɛ jɛgɛn Atene sira kan ani tileɲɛ, a ma se ka a sɔrɔ.

A tɛ sariya ye ko o bin, o bin min tigitigi don a bɛ se ka bɔ, o ye ko nɔgɔn ye, a ɲɛnamalaya ye ka tɔɔrɔ.

Mɔgɔ Tidɛ danfaralen don Hakili Bagantɛ la, i n’a fɔ Sanji danfaralen don Sankolo la.

Ni bin ma sa, bin tɛ bɔ, a bɛ kan, a ka gɛlɛn ka Ego sa, Ne, Ne yɛrɛ, walisa Mɔgɔ ka sɔrɔ.

Karamɔgɔw ani Karamɔgɔmusow Kalanso ni Kolɛziw ni Karamɔgɔso kɔlɔnw, olu bɛ kan ka Etiya ɲɛnamali sira dege ɲɛmɔgɔw ye, o dɔrɔn bɛ se ka Ego saya sɔrɔ.

N’an bɛ dɔ fara kan, an bɛ se ka a fɔ ko Hakili ɲɛnamaliya tɛ yɛrɛraɲɛ ye diɲɛ kɔnɔ, nka a bɛ kɛ yɛrɛraɲɛ ye waati bɛɛ.

Hakili ɲɛnamaliya bɛ sababu saba ye minnu tigitigi don: Fɔlɔ, ka Sa; Filanan, ka Sɔrɔ; Sabanan, ka yɛrɛ di ɲɛnamalaya kama. Sababu tɛ foyi kɛ.

Saya ye Etiya ɲɛnamali ko ye ani Hakili Hakili Bagantɛ sababu kɛlɛ.

Sɔrɔ ye Sigilansɛnko Tɛmɛsirali ko ye, o ko bɛ tali kɛ Sigilansɛnko tɛmɛsirali la, mɔgɔ min bɛ o ko kalanko, o bɛ kan ka sɛbɛn ka an Gnositiya gafe dɔn.

Yɛrɛ di ɲɛnamalaya kama ye ɲumanya diɲɛ bɛɛ kɔnɔ ye.

Ni an ma Hakili ɲɛnamali ɲini, ni an ma Bange-gɛlɛya gɛlɛnw kɛ walisa ka seko ɲininenw bɔ minnu bɛna an bila An yɛrɛ sɔrɔli la, a ka ca ko o seko tɛna bɔ.

Mɔgɔ caman tɛ yen minnu bɛ yɛrɛ sɔrɔ, minnu bɛ kisi ani yɛrɛben ma kɛ a la, mun na Hakili Bagantɛ tɔɔrɔlen bɛna min sɔrɔ min tɛ a ɲini?

A bɛ kan ka yɛlɛma dafalen kɛ nka Mɔgɔ bɛɛ tɛ o yɛlɛma ɲini, olu tɛ a ɲini, olu tɛ a dɔn ani olu bɛ fɔ ani olu tɛ a faamu, a tɛ olu dɛmɛ. Mun na a bɛ kan ka di olu ma minnu tɛ a ɲini?

Tiɲɛ ye ko a ɲɛ, mɔgɔ ka seko kura sɔrɔ walima Fanga kura minnu tɛ a dɔn ani minnu tɛ a fɛ, a bɛ kan ka seko ni fanga sɔrɔ minnu a bɛ miiri ka sɔrɔ nka minnu tɛ a fɛ.
