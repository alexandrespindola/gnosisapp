Hakilakow ye fɛn minnu ye ani minnu mɛn, minnu kalan, mɔgɔ wɛrɛw ye minnu fɔ an ye, minnu kɛra an kan, i n’a fɔ ɲɔgɔn na, olu filɛ walasa ka u mara hakili la.

Karamɔgɔw b’a fɛ u ka kalandenw ka u ka kuma, u ka sɛbɛnniw, minnu sɛbɛnnen bɛ kalanko taabolo kan, dakun bɛɛ, baara gɛlɛnw, ni u ka pointiw ni u ka komaw bɛɛ, i n’a fɔ ɲɔgɔn na, ka u mara hakili la.

Jaabiw tɛmɛni kɔrɔ ye ka minnu fɔ an ye, minnu kalan mekanikiya la, ka hakili kan, ka ɲɛnajɛ fɔ i n’a fɔ jaamɔgɔw, lorow walima kotɔrɔw, minnu bɛɛ mara hakili la.

A wajibiyalen don ko dugukolo kura ka a faamu ko ka ɲɛnajɛ i n’a fɔ radiokɔnsoli disiki ye sɛbɛnniw bɛɛ minnu kɛra hakili la, o tɛ kɔrɔ ka a faamu kosɛbɛ. Hakili tɛ faamu, a tɛ nafa sɔrɔ hakili la ka faamu tɛmɛ, hakili ye tɛmɛnen ta ye, a ye fɛn min sara, fɛn min tɛ ɲɛnama ye.

A wajibiyalen don, a gɛlɛnnen don ani a ka surun kosɛbɛ bi ko lakɔliw, kolejiw ani iniwersitew kalandenw bɛɛ ka faamuya gbɛlɛn kɔrɔ faamuya bonya.

FAAMUYA ye fɛn ye min bɛ kɛ yɛrɛyɛrɛ, a jɛlen don, fɛn minnu bɛ an ɲɛnama ka ca, fɛn minnu bɛ an kɔnɔ ka ca ani minnu bɛ kɛ hɔrɔnya la yɛrɛyɛrɛ i n’a fɔ ɲɛnama sɔrɔli gundo yɛrɛ.

Hakilakow, hakilisigi ye fɛn min sara, a ye tɛmɛnen ta ye ani nɛgɛɲɛmɛn na, a bɛ kɛ kɔrɔtɔ, lɛmɛ, miiriya, nɛgɛɲɛmɛn minnu bɛ an sɔndi mekanikiya la ani ka tugu a kɔ inkońsiyanɛlɛman.

FAAMUYA TƆGƆLEN NA, faamuya gbɛlɛn na, faamuya bonya na, ɲɛnama kɔnɔntan bɛ hakili la, ɲɛgɛlɛnni min bɛ bɔ gundo la minnu bɛ an kɔnɔ ani o ye fɛn bɛɛ ye.

Faamuya tɔgɔlen bɛ jira i n’a fɔ baara yɛrɛmahɔrɔnya, nɔgɔlen, yɛrɛmahɔrɔnya, min bɔlen ɲɛgɛlɛnni gɛlɛnw na; a saniya don siɲɛ caman tɛ. FAAMUYA min kɛra ɲɛnama SECRETO ye, o ka ɲi, a kabakoba don, a bɛ bonya ani a ka kan ka bonya kosɛbɛ.

Baara min sinsinnen bɛ minnu kalan hakili kan, ɲɛnajɛ minnu bɛ an bolo, sariya, ɲɛnabɔ minnu bɛ an kalan, ɲɛnama minnu bɛ mara hakili la, i n’a fɔ ɲɔgɔn na, o ye calculator ye, a sinsinnen bɛ ɲɛgɛlɛnni gɛlɛn kan, a ye dualista ye, a sinsinnen bɛ ɲɛgɛlɛnni konsɛpɔrɛl kan ani a bɛ tugu ɲɛgɛlɛnw ni gɛlɛya kɔ.

O ka baara dilan hakili ma, o ka baara yɛlɛma walasa a ka bɛn hakili la, o ye fɛn ye minnu kɛra, a gɛlɛn don, a tɛ nafa sɔrɔ ɲɛnama na ani o bɛ an tugu ɲɛgɛlɛnw ni gɛlɛya kɔ.

O ka jaabiw tɛmɛ, o ka san kɛ, o bɛɛ bɛ kɛ mɔgɔ minnu hakili ka ɲi.

Ka fɛnw faamu minnu kalan ani minnu bɛ jaabiw tɛmɛ, o ye fɛn ye min ka ca, a tɛ bɛn hakili ma, a ye hakili ɲuman ta ye min tɛ kan ka tɔgɔwɛrɛ hakilikanbaani.

Mɔgɔ minnu bɛ fɛn bɛɛ sinsin ɲɛnama la nɛgɛɲɛmɛnw, teoriw ani hakiliw bɛɛ minnu bɛ mara hakili la, u bɛ taama ɲɔgɔnna ni ɲɔgɔnna bɛɛ kan ani yɔrɔ minnu ɲɔgɔnna bɛ, o yɔrɔ la miiriya bɛ. O mɔgɔw bɛ u ka mɔgɔw, u ka denbaya, u ka denw ɲɔgɔnna ni sigida denw ka denw, ni sigida mɔgɔw ye. U bɛ u ka so, u ka fɛnw, u ka finiw, u ka fɛnw bɛɛ ɲɔgɔnna ni sigida ka fɛnw walima sigida tɔgɔw. U bɛ u ka miiriyaw, u ka denw ka hakili ɲɔgɔnna ni mɔgɔw ka miiriyaw, ni mɔgɔw ka hakili ye ani miiriya bɛ na min bɛ kɛ ɲɛnama gundo ye.

Dugukolo gɛlɛya kosɔn jamana bɛɛ mekanikiya sinsinnen bɛ miiriya kan ani hakilikanbaani. Dunia bɛɛ bɛ miiriya. An bɛ miiriya, fɛnw, mɔgɔw ani an bɛ fɛ ka warika sɔrɔ ani warika ka ca, teoriw kura, miiriyaw kura minnu bɛ mara hakili la, fɛnw kura walasa ka an ɲɔgɔnw ɲɛnama, i n’a fɔ ɲɔgɔn na.

FAAMUYA TƆGƆLEN NA, tigɛlɛn, tɔgɔlen, ɲɛnama yɛrɛyɛrɛ, kanuya yɛrɛyɛrɛ bɛ yen ani minnu bɛ hakili kan.

Fɛnw minnu bɛ hakili la, minnu bɛ mara hakili la, u bɛ ɲɛnafiɲɛ kosɛbɛ sabu hakili tɛ jaati ye. Kalandenw bɛ mara hakili la, nɛgɛɲɛmɛnw, teoriw, sɛbɛnnibɛɛ minnu tɛ nafa sɔrɔ ɲɛnama la sabu a laban na u bɛ ɲɛnafiɲɛ hakili la ka a to a tɛ dɔnniyaka ye.

Mɔgɔ minnu bɛ ɲɛnama kalan ani ka kalan mekanikiya la, mɔgɔ minnu bɛ teoriw mara hakili la, u bɛ hakili halaki, u bɛ a tiɲɛ kosɛbɛ.

An tɛ fɔ ka kan ni kalan gbɛlɛn ani hakilintanya min sinsinnen bɛ faamuya kan. An bɛ cɛn pewagɔgiyɛ kɔrɔ donw na. An bɛ mekanikiya kalan bɛɛ cɛn, hakili bɛɛ, i n’a fɔ ɲɔgɔn na. Hakilakow bɛ bɔ yen yɔrɔ minnu faamuya yɛrɛyɛrɛ bɛ.

An ka kan ka kalan, gafe nafamaw bɛ kɛ, karamɔgɔw bɛ kɛ lakɔliw la, kolejiw, iniwersitew. GURÚ bɛ kɛ, ɲɛnabɔ tɔgɔw, mahatmɛw, i n’a fɔ ɲɔgɔn na, nka a ka kan ka kalan bɛɛ faamuya a bɛɛ la ani ka u mara hakili la.

An tɛ se ka yɛrɛmahɔrɔnya sɔrɔ fo ka an yɛrɛ ɲɔgɔnna ni hakili minnu bɛ mara, ni nɛgɛɲɛmɛn, ni minnu bɛ an bolo ka kɛ ani an tɛ, i n’a fɔ ɲɔgɔn na.

Ni an ye kalanw faamuya, an tɛ baara kɛ ni u ye hakili la, walima ka u yɛlɛma nɛgɛɲɛmɛnw ye.

Yɔrɔ minnu ɲɔgɔnna bɛ minnu bɛ an bolo cɛ ani sɔrɔya minnu bɛ an bolo ka kɛ a laban, yɔrɔ minnu ɲɔgɔnna bɛ an ka ɲɛnama ni nɛgɛɲɛmɛn minnu bɛ an bolo ka bɛn, kanuya tɛ se ka kɛ yen.

ɲɔgɔnna bɛɛ ka jugu, ɲɔgɔnna bɛɛ bɛ siran, miiriya, bonya, i n’a fɔ ɲɔgɔn na. Siran walasa ka minnu sɔrɔ, miiriya kosɔn sigida sɔrɔya, bonya sabu an bɛ an yɛrɛ laɲini ka ca ni mɔgɔw bɛ. Minnu ka kan ɲɛnama la minnu bɛ an bolo, an ka jugu, miiriya, hakilikanbaani, sɔrɔya, i n’a fɔ ɲɔgɔn na, a tɛ kan ka an yɛrɛ laɲini ka saniya, ka bɔ bɔn na, ani ka an yɛrɛ faamuya kosɛbɛ, i n’a fɔ minnu bɛ an bolo ani minnu bɛ an bolo ka kɛ.

A tɛ se ka YO, MI MISMO, halaki ni an ma kalan ka an yɛrɛ filɛ, ka a faamu minnu bɛ an bolo cɛ ani bi a ka kan ka kɛ.

Ni an bɛ fɛ ka faamu, an ka kan ka an karamɔgɔw lamɛn, gurúw, sarakalasebaw, ɲɛnabɔla tɔgɔw, i n’a fɔ ɲɔgɔn na.

Sungurunninw, ani sunguru minnu bɛ na u bɛ ka bonya, ka janto an ka bɔw, karamɔgɔw, ɲɛnabɔla tɔgɔw, gurúw, mahatmɛw, i n’a fɔ ɲɔgɔn na, ɲɛnama kan.

A tɛ se ka kalanw faamuya ni an ma dɔn ka an ka bɔw bonya, karamɔgɔw walima ɲɛnabɔla tɔgɔw.

Hakili mekanikiya minnu bɛ kalan hakili la minnu bɛ bɔ kanuya la, u bɛ hakili ani kanu tiɲɛ ani u bɛ miiriya, siran, bonya, i n’a fɔ ɲɔgɔn na.

Ni an y’a dɔn ka lamɛn hakili la ani kosɛbɛ, fanga kabakoba bɛ bɔ an kɔnɔ, faamuya ka ɲi, nɔgɔlen, min bɔlen mekanikiya bɛɛ la, min bɔlen hakili bɛɛ la, min bɔlen hakili bɛɛ la.

Ni kalanden hakili bɔra hakili gbɛlɛn bɛɛ la min ka kan ka kɛ, a bɛ se ka hakili bolo ani ka tabila periyodiki dɔn kalanden fɔlɔw ye ani ka a faamu ko a ka kan ka Quanta dɔn bachiller ye.

I n’a fɔ an ye ɲɔgɔnfɔ ni lakɔliso karamɔgɔw dɔw ye, an bɛ a faamu ko u bɛ siran fanatisɛ kosɛbɛ pewagɔgiyɛ kɔrɔ donw na. U b’a fɛ kalandenw ka fɛn bɛɛ kalan hakili la hali ni u ma a faamu.

Tuma dɔw la u b’a sɔn ko a ka ɲi ka faamu ka hakili la, nka u bɛ sinsin ko fisiki fɔrmulɛw, sɛmɛwi, matematikiw, i n’a fɔ ɲɔgɔn na, ka kan ka sɛbɛn hakili la.

A jɛlen don ko konsɛpɔrɛ yɛrɛnman don sabu tuma min na fisiki fɔrmulɛ, sɛmɛwi, matematikiw, i n’a fɔ ɲɔgɔn na, faamuyalen kosɛbɛ, n’a ma kɛ hakili kan, nka hakiliw wɛrɛw kan i n’a fɔ hakilinan, subhakili, infrahakili i n’a fɔ ɲɔgɔn na. A tɛ kan ka sɛbɛn hakili la, a bɛ kɛ an ka hakili filen ye ani a bɛ se ka jira i n’a fɔ dɔnniya ɲuman tuma min na ɲɛnama bɛ a ɲini.

Dɔnniya bɛɛ bɛ na walasa ka OMNISIYENSA dɔnniya di an ma, ɲɛnabɔ dɔnniya gundo.

Faamuya kosɛbɛ ani hakili bɛɛ kan a bɛ se ka kɛ, mɛditasiyon inkońsiyanɛlɛman bɛɛ.
