Hakilakow ye ko ka gnɛgɛn ka minnu ye ani ka minnu mɛn, minnu an ye kalan, minnu mɔgɔ wɛrɛw ye fɔ an ye, minnu kɛra an kan, ani wɛrɛw, ani wɛrɛw, olu mara hakili la.

Karamɔgɔw b’a fɛ u kalandenw ka u kuma mara u hakili la, u kumaɲɛw, minnu sɛbɛnnen bɛ kalanko kɛnɛ kan, sɛbɛnɲɛ dafalenw, baarada gɛlɛnw, ni a point ni a virgule bɛɛ, ani wɛrɛw.

Ɛgzamɛnɛw tɛmɛni kɔrɔ ye ka minnu fɔ an ye olu hakili jigin, minnu an ye kalan mekanikiya la, ka hakili daɲɛ fɔ, ka tɛmɛsira ɲɛfɔ i n’a fɔ jaankɛkuluw, i n’a fɔ papegebu, walima kotɔrɔw, minnu bɛɛ an ye mara hakili la.

A mago bɛ sanu kura ka faamu ko ka tɛmɛsira ɲɛfɔ i n’a fɔ radiokɔnsoli disiki hakili la, minnu bɛɛ mara hakili la, o tɛ kɔrɔ ko an ye faamu a dafalen na. Hakilakow tɛ faamuyali ye, a nafa tɛ hakili jigin ka faamuya tɛmɛ, hakili jigin bɛ tɛmɛnenna, a ye fɛn min sanin, fɛn min tɛ ɲɛnamaya.

A wajibiyalen don, a ɲɛnajɛlen don ani a sinsinnen don k’a kɛ tuma bɛɛ kalandenw bɛɛ ye lakɔliw, kolejiw ani iniwersitew la k’a faamu tiɲɛ na faamuyali cɛnsiri kɔnɔ.

FAAMUYALI ye fɛn ye min bɛ kɛ yɛrɛyɛrɛ, a bɛ tigitigi, fɛn minnu bɛ ɲɛnamaya cɛnsiri la, fɛn minnu bɛ dɔn cɛnsiri la ani minnu bɛ kɛ tiɲɛ na k’a kɛ cogoya siranfɛn ye ɲɛnamaya ɲɛda.

Hakili jigin, hakili jigi ye fɛn min sanin, a bɛ tɛmɛnenna ani a bɛ kɛ nafa ye, a bɛ kɛ ɲɛfɔ ye, a bɛ kɛ miiriya ye, a bɛ kɛ miiriyajɛ ye minnu bɛ gnɛgɛn ka gnɛgɛn a mekanikiya la ani ka tɛmɛn hakilimaɲɛ.

FAAMUYALI TIGIYA LA, faamuyali cɛnsiri la, hakili jigin kɔnɔ, hakili jigin bɛna kɛ hakili jigin dɔrɔn ye, dɔrɔn dɔrɔn min bɛ bɔ an kɔnɔ ani o bɛɛ.

Faamuyali tigiya bɛ bɔ kɛwale yɛrɛyɛrɛ, n’a jɛlen, n’a nɔgɔlen, n’a yɛrɛmahɔrɔnyalen dɔrɔn kɛwale miɲɛnenna na; a sanin tɛ miiriya min bɛɛ la. FAAMUYALI min bɛ kɛ KƐWALE SIRANFƐN ye a bɛ tiɲɛ, a bɛ kabakɔrɔ, a bɛ so, ani a bɛ sɔgɔ tiɲɛ na.

Kɛwale min sinsinnen bɛ minnu an ye kalan hakili jigin kan, ɲɛfɔ minnu an bɛ gnɛgɛn ka sɔrɔ, sariya minnu, cogoya minnu an yɛ kalan, dɔn minnu mara hakili la, ani wɛrɛw, a ye kalaki ye, a bɛ miɲɛnenna gnɛgɛn, a ye ɲɔgɔnɲɔgɔn ye, a sinsinnen bɛ miiriya gnɛgɛn kan ani a bɛ tɛmɛn fili ani ɲɛnajɛ la.

K’a kɛ kɛwale ka hakili jigin, ka gnɛgɛn ka kɛwale yɛlɛma walisa a ka bɛn hakili jigin kan minnu bɛ mara hakili la, a ye fɛn min kɛlɛɲɔgɔnɲɔgɔn, fɛn min nɔgɔlen tɛ ani min tɛ se ka tɛmɛn fili ani ɲɛnajɛ la.

Ɛgzamɛnɛw tɛmɛni, sanu tɛmɛni, a bɛ kɛ hakili kunbɛn miɲɛnen min hakili ka ca.

Ka kunnafoni faamu minnu ye kalan ani minnu an bɛna ɛgzamɛnɛw tɛmɛn, a ye fɛn wɛrɛ ye, a tɛ hakili la, a bɛ hakili la min tɛ ɲɔgɔn ɲɔgɔn miiriya la.

Mɔgɔ minnu bɛ gnɛgɛn ka u ɲɛnamaya kɛwaliw bɛɛ sinsin ɲɛfɔw kan, teorijɛw ani hakili jiginw minnu bɛɛ bɛ mara hakili kɔnɔ, u bɛ tɛmɛn ɲɔgɔnɲɔgɔn kan ani ɲɔgɔnɲɔgɔn minnu bɛ yen, iɲɛɲɔgɔnya bɛ yen. U bɛ u yɛrɛ ɲɔgɔn ɲɔgɔn, u denbayaw, u denw, ni jiranw denw, ni jiranw. U bɛ u so ɲɔgɔn ɲɔgɔn, u fɛnw, u faniw, u fɛnw bɛɛ, ni jiranw fɛnw, walima jiranw, walima ɲɔgɔn. U bɛ u miiriya ɲɔgɔn ɲɔgɔn, u denw hakili, ni mɔgɔ wɛrɛw miiriya, ni mɔgɔ wɛrɛw hakili ani iɲɛɲɔgɔnya bɛna kɛ kɛwale siranfɛn ye.

Tiɲɛ kɔnɔ, diɲɛ mekaniki bɛɛ sinsinnen bɛ iɲɛɲɔgɔnya ani miiriya gnɛgɛn kan. Mɔgɔ bɛɛ bɛ mɔgɔ bɛɛ iɲɛ. An bɛ miiriya iɲɛ, fɛnw, mɔgɔw ani an bɛ gnɛgɛn ka warikɛ ani warikɛ ka ca, teorijɛw kura, miiriya kura minnu bɛ mara hakili la, fɛnw kura walisa ka an mɔgɔɲɔgɔnw ɲɛfɔ, ani wɛrɛw.

FAAMUYALI TIGIYA LA, tɔgɔ, tigiya, kanuya tigiya bɛ yen ani kanuya hakili daɲɛ dɔrɔn tɛ.

Fɛn minnu hakili bɛ jigi, minnu mara hakili la, a bɛ tɛmɛ tɔmɛni la sabu hakili tɛ tɔgɔye. Kalandenw bɛ ɲɛfɔw mara, teorijɛw, sɛbɛnɲɛ dafalen minnu tɛ nafa ɲɛnamaya la sabu a bɛ tɛmɛ hakili la ka tɔgɔ to hakili la.

Mɔgɔ minnu bɛ ɲɛnamaya kalan dɔrɔn ani ka kalan mekanikiya la, mɔgɔ minnu bɛ ɲɛnajɛ ka teorijɛw mara hakili kɔnɔ, u bɛ hakili halaki, u bɛ a minɛ tiɲɛ na.

An tɛ an dafɔ kalan cɛnsiri ani hakili dafalen kan min sinsinnen bɛ faamuyali kan. An bɛ hakili dafalen siratigɛ minnu tɛ cɛn tuma kan minnu bɛ kalan. An bɛ mekaniki kalan siratigɛ bɛɛ halaki, hakili mara bɛɛ, ani wɛrɛw. Hakili jigin bɛ bɔ ka bɔ kɔrɔ minnu faamuyalen don.

An mago bɛ ka kalan, gafe nafamaniw mago bɛ, karamɔgɔw mago bɛ lakɔliw, kolejiw, iniwersitew. GURU mago bɛ, siraɲɛfɔbaga sirusɛnw, mahatamaw, ani wɛrɛw, nka a mago bɛ ka kalanw faamu dafalen na ani ka mara hakili la.

An tɛ se ka kɛ yɛrɛmahɔrɔnyalen tiɲɛ na dumɛnni an bɛ ɲɛnajɛ ka an yɛrɛ ɲɔgɔn ɲɔgɔn hakili jigin min bɛ mara hakili la, ɲɛfɔ minnu, minnu an bɛ gnɛgɛn ka sɔrɔ ani minnu an tɛ, ani wɛrɛw.

Dumɛnni an yɛ kalanw faamu tiɲɛ na minnu sɔrɔlen don, an mago tɛ k’u hakili jigin hakili la, walima k’u kɛ ɲɛfɔw ye.

Ɲɔgɔnɲɔgɔn bɛ minnu an ye o ye yɛrɛ ani sisan ni minnu an bɛ gnɛgɛn ka kɛ a la tɛmɛ, ɲɔgɔnɲɔgɔn bɛ an ɲɛnamaya kɛwale ni ɲɛfɔwalima modɛli min an bɛ gnɛgɛn ka an yɛrɛ kɛ a la, kanuya tiɲɛ bɛ se ka kɛ.

Ɲɔgɔnɲɔgɔn bɛɛ ye tɔgɔje ye, ɲɔgɔnɲɔgɔn bɛɛ bɛ siran, iɲɛɲɔgɔnya, bonya, ani wɛrɛw. Siran walasa ka minnu an bɛ gnɛgɛn ka sɔrɔ, iɲɛɲɔgɔnya gnɛgɛnkan, bonya sabu an bɛ an yɛrɛ miiri ka bon mɔgɔ wɛrɛw kan. Fɛn minnu nafamani don ɲɛnamaya kɛwale la minnu an bɛ ɲɛnamaya, an ye ɲɛnajɛ, iɲɛɲɔgɔnyalen, bɔnɔni, ka gnɛgɛn ka kɛ sirusɛnw ye, ka bɔ siro dafalen, ani ka an yɛrɛ faamu cɛnsiri la, i n’a fɔ an ye ani i n’a fɔ an bɛ gnɛgɛn ka kɛ walima i n’a fɔ an bɛ miiri ka kɛ.

A tɛ se ka YO halaki, MI YƐRƐ, ni an ma kalan ka an ɲɛ tɔgɔ, ka dɔn walisa ka minnu an ye tiɲɛ na a yɛrɛ ani sisan kɛwale la ani a kɛwale bɛɛ.

Ni an bɛ gnɛgɛn ka faamu tiɲɛ na an ka an karamɔgɔw mɛn, karamɔgɔw, guruw, sarakalasɛbɛnw, ɲɛfɔbagaw, siraɲɛfɔbagaw, ani wɛrɛw.

Funankɛw, ani funankɛw sanu kura, u ye ka bonya ban, bonya ka an bɛnbaliw, karamɔgɔw, siraɲɛfɔbagaw, guruw, mahatamaw, ani wɛrɛw.

A tɛ se ka kalanw faamu dumɛnni an ma bonya ani ka bonya ka an bɛnbaliw, karamɔgɔw, ɲɛfɔbagaw walima siraɲɛfɔbagaw.

Hakili jigin mekanikiya la minnu an ye kalan hakili la faamuyali tɛmɛ, a bɛ hakili ni kanu yɛlɛma ani ka iɲɛɲɔgɔnya, siran, bonya, ani wɛrɛw.

Dumɛnni an bɛ dɔn ka mɛn cogoya siranfɛn ani cɛnsiri la a bɛ bɔ an kɔnɔ hakili kabakɔrɔ, faamuyali gɛlɛn, n’a jɛlen, n’a nɔgɔlen, a ye yɛrɛmahɔrɔnyalen mekaniki kɛwale bɛɛ la, a ye yɛrɛmahɔrɔnyalen hakili daɲɛ bɛɛ la, a ye yɛrɛmahɔrɔnyalen hakili jigin bɛɛ la.

Ni hakili tɛ gɛlɛn hakili kɔnɔ min kalanden bɛ gnɛgɛn ka a kɛ, a bɛ se ka dɔn hakili dafalen tigɛ walasa ka hakili di walisa ka kunnafoni di kalanw ka sanu filananw ani k’a kɛ ka faamu ka cɛɲi ani Kantaw ka bachelɔri ye.

I n’a fɔ an ye kumakɛ ni karamɔgɔ dɔw ni karamɔgɔw ye lakɔliw filananw an bɛ faamu ko a bɛ siran tiɲɛ na kanuya la kalan kɔrɔ min tɛ cɛn tuma kan. U bɛ gnɛgɛn kalandenw ka kalan hakili la fɔɔ ka faamu.

Tuma dɔw u bɛ sɔn ko a ka fisa ka faamu ka hakili mara nka olu bɛ sigida sin ka formulew de fizikɛ, kemi, matemati, ani wɛrɛw ka mara hakili la.

A jɛlen don o hakili ye fili ye sabu dumɛnni formule de fizikɛ, kemi, matemati, ani wɛrɛw, a faamuyalen don ko hakili kan dɔrɔn tɛ, nka hakili kan wɛrɛw i n’a fɔ hakiliɲɛ, hakilidafalen, hakilidafalenɲɛ ani wɛrɛw. A mago tɛ ka mara hakili la, a bɛna kɛ an psikisa yɛrɛ ye ani a bɛ se ka ɲɛfɔ dɔn siranfɛn yɛrɛyɛrɛ dumɛnni cogoya ɲɛnamaya na a bɛ gnɛgɛn.

Dɔn MIN FAAMUYALEN don bɛna an di cogoya de OMNISIƐNSI, cogoya ɲɛfɔ ka hakili dafalen.

Faamuyali kɔnɔ ani hakili kan bɛɛ a bɛ se ka kɛ ka miiri cɛnsiri la.
