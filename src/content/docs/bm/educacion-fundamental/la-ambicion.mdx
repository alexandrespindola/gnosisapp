HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA KA SABABU JATILEN DON, O MINNU DƆ YE SIRAN YE.

DENMISƐN JƐLENCƐ JƐLENMINƐN MIN BƐ JAALƐN MINƐNW KƐ LAKƆLISOBA YƆRƆW LA, A BƐ SE KA KƐ FUNANKƐ YE N'A SIRANNA FAANTANYA ƝƐ, A YƐRƐ ƝƐ, A ƝƐNAKƆTƐ ƝƐ.

DƆNKIJƐNMINƐN JƐLENCƐ MIN BƐ BAARA KƐ JAMANABAW FƐƐRƐBANƐ DƆ KƆNƆ, A BƐ SE KA KƐ FUNANKƐ YE WALIMA MUSO KƆRƆWLIBA YE SUFƐ, N'A SIRANNA ƝƐNAKƆTƐ ƝƐ, ŊƐNƐ ƝƐ, KƆRƆBAYA ƝƐ, A YƐRƐ ƝƐ, I NI BINKAN.

KARAMƆGƆ ƝUMAN MIN BƐ DUMUNIYƆRƆBA WALIMA HOTƐLIBA KƆNƆ, A BƐ SE KA KƐ GANSTER YE, BANKI FunankƐ YE, WALIMA FunankƐ FƆLƆBƆ YE, N'A SIRANNA A YƐRƐ ƝƐ, A KARAMƆGƆ JƐLEN YƆRƆ ƝƐ, A ƝƐNAKƆTƐ YƆRƆ ƝƐ, I NI BINKAN.

JIRI JIJEŊƐNMINƐN BƐ FƐƐRƐ KƐ KA KƐ ƝUMAN YE. ƐMƐPƆLƆYƐ JƐLENMINƐN MIN BƐ JAGƐLENNW KƆNƆ, KA TAJI, SILIKI, SANTA, N'A ƝƐNABILA, KA A ƝƐNABILA ƝƐNABILA NI ƝƐNA FALSILƐN, A BƐ FƐƐRƐ KƐ FƐƐRƐ KƆ, BAWO A SIRANNA, SIRAN BƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA, FAANTANYA ƝƐ, A ƝƐNAKƆTƐ DABUGUMAN ƝƐ, KƆRƆBAYA ƝƐ, I NI BINKAN.

HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA KA MASIRIYA GELEMAN DON. HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA KA ƝƐNADON SANTA KƆNƆ, KA ƝƐNADON SEYITAN KƆNƆ, KA ƝƐNADON CƐ KƆNƆ, KA ƝƐNADON MUSO KƆNƆ, KA ƝƐNADON MAGƐN ƝƐ, KA ƝƐNADON MAGƐN MƐGƐN ƝƐ, KA ƝƐNADON SƐNƐNTƆ ƝƐ, KA ƝƐNADON JUGUJƐLI ƝƐ.

HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA BƐ MINNU BƐ ɲINI KA FURU KƐ, N'A BƐ MINNU BƐ KƆRƆBA ƝƐNABILA MINNU TƐ FURU FƐƐRƐ SƆN.

HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA BƐ MINNU BƐ ɲINI KA KƐ "MƆGƆ DƆ YE", "KA ƝƐNA KƐ", "KA YƐLƐN" NI DIRIYA MINNI JƐNMAN, N'A BƐ MINNU BƐ KƐ ƝƐNAKƆRƆNNA YE, MINNU TƐ FEN KƆNƆ DUNIYA KƆNƆ, BAWO U HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA KELEN PE BƐ ɲINI KA SE SAN, KA HƆRƆNYA, I NI BINKAN.

HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYAW BƐ DUNIYA KƆNƆ, N'A BƐ HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYAW HƆRƆNYA KƆNƆ. TUMA DƆW LA, HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA BƐ MASIRIYA KƐ MAGƐN MƐGƐN ƝƐ, N'A BƐ KƆRƆN KƆRƆ.

MINNU TƐ HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA KƐ DUNIYA NANJANIN NƐGƐNIN KƆNƆ, O MINNU BƐ HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA KƐ TƆW KƆNƆ, N'A BƐ MINNU TƐ HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA KƐ WARI KƆNƆ, O MINNU BƐ HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA KƐ ƝƐNALƆLILEN ƝƐ.

NE, NE YƐRƐ, NE YƐRƐ PE BƐ HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA DOGO FƐ, KA A SƆRƆ HAKILI SIRRƐW DƆ, KA A FƆ KO: "NE TƐ FEN O FEN HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA KƐ", "NE BƐ NE ƝƐNALƆLILENNW KO", "NE BƐ BAARA KƐ MAGƐN MƐGƐN ƝƐ KA ƝƐNALƆLILEN BƐƐ KƐ".

POLITIKƆ SIWI MIN BƐ FEN BƐƐ LƆN, O BƐ DƎN BƆ DƐMƐNW KƆNƆ N'A BAARAW BƐ ƝƐNADON MAGƐN MƐGƐN ƝƐ, N'A BƆRA BAARA LA, A TƐ ƝƐNABILA KA BƆ A JAMANA KƆNƆ NI DƆLARI MILIYON KƆRƆNFLƆ.

HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA MIN MASIRIYARA NI MAGƐN MƐGƐN ƝƐ, O BƐ TƐKƐLILEN JAGƐLENNW LA.

JAMANABAW BƐ MINNU BƐ HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA KƐ KA MASA HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA ƝƐ.

JAMANABAW BƐ MINNU BƐ DƐN BƆ JAGƐLENNW NI FƐƐRƐBANƐW LA DUNIYA KƆNƆ BAWO U BƐ HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA KƐ U YƐRƐ PE CƐSIRI LA KƆNƆ.

TƆWSIW MIN BƐ TAGAMA KƆNI ƝƐ KƆNƆ KA SE ALA YƆRƆ LA, KA A KƆNƆFƆLI KƆNƆ A LAKANBƐ KƆNƆ, A TƐ HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA KƐ A ƝƐNADON ƝƐNADON ƝƐ, A BƐ SE KA A KA ɲƐNABILA, KA A SANU BƆ A ƝƐNƆGWƐ LA, NKA A ƝUMAN DON KO A BƐ HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA KƐ KƆLƆSIRI ƝƐ, FURA ƝƐ, HƐRA ƝƐ A YƐRƐ YƐRƐ WALIMA KƐNƐMAN DƆ ƝƐ, WALIMA FƆLI YA KƆRƆSI.

AN BƐ CƐ NI MUSO SƆN MINNU YE ALA ƝƐNAKƆRƆNWALIYA KƐ, NKA AN BƐ DAGUN KƆNƆ KO U TƐ U KA DIINƐ KO NI MAGƐN MƐGƐN ƝƐ.

DIINƐ SƐNƐNTƆW, SIWI KƐLENW, ULU, JAAW HƆRƆNYAW, I NI BINKAN. U BƐ ƝINI AN KA KO MAGƐN MƐGƐN ƝƐ.

A KA GƐLƐNNIN KA MƆGƆ SƆRƆ DUNIYA KƆNƆ MIN BƐ A KA DIINƐ, A KA KALIFA, A KA SIWI, I NI BINKAN KO NI MAGƐN MƐGƐN ƝƐ. O KA DAGUN.

DUNIYA BƐƐ JƐNNA HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYAW KƆNƆ. HITILƐRA YELENNA KƐLƐ LA HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA KƆRƆ.

KƐLƐ BƐƐ KA SABABU BƆ SIRAN N'A BƐ HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA LA. ŊƐNƐ GELEMABAW BƐƐ KA SABABU BƆ HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA LA.

DUNIYA BƐƐ BƐ ŊƐNƐ KƐ DUNIYA BƐƐ KƆNƆ SABABU YE HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA, MINNU YE ƝƆGƆN KƆRƆ, N'A BƐ U BƐƐ KƆRƆ.

MƆGƆ BƐƐ BƐ HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA KƐ KA KƐ FEN DƆ YE ŊƐNƐ KƆNƆ, N'A BƐ MƆGƆ MINNU KA SANJƐN, KARAMƆGƆW, BAAW DEENW, TALIBƐW, I NI BINKAN BƐ DENMISƐNNW DƐMƐ, DENMISƐN MUSOO, JAALƐN BƐ MUSOO, ƝƐNAKƆRƆNW, I NI BINKAN KA TAGAMA HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA SIRATƐN GELEMAN FƐ.

KƆRƆBAW BƐ KARAMƆGƆ SIFAW FƆ KO, I KA ɲINI KA KƐ FEN DƆ YE ŊƐNƐ KƆNƆ, KA BAAYA SƆRƆ, KA FURU KƐ JAMANABAW FƐ, KA SE GBƐLƆ, I NI BINKAN.

SANJƐLEN KƆRƆBAW GELEMA, ƝƐNABILEMANW, MINNU KA WATI BƐ TƐMƐ, BƐ ɲINI KA SANJƐLEN KURA HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA KƐ, KA ƝƐNABILEMAN KƐ, KA GELEMA KƐ I NA U YE.

FEN GBƐLƆBA JATILEN, O YE KO MƆGƆ KURAW BƐ "MAREYA" KƐ, KA U YƐRƐ YƐRƐ JI ƝƐNABILA KA U TAGAMA HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA SIRATƐN GELEMAN.

KARAMƆGƆW NI KARAMƆGƆW SIFAW KA KARAMƆGƆ SIFAW NI KARAMƆGƆW SIFAW FƆ KO BAARA TIINBA FEN O FEN TƐ HƐRƐ GƐN, A TƐ ƝƐNABILA KA ƝƐNA ƝƐNABILA TAJIKI, ƐMƐPƆLƆYƐ, SƐNƐKƐLA, MIN BƐ SANTA SƆRƆ, I NI BINKAN ƝƐ.

BAARA JƐLENCƐ BƐƐ KA ƝUMAN DON. BAARA JƐLENCƐ BƐƐ KA MAGƐN DON ŊƐNƐ JƐKƆRƆMA KƆNƆ.

AN BƐƐ TƐ DɔGƆTƆRW YE, GUVƐRINƐRW YE, PEREZIDƐNW YE, DƆGƆTƆRW YE, LAWOYEE YE, I NI BINKAN.

JƐKƆRƆMA KƆNƆ BAARA BƐƐ KA MAGƐN DON, SƐNƐ BƐƐ, BAARA TIINBA FEN O FEN TƐ SE KA ƝƐNABILA.

ŊƐNƐ JAWLA KƆNƆ, ƝƐNALƆLILEN BƐƐ BƐ BAARA KƐ FEN DƆ KƆRƆ, KA MASIRIYA MIN YE KO ƝIN KƆRƆN KƐ.

A BƐ KARAMƆGƆW NI KARAMƆGƆW SIFAW KA BAARA DON KA TALIBƐ BƐƐ KA JƆYƐLEN ƝIN, KA U TALIFƐ U KAN.

MIN BƐ BAARA KƐ ŊƐNƐ KƆNƆ KA BƐN A KA JƆYƐLEN KAN, O BƐ BAARA KƐ NI KO TINBALI N'A BƐ HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA.

KO KA ɲIN KƆRƆ SI KƐ HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA JIBA. JƆYƐLEN YE MIN BƐ ɲUMAN DI AN MA, MIN BƐ SƐNƐ MIN BƐ ƝUMAN DI AN MA NI DƆLƆ, BAWO O YE MIN BƐ AN MƆN, MIN BƐ AN KO.

ŊƐNƐ KURA KƆNƆ, MƆGƆ BƐ BAARA KƐ KA DAGUN N'A BƐ HAKILIYA KƆRƆNFƆRWALIYA BAWO U BƐ BAARA KƐ MINNU TƐ BƐN U KA JƆYƐLEN KAN.

N'I BƐ BAARA KƐ MIN BƐ ƝUMAN DI I MA, I KA JƆYƐLEN TINBALI, I BƐ A KƐ NI KO BAWO I BƐ KO KA I KA JƆYƐLEN, BAWO I KA KOW KƐ ŊƐNƐ KƆNƆ MINNU YE I KA JƆYƐLEN KƐ.

O PE YE KARAMƆGƆW KA BAARA DON. KA U KA KARAMƆGƆ SIFAW NI KARAMƆGƆW SIFAW TALIFƐ, KA U KA TILƆ KƆRƆN ƝIN, KA U TALIFƐ U KA JƆYƐLEN TINBALI SIRATƐN FƐ.
