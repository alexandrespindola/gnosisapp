Ɲɛnɛma san mugan fɔlɔw bɛɛ bɛ kitabuyi di, san bi sɔgɔlenw bɛɛ bɛ ɲɛfɔli di.

San mugan na, cɛɛ ye kɔnkɔn ye; san bi na, jagwɛ; san mugan na, ɲɔgɔn; san bi sɔgɔlen na, sa; san wɔɔrɔ na, wulu; san bi sɔgɔlen na, gberi, san kɔnɔntɔn na, kumakan dɔrɔn ni jigiya.

Waati bɛ fɛn bɛɛ jira: a ye dawulakan ye min ka di kosɛbɛ min bɛ kuma a yɛrɛ ma hali ni mɔgɔ tɛ a ɲininka foyi.

Fɛn si tɛ min kɛra faɲa-hakili sɔgɔlɔn fɛ, min tɔgɔ dara cɛɛ, min waati bɛna ban a halaki.

"FUGIT IRRÉPARABILE TEMPUS", waati min bɛ boli, a tɛ se ka daɲɛ.

Waati bɛ fɛn bɛɛ min bɛ dogolen sisan lase jama ma, a bɛ fɛn bɛɛ min bɛ jɛɲɔgɔn ni jɛɲɔgɔnya la sisan dogo.

Kɔrɔya bɛ i n’a fɔ kanuya, a tɛ se ka dogo hali ni a yɛlɛmɛna kanuya gansanw la.

Kɔrɔya bɛ cɛɛw ka wasa tiɲɛ a bɛ u majigi, nka fɛn dɔ ye ka majigi, a wɛrɛ ye ka bɔ majigilenw na.

Ni saya bɛ gɛrɛ, kɔrɔbaw minnu jigiya tigilen don ɲɛnamaya la, u bɛ a ɲɛnabɔ ko kɔrɔya tɛ donko ye tugun.

Cɛɛw bɛɛ bɛ jigiya sɔn ka ɲɛnamaya kɛ waati jan ka kɛ kɔrɔbaw ye nka kɔrɔya bɛ u siran.

Kɔrɔya bɛ daminɛ san bi sɔgɔlen ni wɔɔrɔ na ka tɛmɛ tɔgɔlen saba saba tɛmɛsiraw kan minnu bɛ an bila fɔɲɔw ani saya la.

Kɔrɔbaw ka gɛlɛyawba bɛɛ bɛ sigi, a tɛ sigi kɔrɔya yɛrɛ kan, a bɛ sigi miiriyawi kan min tɛ sɔn ka yɛrɛnabɔko u ye kɔrɔbaw ye ani ka da a la ko u ye tɔgɔbaw ye i n’a fɔ kɔrɔya ye jɔn ye.

Fɛn ɲuman min bɛ kɔrɔya la, o ye ko mɔgɔ bɛ taamasiyɛn gɛrɛ kosɛbɛ.

N’ yɛrɛ hakili, n’ yɛrɛ, n’ yɛrɛ, a tɛ ɲɛ ka tɛmɛ sanw ani ɲɛnajɛw kan; a bɛ gɛlɛya, a bɛ kɛ gɛlɛn ye, a bɛ kɛ baara ye, o de kosɔn a bɛ kuma cikanw na: "Hakili ani jiginikɔrɔsi fɔ kaburu".

Kɔrɔbaw minnu gɛlɛn, u yɛrɛ hakili bɛ u dɛmɛ ka ladilikan ɲumanw di u ka seko tɛ ɲɛfɔlidan ɲumanw di.

Kɔrɔbaw bɛ a dɔn ko kɔrɔya ye tɔgɔbɔla juguman ye min bɛ u bali saya fɛ ka ɲɛnajɛ sɔrɔ tɔgɔya dɔgɔtɔrɔya la ani ka u yɛrɛ dɛmɛ ka ladilikan ɲumanw di.

N’ yɛrɛ bɛ n’ yɛrɛ dogo, n’ yɛrɛ bɛ a yɛrɛ fan dɔ dogo ani fɛn bɛɛ bɛ tɔgɔda ladilikan ɲumanw ni ladilikan ɲumanw fɛ.

N’ yɛrɛ fan dɔ bɛ n’ yɛrɛ fan wɛrɛ dogo. N’ yɛrɛ bɛ fɛn min tɛ sɔn dogo.

A ɲɛnabɔra kosɛbɛ ɲɛnajɛ ani ɲɛnajɛw fɛ ko ni tɔɲɔw bɛ an bila, a bɛ an dɛmɛ ka miiri ko an ye olu bila.

Faɲa-hakili sɔgɔlɔn dusukun tɛ ɲɛ sanw na, nka a bɛ juguya, a bɛ kɛ kabakuru ye tuma bɛɛ, ni an ye kunafoniw ye tɔgɔya la, kunafoniw, jagwɛw, kɔrɔya la, an bɛna kɛ kosɛbɛ.

Kɔrɔbaw bɛ ɲɛnamaya kɛ tɛmɛnen na, kɔrɔbaw ye ayɛrɛ caman ɲɛfɔli ye, kɔrɔbaw bɛ waati min bɛ ɲɛnamaya la dogo kosɛbɛ, kɔrɔbaw ye hakilikan lakumalen ye.

Sɔrɔli ɲuman dɔrɔn ye ka n’ yɛrɛ hakili yɛlɛma. Ni an y’a lɔn ka saya kɛ waati ni waati, an bɛ sɔrɔ kɔrɔya subilimɛri ma.

Kɔrɔya bɛ ɲɛfɔliba dɔ sɔrɔ, ɲɛnajɛ ani hɔrɔnya minnu ye n’ yɛrɛ yɛlɛma.

Ni miiriya jugu siralen don danmabɔlɔn ani sɔgɔsɔgɔlen na, mɔgɔ bɛ hɔrɔnya sɔrɔ tɔgɔbɔla dɔ tɛ, nka tɔgɔbɔla caman.

A gɛlɛn kosɛbɛ ka sɔrɔ kɔrɔbaw minnu ye janto ɲɛnamaya la, minnu tɛ n’ yɛrɛ jiginniw sɔrɔ tugun, o kɔrɔbaw jɛɲɔgɔnɲɛ don kosɛbɛ ani u bɛ ɲɛnamaya kɛ waati ni waati.

Cɛɛ min ka jɛɲɔgɔnɲɛ kɛ hakilima la. Kɔrɔba min bɛ dɔnniya la, kanuya matigi, a bɛ kɛ jɛɲɔgɔnya ye min bɛ sirada ɲɛfɔlɔn ka sira ɲɛfɔli caman.

Diɲɛ kɔnɔ, kɔrɔbaw kalanbaa dɔw bɛ yen minnu tɛ n’ yɛrɛ jiginniw sɔrɔ. GNÓSTIC ARHAT ninnu janto ani ɲɛnajɛ don i n’a fɔ lɔti flɛri.

KƆRƆBA KALANBAA min ye N’ YƐRƐ BOLOYA ƝƐSUMA yɛlɛma danmabɔlɔn ani sɔgɔsɔgɔlen na, o ye ɲɛfɔli ɲuman ye, hakilima ɲuman, kanuya ɲuman ani seko subilimɛri.

KƆRƆBA KALANBAA min tɛ n’ yɛrɛ sɔrɔ tugun, o ye ɲɛfɔli ɲuman ye, janto ɲuman sɔrɔli.

SUBLIMES KƆRƆBA ninnu, GNÓSTIC ARHAT ninnu ye diɲɛ jɛɲɔgɔn cɛmancɛ waati kɔrɔw la, an bɛ BUDHA hakili sɔrɔ, MOISÉS, HERMES, RAMARKRISHNA, DANIEL, SANTO LAMA, etc., etc., etc.

Kalandenw, colegios ani uniwersitew kalanbaaw, kalanbaaw, denbaya facɛw, kan ka kalanbaaw ni ɲɛnajɛw di sanw ma.

FƐN min tɛ tɔgɔ sɔrɔ, O min ye ɲuman ye, O min ye a dafa, a bɛ ɲɛfɔli saba sɔrɔ: hakilima, kanuya, kumakan.

JIJI ƝUMAN i n’a fɔ FARA ye hakilima cɛmancɛ, i n’a fɔ BA ye kanuya dafalen, i n’a fɔ den ye kumakan.

Denbaya facɛ kɔnɔ, hakilima taamasiyɛn bɛ yen. So ba kɔnɔ, kanuya bɛ yen, denw bɛ kuma taamasiyɛn.

Kɔrɔba Facɛ bɛ denw dɛmɛ bɛɛ sɔrɔ. Facɛ min ka kɔrɔya tɛ se ka baara kɛ, a bɛ da ka denw ka a mara ka a janto.

BA min ka di kosɛbɛ, min ka kɔrɔya tɛ se ka baara kɛ, o de kosɔn a mago bɛ denw ni denmusow la k’a lajɛ ka a kanu ka kanuya in kɛ diinɛ ye.

Min tɛ a Facɛ kanu, min tɛ a BA ɲɛ, a bɛ boli sira kan min bɛ bɔ bolo sinikan, siran sira kan.

Denw tɛ sara ka u Facɛw sara, mɔgɔ si tɛ dafalen diɲɛ in kɔnɔ ani minnu tɛ ɲɛnɛnajɛw minnu ɲɛfɔlen don dugu dɔ la, an bɛ olu sɔrɔ a wɛrɛ la, an bɛɛ sara jiritɔn kelen fɛ.

Dɔw bɛ FARA kanuya yɛlɛma, dɔw fana bɛ FARA kanuya yɛlɛma. Minnu bɛ ɲɛnamaya kɛ cogo nin na, olu ma don sira kan min bɛ taa O ma min tɛ tɔgɔ sɔrɔ.

Den min ma da min bɛ a Facɛ sɔgɔ ani ka a BA min ka di kosɛbɛ ɲinɛ, o ye juguya yɛrɛ ye min bɛ fɛn bɛɛ min ye ɲuman ye sɔgɔ.

HAKILI YƐLƐMA tɛ ɲɛnɛmafili ye, ka facɛ ɲinɛ, ka ba min ka di kosɛbɛ yɛlɛma. HAKILI YƐLƐMA ye hakilima kanuya ani SEKO ɲUMAN ye.

Facɛ kɔnɔ, hakilima taamasiyɛn bɛ yen ani ba kɔnɔ, kanuya siran bɛ yen, min tɛ baara kɛ i n’a fɔ a yɛrɛ ye, o de kosɔn a gɛlɛn kosɛbɛ ka DAFA yɛrɛ sɔrɔ.
