Anu ye se ka a yɛrɛ sɔrɔ ko tariku karamɔgɔ caman minnu bɛ diɲɛ tilebin fan fɛ, olu bɛ BUDDHA, Confucius, Mahoma, Hermes, Quetzacoal, Moisés, Krishna, ani tɔw yɛrɛyɛrɛ.

Tiɲɛni tɛ a la, an ye se fana ka a ye ko karamɔgɔw bɛ dɔgɔyaa ni dɛsɛ ani dɛsɛ laminiw kɛ diinɛkõrõw, Ala ma, miitoolojiyiw ma, ani tɔw. O bɛɛ ye hakili tɛmɛni ye.

Kalandan duguw, karamɔgɔso ani jɛkuluw la, diinɛ koɲɛw ka kan ka ɲɛnabɔ ni jatebɔ gbeleba ye, ni bato gundo gbeleba ye, ni hakili daala tiyɛ ye.

Diinɛ fɛɛrw bɛ jɔnjɔn nafama mara ani u labɛnnen bɛ mɔgɔ kelen kelen bɛɛ hakili jiginni ni tariku ɲɛnajɛw ye ka kɛɲɛ ni kabila kelen kelen bɛɛ ye.

Diinɛ bɛɛ bɛ jɔnjɔn kelen mara, nafama kelen mara, u bɛ danfara kɛ fɛɛrɔ la dɔrɔn.

A tɛ hakilitigi ye Kirisitan ka BUDDHA diinɛ yɛrɛyɛrɛ walima Heburuw walima Hinduw ka diinɛ sabu diinɛ bɛɛ jɔnjɔn kelen kan.

Hakilima caman ka dɔgɔyaa diinɛw ani a jɔnjɔn tɔw ma bɛ Marx ka furakisɛw de kosɔn min bɛ hakili gɛlɛnw bɛɛ fa n’u kɔnɔ bi.

Karamɔgɔw minnu bɛ kalandan duguw, karamɔgɔso ani jɛkuluw la ka kan ka u kalandenw ɲɛnabɔ siratigɛ tiyɛ kan, jatebɔ gbeleba kan an ɲɔgɔn ma.

A ka gɛlɛn ani a man kan fɔlɔ ye ko tiɲɛ bɛɛ bɛ mɔgɔ minɛn min bɛ diinɛ batoso, sɔrɔdasiw, kalandan duguw, karamɔgɔso walima jɛkuluw yɛrɛyɛrɛ teoriw tɔgɔ la.

Kalan bɔli kɔfɛ, kalandenw ka kan ka mɔgɔ suguya bɛɛ sɔrɔ minnu bɛ diinɛw bɛɛ la, kalandan duguw, karamɔgɔso ani a tɛ hakilitigi ye min tɛ se ka a yɛrɛ mara batoso la.

Kalan bɔli kɔfɛ san tan walima tan ni loolu kɔnɔ, kanubagaw ani musow bɛ u yɛrɛ sɔrɔ u jɔlen ani u sinnaw i n’a fɔ mɔgɔ wɛrɛw, u faalen bɛ fuufuuya la ani hakili tɛmɛni i n’a fɔ don fɔlɔ min u donna kalandan dugu la.

A gansan, kalandenw ka kan ka hakili sɔrɔ gɛlɛya wɛrɛw cɛma sabu a tɛ hakili ye. A ka kan ka ɲɛnajɛw kɔnɔma bɛɛ haminiko sɔrɔ, yiri kelen kɔnɔma cɛɲi, sogo dɔgɔyali daɲɛ kunun fɔrɔnkɛ cɛma, muusikiyali ani kulɛri hakilitigiya ɲɛnajɛ cɛɲi.

A ka kan fana ka hakili sɔrɔ ani ka danfara gbeleba bɛɛ faamu, i n’a fɔ jɛkulu sira gɛlɛn ani fiyɛnniya waati min an bɛ ɲɛnama la, siraw faalen bɛ baaw gɛlɛnw na minnu bɛ u denbayaw lamini ani ɲɛnini ka baga, bonw gɛlɛnw minnu la denbaya caman bɛ ɲɛnama, siraw tiɲɛnenw minnu cɛma maraba caman bɛ taama minnu bɛ lɔgɔw tiɲɛn, ani tɔw.

Kalande min bɛ kalan bila, o ka kan k’a yɛrɛ sɔrɔ a yɛrɛ baloya dɔrɔn tɛ ani a ka gɛlɛyaw, a ka kan k’a yɛrɛ sɔrɔ mɔgɔw bɛɛ baloya ani jɛkuluw ka gɛlɛyaw.

Ko gbeleba bɛɛ ye ko kalande min bɛ kalan bila, hali ni hakili labɛnni sɔrɔra, a tɛ hakilitigi ye, a hakili sinnen don, a labɛnnen tɛ ɲɛnama kɛlɛ ma.

Waati sera ka ɲinini ani ka a yɛrɛ sɔrɔ ko min bɛ weele hakili. Dakun, ɛnkisikilopediyaw, olu bɛ kɛ hakili ɲɛnabɔli la tiyɛ.

Ni hakili tɛ, fiyɛnniya tɛ se ka kɛ, nɛnɛma tiyɛ fana tɛ ani a gɛlɛn ka mɔgɔ hakilitigi tiyɛ sɔrɔ ɲɛnama la.

Ko nafama ɲɛnama la dɔrɔn tɛ hakili daɲɛ dɔn, a bɛ an yɛrɛ la a ɲɔgɔnna gundo sɔrɔ.

Caman bɛ minnu bɛ u yɛrɛ sinsin hakilitigi la, jilen tɛ min tɛ a yɛrɛ sinsin hakilitigi la ani Carlos Marx bɛ a yɛrɛ lase a hakilitigiya gbeleba la, a sɛbɛn a materiali fɛnkalan min kɛra diɲɛ ma nafama, sarakalase caman dɔgɔyali minnu bɛ diinɛ dafalen, musow minnu tɛ mɔgɔ kunbɛn sɔrɔdisiw fɛ, Budisiw, Kirisitanw, ani tɔw, batoso caman tiɲɛni, mɔgɔ caman tiɲɛni, ani miliyɔn caman, ani tɔw.

Mɔgɔ bɛɛ bɛ se ka u yɛrɛ sinsin hakilitigi la, a gɛlɛn ka kɛ tiyɛ.

A tɛ kalankan kalanni wɛrɛ sɔrɔli ye, dɔnniya wɛrɛ sɔrɔli, ɲɛnajɛ wɛrɛ sɔrɔli, fɛnw wɛrɛ sɔrɔli mɔgɔw ɲɛ sɔrɔli kama, wari wɛrɛ sɔrɔli ka kititɔn ani polisiti san, ani tɔw. I n’a fɔ a bɛna se ka o min bɛ weele hakili.

A tɛ o MAS ye, i n’a fɔ mɔgɔ bɛ se ka hakili sɔrɔ. U tɛ kɛɲɛ ka kɛɲɛ ni mɔgɔw ye minnu bɛ a lase ko hakili bɛ se ka kɛlɛ MAS sira kan.

A gansan ka faamu tiyɛ ani fɛnw bɛɛ hakili gundo ani hakili sinnen min bɛ o MAS bɔlɔnbɔlɔya min kosɔn, sabu a kɔnɔma a bɛ gundo don gundo la EGO ɲuman, YO, MI MISMO, min bɛ ɲini ani a b’a fɛ tum bɛɛ MAS ani MAS ka bonya ani ka fanga sɔrɔ.

Nin Mefistofeles min bɛ an kɔnɔ, nin SATÁN, nin YO, a bɛ fɔ: YO bɛ wari MAS sɔrɔ, cɛɲi MAS, hakili MAS o tɛ, ɲɛsin MAS, hakilitigiya MAS, ani tɔw.

Mɔgɔ min ka kan ka hakili faamu tiyɛ, o ka kan ka a haminika dɔn, o ka kan ka a ɲɛnama ani ka a kɔrɔbɔ tɛmɛsi gundo la.

Ko minnu mɔgɔw bɛ jɔnjɔn su kɔrɔn cɛma hakili sinnen, dɔnniya hakilitigi, ɲɛnama ɲɛnajɛ, a bɛ kɛ tum bɛɛ halakili MAS ani MAS. A bɛ kɛɲɛ ko u tɛ a bɛɛ faamu gundo sɔrɔ minnu u bɛ jɔnjɔn.

Caman bɛ minnu bɛ kalankan kelen kalan ani k’a bila hakili cɛma ni u ɲɛnajɛnen don dɔnniya wɛrɛ jɔnjɔn kosɔn, nka waati min mɔgɔ bɛ weele ka jaabi dɔnkilida sɛbɛnnen kalankan min u kalan, a bɛ kɛɲɛ ko u tɛ a faamu gundo sɔrɔ, nka YO bɛ dɔnniya wɛrɛ ɲini ani kalankan wɛrɛ hali waati min u ma a ɲɛnama ka dɔnkilida dɔn.

Hakili tɛ kalankan dɔnniya wɛrɛ sɔrɔli ye, walima ɲɛnajɛ wɛrɛ, walima wari wɛrɛ, walima ɲɛsin wɛrɛ, hakili bɛ se ka bɔ an kɔnɔ waati min an bɛ YO bɔlɔnbɔlɔya bɛɛ faamu, waati min an bɛ MAS hakiliminiya bɛɛ faamu.

A gansan ka faamu ko hakili ye MAS daɲɛbɔ minɛn ye. Tiɲɛya la o MAS bɛɛ ye YO hakili bɛɛ ye min bɛ ɲini ani hakili ye a bolo gundo ye.

Mɔgɔ min ka kan ka hakilitigi kɛ tiyɛ, o ka kan k’a yɛrɛ sɔrɔ ka sa dɔrɔn tɛ hakilitigiya gundo kan, a ka kan fana ka kɛ hakili gundo ani hakili sinnen tɔw la.

Waati min YO sara, waati min Y0 bɛ dafalen, ko kelen bɛ bɔ an kɔnɔ min ye SER tiyɛ, SER tiyɛ, hakili tilennen min bɛ ɲini ani a gɛlɛn.

Mɔgɔw bɛ a lase ko hakili ye daala ye, u tɛ kɛɲɛ. YO tɛ daala ye ani hakili ye YO bolo ye.

Hakili ye daala ye sabu a ye SER ye, a ye SER ka jɔnjɔn ye. An tɛ hakili lamini ni hakili.

U tɛ kɛɲɛ PLANE kan ani sira gundo mɔgɔ minnu bɛ a lase ko HAKILI ye ko ye min bɛ se ka foronto fɛn sɛnɛ i n’a fɔ foro waati wala ko min bɛ se ka san i n’a fɔ mɔgɔ bɛ minnu san cɛɲi tɔgɔ la wala a bolo bɛ dakun gbeleba la.

A ka kan ka hakili bɔlɔnbɔlɔya bɛɛ faamu tiyɛ, jaabi bɛɛ, o MAS hakiliminiya min bɛ jɔnjɔn, ani tɔw. O dɔrɔn bɛ fɔnɔ an kɔnɔ sira ni hakilitigiya jɛɲɔgɔnba.

K’a kɛɲɛ ni Mefistofeles min bɛ an kɔnɔ bɛ bɔ, hakili daala taama bɛ bɔ cɛmancɛma an kɔnɔ, fo ka jɛɲɔgɔnba kɛ.

An ka SER tiyɛ ye kanuya ye ani o kanuya la HAKILI tilennen bɛ bɔ min tɛ waati ye.
