Diɲɛ kɔnɔ, kumalaw caman bɛ yen minnu bɛ mɔgɔ kabakoya u ka kuma duman fɛ, nka mɔgɔ damadɔ dɔnna ka lamɛn.

Lamɛn dɔn ka gɛlɛn kosɛbɛ, mɔgɔ damadɔ dɔnna tiɲɛ na ka lamɛn tiɲɛ na.

NI KARAMƆGƆ BƐ KUMA, karamɔgɔmuso, kuma-ɲɛnabɔla, lamɛnnikɛlaw bɛɛ bɛ ɲɛnajɛ kosɛbɛ i n’a fɔ u bɛ tugu kuma-ɲɛnabɔla ka kuma kelen-kelen bɛɛ kɔ, fɛn bɛɛ bɛ ɲɛnajɛ ko u bɛ lamɛn, ko u bɛ ɲɛnajɛ sira kan, nka mɔgɔ kelen-kelen bɛɛ ka miiriya kɔnɔ, sɛbɛnnikɛla dɔ bɛ yen min bɛ kuma-ɲɛnabɔla ka kuma kelen-kelen bɛɛ bayɛlɛma.

SEBƐNNIKƐLA NIN YE NE YE, NE Yɛrɛ, NE Yɛrɛ. O sɛbɛnnikɛla ka baara ye ka kuma ɲɛfɔ cogo ɲuman na, ka kuma bayɛlɛma cogo ɲuman na.

NE bɛ bayɛlɛma ka kɛɲɛ ni a ka ɲɛfɔli tɛmɛnenw, ɲɛfɔli ɲɛfɔlɛw, siranw, yɛrɛbonya, ɲɛnajɛ, miiriya, hakililaw, abɛnɛ, abɛnɛ, abɛnɛ.

Kalandenw sigida la, kalandenmusow, mɔgɔ minnu jɛlen bɛ ka lamɛnnikɛlaw sigiyɔrɔ sigi min bɛ lamɛn, u tɛ lamɛn tiɲɛ na kuma-ɲɛnabɔla lamɛnni na, u bɛ lamɛn u yɛrɛ, u bɛ lamɛn u ka EGO yɛrɛ, u ka EGO MAQUIAVELICO kan min t’a sɔn ka TIƝƐ, TIƝƐ, ƝƐSIRI sɔn.

Siranya kura dɔrɔn de la, hakili bɛ sɔrɔ ni a yɛrɛ bɔra tɛmɛnen waati ka ɲɛgɛn na, ka sɔn kosɛbɛ, an bɛ se ka lamɛn tiɲɛ na ka tɛmɛ ni sɛbɛnnikɛla jugu kan min tɔgɔ ye NE, NE YƐRƐ, NE YƐRƐ, EGO.

Ni hakili sigilen bɛ hakili la, o bɛ tɛmɛnen waati de jaabi.

Hakili min sigilen bɛ san caman tɛmɛnen waati ka ɲɛnabɔliw la, o bɛ se ka sisan ye tɛmɛnen waati ka ɲɛnajɛ nɛgɛnw fɛ dɔrɔn.

N’AN B’A FƐ KA LAMƐN DƆN, ni an b’a fɛ ka lamɛn dɔn walasa ka fɛnw sɔrɔ minnu kura don, an ka ɲɛnamaya kɛ ka kɛɲɛ ni MOMENTANEIDAD ka hakilina ye.

A ka kan ka ɲɛnamaya kɛ waati ni waati tɛmɛnen waati ka ɲɛnajɛ tɛmɛnenw tɛ, ani siniɲasigiw tɛ.

TIƝƐ ye fɛn min dɔnnen tɛ waati ni waati, an ka hakiliw ka kan ka ɲɛnajɛ tuma bɛɛ, ka ɲɛnajɛ kosɛbɛ, ka bɔ ɲɛfɔli tɛmɛnenw na, ɲɛfɔli ɲɛfɔlɛw, walasa ka sɔn tiɲɛ na.

Karamɔgɔw ni karamɔgɔmusow ka kan ka u ka kalandenw kalan ka ɲɛfɔli gbɛlɛn min bɛ lamɛn dɔnni kɔnɔ.

A wajibiyalen don ka dɔn ka ɲɛnamaya kɛ ni hakili ye, ka an ka hakiliw sinsin kokura, ka an ka taamasiyɛnw, an ka miiriyaw, an ka dusukunnataw ɲɛnabɔ.

A tɛ nafa sɔrɔ hakili dɔgɔkunba dɔ sɔrɔ, ni an tɛ lamɛn dɔn, ni an tɛ se ka fɛn kura sɔrɔ waati ni waati.

An mago bɛ ɲɛnajɛ ɲɛnabɔli la, an ka taamasiyɛnw ɲɛnabɔli, an ka mɔgɔw ɲɛnabɔli, fɛnw, abɛnɛ, abɛnɛ, abɛnɛ.

A tɛ se ka ɲɛnabɔ tiɲɛ na ni an tɛ lamɛn dɔn.

Hakili juguw, juguw, minnu tiɲɛna, minnu tiɲɛna, olu tɛ lamɛn dɔn abada, u tɛ fɛn kura sɔrɔ abada, o hakiliw bɛ faamu dɔrɔn, u bɛ faamu cogo ɲuman na dɔrɔn ni sɛbɛnnikɛla Setana min tɔgɔ ye NE, NE YƐRƐ, EGO ka kuma juguw.

Ka ɲɛnabɔ ka gɛlɛn kosɛbɛ waati bɛɛ la. Mɔgɔ dɔ bɛ se ka kɛ mɔgɔ ɲɛnabɔlen ye kosɛbɛ ɲɛnabɔliw la, faniw, funteni, nakɔw, motokariw, teriw, nka a bɛ to ka kɛ juguw ye, ka gɛlɛn, ka ɲɛgɛn.

Min bɛ ɲɛnamaya kɛ waati ni waati, o bɛ taama tiɲɛ na ɲɛnabɔli tiɲɛna sira kan.

Minnu hakili bɛ sɔn, a yɛrɛ bɔra, a dafalen don, a bɛ ɲɛnajɛ, a bɛ taama ɲɛnabɔli tiɲɛna sira kan.

Min bɛ da bila fɛn kuraw bɛɛ la ka tɛmɛnen waati ka ɲɛgɛn bɔ, ɲɛfɔli ɲɛfɔlɛw, ɲɛfɔli tɛmɛnenw, jagɛlɛnw, faaninɛtiw, abɛnɛ, a bɛ taama ni sɔrɔdasi ye ɲɛnabɔli sɔrɔdasi sira kan.

Hakili min tiɲɛna, o bɛ ɲɛnamaya kɛ tɛmɛnen waati kɔnɔ, ɲɛfɔli ɲɛfɔlɛw, yɛrɛbonya, yɛrɛ kanu, ɲɛfɔli tɛmɛnenw, abɛnɛ, abɛnɛ.

Hakili min tiɲɛna, o tɛ fɛn kura ye dɔn, o tɛ lamɛn dɔn, a sigilen bɛ YƐRƐ KANUBAGA la.

MARXISME-LENINISME ka faaninɛtiw tɛ fɛn kura sɔn; u tɛ FƐN BƐƐ KA JƐYIRIBA sɔn, DIMENSIƆN naaninan, yɛrɛ kanubaga kosɔn, u bɛ u yɛrɛ kanu kosɛbɛ, u bɛ jɛɲɔgɔnya kɛ ni u ka hakilina juguw ye minnu bɛ fɛnw ɲɛnabɔ ani NI AN YE U SIGI TIƝƐSIRALI SIGIYƆRƆ LA, NI AN YE U YE U KA SOFISME KA JUGUYA YIRA, U BƐ BOLO SINAN BƆ, U BƐ U KA ŊƐNYƐ KƆNƆ KƆLƆSIW LAJƐ, U BƐ SƆNGƐ SIRANIN DI KA BƆ.

OLU YE HAKILI JUGUW YE, HAKILI JUGUW MINNU TƐ LAMƐN DƆN, MINNU TƐ FƐN KURA SƆRƆ, MINNU TƐ TIƝƐ SƆN K’A YƐRƐ YƐRƐ YƐRƐ KƆNƆ YƐRƐ KANUBAGA LA. Hakiliw minnu bɛ u yɛrɛ kanu kosɛbɛ, hakiliw minnu tɛ HAKILILƆN ƝƐNABƆLIW DƆN, hakili juguw, hakili juguw, minnu bɛ u ka EGO kanu dɔrɔn.

KALAN JƆNJƆN bɛ lamɛn kalan, a bɛ ɲɛnamaya kɛ ni hakili ye kalan.

Karamɔgɔw ni karamɔgɔmusow sigida la, kalandenw ka kan ka u ka kalandenw kalan sira tiɲɛna min bɛ ɲɛnamaya ɲɛnabɔli tiɲɛna la.

A tɛ nafa sɔrɔ ka to san tan ni tan ni duuru kɔnɔ sigidaw la, ani lakɔlikuluw la, ni an bɔra an kɔnɔ, an ye wuluw ye tiɲɛ na an ka miiriyaw, hakilinaw, dusukunnataw ani taamasiyɛnw la.

KALAN JƆNJƆN mago bɛ an na ka taama joona katuguni bɔnsɔn kuraw bɛ KALA KURA daminɛ ɲɛfɔ.

Waati sera ƝƐNAMA JUBA san, waati sera KALAN JƆNJƆN ka ɲɛnama ju ba.

Tɛmɛnen waati ye tɛmɛnen waati ye ani a ye a ka buruju di. An mago bɛ ka waati min bɛ ɲɛnamaya kɛ o ka ɲɛfɔli dafalen faamu.
