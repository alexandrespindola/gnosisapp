An ka gnɛ ka caaman bɛɛ kɔnɔ, o bɔra sanfɛ, sankolo la, tɔɔrɔnw na... A tɛ ɲɛɲi, gnɛ ɲumanba bɔra sɛbɛn "LA" (La Voie Lactée, galaksi min kɔnɔ an bɛ).

Gnɛ ɲuman ka caaman tɛmɛna sɛbɛn "SOL" (Tile) kan, o kɔfɛ sɛbɛn "FA" (Planetari dugukolo) kɔnɔ, a sera diɲɛ in na ka don an yɛrɛ kɔnɔ. An ka bangebagaw ye fari labɛn walasa ka gnɛ min bɔra tɔɔrɔnw na, o sɔn.

Ni an bɛ baara kɛ an yɛrɛ kan kosɛbɛ ani ka an yɛrɛ sara an ɲɔgɔnna, an bɛ segin Urania kɔnɔ kosɛbɛ ni se. An bɛ ɲɛnamaya kɛ diɲɛ in kɔnɔ sabu dɔw kosɔn, walasa fɛn dɔw, sabu jɔnjɔnw dɔw...

A ka gwɛ, an bɛ fɛn caman minnu ka kan ka an ye, ka kalan, ka faamu, ni a bɛ an kɔnɔna an bɛ fɛn dɔ ɲini an yɛrɛ kan, an ka ɲɛnamaya kan... A ka gɛlɛya ka ɲɛnamaya kɛ min ma a ɲɛnamaya sababu dɔn...

An kelen-kelen bɛɛ ka kan ka a ɲɛnamaya kɛ sababu dɔn a yɛrɛ ma, min bɛ a jɔli dimi kaso la... A ka ɲɛ, an bɛ fɛn dɔ min bɛ an ka ɲɛnamaya dimi an bɛɛ kɔnɔ, o min bɛ kan ka an ka kɛlɛ kɛ a ɲɛkɔrɔ la... A tɛ wajibiyayɛ ka an ka taama dimi kɔnɔ, a ka ɲɛ ka fɛn min bɛ an dɔlɔn ani ka an ɲaga kosɛbɛ, ka o kɛ sankolo bugubugu ye.

A tɛ nafa fɛn o fɛn ma ka an yɛrɛ sabati tɔgɔw la, bonya, diplome, wari, hakili sɛgɛsɛgɛliɲɛni suguya, jogo dɔnnen, etc., etc., etc. An man kan ka ɲinɛ fo abada ko munafiqi ani miiriya ka cɛɲiw tiɲɛniw, olu bɛ an kɛ mɔgɔ gɔlɔnw ye, kɔrɔnw, lɔgɔlɔgɔnw, minnu tɛ se ka fɛn kura ye...

Saya nafa caman bɛ a la, ɲuman ani jugu bɛɛ. An ka "KABIR MASABA YESU KRISTO" ka fɛn min ye a ko: "Ayiwa, minnu sara, u ka u sara". Mɔgɔ caman bɛ ɲɛnamaya kɛ hali ni u sara baara bɛɛ la u yɛrɛ kan ani o sababu la, yɛlɛma gbansan bɛɛ la.

U ye mɔgɔw ye minnu bɛ u ka dogmɛ ani laɲinitaw kɔnɔ; mɔgɔw minnu ka gɛlɛya kɔrɔnw la; mɔgɔ kelen-kelenw minnu fa juru kɔrɔnw ye; mɔgɔw minnu bɛ jɔnya la fɛɛrɛ minnu bɛ kuma, sɔgɔsɔgɔ, minnu ma kan ka bɔ, tuma dɔw "dɔnbaw" bɛ dan ko u bɛ tiɲɛ na sabu o fɔra o ye, etc., etc., etc.

Mɔgɔw tɛ ɲini ka faamu ko diɲɛ in ye "Hakili Sannakali" ye, o sababu la a bɛ se ka fɛɛni gbɛlɛn min bɛ an kɔnɔ, o tiɲɛ... Ni mɔgɔ misɛnninw faamuna u bɛ ɲagamiya la, u bɛ jijiji ni fɛɛrɛ ye...

O tuma, mɔgɔw bɛ miiri u yɛrɛ la fɛn ɲuman, u bɛ u yɛrɛ sabati u ka jogo ɲumanw la, u bɛ u yɛrɛ sɛɛnɛɲɛn ko u dafalen, ɲuman, baarakɛlaw, bonya, jɛkuluw, hakilimanw, wajibiyalenw, etc. Ɲɛnamaya ka baara i ko kalanso ye fɛn ɲuman ye, nka ka o ta i ko laban ye a yɛrɛ la, o ye fɛn tiɲɛni ye.

Minnu bɛ ɲɛnamaya ta a yɛrɛ la, i n'a fɔ a bɛ ɲɛnamaya kɛ cɛn cɛn, o ma wajibiya min ka kan ka baara kɛ u yɛrɛ kan walasa ka "Yɛlɛma Gban" sɔrɔ. A ka gɛlɛn, mɔgɔw bɛ ɲɛnamaya kɛ i ko mekaniki ye, u ma fɛn dɔ mɛɛn abada baara kɔnɔ...

Yɛlɛma ka kan ka kɛ, nka mɔgɔw ma dɔn cogo min na yɛlɛma bɛ kɛ; u bɛ tɔɔrɔ kosɛbɛ, u ma dɔn fana sabu minnu kosɔn u bɛ tɔɔrɔ... Wari sɔrɔ tɛ fɛn bɛɛ ye. Mɔgɔw ka ɲɛnamaya minnu bɛ wari sɔrɔ, a bɛ gɛlɛya kosɛbɛ...
