Ɲɛnɛmagatigɛ minnu tɔgɔ bɔra ko mɔgɔ, olu tɛ yɛrɛmahɔrɔnya tigitigi sɔrɔ.

Ɲɛnɛmagatigɛ in ka hakili gosi tɛ kelen ye, o de bɔnɛ ni sunɔgɔ caman sababu ye.

Faramugu kelen ye, a bɛ baara kɛ i n'a fɔ fɛnɲɛnɛma kelen, fo ni a man kɛnɛ.

Nka, Ɲɛnɛmagatigɛ in ka ɲɛnɛma siɲɛɲɛma tɛ hakili kelen ye hali dɔɔni.

Kɔrɔbɔlenba ye, ɲɛnɛnadilan suguya ni gundo ɲɔgɔn suguya minnu bɛ fɔ, olu tɛ hakili jɔli sɔrɔ mɔgɔ kelen-kelen bɛɛ kɔnɔ.

Tiɲɛ na, ɲɛnɛma kɛcogo tɛ baara ɲuman kɛ kelen ye mɔgɔw ka ɲɛnɛma la.

Ɲɛnɛmagatigɛ, a ka ɲɛnɛma kɛcogo, hakili caman ye, "Ne" caman jɛɲɔgɔn ye.

Donkilila minnu ka hakili sigilen don wagati dibi in na, olu bɛ "Ne" bato, olu bɛ a kɛ ala ye, olu bɛ a bila sagofeerew kan, olu bɛ a wele ko "ALTER EGO", "Ne Juru", "Ne Ala", ani tɔgɔ tɛmɛnenw.

"Hakilimanw" tɛ mɔnɛ k'a dɔn ko "Ne Juru" walima "Ne Juru", olu ye Ego kelen min caman jiracogo ye...

Ɲɛnɛmagatigɛ tɛ "Ne Magantigi" sɔrɔ nka "Ne" tɛmɛnen caman minnu tɛ mɔgɔya ye ani minnu tɛ nafa sɔrɔ.

Fɛnɲɛnɛma hakiliman misɛnnin minnu tɔgɔ bɔra ko mɔgɔ, olu bɛ i n'a fɔ so min gosi tɛ, min kɔnɔ, i n'a fɔ matigi kelen tɛ, baaraden caman bɛ yen minnu bɛ ɲini ka tɛmɛ ani ka minnu bɛ a fɛ ka kɛ...

Ɲɛnɛnadilan minnu tɛ sɔngɔ gbɛlɛn ye, olu ka gosi gbele ye ka miiri ko tɔw bɛ "Ne Magantigi ani Minnu tɛ Yɛlɛma" sɔrɔ, minnu tɛ daminɛ ani minnu tɛ ban...

Ni olu minnu bɛ o miiri kɔnɔ ka wuli hakili la walima a tɛ kɛ sabati ye, olu bɛ se ka yira u yɛrɛw ye ko Ɲɛnɛmagatigɛ hakiliman tɛ kelen ye fo ka waati jan.

Ɲɛnɛmagatigɛ hakiliman bɛ yɛlɛma tuma bɛɛ ɲɛnɛma ɲɛda kan.

Ka miiri ko ni mɔgɔ tɔgɔ ye ko Luis, a ye Luis ye tuma bɛɛ, o bɛ i n'a fɔ tɔgɔjugu ɲɛnama dɔ ye...

A jirala a la ko Luis bɛ "Ne" tɔw sɔrɔ a kɔnɔ, Ego tɔw, minnu bɛ u yɛrɛ jira a ka mɔgɔya fɛ waati wɛrɛw la ani hali ni Luis tɛ fɛɛrɛ sɔrɔ, "Ne" tɔw a kɔnɔ —an bɛ a wele ko Pepe— bɛ fɛɛrɛ sɔrɔ ani o cogo la...

Mɔgɔ si tɛ kelen ye waati bɛɛ, tiɲɛ na, a tɛ ɲini ka hakilimanba ye walisa ka mɔnɛ ka yɛlɛma ni yɛlɛmali caman dɔn mɔgɔ kelen-kelen bɛɛ la...

Ka miiri ko mɔgɔ dɔ bɛ "Ne Magantigi ani Minnu tɛ Yɛlɛma" sɔrɔ, o bɛ i n'a fɔ ɲasiran ye ɲɔgɔn ni yɛrɛ yɛrɛw ma...

Mɔgɔ caman bɛ mɔgɔ kelen kɔnɔ, "Ne" caman, o bɛ se ka sɛgɛsɛgɛli kɛ a yɛrɛ fɛ ani a yɛrɛ fɛ, mɔgɔ min wulilen don, hakili sɔrɔlen...
