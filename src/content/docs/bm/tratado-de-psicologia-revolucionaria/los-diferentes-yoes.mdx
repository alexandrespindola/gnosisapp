Fɛnw ka caaman minnu tɛ hakili sɔn, minnu tɔgɔ bɔra ko cɛ, u tɛ sɔrɔ ka yɛrɛmahɔrɔnya dafalen sɔrɔ. Fɛnw ka caaman minnu tɛ hakili sɔn, o bɛ se sababu ye ka gɛlɛyaw ni dusu gɛlɛyaw caman sɔrɔ.

Faratayaso faralen don, a bɛ baara kɛ i ko faratayaso dafalen, fo ni bana bɛ a la. Nka, fɛnw ka caaman minnu tɛ hakili sɔn, u tɛ farataya sɔrɔ hakili ta fan fɛ. A ka gɛlɛn kosɛbɛ, hakili tɛ sɔrɔ ka dilan hakili ta fan fɛ mɔgɔ kelen kelen bɛɛ kɔnɔ, hali ni kalanso caman minnu bɛ ŋaniya sɔrɔ ka kɛ i ko seudo-esotérique ani seudo-occultiste, u ka kuma o kan.

Tiɲɛni, baara tɛ sɔrɔ ka ɲɛ, i ko fɛn dafalen mɔgɔw ka ɲɛnamaya kɔnɔ. Fɛnw ka caaman minnu tɛ hakili sɔn, u bɛ hakili caman ye, o bɛ wele ko "NE".

Dɔnkiliw bɛ "NE" bato, u bɛ a jato ko ala, u bɛ a bla sɔgɔdilanw kan, u bɛ a wele ko "ALTER EGO", "NE SANSIKƆ", "NE ALAA", ani wɛrɛw. "Sabihondow" minnu bɛ ɲɛnamaya kɛ sanjɛɲɛgɛn na, u tɛ sɔn k'a dɔn ko "NE SANSIKƆ" walima "NE KƆNƆGU" ye ego caman ta fan fila ye.

Fɛnw ka caaman minnu tɛ hakili sɔn, u tɛ "NE ɲɛnajɛsira" sɔrɔ, nka "NE" caman minnu tɛ adamadi ye, minnu bɛ ban kosɛbɛ. Bagantɛmɛ minnu bɛ hakili sɔn, minnu tɔgɔ bɔra ko cɛ, u man ɲɛ i ko so minnu labɛnnen tɛ, minnu kɔnɔ, i n'a fɔ patron kelen tɛ, baarakɛlaw caman bɛ yen minnu bɛ ɲinɛ ka mara ani ka baara kɛ i ko u bɛ ɲinɛ...

Seudo-ésotérisme ani Seudo-occultisme ka caaman ka gɛlɛn kosɛbɛ, a bɛ miiri ko wɛrɛw bɛ "NE ɲɛnajɛsira ni kanuya" sɔrɔ minnu bɛ yen tuma bɛɛ, minnu tɛ daminɛ ani ban... Ni mɔgɔ minnu bɛ miiri o kan, ni u wulira hakili la, hali n'a bɛ kɛ waati damadɔ dɔrɔn, u bɛ se ka ɲɛfɔ yɛrɛ yɛrɛ ko fɛnw ka caaman minnu tɛ hakili sɔn, u tɛ kɛ kelen ye waati caman...

Bagantɛmɛ minnu bɛ hakili sɔn, hakili ta fan fɛ, u bɛ yɛlɛma tuma bɛɛ... Ka miiri ko ni mɔgɔ tɔgɔ bɔra ko Louis, ko a bɛ kɛ Louis ye tuma bɛɛ, o bɛ kɛ i ko jɛkulu dɔ min man ɲi... Mɔgɔ minnu tɔgɔ bɔra ko Louis, u bɛ "NE" wɛrɛw sɔrɔ u kɔnɔ, egos wɛrɛw, minnu bɛ u yɛrɛ jira u ka ɲɛnajɛsiraliw fɛ waati caman na, hali ni Louis tɛ sɔn maana kan, "NE" wɛrɛ min bɛ a kɔnɔ, a bɛ wele ko Pepe, a bɛ sɔn maana kan, ani o cogo la.

Mɔgɔ si tɛ kelen ye waati bɛɛ; a tɛ ɲini ka hakilitigiya sɔrɔ walisa ka yɛlɛmaw ni kuntilennafɔlɔw minnu tɛ hakili sɔn mɔgɔ kelen kelen bɛɛ kɔnɔ, o dɔn... Ka miiri ko mɔgɔ dɔ bɛ "NE ɲɛnajɛsira ni kanuya" sɔrɔ, o bɛ kɛ i n'a fɔ juguya ye mɔgɔ ɲɛɛ ani yɛrɛ yɛrɛ ma...

Mɔgɔ caman bɛ ɲɛnamaya kɛ mɔgɔ kelen kelen bɛɛ kɔnɔ, "NE" caman, mɔgɔ minnu wulira, hakili bɛɛ, o bɛ se ka o ɲɛfɔ yɛrɛ yɛrɛ ani yɛrɛ yɛrɛ.
