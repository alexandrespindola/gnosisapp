Ka bɔ ka bɔɲakɔlɔn juguw la ɲɛnagwɛrɛ fitiriw kɔnɔ, o ka gɛlɛn kosɛbɛ nka a wajibiyalen don, ni o tɛ, ɲɛnama bɛ mɔgɔ ci.

Baara min o baara mɔgɔ bɛ a yɛrɛ kan walasa ka hakili ni hakiliya sɔrɔ, o bɛ tali kɛ tuma bɛɛ ka bɔli la min faamuyalen don kosɛbɛ, bawo ɲɛnama ka barika kɔnɔ i n’a fɔ an bɛ a ɲɛnama ɲɛ minɛ cogo min na, a tɛ se ka fɛɛn wɛrɛ dege fo yɛrɛmahɔrɔnya.

An tɛ ɲini ka jɔ yɛrɛmahɔrɔnya dege ɲɛ, a yɛrɛ bɛ a wajibiyalen don ɲɛnama kɔnɔ, nka a ye fɛɛn ye min sigilen don dɔrɔn, a tɛ tiɲɛ ye, an kɔnɔna tiɲɛ.

Ni bagan hakilima misali min tɔgɔ bɔra ko cɛ, a bɔ ma, nka a yɛrɛ yɛrɛ ye ɲɛnama baarakɛyɔrɔw bɛɛ la ani a ka fanga tigɛ kɔnɔbaliya ni yɛrɛ-jatikɛ ni kumakan gbɛlɛn ɲɛmɔgɔya la, fɛɛn tiɲɛ si tɛ se ka dege a kɔnɔ, ka bɔ fɛɛn min bɛ mekanikiyako diɲɛ ta.

Tiɲɛ na, min b’a ɲini ka ɲɛtaa sɔrɔ a kɔnɔ, o wajibiyalen don ka datugu. O bɛ tali kɛ fɛɛn dɔ la min gundo don min jɛlen don ni jɛɲɔgɔnya ye.

Kumakan bɔra waati kɔrɔw la, tuma min na kalanso gundo tun bɛ cɛɛ kɔnɔna dege kan min jɛlen don ni Hermes tɔgɔ ye.

Ni mɔgɔ b’a ɲini fɛɛn tiɲɛ dɔ ka bonya a kɔnɔ, a yɛrɛ bɛ a ɲɛnabɔli ka fanga ka bɔli ɲɛ.

Tuma min na fanga bɔli bɛ mɔgɔ fɛ ani a bɔ ma a gundoya la, a tɛ ɲininkali, a tɛ se ka fɛɛn tiɲɛ dɔ dege a hakili la.

Ɲɛnama tɛmɛnenw b’a fɛ ka an ci gɛlɛyaw la; an wajibiyalen don ka ɲɛnama kɛlɛ lɛrɛw bɛɛ, an wajibiyalen don ka dafiɲɛ tɛmɛli kalan...

Baara nin bɛ ɲɛnama ɲɛ, a ye fɛɛn ye min tɛ kelen ye ni lɛrɛw bɛɛ ye, nka an wajibiyalen don ka a kɛ lɛrɛw bɛɛ la; n bɛ ɲini ka tali kɛ hakili ɲɛnamayeli la.

A yɛrɛ bɛ a ɲɛnabɔli ni an ka ɲɛnama tɛmɛnenw ka jate ye ɲɛ.

Mɔgɔw b’a fɛ fɛnw ka ɲɛ taa u ye, “o ye munna”, bawo fɛɛn bɛɛ wajibiyalen don ka taa u ka ladilikan ɲɛ, nka tiɲɛ juguman ye danfɛn ye, tuma min na mɔgɔ ma yɛrɛwɛlɛma a kɔnɔ, a bɛ a kan, walima a tɛ a kan, a bɛ danfɛnw sɔrɔ tuma bɛɛ.

A bɛ fɔ ani ka sɛbɛn ɲɛnama kan, hakilintan tɔgɔ bɔra kosɛbɛ, nka sigiyeli ɲɛnamayeliw ka ladilikan nin ye danfɛn ye.

Ladilikan nin bɛ taa cɛgɛn na, baarakɛyɔrɔw ka gundo, ka ɲɛ ani ka dafalen; a bɛ fɔ gɛlɛyaw la ko “Bagan Hakilima” min tɔgɔ bɔra cɛɛ, a ye bɛɛn tɛmɛnen ye, hakilitɔn, sɔgɔlen.

“So Jɔnjɔn Ɲuman” tɛna sɔn siigiɲɛnamayeli la abada; a bɛ a ka kanuya bɛɛ kɛ i n’a fɔ faw, furucɛ, n’a ɲɔgɔnna, ani o kosɔn a b’a hakili la a ka fisa a yɛrɛ ye, nka a bɛ kɛ diɲɛw ka lɔɲɔniya dɔrɔn ye ani o ye a dafalen ye.

Ka jɔli kɛ, an bɛ fɔ ko “So Jɔnjɔn Ɲuman” fana bɛ yen min bɛ dafiɲɛ tɛmɛ, min tɛ a ɲini ka ci ɲɛnama fɛ; nka, o mɔgɔw ka tɔɔrɔ diɲɛ kɔnɔ, u tɛ bonya abada.

Tuma min na mɔgɔ bɛ miiri ka bɛn sigiyeli ɲɛnamayeliw ka ladilikan hakiliw la, a bɛ ɲɛnama ɲɛnabɔli sɔrɔ.
