FEBURUWALI tile 20 fo marisi tile 21

An bɛ Egipte ka Kɔsimɔlɔji SU-BA, Jɛgɛjɛgɛda jukɔrɔba, ABSTRACT ABSOLU jɔrɔjɔrɔ tɛmɛnen tɛmin na INISIYATIKI dibi; abisi fɔlɔ min na ondine bɛ Rhin ka sanu mara walima Hakili Senu ka Taa min bɛ ɲɛnabɔli.

JƐGƐJƐGƐDA dilannen don ni hakilitigiya ye ni JƐGƐ fila ye; Jɛgɛ, Jɛgɛ, o ye ISI ka gundo SOMA ye. JƐGƐ YE FƆLƆ FƆLƆ FƆLƆ KRISITIANKIW ka ɲɛnɛma ɲɛjirali ɲɛnama ye.

JƐGƐJƐGƐDA fila minnu bɛ JƐGƐJƐGƐDA la, minnu bɛ fara ɲɔgɔn kan ni ɲɛjirali ye, o ɲɛjirali dalilɛnba dɔw bɛ yen, olu bɛ ELOHIM PRIMORDIAL niɲɔgɔnnaw ɲɛjirali minnu gilisilen bɛ SU-BA jukɔrɔba jiw cɛma.

An y’a ɲɛfɔ a tɛmɛnen kɛrɛnkɛrɛnnenw kɔnɔ ko ƝƆGƆN, SER, ATMAN, niɲɔgɔnnaw fila bɛ yen: muso dɔ, cɛ dɔ.

An y’a ɲɛfɔ ko NIƝƆGƆN SIRALEN, BUDDHI, muso don. An y’a fɔ ani an bɛ a lajɛ walisa a ka ɲɛ, ko NIƝƆGƆN MƆGƆ, MANAS gundɔ, cɛ don.

Furu senu, FURU SƐNƐNƐ KƐLƐKƐLEN TƐMINEN, bɛ ɲɛjirali tuma bɛɛ ni jɛgɛ fila ye minnu bɛ fara ɲɔgɔn kan ni ɲɛjirali ye; laban in ye SER ATMAN ye.

Furu senu, JƐGƐ fila tɛminenninw, bɛ baara kɛ abisi jiw cɛma ni MAHANVANTARA wuliko bɛ se.

JƐGƐ fila minnu tɛ fɔ, olu bɛ baara kɛ ATMAN ka ɲɛmɔgɔya jukɔrɔ, ni dan ɲɛnabɔli wati bɛ se.

Nka, a ka ɲi ka hakili jigi ko ISI ani OSIRIS tɛ se ka baara kɛ JAMA ba la, ni FILOSOFÍA GUNDƆ SIRILEN ka MERCURIO ɲɛnama tɛ. Jukɔrɔ in bɛ seko bɛɛ da la MERCURIO SIGI la.

Dɔgɔkun min ni jɔrɔn jɔrɔn min bɛ tɛmɛn ɲɔgɔn kan, o ye ɲɔgɔn dalajɛ sariyalen don min bɛ TƐMINEN MUSO ni TƐMINEN CƐ cɛma; ɲɔgɔn bɛnna INTEGRAZION ka MONADA ɲɛnama kɔnɔ, min tɛ fɔ, ɲɛnama ni Senu.

BAJI-BA fanba la, Mɔnada bɛ bɔ, SER. JAMA JUKƆRƆBA LA, ELOHIM bɛ wuli walisa ka baara kɛ MAHANVANTARA wuliko la.

JI ye muso jɔrɔn ye minnu dan, olu bɛɛ, minnu bɛ MATER LATINA ni lɛtɛri M la, min bɛ siran kosɛbɛ DIVINA.

KRISTIANYA DALILƐNBA LA, MARÍA ye ISI kelen ye, KOSIMOS ka BA, BAJI-BA-JAMA min tɛ tɛmɛn, abisi jiw jukɔrɔba.

Kuma MARÍA bɛ taran ni lɛtɛri fila ye; fɔlɔ ye MAR ye, min bɛ hakili jigi an ma ni JƐGƐJƐGƐDA JAMA jukɔrɔba. Filanan ye ÍA ye, min ye IO (iiioooo) fɔlɔ ye, BAJI-BA yɛrɛ ye, dɔgɔkun min bɛ ɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ, min fɛ fɛn bɛɛ bɛ bɔ ani min fɛ fɛn bɛɛ bɛ segin; kelen, kelen kelenpe minnu ɲɛfɔlen bɛ duniya kɔnɔ, PRALAYA ba walima halakikan ba kɔfɛ.

Ji gundɔw bɛ tɛgɛ jukɔrɔw kan, nɔrɔjɔrɔn ye a kɛ, olu ye kuma tɛgɛlen ye, KOSIMOS ɲɛnama dɔrɔn, DEEN, ani nin ɲɛnama in bɛ jɔrɔn sɔrɔ i ko tɛgɛ, min bɛ sɔrɔ an ka tile sistema cɛmancɛ la, i ko dusukun an ka fɛɛrɛ kɔnɔ.

TILE ka fɛn fɛn minnu ɲɛnama don, olu ye TƐGƐ ɲɛnama jɔrɔn ye minnu bɛ jɔrɔn sɔrɔ planɛti kelen-kelen bɛɛ cɛmancɛ, olu bɛ planɛti kelen-kelen bɛɛ dusukun kɛ.

Nɔrɔjɔrɔn in bɛɛ, NIN ɲɛnama in bɛɛ, bɛ ɲɛjirali ni niɲɔgɔnnaw wɔɔrɔ ye, minnu bɛ sanfɛ tron ɲɛ, BON-DUSUKUNw kɔnɔ planɛti wɔɔrɔ minnu bɛ TILE SISTEMA LA.

Ji tɛgɛli baara jiw kan, o bɛ furu senu kan. NIƝƆGƆNNINW WƆƆRƆ kelen-kelen bɛɛ, minnu bɛ TRON ɲɛ, olu bɛ bɔ ɲɛnama ɲɔgɔn kɔnɔ, JƐGƐ senu niɲɔgɔnnaw walisa u ka baara kɛ dan ɲɛnabɔli la ni KRIYASAKTY ka seko, kuma tununna ka seko, KANULEN seko ni YOGA.

KANULEN kanulenninw, ɲɛnɛma dususɛnbɔli laban TƐMINEN CƐ ani FURU SƐNƐNƐ niɲɔgɔnnin cɛ, o bɛ ɲɛnama walisa JI SANFƐJIW ka JI JUKƆRƆJIW tɛgɛ.

Nin BAARA in na, MAITHUNA TƐMƐNEN bɛ yen; KRIYASAKTY, kuma daalen.

A bɛ tɛgɛ jira ani a bɛ ji tɛmɛ, a bɛ SANFƐJIW tɛgɛ ni JUKƆRƆJIW ye.

Jɛgɛ fila minnu bɛ tɛgɛ in jira ani ji sanfɛ min tɛmɛnen don KAOS jiw kan fɛn fɛnma walima fɛn dunya walisa dunuɲaw, ɲɛnama jɔrɔ minnu sinna ani ɲɛnama bɛ bɔ.

BAARA bɛɛ bɛ kɛ ni KUMA ɲɛmɔgɔya ye ani ni KANULEN ni YOGA ye.

A daminɛ na, DUNYA ye gundɔ ye, a bɛ jɔrɔn sɔrɔ i ko fɛn, a bɛ tɛmɛ KRISTALIZAZION dilancɛ dɔw kɔnɔ.

DUNYA miliyɔni dɔw bɛ yen jɔrɔjɔrɔ tɛmɛnen na, min bɛ BAJI-BA kɔnɔ.

DUNYA dɔw bɛ bɔ PRALAYA la, a bɛ bɔ JƐGƐJƐGƐDA jiw jukɔrɔba la, dɔw bɛ baara kɛ cɛmancɛ, dɔw bɛ ɲɔgɔn cɛni ji tɛminenninw kɔnɔ.

Fɛn si tɛ se ka kɛ ISI ani OSIRIS la MERCURIO SIGI tɛ, jɛgɛ fila tɛminenninw, olu bɛ kanu, olu bɛ ɲɔgɔn bato ani olu bɛ ɲɛnama tuma bɛɛ ani olu bɛ segin walisa ka da.

Jɛgɛ ye ɲɛjirali sariyalenba ye KRISTIANYA ɲɛnama FƆLƆ. A ka ɲi kojugu ko ɲɛfɔlɔ miliyɔni dɔw ka jɛgɛ gundo ɲɛɲini ɲɛna.

An ka planɛti dugukolo kan, JAMANINW wɔɔrɔ bɛ ɲɛnama ni fari gundɔw ye ani wɔɔrɔ bɛɛ la, laban ye an ta ye, kelen min halakilen don walisa a ka tunu GNOSIS.

JAMANINW wɔɔrɔw bɛ ɲɛnama JINAS ka jɔrɔn na, ƝƐNAAN JƆRƆN na, a bɛ dugukolo kɔnɔ, a bɛ mara JINAS ani regionw dɔw la.

JƐGƐJƐGƐDA san tun tɛna kɛ hɛɛrɛ i ko a kɛra cogo min na. CAUSA CAUSORUM JƐGƐJƐGƐDA ka halakili ye jɔrɔjɔrɔ dibiw ye minnu bɛ GNOSIS tɔgɔjugu ani ka DOTTRINAS AGNÓSTICAS walima ANTI-GNÓSTICAS waajuli, minnu bɛ JƐGƐ jɛmɛtugu, minnu bɛ DINI HAKILIKƐDA da, a bɛ dunuɲa tɔgɔ jɛmɛtugulen na fɛnw na.

An bɛ hakili jigi LUCIO ka se HYPATIA ka dugu la, a bɛ dogo o kɔfɛ MILON ka bon kɔnɔ, a furu PÁNFILA ye siɲɛgɛn siɲɛgɛn dɔ ye. LUCIO bɛ jɛgɛ sɔrɔla (ICTUS, KRISTIANYA dalilɛn dalijɛlen, ɲɛnama, JƐGƐ, jɛgɛ, SOMA, ISI ka gundo).

Jɛgɛlɔnkɛlaw bɛ a sɔrɔ siɲɛ ɲɛmɛni denari mɛlɛnna ni miiriya dɔ ye min bɛ fɔ kosɛbɛ, o minnu tun bɛ miiriya ka sanu kɛmɛ sɔrɔ, satiri dɔ min tɛ jɔ, min bɛ jalaki ɲɛnama ni tuma min tun bɛ KRISTIANYA GNÓSTICO jalaki.

KRISTIANYA AGNÓSTICO walima ANTI-GNÓSTICO ka sɔrɔ ye MARXISTA ka MATERALISTA DIALÉCTICA ye.

GNÓSTICISMO kan, olu ye MATERALISMO misɛnnikɛnniw ye ni DIOS tɛ ani sariya tɛ.

A bɛ se ka jɔyɛrɛ ko JƐGƐJƐGƐDA san halakilen don AGNÓSTICISMO fɛ. GNOSIS tɔgɔjugu ye jɔrɔ min ka gundo ka gɛlɛn JƐGƐJƐGƐDA.

JESÚS el CRISTO ani a ka jɛgɛlɔnkɛlaw tan ni fila, olu y’a daminɛ ni san ye min tun bɛ se ka kɛ jɔrɔjɔrɔ dɔw la.

JESÚS ani a ka TƆGƆJUGUW TAN NI FILA GNÓSTICOS yɛrɛ, olu y’a jira sira min ka ɲi JƐGƐJƐGƐDA san walisa a ka ɲi, GNÓSTICISMO, HAKILIKƐDA JƐGƐLA.

A ka ɲimi kosɛbɛ ko kitaabuw bɛɛ sariyalen SANTA GNOSIS tun sunɔgɔlen don ani ko jɛgɛ sariyalen dalilɛn sunɔgɔlen don.

BAARA. JƐGƐJƐGƐDA taamasiyɛn kɔnɔ, a ka kan ka ɲɛfɔ dɔgɔkun kelen. An bɛ hakili jigi ko daminɛ na KUMA tun bɛ yen ani ko KUMA tun bɛ DIOS la ani ko KUMA tun ye DIOS ye.

Tuma kɔrɔw la, danɲɛnatigɛ saba minnu bɛ an fari bɛɛ la, olu tun bɛ jɔrɔn sɔrɔ an ka fɛɛrɛ bɛɛ kɔnɔ ka bɔ kun na fo senw na, sisan a ka kan ka dɔnniya sanba wɔɔrɔw tɛgɛ tɛgɛ an ka fɛɛrɛ la, walisa seko tununna tɛgɛ.

Lɛtɛri "I" bɛ glandulas PINEAL ani PITUITARIA minnu jɔrɔn sɔrɔ; glandulita fila ninw bɛ kun na, u bɛ fara ɲɔgɔn kan ni kanali wala capilar dɔ ye min ka gɛlɛn kosɛbɛ, a sunɔgɔlen don cɛɲi dɔw la.

PINEAL bɛ sɔrɔ kunba sanfɛ, a pituitaria bɛ sɔrɔ plexo cavernario cɛma ɲɛ cɛma.

Glandulita fila ninw bɛɛ niɲɔgɔn bɛ NIɲƆGƆNNINW ɲɛnama ani ni niɲɔgɔnnaw niɲɔgɔnnaw bɛ ɲɔgɔn cɛɲi, SENSAZIONI bɛ sɔrɔ ani an bɛ a ULTRAS sɔrɔ fɛnw bɛɛ la.

Lɛtɛri "E" bɛ glandula tiroides minnu jɔrɔn sɔrɔ, minnu bɛ YODO BIOLÓGICO jira. Glandula in bɛ sɔrɔ kan la ani a kan na chacra bɛ yen kunmabɛlɛni ka.

Lɛtɛri "O" bɛ chacra minnu jɔrɔn sɔrɔ dusukun na, ɲɛfɔli cɛmancɛ, ani seko cɛncɛ walisa ka Bɔ astrali la, jɔrɔn JINAS, etc.

Lɛtɛri "U" bɛ PLEXO SOLARI minnu jɔrɔn sɔrɔ, minnu bɛ sɔrɔ bolo region na. PLEXO SOLARI nin ye Telepático Cɛmancɛ ani Cerebro emocionari ye.

Lɛtɛri "A" bɛ chacra pulmonar minnu jɔrɔn sɔrɔ, olu bɛ a to an ka hakili jigi an ka ɲɛnama tɛmɛnenw la.

Lɛtɛri "M", min yɛrɛ gundɔlen bɛ i ko consonanti, a bɛ ɲɛfɔ ni da gundɔw ye, a bɛ da da tuma min na, jɔrɔ min bɛ bɔ nɔni na, o ye "M" ye.

Lɛtɛri "M", bɛ ENS SEMINIS minnu jɔrɔn sɔrɔ, ɲɛnama jiw, filozofía gundɔ ka MERCURIO.

Lɛtɛri "S" ye niɲɔgɔnnin dɔ ye, min bɛ tɛgɛ minnu jɔrɔn sɔrɔ an kɔnɔ.

Kɔsigilen sillon ɲɛnama dɔ la, a ka kan ka ɲɛfɔ I. E. 0. U. A. M. S. ka tɛmɛ ni danɲɛnatigɛ kelen-kelen bɛɛ ka bɔ kun na fo senw na.

A ka kan ka ɲɛsɔmɛni ka bɔ ni ɲɛjɔrɔn ɲɔgɔn kan ni ɲɛfɔlɔ min ka ɲi, fo ka fɔni bɔ.

BAARA nin ka kan ka kɛ dɔgɔkun bɛɛ walisa a ka danɲɛma seko ɲɛnama.

JƐGƐJƐGƐDA mara NEPTUNO ye, planɛti fɛn gundo baara ani ka JÚPITER TONANTE, DIOSW ka Ba.

Jɛgɛjɛgɛda ka metal ye ESTAÑO ka Júpiter ye; kabakuruw, AMATISTA, CORALES. Jɛgɛjɛgɛda bɛ SEN mara.

Jɛgɛjɛgɛda jamaba bɛ furuw fila sɔrɔ tuma bɛɛ, den caman. Olu ye niɲɔgɔnnin ye ani u bɛ jɔrɔn sɔrɔ walisa ka baara minnu kɛ. Jɛgɛjɛgɛda ka jamaba ka gɛlɛn ka ɲɛfɔ, olu bɛ ɲɛnama i ko jɛgɛ, fɛn bɛɛ la, nka olu bɛ tɛgɛ fɛn bɛɛ la, ji jɔrɔn bɔlen. Olu bɛ jɔrɔn sɔrɔ fɛn bɛɛ la, nka jukɔrɔla u bɛ dunuɲa fɛn bɛɛ jɛmɛtugulen. Olu ye dusukalanni ɲɛnama ye, ɲɛfɔlɔ, u gundɔ ani jamaba tɛ se ka u ɲɛfɔ.

Jɛgɛjɛgɛda ka jamaba bɛ jɔrɔn sɔrɔ walisa fɛn gundo kɛ, walisa jɛgɛjɛgɛda mara NEPTUNO ye, planɛti ESOTERISMO.

MUSOW JƐGƐJƐGƐDA ka, olu ye dusukalanni kosɛbɛ, olu bɛ dusukalanni kosɛbɛ i ko ɲɛgɛ ɲɛnama; ɲɛfɔlɔ, minnu bɛ jɔrɔn sɔrɔ kosɛbɛ.

Piscianos ka jamaba bɛ miiriya ɲɛnama sɔrɔ, u ye nisɔndiya ye, niɲɔgɔnnin, cɛɲi ni u ka miiriya ye.

Jugu minnu bɛ Piscianos kan, olu ye ka bin ɲɔgɔn na, ka kɔnɔ, ka tɛmɛnen tɛmɛnen ani ka miiriya ka ɲɛnama.

Piscianos bɛ se ka don hakilila kɛlɛɲɛma. Hakili Piscianos tɛ se ka tɛmɛni ɲɛnama sɔrɔ wala a tɛ se ka kɔnɔ ani ka jɛmɛtugulen fɛnw kan minnu ka kan ka kɛ walisa ka ɲɛnama. Olu ye kunun dɔw ye ani u bɛ don kunun kelen kan i ko ɲɔgɔnnin. Piscianos ka kanuleni tuma dɔw la a ka gundɔ, nka a bɛ tɛmɛni tuma dɔw la.

Tuma min na Piscianos bɛ bin ɲɔgɔn na, a tɛ se ka ɲɛfɔ ani a tɛ se ka tɛmɛni, a bɛ ji jɔrɔn kan jɔrɔn na, nka tuma min na u bɛ u jɔrɔ jɔrɔ, u bɛ u kanuleni ka tɛgɛ da ani o tuma na u bɛ u ka maana bɛɛ tɛmɛni.

Piscianos gundɔ ka bon minnu ye GNÓSTICOS ye dɔgɔkun kɛmɛ la, olu bɛ kanuleni ka tɛgɛ minnu tɛ bin ɲɔgɔn na ani dusukalanni ɲɛnama jɔrɔjɔrɔ kosɛbɛ.

Jɛgɛjɛgɛda ka jɔrɔ min ka bon, o bɛ jɔrɔ jɔrɔ kosɛbɛ, JAMAW, MAESTROS, AVATARAS, REYES, INICIADOS, etc., etc.

Jɛgɛjɛgɛda jɔrɔ min ka gɛlɛn, o bɛ miiriya ka LUZURIA, alicoholismo, glotonería, ka kɔnɔ, ka jaajɛ.

Piscianos bɛ ɲɛnama walisa ka taama, nka u bɛɛ tɛ se ka taama. Piscianos bɛ miiriya ka bon ani dusukalanni caman.

A ka gɛlɛn kojugu walisa a ka JɛGƐJƐGƐDA ɲɛfɔ, jɛgɛjɛgɛda dɔrɔn bɛ se ka JɛGƐJƐGƐDA ɲɛfɔ.

Fɛn bɛɛ minnu ka bon walisa jamaba ka ɲɛnama, jɛgɛjɛgɛda kan, a tɛ baara kɛ, nka a ye diplomático ye, a bɛ fara jamaba kan, a bɛ a jira ko a bɛ fara ɲɔgɔn kan ni u ye.

Fɛn min ka gɛlɛn jɛgɛjɛgɛda ka jamaba kan, o ye a ka kan ka ɲɛfɔ ɲɔgɔn na walisa u ka bɔ ɲɔgɔn na, walisa kanuya fila minnu ka gundɔ, olu bɛ u don sira min tɛ se ka bɔ.

Jɔrɔ min ka bon ka JɛGƐJƐGƐDA bɛ tɛmɛnin fɛbɛlɛnw in kan ani a ye kɛ ASTO ye cogo bɛɛ la.

Tuma bɛɛ, Piscianos bɛ tɔjɔ kosɛbɛ n’u ka fɛn bɔɲɔgɔnna u ka san fɔlɔw la.

A ka gɛlɛn ka Pisciano sɔrɔ min sɔrɔlen don ni a ka fɛn bɔɲɔgɔnna a ka san fɔlɔw la.

MUSOW JɛGƐJƐGƐDA minnu ka gɛlɛn kosɛbɛ, olu bɛ don jɔɔn ani alcoolismo.

Musow JɛGƐJƐGƐDA minnu ka bon, olu tɛ bin ɲɔgɔn na, a bɛ i ko ɲɛgɛ dɔ min ka ɲɛnama, i ko LOTA ɲɛnama dɔ min ka ɲɛ.
