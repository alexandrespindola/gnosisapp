Kristo ye tasuma ka tasuma ye, ale ye tasuma ka flama ye, tasuma siginɛtiri.

Kalvari maritiiri ka gɛlɛya kan, Kristo gundo jɛrɛ don ni kumakɛrɛŋkɛrɛnnen dɔ ye min jɛɲɔgɔn na ni lɛtɛri naani ye: INRI. Ignis Natura Renovatur Integram – Tasuma bɛ daɲɛw bɛɛ nɔgɔya tɛmɛsiɲɛ don.

Kristo ka na adamaden dusukun na, a bɛ an yɛlɛma pewu.

Kristo ye LOGOS SOLAR ye, kelenya caman dafalen. Kristo ye ɲɛnamaya ye min bɛ siran diɲɛ bɛɛ kɔnɔ, a ye min ye, min tun bɛ yen tuma bɛɛ ani min bɛna kɛ tuma bɛɛ.

Fɛɛrɛ caman fɔlen don diɲɛ gundo kan; o gundo in dabɔlen don ni injilila naani ye.

A fɔlen don an ye ko Elohimw ye diɲɛ gundo in lase jamana ma; Atlantida masaba kuntigi ye gundo in jira fari ni joli kɔnɔ.

KABIR JESU ba bolo mankan fana tun ka kan ka gundo kelen in jira ɲɛnajɛ la jamana senuma kan.

Hali Kristo ka wuli siɲɛ ba caman Bɛtlɛhɛmu, o tɛ nafili foyi ni a ma wuli an dusukun na fana.

Hali a tun sara ani ka wuli tile saba ɲɛnamaya la, o tɛ nafili foyi ni a ma sara ani ka wuli an kɔnɔ fana.

Ka ɲini ka tasuma nɔgɔya ni a kun sɔrɔ, o ye ɲini ye ka Ala sɔrɔ, min gundo lakalen don tuma bɛɛ tasuma cɛɲi kɔnɔ.

Buruju min bɛ tasuma bilasira (Exode, III, 2) ani Sinayi tasuma dalilɛ kɔfɛ Decalogue diɲɛ (Exode, XIX, 18): ye yira naaninan ye min sababu fɛ Ala yira Musa ye.

Jaspɛ jɛɲɔgɔn naani ani Sardɔniko min mankan ye tasuma mankan ye, min sigilen kɔnɔ mansaya yɛrɛ ma min jɔlen ani min bɛ yɛrɛbɔ, Zan senuman bɛ diɲɛ kisi kuntigi jira. (Apokalipsi, IV, 3,5). "An ka Ala ye tasuma bilasira ye", Paalo senuman sɛbɛnna Heburuw ma.

Kristo min bɛ an kɔnɔ, sankolo tasuma, ka kan ka wuli an kɔnɔ ani ka wuli tiɲɛ na tuma min na an tɛmɛnenba baara Psychology baara kɔnɔ.

Kristo min bɛ an kɔnɔ ka kan ka bɔ an Psychology nɔgɔya la, sababuw kelenw kelenw: YOES SABABUW.

A tɛna se ka kɛ ka EGO sababuw lɔgɔlɔgɔli ta fanba la Kristo min bɛ an kɔnɔ ma wuli an kɔnɔ.

Tasuma ɲɛnamayan ani Filosofal, Kristo min bɛ an kɔnɔ, ye tasuma ka tasuma ye, min nɔgɔlen ka nɔgɔya.

Tasuma bɛ an dafa ani ka an kɔlɔsi yɔrɔ bɛɛ la, a bɛ na an fɛ ni fɛn ye, ji ye ani jamana kelen min bɛ fɛnw mara ani min bɛ a taamasɛnw caman mara.

Sankolo tasuma ka kan ka da an kɔnɔ, o ye Kristo ye min bɛ an kɔnɔ, an ka kisibaga dalilɛ.

Masaba min bɛ an kɔnɔ ka kan ka jɔ an ka Psychology bɛɛ la Maschine Organique sigida duuruw; an ka miiriya bɛɛ, dusukunnata, kɔgɔɲɛ, tɛmɛsiɲɛw Seksɛli.
