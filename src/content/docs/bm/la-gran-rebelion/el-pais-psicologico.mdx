Tiɲɛ na, i n’a fɔ dugukolo min bɛ kɛnɛ kan, an bɛ ɲɛnama sɔrɔ yɛrɛ la, o cogo kelen na, an ka yɛrɛmahɔrɔnya bɛ yen, psikoloji ka jamana.

Mɔgɔw tɛ taa dugu wala yɛrɛjɛ min kɔrɔ, nka a ɲɛnamaya, a ka kan ka jɛɲɔgɔn sɔrɔ psikoloji ka sigiyɔrɔ min na.

Sanga dɔw la, mɔgɔ o mɔgɔ bɛ a dɔn dugu min na, nka psikoloji ka jamana tɛ i n’a fɔ o cogo kelen na, a ka c’a la, mɔgɔw tɛ a filɛ siɲɛ kelen ka jamana, psikoloji ka jamana min na, u bɛ sɔrɔ.

I n’a fɔ diɲɛ kan, jamana bɛ yen, mɔgɔ ɲumanw ni kalanɲumanw bɛ yen, o cogo kelen na, a bɛ kɛ an kelen-kelen bɛɛ ka psikoloji ka yɛrɛjɛ la, sɔsɔli tɛ yen ko jamana dɔw ka gɛlɛn ani ka ɲi.

I n’a fɔ diɲɛ kan, jamana dɔw bɛ yen, wala jamana minnu bɛ siraw kan, u ka gɛlɛn kosɛbɛ, gbalo sugu bɛ yen, o cogo kelen na, a bɛ kɛ an ka psikoloji ka yɛrɛjɛ la an kɔnɔ.

A bɛ bɔ mɔgɔ suguya minnu bɛ an fɛ, ni ɲɔgɔn ka mɔgɔ ɲinumanw bɛ an fɛ, an bɛ taa minɛn na, ani ni u ye kalaɲɛw ye, a tɛ sɔsɔli, an ka daɲɛ bɛna kɛ jɛɲɔgɔnya dugu la.

An ka psikoloji ka jamana kɔnɔ, mɔgɔ kelen-kelen bɛɛ bɛ a ka jɛɲɔgɔnw sɔrɔ, a ka « YOs », olu bɛna taa mɔgɔ kelen-kelen bɛɛ la, yɔrɔ minnu na, u ka kan ka taa, ka bɔ a ka psikoloji ka ɲɛjiraliw la.

Muso ɲuman ani muso boni, cɛmuso ɲuman, taamacɛɲuman, a bɛ ɲɛnamaya sɔrɔ mansiyɔn ɲuman na diɲɛ kan, a ka « YOs » minnu bɛ sɔsɔli la, a bɛ se ka sigi jɛɲɔgɔnyaw ka duguw kɔnɔ a ka psikoloji ka jamana kɔnɔ.

Cɛkɔrɔba boni, tiɲɛ tɛ a la, cɛɲuman, a bɛ se ka sɔrɔ a ka psikoloji ka yɛrɛjɛ la, a bɛ se ka sigi funteniw ka gosi la, a ka jɛɲɔgɔn juguw kosɔn, funteniw ka « YOs », minnu bɛ sunɔgɔ kosɛbɛ a yɛrɛmahɔrɔnya kɔnɔ.

A bakɛni ani a tɔɲɔtɔ, a bɛ se ka kɛ monki ye, o bɛ ɲɛnamaya sɔrɔ, a tɛ foyi dɔn a ka selɛ kɔnɔ, monastɛri dɔw la, a bɛ se ka sigi psikoloji la, mɔgɔ fagala jamana kɔnɔ, pistoletiw, bɔnbaliw, tagasugu dɔnnikɛlaw, o kosɔn « YOs » minnu bɛ iɲɛsin tiɲɛni ma, minnu bɛ sunɔgɔ kosɛbɛ a ka jiwa kɔnɔ.

Foyi kosɔn, a fɔra an ye ko cɛɲi ka ca juguw la, ani ko jugu ka ca cɛɲumanw la.

Senuman caaman minnu labɛnna, olu bɛ ɲɛnama sɔrɔ, olu bɛ ɲɛnama sɔrɔ fana tagasugu ka psikoloji ka duguw la, wala jɛɲɔgɔnya ka so duguw la.

Kuntilenna minnu kan, an bɛ o sigi, o bɛ se ka yaala dɔw la, minnu tɛ foyi dɔn, kalanɲumanw la, hakilimanw la, nka, a tɛ psikoloji dɔnnikɛlaw bɛɛ kan.

Hali a bɛ i n’a fɔ ko a tɛ da a la, seli ka mankan cɛmɛ, tiɲɛ bɛ dafɛ, waati ka surun cɛmɛ, tiɲɛ bɛ dafɛ, sigiyɔrɔ senumanw ka sigiyɔrɔ sanba kɔrɔ, tiɲɛ bɛ a yɛrɛ masiri senumanya ni kuma senuman na.

Senuman minnu bonya ka ca, olu bɛ « YOs » sɔrɔ jɛɲɔgɔnyaw la, funteni la, mɔgɔ fagali la, ani wɛrɛw.

Mɔgɔ juguw jɛɲɔgɔnw minnu masirilen bɛ yɛrɛmahɔrɔnya kɔnɔ.

O kosɔn, senumanw minnu bɛ tarixi kɔnɔ, olu juguya sɔrɔla kosɛbɛ, an ka San Antonio tɔɲɔtaw bɛ hakili la, tiɲɛni suguya bɛɛ minnu kan, an balima Francisco de Asís ka kan ka kɛlɛ.

Nka, senuman ninnu ma fɔ foyi, bakɛniw ka caaman daɲɛ tɛ.

Mɔgɔ kelen-kelen bɛɛ bɛ yɛrɛyɛrɛw jɔli, bakɛniw bɛ ɲɛnamaya sɔrɔ jɛɲɔgɔnya ka psikoloji ka jamana na, ani funteni.

Nka, senumanw ye, ani ni u ma u ka jiwa ka fɛn jugumanw ye fɔlɔ, u bɛna sira jugumanw fɔ a kan, u bɛna ɲinɛ a kɔfɛ, a bɛ se ka kɛ ko u bɛna u yɛrɛ masiri, ani u bɛna a daala u ka kan ka Masaba KUNDALINI ka jiwa kɔrɔ, a bɛna jɛɲɔgɔn juguw sɔrɔ ka bɔ a ka jiwa la, olu bɛ o wɛrɛw don a yɛrɛ ka jamana na.

Dinɛw ka caaman fɔra ɲɛnamaya kan saheli kɔfɛ, ani wɛrɛw.

Ko mɔgɔ juguw kana u ka hakilintɔnw tiɲɛ, fɔlɔ, a bɛ tɛmɛ dugu kan.

Tiɲɛ na, saheli kɔfɛ, kelen-kelen bɛɛ bɛ taa ɲɛnamaya la a ka psikoloji ka jamana na.

Funteni tɛna funteniw ka duguw la, jɛɲɔgɔn tɛna taa ɲɛ ɲɛnama la i n’a fɔ farikolo juguman, tɔɔrɔɲuman, tɔɔrɔɲuman bɛna taa ɲɛ ɲɛnamaya la tɔɔrɔ ka siraw kan, ani tɔɔrɔ, yɔrɔ minnu na murutiw bɛ sɔrɔ, ani pistoletiw ka fɔlɔlɔ bɛ mɛɛn.

Dɔgɔtɔrɔya yɛrɛ, a ka ɲi kosɛbɛ, a bɔra sanfɛ, taraw la, a filila an ka « yo » kɔnɔ minnu bɛ an kɔnɔ.

Dɔgɔtɔrɔya bɛ se ka sira minɛ, a bɛ se ka segin sigiyɔrɔ fɔlɔ la, a bɛ segin taraw la, nka a ka kan ka a yɛrɛ sɔrɔ ka bɔ a ka jɛɲɔgɔn juguw la, olu bɛ a don tiɲɛni yɛrɛ kan.

Waati min na Francisco de Asís ani Antonio de Padua, karamɔgɔw bɛɛ minnu ye Kristo ye, olu ye tiɲɛni ka « yo » sɔrɔ u kɔnɔ, olu juguya sɔrɔla kosɛbɛ, ani sɔsɔli tɛ yen ko bangekɔrɔbaw ka baara ɲuman ni tɔɔrɔ minnu bɔra u yɛrɛ la, olu tɛna u ka fɛn jugumanw bɛɛ minnu bɛ ɲɛnamaya sɔrɔ u kɔnɔ, kɛ kasikɔsɔdɔriye ye. Tiɲɛ na, senuman ninnu bɛɛ ye Kiristo ye ani u seginna sigiyɔrɔ fɔlɔ la, u ka tɔɔrɔ kɔfɛ.

Fɔlɔ, a ka kan, a surun, a tɛ se ka ɲɛnajɛ, ko dɔgɔtɔrɔya min bɛ an fɛ, an ka yɛrɛmahɔrɔnya juguya la, a ka kan ka taa Dɔgɔtɔrɔya la, o cogo kelen na, cɛɲɛna bɛ se ka a ka taama daminɛ a ka yɛrɛmahɔrɔnya la, a ka taa taraw la, a bɛ tɛmɛ a ka kalan kan, a bɛ tɛmɛ a ka sɔrɔ kan ka bɔ dugu kan.

Waati min na, dɔgɔtɔrɔya bɛ sigi an ka yɛrɛmahɔrɔnya juguman kan, an bɛna ɲɛnamaya sɔrɔ psikoloji ka dugu juguw la, hali ni an ye cɛɲumanw ye ɲɛnamaya kɔnɔ.

Kelen-kelen bɛɛ bɛ dɔgɔtɔrɔya sɔrɔ min bɛ a ɲɛjirali, jagokɛla bɛ dɔgɔtɔrɔya sɔrɔ jagokɛ la, o kosɔn a bɛ taa jagokɛw la, a bɛ dɛsɛ fɛnw la minnu bɛ jagokɛw fɛ, sanbagatɔw ani jagokɛlaw.

Cɛ min bɛ dɔnniya sɔrɔ a ka yɛrɛmahɔrɔnya la, dɔnniya bɛ yen, o kosɔn a bɛ fɛn bɛɛ minɛ dɔnniya la, kitabuw, laboratɔriw ani wɛrɛw.

Esotɛrisita bɛ a yɛrɛ kɔnɔ dɔgɔtɔrɔya sɔrɔ, ani a kɔrɔbɔli min bɛ kɛ a ka yɛrɛmahɔrɔnya kan, sɔsɔli tɛ yen ko a bɛ tɛmɛ o kan.

Waati min na, dɔgɔtɔrɔya bɛ sigi yɛrɛmahɔrɔnya kan, a bɛ kɛ dɔgɔtɔrɔya la, o tuma na, segin bɛ daminɛ cɛɛ ka taraw la.
