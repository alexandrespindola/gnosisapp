HANKA wuxuu leeyahay sababo badan, mid ka mid ahna waa waxa loo yaqaan CABSI.

Wiilka is-hoosaysiiya ee ka nadiifiya kabaha ragga kibirkooda badan ee beerta magaalooyinka raaxada leh, wuxuu noqon karaa tuug haddii uu dareemo cabsi uu ka qabo faqriga, cabsi uu ka qabo naftiisa, cabsi uu ka qabo mustaqbalkiisa.

Gabadha tolmoon ee is-hoosaysiiya ee ka shaqaysa dukaanka weyn ee ninka hodanka ah, waxay noqon kartaa tuug ama dhillo habeen keli ah gudihiis, haddii ay dareento cabsi ay ka qabto mustaqbalka, cabsi ay ka qabto nolosha, cabsi ay ka qabto gabowga, cabsi ay ka qabto nafteeda, iwm.

Mudane-bixiyaha xariifsan ee maqaayadda raaxada leh ama hudheelka weyn, wuxuu noqon karaa GANSTER, burcad bankiyo, ama tuug aad u xeel dheer, haddii uu nasiib darro ka dareemo cabsi naftiisa, booskiisa hooseeya ee mudane-bixiyaha, mustaqbalkiisa, iwm.

Cayayaanka aan qiimaha lahayn wuxuu hanka ka galayaa inuu noqdo mid xariifsan. Shaqaalaha saboolka ah ee miiska ka shaqeeya ee u adeega macaamiisha oo si dulqaad leh noo tusaya garbasaarka, shaati, kabaha, isagoo sameynaya ixtiraam badan oo dhoola cadeynaya isagoo si been ah u deggan, wuxuu hanka ka galayaa wax ka badan sababtoo ah wuu cabsanayaa, cabsi badan, cabsi uu ka qabo misery, cabsi uu ka qabo mustaqbalkiisa mugdiga ah, cabsi uu ka qabo gabowga, iwm.

Marka qof uu ka shaqeeyo waxa uu jecel yahay, xirfaddiisa dhabta ah, wuxuu ku sameeyaa JACAYL sababtoo ah wuu JECEL YAHAY xirfaddiisa, sababtoo ah FALCELIYADIISA nolosha waa dhab ahaan kuwa xirfaddiisa.

Taasi dhab ahaan waa shaqada macallimiinta. In la ogaado sida loo hagaajiyo ardayda, in la ogaado awoodahooda, in loo hagaajiyo wadada xirfaddooda dhabta ah.
