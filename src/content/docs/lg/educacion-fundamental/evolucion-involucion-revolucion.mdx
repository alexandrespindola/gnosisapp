Mu bikolwa tubaddeko n'okukakasa nti AMASOMERO AGANIRIRA EBINTU BYEREERE n'AMASOMERO AGANIRIRA EBINTU EBY'OMWOYO byonna bikutte mu KIRAGIRO ky'ENKYUKAKYUKA.

Ebirowoozo ebyomulembe guno ku ntandikwa y'omuntu n'ENKYUKAKYUKA ye eyayita, mu buvumu bwabyo bukyankalanya bwankalanya, tebirina mutindo ogw'okunenyezebwa okw'amaanyi.

Wadde ng'enjigiriza za DARWIN zonna zikkirizigwa ng'ekintu EKITAGGWEERA OKUKKIRIZA ENZIBU ng'omuwandiisi CARLOS MARX n'ENJIGIRIZA YE EY'ENNEENYA ENTEGEKA, abasayansi ab'omulembe guno tebamanyi kintu ku ntandikwa y'omuntu, tebalina kye bamanyi, tebafunanga na kimu mu ngeri entuufu era tebalina bujulizi butuufu, obwenkanya, ku NKYUKAKYUKA Y'OMUNTU.

Kyokka, bwe tutwala abantu ab'edda, kwe kugamba, abo abaaliwo emyaka akakumi abiri oba akakumi asatu nga Yesu Kristo tannazaalibwa, tusanga obujulizi obutuufu, obubonero obuteesittala obw'omutindo gw'omuntu ogwa waggulu, obutategeerekeka eri abantu ab'omulembe guno, era obuliranwa bwe busobola okulagibwa obujulizi obungi, ENKOZESA Z'ABAJJA EZ'EDDA, ENNYUMBA ENNENE EZ'EDDA, EMITEEGO EGIZIIZI, EMPAPULA EZ'EKYAMA n'ebijjukizo eby'edda eby'enjawulo.

Ku bikwata ku MUNTU OW'EDDA, eri ebitonde eby'ekitalo era eby'ekyama eby'endabika efaanana n'ENYAMA EYOLEESA OBWENKANYI kyokka nga za njawulo nnyo, za njawulo nnyo, za kyama nnyo era amagumba gaabyo agazungulukidde gafunika nnyo ebiseera ebimu mu bifo eby'edda eby'ekiseera ky'omuzira oba Eky'omuzira Omutonotono, abasayansi ab'omulembe guno tebamanyi kintu mu ngeri entuufu era ng'omuntu ku gwe.

ESSOMO ERIKWATA KU BWENKANYI ligamba nti ENYAMA EY'OBWENKANYI nga bwe tugimanyi, SI KITONDE KITUFU, ekyagala okuba OMUNTU mu makulu agatuufu ag'ekigambo; obutonde bugikulakulanya okutuuka ku mutindo ogumu ate ne bugireka mu ddembe ery'okugenda mu maaso n'enkulakulana yaalyo oba okufiirwa ebintu byonna ebirina okukulaakulanyizibwa n'okwonooneka.

AMATEEKA g'ENKYUKAKYUKA n'OKUKYUKA nga byonna byekutte bwe buzimbe obw'enjawulo obw'obutonde bwonna era tebikiriza kutuukiriza kintu mu BBWE.

Mu NYAMA EY'OBWENKANYI mulimu ebintu ebikkirizibwa eby'amaanyi ebisobola okukulaakulanyizibwa oba okufiirwa, te kiteekwa kukulaakulanyizibwa. Enkola y'ENKYUKAKYUKA teyandisobodde kubikulakulanya.

Enkulakulana y'ebintu bino ebyekusifu ebikkirizibwa, esobola okubaawo mu mbeera entuufu era kino kyetaaga OKUKOLA EMPEERA ENNYO omuntu kinnoomu era n'obuyambi obutuufu okuva eri ABAKULEMBEZE abaamaze okukola omulimu ogwo mu biseera ebyayita.

Oyo ayagala okukulakulanya ebintu bye byonna ebikkirizibwa okufuuka omuntu, ateekwa okuyingira mu kkubo ly'ENKYUKAKYUKA Y'OMWOYO.

ENYAMA EY'OBWENKANYI lwe LUWERE, EMMEEZA; okuva ku mmeeza eyo omuti gw'obulamu gusobola okuzaalibwa, OMUNTU OWA SABBU, OMUNTU oyo DIOGENES gwe yali anoonyereza nga akutte ettala nga yakoleleza mu nguudo za ATENAS n'omu ttuntu era nga kigwa bubi nnyo nti teyasobodde kumuzuula.

TE MATEEKA nti oluwere luno, emmeeza eno ey'enjawulo ennyo esobola okukulaakulana, ekintu ekitegeerekeka, ekya bulijjo kwe kufiirwa.

OMUNTU OWA SABBU ali njawulo nnyo ku NYAMA EY'OBWENKANYI, ng'eggulu bwe liri ku kire.

Singa oluwere teruffa emmeeza tesobola kumeruka, kyetaagisa, kyanguwa okufa EGO, YO, MI MISMO, paca OMUNTU azaalibwe.

Abakulembeze n'abakulembeze b'Amasomero, Amakolegi n'Yunivasite, balina okuyigiriza abayizi baabwe EKUBBO LY'EMPISA EZ'ENKYUKAKYUKA, mu ngeri eyo yokka kyekisoboka okutuukiriza okufa kwa EGO.

Nga twongereza, tusobola okukakasa nti ENKYUKAKYUKA Y'OMWOYO tekyali kya bulijjo mu nsi eno, naye buli kiseera kifuuka kya kyama era kya kyama.

ENKYUKAKYUKA Y'OMWOYO erina ebintu bisatu eby'enjawulo: Okusooka, Okufa; Ekyokubiri, Okuzaalibwa; Ekyokusatu, Okwefiiriza abantu. Enkola y'ebintu tekyusa kitonde.
