OMUNTU EKIMENYI ye nsolo esinga obunkenke mu kiwonvu kino eky'amaziga, naye alina, EKYEYISA era n'OLUSOSOZE okwetikka EKITIISO kya KABAKA W'OBUTONDE.

"NOCE TE IPSUN" "OMUNTU YEGGERA". Eno ye NJIGIRIZA ya ZAABU eya dda eyawandiikibwa ku bisenge by'ekaali y'omu ssabo lya Delfos mu GEREKI EY'EDDA.

Omuntu, ensolo EMBULUMBA ey'OBWENGE eweebwa erinnya lya MUNTU mu ngeri etali ntuufu, yazimbulukusa ebyuma bingi ebizibu era ebikaluba era amanyi bulungi nti okusobola okukozesa EKIMENYI, emirundi mingi yetaaga emyaka mingi egy'okusoma n'okuyiga, naye bwe kituuka ku YE KENNYINI yeerabira ddala ekintu kino, wadde nga ye kennyini kimenyi ekizibu okusinga ebyo byonna bye yazimbulukusa.

Tewali muntu atajjuziddwa, ebirowoozo eby'obulimba obutaliimu ku ye kennyini, ekisinga obubi kwe kuba nti tayagala kutegeera nti ddala kimenyi.

Ekimenyi ky'omuntu tekirina ddembe lya kutambula, kikola bw'ekimala kukolebwaako ebintu bingi eby'enjawulo omunda n'ebyuma ebikubiddwa ebweru.

Ebintu byonna ebitaliimu, ebikolwa, ebigambo, ebirowoozo, enneewulira, obwagazi, okwegomba, eby'ekiminyi ky'omuntu bireetebwawo ebintu ebikikoleddwako ebweru n'ensonga ez'omunda ezitali za bulijjo era enzibu.

ENSOOLO EY'OBWENGE kinunusa mubi ekigambo kyaayo, erina obujjukirivu n'amaanyi, omuzimu omulamu, ogulina endowooza etali ntuufu, nti asobola OKUKOLA, ate nga mu butuufu talina ky'asobola OKUKOLA.

Lowooza okumala akabanga katono, omusomi omwagalwa, omuzimu ogw'ekiminyi oguddukanyizibwa ekikwaso ekizibu.

Lowooza nti omuzimu ogwo gulina obulamu, gwagala, gwogera, gutambula, gwegomba, gukola entalo, n'ebirala.

Lowooza nti omuzimu ogwo gusobola okukyusa bannanyini bwa buli kaseera. Oteekwa okulowooza nti buli nannyini muntu wa njawulo, alina bye yeekenneenya, engeri ye ey'okwesanyusaamu, okuwulira, okubeerawo, n'ebirala, n'ebirala, n'ebirala.

Nnanyini waamu yenna ng'ayagala okufuna ssente ajja kunyiga obutimbe obuwewo era awo omuzimu gwe gwenyigira mu by'obusuubuzi, nnanyini omulala, eddakiika 30 oluvannyuma oba essaawa eziwerako oluvannyuma, ajja kuba n'ekirowoozo ekitali kye kimu era ajja kuteeka omuzimu gwe okuzina n'okuseka, owekusatu ajja kumuteeka okulwana, owekuna ajja kumukola okwagala omukazi, owekutaano ajja kumukola okwagala omulala, omuwuka ajja kumukola okulwana ne muliraanwa we era amuteeke mu buzibu bwa poliisi, n'owe musanvu ajja kumukola okukyusa amaka ge.

Mu butuufu omuzimu gwaffe ogw'ekyokulabirako tegukoze kintu kyonna naye alowooza nti bw'akoze, alina endowooza nti AKOLA ate nga mu butuufu talina ky'asobola OKUKOLA kubanga talina BULIWO BWAKINNAKYE.

Nga tewali kubuusabuusa buli kimu kibaddewo nga bwetonnya, bwe bukubamu, bwotonda, naye omuzimu omunafu alowooza nti AKOLA; alina endowooza etali ntuufu nti buli kimu yakikoze ate nga mu butuufu talina ky'akoze, bannanyini be ab'enjawulo be beesanyusizza n'omuzimu omunafu ogw'ekiminyi.

Bw'atyo ensolo enafu ey'obwenge bw'eri, omusomi omwagalwa, omuzimu ogw'ekiminyi nga gwetusanze mu kyokulabirako kyaffe ekiggya, alowooza nti AKOLA ate nga mu butuufu talina KY'AKOLA ye ttitee ow'omubiri n'amagumba addukanyizibwa EKIBINA KY'EBINTU EBIRINA AMAANYI AMATONOTONO nga byonna bikola ekyo ekiyitibwa EGO, YE MBUULUMBA.

ENJIRI ENSIZI okuyita ebintu byonna ebyo EMYOYO emibi era erinnya lyabyo ery'amazima lye GIBA.

Bwe tugamba nti YE giba lya EMYOYO emibi eddukanya ekiminyi ky'omuntu, tetugonjola, bwe kityo bwe kiri.

OMUNTU-EKIMENYI talina BULIWO BWAKINNAKYE bwonna talina SER, SER OMUTUFU yekka y'alina AMAANYI G'OKUKOLA.

SER yekka y'asobola okutuwa OBULIWO BWAKINNAKYE OBUTUUFU, SER yekka y'atufuula ABANTU ABATUUFU.

Oyo ddala ayagala okulekeraawo okuba omuzimu ogw'ekiminyi obwangu, ateekwa okuggyawo buli kimu ku bintu ebyo nga byonna bikola YE. Buli kimu ku BINTU ebyo ebizannya n'ekiminyi ky'omuntu. Oyo ddala ayagala okulekeraawo okuba omuzimu ogw'ekiminyi obwangu, alina okutandika n'okukkiriza n'okutegeera engeri ye ey'ekiminyi.

Oyo atayagala kutegeera oba okukkiriza engeri ye ey'ekiminyi, oyo atayagala kutegeera bulungi ekintu kino, takyasobola kukyuka, munafu, mukyawe, singa asiba ejjinja ery'omugugu mu bulago bwe n'alisuula mu nnyanja.

ENSOOLO EY'OBWENGE kimenyiki, naye kimenyiki ekitali kya bulijjo, singa ekiminyiki kino kitegeera nti EKIMENYI, singa kikulizibwa bulungi era embeera bwe ziba zikkiriza, kisobola okulekeraawo okuba ekiminyiki n'afuuka OMUNTU.

Okusingira ddala byonna, kiyangu okutandika n'okutegeera mu bwangu era mu mitendera gyonna egy'obwongo, nti tetulina buliwo bwakinnakye obw'amazima, nti tetulina EKIFO EKIGUMIRE EKY'OBUTIITIIVU, nti mu kaseera akamu tuli muntu omu ate mu kalala, omulala; buli kimu kisinziira ku KINTU ekiddukanya embeera mu kaseera kyonna.

Ekyo ekireeta endowooza y'OBUMU n'OBUNTUUFU bw'ENSOOLO EY'OBWENGE kiva ku luuyi olumu ku kuwulira omubiri gwayo OMUBIRI OGW'OMUBIRI, ku luuyi olulala erinnya lyayo n'amannya ag'ekika era okukomekkereza obujjukirivu n'omuwendo ogumu ogw'emize gy'ekiminyi egiteereddwamu EBUTIITIIVU, oba egyafuniddwa mu kwemalira n'okukoppa obusirusiru.

ENSOOLO EY'OBWENGE enafu teerema kulekeraawo OKUBEERA EKIMENYI, teerema kukyuka, teerema kufuna SER INDIVIDUAL VERDADERO n'afuuka muntu alagira, nga bw'atannaba kuba na bugumu okuggyawo OKUYITA MU KUTEGEERA KW'OMUNDA era mu ntegeera ezigenderera, ku buli kimu ku bintu ebyo EBY'OMUKAATE ebyonna bikola ekyo kye bayita EGO, YE, YE KENNYINI.

Buli KIROWOOZO, buli KUYITA, buli kibi, buli KWAGALA, buli KYEYO, buli kwegomba, n'ebirala, n'ebirala, n'ebirala. buli kimu kirina ENTITY yaakyo ejjumbira era ekibiina ky'EBINTU ebyo byonna YE mbulumbalume ey'OBWENGE ENKYUKULE.

EBINTU ebyo byonna eby'omukaate, abantu abo bonna abaatibwa abantu ABABIRI abakola EGO, tebalina muggalo gwonna gwonna ogw'amazima wakati waabwe, tebalina ntegeera za ngeri yonna. Buli kimu ku BINTU ebyo kisinziira ku mbeera, enkyukakyuka z'ennono, ebibaddewo, n'ebirala.

EKITAMBI KY'OBWONGO kikyusa langi n'ebifo mu kaseera konna, buli kimu kisinziira ku ENTITY eddukanya obwongo mu kaseera kyonna.

Ku KITAMBI ky'obwongo zigenda ziyita mu kukuba ebifaananyi ebya buli kiseera EBY'ENTITIES ez'enjawulo nga byonna bikola EGO oba YO PSICOLÓGICO.

EBINTU eby'enjawulo ebikola YO PLURALIZADO byegatta, byeyawula, bikola ebibinja eby'enjawulo eby'enjawulo okusinziira ku nsonga zaabwe eziriranagana, bakaayana, bakubaganya ebirowoozo, tebamanyigana, n'ebirala, n'ebirala, n'ebirala.

Buli ENTITY eya LEGIÓN eyitibwa YO, buli YO mutono, alowooza okuba kyonna, EGO KYONNA, tasuubira bulabe nti ye kitundu butono.

ENTITY leero elayira okwagala okutaggwaawo omukazi, ejjumbira oluvannyuma ENTITY endala eterina ky'ekikwatagana n'okulayira okwo era awo oluyumba lw'amaka lugwa wansi era omukazi omunafu akaaba anenyeza.

ENTITY leero elayira obwesigwa ku nsonga, ejjumbira enkya ENTITY endala eterina ky'ekikwatagana n'ensonga eyo era awo omuntu yenna aggyawo.

ENTITY leero elayira obwesigwa ku GNOSIS, ejjumbira enkya ENTITY endala ekyawa GNOSIS.

Abasomesa n'Abasomesa ab'amasomero, amatendekero ga waggulu n'amayunivasite, balina okusoma ekitabo kino eky'EBUTIITIIVU ENSIZI era olw'obuntu balina okuba n'obugumu okukulembera abayizi abasajja n'abakazi ku kkubo eryewuunyisa ery'ENKYUKULE Y'OBUTIITIIVU.

Kyetaagisa abayizi okutegeera obwetaavu bw'okwemanyiira mu buli kifo ky'obwongo.

Kyetaaga okukulembera obwongo okwongera okukola, kyetaaga okutegeera bye tuli era kino kirina okutandikira ddala mu ntebe z'essomero.

Tetukkaanya nti ssente zeetaagisa okulya, okusasula obupangisa bw'ennyumba n'okwambala.

Tetukkaanya nti kyetaaga okweteekerateekera obwongo, omulimu, obukugu okufuna ssente, naye ekyo si kyonna, ekyo kyakubiri.

Ekisooka, ekikulu kwe kumanya be tuli, kiki ffe, gye tuva, gye tugenda, kiki ekigendererwa ky'obulamu bwaffe.

Kya nnaku okweyongera okubeera ng'emizimu gy'ekiminyi egyesiga, emitume emibi, abantu-ebyuma.

Kyangu okulekeraawo okubeera ebyuma obwangu, kyangu okufuuka ABANTU ABATUUFU.

Kyetaaga enkyukakyuka ey'omuggundu era eno erina okutandika ddala n'okuggyawo buli kimu ku BINTU ebyo nga byonna bikola YE OMBUULUMBA.

ENSOOLO EY'OBWENGE enafu si MUNTU naye ng'alina munda mu embeera embi ey'obukugu bwonna okufuuka OMUNTU.

Si tteeka nti obukugu obwo bugenda kukula, ekisinga obulamu bwe kuliba nti bugenda kubula.

Kukozesa MAANYI AMAWEREWE gokka obukugu bw'omuntu obwo bwe busobola okukula.

Tulina bingi ebyokuggyawo era bingi bye tulina okufuna. Kifuuka kyetaagisa okukola olukalala okumanya bye tumaze okufuna ne bye tubeera tubeetaaga.

Kimanyiddwa nti YO PLURALIZADO afuluma okukomekkereza, kintu kitaliimu mugaso era kikosa.

KIKAKASIBWA okugamba nti tulina okukula amaanyi agamu, engeri ezimu, obukugu obumu OMUNTU-EKIMENYI gwe yeewa era n'alowooza nti alina naye mu butuufu TALINA.

OMUNTU-EKIMENYI alowooza nti alina INDIVIDUALIDAD ey'amazima, BUTIITIIVU OBUNAKU, OBWAGAZI OBUTEGEERE, AMAANYI G'OKUKOLA, n'ebirala era talina kyonna ku ekyo.

Bwe tuba twagala okulekeraawo okubeera ebyuma, bwe tuba twagala okuzuukusa OBUTIITIIVU, okubeera n'OBWAGAZI OBUTEGEERE obw'amazima, INDIVIDUALIDAD, obukugu bw'OKUKOLA, kyangu okutandika ng'abantu bennyini era oluvannyuma okusaanyawo YO PSICOLÓGICO.

Bwe YO PLURALIZADO esaanyizibwawo ekisigala munda yaffe kyokka ye SER VERDADERO.
