OMUNTU EKIMUBA KA MAGGINA ye nsolo esinga obusaasizi esangibwa mu kiwonvu kino eky'amaziga, naye ye alina, EKYEBYOMBO ne bwe kiba EKIBEEYOOKO okwetikka ETITIBA ey'OKUBA OMUKULU W'OBUTONZI.

"NOCE TE IPSUN" "MUNTU WEEMANYEKO". Eno ye NGERO ENTUUFU EYOBUZIBAAWO Eyawandiikibwa ku bisenge by'Etemplo ya Delfos eteetebwa mu BUGEREKI OBW'EDDA.

Omuntu, ensolo yooyo empavu EYOBUWAKATI ebibi okweyita OMUNTU, yazimbye bbiiriya nnyingi eza maggina agaazibu era agazibu era amanyi bulungi nti okusobola okukozesa MAGGINA, oluusi yeetaaga emyaka emingi okusoma n'okuyiga, naye bwe kituuka ku YE KENNYINI yeerabira ddala ekintu kino, newaakubadde ye kennyini maggina azinga okusinga zonna ze yazimbye.

Tewali muntu yenna atajjudde, ebirowoozo byonna eby'obulimba ku ye kennyini, ekisinga obubi kwe kuba nti tayagala kukitegeera nti ddala ye maggina.

Maggia omuntu talina ddembe ly'okutambula, akola yekka olw'obuvubi obungi obw'enjawulo obw'omunda n'obutendera obw'ebweru.

Ebintu byonna, ebikolwa, ebigambo, ebirowoozo, enneewulira, ensonyi, okwagala, bya maggina omuntu biva ku buvubi obw'ebweru era n'ensonga ez'omunda ez'enjawulo ezitali za mugaso era enziro.

ENSONI EYOBUWAKATI luyina olubi olw'olugambo n'okujjukira n'amaanyi, omunaku omulamu, alina ekirowoozo ekisirusiru, nti asobola OKUKOLA, ng'ate mu butuufu talina ky'asobola KUKOLA.

Lowooza akaseera akamu, omusomi omwagalwa, omunaku ogw'okuzimbwa ogukozesebwa n'obukodyo obuzinga.

Lowooza nti omunaku oyo alina obulamu, ayagala, ayogera, atambula, ayagala, akola entalo, n'ebirala.

Lowooza nti omunaku oyo asobola okukyusa bannanyini buly'omu. Muteekwa okulowooza nti buli nannyini muntu wa njawulo, alina emitendera gye yennyini, obukodyo bwe obw'okusanyuka, okuwulira, okubeerawo, n'ebirala, n'ebirala, n'ebirala.

Nannyini yenna ayagala okufuna ssente ajja kunyiga obukokoolo obumu era olwo omunaku ajja kwewaayo ku by'obusuubuzi, nannyini omulala, eddakiika nga wayiseewo emu ku ttanooba oba eddakiika eziwerako nga ziyiseewo, ajja kuba n'ekirowoozo ekitali kimu era ajja kussa omunaku we okuzina n'okuseka, omusatu ajja kumussa okukwana, omunaana ajja kumukolerera okwagala omukazi, omutaano ajja kumukolerera okwagala omukazi omulala, omukaaga ajja kumukolerera okukwana omuliraanwa n'okwezimbira ekizibu ky'abaserikale, n'omusanvu ajja kumukolerera okukyusa awabeera.

Mu butuufu omunaku ogw'ekyokulabirako kyaffe takoleddeyo kintu kyonna naye alowooza nti yamaze okukola, alina ekirowoozo nti AKOLA ng'ate mu butuufu talina ky'asobola KUKOLA kubanga talina SERA EYE KITAWO.

Nga tewali kubuusabuusa kyonna kyibaddewo nga bwetonnya, bwe lubinda, bwokya omusana, naye omunaku alowooza nti AKOLA; alina ekirowoozo ekisirusiru nti byonna yabikoze ng'ate talina ky'akoze, bannanyini be be beewaddeyo n'omunaku ogw'okuzimbwa.

Bwetyo ensolo empavu bwetyo, omusomi omwagalwa omunaku ogw'okuzimbwa nga ekyokulabirako kyaffe ekiraga, alowooza nti AKOLA ng'ate talina ky'AKOLA luyina lw'omubiri n'omusaayi olukozesebwa EKIBINJA KY'ABA NTU EYAMAANYI EZOBUZIIZI ezigatta wamu ekyo kye kiyitibwa EGO, YE PLURALIZADO.

ENJIRI ENTUFU EYA KRISTAAYO eteeka abatufu bonna EMYOYO EMIBI era erinnya lyabwe elituufu ye LEGIÓN.

Bwe tugamba nti YE kibinja kya EMYOYO EMIBI ekola maggina omuntu, tetubeera tukaba, bwe kityo bwe kiri.

OMUNTU-MAGGINA talina INDIVIDUALIDAD yenna talina SERA, SERA OMUTUUFU yekka y'alina AMAANYI OKUKOLA.

SERA YEKKA y'etusobola okuwa INDIVIDUALIDAD ENTUFU, SERA yekka etufuula ABANTU ABATUUFU.

Oyo ayagala okuleka okubeera omunaku ogw'okuzimbwa, alina okuggwaawo buli omu ku batufu abo abagatta wamu YE. Buli omu ku BATUFU abo abazannya ne maggina omuntu. Oyo ayagala okuleka okubeera omunaku ogw'okuzimbwa, alina okutandika okukkiriza n'okutegeera obumagginisity bwe.

Oyo atayagala kutegeera newaakubadde okukkiriza obumagginisity bwe, oyo atayagala kutegeera bulungi ekintu kino, takyayinza kukyuka, munaku, mubi, singa abadde alina omwala okusiba mu bulago n'okugusuula mu nnyanja.

ENSONI EYOBUWAKATI maggina, naye maggina ey'enjawulo, bwe maggina eno etuuka okutegeera nti ye MAGGINA, bw'ekwatibwa obulungi era embeera bwe zigisobozesa, esobola okuleka okubeera maggina n'efuuka OMUNTU.

Okusinga byonna, kibeere ekiragala okutandika okutegeera munda era ku mitendera gyonna egy'obwongo, nti tetulina individualidad entuufu, nti tetulina CENTRO OMUGUMIZI OW'AMAANYI, nti mu kaseera akamu tuli muntu omu ate mu kalala, mulala; byonna biva ku NTU ekozesa embeera mu kaseera kyonna.

Buli NTU ya LEGIÓN eyitibwa YE, buli YE mutono, alowooza nti ye byonna, EGO EYOKUGGWAAWO, tateebereza nti ye kitundu kitono.

ENTU egamba obwesigwa eby'obwesigwa eri omukazi, egiyimbye oluvannyuma ENTU endala eteriiko kyonna okukola n'obwesigwa obwo era olwo ennyumba eyazimbiddwa ku nsiigwa enzirugavu negwa n'omukazi omunaku n'akaaba.

ENTU egamba obwesigwa eri omulamwa, egiyimbye enkya ENTU endala eteriiko kyonna okukola n'omulamwa ogwo era olwo omuntu n'avaayo.

ENTU egamba obwesigwa eri GNOSIS, egiyimbye enkya ENTU endala ekyaawa GNOSIS.
