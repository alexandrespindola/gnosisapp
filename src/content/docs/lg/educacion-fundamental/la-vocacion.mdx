Okuggyako abantu abalema ddala, buli muntu alina okubeera n'ekigendererwa mu bulamu, ekizibu kwe kumanya buli muntu agasa mu ki.

Bwe waliwo ekintu ekikulu mu nsi eno, kwe kwemanya ggwe kennyini, abataba balungi beebo abemanyi era newankubadde kiyinza okulabika ng'ekitakoleka, kizibu okusanga mu bulamu omuntu yenna alina endowooza y'omulimu gwe ayagala.

Omuntu bw'akakasa ddala omulimu gw'alina okukola mu bulamu, awo olwo omulimu gwe ayagala agufuula butume, eddiini, era afuuka omutume eri abantu.

Omuntu amanyi omulimu gwe ayagala oba ayiga okuguzuula yekka, ayitamu enkyukakyuka enkulu, takyanoonya kuwera, tamutawaanya nnyo sente, ttutumu, kusiima, essanyu lye liri mu ssanyu ly'afuna olw'okuba atenderezza okuyitibwa okw'omunda, okw'amaanyi, obutamanya bw'engeri ye ey'omunda.

Eky'omugaso okusinga byonna kwe kuba nti endowooza y'omulimu gwe ayagala terina ky'ekwatagana n'obuntu bwaffe, kubanga newankubadde kirabika ng'ekyewuunyo obuntu bwaffe bukyawa omulimu gwaffe gwe twagala kubanga obuntu bwaffe buteebereza buyambi bwa sente obungi, ekifo, ettutumu, n'ebirala.

Endowooza y'omulimu gwe twagala, kintu kya ngeri yaffe ey'omunda; kintu kya munda nnyo, kya maanyi nnyo, kya lukunka nnyo.

Endowooza y'omulimu gwe twagala etwala omuntu okukola n'obuvumu obwa ddala n'obuteefaako emirimu egisinga obukambwe ku ssa ly'okubonaabona n'okutya kungi. N'olwekyo kya bulijjo obuntu bwaffe okukyawa omulimu gwaffe ogwa ddala.

Endowooza y'omulimu gwe twagala etukulembera okuyita mu kkubo ly'obuzira obutongole, newankubadde tulina okugumiikiriza n'obumalirivu ebigambo ebibi ebya buli ngeri, obulimbalimba n'obulimba.

Olunaku omusajja lw'ayinza okwogera amazima "MMANYI NDI ANI ERA OMULIMU GWANGE OGWA DDALA GWE GULI GUWA" okuva mu kiseera ekyo ajja kutandika okutambulira mu bugolokofu n'okwagala. Omuntu bw'atyo abeera mu mulimu gwe n'omulimu gwe muli ye.

Mu butuufu abantu batono ddala abayinza okwogera bwe batyo, n'obwesigwa bwonna mu mutima. Abo abaogera bwe batyo be balondemu abo abalina ku mutindo ogwa waggulu endowooza y'omulimu gwe baagala.

Okuzuula omulimu gwaffe ogwa ddala gwe twagala TEKITEEKEBWA MU BUBBUUZA, ekizibu ky'embeera z'abantu ekisinga obubi, ekizibu ekisangibwa mu ntandikwa y'ebizibu byonna eby'abantu.

Okuzuula oba okuyiga omulimu gwaffe ogwa ddala gwaffe kinnoomu, kyenkanankana okuzuula ekyobugagga eky'omuwendo omungi.

Omutuuze bw'azuula n'obukakafu bwonna era nga tateebereza mulimu gwe ogwa ddala era ogwetongole, afuuka olw'ekikolwa kino yekka ATASOBOLA KUZIIKIZIBWA.

Omulimu gwaffe gwe twagala bwe gukwatagana ddala era mu ngeri etaliimu bubuusa na kifo kye tulina mu bulamu, awo olwo tukola omulimu gwaffe ng'obutume obwa ddala, awatali mululu gwonna n'okwegomba kwonna okw'amaanyi.

Awo olwo omulimu mu kifo ky'okutuleetera omululu, obukoowu oba okwegomba okukyusa omulimu, guleetera essanyu erya ddala, ery'amaanyi, ery'omunda newankubadde tulina okugumiikiriza n'obumalirivu amakubo agaluma.

Mu kukola twasobola okukakasa nti ekifo bwe kiba tekikwatagana n'omulimu gw'omuntu gwe yagala, awo olwo alowooza ku EBY'OKUGATTIRAWO.

Enkola y'obuntu bwaffe ye EBY'OKUGATTIRAWO. Sente ezisingawo, ettutumu erisingawo, pulojekiti ezisingawo, n'ebirala. era nga bwe kiri ekya bulijjo omuntu aterera okufuuka munafiki, omunyigiriza, omukambwe, atasaasira, atakkiriziganya, n'ebirala.

Bwe tukkaanya okusoma obuwandiike bw'abakozi ba gavumenti tusobola okukakasa nti emirundi mingi mu bulamu ekifo tekikwatagana n'omulimu omuntu gwe yagala.

Bwe tukkaanya okusoma mu ngeri entuufu ebikonge by'abakozi, tusobola okukakasa nti mu mbeera ntono ddala omulimu gukwatagana n'omulimu omuntu gwe yagala.

Bwe tutunuulira amakulu amakungugege, newankubadde gabeera okuva ebuvanjuba oba ebugwanjuba bw'ensi, tusobola okulaga obutabaawo bw'endowooza yonna ey'omulimu gwe twagala. Abo be bayitibwa "ABAANA ABALUNGI" kati balumba n'emmundu, babba abakyala abatalina musaayi, n'ebirala okutta obukoowu. Nga tebazuulanga kifo kyabwe mu bulamu, batambula nga babuze era bafuuka ABJEEMEREZI ABATALINA KIGANDEERWA okusinziira ku "kukyusaamu katono".

Kyamusango embeera y'abantu ey'akavuyo mu biseera bino eby'akabenje k'ensi yonna.

Tewali muntu yenna amatimba n'omulimu gwe kubanga ekifo tekikwatagana n'omulimu gwe yagala, enkuba ey'okunoonya emirimu egwa kubanga tewali muntu yenna ayagala okufa enjala, naye okusaba tekwatagana n'omulimu abo abasaba gwe baagala.

Bavuzi bangi balina okubeera abasawo oba bayinginiya. Bannyamateeka bangi balina okubeera baminisita era baminisita bangi balina okubeera abaluki b'engoye. Abanaazi b'engatto bangi balina okubeera baminisita era baminisita bangi balina okubeera abanaazi b'engatto, n'ebirala.

Abantu bali mu bifo bye batalina kubaamu, ebyo ebitaliimu kyonna kye bikwatagana n'omulimu gwabwe ogwa ddala kinnoomu gwe baagala, olw'ensonga eno ekyuma ky'abantu kikola bubi nnyo. Kino kifaanana ne mota eyazimbiddwa n'ebitundu bye tekirina kubaamu era ekyavaamu kirina okuba butalina busonga, kufiirwa, busirusiru.

Mu kukola twasobola okukakasa okutuuka ku ntikko nti omuntu bw'ataba na bweteefuteefu bw'omulimu gwe ayagala okubeera omukulembeze, omutendesi w'eddiini, omukulembeze ow'ebyobufuzi oba dayirekita w'ekibiina kyonna eky'ebyomwoyo, eky'ekimera, eky'ebisomesebwa, eky'obwannakyewa, n'ebirala. awo olwo alowooza ku EBY'OKUGATTIRAWO era ayeeyama okukola pulojekiti n'okuzaayo pulojekiti n'ebigendererwa eby'ekyama ebitaliiko musango.

Kitegeerekeka nti ekifo bwe kitabaawo ng'omuntu mulimu gwe ayagala ekyavaamu kwe kunyigiriza.

Mu biseera bino eby'obukakanyavu obungi bye tubeeramu, ekifo ky'omusomesa kitwalibwa mu ngeri y'obusungu abatunzi bangi abataba na mulimu gwonna gwe baagala ogw'obusomesa. Ekyavaamu ky'obulimba obw'ekika ekyo kwe kunyigiriza, obukambwe n'obutabaawo bw'okwagala okwa ddala.

Abantu bangi bakola omulimu gw'obusomesa olw'ekigendererwa eky'okufuna sente okusasula emisomo gyabwe mu ttendekero ly'obusawo, amateeka oba obuyinginiya oba olw'okuba tebalina kirala kye bakola. Abagwamu omuzannyo gw'obulimba obw'omutwe bano be bayizi.

Omusomesa ow'omulimu gwe ayagala ow'ennaku zino kizibu okumuzuula era ssanyu erisinga ly'abayinza okufuna abayizi okuva mu masomero, kolleji ne yunivasite.

Omulimu ogw'omusomesa gwe yagala gunnyonnyolwa mu magezi ekitundu ekyo eky'olulimi ekikwata ku mutima okuva ewa GABRIELA MÍSTRAL ekiyitibwa OKUSABA KW'OMUSOMESA. Musomesa okuva mu bitundu agamba eri OBULUNGI okubeera OMUSOMESA OW'EKISIISA:

"Mpa okwagala okumu, okw'essomero lyange: omuliro gw'obulungi guleme kusobola okumunyagako okusaasira kwange okw'ebiseera byonna. Musomesa, ndeetera okubeera ow'okwagala okw'amaanyi okw'olubeerera n'okufuuka ow'obutaggwaamu maanyi. Gondola okuva mu nze okwegomba okuyonjo okw'obwenkanya obutegeerekeka obukyankalanyizza, ekirowoozo ekisongovu eky'okwekubagiza ekiva mu nze bwe ndumwa, buleme kunduma okubulwa okutegeerwa wadde okunnakuwaza okwerabirwa kw'abo be nasomesa".

"Mpa okubeera maama okusinga ba maama, nsobole okwagala n'okulwanirira nga bwe bakola ekyo EKITALI nyama ya nyama yange. Mpa okutuuka okukola omu ku bawala bange oluyimba lwange olutaliiko kyakunenyezebwa era mbalekere omwo oluyimba lwange olunyoomezza ennyo, bwe mimwa gyange ginaaba tegyakyayimba".

"Ndaga Enjiri yo ng'esoboka mu kiseera kyange, neme okulekulira olutalo olw'olunaku buli lunaku n'ekiseera buli kiseera ku lwe".

Ani ayinza okugera ekifuula obwongo ekyaffe ekikola obulungi ng'omusomesa ng'oyo nga yaweebwa obusaasizi bungi, okuyitira mu ndowooza y'omulimu gwe ayagala?

Omuntu atuuka ku mulimu gwe ayagala okuyitira mu makubo gano asatu: okusooka: OKWEZUULA obusobozi obw'enjawulo. Ekyokubiri: okulaba obwetaavu obwangu. Ekyokusatu: obukulembeze obutera okubaawo obw'abazadde n'abasomesa abaazuula omulimu omuyizi gwe yagala okuyitira mu kutunuulira obusobozi bwe.

Abantu bangi bayize omulimu gwabwe gwe baagala mu kiseera ekizibu ekimu mu bulamu bwabwe, mu maaso g'embeera enzibu eyali yeetaaga okukolebwako amangu.

GANDHI yali munnamateeka yenna, olw'obulumbaganyi obwakolebwa ku ddembe ly'Abahindu mu AFRIKA EY'OMUMASO n'asazaamu tiketi ye ey'okudda mu Buyindi era n'asigala okulwanirira ensonga z'abantu b'eggwanga lye. Obwetaavu obw'ekiseera bwe bwamutwala eri omulimu gw'obulamu bwe bwonna gwe yali ayagala.

Abagabi abalungi ab'abantu, bazudde omulimu gwabwe gwe baagala mu maaso g'akabenje k'embeera, akaali keetaaga okukolebwako amangu. Tujjukire OLIVERIO CROMWELL, kitaawe w'eddembe ly'Abangereza; Benito Juárez, omuweesi w'e Mexico empya; José de San Martín ne Simón Bolívar, bazadde b'obwetwaze bw'Amerika y'Obukiikaddyo, n'ebirala.

YESU, KRISTO, BUDHA, MAHOMMED, HERMES, ZOROASTRO, CONFUCIO, FUHI, n'ebirala. baali basajja abaali mu kiseera ekimu mu byafaayo abamanyi okutegeera omulimu gwabwe ogwa ddala gwe baagala era ne bawulira nga bayitibwa eddoboozi ery'omunda eriva mu MUKULU.

OBUSOMESA OBW'OMUTINDO busaaliddwa okuzuula okuyitira mu nkola ez'enjawulo, obusobozi obwekise obw'abayizi. Enkola ze nkola ez'ebisomesebwa ezaakolebwa okumala akaseera zikozesebwa mu biseera bino okuzuula omulimu abayizi gwe baagala, nga tebuteekebwa mu bubuusa bukambwe, busirusiru era tebasaasira.

Ebibuuzo by'omulimu omuntu gwe yagala byakolebwa abatunzi abalya omukono ogwa ddyo mu busungu nga batwala ekifo ky'abasomesa.

Mu nsi ezimu nga tebannayingira matendekero n'amalimiro, abayizi bateekebwa ku bukambwe bw'obwongo obusinga obubi. Bakuzibwa ebibuuzo ku by'embalirira, obutuuze, obulamu, n'ebirala.

Ekikambwe okusinga mu nkola zino ze zegeezeso ezimanyiddwa ez'obwongo, omutindo Y.Q, ezikwatagana ennyo n'obwangu bw'obwongo.

Okusinziira ku ngeri y'okuddamu, nga bwe ziyitibwa, omuyizi olwo asibwa mu basajja basatu. Okusooka: Ababalirira emibalanguzi. Ekyokubiri: Ensawo y'obulamu. Ekyokusatu: Ensawo y'abantu.

Okuva mu babalirira emibalanguzi kuvaamu abayinginiya. Abazimbi, Ababala emmunyeenye, Abavuzi b'ennyonyi, n'ebirala.

Okuva mu Nsawo y'obulamu kuvaamu abakola eddagala, abaakiwandiika, abasawo, abasawo, n'ebirala.

Okuva mu Nsawo y'abantu kuvaamu bannyamateeka, abawandiisi, abakugu mu byenfuna n'ebiwandiiko, abadukanya amakampuni, n'ebirala.

Enkola y'okusoma mu buli nsi njawulo era kitegeerekeka nti si mu nsi zonna mwe muli ensawo ssatu ez'enjawulo. Mu nsi nnyingi waliwo ensawo emu yokka era nga bwemala omuyizi ayita ku Yunivasite.

Mu mawanga agamu tebakebera busobozi bw'omuyizi bwa mulimu gw'ayagala era ayiingira mu kitongole n'okwegomba okufuna omulimu okwebeesawo, newankubadde kino tekikwatagana n'endowooza ye gy'azaalibwa nayo, n'endowooza y'omulimu gwe ayagala.

Waliwo ensi omubakebera obusobozi bw'abayizi obw'omulimu gwe baagala era waliwo amawanga omuteebakebera. Busirusiru obutamanya okuluŋŋamya abayizi ku mulimu gwe baagala, obutakebera busobozi bwabwe n'endowooza ze baazaalibwa nazo. Basirusiru bye bibuuzo by'omulimu omuntu gwe yagala n'ebigambo byonna ebyo, EGEEZESO Z'OBWONGO, omutindo Y.Q., n'ebirala.

Enkola ezo ez'okukebera omulimu omuntu gwe yagala tezisaana kubanga obwongo bulina ebiseera byabwo eby'akabenje era bwe kukebera kukolebwa mu kiseera ekyo, ekyavaamu kufiirwa n'okubulwa obwangu bw'omuyizi.

Abasomesa basobola okukakasa nti obwongo bw'abayizi bulina, okufaanana n'ennyanja, okukula n'okukendeera, obungi bwabwe n'obutono. Waliwo Enkola y'obulamu mu bususu bw'abasajja n'abakazi. Era waliwo Enkola y'obulamu eri obwongo.

Mu biseera ebimu obususu bw'abasajja busangibwa mu OBUNGI era obw'abakazi mu OBUTONO oba obukyusiddwa. Obwongo era bulina OBUNGI bwabwo n'OBUTONO.

Omuntu yenna ayagala okumanya essomo ly'obulamu tusaba abeere ng'asoma omulimu ogumanyiddwa oguyitibwa ENKOLA Y'OBULAMU oguwandiikiddwa omusomesa ow'amagezi GNÓSTICO ROSA-CRUZ, Dokita Amoldo Krumm Heller, omusawo omukulu mu magye ga Mexico era omusomesa w'obusawo mu ttendekero ly'e Berlin.

Ffe tukakasa n'amaanyi nti akabenje k'omuntu oba embeera ey'obunafu mu bwongo mu maaso g'embeera enzibu ey'okukebera kiyinza okutwala omuyizi okufiirwa mu kiseera eky'okukebera okwetegekera omulimu.

Ffe tukakasa nti obunafu bwonna obw'ekitongole ky'entambula obuleetebwa oboolyawo omuzannyo, okutambula okungi, oba omulimu omukalu ogw'omubiri, n'ebirala. kiyinza okuleetera AKABENJE K'OBWONGO newankubadde obwongo buli mu OBUNGI era ne butwala omuyizi okufiirwa mu kiseera eky'okukebera okwetegekera omulimu.

Ffe tukakasa nti akabenje kyonna akakwatagana n'ekitongole eky'obutonde, oboolyawo mu kugattika n'omukwano gw'omukwano, oba ekitongole eky'enneewulira, n'ebirala. kiyinza okutwala omuyizi okufiirwa mu kiseera eky'okukebera okwetegekera omulimu.

Ffe tukakasa nti akabenje k'omukwano kw'abantu konna, okufa kw'omukwano ogulemeddwa, obunafu bw'omukwano, okukozesa obubi omukwano, n'ebirala. kiyinza okukozesa amaanyi gaabyo agoonya ku bwongo ne kibutwala okufiirwa mu kiseera eky'okukebera okwetegekera omulimu.

Obusomesa obw'omusingi busomesa nti ensigo z'omulimu omuntu gwe yagala ziterekebwa, si mu kitongole kyokka eky'amagezi wabula era ne mu buli kimu ku bitongole ebirala bina ebya Psiko-fiziolojiya y'ekyuma ekikola.

Kyangu okussaayo omwoyo eri ebitongole ebitaano eby'obwongo eziyitibwa Amagezi, Enneewulira, Entambula, Obutonde n'Omukwano. Busirusiru okulowooza nti amagezi kye kitongole kyokka eky'Obumanyi. Bwe kukebera okukolebwa ku kitongole ky'amagezi kyokka n'ekigendererwa eky'okuzuula engeri z'omuntu ez'omulimu gwe yagala, okugattako okukola obutali bwenkanya obunene obuvanga mu butuufu bw'omuntu n'abantu, omuntu agwa mu nsobi kubanga ensigo z'omulimu ogwo si mu kitongole ky'amagezi mwokka mwe zigenderera wabula era ne mu kimu kyonna ku bitongole ebirala bina ebya Psiko-Psikolojiya y'omuntu.

Ekkubo lye tulaba lyokka eririwo okuzuula omulimu ogwa ddala abayizi gwe baagala kwe KWAGALA OKWA DDALA.

Abazadde b'enju n'abasomesa bwe beegatta mu ntegeeragana okunoonyereza mu maka ne mu ssomero, okutunuulira mu bujjuvu ebikolwa byonna eby'abayizi, bayinza okuyiga endowooza ze baazaalibwa nazo ez'omuyizi yenna.

Ekyo kye kkubo lye tulaba lyokka eriyinza okukkiriza abazadde b'enju n'abasomesa okuzuula endowooza abayizi gwe baagala.

Kino kyetaaga OKWAGALA okwa ddala okuva eri abazadde n'abasomesa era kyangu bwe watabaawo kwagala kwa ddala okuva eri abazadde n'aba maama b'enju n'abasomesa abatuufu ab'omulimu gwe baagala abasobola okwewaayo mu butuufu olw'abayigirizwa baabwe, olwo olwo omulimu ng'ogwo gufuuka ogutayinza kukolebwa.

Gavumenti bwe zaagala mu butuufu okutaasa abantu, zeetaaga okugoba abatunzi okuva mu ssabo n'enkundi y'obwagazi.

Emyaka egya tekinologiya emitongole girina okutandika okusasaanya mu bifo byonna enjigiriza y'OBUSOMESA OBW'OMUSINGI.

Abayizi balina okulwanirira eddembe lyabwe n'obuvumu era basalire gavumenti abasomesa abatuufu ab'omulimu gwe baagala. Ku lw'omukisa waliwo ekyokulwanyisa eky'amaanyi eky'okwekalakaasa era abayizi balina ekyokulwanyisa ekyo.

Mu nsi ezimu wamaze okubaawo mu masomero, mu kolleji ne mu yunivasite, abasomesa abaluŋŋamya abamu abatatwala mu butuufu mulimu gwe baagala, ekifo kye batwala tekikwatagana n'endowooza ze baazaalibwa nazo. Abasomesa bano tebasobola kuluŋŋamya balala kubanga wadde bo bennyini tebasobola okweruŋŋamya.

Kyetaagisa mu bwangu abasomesa abatuufu ab'omulimu gwe baagala abasobola okuluŋŋamya abayizi mu magezi.

Kyetaaga okumanya nti olw'obungi bw'obuntu bwaffe, omuntu akola mu bwangu emigugu egy'enjawulo mu kizannyisanyo ky'obulamu. Abalenzi n'abawala balina omugugu ogw'essomero, omulala ogw'ekkubo n'omulala ogw'enju.

Omuntu bw'ayagala okuyiga omulimu omulenzi oba omuwala gwe baagala alina okubatunuulira mu ssomero, mu nju n'okusingira ddala mu kkubo.

Omulimu guno ogw'okutunuulira gusobola okukolebwa abazadde n'abasomesa abatuufu mu mukwano ogw'amaanyi.

Mu busomesa obukadde waliwo era enkola y'okutunuulira ebirango okuggya endowooza z'omulimu. Omuyizi eyayawulwa mu butuuze n'ebirango ebisinze waggulu olwo alondebwa ng'omunnamateeka ayinza okubaawo era oyo eyayawulwa mu bulamu akuliddewo ng'omusawo ayinza okubaawo, era oyo ali mu mbalirira, ng'ayinginiya ayinza okubaawo, n'ebirala.

Enkola eno etaliimu nsonga okuggya mu bantu OMULIMU omuntu gwe yagala mungi nnyo kubanga obwongo bulina obugimu bwabwo n'obunafu si mu ngeri ya byonna eriwo gyokka wabula era ne mu mbeera enjawulo enjigirize.

Abawandiisi bangi abaali abayizi ababi mu ssomero ab'ebigambo baguyidde mu bulamu ng'abasomesa abatuufu ab'olulimi. Abayinginiya abamanyiddwa bangi bulijjo baali balina ebirango ebibi ennyo mu ssomero mu mbalirira era abasawo bangi baagaanyiddwa mu ssomero mu bulamu ne mu ssomo ly'ebitonde.

Kinnaku nti abazadde bangi mu kifo ky'okusoma endowooza z'abaana baabwe balaba mu bo okwongera kw'ÉGO waabwe gwe baagala, YO psikolojiya, NZE KENNYINI.

Abazadde abannyamateeka bangi baagala abaana baabwe okwongera mu woofiisi y'amateeka era bannannyini bizinensi bangi baagala abaana baabwe okwongera okuddukanya ebyenfuna byabwe eby'omululu awatali kubafaako na katono endowooza yaabwe ey'omulimu gwe baagala.

Oyo ayagala bulijjo okulinnya, okwerula ku ntikko y'ekisaala, okwesittaza era obwagazi bwe bwe bufiirwa olwo baagala okutuukiriza okuyitira mu baana baabwe kye batali basobola kutuukiriza ku lwabwe bennyini. Abazadde ab'obwagazi bano bateeka abalenzi n'abawala baabwe mu mirimo n'ebifo ebitagalagana na kyonna n'endowooza yaabwe ey'omulimu gwe baagala.
