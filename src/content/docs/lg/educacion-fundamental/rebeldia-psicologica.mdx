Ekintu kino ekitaliimu bubuze, amazima gano ag'entiisa ageeza omuntu yenna omuyivu, kisobola okukakasibwa mu ngeri ey'enjawulo singa omutambuzi akyalira amasomero, amakolegi ne Yunivasite.

Newankubadde kirabika ng'ekisekererwa, kituufu nti amasomero g'ebyemikono, Yunivasite, n'ebirala bifuuse amakolero ag'ebyobufuzi ag'okukola ebintu bingi mu bwangu.

Mu biro bino eby'okukola ebintu bingi mu bwangu, ekigendererwa kyokka mu bulamu kwe kufuna obukuumi mu by'enfuna. Abantu batya buli kimu era banoonya obukuumi.

Okulowooza okw'obwannakyewa mu biro bino eby'okukola ebintu bingi mu bwangu, kukifuula okubeera okutayinzika kubanga ekika kya Ssomero ekya Mulembe kitongolebwa ku bukulu obwangu.

"Omuyaga Omupya" aganyulwa nnyo mu mbeera eno ey'obunafu mu by'obufuzi. Singa omuntu ayagala okuba owa njawulo, owa njawulo ku balala, buli muntu amusaliriza, buli muntu amuvumirira, akolebwa mu bwangu, takkirizibwa kukola, n'ebirala.

Okwegomba okufuna ssente okusobola okubeerawo n'okusanyuka, okwanguwa okutuuka ku buwanguzi mu bulamu, okunoonya obukuumi, eby'enfuna, okwegomba okugula ebintu bingi okusobola okweraga eri abalala, n'ebirala, bimukakatako okulowooza okutukuvu, okw'obutonde n'okw'obwannakyewa.

Kikakasiddwa ddala nti okutya kuziba omutwe era kukalaaza omutima.

Abantu, batya buli kimu, batidde okuva mu mateeka amakadde agassiddwaawo, okuba ab'enjawulo ku bantu abalala, okulowooza mu ngeri ey'obujeemu, okumenyaamenya enzikiriza zonna ez'ekibiina ky'abantu ekyagambalala, n'ebirala.

Ku lw'omukisa, mu nsi mulimu abatono abeesimbu era abalumirwa, abaagala okwekenneenya mu bujjuvu ebizibu byonna eby'omutwe, naye mu bantu abasinga obungi tetulina n'omwoyo gw'obutakkiriziganya n'obujeemu.

Waliwo ebika by'OBUJEEMU bibiri ebyamaze okuteekebwa mu biti. Ekisooka: Obujeemu bw'Omutwe obw'amaanyi. Ekyokubiri: Obujeemu bw'Omutwe obw'omunda obw'AMAGEZI.
