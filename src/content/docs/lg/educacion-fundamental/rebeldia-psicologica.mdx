Abo beemalirizza okutambula mu nsi yonna nga balina ekigendererwa eky'okunoonyereza ku mawanga gonna ag'abantu, basobodde okukakasa bokka nti obutonde bw'ekisolo kino ekiboggolo ekitegeera ekikyamu kiyitibwa omuntu, buli kiseera bwe bumu, oba mu Bulaaya enkadde oba mu Afirika ekooye obuddu obungi, mu ttaka ettukuvu erya Vedas oba mu Buyindi obw'Ebugwanjuba, mu Austria oba mu China.

Eky'amazima kino, ekituufu ekikangabwa ekyewuunyisa omuntu yenna omunoonyereza, kiyinza okukakasibwa naddala omutambuzi bwakyalira Amasomero, Kollegi ne Yunivasite.

Tutuuse mu mulembe gw'okukola ebintu bingi mu bwangu. Kati buli kimu kikolebwa mu lufuli olw'omulundi gumu era ku mutindo ogwa waggulu. Ennyanguyirizi z'ennyanguyirizi, Emotoka, Ebintu eby'ebbeeyi, n'ebirala, n'ebirala, n'ebirala.

Newankubadde kiyinza okulabika ng'ekisirusiru kitono, kya mazima nti Amasomero g'ebyemikono, Yunivasite, n'ebirala nabyo bifuuse amakolero g'eby'obwongo agakola ebintu bingi mu bwangu.

Mu biseera bino eby'okukola ebintu bingi mu bwangu, ekigendererwa kyokka mu bulamu kwe kufuna obutebenkevu mu by'enfuna. Abantu batya buli kimu era banoonya obutebenkevu.

Okulowooza okw'obwannakyewa mu biseera bino eby'okukola ebintu bingi mu bwangu, kifuuka kumpi tekisoboka kubanga ekika ky'Obuyigirize ekya leero kisinziira ku byangu byokka.

"Omuyaga Omupya" gukkiriziganya nnyo n'obunafu buno obw'obwongo. Singa omuntu ayagala okuba omuwufu, okuba omuwufu ku balala, buli muntu amuvuma, buli muntu amunenyeza, amuggyibwako, takkirizibwa kukola, n'ebirala.

Okwegomba okufuna ssente okubeerawo n'okusanyuka, obwangu obw'okutuuka ku buwanguzi mu bulamu, okunoonyereza obutebenkevu, obw'ebyenfuna, okwegomba okugula ebintu bingi okwenyumiriza mu maaso g'abalala, n'ebirala, bimukomya okulowooza okutukuvu, okw'obutonde n'okw'amangu.

Kisakiddwa ddala nti okutya kunafuya obwongo era kukunguzza omutima.

Mu biseera bino eby'okutya okungi n'okunoonya obutebenkevu, abantu beekweka mu mpuku zaabwe, mu biyumba byabwe, mu nsonda zaabwe, mu kifo we balowooza nti bayinza okufuna obutebenkevu obusingawo, ebizibu ebitono era tebaagala kuvaayo, batya obulamu, batya ebizibu ebipya, okukuba ebirooto ebipya, n'ebirala, n'ebirala, n'ebirala.

Obuyigirize buno bwonna obusukkiridde obwa leero businziira ku kutya n'okunoonya obutebenkevu, abantu batidde, batya n'ekisiikirize kyabwe.

Abantu, batya buli kimu, batya okuva mu mateeka amakadde agassiddwawo, okuba abawufu ku bantu abalala, okulowooza mu ngeri ey'obujeemu, okumenya okusosola kwonna okw'ekitongole ekigwa, n'ebirala.

Ku lw'omukisa, waliwo abantu batono abeesimbu era abategeera abayagala okwekenneenya ebizibu byonna eby'omutima mu bujjuvu, naye mu bangi ku ffe tetulina na mwoyo gwa butakkiriziganya na bujeemu.

Waliwo ebika by'OBUJEEMU bibiri ebyamaze okukwatibwa. Ekisooka: Obujeemu bw'obwongo obw'amaanyi. Ekyokubiri: Obujeemu bw'obwongo obw'omunda obw'OBWENGE.

Ekika ekisooka eky'Obujeemu bwe bukubiriza obukakafu n'obutasobola kukulaakulana. Ekika ekyokubiri eky'Obujeemu bwe BUJJEEMU.

Mu kika ekisooka eky'Obujeemu bw'obwongo tusanga OMUYIIRIZI akubiriza engoye enkadde era n'addaabiriza bbugwe bw'ebizimbe ebikadde obutaleke kubagwa, omuntu adda ennyuma, Omujeemu ow'omusaayi n'omwenge, omukulembeze w'obukulembeze n'enkubagana z'Amasaza, omusajja alina emmundu ku kibegaabega kye, Omufuzi Omukulu asanyukira okutwala abo bonna abatakkiriza okwegomba kwe, enjigiriza ze.

Mu kika ekyokubiri eky'Obujeemu bw'obwongo tusanga BUDDHA, YESU, HERMES, omukyusa, OMUJEEMU OW'OBWENGE, OMUYIIRIZI, abalwanirizi ABAKULU ab'ENKYUKAKYUKA Y'OMUTIMA, n'ebirala, n'ebirala, n'ebirala.

Abo bokka abeeyigiriza n'ekigendererwa ekitaliimu makulu eky'okulinnya ebifo eby'amaanyi mu nnyumba y'obuwangwa, okulinnya, okutambula waggulu ku madaala, okwewulira, n'ebirala, tebalina buziba bwa mazima, ba Bisirusiru mu butonde, abangi, abataliimu, ebitundu kikumi ku kikumi ababbi.

Kyakakasiddwa dda okutuuka ku kumatiza nti singa mu muntu temuli KUKWATAGANIRA kw'endowooza n'enneewulira okw'amazima, newankubadde nga tufunye obuyigirize obungi, obulamu buvaamu obutaggwa, obuzito, obutaweera era obutawulirizigwa okutya okutabalika okwa buli kika.

Wabweru w'okubuusabuusa kwonna era nga tetutya kukyamya, tusobola okukakasa n'obukugu nti awatali buyigirize obw'OMULAMBE, obulamu buvaamu obuluma, obutaliimu mugaso era obw'akabi.

EKISOLO EKITEGEERA kirina EGO EY'OMUNDA erimu emirembe egyawukana ku lw'omukisa egizimba ne BUYIGIRIZE EBUTALI BULUNGI.

OBWE BWENGERA BWE TUTWALIRA MUNDA, bwe nsonga enkulu ey'obuzibu bwaffe bwonna n'okwewandiisa.

OBWENGE OBW'OMUSIINGI bulina okuyigiriza emirembe emipya ENJIGIRIZA yaffe ey'OBWENGE eri OKUSAANYAWO kwa OBWE.

Nga tubuzaabuza bbulooka ezawukana ezikola awamu Ego (OBWE) tusobola okutandikawo mu ffe ekifo ekimalawo eky'okumanya omuntu omu, olwo tujja kuba ABAMALIRIZI.

Nga OBWE BWE TUJJUZIZZA bwe buliwo munda mu buli omu ku ffe, tetumalangawo bulamu bwaffe bokka, wabula era twonoonanga obulamu bw'abalala.

Kigasa ki okusoma amateeka n'okufuuka baloya, singa tukuumira ebyembi? Kigasa ki okukuuma mu bwongo bwaffe okumanya okungi, singa tweyongera okutabukwa? Migaso ki gy'obukugu obw'ebyemikono n'amakolero singa tubukozesa okuzikiriza baliraanwa baffe?

Tekirina mugaso kutendekebwa, kugenda mu bibiina, okusoma, singa mu nkola y'obulamu obwa bulijjo twonoonekagana mu ngeri ey'ennaku.

Ekigendererwa ky'obuyigirize tekisaanye kubeera kukola buli mwaka abanoonya emirimu abapya bokka, ekika ekipya eky'ababbi, abataka abapya abatamanyi na kussaamu kitiibwa Ddiini y'omuliraanwa, n'ebirala.

Ekigendererwa ekituufu eky'OBWENGE OBW'OMUSIINGI kisaanye kubeera kussawo abasajja n'abakazi ABAKULIZIBWA era olw'ekyo okukkiriziganya n'abantu abagezi.

Ku lw'omukisa, abasomesa n'abasomesa b'Amasomero, Kollegi ne Yunivasite, balowooza ku buli kimu, okuggyako okuzuukusa OBWENGE OBW'OMULAMBE obw'ABABBI.

Omuntu yenna ayinza okwegomba n'okufuna ebbaluwa, okusiimibwa, dipulooma n'okutuuka n'okufuuka omukugu mu kifo eky'ebyuma eky'obulamu, naye kino tekitegeeza kuba MUGENZI.

OBWENGE tebuyinza kubeera mbeera ya kwemalirira kwabangi, OBWENGE tebusobola kubeera biva mu kumanya kw'ebitabo obwangu, OBWENGE si busobozi bwa kukola mu butongole n'ebigambo eby'ekikulukuto ku busoomoozi bwonna. OBWENGE si kyemalira kwabangi okw'okujjukira. OBWENGE bwe busobozi obw'okufuna butereevu ESSENCE, ekintu EKITUFU, ekiri mazima.

OBWENGE OBW'OMUSIINGI bwe ssayansi etusobozesa okuzuukusa obusobozi buno mu ffe bokka ne mu balala.

OBWENGE OBW'OMUSIINGI buyamba buli MUNTU okuzuula EBIRINA OMUTINDO eby'amazima ebivaamu ng'omukisa gw'okunoonyereza okw'omunda n'OKUTEGEERA OKW'OMULAMBE KWA SI MWENNYE.

Singa mu ffe temuli KUMANYA BYE MWENNYE, olwo OKUYOLEKA BYE MWENNYE kufuuka OKUKAKASA BYE MWENNYE OKW'OBWEBWANNYE ERA EKIZIKIRIZA.

OBWENGE OBW'OMUSIINGI bwe bweraliikirira okuzuukusa mu buli muntu OKUSOBOZA okwetegeera mu bifo byonna eby'omutima so si kwekuwa obukakafu obw'OKUYOLEKA BYE MWENNYE okukyamu OBWE BWE TUJJUZIZZA.
