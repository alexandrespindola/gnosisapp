Tukizuulizza okuzuula nti abasomesa bangi aba History Universal mu bugwanjuba bw'ensi batera okusekerera BUDDHA, Confucius, Muhammad, Hermes, Quetzacoatl, Musa, Krishna, n'abalala.

Tukizuulizza nti okujooga, okusekerera, n'okujerega okukolebwa abasomesa ku ddiini enkadde, ku bakatonda, ne ku byafaayo, byonna bya busiru.

Mu masomero, amatendekero, ne yunivasite, ensonga z'eddiini zandisaliddwaamu n'ekitiibwa ekingi, n'entiisa ey'amaanyi, n'amagezi ag'omutima.

Enkola z'eddiini zikuuma emisingi egitalekeraawo era zitegekeddwa okusinziira ku bwetaavu bw'abantu ba buli ggwanga n'ebyafaayo byabwe.

Ediini zonna zirina emisingi gye gimu, emisingi egy'olubeerera era zaawukana mu ngeri zokka.

Si kya magezi Omukristayo okusekerera eddiini ya Buddha oba eddiini y'Abayudaaya oba ey'Abahindu kubanga ediini zonna zeesigamiziddwa ku ntobo ze gimu.

Okusekerera kw'abakugu bangi ku ddiini n'abatandisi baazo kuleetebwa obutwa bwa MARXIST obutamiiriza emitima eminafu gyonna mu biseera bino.

Abasomesa b'amasomero, amatendekero, ne yunivasite balina okuluŋŋamya abayizi baabwe mu kkubo ery'ekitiibwa ekituufu eri baliraanwa baffe.

Kya kibi era si kya buntu omuntu omufu ng'asinziira ku njigiriza ya ngeri yonna okusekerera amasinzizo, ediini, obuyigirize, amasomero oba ebibiina eby'omwoyo.

Abayizi bwe bava mu bibiina by'okusoma balina okukolagana n'abantu b'amaddiini gonna, amasomero, obuyigirize era si kya magezi omuntu obutamanya n'akamwenyumwenyu akasaanira mu kifo ekitukuvu.

Abavubuka n'abawala bwe bava mu bibiina oluvannyuma lw'emyaka kkumi oba kkumi na etaano gy'ebyenjigiriza, basangibwa nga bagayaavu era beebase ng'abantu abalala, nga bajjuddemu obwereere era nga tebalina magezi ng'olunaku olwasooka lwe baayingira essomero.

Kyanguwa abayizi, mu bintu ebirala, okukulaakulanya essaza ly'enneewulira kubanga byonna si bwongo. Kyetaagisa okuyiga okuwulira enkolagana ez'omunda ez'obulamu, obulungi bw'omuti oguliwo gumu, okuyimba kw'akanyonyi mu kibira, omuziki ogw'enkolagana n'embala z'omusaayi omulungi.

Kyetaagisa era okuwulira n'okutegeera obulungi enjawulo ezitiisa zonna ez'obulamu, gamba ng'entegeka y'ebyenfuna ekakali n'etali ya kusaasira mu kiseera kino kye tuliimu, enguudo ezijjuddemu bamaama abatali basanyufu n'abaana baabwe abalwadde n'enjala nga basaba ekitundu ky'omugaati, ebizimbe ebibi abantu abaavu enkumi n'enkumi gye babeera, enguudo enseenya abantu enkumi n'enkumi ze batambuliramu mu mmotoka ezikozesa amafuta ago agasaanyawo ebiramu, n'ebirala.

Omuyizi ava mu bibiina alina okukolagana si na bugyafu bwe bwokka na bizibu bye, naye era n'obugyafu bw'abantu bonna n'ebizibu eby'enjawulo eby'omuntu.

Ekisinga obubi kye kino nti omuyizi ava mu bibiina, newankubadde alina okutegeera okw'omutwe, talina magezi, omwoyo gwe gwebase, tategekeddwa bulungi okulwana obulamu.

Ekiseera kituuse okunonyereza n'okuzuula ekyo ekiyitibwa AMAKULU. Enkola y'ebigambo, ekitabo ekikyusibwa, biremwa okutegeeza bulungi AMAKULU,

Nga tewali magezi, tekisoboka kubaawo nkyukakyuka yonna nnungi oba essanyu erya nnamaddala era kikumi ku lukumi okusanga abantu abagezi mu bulamu.

Ekikulu mu bulamu si kumanya kigambo AMAKULU kyokka, wabula okukyekubira mu mbeera yaffe.

Bangi beebaza olw'amagezi, tewali mutamiivu atalyebaza olw'amagezi era Carlos Marx ng'alowooza nti mugezi nnyo, yawandiika eky'obulimba kye eky'eby'obugagga ekitutte ensi okufiirwa emisingi egy'olubeerera, okutta bakabona enkumi n'enkumi ab'amaddiini ag'enjawulo, okukabasanya bannamukadde, Ababuda, Abakristayo, n'ebirala, okuzikiriza amasinzizo mangi, okutulugunya enkumi n'enkumi n'obukadde bw'abantu, n'ebirala n'ebirala.

Omuntu yenna ayinza okwebaza olw'amagezi, ekizibu okuba nga mazima.

Si kufuna mawulire mangi ag'ebitabo, okumanya ebintu bingi, okukubira abantu bingi, okufuna ssente ennyingi okugula abalamuzi n'abapoliisi, n'ebirala nga okutuuka ku kyo ekiyitibwa AMAKULU.

Si na MAAS, engeri gy'oyinza okutuuka okufuna AMAKULU. Abo abalowooza nti amagezi gasobola okuwangulwa n'enkola ya MAAS bakyamu.

Kyanguwa okutegeera mu bujjuvu ne mu bifo byonna eby'obwongo obutategeerekeka n'obutamanyika, ekyo ekiriwo ekitali kirungi ekya MAAS, kubanga mu nsiinya ekwekebwa mu kyama ddala EGO gwe twagala, YO, YE NZE, ayagala era ayagala bulijjo MAAS ne MAAS okukulaakulanya n'okugumya.

Mefistófeles gwe tulina munda, SITAANI ono, YE ono, agamba: NZE nnina ssente ennyingi, obulungi bungi, amagezi mangi okusinga oyo, obuwanguzi bungi, obukujukuju bungi, n'ebirala n'ebirala.

Buli ayagala okutegeera ddala ekyo AMAKULU kye kitegeeza, alina okuyiga okuwulira, alina okukyekubira n'okukikolera ng'ayitira mu kufumiitiriza okw'amaanyi.

Ebintu byonna abantu bye bakuŋŋaanya wakati w'entaana enyivu ey'okujjukira okutali beesigwa, amawulire ag'omutwe, okukubira obulamu, bulijjo bifuuka bubi mu bigambo bya MAAS ne MAAS. Bwe batyo tebatuuka kumanya makulu matuufu ag'ebyo byonna bye bakuŋŋaanya.

Amagezi tegafuna amawulire mangi ag'ebitabo, newankubadde okukubira okungi, newankubadde ssente ennyingi, newankubadde obuwanguzi bungi, amagezi gasobola okukula mu ffe bwe tutegeera enkola yonna eya YO, bwe tutegeera bulungi automatism yonna ey'obwongo eya MAAS.

Kyetaagisa okutegeera nti obwongo bwe busaale obukulu obwa MAAS. Mazima ddala MAAS oyo YE omu bwongo bwe busaba era obwongo bwe ntobo yaayo ey'omusingi.

Buli ayagala okuba omugezi ddala, alina okusalawo okufa si ku mutendera gw'okutegeera omunafu gwokka, wabula ne mu bifo byonna eby'obwongo obutategeerekeka n'obutamanyika.
