Ebirungi n'ebibi tebiriwo. Ekintu kiba kirungi bwe kitugasa, era kibi bwe kitatugasa. Ebirungi n'ebibi nsonga ya kwegomba kwaffe n'okuterekeresa kw'obwongo.

Omusajja eyayiiya ebigambo eby'akabi ebirungi n'ebibi yali Muyatlanti ayitibwa MAKARI KRONVERNKZYON omumemba omwatiikirivu ow'Ekibiina ky'Abasaayansi AKALDAN, ekisangibwa ku lukalu lwa Atlantiki olwazikizibwa.

Omusajja omukadde omusajja ow'amagezi teyateebereza bulabe bunene bwe yali agenda okuleetera abantu, n'okuyiiya ebigambo bye ebibiri.

Abasaayansi ba Atlantiki baasoma nnyo amaanyi gonna AGAKULA, AGAZZA EMABUGA ne GANAKUTA mu BUTONGOLE, naye omusajja ono omukadde ow'amagezi yajjukira ekirowoozo ky'okunnyonnyola ebiri ebisooka n'ebigambo Ebirungi n'Ebibi. Amaanyi ag'ekika EKIKULA yagayita malungi n'amaanyi ag'ekika EKIZA EMABUGA yagabatiza n'ekigambo eky'embi. Amaanyi aganakuuta teyagatuuma mannya.

Amaanyi ago gakolebwa munda mu muntu ne munda mu butonde ng'amaanyi aganakuuta ge nsonga enkulu era eya bwemage.

Emmyaka mingi emabega oluvannyuma lw'okuzikizibwa kwa ATLANTIDA ne POISEDONIS yaayo ennyuvu Platón gy'ayogera mu kitabo kye ekya República, mu ssembeera ya TIKLYAMISHAYANA eya buvanjuba mwalimu kabona omuwanvu ennyo eyakola ensobi enkulu ennyo ey'okukozesa ebigambo ebirungi n'ebibi mu ngeri etategeerekeka okuzimba omusingi gw'empisa. Erinnya ly'omuntu oyo kabona yali ARMANATOORA.

Nga ebiseera bigenda bikuulukuta okuyita mu byasa bingi, abantu baalya obutwa n'ebigambo bino ebibiri ne babifuula omusingi gw'amateeka gaabwe gonna ag'empisa. Ennaku zino ebigambo bino ebibiri omuntu abisanga ne mu supu.

Mu kiseera kino waliwo ABAKYUSA bangi abaagala OKUZZA EBISA BY'EMPISA naye eky'ennaku gye bali n'ensi eno enakuwavu balina obwongo obusibe mu BINENGA n'EBIBI.

Empisa zonna zizimbibwa ku bigambo ebyo ebitono EBIRUNGI n'EBIBI era omuntu yenna ANEEKAKASA EBISA BY'EMPISA mu butuufu MUTABUKUFU.

Ebigambo EBIRUNGI n'EBIBI bibeera bya mugaso bulijjo OKUWEREZA oba OKUSALIRA OMUSANGO ensobi zaffe ffe.

Oyo awereza oba asalira omusango tategeera. Kya magezi okutegeera enkula y'amaanyi AGAKULA naye si kya magezi okugawereza n'ekigambo EKIRUNGI. Kya magezi okutegeera enkola y'amaanyi AGEGAZA EMABUGA naye kiba kya busiru okugasalira omusango n'ekigambo EKY'EMBI.

Amaanyi gonna agatambula buviiriyo gasobola okufuuka amaanyi agatambula munda. Amaanyi gonna AGEGAZA EMABUGA gasobola okukyusibwa ne gafuuka AMAKULA.

Munda mu nkola ezitaliiko kkomo ez'amaanyi mu mbeera EY'OKUKULA mulimu enkola ezitaliiko kkomo ez'amaanyi mu mbeera EY'OKUZA EMABUGA.

Munda mu buli muntu mulimu ebika by'amaanyi eby'enjawulo EBIBALA, EBIZA EMABUGA era bikyuka awatali kulekayo.

Okuwereza ekika ky'amaanyi ekimu n'okusalira omusango ekirala, si kutegeera. Ekikulu kwe kutegeera.

Obumanyirivu bw'AMAZIMA bubadde butera okubaawo mu bantu olw'ensonga etaliimu kyewaliriza ey'obuzibu bw'obwongo. Abantu basibe wakati w'ebizibu EBIRUNGI n'EBIBI.

EKITEBBEREZO KY'OBWAMAGEZI EKIKYUSA EKIBINA kya GNÓSTICO kyesigamiziddwa ku kusoma ebika by'amaanyi eby'enjawulo ebikolera munda mu mubiri gw'omuntu era munda mu butonde.

EKIBINA kya GNÓSTICO kirina EMPISA EZIKYUSA ezitalina kakwate na mpisa za bitabukuufu newaakubadde n'ebigambo ebikuuma era ebizza emabuga eby'EBIRUNGI n'EBIBI.

Munda mu labolatori yoobuzaale ey'omubiri gw'omuntu mulimu amaanyi agakula, agazza emabuga era aganakuuta agasaanidde okusomebwa era okutegeerekeka mu ngeri ey'omunda.

Ekigambo EBIRUNGI kiziyiza OKUTEGEERA kw'amaanyi AGAKULA olw'okuwereza.

Ekigambo EBIBI kiziyiza okutegeera kw'amaanyi AGEGAZA EMABUGA olw'okusala omusango.

Okuwereza oba okusalira omusango tekitegeeza kutegeera. Oyo ayagala okukomya obunafu bwe tasaanidde kubuwereza newaakubadde okubusalira omusango. Kyanguwa OKUTEGEERA ensobi zaffe.

Okutegeera OBUSUNGU ku mitendera gyonna egy'obwongo kikulu okusobola okutuzaala obuteefu n'obuwombeefu.

Okutegeera ebitundu ebitabalika eby'omululu kissa omusingi okusobola okutuzaala okwagala abantu n'okweyagaliza.

Okutegeera OBWENZI ku mitendera gyonna egy'obwongo bwe busuubuzi obwetaagisa okusobola okutuzaala obuyonjo obwa nnamaddala.

Okutegeera OBUTIMA mu bifo byonna eby'obwongo kimala okusobola okutuzaala omwoyo gw'obumu n'essanyu olw'obulungi n'enkula y'abalala.

Okutegeera amalala mu bitundu byonna n'obukulu bwe y'ensonga enkulu okusobola okutuzaala mu ngeri ey'obutonde era ennyangu ekimuli eky'ekitalo eky'obuwombeefu.

Okutegeera ekintu ekyo eky'amaanyi ekiyitibwa obugayaavu, si mu ngeri yaakyo embi gyokka naye ne mu ngeri yaakyo ennyuvu ennyo, kissa omusingi okusobola okutuzaala omwoyo ogw'omulimu.

Okutegeera engeri ez'enjawulo ez'OBULULU n'obululu obungi kyenkanankana n'okuzikiriza emize gy'omutima gw'olubuto gamba ng'embaga, okutamira, okuyigga, okulya ennyama, okutya okufa, okwagala okukuuma YO, okutya okusaanyizibwawo, n'ebirala.

Abasomesa b'amasomero, amatendekero ne yunivasite bagamba abayizi baabwe abasajja n'abakazi okukyusa ng'enkyukakyuka ya YO bw'esoboka, okufuna ebisa ebimu ng'ebisa bwe bisobola okufunibwa YO, n'ebirala.

Kyanguwa okutegeera nti YO tekyuka n'akatono, nti tekiba kimalirivu era oyo eyeegomba ebisa anyweza YO.

OKUMALIRIZA okujjumbira kuzaalibwa munda mu ffe n'okusaanyaawo kwa YO. Ebisa bizaalibwa munda mu ffe mu ngeri ey'obutonde era ennyangu bwe tutegeera obunafu bwaffe obw'omubiri si ku mutendera gw'obwongo gwokka naye ne mu bifo byonna eby'obwongo ebiri wansi n'eby'obwongo ebitaliiko.

Okwagala okukyusa kya busiru, okwagala obutukuvu butima, okwagala ebisa kitegeeza okunyweza YO n'obutwa bw'omululu.

Twetaaga okufa okujjumbira YO si ku mutendera gw'obwongo gwokka naye ne mu buli kanyoolo, kitundu, kifo n'ekkubo ly'obwongo. Bwe tuba twafudde ddala, ekisigala mu ffe kye ekyo EKIMAWULIRE. Ekyo ekijjudde ebisa, Ekyo kye SSEENZA wa BEERI YAAFFE EY'OMUNDA, Ekyo ekitali kya kiseera.

Nga tutegeera mu bujjuvu enkola zonna ezitaliiko kkomo ez'amaanyi agakula agakulakulana munda mu ffe mu kino n'kati. Nga tutegeera mu bujjuvu eby'amaanyi AGEGAZA EMABUGA ebikolebwa munda mu ffe okuva mu kiseera ekimu okudda mu kirala, tusobola okusaanyaawo YO.

Ebigambo EBIRUNGI n'EBIBI bibeera bya mugaso OKUWEREZA n'OKUSALIRA OMUSANGO naye tebireeta kutegeera.

Buli bunafu bulina ebitundu bingi, emisingi, emisingi emabega n'obuziba. Okutegeera obunafu ku mutendera gw'obwongo tekitegeeza okuba nga obutegeedde mu bifo eby'enjawulo eby'obwongo eby'omunda, ebitaliiko, n'ebitakka wansi mu bwongo.

Obunafu bwonna busobola okubula ku mutendera gw'obwongo ne bugenda mu maaso mu bifo ebirala eby'obwongo.

OBUSUNGU bweyimisa ng'omulamuzi. Bangi tebaagala kuba balulu, waliwo abatayogera ku ssente naye baagala amaanyi ag'Obwamagezi, ebisa, okwagala essanyu wano oba oluvannyuma lw'okufa, n'ebirala, n'ebirala, n'ebirala.

Abasajja n'abakazi bangi basanyuka era basindiriza abantu ab'ekika ekirala "BALEETA" kubanga baagala obulungi, obwongo bwabwe obw'omunda bubalimbya, OBWENZI bweyimisa n'amagezi ag'okulaba obulungi.

Abatimanyi bangi baggwamu obutima abatukuvu era bakola eby'okwenenya era beekuba olw'okwagala nabo okutuuka okuba ABATUKUVU.

Abatimanyi bangi baggwamu obutima abo abeewaayo eri abantu era bwe baagala okuba abanene nabo, banyoomoola abo be baggwamu obutima era babassaako obusa bwabwe bwonna obugambidde.

Waliwo abeeraga olw'obukulu, ssente, ettutumu n'ekitiibwa era waliwo abeeraga olw'embeera yaabwe ey'obuwombeefu.

Diógenes yayogeranga olw'ekibbo kye mwe yasulanga era bwe yatuuka ew'omuka Sócrates yamulamusa ng'agamba: "Nnyinnyonnyola amalala go Sócrates, nnyinnyonnyola amalala go". "Yee, Diógenes, n'amalala go onnyinnyonnyola amalala gange". Ye kyaddibwa Sócrates.

Abakazi abeeraga beekuba enviiri, bambala era beekuma n'ebintu byonna bye basobola okusobola okuleeta obutima mu bakazi abalala, naye n'AMALAALA geyimisa n'ekkanzu ey'obuwombeefu.

Ennono egamba nti Arístipo omufirosoofo Omugirimaani ng'ayagala okulaga abantu bonna amagezi ge n'obuwombeefu bwe yayambala ekkanzu enkadde ennyo era ejjudde ebinnya, yakwata mu mukono gwe ogwa ddyo omuggo gw'Obufirosoofo era n'agenda ku nguudo za Atenas. Sócrates bwe yamulaba ng'ajja, yayogera nti: "Amalala go galabika okuyita mu binnya by'engoye zo, ggwe Arístipo".

Bangi bali mu bwetaavu olw'obutonde bw'obugayaavu, naye waliwo abantu abakola ennyo okusobola okweyimirizaawo naye bawulira obugayaavu okusoma era okwejjukira okusobola okusaanyaawo YO.

Bangi bavuddeko OBULULU n'obululu naye eky'ennaku batamira era bagenda okuyigga.

Buli bunafu bw'enjawulo era bukula era bukolebwa mu ngeri engendera okuva ku mutendera oguli wansi ennyo ogw'engero z'Abasaikolojjii okutuuka ku mutendera oguli waggulu ennyo.

Munda mu kulengejjera okw'ekika ky'ennyiriri, n'omusango gw'ekwekera.

Omusango era gweyimisa ng'Omutukuvu, Omutemwa, omuyonjo, Omutume, n'ebirala.

EBIRUNGI n'EBIBI tebiriwo, ebigambo ebyo bikola okunoonyereza obutaano n'okwewala okusoma okw'omunda n'okw'ebbalirira okw'obunafu bwaffe ffe.
