Kikulu nnyo okutegeera mu bujjuvu era mu buli kifo ky'obwongo, ekifo ekikulu ky'okufa kye ki, mu ngeri eno y'emu gye kisobola okutegeera obutafa mu bujjuvu,

Okulaba omubiri gw'omuntu omwagalwa nga gussiddwa mu ssanduuko tekitegeeza nti ottegeera ekibumba ky'okufa.

Amazima ge gategeerekeka mu kiseera kino n'ekirala. Amazima ku kufa tegandibadde kyawukana.

Obuntu bwaffe buli kiseera bwetaaga, nga bwe kityo obuntubulamu, obukuumi ku kufa, obuwandiike obwongerewako, omuntu omukulu atuwa obukuumi obw'ekifo ekirungi era n'ekika kyonna eky'obutafa okuva ku ntaana ettiisa.

Nze kennyini saagala nnyo kufa. Obuntu bwaffe bwetaaga okweyongera. Obuntu bwaffe butya nnyo okufa.

Amazima si nsonga ya kukkiriza oba okubuusabuusa. Amazima tegaalina kakwate na kwesiga, wadde okubuusabuusa. Amazima si nsonga ya birowoozo, mbalirira, ndowooza, ntegeera, nteekateeka, kuteebereza, busosoze, kwesiga, kwetala, n.k.k. Amazima ku kibanja ky'okufa tekiriwukana.

Amazima ku kibanja ky'okufa gasobola okumanyika okuyita mu kumanya kwaffe.

Kizibu okutuusa okumanya okutuufu okw'okufa eri oyo atamanyi.

Omusairi yenna asobola okuwandiika ebitabo ebirungi ku KWAAGALA, naye kizibu okutuusa amazima ku KWAAGALA eri abantu abatannayitamu mu ngeri eyo, tugamba nti kizibu okutuusa amazima ku kufa eri abantu abatanayitamu.

Oyo ayagala okumanya amazima ku kufa ateekwa okubuuliriza, okukola, okunoonyereza nga bwe kiragirwa, mu ngeri eyo y'emu gye tusobola okuzuula amakulu amakulu ag'okufa.

Okulaba n'okumanya okumaze emyaka mingi bitusobozesezza okutegeera nti abantu tebafaayo kutegeera amakulu amakulu ag'okufa; abantu kye bafaayo kye kweyongera mu bulamu obulala era ekyo kyokka.

Abantu bangi baagala okweyongera okuyita mu bintu, ekitiibwa, amaka, okukkiriza, ebirowoozo, abaana, n.k.k., era bwe bategeera nti ekika kyonna eky'okweyongera mu bwongo kibi, ekiseera kimu, kitajjulukuka, tekinnyuvu, olwo nga bewulira nga tebalina bukakafu, nga tebalina bulungi, batya, beeraliikirira, bajjula obutiisa obutaggwaawo.

Tebayagala kutegeera bantu babi, tebayagala kutegeera nti buli kintu kye kikula kiggwaawo mu kiseera.

Tebayagala kutegeera bantu babi nti buli kintu kye kikula kiggwaawo mu kiseera.

Tebayagala kutegeera bantu babi nti buli kintu kye kikula kifuuka kikolwa, kinyigiriza, kikooya.

Kikulu nnyo, kyetaagisa, tekisoboka, okututegeera mu bujjuvu amakulu amakulu ag'okufa, mu ngeri eyo y'emu etya olw'okulekeraawo okubeerawo liggwaawo.

Nga tutunuulira abantu n'obwegendereza, tusobola okukakasa nti obwongo buli kiseera buzibiriddwa mu ekyo ekimanyiddwa era bwetaaga nti ekyo ekimanyiddwa kiggweewo okuyita ku ntaana.

Obwongo obuzibiriddwa mu ekyo ekimanyiddwa, tebusobola kumanya ekyo ekitegeerekeka, ekikulu, ekituufu.

Okumenya butto okuyita mu kutunula obulungi, tusobola okumanya ETERNAL, ATEMPORAL, REAL.

Abo abaagala okweyongera batya okufa era okukkiriza n'embalirira zaabwe zibakolera nga ddagala lya nsiike.

Okufa kwennyini tekulina kye kweraliikiriza, kintu kirungi nnyo, kya waggulu, kiyita ku bigambo, naye obwongo obuzibiriddwa: mu ekyo ekimanyiddwa, kigenda mu mbeera ey'okweraliikirira okuva mu kukkiriza okudda mu kubuusabuusa.

Bwe tumanya mu bujjuvu amakulu amakulu ag'okufa, tuzuula olwo fekka okuyita mu kumanya okw'okufa, nti Obulamu n'Okufa byonna bikola obumu obujjuvu, obumu.

Okufa kwe tuteeka Obulamu. Ekkubo ly'Obulamu likoleddwa n'ebigere by'okufa.

Obulamu bwe ssomo ery'amaanyi era eritukwatako. Okuva nga tuzaaliddwa okutuuka nga tufudde amatika ag'amaanyi ag'enjawulo gajjula omubiri gw'omuntu.

Ekika kyokka eky'amaanyi omubiri gw'omuntu gwe gutasobola okugumira, lwe RAYI Y'OKUFA. Rayi eno erina voltisi y'amasannyalaze ya waggulu nnyo. Omubiri gw'omuntu tegusobola kugumira voltisi eyo.

Nga rayi bw'esobola okubetenta omuti, bwe kityo rayi y'okufa bw'ekulukuta mu mubiri gw'omuntu, emwonoona mu ngeri etasobola kwerabirwa.

Rayi y'okufa etuukanya ekifo okufa, n'ekifo okuzaala.

Rayi y'okufa ereetawo okukakibwa kw'amasannyalaze okw'amaanyi nnyo n'ennangaamu enkulu erina obusobozi, okusalawo okukwataganya obutaffaali obulamu mu ggi lye lyaletaawo.

Rayi y'okufa ekendeeza omubiri gw'omuntu okudda mu bifaananyi byagwo eby'omusingi.

Ekyo amazima ku kufa kye ki, ekifo wakati w'okufa n'okuzala kye ki kintu ekitali kya kiseera era nti okuyita mu ssaayansi y'okutunula y'emu gye tusobola okumanya.
