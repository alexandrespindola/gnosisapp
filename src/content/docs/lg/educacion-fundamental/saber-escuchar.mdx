Mu nsi yonna mulimu abayogera bangi abawuniikiriza olw'obumanyirivu bwabwe mu by'okwogera, naye abantu abamanyi okuwuliriza batono.

Okumanya okuwuliriza kizibu nnyo, abatono be bantu ddala abamanyi okuwuliriza.

OMUSOMESA bw'aba ayogera, omusomesa omukyala, omusomi, abawuliriza balabika nga bawuliriza nnyo, nga bagoberera buli kigambo ky'omwogezi, buli kimu kireeta ekirowoozo nti bawuliriza, nti bali mu mbeera ey'obwegendereza, naye mu mbeera y'obwongo ey'omuntu kinnoomu mulimu omuwandiisi awandiika buli kigambo ky'omwogezi.

OMUWANDEESI ONO YE NZE, YE NZE MWENNYE, YE YE MWENNYE. Omulimu gw'omuwandiisi ono kwe kuggusaamu amakulu, okukyusaamu amakulu g'ebigambo by'omwogezi.

NZE akukyusaamu okusinziira ku bukyamu bwe, enkirowoozo ze, okutya kwe, amalala ge, okweraliikirira kwe, ebirowoozo bye, okujjukira kwe, n'ebirala bingi.

Abayizi mu ssomero, abayizi abakyala, abantu abagattiddwa awamu okukola abawuliriza abawulira, tebawuliriza ddala mwogezi, beewuliriza bo, bawulira EGO yaabwe, EGO yaabwe ennungi ennyo ey'OBUNNALUKALALA, eteyetegefu okukkiriza EKITUufu, EKIKULU, EKIKULU.

Mu mbeera ey'obwegendereza empya yokka, nga OMULAMWA gwesigamiziddwa ku bwongo obw'AMAANYI obutaliiko buzito bwa biseera byayita, mu mbeera ey'OKUKKIRIZA okujjuvu, tusobola okwewuliriza ddala nga tetuyingiliddwamu omuwandiisi omubi ennyo ayitibwa NZE, YE NZE MWENNYE, YE YE MWENNYE, EGO.

Bw'oba bwongo bwateekebwateekebwa ku kujjukira, bwe buzzeemu bye butereeze.

Omulamwa ogwateekebwateekebwa ku byafaayo by'emyaka emingi egyayita, gusobola okulaba ennaffe okuyita mu ndabirwamu ezitatangaala ez'ebiseera byayita.

BWE TUYAGALA OKUMANYA OKUWULIRIZA, bwe tuba twagala okuyiga okuwuliriza okuzuula ebipya, tuteekwa okutambulira ku endowooza y'AKATAMBI AKATONO.

Kikulu nnyo okubeerawo okuva mu kaseera akamu okudda mu kalala nga tetweraliikirira ebiseera byayita, era nga tetuteekateeka biseera byomu maaso.

AMAZIMA bye bitamanyiddwa okuva mu kaseera akamu okudda mu kalala, ebirowoozo byaffe biteekwa okuba nga byegendereza bulijjo, nga biteekateeka obulungi, nga tebiriiko bukyamu, enkirowoozo, okusobola okukkiriza ddala.

Abasomesa n'abasomesa abakyala ab'omu masomero bateekwa okuyigiriza abayizi baabwe amakulu amakulu agakwatagana n'okumanya okuwuliriza.

Kyetaagisa okuyiga okubeerawo mu ngeri ey'amagezi, okunyweza ebirowoozo byaffe, okutangaaza empisa zaffe, ebirowoozo byaffe, enneewulira zaffe.

Tekirina mugaso kufuna bukugu bwa by'amasomero, bwe tutamanya kuwuliriza, bwe tuba tetusobola kuzuula ebipya okuva mu kaseera akamu okudda mu kalala.

Twetaaga okulongoosa obwegendereza, okulongoosa empisa zaffe, okulongoosa abantu baffe, ebintu, n'ebirala bingi.

Tekisoboka kubeera nga tulongoosefu ddala bwe tutamanya kuwuliriza.

Ebirowoozo ebikakanyavu, ebitamanyiddwa, ebyonoonefu, ebyatattagana tebimanyi kuwuliriza, tebimanyi kuzuula bipya, ebirowoozo ebyo bitegeera, bitegeera mu ngeri enkyamu emikutule egitasobola kutegeerekeka ey'omuwandiisi oyo omubi ayitibwa NZE, YE NZE MWENNYE, EGO.

Okulongoosebwa kintu kizibu nnyo era kyetaaga obwegendereza obujjuvu. Omuntu ayinza okubeera omulongoosefu ennyo mu misono, engoye, ebijanjaalo, emmotoka, emikwano, naye era n'aweeyo okweyongera okubeera omukakanyavu, atali mulongoosefu, omuzito.

Asobola okubeerawo okuva mu kaseera akamu okudda mu kalala, atambula ddala mu kkubo ly'okulongoosebwa okw'amazima.

Alina omulamwa ogukkiriza, ogw'amaanyi, omugolokofu, ogwegendereza, atambula mu kkubo ly'okulongoosebwa okw'amazima.

Azigulawo eri ebipya byonna ng'aleka obuzito bw'ebiseera byayita, enkirowoozo, obukyayi, obutakwatagana, obutakyusakyusa, n'ebirala, atambula n'obuwanguzi mu kkubo ly'okulongoosebwa okw'amazima.

Omulamwa ogwonoonefu gubeera gugattiddwa mu biseera byayita, mu nkirowoozo, amalala, okwagala kwe, obukyayi, n'ebirala.

Omulamwa ogwonoonefu tegumanyi kulaba bupya, tegumanyi kuwuliriza, guteekebwateekebwa ku KWAGALA KWE.

Abakugu mu MARXISMO-LENINISMO tebakkiriza bupya; tebakkiriza ENGERI ennya ez'ebintu byonna, EKIBALO eky'okuna, olw'okwagala kwe, beeyagala nnyo, beegattira ku njigiriza zaabwe ez'obukakanyavu, era nga bwe tubateeka mu mbeera y'ebikolwa ebituufu, bwe tubalaga obusirusiru bw'ebigambo byabwe, bayimusa omukono gwabwe ogwa kkono, balaba obugenyi bw'omusaagalizi waabwe, bawa okwewolereza okutaliiko makulu ne bagenda.

Ebyo bye birowoozo ebyonoonefu, ebirowoozo ebyakula, ebitamanyi kuwuliriza, ebitamanyi kuzuula ebipya, ebitakkiriza mazima kubanga biterekeddwa mu KWAGALA KWE. Ebirowoozo ebeeyagala nnyo, ebirowoozo ebitalina MAKULU GA BYA BUWANGWA, ebirowoozo ebikakanyavu, ebirowoozo ebitamanyiddwa, ebiwuliriza EGO yaabwe ennungi.

OBUSINGI BW'ENJIGIRIZA buyigiriza okuwuliriza, buyigiriza okubeerawo mu ngeri ey'amagezi.

Abasomesa n'abasomesa abakyala ab'omu masomero, abakakiiko, yunivasite bateekwa okuyigiriza abayizi baabwe ekkubo ettuufu ery'okulongoosebwa okw'amazima.

Tekirina mugaso okumala emyaka kkumi n'ettaano nga tuli mu masomero, abakakiiko ne yunivasite, bwe tuvaayo nga munda tuli mbizzi mu birowoozo byaffe, ebirowoozo byaffe, enneewulira zaffe n'obulombolombo bwaffe.

Twetaaga OBUSINGI BW'ENJIGIRIZA mu bwangu kubanga emirembe emipya gitegeeza okutandika kw'emirembe emipya.

Ekiseera kituuse eky'ENKYUKAKYUKA EY'AMAZIMA, ekiseera kituuse eky'ENKYUKAKYUKA EY'OBUSINGI.

Ebiseera byayita bye biseera byayita era byazzaawo ebibala byabyo. Twetaaga okutegeera amakulu amakulu ag'akaseera ke tulimu.
