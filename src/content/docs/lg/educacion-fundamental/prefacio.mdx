"Obuyigirize Obukulu" bwe ssaayansi atusobozesa okuzuula enkolagana yaffe n'abantu bannaffe, n'obutonde, n'ebintu byonna. Okuyita mu ssaayansi ono, tumanya engeri obwongo gye bukola kubanga obwongo kye kikozesebwa okumanya era tulina okuyiga okukozesa ekikozesebwa ekyo, kye kintu ekikulu ku bwaffe obw'omunda.

Mu kitabo kino, tuyigirizibwa mu ngeri etegeerekeka engeri y'Okulowooza, okuyita mu kunoonyereza, okwekenneenya, okutegeera, n'okufumiitiriza.

Kitutegeeza, engeri y'okutereezaamu ebifaayo by'obwongo nga twesigama ku bintu bisatu: omuntu, ekintu, n'ekifo; eky'okujjukira kikolebwa okwagala, bwe kityo kibeerawo okussa essira ku ekyo ekisomwa okusobola okukwatibwa mu bwongo. Eky'okujjukira kikyusibwa okuyita mu nkola y'okukyusa ebintu, abayizi abanonyereza ku kukula kwabwe bennyini be bagenda okutandika okumanya.

Eri Abazungu, okusoma kutandika ku myaka 6 oba bwe kibalirirwa nti batandise okukozesa obwongo; eri Abawalabu, naddala Abahindu, obuyigirize butandika okuva mu lubuto; eri Abagnostiki okuva mu mukwano, kwe kugamba, nga tebannaba kuzaala.

Obuyigirize obw'omu maaso bugenda kwesigama ku bitundu bibiri: ekimu kigenda kukolebwa bazadde n'ekirala kigenda kukolebwa abasomesa. Obuyigirize obw'omu maaso bugenda kuteeka abayizi mu kumanya okw'Obwakatonda okuyiga okubeera bazadde. Omukyala ky'etaaga kwe kukuuma, obuyambi, y'ensonga lwaki omuwala yeegattako nnyo taata ng'akyali muto kubanga amulaba nga wa maanyi nnyo; omulenzi yeetaaga okwagala, okufaayo, okusanyusa, y'ensonga lwaki omulenzi yeegattako nnyo maama mu ngeri eteri ya bulijjo. Oluvannyuma, ng'ebintu bya bombi bimaze okukyusibwa, omukyala anoonya omusajja omulungi oba era omusajja amwagala, ng'alina okuba ng'awa okwagala, era, omusajja anoonya omukyala alina ensimbi ez'okubeerawo oba alina omulimu; eri abalala, endabika n'emibiri by'ebikulembeza okusobola okusanyusa ebirowoozo byabwe.

Kyewuunyisa okulaba ebitabo by'essomero, buli kitabo nga kirimu ebibuuzo enkumi n'enkumi, omuwandiisi by'addamu mu buwandiike abayizi basobole okubikwata mu mutwe, eky'okujjukira ekitali kyamanyi kye kiterekero ky'obumanyi abavubuka bwe basoma n'obunyiikivu bungi, obuyigirize obwo obw'omubiri bwokka bubasobozesa okweyimirizaawo nga bamaze okusoma, naye ku bulamu bwe bagenda okubeeraamu tebalina kye bamanyi, bayingira mu bwo nga bazibe, tebayigiriziddwa na kwazaala bintu mu ngeri ey'ekitiibwa, okuyigiriza okwo kukolebwa abamenyi b'amateeka abali mu kisaakate ky'obulimba.

Kyetaagisa omuvubuka okutegeera nti ensigo ezaala omubiri gw'omuntu, kye kintu ekikulu ennyo eri obulamu bw'omuntu (ekika), ya mukisa era n'olwekyo okugikozesa obubi kulyoka kwonoona ezzadde lye. Ku byoto bya Klezia Katolika, omukate oguyitibwa Host okuba ogw'omubiri gwa Kristo gukuumirwa mu kifo ekitukuvu nnyo, ekifananyi ekyo Ekitukuvu; kyakolebwa mu nsigo y'eŋŋaano. Ku kyoto ekiramu, kwe kugamba omubiri gwaffe ogw'omubiri, ensigo yaffe etwala ekifo ky'omukate omutukuvu ogw'Ekikristaayo ogugoberera Kristo Ow'omu byafaayo; mu nsigo yaffe mwe tukuuma Kristo mu butuufu ffe abagoberera Kristo omulamu abeera era akankana mu munda ddala y'ensigo yaffe.

Tulaba n'essira lingi nti abakugu mu by'obulimi abakola okumanya ebimera ebigasa omuntu, bayigiriza abalimi okussa ekitiibwa mu nsigo ze bafukirira mu nnimiro, tulaba nga batereezezza omutindo gw'ensigo okusobola okubala ebirime ebisinga obulungi, nga bakuuma emmere mu masilo amangi, ensigo ze baakoze n'obunyiikivu obungi zireme okubula. Tulaba abasawo b'ensolo, abakola ku bulamu bw'ensolo, bakoze abazaala oba abaggazi abasinga okuba abeeramu omulundi gwa mirundi kikumi okusinga ennyama, ekiraga nti y'ensigo gye bazaala, kye kiviiriddeko omutindo ogwo ogwa waggulu. Eddagala erya gavumenti lyokka, erikola ku kika ky'omuntu, teritubuulira kintu kyonna ku kutereeza ensigo; twennyamira nnyo olw'okulwawo kuno era tutegeeza abasomi baffe nti ensigo y'omuntu kyangu okugitereezaamu, okuyita mu kukozesa emmere esatu ey'omugaso ennyo: okuyita mu ekyo kye tulowooza, kye tufulumya n'omukka n'ekyo kye tulya. Bwe tulowooza ku bintu ebitaliimu mugaso, ku bintu ebitaliimu makulu, ensigo gye tuzaala ejja kuba bwetyo kubanga okulowooza kwe kusalawo ku nzaala eyo. Omuvubuka asoma alina enjawulo ku oyo atali mu muyigirize mu ndabika n'okubeerawo, waliwo enkyukakyuka mu bulamu; Okufuuyira omukka gwa bbiya ogiriidwa mu bbaala ne mu makantini, kuteekawo obulamu bw'abo abatera okugendayo: Abantu abaliira cake, embizzi, bbiya, eby'akaloosa, omwenge n'emmere ereeta ebirowoozo eby'obukaba, babeera obulamu obujeemu obubatwala mu bukaba.

Buli nsolo ezeeyisa obubi ennuuna: endogoyi, embizzi, embuzi era n'ennyonyi z'omu nnyumba newankubadde nnyonyi, nga nkoko ey'omu nnyumba. Kyangu okutegeera enjawulo eriwo wakati w'abo abeeyisa obubi n'abo omuntu be fuula abayonjo ku lw'okubakozesa, laba ensigo z'embalaasi ez'embiro ku z'embalaasi ezitikka emigugu, wakati w'ente ezirwanira n'entunuuzi ezifuluma mu mpapula z'amawulire buli lunaku, enjizzi oba embizzi entunuuzi, newankubadde mu nsolo entono nga embwa enkazi etera okubeera n'omukwano omungi era bulijjo endabika yayo ya mazima, ekintu kye kimu kiba ku musajja eyeeyisa obubi abikka obunafu bwe n'ebintu eby'akaloosa n'amafuta ag'akaloosa. Omuntu bw'afuuka omuyonjo, omutukuvu, era omutakatifu, mu birowoozo, ebigambo, n'ebikolwa, azzaawo obuto obwabula, yeeyisa obulungi mu mubiri ne mu mwoyo era omubiri gwe tegufulumya kawoowo kabi.

Obuyigirize obw'okuva mu lubuto butuukirizibwa butya? Kino kibaawo mu bafumbo abagoberera obuyonjo, kwe kugamba, abatayinza kulahwa nsigo yaabwe mu butagendereza n'essanyu eritali lya lubeerera, bwe batyo: Abaami n'abakyala baagala okuwa omubiri eri ekintu ekipya, bakkaanya era basaba eggulu libalagirire ku kya kuzzaawo emirembe, oluvannyuma mu mbeera ey'okwagala okw'olubeerera babeera awamu nga basanyufu era nga bajaguza, bakozesa ebiseera obutonde bwe businga okubala, nga abalimi bwe bakola okusiga, bakozesa enkola y'okukyusa ebintu nga bagattika nga bba n'omukyala, ekisobozesa okutaataagana kw'obuziba obw'omusajja obugumu era obw'amaanyi, obutereezeddwa okuyita mu nkola ezimanyiddwa olubereberye era okuyita mu ngeri eno ekintu eky'okuzaala ekigenda mu maaso, omukyala bw'amala okukitegeera nti ali lubuto, yeeyawula ku musajja, kwe kugamba, obulamu bw'obufumbo bukomye, kino omusajja omuyonjo asaanidde okukikola mu bwangu kubanga ajjudde ekisa n'amaanyi agasinga ag'omuntu, mu ngeri zonna amufuula omusanyufu omukyala we alemenga okudda mu kweraliikirira wadde ebintu ebifaanana kubanga byonna biba n'obulabe ku mwana ali mu lubuto, kino bwe kireeta obuzibu nga tebukuyamba ebitundu eby'ekyama bye bakola nga balina omukwano omungi abantu abatannafuna ku magezi mu nsonga eno? Ekiwa eby'okulwanyisa eri abaana abangi okuwulira omukwano omubi okuva mu buto era ne basonyiwanga bamaama baabwe mu ngeri ey'ekivve.

Maama amanyi nti awa obulamu eri ekintu ekipya kye yekuuma mu Yeekaalu ye Ennamu, ng'omuwendo ogw'omuwendo, nga awa n'okusaba kwe n'ebirowoozo bye ebimbalangu byonna ebiyinza okukulaakulanya ekitonde ekipya, oluvannyuma ekintu eky'okuzaala kijja nga tekirimu bulumi; mu ngeri ennyangu era eteri ya bulijjo ku kitiibwa kya bazadde be. Abafumbo bakwata endiisa ey'enjawulo etera okuba eya ennaku amakumi ana okutuusa embuto eyaweereza ng'omwana omuto ng'azziddwa mu kifo kye, omusajja amanyi nti omukyala azaala omwana alina okumwesiga era n'okumutunuulira, n'okumukwata mu ngeri ennungi kubanga obukaba bwonna obw'amaanyi bukwata ku mabeere g'omukyala era ne buleeta okuzibikira mu ngeri omusaayi omulungi oguwa obulamu bw'omwana avaamu, omukyala ayagala okussa mu nkola okuyigiriza kuno ajja okulaba nti ensonyi ziggwaawo okuba nti alina okulongoosebwa amabeere olw'okuzibikira okw'olubeerera. Mu kifo awalina obuyonjo, waliyo okwagala n'obuwulize, abaana bagolokoka mu ngeri eteri ya bulijjo era ebintu byonna ebikyamu biggwaawo, bwe bityo obuyigirize obukulu butandika okuteekateeka obuntu bw'ekintu ekipya ekyagala okugenda ku ssomero nga kirina obusobozi okugenda mu maaso n'obuyigirize obukisobozesa okubeera awamu era oluvannyuma okweyimirizaawo.

Mu myaka 7 egisooka, omwana akola obuntu bwe ng'amaze nga myaka egyo gigasa nga emyezi gy'embuto era ekyo ekyetegerezebwa ku muntu aleeteddwa mu mbeera ezo kintu abantu kye batateebereza. Obwakalimagezi kintu eky'Obuntu, tulina okumanya Obuntu.

Nze siyinza kumanya amazima kubanga amazima tegali mu kiseera era Nze ndi mu kiseera.

Okutya n'entiisa byonoona obusobozi obw'okutandikawo. Obusobozi obw'okutandikawo buzimba, okutya kwonoona.

Nga twekenneenya byonna era nga tufumiitiriza, tuzukusa okutegeera okwebase.

Amazima kye kitamanyiddwa okuva mu kaseera okudda mu kaseera, tegalina kakwate ku ekyo omuntu akkiriza oba takkiriza; amazima nsonga ya kugezesa, kutegeera, kwetaba.
