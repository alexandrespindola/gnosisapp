Gavumenti erina OBUTONZI, EGGWANGA lirina OBUTONZI. Poliisi, amateeka, omusirikale, abazadde, abasomesa, abakulembeze b'eddiini, n'abalala, balina OBUTONZI.

Waliwo ebika by'OBUTONZI bibiri. Ekisooka, OBUTONZI OBUTATEGEERA. Ekyokubiri, OBUTONZI OBWETEGEEREVU.

OBUTONZI OBUTATEGEERA oba OBUTATEGEEREVU tebugasa. Twetaaga mu bwangu OBUTONZI OBWETEGEEREVU.

OBUTONZI OBUTATEGEERA oba OBUTATEGEEREVU bujjuza ensi amaziga n'obulumi.

Mu maka n'essomero, OBUTONZI OBUTATEGEERA bukosa OBUTONZI olw'okuba OBUTATEGEEREVU oba OBUTATEGEEREVU.

Abazadde n'abasomesa abatategeera, leero, be bakulembeze abazibe abalala abazibe era nga ebyawandiikibwa ebitukuvu bwe bigamba, bonna bagenda kugwa mu bunnya.

Abazadde n'abasomesa abatategeera batukaka mu buto okukola ebintu ebisirusiru, naye bye balowooza nti byesigamye ku magezi. Bagamba nti ekyo kyekigendererwa kyakutuyamba.

Abazadde be BUTONZI OBUTATEGEERA nga bwe kiragibwa bwe bakola eri abaana baabwe ng'ebisasiro, nga bulijjo balowooza nti bali waggulu okusinga abantu abalala.

Abasomesa abasajja n'abakazi bavaamu okukyawa abayizi abamu oba abayizi abamu abawala, era ne basanyukira oba okukiriza abalala. Oluusi bakangavvula nnyo omuyizi yenna ayoleka obukyayi newankubadde nga oyo si mubi nnyo era ne basasula engereka ennungi nnyo eri abayizi abawala bangi oba abayizi abalamu abatazisaanidde ddala.

Abazadde n'abasomesa mu masomero bateekawo emitindo emibi eri abaana, abawala, abavubuka, abakyala abato, n'ebirala.

OBUTONZI obutalina KWETEGEERA busobola okukola ebintu bya busirusiru byokka.

Twetaaga OBUTONZI OBWETEGEEREVU. Tegeera OBWETEGEEREVU nga okumanya okujjuvu EKIKWATA KU MUNTU KU LULWE, okumanya okujjuvu kw'EBYOMUWENDO BYONNA EBIRIMU.

Oyo yekka alina okumanya ddala okujjuvu KU LULWE, azuukuka mu ngeri ejjuvu. Ekyo kye kibeera okwetegeera.

Buli muntu alowooza nti YE YEETEGEERA, naye kizibu nnyo okusanga mu bulamu omuntu yenna amanyi ddala ku lulwe. Abantu balina ku lwabwe okutegeera okubi ddala.

Okumanya ku lulwo kwetaaga ENKUBE EZ'AMAANYI era EZ'ENTIISA. Olw'okuyita mu KUMANYA KU LULWO OMUFUUKA DALA OKWETEGEERA.

OKUKOZESA OBUTONZI mu ngeri embi kuva ku BWETEGEEREVU. Tewaali BUTONZI BWETEGEEREVU yandituuse okukozesa OBUTONZI mu ngeri embi.

Abalala abafirosoofa balina obukyayi eri OBUTONZI bwonna, bakyawa OBUTONZI. Endowooza ng'eyo ya BULIMBA kubanga mu byonna ebyatondebwa, okuva ku butonde obutono okutuuka ku njuba, waliwo ebipimo n'ebipimo amadaala n'amadaala amaanyi agasinga agafuga era agakulembera n'amaanyi agasinga agafugibwa era agakulemberwa.

Mu kinnya ky'enjuki ekimpi waliwo obutonzi mu NNJUKI ENNAZAALISA. Mu nkolakola yonna ey'ebiwuka waliwo obutonzi n'amateeka. Okuzikiriza omusingi gw'OBUTONZI kyandiretedde OBUTAWULIRA.

OBUTONZI bw'ebiro bino ebizibu bye tulimu BWETEGEEREVU era kirambika nti olw'omulamwa guno ogwa PSYCHOLOGICAL, buboonyabonya, busiba, bukosa mu ngeri embi, buleeta obulumi.

Twetaaga ABASOMESA, abatuukiriza oba abakulembeze ab'omwoyo, ab'obutonzi bwa gavumenti, abazadde, n'ebirala, nga BEETEGEEREVU ddala. Bwe twesobola okukola DALA ENSI ENNUNGI.

Kirusiru okugamba nti tetaaga basomesa na bakulembeze ab'omwoyo.

Kirusiru okugaana omusingi gw'OBUTONZI mu byonna ebyatondebwa.

Abo BEESOBOLA, ABEEKAKASIZA, balowooza nti ABASOMESA n'ABAKULEMBEZE AB'OMWOYO, TEBEETAAGISA.

Tulina okukkiriza OKUZAALIBWA KWAFE n'OBUBI bwaffe. Tulina okutegeera nti twetaaga OBUTONZI, ABASOMESA, ABATUUKIRIZA AB'OMWOYO, n'ebirala. NAYE ABETEGEEREVU okusobola okutukulembera, okutuyamba n'okutulungamya mu magezi.

OBUTONZI OBUTATEGEERA obw'ABASOMESA buzikiriza amaanyi agaleetawo ag'abayizi. Singa omuyizi azannya, omusomesa atategeera amugamba ky'asaanidde okuzannya, omuti oba obusangiro bw'asaanidde okukoppa era omuyizi atidde tatya kuva ku mitindo gy'omusomesa egy'ekyempewo.

Ekyo si kuleetawo. Kyetaagisa omuyizi okufuuka omuleetawo. Okusobola okuva ku mitindo gy'omusomesa atategeera egy'ekyempewo, okusobola okutambuza ebyo byonna by'awulira okukwatagana n'omuti, obulamu bwonna obutambula okuyita mu matabi g'omuti agalukula, amakulu gaagwo aganzivu.

OMUSOMESA OMUTEGEEREVU teyandiwakanyizza okuva ku mutindo okuleetawo omwoyo.

ABASOMESA abalina OBUTONZI OBWETEGEEREVU, tebandisalidde ddala mirowoozo gy'abayizi.

Abasomesa ABATATEGEERA bazikiriza n'OBUTONZI bwabwe obw'OBUTONZI mirowoozo n'amagezi g'abayizi.

ABASOMESA abalina OBUTONZI OBUTATEGEEREVU, bamanyi okukangavvula n'okuteekawo emitindo emisirusiru eri abayizi okweyisa obulungi.

ABASOMESA ABETEGEEREVU bayigiriza n'obukkakkamu obungi eri abayizi baabwe, nga babayamba okutegeera obuzibu bwabwe bwonna, okusobola okutegeera okusobola okukiriza ensobi zaabwe zonna n'okugenda mu maaso n'obuwanguzi.

OBUTONZI OBWETEGEEREVU oba OBWETEGEEREVU tebusobola kuzikiriza BUGEZI.

OBUTONZI OBUTATEGEEREVU buzikiriza BUGEZI era buleeta obuzibu obunene eri abayizi.

Amagezi gatujjira bwe tuba tulina eddembe ddala era ABASOMESA ABALINA OBUTONZI OBWETEGEEREVU bamanyi okussa ekitiibwa mu DDEMBE ERILEETAWO.

Abasomesa ABATATEGEERA balowooza nti bamanyi byonna era ne banyaga eddembe ly'abayizi nga babasalidde amagezi n'emitindo gyabwe egitalina bulamu.

Abasomesa ABETEGEEREVU BAMANYI nti TEBAMANYI era badde ne beesomesa nga balabirira obusobozi obuleetawo obw'abayizi baabwe.

Kyetaagisa abayizi abali mu masomero, amatendekero n'amayunivaasite, okuyita mu mbeera ennyangu ey'ekyempewo abakozesebwa, okutuuka ku kifo ekyaka ekiri mu kubeera abantu abagezi era abeddembe okusobola okwolekera n'obuwanguzi obujjuvu obuzibu bwonna obw'obulamu.

Kino kyetaaga ABASOMESA ABETEGEEREVU, abatuukiriza abafuddeyo ddala eri abayizi baabwe, abasomesa abasasulwa obulungi obusobozi okuba nga tebalina nkyamu yonna ey'ebyensimbi.

Ekyennaku nti buli MUSOMESA, buli muzzadde, buli muyizi, yelowooza YEETEGEEREVU. AZUUKUSE era ekyo kye KISOBO ZIKULU.

Kizibu nnyo okusanga mu bulamu omuntu yenna YEETEGEEREVU era AZUUKUSE.

Abantu baloota omubiri bwe guba gugalamidde era ne baloota omubiri bwe guba mu mbeera ey'okuzuukuka.

Abantu bagoba emmotoka nga baloota; bakola nga baloota; batambula mu nguudo nga baloota, babeera buli ssaawa nga baloota.

Kyakubalamu nnyo nti puleesa afiirwa akasambattuko oba nti mu mmotoka alekawo ekitabo oba ensawo ye. Ekyo kyonna kibeerawo kubanga puleesa alina okutegeera okugalamidde, aloota...

Kizibu nnyo abantu okukkiriza nti bagalamidde, buli muntu yelowooza nti azuukuse. Singa omuntu yenna akkiriza nti alina okutegeera okugalamidde, kirambika nti okuva mu kaseera ako kennyini yanditandise okuzuukuka.

Omuyizi yefiirwa awaka ekitabo, oba eddalizo ly'asaanidde okutwala ku ssomero, okwerabira ng'okwo kulabika ng'okwa bulijjo era bwe kuli, naye kulaga, kulanga, embeera ey'okuloota omuntu gy'alimu.

Abasaabaze ku mutindo gwonna ogw'entambula y'omu kibuga, batera okuyita amakubo, baali bagalamidde era bwe bazuukuka bajja okutegeera nti bayise amakubo era nti kati bagenda kudda ennyuma amakubo agamu.

Si bbeeyi mu bulamu omuntu abeera azuukuse ddala era bw'abaddewo newankubadde nga kaseera katono, nga mu mbeera ez'entiisa etaggwaawo, yeeraba akaseera katono mu ngeri ENNUNGI. Obudde obwo tebwerabirwa.

Omusajja adda awaka oluvannyuma lw'okutambula mu kibuga kyonna, kizibu nnyo okujjukira mu ngeri entonotono ebirowoozo bye byonna, ebyabaddewo, abantu, ebintu, endowooza, n'ebirala n'ebirala n'ebirala. Bw'agezaako okujjukira, ajja kusanga mu kujjukira kw'ebintu ebyabulijjo ebikulu ebitaliimu ebyesigamye ku mbeera ez'okuloota ennyo.

Abayizi abamu ab'ebyendowooza bateekateeka okubeera ABALABE okuva mu kaseera akaakaseera, naye mangu ddala bagwa, oboolyawo bwe basanga omukwano mu luguudo, bwe bayingira edduuka okugula ekintu, n'ebirala era bwe waayitawo essaawa ezimu oluvannyuma bajjukira okusalawo kwabwe okubeera ABALABE era nga bazudde okuva mu kaseera akaakaseera, olwo bajja okutegeera nti baali bagalamidde bwe baali bayingidde mu kifo kino oba kiri, oba bwe baali basanze omuntu ono oba oli, n'ebirala n'ebirala n'ebirala.

Okubeera OMETEGEEREVU kintu kizibu nnyo naye osobola okutuuka mu mbeera eno ng'oyiga okubeera abalabi era abalabirizi okuva mu KASEERA AKA KASEERA.

Bwe tuba twagala okutuuka ku BWETEGEEREVU twetaaga okumanya ku lwaffe mu ngeri ENNUNGI.

Ffenna tulina NZE, YE KENNYINI, EGO bye twetaaga okunnyonnyola okumanya ku lwaffe era n'okufuuka ABETEGEEREVU.

KIRI MU BWANGU OKWETULABA, OKUKEKEREZA n'OKUTEGEERA buli kimu ku buzibu bwaffe.

Kyetaaga okwesoma ffe ku lwaffe mu kitundu ky'omulirogwe, enneewulira, emize, endooliito n'obukaba.

Omulirogwe gulina EMITENDERA mingi, ebitundu oba amadaala AGATATEGEERA ge tulina okumanya obulungi nga tuyita mu KULABIRIRA, OKUKEKEREZA, OKUFUMIITIRIZA MU NZIBA n'OKUTEGEERA OKWENNYINI ENNYO.

Obuzibu bwonna busobola okubula okuva mu kitundu ekitegeera era ne bugenda mu maaso okubeerawo mu mitendera emirala egitalinga omulirogwe.

Ekisooka ekyetaagisa KUZUUKUKA okutegeera OBUBI bwaffe, OKUZAALIBWA n'OBULUMI. Oluvannyuma NZE atandika OKUFA okuva mu kaseera aka kaseera. KIRI MU BWANGU OKUFA KW'OMUNTU PSYCHOLOGICAL.

Nga kufa kwokka kuzalibwa OMUBA ku lwaffe mu bwesimbu. YE YEKKA asobola okukozesa OBUTONZI OBWETEGEEREVU.

OKUZUUKUKA, OKUFA, OKUZAALIBWA. Bino bye bitundu ebisatu eby'okutegeera ebikutuusa ku BULAMU OBWETEGEEREVU OBW'AMAZIMA.

Olina okuzuukuka okufa era olina okufa okusobola okuzalibwa. Oyo afa nga TANAZUUKUKA afuuka OMUTUKUVU OMUJINGA. Oyo azaalibwa nga tanneefa afuuka OMUNTU AKOLA EBIBIRI, ekituufu ennyo n'ekibi ennyo.

Okukozesa OBUTONZI obw'amazima kusobola okukozesebwa abo abalina OMUBA omutegeevu.

Abo abatanalina OMUBA OMUTEGEEVU, abo abatanaba ABETEGEEREVU, batera OKUKOZESA OBUTONZI mu ngeri embi era ne baleeta obuzibu bungi.

ABASOMESA balina okuyiga okulagira era abayizi balina okuyiga okugondera.

ABASAAVU abalangirira okukontana okugondera mu butuufu balina obuzibu kubanga tewali ayinza okulagira mu kutegeera singa tasooka kuyiga kugondera.

Olina okumanya okulagira mu KUTEGEERA era olina okumanya okugondera mu kutegeera.
