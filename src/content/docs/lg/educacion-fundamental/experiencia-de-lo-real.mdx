Ku luggi olw'ekiggwa kya Delfus, waaliwo ekiwandiiko eky'edda ekyali kitooloddwa mu jjinja nga kigamba: "TE SE IPSUM". Yiga okumanya obwomu bwamwe era otegeera obutonde bwonna n'abakatonda.

Essomo ly'okufumiitiriza erigenda waggulu lirina omusingi omukulu okusinziira ku bigambo eby'omulembe ogwo eby'aba HIEROFANTE Abayonaani.

Bwe tuba nga twagala okussaawo omusingi ogutuufu ogw'okufumiitiriza mu ngeri ey'amazima era ng'ekolokolo, kyetaaga okutegeera obwomu bwaffe ku mitendera gyonna egy'obwongo.

Okussaawo omusingi ogutuufu ogw'okufumiitiriza kye kintu kimu, okubeera nga tuli baavu ku bugagga, obuntu bwaffe, okutya, obukyayi, omululu gw'amaanyi ag'ekyama, okwagala ebirivaamu, n'ebirala, n'ebirala, n'ebirala.

Kyeyoleka eri bonna era nga tekyetaaga kubuusabuusa nti oluvannyuma lw'okussaawo OMUSINGI OMUJJUJUVU ogw'okufumiitiriza, obwongo buteerera era ne bubeera mu kimalaalo kinene.

Okusinziira ku ndowooza entuufu, kiba kya busiru okwagala okukyekola ng'otamanyi bwomu bwo.

Kyetaaga okutegeera mu ngeri ENTEVU era mu bitundu byonna eby'obwongo, buli kizibu nga bwe kijja mu bwongo, buli kwegomba, buli kujjukira, buli kamogo akakwata ku bwongo, n'ebirala.

Kyeyoleka eri bonna nti mu kiseera ky'okufumiitiriza, ebikamogo byonna eby'obwongo ebitukwatako, essanyu lyaffe lyonna n'ennaku, okujjukira okutabalika, okukubiriza okungi okuva mu nsi ey'ebweru, okuva mu nsi ey'omunda, okwegomba okwa buli ngeri, okwegomba okwa buli ngeri, obukyayi obukadde, obukyayi, n'ebirala, byolekera ku katandaazi k'obwongo mu lukalala olw'entiisa.

Omuntu oyo yenna ayagala okussaawo mu bwongo bwe omusingi omujjujuvu ogw'okufumiitiriza, ateekwa okussaayo omwoyo gwe gwonna ku bintu ebyo ebirungi n'ebibi eby'okutegeera kwaffe era n'abitegeera mu ngeri entevu si ku mutendera gwokka ogw'obwongo, naye era ne mu bitundu byonna eby'obwongo obutategeerekeka, obutategeerekeka wansi n'obutategeerekeka mu bwongo. Tetuteekwa kwerabira nti obwongo bulina emitendera mingi.

Okusoma ennyo ebintu byonna ebyo kitegeeza okumanya obwomu bwo.

Efirimu yonna ku katandaazi k'obwongo erina olubereberye n'enkomerero. Olukalala lw'ebintu, okwegomba, okwegomba, okwagala okuba waggulu, okujjukira, n'ebirala bwe lukoma, obwongo buteerera era ne bubeera mu kimalaalo kinene NGA TEBULINA birowoozo byonna.

Abayizi ab'omulembe guno abasoma ku bwongo beetaaga okukyekolera BWAZI obw'OKUTANGAZA. Okuyingiza BWAZI mu bwongo bwaffe kutusobozesa okukyekolera, okuwulira, okubeerawo n'ekintu ekyusa, EKINTU ekyo kye KYA MAZIMA.

Wawula obwongo obuteerevu n'obwongo obuteerevu n'amaanyi.

Wawula obwongo obuli mu kimalaalo n'obwongo obusirisiddwa ku bukkakkamu.

Ku mulamwa gwonna ogw'okulambulula, tuteekwa okutegeera nti obwongo bwe buba nga busirisiddwa ku bukkakkamu, mu nsi ey'omunda ne ku mitendera emirala tebubeera buteerera era bulwana okweggya ku buwambe.

Okusinziira ku ndowooza ey'okulambulula, tuteekwa okutegeera nti obwongo bwe buba nga busirisiddwa ku bukkakkamu, mu nsi ey'omunda tebubeera mu kimalaalo, bukkaatiriza era ne bwesamba nnyo.

Obuteerevu obw'amazima n'obukkakkamu obw'obutonde n'obusengevu obw'obwongo, butujjira ng'ekisa, ng'essanyu, oluvannyuma lw'efirimu ey'omunda ennyo ey'obulamu bwaffe ku katandaazi ak'ekitalaano eky'obwongo.

Lumu obwongo bwe buba nga buteerera mu butonde n'obusengevu, lumu obwongo bwe busangibwa mu kimalaalo ekiwoomerevu, okuyingiza BWAZI obw'OKUTANGAZA kujja.

BWAZI tebwangu okubunnyonnyola. Tebulambululika oba okubunnyonnyola, endowooza yonna gye tuwaayo ku bwo esobola okulemwa mu kintu ekikulu.

BWAZI tebusobola kubunnyonnyolwa oba okwolesebwa mu bigambo. Kino kiva ku kuba nti olulimi lw'abantu lukolebwa nnyo okuggyayo ebintu, ebirowoozo n'enneewulira eziriwo; terutuukira ddala okwolesa mu ngeri entuufu era ennamu, ebintu eby'omugaso, ebintu n'enneewulira EZITAAKAWO.

Okugezaako okukubaganya ebirowoozo ku BWAZI mu nsalo z'olulimi olukomereddwa ebintu eby'obulamu, mu butuufu tekyetaaga kubuusabuusa, kiba kya busiru era nga kikyamu ddala.

"BWAZI bwe BUTALIWO, era OBULAMU SI BWAZI".

"EKINTU TEKIJAWULWA KU BWAZI, era BWAZI TEBUJAWULWA KU KINTU".

"EKINTU BWAZI era BWAZI KINTU, KIVA KU BWAZI EBINTU OKUBAAWO".

"BWAZI N'OBULAMU BYEJJUKIRA ERA TEBIIKAANYIZZA". BWAZI N'OBULAMU BYEGATTIRA ERA NE BYEGUMIRA.

"EBINTU EBILINA ENNEEWULIRA ENNENE BWE BIRABA EKINTU, BIRABA ENGERI YAABYO EY'OBULAMU YOKKA, TEBIRABA NGERI YAABYO EYA BWAZI".

"EKIBALO KYONNA EKITANGAZZA kiyinza okulaba mu kiseera kye kimu engeri yonna ey'obulamu n'eya BWAZI.

"BWAZI kigambo kyokka ekiraga obutonde OBUTALIWO era OBUTALI BUTONZI bw'ebintu, era akabonero akalaga embeera ey'okwewaayo n'eddembe eritaggwaawo".

Abasomesa n'abakungu b'amasomero, amatendekero n'yunivasite bateekwa okusoma ennyo ku by'obwongo bwaffe obukyusa era oluvannyuma ne bayigiriza abayizi baabwe ekkubo eribatwala okukyekolera EKYA MAZIMA.

Kisoboka okutuuka ku KYO EBIRABA EBY'EKYA MAZIMA ng'ebirowoozo bikomekkerezeddwa.

Okuyingiza BWAZI kutusobozesa okukyekolera OMUSANA OGULAMU OGWA MAZIMA AGATONGE.

OKUMANYA okwo okuliwo mu butuufu BWAZI, obutalina buntu era obutalina langi, OBWAZI OBUTALINA BUTONDE, bwe MAZIMA AGATONGE, EKISA EKIRIWO MUNSI YONNA.

AMAGEZI GO ge ngeri yaago ey'amazima BWAZI etasaanidde kutunuulirwa nga BWAZI wa BITALIIWO naye ng'AMAGEZI GENNYINI agatakyazizza, agalabala, agaliwo munsi yonna era agasanyusa ge KUKYA, BUDDHA ow'Amagezi Agaliwo Munsi Yonna.

KUKYA KO KWENNYINI OKWA BWAZI n'AMAGEZI agalabala era agasanyusa tebyawukanira ddala. OKUGATTA kwabyo kwe DHARMA-KAYA; EMBERA EY'OKUTANGAZA OKUTUUFU.

KUKYA KO KWENNYINI OKULABALA, OKWA BWAZI era nga tekujawukana na MUBIRI GWA BULIYO obunene, tekuzaalibwa wadde okufa era gwe musana ogutakyuka AMITARA BUDDHA.

Okumanya kuno kumala. Okutegeera BWAZI obw'AMAGEZI GO nga EMBERA ya BUDDHA era n'okukitwala nga KUKYA KO KWENNYINI, kwe kweyongera mu MWOYO OMUTUKUVU owa BUDDHA.

Kuumira OBWENZI bwo nga tebulemuuse mu KUFUNIITIRIZA, weerabire nti oli mu Kufumiitiriza, tolowoozanga nti oli kufumiitiriza kubanga bwe kiba nga kirowoozebwa nti oli kufumiitiriza, ekirowoozo kino kimala okutataaganya okufumiitiriza. Obwongo BWO buteerere okukyekolera EKYA MAZIMA.
