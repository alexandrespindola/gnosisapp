## 21 OMUWEZI GWA KUSATU OKUTANDIKA OKUTUUKA NGA 20 OMUWEZI GWA KUNA

Omuntu alina embeera nnya ez’OBWENZI: OKULOOTA, OKUTUNULA, OKWEYOGERA, N’OKUMANYA EBINTU NGA BWE BIRI.

Lowooza akatambi akatono, omusomi omwagalwa, ennyumba erina emikutu ena. ENSOLO ENTEGEERERA, ekimanyiddwa ng’OMUNTU, etera okubeera mu mikutu ebiri egisooka, naye tekozesa mikutu ebiri egya waggulu.

ENSOLO ENTEGEERERA egabanya obulamu bwayo obulumi n’ennaku wakati w’okuloota okwa bulijjo n’embeera enkyamu ey’OKUTUNULA, ekintu eky’ennaku ennyingi ekiri ekika ekirala eky’okuloota.

Omubiri ogw’omubiri nga gwebase ku kitanda, EGO enziguliddwa mu MIBIRI GYAAGO EMISANA etambula n’obwenge obwebase ng’omuntu atambula nga yeebase ng’atambula mu kitundu kya molekyuuli. EGO mu kitundu kya molekyuuli efulumya ENZIIRO era ne gubeera mu zo, tewali nkola yonna mu NZIIRO zaago, obutakoma, ensonga, ebibaawo, obukola bwa PSÍQUICAS bwonna bukola nga tebulina bulagirizi era ebifananyi eby’omunda, ebifo ebitategeerekeka, ebitali bya mazima, n’ebirala birabika era ne bibula.

EGO ng’ezingiddwa mu MIBIRI GYAAGO EMISANA nga edda eri OMUBIRI OGWA MUBIRI, olwo embeera ey’obwenge eyookubiri ejja, eyitibwa embeera ey’OKUTUNULA, mu butuufu teri kintu kirala wabula ekika ekirala eky’okuloota.

EGO bw’edda eri OMUBIRI GWAAYO OGWA MUBIRI, enziiro zeeyongera mu munda, ekiyitibwa embeera ey’OKUTUNULA ddala kwe KULOOTA NGA OTUNULA.

Enjuba bw’evaayo, emmunyeenye zikweka, naye tezirina kulekeraawo kubaawo; bwe zityo enziiro bwe ziri mu mbeera ey’okutunula, zeeyongera mu kyama, tezirina kulekeraawo kubaawo.

Kino kitegeeza nti ENSOLO ENTEGEERERA ekyamu okuyitibwa OMUNTU, ebeera mu nsi y’enziiro zokka; omubbi omulungi yagamba nti obulamu bwe burooto.

ENSOLO ERINA ENDOWOOZA EVUGA mmotoka ng’eloosa, ekola mu kkolero, mu woofiisi, mu nnimiro, n’ebirala, ng’eloosa, egwa mu laavu mu nziro, ewasa mu nziro; emirundi mitono nnyo mu bulamu, eba ng’ezuukuse, ebeera mu nsi y’enziiro era ekkiriza nnyo nti ezuukuse.

Enjiri Ennya ziragira OKUZUUKUKA, naye ky’ennaku tezigamba ngeri ya KUZUUKUKA.

Okusooka, kyetaagisa okutegeera nti obeera owebase; oluvannyuma lw’omuntu okumanya ddala nti yeebase, olwo ly’ayingira mu kkubo ly’OKUZUUKUKA.

Oyo atuuka OKUZUUKUSA OBWENGE, olwo afuuka EYEYOGERA, afuna OBWENGE OKUMANYA MWENNYE.

Ensobi esinga obubi ey’ABAKOOZI ABAFUNA ABAFAAKAZI n’ABAKOOZI ABAKWEKEREVU abatategeera, kwe kwekuumira nga BEEWEEYOGERA era n’okukkiriza nti buli omu azuukuse, nti abantu bonna balina OBWENGE OKWEYOGERA.

Singa abantu bonna balina OBWENGE OBWAZUUKE ensi yandibadde lusuku lwa Katonda, tewandibadde ntalo, tewandibadde kyaange wadde ekyiyo, buli kimu kyandibadde kya buli omu, twandibadde tubeera mu MYAKA EGYA ZAABU.

Omuntu bw’AZUUKUSA OBWENGE, bw’afuuka EYEYOGERA, bw’afuna OBWENGE OKUMANYA MWENNYE, olwo lw’ajja okumanya AMAZIMA ku mwnenye.

Nga tonnatuuka ku mbeera eyookusatu ey’OBWENGE (OKWEYOGERA), ddala tomanyi MWENNYE, newankubadde ng’olowooza nti omanyi mwonoona.

Kyetaagisa okufuna embeera eyookusatu ey’obwenge, okulinnya ku mutindo ogw’okusatu ogw’ennyumba, nga tonnafuna ddembe okuyita ku mutindo ogw’okuna.

EMBEERA EY’OKUNA EY’OBWENGE, OMUTINDO OGWA KUNA ogw’ennyumba, ddala GW’AMAANYI. Oyo yekka atuuka ku OBWENGE OBW’OKUMANYA EBINTU NGA BWE BIRI, embeera ey’okuna, ayinza okusoma ebintu mu byabwe byokka, ensi nga bw’eri.

Oyo atuuka ku mutindo ogw’okuna ogw’ennyumba, tewali kubuusabuusa MWAKUFUUTWA, amanyi okuyita mu bumanyirivu obutereevu AMAKWATE GE BULAMU N’OKUFA, alina AMAGEZI, obwongo bwe bugazi bwonna bukulaakulanye.

Mu KULOOTA OKW’ENNYO tusobola okuba n’obubonero bw’EMBEERA EY’OKUTUNULA. Mu MBEERA EY’OKUTUNULA tusobola okuba n’obubonero bw’OKWEYOGERA. Mu MBEERA EY’OKWEYOGERA tusobola okuba n’obubonero bw’OBWENGE OBW’OKUMANYA EBINTU NGA BWE BIRI.

Bwe twagala okutuuka ku KUZUUKUKA KWA OBWENGE, ku OKWEYOGERA, tuteekwa okukola n’OBWENGE wano era kati. Mu butuufu wano mu nsi eno ey’omubiri gye tulina okukolera okusobola OKUZUUKUSA OBWENGE, oyo azuukuka wano azuukuka buli wamu, mu nsi zonna ez’obutonde bwonna.

EKIBUMBE KY’OMUNTU kye ZODÍACO ERIMU OMULAMU era mu buli mmunyeenye ekumi na bbiri, obwenge bwebase nnyo.

Kyanguwa okuzuukusa obwenge mu buli kitundu ekimu ku ebyo ekumi na bibiri eby’omubiri gw’omuntu era olw’ekyo ebigezo bya zodiacal we biri.

Aries, afuga omutwe; Taurus, obulago; Géminis, emikono, amagulu n’amawuggwe; Cáncer, akatimba ka thymus; Leo omutima; Virgo, olubuto, ebyenda; Libra, ensigo; Escorpio, ebitundu by’ekisenge; Sagitario, emisuwa gy’amagulu; Capricornio, amakokola; Acuario, ebyenyi; Piscis, ebigere.

Ky’ekyennyamivu ddala nti omuntu ono omulamu owa MICRO-CÓSMOS, yeebase nnyo. Kifuuka ekyetaagisa okutuuka ku KUZUUKUKA KWA OBWENGE mu buli kabonero ka zodiacal ekumi na bbiri ak’obuntu okuyita mu maanyi ag’AMAANYI.

Ekitangaala n’Obwenge bye bintu bibiri eby’ekintu kye kimu; ku mutindo ogw’Obwenge ogwa wansi, omutindo gw’ekitangaala gwa wansi; ku mutindo ogwa waggulu ogw’Obwenge, omutindo gw’ekitangaala gwa waggulu.

Twetaaga OKUZUUKUSA OBWENGE okusobola okukanga n’okutangaaza buli kitundu ekimu ku zodiacal yaffe MICRO-CÓSMICA ekumi na bibiri. Zodiac yaffe yonna erina okufuuka ekitangaala n’ekitiibwa.

Omulimu ne Zodiac yaffe gwatandika n’ekintu kyonna ekiyitibwa ARIES. Omuyigirizwa atuula mu ntebe ennyumirwa ng’obwongo bwe buteerera era nga busirise, nga tebulina ndowooza yonna. Omukkiriza aggala amaaso ge nga tewali kintu kyonna mu nsi kimusobola okumusumbuwa, alowooza nti ekitangaala ekisukkulumu ekya ARIES kijjula obwongo bwe, abeera mu mbeera eyo ey’okufumiitiriza ekiseera kyonna ky’ayagala olwo ayimba Mantram AUM ow’amaanyi ng’aggula akamwa ke n’ennukuta A, ng’agireetako ennukuta U era ng’aggala n’ennukuta M entukuvu.

Mantram AUM ow’amaanyi mu bwamwamanyi butonde BWA KATONDA OBUTYA, kubanga asika amaanyi ga kitaffe, ayagalibwa ennyo, Omwana asinzibwa nnyo n’Omwoyo Omutukuvu omumanyi ennyo. Ennukuta A esika amaanyi ga kitaffe, ennukuta U esika amaanyi ga Mwana, ennukuta M esika amaanyi g’Omwoyo Omutukuvu. AUM Mantram LOGICO ow’amaanyi.

Omukkiriza alina okuyimba Mantram ono ow’amaanyi emirundi ena mu kiseera kino ky’okukola ARIES oluvannyuma ayimirira ng’atunuulidde ebuvanjuba ng’agolola omukono gwe ogwa ddyo okudda mu maaso ng’anyeenya omutwe gwe emirundi musanvu okudda mu maaso, musanvu okudda ennyuma, musanvu ng’atambula ku ludda olwa ddyo, musanvu ng’atambula ku ludda olwa kkono ng’ekigendererwa nti ekitangaala kya ARIES kikola munda mu bwongo nga kizuukusa obutimba bwa pineal ne pituitaria ebitusobozesa okutegeera ENSI EZISUKKULUMA EZ’ENNUNGI.

Kyanguwa nti EKITANGAALA KYA ARIES kikulakulana munda mu bwongo bwaffe nga KIZUUKUSA OBWENGE, nga kikulakulanya amaanyi amakyamu agalirwa mu BITIMBA BYA PITUITARIA ne PINEAL.

ARIES akabonero ka RA, RAMA, omwana gw’endiga. Mantram RA ow’amaanyi, ng’ayimbibwa nga bwe kirina okuyimbibwa, akankaanya emiro gya spinal n’ebifo omusanvu eby’emagneti mu mugongo.

ARIES akabonero ka zodiacal ak’omuliro, alina amaanyi ag’ekitalo era OMUNTU MICRO-CÓSMOS agafuna okusinziira ku ngeri gy’alowoozaamu, gye yawuliraamu era gye yakolaamu.

HITLER, eyazaalibwa ARIES, yakozesa ekika kino ky’amaanyi mu ngeri ey’okuzikiriza, newankubadde, tuteekwa okukkiriza nti mu ntandikwa, nga tannakola busirusiru bwa kusuula abantu mu lutalo olw’okubiri olw’ensi yonna, yakozesa amaanyi ga ARIES mu ngeri ey’okuzimba, ng’ayimusa omutindo gw’obulamu bw’ABANTU BA GERMANY.

Tusobodde okukakasa okuyita mu bumanyirivu obutereevu nti abo abazaalibwa ARIES balwana nnyo ne munnaabwe oba munnaabwe.

Abo abazaalibwa ARIES balina enneyisa ennyingi ey’okulwana nga balwana nnyo mu butonde.

Abo abazaalibwa ARIES bawulira nga basobola okutandika emirimu eminene era n’okugituusa ku nkomerero ennungi.

Waliwo ekibi ekibi mu abo abazaalibwa ARIES okwagala okukozesa bulijjo amaanyi ag’okwagala mu ngeri ey’obugwagwa, sitayiro ya HITLER, ANTISOCIAL n’eyokuzikiriza.

Abo abazaalibwa ARIES basanyukira obulamu obw’obuntu, naye Arijiano bangi basingawo obusirikale era mu kino temuli bwannannyini.

Mu mpisa za Arijiano obumalirivu, okwesiga, okwegomba n’omuwendo omusirusiru ddala bisinga okweyoleka.

Ekyuma kya ARIES kyekyo, ejjinja, RUBÍ, langi, OMUMYUFUUFU, ekintu, OMULIRO.

Ku abo abazaalibwa ARIES kibanikaobufumbo n’abantu abava mu LIBRA, kubanga omuliro n’omukka bitegeeragana bulungi nnyo.

Singa abo abazaalibwa ARIES baagala okusanyuka mu bufumbo, balina okukomya ekibi ky’obusungu.
