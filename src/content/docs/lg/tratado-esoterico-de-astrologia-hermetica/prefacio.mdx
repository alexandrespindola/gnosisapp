## ENNYANJULA

### OMUWANDIISI GARGYA KUICHINES

Waliwo obulaguzi bw'enziggo oba obubale obwetaaga okusoma ennyo okuyiga, ng'obwo abaluguzi b'edda bonna bakozesezza okulagula ebintu eby'omugaso. Era abantu abakulu bakozesezza okumanya okwo, nga Hitler mu Bugirimaani eyakozesa abaluguzi abakugu okumukulembera mu ntalo ze.

Ffe aba Gnostic twawukana n'engeri eyo ey'okusoma, kubanga omuntu abeera kizannyisanyo okusinziira ku bulaguzi, omuntu ataliiko ky'ayinza kukola ku bubonero obulaga ebifo ebyenjawulo n'okuyita kw'emmunyeenye, tumanyi obulaguzi obutuyigiriza okukozesa emmunyeenye era tusobola okwewala ebintu ebyandilaguddwa abakugu mu Bulaguzi bw'Enziggo. Okukola kino kwetaaga okukyusa emibiri gy'omwezi gye twazaalibwa nabyo ne tubeera n'emibiri gy'omusana oba ekitangaala nga tukozesa omizi gwennyini ogw'obuntu bwaffe oba ensigo yaffe ffennyini.

Omulabe atulemesa okulinnya okumanya okw'obwakatonda ye Sitaani waffe oba omukulu w'eggye afuga omubiri gwaffe ogw'omubiri. Engeri esingayo obulungi okuggyawo Omukuumi w'Omulyango nga bwe bamuyita aba kkono kwe kukola omukolo gw'Okutandika eri ffe aba Gnostic gutandika n'okuyingira mu masabo oba Lumisiales, nga tukozesa enjigiriza ezikulemberwa era ezikulizibwa Avatar ow'omu kiseera kya Aquarius "Samael Aun Weor".

Mu kiseera Omutandisi Yesu we yawa enjigiriza ze eri abaagalwa b'ekkubo yagamba: "Nze ndi kkubo, nze ndi mazima, nze ndi bulamu, waaliwo mu biro ebyo omusawo omukulu ayitibwa Simooni Omusawo, eyali ajjudde amaanyi n'obugagga bungi, eyalabisa abagoberezi be: "Singa Yesu yatuuka ku kitaawe ku bw'obulungi bwe, nange Simooni, nange ndituuka ku kitange ku bw'obulungi bwange era kye yakola kwe kugenda mu kkubo ery'obubadi, nga yeewala omwagalwa we, akabi kano kabeerawo buli kiseera abantu abaagalwa b'ekkubo bwe balowooza nti basobola okuyita mu kkubo bokka.

Abo abagenda okufuna amagezi gano mu mikono gyabwe omulundi ogusooka, tubategeeza nti twetaaga emyezi mukaaga egy'okusoma okw'entandikwa, nga tusoma ebitabo bya Samael Aun Weor era nga tukozesa okumanya kwabwe, oluvannyuma singa awa obukakafu nti ayagala okutuukirira ddala, era ayagala obulamu obusingako, olwo, ayingizibwa mu Lumisiales okuyita mu nkola ey'okutendekebwa okw'enjawulo.

Omuntu omwagalwa w'ekkubo bw'aba ategekeddwa okukola ekigezo, ekigezo ekisooka ky'alina okuyitamu kwe kubeera Omukuumi w'Omulyango oba okulwanagana ne Sitaani we yennyini abadde omukulembeze waffe era omusomesa waffe okumala emyaka mingi ddala. Nzijukira mu mwaka gwa 1949, omuyigirizwa wa Hierophant ow'ebyama ebitono Aun Weor, era oluvannyuma lw'okumala emyezi ena ng'ayimirizza ddala ebikolwa by'obukaba, yagezesebwa Omukuumi olunaku lumu nga 27 Ogwomusanvu. Omutandisi oyo yali mu kifo kyokka, yali munda, naye nga tamanyi, bwe yakowoola Omukuumi w'Omulyango, teyamulinda, omutandisi oyo yagamba nti yasooka okuwulira obunnyogovu obw'olumbe, obudde obwali obweru bwali bujjula ekizikiza, nga bwe kuli n'obunnyogovu obwo n'akaloosa akabi ekyamuteesaaza. Yawulira ng'ayagala okudduka, naye amaanyi ga Kristo ge yali akumizza dda mu mubiri gwe okuyita mu nkyukakyuka ey'ekika ky'obusawo, Arcano A. Z. F., yamuwa obuvumu okusigala mu kifo ekyo ekitayagalika. Amangu ago yalaba ensolo ng'ejja gyali ng'efaanana enkima, ejjudde ebyoya, ng'erina amahembe ku kyenyi kyaayo agaaka era nga gasinda bwe gatambula, ng'erina ennyindo n'akamwa ng'endogoyi, era yamugamba: Kati oyagala kundeka? Onsasula otya olw'ebintu bingi bye nkukoledde? Onkyusaayo omusajja gw'otomanyi? Naye yamuddamu ng'ajjudde okutya nti yamuleka, ensolo yamubuukira ng'agala okulwanyisa, omutandisi yamulayira, naye teyamugasa ng'omuntu omunafu atidde, naye yajjukira nti yali Chela wa Kristo era yamulayira mu linnya lya Kristo era n'adda ennyuma katono, buli lwe yamubuukiranga, yasaba obukuumi eri Kristo n'Abasomesa be abaagalwa era n'amugamba: tolina buyinza ku nze, nnaakusinga era ensolo yavaayo ng'etiisatiira eby'ekika kyonna. Okutabukwa kw'omuyigirizwa oyo kwali kungi, olw'okuba Hierophant yali agenze mu kibuga ekirala era ekyo kyali kyetaaga ennaku ssatu obutabaawo, naye bwe yakomawo yamubuuza n'amuddamu: Mbakuyozaayoza, ova mu kigezo ekisooka n'Omukuumi w'Omulyango, omuntu ono ye Sitaani wo, gw'obadde oweereza era ng'oliisa okumala emyaka mingi, era amuliisa atya?, Omuyizi atidde yabuuza era Omukulembeze yamuddamu, aliisa eby'obugwagwa bwo, alyiisa okwegomba kwaffe okubi, ebikolwa eby'obugwagwa, obwenzi, amalaaya n'obulamu obujama, ebyo byonna bye bitundu by'omu ssabo Kristo bye yatwogerako, batunda ssabo lyaffe, kati olina okukozesa enkoni ey'okwagala okuggya ebintu ebyo byonna ebyakufuula omuddu wa Sitaani, kati olina okubiwangula buli kimu ku byo singa ddala oyagala okweggyako obubi n'okutambula mu kkubo eggonvu.

Okuyita mu mulimu guno tusobola okukyalira abagenyi bonna ab'emmunyeenye, okuyingira mu ssabo ly'omutima gw'emmunyeenye, nga tusaba era nga tukolera wamu ne bamalayika ab'emmunyeenye, kireme okuba nti tubeera bizannyisanyo by'embeera, naye okusooka tulina okugoba abatunda mu ssabo lyaffe, tulina okuyiga okukozesa mu kyoto kyaffe. Olw'ekyo, omuntu ayagala okuyita mu mikolo gy'omu Lumisiales; eyo ayiga okwagala n'okuweereza Katonda okusinga ebintu byonna n'omuntu munne, amanyira emikolo egyo era oluvannyuma ategeera nti amawulire gonna agali mu mukolo ogw'okusinza galina akakwate akanywevu ku kyoto ekiramu era azuula ebyewuunyo ebitali bya kutegeeza. JACHIN ne BOAZ eby'oku byoto byetaagisa okukozesa kyoto kyayo era omuntu atuuka n'okuyiga okutambula emitendera omusanvu egyetaagisa okukolera mu kyoto kya Katonda omulamu mu bwetegefu n'omuwala omulungi.

N'enjigiriza ey'okuzuukira tuyiga okutta embuzi gye tulina munda era bwe tutyo tujja okukola ekisibo ky'Omwana gw'Endiga ey'Okuyitako mu kiseera. Bwe tutyo tuva ku Mukama w'Ebiro okubeera n'obulamu obutaggwaawo obujjudde essanyu ery'olubeerera.
