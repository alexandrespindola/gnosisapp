Eguno lwe ntandikwa y'okusoma ku Nzirukanya z'Obufirosoofa bwa Gnostic oba diguli ez'ebweru eza gnosis. Kyabulijjo abayizi bonna okutandikira ku diguli eno era oluvannyuma ne bagenda mu maaso, okutondeka, n'eddiguli esooka, eyokubiri, eyokusatu, n'ebirala. Kiteekwa okutwalibwa mu mutima nti zino si ddiguli za gnosis ez'ekyama. Eddiguli za gnosis ez'ekyama omuwandiisi azifuna bwaba nga mwetegefu okuzifuna. Eddiguli ez'ekyama, ze ddiguli za gnosis ez'amazima, tezisobola kubunyisibwa omuntu yenna azifunye; kino kigaaniddwa. Ani yenna agamba: "nnina ddiguli nnyingi, okutongozebwa kungi", oyo mufu bw'empisa.

Omuntu bw'aba ayagala okuba yinginiya, looya oba omusawo, n'ebirala, ateekwa okweteekateeka okukola ekyo. Omuntu oyo agenda ku ssomero era n'asoma nnyo. Oluvannyuma lw'okubeera n'amagezi amalungi ag'omutwe ku ttabi ly'asoma, olwo anatandika okukozesa by'ayize. Okukozesa kuleeta obutuukirivu. Abantu abakugu abanene, abakugu ab'ebyemikono abanene, abasayansi abanene, n'ebirala, tebatuuse ku kubeera n'amagezi amalungi ag'omutwe ku matabi gaabwe gonna ag'okusoma, wabula bateeka amagezi gano mu nkola. Eteoriya yokka tesobola kuleeta kintu kyonna (okuggyako okusanyuka mu bwenge ku ludda lw'abo abagitegeera). Weekaagala okukozesa.

Soma kursa eno ey'ebisomo era ogisome n'akagezi ak'amazima, n'okwagala okuyiga, n'okwagala okumanya amagezi agasingawo. Naye jjukira nti ggwe olina okukozesa by'oyize, bw'oba oyagala okutuuka ku butuukirivu bw'Omulimu.
