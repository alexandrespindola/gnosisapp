Ensolo eyonsa ey’amagezi eriyitibwa omuntu mu nsobi, teyirina bulamu bwabwo obw’enkalakkalira.

Tewali kubuusabuusa nti obutabaawo bumu bw’endowooza mu muntu, bwe buleetera ebizibu bingi n’ennaku.

Omubiri ogw’omubiri gwe gumu era gukola ng’ekitundu ekijjuvu, okujjako nga mulwadde.

Naye obulamu obw’omunda obw’omuntu tebwonna bumu mu ndowooza.

Ekisinga obubi mu bino byonna, wadde nga amasomero ag’ekika kya Seudo-Esoteric ne Seudo-Occultist gagamba, kwe kubulawo kw’enteekateeka y’endowooza mu munda mu buli muntu.

Mazima ddala mu mbeera ng’ezo, tewali mulimu gwa nkalakkalira ng’ogumu, mu bulamu obw’omunda obw’abantu.

Omuntu, ku lw’embeera ye ey’omunda, bwe bungi bw’endowooza, omuwendo gwa "Nze".

Abataamanya abamasamasa ab’omu mirembe gino egy’ekizikiza, basinza "NZE", bamugulumiza, bamussa ku byoto, bamuyita "ALTER EGO", "NZE OWA WAGGULU", "NZE ALABIKA", n’ebirala n’ebirala n’ebirala.

Tebayagala kutegeera "Abagezi" ab’emyaka gino emibi mwe tuli, nti "Nze owa waggulu" oba "Nze owa wansi", bitundu bibiri eby’Ego yemu eyafuuka obungi...

Omuntu talina mazima "Nze ow’olubeerera" wabula eggulu lyonna erya "Nze" ezitali za buntu era ezitaliiko makulu.

Ensolo embi etategedde bulungi eriyitibwa omuntu mu nsobi, efanana ennyumba eteri mu ntegeka omuli abaddu bangi mu kifo ky’omwami omu, abagala okufuga n’okukola kyonna kye baagala...

Ensobi esinga obunene eya Seudo-Esotericism ne Seudo-Occultism ennafu, kwe kulowooza nti abalala balina oba nti omuntu alina "NZE ow’olubeerera era atakyuka" atalina ntandikwa na nkomerero...

Singa abo abeelowooza bwe batyo bayimusa omwoyo wadde okumala akaseera katono, bandisobodde okukiraga bulungi ku lwabwe, nti omuntu alina amagezi takyuka okumala akaseera kanene...

Ensolo ey’amagezi okuva ku ndaba y’endowooza, ekyuka buli kiseera...

Okulowooza nti omuntu bw’ayitibwa Luis bulijjo ye Luis, kiringa okusaaga okubi ennyo...

Omuntu oyo ayitibwa Luis alina mu ye "Nze" endala, Egos endala, ezeeyoleka okuyita mu mbeera ye mu biseera ebyenjawulo era wadde Luis tayagala mululu, "Nze" omulala mu ye —tumuyite Pepe— ayagala omululu era bwe kityo bwe kyeyongera...

Tewali muntu yenna ali mu ngeri yemu mu ngeri etali ya lubeerera, ddala tekibeetaagisa kuba bagezi nnyo okutegeera obulungi enkyukakyuka n’obuzibu obutabalika obuliwo ku buli muntu...

Okulowooza nti omuntu alina "Nze ow’olubeerera era atakyuka" kyenkanankana okujeemera omuliraanwa n’okujeemera omuntu yennyini...

Munda mu buli muntu mulimu abantu bangi, "Nze" nyingi, kino kisobola okukakasibwa ku lwabwe era mu ngeri entuufu, omuntu yenna ayimuse, alina omwoyo...
