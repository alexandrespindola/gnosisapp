Obuntunsi bwe buli omu ku ffe bw'atambuliza munda ye, buva waggulu, okuva mu Ggulu, okuva mu nnyenye...

Tewali kubuusabuusa nti Obuntunsi obw'ekitalo buva mu nnyukuta "LA" (Ekkubo ly'Amata, ekibiina ky'ennyenye mwe tubeera)

Obuntunsi obw'omuwendo omungi buyita mu nnyukuta "SOL" (Enjuba) ate oluvannyuma mu nnyukuta "FA" (Ekitundu ky'Ensi Enzungulukidde Enjuba) ne buyingira mu nsi eno ne bwoyingira mu munda yaffe.

Abazadde baffe baatonda omubiri ogutuukira ddala okukkiriza Obuntunsi buno obuva mu Nnyenye...

Bwe tunaakolera ennyo ku lwaffe era ne twefiiriza ku lw'abaliraanwa baffe, tujja kudda nga tuwangudde mu kifuba kya Urania...

Tuli mu nsi eno olw'ensonga emu, ku lw'ekintu ekimu, olw'ekintu ekimu eky'enjawulo...

Kya lwatu nti mu ffe mulimu ebintu bingi bye tulina okulaba, okusoma n'okutegeera, bwe tuba nga twegomba okumanya ekintu ku lwaffe, ku bulamu bwaffe...

Kya nnaku ennyo okubeerawo kw'oyo afa nga tamanyanga nsonga lwaki yabeerawo mu bulamu...

Buli omu ku ffe alina okuzuula ku bubwe amakulu g'obulamu bwe, ekintu ekimusibye mu kkomera ly'obulumi...

Kya lwatu nti mu buli omu ku ffe mulimu ekintu ekitumala obulamu era kye twetaaga okulwanyisa n'amaanyi...

Tekyetaagisa nti twandisaana okweyongera okubeera mu maziga, kyetaagisa okukendeeza ddala ebintu ebikutuyamba okuba abanafu era abatali basanyufu ne bivaawo ddala.

Tewali kye tuganyulwa bwe twekuluntaza olw'ebitiibwa, empeta, dipulooma, ssente, magezi matalina mugaso, empisa ze tumanyi, n'ebirala, n'ebirala, n'ebirala.

Tetusaana kwerabira nti obunafu n'amalala agatalina mugaso ag'obuntu obw'obulimba, bitufuula abantu abagwagwaavu, abakadde, abalema-lema, abazitoowerera, abatali basobola okulaba ebipya...

Okufa kuliimu amakulu amangi agalamu ne gamabi.

Ka tulowooze ku kirowoozo ekyo eky'ekitalo eky'okuva eri "KABIR Omusukkulumu Yesu Kristo". "Abafu baziike abafu baabwe". Abantu bangi wadde nga balamu, mu butuufu bafu eri omulimu gwonna oguyinza okukolebwa ku lwabwe era, okukyusibwa kwonna okw'omunda.

Be bantu abaggale mu njigiriza zaabwe n'ebyebalikiriza; bantu abakakanyaziddwa mu kujjukira okw'ennaku ezayita; bantu abajjude okusosola okw'edda; bantu abaddu b'abantu kye bagenda okwogera, abatentebbuka ennyo, abatali beefaako, oluusi "abamanya ebintu byonna" abakkiririza okubeera mu mazima kubanga bwe baagambwa bwe batyo, n'ebirala, n'ebirala, n'ebirala.

Abantu abo tebaagala kutegeera nti ensi eno "Gymnasium ey'Obwongo" ekiyinza okuyambako okusaanyaawo obubi obwo obukwekerevu bwe twetikka munda...

Singa abantu abo abaavu bategeera embeera embi gye balimu, bandikankanye olw'entiisa...

Naye abantu ng'abo bulijjo balowooza ebirungi ku lwabwe; beekuluntaza olw'empisa zaabwe, bawulira nga batuukiridde, balungi, banyumirwa okuweereza, balamu, bagabi, bagezi, batuukiriza obuvunaanyizibwa bwabwe, n'ebirala.

Obulamu obwa bulijjo ng'essomero bwa maanyi naye okubutwala ng'ekigendererwa kyabwo, kya busiru ddala.

Abo abatwala obulamu ku bwabwo, nga bwe butambulizibwa bulijjo, tebategeera bwetaavu bw'okukolera ku bwabwe okusobola okutuuka ku "Nkyukakyuka Enkulu".

Eky'ennaku abantu batambuliza obulamu mu butamanya, tebawuliranga ku kukola kwa munda...

Okyuse kyetaagisa naye abantu tebamanyi okukyuka; babonaabona nnyo era tebamanyi lwaki babonaabona...

Okuba n'essente si kintu kyonna. Obulamu bw'abantu bangi abagagga butera okuba obw'ennaku ennyo...
