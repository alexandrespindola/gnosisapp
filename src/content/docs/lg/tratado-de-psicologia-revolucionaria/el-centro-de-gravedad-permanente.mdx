Olwaleero, obutabaawo bw'omuntu omu ng'omuntu yennyini, kifuula okuba n'entegeera entuukirivu okuba ekizibu.

Bwe kiba nti tewali muntu mu magezi, era buli omu ku ffe alimu abantu bangi, bwe kiba nti tewali muntu avunaanyizibwa, kyandibadde kisirusiru okusaba omuntu okubeera n'entegeera entuukirivu.

Tumanyi bulungi nti omuntu omu alimu abantu bangi, kale amakulu amatuufu ag'obuvunaanyizibwa tegaliwo mu ffe.

Ekyo Omuntu akirangirira mu kaseera akamu, tekisobola kuba kya magezi kubanga Omuntu omulala yenna asobola okukirangirira mu kaseera ekirala.

Ekyennyamiza mu nsonga eno kiri nti abantu bangi balowooza nti balina obuvunaanyizibwa obw'empisa era beekozesa okubeera abantu be bamu.

Abantu abamu mu buli kaseera ke babeeramu, bajja mu masomero ga Gnostic, era balabika n'amaanyi g'okwegomba, era banyumirwa omulimu ogw'ekyama era balayira okuwaayo obulamu bwabwe bwonna eri ensonga zino.

Tewali kubuusabuusa nti ab'oluganda bonna mu kibiina kyaffe batendereza omuvumu ng'oyo.

Omuntu tasobola kuleka kulumwa nnyo essanyu ng'awulira abantu ab'ekika kino, abeesigwa era ab'amazima.

Wabula eby'omukwano tebirwawo, olunaku lumu olw'ensonga eno oba eri, etuufu oba etali ntuufu, ennyangu oba enzibu, omuntu ava mu Gnosis, kale aleka omulimu era okuggumiza ekiyingo, oba okugezaako okwennyonnyola, yeegatta ku kibiina ekirala eky'ekyama era alowooza nti kati agenda bulungi.

Okugenda n'okujja kwonna, okukyusa kwonna okutaggwaawo kw'amasomero, ebika, amaddiini, kiva ku bungi bw'Abantu abalwanira obuyinza bwabwe mu ffe.

Nga buli Omuntu bw'alina endowooza ye, amagezi ge, ebirowoozo bye, kya bulijjo okukyusa endowooza, okutambula okutaggwaawo kw'ekibiina, okuva mu kirowoozo ekimu okudda mu kirala, n'ebirala.

Omuntu yennyini, si kintu kirala okuggyako ekyuma ekikozesebwa Omuntu omu oba omulala.

Abantu abamu ab'ekyama beekozesa, oluvannyuma lw'okuleka ekika kino oba kiri, basalawo okukkiriza nti bali Katonda, balabika ng'ettabaaza ezikyamu era oluvannyuma babula.

Abantu abamu abakubamu akafubo ku mulimu ogw'ekyama era oluvannyuma mu kaseera Omuntu omulala lw'ayingirawo, baleka okusoma kuno olw'okumaliriza era baleekebwa obulamu.

Kya lwatu nti omuntu bw'atalwanyisa bulamu, bumumira era batono abeesimbyewo abatalekebwa obulamu.

Nga tulimu obungi bw'Abantu, enkulu y'obuzito etaloggwaawo tesobola kubeerawo.

Kya bulijjo nti si bantu bonna be beegatta mu mutima. Tumanyi bulungi nti okweegatta mu mutima kw'omuntu kwetaaga entegeera entuukirivu era nga kizibu okusanga omuntu alina enkulu y'obuzito etaloggwaawo, kale tekyewuunyisa nti mutono omuntu atuuka ku kweegatta mu mutima okw'omunda.

Ekyabulijjo kiri nti omuntu anyumirwa omulimu ogw'ekyama era oluvannyuma naguleka; ekyewuunyisa kiri nti omuntu taleka omulimu nagutuuka ku nkomerero.

Mazima era mu linnya ly'amazima, tukirangirira nti Enjuba ekola okukebwa mu labolatori enzibu ennyo era eteesittaza.

Munda mwekisolo ekimanyiddwa ng'omuntu mu nsobi, mulimu ensigo ezikula bulungi zisobola okufuuka abantu b'enjuba.

Wabula kikulu okulambika nti tekiriimu kakalu nti ensigo ezo zigenda kukula, ekiriwo nti ziggwaamu amaanyi era zibula mu ngeri embi.

Mu buli nsonga ensigo ezo ezigenda okutufuula abantu b'enjuba zeetaaga embeera entuufu, kubanga kimanyiddwa bulungi nti ensigo, mu mbeera etali nnungi tezikula, zibula.

Okusobola ensigo entuufu ey'omuntu eterekeddwa mu bitundu byaffe eby'ekyama okukula, twetaaga entegeera entuukirivu n'omubiri omulamu.

Bannasayansi bwe bagenda mu maaso n'okugezaako n'ebitundu eby'ekyama eby'omunda, omukisa gwonna ogw'okukula kw'ensigo ezo gujja kubula.

Newankubadde kikyewuunyisa, ebikere byayitako dda mu nkola efanaananeyo, mu biseera eby'edda ennyo eby'enkaluba y'Ensi yaffe.

Omuntu ajjuzibwa okwewuunya ng'atunuulira ekitiibwa ky'olubiri lw'ebikere. Tewali kubuusabuusa nti entegeera eteereddwa mu kifo kyonna ky'ebikere nungi nnyo.

Abo abayitiddwa abazuukuse baziikiriza mu kiyombo bategeera okuyita mu butereevu, nti ebikere mu biseera bye batayambuka abakugu abasinga obukulu mu nsi, baali bantu abaakola empula y'obuwangwa obw'obusosiyali obw'amaanyi ennyo.

Kale baagyawo abafuzi ab'ekika kyabwe, ebiwayi by'amaddiini eby'enjawulo n'obukyayi obutaliimu, kubanga ebyo byonna byabaggyako amaanyi era baalina okubeera abatongole mu ngeri etuukirivu.

Mu mbeera zino, okuggyawo okwetongola kw'omuntu n'eddembe ly'eddiini, ekisolo mu magezi kyagwa mu kkubo ly'okugwa n'okwonooneka.

Ku byo byonna ebyogeddwa waggulu kwongerebwako okukebwa kw'esayansi; okusasula ebitundu by'omubiri, ebitundu eby'ekyama, okukebwa n'omubiri, n'ebirala, omusulo gwakendeeza butoobutooto n'enkyukakyuka mu ndabika y'ebitundu by'omubiri okutuusa nga bifuuse ebikere bye tumanyi.

Empula yonna eyo, emikolo gyonna egyekuusa ku ntegeera entebeevu ey'ebyobufuzi yakolebwa kyuma era yasigalira okuva ku bazadde okutuuka ku baana; leero omuntu ajjuzibwa okwewuunya okulaba ekifo ky'ebikere, naye tetusobola kuleka kwennyamira olw'obutalina magezi.

Bwe tutakolera ku ffe, tugwa era twonooneka mu ngeri ey'entiisa.

Okukebwa Enjuba kw'ekola mu labolatori y'obuntu, mazima okuggyako okuba okuzibu, kuvaamu ebintu bitono nnyo.

Okutonda abantu b'enjuba kusoboka bwe wabaawo okukolagana okw'amazima mu buli omu ku ffe.

Okutonda omuntu w'enjuba tekisoboka bwe tutateekawo nsooka enkulu y'obuzito etaloggwaawo mu ffe.

Tuyinza tutya okubeera n'entegeera entuukirivu bwe tutateekawo enkulu y'obuzito mu magezi gaffe?

Empula yonna etondeddwa Enjuba, mazima teriimu mugaso mulala mu butonde, okuggyako okuweereza ebyetaagisa eby'obutonde buno n'okukebwa kw'enjuba.

Enjuba bwe nereka okukebwa kwayo, efiirwa omugaso gwonna eri empula ng'eyo era eriwo okuvumirirwa okuzikirira n'okugwa.

Buli empula ebibaddewo ku nsi yaweereza okukebwa kw'enjuba. Ku buli empula Enjuba etuuse ku buwanguzi obumu, ng'ekung'aanya ebibinja by'abantu b'enjuba.

Empula bwe eba evuddeko ebibala byayo, ebuzaawo mu ngeri entuufu oba esanaazibwawo mu ngeri ey'obukambwe okuyita mu katyabaga ak'amaanyi.

Okutonda abantu b'enjuba kusoboka bwe tulwanirira okwetongola okuva ku maanyi g'omwezi. Tewali kubuusabuusa nti Abantu bonna abo be tulina mu magezi gaffe, ba kika kya mwezi.

Mu ngeri yonna kyandibadde tekisoboka okwenyaga okuva ku maanyi g'omwezi bwe tutateekawo nsooka enkulu y'obuzito etaloggwaawo mu ffe.

Tuyinza tutya okusaanyawo obuntu bwonna obungi bwe tulina bwe tutalina entegeera entuukirivu? Mu ngeri ki gye tuyinza okubeera n'entegeera entuukirivu nga tetannateekawo nsooka enkulu y'obuzito etaloggwaawo mu magezi gaffe?

Nga empula eno bwe yali okwenyaga okuva ku maanyi g'omwezi, efuude omugaso gwonna eri amagezi g'enjuba, mu ngeri etaliimu kubuusabuusa yeetumidde okugwa n'okwonooneka.

Tekisoboka nti omuntu omuvuvu avaayo okuyita mu nkola ey'enkyuka. Tumanyi bulungi nti enkyuka n'omukyala munne omugumba okugwa, mateeka abiri agasimba omusingi gw'ekyama kyonna eky'obutonde. Omuntu akyuka okutuuka ku kintu ekitegeerekeka bulungi era oluvannyuma enkola ey'okugwa ejja; buli kwambuka kulimu okukka n'obulala.

Ffe tuli byuma byokka ebifugibwa Abantu ab'enjawulo. Tuweereza ebintu ebyetaagisa eby'obutonde, tetulina buntu obutegeerekeka ng'abakyayi ab'ekyama n'abakugu ab'ekyama bangi bwe balowooza mu nsobi.

Twetaaga okukyusa mangu ddala okusobola ensigo z'omuntu okuvaamu ebibala byazo.

Okukolera ku ffe n'entegeera entuukirivu n'obuvunaanyizibwa obw'empisa obujjuvu tusobola okufuuka abantu b'enjuba. Kino kitegeeza okukwata obulamu bwaffe bwonna eri omulimu ogw'ekyama ku ffe.

Abo abalina essuubi okutuuka ku mbeera y'enjuba okuyita mu nkola y'enkyuka, beekozesa era beetumira okwonooneka.

Mu mulimu ogw'ekyama tetusobola kwewa kitiisa ky'obutaba bagumu; abo abalina ebirowoozo eby'okukyuka, abo abakolera ku magezi gaabwe leero era enkya ne baleekebwa obulamu, abo abanoonya okwewala, okwennyonnyola, okuleka omulimu ogw'ekyama bajja kwonooneka era bagwe.

Abamu balagajjalira ensobi, baleka byonna okutuuka enkya nga balongoosa embeera yaabwe ey'ebyenfuna, nga tebalowooza nti okukebwa kw'enjuba kintu kya njawulo nnyo ku ndowooza yaabwe era n'entegeera zaabwe ezimanyiddwa.

Tekiri mu bwangu okufuuka omuntu w'enjuba bwe tuba tukutte Omwezi munda yaffe, (Ego y'omwezi).

Ensi erina emyezi ebiri; eky'okubiri kiyitibwa Lilith era kiyinzibwa okuba ekitono okusinga omwezi omweru.

Abalondoola emmunyeenye batera okulaba Lilith ng'ekirondo kubanga ntono nnyo. Ogwo gwe mwezi omuddugavu.

Amaanyi agasinga obubi aga Ego gatuuka ku nsi okuva e Lilith era galeeta ebyavaamu ebikyamu n'ebisolo.

Emisango gy'amawulire Amamyufu, abattiddwa abasinga okweraliikiriza mu byafaayo, emisango emibi egisinga, n'ebirala, biva ku muyaga gwa Lilith.

Okukwatibwa omwezi okw'emirundi ebiri okukiikiriddwa mu muntu okuyita mu Ego gwe bamunywa munda ky'etufuula okulemererwa kw'amazima.

Bwe tutalabika kukakibi okugaba obulamu bwaffe bwonna eri omulimu ku ffe n'ekigendererwa ky'okwenyaga okuva ku maanyi g'omwezi agafiridde, tujja kumiirwa Omwezi, okugwa, okwonooneka okwongera mu maanyi mu mbeera ezimu ze tusobola okuyita abatamanyi era abatamanyi.

Ekyennyamiza mu nsonga eno kiri nti tetulina buntu obw'amazima, bwe tuba tulina enkulu y'obuzito etaloggwaawo twandikolera ddala okutuusa nga tufuuse embeera y'enjuba.

Waliwo ekyewandika bingi mu nsonga zino, waliwo okwewala kungi, waliwo okusikiriza kungi, okukola kufuuka okuzibu okutegeera olw'ensonga eyo ekikulu ky'omulimu ogw'ekyama.

Wabula ekifo ekitono kye tulina eky'obukyayi obutaliimu n'Enjigiriza ya Gnostic eteekeddwa ku mulimu ogukolebwa, bisobola okutuweereza ng'omusingi eri ebigendererwa byaffe ebirungi ebikwatagana n'okukebwa kw'enjuba.

Amagezi agakyuka agategeera ekyo kye twogera wano, gasoma essuula eno era oluvannyuma nagibuusa maaso; oluvannyuma kujjawo ekitabo ekirala n'ekirala, era ku nkomerero tuggumiza okwegatta ku kitongole kyonna ekigenda okututundira ppaasipooti ey'eggulu, egenda okwogera naffe mu ngeri esinga essuubi, egenda okutukakasa obulamu obutaliimu buzibu.

Bwe batyo abantu bwe bali, emiguwa gyokka egifugibwa emiguwa etalabika, emiguwa egy'ekyama n'ebirowoozo ebikyamu era nga tebalina entegeera entuukirivu.
