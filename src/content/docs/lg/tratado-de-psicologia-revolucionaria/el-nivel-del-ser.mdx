Mutindo gwaffe gwa mpisa ki?, oba kirungi tugambe: Mutindo gwaffe gw’Obuntu ki?

Nga Mutindo gw’Obuntu bwe tugenda okukyuka mu ngeri ya nkalakalira, okuddamu kw’obunafu bwaffe bwonna, ebifananyi, obunafu n’okubonaabona kujja kweyongera...

Ebintu byonna, embeera zonna, ezibaawo ebweru waffe, ku siteegi y’ensi eno, byoleka byokka kye tulina munda.

N’ensonga entuufu tusobola okukakasa n’obukulu nti “ebweru kyoleka ebiri munda”.

Omuntu bwakyuka munda era enkyukakyuka eyo nkalakalira, ebweru, embeera, obulamu, nabyo bikyuka.

Mbadde ntunuulira mu kiseera kino, (Omwaka gwa 1974), ekibiina ky’abantu abaayeeyeza ettaka ly’omuntu omulala. Wano mu Mexico abantu abo bafuna erinnya eryewuunyisa erya “ABAGWA KU NTAMBULIRA”.

Be baliraanwa b’ekibuga kya Campestre Churubusco, bali kumpi nnyo n’ennyumba yange, ensonga eno y’ensonga lwaki mbasobodde okubayiga okumpi...

Okuba abaavu tekisobola lubeera musango, wabula ekizibu si mu ekyo, wabula mu Mutindo gwabwe ogw’Obuntu...

Buli lunaku balwanagana, batamiira, banyoomaganya bokka na bokka, bafuuka batemu ba bannaabwe abanaku, mazima ddala babeera mu nnyumba ezijjudde obucaafu omw’obukyayi bwe bufuga mu kifo ky’omukwano...

Emirundi mingi ndowoozezza nti singa omuntu yenna ku abo, aggwawo munda obukyayi, obusungu, obugwagwa, okutamiira, okusiiwulukuka, ettima, obwekabiiko, obulimba, obuggya, okwagala, amalala, n’ebirala, n’ebirala, n’ebirala, yandisanyusizza abantu abalala, yandegattiddwa ku bantu abalongooseddwa ennyo, ab’omwoyo, ku Tteeka lya Psychological Affinities; emikwano egyo emipya gyandibadde gya nkalakalira ku nkyukakyuka ey’ebyenfuna n’eby’obuwangwa...

Enkola eyo yandisobodde omuntu oyo, okuva mu “ggaagi”, “ekinaabiro” ekijjudde obucaafu...

Bwe kityo, singa ddala twagala enkyukakyuka nkalakalira, ekisooka kye tulina okutegeera kiri nti buli omu ku ffe (oba mweru oba muddugavu, mukonvu oba mumyunyu, mumanyi oba mutamanya, n’ebirala), ali mu “Mutindo gw’Obuntu” guno oba guli.

Mutindo gwaffe ogw’Obuntu guli gutya? Mwabaddeko mulowooza ku ekyo? Tekyandisobose okuyita ku mutindo omulala singa tetumanyi embeera mwe tuli.
