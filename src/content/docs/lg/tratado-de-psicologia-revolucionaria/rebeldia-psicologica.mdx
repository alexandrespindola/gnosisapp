Tekyewalabisa okulabula abasomi baffe, nti waliwo akabonero k’ekibalangulo munda yaffe...

Tewali kubuusabuusa nti akabonero ako tekasobola kusangibwa mu biseera byayita, oba mu biseera ebijja...

Ayooyo yenna ayagala okuzuula akabonero akakyamu, ateekwa okukanonya wano era kati, munda ye, mu kiseera kino ddala, newankubadde akasekkonda kamu kumaso, newankubadde akasekkonda kamu ennyuma...

Emisalaba ebiri egy’Olutukuvu omugalamivu n’omutendera, bisangibwa, mu kifo kino...

Naffe tusangibwa mu kiseera ku kiseera mu makubo abiri: Omugalamivu n’Omutendera...

Kyeyoleka nti Omugalamivu "musiisiivu", ku gwo kutambulira "Vicente n’abantu bonna", "Villegas n’oyo yenna atuuka", "Don Raimundo n’omuntu yenna"...

Kyeyoleka nti Omutendera gwa njawulo; lye kkubo ly’abajeemu abagezi, ery’abakyukakyuka...

Omuntu bwayijukira yekka, bw’akolera ku yekka, bwatemanyiira na bizibu byonna n’ennaku z’obulamu, mu butuufu atambula mu Kkubo ery’Omutendera...

Mazima ddala tekiba kyangu kumalawo birowoozo bibi; okuleka okumanyiira obulamu bwaffe; ebizibu byonna eby’engeri zonna, bizinensi, amabanja, okusasula ebbaluwa, amabanja g’ennyumba, essimu, amazzi, amasannyalaze, n.b.t., n.b.t., n.b.t.

Abatalina mirimu, abo ab’olw’ensonga eno oba eri balefi ebifo byabwe, emirimu, kyeyoleka nti babonaabona olw’okubulwa ssente n’okwerabira ensonga yaabwe, obutafaayo, oba okumanyiira ekizibu kyabwe, mu butuufu kiba kikaluba nnyo.

Abo ababonaabona, abo abakaaba, abo ababaddewo abasaaga byonna, omusuubuzi omubi mu bulamu, obuteebaza, obulimba oba obufere obumu, mu butuufu beerabira bokka, obwabwe obwa ddala, beemanyiira ddala obulemu bwabwe obw’empisa...

Omulimu ku yekka ye nsonga enkulu ey’Ekkubo ery’Omutendera. Tewali n’omu ayinza okulinnya ku Kkubo ery’Obujeemu Obunene, singa talikolera ku yekka...

Omulimu gwe twogera gwa kika kya Sayikoloji; gukola ku nkyukakyuka eno ey’akaseera akatubaddeko. Twetaaga okuyiga okubeerawo mu kiseera ku kiseera...

Ng’ekyokulabirako, omuntu asangiddwa nga yeesunguwadde olw’ekizibu ekimu eky’omukwano, eky’ebyenfuna oba eky’ebyobufuzi kyeyoleka nti yeerabidde yekka...

Omuntu ng’oyo singa ayimirira akaseera katono, singa atunuulira embeera era n’agezaako okujjukira yekka era oluvannyuma n’afuba okutegeera amakulu g’embeera ye...

Singa alowooza katono, singa alowooza nti buli kimu kiyitawo; nti obulamu bwa mulalu, bwa bwangu era nti okufa kuleeta evvu eri eby’amagero byonna mu nsi...

Singa ategeera nti ekizibu kye mu butuufu si kisinga "ekimyanso eky’omuliro", omuliro omubi ogugwa ennyo, aliraba amangu nga yewunyizza nti buli kimu kikyuse...

Okukyusa obukulembeze obw’ekinnansi kisoboka okuyita mu kujooga okw’ensonga n’Okulowooza ku Yekka okw’Omunda okw’Omuntu...

Kyeyoleka nti abantu bakola ebintu eby’ekinnansi eri embeera ezitali zimu ez’obulamu...

Abantu abanafu! Batera okufuuka abasaaga byonna. Omuntu bw’abatemesa bamwenya; bwe babanyooma, babonaabona. Bawemula singa bawemulwa; bawemula singa bawemulwa; tebaba baddembe n’akatono; baliraanwa baabwe balina amaanyi okubaggya mu ssanyu okubatwala mu nnaku, okuva mu ssuubi okubatwala mu kwesunguwaza.

Buli muntu ku abo abagenda mu Kkubo ery’Omugalamivu, afaanana ekivuga, nga buli omu ku baliraanwa be akubako ky’ayagala...

Ayooyo ayiga okukyusa enkolagana ez’ekinnansi, mu butuufu agenda mu "Kkubo ery’Omutendera".

Kino kitegeeza enkyukakyuka enkulu mu "Mutendera gw’Omuntu" ekivaamu ekyewuunyisa ku "Bujeemu bwa Sayikololoji".
