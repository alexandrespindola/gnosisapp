Abasawo b'emitendera gy'obwongo abamu balinga endowooza ng'akambe akasobola okwawula okuva ku ekyo ekatwegattako era ne katusikamu amaanyi.

Abasawo b'emitendera gy'obwongo ng'abo balowooza nti engeri yokka ey'okuva ku maanyi g'ekintu ekyo n'ekirala kwe kukitunuulira buli kiseera mu ngeri etegeerekeka olw'okugenderera okukitegeera okusobola okukitegeera.

Abantu abo balowooza nti bwe batyo omuntu asobola okwawukana n'ekintu ekyo oba ekirala, ne bwe kiba ku bunene bw'obuwaze bw'akambe.

Mu ngeri eno, bagamba nti, ekintu ekiyawuddwa okumanya, kirabika ng'ekirimba ekitemeddwa.

Okumanya ekintu kyonna, okusinziira ku bo, kitegeeza okukyawula ku bwongo bwaffe n'okukisalira omusango gw'okufa.

Tewali kubuusabuusa nti endowooza ng'eyo, erabika ng'enkakafu nnyo, efaanagana mu nkola.

Ekintu ekiyawuddwa akambe ak'okumanya okuva ku bulamu bwaffe, ekisuuliddwa mu nju ng'endiga embi, kyeeyongera okubeera mu bbanga ly'emitendera gy'obwongo, kifuuka dayimooni omutemesa, kinyiikirira okudda awaka, tekikkiriza mangu, tekiyagaliza kulya mmere embi ey'okuwondoolwa, kinonya omukisa era mu kibuukano ekitono eky'abakuumi kidda mangu mu bwongo bwaffe.

Ekisinga obubi kiri nti mu kintu ekyagobebwa mulimu ebitundu ebitono eby'obwongo.

Abasawo b'emitendera gy'obwongo bonna abalowooza bwatyo, tebannasobola kusaanuusa na kimu ku bintu byabwe, mu butuufu bagaanyi.

Newankubadde ng'ogezaako okwewala ekibuuzo ekyo eky'ekintu KUNDALINI, ekizibu kikulu nnyo.

Mu butuufu, "Omwana Ataasiima" takulaakulana mu mulimu gw'eby'obuwangwa ku lulwe.

Mu ngeri etegeerekeka "Omwana Ataasiima" ye buli omu anyoomoola "ISIS", Maama waffe Omutukuvu ow'Ensi yonna, owa buli omu, omu.

ISIS kye kimu ku bitundu ebyetongodde eby'Obwaffe Bwennyini, naye ekyo kivaamu, omusota ogw'omuliro ogw'amaanyi gaffe ag'ekyamagero, KUNDALINI.

Mu ngeri etateebereka "ISIS" yekka y'alina amaanyi agataalukeka okusaanyaawo ekintu kyonna; kino tekiyinza kukaayanirwa, tekiyinza kukaayanirwa, tekiyinza kukaayanirwa.

KUNDALINI kigambo ekigattiddwa: "KUNDA kijja okutujjukiza ekiwandiiko ekibi KUNDARTIGUADOR", "LINI kigambo kya Atlante ekitegeeza Enkomerero".

"KUNDALINI" kitegeeza: "Enkomerero y'ekiwandiiko ekibi KUNDARTIGUADOR". N'olwekyo kikulu obuteekolanga nsobi mu "KUNDALINI" ne "KUNDARTIGUADOR".

Twagambye dda mu ssuula eyayita nti omusota ogw'omuliro ogw'amaanyi gaffe ag'ekyamagero gwezingiddwa emirundi esatu n'ekitundu munda mu Kintu Ekitendeke ekiri mu ggumba lya Coxígeo, omusingi gw'omugongo.

Omusota bwe gulinnya waggulu, ye KUNDALINI, bwe gukka wansi, kye kiwandiiko ekibi KUNDARTIGUADOR.

Okuyita mu "TANTRISMO BLANCO" omusota gulinnya waggulu mu buwanguzi okuyita mu mugongo, nga giggulawo amaanyi agafuula obwakatonda.

Okuyita mu "TANTRISMO NEGRO" omusota gugwa okuva mu coxis okudda mu magombe ag'omu atomi g'omuntu. Bwatyo bwe bafuuka Dayimooni embi mu ngeri ey'entiisa.

Abo abakola ensobi ey'okuwa omusota ogulinnya waggulu ebintu byonna eby'obubbi n'ebizikiza eby'omusota ogukka wansi, bagaanyi ddala mu mulimu ku bwabwe.

Ebivaamu ebibi eby'ekintu "EKIBI KUNDARTIGUADOR", bisobola okusaanyizibwawo n'ekintu "KUNDALINI".

Tekyandibadde kibi okutangaaza nti ebivaamu ebibi ng'ebyo bikulembeddwamu mu YO PLURALIZADO ey'Emitendera gy'Obwongo egikyusizza embeera.

Amaanyi ag'okulagulwa ag'Omusota ogukka wansi gasudde abantu mu butamanya.

Omusota ogulinnya waggulu gwokka, olw'obuwerezebwa, gusobola okutuzuukusa; amazima gano ge mateeka ag'Amagezi ga Hermética. Kati tujja kutegeera obulungi amakulu ag'omwoyo ag'ekigambo ekitukuvu "KUNDALINI".

Okwagala okulambika buli kiseera kukiikirirwa omukazi omutukuvu, Maria, ISIS, asenya omutwe gw'Omusota ogukka wansi.

Ntangaza wano mu butuufu era awatali kubuusabuusa nti omugga omugattike ogw'ekitangaala, omuliro omulamu n'omunyeenyezi ogw'ensi, gukoleddwa omusota ogulina omutwe gw'ente, ogw'embuzi oba embwa mu Bisenge ebyedda.

Gwe musota omugattike ogw'e Caduceo de Mercurio; gwe musota omutemesa ogwa Edén; naye era tewali kubuusabuusa kwonna, Omusota gw'Ekikomo ogwa Musa ogukoleddwa mu "TAU", kwe kugamba, mu "LINGAM Generador".

Ye "Embuzi Ey'Ekisenge" eya Sabbat ne Baphometo ey'Abatemera Gnósticos; HYLE ey'Obugnostico bwonna; omukira gw'omusota omugattike ogukola amagulu g'Enkoko ya Solo ya ABRAXAS.

Mu "LINGAM NEGRO" ekwata mu "YONI" eky'ekyuma, obubonero bw'Katonda SHIVA, Obwakatonda bw'Ensi yonna, mwe muli ekisumuluzo eky'ekyama okuzuukusa n'okukulaakulanya Omusota ogulinnya waggulu oba KUNDALINI, ku nsonga y'obutayiyirawo mu bulamu "Ekigga kya Hermes Trimegisto", Katonda omukulu emirundi esatu "IBIS DE THOTH".

Twogedde wakati w'emirongo eri abo abamanyi okutegeera. Alina okutegeera ategeere kubanga wano waliwo amagezi.

Abamu ku TÁNTRICOS abazira bakola eby'enjawulo, bazuukusa era bakulaakulanya Ekiwandiiko ekibi KUNDARTIGUADOR, Omusota omutemesa ogwa Edén, bwe bakola mu migenzo gyabwe etemu eritasobola kusonyiyibwa ery'okuyiyira "Omwenge Omutukuvu".
