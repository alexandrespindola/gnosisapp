Kristo ye Muliro gw'Omuliro, Olulimi lw'Olulimi, Ennyota y'Omuliro ey'omu bwengula.

Ku Musalaba gw'Omutabani eyattiddwa ku Kalivari kweri Ekikukuma kya Kristo nga kiragiddwa n'ekigambo kimu eky'ennukuta nnya: INRI. Ignis Natura Renovatur Integram - Omuliro Guzzizza Obutagulwa mu Butonde.

Okujja kwa Kristo mu mutima gw'omuntu, kutukyusa mu ngeri y'omujjiriro.

Kristo ye LOGOS SOLAR, Obumu obungi obutuukirivu. Kristo bwe bulamu obukuba mu bwengula bwonna, bye biri, bye bibadde bulijjo era bye biriba bulijjo.

Bingi ebyayogeddwa ku Kizibu kya Mu bwengula; tekiteebereka nti ekizibu kino kikolebwa enjiri ennya.

Twagambiddwa nti Ekizibu kya Mu bwengula kyaleetebwa Elohim ku nsi; Mukama omukulu owa Atilantisi yakyakulira ekizibu kino mu Mubiri ne mu Masa.

Kabir omukulu YESU naye yateekwa okwakulira ekizibu kye kimu mu lujjudde ku Nsi Entukuvu.

Newankubadde Kristo azaalibwa emirundi lukumi mu Besirehemu, tekiyambaamu n'akatono oba tazaalibwa mu mutima gwaffe.

Newankubadde yafa era n'azuukira ku lunaku olwokusatu okuva mu bafu, tekiyambaamu n'akatono oba tufa era ne tuzuukira mu ffe.

Okugezaako okuzuula obutonde n'omutima gw'omuliro kwe kugezaako okuzuula Katonda, abadde yeeraga bulijjo mu ndabika y'omuliro.

Olusesse olwaka (Okuva, III, 2) n'omuliro ku Sinaayi oluvannyuma lw'okuweebwa amateeka Ekkumi (Okuva, XIX, 18): bye biraga Katonda bwe yalabikira Musa.

Nga ali mu kifaananyi ky'ekintu kya Yasipi ne Sadunika ekya langi y'olulimi, ng'atudde ku Ntebe eyaka era eng'angaala, Yokaana omutukuvu anyumya omwami w'obutonde bwonna. (Okubikkulirwa, IV, 3,5). "Katonda waffe muliro ogumira," Pawulo omutukuvu awandiika mu bbaluwa ye eri Abaebbulaniya.

Kristo ow'omunda, Omuliro ogw'eggulu, alina okuzalibwa mu ffe era azaalibwa mu mazima bwe tumala okukola omulimu gw'Obwongo.

Kristo ow'omunda alina okuggyawo mu Butonde bwaffe obw'Obwongo, ensonga ezireeta ensobi; YOES CAUSAS.

Teziba ze nsonga ezireeta EGO okusaanyizibwawo okutuusa Kristo ow'omunda lwe azaaliddwa mu ffe.

Omuliro omulamu era ogwa Firosofiya, Kristo ow'omunda, ye Muliro gw'Omuliro, obutukuvu obw'obutukuvu.

Omuliro gutwetoolodde era gutunaabisa buli we tuli, gutujjira mu mpewo, mu mazzi era ne mu ttaka lye limu abakuumi n'ebintu byabwe ebyenjawulo.

Omuliro ogw'eggulu gulina okukunkumuka mu ffe, ye Kristo ow'omunda, Omulokozi waffe ow'omunda ennyo.

Mukama wo mu mutima alina okufuga obwongo bwaffe bwonna obw'Enkondo ttaano ez'ekyuma ekyo; embeera zaffe zonna ez'Obwongo, Ezeewuwo, Ennyonnyola, Ensibuko y'Ekikolwa Eky'okwegatta.
