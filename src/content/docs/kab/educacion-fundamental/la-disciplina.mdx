Aseggasen n uɣerbaz, igerdan n tesnawit d tesdawiyin ttarran aṭas n lqima i tnekra dɣa ilaq-aɣ ad tt-neɣreɣ deg yixef-a s tẓerayt.

Wid akk iɛeddan ɣef uɣerbaz, tesnawit, tasdawit, atg. ẓran akken iwata d acu-tent tnekriwin, leqwanin, tixanimin, iɣilaten, atg., atg., atg.

Tnekra d ayen i wumi qqaren ASEMMER N UGERREZ. Iseggasen n uɣerbaz ḥemmlen aṭas ad semmren AGERREZ.

Sselmaden-aɣ ad negrez, ad nesbedd kra mgal kra n tɣawsa nniḍen. Sselmaden-aɣ ad negrez tiɛeqbitin n tasa dɣa nețtuɛẓab nekkni dɣa nexḍu i tbuccḍa akken ad negrez.

Sselmaden-aɣ ad NEGREZ tiɛeqbitin i d-yettawin uɛezẓug, tiɛeqbitin n ur nesselmed, ur nṛuḥ ɣer uɣerbaz, ad nuɣal, ad neḍs, ad nemɛayyer iseggassen, ad nerẓ leqwanin, atg. atg. atg.

Iseggasen d teseggasan sɛan asenɣan amekrud i d-yennan s tnekra nezmer ad nefhem asseḥtiw n ad neḥtrem tuddsa n uɣerbaz, asseḥtiw n ad neɣreɣ, ad nḥader tilisa zdat iseggassen, ad nɛic akken iwata akked wid i neɣra yid-nneɣ, atg. atg. atg.

Yella ger medden asenɣan amekrud i d-yennan akken ma negrez, akken ma nerr, nețțili akter d akter d wid ifehmen, ilelliyen, id yellan s lekmal, wid yesɛan tasedda.

Ur bɣin ad ẓren medden akken ma nnaɣen mgal kra, akken ma negrez-t, akken ma nerra-t, tewser-d FEHM.

Ma nennaɣ mgal tmuccni n tmezzayt, tețruḥ i kra n lweqt, maca imi ur tt-nefhim ara s lekmal deg akk ASTAWA n UXELQ, tețuɣal mbeɛd mi neḍqeqreb taɛessast dɣa nețmessa seg yiwet n tikkelt i akk useggas.

Ma nerra tmuccni n zzna, i kra n lweqt nețțili zeddig ɣef udem (ɣas akken deg ASTAWA nniḍen n UXELQ nkemmel-d nețțili d IṢAȚIRIYEN icemɛaten akken i tzemren ad tbeggent tirga IRUȚIYEN d tlaluyin n yiḍ), dɣa nuɣal s tezmert akter ɣer tmussniwin-nneɣ n zik n IWEN YETTMENNAN IZENNAN, s ssebba n tidett tuddrit n ur nefhim ara s lekmal d acu-t ZZNAN.

Aṭas i d-yetturruden TUCCḌA, wid ittnaɣen mgal-s, wid yetteknun mgal-s s tallit n YIQANUNEN ibernen n tmerrit, maca imi ur fhemen ara s tideț akk amussu n TUCCḌA, tettuɣalen deg usqef TTNUDDIN ur ttilin TUCCḌA.

Aṭas i yetteknun mgal LLERṚUB, wid yelmeden ad t-grezen, maca teqqim tețțili deg ustawe nniḍen n uxelq yettafen, ɣas akken ɣef udem teruḥ seg tikli-nneɣ dɣa deg uḍqeqreb n taɛessast, uxelq yettafen ikellef-aɣ dɣa ngerger dɣa nezzemdeg yellan d llerṛub, mi ur nettraju ara dɣa ahat s ssebba ur yesɛin ARA TAMEQQANT.

Aṭas i yetteknun mgal tismin dɣa i taggara umnen s tezdeg akken ttewsen, maca imi ur tent-fhemen ara tebnin akken tbanent tetteɣalen s amur mbeɛd deg tilisa mi d-numen akken mment.

Ala s ulac akk n tnekriwin, ala deg tilelli tanṣayt, tessegrew deg uxellaq, talalut tayyut n FEHM.

TILELLI TEYYUT ur tezmir ad tili deg UZELMAḍ. Nḥwaj tilelli akken ad NEFHEM tuccḍiwin-nneɣ IȘIȘULUJIKIWIN deg talɣa TAMGRADT.

Nḥwaj S URGANCE ad nesserɣ aqerru dɣa ad nger tikerkiṛin n wuzzal, akken ad nili ilelliyen.

Ilaq ad nemmesla yiman-nneɣ ayen akk iseggassen-nneɣ deg UɣERBAZ d baba-nneɣ d yemma-nneɣ nnan-d akken yelha dɣa yesɛa afayda. Ur yettaf ad nelmed seg tira dɣa ad nemmeddel. Nḥwaj ad nefhem.

Akk amehed i iseggassen d teseggasan ilaq ad yettuqeddem ɣer tmusni n inelmaden. Ilaq ad mhedden acku ad kecmen deg ubrid n FEHM.

Ur yettaf ad nini i inelmaden ad ilin ayagi neɣ ayagi, yelaq ad lemden inelmaden ad ilin ilelliyen akken zemren yiman-nnsen ad msukten, ad ɣren, ad glalen akk azal, akk tɣawsiwin i d-nnan medden akken d tidafaydin, tid yelhan, tid yeṣfan dɣa ur tent-qaɛden dɣa ur tent-mmadelen kan.

Medden ur bɣin ad d-ffren yiman-nnsen, sɛan ixellaqen yeqlen, s tɣenniwin, ixellaqen ur bɣin ad bḍun, ixellaqen imikanitiyen ur bḍun dɣa ala MMADLEN.

Yelaq, d yessetḥeqqen, d tesseḥtiwen ad uɣalen inelmaden d tnelmadin seg tmettant-nsen i tmectuḥt ɣer lweqt n ad ǧǧen TIɣIMAWIN ad ttunefɛen tilelli taṣeḥḥit akken ad d-ffren yiman-nnsen, akken ad bḍun, akken ad fehmen dɣa ur ttulalen s aqerru icemɛaten n tuggdiwin, iɣilaten d tnekriwin.

Ma i inelmaden nnan-as-d d acu ilaq ad xedmen d acu ur ilaq ara ad xedmen dɣa ur ten-teǧǧin ara ad FEHMEN dɣa ad mmeslen, ANWI anda tella taɣiwant-nsen? ACU i tikkelt i d-yefka i taɣiwant?

D acu yesɛan afayda mbeɛd ad nɛeddi imtiḥanen, ad nessewruq akken iwata, ad sɛuɣ aṭas n imdukal ma ur nili ara d iɣiwanten?

Taɣiwant tetteɣal ɣer-nneɣ mi nella ilelliyen akken iwata akken ad neḥqeq yiman-nneɣ, akken ad nefhem, akken ad nger s tuffɣa bla tuggdi ɣer uɣilat d bla tikerkiṛt n Tnekriwin.

Inelmaden ittawfaten, iketlen, iferqen i tnekriwin icemɛaten ur zemren ad ẒREN. Ur zemren ad ilin d iɣiwanten.

Ass-a i baba-nneɣ d yemma-nneɣ n twacult d iseggassen, ayen kan itehlen yasen d acu ad uɣalen inelmaden d tnelmadin d tameɣra, ad uɣalen d udmawen, d iɣerḍalen, d imuhuliyen, d tigellalin n tseddart, tenni d imawiyen ddren dɣa mbeɛd ad zewǧen dɣa ad uɣalen ula d MAKINATIN N ASEMRIW N WARRAC dɣa ayagi kan.

Mi d-bɣan warraw d terbaɛatin ad xedmen kra ajdid, kra yemgarraden, mi ḥessun tesseḥtiw n ad ffɣen seg wazelmaḍ-nni, id tettuɣiten, tizeddiwin tikeddamt, tnekriwin, taɣerma n twacult neɣ umalu, atg., mbeɛd baba-nneɣ d yemma-nneɣ n twacult ttercen akter tikerkiṛin n uḥebbes dɣa qaren i wemdan neɣ terbaɛat: Ur ttxedmet ayagi! ur nedder ara ad nessaɛd-kem deg ayagi, tɣawsiwin-a d tirrugza, atg., atg., atg.

TOTAL amdan neɣ terbaɛat yedda iḥbes deg uḥebbes n tnekriwin, tizerwin, tizeddiwin tikeddamt, tmuɣlin tikeddamt, atg.

ASSELMED AQESSAR yeselmad ad yessemtiw URDEN akked TLELLI.

URDEN bla TLELLI d TMELLAT. TLELLI bla URDEN d TANARKI.

TILELLI d URDEN i d-yekkan s tmusni d LEQSED n ASSELMED AQESSAR.

INELMADEN ilaq ad ttunefɛen tilelli tamerzagt akken ad ẓren yiman-nnsen, akken ad BDUN akken ad D-FREN ayen s tideț, ayen s tidet illan deg YIMAN-NSEN dɣa ayen zemren ad xedmen deg tudert.

Inelmaden d tnelmadin, iserdasen d imḍebbren d deg umata wid iqerben ad ɛicen iferqen i tnekriwin icebḥen, ttettwaɣalen d iḍulaḍen, ur hessun ara i uqlaɛ n wanufriw amdan, ur yesɛin ara aɣunzu.

TNEKRA tețțeɣẓu SENSEBILIȚE amdan dɣa ayagi yedda ibeggen s TMEẒRIWT d TAMESLAḌT.

S ssebba n tnekriwin d leqwanin, medden n tallit-a ḍaɛen akken yeslaq LḥESS dɣa uɣalen d iḍulaḍen d ur yesɛin ara aɣunzu.

Akken ad nili ilelliyen s tideț ilaq ad nili hassasen d imdanen.

Deg iɣerbazen, igerdan n tesnawit d tesdawiyin, sselmaden inelmaden ad rren TASEƐZI deg tɣuriwin dɣa inelmaden d tnelmadin ttrun taseɛzi akken ad tezzuɣren i uɣilat, tɛeẓẓa n umeẓẓuɣ, tɛezza s tkerkiṛt neɣ s taqɛayt, atg. atg. atg. Maca ɣas akken ur ten-sselmaden ara ad FEHMEN AMEK ṣEḤ TASEƐZI TAYYUT.

S tnekra ameselmad ittawaɛze dɣa yettewser tazmert tayyut aṭas n tikkal deg talɣa ur nesɛin ara afayda.

Tazmert tayyut d: talɣa iṣafan n taẓult tesseɣzayen s MAKINAT ORGANIKA.

Nettett dɣa nettsu dɣa akk imahil n tseddaɣ d deg usqef imahil n tidɣer deg taẓult deg tiẓulin tiquranin ttettbeddilen ɣer tẓulin d tzumar yelhan.

Tazmert tayyut d: talɣa n TMEẒLI d n TEZMERT yellan ṣeḥ taẓulit tseɣza umgalu.

Ma nẓer ad nerr TASEƐZI TAYYUT nezmer ad nesseɣzes tazmert tayyut.

Ayɣer ɣur-sen, iseggassen d teseggasan ur sselmaden i imelmaden-nsen d acu-t TASEƐZI TAYYUT.

Anda nessuqqel TASEƐZI nttewser TAZMERT TAYYUT. Nezmer ad nesseɣzes tazmert-nni ma nefk taseɛzi, ma ur nenzul ara akked tɣawsiwin, akked medden, akked tmuɣlin.

Mi nenzul akked medden, akked tɣawsiwin, akked tmuɣlin, nettu iman-nneɣ dɣa mbeɛd nḍaɛ tazmert tayyut deg talɣa yeqlan akter.

D UGEL iwata ad nẓer acku nḥwaj ad nesseɣzes TAZMERT TAYYUT akken ad nekkse TIẒRI dɣa TAZMERT TAYYUT d ALAQ N TUDERT, AGIRDEN n TIẒRI, amuhul akken ad NEKKSE TIẒRI.

Mi nelmed ad ur NETTU YIMAN-NNEɣ, mi nelmed ad nger TASEƐZI gar SUJET; UBBJE d UMRU, nesseɣzes TAZMERT TAYYUT akken ad nekkse TIẒRI.

Yelaq ad nelmed ad nenbeq TASEƐZI akken ad nekkse tmusni maca inelmaden d tnelmadin ur ẓren ara kra ɣef aya acku ISEGɣASEN d TISEGɣASEN-nsen ur ten-sselmed ara.

MI nelmed ad nseqdec TASEƐZI s tazdeg, tnekra mbeɛd tettaḍef.

Ameselmad neɣ tamaselmadt ittawaɛzen i tɣuriwin-nsen, i imalalen-nsen, i urden, UR YEḤWAJ ARA i tnekra n kra n wazal.

D UGEL i iseggassen ad fehmen tesseḥtiw n ad semtiwen s tmusni TLELLI d URDEN dɣa ayagi yezmer s TASEƐZI TAYYUT.

TASEƐZI TAYYUT teẓẓuɣur ayen i wumi qqaren IDENTIFIKACION. Mi IDENTIFIKATION akked medden, akked tɣawsiwin, akked tmuɣlin, teṭṭeffer-d FASINACION dɣa ayaggaru-ya tessemer ṬṬES deg TIẒRI.

Ilaq ad nẓer ad nerr TASEƐZI bla IDENTIFIKACION. MI nerr taseɛzi deg kra neɣ deg yiwen dɣa nettun iman-nneɣ, taggara d FASINACION d ṬṬES n TIẒRI.

Eẓret akken iwata ASEKNAR. Yella yeṭṭes, akk yesteqsi, yesteqsa yiman-is, yella yeqqaq, yeqqar-d am asenmubilu, yenǧa akked taseqflut yettwali, akked ucengal n taseqflut.

INELMADEN d tnelmadin ilaq ad rren taseɛzi deg tɣuriwin bla tuṭṭu yiman-nnsen akken ur tɣellin ara deg ṬṬES UCEBḤAN n TIẒRI.

Ameselmad ilaq ad iwali yiman-is deg tili mi yessegrew amtiḥan neɣ mi illa zdat uɣerbaz neɣ ugar s tmerrit n weselmad, neɣ mi illa yesselmad neɣ yestareḥ neɣ yuɣal akked wid i yelmaden yid-s.

TASEƐZI TIBDATT deg TLETT LQAẒAT: SUJET, UBBJE, UMRU, d tidett TASEƐZI TAYYUT.

Mi ur nexḍu ara i TUCCḌA n IDENTIFIKACION akked medden, tɣawsiwin, tmuɣlin, atg. Nesseɣzes TAZMERT TAYYUT dɣa nesseɣtiw deg-nneɣ ukkisen n TIẒRI.

Win ibɣan ad yekkes TIẒRI deg IWEN IMAWEN, ilaq ad yebdu s UKKIS dagi d tura.

Mi IXELQI yexḍu i TUCCḌA n IDENTIFIKACION akked medden, tɣawsiwin, tmuɣlin, mi yexḍu i TUCCḌA n ad yettu yiman-is, mbeɛd ɣellin deg ffasinacion d ṭṭes.

Tnekra ur tesselmad i inelmaden ad rren TASEƐZI TAYYUT. Tnekra d aḥebbes s tideț i uxellaq.

Inelmaden d tnelmadin ilaq ad lemden ad menzlen TASEƐZI TAYYUT seg wewnan n uɣerbaz akken mbeɛd deg tudert tusdidt, berra n uɣerbaz, ur xedmen ara tuccḍa n ad ttun yiman-nnsen.

Argaz yettafen yiman-is zdat win i yettɛayaren, IDENTIFIKACION yid-s, yenḍaq, yettɣelliy deg ṭṭes n tɛeẓẓug dɣa mbeɛd yejerreḥ neɣ yettemmet dɣa yettruḥ ɣer uḥebbes bla mermer.

Win ur yetteǧǧin ad YENḌAQ akked win i t-yettɛayaren, win ur IDENTIFIKACION ara yid-s, win ur yettun ara iman-is, win yezmeren ad yerr TASEƐZI TAYYUT, ur yezmir ad yefk azal i awalen n win ittɛayaren, neɣ ad t-ijerreḥ neɣ ad t-yemmet.

Akk tuccḍiwin yexḍu wanufriw amdan deg tudert, ttettɛeqqen i ad yettu yiman-is, yella IDENTIFIKACION, yenḍaq dɣa yettɣelli deg uḍes.

Yelha akter i ilemẓi, i akk inelmaden, ad ten-sselmaden UKKIS n TIẒRI di tmella n ad ten-ɣeznen s aṭas n tnekriwin ur yettuɣalan ara ɣer usqef.
