Aselmed aqbur s tazzla-ines d tameqrant, d tamusni taneggarut n iman; deg yal yiwen yella-d ukayad n ugama.

Win yebɣan ad yissin ayen yelhan deg ugama, ilaq-as ad ten-yexdem deg iman-is.

Aselmed yelhan ittekki kan deg usnerni n uẓar, u d ayen daɣen izemmer yal yiwen. D tidet, s idrimen, yal yiwen izemmer ad yecɣer idlisyen.

Ur nekkataɣ mgal tazwara tatrarit, nekkataɣ kan mgal yiswi n usenfufed n wallaɣ.

Aselmed yelhan itteddem kan iẓuran iberrayen iwakken ad yerwel iman-is.

Yal amdan ilemẓi, yal win yettaṭṭafen deg wallaɣ, yella-d ameslayen yelhan iwakken ad yerwel iman-is.

Seg ULƔU-WALLAƔ mebla RUḤ id-tekken ƔAWWAREN u wigi, s wigi i ɣ-yeǧǧan tamurt deg RUYX u deg TNƐEDDUT.

Aẓeṭṭa ur yezmir ara ad aɣ-yefk ad nessen iman-nneɣ s tazzla TAMEGDA UNITAṬAL.

Imawlan ttawin arrac-nsen ɣer Uɣerbaz, ɣer Leqlic, ɣer Taseddawit, ɣer Upulitiknik, atg., iwakken ad lemden aẓeṭṭa, iwakken ad sɛun tuddsa, iwakken ad zmren deg taggara ad ɛiccen.

D tidet, ḥwaǧen ad nessen aẓeṭṭa, ad sɛun tuddsa, maca d ayen iɛeddan, ayen yettwaren, ayen i d-yeqqaren, d tussna n iman-nneɣ, ad nessen anwa i nella, ansi id-nekka, ɣer anda iteddu, d acu-t usentel n tudert-nneɣ.

Deg tudert yella kullec, ttafraḥ, uɣbel, tayri, rreɣba, tafaska, aẓɣal, tezmert, tuggdi, atg., u mi nessnen ad ɛiccen s id-dderfen, mi nefehhem-tt deg kullec TIQSIREN n wallaɣ, ad naf amḍiq-nneɣ deg Tɣiwant, ad negmer aẓeṭṭa-nneɣ, tarrayt-nneɣ iwakken ad neɛcu, ad nḥussu u ad nmeyyez, maca ayen icudden d tikerkas d tissit, aẓeṭṭa yiman-is, ur yezmir ara ad yefk tafhimet, tafhimet tidet.

Aselmed atrar yeffeɣ-d d axṣar axater yefka TMASEƔRA tameqrant i uẓeṭṭa, i tuddsa u d tidet, mi nezmer uẓeṭṭa, iǧǧa amdan d ameqqran ameqqran, isegger afraz-is.

Ad nezmer ad nessen u ad nɛeddel mebla tafhimet n tudert, mebla tussna n iman, mebla tussna tasridt n tira n MI IMAN-IW, mebla tanḍart n tarrayt-nneɣ iwakken ad nmeyyez, ad nḥussu, ad nḥiwwej u ad nexdem, ad nesserɣay kan aɣdar-nneɣ, taɣdemt-nneɣ, tid i d-yettaken ṭrad, aẓu, nnger, aẓɣal.

Asnerni n uẓeṭṭa kan i d-yettaken Imsudusen, Imusnawen, imezdaɣen, imusnawen n teẓṛi, ifuraran n iɣersiwen, imẓarawen n iɣallen imseɣẓanen, atg., atg., atg.

Kullec wigi, kullec wigi imezdaɣen, kullec wigi imẓarawen n Tumbatin Tiṭuṭunin u Tumbatin n Waman, kullec wigi ifuraran i d-yettazzaken iɣersiwen n ugama, kullec wigi iɣawwaren, ayen iɣef mazal ttwassen, d ṭrad u d aseɣẓen.

Ur ssinen ara kullec wigi iɣawwaren, ur fehmen ara tasemlit tameqrant n tudert deg kullec tizrawin-ines.

Aseggas atiknuluji aqbur, tarwa n uḥiwa, temsirin timeqqranin, asemɣi n tẓegwa, imellalen deg tissiwin, iẓeṭṭa n wallaɣ, atg., ḥellen-d iwewten n wuguren i d-yettaken deg tazzla n tella, maca yessaɣleb amdan u deg Tɣiwant, iwed iwedren u iqeffanen.

Ad ɛiccun kan deg TIQSIRETA TAQBUR mebla ad nessen imuren u tamiwin iqeffanen n wallaɣ, yesseɣbel-aɣ-d i d-nekkaɣ-d u i warraw-nneɣ, tixert, imeṭṭawen u tuggdi.

Aḥwaǧ ameqran, ugur i d-yeqqaren n yal YIWEN, n yal amdan, d afehhem n tudert deg tazzla-ines TAMEGDA, UNITOTAL, axater akka kan i nella deg tazzla ad nḥelli uguren-nneɣ.

Tussna tatiknulit yiman-is ur tezmir ara ad teḥlu uguren-nneɣ, kullec uguren-nneɣ iqeffanen.

Ma nebɣa ad nili d IRGAZEN n tidet, INDIVIDUWEN IMEGDA ilaq-aɣ-d ad NEZWAR AGADUM-NNEƔ, ad nessen iman-nneɣ deg kullec imuren n umeyyez, axater TAKNULUJIT mebla tuggdi, tuɣal-d d amseɣẓan, mi ur NEFHEM ara s tidet tasemlit tameqrant n tella, mi ur nessen ara iman-nneɣ deg tazzla TAMEGDA.

Ma IƔES ILEMSAWEN yeqqar s tidet, ma yesnen iman-is, ma yefehem tasemlit tameqrant n tudert, ur yezmir ad ixdem AQERZAM ad YEMMESSI TAƮUM.

Aseggas-nneɣ atiknuluji d ameqran maca yenɣaɣ-aɣ-d kan ad nesserɣay taɣdemt-nneɣ iwakken ad nessufeɣ iman-nneɣ ger iman-nneɣ u ansi di llan terror, aẓu, tuggdi u iwaṭṭaren.

Ur tzemmer ara tuddsa, ur yezmir ara aẓeṭṭa ad aɣ-yefk ayen i d-qqaren DDEH, TFASKA TIDET.

Yal yiwen deg tudert ittuɣen deg tuddsa-ines, deg taɣdemt-ines, deg uḍris-ines u leḥwayeǧ iḥettren ittuɣalen-d d iselfan n tkerri, asemsektay, akerreh, tuggdi.

Aɣref n ṭṭbibat, aɣref n imẓarawen, n imusnawen, n iɣewwaren, atg., yal yiwen deg wigi, yeččur d aẓɣal, asemsektay, asenned, tkerri, atg.

Me bla tafhimet n iman-nneɣ, aɣermaz, tuddsa, aɣref, ttaggarɣ-aɣ ɣer waɣzal u ad nqelleb ɣef tikkertiyen. Kra nniḍen ad qelben ɣef tikkertiyen s tazzla n lqahwa, taɣreft, tazelt, kra nniḍen bɣan ad rwlen s yidammen, amurfin, lkukayin, lmarigwana u kra nniḍen s leɣṛur u uqeyyeɛ, taɣdemt, atg., atg.

Mi nebɣa ad nemzel kullec TUDERT ɣer uẓeṭṭa, ɣer tuddsa, ɣer ukayad iwakken ad nerbeḥ idrimen u idrimen nniḍen, yeffeɣ-d d asnerni, asnerni u taqleb ɣef tikkertiyen.

Ilaq-aɣ-d ad nuɣalen d INDIVIDUWEN IMEGDA, iqeddsen u akka kan izemmer mi nessen iman-nneɣ u ad nseɣẓen YO PSICOLÓGICO.

ASELMED AQBUR deg yiwet tikkelt i deɣyettḥettem almad n uẓeṭṭa iwakken ad nettawi tudert, ilaq-aɣ-d ad nexdem ayen i yifen, ilaq-aɣ-d ad nɛiwen amdan, ad nexdem, ad nḥussu deg kullec imuren-is u deg kullec imuren n wallaɣ, tasemlit n tella.

Ma yella-d win izemmer ad yeqqar ayen ad yeqqar u d ayen nniḍen yelhan axater akka yal yiwen ad yexdem iman-is tarrayt-ines, maca ilemmad tarraytin iǧḍran mebla ad yexdem s iman-is tudert deg tazzla-ines TAMEGDA, ad yawi kan ɣer tazzla.
