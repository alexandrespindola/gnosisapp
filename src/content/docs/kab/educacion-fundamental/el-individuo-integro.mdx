Aslugen amenzu deg wayen yellan d tidet d amedyez aɛmiq n iman-inek; deg yal yiwen seg-neɣ llan akk yisental n ugama.

Win yebɣan ad yessen akk d tisuraf n ugama, ilaq ad tent-yeɣre deg iman-is.

Aslugen akells yettḥebbir kan ɣef uzgay n uɣref, u waya yezmer ad t-yexdem yal yiwen. D tidet belli s idrimen, yal yiwen yezmer ad yeɣ i yiman-is idlisen.

Ur nneqqar ara ur nḥemmel ara tamusni, nneqqar kan ur nḥemmel ara asnerni n uɣref s waṭas.

Aslugen akells d win i d-yettakken kan ttnaɛ aqelɛan akken ad nerwel i iman-nneɣ.

Yal argaz yeqqaren, yal amejhal amɣref, yal wa yesɛa ttnaɛ yelhan akken ad yerwel i iman-is.

Seg UJEHLI s TJEGLI s TNEẒRI, d imeɣlalen i d-yetmettun yerna wigi d win i yewwin tamsertit ɣer UMEJNUḌ d UMERZUG.

Tazmert ur tezmir ara ad aɣ-teǧǧ ad nessen iman-nneɣ s usuffeɣ ALIMAN AKWATA.

Imawlan ceggɛen arrac-nsen ɣer Uɣerbaz, ɣer Useɣti, ɣer Tasmilt, ɣer Uɣerbaz Aɛlayan, atg., akken ad lemden taẓuri, akken ad sɛun aɣerbaz, akken ad zemren ad ɛicen.

D tidet belli ḥwaǧen ad nessin taẓuri, ad nesɛu aɣerbaz, maca ayagi d amsinan, ayen yellan d amenzu, d amenzuɣ, d aslugen, d ad nessen iman-nneɣ, ad nessin anwa i d-nekni, seg anda d-nekki, ɣer anda nteddu, d acu-t d amaggu n tudert-nneɣ.

Deg tudert yella akk, tifrahin, lehmum, leḥmala, asrem, lfeṛḥ, aɛyuf, cbaḥa, tixerzayt, atg. yerna mara nessin ad nɛic deg-s s lebɣi, mara nessefti deg akk yiswiren n wallaɣ, ad naf amkan-nneɣ deg tmetti, ad nexleq taẓuri-nneɣ, tarrayt-nneɣ taɣelnawt akken ad nɛic, ad naru yerna ad nemsekti, maca ayen iɛekkksen d akells i meyya imeṭṭiwen, taẓuri s iman-is, ur tezmir ara ad teffeɣ seg-s amedyez aɛmiq, amedyez n tidet.

Aslugen yellan ass-a yefsus s waṭas acku yefka azal meqqren i taẓuri, i uɣerbaz yerna d tidet belli mara nedhen ɣef taẓuri, ad yuɣal argaz d amatar amkanik, ad yesneɣti tizmert-is yelhan.

Ad nexdem tazmert d tukklit bla amedyez n tudert, bla tussna n iman-nneɣ, bla amger n tidet n tira n IMAN-IW, bla aɣerbaz yessuksen n tarrayt n umsekti, arruy, uray d usekti-nneɣ, ad yeqqel kan akken ad nyesnerni tixser-nneɣ, lgerruḍ-nneɣ, ayen i d-yettakken imezɣiwen, alluɣ, tisselt, aɛyuf.

Asnerni aqellan n taẓuri yeṭṭef ameɣlal amkanik, imussnawen, imeɣlalen, ifiziken imatumen, iɣerraben n iɣersiwen imeɣbanen, iɣerraben n yir aselsa, atg., atg., atg.

Ur yessin ula d yiwen deg wigi d imeɣlalen, ur tfahmen amger akk n tudert deg akk d tilitin-is ur yesɛi tilisa.

Ad nɛic kan deg YISWI AFELLAY bla ma neɣra timura d temnaḍin yellan d iɛemiqen n wallaɣ, anamek-is d ayen i d-yettakken fell-aɣ d fell-arrac-nneɣ tisselt, imeṭṭi d uɣuru.

Aḥwaǧ meqqren, ugur aḥrazen n yal AMDAN, n yal bnadem, d ad nessefti tudert deg usuffeɣ-is AKWATA, AKWATA, acku akken kan i nezmer ad nḥelli uguren-nneɣ ayellilen iɣerfanen.

Tussna taẓuranit s iman-is ur tezmir ara ad teḥlu uguren-nneɣ akkanen, akk d uɣuren-nneɣ iɛemeqen.

Ma nebɣa ad nili d ARGAZEN n tidet, 1NDIVIDUOS INTEGROS ilaq ad N-NQRARIMAN PŚIKOLOJIKAMENTE, ad nessin iman-nneɣ deg akk d timura n umsekti, acku TEKNOLOJIA barra i kra n tukket, ad tuɣal d asentur n unegzum, mara ur NFEHIM de VERDAD akk d amger akk n tili, mara ur nessin ara iman-nneɣ deg usuffeɣ INTEGRA.

Ma YIL YIɣERSIW AɣREF ad IḥEMM iman-ines de VERDAD, ma yessen iman-ines, ma yefhem amger akk n tudert ur yexdim ara AMAZEɣ n UFRACCIAR LÁTOMO.

Aɣerbaz-nneɣ aẓuranit yella d izemran maca yeffeɣ kan ad yesnerni tazmert-nneɣ taɣawant akken ad nenegzem gar-aneɣ yerna deg yal amkan llan tukut, alluɣ, timeẓẓuft d tuḍen.

Ulac aɣerbaz, ulac taẓuri tezmir ad aɣ-tefka ayen i wumi qqaren PLENITUD, FELICIDAD VERDADERA.

Yal wa deg tudert yettɛeyyub s lebɣi deg uɣerfaz-is, deg uɣerbaz-is, deg tarrayt n tudert-is tenzehant yerna ayen d uɣerbaz yuɣal d asenturen n tira, ameslay, akrah, aɣuru.

Amaḍal n yijujeg, amaḍal n yinelmaden, n yimeɣlalen, n yimḥamin, atg., yal wa seg umaḍalen-agi yella d ajedduɣ n uɛeyyub, ameslay, temseɣrat, tira, atg.

ASLUGEN AMENZU mara yessaram ad lemden taẓuri akken ad kecben tudert, ilaq ad yexdem kra n ugur meqqren, ilaq ad yezmer ad yexdem argaz, akken ad yaru, akken ad yaru deg akk d tarwa-s d deg akk timura n wallaɣ, amger n tili.

Ma yella yiwen yesɛa ayen ara d-yini ad t-id-yini yerna ayagi i d-yettini yella d izemran acku akken kan yal wa yexleq iman-is ass-a tarrayt-is, maca ilemmed tarrayin-is n wiyaḍ mara ur yaru ara s iman-is tudert deg usuffeɣ-is AKWATA; yessakar kan ɣer yiswi afellay.
