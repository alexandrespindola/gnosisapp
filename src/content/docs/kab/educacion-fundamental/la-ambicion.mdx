Tameɣrut tesɛa aṭas n ssebbat, yiwet seg-sent d ayen i ttwasemman d AGWAD.

Aqcic ameqqran yellan di tmerriwin n temdinin timeṛkantiyin, yesfifid aɛeqqa n imezdaɣen imeqqranen, yezmer ad yuɣal d amakar ma yella yessaweḍ ad yesɛu agwadd ɣef tɣellabt, agwadd ɣef yiman-is, agwadd ɣef uzekka-s.

Taḍeffart tamecṭuḥt i txeddem di tḥanut taɛzizt n umerkad, tezmer ad tuɣal d taserḍant neɣ d yiwet i itzenzayen iman-is deg yiḍ seg wass ɣer wayeḍ, ma yella tessaweḍ ad tesɛu agwadd ɣef uzekka, agwadd ɣef tudert, agwadd ɣef uɣerda, agwadd ɣef yiman-is, atg.

Ameskar ayeɛzzi deg uɣerbaz ayeɛzzi neɣ di lôtel ameqqran, yezmer ad yuɣal d GANSTER, d aserked n lbunat, neɣ d amakar yeẓẓiden aṭas, ma yella yeḍra-yas ad yesɛu agwadd ɣef yiman-is, ɣef amkan-is ameqqran ameskar, ɣef uzekka-s, atg.

Iɣessul imecṭuḥ yesɛa tameɣrut ad yili d ayeɛzzi. Aɛeggal ameggag i ixeddem di tḥanut yesɛeddiyeḍ ɣer imezdaɣen yerna s ṣṣber aɣ-d-ibeggen taseddaṛt, aqemis, adebsi, ittagal iɣef, ittemẓiweṛ s tmenṭart taẓẓawant, yesɛa tameɣrut ɣer kra nniḍen axaṭer yesɛa agwadd, aṭas n ugwadd, agwadd ɣef tɣellabt, agwadd ɣef uzekka-s yettaweḍen, agwadd ɣef uɣerda, atg.

Tameɣrut d ayen aṭas n wudmawen. Tameɣrut tesɛa udem n waliw yerna tesɛa udem n ccitan, udem n urgaz yerna udem n tameṭṭut, udem n lmeɛna yerna udem n mebla lmeɛna, udem n uɛadil yerna udem n umednub.

Tella tameɣrut deg win i yebɣan ad yaɣ yerna deg uɣerda aɛezri i ittekren zzwaǧ.

Tella tameɣrut deg win i ibɣan s lɛeqel ur yettfakka ara "AD YILI KRA", "AD IBEDD", "AD YALI" yerna tella tameɣrut deg win i yuɣalen d anaɛurek, ur ibɣi kra seg ddunit-a, axaṭer tameɣrut-is weḥd-s d ad yaweḍ ɣer igenwi, ad yeqleɛ, atg.

Llan TAMEƔRUTIN TIMENGAZIN yerna tella TAMEƔRUTIN TRUḤANIYIN. Tikwal tameɣrut tessexdam udem n MEBLA LMEƐNA akk d TASEQRIBANIT.

Win ur yessaɛi ara TAMEƔRUT ddunit-a yemmuten akk d Tɣellabt, YESƐA TAMEƔRUT lḥedda nniḍen, yerna win ur yessaɛi ara TAMEƔRUT idrimen, YESƐA TAMEƔRUT TUDERTIWIN TISSUSSANIN.

I NEKKINI, I YIMAN-IW, I YIMAN-IS, ittemnen i ttɣum tameɣrut, ad tt-yeḍḍef deg yiɣeffen yeffren aṭas n uɣebar yerna iteqqar imiren: "UR SƐIƔ ARA TAMEƔRUT ɣEF WACUḤ", "ḤEMMLEƔ IMAWLAN-IW", "XEDMEƔ S TIZZWAWANT ƔEF TMELILT N MEDDEN AKKEN MA LLAN".

ASERTAY AZERWAN yerna ittzan akk, ittemɛeqṛ tikwal ɣer lɣaci s leɛmala-s i yesseɣlin di tmenṭart, maca mi ittajja amkan, d ayen i yesseqqen ad iṣub seg tmurt-is s kra n wamelien n ddular.

Tameɣrut yeḍḥeqen s udem n MEBLA LMEƐNA, tettɣwer tikwal ɣer imezdaɣen yellan d iḍen.

Llan deg ddunit aṭas n medden i yesɛan tameɣrut ur yelli ara d iɣurifen.

Aṭas n medden i ittaǧan akk temɣar akk d lɣaci n ddunit axaṭer weḥd-sen yesɛan tameɣrut YIMAN-IS YESSEN ẒIDEẒ.

Uɛeqqi i ittmuddu ɣef teɛeqqa ar lǧameɛ yerna ittezzi iman-is yeččuṛ d lamana, ur yesɛi ara tameɣrut di tmenṭart yerna ar ass-a yesɛa tamellaḥ ad yefk mebla ad ibeddel i yiwen, maca d ayen ibanen ad YESƐU TAMEƔRUT LƐEǧAJEB, lεilaj, ṣṣeḥḥa i yiman-is neɣ i kra n twacult-is, neɣ, leslak ameggaru.

Nekni nettanazeɛ i yergazen akk d tilawin i yellan d ddin ṣṣeḥ, maca nettuɣal ad nedmumez ur țḥemmlen ara ddin-nsen s TIZZWAWANT kamel.

Ddin iṣelḥen, tarbaɛin yesɛan azal, tarbaɛin, tuddsa tirit, atg. Tettwasɛa ḥemleɣ TIZZWAWANT-NNEƔ.

D ayen yeɛdel aṭas ad naf deg ddunit-a kra n win i iḥemmel ddin-is, aɣerbaz-is, tarbaɛt-is, atg. S tizzwawant. Ayagi d ayen yeḍḥiqen.

Akk ddunit yeččuṛ d tameɣrutin. Hitler yeffeɣ ɣer umgaru ɣef tameɣrut.

Akk umgaruten sɛan aẓaṛ-nsen di Lɣella akk d TAMEƔRUT. Akk tawaɣit i iɛzẓan aṭas n tudert sɛan aẓaṛ-nsen deg TAMEƔRUT.

Akk ddunit tettidir di lmeḥna mgal akk ddunit ɣef tameɣrut, kra mgal kra nniḍen akk d akk mgal akk.

Akk win yellan di tudert YESƐA TAMEƔRUT AD YILI KRA yerna lɣaci n laɛmer-nsen, imessawen, imawlan n twacult, imeɣnasen, atg. Sersen i yigerdan, i tullas, i tɣerdas, i ilemẓiyen, atg. Ad teḍḍef deg webrid iḥerken n TAMEƔRUT.

Imeqqranen qqaren i inelmaden akk d tnelmadin, ur tettedduḍ ara ad tiliḍ d kra di tudert, ad yuɣal aɛezzi, ad tzuǧeḍ akk d imezdaɣen imerkaniyen, ad tiliḍ d ameqqran, atg. atg.

Tuddsa tameɣrert, tḥerken, tḍerrɛen, teɛdel, bɣan ad ilin daɣen tuddsa timeggura tameɣrutin, tḥerken, d iḍerrɛen am nutni.

Ayen i iɛzẓan aṭas deg wayagi akk, d acu kan lɣaci iṭṭuruḍen ad "TTWAƐEQLEN" daɣen ad ittwasuqnen ɣef webrid-nni iḥerken n TAMEƔRUT.

Imsawen akk d tmsawen ilaq ad sslemdin i INELMADEN akk d TNELMADIN d acu kan ixeddem ṣṣeḥ ur yeɣlin ara di sser, d ayen ur nesɛi ara lmeɛna ad ttqaraɛeɣ s sser ameqqran taqsit n ttaksi, aɛeggal n tḥanut, aflaḥ, imesfaḍ n aɛeqqa, atg.

Akk lxedma ameɣrar d ayen yesɛan azal. Akk lxedma ameɣrar d ayen ilaqen di tudert tmetti.

Ur ḥar ḥar neɣd as-d-nɛedda i inġiniuren, i imenza, i irais, i duqtur, i ġura, atg.

Di tmetti, ilaq ad ilin akk lxedmat, akk lexlaṣ, ur yezmir ara ad yaɣ lxedma ṣṣeḥ deg sser.

Di tudert, yal win isɛeddiyeḍ i wayen yettwaxdem yerna d ayen i iɛzẓan ad yeẓer i acu waxdem yal waḥed.

D udeb n IMSAWEN akk d TMSAWEN ad sufɣen IZEN n yal unelmad yerna ad t-ssuɣun di tmenɣiwt-nni.

Win i ixeddem di tudert s IZEN-IS, ad yexdem s ḤMIL ṣṣeḥ akk d mebla TAMEƔRUT.

LḤMIL ilaq ad ibeddel i TAMEƔRUT. IZEN d ayen i nḥemmel ṣṣeḥ, lexlaṣ-nni i nexdem s lfeṛḥ axaṭer d ayen i ɣ-yeɛjeben, ayen i NḤEMMEL.

Di tudert i d-yeḍra aɣrib, lɣaci xeddmen s urif akk d ɣef tameɣrut axaṭer xeddmen ixedmat ur yezmin ara s tmeɣrut-nsen.

Mi nxeddem di wayen i ɣ-yeɛjeben, di tmeɣrut-nneɣ ṣṣeḥ, ttnaɣen-t s LḤMIL axaṭer ḤEMMLEƔ tmeɣrut-nneɣ, axaṭer TUDERT-NNEƔ i tudert d ṣṣeḥ d tin n tmeɣrut-nneɣ.

Aya d ṣṣeḥ i lxedma n imessawen. Ad ẓren ad ssuɣen inelmaden akk d tnelmadin, ad sufɣen tudertiwin-nsen, ad ten-ssuɣun ɣef webrid n tmeɣrut-nsen ṣṣeḥ.
