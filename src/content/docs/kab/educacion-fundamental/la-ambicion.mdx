Tazmert tesɛa aṭas n sebbat, yiwet seg-sent d ayen iwumi qqaren AGWAD.

Argaz ameɣbun-nni yessizdif icerbuben n yirgazen yesɛan zzhu deg iberdan n temdinin yesɛan ccan, yezmer ad yuɣal d amakar ma yella yugad tigullelt, yugad iman-is, yugad i ussan-is i d-iteddun.

Taḍeffart tameɣbunt-nni yexeddmen deg umagazen ameɣlal n bab n izerfan, tezmer ad tuɣal d tamekart neɣ d taɛekkast deg yiḍ kan, ma yella tuggad ussan-is i d-iteddun, tuggad tudert, tuggad leqdic, tuggad iman-is, atg.

Amesdaɣ yettcekkiɛen deg umezdaɣ neɣ deg uḥerci ameqqran, yezmer ad yuɣal d GANSTER, d amezzegar n ibanki, neɣ d amakar yesɛan lfen, ma yella, aɣrib, yugad iman-is, yugad amkan-is ameɣbun n umesdaɣ, yugad ayen ara yeḍrun yid-s, atg.

Yettwafḍen ameẓẓuɣ yebɣa ad yili d aqeɛɛad. Asexdam ameɣbun-nni yettɛassan ɣer yimura yeqbalen, i d-yesskanayen yeqbalen yeqqarben, t-tcim, yezwiyen, yettɛeggiḍ aṭas yettḥellu-d s tewlaɣt, yebɣa ayen nniden acku yella yugad, aṭas n ugwad, yugad tiɣellalt, yugad ussan-is i d-iteddun d tid yettṭeɣɣiren, yugad leqdic, atg.

Tazmert d tameqqrant. Tazmert tesɛa udem n uqeddac yerna tesɛa udem n ccitan, udem n wergaz yerna udem n tameṭṭut, udem n wazal yerna udem n ur sɛin ara wazal, udem n uɛaqil yerna udem n umekkak.

Tella tazmert deg win yebɣan ad yezweǧ yerna deg win yettɛicin d imezzeb ad yettgallaḥ izweǧ.

Tella tazmert deg win yebɣan s lehmum d ameqqran "AD YILI AYEN", "AD YILI", "AD YALI" yerna tella tazmert deg win yuɣalen d aɛzri, ur yebɣi acemma seg ddunit-a, acku tazmert-is kan d ad yaweḍ ɣer IGGENNI, AD YEKREḤ, atg.

Llant tazmrat n ddunit yerna llant tazmrat n leṛwaḥ. Kra n tikwal, tazmert tettawi tasekkirt n UR SƐIN ARA WAZAL d TIFFAɣA.

Win ur yettazmer ad yili deg ddunit-a tayemmat d TAFRAḤT, yettazmer ad yili deg tin nniden yerna win ur yettazmer ad yesɛu arak, yettazmer ad yesɛu IMENɣAN IḌUREN.

Anect-a, iman-iw, iman-is, yeɛǧeb-it ad yeffer tazmert, ad tt-yerr deg yimukan yeffren aṭas deg uɣesmar yerna yenna-d: "UR TTAZMERƔ ARA ƔER ACEMMA", "ḤEMMLEƔ IMDANEN-IW", "XEDDMƔ TALAḤTILT I ṚṚEṚF ISERMANEN N YIMDANEN".

ASETṬAḤ YEKERHEN yettxebbiɛ deg iberdan, kra n tikwal, iɣerraben s leɛwaj-is ur sɛin ara wazal, meɛna m'ara yeǧǧ amkan, tewɛer ad yeffeɣ seg tmurt-is s kra n yimelyaren n ḍular.

Tazmert yettwassnen s TAKSEKKIRT N UR SƐIN ARA WAZAL, tettawi deg leɣlaḍ i yimdanen yessnen.

Llant deg ddunit aṭas n yimdanen kan azmuren ur yettili d azmar.

Aṭas n yimdanen yeṭṭeqen ɣer yimuras d yezwiyen n ddunit acku kan azmuren AD SELHIN IMANEN-NSEN IWEJDIREN.

Uɛaqil yettɛeddan ɣef tuyat ɣer tmezgida yerna yettḥebbiɛ iman-is s liman meqqren, ur yettazmer ɣer acemma, meɛna yettɛedday-d s lxir ad yefk ur yeqqel seg yiwen, meɛna yettɛedday-d s lxir ad yili UMƐEDDIB, asellek, tẓur deg iman-is neɣ deg yiwen seg twacult-is, neɣ, ad yessufeɣ iman-is i lebda.

Nekni nhegga i yirgazen d tlawin n tidett yeɣran, meɛna nḥuzzu acku ur hemmlen ara din-nsen s UR SƐIN ARA WAZAL.

Ddina yefsusen, tarbaɛtin yefsusen, taɛeqqubt, tuddsa n leṛwaḥ, atg. Wwaɣḍen ad nhemmel s UR SƐIN ARA WAZAL.

Yewɛer aṭas ad naf deg ddunit-a yiwen umdan yhemmel din-is, aɣerbaz-is, tarbaɛt-is, atg. s UR SƐIN ARA WAZAL. Ayen-a d ayen yeɛzzen.

Akk ddunit teččuṛ d tazmrat. Hitler yeṭṭef-d ṭrad s tazmert.

Akk imezɣanen sɛan aẓar deg ugwad d TAZMERT. Akk temsalin timeqqranin deg tudert sɛan aẓar deg TAZMERT.

Akk ddunit tɛic deg imnaɣ mgal akk ddunit s sebba n tazmert, kra mgal kra yerna akk mgal akk.

Akk umdan deg tudert YETTAZMUR AD YILI AYEN yerna imdanen n wessur, ustaḍen, imawlan n twacult, iselmaden, atg. ttkattren i warrac, i teqcicin, i tmeɣratin, i ilemẓiyen, atg. ad ddun deg iberdan yeɣzen n TAZMERT.

Imeqqranen qqaren i yinelmaden, yinelmaden, ilaq ad tilim d ayen deg tudert, ad tuɣalem d ayen s araken, ad tezweǧem yimdanen isɛan arak, ad tilim d ameɣlal, atg. atg.

Tizlatin tiberkanin, tid yellan d tiqdimin, bɣant tilizlatin timeɣradin ad ilint daɣen d tizmar, d tiberkanin, d tid yellan d tiqdimin am nutenti.

Ayen iweɛren aṭas deg waya, d akken imdanen imeqqranen ttaǧǧan "AḌEN" yerna ttaǧǧan ad ttunḍun deg webrid-nni yewɛren n TAZMERT.

Iselmaden ilaq ad slemden i YINELMADEN d YINELMADEN d akken ur yelli yiwen lxedma yehwan yettawḍen ɣer teqɛut, yewɛer ad nwali s teqɛut aḥeffar n texsi, asexdam n wurar, afeḥḥam, yessizdif icerbuben, atg.

Akk lxedma taɣerfant d tecbuḥt. Akk lxedma taɣerfant d ayen yessenqed deg tudert taɣerfant.

Ur d-nlu ara akk ɣer yimuhendisen, imaɣalen, iselwayen, iselmaden, iqeffanen, atg.

Deg tuddsa taɣerfant, ilaqen akk lxedma, akk tmusniwin, ur yelli wa yesɛan lḥeqq ad yehzen.

Deg tudert tagnarant, yal yiwen umdan yeṭṭef i kra yerna ayen iweɛren d ad yessin yal wa ɣer wacu yeṭṭef.

D wajib n YISELMADEN d YISELMADEN ad affen TAƐLIMT n yal yiwen unelmad yerna ad ttunḍun deg wagi-nni.

Win ara ixeddmen deg tudert s TAƐLIMT-IS, ad ixeddem s LEḤMULIḤ N TIDETT d UR SƐIN ARA TAZMERT.

LḤEMLA ilaq ad ttbeddel TAZMERT. TAƐLIMT d ayen iɛejben fell-aneɣ n tidett, d tmusni-nni i nxeddem s lferḥ acku d ayen iɣ-iɛejben, ayen NEḤMEM.

Deg tudert tamirant, s ṣṣeḥ, imdanen xeddmen s uran neɛǧeb yerna s tazmert acku ḍefren lxedma ur yezdiɣen ara d taɛlimt-nsen.

M'ara nexdem deg ayen iɣ-iɛejben, deg taɛlimt-nneɣ n tidett, nexdem-t s LEḤMULIḤ acku NEḤMEM taɛlimt-nneɣ, acku IMƐANEN-NNEƔ ɣer tudert d ayen i zedɣen d taɛlimt-nneɣ.

D ayen i yexdem ustaḍ. Ad yessinen ad ttunḍun yinelmaden-nsen, inelmaden-nsen, ad affen imɛanen-nsen, ad ttunḍun iberdan n taɛlimt-nsen n tidett.
