Ila ilaq ad nḥemmel u ad nețwamel, maca ɣef țameɣṛa n ddunit medden ur ḥemmlen ur țwammalen.

Ayen iwumi qqaren tayri d ayen ur nessin ara medden u țɣelliten-t s tmuɣli d lxuf.

Lemmer ad zmiren medden ad ḥemmlen u ad țwammalen, imenɣan ad uɣalen d leḥwayeǧ ur yezmir ad d-yili fell-aɣ deg ddunit.

Aṭas n zzwaǧen yezmer ad ilin s sεada, aɣbel ur llin akka imi llan leḥqed n zik i jemεen deg umekti.

Lemmer ad sɛun yemdanen imeqqranin ul, ad ttuɣ leεtab n zik u ad εicen s tefreḥ, ččuṛen d sεada taḥeqqanit.

Lεeqel ineɣ tayri, yesnegri-tt. Taseḍwiyin, leɣḍub n zik, tismin n zik, akkagi jemεen deg umekti, yesnegri-tt tayri.

Aṭas n tulawin yeɣḍeben zemrent ad ilint s sεada lemmer ad sɛunt ul meqqren akken ad ttunt ayen yeεeddan u ad εicent deg wass-a i tḥemmelen argaz-nsent.

Aṭas n yirgazen zemren ad ilin s sεada s tulawin-nsen lemmer ad sɛun ul meqqren akken ad semmḥen ɣef tuccḍiwin n zik u ad ttuɣ leεyaḍ d leḥwayeǧ ur neεǧib jemεen deg umekti.

Ilaq, ẓerzur i zzwaǧen ad fehmen lmeεna teɣleq n lweqt.

Irgazen u tulawin ilaq ad ḥussen iman-nsen am wid i ɛerṣen deg wass-a, ad ttuɣ ayen yeεeddan u ad εicen s tefreḥ deg wass-a.

Tayri d leḥqed d leḥwayeǧ ur țemyaεqalen ara. Deg tayri ur yezmir ad yili leḥqed n wudem ma d wudem. Tayri d asemmaḥ n dayem.

Tella tayri deg wid iḥussen leɣben ḥeqqani ɣef leεtab n imdukal-nsen d iɛdawen-nsen. Tella tayri taḥeqqanit deg win ixedmen s wul-is akk ɣef lfayda n yemɣaren, n igellilen, n wid yeḥwaǧen.

Tella tayri deg win iḥussen s tikli d tsegrit i ufellaḥ i yesruqun iberdan s uɣersiw-is, i uɣerfan i yețεezzben, i uɣellay i d-yesuter ṣṣerf u i wuccen imeẓẓi yeṭṭuqen yețmețaten s laẓ deg ubrid.

Mi ara nεiwen ɣef wul-nneɣ akk yiwen, mi ara nεiwen s tikli d tsegrit tajṛa u nesqi tiǧeǧǧigin n tmazirt bla ma ɣ-d-yessuter yiwen, tella tmusni taḥeqqanit, tella tsegrit taḥeqqanit, tella tayri taḥeqqanit.

Aɣbel ɣef ddunit, medden ur sɛun ara tmusni taḥeqqanit. Medden țḥebbiṛen kan ɣef lfayda n yiman-nsen iḍuban, aɣwafen, sselɛut, tussniwin, taseḍwiyin, leεtab, leεqayeq, atg. atg.

Deg ddunit llan aṭas n yemdanen, i yesεan kan tmusni yakreb. Tella tmusni yakreb deg asertay i yesewḥayen, deg wuccen ilaqen ad d-yawi ṣṣut i yessrufun idrimen iwakken ad yawi sselɛut, lqima, amkan, cceḥmat, atg., atg. Ur ilaq ara ad nɣelleḍ aɣersiw d taɣaṭ.

Tmusni taḥeqqanit d tin yellan kan ɣef wul, maca tella tmusni yakreb i yeqqaren ad yawi cceḥmat s tγellit d wuccanen n tsertay, d yiqeṭṭayen ikapitaliyen, d yexrifen ireqben tameṭṭut, atg., atg.

Ilaq a nili d imeqqranin ul. Tmusni taḥeqqanit mačči n lεeqel, tmusni taḥeqqanit d lɣiṭa n wul.

Lemmer ad sɛun medden tmusni, ad ttun akk leḥqed jemεen deg umekti, akk taseḍwiyin n leεtab n aṭas n uzekka u ad lemden ad εicen seg lweqt ar lweqt, dayem s sεada, dayem d imeqqranin ul, ččuṛen d tidett taḥeqqanit.

Aɣbel nekk d umekti u yețεici deg ayen yeεeddan, yebɣa ad yuɣal dayem ɣer ayen yeεeddan. Ayen yeεeddan yesnegri medden, yesnegri s sεada, ineɣ tayri.

Lεeqel yeqnen deg ayen yeεeddan ur yezmir ad yefhem s lekmal lmeεna teɣleq n lweqt i nețεici.

Aṭas n yemdanen i ɣ-d-yuran țqelliben ɣef usseḥmu, țessutren adɣaɣ aqdim iwakken ad sseḥlun ul-nsen yezḍen, maca uẓil fell-asen win yețḥebbiṛen ɣef umeɣbun.

Aṭas n yemdanen i ɣ-d-yuran ad ɣ-d-ḥkun ḥala i deg llan, maca d uẓil fell-asen win i yeqqimen d aɣrum i ad t-yečč ad yeqsem-it akked wiyaḍ.

Ur bɣin ara medden ad fehmen belli deffir n kra yellan yella ssebba u s beddel n ssebba ad nebeddel ayen yellan.

Nekk, nekk-nneɣ i neḥemmel, d tazmert i yeεeddan deg lejdud-nneɣ u d-yebdan kra n ssebbat n zik ayen yellan tura i yesɣarayen tmeddurt-nneɣ.

Neḥwaǧ TMUSNI iwakken ad nebeddel ssebbat u ad nebeddel ayen yellan. Neḥwaǧ tmusni iwakken ad nesseḥyu s ṣṣwab uɣerrabu n tmeddurt-nneɣ.

Neḥwaǧ tmusni iwakken ad nebeddel tmeddurt-nneɣ s lekmal.

Tmusni taḥeqqanit mačči n lεeqel. Tasegret taḥeqqanit d leḥnana taḥeqqanit, ur zemrent ara ad ilint s lxuf.

Ilaq ad nefhem belli lxuf yesnegri tasegret, yesnegri tmusni n wul u yesnegri deg-nneɣ lɣiṭa iεeqqen n TAYRI.

Lxuf d juḍa n akk aɣbel, d ssebba yeffren n akk imenɣi, d ssem i yețțawwin iqerruyen iyesnegriyen u ineɣen.

Yiselmaden d yiselmadtiyin n uɣerbaz d yesdawiyen ilaq ad fehmen ẓerza n ussiweḍ n inelmaden-nsen d inelmadtiyin-nsen deg ubrid n tmusni taḥeqqanit, lqima, d tidett n wul.

Medden iḥerqan iḍuban n teswiεt yeεeddan, mačči ad fehmen ayen yellan ssem n lxuf, rebbun-t am tajeǧǧigt n tewsemt. Ayen i d-yerran iḍuban d aɣbel d lmeṛḍa.

Yiselmaden d yiselmadtiyin ilaq ad fehmen ssaεa i deg nețεici, ḥala i deg nella u ẓerza n uqamen n tsutatin tijdiḍin ɣef tisekkwin n tikli tujjart i yellan deg uɣrab yes-s tazmert taɣarimant i deg imiren n leɣben d leεtab i yebdan gar tuɣmi n tefkir.

Aselmed bu ṣṣeḥ yeqqim ɣef tsikolojit tujjart u ɣef tikli tujjart, akked uɣrab ajdid n tazmert n tazwara tajdiṭ.

Lmeεna n lemεawna ad tessusem akk imenɣi iweḥci n teɣṛeq tmeεṭuṭ. Iṭṭef-aɣ-d fell-aɣ ma nezmer ara ad nemεawan mi nseɣẓen leqrar n tmusni taḥeqqanit tuǧǧart.

Zerzur ad nefhem s lekmal, mačči kan deg uɣrab n lεeqel, maca daɣen deg uɣraben i yeffren deg lεeqel ur nessin u lεeqel ur nessen ayen yellan d uẓẓen n tmusni d weḥcic n teɣṛeq. Mi ara nessen ayen yellan deg-nneɣ d teɣṛeq u d uẓẓen n tmusni, ad d-tekker deg ul-nneɣ lɣiṭa iεeqqen n TAYRI TAḤEQQANIT u n TMUSNI TAẒEẒT i yellan mačči n lεeqel.
