Yennayer seg imuras meqqren n tussna tamentilt d ad awḍen ɣer TADUKLI TAMEQQRANT.

Lemmer YIWWEN ad yili YAL WA, ugur n TADUKLI TAWSENMET ad yefrun s tmusni tameẓẓlant, maca ɣef ussexser n umadal YIWWEN yella deg yal amdan deg talɣa ILEGANEN.

YIWWEN ILEGANEN d sebba tunṣibt n temlilit-nneɣ taberkant.

Lemmer nzemmer ad nẓer iman-nneɣ deg lemri n tfekka-nneɣ akken nella WSENMET, s temlilit-nneɣ taberkant, ad nɣaweḍ ɣer taggara yeḥzenen n ula nettkel ɣef tiddukla n tidet.

Tafekka n umdan d takerrust tameqqrant tettwaqader sɣur YIWWEN ILEGANEN i yettwaɣren s waṭas sɣur TUSNA TAMENTILT TARUGET.

Ad ɣreɣ aɣmis yeqqar YIWWEN IMUSNAWEN; Ad hadirɣeɣ ɣer tmeɣra yeqqar YIWWEN UMARUS; ɣer LƐEFRIṬ s tmeɣra yeqqar YIWWEN N TMOVEMENT, yif-it ad d-zzeɣ ad lewwzeɣ, YIWWEN ur yebɣi ad yezzi yeqqar YIWWEN N UMMERD, ḍer-i laẓ ad ččeɣ, atg.

Yal yiwen seg YIWWEN IMECṬAḤ i yesbedden TMEKTA, yebɣa ad yefreq, ad yili d ameqqran, d agellid.

S tafat n tussna tamentilt taruget nezmer ad nefhem belli YIWWEN d ileganen, Tafekka d takerrust.

YIWWEN IMECṬAḤ ttaɣen wagar gar-asen, ttekaren ɣef tmehbelt, yal yiwen yebɣa ad yili d aqerru, d agellid, d ameqqran.

Ayagi yessufeɣ-d lḥala n wemḥeq tawsenmet deg i yettidir aɣersiw iɛeqqlen iwumi qqaren D AMDAN.

Ilaq ad nefhem ayen yeɛnan awal n UMEMḤEQ deg TUSNA TAMENTILT. Ad yemḥeq d ad yeɣleb, ad yefreq, ad yeqqerreq, ad yeḍfer, atg.

Sebba tameqqrant n UMEMḤEQ AWESNMET d isser i d-yettbinen s tiddi iɛebbanen d yeshlalen.

Isser d ameɣbun, llan imeɣban n tnekra i t-yessefhem. Isser d aserɣes aberkan n yal takerrust tamettit. Iɣeslanen ttennunun ad ssefhmen isser.

Aɛziz yesser ɣef aɛziz yebɣa ad yili d aɛziz. Igellilen yessen ɣef iɛezzizen bɣan ad ilin d iɛezzizen. Win yura yesser ɣef win yura yebɣa ad yaru yif-it. Win yesɛan tajriba yeser ɣef win yesɛan tajriba meqqren yebɣa ad yesɛu ugar-t.

Ayagi akk d aɣbel. Ula d ayagi yezmer ad yawi lferḥ n tidet i wul yeɛyan. Ayagi akk yettaweɛ zzyada n timeqqunt deg tnefsit-nneɣ, timɣurin n uɣbel, anegmu yal ass d ugar.

YIWWEN ILEGANEN yettak-d tazmert ɣer wallalen uɣriben, ɣer usser-agi, ad yezzi, ad yejemmeɛ, ad yeṭṭef, ɣas ma yella s wallalen n umahil n wayeḍ, isem-is timɣurin, timlilit, tawekt.

Lɣaci ttidiren s tussna yeṭṭsenen ur ẓeren ara belli d isser, d timeqqunt, d akerriḥ, d asenqad, dɣa m'ara d-yasen fell-asen ayagi akk, ad ssefḥem, ad ḥekkem, ad tanin, maca ur fehmen ara.

Amahil n tfekka akk yella ɣef YUGAR. YUGAR d aserɣes aberkan n isser.

YUGAR d amḍiq amesmir n tfekka. Yal amḍiq amesmir d AɛEZZBAN. Amdg.: D ugar iɛeqqlen seg-k. Fulani seg-k yugar tikkilin. Fulana tefɣ-kem, ugar tmusni, ugar lɣir, ugar teẓẓi, atg. atg.
