Adɣal yettcameɛniɣen ṭṭbiɛa n tameẓriwt d tmusni neɣ d tifratin, ɣur win yeqqaren ɣur-s tmusni neɣ tifratin, ad as-tinin d win yesɛan tameẓriwt.

Nekkni nerra-d belli tameẓriwt ɣur wemdan, ur yelli fell-as fell-an, d tajreɣt n tmusni n zdaxel-ines, teggugem fell-asen imḍiqen n walluy.

Tettarra tameẓriwt i wemdan ad yessen iman-is.

Tettarra tameẓriwt i wemdan tmusni s wayen yellan, anda yella, s wayen yessnen s tideɣ, s wayen ur yessin s tideɣ.

Tessalay tamsikulant tanekref n tmusni belli d amdan kan i yezmer ad yessen iman-is.

Nekkni kan i yezmer ad yessen ma yella yesɛa tameẓriwt deg yiwen n tegnit neɣ ala.

Nekkni kan i yezmer ad yessen tameẓriwt-ines ma tella deg yiwen n tegnit neɣ ala.

Amdan kan ur yelli wiyaḍ i yezmer ad iẓer deg yiwen n lawan belli uqbel lawan-nni, ur yesɛi tameẓriwt, tella teṭṭes, imiren ad yettu tagmert-nni neɣ ad tt-iḥrez am yettḥarzen tagmert yesɛan lqima.

Yella urgaz ad nessen belli tameẓriwt ɣur aɣersiw yesɛan aqlal ur telli teɣzi.

Tameẓriwt ɣur aɣersiw yesɛan tmusni i wumi qqaren amdan, teṭṭes s waṭas.

Drus, drus waṭas lawan anda tameẓriwt teqqar-d, aɣersiw yesɛan tmusni yeṭṭef axeddim, yesrewel tiɣerḍa, yesseqɛad, yemmut, atg. Tameẓriwt-ines teṭṭes deg lawan anda i teqqar-d.

Tudert n wemdan d tudert n targit, maca ittetmen belli yeqqar-d ur yettqadar ara belli ittaragi, belli tameẓriwt-ines teṭṭes.

Ma yella wiyaḍ yeqqar-d, ad iḥissu s leḥya, ad yefhem seg-s tilawin-is.

Tudert-a d tilawin, d tuggdiwin, d tissas.

Ma yella win yessawen, ad yesserwel abrid ar tɣemmra, ɣur aɣlan yellan deg tidi.

Anda amdan yeqbel belli tameẓriwt-ines teṭṭes, ad tafeḍ yessefraq-ed.

Tiwriqiwin timenzayin n tsikulant taɣurbast i yettekren tilawin n tameẓriwt, tḥussunt ɣur-sent tadfi n ṭṭes. Irgazen-nsen tṭessen s waṭas deg tallit ideggren.

Win yettcameɛniɣen tameẓriwt d wallalen n tmusniwin, ixemmem, iḥuss, iḥettem iḍarren, ḥussen s tilawin, tṭessen s waṭas.

Win yettqadaren tilawin n tameẓriwt maca tekren tirbitin n tmeẓriwt, teqqaren teḍḍer tagmert n tameẓriwt, tṭessen n tameẓriwt.

Win yeqqar-d deg lawan, yessen s tagmert-ines belli yella tirbitin n tameẓriwt yettafeɣ-tt seg-s.

Amezwaru lawan. Acḥal lawan anda neqqar-d s tameẓriwt?

Sin teɣzi. Acḥal n tikkal anda neqqar-d tameẓriwt?

Krad. TAMEKDAḤT D TRUẒI. S wayen yesɛa tameẓriwt?

Tsikulant tanekref d Philokalia n zik tettarra belli s iɣallen-nni n wazal yesɛan azamul yesɛan amur deg-sen, yezmer ad yeqqar-d tameẓriwt ad tt-yeǧǧ teɣzi ad tt-iqader.

Aselmed d azgen yesɛan tadfi ad tqaren-d tameẓriwt. Ur sɛin ara lqima cṛa neɣ meṛẓuq iseggasen n tira deg uɣerbaz, deg ulɣu neɣ deg taseddawit, ma nra ad nexleq seg uɣerbaz am wemɣar yeṭṭsen.

Ur d leɣḍer ara ma nerra-d s yiwen n uɣal yezmer aɣersiw yesɛan tmusni ad yeqqar-d s iman-is deg snat n tseddaṭ.

Yettban belli deg tura yella iseffunen i d-nlaq ad ten-neɣreɣ s uɣerruj n Diogenes, iseffunen-a yella deg-sen Irgazen n tideɣ, Buddha, Ɛisa, Hermes, Quetzacoatl, atg.

Iseɣrasen-a n Din yesɛan tameẓriwt teɣzi, llan d Iɣeflan.

Adɣal ulac tameẓriwt deg-sen. Timɣurt n tameẓriwt teɣzi, tekka-d seg tnekra d wallalen n tmusniwin.

Amdan i yettḍebbiɛen tasekla n tirza iwakken ad d-yekker tudert-is, yezmer s tideɣ ad d-yekker acḥal n tikkal yesseqɛad, acḥal n warraw yesɛa, anwi i d yimawlan-is, d imelmaden-is atg., maca ayagi ur yettɛin ara tameẓriwt, aya d tnekra n tigawin ttidgen, ayagi kan.

Ilaq ad nerr ayen nnan deg iḍrisen iɛeddan. Yella arbuɛ n tmura n tameẓriwt. Ţ-Ţidgi, tala, awtal d tameẓriwt tanemhalit.

Aɣersiw yesɛan tmusni yetwaɣleḍ umi qqaren amdan, itteddu deg snat kan n tmura-nni. Yiwen umur n tudert-is ittɛeddi deg ṭṭes neɣ ittusmeɣ-it tala, ayagi ula d ayagi d ṭṭes.

Amdan i yettḍesin d taragi, ittetmen belli yella tala seg tewwurt ar tama, maca s tideɣ deg tama-nni, itetteddu ittaragi.

Ayagi icuba uzzal, itteffen itran ɣef lǧiha n tafat maca qqimen qqimenɣas deg allen ur ten-ttafen ara.

Deg tudert tameɣlalit amdan ur yessin awtal ula tameẓriwt tanemhalit.

Maca adɣal yesɛa zzux ula d amdan yesɛa awtal, aɣersiw yesɛan tmusni ittetmen s lekmal belli yesɛa awtal yettqadar-d, ur yettqadar ara ma nnan-as belli itteṭṭes neɣ yedder deg tidi.

Yella lawananda iseffunen anda aɣersiw yesɛan tmusni yettqadar-d maca lawan-nni d idrusen, zemren ad d-ban deg yiwet n tsedaṭ n tidi, deg yiwen n laman, deg yiwen n tegnit, deg yiwen n tegnit ayen ur yettaraji, atg.

D tuggdi ma aɣersiw yesɛan tmusni ur yesɛa ara tannumi deg tmura n tameẓriwt izeḍmen, ur yezmir ad tent-yemmekti, ur yezmir ad tent-yeǧǧ teɣzi.

Maca aselmed yettarra belli amdan yezmer ad yefren tannumi n tameẓriwt ad yesɛu awtal.

Tsikulant tanekref tesɛa tɣawsiwin iwakken ad tqaren tameẓriwt.

Ma nra ad nqar-d tameẓriwt, ila ad nebdu s unadi, asselmed imiren ad nesfeḍ kullec igellalen deg ubrid, deg idlis-a nesselmed abrid ad nqar-d tameẓriwt seg umɣar n uɣerbaz.
