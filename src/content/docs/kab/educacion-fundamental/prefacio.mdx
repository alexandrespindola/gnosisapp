"Aselmed aqdim" d tussna i ɣ-d-iţţaken afud ad naf assaɣ-nneɣ akk-d yimdanen, akk-d ugama, akk-d yal ayen yellan. S tussna-yagi, neţḥuss amek i yettxeddim uɣil, acku uɣil d allal n tmusni, yerna ilaq a nemẓer ad nseddu allal-nni, i d aẓar alemmas n yiman-nneɣ afsayan.

Deg udlis-agi, s wudem yemmalen, ttuslemden-aɣ amek ara nemeyyez, s umahil n unadi, azenziɣ, tamefhemt d wemseflay.

Yettak-aɣ-d isallen ɣef wamek ara nessiɣti yesmektiyen n tmemri, sseqdac yal ass kraḍ n yiferdasen: amdan, tɣawsa d umkan; tmemri tettnazuɣ s wul, ihi yessefk ad nesɛu ul ɣer wayen nettɣar iwakken ad iţţuɣzen deg uɣil. Tmemri teţţusemɣay s usnerni alkimi i ara ssnen kra kra inelmaden i yesɛan fell-as afud ad snernin iman-nsen.

I umalu, taɣuri tebda di 6 iseggasen, neɣ mi ara yesɛu umdan tamusni; i umalu ugmuḍ, ladɣa Ihindu, tarbiyya tebda seg uzɣar; i Inugnus seg tayri, neɣ uqbel n tdersi.

Tarbiyya i d-iteddun ad tesɛu snat n tegrawin: yiwet s ufus n yimawlan d yiwet nniḍen s ufus n yiselmaden. Tarbiyya i d-iteddun ad tessers inelmaden deg tmusni taruhanit n wamek ara ilin d yimawlan. Tameṭṭut ayen teḥwaǧ d taḥḥettemt, d leḥḍana, ɣef wayagi i tettarew teqcict ɣer baba-s mi tecṭuh acku tetteẓra-t d bab n tezmert; ilemẓi yeḥwaǧ tayri, leɛnaya, d wuzzalen, ɣef wayagi i yettarew ilemẓi ɣer yemma-s s tmuɣli takmamant. Ḍefir, mi ara tenɣel tmuɣliwin n snat-nsen, tameṭṭut tettnadi ɣef yiwen ara tt-iɛejben neɣ daɣen argaz ara tt-iḥemmlen, mi ara ili d nettat i ilaq ad tefk tayri, d ilemẓi yettnadi ɣef tameṭṭut i yesɛan aɣbalu n tudert neɣ i yesɛan tmerrit; i wiyaḍ, tettwaqdec teskert d teɣri n tfekka i tmuɣliwin-nsen.

Yeţɛeğğib ad nẓer iḍrisen n yiɣerbazen, yal adlis yesɛa alaf n yesteqsiyen, i iţarra umeskar s tira iwakken ad ten-ḥefḍen yinelmaden deg uɣil, uɣil ucḥan d amḍiq n tmusni i ɣef ttnadin yilemẓiyen s wayes; tarbiyya-nni d tagnayt tewzel tenneḍfen ad rebḥen tudert mi ara fakken taɣuri, maca ɣef tudert i deg ara ddren ur ssinen acemma, ad kcemmen ɣer-s d iderɣalen, ur sen-uslemden ara ad rnun tewselt s wudem yettnernin, aselmed-nni yella deg ufus n wid yettidiren deg tili n leḥya.

Ilaq ilemẓi ad yefhem d akken tazzla i d-iţţekken deg tfekka n wemdan, d aferdis aâlayen i tudert n wemdan (tawselt), d ţţberka ihi lɣelluy-ines ad yesderɛer dderya-s. Deg umdaɣ n Tikilsit Takatilit, ttwaḥezzen deg usagri s ccbaḥa tameqqrant tawsit am nettat i tfekka n Lmasiḥ, aseklu-nni yezzayri; yettwakkes-d seg tazzla n yirden. Deg umdaɣ idder, neɣ tfekka-nneɣ tafizikit, tazzla-nneɣ teṭṭef amkan n tewsit taqedsant n Tmasiḥit i teḍfer Lmasiḥ Amzruy; deg tazzla-nneɣ neḥrez Lmasiḥ deg tɣerma wid i yeddfer Lmasiḥ idder i yettidiren deg tlemmast n tazzla-nneɣ.

Nettmuqul s ufus irefden deg-s afud meqqren d akken tarayin i yesɛan ɣur-sen tmusni n yimɣan iqeddcen i wemdan, sselmaden i iɣerraben ad sεun asirem ɣef zzerriεa i ssersen deg yigran, nettẓara amek i semɣaren leqima n zzeraɛ iwakken ad d-teffeɣ tebzert yeshlanen, mi ḥezzen deg yiɣerda n umeɣrus aqquran iwakken ur ţruḥun zzeraɛ i d-yeffɣen s wayes. Nettẓara amek i ḥezzen ɣef yedlawen, i yeddmen deg ufus-nsen amek ara ţsidiren iɣersiwen, ssawḍen ad d-ssuffɣen imesmar neɣ aseqqa i yufan 100 n tikkal afud n uɣrum, ayagi yebɣa ad yini d akken d tazzla i d-ttffɣen, d sebba n azal-nni ɛlayen. Anagar tasnajya tunṣibt, i yeddmen deg ufus-nsen i twselt n wemdan, ur aɣ-d-tenni acemma ɣef usnerni n tazzla; neţḥettem s ṣṣeḥ tɛeggilt-agi, d neţţarra isallen i iqariyen-nneɣ d akken tazzla n wemdan d ţţaɛeqliqt ad tt-nesnerni, mi nseqdec yal ass tlata n yimɣan ineşliyen: s wayen nettmeyyiz, wayen netteffes d wayen netteţ. Ma nettmezyyiz anagar deg lehdur, deg tɣawsiwin ur nfeɛ, ur yesɛin azal, akka ara tili tazzla i d-nettɣimmi, acku amezyiz d aferdis i tt-yettqelleben. Ilemẓi yettɣar yemyal ɣef win ur yettɣur deg wudem d tluga, yella beddel deg tmezdayt; Anezḍad n tuffsatin i tettwaggzen deg yiberdan d teɣriwin, yesɛa azal ɣef tudert n wid i tettilin ɣer imkan-nni: Medden i tettetten tikeḥḥatin, ilef, lbirra, tiḥerciwin, lkulu d yimɣan afrodisiak, ddren tudert tasertant i ten-yettawin ɣer tmeɣra.

Yal aɣersiw amestarti d iheddan: iɣyal, ilef, iɣersiwen d ula d yefṛax n tferxin ɣas ma d ifṛax, am ucekkuɛ n uxxam. Yeshel ad nẓer ifṛaq yellan gar imestartiyen d wid wemdan yeqerḥen s tezmert iwakken ad ten-yeẓlem, walit tgemmuεin n uɛewdiw n tmezwart ɣer iɛewdiwen n uqerrac, gar izgaren n temnaɣt d imesmar i d-yeţeffɣen yal ass seg teɣmirt, aberrac neɣ ilef amesmar, ula deg iɣersiwen imecṭuḥen am uɣerdam i d bab n sserta tameqqrant yerna yal ass wudem-is d aweḥci, akka ayella gar argaz amestarti i yettɣummun tissi-s s yiselɣaɣen d lɛeter. Mi yelli wemdan d afeqran, d azedgan d usɣar, deg amezyiz, awal d umahil, yettḥessu ɣef tlemsiţ i yeṛwan, yetţerrni deg tfekka d teṛwiḥt yerna tfekka-s ur tettɣimi ara deg tissi.

Amek ara tili tarbiyya uqbel n tlalit? Ayagi yettdaru gar iɛeqliyen i yeddfer tfeqranit, neɣ ur ttruḥun tazzla-nsen deg tferki d tayri tazurant, akka: Iɛeqliyen bɣan ad fken tfekka i wayen yettlalun d amaynu, msefhamen wway gar-asen yerna ssuturen i Jennat ad ten-sidin i temlilit n tdersi, Ḍefir deg tikli n tayri dimanen d imeqqranen, seqdacen tallit i deg ugama yefreq, amek ayxedmen iɣerraben ad zren, seqdacen ubrid n usnerni alkimi i yemsiwiḍen am urgaz d tameṭṭut, ayagi yettarra afeqqa n usefṛer yeqwan d ucbuḥan, yesnerna s leqraya yellan uqbel yerna tettdaru s ubrid-agi temlilit n tdersi taruhanit, mi ara tḥuss tameṭṭut d akken tella s tadist, teffeɣ seg urgaz, neɣ tudert taɛeqlit tettfakka, ayagi ilaq ad t-yexdem urgaz s ţţaɛeqliqt acku yeččur d lḥerma d tezmert ɣef tɣerma, s yal ubrid yettarra tudert-is d yelhan i tmeṭṭut-is iwakken ur tteddu ara ɣer lxiq neɣ tɣawsiwin itmetaten acku ayagi yekkat ɣef yebbiṭ i yettnernin, ma ayagi yessenɣal mani ara tili tsiwiḍt i xedmen medden s tayri yesɣedlen i ur yessinen acemma deg wamek-agi? Ayagi yettak ssebba i waṭas n warrac ad ḥussen s sserta yesdawalen seg tmeẓyan d wesserɣayen i yemma-tsen s wudem yeqqaren.

Yemma teẓra d akken teţţak tudert i wayen i d-yezgan amaynu i tḥezzeg deg Uḍebsi-s Idder, am tɣawsa yesɛan azal, tettak-as s tẓallit-is d amezyizen-is iɣelben i ara tnernin i ilemẓi amaynu, Ḍefir yettban-d ussan n tlalit war uɣebbaẓ; s wudem yesnen d ugaman i teɣra n yimawlan-is. Iɛeqliyen ḥezzen tamettant i d-iteddun s 40 n wussan alamma tuɣal ɣer umkan-is tarbaεt iqeddcen am tmurt i wayen yellan d amaynu, yessen urgaz d akken tameṭṭut i yetterbin agraw ad tt-iwuzzel d ad tt-iwali, s tulmasin yesnen acku yal wudem yesɣedlen yettara ɣer iẓri n yemma d yettawi ɣer yiberdan i deg ttɛeddayen aman i yettak tudert, i warrac n iɛebbuẓen-is, tameṭṭut i yebɣan ad tesseqdec aselmed-agi ad twali tettenquṣ tissi n uqerrac n yezzan s usnerni. Anda llan tfeqranit tella tayri d tḍuqqna, warrac ṭṭeqqimen s wudem ugaman yerna yal ccer yeḍḍaɛ, akka i tebda tarbiyya-yagi taqdimt i tḥezza n tmezdayt i wayen yellan d amaynu i ara yedda ɣer uɣerbaz i yemsubben ad yeddfer taɣuri ara tt-yerrun ad idder yerna ḍefir ad irebba ɣef yiman-is aɣrum n yal ass.

Deg imezwura 7 n yiseggasen yebni uqcic tmezdayt-is i yiman-is, d ayen yesɛan azal am agguren n tadist d wayen nettṛağu seg wayen yettwawin deg ṣifah aɣbelan d ayen ur nessaramen ara imdanen. Afrux d amahil n Win yellan, ilaq ad nesseɛru Win yellan.

Yiman ur yezmir ad yessen tideţ acku tideţ ur tettili ara i wakud yerna yiman yella.

Taggadit d lxuf sderɛen ifadden ilelli. Tamezwart tettwaxdem, lxuf yeddes.

Nessenziɣ yal tɣawsa d nettmezyyiz, nekkess seg tiṭit tseddet.

Tideţ d ayen ur nessin seg imir ɣer imir, ur tesɛi acemma akk-d wayen yettammen wemdan neɣ ur yettamen ara; tideţ d anwaḍ ad nettwali, ad nedder, ad nefhem.
