Tamacḥuṭ d ayen yettbanen, war ccekk, d ayen i d ixerbiyen akk, i d ayen yesɛan rricwa tameqqrant yellan deg umaḍal.

Tarrayt n tmettant ayen yugaren akk d astehlek n tudert n watmaten-nneɣ.

D ayen yesseɣwan lxuf, win yeṭṭfen tasekurt-is i tqeṭṭil ad ineqq tulmutin tilemmaẓin deg teẓgi, maca alef n tikelt yugar-iten fell-asen, alef n tikelt yugar-iten fell-asen, d win yeqqnen atmaten-is.

Ur nettaṭṭaf kan s tmesrifgin, tisekura, tbuṭuṭin, tpesṭuṭin neɣ tbumatumin tiẓerriyin, nezmer ad neqq s tmuɣli ara yejreḥ ul, tmuɣli i d-isserɣuben, tmuɣli teččur d tiḥeqqirt, tmuɣli teččur d timḥeqranit; neɣ nezmer ad neqq s tigawt ur nezmir ad nessufeɣ fell-as, s tigawt taberkant, neɣ s tuqqa, neɣ s awal yessexṣiren.

Amaḍal yeččur d wid ineqqen imawlan-nsen, wid ineqqen yemma-tsen ur nezmir ad nessufeɣ fell-asen, ineqqen imawlan-nsen, ama s tmuɣli-nsen, ama s awal-nsen, ama s tigawin-nsen yeqquran.

Amaḍal yeččur d yirgazen ur ẓrin ara neqqen tilawin-nsen, d tilawin ur ẓrin ara, neqqen irgazen-nsent.

Akken ad terbuḥ tazzla n twaɣit deg umaḍal-a yeẓẓayen deg-neɣ, bnadem yeqq ayen iḥemmel aṭas.

Ur yettidir bnadem s weɣrum kan, maca daɣen s wayen yemxalafen deg tmuɣli tamsiklawit.

Aṭas n irgazen i yezmer ad ddren aṭas ma ur tessufeɣ ara fell-asen tmeṭṭut-nsen.

Aṭas n tilawin i yezmer ad ddrent aṭas ma ur tessufeɣ ara fell-as urgaz-is.

Aṭas n yimawlan i yezmer ad ddren aṭas ma ur tessufeɣ ara fell-asen arrac-nsen d yessetma-tsen.

Amahlu yewwin win iḥemmlen ɣur-neɣ ɣer uẓekka yesɛa sebba, awal i tqeṭṭil, tmuɣli i yjerḥen, tigawin ur nezmir ad nessufeɣ fell-asen, atg.

Tameddurt-a i tɣuṛ d tḥerrect teččur d wid ineqqen war ma fehmen, ttarran iman-nsen d ilemmaẓen.

Iḥebbsen ččuren d wid ineqqen, maca ayla yellan d ixrebiyen yugar-iten fell-asen, ttarran iman-nsen d ilemmaẓen, ttilin tilelli.

Ulac tarrayt n tmettant tezmer ad tesɛu ssebba. Ma tenqeḍ win i wiyad ur teḥlliḍ ara ugur deg tudert.

Tnegluft ur teḥelli ara ugur. S umzayer n temdinin tilemmaẓin, s uneqq n imelyunen n medden ur teḥlliḍ ara acemma.

Tnegluft d ayen yeqquran, yeẓẓayen, yesseɣwan lxuf, yeḥreqen. Imelyunen n tmessuqeltin tibendmiyin iḍesen, ur fecḥen ara, ttelqint ɣer tnegluft akken ad stehlekn aṭas n tmessuqeltin tibendmiyin ur fecḥen ara.

Aṭas n tikelt, yekfi lɣaci n umaḍal, neɣ ad yuɣal ad yeɣleb yitri deg igenni, akken ad telqiḍ imelyunen n yirgazen ɣer tnegluft.

Tmessuqeltin tibendmiyin ur sɛint ara lḥeqq, ttkennunt s tarrayt taɣurbant mi ara a tent-trewḥent tilisa n teɣriwin n umaḍal.

Lemmer ad ffeɣen medden, lemmer seg uɣerbaz ad sselmden ɣer inelmaden d tlemẓiyin deg ufehem n ayen i d tiḥeqqirt d tnegluft, ad yuɣal wayen-nniḍen, ulac win ara yettlqin ɣer tnegluft, tilisa tinneqqrin n umaḍal ad tent-nexdem s tarrayt yemxalafen.

Tnegluft teṭṭef i ccedd, ɣer tudert n lɣiwan, ɣer umyiggez n wayen yellan d ayen ara kteren, ɣer tɣanimt, ɣer tserḍaɛt, ɣer tmeqqelt n idammen, d ayen ur yettemtawa ara d tameddurt.

Irgazen deg tnegluft akk d ibawaten, d wid iḍaqqaren, d wid yesɛan tilemmaẓin ččurent d tmedalin d wid ibawaten akk, d wid iḍaqqaren.

Win iqsed ad yeqq iman-is yettban-d d ameqran maca d abawaɛ acku iḍaqqar tudert.

Ameqran deg ussan d win iqsed ad yeqq iman-is deg ussan i tɣuṛ, yexdem taɣellist n win iqsed ad yeqq iman-is.

Taɣellist n win iqsed ad yeqq iman-is tesxerbit d taɣella n umezwar.

Ma nmuqel s leɛnaya ɣer tikli n uɛesker deg tnegluft, tikli-s, tmuɣli-s, awal-is, addan-is deg umenɣi, nezmer ad neqqen belli d abawaɛ.

Aselmaden d teselmadin n uɣerbaz, uɣerbaz amenzu, ttasdawit, ilaq ad sselmden ɣer inelmaden-nsen d tlemẓiyin-nsen tideț ɣef tnegluft. Ilaq ad awin inelmaden-nsen d tlemẓiyin-nsen ad tajerben tideț-a.

Ma lɣaci sɛan lḥeqq ɣef ayen yellan d tideț tameqqrant n tnegluft, ma aselmaden d teselmadin ssellmen ɣer imelmaden-nsen d tlemẓiyin-nsen, ulac aɣella ara yettwaɣen ɣer tamaɛra.

Aɣerbaz ila ad yettunefk deg uɣerbaz amenzu akk, deg uɣerbaz amenzu alemmas d ttasdawit, acku d seg uɣerbaz, ideg ilaq ad nexdem i talwit.

D ayen iɛjlen akken ad ǧǧen tsuta tajdiṭ ad ffeɣen ɣer ayen yellan d tɣellist d tnegluft.

Deg uɣerbaz, uɣerbaz amenzu, ttasdawit, ilaq ad nefhem tiḥeqqirt d tnegluft deg wayen akk yesɛa.

Tsuta tajdiṭ ila ad fehmen belli wid iɣuṛan s tmuɣli-nsen ț-țerkawin, ttqedimen ɣer ilemmaẓen, ttawin-ten am ugenduz ɣer tamaɛra.

Ilemazyen ur ilaq ad țwazelɣen s tɣawsiwin n tnegluft, neɣ s ssebba n wid iɣuṛan, acku ɣer ssebba nniḍen tella ssebba nniḍen, ɣer rray nniḍen yella rray nniḍen, maca ulac deg tɣawsiwin neɣ rrayen d tideț ɣef tnegluft.

Wid iɣuṛan sɛan imelyunen n ssebbat akken ad ddmen tnegluft, akken ad awin ilemmaẓen ɣer tamaɛra.

Ayen i d-yehmen mačči d ayen yettuwexxer ɣef tnegluft, maca ad tajreb tideț n wayen yellan d tnegluft.

Ur nettmeslay ara mgal tɣawsa neɣ mgal useḥṛef, neqqar kan ilaq ad tajreb tideț ɣef tnegluft, syen nezmer ad naɛzel iman-nneɣ ad nsemḥassef, ad nseḥṛef.

Ur nezmir ara ad tajreb tideț n ur nneqq, ma neɣleq tameɣṛa tilemẓit.

Tameɣṛa kan tilemẓit tezmer ad taweḍ ad tajreb tideț ɣef tnegluft.

Aselmaden d teselmadin ur ilaq ad fken kan taɣewsa s unekcum ɣer inelmaden-nsen d tlemẓiyin-nsen. Aselmaden ilaq ad sselmden ɣer inelmaden-nsen ad xedmen aql, ad tajreb TIDET.

Lǧens-a i tɣuṛ d tḥerrect ur yettxemmem ara ad ineqq. Ayen-a n u neqq d u neqq, d ayen yettilin deg lǧens n bnadem.

S tilifizyu d ssineima, irgazen n taɣellist ttaɣen tixemmemin-nsen n tumukin.

Arrac d teqcicin n tsuta tajdiṭ ttafen kull ass s tilifizyu, s tullisin n warrac, s ssineima, tazɣunt atg., tazɣunt tameqqrant n leɛqel iqettel, tira n tmesrifgin, tumukin yesselhuɣen atg.

Ur nezmir ad nexdem tilifizyu war ma nufa awal ččuren d timḥeqranit, tmesrifgin, taɣella.

Ulac ayen xeddmen rruḥmat n umaḍal mgal uɣuni n taɣellist.

Iqelɛen n warrac d ilemazyen ttwawin sɣur irgazen n taɣellist, s ubrid n tumukin.

Yeɣleb rray n u neqq, yeffeɣ s ssineima, s tullisin atg. yuɣal yemɛawan i yal yiwen.

Wid yefɣen deg wussan-a i tɣuṛan ttusellmeden i tumukin ttaṭṭafen akken ad stehlekn, ttferriḥen mi ara walin wiyaḍ mmuten. Akken asen-sselmden deg tilifizyu n uxxam, deg ssineima, deg tullisin, deg tazɣunt.

ɣer yal tama tɣaleb taɣellist, ulac ayen xeddmen rruḥmat akken ad heggen taɣella seg uẓur-is.

Ilaq aselmaden d teselmadin n uɣerbaz, uɣerbaz amenzu alemmas, ttasdawit, ad ṭsen s leɛli, ad rwen igenni d tmurt akken ad seggmen amahlu-a deg uɣerbaz.

D ayen iɛjlen aselmaden d teselmadin n uɣerbaz, uɣerbaz amenzu alemmas, ttasdawit, ad ṭsen s leɛli ad ssutren ɣer rruḥmat n umaḍal akken ad ḥebbsen ssineima, tilifizyu atg.

Tumukin ttarun s lɛali acku tilisa-nni akk n idammen ddan am akken netteddu ad yessiwel wass ideg ulac win ara yettidir deg iberdan s tilelli war ma iḍaqqar ad yemmet.

Lradio, ssineima, tilifizyu, tazɣunt n idammen, fkan ad stehlekn tumukin, ǧǧan-ten ad yuɣal ad yeffeɣ deg uqelɛen ur yettaṭṭafen, yeffeɣ ad yejreḥ ad yeqq bnadem nniḍen.

S teɣriwin-nni akk ideg yeffeɣ u neqq, iqelɛen n tid yellan d ilemmaẓin deg u neqq ttmegrawalen yides yerna ar ass-a ttaǧǧan iman-nsen ɣer wa ad stehlekn acku walan deg ssineima neɣ deg tilifizyu.

Aselmaden d teselmadin d wid yessellmaden ɣer medden ttwawexxer acku ilaq ad țnaɣen ɣef tsuta tajdiṭ ad ssutren ɣer rruḥmat n umaḍal ad ḥebbsen tilisa n idammen, akken ad ḥebbsen akk tilisa ɣef wid ineqqen, wid yettakren atg.

Amenɣi n uselmaden d teselmadin ilaq ad yeffeɣ ad yessiwel ɣer tteryust d lbus.

Lǧens n uteryust d win yellan d abawaɛ yerna d tumukin. Uteryust yettqelleɛ kullec i iman-is ad yeqq akken ad yefferḥ lɣaci.

Lǧens n ubus d win yellan d ayla n tumukin, deg ussan ideg ttwajreḥen ad yeqqen ad yefferḥ lɣaci.

Ayla-a n tilisa n idammen d ayen yesserkaben di meyya deg meyya d ayen yesserɣuben iqelɛen ɣer ubrid n tumukin. Ma nebɣa a nnaɣ ɣef talwit n umaḍal, ilaq ad nebdu amenɣi ameqran mgal tilisa n idammen.

Mi ara yettili deg uqelɛu n bnadem kra n tɣawsiwin iɣurbiyen ad tilint tnegluft war ma tegguma.

Deg uqelɛu n bnadem tillin tɣawsiwin yeṣṣurufen tnegluft, tɣawsiwin-nni d timḥeqranit, taɣella deg wayen akk yesɛa, bu yiman, zzɛaf, lxuf, amezɣan n tumukin, rrayat n tnegluft yeffɣen s tilifizyu, s lradio, s ssineima atg.

Tawilast ɣef talwit, imraz n NOBEL N TALWIT yeffeɣ uẓen nnsen mi ara yettili deg bnadem kra n tɣawsiwin tamsiklawin i yeṣṣurufen tnegluft.

Ar ass-a aṭas n wid yellan d wid ineqqen sɛan amraz n NOBEL N TALWIT.
