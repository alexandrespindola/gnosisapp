Talwit ur tezmir ara a d-tas sɣur wallaɣ, acku ur telli n wallaɣ. Talwit d lhiba yeẓẓan n wul ilemman.

Talwit ur telli d ayen n leqdicat, pulis agraɣlan, Tuddsa n Yeɣlanen Yedduklen, Tuddsa n Temrikt, umuɣen agraɣlanen neɣ iɣalen imsedɣalen ittnadin s yisem n telwit.

Ma yella nebɣa tideț n telwit, ilaq a nelmed ad nedder am win yettɛassan deg tallit n umenɣi, yal ass neddukel d win i nettemkel, s wallaɣ yettwaggaden, acku talwit ur telli d ayen n tmuɣliwin tiranin neɣ d ayen n tirga yelhan.

Ma yella ur nelmed ara ad nedder deg tallit n uɛiwez yal akud, abrid yettawin ɣer telwit ad yuɣal d ayen ibɛaden, yeẓẓuɣer, umbaɛd ad yuɣal d ayen iweɛren, ad yawi ɣer abrid yeɣleq.

Ilaq a neɛqel, ẓẓayen ad neẓer belli talwit taḥeqqanit n wul ilemman ur telli d axxam ara nruḥ ɣur-s anda ara aɣ-ttraǧu teqcict yelhan. Talwit ur telli d tmeɣra, d amkan, atg.

Ad neddu deffir n telwit, ad nqelleb fell-as, ad nexdem fell-as leqdicat, ad nennaɣ fell-as s yisem-is, ad nessenɣes fell-as, ad nessenɣes tuddsa akken ad nxeddem fell-as, atg., d ayen ur yesɛi ara lmeɛna, acku talwit ur telli n wallaɣ, talwit d lhiba yelhan n wul ilemman.

Talwit ur tettuzuɣu neɣ tettzenzi, ur nezmir a tt-njemmeɛ s unagraw n uɛiwez, taɣḍent tisufɣanin, pulis, atg.

Deg kra n tmura, iɣalen n tmurt ttddun deg yigran, ttnɣalen tudrin, ttneɣen medden, ttḍeggiren i wid yettaɣen, akk-agi s yisem n telwit. Agemmuḍ n yir-leɛmala-agi d assenqed n ttɛassubt.

Taggugt tessalay-d taggugt nniḍen, tiɛezzitt tessalay-d tiɛezzitt nniḍen. Talwit ur tezmir ara a tt-nexdem, talwit ur tezmir ara a d-tili s taggugt. Talwit kan ara d-tas ɣur-neɣ mi ara nsensi iman-nneɣ, mi ara nesnegger deg ulawen-nneɣ akk imezdayen n taknust i d-yettawin imenɣiyen.

Ma yella nebɣa talwit, ilaq a neẓer, ad nessenqed, ad nwali, ttabla meṛṛa, ur kan d axxam-is.

Talwit tetlal deg-neɣ mi ara nebeddel aṭas deg ulawen-nneɣ.

Ameslay ɣef taɣḍent, tuddsa n PRO PAZ, uɛiwez, atg., d tikta iḍulen, tikta, deg ugaru n tudert, d tikta iḍulen n ttabla meṛṛa n tilin, ur zmiren ara ad frunt amek ara d-tefkent tilufa n telwit deg unagraw-is, meṛṛa d tid iweɛren.

Ilaq a neẓer ttabla deg unagraw-is meṛṛa, tilufa n ddunit d tilufa n umdan; ma yella umdan ur yesɛi ara talwit deg ul-is, tmetti, ddunit ad tedder deg umenɣi.

Iselmaden n uɣerbaz, ilemmasen, tesdawiyin, ilaq ad xedmen ɣef telwit, ma ulac ad ḥemmlen ttɛassubt d taggugt.

D aɣezzfan, d ilaq ad nessenqed i inelmaden n tmetti tamaynut abrid ara teddun, abrid n ul ara yaɣ-yawin s tideț ɣer telwit taḥeqqanit n wul ilemman.

Medden ur zmiren ara ad fehmen ayen i d talwit deg ul-is, ḥemmlen kan ur tettɛedda ara yiwen deg abrid-nsen, ur ttɛezzben, ur ttnɛeqqen, ɣas ma yella ttleɣben s tḥeqqanit n ussegdi d usneqqi d useɣɣaṛ n tudert i watmaten-nsen.

Medden ur ɛeddan ara ɣef telwit taḥeqqanit, yesɛan kan fell-as tiɣriwin ur yesɛan ara lmeɛna, tmuɣliwin tiranin, tikta ixelḍen.

I imaklafen talwit ad tili d ssɛada akken ad ikerḍen ur yettwarek ara sɣur pulis deg abrid-nsen. I wid ittizenzin, talwit ad tili d azamul akken ad ssedɣen tizenzit-nsen deg yal amkan ur yettwarek ara sɣur iserdasen. I wid itenɣalen aɣref, talwit ad tili ad zenzun s yimali aṭas, ad segdhen s yeɣrem d yeffus ur ttwarek ara sɣur iserdasen unṣiben n unabaḍ. I timɣarin talwit, ad tili ad lḥeqqen deg leḥwayeǧ-nsent n ssɛada, ad segdhen akk irgazen s yilelli, ur ttwarek ara sɣur iserdasen n ṣṣeḥḥa neɣ n pulis i wakken ad xedmen deg tudert-nsent.

Yal yiwen yeskar deg wallaɣ-is xemsin n yelf n tmuɣliwin ur yesɛan ara lmeɛna ɣef telwit. Yal yiwen yettnadi ad yeskar deg leḥwayeǧ-is yiman-is s ukbal yesɛan tikta ixelḍen, tamɛellatin, tiɣriwin d tikta ur yesɛan ara lmeɛna ɣef ayen i d talwit.

Yal yiwen yebɣa talwit deg umeknu-ines, s umata ɣer leḥwayeǧ-is, ɣer leɛwayed-is, ɣer leɛwayed ixelḍen, atg. Yal yiwen yebɣa ad yeɣleq iman-is deg ukbal yettḥififen, deg umata, akken ad yedder talwit-is, s umata.

Aɣref yettnaɣ ɣef telwit, yesmennad-itt, yebɣa-tt, maca ur yessen ara ayen i d talwit. Aɣref kan yebɣa ur ttɛezzben, ad xedmen yal yiwen ddiḥ-is deg lehna-ines. Ayen i wumi qqaren talwit.

Ur tettɛawan ara ddiḥ ara xedmen medden, yal yiwen yumen belli ayen ixeddem yelha. Aɣref yettɣama ad yelqeɣ ɣer ibeɣuren. Ma yella aqebac yeɣleb aɣbel, ad yesew akken aɣbel-is ad iḥeqqeq. Ma yella aqebac yeḍsa, ad yesew akken aḍsa-ines ad yeggen. Aqebac daɣen yettwali ayen i d vicios n alcohol. Akka i d akk medden, i yal azag, ttnadin fell-as talɣut, ur yelli win yettwamnen d aqeddaḥ, akk ttxemmemen ɣef tidet d leɛnaya.

Llan aṭas n yettḍurrunen yettwamnen s umata belli talwit d azamul ad ddren ur txedmen ara, deg lehna-nsen d ur țțeɣmiden deg ddunit yellan d leḥwayeǧ n tmuɣliwin tiranin yelhan.

Fell-asent llan aṭas n tmuɣliwin d tikta ixelḍen ɣef telwit. Deg ddunit-agi iweɛren anda nedder: yal yiwen yettnadi talwit-is deg umata, talwit n tmuɣliwin-is. Medden bɣan ad walin deg ddunit talwit n tirga-nsen, aswir-nsen usufɣan n telwit, ɣas akken deg ulawen-nsen yal yiwen yewwi deg ul-is amezdayen n taknust i d-yettawin imenɣiyen, tɛeddiwin, tilufa n yal aẓayer.

I tallit-agi n tmerwitin n ddunit akk win yebɣan ad yettwassen ad yeskar tuddsa PRO-PAZ, ad yeskar lɣerba, ad yuɣal d win ara d-yettnadin talwit. Ur ilaq a nețțu belli aṭas n imsiyasen sserɣen armud n NOBEL n telwit ɣas ma yella llan fell-asen akk adɣaɣ meṛṛa, deg yiwen neɣ deg wayeḍ umeknu fɣen ad neɣen deg tifses kra n medden, mi walan deg tixḍert ad ttwatteɣlen.

Llan daɣen iselmaden taḥeqqaniyen n ulmud yettfeɣen deg yal amkan n tmurt taɣewṣa n usensi n YO. Iiselmaden-agi ẓran s usuddi-nsen belli kan s usensi n Mefistófeles i nețțeɛraq deg ul, ad d-tass ɣur-neɣ talwit n wul.

Mi ara yili deg yal yiwen lkerh, tɛeyni, tismin, leɣḍeb, taɣyul, issegriw, ttɛeyyit, leḥya, atg. atg. ad ilin imenɣiyen ur yettwakteɣ ara.

Nessen aṭas n medden deg ddunit ttɣilen ɣef iman-nsen ad ufan talwit. Mi nessenqed akk medden-agi, nezmer ad nessenqed belli ur ssinen ara talwit, kan qqimen deg yiwen n leɛwayed usufɣan d win yesserɣayen neɣ deg kra n tamɛellatin tusufɣant, atg., maca s tideț medden-agi ur ɛeddan ara ɣef telwit taḥeqqanit n wul ilemman. S tideț medden-agi d imeqqranen kan sskarun talwit-nsen tagensat deg tnedda-nsen s umata ad zmiren ad ɛeddin ɣef TALWIT TAḤEQQANIT N WUL.

D ayen ur yesɛin ara lmeɛna ad nqelleb ɣef telwit deg ukbal ixelḍen n tmennaɛatin-nneɣ, tamɛellatin-nneɣ, isurafen-nneɣ, leḥwayeǧ-nneɣ, leɛwayed-nneɣ, atg.

Mi ara yili deg wallaɣ imezdayen n taknust i d-yettawin tɛeddiwin, dduriyatin, tilufa, imenɣiyen, ur tella ara talwit taḥeqqanit.

Talwit taḥeqqanit tasekka ɣer tilelli yuẓen s tɣiwanin.

Tilelli n wul ilemman tettawi lhiba yelhan n telwit deg ul.

D taɣezzfant ad nefq ɣef tilelli n tdukkli d lhiba n leḥqum.

D taɣezzfant ad nefq ɣef tilelli n awal. Ilaq awalen-nneɣ ad awin deg iman-nsen tuga n tlelli. Ur ilaq ad nesmers awalen ur numifen ara, d wid ur yelhan ara, d wid yezzelen, d wid ur yesɛan ara lmeɛna.

Yal awal ad yili d tazwelt s tideț, yal awal ad yili yeččur d tilelli n umruḥ. D ayen yettɛezzben aṭas ad nemmeslay mi ara ilaq ad neqqim, ad neqqim mi ara ilaq ad nemmeslay. Llan usukim n idammen, llan awalen iweɛren.

Kra n akuden nemmeslay d azag, kra n akuden neqqim daɣen d azag. Yiwen ilaq ad yemeslay mi ara ilaq ad yemeslay, ad yeqqim mi ara ilaq ad yeqqim.

Ur țțaɣen ara iman-nneɣ s awal, acku ayagi d ayen yettwaɛeqlen.

Yal awal ad yettwaɛeqlen uqbel ad nemmeslay acku yal awal yezmer ad d-yawi deg ddunit aṭas n wayen yelhan, aṭas n wayen ur nelhi ara, aṭas n fayda, aṭas n uɣḍal.

Ilaq ad neɛiwed ɣef tmuɣliwin-nneɣ, tiknust, albas d leɛmala n yal aẓayer. Ad ilin tmuɣliwin-nneɣ, albas-nneɣ, amek ara neqqim deg ttabla, amek ara nesɛi leɛwayed deg tɣeṭṭast, amek ara nsawal i medden deg usalay, deg uɣerbaz, deg iberdan, atg. yal akud yettwaččur d tilelli d tumert.

Ilaq ad nefq ɣef tilelli n leḥnana, ad neḥsi tilelli n uẓawan yelhan, ad neḥmel tilelli n tẓuri, ad neḥsi awalen-nneɣ deg tɣiwanin, ulawen d leɛmala.

Tilelli tayeffust kan ara tetlal deg-neɣ mi ara yemmut YO deg unagraw, meṛṛa d tid iweɛren.

Nekwni nella d wid yedɛen, d wid iɣezznen, d wid yeggran mi ara nili deg ul yeddren n YO taknust. Tilelli deg unagraw ur tezmireḍ deg-neɣ mi ara yili YO PLURALIZADO.

Ma nebɣa TALWIT taḥeqqanit ilaq ad nesseɣẓer YO ɣer polvareda cósmica. Akka kan ara tili deg-neɣ tilelli deg ul. Tilelli-nni ara d-yaweḍ ɣur-neɣ tilelli n tayri d TALWIT taḥeqqanit n wul.

TALWIT TADDERD ad d-tawi tɣiwanin deg yiwen, ad isekkes aɣbel, ad yaɣ-ččur d ṣṣeḥḥa taḥeqqanit.

Ilaq ad neẓer belli wallaɣ ur yezmir ara ad yefhem ayen i d TALWIT taḥeqqanit. D aɣezzfan ad neɛqel belli talwit n wul ilemman ur d-tettuɣal ara ɣer-neɣ s lɛeṛṣ, neɣ s umeknu ad nili deg kra n tmetti neɣ tuddsa iqeddcen akken ad nessenɣes PAZ.

Talwit taḥeqqanit tettuɣal ɣur-neɣ deg unagraw ussan d yelhan mi ara nnernu tilelli deg wallaɣ d wul, mi ara nuɣal am iderɣalen d wul yelhan, ad neḥsu akk ayen i d yelhan d ayen iweɛren, akk wayen d yelhan d ayen ur nelhi ara, akk wayen yesɛan ẓẓayen d ayen yettwazzelen.

Ilaq a nnernu tulut i neḍɛen, deg wallaɣ am wul.

TALWIT d ayen ibeɛden, d ayen yebdalen, ur d ayen yettwakksen ara s wallaɣ ur yezmir ara ad yili d agemmuḍ n usirem, agemmuḍ n tikta. Talwit d tuga teẓẓuɣert yellan akken, ur yelhi ur yedɛi, tuga yellan akken ur yezmir ad yemmet, tuga i d-yekken sɣur ABSOLUTO.
