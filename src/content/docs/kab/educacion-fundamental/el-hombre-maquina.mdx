Argaz amekkan d awḥic ameqqran akk deg wemɣar-a n yimṭawen, maca yella-s, TAZWARA dɣa armi d TMEẒẒYA ad yessers iman-is AGellid n UGAMA.

"Cekket iman-ik" "Argaz, ḥess iman-ik". D tamawt ɣlayen taqburt yuran ɣef yiɣerban ur netɣelleb n lǧameɛ n Delfus deg Tmazɣa Taqdimt.

Argaz, awḥic amegrud aɣersan ur nɣelben iman-is am Argaz, yesnulfa-d alef n tmakin iɣbelɣlen iweɛren yerna yessen mliḥ akken ad yesqeddec TMEKKANT, yesseḥwaǧ imalasen ussan d yiseggasen n leqraya d ulmad, maca m'ara d-yas ɣef iman-is, yettu akk ayagi, ɣas akken netta s timmad-is d tamakint iweɛren akteṛ n akk tmakin yesnulfa-d.

Ulac argaz ur yellan d ufulles n tmuɣliwin ur ṣeḥḥan ɣef iman-is, ayen iwɛren akk d akken ur yebɣi ad iḥess akken d tamakint s tidet.

Tamekkant n ufgan ur tesɛi tilelli n umussu, teqqeddec kan s waṭas n teɣḍent d tuccḍiwin n berra.

Akk umussu, tiggawtin, awal, tmuɣliwin, ussan, imussuten, icehhiyen, n tmekkant n ufgan d win yessebba-ten yiswi-nni n berra d waṭas n tsebbitin n daxel ur nḍehher.

Awḥic Aɣersan d aqeccac amegrud yesmeslayen yettuklal i wamekta d tudert, d asekkut idder, yesɛa tazzla taɣurart, d akken yezmer ad YEXDEM, mi ara d-tettban s tideț ur yezmir ad YEXDEM kra.

Teqqimet-d fell-ak, ay ameqqran, asekkut aɣerrabu yeqqimen ɣef umeskan.

Teqqimet-d fell-ak, d akken asekkut-nni yesɛa tudert, yeɛceq, yesmeslay, yettlekku, yettecehhi, yettaɣ tasrawt, atg.

Teqqimet-d fell-ak, d akken asekkut-nni yezmer ad ibeddel imawlan yal lawan. Ilaq-ak ad teqqimed-d fell-ak, d akken yal bab n yemdanen yemxallafen, yesɛa imeqqran-is iman-is, taɣult-is iman-is akken ad yesteqsi, ad iḥess, ad idder, atg., atg., atg.

Bab-a ur yellan yebɣi ad yessufeɣ idrimen ad yettezzen kra n tibeltin, ɣas akken asekkut-nni ad yettwafeg ɣer usnas, bab-nniḍen, azgen n tsaɛa deffir neɣ kra n tsaɛatin deffir, ad yesɛu tamawt yemxallafen ad yessekker asekkut-is ad yettuɣed, ad yettaḍsa, wis kra ad yerr ad yetnaɣ, wis kra ad yerr ad yeɛceq taḍsa, wis kra ad yerr ad yeɛceq tayeḍ, wis kra ad yerr ad yetnaɣ yigara-s ad yesseḥwaǧ aɣyul n tɣalt, wis kra ad yessebdel amkan-is.

S tideț asekkut-nni n udem-nneɣ ur yexdim kra, maca yettaɣ iman-is d win yexdem, yesɛa tazzla n wayen YEXDEM, mi ara d-tettban s tideț ur yezmir ad YEXDEM kra, acku ur yesɛi SER IGLAN.

Berra n tayet-a, akk yettuɣ-d am mi ara ad yeɣli yiɣil, mi ara ad yeqreq usigna, mi ara ad yeqwa yiṭij, maca asekkut amegrud yettaɣ iman-is d win YEXDEM; yesɛa TAZZLA taɣurart d akken akk yexdem-it mi ara d-tettban ur yexdim kra, d imawlan-is i yesteqsan yis-s asekkut aɣerrabu.

Akken ayagi, d awḥic aɣersan amegrud, ay ameqqran, d asekkut aɣerrabu am wayen n udem-nneɣ d ameskan, yettaɣ iman-is d win YEXDEM, mi ara d-tettban ur YEXDEM kra, d aqeccac n weksil d yefsan yellan ɣef LEŽYUN N TUDḌRIN TIZERFANIWIN deg umuɣ ad t-semmin EGU, I-NNEɣ YESSUFEƔN.

LƐEHDI ƐISAWAN yettarra akk tidḍrin-a d TAZNANTIN d isem-is ṣeḥḥan d LEŽYUN.

Ma neqqar d akken I-NNEɣ d ležyun n TZANANTIN ttafen tamekkant n ufgan, ur nețɣelliṭ, akken ayagi.

ARGAZ-TAMEKKANT ur yesɛi TILawt kra, ur yesɛi SER, kan SER TILawt yesɛa TAZMERT AD YEXDEM.

KAN SER yezmer ad yaqq-aneɣ TILawt, kan SER yezmer ad yaqq-aneɣ s ARGAZEN TIDET.

Win yebɣan s tideț ad yeḥbes ad yili d asekkut amekkan, ilaq ad yekkes yal taɣḍent deg umuɣ d aẓaṛ I-NNEɣ. Yal taɣḍent deg umuɣ d tidḍrin tturaren tamekkant n ufgan. Win yebɣan s tideț ad yeḥbes ad yili d asekkut amekkan, ilaq ad yebdu ad yamen, ad yemfel tamekkaniyya-s iman-is.

Win ur yebɣi ad yemfel, ad yaɣ tamekkaniyya-s, win ur yebɣi ad iḥuss ayagi, ur yezmer ara ad ibeddel, d ameɣbun, d amejḥud, yif-it ad yelqeḍ aɣerda ɣer wallaɣ-is ad yerr ɣer tama n lebḥeṛ.

Awḥic Aɣersan d tamakint, maca d tamakint yemxallafen mliḥ, ma tamekkant-a tefhem d akken d TAMEKKANT, ma tettwaḥeqreq mliḥ ma ɣlin fell-as tarrayin, tezmer ad teḥbes ad tili d tamakint ad tuɣal d ARGAZ.

Sdat n wayagi, d ayen yewzen ad neɛqel deg ul ad neɛqel akk iɣallen n wallaɣ, d akken ur nesɛi TILawt, ur nesɛi AMAKAN IGAN AZAYYEL N TƐELMIT, d akken deg lawan yettwaḥettmen nexdem imdanen, deg nniḍen, nexdem tayeḍ; akk yeqqim ɣef taɣḍent taɣḍent tawerɣant deg yal lawan.

Ayen yessebba-d TAZZLA n TADDART d TEKTI N UZMER n UWEḤC Aɣersan d tama n uḥissi yesɛa YIGILEM-IS UɣSAR, seg tama nniḍen isem-is d yismawen-is akked amekta d kra n wid n tɣuri n usnas yettwanɣan deg-s seg TƐELEM, neɣ yettwalqamen s timmad s tukka taɣurart.

Awḥic Aɣersan amegrud ur yezmir ara ad yeḥbes ad YILI D TAMEKKANT, ur yezmir ara ad ibeddel, ur yezmir ara ad yaɣ SER IGLAN TILawt, ad yuɣal d argaz yeḥqen, deg ayen ur yesɛi ara AZAL ad YEKKES MEDIANTE TAƐELMIT DEG ULEB d yexḍan yessuffeɣd-it, ɣer yal taɣḍent METAFISICAS deg umuɣ ad t-semmin EGU, I-NNEɣ, N NEKKINI.

Yal TAMEɣRA, yal UDSEN, yal tuccḍa, yal UQEṢṚ, yal TƐECQIT, yal ACEHHI, atg., atg., atg. Yesɛa taɣḍent-is tameɣrant, yal umuɣ n akk tidḍrin-a d I-NNEɣ YESSUFEƔN n PSICOLOGIA REVOLUCIONARIA.

Akk tidḍrin-a METAFISICAS, akk YOES-a deg umuɣ d tidḍrin UGU, ur yesɛi ara taɣult ɣer gar-asen, ur yesɛi ara tuddma n ɣures kra. Yal taɣḍent deg umuɣ teqqim ɣef tarrayin, abaddu n teɣḍent, ayen yettuɣ-d, atg.

TIBEGGEZT N WALLAƔ tettbeddil deg ini d imelyan deg yal lawan, akk yeqqim ɣef taɣḍent deg yal lawan tettwaɣ-ed wallaɣ.

S TIBEGGEZT n wallaɣ ɣlin deg tuɣda taɣḍent yemxallafen deg umuɣ d tidḍrin UGU neɣ YO PSICOLÓGICO.

Tidḍrin yemxallafen deg umuɣ d YO YESSUFEƔN ttɣimin, ttwaḥeqqen, ad ṣuɣɣen kra n imɣaren deg uɣanim n udem yeqqimen ɣef tlata-nsent, ad ttnaɣent gar-asent, ad ttmeslayent, ad ttwagdalent, atg., atg., atg.

Yal taɣḍent n LEŽYUN yesemmin YO, yal YO ameɣrud, yettaɣ iman-is d akk, EGU TOTAL, ur yesɛi ara tilawt ɣef wanda d netta d azal kan.

Taɣḍent-a ar ass-a teggul taɛecqit ɣer taḍsa, tettwaḥeqqer deffir s taɣḍent-nniḍen ur tesɛi ara kan ayagi deg tayet-a, ɣas akken taɣult n uɣanim terkeɛ ɣer akal, taḍsa amegrud tetteddem.

Taɣḍent-a ar ass-a teggul talɣi ɣer tama, tettwaḥeqqer azekka s taɣḍent-nniḍen ur tesɛi ara kra kan ayagi deg tama-nni, ɣas akken yiwen-nni yettɣimi.

Taɣḍent-a ar ass-a teggul talɣi ɣer GNOSIS, tettwaḥeqqer azekka s taɣḍent-nniḍen teɛzeq GNOSIS.

Imessuten d yimessuta n tɣuri, Ikula d Timdinin, ilaq ad leqqmen adlis-a n TƐELEM ASEKKLAN ad yesɛun azalen ad ḥeqqen yinelmaden d yinelmaden s abrid n TƐEMMI n TƐELMIT.

Ilaq ad fehmen yinelmaden ayen yewzen ad ḥessun iman-nsen deg akk tamurt n wallaɣ.

Ilaq ad yesɛun imeqqranen yewzenen, ilaq ad neɛqel ayen nexdem ayagi ilaq ad yebdu seg yiselmaden n Ikula.

Ur neggal d akken idrimen yewzenen ad nekk, ad nɣellem yiɣerban n wexxam ad nellseɣ.

Ur neggal d akken ilaq taɣuri, taqbaylit, tamezruyt ad nekk idrimen, maca ayagi mačči d akk, ayagi d asekkad.

Ayen yellan d amezwaru, ayen yewzen d akken ad nessen d wanwa nexdem, d acu nexdem, seg wanda nuɣal-d, ɣer wanda nella nɛeddi, d acu iswi n tuddert-nneɣ.

D ayen idemmer ad nkemmel am issekuten imekkaten, imettuten meɣbunin, argazen-tmekkanin.

D ayen ilaq ad neḥbes ad nili kan d tmekkanin, d ayen ilaq ad nuɣal d ARGAZEN TIDET.

Ilaq abaddu aẓaylal ad yebdu mliḥ s TKEKKSA n yal taɣḍent deg umuɣ tettwaḥeqqen d YO YESSUFEƔN.

Awḥic Aɣersan amegrud mačči d ARGAZ, maca yesɛa deg-s deg tallit n uqbel, akk tazmarin ad yuɣal d ARGAZ.

Mačči d azref d akken tazmarin-a ad twesseɛent, d akken ayen yellan d tilawt ad tent-nefk.

KAN MEDIANTE Aɣelluyin IMAJUREN zemrent ad twesseɛent tazmarin-a n ufgan.

Aṭas nesɛa ad nekkes, aṭas nesɛa ad nalqem. Ilaq ad nexdem aglam ad nessen acu yesseḥwaǧ-aneɣ ad nwekkes, acu yesseḥwaǧ-aneɣ.

D tidet d akken YO YESSUFEƔN yeffeɣ, d ayen ulac fell-as uzal, d ayen yettḍuru.

D AYEN YEẒẒẒEN ad nini ilaq ad nwesseɛ kra n tezmert, kra n yisegtal, kra n tezmarin ARGAZ-TAMEKKANT yettarra iman-is ad yesɛu tazmert, maca s tidet MAČČI-YESƐI.

ARGAZ-TAMEKKANT yettaɣ iman-is d akken yesɛa TILawt, TƐELMIT TIBBEEƔT, TEẒMERT TEƐLEM, TAZMERT AD YEXDEM, atg. Ur yesɛi kra deg wayagi.

Ma nebɣa ad neḥbes ad nili d tmekkanin, ma nebɣa ad d-nebbwed TƐELMIT, ad nesɛu TEẒMERT TEƐLEM, TILawt, tazmert AD NEXDEM, d ayen yewzen ad nebddu ad nessin iman-nneɣ ad nessebded YO PSICOLÓGICO.

Mi ara ad YESSESBIDD YO YESSUFEƔN ad yaɣ iman-nneɣ kan SER TILawt.
