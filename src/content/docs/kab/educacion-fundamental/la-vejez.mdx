Arbaɛin iseggasen imenza n tudert aɣ-d-yefkan adlis, tlatin nniḍen ad t-nefhem.

Deg ɛecrin iseggasen, argaz d tawdaṭṭuft; deg tlatin, d izem; deg arbaɛin, d agmal; deg xemsin, d azrem; deg ssiṭṭin, d aydi; deg sebɛin, d ameqqur, deg tmenyin, ala kan taɣect d umalu.

Akud yessegli-d ayen akk yellan: d amsiwḍ yelhan aṭas yettmeslay i yiman-is ula ma ulac win t-yesteqsan.

Ulac ayen yettwaxedmen s ufus n Uɣersiw Afennan, i tettusemman sɣalat d argaz, ara yettwaɣsen sɣur ukud ar taggara.

"FUGIT IRRÉPARABILE TEMPUS", akud yettarwen ur yezmir ad yettweṣlaḥ.

Akud yessegli-d i weɣrem ayen akk yeffren tura, yeteffer daɣen yetɣum ayen akk yettfeǧǧiǧen deg tallit-a.

Tugut d amzun leḥmala, ur yezmir ad yeffren ɣas ma yelbes llebsa n tilemẓi.

Tugut tessers zzɛama n yirgazen tessendreɣ-ten, maca ayen nniḍen ad tiliḍ d aɛeqli wayeḍ ad tɣelliḍ s dderɣel.

Mi ara qerben lmut, imɣaren yettwaɣun deg tudert ufan tugut ur telli ara d taɛburt.

Irgazen akk țțarǧun ad ɛicen tudert t uzunin, ad uɣalen d imɣaren, maca tugut tessawaɛ-iten.

Tugut tebda deg xemsin d sdis iseggasen, tettkemmil deffir-s deg tallitin tis sebɛa i aɣ-d-yettewwin ar tmuqran d lmut.

Tawaɣit tameqqrant yellan deg imɣaren, mačči deg wayen yellan d imɣaren, meɛna deg tɛeqqult n ur bɣu ara ad țuqiɛen belli d imɣaren, deg teɣdemt n ad amnen belli d ilemẓiyen amzun tugut d tajeṛṛumt.

Ayen yellan d ayen yelhan akk deg tugut, d akken ad d-naf iman-nneɣ qrib ɣer tmeddit.

Nekk Aɣennan, iman-iw, ego, ur yettnerni ara s iseggasen d tɣuri; yettɛeqqed, yuɣal d ayen yewɛren ugar, d aqeddec ugar, ɣef way-a i d-yenna lemtel n weɣref: "Lǧensa d ṣṣura ar tmeqbert".

Nekk Aɣennan n imɣaren iweɛṛen yettɛawan iman-is i yal waḥed s nṣiḥa yelhan imi ur yezmir ad yefk lemtel ur neṣfi ara.

Imɣaren ẓran mliḥ belli tugut d amsermi d imeqqran i ten-iɣelben ɣef lmut, ad ț-țmenɛen seg zzhu n tilemẓi tummest, u wolen ad ɛawnen iman-nsen s nṣiḥa yelhan.

Nekk yeffer Nekk, Nekk yeffer tama seg iman-is yernu akk yettwareqem s awal yessuruf d nṣiḥa yelhan.

Tama seg iman-iw teffer tama nniḍen seg iman-iw. Nekk yeffer ayen ur t-iɛeǧben.

Yettubeggen s lekmal s tmuqqna d tɣuri, mi ara aɣ-ṭṭsen lɛuyub ad nḥess belli d nukni i ten-iṭsen.

Ul n Uɣersiw Afennan ur yuɣal ara d ayen yelhan s iseggasen, meɛna d ayen n diri ugar, daymen yettwaṭṭaf s azru, ma nella deg tilemẓi d imaxsa, d ikeddaben, d irufsan, deg tugut ad nili d ayen n diri ugar.

Imɣaren d-ɛicen deg iɛeddan, imɣaren d taggara n aṭas n iḍelli, imɣaren ur ẓran ara akud anda nella, imɣaren d takti yettjemmɛen.

Abrid aneggaru ad nawi ar tmuqran d tamqeddist d ad neslelli Nekk Aɣennan. Mi ara nelmed ad nmett sḍeɛ-sḍeɛ, ad naweḍ ar tmuqran tuddist.

Tugut tesɛa anamek d ameqqran, n tumert d tilelli i wid yesslen Nekk.

Mi ara mmten tixidas s ṣṣifa taneggarut, temmed, yeqqim waḥed ilelli mačči seg umɛellem waḥed, meɛna seg aṭas n yemɛellmen.

Yewɛer aṭas ad naf deg tudert imɣaren imsɛudiyen ur sɛin ara ula d ifadden n Nekk, aṣenf-a n imɣaren d wid yeḍeqqen aṭas d wid yettidiren sḍeɛ-sḍeɛ.

Argaz yessuqesmem deg tmusni. Amɣar deg tmusni, agellid n leḥmala, yuɣal d ayen yettfeǧǧiǧen ittehdi-d s tmusni leǧnas n leqrun ur yettḍusin.

Deg umaḍal llan d llan tura kra n Yemɣaren Imselmaden ur sɛin ara ula d ifadden ineggura n Nekk. Arhat Gnósticos-a d wid yettḍeqqen aṭas d wid yellan d udinanen am ujeǧǧig n Lotus.

Amɣar Amqeddes Amɛallem yesslen Nekk yettwasemmlilen deg ṣṣifa tamqeddist d taneggarut d taggara tagemmat n Tmusni Tamqeddist, n Leḥmala Tdinant D Tazmert Tuddast.

Amɣar Amɛallem ur yesɛi ara Nekk, d tagemmat tameqqrant n Udinan.

Imɣaren-nni Tuddsen, Arhat Gnósticos-nni sfeǧǧeǧen-d amaḍal seg tallitin iɛeddan, ad nmekti BUDHA, MOISÉS, HERMES, RAMARKRISHNA, DANIEL, SANTO LAMA, atg., atg., atg.

Imselmaden n uɣerbaz, n ulugan d tesdawiyin, timselmadin, imawlan n twacult, ilaq ad slemden i leǧyal ijdiden ad qadren ad ɛezzen imɣaren.

Ayen ur yesɛi ara isem, Ayen yellan d udinan, Ayen yellan d ayen n ṣṣeḥ, yesɛa tlata wudmawen: Tmusni, Leḥmala, Awal.

Udinan am Baba d Tmusni Takawsant, am Yemma d Leḥmala Taggagant, am mmi d Awal.

Deg Baba n twacult yella usigna n tmusni. Deg Yemma n yexxamen tella Leḥmala, arrac d usigna n awal.

Baba amɣar yuklal akk tɛawint n warraw. Baba amɣar ur yezmir ad yaqeddec, d ayen n lḥeqq ad t-ɛawnen warraw ad t-qadren.

Yemma Ḥninet taɣar ur tezmir ara ad taqeddec ɣef way-a ilaq ad walin warraw d tullas fell-as ad ț-țḥamlen ad ț-țjaɛlen seg leḥmala-nni d ddin.

Win ur nessin ad iḥemmel Baba-s, win ur nessin ad iqadeṛ Yemma-s, yettekka abrid n ufus ayeffus, abrid n tuccḍa.

Arrac ur sɛin ara lḥeqq ad ḥasben imawlan-nsen, ulac win yeggren deg umaḍal-a, wid ur nesɛi ara tuccḍiwin deg tama, nesɛa-tent deg tama nniḍen, akk nettwaqeḍɛen s iɣelben yemgaraden.

Kra ṭṭsen Leḥmala n Baba, kra ț-țeḍsaɣen Leḥmala n Baba. Wid yettidiren akka deg tudert ur kecmen ara ula d abrid ara yessawḍen ɣer ayen ur yesɛi ara isem.

Mmi ur neknen ur iḥemmel Baba-s, u ittu Yemma-s Ḥninet d aɣerbali s ṣṣeḥ i icekmen ayen akk yellan d udinan.

Tagrawla n Tazzla ur tenna ara Tameḥqranit, ad nettu Baba, ad nessenqes Leḥmala n Yemma Ḥninet. Tagrawla n Tazzla d Tmusni Leḥmala d Tazmert Taggemmat.

Deg Baba yella usigna n tmusni deg Yemma tella tɣalt n Leḥmala tilellit, bla tament-is tazdigt d ayen iwumi ijehd aṭas ad naweḍ ar Tagemmatin Tiddusatin tinni.
