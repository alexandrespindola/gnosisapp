Arbaɛin iseggasen imezwura n tudert nneɣ fkan-aɣ-d adlis, tlatin iseggasen i d-iteddun d aḥerrez.

Deg ɛecrin iseggasen argaz d aweṭṭuf, deg tlatin d izem, deg arbaɛin d agmal, deg xemsin d azrem, deg seṭṭin d aydi, deg sebɛin d ayḍiḍ, deg tmanyin d taɣect d tili kan.

Akud iden-d ayen akk yellan: d aqeddac yesɛan azal meqqren, yețmeslay i iman-is ula ma ulac win i s-yesteqsan.

Ulac ayen i yexdem ufus n uɣersiw ameqqran, ițțusemman d argaz s uɣalat, ur yețɣimi ara akud ad t-ixreb.

"FUGIT IRRÉPARABILE TEMPUS", akud i yeffer ur yețțuɣal.

Akud issuffuɣ-d ayen akk yellan tura yeffren, yeffer yeqqen ayen akk yellan tura yeẓɣal s lbenna.

Tugdut tettecbiḥ i leḥmala, ur tezmireḍ ad tt-teffreḍ ɣas ma tessebradeḍ-tt s llebsa n ilemẓi.

Tugdut tessider tikukrin n yirgazen yesserɣay-iten, maca ayen d tameɣdut yellan d asemɣar d ayen-nniḍen, d teɣzi n tserriḥ.

Mi d-iqreb lmut, imɣaren i yeɣwuḍen deg tudert ufan tugdut ur telli ara d aqrebɛ.

Irgazen akk s umata ssaramen ad ddren tudert ɣezzifen ad yuɣalen d imɣaren maca tugdut tettegwra-ten.

Tugdut tebda deg xemsa u seṭṭin iseggasen, tețțuɛawed s talliyin iseggasen iɣezzifayen iɣ-yuwḍen ɣer teɣzi d lmut.

Tameɣra tameqqrant akk n yimɣaren teẓẓuɣur, ur deg tidet-nniɣ i yellan d imɣaren, maca deg lemqem n ur tebɣin ad t-qedren, deg teɣzi n urgazen ad amnen d ilemẓiyen am akken tugdut d lɛib.

Ayen yelhan akk yellan deg tugdut, d amkan-nni anda tezmireḍ ad t-tafeḍ ur teqqimeḍ ara aṭas.

YI PSICOLÓGICO, MI MISMO, EGO, ur iteṭṭiq ara s iseggasen d ugerrez, yețɣaḍ, yuɣal d aɣlad, d amecṭuḥ aṭas, ɣef ayagi yeqqar uḥeqqiq: "GENIO Y FIGURA HASTA LA SEPULTURA".

YI PSICOLÓGICO n yimɣaren iɣezzifayen yeṭṭef iman-is s nṣayḥat yelhan ɣef tuccḍa n ucukal ad yefk imedyaten iɛiban.

Imɣaren ẓran mliḥ tugdut d amazday yesɛan aglawi d ameqqran iɣ-yeqqenen s lɛiqab n lmut, ad neɛceq i lɛeqliyat n ilemẓiyen iɣezzifayen, ugin ad ttawlen iman-nsen ad fken nṣayḥat yelhan.

YI yeffer YI, YI yeffer azal seg iman-is, ayagi akk yeṭṭaf s tseddiwin tseddanin d nṣayḥat yelhan.

AZAL seg MI MISMO yeffer azal-nniḍen seg MI MISMO. YI yeffer ayen ur d-yehlal.

Yettubeddar s yiman-is, mi ɣ-d-ǧǧan lɛuyub nesɛa lxiṛ ad neḥseb d nekni i ten-iǧǧan.

Ul n UƔERSAW AMEQQRAN ur yuɣal ara yelha s iseggasen maca d aɣerbuz, ma nella d aɣeblan, d ukaddab, d aɛebbad deg ilemẓi, deg tugdut ahat ad nili d axṣir.

Imɣaren ddren deg iɛeddan, imɣaren d afrux n ayur, imɣaren ṭṭfen deg lawan i deg nedder, imɣaren d afrux imezwer.

Tarrayt kan i tezmireḍ ad tawḍeḍ ɣer teɣzi tameqqrant, ad tessesseɣdeḍ YI PSICOLÓGICO. Mi neẓra ad nmutt deg lawan ɣer lawan, nețaɣli ɣer tugdut tseddart.

Tugdut tesɛa azal meqqren, n tserriḥ d tlelli i wid i yessessenɣen YI.

Mi mmuten tissas deg telɣa tunṣibt, d tin yakk, ad yeɣli ɣef yiwen n bab, maca seg waṭas n babaten.

D aɣlad ad tafeḍ deg tudert imɣaren imenza ur tesɛan ara ɣas ma d ayen yeqqimen seg YI, tamsalt-nni n yimɣaren d aseɛdi aqeffan, ddren seg lawan ɣer lawan.

Argaz i yeqqur deg TMAƐRFTA. Ameɣlal deg yissen, bab n leḥmala, yuɣal d aɣesmar n tafat i yetɛawan i uɣerbal n lqernat ur țțiḥsab.

Deg umaḍal llan llan tura AMSTANEN IGELLIDEN ur tesɛan ara ɣas ma d ayen yeqqimen seg YI. ARHAT GNÓSTICOS-agi d amaɣzulen d yisedsanen am ujeɣlal n llotus.

AMEƔLAL AJEBHDAR I YESSENɣEN YI YESƐAN AGATA deg telɣa tunṣibt d tseddart d lqima i tesɛa TMAƐRFTA TASEDDART, N LEḤMALA TASEDDART D TLELLI TAQƐUNT.

AMEƔLAL AJEDDAR i ur nesɛi ara YI, deg tidet d aberreɣ aqeffan n SER DIVÍNAL.

AMSTANEN-AGI ISEDDANEN, ARHAT GNÓSTICOS-AGI seddaden umaḍal seg yiwet tallit, ad nectu BUDHA, MOISÉS, HERMES, RAMARKRISHNA, DANIEL, SANTO LAMA, etc., etc., etc.

Imɛellmen n yiɣerbazen, n yiɣerbazen imeqqranen d temsirin, imawlan, ilaq ad slemden i tgelda tarat ad qadren d ad țɛeqlen imɣaren.

AYEN ur nesɛi isem, AYAGI id DIVINAL, AYAGI id ayen yellan, tesɛa tlata n wallalen: TMAƐRFTA, LEḤMALA, AWAAL.

LO DIVINAL am UMA d TMAƐRFTA TCUKALANT, AM YEMMA d LEḤMALA TAQƐUNT, am mmi d AWAAL.

Deg UMA n twacult yella uḥeqqiq n tmɛerft. Deg YEMMA n wexxam yella LEḤMALA, arraw ttawlen awal.

Uma amɣar yuklal tagullelt n warraw. Uma amɣar ur yezmir ara ad yexdem ilaq arraw ad t-ḥerzen ad t-ɛeqlen.

YEMMA i yeɛzizen amɣart ur tezmire ara ad texdem ɣef ayagi ilaq arraw d yelli-s ad walin fell-as ad ț-ḥemmlen ad rren seg leḥmala-nni religion.

Win ur yezmir ara ad iḥemmel UMA-s, win ur yezmir ad yɛelleq YEMMA-s yettala s abrid n ufus aẓelmaḍ, s abrid n tuccḍa.

Arraw ur sɛin ara azref ad ḥasben IMAWLAN-nsen, ulac win yelhan deg umaḍal-agi d wid ur nesɛi lɛuyub deg yiwet tama, nesɛa-ten deg tayeḍ, akk nțuqes s tiqesmarin d-iwet.

Kra ḍeqqsen azal i LEḤMALA N UMA, kra daɣen țțeḍsaɣen i LEḤMALA N UMA. Wid i yettɛawaden am-agi deg tudert ur kecmen ara s abrid i yettawin ɣer AYAGI ur nesɛi isem.

Mmi i yeffren i iɛuzz UMA-s yettettu YEMMA-s iɛuzzan deg tidet d aɛeqqal i yeffren akk ayen yellan d DIVINAL.

TAREVOLUCIONT N TESSEKREF TAMEQQRANT ur tɛeddi ara I LƔADAR, ad tettut UMA, ad ḍeqqes deg YEMMA iɛuzzan. TAREVOLUCIONT N TESSEKREF TAMEQQRANT d TMAƐRFTA LEḤMALA D ȚILI TAQƐUNT.

Deg UMA yella uḥeqqiq n tmɛerft deg YEMMA tella tnaẓaɛt i ddren n LEḤMALA bla leḥwayeǧ-is iṣfan, ur tezmireḍ ara ad taweḍ ɣer REALIZACIONES INTIMAS yesɛan azal.
