Aɣerda-agi yeṭṭefren taɣamsa n uɣerbaz n ugdud mačči d inelmaden neɣ tinelmadin n tezwarayt, ulemmas neɣ tasnawit, maca d IMWALIHEN d YISEL MADEN.

Ma imwalih d yiselmaden ur ẓran iman-nsen, ma ur zmiren ara ad fehmen aqcic, taqcict, ma ur ssinen ara ad fehmen ayen i ten-yerran ɣer yal taqcict, yal aqcic, ma iɣil-iten kan ad kemlen aɣbalu n lefhama n wid i ten-yeqran, amek ara d-nexleq tawsit tajḍiṭ n usselmed?

Aqcic, anelmad, tanelmadt, ṛuḥen ɣer Uɣerbaz akken ad steqsiyen ɣef wayen i ten-yesɛeddayen, maca ma d iselmaden, tiselmadin, ttidiren deg uxfaf n ixemmimen, ur bɣin ara ad beddlen, am wakken ara yili anelmad, tanelmadt.

Iselmaden ilaq ad ḥessben iman-nsen, ad ẓren iman-nsen, ad snernin tamusni-nsen, ad fehmen belli nteddu ɣer Tallit Tajḍiṭ.

S beddel n iselmaden, ad yebeddel uɣerbaz n ugdud.

Ad tessleḍ iselmaden d ayen i tɛetba, acku win yeɣran aṭas, win yesɛan agemmay, win i yessekcamen, win i ixedmen am uselmad n Uɣerbaz, yeqqim am wakken yella, aqal-is yettwattuq deg semmus n ṛebɛa alaf n teẓriyin i yeɣra yerna ur yettbeddil ara, mačči s rṣaṣ.

Iselmaden d tiselmadin ilaq ad slemden AMNEK IKKAN AD NXEMMEM, maca deg nnfaq, ttagwaden kan ad slemden AYEN I KKEN ARA NXEMMEM.

Imwalih d yiselmaden ddan-d deg wuguren iweɛren n yedrimen, imettiyen, ḥemmṛen, atg.

Imwalih d yiselmaden zedɣen aṭas deg wuguren d lemriḍ-nsen, ur ḥemmlen ara s tidet ad ɣren ad ḥlun wuguren i d-ttekkren ilmeẓyen d telmeẓyin n "Taqqiɛt Tajḍiṭ".

Yella leḥzen ameqran n uqelleb, n tḥila, n tmetti, maca imwalih d yiselmaden ččuṛen d tukriwin d wuguren i ttekkren iman-nsen, ttmektayen kan ɣef udrim n arrac-nsen, akken ad asen-fken lxedma akken ur ttkkat ara s laẓ d ayen kan.

Akkken ur nɣil ara, aṭas n yimawlan ur ḥemmlen ara arraw-nsen s tidet, lemmer ḥemmlen-ten, ad nnuɣen ɣef rreḥma n yal yiwen, ad ttagwaden wuguren n Uɣerbaz n Ugdud s tɣawsa n ad tbeddel s tidet.

Lemmer imawlan ḥemmlen arraw-nsen s tidet, ur d-ttilin ara ṭṭraḥat, ur ttbanen ara aṭas tawacult d taɣerma deg wayen i yellan ɣef ddunit, acku aya i d-yettarran wuguren, ṭṭraḥat, ibḍan iweɛren, lḥala n ǧahennama ɣer arrac-nneɣ d yessetmaten-nneɣ.

Medden ɣren, heggin iman-nsen akken ad ilin d imedyazen, imhendisiyen, imḥeqqanen, atg. maca deg umkan ur heggin ara iman-nsen ɣef lxedma yugaren akk d tin yettɛetban am tin ad ilin d imawlan.

Tawacult-agi n leḥder, tira n leḥder ɣer lejdud-nneɣ, taɣbalut-agi n tɣezrant n tawacult, ḍḍuṛt i meṛṛa deg meyya, acku ttbeddil ɣer taɣbalut n uɣurru d weḥlaw yettkemmilen deg tmetti.

Snerni, Tagrewla s tidet, tezmermer kan ma neɣleɣ leḥyuḍ-agi n Ccinwa i nefferḍen, i nefferḍen ɣef ddunit.

Nekkni meṛṛa d Tawacult Iwetran yerna d ḍḍuṛt ad neṭṭaxeɛ-nneɣ, ad nɣil kan d tawacult wid i zedɣen yid-nneɣ, atg.

Leḥder n Tawacult iweɛren iǧǧan ad yeḥbes snerni n tmetti ibḍu inemdanen, iɣelles ṭṭraḥat, idukan, ḥemmlen-t, wuguren n yedrimen, atg.

Ma imawlan ḥemmlen arraw-nsen s tidet, ad ɣlin d aɣebbar ṣṣuṛ, leḥyuḍ iweɛren n tɣezrant dɣa ad teḥbes tawacult ad tili d tɣelɣelt taɣezrant d umezɣer.

Ma ɣlin leḥyuḍ iweɛren n tawacult, dɣa tella tikwal n tlilli s imawlan d yemmawlan, s iselmaden d tiselmadin, s tmetti.

Ayen i d-ittekken deg Tlilli s tidet, d Tbeddil N Tmetti s tidet, d Tagrewla s tidet n uɣerbaz ɣer ddunit yugaren.

Iselmad ilaq ad yili d ameɣlal yugaren, ilaq ad yesdukul imawlan d yemmawlan, ɣer tezrart n Yimawlan d Usiwel-iten ɣer tafat.

Ilaq ad fehmen imawlan belli lxedma n uɣerbaz n ugdud tettwaxdem ɣef lebni yeqwan n tɛawant gar Yimawlan d Yiselmaden.

Ilaq ad nini i Yimawlan belli Aɣerbaz Amsadda yella d ilaq akken ad nrefdeɣ tawsiwin tajḍiṭin.

Ilaq ad nini i Yimawlan belli Asnerni Aqleɛɛi yella d ilaq maca mačči d akk, ilaq ayen yugaren, ilaq ad neslemden i ilmeẓyen d telmeẓyin ad ssinen iman-nsen, ad ssinen ixetaɛen-nsen, axxasen-nsen Imeqqranen n Uqelleb.

Ilaq ad nini i Yimawlan belli arrac ilaq ad ttwaɛellequen s Tayri mačči s leḥriṛ n Iɣersiwen.

Iḍerren d aweɛṛan ad nesseddaɛ leḥriṛ-nneɣ n iɣersiwen, timukriwin-nneɣ iderren n tiɣersiwin, tukriwin-nneɣ tiweɛṛanin n tiɣersiwin deg wid i d-yezgan deg lekbur-nneɣ.

Arraw d yessetmaten d tisuddaɛ-nneɣ yerna d ajenḥal ad neṭṭaxeɛ ddunit s tisuddaɛ n tiɣersiwin.

Iselmaden d Tiselmadin n Uɣerbazen, Imedden, Tiguniversiyin ilaq ad yesdukulen deg uɣerbaz, Yimawlan d Yemmawlan s tɣawsa tajḍiṭ n ad asen-slemden abrid n tamasit ɣer arraw-nsen d ɣer Tmetti d ɣer Ddunit.

Iselmaden sɛan aɣdem n ad ḥessben iman-nsen d ad d-wessen Yimawlan d Yemmawlan.

Nḥwaj tayri s tidet akken ad nebeddel ddunit. Nḥwaj ad nesdukul iman-nneɣ akken ad nerfed gar-aneɣ Merra, Agʷadir Umezɣer n Tallit Tajḍiṭ i yellan deg tura yebda gar uɣelli n ixemmimen.
