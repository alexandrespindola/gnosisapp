Agur ameqqran akk deg uɣerbaz ur yelli deg yinelmaden n tezwarit, alemmas neɣ uneggaru, meɛna deg YIMAWLAN ed YISELMADEN.

Ma yimawlan d yiselmaden ur ssinen iman-nsen, ma ur zmiren ara ad fehmen aqcic, taqcict, ma ur ssinen ara mliḥ assaɣen-nsen d yimexluqen-agi yebdan tudert, ma ḥebben kan ad kecmen aqelmun n wid yettɛellimen, amek ara nexleq assaɣ amaynut n usselmed?

Aqcic, anelmad, tanelmadt, tteddun ɣer Uɣerbaz akken ad gren tiṭ ɣef wayen sersen fell-asen, meɛna ma d iselmaden, tiselmadin, ttwalin kan s tuffɣaṭ, d imzikren, d wid yettɛeggilen, ad yili daɣen umedyaz, tamedyazt.

Wid yesselmaden ilaq ad ɛiwdin ad lemden, ad ssinen iman-nsen, ad ɛiwdin ɣef akk tmusni-nsen, ad fehmen belli nettkecm ɣer Tallit Tamaynut.

Ma beddlen wid yesselmaden, ad yebeddel daɣen uɣerbaz.

Ad NESSLEM ASSELMAD d ayen iɛessan aṭas acku win yeɣran aṭas, win yesɛan agerdas, win yesɛan ayen ara yesselmed, win iqeddcen d aselmad deg Uɣerbaz, yeqqel akken yella, aqelmun-is yeqqen deg semmus n ilew n tɣuriwin i yeɣra ur yettbeddil ara ḥaca ma tewwet-it-id tbuqalt.

Iselmaden d tiselmadin yessefk ad slemden AMENZU ARA YEḌRU, meɛna s tɛessast ttɛawaden iman-nsen ad slemden AYEN ILIQ ARA YEḌRU.

Imawlan d yiselmaden ttɛicin ččuṛen d tugdiwin yesḥezznen deg umezruy n tdamsa, tmetti, lḥess, atg.

Imawlan d yiselmaden ttɛawaden iman-nsen deg wuguren-nsen, ur ḥebben ara aṭas ad ɛeqlen aɣtusen n wid yettɛellimen n "TEBRUṬ TAYEMMAT".

Yella usfukkel meqqren deg aqelmun, takti d tmetti, meɛna imawlan d yiselmaden ččuṛen d tmeɣṛad akk d tugdiwin deg iman-nsen, ur sɛin ara lweqt iwakken ad myalaren ɣef taggaẓt tadamsant n warraw-nsen, ad sen-fken tizedɣa akken ur tmettat ara s laẓ, d aya kan.

D lawan ad neqqaren, aṭas n lewhi ma yella kra n yimawlan ur yeḥmil ara warraw-nsen s tidet, ma ḥemmlen-ten ad nnaɣen ɣef tlata, ad ɛeqlen ɣef wuguren n UɣERBAZ akken ad ttbeddilen s tidet.

Ma ḥemmlen yimawlan warraw-nsen s tidet, ur yelli ara wimenɣi, ur ttɛalen ara aṭas ɣef twacult d tmurt ɣef tmeɣra n ddunit, acku aya yesseqqel wuguren, imenzaɣ, tigawin i yettɛebbisen, d amḍiq n ǧaḥim i warraw-nneɣ d yelli-nneɣ.

Medden ttɣara, ttheggiṛen-d iman-nsen akken ad uɣalen d ddewla, d inġiniryen, d imḥeqqanen, atg. meɛna ur heggiṛen ara iman-nsen ɣef tɛekkast iweɛren aṭas, d wacu ara yeqqimen d Imawlan n twacult.

Tazwara-agi n twacult, n teɣzint ɣer wid yettmettaten, tasekta n usebɣes n twacult, teǧhed s meṛṛa yimɣi, acku teqqel d amgalad n usfukkel akk d usider n tmetti.

Asnerni, Tagrawla s tidet, zmaren ad ilin kan ma neddem tiɣaltin n Ccinwa s ussegraɣ i iqettɛen gar-aneɣ, i ɣ-yettḥebbsen ɣef ddin n ddunit.

Nekkni akk d TWACULT YETTWALINAN, ur ilaq ara ad neqqat gar-aneɣ, ad nḥseb kan am twacult wid yettɛicin yid-neɣ, atg.

Tazwara TAĠEZDA deg TWACULT tettɛeqqel asnerni n tmetti, teqqattaɛ imdanen, tettwaxxer-d imenzaɣ, tiɣaltin, tirzaf, wuguren n tdamsa, atg.

Asmi ara yeḥmmlen yimawlan warraw-nsen s tidet, ad ɣlin akken ma llan tiɣaltin, tuṛin iweɛren n ussegraɣ, imiren twacult ad teǧǧ ayen tettidir akken ma tella deg teɣzint d ugur.

Asmi ara ɣlint tiɣaltin deg teɣzint n twacult, yili dɣa tagmat yellan d yimawlan d yessetma n twacult, d iselmaden d tiselmadin, d tmetti meṛṛa.

Agzul n TAGMAT S TIDET, d TABDILT TAẒERFANT N TMETTI, d TEĠRAWLA tatentitt deg temḥeqqani n USSELMED ɣer ddunit yifen.

ASELLMAD ilaq ad yili d amessawar aṭas, ilaq ad yessaweḍ Imawlan d Yessetma n twacult, ɣer temlilit n Yimawlan n twacult akken ad sen-d-yawi awal.

Ilaq Imawlan n twacult ad fehmen belli tɛekkast n uɣerbaz tettawi ɣef llsas yeqqen n tdukli gar Imawlan n twacult ed Yiselmaden.

Ilaq ad d-nini i Imawlan n twacult belli USSELMED AMEQQREN yeqqen akken ad neɛlu tizrigin timaynutin.

Ilaq ad d-nini i Imawlan n twacult belli tira tazeqqit tella, meɛna ur telli d ayen meṛṛa, ilaqa-d ayen nniḍen, ilaqa-d ad nesselmed i wid yettɛellimen ad ssinen iman-nsen, ad ssinen tixuṭṭatin-nsen, tiɣaltin-nsen deg yixunen n Tusnakt.

Ilaq ad d-nini i Imawlan n twacult belli arrac ilaq ad ttekksen s LḤUBB mači s TAZMERT N LEḤYUAN.

Tettili d tɛessast iweɛren ad nessegla ayen nebɣa, tizmar-nneɣ timellalin, lḥess-nneɣ iweɛren d tmuɣliwin-nneɣ deg lḥiwan deg tarwa-nneɣ.

Arraw d yelli-tneɣ d tigawin-nneɣ nekkni yiman-nneɣ, d lxir ad nfekk ddunit d tigawin deg leḥyuwan.

Iselmaden d Tiselmadin n Uɣerbaz, Uɣezdis d Tesdawiyin ilaq ad ssedduan deg tmezgunt, Imawlan d Yessetma n twacult s tmuɣli ad sen-d-slemden abrid n tɛekkast tazelmaṭ ɣer warraw-nsen d tmetti d ddunit.

WID YESSELMADEN sɛan-t-id d turart ad ɛiwden ad lemden iman-nsen ed ad gren tiṭ ɣef Imawlan d Yessetma n twacult.

Neḥwaǧ ad nḥemmel s tidet akken ad nbeddel ddunit. Neḥwaǧ ad neddukkel akken ad neɛlu gar-aneɣ akk, Amezwar n Tallit Tamellalt yellan deg lweqt-agi i yellan yettwaxxer gar uɣerbaz ameqqran n umahil.
