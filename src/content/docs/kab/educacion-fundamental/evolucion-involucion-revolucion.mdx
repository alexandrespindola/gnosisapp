Deg umezruy, nezmer ad nẓer belli ama Aɣerbaz n Tmaterializm, ama Aɣerbaz n Tespiritwalizm ddukklen meṛṛa deg dogma n Tevolucyu.

Rrayat tura ɣef asuqef n umdan d Tevolucyu-ines tura, deg unekcum, d sufistri n lexsas kan, ur ttaṭṭafen ara adar deg yiwen wanda i d-yewwi ad yili useqsi d asqerdec.

Ɣas akken d tigziwin n DARWIN iqeblen am taddart n liman ameqran ɣer CARLOS MARX d Tmaterializm Dialectique-ines yesnernayen, ur ẓri ara imusnawen tura ɣef asuqef n umdan, ulac ayen d-nnan, ulac ayen s yiman-nsen, ula ulac ayen i s-d-yefkan ttawilat tidetti i t-ttberriḥen ɣef Tevolucyu n Wudem n Wumdan.

Akkken ma yella, ma nerra leɛtab ɣer tmetti i deg yella umdan, yeɛni, tin i d-yefɣen seg ɛecrin neɣ tlatin alef iseggasen uqbel Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, ad naf ttawilat tidetti, talamin ur yesṛuḥuyen ara i wudem ayeggran n umdan, ur yettwaɛqel ara ɣer medden tura, d tilin-ines tezmer ad tberreh s waṭas n lehdur, Tiǧerfatin iqdimen, Tipiramidin iqdimen, Timensunin tiɣribin, Tigratin timsebrinin, d Leɛwayed iqdimen yemgaraden.

Akkken ma yella ɣer Umdan Uqbel Amezruy, ɣer imexluqen nniḍen d imsebrinin, ẓran am Aɣersiw Aɛeqli, ma yella mgarraden, mgarraden aṭas, imsebrinin aṭas, iɣsan-nsen yettwaḥerzen deg yigan iqdimen aṭas deg wussan n Yeglasiyal neɣ Preglasiyal, ulac ayen ẓran imusnawen tura s tidett d tajriba s yiman-nsen.

Tussna n Ugnostik tlemmed belli Aɣersiw Aɛeqli am akken i t-nessen, ur yelli ara d Agmam Aɣerfan, mazal ur yelli ara d Umdan deg wawal n tidett; Tudert tesserɣas armi d aswir kan yellan, dɣa teṭṭef deg-s tixxutert i tesseɣɣas neɣ tesseɣɣas tilufa-ines dɣa tessedɣel.

Tallelin n Tevolucyu d Tinvolucyu d timɣariwin tixaqanin n tudert dɣa ulac ayen sɛant d Turealit Intime n Usir.

Deg Aɣersiw Aɛeqli llant tilufa timerẓagin i zemren ad ssnernayen neɣ ad sṛuḥuyen, ur telli ara d tallelt ad ssnernayen. Timɣariwin n Tevolucyu ur zemrent ara ad ssnernayen.

Asnernay n tilufa-agi yellan, yezmer ad yili kan deg wanda i llan leḥsab daqquqan, d wannect-a yewwi ad ilin Ɣellaten Uqbel-Nniḍen yellan d yiman d tallalt tettuḥeqqeqen sɣur imsellmaden nni i yexdemen axeddim-agi deg yezri.

Win yebɣan ad yesnerni tilufa-ines yellan ad yuɣal d umdan, yewwi ad yekcem ɣer ubrid n Tagrawla n Tizzwalt.

Aɣersiw Aɛeqli d Aɣrud, Tazzla; Seg tazzla-agi yezmer ad ilul Aɣerbaz n Tudert, Umdan n Tidett, Umdan nni i yettnadi DIOGENES s tafat tessedɣel deg yiberdan n ATENAS d Midyou d win ur yezmir ara ad yaf.

Ur telli ara d Tallelt belli aɣrud-agi, belli tazzla-agi meqqren ad tesnerni, ayen yellan, ayen n tudert dɣa ad iruḥ.

Umdan n Tidett yettemgarrad aṭas seg Aɣersiw Aɛeqli, am Tella ɣer Asegni.

Ma yella aɣrud ur yemmut ara, tazzla ur tesseɣɣas ara, yewwi, yewwi ad yemmet Ego, You, Mi Mismo, paca ad ilul Umdan.

Imsellmaden d Imseɛraqen n Aɣerbazen, Timseggez d Tiuniversité, yewwi ad sselmden i inelmaden-nsen Abrid n Tega Tiɣiwanin, dɣa kan akka yezmer ad yili tallet n Ego.

Ɣef usnernay, nezmer ad nini belli Tagrawla n Tizzwalt ur telli ara kan d taɣezfant deg umaḍal-agi, maca yal tikkelt tura d tin i d-yewwi aṭas aṭas.

Tagrawla n Tizzwalt sɛa tlata ttawilat yettwaẓelgen daqquqan: Uqbel, Ad mmten; Sin, Ad lhun; Krad, Ad țqeddimen ɣef tmetti. Aderɣal n ttawilat ur yesselɣay ara aɣrud.

Ad mmten d Aɣerbaz n Tega Tiɣiwanin d Tallet n YO Psikologik.

Ad lhun d Aɣerbaz n Transmutasyon Sexuelle, leɛnaya-agi tella deg Tsexulujit Transcendentale, win yebɣan ad yelmed asentel-agi, yewwi ad yaru ɣur-na ad yissen idlisen-nneɣ Gnósticos.

Ad țqeddimen ɣef tmetti d Tarwa n Umaḍal Yuɣal-Daqquqan.

Ma yella ur nebɣi ara Tagrawla n Tizzwalt, ma yella ur nexdim ara Ɣellaten Uqbel-Nniḍen i t-tessnernayen i tessnernayen ttawilat-nneɣ i n-yettawin ɣer Tawreali2asyon Intima, d tidett belli tilufa-agi ur tesnernayen ara.

Tin i Ttwarealizen dɣa ɛezzen aṭas, tin i yettwaselleken dɣa ulac deg-s talast, acuɣer yewwi ad yesɛu Aɣersiw Aɛeqli gar n tḥilaṭ-ines win ur yebɣi?

Yettwaḥwaj ad yili beddel ameqran akken ma yella d win daqquqan maca ulac agmam i yebɣan abeddel-agi, ur t-ttḥussun ara, ur t-ẓrin ara dɣa ttwannen-asen-dɣa ur t-fehmen ara, ur t-ɛeqlen ara, ur sen-yefki ara azal. Acuɣer yewwi ad asen-teffken s ddurma win ur yettḥussun?

Tidett d uqbel ad yesɛu yiwen Ɣellaten Tura neɣ Tzemmar Tura, i ur yessin ula iḍelli yettwaḥwaj ad yesɛu ɣellaten d tzemmar i ɣilen yesɛa maca deg tidett ur yesɛa ara.
