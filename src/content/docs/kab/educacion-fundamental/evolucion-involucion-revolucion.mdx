Deg wayen i nelmed deg tilawt, nufa ama ɣer TEKNA d-IMATERYALEN ama d-TEKNA d-IRUḤANIYEN, llan dima ṭṭfen deg DOGMA n TUDERTI.

Tikta tajdiṭin ɣef uẓar n wemdan d tudert-is iɛeddan, deg lehqaq d ṣṣufistri uḥric, ur zmiren ara ad ṭṭfen deg uqader ar yettɛemeq.

Ɣas akken llan akk tiyurin n DARWIN yettwaqbelen am tikti taẓeṭṭa n CARLOS MARX d temateryalizmt-is yettwaɛerfen, ulac ayen ssnen isensiwen n zman-a ɣef uẓar n wemdan, ulac ayen ẓran, ulac ayen ḥussan s tarrayt tusridt, dɣa ur sɛin ara akk idlisen iṣeḥḥan, ẓidan, ɣef TUDERTI TAWMIDANT.

Ma nerra iman-nneɣ ɣer talsa taneggarut, neɣni tin n ɛecrin neɣ tlatin alaf n yiseggasen uqbel Ɛisa Lmasiḥ, ad naf idlisen iṣeḥḥan, iɣeblan yessuturen ɣef tawsit n wemdan yugaren, ur yettafehhem ara i medden n zman-a, dɣa tilin-is tezmer ad tesseɣṛet s aṭas n tmeskanin, ijeruglifiwen iqdimen, tuga iqdimen, tizeggagin timezdayin, tira-nniḍen tigejdanin d iɣerfan n zik.

Ɣef WEMDAN UQBIL-AMAZRAY, ɣer imexlaq imezdayen d iraqasen n ṣṣifa yeqqerben ɣer Aɣersiw AZDAN, meɛna yemxalafen, yemyarawen, mezwaren dɣa iɣessan-is yettwaḍfen ɣer lqaɛa deg imkanat iqdimen n wakud n Tegrest neɣ uqbel Tagrest, ulac ayen ssnen isensiwen n zman-a s tarrayt taṣeḥḥit d tajerbit tusridt.

TUSSNA TAGNUSTANT tessawal d akken Aɣersiw AZDAN akken i t-nessen, ur yelli ara d AGMAR ILEQQAN, mazal ur yelli ara d AMDAN s ṣṣifa tmeqqrant n awal; ṭṭbiɛa tessefɣay-it alamma d tama, imiren teṭṭegg-it tesseḍsa-t deg tlelli tumḍint akken ad yessawḍ asnerni-s neɣ ad ixesser akk tizmert-is ad yuɣal ɣer deffir.

ILAḤEN n TUDERTI d Tin-d-YUƔALEN d tiɣerday n tecṛiɛt n ṭṭbiɛa kamel dɣa ur sɛint ara akk ayen yeqqnen ɣer TUDERT TAJDIDT TUSLIGT n Uɣris.

Deg Aɣersiw AZDAN llant tizmert timeqqranin zemrent ad ilint neɣ ad xesrent, ur yelli ara d alaɣma ad ilint. Tamikkanit n TUDERTI ur tezemmer ara ad tent-tessefɣay.

Asnerni n tizmert-a teffesrit, yezmer kan deg tlufa yuḍen dɣa yessutur ǦEHUD IMESSIYEN irsalen d tallat ǧehdent ɣer lejdud-nni i yexedmen ixeddimen-nni deg ayen iɛeddan.

Win ibɣan ad yessnerni akk tizmert-is teffesrit akken ad yuɣal d argaz, ilaq ad yekcem ɣer ubrid n TEGLU TILAWIT.

Aɣersiw AZDAN d ANEGRAR, d ẒERRIƐ; seg zerriɛ-agi tezmer ad tlul TAZERBIT N TUDERT, AMDAN ASEḤIḤ, argaz-nni yeqqaren DIUJINIS s tafat yesɛaten tafat deg wiberdan n ATINAS deg lweqt n Ṭehr, dɣa ur yezmir ara ad t-yaf.

UR YELLI ARA D ALAƔMA d akken anegrar-agi, d zerriɛ-agi tayeẓẓant ad tnerni, ayen yettwaqbelen, ṭṭbiɛa ad ixesser.

AMDAN ASEḤIḤ yemgarad aṭas ɣef Aɣersiw AZDAN, am RAYU ɣer ugenses.

Ma anegrar ur yemmut ara zerriɛ ur telul ara, ilaq, ilaḥen ad yemmut IGU, IW, AYEN NEKK, akken ad ilul Argaz.

Ilmudanen d Tlmadin n TEKNA, TIƐUṚṚUMT d TESDAWITEN, ilaq ad slemden i inelmaden-nsen UBRID n TILELLI TEGLAWIT, akka kan i nezmer ad nerr lmut n IGU.

Nessenṭaq akka akken ad nini d akken TEGLU TILAWIT ur telli ara d ayen icebḥen deg ddunit-a, maca yal tikelt tettuqellib d tettuqellib.

TEGLU TILAWIT tesɛa tlata n yidisen yettwaɛerfen: Amezwaru, ad mmten; Wis sin, ad tlulen; Wis tlata, ad sɛun ussirem ɣer talsa. Tarrayt n yidisen ur tbeddil ara agmar.

AD MMTEN d ayen n TILELLI TEGLAWIT d TUKKI n IW APSICULUJIK.

AD TLULEN d ayen n TURRI TAKSARIT, ayagi d asɣan ɣer TSAKSULUJIA TRANSCINDINTAL, win ibɣan ad yelmed amedal-a, ilaq ad aɣ-yaru dɣa ad yessen idlisen-nneɣ ijnustanen.

USSREM ɣer talsa d ṬṬABƐA TILIWLAT TANAGGALT.

Ma ur nebɣi ara TEGLU TILAWIT, ma ur nexdim ara ǦEHUD IMESSIYEN akken ad nessnerni tizmert-a teffesrit ara yaweḍ ɣer TUDERT TAJDIDT TUSLIGT, yebna d akken tizmert-nni ur ttilin ara.

D cwiṭ kan i TUDERT TAJDIDT, wid yettuselken dɣa deg wayagi ulac deg-s tirrugza, acuɣer i ilaḥeq Aɣersiw AZDAN ameskin ayen ur yebɣi ara?

Nettɛawiq abeddel ameqran kamel dɣa ur yettwaqbel ara akk i medden-nni ibɣan abeddel-nni, ur t-bɣin ara, ur t-ssinen ara dɣa ttwalin-ten ur t-fihmen ara, ur sen-yettunafɛ ara. Acuɣer ilaq ad ten-id-nefk s lǧebṛ ayen ur bɣin ara?

Tideț d akken uqbel ad yesɛu umdan TIḤUḤAḌ TAJDIṬIN neɣ IZMER ijdiden, ayen ur yessin ara, dɣa mazal ur yesɛi ara, ilaq ad yesɛu tiḥuḍ d izmar ayen yettwalin d akken yesɛa, maca deg lehqaq ur yesɛi ara.
