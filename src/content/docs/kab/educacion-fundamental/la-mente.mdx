S usqamuṛ s wayen i nɛedda yis, nufa d lmuḥal ad nefhem ayen umi qqaren TAƔURI, alamma nefhem s tɣerɣert talast taɣezfant n UXEMMEM.

Wid iɣilen belli UXEMMEM d AƔESRUS, ɣelṭen aṭas. UXEMMEM d AMAZDAR, d asemmaṭ, yezmer ad yeffer seg TEKKEST, yezmer deg kra n tegnatin n tmerrit neɣ deg usenṭeṭ yellan d amaɛiwen, ad yeṭṭef iberdan ɣer yimukan ibɛiden aṭas akken ad iẓer u ad isel i wayen yeḍran deg yimukan-nni.

Deg iɣerbazen n PARAPSICOLOGÍA, ttilin-d ujiṛṛden yesɛan azal s wid yellan deg tegnit n TMERRIT.

Aṭas n wid yellan deg tegnit n TMERRIT i yessawḍen ad d-ssawḍen s tɣerɣert fell-asen iɣtasen ɣef wayen yeḍran, imdanen d tegnatin i yellan deg tmerrit-nsen, i yellan deg yimukan ibɛiden.

Imsednawen ssawḍen ad ɛerḍen, deg yirawen-nni, tidet n YEƔTASEN-NNI. Ssawḍen ad ɛerḍen tidet n wayen yedran, taɣerɣert n WAYEN YEḌRAN.

S ujiṛṛden-agi n iɣerbazen n PARAPSICOLOGÍA, yettwaɛerḍen s lekmal s usqamuṛ d ussaram belli AƔESRUS mačči d UXEMMEM.

S tidet yerna s tideț n tideț, nezmer ad d-nini belli uxemmem yezmer ad ibbwi s ddaw n wakud d tallast, d yiman-is seg uɣersrus, akken ad iẓer u ad isel i tɣawsiwin yeḍran deg yimukan ibɛiden.

TIDET n TASTARIWIN EXTRA-SENSORIALES yettwaɛerḍen akk-n, yerna anagar ameɛluq n laɛqel neɣ aɛeqli, i yezmer ad t-id-yekker ad yenkeṛ tidet n TASTARIWIN EXTRA.

Aɣersrus yettwaxdem akken ad yefser afares meɛna mačči d afares. Aɣersrus anagar d ameskan n UXEMMEM, mačči d uxemmem.

Ixṣuṣ-aɣ ad nɣer uxemmem s lekmal, ma yella newwi-d deg ul ad nessen s tɣerɣert ayen umi qqaren TAƔURI.

Aqcicten d ilemẓiyen, d yergazen d tlawin, sɛan ixemmemen i yellan d imḍiqen, d isehlen, d wid ur ɛeṭṭilen ara, d wid iwulmen, atg.

Aṭas n warrac d ilemẓiyen i yeḥemmlen ad steqsiyen i yimawlan-nsen d yiselmaden-nsen, ɣef tɣawsiwin-agi neɣ tid-nni, bɣan ad ẓren kra n tɣawsiwin, bɣan ad ẓren ugar, uɣur steqsayen, tmuɣlin, ttwalin kra n yimḍanen i ttaɣen iɛeqliwen meqqren neɣ ur ttḥussun ara.

Akken i ɛeddan iseggasen, akken i nteddu deg leɛmer, uxemmem yettales deg wanda deg wanda.

Uxemmem n yimɣaren yettwaḥkem, yettwazdiɣ, ur yettbeddil ara ula s tukka n tbuciṭ.

Iɣalen d ayen, ad mmten d ayen, ur ttbeddilen ara, kullec ttaɣen-t-id seg yiwen umḍiq iqimen.

"ICUCEN" n yimɣaren, iḥellilen-nsen, afaresen iqimen, atg. tbanen akk-n d AZRU, d ADƔAƔ i ur yettbeddilen s wacu-t-id. Ɣef wayagi i d-yenna lemtel n wawal "GENIO Y FIGURA HASTA LA SEPULTURA".

Ilaq s uzɣer ad gren imselmaden d tiselmadin i yemmuggen akken ad ṣubben TUDERT n yinelmaden d tnelmadin, ad ɣren aṭas uxemmem, akken ad zmiren ad sseɣẓnen i tiddiwin yellan d tidjerḍin s tmusni.

Yettḥazzen fell-aɣ ad nefhem deg uzɣer, amek s ussan i yettwasdiɣ UXEMMEM deg wanda deg wanda.

UXEMMEM d amegriḍ n TIDET, n tidet. UXEMMEM yettmesnaw TAƔURI.

Win i yessawḍen ɣer leɛmer ur yezmir ara ad IḤEMMEL acku uxemmem-is yettwajerḥ s usaramen n usaramen, iḥellilen, afaresen iqimen am tgejjat n wuzzal, atg.

Llant dinna, iɣalen azegzawen iɣilen d izmer ad IḤEMMELEN MAZAL, meɛna ayen yeḍran d akken iɣalen-nni yettwajerḥen s tbaɣestin tisengalen yellan d tidjerḍin, dɣa ttfettiren gar TEBƔEST d TAƔURI.

Kullec "IƔAL AZEGZAW" d "TEƔALT AZERZAWT" tteddun s tegnatin tiḥerractin tiserreḥyin uqbel ad mmten dɣa ttɣilen d TAƔURI.

TAƔURI n yimɣaren d lmuḥal acku uxemmem yettmesnaw-it s "ICUCEN" AFARISEN IQUIMEN", IHELILLEN", "TIRUFI", "USSARAMEN", TEGZIWIN", tbaɣestin tisengalen, atg. atg. atg.

UXEMMEM d aɛdaw ameqqran n TAƔURI. Deg tmura SUPERCIVILIZADAS, TAƔURI ur tella ara acku uxemmem n yemdanen anagar yebbwi i iɣerbazen, tiḥsabin n lbanka, gazwal d celluloide.

Llant aṭas n teqvavciwin i uxemmem yerna uxemmem n yal yiwen yettwaḥkem mliḥ deg teqvavciwt.

Kra sɛan UXEMMEM yettwaqvevcey deg COMUNISMO ABOMINABLE, kra-nniḍen sɛan-t yettwaqvevcey deg CAPITALISMO ur yesɛin ara ṛreḥma.

Llan wid sɛan UXEMMEM YETTWAQVAVCEY deg tirufi, deg tkerhi, deg uɛerqed ad yuɣal d ameṛkanti, deg uɣerbaz yellan d ilemẓi, deg turrit ɣer yimdanen yemmuggen, deg turrit ɣer usaramen-nsen yimanen, deg uguren-nsen n twacult, atg. atg. atg.

Yemdanen ḥemmlen ad qvevcen UXEMMEM, drus ma d wid i d-yeǧǧan deg tidet ad uɣalen pedazos teqvavciwt.

Ixṣuṣ-aɣ ad NƐEZLEN, UXEMMEM meɛna yemdanen ḥemmlen aklact, d ayen yudden ad tafem kra deg tudert i ur yesɛin ara UXEMMEM yettwaqvevcey mliḥ.

Imselmaden d tiselmadin yessefk ad slemden i yinelmaden-nsen d tnelmadin-nsen tɣawsiwin-agi kullec. Yessefk ad slemden i tiddiwin tidejrḍin ad ẓren uxemmem-nsen yimanen, ad t-ttmuɣlen, ad t-fhmen, anagar akka s tɣerɣert n uzɣer nezmer ad nenjem ad yettales uxemmem, ad iṣemmed, ad yettwaqvevcey.

Ayen kan i yezmer ad yebeddel ddunit d ayen umi qqaren TAƔURI, meɛna uxemmem yettmesnaw TAƔURI.

Ixṣuṣ-aɣ ad NƔER uxemmem-nneɣ yiman-nneɣ, ad t-nettmuɣal, ad t-neqreṣ deg uzɣer, ad t-nefhem s tidet. Anagar akka, anagar nekkni neɣli d ayla n iman-nneɣ, n uxemmem-nneɣ yimanen, ad negmer ameɣriḍ n TAƔURI yerna ad nuɣal d sεada s tidet.

Widak i yettidirin ttamnin deg taƔURI, widak i yettidirin gren tiḥerractin ɣef TAƔURI, widak i yebɣan ad tezdem TAƔURI s leɣwer n tayri-nsen d tirẓi-nsen, tiḥerractin d ttamaniyin, tira d iḥellilen, tegziwin d ussaramen, atg. ur zmiren ara ad ẓren s tidet d acu-t TAƔURI, deg tidet uɣalen d iɛdawen n TAƔURI.

Ilaq ad nefhem s tɣerɣert acu-ten iɣtasen n uxemmem deg tegnit n tummin n tegziwin.

Aselmad, tiselmadin yettɛeyyafen aṭas n tikkal s lḥeqq meɛna kra tikkal s tmeɛlulqa dɣa war ssbebb, war ma nefhem belli yal tɛeyyfat ur telli d lḥeqq tettwaḍef deg uxemmem n yinelmaden, afares n tekkest-nni yeɣlu, yettili d tgerḍa n TAƔURI i USELMAD, i TISELMT.

UXEMMEM yettmesnaw TAƔURI yerna ayagi d tɣawsa i YESLMADEN d TISELMTIN n uɣerbaz, uɣerbaz ameqqran d tseddawiyin ur yessefk ara ad ttun qattɛ.

Ilaq ad nefhem deg uzɣer iɣtasen-nni n uxemmem akken ad negmer tazzla n TAƔURI.

Ur ilaq ara ad nili d baba-s neɣ d yemma-s n twacult, yessefk ad nessin ad NḤEMMLEM. Baba-s d yemma-s n twacult ttɣilin d ḥemmlen arrac-nsen d tullas-nsen acku sɛan-ten, acku nsen, acku ten-ṭṭfen, am win yesɛan takerrust, taɣlamt, taddart.

Anamur-agi n ṭṭafa n tuṭṭfa, yettwabdel s TAƔURI meɛna ur yezmir ara ad yili d TAƔURI.

Imselmaden d tiselmadin n taddart-nneɣ tis snat i yellan d aɣerbaz, ttɣilin d ḥemmlen inelmaden-nsen, tnelmadin-nsen, acku nsen am yakka, acku ten-ṭṭfen, meɛna mačči d TAƔURI. Anamur n ṭṭafa neɣ tuṭṭfa MAČČI D TAƔURI.

UXEMMEM yettmesnaw TAƔURI yerna anagar ma nefhem kullec d tiḥerɣatin iɣlanen n uxemmem, tannerzit-nneɣ ur tezdiɣ n uɣemmem, izgaren-nneɣ n ccɣel, iẓalen imaṭlayen, timserḍa ur nḥussu ara, tannerzit yeɣlu ad nwali tɣawsiwin, atg. nezmer ad nedder, ad nessaram deg TIDEȚ ayen ur yelli ara ɣer wakud, ayen umi qqaren TAƔURI.

Widak i yebɣan TAƔURI ad tuɣal d axal n tmeskan-nsen yimanen, widak i yebɣan ad teddu TAƔURI ɣer yiberdan iɣlanen n iḥellilen-nsen, tiḥerractin, tugdiwin, tegziwin n tudert, tannerzit yesɛan iman-is ad twali tɣawsiwin, tannerzit yeɣlu n uɣemmem, atg. negmaren deg tidet s TAƔURI acku ayagi qattɛ ur yezmir ara ad yettwanɣel.

Widak i yebɣan TAƔURI ad tezdem am AKKEN BƔIƔ, am AKKEN ƐERQADEƔ, am AKKEN TTƐEQQAALEƔ, tgerḍen TAƔURI acku CUPIDO, ILLU n TAƔURI, ur yelhi ara ad yeffer ad yettwaklɛ s IMAN.

Ilaq ad nenjem s IMAN, s MI MISMO, s SI MISMO akken ur neger azɣer n TAƔURI.

IMAN d tafrawt n tegziwin, tiḥerractin, tugdiwin, tkerhiyin, tbaɣestin, tegziwin, imanen, tismin, tazerzeg, tbaɣest, atg. atg. atg.

Anagar ma nefhem yal lɣella s uzɣer, anagar ma nettɣer, nettmuɣal s tidet ur telli anagar deg temnaṭ n laɛqel, meɛna ula deg yal tirmatin taɣezfant n uxemmem, tenger yal lɣella, ntemtat deg wanda deg wanda. Akka kan, nesɛa tamesnawa n IMAN.

Widak i yebɣan ad qvevcey TAƔURI deg teqvavciwt tawḥiṭ n iman, tgerḍen TAƔURI, qqimen war-s, acku TAƔURI ur tezmir ara ad tettwaqvevcey qattɛ.

Maɛna tɛejjeb yemdanen bɣan TAƔURI ad tezdem ɣer izgaren-nsen yimanen, tiḥerractin, izgaren, atg. yemdanen bɣan ad yenɣel IMAN i TAƔURI yerna ayagi d lmuḥal acku TAƔURI ur tṭaɛet ara IMAN.

Ṭṭuṭun n wid imesbaɣesten, neɣ ahat ad d-nini tbaɣestin tisengalen, ayen i yettilin aṭas deg ddunit-agi, ttɣilin belli TAƔURI yessefk ad teddu s teɣerɣert deg yiberdan n tayri-nsen yimanen, tulufanin, tiɣlatin, atg. dɣa ɣelṭen akk.

Ad nemmeslay ɣef sin!, qqaren imesbaɣesten neɣ tbaɣestin tisengalen, ayen i yellan d aṭas deg ddunit-agi, dɣa ttilin-d temseɣẓent, tiḥerractin, tilɣatin d tmuɣlin. Yal yiwen yenna kra, yegger tiḥerractin-is, tiɣlatin-is, tannerzit-is ad wali tɣawsiwin n tudert dɣa yebɣa ad tesseɣznet TAƔURI am tɣerɣurt n ussegzel ɣer yiberdan n wuzzal i tettwaɛemmeren s uxemmem.

Acu kan ɣelṭen wid imesbaɣesten neɣ tbaɣestin! Acu kan beɛden seg tidet.

TAƔURI ur tṭaɛet ara IMAN dɣa mi bɣan izewǧen ad gren azɣer ɣer umeẓẓuɣ dɣa ad t-nɣlen, teffreɣ teǧǧa ṭṭuṭu deg leɛqel.

UXEMMEM yesɛa leɣwer ur nezdiɣ n ubeddel. Argaz yettbeddel tiɛeggalt s tiɛeggalt-nniḍen. Tameṭṭut tettbeddel argaz s argaz-nniḍen. Aselmad yettbeddel anelmad s anelmad-nniḍen, tanelmadt s tanelmadt-nniḍen am ma d akk yinelmaden-is ur urren ara ad ttwaḥemmclen s yiwen. Deg tidet kullec aɛeyyaf d ABOMINABLE.

Win ittmuɣalen tagrest taɣezfant n tafat dɣa ittbeddel-itt s tayeḍ, ur yessen ara s tidet ad yefhem tazzla i yellan ɣer wallen-is.

Win ittmuɣalen adrar yellan d ilemẓi dɣa ittbeddel-itt s wayeḍ i t-iwala ḍelli, ur yelli ara s tidet ittfehhimen tazzla n udrar i yellan ɣer wallen-is.

Anda yella UBEDDEL ur tella ara TAƔURI VERDADERO. Baba-s d yemma-s i iḥemmlen arrac-nsen s tidet, qattɛ ur ten-beddilen s yiwen, ḥemmlen-ten dɣa d ayen kan.

Argaz yettḥemmillen s tidet tameṭṭut-is, qattɛ ur yexdam aɣalat ad ibeddel-itt s yiwen, ḥemmel-itt dɣa d ayen kan.

USELMAD neɣ TISELMT i iḥemmlen inelmaden-nsen d tnelmadin-nsen, qattɛ ur ten-yessebḍen ara, ur ten-ttbeddilen ara gar-asen, ḥemmlen-ten s tidet dɣa d ayen kan.

Uxemmem Yeqsem ɣer ubbeddel, uxemmem yakli n DUALISMO, yettmesnaw TAƔURI.

Uxemmem yeqsem s tsedduḥra n wid yettbeddilen ur yezmir ad yefhem ayen d ajdid, yettales, iṣemmed.

UXEMMEM YESƐA AṬAS N TAẒṚIWIN, Temnaṭin, iwunak taɣezfant, iɣedṛen, meɛna ayen yellan d ilemẓi d ESENCIA, CONCIENCIA yerna tella deg L وسط.

Mi yenjem DUALISMO, mi yuɣal uxemmem INTEGRA, SERENA, QUIETA, PROFUNDA, mi ur yettbeddel ara, dɣa tekkser ESENCIA, CONCIENCIA dɣa ilaq ad yili d afares adevlan n EDUCACIÓN FUNDAMENTAL.

Ad nesbeddel gar OBJETIVO d SUBJETIVO. Deg OBJETIVO tella tamusni tekkes. Deg SUBJETIVO tella tamusni tetteddem, SÚBCONCIENCIA.

Anagar CONCIENCIA OBJETIVA i yezmer ad yefraḥ deg CONOCIMIENTO OBJETIVO.

Tilɣa tameɣrust i ttaɣen tura Inelmaden d Tnelmadin n akk Uɣerbazen, Uɣerbazen imeẓyanin d Tseddawiyin, d SUBJETIVA taḍfert taḍfert.

CONOCIMIENTO OBJETIVO ur yezmir ara ad yettwaɣ deg war CONCIENCIA OBJETIVA.

Inelmaden d Tnelmadin ilaq ad ssawḍen tamezwarut ɣer AUTOCONCIENCIA dɣa rnu ɣer CONCIENCIA OBJETIVA.

Anagar s uBRID N TAƔURI i nezmer ad nawḍeɣ ɣer CONCIENCIA OBJETIVA d CONOCIMIENTO OBJETIVO.
