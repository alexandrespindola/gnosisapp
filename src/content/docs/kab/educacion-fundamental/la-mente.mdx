S us-n التجربة, nezmer ad neẓer belli d impossible ad nefhem ayen iwumi qqaren tayri, armi nefhem s tmerniwt taɣewwaret n ugur yellan ɣef UXemmem.

Wid yeṭṭfen belli UXemmem d Ugelmam, ɣelṭen aṭas. UXemmem d Tazmert, d aḥeqqi, yezmer ad yeffeɣ seg Tɣawsa, yezmer deg kra n tegnatin n lɣella neɣ deg yiḍ n tidi, ad yefriren ɣer imukan ibaɛden ad yeẓer u ad yeslel ayen yeqqimen deg imukan-nni.

Deg iduba n PARAPSICOLOGÍA xeddmen tajriba yettwassen maḍi ɣef wid yellan deg tegnit n LɣELLA.

Aṭas n wid yellan deg tegnit n LɣELLA zemren ad ssiflen s leɣlaḍ ɣef yiseɣras ɣef temsal, imdanen u tegnatin i yellan deg tallit n lɣella deg ibeɛden.

Imusnawen zemren ad ssiɛden deg imir i tejriba-nni, lḥeqq n isallen-nni. Zmaren ad ssiɛden lḥeqq n wayen yeḍran, ṣṣeḥḥ n wayen yeḍran.

S tajriba-agi n iduba n PARAPSICOLOGÍA, yettubeggen s timɛayin d التجربة belli Ugelmam mačči d UXemmem.

S tideț u s lḥeqq nezmer ad nini belli uxemmem yezmer ad yezzi s tallit d tallunt, yettwasemɣer seg ugelmam, ad yeẓer u ad yeslel ayen yellan deg imukan ibaɛden.

TIDEȚ n EXTRA-PERCEPCIONES SENSORIALES tettuɣer-d aṭas, ala i aserɣu neɣ i bu-tɣuru yezmer ad t-id-yekker ad iɣurr lḥeqq n EXTRA PERCEPCIONES.

Ugelmam yettwaxdem ad yessali amektub maca mačči d amektub. Ugelmam ala d allal n UXemmem, mačči d uxemmem.

Ilaq aɣ ad neɣer uxemmem s tmerniwt ma nebɣa s tideț ad neɛreḍ s tmerniwt ayen iwumi qqaren tayri.

Tarrwa d ilemẓiyen, d yergazen d tilawin, ṭṭfen axemmem yellan d win yessaɣen, yellan d win tḥeyyilen, yellan d win yettḥuzzun, yellan d win d-yettasen s urrif, atg.

Aṭas n yarrwa d ilemẓiyen yellan d win yellan yeṭṭfen imaren tqeccin i yimawlan-nsen d yiselmaden-nsen, ɣef wayen i ttidiren, bɣan ad gren akken yella bɣan ad gren yessen, yellan d win tqeccin, tqeccin d yettwali kra n yiseɣras i ɣef teḥqiren imeqqranen neɣ ur ten-qriben.

Akkenni ttaɛḍin iseggasen, akkenna nekkat deg leɛmer, uxemmem yeqqar aɣ yeqqar.

Uxemmem n leɛjuz yettwaxdem, yeẓẓi, ur yettbeddil ara ula d ddaw lmedfaɛ.

Iɣellaqen akka u akka ad mmten, ur ttbeddil ara, ayen akk ttaɣen seg yiwet n tɣara.

"CHOCHERA" n leɛjuz, tilawin-nsen, imektiyen-nsen yettwaxedmen, atg. d lḥerma, d adɣaɣ ur yettbeddil ara ula d udem. S wakka ad yini "GENIO Y FIGURA HASTA LA SEPULTURA".

Yettwaḥwas maḍi belli iselmaden d tiselmadin yellan d win yeṭṭfen ad yewwin PERSONALIDAD n inelmaden d tnelmadin, ad ɣren uxemmem iɛedden akken ad zmaren ad swerdin tilin-tudert s tmusni.

Yettɛeyyeb ad neɛreḍ aɛḍen, amek deg tallit yella yella yettwaxdam UXemmem.

UXemmem d win yemmuten n LḤEQQ, n tideț. UXemmem yettṛeggiɛ TAYRI.

Win yettwaṭasen mačči d win yettwaɣsen i TAYRI acku uxemmem-is yeččur d tajribiwin n tɛeyyeb, tilawin, imektiyen i yellan d win yettwaxedmen am tlemmast n wuzzal, atg.

Llan dinna, leɛjuz yerɣan yeqqar yezmer ad iɛreq amaḍaɣ, maca ayen i yellan yeḍran d win leɛjuz-nni ččuren d tagnatin tisexsiwin d tid yellan yeččuren tayri.

Ayen akk "VIEJO VERDE" u "TODA VIEJA VERDE" ttɛeddin deg tagnatin yellan d tid yeččuren d uzelmeg d tid yellan, qbel ad mmten u qaren akka d TAYRI.

TAYRI n leɛjuz d imposible acku uxemmem yettṛeggiɛ-itt s "CHOCHERAS" IDEAS FIJAS", PREJUICIOS", "CELOS", "EXPERIENCIAS", RECUERDOS", tagnatin tisexsiwin, atg. atg. atg.

UXemmem d win yerɣen n TAYRI. Deg tmura yellan d SUPERCIVILIZADAS TAYRI ur tettili ara acku uxemmem n widala yella yerna-d ɣer iduba, yesbaɛ tidi n yebanki, gasolina d celuloide.

Llan aṭas n tidula i uxemmem u uxemmem n yal wa yella yettwaddu maḍi.

Kra ṭṭfen UXemmem yettwaddu deg ABOMINABLE COMUNISMO, kra yellan, deg win yettwaxedmen deg CAPITALISMO.

Llan wid yeṭṭfen UXemmem EMBOTELLADA deg tɣerdayt, deg tkerkas, deg ureɣ ad yeggen, deg tɣara taɣezzfant deg waddad amaɣlal deg tḥella ɣer widala, deg tḥella ɣer wayen i ttreqqen, deg uguren n twacult, atg. atg. atg.

I widalaḥemmlen ad sduwen UXemmem, d kra n win yellan d win yegren aɛreḍ s tideț ad yerr tidula.

Ilaq aɣ a NERṚEṚ, UXemmem maca i widalaḥmmlen taseddawit, d kra n win yellan ad nelli deg tudert ur ṭṭef ara UXemmem yettwaddu maḍi.

Iselmaden d tiselmadin ilaq ad sselmeden i inelmaden d tnelmadin-nsen ayen akk. Ilaq ad sslemden i tlin-tudert ad anfen uxemmem-nsen, ad qriben-t, ad t-ɛerḍen, ala akka s TƐERRIWT yella ad qqimen ad neẓẓer uxemmem ad yeɣɣi, ad yeddem, ad yemmes.

Ala ayen yezmer ad yebdel ddunit ayen iwumi qqaren TAYRI, maca uxemmem yettṛeggiɛ TAYRI.

Ilaq aɣ ad NEƔER uxemmem-nneɣ, ad t-qriben, ad t-anfen ɛedden, ad t-neɛreḍ s tideț. Ala akka, ala nettwenneɛ amuqranen i iman-nneɣ, i uxemmem-nneɣ, ad nenɣ uxemmem n TAYRI u ad nelli d assefruh s tideț.

Wid yettidirin ffanten ɣef TAYRI, wid yettidirin xedmen tiɣratin ɣef TAYRI, wid yellan, bɣan TAYRI ad tewwet seg wayen tḥemmlen d wayen ur teḥemmlen, tiɣratin d ffantas, tignatin d tilawin, timsal d التجربة, atg. ur zmaren ad gren aɛreḍ ayen d tayri, yella yerna-d iqqimen d win yettwaɛdawen n TAYRI.

Ilaq ad neɛreḍ s tmerniwt ayen d aqriben n uxemmem deg waddad n usnuffeɣ n التجربة.

Aselmad, tiselmadettɣeyyiren aṭas n tikkal s lḥeqq maca kra n tikkal b tufɣa u war sebba, war ad neɛreḍ belli ayen akk yettwaɣsen ur yeɛdil ara yeqqar yettwaqqen deg uxemmem n inelmaden, assileɣ n akken yella yettwexdam, yella d asemɣer n tareqqɛut n TAYRI i MAESTRO, i la MAESTRA.

UXemmem yettṛeggiɛ TAYRI u akka yellan ayenMAESTROS u MAESTRAS n yiɣerbazen, Kolegios d Universidades ur ilaq ad țțun.

Ilaq ad neɛreḍ ɛedden akk id akken yellan aqriben n uxemmem i d-yeɣraen s tḥulya n TAYRI.

Mačči d tama ad nelli d baba neɣ d yemma n twacult, ilaq ad nessin AƔREQ. I yimawlan d yimmaten n twacult yeqqar ssnen tareqqɛut i tarwa-nsen acku ṭṭfen-ten, acku d wid-nsen, am win yeṭṭfen tbeskilt, taɣruẓa, taddart.

S tɣara n tareqqɛut, taseddawit, yeqqar iɛerrḍ s TAYRI maca ur yezmer ad nelli d TAYRI.

Iselmaden d tiselmadin n taddart-nneɣ tis snat iwumi qqaren taɣerbaz, yeqqar ssnen tareqqɛut i disipulos-nsen, i disipulas-nsen, acku d wid-nsen am winakka, acku ṭṭfen-ten, maca akka mačči d TAYRI. Tɣara n tareqqɛut neɣ taseddawit MAČČI D TAYRI.

UXemmem yettṛeggiɛ TAYRI u ala neɛreḍ akk i funktionalismos tettuɣen n uxemmem, tɣara-nneɣ taɣezzfant n tira, tidett-nneɣ tulmest, tid yettwanekken, tɣara tettuɣen n tẓerɣat i tɣawsiwin, atg. nezmer ad nili d assefruh, ad neggan si VERDAD ayen ur yelli ara deg tallit, ayen iwumi qqaren TAYRI.

Wid yellan, bɣan TAYRI ad teqqim d tasa n tmakint-nsen taɣezzfant, wid yellan, bɣan TAYRI ad tezzi seg iduba tettuɣen n tilawin-nsen, tiɣratin, tmeslaɣɣin, التجربة n tudert, taɣara tameɣlal n tẓerɣat i tɣawsiwin, tɣara tettuɣen n tira, atg. d win yesɛeddayen d TAYRI acku akka ur tettwamzel ara.

Wid yellan, bɣan TAYRI ad tewwet am NEKK YEQQAREQ, am NEKK EBREƔ, am NEKK TTIRI, rɣan TAYRI acku CUPIDO, DIOS n TAYRI, ur t-id-yekker ad yeqqar ad yeddem taseddawit ɣef YO.

Ilaq ad neɣɣer s YO, s MI MISMO, s SI MISMO ad t-id-yezzi tarrwa n TAYRI.

EL YO d adɣar n imektiyen, tiɣratin, tmeslaɣɣin, tkerkasin, tagnatin, tixsiwin, tigezzatin, tmeddiyin, d tkerkasin tisexsiwin, atg. atg. atg.

Ala neɛreḍ yal adɣar yettwamzel; ala neɣreɣ-it, neqrib-it deg ugemmaḍ, maca ula deg tmazirt d-yellan deg widala n uxemmem, yella yesɛedda yal adɣar, nelli deg tallit s tallit. Akka u ala akka neqqar taseddawit n YO.

Wid yellan, bɣan ad sduwen TAYRI deg tidula tiɣezzfant n yo, rɣan TAYRI, qqimen war nettat, acku TAYRI ur yezmer ara ad tettuɣer.

Taseddawit aɣ tqriben widala ad tettuqen ad tewwet TAYRI yella akken llan tideten-nsen, tiɣratin, tulmest-nsen, atg., widala bɣan TAYRI ad tewwet ɣer YO u akka d win yettwassen acku TAYRI ur yettiḥussu ara ɣer YO.

Iseɣwanen n tɣara, neɣ akken ad d-nini tagnatin, tagnatin yettwanefken, yeqqar TAYRI yeqqar ad yezzi akken ɣer iduba n tiɣratin-nsen, ticṛutwin-nsen, ɣelṭen, deg akka yellan tettuɣen.

Nemeslay ɣef tissegnatin!, ad yinien tɣara neɣ wid yellan d wid ɣef teṣṣuḍen tisexsiwin, d ayen i yellan iḍerren deg ddunit-agi, u yella d-yezzin deg teɣratin, tiɣratin, isehwen d isenfufen. Yal wa yeqqar wayen, yella id tɣratin-is, tiɣratin-is, tɣara-s ad tẓer tɣawsiwin n tudert u yeqqar TAYRI yeqqar ad tewwet am tmakint n ferrocarril deg iduba wuzzal yettwaxedmen seg uxemmem.

Kufɣan ɣellten iseɣwanen neɣ tagnatin!, akken ibaɛden seg lḥeqq.

TAYRI ur tettḥussu ara ɣer YO u tallit bɣan tikwit deg leɛnaq u ad yeddmen, teggen ad t-id-yezzi tiɣratin deg tɛeyyeb.

UXemmem yeṭṭef ad yeqqar. Irgazen yeqqar aɣ tili s tayeḍ. Tala tqariɣ deg irgazen s tayeḍ. Aselmad yeqqar aɣ inelmaden s tayeḍ, d tanelmadt s tayeḍ am akken n inelmaden-is ur gren ara tama yellan. Tideten akk yeqqar d win ABOMINABLE.

Win yettwalien tẓerɣat n tafukt yellan d tid yessnen u yeqqar aɣ-itt s tayeḍ, ur issin ara ad yeɛreḍ tḥulya i yellan di nnig n wallen-is.

Win yettwalien udrar yellan d win yesnen u yeqqar aɣ-itt s wayeḍ i wala deg izelli, ur yella aɛreḍ tḥulya n udrar i yellan di nnig n wallen-is.

Dinna yella COMPARACIÓN ur tettili ara TAYRI VERDADERO. Baba d yemma ssnen tareqqɛut i warraw-nsen s tideț, ur t-id-yekker ad t-ttqaren s walu, ssnen tareqqɛut u akka yella.

Aɛessas yella ssnen tareqqɛut i tala-s, ur yemdel ara ɣelṭa ad t-ittqar s walu, ssnen tareqqɛut u akka yella.

EL MAESTRO neɣ la Maestra ssnen tareqqɛut i inelmaden d tnelmadin-nsen, ur ten-id-yekker ad ten-yeqqar d akken, ur ten-id-yekker ad ten-ittqaren ger-asen, ssnen tareqqɛut s tideț u akka yella.

UXemmem IYEBDAD s tqaritan, uxemmem d akli n DUALISMO, yettṛeggiɛ TAYRI.

UXemmem yettwabḍen s wayen yellan d win tɣerret n win yellan mačči d win yezmer ad yeɛreḍ ayen yezzin, yeddem, yemmes.

UXemmem ṬṬEF AṬAS IƐEDDEN, Tmazirat, tmazirat widala, iregɣaten, maca wayen yesnen d ESENCIA, CONCIENCIA u yella deg alemmas.

Tallit yella DUALISMO yesɛedda, tallit yella uxemmem yesnen, SERENA, QUIETA, PROFUNDA, tallit yella ur ittaqar ara, dinna iɣedda LA ESENCIA, LA CONCIENCIA u akka ilaq ad yilli aɣbalu n EDUCACIÓN FUNDAMENTAL.

Ad nbeɛɛden gar OBJETIVO d SUBJETIVO. Deg OBJETIVO llan tɣerred yettɣadden. Deg SUBJETIVO llan Tɣerred d win tidi, SUBCONCIENCIA.

Ala la CONCIENCIA OBJETIVA yezmer ad yeɣer CONOCIMIENTO OBJETIVO.

Isallen n tidi i yettwassnen s Tidirin inelmaden d tnelmadin n akk iɣerbazen, Kolegios d Universidades, d win yella SUBJETIVA ad d-yezzi deg waggen.

El CONOCIMIENTO OBJETIVO ur yezmer ara ad yeddem war CONCIENCIA OBJETIVA.

Inelmaden d tnelmadin ilaq ad d-uɣen ɣer AUTOCONCIENCIA u deg imir ɣer CONCIENCIA OBJETIVA.

Ala seg CAMINO DEL AMOR nezmer ad neqqim ɣer CONCIENCIA OBJETIVA u CONOCIMIENTO OBJETIVO.

Ilaq ad neɛreḍ aqriben n UGUR YELLAN ƔEF UXemmem ma nebɣa s tideț ad nezzi CAMINO DEL AMOR.
