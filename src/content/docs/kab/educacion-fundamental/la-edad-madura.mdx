Argaz n leɛmer-is ilaq ad yissin amek ara iddabber ɣef uxxam-is, ad yeqqedem arraw-is.

Wid yețɛeẓẓun ad sbedden axxam d rrbeḥ deg wuɣurrufen d wid yuklalen ɣef tidett aɣennej.

Ur nețdafaɛ ara tiḥdaya neɣ d lemser, snat n teɣratin-a ḥerramen.
