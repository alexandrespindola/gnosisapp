Asmi ara tugwad ifurken, ttarun ddaw wafriwen iḥninen n tyaziṭ iwakken ad afen laman.

Yal ameccaq yugwaden yettazzal ɣer yemma-s, acku ɣur-es yettamen.

Ihi, yettubeggen-d belli AGWAD akk d unadi ɣef LAMAN llan daymen mazal ttwaqqnen.

Argaz yugwaden ad t-ihjumen yemcumen yettnadi laman deg tmeslayt-is.

Tamurt yugwaden ad tt-yehjem tmurt nniḍen, ad taɣ armatucen, tigelfatin, itekkiren n ṭṭrad, ad d-tesbedd iserdasen yerna ad tbedd i ṭṭrad.

Aṭas n wid ur nessin ara ad xedmen, yettwagwaden ɣer taluft ttunadin laman deg tmuḥqranit, yerna uɣalen d imakazen, imeṭṭafen, atg...

Aṭas n tilawin ur nessin ara, yugwaden ɣer tuggdi n taluft uɣalent d tlawin n zzenqa.

Argaz yesḥussen yugwad ad yeǧǧ wergaz-is, yettnadi laman deg tmeslayt-is, yettneɣ yerna deffir-s yettruḥ ɣer lḥebs.

Tameṭṭut yesḥussen tettneɣ umegrad-is neɣ wergaz-is, akka i tuɣal d tameṭṭut yettnɣen.

Tugwad ad teǧǧ wergaz-is yerna tebɣa ad t-teslamen, tettneɣ tayeḍ neɣ tettuɣal ad t-tneɣ.

Axxam yugwaden amdan ur yettxuḍuḍ ara axxam, yesṭerb i tɛaqdin, imḍebbren, idpulen, atg., iwakken ad t-iselmen, ma d taǧǧalt taḍelt i yellan d amdan n warrac ur tezmir ara ad tesseɣ amdan n wayen akk yesṭerben, ma d aɛdawen akk n temdint dima xedmen akka, deffir-s ad teffeɣ taḍelt s warrac-is ad ḍsen deg yiberdan neɣ deg yigenwan n temdint.

Ttrad akk yesɛa tamuli-s deg ugwad.

Tgestapuyen, imeḥtanen, iɣerbazen n tmerwut, Siberias, iḥebsan iweɛɛren, aseɣli ɣer tneɣrit, lxedma s tayri, tneɣri s tmes, atg. yesɛan tamuli-nsen deg ugwad.

Tidukanin tthejjmen tidukanin nniḍen ɣef ugwad; ttunadin laman deg tmerwut, ttmenden belli s tneɣri, s uḥeggem, atg. zemren ad uɣalen d ɣelben, d iqewwiyen, d imeqqranen.

Deg yexxamen n pulis aɣref, iɣerbazen n tmerwut, atg. ama deg agafa ama deg unzul, ttewqen imesseqtiyen, tugwaden deg-sen, bɣan ad ten-serrḥen iwakken ad afen laman i tdukli.

Tmuḥqranit akk, ṭṭrad akk, tneɣri akk, yesɛan tamuli-nsen deg ugwad akk d unadi ɣef laman.

Zik, yella unẓiḥ gar imdanen, ass-a, ugwad akk d unadi ɣef laman fukken taɣawt iweɛɛren n unẓiḥ.

Amddakkel yettixḍib deg umeddakkel-is, yugwad ad t-yuɣ neɣ ad t-yeḍḍer neɣ ad t-yexḍeb, yerna llan dima ɛeqlen-d iɛeqlen akk d imaẓalen am wi: "Ur ttedduɣ ara s deffir i umeddakkel-ik ameqqran". AHYITLERIYEN ttwalin-d belli AYA-a TELLA D URAR.

Ass-a, ameddakkel yugwad ameddakkel-is yerna dima yettwasen iwakken ad yekkes iman-is. Ass-a ur yelli ara unẓiḥ gar imeddakwlen. Ugwad akk d unadi ɣef laman fukken taɣawt iweɛɛren n unẓiḥ.

Castro Rus deg Cuba yettneɣ iɛeqlen n imezdaɣen yettwagwad belli ad t-fukken; Castro yettnadi laman s tneɣri. Yettamen belli akka i zemmer ad yaf laman.

Stalin, amcum akk d idammen Stalin, yessegrew Russya s tudrin-is n idammen. D tarrayt-a i yellan iwakken ad yettnadi laman-is.

Hitler yebna Gestapo, Gestapo iweɛɛren, i laman n tdukli. Ur yelli ara ccekk belli yettwagwad ad t-sserḥen, yerna ɣef way-a i yesbedd Gestapo iweɛɛren.

Taggugti akk n ddunit-a yesɛan tamuli-nsent deg ugwad akk d unadi ɣef laman.

Iselmaden akk d teselmadin n uɣerbaz ilaq ad slemden i yinelmaden akk d tnelmadin talwit n teɣzi.

D agguẓẓel belli arrac akk d teqcicin tteččuren s ugwad seg uxxam-nsen.

Arrac akk d teqcicin ttemḥeyyalen, ttettwakksen, ttettwagwaden, ttettuɣaben, atg.

D tammara n yemma-s akk d baba-s i yellan d iselmaden, ad gwaden ameččaɛ akk d ilemẓi iwakken ad yaɣer.

Di tama, ttwalin-d i ameččaɛ akk d ilemẓi belli ma ur yaɣren ara ad ssutren tiwizi, ad zegren deg yiberdan yettwagwaden, ad xedmen lxedmatin iḍellin am ussisa n uɣumri, ad rebbin iferden, ad zzenzen ibriḍen, ad xedmen deg lxla, atg. atg. atg. (Am akken lxedma tegga d tmuḥqranit)

Deg taɣbalut, deffir awal akk n yemma-s akk d baba-s yella ugwad ɣef mmi-s akk d unadi ɣef laman i mmi-s.

Ayen i yettwaggwaden deg wayen akk nesseɛreḍ-d, d belli ameččaɛ akk d ilemẓi ttmexṣaren, ttččuren s ugwad yerna deffir-s deg tudert xedmen amdan ččuren s ugwad.

Yemma-s akk d baba-s i yellan d iselmaden i yesɛan agguẓẓel ad gwaden arrac akk d teqcicin, i ilemẓiyen akk d tlufaḍin, s ttawil ur yettwaẓri ara ttheyyalen deg ubrid n tmuḥqranit, acku am akken i nesseɛreḍ-d, tmuḥqranit akk yesɛan, tamuli-s deg ugwad akk d unadi ɣef laman.

Ass-a, ugwad akk d unadi ɣef laman uɣalen tamurt iweɛɛren deg uzaɣar d iger iweɛɛren. Aɣlaf akk yugwad. Aɣlaf akk yebɣa laman.

Zik yella zemren ad tudduɣ s tilelli, ass-a tilisa ččuren s iɛessasen iɛeqlen, ttewqen udiwen akk d ifakkan n yal ɣerf iwakken ad sɛun azref ad zegren seg tmurt ɣer tayed.

Ayen akk d ayen d ayen d amaggar n ugwad akk d unadi ɣef laman. Yettwagwad udiw, yettwagwad udiw yerna yettnadi laman deg udiwen akk d isura n yal ɣerf.

Iselmaden akk d teselmadin n uɣerbaz, n uɣerbaz amenzu, n tesdawiyin ilaq ad fehmen aɣerɣer n wayen akk yerna ad ɛawanen i lɛiḍ n ddunit, s tussna ad slemden i tuddar tinaynin, ad asen-d slemden ubrid n teɣzi n unẓiḥ.

D AJEBBAR ad slemden i tuddar tinaynin ad ur gwadent, ad ur ttunadint laman deg wacuḍi neɣ deg win ur nezmir.

D ilezmat belli amdan akk ad yelmed ad yeɛteb ɣef iman-is.

Ugwad akk d unadi ɣef laman d iḍaqen iweɛɛren i uɣalen tudert d iger iweɛɛren.

Anda-anda ttizgan d iḥeqqaren, i yugwaden, i iḍaqen i yellan dima ttunadin laman.

Yettwagwad tudert, yettwagwad tmettant, yettwagwad ayen ara d-inin, "ayen yellan yettwagal", ad tḍeqqɛ tazɣint tanemdayt, tazɣint taduklit, tiggugti, idrimen, axxam icebḥen, tameṭṭut icebḥen, wergaz iweɛɛren, lxedma, ad taɣ lxedma, ad taɣ lxedma, ad teqqel fell-as lxedma, imsuyen, tikerrat, atg. atg. atg. yal ayen yettwagwad, ttizgan ɣer yal tama iḥeqqaren, i yugwaden, i iḍaqen, yerna wacuḍi ur yettamen ara iman-is d aḥeqqar, akk ttwaqqen d iqewwiyen, d igellilen, atg.

Deg akk tudrin tadukli llant d imaḍalen akk d tmaḍalin n umeddi i yettwagwaden ad ten-taɣen yerna ɣef way-a aɣlaf akk yettnadi laman i ttunadin ad uɣalen dima uɣalen uɣalen s uɣalen, xedmen tudert dima texzaz, dima tesɛa leɛtab, dima teṭṭef, dima teɣẓẓi.

Tiɣra akk, tiɣra timeqqaḥ, tɣiṭma akk, atg., yesɛan tamuli-nsen deg ugwad akk d unadi ɣef laman.

Iwakken ad ur ttaɣen ara idrimen, tazɣint, adabu, tiggugti, ttwaɛawen tɣariwin, tɣariwin, ttewqen, ttexlusen iwakken ad wqen s tuffra, atg.

Iɛeqlen n tmurt ad uɣalen ɣer tmuɣli n ussid ad sɛun iɣalen s ussid yerna ad ten-xelṣen s idrimen iweɛɛren, s ttawil yettwaggwaden ad negren win akk ara d-yeglun.

Tḥemmel adabu ɣef adabu iman-is yerna ad t-iselmen i lɛiḍ n idrimen d idammen.

Ibriḍen dima ttawin xabaren n aṭas n tikkelt n tmettant.

Aṭas n wid ttmenden belli win yettneɣ iman-is d aɣellil, maca win yettneɣ iman-is d aḥeqqar i yugwaden tudert yerna yettnadi laman deg uɣal s uɣalen n tmettant.

Amdan n ṭṭrad i yettwassnen am imdanen iḍaqen d iḥeqqaren, maca asmi ara mlilen s wudem d wudem s tmettant, tuggdi-nsen teqqaḥ, uɣalen d iɣersiwen iweɛɛren ttunadin laman i tudert-nsen, xedmen taɣbalut tatrart mgal tmettant. Deffir-s ttwaɛawen d AYELLILEN.

Ugwad ɣer tikelt yettwaɛwan s teɣzi. Win yettneɣ iman-is yettwakal am aqellil, win yettrebbin taɣawsa-s yettwakal am aqellil, maca deg unẓiḥ wid yettneɣen iman-nsen d wid yettrebbin tiɣawsiwin-nsen d iḥeqqaren.

Win ur yettwagwaden ara tudert ur yettneɣ ara iman-is. Win ur yettwagwaden deg wacuḍi ur yettrebbin ara taɣawsa-s ɣer tlata.

D AJEBBAR i iselmaden akk d teselmadin n uɣerbaz ad slemden i umdan s ttawil uzzil akk d uzzil, ayen yellan d ɣerz n unẓiḥ d ayen yellan d ugwad.

Ugwad akk d unadi ɣef laman uɣalen ddunit d iger iweɛɛren.
