Asmi ara ixefḍen ifrax, tteddaqen ddaw wafriwen n tyaziṭ iḥemmlen akken ad afen laman.

Uqcic i ixefḍen yetazzal ad af deg yemma-s axater ɣur-s yettḥulfu d laman.

Yettubeggen-d ihi belli AGWAD d unadi ɣef TILAS (laman) llan dima d imdukkal.

Argaz i ixefḍen ad t-ṭṭfen imeḥzuzen, yettnadi laman deg uqemuc-is.

Tamurt i ixefḍen ad tt-ḍefren tmura niḍen, ad taɣ tmedyazin, iḍeffuren, lbapur n ṭṭrad u ad tezdem iserdasen, u ad tenker ɣer ṭṭrad.

Aṭas n medden ur nessin ara ad xedmen, i ixefḍen deg lehmum, ttnadin laman deg uḍuli, u ttuɣalen d imeḥzuzen, imekkaten, atg...

Aṭas n tilawin ur nesɛi ara lefhama i ixefḍen deg lehmum, ttuɣalen d tiḥdayin.

Argaz aɣyur i ixefḍen ad yeǧǧ taḍeggalt-is, yettnadi laman deg uqemuc, yenɣa u deffir-s ilaq ad iṛuḥ ɣer lḥebs.

Tameṭṭut taɣyurt tenɣa taɛribt-is neɣ argaz-is, akka ttuɣal d tameẓẓagt.

Nettat texḍa ad teǧǧ argaz-is u tebɣa ad t-teḥrez, tenɣa ta niḍen neɣ teḥseb ad t-tenɣ.

Bab n wexxam i ixefḍen belli medden ur t-xellsen ara akkri n wexxam, yetḍelleb lɛeqed, idiman, aḍebsi, atg., akken ad yeqqen laman, u ma tettmettat d taɣribt u teččur d tarwa ur tezmir ara ad tesselḥu talba-agi, u ma yelɣeq bab n ixamen n temdint merra akka, deg taggara taɣribt ad teṛuḥ ad tseddu deg ubrid neɣ deg iɣarbazen n temdint akked tarwa-s.

Ṭṭrad merra yesɛa aẓar-is deg ugwadd.

Gestapos, aɛeyyeḍ, iɣerbazen n uḥebbas, siberias, iḥebbasin isehbaren, imnufayen, iqeddacen izemḥen, tira n tmedyazin, atg. sɛant aẓar-is deg ugwadd.

Tigawin ttkerrḍent ɣer tigawin niḍen axater ugwadd; ttnadin laman deg udurar, ttwalin belli s tmettant, taẓzert, atg. zemrent ad ilint d timseddar, timsebranin, tisɛayin.

Deg yexxamen n بوليس iufraren, ضد التجسس, atg. ama deg ugmuḍ ama deg umalu, ttaɛeyyḍen ɣef imsefsaren, ttagwaden deg-sen, bɣan ad ten-sseknun s tɣawsa i zemren ad afen laman i uwanek.

Aḍuli merra, ṭṭrad merra, timeẓẓagin merra, sɛant aẓar-is deg ugwadd d unadi ɣef laman.

Deg wussan niḍen llan zeddigi gar medden, ass-a ugwadd d unadi ɣef laman, ufsen tafat yesɛan lbenna iɛeqqlen n zeddigi.

Amddakkel yetḥezzir amddakkel-is, i axefḍen ad t-yakren, ad t-yeɣben, ad t-yesseqdec, u llan daɣen iwulen imcumen am wa: "WER TETTUƐẒER ARA TASEMMAMT I YEMDUKKEL-IK AMAZWARU". Hitlerianiin qqaren belli AWUL-AGI yeqqim seg WARƔ.

Amddakkel ihi ixeffḍ amddakkel-is u yessenqed IWULEN akken ad yeqqen iman-is. Ulac zeddigi gar imdukkal. Ugwadd d unadi ɣef laman ufsen tafat yesɛan lbenna iɛeqqlen n zeddigi.

Castro Rus deg Kuba yenɣa ɣef tmila n imezdaɣen i ixeffḍen ad t-fsen; Castro yettnadi laman s tmettant. Yettamen belli akka i zemmer ad yaf laman.

Stalin, amcum d aqehhar Stalin, yexneq Rrusia s ussenger-is yessanen idammen. Akka i yettnadi laman-is.

Hitler yesselhu Gestapo, Gestapo isehbaren, akken ad yeqqen laman n uwanek. Ur yeɛdil ara belli i axefḍen ad t-fsen u ɣef waya i d-yeffeɣ Gestapo yessanen idammen.

Lehmum merra n ddunit-agi sɛan aẓar-is deg ugwadd d unadi ɣef laman.

Iselmaden d tisselmadin n uɣerbaz ilaq ad slemden i yinelmaden d tnelmadin tilawt n ttqahḥa.

D lɛib belli i warrac d tullas yeččur ugwadd seg yexxamen-nsen.

Arrac d tullas ttaḥkimin fell-asen, ttakafen, ttegniqen, ttwatten, atg.

D lɛadda n watmaten d iselmaden, ad ɣniqen arrac d ilemẓiyen akken ad ɣren.

Ɣef umata qqaren i warrac d ilemẓiyen ma ur tɣerrem ara ilaq ad tessutrem tilelli, ad tesḍuɛrem taɣyalin deg iberdan, ad txedmem leqdic d imecṭaḥ am ad tecseddem tixerrițin, ad teɛebbadem tɣuriwin, ad tzenezem iɣmisen, ad txedmem deg uzɣal, atg. atg. atg. (Amzun leqdic d ulɣu).

Deg ubeddel, deffir imeslayen-agi merra n watmaten d iselmaden, yella ugwadd ɣef mmi-s d unadi ɣef laman i mmi-s.

Tazmalt n wayen akk nessawal, belli arraw d ilemẓiyen ttuɣalen d imdanen u ččuren ugwadd u deffir-s deg tudert praktikit llan d imdanen ččuren ugwadd.

Watmaten d iselmaden yesɛan rreḥmet n ucḍad n ugwad n warrac d tullas, i ilemẓiyen d teqcicin, deg ṣṣifa ur ttɛeqqlen ara iten-srusun deg ubrid n uḍuli, acku am akken newwi-d imeslayen, aḍuli merra yesɛa, aẓar-is deg ugwadd d unadi ɣef laman.

Ass-a ugwadd d unadi ɣef laman srusun akal arqaq d ǧaḥim isehbaren. Ddunit merra texḍa. Ddunit merra tebɣa tilas.

Deg wussan niḍen tzemrem ad teddum s tlelli, tura tilisa ččurent d iɛessasen imsellḥen, ḍellben iɣessiwen d isettan n yal ṣṣifa akken ad sɛun azref ad ɛeddin seg tamurt ɣer ta niḍen.

Ayagi merra d leḥsab n ugwadd d unadi ɣef laman. Texḍan seg wayeḍ yettuddun, texḍan seg wayeḍ yettasen u ttnadin laman deg iɣessiwen d ipapirat n yal ṣṣifa.

Iselmaden d tisselmadin n uɣerbaz, n ikuleǧen, n tesdawiyin ilaq ad fehmen rreḥba n wayagi merra u ad ɛawnen i lxir n ddunit, s tussna n uselmed i tiddar tutrimt, s uselmed i ubrid n ttqahḥa taḥeqqanit.

D TESSAS ad nesselmed i tiddar tutrimt ur texḍa u ur tettnadi tilas deg kra neɣ deg yiwen.

D lazem yal yiwen ad yelmed ad yeɛteb akter deg iman-is.

Ugwadd d unadi ɣef TILAS d tɣuṛutin tiɛeqqlin i srusun tudert d ǧaḥim isehbaren.

Anda ma tṛuḥem tella tegmaẓ, ugwadd, lweɛḍafen yeteddun dima ad nadin ɣef TILAS.

Texḍan seg tudert, texḍan seg tmettant, texḍan seg wayen ara qqaren, "ɣef wayen qqaren", ɣef ad yeǧǧ amkan aseddan, amkan asertan, tazmert, idrimen, axxam yelhan, taḍeggalt telḥeq, argaz yelhan, leqdic, ṣṣuɣ, lmonopolio, iɣerbazen, takerrust, atg. atg. atg. ɣer wayen merra texḍan, tella tegmaẓ s lekter, ugwadd, lweɛḍafen, maca ulac win yettamnen iman-is d agmuẓ, merra ttgallan d imseddar, tqahḥin, atg.

Deg yal ṣṣenf aseddan llan tmila d tmila n leṣlaḥ texḍan ad ten-jeǧǧen u ɣef waya ddunit merra tettnadi tilas i ilaqen ad ilint dima s lekter d lekter tiɛeqqlin, srusunt-d d ṣṣeḥ tudert dima tetwaggas, dima teɛdil, dima tezḍem, yesserɣel, ulac rreḥba.

Iɣeblan merra, d aɛeyyeḍ merra, ɣelliten, atg., sɛant aẓar-is deg ugwadd d unadi ɣef laman.

Akken ur tjeǧǧen ara taẓart, amkan, tazmert, tazmert, ttrewsin iɣeblan, iɣeblan n tɣuriwin, ttenɣan, ttaxellsen akken ad tenɣen deg usufi, atg.

Iɛezzayen n akal arqaq gren-d s lekter ad sɛun timeẓẓagin xellsen u sɛant adrimen, akken ad sseddaqen yal win yetḥezziren ad ten-yessebɛed.

Ḥemmlen tazmert ɣef tazmert iman-is u ttqen-tt seg ubeddel n idrimen d idammen aṭas.

Iɣmisen dima ttaken lexbura n aṭas n temsal n ukukru.

Aṭas ttamnen belli win i yeṭṭfen iman-is d amseddar maca ṣṣeḥ win i yeṭṭfen iman-is d agmuẓ yesɛan ugwadd ɣef tudert u yettnadi laman deg yiɣallen yesɛan tiddi n tmettant.

Kra n yiserdasen n ṭṭrad uɣalen d imdanen d leɛḍafen, maca mi ttwalan udem ɣer udem akked tmettant, ugwadd-nsen yella d asehbar, i uɣalen d iɣersiwen yesɛan timettaniwin ttnadin laman i tudert-nsen, s usemɣer ara yeɛdun ɣef tmettant. Imiren i sen-yeffeɣ sswal d IHEROYEN.

Ugwadd yezmer ad yeɣleb ttqahḥa. Win i yeṭṭfen iman-is yeqqim d amseddar aṭas, win i yeṭṭfen uqemuc yeqqim d amseddar aṭas, maca deg ṣṣeḥ imdanen yeṭṭfen iman-nsen d wid i yeṭṭfen uqemuc d tigmiẓin aṭas.

Win ur nexḍa ara ɣef tudert ur yettfeṭṭaḥ ara iman-is. Win ur nexḍa ara seg yiwen ur yetṭebbiɛ ara uqemuc ɣer tseddiṭ.

D TESSAS belli iselmaden d tisselmadin n uɣerbaz ad slemden i umezdaɣ deg ṣṣifa telfawt u teqqint, ayen yellan d TTQAḤḤA s ṣṣeḥ d ayen yellan d ugwadd.

Ugwadd d unadi ɣef TILAS srusun ddunit d ǧaḥim isehbaren.
