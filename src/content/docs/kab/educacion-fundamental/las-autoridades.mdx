Adabu yesɛa UNABAḌ, UDWEL yeṭṭef UNABAḌ. Imḍebbren, ccɛa, aserdas, imawlan, iselmaden, inebgaden n ddin, atg., ṭṭfen UNABAḌ.

Llan sin n leṣnaf n UNABAḌ. Amezwaru, UNABAḌ AGWERMAW. Wis sin, UNABAḌ AGDAL.

Ur sɛin ara lmeɛna INABAḌEN AGWERMAWEN neɣ AGWERMAWEN. Neḥwaǧ s tɣawsa IMNBAḌEN IMAḌALEN.

INABAḌEN AGWERMAWEN neɣ AGWERMAWEN čča ddunit d imeṭṭawen d leḥzen.

Deg uxxam d tesdawit INABAḌEN AGWERMAWEN tettɛebbit UNABAḌ imi kan d AGWERMAWEN neɣ AGWERMAWEN.

Imawlan d iselmaden i yellan d igwermawen, ass-a d inebgaden iderɣalen i iderɣalen, akken i d-nnan tira iqedsen, ad ṛuḥen meṛṛa ɣer lebḥar.

Imawlan d iselmaden i yellan d igwermawen ttḥettimen-aɣ deg wegrud ad nexdem ayen ur yesɛi lmeɛna, maca nutni ttḥasaben-t d ayen yesɛan lmeɛna. Qqaṛen-d belli d ayen yelhan fell-aɣ.

Imawlan d INABAḌEN AGWERMAWEN akken i d-ibeggen ttiɛamul n tarwa am akken d iɣersiwen, am akken d nutni ay yugaren at n wemdan.

Iselmaden d iselmadin ttuggun i ulenmaden neɣ ulenmadin, ttaɛqalben neɣ ttsaɛdin wiyaḍ. Tikwal ttaɣen s waṭas yal anelmad yettwagun ɣas ma ulac ayen yesseɣran, ttsellikin s tmenɣit i wuqader ameqqran i waṭas n ulenmaden neɣ ulenmadin i ur yesɛin ara lḥeqq-nsen.

Imawlan d iselmaden n tesdawit ddin ṛṛayen iɣelben i warrac, teqcicin, ilmeẓyen, tiqcicin, atg.

INABAḌEN ur yesɛin ara TIMAḌALIN xir ayen ur yesɛi lmeɛna i zemren ad xedmen.

Neḥwaǧ INABAḌEN IMAḌALEN. Yella lmeɛna n TIMAḌALIN d TIƐRIFTA TAMATART n YIXEF-IW, tiɛriḍt tummidt n YIZWARIWEN INNERNIWEN nneɣ.

Anagar win yesɛan deg tidet tiɛriḍt tummidt n YIXEF-IS, yerẓag deg lekmal. D ayagi ay d TIMAḌALIN.

Akk ddunit ttamen belli ttɛeqalen YIXEF-nsen, maca ayen yewɛren ad naf deg tudert win yessen tidet ixef-is. Iɣwran sɛan ɣef yixef-nsen tiyita iɣelben s lekmal.

Ad tessen yixef-ik teḥwaǧ TAɣZIN d TMEQQRANIN. Anagar s TIƐRIFTA n YIXEF AD YAWEḌ VERDADEMENTE ɣer TIMAḌALIN.

AɛEBBIḌ n UNABAḌ d sebba n AGWERMAW. Ulac UNABAḌ IMAḌALEN ara yaweḍ ɣer AɛEBBIḌ n UNABAḌ.

Kra n imselken ddan mgal akk UNABAḌ, ugugen INABAḌEN. Lɛeqliya-yagi d TIKERKAS imi deg akk ayen yexleq Rebbi, seg ucikrit ɣer tafsut, llan tiserɣatin d tiserɣatin, tigratin d tigratin, iserdasen i yifen i d-yeṭṭfen d iḍebbiren d iserdasen idrawen i yettwaṭfen d yettwaḍebbren.

Deg tazettat n tazzurin kan ad naf unabaḍ deg TEQJATT. Deg yal tgezza n tzerɣanin llan unabaḍ d uɣurar. AɛEZZU n leqdic n UNABAḌ ad yawi ɣer ANARQUÍA.

INABAḌEN n tallit-a iɣelben i nettidiren d AGWERMAWEN d ayen iban belli ɣef sebba n ayagi AMYELMAW, ttarran iɣilas, ttseɛdin, ttuɣen urɣuɣ.

Neḥwaǧ IMSELMANEN, imusnawen neɣ inebgaden n taṛwiḥanit, inabaḍen n unabaḍ, imawlan, atg., s lekmal IMAḌALEN. Anagar akka ay nezmer ad nexdem deg tidet DDUNIT YELHAN.

D ur yesɛi lmeɛna ad ttnadin ara belli ur teḥwiǧ ara iselmaden d inebgaden n taṛwiḥanit.

D ur yesɛi lmeɛna ad ttnadin ara leqdic n UNABAḌ deg akk ayen yexleq Rebbi.

Wid yellan d IXEF-INES, YESɛAN IZELɣAN, ttnadin belli IMSELMANEN d INEBGADEN N TAṚWIḤANIT, UR TEḤWIǦ ARA.

Ad nessaweḍ ad neɛqel AMANAZ d TLEKKEMT nneɣ. Ad nefehem belli neḥwiǧ INABAḌEN, IMSELMANEN, IMUSNAWEN N TAṚWIḤANIT, atg. MACA IMAḌALEN akken ad zmiren ad nḍebbiren, ad neɛiwnen d ad neddwen s ṣṣwab.

UNABAḌ AGWERMAW n IMSELMANEN yettaɣ aẓayer n yinelmaden. Ma yella yesselmad anelmad, aselmad agwermaw yeqqar-as ayen ara yesselmed, ttejra neɣ lmanẓar ara ad yelqeḍ d anelmad yeggad ur yessafay ad yeṭṭef aɣurar azwir n uselmad.

Ayagi mačči d aɣayer. Yessa ad yuɣal unelmad d aɣyayar. Ad yessafay ad iffeɣ seg uɣurar agwermaw n USELMAD AGWERMAW, akken ad yessiwel ayen yettḥuss s lekmal n ttejra, akk ayen d-ttawin ttejra, akk lmeɛna-s tameqqrant.

USELMAD IMAḌALEN ur yesɛi ara mgal tḥerri n wul.

IMSELMANEN yesɛan UNABAḌ AGDAL, ulac ass ara ad xesseṣen tamɣart n ulenmaden.

Iselmaden AGWERMAWEN ttaɣen s UNABAḌ-nsen tamɣart d tussna n ulenmaden.

IMSELMANEN yesɛan UNABAḌ AGWERMAWEN, ẓran kan ad ɣuḍen d ad ssinent aɣurar ur yesɛi lmeɛna akken ad ilenmaden ad zewqen iman-nsen.

IMSELMANEN IMAḌALEN sellemaden s ṣṣber ameqqran ulenmaden-nsen, ttɛawanen-ten ad fehmen uɣurifen-nsen, akken ad fehmen ad ttaɣen ɣef akk tuɣma nnsen d ad zenzaren s lekmal.

UNABAḌ AGDAL neɣ IMAḌALEN ulac ass ara yesseɣren TUSSNA.

UNABAḌ AGWERMAW yettaɣ TUSSNA d yettawi ttaḍa n yinelmaden.

Tussna kan ttaweḍ-aɣ mi nella deg tḥerri n tidet d IMSELMANEN yesɛan UNABAḌ IMAḌALEN ẓran tidet ad qadren TḤERRI N UTFAY.

Iselmaden AGWERMAWEN ttnadin belli ẓran akk d ttxesseṣen taɣdemt n yinelmaden ttɣimin tussna-nsen s uɣurar ur yesɛi tudert.

Iselmaden IMAḌALEN ẓran ur ẓran ara d ttnuddun ad lemden ttamnen s yiɣwan n ulenmaden-nsen.

Yessa ad ulenmaden n tseddawin, tesdawin d timseddawin, ad ɛeddin seg tiddwa n yigmamen ttwaḍebbren, ɣer tiddwa-a tennezmert n irgazen d tulawin d tḥerri akken ad zmiren ad qqaren mgal akk uɣurifen n tallit.

Ayagi teḥwaǧ IMSELMANEN IMAḌALEN, imusnawen i yettɛeqilen s tidet ɣef ulenmaden-nsen, iselmaden ttwakkksen s lɣella akken ad ur sɛin ara taɣwest n walyen n lɣella.

S lxuf akk USELMAD, akk bab n uxxam, akk anelmad, ttamen belli YIXEF-IS IMAḌALEN. YERẒAG d ayagi ay d TAɣZINT-IS TAMEQQRANT.

D ayen yesɛan ddqiqa ad naf deg tudert kra n wemdan IMAḌALEN d YERẒAG.

Iɣwran ttrewn mi yettɣumef tfekka d ttrewn mi tella tfekka deg taddart.

Iɣwran ttmuddun iɣerran, ttremḍen ttremḍen, ttkerrḥen deg iberdan ttremḍen, ttidiren deg yal ssɛa ttremḍen.

D ayen yesɛan ddqiqa ad yettwet uḥkaymiḍ tiserfit neɣ ad yeǧǧ deg taɣerrat kra n udlis neɣ taɣerrat-is. Akk agi yeḍru imi lḥekmaymiḍ yesɛa tiṭṭ tɣumḍ, ttrew...

D ayen yewɛren ad qeblen lɣwran belli ɣumḍen, akk ddunit ttamen belli yerẓag. Ma yella wa yeɛqel belli yesɛa tiṭṭ-is tɣumḍ, d ayen iban belli seg tazzwara-yagi ad ibdu ad yewɛer.

Anelmad yettu deg uxxam adlis neɣ takerrast i yessefk ad yawi ɣer tesdawit, tuḍfa-yagi teqqar-d ayen normal d d aɣbalu, maca d-teqqar-d, tbeggen-d, assad n tregniwint deg tiṭṭ n wemdan.

Imesɛudiyen n usensu n tɣiwanin, ttɛeddin tikwal ɣef ubrid, llan ɣumḍen d mi yerẓag d uɣalen ad fehmen belli ɛeddan ɣef ubrid d as-d-tekker ad uɣalen s udabu kra n iberdan.

D ur yesɛi lɣella deg tudert yella wemdan yerẓag d mi yella akka kra n lweqt, am deg uɣurifen n tergagit, tettwali i yixef-is deg yiwen lekmal. Tallitin-agi ur tettettut.

Argaz i d-yuɣalen ɣer uxxam-is mi yesɛedda akk tɣiwant, d ayen yewɛren ad yettmekti s wazal akk ixemmam-is, imeqqimen, irgazen, tɣawsiwin, tira, atg. atg. atg. mi ara ad yettmekti, ad naf deg umekti-s tilefqiwin timeqqranin i d-yeggraren d lɣella i ttegniwa n tregniwint.

Kra n ulenmaden n tusnakt ttuddun ad tidiren ALERTAS n lweqt ɣer lweqt, maca ttɣumuɣen, ma yella qqaren lɣella deg ubrid, mi d-kecmen ɣer kra n uɣerrabu akken ad ɣsen kra n tɣawsa, atg. d mi mi ssɛat tebda ad mektin tameɣra-nsen ad tidiren ALERTAS d DESPIERTOS n lweqt ɣer lweqt, d uɣalen ad fehmen belli ɣumḍen mi kecmen ɣer lɣerra-yagi, neɣ mi qqaren lɣella ɣer wemdan-yagi, atg. atg. atg.

Ad yili IMAḌALEN d ayen yewɛren maca ad yaweḍ ɣer assad-a ad yelmed ad yidir alertas d yettḥezzib n MOMENTO EN MOMENTO.

Ma nella nebɣa ad nawi ɣer TIMAḌALIN neḥwaǧ ad nessin yixef-neɣ deg yiwen lekmal.

Meṛṛa nella nesɛa YO, MI MISMO, EGO i neḥwaǧ ad neẓra akken ad nessin yixef-neɣ d ad nuɣalen IMAḌALEN.

D TTAɣWSA AUTO OBSERVARNOS, ANALIZAR d COMPRENDER yal yiwen n tɣawsiwin nneɣ.

Yessa ad nessen yixef-neɣ deg tɣiwant n tamɣart, tɣemrawin, leɛwayed, irgazen d teqcicin.

Tamɣart tesɛa aṭas NIVELES, timnaḍin neɣ iddepartiment SUBCONSCIENTES i yessa ad nessin s usser seg OBSERVACIÓN, el ANÁLISIS, taɣẓint n FONDO d PROFUNDA COMPRENSIÓN ÍNTIMA.

Kra n tɣawsa tezemmer ad teɣẓer seg temnaḍt n tusnakt d ad tesseɛ di teɣwin tɣumḍ deg tamɣart.

Ayen i yettwaḥwaǧ amezwaru d DESPERTAR akken ad nefehem amanaz-nneɣ, AMANAZ d URGUɣ. Dinna ibdu YO ad IMMAT seg lweqt ɣer lweqt. D TTTAɣWSA taɣezzfant n TUɣAMT N YO PSICOLÓGICO.

Kan mi nmatt d-yezzi SER tidet CONSCIENTE deg-neɣ. Kan SER i zemmer ad yessexdem tidet AUTORIDAD CONSCIENTE.

DESPERTAR, MORIR, NACER. Agi d tlata n lweqt n amyellmaw i yesɣawiyen-aɣ ɣer VERDADERA EXISTENCIA CONSCIENTE.

Yessa ad tewɛar akken ad IMMATTA d yessa ad temmett akken ad NACER. Win yemmuten ur yewɛir d-yugglen ɣer SANTO ESTÚPIDO. Win NACER ur yemmut d-yuggan ɣer INDIVIDUO n DOBLE PERSONALIDAD, tajast d tiyellma.

Asexdem n AUTORIDAD tidet kan zemren ad ssixedmen wid yesɛan SER consciente.

Wid ur sɛin ara SER CONSCIENTE, wid ur yelli ara IMAḌALEN, ttɛebbit UNABAḌ d yettawin aṭas n aɣyul.

IMSELMANEN yessa ad lemden ad qqaren d ulenmaden yessa ad lemden ad yekkren.

Wid PSICÓLOGOS i d-yettwaxxeren ɣer ttaqrar ddan aṭas imi ulac win zemren ad yeqqar deg tiṭṭ ma yella ur yelmid ad yekkren.

Yessa ad tessin ad taqra CONSCIENTEMENTE d yessa ad tessin ad telmid conscientemente.
