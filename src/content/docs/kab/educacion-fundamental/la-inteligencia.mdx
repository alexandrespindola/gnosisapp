Nettfahem belli aṭas n yiselmaden n umezruy n ddunit deg umalu n ddunit tteḍsen ɣef Budda, Kunfutsi, Muḥemmed, Ḥermis, Ketzakoal, Musa, Krishna, atg.

Bla ccekk, nufa daɣen ar tmerreẓ, taseḥqeq, ttsexxṛ-ayen d-ttɛeddin yiselmaden ɣef ddinat iqdimen, ɣef yillulen, ɣef tmucuha, atg. Ayagi akk d laqṣur n uɣil.

Deg yiɣerbazen, tesnawiyin, d tesdawiyin, ilaq ad ttmeslayen ɣef umawal n ddin s leḥṛama, s wazal meqqren, s tmusni i d-ixelqen.

Tarrayin n ddin ḥerzent azal ur yetmattaten, yettwamudden s ungal dɣa ɣef leḥwaǧat tusnaktin d umezruy n yal agdud, yal tdersi.

Ddinat akk sɛant iɣsan imseddafen, azalen ur yetmattaten, ttemyagaren kan deg tarrayt.

Mačči d aɣil ma yella d amasiḥi itteḍsa ɣef ddin n Budda neɣ ddin Uday neɣ Hindu axaṭer ddinat akk beddent ɣef ulsar amsedda.

Taseḥqeq n waṭas n yineẓriyen ɣef ddinat d imesbedden-nsent yusa-d ɣef ssem MARKSISTI i yessasnen iɣallen iḍaɛfen deg lweqt-a.

Yiselmaden n yiɣerbazen, tesnawiyin d tesdawiyin ilaq ad ddun d inelmaden-nsen deg ubrid n leḥṛama i wiyaḍ.

D lɛib dɣa d lexsas ma yella d aqeṛṛay s yisem n tnazri tanettalt, itteḍsa ɣef lǧameɛ, ddinat, tarbaɛt, iɣerbazen neɣ tiddukliyin timanen.

Mi ara ǧǧen iɣerbazen, inelmaden ad mlilen d imdanen n ddinat akk, iɣerbazen, terbaɛt, ula d aɣil ma ulac lxir ad ḥerzen laẓla deg lǧameɛ.

Mi ara ǧǧen iɣerbazen deffir ḥdac neɣ mraw-d-ssemus iseggasen n ulmud, ilemẓiyen d telmeẓyin ttufɣen d uɣyal d uṭṭes am wiyaḍ n yemdanen, ččuṛen d ulac d laqṣur n tmusni am ass amenzu mi kcemren ɣer uɣerbaz.

Yewwi-d ɣef inelmaden ger wayen-niḍen ad snernuyen ammas n wul axaṭer ur yelli kra d tussni. Yewwi-d ad nlemmed ad nḥuss i tesfatin ɣef ddunit, tẓuri n ttejṛa waḥdes-s, ucennu n uferṭeṭṭu deg tẓegwa, taseɣruyt n uẓawan d ini n tengeḍt tumlilt.

Yewwi-d daɣen ad nḥuss ad nefhem ɣer daxel akk ineglafen n tudert, am ungal n tmetti yeɣdarren deg lweqt-a deg nettidir, iberdan ččuṛen d yemma-s ur nzufan ara iddan d arrac-nsent iččan yerna ẓelben aɣrum, lebniat yemmuɣben anda ttidiren waṭas n twaculin tiẓuẓarin, iberdan iṭṭuqen anda ttizwiren waṭas n tkerrusay i yettlawin s zzit iḍuṛṛen iɣsan, atg.

Anelmad ara yeǧǧen iɣerbazen ilaq ad yamel ala d yiman-is d ugur-is, maca daɣen d yiman n yemdanen akk d waṭas n uguren n tmetti tamdanit.

Ayen yugaren akk d akken anelmad ara yeǧǧen iɣerbazen, ɣas ma yeqweɛ, ur yesɛi tmusni, tmuɣli-s teṭṭes, yettwahyyeɛ s tuẓẓda i uɣezdi ɣef tudert.

Yuwi-d lweqt ad nadin ad nfen ayen i qqaren TMUSNI. Amawal, tasenselkimt ur zmiren ara ad ṣefdin TMUSNI s wazal-is,

Bla tmusni ulac aleqliɛ aqqaṛan neɣ tamellaḥt ṣeḥḥan yerna drus deg tudert ad naf imdanen yesɛan tmusni.

Ayen yesɛan azal deg tudert mačči ala ad nessen awal TMUSNI, maca ad nɛic deg iman-nneɣ anamek-is.

Aṭas i yeṭṭamɛen deg tmusni, ulac aserkas ur yeṭṭamaɛ ara deg tmusni d Karl Marx yeḥseb iman-is yesɛa tmusni, yura taseɣruyt-is tamateryalt i yexṣren ddunit azal ur yetmattaten, tteɣḍaṛ n waṭas n yiqeccaden n ddinat yemgerraden, leḥṛam n yergazen irewlen, Budda, Imasiḥiyen, atg., aɣṛuṛ n waṭas n lǧameɛ, tɛeddiɛt n waṭas d imelyunen n yemdanen, atg atg. atg.

Kra yesɛan yezmer ad yeṭṭamaɛ deg tmusni, ayen iweɛṛen ad tili ṣeḥḥan.

Mačči s uzɣar n uselmed n idlisen, uselmed, asileɣ, kra ad yessewhimen imdanen, idrimen ad ɣsen iqeddaɛen d ulisan; atg. amek ara taweḍ ayagi i qqaren TMUSNI.

Mačči s AYEN NIḌEN, amek zemren ad awḍen ad sɛun TMUSNI. Tɣelṭen s uswir wid i yeḥseb belli tmusni tezmer ad tili s usnerni n AYEN NIḌEN.

Yewwi-d ad nefehem ɣer daxel dɣa deg yal aḥric n wallaɣ imseɣfen d wallaɣ ur nesɛi iman fell-as, ayen yellan d usnerni n AYEN NIḌEN iḍuṛṛen, axaṭer deg daxel yeffren s tuffra taɣerfant AYI-INU IṬEBYAN, NEKK, YIMAN-IW, yebɣa yerna yettnadi dima AYEN NIḌEN d AYEN NIḌEN ad yeqwa yerna ad yesserɣu.

AMIFISTUFILIS-A i nerra daxel, CCEṬAN-A, NEKK-A, yeqqaṛ: NEKK sɛiɣ AYEN NIḌEN n idrimen, tẓuri, tmusni ugar ɣur win-a, azal, ttḥila, atg atg. atg.

Win yebɣan s tideț ad yefhem ayen d TMUSNI, ilaq ad yelmed ad taḥuss, ad iɛic ad tt-yessileɣ s usenqed ɣer daxel.

Ayen akk yessegrawn medden ger tmedyazt tirmes n usmekti ur neṭṭifeɣ, uselmed deg wallaɣ, usileɣ n tudert, ttawi-d dima ɣer uɣrir n AYEN NIḌEN d AYEN NIḌEN. Akka ur ttwaɣen ara ɣer unamek n wayen akk ara ssegrawn.

Aṭas i yeqqaren idlis deffir ad t-ssersen deg usmekti ḥessan ɣef uzɣar n uselmed, maca mi ara ttwaɣen ad rren ɣef uselmed yettwarun deg idlis i qqaren, ulac-nsen ara yessinen unamek ɣer daxel n uselmed, maca NEKK yettnadi AYEN NIḌEN d AYEN NIḌEN n uselmed, AYEN NIḌEN d AYEN NIḌEN n idlisen ɣas ma ulac win yezdin uselmed n win ur yekṛeh.

Tmusni ur tt-yettawi uzɣar n idlisen, neɣ s usileɣ, neɣ s idrimen, neɣ s wazal, tmusni tezmer ad d-tekker deg-neɣ mi ara nefhem ayen akk d NEKK, mi ara nefhem ɣer daxel tuḍṭa tusnakt n AYEN NIḌEN.

Yewwi-d ad nefhem belli allax d ammas n AYEN NIḌEN. Ṣeḥḥan AYEN NIḌEN-a d yiman usnaki i yettalaben yerna allax d iɣef-is amenzu.

Win yebɣan ad yilli d aɣil ṣeḥḥan, ilaq ad yemmet ala d uswir n wallaɣ azawwiṛi, maca daɣen deg yal aḥric mseɣfen d win ur nesɛi iman fell-as deg wallaɣ.

Mi yemmut NEKK, mi ara NEKK yenneɣḍ, ayen i d-yeqqimen kan deg-neɣ d WIN-NEK ṣeḥḥan, WIN-NEK tideț, tmusni iḍebyaten i yettnadin yerna iweɛṛen.

Medden ttamnen belli allax d win ixelqen, tɣelṭen. NEKK mačči d win ixelqen yerna allax d iɣef amenzu n NEKK.

Tmusni d win ixelqen axaṭer telli n WIN-NEK, d aɣbalu n WIN-NEK. Ur ilaq ad nerr allax d TMUSNI.

Tɣelṭen s uswir dɣa s aleqliɛ wid i yeḥseb belli TMUSNI d kra yellan yezmer ad yettwaru am ujejjiḍ n leḥnana NEɣ kra tezmer ad tteɣseḍ am akken ttɣesen iduba n teɣdemt neɣ tili tesɛi taɣult meqqren.

Yewwi-d ad nefhem ɣer daxel akk ayen yeddan deg wallaɣ, akk uɣalen, AYEN NIḌEN-A tusnaki yessagrawen, atg. Akka kan ara d-teffɣeɣ deg-neɣ s tarrayt dɣa tamenzut times d-yelqen n TMUSNI.

Akk Mefistufilis-A i nerra daxel yettenneɣḍ, times n tmusni tixelqit tettban-d cwiṭ cwiṭ deg-neɣ, armi ad tesfeǧjeǧ s uṛeqqaq.

WIN-NEK ṣeḥḥan d LEḤBEB, seg LEḤBEB-a tetteddu-d TMUSNI iḍebyaten d taṣeḥḥant mačči n lweqt.
