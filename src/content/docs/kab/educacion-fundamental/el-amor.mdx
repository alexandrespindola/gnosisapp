Seg ussan ɣef yidisen n uɣerbaz, inelmaden d inelmaden ttfaqen, s tmuɣli timseddart, d acu-t wayen iwumi qqaren Tayri.

Tuggdi d uzelluɣ yettarran s tayri, maca macci d tayri.

Inelmaden d inelmaden zelluɣen ɣef yimawlan-nsen d yiselmaden-nsen, yerna yessefk ad ten-qadren yerna ggwaden-ten deg yiwet n tikkelt.

Iɛeggayen d tlemẓiyin, ilemẓiyen d tullas zelluɣen ɣef yimawlan-nsen ɣef wayen yeɛnan llebsa, lmakla, idrimen, tmezduɣt, atg. yerna iban d acu-t belli ttnadin ɣef taggaẓt, ssnun belli zelluɣen ɣef yimawlan-nsen ɣef wayagi ten-qadren yerna ula ten-ggwaden, maca ayagi macci d tayri.

Akken ad nmel d acu i d-neqqar, nezmer ad nbeddel s ṣṣeḥ d akken yal iɛeggal neɣ taɛeggalt, ilemẓi neɣ talemẓit, yesɛa taɣḍeft yugaren gar umeddakwlen-is n uɣerbaz, ɣef yimawlan-is.

S tidett iɛeggayen, tlemẓiyin, ilemẓiyen d tullas, ttmesslayen gar-asen, gar umeddakwlen-nsen, ɣef yisefra yellan deg wulawen-nsen ayen ur d-ttmeslayen ara ass-a ur d-ttmeslayen ara yimawlan-nsen.

Ayagi d ayen i d-yessenfalin belli ur yelli ara laman s tidett gar warraw d yimawlan, ur tella ara tayri s tidett.

Yessefk akken ad nefhem belli tella tafawalt meqqren gar tayri d wayen i d-yettbanen d leqder, taggwadi, uzelluɣ, tuggdi.

Yessefk ad nessen ad neqder imawlan-nneɣ d yiselmaden-nneɣ, maca ur yeffuk ara ad nerre leqder d tayri.

Leqder d tayri yessefk ad ilin ttidirent gar-asen, maca ur yeffuk ara ad nerr yiwen d wayeḍ.

Imawlan ggwaden ɣef warraw-nsen, arjunt-asen ayen yelhan, aselmad yelhan, tameɣra yelhan, taɛessast, atg. yerna ttnadin deg taggwadi-nni d tayri s tidett.

Yessefk ad nefhem belli bla tayri s tidett ur yezmir ara yimawlan d yiselmaden ad awin ɣer wurar i tuddar tamaynut s ṣṣwab ɣas ma llan yisumar yelhan.

Abrid yessakken ɣer tlemẓit yeččuṛ d yisumar yelhan aṭas.

Nettwali amedya yettwasnen deg umaḍal akk "INREBLEN BLA SSUK". D taɣwessa taɣurfant i yettwasnen deg umaḍal, akken ma tella. Aṭas n "IƐEGGAWEN YELHAN", yuɣal d win yettwaḥemmlen aṭas ɣer yimawlan-nsen, yuɣal d win yettwakksen, yuɣal d win yettwaryafen, uɣalen ɣer medden ur yettwaɛessasen ara, tenɣen uɣalen uɣalen ɣef tulawin, serqen, uggren s idisan n widak yessḍuɛun deg yal tama, ur ttwaliɛen ara iselmaden d yimawlan n twacult, atg. atg. atg.

"INREBLEN BLA SSUK" d ayen yettwakksen seg wugar n tayri s tidett.

Anda yella tayri s tidett ur yezmir ara ad ilin "INREBLEN BLA SSUK".

Lemmer imawlan n twacult hemmlen s tidett arraw-nsen ad ssnun ad ten-id-sɛeddin s ṣṣwab yerna ur ttilin ara "INREBLEN BLA SSUK".

Inreblen bla ssuq d ayen yettwakksen seg usɛeddiɛ yelhan.

Imawlan n twacult ur yesɛin ara tayri i yettaɛnan ad sɛeddun s ṣṣwab arraw-nsen.

Imawlan n twacult tamirant ttɣamin kan deg idrimen yerna tefkan-as i mmi-s ugar d ugar, tamekla taneggarut, uɣalen dllebsa taneggarut, atg. maca ur hemmlen ara s tidett ur ssinen ara ad hemmlen ɣef wayagi "inreblen bla ssuq".

Tineggura n tagi teɛdel ɣer waygar n tayri s tidett.

Tudert tamirant teɛdel ɣer yiɣzer bla lqeyyeɛ, bla lqeyyeɛ.

Deg ugelmam lqeyyeɛ n tudert, zemren ad idiren aṭas n tmura, aṭas n yeselman, maca aɣzer yellan ɣer yiri n ubrid, qrib yeqqar ɣef yicelliwin yeqqaren n tfukt yerna s tidett ayen i d-yeqqimen d adɣaɣ, d tameḥzelt, d tɣerma.

D imken ad neɛqel tabelluḥt n tudert s akken ma tella, ma ur nlemmed ara ad nehder.

Medden ttnadin deg uqader d taggwadi s wayen iwumi qqaren tayri.

Neqqader wid yellan fell-aɣ yerna nettagwad-ten yerna neqqaɛ kan belli nteḥmil-ten.

Iɛeggayen ttagwaden imawlan-nsen d yiselmaden-nsen yerna ten-qadren yerna ttnadin kan belli ten-teḥmilen.

Taggwad iɛeggal amesswi, tafirla, tamsirt yelhan deg uɣerbaz neɣ deg uɣerbaz, atg. yerna yettaɛṛef kan belli yetteḥmil imawlan-is d yiselmaden-is maca s tidett itettagwad kan.

Nezelluɣ i umahil, ɣer umazur, ntagwad ɣer tayri, ad neqqim bla axeddim yerna nttɛedda kan belli nteḥmil umazur yerna teɣramen ɣef yidles-is, tneɛqel ɣef tugniwin-is maca macci d tayri, taggwadi.

Aṭas n medden yesɛan tuggdi ad ḥessben yiman-nsen deg yisefra n tudert d lmut, taggwadi ad steqsin, ad nnadin, ad fehmen, ad ɣren, atg. yerna qqaren YO AMO A DIOS, Y CON ESO ES SUFICIENTE!.

Tennadin belli teḥmilen AṚEBBI maca s tidett UR TEḤMIL ara, taggwadi.

Deg tallit n umgaru tmeṭṭut teḥsu belli teqqar i argaz-is ugar dɣa tɛeggad aṭas n tazzla-is ɣer wexxam, maca s tidett ur tetteḥmil ara, ttagwad kan ad teqqim bla argaz, bla taɛessast, atg. atg. atg.

Taklaḥt taɣurfant, uzelluɣ, ad zelluɣ ɣef yiwen, macci d tayri. D TAGGWADI kan yerna ayagi akk.

Iɛeggal deg tira-is zelluɣ ɣer MASSA neɣ TAMESTUT yerna iban belli ittagwad ɣer TEXPORTACIÓN, tamsirt yelhan, ɣer uɣrem yerna aṭas n tikkal yettaɛṛef kan belli TEḤMIL maca ayen yettḍerun d akken itettagwad.

Mi tmeṭṭut tella deg tlalit neɣ deg tuɣẓint n lmut ɣef yal aɣezzawan, argaz itettɛedda kan belli yetteḥmil aṭas, maca s tidett ayen yettḍerun d akken ittagwad ad tt-ifeq, zelluɣ ɣef lɣerḍ-is deg waṭas n tɣawsiwin, am, d lmakla, ssɛada, tuggdi n tlebsa, tɛessast, atg. yerna ttagwad ad tt-ifeq. Macci d tayri.

Medden akk qqaren belli taɣwessant akk i medden akk maca ulac: D ayen yettḥussun ad tafedh deg tudert yiwen i yessnen ad YAḌ S TIḌḌET.

Lemmer imawlan ḥemmlen s tidett arraw-nsen, lemmer arraw ḥemmlen s tidett imawlan-nsen, lemmer iselmaden ḥemmlen s tidett inelmaden-nsen ur yezmir ara ad ilin imgerraden. Imgerraden ad ilin d imken deg yiwen seg meyya.

Acu i d-yettḍurun d akken medden ur fehmen ara d acu-t tayri, dɣa yal taggwadi d yal taklaḥt taɣurfant, d yal taseɛɛadt, atg. tettennadin deg wayen iwumi qqaren tayri.

Medden ur ssinen ara ad YAḌ, ma medden ssinen ad yaḍen, tudert ad tili d lǧennet.

IWERḌAN ttnadin belli tteḥmilen dɣa aṭas ula zemren ad uggren s idammen belli tteḥmilen. MASSA Kan ttidiren. Tasɛadt, tgellet n idrimen dɣa tezwar ɣer lqaɛa.

TASƐAḌT tetteḥmil TURT d WUL. Yal TASƐAḌT itettɛedda kan deg wul.

D aḥerfi ad tafedh deg tudert yal tmeṭṭut yettwaɣen. Abunden tmeṭṭutin n TSEƐAḌIN maca d aḥerfi aṭas ad tafedh tameṭṭut n ɣef wul.

Igellil akk cennun ɣer TAYRI maca ur ssinen ara d acu-t TAYRI yerna ttnadin ɣer TSEƐAḌT ɣer TAYRI.

Ma yella kra yettɛedday deg tudert-agi, UR TTEƐDELL ARA ɣer TSEƐAḌT ɣer TAYRI.

LA PASIÓN es el veneno más delicioso y más sutil que se pueda concebir, siempre termina triunfando a precio de sangre.

TASƐAḌT d SEXUAL di meyya, TASƐAḌT d aɣersiw maca kra tikkal ula teɣzi yerna teǧehhed aṭas. Di yal tikkelt yettennadi ɣer TAYRI.

Iselmaden d tiselmadin yessefk ad slemden i inelmaden, ilemẓiyen d tullas, ad frarqen gar TAYRI d TSEƐAḌT. Kan akken ad sɛeddin kra n yisefra deg tudert.

Iselmaden d tiselmadin uɣuren ad idhren taɛeggeẓt n inelmaden yerna ɣef wayagi yessefk ad ten-idheyyen s ṣṣwab akken ad ur uɣalen ara d iɣezzawanen deg tudert.

Yessefk ad nefhem d acu-t TAYRI, wayen ur yezmir ara ad yettwaqfel d taseɛɛadt, tigrawin, taggwadi, azelluɣ, taseddiqt taɣurfant, atg. atg. atg.

TAYRI ɣas ma ulac deg warraw n adam, maca ula d kra yellan d win yezmer ad YETTWAḌḌEF, ad yaɣ, ad yekkes am ujeǧǧig n wussu, atg.

TAYRI yessefk ad ILUL degneɣ yerna ILUL kan mi nefhem s lqeyyɛ d acu-t AKRIS i d-newwi degneɣ, d acu-t TAGGWADI, LA PASIÓN SEXUAL, tuggdi, taklaḥt taɣurfant, uzelluɣ, atg. atg. atg.

Yessefk ad nefhem d acu-ten yisemɣaren IWAQURAN, yessefk ad nefhem amek yettwaɛdalen degneɣ mačči kan deg uqellaɛ uɣurfan n tudert, maca ula deg yiqellaɛen nniḍen yettwaɣen d wid ur yettwassnen n SUBCONSCIENTE.

Yessefk ad nekkseɣ seg yiqerraɛen yemgarraden n uɣerbaz yisemɣaren nni. Kan akken dɣa ilul degneɣ seg yiman-is d wesɣan, wayen iwumi qqaren TAYRI.

D imken ad nerzu aḍal bla talɣa n TAYRI. Kan TAYRI tzemmer s tidett ad teggar aḍal.
