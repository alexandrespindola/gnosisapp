AZWEL

Seg V.M. GARGHA KUICHINES

"Ttagrawla Tameqqrant" n Umasɣan Ameqqran Samael Aun Weor tebzegzay-d s telɣiwt amek i neṭṭef amkan deg tudert.

Ilaq ad neqleɛ ayen akk i ɣ-iqnen ɣer rruman n ddunit-a.

Dagi i nnejmaɛ tira n yal agezdan i wakken ad neǧǧ amur i usentel n win ara yeṭṭef abrid n Tameɣra mgal iman-is.

Tiftilin akk n umawal-a tesserriḥ-d ɣer tneɣɣi n Yiyyaten-nneɣ, akken ad nserreḥ Tameddurt-nneɣ i d ayen yesɛan azal deg-nneɣ.

Yiyye ur yebɣi ara ad yemmet, bab-is itett-it wugur.

Deg ddunit d agerruj n wid ur nezmir, tugdi tezzi-d ɣef yal tama.

"ULAC AYEN YESSEGLUFEN, AYEN YELLAN D IRAKAZEN UR NEZMIRARA".

# AGEZDAN 1

Amdan yettwexser si tegẓert taɣerfant; ayen yellan s ufella yessegra-d kullec. Ḥnana tettwaɣez. Tixerci tesɛa wid ittabaɛen. Tilelli ur telli ara axaṭer lɣaci ttidiren zedɣen, ḥeznen.

Lxeṣṣa n wid yettɛeqqiben deg ufus n wid yettḍallilen n yal tawsit.

# AGEZDAN 2

Lǧuɛ d lemɣella ttnernin yal taftit, lɣawali ikimiyen tneɣɣen atmusfir n tmurt, maca yella d asafar mgal cceṛ i ɣ-yezzin: "Tessentiyazt Tusnakt" neɣ asexdem n uẓru n wemdan nessers-it d Tẓuri deg usensenni-nneɣ, sinna d Tafat d Times mi nlemmed amek ara nsemraray 3 n imsellinen n usellek n tmusni: 1. Tmettant n wuguren-nneɣ. 2. Ad nesbedd igerdan n tafat deg-nneɣ. 3. Ad nqeddem i Taleffust Taɣribit (Amdan).

Akal, aman d waḍu, ttwakessen s sebba n tameddurt-a; ur yettaweḍ ara ddheb n ddunit i wakken ad yesselḥu cceṛ; ad aɣ-yessenqed ddheb ilelli i netteddukul kullec, izuran-nneɣ n tidet, nessumer-it s lebɣi s tmusni n sebba, akken ad nezmir ad nessenqef ddunit d ad neqded s tmusni d-yekren.

Nettbedd-d Tserdasn n Umeslek n ddunit s wid akk yesɛan leḥmala i d-iqelben s umesɣan n Acwariu, ɣer tira n Tselɣa n tmersilt i aɣ-yesserḥen si yal cceṛ.

Ma tettesnimɣureḍ iman-ik, ttessenimɣur ddunit.

# AGEZDAN 3

I waṭas, lferḥ ur yelli ara, ur ẓrin ara belli d axeddim-nneɣ, belli d aɣenjar-is, d imsedduyen-is; nesbedd-it s ddheb-nneɣ ilelli, Izuran-nneɣ.

Mi nella d imeqqranen nettḥussu d ameqqranen, maca ttlaɛen-d taftitin-a; ma ur tesɛiḍ ara tagella ɣef wallaɣ-ik taɣerfant, aț-țiliḍ d akli-is, axaṭer nettat ur tettɛayyad ara s wacemma. Ilaq ad ttidireḍ deg Ddunit war ma teddiḍ d akli-is.

# AGEZDAN 4 YEMMESLAY-D ƔEF TILALT

Tilelli teḥweǧ-aɣ, nesseɣ-d ad nili d ilelliyen, maca ttmeslayen-d fell-aɣ d ayen diri, nettruḥu s leḥǧab, akkagi nettuɣal d wid yettḍeggiren d neḍriren, d neɣriren i yettɛemmiden.

Win ittɛawaden ttɛawaden izenzaren n tdiɛit, d amcceɛ zdat win i d-tɛeqqeb; axaṭer awa d aqejmar, d tameẓẓuɣt neɣ d win yettɣellten s tidet; win ittɛawaden ittɛawaden-iten d inelmaden n tmuɣri, d amcceɛ deg tissist. "Rfut Tideț aţ-țiliḍ d ilelliyen". Maca amek ara yessaweḍ win yettakken sɣur Tideț? Deg waddad-a, yettɣelli yal taftit s usentel uɣrib, Tideț.

Tideț d agerruj n Bab-nneɣ Ḥnini, akka am Lameɛna. Amek ara yesɛu lameɛna win yettakken, ma d-yettunefk-d sɣur Baba? Tufatin n Baba ur yezmir ara ad yewwi win yeččuṛen d wuguren, d tmeskitin, d anezdi n tagella d teɣellawt. Nella d aklanen n uɣurruy-nneɣ; rewel sɣur Win Yettwaliwin i yemmeslayen ɣef wayen i nettwalin deg-s; win yettzenzi igenni d kullec a s-yettwakkes.

"Anwa i d ilelli? Anwa i d-yessewweḍ tilalt-a? Acḥal i yuɣalen d imezwura? Awa, Awa, Awa!", (Samael). Win yettakken ur yezmir ad yili d ilelli, axaṭer yella mgal Win Ḥnini i d Tideț tezgert.

# AGEZDAN 5 YEMMESLAY-D ƔEF UZELLUƔ N UɣERRU

Kullec yettruḥ, yettɛawad-d, yettaɛli yettɣelli, yettruḥ yettɛawad-d; maca lɣaci ttaɣen-d kan deg wallaɣ ayen yettɣerrin ɣer tlisa-s zdat wayen yettɣerrin ɣer yiman-is d akkagi i ttḥekkimen deg yilel n tudert-nnsen, ssexdamen tiẓri-nnsen i d tid yettɣelten i wakken ad sseqsan asemlili n tmezdaɣ-is; d netta d acu? Mi yemmat umdan ɣef yiyyaten-is neɣ wuguren-is, yettserreḥ, yettserreḥ si waṭas n iseklaṭen i nesbedd-d, yesseɛɛa-d anezdi n tilelli.

Tafugra d tid tettwaggzan, ilaq ad nraǧǧu azzayen, d leḥsab n tɛewniqt.

Tamentilt teqqim terriken sdat umeslay yettwacḍen, tameẓẓuɣt tettɣenni zdat tideț tezgert. "Seg tneɣɣi kan n wayen yettɣellten ad d-tban Tideț" (Samael).

# AGEZDAN 6 TAZWARA D TIDEȚ

Yelha ad yeqqer win yeqqaren s tilelli agezdan-a akken ad yeqqer ad yettaɣ-t s wacu yettɣellten; ma nella nesɛa wuguren n wallaɣ, d tmeskitin, d taɣeznant, tazwarin-nneɣ ahat ad ilint d tid yettɣellten; aya n: "Akka i tella axaṭer ssudneɣ-t", seg win yettɛaqalen, kullec yesɛa tiẓriwin, tiɣerɣart, iẓuran, taɛlayt d taddart, tuggza, lawan, anda win yettɛaqalen yettwali ayen s wacu i yettɣelli, yettessawal-it s lḥerma, i gessawal ɣer wid yeqqaren.

# AGEZDAN 7 TUSNANT N TMUSNI

Nessen d aya i ɣ-yesselmaden, belli nezmer ad nesselek tmusni-nneɣ kan ma nxeddem u nxelles s lebɣi.

Win yettḥussun deg Ubrid yettsekkir Tẓuri n tazwara n tmusni mi yettaru ayen yettɛemmid deg tudert-is.

Aselmad yesɛan afud, yettɛemmid deg Ugaraw n Tudert, ur yettaru ara Ugaraw-a, iṭṭafar-it am imqerri deg tmeɣra n tudert; din am deg ssima, imqerri ttbegginen-d ayen yettɛaggwen neɣ ayen yettwaɛaggwen. Aselmad n Tudert d win yesselmaden ayen yelhan yellaɛen i win yettḥussun deg ubrid, itteddu s ayen yelhan am wa, Tmeɣra teṭṭafaɛ-it, lɣaci ttabaɛen-t s Leḥmala.

"Tamusni d Tafat i d-yerɣan" (Samael Aun Weor) i win yettḍsen yettɛemmid s Tafat n Tmusni, ayen i d-yeffɣen ɣer uderɣal s Tafat n Tafukt.

Mi tettaɛli talɣa n tmusni-nneɣ, yal yiwen yessuden deg wayen yellan deg-s, wayen yellan d-a.

# AGEZDAN 8 TIWERSIFT TUSNANT

Lɣaci zdat ttɣawsiwin n tmeɣra ḥeznen, ttaɣen-d kan ad ɛeddin; tussna teqqen-d tizemmar yerna tessemma-tent s yismawen yettɛeqqben, akken wid ur nessin ara ad sseḥyifen.

Llan d imelyunen n imuddiren i yessnen ismawen n yuguren-nnsen, maca ur ẓrin ara amek ara ten-neɣ.

Umdan yettɣelli s lemɣawla igerdan i d-isbedd, maca ur yezmir ara ad yemraray igerd-is: Agiles anda yettḥetti yal taftit; umdan i wakken ad t-yessen, yettḥetti ɣur-s ayen i tessenɣinen ɣer usensenni s ẓẓewqat neɣ s tid yettwaɣzanin; maca umdan yettunefk ɣur-s ad t-yessegzi, ad ineɣ wuguren-is, tmeskitin-is, ttaɣeznant-is, atg., ur yezmir ara, yesseɣ d azɣar yal ass d aya.

# AGEZDAN 9 ANTICRISTU

Nettewwi-t deg-nneɣ. Netta ur aɣ-yettajja ara ad nessiweḍ ɣer Bab-nneɣ Ḥnini. Maca mi nesseɣḍ-it kullec ɣur-s, d aṭas deg umeslay-is.

Anticristu yexser agerruj n Lameɛna, Asɣur, Tmeẓẓuɣt, atg. "Argaz" ittɛebbed tussna-s d yettabaɛ-itt.

# AGEZDAN 10 YYAD N WALLAƔ

Ilaq ad nettwali deg axeddim-nneɣ yal taftit, ad nessen ma ayen i nexdem ittesnimɣur-aɣ, axaṭer tneɣɣi uɣrib ur aɣ-teffriɛ ara. Aya yettak-aɣ kan ad nessen belli nella d wid yeqqilen aṭas, maca aya yelha mi nneɣ deg-nneɣ cceṛ-nneɣ, akken ad ntesnimɣur akken yebɣa Umessih yettidir i nettewwi deg tissist i wakken ad nessenqef d ad nessenfali Amdan.

Ad nselmed ad nekrah, aya ssnan-t kullec, maca ad nselmed ad nettaɣ, aya yettɛawqel.

Qqar s lebɣi win yeqqaren aya, ma yebɣaḍ ad tneɣ si izuran cceṛ-ik.

# AGEZDAN 11 ƔER 20

Lɣaci teḥla-yasen ad meslayen, ad sbedden ɣer wid akk amek i ttwalin, maca ulac win yebɣan ad yessen iman-is, i d ayen i yesɛan azal deg Ubrid n Tselɣa.

Win ittakkren yettakken aṭas ittezzi ɣer lemoda; Tafat d tmusni, mi tessenfali deg-nneɣ, d axeddim aɛlayan. "Seg yexdimen-is a tent-tessnem", i d-yenna Ɛisa Umessih.

Ur d-yenna ara seg tɣersiwin i xedmen. Gnusiyen... kret!!!

Argaz yesɣuruy neɣ yessefraren ixeddem akken yebɣa i wallaɣ-is neɣ tisfifraren-is. Awa am yiqerriḍen d imcceɛen, sselɣen-d ayen i ten-yerran d igerwan d yessudunen neɣ ttunefken am tideț n Sidi Rebbi, ayen i ttwakken sɣur Ukettar imeqqran akken am wa.

Anda id-yella Tafat, tella d tmusni. Tameddurt d axeddim n taḍult, awa ur d-yettili ara seg tafat.

Deg agezdan 12 yettmeslay-d ɣef 3 n yellaɣen i nettewwi: Agwallal Sensual neɣ n tẓri, Agwallal Aberkan; awa d win yettamnen kullec yettuselɣayen iḍfar akken yebɣa win yettɛaggwen neɣ win yettɛawanen; mi yettunefk s tmusni, d amɣar d ameqqran, yettbedd am tazzla n uqeddem; ayen yettuzellɣen deg ugwallal aberkan yessbedd aɣurruy-nneɣ.

Win yesɛan lameɛna n tidet, ur yeḥwaǧ ara ad yeqqer; win ittakkren ur yezmir ara ad yesɛu lameɛna, agerruj n Sidi Rebbi d usudem s tidet, neɣ agwallal deg-nneɣ, i d-nebzen mi neqqen i wid ur nebɣi ara i d-nettewwi deg Tmeskert-nneɣ.

Agerruj n uẓri n wuguren-nneɣ, sinna a tent-nezdem d sinna ad ten-neɣ s tɛiwni n yemma-tneɣ RAM-IO, yettajja-aɣ ad neqqleb ur nili ara d aklanen n wid yettɣerrin deg uɣurruy.

Yiyye, Aɣrem, d aɣwerru deg-nneɣ; Ser kan i yesɛan afud ad yessbedd aɣerru deg-nneɣ, deg Tmeskert-nneɣ.

Seg usudem s lebɣi n agezdan 13, nettɛeqqeb ayen yettɛemmiden s Win Yettwalin Yettɣellten, mi d-yezdaɣ s Yiyyaten ur nebɣi ara n yal gma n Ubrid. Mi netyettwali iman-nneɣ nettaǧǧa ad nemmeslay d ayen diri n wiyiḍ.

Ser d Uẓri, ilaq ad ttwazganet gar-asen; akka i yettalel tmerga. Uẓri, war tmusni n Ser, yettɣelli d aɣwerru n wallaɣ n yal tawsit; yettalel lqeccac.

Ma yella Ser yugar Uẓri, yettalel win yettḥussun yettɛaqqeb. Agezdan 14 yettunefk ɣur-s iɣerran imeqqranen i wakken ad nessen iman-nneɣ; Nella d Sidi Rebbi i d-yerɣan, i yesɛan aɣerbuz am idis-is ur yettunefk ara; ad tneɛli akk aya d aserreḥ, ad inin...

"Taddart teqqen s tɣermit n Uqerriḍ, s tqermut n Uselmad, s taɣawsa n uɛeggar, s tɣawsa n Mass d tqermut n Umessih" (Samael).

Yemma-tneɣ Taɣarastit Marah, Meryem neɣ RAM-IO akka am i t-nessawal gnusiyen, d tameɣrut gar baba-nneɣ Ḥnini d nekkni, tameɣrut gar Sdi Rebbi uɣriben n tmeɣra d usenɣar; seg-s d seg-s, iɣerɣalen n tmeɣra tṭafɛen-aɣ. D Taɣarastit-nneɣ Taɣarastit, tameɣrut gar Tmeɣrut Taɣarastit Taɣarastit n ddunit d agiles-nneɣ, i wakken ad nessiwḍ ɣer ttɣawsiwin timɣranin d ad nqeded ɣer iɣriben-nneɣ.

Seg Umerlil Sexual ɣer tmeṭṭut yettqeddmen, argaz yettarwa tameṭṭut, tmeṭṭut tettarwa argaz; yemma-tneɣ RAM-IO d nettat kan i yezmiren ad qqlen d iɣerɣalen n wakud i Yiyyaten-nneɣ d iɣerfan-nnsen. S tiktawin tinselḥayin ur nezmir ara ad nessen ayen yettɣerrin ɣer Ser, axaṭer tissurifin d iqarren, iččuṛen d wuguren, akka am wacu yebɣa bab-is; ḥwaǧent ttasnireɣɣen, i neqqen deg-nneɣ wuguren, tmeskitin, ttaɣeznant, tiɛeqqabin, ttagmatin, d ayen akk yesseɣ-en wallaɣ aɣerfan, i ɣ-yettunefken aṭas n tuga.

Deg agezdan 18 nettali, akken yebɣa Uɣrib, belli akka am akken i nettidir deg tmurt neɣ deg amkan n tmurt, akka am akken deg tγeri-nneɣ yella umkan anda nettaɣ amkan. Ɣer win yeqqaren agezdan-a akken ad tessneḍ gar-ak deg-i tanirt, tɣert neɣ amkan i tettiliḍ.

Mi nessaɣ-d i yemma-tneɣ RAM-IO netneɣɣ Yiyyaten-nneɣ yessatanen d nserreḥ deg 96 n tɣerɣatin n tmusni, n ayen yuɣalen. Takerhi ur aɣ-yettajja ara ad nettali deg.

Win ittakkren yettḍawer mgal Baba-s d win yettqeddsen mgal Uɣerfi Qedsan; yettqeddes deg wallaɣ, deg wawal d yexdimen.

Llan d imɣanen yettmeslayen s lemɣawla i iman-nnsen, ttekkesen aṭas n wid ur nessinen, maca ma nezdmen axeddim-nsen, nettali tneɣɣi d anarqui; tudert tezga tettajja ad ttwaneɣ.

Deg agezdan 19, yettunefk ɣur-s tafatin akken ur ɣ-d-ittili ara agurruy ad nili d imeqqranen. Nella kullec d inelmaden i yettqeddemen ɣer Umasɣan; ayen yettɛeqqiben i win yettɛeqqiben yettḥussu ad t-iɛeqqeb d win yettɛaqqeb, i win ur yeɣɣayen ara. Mi nmergem belli laɣerru ilaq ad t-neɣ, ma yella win i ɣ-yettɛawanen deg leɣmel-a ilaq ad neččekru.

Lameɛna d tmusni tezgert, d talɣa tazedgirt n Ser, "ttɣawsiwin n tmusni yuɣalen ggten ɣer ttɣawsiwin i d-yettunefken s lɣawali" (Samael).

Deg agezdan 20, yettunefk ɣur-s tiɣerratin akken ad nessenger aṣemmiḍ n agur deg tazwara anda nettesnimɣur d neẓẓu.

# AGEZDAN SEG 21 ƔER 29

Deg 21 yettmeslay-d d yesselmad ad nettaḥḥaql d ad nezdum, ad nessen ad neqqleb. Win ur yezmir ad yesteqsi ur yezmir ara ad yesselɣu Aɣrem.

Deg 22 yettmeslay-d ɣef "AWEḌ D UCCAW". D tewselt i yettmeslay-d ɣef uweḍ; ma ur nebɣi ara ad nɛawed iɣsan id-yedderɣelen, ilaq ad nesseɣɣ Ayyaten, i ɣ-d-yettddukulen; yettunefk-aɣ ad nessenfali tajumma n yarrac-nneɣ. Uccaw yettunefk ɣer ttɣawsiwin n tudert-nneɣ, mi nesɛa agiles.

Umessih deg yiman d times n times; ayen i nettwali d ayen i netḥussu d agerruj deg agiles n times n Umerruk. Ad da-tili times n Umerruk d umeslay i d-yettunefken deg tudert-nneɣ, times-a tettarreẓ deg yal azɣar n yiqerra-nneɣ, i ilaq ad nessezgzi uqbel s 5 n iɣersiwen n Tmeɣra, nttunefk yemma-tneɣ RAMIO.

"Win i d-yettalalen ilaq ad yelmed ad yidir s ḍeɛfan; akka i yettwaru".

Deg agezdan 25, Uselmad yettmeslay-d ɣef tama yettwanɣez d nekkni, i d-nettunefk am akken nella d taɣerɣara n ssima, sinna, nettali wuguren-nneɣ deg tilisa n wiyaḍ.

Akkagi i d-yettddukulen s tidet win yettɣellten; akken am tissurifin-nneɣ i aɣ-ittakken, akka am akken nella d ikettaren; tissurifin yettwanɣez yettɣellilen mi tent-nesseɣlu war ma neqqen wuguren-nneɣ.

Deg agezdan 26 yettmeslay-d ɣef 3 n ukeffafen, igrawanen n Hiram Abiff, Umessih Deg: 1.- Agwallal 2.- Ttamettirit 3.- Anezdi

Yal yiwen deg-nneɣ nettewwi deg Tmeskert-nneɣ i 3 n ukeffafen.

Yesselmed belli Umessih Deg yella d azdig d tazdeg, yettɛawan akken ad nqelleɛ imelyunen n wid ur nebɣi ara i nettewwi deg-nneɣ. Deg agezdan-a yettunefk ɣur-s belli Umessih Yeffren d Mass n TZWEL TAZEGZERT, yettwarrej seg yiqeddacen, seg yemɣaren d tira n tzeqqa.

Deg agezdan 28, yettmeslay-d ɣef Ugerruj-Umdan d tuzzmazt gar yal yiwen fell-as.

Aɛeqqab n Udan akken ad yeqqleb d Ugerruj-Umdan d timeɣriwin d timeɣriwin mgal iman-is, mgal ddunit d mgal ayen akk yettḍeggiren ddunit-a d tid yellan d tmuɣri.

Deg agezdan 29, agezdan aɣarrab, yettmeslay-d ɣef Uɣerruj Qedsan, tagellidt n Hermes, tabuqalt n Sliman; Uɣerruj Qedsan yettbeggin s telɣiwt i Yuni tameṭṭut, tsexri, asuma n yemɣeren anda ttessen Sdi Rebbi Qedsanen.

Tabuqalt-a n tzemmart ur tezmiret ara ad teɣweḍ deg Tzeqqa n tmuzɣi, neɣ deg tudert n Uqeddac gnusiy.

Mi ftim gnusiyen a tent-yessen tmuzɣi-a, a tent-yeqqleb tudert-nsen d teɣmert d ad yettqeddem am Uqeddac deg Tzeqqa Taɣarastit n Leḥmala.

Ad tili tilelli taɣezfant tettili deg wul-ik.

GARGHA KUICHINES
