ASEGGWEN
Sɣur: V.M. GARGHA KUICHINES

"TUGWADI MEQQREN" n Umasɣan Amaɣus Samael Aun Weor tesbeggin-aɣ-d deg wudem ibanen amek yella wemkan-nneɣ deg tudert.

Ilaq ad neqleɛ ayen akk iɣ-ireksen ɣer umaɣad iḍeɛɛu deg tudert-a.

Deg umḍiq-a, nesdukkel aselmed n yal agezzum akken ad nesseɛref amdan izem deg usawen ɣer Wawal mgal iman-is.

Tiselɣa akk n umawal-a tesserɣay ɣer usenger n Yuyaten-nneɣ, akken ad nesellek Assaɣ yellan d ayen yesɛan azal deg-neɣ.

Yuy ur yebɣi ara ad yemmet, yemma ameqqran yetḥuss d amectuh ɣef lɛib.

Deg umaḍal, ttilin aṭas n wid ur nezmir, tuggdi teẓẓuɣer yal amkan.

"ULAC AYEN YETTWAGGADEN, AYEN YELLAN D IMRU YETTWAGGADEN".

AGEZZUM 1

Tameddurt teffeɣ-itt cbaḥa taɣerfant; ayen yellan deg uɛebbar yesserɣay kullec. Ḥunna ur tettwassen ara. Taneḥḥast tesɛa wid it-teḍferen. Tɣawla ulac-itt acku imdanen ttidiren uɣalen s tussda d tɣaltin.

Taqdert n wid yettwaggaden deg ifassen n yemdanen yeṭṭfen ɣer wayen akk yettwaggaden.

AGEZZUM 2

Lǧuɛ d tissas zdirent seg yiwet n teswiɛt ɣer tayeḍ, tikimin tessengirent taɣaltin n tegnawt n tegnit, maca tella ddwa mgal ccer iɣ-dd-yezzin: "Temẓi Tussnant" neɣ asnerni n iẓri n ufares n ufares n ufares n ufares ad t-nernu deg uɣerbaz-nneɣ amdan, sin akin ɣer Tafat d Timess mi nlemmed amek ara nerrnu 3 n tiselɣa n usikker n tmusni: 1. Mmut n lɛuyub-nneɣ. 2. Ad nessenṭer igerdan n tafat deg-neɣ. 3. Ad neqdec ɣer yellan d Warar (Tameddurt).

Akal, aman, d aḍu, kkan-d s ccer n temdint-a yellan; ur yettaweḍ ara ddheb n umaḍal akken ad nqedder ccer; ad yesseqdec ddheb aɣewwiɣ i nekkni akk nferes, iẓri-nneɣ i nekkni, mi t-nessqeddec s tmusni, akka i nezmer akken ad nsewjed amaḍal d ad neqdec s tmusni tukkset.

Nekni nessenter Asɣar n Uselles Amaḍlan s akk wid izem yeṭṭfen tizegzewt s Uvatara n Aquario, s tselmadt n Tmesriḥt ara ɣ-dd-isellken seg wayen akk yettwaggaden.

Ma tessewjed-d iman-ik, tessewjed-d amaḍal.

AGEZZUM 3

I aṭas n wid ur ttilif ara yelḥeq, ur ssinen ara d ccɣel-nneɣ, d nekkni i ten-nessenṭren, nessenteren; nessenter-itt s ddheb aɣewwiɣ-nneɣ, Iẓri-nneɣ.

Mi nettfeṛḥ, netḥuss d yelḥeq, maca d tazlitt tissas-a; ma ur tesɛiḍ ara amendeff ɣef wallen-ik n tegnit, ad tiliḍ d aklan-is, acku ur tettɛeyyu ara d ayen. Ilaq ad tidireḍ deg Umaḍal war ma tiliḍ d Aklan-is.

AGEZZUM 4 YEMMESLAY ƔEF TLRUYT

Tlarruyt teɛǧeb-aɣ, nebɣa ad nili d ilalen, maca ttemmeslayen ɣef wemdan yettwaggaden d nekkni nettwaggaden d akka i nettuɣal d ilelli d ttaɛqabt ad nɛeddi ɣer wid yettwaggaden.

Win yettɛawaden tilisa n tewɛidat, d win yesɛan ccer d win it-nessenṭren, acku win-a yezmer ad yeɛdel s tɛekkwin, tameḥqurt neɣ d tikerkas ibanen; win yettɛawaden it-yettɛawanen am wid yettɛawanen aḍu, d win yettwaggaden deg tazmert. "Afet ɣer Tideț d nettat ara ɣ-dd-isellken". Maca amek ara yessiweḍ ɣer Tideț win yettkerhen? Deg talɣa-a itteɣɣay yal ass seg uɣalen, Tideț.

Tideț d ayen yettwasfen ɣef Baba Mazal Ḥemmel, am tidi. Amek ara yesɛu tidi win yettkerhen, ma d tẓallit n Baba? Tẓallin n Baba ur nezmir ad tent-nesɛu win yeččuṛen d lɛuyub, tikkisin, tineɣrasin n tezmart d teɣḍalt. Nekni d aklan n yinumak-nneɣ; ewwet seg Win Yessufuɣen-d Tafat yettemmeslayen ɣef ayen it-ittwali deg tɣerma-s; akka i tzenz tazellult d kullec ara yettwakkes. "Anwa yellan d ilelli? Anwa iwexxren tlarruyt tamɣarant? Acḥal ɣ-ten-iẓerren? Ay, Ay, Ay!", (Samael). Win yettkerhen ur yezmir ad yili d ilelli acku yella mgal Win Mazal Ḥemmel yellan d Tideț tṣeggiḍ.

AGEZZUM 5 YEMMESLAY ƔEF UMGARU N TALA TAZELLULT

Kullec yekkat d yettwakkes, yeslek d yekkat, yetteffeɣ d yettɛawad; maca imdanen aṭas yessnen ɣer uffeɣ d ucɣaṛ n win iǧerran ɣef uffeɣ d ucɣaṛ-is d akka yella deg yilel yessawalen n tudert-is, yesseqdec ttawiɛat-is i ttɛeyyunin akken ad yesqedder aseɣẓen n win iǧerran; d netta d acu? Mi yettemmet amdan Yuyaten-is neɣ lɛuyub-is yettwasellek, yettwasellek seg aṭas n izuran i nekkni nessaɣ, yeɣṭed amqerqur seg wid nessaɣ d ittaɣ-itt tazmert n tlarruyt.

Tillisa ad ilint s wudem yettwaggaden, ila-ɣ-aɣ ad naf amgaru iddukklen, amezgen yellan deg leḥkem.

Tamussni tetḥudd d tettwaggad zdat ccɣel yettwaggaden d yinumak yettenfufuden zdat tideț tṣeggiḍ. "Anagar mi nesenger tikerkas tettas-d Tideț" (Samael).

AGEZZUM 6 YINUMAK D TILUT

Yettwasqeddec akken ad yelmed win yeɣran agezzum-a akken ad iḥader ma yettwaɣzen s timseddiyin tikerkasin; mi tesɛaɣ lɛuyub tussnant, tikkisin, leɛwayed, yinumak-nneɣ ad ilin tikerkasin; aya n: "Akka i tella acku iẓriɣ", d win yettwaggaden, kullec yesɛa imuqilen, tilisa, iɣesɣan, aɛlalen d iserdanen, teɣziwin, tissas, anda Win i ẓẓiɣen ɣef tixidas ittwali tɣawsiwin s wudem-is, it-yessezzer d teɣḍalt, yettak tizdegɣa ɣer wid it-yesseɣḍalen.

AGEZZUM 7 TADIALECTIKA N TMUSNI

Nessen d aya it-nesselmaden, anagar i nezmer ad nessekker tmusni ɣef ccɣel yesɛan tmusni d tiggadin izem.

Win yettɛawanen deg ubrid iweqqɛɛ tazmert n amecṭuh n tmusni mi yettwaɣzen s yid-id yellan deg tudert-is.

Umɣar yesɛan tazmert, yettɛawan deg Udem n Tudert, ur yettwaɣzen ara s wudem-a, yettḥuss am win it-ittwalin deg lfuṛǧa n tudert; dinna am deg sinima, wid it-ittwalin ḍefren win yettɛawanen neɣ win yettwaggaden. Umɣar n Tudert d win it-nesselmaden tɣawsiwin yelhan d tid yesɛan afares ɣer win yettɛawanen deg ubrid, it-yessezzer yelhan ɣef ayen llan, Taḥeqqeqt Tameqqrant teṭṭaɛ-as-d, imdanen dferen-t s TMILEWT.

"Tmusni d Tafat win ur nessin ara it-ittɣim" (Samael Aun Weor) ɣer win yettɣim it-yedḍru s Tafat n Tmusni, ayen i yellan ɣer uɛmir s Tafat n Tafukt.

Mi tessaɛɛ tɣaltin n tmusni-nneɣ, yiman it-yexḍaren deg taɣerma ayen yellan, ayen yellan.

AGEZZUM 8 TALAL TUSSNANT

Imdanen zdat tɣawsiwin yettwalin n Taḥeqqeqt Tameqqrant ttwaggaden d ttɛeyyunin ad ɛeddin; tamussni ttettak-iten, yettak-asen isemɛan iweɛɛren, akken wid ur nessin ara ad uɣalen ur ttemḍaraten ara.

Ttilin aṭas n wid yessnen isem n iɣallen-nsen, maca ur ssinen ara amek ara ten-ssengren.

Amdan yesseqdec s telwit iḍisan yeɛeḍlen i tferes, maca ur yessin ara amek ara yesseqdec taɣalt-is: Agraw anda yettuɣal seg Yiwet n Tessiwɛt ɣer Tayeḍ; amdan akken ad it-yessin, yedḍru yid-s, ayen i yellan ɣer uɣerbaz s tixerbac neɣ tiseɣẓanin; maca amdan it-ttemmɛawed akken ad it-yers, mi yettemmet lɛuyub-is, leɛwayed-is, tikkisin-is, atg., d ur yezmir ara, it-ittɣim s wussan d afus mazal.

AGEZZUM 9 ANTICRISTO

Nekki yid-neɣ deg. Ur ɣ-ireggel ara ad nessiweḍ ɣer Baba Mazal Ḥemmel. Maca mi nerrnu-t kullec yettwaqqed deg udmawen-is.

Anticristo yettaker yelhan yellan s talɣa n Tmasiḥt n Tidi, Tberruɛt, Tḥeqṛit, atg. "Argaz" yettaɣ-itt s tmusni-s d yettaɛraq-itt.

AGEZZUM 10 YUY TUSSNANT

Ilaq ad nessemɣar iman-nneɣ deg ccɣel seg Yiwet n Tessiwɛt ɣer Tayeḍ, akken ad nessen ma ayen nessaɣ aɣ-yessewjed, acku usenger iǧerran ur aɣ-iseqdec ara. Anagar it-yaɣ aɣ-dd-issemmalen belli nekkni d imessengren yelhan, maca aya yelha mi nesseɣ deg-neɣ ccer-nneɣ, akken ad nsewjed iman-nneɣ s Tmasiḥ yellan deg tazmert akken ad nessufɣ Tafat d ad nessewjed Aɛeṣbu Amdan.

Ad nesselemed akken ad naker, aya t-ssnen akk, maca ad nesselemed akken ad nḥemmel, aya d ayen iweɛɛren.

Lemmed s talwit win yeɣran agezzum-a, ma tebɣiḍ ad tesseɣ seg uẓar ccer-ik i nekkni.

AGEZZUMEN 11 ƔER 20

Imdanen teɛǧeb-as ad ɛedlen, ad ssersen ɣer wiyaḍ amek ɣer-sen ttittwalin, maca ulac win yebɣan ad yessin iman-is, d ayen i yesɛan azal deg Ubrid n Tmesriḥt.

Win yettemmeslayen aṭas n tikerkasin yella deg talɣa; Tafat d tmusni d mi tettban deg-neɣ, d akken ad neggen ccɣel uɛla. "S ccɣel-nsen ara ten-tessnem", yenna Ɛisa Tmasiḥ.

Ur yenna ara d akken s ttaɣwal i teggen. !!!Gnósticos... sekker!!!

Amdan ittwaḥraz neɣ yellan yellan ittekkat s uḥraz-is neɣ ussan-is. Wigi am wid yeḥkemn yettwaggaden, ttɛeyyunin ayen it-yessnen, d ttemḥekmen neɣ ttaken am tideț n Rebbi, ayen yettemmeslayen gar-asen yettwakken.

Anda tella tafat, tella tmusni. Taggada d ccɣel n tallit, aya ur yekkaten seg tafat.

Deg agezzum 12 yettemmeslay ɣef 3 n wallen i nesɛa: Allen Tmeṭṭut neɣ n ttawiɛat, Allen tiddi; aya d ayen yettɣimin akk ayen it-yessnen d yettaḥkem s wudem n win iɛerḍen neɣ win yettɛawanen; mi tettelḥaq s tmusni, d amedday it-yessemlalen, yettaɣal d aɣrawan n ccɣel; tɣawsiwin yettwaɣzen deg wallen tiddi teggen yinuman-nneɣ.

Win yesɛan tidi tidet, ur yeḥwaǧ ara ad yaɣimin; win yettkerhen ur yezmir ad yesɛu tidi, yettwassen ɣer Rebbi d timessaɣ yettwakkan, neɣ allen tiɣerma, ara nessiweḍ mi nakkes Mmut ɣer wid ur nebɣi ara i nessaɣ deg Psiquis-nneɣ.

Yelha ad nessen lɛuyub-nneɣ, sin akin ad ten-naɛred d sin akin ad ten-nessenger s tɛawent n yemma-tneɣ RAM-IO, tetteggani aɣ-tt-essewjed, ad ttili d aklan n wid yeɣman ɣer wayen akk it-yaɣmen.

Yuy, Ego, d uɣil deg; anagar Ser yesɛan tazmert akken ad yesseɣzel uɣil deg-neɣ, deg Psiquis-nneɣ.

Seg uleḥḥu yesɛan talwit n agezzum 13, nufa ayen yedḍran ɣer Win Ibanen Iɛeḍlen, mi yettmagar Yuyaten ur nebɣi ara n ulac-aɣ. Mi nessaɣleḍ iman-nneɣ neḥder ad nemmeslay ccer ɣef wemdan.

Ser d Sser ad ten-ɛelqen gar-asen; akka tettalɣa taɛqilt. Sser, war tmusni n Ser, yettak tikerkeṛ tussnant n kullec; yettalɣa win yettakaren.

Ma d Ser ameqqran n Sser, yettalɣa Win Iṣaḥan Iɛeḍlen. Agezzum 14 yettak ɣer tiselɣa akken ad nessen iman-nneɣ; Nekni d Rebbi yettwassen, yid-s leḥkem it-yezzin ur yettwakaten ara; ad neḥder kullec aya d Aselles, akken yettemmɛawed...

"Aɣeltan yekkat s tissi n Yeḥkem, s tessi n Umasɣan, s tessi n Win Yettemmagaren, s tɣaltin n Win Yettwassnen d tɣaltin n Tmasiḥ" (Samael).

Tameṭṭut-nneɣ Taɣerfant Marah, María neɣ RAM-IO am nekni s yisem Gnósticos, d amedday gar baba Mazal Ḥemmel d nekkni, amedday gar igerdan yellan deg Taḥeqqeqt Tameqqrant d amessaɣ; s ɣur-s d ɣur-s, igerdan n Taḥeqqeqt Tameqqrant ttaɛraq-aɣ. D Deva-nneɣ Taɣerfant, amedday gar Tameṭṭut Taɣerfant i tɣaltin n umaḍal d taɣalt-nneɣ taɣerfant, akken ad nessiweḍ ɣer isennaten d ad neqdec ɣer imdanen yellan am nekkni.

Seg ukkat ussexsi yellan yid-s tmeṭṭut Sacerdotisa, argaz yettuɣal d tameṭṭut d tmeṭṭut tettwasellek; Yemma-tneɣ RAM-IO d tin kan i nezmer ad nɣelles ad nɣelles aɛeqqa deg wayen yellan deg waɛquqen-nneɣ d iluɣman-nsen. S tselɣiwin yettwassen ur nezmir ad nessen tɣawsiwin n Ser, acku ttawiɛat d iɣerfan yettwaggaden, yeččuṛen d lɛuyub, am win yellan d win-is; yezmer ad ten-nessukkes, mi nettemmet deg-neɣ lɛuyub, tikkisin, leɛwayed, aɣellaɛen, tineɣrasin, d kullec i yessnen ɣer wallen n tegnit, aṭas n tudditin ɣer-neɣ yettaɣen.

Deg agezzum 18 nettali, s wudem n Uɣara, belli am akken nettidir deg tama neɣ amkan n tegnit, akka daɣen deg tɣerma-nneɣ yella umkan tussnant anda nekkni nufan.

Lemmed s talwit win yeɣran agezzum-a akken ad tessnem deg tɣerma-s deg barrio, colonia neɣ umkan nufa.

Mi nesseqdec tameṭṭut-nneɣ taɣerfant RAM-IO nesseɣ Yuyaten-nneɣ yellan d Cṭan d nettwassellkem deg 96 n leḥkem n tmusni, n aṭas yellan yerkan. Aker ur ɣ-ireggel ara ad nelḥu deg.

Win yettkerhen yeɣdem mgal Baba-is d win yettzerḍen mgal Ṛuḥ Quddus; yettzerḍen deg umyissen, awal d ccɣel.

Ttilin ileɣman i yettemmeslayen yelhan seg yiman-nsen, ssenṭren aṭas n wid ur nessin ara, maca ma, tettwaɛred ccɣel-nsen, nufa usenger d tixerbit; tudert timanet uqerben ad ten-ggerwen d ten-ttettun.

Deg agezzum 19, yettak-aɣ tafat akken ur neɣli ara deg umaɣad n tḥussi n tiddi. Nekni akk d inelmaden n tqeddec n Avatara; ɣer win yettkemmilen yettɛeyyu ma it-lɣun d win i ẓẓiɣen, ur t-sajjden.

Mi neɛqel belli taɣalt ila-ɣ-aɣ ad tt-nessenger, ma yella win iɣ-iɛawnen deg ccɣel-a iweɛɛren ilaq ad neqqedder.

Tidi d tmusni tṣeggiḍ, tamussni yettwakkan s Ser, "tikkisin n tmusni egoica dgarrawen d tikkisin s ddwan" (Samael).

Deg agezzum 20, yettak-aɣ ɣer tiselɣiwin akken ad nesseɣreb dɣaɣ iṭuraren deg tiddi anda nekki nessiweḍ d nessnerni.

AGEZZUMEN SEG 21 ƔER 29

Deg 21 yettemmeslay-aɣ d yesselmad-aɣ ad naɛred d ad nlemmed, ad nessin ad nbeddel. Win ur yessin ara ad yewwet ur yezmir ad yesseɣreb Ego.

Deg 22 yettemmeslay-aɣ ɣef "Aɣlal D Aseɣẓen". D yeshel wamek it-yettemmeslay ɣef aɣlal; ma ur nebɣi ara ad nɛawed iḍisan yeɛḍlen, ila-ɣ-aɣ ad nesseɣreb Yuyaten, iɣ-tt-yettaken; yettwasseldem-aɣ ad nessewjed tɣaltin n warraw-nneɣ. Aseɣẓen yettwakkat ɣer tɣawsiwin n tudert-nneɣ, mi tesɛaɣ taɣalt taɣerfant.

Tmasiḥ deg yellan d imes d ames; ayen nettwali d netḥussi d tama taɣerfant n umes Crístico.

Aɣlal n umes Crístico d ayen ameqqran ɣer tudert-nneɣ i nekkni, ames-a yeṭṭef deg wayen akk yettwaggaden deg iberdan-nneɣ neɣ allen-nneɣ, ara nersin kan ɣer 5 n iɣerfan n Taḥeqqeqt Tameqqrant, akken ad neqdec deg tqeddec n Yemma-tneɣ Taɣerfant RAMIO.

"Win yebdan ila-as ad tidir s wudem yettwaggaden; akka yura".

Deg agezzum 25, Umasɣan yettemmeslay-aɣ ɣef tama ur nessin ara n iman-nneɣ, d win nettak am akken yella d takerrust tessufuɣen s sinima, d sin akin, nettali lɛuyub-nneɣ deg tɣaltin iǧerran.

Aya akk yettak aɣer wid yettɣimin deg tikerkas; am akken ttawiɛat-nneɣ tkerhen akka nekni nkerhen; ttawiɛat yedren teggen tixerbiɛt mi nessekker-itent war ma nettemmet lɛuyub-nneɣ.

Deg agezzum 26 yettemmeslay ɣef 3 n wid ttrejjin, iɛdawen n Hiram Abiff, Tmasiḥ deg yellan, iɣriten n: 1.- Allen 2.- Tineɣra N Awi 3.- Tineɣra

Yal yiwen gar-neɣ nessaɣ deg psiquis-nneɣ ɣer 3 n wid ttrejjin.

Yesselmed-aɣ belli Tmasiḥ deg yellan tṣeggiḍ d talwit, teɛawan-aɣ akken ad nesseɣreb aɣelben i nebɣi ara i nessaɣ deg. Deg agezzum-a yettwasseldem belli Tmasiḥ deg yellan d Amɣar n TUGWADI MEQQREN, yettwakken sɣur imessaɣen, sɣur iɣellaten d sɣur yura n tɣalt.

Deg agezzum 28, yettemmeslay ɣef Super-Argaz d ugerru totali n aṭas ɣef.

Lḥiǧǧat n Humanoide akken ad yettuɣal d Super-Argaz d ttaggin d ttaggin mgal yiman-is, mgal amaḍal d mgal kullec it-yettak amaḍal-a n tissas.

Deg agezzum 29, agezzum yellan deg wuggar, yettemmeslay ɣef Sacer Grial, tasa n Hermes, tasa n Salomón; Sacer Grial yettedder deg ussaxsi kan ɣer Yoni tmeṭṭut, ussexsi, soma n yemyal yella amdan yettessen yella d Rebbi yesɛan tizdegɣa.

Tasa-a n telwit ur tezmir ad tgerrez deg ulac amqerqur n ugerru, neɣ deg tudert n Umasɣan Gnóstico.

Mi yettɛeqel gnosticen amqerqur-a, ad beddlen tudert tajennant-nsen d ulɣu ad ten-yeqdec am umassaɣ deg Amqerqur Aɣerfan n Tmilewt.

Talwit deg yettwaɛzel ad teḥkem deg ul-ik.

GARGHA KUICHINES.
