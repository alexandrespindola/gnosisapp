Nessaḍ ɣer yiwen n wemḍiq yesɛan isennanen aṭas, bɣiɣ ad d-mmeslayeɣ ɣef umeslay-agi n Kundalini, izerman yecɛalen n tezmert-nneɣ tasusrant, yettwader-d deg waṭas n yeḍrisen n tmusni talemmast.

Bla ccekk Kundalini yesɛa aṭas n yisallen yerna d ayen yessefraḥen ad yettwetsuqsi.

Deg yeḍrisen n lkimya talemmast, Kundalini d uḍris astral n temẓi taqedsit, STELLA MARIS, TAZZURT N YILEL, i d-ițțawi fell-asen ifadden i wid yqeddcen deg Umahil Ameqqran.

Ger Iẓṭikiyyen nettat d TONANTZIN, ger Iyunaniyen d CASTA DIANA, yerna deg Maṣer d ISIS, TAYEMMAT TAṚUMIT iɣef ur yessegwer ula d yiwen umdan ad yekkes ɣef uɣilif-is.

Ulac ccekk deg-s belli Tasɣent Tazuranit taqedsit ur teḥbis ara ad tẓalla i Tayemmat Taṛumit Kundalini; s tselbi d MARAH, neɣ ad d-nini RAM-IO, MARIA.

Ayen ur d-ibanen ara sɣur ddiyanat tuddṣanit, ɣas akken deg wayen yerzan tizi uzɣer neɣ taɣerfant, d ṣṣifa n ISIS deg umuɣ-ines ufessas.

S tselbi, d s tuffra kan i lemden i wid yettwaseɣzanen belli Tayemmat-agi Taṛumit tella-d s umuɣ deg yal yiwen seg yimdanen.

Ur yezmir ara ad d-nernu s wazal meqqren belli Ṛebbi-Tayemmat, REA, CIBELES, ADONÍA neɣ amek nebɣa a s-nsemmi, d amgired n iman-nneɣ ufessas dagi d tura.

Ma nɣerrez awal ad d-nini belli yal yiwen seg-neɣ yesɛa Tayemmat-is Taṛumit yellan d taɣssa, d tafessast.

Llant aṭas n Tmuḍriwin deg igenni am akken llant teflukin i d-yellan fell-as udem n tmurt.

Kundalini d tazmert tasusrant i d-yeǧǧan ad yili ddunit, d ṣṣifa n BRAHMA.

Deg ṣṣifa-s taseksumt i d-ibanen deg tanatument tuffrit n umdan, KUNDALINI tettwaqqen tlata tikelt d uẓẓun deg tlemmast n ṣṣut yellan deg uɣesmar n uḥerfiw.

Dinna tteddukul am akken i tella teɣirdem Tafessast Taṛumit.

Deg tlemmast n Chakra-nni neɣ lɣerfa tella tagemrawt taɣarast neɣ YONI anda yettwaṣber LINGAM awtem.

Deg LINGAM-agi atuman neɣ amsusran i d-yemmalen tazmert tuddsa n BRAHMA, tettwaqqen teɣirdem tameqqrant KUNDALINI.

Tageldunt ticeɛɛant deg talɣa-s n teɣirdem, tettakker s tuffra n tuffra n tezɣent takimant i sellemdeɣ s ṣṣeḥ deg umahil-iw yettusemman: «Tasusrit n Wureɣ Iceɛɛanen».

Bla ccekk, mi tekkker tezmert-agi taṛumit, telɣa-d s uzaglu ɣef ugensu amellal asɣunay iwakken ad d-yernu deg-neɣ tezmar i d-yeǧǧan ad naru.

Deg ṣṣifa-s taneflit tarumit taɣeslant, teɣirdem taqedsit teffer deg wayen yellan d afizik, d anatum, deg yiwet n tɣelt, am akken i d-nniɣ iman-nneɣ, maca yettwafken.

Ur yesɛi ara dɣel iwakken ad sselmadeɣ deg umahil-agi taknikt i usakkaṛ n teɣirdem taqedsit.

Bɣiɣ kan ad ssebdedeɣ tayri ɣef tidett taceṭṭaḥt n Ego yerna ɣef tuggza tenezgelt yerzan ad ttewfekken agiman-is ur n yimdanen.

Aql-nni s iman-is ur yezmir ara ad ybeddel s tsertit kra n lɛib aseksum.

Aql-nni yezmer ad yettara lɛib ɣef yiman-is, ad t-yefk seg yiwen n tseddaṛt ɣer wayeḍ, ad t-yeffer ɣef yiman-is neɣ ɣef wiyaḍ, ad t-iṣeggerrew maca ur t-yekkes ara s tselbi.

Taẓurt d aḥric ameɣrar, maca ur yelli ara d ayen akk, ilaq ad yettwakes.

Lɛib yettwazran ilaq ad yettwatesuqsi yerna ad yettwafehhem deg umuɣ aẓuran uqbel ad nbeddu ad t-nekkis.

Nḥwaj tazmert yugar aql-nni, tazmert i yezmer ad yesseɣli atuman akken i yebɣu d ayen yellan d ilem-lɛib i yettwafen yerna yettwahkem fell-as s ṣṣeḥ.

S ṣṣeḥ tazmert-agi tella-d deg uzɣer meqqren ugar n tfekka, n tayriwin yerna n aql-nni, ɣas ma yesɛa ayen i d-yettbinen deg uɣesmar n tlemmast n uḥerfiw, am akken i d-nefka takti deg iferdisen iɛeddan n yixef-agi.

Mi nefehhem s ṣṣeḥ kra n wayen yellan d ilem-lɛib, ilaq ad nezgel deg tẓallit taɣezfant, ad nẓalleɣ, ad nsuqqes, ad nssuter seg Tayemmat-nneɣ Taṛumit tafessast ad tessenger ayen yellan d ilem-lɛib i nefhem uqbel.

D tagi i d taknikt taɣerfant i nḥwaj i wakken ad nekkes igiman ur neṣfi ara i nḍegger deg ugensu-nneɣ.

Tayemmat Taṛumit Kundalini tesɛa tazmert ad tesseɣli ɣer iɣilifen kra n agiman aseksum yellan d imesli, ur n yimdanen.

War tuddsa-yagi, war asenfar-agi, yal amḥawel iwakken ad yettwaṭeq Ego yegga-d ulac, d aɛeẓẓug, d aḥeqqi.
