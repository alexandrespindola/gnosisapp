Llantat deg umadal aṭas n yemyagen n wallus ur yesɛin ara amudem ilaw d wid yettwaxdamen s tikerkas taẓalit.

War ccek, ssmeɛ taẓalit n tikerkas yesɛaṭṭef deg wallaɣen n wemdan s tɣara icemḥen seg lqern wis tman d mraw.

Uqbel lqern-nni, tegzirt yettwassnen Nontrabada neɣ Yetterɣen, yellan zdat lebḥer n Spanya, tettban-d tettwafaɣ deg ufus yal ass.

Ulac ccek belli tegzirt-agi tella deg tuga tis ṛebɛa. Aṭas n tmacahuɣin yezdin d tegzirt-agi tummich.

Deffir lqern wis tman d mraw, tegzirt-agi yettwabedren tḍegger deg lebda, ulac win yeẓran fell-as.

Deg lawan n Ugellid Arturo d irkelliyen n tɣerma tunɣilt, iɣsanen n ugama ttbanen deg yal tama, ttekkan deg tmurt-nneɣ tagewreɣt.

Aṭas n tullisin ɣef yecbaben, leǧnun d tmeɣṛasin mazal aṭas-itent deg Erim tazegzawt, Irlanda; aɣbel, ayen akk ur yesɛin aɣbel, ayen akk yelhan n taṛwiḥt n ddunit, ur yettwaḥṣasen ara seg wemdan ɣef ddemma n tmusniwin n yemyagen n wallus d usemɣer ameqqran n Uɣersiw n Nefs.

Ass-a yemyagen ttḍsen ɣef wayen akk-agi, ur t-qebleɣ ara ɣas ulamma deg ul ur ufin ara tazmert.

Lemmer ad fehmen medden belli nesɛa tlata wallaɣen, ad yili wayeḍ urar, yezmer ad sḥassen ugar s usslel-agi.

Aɣbel, imeqqranen war tussna, yeṭṭfen deg yegbaren n tmusniwin-nsen idewwaren, ur sɛin ara lawan ad ɛessen s usslel-nneɣ s tidet.

Agduden-agi imeɣbanen ḥesben iman-nsen d wid iwatan, ffuken s tallus amcum, ḥesben d nutni i yellan deg ubrid ussid, ur ẓran ara belli ttddun deg ubrid ur yesɛin ara ifuk.

S yisem n tidet, ilaq-aɣ ad nini belli deg tgemmi, nesɛa tlata wallaɣen.

Amezwaru nezmer d ilaq aṭ-n-nesseɣ awal Aɣil Ussensi, wis sin ad t-nesseɣ awal S yisem Aɣil Agemraw. Wis tlata ad t-nesseɣ awal Aɣil Agensa.

Tura ad nessenɣer yal yiwen seg wallaɣen-agi deg telqayt d tɣara tagdilant.

War ccek, Aɣil Ussensi yessufeɣ igzuren-is n udeg-is s ddaw ussensiwen inẓagen ibeṛṛaniyen.

Deg wamek-agi, Aɣil Ussensi d aɣessar d aɣeṛfan, ur yezmir ad yeqbel ayen ur yettwabedren ara s tlufatt.

Aken ma llan igzuren n udeg-is n Uɣil Ussensi sɛan s asalas afaylu inẓagen ibeṛṛaniyen, war ccek ulac ayen yezmer ad yeẓer ɣef ayen yellan s tidet, ɣef tidet, ɣef tmuɣliwin tummicin n tudert d lmut, ɣef taṛwiḥt d ṛṛuḥ, atg.

I yemyagen n wallus, yeṭṭfen deg ussensiwen ibeṛṛaniyen d wid yellan deg igzuren n udeg-is n wallaɣ ussensi, usslel-nneɣ ussiddiqen ḥesben-t d aɣyul.

Deg uɣur n wayen ur yellan d aɣur, deg ddunit n wayen ur yettuqabal, sɛan lḥeqq imi ttwaqecqen s ddunit ussensi ibeṛṛani. Amek yezmer Aɣil Ussensi ad yeqbel ayen ur nexdim ara s ussensi?

Lemmer afaylu n ussensi ad yeqqim s usrus ara yessali yal imdanen n Uɣil Ussensi, d awḍan belli widin ad ssufɣen igzuren ussensiwen.

Aɣil Agemraw yemgarad, maca, ur yeẓer ara ayen s tidet, yettḥessib kan d aya kan.

Deg Uɣil Agemraw tella tisselbiwin tineslem, taritin ur tettuɣal ara, atg.

Aɣil Agensa d aɣbalu i tjerrba tijḍiḍant n tidet.

War ccek Aɣil Agensa yessufeɣ igzuren-is n udeg-is s ddaw afaylu ara yefken wallus uɣmir yelḥeq-d S Ser.

War ccek taṛwiḥt tezmer ad tɛic d ad tɛeddi seg ayen yellan s tidet. Ulac ccek belli taṛwiḥt teẓra s tidet.

Maca, i tban taṛwiḥt teḥwaǧ tameɣrest, imɣi n lxedma d waya deg iman-is d Aɣil Agensa.

Taṛwiḥt teẓra tijḍiḍant i tidegti n yal tikti tagaman d war Aɣil Agensa tezmer ad ttban.

Ad neldin Aɣil Agensa ad yili d ayen yewwan iwakken ad nffeɣ seg ddunit n ccekk d tennuft.

Aya yeɛni belli ala mi neldi Aɣil Agensa ara ilal liman ussid deg wemdan.

Mi nettmuqul s uɣur wayeḍ, ad nini belli tikerkas tagerfant d tawsit tisselbiwin n tennuft. War ccek, imeqqranen war tussna ttawin seg yiḍ d wass tikerkas.

Liman d allus tijḍiḍant i wayen yellan s tidet; tmusni tameqqrant; tɛeddi seg wayen yugar tafekka, ssura d wallaɣ.

Fruq gar liman d tisselbiwin. Tisselbiwin ttwakellxen deg Uɣil Agemraw, liman d tawsit n Uɣil Agensa.

Aɣbel, dima yella amudem deg wanda medden ḥesben tisselbiwin d liman. Ɣas yettban d awehmus ad nsedder awal-agi: "WIN YESƐAN LIMAN USSID UR IḤWAǦ ARA AD YAṬȚAMEN".

Aken liman ussid d tmusni tadurart, tasniremt uɣmir, tajerrba tijḍiḍant.

Yedda-d yella deg wanda ḥesben liman d tisselbiwin deg aṭas n leqṛun d tura d aqsiḥ ad nessefhem i medden belli liman d tmusni ussid d tisselbiwin war tɛeqqal.

Imdanen yessnen n wallaɣ agensa sɛan s ssrus n gar uluma afaylu-agi ameqqran n tmusni yellan deg taṛwiḥt.

Win yeldin Aɣil Agensa yettmekta tudert-is id-yezrin, yeẓra tmuɣliwin tummicin n tudert d lmut, maci ɣef wayen i yeɣṛa neɣ ur yeɣṛa ara, maci ɣef wayen i d-yenna wayeḍ neɣ ur d-yenni ara, maci ɣef wayen i t-yeɛǧeb ad yeṭṭamen neɣ ur yeɛǧeb ara, maca ɣef tajerrba tijḍiḍant, tadurart, tameqqrant s tidet.

Aya i d-neqqar ur iɛǧeb ara i wallaɣ ussensi, ur yezmer ad t-yeqbel imi yeffeɣ seg yexxamen-is, ur yesɛi ara ayen yezdin d ussensiwen inẓagen ibeṛṛaniyen, d ayen ibeṛṛaniyen i yigzuren-is n udeg-is, i wayen i t-sslemden deg uɣerbaz, i wayen i yelmed deg idlisen yemgarraden, atg., atg., atg.

Aya i d-neqqar ur t-yeqbel ara Aɣil Agemraw imi deqs n lḥal yeggul tisselbiwin-is, yessefḍay ayen i t-sslemden yesselmaden-is n ddin ad yettmekti, atg.

Yesu Lqader Ameqqran yenna i inelmaden-is: "Ḥadret seg uɣersiw n isadusiwen d uɣersiw n ifarisiwen".

Yettban belli Yesu Lmasiḥ s uɣader-agi yeqsed i tidawin n igerfanen isadusiwen d ifarisiwen ifṛen.

Tidawt n isadusiwen tella deg Wallaɣ Ussensi, d tidawt n xemsa ussensiwen.

Tidawt n ifarisiwen tella deg Wallaɣ Agemraw, aya ur yettwaxlaf ara, ur yettwaẓẓun ara.

Yettban belli ifarisiwen ttruḥun ɣer ddin-nsen iwakken ad qqaren fell-asen d wid yelhan, iwakken ad banen zdat wiyaḍ, maca ur xedmen ara deg iman-nsen.

Ur yezmer ara ad neldin Aɣil Agensa mi ur nelmiden ara ad nmeyyez s taɣersawt.

War ccek mi ibda yiwen ad iɛesses iman-is d tama belli yebda ad imeyyez s taɣersawt.

Deg wanda ur yeqbel yiwen ara tidet n Taɣersawt-is d ṣṣifa ad ttbeddel s ussid, war ccek ur yesḥissi ara i ṣṣifa n tɛessast taɣersawt.

Mi yeqbel yiwen tidawt n ugar d yefhem ṣṣifa ad yekkes iḍrisen yemgarraden i yettɛebba deg taṛwiḥt-is s usnas ad yebḍu taṛwiḥt, taẓɣelt, war ccek seg yiḍ d wass yebda taɛessast taɣersawt.

Yettban dɣa belli ad yekkes imdanen ur neɛǧeb seg taṛwiḥt-nneɣ i ttɛebbaɣ yekka-d s uldi n Wallaɣ Agensa.

Ayen akk-agi yeɛni belli ulidi-agi d ayen yettwaqedcen s tɣara iṣawen, aken ma ntekkes imdanen ur neɛǧeb ara i n-nesɛa deg taṛwiḥt-nneɣ.

Win yekkksen imdanen ur neɛǧeb ara deg wadda-s seg yiḍ d wass, tban-d belli ad yeldi wallaɣ-is agensa seg yiḍ d wass.

Yiwen wemdan akka ad yesɛu liman akmal. Tura ad tfemhem awalen n Lmasiḥ mi yenna: "Lemmer tesɛam liman am uɣerɣur n lxerḍel ad tsedwrem idurar".
