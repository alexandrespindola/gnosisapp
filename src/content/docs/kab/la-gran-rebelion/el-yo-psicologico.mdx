Ayen-agi n yiman-iw, d acu-yi, ayen i ixemmem, iḥussun, ixedmen, d ayen ilaq ad naɛreḍ ad nessen iwakken ad nessen lqayda.

Llan aṭas n tmuɣliwin yelhan i d-yezzin fell-aneɣ, yesḥusayen; maca ur nezmir ara ad nessexdem ayen-agi ma ur nessin iman-nneɣ.

Yelha ad tɣureḍ tasekla neɣ ad terwleḍ kra s tɣuri n yedlisen iberdan, maca d tiṭṭ n tidet ma tuɣaleḍ d amẓiwri ur tessin acemma ɣef yiman-ik, ɣef "nekkini", ɣef usirem n ufgan i nesɛa.

Yal yiwen yeḥrek ad ixemmem ayen i yeɛǧeb-as, dɣa s yesɣan nɣelb aqqaɛ n unegmar ur nɣel-ara s umdan, yeɛni yezmer ad yerr seg uzrem d aɛewdiw, seg uɛewdiw d azrem; aṭas n imẓiwriyen i yetturaren s lɛeqliya.

Ad tiliḍ d amẓiwri ur yeɛni ara ad tiliḍ d aqbayli. Imejhalen yeẓran, ṭṭfen am imɣan imcumman, ur ssinen ara, ula ma ur ssinen ara ur ẓran ara.

Anlam n umejhal yeẓran, d wid i gɣilen ad ẓran, ula d iman-nsen ur ssinen ara.

Nezmer ad naruɣ timuɣliwin yelhan ɣef "nekkini" n Tasekla n Tmetti, maca mačči ayen-agi i ɣ-yehwan deg yedris-agi.

Ilaq a nissin iman-nneɣ s ubrid aɣdidini war ttɛeddiḍ ɣef uɣilif n wextiyar.

Ur yezmir ad d-yili ayen-agi ma ur nqadaɛ iman-nneɣ deg yal taɣawsa, seg yiwet n teswiɛt ɣer tayeḍ.

Mačči d amek ara nẓer iman-nneɣ s tmuɣli n tmuɣli neɣ s tɣawsa tamatut.

Ad nẓer iman-nneɣ am akken nella d ayen i ɣ-yehwan; anagar akka ara nawḍeɣ ɣer tmusni taɣerfant n yiman-nneɣ.

Ma yella d ayen ur nettmeneɛ, nella neɣleṭ deg iman-nneɣ.

Aṭas n tɣawsiwin i ɣ-neɣɣil ur tent-nesɛi, aṭas n tɣawsiwin i ɣ-neɣɣil nesɛa, ur tent-nesɛi.

Nernaɣ isukalen nɣelden ɣef yiman-nneɣ, ilaq a nxedmen tasɣent iwakken ad nessen ayen i ɣ-yeğğan d ayen i ɣ-ixuṣṣen.

Nettamen nesɛa lalla neɣ lalla n tlata tilqaɛ i ur nesɛi ara, aṭas n lɛali i nesɛa s tidet, nettujguɛ.

Nekni d agdud iṭṭsen, ur nḥussu, dɣa d ayen-agi i yewɛren. S uɣilif, nexmem ɣef yiman-nneɣ ayen yelhan, ula ma nettmenɛ ur neẓri ara nella neṭṭes.

Tira yeqdsen tetteddu deg yeḥwaǧ n tukksa, maca ur tessemɛin ara amek ara nessiwḍ ɣer tukksa-agi.

Ayen i yewɛren akk, d win i yeɣran tira yeqdsen ur yetfehhem ara ula ma iṭṭes.

Akk lɣaci ɣilen ad ssinen iman-nsen, ula ma nettmenɛ ur yettmenɛin ara ad d-tili "tamɣart n waṭas".

Tidet n yiman ussiklaw n yal yiwen d win yesɛan aṭas, yettban am waṭas.

S waya, nebɣa a nini nesɛa aṭas n yimanen, mačči d yiwen am wakken ɣilen yimejhalen yeẓran.

Ad tenkreḍ tamɣart n waṭas d ayen ad terḍleḍ iman-ik, imi s tidet ad yili d aɣbalu n uɣbel ad tujuɛ tiɣersiwin tiɣerfantin i yesɛa yal yiwen seg-neɣ.

Ad ɣreɣ aɣmis, inna yiman n lɛeqliya; ad d-yesserɣ yir-ayen, inna yiman n umussu; hemmleɣ ad ṛuḥeɣ ad rekkbeɣ taɛekkumt. D acu taɛekkumt neɣ d acu aɣrum iẓeṭṭen, iɣaɣ yiwen nniḍen; hemmleɣ ad ččeɣ, uẓileɣ.

Ma nezmer ad nẓer deg tmejrit d tameqqrant, amek nella, ad naf iman-nneɣ s yiman-nneɣ deg ubrid aɣdidini tamɣart n waṭas.

Tudert n ufgan d taɣyult yeṭṭfen iɛeqqaḍ ur nettwali.

Yiman i d-yeqqaren ass-a aḥulfi i lebda i Tmusni, yettwaggra s yiman nniḍen ur nesɛi ara wayen yesɛan iḥulfi; dɣa amdan yeṭṭiri.

Yiman i d-yeqqaren ass-a aḥulfi i lebda i tmeṭṭut, yettwaggra s yiman nniḍen ur nesɛi ara wayen yesɛan iḥulfi; dɣa amdan ihemmlt nniḍen, aɣerbaz n lekɣaḍ yettaɣ. Anegmar ur nɣelb ara s umdan, d axxam yeččuren d waṭas n lɣaci.

Ur yelli ara lḥesma ula d tameɣra ger yimanen-agi, akk ten-ttnaɣen ger-asen, ttentamnen ad ṭṭfen tafella. M'ara yelḥeq yiwen seg-sen ad ṭṭef ammasen iqesḥanen n tkartit tadersant, yettḥussu d netta kan, d agellid, maca deg taggara yettwakkes.

Ma neẓra tɣawsiwin seg tama-yagi, newweḍ ɣer tmuɣli taɣerfant, yeɛni d aɣersiw ussiklaw ur yesɛi ara anamk n tidet n tukki.

Bla ccek, ayen ara d-tini tkartit neɣ ad t-texdem deg yiwet n teswiɛt, yettɛawed kan ɣef leṣnaf n yiman i tt-yettṭfen deg teswiɛin-nni.

Qqaren, Ɛisa Anazari yessufeɣ seg tfekka n Maryam Al-Magdaliya sebɛa ijennanen, sebɛa n yimanen, d udmawen udmawan n sebɛa leɛyub timeqqranin.

S tidet, yal yiwen seg sebɛa ijennanen-agi d aqerru n tgelda, ihi ilaq a nerr am asɣen uɣmir, d akken Lmasiḥ uɣerfan yezmer ad yessufeɣ seg tfekka n Al-Magdaliya alaf n yimanen.

Ma nexmem akk tɣawsiwin-agi, nezmer ad nefhem s clar d akken d ayen kan yelhan i nesɛa deg uɣerbaz-nneɣ d AYEN S YESSAR, s uɣilif netta yellan deg tqeɛɛut ger akk yimanen-agi n Tasekla n Tmetti tameɣlawit.

D ayen ur nehwan ara, yessar yettɛedda daymen s tuɣẓunt-is.

Bla ccek, yessar neɣ yefhem ayen i d-yesɛan, yeṭṭes deg lqayda.
