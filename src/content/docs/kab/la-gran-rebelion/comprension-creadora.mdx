Ila d tussna ilaq ad ttwaɛedlen akken ad sbedden deg nnefs-nneɣ tmes n ufehhem.

Asmi tussna teɛḍa-t wayen i nella, teffeɣ-d tumert n lɛeqliya n yal ṣṣifa.

Ma yella wayen i nella yugar tussna yezmer ad d-yawi iḥala yecban win n wali ẓẓeṛfan.

Deg umur n tudert n tilawt, ilaq ad nqeddec fell-aɣ iwakken ad naf iman-nneɣ.

D tudert n tilawt i d aḥlaw n uẓawan anefsan s wayes nezmer ad naf lɛuyub-nneɣ.

Deg teswiɛt n uɛiweẓ, taẓẓla, akk d wayen yellan d amaynut, nezmer ad nessenqes s yiman-nneɣ belli lɛuyub yeffren ttaɣen-d iman-nsen s iman-nsen.

Yettban-d belli lɛib yettwafhamen ilaq ad nqeddec fell-as s lebɣi iwakken ad t-newwet seg nnefs-nneɣ.

Uqbel kullec, ur ilaq ara ad nezdi iman-nneɣ d uɣilif-iw ma yella nebɣa s tidet ad t-nekkes.

Ma yella nella ɣef tlawḥt, nebɣa ad tt-nekkes akken ad tt-nessers ɣer yiɣil n uɣerbaz, ur yezmir ara ad yili ma yella mazal nella fell-as.

Iban-d belli ilaq ad nebdu s uweṭṭeɛ tlawḥt seg iman-nneɣ, a nekkes iman-nneɣ fell-as, imiren s yifassen-nneɣ ad nerr tlawḥt ɣer yiɣil.

S umata, ur ilaq ara ad nezdi iman-nneɣ d ugensu anefsan ma yella nebɣa s tidet ad t-newwet seg nnefs-nneɣ.

Asmi yiwen yezdi iman-is d wa neɣ d wa, s tidet i t-yeǧhed mgal ad t-yeḥḍer.

Ad neqbel belli yiwen seg iɣilfen n tbaṭelt iweḍ ɣer iɣerraben yellan deg tlemmast-nneɣ tatlayt iwakken ad yesken deg telqayt n wallaɣ tifelwa yellan d timḥizwa d tkerkas n tecḍit n tgelda, ma yella nezdin iman-nneɣ d tifelwa timeqranin, war ccekk, aɣilif-nni n tbaṭelt ad iǧhed aṭas.

Lameɛna ma yella, deg umkan ad nezdin iman-nneɣ d tidet-nni, a t-newwet seg nnefs-nneɣ a t-neḥseb d cciṭan ikecmen, iban-d belli ad teffeɣ-d deg tlemmast-nneɣ tafekka.

Deg deffir, nezmer ad neɛzel yiwen seg tɣellalin akken ad nesseḥbu iman-nneɣ.

Ayen i tt-yeqsen i medden d azday n iman-nsen, d ayen i ittḥeznen.

Ma yella medden ssnen tamussni n waṭas, ma yella fehmen s tidet belli ula d tudert-nsen ur teḥkim ara fell-asen, ur ttaɣen ara lɣalat n azday n iman-nsen.

Tifelwa n zzɛef, tifelwa n tismin, atg., deg umur n tudert n tilawt yezmer ad d-yawi fayda asmi nella dima nqeddec ɣef iman-nneɣ deg taggaẓt.

Imi nessen belli ula d ixemmimen-nneɣ, ula d tumniyin-nneɣ, ula d igiten-nneɣ ur n-iḥkim ara fell-asen.

War ccekk, aṭas n iɣilfen ikecmen am d imeksasen n diri akken ad rsen deg wallaɣ-nneɣ ixemmimen, deg wul-nneɣ tmuskiwin, deg tlemmast-nneɣ iɣiten n yal ṣṣifa.

Yettḥezzen belli ur n-iḥkim ara fell-aɣ iman-nneɣ, belli tidetin timsedduyin aɣ-xeddmen ayen i asen-yehwa.

Aɣyul ur neɛqal ara ayen i d-yettḍerrun yid-neɣ, nettkel am tidet-nni yellan d timeẓẓyanin tettwakkan s yifassen ur nettwali ara.

Ayen yellan d aẓayan yella deg-s d akken deg umkan ad naɣnaɣ akken ad naf iman-nneɣ seg iẓuran-nni uffren, nettkel ɣef lɣalat n usnerni-nsen, d ayen i d-yettili asmi nezdin iman-nneɣ.

Yal tagrest deg ubrid, yal tira n tewwacult, yal aɣyay ger wat zewǧen, yettili war ccekk ɣef uɣilif-nni, d ayen ur ilaq ara ad nettu.

Tudert n tilawt d tmesfert tanefsant ideg nezmer ad nẓer iman-nneɣ akken nella.

Lameɛna uqbel kullec, ilaq ad nefhem uḥettem n unadi ɣef iman-nneɣ, uḥettem n ubeddel s lemmerni, akka kan ara yili lebɣi deg-neɣ akken ad nqelleb s tidet ɣef iman-nneɣ.

Win iɛeqlen s wanda i yettidir, aberreɣ, win yettɛeṭṭilen, win yesɣaṛan, ur yettḥiss ara lebɣi n unadi ɣef iman-is, ad iḥemmel iman-is aṭas, ur yebɣi ara ad ibeddel tikli-ines d wamek yella.

S yiwet n talɣa yellan d tiddi, a d-nini belli deg kra n tmezguniwin, tullisin d temɣarin n tudert n tilawt, kecmen waṭas n iɣilfen i ilaqen ad nettwafhemen.

Afehhem yeqqen aṭas, ɣef ayagi i ilaq ad nesselqes dima, ayen i nefhemen ass-a s yiwet n talɣa, azekka ad t-nefhem akken ilaq.

Ma neẓra lecɣal seg talɣa-yagi, nezmer ad nessenqes s yiman-nneɣ amek d ifaydi yal teswiɛin n tudert asmi tent-nessuqqaɛ am tmesfert n unadi ɣef iman-nneɣ.

Ur nezmir ara ad nini belli tullisin, tmezguniwin d temɣarin n tudert n tilawt dima zeddigent d timeqranin, tanḍart-agi ad tili d taɣellat.

Lameɛna, ɣas akken d taɣellat yal taɣawsa n tilawt, d tamarant am aḥlaw anefsan.

Aɛmel i yeqqnen ɣer ugerrez n ibeddelen i yesɣaṛan i iman-iw, yettili d ayen yeqsen aṭas.

Ger lexyalat n usefru yeffren d lɣalat. Ger rriḥa taḥellet n iẓerfan, yeffren d lɣalat.

Lɣalat yezmer ad yuɣal d ayen yeddan gar yiman-is akken ad yettuɣal d tiddi, d ayen yesxeɛben akken ad yuɣal d taḥellwit.

Lɣalat yels-d taqendurt n uqeṛṛay, taqendurt n unelmad, taqendurt n umaṭṭaf, taqendurt n umɣar d taqendurt n Lmasiḥ.

Afehhem d aɣbalu, lameɛna deg uqelleɛ n ugensen anefsan, mačči d ayen akk, amek ara nẓer deg ssuq ilaq.

Yella deg uḍar, mačči deg deffir, ad nesseḥsi yal aɣilif akken ad t-newwet seg nnefs-nneɣ, lameɛna mačči d ayen akk, yettwaḥwaǧ ayen ugar, ẓer ssuq mraw d seddis.
