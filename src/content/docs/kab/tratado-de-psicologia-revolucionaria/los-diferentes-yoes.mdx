Aɣersiw amsuṭaḍ afehmaw yettwasemman d amdan sɣalatt, ur yesɛi tidet yiwet n tewsit tanemhalt. War ccekk, d tuggta-agi n tseddaṛt tanemhalt deg Uɣersiw-agiman i d ssebba n waṭas n tugdiwin d wurfan.

Tiddi tafizikit d taseddart yakk d tameqqrant, teqeddec am akken d tawacult tuzziglant, ɣas ma tuder. Maca, tudert tanemhalt n Uɣersiw-agiman ur d-teqqen ara am akken d taseddaṛt tanemhalt. Ayen yugaren akk deg waya, ɣas ma ttinin-t tɣerbatin yemgaraden n telɣiwin Taseudit d Tuffirt, d tuɣẓint n tseddaṛt tanemhalt deg ugerruj ussid n yal yiwen.

S tidet, deg umussu-agi ur yelli ara lxedma imduden am akken d tameqqrant deg tudert tanemhalt n medden. Uɣersiw-agiman, ɣef wayen icudden ɣer wayen yellan deg ugerruj-is, d tseddaṛt tazelmaṭ, d jemɛa n "Nek".

Imɣeflen imeɣṛasen n tallit-agi taḍulumt ttqeddicen ɣef "Nek", ttεebbiden-t, ttarren-t d illu, ssersen-t deg tmezday, ssawalen-as "Nek niḍen", "Nek Aɛlayen", "Nek Aqedsan", atg., atg., atg. Ur bɣin ara ad fehmen "Imeɣfelen" n laṣel-agi aberkan i nettidir deg-s, belli "Nek Aɛlayen" neɣ "Nek War Aɛlayen" d sin yecḍanen seg Ugu yemmezlen...

Uɣersiw-agiman ur yesɛi s tidet "Nek Yeddren" maca d agellid n "Nek" yemgaraden imerwaten d imɣelben. Aɣersiw amektaz aɣersiw yettwasemman d amdan sɣalatt yecba am akken d axxam deg uḍuṛṛuy anda maḍi d bab n uxxam, llan waṭas n iqeddacen i bɣan yal ass ad fernen d ad xedmen ayen i sen-yehwan...

Aḍɣaṛ aberkan akk n Taseuditt d Tuffirt yettwalasan dɣalatt d uselku, d asumer belli wiyaḍ sɛan neɣ yesɛa yiwen "Nek Yeddren Yettbeddilen" war azwel d war taggara... Ma widak yettxemmimen akka ad d-sersen tamusni ɣas ma d tallit kan, ad ssefhamen akken yelha i yiman-nsen belli Uɣersiw-agiman afehmaw ur yettili ara d yiwen i tallit ɣezzifen...

Aɣersiw amsuṭaḍ afehmaw, seg uneẓar anemhal, yettbeddil yal ass... Ad ttxemmimeḍ belli ma yella yiwen isem-is Luis d Luis yal ass, yettili-d kra am akken d taḍsa iǧehden... Amdan-agi yettwasemman Luis yesɛa deg yiman-is "Nek" niḍen, Igu niḍen, i d-yeffɣen seg tewsit-is deg tallitin yemgaraden yerna ɣas ma ur yehwi ara i Luis tira, "Nek" niḍen deg-s —ad as-nsemmu Pepe— yehwa-t tira akka...

Ulac yiwen n umdan i d yiwen s wazal; ur ihwaj ara s tidet ad tiliḍ d afehmaw s waṭas akken ad tefhemeḍ mliḥ beddel d tuqqna n yal yiwen... Ad ttxemmimeḍ belli yesɛa yiwen "Nek Yeddren Yettbeddilen" yessufuɣ seg-s dɣa azɣar ɣef wiyad d yiman-is...

Deg ugerruj n yal yiwen nettidir waṭas n medden, waṭas n "Nek", waya zemren ad t-id-yessefhem yiman-is dɣa yal yiwen yerqen, yesɛan tamusni...
