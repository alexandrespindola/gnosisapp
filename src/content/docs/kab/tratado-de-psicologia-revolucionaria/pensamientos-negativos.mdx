Ameẓẓuɣ meqqren d uqader meqqren d ayen ur nettamen ara deg tallit-a anda tuɣal tmetti ɣer deffir.

Seg Uẓar Amenzu, ttilin-d kra n ixemmimen ur d-ittasen ara seg "Nekk" d amɣar, akken ttamnen yimusnawen ur nessin, maca seg "Nekkawen" yemxalafen deg yal yiwen seg-neɣ.

Mi ara yettxemmim yiwen wergaz, yettamen s ṣṣeḥ belli netta s timmad-is d netta ara yettxemmimen.

Ur yebɣi ara ad iẓer amek yella umaḍal ameqqran, belli ixemmimen yemxalafen ay d-izegren fell-as, ur d-ittasen ara seg "Nekkawen" yemxalafen ay nesɛa deg-neɣ.

Aya yebɣa ad yini ur nelli ara d imdanen yettxemmimen s tidet; ur nessin ara ad nexdem tikta s timmad-nneɣ.

Lakin, yal yiwen seg "Nekkawen" yemxalafen ay nɛebban deg-neɣ, isseqdec Uẓar-nneɣ Amenzu, isseqdec-it yal tikkelt ay izmer ad yexmem.

Awal ur yesɛi lmeɛna ad nḥesseb iman-nneɣ ɣer umuɣ n ixemmimen usḥiqen d tixerḍiɛin, ma nessen belli d ayla n yal yiwen.

D ṣṣeḥ belli axemmem-a usḥiq d tixerḍiɛ yezmer ad d-yas seg "Nekk" ay isseqdec Uẓar-nneɣ Amenzu s lbaṭel.

Llan ixemmimen usḥiqen n mkul tameẓla: Ccik, tugar, tayri-nnec tucbiḥt ɣer umdan nniḍen, tseḍwit taberkant, tseḍwit tadinit, tseḍwit tasertit, tseḍwit ɣef imdukkal neɣ ɣef tawacult, tmuɣli, zzina, talwit, urrif, zzux, usmekti, tiɣri, tmeḥdla, tagaɣit, tmeddit, tirẓi, atg., atg., atg.

S tidet, aṭas n lɛuyub ay nesɛa, ɣas ma nesɛa iles n wuzzal d alef n ilsawen i wakken ad d-nheder, ur nezmir ad ten-id-nḥas s lekmal.

Am tuggni neɣ d amqeddem i wayen i d-neqqim deg-s, ad nḥseb iman-nneɣ ɣer umuɣ n ixemmimen usḥiqen d ayen ur yebɣi ara ad yili.

Akken ma yella ur yezmir ad yili lǧehd bla ssebba, ad d-nini s ṣṣeḥ belli ur yezmir ad yili axemmem s timmad-is, seg tlalit kan...

Tudert gar umexmem d uxemmem teban; yal axemmem usḥiq yesɛa aẓar-is deg umexmem yemxalafen.

Deg yal yiwen deg-neɣ llan aṭas n imexmamen usḥiqen, am ixemmimen n mkul tameẓla.

Ma nwala ɣer temsalt-a seg uẓar n "Imexmamen d Ixemmimen", yella yal yiwen seg "Nekkawen ay nɛebban deg Wul-nneɣ, d imexmem yemxalafen s ṣṣeḥ.

Ur yettwaḥseb ara belli deg yal yiwen deg-neɣ llan aṭas n imexmamen; maca, yal yiwen seg-sen ɣas ma d aḥric kan, yettamen belli netta d akk, deg tikkelt kan...

Imdanen yettarun tidet deg-sen, imdanen yettḥessiben iman-nsen d imeqqranen, imdanen iḥemmlen iman-nsen, imdanen yettḥessiben iman-nsen d imesluben, ur qeblen ara tamukrist n "Aṭas n Imexmamen" acku tḥemmlen iman-nsen s waṭas, tḥussun iman-nsen d "Baba-s n Tarzan" neɣ "Yemma-s n Yifsan"...

Amek imdanen-a ur nǧehden ara ad qeblen tikta belli ur sɛin ara tikta s timmad-nsen, tameɣbant, tameqqrant?...

Maca imusnawen-a ttamnen s iman-nsen ayen yellan d yessefk, ḥettan ttafen taksi n Aristippus i wakken ad s-d-seknen tamusni d tlemmast...

Yella-d ungal n tsutin belli Aristippus, mi yebɣa ad d-yesken tamusni d tlemmast, yelsa taksi taqdimt teččur d tɣersiwin d tqiccan; yeṭṭef deg ufus-is aẓẓelmud n Tfiluzuft, yedda deg iberdan n Atenas...

Qqaren mi t-id-iwala Socrates, yessawel s ṣṣut ɛlayen: "A Aristippus, iban-d zzux-ik seg tqiccan n lbisa-k!".

Win ur yettidir ara deg wadda n Uɣilas Aẓidan, Uɣilas Amaynut, yettxemmim belli yettxemmim, yettḥisseb iman-is ɣer mkul axemmem usḥiq s lfehma.

Seg waya, isnernay lǧehd aɣezzfan n "Nekk Usḥiq", ameɣras n uxemmem-nni.

Ar mi ara nḥesseb iman-nneɣ ɣer axemmem usḥiq, ar mi ara nili d aklan n "Nekk" ay t-ixedmen.

Ɣer tama n Gnosis, Ɣer Ubrid Uffir, ɣer lxedma ɣef yiman-nneɣ, tayri-nneɣ tayemmat tella-d deg "Nekkawen" ay yekkren Gnosis, lxedma tafelsuft, acku ur ẓerrin ara belli tudert-nsen deg wul-nneɣ tettwaqqen s Gnosis d lxedma.

"Nekkawen Usḥiqen" d ibeddilen ttqelbin lḥala ɣef tṭabla n tikta ay itwaḥerzen deg Uẓar-nneɣ Amenzu, yezmer ad d-yawi irden n tikta tixerḍiɛin d usḥiqen.

Ma neqbel ixemmimen-nni, "Nekkawen Usḥiqen" ay iḥettmen Uẓar-nneɣ Amenzu deg tikkelt kan, ur nezmir ad nserreḥ iman-nneɣ seg wayen ara d-yaf.

Ur yettun ara ad nɛqel belli yal "Nekk Usḥiq" "Yettɣuṛṛu" iman-is d "Iɣuṛṛu", azalen: Yeskaddab.

Yal tikkelt mi ara neḥsus s uɣelluy n lǧehd, mi ara yemdel win yebɣan Gnosis, lxedma tafelsuft, mi ara yeflali lḥamu-ines yerna yeǧǧa ayen yellan d yessefk, iban belli iɣuṛ-it-id kra n Nekk Usḥiq.

"Nekk Usḥiq n Tagaɣit", yesserɣay tixxamin yesɛan azalen meqqren yerna yettarra igerdan d ixṣaren.

"Nekk Usḥiq n Tsedḍwit", yeskaddeb fell-asen yerna yesseɣri tudert-nsen.

"Nekk Usḥiq n Zzux Uɣelben" yeskaddeb ɣef wid yesɛan leɛnaya n Ubrid yerna, mi ara ḥussen iman-nsen d imusnawen, ad kṛhen Ustad-nsen neɣ ad t-sxuɛen...

Nekk Usḥiq yessawal ɣer tilawin-nneɣ, ɣer tira-nneɣ, ɣer ayen yellan d azalen ɣur-nneɣ, ɣer tidet-nneɣ, yerna, s tferḍet yesɛan lqima tameqqrant, yesken-d kra deg tafat tukeddabt, kra ay yesserɣayen yerna iteddu-d uxessaṛ...

Maca, mi ara yaf yiwen "Nekk" deg lxedma, mi ara yelmed ad yidir deg wadda n uɣilas, tiɣuṛṛa-nni teqqen...
