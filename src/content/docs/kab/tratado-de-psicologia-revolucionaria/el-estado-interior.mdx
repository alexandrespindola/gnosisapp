Aseqqem n wallaɣ deg wayen yeḍran deg berra, d ayen yelhan i tudert... Yal ayen i d-yeḍran s tmusni, yesseḥwaj allaɣ yettwassnen...

Lameɛna, ɣas akken, mi ara d-ẓren medden tudert-nsen, ḥesben-tt d ayen kan yeḍran deg berra... A yazit nsen! Ḥesben belli lemmer ur asen-d-yedri waya neɣ waya, tudert-nsen ad ilḥu...

Ḥesben belli d ṣṣeḥ kan i d-yeffeɣ fell-asen, u iṛuḥ-asen ussan-nsen i sɛaden... Ḥeznen ɣef wayen i sen-yeṛwan, țrun ɣef wayen ḥeqṛen, țermun mi d-mmektayen ayen i d-yeḍran yid-sen...

Ur bɣin ara ad fehmen medden belli ur yelli umgerrud d tudert, u tezmert ad teddreḍ s tmusni teɛla kan ɣef ṣṣifa n wallaɣ deg wul... Ur yelli d acu i d-yedṛun deg berra yelhan, ma ur nelli ara deg wallaɣ yettwassnen, ayen yelhan ad n-tettwali d umgerrud, d ayen yeɛyan neɣ d ayen ur neḥmil...

Yella win yețṛaǧun lḥifla n tameɣra, d ayen i d-yeḍran, lameɛna yezmer ad yeqqim yețxemmim deg ussan-nni, ur yettɛazza deg uẓri, u ad yuɣal kullec d imɣid u d asemmiḍ am ttawil...

Tadyant tessen-aɣ belli ur kkul medden i d-ițɛazzan lmakla neɣ ccḍeḥ... Ur yelli ara win yețɛeyyun deg lḥiflat yellan, u ayen yelhan yețɛazza kra, u yesserɣay wiyaḍ...

Drus aṭas i d-yessnen amek ara d-jemɛen ayen yeḍran deg berra d wayen yellan deg wul... D ayen yesseḥzen mi ur ssinen ara medden amek ara ddren s tmusni: țrun mi ilaq ad seḍsen, u seḍsen mi ilaq ad țrun...

Amennuɣ yemgarad: Amusnaw yezmer ad ifraḥ lameɛna ur yețțawi ara axxam-is, yeḥzen lameɛna ur yețeɛjeb ara u yețḥeqqer... yesmeḥ deg tlelli; yețḥudd deg tmeɣra; d aseḥbib gar tkerkas, atg.

Medden yețḥezznen u țḥeqqiṛen ḥesben tudert d ayen diri u ur bɣin ara ad ddren... Yal ass nețwali medden ur sɛin ara lfeṛḥ, lameɛna - d ayen yugaren - sseɣlayen tudert n wiyaḍ...

Medden am wigi ur țbeddilen ara ma ddren yal ass seg tmeɣra ɣer tmeɣra; aṭṭan n tmusni yella deg wul-nsen... medden am wigi sɛan allaɣen diri...

Lameɛna wid yellan am wigi zellun iman-nsen d wid yellan d ṣṣeḥ, d imqeddsen, d wid yelhan, d wid yesɛan azal, d wid yettqedimen, d wid yemmuten fell-aɣ, atg., atg., atg. D medden yesɛan iman-nsen d imeqqranen; medden iḥemmlen iman-nsen aṭas...

Wid ițḥaznen fell-asen u yettnadin yal ttawil akken ad rewlun ɣef ṭṭbiɛa-nsen... Medden am wigi ɛaden ɣef tmuɣliwin diri, u d ayen iban belli ɣef ayagi xellaqen yal ass allen n tmusni i d-yellan dɣif...

Ayen diri, tmuɣliwin n ṣṣeḥ, tlawinat, ideqqi, uguren, atg., d ayen yellan kan i wid ur nessin ara amek ara ddren... Yezmer yiwen ad yesnerni tamusni-ines, lameɛna drus medden i yelmden ad ddren s ṣṣeḥ...

Mi ara yebɣu yiwen ad yefreq ayen yeḍran deg berra d wallaɣen deg tmusni, iban belli ur yezmir ara ad yili s wazal-is. Wid yelmden ad jemɛen s tmusni ayen yeḍran deg berra d wallaɣen yellan deg wul, țeddun deg ubrid n uzwir...
