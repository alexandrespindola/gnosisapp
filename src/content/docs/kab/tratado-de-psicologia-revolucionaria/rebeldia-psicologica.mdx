Ur nezmer ad n-id-nesmekti i wid iɣuruyen, yella-d umkan ussnan deg iman-nneɣ...

Bla ccek, amkan-a, ur yettili ara deg yizri, ur yettili ara daɣen deg ussan ara d-yasen...

Win yebɣan ad yaf amkan-a ussanen, ilaq ad t-iɛawed deg da akked tura, deg wul-is, deg useggas-agi kan, ur yettɣimi ara aseggas ɣer zdat, ur yettɣimi ara aseggas ɣer deffir...

Sin yiɣezdisen Ighezfi d Uhrawi n Ssalib Imezdi, tt-tilin deg umkan-a...

Nettili-d seg useggas ɣer useggas zdat sin iberdan: Uhrawi d Ughezfan...

Yettban-d belli Uhrawi d "aɛeffan" aṭas, fell-as tt-teddun "Vicente akk d medden meṛṛa", "Villegas akk d win yusan", "Don Raimundo akk d ddunit meṛṛa"...

Yettban-d belli Ughezfan yemxalef; d abrid n yemɛaṣiyen ifehhmen, d win n Yemrawliyen...

Mi ara yettmekti yiwen iman-is, mi ara yexdem ɣef iman-is, mi ur yettemcabiɛ ara d uguren d lehmum n tudert, s tideṭ yedda deg Ubrid Ughezfan...

S tideṭ ur yettili ara d axeddim asehlel ad tekkes tuggdiwin tilesnin; ad teffeɣ mcabiɛ akk d uɣerbaz n tudert-nneɣ; uguren n mkul taliwin, tijara, tuɣalin, takerrist n idrimen, lexlas n tebrat, timɣart, tilifun, aman, tafat, atg., atg., atg.

Iɣezzayen, wid i yeɣlin ɣef tili neɣ ɣef tayeḍ, ɣef tili, ɣef lxedma, s tideṭ ttaɣen lehmum ɣef tugga n idrimen, ad tettuɣen ɣef tili, ur ttettɛebbaɛen ara, ur ttettemcabiɛen ara d ugur-nsen, yettili-d s tideṭ d ayen yessḍeqsifen.

Wid yettɛeggiḍen, wid yettruɣen, wid i d-yuɣalen d timeddurin n lxiyana, n wayen ur yexseṛ ara deg tudert, n Tuggdi, n Tkerki neɣ n tɣeddaṛt, s tideṭ ttaɣen iman-nsen, seg Win-nsen Amaziɣ, ttemcabiɛen akk d twenza-nsen...

Lxedma ɣef iman-is d tameɣra tagejdant n Ubrid Ughezfan. Ulac win ara yeddun deg Ubrid n Tɛesrit tameqqrant, ma yella ur yexdim ara ɣef iman-is...

Lxedma ɣef wayen i nettmeslay d tagella n Tsekkinin; ttekkat-d kra n beddel n useggas i deg nenɛem. Ilaq a nlmed a neddu seg useggas ɣer useggas...

Ɣer umedya, yiwen n wexxam i yettili d aqessar ɣef kra n ugur n ul, n idrimen neɣ n tsertit, yettban-d belli yettu iman-is...

Axxam-a ma yella yettɛeqqel aseggas kan, ma yella yettmuqul s tewenza, yesɛan ad yettmekti iman-is, sin akin ad iɛeṛṛed ad yefhem sser n tewenza-s...

Ma yella yettxemmem kra, ma yella yettxemmem belli kullec iɛedda; belli tudert d asɣen, d taɛebbant, belli lmut tessers ar iɣessan kullec ur neqqim...

Ma yella yefhem belli ugur-is deg ubrid ur yettili ara d "taɣessa n umɣi", d times teɣzifent ara yettfuɣen, ad yettwali aseggas-a s ṣṣurpreza belli kullec yettbeddel...

Ad nbeddel tagrawin tikerrasin yezmer ma nmuqel s tmusni d Tmusni Talsint deg Win...

Yettban-d belli medden ttgellin tikerrasin zdat tewenza n tudert...

A medden imeɣban!, Kulwaḥ ttgellin d timeddurin. Mi ara asen-d-ɛewwiden, ttmeggiren; mi ara ten-d-ɛessben, ttaɣen lehmum. Ttɛeyyiṛen ma yella ttɛeyyiṛen-ten; ttrajun ma yella ttrajun-ten; ur tt-tilin ara d ilelliyen; idda-nsen sɛan tazmert ad ten-ddun seg lfeṛḥ ɣer lehmum, seg usirem ɣer teɣzi...

Mkul axxam n wid yeddun deg Ubrid Uhrawi, yettemcabi d amusnaw amennaf, anda mkul yiwen seg yidda-s yettales-d wayen i d-yusan...

Win yelmaden ad yebeddel tagrawin tikerrasin, s tideṭ yettkessen "Ubrid Ughezfan".

Ayagi yettmeslay-d beddel agejdan deg "Tasniremt n Win", tamesniremt taɛezzant n "Tɛesrit Tsekkinint".
