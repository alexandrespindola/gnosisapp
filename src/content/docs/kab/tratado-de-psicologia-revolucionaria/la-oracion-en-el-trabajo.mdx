Lemɛayna, asefti d usentem, d tlata yifadden imeqqranen n tneɣṛa.

Amenzu: a nleḥḥu deg-s. Wis sin: a nsefti. Wis tlata: a nsemmet.

Imiẓla deg umgaru, amenzu a ten-nleḥḥu deg-sen; wis sin a ten-nsefti; wis tlata a ten-nneɣ.

Deg ugaraw-n-garaw yella ufran n iman d uɣelleḍ n iman. Win igellun i tmettant s wid yettemcabin fell-as, igellu daɣen i ufran n iman.

Akken yella kra n usatal n tudert, ɣas akken ur yesɛi ara azal, ur yelli ara fell-as ccek, yesɛa s sebba amseḍru daxel-nneɣ, aggur apsixi, "nekk".

Ufran n iman yezmer ad yili mi ara nili deg wassaɣ n uɣilif, asatal amaynut.

"Nekk", yettwafhem in fraganti, ilaq ad yettuleḥḥu deg uɣilif deg wallaɣ-nneɣ, ul-nneɣ d tsegda-nneɣ.

Nekk, anda yella kra n leḥcic yezmer ad yegelleḍ deg ul am tayri, deg wallaɣ am tmusni, maca mi ara nerr tira i tsegda, a nḥuss kra n uɣilif amurḍin ur yemgaraden ara.

Asefti n kra n Nekk ilaq ad yeqqim. Ilaq a t-nessens deg umḍiq n wid yettuccetan d a t-nsefti s tuẓẓda.

Kra n uɛeyyu, assekned, assider, ilaq ad yettwakkes, ma yella s tidet nebɣa ad nwali "Nekk" i nebɣa ad n-nekkes seg ipsixisnneɣ.

Asentem yemgared; ur yezmir ara ad yili asentem i kra n "Nekk", bla ma yettwaẓra yettwasefta.

Tẓallit deg lxedma apsixulujit d talɣa i tneɣṛa. Neḥwaǧ taẓẓalit yugaren wallaɣ, ma yella s tidet nebɣa ad nesseɣṛeb kra n "Yo".

Wallaɣ iman-is ur yezmir ad yesseɣṛeb kra n "Yo", ayagi d ayen ur yettwarfed, ur yettwaxṣen.

Tẓallit d awal akk d Rebbi. Ilaq a neẓẓel i Rebbi Taymat deg Ugerruj-nneɣ, ma yella s tidet nebɣa ad nesseɣṛeb "Yoes", win ur yeḥemmel ara Taymat-is, mmi-s amnegfu, ad yeɣleb deg lxedma ɣef iman-is.

Yal wa seg-neɣ yesɛa Taymat-is Taɛlayant taneggarut, taneftarit, nettat iman-is d tama seg iman-nneɣ, maca seg-s i d-yekka.

Tudrin teqdimin akk seɛden "Rebbi Taymat" deg ugerruj-nneɣ. Tallit tameṭṭut n Wid Dawmen d ISIS, MARÍA, TONANZIN, CIBELES, REA, ADONIA, INSOBERTA, atg., atg., atg.

Ma yella deg wayen n ddunit yesɛa baba-tneɣ d yemma-tneɣ, deg wayen yellan d aɣerfan deg iman-nneɣ, yesɛa daɣen Baba-tneɣ yellan deg usuter d Taymat-nneɣ Taɛlayant KUNDALINI.

Llan baba-tneɣ deg igenwan akken llan irgazen deg tmurt. Rebbi Taymat deg ugerruj-nneɣ d tallit tameṭṭut n Baba-tneɣ yellan deg usuter.

Nettat d Nettat s tideț d snat n teggura n Ugerruj-nneɣ iman. Ur yelli fell-as ccek Nettat d Nettat d iman-nneɣ s tidet yugaren "Yo" n Apsixulujia.

Nettat tettwaɣelleḍ deg Nettat yettuḥar, yettuder, yettusellem. Nettat tettekkes tid yellan ur neḥwaǧ ara daxel-nneɣ, i nekkren, s lexdeɛ i lmudiɛat ɣef iman-is.

Mi ara nemmet s tuẓẓda, mi ara ad kksen akk tid yellan ur neḥwaǧ ara seld lxedma i lmudiɛat d yixṣasen i lmudiɛat a nesseɣṛeb d a nesseɣṛeb akk d "Baba-Taymat", seld ad nili d Ṛebbit uɣezfanen i lmudiɛat, yugaren ayen yelhan d ayen diri.

Taymat-nneɣ Taɛlayant taneggarut, tanefriqt, s taẓẓalt-is yezmer ad ten-yesseɣṛeb, ad ten-yesseɣṛeb i yiɣessan i kra n "Yoes", i yettwaẓran yettwaseftan.

Ur yelli deg yiman-is kra n taɣawsa tameqqrant i tẓallit i Taymat-nneɣ Taɛlayant daxel. Ilaq a nili d ayen yettwassnen d isehlen deg tuddsa deg Nettat. Aqcic i yettuddus i yemma-s, ur yesɛi ara tira tifelwitin, yeqqar ayen i d-yekkan seg ul-is d ayagi kan.

Ulac kra n "Yo" yettneɣṛen s tura; Taymat-nneɣ Taɛlayant ilaq ad txeddem d ḥetta ad tḥemmel aṭas s tura, ad tḥemmel aṭas i kra n uɣrib n kra n "Yo".

Walit tizwiri, sɣurrez tẓallit-nwen ɣer daxel, steqsiyit deg ugerruj-nwen i Señora-nwen Taɛlayant d s tẓallit i lmudiɛat tizmert a tt-sawlit. Rǧut atesseɣṛeb ayagi n "Yo" i tleḥḥum yettwasefta.

Asekkil n tmuɣli-nwen tanaftawt, akken ad yeqqel d win yettwassen, a wen-yeğğ ad tẓrem abrid azzayez deg lxedma-nwen.

Assaɣ, asefti, d talɣa, maca tḥwaǧit kra n ayen yugaren ma yella s tidet nebɣa ad nesseɣṛeb "MI IMAN".

Wallaɣ yezmer ad yefk i iman-is asɣer i kra n uɣilif, ad t-yegger seg uxxam ɣer uxxam, ad t-yesseɣṛeb, ad t-yesseḥbi, atg., maca ur yezmir ara ad t-yebdel s tuẓẓda.

Tḥwaǧit "taẓẓalt tasemmant" yugaren wallaɣ, taẓẓalt tameẓɣut yezmer ad ten-yerrem aɣessan i kra n uɣilif.

STELLA MARIS, Taymat-nneɣ Taɛlayant, tesɛa taẓẓalt-agi, yezmer ad teseɣṛeb kra n uɣilif apsixulujit.

Taymat-nneɣ Taɛlayant, ttedder deg ugerruj-nneɣ, yugaren tafekka, nniyat d wallaɣ. Nettat iman-is d taẓẓalt taneggart yugaren wallaɣ.

Taymat-nneɣ Takusmit taneggarut, tanefriqt, tesɛa tussna, tayri d taẓẓalt. Deg-s yella asseɣti s tuẓẓda.

Tniyyat yelhan d asseɣzi amezwaru n tid i t-id-yetteddun, ur yesɛa ara azal, ur ttidiren ara.

Ur yesɛi ara azal asseɣzi: "ur ttili ara d awessur"; Yoes n leḥcic akk ad ttɣimen akka, ad ilin deg ugerruj n ipsixisnneɣ.

Ur yesɛi ara azal asseɣzi yal ass: "ur ttili ara d aweɛri". "Yoes" n uɛeryal ad ttɣimen akka, ad ilin deg ifadden apsixulujit.

Ur yesɛi ara azal asseɣzi yal ass: "ur ttili ara d aqerfa". "Yoes" n tkerfa ad ttɣimen akka, ad ilin deg iberdan imeggura n ipsixisnneɣ.

Ur yesɛi ara azal ad neffeɣ seg ddunit d ad neqqim deg udar neɣ ad nedder deg kra n tɣzert; "Yoes" daxel-nneɣ ad ttɣimen akka, ad ilin.

Kra n yineɣmisen yellan deg tɣezrin s lexdeɛ n tisselbiwin yewwḍen ɣer takremt n imeqqranen d ad ttwaɣellḍen ɣer igenwan, anda ufan d slan i wayen ur yezmir ara ad t-fehmen imdanen; maca "Yoes" ttɣimin akka, ad ilin deg ugerruj-nsen.

Ur yelli fell-as ccek Esencia yezmer ad teffeɣ seg "Yo" s lexdeɛ n tisselbiwin d ad taɛref takremt, maca, seld lferḥ, tettraǧu ɣer ugerruj n "Mi Iman".

Win yezgan ɣer takremt, ur yettwaseɣṛeb ara "Ego", ttmudduen d ayen i wumi nnan ad yaweḍ ɣer tlelli, ttukerrihen iman-nsen, ttmudduen d Masas d ḥetta ad yettɣellḍen ɣer Involución yettwafḍen.

A d-nsemmet fell-awen ɣef uɛelleq amekrim, ɣef takremt d lferḥ n Ulmi deg uɣrib n EGO.

Nebɣa kan ad nerr tira ɣef tḥwaǧit n usseɣreb n "Yoes" i tili n tlelli taneggarut.

Esencia n kra n uneɣmis i lmudiɛat, yezgan ad teffeɣ seg "Yo", yesseɣzi ayagi seld lmut n tafekka, yettɛerref takremt s kra n lawan d seld yettuɣal am Genio n tiftilin n Aladino ɣer ugerruj n tfeltilit, ɣer Ego, ɣer Mi Iman.

Selda ur yettɣimi ara gar-asen ad yettarra ɣer tafekka tamaynut, i usentem n useɣzi n tudert-is ɣef ugerruj n tudert.

Aṭas n imuddiren i yettwassen deg tɣezrin n Himalayas, deg Asía Taɣerfant, tura d imdanen yellan d ibawen, d ayen yellan d isehlen deg ddunit-agi, ɣas akken wid i t-ittebɛen mazal ttwaraǧun ttwassaman.

Kra n usentem n tlelli ɣas akken d ameqqran ayagi, ma ur yesɛi ara deg allen tḥwaǧit n usseɣreb n Ego, d ayen yettusemmet fell-as ɣer uɣelleb.
