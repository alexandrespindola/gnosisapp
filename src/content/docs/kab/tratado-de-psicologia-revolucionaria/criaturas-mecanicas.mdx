Ur nezmir ara ad nenkeṛ amenzu n tuɣalin iteddun deg yal tallit n tudert-nneɣ.

Tidet deg yal ass n tilin-nneɣ, tella tennalit n wayen yeḍran, tegnatin n tmuɣli, awal, ayen i nebɣa, ixemmimen, lebɣi, atg.

D ayen yettbanen mliḥ, mi ara ur nezmir ara ad nessenqad iman-nneɣ, ur nezmir ara ad nerr d tamuɣli tenɣalt telɣut n tennalit-agi yal ass.

Yettban-d daɣen belli win ur yesɛi ara kra n lferḥ s usenqad n iman-is, ur yebɣi ara daɣen ad yexdem iwakken ad yaweḍ ɣer beddel aqbayli deg wayen yella.

Aɣbel ɣef uɣbel, llan wid yebɣan ad beddlen mbla ma xedmen ɣef yiman-nsen.

Ur nenkeṛ ara tidet n yal wa yesɛa azref ɣer tumert taḥeqqanit n ddewla, maca d tidet daɣen, belli tumert ad tili ayen yugaren win i nezmer, ma ur nexdim ara ɣef yiman-nneɣ.

Yezmer ad yebeddel umdan deg ddaw n wul-is, mi ara ad izmer s tidet ad yebeddel tikkinin-is ɣer wayen yeddan yal ass.

Lameɛna ur nezmir ara ad nbeddel tikkinin-nneɣ ɣer wayen yeddan deg tudert n tira, ḥaca ma nexdem s tidet ɣef yiman-nneɣ.

Nḥwaj ad nbeddel tarrayt-nneɣ n umexxi, ad nili d kraɛniqen, ad nerr d wid yesɛan tamusni, ad neddem tudert s tarrayt yemgerraden, deg unamek-ines aḥeqqani d taɣdimt.

Lameɛna, ma neddukkel akka akken nella, nsenfari iman-nneɣ s tarrayt-agi yal ass, nennali imalasen-nneɣ, s tenɣalt n zman, yal tamellaḥ i beddel ad teffeɣ.

Ma yella umdan yebɣa ad yissen iman-is, ilaq ad yebdu s usenqad n tmehwa-ines, ɣer wayen yeddan deg ussan n tudert.

Ur neqsed ara s waya, belli ur ilaq ara ad yessenqad umdan iman-is yal ass, neqsed kan ad nsemmes belli ilaq ad yebdu s usenqad n wass amezwaru.

Deg wayen akk ilaq ad yili abeddi, ad yebdu s usenqad n tmehwa-nneɣ deg wass n tudert-nneɣ, d abeddi yelhan.

Ad nessenqad tikkinin-nneɣ timikkanizin ɣer wayen akk yeddan deg tayda, axxam, imensi, uxxam, abrid, axeddim, atg., atg., atg., ayen yeqqar umdan, yessuḥ yettxemmem, d ayen yelhan.

Ayen yesɛan azal, ad nezmer ad nbeddel tikkinin-agi, lameɛna ma nella nessen belli nlaqen, ur nsenfari iman-nneɣ s tmehwa.

Zdat yal ass, nḥwaj ad nefhem belli nlaqen i maschinen, d iberqas itebbren s igensayen imesbaḍen, s Yiwaten yetterwen.

Deg iman-nneɣ ddren waṭas n medden, ur nelli ara yiwet; tikwal tettban-d deg-neɣ taɣdemt, tikwal tettedderɣal, deg tallit niḍen tettban-d tellaɣett, ttneɛɛim, umbeɛd tella teɣdemt d tameɣbunni, umbeɛd d walders, umbeɛd d umeskaddab, atg.

Nesɛa medden n yal adeg deg yal wa deg-neɣ, Yiwaten n yal ass. Tudert-nneɣ mačči d aberqas, d uɛeqal itmeslayen, d ayen yettmeslayen.

Ad nebdu s usenfar n iman-nneɣ deg kra n tallit seg wass; nḥwaj ad nneffeɣ ad nili d maschinen ɣas deg tallit taqessran seg ussan, ayagi ad yessenfel ɣef tilin-nneɣ.

Mi ara nessenqad iman-nneɣ, ur nxeddem ara ayen yebɣa Yiwat agi neɣ agi, tettban-d belli nebda ad neffeɣ ad nili d maschinen.

Tallit kan, deg ara nili nesɛa tmuɣli, ad neffeɣ ad nili d machine, ma nettellaɣet, ad yebeddel ɣef yakan waṭas n tegnatin tircin.

Aɣbel, nxeddem yal ass tudert tamikkanizit, taneɣruft, tatut. Tennalit n wayen yeddan, d leɛwayed-nneɣ d yiwet, ur nebɣi ara ad ten-nbeddel, d abrid amikkanizi anda yetteddu uɣerruj n tilin-nneɣ, lameɛna nettxemmim fell-aɣ ayen yelhan...

Anda i yella waṭas n "MITOMANOS", wid yumen belli d Ṛebbi; tidet timikkanizin, taneɣruft, medden seg ucqaq n tmurt, d iberqasen itebbren s Yiwaten yemgerraden; medden akka ur xedmen ara ɣef yiman-nsen...
