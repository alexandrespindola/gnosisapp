Deg ugar n tudert, nettaf yal ass amgarrad i d-yettmettilen. Imedyazen yesɛan axxam yelhan d aṭas n imdukkal, zemren ad ɛeddin tikwal tiɣellest... Imazɣanen iraqalen n uɣrab d umendil neɣ medden n tewsit talemmast, ttilin tikwal ttidiren s useɛdi.

Aṭas n iɣellaten isemyunanen ttnadin deg tazzla n sex d tiḍut meqqren ttmaggant i tmenɣiwt n urgaz... Imedyazen n tmurt d iẓemmar gar tqeffazin n wureɣ, deg wussan-agi ur zmiren ad idiren mbla "ɛessas"... Irgazen n udabu ttazzalen izenqan, ur llin ur tlelli, ttilin yal ass ttimlilen medden yesɛan aselḥu ar d teẓreḍ...

Ad nezreɣ lḥala-agi akken yelha. Ilaq a nessin d acu-t tudert. Yal wa yella tlelli-s ad yini amek yebɣa... Ad inin ayen ad inin, mačči d tidett ulac win yessnen kra, tudert tettili d ugur ur yessefham yiwen...

Mi ara bɣun medden ad aɣ-d hekkun mbla ccerṭ amezruy n tudert-nsen, ttaren iseflanen, ismawen d yisemawen, iḍaren, atg., ttemɣalen mi ara xedmen timuɣliwin-nsen... Imazɣanen-agi imeɣbanen ur ssinen timuɣliwin-nsen meqqren acku iseflanen, ismawen d iḍaren, d ayen ibanen deg umezruy, ur d-iban ara wayen yellan deg wul...

D tazzla ad nessin "teɣmiwin n lfen", yal aseflan yella deg-s neɣ yella deg-s amek yella wul. Teɣmiwin d timezgatin d iseflanen d iɣella, iseflanen ɣer barra ur tellin ara akk...

Ulac akk deg-s teɣmiwin timezgatin zemrent ad tidirent d iseflanen ɣer barra neɣ ad d-tekken seg-sen, neɣ ur yelli ara gar-asen kra... Deg yal lḥal, teɣmiwin d iseflanen mgaraden. Macci yal ass iseflanen ttidiren d teɣmiwin yeqreben.

D tidett yella araḍ amazɣati ara ad yemlili d useflan ɣer barra... Aṭas n tikkal aseflan ur nettṛaǧu, d ayen ara aɣ-d yefken lawan yelhan...
