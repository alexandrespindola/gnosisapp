Deg ubrid n tudert n tidet, nettaf daymen imxalaf i deg netɛeǧǧeb. Medden yesɛan aṭas n idrimen, yesɛan iɣerban yelhan d waṭas n imdukkal, zemren ad ḍuqen aṭas. .. Imaziɣen d imesluben neɣ medden n ungal s umata, ttɛicen aṭas n tikkal di tferḥent.

Ad neɣre taɣellist-a s tugett. Yessefk ad neẓer d acu-tt tudert. Yal yiwen yeḥwaǧ tilelli ad yaru amek yebɣa...
