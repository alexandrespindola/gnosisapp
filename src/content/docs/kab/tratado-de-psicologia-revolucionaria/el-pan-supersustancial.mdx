Ma nwala mliḥ kra n wass deg tudert-nneɣ, ad nẓer belli s tideț ur nessin ara amek ara nɛic s tmuɣli.

Tudert-nneɣ tețțemcabi i uɣerruj yetteddun, yettḥerrik deg iberdan iḍelben n lɛادات imekkaniyen, yesɣunnen, n tellaḍt tilellit d tin teɣɣuṛt.

Ayen yessewhamen deg umedya-agi d akken ur yețțuɣal ara ɣur-nneɣ ad nbeddel lɛادات, yettban-d ur neṭṭuq taɛyit si teṭṭuft-nneɣ i yiman-nneɣ amennuɣ.

Lɛادات ɣur-nneɣ ḥerṣent-نهغ, akteṛ nețḥesab iman-nneɣ ⵉlleliyen; ncemmeḍ mliḥ بصح nețḥesab iman-nneɣ Apolos...

Nekkni d iɣerman imekkaniyen, ssbb yugaren akken ad teffeɣ fell-aneɣ yal iḥulfan n tidett n wayen i nexddem deg tudert.

Netthirrik yal ass deg uɣerruj aqdim n lɛادات-nneɣ tiqdimin d timaɛna ur nesɛi, akka ay yessefraren ur nesɛi ara tudert n tidett; deg umkan ad nɛic, netteffeɣ s lexyab, ur nețțaɣ ara igezzilen imaynuten.

Ma yella win ibedden ass-is s tmuɣli, d ayen iwalan akken ass-agi ad yemmgarad aṭas ɣef ussan-nniḍen.

Mi ara yefk yiwen akk tudert-is, am wass-nni i yettidir, mi ur yețțaǧǧa ara i uzekka ayen ilaq ad yexdem ass-a, ad yaweḍ s tidet ad yeẓer ayen i d tagnit ad yexdem ɣef yiman-is.

Ur yeɛdil yiwen wass seg azal; ma yella s tideț nebɣa ad nbeddel iman-nneɣ s uɣrud, ilaq-aɣ ad nẓer iman-nneɣ, ad nemuqlu iman-nneɣ d ad neɛqel iman-nneɣ yal ass.

Lameɛna, iɣerman ur bɣin ara ad ẓren iman-nsen, kra sɛan akk aɣilif ad xedmen ɣef yiman-nsen, țseddiqen tasɣent-nsen s yiwet n tefyirt am ta: "Lexḍamt deg uɣerbaz ur tețțaǧǧa ara ad nexdem ɣef yiman-nneɣ". Awalen-agi ur nesɛi ara lmeɛna, d irqeqen, d ilelliyen, d timaɛna ur nesɛi, ayen kan yettaɛanen ad ssedqen asseɛyu, akraɛ, ulac tayri ɣef Tɣawsa Tameqqrant.

Iɣerman-agi, ɣas sɛan aṭas n tmusniwin tiruḥaniyin, d ayen iwalan akken ur țbeddilen ara abaden.

Ad nemuqlu iman-nneɣ d ayen yeẓɣel, ur yelli-d uzekka, ur yelli-d asyeffeɣ. Tafada n yiman n uneggaru d tin yesɛan azal tameqqrant i ubeddel n tidett.

D acu-t ḥal-ik apsykuluji mi ara teddukkel? D acu-t ḥal n wul-ik deg uqeṛṛay? Tellaḍ-t ṭṭfaṛ amɛellem?, د yelli-k? علاش تلاضف ططافر؟ D acu-t ayen ديما كيزوهر؟, Atg.

Ad tețțeɣ ssiɣaṛu neɣ ad teččeɣ drus ur d-yettawi ara akk ubeddel, lameɛna ma yella yettban-d kra n umaɣlaɣ. Nessin mliḥ akken aɛyac d tečči d wid ur nɣenniɣ d iɣersiwen.

Ur yelhi ara ma yella yiwen yettwaṣen ɣer Ubrid Uweḥḥi, يسعى جسم, يوڨاران مليح د يسعة عبد الكريم و خرجا سڨري دي توقا دكمال. Ayagi ad yesseken tečči, laɣwa d ablaɣ.

Tudert n yal ass, lexḍamt, leɛmeṛ, ɣas sɛan azal i tellaḍt, țɣimin tizzla n tmuɣli.

Ad tessneḍ akken tudert d tizzla ur d-yettawi ara d neɛqel-itt. Aɛeqel d-yettawi-d tafada n yiman d lexḍamt tameqqrant ɣef yiman.

Iwakken ad texḍmeḍ ɣef yiman-ik, d ayen ilaq ad texḍmeḍ ɣef tudert-ik n yal ass, ass-a s timmad-is, imiren ad teɛqleḍ ayen d tagnit tefyirt-nni n Uẓallu n Mass: "Fken-aɣ aɣṛum-nneɣ n yal ass".

Tefyirt "Yal Ass", tagnit "Aɣṛum asuɣeṛ-aɣ" deg ugris neɣ "Aɣṛum n Uffella".

Lgnus tefka aɣṛum-agi n Tudert deg umeldi yesɛan sin imaruten n tmusniwin d iẓuran ay ɣ-yettaǧǧan ad nseɣli aseɣẓan apsykuluji.

Yal tikelt mi ara nesseɣli ɣer tafirest takusmit ta neɣ ta "Nek", nessufuɣ tajriba apsykuluji, netteč "Aɣṛum n Tmusni", nețțaɣ tamusni tamaynut.

Lgnus tefka-aɣ "Aɣṛum Aswasɣer", "Aɣṛum n Tmusni", d tețțaǧǧa-d s teɣzi tudert tamaynut ay tebda deg yiwen iman-is, deg yiwen iman-is, daɣ d tura.

Tura, ih, yiwen ur yezmir ad ybeddel tudert-is neɣ ad ibeddel kra yellan deg tmennaɛtin imekkaniyin n tellaḍt, ɣas ulac fell-as ɛawen n tmusniwin timaynutin d ad yeṭṭef aqbayli.

Lgnus tefka tmusniwin-nni timaynutin d teșșelmad "amudus ubṛaḍu" s wayes yezmer yiwen ad yettwaɛawen s Iẓuran yugaren tayessa.

Ilaq-aɣ ad nesseɣzu igerdan n wessud-nneɣ iwakken ad neṭṭef tmusniwin d iẓuran ay d-yettasen seg igerdan n Uffella.

Deg lexḍamt ɣef yiman ur yelli ara wayen yettwaẓenẓren. Yal tafekka ɣas teẓẓi deg azal, teḥwaj ad tețțwaẓra. Yal iḥulfan ulac, tamennaɛt, atg., Ilaq ad tețțwaẓra.
