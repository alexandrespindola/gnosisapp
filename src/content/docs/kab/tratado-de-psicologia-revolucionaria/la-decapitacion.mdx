Akken i nettwali iman-nneɣ, nettafeḍ deg wul-nneɣ s wacḥal ilaq ad nessusef seg iman-nneɣ ayen akk i ɣ-yeǧǧan d ayen yettufeggden.

Tagnit timeqqranin yettwakksen seg tudert, tagnit iweɛren, ayen iɣelben, d ayen yelhan i usekles-iman n ṣṣeḥ.

Deg lawan-nni ur nettḥussu ara, deg wussan iweɛren, ttlal-d ṛṛuḥ-nneɣ iɣelben s tuffra, ɣas akken nettɛassel, yettban-aɣ-d iman-nneɣ.

Tallit yesḥan deg tudert, d tallit ur nesɛi ara amḍiq i uxeddim ɣef iman-nneɣ.

Llan kra n lawan deg tudert iweɛren i deg ilaq ad neţɛerreḍ iman-nneɣ ɣef wayen i d-yeḍran ad nettu iman-nneɣ, deg lawan-nni nettettix nniɣ; ma nɛassel, ma deg lawan-nni, ma nebda ur nerri ara iman-nneɣ, ad naf kra n ṛṛuḥ-nneɣ ur nettḥussu ara neɣ llan.

Tamusni i uxeddim ɣef iman-nneɣ, tettwaɛetteb deg yal bnadem; ma nexdem s tidet, ad nettwali iman-nneɣ deg yal lawan; tamusni-nni ad tennerni cwiṭ cwiṭ.

Akken i tennerni tamusni i uxeddim ɣef iman-nneɣ s uxeddim yettewasnen, ad nebda nettḥussu s yiman-nneɣ, deg ayen i nettɛici, seg wayen ur nesɛi ara fell-as awal.

Zdat tamusni i uxeddim ɣef iman-nneɣ, yal ṛṛuḥ i yellan deg wul-nneɣ, ad yeṭṭef ṣṣura-nni s tuffra yellan d ayen yettusɛan seg ccwami yellan yiman-is. War ccekk ṣṣura n yal ṛṛuḥ seg waya tesɛa ṭṭɛam n tussna, yettwassen s wayes ara neḥrez, nṭṭef, nesṭṭef seg laṣel-is n ṣṣeḥ, d ccwami i t-yeǧǧan.

Deg tazwara, win yebɣan tussna ur yessen ara s wayes ara yebdu, zdat lazem ad yexdem ɣef yiman-is ma yella yettwaḥeqqer.

Ma nerbaḥ lawan iweɛren, tagnit ur yesɛi ara lxir, lawan ur yelhi, ma nɛassel ad naf ccwami-nneɣ i yettbennun iman-is, ṛṛuḥ-nneɣ ilaq ad yettusefṣi s lemɣawla.

Yal tikkelt nezmer ad nebdu s lɣeḍab neɣ s tayri, neɣ s lawan ur yelhi n zzhu, atg.

Ilaq ad nqabel ayen akk i d-yettwakkasen deg yiman-nneɣ deg wussan, ma nebɣa aɛeddi n ṣṣeḥ.

Uqbel ad neṭṭes, ilaq ad nwali ayen i d-yeḍran deg wass, tagnit yellan d tiɛewwiqt, taḍsa n Aristophanes d tamecṭuḥt n Socrates.

Yal tikkelt i nettjerreḥ yiwen s taḍsa, neɣ i nesḥussi yiwen s tamecṭuḥt neɣ s allen ur yelhin.

Ad nettuftal belli deg tussna n ṣṣeḥ, ayen yellan deg umḍiq-is yelha, ayen ur yellan ara deg umḍiq-is ur yelhi.

Aman deg umḍiq-is yelha, ma deg uxxam ur yelhi ara, ad yerr uxxam-nni d ayen ur yelhi.

Times deg tbeɛda d umḍiq-is, tella d ayen i nesseqdac d ayen yelhan, ma deg uxxam tesseṛɣay tiɣawsiwin ur tella ara d ayen yelhan.

Tawenza ay tella, ma tella deg umḍiq-is tella d ayen yelhan, ma ur tella ara deg umḍiq-is, tella d ayen ur yelhi. S tawenza, nezmer ad nḍuṛṛ yiwen. Ilaq ad nesseḍru tawenza deg umḍiq-is.

D acu ara tiniḍ ɣef yiwen uqacuc i yettberriḥ awal n Rebbi deg yiwen uxxam n zzhu? D acu ara tiniḍ ɣef yiwen urgaz iṣebren d yettḥammlen i yellan yettberrik i yiwen wedrim n igrawliwen i yettɛerrḍen ad gren tiḥṛuct i tmeṭṭut-is d yessi-s? D acu ara tiniḍ ɣef tḥemmilemt-agi i yellan tewweḍ ɣer taggara? D acu ara txemmimeḍ ɣef tḥemmilemt n yiwen urgaz, deg umḍiq ad yawi učči ɣer uxxam-is, ad ibḍu idrimen ɣef wid ițmennen ccwami? D acu ara tiniḍ ɣef urgaz iqeddcen deg lawan-is i yefkan axenjar i yiwen umeqtaɛ?

Ad-aɣ-d-tesmektay ay amɣer belli ger iḍrisen yettwakksen daɣen d ccwami. Llan aṭas n tawenza ger wid yellan d yemcumen, d aṭas n ccer ger wid yellan d iweznen.

ɣas ma yella yettban-aɣ-d d ayen ur neţţamen ara deg ṣṣifa n tẓallit, daɣen yettwaffer ccwami.

Ccwami yettɣummu iman-is s tuṣṣda, yesseqdac tawenza iqerḥen, yettban-aɣ-d am win yettwanɣan d yella yettqeddec deg leǧwameɛ.

Akken i tennerni tmusni i uxeddim ɣef iman-nneɣ s uxeddim yettwakksen, ad nebda nettmuqul ṛṛuḥ-nneɣ d ccwami i yeǧǧan temẓi-nneɣ d ṣṣifa-nneɣ, ama yella d idammen neɣ d imeslub, d imeɣlaɣ neɣ d uɣsan.

ɣas ma ur tumin ara, ay amɣer, deffir temẓi i nesɛa yettwaffer ger iɣzer n yiman-nneɣ ccwami iqerḥen.

Ad nwali ccwami, ad nwali ayen yettwaxleqen seg ǧahennama deg wayes tella tmusni-nneɣ, yettban-aɣ-d s tennerni i yellan deg tmusni i uxeddim ɣef iman-nneɣ.

Akken bnadem ur yessefsaye ara ccwami-nni i d-yellan seg ǧahennama, ccwami ur nlaq ara, war ccekk deg iɣzer, ad yeqqim d ayen ur iẓer ara ara, d ccwami, d ayen yettufeggden.

Ayen iweɛren deg waya, d win yettufeggden ur yessen ara belli yettufegged, yettḥussu iman-is d ṣṣifa, d aḥeqqani, d bnadem yelhan, d yella yettcettaqi ɣef wigi-nniḍen ur t-fhimen ara, yettḥezzin ɣef wid ur yettqadaren ara, yeqqar ur t-fhimen ara, yettru yeqqar yella d ayen iṣaben, rran-as ttar s ccwami, atg.

Tamusni i uxeddim ɣef iman-nneɣ teqqar-aɣ-d s yiman-nneɣ d ṣṣifa s uxeddim yettwaɛeqqlen, deg lawan-is i nessuffuɣ acu-nniḍen ṛṛuḥ (acu-nniḍen ccwami), yettwaḥeqqer deg tagnit iweɛren d lawan ur nesɛi fell-as awal.

Txammemdeḍ ma tella deg tudert acu i k-iɛeǧben aṭas neɣ i k-yuggen? Txammemdeḍ ɣef tɣawsiwin i tettaǧan bnadem ad ixdem? Acuɣer tebɣiḍ uxxam yelhan? Acuɣer tebɣiḍ takerrust n lejdid? Acuɣer tebɣiḍ ad teqqimeḍ deg ṣṣifa-nni? Acuɣer tettmenniḍ ur tiliḍ ara d win yettmennin? D acu i k-yerẓan deg lawan-is? D acu i k-yeǧǧan tḥussuḍ s tferḥa iḍelli? Acuɣer tḥussuḍ iman-ik sennig flan neɣ flana deg lawan-nni? ɣer ssaɛa i tḥussuḍ iman-ik sennig yiwen? Acuɣer tenɣaseḍ mi telliḍ tettmeslayeḍ ɣef wayen teraḥeḍ? Ur tezmir ara ad tsettmeḍ mi qqaren yir awal ɣef yiwen? Teṭṭfeḍ abuqal n tismin ɣef leḥmala? Teqbleḍ ad tecceḍ ɣas ma ur tesɛiḍ ara ccwami, ɣas ma yella tḥussuḍ iman-ik d urgaz? Tella tettamen belli tella tettban-d tidet deg awal-nni? D mi telliḍ tessaɛdiḍ iman-ik, d mi telliḍ tettmeslayeḍ ɣef wayen teraḥeḍ teqqareḍ-as-nni ɣer wigi-nniḍen, tefhemeḍ belli telliḍ d win yettmennin?

Tamusni i uxeddim ɣef iman-nneɣ, ɣas ma teǧǧa-k ad twaliḍ ṛṛuḥ i telliḍ tessefsayeḍ, ad teǧǧa-k ad twaliḍ daɣen ayen i tettraǧuḍ d uxeddim-ik deg wul.

Deg tazwara ccwami i d-yellan seg ǧahennama, ccwami deg yiman-ik, ur yelhi d yenger sennig leḥyuwan meqqren i yellan deg yill d tẓegwa; akken telliḍ tettḍelliɛeḍ deg uxeddim-ik, tezmert ad tban-d s tmusni i uxeddim ɣef iman-nneɣ belli ccwami-nni tella tettwakkes, tettmeẓẓi.

Yelha ma nessen belli leḥyuwan-nni ttmeẓẓiyen, tettwakksen, tettmeẓẓi, teqqimen, tettbeddil iman-is, tettuyal d tmeẓẓugt; deg taggara ad ttwareqmeḍ, tuɣal d aɣebbar n lkawn, tettuɣal-d tmusni i tettwaɛetteben, tettuɣal d tilelli.

War ccekk aqel ur yezmir ara ad ibeddel ccwami deg wul; ttɛeqqel yezmer ad yefk isem i ccwami, ad yessebded-itt, ad yessuffuɣ-itt seg uswir ɣer wa, atg., ma ur yezmir ara ad yessefsay-itt, ad yeqḍeɛ-itt.

Nettḥwaj s lemɣawla lqedra meqqren sennig aqel, ta yesɛan lqedra ad teqḍeɛ ccwami ɣer aɣebbar n lkawn.

Yella deg yiman-nneɣ lqedra-nni n uzrem, times yelhan i semman leqbayel iqeddsen isem n ṣṣura: Stella Maris, Tameṭṭut n Yill, laṣel n tussna n Hermes, Tonantzin n Mexique Azteca, ccwami n yiman-nneɣ i tella yettmettili s uzrem iqeddsen n Leḥwaǧ Meqqren.

Ma deffir mi nwali d nefhem s ṣṣeḥ ccwami (acu-nniḍen ṛṛuḥ), nettḥellil i Tmeṭṭut-nneɣ n Lkawn, deg yiman-nneɣ, ad tessusef, ad teqḍeɛ ɣer aɣebbar n lkawn, ccwami-agi, ṛṛuḥ-agi, ayen i nettwali deg uxeddim-nneɣ deg wul, tezmert ad tettamen belli ad tettwakkes.

Akkagi ad nexdem uxeddim iqerḥen, yettwakksen, acku ur yella ara ṛṛuḥ, yezmer ad yettwaqḍeɛ s lemɣawla. Tamusni i uxeddim ɣef iman-nneɣ, tezmert ad twali taɣellist i uxeddim yellan yettwaɛeqqlen ɣef wayen yellan yettufeggden.

Stella Maris ɣas ma tettban-aɣ-d d ayen ur neţţamen ara, d ṣṣifa i yettmettilen tazmert n sex bnadem.

Stella Maris tesɛa lqedra iqerḥen ad tessusef ccwami i yellan deg wul.

Aqeṭṭuɛ n Yuḥenna Buṭabṭes d ayen i ɣ-yeǧǧan ad nexmem, ur yezmir ara ad yeqqed ccwami, ma ur neɛedda ara ɣer uqeṭṭuɛ.

Yiman-nneɣ i d-yellan, Tonantzin, Stella Maris am tazmert yellan teṭṭef yella deg iɣzer n yiman-nneɣ, teḥrez lqedra i t-yeǧǧan ad teqḍeɛ yal ṛṛuḥ uqbel ad yeɛber.

Stella Maris d times i yettwafken deg yal tɣawsa tegreɣ d tɣawsiwin.

Lmeṛyuḍ n wul yezmer ad yessebded taɣellist i times-nni, imiren uqeṭṭuɛ ad yezmir.

Kra n ṛṛuḥ i yettwaqḍaɛen deg tazwara n uxeddim, wiyaḍ deg tlemmast, d wiyaḍ deg taggara. Stella Maris am tazmert n sex tesɛa tamusni i uxeddim ara yexdem, yexdem uqeṭṭuɛ deg lawan-is.

Akken ur yettwaqḍaɛ ara ccwami-nni i yellan deg wul, akk ccwami, akk aɣbel, akk ccetmat, akk akreḍ, akk tissirt, akk tmenna n idrimen neɣ tazmert, atg., ɣas ma nḥussu iman-nneɣ d imdanen yesɛan ccan, yettqeddcen s awal-nsen, d tidet, yesɛan leḥmala, yelhan deg wul, atg., ur nettaɛbir ara ad nili d iẓekwan i yettwaṣebɣen, yelhan seg barra, ma seg wadda ččuren s ccwami.

Taɣerḍa aqbaylit, tussna, tamusni ɣef tira iqeddsen, ama seg usammer neɣ seg umalu, seg ugafa neɣ seg unẓul, aseɣẓen, lukan nettamen iman-nneɣ d ayen yettwakelsen, asɣen gar-aneɣ, atg., ur telhi akka, acku tella deg iɣzer ayen i nettuɣuṛ, ccwami i d-yellan seg ǧahennama, ccetmat, ccwami yettwaffen deffir ṣṣura yelhan, deffir udem yellan yettwaḥesben, s wadda llebsa yellan yeqqes fell-as ucennu.

Ilaq ad nili d tidet s iman-nneɣ, ad nastenyaq acu nebɣa, ma nusa ɣer tussna n Gnóstica ɣef tɛejjubt, ma tidet ur nɛeddi ara ɣer uqeṭṭuɛ ayen i nettraǧu, imiren netɣuṛ iman-nneɣ, nettdafaɛ ɣef ccwami-nneɣ, netqeddec s tmesxuṭṭi.

Deg uɣerbzen i yettwaḥesben, deg tussna d lukan yella aṭas n wid yellan tɛerqen s tidet, bɣan ad afen iman-nsen ma ur qeddcen ara ɣef ccwami yellan deg wul-nsen.

Aṭas n lɣaci yeqqaren s niyya yezmer bnadem ad yerr iman-is d ucennu. War ccekk, akken ur nexdem ara ɣef ṛṛuḥ yellan deg wul-nneɣ, ad yeqqim yella s wadda allen yellan yesɛan leḥmala d ṣṣifa yelhan.

Tewweḍ-d ssaɛa ad nessen belli nelli d imcumen i yettwaɣummen s llebsa n ucennu; ulli s aglim n uɣilas; imesɣasen i yelsan llebsa n yimusnawen; imeddukwlen yettwaffen deffir tikta iqeddsen n umnar, atg.

ɣas ma nbaneḍ yelhan deg leǧwameɛ, neɣ deg tɣermaz n tafat, ɣas ma nban-d iṣṣeber d yelhan i wigi-nniḍen, ɣas ma nella d iqebburen d iḍeffren deg iɣzer n yiman-nneɣ qqimen akk ccwami n ǧahennama d ccwami yellan deg imenɣi.

Deg tussna n tugwmi nettwali lazem n ubeddel ɣer laṣel d waya yezmer ad yeqqed ma neqqar yiman-nneɣ imenɣi ɣer lmut.

S tidet, ur nesɛi ara azal, nelli yal wa d tḥeqqirt n tmurt, ayen iqerḥen.

ṣṣifa Yuḥenna Buṭabṭes i ɣ-yesɣer tadawt n ṣṣeḥ: AD NEMMET DEK WUL-NNEɣ S UQEṬṬUɛ n TUGWMI.
