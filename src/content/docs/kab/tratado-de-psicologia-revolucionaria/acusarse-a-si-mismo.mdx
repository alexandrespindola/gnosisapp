Tazɣent-nneɣ yellan deg yal yiwen seg-neɣ tettas-d seg yigenni, seg itran... Bla ccek, tazɣent-nneɣ iḍehṛen tettas-d seg ucebbub "LA" (Abrid-n-weɣẓaẓ, Tagelda anda nedder).

Tazɣent-nneɣ yesɛan azal tɛeddi-d seg ucebbub "SOL" (Tafukt) dɣa seg ucebbub "FA" (Tigezdu dgi teddren tmerriwin) tkecem ɣer ddunit-a dɣa tekkcem deg tɣemmalt-nneɣ. Imaziɣen-nneɣ xelqen-d tafekka yesɛan asseɣti bac ad tesseqbel tazɣent-a i d-yettasen seg yitran...

Ma nexdem s tezmert ɣef yiman-nneɣ dɣa nessekfel iman-nneɣ ɣef wiyad, ad nuɣal s taseɣlewt ɣer wul n Urania... Nedder deg ddunit-a ɣef kra n ssebba, ɣef kra, ɣef kra n uɣbalu yeffren...

D tidet, yella deg-neɣ aṭas i ilaqen ad nẓer, ad neɣreɣ, ad nefhem, ma nebɣa s tidet ad nessen kra ɣef yiman-nneɣ, ɣef tudert-nneɣ... Tettḍeggiṛ tudert n win yemmuten mbla ma yessen ssebba n tudert-is...

Yal yiwen seg-neɣ ilaq ad yaf s iman-is anamek n tudert-is, ayen i t-iṭṭfen d amekḥul deg lḥebs n lemriḍ... Tettban-d, yella deg yal yiwen seg-neɣ ayen i yesmeɛṭiyen tudert-nneɣ dɣa ilaq a nnaɣ ɣur-s s tezmert... Ur ilaq ara a neqqim deg lḥif, ilaq a nesɣer ahat ayen i ɣ-yeǧǧan d iderɣalen d wid ur neẓẓufɣen ara.

Ulac fayda ma neqqaɛ iman-nneɣ s yisemawen, ccan, diplomaten, idrimen, iɣil iderɣal yeffren, tikliwin yettwassnen, atg., atg., atg. Ur ilaq ara a nettu maḍi belli ttɛeqlin d tizemmar timeẓyanin n tɣerɣer n tidet yessenɣayen aɣ-d aɣellaɣ d imeqqranen, wid iquddmen deg lawan, wid yettarran ɣer deffir, ur nezmir ara ad nẓer ayen ijdiden...

Lmut tesɛa aṭas n inumak iḍehṛen d wid ur nḍehṛen ara. Ma neɣreq ɣer tadyant iḍehṛen n "Gran KABIR Ɛisa Lmasiḥ": "Ad zemren lmegtin ad meḍlen lmegtin-nsen". Aṭas n medden ɣas ddirin ḥeqqan meɛden i yal axeddim yezmer ɣef yiman-nsen dɣa seg-s, i kra n ubiddel n wul.

D medden yettwaṭṭfen ger ddɣmaten-nsen d liman-nsen; d medden yettwasḍalen deg usmekti n aṭas n wass iɛeddan; d imdanen yeččuren d ttɣilitin zik; d medden d aklan n ayen ara d-inin medden, d wid ur neqqar ara d ur nettili ara deg yal waḍu, am tikkal "d wid yessnen" ugin belli tella tideț acku akka i sen-d-qqaren, atg., atg., atg.

Ur bɣan ara wid-ak ad fehmen belli ddunit-a d "Agemmuḍ adawsan" seg-s ad nezmer ad nessenger tiɣmeri-nni yeffren i nettwawi deg wul... Ma fehmen wid-ak igellilen ḥala anda llan, ad tergigin seg lxuf...

Acu kan, medden-a dɣa ṭṭfen iman-nsen d axir; ttɛeqqin tikliwin-nsen, ṭṭfen iman-nsen d wid yelhan, d wid i tɛawanen, d wid yesɛan azal, d wid i tɛawanen, d wid i tɛawanen, d wid yesɛan tmusni, d wid yesɛan lexlaṣ n tewwura-nsen, atg. Tudert taywalt am tɣuri tezmer aṭas, maca ma nettwawi-tt d taggara yiman-is, d tidet ur yesɛi anamek.

Wid ṭṭfen tudert s yiman-is, amek tedder yal ass, ur fehmen ara tallit n uxeddim ɣef yiman-nsen bac ad t-tawen ɣer "Ubiddel Aǧeddan". Aɣdem, medden ddirin am tmekkanit, ur ssɛan ara ad slen ɣef uxeddim n wul...

Ad ibeddel ilaq, maca medden ur ssinen ara amek ad beddlen; ttṛuɣun aṭas ula d ur ssinen ara ssebba mi ttṛuɣun... Ad tesɛuḍ idrimen mačči d ayen akk. Tudert n aṭas n medden i yesɛan aṭas n idrimen tella d tidet tettḍeggiṛ...
