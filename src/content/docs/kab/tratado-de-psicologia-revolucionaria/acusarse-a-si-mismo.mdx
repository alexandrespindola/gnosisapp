Tameẓri i yal yiwen seg-neɣ yella deg ul-is, yekka-d seg uɣerfan, seg igenni, seg itran...

Bla ccekk, tameẓri ayiḥercan tekkad-d seg ucewwiq "LA" (Abrid n Yimɣi, Tagelda anda nettidir)

Tameẓri ayiḥercan tedda s ucewwiq "SOL" (Iṭij), dɣa s ucewwiq "FA" (Tamnaṭ n Itran) tkcem ɣer ddunit-a tekcem deg ul-nneɣ.

Yimawlan-nneɣ xelqen-d tafekka iwulmen i wannar n Tmeẓri-agi i d-yekkan seg Yitran...

Ma nexdem s lǧehd ɣef yiman-nneɣ, ma nesseɛfu iman-nneɣ ɣef xater n watmaten-nneɣ, a n-uɣal d imnasuren ɣer uɣerfan n Urania...

Nettidir deg ddunit-a ɣef kra n ssebba, ɣef kra n leḥwayeǧ, ɣef kra n tɣawsa yesɛan azal...

D tidet, yella deg-neɣ aṭas i ilaqen ad neẓer, ad naɣer, ad nefhem, ma yella neɣ tmennaɣ s tidet ad nẓer kra ɣef yiman-nneɣ, ɣef tudert-nneɣ...

D tagmatt tameqqrant tudert n win yemmuten ur yessin ssebba n tudert-is...

Yal yiwen seg-neɣ yessefk ad yaf s yiman-is anamek n tudert-is, ayen i t-yeṭṭfen d amekraz deg lḥebs n leɛtab...

Yettban-d, yella deg yal yiwen seg-neɣ kra yessɣarayen tudert-nneɣ i ɣer ilaq ad naɣ ulaɣ d-s lǧehd...

Ur ilaq ara ad nkemmel deg tezɣent, ilaq ad nerr d aɣebbar asertan ayen i ɣ-yeǧǧan d iderɣalen d wid yettmennin...

Ulac ayen yessaɛan azal ma nuzzel ɣef yismawen, leḥram, diplumat, idrimen, tamusni taneslit, tizzawin yettwassnen, atg., atg., atg.

Ur yessefk ara ad nettu maḍi, tizeḍt d lɣurur ibuhlal n tɣermiwt ikeddaben, snernayen-d fell-aɣ agrawli, lɣaci ikerfen, iqdimen, iderɣalen, ur zmiren ad ẓren ayen yellan d amaynut...

Lmut tesɛa aṭas n wanamken, ama d aẓidanen, ama d imezɣanen.

Ad neẓer tameẓri ayiḥercanen n "KABIR Ameqqran Ɛisa Lmasiḥ". "Ad urssen lmeggtin lmeggtin-nsen". Aṭas n lɣaci ɣas ma ttidiren, mmuten i lxedma ɣef yiman-nsen, i leɛtab n ul...

D lɣaci yesɛan iman deg iɣalen d iɣallen-nsen; lɣaci yeqquren deg usmekti n azal; igerdan yeččuren d uɣmir n zik; lɣaci d aklan n wayen ara d-nnan, d imdanen iɛeqlen, ur yesɛi ara fell-asen, tikwal d "imeɣnasen" umnen yella d tidet acku akka i sen-t-id-nnan, atg., atg., atg.

Ur bɣin ara ad fehmen lɣaci-agi, ddunit-a d "Asinar Asiglufan" s wayes yezmer ad yennerɣ ayen yellan d diri deg ul...

Lemmer fehmen lɣaci-agi amek llan, ad tekkenḍiren...

Lameɛna, lɣaci-agi ḥesben dima iman-nsen d yelhan; ttezuxun s tizzawin-nsen, ttḥussun iman-nsen d iderɣalen, d wid yelhan, d wid yettɛawanen, d wid iɛuzzanen, d wid yettseddiqen, d wid ifehmen, d wid yettkemmilin i tuɣmas-nsen, atg.

Tudert tmeɣrut d taɣult yellan d tameqqrant, lameɛna ad tt-nḍefren d taggara, d tavgast n tidet.

Wid yettḍefren tudert s yiman-nsen, am wakken tettidir yal ass, ur fehmen ara ilaq ad xedmen ɣef yiman-nsen iwakken ad ɛeddin "Abeddel Amaynut".

Aɣuru, lɣaci ttidiren d imikanikanen, ur sliɛen ara yella-d lxedma deg ul...

Abeddel yella d ayen ilaqen, lameɛna lɣaci ur ssinen ara amek ara beddlen; ttwɛezzaben aṭas ur ssinen ara ssebba...

Ad yesɛu idrimen ur yelli ara d ayen akk. Tudert n aṭas n lɣaci yesɛan aṭas n idrimen tella d tagmatt tameqqrant...
