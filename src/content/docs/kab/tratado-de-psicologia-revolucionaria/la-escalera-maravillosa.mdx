Ila ad nebɣu beddil n tideț, ad nffeɣ seg tudert-agi yesɛuẓẓuyn, seg tudert-agi i d amkanizimu kan, yewɛeṛ... Ayen i ilaq ad nefhem s ṣṣeḥ d akken yal yiwen seg-neɣ, ama d burgwazi neɣ d proletari, yeɛmer neɣ d am الوسط, d ameṛkanti neɣ d ameɣbun, yella s tidet deg waṭas n المستويات del Ser...

 المستويات del Ser n umekṛani yemxalaf ɣef win n win ur yeswa ara, win n tmeṭṭut i tetzneɣ yemxalaf aṭas ɣef win n teqcict. Ayen i nessewḥi yella d irrefutables, irrébatibles... Mi nessawḍ ɣer tama-agi n wammud-nneɣ, ur nxessa ula d acemma ma nexmem ɣef tseddaṛt i tɛali seg wadda ɣer yefsus, d tin ibanen yerna tesɛa aṭas n tseddarin...

Yella-d deg tseddaṛt deg wayen nnexdem; tseddarin seddawah ad ilin medden yugaren neɣ, tseddarin sdat ad ilin medden yif-aɣ... Deg Vertical extraordinary-agi, deg tseddaṛt ifazen-agi, yella-d akken ad naf deg المستويات del Ser... yal yiwen yemxalaf yerna ulac win izemren ad yeddweṛ fell-as...

Ur nheddeṛ ɣef udem yecbaɛen neɣ ur yecbiɛ ara, neɣ ayen yeɛnan leɛmer. Llan medden d ilemẓiyen d imɣaren, imɣaren i mazal ur zemren ad mmten yerna llan iggurdan i d ilulen... Ayen yeɛnan lweqt d iseggassen; ayagi n tlalit, ad yemɣur, ad yesnerni, ad izewweǧ, ad yessili, ad yeɣbel yerna ad yemmet, d aseqqef ɣef Horizontal...

Deg "Escalera Maravillosa", deg Vertical tamɛna n lweqt ur t-tessedhuɣuɣ. Deg tseddarin n tseddaṛt-agi, nezmer ad naf المستويات del Ser... asirem amkanizimu n medden ur yesɛi ara azal; ttamnen akken s lweqt ayen ara yilin d yifazen; akka xemmimen jeddud-nneɣ d jeddud-nneɣ-nni; tiktiwin t-tessken-d ayen yemxalafen...

" المستويات del Ser" d ayen yeḥsaben yerna ayagi d Vertical; nexdem deg tseddaṛt maca nezmer ad nɛali ɣer tseddaṛt nniḍen... "Escalera Maravillosa" i nessewḥi yerna yeɛnan " المستويات del Ser" yemxalafen, s tidet, ulac ayen yesɛan assaɣ ɣer lweqt lineal... " المستويات del Ser" yefazen yella sdat-neɣ deg kra n lweqt...

Ur yelli deg ussutrem Horizontal i yeɛyan, maca da d tura; deg ulawen-nneɣ; deg Vertical... Yebanen yerna yal yiwen izmer ad ifhem, akken snat n telmatin - Horizontal yerna Vertical - ttaggrant deg kra n lweqt deg ul-nneɣ Psicológico yerna tesɛent Cruz...

 الشخصية ad teẓẓuɣer yerna ad tesserwet deg telmat n tudert Horizontal. Ad d-tlal yerna ad temmet deg lweqt lineal; d tin yemɣiren; ulac uzekka i الشخصية n win yemmuten; mačči d Ser... المستويات del Ser; Ser s timmad-is, mačči n lweqt, ulac ayen yesɛan assaɣ ɣer Telmat Horizontal; tella deg ulawen-nneɣ. Tura, deg Vertical...

Yella-d d amesɣar ad nqelleb ɣef Ser-nneɣ s timmad-neɣ s berra... Ur nxessa ula d acemma ad nesbedd am corolario ayagi: Amezruy, adeg, ad snernin, atg., deg amaḍal físico exterior, s wudem-agi ad d-yili exaltación auténtica, reevaluación del Ser, ad iɛeddi ɣer tseddaṛt yefazen deg " المستويات del Ser"...
