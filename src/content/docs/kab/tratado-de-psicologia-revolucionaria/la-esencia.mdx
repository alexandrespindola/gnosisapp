Ayen i yeǧǧan yal aqcic ad yeqbeḥ u ad iɛǧeb, d tisselwit-ines; ayagi yella d tidet-ines...

Tegrawt tanormalt n tisselwit deg yal lxelq, d ayen icuḥen, i d-yezzin...

Teffɣa temɣer tfekka n wemdan ɣef leqwanen tibyulujiyin n telmest, maca ayagi yettwaḥsab d ayen i d-yesɛan tilisa i tisselwit...

Tesselwit tzemmer ad temɣer weḥd-s, war ɛiwen, ɣer kra n tɣawsiwin timecṭuḥin...

A d-nini tidet, mbla tazɣent, tegrawt tumegrawt, taẓarurt n tisselwit, tezmmer kan deg kra n tlata, rebɛa, xemsa iseggasen imezwura n lɛmer, yeɛni, deg tmerḥelt tamezwarut n tudert...

Lɣaci ḥesben tegrawt u tezrawt n tisselwit tettili daymen s wudem yettemsalan, ɣef leḥsab n tmikkanikt n ubeddel, maca ɣef leḥsab n lgnosisme amadlan yesselmed-d belli ayagi ur d-yettili ara akka...

Axaṭer ad temɣer tisselwit akter, ilaq ad yili kra n wayen yesɛan azal, ilaq ad yili kra n wayen yebɣan ad yettwaxdem.

Bɣiɣ ad d-hedreɣ ɣef lxedma ɣef yiman-ik. Tezrawt n tisselwit tezmmer kan ɣef leḥsab n lxedmat i d-yesɛan leɛqel, d leɛtab n reẓyan...

Ilaq ad nefhem belli lxedmat-agi ur ḥedren ara ɣef umeskar, lebnuk, tnejjaṛt, lebni, ad asen-serrḥen i leɣḍat n rrdu uɣur d-ttawin iɣeblan n uffis...

Lxedma-agi teḍfer yal yiwen yesnernan tawsit; d ayen yesɛan assaɣ psikoloji...

Yal yiwen seg-neɣ yessnen belli yella deg-neɣ ayen i wumi neqqar EGO, NEKK, YIMAN-IW, YIMAN-IW...

Aya tawaɣit, tisselwit tella tqeṭɛen, tezzenzeɣ deg ECO u ayagi d ayen ur neɛǧib ara.

Ad nessefru YO Psixologik, ad nḥez tirza-ines uɣur d-ttawin tɣawsiwin ur neɛǧib ara, d ayen yesɛan azal, ur yettɛeṭṭilen, ur yettwaxxeren... akka ay d-yettili ssebba n lxedma ɣef yiman-ik.

Ur nezmir ad nerr tisselwit tilellit, mbla ma nessefreɣ YO Psixologik...

Deg tisselwit tella ddineyya, lBUDDHA, tmusni, tirza n leɛtab n Baba-tneɣ yellan deg igenwan u yal leɣḍat i neḥwaǧ i WAUTO-REALIZACION INTIMA DEL SER.

Yiwen ur yezmir ad yeffer YO Psixologik mbla ma yeffer tirza tiliyin ur n yimdanen i nettara deg-neɣ...

Ilaq ad nerr d iɣebar aɣucaf ameqqran n tallit-agi: zzux yellan d leḥsab deg wul; temmerẓuɣt ur nettwatțaṭṭafen i yerran tudert d amrar: tixessit ur neɛǧib ara; tazzla i d-ttawin tawaɣitiwin; timsekranin; leḥram n tezni i yettaɣen akk ayen yettuɣen; atg., atg., atg.

Akken tɣawsiwin-agi merra ad rrant d aɣebbar akuzmik, tisselwit, ɣer tama n tlellet, ad temɣer u ad tezrew s tmusni...

Tidet, mi yemmut YO Psixologik, ad tfeǧǧeǧ deg-neɣ tisselwit...

Tisselwit tilellit tettak-aɣ tucbiḥt deg ulawen; seg tucbiḥt-agi d-ttasen ṣṣeḥḥa taneggarut d tayri tidett...

Tisselwit tesɛa aṭas n tɣawsiwin ɣef leqwaneɛ u adrar ur nettwafakkanen...

Mi "Nettemmut deg Yiman-nneɣ", mi nessefreɣ YO Psixologik, ad neɛǧeb s tɣawsiwin d yizarazen n tisselwit...
