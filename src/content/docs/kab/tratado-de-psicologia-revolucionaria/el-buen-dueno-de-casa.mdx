Adawi ɣef yir tawaɣit yellan deg tudert, deg tallit-agi yeṭṭaṭṭen, d ayen iweɛren aṭas, maca d ayen ilaqen, m'ulac a tt-tekkes tudert.

Amedya n lxedma i gxeddem yiwen ɣef yiman-is akken ad yaweḍ ɣer usnerni n leṛwaḥ d teṛwiḥaniyt, yezdi daymen ɣer uzellu meqqer fell-as, acku s tɣella n tudert akken i tt-nedder daymen, ur nezmir ad nesnerni acemma nniḍen anagar tewsit.

Ur nebɣi ara deg wacu yellan ad neɛreḍ ad neggez ɣer usnerni n tewsit, d ayen ibanen tḥwaj tudert, maca d ayen yellan d ugensay, ur d tideț, ur d ayen yellan degneɣ.

Ma yella aɣersiw aglam degɣelt yețțusemman d amdan ur yezzel ara iman-is, maca yemmal-d iman-is ɣer wayen akk yeḍran deg tudert tameɣzunt, yesselqeq tazmert-is deg waɣzen ur nesɛi lfayda d usnerni yiman-is d wawal ur nesɛi lmeɛna d wayen ur d-yettawi ara lfayda, ulac ayen yellan d tideț i yezmer ad yesnerni deg-s, ɣir ayen yellan ɣer ddunit n temsekti.

D tidet win yebɣan s tideț ad yesnerni deg yiman-is aseɣẓan, ilaq ad yettwaɣleq. Ayagi d ayen yellan d agensay, yezda akked ssus.

Awal-agi yusa-d seg zik, mi llan sser sselmaden tarbaɛt ɣef usnerni agensay n wemdan yeqqnen ɣer yisem n Hermes.

Ma yella nebɣa ad d-yekker wayen yellan d tideț degneɣ, d ayen ibanen ilaq ad neḥذر ɣef tezmart taɣersawt.

Mi nezga nserriḥ i tezmart-nneɣ, ur nezli ara iman-nneɣ deg wudmawen-nneɣ, ur yettusekkal ara ur nezmir ara ad naweḍ ɣer usnerni n wayen yellan d tideț degneɣ.

Tudert taɣersawt tebɣa ad aɣ-tekkes ur teɛtil, ilaq ad nnaɣ ɣer tudert yal ass, ilaq ad nelmed ad neɛreq mgal tafsut.

Lxedma-yagi tetteddu mgal tudert, d ayen yemxalafen ɣef wayen yellan yal ass, maca ilaq a t-nexdem yal taswiɛt, bɣiɣ ad d-mmeslayeɣ ɣef Trawla n Tmuɣli.

D ayen yebanen ma yella tewɛit-nneɣ ɣer tudert taɣersawt dɣelt, ma yella nermen akk ad aɣ-tedduɛ yesɛa, ad d-yasen wayen ur nɛaqaḍ ara...

Lɣaci bɣan ad ten-id-yeɣli wayen yesɛan azal "akka kan", acku kullec ilaq ad yelḥu akken i tt-heggwan, maca tidet mačči akka, ma yella ur tbeddleḍ ara seg wensay, ɣas ur iɛǧib-ik ara aț-țiliḍ daymen d amezdak, d tewwiṭ.

Ttafen ad arun ɣef tudert aṭas n yekkaten ur nesɛi lmeɛna, maca Amawal-agi n Tsekkiwsa Taɣellawt yemxalaf.

Tarbaɛt-agi tetteddu ɣer yibawen, ɣer wayen yellan, d ayen yebnan d ayen yebanen, tetteddu s teɣdemt d akken "Aɣersiw Aɣellaw" yeɣlet yețțusemman d amdan, d amdan yettelḥu s temsekti, ur yesɛi tmuɣli, yeqqen.

"Bab n Taddart Ayiḥ" ur yettqabal ara Tsekkiwsa Taɣellawt, yexdem kullec wayen i t-ilaqen am baba, am urgaz, atg., dɣa dɣa yeqqar deg yiman-is ayen yellan d ayen yiḥen, maca yettqeddem ɣer tɣawsiwin n ugama, dɣa dɣa d ayagi kan.

Ngal-as a d-nini daɣen yella "Bab n Taddart Ayiḥ" i yettnaɣen mgal tafsut, ur yebɣi ara ad t-tekkes tudert, maca imdanen-agi drusit deg ddunit, ur ttɣumin ara.

Mi netxemmim akken i d-ttawin tiktiwin n Wawal-agi n Tsekkiwsa Taɣellawt, nettawi tamuya yellan d tidet ɣef tudert.
