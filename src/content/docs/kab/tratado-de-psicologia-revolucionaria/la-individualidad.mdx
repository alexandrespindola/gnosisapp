Aɛeqli ad yamen iman-is "Yiwen", d awal n lekdeb ur yesɛi lmeɛna; ahat tazzla yella deg yal yiwen seg-neɣ.

Ahat nebɣu iman-neɣ sennig kullec, ur nettxemmim ara ad neɛqel belli ur nesɛi ara timmadit taḥeqqanit.

Ayen yellan d ameqqran, neɣtaleb daɣen i iman-nneɣ belli yal yiwen seg-neɣ yesɛa timmadit d tazzla tizeɣwanin.

Aɣrib fell-aneɣ! Acḥal d imaɛzuzen i nella! Ulac ccek belli tijahla d lmuṣiba tameqqrant.

Deg yal yiwen seg-neɣ llan aṭas n wid yemxalafen, leṣnaf yemxalafen, iman-nneɣ neɣ medden yettnaɣen gar-asen, tettnaɣen ɣef tselwit, ur sɛin ara leqrar neɣ timlilit.

Lemmer neɛqel, lemmer d-nekker seg tigi d ussan, acḥal ara yili d yemxalafen tudert.

Ahat s tukerḍa n teɣdemt-nneɣ, tussna tueɛṛan d wamek nwala iman-nneɣ d tayri-nneɣ, tebɣu-aɣ, tetɛebbu-aɣ, ur aɣ-d-tettaǧǧa ara ad-nettmekti iman-nneɣ, ad nwali amek nella..

Nettamen nesɛa yiwen wurar mi nella nesɛa aṭas n wuraren yemxalafen. (Yal iman yesɛa ayen-ines)

Tasgiwin tameqqrant n timmadit-a d tin yuɛṛan; uraren n daxel yettnaɣen, ttidiren deg nnfaq dima, ttqeddicen deg tɣemrawin yemxalafen.

Lemmer nesɛa timmadit taḥeqqanit, lemmer nesɛa yiwen n weḥdu s umḍiq n tugget, ad nesɛu daɣen tumert n lebɣi, tazzla teqqar, urar unṣib, unṣib.

Ad nebeddel d ayen-ilan, maca ilaq ad nebdu s tidet yellan yid-neɣ.

Nettḥwaj ad nexdem asuqil n laman yellan yid-neɣ ad nessin ayen yellan ɣur-neɣ d wayen iɣ-iqṣen.

Yezmer ad teffɣeḍ timmadit, maca ma nettamen nesɛa-tt ad teṛwəl tukerḍa-a.

D taggara ur nettnadi ara ad naweḍ ayen i nettamen nesɛa-t. Tigi teǧǧa-aɣ nettamen nella d imdanen n timmadit yerna llan deg umadal uɣerbazen yesselmden akka.

D ẓẓay ad nnaɣent ɣef tigi, teǧǧa-aɣ nettban am nekni d wagi, mi nella d iɛeryanen, d ileḥḥuyen d wid ur nettɣezzir ara.

Nettxemmim nekni d irgazen, mi nella d wid yellan kan d tiɣersiyin teɛqilen ur nesɛi timmadit.

Wid yettqelliben d imdanen n igenwan, d imaḥatma, atg., bla ma ttḍeqqifen belli ur sɛin ara tazzla taglawant d wurar unṣib.

Wid iḥemmlen deg wallen-nsen aṭas iman-nsen, ur ttamnen ara abrid n tugget n wallen-nsen deg daxel-nsen.

Iɛeryanen s zzux yellan yid-sen, ur qqaren ara akk taɣawsa-a...

D tazwara ad nnaɣent ɣef tmettant ɣer tigi ɣef iman-nneɣ, ma ur nebɣi ara ad nili d iɛeggalen n tussniwin tiggugmanin d tɛerqatin iɣriben dɣa daɣen yettɣelliben tukerḍa n tellaḍt n daxel.

Aɣersiw yeɛqel yettuɣ s tigi-ines, ittaɣ-d d argaz n teɣlalt mi nella kan d aẓẓaḍ i d-yettili deg uɣerbal n tmurt.

Amekkukru ur yettamnen ara awal-a yellan deg igiten sennig; iwet dɣa yiman-is d amenzu deg uɣerbaz deg wayen i t-qaren; bla ma ittaɣ belli tigi d tin yellan kan d ulluɣ, "ulluɣ siwa d tigi".

Tigi d tezmert taḥeqqanit i tqeddicen deg umadal ɣef medden akk d tǧǧa amdan yeɛqel deg tgi, teǧǧa-t yettamen yella d argaz, yesɛa timmadit taḥeqqanit, urar, tazzla teqqar, tazzla taglawant, atg., atg., atg.

Mi nettmen nekni d yiwen, ur nezmir ara ad nerrez seg wanda yella deg iman-nneɣ, nettɣimi nexzer yerna deg taggara nedḍer, nerẓa.

Yal yiwen seg-neɣ yella deg tellaḍt taglawant yettwaḥedden yerna ur nezmir ad neffeɣ deg-s, ma ur nessekti ara tigi neɣ medden yettidir deg tfekka-nneɣ.

D tazzla belli s wamek nwala iman-nneɣ ad nwali medden i ttidiren deg tmuski-nneɣ i nettḥwaj ad nesfeḍ akken ad naweḍ abeddel aquran.

Tasleḍt-a, amek nwala iman-nneɣ, tebeddel s tɣerma tulufawin yellan ɣef iman-nneɣ dɣa am wayen yella d asuffeɣ ɣef lmeɣḍer n tellaḍt belli ur nesɛi timmadit taḥeqqanit.

Mi ur nettmuqul ara iman-nneɣ, ad tidiren deg tgi belli nekni d Yiwen dɣa taggara tudert-nneɣ ad tili d tunqiqant.

Ur yezmir ara ad nebɣu yid-neɣ imdanen am nekni mi ur nessuffeɣ ara abeddel n daxel deg tmuski-nneɣ.

Abeddel unṣib yettḥwaj assufeɣ n wallen i nɣimi yid-neɣ.

Ur nezmir ara ad nesfeḍ wallen-a ma ur ten-nmuqul ara deg daxel-nneɣ.

Wid yettḥussun yiman-nsen d Yiwen, yettxemmimen ɣef iman-nsen sennig kullec, ur yettamnen ara abrid n aṭas, ur bɣin ara daɣen ad muqulen wallen dɣa tukerḍa n ubeddel teǧǧa-ten d tiɣerfanin.

Ur yezmir ara ad tebeddel ma ur tessufeɣ ara, maca min yesɛan timmadit ma yeqbel belli ilaq ad tessufeɣ, ur yesɛi ara lɣerḍ ɣef wayen ilaq ad tessufeɣ.

Empero, ur yellaḥet ad nettu belli min yettamen yella d yiwen, yettkeɛɛud yettmen yessen ayen ilaq ad tessufeɣ, maca deg tidet ur yessen ara yella ur yessin ara, yella d agajel ilemẓi.

Nettḥwaj "ad nesseɣẓaṛ iman-nneɣ" akken "ad nili d unṣiben", maca min yettamen yesɛa timmadit ur yezmir ara ad yesɛi iman-is.

Timmadit d tin yella d iqeddsen deg meṛṛa, drus i ten-yesɛan, maca kullec yettxemmim yesɛa-tt.

Amek ad nessefeḍ "Iman", ma nettamen nesɛa "Iman" Yiwen?

Tidet kan min ur yettaɣ ara iman-is seriḥ yettxemmim yesɛa Iman Yiwen.

Empero ilaq ad nebɣu tizelɣatin deg usselmed-a acku yella amezraq aglawan ad nessufeɣ timmadit taḥeqqanit yid-sent tameddurt n wamek "Yella sennig" neɣ kra am tɣerma-a.

Timmadit Taqedsant tella sennig tulufawin n "Iman", d ayen yellan, ayen i yellan dima d wayen yellan dima ad yili.

Timmadit tanṣibt d lkayen d d sebba n lkayen n lkayen, d lkayen.

Tessfeḍ gar lkayen d Iman. Wid yettxalṭen iman yid-sen, tidet ur muqulen ara iman-nsen seriḥ.

Mi teqqim tissirt, tazzla, tettwakkeṛ deg tallit-a n tulufawin n Iman i nella yid-neɣ deg daxel, abeddel aquran yella ayen yellan sennig Taɣdemt.
