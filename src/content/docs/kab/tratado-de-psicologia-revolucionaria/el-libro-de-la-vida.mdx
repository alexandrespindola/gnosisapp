Win yellan d win tmeddurt-is. Ayen iteddun seld lmut, d tmeddurt. D ayagi ay d anamek n udlis n tmeddurt yeldin s lmut.

Ma nmuqel ɣer temsalt-a s yiwet n tmuɣli tasengama kan, ass am wass deg tudert-nneɣ, d talɣa tamecṭuḥt n tudert kamel.

Seg wayagi akk, nezmer ad d-negzem ayagi: Ma yella wer yexdem ara umdan ɣef yiman-is ass-a, ur itbeddil ara abaden.

Asmi ara d-yini belli yebɣa ad yexdem ɣef yiman-is, ur yexdem ara ass-a, yettaɛxer ar uzekka, awal-a ad yeqqim d asenṭeq kan, acku deg ass-a ay tella talɣa n tudert-nneɣ kamel.

Yella yiwet n teɛṛabt deg wawal n lɣaci yeqqar: "Ur ttaǧǧa uzekka ayen i tezmert ad t-txedmeḍ ass-a".

Ma yella yenna umdan: "Ad xedmeɣ ɣef yiman-iw uzekka", ur itexdem ara abaden ɣef yiman-is, acku daymen ad yili uzekka.

Ayagi icuba aṭas i yiwen n usmekti, aɣmis neɣ tira i ssrusun kra n yimzenza deg tdukkan-nsen: "ASSA UR TTILI ARA LAKREDDI, UZEKKA IH".

Asmi ara d-yas win yeḥwaǧen a kreddi, ad yettmagar aɣmis-nni icemten, ma yuɣal-d azekka, ad yaf daɣen aɣmis-nni neɣ tira-nni.

D ayagi ay qqaren deg tesnagal "taḍunt n uzekka". Asmi ara d-yini umdan "uzekka", ur itbeddil ara abaden.

Ilaq-aɣ s tɣawsa taɛeffast, ur nettɛeṭṭil, ad nexdem ɣef yiman-nneɣ ass-a, ur nettargigi deg lɣaci deg ussan i d-iteddun neɣ deg tegnit tameqqrant.

Widak yeqqaren: "Ad ṛuḥeɣ ad xedmeɣ ayagi neɣ ayagi, umbeɛd ad xedmeɣ ɣef yiman-iw". Ur xedmen ara abaden ɣef yiman-nsen, d widak ay d imezdaɣ n tmurt yettwabedren deg tira iqeddsen.

Ssneɣ yiwen n umeqqran n wakal yeqqar: "Ilaq-iyi ad qedmeɣ iman-iw, umbeɛd ad xedmeɣ ɣef Yiman-iw".

Asmi yuḍen s lmut ẓureɣ-t, steqsaɣ-t: "Mazal tebɣiḍ ad qedmeḍ iman-ik?"

"Sḥusseɣ aṭas imi sruɣ akk lawan", i yi-d-yerra. Kra n wussan umbeɛd yemmut, seld mi iɛqel lɣella-s.

Argaz-nni yesɛa aṭas n wakalen, maca yebɣa ad yagmer tiɣermatin i d-yezzin, "ad yeqdem iman-is", akken ad yeqqim wakal-is yettwasersen s rebɛa iberdan.

"Ikufez i yal ass leɛtab-is!", i yenna KABIR JESÚS Ameqqran. Ad nessen-iman-nneɣ ass-a, ɣef wayen yeɛnan ass yettarran daymen, talɣa tamecṭuḥt n tudert-nneɣ kamel.

Asmi ara yebdu umdan ad yexdem ɣef yiman-is, ass-a mi ara yessenɣet deg leḥzen d lemḥan-is, yettezzi ɣer ubrid n tseddaɛ.

Ur yezmir ad yekkes ayen ur nessin ara. Ilaq-aɣ ad nessenɣet uqbel deg tuccḍiwin-nneɣ.

Ilaq-aɣ ur kan nessin ass-nneɣ, maca daɣen assaɣ yellan gar-aneɣ. Yella kra n wass am wass yal yiwen yettɛici-t, ala ma llan kra n wayen ur nettban ara, ur neɛdel ara.

Yesɛa azal ad nessenɣet deg tulda n yal ass, tɛawint n wawal d wayen yeḍran, i yal yiwen, atg.

Tɛawint-nni neɣ tulda n wayen yeḍran d wawal, yuklal ad yettwazdel, tettawi-aɣ ɣer tussna n yiman-nneɣ.
