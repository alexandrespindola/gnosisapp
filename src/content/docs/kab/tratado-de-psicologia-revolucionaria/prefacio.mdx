Amezruy-agi n Tmusni n Tneflit Tagrawla d Izmaziwen i d-yefka Uselmad i watmaten deg umulli n tlalit n 1975. D Amaru agrawlan i ɣ-isselmed ad nenɣ tamusni.

Ar ass-a, inelmaden qeblen ad qernen tamusni, am umɣar aserdas yettwaḍefren ɣer watmaten-is. Nekkini ula d nekk ufiɣ-d tiknikiyin i ttaḍefreɣ iqernen tamusni, maca teɣli-d tikkelt anda nebɣa ad nemmet-itt, ad neṣfu-tt, s tiknik n Uselmad Samael i ɣ-d-yefka tiknikiyin s tilqey, s talɣa tamhezdayt, d tukkimt.

Mi ara temmet tamusni, anda daɣen ara tban tṛuḥ-nneɣ s ccbaḥa-s tafessast, kullec ad ibeddel fell-aɣ, aṭas i yesseqsayen amek ara xedmen mi ara d-ffɣen aṭas n tukkiwin deg yiwet n tikkelt, neqqar-asen ad ṣfun kra, yellan ad ttaraǧun, wiyiḍ zemren ad ten-qernen iwakken ad ten-ṣfun umbaɛd.

Deg UTWEḤḤID AMAYNU; yesselmed-aɣ amek ara nbeddel asebter n tudert-nneɣ, ad neɣɣ: Aɣezfan, ṭṭmeɛ, tissas, cchawat, zzux, aɛyaw, tḥanut, aray, atg. D wajeb ad nḥekkef aqerruy aṛumman, ad nerr taknut tazellulant iwakken ad d-teṭṭef Tamusni Tadawit n Uqerruy Aldaw. Deg utweḥḥid-agi yesselmed-aɣ amek ara nweɛɛer, astal amdan n Wemdan, ad nbeddel astal-agi. Yezmer ad yili mi ara nesruḥ tukkiwin-nneɣ.

Kul beddel deg wegdud yesdukul-d beddel deg berra. Astal n Wemdan i d-ibedder Uselmad deg wungal-agi yettwabedder i lɛadda anda nettaf iman-nneɣ.

Deg UṬṬU AMAYNU; yessuqil-d belli astal n Wemdan d usenfar anda nufa iman-nneɣ nerraren deg tseɣẓent n Tudert, mi ara nali deg tseɣẓent-agi netteddu qeddem, maca mi ara neqqim am akken nella yetsɛeb, yeɣzif, yeqɛey, yeqqar.

Deg UṬṬU UWIS; yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef tḍerrast Tadelsant, yesselmed-aɣ belli amḍiq Adelsan n tbeddu yella deg yiman-nneɣ, yeqqar-aɣ belli abrid agrawlan neɣ amagnu d aẓar n Irrebel, n wid yețnadinen ad beddelen deg wussan, armi ayen xedmen ɣef yiman-nsen d tɣara tamsisit n abrid agrawlan; Imedlanen ttemsayalen deg ubrid ahraw deg tseɣẓent n tudert.

Deg UṬṬU UREKBAN; yeḥdder amek ara yili beddel, ccbaḥa n uqcic teḥkem belli ur yesnerni ara tukkiwin-is, nettwali mi ara ttnernint deg uqcic, itefcel deg ccbaḥa-s Tameggarut. Mi ara nṣfu tukkiwin, Tṛuḥ ad teban deg tulya-s, wagi ad ttwalin medden s wallen kan, ula d ccbaḥa n Tṛuḥ d tin yessimẓayan tafekka turman.

Deg UṬṬU UḤAMMIS; Yesselmed-aɣ amek ara nesseqdec leɛwayed-agi Adelsan, yesselmed-aɣ amek ara nesruḥ tilqey tufennet i neddukel deg, (tukkiwin); yesselmed-aɣ ad nexdem ɣef yiman-nneɣ, iwakken ad naweḍ ad nbeddel Nṣaḥ.

Ad nbeddel d wajeb, maca medden ur ẓerrin amek ara beddelen, ttɛebbaɛn aṭas, ttqanɛen ayen id-yeffɣen, ɣer wiyaḍ, ur ẓerrin belli d nutni kan i yesɛan tumast ɣef leɛwayed n Tudert-nsen.

Deg UṬṬU UḤESSIS; yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef tudert, yeqqar-aɣ belli tudert tban-d d aɣbel ur yesɛi ara ttfahem: Awanen d Igduden yerna tennuyin d Igduden.

Deg UṬṬU UWIS S SEBƐA; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef wawanen Igduden, yesselmed-aɣ ifirri ara yilin gar wawanen n ttɛeqliyya, yerna tennuyin tiɣerɣrant n tudert turmant.

Mi ara nbeddel awanen imesluben n ttɛeqliyya, wagi ad d-yewwi beddel agrawlan deg-neɣ.

Yettmeslay-aɣ deg UTTEZƐA AMAYNU ƔEF WAYEN YEVṚUN I YIMGIREN; yesselmed-aɣ ad nezdem awanen Adelsanen iselbaɛen, yerna wawanen Igduden imesluben, yesselmed-aɣ ad nerr ṛṛay deg uxxam-nneɣ Adelsan yermes. Tudert tagdudant tesdukul-d tuddmin tigdudanin, ma yella waɛrren a ten-id-yawi wawanen imesluben ur nezdiɛ. Ayen yellan deg berra d afraw n wayen yellan deg wegdud, beddel deg wegdud ad d-yawi aɣerras amaynu.

Awanen Igduden iselbaɛen ad aɣ-errin d iɛeggalen ur nezmir ad nḥudd iman-nneɣ ɣef timserɣuyt tamdan, yesselmed-aɣ ad ur nttwaɛnazen s ulac ayen yevṛun, iwakken ad nemmekti belli kullec yettaɛddi, yessefk ad nelmed ad nwali tudert am filim, deg drama yessefk ad nili d wid yenṭeqen, ur nttwaɛnazen s drama.

Yiwen seg warraw-iw yesɛa Ttijat anda ttwafrawn ifilimen imezdaɣen, tessiliɣ mi ara xedmen Inamuren i ten-yexḍen Oscaren; deg wass am wass, mmi-s εalvaro yeqqar-iyi-d ɣer filim anda xedmen Inamuren s Oscaren, ɣer taɛreqa rriɣ-as belli ur zemreɣ ara ad ṛuḥuɣ iwakken ɛeqleɣ drama amdan yifen wayen n filim-is, anda kul Inamuren gan Oscaren; yesseqsa-yi: Acu-t drama-yagi?, rriɣ-as, drama n Tudert; Yekemmel, maca deg drama-yagi kulna nettareḍ, nniɣ-as: Nekk areḍeɣ am amaẓẓag n Drama. Acuɣer? Rriɣ-as: Imi ur ttwaseɣdeɣ ara s drama, xedmeɣ ayen yessefk ad xedmeɣ, ur ttɛassaɣ wala ttezɣiɣ s wayen yettaɣen drama.

Deg UṬṬU UƐECṚA; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef yeɣḍisen imgirren, yessuqil-d belli deg tudert tagdudant n medden ulac areḍ aḥerfi i yilin imi d tigget n yeɣḍisen, ɣef wayagi aṭas n beddlen deg tudert n yal yiwen seg wid iqeddmen drama: tissas, dḍessa, imeṭṭawen, aɣezfan, tissassuf, ayagi d tɣaratin i ɣ-d-ibedren aṭas n beddlen d tuggdiwin i ɣ-d-tewwint yeɣḍisen n yimgi n yiman-nneɣ.

Deg UṬṬU UƐECṚA YIWEN; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef Ego-nneɣ i ɣ-ɛeẓzen, yeqqar-aɣ belli yeɣḍisen d aɣla psiki i yellan dima, yeqqar-aɣ-d amek ara nttwaẓer yiman-nneɣ deg wegdud, akka ad naf aṭas n yeɣḍisen i yettɛicin deg yimgi n yiman-nneɣ.

Deg UṬṬU UƐECṚA SIN; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef Beddel Aɣerran, deg dinna yesselmed-aɣ belli ulac beddel deg psiki-nneɣ mbla ad nettwaẓer s tumert kul tigget-nni n tewtilin tilawaniyin i neddukel.

Mi ara nelmed belli ur nelli d yiwen maca aṭas deg-neɣ, nerraren deg ubrid n tmusni-iman. Tamusni d ttfahem ur ttaḍefren ara, tamenzut d tin n uqerruy tis snat d tin n wul.

AṬṬU TLATAƐECṚA; Amaẓẓag d wayen ittwaẓren, deg dinna yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef azerrar n ttwaẓer tagdudant i yiman-is, yella win yexedmen s tidet ɣef yiman-is, yeḥerres ad yebɛed tiggetin ur nebɣi i neddukel deg.

Iwakken ad nezdi tmusni-iman yessefk ad nttwabḍu ɣer amaẓẓag d win ttwaẓren, mbla abḍu-yagi, ɣas ur nezmir ara ad naweḍ ɣer tmusni-iman.

Deg UṬṬU UKEBḌAN; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef Iɣiten Imselbaɛen; nettwali belli kul yeɣḍisen yesɛan taɣessa, ttafaden seg wammas Aɣisan iwakken ad cerrgen imyazdaɣ, imalen, ttɛeqliyya, atg., ayen i d-yessenqes belli ur nesɛi ara aqerruy adɣar, nettwali deg utweḥḥid-agi belli yeɣḍisen ttafaden s tuffɣa seg wammas-nneɣ amɣaw.

Deg UṬṬU UḤAMMISƐECṚA; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef Timdanit, deg dinna ad yefhem yiwen belli ur nesɛi ara ttɛeqliyya wala aray-iman, wala timdanit, s ttwaẓer tilawt nezmer ad nwali medden i yettɛicin deg psiki-nneɣ (yeɣḍisen), yessefk ad ten-ṣfu iwakken ad naweḍ ɣer Tbeddel Tagrawlant, imi timdanit d tameqqrant, nettwali ɛadda n Usselmaden n Uɣerbaz i yettzuzunen arrac deg tudert-nsen, akka ad awḍen ɣer tselluf i imi daɣen ttwaseɣḍen s drama n tudert.

Iweḥḥiden i d-yeqqimen seg 16 ɣer 32 gan dima s tuffɣa i kul wid ibɣan ad ffɣen seg tegget, i wid ibɣan ad ilin d kra deg tudert, i igḍaḍ yellan deg yigenni, i imseɣlawen n ttɛeqliyya yerna ul yesɛan tigullelt, i win yeǧǧan tkerza n ugellil, d wi yeṭṭuɛen tɣelmi deg ufus n uɣella.

UṬṬU UḤESSISƐECṚA; yettmeslay-aɣ Uselmad ɣef umaru n tudert, d laɛqel ad nwali tɛawent n imyazdaɣen n yal ass, tikerkera n wayen n wass-nni ad aɣ-dewwin ɣer tmusni taɛlayant.

Deg UṬṬU UWIS S SEBƐAƐECṚA; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef tɣersatin tigensatin, yeqqar-aɣ mi ara ur yettwaẓer yiwen yiman-is ur yezmir ara ad yegzu tɛawent war ttɛeqbi n yal ass, win ur yebɣi ara ad iẓer yiman-is daɣen ur yebɣi ara ad yexdem iwakken ad yaweḍ ɣer Tbeddel Tagrawlant, imgi n yiman-nneɣ kan d tameqḍeḥt, d aqcic yettmeslayen, d kra n tɣessa, nella d wid yettɛawaden tennuyin, tallelin-nneɣ d tin-nni kan, ɣas ur nebɣi ara ad tent-nbeddel.

UṬṬU UWIS S TMANYAƐECṚA; yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef Uɣrom-nneɣ Aɣerfan-Ufrin, tallelin-nneɣ aɣ-qqimen iqersen, nella d medden tigensatin iṭṭuqten s tallelin iɛeqqranin, yessefk ad d-newwi beddlen deg wegdud. Twaẓer-iman d laɛqel.

UṬṬU UƐECṚA ṬṬEZƐA; yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef win yesɛan axxam, yessefk ad nezziɛ iman-nneɣ ɣef drama n tudert, yessefk ad nḥudd tirẓi n psiki, areḍ-agi yettḍegger ɣer tudert, yella kra i yeffen deg tudert n yal ass.

Mi ara ur yettbeddel yiwen wegdud-is ur yella dima d aɛeggal n tuddmin. Win yesɛan axxam d win i d-yezgan mgal aɣerras, wid ur yebɣi ara ad tent-tedder tudert gan dima.

Deg UṬṬU AƐECṚIN; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef sin waggagen, yeqqar-aɣ belli tamusni tanṣaḥt i yezmer ad d-yewwi deg-neɣ beddel agrawlan, yesɛa ɣer tikkelt twaẓer taɣerfant n yiman-is. Twaẓer tagdudant d abrid iwakken ad nbeddel s tɣerfant, s ttwaẓer-iman, nelmed ad nemsawal deg ubrid Agdud,

Aras n ttwaẓer-iman deg yiman-is yeffeɣ seg uɣerras deg tɣennawt tamdanit, maca aras-agi d-yettnerni mi ara nteḥerres deg ttwaẓer n yiman-is, amek nella nelmed ad nemsawal deg wemkan n berra, akka daɣen s areḍ Adelsan ɣef yiman-is nelmed ad nemsawal deg wemkan Agdud.

Deg UṬṬU UƐECṚA YIWEN; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef ttwaẓer n yiman-is, yeqqar-aɣ belli ttwaẓer n yiman-is d abrid aɣerfan iwakken ad naweḍ ɣer beddel agrawlan, ad nezdi ur yelli ad nettwaẓer, ur yessefk ara ad nxeleq ayen nezdi s ayen ittwaẓren.

Twaẓer n yiman-is d miyya deg miyya teqwan, d abrid n beddel n yiman-is, mi ara yezdi ayen yellan d amellun ur yelli. Assaɣ agellil d-yettas seg tama teẓran, mi ara iɣiten d ttḥassas ttafaden seg tama ittwaẓren. Ad yezdi d kra Tigensat, d amellun; deg umeddar ttwaẓer n yiman-is d tagrawla n ttɛeqliyya.

Deg UṬṬU UƐECṚA SIN; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef Awal, yeqqar-aɣ ad nbeqqen, anect-agi n "nettmeslay iman-nneɣ" yetḍuru, imi yeɣḍisen-nneɣ ttemɣalen yiwet ɣer tayet, mi ara tkecmem attwali iman-ik yerna tgafell s wayen tzerreɣen.

Deg UṬṬU UƐECṚA TLATA; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef wemkan n tuddmin, yeqqar-aɣ belli llan kra n wawanen n tuddmin, iɣelben s tafekka-nneɣ, s wemkan deg berra tuddmin n wergaz s yiman-is, ayen ur yesɛi ara azal ɣer tegget n medden, i medden kan iɛeqlen sin imenzu n tuddmin. Yessefk ad nɣer iwakken ad nezdi anwa seg kra n wawanen-agi nexṣar.

Lexṣas n ṣfu deg wegdud yexṣer ad yili deg tuddmin yiman-is, ayagi ad yaqqim deg idelli, mi ara tkecmeḍ aɣbel, aɣerras-ik yemsellek, tḥawess, mmekti "iman-ik" wagi ad yefk imqimen n tafekka-k taɣessa-nniḍen.

Deg UṬṬU UƐECṚA UKEBḌAN; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef tacennayt Tadelsant, yeqqar-aɣ ɣef tcennayin, ttḥuddiman, nettḥussu nettwaɛraben, atg., ad ttamnen belli wiyaḍ yesɛan tumast deg ayen akk aɣ-yettɣaren, deg umeddar tamɣurta tettafden am areḍ-nneɣ, akka ɣas ur nezmir ara ad nḥessi iman-nneɣ. Argaz yellan deg imyazdaɣen i d-yeffɣen yezmer ad yuɛɛer neɣ ur yuɛɛer, wagi ur yelli d afud iwakken ad nttwaẓer iman-nneɣ, ad nḥessi iman-nneɣ, ad nelmed ad nesameḥ d laɛqel ɣer ttḥessin-nneɣ deg wegdud. Tasekla n Tarẓi tewwi wid ibɣan ad arḍen s tidet ad beddelen, yal yiwen yettaɣ tacennayt-is Tadelsant.

Amekti yeqqerzen n wayen yellan yaqqimen-aɣ ɣer uzekka ur aɣ-yettaǧǧan ara ad nɛic ass-a i aɣ-yezzenzen. Iwakken ad nɛeddi ɣer astal aɛlayan yessefk ad neǧǧ ad nili ayen nella, ɣef yal yiwen seg-neɣ llan astalen iɛlayen i nelzem ad nali.

Deg UṬṬU UƐECṚA UḤAMMIS; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef Tuɣalin d Tikerkera yeqqar-aɣ belli Tasekla n Tarẓi d tbeddel, tẓerzegt, ttḥessin wer ttfahem; win ur yebɣi ara ad yessi iman-is, ad ibeddel, ad yeffeɣ seg wakud-is imi ma ur yennerni ara yeqqim deg ubrid n tuɣalin, imi ur yezmir ara ad yezdi; s wudem n tidet ɣer-s taɣerfant, nella d timeqḍaḥ i yettɛawaden tilɣi n tudert. Mi ara neḥsus ɣef tuddmin-agi ad nezdi belli nella d Inamuren i yexdemen s wacu i yella deg drama n tudert n yal ass.

Mi ara nesɛu tazmert ad neẓren ad nettwaẓer ayen i yexdem yerna yettɛamel tafekka turmant-nneɣ, nerraren iman-nneɣ deg ubrid n ttwaẓer taɛeqliyt yerna nettwali belli yiwet d ttɛeqliyya, tin yezdin, wayet d tin i yexdem yerna yettwataɛ, aqli-nneɣ tafekka-nneɣ. Taɣersi n tudert teqsed yerna teḥzen ɣer win ur yessen ara ad yesselɣ tafat deg wegdud, yettṣufu gar tarza-s gar tirugza tigranin, yeɣḍisen-nneɣ ttɛicin s tuffɣa deg tirugza.

Deg UṬṬU UƐECṚA UḤESSIS; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef Ttɛeqliyya Tmeẓyant, yeqqar belli mi ara d-ilul uqcic tettnerni Esencia, wagi ad yefk i uqcic ccbaḥa, umbaɛd akka itessnerni yimgi n yiman-is ad ttnernint daɣen yeɣḍisen i d-yettasen seg tudrin iɛeqranin, ad yefcel deg ccbaḥa timmadit.

Deg UṬṬU UƐECṚA UWIS S SEBƐA; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef Publio d Aferiziyu, yeqqar belli yal yiwen yettɛadal s wayen yesɛa, dɣa aɣbel n kul yiwen ad yesɛu kra: izwal, aɣlan, idrimen, isem, amkan amaɛayan, atg. Argaz d tmeṭṭut ṭṭuqten zzux yellan d win i yeḥwaǧen aṭas n win i yeḥwaǧen iwakken ad yidir, argaz yettɛadal kan ɣef izuyaz deg berra, ula d netta d amarud imi deg wass anda yeffeɣ seg izuyaz ad yebdel ɣer argaz iḥeznen deg wemkan.

Mi ara netḥussu nugar wiyaḍ nettqellib yeɣḍisen-nneɣ yerna neqreḥ ad naweḍ ad nili d aseɛdi. ɣer areḍ-agi agdud ttnucunatin-nneɣ d tigawt yessegzayan s kul tennuft tagdudant, mi ara nettwaẓer iman-nneɣ nezmer ad nezdem izuyaz anda nettɛadal, ilaq ad nerr aṭas ṛṛay ɣer wayen aɣ-yeɛqben neɣ aɣ-yerkan, akka ad naf izuyaz Adelsanen ɣef-sen.

Deg ubrid-agi n ttḥessin win yettamen-d d win yugar wiyaḍ yedder neɣ yettuɣal. Deg ubrid Aɣerfan n tudert-iw yella beddel mi ara ttmuxuɣ s alaf n lɣerṣ, lɣerṣaḥ d tismin, xedmeɣ deg uxxam-iw uɣurma n "ubariyu" ǧǧiɣ tamawt n "nekk d win id-yettawsin kullec i uxxam-agi", iwakken ad tḥussuɣ d alemsaḥi aḥazin, amuḍin ur yesɛiɣ ulac deg tudert, kullec yebdel deg tudert-iw imi ttwafɣeɣ: asuer, almuḍ d lemɣewma, llebsa teṣfa d azref ad ttegganɣ deg tneqqiț i tin yesɛan ( tameṭṭut Taseddawit) maca ayagi kan iṭul wassan imi axxam-nni ur yuker ara ṛṛay n ugraw neɣ tiknikt n umenɣi. Yessefk ad nelmed ad nbeddel, d ayen n diri ɣer ayen yelhan, tirugza ɣer tafat, tissennent ɣer tayri, atg.

Win yellan d tideț Ur yettwataɛ wala yetfhem tissenniwin n yeɣḍisen id-yeffɣen seg iɛeggalen neɣ imdukal. Wid tḥussun s ṛṛeyya-nni d yeɣḍisen i yettwaṭṭafen ṛṛuḥ-nneɣ, nutni ttemɣalen yerna ttuɣalen s timserɣuyt d tezɣent, nutni yesɛan azal ad rzun mgal Lmasiḥ deg wegdud, mgal taẓɣa-nneɣ yiman-is.

Mi ara sseqsayen inelmaden aɣerbuɣ iwakken ad sḥelfen aɣbel n tuḍen, nettenhuɣ-asen ad ǧǧen aɣezfan, wid xedmen-tt ttafaden s lɣerṣ.

Deg UṬṬU UƐECṚA ṬṬAM; Yettmeslay-aɣ Uselmad ɣef aray, yeqqar-aɣ yessefk ad nexdem deg areḍ-agi n Baba, maca inelmaden ttamnen belli areḍ s arcano A.Z.F., areḍ ɣef yiman-nneɣ, areḍ s tlata ibeddilen ttɛeqliyya-nneɣ, yessefk ad nettewwex gar-neɣ, ad nbeddel Prometeo i nezzeɣ deg wegdud gar-neɣ.

Aray Yettwakken d areḍ-nneɣ, ayen ibɣu yili deg tuddmin anda nenfa iman-nneɣ.

Ibeddi n Aray d-yettas d ttaɣ-n ṣfu tukkiwin-nneɣ yerna tegma ttaɛerreb-aɣ.

Deg UṬṬU UƐECṚA ṬṬEZƐA; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef aqarruy, yeqqar-aɣ belli awanen ifessasen gar tudrin-nneɣ d win ur nezmir ad nettwaẓer iman-nneɣ, ayagi kan yezmer deg areḍ n tudert, deg tuddmin timettiyin, tilɣiyin, uraren, deg tudert n yal ass d win iɛeqlen yeɣḍisen-nneɣ. Aras n ttwaẓer tagdudant, yeffeɣ seg uɣerras deg kul aɣersiw, aras-agi d-yettnerni s tulɣa tagnawt s ttwaẓer i nexdem, seg tikkelt ɣer tayet s useqdec dima.

Kullec ayen i d-yeffɣen seg uɣerras yella diri yerna diri yeffeɣ ad yili mi ara yella deg uɣerras-is, mi ara yessefk ad yili.

S tazmert n Tarbaɛt Yemma deg-neɣ, Yemma RAM-IO kan nezmer ad nesruḥ yeɣḍisen n astalen imgirren n uqerruy, amaru ad t-tafen wid yeqqaren deg kra n ungalen n V.M. Samael.

Stella Maris d tɣuri n tesrelt, tazmert tineslemt, nettat yesɛan tazmert ad tesruḥ tumɛanen i neddukel deg uɣerbaz Adelsan-nneɣ.

"Tonazin" yesseɣṛeb yiwen yeɣḍis Adelsan.

Deg UṬṬU TLATAƐECṚA; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef wammas n Tɣawsa Tagrawlant, yeqqar-aɣ belli yal yiwen yella d tmacint n umaɛun n yeɣḍisen n ddhen i t-yesɛan, dɣa amdan ur yesɛi ara ammas n tɣawsa tagrawlant, dɣa yella llexṣas n leɛnaya iwakken ad naweḍ ɣer ttlata tudrin i yiman-is n Wemdan; yessefk ad nili dima deg uɣerras-is, ayagi ad naweḍ mi ara nesruḥ egos neɣ yeɣḍisen i neddukel.

Ma yella ur nexdim ara ɣef yiman-nneɣ ad ntuɣal ɣer deffir yerna ad nesruḥ, areddem n tbeddu yettarra-aɣ ɣer ubrid n usnerni, yettara-aɣ ɣer wawal Angelico-devico.

Deg UṬṬU TLATAƐECṚA YIWEN; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef uẓar Esoterico Gnostico, yeqqar-aɣ yessefk ad nebḥas yeɣḍisen i yettwanzen neɣ nettaɛraf-iten, yeqqar-aɣ-d yiwet ad ten-nekren d ttwaẓer, ayagi ad d-yawin asnerni n tafat deg wegdud-nneɣ.

Tasuqqest n yeɣḍisen i nebḥas yessefk ad yeddukel d imaɛanen i wiyaḍ ad tent-nefruḥ iwakken ad tili taɣella seg umenɣiwin neɣ yeɣḍisen i d-yezgan d assegzi ɣer tamdellit-is yiman-is.

Deg UṬṬU TLATAƐECṚA SIN; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef Talɣa deg Areḍ, yeqqar-aɣ belli Twaẓer, Aɣaras d Taɛreqa d tlata id-yellan d amadal deg tsuqqest n Yo. 1°— tettwaẓer, 2°—Yettwahḍer, 3°—Tettwareḍ; akka ara nexdem d imessasen deg umenɣi. Aras n ttwaẓer tagdudant amek ara yettnerni ad aɣ-d-yawi asnerni agnaw n areḍ-nneɣ.
