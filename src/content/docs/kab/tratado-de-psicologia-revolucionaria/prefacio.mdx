Amezruy-agi n Tnefsut Tadelsant d Izwel amaynut i yefka Umɣar i watmaten deg wass n Tlalit n 1975. D Akud ikemlen i ɣ-isselmaden amek ara nneq yirdalen. Ar tura, ilemẓiyen ttafen deg usserki n yirdalen kan, am uɛessas aserdasi i d-yeqqar fell-asen, nekni nelli d imusnawen deg usserki n yirdalen, maca tura yewweḍ-d lweqt anda i nelzem ad ten-nneq, ad ten-nekkkes, s ttiknik n Umɣar Samael i ɣ-d-yefka tifratin s tmuɣli, taɣara d ttiddukla.

Mi mmaten yirdalen, am wakken ara d-iban Ruh s tezmert-is imeqqranen, kullec yettbeddel fell-aneɣ, aṭas i d-yettseqsi amek ara xedmen mi d-ffɣen aṭas n yirdalen deg yiwet n tikkelt, i wigi, nella-yasen ad kkessen kra, wid yellan ad ǧǧun ad țraǧun, ar tikkelt nniḍen ad ten-sserkun bac ad ten-kkessen.

Deg YIWET TAFLA; yesselmad-aɣ amek ara nbeddel asebtar n tudert-nneɣ, ad nessenger: Iɣil, ṭṭmaɛ, usḥissi, aḥeqqaq, usḥissi, uɣilif, tayri, ayen nebɣa, atg. Yella d ilaq ad neḥkem ɣef laqel n ddunit, ad nerra talsa sdat-neɣ bac ad tesserɣ lḥekma tabeddit n laqel amesbaṭi, deg tafla-yagi yesselmad-aɣ amek ara nḥess lqima n zzux n zzhu-nneɣ, ad nbeddel lqima-agi. D ayen yellan mi nessenger yirdalen-nneɣ.

Kullec i d-yettawi beddel ger ddaxel, yessawaḍ-d ad beddel berra. Lqima n zzhu i d-yesseḥsib Umɣar deg wungal-agi, tettmeslay ɣef lḥala anda nella.

Deg TNAYEN TAFLA; yessefhem belli lqima n zzhu d asakal anda i nella deg takat n Tudert, mi nelɣu takat-agi nettenniɣni, maca mi neqqim deg yiwet n lḥala yetteffeɣ-aɣ lferḥ, nxeṣṣer laɛnaya, nețḥezzen, nețqețțiɛ.

Deg UMƐAWAN TAFLA; yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef leɛnaya Tadelsant, yesselmad-aɣ belli tiɣri Tadelsant-nneɣ tella deg ddaxel-nneɣ, yenna belli abrid awraq neɣ uqdim d iẓẓayer i wid yesεan leɛnaya, i wid yeqqaren ad beddlen tura, ad nqadder iman-nneɣ, d ayen yellan deg abrid awraq; Iḥumaniḍen tteddun deg abrid afellay deg takat n tudert.

Deg UBUDLAN TAFLA; yessenfal amek ara yella beddel, ccbaḥa n ugrawli tettwasileɣ s tɣara nniḍen, ur yessaweḍ ara ad yessenkren yirdalen-is, mi nettwali amek ara d-kkren deg ugrawli yettruḥ ccbaḥ-is aṣlani. Mi nekkess yirdalen, Ruh yettban s tezmert-is tameqqrant, d ayen iḥussen medden s wallen kan, ula ccbaḥa n Ruh i yessimɣuren tafekka.

Deg TAFLA UḤAMUM; Yesselmad-aɣ amek ara neḥkem deg ussan n Tnefsut, yesselmad-aɣ abrid bac ad nekkess ayen ur neḥsib ara i nella deg ddaxel-nneɣ (yirdalen); yesselmad-aɣ ad nqeddem iman-nneɣ, bac ad nessewweḍ ad beddel amagnu.

Beddel yella d ilaq, maca medden ur ssinen amek ara beddlen, ttnuɣnayen aṭas, ttafen deg usserki ɣef wiyaḍ kan, ur ssinen belli nutni kan i d imḍebbren ɣef tudert-nsen.

Deg TAFLA UṢADIS; yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef tudert, yenna-aɣ belli tudert tella d ugur ur yefhim yiwen: Iḥala ttilin deg ddaxel u yesɣunen ttilin berra.

Deg TAFLA UƐEBƐUD; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef iḥala deg ddaxel, yesselmad-aɣ amgarad yellan ger iḥala n laɛeqliya, u iɣewwaren iɛemlanen n tudert.

Mi nebḍed iḥala ur yesεin ara lmeɛna n laɛeqliya, yella beddel amagnu deg-neɣ.

Yettmeslay-aɣ deg TAFLA UMƐAWAN ɣEF YIƔEWWRAREN IMASSAWIYEN; yesselmad-aɣ amek ara nesseɣti iḥala Tadelsanit ur nelli s tiddukla, iḥala n ddaxel ur nelli s lmeɛna, yesselmad-aɣ ad nesserked deg wexxam-nneɣ n ddaxel yellan d amsareɣ, tudert n ddaxel tessaweḍ-d leḥwayeǧ berra, ma llan d aɣriben, d axaṭer llan iḥala n ddaxel ur nelli s lmeɛna. Ayen yellan berra d tmuɣli n wayen yellan ddaxel, beddel deg ddaxel yettarra-d tura nizam amaynut n leḥwayeǧ.

Iḥala ur nelli s lmeɛna ger ddaxel ttarra-yaɣ d asirem fell-aɣ deg ddɣel n medden, yesselmad-aɣ ad nerr iman-nneɣ ger iɣewwaren, ad nḥess belli kullec yettaɛeddi, ilaq ad nelmed ad nwali tudert am film, deg udaram nelzem ad nili d iḥussanen, ur nerra iman-nneɣ deg udaram.

Yiwen n mmi yesεa uẓawan anda ttneddamen ifilmen iεerḍanen, yettwaččaṛ mi xedmen imzennayen yellan d imezyanen deg usselɣu; deg yiwen wass amgarrad, mmi Alvaro i d-ɣaɣ ad ṛuḥeɣ ad waliɣ film anda xedmen yimzennayen yellan d imezyanen deg usselɣu, deg leɛnaya-nni, rriɣ-as belli ur zmireɣ ara ad ṛuḥeɣ, axaṭer ttaɣ-it laɛnaya ad waliɣ udaram amdan i yifen film-is, anda kulleṣ yimzennayen llan d imezyanen deg usselɣu; yesteqsa-yi: Ma d acu udaram-agi?, rriɣ-as, d udaram n tudert; yesteɣreq-aɣ, maca deg udaram-agi kulleṣ nettqeddem, yesselmaɣd-iyi: Nekk qeddmeɣ am aḥussan deg udaram-agi. Acuɣer? Rriɣ-as: Axaṭer ur ḥessseɣ ara iman-iw deg udaram, xedmeɣ ayen ilaq ad xedmeɣ, ur feṛḥeɣ neɣ ḥezneɣ s wayen yellan deg udaram.

Deg TAFLA UƐAČUR; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef yiwen n yiman amgarrad, yessenfel belli deg tudert n medden, ur yelli ara qeddem s tiddukla axaṭer d agemmuḍ n yimanen, dɣa acuɣer yella beddel aṭas deg tudert n yal yiwen deg yiqeddmen n udaram: usḥissi, taḍsa, imeṭṭawen, rreḥba, tuɣma, dɣa ayen i d-yettbeggin beddel u d tagrawt n wayen i d-yezḍen yimanen n zzux-nneɣ.

Deg TAFLA UYETRIW; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef Ego-nneɣ i ɣ-iɛzizen, yenna-aɣ belli yimanen ttilin d azal Tinefsut ma llan s lxiṛ ma ur llin ara, yesselmad-aɣ aɛemmel n tisselliɣ tazenzult ger ddaxel, akka ara nwali aṭas n yimanen i yettidiren deg ddaxel n zzux-nneɣ.

Deg TAFLA USINƔAL; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef Beddel Amagnu, din dinna yesselmad-aɣ belli ur yelli ara beddel deg Tnefsut-nneɣ bla tisselliɣ ḥeqqan n kullu agraw n tɣawsiwin n ddaxel i nella ger-neɣ.

Mi nelmed belli ur nelli ara yiwen maca aṭas ger-neɣ, nella deg abrid n tussna iman. Tussna d Tafhemt ur ttaɛdan ara, wayen amezwaru d i laqel, wayen umḍiq d i wul.

TAFLA TILATAČUR; Aḥussan d wayen yettwali, din dinna yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef umeẓlay n tisselliɣ iẓenzulan i deg ddaxel i yqeddem ḥeqqan ɣef iman-is, i yebɣan ad yekkes tɣawsiwin ur yettwafen ara ger-neɣ.

I tussna iman, ilaq ad nebḍu iman-nneɣ ɣef aḥussan d wayen yettwali, bla abḍu-yagi ur nezmir ara ad nessewweḍ tussna iman.

Deg TAFLA UBUDLANČUR; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef imyussuten iberkanen; u nettnaɛlam belli yimanen kulleṣ sɛan tussna, tettɛawan ger usentur tussna-nneɣ bac ad ssiwḍent tixxamen, tiẓriwin, aḥilan, atg., ayen i d-yettbeggin belli ur nelli ara laqel ibeddlen, nettnaɛlam deg tafla-yagi belli yimanen sewwaṛen usentur tussna-nneɣ s tɣara tameqqrant.

Deg TAFLA UḤAMUMČUR; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef Tiddas, din dinna yettban yiwen belli ur nelli ara tamussni neɣ laɛnaya n yiman, neɣ tiddas, ger tisselliɣ n ddaxel, nezmir ad nwali medden i yettidiren deg Tnefsut-nneɣ (yimanen) i ilaq ad nekkess bac ad nessewweḍ Beddel Amagnu, mi d tiddas tella d āmellal, nettnaɛlam lḥala n iselmaden n uɣerbaz i yettṣeggiden ɣef ilemẓiyen kulleṣ tudert u akka ara tteddun i tnekra axaṭer ur fhimen ara amdan n tudert.

Tifalwin-nniḍen ger 16 ar 32 ttilin d imezyanen i medden yellan i yebɣan ad ffɣen ger agraw, i wid yeqqaren ad ilin acu kan deg tudert, i igudar ilan, i wid i d-yettbeddilen laɛeqliya d Ruh ur nelli ara yettwattagen, i wid iɛezlen iɣes n goma, i deg iḍegger azɣen-is sdat usɣun n yal waɛrab.

TAFLA USADISČUR; yettmeslay-aɣ Umɣar ɣef tektabt n tudert, ilaql ad nesseḥsab s tekrart n awalen yal ass, tikerrist n leḥwayeǧ n yiwen wass, kullec-nni yessaweḍ-aɣ tussna tameqqrant.

Deg TAFLA UƐEBƐUDČUR; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef tulmas timkanikyin, yenna-aɣ mi ur yeqqim ara yiwen ad yesseḥsab iman-is, ur yezmir ad iɛerḍ ad yaf tikerrist ur yettḥebsen ara yal ass, win ur yebɣi ara ad yesseḥsab iman-is, ur yebɣi ara ad yeqeddem bac ad yessewweḍ Beddel Amagnu ḥeqqani, zzux-nneɣ d tabellust kan, d amɣar yettmeslayen, d ayen yellan d amkaniky, d tekrart n wayen yettḥedrun, l عادت-nneɣ d yiwet, ur nebɣi ara ad ten-nbeddel.

TAFLA UMƐAWANČUR; yella d ɣef Agrum Aɛlayan-Aɛlayen, عادت ttɣamayen-aɣ d iyerfan, nelli d medden imkaniky yella fell-aɣ l عادت iɣeddan, ilaq ad nessewweḍ beddel ger ddaxel. Tisselliɣ yella d ilaq.

TAFLA UƐAČURČUR; yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef bab n wexxam, ilaq ad neselleɣ iman-nneɣ ger udaram n tudert, ad neḥrez imireḍ Tadelsanit, aɛemmel-agi yedda mgal tudert, yella d ayen yemgarraden aṭas ɣef tudert yal ass.

Mi ur yebḍed ara yiwen deg ddaxel yili d aɣrib n tikeltin kulleṣ. Bab n wexxam yellan d amnay d win yettemlil mgal tɣara, wid ur yebɣi ara ad ten-yaɣer tudert ttilin d kraḍni.

Deg TAFLA UƐACUR; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef snat n ddunitin, yenna-aɣ belli tussna ḥeqqan i yezmiren ad yessewweḍ ger-neɣ Beddel Amagnu ameẓlay, yella d ɣef tisselliɣ ḥeqqan n yiman-is. Tisselliɣ deg ddaxel d abrid bac ad nebḍed iman-nneɣ ger ddaxel, s tisselliɣ n yiman-is, nelmed ad neddu deg abrid n ddaxel, tamentilt n tisselliɣ n yiman-is tella d tagrawt deg taɣzert tanumant, maca tamentilt-agi tella d temeqqrant mi neddu deg tisselliɣ n yiman-is, am wakken nelmed ad neddu deg ddunit berra, akka daɣen ger aɛemmel Tadelsanit ɣef yiman-is nelmed ad neddu deg ddunit n ddaxel.

Deg TAFLA UYETRIWČUR; yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef tisselliɣ n yiman-is, yenna-aɣ belli tisselliɣ n yiman-is d abrid iɛemlanen bac ad nessewweḍ Beddel Amagnu, ssinen ur yelli ara ad waliɣ, ur ilaq ara ad nbeddel ssinen d waliɣ.

Tisselliɣ n yiman-is tella d meyya deg meyya teɣdemt, d abrid n beddel n yiman-is, mi ssinen i yellan d aḥessas ur yelli ara. Laɛnaya ttaggi d tameqqrant tella d tadjert n waliɣ, mi tixxamen d iɣewwaren ttilin d tadjert n wayen yettwali. Ssinen d ayen yellan d amkaniky, aḥessas; deg umbeddel tisselliɣ n yiman-is d aɛemmel n laɛeqliya.

Deg TAFLA USINƔALČUR; yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef Tamseɣret, yenna-aɣ ad nesseǧhed, neɣ ta "amessiḥ kan" yessawaḍ uɣiɣad, axaṭer d yimanen-nneɣ ttnaɣen ger usen, mi tettwaḥsebbed tettmeslayeḍ وحدك, sselleɣ ɣef iman-ik, tafeḍ tiliṭ teggedeḍ.

Deg TAFLA UTILATAČUR; yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef ddunit n leɛlaqat, yenna-aɣ belli llan kraḍ n iḥala n leɛlaqat, ttḥebsiben d tafekka-nneɣ, d ddunit berra d leɛlaqa n urgaz s yiman-is, ayen ur yesεin ara azal i waṭas n medden, medden ḥussen kan ɣef sin n yimawalen n leɛlaqat. Ilaq ad neɣṛeɣ bac ad nessen s izgan n kraḍ-agi illa-yaɣ lxilaf.

Lxilaf n usserki deg ddaxel yettarra-yaɣ ur nelli ara ɣer yiman-nneɣ, ayen yettarran-yaɣ ad neqqim deg ẓulum, mi tettwafteḍ teččiwwteḍ, teɣneqreḍ, tesɛuḍ lxilaf, essuɣay (iman-ik) u ayen yettarran ixliwen n tafekka-ik ad stufuɣen s uḥric amgarrad.

Deg TAFLA UBUDLANČUR; yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef tucḍa Tadelsanit, yenna-aɣ ɣef tucḍatin, aḥraziman, ḥussen tebbḍin-aɣ, atg., umen belli wiyaḍ sɛan lxilaf ɣef kullec i ɣ-yettdrun, deg umbeddel ttnafen ttiddukla am aɛemmel-nneɣ, akka ur nezmir ara ad neǧhed iman-nneɣ. Argaz yettwaḥebsen deg tixxamen i yessegraw nezmer ad nili d afray ma ur nelli ara, ayagi ur yelli ara d aɛemmel bac ad nselleɣ ɣef iman-nneɣ u ad neǧhed iman-nneɣ, elmed ad semmḥeḍ d ilaq i tǧihda-nneɣ deg ddaxel. Tanḍart n Lxeṛ yella d ameɣlal ɣef tanḍart n urgaz yesεan tazmert. "Allen i allen, tuɣmest i tuɣmest". Agnusis tella d tameɣrur i wid yeqqaren ad qedmen u ad beddlen ḥeqqan, yal wa yettcennu tucḍa-s Tadelsanit.

Aḥessan aɣrib n tɣawsiwin iɛacen ttḥebsen-aɣ ar wayen iɛeddan, ur ttzmiri ara ad neɛc di tura i ɣ-yessimneɛɣuren. Bac ad nɛeddi i aswir ameɣlal ilaq ad neǧǧ ayen i nelli, ɣef yal yiwen ger-neɣ yellan aswir ameɣlal i yelzem ad nelɣu.

Deg TAFLA UḤAMUMČUR; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef tuɣalin d Tekrara, yenna-aɣ belli Agnusis d beddel, asegyed, tijheḍt ur tettḥebs ara; win ur yebɣi ara ad iǧehd iman-is, ad ibeddel, ad yexser lweqt-is axaṭer gar uganiɣ ad yeɣli, ur yezmir ad yessefraq i yiman-is; s tiddukla yettɣimi iɣimi kan V.M. belli nella d tiɣennuštin nettkerred tɣawsiwin n tudert. Mi nesseɣti ɣef tɣawsiwin-agi, nettnaɛlam belli nella d imzennayen i yqeddemen bla ttawil deg udaram n tudert yal ass.

Mi nesεa tazmert bac ad neɛser iman-nneɣ bac ad nwali acu ara tteg u ara yessenfell tafekka-nneɣ, netteggu iman-nneɣ deg abrid n tisselliɣ s laɛeqliya u nwali belli acu yettɛeddin, ayen yessinen, u acu yettɛeddin d win yettqeddemen u yettḍebbiɛen neɣ taffekka-nneɣ. Tameɣra n tudert tella d tteggan u d taɣribt i win ur yessin ara ad yesselɣ taɣuɣat deg ddaxel, yettuɣ deg agerruj-is ger tikkeltin tikṛadan, iɣeẓzan, yimanen-nneɣ ttidiren s lferḥ deg iɣeẓzan.

Deg TAFLA UṢADISČUR; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef Tussna n Yigrawli, yenna belli mi yettlulu urgrawli d tɛeddut i yella, ayagi yefka ccbaḥa i ugrawli, mi yezda zzhu yettɛeddi yimanen i d-yusan seg tudert iɛeddan u yettxeṣṣer ccbaḥa aṣlani.

Deg TAFLA UƐEBƐUDČUR; Yessawaḍ Argaz n Lɣalat d ufarisi, yenna belli yal wa yettɛeddi ɣef acu yesεa, dɣa aṭas n medden seεan acu: Izwal, leɛmer, idrimen, cciεa, tallit tanmettit, atg. Argaz d tameṭṭut yettwajren s zzux d wid i teḥwaǧ tiddukla bac ad idiren, argaz yettɛeddi ɣef tɣara berra, daɣen yella d awḍif axaṭer ass nniɣ deg yexser tɣara-nni yettraǧu ad yettraǧu yili d argaz yerɣan aṭas deg ddunit.

Mi neḥseb iman-nneɣ d imeɣranen ɣef wiyaḍ nettwaliɣ yimanen-nneɣ u nesserriɣ s ayagi ad nessewweḍ ad nili d imezyanen. I aɛemmel usitar, izwal n yiman-nneɣ ttilin d uɣriben i yettɛawanen ad nesγer deg Ruh, mi nessaɛdil iman-nneɣ nezmir ad nɣum sɣarat ɣef acu netɛeddi, ilaq ad nesɣer aṭas af tɣawsiwin i ɣ-yerrun neɣ i ɣ-iɣaben, akka ara naf tɣarat Tadelsanit ɣef acu illa-yaɣ.

Deg abrid-agi n utǧiḥ, win yettwali iman-is d imeɣlal ɣef wayeḍ yettɣimi neɣ yedda ar deffir. Deg aɛemmel Anessili n tudert-iw yella beddel ameɣlal mi ttaɣ-iyi leḥwayeǧ, aɣṛab d imɣuren, xedmeɣ deg wexxam-iw aɣewwar n "amɣar", ǧǧiɣ aseffud n "nekni d aɛṭṭaw i kulleṣ deg wexxam-agi", bac ad ḥasseɣ aḥercani, amḍi d bla acu deg tudert, kullec yebḍed deg tudert-iw axaṭer yettwatteg-iyi: Tiftirt, imensi d aɣrum, llebsa dima d taṣfiḥt d azref ad ǧǧeɣ deg udan-agi am yettɛassan-i (tamettut tessefraq) maca ayagi yegga kan ussan axaṭer axxam-nni ur yi-ireddi ara tilawt neɣ abrid n umjahed. Ilaq ad nelmed ad nbeddel, lxir s lxiṛ, iɣeẓzan ar tafat, tukra ar tayri, atg.

Ser ḥeqqani ur yettiɣeddin ara neɣ ifhem tuɣlatin n yimanen i ɣ-d-yettḍeggiren iɛdawen neɣ imdukkal. Wid iḥussen s ttɣirat-nni d yimanen i yettḥebsen Ruh-nneɣ, nitni ttɛağalen u rran s rreḥba d iɣil, tennaɣ yella i yella mgal Lmasiḥ ger ddaxel, mgal zzerε-nneɣ.

Mi steqsan ilemẓiyen amek ara nɛiwz bac ad nesseɣti iḍerren, neɛiwz-asen bac ad ǧǧen iɣil, wid i t-xedmen ttfafen.

Deg TAFLA UƐAČURČUR; Yettmeslay-aɣ Umɣar ɣef laɛnaya, yenna-aɣ ilaq ad neqdem deg aɛemmel-agi n Baba, maca ilemẓiyen ttamnen belli yettqeddem s arcano A.Z.F., aɛemmel ɣef iman-nneɣ, aɛemmel s tlata n izgan i d-yettserḥen i laɛeqliya-nneɣ, ilaq ad nessewweḍ ger ddaxel-nneɣ, ad nessefreɣ Prometeo i nella ger-neɣ yettwaḥebsen. Laɛnaya Yettwaɣḍem d aɛemmel-nneɣ, d acu kan ara yili tikelt anda illa-yaɣ.

Asfreɣ n laɛnaya yussa-d gar usserki n yirdalen-nneɣ, tulɣa tettaɣ-anaɣ.

Deg TAFLA UƔIMAWČUR; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef uɣerreẓ n uqerre, yenna-aɣ belli tikeltin tizenzulin n tudert-nneɣ ttilin d uḥercani ɣer iman-nneɣ, ayagi yettwassewweḍ kan deg aɛemmel n tudert, deg leɛlaqat tanmettiyin, isemganen, aɛaṛef, ɣer taggara deg tudert yal ass, dɣa aṭas n yimanen i yellan d aɣriben. Tamentilt n tisselliɣ n ddaxel, tella d tagrawt deg yal amdan, tamentilt-agi tella d tameqqrant deg tisselliɣ i nteggan, seg tikelt ar tikelt u deg ussemres ur yettḥebsen ara.

Kullec yellan berra n umkan yella d uɣiɣad u wayen yellan d uɣiɣad yerra ad yili d ayen yellan d ameẓyan mi yella deg umkan-is, mi ilaq ad yili.

S tazmert n Tallayt Umyal ger-neɣ, Umyal RAM-IO nezmer ad nessenger yimanen n tɣara n laqel amgarrad, tifrat ttilin iɣurdan deg waṭas n wungalen n V.M. Samael.

Stella Maris d asɣar asitar, tazmert tanumant, nettat tesεa tazmert ad tessenger ttwaḥṣer i nella ger ddaxel Tadelsanit-nneɣ.

"Tonazin" yeɣreẓ yal yiwen n yiman Tadelsanit.

Deg TAFLA UTILATAČUR; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef Sentur n Laɛbar Tabeddit, yenna-aɣ belli yal wa yella d amusni n tɣara n yimanen i t-yezzin, u ayagi yettarra amdan ur yesεi ara sentur n laɛbar tabeddit, dɣa illa kan lxilaf bac ad nessewweḍ azemmar iman n Ruh; yeḥwaǧ tettuɣra n yiswi u ayagi yettwassewweḍ ɣef uɣerreẓ n Egos neɣ yimanen i nella ger-neɣ.

Ma ur neqdim ara ɣef iman-nneɣ nettwaliɣ u nesɣunnu. Aɛemmel n Tannilit yeggad-aɣ deg abrid n tulɣa, yessaweḍ-aɣ ɣer lḥala Anjil-dévico.

Deg TAFLA UTILATA YIWENČUR; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef uɣerbaz n Esotérico Gnóstico, yenna-aɣ belli yelzem ad nḥess iman-nneɣ neɣ nessen-it, d ilaq bac ad nezmer ad nessenğer, tisselliɣ terriɣ taziri n tafat deg ddaxel-nneɣ.

Aserki n yimanen i neɣṛaɣ yelzem ad yedda s ttewkil i wiyaḍ ad ten-nselmed bac ad ffɣen ger ssitanen neɣ yimanen i yessawaḍen i tɣawsa-nsen.

Deg TAFLA UTILATA TNAYENČUR; Yettmeslay-aɣ ɣef Talallit deg aɛemmel, yenna-aɣ belli Tisselliɣ, Aɣẓan u Aɛemmel d tlata n izgan i yellan deg tnegrit n Yo. 1°—yettwali, 2°—yettɣezzen, 3°—yettqeddem; akka ara yettwaɣḍem imsellaten deg umjahed. Tamentilt n tisselliɣ n ddaxel mi tettwasseɣreɣ teqqar-anaɣ amek ara telɣu aɛemmel-nneɣ.

Asegraw 25 deg ussan n tlalit n 1951 yenna-aɣ Umɣar da deg temdint n Ciénaga u deg deffir-nni yessenfel deg Izwel n Tlalit n 1962, akka: "Nekki llaɣ yidwen ar tettwalim Lmasiḥ deg wul-nwen".

ɣef uɣaras-is tella taɣeẓrant n ugdud n Acuario u tiddukla n Tayri tella d tameqqrant ger tussna n Gnóstico, ma tebɣiḍ ad ttedduḍ d tiddukla n Tayri, ilaq ad ǧǧeḍ tukra, deg tɣara-is tameẓyant, ayagi yeggad-aɣ bac ad yeffeɣ ugrawli n warɣ, agrawli n alquimia, mmi n tumert, Lmasiḥ ger ddaxel i yettidiren u yettmeslayen deg tazzwart s tazmert-nneɣ iɣḍimen. Akka ara nessewweḍ tamettant n leǧyunat n yimanen ssitanen i nella ger-neɣ u nessewweḍ i tiddukla, i beddel kulleṣ.

Tiddukla-agi tāmellalt ur ttfehimin ara iḥumaniḍen n tallit-agi, maca ilaq ad nemaɣ bac ten-nili deg kulleṣ ddinat, bac ad bɣun tudert i yifen, yettwaḍefren ger tulmas yellan i yifen, tadamsa-yagi n tiddukla tettuɣal-anaɣ ar tiddukla n Lmasiḥ ger ddaxel, mi nella deg aɛemmel nebḍed lɣedda n taɣzert.
