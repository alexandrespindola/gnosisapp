War d-iban belli deg unadi ɣef Yiman-iw, ilaq ad nebḍu, s tmusni, gar wayen iḍerran di tudert d talliyin n lɣaci deg ulawen-nneɣ.

Ilaq-aɣ ad nẓer anda nella, ama ɣer tallit n lɣaci deg ul-nneɣ, ama ɣer wayen iḍerran di tudert-nneɣ. Tudert, s timmad-is, d ammud n wayen iḍerran deg uzemz d wemkan...

Win yennan: "Tudert d ṭṭna n leɛtab i yet-tɛebba wemdan deg Rruḥ-is..." Yal yiwen yexḍer ad yexmeḍ akken yebɣa; nekk uɣaleɣ belli i tmerniwt n kra n teswiɛt, iḍerr-ed fell-as ussegdef d tiɣellest... Yal ayen iḍerran yesɛa adeg-is, talliyin n lɣaci deg ulawen-nneɣ daɣen, yal yiwet tesɛa lḥala-s; ayagi d tidet, ur yezmir yiwen ad yettakkeɣ fell-as...

Ayen i d-iḥettem ccɣel ɣef Yiman-nneɣ d talliyin n lɛeqliya deg ulawen-nneɣ... Ulac win ara yekkir ad yini ur nɛebbi ara aṭas n tɣellisin deg ulawen-nneɣ, ulac daɣen win ara yekkir ad yini ur llin ara talliyin n lɛeqliya tilawin... Ma yella nebɣa ad nebeddel s tidet, ilaq-aɣ, s tɣawsa taɛeffant, ad nbeddel talliyin-nni n lɛeqliya tilawin...

Asbeddel n talliyin n lɛeqliya tilawin ad yawi-d asbeddel meqqren deg tudert... Ma yella nexdem fell-asent, ayen iḍerran ara ɣ-yeɛqen deg tudert ur yezmir ara ad ɣ-yejreḥ s teflast...

Neqqa-d awal ara yettwafehmen kan ma nettwali-t, ma netḥussu-t deg wemkan anda iḍerray... Win ur nexdim ara ɣef yiman-is, ad yuɣal d amsebrer ɣer wayen iḍerran; d am teɣrumt n ttreb deg waman imeqqranen n lebḥer...

Ayen iḍerran ur yeḥbis ara ad yettbeddil deg waṭas n leṣnaf-is; iḍerr-ed yiwen deffir wayeḍ, d ayla... Llant tidet kra n tɣawsiwin yelhan, llan kra n tɣawsiwin diri; kra n wayen iḍerran yelha neɣ yedder fell-as... Asbeddel n kra n wayen iḍerran yezmer; ad nbeddel agmad, ad nbeddel lḥala, atg, tella ger wayen yezmer ad yili.

Lakin, llant tɣawsiwin ur nezmir ad asent nbeddel; deg lḥalat-agi ilaq ad asent naɣ, ɣas ma llant kra seg-sent ṭṭuqtent, ɣas ma llant kra seg-sent ur nḥemmel ad asent nwali... War d-iban belli ur nettwali ara lɣem ma ur nezmir ad asent nbeddel...

Ilaq ad nḥaseb tudert d ammud n talliyin deg ul-nneɣ; umezruy n tidet n tudert-nneɣ yebna ɣef talliyin-nni akk... Ma nerra dduṛ ɣer tudert-nneɣ meṛṛa, nezmer ad nwali belli aṭas n lḥalat ur neḥmil ara iḍran-d ɣef talliyin n lɛeqliya tilawin...

Alejandro Magno, ɣas ma yella yettaɣ taẓɣent, yeqbel ɣer leḥwayej ara t-yekksen tudert... Francisco I yemmut ɣef sebba n zen iɛafen, ayen i d-yemmekti umezruy... Mi akken i yettwanɣa Marat sɣur yiwet n tmenhazt, yemmut ɣef zzux d tḥasdat, iɛteqed iman-is d win yellan ɣef ṣṣwab...

Tullas n Parc des Cerfs (Parc des Cerfs) kkessent-as-d tudert i urgaz ameqqran yeznan Luis XV. Aṭas n medden i yemmuten ɣef tamaɛ, ɣef lɣeḍba, ɣef tismin, ayagi ẓran-t-id yimassanen n laɛqel.

Mi akken i tserseɣ lebɣi-nneɣ ɣef kra ur yesɛi ara laman, ad nɣellu ɣer temqebbert... Otelo ɣef sebba n tismin yuɣal d bu tmegreḍ, lḥebs yeččuṛ d wid yettuɣalen ɣef ṣṣwab.
