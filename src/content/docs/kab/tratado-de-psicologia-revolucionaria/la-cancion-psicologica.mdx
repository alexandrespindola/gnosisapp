Tura d tallit ideg ilaq ad nemmeslay s tmuɣli tameqqrant ɣef wayen umi qqaren "asusru amenẓu".

Ur yelli kra n ccekk ɣef umuɣur n "asusru n tiṭṭ". Ayagi, zdat n usserɣed n lefhama, ittaɣ-aɣ aṭas n tezmert.

Lemmer ad ur d-yeɣli yiwen deg uɣalat n ussidmer d iman-is, asusru amenẓu yella d ayen iwɛren.

Mi ara yessidmer yiwen d iman-is, ittaɣ-aɣ aṭas n laman, yesseɣṛas iman-is, isusru iman-is, ixemmem belli yelha i fulan, i zutan, i tameṭṭut, i tarwa, atg., d acu kan ur yelli win id-t-issnen, atg. Deg umata, d awlaya akk wiyyaḍ d imcumen, d imehbal.

Gar tarrayin i d-yezmer asusru amenẓu, tawaɣit ɣef wayen i zemren ad xemmem fell-ak wiyyaḍ; ahat ad ggullen belli ur nella d imensa, d imezwura, d imɣaren, d wid yesɛan lǧehd, atg.

Ayen yellan d aɣrib akk deg wayagi, nettuḥḥas ɣef umuɣur n tezmert tameqqrant i d-yettawi ɣur-naɣ tawsit n tawaɣit-agi.

Aṭas n tɣawsiwin ur ilaqen ɣer kra n medden ur nɣir kra n diri, d tawaɣit i d-yekkan seg ususru amenẓu.

Deg umru-agi, mi ara ttaɣen laman ɣer iman-nsen, isusru iman-nsen akka, d ayen iban belli I nɣal-ittiniɣ I-nniḍen ladɣa ma nra ad d-nefk adrar-nsen imɣar-it deg lɣerba.

Yessidmer yiwen d iman-is yesseɣṛas aṭas ɣef taddart-is d yellan gar-asen ad yessebter.

Akka i ixemmem fulan, zutan, amaɣlaf, tameɣlaft, ajirnaɣ, umḥaddi, ameddakel, atg., atg., atg., ur t-id-xenṣen am wakken ilaq ɣas akken deg lxiṛ-is d yettwassnen d yettwaxzan deg waya, yettwagg uguren d yettuxsiṛen i ddunit meṛṛa.

Yettwabdar belli deg lxedma tusnakt Gnustiq, yella umɣur n tezmert ala s smah i wiyyaḍ.

Ma yella yiwen yettidir deg tallit, deg lawan, yettɛedday fell-as ayen i t-ixenṣen, ayen i t-yexden, deg uɣilif i d-yessawḍen, daymen s tezlatin-is, ur yelli kra ara yemɣin deg-s.

Taẓallit n Mass-nneɣ tenna: "Semmeḥ-aɣ idrimen-nneɣ akken i nesseɛfu wid i ɣ-ixenṣen".

Iɣil-is i yiwen idrimen, aɣilif ɣef diri i t-yexden wiyyaḍ, atg., yesseɣlay umɣur amenẓu n teṛwiḥt.

Ɛisa, Ameqqran KABIR, yenna: "Ɛaqel d umeḥrib-ik ar tura, deg umecwar, ar ur k-t-yefk umeḥrib i uḥekkam, uḥekkam i umeɛzuz, ad tetteddukled ɣer lḥebs. S tideț nniɣ-ak belli ur tettufɣeɣ ɣer din, ar akken ara tixellṣed tilisa-a". (Matta, V, 25, 26)

Ma ɣ-ixenṣen, ilaq aɣ-nexḍem. Ma nettraǧu ad ɣ-nexelles ar tilisa, ilaq aɣ-nexelles zdat tilisa.

D ayagi i d "Asutel n Talin", "Allen s allen d uɣmam s uɣmam". "Asegzel iɛewwajen", ur yesɛi anamek.

Taɛfu, taɛssast i tettwaxdem d usefsus i wiyyaḍ ɣef diri i ɣ-yexden, daɣen ɣur-neɣ tettwattraɣ ɣas akken nettaɣ iman-nneɣ d ilemma.

Anekcum s ddaw usutel ur ilaqen d aɣalat, yelha ad nerr iman-nneɣ s ddaw usmussen imaynuten.

Usutel n Ṛreḥma d usmussen yeflalen ɣef usutel n wergaz izewriren: "Allen s allen, uɣmam s uɣmam".

D ayen yeffɣen, yettwabdal, ur yettwaɛṭṭel, ad nerr iman-nneɣ s tmusni s ddaw usmussen ifennanen n lxedma tusnakt Gnustiq, ad nttu belli ɣ-ixenṣen d ad nesseɣreɣ deg teṛwiḥt-nneɣ yal tarrayt n ususru.

Ad ur neqqbel deg-neɣ, issiwlen n tiggugri, ttbuɣuḍ, tagnitwat tarugant, tawaɣit ɣef diri i ɣ-yexden, izewri, tumert, tatuturt i ttaɣen, atg., atg., atg.

Gnusis tettwassemɣer i wid yebbɣun s tideț i yebɣan ad xedmen d ad beddlen.

Ma nneqqen ɣer medden nezmer ad nessenqed deg wudem ayagi, belli yal yiwen yesɛa tacennut-is.

Yal yiwen yettacen tacennut-is tusnakt; bɣiɣ ad meslay s tmuɣli tamqrant ɣef tamsalt-a n ttaɣit tusnakt; ad nḥuss belli ɣ-ixenṣen, ad netqelleb, ad nessusru iman-nneɣ, atg.

Ahat medden "yettacen tacennut-nsen, akka akken kan", mbla ma yella win id-t-id-yessugten, mbla ma yella win id-t-id-iɛawnen d tikkelt nniḍen s kra n tkeṛṛusin n ccṛab...

Nenna belli ilaq ad nessemɣer tacennut-nneɣ i yexsan; ayagi ittaɣ-aɣ tezmert deg, iṭṭef-aɣ aṭas n tezmert.

Deg temsaliyin n Tusnakt Taɣellawt, yiwen yettacen aṭas yelha, —ur nemmeslay ɣef taɣect teqcicḥant, neɣ ɣef cna azdayan—, s tideț ur yezmer ad iɛeddi iman-is; iteqqad deg yezri...

Yiwen yettwaḥǧeb s tezlatin tixsan ur yezmir ad ibeddel Tissa n Ugama-s; ur yezmir ad iɛeddi wayen yella.

Aɣellaḍ ɣer Tissa tella teɣzi n ugama, ilaq ad neǧǧ akken nella; ḥwaǧen-t ad ur nelli akken nella.

Ma nḍel akken nella, ur nezmer ad iɛeddi ɣer Tissa tella teɣzi n ugama.

Deg yigran n tudert d azdayan ttidiren tɣawsiwin ur yettwamenni. Aṭas mi ara yeddukel yiwen d waṭas, ala imi i t-ishel ad t-yacen tacennut-is.

Uɣur ara yelhu tawsit n assaɣen-agi d iɛeddun mi ara yettwanɣal i umcennu ad yessekket, ad ibeddel adisqi, ad yemeslay ɣef tɣawsa-nniḍen, atg.

Dɣa umcennu yessawalen, ad iṛuḥ ad iqenneɛ ameddakel imaynuten, yiwen yeqqnen ad t-isel ɣef tallit ur nezmir ad nḥaseb.

Amedyez ittraǧu umcennu, yiwen ad t-yemmezwer, akken yella yessel aṭas ad yemmezwer yiwen waṭas.

Ad yemmezwer yiwen waṭas ilaq ad yemmezwer iman-is.

Uɣur ara yelhu umcennu yelha i ggullen yemmezwer iman-is.

Aṭas n imcennuten yettwaɣen qqaren tacennut ur yemmezwerren d ḥellmen s ddunit teqcicḥant ideg d nutni i d iferrajen tella teɣzi n ugama.

Acu kan, ur yelli aṭas n imcennuten d medden, daɣen llan wid yettwassenfaren; ur ccen tacennut-nsen deg wudem, maca ssufɣen-tt deg uffer.

D medden xedmen aṭas, ɛeddan aṭas, ttḥussun ttwaɣen, ixemmem belli tudert t-ixenṣen ayen i yellan ur d-yeɣli ad t-id-ggen.

Ttḥussun s talɣa d tumat n tuɣmest, issiwlen n leɣwba d uxsin s lexwaṣ, taɛyi tamat neɣ timɣura id yellan gar-asen yettwaqbalent tummeslayin.

Ur yelli ccekk tezlatin tesseɣlent iberdan deg umecwar n ussidmer amenẓu n Ugama.

Uɣur ara yelhu, tezlatin-agi timat id yettwaffren, ɛeddan ur t-id-ḥussun i iman-nsen ala mi ara ten-nerr ɣer wudem.

D ayen iban yal udem i yiman-is, yeǧǧ ad ikcem tafat i iman-is, deg tumat-is.

Kra n ubeddel amenẓu ad yella deg teṛwiḥt-nneɣ ala mi ara yettwaṣegger i tafat n udem i yiman-is.

Ilaq aɣ-nleqqem i iman-nneɣ nella wedjen, akken nella assaɣ d medden.

Mi ara yella yiwen wedjen, "Yugen" yettwabeddlent, tummeslayin yettwabeddlent, tagnitwat tarugant, atg., d tid yettwabeddlent.

Ur yelli daymen i tella gar-neɣ, yal i tella gar-neɣ wedjen. D ayen imezdi kan, d ugama kan, ad yella gar-neɣ ur nelli d medden ɣer taddart. "Yugen" yettwaḥsben d yettwaɣen ttidiren mi ara nelli wedjen.

Ma nebɣa ad nebdel deg usugen nneɣ ḥwaǧen-t ad nseɣrew i uɣilif-nneɣ.

Aṭas n tikkal nessawaḍ i uɣilif-nneɣ deg tezlatin id-yessalin neɣ ur nessalay ara.
