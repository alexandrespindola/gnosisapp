Yella d ayen iwalan yerna ur yewɛir ara ad t-tefhemḍ, melmi ara yebdu yiwen ad iɛass iman-is s tidet ɣef tmuɣli ur nelli d yiwen meɛna d aṭas, yebda s tidet ad ixdem ɣef ayen akk yesɛa deg-s.

D ugur, d asensaɣ, d tsebba, i lxedma n Usenqed Uḍebsi i iman, d uguren imeskanen: Tikerkas (Lḥella n Waɛli, ad ț-țamneḍ d Rebbi) Timserɣust (Ad țamneḍ deg "nekk" yettwamɣaren; ad tɛebdeḍ yal assaɣ n Alter-Ego) Tafrasit. (Timussni, ad ț-țamneḍ iman-ik d win yettawin, ad ț-țimɣureḍ, ad ț-țamneḍ ur tɣelleḍ ara, d zzux aseɣlani, d win ur nezmir ad yettwali tmuɣli n wiyaḍ)

Melmi ara tkemmleḍ s laman ur yesɛi lmeɛna belli d yiwen i telliḍ, belli tesɛiḍ "nekk" yettwamɣaren, yella d ayen yugar ayen ur yezmir ara lxedma s tidet ɣef iman-ik.

Win yettamnen dima d yiwen, ur yezmir ara ad yeffeɣ ɣef tɣawsiwin-is ur neḥmil ara. Ad iḥseb yal afriwen, awal, arzu, ayen yessḥassen, akukru, tukksa, atg., atg., atg., am tɣawsiwin yemxallafen, ur țbeddilent ara, n lɛadda-s yerna ad isqeddec iman-is zdat n wiyaḍ dɣa ad yeqqar belli d uguren-agi d iwersiyen...

Win iqeblen tameslayt n aṭas n "nekk", ad yefhem ɣef asenqed belli yal arzu, awal, tɛekkumt, akukru, atg., yeqqen ɣer "nekk"-agi neɣ wayeḍ yemxallafen...

Yal aserkal n Usenqed Uḍebsi i iman, yexdem s tidet deg iman-is yerna yettḥawal ad yessusem seg Tfekka-s tɣawsiwin ur neḥmil ara yesɛa deg-s...

Ma yella win s tidet yebda ad iɛass iman-is ɣer daxel, ad yettwabḍu ɣef sin: Aɛessas d Win Yetwaɛassen.

Ma yella tabḍit-agi ur tḍerru ara, d ayen iwalan belli ur nezmir ara ad nekcem s axxam deg iberdan yessewhamen n Tussna n Yiman.

Amek ara nezmir ad nɛass iman-nneɣ ma nɛedda aɣalat ur neḥwaj ad nebḍu gar Aɛessas d Win Yetwaɛassen?

Ma yella tabḍit-agi ur tḍerru ara, d ayen iwalan belli ur nezmir ara ad nekcem s axxam deg ubrid n Tussna n Yiman.

Ulac ccekk melmi tabḍit-agi ur tḍerru ara, nkemmel nettwaqnen ɣer yal tɣawsiwin n "nekk" Yettwagerrzen...

Win yettwaqnen ɣer tɣawsiwin yemxallafen n "nekk" Yettwagerrzen, dima d ameɣbun n tallalt.

Amek ara ibeddel tallalt win ur neɛri iman-is? Amek ara iɛer iman-is win ur yeɛsisa iman-is ɣer daxel? Amek ara yezmir yiwen ad iɛass iman-is ma yella ur yettwabḍu ara d Aɛessas d Win Yetwaɛassen?

Tura, ulac win yezmir ad ibeddel ɣer leɛmer-is ma yella ur yezmir ara ad yeqqar: "Arzu-yagi d "nekk" aɣersiw ilaq ad t-nekkser"; "Afriwen-agi yellan s timserɣust d "nekk" wayeḍ i yi-iɛerrqen yerna ḥwaǧeɣ ad t-swehḥiɣ"; "Ayen yessḥassan i yettjeraḥen ul-iw d "nekk" yekkren i ḥwaǧeɣ ad t-rreɣ d aɣebbar adigag"; atg., atg., atg.

D ayen iwalan belli ayagi ur yezmir ara i win ur yettwabḍu gar Aɛessas d Win Yetwaɛassen.

Win yettarran yal tɣawsiwin-is Tfekkawin am tɣawsiwin n "nekk" Yiwen, Yeddes yerna Yetwamɣaren, yettwaqen i akk aɣlatan-is, yesɛa-ten yeqqnen ɣer iman-is, ɣef wayagi yeǧǧa tizemmar ad yebḍu seg Tfekka-s.

D ayen iwalan belli wid-agi ur zmiren ara ad beddelen ɣer leɛmer-nsen, d imdanen yettwaḥukmen ɣer tserɣint tameqqrant.
