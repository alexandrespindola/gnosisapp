Yettwabḍaḥ mliḥ yerna ur yettiɛeb ara ad tfehmeḍ belli mara ad yebdu yiwen ad iɛass iman-is s tidet si tama n win ur nelli d yiwen meɛna d aṭas, itebdaɛ di tidet ad yexdem ɣef ayen akk i d-yettawi deg-s.

D abuddu, d uɣurru, d ugur, i lxedma n Uzelluɣ Intim Iman-is, tawaɣit timegziyin agi: Amitumani (Ddelɛ n Umeqqran, ad ttaruḍ iman-ik d Aqliq), Tagulatrit (Ad amneḍ s Yiwen Udayim; ad tɛebdeḍ yal asside n Alter-Egu), Paranwiya (Tamusni, Tukfifin, Tamellayt, Ad ttaruḍ iman-ik ur yetɣellit, zzux n usirem, win ur nezmir ad iẓer tmuɣli n wiyaḍ).

Mara ad tekkkem di tmuɣli ur nesseɛna lmeɛna belli Nekk d yiwen, belli yesɛa Yiwen Udayim, iḍegger-d ayen kter n lɣerba ɣef lxedma s tidet ɣef yiman-ik. Win yettarun iman-is d Yiwen, ur yezmir ara ad yexḍu i tɣawsiwin-is ur neţɣilen. Ad iɛeddi yal tmuɣli, tamussni, lebɣi, imettawen, leεnaya, tayri, atg., atg., atg., am tikliwin yemxalafen, ur yettbeddelen, n ugama-s yiman-is yerna ad iqenneɛ zdat wiyaḍ iteddun-d tawaɣit agi neɣ agi n yiman-is d tidet yerzan dderya…

Win yettqebilen Tlɣuɣa n Waṭas n Yiwanes, yettfehem ɣef leɛnaya belli yal lebɣi, tmuɣli, tikli, leɛnaya, atg., tḍegger-d i Wa agi neɣ wayeḍ yemxalafen, yettbeddilen… Yal anesbaɣur n Uzelluɣ Intim iman-is, yexdem s tidet deg-s yiman-is yerna iqeddem amur-is ad yeqqeɛ si taɣawsa-s timegziyin timegziyin ur neɣilen i d-yettawi deg-s…

Ma yiwen s tidet yerna s wul-is yebdaɛ ad iɛass deg-s, iṭṭef iman-is ad yeqqim d sin: Amɛiessu d win iɛussen. Ma abḍi agi ur yettili ara, yettwabḍaḥ belli ur nezmir ara ad nekcem taxxamt ɣer Ubrid yessegmaɣen n Tukti Iman-is. Amek ara nezmir ad nɛasses iman-nneɣ ma nḍegger tɣellit n ur neɣri ad nebḍu gar Amɛiessu d Win iɛussen?

Ma abḍi agi ur yettili ara, yettwabḍaḥ belli ur nezmir ara ad nekcem taxxamt ɣer ubrid n Tukti Iman-is. Ur yettṛaggu ara, mara abḍi agi ur yettili ara, nekcem-d nettqadar yakk tikliwin n Nekk yettwafraren… Win yettqedren yakk tikliwin n Nekk yettwafraren, itettɣuṛṛu yal ass i tewtilin.

Amek ara yezmir ad ibeddel tewtilin win ur nessin iman-is? Amek ara yezmir ad yessen iman-is win ur yeɛsiss ara deg-s? Amek ara yezmir yiwen ad iɛassess iman-is ma ur yebḍi ara zik gar Amɛiessu d Win iɛussen?

Tura, ur yezmir yiwen ad ibeddel s tɣawsa kan mara yezmer ad yeqqar: "Lebɣi agi d Nekk aɣersiw i yessefk ad skecmeɣ"; "Tmuɣli agi tɛettil d Nekk wayeḍ yettɛeṭṭalen i yiman-iw yerna yessefk ad mesbeɣ"; "Tamussni agi tettɣimi-d ul-iw d Nekk i d-ikecmen i yessefk ad rreɣ d aɣebbar asennan"; atg., atg., atg. D tidet, agi ur yettili ara i win ur yebḍin ara gar Amɛiessu d Win iɛussen.

Win yettawin yakk tikliwin-is timegziyin am tikliwin n Yiwen, Yiman yerna Udayim, yettwafraren deg-s yakk tɣellitin-is, yesɛa-ten yakk yettmunen ɣur-s, iɣell-d ɣef yiman-is lḥeqq ad yexḍu seg-sen si taɣawsa-s. D tidet, irgazen agi ur zemren ad beddelen s tɣawsa kan, d irgazen yettwaḥettmen ɣer lɣerba s waṭas.
