21 MARS AR 20 YEBRIR

Llanen rebɛa waddaden n TIZZIT i wemdan: IḌES, TIZZIT TAḌSA, TIZZIT TAZDAYT d TIZZIT TUVIGT.

Tsureḍ, ay ameqqran, axxam yesɛan rebɛa leɛliwat. Aɣersiw AMSAV yettusemman D-AMDAN yettidir s umata deg sin n leɛliwat n wadda, maca ur yettsexdam ara lebda sin n leɛliwat n ufella.

Aɣersiw AMSAV yeqqisem tudert-is i d-yettawin lemriḍ d tawaɣit gar y ides amatu d waddad yettusemman TAḌSA, i d-yettilin s tuffɣa d tamiḍi n y iḍes.

Mi ara y iṭṭes tfekka tafizikit deg wurtiw, IGU yettwakken deg TFUKA-INES TIZIRI yettedddu s tizzit yettgen am win yettḍurun am win yettḥerrikhen s tlelli deg tama tamikrulart. IGU deg tama tamikrulart yettalas IDES d yettidir deg-sen, ur yelli ara usenges deg IDES-INES, tessek, sebba, aratak, yal tigawin n TMEKKAL ttxedment mbla amsal-ines, ttbanent d ttgujrent tigawin tilaɣmawin, tixutaɛiwin tiberkanin, ur ṣehḥant ara, atg.

Mi ara yuɣal IGU yettwakken deg TFUKA-INES TIZIRI ar TFUKKA TAFIZIKIT, yettuɣal-d waddad wis sin n tizzit yettusemman waddad n TAḌSA, i d-yettilin deg temel d tamiḍi n y iḍes.

Mi ara yuɣal IGU ar TFUKKA-INES TAFIZIKIT, yettkemmil y ides deg tlemmast, wadad yettusemman TADDART N TAḌSA d SOÑAR DESPIERTO s tidet.

Mi ara yefɣed yiṭij, ttuffɣent yitran, maca ur ttgujurent ara; akka i d-ttilin y ides deg waddad n taḍsa, ttkemmilen s tuffra, ur ttgujurent ara.

Ayagi yebgen belli Aɣersiw AMSAV yettusemman D-AMDAN yettidir kan deg amaḍal n y ides; s ṣṣeḥ imla-d Umaru belli tudert d iḍes.

Aɣersiw Aqellel yettseḥḥad tiɛerrabin yettḍuru, yettxeddem deg tmezdaɣt, deg uɣerbaz, deg iger, atg., yettḍuru, yettɛeceq deg y ides, yezweǧ deg y ides; waqila, waqila aṭas deg tudert, yella yuḍen, yettidir deg amaḍal n y ides d yumen s timmad-is belli yuḍen.

Arbaɛ Evangiles d-ttalaben ASYUD, maca s tuffɣa ur d-qqaren ara amek ara NEYYEḌ.

Qbel kullec, ilaq ad nefhem belli neslaḍ; kan mi ara yefhem yiwen belli yeslaḍ, yekcem s tidet deg ubrid n USYUD.

Win ara yewweḍ ar USYUD N TIZZIT, yettuɣal D-AUTO-CONSCIENTE, yettagmam TIZZIT S TIMMAD-IS.

Tuccḍa takemlalt n aṭas n PSEUDO ESOTERISTAS d PSEUDO OCULTISTAS iduqqaɛen, d usemɣer n AUTO-CONSCIENTES d umen ukud belli amaḍal merra yuḍen, belli akk iglanen agmamen AUTO-CONCIENCIA.

Lemmer akk iglanen sɛan TIZZIT YUḌEN Tamurt ad tili d lijenna, ur yelli ara deg-s imennaɣen, ur yelli ara d aɛyiw-inu d aɛyiw-ik, kullec ad yeqqen i kullec, ad neddir deg EDAD DE ORO.

Mi ara yewweḍ yiwen ar USYUD N TIZZIT, mi ara yettuɣal D-AUTO-CONSCIENTE, mi ara yettagmam TIZZIT S TIMMAD-IS, dɣa mi ara yessen tidet fell-as.

Qbel ad yewweḍ ar waddad wis tlata n TIZZIT (AUTO-CONCIENCIA), yiwen ur yessen tidet S TIMMAD-IS, ɣas akken yumen belli yessen-it.

D asertan ad yettagmam waddad wis tlata n tizzit, ad yeqqel ɣer leɛliwa wis tlata n uxxam, qbel ad yesɛu izref ad yessuffeɣ ɣer leɛliwa wis rebɛa.

WADDAD WIS REBɛA N TIZZIT, LEɛLIWA WIS REBɛA n uxxam, d FORMIDABLE s tidet. Kan win ara yewweḍ ar TIZZIT TUVIGT, ar WADDAD WIS REBɛA, yezmer ad yeles tiɣawsiwin deg-sen, amaḍal amek yettilin.

Win ara yewweḍ ɣer leɛliwa wis rebɛa n uxxam, yella barra n kullec ILLUMINADO, yessen s tamsegla tadrimt n MISTERIOS DE LA VIDA Y DE LA MUERTE, yagmam ASSAƐA, asengers-is ESPACIAL yettwaqeddcen akk.

Deg IḌES ADERRAN nezmer ad nesɛu tiɣimi n WADDAD N TAḌSA. Deg WADDAD N TAḌSA nezmer ad nesɛu tiɣimi n AUTO-CONCIENCIA. Deg WADDAD N AUTO-CONCIENCIA nezmer ad nesɛu tiɣimi n TIZZIT TUVIGT.

Ma nebɣa ad newweḍ ar USYUD N TIZZIT, ar AUTO-CONCIENCIA, ilaq ad nexdem akken TIZZIT dagi d tura. Dɣa deg amaḍal afiziki dagi ideg ilaq ad nexdem akken ara NESSYEḌ TIZZIT, win ara yuḍen dagi ad yuḍen deg akk tmura, deg akk tigawin n Universo.

ORGANISMO HUMAN d ZODÍACO VIVIENTE d deg yal yiwen seg tleɛɛac n tmezdayin-is, yettider yiḍes s tiddi.

D aɣersiw ad nessyeḍ tizzit deg yal yiwen seg tleɛɛac n tmezdayin n organismo umano d ɣef waya i d-ttilin tixdamin zodiacales.

Aries, yettḥekkem ɣef uqerruy; Taurus, ɣef urẓi; Géminis, ɣef iɣalen, iḍarren d tegnatin; Cáncer, ɣef teɣzert timo; Leo, ɣef ul; Virgo, ɣef udris, tiberdanin; Libra, ɣef tiɣitatin; Escorpio, ɣef tmeɣriyin timeɣdurin; Sagitario, ɣef tiselɣiwin timuzɣunin; Capricornio, ɣef tgecrar; Acuario, ɣef tɛeṣṣabt; Piscis, ɣef iḍarren.

D tawaɣit s tidet belli zodíaco viviente yagi n MICRO-CÓSMOS amdan, yettider s tiddi. Yettwaxdam ad yettwaɛɛed ɣef azal n SUPER-ESFUERZOS, USYUD N TIZZIT deg yal yiwen seg tleɛɛac n SIGNOS ZODIACALES-NEɣ.

Tafawet d Tizzit d sin n tilawin n yiwet n teɣawsa; ar tazwara tameẓẓuɣt n Tizzit, tameẓẓuɣt n tafawet; ar tazwara tameqqrant n Tizzit, tameqqrant n tafawet.

Neḥwaǧ ad NESSYEḌ TIZZIT akken ad yeččur d tḥerci d ad yesteqsi yal yiwen seg tleɛɛac n tmezdayin n zodíaco MICRO-CÓSMICO-NEɣ. Zodíaco-NEɣ merra ad yettuɣal d tafawet d tilelli.

Lexdem akken Zodíaco-NEɣ yekcem s tidet akken ARIES. Yesseɣres DISCÍPULO deg ufus yessawaḍen s aqelleb d uqelleb yessebɣas d deg uqelleb n yidmaɛen. Yessebɣes ameddakw-is uɣalent iḍen-is akken ur yella ara deg umaḍal ara t-yeɣleb, yessureḍ belli tafawet tazdayt n ARIES tesseɣres aqerruy-is, yeqqim deg waddad n tɣuri ayagi akken yebɣa yerna ad yecnu Mantram AUM yessebɣas mliḥ timi s A, ad yettwaqeddec s U d ad t-yerr s M idesen.

Mantram AUM yessawaḍen deg timmad-is d tellaɣza TERRIBLEMENTE DIVINA, acku yettalas tizerza n BAB, aṭas i yuɣalen, n AƐARRAS aṭas i yettwaqadren d n ESPÍRITU SANTO aṭas yessen. La vocal A tettelqem tizerza n BAB, la vocal U tettelqem tizerza n AƐARRAS, la vocal M tettelqem tizerza n ESPÍRITU SANTO. AUM d MANTRAM LOGICO yessawaḍen.

Ameddakw ilaq ad yecnu MANTRAM yessawaḍen yagi s rebɛa tikkal deg tixdimt yagi n ARIES yerna ad yeddem-d amkan yessebɣas ufus-is ayeffus akken ad yezzi aqerruy-is s sebɛa tikkal ar zdat, sebɛa ar deffir, sebɛa ad yezzi s azzal ayeffus, sebɛa ad yezzi s azzal ayezdeɣ s tixeddimt n tafawet n ARIES tettxeddem deg tlemmast n uqerruy tesseɣras tiɣlin pineal d pituitaria i d-yettaken azref n tazɣint n DIMENSIONES SUPERIORES DEL ESPACIO.

D aɣersiw belli tafawet n ARIES ttwajettent deg tlemmast n uqerruy-nneɣ tesseɣras TIZZIT, tesseɣras azmizen tiserɣanin yettwakken deg TIƔLIN PITUITARIA d PINEAL.

ARIES d assegget n RA, RAMA, iɣul. MANTRAM RA yessawaḍen, mi ara yettwanu amek yettilin, yessebɣas times espinales d tleɛɛac n umerkezan imuqqaḥan n espina dorsal.

ARIES d asegget zodiacal n tmes, yagmam tazmert tesseɣras d MICRO-CÓSMOS AMDAN yettallas-it akken yebɣa yettxemmim, yettḥussu d yettqelles.

HITLER, i d-yettilin nativo de ARIES, yettsexdam tigawt yagi n tazmert deg temsalt tamerḍelt, maca, ilaq ad nessen belli deg uzwir, qbel ad yexdem aɣleb n uzar d ad yettalas ar temɣart ar tegrawla tis snat n umaḍal, yettsexdam tigawt n tazmert n ARIES deg temsalt tajetment, yessuli aseɣẓan n tudert n PUEBLO ALEMÁN.

Nezmer ad nessen s tamsegla tadrimt belli nativos de ARIES ttnaɣen aṭas akken yezweǧ neɣ tzeǧǧuǧ.

Nativos de ARIES sɛan tesseɣra taḍeqliwt ar tnaɣ yella aṭas yettnaɣen deg wanzaren.

Nativos de ARIES tthussun ttuzemmeren ad ttellqmen deg tɣersiwin timeqqranin d ad ten-yessuffɣen ar ṣṣeḥ.

Yella deg nativos de ARIES aɣleb aɣersiw n uḥareb n usexdem lebda tazmert n uray deg temsalt tagmamat, estilo HITLER, ANTISOCIAL d tamerḍelt.

Ar nativos de ARIES tεeğben-asen aṭas tudert tazdayt, maca aṭas n ARIANOS tḥemmlen armi d ur yelli ara tazdayt.

Deg leɛnaya n ARIANOS ttwalint tamenna, tameɣrara deg tmiman-nsen, tamegṛa d azal yessawaḍen d aɣleb.

Aɣersiw n ARIES d HIERRO, azru, RUBÍ, akal, ROJO, anẓul, FUEGO.

Ar nativos de ARIES yettuɣal zzwaǧ d iglanen n LIBRA, acku times d uḍu yettḥusan mliḥ.

Ma nativos de ARIES bɣan ad ilin deg zzwaǧ, ilaq ad fukken akken aɣleb n wurba.
