22 MAYU AR 21 YUNYU

Aɣerbaz d tazzla, ayen ara yawḍen ɣer usirem n tamusni. Amdya: tetteddum deg webrid s tmuqranit; tettemlillim deg ubrid-nni akked tmeɣra; țɛeggiḍen lɣaci, țmeslayen yiselwayen n wegdud, tteḍraḍent icenyalen deg wehwa, lɣaci uɣalen am yumerḍlen, kul wa yetmeslay, kul wa yetɛeggiḍ.

Tameɣra-nni tecbeḥ; tuɣalem tettut kullec, tuɣalem tettemlillim d lɣaci, awalen n wid yettekkan deg-s țețțamen-kkun.

Tecbeḥ tameɣra-nni u tettuɣalem iman-nwen, tettemlillim d tameɣra-nni deg webrid, ur tettxemmemim ɣef kra nniḍen, tetɛejjibem, tura teɣlim ɣer usirem n tamusni; tettemlillim akked lɣaci yettɛeggiḍen, ula d kunwi tettɛeggiḍem u tettḍeggirem ɣer idɣaɣen d rruṣyat; tettuɣalem tețrejjem, tettutum anwa i tellim, tettutum kullec.

Ad nefk tura amdya yifen wayiḍ: Tellim deg texxamt-nwen teqqimem zdat tilifizyu, tbanent tifelwatin n wid yettekkan deg umezgun, tella ddwa, tecbaḥ tilifizyu, ayen ara yawḍen ɣer tezzi n tmuɣli-nwen, tettuɣalem iman-nwen, tettɛeggiḍem s tferḥat, tettemlillim akked wid yettekkan deg umezgun, akked ddwa.

Tazzla tura tecbaḥ, ur tettxemmemim ɣef iman-nwen, teɣlim ɣer usirem d aɣezfan, deg imir-nni tebɣam kan ad tẓrem lefreḥ n urgaz yellan deg tilifizyu, deg imir-nni tebɣam kan taɛeqqabt i yellan deg-s.

llan wid ara yawḍen ɣer tazzla d usirem. Lɣaci tettemlillim akked wid yellan dinna, tiktiwin d kullec ara yawḍen ɣer tazzla d usirem.

Lɣaci ttidiren s tamusni yellan teṭṭes, xedmen ttxemmimem, seqdacen tikarṛubin ttxemmimem, ula d anwa ara yenɣen wid i teddun deg iberdan ttxemmimem, tɛebban deg ixemmimen-nsen.

Deg ssɛayat iqerbsen deg tfekka, l-EGO (nekk) iteffeɣ seg tfekka u yettawi usirem-is anda ara yeddu. M'ara yuɣal ɣer tfekka, m'ara yekcem ɣer usirem n ussusen, yettafa-d usirem-is u yettɛeddi tudert-is ttxemmimem.

Wid yemmuten tteggan ad llint, maca l-EGO, Nekk, yeqqim deg temnaḍin timsuḍaf ɣer deffir n lmut. Deg ssɛa n lmut l-EGO yettegg usirem-is, d nnuba-s u yettidir deg umaḍal n wid yemmuten d usirem-is, yettɛeddi ttxemmimem, d tamusni teṭṭes, yettaḍ deg webrid am wa yeṭṭes.

Win yebɣan ad issussu tamusni yessefk ad tt-ixeddem da d tura. Nesɛa tamusni tettekk deg tfekka u ɣef wayi nesseqdeq-tt da d tura. Win yessenḥuy tamusni da deg umaḍal-agi yessenḥuy deg umaḍalen.

Win yessenḥuy tamusni deg umaḍal-agi, yessenḥuy deg temnaḍin ṛebɛa, semmus, seddis d sa.

Win yebɣan ad yidir s tamusni deg umaḍalen, yessefk ad issussu da d tura.

Lǧil n lexbar yella ɣef wayi issusem, maca lɣaci ur fahmen.

Lɣaci teṭṭes u tețțamen belli ssusmen, m'ara yesɛeddi yiwen deg-sen belli yeṭṭes, d lemtel yellan da issusem.

Yewɛer ad d-tefham yiwen belli tamusni-s teṭṭes, lɣaci ur qebblen ara tideț-agi belli ttidiren teṭṭesen.

Win yebɣan ad issussu tamusni yessefk ad yeqqedim deg imir deg imir tamuɣli i iman-is.

Aqli ad yeqqel iman-is deg imir deg imir, d axeddim yetɛebban.

Yekfa yiwen n imir, yiwen n wussan ad d-iṭṭes.

Yessefk ad nẓer ixemmimen, iḥulfan, arayen, idles, timila, tilisa.

Kull ixemmem, kull aḥulfu, kull aḥerrak, kull axeddim, kul ttilisa yessefk ad tettuẓrem deg yiman m'ara ḥerken deg tebɣest nneɣ; kra n twenza deg tezzi teqqen akken ad neɣli deg usirem n tamusni.

Aṭas n tikal tteddum deg webrid tețɛebbem deg ixemmimen-nwen, tettemlillim d ixemmimen-nni, tetɛejjibem, ttxemmimem, deg yiwen n imir yettɛeddi yiwen umeddakkel, yeḥya-kkun, ur t-tteǧǧibim ara ḥaca imi ur t-tẓerem ara, tețrajjam, yettɣezzeb umeddakkel, yettamen belli ur tessinedim ara, umma tella tɣezzibem, umeddakkel-nni yettɛeddi ttxemmimem, lukan yella issusem ur yettbeddil ara axemmem-is, ad yaf belli teṭṭesem.

Aṭas n tikal tettɣellitem deg tewwurt d tettdukkilem anda ur yessefk ara ad teddukkilem, imi teṭṭesem.

Tetteddum deg tkarrubt deg temdint, ilaq ad tersum deg yiwen webrid, maca tettemlillim, tetɛejjibem, ttxemmimem akked tnezzut deg uɣerbaz-nwen, neɣ akked imekti, neɣ akked tayri, deg yiwen n imir tẓerm belli teɛddam abrid, teqqinem tkarrubt u tuɣalem-d ɣer deffir kra n iberdan.

Yewɛer ad teqqimem s susen deg imir deg imir maca d ayen ilaqen.

M'ara nessen ad nedder s susen deg imir deg imir, ad neǧǧ ad nɛeddi ttxemmimem da u ɣer deffir n tfekka.

Ilaq ad nessen belli lɣaci m'ara teṭṭes tteffɣen seg tfekkiwin-nsen, maca ttawin usirem-nsen, ttidiren deg umaḍalen ttxemmimem u m'ara uɣalen ɣer tfekka, yettɛeddi usirem-nsen.

M'ara yesnen yiwen ad yidir s susen deg imir ɣer imir, ad yeǧǧ ad yɛeddi da u deg umaḍalen.

Ilaq ad nessen belli l-EGO (nekk) yella d lqaleb deg tfekkiwin-ines, yetteffeɣ seg tfekka m'ara teṭṭes tfekka, maca l-EGO yettidir teṭṭes deg umaḍalen.

Deg tfekkiwin-nni tella ula d l-ESENCIA, ALMA, FRACCIÓN DE ALMA, BUDHATA, tamusni. Tamusni-nni yessefk ad nessenḥuy da d tura.

Da deg umaḍal-agi nesɛa tamusni, da yessefk ad nssenḥuy, ma yella nebɣa ad neǧǧ ad nɛeddi ttxemmimem u ad nedder s tamusni deg umaḍalen.

Argaz s tamusni yella deg ssus deg teqqimt-is, yettidir, yexdem, yetɛeddi s tamusni deg umaḍalen.

Argaz s tamusni ur yesɛi ara uguren n ussuḍaf, ugur n ussuḍaf ɣer leɛnaya d winna d win yettɛeddin deg tesṭunǧujt.

Argaz issussan ur yettɛebbaɛ ara ad yessen ad yessaḍ, yettidir s tamusni deg umaḍalen, teṭṭes tfekka-s.

Argaz issussan ur yettɛeddi ara ttxemmimem, deg tegrest yettidir deg temnaḍin anda d-ttɛeddin lɣaci ttxemmimem, maca d tamusni tella s susen.

Argaz issussan yeqqen ɣer LOGIA BLANCA, iẓra temziɣen n GRAN FRATERNIDAD UNIVERSAL BLANCA, yettemlil akked GURÚ-DEVA, m'ara teṭṭes tfekka.

Tamuɣli ɣer iman seg imir ɣer imir tennernay tissas, dɣa nezmer ad neẓer usirem n lɣaci deg webrid.

Tissas tella deg iman-is, tiṭ, imeẓẓuɣen, ancam, taɛeqqabt. Tissas d axeddim n tamusni tella s susen.

CHACRAS, s wacu yettmeslay tira n ucḍaḍ, d wacu yeqqen ɣer tissas, d acu i d-yella deg ufus, d wacu i d-yellan deg tfukt.

Ma yella tamuɣli ɣer iman seg imir ɣer imir, d wacu ara yessenḥuyen tamusni, d wacu ara yessnen ad yesseqdec tamuɣli.

Inelmaden n GNÓSTICOS yessefk ad ssen ad bḍun tamuɣli deg tlata n uḥric: SUJETO, OBJETO, LUGAR.

SUJETO. Ur teɣli ara deg tuṭṭu n yiman zdat kra.

OBJETO. Ad tẓerm amek tella kra, kra ara yettbanen, kra ara yedrun, kra ara yesɛan azal, mbla tuṭṭu n yiman.

LUGAR. Azrar n tmuɣli n temnaḍin anda nella, aqli ad nesteqsi iman-nneɣ: Anwa temnaḍt i d-tella?, Acuɣer lliɣ da?

Deg-s LUGAR, yessefk ad nerr tamuɣli ɣer L-DIMENSIÓN, ad d-yili lḥal ad naf iman-nneɣ deg temnaḍt ṛebɛa neɣ semmus n tebɛa deg imir n tmuɣli; aql-aɣ ad nektu belli tebɛa tesɛa sa n temnaḍin.

Deg umaḍal-agi yella kan azrar n tɣerɣit. Deg temnaḍin n tebɛa, tella kan kanun n LEVITACIÓN.

M'ara nwali taɣiwant, ur yessefk ara ad nettu kanun n sa n temnaḍin, neqqin ad nesteqsi iman-nneɣ: Deg anwa temnaḍt lliɣ?, u ilaq, ama d tayṣimt, ad d-neskuddi kan cwiṭ akken ad nerfed deg waɣas. Ma nerfed uɣur aql-aɣ deg ɣer deffir n tfekka. Ur yessefk ara ad nettu belli m'ara teṭṭes tfekka, l-EGO s tfekkiwin yella deg teṛṛum u l-ESENCIA, yettidir am wa yeṭṭes.

Bḍu n TEZZI gar SUJETO, OBJETO, LUGAR, iweṣṣi ɣer ususem n tamusni.

Aṭas n inelmaden n GNÓSTICOS m'ara ɛuḍen axeddim-agi, a BḍU n TEZZI deg tlata n yidgan, ɣer yesteqsiyen-agi, deg usirem n ussusen, m'ara llin deg umaḍalen.

D ayen yellan da belli axeddim yeddan seg imir ɣer imir, ara yuɣalen d taɣawsa, kra ara yellan deg iɣerruba n uɣerbaz, ad yuɣal-d deg tessut, m'ara nili ɣer deffir n tfekka u lefreḥ d ususem n tamusni.

GÉMINIS d tagrest, teqqen ɣer PLANETA MERCURIO. GÉMINIS yettḥekkem ɣef tuɣmas, idurar d akkren.

PRÁCTICA. Deg SIGNO ZODIACAL DE GÉMINIS, inelmaden n Gnósticos yessefk ad qerben deg teɣmest u ad refden tfekka. Dɣa ad tenfsem tiẓɣelt s semmus n tikal u ad teffɣem tiẓɣelt s semmus n tikal, m'ara tenfsem ad tecḍem belli tiṭṭ yellan tura deg tɣellist tettɛawan deg iberdan. M'ara tenfsem ad teldiḍ akkren d tuɣmas ɣer yeffus d zelmaḍ, m'ara teffɣem ad tettkellitem akkren d tuɣmas.

Aɣeddu n GÉMINIS d MERCURIO, azṛu d BERILO ORO, ini d AMARILLO.

Wid yeddan deg GÉMINIS ḥemmlen aṭas iberdan, xellḍen m'ara ḥeqren leɛnaya, bɣan ad ḥellen kullec s uɣerbaz, teɣḍiben s leɛnaya, d tidirent, yettbeddilen, zeɣzen, yettɛejjiben, tudert-nsen teččur d lefreḥ d twenza, yesɛan azal.

Wid yeddan deg GÉMINIS uguren-nsen d win yellan yellan d DOBLE PERSONALIDAD ara yawḍen deg Griegos d CASTOR y PÓLUX.

Argaz yellan deg GÉMINIS ur yessin ara amek ara yeddu, d DOBLE PERSONALIDAD.

Deg yiwen imir ara yili argaz yellan deg GÉMINIS d ameddakkel, d win ara yefken tudert-is ɣef tdukkla, ɣef argaz yettḥemmel, maca deg imir nniḍen, yezmer ad ixeddem kra nniḍen ɣef argaz-nni.

Tawilt yellan deg GÉMINIS teɛdel u ɣef wayi ur yessefk ara tdukkla.

Ugur yellan deg wid yeddan deg GÉMINIS, d win yettɛawan ad yehzen axeddim.

Wid yeddan deg CASTOR d PÓLUX. Tessinem, deg tebɛa taɣiwant d wacu yellan deg taɣiwant, tizegzewt, leḥmu, tafat, trisiti, timessi yellan yellan d tid yefsen.

Leḥluf n GÉMINIS yella vital deg COSMOGÉNESIS. Taɣiwant tella d tafukt yellan taɛrurt yettwasekmer alama tella d tagrest.

Akk a beddalen-agi deg tebɛa ddan akken CASTOR d PÓLUX.

Deg tallit-agi tudert tuɣal ɣer ABSOLUTO u taɣiwant bedda tettbeddel ɣer TIZIZWAT. Nekwni nnenna belli deg taɣiwant TIRA d aɣref, azrar n taɣiwant yettnernay.

Seg tasusent nezmer ad nini belli CASTOR d PÓLUX d leṛwaḥ.

Win yellan deg YIMAN, deg kuleɛɛdi, yesɛa snat leṛwaḥ, ESPIRITUAL, d HUMANA.

Deg ANIMAL INTELECTUAL yella YIMAN, YIMAN ur yettlal ara, maca yella ittawi kul waɣerbaz, ESENCIA; d FRACCIÓN n ALMA HUMANA; BUDHATA.

Yessefk ad nessen belli BUDHATA, ESENCIA, tella deg tfekkiwin.

Aqli ad nini ESENCIA tella s leɛnaya deg tfekka n teṛṛum. Wid yellan deg uzɣar bedden.

Teggugri ɣer umaḍalen teggan kan ad yesnerni tfekkiwin d L EGO, s usɣen. Kan s wayi yessnernay taɣiwant, teffeɣ-d ESENCIA.

Beddu-agi n beddalen n taɣiwant deg TIZIZWAT.

Géminis yella yeqqen ɣer tuɣmas, idurar d tenfes. Hekku yella iɛeqqeb i tebɛa.

TAƔIWAN ula d nettat tettneffes. Taɣiwant tettneffes SULPHUR vital n TEFEKKAKT u dɣa teffeɣ uɣal d SULFHUR; yella yegda argaz yettneffes uɣanim d yetteffeɣ d anhídrido carbónico.

Tira-agi yellan tkeččem u teffeɣ s tebɣest n taɣiwant.
