Este es el grado de introducción a los Estudios Filosóficos Gnósticos o grados externos de la gnosis.

D ayy ar tazedwert i usnifel ɣer Uɣerbaz Afiluzufi Anusṭik neɣ iduraren i berra n tusnakt. D amdan kan ad yebdu yal anelmad s iduriyen-a, imiren ad ikemmel, deg uswir, s iduraren amezwaru, wis sin, wis tlata, atg. Ilzem ad yettwaḥsab mliḥ belli wigi mačči d iduraren izɣermanen inusṭiken. Iduraren izɣermanen inusṭiken yettṭṭef-iten unelmad mi yella ihegga iman-is. Iduraren izɣermanen, wid yellan d iduraren inusṭiken n ṣṣeḥ, ur yezmir yiwen ad ten-yessenɣel imi ten-yuwi; aya yettwagdel. Win ara yeqqar: "nekini ɣur-i aṭas n iduraren, aṭas n tizwiriwin", d amehdal.

Ma yebɣa yiwen ad yeqqel d amhandes, d amuɣal neɣ d ameṭṭbib, atg., ilzem ad iheggi iman-is i waya. Win-a ad iruḥ ɣer uɣerbaz ad iɣer aṭas. Mbeɛd mi yesɛa tamusni taɣawsawt yelhan ɣef yixef anda yeqqar, imiren ad yebdu yal ammud n wayen yelmed. Ammud ittɣimi-d tameqqrant. Iɣella sselmaden, imaɣnasen imeɣranen, issensiyen imeɣranen, atg., ur d-wwiḍen kan ad sɛun tamusni taɣawsawt yelhan ɣef ixefawen n usseqsi-nsen, maca rran tamusni-yagi ɣer ammud. Aɣawas weḥd-s ur yezmir ad d-yawi acemma (anagar aḥric afelsafi i wid t-yefhmen). Illaḥ ammud.

Ɣer asegmi-yagi n tewriqin, ɣer-t s tuggta taḥeqqanit, s unamek ad tlemdeḍ, s unamek ad tessenḍ tusnakt taɛlayant. Maca ɛqel belli ilzem-ak ad tessekcemḍ ɣer ammud wayen tlemed, ma tebɣiḍ ad tawḍeḍ ɣer tameqqrant n Leqdic.
