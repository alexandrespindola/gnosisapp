There are three stages of translation:

1.  **I can't translate a word.** This is the most common reason. If I can't translate a word, I usually can't translate the entire sentence.
2.  **I need more context.** Although some words and sentences can be translated by themselves, a full understanding of the text requires some context. This is especially true for nuance, humor, and satire.
3.  **I can translate everything.** The English is clear and concise, and I know what to do with it.
