Ad-ittwassnen nfs s yiman-nnk gh wawnk, ur illi mani nttfa ayad, mara nra ad nnaf amdanatn gh walln ntfhamt nffusn.

Bla cck, amdanatn gh wawnk nffusn ar ttwasuddan s ifrkiten ittwasuddan.

Mani tlla tudrt gh wawnk tzgga lqadimat lli itzdɣn ɣid, ar nttalb gh tazla mqqurn, ad nsfu kullu amdanatn gh walln ɣur nttafhamt ddan.

Ad nsudd amdanatn gh walln lli ghrn ddan gh wawnk, ayad d-illa gh ukhdam mara nra ad nbdl kullu tigawt n kuynna lli nnkr.

Ad nbdl ismunn nɣ d kuynna, ayad imkn mara nra ad nsfu gh wawnk nɣ kuynna lli gh ddan.

Aḥwalatn gh brra lli ighdmn, ar ttggun ur illi mad tn yghdmn, nɣ ar ttggun ttusbughln s usudd n amdanatn gh wawnk lli gh ghrn ddan.

Yan iskr ad ibdl tigawt n kuynna lli nnkr lli gh ddan, mara ishlul gh wawnk. Win ur ittusuddun amdanatn gh walln lli ghrn ddan, ar ittini ylla drus iqqwan, ar ittilli d amktay n tmatart.

Ad nskr adrfi gh tgmmi nɣ lli ttuxrfan, ayad iga azzulan mara nra ad nbdl tilit lli ttusbughlan.

Aytma ar ttackan ɣf kuynna, ar ttlulun, ar ttmttawn, ar ttgaddaln, ar ran ad bdlnt tudrt, ad ffghn gh tmughra lli gh llan, macc ur ar sswuriynt ɣf iman nsn.

Ur ran aytma ad ṭṭfn is tudrt gh wawnk tzgga aḥwalatn gh brra, mara illa d imṭṭawn, ssbab ar ttggan amdanatn gh wawnk lli gh ghrn ddan.

Win brra ar igadda d iskl lli gh wawnk, win ibdln gh wawnk ar iskr adrfi djdid n kuynna.

Aḥwalatn gh brra ur ttillint ad mqqurnint, zun d mani tlla tɣarast lli gh ntraṛaɣ ɣur.

Ad tɣamaɣ tlla tɣarast mara yat issutul? Ad tstqblt s yat tmara lli ur ittaḥwan n tmattarin n aytmatnnk?

Manik s tɣarast lli ttraṛamt ɣur tɣḍra n win turayt? Ad tɣamaɣ tlla d usmmn n tmrart? Ad tsmmat? Ad tɣamaɣ tlla gh tgmmi n uzmz?

Asbitarat, iduqran, tgmmi n uzmz, ar ffilln s win izddɣn d win ittuffghn lli traṛan s yat tɣarast lli gh ghrn ddan ɣur aḥwalatn gh brra.

Asafu izuran lli yan izmir ad iswuriy gh tudrt, iga amdan gh walln izuran.

Yan izmir ad ysbɣr iɣlbn d ad ysmjujjer iskhayln s amdanatn gh wawnk lli izuran.

Amdanatn gh wawnk lli gh ghrn ddan ar ttggun d imktayn imhḍarn n tmuɣra n aytma.

Lmddat ad tsaggamt ɣur imarasn lli nnkr gh tudrt n unẓul s yat tɣarast gh wawnk lli izuran...

Ur at tswuriynt s kuynna; attawalt is kullu idrus; lmddat ad tẓram tudrt zun d yat tfilimt d ad tstqblt tifawt...

Ur at tttut is aḥwalatn ur illi man tn yghdmn ar zmrn ad akkum ɣur tmttant, mara ur tssuft amdanatn gh walln lli gh ghrn ddan gh walln ntfhamt nwn.

Kuynna gh brra izdi, bla cck, i waraq lli izuran; zun d, amdan gh walln lli zuran.
