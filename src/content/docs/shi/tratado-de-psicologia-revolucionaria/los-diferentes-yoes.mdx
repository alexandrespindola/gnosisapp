Iɣaras uɣanim arẓẓal ittyuran sɣalat ɣas amdan, ur igi illa yat tmagit imdinin.

Ur ittrwuḍ bli ur illa wamun asikulan ɣur umanuḍ, iga ssbab n taskiwin d tiẓẓiḍin bahra.

Tafsut tifiskayt tga yat d tettssɣar ɣun tga kullutnsnt, maɣar tmdḍ.

Macc, tudrt n ugns n umanuḍ ur tgi abdd yan umun asikulan.

Aẓẓan bahra ɣ kullu mayad, ḥtta ma rad nnin middn n tmdras timsdadtin d tmsḍadtin ittwarggn, iga tddɣart n ussuddu asikulan ɣ ugns n kullu yan.

S tidt ɣ tgzmtad, ur illi kra n uxddam idusn ɣun tga kullu, ɣ tudrt n ugns n middn.

Umanuḍ, ɣ udɣar n tgnsitnsnt, iga bahra n tsikulin, yat tajmmaɛt n "Nkkni".

Imjjahd arslann n wakud ad idusn, ar ssuttn i "Nkk", ar ttḥrmn, ar ttsskar ɣ iɣarasn, ar ttinin "ALTER EGO", "Nkk Izwarn", "Nkk Igdudan", wAllahu Aɛlam.

Ur urnin ad ssinn "Imɛlman" n tallit ad idusn ɣ nzdaɣ, bli "Nkk Izwarn" ulaɣ "Nkk Igzzuln", gan snat tɣssa n ugnu aɣlilan...

Umanuḍ ur illi kra n "Nkk Imdinin" maca bahra n "Nkkni" infrahuman d imsdadtin.

Aɣaras arẓẓal amskir ittyuran sɣalat amdan, iga aɣarast n yat tgmmi ɣur ur illi tayri, ɣir middn bahra ittyuran ad ttinin ad frqin d ad skrn ma rannin...

Aɣalat izwarn n usḍad imsdad d usḍad ittwarggn ittawiɣ iɣarast bli wiyyaḍ uranin ulaɣ ili yat "Nkk Imdinin d Ur Ittbddln" bla tazwrt ulaɣ taggara...

Maɣar inn uɣaln middn ad gzzmn xf tudrt ḥtta ɣ yan uɣaras, raddin ssɣzn s tidt, bli aɣaras arẓẓal ur igi yan yat tizi bahra...

Aɣaras arẓẓal zɣ unɣil asikulan, ar ittbddl s tidt...

Ad nttɣar bli maɣar ittyura yan ism n Lwiz iga dima Lwiz, ar ittilin iɣartn am yat tḍṣṣaḍt ur idusn...

Asujjaɛ afuḍ d Lwiz ili ɣugnsnsnt "Nkkni" nniḍnin, ugnun nniḍnin, ar ssfrun xf tɣḍḍutnsnt ɣ iɣarasn imzzrayn, waɣar Lwiz ur ittaɣ tayri n uɣaras, "Nkk" nniḍn ɣugns -- ar nttini Asif -- ittaɣ tayri n uɣaras d manɣad...

Urd illa yan amddak amggar s tɣawsa tamggart, ur ittaɣ ad igi bahra amelmad matad ad ssinn middn n unamk azzayri d tɣawsiwin nnaɣdranin n kullu yan...

Ad nttɣar bli ili yan "Nkk Imdinin d Ur Ittbddln" iga s tidt yat tɛddabt ɣizd uggadda n maddn d uggadda nugharasnɣ...

Ugns n kullu yan uranin maddn bahra, "Nkkni" bahra, mayad ittaɣ ad yaskr s ugharasnsnt, ɣ ugharas udmran, kullu yan uggan, ittwaran...
