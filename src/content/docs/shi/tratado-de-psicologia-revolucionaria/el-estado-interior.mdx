Asmuna gigan ɣef ad ssḍfu yan igerdan uzɣar d waddad n ul ɣef akud nns, arat ad iga tt tmassant n tudrt...

Kra igan tamattart ittusḍfan ɣur ul trwas i waddad n ul nns...

Macc, ur yugi yan, mddn imqqurn ɣur iftasn n tudrt nnsn, ar ttnin mas tudrt iga tt kra yattuyn ɣar lbarrani...

Ayyuz n mddn! ar ttsmun mas ma llan kra yattuyn ur aɣ d yucki, tudrt nɣ ad tgi zɣd...

Ar sawaln mas tɣzift tẓra tnɣd, ɣmmn ar tɣmrt n tfaska...

Ar ttnɣaln kra nɣmmn, ar ttrun kra yattuyn ar ttnɣaln, ar ɣarasn kra yattuyn imqqurn d mddn imqqurn...

Ur gin kra yattuyn mas asgrag n tudrt ur iga tt tudrt, mas tamagart i ad tili tudrt ar tga zɣ waddad n ul nns...

Ur yugi yan, kra iga tt waddad n tudrt yaggugn, mac ur nḍfar kra ɣur ul, yattuyn imqqurn zun d urta, kra yattuyn ɣur ul...

Yan ar ittmara ad issnta tafaska n tslit, kra iga tt waddad, mac ittyalul mas ar yattay ɣur akud, mas urta ar yaf ul, mas kra iga tt waddad...

Tugga tsslmda yaɣ mas ur kul mddn ttanan kra ɣur umgr n kra...

Ur yugi yan kra yattuyn ɣur tifaskiwin imqqurn, kra zun d urta ar issɣlay mddn ɣur ul...

Iɣarasn snin kra mddn ar sḍafan igerdan uzɣar d waddad n ul...

Ur yugi yan mas mddn ur gin kra ɣur ul, ar ttrun ɣur lli s izdin ad ssnin, ar ssnin ɣur lli s izdin ad ttrun...

Taslḍt ur trwas: Ayt tmassant izdar ad issin mac ur izdar ad issnta tajjart; Isɣlay mac ur izdar ad yassk; Inssur ɣur kra yattuyn; Inssa ɣur kra yattuyn...

Mddn imsɣlayn d imsɣlayn n wul ar ssnin kra n tudrt, ar ttramn ad ddarn...

Kul ass ar nẓra kra mddn ur gin kra ɣur ul, mac ur yugi yan, ar gin tudrt n mddn nɣd...

Mddn zun ad ur ttsɣlayn kra ɣur tifaskiwin, taḍunt n ul ɣur ul nnsn...mddn zun ad ar ɣarasn kra n ul ittuskrn...

Macc, igerdan zun ad ar ttanan kra ɣur ul, mddn isɣlayn, mddn igguran, mddn ittmuran, mddn imqqurn...

Mddn zun ad ar ttnin kra ɣur ul, mddn ar tttmun kra ɣur ul...

Mddn zun ad ar ttnin kra ɣur ul, ɣarasn kra n ul...

Mddn zun ad ittuslmda i waddad igerdan, ar gan kra yattuyn n ul...

Yattuyn ittyaran, idrimn ttggan, izrfan, imukrisn, ar ttilin ɣur mddn lli ur issinn ad ddarn...

Kul yan izdar ad yali s tmassant, mac snin kra mddn lli ttuslmda ad ddarn kra...

Akud lli izdar yan ad isrɣi igerdan uzɣar ɣur waddad n ul, ar isskn mas ur izdar ad yili kra...

Mddn lli ttuslmda ad sḍfun igerdan uzɣar d waddad n ul, ar ttddun ɣur ibrdan n kra...
