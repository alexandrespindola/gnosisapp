Urda d urriḥ is ur ttilin ikšudn, ar aɣ-d-iban s waḥdu-nnes belli iɛuqqa ad n-bḍu gar wayda ittggan zzman d ddunit d waddadn igan imɛummrn n tmuḍri.

Nḥwaj s tarwa ad nssen mani nlla g yan laḥẓa, ɣmkann g wannaɣ illa g ul-nnɣ, ɣmkann g wannaɣ iga yan laḥdis igan n tbaɛet ittggan yan zzman.

Tudrt s yixf-nnes tga yat tɛssast n wayda ittggan zzman d wannaɣ n imukan...

Yan inn: "Tudrt tga taznṭṭa, n wuḍan da ittaggaggʷa ufgan ɣ ul-nnes..."

Kullu ufgan ɣur-s tala ad issn ɣmklli ira; nkk ar umneɣ belli ixxam n yan laḥẓa n tfunast ittggan yan zzman yaggʷugn, ar as-d-ittḍḍfar ussikk d uḥlku...

Kullu laḥdis ɣur-s tamatart nnes, wannaɣ n ul da illa ɣmkann; ayad ur yttuskir, ur yttuḍfr...

S tidt lxddamt igan ukanin s yixf-nnes tga wannaɣ illa g ul n ufgan...

Ula yan ad inkkar belli ɣ ul-nneɣ nɣar aṭṭas n imjyan d belli llan waddadn ur iganin ṣḥiḥn...

Ma tɣnnjaɣ ad n-bḍu s tidt, nḥwaj ad n-bḍu s tarwa d ar zikk, ad nssɛdil waddadn ur iganin ṣḥiḥn...

Tssɛdilt n waddadn ur iganin ṣḥiḥn, tttaggʷa ibddiln ɣ wayda da ittggan zzman d ddunit...

Ma izzri ufgan xf waddadn ur iganin ṣḥiḥn, ur yttẓḍar ad yuriḍi zzig zzid ukan...

Nqqa as da tɛqqabt ad tẓḍar ad tftaɣ ma tzmurt ad tzmurt g zzman d ddunit...

Win ur ittzurur xf yixf-nnes ar ittaggaɣ g zzig zzid ukan; zun d ufgan ɣ ul-nnes g aman n ugaru...

Wayda ar ittbddil g timttaɛ-nns; ar d-ttackan zun d aman, gan tmttaɛ...

Tidt illa wayda iga ṣḥiḥn d wannaɣ iga nker; kra n tmatart ad gan xir wala tayyaḍ...

Ad n-bddil kra n wayda ayad ittzḍar; ad n-bddil ibdln, ad nssɛdil timttaɛ, ayad illa g timmad n tzḍarin.

Ayuwa illa timttaɛ ur ittzḍar ad tbddil; g aya ukan ilzem ad tqbal s tmuḍri, ad tgan kra n tɛqqabin ttazzan aṭṭas d ukanin...

Urda d urriḥ ar ittilin ikšudn, ar ittaggaɣ waḍu ma ur nzzgil xf mzɣura da ttackan...

Illa ad nẓrr tudrt zun d tɛssast n wannaɣ illa g ul-nneɣ; tarikt igan ṣḥiḥt n tudrt-nneɣ tga wannaɣ illa g ul-nneɣ...

Ma nrzf tudrt-nneɣ, ad nẓrr s yixf-nneɣ belli aṭṭas n timttaɛ ur iganin ṣḥiḥn uckan zzig waddadn ur iganin ṣḥiḥn...

Alejandro Magno, acku ur irriḥ, yurri s wannaɣ gan aɣaras n lmut...

Francisco I immut s tmttut ur igan ṣḥiḥt, wannaɣ tssnent tarikt...

Ma ttwassn Marat s tmeṭṭut ur igan ṣḥiḥt, ar immut s tɣarast n imrɣ d uḥssad, ittaɣyul belli iga ṣḥiḥt...

Tmeṭṭut n Parc n imɣar ar ttaggant iyyaw tudrt n Luis XV...

Aṭṭas n ufgan da immutn s tɛdawt, s uɣal d uḥssad, aya ssnent imẓlayn...

Mqqar ma tɛzzuf tmɣart-nneɣ s wannaɣ ur igan ṣḥiḥt, anɣal ad nfuɣ s tuga...

Otelo s uḥssad iga anflus, s tɛqqabt ar ittrarun win ur iganin ṣḥiḥn...
