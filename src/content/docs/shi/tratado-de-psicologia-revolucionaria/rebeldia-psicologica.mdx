Ur illis ad isul, ad nsker yat tmikt i wid akkʷ ittaɣn gh urannnns, is illa yan uḍɣar imatmatikan ɣ daxel nɣ...

Bla cekk, uḍɣar nna ur ittaf mara ɣ izri, wala ɣ uznza...

Win ira ad iggiz uḍɣar a g usɣnaɣ, ad t iggʷz ɣ da ɣ assad, ɣ daxel nns, s ddqiqa ɣ lḥal ad, ur yan usinya ɣ zdat, wala yan usinya ɣ deffir...

Sin ifggen ifalkanin d win yaggʷgnin n ṣṣlib n tiddi, ttafn gh uḍɣar a...

Ndda gh assad ɣ assad zdat sin ibrdan: win yaggʷgn d win ifalkan...

Yulli-d ghir win yaggʷgn ar "iqucc", acku gis illa "Vicente d kraygat yan", "Villegas d win akkʷ iran ad d-yawḍ", "Don Raimundo d kraygat mad ittaɣn"...

Iḍhr is win ifalkan yagr; iga abrid n wid ilan tifawt, win isyafn...

Ma yut yan iman nns ɣ bal, ma yut yan kra ɣ iman nns, ma ur ibḍa kra ɣ wuguren d idammn n tudrt, s tidt illa gh abrid ifalkan...

S tidt ur ittili mara ad ishl ad isufu timukrisin timgarin; ad ittu kraygat abḍu d ubrida n tudrt nɣ; uguren n kraygat anaw, isbbabn, daynutn, afssay n tiṛṛatin, tiɣrmin, tilifun, aman, tafawt, d wiyad...

Win ur illan kra s ur igin, wid akkʷ ur illan tawuri, s sbab n wayad wala wayad, s tidt ar ttrun s tɣssa n idrimn, ad ttun ammas nnsn, ad ur fttun kra s ugur nnsn, illa gh lḥal ṭṭafsit...

Wid ittrun, wid irzzun, wid akkʷ yujjan, s tɛkka, s ur ttufsan ɣ tudrt, s tmamkt, s usmtta, wala s waylli irwḍn...

Tawuri ɣ iman nɣ iga tt asyafa amqqran n ubrida ifalkan. Ur illa yan ir ad ilkm abrda n usyafa amqqran, ma ur igi kra ɣ iman nns...

Tawuri nna nttini urk ɣaf iga yan wanaw ajujman; ar ttutut kra n usnfl ɣ wakud nna nlla gh. Nssur ad nlmdd ad nddr ɣ wakud ɣ wakud...

Msl, yan ufgan ilan kra n ugur ajujman, aqdiman wala asrtan, s tidt ittu iman nns...

Ufgan nna ma yul tiddi, ma irula iḍn, ma yut kra ad iggzu anamk n wannar nns...

Ma ismun kra, ma ismun is kulchi izri; is tudrt tlla d tiɣratin, illa d tazllaft, is tmmut tsmun ɣ tmttant kraygat mad ittaɣn...

Ma iggzu is ugur nns ɣ tɣarast ur igi mara yat tmrurt, yat tiniri isiggln nna gis tɣamaɣ, ad iẓr ɣ yat tɣarast d tunṭfa is kulchi ibddl...

Asnfl n wannarn imanawen yumma s usmuqqul ajujman d tinṭft Imanawt i Tudrt...

Iḍhr is middn ar ttnɣaln imanawn zdat tɣawsiwin n tudrt...

Ay middn iggutn!, Ar ttilin d wid yujjan. Ma iran kra ad tn-islmmn ar ttzggʷaɣn; ma iran kra ad tn-iswḥln, ar ttrun. Ar ttnɣaln ma iran kra ad tn-inɣl; ar ttnɣaln ma iran kra ad tn-iɣl; ur gin mara ilili; imddkkal nnsn lan tazmart ad tn-yawi zɣ tffuggha ɣ ttaragit, zɣ usaram ɣ ugrẓẓi...

Kraygat yan zɣ wid illa gh ubrda yaggʷgn, icba yat tallunt tazawttayt, mani kra zɣ imddkkal nns ar ittgga mad iran...

Win ilmdn ad isnfl tiṛṛatin imanawn, gh lḥal yut "Abrda Ifalkan".

Wayad iga yan usnfl amqqran ɣ "Asstti n Tudrt" tuga tamaɣzut n "Usyafa Ajujman".
