Seggʷ izwarn d tmaziɣt, ittaggʷa uzɣar n tudrt nɣ d wiyyad mqqar d taziriwin n ufgan, s kra n imukrisn n walliḥ, timukrisin n twja, imukrisn n wusɣar d uskil.

Yubah belli g izwarn d tmaziɣt, bla kra n imukrisn, kullu imukrisn ayad ur ttassun ɣurɣ attan illa waddur mqqur, macc maɣar ntaggʷa ufgan mqqur, ikkatn usteqsiw ¿Man aɣ nkki? ¿Maniɣ d-kkigh? ¿Maɣar ad tɛɛzzbaɣ? ¿Man arzzu n tudrt ayad? attan

Kullu g ubrid n tudrt nssutr iseqsiwn ayad, kullu illa yat tikklt nra ad nsiggil, ad nsteqsi, ad nssan "mayd" n waṭṭas n walliḥ, imukrisn, imnɣan d uɛzzba, macc ttaruɣla ntaggʷa g yat tasnflulit, g yat tazrart, g yat tamntilt g mayd inna uman, g mayd aɣ-d-issuter kra n urgaz imzwar, attan

Nefflḥ tazgart taḥeqqanit d tilelli n ul, bac ur nnɛur ad nẓur taɣẓawt s kullu tarrayt nns, ntɛllal ɣar mayd nnin wiyyad, yubah belli ntaggʷa g ubrid ur nẓḍar.

Tamdunyt tuklit tanɛmmast tɛzza seg udwas ad uminn g Rebbi.

Tamdunyt takarit taḍrislit tɛzza g wid uman g Rebbi, macc g usun kullu zun ayad, kra n tazrarin, imukrisn n ufgan, isɣawsa n walliḥ. Ur ttumntilt, ur ttumnɣur, ur ttusteqsi, ur nnɛur ad nɛur taɣẓawt.

Walliḥ innur ad umin, ad ittnɣur, ad iẓur, ad isiggil, attan, macc ur nnɛur ad nɛur taɣẓawt.

Innurn agh ad numn g tfukt awd ur numn g-s, awd ad ntettnɣur g-s, macc itri ad icciɣ tiddi d tudrt i kullu mayd illa bla imntaln nnɣ ur ttutun i yan waddur.

Sdat n tumntilt tarɣalt, sdat n ttumnɣur d ttusteqsi, ttaẓḍan waṭṭas n imqitn n tazdayt tadɛart d waṭṭas n tusnaktin ur ẓḍarn n waddur adɣar usɣawsaɣ nkki.

Tamdunyt tuklit tanɛmmast d tamdunyt takarit tanɛmmast illan kullu yat tarrayt d zun tarrayt nns d tarrayt uɣalad nns, ttaẓḍa tazdayt nns tusɣart. Mayd iga tazdayt g idɣar tuklit tanɛmmast iga tazdayt ur tusɣart g idɣar takarit tanɛmmast d viceversa.

Tazdayt tɛllal ɣar ttaggin ufgan, seg idɣar, seg waddar. Mayd iga tazdayt g yat tamurt g yat tamurt nniḍn iga tazdayt ur tusɣart d mayd iga tazdayt g yat tikklt, g yat tikklt nniḍn iga tazdayt ur tusɣart. Tazdayt ur illa yan waddur ibaynn, ad nzuzzur s-s usun, tattuɣla tazdayt tadjḍidt g yat tiɣmit.

Tussna tadlsant ur tesslmad tazdayt, tussna tadlsant tesslmad TASNFLULIT TANFLAYT d mayd ar tnḥwaj tiddas timaynutin.

Seg yiḍ amqrann n taziriwin, g kullu tikkal, illa ufgan ayaggʷra seg tamdunt ad siggilln TAɣẒAWT.

Iɣẓan ad yaggʷr ufgan seg tamdunt ad siggilln TAɣẒAWT acku tlla g udɣar n tamdunt d g udɣar ufgan g da d g assa.

TAɣẒAWT illa ma ur nssin seg tikklt ɣar tikklt, ur ttufraq seg tamdunt d ur ttuzzagay seg imddurkn nnɣ zun nɛur ad nɣur ttaɣ.

Iɣẓan ad nni: kullu taɣẓawt iga taɣẓawt g umyaɣ d kullu taɣẓawt iga kra n tuṭṭfit.

TAɣẒAWT taradit d tga ay illi awd ur illi, ur ttaɣla attan illa umyaɣ, ur ttaɣla attan iga kra n tuṭṭfit.

Iɣẓan ad nni: taɣẓawt iga seg waddar d mayd iga g yat waddar g yat waddar nniḍn ur tga.

TAɣẒAWT ur illa mayd izdin d waddar. TAɣẒAWT tarakkamt. NKKI iga waddar d zun ur nnɛur ad issan TAɣẒAWT.

Iɣẓan ad nzɣar tiɣziwin tiddurin, tinaddarin, tirwatin. Ufgan ar skarabn tusnaktin d tazrarin s mayd iga TAɣẒAWT.

TAɣẒAWT ur illa mayd izdin d tazrarin d tiɣziwin tiddurin, acku ti iga ɣar isɣawsa ur dɣaran seg walliḥ.

TAɣẒAWT iga ma ur nssin seg tikklt ɣar tikklt d ɣar nnɛur ad tettɛur g umzaray nkki asaklan.

Taɣẓawt ur ttuga n wawal, tusnaktin, tazrarin. Taɣẓawt ɣar nnɛur ad tettissin seg tɛurrt ibaynn.

Walliḥ ɣar innur ad iẓur d tazrarin ur illa mayd izdin d taɣẓawt.

Walliḥ ur nnɛur ad isiggil TAɣẒAWT.

Imasnɣan, timasnɣanin n iskila, izdayn, tusnaktin, ttinnurn ad ɛurn taɣẓawt d ad ssisn ubrid i imɣarn d timɣarnin.

TAɣẒAWT ttuga n tɛurrt ibaynn, ur ttuga n tusnaktin, tazrarin awd tusnaktin.

Nnurn azdar ad nɣra macc nḥwaj ad nɛur s iman nnɣ d s tarrayt tibaynn mayd illa n taɣẓawt g kullu tusnakt, tusnakt, tazrart, attan

Nḥwaj ad nɣra, ad nzuzzur, ad nsteqsi, macc nḥwaj daɣ URGENCIA inaplazable ad nɛur TAɣẒAWT illa g kullu ma nnɣra.

Ur nnɛur ad nɛur TAɣẒAWT maɣar walliḥ illa ilɣaw, ittɛzzba, ittɛzza s tazrarin tizzayyin.

ɣar nnɛur ad nɛur TAɣẒAWT mi illa walliḥ iɣẓan, mi illa walliḥ g tussma.

Imasnɣan d timasnɣanin n iskila, izdayn d tusnaktin, ttinnurn ad ssin i imɣarn d timɣarnin ubrid n uɣalad n usun mqqur.

Ubrid n uɣalad n usun mqqur ar nssassaɣ ɣar uɣzzan d tussma n walliḥ.

Mi illa walliḥ iɣẓan, ikfu seg iɣarasn, inarawn, tazrarin, attan, mi illa walliḥ g tussma ar d-ittegga ɣurɣ taɣẓawt.
