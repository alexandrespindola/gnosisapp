Ad ssnn maydd ad nxmmim iḍr sḥal. Imawlan nnɣ, isrdasn nnɣ, imslmadn nnɣ, middn da itaran idlisn, d wiyyaḍ d wiyyaḍ, kuyan d amṛṛukḍu s tɣarast nns, kuyan ira ad nxmmim s maydd ttinin, s maydd gan iɣrudan nns, s tmattarin nns, s tsnsityin nns, d wiyyaḍ.

Imṛṛukḍutn n walln zznɣn zun izribn. Illa kra n tgdda mẓṛmḍa ad tẓṛbi walln n wiyyaḍ, ad tṣbbiṭt, ad ttḍalb ad tddr ɣ waddagn n tnɣmsin, tisnsityin, imrsal, d wiyyaḍ.

Imllayn d imlyarn n IMṚṚUKḌUTN n walln ur arn uggzn tifawt i walln n kra. Ma illa yan ur ixmmim zund nttni, ttun t tɛib, amɣar, amḍan, d wiyyaḍ.

Aytmaḍn kuyan ira ad yẓṛbi wayyaḍ, kuyan ira ad yɣḍṛ tifawt tamttit n wiyyaḍ. Ul kra iran ad yḥtṛm tifawt n usawl n wiyyaḍ. Kuyan ittḥuss iḥḍṛ, izḍar, ifulki, d ira zund izwar ad ddun wiyyaḍ zund nttan, ad t uɣln zund amudl nns, ad xmmimn zund nttan.

Ttamḍlḍ d waḍu ɣ walln. Zṛt IMZRAG, d usɣl nnsn s uɣmis, rradio, tilifizyun, d wiyyaḍ.

Usɣl amzirg ittgga s tɣarast tamṛṛukḍut: sɣut ṣabun anflan! Iḍarn anflan! Tnt d tnt n wuzzrn! Tnt d tnt n ddulaṛ! Sɣut ḍarḍ! Imikḍ! Ur t tḍjj i was g usns! Ilzm ad yili ḍarḍ! D wiyyaḍ. Iɣḷḷay aɣ ttinin ma ur tfḍḍɣt ad k nɣlɣ ɣ lḥbs, ula ad k nɣ.

Baba ira ad iḥḍḍu i mmi nns iɣrɣdan nns s tẓḍurt, isrdas n usinary ar ittgḍḍa, ittṣḍi, d ittgga tazart maḥḍr maḥḍr ma ur yaɣul ufṛux ula tafrxuxt ar tsḍi s iɣrɣdan n usrdas.

Grz n igḍḍn ira ad tẓṛbi alln n wiyyaḍ n grz-ns. Taɣdda ad tẓṛbi alln n wiyyaḍ tẓhṛ s walln ma nɣri azwl azgrar n mzik zgrar.

Aytmaḍn llan d llan IMṚṚUKḌUTN IRGAN gan iẓḍan ad ẓṛbin iɣrfan. Imṛṛukḍutn irgan da ttinin maydd ilzm middn ad xmmimn. Maḥḍr adas! Win iran ad ixmmim s tifawt: ntta ar itddru zund izwar s iɣarbazn n tẓrbayt, s sibirya, s lḥbs, s imursn n tẓḍurt, s uɣrum, s uḍllɛ, s tmdḍart, d wiyyaḍ.

Ula ISMSRAS d ISMSRASIN, ula IMAWLAN, ula idlisn, ur iran ad sḍɛn mntak ad nxmmim.

Iɣwl i middn ad ḍalbn wiyyaḍ ad xmmimn zund zund ar ttmunn ad ili, d ittraraḍ ass izwar kuyan ɣ wannak d amṛṛukḍu s tɣarast nns, kuyan ittmunn s walln as ttmunn, kuyan ittmunn s walln as ilzm imḍfar ad xmmimn zund nttan, acku ntta ad izḍarn zḍar.

Imawlan n tawja, isrdasn, imqḍmn, d wiyyaḍ, ar ttɣnn d ar tɣnn i wiyyaḍ ẓḍṛn nnsn.

Iẓḍi waḍu ad isɣiɣn n igḍḍn ad sɣḍḍn ḥtṛam i wiyyaḍ, ad yɣḍṛ alln n wiyyaḍ, ad yuḍṭṭq, ad isɣṭl, ad iẓṛbi, ad iṭṭaḍ alln n wiyyaḍ.

Argaz ira ad iḥḍḍu i tmṭṭut iɣrɣdan nns ɣ uqqa nns s tẓḍurt, ttɛlim nns, iɣrɣdan nns, d wiyyaḍ, d tmṭṭut tara ad tgga amnnak.

Mraw tnḍaḥ argaz d tmṭṭut ttraḍan acku ur tmunn iɣrɣdan nnsn.

Ur iran iɛḍfan ad faḥmn tɣssa ad ttḥtṛmn tifawt tamttit n wiyyaḍ.

Ul izḍar lɛḍu ad iẓṛbi alln n lɛḍu wayyaḍ. Kuyan ɣ ṣṣaḥ gan izḍarn n uḥtṛam. Kuyan izḍar ad ixmmim zund ira, ad iḥḍḍu din nns, ad yili ɣ trabbut tadlsant din ira.

I ifṛuxn d tifrɣutn n usinary ittḍalbn ad xmmimn s tẓḍurt ɣ waddagn d waddagn macc ur ttulmḍn ad srksn alln.

Alln n ifṛuxn d irɣaḍn, tifawt nɣ, ilulmt, d alln n iɣḍḍan gant qḍḥt, tṣṣḍiḍt, zun aẓḍu ɣ uḍffu, ur tbdḍl, ur izḍar ad tbdḍl.

Alln n ifṛuxn d iḍlmḍan gan zḍarn ad bdḍln, izḍar ad tbḍḍln.

I ifṛuxn d iḍlmḍan izḍar ad tn nsslmd mntak ad NXMMIM. I iɣḍḍan iḍr sḥal ad tn nsslmd mntak ad NXMMIM acku nttni gann zund gann d ḍḍfrn zund nnɛmn.

Iḍr sḥal ad nḥll afgan ɣ tudrt ira ad ybḍḍl s wazal.

Alln n middn tttga tbddu zɣ tfṛuxt. Wannak ad gan imawlan d isrdasn n usinary ar tzḍarn ad tggn. Ar ttifḍḍin s usslmd n tɣarast i alln n ifṛuxn d iḍlmḍan.

Alln tttuɣln ɣ uḍffu gant ṣṣaḥ alln tttuḍyṛn, alln timẓrbbin.

Ilzm as ISMSRAS d ISMSRASIN n usinary ad ẓḍḍḍn uḍḍagn n walln.

Iḍfar as isrdasn ad ssnn ad srksn alln n ifṛuxn ɣ tifawt izḍarn as ur ttḥḍḍun dɣ.

Iḍfar as isrdasn ad sslmdn i uslmḍn d tislmḍnin mntak gan ad NXMMIM.

Ilzm as isrdasn ad faqn i tɣssa ad sslmdn i uslmḍn d tislmḍnin abrid n uɣḍṛ, tamḍluɛt, tadrsut.

Ul kra n afgan dars tadrsut izḍar ad yaɣul s tɣarast tadrust kra. Ilzm izwar ad nzḍar. Ad fhm, ad nqqlqḍ, dat ad nɣul.

Ḥtta ɣ izgḍan wiyyaḍ ad nini ul tɣssa ad nɣul, macc ad nzḍar, ad ngḍḍ, ad ngḍḍuɛ d ad nfhm. Ma tlla tadrsut tlla grz, taɣult ur tzḍar.

Ul tɣssa ad nɣnn aqqa nnɣ s tɣmrt tamttit ma nffiɣ zɣ usinary UR NSSN AD NXMMIM d Nttddru zun IGḍṬṬAN DDA TDDURN, zun makinat, da nɣḍṛn asɣar amḍa n imawlan nnɣ, irbbaḥn nnɣ, d dɣ nɣ yattun nnɣ, d wiyyaḍ.

Ad nɣḍṛ amḍaḍ, ad nddr tudrt n makinat, zɣ tgmmi s lburu d zɣ lburu s tgmmi, ad nzywḍ ad ntgga i makinat ad tggnt ifṛuxn, wannak ur igi tudrt d maɣad nɣri, d maɣad ntddru s usinary d s usinar d s tasmɣurt ɣ mraw n isggasn ula ḥḍṭṭax, izḍar as ur nɣri.
