Tazit tamatant tella g imikk n tlata d mraw isggʷasn ar xmsin d ṣḍis isggʷasn.

Argaz n tazit tamatant ilaq ad issn amk ad ihkm tgmmi nns d ad yssflḥ i warrac nns.

G tudrt tanammast kullu argaz n tazit tamatant iga agllid n twja. Argaz ur isnkr tgmmi nns d iqqrn nns g igʷnna d tazit tamatant ur t isnkar, iga zɣ wulli ur ifulkin.

Wulli ttraynin ad skrn tgmmi d iqqrn g ugʷrza gan win istaḥqn afus.

Infi n tumert itdda ar imizar d itra ad jamɛn iqqrn imqqṛan. Adɣar n ufgan illa yat tmat, yat tmjga d yat tɣmmrt. Ilaq ad ila yat tmat, yat tgmmi ixf nns, idɣarn, tɣawsiwin ad yddm adɣar f ixf nns, maca ur illa ḥaja ad jamɛn iqqrn imqqṛan bac ad iddr.

Nkkʷni ur nẓẓṛaf iqqrn wala tumert, sin imizar aṛumman.

Mqqar darsn wulli tɣʷlin gr uglay n tumert d mqqar wulli tɣʷlin gr uglay n iqqrn.

Ilaq ad ila yan yat tqqrt taramt, ar takka tgmmi irɣan s izugag imaran, yan uɣbalu ikkan n daxl, ad ila kullu mddn xirn d ad ur ittaɣ. Awddɣ amaynu i kullu ufgan.

Tumert, attaɣ, imaras d tmaẓuɣt ur illa ad llin g yan umazir ittlmadn d izdin.

Uda tlla tmnba maca nḥwaǧ ad tt nskr. Maɣar illa yan urgaz ur illa yat tmat, yat tmjga d yat tɣmmrt, tmnba ur tddi ula g yat tɣara tazaykut.

Agllidn n twja ilaq ad darsn tntmbinin, tifawt, ur jju asn ddu kullu tmqqirt, ibbujadn, izɣwarn, imḥqqaṛn, d wagh udɣaṛ.

Kullu argaz amɣar issn zɣ tmguri ixf nns is warrac tmmuddun imdl nns d is maɣar illa imdl anflas ad ittaɣ arrac nns iɣarasn n imirri.

Yaggug s tidt is argaz amɣar ila kra n tmɣarin d iddr g izɣwar, timqqirtin, isgʷdʷa, d wagh udɣaṛ.

F argaz amɣar tlla tamnka n kullu twja d izri is maɣar illa itdda g iɣarasn n imirri, ad iddkk amyaẓ n gr igʷdudn, amyaẓ, iɣul.

Baba d ymma ilaq ad tssnn ifaraz gr inawalen. Uda ar yffɣd is taḍḍant n trbatin tqqra tazrfiyt, tkimia, tajbrt, d wagh udɣaṛ. Tazrfiyt n tmtṭut tettfara zɣ urgaz, imudar amyaẓ illa g izikar d maca gan ur sḥwaǧn ula d winn wulli ibdl tazrfiyt n tmtṭut.

Ilaq ad gan baba d ymma n twja ad tanin s wul zɣ kullu tazrfiyt tga tafart g kullu asnml n tɣriwin n imdlann.

Tamtṭut ilaq ad ttlmad qri, ktb, ad traṛ ṛṛubab, tssdg, tssfrgl d g uɣaras anammast kullu inɣmasn inawalen.

Ar tmtṭut ar ttqqrin zɣ tɣriwin af usnfl n tɣriwin iga yat tazdayt igaddaɣn s imma d amk i tga tayri.

Uda ar yffɣd ad tɣʷʷlin tazrfiyt n tmɣarin s imarasn isḥqn d izwarn zɣ izikar.

Ilaq kullu baba d ymma n twja iqadmn imɣarriwn n tɣriwin, imdlann d tɣriwin d tazdayin s imḍuɣn af aqqrn n tmtṭut ar tili taɣara nns igaddaɣn. Uda ar yffɣd ad yṣlḥn timɣarin timlitarit, ad ttnḍfar imlitarit d azzḍu n imḍuɣn g iɣarasn n tamdint amk i ganin izikar.

Tamtṭut ilaq ad tili tayrit d urgaz ad ili izikar.

Amyaẓ gr sin imṭawan, uzɣar, iga aɣrḍas n tasmmgurt d taɣrdagt.

Tirbatin iɣʷlin g imarasn izwarn d iddrin ttusqqarn d uda yan ifullkn g dsn.

G tudrt tazaykut izri ad skrn tmɣarin tirzirin tinqqisin, aqqrn n tmguri, tiknukraft, taqqunt, tiqqatin, asflḥ, d wagh udɣaṛ, d wagh udɣaṛ, d wagh udɣaṛ.

Anammas, tmtṭut ilaq ad tili kan tazdayt ar tudrt n tgmmi, maca s tmttant n tallit illan g izri nkkʷni, tmtṭut tḥwaǧ ad tgan bac ad ttta d ad tddr.

G yat tmguri zɣ tidt tttlmadn d izdin tmtṭut ur tḥwaǧ ad tgan brr n tgmmi bac ad tddr. Aya n tgan brr n tgmmi iga tmttant n tɣrdagt.

Argaz illan g izri ismml tazdayt tanflast, d ittaɣ ar tmɣart tafart nns igaddaɣn, ittaɣ tt brr n tgmmi d ikka tt tmguri.

Tamtṭut ibdln s "azikar" s tzrfiyt n urgaz, issṭayn sikaran d itqqra timlɣatin, tafggugt s tskrriwin yafn ar ddu n tsukt ula igaẓn tiqqatin, iga aɣrḍas n urgazn ismmaln n tallit illan g izri nkkʷni, azdaɣ i tmguri izwran.

Tamtṭut tbḍel s tamzgalt tazaykut, tbibit tlla tlla tɣʷri, tamat n tɣrdagt, tɣʷri, tmmara ad tddm agʷdi f warrac nns bac ad ur tifḍ tayri nns iga aɣrḍas umḍal n yat tazdayt tanflast.

Ilkm tallit ad nskr usrdas n tslit tamgurt s urgazn d tmɣarin n taɣarast igaddaɣn illan zɣ tidt uɣarasn ad nnɣn f tazdayt tanflast.

Ilkm tallit ad nskr g imdl yat tazdayt tamaynut, yat tmguri tamaynut.

Tamtṭut tga imḍl n tgmmi d maɣar illa imḍl aya issngu, izḍɣ d iddr g aẓun n kullu usnfl, aɣrḍas n tudrt tamgurt ad iga azzḍu.

Azikar yttfara, urdda iga ad yḍfr lḥlaln af ad yqqra tbibit, tazrfiyt, tkimia, tazrfiyt, tqanun, tmguri, tasɣnti, d wagh udɣaṛ, d wagh udɣaṛ, d wagh udɣaṛ.

Yan uɣriw amlitarit n izikar uda ar yffɣd, maca yan uɣriw amlitarit n tmɣarin ula ar yffɣd, iga azzḍu amyaẓ.

Itga umḍl ad nẓṛ tid sswal tmdakʷlin tɣarasin iɣʷlin ad ttddmn warraw nns gr izẓlin nns ttddun am urgazn i iẓḍiɣarn n tamdint.

Aya ur itml ula aqqrn n tazdayt g izikar maca ḍfr izri g ad inin ad aqqrn n tazdayt g urgaz.

Argaz, argaz n tidt, argaz izikar ur izmir ad yqbl lamas yan umdl amlitarit n tmɣarin. Tumert tazikart, tifawt tazikart n urgaz tazrfiyt n urgaz issmyaɣ ur s yffiɣd i udɣar n udɣar imyaẓ ar idml usmyaɣ n ufgan.

Nḥwaǧ ad turri tmtṭut ar tgmmi nns, ar tɣara nns, ar tayri nns tanammast, ar tlilit nns tazaykut, d ar tidt nns tartut. Nḥwaǧ ad naggu s kullu udɣar n udɣar d ad nskr f udem n tanzruyt yat tazdayt tamaynut d yat tlila tamaynut.

Baba d ymma n twja d imɣarriwn ilaq ad tssnn ad tanin id uɣarasn d tmɣarin timaynut s tifawt d tayri n tidt.

Iẓṭa imɣarrin ur kan yqqrn tudrin d ad lmden yan unɣmas ula ad qqran tudrt tizwrt. Izri is izikar issnn amyaẓ n tamnka d ad tddun s igʷr iẓdɣn d tayri tayḥsut.

F udɣar n urgaz amɣar tlla tamnka n yat tayri, n kra n ẓaḍu d kra n tḍḍant.

Argaz amɣar s tumert tayḥsut, izɣẓn, iẓḍm, iẓḍm, amaras, d wagh udɣar, ittyara zɣ twja nns d zɣ kullu imdan.

Argaz amɣar itzday imdan s iznnayan nns, imlitanin, inḍwan, iziɣr, isgʷdʷa n kullu usnfl, iga agʷrzin i kullu imdan d ur ila kan ixf nns wulli iɣul maca ur t ila kan ixf nns maca ikka iɣul i twja nns d iddkk iɣul d amyaẓ i kullu imdl.

Izri is argaz amɣar issn ad iddr tallit nns s tfulki. Ttḥḥiɣ iḍil is argaz amɣar issn is igʷnna tzra.

Yaggug ad ira ad iṛẓm g tizit imyaẓ d umdl n igʷnna.

Kullu tallit n tudrt illa tayri nns, d ar ttsnn ad ttddrn.

Argaz amɣar ilaq ad igan s tmjgga mqran ukan ad idda ugʷrza amk tayda ttɣarasn g uɣaras n tzdayt ad ttaɣn izẓawn i tayda nns ukan ad idda ugʷrza, ula udɣar ilaq ad igan s tlilit d tgaddaɣn argaz amɣar.

Mqqar darsn izikar imɣarrin ttudmn uglay n kulun imdl nns igaddaɣn, d tallit ad ilkm tazit tamatant illa zɣ tayffut, izdar, tumert, ur sfulkin.

Zɣ tidt ar yaggug ad nẓṛ argazn imɣarrin ad ttaɣn timdyazin n igʷnna mbla ad issnn is ar izdar d is igʷnna tzra.

Yan zɣ tiẓẓumin mqranin n tazdayt aya izwran iga tɣʷri n tazzult.

G igʷnna mqqar darsn ttddun ar tiɣri d tallit ad ilkm tazit tamatant ur darsn nskr yat tgmmi, ur darsn nskr yan iqqrn, ur darsn illa yan unɣmas iqqrn, tddrn zɣ tsɣrirt ar tsɣrirt ṭṭlbn ad imsu, izdar amyaẓ, igʷrzin, tumert.

Agllidn n twja d imɣarriwn ilaq ad skrn isyafal amyaẓ g izikar asnfl ad tanin g iɣarasn izɣẓn s umdl iqqrn ad gan yan imdl iqqrn.
