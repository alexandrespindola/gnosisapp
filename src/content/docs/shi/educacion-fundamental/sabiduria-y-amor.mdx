Tamusni d tayri gan snat tgejda timqqranin n kullu tamadunt izddign.

G yat tssi n tmizar n tɛadala, ilaq a nessers tamusni, g tssi yaḍnin ilaq a nessers tayri.

Tamusni d tayri ilaq ad myaggḍn. Tamusni bla tayri tga yan ufgag abɛɛac. Tayri bla tamusni tasmunɣ aɣ s uɣalat "TAYRI TGA ASERTI MACC TAYRI IMAKAN".

Ilaq ad nɣr bahra d ad nrfed tiytiwin, maca daɣ ilaḥḥ fellanes ad nɛmmer gnes lxir.

Tiyit bla lxir yattuyn imɣar gnes, ar tettɣʷṛ aɣawal nna as ttinin abribi.

Lxir izddign imɣar gnes maca bla tiytiwin tinmzrayin, ittawḍ s iqddasn imjahl.

Yan uqddas amajahl igan lxir yattuyn bahra, maca acku ur illi ɣures tiytiwin tinmzrayin, ur iswiru ad yskr kra acku ur issin matta skrn.

UQDAS amajahl igan tazmert n uskər maca ur iswiru ad yskr acku ur issin matta skrn.

Tiyit tinmzrayt bla lxir yattuyn imɣar tga tayrza tinmzrayt, tujddirt, zzɛama, wagh... wagh.

D kukru amaḍlan wis sin, imyaṛn n imusnawen id ur illi ɣuṛsen kra n ugugir lxir s yism n tussna d ufgan, skren tiɛṛṛmtin imqqranin s yat turda ad skren ujiṛṛubn imusnawen.

Nḥwaj ad nɣṛ tamadunt tinmzrayt tazdawit maca izddign bahra d lxir amtti amazdar.

Nḥwaj yan umɣar aɣlal d yat tusnakt aɣlalt ma ikkan nttan s tidt nra ad nɛdi uɣaras ad nɛmmer gnes lxir yattuyn n tidt.

Yallaḥ mad iɣʷṛan acku ɣas ur illi tayri middn ar ssqsiyn asggʷs n tussna s yat turda tabɛɛact.

Inelmadn d tnɛlmadin ḥwaǧn ad ɣren tussniwin, umezruy, tusnakt, wagh... wagh.

Ilaq ad nrfed tiytiwin tizrigin, s yat turda ad nɛawn iɣʷṛdan.

Aɣra illaḥ. Asmun n tiytiwin tiganin iga wajib, maca agʷdi ur iga wajib.

Bahra n middn ar smunun tiytiwin s agʷdi; ɣuṛsen agʷdi n tudrt, n tmatnt, n laẓ, n tɛyiyt, matta ad nnin, wagh, dɣiḍn ssbab ar ɣran.

Ilaq ad nɣr s tayri i imddukkal nnes s urgi nsen ad nɣddu afus, maca ur illi nra ad nɣr s agʷdi.

G tudrt n tudra, nzḍar ad nzḍar belli kullu inelmadn nna ɣran s agʷdi, ar ttbeddaln ɣar abribi.

Nḥwaj ad nttḥerrez d iman nnes iḥewwc ad nsawwar iman nnes ad nsufɣ giman nnes kullu imzguren n ugʷdi.

Ur ilaq ad nttu g tudrt belli agʷdi igan bahra imzguren. S kra n wakuden agʷdi ar ittmyaɣ d tmazdart. Asrdasn g iberdan n kukru ar ittbayan mazdaren maca g tidt ar ttmuggurn d ar ttnaɣn s ssbab n ugʷdi. Yan ur iddrn daɣ ar ittbayan g tisit tamzwarut igan amazdar maca g tidt iga aqṛbac igan ugʷdi n tudrt.

Kullu abribi g tudrt ar ittbayan mazdar maca g uzgzur iga aqṛbac. Ibribiyen ar ssqsiyn tiwuri d tazmert s yat turda tabɛɛact mad akk ɣuṛsen agʷdi. Amedya; Castro Rúa; g Cuba.

Nekknti ur nngiḍ ar tasirit n tudrt n tudra wala ar uɣaras n tussna, maca ar ncddi tanqqast n tayri.

Tussna d tasiratin n tudrt ar ttɣʷṛnt mad ur illi tayri.

EL EGO ar ittaṭṭaf tasiratin d tiytiwin tinmzrayin mad ur illi nna as ttinin tayri.

EL EGO ar ittɛḍa tasiratin d tussna mad ssqsi tasmɣurt n iman nnes.

Ad nɛdi EL EGO, NEKK, MI MISMO, tasiratin d Tussna ar ttɣiman g ifassen n SER INTIMO d kullu uɛḍḍa ar ittuɣal ur iwərkan.

Kullu anlmad ilaq ad izuzzur ɣar uɣaras uzrigi d ad iɣr bahra g uzgzur kullu tiyuriyin nna ittmyaɣn d tuwuri nnes.

Aɣra, tussna, ur tɣʷṛn ulaḥed maca ur ilaq ad nɛḍu tussna.

Nḥwaj ad nɣr ur nɛḍu aklilu. Ar ittɛḍa aklilu yan ira ad iɣr tiyuriyin n imyaṛn n tusniwin, yan ira ad iɣʷṛḍ wiyyaḍ s tussna, yan ar iskr tayrza f aklilu n wiyyaḍ, wagh. wagh. wagh.

Ilaq ad nɣr tiɣawsiwin tiserɣinin d tiɣawsiwin tilxiritin iḥewwc ad illi ɣuṛnes aklilu izddign.

IGA wajib ad ngḍar ɣar SÍNTESIS tinmzrayt d ɣar síntesis tilxirit mad ikkan nttan s tidt nra yan aklilu izddign.

Inmaln d tmalin n tɣiwaniwin, izdɣarn, Tsdawiyin, wagh, ilaq ad ɣren g uzgzur Sicología nnes aɣlalt mad ikkan nttan s tidt uran ad zdɣen inelmadn nnsen ɣar uɣaras n REVOLUCIÓN tazddigt.

Ilaq ad arfden inelmadn SER ESPIRITUAL, ad ɛmmren giman nnsen SER VERDADERO, iḥewwc ad uɣalen dɣar ad ffɣn seg tɣiwant beddln ɣar imezdaɣ iswṛun ur ɣarn abribiyen imjahl.

Ur tswiru kra Tamusni bla Tayri. Tussna bla Tayri ɣas tggʷṛ ibribiyen.

Tamusni segnes tga SUSTANCIA Atómica, amalu Atómico nna ɣas ilaq ad yskr yan igan tayri s tidt.
