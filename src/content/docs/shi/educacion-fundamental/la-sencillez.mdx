Ilzem ad nsner d ad nssner tamssni idusn, acku nettat ay ittawin i ufgan tilelli tazaydant ɣ tudrt. Bla tamssni ur yumkin ad nssḍḍu tifrat tunṣibt n unagraw ɣ uznzuṛ.

Istimassan d tstimassatin n tmazdart, id lkulij d tasdawiyin ad dḍfṛn inlmuden nsen ɣ ubrid n tmssni ixf nnk.

ɣ uzwir nna izrin nɣraɣ s usnay mliḥ gisn iẓẓuṛan n tigmmi, u ma yuran ad nsḍfa akʷ timitar n tusḍa, maqqar gant tid ddin, tid tɣrma, ddu. ilzem ad nssḍfa tirrugza ɣ ma yga ẓẓṛaḥ acku arakkaɣ ad nḍfaɣ s usnfl tirrugza s timmiqq d usnay ɣ iẓẓuṛan ur ytbddaḍn n tigmmi.

Tirrugza tessɣaray izzwagn, tirrugza tessɣaray tiwiziwin, tirrugza tga ddabz ddinan, irɣan imqran, tdlatin d wazzarn n ma akk yuran.

Tigmmi s ma akk gisn ur ytbddaḍn n timitar tezriɣ ɣar tiɣriwin idusn. Tlla tigmmi ɣ winna yuran idusn ɣ usnfl ɣ imqqran s wiyyaḍ. Tlla tigmmi ɣ winna igan afnay lli igan aylli akk ittafa ad iɣleb ifnayn n wiyyaḍ. Tlla tigmmi ɣ kra n ufgan yuran iɣaras acku yuran iɣaras acku ylla ɣ uzmz nns kra n ufgan ittmallaḥn s iɣaras.

Azal n ad ig ufgan amallaḥ, azal n ad ig ufgan iɣaran, azal n ad ig ufgan amqqran yga s udɣar tigmmi.

Imallahn s iɣaras nsen gan iḍuṛan mqqurnin. Ittaɣa d unzwi ɣ usmmḍi n ufgan lli ikkan d amallaḥ.

ɣ yat tiklt yan umdyaz iɣuḍn ulaɣur izziɣ i tiggura nns ad issrus ɣ fusnns yat tmdyazt tẓẓḍaṛt issyafaṛn gisn i umallaḥ n uzmz nɣ. Umdyaz iqqraɣ ulaɣur lli ad iznz aɣrum i tfkka nns iɣuḍn, izwriɣ.

Kra akk yura umdyaz ar nnkʷrḍaɣ yat. Tkka d tammara nns ad iɣḍ ufgan amllaḥ s walln nns iluɣmayn d uḍaɣ yqqn tiggurt, u inna i umdyaz nɣit: "ddun, anɛam, ddun... ur ɣriɣ ɣinn, ur srsɣ azal i ma akk izznin... ur srsɣ azal i tɣrma ɣ waddur ɣ ddunit acku tamatayt... Nck fkiɣ adɣar i ddɣr d ttwaḍɛt. Umdyaz ur illan igt akk ad iznz aɣrum ddi tɣḍ n umallaḥ, awal lli issɣarayn, uṭa n iɣɣm, d wul nns issɣaran d tlit nns istbrzn ɣ iznqqan n tmdint lḥis... lḥis... lḥis.

Iɣrm n tazzant ad izzigz s ussan n tmssni tunṣibt acku nettat tga tamaynut.

Asmmkti d umẓlu ur gin imaynutn. Asmmkti yga abriḍ n uzwir. Asmmkti d umẓlu gan tumrt.

Tamssni tunṣibt tga uṭṭun asnaman n ddɣr akkʷ.

Imiktiwn n usmmkti ur yumkin ad awnɣ dduɣr acku gant n uzwir, u ar nnkʷrḍaɣ gant tumrt.

Tamssni ur tgi ta uzwir wala uzmz nddat. Tamssni tga ta uzmz lli nzdaɣ ɣinn d nttaɣ d ɣilad. Asmmkti ar ittddaw azal n uzmz nddat.

Ilzem ad nɣr tussna, tflsft, tazuri d ddin maca ur ilzem ad ntnɛawn f ussnfl i usmmkti acku ur iḍɣaṛ.

Ur iḍɣaṛ ad nssrus tussna ɣ ubriḍ n usmmkti. Ur iḍɣaṛ ad nsɣar ɣ ubrid n uzwir tussna lli yuran ad nssḍfaɣ.

Ur yumkin ad nḍfuɣ d mgal n inɣira, d mgal n tamẓẓaṛt, d mgal n tussna, maca nɣaɣ mgal ad nssrus tussna tdrin ɣ ubriḍ inqqrn n usmmkti.

Ilzem ad nɣr, ilzem ad ndffir, ilzem ad nnagraw maca ilzem ad ndddwa mliḥ ad nssḍfa ɣ ma akk yuran ɣ idɣarn n ul.

Ufgan imqran ittmallaḥn ittmallaḥn s tmssni idusn d ddar nns imllaḥn.

Aylli akk imqran ɣ tudrt ur iga arakkaɣ illa ɣ ubriḍ n usmmkti, maca aylli nssḍfa ur iɣraɣ ɣ idɣarn n ul maca ɣ iggi n waṭṭan ur ytbddaḍn n ul.

Tussna, tamẓẓaṛt ilzem ad ddiɣr ad nɣr. Asggʷas n tussna, asggʷas n inɣira ad yuɣln ɣ tmssni tunṣibt tanayyut yumkin ad nssḍfa akʷ kra acku tmssni tdiɣr tazaydit.

ɣ ufgan imqran ur tlli kra n tnḍfart ɣ ul acku tinfarkt akkʷ ɣ ul tga tasmmkti. Ayyu aqnṭṛan nna nddaɣ d ɣ ul iga asmmkti issɣaran.

Imlann n tudrt ilzem ad yuɣln ɣ tmssni tunṣibt.

Asggʷas n umlan ur yuɣln ɣ tmssni, asggʷas n umlan ɣ usmmkti gan ismmḍi n ubriḍ ɣ ictta ɣ usmllaḥ ususnɣ.

Ilzem ad nssn mas ddɣr asnaman yssɣran kra n waddur yga ar nnkʷrḍaɣ awal n usmmkti, usmllaḥ n ususnɣ ictta ɣ ufalkay.

Ufgan imqran illa ddar nns ɣ tirrugza acku yuɣlnt ɣ tmssni, yuɣlnt ɣ tmssni tabaydant.

Tumrt d tumrt ar ttnmalan ɣar. Arakkaɣ iɣ mad ittawi uɣanim, arakkaɣ immt umlan tga d tmssni. Arakkaɣ iga ussan n usnfl.

Ufgan ittnḍfaṛn ddar nns illa ɣ umlan.

Arakkaɣ ar isskan mas ur igi tamssni tabaydant acku asggʷas n umlan yuɣlnt ɣ tmssni ɣ idɣarn n ul tddnt ɣ umlan yuɣlnt ɣ tmssni.

Ilzem ad nɣr maca ur ilzem ad nɣṭṭiɣ ɣ ubrid n umlan acku arakkaɣ ul tnḍfaṛ ul ɣ ul. Ilzem ad nzdaɣ tudrt s ṣṣaḥ d ad nsnflumlant akkʷ ɣ tmssni tunṣibt tabaydant.

Winnat lli yran s uzɣuṛ amqqran ad igi tmssni imllaḥn ad nkʷrḍ amatu, ad nɣʷmm amazzir, ad nzdaɣ ɣ iɣʷrmn imzwign d ad nslkam arkmbal d ad nḥzzal kra n uṣukri n iḍaṛn, ar nnkʷrḍaɣ gan s uzɣuṛ.

Aṭṭas n iɣṛman imzwign, aṭṭas n iɣʷrman imzwign, aṭṭas n izzarrann gan ul nsn inḍfaṛn d idrusn.

Ur igt azal ad tazlɣ ɣ ddunit ad tzdaɣt am ussmḍi imzwig asggʷas n umlan illa ɣ kra n ussan lli ur tnfaɛn tazlɣa n ul.

Ur igt azal ad tzdaɣt am iɣʷrman imzwign ad ttnuraɣt ad igi tudrt n imallaḥn asggʷas n umlan illa ɣ kra n ussan lli ur tnfaɛn tazlɣa n ul.

Winnat lli ysnflun tussna asnaman ɣ tmssni tunṣibt tabaydant, winnat lli ysnflun umlan n tudrt ɣ tmssni tunṣibt ɣar ur illi kra ɣ usmmkti, ar zdɣn ɣ uzmz lli ɣ llan igt kra d nttaɣ d ufulki, yuɣln imllaḥn d imqran maqqar zdan ɣ iɣʷrman imqqurnin d ɣ uzmz n tudrt tamdint.

Inqqiɛn imẓẓiyn ar uzmz n sa s ussan n ufulki d usnfl ususn acku ar ttnmalan d kra n walli ɣ uẓɣaṛn asssḍn gisn d waddur n tudrt ɣar.

Ilzem ad nssḍɣar timẓẓut issɣran ɣ ul d ɣ ul nɣ. Ilzem ad nssḍɣar turrut nɣ anɣ turaɣ d nɣʷmdd ad nɣʷmdd tudrt.

Imlann d inɣira ysnflun ɣ tmssni ɣar ur ilin kra ɣ ubriḍ n usmmkti, u ar nkʷrḍ d ngi imllaḥn, imqran, turrutnin, afalkawnin.

Asddwa ɣar umlan d tussna, unɣuḍu mliḥ, tsnflult tanayyut ysnflun ma akk yuran ɣ tmssni tunṣibt tabaydant. Arakkaɣ iga ubrid n tudrt ufulkin n tmẓẓaṛt d tayri.
