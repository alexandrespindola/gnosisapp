Urraz akkʷ aẓẓawan ɣar usslmd anaẓur ma iga uḥujaj n unlmadn d unlmadat n usinan, usinan anammas ula usinan akkʷ uflla, maca d imzwura d isrdasn.

Ma ur ẓḍarn imzwura d isrdasn ad ssinen iman nsn, ma ur ẓḍarn ad fhmen arrac, tiḥbibin, ma ur ẓḍarn ad fhmen sg illa gr-asn d tiddiwin ad illan ar aɣlin, ma ttfkan azal i ugmar n aqqln n inlmuden nsn, manza ɣad nḥluq yan wanaw amaynu n usslmd?

Arra, unlmad, taḥbibt, ar ttaɣn ɣar tinml bac ad afn afus n usslmd d ussna, maca ma gan isrdasn, tisrdasin, ɣar ugns n aqqln iḍf, imaziɣn, iggurran, ittɣlin ɣar deffir, akud ann ayga unlmad, taḥbibt.

Imssalda nttni ar ttḥwajjan ad ɣrn iman nsn, ad ssinen iman nsn, ad sfliden i kullu ma ssn, ad fhmen mas nttini ar nkcm ɣar tasut tamaynut.

S uɣyur n imssalda, ar ittbddl usslmd anaẓur.

Asslmd n umsald aẓẓawan acku kullu ma iɣra bahra, kullu ma illa yut targa kullu ma ira ad isslmd, ar iswingm am srdas ɣar tinml, iga zun kra illan ɣar ugayyu n waḍu ɣar tmrawin n alaf n tiggawin li iɣra ur ittbddl ula s tmdyazt.

Isrdasn d tisrdasin izddɣn ad ssalmdn maniɣ s ittswingm, maca aɣaras n tɣʷssa ar ttɣawsan bac ad ssalmdn maniɣ ɣad swingmn.

Imzwura d isrdasn ttidrren d ugns n tɣawssiwin iqburnin n idrimn, timttiwin, tmslilin, d gigan.

Imzwura d isrdasn llan bahra gʷrɣn s tɣawssiwin nsn, ur llin zund illa gr-asn amussu bac ad ɣrn ad ḥlun urmazn i illa gr ilmmaẓn d tmlluẓin n "Tudrt Tamaynut".

Illa kra n tɣʷssa n wazzal, tayri d timtti, maca imzwura d isrdasn ɣḍarn s uḍuf d tɣawssiwin niman nsn ur llin d wakud bac ad swingmn ɣar uẓẓawan n idrimn n arrac, ad asn fkən taẓuri bac ur mmutn s llaẓ ulaḥ.

Aɣaras d imzaray ɣar tnmirt, bahra n imzwura n igʷmma ur ttarmn arrac nsn s tidt, ma gan ttarmn-tn, ad ddukn bac ad afn tɣawssiwin iḥlanin, ad ɣawsan ɣar urmazn n USSLMD ANAẒUR s uẓẓawan bac ad afn bddln s tidt.

Ma gan imzwura n igʷmma ttarmn arrac nsn s tidt, ur illa kra n imnɣi, ur illa kra n igʷmma d tqbilt bahra zund illa gr-asn d uɣrf n umaḍal, acku ayann ar ittawi urmazn, imnɣiyen, ibḍiyyen izdimmn, tamurt n jahnnam i arrac nneɣ d tiḥbibin.

Afgan ar iɣra, ar iḥḍu iman nns bac ad ig amdwal, umhandis abridi, d gigan, maca ur iḥḍu iman nns ɣar tawuri aẓẓawant bahra d aẓẓawant acku iga ad ig amazwar n igʷmma.

Illa kra n uẓɣur n igʷmma, illa kra n tggʷra n tayri i imzwura nneɣ, illa kra n tasrtit n tɣʷssa n igʷmma, ur illa kra n ussan ɣar yat tmatart, acku ar tggʷra tmatart n tɣʷssa d timtti.

Adɣar amzwaru, tagrawla s tidt, ar illa ulaḥ s uɣyur n tiggurmatin n Sinwa li akkʷ nqqlan, li akkʷ nkksan sg uɣrf n umaḍal.

Nttini kullu iga yat tawja d ur illa kra n ussan ad nɛzzb wan nnick, bac ad ngu zun iga tawyjit kra n tiddiwin li d nttini nttidr, d gigan.

Tagʷmma n USLALU UTTAJUR ar tskr tagʷmma n timtti ar tgawan imdan, ar tawi imnɣiyyen, tisutiwin, tizzwaɣin, urmazn n idrimn, d gigan.

Akud li ɣad ttarmn imzwura n igʷmma arrac nsn s tidt, ɣad ɣlin tigmmiwin, isurar iqburnin n tɣʷssa uɣyur ulaḥ ɣad tga tawja yat tuzzya tajur d ur illa kra n ussan.

Uɣyur n isurar ittawjurn n igʷmma, ar illa gr-anɣ tmunt iḥlan gr kullu imzwura d yimmatn n igʷmma, gr isrdasn d tisrdasin, gr timtti kullut.

Aɣaras n TLILT TIDT, iga uɣyur n TIMTTI TIDT, tagrawla ɣar uɣaras n USSLMD bac yan umaḍal ilan.

AMSALD izddɣ ad ig izddar bahra, izddɣ ad yssudu imzwura d yimmatn n igʷmma, iɣf n tɣʷssa n imzwura n igʷmma bac asn isssfalad.

Izdɣ i imzwura n igʷmma ad fhmen mas tawuri n usslmd anaẓur ar ttuskra ɣar tɣʷssa ilan n tdukli gr imzwura n igʷmma d isrdasn.

Izdɣ ad tn inin i imzwura n igʷmma mas USSLMD ALɣWAN izddɣ bac ad rafən tasutin timaynutin.

Izdɣ ad tn inin i imzwura n igʷmma mas tɣʷssa n aqqln izddɣ maca ur illa kra, izddɣ kra n tggʷra, izddɣ bac ad ssalmdn i ilmmaẓn d tmlluẓin ad ssinen iman nsn, ad ssinen urmazn nsn li illa gr-asn urmazn n tɣʷssa niman nsn.

Izddɣ ad tn inin i imzwura n igʷmma mas arrac ar ttkksan s tayri ula s uɣyur n tmɣart.

Iga kra n wazzal d unguz ad nḥḍu tɣawssiwin nneɣ n iɣil, tamɣart nneɣ ilan, tmslilin nneɣ ilan, tɣawssiwin nneɣ imazalanin ɣar arrac nneɣ li ɣad ɣlin.

Ilmmaẓn d tiḥbibin nttni kra li nttini nḥḍu d iga kra n ungruj ad nɣil amaḍal s kra li ilan.

Isrdasn d tisrdasin n tinml, isinan d tsddawiyin izddɣ ad sswdun ɣar usinan n iswi, i imzwura d yimmatn n igʷmma d uẓẓawan bac asn ssalmdn abrid n tɣʷssa n tɣawssiwin ɣar arrac nsn d ɣar timtti d umaḍal.

IMSSALDA llan gr-asn tawuri ad ɣrn iman nsn d ad ssalmdn i imzwura d yimmatn n igʷmma.

Nḥwaj ad narm s tidt bac ad nɣyur amaḍal. Nḥwaj ad nddukla bac ad nraf gr-anɣ, tagadirat iḥlan n Tasut Tamaynut li ɣad tbda ɣar wakud li ɣad igli ugʷmmar n uswingm.
