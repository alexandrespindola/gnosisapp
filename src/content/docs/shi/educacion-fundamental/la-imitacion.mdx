Iga ttyubda belli ixuf iṭṭaf afus n ussurif illa wassif. Lḥala n tɣrmiwin n middn mqqurnin, ur illi kra n cck belli tlla ɣ ixuf.

Yawi uḍrir amquran ɣef yemma-s ɣef atig n laman. Argaz amquran yawi ɣef tmtut-ns issin is t yut mliḥ. Tmtut amqurant tawi ɣef urgaz-ns d warraw-ns tssin is tn tɛzza mliḥ.

Zɣ tmuɣli n tfganit, yufa-d mliḥ ulaḥya issin belli ixuf ɣassa ittbadl s lbss n tayri.

Middn nna ur illi gisn tuggt n tɣɣratin tisimanin, middn nna gan ifqiren s ukkuz, ar ttqllbn zunza ɣef kra ad kmmln.

Middn nna gan ifqiren s ukkuz, ar ttddrn daym s iftnan, daym ɣ tɛccacin, tisknin, tiɣriwin tilwmanin, ddu.

Middn nna gan ifqiren s ukkuz ar ttddrn zɣ ixuf ɣef ixuf, zun ddu tga, ar ttawi ɣef urgaz, ɣef tmtut, ɣef imawlan, ɣef warraw, ɣef tmdditn tiqdimin nna yaggugn d yuffgen, ddu.

Kullu amɣar amɣzun d ufqir ɣ tmuɣli n tfganit, ɣaylli akk iẓlin s ixuf d ar ittɛraq s tmawsit n udrim, ɣef tmdditn n twja, ɣef irbaɛn, ɣef imktiwn-ns, zun d ar ittqllab ɣef laman. Ha yat tɣawsa nna kullun nɣ nẓṛ ukan nẓṛ mliḥ imɣaren.

Mqqar kullu imalaln illa ixuf ar ttzginn zdat uɣrf n tɣratin. Ṭbiɛn yat tmdda, anwa-t n ɣil, anwa-t n twja, agdud, ddu.

S tidt kullu tmdda tga yan uɛawd n bla tɛqqalt, tɛṛṛam, ur illi gisn kra n watig.

Kullu middn illa gisun yat tmyaqqant n ad msalan ɣef wayyaḍ. ɣaylli n ad msalan iga tifawt n ixuf.

Middn amquran ar msalaɣn kullu wid akk nna yakkan afus. Ar msalaɣn argaz, tmtut, warraw, iɣrma, imddakwal nna yḥudda, ddu.

Ad MSALAɣ iga tagmat n ixuf. Ad MSALAɣ ad ittḍfa kullu assurf illa wassif.

ɣ tizlatin, ɣ timrsalin, ɣ tsdawitin, imassan d imassatin ar ittɛccun aɣlṭ ad sɣrun imzwura d tmezwarin, ɣaylli akk ism as Ad MSALAɣ.

ɣ tizlatin n ussiggl d usksl ar sɣrun inlmudn ad sɣtan, ad kksln tugniwin n tajjart, ixxaman, idrar, imudar, ddu. Ɣaylli ur itga assurf. Ɣaylli iga Ad MSALAɣ, AFSAN.

Ad SSURF ur iga Ad MSALAɣ. Ad SSURF ur iga AFSAN. Ad SSURF iga ad nsmɣur, ad nsrs yan usɣar nna yawsan afus, tasut n tafukt tazggʷaɣt, tizi n ṣṣbaḥ s tiɣriwin-ns, ddu.

Illa assurf s tidt ɣ tẓuri n Ccinwa d ɣ tapanit n ZEN, ɣ tẓuri ttjridt d Semi-Ttjridt.

Anwa ikksl Ccinwa n CHAN d ZEN ur yutta ɣef Ad MSALAɣ, ad afsan. Imkkslen n Ccinwa d tapanit: ar ttɛɛccan ar ssursun d ar ttlqqqn ad ssursn.

Imkkslen n ZEN d CHAN, ur msalaɣn, ar SSURFN, ɣaylli tga cɣl-nnsun.

Imkkslen n CCINA d tapanit ur yutta ɣef ad kksln anwa afsan tmtut, ar ttɛɛccan ad srsn tẓuri-nnsn ttjridt.

Imkkslen n CCINA d tapanit ur msalaɣn ula d tasut n tafukt tazggʷaɣt, ar ttɛɛccan ad srsn ɣ tẓuri ttjridt kullu lḥss n umẓi.

Ɣaylli mqqurn ur iga Ad MSALAɣ, ad sɣtuɣn ɣ ubrkan nɣ ɣ umlil; ɣaylli mqqurn iga ad nḥss mliḥ atig n tẓuri d ad nssn ad srsn, maca afad aylli ur illi ixuf, attawi ɣef iɣulfan, ɣef tmdda, nɣ ixuf ɣef ɣaylli ad qqarn nɣ ɣef uzmmim n umassan.

Iga imalaln ɣef aylli akk imassan d imassatin ad fhmen aḥwaj n belli inlmudn d inlmudat ad sɣrawn afus n ussurif.

Aflla n kullu tamɣriwin yufa-d aɛqqal ad sɣrun, imzwura ad MSALAɣ. Yugar ad sɣrun ad ssursun.

Ad mddn s lḥal iga asurdi yṭṭsn ur issin, nna issin ad MSALAɣ.

Ar nmsalaɣn lbss ajnan d zɣ usalaɣ aylli tḍfran tijradin n tmddit.

Ar nmsalaɣn turdiwin ajnan mqqar aylli ttɛlqn mliḥ.

Ar nmsalaɣn tucḍawin, ar nmsalaɣn kullu ɣaylli yutn, ɣaylli daym ar ittɛawdan ɣ wakud, ddu.

Iga aḥwaj belli IMASSAN D IMASSATIN n tizlatin ad sɣrun i inlmudn ad fkrn iman-nnsn s yat tɣɣra tazaykut.

Imassan ad ufyawn i inlmudn kullu tagntatin afad ad ḥbsn ad gntin AWTUMATIN IMITATIVIN.

Imassan ad ussn i inlmudn tigntatin yufn afad ad sɣrawn afus ussurif.

Iga imalaln belli inlmudn ad ssn tidt n tlili, afad bla ixuf ukan ad lmdn ad fkrn iman-nnsn, tazaykut.

Akal nna ittddrn d amɣzun ɣef ɣaylli ad qqarn, akal nna ITMSALAɣ, s ixuf ɣef ad yuffugn timdditn, iɣulfan, turdiwin, ddu. Ur iga akal ussurif, ur iga akal azayku.

Akal n middn iga zun akk tazurart nna yṭṭfqn d tzmmimt s saa tzmmimin, tazurart anda ur illi kra ajdid ad yuɣal, tazurart anda ur ikcm uɣnfus, tazurart anda ar tḥkum iman d tiɛqqi.

Ɣaylli AJDID ukan ad yuɣal anda ur illi ixuf, anda ur illi Ad MSALAɣ, anda ur llin attawi ɣef tɣawsiwin, ɣef idrimn, ɣef middn, ɣef tmdditn, ɣef turdiwin, ddu.

Middn ar ttddrn d imɣzunen ɣef iftnan, ɣef tḥessad, ɣef turdiwin n twja, ɣef ibdidn, ɣef tumrast n ad arḥn iḍṛfa, ad ɣlin, ad alin, ad mmddun ɣef taɣzut, ad ɛzzin iman-nnsn, ddu.

Iga imalaln belli IMASSAN d IMASSATIN ad sɣrun i inlmudn-nnsn imzwura d tmezwarin, aḥwaj n ur ad MSALAɣn kullu aswir-a iqqurn d yuffgen n tɣawsiwin tiqdimin.

Iga imalaln belli INLMUDN ad lmdn ɣ tizlatin ad ssursun tazaykut ad fkrn tazaykut, ad hssn tazaykut.

Inlmudn d inlmudat ar ɛddan ɣaylli yufn ɣ tudrt-nnsn ɣ tizlatin ar ttɣaman iɣarasn maca ur ilin wakud afad ad fkrn ɣ kullu tɣawsiwin-a.

ɛcṛa nɣ ḥmct imalasn ɣ tizlatin ar ttddrn tudrt n isurdin ur issin d ar ttẓẓawn zɣ tizlatin s tmussni nna yṭṭsn, maca ar ttẓẓawn zɣ tizlatin zun d ssn mliḥ.

Akal n ufgan ar ittḍfa ger imɣan imḍfaln d imɣan ittraranin.

Ad ufgan ur yumin ad ifkr s yat tlili n tidt acku yla d ixuf.

Ad ufgan yla d ixuf i tudrt, ixuf i iman, ixuf i ɣaylli ad qqarn, iqqar belli qqaren, i tisknin, ad iḍḍɛa amuday, ad yuffugn iɣulfan, belli yan ad as yaggug argaz nɣ ad as yaggug tmtut, ddu., ddu.

ɣ tizlatin ar nttasalaɣ d ar ntẓẓu zɣ tizlatin ntbadl d IMITALEN.

Middn ar ttmsalaɣn s ixuf ɣef ɣaylli ad qqarn imalaln yaḍn, inlmudn d inlmudat ar ttmsalaɣn acku IMASSAN illaɣn ttɛrqan s tidt inlmudn ifqiren, ar tn ttɛrqan ɣ kullu tizi, ar tn ttɛrqan s taɣri nna yuffgen, ar tn ttɛrqan s tɣarasin n tɛqqabin, ar tn ttɛrqan s usɣzzif, ddu.

Iga aḥwaj belli inlmudn d inlmudat n tizlatin, tirmst nɣ tsdawit, ad ttguggn zɣ kullu IMITALEN afad ad ttgntin issurifen n tidt.

Ar ttɛlqn imassan d imassatin nna ittwamnɣn belli inlmudn d inlmudat aḥwaj n Ad MSALAɣ afad ad lmdn. Win ITMSALAɣ ur ilmmd, win ITMSALAɣ ittbadl d AWUTUMAT d ɣaylli ɣaylli akk.

Akal nna issin ukan ad MSALAɣ iga MAKINIKA, tga tamakint nna ttɛccat, UR iga asssurif, ur yumin ad issurif, ur ifkr s tidt, ar ittɛawda ukan d ɣaylli akk.
