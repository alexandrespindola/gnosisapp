Ma iga Unck (YO) yan, isuggi n Udus Afsawan ad itwaskr s yat tɣaras arɣasn, macc iẓḍaṛ uḍnyat Unck illa g kraygat yan s tɣaras Tamagḍurt.

Unck Amagḍur iga-t ssbab amqqran n kullu tmẓẓigin nnaɣ n zdɣ.

Ma nẓḍaṛ ad nẓr iman nɣ g yan lmir amssḍḍan zun d mad ngga Afsawan s kullu tmẓẓigin nnaɣ n zdɣ, ad nawḍ s tnbḍit tanqqrist mas urta nɣlla Tilawt n Yan.

Ssbab amqqran n Usidgr Afsawan iga-t lḥsd nna izdin ad izzṛi kra n tikkal s tɣaras ifljan d tayyawin.

Lḥsd iga-t arbib d illan igʷmmaḍ n imassn bac ad t-nssḍf. Lḥsd iga-t tirmt tusligt n kullu takumiwit tanmtit. I Imbuɣasn t-izzgil ad ssḍfn lḥsd.

Ulfann ur qnnɛn s buɛssu, asɣɣur d uzduɣ. Tirmt tusligt n lḥsd ɣef utumubil n wayyaḍ, ɣef tgmmi n wayyaḍ, ɣef lbast n ubḍri, ɣef idrimn idrusn n umdkkal ula n ayyawn, d s wayyur. Ittuyskarn iridn ad tɣiwtnt, ad awrn tissas d tissas, lbsat, kra, iflsn bac ur ngga arɣasn ɣef wayyaḍ d s wayyur.

Mad iḍaɛn ukan ɣef ayad akkʷ iga-t mas tamɣart n tagʷrt n uzmz, iflsn, tissas, idrimn d s wayyur ittnɛḍn Unck Amagḍur nna ittnqqlan ɣinna g tmẓẓigin nnaɣ n zdɣ, ad ttfrk tmara, tiɣrfatin ɣriɣtinin d tiɣratin timɣlalin n tfgga nnaɣ n zdɣ d s wayyur.

Kullu ayad iga-t asgʷasn. Ur illi ɣinna mad izdin ad ibbiḥ azɣar ifljan i walli iraɣ. Kullu ayad ittuyskarn tamɣart n uḥṛrac g tfggawnnaɣ, tumḍnt n asgʷasn, abbiḥ kraygat tikklt d uɣssan.

Unck Amagḍur ittɣal kullu ssḍfn ula i tsrfatin arqqanin d ɣinna g tɣaras n lḥsd, ad taɣ, ad tqql, ad tɣiwitnt ula nttat ɣef lxdmt n wayyaḍ, ttinin-as asmgʷri, asmgʷra, tazdayt d s wayyur.
