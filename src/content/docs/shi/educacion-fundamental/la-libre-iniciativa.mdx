Millions n imzwura gigan n inelmaden ɣ kullu tmizar n ddunit kullu yat ass ttilin s tmazirt n usinan d tasdawit bla mayrun, mattawsen, ur illi kra n tussna, ur ssin maniɣ d manɣ.

Inelmaden uran ad qqran tusnakt, takimit, tasengama, tuggrafit, d wiyyad.

Aqqlaɣ n inelmaden ar ittaɣ kullu yat ass isfka, macc ur ittaɣ yan ussan ad istaf ad isawal maniɣ d manɣ n isfka nna, maniɣ tlkm tɣawsa n isfka nna.

Maniɣ ar nflu isfka nna? Maniɣ ar nflu isfka nna?

Inelmaden darsn tuddurt tasngirtit, aqqran is uran ad ran isfka iggutn ig gutn, ad tn skrn ɣ tmktit ur ittnammn, waxxa ad.

Ur ittfaɣ ɣ wul n inelmaden ad swaln f maɣ illa usinan, ar ttilin s usinan acku imzwura nnsn ad tn yuran, waxxa ad.

Ula inelmaden, ula islmadn, ula islmadat ar sawaln i iman nnsn: Maniɣ aɣ illa ɣaɣ? Maniɣ ddaɣ? Maygat s ttɣawsa nɣ ittawin ɣaɣ?

Islmadn, islmadat, inelmaden irgazn d inelmaden tmɣarin, darsn tussna d tdrus, ar ttuskirn am iɣarasn, ar ttilin s usinan bla mayrun, bla kra n tussna, ur ssin maniɣ d manɣ.

Illaḥ ad nffu am iɣarasn, ad nbdd tussna, ad naf iman nna maɣ iga wannay nna isɣatarn bac ad nffu i mtiḥan, bac ad nqqra, bac ad nddar ɣ yat tmazirt bac ad nqqra kullu yat ass bac ad nffu usggwas d ad ngar ssusmi, idɣaɣn, tɣawsiwin, nqqrra kura, nttala ɣ usinan d wiyyad.

Islmadn d islmadat uran ad uɣaln ar tturrin ad ssn sɣ usinan, bac ad awnɣn inelmaden ad bddn tussna.

Iɣrat mas ad nẓr iɣarasn ittqqimn ɣ tɣmrawin n usinan, ttilin isfka uran ad tn skrn ɣ tmktit bla mayrun d manɣ.

Iɣarasn ar ttxdmn iwi acku ad ffu usggwas; qqarnasn is uran ad ḥḍrn iman nnsn bac ad ɣyyn tudrt, ad afn amal, d wiyyad. Ar qqran s tɣawsiwin ur illan ɣ wallɣ nnsn f imal, ur ssin ɣilad, ur ssin s ttɣawsa maniɣ ar qqran tasengama, takimit, tuderri, tusnakt, tuggrafit, d wiyyad.

Tmarikin tiẓḍar ar qqrant bac ad zḍarn ad afn argaz ifulkin, niɣ ad ɣyyn tudrt d ad ddun ḥḍat ad illa argaz ur as izḍar niɣ ad dggʷrn niɣ ad ggʷrn tmeɣra nnsn.

Waxxa ad illan ɣ wallɣ acku ur ssin imal nnsn maniɣ d manɣ ar ttmmutn.

Tuddurt ɣ usinan tlla bdda, ur tlla kra, ur tlla tussna, aqqaɣ ar ttaɣ mad ittanfaɛn ɣ tudrt nns.

Illaḥ ɣilad ɣ usinan ad nffu usggwas waxxa ad.

Zikk illa kra n ɣilli maɣ illa usggwas. ɣilad ur illi kra n ɣilli. Imzwura n waddrn tnt ittaɣn ɣ ssr islmad niɣ aslmadat d aqqaɣ ad illa ur yufi kra ɣ usinan, aqqay ad iffu usggwas.

Taɣarasn n usinan ar skrrant tɣawsiwin i islmad bac ad ffu usggwas, ittutmaɣ, waxxa ur ttssinn "J" n maɣ ittslmad aslmadi, aqqant ar dffun ɣ mtiḥan d ar ttffun usggwas.

Llan iɣarasn d tɣarasn imqqurn bac ad ffun usggwas. Aqqay iga tɣawsa ifulkin ɣ imqqurn.

Yan aqqaɣ ittffu yat mitḥan (yat mitḥan n wawal) ur illa kra n tussna f maɣ ilmma.

Aqqaɣ ar ittutlay am tayuḍaḍt d ar ittwali madda ittilmd ɣ maygan.

Ur iga tussna ɣ magan nna, iga tmktit d tayuḍaḍt n maɣ nttalmad waxxa ad.

Ad nffu i mtiḥan, ad nffu usggwas ur iga ntahya. ɣ tudrt naf middn imqqurn ur ufin ɣ usinan ɣ mtiḥan. Naf middn ar tirn d tusnakt ur ufin ɣ usinan ɣ mtiḥan ɣ wawal d tusnakt.

Nssn yan aqqaɣ ur issin madda iga tuderri ar ibdda tmaɣut bac ad yafu ɣ mtiḥan n tuderri. ɣilad illa ar tirr f madda iga tuderri.

Ad nffu usggwas ur iga ntahya. Llan middn ur ttfunn usggwas d darsn ntahya.

Illa kra izwaren ad nffu usggwas, illa kra izwaren ad nqqra magan aqqraɣ nna iga ad nssn s tidt maɣ nqqra.

Islmadn d islmadat uran ad zḍarn bac ad awnɣn inelmaden bac ad bddn tussna; amdal n islmadn d islmadat uran ad nttawi tussna n inelmaden. URGENTE mas ad ggrn inelmaden tudert nnsn.

Ad nqqra tmktit, ad nqqra am tayuḍaḍt, iga tɣawsiwin ur illan.

Inelmaden uran ad qqran magan isɣatarn bac ad tn skrn ɣ tmktit nnsn bac ad "ffun usggwas" d daɣ ɣ tudrt magan nna ur ttutmaɣn acku ur illan.

Iɣarasn ar qqran bac ad afn amal d ad ɣyyn tudrt d daɣ maqqar afn aman nna, maqqar ggrn tusnimɣurin, imjjyan, d wiyyad, waxxa ad skrrn isɣar n zikk, ar zzwan, ar ittggan, d ar ttmmutn bla ma bddu tussna, ar ttmmutn bla tussna n tudrt nnsn.

Tmarikin ar zzwan, ar skrrn tarwa, ar ttmmɣn d imjiran, d argaz, d tarwa, ar ttfrqn d ar zzwan daɣ, ar ttddggʷrn, ar ttggʷrn tiɣarasn, d ar ttmmutn baɛd ma ddarn tdrus, ur illi kra n tussna, ar ttuskirn am zikk asɣar ifɣar n tudrt.

Ur uran islmadn d islmadat ad ssnn mas amdan illa ddrus ɣ tussna. Illaḥ mas d islmadn ad bddn bac ad awnɣn inelmaden.

Ur ttnafɛn mad nffu aqqlaɣ iqquran d ad nskr Danté, Homero, Virgilio, d wiyyad, maygan illa ddrus tussna illa ma nssn kra f iman nna, maɣ nqqra, tudrt.

Maɣ ittafaɛ usinan illa ur ngi kra, illa ur nssn maɣ tidt?

Tussna n tidt ur tga ad nqqra d ad ntra. Waxxa mad illa ar izḍar ad yaqqra d ad yara.

Uran ad nggʷr ntahya d ntahya ar tbdd ddrus ma tbdd tussna.

Timitar illa dars tiẓḍar d tdrus tussna.

Uran ad nbdd tussna, uran ad nggʷr tagʷnsayt n tussna. Uraq ad illa dars tussna.

Amdan ur ittrun waxxa illa ddrus adar nns, is ittru daɣ ad illa ɣ tudrt.

Illaḥ ad nffu ittru, illaḥ ad nbdd tussna d asɣar nna uran ad ibdu sɣ tgmra d sɣ usinan.

Amdal n islmadn uran ad ittawi tussna n inelmaden d ur waxxa tmktit.

Inelmaden uran ad zḍarn ad istafn iman nnsn d ur waxxa ad ittutlayn am tayuḍaḍt.

Islmadn uran ad ttaln bac ad skrn ibddaɣn ɣ inelmaden.

Islmadn uran ad awen inelmaden tagmrt ad mmagrn d ad skrn tɣawsiwin ifulkin.

Ur illa mas ad awen ad aqqran kra n tɣawsiwin ɣ usinan.

Uran inelmaden ad mmagrn bac ad zḍarn ad istafn iman nnsn. Illaḥ ad mmagrn bac ad zḍarn ad skrn magan qqran.

Ibddaɣ iga aɣaras f ntahya. Aqqaɣ illa dars ibddaɣ ur izḍar ad yassawal d ar ittaɣ amaḍal n tɣawsiwin maqqran ntiɣ.

Ur ttnafɛn ma ar sawaln islmadn illa darsn ibddaɣ. Islmadn uran ad illi darsn tagmrt. Islmadn illan dars ibddaɣ ar tturirn, d wiyyad, ur zḍarn ad ggʷrn tidt.

Asɣar n usinan iga ad nffu ibddaɣ d ad nbdd tussna.

Maɣ ar nttafaɛ illa ad nffu mtiḥan illa ur ntilla ibddaɣ d ur nssn kra?

Islmadn darsn usɣar ad awnɣn inelmaden sɣ usinan bac ad nafɛn ɣ tudrt, mac illa ibddaɣ ur iga ad illi kra.

Amdan illa dars ibddaɣ ur izḍar ad yassawal mad sawaln wiyyad.

Amdan illa dars ibddaɣ ur illa dars ad istaf.

Usɣar n usinan iga ad awn iwi ɣ usinan bac ad illi tagmrt bla ibddaɣ.

Illaḥ mas d inelmaden ad mmagrn bac ad zḍarn ad istafn iman nnsn.

Ad illa inelmaden s iman nnsn, s tagmrt zḍarn ad skrn maqqran tɣawsiwin nna ad ttqqran, ad skrn d waxxa ad llan.

Illaḥ mas illa ad istaf bac ad tbd ntahya ɣ inelmaden.

Illaḥ ad naf iwi ad tirn d ad skrn mad uran s iman nnsn i inelmaden bac ad zḍarn ad ssnn maɣ qqran.

Tiɣrad n ufgan illa bddaɣ d illaḥ ad nbdd ar ttffu ibddaɣ ɣ middn.

Uran ad nssn tudrt nna d asɣar ad uran ad ibdu ɣ usinan.

Ur ttnafɛn ma nqqra ɣ usinan illa nffu ur nssn kra.

Ad nffu ibddaɣ d ad nffu adnaf s iman ar ittawi timitar.

S iman nnsn inelmaden uran ad awn ttagmrt ɣ kullu usinan ad swaln ɣ yat tmgguri madd qqran.

Waxxa illa tagmrt d ad skrn, ad istafn, ad suln, ad skrn maqqran nqqra, ad naf ad nggʷr tussna n magan d waxxa ad nttutlay am tayuḍaḍt maɣ nskr ɣ tmktit.
