Ur illi d uɣil ur llin. Aylli iga winu iga ṣaḥ, aylli ur igi winu ur iga ṣaḥ. Ur illi d uɣil iga asers n tɣawsiwin yattan d tiɣessa n wul.

Argaz iraḥan ismawen n uɣil d ulli iga Atlanti ism as Makari Kronvernkzyon yan zɣ imzwura ɣ tmazirt n Akaldan, illan ɣ ugld n Atlantis.

Ur izmir ad ittaggʷa ayt ussan zikk d amqqran nnes illa ad yawi s tugtt ɣ ufgan, s users n sin ismawen nnes.

Imussniwn n Atlantis gren afus nsnn ɣ kra iga t yan zɣ imikkun izddɣn, imikkun ikššumn d wiyyaḍ illan ɣ wammas n tmtta, maccan argaz amusni zikk d amqqran nnas izddɣn illa d tɣessa nnas ad issḍfr sin n imzwura n ismawen n uɣil d ulli. S imikkun izddɣn issaɣ asen ism n ṣaḥ d imikkun ikššumn issaɣ asen ism n uɣil.

Imikkun ad illa ɣ ufgan d tmtta illa imikk illan ɣ wammas nnsn iga t asers d ulmmud.

Tugtt n isggʷasn ḍart n utṭḍs n Atlantis d Poseidonis nnes yattunn Platón ɣ tmnukda nnes, illa yan uɣanim ɣ tamazirt taẓɣurant Tiklyamishayana iraḥn tigmmi n uɣil d ulli. Argaz n uɣanim illa Armanatoora.

S yat ɣ tmzgida n tmtti s tudrt z sin ismawen ad, tnɣuba tmuntt d isnḍfn nnsn. ɣassa ar nttafa sin ismawen ad ɣ usikn.

ɣassa llan tugtt n imqqran iraḥn tsslsa n tmtti maccan imikkun nnssn illan gr uɣil d ulli.

Tmuntt tkka tt ɣ sin ismawen n uɣil d ulli.

Sin ismawen n uɣil d ulli ar srs nntḥujja s uɣil nɣ ar nttḥkum f wiyyaḍ.

Win iran ad issn ur illa ira ad issn amzwaru. Iga t kra n tɣessa ad tassntkra tasrtit maccan ur iga t kra n tɣessa ad ntḥujja as s yism n ṣaḥ. Iga t kra n tɣessa ad ntḥujja ɣ tin izddɣn maccan ad nttḥkum as s yism n uɣil.

Kra iga t imikk illa zdat izmir ad yssḍfr s imikk win illan ɣ wammas. Kra iga t imikk ikššum izmir ad yssḍfr s imikk zddɣn.

Illa ɣ imikkun n tmtta illa imikkun n izddɣn illa imikkun n tmtta illa imikkun n ikššumn.

Illa ɣ ufgan illa imikkun isrttitnn, imikkun ikššumnin d wiyyaḍ ikššumnin.

Ad ntḥujja kra n imikkun ntḥukum f kra, ur iga t ad nssn.

Tudrt n tidt tlla ɣ tmuntt s yat n tmttat tkššumn.

Tafrakt ittnkrin n uswir n Gnóstica illa ɣ tmssnti n kra iga t yan zɣ imikkun illa ɣ ufgan d tmtta.

Aswir n Gnóstica illa ɣ yan umurs ittnkrin ur illi s tmuntt n imqqran nɣ s ismawen n uɣil d ulli.

Illa ɣ tigmmi n tmssnti n ufgan illa imikkun izddɣn, ikššumn d wiyyaḍ illa ad ttssn d ad ttnttqnnq.

Isem n uɣil itṭaf ad nssn imikkun izddɣn s tḥujjit.

Isem n uɣil itṭaf ad nssn imikkun ikššumn s tḥkumt.

Ad ntḥujja nɣ ad nttḥkum ur iga t ad nssn. Win iran ad iffuɣ s tknnsit nnes ur ira ad ttḥujja nɣ ad ittḥkum. Iggʷra ad nssn tknnsit nnaɣ.

Ad nssn aɣzzaf ɣ uswir n uɣaras n tɣessa nɣ ad dars nssntkra tssidt d waman.

Ad nssn imikkun n iɣurfan d ufsur illa ad dars nssntkra tmlla d tmazirt.

Ad nssn tmnnawit ɣ uswir n uɣaras illa ad dars nssntkra tudrt n ttbut.

Ad nssn tɣessa ɣ uɣaras n tɣassa illa ad dars nssntkra tudrt s tamunt.

Ad nssn imikkun n tɣessa ɣ tɣassa n uswir ad dars nssntkra tiɣessa n tɣessa.

Ad nssn aylli iga tarẓẓumt n tɣessa nɣ ad dars nssntkra iswi d tɣessa.

Ad nssn imikkun n tɣessa illa ad nḥbu timsal nns illa imikkun win llanin kraɣn ikkattin ɣ tɣessa.

Imzwura d imzwura n uswir ar srsn sswdduḥn ad srḥbn s tɣessa.

Iggʷra ad nssn ur illa ṣaḥ imik nɣ ad nssntkra tudrt s yat n tin n uɣaras.

Tudrt tlla ɣ uɣaras n uswir. Tiɣessa tlla ɣ tudrt nnaɣ asggʷas nnaɣ d tɣessa.

Win iran ad irḥu, ad iran tiɣessa nɣ ad ittɣal tɣessa nɣ ad iran tudrt s timnnawt illa ad issmqqur uɣaras.

Nḥwjj i timttat n uswir ad nttḥbu uswir nnaɣ. Asmi d nmut ur illa ɣinn illa kra iga t winna iga ṣaḥ. Winna iṭṭafn tɣessa winna iga tmtta n ttbut nnaɣ ur illi z usggʷas.

S tmssnti n kra iga t yan zɣ imikkun n tmtta n izddɣn illan ɣ wammas nnaɣ.

Ikkattinn ur illi ɣ uɣaras ad ntḥujja nɣ ad nttḥkum ɣ udm nnaɣ.

Kra iga t imik illa tɣessa, illa winn issḍfrn illa winn ikššumn illa winn illan ɣ tɣessa. Ad nssn imikkun nɣ ur illi ɣ ufgan illa win illan ɣ tɣessa.

Kra iga t win izmir ad iffuɣ z uswir.

Uɣaras issḍfr s tadra n uḥkkam. Tugtt ar ttirin ad ur ilin ur illin maccan ar ttirin tiɣessa d tdusi.

Tugtt z argaz d tmɣart ar ttgaggʷan ɣ udm n uɣaras "DIZQUE" acku ar ttriran tmdyazt, uswir nnssn ttgaggʷan s tɣessa issḍfrn.

Tugtt n imjddarn ard tnɣuban d imzwura nɣ illa ad ɣinn ɣ ussan nnssn ḥbu tɣessa.

Tugtt n imjddarn ard tnɣuban ɣ imzwura nɣ illa ad darsn yawi s tɣessa d tdusi ar ttzzalayn f wiyyaḍ.

Illa win ar izzdɣ s tɣessa nns.

Diogenes ar izzdɣ s uḍuf illa ɣinn d asmi d illa d Socrates isla ar inni: "Ar ttgddma tɣessa nk Socrates, ar ttgddma tɣessa nk". "Ih Diogenes, s tɣessa nk ar ttgddma tɣessa nnu".

Tmɣarin win ttgaggʷan ard ttffɣn isqqʷm nnssn d udm nnssn bac ad ttgaggʷan ɣ tmɣarin win yaḍn.

Ar ittini asgd as Aristipo amusnav iwtta ad ismlil ɣ akkʷ aylli illa s tɣessa nns d tudrt nns isslsa tazzit imqqurn d igjda, issɣa tazkka ɣ ufus nns idman isrḥ s tisqqitin n Atenas. Asmi d illa Socrates inna: "Ttggaggʷa tɣessa nk z igjda n tazit nk, oh Aristipo".

Tugtt ɣ illa ɣ tɣessa nɣ illa ayt isrḥbn kra iga t yan zɣ uswir nns bac ad yssntkra tadusi.

Tugtt n win iḍarn tudrt d tiɣessa maccan ar ttzullun.

Kra iga t imikk illa tɣessa nns illa winn issḍfrn illa winn illan ɣ wammas.

ɣ tɣessa illa win issḍfrn.

Kra iga t win n tɣessa.

Ur illi d uɣil, sin ismawen ad ad srs nnḥujja.
