ilzem ad nafham s ttmam d deg akk igran n uxemmem aylli igan lmut s tidett, xaf aylli ad yili, s tidett, ad nafham s kamel mayga tifawt.

Ait n ufus n ufgan n umeddakkel nnegh yedda deg tasaft ur ittaggim is nafham sser n lmut.

Tidett tga aylli ur nessin deg imik s imik. Tidett f lmut ur izmir ad tili d tawala.

Anu yebgha daymen, am ma yella d amecta, aman n lmut, taminet tanzzult, kra n tgelda ayghan ad anegh aman n waddad yaggugen d ullus n tifawt deg deffir n ugeffur ayhewwlen.

NEKKINI ur illi ayd yebgha ad yemmet. Anu yebgha ad yelhu. Anu yettagad lmut.

Tidett ur tgi tabrat n atig d uskar. Tidett ur illi ayda yettaf deg temment, ur deg uskar. Tidett ur tgi tabrat n igharasen, tigert, tanumi, tigert azzelan, taggugt, azref, taggugt, asawal, igriwen, atg. Tidett f sser n lmut ur tgi tawala.

Tidett f sser n lmut illa yili ad nafham yezzi s tassemmirt tazzelant.

Yuggen ad nerzu tassemmirt tizerfit n lmut i win ur tt issinen.

Kra n umdyaz izmir ad yaru idlis amenhrayen n tayri, maca yuggen ad nerzu tidett f tayri i medden ur tt id yufin deg tazzelant, am annini nga is yuggen ad nerzu tidett f lmut i medden, ur tt id yufin.

Win yebghan ad yssen tidett f lmut ilzem ad issers, ad yessers s yiman nnes, ad yetsnuqqes am ma yella dayes, xaf aylli ad nerra awal i ma daynna yessnen n lmut.

Asseqsi d tassemmirt n wussan agguggen an-yufan ad nafham is medden ur ttafhamen s tidett ma daynna yessnen n lmut; i medden aylli dayda yettaf s tidett tga ad lhuyen deg deffir aylli d aylli.

Medden agguggen bghan ad lhuyen s imalen, atig, tawja, asirem, igharasen, arrac, atg., d mani ad fhemmen is kra n lhul n umkan tagnawt tga ur tessa'ay, taneggart, tedda, tekkus, mani ad zzin i medden ur illa aman, ur issinen, atg, ahedren, azzullen s tarwa n tuggna.

Ur bghan ad fhemmen medden ijjijen, ur bghan ad fhemmen is ma yella daylli yettawsen deg tallunt.

Ur bghan ad fhemmen medden ijjijen is ma yella daylli yettawsen itta'ay deg tallunt.

Ur bghan ad fhemmen medden ijjijen is ma yella daylli yettawsen yettwaqqaf d aman, ittujerreb, idrusen.

ilzem, illa, tella, ad nili nessen s ttmam i ma daynna yessnen n lmut, xaf aylli ad tbeddel taggazt ad nekkes ad nili.

S usseqsi s ttmam i medden, izmir an ad naf is uxemmem yella daymen yettwaqqaf deg ma daylli yessen d yebgha ad annili ma daylli yessen yettawsen deg deffir ugeffur.

Uxemmem yettwaqqafen deg ma daylli yessen, ur izmir ad yuf ma daylli ur yessen, tidett, tizerfit.

Xaf yezzi s ubbus n tallunt s ttirra n tifrat tazzelant, izmir an ad naf ATMA, UR TALLUNT, TIDETT.

Win yebghan ad lhuyen agaggaz deg lmut d asirem nnsen d tigert nnsen ur ten-yessaffen ad qqimen.

Lmut s yiman nnes ur tilla aydda yettaqqadan, tga kra yelhan, yesqqa'an, ur ittunafen, maca uxemmem yettwaqqafen: deg ma daylli yessen, ar ittettew deg ugriw ad yelhan aylli yezzin seg temment ar uskar.

Mani ad nili s tidett nafhamen s ttmam i ma daynna yessnen n lmut, nerra awal i yiman nnegh yezzi s tassemmirt tazzelant, is tuddurt d lmut ggwan akk ijj n ussul.

Lmut tga amkan n tuddurt. Abrid n tuddurt yella yessuddes s udmawen n igeddar n lmut.

Tuddurt tga tuderfet yettwagerwen d yettwagerwen. Seg wass n tlalit ar lmut ttettewen deg ufgan kra n tuderfet.

Awal i ma daynna tuderfet ayghan afgan ur izmir ad tessers, tga TIRRI N LMUT. Tirri adi tella tuderfet tamenca'yt n tuggna. Afgan ur izmir ad tessers tuderfet adi.

Am amaytir izmir ad yeffer ijj n tgemmi, am annini TIRRI N LMUT mani ad tettew deg ufgan, ad ten-teffer s ttmam.

Tirri n lmut tettwessan d usufghan lmut, d usufghan tlalit.

Tirri n lmut tettagger tanekkizen tamenca'yt n tuggna d ijj n tggurt d tayessa yella, ayghan agerwen n ilummen deg ubbayyu yettewfakan.

Tirri n lmut tesserd afgan ar ilummens nnes izzilen.

EGO, ANU n tuderfet, ittawsen deg warraw nnegh yezzi s tuksa.

Ma tga tidett f lmut, ma tga ussan deg ger lmut d tewfaket tga kra ur illan deg tallunt d yezzi s tussna n tifrat izmir an ad nessi.

Igerramen d Igerramten n Izdayen, Tigemmatin d Tisdawiyin, ilzem ad sselmaden i inelmaden nnsen, abrid ayghan ar tassemmirt n TIDETT, n TIDETT.
