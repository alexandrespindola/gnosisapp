Yurra d-iga-d-tewwet ula ad-neffeɣ akʷ fell-as iḥecmilen ifellusen d-tikʷerhaz deg uɣurruy n-tmgrawt.

Ilaq ad-nefhem s-talɣa yessuffɣen d-yesḥeqqen uɣurruy n-tmgrawt i-warrac d-teqcicin, sgʷi-sen akkʷ.

Deg 14 ussan, tettban i-gʷris deg tfekka n-warrac tinerrgi-n-tmgrawt i-ttezzi-n-tmgrawt mi tettɣell fell-as deg unagraw neuro-sympathique.

Anaw-ad n-tinerrgi-n-wexṣaṣ tetbeddel tfekka n-ufgan, tettbeddel taɣect deg urgaz, tettbeddel tawuri n-tmagit n-teqcict.

Tfekka n-ufgan d-tasila yessuffuɣen igʷrisen i-tgezzumin deg isuffaɣen yessudden.

Adɣaṛan i-nessa s-wurẓum tettaɣ-d aṭaṣ n-beddel d-tesrifuɣin armi tettɛeddi deg usuffeɣ asemi-solide, asemi-liquide i-yettawsen Paracelse s-tɣarast n-Ens-Seminis (Tawwurt n-Semen)

Aɣersa n-wemɣi-n-waman-ad yettwaxxen, yettɛawwaqn, yesɛa deg-s amecṭuḥ, deg talɣa tgezzumt akkʷ imɣan n-tudert.

Gnosticisme yettarra deg userm a-CAOS i-yeffɣen seg-s s-tiggtudert.

Imusaɣen iqedimen medioevales am Paracelse, Sendivogius, Nicolás Flamel, Raymundo Lulio, d-wiyaḍ, ɣran s-tmesɣelt t-meqqʷran ENS-SEMINIS neɣ mercurio n-t فلسفة تفريت.

VITRIOLO-ad, d-elixir yessuffuɣen s-tmusni, i-tlelli deg iɣerman aseminales.

Deg mercurio-ad n-tmusni taqburt, deg userm-ad, ufan akkʷ iswiren n-tgemmi.

D-amcum adɣar i-aṭaṣ n-ilemɣanen ɣas-tella tuddsa psicológica yessaɣen ad-fkeɣ i-tɣerṣa n-masturbación neɣ ad-kʷsen s-iberdan infra-sexual n-homosexualismo.

I-warrac d-ilemmɣanen nerra-asen tinfalin intelektualit fell-asen aṭaṣ n-udmawen n-tmagit d-nerrasen s-iberdan n-deportismo i-yettɣallalen tudert s-tgemmi, maca s-taruga mi tettban tinerrgi n-tmgrawt yessuffuɣen talemɣart, akkʷ imawlan n-twacult am asselmaden n-timizar, ɣas-ɣur-sen puritanismo ifellus d-taɣma ifellusen, ɛawden ad-ssusmen s-ucettim.

Llɣen ussusen ittɛeddan d-llɣen awal ifellusen. Ad-ssusmem fell-as uɣurruy n-tmgrawt d-ucettim. Ad-yettmeslay s-ɣers deg uɣurruy n-tmgrawt tettaɣ-d ula d-nettat ucettim.

Ma s-susmen imawlan d-asselmaden, ad-ttmeslayen imeqqranen sexuales d-tudert tetteɣ-d ilemmɣanen isuffaɣen.

Ma ur yezmir ulemɣan ad-yenṭeq i-imawlan wala i-asselmaden, ad-yeɛya ass-ɣa i-iwizlan n-timizar yeqqanen ad-wsen s-iberdan ifellusen. Tudert ur tettɣalla aṭaṣ n-wakud d-ulemɣan ajdid ad-yiwi fell-as taɛya fell-as s-iɣerman ifellusen ad-yekkes i-tɣerṣa n-masturbación neɣ ad-yekkes s-iberdan n-homosexualismo.

Tɣerṣa n-masturbación tetteɣ tuggʷga akkʷ tinerrgi taɛeqqalt. Ilaq ad-neɛda ad-tella tdukla yessɣanen ger userm d-uɛeqqal. Ilaq ad-neɛqql userm. Ilaq ad-nerru uɛeqqal.

Uɛeqqal yennerru ad-yeqqen tinerrgi n-tmgrawt, ad-yessudsen, ad-ttbeddel deg tinerrgi taɛeqqalt.

Deg talɣa-agi yeqqen userm yettɛeqqlen d-uɛeqqal yettnerru.

Tussna Gnóstica tettɣara adɣar endocrinología d-tesɣara tɣerṣa d-unagrawan bac ad-ttbeddel tinerrgi n-tmgrawt, maca tammurt-agi ur tettɛedda deg udlis-ad.

Ma yebɣa aɣaruɣen inɣmasen deg Gnosticismo ilaqa ad-yeɣer idlisen-nneɣ Gnósticos d-ad-yekkes deg imalasen-nneɣ.

Ilemmɣanen ad-ssudsen tinerrgi n-tmgrawt ad-ttḥassben tɣimit taɛeqqalt, ad-lmed mussiqa, la-sculpture, la-pintura, ad-ɛeddun iɣzeran s-idurar ɛlayen, d-wiyaḍ.

Aṭaṣ n-wudmawen i-yeggʷran ad-yilin yessiɣsen!

Aṭaṣ n-iɛeqqal i-yettbeddelen! Akkʷ ɣas-tella tɣerṭiṭ deg wakud yessudsen.

Tɣerṣa n-masturbación, am deg ilemmɣanen am deg tegʷmam, tuɣal tessaɣen ugar n-ssawal n-ifassen.

Timɣarin i-yettɛezzben ččuren urgazen d-tilawin i-yettɣallalen uɛeqqal deg tɣerṣa n-masturbación. Tudert n-masturbadores d-timɣari.

Tɣerṣa n-homosexualismo tesseɣ tuga n-t-raza-agi caduca d-perversa.

Tezwar ad-tettɛawan deg timizar am Inglaterra i-yettɛawanen deg kulṭur d-súper-civilizados, llɣen i-yettɣerren deg-sen l-películas n-anaw homo-sexual.

Tezwar ad-tettɛawan deg-sen deg Inglaterra anda illa imehilen bac ad-yezdiwen zwaj n-anaw homosexual.

Deg imɣuren metrópolis n-ddunyat illa tura prostíbulos d-clubes n-anaw homo-sexual.

Tdukla tenebrosa n-iɛdawen n-teṭṭut, ass-agi llaẓent tuddsa i-yettbeddelen i-tezdayt-nsen.

Aṭaṣ n-iɣarruɣen i-yezwaren ad-tessiwlen aṭaṣ n-agi n-tezdayt yettbeddelen maca ur ilaqa ad-nettu deg akkʷ wakuden n-umezruy llan akkʷ tezdayin n-ucettim.

Tdukla morbosa n-iɛdawen n-teṭṭut, teffeɣ akkʷ tezdayt n-ucettim.

Iɛdawen n-teṭṭut ttaɣen akkʷ neɣ ɣas akkʷ tawurin i-yeqqnen deg uɣerrem burocrático.

Mi yettɣell homo-sexual s-la cárcel, iɣrem ad-yeffeɣ s-imdanen i-yeqqnen seg urgazen i-yeqqnen n-tdukla n-ucettim.

Ma yettɣell afeminado deg desgracia, iɣrem ad-yeɣrem deg imawalen n-akkʷ iǧehhen siniestros n-tdukla n-ucettim.

Iɣriman tenebrosos n-homo-sexualismo ittɛawananen gar-asen s-uniforme i-ssenɛan.

Yezwar ad-neɛda imaricones ssaɛan uniforme, maca akka illa. Uniforme n-homo-sexuales yettɛawan akkʷ moda i-yeqqnen. Imaricones ssaɛan akkʷ moda yessudsen. Mi tettɛedda moda tuɣal tessaɣen, imiren ssaɛan tayeḍ. Deg talɣa-agi uniforme n-tdukla n-ucettim illa ijeddid.

Akkʷ tamdinin imqqranen n-ddunyat, ass-agi llaẓent millones n-homo-sexuales.

Tɣerṣa n-homo-sexualismo tessenɛa tallaḍt yettɣessen deg talemɣart.

Aṭaṣ n-timizar n-ilemmɣanen urgazen d-tegʷmam d-prostíbulos n-anaw homo-sexual.

Millones n-tegʷmam ilemmɣanen ttɣallalen s-wul deg iberdan tenebrosos n-iɛdawen n-urgaz.

Millones n-ilemmɣanen n-anaw tamettut d-homo-sexuales. Tdukla n-ucettim ger homo-sexualismo tamettut illa ccaḥal, am tdukla n-ucettim ger anaw urgaz.

D-azelmad ad-neffeɣ s-tiɣri-n-tgemmi fell-as iḥecmilen ifellusen d-ad-neskʷen i-ilemmɣanen n-sin anawen, franchement akkʷ misterios sexuales.

ɣas-akka ad-ttɣallalen tira timajjawin s-tusna n-REGENERACIÓN.
