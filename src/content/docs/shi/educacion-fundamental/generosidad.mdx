Illa yakka ad afgan s wawal nk ad ttyafgan, macc ur ttyafgannt middn wala afgant.

Win ar as ttinin tayri, illa yan umya ur ittawssan i middn, d ar as tggilin d tarwla d lxuf.

Awalay ad afgan middn d ad ttyafgannt, iman ad ur yiri kra n tnggura f tmazirt.

Aṭas n tzdayin i ilan ad zzayrnt, macc ur zzayrnt, ssbab nni tgrbitin i gginin g ufgan.

Awalay ad illi g middn iẓḍaṛn ad smahnt, ad ttun i waylli iḍurn zdɣn g timmad, fulknt.

Ufgan ikkat tayri, ar ttahlu. Tiḥjitin, tiṣmiḍin iẓlin, tiḍaf iẓlin, kullu mayad i gginin g ufgan ar tthulun tayri.

Aṭas n tmɣarin imttawnin zaydnt awalay ad illi gisnt iẓḍaṛn ad smahnt s ad ttunt waylli iḍurn zdɣn g timmad, ad zzayrnt d urgaz nsnt.

Aṭas n irggaz iẓḍaṛn ad zayrnt d tmɣarin nsnt awalay ad illi gisnt iẓḍaṛn ad smamnt i imikk n iḍurn zdɣn g timmad, ad zzayrnt d tmɣart nsnt.

Illa yakka ad fhmnt tzdayin taẓḍurt n wakud.

Irggaz d tmɣarin yakka ad isasnɣun i mzzwura zzdaynin, ad ttun waylli iḍurn zdɣn g timmad, ad zzayrnt s tfukt.

Tayri d tgrbitin ilan imikk ur yufi imikk. G tayri ur illi kra n tgrbit. Tayri tlla d smmḥ.

Tlla tayri g win isanɣn s tidt f tarwla n imddkʷal nsnt d imskirinn nsnt. Tlla tayri s tidt g win iskarn s wul nsnt afad ad izayd i win iḥqqran, i iglliln, i imuḥtajin.

Tlla tayri g win isnɣn s wul d zzigz f ufllaḥ yutuyy s waman f yigran, f ugʷrmmi yugʷmn, f umqqran yutuyy kra n uɣṛum d uydi iḥqqran i gʷmriḍ ittnɣan s laẓ ɣ tirirt n ubrid.

Mkan ad nɛawn yan s wul nsnt, mkan ad ndu s wul d zzigz i uggʷra d nssu uggʷaḍn n tmazirt, illa g uya tafukt, illa g uya tayri s tidt.

Macc ur zzaydnt middn, ur uɣent g tafukt. Midn ar ttaɣn g imssudutn nsnt iḥqqran, ttaɣn g win aydditn nsnt, ttaɣn g win ittaɣn amussu, win izaydnt, win iran ad fhmnt, win ẓlin s tmuḥit d uɣrrubu nnsnt, aylli zzaydnin ɣin.

G ddunit illa aṭas n middn, ilan kan tafukt iṣṣḍan. Tlla tafukt iṣṣḍan g usrtan aɛkkak, g ufalkay iɣʷddan idrimn afad ad yaf iḥrqan, ad yaf lqima, ad yaf arzzun, ad yaf timlalayin, aylli zzaydnin ɣin. Ilaḥyan ad ngga uggʷmar d tɣrda.

Tafukt s tidt tlla d ur illi gis kra n waylli ad ur izdɣn. Macca zaydnt ad ngga tafukt n usrtan aɛkkak, tafukt n imsqqammn iman, tafukt n iɛkkakn i iran tmɣart, aylli zzaydnin ɣin.

Yakka ad ngga s wul nsnt. Tafukt s tidt ur tlli g ufgan, macca nttat d tafrut n wul.

Awalay ad illi g middn tafukt ad ttun uggʷran i gginin g ufgan nsnt, ad ttun tmuḥit iḍurn zdɣn g ufgan nsnt, ad ẓḍaṛn ad zdɣn timmad s timmad, ad zzayrnt, s tidt.

Macc aẓnkʷḍ "Nkkʷni" tlla g ufgan d ar tskrra i waylli iḍurn zdɣn g ufgan. Waylli iḍurn zdɣn g ufgan ikkat middn, ar ithulun tfulki, ar ithulun tayri.

Ufgan ittuyydn g waylli iḍurn zdɣn g ufgan ur iẓḍaṛ ad ifhm s tidt taẓḍurt n wakud i nzdɣ.

Aṭas n middn i nttaran, ttssrsn tayri, ttssrsn umya iẓḍaṛn ad isḥlu wul nsnt, macc imikk ad uɣn g win issrsn i win yugʷmn.

Aṭas n middn i nttaran afad ad nnɣn aḥwal i gginin g ddunit, macc imikk ad fkin aɣṛum yutuyy afad ad tmssawaln d win iḥqqran.

Ur uɣn middn ad fhmnt ar iza zun kullu kra illa ssbab ns, d awalay ad nssfls ssbab ad nsfls kra illa.

"Nkkʷni", nkkʷni yasin, tlla d tẓḍurt i zdɣn g imzwura nnɣ, d win iskrn imikk n isbbab iẓlin i tmuḥit iḍurn zdɣn g ufgan, aylli iẓḍaṛn ad isflsn tudrt nnɣ.

Nḥwaj tafukt afad ad nssfls isbbab, ad nssfls kra illa. Nḥwaj tafukt afad ad ndu ufluk n tudrt nnɣ.

Nḥwaj tafukt afad ad nssfls tudrt nnɣ s wul.

Tafukt ilan ur tlli g ufgan. Tafukt s tidt d tayri s tidt, ur iẓḍaṛn ad illin zz lxuf.

Yakka ad nfhm lxuf ikkat tafukt, ar ithulun tafukt n wul, ar ithulun g nkkʷni lafrut n tayri.

Lxuf d azur n kra n uɛkkak, d azur n kra n iman, d aẓnkʷḍ ittnɣan.

Imalsn d imalsatn n idufr d tusdawt d tzdayin yakka ad fhmnt ẓḍuṛt n uslkʷn i inmlan nsnt ɣ tirirt n tafukt s tidt, lqima, d tidt n wul.

Middn iḥqqran d iyṭiln n tusut iḍurn zdɣn g ufgan, is ar asn fhmnt mayd lxuf ad, skrn as zun d ajjṛa ɣ tɣrmt. Kra illa n uslakʷ ad iɛkkak, iḍɣar d tanarkit.

Imalsn d imalsatn yakka ad fhmnt timmdit i nzdɣ, aḥwal i nllan d ẓḍuṛt n ulnu tusutin yasin f azur n yat tatikit tanmsastayt i tlla d tazuri, i tlla d ussan i nllan g tayri d tdawt i izaydnin g tirirt iḥqqran d ugʷmmir.

Tusna taẓḍurt tlla f yat psikolujit tanmsastayt d yat tatikit tanmsastayt, ilan aɣaras d uzmz yasin.

Aɛnay n tɣʷmt yakka ad isḥḍa wawal yutuyy. Ur iẓḍaṛ ad issin ad yɛawn, macc yakka ad nssufɣ azur n tafukt s tidt.

Yakka ad nfhm s wul, ur kan g uswir amussan, macca g imikk n ufgan, mayd ur tlli tafukt d tazzit n uɛkkak. Iza nskrra mayd illa g nkkʷni uɛkkak d mayd ur tlli tafukt, illa g wul nnɣ tafrut ilan n tayri s tidt d tafukt ur tllin g ufgan.
