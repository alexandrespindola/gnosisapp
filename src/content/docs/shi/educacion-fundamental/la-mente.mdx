S ixef uɣurfan nneɣ, nufa belli ur nezmir ad nefhem AYEN YETTUSEMMAN TAWENGI, armi nefhem s TELQAQ, iluɣma idawsen n UXEMMEM.

Win yeqqaren belli UXEMMEM d UGERSI, ɣalen kter. UXEMMEM d TAZMERT, ifes, izmer ad yefreq ɣef TFELST, izmer deg kra n tewqiwin tignutin, neɣ deg uṭṭes alɣani, ad yessufeɣ iman-is ɣer imukan ibeɛden iwakken ad iẓer, ad isel i wayen yellan deg imukan-nni.

Deg usayes n TRAMESNASIYULUZIT, ttilin kra n ugerraren yettwakelsen ɣef medden deg wadda ITEGNUTEN.

Aṭas n medden deg wadda ITEGNUTEN, uffɣen-d s lekmal ɣef tinfusin, imdanen, akken daɣen ɣef tilawin i d-yellan deg teswiɛt-nsen tignutin ɣef ibeɛden.

Imussnawen, ssyen deg ugerraren-nni, sseqqnen-d tidet n tɣawsiwin-nni, ttidiren d tidet n wayen yellan, d lekmal n tektiwin.

S ugerraren-agi n usayes n TRAMESNASIYULUZIT, tettwabeyen s lekmal ɣef allen d lexrib belli UGERSI ur yelli d UXEMMEM.

Tidet akken ma tella, nezmer ad nini belli uxemmem izmer ad iminig deg uzmz d usenzi, yefreq ɣef ugersi iwakken ad iẓer, ad isel i tɣawsiwin yellan deg imukan ibeɛden.

TIDET n tilawin TIBERANIYIN, tettwabeyen s lekmal, ɣas ad yemɣar yiwen, neɣ ad yuɣal d umaɛfuq, ur izmir ad yenkeṛ tidet n tilawin tiberaniyin.

Ugersi tettwaxdem iwakken ad tessufeɣ tamusni meɛna ur tettili d tamusni. Ugersi d agerruj n UXEMMEM, ur yelli d uxemmem.

Nettewḥwaj ad nɣer uxemmem s lekmal ma nebɣa ad nessin s telqaq wayen yettusemman Tawengi.

Igujilen d ilemẓiyen, d irgazen d tulawin, ɣur-sen ixemmam yelhan, yeččuren d lfehm, ɣur-sen tazmert tameqqrant iwakken ad fehmen tɣawsiwin.

Aṭas n warrac d ilemẓiyen i hemmelen ad seqsiwen imawlan-nsen d yiselmaden-nsen ɣef tɣawsiwin-agi neɣ-tin, bɣan ad ssinen kra, bɣan ad ssinen kter, ɣef way-agi seqsayen, ttawalin, ttwalin kra n tektiwin ireqqiqen iɣef ur ttawalin uɣurfan neɣ ur ttkassen fell-asen.

Am akken ttilin iseggusa, am akken nettnerni deg leɛmer, uxemmem yettarra deg wadda kra s kra.

Uxemmem n imɣaren yuɣal d ayen i yettwabedlen, ur yettbeddil ara ɣas ad ttekksen fell-as s lḥerraz.

Imeɣraren uɣalen akkenni, ad mmuten akkenni, ur tbeddilen ara, kullec ttwalin-t seg yiwen wudem.

"TUCACET" n imɣaren, tɛeddi-nsen, leɛqayed-nsen yettwaxedmen, akkenni yettarraren am AYḌI, AYDA i ur yettbeddilen s wudem. ɣef way-agi qaren deg umedya "IẒURAN AKK D TALɣA AR QEBRI".

Yella d AYEN YESSAREW i yiselmaden i yettwabedlen s usuffeɣ n UBDAD n inelmaden, ad ɣren uxemmem s lekmal iwakken ad slemden isutriyen imaynuten s lfehm.

Yella d AYEN YESSAɣYUR ad nefhem s lekmal am akken deg uzmz yettarra UXEMMEM deg wadda kra s kra.

UXEMMEM d ameqqraj n TIDET, n wayen yellan d ṣṣeḥ. UXEMMEM YESNEG TAWENGI.

Win yuɣalen d amɣar ur yettidir ara i TAWENGI, acku uxemmem-is yeččur d lexrib iɣesden, tiɛeddiwin, leɛqayed yettwabedlen am tqecčučt n uzal, atg.

Llan kra imɣaren izewwajen i yettaɛren iman-nsen belli mazal-iten zmeren ad TIDREN TAWENGI, meɛna ayen yellan d ameqqran d akken imeɣraren-nni yeččuren d tɣawsiwin tisexsiyanin tizemniyin, ttarun PASYUN ɣer TAWENGI.

KUL "AMɣAR AZEGZA" AKK D "TMɣART TAZERGA", ɛeddan ɣef tewqiwin tiḥerqanin meqqren uqbel ad mmuten, ttarun ay-agi d TAWENGI.

TAWENGI n imɣaren ur telli d tella acku uxemmem YESNEG-TT s "TUCACET" TIƐEDDIYIN", "LEƐQAYED YETTWAXEDMEN", "ASENƐU", "LEXRIB", "TLEMẒIT", tayri taɣawsa, atg. atg. atg.

UXEMMEM D AGAZZAN AMECṬUḤ n TAWENGI. Deg iwunak YEZZUZEN kter, TAWENGI ur telli acku uxemmem n medden iteffes kan ɣer isayes, uɣurfan n lbunya, lgaz akken daɣen ɣer tusnakt.

Llan aṭas n teqraɛen i uxemmem, uxemmem n kul yiwen yettwaxzen deg teqraɛt-is.

Kra ɣur-sen UXEMMEM yemlax deg QMULISM AYEN YESSAɣYURAN, wiyaḍ ɣur-sen yemlax deg KAPITALISM war iḥnin.

Kra llan wid ɣur-sen UXEMMEM yemlax deg usenɛu, deg tikrahit, deg lebɣi ad yuɣal d ameṛkanti, deg wadda aɛli deg tmetti, deg wadda azɣil deg tuẓuft i medden yettwabedlen, deg tuẓuft i lexrib-nsen, deg iluɣman-nsen n twacult, atg. atg. atg.

Medden hemmelen ad mlaxen UXEMMEM, kra kan wid i yettakken iman-nsen iwakken ad rzun tiqraɛen.

Newḥwaj ad NƐELLEQ UXEMMEM, meɛna medden hemmelen aɣella, d ayen yesnen ad naf yiwen deg tudert ur yesɛi UXEMMEM yettwamlxen.

Iselmaden ilaq ad slemden inelmaden-nsen tɣawsiwin-agi. Ilaq ad slemden isutriyen imaynuten iwakken ad ɣren uxemmem-nsen, ad ttawalin fell-as, ad fehmen-t, anagar ɣas am akken deg LFEHM n tɣawsa, nezmer ad nḥudd uxemmem ad yuɣal deg wadda, ad yeqḍer, ad yemlax.

Ayen kan izemren ad ibeddel ddunit d ayen yettusemman TAWENGI, meɛna UXEMMEM YESNEG TAWENGI.

Newḥwaj AD NEɣER uxemmem-nneɣ, ad ttawalin fell-as, ad nqelleb deg-s, ad nefhem-t. Anagar akkenni, anagar ad nuɣal d imaɛzal n iman-nneɣ, n uxemmem-nneɣ, ad neqqer ameqqraj n TAWENGI ad nedder d ṣṣeḥ.

Widak yetteddren s tira deg tɣawsiwin ɣef TAWENGI, widak yetteddren s usuffeɣ n leṣyuḥ ɣef TAWENGI, widak bɣan TAWENGI ad tella s wadda n lebɣi-nsen, tigelliyin-nsen, leṣyuḥ-nsen, tinfusin-nsen, tizemmiyin-nsen, lexrib-nsen, atg. ur zemren ad ssinen tidet n wayen yellan d TAWENGI, d ṣṣeḥ uɣalen d igazzanen n TAWENGI.

Yella d AYEN YESSAREW ad nefhem s TELQAQ wayen yellan d arraten n uxemmem deg wadda n umlal n lexrib.

Aselmad, taselmadt yettɛayyaden aṭas n tikkal s tidet meɛna kra tikkal s ttzel akken daɣen war sebba n ṣṣeḥ, war tfehmt belli kul tɛeyyadt war ssebba tettegg deg uxemmem n inelmaden, afaylu n umbad ayagi, yettili d asɣew n TAWENGI ɣer USELMAD, ɣer TSELMADT.

UXEMMEM YESNEG TAWENGI akken daɣen d tɣawsa i ilaqa ad ttun imessasen n islmaden n iskulan, uɣerbazen d tseddawiyin.

Yella d AYEN YESSAREW ad nefhem s lekmal akk arraten-nni n uxemmem i yettawḍen ɣer lhila n TAWENGI.

Ur yelli d akken ad nili d baba neɣ d yemma n twacult, ilaq ad nessin ad TNEDDREN. Imrawlan ttaren iman-nsen belli ttaɣen TAWENGI ɣer arrac-nsen acku ɣur-sen, acku n-nsen, acku ɣur-sen am win yesɛan tbalist, tamubil, axxam.

Taɣessa n umtaɛ deg tuẓuft, tettaru ɣer TAWENGI meɛna ur tezmer ad tili d TAWENGI.

Iselmaden d tiselmadin n wexxam-nneɣ wis sin yelli d uɣerbaz, ttaren iman-nsen belli ttaɣen TAWENGI ɣer inelmaden-nsen, ɣer tnelmadin-nsen acku ɣur-sen am akkenni, acku ɣur-sen meɛna ay-agi ur yelli d TAWENGI. Taɣessa n umtaɛ tuẓuft UR TELLI D TAWENGI.

UXEMMEM YESNEG TAWENGI, ɣas ma nefhem akk arraten n iluɣma n uxemmem, tumast-nneɣ s ubad n teɣrufin, tikeliwin-nneɣ iluɣma, tɣawsiwin tewtamin, tamusnakt yettwabedlen, ammar ayettwabedlen s wayes nwala tɣawsiwin, atg. ad naweḍ ad nedder, ad nexreb TIDET ayen ur yelli deg uzmz, ayen yettusemman TAWENGI.

Win bɣan TAWENGI ad yuɣal d agẓum n tusnakt-nsen, win bɣan TAWENGI ad tleḥḥu deg iberdan n teɣrufin-nsen, tizmaẓ-nsen, tuggdiwin-nsen, lexrib-nsen n tudert, ammar n tayri ad walin tɣawsiwin, atg. ttakkasen s tidet ɣef TAWENGI acku ayagi ur yettak ara amkan.

Win bɣan TAWENGI ad tella am AKKEN BɣIɣ, am AKKEN HEɣBɣ, am AKKEN XEMMEMɣ, sɣewen ɣef TAWENGI acku KUPIDU, ULAH N TAWENGI, ur yeqqen ara ad yeɣlu ɣer wadda n UMAN.

UMAN d ameqqraj n tlemẓitin, tizmaẓ, tuggdiwin, tikrahin, tirɣiwin, lexrib, idɣaɣen, asenɛuen, tɣawsiwin tisexsiyanin, atg. atg. atg.

Yella d ayen yessaɣyuran medden bɣan ad tedder TAWENGI s wadda n tikeliwin-nsen, lebɣi-nsen, tɣawsiwin-nsen, atg., medden bɣan TAWENGI ad tili deg wadda n UMAN akken daɣen d ayen ur yemken ara acku TAWENGI ur tettaɛra ara deg UMAN.

Iɛecqaq n tawengin, neɣ ayen yelhan ad nini i tidet n seksu, ayen yellan aṭas deg ddunit-agi, ttaren iman-nsen belli TAWENGI ilaq ad tleḥḥu deg wadda n lebɣi-nsen, tɣawsiwin-nsen, arraten-nsen, atg. akken daɣen ɣalen kter.

Nini-d ɣef sin, qqaren wid yetteddren neɣ wid yetteddren seksu, ayen i d-yellan deg ddunit, sya dɣa ttilin tmeslayin, tiṣyuḥ, tɣawsiwin d ususim. Kul yiwen yeqqar kra, yesuffeɣ tiṣyuḥ-is, lebɣi-is, ammar ayetwala tɣawsiwin n tudert, ibɣa TAWENGI ad terwel am takerrust n temmart deg iberdan uzalen i yettwaxedmen seg uxemmem.

UXEMMEM yesɛa lɣella n umqabal. Argaz yeqqabal tislit yiwet ɣer tayeḍ. Tamettut teqqabal argaz yiwen ɣer uyaḍ. Aselmad yeqqabal inelmaden yiwen ɣer uyaḍ, yiwet ɣer tayeḍ am akken inelmaden-is ur sseḥwaqn ad yettwaɛqal-iten. Tidet kul amqabal d AYEN YESSAɣYURAN.

Win yettwalin tullya taɣezzft n tafukt yeqqabal-itt ɣer tayeḍ, ur yessin ad yessinen ayen yellan zdat allen-is.

Win yettwalin adrar yellan d acḥal yeqqabal-it ɣer uyaḍ i yettwala iḍelli, ur yelli d ṣṣeḥ ɣer lfehm n ṣṣeḥ n udrar i yellan zdat allen-is.
