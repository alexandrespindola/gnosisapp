Kukhona izimfundiso ezimbili, imfundiso yeso kanye nemfundiso yenhliziyo, kukhona ulwazi lwangaphandle nolwangaphakathi noma oluzibukayo, ulwazi lobuhlakani noma lokufunda kanye nolwazi lokwazi noma oluphilayo. Ulwazi lokufunda noma lobuhlakani lusiza ekuhlaliseni nasekutholeni ukuziphilisa kwethu. Ulwazi oluzibukayo nolokuqaphela noma lokuqaphela kwethu lusiholela olwazini olungcwele olubaluleke kakhulu, ngoba lowo owaziyo kufanele azazi yena.

Izinzwa ezinhlanu zangaphandle zisivumela ulwazi abalubiza ngokuthi oluyizinto ezibonakalayo futhi eziyisikhombisa zangaphakathi zisivumela ukuthi sazi lokho okubizwa ngokuthi i-esoteric noma okufihliwe, lezi zinzwa yilezi: ukubona, ukucaciseleka, i-polivision, ukuzwa okufihliwe, umuzwa, i-telepathic kanye nenkumbulo yezimpilo ezedlule. Izitho zayo yilezi: i-pineal, i-pituitary (amagland njengoba esebuchosheni), i-thyroid (apula lentamo), inhliziyo kanye ne-solar plexus noma i-epigastrium (ngaphezulu kwenkaba); ngalezi siyazi imizimba eyisikhombisa (7) yomuntu: Umzimba, obalulekile, we-astral, wengqondo, owakha imizimba emine yesono eyi-lunar protoplasmatic kanye neminye emithathu eyimizimba yentando, yomphefumulo nomoya, ecebisa ulwazi lokwazi, lolu lwazi luphilile ngoba silwenza luphile, lwakha lokho abantu benkolo nezazi zefilosofi abakubiza ngokuthi umphefumulo.

Uma sithuthukisa izinzwa sithuthukisa ulwazi lwethu. Izinzwa ziyathuthuka lapho sisusa amaphutha, uma singabaqambimanga izinzwa zethu zingabaqambimanga, uma singabaqili, izinzwa zethu nazo zinjalo.

Kule mpucuko kufanele sibuyise amaphutha ethu ukuze sithuthukise abazisi bethu noma izinzwa. Yazi mngani isiko le-Gnostic elisifundisa iMfundo Eyisisekelo esukela ekukhulelweni kuze kube sebudaleni obukhulu.
