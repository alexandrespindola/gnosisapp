INdishi Eyingcwele iyakhanya ebusuku obujulile bazo zonke izikhathi. AmaQhawe eNkathi Ephakathi ngesikhathi seMpi Yenkolo ayifuna ngaphandle kwempumelelo iNkomishi Eyingcwele ezweni Elingcwele kodwa ayizange ayithole.

Ngesikhathi u-Abrahamu uMprofethi ebuya emuva empini yokulwa namakhosi aseSodoma naseGomora, kuthiwa wahlangana noMelkisedeki, uGenie woMhlaba. Impela lokhu Kukhulu Kwakuhlala enqabeni eyakhiwe kahle kuleyo ndawo lapho kamuva kwakhiwa khona iJerusalema, idolobha elithandwa abaProfethi.

Inganekwane yamakhulu eminyaka ithi futhi lokhu kwaziwa yibo bobabili abangcwele nabantu, ukuthi u-Abrahamu wagubha Ukugcotshwa kweGnostic ngokuhlanganyela isinkwa newayini phambi kukaMelkisedeki.

Akudingekile ukusho ukuthi ngaleso sikhathi u-Abrahamu wanikela okweshumi nokuvuthwa kuMelkisedeki njengoba kubhalwe eNcwadini yoMthetho.

U-Abrahamu wamukela iNkomishi Eyingcwele ezandleni zikaMelkisedeki; kamuva kakhulu ngesikhathi le nkomishi yaya ethempelini laseJerusalema.

Akungabazeki ukuthi iNdlovukazi yaseSheba isebenze njengomlamuli waleli qiniso. Waziveza phambi kweNkosi uSolomoni eneNkomishi Eyingcwele futhi ngemuva kokumhlola izivivinyo eziqinile wamnika leli gugu eliyigugu.

IKabir Enkulu uJesu waphuza kule nkomishi emcimbini ongcwele wesidlo sokugcina njengoba kubhalwe kumaVangeli Amane.

UJosefa wase-Arimatheya wagcwalisa iNkomishi ngegazi elaligeleza ezilondeni zeThandekayo eNtabeni Yezigebengu.

Lapho amaphoyisa aseRoma ehlasela indawo yalo Senator okukhulunywa ngaye awazange alithole leli gugu eliyigugu.

Lo Senator waseRoma akazange afihle kuphela leli gugu eliyigugu, kodwa futhi kanye nalo wagcina ngaphansi komhlaba umkhonto weLongibus owawuhlabe ngalo isosha laseRoma ohlangothini lweNkosi.

UJosefa wase-Arimatheya wavalelwa ejele elibi ngokungafuni ukunikeza iNkomishi Eyingcwele.

Lapho lo Senator okukhulunywa ngaye ephuma ejele, wathatha iNkomishi Eyingcwele waya eRoma.

Lapho efika eRoma, uJosefa wase-Arimatheya wathola ukushushiswa kukaNero kumaKristu futhi waya ngasemanzini aseMedithera.

Ngobunye ubusuku, ingelosi yabonakala kuye emaphusheni yakhe yathi: "Le nkomishi inamandla amakhulu ngoba iqukethe igazi loMhlengi Wezwe." UJosefa wase-Arimatheya, elalela imiyalo yengelosi, wangcwaba le nkomishi ethempelini eliseMontserrat, eCatalonia, eSpain.

Ngokuhamba kwesikhathi le nkomishi ayisabonakala kanye nethempeli nengxenye yentaba.

INkomishi Eyingcwele iyisiqukathi sikaHermes, inkomishi kaSolomoni, umphongolo oyigugu wazo zonke izithempeli zezimfihlakalo.

EMphongolweni wesivumelwano, iNkomishi Eyingcwele ayizange iphuthe nanini ngendlela yenkomishi noma i-gomor, lapho kwakugcinwe khona imana lasehlane.

INkomishi Eyingcwele ihlukanisa ngendlela egcizelelayo i-YONI yobulili besifazane, ngaphakathi kwale nkomishi engcwele kukhona umpe wokungafi, iSoma yemikhuba, isiphuzo esiphezulu soNkulunkulu Abangcwele.

UKrestu Obomvu uphuza eNkomishini Eyingcwele ngehora eliphakeme leCristification, njengoba kubhalwe eVangelini leNkosi.

INkomishi Eyingcwele ayikaze iphuthume e-altare lethempeli. Ngokusobala, uMpristi kufanele aphuze iwayini lokukhanya eNkomishini Eyingcwele.

Kungaba yize ukucabanga ithempeli lezimfihlakalo lapho inkomishi ebusisiwe yazo zonke izikhathi yayingekho.

Lokhu kusikhumbuza uGinebra, iNdlovukazi yamaJina, lowo owathela iwayini kuLancelot ezinkomishini ezimnandi zeSUFRA neMANTI.

Onkulunkulu abangafile bondliwa ngesiphuzo esiqukethwe eNkomishini Eyingcwele; labo abazondayo iNkomishi Ebusisiwe, bayahlambalaza umoya oyingcwele.

Umuntu Oyisimangaliso kufanele azondle ngompe wokungafi oqukethwe yisiqukathi esingcwele sethempeli.

Ukuguqulwa kwamandla okudala kubalulekile uma ufuna ukuphuza esitsheni Esingcwele.

UKrestu Obomvu uhlala eshintsha, uhlala eyisihlubuki, uhlala eyiqhawe, uhlala enqoba, uyaphongoloza onkulunkulu ngokuphuza esitsheni segolide.

Phakamisani inkomishi yenu kahle futhi niqikelele ukuthi anithululi ngisho nethonsi lewayini eliyigugu.

Khumbulani ukuthi isiqubulo sethu esingcwele sithi THELEMA (intando).

Ekususeni kwenkomishi - umfanekiso oyisimboli wesitho sangasese sowesifazane - kuvela amalangabi akhanya ebusweni obuvuthayo boMuntu Oyisimangaliso.

Onkulunkulu abangachazeki bama-galaxies wonke baphuza njalo isiphuzo sokungafi esitsheni saphakade.

Ukubanda kwenyanga kukhiqiza ukuguquka emuva ngesikhathi; kuyadingeka ukuphuza iwayini elingcwele lokukhanya esitsheni esingcwele se-Alchemy.

Okububende kwamakhosi angcwele, umqhele wobukhosi, negolide elivuthayo kungokukaKrestu Obomvu kuphela.

INkosi Yombani Nokuduma iphethe iNkomishi Eyingcwele esandleni sayo sokunene iphuze iwayini legolide ukuzondla.

Labo abathulula Isiqukathi sikaHermes ngesikhathi sokuhlanganiswa kwamakhemikhali, empeleni baba izidalwa ezingaphansi kwabantu ezweni elingaphansi.

Konke esikubhale lapha kuthola imibhalo ephelele encwadini yami enesihloko esithi "Umshado Ophelele".
