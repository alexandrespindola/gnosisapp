Kungekudala izigidi zabantu e-Afrika, e-Asia naseLatin America zingase zife yindlala.

Igesi ekhishwa yi-"Spray" ingawuqeda nya umoya we-Ozone emkhathini womhlaba.

Ezinye izazi ziyabikezela ukuthi ngonyaka weziNkulungwane Ezimbili kuzophela umhlabathi ongaphansi komhlaba wethu.

Izilwane zasolwandle ziyafa ngenxa yokungcola kwezilwandle, lokhu sekufakazelwe.

Ngokungangabazeki uma siqhubeka ngale ndlela, ekupheleni kwaleli khulu leminyaka bonke abantu abahlala emadolobheni amakhulu kuzodingeka basebenzise Izifihla-buso Zomoya-mpilo ukuze bazivikele entuthwini.

Uma ukungcola kuqhubeka ngendlela eyethusayo yamanje, kungekudala ngeke kusaba nokwenzeka ukudla izinhlanzi, lezi ezihlala emanzini angcole kangaka zizoba yingozi empilweni.

Ngaphambi konyaka weziNkulungwane Ezimbili kuzoba nzima ukuthola ulwandle lapho umuntu angabhukuda khona ngamanzi ahlanzekile.

Ngenxa yokusetshenziswa ngokweqile nokuxhashazwa kwenhlabathi nomhlabathi ongaphansi, maduze nje umhlaba ngeke usakwazi ukukhiqiza izinto zezolimo ezidingekayo ukuze abantu bondleke.

"Isilwane Esihlakaniphile", okuyinto engaqondakali ukuthi kubizwa ngokuthi umuntu, ngokungcolisa izilwandle ngemfucumfucu eningi kangaka, ngokufaka ushevu emoyeni ngentuthu yezimoto nezimboni zayo kanye nokucekela phansi uMhlaba ngeziqhumane zawo ze-athomu ezingaphansi komhlaba kanye nokusebenzisa kabi izinto eziyingozi emhlabathini, kusobala ukuthi uMhlaba uwubeke ebunzimeni obude nobesabekayo obuzophela ngempela ngenhlekelele Enkulu.

Kunzima ukuthi umhlaba uzokwazi ukudlula umkhawulo wonyaka weziNkulungwane Ezimbili, ngoba "Isilwane Esihlakaniphile" siwucekela phansi umvelo ngesivinini esikhulu.

"Isilwane Esincelisayo Esihlakaniphile", okuyinto engaqondakali ukuthi kubizwa ngokuthi umuntu, sizimisele ngokucekela phansi uMhlaba, sifuna ukwenza ukuthi ungalingani ukuhlala kuwo, futhi kusobala ukuthi siyakufeza lokhu.

Ngokuphathelene nezilwandle, kusobala ukuthi zonke izizwe seziziguqule zaba uhlobo lweDoti Elikhulu.

Amaphesenti angamashumi ayisikhombisa ayo yonke imfucu-mucu yomhlaba iya olwandle ngalunye.

Inani elikhulu likawoyela, izibulala-zinambuzane zazo zonke izinhlobo, izinto eziningi zamakhemikhali, amagesi anobuthi, amagesi e-neurotoxic, izinto zokuhlanza, njll., Kuyaqothula zonke izinhlobo eziphilayo e-Ocean.

Izinyoni zasolwandle kanye nePlankton ebaluleke kakhulu ekuphileni, ziyafa.

Ngokungangabazeki ukuqothulwa kwe-Marine Plankton kunzima ngokungenakulinganiswa ngoba le microorganism ikhiqiza amaphesenti angamashumi ayisikhombisa omoya-mpilo Womhlaba.

Ngokocwaningo lwesayensi, kuye kwaqinisekiswa ukuthi ezinye izingxenye ze-Atlantic nePacific sezivele zingcoliswe yimfucu-mucu enomsoco, umkhiqizo weziqhumane ze-athomu.

Emadolobheni ahlukene emhlabeni, ikakhulukazi eYurophu, amanzi ahlanzekile ayaphuza, ayakhishwa, ayahlanzwa bese aphinde ayaphuza.

Emadolobheni amakhulu "Agcwele Ukucivilizeka", amanzi anikezwa etafuleni adlula ezilwaneni zabantu izikhathi eziningi.

Edolobheni laseCúcuta, elisemngceleni weVenezuela, iRiphabhulikhi yaseColombia, eNingizimu Melika, izakhamuzi ziphoqeleka ukuthi ziphuze amanzi amnyama nangcolile omfula ophethe yonke imfucu-mucu evela ePamplona.

Ngifuna ukukhuluma ngendlela eqinile ngomfula iPamplonita oye waba yingozi kakhulu "ePerla del Norte" (Cúcuta).

Ngenhlanhla manje kukhona okunye ukuhambisa amanzi okuhlinzeka iDolobha, ngaphandle kokuyeka ukuphuza amanzi amnyama omfula iPamplonita.

Izihlungi ezinkulu, imishini emikhulu, izinto zamakhemikhali, zama ukuhlambulula amanzi amnyama emadolobheni amakhulu aseYurophu, kodwa izifo ziyaqhubeka nokusakazeka ngalawo manzi amnyama angcolile aseyedlule ezilwaneni zabantu izikhathi eziningi.

Ososayensi abaziwayo beBacteriology bathole emanzini okuphuza eziNhloko-dolobha ezinkulu, zonke izinhlobo ze: amagciwane, i-colibacillus, i-pathogens, amagciwane e-Tuberculosis, i-Typhus, i-Smallpox, i-Larvae, njll.

Nakuba kubonakala kuyinto engakholeki ngaphakathi kwezitshalo zamanzi ezihlanzekayo emazweni aseYurophu, kutholwe amagciwane omuthi wokugoma wePoliomyelitis.

Ngaphezu kwalokho, ukuchitha amanzi kuyethusa: Ososayensi besimanje bathi ngonyaka we-1990 i-humanoid ehlakaniphile izofa yomile.

Okubi kakhulu kukho konke lokhu ukuthi imithombo yamanzi ahlanzekile angaphansi komhlaba isengozini ngenxa yokuhlukunyezwa kweSilwane Esihlakaniphile.

Ukuxhashazwa ngaphandle kwesihe kwemithombo kawoyela, kuyaqhubeka nokuba yingozi. Uwoyela okhishwa ngaphakathi emhlabathini, udabula emanzini angaphansi komhlaba futhi uwayingcolise.

Njengoba kulandela lokhu, uwoyela wenze amanzi angaphansi komhlaba woMhlaba angaphuzeki isikhathi esingaphezu kwekhulu leminyaka.

Ngokusobala ngenxa yakho konke lokhu, imifino iyafa ngisho nenqwaba yabantu.

Manje ake sikhulume kancane ngomoya obaluleke kakhulu ekuphileni kwezidalwa.

Ngokuphefumula ngakunye, amaphaphu athatha ingxenye yelitha lomoya, okungukuthi, cishe amamitha ayishumi nambili ayizikwele ngosuku, phindaphinda leyo mali ngeZigidi Eziyizinkulungwane Ezine namakhulu amahlanu zabantu abahlala eMhlabeni bese siba nenani eliqondile lomoya-mpilo osetshenziswa yisintu sonke nsuku zonke, ngaphandle kwalowo osetshenziswa yizo zonke ezinye izidalwa zezilwane ezihlala ebusweni boMhlaba.

Yonke imoya-mpilo esiyiphefumulayo isemkhathini futhi kungenxa yePlankton esiyicekela phansi manje ngokungcola kanye nomsebenzi we-photosynthetic wemifino.

Ngeshwa, imithombo yomoya-mpilo isiyaphela.

Isilwane Esincelisayo Esihlakaniphile okuyinto engaqondakali ukuthi kubizwa ngokuthi umuntu, ngezimboni zaso eziningi sinciphisa ngokuqhubekayo inani lemisebe yelanga, edingeka kakhulu futhi ebalulekile ku-photosynthesis, yingakho inani lomoya-mpilo okhishwa yizitshalo njengamanje, lincane kakhulu kunalelo lekhulu leminyaka eledlule.

Okubi kakhulu kulo lonke le nhlekelele yomhlaba ukuthi "Isilwane Esihlakaniphile", siyaqhubeka nokungcolisa izilwandle, sicekela phansi iPlankton futhi siqeda imifino.

"Isilwane Esihlakaniphile", siyaqhubeka ngenhlanhla sicekela phansi imithombo yaso yomoya-mpilo.

"I-Smog", "i-Humanoid Ehlakaniphile" eyikhipha njalo emoyeni; ngaphezu kokubulala ifaka impilo yePlanethi Yethu engozini.

"I-Smog", ayiqothuli nje kuphela imithombo yomoya-mpilo, kodwa futhi, ibulala abantu.

"I-Smog", idala izifo ezingajwayelekile neziyingozi ezingenakwelapheka, lokhu sekufakazelwe.

"I-Smog", ivimbela ukungena kokukhanya kwelanga nemisebe ye-ultraviolet, idale ngalokho, iziphazamiso ezinkulu emkhathini.

Kuyeza inkathi yezinguquko zesimo sezulu, ama-glaciation, ukuthuthuka kwamakhaza asezindaweni ezipholile kuya e-Equator, iziphepho ezethusayo, ukuzamazama komhlaba, njll.

Ngenxa hhayi yokusetshenziswa, kodwa ukuhlukunyezwa kwamandla kagesi ngonyaka weziNkulungwane Ezimbili, kuzoba nokushisa okwengeziwe kwezinye izifunda zePlanethi Yethu futhi lokhu kuzosiza enqubweni yeNguquko yama-Axes oMhlaba.

Maduze nje izigxobo zizokwakhiwa e-Equator yoMhlaba, futhi le yokugcina izoba yiziGxobo.

Ukuncibilika kweziGxobo kuqalile futhi iZikhukhula Zomhlaba Wonke ezintsha ezandulelwa umlilo ziyasondela.

Eminyakeni eyishumi ezayo, "i-Carbon Dioxide" izophindaphindeka, ngaleso sikhathi lesi sici samakhemikhali sizokwakha ungqimba olukhulu emkhathini woMhlaba.

Leso sihlungi noma ungqimba, ngeshwa sizoyigwinya imisebe yokushisa futhi sizosebenza njengendlu yokushisela izinto ezibulalayo.

Isimo sezulu somhlaba sizofudumala ezindaweni eziningi futhi ukushisa kuzoncibilikisa iqhwa leziGxobo, kukhuphule ngalokho izinga lezilwandle ngokumangalisayo.

Isimo sibi kakhulu, umhlabathi ovundile uyaqala ukunyamalala futhi nsuku zonke kuzalwa abantu abangamakhulu amabili ezinkulungwane abadinga ukudla.

Inhlekelele yomhlaba yendlala ezayo, ngokuqinisekile izoba yesabekayo; lokhu sekuvalelise emnyango.

Njengamanje, abantu abayizigidi ezingamashumi amane bafa minyaka yonke ngenxa yendlala, ngenxa yokuntuleka kokudla.

Ukwenziwa kwezimboni zobugebengu kwamahlathi kanye nokuxhashazwa ngonya Kwezimayini No-oyela kushiya uMhlaba usuguquke waba ugwadule.

Nakuba kuyiqiniso ukuthi amandla enuzi abulala isintu, kuyiqiniso futhi ukuthi njengamanje kukhona futhi, "Imisebe Yokufa", "Amabhomu e-Microbial" nezinye izinto eziningi ezicekelayo phansi ngendlela esabekayo, ezinonya; eyaqanjwa ososayensi.

Ngokungangabazeki ukuze uthole amandla enuzi, kudingeka amanani amakhulu okushisa okunzima ukuwalawula futhi okungadala inhlekelele nganoma yisiphi isikhathi.

Ukuze kutholakale amandla enuzi, kudingeka amanani amakhulu ezimbiwa ezinomsoco, lapho kusetshenziswa khona amaphesenti angamashumi amathathu kuphela, lokhu kubangela ukuthi umhlabathi ongaphansi womhlaba uphele ngokushesha.

Imfucu-mucu ye-athomu esala emhlabathini ingaba yingozi ngendlela esabekayo. Ayikho indawo ephephile yemfcumfucu ye-athomu.

Uma igesi elivela endaweni yokulahla imfucu-mucu ye-athomu lingaphunyuka, noma ngabe lincane kangakanani, izigidi zabantu zizofa.

Ukungcoliswa kokudla namanzi kuza nezinguquko zofuzo kanye nezilo zabantu: izidalwa ezizalwa zikhubazekile futhi ziyizilo.

Ngaphambi konyaka we-1999, kuzoba nengozi enukliya enkulu ezodala ukwethuka kwangempela.

Ngokuqinisekile isintu asikwazi ukuphila, sonakele ngendlela esabekayo futhi siye sazilahla emgodini.

Okubi kakhulu kukho konke lokhu, ukuthi izici zalokhu kubhubhisa, okuyilezi: izindlala, izimpi, ukubhujiswa kwePlanethi esihlala kuyo, njll., Zingaphakathi kithi, sizithwele ngaphakathi, ezingqondweni zethu.
