UKristo ungumlilo woMlilo, ilangabi lelangabi, isignesha yezinkanyezi yomlilo.

Emnqamlezweni woMfelâ€™ ukholo waseKhalvari kuchazwe Imfihlakalo kaKristu ngegama elilodwa elinezinhlamvu ezine: INRI. Ignis Natura Renovatur Integram - Umlilo Uvuselela Ngokuqhubekayo Imvelo.

Ukuza kukaKristu enhliziyweni yomuntu, kusishintsha ngokuphelele.

UKristo uyi-LOGOS SOLAR, Ubunye Obuphelele Obuningi. UKristo yimpilo eshayayo endaweni yonke, yilokho eyikho, yilokho ehlale eyikho nalokho eyohlala iyikho.

Kuningi okushiwo ngeDrama Yezulu; ngokungangabazeki le Drama yakhiwe ngamaVangeli amane.

Sitshelwe ukuthi iDrama Yezulu ilethwe ngama-Elohim emhlabeni; iNkosi Enkulu yase-Atlantis yamelula le drama Ngokwenyama Negazi.

INKOSI ENKULU KABIR UJESU KUFANELE ukuthi naye wamelula le Drama Esidlangalaleni eZweni Elingcwele.

Noma uKristu engazalwa izinkulungwane zezikhathi eBhelehema, akusizi ngalutho uma engazalwa nasezinhliziyweni zethu.

Noma efile wavuka ngosuku lwesithathu kwabafileyo, akusizi ngalutho lokho uma engafi futhi avuke nakithi.

Ukuzama ukuthola imvelo kanye nengqikithi yomlilo kuwukuzama ukuthola uNkulunkulu, omandla akhe abonakala ngaphansi kokubukeka okushisayo.

Isihlahla esivuthayo (Eksodusi, III, 2) nomlilo waseSinayi ngemuva kokunikezwa kweMithetho Eyishumi (Eksodusi, XIX, 18): yizinto ezimbili uNkulunkulu abonakale ngazo kuMose.

Ngomfanekiso womuntu weJasepe neSardonike ombala welangabi, ohlezi eSihlalweni Sobukhosi esikhanyayo nesiqhumayo, uSan Juan uchaza umnikazi weNdawo Yonke. (IsAmbulo, IV, 3,5). "UNkulunkulu wethu ungumlilo oqothulayo", kubhala uSan Pablo eNcwadini yakhe eya kumaHeberu.

UKristo osondelene, uMlilo Wasezulwini, kufanele azalwe kithi futhi uzalwa ngempela lapho sithuthuke ngokwanele emsebenzini Wengqondo.

UKristo osondelene kufanele aqede eMvelweni yethu Yengqondo, izimbangela zefanayo zamaphutha; ama-YOES CAUSAS.

Ngeke kwenzeke ukuqedwa kwezimbangela ze-EGO inqobo nje uma uKristu Intimo engakazalwa kithi.

Umlilo ophilayo nowamaFilosofi, uKristu osondelene, ungumlilo woMlilo, okumsulwa kokumsulwa.

Umlilo uyasizungeza futhi usigeza kuzo zonke izindawo, uza kithi ngomoya, ngamanzi nangomhlaba ofanayo ongabalondolozi kanye nezinto zabo ezihlukahlukene.

Umlilo Wasezulwini kufanele uqandeke kithi, unguKristu intimo, uMsindisi wethu wangaphakathi ojulile.

INkosi Esondele kufanele ithathe yonke i-Psiquis yethu yamaSilinda amahlanu omshini we-Organic; yazo zonke izinqubo zethu Zokucabanga, Zomzwelo, Zezimoto, Ezingokwemvelo Zobulili.
