Abanye bezazi zengqondo bafanekisa ukuqaphela njengommese okwazi kakhulu ukusihlukanisa nalokho okunamathele kithina futhi kusikhiphe amandla.

Lezo zazi zengqondo zikholelwa ukuthi ukuphela kwendlela yokubalekela amandla alokhu noma lolo "mina" ukukuqaphela ngokuya ngokuya ngokucacileyo ngenhloso yokuqonda ukuze siziqaphele sona.

Labo bantu bacabanga ukuthi ngaleyo ndlela umuntu ekugcineni uyahlukana naleli noma lelo "mina", noma ngabe kungubukhulu bensingo yommese.

Ngale ndlela, bathi, "mina" ehlukaniswe ukuqaphela, ibukeka njengesitshalo esisikiwe.

Ukuqaphela noma yiliphi "mina", ngokusho kwabo, kusho ukulihlukanisa ne-Psiquis yethu futhi siligwebele ukufa.

Ngokungangabazeki lo mqondo, obukeka uqinisekisa kakhulu, uyasilela ekusebenzeni.

"Mina" esisikwe ngommese wokuqaphela kumuntu wethu, silahlwe ekhaya njengemvu emnyama, siyaqhubeka endaweni yengqondo, siba yidemoni eliyengayo, siyaphikelela ngokubuyela ekhaya, asizivumeli kalula, asifuni neze ukudla isinkwa esibabayo sokudingiswa, sifuna ithuba futhi ngokunganaki okuncane kwabalindi sizinza futhi ngaphakathi kwe-psiquis yethu.

Okubi kakhulu ukuthi ngaphakathi "kwami" okudingisiwe kuhlala kuvalelwe amaphesenti athile obukhona, bokuqaphela.

Zonke lezo zazi zengqondo ezicabanga kanjalo azikaze zikwazi ukuchitha noma iyiphi "yezona", empeleni zehlulekile.

Noma ngabe uzama kangakanani ukugwema leyo ndaba ye-KUNDALINI, inkinga inzima kakhulu.

Empeleni "INdodana Engabongiyo" ayikaze ithuthuke emsebenzini we-esoteric ozithobayo.

Ngokusobala "INdodana Engabongiyo" yinoma ngubani odelela i-"ISIS", uMama wethu Ongcwele Wemvelo, oqondile, ngamunye.

I-ISIS ingenye yezingxenye ezizimele zoqobo lwethu, kepha ethathwe, iNyoka yomlilo yamandla ethu omlingo, i-KUNDALINI.

Ngokusobala i-"ISIS" kuphela enamandla aphelele okuchitha noma yiliphi "mina"; lokhu kuqinisekile, akunakuphikwa, akunakuphikiswana.

I-KUNDALINI yigama elihlanganisiwe: "I-KUNDA isikhumbuza iSitho esinyanyekayo se-KUNDARTIGUADOR", "i-LINI yigama le-Atlante elisho ukuphela".

"I-KUNDALINI" isho ukuthi: "Ukuphela kwesitho esinyanyekayo se-KUNDARTIGUADOR". Ngakho-ke kuphuthuma ukungadidanisi "i-KUNDALINI" ne-"KUNDARTIGUADOR".

Sesishilo esahlukweni esedlule ukuthi iNyoka Yomlilo yamandla ethu omlingo isongwe izikhathi ezintathu nengxenye ngaphakathi kwesikhungo esithile se-Magnetic esitholakala ethanjeni le-Coxígeo, isisekelo somgogodla.

Lapho iNyoka ikhuphuka, yi-KUNDALINI, lapho yehla, isitho esinyanyekayo se-KUNDARTIGUADOR.

Nge-"TANTRISMO EMHLOPHE" inyoka inyuka ngokuphumelelayo ngomsele womgogodla, ivusa amandla ahlanza.

Nge-"TANTRISMO EMNYAMA" inyoka iwela isuka ku-coccyx iye ezihogweni ze-athomu zomuntu. Yile ndlela abaningi ababa ngamademoni amabi ngendlela esabekayo.

Labo abenza iphutha lokuthi inyoka enyukayo inazo zonke izici zangakwesobunxele nezimnyama zenyoka eyehlayo, bayehluleka ngokuqinisekile emsebenzini wokuzithoba.

Imiphumela emibi "YELUNGU ELINYANYEKAYO LE-KUNDARTIGUADOR", ingaqedwa kuphela nge-"KUNDALINI".

Akukubi ukucacisa ukuthi leyo miphumela emibi ibekwe "KUMINA ELININGI" le-Psychology Revolution.

Amandla e-Hypnotic eNyoka eyehlayo agcine isintu sicwiliswe ekungazini.

Yinyoka enyukayo kuphela, ngokuphambene, engasivusa; leli qiniso liyiqiniso le-Hermetic Wisdom. Manje sesizoqonda kangcono incazelo ejulile yegama elingcwele elithi "KUNDALINI".

Intando eqondayo ihlala imelwe owesifazane ongcwele, uMariya, ISIS, ochoboza ikhanda leNyoka eyehlayo.

Ngiyaqinisela lapha ngokungagwegwesi nangokucacile ukuthi ukugeleza okukabili kokukhanya, umlilo ophilayo ne-astral womhlaba, kufanekiselwe inyoka enekhanda lenkunzi, lembuzi noma lenja eMimisweni Yasendulo.

Yinyoka ephindwe kabili yeCaduceus kaMercury; inyoka eyengayo yase-Eden; kepha futhi ngaphandle kokungabaza, iNyoka yethusi likaMose elukwe "TAU", okungukuthi, "LINGAM Generador".

"Yimbuzi" yeSabbat kanye neBaphomet ye-Gnostic Templars; i-HYLE ye-Universal Gnosticism; umsila wenyoka ophindwe kabili owakha izinyawo ze-Solar Rooster ye-ABRAXAS.

Ku-"LINGAM EMNYAMA" efakwe ku-"YONI" yensimbi, izimpawu zikaNkulunkulu SHIVA, ubuNkulunkulu bamaHindu, kukhona ukhiye oyimfihlo wokuvusa nokuthuthukisa iNyoka enyukayo noma iKUNDALINI, uma nje ungachithi nanini empilweni "Isitsha sikaHermes Trimegisto", uNkulunkulu omkhulu izikhathi ezintathu "IBIS DE THOTH".

Sikhulume phakathi kwemigqa kulabo abakwazi ukuqonda. Noma ngubani onokuqonda makaqonde ngoba lapha kunokuhlakanipha.

Ama-TÁNTRICOS amnyama ahlukile, avusa futhi athuthukise iLungu elinyanyekayo le-KUNDARTIGUADOR, iNyoka eyengayo yase-Eden, lapho benza ubugebengu obungenakuthethelelwa bokuchitha "Iwayini Elingcwele" emikhosini yabo.
