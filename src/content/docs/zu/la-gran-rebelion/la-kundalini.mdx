Sesifikile endaweni ebucayi kakhulu, ngithanda ukukhuluma ngale ndaba yeKundalini, inyoka evuthayo yamandla ethu omlingo, ekhulunywa ngayo emibhalweni eminingi yobuhlakani baseMpumalanga.

Ngokungangabazeki iKundalini inemibhalo eminingi futhi iyinto ekufanele ukuyiphenya.

Emibhalweni ye-Alchemy yangeNkathini Ephakathi, iKundalini iyisibonakaliso sezinkanyezi zesidoda esingcwele, i-STELLA MARIS, i-VIRGIN OF THE SEA, eqondisa ngokuhlakanipha izisebenzi ze-Great Work.

Phakathi kwama-Aztec ungumama u-TONANTZIN, phakathi kwamaGreki yi-CASTA DIANA, futhi eGibhithe u-ISIS, uMama Ongcwele okungekho muntu ofile owake wayiphakamisa isigqubuthelo.

Akungabazeki ukuthi ubuKristu be-Esoteric abuzange buyeke ukukhonza uMama Ongcwele uKundalini; ngokusobala ungu-MARAH, noma kungcono sithi RAM-IO, MARIA.

Okungazange kucaciswe yizinkolo ezigxile kwezivamile, okungenani kulokho okuphathelene nombuthano we-exoteric noma womphakathi, isici se-ISIS esimweni sayo somuntu ngamunye.

Ngokusobala, imfihlo kuphela lapho kwafundiswa khona abaqalayo ukuthi lo Mama Ongcwele ukhona ngamunye ngaphakathi komuntu ngamunye.

Akukubi ukucacisa ngendlela egcizelelayo ukuthi uNkulunkulu-Mama, REA, CIBELES, ADON√çA noma ngabe sifuna ukumbiza kanjani, uyindlela ehlukile yoqobo lwethu ngalunye lapha futhi manje.

Ngokuqondile sizothi ngamunye wethu unomama wakhe ongcwele ozimele, ngamunye.

Banomama abaningi emazulwini njengoba kunezidalwa ezikhona emhlabeni.

I-Kundalini ingamandla angaqondakali enza umhlaba ube khona, isici se-BRAHMA.

Esicini sayo sokusebenza kwengqondo esibonakala ekwakhekeni okufihliwe komzimba womuntu, i-KUNDALINI iphothene kathathu nengxenye ngaphakathi kwendawo ethile kazibuthe etholakala ethanjeni le-coccyx.

Lapho ilele khona ifile njenganoma iyiphi inyoka iNkosazana Engcwele.

Phakathi nendawo yaleyo Chakra noma indawo yokuhlala kukhona unxantathu wensikazi noma i-YONI lapho kusungulwe khona i-LINGAM yesilisa.

Kule LINGAM ye-athomu noma yomlingo emelela amandla okudala ocansi ka-BRAHMA, kupoteke inyoka ephakeme i-KUNDALINI.

Indlovukazi evuthayo emfanekisweni wayo wenyoka, iyavuka nge-secretum secretorum yobuqili obuthile be-alchemist engibufundise ngokucacile emsebenzini wami onesihloko esithi: "Imfihlakalo Yokuqhakaza Kwegolide".

Ngokungangabazeki, lapho leli mandla laphezulu livuka, likhuphuka linqobile ngomzila womgogodla ukuze lithuthukise kithi amandla ahloniphekile.

Esicini sayo esiphakeme esiphakeme esingaphansi kwengqondo, inyoka engcwele idlulela kulokho okuvamile kwe-physiological, anatomical, esimweni sayo sobuhlanga, njengoba sengishilo kakade iyona Ngqondo yethu, kodwa etholakele.

Akuyona inhloso yami ukufundisa kule ncwadi indlela yokuvusa inyoka engcwele.

Ngifuna nje ukugcizelela ubukhali beqiniso be-Ego kanye nesidingo sangaphakathi esihlobene nokuqedwa kwezinto zayo ezahlukahlukene ezingezona ezobuntu.

Ingqondo ngokwayo ayikwazi ukushintsha ngokuqinile noma yikuphi ukukhubazeka kwengqondo.

Ingqondo ingakwazi ukubhala noma yikuphi ukukhubazeka, ikudlulise isuka kwelinye izinga iye kwelinye, ikufihle kuyo noma kwabanye, ikuthethelele kodwa ingakuqedi ngokuphelele.

Ukuqonda kuyingxenye ebalulekile, kodwa akuyona yonke into, kuyadingeka ukukhipha.

Iphutha elibonwayo kufanele lihlaziywe futhi liqondwe ngokugcwele ngaphambi kokuba kuqhubeke nokuqedwa kwalo.

 sidinga amandla aphakeme kunengqondo, amandla akwazi ukuhlakaza i-atomically noma yimuphi umuntu-ukukhubazeka esike sawuthola futhi sawugweba ngokujulile ngaphambilini.

Ngenhlanhla amandla anjalo alele ngokujulile ngale komzimba, izenzo kanye nengqondo, nakuba enabameli bawo abaqondile ethanjeni eliphakathi le-coccyx, njengoba sesichazile ezigabeni ezedlule zalesi sahluko.

Ngemva kokuqonda ngokugcwele noma yikuphi ukukhubazeka komuntu, kufanele sizicwilise ekuzindleni okujulile, sinxuse, sithandaze, sicele kuMama wethu Ongcwele ozimele ngamunye ukuthi aqede ukukhubazeka komuntu oqondwe ngaphambilini.

Lobu ubuchwepheshe obuqondile obudingekayo ukuze kuqedwe izinto ezingafuneki esizithwele ngaphakathi kithi.

UMama Ongcwele uKundalini unamandla okunciphisa noma yikuphi ukuhlanganiswa kwengqondo okuyize, okungenabuntu kube umlotha.

Ngaphandle kwale didactics, ngaphandle kwale nqubo, yonke imizamo yokuqedwa kwe-Ego ayiphumeleli, ayisizi ngalutho, iyize.
