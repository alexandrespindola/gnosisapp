Enye yezinkinga ezilukhuni kakhulu zalesi sikhathi impela ukuba yinkimbinkimbi yethiyori.

Ngokungangabazeki, kulezi zikhathi, izikole ze-pseudo-esoteric kanye ne-pseudo-occultist zande ngokwedlulele lapha, nalaphaya, nalapho.

Ukudayiswa kwemiphefumulo, izincwadi, kanye nethiyori kuyesabeka, kunzima ukuthola umuntu okwaziyo ukuthola indlela eyimfihlo phakathi kwamanethiwekhi emibono ephikisanayo.

Okubuhlungu kakhulu ngakho konke lokhu ukuthakazelisa kwengqondo; kukhona ukuthambekela kokuzondla ngokwengqondo ngokuphelele ngakho konke okufika engqondweni.

Abahlwempu bengqondo abasaneliswa yiyo yonke leyo layibrari engaphakathi kanye nohlobo oluvamile olugcwele ezimakethe zezincwadi, kodwa manje, futhi njengesiphelo sezinsuku, bazigcwalisa futhi bazilimaze nge-pseudo-esotericism kanye ne-pseudo-occultism eshibhile egcwele yonke indawo njengokhula.

Umphumela wazo zonke lezi zilimi ukudideka nokudideka okusobala kwezigebengu zengqondo.

Ngempela okubalulekile ukuzazi ngokujulile kuwo wonke amazinga engqondo.

Ubumnyama ukungazi; ukukhanya ukuqwashisa; kufanele sivumele ukukhanya kungene ebumnyameni bethu; ngokusobala ukukhanya kunamandla okunqoba ubumnyama.

Ngeshwa, abantu bazivalele ngaphakathi endaweni enuka kabi nengcolile yengqondo yabo, bekhonza ubugovu babo obathandayo.

Abantu abafuni ukuqaphela ukuthi abayilawuli impilo yabo, impela wonke umuntu ulawulwa ngaphakathi abantu abaningi, ngifuna ukubhekisa ngokuqinile kubobonke lobo buningi bobumina esibuthwe ngaphakathi.

Ngokusobala, ngamunye waleyo miyale ufaka engqondweni yethu lokho okufanele sikucabange, emlonyeni wethu lokho okufanele sikusho, enhliziyweni lokho okufanele sikuzwe, njll.

Kulezi zimo, ubuntu bomuntu bumane buyirobhothi elibuswa abantu abahlukahlukene abaphikisana ngobukhulu futhi abalangazelela ukulawula okuphezulu kwezikhungo ezinkulu zomshini ophilayo.

Egameni leqiniso, kufanele siqinisekise ngokujulile ukuthi isilwane esikhaliphile esimpofu okuthiwa umuntu ngephutha, nakuba sizicabanga ukuthi silinganisele kakhulu, siphila ngokungalingani okuphelele ngokwengqondo.

Isilwane esihlakaniphile akusiyona nhlobo eseceleni, ukuba bekunjalo ngabe silinganiselwe.

Ngeshwa, isilwane esikhaliphile sinemicu eminingi futhi lokho kufakazelwe ngokugcwele.

Umuntu oyizinhlanga angalinganisela kanjani? Ukuze kube nokulinganisela okuphelele, kudingeka ukuqaphela okuvukile.

Ukukhanya kuphela kokuqwashisa okuqondiswe hhayi kusukela emakhoneni kodwa ngokugcwele maphakathi kithi, kungaqeda umehluko, ukuphikisana ngokwengqondo futhi kusimisele ukulinganisela kwangempela kwangaphakathi.

Uma sichitha lonke lelo qoqo labomina esinalo ngaphakathi, kuza ukuvuka kokuqwashisa futhi njengokulandelana noma umphumela wokulingana kwe-psyche yethu.

Ngeshwa, abantu abafuni ukuqaphela ukungazi abaphila kukho; balala kakhulu.

Ukube abantu bebephephile, ngamunye ubezizwa abanye ngaphakathi kwabo.

Ukube abantu bebephephile, abanye bethu bebezosizwa ngaphakathi.

Khona-ke ngokusobala izimpi bezingeke zibe khona futhi wonke umhlaba ubuyoba yipharadesi ngempela.

Ukukhanya kokuqwashisa, okusinika ukulingana okungokwengqondo kweqiniso, kuza ukuzosungula into ngayinye endaweni yayo, futhi lokho obekungqubuzana kakhulu nathi, empeleni kuhlala endaweni yako efanele.

Kukhulu ukungazi kwezixuku kangangokuthi azikwazi nokuthola ubuhlobo obukhona phakathi kokukhanya nokuqwashisa.

Ngokungangabazeki ukukhanya nokuqwashisa kuyizici ezimbili zento efanayo; lapho kunokukhanya khona kukhona nokuqwashisa.

Ukungazi ubumnyama futhi lokhu okugcina kukhona ngaphakathi kwethu.

Kukuphela ngokuzibheka ngokwengqondo lapho sivumela khona ukukhanya ukuthi kungene ebumnyameni bethu.

"Ukukhanya kwafika ebumnyameni kodwa ubumnyama abuzange bukuqonde".
