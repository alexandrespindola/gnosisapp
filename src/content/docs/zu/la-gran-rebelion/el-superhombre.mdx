Ikhodi evela e-Anahuac ithi: "ONkulunkulu badala abantu ngokhuni, emva kokubadala babahlanganisa nobungcwele"; bese yengeza: "Akubona bonke abantu abakwazi ukuhlanganiswa nobungcwele".

Akungatshazwa ukuthi into yokuqala edingekayo ukudala umuntu ngaphambi kokuthi ahlanganiswe nalokho okuyikho.

Isilwane esihlakaniphile okuyisona esibizwa ngokuthi umuntu ngeze sibe umuntu.

Uma siqhathanisa umuntu nesilwane esihlakaniphile, singazibonela ngokwethu ukuthi isilwane esihlakaniphile nakuba sifana nomuntu ngokomzimba, ngokwengqondo sihluke ngokuphelele.

Ngeshwa wonke umuntu ucabanga ngokuyiphutha, bacabanga ukuthi bangabantu, bazibiza ngabo.

Besilokhu sikholelwa ukuthi umuntu uyinkosi yendalo; isilwane esihlakaniphile kuze kube yimanje asikabonisi ngisho nokuba yinkosi yaso; uma ingeyona inkosi yezinqubo zayo zengqondo, uma ingakwazi ukuziqondisa ngokuthanda, ngeke ikwazi ukubusa imvelo.

Ngeke sikwamukele umuntu ophenduke isigqila, ongakwazi ukuzibusa futhi ophenduke ithoyizi lamandla obuqhwaga bemvelo.

Noma uyinge-nkosi yendawo yonke noma cha; kulokhu kokugcina ngokungangabazeki kuboniswa iqiniso eliqondile lokuthi akukafiki esimeni somuntu.

Ngaphakathi kwezindlala zobulili zesilwane esihlakaniphile ilanga lifake imbewu yomuntu.

Ngokusobala leyo mbewu ingakhula noma ilahleke unomphela.

Uma sifuna ukuthi leyo mbewu ikhule, kuyadingeka ukuthi sibambisane nomzamo ilanga eliwenza ukudala abantu.

Umuntu oqotho kufanele asebenze kanzima ngenhloso ecacile yokususa kuye izinto ezingafuneki esizithwele ngaphakathi kithi.

Uma umuntu wangempela engazisusi kuye lezo zinto, uzohluleka kabuhlungu; uzophenduka isisu esiphunyekile sikaMama Wendawo Yonke, isihluleki.

Umuntu osebenza ngempela kuye ngenhloso yokuvusa ukwazi, angahlanganiswa nobungcwele.

Ngokucacile umuntu welanga ohlanganiswe nobungcwele, empeleni nangokomthetho uba yi-SUPER-MAN.

Akulula kangako ukufika ku-SUPER-MAN. Akungatshazwa ukuthi indlela eholela ku-SUPERMAN ingaphezu kokuhle nokubi.

Into enhle lapho isisiza, ibe yimbi lapho ingasisizi. Phakathi kwezintambo zevasi kukhona nobugebengu obucashile. Kunobuhle obuningi kumubi nobubi obuningi komuhle.

Indlela eholela ku-SUPER-MAN iyiNdlela Yenceku Yensingo; le ndlela igcwele izingozi ngaphakathi nangaphandle.

Okubi kuyingozi, okuhle nakho kuyingozi; indlela eyesabekayo ingaphezu kokuhle nokubi, inesihluku esibi.

Noma iyiphi ikhodi yokuziphatha ingasimisa ohambweni oluya ku-SUPER-MAN. Ukunamathela kulezo noma lezo zinsuku zakudala, kulezo noma lezo zigcawu kungasimisa endleleni eya ku-SUPER-MAN.

Imithetho, izinqubo, nakuba zihlakaniphile kangakanani, uma zigxile kulolu noma lolu shiseko, kulolu noma lolu bandlululo, kulolu noma lolu qondo kungasithiya ekuthuthukeni kokuthuthukela ku-SUPER-MAN.

I-SUPER-MAN yazi okuhle kokubi nokubi kokuhle; iphethe inkemba yobulungiswa bendawo yonke futhi ingaphezu kokuhle nokubi.

I-SUPER-MAN ngemva kokucekela phansi kuye zonke izindinganiso ezinhle nezimbi, iphenduke into okungekho muntu oyiqondayo, iligiya, ilangabi lomoya wendawo yonke yokuphila elikhanya ebusweni bukaMose.

Esitolo ngasinye sendlela i-anacoreta ethile inikeza izipho zayo ku-SUPER-MAN kodwa lokhu kuqhubeka nohambo lwayo ngale kwezinhloso ezinhle zama-anacoreta.

Lokho okwashiwo abantu ngaphansi kwendawo engcwele yamathempeli kunobuhle obuningi, kodwa i-SUPER-MAN ingaphezu kwamazwi anenhlonipho abantu.

I-SUPER-MAN iligiya futhi izwi layo liyaduma eliqeda amandla okuhle nokubi.

I-SUPER-MAN iyakhanya ebumnyameni, kodwa ubumnyama buyayizonda i-SUPER-MAN.

Izixuku zibiza i-SUPER-MAN njengengendawo ngenxa yokuthi ayilingani ngaphakathi kwama-dogma angenakuphikiswa, noma ngaphakathi kwemisho enenhlonipho, noma ngaphakathi kokuziphatha okuhle kwabantu abathanda ukuzithoba.

Abantu bayayizonda i-SUPER-MAN futhi bayibethelela esiphambanweni phakathi kwezigilamkhuba ngoba abayiqondi, ngoba bayayahlulela kusengaphambili, beyibuka ngelensi yengqondo yalokho okukholelwa ukuthi kungcwele nakuba kuyinto embi.

I-SUPER-MAN ifana nombani owela phezu kwababi noma njengokukhanya kwento engaqondakali futhi elahleka emva kwalokho emfihlakalweni.

I-SUPER-MAN ayingcwele noma iyathandeka, ingaphezu kobungcwele nokuthandeka; kodwa abantu bayibiza ngokuthi ingcwele noma iyathandeka.

I-SUPER-MAN iyakhanya okwesikhashana phakathi kobumnyama balomhlaba bese inyamalala kuze kube phakade.

Ngaphakathi kwe-SUPER-MAN kukhanya kakhulu i-Red Christ. I-Christ eguqukayo, iNkosi Yezinguquko Ezinkulu.
