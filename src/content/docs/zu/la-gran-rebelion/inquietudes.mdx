Akungabazeki ukuthi phakathi kokucabanga nokuzwa kukhona umehluko omkhulu, lokhu akuphikiseki.

Kukhona ukungathembeki okukhulu phakathi kwabantu, kungumoya obandayo walokho okungenamsebenzi, okungajulile.

Izixuku zikholelwa ukuthi okubalulekile yilokho okungabalulekile, zicabanga ukuthi imfashini yakamuva, noma imoto yakamuva, noma lolu daba lomholo oyisisekelo yilona kuphela elibalulekile.

Babiza ngokuthi kubalulekile umbiko wosuku, i-adventure yothando, impilo yokuhlala phansi, ingilazi yotshwala, umjaho wamabhiza, umjaho wezimoto, umbukiso wezinkunzi, inhlebo, ukunyundela, njll.

Ngokusobala, lapho indoda yosuku noma owesifazane we-salon yobuhle bezwa okuthile nge-esotericism, njengoba lokhu kungekho ezinhlelweni zabo, noma ezingxoxweni zabo, noma ezinjabulweni zabo zobulili, basabela ngokungazi ukuthi yini ebandayo eyethusayo, noma bamane bahlanekezele umlomo, baphakamise amahlombe, futhi bahoxe ngokunganaki.

Lokho kungabi nandaba ngokwengqondo, lobo buzizwe obethusayo, kunezisekelo ezimbili; okokuqala ukungazi okukhulu, okwesibili ukungabikho okuphelele kwezinkinga ezingokomoya.

Kuyintuleka ukuxhumana, ukushaqeka kukagesi, akekho owakunika esitolo, futhi phakathi kwalokho okwakukholelwa ukuthi kubalulekile, ingasaphathwa eyokuthi ezinjabulweni zombhede.

Ukube umuntu ubekwazi ukunika isilima esibandayo noma owesifazane ongajulile ukuthinta kukagesi kwamanje, inhlansi yenhliziyo, okuthile okungajwayelekile, okuthile okuseduze kakhulu, mhlawumbe-ke yonke into ibingahluka.

Kepha okuthile kuthatha indawo yezwi eliyimfihlo, ukushaya kwenhliziyo kokuqala, ukulangazelela okujulile; mhlawumbe ubuwula, isigqoko esihle kunoma yikuphi ukukhangisa noma ikhabethe, uswidi omuhle kakhulu endaweni yokudlela, ukuhlangana nomngani kamuva ongabalulekile kithi, njll.

Ubuwula, ubuwula okungabalulekile, yebo banamandla ngesikhathi esithile okwanele ukuqeda ukukhathazeka kokuqala okungokomoya, ukulangazelela okujulile, inhlansi encane yokukhanya, ukushaya kwenhliziyo okusikhathaze okwesikhashana ngaphandle kokwazi ukuthi kungani.

Ukube labo abayizidumbu eziphilayo namuhla, abahambahamba ebusuku ababandayo bekilabhu noma bamane abathengisi bezambulela esitolo esisemgwaqweni omkhulu, ababezange bacindezele ukukhathazeka kokuqala okujulile, ngabe ngalesi sikhathi bayizinkanyezi zomoya, abalandeli bokukhanya, amadoda eqiniso ngomqondo ophelele wegama.

Inhlansi, ukushaya kwenhliziyo, ukububula okuyimfihlakalo, into ethile, kwake kwezwakala ngumthengisi wenyama wasekhoneni, umgqugquzeli wezicathulo noma udokotela ophambili, kepha konke kwaba yize, ubuwula bobuntu buhlala buqeda inhlansi yokuqala yokukhanya; ngemuva kwalokho kuyaqhubeka umoya obandayo wokunganaki okwesabekayo.

Ngokungangabazeki abantu bayagwinywa yinyanga kusasa noma kamuva; leli qiniso aliphikiseki.

Akekho umuntu empilweni ongakaze ezwe ukushaya kwenhliziyo, ukukhathazeka okungavamile, ngeshwa noma yini evela ebuntwini, noma ngabe iyisilima kangakanani, kwanele ukunciphisa lokho okwasikhathaza ngesikhathi sokuthula kobusuku kube uthuli lwe-cosmic.

Inyanga ihlala iwinile lezi zimpi, iyazondla, izondla ngokuqondile ngobuthakathaka bethu.

Inyanga iyimishini emangalisayo; isidalwa senyanga, esingenazo nhlobo izinkinga zokukhanya kwelanga, asihambisani futhi sihamba emhlabeni wamaphupho aso.

Ukube umuntu ubengenza lokho okungenziwa muntu, okungukuthi, ukuvuselela ukukhathazeka okujulile okungase kuvele emfihlakalweni yobusuku obuthile, akungabazeki ukuthi ngokuhamba kwesikhathi ubengafana nobuhlakani belanga futhi ngaleyo ndlela abe yindoda yelanga.

Yilokho kanye iLanga elikufunayo, kepha lezi zithunzi zenyanga ezibanda kangaka, ezingenandaba futhi ezingenandaba, zihlala zigwinywa yinyanga; ngemuva kwalokho kuza ukulingana kokufa.

Ukufa kulinganisa yonke into. Noma yisiphi isidumbu esiphilayo esingenazo izinkinga zelanga, siwohloka kabi kancane kancane kuze kube yilapho sinyanga sigwinya.

ILanga lifuna ukudala abantu, lenza lokho kuhlolwa elabhorethri lemvelo; ngeshwa, lokho kuhlolwa akuzange kumnikeze imiphumela emihle kakhulu, inyanga igwinya abantu.

Kodwa-ke, lokhu esikushoyo akukhathazi muntu, ingasaphathwa eyokuthi izihlakaniphi ezingenalwazi; bazizwa bengumama wamachwane noma ubaba kaTarzan.

ILanga lifake ngaphakathi kwezindlala zobulili zesilwane esihlakaniphile okuyiphutha elibizwa ngokuthi indoda, amagciwane athile elanga athi uma athuthukiswe ngokufanele angasiguqula abe ngamadoda eqiniso.

Kodwa-ke ukuhlolwa kwelanga kunzima kakhulu ngenxa yomoya obandayo wenyanga.

Abantu abafuni ukubambisana neLanga futhi ngaleyo ndlela ngokuhamba kwesikhathi amagciwane elanga awohloka, ayonakala futhi alahleke ngendlela edabukisayo.

Umgogodla oyingcweti womsebenzi weLanga usekuchithweni kwezinto ezingafuneki esiziphethe ngaphakathi.

Lapho uhlanga lwabantu lulahlekelwa yintshisekelo yalo yonke emibonweni yelanga, iLanga liyayibhubhisa ngoba alisalusebenziseli ukuhlolwa kwalo.

Njengoba lolu hlanga lwamanje seluphenduke inyanga engabekezeleleki, engajulile futhi eyimishini, alusalusebenzeli ukuhlolwa kwelanga, isizathu esanele sokuthi luzobhujiswa.

Ukuze kube nokukhathazeka okungokomoya okuqhubekayo, kuyadingeka ukudlulisela isikhungo esinamandla samandla kwisiqu, ekuqapheleni.

Ngeshwa abantu banesikhungo samandla okudonsela phansi ebuntwini, ekhofi, ebha, emabhizinisini asebhange, endlini yokuqokwa noma emakethe, njll.

Ngokusobala, zonke lezi zinto zobuntu futhi isikhungo samandla azo sidonsela kuzo zonke lezi zinto; lokhu akuphikiseki futhi noma yimuphi umuntu onengqondo angakuqinisekisa ngokwakhe nangokuqondile.

Ngeshwa, lapho befunda konke lokhu, izigebengu zengqondo, ezijwayele ukuxoxa kakhulu noma ukuthula ngokuqhosha okungenakubekezeleleka, zikhetha ukulahla incwadi ngokudelela futhi zifunde iphephandaba.

Iziphuzo ezimbalwa zekhofi elihle nomlando wosuku kuyizakhi ezinhle kakhulu zezilwane ezincelisayo ezihlakaniphile.

Kodwa-ke, bazizwa bebaluleke kakhulu; ngokungangabazeki ukuhlakanipha kwabo kubenza babe nezinkolelo-ze, futhi lezi zinto zohlobo lwelanga ezibhalwe kule ncwadi engafanele zibakhathaza kakhulu. Akungabazeki ukuthi amehlo e-bohemian ama-homunculus esizathu ngeke alinge ukuqhubeka nokutadisha lo msebenzi.
