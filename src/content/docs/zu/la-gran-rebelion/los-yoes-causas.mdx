Izinto eziningi ezakha ubuzwilili bomuntu zinomsuka oluthile.

Imizimba yezimbangela ixhumene nemithetho Yezimbangela Nomphumela. Kusobala ukuthi ayikwazi ukuba khona imbangela ngaphandle komphumela, noma umphumela ngaphandle kwembangela; lokhu akunakungatshazwa, akunakuphikiswa.

Bekungeke kwenzeke ukuthi siqede izinto ezahlukahlukene ezinobulwane esiziphethe ngaphakathi kwethu uma singazisusi ngokuphelele izimbangela zangaphakathi zamaphutha ethu engqondo.

Ngokusobala imizimba yezimbangela ihlotshaniswa eduze nezikweletu ezithile zeKarma.

Ukuzisola okujulile kuphela nezivumelwano ezifanele namakhosi omthetho, ezingasinika injabulo yokuthola ukuhlanganiswa kwazo zonke lezo zinto eziyizimbangela ezingasiholela ekuqedweni kokugcina kwezinto ezingafuneki ngandlela thile.

Izimbangela zangaphakathi zamaphutha ethu, nakanjani zingaqedwa ngokwazo ngenxa yomsebenzi ophumelelayo weCristo Intimo.

Ngokusobala imizimba yezimbangela ivame ukuba nobunzima obukhulu.

Isibonelo: Umfundi we-esotericism angakhwabaniswa umfundisi wakhe futhi ngokulandelana umfundi omusha angaphenduka abe yingabazane. Kulolu daba oluqondile umzimba oyimbangela owadala lelo phutha, ungahlukana kuphela ngokuzisola okukhulu kwangaphakathi nangokuxoxisana okukhethekile kakhulu kwe-esoteric.

I-Cristo yangaphakathi ngaphakathi kwethu isebenza kakhulu iqeda izimbangela eziyimfihlo zamaphutha ethu ngokusekelwe emisebenzini eqaphelayo nokuhlupheka kokuzithandela.

Inkosi yobuhle kufanele iphile ekujuleni kwethu okuseduze yonke idrama yasemkhathini.

Umuntu uyamangala lapho ebuka ezweni lezimbangela zonke izinhlupheko iNkosi Yobuhle edlula kuzo.

Ezweni lezimbangela uCristo oyimfihlo udlula kuzo zonke izinhlungu ezingachazeki zeVia Crucis yakhe.

Ngokungangabazeki uPilatu ugeza izandla zakhe futhi azithethelele kodwa ekugcineni agwebe othandekayo ekufeni esiphambanweni.

Kuvela kungajwayelekile kumqalisi obonayo ukukhuphukela eKhalvari.

Ngokungangabazeki ukwazi kwelanga okuhlanganiswe neCristo Intimo, okubethelwe esiphambanweni esikhulu seKhalvari, kukhuluma imisho esabekayo okungavunyelwe abantu ukuba bayiqonde.

Umusho wokugcina (Baba wami ezandleni zakho ngiyawuphathisa umoya wami), ulandelwa ukuduma kwezulu nemidumo nokuzamazama komhlaba okukhulu.

Kamuva uCristo yangaphakathi ngemva kokukhululwa uyabekwa engcwabeni lakhe elingcwele.

Ngokufa uCristo yangaphakathi ubulala ukufa. Kamuva kakhulu ngesikhathi uCristo yangaphakathi kufanele avuke kithi.

Ngokungangabazeki uvuko lukaCristo luyeza ukuzosiguqula ngokuphelele.

Noma iyiphi iNkosi Evukile inamandla amangalisayo phezu komlilo, umoya, amanzi nomhlaba.

Ngokungangabazeki amaNkosi Avukile athola ukungafi, hhayi nje ngokwengqondo kodwa nangokomzimba.

UJesu INkosi Enkulu usaphila nomzimba ofanayo womzimba ayenawo eZweni Elingcwele; I-Count San German eyayiguqula umthofu ibe yigolide futhi yenza amadayimane ekhwalithi engcono kakhulu phakathi nekhulu le-XV, XVI, XVII, XVIII, njll., isaphila namanje.

I-Count Cagliostro eyimfihlakalo futhi enamandla eyathusa iYurophu ngamandla ayo phakathi nekhulu le-XVI, XVII nele-XVIII iyiNkosi Evukile futhi isagcina umzimba wayo ofanayo.
