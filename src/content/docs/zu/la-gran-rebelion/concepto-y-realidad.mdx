Ubani noma yini engaqinisekisa ukuthi umqondo kanye neqiniso kugcina kufana ncamashi?

Umqondo uyinto eyodwa kanti iqiniso kuyinto ehlukile futhi kukhona umkhuba wokuzazisa kakhulu ngemiqondo yethu.

Iqiniso elilingana nomqondo cishe into engenakwenzeka, nokho ingqondo ekhohliswe umqondo wayo ihlala icabanga ukuthi lona kanye neqiniso kuyafana.

Kunoma iyiphi inqubo yengqondo ehlelwe ngendlela efanele ngendlela enengqondo eqondile, kuphikiswana nenye ehlukile eyakheke ngokuqinile ngendlela efanayo noma ephakeme, pho-ke yini?

Izingqondo ezimbili eziqeqeshwe ngokuqinile ngaphakathi kwezakhiwo ezinzima zengqondo ziyaxoxisana, ziyaphikisana, mayelana nokuthi yikuphi iqiniso ngalinye elikholelwa ekunembeni komqondo walo kanye nasemangeni omqondo womunye, kodwa yikuphi kuzo okuyiqiniso?, Ubani ongangena ngobuqotho njengabaqinisekisi kwelinye icala noma kwelinye?, Kwikuphi kuzo, umqondo neqiniso okugcina kufana?

Ngokungangabazeki ikhanda ngalinye liyizwe futhi kuyo yonke naku ngamunye wethu kukhona uhlobo oluthile lobuqaba oluyi-pontifical nolunobushiqela olufuna ukusenza sikholelwe ekulinganeni okuphelele komqondo neqiniso.

Noma ngabe izakhiwo zokucabanga ziqine kangakanani, akukho lutho olungaqinisekisa ukulingana okuphelele kwemiqondo neqiniso.

Labo abazivalele ngaphakathi kwanoma iyiphi inqubo ye-intellectual logistics bahlala befuna ukwenza ukuthi iqiniso lezinto ezibonwayo lihambisane nemiqondo eyakhiwe futhi lokhu kumane nje kuwumphumela wokukhohliseka kokucabanga.

Ukuvulelekela okusha kulula kanzima kwe-classic; ngeshwa abantu bafuna ukuthola, ukubona kuzo zonke izenzakalo zemvelo ubandlululo lwabo, imiqondo, imiqondo yangaphambili, imibono kanye nemibono; akekho okwazi ukwamukela, ukubona okusha ngengqondo ehlanzekile futhi engazenzisi.

Ukuthi izenzakalo zikhulume nesihlakaniphi kungaba yikho okukhonjiswayo; ngeshwa izihlakaniphi zalezi zikhathi azikwazi ukubona izenzakalo, zifuna kuphela ukubona kuzo ukuqinisekiswa kwayo yonke imiqondo yazo yangaphambili.

Nakuba kubonakala kungakholeleki ososayensi banamuhla abazi lutho ngezimo zemvelo.

Lapho sibona ezimeni zemvelo kuphela imiqondo yethu, ngokuqinisekile asiziboni izimo kodwa imiqondo.

Nokho, becasukile ososayensi abayiziwula ngenxa yobuhlakani babo obukhangayo, bakholelwa ngobuwula ukuthi umqondo ngamunye wabo ulingana ngokuphelele nalesi noma lesiya simo esibonwayo, lapho iqiniso lihluke.

Asiphiki ukuthi izitatimende zethu ziyenqatshwa yibo bonke abazivalele yile nqubo ethile yokuhlela; ngokungangabazeki isimo sobupapa nesokungaguquguquki sohlakanipho asinakukwamukela nganoma iyiphi indlela ukuthi lo noma lowo mqondo owenziwe ngendlela efanele awuhambisani ncamashi neqiniso.

Ngokushesha nje lapho ingqondo, ngezinzwa, ibona lesi noma lesiya simo, ngokushesha iyashesha ukusihlukanisa ngaleli noma leli gama lesayensi elizosiza ngokungangabazeki njengesichibi sokufihla ukungazi komuntu.

Ingqondo ayikwazi ngempela ukwamukela okusha, kodwa iyazi ukuthi ingasungula kanjani amagama ayinkimbinkimbi kakhulu afuna ukuthi ngiwenze ngendlela ekhohlisayo lokho okungaziwa ngokuqinisekile.

Sikhuluma kulokhu ngomqondo kaSocrates, singasho ukuthi ingqondo ayigcini nje ngokungazi, kodwa futhi, ayazi ukuthi ayazi.

Ingqondo yesimanje iyancane kakhulu, iye yagxila ekusunguleni amagama enziwe aba nzima kakhulu ukuze ifihle ukungazi kwayo.

Kunezinhlobo ezimbili zesayensi: eyokuqala imane nje iyileso silondolozi semibono engaphansi kokuzithoba esiyithola kakhulu lapho. Eyesibili isayensi emsulwa yalabo abakhanyiselwe, isayensi yenhloso yokuBakhona.

Ngokungangabazeki bekungeke kwenzeke ukungena enkundleni yemidlalo yesayensi ye-cosmic, uma singazange sizifele ngaphambili.

Sidinga ukuhlakaza zonke lezo zinto ezingathandeki esizithwele ngaphakathi kwethu, futhi lapho zihlanganiswe ndawonye, zenza ngokwazo, i-Yo ye-Psychology.

Ngesikhathi umuzwa omkhulu wokuba uqhubeka nokuba sezingeni eliphansi phakathi kwami, phakathi kwemiqondo yami kanye nemibono engezansi, kutholakala ukuthi akunakwenzeka ukwazi ngokuqondile iqiniso elinzima lezimo zemvelo ngokwazo.

Isihluthulelo selabhorethri yemvelo, ingelosi yokufa isiphethe esandleni sayo sokunene.

Kuncane kakhulu esingakufunda ngesimo sokuzalwa, kodwa ngokufa singafunda konke.

Ithempeli elingcwele lesayensi emsulwa litholakala ekujuleni kwethuna elimnyama. Uma imbewu ingafi isitshalo asizalwa. Okusha kuza ngokufa kuphela.

Lapho i-Ego ifa, ukuqaphela kuyavuka ukuze kubone iqiniso lazo zonke izenzakalo zemvelo njengoba zinjalo ngokwazo futhi ngokwazo.

Ukuqaphela kuyakwazi lokho okuzizwisa ngokuqondile, iqiniso eliluhlaza lempilo engaphezu komzimba, imizwelo nengqondo.
