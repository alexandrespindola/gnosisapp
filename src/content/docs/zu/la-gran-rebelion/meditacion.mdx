Empilweni into ebaluleke kakhulu ushintsho olukhulu, oluphelele futhi olungaguquki; okunye ngempela akubalulekile ngisho nangancane.

Ukuzindla kubaluleke kakhulu uma sifuna ngobuqotho ushintsho olunjalo.

Asifuni ngandlela thile ukuzindla okungenamsebenzi, okungajulile nokuyize.

Kudingeka sibe abantu abazimisile bese siyeka ubuningi bobuwula obugcwele lapho ku-pseudoesotericism ne-pseudo-occultism eshibhile.

Kufanele sikwazi ukuzimisela, kufanele sikwazi ukushintsha uma singafuni ngempela ukwehluleka emsebenzini we-esoteric.

Umuntu ongakwazi ukuzindla, ongangile, oyisiphukuphuku, ngeke akwazi ukuqeda ubu-Ego; uyohlala eyisidwedwe esingenamandla olwandle oluvunguza kakhulu lwempilo.

Iphutha elitholwe endaweni yokuphila okusebenzayo, kufanele liqondwe ngokujulile ngenqubo yokuzindla.

Izinto zokufundisa zokuzindla zitholakala ngqo ezenzakalweni ezahlukahlukene noma ezimweni zansuku zonke zokuphila okusebenzayo, lokhu akunakuphikiswa.

Abantu bahlala bekhononda ngezenzakalo ezingathandeki, abakaze bakwazi ukubona usizo lwalezo zenzakalo.

Thina esikhundleni sokukhononda ngezimo ezingathandeki, kufanele sikhiphe kuzo, ngokuzindla, izinto eziwusizo ekukhuleni kwemizwelo yethu.

Ukuzindla okujulile ngaleso simo esithile esijabulisayo noma esingathandeki, kusivumela ukuthi sizizwele ngokwethu ukunambitheka, umphumela.

Kuyadingeka ukwenza umehluko ophelele ngokwengqondo phakathi kwalokho okuyikho ukunambitheka komsebenzi nokunambitheka kwempilo.

Kunoma yikuphi, ukuze uzizwele ukunambitheka komsebenzi, kudingeka ukuguqulwa okuphelele kwesimo sengqondo esivamile esithatha ngaso izimo zokuphila.

Akekho ongasithokozela isimo somsebenzi uma enza iphutha lokuzihlanganisa nezenzakalo ezahlukahlukene.

Impela ukuzihlanganisa kuvimbela ukwazisa okufanele ngokwengqondo kwezenzakalo.

Lapho umuntu ezihlanganisa naleso senzakalo, akaphumeleli ngandlela thile ukukhipha kuso izinto eziwusizo ekuzitholeni kanye nokukhula kwangaphakathi kwengqondo.

I-Esoterist esebenzayo ebuyela ekuzihlanganiseni ngemuva kokulahlekelwa ukugada, izophinde izwe ukunambitheka kwempilo esikhundleni sokunambitheka komsebenzi.

Lokhu kukhombisa ukuthi isimo sengqondo sokwengqondo esiguqulwe ngaphambili, sesibuyele esimweni saso sokuzihlanganisa.

Noma yisiphi isimo esingathandeki kufanele sakhiwe kabusha ngemicabango eqaphelayo ngenqubo yokuzindla.

Ukwakhiwa kabusha kwanoma yisiphi isimo kusivumela ukuthi siqinisekise thina ngokwethu nangokuqondile ukungenelela kwama-yos amaningi abamba iqhaza kuwo.

Izibonelo: Isimo somona wothando; kuwo kungenelela ama-yos olaka, umona ngisho nenzondo.

Ukuqonda ngamunye wala ma-yos, ngamunye walezi zinto, kusho eqinisweni ukuzindla okujulile, ukugxila, ukuzindla.

Umkhuba obonakalayo wokubeka abanye icala uyisithiyo, isithiyo ekuqondeni amaphutha ethu.

Ngeshwa, kuvela umsebenzi onzima kakhulu ukuqeda kithi umkhuba wokubeka abanye icala.

Egameni leqiniso kufanele sisho ukuthi yithi kuphela abanecala lezimo ezahlukahlukene ezingathandeki zokuphila.

Izenzakalo ezahlukahlukene ezijabulisayo noma ezingathandeki zikhona sinathi noma ngaphandle kwethu futhi ziphindaphindeka ngokuzenzakalelayo ngokuqhubekayo.

Ngokuqala ngalesi simiso, ayikho inkinga engaba nesisombululo sokugcina.

Izinkinga zempilo futhi ukube bekukhona isisombululo sokugcina impilo ibingeke ibe impilo kodwa ukufa.

Ngakho-ke kungaba khona ukuguqulwa kwezimo nezinkinga, kodwa azisoze ziyeka ukuphindaphindeka futhi azisoze zaba nesisombululo sokugcina.

Impilo iyisondo eliphenduka ngokuzenzakalelayo nazo zonke izimo ezijabulisayo nezingathandeki, njalo zibuya.

Asikwazi ukumisa isondo, izimo ezinhle noma ezimbi zihlale zicutshungulwa ngokuzenzakalelayo, singakwazi kuphela ukushintsha isimo sethu sengqondo ngezenzakalo zempilo.

Njengoba sifunda ukukhipha izinto zokuzindla phakathi kwezimo zokuphila, sizoziqonda ngokwethu.

Kunoma yisiphi isimo esijabulisayo noma esingathandeki kukhona ama-yos ahlukahlukene okumele aqondwe ngokuphelele ngenqubo yokuzindla.

Lokhu kusho ukuthi noma yiliphi iqembu lama-yos elingenelela kunoma iyiphi idrama, ihlaya noma inhlekelele yokuphila okusebenzayo, ngemuva kokuqondwa ngokuphelele kufanele liqedwe ngamandla kaNkulunkulu uMama uKundalini.

Njengoba sisebenzisa umqondo wokuqaphela ngokwengqondo, lo wokugcina nawo uzoqala ukukhula ngokumangalisayo. Ngakho-ke singakwazi ukubona ngaphakathi hhayi kuphela ama-yos ngaphambi kokuthi asetshenziswe, kodwa futhi ngaso sonke isikhathi somsebenzi.

Lapho lawa ma-yos enqunywa amakhanda futhi achitheka, sizwa ukukhululeka okukhulu, injabulo enkulu.
