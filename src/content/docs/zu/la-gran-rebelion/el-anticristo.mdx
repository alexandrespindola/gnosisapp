Ukuhlakanipha okucwebezelayo njengokusebenza okubonakalayo kwe-Psychological Self, ngokungangabazeki kungaMphikukristu.

Labo abacabanga ukuthi uMphikukristu ungumuntu ongavamile ozelwe kuleyo naleya ndawo emhlabeni noma ovela kuleliya nalelo lizwe, ngempela banamanga ngokuphelele.

Sishilo ngendlela egcizelelayo ukuthi uMphikukristu akuyena umuntu othize, kodwa bonke abantu.

Ngokusobala uMphikukristu usekujuleni komuntu ngamunye futhi uziveza ngezindlela eziningi.

Ingqondo esetshenziselwa umoya iyasiza; ingqondo ehlukaniswe nomoya ayisizi ngalutho.

Ekuhlakanipheni okungenayo ingokomoya kuvela izigebengu, ukubonakaliswa okucacile kukaMphikukristu.

Ngokusobala isigebengu ngokwaso futhi ngokwaso singuMphikukristu. Ngeshwa, umhlaba wamanje nazo zonke izinhlekelele zawo nosizi olukhulu ubuswa nguMphikukristu.

Isimo esiyingozi lapho isintu samanje sitholakala khona ngokungangabazeki sibangelwa uMphikukristu.

Lowo onecala uPaul waseTarsu akhuluma ngaye ezincwadini zakhe ngempela ungumbono onzima walezi zikhathi.

Onecala usefikile futhi uzibonakalisa yonke indawo, impela unomusa wokuba yonke indawo.

Uxoxa emakhefi, uxoxisana e-UN, uhlala kamnandi eGeneva, wenza izivivinyo zaselabhorethri, aqambe amabhomu e-athomu, amarokhethi aqondiswayo, amagesi abulalayo, amabhomu amagciwane, njll, njll, njll.

Ethathekile uMphikukristu ngokuhlakanipha kwakhe, ukukhetheka okuphelele kwalabo abazaziyo, ukholelwa ukuthi uyazazi zonke izenzakalo zemvelo.

UMphikukristu, ezicabangela ukuthi wazi yonke into, evalelwe phakathi kwazo zonke izinto ezibolile zemibono yakhe, uyalenqaba ngokuphelele noma yini ebonakala njengoNkulunkulu noma ekhonzwayo.

Ukuzanelisa kukaMphikukristu, ukuqhosha nozikhukhumezi anayo, kuyinto engabekezeleleki.

UMphikukristu uyayizonda kakhulu imikhuba yobuKristu yokholo, ukubekezela nokuthobeka.

Wonke amadolo ayakhothama phambi kukaMphikukristu. Ngokusobala lowo uqambe izindiza ezisheshayo, imikhumbi emangalisayo, izimoto ezintsha ezikhazimulayo, imithi emangalisayo, njll.

Kulezi zimo, ubani ongabaza uMphikukristu? Noma ngubani onesibindi kulezi zikhathi ukuzikhulumela ngokumelene nazo zonke lezi zimangaliso nezimangaliso zendodana yokulahleka, uzizwisa ngokwakhe ukuhlekwa ngontanga yakhe, ukucasuka, ukugconwa, isiqu sesiwula nesingenalwazi.

Kunzima ukwenza abantu abathanda izinto ezibucayi nabafundile baqonde lokhu, bona ngokwabo bayasabela, bayamelana.

Kuyacaca ukuthi isilwane esihlakaniphile okuyiphutha okuthiwa umuntu, iyirobhothi ehlelelwe i-kinder, isikole samabanga aphansi, esiphakeme, esilungiselelayo, eyunivesithi, njll.

Akekho ongaphika ukuthi irobhothi ehlelelwe liyasebenza ngokohlelo, ngandlela thile alikwazi ukusebenza uma lisuswa ohlelweni.

UMphikukristu wakhe uhlelo okuhlelwa ngalo amarobhothi afana nabantu alezi zikhathi zokwehla.

Ukwenza lokhu kucaciswa, ukugcizelela engikushoyo, kunzima ngendlela esabekayo ngoba kuphume ohlelweni, akukho robhothi efana nomuntu engavuma izinto ezingaphandle kohlelo.

Le ndaba inzima kakhulu futhi ukuminyanisa kwengqondo kukukhulu kakhulu, ukuthi ngandlela thile, irobhothi elinjengomuntu, ngeke lisole nakancane ukuthi uhlelo alusizi, ngoba lihlelwe ngokuvumelana nohlelo, futhi ukungabaza lona kuzobonakala kuyizihlubuki, into engahambisani neqiniso nengenangqondo.

Ukuthi irobhothi lingabaza uhlelo lwayo luyinto engathandeki, into engenakwenzeka ngokuphelele ngoba ukuba khona kwalo kuncike ohlelweni.

Ngeshwa, izinto azinjengoba kucabanga irobhothi elifana nomuntu; kunenye isayensi, okunye ukuhlakanipha, okungamukeleki kurobhothi elifana nomuntu.

Irobhothi elifana nomuntu liyasabela futhi lilungile ekusabeleni ngoba alikahlelelwa enye isayensi noma enye isiko, noma lutho oluhlukile ohlelweni lwalo olujwayelekile.

UMphikukristu wakhe izinhlelo zerobhothi elifana nomuntu, irobhothi likhothama ngokuthobeka phambi kwenkosi yalo. Irobhothi lingalungabaza kanjani ukuhlakanipha kwenkosi yalo?

Ingane izalwa ingenacala futhi ihlanzekile; ubunjalo obuziveza kuzo zonke izidalwa buyigugu kakhulu.

Ngokungangabazeki imvelo ifaka ebuchosheni bezinsana zonke lezo zinto zasendle, zemvelo, zasendle, zomhlaba, ezizenzakalelayo, ezibalulekile ekuthumbeni noma ekubambeni amaqiniso aqukethwe kunoma iyiphi into yemvelo ebonakala ezithweni zomzimba.

Lokhu kusho ukuthi ingane esanda kuzalwa ingakwazi ngokwayo ukuthola iqiniso lalesi senzakalo semvelo, ngeshwa uhlelo lukaMphikukristu luyangenelela futhi izimfanelo ezimangalisayo imvelo eziye yazifaka ebuchosheni bomntwana osanda kuzalwa ziyabhujiswa ngokushesha.

UMphikukristu uyakwenqabela ukucabanga ngendlela ehlukile; sonke isidalwa esizalwayo, ngokomyalo kaMphikukristu kufanele sihlelelwe.

Akungabazeki ukuthi uMphikukristu uyayizonda kakhulu leyo muzwa oyigugu woKuba, owaziwa ngokuthi "ikhono lokubona ngokushesha amaqiniso omhlaba".

Isayensi ehlanzekile, ehlukile kukho konke ukubola kwemibono yaseyunivesithi ekhona lapha, laphaya nalapho, iyinto engamukeleki kurobhothi kaMphikukristu.

Izimpi eziningi, indlala nezifo ziye zasakazwa uMphikukristu kuwo wonke umhlaba, futhi akungabazeki ukuthi uzoqhubeka nokuzisakaza ngaphambi kokuba kufike inhlekelele yokugcina.

Ngeshwa isikhathi sokuhlubuka okukhulu okwamenyezelwa yibo bonke abaphrofethi sesifikile futhi akekho umuntu ongalokotha azikhulumele ngokumelene noMphikukristu.
