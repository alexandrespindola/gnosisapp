Mayelana nomqondo Wenkululeko, ovezwe njalo ngendlela engalungile ngandlela-thile, kwenziwe amaphutha amakhulu.

Impela kulwiwa ngezwi, kukhishwa iziphetho ezingenangqondo, kwenziwe izenzo zobuqhwaga zazo zonke izinhlobo, futhi kuchitheka igazi ezinkundleni zempi.

Igama elithi Inkululeko liyathandeka, wonke umuntu uyalithanda, nokho, akukho ukuqonda kweqiniso ngalo, kunokudideka mayelana naleli gama.

Igama elithi Inkululeko, alinakukwazi ukuqondakala ngokushesha ngumqondo wobuqiniso.

Ngamunye unayo imibono ehlukene ngaleli gama: imibono yomuntu siqu yabantu abangenakho nhlobo ukuba yiqiniso.

Umqondo Wenkululeko ungakwazi nje ukuqondakala ngokugcwele uma amaketanga ejele lethu lengqondo ecekelwe phansi.

Uma nje "mina ngokwami" ngikhona ukuqwashisa kuzobe kusentolongweni; ukuqaqa ejele kungenzeka kuphela ngokucekela phansi kwe-Buddhist, ngokuchitha mina, ngokuyinciphisa ibe umlotha, ibe uthuli lwendawo yonke.
