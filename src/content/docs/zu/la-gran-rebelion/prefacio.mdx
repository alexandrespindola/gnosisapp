ISINGENISO

Ngu: V.M. GARGHA KUICHINES

"IVUKELA ELIKHULU" likaMfundisi ohloniphekile uSamael Aun Weor lisibonisa ngokusobala isikhundla sethu empilweni.

Kufanele siqede konke okusibopha ezintweni eziyize zaleli mpilo.

Lapha siqoqa imfundiso yesahluko ngasinye ukuze siqondise iqhawe elinesibindi elizozibandakanya neMpi yokulwa nalo ngokwalo.

Zonke izihluthulelo zalo msebenzi ziholela ekubhujisweni kwama-Yo ethu, ukuze sikhulule i-Essence okuyiyona nto ebalulekile kithi.

I-Yo ayifuni ukufa futhi umnikazi uzizwa ephansi kunesici.

Emhlabeni kunabantu abaningi abangakwazi futhi ukwesaba kubanga umonakalo yonke indawo.

"AZIKHO IZINTO EZINGENZEKI, OKUKHONA AMADODA ANGAKWAZI".

# ISAHLUKO 1

Isintu asinandaba nobuhle bangaphakathi; okungaphezulu kuvala yonke into. Isihawu asaziwa. Unya lunabalandeli. Ukuzola akukho ngoba abantu bahlala bekhathazekile futhi bephelelwe yithemba.

Isiphetho sabahluphekayo sisezandleni zezoni zazo zonke izinhlobo.

# ISAHLUKO 2

Indlala nokuphelelwa yithemba kuyanda njalo futhi amakhemikhali abhubhisa umkhathi womhlaba, kodwa ikhona ikhambi lobubi obusizungezile: "Ubumsulwa Besayensi" noma ukusetshenziswa kwembewu yomuntu, ukuyiguqula ibe yi-ENERGY elabhorethri yethu yobuntu bese iba yi-Light and Fire lapho sifunda ukusingatha izici ezi-3 zokuqwashisa: 1. Ukufa kwamaphutha ethu. 2. Ukwakha imizimba yelanga ngaphakathi kithi. 3. Ukukhonza Intandane Empofu (Isintu).

Umhlaba, amanzi nomoya kungcoliswa yimpucuko yamanje; igolide lomhlaba alanele ukulungisa ububi; masiyisebenzise igolide eliwuketshezi esilizikhiqizela thina sonke, imbewu yethu, sisebenzisa ngokuhlakanipha ngokwazi isizathu, ngaleyo ndlela sizikwazi ukuthuthukisa umhlaba nokukhonza ngokuqaphela okuvukile.

Sakha iButho Lokusindisa Lomhlaba Ngezwe Lonke nalawo maqhawe anesibindi azovala izikhundla ne-Avatara ye-Aquarius, ngeMfundiso yokuKristiswa ezosikhulula kukho konke okubi.

Uma uzithuthukisa, umhlaba uyathuthuka.

# ISAHLUKO 3

Kwabaningi injabulo ayikho, abazi ukuthi ingumsebenzi wethu, ukuthi singabakhi bayo, abakhi; siyakha ngegolide lethu eliwuketshezi, iMbewu yethu.

Uma sijabule sizizwa sijabule, kodwa leyo mizuzwana iyaphela; uma ungenalo igunya phezu kwengqondo yakho yasemhlabeni, uzoba yisigqila sayo, ngoba ayaneliseki ngalutho. Kufanele siphile eMhlabeni ngaphandle kokuba yisiGqila sayo.

# ISAHLUKO 4 UKHULUMA NGENKULULEKO

Inkululeko iyasithakazelisa, singathanda ukukhululeka, kodwa bakhuluma kabi ngomuntu bese siyathakathwa bese sigcina sesiyizigqila futhi sigcina sesiyizigilamkhuba.

Lowo ophinda izinhlobo ezinonya, uyingozi kakhulu kunalowo ozidalayo, ngoba lo angaqhubeka ngomona, umhawu noma ngephutha eliqotho; ophindayo ukwenza njengomfundi othembekile wobubi, uyisilima esinamandla. "Funani Iqiniso futhi lizokukhulula". Kodwa iqamba-manga lingalifinyelela kanjani Iqiniso? Kulezo zimo, usondela njalo kulo lwesinye isiphetho, Iqiniso.

Iqiniso liyimfanelo kaBaba Othandekayo, njengoba kunjalo nokholo. Umqambimanga angaluthola kanjani ukholo, uma kuyisipho sikaBaba? Izipho zikaBaba azikwazi ukwamukelwa yilowo ogcwele amaphutha, ububi, izifiso zamandla nobukhulu. Siyizigqila zezinkolelo zethu; balekela umboni okhuluma ngalokho akubona ngaphakathi; lowo uthengisa iZulu futhi konke kuzothathwa kuye.

"Ngubani okhululekile? Ubani ozuze inkululeko edumile? Bangaki abazikhululile? Maye! Maye! Maye!", (Samael). Lowo oqambayo akasoze akhululeka ngoba umelene noMthandekayo oyiqiniso elihlanzekile.

# ISAHLUKO 5 UKHULUMA NGOMLAWU WA-PENDULUM

Konke kuyageleza futhi kuyabuya, kuyenyuka futhi kwehle, kuya futhi kuza; kodwa abantu banesithakazelo esengeziwe ekunyakazeni komakhelwane kunokunyakaza kwabo futhi ngaleyo ndlela bahamba olwandle olunesiphepho lokuba khona kwabo, besebenzisa izinzwa zabo ezinamaphutha ukufanelekela ukunyakaza komakhelwane; futhi yena ini? Lapho umuntu ebulala ama-yo akhe noma amaphutha, uyazikhulula, uyazikhulula emthethweni eminingi yemishini, ephula elinye lamagobolondo amaningi esiwakha futhi ezwa ukulangazelela inkululeko.

Izigebengu ziyohlale ziyingozi, kufanele sifune umgudu olungile, inaliti yesikali.

Isizathu sithobeka ngenhlonipho phambi kweqiniso elifeziwe futhi umqondo uyanyamalala phambi kweqiniso elicacile. "Kuphela ngokuqeda iphutha lapho kufika Iqiniso" (Samael).

# ISAHLUKO 6 UMQONDO NEQINISO

Kuyasiza ukuthi umfundi alifundisise leli sahluko ukuze agweme ukuqondiswa yizinto eziyiphutha; uma nje sinezinkinga ezingokwengqondo, izenzo ezimbi, iziphazamiso, imiqondo yethu nayo iyoba nephutha; lokhu kokuthi: "Kunjalo ngoba ngikuqinisekisile", kungobuwula, konke kunezingxenye, amakhona, amaza, phezulu naphansi, amabanga, izikhathi, lapho isiwula esiseceleni sibona izinto ngendlela yaso, siziphoqa ngobudlova, sesabisa izilaleli zaso.

# ISAHLUKO 7 I-DIALECTIC YOKUQAPHELA

Siyazi futhi lokho kuyasifundisa, ukuthi singakwazi kuphela ukuvusa ukuqwashisa ngesisekelo somsebenzi oqondayo nokuhlupheka kokuzithandela.

Umdevoti weNdlela uyachitha i-ENERGY yencane iphesenti lokuqwashisa lapho ezihlanganisa nezenzakalo zokuba khona kwakhe.

Uthisha oqeqeshiwe, obambe iqhaza kuDrama Yokuphila, akazihlanganisi nalelo drama, uzizwa njengesibukeli kunkundla yokuphila; lapho njengakuma-movie, izibukeli ziyathanda umenzi wobubi noma olinyazwayo. INkosi Yokuphila yilowo ofundisa izinto ezinhle neziwusizo kumdevoti wendlela, zibenze babe ngcono kunalokho abayikho, uMama Wendalo uyamlalela futhi abantu bayamlandela NGE-UTHANDO.

"Ukuqwashisa kuyi-Light engabonwa engqondweni" (Samael Aun Weor) okwenzeka kumuntu olele nge-Light yokuqwashisa, yilokho okwenzeka kumuntu oyimpumputhe nge-Light yeLanga.

Lapho iradiyasi yokuqwashisa kwethu yanda, umuntu ngokwakhe uzizwela ngaphakathi okungokoqobo, okuyikho.

# ISAHLUKO 8 I-SCIENTIFIC JARGON

Abantu bayesaba izenzakalo zemvelo futhi balindele ukuthi zidlule; isayensi iyazilebula futhi izibiza ngamagama anzima, ukuze abantu abangazi lutho bangaphinde bazihlupe.

Kunezigidi zezidalwa ezazi amagama okubi kwazo, kodwa azazi ukuthi zingazibhubhisa kanjani.

Umuntu uyisingatha ngokumangalisayo izimoto eziyinkimbinkimbi azidalayo, kodwa akazi ukuthi angayisingatha kanjani eyakhe imoto: Umzimba ahamba ngawo njalo; ukuze umuntu awazi, kwenzeka kuye, lokho okwenzeka elabhorethri engcolile noma engcolile; kodwa umuntu utshelwa ukuthi ayihlanze, ngokubulala amaphutha akhe, imikhuba, izenzo ezimbi, njll., futhi akakwazi, ukholwa ukuthi ukugeza kwansuku zonke kwanele.

# ISAHLUKO 9 U-ANTICHrist

Siyamphethe ngaphakathi. Akasivumeli ukuthi sifinyelele kuBaba Othandekayo. Kodwa lapho simnqoba ngokuphelele, uzivezela ngezindlela eziningi.

U-Antichrist uyazizonda izimfanelo zobuKristu zokholo, ukubekezela, ukuthobeka, njll. "Indoda" iyayithanda isayensi yayo futhi iyalalela.

# ISAHLUKO 10 I-PSYCHOLOGICAL YO

Kufanele sizibheke ezenzweni njalo, sazi ukuthi lokho esikwenzayo kuyasithuthukisa, ngoba ukubhujiswa kwabanye akusisizi ngalutho. Lokhu kusiyisa kuphela ekukholweni ukuthi singababhubhisi abahle, kodwa lokhu kulungile lapho sibhubhisa ububi bethu kithi, ukuze sizithuthukise ngokuvumelana noKristu ophilayo esimphatha njengamandla okukhanyisa nokuthuthukisa uhlobo lweSintu.

Ukufundisa ukuzonda, lokho wonke umuntu uyakwazi, kodwa ukufundisa UKUTHANDA, lokho kunzima.

Funda ngokuqikelela mfundi othandekayo lesi sahluko, uma ufuna ukubhubhisa ububi bakho ngokwakho kusukela ezimpandeni.

# IZAHLUKO 11 KUYA KU-20

Abantu bayathanda ukunikeza imibono, ukwethula abanye njengoba bebabona, kodwa akekho ofuna ukuzazi, okuyikho okubalulekile eNdleleni YokuKristiswa.

Lowo oqamba amanga amaningi usesimeni; ILight iwukuqwashisa futhi lapho lokhu kubonakala kithi, kungokwenza umsebenzi ophakeme. "Ngezithelo zabo niyobazi", kusho uJesu Kristu.

Akashongo ukuthi ngezihlaselo abazenzayo. Ama-Gnostic... phaphamani!!!

Indoda ehlakaniphile noma enomzwelo isebenza ngokuvumelana nobuhlakani bayo noma imizwelo yayo. Laba njengabahluleli bayesabeka, bezwa lokho okubafanele futhi bahlulele noma banikeze njengeqiniso likaNkulunkulu, lokho umqambimanga omkhulu kunabo abakuqinisekisayo.

Lapho kunokukhanya, khona ukuqwashisa. Ukukhuluma kabi kungumsebenzi wobumnyama, lokho akuveli ekukhanyeni.

Esahlukweni 12 kukhulunywa ngezingqondo ezi-3 esinazo: Ingqondo Yezinkanuko noma yezinzwa, Ingqondo Ephakathi; lena yiyona ekholelwa kukho konke ekuzwayo futhi ihlulele ngokuvumelana nomenzi wobubi noma umvikeli; lapho iqondiswa ukuqwashisa, ingumlamuli ohloniphekile, iba yithuluzi lesenzo; izinto ezifakwa engqondweni ephakathi zakha izinkolelo zethu.

Lowo onokholo lweqiniso, akadingi ukukholelwa; umqambimanga ngeke abe nokholo, imfanelo kaNkulunkulu nolwazi oluqondile, noma ingqondo yangaphakathi, esiyithola lapho sibulala labo abangafuneki esibathwala ePsiquis yethu.

Ubuhle bokwazi amaphutha ethu, bese siyahlaziya futhi kamuva siwabhubhise ngosizo lukamama wethu uRAM-IO, kusivumela ukuthi sishintshe futhi singabi yizigqila zababusi abancane abavela kuzo zonke izinkolelo.

I-Yo, i-Ego, iyinhlangano ngaphakathi kwethu; yi-Ser kuphela enamandla okusungula ukuhleleka ngaphakathi kithi, ePsiquis yethu.

Ekusifundeni ngokuningiliziwe isahluko 13, siyaqaphela lokho okwenzeka kuMboni Onamaphutha, lapho ehlangana nama-Yo angafuneki wanoma yimuphi umfowethu osemncane oyiNdlela. Lapho sizibheka siyayeka ukukhuluma kabi ngomuntu.

I-Ser ne-Saber, kufanele zilingane; kanjalo kuzalwa ukuqonda. Ukwazi, ngaphandle kolwazi lwe-Ser, kuletha ukudideka kwengqondo kwazo zonke izinhlobo; kuzalwa isigebengu.

Uma i-Ser inkulu kuno-Saber, kuzalwa ungcwele oyisiphukuphuku. Isahluko 14 sisinikeza izihluthulelo ezinhle kakhulu zokuzazi; SingumNkulunkulu waphezulu, sinendlu yezicukuthwane esizungezile engasilona elayo; ukuyeka konke lokho ukukhululeka futhi abasho...

"Ubugebengu bugqoka ingubo kaMahluleli, ingubo kaThisha, isambatho sompofu, isudi yeNkosi ngisho nengubo kaKristu" (Samael).

UMama wethu Waphezulu uMarah, uMariya noma uRAM-IO njengoba simbiza kanjalo ama-gnostic, ungumlamuleli phakathi kukababa Othandekayo nathi, umlamuleli phakathi kwaboNkulunkulu bemvelo kanye nomthakathi; ngaye futhi ngaye, izinto zemvelo ziyasilalela. Uyi-Deva yethu Yaphezulu, umlamuleli phakathi kukaNkulunkulukazi Obusisiwe uMama womhlaba kanye nemoto yethu engokomzimba, ukuze sifeze izimangaliso ezimangalisayo futhi sikhonze abantu esifana nabo.

Emshweni Wobulili nomfazi uMpristikazi, indoda iba yisifazane futhi umfazi abe ngumuntu; UMama wethu uRAM-IO uyena kuphela ongabuyisela i-cosmic dust ku-Yo yethu namabutho ayo. Ngemithetho enomzwelo asikwazi ukwazi izinto ze-Ser, ngoba izinzwa ziyithuluzi eliwugqinsi, eligcwele amaphutha, njengoba injalo nenkosi yazo; kuyadingeka ukuthi zithathelwe izintatheli, ngokubulala amaphutha, ububi, iziphazamiso, okunamathelisayo, izifiso, nakho konke okuthokozisayo engqondweni yasemhlabeni, okusinikeza ukungabaza okukhulu.

Esahlukweni 18 siyabona, ngokomthetho wokulinganisa, ukuthi njengoba siphila ezweni noma endaweni yomhlaba, kanjalo nasenhliziyweni yethu kukhona indawo engokwengqondo lapho sitholakala khona. Funda mfundi othandekayo lesi sahluko esithakazelisayo ukuze wazi ngaphakathi ukuthi yikuphi ebusheni, ikholoni noma indawo otholakala kuyo.

Lapho sisebenzisa uMama wethu waphezulu uRAM-IO sibhubhisa ama-yo ethu kasathane futhi sizikhulula emithethweni engama-96 yokuqwashisa, ebusweni obuningi obubolile. Inzondo ayisivumeli ukuba sithuthuke ngaphakathi.

Umqambimanga wona uNkulunkulu wakhe futhi isifebe wona uMoya oNgcwele; uyaziphatha kabi emicabangweni, emazwini nasezenzweni.

Kukhona ababusi abancane abakhuluma izimangaliso ngabo, bayenga abantu abaningi abangazi lutho, kodwa uma, umsebenzi wabo uhlaziya, sithola ukubhujiswa nobuhlongandlebe; i-vide ngokwayo noma iyazimisela ukuzihlukanisa nokuzikhohlwa.

Esahlukweni 19, kusinika ukukhanya ukuthi singaweli ekukhohlisweni kokuzizwa singcono. Sonke singabafundi abakhonza i-Avatara; isihluku siyazisola ngokulinyazwa futhi isiwula, ukuthi asidumiseki. Lapho siqonda ukuthi ubuntu kufanele sibubhubhise, uma umuntu esisiza kulowo msebenzi onzima kufanele sibonge.

Ukholo ulwazi olumsulwa, ukuhlakanipha kokuhlangenwe nakho okuqondile kwe-Ser, "izinkolelo-ze zokuqwashisa kwe-egoic ziyafana nezinkolelo-ze ezibangelwa izidakamizwa" (Samael).

Esahlukweni 20, kusinikeza izihluthulelo zokuqeda amakhaza enyanga phakathi nendawo esithuthuka futhi sikhule kuyo.

# IZAHLUKO 21 KUYA KU-29

Ku-21 kusikhuluma ngakho futhi kusifundisa ukuthi sizindle futhi sizindle, sazi ukuthi singashintsha. Lowo ongazi ukuthi angazindla kanjani ngeke akwazi ukuchitha i-Ego.

Ku-22 kusikhuluma ngo-"UKUBUYELA NOKUPHINDWA". Kuyindlela elula njengoba kusikhuluma ngakho ngokubuya; uma singafuni ukuphinda izigcawu ezibuhlungu, kufanele sihlukanise ama-Yo, asethulela zona; sifundiswa ukuthuthukisa ikhwalithi yezingane zethu. Ukubuya kuhambisana nezenzakalo zokuba khona kwethu, lapho sinomzimba ongokomzimba.

UKristu osondelene umlilo womlilo; lokho esikubonayo nesikuzwayo yingxenye engokomzimba yomlilo kaKristu. Ukuza komlilo kaKristu yisenzakalo esibaluleke kakhulu empilweni yethu, lo mlilo uthatha yonke inqubo yamasilinda ethu noma ubuchopho bethu, okumele siqale siwahlanze ngezinto ezi-5 zeNdalo, sisebenzisa izinkonzo zikaMama wethu Obusisiwe uRAMIO.

"Umuntu Oqalisiwe kufanele afunde ukuphila ngendlela eyingozi; kubhalwe kanjalo".

Esahlukweni 25, uThisha usikhuluma ngohlangothi olungaziwa lwethu, esilukhombisa njengokungathi siwumshini wokukhombisa i-movie, bese sibona amaphutha ethu esikrinini somunye.

Konke lokhu kusikhombisa abantu abaqotho abaphazamisekile; njengoba izinzwa zethu zisiqambela amanga ngendlela efanayo singabaqambi bamanga; izinzwa ezifihlekile zibangela izinhlekelele lapho sizivusa ngaphandle kokubulala amaphutha ethu.

Esahlukweni 26 kusikhuluma ngabakhapheli abathathu, izitha zikaHiram Abiff, uKristu Wangaphakathi, amademoni: 1.- Ingqondo 2.- Intando Embi 3.- Isifiso

Ngamunye wethu siphethe abakhapheli abathathu e-psiquis yethu.

Kusifundisa ukuthi uKristu Wangaphakathi eyihlanzeko nokuphelela, usisiza ukuba siqede izinkulungwane zabantu abangafuneki esibaphethe ngaphakathi. Kuleso sahluko kusifundiswa ukuthi uKristu Oyimfihlo yiNkosi ye-REBELLION ENKULU, enqatshwa abaPristi, abadala nababhali bethempeli.

Esahlukweni 28, kusikhuluma nge-Super-Man nokungazi ngokuphelele kwezixuku ngaye.

Imizamo ye-Humanoid yokuba yi-Super-Man iyizimpi nezimpi ngokumelene nayo, ngokumelene nomhlaba nakho konke okuzama lo mhlaba wosizi.

Esahlukweni 29, isahluko sokugcina, kusikhuluma nge-Holy Grail, isitsha sika-Hermes, indebe kaSolomoni; I-Holy Grail ifanekisela ngendlela eyingqayizivele i-Yoni yesifazane, ubulili, i-soma yama-mystics lapho boNkulunkulu Abangcwele bephuza khona.

Le ndebe yenjabulo ayinakushoda kunoma yiliphi iThempeli lezimfihlakalo, noma empilweni yoMpristi we-Gnostic.

Lapho ama-gnostic eqonda le mfihlakalo, kuzoshintsha impilo yawo yomshado futhi i-altare eliphilayo lizowakhonza ukuthi asebenze njengompristi eThempelini LoThando Eliphezulu.

Kwangathi ukuthula okujulile kungabusa enhliziyweni yakho.
