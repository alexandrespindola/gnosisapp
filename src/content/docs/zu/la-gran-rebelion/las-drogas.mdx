Ukuzihlukanisa ngokwengqondo komuntu kusenza sikwazi ukubona ubunjalo obungenamusa bezinga eliphezulu ngaphakathi ngamunye wethu.

Uma umuntu ekwazile ukuziqinisekisa ngokuqondile iqiniso elibambekayo labantu ababili kumuntu oyedwa, ophansi ezingeni elivamile elivamile, ophezulu nge-okthava ephakeme, khona-ke konke kuyashintsha futhi kulokhu sizama ukwenza empilweni ngokuhambisana nezimiso eziyisisekelo eziphethwe ekujuleni KOKUBA kwakhe.

Njengoba impilo yangaphandle ikhona, kanjalo nempilo yangaphakathi ikhona.

Umuntu wangaphandle akuyena konke, ukuzihlukanisa ngokwengqondo kusifundisa iqiniso lomuntu wangaphakathi.

Umuntu wangaphandle unendlela yakhe yokuba, uyinto enezimo eziningi nokusabela okujwayelekile empilweni, unodoli ohambiswa yimicu engabonakali.

Umuntu wangaphakathi uyi-SER eyiqiniso, icutshungulwa kweminye imithetho ehluke kakhulu, ayisoze yakwazi ukuguqulwa ibe irobhothi.

Umuntu wangaphandle akashayi isigwedlo ngaphandle kwendololwane, uzizwa njengokuthi ukhokhelwe kabi, uyamzwela, uzicabangela kakhulu, uma eyisosha ufisa ukuba yijenerali, uma eyisisebenzi efektri uyakhononda uma engakhushulwa, ufuna ukuthi izenzo zakhe ezinhle ziqashelwe ngokufanele, njll.

Akekho ongafinyelela EKUZALWENI KWESIBILI, azale kabusha njengoba iVangeli leNkosi lisho, uma eqhubeka nokuphila ngokwengqondo yomuntu ophansi ovamile.

Lapho umuntu eqaphela ubuze bakhe kanye nosizi lwangaphakathi, lapho enesibindi sokubuyekeza impilo yakhe, ngokungangabazeki uyazazi ukuthi akanawo amakhono anoma yiluphi uhlobo.

"Babusisiwe abampofu emoyeni, ngoba bayokwamukela umbuso wamazulu."

Abampofu emoyeni noma abaswele emoyeni, ngempela yilabo abaqaphela ubuze babo, ukungabi namahloni kanye nosizi lwangaphakathi. Lolo hlobo lwezidalwa ngokungangabazeki luyathola ukukhanyiselwa.

"Kulula ukuthi ikamela lilingene ngentunja yenaliti, kunokuba isicebi singene embusweni wamazulu."

Kuyabonakala ukuthi ingqondo ecebiswe yizenzo eziningi ezinhle, imihlobiso nezindondo, izimfanelo ezihlonishwayo zomphakathi kanye nemibono eyinkimbinkimbi yezifundo, ayimpofu emoyeni futhi ngenxa yalokho ayisoze yakwazi ukungena embusweni wamazulu.

Ukuze ungene eMbusweni, ingcebo yokholo iba ebaluleke kakhulu. Uma ukuzihlukanisa ngokwengqondo kungenzeki kumuntu ngamunye wethu, UKHOLO luba yinto engaphezu kokungenakwenzeka.

UKHOLO lulwazi olumsulwa, ukuhlakanipha okungokwesipiliyoni okuqondile.

UKHOLO ukuzizwisa okuqondile kwalokho okuyiqiniso; okuhlangenwe nakho okuhle komuntu wangaphakathi; ukuqonda okungcwele okuyiqiniso.

Umuntu wangaphakathi, lapho eziqonda ngokuqondile imihlaba yakhe yangaphakathi ngokuhlangenwe nakho okuyimfihlakalo, kuyabonakala ukuthi uyayazi futhi imihlaba yangaphakathi yabo bonke abantu abahlala ebusweni bomhlaba.

Akekho ongalwazi imihlaba yangaphakathi yeplanethi yoMhlaba, yesimiso sonozungezilanga kanye nomthala esihlala kuwo, uma engazange azi imihlaba yakhe yangaphakathi kuqala. Lokhu kufana nomuntu ozibulalayo obalekela impilo ngomnyango ongamanga.

Okutholwayo okungeziwe komuntu oluthayo kunezimpande zabo ezithile kulungu elinengekayo le-KUNDARTIGUADOR (inyoka eyenga ye-Edene).

Ukuqwashisa okuboshelwe phakathi kwezinto eziningi ezakha i-Ego kucutshungulwa ngenxa yokuboshwa kwako.

Ukuqwashisa kwe-egoic kuba, esimweni se-comatose, ngemicabango yokukhohlisa efana kakhulu naleyo yanoma yimuphi umuntu osesimweni sethonya lomuthi othile.

Singabeka lolu daba ngale ndlela elandelayo: imicabango yokukhohlisa yokuqwashisa kwe-egoic ilingana nemicabango yokukhohlisa ebangelwa izidakamizwa.

Ngokusobala lezi zinhlobo ezimbili zemicabango yokukhohlisa zinezimbangela zazo zangempela kulungu elinengekayo le-KUNDARTIGUADOR. (Bheka isahluko XVI salencwadi).

Ngokungangabazeki izidakamizwa ziqeda imisebe ye-alfa, ngakho-ke ngokungangabazeki ukuxhumana okuyimvelo phakathi kwengqondo nobuchopho kuyalahleka; lokhu empeleni kuphumela ekwehlulekeni okuphelele.

Umlutha wezidakamizwa uguqula umkhuba ube inkolo futhi ecabanga ngendlela ephambene ukuthi uzothola lokho okuyiqiniso ngaphansi kwethonya lezidakamizwa, engazi ukuthi okutholwayo okungeziwe okukhiqizwa yi-marijuana, i-L.S.D., i-morphine, amakhowe okukhohlisa, i-cocaine, i-heroin, i-hashis, amaphilisi okuthoba ngendlela eyeqile, ama-amphetamines, ama-barbiturates, njll., njll., njll., yimicabango yokukhohlisa eyakhiwe yisitho esinengekayo se-KUNDARTIGUADOR.

Abalutha bezi-dakamizwa bevolovo, bewohloka ngokuhamba kwesikhathi, ekugcineni bazicwilisa ngendlela eqinisekile ngaphakathi kwemihlaba yesihogo.
