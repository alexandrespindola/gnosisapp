Emsebenzini ojulile wangaphakathi, ngaphakathi kwendawo yokuqikelela komqondo wami, kufanele sithole ngqo yonke imidlalo yendawo yonke.

UKristu Wangaphakathi kufanele asuse zonke izinto ezingafuneki esizithwele ngaphakathi kwethu.

Ukuhlanganiswa kwemicabango eminingi ekujuleni kwemicabango yethu kuyakhala, kufuna ukubethelwa kweNkosi yangaphakathi.

Ngokungangabazeki ngamunye wethu uphethe abahlaseli abathathu engqondweni yakhe.

UJudas, idemoni lesifiso; uPilatu idemoni lengqondo; UKayafase, idemoni lobubi.

Laba bahlaseli abathathu babethela inkosi yokuphelela ekujuleni komphefumulo wethu.

Lezi izinhlobo ezintathu eziqondile zezinto ezingezona zobuntu ezibalulekile kumdlalo wendawo yonke.

Ngokungangabazeki le midlalo okukhulunywe ngayo ibilokhu iphilwa ngasese ekujuleni kokwazi okuphezulu kokuba khona.

Akukhona-ke, ukuthi idrama yendawo yonke iyimpahla yeGran Kabir uJesu njengoba abantu abanolwazi lokungazi bahlala becabanga.

Abaqalayo bayo yonke iminyaka, iNkosi yawo wonke amakhulu eminyaka, kuye kwadingeka ukuba baphile le midlalo yendawo yonke ngaphakathi kwabo, lapha nanjengamanje.

Nokho, uJesu iGran Kabir wayenesibindi sokumela le midlalo yangaphakathi esidlangalaleni, emgwaqweni futhi emini, ukuze avule incazelo yokuqala kubo bonke abantu, ngaphandle kokuhlukahluka kohlanga, ubulili, isigaba noma umbala.

Kuyamangalisa ukuthi kukhona umuntu ongayifundisa umdlalo ojulile kuzo zonke izizwe zomhlaba esidlangalaleni.

UKristu Wangaphakathi, njengoba engeyena umuntu ofisa inkanuko kufanele azisuse kuye izakhi zengqondo zokufisa inkanuko.

UKristu Wangaphakathi njengoba ngokwakhe eyukuthula nothando kufanele azisuse kuye izakhi ezingafuneki zentukuthelo.

UKristu Wangaphakathi, njengoba engeyena umuntu onamanga kufanele azisuse kuye izakhi ezingafuneki zokuhaha.

UKristu Wangaphakathi, njengoba engeyena umuntu onomona kufanele azisuse kuye izakhi zengqondo zomona.

UKristu Wangaphakathi njengoba eyikuthobeka okuphelele, ukuthobeka okungenamkhawulo, ubulula obuphelele, kufanele azisuse kuye izinto ezinengekayo zokuqhosha, ize, ukuzikhukhumeza.

UKristu Wangaphakathi, izwi, iLogos yoMdali ehlala iphila njalo, kufanele isuse ngaphakathi kwethu, kuyo ngokwayo futhi ngayo ngokwayo izinto ezingafuneki zokungenzi lutho, ubuvila, ukunganqamuki.

INkosi Yokuphakama ejwayele konke ukuzila ukudla, epholile, engalokothi ibe ngumngane wokuphuza nokudla okukhulu kufanele isuse kuyo izinto ezinengekayo zokuminza.

I-symbiosis eyinqaba kaKristu-Jesu; uKristu-Indoda; ingxube engavamile yezulu nobuntu, ephelele futhi engaphelele; njalo njalo ukuhlola kweLogos.

Okuthakazelisa kakhulu ngakho konke lokhu ukuthi uKristu oyimfihlo uhlala enqoba; othile ohlala ehlula ubumnyama; othile osusa ubumnyama ngaphakathi kwakhe, lapha nanjengamanje.

UKristu Oyimfihlo yinkosi Yokuvukela Okukhulu, enqatshwa abapristi, abadala nababhali bethempeli.

Abapristi bayamzonda; okungukuthi, abamqondi, bafuna iNkosi Yokuphakama iphile kuphela ngesikhathi ngokuvumelana nemibono yabo engantengantengi.

Abadala, okungukuthi, abahlali bomhlaba, abanikazi bezindlu abahle, abantu abahlakaniphile, abantu abanolwazi bayamzonda iLogos, uKristu Obomvu, uKristu Wokuvukela Okukhulu, ngoba ephuma emhlabeni wemikhuba yabo emidala, esabela futhi ephethwe amatshe ezikhathini eziningi ezedlule.

Ababhali bethempeli, izigebengu zobuhlakani bayamzonda uKristu Wangaphakathi ngoba uyinto ephikisayo noMphikukristu, isitha esichazwe ngaso sonke leso sikhohlakaliyo sethiyori zasenyuvesi esichichima ezimakethe zemizimba nemiphefumulo.

Abahlaseli abathathu bayamzonda uKristu Oyimfihlo futhi bamholele ekufeni ngaphakathi kwethu nasezindaweni zethu zengqondo.

UJudas idemoni lesifiso lihlala lishintsha inkosi ngemali engamashumi amathathu esiliva, okungukuthi, ngotshwala, imali, udumo, ize, ubufebe, ukuphinga, njll.

UPilatu idemoni lengqondo, uhlala egeza izandla zakhe, uhlala ezibiza njengomsulwa, akaze abe necala, uhlala ezithethelela yena nabanye, ufuna izindlela zokugwema, izindlela zokuphunyuka ukuze agweme imithwalo yakhe, njll.

UKayafase idemoni lobubi uhlala ekhaphela inkosi ngaphakathi kwethu; INdoda Ethandekayo eyinikeza intonga yokwelusa izimvu zayo, nokho, umkhapheli ohlambalazayo uguqula i-altare libe umbhede wokuzijabulisa, ufebe ngokungapheli, uyaphinga, athengise amasakramenti, njll.

Laba bahlaseli abathathu benza inkosi eyintandokazi yangaphakathi ihlupheke ngasese ngaphandle kwanoma yisiphi isihawu.

UPilatu uyamfakazela umqhele wameva ezindaweni zakhe, mina okhohlakele ngiyamshaya, ngiyamehlisa, ngiyawuqalekisa endaweni engokwengqondo yangasese ngaphandle kwanoma yiluphi uhlobo lwesihe.
