Ukucabanga ngokujulile nangokunakekela ngokugcwele kubukeka kuyinto exakile kule nkathi yokwehla nokonakala.

Evela eSikhungweni Sobuhlakani kuvela imicabango ehlukahlukene, engaveli ku- "Mina" oqhubekayo njengoba abantu abangaqondi, abafundile becabanga ngobuwula, kodwa kwabo "Mina" abahlukene kulowo nalowo kithi.

Lapho indoda icabanga, ikholelwa ngokuqinile ukuthi yona ngokwayo futhi izicabangela yona.

Isidalwa esincelisayo esikhaliphile asifuni ukuqaphela ukuthi imicabango eminingi edlula ekuqondeni kwayo, imvelaphi yayo ikubo "Mina" abahlukene esibaphethe ngaphakathi.

Lokhu kusho ukuthi asibona abantu abacabangayo beqiniso; asikabi nayo ingqondo yomuntu ngamunye.

Nokho, ngamunye walabo "Mina" abahlukene esibathwele ngaphakathi, usebenzisa iSikhungo sethu Sobuhlakani, usisebenzisa ngaso sonke isikhathi lapho engacabanga.

Kungaba yize ngempela, ukuzihlobanisa nalomcabango ongemuhle noma olimazayo, sikholelwa ukuthi impahla yethu.

Ngokusobala lo noma lowo micabango emibi ivela kunoma yimuphi u-"Mina" oye wasebenzisa kabi iSikhungo sethu Sobuhlakani ngesinye isikhathi.

Imicabango emibi ikhona yezinhlobo ezahlukene: Ukusola, ukungathembi, ububi komuntu omunye, umona wothando, umona ongokwenkolo, umona wezombusazwe, umona wobungane noma wohlobo lomndeni, ukuhaha, inkanuko, impindiselo, intukuthelo, ukuziqhenya, umhawu, inzondo, intukuthelo, ukweba, ukuphinga, ubuvila, ukuminza, njll., njll., njll.

Empeleni maningi kangaka amaphutha engqondo esinawo, ukuthi noma ngabe besinokunambitha okwenziwe ngensimbi namazwi ayinkulungwane okukhuluma, besingeke sikwazi ukuwabalula ngokuphelele.

Njengomphumela noma isiphetho salokho okushiwo ngenhla, kuba yinto eyisimungulu ukuzihlanganisa nemicabango emibi.

Njengoba kungenakwenzeka ukuthi kube khona umphumela ngaphandle kwembangela, sigcizelela ngokugcwele ukuthi akukaze kube khona umcabango ngokwawo, ngokuzenzakalelayo...

Ubudlelwano phakathi komcabangi nomcabango buyabonakala; umcabango ngamunye omubi unemvelaphi yawo kumcabangi ohlukile.

Kulowo nalowo kithi kukhona abacabangi abaningi ababi, njengoba kunemicabango yalolo hlobo.

Uma le ndaba ibukwa ngombono obanzi "Wabacabangi Nemicabango", kwenzeka ukuthi ngamunye "Wabomuntu esibathwele eNgqondweni yethu, ngokuqinisekile umcabangi ohlukile.

Ngokungangabazeki ngaphakathi komunye nomunye wethu, kukhona abacabangi abaningi kakhulu; nokho, ngamunye walaba nakuba eyiyingxenye nje, uzicabangela njengento yonke, ngomzuzu othile...

Abantu abanokuziqhenya, abazithandayo ngokweqile, abantu abazazisayo, abantu abaxhaphazayo, abasoze bawamukela umqondo "Wobuningi Babacabangi" ngoba bazithanda kakhulu, bazizwa "beyindoda kaTarzan" noma "umama wezintshontsho"...

Banganamukela kanjani abantu abanjalo abangajwayelekile umqondo wokuthi abanayo ingqondo yomuntu ngamunye, ehlakaniphile, emangalisayo?...

Nokho labo Abahlakaniphile bazicabangela okungcono kakhulu futhi baze bagqoke ingubo ka-Aristippus ukuze babonise ukuhlakanipha nokuthobeka...

Indaba yasendulo ilandisa ukuthi u-Aristippus, efuna ukubonisa ukuhlakanipha nokuthobeka, wagqoka ingubo endala egcwele izicephu nezimbobo; wabamba ngesandla sakhe sokudla Intonga Yefilosofi waya emigwaqweni yase-Athene...

Kuthiwa lapho uSocrates embona eza, wathi ngezwi elikhulu: "O Aristippus, ubuze bakho buyabonakala ngezimbobo zengubo yakho!".

Lowo ongahlali njalo esesimweni sokuqapha, ukuthola izinto ezintsha, ukuqapha, ecabanga ukuthi uyacabanga, uzihlanganisa kalula nanoma yimuphi umcabango ongemuhle.

Ngenxa yalokhu, ngeshwa uqinisa amandla amabi ka-"Mina Ongemuhle", umbhali womcabango ofanele okukhulunywa ngawo.

Lapho sizihlanganisa kakhulu nomcabango ongemuhle, lapho siyoba yizigqila kakhulu "Zomuntu" ofanele owohlobo lwawo.

Ngokuphathelene ne-Gnosis, iNdlela Eyimfihlo, umsebenzi wokuzisebenzela, izilingo zethu ezithile zitholakala ncamashi "KuboMina" abazonda i-Gnosis, umsebenzi we-esoteric, ngoba abazazi ukuthi ubukhona babo ngaphakathi kwengqondo yethu busongelwa kakhulu yi-Gnosis nomsebenzi.

Labo "Mina Ongemuhle" nabahluphi kalula bathatha izindima ezinemibono ethile egcinwe eSikhungweni sethu Sobuhlakani futhi ngokulandelana badala ama-current engqondo ayingozi futhi ayalimaza.

Uma samukela leyo micabango, labo "Mina Ongemuhle" okuthi ngesikhathi esithile balawule iSikhungo sethu Sobuhlakani, ngeke sikwazi ukuzikhulula emiphumeleni yabo.

Akufanele nanini sikhohlwe ukuthi wonke "Mina Ongemuhle" "UziKhehlisa" futhi "Uyakhohlisa", isiphetho: Uqamba amanga.

Ngaso sonke isikhathi lapho sizwa ukulahlekelwa amandla ngokungazelelwe, lapho umfakisicelo edumele, nge-Gnosis, ngomsebenzi we-esoteric, lapho elahlekelwa intshisekelo futhi eshiya okungcono, kusobala ukuthi ukhohliswe ngu-Mina Ongemuhle othile.

"U-Mina Ongemuhle Wobufebe", uqeda amakhaya amahle futhi wenze izingane zingajabuli.

"U-Mina Ongemuhle Womona", ukhohlisa izidalwa ezithandana kakhulu futhi ucekela phansi injabulo yazo.

"U-Mina Ongemuhle Wokuqhenya Okungokomoya" ukhohlisa abazinikele eNdleleni futhi laba, bezizwa behlakaniphile, bayamzonda uMfundisi wabo noma bayamkhaphela...

U-Mina Ongemuhle ukhangela kokuhlangenwe nakho kwethu siqu, ezinkumbulweni zethu, ezifisweni zethu ezinhle kakhulu, ebuqothweni bethu, futhi, ngokukhetha ngokuqinile konke lokhu, wethula okuthile ekukhanyeni okungelona iqiniso, okuthile okuthakazelisayo futhi kuza ukwehluleka...

Nokho, lapho umuntu ethola "uMina" esenzweni, lapho efunde ukuphila esesimweni sokuqapha, ukukhohlisa okunjalo kuba yinto engenakwenzeka...
