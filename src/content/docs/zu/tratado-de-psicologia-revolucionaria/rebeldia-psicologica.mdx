Akukubi kubakhumbuze abafundi bethu ukuthi kukhona iphuzu lezibalo ngaphakathi kwethu... Ngokungangabazeki lelo phuzu alitholakali esikhathini esedlule, futhi alitholakali nasesikhathini esizayo...

Noma ubani ofuna ukuthola lelo phuzu eliyimfihlakalo, kufanele alifune lapha manje, ngaphakathi kwakhe, ngalo mzuzu, hhayi umzuzwana phambili, hhayi umzuzwana emuva... Izinti ezimbili eziqonde ngqo nezivundlile zeSiphambano Elingcwele, zitholakala kuleli phuzu...

Ngakho-ke sithola ukuthi njalo ngalo mzuzu sibhekene nezindlela ezimbili: okweVundla nokweQonda... Kuyabonakala ukuthi okweVundla "kuyaxaka", kulandelwa "uVicente nabo bonke abantu", "uVillegas nabo bonke abafikayo", "uDon Raimundo nabo bonke abantu"...

Kuyacaca ukuthi okuQondile kuhlukile; yindlela yezihlubuki ezihlakaniphile, eyamaGuquko... Lapho umuntu ezikhumbula, lapho esebenza kuye, lapho engazihlanganisi nazo zonke izinkinga nezinhlupheko zokuphila, empeleni uhamba ngeNdlela Eqondile...

Impela akulula neze ukuqeda imizwelo emibi; ukulahlekelwa yikho konke ukuzihlanganisa nendlela yethu yokuphila; izinkinga zazo zonke izinhlobo, amabhizinisi, izikweletu, izinkokhelo, izindlu, ucingo, amanzi, ugesi, njll., njll., njll. Abantu abangasebenzi, labo okuthe ngenxa yesizathu esithile noma esinye balahlekelwa umsebenzi, umsebenzi, ngokusobala bayahlupheka ngenxa yokuntuleka kwemali futhi ukukhohlwa icala labo, ukungakhathazeki, noma ukuzihlanganisa nenkinga yabo, empeleni kunzima kakhulu.

Labo abahluphekayo, labo abakhala, labo abaye baba yizisulu zokukhaphela, zenkokhelo embi empilweni, zokungabongi, zokunyundelwa noma zokukhwabanisa, empeleni bayazikhohlwa, ukuba kwabo kwangempela kwangaphakathi, bazihlanganisa ngokuphelele nenhlekelele yabo yokuziphatha...

Umsebenzi wokuzithuthukisa uyisici esiyinhloko seNdlela Eqondile. Akekho ongangena eNdleleni Yokuvukela Okukhulu, uma engazange asebenze kuye... Umsebenzi esikhuluma ngawo uwuhlobo lwengqondo; ibhekene noguquko oluthile lwamanje esizithola sikukho. Sidinga ukufunda ukuphila njalo ngalo mzuzu...

Ngokwesibonelo, umuntu ozithola ephelelwe yithemba ngenxa yenkinga ethile yothando, yezomnotho noma yezombusazwe ngokusobala uzikhohliwe... Umuntu onjalo uma emisa isikhashana, uma ebheka lesi simo bese ezama ukuzikhumbula bese ezama ukuqonda umqondo wesimo sakhe sengqondo... Uma ecabanga kancane, uma ecabanga ukuthi konke kuyadlula; ukuthi impilo ingumfanekiso nje, iyaphela futhi nokuthi ukufa kunciphisa zonke izinto eziyize zezwe zibe umlotha...

Uma eqonda ukuthi inkinga yakhe empeleni ayilutho ngaphandle "kwelangabi lika petate", umlilo wesikhashana ocima maduze, uzobona ngokumangala ukuthi konke kushintshile... Ukuguqula ukusabela okuzenzakalelayo kungenzeka ngokusebenzisa ukubhekana okucacile kanye nokuzindla Ngaphakathi Kokuba...

Kuyacaca ukuthi abantu basabela ngokuzenzakalelayo ezimweni ezihlukahlukene zokuphila... Abantu abampofu!, Bavame ukuba yizisulu. Lapho othile ebancoma bayamoyizela; lapho behlukunyezwa, bayahlupheka. Bayathuka uma bethukwa; bayalimaza uma belinyazwa; abalokothi bakhululeke; abantu abanjengabo banamandla okubasusa enjabulweni bayise osizini, ethembeni bayise ekuphelelweni yithemba.

Umuntu ngamunye walabo abahamba ngeNdlela Evundlile, ufana nensimbi yomculo, lapho ngamunye wabantu abanjengabo edlala lokho afuna ukukwenza... Lowo ofunda ukuguqula ubudlelwano obuzenzakalelayo, empeleni ungena "eNdleleni Eqondile". Lokhu kumelela ushintsho oluyisisekelo "eSilinganisweni Sokuba" umphumela oyingqayizivele "wokuVukela kwengqondo".
