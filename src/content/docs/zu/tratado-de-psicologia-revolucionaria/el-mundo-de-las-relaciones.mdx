Umhlaba wobudlelwano unezici ezintathu ezihluke kakhulu okudingeka sizicacise ngokunembile.

Okokuqala: Sihlobene nomzimba womhlaba. Okusho ukuthi, nomzimba ophilayo.

Okwesibili: Siphila emhlabeni futhi ngokulandelana okunengqondo sihlobene nomhlaba wangaphandle kanye nezindaba ezisithintayo, umndeni, amabhizinisi, imali, izindaba zomsebenzi, umsebenzi, ezombusazwe, njll., njll., njll.

Okwesithathu: Ubudlelwano bomuntu naye uqobo. Kubantu abaningi lolu hlobo lobudlelwano alubalulekile neze.

Ngeshwa abantu banesifiso sokuba nobuhlobo nezinhlobo ezimbili zokuqala kuphela, bebheka ngendelelo enkulu uhlobo lwesithathu.

Ukudla, impilo, imali, amabhizinisi, empeleni yizinto ezikhathaza kakhulu "Isilwane Esihlakaniphile" okuthiwa "umuntu" ngephutha.

Manje: Kuyacaca ukuthi kokubili umzimba ophilayo nezindaba zomhlaba zingaphandle kwethu.

Umzimba Womhlaba (umzimba ophilayo), kwesinye isikhathi uyagula, kwesinye isikhathi uphilile njalo njalo.

Sihlala sikholelwa ukuthi sinolwazi oluthile ngomzimba wethu, kodwa eqinisweni ngisho nososayensi abahamba phambili emhlabeni abazi okuningi ngomzimba wenyama negazi.

Akungabazeki ukuthi umzimba ophilayo ngenxa yenhlangano yawo enkulu futhi eyinkimbinkimbi, ngokuqinisekile ungaphezu kokuqonda kwethu.

Mayelana nohlobo lwesibili lobudlelwano, sihlala siyizisulu zezimo; kuyadabukisa ukuthi asikaze sifunde ukudala izimo ngokuqaphela.

Baningi abantu abangakwazi ukuzivumelanisa nanoma yini noma nanoma ubani noma babe nempumelelo yangempela empilweni.

Lapho uzicabanga usebenzisa i-engeli yomsebenzi we-esoteric we-Gnostic, kuyadingeka ukuthola ukuthi yikuphi kulezi zinhlobo ezintathu zobudlelwano esingenzi kahle kuzo.

Kungase kwenzeke ukuthi sihlobene ngephutha nomzimba ophilayo futhi ngenxa yalokho siyagula.

Kungenzeka ukuthi asihlobene kahle nomhlaba wangaphandle futhi ngenxa yalokho sibe nezingxabano, izinkinga zezomnotho nezenhlalakahle, njll., njll., njll.

Kungenzeka ukuthi asihlobene kahle nathi uqobo futhi ngokulandelana sihlupheke kakhulu ngenxa yokuntula ukukhanyiselwa kwangaphakathi.

Ngokusobala uma isibani segumbi lethu singaxhunyiwe kukagesi, indlu yethu izoba sebumnyameni.

Labo abahlushwa ukuntuleka kokukhanyiselwa kwangaphakathi, kufanele baxhume izingqondo zabo neZikhungo eZiphezulu zeNgqondo yabo.

Ngokungangabazeki sidinga ukusungula ubuhlobo obufanele hhayi kuphela noMzimba wethu Womhlaba (umzimba ophilayo) kanye nomhlaba wangaphandle, kodwa futhi nengxenye ngayinye yeNgqondo yethu.

Abagulayo abanethezekile abakhathele odokotela nemithi eminingi kangaka, abasafuni ukwelashwa futhi iziguli ezinamathemba zilwela ukuphila.

E-Casino yaseMonte Carlo izigidi zabantu ezalahlekelwa yimpahla yabo ngokugembula, zazibulala. Izigidi zomama abampofu ziyasebenza ukuze zisekele izingane zabo.

Banengculaza abafisa abacindezelekile abathi ngenxa yokuntuleka kwamandla engqondo nokukhanyiselwa okuseduze, bayekile umsebenzi we-esoteric ngokwabo. Bambalwa abazi ukuthi bangazisebenzisa kanjani izinkinga.

Ngezikhathi zokulingwa okuqinile, ukudangala nokuchitheka, umuntu kufanele anikele ekukhunjweni okuseduze kwakhe.

Ekujuleni kwethu ngamunye kukhona i-TONANZIN yase-Aztec, i-STELLA MARIS, i-ISIS yaseGibhithe, uNkulunkulu Mama, esilindele ukwelapha inhliziyo yethu ebuhlungu.

Lapho umuntu ezinikeza yena ngokwakhe ukushaqeka kwe-"Ukukhumbula Wena", ushintsho oluyisimangaliso ngempela luyenzeka kuwo wonke umsebenzi womzimba, ukuze amangqamuzana athole ukudla okuhlukile.
