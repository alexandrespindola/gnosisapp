Kufanele silangazelele ushintsho lweqiniso, siphume kulo mkhuba omdidayo, kulobu mphilo obuyisimiso, obudinisayo... Okokuqala okufanele sikuqonde ngokucacile ukuthi ngamunye wethu, noma ngabe ungumuntu ophakathi nendawo noma oyisisebenzi, ocebile noma ophakathi, isicebi noma ompofu, uzithola ekulenzeleni elithile lezinga lokuba yikho...

Izinga lokuba yikho likadakwa lehlukile kunelikamuntu ozithibayo futhi elephethelene lihluke kakhulu kunelikantombazane. Lokhu esikushoyo akunakuphikiswa, akunakuphikiswana... Uma sesifika kule ngxenye yesahluko sethu, akukho esilahlekelwa yikho ngokuzicabanga ngesitebhisi esisuka phansi siye phezulu, siqonde phezulu futhi sinamalunga amaningi...

Ngokungangabazeki sitholakala kwelinye lala malunga; amalunga angezansi kuzoba nabantu ababi kakhulu kunathi; amalunga aphezulu kuzoba nabantu abangcono kunathi... Kule Vertical engavamile, kulesi sitebhisi esimangalisayo, kucacile ukuthi singathola wonke Amazinga Okuba Yikho... umuntu ngamunye uhlukile futhi akekho ongakuphika lokhu...

Ngokungangabazeki asikhulumi manje ngobuso obubi noma obuhle, futhi akukhona nje ngeminyaka. Kunabantu abasha nabadala, asebekhulile asebezokufa nezingane ezisanda kuzalwa... Indaba yesikhathi neminyaka; lokho kokuzalwa, ukukhula, ukukhula, ukushada, ukuzala, ukuguga nokufa, okukaHorizontal kuphela...

"Esitebhisini Esihle", kuVertical umqondo wesikhathi awukwazi. Ezingeni elinjalo singathola kuphela "Amazinga Okuba Yikho"... Ithemba eliyisimiso labantu alisizi ngalutho; bakholelwa ukuthi ngokuhamba kwesikhathi izinto zizoba ngcono; yilokho okwakucabanga ogogo nomkhulu bethu; amaqiniso ayeza ukuzobonisa okuphambene nalokho...

"Izinga Lokuba Yikho" yilona elibalulekile futhi lokhu kunguVertical; sizithola sikwelinye ilunga kodwa singakwazi ukukhuphukela kwelinye ilunga... "Isitebhisi Esihle" esikhuluma ngaso futhi esisho "Amazinga Okuba Yikho" ahlukene, ngokuqinisekile, ahlangene nhlobo nesikhathi esiqondile... "Izinga Lokuba Yikho" eliphezulu liphezulu ngaphezu kwethu njalo...

Akusikho esikhathini esizayo esikude esiqondile, kodwa lapha manje; ngaphakathi kwethu; kuVertical... Kuyabonakala futhi noma ubani angaqonda, ukuthi imigqa emibili —Horizontal neVertical— ihlangana njalo ngaphakathi kwePsychological yethu futhi yakha iSiphambano...

Ubuntu buyathuthuka futhi buziveza emgqeni oqondile weMpilo. Buzalwa futhi bufela ngaphakathi kwesikhathi sabo esiqondile; buyaphela; alikho ikusasa lobuntu bomuntu ofile; akuyona iSer... Amazinga eSer; iSer ngokwayo, ayiyona eyesikhathi, ayihlangene nhlobo noMgqa Oqondile; itholakala ngaphakathi kwethu. Manje, kuVertical...

Kungaba yize ngempela ukufuna iSer yethu ngaphandle kwethu... Akukubi ukusho lokhu njengoba kuhlanganisa konke okulandelayo: Iziqu, iziqu, ukukhushulwa, njll., ezweni elingaphandle elingokwenyama, ngeke kuholele ekuthuthukisweni kwangempela, ekuhlolweni kabusha kweSer, ekukhuphukeleni elungeni eliphezulu "Kamazinga eSer"...
