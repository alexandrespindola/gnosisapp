Umongo esinawo ngaphakathi ngamunye wethu uvela phezulu, emazulwini, ezinkanyezini...

Ngokungangabazeki umongo omuhle uvela ku-note "LA" (Umthala iMilky Way, umthala esihlala kuwo)

Umongo omuhle udlula ku-note "SOL" (Ilanga) bese kuthi ku-note "FA" (Indawo yamaplanethi) ungena kulo mhlaba ungene ngaphakathi kwethu.

Abazali bethu bakha umzimba olungele ukwamukela lo mongo ovela ezinkanyezini...

Ngokusebenza kanzima kithi ngokwethu nokuzidela ngabantu esiphila nabo, siyobuyela sinqobile esifubeni esijulile se-Urania...

Siphilela isizathu kulo mhlaba, senzelwe okuthile, ngesizathu esithile esikhethekile...

Ngokusobala kukhona okuningi okumele sikubone, sikufunde futhi sikuqonde, uma ngabe sifisa ngempela ukwazi okuthile ngathi ngokwethu, ngempilo yethu...

Kuyadabukisa impilo yalowo oshona engawazi umongo wempilo yakhe...

Ngamunye wethu kumele azitholele mathupha umqondo wempilo yakhe, lokho okumgcina eboshiwe ejele lobuhlungu...

Ngokusobala kunokuthile ngaphakathi kithi ngamunye okusenza impilo ibe buhlungu futhi okumele silwe nakho ngokuqinile...

Akudingekile ukuthi siqhubeke sisebuhlungwini, kumele sikunciphise kube uthuli lwasemkhathini lokho okusenza sibuthakathaka futhi singajabuli kangaka.

Akusizi ngalutho ukuziqhenya ngeziqu, izindondo, izitifiketi, imali, ukucabanga okuyize okungaphansi komuntu, izimfanelo ezaziwayo, njll, njll, njll.

Akumele sikhohlwe ukuthi ubuzenzisi nokuziqhenya okuyize kobuntu bamanga, kusenza sibe abantu abayiziyatha, abadala, ababambekayo, abasabelayo, abangakwazi ukubona okusha...

Ukufa kunezincazelo eziningi ezinhle nezingezinhle.

Ake sicabangele lokho kubona okukhulu "kukaKABIR Omkhulu uJesu Kristu". "Abangasekho mabangcwabe abangasekho". Abantu abaningi yize bephila empeleni bafa kukho konke okungenzeka ukusebenza kubo ngokwabo ngakho-ke, nganoma yiluphi ushintsho oluseduze.

Bangabantu abavalelwe emibonweni nasezinkolelweni zabo; abantu abaqandisiwe ezinkumbulweni zakudala; abantu abagcwale ubandlululo lwemvelo; abantu abayizigqila zalokho abantu abazokusho, abafudumele ngokwesabekayo, abangenandaba, kwesinye isikhathi "abahlakaniphile" abaqiniseka ukuthi baseqinisweni ngoba batshelwe kanjalo, njll, njll, njll.

Abafuni labo bantu ukuqonda ukuthi lo mhlaba "uyiGymnasium Yengqondo" okungenzeka ngayo ukuqeda lobo bubi obuyimfihlo sonke esinabo ngaphakathi...

Ukuba labo bantu abampofu bebeqonda isimo esidabukisa kangaka abakuso, bebezothuthumela ngovalo...

Noma kunjalo, abantu abanjalo bahlala becabanga okungcono ngabo; bayaziqhenya ngezimfanelo zabo, bazizwa bephelele, benomusa, besiza, bephakeme, benomusa, behlakaniphile, befeza izibopho zabo, njll.

Impilo esebenzayo njengesikole inhle kakhulu kodwa ukuyithatha njengenhloso ngokwayo, akunasisekelo.

Labo abathatha impilo ngokwayo, njengoba iphilwa nsuku zonke, abakaqondi isidingo sokusebenza kubo ngokwabo ukuze kufezeke "Ushintsho Olukhulu".

Ngeshwa abantu baphila ngokuzenzakalelayo, abakaze bezwe lutho ngomsebenzi wangaphakathi...
