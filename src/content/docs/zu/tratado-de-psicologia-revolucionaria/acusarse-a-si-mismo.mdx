Umgubo okukhona ngaphakathi kithi ngamunye uvela phezulu, eZweni, ezinkanyezini... Ngokungangabazeki umgubo omangalisayo uvela ku-note "LA" (i-Milky Way, i-Galaxy esiphila kuyo).

Umgubo oyigugu udlula ku-note "SOL" (Ilanga) bese kuthi ngemuva kwe-note "FA" (Indawo Yamaplanethi) ungena kulo mhlaba ungene ngaphakathi kithi. Abazali bethu bakhe umzimba ofanele ukwamukela lo mgubo ovela ezinkanyezini...

Ngokuzikhandla ngokusebenza kithi futhi sizidelela abantu esiphila nabo, sizobuyela sinqobile ekujuleni kwe-Urania... Siphila kulo mhlaba ngesizathu esithile, ngenxa yento ethile, ngenxa yento ekhethekile...

Ngokusobala kukhona okuningi okumele sikubone, sikufunde futhi sikuqonde ngaphakathi kithi, uma ngempela sifisa ukwazi okuthile ngathi, ngempilo yethu... Kuyadabukisa impilo yalowo ofa engasazi isizathu sempilo yakhe...

Ngamunye wethu kumele azitholele incazelo yempilo yakhe, lokho okumenza abhajwe ejele lobuhlungu... Ngokucacile kukhona okuthile ngaphakathi kithi okusenza sibe nempilo ebuhlungu futhi okumele silwe nakho ngokuqinile... Akudingeki ukuthi siqhubeke sisebunzimeni, kubalulekile ukunciphisa kube uthuli lwe-cosmic lokho okusenza sibe buthakathaka futhi singajabuli.

Akusizi ngalutho ukuziqhenya ngeziqu, izindondo, ama-diploma, imali, ubuhlakani obungenamsebenzi, izimfanelo ezaziwayo, njll, njll, njll. Akufanele nanini sikhohlwe ukuthi ubuzenzisi nokuziqhenya kobuntu bamanga kusenza sibe abantu abayiziphukuphuku, abagugele, abathanda ukubambezela, abaphikisayo, abangakwazi ukubona okusha...

Ukufa kunezincazelo eziningi ezinhle nezingezinhle. Ake sicabangele lokhu okumangalisayo okushiwo "uJesu Kristu oMkhulu KABIR": "Abantu abafile mabangcwabe abafile babo". Abantu abaningi yize bephila empeleni bafilele wonke umsebenzi ongase ube khona ngabo futhi ngaleyo ndlela, nganoma yiluphi ushintsho olungaphakathi.

Bangabantu abagxile ezinkolelweni zabo nasezinkolelweni zabo; abantu abaqinisiwe ezinkumbulweni zezinsuku eziningi ezedlule; abantu abagcwele ubandlululo lwakudala; abantu abayizigqila zalokho abanye abazokusho, abafudumele ngokwesabekayo, abangenandaba, kwesinye isikhathi "bahlakaniphile" beqiniseka ukuthi baseqinisweni ngoba batshelwe kanjalo, njll, njll, njll.

Laba bantu abafuni ukuqonda ukuthi lo mhlaba "uyiGymnasium Engokwengqondo" lapho kungaba nokwenzeka ukuqeda lobo bubi obuyimfihlo sonke esinabo ngaphakathi... Ukube laba bantu abampofu bebeqonda isimo sabo esidabukisayo kangaka, bebezothuthumela ngokwesaba...

Kodwa-ke, abantu abanjalo bahlala becabanga okuhle ngabo; bayaziqhenya ngezimfanelo zabo, bazizwa bephelele, benomusa, bekhonza, bephana, benobubele, behlakaniphile, befeza imisebenzi yabo, njll. Impilo yansuku zonke njengesikole inhle kakhulu, kodwa ukuyithatha njengenhloso yodwa, akunamqondo.

Labo abathatha impilo ngokwayo, njengoba iphilwa nsuku zonke, abakaqondi isidingo sokuzisebenzela ukuze bazuze "Ushintsho Olukhulu". Ngeshwa abantu baphila ngomshini, abakaze bezwe lutho ngomsebenzi ongaphakathi...

Ukushintsha kuyadingeka, kodwa abantu abazi ukuthi bangashintsha kanjani; bahlupheka kakhulu futhi abaze bazi ukuthi kungani behlupheka... Ukuba nemali akuyona yonke into. Izimpilo zabantu abaningi abacebile zivame ukuba yize ngempela...
