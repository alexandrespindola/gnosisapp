Kucace kakhulu futhi akunzima ukukuqonda ukuthi, uma umuntu eqala ukuziqaphela ngokujulile ngombono wokuthi akamunye kodwa uMningi, uqala ngempela ukusebenzela konke lokho akuphethe ngaphakathi.

Yisithiyo, isikhubekiso, isiqhumiso somsebenzi wokuZiqaphela Ngokujulile, amaphutha alandelayo engqondo: Ukuqamba amanga, (Ukuqhosha ngokweqile, ukuzibona ungunKulunkulu) Ukuzithanda, (Ukukholelwa kuMina Omi Phakade; ukukhonza noma yiluphi uhlobo lwe-Alter-Ego) Ukuqhosha. (Ukwazi konke, Ukuzanelisa, ukuzidla, ukuzibona ungenaphutha, ukuziqhenya ngokomoya, umuntu ongakwazi ukubona umbono womunye umuntu)

Uma kuqhubekwa nenkolelo engenangqondo yokuthi umuntu unguye yedwa, ukuthi unayo i-Yo ehlala njalo, kuba yinto engaphezu kokungenzeki ukusebenza ngokujulile kumuntu uqobo.

Umuntu ohlala ezibona Eyedwa, ngeke akwazi ukuzihlukanisa nezinto zakhe ezingafuneki. Uzobheka yonke imicabango, imizwa, izifiso, imizwelo, uthando, njll., njll., njll., njengemisebenzi ehlukene, engashintsheki, yemvelo yakhe futhi aze azithethelele nakwabanye ngokuthi amaphutha akhe athile ayizakhi zofuzo...

Umuntu owamukela iMfundiso yamaYo amaningi, uqonda ngokusekelwe ekuziqapheleni ukuthi yonke intshisekelo, umcabango, isenzo, uthando, njll., kuhambisana naleli noma leli Yo elihlukile, elihlukene...

Noma yimuphi umdlali wezemidlalo we-Auto-Observation ejulile, usebenza ngokujulile ngaphakathi kwakhe futhi uzama ukususa eNgqondweni yakhe izinto ezahlukahlukene ezingafuneki azithwele ngaphakathi...

Uma umuntu eqala ngempela futhi ngobuqotho obukhulu ukuziqaphela ngaphakathi, ugcina ngokuhlukana kabili: Umqapheli kanye nalowo oqashelwayo.

Uma lokho kwehlukana kungazange kwenzeke, kusobala ukuthi asisoze sathatha isinyathelo esiya phambili eNdleleni emangalisayo YokuZazi.
