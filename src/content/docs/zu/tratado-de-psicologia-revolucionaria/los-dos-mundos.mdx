Ukubuka nokuzibuka wena siqu yizinto ezimbili ezihluke ngokuphelele, nokho, zombili zidinga ukunakwa.

Ekubukeni ukunaka kuqondiswe ngaphandle, ezweni elingaphandle, ngamawindi ezinzwa.

Ekuzibukeni komuntu siqu, ukunaka kuqondiswe ngaphakathi futhi ngalokhu izinzwa zokubona ngaphandle azisizi, isizathu esanele kakhulu sokuthi kube nzima ngomuntu osaqalayo ukubuka izinqubo zakhe zengqondo ezijulile.

Iphuzu lokuqala lesayensi esemthethweni ohlangothini lwayo olusebenzayo, yilokho okungabonwa. Iphuzu lokuqala lomsebenzi wokusebenzela wena, ukuzibuka, lokho okuzibukeka.

Ngokungangabazeki la maphuzu amabili okuqala ashiwo ngenhla, asiholela ezindleleni ezihluke ngokuphelele.

Kungenzeka yini ukuthi othile aguge ecwile phakathi kwama-dogma avumelanayo esayensi esemthethweni, efunda izenzakalo zangaphandle, ehlola amangqamuzana, ama-athomu, ama-molecule, amalanga, izinkanyezi, ama-comet, njll., ngaphandle kokuzwa ngaphakathi kuye noma yiluphi ushintsho olukhulu.

Uhlobo lolwazi oluguqula umuntu ngaphakathi, alusoze lwatholakala ngokubuka kwangaphandle.

Ulwazi lweqiniso olungadala ngempela ushintsho olukhulu lwangaphakathi kithi lusekelwe ekuzibukeni ngqo komuntu siqu.

Kuyadingeka ngokushesha ukutshela abafundi bethu be-Gnostic ukuthi bazibheke bona siqu futhi nokuthi kufanele bazibheke ngaziphi izindlela kanye nezizathu zalokho.

Ukubuka kuyindlela yokushintsha izimo zemishini zomhlaba. Ukuzibuka kwangaphakathi kuyindlela yokushintsha ngaphakathi.

Njengoba kulandela noma njengomphumela wakho konke lokhu, singakwazi futhi kufanele siqinisekise ngendlela egcizelelayo, ukuthi kunezinhlobo ezimbili zolwazi, lwangaphandle nolwangaphakathi futhi ngaphandle kokuthi sinaso kithi isikhungo se-magnetic esingakwazi ukuhlukanisa izinga lolwazi, lokhu kuhlanganiswa kwamazinga amabili noma ukuhleleka kwemibono kungasiholela ekudidekeni.

Izimfundiso ezinhle eziyimfihlakalo ezingamanga ezinomlando oqinile wesayensi, zingaphansi kwalokho okungabonwa, nokho zamukelwa abantu abaningi abafisa ukuthola ulwazi lwangaphakathi.

Ngakho-ke sizithola siphambi kwemihlaba emibili, yangaphandle neyangaphakathi. Owokuqala walokhu ubonwa yizinzwa zokubona zangaphandle; owesibili ungabonwa kuphela ngomqondo wokuzibuka ngaphakathi.

Imicabango, imibono, imizwelo, izifiso, amathemba, ukudumazeka, njll., kungaphakathi, akubonakali ezinzweni ezivamile, ezivamile futhi nokho kuyiqiniso kithi kunetafula lokudlela noma izihlalo zegumbi lokuhlala.

Impela siphila kakhulu emhlabeni wethu wangaphakathi kunowangaphandle; lokhu akunakuphikwa, akunakuphikiswana.

Emhlabeni wethu Wangaphakathi, emhlabeni wethu oyimfihlo, siyathanda, siyafisa, siyasola, siyabusisa, siyaqalekisa, siyalangazelela, siyahlupheka, siyajabula, siyakhungatheka, siyaklonyeliswa, njll., njll., njll.

Ngokungangabazeki imihlaba emibili yangaphakathi neyangaphandle ingaqinisekiswa ngokuhlangenwe nakho. Umhlaba wangaphandle yilokho okungabonwa. Umhlaba wangaphakathi yilokho okuzibukeka kumuntu siqu futhi ngaphakathi kwakhe, lapha nanjengamanje.

Noma ubani ofuna ngempela ukwazi "Imihlaba Yangaphakathi" yeplanethi yoMhlaba noma yoHlelo Lwelanga noma woMthala esiphila kuwo, kufanele azi kuqala umhlaba wakhe osondelene, impilo yakhe yangaphakathi, ethize, "Imihlaba yakhe Yangaphakathi".

"Muntu, zazi wena futhi uzokwazi iNdawo Yonke kanye noNkulunkulu".

Lapho umuntu ehlola kakhulu le "Mhlaba Wangaphakathi" obizwa ngokuthi "Umuntu Siqu", lapho eqonda kakhulu ukuthi uphila ngesikhathi esisodwa emihlabeni emibili, emaqinisweni amabili, ezindaweni ezimbili, zangaphandle nezangaphakathi.

Ngendlela efanayo nokuthi umuntu udinga ukufunda ukuhamba "ezweni elingaphandle", ukuze angaweli emgodini, angaduki emigwaqweni yedolobha, akhethe ubungane bakhe, angazihlanganisi nabantu ababi, angadli ubuthi, njll., ngaleyo ndlela futhi ngomsebenzi wengqondo wokusebenzela wena, sifunda ukuhamba "eMhlabeni Wangaphakathi" ongahlolwa ngokuzibuka.

Empeleni umqondo wokuzibuka womuntu siqu ukhubazekile ohlotsheni lwabantu olonakele lwalenkathi emnyama esiphila kuyo.

Njengoba siqhubeka ngokuzimisela ekuzibukeni, umqondo wokuzibuka osondelene uzothuthuka kancane kancane.
