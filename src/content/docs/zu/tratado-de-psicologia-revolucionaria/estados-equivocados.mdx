Ngokungangabazeki ekubukeni ngokuqinile koMina, kuhlala njalo kubalulekile futhi kudingeka ukuthi kwenziwe umehluko ophelele onengqondo maqondana nezenzakalo zangaphandle zempilo yansuku zonke kanye nezimo zangaphakathi zokuqwashisa.

Sidinga ngokushesha ukwazi lapho sizithola sikhona ngesikhathi esithile, kokubili maqondana nesimo sangaphakathi sokuqwashisa kanye nemvelo eqondile yesenzakalo sangaphandle esisehlelayo. Impilo ngokwayo iwuchungechunge lwezigameko ezicutshungulwa ngesikhathi nendawo...

Othile wake wathi: "Impilo iwuchungechunge lokuhlushwa umuntu aluphethe lusontekile Emphefumulweni..." Wonke umuntu ukhululekile ukucabanga ngendlela afuna ngayo; ngikholelwa ukuthi injabulo yesikhashana esidlulayo ihlale ilandelwa ukudumazeka nobuhlungu... Isenzakalo ngasinye sinokunambitheka kwaso okukhethekile futhi izimo zangaphakathi ngokwazo zihlukile; lokhu akunakuphikwa, akunakuphikiswana...

Impela umsebenzi wangaphakathi kuye uqondiswe ngokuqinile ezimweni ezahlukene zengqondo zokuqwashisa... Akekho ongaphika ukuthi ngaphakathi kithi sithwele amaphutha amaningi nokuthi kunezimo ezingalungile... Uma sifuna ngempela ukushintsha, sidinga ngokushesha nangokubaluleke kakhulu ukuguqula ngokuphelele lezo zimo ezingalungile zokuqwashisa...

Impela kukhona izenzakalo ezinhle nezimbi; eminye imicimbi izoba ngcono noma ibe yimbi kuneminye... Ukushintsha imicimbi ethile kungenzeka; ukuguqula imiphumela, ukuguqula izimo, njll., impela kungaphakathi kwenani lamathuba.

Nokho kukhona izimo eziyiqiniso ezingenakuguqulwa ngempela; kulezi zimo zokugcina kufanele zamukelwe ngokuqaphela, nakuba ezinye zingaba yingozi kakhulu futhi zibuhlungu... Ngokungangabazeki ubuhlungu buyanyamalala uma singazihlanganisi nenkinga evelile...
