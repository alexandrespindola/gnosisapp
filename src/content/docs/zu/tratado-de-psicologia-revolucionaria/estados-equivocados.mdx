Ngesinye isikhathi ngokuqinisekile, ekuzihloleni ngokuqinile, kuhlale kudingeka futhi kungagwemeki ukwenza umehluko ophelele onengqondo maqondana nezenzakalo zangaphandle zokuphila okusebenzayo kanye nezimo zangaphakathi zokuqwashisa.

Sidinga ngokushesha ukwazi lapho sitholakala khona ngesikhathi esithile, kokubili maqondana nesimo sangaphakathi sokuqwashisa, kanye nemvelo ethize yesehlakalo sangaphandle esisehlelayo.

Impilo ngokwayo iwuchungechunge lwezenzakalo eziqhutshwa ngokuhamba kwesikhathi nesikhala...

Omunye wake wathi: "Impilo iyiketanga, lezinhlupheko umuntu azisonge eMoyeni..."

Wonke umuntu ukhululekile ukucabanga ngendlela afuna ngayo; ngikholelwa ukuthi izinjabulo zesikhashana nje zeso, zihlala zilandelwa ukudumazeka nobuhlungu...

Sonke isehlakalo sinokunambitheka kwaso okukhethekile, futhi izimo zangaphakathi zinezinhlobo ezahlukene; lokhu akunakuphikwa, akunakuphikwa...

Ngokuqinisekile umsebenzi wangaphakathi kuye uqondiswe ngokuqinile ezimweni ezahlukahlukene zengqondo zokuqwashisa...

Akekho ongangabaza ukuthi ngaphakathi kwethu sithwele amaphutha amaningi nokuthi kukhona izimo ezingalungile...

Uma sifuna ngempela ukushintsha, sidinga ngokushesha futhi ngokuphuthuma okukhulu, ukuguqula ngokuphelele lezo zimo ezingalungile zokuqwashisa...

Ukuguqulwa okuphelele kwezimo ezingalungile, kuqala izinguquko eziphelele emkhakheni wokuphila okusebenzayo...

Uma umuntu esebenza ngokuzimisela ezimweni ezingalungile, ngokusobala izenzakalo ezingathandeki zokuphila, ngeke zisakwazi ukumlimalaza kalula...

Sisho okuthile okungaqondakala kuphela ngokukuzwa, ngokukuzwa ngempela emkhakheni wamaqiniso...

Lowo ongazisebenzeli uhlala eyisisulu sezimo; unjengokhuni oluyize emanzini anesiphepho olwandle...

Izenzakalo ziyashintsha njalo ekuhlanganisweni kwazo okuningi; ziza zilandelana ngamaza, ziyimibono...

Ngokuqinisekile kukhona izenzakalo ezinhle nezimbi; eminye imicimbi izoba ngcono noma ibe yimbi kakhulu kuneminye...

Ukuguqula imicimbi ethile kungenzeka; Ukushintsha imiphumela, ukuguqula izimo, njll., ngokuqinisekile kungaphakathi kwenani lamathuba.

Nokho kunezimo eziqinisekile ezingenakuguqulwa ngempela; kulezi zimo zokugcina kufanele zamukelwe ngokuqaphela, nakuba ezinye ziphethe izingozi ezinkulu ngisho nobuhlungu...

Ngesinye isikhathi ngokuqinisekile ubuhlungu buyanyamalala uma singazihlanganisi nenkinga evelile...

Kufanele sibheke impilo njengochungechunge olulandelanayo lwezimo zangaphakathi; umlando oyiqiniso wempilo yethu ikakhulukazi wakhiwa yizo zonke lezo zimo...

Lapho sibukeza konke ukuphila kwethu, singaziqinisekisa ngokwethu ngqo, ukuthi izimo eziningi ezingathandeki zenzeka ngenxa yezimo ezingaphakathi ezingalungile...

U-Alexander the Great nakuba ayelokhu elinganiselwe ngokwemvelo, wanikela ngokuziqhenya ezinqobeni ezambangela ukufa...

UFrancis I washona ngenxa yokuphinga okungcolile nokunyanyekayo, umlando osakhunjulwa kahle...

Lapho uMarat ebulawa ngundela omubi, wayefa ngokuzidla nomona, wayezicabanga ukuthi ulungile ngokuphelele...

Izintokazi ze-Park of the Servants ngokuqinisekile zakuqeda ngokuphelele amandla okubalulekile omfeba osabekayo obizwa ngokuthi u-LUIS XV.

Baningi abantu abafela ukuhaha, intukuthelo noma umona, izazi zengqondo ziyakwazi lokhu kahle...

Uma nje intando yethu iqinisekiswa ngokungenakuhoxiswa ekuthambekeni okungenangqondo, siba ngabazongenela ipanteon noma ithuna...

U-Otelo ngenxa yomona waba yisibulali futhi ijele ligcwele abanamaphutha abaqotho...
