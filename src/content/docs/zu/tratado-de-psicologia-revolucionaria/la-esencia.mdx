Okwenza ingane esanda kuzalwa ibe yinhle futhi ithandeke yisiqu sayo; lesi siqu ngokwaso siqukethe iqiniso layo langempela...

Ukukhula okuvamile kwesiqu kuzo zonke izidalwa, ngokuqinisekile kusasele kancane, kusaqala...

Umzimba womuntu uyakhula futhi uyathuthuka ngokuvumelana nemithetho yebhayoloji yohlobo, nokho amathuba anjalo ngokwawo anomkhawulo omkhulu esiqwini...

Ngingabaza ukuthi isiqu singazikhulisa sodwa ngaphandle kosizo, ngezinga elincane kakhulu...

Uma sikhuluma ngokusobala nangokuqondile, singasho ukuthi ukukhula okuzenzakalelayo nokwemvelo kwesiqu, kungenzeka kuphela phakathi neminyaka emithathu, emine, nemihlanu yobudala, okungukuthi, esigabeni sokuqala sokuphila...

Abantu bacabanga ukuthi ukukhula nokuthuthuka kwesiqu kuhlala kwenzeka ngokuqhubekayo, ngokuhambisana nemishini yokuziphendukela kwemvelo, kepha i-Gnosticism Yomhlaba Wonke ifundisa ngokucacile ukuthi lokhu akwenzeki kanjalo...

Ukuze isiqu sikhule kakhulu, kukhona into ekhethekile okumele yenzeke, kukhona into entsha okumele yenziwe.

Ngifuna ukugcizelela umsebenzi umuntu azisebenzela wona. Ukuthuthuka kwesiqu kungenzeka kuphela ngemisebenzi eqaphelayo nobuhlungu bokuzithandela...

Kuyadingeka ukuqonda ukuthi le misebenzi ayikhulumi ngezindaba zomsebenzi, amabhange, ubukhaliphi, ukwakha, ukulungisa izitimela noma izindaba zasehhovisi...

Lo msebenzi ngowomuntu wonke othole ubuntu; kumayelana nento engokwengqondo...

Sonke siyazi ukuthi sinakho ngaphakathi kwethu lokho okubizwa ngokuthi i-EGO, MINA, NGIZIMISE, OKWAMI...

Ngeshwa, isiqu sivalelwe, sibhajwe, phakathi kwe-EGO futhi lokhu kuyadabukisa.

Ukuncibilikisa i-YO Engokwengqondo, ukuqaqa izinto zayo ezingafuneki, kuyadingeka ngokushesha, akunakuhlehliswa, akunakuhlehliswa... injalo incazelo yomsebenzi umuntu azisebenzela wona.

Ngeke neze sikwazi ukukhulula isiqu ngaphandle kokuhlakaza i-YO Engokwengqondo kuqala...

Esiqwini kukhona Inkolo, i-BUDDHA, Ukuhlakanipha, izinhlayiya zobuhlungu bukaBaba wethu osemazulwini nayo yonke imininingwane esiyidingayo YOKUZIQAPHELA NGAPHAKATHI KOBUKHONA.

Akekho ongakuqeda i-YO Engokwengqondo ngaphandle kokususa kuqala izinto ezingenabuntu esizithwele ngaphakathi...

Sidinga ukunciphisa ubugovu obubi balezi zikhathi bube umlotha: umona ngeshwa osekuyinsika eyimfihlo yesenzo; ukuhaha okungenakubekezelelwa okwenze impilo yaba buhlungu kangaka: ukunyundela okunyantisayo; inkohliso edala izinhlekelele eziningi kangaka; ubudakwa; inkanuko engcolile enephunga elibi kangaka; njll., njll., njll.

Njengoba zonke lezo zinto ezinengekayo zincishiswa zibe uthuli lwe-cosmic, isiqu ngaphezu kokukhululeka, sizokhula futhi sithuthuke ngokuvumelana...

Ngokungangabazeki lapho i-YO Engokwengqondo ifile, isiqu siyakhanya ngaphakathi kwethu...

Isiqu esikhululekile sisinika ubuhle obungaphakathi; injabulo ephelele nothando lweqiniso kuphuma kulobo buhle...

Isiqu sinemizwa eminingi yokuphelela namandla emvelo angavamile...

Lapho "Sifa Kithi Uqobo", lapho sichitha i-YO Engokwengqondo, sijabulela imizwa namandla ayigugu esiqu...
