Yilokho okwenza umntwana osanda kuzalwa abe muhle futhi athandeke, iNhlansi yakhe; lokhu ngokwako kwakha iqiniso lakhe langempela... Ukukhula okujwayelekile kweNhlansi kuzo zonke izidalwa, impela kuncane kakhulu, kusaqala...

Umzimba womuntu uyakhula futhi uthuthuke ngokwemithetho yebhayoloji yohlobo, nokho amathuba anjalo ngokwawo anomkhawulo omkhulu ngeNhlansi... Ngokungangabazeki iNhlansi ingakhula yodwa ngaphandle kosizo, ngezinga elincane kakhulu...

Uma sikhuluma ngokuqondile futhi ngaphandle kokugwegwesa, sizothi ukukhula okuzenzakalelayo nokwemvelo kweNhlansi, kungenzeka kuphela phakathi neminyaka emithathu, emine, nemihlanu yokuqala yobudala, okungukuthi, esigabeni sokuqala sokuphila... Abantu bacabanga ukuthi ukukhula nokuthuthuka kweNhlansi kwenzeka ngaso sonke isikhathi ngokuqhubekayo, ngokwemechanic yokuziphendukela kwemvelo, kodwa i-Gnosticism Universal ifundisa ngokucacile ukuthi lokhu akwenzeki ngaleyo ndlela...

Ukuze iNhlansi ikhule kakhulu, kukhona okukhethekile okumele kwenzeke, kukhona okusha okumele kwenziwe. Ngifuna ukukhuluma ngokuqinile ngomsebenzi umuntu awenza kuye ngokwakhe. Ukuthuthuka kweNhlansi kungenzeka kuphela ngesisekelo semisebenzi eqaphelayo nokuhlupheka kokuzithandela...

Kuyadingeka ukuqonda ukuthi lemisebenzi ayikhulumi ngezindaba zomsebenzi, amabhange, ubukhenketha, ukwakha, ukulungiswa kwezitimela noma izindaba zasehhovisi... Lomsebenzi ungowomuntu wonke osevele wakha ubuntu; kumayelana nokuthile okungokwengqondo...

Sonke siyazi ukuthi sinakho ngaphakathi kwethu lokho okubizwa ngokuthi i-EGO, MINA, MINA NGOKWAMI, MINA... Ngeshwa iNhlansi itholakala ivaliwe, iboshiwe, phakathi kwe-EGO futhi lokhu kuyadabukisa. Ukuchitha i-YO Psychológica, ukuhlakaza izakhi zayo ezingafuneki, kuphuthuma, akukwazi ukuhlehliswa, akukwazi ukuhlehliswa... kunjalo-ke incazelo yomsebenzi umuntu awenza kuye ngokwakhe. Ngeke sikwazi ukukhulula iNhlansi ngaphandle kokuhlakaza ngaphambilini i-YO Psychológica...

ENhlansini kukhona iNkolo, i-BUDDHA, ukuhlakanipha, izinhlayiya zobuhlungu bukaBaba wethu oseMazulwini kanye nayo yonke imininingwane esiyidingayo ukuze SIZIFEZE NGOKUJULILE njengoMuntu. Akekho ongaqeda i-YO Psychológica ngaphandle kokususa ngaphambilini izakhi ezinonya esizithwele ngaphakathi...

Sidinga ukushisa sibe umlotha isihluku esesabekayo salezi zikhathi: umona obusuyinto ebalulekile eyenza abantu benze izinto; ukuhaha okungenakubekezelelwa okwenze impilo yaba buhlungu kangaka: ukuhleba okwenyanyekayo; inkulumo yamanga ebangela izinhlekelele eziningi kangaka; ubudakwa; inkanuko engcolile enuka kabi kangaka; njll, njll, njll.

Njengoba zonke lezo zizonzo ziqala ukuncishiswa zibe yimpushana yendawo yonke, iNhlansi ngaphezu kokukhululeka, izokhula futhi ithuthuke ngokuvumelana... Ngokungangabazeki lapho i-YO Psychológica isifile, iNhlansi iyakhanya kithi...

INhlansi ekhululekile isinikeza ubuhle obujulile; kulobu buhle kuphuma injabulo ephelele kanye Nothando lweqiniso... INhlansi inemizwa eminingi yokuphelela namandla emvelo angavamile... Lapho "Sifa kithi ngokwethu", lapho sichitha i-YO Psychológica, sijabulela imizwa namandla ayigugu eNhlansi...
