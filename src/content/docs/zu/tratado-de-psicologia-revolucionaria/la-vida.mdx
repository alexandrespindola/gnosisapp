Emkhakheni wempilo yangempela, sihlala sithola umehluko osimangazayo. Abantu abacebile abanezindawo zokuhlala ezinhle kakhulu nabangani abaningi, kwesinye isikhathi bahlupheka kakhulu... Izisebenzi eziphansi eziyisebenza kanzima noma abantu besigaba esimaphakathi, ngokuvamile baphila ngenjabulo ephelele.

Izigidigidi eziningi zikhungethwe ukungabi namandla ngokocansi, futhi abesifazane abacebile balilela ukungathembeki komyeni... Abacebile bomhlaba babukeka njengezinkozi phakathi kwezindlu zegolide, ngalezi zikhathi abakwazi ukuphila ngaphandle "kwabalindi bomzimba"... Izikhulu zombuso zihudula amaketanga, azikhululeki neze, zizungezwe ngabantu abahlomile baze bayodonsa.

Asiwuhlolisise lesi simo ngokucophelela. Sidinga ukwazi ukuthi yini impilo. Wonke umuntu ukhululekile ukuba nombono ngendlela afuna ngayo... Noma ngabe bathini, ngokuqinisekile akekho owaziyo lutho, impilo iyinkinga okungekho noyedwa oyiqondayo...

Lapho abantu befuna ukusixoxela mahhala indaba yempilo yabo, bacaphuna izehlakalo, amagama nezibongo, izinsuku, njll., futhi bazizwa benelisekile ngokwenza izindaba zabo... Laba bantu abampofu abazi ukuthi izindaba zabo aziphelele ngoba izehlakalo, amagama nezinsuku, kuyingaphandle nje kuphela yefilimu, kulahlekile ingaphakathi...

Kuphuthuma ukwazi "izimo zokuqaphela", isehlakalo ngasinye sihambisana naleso naleso simo somzwelo. Izimo zingaphakathi futhi izehlakalo zingaphandle, izehlakalo zangaphandle azizona zonke...

Iziqondiswa njengezimo zangaphakathi ukuthambekela okuhle noma okubi, ukukhathazeka, ukucindezeleka, inkolelo-ze, ukwesaba, ukusola, isihawu, ukuzicabangela, ukuzilinganisela ngokweqile; izimo zokuzizwa ujabule, izimo zenjabulo, njll., njll., njll.

Ngokungangabazeki izimo zangaphakathi zingahambisana ncamashi nezehlakalo zangaphandle noma zidalwe yizona, noma zingabi nakho nhlobo ukuhlobana nazo... Kunoma yikuphi, izimo nezehlakalo zihlukile. Akuzona zonke izikhathi izenzakalo ezihambisana ncamashi nezimo ezihambisanayo.

Isimo sangaphakathi sesehlakalo esijabulisayo singase singahambisani naso. Isimo sangaphakathi sesehlakalo esingathandeki singase singahambisani naso. Izehlakalo ebezilindelwe isikhathi eside, lapho zifika sazizwa sengathi kukhona okushodayo...

Impela bekushoda isimo sangaphakathi esifanele okwakufanele sihlanganiswe nesehlakalo sangaphandle... Izikhathi eziningi isehlakalo esasingalindelwe siba yileso esisihlinzeke ngezikhathi ezinhle kakhulu...
