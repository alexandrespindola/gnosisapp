Kodwa-ke, ngeshwa abantu uma bebheka izimpilo zabo, bacabanga ukuthi zona ngokwazo zakhiwe kuphela izenzakalo zangaphandle...

Maye abantu! bacabanga ukuthi ukube kwakungazange kwenzeke lesi noma lesiya senzakalo, impilo yabo ibingaba ngcono...

Bacabanga ukuthi inhlanhla yabahlangabeza nokuthi balahlekelwa yithuba lokujabula...

Impela akunandaba ukuthi zizinhle kangakanani izenzakalo zangaphandle zempilo, uma singekho kulezo zikhathi esimweni sangaphakathi esifanele, izenzakalo ezinhle kakhulu zingasibonakala ziyisicefe, zicasula noma zimane nje ziyisidina...

Umuntu ulilindele ngabomvu idili lomshado, yisenzakalo, kodwa kungenzeka ukuthi umuntu ukhathazeke kakhulu ngesikhathi sangempela somcimbi, kangangokuthi angajabuleli lutho kuwo futhi konke lokho kuphenduke kube yize futhi kubande njengomthetho olandelwayo...

Okuhlangenwe nakho kusifundise ukuthi akubona bonke abantu abaya edilini noma emdansweni abajabula ngempela...

Bambalwa kakhulu abantu abakwazi ukuhlanganisa ngokwethembeka isenzakalo sangaphandle nesimo sangaphakathi esifanele...

Kuyadabukisa ukuthi abantu abakwazi ukuphila ngokuqaphela: bayakhala lapho kufanele bahleke futhi bayahleka lapho kufanele bakhale...
