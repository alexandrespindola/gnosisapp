Eminyakeni engama-25 edlule ngoKhisimusi ka-1951 uMfundisi wasitshela lapha edolobheni laseCiénaga futhi kamuva wakuchaza eSigijimini sikaKhisimusi sika-1962, okulandelayo: "Ngisohlangothini lwenu kuze kube yilapho senakhe uKristu Enhliziyweni yenu".

Emahlombe akhe kunomthwalo wemfanelo wabantu base-Aquarius futhi imfundiso yoThando iyanda ngolwazi lwe-Gnóstico, uma ufuna ukulandela imfundiso yoThando, kufanele uyeke ukuzonda, ngisho nokubonakaliswa kwayo okuncane kakhulu, lokhu kusilungiselela ukuthi kuvele ingane yegolide, ingane ye-alchemy, indodana yobumsulwa, uKristu Wangaphakathi ophilayo futhi oshaya ekujuleni kwamandla ethu Okudala. Ngakho sifinyelela ekufeni kwamabutho obumina bukaSathane esiwagcina ngaphakathi futhi sizilungiselela ukuvuswa, ushintsho oluphelele.

Le Mfundiso Engcwele ayiqondwa abantu balenkathi, kodwa kufanele silwele bona ekukhulekeleni zonke izinkolo, ukuze bafisele impilo ephakeme, eqondiswa izidalwa eziphakeme, le nhlanganisela yemfundiso isibuyisela emfundisweni kaKristu Wangaphakathi, lapho siyisebenzisa sizoshintsha ikusasa lesintu.
