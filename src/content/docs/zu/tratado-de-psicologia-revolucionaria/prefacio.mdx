Umqondo wokuzibuka ngaphakathi njengoba uqhubeka uthuthuka uzosivumela ukubona ukuqhubeka okuqhubekayo komsebenzi wethu.

Eminyakeni engama-25 edlule ngoKhisimusi ka-1951 iNkosana yasitshela lapha edolobheni laseCiénaga futhi kamuva iyachaza KuMlayezo KaKhisimusi ka-1962, okulandelayo: "Ngingakini kuze kube yilapho senakhe uKristu Enhliziyweni yenu".

Emahlombe abo kukhona umthwalo wemfanelo wabantu base-Aquarius kanye nemfundiso Yothando iyanda ngolwazi lwe-Gnostic, uma ufuna ukulandela imfundiso Yothando, kufanele uyeke ukuzonda, ngisho nasembonisweni walo omncane, lokhu kusilungiselela ukuze kuvele ingane yegolide, ingane ye-alchemy, indodana yobumsulwa, uKristu Wangaphakathi ophilayo nowamukelayo ekujuleni kwamandla ethu Okudala.
