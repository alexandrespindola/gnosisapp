Izisekelo zobusha zitholakala ekhaya, esikoleni nasemgwaqweni. Intsha eyakhelwe phezu kwesisekelo SEMFUNDO EYISISEKELO empeleni IYAKHA futhi ngokuyisisekelo IYAHLONIPHA.

Intsha eyakhelwe phezu kwezisekelo ezingamanga ngumphumela onengqondo wendlela engalungile.

Iningi lamadoda lisebenzisa ingxenye yokuqala yempilo yabo ekwenzeni ingxenye esele yayo ibe lusizi.

Ukweqisa kobusha kuyizincwadi ezikhishwa ngesikhathi sokuguga ezikhokhwa ngenzalo ebiza kakhulu eminyakeni engamashumi amathathu.

Ngaphandle KWEZEMFUNDO EYISISEKELO ubutsha buba ukudakwa okuqhubekayo: umkhuhlane wephutha, utshwala nesifiso sezilwane.

Konke umuntu okumele abe yikho empilweni yakhe kutholakala esimweni esingaba khona phakathi neminyaka yokuqala engamashumi amathathu ekhona.

Kuzo zonke izenzo ezinhle zabantu esinalo ulwazi ngazo, kokubili ezikhathini ezedlule nakwesethu, iningi lazo liqalwe ngaphambi kweminyaka engamashumi amathathu.

Indoda esifinyelele eminyakeni engamashumi amathathu ngezinye izikhathi izizwa sengathi iphuma empini enkulu lapho ibone khona abangani abaningi bewela phansi ngemva komunye.

Lapho befika eminyakeni engamashumi amathathu amadoda nabesifazane sebelahlekelwe yikho konke ukuphila kwabo nomdlandla futhi uma behluleka emabhizinisini abo okuqala, bagcwaliswa yithemba lokuthi ngeke baphumelele futhi bayayeka umdlalo.

Imibono yamanga yokuvuthwa ilandela imibono yamanga yobutsha. Ngaphandle Kwemfundo Eyisisekelo ifa lokuguga livame ukuba ukungabi nathemba.

Ubutsha buyadlula. Ubuhle yinkazimulo yobutsha, kodwa buyinkohliso, abuhlali.

Ubuswa yilangabi elishisayo lehlobo elishesha ukushona. Intsha ithanda ukuchitha izinto ezibalulekile ekuphileni kobusha.

Kukhona isikhathi sakho konke empilweni. Ukuthi umuntu osemusha uyashada kuyinto evamile, kodwa ukuthi ixhegu liyashada yinto ewubuwula.

Empeleni siye sakwazi ukubonisa ngemva kweminyaka eminingi yokubheka, ukuthi yonke iqhawe elingashadile liphenduka umyeni onomona.

Kuyadingeka ukuthi ungaphambanisi UTHANDO NENKANUKO. Izithandani ezisencane namantombazane amancane, azikwazi ukuhlukanisa phakathi kothando nenkanuko.

Miningi imibhangqwana ehlulekayo emshadweni ngenxa yokungahambisani kwezimilo.

Owesifazane oshada nensizwa enomona, ethukuthele, enolaka, uzoba yisisulu somkhohlisi.

Insizwa eshada nowesifazane onomona, onolaka, othukuthele, kusobala ukuthi kuzodingeka ichithe impilo yayo esihogweni.

Ukuze kube nothando lweqiniso phakathi kwabantu ababili, kuyaphoqa ukuthi kungabi khona nkanuko yezilwane, kubalulekile ukuchitha i-EGO yomhawu, kuyadingeka ukuhlakaza intukuthelo, kubalulekile ukungabi nazithakazelo kuwo wonke ubufakazi.

I-EGO ilimaza amakhaya, MINA NGINGAZILIMAZA ngiyawonakalisa ubunye. Uma abasha namantombazane befunda iMFUNDO yethu EYISISEKELO futhi bezimisele ukuchitha i-EGO, kusobala kuzo zonke izindlela ukuthi bazokwazi ukuthola indlela YOMSHADO OPHELELE.

Ukuchitha i-EGO kuphela lapho kungaba khona injabulo yangempela emakhaya. Abantu abasha namantombazane abafuna ukujabula emshadweni sibatusa ukuthi bafunde ngokujulile iMFUNDO yethu EYISISEKELO futhi bachithe i-EGO.

Abazali abaningi bemindeni banomona ngamabintana abo ngendlela eyesabekayo futhi abafuni ukuthi abe namasoka. Isenzo esinjalo siyinto engenangqondo ngekhulu lamaphesenti ngoba amantombazane adinga ukuba namasoka futhi ashade.

Umphumela wokuntuleka okunjalo kokuqonda amasoka ngasese, emgwaqweni, ngengozi njalo yokuwela ezandleni zeqhawe eliyengayo.

Amantombazane kufanele ngaso sonke isikhathi abe nenkululeko yokuba nesoka lawo, kodwa ngenxa yokuthi abakayichithi i-EGO, kunconywa ukuthi bangashiywa bebodwa nesoka.

Abantu abasha namantombazane kufanele babe nenkululeko yokuzenzela amaphathi abo ekhaya. Iziphazamiso ezihlanzekile azilimazi muntu futhi Intsha idinga ukuba neziphazamiso.

Olimaza intsha utshwala, ugwayi, ubufebe, imikhosi yobunewunewu, ubunewunewu, izindawo zokuphuzela, amaklabhu obusuku, njll.

Amaphathi omndeni, imidanso ehloniphekile, umculo omuhle, ukuhamba ezweni, njll, akukwazi ukulimaza muntu.

Ingqondo iyalimaza uthando. Abantu abaningi abasha balahlekelwe yithuba lokushada nabesifazane abahle ngenxa yokwesaba kwabo ezomnotho, izinkumbulo zangaphambilini nezinkathazo ngekusasa.

Ukwesaba impilo, indlala, ubumpofu kanye namaphrojekthi ayize engqondo kuba yimbangela eyisisekelo yakho konke ukuhlehliswa komshado.

Baningi abasha abahlose ukungashadi kuze kube yilapho benenani elithile lemali, indlu yabo, imoto yohlobo lwakamuva nezinye izinto eziyiziwula eziyinkulungwane njengokungathi konke lokho kuyinjabulo.

Kuyadabukisa ukuthi lolo hlobo lwabesilisa luphuthelwa amathuba amahle omshado ngenxa yokwesaba impilo, ukufa, ukuthi abantu bazothini, njll.

Uhlobo olunjalo lwamadoda luhlala lungashadile impilo yalo yonke noma lishada sekwephuze kakhulu, lapho isikhathi sikhona sokukhulisa umndeni nokufundisa izingane zawo.

Empeleni konke umuntu akudingayo ukuze asekela umkakhe nezingane zakhe ukuba nomsebenzi noma umsebenzi othobekile, yilokho kuphela.

Amantombazane amaningi ahlala engashadile ngenxa yokukhetha umyeni. Abesifazane ababalayo, abanesithakazelo, abanobugovu bahlala bengashadile noma bahluleka ngokuphelele emshadweni.

Kuyadingeka ukuthi amantombazane aqonde ukuthi wonke umuntu uyadumazeka owesifazane onesithakazelo, obalayo, onobugovu.

Abanye abesifazane abasha abafisa ukudoba umyeni bazipenda ubuso ngokweqile, bayazikhipha amashiya, bayazisondeza izinwele, bagqoke izigqoko zekhanda namaqanda okuzenzisa, laba besifazane abakuqondi ukusebenza kwengqondo kwabesilisa.

Indoda ngokwemvelo iyabazonda onodoli abapendiwe futhi iyakuncoma ubuhle obungokwemvelo ngokuphelele nokumamatheka okungenacala.

Indoda ifuna ukubona kowesifazane ubuqotho, ubulula, uthando lweqiniso olungenazithakazelo, ubumsulwa bemvelo.

Amantombazane afuna ukushada adinga ukuqonda ngokujulile ukusebenza kwengqondo yobulili besilisa.
