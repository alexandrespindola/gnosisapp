UKUQALA kunezizathu eziningi, futhi esinye sazo yilokho okubizwa ngokuthi UKWESABA.

Umfanyana othobekile ogeza izicathulo zamadoda azidlayo emapaki amakhulu emadolobha, angaphenduka isela uma eqala ukwesaba ubumpofu, ukwesaba yena ngokwakhe, ukwesaba ikusasa lakhe.

Intombazane ethobekile engumthungi esebenza esitolo esikhulu somnini mandla, ingaphenduka isela noma isifebe ngobusuku obubodwa, uma iqala ukwesaba ikusasa, ukwesaba impilo, ukwesaba ukuguga, ukwesaba yona ngokwayo, njll.

Uwaiter omuhle wasendaweni yokudlela kanokusho noma ehhotela elikhulu, angaphenduka iGANSTER, abe ngumhlaseli wamabhange, noma isela elihle kakhulu, uma ngeshwa eqala ukwesaba yena ngokwakhe, ukwesaba isikhundla sakhe sokuba uwaiter, ukwesaba ikusasa lakhe, njll.

Isinambuzane esingenamsebenzi sifisa ukuba sihle. Umsebenzi ompofu owenza umsebenzi wokuthengisa obhekene namakhasimende futhi osibonisa ngesineke uthayi, ihembe, izicathulo, enza ukukhothama okuningi futhi emoyizela ngobumnene obenziwe, ufisa okungaphezu kwalokho ngoba uyesaba, uyesaba kakhulu, ukwesaba ububi, ukwesaba ikusasa lakhe eliyingozi, ukwesaba ukuguga, njll.

UKUQALA kuyizinto eziningi. UKUQALA kunobuso bomsindisi kanye nobuso bukaDeveli, ubuso bendoda kanye nobuso bowesifazane, ubuso bezithakazelo kanye nobuso bokungabi nandaba, ubuso bomuntu olungile kanye nobuso somuntu oyisoni.

Kukhona UKUQALA kulowo ofuna ukushada nakulowo MDALA ozelwe oyedwa oziphazamisayo othanda umshado.

Kukhona UKUQALA kulowo ofisa ngobuqili obungenamkhawulo "UKUBA KUNGA OKUNYE", "UKUQALA", UKUGIBELA" futhi kukhona UKUQALA kulowo ozenza ANACORETA, ongafuni lutho kuleli zwe, ngoba UKUQALA kwakhe okuwukuphela kwalo ukufinyelela IZULU, UKUKHULULEKA, njll.

Kukhona IZIDINGO ZOMHLABA kanye NEZIDINGO ZOMUYA. Ngezinye izikhathi UKUQALA kusebenzisa imaski YOKUNGENANDABA kanye NOKUZIDELA.

Lowo ongaQALI kuleli zwe elibi KANYE NOKUDABUKISA, UQALA okunye futhi lowo ongaQALI imali, UQALA AMANDLA ENGQONDO

I-YO, i-MYSELF, i-SELF ithanda ukufihla UKUQALA, ukuyifaka emagumbini ayimfihlo engqondo bese ithi: "ANGIQALI LUTHO", "NGIYABATHANDA ABANTU BAKITHI", "NGISEBENZA NGOKUNGENANDABA NGENXA YENHLALA YABO BONKE ABANTU".

USOMPOLITIKI weFOX futhi owaziyo konke, ngezinye izikhathi uyamangaza izixuku ngemisebenzi yakhe ebonakala ingenandaba, kepha uma eshiya umsebenzi, kuvamile ukuthi aphume ezweni lakubo nenqwaba yezigidi zamaRandi.

UKUQALA okufihlwe ngeMASKI YOKUNGENANDABA, kuvame ukukhohlisa abantu abahlakaniphile.

Kukhona abantu abaningi emhlabeni ABAQALA kuphela ukuthi bangabi neZIQALO.

Baningi abantu abayeka zonke izinto zokunethezeka nezokuziqhenya zomhlaba ngoba BAQALA kuphela UKUZIPHENDUKELA KWABO KOKUPHELELE.

Umuntu olahlwa owela ngamadolo ethempelini futhi ozishaya egcwele ukholo, kubonakala engakuqali lutho futhi aze azinike ukunikeza ngaphandle kokuthatha lutho kunoma ubani, kepha kusobala ukuthi UQALA ISIMANGA, ukwelashwa, impilo yakhe noma yelungu lomndeni, noma ukusindiswa kwaphakade.

Siyabahlonipha abesilisa nabesifazane abangokwenkolo ngempela, kodwa siyazisola ngokuthi abaluthandi ukholo lwabo ngokuKUNGENANDABA okuphelele.

Izinkolo ezingcwele, amahlelo amakhulu, ama-oda, izinhlangano zomoya, njll. Bafanelwe UTHANDO LWETHU OLUNGENAKUKHATHALA.

Kuyivelakancane ukuthola emhlabeni umuntu othanda inkolo yakhe, isikole sakhe, ihlelo lakhe, njll. ngokungenandaba. Lokho kuyadabukisa.

Wonke umhlaba ugcwele izifiso. UHitler waqala impi ngenxa yokufisa.

Zonke izimpi ziqala ngokwesaba kanye NOKUQALA. Zonke izinkinga ezinkulu zokuphila ziqala ngoKUQALA.

Wonke umuntu uphila emzabalazweni omelene nomuntu wonke ngenxa yokufisa, abanye bamelene nabanye futhi bonke bamelene nabo bonke.

Wonke umuntu empilweni UQALA UKUBA KUNGA OKUNYE futhi abantu abaneminyaka ethile, othisha, abazali, abaqeqeshi, njll. bakhuthaza abafana, amantombazane, izintokazi, intsha, njll. ukuthi baqhubeke nendlela esabekayo YOKUQALA.

Abantu abadala batshela abafundi ukuthi kufanele ube into ethile empilweni, ucebe, ushade nabantu abayizigidi, ube namandla, njll. njll.

Izizukulwane ezindala, ezesabekayo, ezimbi, ezigugile, zifuna ukuthi izizukulwane ezintsha nazo zifise, zibe zimbi, futhi zibe zibi njengabo.

Okubi kakhulu kukho konke lokhu ukuthi abantu abasha bazivumela ukuba "BANGANYATHE" futhi bazivumela ukuba baholelwe kuleyo ndlela esabekayo YOKUQALA.

Othisha kufanele bafundise ABAFUNDI ukuthi akukho msebenzi ohloniphekile ofanelwe ukubukelwa phansi, kuwubuwula ukubukela phansi umshayeli wetekisi, umsebenzi obhekene nekhawunta, umlimi, umgcini wezicathulo, njll.

Yonke imisebenzi ethobekile yinhle. Yonke imisebenzi ethobekile iyadingeka empilweni yomphakathi.

Akuzona zonke izidalwa zokuzalwa zabunjiniyela, ababusi, omongameli, odokotela, abameli, njll.

Ehlanganweni yomphakathi zonke izinhlobo zemisebenzi ziyadingeka, yonke imisebenzi, akukho msebenzi ohloniphekile ongake udeleleke.

Empilweni yangempela umuntu ngamunye usebenzela okuthile futhi okubalulekile ukwazi ukuthi ngamunye usebenzela ini.

Kungumsebenzi wabafundisi ukuthola UBUCWEBE bomfundi ngamunye futhi bamyalela kulowo mqondo.

Lowo osebenza empilweni ngokuvumelana noBUCWEBE bakhe, uzosebenza NGOBUQOTHO BANGEMPELA futhi ngaphandle KOKUQALA.

UTHANDO kufanele luthabathe indawo YOKUQALA. UBUCWEBE yilokho esikuthanda ngempela, yilowo msebenzi esiwenza ngenjabulo ngoba yilokho esikujabulelayo, yilokho ESITHANDAyo.

Empilweni yanamuhla ngeshwa abantu basebenza bengathandi futhi ngenxa yokufisa ngoba basebenzisa imisebenzi engahambelani nobuciko babo.

Uma umuntu esebenza ngalokho akuthandayo, ngobuciko bakhe beqiniso, ukwenza NGOBUQOTHO ngoba UYABUTHANDA ubuciko bakhe, ngoba IZIMO zakhe zokuphila yizona kanye zobuciko bakhe.

Yilowo kanye umsebenzi wabafundisi. Ukwazi ukuqondisa abafundi babo, ukuthola amakhono abo, ukubaqondisa endleleni yobuciko babo beqiniso.
