Iminyaka engamashumi amane yokuqala yokuphila isinika incwadi, amashumi amathathu alandelayo isinika incazelo.

Eminyakeni engamashumi amabili indoda iyipikoko; emashumini amathathu, ibhubesi; emashumini amane, ikamela; emashumini amahlanu, inyoka; emashumini ayisithupha, inja; emashumini ayisikhombisa, inkawu, futhi emashumini ayisishiyagalombili, izwi nesithunzi nje.

Isikhathi sembula zonke izinto: siyisilomo esithokozisayo esizikhulumelayo noma singabuzwa lutho.

Ayikho into eyenziwe yisandla SESILWANE ESINOBUNGCWELE esingabizwa ngendoda, okuyothi ngokushesha noma kamuva isikhathi singayilimazi.

"FUGIT IRRÉPARABILE TEMPUS", isikhathi esibalekayo asinakulungiswa.

Isikhathi sidalula emphakathini konke okufihliwe manje futhi sifihle konke okukhanya ngokukhazimulayo njengamanje.

Ukwaluphala kufana nothando, akunakufihlwa noma kungagqokiswa ngezingubo zobusha.

Ukwaluphala kunciphisa ukuziqhenya kwabantu futhi kubathobise, kepha okunye ukuthobeka futhi okunye ukuthotshiswa.

Lapho ukufa kusondela, asebekhulile abaphoxekile ngokuphila bathola ukuthi ukuguga akuseyona umthwalo.

Bonke abantu banethemba lokuphila isikhathi eside nokukhula, kodwa ukuguga kuyabathusa.

Ukwaluphala kuqala ngeminyaka engamashumi amahlanu nesithupha bese kuqhubeka ngezikhathi eziyisikhombisa ezisiholela ekugugeni nasekufeni.

Inhlekelele enkulu kunazo zonke zabantu abadala akukhona ukuguga, kodwa ubuwula bokungafuni ukuqaphela ukuthi bagugile nokungabi nabulungisa bokuzicabanga besebasha njengokungathi ukuguga yicala.

Into engcono kakhulu ngobudala ukuthi umuntu useduze kakhulu nomgomo.

UBUYE BENGQONDO, MINA, i-EGO, ayithuthuki ngeminyaka nangokuhlangenwe nakho; iba yinkimbinkimbi, iba nzima, iba yinkathazo, ngakho-ke isisho esivamile sithi: "UBUNGCWELE NOMUMO KUZE KUBE SEKUNGWENI".

UBUYE BENGQONDO babadala abanzima buziduduza ngokunikeza izeluleko ezinhle ngenxa yokungakwazi kwabo ukunikeza izibonelo ezimbi.

Abadala bazi kahle ukuthi ukuguga yisihluku esibi esibavimbela ngokufa, ukujabulela ubumnandi bobusha obuyize futhi bakhetha ukuziduduza ngokunikeza izeluleko ezinhle.

Mina ngifihla mina, mina ngifihla ingxenye yami futhi konke kubhalwe ngemishwana ephakeme nezeluleko ezinhle.

Ingxenye YAMI ifihla enye ingxenye YAMI. Mina ngifihla lokho okungangivumeli.

Kufakazelwe ngokuphelele ngokubheka nokuhlangenwe nakho ukuthi lapho amaphutha esishiya siyajabula ukucabanga ukuthi yithi esiwashiyile.

Inhliziyo YESILWANE ESINOBUNGCWELE ayibi ngcono ngeminyaka, kodwa iba yimbi kakhulu, njalo iba yitshe futhi uma ebusheni besinobugovu, siqamba amanga, sithukuthele, ebudadeni siyoba njalo kakhulu.

Abadala bahlala esikhathini esidlule, abadala bangumphumela wezolo eziningi, asebekhulile abasazi isikhathi esiphila kuso, abadala bayinkumbulo eqoqiwe.

Okuwukuphela kwendlela yokufinyelela ebudaleni obuphelele ukuchitha UBUYE BENGQONDO. Lapho sifunda ukufa njalo, sifinyelela ebudaleni obukhulu.

Ukwaluphala kunencazelo enkulu, yokuthula nenkululeko kulabo asebevele bachupha uMina.

Lapho izinkanuko zife ngendlela eqondile, ephelele neqinisekile, umuntu ukhululeka hhayi enkosini eyodwa, kodwa ezinkosini eziningi.

Kunzima kakhulu ukuthola ekuphileni asebekhulile abangenacala abangasenazo ngisho nezinsalela zikaMina, lolo hlobo lwabantu abadala lujabule kakhulu futhi luphila isikhashana.

Indoda emhlophe eBUHLAKANIPHENI. Umdala olwazini, inkosi yothando, empeleni uba yisibani sokukhanya esiqondisa ngokuhlakanipha ukugeleza kwamakhulu eminyaka angenakubalwa.

Emhlabeni kuye kwaba khona futhi kukhona njengamanje ABAFUNDISI ABAKHULU asebengenazo ngisho nezinsalela zokugcina zikaMina. Laba BAQONDISI be-GNÓSTIC abajwayelekile futhi bangcwele njengembali ye-lotus.

UMFUNDISI OMHLONISHWAYO OMDALA ochithe UBUYE OBUHLUKANISELEKILE ngendlela eqondile neqinisekile uyindlela ephelele yokubonakaliswa KOKUHLAKANIPHA OKUPHELELE, UTHANDO LWAPHEZULU NAMANDLA APHEZULU.

UMFUNDISI OMDALA ongasenawo uMina, empeleni uyingxenye ephelele yeSER DIVÍNAL.

Laba BADALA ABAPHAKEME, laba BAQONDISI be-GNÓSTIC bakhanyise umhlaba kusukela ezikhathini zasendulo, masikhumbule uBUDHA, uMOSHESHI, uHERMES, uRAMARKRISHNA, uDANIEL, iLAMA ENGCWELE, njll., njll., njll.

Othisha bezikole, amakolishi namanyuvesi, othisha, abazali, kufanele bafundise izizukulwane ezintsha ukuhlonipha nokuhlonipha asebekhulile.

LOKHO okungenagama, LOKHO okunguBUCAPHELI, LOKHO okuyiNGEMPELA, kunezici ezintathu: UKUHLAKANIPHA, UTHANDO, IZWI.

OKWAPHEZULU njengoBABA UKUHLAKANIPHA KOMHLABA, NJENGOBA IZULU LINGUTHANDO OLUNGAZE LUPHELE, njengoba indodana iyilo IZWI.

EBabeni womndeni kukhona uphawu lokuhlakanipha. Kumama wekhaya kukhona UTHANDO, izingane zifanekisela izwi.

UBaba osekhulile ufanelwe yiyo yonke indodana. UBaba osegugile akasakwazi ukusebenza futhi kulungile ukuthi izingane zimbheke futhi zihloniphe.

Umama othandekayo usekhulile akasakwazi ukusebenza futhi ngakho-ke kuyadingeka ukuthi amadodana namadodakazi amnakekele futhi amthande futhi enze lolo thando lube inkolo.

Lowo ongakwazi ukuthanda uBaba wakhe, lowo ongakwazi ukuthanda uMAMA wakhe, uhamba endleleni yesandla sobunxele, endleleni yephutha.

Izingane azinalo ilungelo lokwahlulela abazali bazo, akekho ophelele kulo mhlaba futhi labo esingenabo amaphutha athile ngasohlangothini olulodwa, sinabo ngakolunye, sonke sinqunywa yisikele esifanayo.

Abanye bayalidelela UTHANDO LOBABA, abanye baze bahleke UTHANDO LOBABA. Labo abaziphatha kanjalo ekuphileni abakangeni ngisho nasendleleni eholela KULOKHO okungenagama.

Indodana engabongiyo emazonda uBaba wayo futhi imkhohlwe uMama wayo empeleni iyisipoki sangempela esizonde konke OKUPHEZULU.

INGUQUKO YOBUCHWEPHESHE akusho UKUNGENANGCOBO, ukukhohlwa ubaba, ukwedelela uMama othandekayo. INGUQUKO YOBUCHWEPHESHE UKUHLAKANIPHA KOTHANDO NAMANDLA APHELELE.

KUbaba kukhona uphawu lokuhlakanipha futhi kuMama kukhona umthombo ophilayo WOTHANDO ngaphandle kwesigaba esihlanzekile okunzima ngempela ukufeza UKUQAPHELA OKUPHAKEME kakhulu.
