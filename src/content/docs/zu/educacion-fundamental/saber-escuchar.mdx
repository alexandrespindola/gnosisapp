Emhlabeni kunabakhulumi abaningi abamangaza ngobuciko babo bokukhuluma, kodwa bambalwa abantu abakwazi ukulalela.

Ukwazi ukulalela kunzima kakhulu, bambalwa ngempela abantu abakwazi ukulalela ngempela.

UMA KUKHULUMA UTHISHA, uthisha wesifazane, isikhulumi senkomfa, ihholo libonakala lilalele ngokucophelela, njengokulandela yonke imininingwane yegama ngalinye lesikhulumi, konke kunikeza umbono wokuthi bayalalela, wokuthi basesimweni sokuqapha, kodwa ekujuleni kwengqondo yomuntu ngamunye kunomabhalane ohumusha igama ngalinye lesikhulumi.

LOMABHALANE YIMI, NGIMINA, MINA. Umsebenzi kamabhalane oshiwo uwukuhumusha kabi, ukuchaza kabi amazwi esikhulumi.

IMINA liyachaza ngokwebandlululo lalo, imiqondo eyakheke ngaphambilini, ukwesaba, ukuziqhenya, ukukhathazeka, imibono, izinkumbulo, njll., njll., njll.

Abafundi esikoleni, abafundi besifazane, abantu abahlanganisiwe bakha ihholo elilalelayo, empeleni abalaleli isikhulumi, bazilalele bona, balalele i-EGO yabo, i-EGO yabo ethandekayo ye-MAQUIAVELIAN, engazimisele ukwamukela OKUYIQINISO, OKUYIQINISO, OKUBALULEKILE.

Kuphela esimweni sokuqapha OKUSHA, ngENGQONDO EZIZENZAKALELAYO ekhululekile esindweni lesikhathi esidlule, esimweni sokWAMUKELA ngokugcwele, singalalela ngempela ngaphandle kokungenelela kwalowo mabhalane omubi kakhulu obizwa nge-MINA, NGIMINA, MINA, I-EGO.

Lapho ingqondo iboshelwe yinkumbulo, iphinda kuphela lokho ekunqwabelile.

Ingqondo eboshelwe okuhlangenwe nakho kwezolo eziningi, ingabona kuphela okwamanje ngamehlo anenkungu esikhathi esidlule.

UMA SIFUNA UKWAZI UKULALELA, uma sifuna ukufunda ukulalela ukuze sithole okusha, kufanele siphile ngokuvumelana nefilosofi YOKWESIKHASHANA.

Kuphuthuma ukuphila umzuzu nomzuzu ngaphandle kokukhathazeka ngesikhathi esidlule, nangaphandle kwamaphrojekthi esikhathi esizayo.

IQINISO yinto engaziwa umzuzu nomzuzu, izingqondo zethu kufanele zihlale ziqaphile, zinake ngokugcwele, zikhululekile ekubandlululeni, imiqondo eyakheke ngaphambilini, ukuze zikwazi ngempela ukwamukela.

Othisha besilisa nabesifazane kufanele bafundise abafundi babo incazelo ejulile efihlwe kulokho kwazi ukulalela.

Kuyadingeka ukufunda ukuphila ngokuhlakanipha, ukuqinisa izinzwa zethu, ukucwenga ukuziphatha kwethu, imicabango yethu, imizwa yethu.

Akusizi ngalutho ukuba nesiko elikhulu lezemfundo, uma singazi ukulalela, uma singakwazi ukuthola okusha umzuzu nomzuzu.

Sidinga ukucwenga ukunaka, ukucwenga ukuziphatha kwethu, ukucwenga abantu bethu, izinto, njll., njll., njll.

Akunakwenzeka ukucwenga ngempela lapho singazi ukulalela.

Izingqondo ezigwegwe, ezinzima, ezonakele, ezishwabene azikaze zazi ukulalela, azikaze zazi ukuthola okusha, lezo zingqondo ziqonda kuphela, ziqonda kuphela ngendlela eyiphutha ukuhunyushwa okungenangqondo kwalowo mabhalane oyisimungulu obizwa nge-MINA, NGIMINA, I-EGO.

Ukucwenga kuyinto enzima kakhulu futhi kudinga ukunaka okugcwele. Umuntu angaba umuntu ocwengeke kakhulu kumafashini, amasudi, izingubo, izingadi, izimoto, ubungane, kodwa nokho aqhubeke esezintweni eziyimfihlo abe lukhuni, abe yigwegwe, abe nzima.

Lowo owazi ukuphila umzuzu nomzuzu, uhamba ngempela endleleni yokucwenga kweqiniso.

Lowo onengqondo eyamukelayo, ezizenzakalelayo, ephelele, eqaphile, uhamba endleleni yokucwenga okuyiqiniso.

Lowo ovulekele konke okusha elahla isisindo sesikhathi esidlule, imiqondo eyakheke ngaphambilini, ubandlululo, ukungathembeki, ukushisekela, njll., uhamba ngokunqoba endleleni yokucwenga okusemthethweni.

Ingqondo eshwabene iphila ibotshwe esikhathini esidlule, emiqondweni eyakheke ngaphambilini, ukuziqhenya, ukuzithanda, ubandlululo, njll., njll.

Ingqondo eshwabene ayikwazi ukubona okusha, ayikwazi ukulalela, iboshelwe UKUZITHANDA.

Abashisekeli be-MARXISM-LENINISM abamukeli okusha; abavumi ISICI SESINE sazo zonke izinto, ISILINGANISO SESINE, ngenxa yokuzithanda, bayazithanda kakhulu, babambelela kumathiyori abo ayize ayize futhi Lapho sibabeka endaweni yeqiniso, lapho sibabonisa ukungabi nangqondo kwezindaba zabo zobuqili, baphakamisa ingalo yabo yesobunxele, babheka izandla zewashi labo, banikeze izaba ezigwemayo bese behamba.

Lezo yizingqondo ezishwabene, izingqondo ezindala ezingazi ukulalela, ezingazi ukuthola okusha, ezingayamukeli iqiniso ngoba zibotshwe EKUZITHANDENI. Izingqondo ezizithanda kakhulu, izingqondo ezingazi NGOKUCWENGA KWEZAMASIKO, izingqondo eziyigwegwe, izingqondo ezinzima, ezilalela kuphela i-EGO yazo ezithandekayo.

IMFUNDO EYISISEKELO ifundisa ukulalela, ifundisa ukuphila ngokuhlakanipha.

Othisha besilisa nabesifazane bezikole, amakolishi, amanyuvesi kufanele bafundise abafundi babo indlela eyiqiniso yokucwenga impilo kweqiniso.

Akusizi ngalutho ukuhlala iminyaka eyishumi nanhlanu sifakwe ezikoleni, amakolishi namanyuvesi, uma siphuma ngaphakathi siyizingulube zangempela emicabangweni yethu, imibono yethu, imizwa yethu nemikhuba yethu.

IMFUNDO EYISISEKELO iyadingeka ngokuphuthuma ngoba izizukulwane ezintsha zisho ukuqala kwenkathi entsha.

Isikhathi se-REVOLUTION YE-IQINISO sesifikile, umzuzu we-REVOLUTION EYISISEKELO sesifikile.

Isikhathi esidlule siyisikhathi esidlule futhi sesithele izithelo. Sidinga ukuqonda incazelo ejulile yomzuzu esiphila kuwo.
