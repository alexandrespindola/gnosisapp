INDODA YOMSHINI yisilwane esingenamingcele kulesi sigodi sezinyembezi, kodwa inayo, i-PRETENSIO ngisho ne-INSOLENCIA YOKUZIQOKELA njengeNKOSI YEMVELO.

"NOCE TE IPSUN" "MDODA ZAZI WENA". Lesi isaga sasendulo seGOLIDE esilotshwe ezindongeni ezingenakunqotshwa zethempeli laseDelphi eGRECIA YASENDULO.

Indoda, leso SILWANE esihlakaniphile esizibiza ngokungeyikho ngokuthi YINDODA, iqambe izinkulungwane zemishini eyinkimbinkimbi nenzima futhi yazi kahle ukuthi ukuze ikwazi ukusebenzisa UMSHINI, kwesinye isikhathi idinga iminyaka emide yokufunda nokufundiswa, kodwa uma kukhulunywa NGAYO, isikhohlwa ngokuphelele yilesi siqiniso, nakuba yona ngokwayo iwumshini oyinkimbinkimbi kakhulu kunayo yonke eyiqambile.

Akekho umuntu ongenayo imibono engamanga nhlobo ngaye, okubi nakakhulu ukuthi akafuni ukuqaphela ukuthi empeleni uwumshini.

Umshini womuntu awunayo inkululeko yokuhamba, usebenza kuphela ngamathonya amaningi ahlukahlukene angaphakathi nokushayisana kwangaphandle.

Yonke iminyakazo, izenzo, amazwi, imibono, imizwelo, imizwa, izifiso, zomshini womuntu zibangelwa amathonya angaphandle nangezizathu eziningi zangaphakathi ezingavamile nezinzima.

ISILWANE ESIHLAKANIPHILE yisidudla esimpofu esikhulumayo ngenkumbulo nokuphila, unodoli ophilayo, onombono oyisiwula wokuthi angakwazi UKWENZA, lapho empeleni eqinisweni kungekho lutho angalwenza.

Cabanga okwesikhashana, mfundi othandekayo, unodoli womshini ozisebenzelayo olawulwa umshini oyinkimbinkimbi.

Cabanga ukuthi lowo nodoli uyaphila, uyathandana, uyakhuluma, uyahamba, uyafisa, wenza izimpi, njll.

Cabanga ukuthi lowo nodoli angashintsha abanikazi njalo. Kufanele ucabange ukuthi umnikazi ngamunye ungumuntu ohlukile, unezici zakhe, indlela yakhe yokuzijabulisa, ukuzwa, ukuphila, njll., njll., njll.

Noma yimuphi umnikazi ofuna ukuthola imali uzocindezela izinkinobho ezithile bese unodoli uzizinikezela ebhizinisini, omunye umnikazi, isigamu sehora kamuva noma amahora ambalwa kamuva, uzoba nombono ohlukile futhi abeke unodoli wakhe ukuba adanse futhi ahleke, owesithathu uzomfaka ukuba alwe, owesine uzomenza athandane nowesifazane, owesihlanu uzomenza athandane nomunye, owesithupha uzomenza alwe nomakhelwane futhi azidalele inkinga yamaphoyisa, owesikhombisa uzomenza ashintshe ikheli.

Empeleni unodoli wesibonelo sethu akazange enze lutho kodwa ukholelwa ukuthi wenze, unombono wokuthi WENZA lapho eqinisweni kungekho lutho angakwenza ngoba akanayo i-INDIVIDUAL SER.

Ngaphandle kokungabaza konke kwenzeka njengokungathi liyana, lapho kuduma, lapho ilanga lishisa, kodwa unodoli ompofu ukholelwa ukuthi WENZA; unombono oyisiwula wokuthi ukwenze konke lapho empeleni kungekho lutho akwenzile, abanikazi bakhe abazijabulise ngaye unodoli ompofu womshini.

Kunjalo isilwane esihlakaniphile esimpofu, mfundi othandekayo unodoli womshini njengalowo wesibonelo sethu esicacisayo, ukholelwa ukuthi WENZA lapho eqinisweni kungekho lutho AKWENZA uyisidudla senyama negazi elilawulwa yi-LEGION OF SUBTLE ENERGETIC ENTITIES ehlanganisa lokho okubizwa ngokuthi i-EGO, i-I PLURALIZED.

IVANGELI LOBUKRISTU ibiza zonke lezo zinhlangano ngamaDEMONI futhi igama lawo langempela yi-LEGION.

Uma sithi mina yiqembu lamaDEMONI alawula umshini womuntu, asidlisi ngendlala, kunjalo.

INDODA-UMSHINI ayinayo INDIVIDUALIDAD ayinakho i-SER, yi-SER EQINISO kuphela enamandla OKWENZA.

I-SER kuphela engasinika i-INDIVIDUALIDAD YEQINISO, yi-SER kuphela esisenza SIBE AMADODA EQINISO.

Lowo ofuna ngempela ukuyeka ukuba unodoli womshini olula, kufanele aqede ngasinye salezo zinhlangano ezihlanganisa i-I. Ngasinye SALEZO ZINHLANGANO ezidlala ngomshini womuntu. Lowo ofuna ngempela ukuyeka ukuba unodoli womshini olula, kufanele aqale ngokuvuma nokuqonda umshini wakhe.

Lowo ongafuni ukuqonda noma ukwamukela umshini wakhe, lowo ongafuni ukuqonda kahle leli qiniso, akasakwazi ukushintsha, akanakho injabulo, usizi, kungcono ngaye ukuba abophele itshe lokugaya entanyeni yakhe aziphose ekujuleni kolwandle.

ISILWANE ESIHLAKANIPHILE ngumshini, kodwa umshini okhethekile kakhulu, uma lo mshini uqonda ukuthi UMSHINI, uma uqhutshwa kahle futhi uma izimo zivumela, ungayeka ukuba umshini futhi ube YINDODA.

Ngaphezu kwakho konke, kuphuthuma ukuqala ngokuqonda ngokujulile nakuwo wonke amazinga engqondo, ukuthi asinakho ukuzimela kweqiniso, ukuthi asinalo IZIKO ELINGAGUNYELWE LOKUQAPHELA, ukuthi ngesikhathi esithile singumuntu oyedwa futhi ngesinye isikhathi, omunye; konke kuncike KUMLOMO olawula isimo nganoma yisiphi isikhathi.

Lokho okuqala i-ILUSION ye-UNITY ne-INTEGRITY YE-SILWANE ESIHLAKANIPHILE ngakolunye uhlangothi yimizwa enayo UMQULU wayo WENYAMA, ngakolunye uhlangothi igama layo nesibongo futhi ekugcineni inkumbulo nenani elithile lemikhuba yemishini etshalwe kuyo nge-EDUCATION, noma etholwe ngokulingisa okuyisiwula nokuyisiwula.

ISILWANE ESIHLAKANIPHILE esimpofu ngeke sikwazi ukuyeka UKUBA UMSHINI, ngeke sikwazi ukushintsha, ngeke sikwazi ukuthola I-SER INDIVIDUAL YEQINISO futhi sibe umuntu osemthethweni, inqobo nje uma ingenaso isibindi SOKUQEDA NGOKUQONDA OKUJULILE futhi ngokulandelana, ngasinye salezo zinhlangano ze-METAFÍSICAS ezihlanganisa lokho okubizwa ngokuthi i-EGO, i-I, MINA.

Yonke IMBONO, yonke IPHASIKA, lonke iphutha, konke UKUTHANDA, yonke INZONDO, yonke intando, njll., njll., njll. inesilinganiso sayo SOMTHETHO futhi isethi yazo zonke lezo ZINHLANGANO yi-I PLURALIZED ye-PSICOLOGÍA REVOLUCIONARIA.

Zonke lezo ZINHLANGANO ze-METAFÍSICAS, wonke lawo ma-YOE ahlanganisa i-EGO, awanalo uxhumano lwangempela phakathi kwazo, awanalo ulwazi lwanoma yiluphi uhlobo. Ngasinye salezo ZINHLANGANO sincike ngokuphelele ezimweni, ekushintsheni kwemibono, ezenzakalweni, njll.

ISIKRINI SENGQONDO sishintsha imibala nezigcawu njalo, konke kuncike KUMLOMO olawula ingqondo nganoma yisiphi isikhathi.

NgesIKRINI sengqondo kudlula kulandelana okuqhubekayo KWEZINHLANGANO ezihlukene ezakha i-EGO noma i-YO PSICOLÓGICO.

IZINHLANGANO ezihlukahlukene ezakha i-I PLURALIZED ziyazihlanganisa, ziyazihlukanisa, zakha amaqembu akhethekile ngokuvumelana nokufana kwazo, ziyaxabana, ziyaphikisana, ziyazana, njll., njll., njll.

UMLOMO ngamunye we-LEGIÓN ebizwa ngokuthi yi-I, i-I encane ngayinye, ikholelwa ukuthi yiyo yonke, i-EGO ESEZONKE, ayisoli ngisho kude ukuthi yena uyingxenye encane kakhulu.

UMLOMO othi namuhla uthanda owesifazane kuze kube phakade, ususwa kamuva ngomunye UMLOMO ongahlangene nhlobo naleso sifungo bese inqaba yamakhadi iyawa bese owesifazane ompofu ekhala edumele.

UMLOMO othi namuhla uthembekile esizathwini, ususwa kusasa ngomunye UMLOMO ongahlangene nhlobo naleso sizathu bese umuntu uyahoxa.

UMLOMO othi namuhla uthembekile ku-GNOSIS, ususwa kusasa ngomunye UMLOMO ozonda i-GNOSIS.

Othisha nabafundisi bezikole, amakolishi namanyuvesi, kufanele bafunde le ncwadi ye-EDUCACIÓN FUNDAMENTAL futhi ngobuntu babe nesibindi sokuyalela abafundi ngendlela emangalisayo YE-REVOLUCIÓN DE LA CONCIENCIA.

Kuyadingeka ukuthi abafundi baqonde isidingo sokuzazi bona ngokwabo kuzo zonke izindawo zengqondo.

Kudingeka umbono ohlakaniphile osebenzayo kakhulu, kudingeka ukuqonda ukuthi siyini futhi lokhu kufanele kuqale kusukela ebhange elifanayo leSikole.

Asiphiki ukuthi imali iyadingeka ukuze sidle, sikhokhele intela yendlu sigqoke.

Asiphiki ukuthi kudingeka ukulungiselela okuhlakaniphile, umsebenzi, ubuchwepheshe ukuze kutholwe imali, kodwa akugcini lapho, lokho kuyasekeleka.

Okokuqala, okuyisisekelo wukwazi ukuthi singobani, siyini, sivela kuphi, siyaphi, iyini injongo yokuphila kwethu.

Kuyadabukisa ukuqhubeka njengonodoli abazenzakalelayo, abantu abafayo abahluphekayo, abantu-imishini.

Kuphuthuma ukuyeka ukuba imishini nje, kuphuthuma ukuba YIMADODA EQINISO.

Kudingeka ushintsho olukhulu futhi lokhu kufanele kuqale ngempela NGOKUQEDWA ngasinye salezo ZINHLANGANO ezakha ndawonye i-I PLURALIZED.

ISILWANE ESIHLAKANIPHILE esimpofu asiyena UMDODA kodwa sinazo zonke izinto ezingenzeka ngaphakathi kuso esimweni sokucasha, ukuze sibe UMDODA.

Akusona isenzo sokuthi lezo zinto ezingenzeka ziyakhula, okwemvelo kakhulu ukuthi zilahleke.

NGE-SUPER-IMIZAMO emikhulu kuphela lapho lezo zinto ezingenzeka zabantu zingakhula.

Kuningi okumele sikuqede futhi kuningi okumele sikuthole. Kuyadingeka ukwenza i-inventri ukuze wazi ukuthi yini eseqile futhi yini esishodayo.

Kuyacaca ukuthi i-I PLURALIZED iyaseqa, iyinto engenamsebenzi neyingozi.

KUNENGQONDO ukusho ukuthi kufanele sikhulise amandla athile, amathuba athile, amakhono athile INDODA-UMSHINI ezizibiza ngawo futhi ekholelwa ukuthi inawo kodwa empeleni eqinisweni AYINAWO.

INDODA-UMSHINI ikholelwa ukuthi inayo INDIVIDUALIDAD yangempela, CONCIENCIA DESPIERTA, INTANDO YOKUQAPHELA, AMANDLA OKWENZA, njll. futhi ayinalutho lwalokho.

Uma sifuna ukuyeka ukuba imishini, uma sifuna ukuvusa I-CONCIENCIA, sibe ne-INTANDO YOKUQAPHELA yangempela, INDIVIDUALIDAD, ikhono LOKWENZA, kuphuthuma ukuqala ngokuzazi bona bese sichitha i-I PSICOLÓGICO.

Lapho i-I PLURALIZED ichitheka kusala ngaphakathi kithi kuphela i-SER YEQINISO.
