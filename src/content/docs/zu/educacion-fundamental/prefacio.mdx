"Imfundo Eyisisekelo" isayensi esisivumela ukuthi sithole ubudlelwano bethu nabantu, nemvelo, nazo zonke izinto. Ngalolu lwazi siqonda ukusebenza kwengqondo ngoba ingqondo iyithuluzi lolwazi futhi kufanele sifunde ukulisebenzisa lelo thuluzi, eliyingqikithi eyisisekelo yomuntu ngokwengqondo.

Kulo msebenzi sifundiswa ngendlela ecishe ibe yeqiniso indlela yokucabanga, ngokusebenzisa uphenyo, ukuhlaziywa, ukuqonda nokuzindla.

Isazisa ukuthi singazithuthukisa kanjani izinkumbulo zememori ngaso sonke isikhathi sisebenzisa izici ezintathu: umuntu, into nendawo; inkumbulo iqhutshwa yintshisekelo, ngakho-ke kufanele sibeke intshisekelo kulokho okufundwayo ukuze kuqoshwe enkumbulweni. Inkumbulo ithuthukiswa ngenqubo yokuguqulwa kwe-alchemical abafundi abazothi kancane kancane bayazi abanentshisekelo ekuthuthukisweni kwabo siqu.

Kwabantu baseNtshonalanga, ukufunda kuqala lapho beneminyaka engu-6, okungukuthi lapho kulinganiselwa ukuthi banengqondo; kwabantu baseMpumalanga, ikakhulukazi amaHindu, imfundo iqala kusukela ekukhulelweni; kumaGnostic kusukela ebudlelwaneni bothando, okungukuthi, ngaphambi kokukhulelwa.

Imfundo yesikhathi esizayo izobandakanya izigaba ezimbili: esinye siphethwe abazali kanti esinye siphethwe othisha. Imfundo yesikhathi esizayo izobeka abafundi olwazini lwezulu lokufunda ukuba ngabazali. Okudingwa ngumuntu wesifazane isivikelo, usizo, yingakho intombazane inamathela kakhulu kuyise lapho iseyingane ngoba imbona eqinile futhi enamandla; umfana udinga uthando, ukunakekelwa, imanja, yingakho umfana enamathela kakhulu kunina ngomuzwa wemvelo. Kamuva, lapho izinzwa zombili ziphazamiseka, owesifazane ufuna umyeni omuhle noma indoda emthandayo, lapho kuyilapho yena okufanele anikeze uthando, futhi, indoda ifuna owesifazane onezindlela zokuphila noma onomsebenzi; kwabanye, ubuso nokuma komzimba kuyagqama ezinzweni zabo.

Kuyamangalisa ukubona izincwadi zesikole, umsebenzi ngamunye unemibuzo eyizinkulungwane, umbhali ayiphendula ngokubhala ukuze abafundi bayifunde ngekhanda, inkumbulo engathembekile iyindawo yokugcina ulwazi abasha abalufunda ngentshiseko enkulu, leyo mfundo yezinto ezibonakalayo ngokuphelele iyabahlomisa ukuthi baziphilise lapho beqeda izifundo zabo, kodwa abazi lutho ngempilo abazophila kuyo, bangena kuyo beyizimpumputhe, abazange bafundiswe ngisho nokuthi bangazala kanjani izinhlobo ngendlela ephakanyisiwe, leyo mfundiso iphethwe izigebengu emthunzini wokungabi nandaba.

Kuyadingeka ukuthi intsha iqonde ukuthi imbewu ekhiqizwa umzimba womuntu, iyona nto ebaluleke kakhulu empilweni yomuntu (uhlobo), ibusisiwe futhi ngenxa yalokho ukuyisebenzisa kabi kuzolimaza inzalo yayo. E-altare leSonto LamaKatolika, ihosti igcinwa endaweni engcwele ngenhlonipho enkulu njengoba imele umzimba kaKristu, lowo mfanekiso ongcwele; yakhiwe ngembewu kakolweni. E-altare eliphilayo, okungukuthi umzimba wethu, imbewu yethu ithatha indawo yohosti engcwele yobuKristu elandela uKristu Womlando; embewini yethu siqu sigcina uKristu esisekelweni kulabo esilandela uKristu ophilayo ophila futhi oshaya ekujuleni kwembewu yethu.

Ngentshisekelo enkulu sibona ukuthi izazi zezolimo eziphethe ulwazi lwezitshalo ezisebenzela umuntu, zifundisa abalimi ukuba bahloniphe imbewu abayithela emasimini, sibona ukuthi bathuthukise ikhwalithi yembewu ukuze bakhiqize izilimo ezingcono, begcina okusele okukhulu okusanhlamvu, ukuze imbewu engazange ikhiqizwe ngentshiseko enkulu ingalahleki. Sibona indlela odokotela bezilwane, abaphethe ukuphathwa kwempilo yezilwane, abakwazile ngayo ukukhiqiza abazali noma izilwane ezinkulu zembewu ezibiza izikhathi eziyikhulu ngaphezu komkhiqizo wenyama, okubonisa ukuthi yimbewu abayikhiqizayo, isizathu sentengo ephakeme kangaka. Yilapho kuphela imithi esemthethweni, ephethe ukunakekela izinhlobo zabantu, engasitsheli lutho ngokuthuthukiswa kwembewu; siyazisola ngalokhu kubambezeleka futhi sazisa abafundi bethu ukuthi imbewu yomuntu kulula kakhulu ukuyithuthukisa, ngokusebenzisa okuqhubekayo izinto ezintathu eziyisisekelo: ngalokho esikucabangayo, esikuphefumulayo nesikudlayo. Uma sicabanga kuphela ngezinto ezingacacile, ngezinto ezingenamsoco, ezingenamsebenzi iyoba njalo imbewu esiyikhiqizayo ngoba umcabango unquma ukuthi umkhiqizo. Intsha efundayo ihluke kuloyo ongafundile ngokubukeka nangokuba khona, kunoshintsho ebuntwini; Iqiniso lokuphefumula ubhiya ogayiwe emabhareni nasemakhanisini, linquma impilo yabantu abavame kulezi zindawo: Abantu abandla ngamakhekhe, ingulube, ubhiya, iziqholo, utshwala nokudla okuyizisu, baphila impilo yothando ebaphetha ngokuphinga.

Zonke izilwane eziphingayo ziyanyanyeka: izimbongolo, izingulube, izimbuzi ngisho nezinkukhu zasendlini naphezu kokuba yizinyoni, njengoba kunjalo nenkukhu yasendlini. Ungabona kalula umehluko okhona phakathi kweziphingi nalabo abantu abazenza abamsulwa ngenkani ukuze bazisebenzise, ​​bheka ama-gonads ehhashi lomjaho nelamahhashi okuthwala, phakathi kwezinkunzi ezilwayo nezilwane ezinkulu zembewu ezivame ukuvela emaphephandabeni, ingulube enkunzi, ngisho nasezilwaneni ezincane njengegundane elithanda kakhulu, futhi ukubukeka kwalo kuhlala kuyenyanyeka, into efanayo yenzeka endodeni ephingayo emboza iphunga layo elibi ngeziqholo nezimonyo. Lapho umuntu eba msulwa, ehlanzekile futhi engcwele, emcabangweni, emazwini nasezenzweni, uphinde athole ubuntwana obulahlekile, uyathuthukisa umzimba nomphefumulo futhi umzimba wakhe awujulukile.

Imfundo yangaphambi kokubeletha itholakala kanjani? Lokhu kwenzeka phakathi kwabantu abashadile abalandela ubumsulwa, okungukuthi, abangalahlekelwa imbewu yabo ngokungakhathali nangenjabulo yesikhashana, kanje: Abashadile bafuna ukunikeza umzimba isidalwa esisha, bayavumelana futhi bacele ukuthi iZulu liqondiswe ekutholakaleni kokukhulelwa, bese bethi bethanda unomphela bahlala ndawonye bejabule futhi begubha, basebenzisa isikhathi lapho imvelo ivuleke kakhulu, njengoba kwenza abalimi ukutshala, basebenzisa inqubo yokuguqulwa kwe-alchemical ngokuhlangana njengendoda nomfazi, evumela ukuphunyuka kwe-sperm enempilo futhi enamandla, ethuthukiswe yizindlela ezazaziwa ngaphambili futhi ngale ndlela kwenziwa ukukhulelwa kokukhu-luleka, lapho nje owesifazane eqaphela ukuthi ukhulelwe, uyazehlukanisa nendoda, okungukuthi, impilo yomshado iyaphela, lokhu kufanele kwenziwe kalula yindoda emsulwa ngoba igcwele umusa namandla angaphezu kwawomuntu, ngezindlela zonke yenza impilo yomkakhe ibe mnandi ukuze angaphendukeli ekucasulweni noma ezintweni ezifanayo ngoba konke lokhu kunomthelela emntwaneni osakhula, uma lokhu kubangela umonakalo ukuthi ngeke yini kube ukuhlangana abantu abangalitholanga nanini iseluleko kule ndlela abayenza ngayo ngendlela enkanukayo? Okunikeza izizathu zokuthi izingane eziningi zizwe uthando olubi kusukela zisencane futhi zihlaze omama bazo ngendlela eyihlazo.

Umama uyazi ukuthi unikeza impilo esidalweni esisha asigcina eThempelini lakhe Eliphilayo, njengento eyigugu, enikeza ngemikhuleko nemicabango yakhe izimo ezinhle ezizokhulisa isidalwa esisha, bese kuza isehlakalo sokuzalwa ngaphandle kobuhlungu; ngendlela elula nengokwemvelo enkazimulweni yabazali bayo. Lo mbhangqwana ugcina ukudla okunempilo okuvame ukuba yizinsuku ezingamashumi amane kuze kube yilapho isibeletho esisebenze njengombeleko wesidalwa esisha sibuyela endaweni yaso, indoda iyazi ukuthi owesifazane okhulisa ingane kufanele amnake futhi amcabangele, ngothando olunempilo ngoba noma iyiphi indlela yothando enobudlova ithinta amabele kamama futhi iletha ukuvinjelwa emiseleni lapho okugeleza khona uketshezi oluyigugu olunikeza impilo, ingane yesibeletho sakhe, owesifazane ofuna ukusebenzisa le mfundiso uzobona ukuthi kuyanyamalala ukuhlazeka kokuthi ahlinzwe amabele ngenxa yokuvinjelwa okuqhubekayo. Lapho kunobumsulwa khona kukhona uthando nokulalela, izingane ziyakhula ngendlela engokwemvelo futhi konke okubi kuyanyamalala, kanjalo iqala le mfundo eyisisekelo yokulungiselela ubuntu besidalwa esisha esizoya esikoleni siqeqeshelwe ukuqhubeka nemfundo ezosivumela ukuthi siphile ndawonye futhi kamuva sizitholele isinkwa sansuku zonke.
