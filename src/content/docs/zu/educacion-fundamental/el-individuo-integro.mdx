IMFUNDISO EYISISEKELO ngomqondo wayo wangempela wukuqonda okujulile ngomuntu uqobo; ngaphakathi komuntu ngamunye kutholakala yonke imithetho yemvelo.

Noma ubani ofuna ukwazi zonke izimangaliso zemvelo, kufanele azifunde ngaphakathi kwakhe.

Imfundo yamanga ikhathazeka kuphela ngokucebisa ingqondo futhi noma ubani angakwenza lokho. Kusobala ukuthi ngemali, noma ubani angakwazi ukuthenga izincwadi.

Asiphikisani namasiko engqondo, siphikisana kuphela nokulangazelela okweqile kokuziqongelela engqondweni.

Imfundo yamanga yengqondo inikeza kuphela izindlela zokuphunyuka ezicashile zokubalekela umuntu uqobo.

Wonke umuntu ofundile, wonke umuntu okhohlakele ngokwengqondo, uhlala enezaba ezinhle zokumvumela ukuba abalekele yena.

UBUHLAKANI ngaphandle KWEZINGOKOMOYA buveza IZIGEBENGU futhi lezi ziholele isintu ESIBHIDLANGEKWENI nASEMBHUBHISWENI.

Ubuciko ngeke bukwazi ukusenza sazi thina ngokwethu ngendlela ePHUMELELE NGEZIKHATHI ZONKE.

Abazali bathumela izingane zabo eSikoleni, eKolishi, eNyuvesi, ePolitekniki, njll., ukuba zifunde ubuciko obuthile, ukuze zibe nomsebenzi othile, ukuze ekugcineni zikwazi ukuziphilisa.

Kuyacaca ukuthi sidinga ukwazi ubuciko obuthile, ukuba nomsebenzi, kodwa lokho kuyinto yesibili, okuyinto yokuqala, okuyisisekelo, ukuzazi thina, ukwazi ukuthi singobani, sivela kuphi, siya kuphi, uyini umgomo wokuba khona kwethu.

Empilweni kunakho konke, intokozo, usizi, uthando, isifiso, injabulo, ubuhlungu, ubuhle, ububi, njll. futhi lapho sikwazi ukukuphila ngamandla, lapho sikuqonda kuwo wonke AMANDLA engqondo, sithola indawo yethu emphakathini, sidala ubuciko bethu, indlela yethu ekhethekile yokuziphilisa, yokuzwa nokucabanga, kodwa okuphambene kuyamanga ngamaphesenti ayikhulu, ubuciko ngokwabo, ngeke buze bube yimbangela yokuqonda okujulile, ukuqonda kweqiniso.

Imfundo yamanje ibe yihlazo elikhulu ngoba inikeza ukubaluleka OKUNGEKHO EBUNTWINI kubuciko, emsebenzini futhi kuyacaca ukuthi ngokugcizelela ubuciko, iguqula umuntu abe umshini ozisebenzelayo, ibhubhisa amathuba akhe angcono kakhulu.

Ukuhlakulela ikhono nokusebenza ngaphandle kokuqonda impilo, ngaphandle kokuzazi, ngaphandle kokubona ngqo inqubo YOKUTHI NGIYIMINA, ngaphandle kokutadisha ngokucophelela indlela yethu yokucabanga, yokuzwa, yokufisa nokwenza, kuzosiza kuphela ukwandisa unya lwethu, ubugovu bethu, lezo zici zePsychological ezibangela impi, indlala, ubumpofu, ubuhlungu.

Ukuthuthukiswa okukhethekile kwezobuchwepheshe kuveze oMakhenika, Ososayensi, ochwepheshe, izazi ze-athomu, abahlukanisa izilwane ezimpofu, abasunguli bezikhali ezibhubhisayo, njll., njll., njll.

Bonke labo ochwepheshe, bonke labo basunguli beBomb Atomiki neBomb leHydrogen, bonke labo bahlukanisi bezilwane abahlukumeza izidalwa zemvelo, zonke lezo zigebengu, okuwukuphela kwento ezisebenzela yona ngempela, eyempi nembubhiso.

Akukho abakwaziyo bonke labo gebengu, abaqondi lutho ngenqubo ephelele yempilo kuzo zonke izimo zayo ezingapheli.

Inqubekela phambili yezobuchwepheshe jikelele, izinhlelo zokuhamba, imishini yokubala, ukukhanya kukagesi, amakheshi ngaphakathi kwezakhiwo, ubuchopho be-elekthronikhi bezinhlobo zonke, njll., zixazulula izinkulungwane zezinkinga ezicutshungulwa ezingeni elingaphezulu lokuba khona, kodwa zingenisa kumuntu nakumphakathi, izinkinga eziningi ezibanzi nezijulile.

Ukuphila kuphela KWEZINGA ELINGAPHEZULU ngaphandle kokucabangela izindawo ezahlukene nezifunda ezijule kakhulu zengqondo, empeleni kusho ukuzidonsela thina nezingane zethu, usizi, isililo nokuphelelwa yithemba.

Isidingo esikhulu, inkinga ephuthuma kakhulu yomuntu NGAMUNYE, yomuntu ngamunye, ukukuqonda impilo ngendlela yayo EPHUMELELE, ESEBENZAYO, ngoba yile ndlela kuphela esikwazi ngayo ukuxazulula ngempumelelo zonke izinkinga zethu eziseduze.

Ulwazi lobuchwepheshe ngokwalo alusoze lwazixazulula zonke izinkinga zethu zePsychological, zonke izinkinga zethu ezijulile.

Uma sifuna ukuba AMADODA eqiniso, AMAQEMBU AQHELEKILE kufanele SIHLOLE NGOKUZENZAKALELAYO NGOKWENGQONDO, sizazi ngokujulile kuzo zonke izindawo zokucabanga, ngoba UBUCHWEPHESHE ngaphandle kokungabaza, buba yithuluzi elilimazayo, lapho SINGAYIQONDI NGEMPELA yonke inqubo ephelele yokuba khona, lapho singazazi ngendlela EPHUMELELE.

Uma ISILWANE SOMBONO singathanda NGEQINISO, uma singazazi, uma besizikuqonda inqubo ephelele yempilo, ngabe akukaze SENZE UBUGEBENGE BOKUHLUKANISA I-ATHOMU.

Inqubekela phambili yethu yezobuchwepheshe iyamangalisa kodwa ikwazile kuphela ukwandisa amandla ethu okuhlasela ukuze sibhubhisane futhi yonke indawo kubusa ukwesaba, indlala, ukungazi nezifo.

Akukho msebenzi, akukho buchwepheshe obungasinika lokho okubizwa ngokuthi i-PLENITUDE, INJABULO YEQINISO.

Yilowo nalowo empilweni uhlupheka kakhulu emsebenzini wakhe, emsebenzini wakhe, endleleni yakhe yokuphila ejwayelekile futhi izinto nemisebenzi iphenduka amathuluzi omhawu, wokuncoma, wenzondo, wentukuthelo.

Umhlaba wodokotela, umhlaba wabaculi, bonjiniyela, abameli, njll., ngamunye waleyo mihlaba, ugcwele ubuhlungu, ukuncoma, ukuncintisana, umona, njll.

Ngaphandle kokuqonda thina ngokwethu umsebenzi nje, umsebenzi noma umsebenzi, kusiletha ebuhlungwini nasekufuneni izaba. Abanye bafuna izindlela zokuphunyuka ngotshwala, i-cantina, itaveni, iklabhu, abanye bafuna ukuphunyuka ngezidakamizwa, i-morphine, i-cocaine, i-marijuana nabanye ngobunewabu nokuziphatha okubi, ngokocansi, njll., njll.

Lapho umuntu efuna ukunciphisa YONKE IMPILO ibe ubuciko, ibe umsebenzi, ibe yindlela yokuthola imali eningi futhi, umphumela uba isithukuthezi, isikhathi esibi nokufuna izindlela zokuphunyuka.

Kufanele sibe YINDODA EPHUMELELE, ephelele futhi lokho kungenzeka kuphela ngokuzazi nokuchitha UMUNTU WENGQONDO.

IMFUNDISO EYISISEKELO ngesikhathi esifanayo ivuselela ukufundwa kwekhono lokuziphilisa, kufanele yenze okuthile okubaluleke kakhulu, kufanele isize umuntu, ukuba azizwele, azizwele kuzo zonke izici zakhe nakuzo zonke izindawo zengqondo, inqubo yokuba khona.

Uma umuntu enokuthile angakusho makukusho futhi lokho kokukusho kuyathakazelisa kakhulu ngoba ngaleyo ndlela yilowo nalowo uzenzela indlela yakhe, kodwa ufunda izitayela zabanye ngaphandle kokuba esezitholele mathupha impilo ngendlela yayo EPHUMELELE; kuholela kuphela ekungajulini.
