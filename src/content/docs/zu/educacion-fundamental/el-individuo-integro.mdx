IMFUNDISO EZIYISISEKELO ngomqondo wayo weqiniso ukuziqonda ngokujulile; ngaphakathi komuntu ngamunye kukhona yonke imithetho yemvelo.

Noma ngubani ofuna ukwazi zonke izimangaliso zemvelo, kufanele azifunde ngaphakathi kwakhe.

Imfundo yamanga ikhathazeka kuphela ngokucebisa ingqondo futhi noma ngubani angakwenza lokho. Kusobala ukuthi ngemali, noma ngubani angakwazi ukuzithengela izincwadi.

Asiphikisani nesiko lokuhlakanipha, siphikisana kuphela nesifiso esidlulele sokuziqongelela ulwazi engqondweni.

Imfundo yamanga yokuhlakanipha inikeza kuphela izindlela ezicashile zokubalekela wena.

Wonke umuntu ohlakaniphile, wonke umuntu okhohlakele ngokwengqondo, uhlale enezaba ezimangalisayo ezimvumela ukuthi azibalekele yena.

KUPHUMA IZIGILAMKHU EBUHLAKANIPHENI NGAPHANDLE KOKOMOYA futhi lezi ziholele isintu ESIZHUNGUNGUFUZENI NASEMBHUBHISENI.

Ubuchwepheshe abunakusenza sikwazi ukuzazi ngendlela EPHELELE EYODWA-YONKE.

Abazali bathumela izingane zabo eSikoleni, eKolishi, eNyuvesi, ePolitekniki, njll., ukuthi ziyofunda ubuchwepheshe obuthile, ukuthi zibe nomsebenzi othile, ukuze ekugcineni zikwazi ukuziphilisa.

Kusobala ukuthi sidinga ukwazi ubuchwepheshe obuthile, sibe nomsebenzi, kodwa lokho kuyasekeleka, okuyinhloko, okuyisisekelo, ukuzazi, ukwazi ukuthi singobani, sivela kuphi, siyaphi, iyini injongo yokuba khona kwethu.

Empilweni kunakho konke, injabulo, ukudabuka, uthando, inkanuko, intokozo, ubuhlungu, ubuhle, ububi, njll. futhi lapho sikwazi ukuyiphila ngamandla, lapho siyiqonda kuwo wonke amazinga engqondo, sithola indawo yethu emphakathini, sakha ubuchwepheshe bethu, indlela yethu ekhethekile yokukuphila, yokuzizwa nokucabanga, kodwa okuphambene nalokho kuyiqiniso ngamaphesenti ayikhulu, ubuchwepheshe ngokwabo, abunakukhiqiza ukuqonda okujulile, ukuqonda kweqiniso.

Imfundo yamanje iphumelele ukwehluleka okuphelele ngoba inikeza ukubaluleka OKUDLULELWAYO kubuchwepheshe, emsebenzini futhi kusobala ukuthi ngokugcizelela ubuchwepheshe, iphendula umuntu abe ngumshini wokuzenzakalela, ibhubhisa amathuba akhe angcono kakhulu.

Ukuhlakulela ikhono nokusebenza kahle ngaphandle kokuqonda impilo, ngaphandle kokuzazi, ngaphandle kokubona ngokuqondile inqubo yeMI OKWAMI, ngaphandle kokutadisha okuningiliziwe indlela yami yokucabanga, yokuzizwa, yokufisa nokwenza, kuzosiza kuphela ukwandisa isihluku sethu, ubugovu bethu, lezo zinto ezingokwengqondo ezikhiqiza impi, indlala, ubumpofu, ubuhlungu.

Ukuthuthukiswa okukhethekile kobuchwepheshe kukhiqize Omakhenikha, Ososayensi, ochwepheshe, izazi ze-athomu, abahlinzeki abahlukumeza izilwane ezimpofu, abasunguli bezikhali ezibhubhisayo, njll., njll., njll.

Bonke labo ochwepheshe, bonke labo basunguli bamabhomu e-Atomic namabhomu e-Hydrogen, bonke labo bahlinzeki abahlukumeza izidalwa zemvelo, zonke lezo zigilamkhu, okuwukuphela kwento abayisebenzelayo ngempela, yimpi nembubhiso.

Akukho abakwaziyo bonke labo zigilamkhu, abaqondi lutho ngenqubo ephelele yokuphila kuzo zonke izimo zayo ezingenamkhawulo.

Inqubekelaphambili yezobuchwepheshe ejwayelekile, izinhlelo zezokuthutha, imishini yokubala, ukukhanyisa kukagesi, izileyi ngaphakathi kwezakhiwo, ubuchopho be-elekthronikhi bayo yonke inhlobo, njll., kuxazulula izinkulungwane zezinkinga ezicutshungulwa ezingeni elingaphezulu lokuba khona, kodwa kwethula kumuntu nakumphakathi, inqwaba yezinkinga ezibanzi futhi ezijulile.

Ukuphila kuphela ELEVELI ELINGAPHEZULU ngaphandle kokucabangela izindawo ezahlukene nezifunda ezijulile zengqondo, kusho eqinisweni ukuzilethela thina nezingane zethu, usizi, izinyembezi nokuphelelwa ithemba.

Isidingo esikhulu kunazo zonke, inkinga ephuthuma kakhulu yomuntu ngamunye, yomuntu ngamunye, ukuqonda impilo ngendlela yayo EPHELELE, EYODWA-YONKE, ngoba yileyo ndlela kuphela esisesimweni sokukwazi ukuxazulula ngempumelelo zonke izinkinga zethu eziseduze.

Ulwazi lobuchwepheshe ngokwalo alunakuzixazulula zonke izinkinga zethu ezingokwengqondo, zonke izinkinga zethu ezijulile.

Uma sifuna ukuba AMADODA eqiniso, ABANTU ABAPHELELE kufanele SIZIHLOLA ngokwengqondo, sizazi ngokujulile kuzo zonke izindawo zokucabanga, ngoba UBUCHWEPHESHE ngaphandle kokungabaza, buba yithuluzi elibhubhisayo, lapho SINGAYIQONDI ngempela yonke inqubo ephelele yokuba khona, lapho singazazi ngendlela EPHELELE.

Ukuba ISILWANE ESICABANGAYO besithanda ngeqiniso, ukuba besizazi, ukuba besiqonde yonke inqubo yokuphila, ngabe asikaze senze UBUGEBENENGU BOKWEHLUKANISA I-ATHOMU.

Inqubekelaphambili yethu yezobuchwepheshe iyinhle kakhulu kodwa isikwazile kuphela ukwandisa amandla ethu okuhlasela ukuze sibhubhisane futhi yonke indawo kubusa ukwesaba, indlala, ukungazi nezifo.

Akukho msebenzi, akukho buchwepheshe obungasinika lokho okubizwa ngokuthi UKUPHELA, INJABULO YEQINISO.

Wonke umuntu empilweni uhlupheka kakhulu emsebenzini wakhe, emsebenzini wakhe, endleleni yakhe yokuphila evamile futhi izinto nemisebenzi iba yizinsimbi zomona, ukuhleba, inzondo, ukuba munyu.

Umhlaba wodokotela, umhlaba wabaculi, bonjiniyela, abameli, njll., ngamunye waleyo mihlaba, ugcwele ubuhlungu, ukuhleba, ukuncintisana, umona, njll.

Ngaphandle kokuqonda thina uqobo, umsebenzi nje, umsebenzi noma umsebenzi, sisiholela ebuhlungwini nasekufuneni izindlela zokubalekela. Abanye bafuna izindlela zokubalekela ngotshwala, indawo yokuphuzela utshwala, indawo yokucima ukoma, iklabhu yasebusuku, abanye bafuna ukuphunyuka ngezidakamizwa, i-morphine, i-cocaine, i-marijuana nabanye ngobunethezekile nokonakala ngokobulili, njll., njll.

Lapho ufuna ukunciphisa YONKE IMPILO ibe ubuchwepheshe, ibe umsebenzi, ibe yindlela yokuthola imali eningi, umphumela uba yisithukuthezi, ukukhathazeka nokufuna izindlela zokubalekela.

Kufanele siphenduke ABANTU ABAQHUBELEKAYO, abaphelele futhi lokho kungenzeka kuphela ngokuzazi nokuncibilikisa UBUYE BENGQONDO.

IMFUNDISO EZIYISISEKELO ngesikhathi esifanayo ikhuthaza ukufunda ubuchwepheshe bokuziphilisa, kufanele yenze okuthile okubaluleke kakhulu, kufanele isize umuntu, ukuthi azizwe, azizwe kuzo zonke izici zayo nazo zonke izindawo zengqondo, inqubo yokuba khona.

Uma umuntu enokuthile angakusho makakusho futhi lokho kokukusho kuyathakazelisa kakhulu ngoba ngaleyo ndlela wonke umuntu uzenzela esakhe isitayela, kodwa ufunda izitayela zabanye ngaphandle kokuzizwela ngqo impilo ngendlela yayo EPHELELE; kuholela kuphela ekungajulini.
