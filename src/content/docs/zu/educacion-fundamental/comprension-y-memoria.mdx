Ukukhumbula kuzama ukugcina engqondweni lokho esikubonile nesikuzwile, esikufundile, lokho abanye abantu abasitshele kona, okusihluzele, njll. njll. njll.

Othisha bafuna abafundi babo bagcine ezinkumbulweni zabo amazwi abo, imisho yabo, lokho okubhalwe ezincwadini zesikole, izahluko zonke, imisebenzi esindayo, nazo zonke izimpawu zokubhala, njll.

Ukubhala izivivinyo kusho ukukhumbula lokho esikutshelwe, lokho esikufunde ngaphandle kokuzindla, ukuveza inkumbulo ngamazwi, ukuphinda njengopholi, izinyoni noma amaparrot, konke esikugcine enkumbulweni.

Kuyadingeka ukuthi isizukulwane esisha siqonde ukuthi ukuphinda njenge-Radioconsola wonke amarekhodi enziwe enkumbulweni akusho ukuthi usuqonde ngokugcwele. Ukukhumbula akusho ukuqonda, akusizi ngalutho ukukhumbula ngaphandle kokuqonda, inkumbulo ngeyakudala, into efile, into engasena mpilo.

Kubalulekile, kuyaphuthuma futhi kuyinto ebalulekile ukuthi bonke abafundi bezikole, amakolishi namanyuvesi bakuqonde ngempela ukubaluleka okujulile kokuqonda okujulile.

UKUQONDA kuyinto esheshayo, eqondile, into esiyizwa kakhulu, into esiyizwa ngokujulile futhi engenakugwenywa ukuthi iba YINHLOKO yangempela esebenza ngokuqaphela.

Ukukhumbula, ukukhumbula kuyinto efile, okwakudala futhi ngeshwa kuba yinhloso, isiqubulo, umqondo, umbono esifuna ukuwulingisa ngomshini futhi siwulandele singaqapheli.

EKUQONDENI KWEQINISO, ekuqondeni okujulile, ekuqondeni okujulile kwesizinda kukhona kuphela ingcindezi yangaphakathi yokwazi, ingcindezi eqhubekayo ezalwa yisisindo esithwele ngaphakathi futhi yilokho kuphela.

Ukuqonda kweqiniso kuzibonakalisa njengesenzo esizenzakalelayo, esingokwemvelo, esilula, esikhululekile enqubweni ecindezelayo yokukhetha; emsulwa ngaphandle kokungabaza kwanoma yiluphi uhlobo. UKUQONDA okuguqulwe kwaba YINHLOKO EYIMFIHLO yesenzo kuyamangalisa, kuyamangaza, kuyakha futhi kuyahlonipha ngokuyisisekelo.

Isenzo esisekelwe ekukhunjulweni kwalokho esikufundile, umgomo esiphishekela kuwo, umthetho, ukuziphatha esiye safundiswa, okuhlangenwe nakho okuye kwaqoqwa enkumbulweni, njll., kuyabala, kuncike kokukhetha okucindezelayo, kuwuhlangothi olubili, kusekelwe ekukhetheni komqondo futhi kuholela kuphela ekukhukhumezeni nasebuhlungwini.

Lokho kokuzivumelanisa nesenzo ekukhunjulweni, lokho kokuzama ukushintsha isenzo ukuze sihambisane nezinkumbulo eziqoqwe enkumbulweni, kuyinto yokuzenzisa, engenangqondo engenakuzenzakalela futhi engenakusiholela ekukhukhumezeni nasebuhlungwini.

Lokho kokubhala izivivinyo, lokho kokudlula unyaka, kwenziwa yinoma yimuphi umuntu oyisiwula onomthamo omuhle wesu nenkumbulo.

Ukuqonda izifundo esizifundile futhi esizohlolwa kuzo, kuyinto ehluke kakhulu, ayihlangene nenkumbulo, ngeyobuhlakani beqiniso okungafanele budidaniswe nokuhlakanipha.

Labo bantu abafuna ukusekela zonke izenzo zokuphila kwabo emigomweni, ezinkolelweni nasezinkumbulo zazo zonke izinhlobo eziqoqwe emakamelo okugcina enkumbulo, bahlala behamba beqhathanisa nokuqhathanisa futhi lapho kukhona ukuqhathanisa kukhona nomona. Laba bantu baqhathanisa abantu babo, amalungu emindeni yabo, izingane zabo nezingane zomakhelwane, nabantu bomakhelwane. Baqhathanisa indlu yabo, ifenisha yabo, izingubo zabo, zonke izinto zabo, nezinto zomakhelwane noma zomakhelwane noma zomakhelwane. Baqhathanisa imibono yabo, ubuhlakani bezingane zabo nemibono yabanye abantu, nobuhlakani babanye abantu bese kufika umona oguqulwa ube yinhloko eyimfihlo yesenzo.

Ngeshwa lomhlaba wonke, yonke indlela yokusebenza komphakathi isekelwe emoneni nasemoyeni wokuthenga. Wonke umuntu uyamona wonke umuntu. Sinomona ngemibono, izinto, abantu futhi sifuna ukuthola imali nemali eyengeziwe, izinkolelo ezintsha, imibono emisha esiyiqoqa enkumbulweni, izinto ezintsha zokukhanyisa abantu esifana nabo, njll.

EKUQONDENI KWEQINISO, okusemthethweni, okuyiqiniso, kukhona uthando lweqiniso futhi akukhona nje ukuvezwa kwenkumbulo ngamazwi.

Izinto ezikhunjulwayo, lokho okwethenjiswa inkumbulo, ngokushesha kuyalibaleka ngoba inkumbulo ayithembekile. Abafundi bafaka emakamelo okugcina enkumbulo, imigomo, izinkolelo, imibhalo ephelele engasizi ngalutho empilweni yangempela ngoba ekugcineni iyanyamalala enkumbulweni ingashiyi mkhondo.

Abantu abaphila ngokufunda nokufunda nje ngaphandle kokuzindla, abantu abajabulela ukugcina izinkolelo phakathi kwamakamelo okugcina enkumbulo bayayibhubhisa ingqondo, bayilimaza kabi.

Asimelene nokufunda kweqiniso okujulile nokuqaphela okusekelwe ekuqondeni okujulile. Sigxeka kuphela izindlela eziphelelwe yisikhathi ze-pedagogy ezingafanele. Sigxeka yonke indlela yokufunda ngomshini, konke ukukhumbula, njll. Ukukhumbula kuyadingeka lapho kukhona ukuqonda kweqiniso.

Sidinga ukufunda, kudingeka izincwadi eziwusizo, kudingeka othisha bezikole, amakolishi, amanyuvesi. Kudingeka i-GURU, abaqondisi bokomoya, ama-mahatma, njll. kodwa kuyadingeka ukuqonda izimfundiso ngokugcwele hhayi nje ukuzifaka phakathi kwamakamelo okugcina inkumbulo engathembekile.

Ngeke sikhululeke ngempela uma sisathanda ukuziqhathanisa nenkumbulo eqoqwe enkumbulweni, nomgomo, nalokho esifisa ukuba yikho futhi asisiyikho, njll. njll.

Lapho siziqonda ngempela izimfundiso esizitholile, asidingi ukuzikhumbula enkumbulweni, noma ukuziguqula zibe yimigomo.

Lapho kukhona ukuqhathanisa kwalokho esiyikho lapha manje nalokho esifuna ukuba yikho kamuva, lapho kukhona ukuqhathanisa kokuphila kwethu kwangempela nomgomo noma imodeli esifuna ukuzivumelanisa nayo, ngeke kube khona uthando lweqiniso.

Konke ukuqhathanisa kuyenyanyeka, konke ukuqhathanisa kuletha ukwesaba, umona, ukuziqhenya, njll. Ukwesaba ukungakufinyeleli lokho esikufunayo, umona ngenqubekela phambili yomunye umuntu, ukuziqhenya ngoba sizibona singcono kunabanye. Okubalulekile empilweni yangempela esiyiphilayo, kungakhathaliseki ukuthi sibabi, sinomona, sinobugovu, sinokuhaha, njll., ukungazenzi abangcwele, ukuqala phansi, nokuziqonda thina ngokujulile, njengoba sinjalo hhayi njengoba sifuna ukuba yikho noma njengoba sizenza ukuthi siyikho.

Akunakwenzeka ukuchitha i-YO, i-MI MISMO, uma singafundi ukuzibheka, ukubona ukuze siqonde lokho esiyikho ngempela lapha manje ngendlela ephumelelayo futhi esebenza ngokuphelele.

Uma sifuna ngempela ukuqonda kufanele silalele othisha bethu, othisha, ama-guru, abapristi, abafundisi, abaqondisi bokomoya, njll., njll.

Abafana, namantombazane esizukulwane esisha balahlekelwe umuzwa wokuhlonipha, wokukhonza abazali bethu, othisha, abaqondisi bokomoya, ama-guru, ama-mahatma, njll.

Akunakwenzeka ukuqonda izimfundiso lapho singakwazi ukuhlonipha nokuhlonipha abazali bethu, othisha, abafundisi noma abaqondisi bokomoya.

Ukukhumbula nje ngomshini lokho esikufunde ngekhanda kuphela ngaphandle kokuqonda okujulile, kuguqula ingqondo nenhliziyo futhi kuveza umona, ukwesaba, ukuziqhenya, njll.

Lapho sazi ngempela ukulalela ngendlela eqaphelayo nejulile kuphakama ngaphakathi kwethu amandla amangalisayo, ukuqonda okumangalisayo, okungokwemvelo, okulula, okukhululekile kuyo yonke inqubo yomshini, okukhululekile kukho konke ukucabanga, okukhululekile kukho konke ukukhumbula.

Uma ubuchopho bomfundi bukhululwa emzamweni omkhulu wenkumbulo okufanele awenze, kuzokwazi ukufundisa isakhiwo somongo kanye netafula lezinto ezithile kubafundi bezinga lesibili futhi benze baqonde ubudlelwano bama-Quanta kumuntu oneziqu ze-bachiller.

Njengoba sixoxe nothisha abathile bezikole zamabanga aphakathi siyaqonda ukuthi bayathuka ngokushisekela ngokweqile i-pedagogy yakudala engasekho. Bafuna abafundi bafunde konke ngekhanda yize bengakuqondi.

Ngezinye izikhathi bayavuma ukuthi kungcono ukuqonda kunokukhumbula ngekhanda kodwa-ke bayaphikelela ngokuthi amafomula we-physics, i-chemistry, izibalo, njll. kufanele aqoshwe enkumbulweni.

Kucacile ukuthi lo mqondo ungamanga ngoba lapho ifomula ye-physics, i-chemistry, izibalo, njll., iqondwa kahle hhayi nje ezingeni lobuhlakani, kodwa futhi nakwamanye amazinga engqondo njengokungazi, okungazi, okungezansi, njll. njll. njll. Akudingekile ukuthi iqoshwe enkumbulweni, iba yingxenye ye-psyche yethu futhi ingazibonakalisa njengolwazi oluzenzakalelayo olusheshayo lapho izimo zokuphila zikudinga.
