Izigidi zabafundi bamazwe ngamazwe emhlabeni wonke ziya esikoleni naseyunivesithi nsuku zonke ngokungenalusizo, ngokuzenzakalela, ngokungagxili, ngaphandle kokwazi ukuthi kungani, noma yini injongo.

Abafundi bayaphoqeleka ukuthi bafunde izibalo, iFiziksi, iKhemistri, iJografi, njll.

Izingqondo zabafundi zithola ulwazi nsuku zonke kodwa azikaze zime isikhashana zicabange ukuthi kungani lolo lwazi, injongo yalolo lwazi.

Kungani sizigcwalisa ngalolu lwazi? Siyazigcwaliselani ngalolu lwazi?

Abafundi baphila impilo yomshini ngempela futhi bazi kuphela ukuthi kufanele bathole ulwazi lobuhlakani futhi balugcine lugcinwe enkumbulweni engathembekile, yilokho kuphela.

Abafundi abacabangi ngalokho okuyimfundo ngempela, baya esikoleni, ekolishi noma eyunivesithi ngoba abazali babo bayabathumela futhi yilokho kuphela.

Akubafundeli, noma othisha abacabangi ukuzibuza: Kungani ngilapha? Ngizele ukuzokwenzani lapha? Yini ngempela injongo eyimfihlo yangempela engiletha lapha?

Othisha, abafundi besilisa nabesifazane, baphila nokuqwashisa okulele, baziphatha njengabantu abazenzakalelayo, baya esikoleni, ekolishi naseyunivesithi ngokungenalusizo, ngokungagxili, ngaphandle kokwazi ngempela lutho mayelana nokuthi kungani, noma yini injongo.

Kuyadingeka ukuyeka ukuba yimishini, ukuvusa ukuqwashisa, ukuthola ngokwakho ukuthi iyini le mpi esabekayo yokudlula izivivinyo, ukufunda, ukuhlala endaweni ethile ukuze ufunde nsuku zonke futhi udlulise unyaka futhi uhlushwe ukwethuka, izinkathazo, ukukhathazeka, ukuzilolonga kwezemidlalo, ukulwa nabafundi ofunda nabo esikoleni, njll, njll, njll.

Othisha kufanele baqaphele ngokwengeziwe ukuze babambisane besuka esikoleni, ekolishi noma eyunivesithi besiza abafundi ukuthi bavuke ukuqwashisa.

Kuyadabukisa ukubona abantu abaningi ABAZENZAKALELAYO behlezi emabhentshini ezikole, amakolishi namayunivesithi, bethola ulwazi okufanele balugcine enkumbulweni ngaphandle kokwazi ukuthi kungani noma yini injongo.

Abafana bakhathazeka kuphela ngokudlulisa unyaka; batshelwe ukuthi kufanele bazilungiselele ukuziphilisa, ukuthola umsebenzi, njll. Bafunda bakha imibono eyinkulungwane engqondweni mayelana nekusasa, ngaphandle kokulwazi ngempela usuku lwanamuhla, ngaphandle kokwazi isizathu sangempela okufanele bafunde ngaso iFiziksi, iKhemistri, iBhayoloji, i-Arithemetiki, iJografi, njll.

Amantombazane esimanje afunda ukuze abe nokulungiselela okubenza bakwazi ukuzitholela indoda enhle, noma ukuziphilisa futhi azilungiselele kahle uma kwenzeka indoda iwashiya, noma uma eba ngumfelokazi noma engashadi.

Imibono emsulwa engqondweni ngoba empeleni abazi ukuthi ikusasa labo liyoba njani noma bazokufa ngamuphi unyaka.

Impilo esikoleni ayicacile, ayihambisani, ayigxili, umntwana wenza ukuthi ngezinye izikhathi afunde izifundo ezithile ezingasizi ngalutho empilweni yangempela.

Namuhla into ebalulekile esikoleni ukudlulisa unyaka futhi yilokho kuphela.

Kwezinye izikhathi bekunokuziphatha okuhle okungenani kulokhu kokudlulisa unyaka. Manje akukho UKUZIPHATHA okunjalo. Abazali bangathembisa ngemfihlo enkulu uthisha noma uthisha futhi umfana noma intombazane noma ngabe UNGUMFUNDI OMBI KAKHULU, uzodlulisa unyaka NGOKUNGAGWEMEKI.

Amantombazane esikoleni avame ukwenza intshebe kuthisha ngenhloso YOKUDLULISA UNYAKA futhi umphumela uvame ukuba muhle kakhulu, noma ngabe abaqondi noma "u-J" walokho okufundiswa uthisha, noma kunjalo baphumelela ezivivinyweni futhi badlulise unyaka.

Kunabafana namantombazane abahlakaniphe kakhulu ukudlulisa unyaka. Lokhu kuyindaba yobuqili ezimweni eziningi.

Umfana onqoba ukuhlolwa okuthile (ukuhlolwa okuthile okuyisiphukuphuku) akusho ukuthi unokuqwashisa okuyiqiniso, okuyiqiniso ngaleyo ndaba ahlolwe kuyo.

Umfundi uyaphinda njengomlobokazi, i-parakeet noma i-parrot futhi ngendlela yomshini leyo ndaba ayifundile futhi ahlolwe kuyo.

Lokho akukona ukuba UZIQAPHELE ngaleyo ndaba, lokho ukukhumbula ngekhanda nokuyiphinda njengama-parrot noma ama-parakeet lokho esikufundile futhi yilokho kuphela.

Ukudlulisa izivivinyo, ukudlulisa unyaka, akusho ukuthi UHLAKANIPHILE KAKHULU. Empilweni yangempela sesike sazazi abantu abahlakaniphile kakhulu abangazange baphumelele ezivivinyweni esikoleni. Sesike sazazi ababhali abahle kanye nezazi zezibalo ezinkulu ababengabafundi ababi kakhulu esikoleni futhi abangazange bazidlulise kahle izivivinyo zohlelo lolwimi nezibalo.

Siyayazi indaba yomfundi owayemubi kakhulu ku-ANATOMY futhi okwathi ngemva kokuhlupheka okukhulu wakwazi ukuphumelela ezivivinyweni ze-ANATOMY. Namuhla lo mfundi ungumbhali womsebenzi omkhulu we-ANATOMY.

Ukudlulisa unyaka akusho ngempela ukuthi uhlakaniphile kakhulu. Kunabantu abangakaze badlulise unyaka futhi abahlakaniphile kakhulu.

Kukhona okubaluleke ngaphezu kokudlulisa unyaka, kukhona okubaluleke ngaphezu kokufunda izifundo ezithile futhi ngokuqondile ukuba nokuqwashisa OKUQONDILE okuphelele okucacile nokukhanyayo ngalezo zifundo ezifundwayo.

Othisha kufanele bazame ukusiza abafundi ukuvusa ukuqwashisa; yonke imizamo yothisha kufanele iqondiswe ekuqwashiseni abafundi. KUYAPHUTHUMA ukuthi abafundi bazenze babe abaqaphela ngokugcwele ngalezo zifundo abazifundayo.

Ukufunda ngekhanda, ukufunda njengama-parrot, kuwubuwula ngokuphelele ngomqondo ogcwele wegama.

Abafundi bayaphoqeleka ukuthi bafunde izifundo ezinzima futhi bazigcine enkumbulweni yabo ukuze "BADLULISE UNYAKA" futhi kamuva empilweni yangempela lezo zifundo azigcini ngokuba yize kodwa futhi ziyakhohlakala ngoba inkumbulo ayithembekile.

Abafana bafunda ngenhloso yokuthola umsebenzi nokuziphilisa futhi kamuva uma benenhlanhla yokuthola lowo msebenzi, uma beba ngochwepheshe, odokotela, abameli, njll., okuwukuphela kwento abayitholayo ukuyiphinda indaba efanayo njengangaphambili, bayashada, bayahlupheka, baba nezingane futhi bafa ngaphandle kokuvuka ukuqwashisa, bafa ngaphandle kokuqwashisa ngempilo yabo. Yilokho kuphela.

Amantombazane ayashada, akha amakhaya awo, aba nezingane, axabana nomakhelwane, nomyeni, nezingane, ayahlukana futhi aphinde ashade, aba ngabafelokazi, aguga, njll. ekugcineni afe ngemva kokuphila ELELE, ENGANAKILE, ephinda njengenjwayelo IDRAMA ELIBUHLUNGU elifanayo lokuba khona.

Othisha abafuni ukuqaphela ngokuphelele ukuthi bonke abantu banokuqwashisa okulele. Kuyaphuthuma ukuthi othisha nabo bavuke ukuze bakwazi ukuvusa abafundi.

Akusizi ngalutho ukugcwalisa amakhanda ethu ngemibono-ngqondo nokunye okuningi futhi sicaphune uDante, uHomer; uVirgil, njll., uma sinokuqwashisa okulele uma singenakho ukuqwashisa okuyiqiniso, okucacile futhi okuphelele ngathi, ngezifundo esizifundayo, ngempilo yangempela.

Iyasizani imfundo uma singazenzi abaqambi, abaqaphile, abahlakaniphile ngempela?

Imfundo yeqiniso ayiqukethe ukwazi ukufunda nokubhala. Noma yimuphi umuntu oyisiphukuphuku, noma yimuphi umuntu oyisilima angakwazi ukufunda nokubhala.

Sidinga ukuba SIHLAKANIPHE futhi UBUHLAKANI buvuka kuphela kithi lapho Ukuqwashisa kuvuka.

Isintu sinamaphesenti angamashumi ayisishiyagalolunye nesikhombisa se-SUBCONSCIOUSNESS namaphesenti amathathu OKUQAPHELA.

Sidinga ukuvusa Ukuqwashisa, sidinga ukuguqula i-SUBCONSCIOUS ibe yiqaphile. Sidinga ukuba namaphesenti ayikhulu okuqwashisa.

Umuntu akagcini nje ngokuphupha lapho umzimba wakhe uyenga, kodwa futhi uyaphupha lapho umzimba wakhe ungalali, lapho esesimeni sokuphaphama.

Kuyadingeka ukuyeka ukuphupha, kuyadingeka ukuvusa ukuqwashisa futhi le nqubo yokuvuka kufanele iqale ekhaya nasesikoleni.

Umzamo wothisha kufanele uqondiswe EKUQWASHISENI abafundi futhi hhayi enkumbulweni kuphela.

Abafundi kufanele bafunde ukuzicabangelela futhi bangagcini nje ngokuphinda imibono-ngqondo yabanye njengama-parrot noma ama-parakeet.

Othisha kufanele balwele ukukhipha ukwesaba kubafundi.

Othisha kufanele bavumele abafundi, inkululeko yokungavumelani nokugxeka kahle nangendlela eyakhayo yonke imibono-ngqondo abayifundayo.

Akunangqondo ukubaphoqelela ukuthi bamukele ngendlela YE-DOGMATIC yonke imibono-ngqondo efundiswa esikoleni, ekolishi noma eyunivesithi.

Kuyadingeka ukuthi abafundi bayeke ukwesaba ukuze bafunde ukuzicabangelela. Kuyaphuthuma ukuthi abafundi bayeke ukwesaba ukuze bakwazi ukuhlaziya imibono-ngqondo abayifundayo.

Ukwesaba kungenye yezithiyo zobuhlakani. Umfundi owesabayo AKALINGE ukungavumelani futhi wamukela njengesihloko SOKUKHOLWA OKUYIMPUMPUTHE, konke okushiwo ngababhali abahlukene.

Akusizi ngalutho ukuthi othisha bakhulume ngobuqhawe uma bona ngokwabo besaba. Othisha kufanele bakhululeke ekwesabeni. Othisha abesaba ukugxekwa, lokho okuzoshiwo, njll., ABAKWAZI ukuba bahlakaniphe ngempela.

Inhloso yeqiniso yemfundo kufanele kube ukuqeda ukwesaba nokuvusa ukuqwashisa.

Kusizani ukudlulisa izivivinyo uma siqhubeka nokwesaba nokunganaki?

Othisha banesibopho sokusiza abafundi kusukela emabhentshini esikole ukuthi babe usizo empilweni, kodwa uma nje kukhona ukwesaba akekho ongasiza empilweni.

Umuntu ogcwele ukwesaba akalinge ukungavumelani nombono womunye.

Umuntu ogcwele ukwesaba akanakho ukuzenzela.

Kungumsebenzi wawo wonke uthisha, ngokusobala, ukusiza wonke umfundi ngamunye esikoleni sakhe ukuthi akhululeke ngokuphelele ekwesabeni, ukuze akwazi ukwenza ngokuzenzakalelayo ngaphandle kwesidingo sokutshelwa, sokuthunywa.

Kuyaphuthuma ukuthi abafundi bayeke ukwesaba ukuze bakwazi ukuba nokuzenzela okuzenzakalelayo nokudala.

Lapho abafundi ngokuzithandela, ngokukhululeka nangokuzenzakalelayo bekwazi ukuhlaziya nokugxeka ngokukhululeka leyo mibono-ngqondo abayifundayo, bayobe sebeseyeka ukuba yizinto zemishini, ezingagxili futhi eziyisiphukuphuku nje.

Kuyaphuthuma ukuthi kube khona ukuzenzela okukhululekile ukuze kuvele ubuhlakani bokudala kubafundi besilisa nabesifazane.

Kuyadingeka ukunikeza inkululeko YOKUVEZA OKUDALAYO okuzenzakalelayo nokungacindezelwa kwanoma yiluphi uhlobo, kubo bonke abafundi besilisa nabesifazane ukuze bakwazi ukuqaphela lokho abakufundayo.

Amandla okudala akhululekile angavezwa kuphela lapho singesabi ukugxekwa, lokho okuzoshiwo, ubushiqela bothisha, imithetho njll njll njll.

Ingqondo yomuntu ilinyazwe ukwesaba nokukholelwa izinto ngaphandle kobufakazi futhi KUYAPHUTHUMA ukuyivuselela ngokuzenzela okuzenzakalelayo nokukhululekile ekwesabeni.

Sidinga ukuziqaphela impilo yethu siqu futhi le nqubo yokuvuka kufanele iqale kusukela emabhentshini esikole ngokwawo.

Imfundo ngeke isisize lutho uma siphuma kuyo singanakile futhi silele.

Ukuqedwa kokwesaba nokuzenzela kuzoholela esenzweni esizenzakalelayo nesimsulwa.

Ngokuzenzela okukhululekile abafundi besilisa nabesifazane kufanele babe nelungelo kuzo zonke izikole ukuxoxa emhlanganweni yonke imibono-ngqondo abayifundayo.

Kungale ndlela kuphela, ngokukhululwa ekwesabeni nangenkululeko yokuxoxa, ukuhlaziya, UKUCABANGELA, nokugxeka kahle lokho esikufundayo, singenza ngalezo zifundo futhi hhayi nje ama-parrot noma ama-parakeet aphinda lokho esikuqoqayo enkumbulweni.
