Ngaphandle kwabantu abakhubazeke ngokuphelele, wonke umuntu kufanele abe nokuthile akwenzelayo empilweni, ubunzima busekwazini ukuthi umuntu ngamunye ulungele ini.

Uma kukhona into ebaluleke ngempela kulo mhlaba, ukuzazi, kuyivelakancane lowo ozaziyo futhi noma kungabonakala kuyamangalisa, kunzima ukuthola empilweni umuntu osemnkantshubomvu ngomsebenzi wakhe.

Uma umuntu eqiniseke ngokugcwele ngendima okufanele ayidlale ekubeni khona, khona-ke wenza umsebenzi wakhe wokuziphilisa ube wubutumeli, inkolo, futhi empeleni nangezizathu ezifanele uba umphostoli wesintu.

Lowo owaziyo umsebenzi wakhe wokuziphilisa noma lowo owuthola ngokwakhe, ubhekana noshintsho olukhulu, akasafuni impumelelo, akamkhathazi kakhulu imali, udumo, ukubonga, injabulo yakhe isekujabuleni okulethwa ukusabela obizweni olusondele, olujulile, olungaziwa lweqiniso lakhe langaphakathi.

Okuthakazelisa kakhulu kukho konke lokhu ukuthi umqondo WOMSEBENZI WOKUZIPHILISA awuhlangene neze noBUYEBO, ngoba noma kungabonakala kuyaxaka, UBUYEBO buyawuzonda umsebenzi wethu wokuziphilisa ngoba UBUYEBO bufisa kuphela imali engenayo enenzuzo, isikhundla, udumo, njll.

Umqondo WOMSEBENZI WOKUZIPHILISA, yinto engokWETHU isiqu SANGAPHAKATHI; kuyinto engaphakathi kakhulu, ejulile kakhulu, esondele kakhulu.

Umqondo womsebenzi wokuziphilisa uholela umuntu ukuba aqalise ngobuqotho obukhulu nangenhloso yangempela izinkampani ezimbi kakhulu ngazo zonke izinhlobo zokuhlupheka nokuhlushwa. Ngakho-ke kuvamile ukuthi UBUYEBO buzonde umsebenzi wangempela wokuziphilisa.

Umqondo WOMSEBENZI WOKUZIPHILISA empeleni usiyisa endleleni yobuqhawe obusemthethweni, noma kufanele sibekezelele ngobuqotho zonke izinhlobo zokuhlambalaza, ukukhaphela nokunyundela.

Ngosuku umuntu angakwazi ukusho iqiniso "NGIYAZI UKUTHI NGINGUBANI FUTHI YINI UMSEBENZI WAMI WANGEMPELA WOKUZIPHILISA" kusukela ngaleso sikhathi uzoqala ukuphila ngokulunga neqiniso nothando. Umuntu onjalo uphila emsebenzini wakhe futhi umsebenzi wakhe uphila kuye.

Eqinisweni bambalwa kakhulu abantu abangakwazi ukukhuluma kanje, ngobuqotho beqiniso benhliziyo. Labo abakhuluma kanjalo bakhethiwe labo abanawo umqondo WOMSEBENZI WOKUZIPHILISA ngezinga elidlulele.

Ukuthola noma ukuthola umsebenzi wethu wangempela ngamunye wokuziphilisa, empeleni kufana nokuthola ingcebo eyigugu kakhulu.

Lapho isakhamuzi sithola ngokuqiniseka okuphelele nangaphandle kokungabaza umsebenzi waso weqiniso nosemthethweni, siba NGUMUNTU ONGENAKUTHATHWA umsebenzi ngalowo mcimbi nje.

Lapho umsebenzi wethu wokuziphilisa uhambisana ngokuphelele nangesimo esiphelele nendawo esiyithathayo empilweni, khona-ke sisebenzisa umsebenzi wethu njengobutumeli beqiniso, ngaphandle kobugovu futhi ngaphandle kwesifiso samandla.

Khona-ke umsebenzi esikhundleni sokusidalela ubugovu, isithukuthezi noma izifiso zokushintsha umsebenzi, usilethela injabulo yangempela, ejulile, esondele noma kufanele sibekezelele ngesineke izinhlungu ezibuhlungu.
