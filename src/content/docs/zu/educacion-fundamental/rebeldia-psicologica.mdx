Labo bonke abaye bazinikela ekuhambeni kuwo wonke amazwe omhlaba ngenjongo yokufunda ngokuningiliziwe zonke izinhlanga zabantu, bakwazile ukuzibonela ngokwabo ukuthi imvelo yalesi SILWANE esihlakaniphile esibizwa ngokuyiphutha ngokuthi umuntu, ihlala ifana njalo, kungakhathaliseki ukuthi iseYurophu endala noma e-Afrika ekhathele yobugqila obungaka, ezweni elingcwele lamaVeda noma eNtshonalanga Indies, e-Austria noma eChina.

Leli qiniso eliqandayo, leli qiniso elikhulu elimangaza wonke umuntu ofundayo, lingaqinisekiswa ngokukhethekile uma isihambi sivakashela Izikole, Amakolishi namaNyuvesi.

Sesifikile esikhathini sokukhiqiza ngobuningi. Manje yonke into ikhiqizwa ngokulandelana futhi ngezinga elikhulu. Uchungechunge lwezindiza, Izimoto, Izimpahla Zikanokusho, njll, njll, njll.

Noma kungase kuzwakale kuhlekisa, kuyiqiniso ukuthi Izikole Zamakhono, AmaNyuvesi, njll. nazo seziphenduke izimboni zobuhlakani zokukhiqiza ngobuningi.

Kulezi zinsuku zokukhiqiza ngobuningi, okuwukuphela komgomo ekuphileni ukuthola ukulondeka kwezezimali. Abantu bayesaba yonke into futhi bafuna ukuphepha.

Ukucabanga okuzimele kulezi zikhathi zokukhiqiza ngobuningi, sekucishe kwangenzeka ngoba uhlobo lwesimanje Lwezemfundo lusekelwe ekululameni nje.

"IGagasi Elisha" liphila ngokwaneliseka kakhulu ngalesi simo esiphakathi nendawo ngokwengqondo. Uma umuntu efuna ukuhluka, ehlukile kwabanye, wonke umuntu uyamhlambalaza, wonke umuntu uyamgxeka, uyahlukunyezwa, uyenqatshelwa umsebenzi, njll.

Isifiso sokuthola imali yokuziphilisa nokuzijabulisa, isidingo esiphuthumayo sokuthola impumelelo ekuphileni, ukufuna ukuphepha, kwezezimali, isifiso sokuthenga izinto eziningi ukuze sibukise kwabanye, njll., kubeka umkhawulo ekucabangeni okumsulwa, okungokwemvelo nokuzenzakalelayo.

Kuye kwaqinisekiswa ngokuphelele ukuthi ukwesaba kwenza ingqondo ibe buthuntu futhi kuqinise inhliziyo.

Kulezi zinsuku zokwesaba okukhulu nokufuna ukuphepha, abantu bayazifihla emihumeni yabo, emihumeni yabo, ekhoneni labo, endaweni lapho bekholelwa ukuthi bangaba nokuvikeleka okukhulu, izinkinga ezimbalwa futhi abafuni ukuphuma lapho, besaba impilo, besaba uhambo olusha, okuhlangenwe nakho okusha, njll., njll., njll.

Yonke le mfundo yesimanje ENGXENYWAYO isekelwe ekwesabeni nasekufuneni ukuphepha, abantu bethukile, besaba ngisho nomthunzi wabo.

Abantu, besaba yonke into, besaba ukuphuma emithethweni emidala esunguliwe, ukuhluka kwabanye abantu, ukucabanga ngendlela yezinguquko, ukwephula konke ukucwaswa koMphakathi owohlokayo, njll.

Ngenhlanhla, kukhona abambalwa abaqotho nabaqondayo emhlabeni, abafisa ngempela ukuhlolisisa zonke izinkinga zengqondo, kodwa iningi lethu alinayo ngisho nomoya wokungavumelani nokuvukela.

Kunezinhlobo ezimbili ZOKUVUKELA osekuhlukaniswe ngazo ngokufanele. Okokuqala: Ukuvukela Kwengqondo okunobudlova. Okwesibili: Ukuvukela Kwengqondo okujulile KOBULUNGISELELI.

Uhlobo lokuqala Lokuvukela lusabela ngokuqinile, luyalondolozeka futhi luyabambezela. Uhlobo lwesibili Lokuvukela LUYINGUQUKO.

Ohlotsheni lokuqala Lokuvukela Kwengqondo sithola UMSHINTSHI olungisa izingubo ezindala futhi alungise izindonga zezakhiwo ezindala ukuze zingawi, uhlobo olubuyela emuva, uMguquli wegazi notshwala obuqinile, umholi wezempi kanye nokuketulwa kukahulumeni, indoda ephethe isibhamu ehlombe, uMbusi othokozela ukubeka odongeni bonke labo abangazamukeli izinkanuko zakhe, imibono yakhe.

Ohlotsheni lwesibili Lokuvukela Kwengqondo sithola uBUDDHA, uJESU, uHERMES, umguquli, ISIHLUHLI ESIHLAKANIPHILE, umuntu ONOMBONO, amaQHAWE amakhulu ENGUQUKO YOKWAZI, njll., njll., njll.

Labo abazifundisa kuphela ngenhloso engenangqondo yokukhuphukela ezikhundleni ezinhle kakhulu ngaphakathi komgogodla wokulawula, ukukhuphuka, ukugibela phezulu esitebhisini, ukuzenza bazizwe, njll., abanakho ukujula kweqiniso, Bayizilima ngokwemvelo, abangajulile, abangenalutho, amaphesenti ayikhulu abaqhwagi.

Sekuye kwafakazelwa ngokwanele ukuthi lapho kungekho ukuhlanganiswa kweqiniso komcabango nomuzwa kumuntu, ngisho noma sithole imfundo enkulu, impilo iba engaphelele, iyaphikisana, iyisicefe futhi ihlushwa ukwesaba okungenakubalwa kwazo zonke izinhlobo.

Ngaphandle kokungabaza nanoma yikuphi ukwesaba ukwenza iphutha, singaqinisekisa ngokugcizelela ukuthi ngaphandle kwemfundo EHLANGANISIWE, impilo iba yingozi, ayisizi futhi iyingozi.

ISILWANE ESIHLAKANIPHILE sinomuntu OYEDWA WANGAPHAKATHI owakhiwe ngokudabukisayo IZINTO ezihlukene eziqiniswa YIMFUNDO EPHUTHILE.

UMUNTU OBUNINGI sonke esinaye ngaphakathi, uyimbangela eyinhloko yazo zonke izinkinga zethu nokuphikisana.

IMFUNDO EYISEKELO kufanele ifundise izizukulwane ezintsha iDIDACTIC yethu yengqondo YOKUCHITHWA KOMUNTU.

Kungokuchitha kuphela izinhlangano ezihlukahlukene ezakha i-Ego (UMUNTU) lapho singazimisela khona isikhungo esihlala njalo sokwazi komuntu ngaphakathi kwethu, ngakho-ke siyoba NQOTHILE.

Ngenkathi UMUNTU OBUNINGI esakhona ngaphakathi kwethu ngamunye, ngeke nje sizikhathaze impilo kodwa futhi sizokhathaza abanye.

Kusizani ukuthi sifunde umthetho futhi sibe abameli, uma siqhubeka nezimpi? Kusizani ukunqwabelanisa ulwazi oluningi ezingqondweni zethu, uma siqhubeka nokudideka? Zisiza ngani izakhono zobuchwepheshe nezimboni uma sizisebenzisa ukubhujiswa kontanga yethu?

Akusizi ngalutho ukuzifundisa, ukuya emakilasini, ukufunda, uma ngenqubo yokuphila kwansuku zonke sizibhubhisa kabi.

Umgomo wemfundo akufanele ube ukukhiqiza kuphela abafuna imisebenzi abasha minyaka yonke, uhlobo olusha lwabaqhwagi, izilima ezintsha ezingazi nokuthi zihloniphe inkolo yomakhelwane, njll.

Umgomo weqiniso WEZEMFUNDO EYISEKELO kufanele kube ukudala amadoda nabesifazane abaqinisekile BEHLANGANISIWE ngakho-ke abaqondayo nabahlakaniphile.

Ngeshwa, Othisha nabafundisi Bezikole, Amakolishi namaNyuvesi, bonke bayacabanga, ngaphandle kokuvusa UHLAKANIPHO OLUPHELELE LWABAFUNDI.

Noma yimuphi umuntu angahawukela futhi athole iziqu, izindondo, ama-diploma futhi aze aphumelele kakhulu emkhakheni wemishini wokuphila, kodwa lokhu akusho ukuthi uhlakaniphile.

UHLAKANIPHO alusoze lwaba ukusebenza kwemishini, UHLAKANIPHO alukwazi ukuba umphumela wolwazi olulula lwezincwadi, UHLAKANIPHO akulona ikhono lokusabela ngokuzenzakalelayo ngamazwi acwebezelayo kunoma yiziphi izinselele. UHLAKANIPHO akulona ukukhuluma nje ngenkumbulo. UHLAKANIPHO yikhono lokuthola ngqo UMQONDO, OKUYIQINISO, lokho OKUYIQINISO ngempela.

IMFUNDO EYISEKELO isayensi esivumela ukuthi sivuse leli khono kithi nakwabanye.

IMFUNDO EYISEKELO isiza UMNTU ngamunye ukuthola IZIMISO zeqiniso ezivela njengomphumela wophenyo olujulile NOKUQONDISISA OKUPHELELE KWAKHE.

Lapho kungekho ukuzazi kithi, khona-ke UKUZIVEZA kuphenduka UKUQINISEKISA NGOKUQHATHA NOKUBHUJISAYO.

IMFUNDO EYISEKELO ikhathazeka kuphela ngokuvusa kumuntu ngamunye IKHO-NO lokuqonda yena kuzo zonke izindawo zengqondo hhayi nje ukuzinikela ekuzithokoziseni kokuziveza ngendlela engafanele komuntu OBUNINGI.
