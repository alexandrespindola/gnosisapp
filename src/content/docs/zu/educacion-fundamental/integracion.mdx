Esinye sezifiso ezinkulu ze-psychology ukufinyelela kuKUHLANGANISWA OKUPHELELE.

Ukuba i-YO ibiyililodwa, inkinga ye-INTEGRATION YE-PSYCHOLOGICAL ibizoxazululwa kalula kakhulu, kodwa ngeshwa lomhlaba i-YO ikhona ngaphakathi komuntu ngamunye ngendlela ye-PLURALIZED.

I-YO PLURALIZED iyimbangela eyinhloko yazo zonke izinkinga zethu zangaphakathi.

Ukuba besingazibona esibukweni somzimba wonke njengoba sinjalo NGOKWENGQONDO ngazo zonke izinkinga zethu zangaphakathi, besingafinyelela esiphethweni esibuhlungu sokuthi asikabi nabo ubuntu beqiniso.

Umzimba womuntu uwumshini omuhle olawulwa yi-YO PLURALIZED efundwa ngokujulile yi-PSYCHOLOGY ENGUQUKO.

Isicebi siyabamonela abacebile futhi sifuna ukuceba nakakhulu. Abampofu bayabamonela abacebile futhi bafuna ukuba abacebile nabo. Obhalayo uyabamonela obhalayo futhi ufuna ukubhala kangcono. Onolwazi oluningi uyabamonela onokuhlangenwe nakho okukhulu futhi ufisa ukuba nokuningi kunalowo.

Abantu abaneliseki ngesinkwa, indawo yokukhosela.
