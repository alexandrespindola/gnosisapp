Kubalulekile ngokushesha ukuqonda ngokujulile nangazo zonke izindawo zengqondo, lokho okuyikho ngempela UKUFA ngokwako, kuphela ngaleyo ndlela lapho kungenzeka khona, ngempela ukuqonda ngokuphelele lokho okuyikho ukungafi.

Ukubona umzimba womuntu othandekayo uphakathi kwebhokisi, akusho ukuthi usuwuqondile imfihlakalo yokufa.

Iqiniso yinto engaziwa umzuzu nomzuzu. Iqiniso mayelana nokufa alikwazi ukuba yinto ehlukile.

UBUYE buba njalo, njengoba kungokwemvelo, isiqinisekiso sokufa, isiqinisekiso esingeziwe, igunya elithile elizoqinisekisa ukuthi sithola isikhundla esihle nanoma yiluphi uhlobo lokungafi ngale kwethuna elishaqisayo.

MINA NGINGAMI anginayo intshisekelo enkulu yokufa. UBUYE bufuna ukuqhubeka. UBUYE besaba kakhulu ukufa.

IQINISO aliyona into yokukholwa noma yokungabaza. Iqiniso alihlangene nokukholwa, noma nokungabaza. Iqiniso aliyona into yemibono, imibono-ngqondo, imibono, imiqondo, imicabango esengaphambili, izinto ezicatshangwayo, ubandlululo, iziqinisekiso, izingxoxo, njll. Iqiniso mayelana nemfihlakalo yoKufa aliyona into ehlukile.

Iqiniso mayelana nemfihlakalo yokufa lingaziwa kuphela ngokusebenzisa isipiliyoni esiqondile.

Kuyinto engenakwenzeka ukuxhumana nesipiliyoni SANGEMPELA sokufa kulowo ongasazi.

Noma yimuphi imbongi angabhala izincwadi ezinhle ZOTHANDO, kepha kuyinto engenakwenzeka ukuxhumana ne-IQINISO mayelana NOTHANDO kubantu abangakaze balubone, ngendlela efanayo sithi akunakwenzeka ukuxhumana iqiniso mayelana nokufa kubantu, abangazange bakubone.

Noma ngubani ofuna ukwazi iqiniso mayelana nokufa kufanele aphenye, azame yena mathupha, afune ngendlela efanele, kuphela ngaleyo ndlela lapho singathola khona incazelo ejulile yokufa.

Ukubheka nesipiliyoni seminyaka eminingi kusenze saqonda ukuthi abantu abanaso isifiso sokuqonda ngempela incazelo ejulile yokufa; abantu into kuphela abanentshisekelo ngayo ngempela ukuqhubeka ngale futhi yilokho kuphela.

Abantu abaningi bafisa ukuqhubeka ngezinto ezibonakalayo, isithunzi, umndeni, izinkolelo, imibono, izingane, njll., futhi lapho beqonda ukuthi noma yiluphi uhlobo lokuqhubeka kwengqondo luyize, luyadlula, luyashesha, aluzinzile, khona-ke bezizwa bengenazo iziqinisekiso, bengavikelekile, bayethuka, bayesaba, bagcwaliswa ukwesaba okungapheli.

Abafuni ukuqonda abantu abampofu, abafuni ukuqonda ukuthi konke okuqhubekayo kwenzeka ngokuhamba kwesikhathi.

Abafuni ukuqonda abantu abampofu ukuthi konke okuqhubekayo kuyancipha ngokuhamba kwesikhathi.

Abafuni ukuqonda abantu abampofu ukuthi konke okuqhubekayo kuba yinto eyenziwa ngomshini, into eyenziwa njalo, into eyisicefe.

Kuyinto ephuthumayo, kuyadingeka, kubalulekile, ukuzenza siqaphele ngokugcwele incazelo ejulile yokufa, kuphela ngaleyo ndlela lapho ukwesaba ukuyeka ukuba khona kuphela kunyamalala.

Ngokubheka ngokucophelela isintu, singaqinisekisa ukuthi ingqondo ihlala ivaleleke kulokho okwaziwayo futhi ifuna ukuthi lokho okwaziwayo kuqhubeke ngale kwethuna.

Ingqondo evaleleke kulokho okwaziwayo, ayisoze yakwazi ukuzwa lokho okungaziwa, okwangempela, okuyiqiniso.

Ngokwephula kuphela ibhodlela lesikhathi ngokuzindla okufanele, singakwazi ukuzwa OKUPHAKADE, OKUNGENASIKHATHI, OKWANGEMPELA.

Labo abafisa ukuqhubeka bayakwesaba ukufa futhi izinkolelo zabo kanye nemibono yabo zibasiza kuphela njengesidambisa-zinhlungu.

Ukufa ngokwako akunalutho olwesabekayo, kuyinto enhle kakhulu, ephakeme, engenakuvezwa, kepha ingqondo evalelekile: kulokho okwaziwayo, ihamba kuphela ngaphakathi kwesiyingi esiyingozi esisuka ekukholweni siye ekungabazeni.

Lapho ngempela siba siqaphele ngokugcwele incazelo ejulile nebanzi yokufa, sithola khona ngokwethu ngokusebenzisa isipiliyoni esiqondile, ukuthi iMpilo nokufa kwakha yonke into ephelele, i-uni-total.

Ukufa kuyindawo yokugcina iMpilo. Indlela yeMpilo yakhiwe ngezinyathelo zamahhashi okufa.

Impilo ingamandla anqunyiwe nanqumayo. Kusukela ekuzalweni kuya ekufeni izinhlobo ezahlukene zamandla zigobhoza ngaphakathi komzimba womuntu.

Uhlobo olulodwa kuphela lwamandla umzimba womuntu ongakwazi ukumelana nalo, yi-RAY YOkufa. Le ray inamandla kagesi aphezulu kakhulu. Umzimba womuntu awukwazi ukumelana namandla amakhulu kangaka.

Njengoba nje umbani ungakwazi ukunqamula isihlahla, kanjalo ne-ray yokufa ngokugobhoza ngomzimba womuntu, iwucekela phansi ngokungenakugwenywa.

I-ray yokufa ixhumanisa isenzakalo sokufa, naleso sokuzalwa.

I-ray yokufa ibangela ukungezwani kukagesi okuseduze kakhulu kanye nenothi elithile eliyinhloko elinamandla, elinqumayo lokuhlanganisa izakhi zofuzo ngaphakathi kweqanda elivundile.

I-ray yokufa inciphisa umzimba womuntu ezingxenyeni zawo eziyinhloko.

I-EGO, i-BUYE enamandla, iyaqhubeka kuzozalo lwethu ngeshwa.

Lokho okuyiqiniso mayelana nokufa, lokho okuyisikhathi esiphakathi kokufa nokukhulelwa kuyinto engeyona eyesikhathi nokuthi kuphela ngesayensi yokuzindla lapho singakwazi ukuzizwela khona.

Othisha kanye Noothisha Bezikole, Amakolishi kanye Neminyuziyamu, kufanele bafundise abafundi babo nabo ukuthi bangayithola kanjani indlela eholela ekuboneni OKWANGEMPELA, OKUYIQINISO.
