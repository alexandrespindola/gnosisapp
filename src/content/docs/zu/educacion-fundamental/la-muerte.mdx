Kubalulekile ngokushesha ukuthi siqonde ngokujulile nangazo zonke izici zengqondo, ukuthi yini ngempela UKUFA ngokwako, kuphela ngaleyo ndlela lapho kungenzeka ngempela ukuthi siqonde ngokuphelele ukuthi kuyini ukungafi.

Ukubona umzimba womuntu omthandayo uphakathi kwebhokisi, akusho ukuthi ubuqonde imfihlakalo yokufa.

Iqiniso yinto engaziwa njalo ngomzuzu. Iqiniso ngokufa alikwazi ukuba ngaphandle.

Umuntu UYENA uhlale efuna, njengoba kuvamile, umshwalense wokufa, isiqinisekiso esingeziwe, igunya elizoba nesibopho sokuqinisekisa isikhundla esihle kanye nanoma yiluphi uhlobo lokungafi ngale kwethuna elisabekayo.

UMINA AKAZIMISELE ukufa. UMUNTU UYENA ufuna ukuqhubeka. UMUNTU UYENA uyesaba kakhulu ukufa.

IQINISO akuyona indaba yokukholwa noma yokungabaza. Iqiniso alihlangene nakancane nokukholwa, noma nokungabaza. Iqiniso aliyona indaba yemibono, imibono, imibono, imiqondo, imiqondo eyandulelayo, iziphakamiso, ukucwaswa, izitatimende, izingxoxo, njll. Iqiniso ngemfihlakalo yokufa aliwona umthetho ohlukile.

Iqiniso ngemfihlakalo yokufa lingaziwa kuphela ngokusebenzisa isipiliyoni esiqondile.

Kuba nzima ukuxhumana nesipiliyoni sangempela sokufa kulowo ongasilazi.

Noma yimuphi imbongi angabhala izincwadi ezinhle ZOTHANDO, kodwa kuba nzima ukuxhumana NEQINISO mayelana NOTHANDO kubantu abangakaze baluzwe, ngendlela efanayo sithi akunakwenzeka ukuxhumana iqiniso ngokufa kubantu, abangakaze baluzwe.

Noma ubani ofuna ukwazi iqiniso ngokufa kufanele aphenye, azizwele yena, afune ngendlela efanele, kuphela ngaleyo ndlela lapho singathola khona incazelo ejulile yokufa.

Ukubheka kanye nesipiliyoni seminyaka eminingi kusivumele ukuba siqonde ukuthi abantu abanandaba nokuqonda ngempela incazelo ejulile yokufa; into kuphela abantu abanendaba nayo ngempela ukuqhubeka ngale kwalokho futhi yilokho kuphela.

Abantu abaningi bafisa ukuqhubeka ngezinto ezibonakalayo, isithunzi, umndeni, izinkolelo, imibono, izingane, njll., futhi lapho beqonda ukuthi noma yiluphi uhlobo lokuqhubeka ngokwengqondo luyize, luyadlula, luyashabalala, aluzinzile, bese bezizwa bengenazo iziqinisekiso, bephephile, bayesaba, bayathuka, bagcwaliswa ukwesaba okungapheli.

Abafuni ukuqonda abantu abampofu, abafuni ukuqonda ukuthi konke okuqhubekayo kwenzeka ngokuhamba kwesikhathi.

Abafuni ukuqonda abantu abampofu ukuthi konke okuqhubekayo kuyabola ngokuhamba kwesikhathi.

Abafuni ukuqonda abantu abampofu ukuthi konke okuqhubekayo kuba yimishini, okujwayelekile, okuyisicefe.

Kuphuthuma, kuyadingeka, kubalulekile, ukuthi siziqaphele ngokugcwele incazelo ejulile yokufa, kuphela ngaleyo ndlela lapho ukwesaba ukuyeka ukuba khona kunyamalala khona.

Ngokubheka ngokucophelela isintu, singaqinisekisa ukuthi ingqondo ihlale ivalelwe kokwaziwayo futhi ifuna ukuthi lokho okwaziwayo kuqhubeke ngale kwethuna.

Ingqondo evalelwe kokwaziwayo, ayisoze yakwazi ukuzwa okungaziwa, okungokoqobo, iqiniso.

Kuphela ngokuphula ibhodlela lesikhathi ngokuzindla okuyikho, lapho singakwazi khona ukuzwa OKUPHAKADE, OKUNGAPHELI, OKUNGOQOBO.

Labo abafisa ukuqhubeka bayakwesaba ukufa futhi izinkolelo zabo kanye nemibono yabo zibasebenzela kuphela njengesidambisa-zinhlungu.

Ukufa ngokwako akunalutho olwesabekayo, kuyinto enhle kakhulu, ephakeme, engenakuvezwa, kodwa ingqondo evalelwe: kokwaziwayo, ihlale izungeza ngaphakathi komjikelezo oyindilinga ovela ekukholweni kuya ekungabazeni.

Lapho siziqaphela ngempela ngokugcwele incazelo ejulile nenjulile yokufa, sibe sesithola ngokwethu ngokusebenzisa isipiliyoni esiqondile, ukuthi Impilo nokufa kuyakhela ubunye obuphelele, obuphelele.

Ukufa yindawo yokugcina impilo. Indlela yempilo yakhiwe ngezinyathelo zamahhashi okufa.

Impilo Ingamandla anqunyiwe nanqumayo. Kusukela ekuzalweni kuze kufike ekufeni izinhlobo ezahlukene zamandla zigobhoza ngaphakathi komzimba womuntu.

Uhlobo olulodwa kuphela lwamandla umzimba womuntu ongenakukwazi ukumelana nalo, i-RAY YOKUFA. Le ray inamandla kagesi aphezulu kakhulu. Umzimba womuntu awukwazi ukumelana nalowo mandla.

Njengoba nje umbani ungawuqhekeza umuthi, kanjalo ne-ray yokufa lapho igeleza ngomzimba womuntu, iyawubhubhisa ngokungenakugwemeka.

I-ray yokufa ixhumanisa isenzakalo sokufa, nesenzakalo sokuzalwa.

I-ray yokufa idala ukungezwani kukagesi okujulile kakhulu kanye nenothi elithile eliyisihluthulelo elinamandla, elinqumayo okuhlanganisa izakhi zofuzo ngaphakathi kweqanda elivundile.

I-ray yokufa inciphisa umzimba womuntu ube yizakhi zawo eziyisisekelo.

I-EGO, UMUNTU ongumuntu wamandla, uyaqhubeka kubazukulu bethu ngeshwa.

Ukuthi iyini iqiniso ngokufa, ukuthi yisiphi isikhawu esiphakathi kokufa nokukhulelwa kuyinto engahambisani nesikhathi nokuthi kuphela ngesayensi yokuzindla lapho singakwazi khona ukuzwa.

Othisha Nabafundisi Bezikole, Amakolishi Namanyuvesi, kufanele bafundise abafundi babo nendlela eholela esipiliyoni SALOKHO OYIKHO, SEQINISO.
