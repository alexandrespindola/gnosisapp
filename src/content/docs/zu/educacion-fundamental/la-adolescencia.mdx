Sekuyisikhathi sokuthi siyeke unomphela ukuzithoba kwamanga kanye nokucwaswa okuhlobene nenkinga yezocansi.

Kuyadingeka ukuqonda ngokucacile nangokuqondile inkinga yezocansi yentsha yabobubili ubulili.

Uma umntwana eneminyaka eyishumi nane ubudala, amandla ocansi avela emzimbeni womntwana, ayezayo ngenkani ngohlelo lwezinzwa.

Lolu hlobo olukhethekile lwamandla lushintsha umzimba womuntu, lushintsha izwi lomfana futhi lubangele umsebenzi wama-ovari kumuntu wesifazane.

Umzimba womuntu uyifektri yangempela eshintsha izinto eziluhlaza zibe izinto ezincane ezibalulekile.

Ukudla esikuphatha esiswini kudlula ezinguqukweni eziningi kanye nokucwengisiswa kuze kufike ekugcineni kulokho kuthinta okuyisimbi, okuyisiwuketshezi okukhulunywe ngakho nguParacelsus ngegama elithi Ens.-Seminis (iSiqu seSemen)

Leli glasi eliwuketshezi, eliguquguqukayo, elithambile, leli sperma, liqukethe ngaphakathi kwalo, ngendlela engenzeka zonke izinhlamvu zempilo.

I-Gnosticism ibona ku-sperma i-CHAOS lapho impilo ivela khona ngamandla.

Ama-Alchemists amadala ase-Middle Ages afana noParacelsus, Sendivogius, Nicolás Flamel, Raymundo Lulio, njll., afunde ngokuhlonipha okujulile i-ENS-SEMINIS noma i-mercury yefilosofi eyimfihlo.

Le VITRIOLO, iyisibulala-zinambuzane seqiniso esenziwe ngobuhlakani, ngemvelo ngaphakathi kwe-seminal vesicles.

Kule mercury yokuhlakanipha kwasendulo, kuleli semen, kutholakala ngempela wonke amathuba okuba khona.

Kuyadabukisa ukuthi intsha eningi ngenxa yokuntuleka kokuqondiswa kwengqondo kwangempela izithoba emkhubeni wokushaya indlwabu noma iphambuke ngendlela edabukisayo ngendlela engaphansi ngokocansi yobungqingili.

Izingane nentsha banikezwa ulwazi lobuhlakani ngezihloko eziningi futhi bafakwa endleleni yezemidlalo, ukusetshenziswa kabi okufushisa impilo ngendlela edabukisayo, kepha ngeshwa lapho amandla ocansi avela okuyiqala intsha, bobabili abazali nemibuthano yesikole, esekelwe ekuhlanzekeni kwamanga nasezimilweni zobuwula, banquma ukuthula ngobugebengu.

Kukhona ukuthula okuyicala futhi kukhona amazwi ahlazisayo. Ukuthula ngenkinga yezocansi kuyicala. Ukukhuluma ngokungeyikho ngenkinga yezocansi nakho kuyicala.

Uma abazali nothisha bethula, abantu abaphambeneyo ngokocansi bayakhuluma futhi izisulu yintsha engenalwazi.

Uma intsha ingakwazi ukuxhumana nabazali noma othisha, izobe isithintana nontanga bayo besikole okungenzeka ukuthi sebevele bephambukile endleleni eyiyo. Umphumela awuthathi isikhathi eside futhi intsha entsha ilandela izeluleko ezingamanga izozithoba emkhubeni wokushaya indlwabu noma iphambuke endleleni yobungqingili.

Umkhuba wokushaya indlwabu ucekela phansi amandla obuchopho. Kuyadingeka ukwazi ukuthi kukhona ubudlelwano obuseduze phakathi kwesemen nobuchopho. Kuyadingeka ukuthi ubuchopho busebenzise isemen. Kuyadingeka ukuthi isemen isebenzise ubuchopho.

Ubuchopho busebenzisa isemen ngokuguqula amandla ocansi, ngokuwaphakamisa, ngokuwaguqula abe amandla obuchopho.

Ngale ndlela isemen isebenzisa ubuchopho futhi ubuchopho busebenzisa isemen.

Isayensi ye-Gnostic ifunda i-endocrinology ngokujulile futhi ifundisa izindlela nezinhlelo zokuguqula amandla ocansi, kepha le yindaba engalingani ngaphakathi kwale ncwadi.

Uma umfundi efuna ulwazi nge-Gnosticism kufanele afunde izincwadi zethu ze-Gnostic futhi angene ezifundweni zethu.

Intsha kufanele iphakamise amandla ocansi ngokuhlakulela umqondo wobuhle, ngokufunda umculo, umbazo, ukudweba, ukwenza uhambo oluya ezintabeni eziphakeme, njll.

Yiziphi izinhlelo ebezingaba zinhle zibuna!

Yiziphi izihloko ezikhethwayo ezinga ziphele! Konke ngenxa yokuntuleka kokumemeza okuyisixwayiso ngesikhathi esifanele.

Umkhuba wokushaya indlwabu, kokubili entsheni nakwabesifazane abancane usuvame kakhulu kunokuwasha izandla.

Izibhedlela zabantu abanesifo sengqondo zigcwele amadoda nabesifazane abacekele phansi ubuchopho babo ngomkhuba wokushaya indlwabu. Isiphetho sabashaya indlwabu yisibhedlela sabantu abanesifo sengqondo.

Umkhuba wobungqingili ubole izimpande zalolu hlanga oludala nolonakele.

Kubonakala kungakholeki ukuthi emazweni afana neNgilandi azazisa ngokuthi azihloniphekile futhi athuthukile, kukhona ama-cinema lapho kuboniswa khona amafilimu ohlobo lobungqingili.

Kubonakala kungakholeki ukuthi kungukuthi eNgilandi lapho sekwenziwa khona imizamo yokwenza ngokusemthethweni imishado yohlobo lobungqingili.

Emadolobheni amakhulu omhlaba kukhona njengamanje izindlu zokudayisa ngomzimba kanye nama-club ohlobo lobungqingili.

Inhlangano ebumnyameni yezitha zabesifazane, namuhla inezinhlangano ezonakele ezimangaza ngobuzalwane bazo obonakele.

Abafundi abaningi bangase bamangale kakhulu ngalokhu "kobuzalwane obonakele" kodwa akufanele sikhohlwe ukuthi kuzo zonke izikhathi zomlando kuye kwaba khona ubuzalwane obuhlukahlukene bobugebengu.

Inhlangano egulayo yezitha zabesifazane, ngaphandle kokungabaza, ingubuzalwane bobugebengu.

Izitha zabesifazane zihlala njalo noma cishe njalo zithatha izikhundla ezibalulekile ngaphakathi kwendlu yezinyosi yeziphathimandla.

Uma umuntu ongumngqingili eya ejele, ngokushesha uyakhululwa ngenxa yethonya elifanele labantu ababalulekile benhlangano yobugebengu.

Uma owesifazane ehluleka, ngokushesha uthola usizo lwezimali kuzo zonke izinhlamvu ezimbi ze-Cofradía del del delito.

Amalungu amnyama obungqingili ayazana ngokugqoka umfaniswano.

Kuyamangalisa ukwazi ukuthi ama-faggots agqoka umfaniswano, kodwa kunjalo. Umfaniswano wabantu abangqingili uhambisana nayo yonke imfashini eqalayo. Abantu abangqingili baqala yonke imfashini entsha. Uma imfashini iba yinto evamile, khona-ke baqala enye. Ngale ndlela umfaniswano wenhlangano yobugebengu uhlala umusha.

Wonke amadolobha amakhulu emhlabeni, namuhla anezigidi zabantu abangqingili.

Umkhuba wobungqingili uqala uhambo lwawo oluyihlazo ngesikhathi sobusha.

Izikole eziningi zentsha yabesilisa nabesifazane ziyizindlu zokudayisa ngomzimba zohlobo lobungqingili.

Izigidi zabesifazane abasebasha bahamba ngokuzimisela ngendlela emnyama yezitha zamadoda.

Izigidi zentsha yabesifazane ingabantu abangqingili. Inhlangano yobugebengu phakathi kobungqingili besifazane iqine njengenhlangano yobugebengu phakathi nobulili besilisa.

Kuphuthuma ukuyeka ngokuphelele nangokungapheli ukuzithoba kwamanga nokukhomba entsheni yabobubili ubulili, ngokuqondile zonke izimfihlakalo zocansi.

Kungale ndlela kuphela izizukulwane ezintsha ezingaqondiswa endleleni YOKUVUSELELWA.
