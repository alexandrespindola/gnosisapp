Othisha bezikole, amakolishi kanye namanyuvesi babeka ukubaluleka okukhulu kakhulu esiyalo futhi kufanele sikufundisise kulesi sahluko ngokucophelela.

Sonke esike saya ezikoleni, emakolishi, emanyuvesi, njll. Siyakwazi kahle ukuthi ziyini iziyalo, imithetho, izibhaxu, ukuthethiswa, njll., njll., njll.

Isiyalo yileso esibizwa ngokuthi UKUTHUTHUKISA UKUMELANA. Othisha bezikole bathanda ukuthuthukisa UKUMELANA.

Sifundiswa ukumelana, ukumisa okuthile ngokumelene nokunye okuthile. Sifundiswa ukumelana nezilingo zenyama futhi siyazishaya futhi sizithobe ukuze simelane.

Sifundiswa UKUMELANA nezilingo ezilethwa ubuvila, izilingo zokungafundi, zokungayi esikoleni, zokudlala, zokuhleka, zokuhleka othisha, zokwephula imithetho, njll., njll., njll.

Othisha banomqondo ongaqondile wokuthi ngesiyalo singaqonda isidingo sokuhlonipha ukuhleleka kwesikole, isidingo sokufunda, ukuziphatha kahle phambi kothisha, ukuziphatha kahle nontanga, njll., njll., njll.

Kukhona phakathi kwabantu umqondo ongaqondile wokuthi lapho simelana kakhulu, lapho senqaba kakhulu, siba abaqondayo ngokwengeziwe, abakhululekile, abagcwele, abanqobayo.

Abantu abafuni ukuqaphela ukuthi lapho silwa kakhulu nokuthile, lapho simelana kakhulu nakho, lapho sikwenqaba kakhulu, yingcosana YEQONDO.

Uma silwa nomkhuba wokuphuza, uzonyamalala isikhashana, kodwa njengoba singawuqondisisanga ngokuphelele kuWO WONKE AMAZINGA ENGQONDO, uzophinda ubuya kamuva lapho singaqaphile futhi siphuzela unyaka wonke ngesikhathi esisodwa.

Uma senqaba umkhuba wobufebe, okwesikhashana siyoba abamsulwa kakhulu ngokubonakala (noma kunjalo KWEZINYE IZINGA ZENGQONDO siyaqhubeka nokuba amaSATIYA amabi njengoba kungabonisa amaphupho ATHONTONGALO kanye nokuchitheka kobusuku), futhi kamuva sizobuyela ngamandla amakhulu ezindleleni zethu zakudala ZABAFEBENI ABANGAPHINDIMISEKI, ngenxa yeqiniso eliqondile lokungakuqondi ngokuphelele ukuthi buyini UBUFEBE.

Baningi abenqaba UKUHWAHWA, abalwa nakho, abaziyala ngokumelene nakho ngokulandela IZIMISO ezithile zokuziphatha, kodwa njengoba bengaliqondanga ngempela lonke inqubo YOKUHWAHWA, empeleni bagcina ngokuthi BAYAHWAHELA ukungahwaheli.

Baningi abaziyala ngokumelene NENTUKUTHELO, abafunda ukumelana nayo, kodwa iyaqhubeka nokuba khona kwamanye amazinga engqondo engaphansi, noma ngabe ngokubonakala isinyamalele esimweni sethu futhi ekunganakeni okuncane kokugada, ingqondo engaphansi iyasikhaphela bese siyaduma futhi sikhanyise imibani sigcwele intukuthelo, lapho singalindele futhi mhlawumbe ngesizathu esingenalo NOKUBUBALULEKA OKUNCANE.

Baningi abaziyala ngokumelene NOMONA futhi ekugcineni bakholelwa ngokuqinile ukuthi sebebuqede kodwa njengoba bengabuqondisisanga kusobala ukuthi buyavela futhi endaweni yesigameko lapho sesicabanga ukuthi bufile kahle.

Kuphela ngokungabikho ngokuphelele kweziyalo, kuphela enkululekweni yangempela, lapho kuvela khona engqondweni, ilangabi elivuthayo LOKUQONDA.

INKULULEKO YOKUDALA ayisoze yaba khona ESAKHETINI. Sidinga inkululeko YOKUQONDA amaphutha ethu ENGQONDO ngendlela EPHELELE.

Sidinga NGOKUPHUTHUMA ukuwisa izindonga futhi siphule amaketanga ensimbi, ukuze sikhululeke.

Kufanele sizitholele mathupha konke lokho Othisha bethu eSikoleni kanye Nabazali bethu abasitshele ukuthi kulungile futhi kuyasiza. Akwanele ukufunda ngekhanda nokulingisa. Sidinga ukuqonda.

Wonke umzamo woThisha kufanele uqondiswe ekuqapheleni kwabafundi. Kufanele bazame ukuthi bangene endleleni YOKUQONDA.

Akukaneli ukutshela abafundi ukuthi kufanele babe yilokhu noma yilokhu, kuyadingeka ukuthi abafundi bafunde ukukhululeka ukuze bakwazi bona ngokwabo ukuhlola, ukufunda, ukuhlaziya zonke izindinganiso, zonke izinto abantu abathi ziyazuzisa, ziyasiza, ziyahlonipheka futhi bangazamukeli nje futhi bazilingise.

Abantu abafuni ukuzitholela mathupha,, banezingqondo ezivaliwe, eziyisiphukuphuku, izingqondo ezingafuni ukuphenya, izingqondo ezinjengomshini ezingalokothi ziphenye futhi ZILINGISE kuphela.

Kuyadingeka, kuphuthuma, kubalulekile ukuthi abafundi kusukela besebancane kuze kube yisikhathi sokushiya AMAKHASO bajabulele inkululeko yangempela yokuthola bona ngokwabo, yokubuza, yokuqonda futhi bangakhawulelwa yizindonga ezichichimayo zokuvinjelwa, ukuthethiswa neziyalo.

Uma abafundi betshelwa ukuthi yini okufanele bayenze nalokho okungafanele bakwenze futhi abavunyelwe UKUQONDA nokuzitholela, INGABE ikuphi-ke ukuhlakanipha kwabo? YILUPHI ithuba elinikezwe ukuhlakanipha?

Kusiza ngani-ke ukubhala izivivinyo, ukugqoka kahle kakhulu, ukuba nabangani abaningi uma singahlakaniphile?

Ukuhlakanipha kuza kithi kuphela uma sikhululeke ngempela ukuziphenyelela mathupha, ukuqonda, ukuhlaziya ngaphandle kokwesaba ukuthethiswa futhi ngaphandle kwesibhaxu Seziyalo.

Abafundi abanesibindi, abesabayo, abangaphansi kweziyalo ezimbi abasoze BAKWAZI. Abasoze bakwazi ukuhlakanipha.

Namuhla Abazali kanye NoThisha, okuwukuphela kwento abayikhathalelayo ukuthi abafundi benze umsebenzi, babe odokotela, abameli, onjiniyela, izisebenzi zasehhovisi, okungukuthi imishini ephilayo futhi bese beshada futhi baguquke babe IMISHINI YOKWENZA IZINGANE futhi yilokho kuphela.

Lapho abafana noma amantombazane efuna ukwenza into entsha, into ehlukile, lapho bezwa isidingo sokuphuma kuleso sakhiwo, ubandlululo, imikhuba ephelelwe yisikhathi, iziyalo, amasiko omndeni noma isizwe, njll., khona-ke abazali baqinisa amaketanga ejele futhi batshele umfana noma intombazane: Ningakwenzi lokho! asizimisele ukukusekela kulokho, lezo zinto ziyahlanya, njll., njll., njll.

SEWONKE umfana noma intombazane iboshwe ngokusemthethweni ngaphakathi ejele leziyalo, amasiko imikhuba ephelelwe yisikhathi, imibono esidala, njll.

IMFUNDISO EYISISEKELO ifundisa ukuhlanganisa UKUHLELEKA nENKULULEKO.

UKUHLELEKA ngaphandle kwENKULULEKO KUBUHLUNGU. INKULULEKO ngaphandle KOKUHLELEKA YIQHINGA.

INKULULEKO NOKUHLELEKA okuhlanganiswe ngokuhlakanipha kwakha ISISEKELO SEMFUNDISO EYISISEKELO.

ABAFUNDI kufanele bajabulele inkululeko ephelele yokuzitholela mathupha, YOKUBUZA ukuthola lokho ngempela, lokho okuyiqiniso NGABO FUTHI nalokho abangakwenza ekuphileni.

Abafundi, amasosha namaphoyisa futhi ngokuvamile bonke labo bantu okudingeka baphile ngaphansi kweziyalo eziqinile, bavame ukuba nonya, bangabi nazwelo ebuhlungwini bomuntu, babe ngenhliziyo embi.

ISIYALO siyayiqeda INZWELO yomuntu futhi lokhu sekubonakaliswe ngokuphelele ngokUBUKELA kanye NOKUHLOLA.

Ngenxa yeziyalo nemithetho eminingi kangaka, abantu balesi sikhathi balahlekelwe ngokuphelele INZWELO futhi baguquke baba nonya futhi babe ngenhliziyo embi.

Ukuze ukhululeke ngempela, udinga ukuba nozwelo kakhulu futhi ube ngumuntu onesihe.

Ezikoleni, emakolishi nasemanyuvesi, abafundi bafundiswa ukuthi BAGXILE emakilasini futhi abafundi bagxila ukuze bagweme ukuthethiswa, ukudonswa izindlebe, ukushaywa ngesibhaxu noma ngombusi, njll., njll., njll. Kodwa ngeshwa abafundiswa UKUQONDA NGEMPELA ukuthi kuyini UKUQAPHELA NGOKUQONDILE.

Ngesiyalo umfundi uyagxila futhi usebenzisa amandla okudala kaningi ngokungenasidingo.

Amandla okudala uhlobo olucashile kakhulu lwamandla akhiqizwa yi-MACHINE ORGANIC.

Siyadla futhi siyaphuza futhi yonke imisebenzi yokugaya ekujuleni iyimisebenzi yokucwenga lapho izinto eziluhlaza ziphenduka izinto namandla awusizo.

Amandla okudala yilokhu: uhlobo lwendaba namandla acashile kakhulu akhiqizwa umzimba.

Uma sazi ukuthi singagxila NGOKUQONDILE singonga amandla okudala.

Ngeshwa othisha abazifundisi izifundo zabo ukuthi kuyini UKUQAPHELA NGOKUQONDILE.

Nomaphi lapho siqondisa khona UKUQAPHELA sichitha AMANDLA OKUDALA. Singawonga lawa mandla uma sihlukanisa ukunaka, uma singazihlanganisi nezinto, nabantu, ngemibono.

Lapho sizihlanganisa nabantu, nezinto, ngemibono, siyazikhohlwa futhi silahlekelwa AMANDLA okudala ngendlela edabukisa kakhulu.

KUPHUTHUMA ukwazi ukuthi sidinga ukonga AMANDLA OKUDALA ukuze sivuse UKUQAPHELA nokuthi AMANDLA OKUDALA ANGAMANDLA APHELA, IMOTO YOKUQAPHELA, ithuluzi LOKUVUSA UKUQAPHELA.

Lapho sifunda ukungazikhohlwa, lapho sifunda ukuhlukanisa UKUQAPHELA phakathi KOMUNTU; INHLOSO kanye NENDAWO, songa AMANDLA OKUDALA ukuze sivuse UKUQAPHELA.

Kuyadingeka ukufunda ukuphatha UKUQAPHELA ukuze sivuse ukwazi kodwa abafundi abazi lutho ngalokhu ngoba OTHISHA babo ababazisanga.

LAPHO sifunda ukusebenzisa UKUQAPHELA ngokuzithandela, isiyalo siyashiyeka.

Umfundi oqaphele amakilasi akhe, izifundo zakhe, ukuhleleka, AKADINGI siyalo sanoma yiluphi uhlobo.

KUPHUTHUMA ukuthi OTHISHA baqonde isidingo sokuhlanganisa ngokuhlakanipha INKULULEKO NOKUHLELEKA futhi lokhu kungenzeka ngokuqaphela NGOKUQONDILE.

UKUQAPHELA NGOKUQONDILE akubandakanyi lokho okubizwa ngokuthi UKUZIBANDAKANYA. Lapho sizIBANDAKANYA nabantu, nezinto, ngemibono, kuza UKUTHATHEKA futhi lokhu kokugcina kukhiqiza UKULALA eKUQAPHELENI.

Kufanele ukwazi ukugxila ngaphandle KOKUZIBANDAKANYA. LAPHO sigxila kokuthile noma kumuntu othile futhi sizikhohlwa, umphumela kuba UKUTHATHEKA NOKULALA KOKUQAPHELA.

Qaphela ngokucophelela i-CINEASTA. Ulele, akazi lutho, uyazikhohlwa, uyize, ubonakala eyisihambi, uphupha ngefilimu ayibukayo, neqhawe lefilimu.

ABAFUNDI kufanele bagxile emakilasini ngaphandle kokuzikhohlwa ukuze bangaweli EBUDALENI OBESABISAYO BOKUQAPHELA.

Umfundi kufanele azibone esesiteji lapho ethula isivivinyo noma lapho ephambi kwebhodi noma egaxamekile ngomyalelo kathisha, noma lapho efunda noma ephumula noma edlala nontanga yakhe.

UKUQAPHELA OKUHLUKANISEKILE IZIQEPHU EZINTATHU: UMNIKAZI, INHLOSO, INDAWO, empeleni KUYIQINISO UKUQAPHELA NGOKUQONDILE.

Lapho singalenzi IPUTHA LOKUZIBANDAKANYA nabantu, izinto, imibono, njll. songa AMANDLA OKUDALA futhi sithuthukise kithi ukuvuka KOKUQAPHELA.

Noma ngubani ofuna ukuvusa UKUQAPHELA EMAZWENI APHEZULU, kufanele aqale ngokUVUKA lapha nakhona manje.

Lapho UMFUNDI enza iphutha LOKUZIBANDAKANYA nabantu, izinto, imibono, lapho enza iphutha lokuzikhohlwa, khona-ke uwela ekuthungeni nasekulaleni.

Isiyalo asibafundisi abafundi ukuthi BAGXILE NGOKUQONDILE. Isiyalo siyijele langempela lengqondo.

Abafundi kufanele bafunde ukuphatha UKUQAPHELA NGOKUQONDILE kusukela emabhentshini esikole ukuze kamuva empilweni esebenzayo, ngaphandle kwesikole, bangalenzi iphutha lokuzikhohlwa.

Indoda ezikhohlwayo phambi komthukiyo, izihlanganisa naye, iyathandeka, iwela ebuthongweni bokungazi futhi bese iyalimaza noma ibulala bese iya ejele ngokungenakugwemeka.

Lowo ongazivumeli ukuba ATHONTHWE ngumthukiyi, lowo ongazihlanganisi naye, lowo ongazikhohlwi, lowo okwaziyo ukugxila NGOKUQONDILE, ngeke akwazi ukunikeza amagama omthukiyi inani, noma ukumlimaza noma ukumbulala.

Onke amaphutha umuntu awenzayo ekuphileni abangelwa ukuthi uyazikhohlwa, uyazihlanganisa, uyathandeka futhi uwela ebuthongweni.

Kungaba ngcono entsheni, kubo bonke abafundi, ukuthi bafundiswe UVUKO LOKUQAPHELA esikhundleni sokubagqilaza ngeziyalo eziningi ezingenangqondo.
