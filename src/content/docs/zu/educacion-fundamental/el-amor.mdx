Kusukela emabhentshini esikole, abafundi kufanele baqonde ngokuphelele lokho okubizwa ngokuthi UTHANDO.

UKWESABA nokUNCINTELA kuvame ukudideka ne-UTHANDO kodwa akulona UTHANDO.

Abafundi bancike kubazali babo nothisha futhi kucacile ukuthi bayabahlonipha futhi bayabesaba ngesikhathi esisodwa.

Abantwana, intsha, izintombi zancike kubazali babo ngokuqondene nezingubo, ukudla, imali, indawo yokuhlala, njll. futhi kucaca ukuthi bazizwa bevikelekile, bayazi ukuthi bancike kubazali babo yingakho bebahlonipha futhi baze babesabe, kodwa lokho akulona UTHANDO.

Njengesibonelo salokho esikushoyo, singaqinisekisa ngokunembile ukuthi yonke ingane, umfana noma intombazane, intsha noma intombi, ithemba abangani bayo besikole kakhulu kunabazali bayo.

Empeleni izingane, intsha nezintombi zikhuluma nozakwabo izinto eziyimfihlo ezingasoze zakhuluma ngazo nabazali bazo.

Lokhu kusibonisa ukuthi akukho ukwethembana kweqiniso phakathi kwezingane nabazali, ukuthi alukho UTHANDO lweqiniso.

Kuyaphuthuma ukuqonda ukuthi kunomehluko omkhulu phakathi kwe-UTHANDO nalokho okuyinhlonipho, ukwesaba, ukuncika, ukwesaba.

Kuyaphuthuma ukwazi ukuhlonipha abazali nothisha bethu, kodwa singayididanisi inhlonipho ne-UTHANDO.

INHLONIPHO ne-UTHANDO kufanele zihlangane ngokusondele, kodwa akufanele sididanise okukodwa nokunye.

Abazali bayabathanda abantwana babo, babafisela okuhle, umsebenzi omuhle, umshado omuhle, ukuvikeleka, njll. futhi badidanisa lokho kwesaba ne-UTHANDO lweqiniso.

Kuyadingeka ukuqonda ukuthi ngaphandle kwe-UTHANDO LWEQINISO akunakwenzeka ukuthi abazali nothisha baqondise izizukulwane ezintsha ngokuhlakanipha noma ngabe kunezinhloso ezinhle kakhulu.

Indlela eholela ESIZWENI igandaywe NGEZINHLOSO EZINHLE KAKHULU.

Sibona isimo esaziwa umhlaba wonke "SABAVUKELI ABAZILAHLELA". Lona wumqedazwe wengqondo osabalale emhlabeni wonke. Inqwaba "YABANTWANA ABAPHILA KAHLE", okuthiwa bathandwa kakhulu abazali babo, bonakala kakhulu, bathandeka kakhulu, bahlasela abantu abangenakuzivikela, bashaye futhi badlwengule abesifazane, bayebe, bajikijele ngamatshe, bahamba ngamaqembu ezigelekeqe bebulala yonke indawo, abahloniphi othisha nabazali, njll. njll. njll.

"ABAVUKELI ABAZILAHLELA" bangumphumela wokuntuleka kwe-UTHANDO lweqiniso.

Lapho kukhona i-UTHANDO lweqiniso, akukwazi ukuba khona "ABAVUKELI ABAZILAHLELA".

Ukube abazali bebezithanda ngempela izingane zabo bebezokwazi ukuziqondisa ngokuhlakanipha futhi ngaleso sikhathi bekungeke kube khona "ABAVUKELI ABAZILAHLELA".

Abavukeli abazilahlelayo bangumphumela wokuqondiswa okungalungile.

Abazali abakabi nalo i-UTHANDO olwanele lokuzinikela ngempela ekuqondiseni izingane zabo ngokuhlakanipha.

Abazali banamuhla bacabanga ngemali kuphela futhi banikeza ingane okuningi nokuningi, nemoto yakamuva, nezingubo zakamuva, njll. kodwa abathandi ngempela abakwazi ukuthanda futhi yingakho "kunabavukeli abazilahlelayo".

Ukungajulile kwalesi sikhathi kubangelwa ukuntuleka kwe-UTHANDO LWEQINISO.

Impilo yanamuhla ifana nechibi elingenakujula, elingenakujula.

Echibini elijulile lempilo, izidalwa eziningi zingaphila, izinhlanzi eziningi, kodwa ichibi eliseduze nomgwaqo, liyoma ngokushesha ngemisebe yelanga eshisayo futhi ngaleso sikhathi okuwukuphela kwento esele udaka, ukubola, ububi.

Akunakwenzeka ukuqonda ubuhle bempilo ngayo yonke inkazimulo yayo, uma singazange sifunde UKUTHANDA.

Abantu badidanisa inhlonipho nokwesaba nalokho okubizwa ngokuthi UTHANDO.

Siyabahhlonipha abaphathi bethu futhi siyabesaba bese sikholwa ukuthi siyabathanda.

Izingane ziyabesaba abazali bazo nothisha futhi ziyabahlonipha bese zikholwa ukuthi ziyabathanda.

Ingane yesaba isiswebhu, isando, amamaki amabi ukusolwa ekhaya noma esikoleni, njll. bese ikholwa ukuthi iyabathanda abazali bayo nothisha kodwa empeleni iyabesaba nje.

Sincike emsebenzini, kumqashi, sesaba ubumpofu, ukushiywa ngaphandle komsebenzi, bese sikholwa ukuthi siyamthanda umqashi futhi size silwele izintshisakalo zakhe, sinakekele impahla yakhe kodwa lokho akulona UTHANDO, lokho ukwesaba.

Abantu abaningi bayesaba ukuzicabangela ngokwabo izimfihlakalo zokuphila nokufa, ukwesaba ukubuza, ukuphenya, ukuqonda, ukufunda, njll. bese bethi NGIYAMTHANDA UNKULUNKULU, FUTHI LOKHO KWANELE!

Bakholwa ukuthi bayamthanda UNKULUNKULU kodwa empeleni ABATHANDI, bayesaba.

Ubugqila ngokwengqondo, ukuncika, ukuncika kumuntu othile, akulona UTHANDO. Ukwesaba kuphela futhi yilokho kuphela.

Ingane ezifundweni zayo incike KOTHISHA noma KUTHISHA futhi kucacile ukuthi yesaba UKUXOSHWA, amamaki amabi, ukusolwa futhi izikhathi eziningi ikholwa ukuthi IYAMTHANDA kodwa okwenzekayo ukuthi iyamwesaba.

Lapho umfazi esezobeletha noma esengozini yokufa ngenxa yanoma yisiphi isifo, umyeni ukholwa ukuthi uyamthanda kakhulu, kodwa empeleni okwenzekayo ukuthi wesaba ukumlahlekela, umncike ezintweni eziningi, ezifana nokudla, ubulili, ukugezwa kwezingubo, ukuthintwa, njll. futhi wesaba ukumlahlekela. Lokho akulona UTHANDO.

Wonke umuntu uthi uyamthanda wonke umuntu kodwa akunjalo: Kuvame kakhulu ukuthola othile empilweni owazi UKUTHANDA NGEQINISO.

Ukube abazali bebezithanda ngempela izingane zabo, ukube izingane bezibathanda ngempela abazali bazo, ukube othisha bebeyobathanda ngempela abafundi babo, bekungeke kube khona izimpi. Izimpi bezingeke zenzeke ngekhulu lamaphesenti.

Okwenzekayo ukuthi abantu abaqondanga ukuthi luyini uthando, futhi konke ukwesaba nakho konke ukugqilazeka ngokwengqondo, nayo yonke intshiseko, njll. bayikanganisa nalokho okubizwa ngokuthi UTHANDO.

Abantu abakwazi UKUTHANDA, ukube abantu bebeyokwazi ukuthanda, impilo ngempela ibiyoba yipharadesi.

ABATHANDANAYO bakholwa ukuthi bayathanda futhi abaningi bangaze bafunge ngegazi ukuthi bayathanda. Kodwa BAZITHANDELA kuphela. Seleneliswe INTANDO, inqaba yamakhadi iyawa.

INTANDO ivame ukukhohlisa INGQONDO nENHLIZIYO. Wonke UMUNTU OZITHANDELAYO ukholwa ukuthi UYATHANDA.

Kuvame kakhulu ukuthola empilweni umbhangqwana othandanayo ngempela. Miningi imibhangqwana EZITHANDELAYO kodwa kunzima kakhulu ukuthola umbhangqwana OTHANDANAYO.

Bonke abaculi bahlabelela nge-UTHANDO kodwa abazi ukuthi luyini i-UTHANDO futhi badidanisa INTANDO ne-UTHANDO.

Uma kukhona into enzima kakhulu kulokhu kuphila, AKUWUKUDIDANISA INTANDO ne-UTHANDO.

INTANDO iyobuthi obumnandi kakhulu futhi obucashile obungacabangeka, njalo igcina iqeda ngentengo yegazi.

INTANDO INGOKOBULILI ngekhulu lamaphesenti, INTANDO yisilo kodwa ngezinye izikhathi iphinde icwengeke futhi icashile kakhulu. Njalo idideka ne-UTHANDO.

Othisha kufanele bafundise abafundi, intsha, nezintombi, ukuhlukanisa phakathi kwe-UTHANDO ne-INTANDO. Kungale ndlela kuphela lapho bayogwema izinhlekelele eziningi kamuva empilweni.

Othisha baphoqeleka ukuba bakhe isibopho sabafundi futhi yingakho kufanele babalungise kahle ukuze bangabi yizinhlekelele empilweni.
