Kuyadingeka ukuthanda nokuthandwa, kodwa ngeshwa lomhlaba abantu abathandi futhi abathandwa.

Lokho okubizwa ngokuthi uthando yinto engaziwa kubantu futhi bayiphambanisa kalula nothando olukhulu kanye nokwesaba.

Ukuba abantu bebenothando futhi bethandwa, izimpi bezingeke zenzeke nhlobo emhlabeni.

Imishado eminingi ebingajabula ngempela, ngeshwa ayijabuli ngenxa yezikhalazo ezindala eziqoqwe enkumbulweni.

Ukuba abashadile bebenomusa, bebezoyikhohlwa iminyaka edlule ebuhlungu futhi baphile ngokugcwele, begcwele injabulo yangempela.

Ingqondo iyabulala uthando, iyalichitha. Okuhlangenwe nakho, ukucasuka okudala, umona wakudala, konke lokhu kuqoqwe enkumbulweni, kuyalichitha uthando.

Amakhosikazi amaningi athukuthele angajabula ukuba ebenomusa owanele wokukhohlwa okwedlule futhi aphile esikhathini samanje emthanda umyeni.

Amadoda amaningi abengajabula ngempela ngamakhosikazi awo ukuba abenomusa owanele wokuthethelela amaphutha amadala futhi akhohlwe izikhalazo nezinsizi eziqoqwe enkumbulweni.

Kuyadingeka, kuphuthuma ukuba imishado iqonde incazelo ejulile yesikhathi.

Amadoda namakhosikazi kufanele bazizwe njengabasanda kushada, bekhohlwa okwedlule futhi bephila ngenjabulo esikhathini samanje.

Uthando nezinhlansi yizinto ezingahambisani nhlobo. Othandweni akukwazi ukuba khona izinhlansi zanoma yiluphi uhlobo. Uthando yintethelelo yaphakade.

Kukhona uthando kulabo abazwa usizi lwangempela ngokuhlupheka kwabangane babo nezitha zabo. Kukhona uthando lweqiniso kulowo osebenza ngenhliziyo yonke ngenhlalakahle yabathobekile, abampofu, abaswele.

Kukhona uthando kulowo ngendlela ezenzakalelayo futhi engokwemvelo ozwela umlimi onisela imisele ngesithukuthuku sakhe, umuntu wasemzaneni ohluphekayo, umncengi ocela uhlamvu lwemali kanye nenja ethobekile edabukile negulayo efa yindlala eceleni komgwaqo.

Lapho sisiza othile ngenhliziyo yonke, lapho sinakekela isihlahla ngendlela engokwemvelo nezenzakalelayo futhi sinisela izimbali engadini ngaphandle kokuba kukhona osicela ukuba senze kanjalo, kukhona umusa oyiqiniso, ukuzwelana kweqiniso, uthando lweqiniso.

Ngeshwa emhlabeni, abantu abanawo umusa wangempela. Abantu bazikhathaza kuphela ngempumelelo yabo yobugovu, izifiso, impumelelo, ulwazi, okuhlangenwe nakho, ukuhlupheka, ubumnandi, njll. njll.

Emhlabeni kukhona abantu abaningi, abanomusa wamanga kuphela. Kukhona umusa wamanga kumuntu wezombusazwe ohlakaniphile, impungushe yokhetho echitha imali ngenhloso yobugovu yokuthola amandla, isithunzi, isikhundla, ingcebo, njll., njll. Akufanele siphambanise inkunzi nembuzi.

Umusa weqiniso awunazisusa nhlobo, kodwa ungaphambaniswa kalula nomusa wamanga wobugovu wezimpungushe zezombusazwe, izigebengu zombuso wemali, izifebe ezihalalisa owesifazane, njll. njll.

Kufanele sibe nomusa enhliziyweni. Umusa weqiniso awuveli Engqondweni, umusa oyiqiniso uyiphunga lenhliziyo.

Ukuba abantu bebenomusa bebezoyikhohlwa yonke imizwa yokucasuka eqoqiwe enkumbulweni, konke okuhlangenwe nakho okubuhlungu kweminyaka eminingi edlule futhi bafunde ukuphila njalo ngomzuzu, bahlale bejabule, bahlale benomusa, begcwele ubuqotho beqiniso.

Ngeshwa mina ngiyinkumbulo futhi ngiphila okwedlule, ngihlale ngifuna ukubuyela kokwedlule. Okwedlule kuphela ngabantu, kuyayichitha injabulo, kuyalibulala uthando.

Ingqondo evalelwe okwedlule ayisoze yaqonda ngokugcwele incazelo ejulile yesikhathi esiphila kuso.

Baningi abantu abasibhalela befuna induduzo, becela umuthi obalulekile wokwelapha izinhliziyo zabo ezibuhlungu, kodwa bambalwa abazikhathazayo ngokududuza abasosizini.

Baningi abantu abasibhalela besilandisa ngesimo esidabukisayo abaphila kuso, kodwa bayivelakancane labo abaqhekeza isinkwa esisodwa okufanele sibondle ukuze basabelane ngaso nabanye abaswele.

Abantu abafuni ukuqonda ukuthi ngemuva kwayo yonke imiphumela kukhona imbangela nokuthi ngokushintsha imbangela kuphela lapho sishintsha umphumela.

Mina, mina wethu othandekayo, ngamandla aphile kokhokho bethu futhi abangele izimbangela ezithile zangaphambili emiphumela yazo yamanje elawula ubukhona bethu.

Sidinga UMUSA wokuguqula izimbangela nokuguqula imiphumela. Sidinga umusa wokuqondisa ngobuhlakani umkhumbi wobukhona bethu.

Sidinga umusa wokuguqula impilo yethu ngokuphelele.

Umusa osemthethweni osebenzayo awuveli engqondweni. Ukuzwelana okuyiqiniso nothando oluqotho, akusoze kwaba umphumela wokwesaba.

Kuyadingeka ukuqonda ukuthi ukwesaba kuyakuchitha ukuzwelana, kuphelisa umusa wenhliziyo futhi kuqede kithi iphunga elimnandi LOTHANDO.

Ukwesaba kuyimpande yakho konke ukukhohlakala, imvelaphi eyimfihlo yayo yonke impi, ushevu obulalayo owohloka futhi obulalayo.

Othisha nothisha bezikole, amakolishi namanyuvesi kufanele baqonde isidingo sokuqondisa abafundi babo kanye nabafundi endleleni yomusa weqiniso, isibindi, nobuqotho benhliziyo.

Abantu abagugile nabayiziphukuphuku besizukulwane esidlule, esikhundleni sokuqonda ukuthi yiluphi lolo shevu lokwesaba, balutshala njengembali ebuhlungu ye-greenhouse. Umphumela walokho kuziphatha kwaba ukukhohlakala, isiphithiphithi, kanye nokungabi namthetho.

Othisha nothisha kufanele basi qonde isikhathi esiphila kuso, isimo esibucayi esikuso kanye nesidingo sokukhulisa izizukulwane ezintsha phezu kwezisekelo zokuziphatha okuguquguqukayo okuhambisana nenkathi ye-athomu okwamanje kwalezi zikhathi zosizi nobuhlungu eqala phakathi kokuduma okuhloniphekile komcabango.

IMFUNDO EYISISEKELO isekelwe ku-psychology eguquguqukayo kanye nokuziphatha okuguquguqukayo, okuhambisana nesigqi esisha sokudlidliza senkathi entsha.

Umuzwa wokubambisana kufanele uqede ngokuphelele ukulwa okubi komncintiswano wobugovu. Kwenza kube nzima ukwazi ukusebenzisana lapho singabandakanyi isimiso somusa osebenzayo noguquguqukayo.

Kuphuthuma ukuqonda ngokugcwele, hhayi kuphela ezingeni lengqondo, kodwa futhi nasemakhoneni ahlukene angazi lutho engqondweni engazi lutho nengeyona engazi lutho ukuthi kuyini ukuntuleka komusa kanye nokwesabeka kobugovu. Kuphela ngokwenza siqaphele ukuthi buyini ubugovu kithi kanye nokuntuleka komusa lapho kuvela khona enhliziyweni yethu iphunga elimnandi LOTHANDO LWEQINISO kanye NOMUSA OSEBENZAYO ongeveli engqondweni.
