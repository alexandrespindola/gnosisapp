Ukubulala ngokusobala futhi ngokungangabazeki, isenzo esicekelayo phansi kakhulu nesobubhedengu obukhulu kunazo zonke ezaziwayo emhlabeni.

Uhlobo olubi kakhulu lokubulala ukubhubhisa impilo yabanye abantu.

Kuyinto esabekayo futhi eshaqisayo umzingeli obulala izidalwa ezingenacala zasehlathini ngesibhamu sakhe, kodwa umuntu obulala abanye abantu uyesabeka kakhulu ngokuphindwe izinkulungwane, unengeka kakhulu ngokuphindwe izinkulungwane.

Akubulawa nje kuphela ngemishini yokudubula, izibhamu, izikhali ezinkulu, izibhamu noma amabhomu enuzi, kodwa futhi umuntu angabulala ngokubukeka okulimaza inhliziyo, ukubukeka okuthobayo, ukubukeka okugcwele ukwedelela, ukubukeka okugcwele inzondo; noma umuntu angabulala ngesenzo sokungabongi, ngesenzo esibi, noma ngokuthuka, noma ngezwi elihlabayo.

Umhlaba ugcwele ababulali babazali, ababulali bomama abangabongiyo abaye babulala obaba nomama babo, ngamehlo abo, ngamazwi abo, ngezenzo zabo zonya.

Umhlaba ugcwele amadoda angabazi ukuthi abulale omkawo nabesifazane abangabazi ukuthi abulale abayeni babo.

Okubi kakhulu, kulomhlaba ononya esiphila kuwo, isintu sibulala lokho esikuthanda kakhulu.

Umuntu akaphili ngesinkwa sodwa, kodwa nangokwezici ezahlukahlukene zengqondo.

Banabantu abaningi abebengaphila isikhathi eside ukube omkabo babebavumele.

Banabafazi abaningi abebengaphila isikhathi eside ukube abayeni babo babebavumele.

Banobaba nomama abaningi abebengaphila isikhathi eside ukube izingane zabo bezibavumele.

Isifo esiyisa othandiweyo wethu ethuneni sidalwa yizizathu, amazwi abulalayo, ukubukeka okulimazayo, izenzo zokungabongi, njll.

Lomphakathi osegugile futhi onokuziphatha okubi ugcwele ababulali abangaqapheli abazenza abangenacala.

Amajele agcwele ababulali, kodwa uhlobo olubi kakhulu lwezigelekeqe luzenza ezingenacala futhi luhamba ngokukhululeka.

Alikho uhlobo lokubulala olungaba nesizathu. Ukubulala omunye umuntu akuxazululi nkinga empilweni.

Izimpi azikaze zixazulule nkinga. Ukudubula amadolobha angenakuzivikela nokubulala izigidi zabantu akuxazululi lutho.

Impi iyinto enokhahlo kakhulu, enesihluku, eyesabekayo, enengekayo. Izigidi zemishini yabantu elele, engaqapheli, eyiziwula, ziqala impi ngenhloso yokubhubhisa ezinye izigidi zemishini yabantu engaqapheli.

Ngezinye izikhathi, inhlekelele yomhlaba wonke endaweni yonke, noma ukuma okubi kwezinkanyezi esibhakabhakeni, kwanele ukwenza izigidi zamadoda ziqale impi.

Imishini yabantu ayazi lutho, ihamba ngendlela ecekela phansi lapho uhlobo oluthile lwamaza endawo yonke luyilimaza ngasese.

Ukube abantu bebengavusa ukuqwashisa, ukube kusukela ezitulweni zaseSikoleni abafundi bebeyofundiswa ngokuhlakanipha, bebeyothathelwa ekuqondeni ngokuqaphela ukuthi buyini ubutha nempi, bekuzoba yindaba ehlukile, akekho obezokwethamela impi futhi amagagasi ayinhlekelele endawo yonke ayezobe esetshenziswe ngendlela ehlukile.

Impi inuka ukudla abantu, impilo yezimpophoma, unya olubi kakhulu, umnsalo, umcibisholo, umkhonto, umkhosi wegazi, ayihambisani nhlobo nempucuko.

Wonke amadoda empini angamagwala, ayesaba futhi amaqhawe agcwele izindondo ngempela angamagwala amakhulu, ayesaba kakhulu.

Umuntu ozibulalayo naye ubonakala enesibindi kakhulu, kodwa uyigwala ngoba wayesaba impilo.

Iqhawe ekujuleni liyingilosi yokuzibulala okwathi ngesikhathi sokwesaba okukhulu lenza isenzo sobuwula sokuzibulala.

Ubuwula bomuntu ozibulalayo budideka kalula nesibindi seqhawe.

Uma siqaphela ngokucophelela ukuziphatha kwamasosha ngesikhathi sempi, izindlela zawo, ukubukeka kwawo, amazwi awo, izinyathelo zawo empini, singabufakazela ubugwala bawo obuphelele.

Othisha besikole, amakolishi, amanyuvesi, kufanele bafundise abafundi babo iqiniso ngempi. Kufanele baholele abafundi babo ekuzameni ngokuqaphela leli Qiniso.

Ukube abantu bebeqaphela ngokugcwele ukuthi liyini leli Qiniso elikhulu lempi, ukube othisha bebezokwazi ukufundisa abafundi babo ngokuhlakanipha, akekho isakhamuzi ebesiyokwethamelwa ekubulaweni.

Imfundo Eyisisekelo kufanele ifundiswe khona manje kuzo zonke Izikole, Amakolishi kanye Namanyuvesi, ngoba kusezitulo zeSikole lapho kufanele kusebenzelwe UKUTHULA khona.

Kuphuthuma ukuthi izizukulwane ezintsha ziqaphele ngokugcwele ukuthi buyini ubugebengu nokuthi yini impi.

Ezikoleni, emakolishi, emanyuvesi, kufanele kuqondwe ngokujulile ubutha nempi kuzo zonke izici zazo.

Izizukulwane ezintsha kufanele ziqonde ukuthi asebekhulile ngemibono yabo esindayo neyisiphukuphuku, bahlale beqeda intsha futhi bayiyise njengezinkomo endlini yokubulawa.

Intsha akumele ivumele ukuyengwa yimibono yempi, noma izizathu zabadala, ngoba isizathu esisodwa siphikisana nesinye isizathu futhi umbono ophikisana nomunye, kodwa akukho ukubonisana noma imibono eyiqiniso ngempi.

Asebekhulile banezizathu eziyizinkulungwane zokuthethelela impi futhi bayise intsha endlini yokubulawa.

Okubalulekile akusikho ukubonisana ngempi kodwa ukuthola iqiniso lalokho okuyimpi.

Asizichazi ngokumelene neSizathu noma ukuhlaziywa, sifuna ukusho kuphela ukuthi kufanele siqale sithole iqiniso ngempi bese sikwazi ukuzinikeza ukunethezeka kokucabanga nokuhlaziya.

Akunakwenzeka ukuthola iqiniso LOKUNGABULALI, uma singabandakanyi ukuzindla okujulile.

Ukuzindla okujulile kuphela okungasiholela ekutholeni iqiniso ngempi.

Othisha akumele nje banikeze abafundi babo imininingwane yobuhlakani. Othisha kufanele bafundise abafundi babo ukuthi bayiphatha kanjani ingqondo, ukuthola IQINISO.

Lolu Hlanga Olugugile futhi Olonakele alusacabangi ngaphandle kokubulala. Lokhu kubulala nokubulala, kungokwalolo hlobo lwesintu olonakele.

Ngethelevishini nangezithombe, abenzi bobubi basabalalisa imibono yabo yobugebengu.

Izingane ezincane zesizukulwane esisha zithola nsuku zonke ngokusebenzisa isikrini se-TV kanye nezindaba zezingane nezithombe, umagazini njll., Umthamo omuhle onobuthi wokubulala, ukudubula, ubugebengu obuthusayo, njll.

Awusakwazi ukwenza ithelevishini isebenze ngaphandle kokuhlangana namazwi agcwele inzondo, izibhamu, ububi.

Akukho lutho olwenziwa ohulumeni bomhlaba ngokumelene nokusakazwa kobugebengu.

Izingqondo zezingane kanye nentsha ziqhutshwa ngabenzi bobubi, endleleni yobugebengu.

Umbono wokubulala ususakazeke kangaka, usabalale kangaka ngamafilimu, izindaba, njll. ukuthi usujwayeleke ngokuphelele kuwo wonke umuntu.

Izihlubuki zokugcina zifundiswe ubugebengu futhi zibulala ngenjabulo yokubulala, zijabulela ukubona abanye befa. Bakufunde kanjalo ethelevishini yasekhaya, ezithombeni, ezindabeni, kumamagazini.

Ubugebengu buyabusa yonke indawo futhi ohulumeni abenzi lutho ukulungisa umuzwa wokubulala kusukela ezimpandeni zawo.

Kuyithinta othisha bezikole, amakolishi kanye namanyuvesi, ukukhuza nokunyakazisa izulu nomhlaba ukulungisa lolu bhubhane lwengqondo.

Kuphuthuma ukuthi othisha besikole, amakolishi kanye namanyuvesi, benze isililo bese becela bonke ohulumeni bomhlaba ukuthi bavimbele izithombe, ithelevishini, njll.

Ubugebengu buyanda kakhulu ngenxa yayo yonke le mibukiso yegazi futhi ngendlela esihamba ngayo kuzofika usuku lapho kungekho muntu ozokwazi ukuzulazula emigwaqweni ngokukhululekile ngaphandle kokwesaba ukubulawa.

Umsakazo, Izithombe, Ithelevishini, Amamagazini egazi, banikeze ukusakazwa okunjalo kobugebengu bokubulala, bakwenza kwathandeka kangaka ezingqondweni ezintekenteke nezizonakele, ukuthi akekho osazilingwa ukunyakazisa inhliziyo yakhe ukuze afake umuntu omunye isibhamu noma ummese.

Ngamandla okusakazeka kobugebengu bokubulala, izingqondo ezintekenteke sezijwayele kakhulu ubugebengu futhi manje zize zizinikeze ukunethezeka kokubulala ngokulingisa lokho ezikubone ezithombeni noma ethelevishini.

Othisha abayibo bafundisi bomphakathi baphoqelekile ngokugcwaliseka komsebenzi wabo ukulwela izizukulwane ezintsha ngokucela ohulumeni bomhlaba ukuthi bavimbele imibukiso yegazi, ekugcineni, ukwesula zonke izinhlobo zamafilimu mayelana nokubulala, amasela, njll.

Umzabalazo wothisha kufanele uze ufike nasemdlalweni wezinkunzi kanye nasesibhakeleni.

Uhlobo lomdlali wezinkunzi luhlobo oluyigwala nolunobugebengu kakhulu. Umdlali wezinkunzi ufuna zonke izinzuzo zakhe futhi ubulala ukuze ajabulise umphakathi.

Uhlobo lomdlali wesibhakela luhlobo lwesilo sokubulala, ngendlela yaso yobudlova elimala futhi ebulale ukuze ijabulise umphakathi.

Lolu hlobo lwemibukiso yegazi lunokhahlo ngamaphesenti ayikhulu futhi lukhuthaza izingqondo ngokuziqondisa endleleni yobugebengu. Uma sifuna ngempela ukulwela Ukuthula Komhlaba, kufanele siqale umkhankaso ojulile ngokumelene nemibukiso yegazi.

Ngenkathi kukhona izici ezicekelayo phakathi kwengqondo yomuntu, kuzoba nezimpi ezingagwemeki.

Ngaphakathi kwengqondo yomuntu kukhona izici ezikhiqiza impi, lezo zici yinzondo. ubudlova kuzo zonke izici zalo, ubugovu, intukuthelo, ukwesaba, izifiso zobugebengu, imibono yempi esatshalaliswa ithelevishini, umsakazo, izithombe, njll.

Ukusakazwa koxolo, imiklomelo ye-NOBEL YOKUTHULA akuzwakali ngenkathi kukhona izici zengqondo ngaphakathi komuntu ezikhiqiza impi.

Njengamanje ababulali abaningi banomklomelo we-NOBEL YOKUTHULA.
