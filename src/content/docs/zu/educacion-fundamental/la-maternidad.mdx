Impilo yomuntu iqala njengamaseli alula, njengoba kunjalo, ngesikhathi esisheshayo samaseli aphilayo.

Ukukhulelwa, ukukhula kombungu esiswini, ukuzalwa, yihlale kuyintathu emangalisayo futhi eyesabekayo okuqala ngayo impilo yanoma yisiphi isidalwa.

Kuyamangalisa ngempela ukwazi ukuthi izikhathi zethu zokuqala zokuphila kufanele siziphile kokuncane ngokungenamkhawulo, siguquke ngamunye wethu abe iseli elilula elincane kakhulu.

Siqala ukuba khona ngendlela yeseli elingabalulekile futhi siqede impilo yethu sigugile, sesikhulile futhi sigcwele izinkumbulo.

UBUYE bungenkumbulo. Abantu abaningi asebekhulile abaphili nakancane esikhathini samanje, abantu abaningi asebekhulile baphila ngokukhumbula okwedlule kuphela. Wonke umuntu omdala uyizwi nesithunzi nje. Wonke umuntu osekhulile ungumoya wesikhathi esedlule, inkumbulo eqoqiwe futhi yilokhu okuqhubeka kumaGenes ozalo lwethu.

Ukukhulelwa komuntu kuqala ngezikhathi ezisheshayo ngokumangalisayo, kepha ngezinqubo ezahlukahlukene zokuphila ziya ngokuya ziba buthuntu futhi zibuthuntu.

Abafundi abaningi kufanele bakhumbule ubudlelwano besikhathi. Isinambuzane esincane esiphila amahora ambalwa kuphela ntambama ehlobo, kubonakala sengathi asiphili cishe, kepha empeleni siphila konke umuntu aphila eminyakeni engamashumi ayisishiyagalombili, okwenzekayo ukuthi siphila ngokushesha, umuntu uphila eminyakeni engamashumi ayisishiyagalombili konke okushiwo yizwe eminyakeni eyizigidi.

Lapho i-Zoosperm ihlangana neqanda, kuqala ukukhulelwa. Iseli impilo yomuntu eqala ngalo, liqukethe ama-chromosome angamashumi amane nesishiyagalombili.

Ama-chromosome ahlukaniswe abe yizakhi zofuzo, ikhulu lokugcina noma okungaphezulu kwakho ngokuqinisekile lokho okuyi-Chromosome.

Izakhi zofuzo kunzima kakhulu ukuzifunda ngoba ngasinye sakhiwa ama-molecule ambalwa adlidliza ngesivinini esingenakucatshangwa.

Umhlaba omangalisayo weGenes wakha indawo ephakathi phakathi komhlaba onezinhlangothi ezintathu nomhlaba wohlangothi lwesine.

Ama-athomu efuzo atholakala kumaGenes. UBUYE OBUPHILA NGOKWENGQONDO bokhokho bethu, buza ukuzogcwalisa iqanda elivundile.

Kulesi sikhathi se-Electro-technical nesayensi ye-athomu, akukho neze ukweqisa ukusho ukuthi umkhondo we-electro-magnetic owashiywa ngokhokho owakhipha umoya wokugcina uprintwe kumaGenes naku-chromosomes eqanda elivundiswe yinzalo.

Indlela yokuphila yakhiwa izinyawo zamahhashi okufa.

Ngesikhathi sokuphila, izinhlobo ezahlukahlukene zamandla zigijima ngomzimba womuntu; uhlobo ngalunye lwamandla lunohlelo lwalo lokusebenza, uhlobo ngalunye lwamandla lubonakala ngesikhathi salo nangehora lalo.

Ngemuva kwezinyanga ezimbili zokukhulelwa sinomsebenzi wokugaya ukudla futhi ngemuva kwezinyanga ezine zokukhulelwa amandla okushayela angena, ahlobene kakhulu nezinhlelo zokuphefumula nezemisipha.

Isibukwane sesayensi sokuzalwa nokufa kwazo zonke izinto siyamangalisa.

Izazi eziningi zithi kunokufana okusondelene phakathi kokuzalwa kwesidalwa somuntu nokuzalwa kwemihlaba endaweni engaphandle.

Ngemuva kwezinyanga eziyisishiyagalolunye ingane iyazalwa, ngemuva kwezinyanga eziyishumi ukukhula kuqala ngakho konke ukusebenza komzimba okumangalisayo nokuthuthuka okulinganayo nokuphelele kwezicubu ezihlanganayo.

Lapho i-fontanel yangaphambili yezingane ezisanda kuzalwa ivaleka eminyakeni emibili noma emithathu ubudala, kuyisibonakaliso sokuthi uhlelo lobuchopho bomgogodla seluqediwe ngokuphelele.

Ososayensi abaningi bathi imvelo inekhono lokucabanga futhi leli khono lokucabanga linikeza isimo esiphilayo kukho konke okukhona, kukho konke okuke kwaba khona, kukho konke okuzoba khona.

Inqwaba yabantu iyathuka ngekhono lokucabanga futhi abanye baze balibize ngokuthi "UMUNTU OHLANYAYO WENDLU".

Kunokudideka okuningi mayelana negama elithi UKUCABANGA futhi baningi abadida UKUCABANGA NE-FANTASY.

Izazi ezithile zithi kunezindlela ezimbili zokucabanga. Eyokuqala bayibiza nge-MECHANICAL IMAGINATION kanti eyesibili bayibiza nge-INTENTIONAL IMAGINATION: Eyokuqala yakhiwa udoti wengqondo kanti eyesibili ihambisana nalokho esinakho okufaneleke kakhulu nokunenhlonipho.

Ngokusebenzisa ukubheka nokuhlangenwe nakho siye sakwazi ukuqinisekisa ukuthi kukhona futhi uhlobo lwe-SUB-MECHANICAL IMAGINATION MORBID INFRACONSCIOUS ne-SUBJECTIVE.

Lolo hlobo lwe-SUB-AUTOMATIC IMAGINATION lusebenza ngaphansi kwe-INTELLECTUAL ZONE.

Izithombe eziyizithombe zobulili ezingcolile, amabhayisikobho agulayo, izindaba ezinongwe ngezincazelo ezimbili, amahlaya agulayo, njll., ngokuvamile zenza i-SUB-MECHANICAL IMAGINATION isebenze ngokungazi.

Ukuhlaziywa okujulile kusiholele esiphethweni esinengqondo sokuthi amaphupho ashisayo nokungcola kwasebusuku kungenxa ye-SUB-MECHANICAL IMAGINATION.

UBUMQULU OBUPHELELE abunakwenzeka uma nje i-SUB-MECHANICAL IMAGINATION isekhona.

Kucace bha ukuthi i-CONSCIOUS IMAGINATION yehluke kakhulu kulokho okubizwa nge-MECHANICAL IMAGINATION, SUBJECTIVE, INFRACONSCIOUS. NGOKUNGQONDONGQONDO.

Noma yikuphi ukumelwa kungathathwa ngendlela EZIZITHOBA NOKUZIZISA, kodwa i-SUB-IMAGINATION yohlobo lwemishini, engaphansi kwengqondo, engaphansi kwengqondo, engazi lutho ingasikhohlisa ngokusebenza ngokuzenzakalelayo ngemibala nezithombe ezithandekayo, ezithandekayo, ezithile.

Uma sifuna UBUMNQULU OBUQHUBEKAYO, obuphelele, obujulile, sidinga ukuqapha hhayi kuphela i-CONSCIOUS IMAGINATION, kodwa futhi ne-MECHANICAL IMAGINATION kanye ne-UNCONSCIOUS, AUTOMATIC, SUBCONSCIOUS, SUBMERGED SUB-IMAGINATION.

Akufanele sikhohlwe neze ngobudlelwano obusondelene obukhona phakathi KOBULEILI NEKHONO LOKUCABANGA.

Ngokuzindla okujulile kufanele siguqule zonke izinhlobo zokucabanga kwemishini nazo zonke izinhlobo ze-SUB-IMAGINATION ne-INFRA-IMAGINATION AUTOMATIC, zibe yizithombe ezingazi lutho, ezizimele.

I-OBJECTIVE IMAGINATION ngokwayo idala ngokuyisisekelo, ngaphandle kwayo umqambi ubengakwazi ukuzwisisa ucingo, umsakazo, indiza, njll.

IKHONO LOKUCABANGA LOWESIFAZANE okhulelwe libalulekile ekuthuthukisweni kombungu. Kuqinisekisiwe ukuthi wonke umama angakwazi ngeKHONO LAKHE LOKUCABANGA ukuguqula ingqondo yombungu.

Kuphuthuma ukuthi owesifazane okhulelwe abuke imidwebo emihle, imihlaba emangalisayo, futhi alalele umculo wakudala namagama avumelanayo, ngaleyo ndlela angasebenza engqondweni yesidalwa asithwele ngaphakathi ngokuphathelene.

Owesifazane okhulelwe akufanele aphuze utshwala, abheme, noma abuke izinto ezimbi, ezingemnandi ngoba konke lokhu kuyalimaza ukuthuthukiswa okuvumelanayo kwesidalwa.

Kufanele wazi ukuthi ungazithethelela kanjani zonke izinkanuko namaphutha owesifazane okhulelwe.

Amadoda amaningi angabekezeleli futhi angenalo ukuqonda kweqiniso, ayathukuthela futhi athuke owesifazane okhulelwe. Izinhlungu zakhe, izinsizi ezibangelwa umyeni ongenayo ikhwalithi, zithinta umbungu osesimweni sokukhulelwa, hhayi nje ngokomzimba kodwa nangokwengqondo.

Uma sicabangela amandla ekhono lokucabanga lokudala, kunengqondo ukusho ukuthi owesifazane okhulelwe akufanele abuke izinto ezimbi, ezingathandeki, ezingavumelani, ezinengekayo, njll.

Sesifikile isikhathi sokuba ohulumeni bakhathazeke ngokuxazulula izinkinga ezinkulu ezihlobene nobubele.

Akuhambisani neze nokuthi emphakathini oziqhenyayo ngokuba ngowobuKristu nowentando yeningi, abantu abakwazi ukuhlonipha nokuhlonipha umqondo ongokwenkolo wobubele. Kuyesabeka ukubona izinkulungwane zabesifazane abakhulelwe bengenalutho, beshiywe ngumyeni nomphakathi, bencele isinkwa noma umsebenzi futhi ngokuvamile benza umsebenzi olukhuni womzimba, ukuze bakwazi ukusinda nesidalwa abasithwele esiswini sabo.

Lezi zimo ezingaphansi komuntu zomphakathi wamanje, lobu bunzima nokuntuleka kokuzibophezela kwabaholi kanye nabantu kusitshela ngokucacile ukuthi intando yeningi ayikabi khona.

Izibhedlela ezinamawadi azo ababelethisi azikaze ziyixazulule inkinga, ngoba labo besifazane bangafika kuphela kulezo zibhedlela lapho ukubeletha kusondela.

Kudingeka ngokushesha amakhaya okuhlala, amadolobha angempela engadi ahlonyiswe ngamahholo nezindawo zokuhlala zabesifazane abakhulelwe abampofu kakhulu, imitholampilo nezindawo zezingane zabo.

Lezi zindlu zokuhlala ziyindawo yokuhlala yabesifazane abampofu abakhulelwe, zigcwele zonke izinhlobo zokunethezeka, izimbali, umculo, ukuvumelana, ubuhle, njll., zingayixazulula ngokuphelele inkinga enkulu yobubele.

Kufanele siqonde ukuthi umphakathi womuntu umndeni omkhulu futhi ayikho inkinga yangaphandle ngoba yonke inkinga ngandlela thize ithinta wonke amalungu omphakathi embuthanweni wawo. Kuyinto engenangqondo ukucwasa abesifazane abakhulelwe ngenxa yokuthi bampofu kakhulu. Kuyicala ukubabukela phansi, ukubadelela noma ukubafaka endaweni yokukhosela yabadla izambane likapondo.

Kulo mphakathi esiphila kuwo, akukwazi ukuba khona abantwana nabazali bokutholwa, ngoba sonke singabantu futhi sinamalungelo afanayo.

Sidinga ukwakha intando yeningi yangempela, uma ngempela singafuni ukugwinywa yiBukhomanisi.
