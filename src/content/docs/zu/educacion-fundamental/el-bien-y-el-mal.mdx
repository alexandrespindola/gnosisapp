AKUKHO OKULUNGILE NOKUBI. Into enhle yilapho isifanele, futhi imbi lapho ingasifanele. OKULUNGILE NOKUBI kuyindaba yokuzithanda wena kanye nemikhuba yengqondo.

Indoda eyasungula amagama abhubhisayo OKULUNGILE NOKUBI kwakungu-Atlante obizwa ngokuthi u-MAKARI KRONVERNKZYON, ilungu elihlonishwayo le-Scientific Society AKALDAN, etholakala ezwenikazi elacwila i-Atlante.

Akazange asole umuntu omdala ohlakaniphile wasendulo umonakalo omkhulu kangaka ayezowubangela esintwini, ngokusungula amagama akhe amabili amancane.

Izazi ze-Atlante zafunda ngokujulile wonke amandla OKUTHUTHUKA, OKUQHUBEKA NOKUNGATHATHI HLAHLOMBOLO WEMVELO, kodwa lo muntu omdala ohlakaniphile wathola umqondo wokuchaza amabili okuqala ngamagama athi OKULUNGILE NOKUBI. Wabiza amandla ohlobo LOKUTHUTHUKA ngokuthi mahle kanti amandla ohlobo LOKUQHUBEKA wawabhapathiza ngegama elithi mabi. Akazange awanikeze amagama amandla angathathi hlangothi.

Lawa mandla acutshungulwa ngaphakathi komuntu nangaphakathi kwemvelo, amandla angathathi hlangothi ayindawo yokusekela nokulinganisela.
