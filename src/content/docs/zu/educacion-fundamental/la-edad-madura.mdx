Iminyaka yobudala obukhulile iqala eminyakeni engamashumi amathathu nanhlanu, iphele eminyakeni engamashumi amahlanu nesithupha.

Indoda esikhulile kufanele ikwazi ukubusa umuzi wayo nokuhola abantwana bayo.

Empilweni evamile, yonke indoda esikhulile iyinhloko yomndeni. Indoda engakawubumbi umuzi wayo nengcebo yayo ngesikhathi iseyintsha futhi isikhulile, ayisayikukwenza lokho, empeleni iyahluleka.

Labo abazama ukwakha umuzi nengcebo ngesikhathi sebekhulile ngempela bafanelwe isihawu.

UBUYEZA bokuhaha buyaqisa futhi bufuna ukuqongelela ingcebo enkulu. Umuntu udinga isinkwa, indawo yokukhosela, nokukhosela. Kuyadingeka ukuba nesinkwa, umuzi wakho, izingubo, amasudi, amajazi okumboza umzimba, kodwa akudingeki ukuba uqongelele imali eningi ukuze ukwazi ukuphila.

Asilweli ingcebo noma ubumpofu, kokubili lezi zimo zibonakala.

Baningi abagxumeka odakeni lobumpofu, futhi bakhona abaningi abagxumeka odakeni lwengcebo.

Kuyadingeka ukuba nengcebo encane, okungukuthi, umuzi omuhle onezingadi ezinhle, umthombo oqinisekile wemali engenayo, uhlale uhlanzekile, futhi ungalambi. Lokhu kuvamile kuwo wonke umuntu.

Ubumpofu, indlala, izifo, nokungazi akufanele kube khona ezweni elizibiza ngokuthi liyazihlonipha futhi liphucukile.

Intando yeningi ayikabikhona, kodwa sidinga ukuyidala. Uma nje kukhona isakhamuzi esisodwa esingenasinkwa, indawo yokukhosela, nendawo yokukhosela, intando yeningi empeleni iyiqeda kuphela ukuba yinto enhle.

Izinhloko zemindeni kufanele ziqonde, zihlakaniphe, zingalokothi ziphuze iwayini, zidle kakhulu, zidakwe, zibe ngamagwala, njalonjalo.

Yonke indoda esikhulile iyazi ngolwazi lwayo ukuthi abantwana balingisa isibonelo sayo nokuthi uma lesi sakamuva singalungile, siyomaketha izindlela ezingenangqondo kubantwana bayo.

Kuyisiwula ngempela ukuthi indoda esikhulile ibe nabesifazane abaningana futhi iphile ngokudakwa, amadili, imikhosi yobunqunu, njalonjalo.

Indoda esikhulile inesibopho sawo wonke umndeni, futhi kucacile ukuthi uma ihamba ngezindlela ezingalungile, izoletha ukungahleleki okwengeziwe emhlabeni, ukudideka okukhulu, ubuhlungu obukhulu.

Ubaba nomama kufanele bakuqonde umehluko phakathi kobulili. Akunangqondo ukuthi amadodakazi afunde i-physics, i-chemistry, i-algebra, njalonjalo. Ubuchopho bowesifazane buhlukile kobendoda, izifundo ezinjalo zivumelana kakhulu nobulili bobudoda, kodwa azisizi ngalutho futhi zilimaza ingqondo yabesifazane.

Kuyadingeka ukuthi obaba nomama bemindeni balwe ngenhliziyo yonke ukuze bakhuthaze ushintsho olubalulekile kuzo zonke izinhlelo zezifundo zezikole.

Owesifazane kufanele afunde ukufunda, ukubhala, ukudlala upiyano, ukuluka, ukuthunga nokukrolwa, futhi ngokuvamile zonke izinhlobo zemisebenzi yabesifazane.

Owesifazane kufanele alungiselelwe kusukela ezihlathini zesikole ukuba abe nomsebenzi ophakeme ohambisana naye njengoMAMA nanjengomfazi.

Akunangqondo ukulimaza ubuchopho babesifazane ngezifundo eziyinkimbinkimbi nezinzima ezifanele ubulili bobudoda.

Kuyadingeka ukuthi bobabili abazali bemindeni nothisha ezikoleni, emakolishi nasemayunivesithi bazikhathaze kakhulu ngokuletha owesifazane ebufazini obuhambisana naye. Kuwubuwula ukwenza amabutho owesifazane, ukubaphoqa ukuba bahambe ngamafulegi nezigubhu emigwaqweni yamadolobha njengokungathi bayimiduna.

Owesifazane kufanele abe yintokazi futhi indoda kufanele ibe yindoda.

Ubulili obuphakathi, ubutabane, bungumkhiqizo wokonakala nobugovu.

Izintokazi ezinikela ezifundweni ezinde nezinzima ziyaguga futhi akekho ozishadayo.

Empilweni yanamuhla, kufanelekile ukuthi abesifazane babe nemisebenzi emifushane, isiko lobuhle, ukuthayipha, ukubhala ngesandla, ukuthunga, i-pedagogy, njalonjalo, njalonjalo.

Ngokuvamile, owesifazane kufanele anikezelwe kuphela empilweni yasekhaya, kodwa ngenxa yobunjalo balesi sikhathi esiphila kuso, owesifazane udinga ukusebenza ukuze adle futhi aphile.

Emphakathini oziphatha ngendlela efanele futhi ophucukile, owesifazane akadingi ukusebenza ngaphandle komuzi ukuze akwazi ukuphila. Lokhu kusebenza ngaphandle komuzi kuwubukhali bezinhlobo ezimbi kakhulu.

Indoda yanamuhla eyonakele idale uhlelo lwamanga lwezinto, futhi yenze owesifazane walahlekelwa ubufazi bakhe, yamkhipha emzini wayo futhi yamguqula waba yisigqila.

Owesifazane oguqulwe waba "indoda" ngengqondo yendoda, obhema ugwayi futhi efunda iphephandaba, enqunu ingxenye eneziketi ezingaphezu kwamadolo noma edlala ibhasikidi, ungumphumela wamadoda amadeveli alesi sikhathi, inkinga yomphakathi yempucuko eyafa.

Owesifazane oguqulwe waba yinhloli yanamuhla, udokotela obhema izidakamizwa, owesifazane ongowokuqala kwezemidlalo, isidakwa, ongazimisele onqaba ukuncelisa izingane zakhe ukuze angalahlekelwa ubuhle bakhe, uyisibonakaliso esibi sempucuko yamanga.

Sekuyisikhathi sokuhlela ibutho lokusindisa umhlaba ngamadoda nabesifazane abanenhliziyo enhle abazimisele ngempela ukulwa nalolo hlelo olungamanga lwezinto.

Sekuyisikhathi sokusungula impucuko entsha emhlabeni, isiko elisha.

Owesifazane uyitshe legumbi lomuzi, futhi uma leli tshe lilinyazwe kabi, ligcwele imiphetho nokukhubazeka kwazo zonke izinhlobo, umphumela wokuphila komphakathi uzoba yinhlekelele.

Indoda ihlukile, yehlukile, futhi ngenxa yalokho ingazivumela ukutadisha umuthi, i-physics, i-chemistry, izibalo, umthetho, ubunjiniyela, i-astronomy, njalonjalo, njalonjalo.

Ikolishi lempi yezinsizwa alinangqondo, kodwa ikolishi lempi labesifazane, ngaphezu kokungabi nangqondo, libonakala liyisihluku ngokwesabekayo.

Kuyamangalisa ukubona abazoba ngabafazi besikhathi esizayo, omama besikhathi esizayo okufanele baphathe ingane phakathi kwebele labo behamba njengamadoda emizileni yomuzi.

Lokhu akukhombisi kuphela ukulahlekelwa ubufazi ebulilini, kodwa futhi kubeka umunwe esilondeni, kuveza ukulahlekelwa ubudoda endodeni.

Indoda, indoda yangempela, indoda eyiqiniso ayikwazi ukwamukela ukuthi i-Lamas umbukiso wempi wabesifazane. Ukungabaza kobudoda, i-idiosyncrasy yengqondo yendoda, umcabango wendoda uzwa isihluku sangempela ngalolu hlobo lweminyezo olukhombisa ngokugcwele ukonakala komuntu.

Sidinga ukuthi owesifazane abuyele emzini wakhe, ebufazini bakhe, ebuhleni bakhe bemvelo, ekungeneni kwakhe kokuqala, nasekululeni kwakhe kweqiniso. Sidinga ukuqeda lonke lolu hlelo lwezinto futhi sisungule empucuzweni yomhlaba entsha kanye nokubaza okusha.

Abazali nabafundisi kufanele bakwazi ukukhulisa izizukulwane ezintsha ngokuhlakanipha kweqiniso nothando.

Amadodana akufanele athole kuphela ulwazi lwengqondo futhi afunde umsebenzi noma athole iziqu zobuchwepheshe. Kuyadingeka ukuthi abafana bazi umqondo womthwalo wemfanelo futhi bahambe ngendlela yobuqotho nothando oluqaphelayo.

Emagxalabeni endoda esikhulile kuthwele umthwalo wemfanelo womfazi, abantwana, namadodakazi.

Indoda esikhulile enomqondo ophezulu womthwalo wemfanelo, ehlanzekile, ehluzekile, elinganiselwe, enesimilo esihle, njll., iyahlonishwa umndeni wayo nazo zonke izakhamizi.

Indoda esikhulile ethukuthelisa abantu ngokuphinga kwayo, ubufebe, ukungenami, ukungabi nabulungisa kwazo zonke izinhlobo, iba yinto enyanyekayo kubo bonke abantu futhi ayizibangeli kuphela ubuhlungu, kodwa futhi ibabanga abantu bomndeni wayo futhi iletha ubuhlungu nokudideka kuwo wonke umhlaba.

Kuyadingeka ukuthi indoda esikhulile ikwazi ukuphila isikhathi sayo ngendlela efanele. Kuphuthuma ukuba indoda esikhulile iqonde ukuthi ubusha sebudlulile.

Kuyahlekisa ukufuna ukuphinda ubudala obufanayo nezigameko zobusha ebuntwini.

Isikhathi ngasinye sokuphila sinobuhle baso, futhi kufanele wazi ukuthi ungabuphila kanjani.

Indoda esikhulile kufanele isebenze kanzima kakhulu ngaphambi kokuba ifike ebuntwini, njengoba nje intuthwane isebenza ngokubikezelayo ithwala amaqabunga iwayise esidlekeni sayo ngaphambi kokuba kufike ubusika obubandayo, ngendlela efanayo indoda esikhulile kufanele isebenze ngokushesha nangokuqapha.

Amadoda amaningi amancane asaphaza kabi zonke izinto zawo ezibalulekile zokuphila, futhi uma efika eminyakeni yobudala obukhulile, atholakala eyizimpumputhe, ezesabekayo, eziyizintandane, ehluleka.

Kuyahlekisa ngempela ukubona amadoda amaningi akhulile ephinda imidlalo yobusela yobusha engazi ukuthi manje ayesabekayo nokuthi ubusha sebudlulile.

Enye yezingozi ezinkulu kakhulu zalempucuko eyafa iwumkhuba wotshwala.

Ebusheni, abaningi bazinikela ekudakweni, futhi uma iminyaka yobudala obukhulile ifika, abayiwakhi umuzi, abayiwakhi ingcebo, abanalo umsebenzi okhokhelwayo, baphila besuka esitolo beya esitolo bekhulekela utshwala, besabeka, benyanyeka, beyizintandane.

Izinhloko zemindeni nabafundisi kufanele banake ngokukhethekile abantu abasha, babahlahlele ngendlela efanele ngenhloso enempilo yokwenza umhlaba ube ngcono.
