Sithole ukuthi othisha abaningi bomlando wendawo yonke entshonalanga yezwe bavame ukugcona uBUDDHA, uConfucius, uMahoma, uHermes, uQuetzacoal, uMose, uKrishna, njll.

Ngaphandle kokungabaza, siye sakwazi ukuthola ngokugcwele ukucasuka, ukuhlekisa, ukushosholoza okwenziwa othisha besilisa nabesifazane ngokumelene nezinkolo zasendulo, ngokumelene nonkulunkulu, ngokumelene nezinganekwane, njll. Konke lokho kuwukuntula ukuhlakanipha.

Ezikhungweni zemfundo, amakolishi kanye namanyuvesi, izindaba zenkolo kufanele ziphathwe ngenhlonipho eyengeziwe, ngomqondo ophezulu wokukhulekela, ngobuhlakani beqiniso bokudala.

Izindlela zenkolo zigcina izindinganiso zaphakade futhi zihlelelwa ngokwezidingo zengqondo nezomlando zabantu ngabanye, zohlanga ngalunye.

Zonke izinkolo zinezimiso ezifanayo, izindinganiso ezifanayo zaphakade futhi zihlukile kuphela ngefomu.

Akuhlakaniphile ukuthi umKristu ahlekise ngenkolo yeBuddha noma ngenkolo yamaHeberu noma yamaHindu ngoba zonke izinkolo zisekelwe ezisekelweni ezifanayo.

Ukulingisa okwenziwa izifundiswa eziningi ngezinkolo nabasunguli bazo kungenxa yobuthi beMARXIST obuthi ngalezi zinsuku budambise zonke izingqondo ezibuthakathaka.

Othisha ezikoleni, emakolishi naseyunivesithi kufanele baqondise abafundi babo endleleni yenhlonipho yeqiniso ngomunye nomunye.

Kuyingozi futhi akufaneleki ukuthi umuntu oyisilima ogameni lenkolelo yohlobo oluthile, ahlekise ngamathempeli enkolo, amahlelo, izikole noma izinhlangano ezingokomoya.

Lapho beshiya amakilasi okufunda, abafundi kufanele babhekane nabantu bazo zonke izinkolo, izikole, amahlelo futhi akukona ukuhlakanipha ukuthi abazi nokuthi bangaziphatha kanjani ethempelini.

Lapho beshiya amakilasi ngemuva kweminyaka eyishumi noma eyishumi nanhlanu yokufunda, intsha itholakala ipholile futhi ilele njengabanye abantu, igcwele ubuze futhi intula ukuhlakanipha njengosuku lokuqala bangena esikoleni.

Kuphuthuma ukuthi abafundi phakathi kwezinye izinto bathuthukise isikhungo semizwa ngoba akuyona yonke into eyintando. Kuyadingeka ukuthi kufundwe ukuzwa ukuzwana okusondele kwempilo, ubuhle bomuthi olimele yedwa, iculo lenyoni encane ehlathini, i-symphony yomculo nemibala yokushona kwelanga okuhle.

Kuyadingeka futhi ukuzwa nokuqonda ngokujulile zonke izinhlukano ezimbi zempilo, njengoba ziyinhlelo yomphakathi enonya nengenamusa yalesi sikhathi esiphila kuso, imigwaqo egcwele omama abangenalwazi abanezingane zabo ezingondlekile nezilambile becela ucezu lwesinkwa, izakhiwo ezimbi lapho kuhlala khona izinkulungwane zemindeni empofu, imigwaqo enengekayo lapho kuzungeza khona izinkulungwane zezimoto eziqhutshwa ngalabo baphethiloli abalimaza izinto eziphilayo, njll.

Umfundi oshiya amakilasi kufanele abhekane hhayi kuphela nobugovu bakhe siqu nezinkinga zakhe, kodwa futhi nobugovu babo bonke abantu nezinkinga eziningi zomphakathi wesintu.

Okubi kunakho konke ukuthi umfundi oshiya amakilasi, noma enokulungiselela ngengqondo, akanabo ubuhlakani, unembeza wakhe ulele, akakulungiselele kahle ukulwa nempilo.

Sekuyisikhathi sokucwaninga nokuthola ukuthi yini le nto ebizwa ngokuthi UBUHLAKANI. Isichazamazwi, i-encyclopedia, iphelelwa amandla okuchaza ngokungathi sína UBUHLAKANI.

Ngaphandle kobuhlakani akukaze kube khona ushintsho olukhulu noma injabulo yeqiniso futhi kuyivelakancane empilweni ukuthola abantu abahlakaniphile ngempela.

Okubalulekile empilweni akukhona nje ukwazi igama elithi UBUHLAKANI, kodwa ukuzwa nathi ngokwethu incazelo yalo ejulile.

Baningi abazenza abahlakaniphile, akekho ovilaphayo ongazenzi ohlakaniphile futhi uCarlos Marx ezenza ohlakaniphe ngokweqile, wabhala intlekisa yakhe yokuthanda izinto ezibonakalayo ebiye yabiza umhlaba ukulahlekelwa izindinganiso zaphakade, ukudubula izinkulungwane zabapristi bezinkolo ezahlukahlukene, ukudlwengula izindela, amaBuddha, amaKristu, njll., ukubhujiswa kwamathempeli amaningi, ukuhlukunyezwa kwezinkulungwane nezigidi zabantu, njll njll. njll.

Noma ngubani angazenza ohlakaniphile, okunzima ukuba yiyo ngempela.

Akukhona ukuthola ulwazi oluningi oluvela ezincwadini, ulwazi oluthe xaxa, okuhlangenwe nakho okwengeziwe, izinto eziningi zokujabulisa abantu, imali eyengeziwe yokuthenga amajaji namaphoyisa; njll. njengoba kuzofeza lokho okubizwa ngokuthi UBUHLAKANI.

Akukona ngalokho OKUNINGI, njengoba ungafinyelela ekubeni UBUHLAKANI. Baphambuke ngokuphelele labo abacabanga ukuthi ubuhlakani bungatholakala ngenqubo YEZINYE.

Kuphuthuma ukuqonda ngokujulile kuzo zonke izindawo zengqondo ezingaphansi kwengqondo nezingaziqondi, ukuthi iyini leyo nqubo eyingozi YEZINYE, ngoba ekujuleni kufihlwe ngasese kakhulu i-EGO ethandekayo, i-ME, i-SELF YAMI, efisa futhi efuna njalo OKUNINGI KANYE NOKUNINGI ukuze ikhuluphale futhi iqine.

Lo Mefistófeles esinawo ngaphakathi, lelo SATAN, lelo ME, lithi: NGINEMALI OKUNINGI, ubuhle obuningi, ubuhlakani obuningi kunalowo, udumo oluthe xaxa, ubuqili obuningi, njll njll. njll.

Noma ngubani ofuna ngempela ukuqonda ukuthi buyini UBUHLAKANI, kufanele afunde ukubuzwa, kufanele abuphile futhi abuzwe ngokuzindla okujulile.

Konke okubuthwa abantu phakathi kwethuna elibolile lenkumbulo engathembekile, ulwazi lwengqondo, okuhlangenwe nakho kwempilo, kuhlala kuhunyushwa ngokuyingozi ekugcineni kokuthi OKUNINGI NOKUNINGI. Ngakho-ke abaze bayazi incazelo ejulile yakho konke lokho abakuqoqayo.

Abaningi bafunda incwadi bese beyibeka phakathi kwememori, benelisekile ngokuthi sebeqoqe ulwazi oluthe xaxa, kodwa lapho bebizwa ukuthi baphendule ngemfundiso ebhalwe encwadini abayifundile, kuvela ukuthi abayazi incazelo ejulile yemfundo, kodwa I-ME ifuna ulwazi oluthe xaxa nokuningi, izincwadi eziningi ezengeziwe noma ngabe ayikabuzi imfundiso yanoma yiziphi zazo.

Ubuhlakani abutholakali ngolwazi oluthe xaxa oluvela ezincwadini, noma ngolwazi oluthe xaxa, noma ngemali eyengeziwe, noma ngedumela elikhulu, ubuhlakani bungaqhakaza kithi lapho siqonda yonke inqubo YE-ME, lapho siqonda ngokujulile yonke leyo ndlela yokuzenzekela engokwengqondo YOKUNINGI.

Kubalulekile ukuqonda ukuthi ingqondo iyisikhungo esiyisisekelo SOKUNINGI. Ngempela lokho OKUNINGI kuyi-ME yengqondo efanayo eyidingayo futhi ingqondo iyisizinda sayo esiyinhloko.

Noma ngubani ofuna ukuba ohlakaniphile ngempela, kufanele azimisele ukufa hhayi kuphela ezingeni elingenasisekelo lengqondo, kodwa futhi nazo zonke izindawo ezingaphansi kwengqondo nezingaziqondi zengqondo.

Lapho i-ME ifa, lapho i-Y0 ichitheka ngokuphelele okuwukuphela kwento esala ngaphakathi kwethu yi-SER yeqiniso, i-SER yeqiniso, ubuhlakani obungokomthetho obufiseleka kakhulu futhi obunzima kakhulu

Abantu bakholelwa ukuthi ingqondo iyakha, bayaphambuka. I-ME ayilona umdali futhi ingqondo iyisizinda esiyinhloko se-ME.

Ubuhlakani buyadala ngoba bungobuka-SER, buyimfanelo ka-SER. Akufanele sidide ingqondo NOBUHLAKANI.

Baphambuke ngendlela EJULILE nangefomu elikhulu labo abacabanga ukuthi UBUHLAKANI buyinto engatshalwa njengembali yasendlini yezitshalo noma into engathengwa njengoba kuthengwa izihloko zobukhosi noma ngokuba nomtapo wezincwadi omkhulu.

Kuyadingeka ukuqonda ngokujulile zonke izinqubo zengqondo, konke ukusabela, lokho OKUNINGI okungokwengqondo okubuthayo, njll. Kuphela lapho kuvela khona ngaphakathi kwethu ngendlela engokwemvelo neyokuzithandela ilangabi elivuthayo LOBUHLAKANI.

Njengoba uMefistófeles esimthwele ngaphakathi eya echitheka, umlilo wobuhlakani bokudala uya ubonakala kancane kancane ngaphakathi kwethu, kuze kukhanye kakhulu.

I-SER yethu yeqiniso WUTHANDO futhi kulelo THANDO kuzalwa UBUHLAKANI beqiniso nobungokomthetho okungekona isikhathi.
