Kuyabonakala ke ukuthi UKWESABA nokufuna UKUPHEPHA kuhlale kuhlobene kakhulu.

Umuntu owesaba ukuhlaselwa izigebengu ufuna ukuphepha ngesibhamu sakhe.

Izwe elesaba ukuhlaselwa elinye izwe, lizothenga izibhamu, izindiza, imikhumbi yempi futhi lihlomise amabutho lizilungiselele impi.

Abantu abaningi abangakwazi ukusebenza, besaba ubumpofu bafuna ukuphepha ecaleni, babe ngamasela, abaphangi, njll...

Abesifazane abaningi abantula ubuhlakani besaba ubumpofu baphenduke izifebe.

Indoda enamawala yesaba ukulahlekelwa umkayo ifuna ukuphepha ngesibhamu, ibulale bese kuba sobala ukuthi izogcina ejele.

Umfazi onomona ubulala imbangi yakhe noma umyeni wakhe kanjalo abe ngumbulali.

Uyesaba ukulahlekelwa umyeni wakhe futhi efuna ukumqinisekisa ubulala omunye noma enqume ukumbulala.

Umninimuzi owesaba ukuthi abantu ngeke bamkhokhele irenti yendlu, udinga izinkontileka, ababambeli, idiphozithi, njll., efuna ukuziqinisekisa kanjalo futhi uma umfelokazi ompofu ogcwele izingane engakwazi ukugcwalisa lezi zimfuneko ezesabekayo, futhi uma bonke abaninindlu edolobheni benza okufanayo, ekugcineni ompofu kuzodingeka alale nezingane zakhe emgwaqweni noma emapaki edolobheni.
