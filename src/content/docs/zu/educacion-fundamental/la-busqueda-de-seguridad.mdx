Lapho amachwane esaba, azifihla ngaphansi kwamaphiko omama enkukhu afuna ukulondeka.

Ingane esethukile iyagijima iyofuna unina ngoba iseduze kwakhe izizwa iphephile.

Ngakho-ke kufakazelwe ukuthi UKWESABA nokufuna UKULONDEKA kuhlale kuhlobene kakhulu.

Umuntu owesaba ukuhlaselwa yizigebengu ufuna ukulondeka ngesibhamu sakhe.

Izwe elesaba ukuhlaselwa ngelinye izwe, lizothenga izibhamu, izindiza, imikhumbi yempi futhi lihlome amabutho lizilungiselele impi.

Abantu abaningi abangakwazi ukusebenza, besaba ubumpofu bafuna ukulondeka ebugebengwini, bese beba amasela, abahlaseli, njll...

Abesifazane abaningi abangenabo ubuhlakani besaba ubumpofu baphenduka izifebe.

Indoda enomona yesaba ukulahlekelwa umkayo futhi ifuna ukulondeka ngesibhamu, iyabulala bese kuthi ngemuva kwalokho iyoboshwa ejele.

Umfazi onomona ubulala imbangi yakhe noma umyeni wakhe abe umbulali.

Uyesaba ukulahlekelwa umyeni wakhe futhi efuna ukuziqinisekisa ngaye ubulala omunye noma azinqumele ukumbulala.

Umninindlu owesaba ukuthi abantu ngeke bayikhokhele imali yokuqasha indlu, ufuna izinkontileka, abaqinisekisi, amadiphozithi, njll., efuna ukuziqinisekisa kanjalo futhi uma umfelokazi ompofu ogcwele izingane engakwazi ukugcwalisa lezi zimfuneko ezinkulu, futhi uma bonke abaninindlu bomuzi benza okufanayo, ekugcineni lowo ntokazi uzodingeka ukuthi ayolala nezingane zakhe emgwaqweni noma ezingadini zomuzi.

Zonke izimpi ziqala ngokwesaba.

AmaGestapo, ukuhlukunyezwa, izinkambi zokuhlushwa, amaSiberia, amajele amabi, ukudingiswa, umsebenzi onzima, ukudutshulwa, njll. kuqala ngokwesaba.

Izizwe zihlasela ezinye izizwe ngenxa yokwesaba; zifuna ukulondeka ngobudlova, zicabanga ukuthi ngokubulala, ngokuhlasela, njll. zingazenza ziphephe, ziqine, zibe namandla.

Emahhovisi amaphoyisa ayimfihlo, ezokulwa nokuhlola, njll. empumalanga nasentshonalanga, izinhloli ziyahlukunyezwa, kuyesatshwa ngazo, zifuna ukuzivuma icala ngenhloso yokuthola ukulondeka koMbuso.

Wonke amacala, zonke izimpi, bonke ubugebengu, baqala ngokwesaba nokufuna ukulondeka.

Ezikhathini ezedlule kwakukhona ubuqotho phakathi kwabantu, namuhla ukwesaba nokufuna ukulondeka kukuqedile ukunuka kamnandi okumangalisayo kobuqotho.

Umngane uyamsola umngane wakhe, uyesaba ukuthi uzomeba, amkhohlise, amsebenzise kabi futhi kukhona neziqu ezisilima neziyingozi ezinjengokuthi: "UNGALOKOTHI UJIKISE UMHLANE WAKHO KUMNGANE WAKHO OMKHULU". AbakwaHITLER babethi lesi SIQU siyigolide.

Sekuyaye umngane amesabe umngane wakhe aze asebenzise neZIQU ukuze azivikele. Akusekho buqotho phakathi kwabangane. Ukwesaba nokufuna ukulondeka kukuqedile ukunuka kamnandi kobuqotho.

UCastro Rus eCuba udubule izinkulungwane zezakhamuzi ngoba esaba ukuthi zizomyeka; UCastro ufuna ukulondeka ngokudubula. Ukholelwa ukuthi angakuthola kanjalo ukulondeka.

UStalin, uStalin omubi nonesihluku, wangcolisa iRussia ngokuhlanzwa kwegazi lakhe. Leyo kwakuyindlela yokufuna ukulondeka kwakhe.

UHitler wahlela amaGestapo, amaGestapo amabi, ukuze kulondeke uMbuso. Akungabazeki ukuthi wayesaba ukuthi uzokhishwa esikhundleni yingakho asungula amaGestapo anegazi.

Zonke izinhlungu zaleli zwe ziqala ngokwesaba nokufuna ukulondeka.

Otitshalakazi notitshala besikole kufanele bafundise abafundi ubuhle besibindi.

Kuyadabukisa ukuthi abafana namantombazane bagcwaliswa ukwesaba kusukela emakhaya abo.

Abafana namantombazane bayasongelwa, bayatshaywa, bayesatshiswa, bayashaywa ngenduku, njll.

Kuyisiko labazali nomama ukwesabisa ingane nensha ngenhloso yokuthi ifunde.

Ngokuvamile kuthiwa ezinganeni nakubantu abasha ukuthi uma bengafundi kuzodingeka ukuthi bacele, bazulazule belambile emigwaqweni, benze imisebenzi ephansi kakhulu njengokugeza izicathulo, ukuthwala imithwalo, ukuthengisa amaphephandaba, ukusebenza ekulimeni, njll. njll. njll. (Kunjengokungathi umsebenzi uyicala)

Ekujuleni, ngemuva kwazo zonke lezi zinkulumo zabazali notitshala, kukhona ukwesaba ngendodana nokufuna ukulondeka kwendodana.

Okubi ngakho konke lokhu esikushoyo ukuthi ingane nensha bayazidelela, bagcwala ukwesaba futhi kamuva empilweni yangempela baba abantu abagwele ukwesaba.

Abazali notitshala abanokunambitheka okubi kokwesabisa abafana namantombazane, intsha nezintombi, ngendlela engaqondile bayabaholela endleleni yobugebengu, ngoba njengoba sesishilo, bonke ubugebengu buqala ngokwesaba nokufuna ukulondeka.

Namuhla UKWESABA nokufuna UKULONDEKA kwenze iplanethi yomhlaba yaba yisihogo esesabekayo. Wonke umuntu uyesaba. Wonke umuntu ufuna ukulondeka.

Ezikhathini ezedlule wawungahamba ngokukhululeka, manje imingcele igcwele onogada abahlomile, kudingeka amaphasipoti nezitifiketi zazo zonke izinhlobo ukuze ube nelungelo lokudlula ukusuka kwelinye izwe uya kwelinye.

Konke lokhu kuwumphumela wokwesaba nokufuna UKULONDEKA. Kuyesatshwa ohambayo, kuyesatshwa ofikayo futhi kufunwa ukulondeka kumaphasipoti namaphepha azo zonke izinhlobo.

Otitshalakazi notitshala basesikoleni, emakolishi, emayunivesithi kufanele bakuqonde ukwesabeka kwakho konke lokhu futhi basebenzisane ukuze kube nenzuzo yomhlaba, ngokwazi ukufundisa izizukulwane ezintsha, bebafundisa indlela yesibindi sangempela.

KUYADINGEKA ngokushesha ukufundisa izizukulwane ezintsha ukuthi zingesabi futhi zingafuni ukulondeka kunoma yini noma kunoma ubani.

Kuyadingeka ukuthi wonke umuntu afunde ukuzethemba ngokwengeziwe.

UKWESABA nokufuna UKULONDEKA kuwubuthakathaka obubi kakhulu obenze impilo yaba yisihogo esesabekayo.

Kuyo yonke indawo kunenqwaba yamagwala, abesabi, ababuthakathaka abahlala befuna UKULONDEKA.

Kuyesatshwa impilo, kuyesatshwa ukufa, kuyesatshwa lokho okuzoshiwo, "lokho okuthiwa kuthiwa", ukulahlekelwa isikhundla senhlalo, isikhundla sezombangazwe, udumo, imali, indlu enhle, umfazi omuhle, umyeni omuhle, umsebenzi, ibhizinisi, umonopoli, ifenisha, imoto, njll. njll. njll. yonke into iyesatshwa, kunenqwaba yamagwala, abesabi, ababuthakathaka yonke indawo, kodwa akekho ozicabanga njengegwala, wonke umuntu uthi uqotho, unesibindi, njll.

Kuzo zonke izigaba zenhlalo kukhona izinkulungwane nezigidi zezintshisakalo okwesatshwa ukuzilahlekela futhi ngenxa yalokho wonke umuntu ufuna ukulondeka okuthi ngokuba nzima kakhulu, kuyenze impilo ibe nzima ngokwengeziwe, ibe nzima ngokwengeziwe, ibe buhlungu ngokwengeziwe, inesihluku futhi ayizweli.

Konke ukuhleba, konke ukunyundela, izinyathelo, njll., ziqala ngokwesaba nokufuna ukulondeka.

Ukuze kungalahlekelwa ingcebo, isikhundla, amandla, udumo, kusatshalaliswa inkohliso, izindaba ezingekho, kuyabulawa, kuyakhokhwa ukuthi kubulawe ngasese, njll.

Abanamandla omhlaba baze bazitike ngokuba nababulali abaqashiwe futhi abakhokhelwa kahle, ngenhloso embi yokususa wonke umuntu osongela ukubafiphaza.

Bayawathanda amandla ngenxa yamandla ngokwawo futhi baziqinisekisa ngemali nangegazi eliningi.

Amaphephandaba ahlala enikeza izindaba ngezigameko eziningi zokuzibulala.

Abaningi bakholelwa ukuthi ozibulalayo unesibindi kodwa empeleni ozibulalayo uyigwala elesaba impilo futhi elifuna ukulondeka ezingalweni eziwohlokile zokufa.

Amanye amaqhawe empi ayaziwa njengabantu ababuthakathaka namagwala, kodwa lapho bebhekene ubuso nobuso nokufa, ukwesaba kwabo kwakukukhulu kangangokuthi baguquka baba yizilo ezibi ezifuna ukulondeka empilweni yabo, benza umzamo omkhulu wokulwa nokufa. Bese kuthiwa bangAMAPHARA.

Ukwesaba kuvame ukudideka nesibindi. Ozibulalayo ubonakala enesibindi kakhulu, olayisha isibhamu ubonakala enesibindi kakhulu, kodwa eqinisweni abazibulalayo nabahlomile bangamagwala kakhulu.

Lowo ongesabi impilo akazibulali. Lowo ongesabi muntu akalayishi isibhamu okhalweni.

KUYADINGEKA ngokushesha ukuthi otitshalakazi notitshala besikole bafundise isakhamuzi ngendlela ecacile futhi eqondile, ukuthi SIBINDI sangempela siyini nokuthi ukwesaba kuyini.
