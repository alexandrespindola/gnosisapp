Inkinga enkulu kakhulu yeZEZIMFUNDO ZOMPHAKATHI ayibo abafundi basesikoleni samabanga aphansi, esesekondari noma esezingeni eliphezulu, kodwa yibo ABAZALI kanye NOTHISHA.

Uma Abazali Nothisha bengazazi bona, bengakwazi ukuqonda umntwana, uma bengakwazi ukuqonda ngokujulile ubudlelwano babo nalezi zidalwa ezisanda kuqala ukuphila, uma bekhathazeka nje ngokuhlomisa izingqondo zabafundi babo, singakwazi kanjani ukudala uhlobo olusha lwemfundo?

Umntwana, umfundi, uya eSikoleni eyothola isiqondiso esiqondayo, kodwa uma Othisha banomqondo omncane, belondolozela, belwa nezinguquko, bevimba intuthuko, umfundi uyoba njalo.

Abafundisi kufanele bazifundise kabusha, bazazi bona, babuyekeze lonke ulwazi lwabo, baqonde ukuthi singena eNkathini Entsha.

Ngokuguqulwa kwabafundisi, kuguqulwa nemfundo yomphakathi.

UKUFUNDISA UMQEQESHI yinto enzima kakhulu ngoba wonke umuntu ofunde kakhulu, wonke umuntu onayo imiqulu, wonke umuntu okumele afundise, osebenza njengothisha eSikoleni, useyilokho ayikho, ingqondo yakhe isivaleke izinkulungwane ezingamashumi amahlanu zemibono ayifundile futhi ayisashintshi nakancane.

Othisha kufanele bafundise INDLELA YOKUCABANGA, ngeshwa bakhathazeka nje ngokubafundisa LOKHO OKUFANELE BAKUCABANGE.

Abazali Nothisha baphila begcwele izinkathazo ezimbi zezomnotho, zezenhlalo, zemizwa, njll.

Abazali Nothisha bagxile kakhulu ezinkingeni zabo siqu nosizi, abanandaba ngempela ngokutadisha nokuxazulula izinkinga ezivezwa abafana namantombazane "WESIZUKULU ESISHA".

Kunokwehla okukhulu kwengqondo, kokuziphatha nokwenhlalo, kodwa abazali nothisha bagcwele ukukhathazeka nokukhathazeka komuntu siqu futhi banesikhathi sokucabanga kuphela ngesimo sezomnotho sezingane, ukubanikeza umsebenzi ukuze bangafi yindlala futhi yilokho kuphela.

Ngokuphambene nenkolelo evamile, iningi labazali alibathandi ngempela abantwana babo, ukuba bebathanda, bebezolwela inhlalakahle yomphakathi, bezokhathazeka ngezinkinga ZEZEZIMFUNDO ZOMPHAKATHI ngenhloso yokufeza ushintsho lwangempela.

Uma Abazali bebethanda ngempela izingane zabo, bekungeke kube khona izimpi, umndeni nesizwe bekungeke kugqame kakhulu ngokumelene nomhlaba wonke, ngoba lokhu kudala izinkinga, izimpi, ukuhlukana okuyingozi, isimo esibi sezingane zethu.

Abantu bayafunda, bazilungiselela ukuba odokotela, onjiniyela abameli, njll. kodwa abazilungiseleli umsebenzi obucayi futhi onzima kakhulu okungukuba Abazali.

Lobobugovu bomndeni, lokho kuntuleka kothando kubantu esiphila nabo, leyomgomo wokuzihlukanisa komndeni, awunangqondo ngamaphesenti ayikhulu, ngoba uphenduka isici sokonakala nokwehla kwenhlalakahle okuqhubekayo.

Inqubekela phambili, iNguquko yeqiniso, kungenzeka kuphela ngokuwisa lezozindonga zaseChina ezidumile ezisehlukanisayo, ezisihlukanisa nomhlaba wonke.

Sonke SIWUMNDENI OWODWA futhi akuvamile ukuzihlupheka, ukucabanga njengomndeni abantu abambalwa kuphela abahlala nathi, njll.

Ukukhetha UBUMNEME BOMNDENI kuvimbela inqubekela phambili yezenhlalo, kuhlukanisa abantu, kudala izimpi, izigaba, amalungelo, izinkinga zezomnotho, njll.

Lapho Abazali bethanda ngempela izingane zabo, izindonga, izingcingo ezinyanyekayo zokuzihlukanisa ziyowa zibe uthuli bese umndeni uyeka ukuba yisiyingi sobugovu nesiwubuwula.

Ngokuwa kwezindonga zobugovu zomndeni, khona-ke kunobudlelwano bobuzalwane nabo bonke abanye abazali, nothisha, nabo bonke umphakathi.

Umphumela WOBUZALWANE BEQINISO, USHINTSHO LWEZENHLALAKAHLE LOQINISO, IGUQUKO YANGEMPELA yegatsha LEZEMFUNDO yomhlaba ongcono.

UMFUNDISI kufanele aqaphele kakhulu, kufanele aqoqe Abazali, iBhodi Labaqondisi LabaZali futhi abakhulumele ngokucacile.

Kuyadingeka ukuthi Abazali baqonde ukuthi umsebenzi wezemfundo yomphakathi ufezeka ngesisekelo esiqinile sokubambisana phakathi kwabazali nothisha.

Kuyadingeka ukutshela Abazali ukuthi IMFUNDO EYISISEKELO iyadingeka ukuze kuphakanyiswe iZizukulwane EZINTSHA.

Kubalulekile ukutshela Abazali ukuthi ukuqeqeshwa kwezingqondo kuyadingeka kodwa akukona konke, kudingeka okungaphezulu, kudingeka ukufundisa abafana namantombazane ukuzazi, ukwazi amaphutha abo siqu nokushiyeka kwabo ngokwengqondo.

Kufanele sitshele Abazali ukuthi izingane kufanele zizalwe NGENHLOSO HAWU HAWU hhayi NGENKANUKO YEZILWANE.

Kuya kuba nenzondo nonya ukudlulisa izifiso zethu zezilwane, izinkanuko zethu zobulili ezinobudlova, imizwa yethu egulayo nemizwelo yezilwane kwabazukulu bethu.

Amadodana Namadodakazi yimizamo yethu siqu futhi kuyisenzo sobugilwazi ukuthelela uMhlaba ngemizamo yezilwane.

Othisha beziKolo, amaKolishi namaYunivesithi kufanele bahlangane ehholo lezinkundla, nabo bonke Abazali ngenhloso enhle yokubafundisa indlela yomthwalo wemfanelo wokuziphatha ezinganeni zabo nakubantu kanye noMhlaba.

ABAFUNDISI banesibopho sokUZIFUNDISA kabusha nokuqondisa Abazali.

Sidinga ukuthanda ngempela ukuze siguqule umhlaba. Sidinga ukuhlangana ukuze sakhe phakathi kwethu sonke, iThempeli elimangalisayo leNkathi Entsha okwamanje esiqala phakathi kokuduma okukhulu kwemicabango.
