Inkinga enkulu kakhulu YE-EMFUNDO YOMPHAKATHI ayibona abafundi bamabanga aphansi, esekondari noma abasekolishi, kodwa ABAZALI NABAFUNDISI.

Uma Abazali Nabafundisi bengazazi bona, bengakwazi ukuqonda umntwana, intombazane, uma bengakwazi ukuqonda ngokujulile ubudlelwano babo nalezi zidalwa eziqala ukuphila, uma bekhathazeka kuphela ngokuhlakulela ingqondo yabafundi babo, singayakha kanjani uhlobo olusha lwemfundo?

Umntwana, umfundi, intombazane, uya eSikoleni ukuthola isiqondiso esiqaphelayo, kodwa uma Abafundisi, Othisha, benomqondo omncane, bengamagugu endabuko, bengabalandeli bobudlova, bengabavimbi intuthuko, kuyoba njalo nomfundi, intombazane.

Abafundisi kumele bafundiswe kabusha, bazazi bona, babuyekeze lonke ulwazi lwabo, baqonde ukuthi singena eNkathini Entsha.

Uma abafundisi beziguqula, imfundo yomphakathi iyaguquka.

UKUFUNDISA UMFUNDISI yinto enzima kakhulu ngoba wonke umuntu ofunde kakhulu, wonke umuntu onayo i-degree wonke umuntu okumele afundise, osebenza njengothisha weSikole, useyilokho ayikho, ingqondo yakhe ivaleke ngemibono engamashumi ayisihlanu ayizinkulungwane ayifundile futhi ayisaguquki ngisho nangezibhamu.

Othisha kumele bafundise INDLELA YOKUCABANGA, kodwa ngeshwa bakhathazeka kuphela ngokubafundisa OKUMELE BACABANGE NGAKHO.

Abazali Nabafundisi baphila begcwele izinkinga ezimbi zezomnotho, zenhlalo, zemizwa, njll.

Abazali Nabafundisi bagxile kakhulu kwezabo izingxabano nezinhlungu, abanangayo intshisekelo yangempela yokufunda nokuxazulula izinkinga ezibekwa abafana namantombazane "BESIZUKULWANE ESISHA".

Kukhona ukonakala okukhulu kwengqondo, okuziphatha nokwenhlalo, kodwa abazali nothisha bagcwele ukukhathazeka nezinkinga zomuntu siqu futhi banesikhathi kuphela sokucabanga ngohlangothi lwezomnotho lwezingane zabo, ukubanika umsebenzi ukuze bangafi yindlala futhi yikho kuphela.

Ngokuphambene nenkolelo evamile, iningi labazali alizithandi ngempela izingane zabo, ukuba bebezithanda, bebezolwela inhlalakahle evamile, bezokukhathalela izinkinga ZE-EMFUNDO YOMPHAKATHI ngenhloso yokufeza ushintsho lweqiniso.

Ukuba abazali bebeyithanda ngempela izingane zabo, bekungeke kube khona izimpi, bekungeke kugqanyiswe umndeni nesizwe ngokuphambene nomhlaba wonke, ngoba lokhu kudala izinkinga, izimpi, ukuhlukana okuyingozi, imvelo esabekayo ezinganeni zethu.

Abantu bayafunda, bazilungiselela ukuba odokotela, onjiniyela abameli, njll. kanti kunalokho abazilungiseleli umsebenzi omubi kakhulu futhi onzima kunayo yonke okungukuthi ukuba Abazali.

Lobobugovu bomndeni, lokho kuntuleka kothando ngabanye abantu, leyonqubomgomo yokuzihlukanisa komndeni, ayinangqondo ngamaphesenti ayikhulu, ngoba iphenduka isici sokonakala nokonakala okuqhubekayo komphakathi.

Intuthuko, iNguquko yangempela, kungenzeka kuphela ngokudiliza lezo zindonga ezidumile zaseShayina ezisehlukanisayo, ezisihlukanisa nomhlaba wonke.

Sonke SINGUMNDENI OWODWA futhi akunangqondo ukuzihlukumeza, ukucabangela njengomndeni kuphela abantu abambalwa abahlala nathi, njll.

Ukukhetha OKUNOBUGOVU KOMNDENI kuvimba intuthuko yomphakathi, kuhlukanisa abantu, kudala izimpi, izigaba ezinamalungelo, izinkinga zezomnotho, njll.

Uma abazali bebezithanda ngempela izingane zabo, bebezowa njengothuli izindonga, izithiyo ezinengekayo zokuzihlukanisa futhi lapho-ke umndeni ubezoyeka ukuba yindilinga enobugovu nengenangqondo.

Uma kuwile izindonga zobugovu zomndeni, khona-ke kube khona ubudlelwano bobuzalwane nabo bonke abanye obaba nomama, noThisha, nabo bonke umphakathi.

Umphumela WOBUNYE BANGEMPELA, UKUGUQUKA KOMPHAKATHI KWEQINISO, INGUQUKO yangempela yegatsha LEMFUNDO yomhlaba ongcono.

UMFUNDISI kumele aqaphele kakhulu, kumele abuthe Abazali kanye noMama, iBhodi Labaqondisi Labazali, abatshele ngokucacile.

Kuyadingeka ukuthi Abazali baqonde ukuthi umsebenzi wemfundo yomphakathi wenziwa ngesisekelo esiqinile sokubambisana phakathi Kwabazali NoThisha.

Kuyadingeka ukutshela Abazali ukuthi IMFUNDO EYISISEKELO iyadingeka ukuze kuphakanyiswe Izizukulwane Ezintsha.

Kubalulekile ukutshela Abazali ukuthi ukuqeqeshwa kwengqondo kuyadingeka kodwa akukhona konke, kudingeka okuthile okwengeziwe, kudingeka ukufundisa abafana namantombazane ukuzazi, ukwazi amaphutha abo siqu ukushiyeka kwabo siqu Ngokwengqondo.

Kumele sitshele Abazali ukuthi izingane kumele zizalwe NGE-UTHANDO hhayi NGE-USHUKELA WEZILWANE.

Kubuhlungu futhi kuyisihluku ukubeka izifiso zethu zezilwane, izifiso zethu zobulili ezinodlame, ukuthanda izinto ezibuhlungu nemizwelo yezilwane kulandelana lwethu.

Amadodana Namadodakazi yizinto esizibonakalisa zona futhi kuyicala ukuthelela Umhlaba ngezinto zezilwane.

Othisha bezikole, amakolishi namayunivesithi kumele bahlanganise ehholo lemibukiso, Abazali kanye noMama ngenhloso enempilo yokubafundisa indlela yokuziphatha kahle nezingane zabo kanye nabaMphakathi kanye noMhlaba.

ABAFUNDISI banesibopho SOKUZIFUNDISA kabusha futhi baqondise Abazali kanye noMama.

Sidinga ukuthanda ngempela ukuze siguqule umhlaba. Sidinga ukuhlangana ukuze siphakamise phakathi kwethu sonke, iThempeli elimangalisayo leNkathi Entsha eqala ngalezi zikhathi phakathi kokuduma okukhulu komcabango.
