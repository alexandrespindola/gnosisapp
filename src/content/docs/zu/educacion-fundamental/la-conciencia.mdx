Abantu bavame ukudidanisa UKWAZI ne-INTELIJENSI noma ne-INTELLECT, futhi umuntu ohlakaniphe kakhulu noma one-intellectual kakhulu, bamnikeza isiqu sokuthi wazi kakhulu.

Thina sithi UKWAZI kumuntu, ngaphandle kokungabaza nangaphandle kokwesaba ukukhohliseka, kuyindlela ekhetheke kakhulu YOKUTHOLA ULWAZI LWANGAPHAKATHI oluzimele ngokuphelele kuwo wonke umsebenzi wengqondo.

Ikhono LOKWAZI lisivumela ukuthi sazi NGOKWETHU.

UKWAZI kusinika ulwazi oluphelele ngalokho OKUYIKO, lapho kukhona, ngalokho okwaziwa ngempela, ngalokho okungaziwa ngokuqinisekile.

I-PSYCHOLOGY EGUQUKELAYO ifundisa ukuthi umuntu kuphela ongazazi yena.

Ngu thina kuphela esingazi ukuthi siyazazi yini ngesikhathi esithile noma cha.

Ngumuntu oyedwa kuphela ongazi ngokwazi kwakhe, nokuthi kukhona yini ngesikhathi esithile noma cha.

Umuntu ngokwakhe, futhi kungekho omunye ngaphandle kwakhe, angabona okwesikhashana, okomzuzwana ukuthi ngaphambi kwaleso sikhathi, ngaphambi kwaleso sikhathi, wayengazi ngempela, wayenokwazi kwakhe kulele kakhulu, kamuva uzokhohlwa lokho okuhlangenwe nakho noma akugcine njengenkumbulo, njengenkumbulo yokuhlangenwe nakho okunamandla.

Kuphuthuma ukwazi ukuthi UKWAZI esilwaneni ESIZIZWAYO akuyona into eqhubekayo, ehlala njalo.

Ngokuvamile UKWAZI esilwaneni SEZINGQONDO esibizwa ngumuntu, lulele ngokujulile.

Ziyivelakancane, ziyivelakancane izikhathi lapho UKWAZI kuvukile; isilwane esihlakaniphile siyasebenza, sishayela izimoto, siyashada, siyafa, njll. sinokwazi kulele ngokuphelele futhi ngezikhathi ezimbalwa kakhulu kuyavuka:

Impilo yomuntu yimpilo yamaphupho, kodwa ukholelwa ukuthi uphapheme futhi akasoze avuma ukuthi uyaphupha, nokuthi unokwazi kulele.

Uma othile ebengavuka, ubengazizwa enenhloni kakhulu ngaye, ubengaqonda ngokushesha ubudlabha bakhe, ubuwula bakhe.

Le mpilo iyahlukumeza ngendlela eyesabekayo, iyinhlekelele ngendlela eyesabekayo futhi ayivamile ukuba yinhle.

Uma isibhakela besingavuka ngokushesha phakathi nomdlalo, ubeyobheka izethameli ezihloniphekile enenhloni bese ebalekela isibukeli esibi, ngokumangala kwezixuku ezilele nezingazi.

Lapho umuntu evuma ukuthi UNOKWAZI OLUYELELE, ningaqiniseka ukuthi useqala ukuvuka.

Izikole ezisabela emuva ze-Psychology endala eziphika ukuba khona KOKWAZI ngisho nokungabi nasidingo kwalelo gama, zimangalela isimo sokulala esijulile. Izilandeli zalezo Zikole zilala ngokujulile esimweni esicishe sibe ngaphansi kokwazi futhi singazi.

Labo abaphazamisa ukwazi nemisebenzi yePsychological; imicabango, imizwa, imizwelo yezimoto nemizwa, abazi lutho ngempela, balala ngokujulile.

Labo abavuma ukuba khona KOKWAZI kodwa baphike ngokuphelele amazinga ahlukene okwazi, bamangalela ukuntuleka kokuhlangenwe nakho okwaziyo, ubuthongo bokwazi.

Wonke umuntu oye wavuka okwesikhashana okungenani kanye, wazi kahle ngolwazi lwakhe ukuthi kunamazinga ahlukene okwazi abonwa kuye.

I-PSYCHOLOGY EGUQUKELAYO ne-PHILOKALIA yasendulo bathi ngemizamo emikhulu YOKUZIDELA yohlobo olukhethekile, umuntu angavusa ukwazi futhi akwenze kuqhubeke futhi kulawuleke.

IMFUNDO EYISISEKELO ihlose ukuvusa UKWAZI. Akusizi ngalutho iminyaka eyishumi noma eyishumi nanhlanu yezifundo eSikoleni, eKholeji naseYunivesithi, uma siphuma emakilasini siyizilwane ezizenzakalelayo ezilele.

Akukhona ukweqisa ukusho ukuthi ngemizamo emikhulu ethile, ISILWANE ESIZIZWAYO singakwazi ukuzazi okwemizuzu embalwa nje.

Kuyacaca ukuthi kulokhu kuvame ukuba khona izimo ezimbalwa ezingavamile namuhla okudingeka sizifune ngesibani sikaDiogenes, lezo zimo ezingavamile zimelelwa ABANTU BANGEMPELA, uBUDDHA, uJESU, uHERMES, uQUETZACOATL, njll.

Labasunguli BEZINKOLO babenOKWAZI OKUQHUBEKAYO, babengabaKHANYISIWE abakhulu.

Ngokuvamile abantu ABABAzi bona. Ukuzikhohlisa ngokuthi siyazazi ngokuqhubekayo, kuzalwa yinkumbulo nayo yonke imisebenzi yokucabanga.

Umuntu oziprakthiza umsebenzi wokubheka emuva ukuze akhumbule yonke impilo yakhe, angakhumbula ngempela, akhumbule ukuthi ushade kangaki, wazala izingane ezingaki, obani abazali bakhe, abaFundisi bakhe, njll., kodwa lokhu akusho ukuvusa ukwazi, lokhu kumane kuyikhumbula izenzo ezingazi futhi yilokho kuphela.

Kuyadingeka ukuphinda lokho esesikushilo ezahlukweni ezedlule. Kunezimo ezine ZOKWAZI. Lezi yilezi: UKULALA, isimo SOKUPHAPHAMELA, Ukuzazi kanye NOKWAZI OKUJWAYELEKILE.

ISILWANE ESIHLUGASEKILEYO esimpofu okungelona iQINISO esibizwa ngokuthi UMUNTU, siphila kuphela kwezimbili zalezo zimo. Ingxenye yempilo yayo ichithelwa ebuthongweni kanti enye ichithelwa kulokho okubizwa ngokuthi ISIMO SOKUPHAPHAMELA, naso futhi okuyibuthongo.

Umuntu olele futhi ophuphayo, ukholelwa ukuthi uyaphaphama ngeqiniso lokuthi ubuyela esimweni sokuvuka, kodwa eqinisweni phakathi nalesi simo sokuvuka uyaqhubeka nokuphupha.

Lokhu kufana nokusa, izinkanyezi ziyashabalala ngenxa yokukhanya kwelanga kodwa ziyaqhubeka nokuba khona nakuba amehlo enyama engaziboni.

Empilweni evamile, umuntu akazi lutho NGOKUZAZI futhi okuncane kakhulu NGOKWAZI OKUJWAYELEKILE.

Nokho abantu bayazigqaja futhi wonke umuntu uzicabanga ukuthi UYAZAZI; ISILWANE ESIZIZWAYO sikholelwa ngokuqinile ukuthi siyazazi futhi asisoze samukela ukutshelwa ukuthi silele nokuthi siphila singazi ngathi.

Kunezikhathi ezingavamile lapho ISILWANE ESIZIZWAYO sivuka, kodwa lezo zikhathi ziyivelakancane, zingamelwa umzuzwana wengozi enkulu, ngesikhathi somzwelo omkhulu, esimweni esisha, esimweni esisha esingalindelekile, njll.

Kuyishwa ngempela ukuthi ISILWANE ESIZIZWAYO esimpofu asinakho ukulawula kulezo zimo ezidlulayo zokwazi, ukuthi asikwazi ukuzibiza, ukuthi asikwazi ukuzenza ziqhubeke.

Nokho IMFUNDO EYISISEKELO ithi umuntu angaFINYELELA ekulawuleni UKWAZI futhi athole UKUZAZI.

I-PSYCHOLOGY EGUQUKELAYO inezindlela zezinqubo zesayensi ZOKUVUSA UKWAZI.

Uma sifuna UKUVUSA UKWAZI, sidinga ukuqala ngokuhlola, ukutadisha bese siqeda zonke izithiyo ezivele endleleni, kule ncwadi sifundise indlela yokuvusa UKWAZI kusukela emabhentshini eSikole.
