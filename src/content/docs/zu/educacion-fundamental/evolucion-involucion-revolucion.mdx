Imibono yesimanje ngemvelaphi yomuntu kanye NOKUZIVEZELA kwakhe okudlule, empeleni iyi-SOFISTRY ESHIBHILE, ayikwazi ukumelana nesigaba esibucayi esijulile.

Ngokuqondene NOMUNTU WANGAPHAMBILI KOMHLABA, kulezi zilwane ezingajwayelekile neziyimfihlakalo ezibukeka zifana neSILWANE ESINOBUNGCWELI kodwa ezihlukile, ezihluke kakhulu, eziyimfihlakalo kangaka futhi amathambo azo adumile afihlwe ngokujulile kwezinye izikhathi ezindaweni zakudala zenkathi yeQhwa noma i-Preglacial, ososayensi besimanje abazi lutho ngokuqondile nangokuhlangenwe nakho okuqondile.

IMITETHO YOKUZIVEZELA kanye NE-INVOLUTION iyimfoloko yemishini yayo yonke imvelo futhi ayihlangene nokuzazisa OKUJULILE KWE-SER.

Ngaphakathi KWESILWANE ESINENHLAKANIPHO kunamathuba amakhulu angathuthukiswa noma alahleke, akuyona umthetho ukuthi athuthukiswe. Imishini YOKUZIVEZELA ayikwazi ukuwathuthukisa.

Ukuthuthukiswa kwamathuba anjalo afihlekile, kungenzeka kuphela ezimeni ezichazwe kahle futhi lokhu kudinga IMIGOMO EMKHULU YOMUNTU ngamunye kanye nosizo oluphumelelayo oluvela KULABO BAHOLI asebenze lelo msebenzi esikhathini esidlule.

Noma ubani ofuna ukuthuthukisa wonke amathuba akhe afihlekile ukuze abe umuntu, kufanele angene endleleni YOKUVUKELWA KWENGQONDO.

ISILWANE ESINENHLAKANIPHO YINHLANYISO, IMBU; kuleyo mbewu kungazalwa UMNTHI WOKUPHILA, UMNTU WEQINISO, lowo MUNTU owayefunwa nguDIOGENES ngesibani esivuthayo emigwaqweni yase-ATHENS emini bebade futhi ngeshwa akakwazanga ukumthola.

AKUWONA UMTHETHO ukuthi le nhlanyiso, ukuthi le mbewu ekhetheke kangaka ingathuthuka, okuvamile, okungokwemvelo ukuthi ilahleke.

UMUNTU WEQINISO uhluke kakhulu KWISILWANE ESINENHLAKANIPHO, njengoba UMBANE uhluke efwini.

Uma inhlanyiso ingafi imbewu ayihlumi, kuyadingeka, kuphuthuma ukuthi kufe i-EGO, i-YO, i-MI MYSELF, paca ukuze kuzalwe UMNTU.

Othisha Nabathisha Bezikole, Amakolishi Namayunivesithi, kufanele bafundise abafundi babo INDLELA YENDLELA YOKUZIPHATHA KOBUVUKELI, yilapho kuphela kungenzeka ukufeza ukufa kwe-EGO.

Ngokugcizelela singaqinisekisa ukuthi UBUVUKELI BENGQONDO abugcini nje ngokuba yivelakancane kulo mhlaba, kodwa buya ngokuya buba yivelakancane futhi buba yivelakancane.
