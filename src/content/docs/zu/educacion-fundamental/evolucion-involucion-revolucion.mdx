Imibono yesimanje ngemvelaphi yomuntu kanye NOKUZIPHENDUKELA KWEMVELO kwakhe kwangesikhathi esidlule, empeleni IWUKUQHATHA okushibhile nje, ayikwazi ukumelana nesigaba esinzulu sokugxeka.

Naphezu kwazo zonke izinkolelo-mbono zika-DARWIN ezamukelwa njengento YOKUKHOLWA ngamehlo avaliwe ngu-CARLOS MARX kanye NOBUKHO bakhe BOKUKHULUMA NGEZINTO EZIBONAKALAYO, ososayensi besimanje abazi lutho ngemvelaphi yomuntu, akukho abakwaziyo, akukho abaye babhekana nakho ngokuqondile futhi abanabo ubufakazi obuthile obuqondile, obuqondile, mayelana NOKUZIPHENDUKELA KWEMVELO KOMUNTU.

Ngokuphambene nalokho, uma sithatha isintu somlando, okungukuthi, iminyaka engamashumi amabili noma engamashumi amathathu edlule ngaphambi kukaJesu Kristu, sithola ubufakazi obuqondile, izimpawu ezingaphikiki zohlobo oluphakeme lomuntu, olungenakucaciswa kubantu besimanje, futhi ubukhona balo bungaboniswa ngezinkulungwane zobufakazi, ama-Hieroglyph amadala, ama-Pyramid asendulo, ama-monolith angavamile, ama-papyrus ayimfihlakalo kanye nezikhumbuzo ezihlukahlukene zasendulo.

Ngaphakathi KESILWANE ESINEKHONO LOKUCABANGA kunamathuba amakhulu angathuthukiswa noma alahleke, akusiyo umthetho ukuthi athuthukiswe. Umshini wokUZIPHENDUKELA KWEMVELO awukwazi ukuwathuthukisa.

Ukuthuthukiswa kwamathuba anjalo afihlekile, kungenzeka kuphela ezimeni ezichazwe kahle futhi lokhu kudinga IMZAMO EPHAKEME kakhulu yomuntu ngamunye nosizo olusebenzayo oluvela KULABO THISHALA abasebenze lowo msebenzi esikhathini esidlule.

Noma ubani ofuna ukuthuthukisa wonke amathuba akhe afihlekile ukuze abe umuntu, kufanele angene endleleni YOKUVUKELA UMGOMO.

Uma inhlamvu ingafi imbewu ayihlumi, kuyadingeka, kuphuthuma ukuthi kufe i-EGO, mina, mina ngokwami, ukuze kuzalwe UMUNTU.

Ngokugcizelela, singaqinisekisa ukuthi UKUVUKELA UMGOMO akugcini nje ngokuba yivelakancane kulo mhlaba, kodwa kuya ngokuya kuba yivelakancane ngokwengeziwe.
