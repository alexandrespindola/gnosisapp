Othisha nabothisha bezikole, amakolishi, kanye namanyuvesi, kufanele bafundisise ngokujulile nge-PSYCHOLOGY ENGUQULO efundiswa yi-INTERNATIONAL GNOSTIC MOVEMENT.

I-PSYCHOLOGY yenguquko eqhubekayo ihluke ngokuphelele kunoma yini eyake yaziwa ngaphambilini ngaleli gama.

Ngaphandle kokungabaza, singasho ngaphandle kokwesaba ukuthi ekhulwini leminyaka elidlule, kusukela ebusukwini obujulile bayo yonke iminyaka, i-PSYCHOLOGY ayikaze iwele phansi njengamanje kule nkathi "YOMVUKELI ONGESONA ISIZATHU" namadodana e-ROCK.

I-Psychology ehlehlayo neyokuzenzisa yalezi zikhathi zanamuhla, ukuze kugcwaliswe inhlekelele, ilahlekelwe ngeshwa injongo yayo yokuba khona, futhi konke ukuxhumana okuqondile nemvelaphi yayo yangempela.

Kulezi zikhathi zokwehla kwamandla ocansi nokucekelwa phansi okuphelele kwengqondo, akusenakwenzeka kuphela ukuchaza ngokunemba okuncane igama elithi PSYCHOLOGY kodwa futhi izifundo eziyisisekelo ze-Psychology azaziwa ngempela.

Labo abacabanga ngephutha ukuthi i-PSYCHOLOGY iyisayensi yesimanje yakamuva nje, badidekile ngempela ngoba i-PSYCHOLOGY iyisayensi yakudala kakhulu eyavela ezikoleni ezindala ze-MYSTERIES ARCHAIC.

Kuhlobo lwe-SNOB, isela lesimanje kakhulu, i-retard, akunakwenzeka ukuchaza lokho okwaziwa njenge-PSYCHOLOGY ngoba ngaphandle kwale nkathi yesimanje, kusobala ukuthi i-PSYCHOLOGY ayikaze ibe khona ngaphansi kwegama layo siqu ngoba ngezizathu ezithile, ihlale isisolwa ngokuthambekela kokuchitha isithunzi kwezepolitiki noma zenkolo, ngakho-ke kwadingeka izembese ngezembatho eziningi.

Kusukela ezikhathini zasendulo, ezigcawini ezahlukene zetheyetha yempilo, i-PSYCHOLOGY ihlale idlala indima yayo, izifihla ngobuhlakani ngengubo yefilosofi.

Emaphethelweni eGanges, eNdiya Engcwele yamaVEDAS, kusukela ebusukwini obusabisayo bamakhulu eminyaka, kunezinhlobo zeYOGA, empeleni, ezingama-PSYCHOLOGY YOKUHLOLA emsulwa, yezindiza eziphakeme.

Ama-YOGA ayisikhombisa ahlale echazwa njengezindlela, izinqubo, noma izinhlelo zefilosofi.

Ezweni lama-Arabhu, izimfundiso ezingcwele zama-SUFIS, ngokwengxenye ezingokomoya, ngokwengxenye ezingokwenkolo, ziyizinhlelo zokusebenza ngokuphelele ZE-PSYCHOLOGICAL.

E-Europe endala ebolile kuze kube semnkantheni wamathambo anezimpi eziningi, ubandlululo lobuhlanga. Inzondo yenkolo, yezombangazwe njll. kuze kube sekupheleni kwekhulu leminyaka elidlule, i-PSYCHOLOGY yazifihla ngengubo yeFilosofi ukuze ingabonakali.

I-Philosophy ngaphandle kwazo zonke izigaba zayo kanye nezigaba ezingaphansi njenge-Logic, theory of knowledge Ethics, Aesthetics, njll., ngaphandle kokungabaza ngokwayo, ukuzindla okusobala, ukwazi okuyimfihlakalo KOBUKHONA, ukusebenza kokucabanga komqondo ovukile.

Iphutha lezIKOLE EZININGI ZE-PHILOSOPHICAL ukuthi zibheke i-Psychology njengento ephansi kune-PHILOSOPHY, njengento ehlobene kuphela nezici eziphansi kakhulu nezingabalulekile zemvelo yomuntu.

Isifundo sokuqhathanisa seziNkolo sisivumela ukuba sifinyelele esiphethweni esinengqondo sokuthi ISAYENSI YE-PSYCHOLOGY ihlale ihlotshaniswa ngokusondelene nazo zonke IZIMISO ZENKOLO. Noma yisiphi isifundo sokuqhathanisa seziNkolo sibonisa ukuthi ENCAZULWENI ENGCWELE elungile yamazwe ahlukene nezikhathi ezahlukene, kunamagugu amahle esayensi YE-PSYCHOLOGICAL

Ukucwaninga okujulile emkhakheni we-GNOSTICISM kusivumela ukuthi sithole leyo nhlanganisela emangalisayo yababhali abahlukene be-Gnostic evela ezikhathini zokuqala zobuKristu futhi eyaziwa ngesihloko esithi PHILOKALIA, esasetshenziswa nanamuhla eSontweni LaseMpumalanga, ikakhulukazi ukufundisa izindela.

Ngaphandle kokungabaza futhi ngaphandle kokwesaba okuncane ukuwela ekukhohlisweni, singasho ngokuqinile ukuthi i-PHILOKALIA iyinto ebalulekile YE-PSYCHOLOGY YOKUHLOLA emsulwa.

EZIKOLWENI ZASENDULO ZEMFIHLO zaseGrisi, eGibhithe, eRoma, eNdiya, ePheresiya, eMexico, ePeru, e-Asiriya, eKhalidiya, njll. njll. njll., i-PSYCHOLOGY ihlale ixhumene nefilosofi, i-Real objective Art, isayensi nenkolo.

Ezikhathini Zasendulo i-PSYCHOLOGY yayifihlwa ngobuhlakani phakathi kwezimo ezinhle zabaDansi abaNgcwele, noma phakathi kwendida yama-Hieroglyphs angaqondakali noma imifanekiso eqoshiwe emihle, noma ezinkondlweni, noma enhlekeleleni ngisho nasemculweni omnandi wamathempeli.

Ngaphambi kokuthi Isayensi, Ifilosofi, Ubuciko kanye neNkonzo zehlukane ukuze zijike ngokuzimela, i-PSYCHOLOGY yabusa ngokukhululekile kuzo zonke IZIKOLE ZASENDULO ZEMFIHLO.

Lapho amaKolishi Abaqalayo evala ngenxa ye-KALIYUGA, noma i-AGE ELIMNYAMA esisaphethwe yiyo, i-PSYCHOLOGY yasinda phakathi kokufanekisela kweZIKOLE EZINHLUKENENI ze-ESOTERIC ne-PSEUDO-ESOTERIC Yomhlaba WAMUHLA ikakhulukazi phakathi kwe-GNOSTIC ESOTERICISM.

Ukuhlaziywa okujulile nokucwaninga okujulile, kusivumela ukuthi siqonde ngokucacile ukuthi izinhlelo ezahlukene nezimfundiso ze-Psychological ezazikhona esikhathini esidlule futhi ezikhona manje, zingahlukaniswa ngezigaba ezimbili.

Okokuqala.- Izimfundiso njengoba izifundiswa eziningi zicabanga ngazo. I-Psychology yesimanje empeleni ingeyesigaba.

Okwesibili.- Izimfundiso ezifunda umuntu ngokombono WENGUQUKO YOKWAZI.

Lezi zokugcina ziyizimfundiso zokuqala ngempela, ezindala kakhulu, yizona kuphela ezisivumela ukuthi siqonde imvelaphi ephilayo ye-Psychology kanye nencazelo yayo ejulile.

Lapho sonke sesiqonde ngokugcwele kuwo wonke AMAZINGA ENGQONDO, ukuthi kubaluleke kangakanani ukutadisha umuntu ngokombono omusha WENGUQUKO YOKWAZI, sizoqonda-ke ukuthi i-Psychology iyisifundo sezimiso, imithetho kanye namaqiniso ahlobene kakhulu NENGUQUKO EJULILE kanye neqinisekile YOMUNTU.

Kuphuthuma ukuthi Othisha nabothisha bezikole, amakolishi, namanyuvesi, baziqonde ngokugcwele isikhathi ESINZIMA esiphila kuso kanye nesimo esiyinhlekelele sokudideka kwengqondo isizukulwane esisha esikuso.

Kuyadingeka ukuthi kuqondiswe i-"NEW WAVE" endleleni YENGUQUKO YOKWAZI futhi lokhu kungenzeka kuphela nge-PSYCHOLOGY ENGUQULO YEMFUNDO EYISISEKELO.
