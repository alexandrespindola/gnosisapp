Othisha nabafundisi ezikoleni, emakolishi nasemayunivesithi, kufanele batadishe ngokujulile i-PSYCHOLOGY REVOLUTIONARY efundiswa yi-GNOSTIC INTERNATIONAL MOVEMENT.

I-PSYCHOLOGY ye-REVOLUTION eqhubekayo ihluke ngokuphelele kunoma yini eyayaziwa ngaphambili ngaleli gama.

Ngaphandle kokungabaza, singasho ngokungesabi ukuthi phakathi namakhulu eminyaka asidlulile, kusukela ebusuku obujulile bezinkathi zonke, i-PSYCHOLOGY ayikaze iwe phansi njengamanje kule nkathi ye "REBEL WITHOUT A CAUSE" kanye nabagibeli bamahhashi be-ROCK.

I-Sicology elibazisayo neyokubuyisela emuva yalezi zikhathi zanamuhla ukuze kuqedelwe inhlekelele, ngeshwa ilahlekelwe umqondo wayo wokuba, nakho konke ukuxhumana ngqo nemvelaphi yayo yangempela.

Kulezi zikhathi zokwehla kwamandla ocansi nokonakala okuphelele kwengqondo, akusenzeki kuphela ukuchaza ngokunemba okuncane igama elithi PSYCHOLOGY, kodwa nezihloko eziyisisekelo zeSicology azaziwa ngempela.

Labo abacabanga ngephutha ukuthi i-PSYCHOLOGY iyisayensi yesimanje yakamuva, badidekile ngempela ngoba i-PSYCHOLOGY iyisayensi yasendulo enomsuka wayo ezikoleni ezindala ze-ARCAIC MYSTERIES.

Kuhlobo lwe-SNOB, isigebengu sanamuhla, i-retard, akwenzeki ukuchaza lokho okwaziwa njenge-PSYCHOLOGY ngoba ngaphandle kwalesi sikhathi sanamuhla, kusobala ukuthi i-PSYCHOLOGY ayikaze ibe khona ngaphansi kwegama layo ngenxa yokuthi ngezizathu ezithile, yayisolwa njalo ngokuthambekela kokuketula kwezombusazwe noma kwezenkolo ngakho-ke kwadingeka izifihle ngezembatho eziningi.

Kusukela ezikhathini zasendulo, ezindaweni ezihlukahlukene zetheyetha yokuphila, i-PSYCHOLOGY yayihlala imele indima yayo, ifihlakele ngokuhlakanipha ngengubo yefilosofi.

Ogwini lweGanges, eNdiya Engcwele yamaVEDA, kusukela ebusuku obethusayo bazo zonke izinkathi, kunezindlela zeYOGA okuyizinto eziyigugu, eziyiPSYCHOLOGY yokuhlola ezingeni eliphezulu.

AmaYOGA ayisikhombisa ahlala echazwa njengezindlela, izinqubo, noma izinhlelo zefilosofi.

Ezweni lama-Arabhu, izimfundiso eziNgcwele zamaSUFIS, ezinye ziyi-metaphysical, ezinye ezingokwenkolo, empeleni zisezingeni lePSYCHOLOGICAL ngokuphelele.

EYurophu endala ebole kuze kufike emathanjeni ngezimpi eziningi, ubandlululo lobuhlanga. Ezenkolo, ezombusazwe njll. kuze kube sekupheleni kwekhulu elidlule, i-PSYCHOLOGY yazifihla ngengubo yeFilosofi ukuze ingabonakali.

IFilosofi naphezu kwazo zonke izigaba zayo kanye nezigaba ezincane njengokuthi iLogic, inkolelo yolwazi, i-Ethic, i-Aesthetics, njll., ngaphandle kokungabaza ngokwayo, i-AUTO-REFLECTION EVEMENT, i-COGNITION MYSTIC OF BEING, i-FUNCTIONALISM COGNITIVE OF THE CONSCIENCES.

Iphutha leZIkoleni eziningi zeFilosofi ukucabangela i-psychology njengento ephansi kune-FILOSOPHY, njengento ehlobene kuphela nezici eziphansi kakhulu futhi ezingabalulekile zemvelo yomuntu.

Ezikhathini Zasendulo, i-PSYCHOLOGY yayifihleke ngokuhlakanipha phakathi kwezimo ezinhle zabaDansi abangcwele, noma phakathi kwemfihlakalo ye-Hieroglyphics eyinqaba noma izithombe ezinhle, noma ezinkondlweni, noma enhlekeleleni ngisho nasemculweni omnandi wamathempeli.

Ngaphambi kokuba Isayensi, iFilosofi, Ubuciko kanye neNkolo zihlukane ukuze ziqhubeke ngokuzimela, i-PSYCHOLOGY yabusa ngokukhululeka kuzo zonke IZIKOLE ZASENDULO ZEZIMFIHLO.

Ukuhlaziywa okujulile kanye nocwaningo olujulile, kusivumela ukuthi siqonde ngokucacile ukuthi izinhlelo ezihlukene nezimfundiso zePsychological ebezikhona esikhathini esidlule futhi ezikhona manje, zingahlukaniswa ngezigaba ezimbili.

Okokuqala.- Izimfundiso njengoba izifundiswa eziningi zicabanga ngazo. ISicology yesimanje empeleni ingeyalesi sigaba.

Okwesibili.- Izimfundiso ezitadisha umuntu ngokombono we-REVOLUTION YENGCONO.

Lezi zokugcina empeleni ziyizimfundiso zangempela, ezindala kakhulu, yizona kuphela ezisivumela ukuthi siqonde imvelaphi ephilayo yeSicology kanye nencazelo yayo ejulile.

Lapho sonke siziqonda ngokuphelele kuwo wonke AMAZENGA ENGQONDO, ukuthi kubaluleke kangakanani ukutadisha umuntu ngokombono omusha we-REVOLUTION OF CONSCIENCE, sizoqonda ukuthi iSicology ukutadisha izimiso, imithetho namaqiniso ahlobene kakhulu ne-TRANSFORMATION RADICAL nokuphela komUNTU.
