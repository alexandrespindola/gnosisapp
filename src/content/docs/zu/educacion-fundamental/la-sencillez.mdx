Kuyashesha, kubalulekile ukuthuthukisa ukuqonda okudala ngoba kuletha inkululeko yangempela yokuhlala kumuntu. Ngaphandle kokuqonda akunakwenzeka ukuthola ikhono langempela lokuhlaziya okujulile.

Othisha bezikole, amakolishi namanyuvesi kufanele bahole abafundi babo endleleni yokuqonda okuzigxekayo.

Esahlukweni sethu esidlule sesivele sihlolisise kabanzi izinqubo zomhawu futhi uma sifuna ukuqeda wonke amathunzi omona, kungakhathaliseki ukuthi angokwenkolo, anothando, njll. kufanele siqaphele ngokugcwele ukuthi umhawu uyini ngempela, ngoba kuphela ngokuqonda ngokujulile nangokuyimfihlo izinqubo ezingapheli zomhawu, singakwazi ukuqeda umona wazo zonke izinhlobo.

Umona ucekelisa phansi imishado, umona ucekelisa phansi ubungane, umona ubangela izimpi zenkolo, inzondo yobuzalwane, ukubulala nokuhlupheka kwazo zonke izinhlobo.

Umona nawo wonke amathunzi awo angapheli ucashe ngemuva kwezinjongo ezinhle. Kukhona umhawu kulowo oye waziswa ngokuba khona kwabangcwele abakhulu. Ama-Mahatma noma ama-Guru, nawo afisa ukuba ngumuntu oyingcwele. Kukhona umhawu kumuntu othanda abantu osebenzela ukwedlula abanye abathanda abantu. Kukhona umhawu kuyo yonke ingane enesifiso sezimfanelo ngoba inemibiko, ngoba emqondweni wayo kunemininingwane ngokuba khona kwabantu abangcwele abagcwele izimfanelo.

Isifiso sokuba ngcwele, isifiso sokuba nesimilo, isifiso sokuba mkhulu sisekelwe kumhawu.

Abangcwele nezimfanelo zabo zibangele umonakalo omkhulu. Sikhumbula indaba yendoda eyayizibheka njengengcwele kakhulu.

Ngesinye isikhathi imbongi elambile nehluphekayo yangqongqoza emnyango wayo ukuze ibeke ezandleni zayo ivesi elihle elinikezelwe ngokukhethekile kumngcwele wendaba yethu. Imbongi yayilinde kuphela uhlamvu lwemali ukuze ithenge ukudla komzimba wayo okhathele nokugugile.

Imbongi yayingacabangi lutho ngaphandle kokuthukwa. Kwamjabulisa kakhulu lapho umngcwele, enokubukeka kokuhlonipha futhi ebhodla ubuso, evala umnyango etshela imbongi enenhlanhla: "suka lapha mngane, hamba, hamba ... angizithandi lezi zinto, ngiyakwenyanya ukuthopha ... angizithandi izinto eziyize zaleli zwe, lokhu kuphila kuyinkohliso ... ngilandela indlela yokuthobeka nokuzithoba. Imbongi enenhlanhla eyayifuna nje uhlamvu lwemali esikhundleni salokhu yathola ukuthukwa komngcwele, izwi elilimazayo, impama, futhi ngenhliziyo ebuhlungu nethikithi liphenduke izicucu yahamba emigwaqweni yomuzi kancane ... kancane ... kancane.

Isizukulwane esisha kufanele sikhuphuke phezu kwesisekelo sokuqonda kwangempela ngoba lokhu kudala ngokuphelele.

Inkumbulo nokukhumbula akudali. Inkumbulo iyithuna lesikhathi esidlule. Inkumbulo nokukhumbula ukufa.

Ukuqonda kweqiniso kuyisici esingokwengqondo sokukhululwa okuphelele.

Izinkumbulo zenkumbulo azisoze zasilethela inkululeko yangempela ngoba zingezesikhathi esidlule ngakho-ke zifile.

Ukuqonda akuyona into yesikhathi esidlule noma yesikhathi esizayo. Ukuqonda kungokwesikhathi esiphila kuso lapha namanje. Inkumbulo njalo iletha umqondo wesikhathi esizayo.

Kuyashesha ukutadisha isayensi, ifilosofi, ubuciko nenkolo, kodwa izifundo akufanele zithembeke ekwethembekeni kwenkumbulo ngoba lokhu akwethembekile.

Kuyize ukubeka ulwazi ethuneni lenkumbulo. Kuwubuwula ukungcwaba emgodini wesikhathi esidlule ulwazi okufanele siluqonde.

Asisoze sakwazi ukukhuluma ngokumelene nokutadisha, ngokumelene nokuhlakanipha, ngokumelene nesayensi, kodwa kuphambene nokubeka amagugu aphilayo olwazi phakathi kwethuna elibolile lenkumbulo.

Kuyadingeka ukufunda, kuyadingeka ukucwaninga, kuyadingeka ukuhlaziya, kodwa kufanele sizindle ngokujulile ukuze siqonde kuwo wonke amazinga engqondo.

Umuntu olula ngempela uyaqonda ngokujulile futhi unengqondo elula.

Okubalulekile ekuphileni akukhona lokho esikuqoqe ethuneni lenkumbulo, kodwa lokho esikuqondayo hhayi kuphela ezingeni lobuhlakani kodwa nasezindaweni ezihlukahlukene ezingaphansi kwengqondo ezingaqondakali zengqondo.

Isayensi, ulwazi, kufanele luphenduke ukuqonda okusheshayo. Lapho ulwazi, lapho ukufunda kuphenduke ukuqonda okudala kwangempela singaqonda zonke izinto ngokushesha ngoba ukuqonda kuba okusheshayo, okusheshayo.

Kumuntu olula akukho zinkinga engqondweni ngoba yonke inkinga yengqondo ibangelwa inkumbulo. Mina Maquiavellian esinayo ngaphakathi yinkumbulo enqwabelene.

Okuhlangenwe nakho kwempilo kufanele kuphenduke ukuqonda kweqiniso.

Lapho okuhlangenwe nakho kungaphenduki ukuqonda, lapho okuhlangenwe nakho kuqhubeka enkumbulweni kwakha ukubola kwethuna lapho kushiswa khona ilangabi eliyize nelikaLusifa lobuhlakani

Kuyadingeka ukwazi ukuthi ubuhlakani bezilwane obungenazo nhlobo izinto ezingokomoya bumane nje buyindlela yokukhuluma ngenkumbulo, ikhandlela lethuna elivuthayo phezu kwethuna.

Umuntu olula unengqondo ekhululekile ekuhlangenwe nakho ngoba lokhu sekuphenduke ukuqaphela, kuphenduke ukuqonda okudala.

Ukufa nokuphila kuhlobene kakhulu. Kuphela ngokufa kwembewu lapho kuzalwa isitshalo, kuphela ngokufa kokuhlangenwe nakho lapho kuzalwa ukuqonda. Lena inqubo yoshintsho lwangempela.

Umuntu oyinkimbinkimbi unenkumbulo egcwele okuhlangenwe nakho.

Lokhu kubonisa ukuntuleka kwakhe kokuqonda okudala ngoba lapho okuhlangenwe nakho kuqondwa ngokuphelele kuwo wonke amazinga engqondo kuyeka ukuba khona njengokuhlangenwe nakho futhi kuzalwe njengokuqonda.

Kuyadingeka kuqala ukuthi uzizwele, kodwa akufanele sihlale endaweni yokuhlangenwe nakho ngoba ngaleso sikhathi ingqondo iba yinkimbinkimbi futhi ibe nzima. Kuyadingeka ukuthi uphile impilo ngamandla futhi uguqule konke okuhlangenwe nakho kube ukuqonda okudala kwangempela.

Labo abacabanga ngephutha ukuthi ukuze siqonde, sibe lula futhi sibe lula kufanele silahle izwe, siphenduke abaceli, sihlale emijondolo ehlukene futhi sigqoke izindwangu zokugqoka esikhundleni sesudi enhle, banephutha ngokuphelele.

Abantu abaningi abahlala bodwa, abahlali bebodwa abaningi, abaceli abaningi, banemiqondo eyinkimbinkimbi futhi enzima.

Akusizi ngalutho ukuhlukana nezwe futhi uphile njengabantu abahlala bodwa uma inkumbulo igcwele okuhlangenwe nakho okubeka umkhawulo ekuhambeni kwamahhala kwemicabango.

Akusizi ngalutho ukuhlala njengabantu abahlala bodwa befuna ukuphila impilo yabangcwele uma inkumbulo igcwele ulwazi olungaqondwanga kahle, olungenziwa ukuqaphela ezindaweni ezihlukene, emaphaseji nasezifundeni ezingazi lutho zengqondo.

Labo abaguqula ulwazi lobuhlakani lube ukuqonda okudala kwangempela, labo abaguqula okuhlangenwe nakho kwempilo kube ukuqonda kwangempela okujulile abanayo lutho enkumbulweni, baphila kusukela kumzuzu kuya kumzuzu begcwele ubuningi beqiniso, sebephenduke abalula futhi abalula nakuba behlala ezindaweni ezinhle futhi ngaphakathi komjikelezo wempilo yasemadolobheni.

Izingane ezincane ngaphambi kweminyaka eyisikhombisa zigcwele ubulula nobuhle bangaphakathi beqiniso ngenxa yokuthi kuphela UKUQALA okuhlala impilo kubonakala ngazo lapho kungekho nhlobo mina LENGQONDO.

Kufanele sithole kabusha ubuntwana obulahlekile, ezinhliziyweni zethu nasemiqondweni yethu. Kufanele sithole kabusha ubumsulwa uma sifuna ngempela ukujabula.

Okuhlangenwe nakho nokutadisha okuguqulwe kwaba ukuqonda okujulile akushiyi nsukwana ethuneni lenkumbulo futhi-ke, siba lula, sibe lula, sibe msulwa, sijabule.

Ukuzindla okujulile ngalokho okuhlangenwe nakho nolwazi olutholakele, ukuzigxeka okujulile, ukuhlaziya okungokwengqondo okuseduze kuyaguqula, kuguqule yonke into ibe ukuqonda okudala okujulile. Lena yindlela yenjabulo yangempela ezalwa ukuhlakanipha nothando.
