Ukuhlakanipha NOThando yizinsika ezimbili eziyinhloko zempucuko yangempela.

Endishini yesilinganiso sobulungiswa kufanele sibeke UKUHLAKANIPHA, kwenye indishi kufanele sibeke UTHANDO.

Ukuhlakanipha NOThando kufanele kulinganiswe. Ukuhlakanipha ngaphandle kothando kuyinto ebhubhisayo. Uthando ngaphandle kokuhlakanipha lungasiholela ekuphambukeni "UTHANDO LUNGUMTHETHO KODWA UTHANDO OLUQAPHELAYO".

Kuyadingeka ukutadisha kakhulu nokuthola ulwazi, kodwa futhi KUYAPHUTHUMA ukuthuthukisa NGAPHAKATHI kwethu ISIMO SOMOYA.

Ulwazi ngaphandle KWESIMO SOMOYA esithuthukiswe kahle ngendlela ehambisanayo ngaphakathi kithi, kuba yimbangela yalokho okubizwa ngokuthi UKUQOLA.

ISIMO esithuthukiswe kahle ngaphakathi kithi kodwa ngaphandle kolwazi lwengqondo lwanoma yiluphi uhlobo, siqala ABAngcwele abayiziwula.

UMngcwele oyisiwula unayo ISIMO SOMOYA esithuthukiswe kakhulu, kodwa njengoba engenalo ulwazi lwengqondo, akakwazi ukwenza lutho ngoba akazi ukuthi angakwenza kanjani.

UMGCWELE oyisiwula unamandla okwenza kodwa akakwazi ukwenza ngoba akazi ukuthi angakwenza kanjani.

Ulwazi lwengqondo ngaphandle KWESIMO SOMOYA esithuthukiswe kahle lubangela ukudideka kwengqondo, ububi, ukuziqhenya, njll., njll.

Sidinga ukuzakhela isiko elinamandla lengqondo kodwa elilinganiswe kakhulu nobuMoya beqiniso obuqaphelayo.

Sidinga i-ETHICS EVUSELELAYO kanye NE-PSYCHOLOGY EVUSELELAYO uma sifuna ngempela ukuchitha i-YO ukuze sithuthukise ISIMO SIKAMOYA esisemthethweni ngaphakathi kithi.

Kuyadabukisa ukuthi ngenxa yokuntuleka kothando abantu basebenzisa IQOLO ngendlela ebhubhisayo.

Abafundi abesilisa nabesifazane badinga ukutadisha isayensi, umlando, izibalo, njll., njll.

Kuyadingeka ukuthola ulwazi lobungcweti, ngenhloso yokusiza umakhelwane.

Ukufunda kuyadingeka. Ukunqwabelanisa ulwazi oluyisisekelo kubalulekile, kodwa ukwesaba akubalulekile.

Abantu abaningi banqwabelanisa ulwazi ngenxa yokwesaba; besaba impilo, ukufa, indlala, ubumpofu, ukuthi abantu bazothini, njll., futhi ngaleso sizathu bayafunda.

Kufanele sifunde ngothando oluya kubantu esiphila nabo ngenhloso yokubasiza kangcono, kodwa akufanele neze sifunde ngenxa yokwesaba.

Empilweni yangempela sikwazile ukuqinisekisa ukuthi bonke labo bafundi abafunda ngenxa yokwesaba, ngokushesha noma kamuva baba izigebengu.

Sidinga ukwethembeka kithi ukuze sizizwele futhi sithole ngaphakathi kithi zonke izinqubo zokwesaba.

Akufanele sikukhohlwe nanini empilweni ukuthi ukwesaba kunezigaba eziningi. Ngezinye izikhathi ukwesaba kudidaniswa nobuqhawe. Amasosha enkundleni yempi abonakala enesibindi kakhulu kodwa empeleni ayanyakaza futhi alwa ngenxa yokwesaba. Umuntu ozibulalayo naye ubonakala enesibindi kakhulu ekuqaleni kodwa empeleni uyigwala elesaba ukuphila.

Asilokothi siphikise okuhlangenwe nakho kwempilo yangempela noma ukukhuthaza ingqondo, kodwa siyakulahla ukuntuleka kothando.

Ulwazi kanye nokuhlangenwe nakho kwempilo kuyabhubhisa lapho kukhona ukuntuleka kothando.

I-EGO ivame ukubamba okuhlangenwe nakho nolwazi lwengqondo lapho kungekho lokho okubizwa ngokuthi UTHANDO.

I-EGO isebenzisa kabi okuhlangenwe nakho kanye nengqondo lapho iyisebenzisa ukuze iziqinise.

Ngokuqaqa i-EGO, YO, MINA, OKWAMI, okuhlangenwe nakho kanye Nengqondo kusala ezandleni ZE-SER INTIMO futhi konke ukuhlukumeza kwenza kungenzeki.

Wonke umfundi kufanele aqondiswe yindlela yobungcweti futhi afunde ngokujulile zonke izinkolelo ezihlobene nomsebenzi wakhe.

Ukufunda, ingqondo, akulimazi muntu, kodwa akufanele siyisebenzise kabi ingqondo.

Uhlukumeza ingqondo lowo ofuna ukutadisha izinkolelo zemisebenzi ehlukene, ofuna ukulimaza abanye ngengqondo, lowo osebenzisa udlame engqondweni yomunye, njll. njll. njll.

Kuyadingeka ukutadisha izifundo zobuchwepheshe kanye nezifundo zikamoya ukuze ube nengqondo elinganiselayo.

KUYAPHUTHUMA ukufika KU-SYNTHESIS yengqondo kanye ne-synthesis ENGOKOMOYA uma sifuna ngempela ingqondo elinganiselayo.

Othisha nabafundisi bezikole, amakolishi, amanyuvesi, njll., kufanele bayifundisise iSicology yethu Evuselelwayo uma befuna ngempela ukuholela abafundi babo endleleni YOKUVUSELELA OKUYISISEKELO.

Kuyadingeka ukuthi abafundi bathole ISIMO SOMOYA, bathuthukise ngaphakathi kwabo ISIMO SEQINISO, ukuze baphume eSikoleni baguqulwe babe abantu abanomthwalo wemfanelo futhi bangabi IZIGEBENGU eziyiziwula.

Ukwazi akusizi ngalutho ngaphandle kothando. Ingqondo ngaphandle kothando iveza izigebengu kuphela.
