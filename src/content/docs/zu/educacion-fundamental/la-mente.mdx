Labo abacabanga ukuthi INGQONDO IWUBUNGOBU, baphambuke ngokuphelele. INGQONDO INAMANDLA, iyacasha, ingazimelela NGOKWEMPILO, ingakwazi ezimweni ezithile zokulala noma ngesikhathi sokulala okuvamile, ukuzithutha iye ezindaweni ezikude kakhulu ukuyobona nokuzwa okwenzekayo kulezo zindawo.

Emajabhini e-PARAPSICOLOGIA kwenziwa izilingo ezibalulekile ngeziguli ezisesimweni SE-HYPNOTIC.

Izifundo eziningi ezisesimweni SE-HYPNOTIC ziye zakwazi ukunikeza imininingwane eningiliziwe mayelana nezenzakalo, abantu kanye nezimo ezenzeka ngesikhathi sokukhulelwa kwazo kwe-hypnotic emagameni akude.

Ososayensi bakwazile ukuqinisekisa ngemuva kwalezo zilingo, iqiniso LALEZO ZAZISO. Bakwazile ukuqinisekisa ukuthi yiqiniso lezehlakalo, ukunemba KWEZINDABA.

Ngalezi zilingo zamajabhini e-PARAPSICOLOGIA kufakazelwe ngokuphelele ngokubukwa nangolwazi lokuthi UBUNGOBU akuyona INGQONDO.

Ngeqiniso nangeqiniso lonke singasho ukuthi ingqondo ingahamba ngesikhathi nendawo, ngokuzimela ebuchosheni, ukuyobona nokuzwa izinto ezenzeka ezindaweni ezikude.

IQINISO LOKUPHAKATHI KWEMICIMBI EYENZEKA NGAPHANDLE KWENGQONDO selivele lifakazelwe NGOKUPHELELE futhi umuntu osangene ngokuphelele noma isiphukuphuku kuphela, angacabanga ngokuphika iqiniso LOKUPHAKATHI KWENZWA NGAPHANDLE KWENGQONDO.

Ubongo benzelwe ukucubungula umcabango kodwa akuyona umcabango. Ubongo buyithuluzi kuphela LENGQONDO, akuyona ingqondo.

Sidinga ukufundisisa ingqondo uma sifuna ngempela ukwazi ngokUPHELELE lokho okubizwa ngokuthi UTHANDO.

Izingane kanye nentsha, abesilisa nabesifazane, banengqondo eguquguqukayo, ethambile, esheshayo, eqaphile, njll.

Ziningi izingane nentsha ezithanda ukubuza abazali bazo nothisha, mayelana nezinto ezinjalo, zifisa ukwazi okwengeziwe zifuna ukwazi futhi yingakho zibuza, ziyabuka, zibona imininingwane ethile abadala abayidelelayo noma abangayiboni.

Njengoba iminyaka ihamba, njengoba sikhula ngeminyaka, ingqondo iyaqina kancane kancane.

Ingqondo yabantu abadala igxilile, iqine njengamatshe, ayisashintshi ngisho nangezibhamu.

Abantu abadala sebeyilokhu abayikho futhi bafa njalo, abashintshi, baphatha yonke into ngombono owodwa.

"UKUQHAWUKA" kwabantu abadala, ubandlululo lwabo, imibono egxilile, njll. kubonakala kukonke njengeDwala, ITSIKA elingashintshi nganoma iyiphi indlela. Yingakho isisho esivamile sithi "UKULUNGELA NOKUTHENGA KUZE KUYA ETUMBENI".

Kuyaphuthuma ukuthi othisha nothisha abanomthwalo wemfanelo wokwakha UMLANDO wabafundi, bafundisise ingqondo, ukuze bakwazi ukuqondisa izizukulwane ezintsha ngobuhlakani.

Kubuhlungu ukuqonda ngokujulile, ukuthi ngokuhamba kwesikhathi INGQONDO iyaqina kanjani kancane kancane.

INGQONDO iwumbulali WEQINISO, weqiniso. INGQONDO ibhubhisa UTHANDO.

Umuntu ofika esikhathini eside akayisakwazi UKUTHANDA ngoba ingqondo yakhe igcwele okuhlangenwe nakho okubuhlungu, ubandlululo, imibono egxilile njengephuzu lensimbi, njll.

Kukhona abantu abadala abaluhlaza abazicabanga ukuthi bayakwazi UKUTHANDA NAMANJE, kodwa okwenzekayo ukuthi labo bantu abadala bagcwele izinkanuko zobulili zokuguga futhi badideka NGESIKHATHI NGEUTHANDO.

YONKE "INDODA ENDALA ELUHLAZA" KANYE "NOWESIFANAZANE OMDALA OLUHLAZA" badlula ezimweni ezesabekayo zenkanuko ngaphambi kokufa futhi bakholelwa ukuthi LUTHANDO.

UTHANDO lwabantu abadala alunakwenzeka ngoba ingqondo ilubhubhisa nge "ZITHINTITHWA" IMIBONO EFIXED", "UBUDLELALELA", "UFUFU", "OKUFUNA", izinkanuko zobulili, njll. njll. njll.

INGQONDO isitha esibi kunazo zonke ZOTHANDO. Emazweni AYENZIWE NGOKUPHAKATHI UTHANDO alusakho ngoba ingqondo yabantu imane inuka izimboni, ama-akhawunti asebhange, uphethiloli kanye ne-celluloid.

Kunamabhodlela amaningi engqondo futhi ingqondo yomuntu ngamunye ibhodlela kahle.

Abanye banengqondo ebhodlela KUBUKHOMUNISI OBUTHUSAYO, abanye bayibhodlela KUBUCAPITALISIM OBUNGENAMUSA.

Kukhona abanengqondo EBODLELE ezifweni, enzondweni, efisweni lokuceba, endaweni enhle yomphakathi, ekuphelelweni yithemba ekunamatheleni kubantu abathile, ekunamatheleni ekuhluphekeni kwabo, ezinkingeni zabo zomndeni, njll. njll. njll.

Abantu bathanda ukubhodlela INGQONDO, bambalwa abazimisele ngempela ukuthi babuyisele izingcezu zebhodlela.

Sidinga UKUKHULULA, INGQONDO kodwa abantu bayakujabulela ukugqilazeka, akuvamile ukuthola umuntu empilweni onengqondo ebhodlela kahle.

Othisha nothisha kufanele bafundise abafundi babo zonke lezi zinto. Kufanele bafundise izizukulwane ezintsha ukuthi ziphenye ingqondo yazo, ziyibukele, ziyiqonde, kuphela ngaleyo ndlela ngokuQONDISISA ngokujulile singagwema ukuthi ingqondo iqine, ibe yiqhwa, ibhodlela.

Okuwukuphela kwento engashintsha umhlaba yilokho okubizwa ngokuthi UTHANDO, kodwa ingqondo iyabhubhisa UTHANDO.

Sidinga UKUFUNDA ingqondo yethu, siyibukele, siyiphenye ngokujulile, siyizwise ngempela. Kungale ndlela kuphela, ngokuzenza abaphathi bethu, ingqondo yethu, sizobulala umbulali WOTHANDO futhi sizojabula ngempela.

Labo abahlala bephupha ngendlela enhle NGOUTHANDO, labo abahlala benza amaphrojekthi NGOUTHANDO, labo abafuna ukuthi UTHANDO lusebenze ngokuvumelana nokuthanda nokungathandi kwabo, amaphrojekthi namaphupho, imithetho nokubandlulula, izinkumbulo nokuhlangenwe nakho, njll. abasoze bakwazi ngempela ukwazi ukuthi UTHANDO luyini, empeleni sebephenduke izitha ZOTHANDO.

Kuyadingeka ukuqonda ngokUPHELELE ukuthi ziyini izinqubo zengqondo esimweni sokuqongelela okuhlangenwe nakho.

Uthisha, uthisha uvame ukusola ngokufanele kodwa ngezinye izikhathi ngobuwula nangaphandle kwesizathu sangempela, ngaphandle kokuqonda ukuthi konke ukusola okungenabulungiswa kufakwa engqondweni yabafundi, umphumela wenqubo enjalo eyiphutha, imvamisa ukulahlekelwa UTHANDO LOTHISHA, LOTHISHA.

INGQONDO iyabhubhisa UTHANDO futhi lokhu kuyinto OTHISHA NOTHISHA bezikole, Amakolishi namanyuvesi akufanele bayilibale.

Kuyadingeka ukuqonda ngokujulile zonke lezo zinqubo zengqondo eziqeda ubuhle BOTHANDO.

Akwaneli ukuba ngubaba noma ngumama womndeni, kufanele wazi UKUTHANDA. Abazali bakholelwa ukuthi bayazithanda izingane zabo ngoba banazo, ngoba ngezaso, ngoba baziphethe, njengomuntu ophethe ibhayisikili, imoto, indlu.

Lowo muzwa wokuba nokuncika, uvame ukudideka NOUTHANDO kodwa awusoze waba UTHANDO.

Othisha nothisha bekhaya lethu lesibili okuyisikole, bakholelwa ukuthi bayabathanda abafundi babo, ngoba bangababo njengoba benjalo, ngoba bayabanikazi, kodwa akulona UTHANDO. Umqondo wokuba nokuncika AKUYONA UTHANDO.

INGQONDO iyabhubhisa UTHANDO futhi kuphela ngokuqonda yonke imisebenzi eyiphutha yengqondo, indlela yethu engenangqondo yokucabanga, imikhuba yethu emibi, imikhuba ezenzakalelayo, ezenzekelayo, indlela eyiphutha yokubona izinto, njll. singakwazi ukuphila, ukuzwa NGEQINISO lokho okungekona okwesikhashana, lokho okubizwa ngokuthi UTHANDO.

Labo abafuna ukuthi UTHANDO luphenduke ucezu lomshini wabo ojwayelekile, labo abafuna ukuthi UTHANDO luhambe ezindaweni eziyiphutha zobandlululo lwabo, izifiso, ukwesaba, okuhlangenwe nakho kwempilo, indlela yobugovu yokubona izinto, indlela eyiphutha yokucabanga, njll. bagcina ngokuqeda UTHANDO ngoba alusoze luzivumela ukuba luncishwe.

Labo abafuna ukuthi UTHANDO lusebenze NJENGA engifuna, NJENGA engifisa, NJENGA engicabanga, balahlekelwa UTHANDO ngoba i-CUPIDO, UNKULUNKULU WOTHANDO, akazimisele neze ukuzivumela ukuthi aboshwe yiMI.

I-MI iyinqwaba yezinkumbulo, izifiso, ukwesaba, inzondo, izinkanuko, okuhlangenwe nakho, ubugovu, umona, ukuhaha, inkanuko, njll. njll. njll.

Kungokuqonda iphutha ngalinye ngokuhlukana; ngokuyafunda, ngiyabuka ngokuqondile hhayi esifundeni sobuhlakani kuphela, kodwa nakuzo zonke izigaba ezingaphansi kwengqondo, iphutha ngalinye liyanyamalala, siyafa njalo. Ngaleyo ndlela kuphela sifeza ukuhlanganiswa kweMI.

Labo abafuna ukubhodlela UTHANDO ngaphakathi kwebhodlela elithusayo le-mi, balahlekelwa UTHANDO, basala ngaphandle kwalo, ngoba UTHANDO alusoze lwabhodlelwa.

Ngeshwa abantu bafuna ukuthi UTHANDO luziphathe ngokuvumelana nemikhuba yabo, izifiso, amasiko, njll., abantu bafuna ukuthi UTHANDO luzithobe kuMI futhi lokho akunakwenzeka ngoba UTHANDO alulaleli uMI.

Imibhangqwana ethandanayo, noma kangcono ngisho nabathanda ezocansi, yiyona evame kakhulu kulo Mhlaba, bacabanga ukuthi UTHANDO kufanele luhambe ngokwethembeka ezindaweni zezifiso zabo, izinkanuko, amaphutha, njll., futhi kulokhu baphambuke ngokuphelele.

Masikhulume ngababili!, kusho abathandanayo noma abathanda ezocansi, okuyinto eningi kakhulu kulo Mhlaba, bese kuza izingxoxo, amaphrojekthi, izifiso nezililo. Umuntu ngamunye usho okuthile, uveza amaphrojekthi akhe, izifiso zakhe, indlela yakhe yokubona izinto zempilo futhi ufuna ukuthi UTHANDO luhambe njengomshini wesitimela ezindaweni zensimbi ezidwetshwe yingqondo.

Umuntu obukela intaba enhle futhi ayiqhathanise nenye ayibone izolo, akasiqondi ngempela ubuhle bentaba anakho emehlweni akhe.
