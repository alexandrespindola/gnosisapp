Uhulumeni unegunya, uMBUSO unegunya. Amaphoyisa, umthetho, isosha, abazali, othisha, abaholi benkolo, njll., banegunya.

Kunezinhlobo ezimbili zegunya. Okokuqala, IGUNYA ELINGAZI LUTHO. Okwesibili, IGUNYA ELIQAPHELAYO.

Alusizi ngalutho IGUNYA ELINGAZI LUTHO noma ELINGAPHAPHAMI. Sidinga NGOKUPHUTHUMA IGUNYA ELIZIQAPHELAYO.

IGUNYA ELINGAZI LUTHO noma ELINGAPHAPHAMI ligcwalise umhlaba ngezinyembezi nobuhlungu.

Emakhaya nasezikoleni IGUNYA ELINGAZI LUTHO liyalisebenzisa kabi IGUNYA ngenxa yokuthi LINGAZI LUTHO noma ALIZIPHAPHAMELI.

Abazali nothisha abangaphaphami, namuhla, bayizimpumputhe ezihola izimpumputhe futhi njengoba imibhalo engcwele isho, bayokuwela bonke emgodini.

Abazali nothisha abangaphaphami bayasiphoqa ebuntwaneni ukuba senze izinto ezingenamsebenzi, kodwa abakubheka njengokunengqondo. Bathi lokho kwenzelwa ukusizakala kwethu.

Abazali bayIGUNYA ELINGAZI LUTHO njengoba kufakazelwa iqiniso lokuthi baphatha izingane njengodoti, njengokungathi bona bangabantu abaphakeme kunohlobo lomuntu.

Othisha besilisa nabesifazane bagcina sebenenzondo ngabanye abafundi besilisa noma abafundi besifazane, futhi bephatha noma bejabulela abanye. Ngezinye izikhathi bajezisa kakhulu noma yimuphi umfundi okubiweyo ngisho noma lo mfundi engeyena omubi futhi bavuzwa ngamamaki amahle abafundi abaningi besilisa noma abafundi besifazane abangawufanele ngempela.

Abazali nothisha basesikoleni babeka imithetho eyiphutha yezingane zabafana, amantombazane, intsha, izintokazi, njll.

IGUNYA elingenayo IMUQAPHELO ingenza izinto ezingenamsebenzi kuphela.

Sidinga IGUNYA EZIQAPHELAYO. IMUQAPHELO ngukuQAPHELA OKUPHELELE KWEZAZO, ulwazi oluphelele lwazo ZONKE IZIMPILO ZANGAPHAKATHI zethu.

Ngu lowo kuphela onolwazi oluphelele lweZAWO ngempela oluvukile ngokuphelele. Lokho ukuba OQAPHELAYO.

Wonke umuntu ukholwa ukuthi UYAZIQAPHELA, kodwa kunzima kakhulu ukuthola empilweni umuntu omazi ngempela. Abantu banemibono eyiphutha ngokuphelele ngabo.

Ukuzazi kudinga IMIZAMO EMIKHULU NETHUSAYO. Kungokusebenzisa UKWAZI NGEZAWO kuphela lapho umuntu afinyelela khona ngempela EMUQAPHELO.

UKUSETSHENZISWA KABI kweGUNYA kubangelwa UKUNGAPHAPHAMI. Akukho IGUNYA ELIZIQAPHELAYO eliyoke lifinyelele EKUSETSHENZISWENI KABI kweGUNYA.

Ezinye izazi zefilosofi zimelene nalo lonke IGUNYA, ziyalizonda IGUNYA. Indlela yokucabanga efana naleyo NGEQINISO ngoba kukho konke okudaliwe, kusukela egciwaneni elincane kuya elangeni, kukhona izikali nezikali amazinga namazinga, amandla aphezulu alawulayo aqondisayo namandla aphansi alawulwayo aqondiswayo.

Kukhona igunya ENKOSIKAZI ekamelweni lezinyosi elilula. Kunegunya nemithetho kunoma yisiphi isidleke sezintuthwane. Ukubhujiswa komgomo weGUNYA kungaholela EKUNGABUSA.

IGUNYA lezikhathi ezibucayi esiphila kuzo LINGAPHAPHAMI futhi kucacile ukuthi ngenxa yale nkinga engokwengqondo, liyabopha, liyabophelela, liyahlukumeza, liyabangela ubuhlungu.

Sidinga OTHISHA, abaqeqeshi noma abaqondisi abangokomoya, abaphathi bombuso, abazali, njll., ABAZIQAPHELE ngokugcwele. Kungale ndlela kuphela lapho singenza ngayo ngempela UMHLA WOKUTHUTHUKA.

Kuyisiwula ukusho ukuthi akudingeki othisha nabaqondisi abangokomoya.

Akunangqondo ukunganaki umgomo weGUNYA kukho konke okudaliwe.

Labo ABAZIMELE, ABAGQAYILEYO, banombono wokuthi OTHISHA NABAQONDISI ABANGOKOMOYA, ABAFUNI SEKELA.

Kufanele siqaphele UKUBANTWANA NOBUBI bethu. Kufanele siqonde ukuthi sidinga IGUNYA, OTHISHA, ABAQEQESHI ABANGOKOMOYA, njll. KODWA ABAZIQAPHELE ukuze basiqondise, basisize futhi basihole ngobuhlakani.

IGUNYA ELINGAPHAPHAMI labaTHUTHI libhubhisa amandla okudala abafundi besilisa nabafundi besifazane. Uma umfundi ependa, uthisha ongaphaphami uyamtshela ukuthi kufanele apende ini, isihlahla noma indawo okufanele ayikopishe futhi umfundi ethukile akalinge aphume emithethweni yomshini yothisha.

Lokho akukhona ukudala. Kudingeka ukuthi umfundi abe umdali. Ukuthi uyakwazi ukuphuma emithethweni engaqaphile kaMTHUTHI ONGAPHAPHAMI, ukuze akwazi ukudlulisa konke akuzwayo maqondana nesihlahla, lonke uthando lwempilo olujikeleza emaqabungeni esihlahla anezintununtunu, lonke uphawu lwaso olunzulu.

UMTHUTHI OQAPHELAYO angeke amelane nokudala okukhululayo komoya.

OTHISHA abaneGUNYA ELIQAPHELAYO, abasoze bayalima ingqondo yabafundi besilisa nabafundi besifazane.

Othisha ABANGAQHAPHAMI babhubhisa ingqondo nobuhlakani babafundi besilisa nabafundi besifazane ngeGUNYA labo.

OTHISHA abaneGUNYA ELINGAPHAPHAMI bazi kuphela ukuthi bangajezisa futhi babeke imithetho eyisiwula ukuze abafundi baziphathe kahle.

OTHISHA ABAZIQAPHELAYO bafundisa abafundi besilisa nabafundi besifazane ngesineke esikhulu, babasiza ukuba baqonde ubunzima babo ngabanye, ukuze ngokukuqonda bakwazi ukudlula wonke amaphutha abo futhi bathuthuke ngempumelelo.

IGUNYA ELIQAPHELAYO noma ELIZIQAPHELAYO alisoze labhubhisa UKWAZISA.

IGUNYA ELINGAPHAPHAMI libhubhisa UKWAZISA futhi libangele ukulimala okukhulu kubafundi besilisa nabafundi besifazane.

Ukuhlakanipha kuza kuphela kithi lapho sijabulela inkululeko yangempela futhi OTHISHA abaneGUNYA EZIQAPHELAYO bazi ngempela ukuthi bayayihlonipha INKULULEKO YOKUDALA.

OTHISHA ABANGAQHAPHAMI bakholwa ukuthi bazi konke futhi banyathela inkululeko yabafundi ngokuthukulula ukuhlakanipha kwabo ngemithetho yabo engenampilo.

OTHISHA ABAZIQAPHELAYO BAYAZI ukuthi ABAZI futhi baze bazithokozise ngokufunda ngokubheka amakhono okudala abafundi babo.

Kudingeka ukuthi abafundi abasezikoleni, emakolishi nasemayunivesithi, badlulele esimweni esilula samashini aqeqeshiwe, esimeni esikhazimulayo sabantu abahlakaniphile nabenkululeko ukuze bakwazi ukubhekana ngempumelelo enkulu nabo bonke ubunzima obukhona.

Lokhu kudinga OTHISHA ABAZIQAPHELAYO, abanekhono abazikhathalela ngempela izifundo zabo, othisha abahola kahle ukuze bangabi nakho ukukhathazeka ngemali yanoma yiluphi uhlobo.

Ngeshwa wonke uMTHUTHI, wonke umzali, wonke umfundi, uzikholelwa ukuthi UYAZIQAPHELA. UVUKILE futhi lelo IPHUTHA lakhe ELIKHULU.

Kuyivelakancane ukuthola empilweni noma yimuphi umuntu OQAPHELAYO futhi OVUKILE.

Abantu bayaphupha lapho umzimba ulele futhi bayaphupha lapho umzimba usesimweni sokuvuka.

Abantu bashayela izimoto, bephupha; basebenza bephupha; bahamba emigwaqweni bephupha, baphila isikhathi sonke bephupha.

Kuyinto engokwemvelo ukuthi uprofesa akhohlwe isambulela noma ashiye incwadi ethile noma isikhwama sakhe semali emotweni. Konke lokho kwenzeka ngoba uprofesa ulele, uyaphupha...

Kunzima kakhulu ngabantu ukwamukela ukuthi balele, wonke umuntu uzikholelwa ukuthi uvukile. Uma othile engamukela ukuthi unemizwa yakhe elele, kucacile ukuthi kusukela ngaleso sikhathi angasoqala ukuvuka.

Umfundi wesilisa noma owesifazane uyakhohlwa ekhaya incwadi, noma incwajana okufanele ayiyise esikoleni, ukukhohlwa okunjengalokhu kubonakala kujwayelekile kanti kunjalo, kodwa kubonisa, kubonisa, isimo sokuphupha isimo somuzwa womuntu esikuso.

Abagibeli banoma iyiphi insizakalo yezokuthutha zasemadolobheni, bavame ukudlula ngezinye izikhathi emgwaqweni, bebelele futhi lapho bevuka bayabona ukuthi badlule emgwaqweni futhi manje kuzodingeka ukuthi babuyele emuva ngezinyawo emgwaqweni othile.

Kuvamile ukuba umuntu avuke ngempela empilweni futhi lapho evuke ngisho okwesikhashana, njengoba kunjalo ngezigameko zokwesaba okukhulu, uzibona okwesikhashana ngendlela EPHETHELELE. Lezo zikhathi azisoze zalibaleka.

Indoda ebuyela ekhaya ngemuva kokuhamba kuyo yonke idolobha, kunzima kakhulu ukukhumbula ngendlela enemininingwane yonke imicabango yayo, izigameko, abantu, izinto, imibono, njll. njll. njll. lapho izama ukukhumbula, izothola izikhala ezinkulu enkumbulweni yayo ezihambisana ngqo nezimo zokuphupha ezijulile.

Abanye abafundi bezokusebenza kwengqondo bahlose ukuphila BEQAPHELE njalo, kodwa ngokushesha bayalala, mhlawumbe lapho bethola umngane othile emgwaqweni, lapho bengena esitolo esithile ukuze bathenge okuthile, njll. futhi lapho bekhumbula amahora amaningi kamuva isinqumo sabo sokuphila BEQAPHELE futhi BEVUKILE njalo, bese beqaphela ukuthi bebelele lapho bengena endaweni ethile, noma lapho behlangana nomuntu othile, njll. njll. njll.

Ukuba OQAPHELAYO kuyinto enzima kakhulu kodwa umuntu angafinyelela kulesi simo ngokufunda ukuphila ngokuqapha nokuqapha NJALO.

Uma sifuna ukufinyelela EMUQAPHELO sidinga ukuzazi ngendlela EPHETHELELE.

Sonke sinesITHUNZI, INAMI, i-EGO esidinga ukuyihlola ukuze sizazi futhi sibe ABAZIQAPHELE.

KUPHUTHUMA UKUZIBHEKA, UKUHLABHULA nokUQONDA ngakunye kokubi kwethu.

Kudingeka ukuba sizifundele emkhakheni wengqondo, imizwa, imikhuba, imizwelo nobulili.

Ingqondo inamazinga amaningi, izindawo noma iminyango ENGAPHAPHAMI okufanele siyazi ngokujulile ngokusebenzisa UKUBUKA, UKUHLABHULA, UKUCABANGA OKUJULILE nokUQONDA OKUJULILE KOSOSIZI.

Noma yiliphi iphutha linganyamalala endaweni yezengqondo futhi liqhubeke likhona kwamanye amazinga angaphaphami engqondo.

Into yokuqala edingekayo UKUVUKA ukuze uqonde UBUMBI, UKUBANTWANA NOBUHLUNGU bethu. Bese i-SELF iqala UKUFA isikhashana. UKUFA KWEZENGQONDO NGOKUPHUTHUMA.

Ngokufa kuphela kuzalwa uBUKAZI obuqaphile ngempela kithi. Yena kuphela ongangena EBUTHO ELIQAPHELAYO eliyiqiniso.

VUKA, FA, ZALA. Lezi yizigaba ezintathu zengqondo ezisiholela EKUHLUKUMEZEKENI OKUQAPHELAYO KWEQINISO.

Kufanele sivuke ukuze SIFA futhi kufanele sife ukuze SIZALE. Lowo ofa ngaphandle kokuthi AVUKE uba NGUMUNTU ONGCWELE OYISIwULA. Lowo OZALWA ngaphandle kokuthi afe uba NGUMUNTU ONGEZIMPILO EZIMBILI, elilungile kakhulu nelimbi kakhulu.

Ukuvivinya IGUNYA leqiniso kungavunyelwa kuphela yilabo abaneBUKAZI obuqaphile.

Labo abangenabo BUKAZI OQAPHELAYO okwamanje, labo abangakaziQAPHELI okwamanje, bavame UKUSETSHENZISWA KABI IGUNYA futhi babangele umonakalo omkhulu.

OTHISHA kufanele bafunde ukuyala futhi abafundi kufanele bafunde ukulalela.

Lezo ZENGQONDO ezimemezela ngokumelene nokulalela zinephutha kakhulu ngoba akekho ongayala ngokuqapha uma engakafundi ukulalela.

Kufanele wazi ukuthi ungayala NGOKUQAPHELA futhi kufanele wazi ukuthi ungalalela kanjani NGOKUQAPHELA.
