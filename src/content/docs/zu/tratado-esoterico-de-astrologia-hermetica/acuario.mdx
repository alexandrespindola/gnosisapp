Ngesikhathi i-YO ithathekiswa yi-GNOSIS, ifunga ukwethembeka okuphakade ku-MOVIMIENTO GNÓSTICO yethu; lokhu kuthakazelela kuqhubeka kuze kube yilapho enye i-YO ephikisana nalezi zifundo, ithatha amandla, emva kwalokho sibona ngokumangala ukuthi umuntu uyahoxa futhi aze abe yisitha sethu.

I-YO efunga NAMUHLA UTHANDO LOKUPHAKADE kumfazi, iphazanyiswa kamuva yi-YO ethile engahlangene naleso sifungo bese umfazi ahlushwa ukudumazeka.

I-YO enjalo ilandela ngokuzenzakalelayo enye futhi abanye bahlala bebonakala bephelezelwa abanye, kodwa akukho hlelo noma isistimu phakathi kwabo bonke labo YOES.

Yonke yalezo zinto i-YO ikholelwa okwesikhashana ukuthi iyikho konke, kodwa empeleni ayiyona ingxenye yangaphakathi yemisebenzi yethu, noma ngabe inomuzwa wokuthi iyiyo yonke, iqiniso, umuntu ophelele.

Okuthakazelisayo ukuthi siyakholelwa i-YO yesikhashana, ngisho noma ngemuva kwemizuzu ethile leyo YO iphazanyiswa enye i-YO. I-EGO LUNAR isamba sama-YOES okufanele siqedwe ngendlela ephelele.

Kuyadingeka ukwazi ukuthi ngayinye yamacilindri amahlanu omshini inezici zayo okungafanele neze siziphambanise.

Phakathi kwama-centros amahlanu omshini kunomehluko wejubane.

Abantu bayakuncoma kakhulu ukucabanga, kodwa eqinisweni i-CENTRO INTELECTUAL iyona ehamba kancane kakhulu. Emva kwalokho, noma ngabe ishesha kakhulu, kufika ama-CENTROS INSTINTIVO kanye nokuthi i-MOVIMIENTO noma i-MOTOR, enesivinini esifanayo. Esheshayo kunabo bonke i-CENTRO SEXUAL, bese ilandelwa ngokulandelana kwejubane, i-CENTRO EMOCIONAL.

Kunomehluko omkhulu wejubane phakathi kwama-CENTROS amahlanu omshini.

Ngokufunda ngaphakathi kwethu ngokuzibuka, sizobona ngeso lengqondo ukuthi i-MOVIMIENTO ishesha kune-CABANGA nokuthi umuzwa ushesha kunanoma yimuphi umsebenzi kanye nemicabango yonke.

Izikhungo ze-MOTOR ne-INSTINTIVO zinezikhathi eziyizi-TREINTA MIL ngaphezu kwe-CENTRO INTELECTUAL. I-CENTRO EMOCIONAL uma isebenza ngejubane eliyayo, ishesha izikhathi eziyizinkulungwane ezingamashumi amathathu kune-CENTROS MOTOR ne-INSTINTIVO.

I-CENTROS ehlukene inezikhathi zayo ezihluke ngokuphelele. Ijubane lezikhungo lichaza inani elikhulu lezinto ezaziwayo isayensi evamile evamile engenakukwazi ukuzichaza; kwanele ukukhumbula ijubane elimangalisayo lezinqubo ezithile zengqondo, ze-fisiológicas nezengqondo.

Yonke i-CENTRO ihlukaniswe ngezingxenye ezimbili: ezinhle nezingalungile; lokhu kuhlukaniswa kucace ngokukhethekile kwi-CENTRO INTELECTUAL naku-CENTRO INSTINTIVO.

Wonke umsebenzi we-CENTRO INTELECTUAL uhlukaniswe ngezingxenye ezimbili: UKUQINISEKISWA kanye NOKUPHIKA; Yebo CHA, i-TESIS kanye ne-ANTÍTESIS.

Ku-CENTRO INSTINTIVO kukhona umzabalazo ofanayo phakathi kokujabulisayo nokungajabulisayo; imizwa ejabulisayo, imizwa engajabulisayo futhi yonke leyo mizwa ihlobene nemizwa emihlanu: ukubona, ukuzwa, ukuhogela, ukunambitha, ukuthinta.

Ku-CENTRO MOTOR noma i-MOVIMIENTO, kukhona umzabalazo phakathi kwe-MOVIMIENTO ne-REPOSO.

Ku-CENTRO EMOCIONAL kukhona imizwa ejabulisayo nengajabulisi: intokozo, uzwela, uthando, ukuzethemba, njll, zinhle.

I-EMOCIONES engajabulisi, njengokukhathazeka, umona, umhawu, intukuthelo, ukucasuka, ukwesaba, zonke zimbi.

Ku-CENTRO SEXUAL kukhona ukukhanga nokunengwa, ubumsulwa kanye nokunethezeka ngokungapheli.

I-ANIMAL INTELECTUAL inikela ngokuzijabulisa uma kudingeka, kodwa ayikwazi ukunikela ngokuhlupheka kwayo.

Noma ngubani ofuna UKUCHITHA I-YO PLURALIZADO, kufanele anikele ngokuhlupheka kwayo. I-CELOS ikhiqiza ukuhlupheka, uma siqeda i-celos, ukuhlupheka kuyafa, kuncishiswa ubuhlungu.

I-IRA ikhiqiza i-DOLOR; uma siqeda nge-IRA siphungula i-DOLOR, siyayibhujiswa.
