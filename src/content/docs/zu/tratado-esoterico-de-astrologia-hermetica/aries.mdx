## 21 KUMASHI 20 KUHLANGANISA NOMHLAZI 20 ENHLANGLA 

Kunemimo emine yezinga LOKUQAPHELA engaba khona kumuntu: UBUDLELE, UKUQAPHELA KWEMINI, UKUZIQAPHELA kanye NOKUQAPHELA OKUNEMBA. 

Ake ucabange okwesikhashana, mfundi othandekayo, indlu enezitezi ezine. ISILWANE ESINOBUNGCWELI okuyisona esibizwa ngokuthi UMNUNTU siphila ngokujwayelekile ezitezi ezimbili ezingezansi, kodwa akusoze empilweni yaso sasebenzisa izitezi ezimbili ezingaphezulu. 

ISILWANE ESINOBUNGCWELI sihlukumeza impilo yaso ebuhlungu nenesabekayo phakathi kobuthongo obuvamile kanye nalokho okubizwa ngokuthi isimo SOKUQAPHELA, okuyinto edabukisayo futhi enye indlela yobuthongo. 

Ngenkathi umzimba womuntu ulele embhedeni, i-EGO isongwe EMIBIMBINI YAYO YENYANGA ihamba nokuqaphela okuqwathile njengomuntu oqwashile ehambahamba ngokukhululeka endaweni yama-molecule. I-EGO endaweni yama-molecule ikhiqiza AMAPHUPHO futhi iphila kuwo, akukho ngqondo kunoma yimaphi AMAPHUPHO ayo, ukuqhubeka, izimbangela, imiphumela, yonke imisebenzi YENGQONDO isebenza ngaphandle kwesiqondiso futhi kuvela futhi kunyamalale izithombe ezingaqondakali, izigcawu ezingahlelekile, ezingacacile, ezingaphelele, njll. 

Lapho i-EGO isongwe EMIBIMBINI YAYO YENYANGA ibuyela EMZIMBENI WOMUNTU, bese kuqala isimo sesibili sokuqaphela okubizwa ngokuthi isimo SOKUQAPHELA, okuyinto empeleni engesinye isimo sobuthongo. 

Lapho i-EGO ibuyela EMZIMBENI wayo WOMUNTU, amaphupho ayaqhubeka ngaphakathi, lokho okubizwa ngokuthi ISIMO SOKUQAPHELA empeleni UKUPHUPHA UVUKILE. 

Lapho ilanga liphuma, izinkanyezi ziyacasha, kodwa aziyeki ukuba khona; kanjalo namaphupho esimweni sokuqapha, ayaqhubeka ngasese, awayeki ukuba khona. 

Lokhu kusho ukuthi ISILWANE ESINOBUNGCWELI okuyisona esibizwa ngokuthi UMNUNTU, siphila kuphela emhlabeni wamaphupho; ngesizathu esihle imbongi yathi impilo iyiphupho. 

ISILWANE ESIQHUBEKA NGOKUNENGQONDO siqhuba izimoto siphupha, sisebenza efektri, ehhovisi, ensimini, njll., siphupha, siyathandana emaphusheni, siyashada emaphusheni; akuvamile, akuvamile impela empilweni, sivukile, siphila emhlabeni wamaphupho futhi sikholelwa ngokuqinile ukuthi sivukile. 

AmaVangeli Amane afuna UKUVUKA, kodwa ngeshwa awasho ukuthi UKUVUKA kanjani. 

Ngaphezu kwakho konke, kuyadingeka ukuqonda ukuthi ulele; kuphela lapho umuntu eqaphela ngokugcwele ukuthi ulele, uqala ngempela indlela YOKUVUKA. 

Lowo ofinyelela EKUVUKENI UKUQAPHELA, ube ESENAWO - UKUQAPHELA, athole UKUQAPHELA NGAYE. 

Iphutha elibi kakhulu LABANINGI ABALANDELI BEMIBONO YEZIMFIHLO abangazi futhi ABAFUNDISI BEZIMFIHLO abangazi, ukuzenza ABANOKUZIQAPHELA futhi bakholwe nokuthi wonke umuntu uvukile, ukuthi bonke abantu BANOKUZIQAPHELA. 

Ukuba bonke abantu BANE-UKUQAPHELA OKUVUKILE Umhlaba ngabe uyipharadesi, bekungeke kube khona izimpi, bekungeke kube khona okwami nokwakho, konke ngabe kungokwabo bonke, besizophila ENKATHINI YEGOLIDE. 

Lapho umuntu EVUSA UKUQAPHELA, lapho ezithola ENAZO - UKUQAPHELA, lapho ethola UKUQAPHELA NGAYE, yilapho ekugcineni eza ukwazi IQINISO ngaye. 

Ngaphambi kokuthola isimo sesithathu SOKUQAPHELA (UKUZIQAPHELA), umuntu empeleni akazazi YENA, noma ngabe ekholwa ukuthi uyazazi. 

Kuyadingeka ukuthola isimo sesithathu sokuqaphela, ukukhuphukela esitezi sesithathu sendlu, ngaphambi kokuba nelungelo lokudlulela esitezi sesine. 

ISIMO SESINE SOKUQAPHELA, ISITEZI SESINE sendlu, siyethusa ngempela. Kuphela lowo ofinyelela EKUQAPHELENI OKUNEMBA, Esimeni SESINE, ongakwazi ukufunda izinto ngokwazo, umhlaba njengoba unjalo. 

Lowo ofinyelela esitezi sesine sendlu, ngokungangabazeki UYAKHANYISELWA, wazi ngamava aqondile IZIMFIHLO ZEMPILO NOKUFA, unobuGcwele, umqondo wakhe WENDAWO uthuthukile ngokugcwele. 

Ngesikhathi SOKULALA OBUJULE singaba nezithunzi ZESIMO SOKUQAPHELA. Ngesikhathi SESIMO SOKUQAPHELA singaba nezithunzi ZOKUZIQAPHELA. Ngesikhathi SESIMO SOKUZIQAPHELA singaba nezithunzi ZOKUQAPHELA OKUNEMBA. 

Uma sifuna ukufinyelela EKUVUKENI KOKUQAPHELA, KU-UKUZIQAPHELA, kufanele sisebenze NGE-UKUQAPHELA lapha nakanje. Kulapha impela kulomhlaba womzimba lapho kufanele sisebenze khona ukuze SIVUSE UKUQAPHELA, lowo ovuka lapha uvuka kuyo yonke indawo, kuzo zonke izilinganiso zeNdawo Yonke. 

UMZIMBA WOMUNTU yi-ZODIAC EPHILAYO futhi ekuhlanganisweni ngakunye kwezinkanyezi zayo eziyishumi nambili, ukuqaphela kulele ngokujulile. 

Kuphuthuma ukuvusa ukuqaphela engxenyeni ngayinye yezindawo eziyishumi nambili zomzimba womuntu futhi ngenxa yalokho kukhona izivivinyo ze-zodiac. 

I-Aries, ibusa ikhanda; I-Taurus, umphimbo; I-Gemini, izingalo, imilenze kanye namaphaphu; I-Cancer, indlala ye-thymus; I-Leo inhliziyo; I-Virgo, isisu, amathumbu; I-Libra, izinso; I-Scorpio, izitho zokuzala; I-Sagittarius, imithambo ye-femoral; I-Capricorn, amadolo; I-Aquarius, imilenze yezinyawo; I-Pisces, izinyawo. 

Kuyadabukisa ngempela ukuthi le-zodiac ephilayo yendoda YE-MICRO-COSMOS, ilele ngokujulile kangaka. Kubalulekile ukufeza ngesisekelo SEMIZAMO EKULU KAKHULU, UKUVUSWA KOKUQAPHELA kusiginesha ngayinye yethu ye-ZODIAC eziyishumi nambili. 

Ukukhanya Nokuqaphela kuyizimo ezimbili zento eyodwa; ngezinga eliphansi lokuQaphela, izinga eliphansi lokukhanya; ngezinga elikhulu lokuQaphela, izinga elikhulu lokukhanya. 

Sidinga UKUVUSA UKUQAPHELA ukuze sikhanyise futhi sikhazimulise ingxenye ngayinye yezindawo eziyishumi nambili ze-zodiac yethu ye-MICRO-COSMIC. Yonke i-zodiac yethu kufanele ibe ukukhanya nenkazimulo. 

Umsebenzi ne-Zodiac yethu uqala nge-ARIES ngqo. UMfundi uhlala esihlalweni esinethezekile nengqondo ezolile nokuthula, engenalutho izinhlobo ezihlukahlukene zemicabango. Umkhulekeli uvala amehlo akhe ukuze kungekho lutho oluvela emhlabeni olumphazamisayo, cabanga ukuthi ukukhanya okumsulwa kwe-ARIES kugcwala ubuchopho bakho, hlala kuleso simo sokuzindla sonke isikhathi osifunayo bese ucula i-Mantram AUM enamandla uvula kahle umlomo nge-A, uyisondezele nge-U futhi uyivale nge-M engcwele. 

I-Mantram AUM enamandla ngokwayo iyindalo ENGCWELE KAKHULU, ngoba ihehe amandla kaBABA, othandwa kakhulu, weNDODANA ethandwa kakhulu kanye noMoya oNgcwele ohlakaniphe kakhulu. Unkamisa A udonsela amandla kaBABA, unkamisa U udonsela amandla weNDODANA, unkamisa M udonsela amandla oMoya oNgcwele. I-AUM iyi-MANTRAM LOGICAL enamandla. 

Umkhulekeli kufanele acule le MANTRAM enamandla izikhathi ezine ngesikhathi sokuzivocavoca kwe-ARIES bese ekhuphuka emi ebheke empumalanga welula ingalo yakhe yangakwesokudla phambili enyakazisa ikhanda lakhe izikhathi eziyisikhombisa phambili, eziyisikhombisa emuva, eziyisikhombisa ezijikelezayo ohlangothini lwesokudla, eziyisikhombisa zijikelezisa ohlangothini lwesobunxele ngenhloso yokuthi ukukhanya kwe-ARIES kusebenze ngaphakathi kobuchopho kuvusa izindlala ze-pineal ne-pituitary ezisivumela ukubona IZILINGANISO EZINGAPHEZULU ZESIKHALA. 

Kuphuthuma ukuthi UKUKHANYA KWE-ARIES kukhule ngaphakathi kobuchopho bethu kuvuse UKUQAPHELA, kuthuthukisa amandla ayimfihlo aqukethwe EZINDLANENI ZE-PITUITARY ne-PINEAL. 

I-ARIES iyisifanekiselo se-RA, RAMA, imvu. I-MANTRAM RA enamandla, iyicula njengoba kufanele, yenza umlilo we-spinal vibrate kanye nezikhungo eziyisikhombisa ze-magnetic zomgogodla. 

I-ARIES iwuphawu lwe-zodiac lomlilo, inamandla amakhulu futhi INDODA YE-MICRO-COSMOS iyawabamba ngokuya ngendlela yayo yokucabanga, yokuzizwa kanye nokusebenza. 

U-HITLER, owayengumuntu wase-ARIES, wasebenzisa lolu hlobo lwamandla ngendlela ebhubhisayo, nokho, kufanele siqaphele ukuthi ekuqaleni, ngaphambi kokwenza ubuwula bokuvulela isintu impi yezwe yesibili, wasebenzisa amandla e-ARIES ngendlela eyakhayo, ekhuphula izinga lempilo LABANTU BASEJERMANI. 

Sikwazile ukuqinisekisa ngamava aqondile ukuthi abantu bomdabu base-ARIES baxabana kakhulu nomlingani noma umlingani. 

Abantu bomdabu base-ARIES bathambekele kakhulu ekuxabaneni bangabantu bemvelo abanobudlova. 

Abantu bomdabu base-ARIES bazizwa bekulungele ukungena ezinkampanini ezinkulu futhi baziqede ngokuphumelelayo. 

Kukhona isici esibi kubantu bomdabu base-ARIES sokufuna ukusebenzisa amandla entando ngendlela yobugovu njalo, isitayela sika-HITLER, ESINGAVUMELANI NABANTU futhi esicekelayo phansi. 

Abantu bomdabu base-ARIES bayakujabulela kakhulu ukuphila okuzimele, kodwa abantu abaningi base-ARIAN bakhetha ezempi futhi kulokhu akukho ukuzimela. 

Esicini abantu base-ARIAN bagqamisa ukuziqhenya, ukuzethemba, isifiso esikhulu kanye nenani elihlanya ngempela. 

Insimbi ye-ARIES yi-IRON, itshe, yi-RUBY, umbala, yi-RED, isici, yi-FIRE. 

Abantu bomdabu base-ARIES balungele umshado nabantu base-LIBRA, ngoba umlilo nomoya kuqondana kahle kakhulu. 

Uma abantu bomdabu base-ARIES befuna ukujabula emshadweni, kufanele baqede isici sentukuthelo.
