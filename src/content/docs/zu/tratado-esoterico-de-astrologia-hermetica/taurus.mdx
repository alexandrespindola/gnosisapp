## 21 KWEKA-APRILI KUYA KU-20 KWEKA-MEIYO

Njengoba iTaurus iyiSIQINISEKISO SEZODIAC esilawula iLARYNX YOKUDALA, leso SIBELETHO esimangalisayo lapho kuzalwa khona iZWI, uMSHO, kufanelekile ukuthi, ngeGROSSO MODO, siqonde kulesi sifundo amazwi kaJUAN, lapho athi: "Ekuqaleni kwakukhona uMSHO futhi uMSHO wawunoNkulunkulu futhi uMSHO wawunguNkulunkulu, ngaye zonke izinto zenziwa futhi ngaphandle kwakhe akukho lutho olwenziweyo olwaluyokwenziwa."

Kukhona imikhakha eyisikhombisa yemihlaba, amakosmos ayisikhombisa adalwe ngamandla oMshO, ngomculo, ngomsindo.

Umkhakha wesihlanu wemihlaba wakhiwa yiyo yonke imihlaba yesistimu yelanga.

Umkhakha wesithupha wemihlaba uMhlaba ngokwawo, nezilinganiso zawo eziyisikhombisa nezifunda ezigcwele izidalwa ezingapheli.

Umkhakha wesikhombisa wemihlaba wakhiwa yilezo zindawo eziyisikhombisa ezigxile emhlabeni noma IMIMANDLA YASESIHOGO yoMbuso Wezimbiwa ogxile ngaphansi kogqinsi loMhlaba.

Umculo, uMSHO, obekwe yi-LOGOS kuma-octave womculo ayisikhombisa, usekela iNdawo Yonke iqine ohambweni lwayo.

Umkhakha wokuqala wemihlaba, inothi DO. Umkhakha wesibili wemihlaba, inothi SI. Umkhakha wesithathu wemihlaba, inothi LA. Umkhakha wesine wemihlaba, inothi SOL. Umkhakha wesihlanu wemihlaba, inothi FA. Umkhakha wesithupha wemihlaba, inothi MI. Umkhakha wesikhombisa wemihlaba, inothi RE. Ngemuva kwalokho yonke into ibuyela ku-ABSOLUTE ngenothi DO.

Ngaphandle komculo, ngaphandle koMSHO, ngaphandle KWEZWI ELIKHULU, bekuyokwenzeka ukuthi kube khona amaKHOSMOSI AYISIKHOMBISA amangalisayo.

DO-RE-MI-FA-SOL-LA-SI. SI-LA-SOL-FA-MI-RE-DO. Amanothi ayisikhombisa e-GRAN ESCALA DEL VERBO CREADOR, azwakala kukho konke okudalweyo, ngoba ekuqaleni kwakukhona uMSHO.

Umkhakha wokuqala wemihlaba ubuswa ngokuhlakanipha UMTETHO OWODWA, ngUMTHETHO OMKHULU. Umkhakha wesibili wemihlaba ubuswa IMITHETHO EMITHATHU. Umkhakha wesithathu wemihlaba ubuswa yimithetho eyisithupha. Umkhakha wesine wemihlaba ubuswa yimithetho eyishumi nambili. Umkhakha wesihlanu wemihlaba ubuswa yimithetho engamashumi amabili nane. Umkhakha wesithupha wemihlaba ubuswa yimithetho engamashumi amane nesishiyagalombili. Umkhakha wesikhombisa wemihlaba ubuswa yimithetho engamashumi ayisishiyagalolunye nesithupha.

Lapho kukhulunywa ngeZWI, kukhulunywa futhi ngomsindo womculo, ngeZIQONDISO, NGOMLILO nemithetho yaso emithathu yeMAHAVAN neCHOTAVAN esekela i-UNIVERSO iqine ohambweni lwayo.

ABA-SEUDO-OCULTISTAS kanye ne-SEUDO-ESOTERISTAS, bavele bakhulume nge-MICROCOSMOS, kanye ne-MACROCOSMOS, bacaphune imikhakha emibili yemihlaba kuphela, ngenkathi empeleni kukhona AMAKHOSMOSI AYISIKHOMBISA, imikhakha eyisikhombisa yemihlaba esekelwa yiZWI, ngomculo, nge-FIAT LUMINOSO kanye nespermatic yesikhashana sokuqala.

Ngamunye wamaKHOSMOSI AYISIKHOMBISA ngaphandle kokungabaza uyinhlangano ephilayo ephefumulayo, ezwayo futhi ephilayo.

Ngokombono we-ESOTERIC, singaqinisekisa ukuthi yonke inqubekela phambili eya phezulu, ingumphumela wentuthuko eya phansi. Akwenzeki ukukhuphuka ngaphandle kokwehla. Okokuqala kufanele wehle bese uyakhuphuka.

Uma sifuna ukwazi iKHOSMOS, kufanele siqale sazi ezimbili ezihambisanayo, leyo engaphezulu kwaleyo kanye naleyo engaphansi, ngoba zombili zinquma zonke izimo nezenzakalo ezibalulekile zeKHOSMOS esifuna ukuyitadisha, ukuyazi.

Isibonelo: Kulesi sikhathi lapho ososayensi belwela ukunqoba isikhala, kwenziwa intuthuko enkulu ngeshwa encane, emhlabeni we-athomu.

Ukwakhiwa KWAMAKHOSMOSI AYISIKHOMBISA kwakwenzeka kuphela ngomshO, ngeZWI, ngomculo.

Abafundi bethu be-GNÓSTIC akufanele nanini bakhohlwe ukuthi ziyini izikhwepha ezintathu ezibizwa nge-FATHER-SON-HOLY SPIRIT. Lezi zikhwepha ezintathu zakha i-SAGRADA TRIAMAZIKAMNO.

Lokhu kuyiSIQINISEKISO ESIQINILE, UKUPHIKA OKUQINILE, UKUBUYISELANA OKUQINILE; UNKULUNKULU OYINGCWELE, OQINILE OYINGCWELE, ONGAFA OYINGCWELE.

Kugezi, lezi yiziphetho ezintathu ezi-POSITIVO-NEGATIVO-NEUTRO. Ngaphandle kokuhlangana kwalezi ziphetho ezintathu, kwenzeka konke ukudalwa.

KuSayensi ye-ESOTÉRICA GNÓSTICA, IZIKHWEPHA EZINTATHU ezizimele zinamagama alandelayo: SURP-OTHEOS; SURP-SKIROS; SURP-ATHANATOS. ISIKHWEPHA ESIPHAKAMISA, ESIQINISEKILE, ESIHLE. ISIKHWEPHA ESIKHESHA, ISIKHWEPHA SOKUPHIKA, ISIKHWEPHA SOKUMELANA. ISIKHWEPHA SOKUBUYISELANA, ISIKHWEPHA SOKUKHULULA, ISIKHWEPHA SOKUQEDWA.

Lezi zikhwepha ezintathu ku-RAYO DE LA CREACIÓN zibukeka ziyimigomo emithathu, yimicabango emithathu, yizinyunithi ezintathu. Ngasinye salezi zikhwepha ezintathu siqukethe KUZO ZONA zonke amathuba alezi zintathu. Kodwa endaweni yazo yokuhlangana, ngasinye sazo sibonisa isiqalo salo kuphela: esihle, esikheshayo noma esiqedayo.

Kuyathakazelisa kakhulu ukubona izikhwepha ezintathu zisebenza: ziyahlukana, ziyadedelana bese ziPHINDE ZIHLAngANE ukuze zakhe izintathu ezintsha ezivela emhlabeni, okudalwa okusha.

Ku-ABSOLUTE, izikhwepha ezintathu yi-LOGOS UNIQUE, i-EJÉRCITO DE LA VOZ ngaphakathi KOBUNYE OKUKHULU bempilo ekhululekile ekuhambeni kwayo.

INQUBO YOKUDALA ye-SAGRADA TRIAMAZIKAMNO CÓSMICA COMÚN yaqala ngomshado wobulili beZWI ngoba ekuqaleni kwakukhona uMSHO, futhi uMSHO wawunoNKULUNKULU futhi uMSHO wawunguNkulunkulu. Ngaye zonke izinto zenziwa futhi ngaphandle kwakhe akukho lutho olwenziweyo, olwaluyokwenziwa.

Ngokuhambisana ne-SAGRADA LEY DE HEPTAPARAPARSHINOKH (UMTHETHO OYISIKHOMBISA), kwakhiwa amathempeli ayisikhombisa ku-CAOS ukuze kwakhiwe lesi sistimu selanga.

Ngokuhambisana ne-LEY SAGRADA DE TRIAMAZIKAMNO (UMTHETHO OYIMITHATHU), ama-ELOHIM ahlukaniswa ngamaqembu amathathu ngaphakathi kwetempeli ngalinye ukuze acule ngokuhambisana ne-LITURGIA DEL FUEGO.

Umsebenzi wokwenza i-PRAKRITI ivunde, okungukuthi, i-CAOS, i-MADRE CÓSMICA, i-GRAN VIENTRE, njalo umsebenzi we-TEOMERSMALOGOS engcwele kakhulu, ISIKHWEPHA SESITHATHU.

Ngaphakathi kwetempeli ngalinye kwahlelwa amaqembu amathathu ngale ndlela; Okokuqala, UMphristi. Okwesibili, uMphristikazi. Okwesithathu: iqembu elimaphakathi lama-ELOHIM.

Uma sicabangela ukuthi ama-ELOHIM yi-ANDRÓGINOS, khona-ke kucacile ukuthi kwadingeka ukuthi aguqule ngokuzithandela abe yisimo SObUNGENI, SObUMAME kanye NE-NEUTRA, ngokuhambisana ne-SAGRADA TRIAMAZIKAMNO CÓSMICA COMÚN.

UMpristi kanye noMpristikazi phambi kwe-altare nasendlini ephansi yetempeli, iculo le-ANDRÓGINO lama-ELOHIM.

AMARETHIYUMLA OMLILO ayeculwa futhi umshado wobulili weZWI wenza i-GRAN VIENTRE ye-CAOS yakhula futhi yazalwa i-UNIVERSO.

Izingelosi zidala ngamandla eZWI. ILaringe yisibeletho lapho kuzalwa khona iZWI.

Kufanele SIQUQULE ISAZISO ngeZWI, kwi-LARINGE YOKUDALA, ukuze ngelinye ilanga nathi sikwazi ukubiza i-FIAT LUMINOSO KANYE NESPERMÁTICO yesikhashana sokuqala.

ISAZISO silele ebhokisini lethu, asiqapheli ngeZWI, sidinga ukwenza NGOKUPHELELE UKUQAPHELA IZWI.

Bathi ukuthula kuyigolide. Sithi kukhona ukuthula kobugebengu. Kubi ngendlela efanayo ukukhuluma lapho kufanele uthule, njengokuthula lapho kufanele ukhulume.

Kunezikhathi lapho ukukhuluma kuyicala, kunezikhathi lapho ukuthula kuyelinye icala.

Kufana nembali enhle, egcwele imibala, kodwa entula iphunga, amazwi amahle angenanzuzo alowo ongenzi ngokuvumelana nalokho akushoyo.

Kodwa kufana nembali enhle, egcwele imibala futhi egcwele iphunga, amazwi amahle navundile alowo osebenza ngokuvumelana nalokho akushoyo.

Kuphuthuma ukuqeda ngokuqwashisa kweZWI, kuyadingeka ukukhuluma ngokuqondile, ngendlela EQAPHELAYO nangesikhathi esifanele. Sidinga ukwenza I-ISAZISO soMshO.

Kunomthwalo wemfanelo emazwini futhi ukwahlulela ngeZWI kuyize. Akekho onelungelo lokwahlulela muntu; kuyinto engenangqondo ukunyundela umakhelwane; kuwubuwula ukuhleba impilo yabanye.

Amazwi anobugebengu asilela ngokushesha noma kamuva, njengombani wempindiselo. Amazwi onyevayo, anonya, njalo abuyela kulowo obize ayephendulwa amatshe alimazayo.

Ngezikhathi ezedlule, lapho abantu babengakazihlanganisi kangaka nalesi sivivilizi esingamanga, abahlaziyi babeyisa imfuyo esibayeni becula ngendlela emnandi futhi yemvelo elula.

I-TORO, i-VACA, indlovukazi encane, ziyathinteka ngomculo, zihambelana nesibonakaliso se-zodiacal se-TAURUS, iqoqo lezinkanyezi zeZWI, lomculo.

Ku-GRAN ALEGORÍA PURANICA, umhlaba olandelwa yi-PRITHU ubalekela ukuziguqula ube yi-VACA futhi uziphumulele e-BRAHAMA. Kodwa le BRAHAMA ingumuntu wokuqala we-TRIMURTI Indostán. I-VACH, i-VACA ingumuntu wesibili kanti i-VIRAH VARÓN DIVINO, indlovukazi encane, i-KABIR, i-LOGOS, ingumuntu wesithathu.

IBRAHAMA ingumzali. I-VACA ingumAMA ONGCWELE, i-CAOS; I-TERNERILLO yi-KABIR, i-LOGOS.

Umzali, Umama, Indodana, yi-TRIMURTI PURANICA. Umzali UWUKULWAZISA. UMama ULUTHANDO, iNdodana yi-LOGOS, iZWI.

I-VACA ASTRAL yezinyawo ezinhlanu umphenyi u-Olcott akholelwa ukuthi uyibona ngokomzimba, phambi kwe-hipogeo emangalisayo yase-Karli; inkomo exakile futhi engaqondakali umphangi othile omncane ayibona e-Andes, njengoba umgcini ongaqondakali walezo ncebo ezazifunwa ngabaphangi be-ranchería yakhe, imelela uMAMA ONGCWELE, u-REA, u-CIBELES, okhule ngokuphelele ENDODENI YEQINISO, kuMHLENGI oZIPHETHILE.

I-GAUTAMA i-BUDHA noma i-GOTAMA, isho ngokoqobo umshayeli we-VACA. Wonke umshayeli, wonke umshayeli we-VACA, angasebenzisa umlilo we-JAINO we-VACA ukungena emhlabeni, izigodlo, amathempeli namadolobha ase-JINAS.

I-TAURUS isimema ku-REFLEXIÓN. Asikhumbule ukuthi i-MERCURIO yeba amaVAKA ELANGA.

I-TAURUS ilawula i-LARINGE YOKUDALA. Kuphuthuma ukuthi i-KUNDALINI ichume ezindebeni zethu ezivundile ezenziwe ZWI, yile ndlela kuphela esingasebenzisa ngayo umlilo we-JAINO ukungena eMBUSWENI wama-JINAS.

Kulesi sikhathi se-TAURUS kufanele siyi-Luz iye ebhokisini lethu lokudala ngenhloso yokukulungiselela ukuza KOMLILO.

Umfundi akahlale esitulweni esikhululekile; avale amehlo ukuze kungabikho lutho lwaleli zwe leze neliyisiwula olumphazamisayo, akhiphe ingqondo, akhiphe engqondweni yakhe zonke izinhlobo zemicabango, izifiso, izinkathazo, njll. Manje cabanga ukuthi I-Luz eqoqwe ngesikhathi se-ARIES esiqwini sayo, ekhanda layo, idlulela ebhokisini lokudala nge-TAURUS.

Ozinikele makahlabelele i-MANTRAM AUM. Avule kahle umlomo nge-A, acabange ukuthi I-Luz yehla isuka ekhanda iya ebhokisini; akhulume i-U, ecabanga ngobuqotho ukuthi i-luz igcwalisa umphimbo; umlomo kufanele uzungezwe kahle ukuze kuculwe u-U.

Uhlamvu lokugcina ngu-M, evale izindebe, akhiphe noma aphonse umoya ngamandla njengokuthi aqeda amabibi omphimbo. Lo msebenzi wenziwa ngokucula i-Mantram AUM enamandla izikhathi ezine.

Ekuzilolongeni sikwazile ukufakazela ukuthi ama-TAURINOS akufanele ashade nabantu base-ACUARIO, ngoba bayahluleka ngokungenakugwenywa, ngenxa yokungahambisani kwemikhuba.

Isibonakaliso se-TAURUS siqinile, somhlaba, sithambekele ekuzinzeni futhi njengoba isibonakaliso se-ACUARIO sinomoya, siyashukuma siyaziguqula, kucacile ukuthi asihambisani.

Ama-TAURINOS afana nenkabi, athobekile futhi ayasebenza, kodwa lapho ethukuthele, esabeka njenge-TORO.

Ama-TAURINOS adlula ezimpilweni zawo EZEHLAKALO EZINKULU ZOTHANDO, ayabekelwa eceleni, ayalondolozwa, alandela igxathu negxathu, njengenkabi, endleleni edwetshiwe.

Ama-TAURINOS ayazwela kakhulu, intukuthelo kuma-TAURINOS ikhula kancane kancane futhi ivame ukuqala ngokuqhuma okunamandla kwe-volcanic.

Uhlobo oluqhakazile lwe-TAURUS ngokuvamile lunobugovu kakhulu, luyadlisa, luyaxabana, luthanda izinto ezishisayo, luyathukuthela, luyazidla.

Uhlobo oluphezulu lwe-TAURUS lugcwele UTHANDO, luyawuthanda umculo wakudala, Ukuhlakanipha, lusebenzela isintu ngenjabulo, lihlakaniphe kakhulu, liyazwelana, lithembekile, liqotho ebunganeni, lingumzali omuhle, umama omuhle, umngane omuhle, umfowethu omuhle, isakhamuzi esihle, njll.

UBUNGCWELE OBUMANGALISAYO be-MITRAICO TORO obungaqondwa ngabantu abangajulile balesi sikhathi ESIQINILE sekhulu lamashumi amabili, kamuva buwohloka laba wukukhulekela ITHOLE LEGOLDEN.

Ngemuva kwe-TORO kuza i-ORIÓN enkulu. Umbono we-TAURUS nezifunda zayo eziseduze uyamangalisa ngempela.
