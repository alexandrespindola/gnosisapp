## ISANDULELA

### NGU MNUMZANE GARGHA KUICHINES

Kukhona i-Astrology yesayensi noma yezinombolo edinga izifundo ezibanzi zokuyifunda, ngayo ababhuli bezinkanyezi bazo zonke izikhathi benze izibikezelo ngezenzakalo ezibalulekile. Abantu abakhulu njengoHitler eJalimane nabo basebenzise lolu lwazi, owasebenzisa ababhuli bezinkanyezi abaqeqeshiwe ukuba babaqondise ekuhlaseleni kwabo kwempi.

Thina maGnósticos siyazehlukanisa naleyo ndlela yokufunda, ngoba ngayo umuntu uyithoyizi ngokwezibikezelo, ongenakuzivikela ezimpawu ezimaka izikwele ezihlukene nokudlula kwezinkanyezi, sazi nge-astrology esifundisa ukuthi singazisebenzisa kanjani izinkanyezi futhi kanjalo singazigwema izenzakalo ezingabikezelwa ngabathweswe iziqu ze-Numerical Astrology. Ukuze senze lokhu, sidinga ukushintsha imizimba yenyanga esizalwa nayo, ngemizimba yelanga noma ekhanyayo sisebenzisa impande yalo kanye yobukhona noma imbewu yethu.

Isitha esisivimba ukuthi sifinyelele olwazini olungcwele yi-Satanic Self yethu noma iNhloko yemikhosi ebusa umzimba wethu. Indlela ephelele kakhulu yokuqeda uMgcini woMnyango njengoba bebiza abakweta kwesokunxele yinqubo yokuqalisa, okuthi kithina maGnósticos iqala ngokungena ezindaweni ezingcwele noma amaLumisiales, sisebenzisa izimfundiso eziqondiswe futhi zikhuthazwe yi-Avatar ye-Aquarius "Samael Aun Weor".

Ngesikhathi lapho uJesu oqalisiwe anikeza izimfundiso zakhe kubazinikele bendlela wathi: "Ngingiyindlela, ngiyiqiniso, ngiyimpilo, kwakukhona ngalezo zikhathi isazi semilingo esikhulu okuthiwa uSimoni isazi semilingo, sigcwele amandla nengcebo enkulu, owabonisa abafundi bakhe: "Uma uJesu efika kuyise ngemisebenzi yakhe, mina Simoni, ngizofika futhi kuyise ngemisebenzi yami futhi okwenza kwaba ukulandela indlela engakwesokunxele, ezihlukanisa nothandekayo wakhe, le ngozi ihlale ikhona lapho abazinikele bendlela bekholelwa ukuthi bona ngokwabo bangadlula indlela.

Kulabo abathola lokhu kuhlakanipha okokuqala ngqa, siyakwazisa ukuthi kudingeka izinyanga eziyisithupha zezifundo zokuqala, ukutadisha imisebenzi kaSamael Aun Weor nokusebenzisa ulwazi lwabo, bese uma benikeza ubufakazi bokuthi bafuna ukugcwaliseka ngokujulile, futhi bafuna impilo ephakeme, khona-ke, banikezwa ukungena kumaLumisiales ngenqubo eqeqesha ngokukhethekile.

Lapho ozinikele endleleni elungiselelwa isifundo sokulingwa, uvivinyo lokuqala okumele aludlulise ngolwe-Guardian of the Threshold noma ukubhekana noSathane wakhe okade engumqondisi wethu noMfundisi amakhulu eminyaka. Ngiyakhumbula ukuthi emuva ngonyaka we-1949, umfundi weHierofante yeMihlatshelo Emincane ngaleso sikhathi u-Aun Weor, futhi ngemuva kwezinyanga ezingaphezu kwezine zokuhlanzeka okuphelele wahlushwa ukuhlolwa kukaMgcini ngolunye usuku ngoJulayi 27. Lowo mqalisi wayesendaweni eyodwa, wayesengaphakathi, kodwa wayengazi, lapho enxusa uMgcini woMnyango, akazange alinde, lowo mqalisi wathi waqala wazizwa ebanda ngokufa, isikhathi esasicacile sasiya siba mnyama kakhulu, futhi nokubanda kanye nephunga elibi elamenza waqhaqhazela ngokwesaba, wazizwa efisa ukubaleka, kodwa amandla kaKristu ayesevele eqoqe emzimbeni wakhe ngokuguqulwa kwe-alchemy, i-Arcano A. Z. F., yamnika isibindi sokuhlala kuleyo ndawo engathandeki. Ngokushesha wabona isilwane ngesimo sentshukwini siza kuye, sinoboya ngokuphelele, sinezimpondo ebunzini ezakhanya futhi zakhala lapho zinyakaza, sinekhala nomlomo ofana nomnyuzi, wase esethi: Ngakho ufuna ukungishiya? Ungikhokha kanjalo ngayo yonke into engikusize ngayo? Ungishintsha ngaleyo ndoda ongayazi? Wamphendula egcwele ukwethuka wathi uyamshiya, isilo samgabadela ngenhloso yokuhlasela, umqalisi wasincenga, kodwa akuzange kusize ngalutho lokho kunxusa okubuthakathaka kwendoda ebuthakathaka futhi ethukile, kodwa wakhumbula ukuthi unguChela kaKristu futhi wasincenga egameni likaKristu futhi kanjalo wahlehla kancane, njalo lapho emgabadela, wacela isivikelo kuKristu nakuBafundisi bakhe abathandekayo futhi wathi: awusakwazi ukumelana nami, ngizokunqoba futhi isilo sahoxa sishosholoza izinsongo zakho konke uhlobo. Ukukhathazeka kwalowomfundi kwaba kukhulu, ngenxa yokuthi iHierofante yayihambe ngendlela eya kwelinye idolobha futhi lokhu kwakudinga okungenani izinsuku ezintathu zokungabikho, kodwa lapho ebuya wambuza futhi waphendula: Ngiyakuhalalisela, uphume kahle ovivinyweni lokuqala noMgcini woMnyango, lesi sidalwa ngusathane wakho, obumkhonza futhi wamondla amakhulu eminyaka, futhi ngimondla kanjani?, Kubuza umfundi owethukile futhi iNkosi yaphendula, zondla ngezinkanuko zakho eziphansi, zondla ngezifiso zethu eziphansi, izinkanuko ezinesihluku, ubufebe, ukuphinga, izindlu zezifebe nempilo engcolile, konke lokhu kwakha abathengisi bethempeli uKristu asikhulume ngabo, bathengisa ngethempeli lethu, manje kufanele ukhiphe bonke labo bathengisi abakugqilaze kuSathane ngesiswebhu sentando yakho, manje kufanele ubanqobe ngamunye ngamunye uma ufuna ngempela ukuzikhulula ebubini futhi uthathe indlela emhlophe.

Ngomsebenzi lo sizokwazi ukuvakashela bonke abahlakaniphile bezinkanyezi, sibe khona ethempelini lenhliziyo yezinkanyezi, sicela futhi sisebenze nezingelosi zezinkanyezi, ukuze singabi amathoyizi ezimo, kodwa okokuqala kufanele sixoshe abathengisi ethempelini lethu, kufanele sifunde ukukusebenzisa eAltare lethu. Ukuze senze lokhu, ozinikele uya njalo emikhosini yamaLumisiales; lapho efunda ukuthanda nokukhonza uNkulunkulu ngaphezu kwazo zonke izinto nomakhelwane, ujwayelana nalezo zenzo futhi kamuva uyaqonda ukuthi lonke ulwazi lomkhosi wokukhonza lunobudlelwano obuseduze ne-altare eliphilayo futhi uthola izimangaliso ezingavunywa. AmaJACHIN noBOAZ ama-altare ayadingeka ukuphatha i-altare lakhe futhi kufika isikhathi lapho efunda khona ukunikeza izinyathelo eziyisikhombisa ezidingekayo ukusebenza ngokuzithandela e-altare likaNkulunkulu ophilayo naphambi konkulunkulukazi obusisiwe.

Ngemfundiso yovuko sifunda ukubulala imbuzi esiyiphatha ngaphakathi futhi ngaleyo ndlela ekuhambeni kwesikhathi sizokwakha umhlambi weMvana yePhasika. Ngakho siyazikhulula eNkosini Yesikhathi ukuze siphile impilo engenamkhawulo egcwele injabulo yaphakade.
