## KUSUKELA NGOMHLA KA-20 FEBHUWARI KUYA KUMHLA KA-21 MASHI

Sifikile EBUSUKU-BOMAMA beCosmology yaseGibhithe, ulwandle olujulile lweZinhlanzi (PISCIS), ubumnyama OBUNGENAMKHAWULO obuqalisayo bomkhathi OYINQAYIZIVE olungile; into yokuqala yomgodi lapho ama-ondinas egcina khona IGOLIDE leRHIN noma UMLILO WOKUCABANGA OKUNGUVELAPHI NOBUQAMBI.

IZINHLAZI (PISCIS) zifanekiselwa ngokuhlakanipha yIZINHLAZI ezimbili; inhlanzi, iNhlanzi, yiSOMA YEZIMFihlakalo ZIKA-ISIS. INHLANZI IWUMFANEKISO OPHILAYO WOBBUKRESTU BE-GNOSTIC BOKUQALA.

IZINHLAZI EZIMBILI ze-PISCIS ezihlanganiswe nge-hyphen zinenjongo EJULILE ye-GNOSTIC, zimele imiPHEFUMULO emibili yama-ELOHIM ENDULO egxile phakathi kwamanzi ajulile OBUSUKU-BOMAMA.

Sesike sachaza, ezahlukweni ezedlule ukuthi uMUNTU ONGAPHAKATHI, UMSUKA, i-ATMAN, unemiPHEFUMULO emibili: owesifazane, owesilisa.

Sesike sachaza ukuthi imiPHEFUMULO EBUKRISTU, i-BUDDHI, ingeyesifazane. Sesike sasho futhi siyaphinda ukuthi, umphefumulo womuntu, i-MANAS ephezulu, ingowesilisa.

Umbhangqwana ongcwele, UMUSHADO Ongcwele ONGAPHELI, uhlala ufanekiselwa yizinhlanzi ezimbili ezihlanganiswe yi-hyphen; le yokugcina i-SER ATMAN.

Umbhangqwana ongcwele, iZINHLAZI ezimbili zaphakade, zisebenza phakathi kwamanzi omgodi lapho kufika ukusa kwe-MAHANVANTARA.

IZINHLAZI ezimbili ezingachazeki zisebenza ngaphansi kokuqondisa kwe-ATMAN, lapho kufika isikhathi sokusa kwendalo.

Nokho, kuhle ukukhumbula ukuthi u-ISIS no-OSIRIS babengeke bakwazi ukusebenza emSEBENZINI OMKHULU, ngaphandle kwe-MERCURY edumile YEFILOSOFI ENGEYONA EYEMVELO. Kule MERCURY YEZOCANSI kutholakala isihluthulelo sawo wonke amandla.

Isiyingi esinomugqa oqondile oweluliwe siwumfanekiso wobungcwele obukhulu BOMSESFAZANE ONGAPHAKADE noMUSA ONGAPHELI; ukuhlanganiswa kwezinto eziphambene kuMONAD EBALULEKILE, engenakuchazeka futhi engcwele.

Kusuka KUKAMAMA Omkhulu-INDAWO kuvela i-Monad, i-SER. Phakathi NOLWANDLE Olukhulu kuphakama ama-ELOHIM ukuzosebenza ekusaseni le-MAHANVANTARA.

AMANZI ayinto yesifazane yakho konke okudaliwe, lapho kuvela khona i-MATER LATINA, kanye nohlamvu M, OLUYINGOZI ngokwesabekayo.

EBUKRESTWENI BE-GNOSTIC, uMariya uyi-ISIS ngokwakhe, UMAMA WE-COSMOS, UMAMA-INDAWO waphakade, amanzi ajulile omgodi.

Igama elithi MARÍA lihlukaniswe ngezilimi ezimbili; eyesibili i-MAR, esikhumbuza ulwandle olujulile lwe-PISCIS. Owesibili uyi-ÍA, okuyinguqulo ye-IO (iiioooo), igama elihloniphekile LIKAMAMA-INDAWO, umbuthano WALUTHO, lapho yonke into iphuma khona nalapho yonke into ibuyela khona; uMUNYE, uMUNYE OYEDWA wendawo yonke ebonakalisiwe, ngemva kobusuku bePralaya enkulu noma ukuqedwa.

Amanzi aphezulu ahlukanisiwe nawaphansi, kwaba khona ukukhanya, okungukuthi, kwavuka ekuphileni iZWI eliphefumulayo leCOSMOS, INDODANA, futhi lokhu kuphila kwathatha njengento edluliselayo iLanga, elitholakala phakathi nendawo yohlelo lwethu lwelanga, njengenhliziyo ngaphakathi komzimba wethu.

Ukudlidliza okuvundile kweLanga empeleni kuwUMLILO OPHILAYO Oyisisekelo ocindezelwe phakathi nendawo yeplanethi ngayinye, wakha inhliziyo yalowo nalowo.

Konke lokho KUKHANYA, konke lokho KUPHILA, kumelelwe yimimoya eyisikhombisa phambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi, ngaphakathi kwethempeli-Inhliziyo yeplanethi ngayinye eyisikhombisa yohlelo Lwelanga.

Umsebenzi wokuhlukanisa amanzi kumanzi, uhambisana nombhangqwana ongcwele. Ngamunye WEMIMOYA EYISIKHOMBISA phambi kwesihlalo sobukhosi, waphuma KUYE, kumbhangqwana ongcwele WEZINHLAZI ukuzosebenza ekusaseni lendalo ngamandla e-KRIYASAKTY, amandla ezwi elilahlekile, amandla ENTANDO noYOGA.

UTHANDO lweZOTHANDO, UKUTHANDA OKUYIMFIHLO komlilo wokugcina phakathi KOMYENI ONGAPHAKADE noMFazi ONGCWELE, kubalulekile ekuhlukaniseni AMANZI APHEZULU NAMANZI ANGAPHANSI.

Kulo MSEBENZI kukhona i-MAITHUNA TRANSCENDENTAL; I-KRIYASAKTY, IZWI ELIDALAYO.

Uletha UMLILO futhi uguqula amanzi ehlukanisa APHEZULU KWA-ANGAPHANSI.

IZinhlanzi ezimbili zibe sezikhipha lowo mlilo nalowo manzi aphezulu aguquliwe phezu kwamanzi e-CAOS phezu kwendaba ye-cosmic noma yezinto ezibonakalayo yamazwe, phezu kwemisebe yobukhona obulele futhi kuvela impilo.

Wonke umsebenzi wenziwa ngosizo LWEZWI NEZINTANDO noYOGA.

Ekuqaleni, INDAWO YONKE iyacashile, kamuva icindezela ngokwezinto ezibonakalayo, idlula ezikhathini ezilandelanayo ze-CRYSTALLIZATION eqhubekayo.

Kukhona izigidi ZEZINDAWO ZONKE endaweni engenamkhawulo, phakathi kwesifuba SIKAMAMA-INDAWO.

Ezinye IZINDAWO ZONKE ziyaphuma ku-PRALAYA, ziphuma emanzini ajulile e-PISCIS, ezinye zisebenza ngokugcwele, ezinye ziyachitheka phakathi kwamanzi aphakade.

Akukho lutho abangakwenza u-ISIS no-OSIRIS ngaphandle kwe-MERCURY YEZOCANSI, izinhlanzi ezimbili zaphakade, ziyathandana, ziyakhulekela futhi zihlala zidala futhi ziphinde zibuye zizodala.

Inhlanzi iyisibonakaliso esingcwele kunazo zonke se-GNOSTICISM YOBUKRESTU BOKUQALA. Kuyadabukisa ukuthi izinkulungwane zabafundi bokufihla ziye zakhohlwa i-GNOSIS yenhlanzi.

Eplanethini lethu Umhlaba kuhlala iziNTSHAYELWA eziyisikhombisa zinemizimba ebonakalayo futhi kuzo zonke eziyisikhombisa, eyokugcina yithi, okuwukuphela kwayo okwehlulekile ngoba ilahlekelwe i-GNOSIS.

Ezinye iziNTSHAYELWA eziyisithupha zihlala esimweni se-JINAS, KWE-DIMENSION YESINE, kakade ngaphakathi KOBUKHONA BOMHLABA, kakade ezifundeni eziningi nasezifundeni ze-JINAS.

IMINYAKA ye-PISCIS kwakungamele ngabe iyihlazo njengoba yayiyinjalo ngempela. Imbangela ye-causorum yokwehluleka kwe-PISCIAN kwakubangelwa izinto ezithile ezimnyama ezakhetha i-GNOSIS futhi zashumayela IZIMFUNDISO ezithile ze-AGNOSTIC noma eZIYALUKA i-GNOSIS, zibukela phansi i-FISH, zilahla INKOLO YOKUHLAKANIPHA futhi zicwila isintu ekuthandeni izinto ezibonakalayo.

Masikhumbule u-LUCIO efika edolobheni lase-HYPATIA, abese ehlala endlini ka-MILON, umkakhe okuthiwa u-PÁNFILA ungumthakathi omubi. U-LUCIO uphuma ethubeni lokuthenga inhlanzi (I-ICTUS, UMFANEKISO WOBUKRESTU BE-GNOSTIC OBUQALA, INHLANZI, INHLANZI, SOMA, YEZIMFihlakalo ZIKA-ISIS).

Abadobi bayamthengisela ngama-denarii angamashumi amabili alusizi futhi ngendelelo ethusayo, abebefuna ukuyithengisa ngama-escudos ayikhulu ngaphambili, okuyisilinganiso esibi lapho kugoqwe khona indelelo enkulu nge-GNOSTIC YOBUKRESTU EYIQALASEYO.

Umphumela we-AGNOSTIC noma i-ANTI-GNOSTIC YobuKristu, kwaba yi-DIALECTIC MATERIALIST MARXIST.

IMPENDULO emelene ne-GNOSTICISM kwakuyizinto ezibonakalayo ezinengekayo ezingenayo INKULUNKULU futhi engenamthetho.

Kungaqinisekiswa ukuthi iminyaka ye-PISCIS yehlulekile ngenxa ye-AGNOSTICISM. Ukukhaphela i-GNOSIS kwakuyicala elibi kakhulu leminyaka ye-PISCIS.

UJESU Kristu nabadobi bakhe abayishumi nambili, BAQALA inkathi okungase kube yinkathi yezibani ezinkulu.

UJESU nabahambi bakhe ABAYISHUMI NAMBILI ze-GNOSTIC bakhombisa indlela eqondile yezikhathi ze-PISCIS, i-GNOSTICISM, ukuhlakanipha kwe-FISH.

Kuyadabukisa ukuthi zonke izincwadi ezingcwele ze-SANTA GNOSIS bezishisiwe futhi ukuthi umfuziselo ongcwele wenyoni wawukhohliwe.

PRACTICE. Ngesikhathi sesibonakaliso se-PISCIS kufanele uvokalise ihora elilodwa nsuku zonke. Asikhumbule ukuthi ekuqaleni kwakukhona iZWI nokuthi iZWI lalinoNkulunkulu nokuthi iZWI lalinguNkulunkulu.

Abantu bomdabu base-Piscis ngokuvamile banabafazi ababili, izingane ezimbalwa. Bangabezesimo esiphindwe kabili futhi banesimo semisebenzi emibili noma imisebenzi yezandla. Abantu bomdabu base-Piscis kunzima kakhulu ukubaqonda, baphila njengenhlanzi, kukho konke, kodwa bahlukanisiwe kukho konke ngento ewuketshezi. Bazivumelanisa nakho konke, kodwa ngokujulile bayazidelela zonke izinto zomhlaba. Bazwela ngokungenakuqhathaniswa, banobuhlakani, bajulile futhi abantu abanakubaziqonda.

Abantu bomdabu base-Piscis banesimo esihle sokufihla, ngenxa yokuthi i-Piscis ilawulwa yi-NEPTUNE, iplanethi ye-ESOTERISM.

Abesifazane be-PISCIS banemizwa kakhulu, bazwela njengembali ecwile kakhulu; banobuhlakani, bayathinteka.

Ingozi yamaPiscian iwukuwela ebuvileni, ekungakunaki, ekupheleni kwamandla nasekungenandabala ngokuphila.

AmaPiscian angaze afinyelele ekuntuleni isibopho sokuziphatha. Ingqondo yamaPiscian iyashintshashintsha phakathi kokuqonda okusheshayo noma okuyingozi, ubuvila nendelelo ngezinto ezidingeka kakhulu ekuphileni. Zingamagama amabili aqatha futhi ngokushesha nje lapho ziwela kokunye okwedlulele njengakwelinye. Intando yamaPiscian ngezinye izikhathi iqinile, kodwa iyashintsha kwezinye izikhathi.

Lapho amaPiscian ewela ekungazwani nasekupheleni kwamandla aphezulu, evumela ukuba athathwe umfula wokuphila, kodwa lapho esebona ubukhulu bokuziphatha kwawo, asebenzisa intando yawo yensimbi futhi abe eseshintsha ngokuphelele inkambo yobukhona bawo.

AmaPiscian ohlobo oluphezulu yi-GNOSTIC ngekhulu lamaphesenti, anentando yensimbi engagwegwezi futhi anomqondo ophakeme kakhulu wesibopho sokuziphatha.

Uhlobo oluphakeme lwe-PISCIS lunikeza ABANTU ABAKHANYISIWE, AMAKHOSI, AMA-AVATARAS, AMAMKHULU, ABANTU ABAQALISAYO, njll., njll.

Uhlobo olungezansi lwe-PISCIS lunokuthambekela okuphawulekayo EKUNJABULENI, ekudakisweni utshwala, ekuminzeni, ebuvileni, ekuziqhenyeni.

AmaPiscian athanda uhambo, kodwa akubona bonke abangahamba. AmaPiscian anomcabango omkhulu kakhulu kanye nokuzwela okukhulu.

Kunzima kakhulu ukuqonda ama-PISCIAN, ama-PISCIAN kuphela angawaqonda ama-Piscian.

Konke lokhu okubaluleke kakhulu kubantu abavamile, akubalulekile ngalutho kumaPiscian, kodwa anobuchule, azivumelanisa nabantu, abonakala evumelana nabo.

Okuyingozi kakhulu kubantu bomdabu base-Piscis, ukuthi kufanele bazichaze ngokucacile endabeni yomshado, njengoba cishe njalo izithando ezimbili eziyisisekelo, zibenza bangene emigwaqweni engenayo indlela.
