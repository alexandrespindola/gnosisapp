## 23 SEPTEMBER KUYA 23 OCTOBER

Ingqondo esixegaxeni yaseNtshonalanga, ngokwakha i-DOGMA ENGAGUQULUKI yokuziphendukela kwemvelo, yazikhohlwa ngokuphelele izinqubo zokubhujiswa kweMvelo. Kuyaxaka ukuthi ingqondo esonakele ayikwazi ukucabanga inqubo ephambene, YOKUHLANGANISA, NGESIKALI ESINKULU.

Ingqondo ekulesi simo sokuxega idida ukuwa nokwehla futhi inqubo yokubhujiswa, ukuchithwa ngesilinganiso esikhulu, ukonakala, njll., ikufanele njengoshintsho, inqubekela phambili, UKUZIPHENDUKELA KWEMVELO.

Konke KUYAZIPHENDULELA FUTHI KUYAHLANGANISA, kuyakhuphuka futhi kwehle, kuyakhula futhi kuyancipha, kuya futhi kuza, kuyageleza futhi kubuyele emuva; kukho konke kukhona i-systole ne-diastole, ngokulandela uMthetho wendololwane.

UKUZIPHENDUKELA KWEMVELO nodadewabo oyisihlobo UKUHLANGANISA, yimiThetho emibili ethuthuka futhi isebenze ngokuhambisana nangokuvumelana kukho konke okudaliwe.

UKUZIPHENDUKELA KWEMVELO kanye NOKUHLANGANISA kwakha i-axis yemishini yemvelo.

Isigaba sokugcina SOKUHLANGANISWA KWEZIZWE YISIMO SESIFOSILI, ngemva kwalokho kuza UKUQEDWA kwalabo abalahlekile.

Okuwukuphela kwento ESINDISAYO kuyo yonke leyo nhlekelele, okuwukuphela kwento ENGENZIKEKI yilona QINISO, i-BUDHATA, LEYO NGXENYE YOMOYA WOMUNTU umpofu I-ANIMAL INTELECTUAL ephethe ngaphakathi KWEMIZIMBA YAYO YENYANGA.

UKUHLANGANISWA E-MUNDO-INFIERNOS kuhlose ngokuqondile ukukhulula i-BUDHATA, UMOYA WOMUNTU, ukuze kusukela ekuhlanganisweni kwasekuqaleni kuqale kabusha ukukhuphuka kwayo OKUPHENDUKILE ngezikali zamaminerali, zezitshalo, zezilwane, kuze kufinyelele ezingeni le-ANIMAL INTELECTUAL okungelona iqiniso elibizwa ngeNDODA.
