## 22 KUSUKAMAYESI KUYA KUMHLAZI WAMASHUMI AMABILI NAMBILI KUHLANGOTHI LUKAHLANGA

Ukuthi UZIBONE nokuthatheka kuholela EPHUPHENI LOKWAZI. Isibonelo: Nihamba ngokuthula emgwaqweni; ngokushesha nihlangabezana nombhikisho womphakathi; izixuku ziyakhuluma, abaholi bomphakathi bayakhuluma, amafulegi ayavevezela emoyeni, abantu babukeka sengathi bayahlanya, wonke umuntu uyakhuluma, wonke umuntu uyamemeza.

LOWO MBHIKISHO WOMPHAKATHI uthakazelisa kakhulu; seniyikhohliwe yonke into obekumele niyenze, nizihlanganisa nezixuku, amazwi ezikhulumi ayaniqinisekisa.

UMBHIQIZO WOMPHAKATHI uthakazelisa kangangokuthi senikhohliwe NINA NINODWA, senizihlanganise kakhulu NALOWO MBHIKISHO WASESITALADINI, ukuthi anisacabangi ngelinye, nithathekile, manje niwela ephusheni lokuqonda; nixinyelele nezixuku ezimemezayo, nani niyamemeza ngisho niphonsa amatshe nokuthuka; niphupha kamnandi, anisazi nokuthi ningobani, seniyikhohliwe yonke into.

Manje asibeke esinye isibonelo esilula: Nisesendlini yenu nihlezi phambi kwethelevishini, kuvela izigcawu zama-cowboy, kunokudubulana, amadrama abathandanayo, njalonjalo.

I-movie ithakazelisa kakhulu, idonse ukunaka kwenu ngokuphelele, senizikhohliwe NINA NINODWA, ukuthi ngisho niyamemeza nithokozile, senizihlanganise nama-cowboy, nokudubulana, nembhangqwana yezithandani.

Ukuthakazeleka kuyesabeka manje, ngisho nakude anisakhumbuli nina, seningene ephusheni elijulile, ngalezo zikhathi nifuna ukubona ukunqoba kweqhawe le-movie, ngalezo zikhathi nifuna inhlanhla engase imhlele.

Kuyizinkulungwane nezigidi zezimo ezenza UKUZIBONA, UKUTHATHEKA, IPHUPHO. Abantu BAZIBONA nabantu, imibono futhi konke uhlobo LOKUZIBONA lilandelwa UKUTHATHEKA nephupho.

Abantu baphila ngokuqonda okulele, basebenza bephupha, bashayela izimoto bephupha futhi babulala abahamba ngezinyawo abahamba bephupha emigwaqweni, bedelelwe emicabangweni yabo.

Ngesikhathi sokuthula komzimba, i-EGO (MINA) iyaphuma EMZIMBENI WOMSINDO iphathe amaphupho ayo noma ngabe iya kuphi. Lapho ibuyela emzimbeni, lapho ingena esimweni SOKUPHAPHAMELA, iqhubeka namaphupho ayo afanayo futhi ichitha impilo yayo yonke iphupha.

Abantu abafayo bayayeka ukuba khona, kodwa i-EGO, MINA, iyaqhubeka ezifundeni ZESENSIBLE NGAPHEZU kokufa. Ngehora lokufa i-EGO iphatha amaphupho ayo, ubunono bayo futhi iphile emhlabeni wabafileyo ngamaphupho ayo, iyaqhubeka iphupha, ngokuqonda okulele, iyazula njengompetha, ilele, ingazi.

Noma ngubani ofuna UKUPHAPHAMELA UKUQONDA kufanele akusebenzele lapha namanje. Sinokuqonda OKUHLANGANISIWE yingakho kufanele SIKUSEBENZELE lapha namanje. Noma ngubani OKUPHAPHAMELA UKUQONDA lapha kulo mhlaba uyaphaphama kuyo yonke imihlaba.

Noma ngubani OKUPHAPHAMELA UKUQONDA kulo mhlaba OYISITHATHU, uyaphaphama kuyo INE, ESIHLANU, ESIYISITHUPHA kanye neyesikhombisa.

Noma ngubani ofuna ukuphila NGOKUPHAPHAMELA emihlabeni EPHEZULU, kufanele APPHAPHEME lapha namanje.

AmaVangeli amane agcizelela isidingo SOKUPHAPHAMELA, kodwa abantu abaqondi.

Abantu balala kakhulu, kodwa bakholelwa ukuthi bayaphaphama, lapho umuntu amukela ukuthi ulele, kuwuphawu olucacile ukuthi usezoqala ukuphaphama.

Kunzima kakhulu ukwenza abanye abantu baqonde ukuthi benokuqonda okulele, abantu abamukeli neze iqiniso elibi kakhulu lokuthi balele.

Noma ngubani ofuna UKUPHAPHAMELA UKUQONDA kufanele azijwayeze okwamanje EKUKHUMBULENI NGOKUJULILE KOKUKHUMBULA YENA UQOBO.

Lokhu kuzikhumbula nina qobo okwamanje, empeleni kuwumsebenzi onzima.

Kwanele umzuzwana, umzuzwana wokukhohlwa ukuze uqale ukuphupha kamnandi.

Sidinga NGOKUPHUTHUMAYO ukuqapha yonke imicabango yethu, imizwa, izifiso, imizwelo, imikhuba, izifiso zemvelo, amandla ocansi, njalonjalo.

Yonke imicabango, yonke imizwelo, yonke iminyakazo, yonke isenzo semvelo yonke amandla ocansi, kufanele zizibukwe ngokushesha njengoba zivela EMQONDWENI wethu; noma yikuphi ukunganaki ekunakekeleni, kwanele ukuwela ephusheni LOKUQONDA.

Ngezikhathi eziningi nihamba emgwaqweni nizidelile emicabangweni yenu, nizinikele kulolo cabango, nithathekile, niphupha kamnandi; ngokushesha kudlula umngane eceleni kwenu, uyanibingelela, aniphenduli ukumbingelela ngoba animboni, niyaphupha; umngane uyathukuthela, ucabanga ukuthi aninayo imfundo noma kungenzeka ukuthi nithukuthele, nomngane uyaphupha, ukube uyaphaphama ubungeke uzenzele lokhu kuqagela, ubengabona ngokushesha ukuthi nilele.

Izikhathi eziningi eniphambuka ngazo emnyango nishayele lapho bekungamele nishayele khona, ngoba nilele.

Nigibela imoto yezokuthutha yedolobha, kufanele nihle ngomgwaqo othize, kodwa nizinikele, nithathekile, niphupha kamnandi ngebhizinisi engqondweni yenu, noma ngenkumbulo, noma ngothando, ngokushesha nithola ukuthi senidlule umgwaqo, nenza imoto ime bese nibuyela emuva ngezinyawo imigwaqo embalwa.

Kunzima kakhulu ukuhlala uphapheme okwamanje, kodwa KUDINGEKA.

Lapho sifunda ukuphila siphapheme okwamanje, khona-ke siyayeka ukuphupha lapha nangaphandle komzimba.

Kuyadingeka ukwazi ukuthi abantu lapho belele baphuma emizimbeni yabo, kodwa bahamba namaphupho abo, baphila emihlabeni yangaphakathi bephupha futhi lapho bebuyela emzimbeni, bayaqhubeka namaphupho abo, bayaqhubeka bephupha.

Lapho umuntu efunda ukuphila EPHEPHILE okwamanje, uyeka ukuphupha lapha nasemhlabeni yangaphakathi.

Kuyadingeka ukwazi ukuthi i-EGO (MINA) isongwe EMZIMBENI WENYANGA, iYAPHUMA EMZIMBENI WOMSINDO lapho umzimba ulele, ngeshwa i-EGO iphila ilele emihlabeni YANGAPHAKATHI.

Ngaphakathi EMZIMBENI WENYANGA kukhona ngaphezu kwe-EGO, lokho okubizwa ngokuthi UKUPHILA, UMPHEFUMULO, IQHEZU LOMPHEFUMULO, i-BUDHATA, UKUQONDA. YILOLO LOKUQONDA okumele sikuvuse lapha namanje.

Lapha kulo mhlaba sinokuqonda, lapha kufanele SIKUPHAPHAMELISE, uma ngabe sifuna ngempela ukuyeka ukuphupha futhi siphile siphapheme emhlabeni ophezulu.

UMUNTU onokuqonda okuphapheme ngenkathi umzimba wakhe uphumule embhedeni wakhe, uyaphila, uyasebenza, usebenza ngokuqaphela EMINHLABENI EPHEZULU.

Umuntu OQAPHELAYO akanazinkinga ZOKUPHINDA, inkinga yokufunda UKUPHINDA ngokuthanda kungokwabaleyo abALELEYO kuphela.

Umuntu OPHEPHILE akakhathazeki nokufunda ukuziphinda, uphila ngokuqaphela EMINHLABENI EPHEZULU, ngenkathi umzimba wakhe ulele embhedeni.

Umuntu ophapheme akasaphathi, ngesikhathi sokuphumula komzimba uphila kulezo zifunda lapho abantu bahamba bephupha khona, kodwa NGOKUQONDA OKUPHAPHEME.

UMUNTU OPHEPHILE uxhumana neLOGIA EMHLOPHE, uvakashela AMA THEMPELI wobaQATHI bomhlaba wonke OMHLOPHE, uxoxa neGURU-DEVA yakhe, ngenkathi umzimba ulele.

Ukukhumbula OKUJULILE YENA UQOBO okwamanje, kuthuthukisa umuzwa WENDAWO futhi singabona ngisho namaphupho abantu abahamba emigwaqweni.

UMUZWA WENDAWO uhlanganisa KUWO UQOBO, ukubona, ukuzwa, ukuhogela, ukunambitha, ukuthinta, njalonjalo. UMUZWA WENDAWO UYIKHO UKUQONDA OKUPHEPHILE.

Ama-CHAKRAS, okukhulunywa ngawo kuncwadi eliyimfihlo, maqondana nomuzwa wendawo, yini ilangabi lokuqanda, maqondana neLanga.

Noma ukukhumbula OKUJULILE YENA UQOBO okwamanje, kubalulekile UKUPHAPHAMELA UKUQONDA, akubalulekile kangako ukufunda ukusingatha UKUNAKEKELA.

Abafundi be-GNÓSTIC kufanele bafunde ukwahlukanisa UKUNAKEKELA ngezingxenye ezintathu: ISENZO, I-OBJECT, INDAWO.

ISENZO. Ungangeni ekukhohlweni YENA UQOBO kunoma yikuphi ukubonakala.

INHLOSO. Qaphela ngokuningiliziwe yonke into, konke okubonwayo, lonke iqiniso, sonke isenzakalo noma ngabe sibonakala sibalulekile kangakanani, ngaphandle KOKUKHOHLWA YENA UQOBO.

INDAWO. Ukunakekela okuqinile kwendawo lapho sizithola sikhona, sizibuze NINA QOBO: Yiliphi leli zindawo? Kungani ngilapha?

Ngaphakathi kwale ndawo ye-FACTOR, kufanele sifake udaba LWEMIBALA, ngoba kungenzeka ukuthi sizithola sisesithathwini noma kwesesihlanu SOMBALA wemvelo ngesikhathi SOKUNAKEKELA; masihlale sikhumbula ukuthi imvelo inemibala eyisikhombisa.

Ngaphakathi KOMHLABA OKUYISITHATHU kulawula umthetho wamandla adonsela phansi. Ngaphakathi KWEMIBALA EPHEZULU yemvelo, kukhona Umthetho WOKUQHAQHAMUKA.

Lapho sibuka indawo, akufanele neze sikukhohlwe udaba lwemibala eyisikhombisa yemvelo; kufanelekile ukuzibuza NINA QOBO: Ngikuphi ISIMBALA?, bese kuyadingeka, njengendlela yokuqinisekisa, ukunikeza igxathu elide ngangokunokwenzeka ngenhloso yokuntanta endaweni ezungezile. Kunengqondo ukuthi uma sinyakaza kungenxa yokuthi sizithola singaphandle KOMZIMBA WOMSINDO. Akufanele neze sikhohlwe ukuthi lapho umzimba womsindo ulele, i-EGO EMZIMBENI WENYAKA kanye ne-ESSENCE ngaphakathi, iyazula ingazi njengomphathi EMHLABENI WOMGOMO.

UKUHLAUKANISWA KOKUNAKEKELA phakathi KUKA-SUBJECT, OBJECT, PLACE, kuholela EKUHLANGANISWENI kokuqonda.

Abafundi abaningi be-GNÓSTIC ngemuva kokujwayela lokhu kuzilolonga, lokhu kuhlukaniswa KOKUNAKEKELA ngezingxenye ezintathu, kule mibuzo, kuleli gxathu, njalonjalo, ngesikhathi sokuthula, okwamanje, bagcina beziqhuba ngesikhathi sokulala komzimba womsindo, lapho bebese bemi ngempela emihlabeni ephezulu futhi benikeza igxathu elidumile lokuhlola, bayaqabula kamnandi endaweni ezungezile; khona-ke baphaphama BENOKUQONDA, khona-ke bakhumbula ukuthi umzimba womsindo usulele embhedeni futhi wagcwala injabulo wakwazi ukuzinikela ekutadisheni IZIMFIHLO zokuphila nokufa, KWIMIBALA EPHEZULU.

Kucacile ukusho ukuthi ukuzivocavoca okwenziwa okwamanje nsuku zonke, okuba umkhuba, umkhuba, kubhalwa kakhulu ezindaweni ezahlukahlukene ZENGQONDO, ukuthi ngemuva kokuthi kuphindwe ngokuzenzakalelayo ngesikhathi sokulala, lapho siphuma ngempela emzimbeni futhi umphumela uwUKUPHAPHAMELA kokuqonda.

IGÉMINIS wuphawu lomoya, olawulwa yiPLANETA MERCURIO. IGÉMINIS ilawula amaphaphu, izingalo nemilenze.

PRACTICE. Ngesikhathi se-ZODIACAL SIGN OF GÉMINIS, abafundi be-Gnostic kufanele balale ngomhlane futhi baphumule umzimba. Bese kufanele uhogele umoya izikhathi ezinhlanu bese uyawukhipha ezinye ezinhlanu; lapho uhogela kufanele ucabange ukuthi ukukhanya okwakuke kwabuthelelwa ku-larynx, manje kusebenza kuma-bronchi kanye namaphaphu. Lapho uhogela izingalo nemilenze zizovuleka kwesokudla nakwesokunxele, lapho uhogela izingalo nemilenze zizovala.

Insimbi ye-GÉMINIS yi-MERCURIO, itshe yi-BERILO ORO, umbala OPHUZI.

Abantu bomdabu base-GÉMINIS bayathanda kakhulu ukuhamba, benza iphutha lokudelela izwi elihlakaniphile lenhliziyo, bafuna ukuxazulula yonke into ngengqondo, bathukuthela kalula, banamandla amakhulu, bayahlukahluka, bayashintshashintsha, bayacasuka, bahlakaniphile, izimpilo zabo zigcwele impumelelo nokuhluleka, banenani lobuhlanya.

Abantu bomdabu baseGemini banenkinga nge-DUALISMO yabo engavamile, ngalobu BUBU BOMBILI obubakhombisayo futhi obuvezwe kumaGrikhi ngalabo bazalwane ABAZIJULELA ababizwa ngeCASTOR noPÓLUX.

Umdabu wase-GÉMINIS akasoze azi ukuthi uzoya kanjani kuleli cala noma lelo cala, ngenxa yobuBUBI BOMBILI.

Noma yisiphi isikhathi esinqunyiwe umdabu wase-GÉMINIS uba ngumngane oqotho kakhulu, okwazi ukudela ngisho nokuphila kwakhe siqu ngobungane, kumuntu amnike uthando lwakhe, kodwa nganoma yisiphi esinye isikhathi, uyakwazi ukwenza amahlazo amabi kakhulu kulowo muntu othandekayo.

Uhlobo oluphansi lwe-GÉMINIS luyingozi kakhulu yingakho ubungani balo bungathandeki.

Iphutha elibi kakhulu labantu bomdabu base-GÉMINIS, yisimo sokwahlulela ngamanga bonke abantu.

Amawele i-CASTOR ne-PÓLUX asimema ekuzindleni. Kwaziwa ukuthi emvelweni indaba ebonakalisiwe namandla afihlekile amelelwe ekushiseni, ekukhanyeni, kugesi, emandleni amakhemikhali kanye namanye aphezulu asengakaziwa kithi, ahlala ecutshungulwa ngendlela ephambene futhi ukubonakala kwelinye kuhlala kubangela i-ENTROPY noma UKUNYAMALALA kwelinye, kungekho okunye noma okuncane kunalokho ABAZALWANE ABAZIJULELA I-CASTOR ne-PÓLUX, uphawu lalesi simo kumaGrikhi. Babephila futhi befa ngokushintshana njengoba bezalwa futhi befa ngokushintshana, bebonakala futhi benyamalala, lapho indaba namandla.

Inqubo ye-GÉMINIS ibalulekile ku-COSMOGÉNESIS. Umhlaba wokuqala kwakuyilanga elacwiliswa kancane kancane ngezindleko zendandatho elufiphele, kuze kube yisimo esidabukisayo sesiliva esifihliwe, lapho kunqunywa khona ngemisebe noma ngokupholisa ifilimu yokuqala eqinile yeglobhu lethu ngenqubo yamakhemikhali yokusakazeka noma i-ENTROPY yamandla enza izimo ezinzima zendaba esiyibiza ngokuthi iziqinile neziwuketshezi.

Zonke lezi zinguquko emvelweni zenziwa ngokuhambisana nezinqubo ezisondele kakhulu ze-CASTOR ne-PÓLUX.

Ngezikhathi zale khulu leminyaka LAMASHUMI AMABILI NAMBILI, impilo isiqale ukubuyela KOKUPHELELE futhi indaba ewubukhazikhazi iqala ukuguquka ibe AMANDLA. Kutshelwe thina ukuthi ku-QUINTA RONDA UMHLABA uzoba yisidumbu, iNYANGA entsha nokuthi impilo izothuthuka nazo zonke izinqubo zayo zokwakha nokucekela phansi, ngaphakathi komhlaba we-ether.

Ngokombono we-ESOTÉRICO singaqinisekisa ukuthi i-CASTOR ne-PÓLUX yimiphefumulo eyizimpande.

ISIKHATHI, OKUQHATHA kithi ngamunye, inemiqondo emibili EYINGANE, EYOMoya, neYobuntu.

Esilwaneni Esihlakaniphile esivamile futhi esivamile, i-SER, i-ÍNTIMO, AYIZALI futhi ayifi, futhi ayi-REENCARNA, kodwa yebo ithumela KUMUNTU ngamunye omusha, i-ESSENCE; lokhu kuyingxenye yomphefumulo WOMUNTU; i-BUDHATA.

Kuyaphuthuma ukwazi ukuthi i-BUDHATA, i-ESSENCE, ifakwa ngaphakathi EMZIMBENI WENYAKA okumbeswa ngayo i-EGO.

Sikhuluma ngendlela ecacile, sizothi i-ESSENCE ngeshwa ifakwe ebhodleleni phakathi kwe-EGO LUNAR. Abangenalutho bayehla.

Ukwehla emhlabeni-ESIHOGWENI, kuhlose kuphela ukucekela phansi IMZIMBA YENYAKA kanye ne-EGO, ngokusebenzisa ukungenwa yimvelo okucwiliselwe. Kuphela ngokucekela phansi ibhodlela, i-ESSENCE iyaphunyuka.

Zonke lezo zinguquko ezingapheli ze-MATERIA in AMANDLA namandla ku-materia, zihlala zisikhuthaza ukuba sizindle nge-GÉMINIS.

IGéminis ihlobene kakhulu nama-bronchi, amaphaphu nokuphefumula. I-MICROCOSMOS-INHLANGANO yenziwe ngomfanekiso nokufana kwe-MACRO-COSMOS.

UMHLABA futhi uyaphefumula. Umhlaba uhogela i-SULPHUR ebalulekile ye-SUN bese uyayikhipha njengoba iguqulwe yaba yi-SULFHUR yasemhlabeni; lokhu kufana nomuntu ohogela umoya-mpilo ohlanzekile bese ewukhipha njengoba uguqulwe waba yi-anhydride carbonic.

Lona wave wokuphila, ngenye indlela okwenyuka nokwehla, iqiniso le-systole ne-diastole, ugqozi nokuphefumula kuvela ekujuleni kwesifuba somhlaba.
