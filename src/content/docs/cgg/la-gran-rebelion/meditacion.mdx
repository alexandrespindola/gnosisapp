Omu bwomeezi, ekikulu niyo empinduka erikukirayo, egitahinduka, neyehweere; ebindi tibirikukirayo.

Okuteekateeka nikikuru munoobu twaba nitwenda empinduka egi.

Titureebire okuteekateeka okutari kukuru, okwomutwe, n'okwefu.

Nitwetaga kugumira kandi tureka ebiniga bingi ebiriyo omu by'obushangwa n'obuhandiiki obutari bwa mazima.

Oshemereire kumanya kugumira, oshemereire kumanya kuhinduka obu orikwenda kutahwa omu murimo gw'obushangwa.

Otagizire okuteekateeka, omu ndengo, otafookire, tarikubaasa kuhweereza Ego; aryahurra nk'omuti omugumu omu nyanja y'obwomeezi.

Enshobi erikushuuruurwa omu by'obwomeezi, eshemereire kwetegyerezebwa munoobu kurabira omu bwingi bw'okuteekateeka.

Ebiragiro by'okuteekateeka biriyo omu bintu ebirikubaaho buri izooba omu bwomeezi, eki kikuru.

Abantu bahakanira aha bintu ebirikutahaza, tibarikumanya omugasho gw'ebintu ebyo.

Ihanga erya Itwe omu mwanya gw'okuhakanira aha bintu ebirikutahaza, tushemereire kurugaho, okurabira omu kuteekateeka, ebintu ebirikutugasira omu kukura kw'emyoyo yaitu.

Okuteekateeka kw'ahaiguru aha mbeera erikushemeza nari erikutahaza, nikutureetera okuhurra omu mibiri yaitu oburyohe, ebiraashe.

Nikikuru kugyeranisiza eby'emyoyo by'ahaagati y'oburyohe bw'omurimo n'oburyohe bw'obwomeezi.

Omu mbeera yoona, okuhurra oburyohe bw'omurimo, nikyetaga ekikuru kyona ky'omulingo omuntu arikutwaara embeera z'obwomeezi.

Tihariho orikubaasa kwegomba oburyohe bw'omurimo obu araarikukora enshobi y'okwegyendesereza n'ebintu ebirikubaaho.

Kandi okwegyendesereza nikuzibira okwetegyereza kw'eby'emyoyo kw'ebintu ebirikubaaho.

Obu omuntu araakwegyendesereze n'ekintu eki nari kiri, omu mbeera yoona tarikubaasa kurugaho ebintu ebirikugasira omu kushuuruura omuntu n'okukura omunda kw'obwengye.

Omukozi w'eby'obushangwa ogu arikugaruka aha kwegyendesereza bwanyima y'okutangirira, nagaruka kuhurra oburyohe bw'obwomeezi omu mwanya gw'oburyohe bw'omurimo.

Eki nikireeta ngu omulingo gw'eby'emyoyo ogwahindurwa, gutwarize omutindo gw'okwegyendesereza.

Embeera yoona erikutahaza eshemereire kwombekerwa butoosha kurabira omu kweshushaniiza okw'obwengye kurabira omu bwingi bw'okuteekateeka.

Okwombeka butoosha omusheenyi nikutureetera okuteeraho itwe bonka kandi omu mulingo ogworobi okujumburwa kw'eby'omubiri eby'okujumbura omumwe.

Eby'okureeberaho: Omusheenyi gw'obushumba bw'okugonza; omumwo nibajumbura eby'omubiri by'obusungu, obushumba, n'okuhinga.

Kwetegyereza buli kimwe aha by'omubiri ebyo, buli kimwe aha bintu ebyo, nikishobora okuteekateeka, obuteekateeka munoobu.

Ebirooto by'okujuma abandi niyo miziro, ekirikuzibira okwetegyereza enshobi zaitu itwe.

Ky'obusaasi kureetera omurimo ogurikukora kuhweereza omu itwe birooto by'okujuma abandi.

Omu iziina ly'amazima tushemereire kugamba ngu itwe niyo twabaasa kuba tumwenye aha mbeera ezirikukirayo omu bwomeezi.

Ebintu ebirikushomesa nari ebirikutahaza biriyo naitwe nari hataliho itwe kandi nibigarukamu obunaku buri izooba omu mulingo ogutari gw'obwengye.

Okurugaho aha mugambo ogu, obuzibu tiburikubaasa kugira omugasho ogw'aha muheru.

Obuzibu buriyo omu bwomeezi kandi obu haraabe hariyo omugasho ogw'aha muheru obwomeezi tiburaabe bwomeezi, kureka kufa.

Hanyuma hariho okuhindura omu mbeera n'obuzibu, kureka tibalekere kugarukamu kandi tibaraagira omugasho ogw'aha muheru.

Obwomeezi n'omupiira ogurikwekyanga omu mulingo ogutari gw'obwengye n'embeera zoona ezirikushomesa n'ezirikuhuurana, obunaku obutahwaaho.

Titurikubaasa kureka omupiira, embeera nungi nari mbi zirikukorwa omu mulingo ogutari gw'obwengye, nitubaasa kuhindura omulingo gwaitu aha bintu by'obwomeezi.

Nk'oku turikwega kushuuruura ebintu by'okuteekateeka omu nganda ezi omu mbeera z'obwomeezi, nitwegye kushuuruura itwe.

Omu mbeera yoona erikushemeza nari erikutahaza hariho eby'omubiri bingi ebishemereire kwetegyerezebwa n'obwingi bw'okuteekateeka.

Eki nikishobora ngu ekibiina kyona ky'eby'omubiri ekirikujumbura omu dilaama, obuseegu nari obusaasi bw'obwomeezi, bwanyima y'okwetegyerezebwa omu bujumu, kirashemereire kuhweereza kurabira omu maani gwa Maria Omutaagwa Kundalini.

Obu turaakukorese omulingo gw'okureeba kw'eby'emyoyo, ogurikuhereza gugenda kukura omu mulingo ogurikushangaaza. Hanyuma nitubaasa kukwatana omunda hatariho kureka eby'omubiri bwanyima y'okukorwa, kureka na hati omu murimo.

Obu eby'omubiri ebi biba bikwatsiibweho kandi bikahweerezebwa, nituhurra obusingye bwingi, okushemererwa kwingi.
