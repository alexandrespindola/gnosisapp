Omu buli omwe nafuura akakwate, emiteekateekyereza erikuhwaara n'engeri y'okuteekateeka, kandi hataroho omuntu orikwigurura amanya, eki nikyo mazima.

Obwongo bw'omuntu otarikukwata aha bintu bukajuma, bwoneekire kandi burikukwata aha bintu.

Buzima okwetegyereza kw'abantu obunaku obu kufaanana ekintu ky'obuhangwa ekiragwire kikuru ekitarikukora, ekitarikubaasa kukora kintu kyona ekiriyo.

Hatarimu bukuru aha bwongo, burikukora omu mateeka maingi agarikukora.

Omuntu weena aine akakwate ke n'amateeka agaagumire agu arikukora nago kandi agarikwakaaga nago obutoosha.

Ekirikukirayo obubi omu nshonga egi yoona n'okuba ngu obukaikuru bw'emiheeko bukwatanisa obukaikuru bw'amateeka agarikuhwaara.

Omuntu weena tatangaaza, omutwe gweena n'ensi kandi hataroho okubaasa ngu omu kabyama k'omutwe kwingi harimu obushobi bw'okuhereza n'obushema obutarikugumirwa.

Kwonka akakwate k'abantu abingi tikarikukwata aha kintu kyona ekirikutaana omu bwongo obu barimu.

Abantu aba ab'omuriinzi abaine obwongo bw'eminya bagamba ngu nibo barungi, bagamba ngu nibo baine omutima murungi, bagamba ngu baine akakwate kakuru.

Abatarimu bwaanzi nibo barikukora, ahakuba buzima, turikugamba omurundi ogu omu muringo gwa Socratic, turikugamba "tibarikumanya, kureka, bagamire ngu tibarikumanya".

Ababira b'obwongo nibagumira aha mateeka agaagumire omu bwire bw'enyima nibaterekereza omu muringo gw'amagara gwabo n'okukwega kutaikiriza ekintu kyona ekirikubaasa kutaana omu mateeka gaabo ag'ebyoma.

Abaziriki abataikirizibwe nibatekateeka ngu ekintu kyona ekirikurugamu omu muhanda murungi gw'ebyabo ebiragwire nikishema omu miringo yoona. N'ahabw'ekyo abantu aba abakundiire akakwate kagumire nibeehendera omu miringo mibi.

Nibegamba ngu nibo baine obwaanzi abaziriki b'omuriinzi, nibareeba n'omugayo abaine omutima gw'okwega omu mateeka gaabo agarikuduka n'obwire, ekirikukirayo obubi n'okuba ngu tibarikukwata aha mazima agaabo.

Obugufu bw'obwongo bw'abantu abariyo nibarikwenda ebintu byona, nibarikugumira aha kuhereza aha kintu ekirikwoleka ebyamazima, aha kintu ekitarimu bwongo.

Tibarikuza kwetegyereza abantu abaine okwetegyereza okutari kukuru n'okutaikiriza ngu okukora ebintu ebyamazima kurikuza omu kuhatiisa kw'ego.

Hataroho okukanganisa omu muringo gwona okubaasa kumanya ebitahurirwa by'obwomeezi n'okufa okuhitsya obu twingurwire omu bwongo obwomunda.

Tikurikukora kubi kugarukamu omu mutwe ogu ngu okwetegyereza kukuru kwa Ruhanga nikwo kurikubaasa kumanya amazima.

Obwongo obwomunda nibubaasa kukora n'ebintu ebirikuza okuruga omu kwetegyereza kwa Ruhanga ow'ensi yoona.

Obwongo oburiyo, n'okugamba kwabo kw'omurungi, hataroho eki barikubaasa kumanya aha kintu ekirikurugamu omu mateeka gaabo.

Tukamaize kumanya ngu ebigambo by'okugamba kw'omurungi nibikorwa n'ebintu ebirikuza okuruga omu kutwaririza okwa hara.

Abo abarikutaana omu miringo yabo ey'obwongo n'amateeka agagumire, obutoosha nibakwega aha biteekateeko ebi ebitarikuhikaana.

Okukomoorora EGO omu muringo murungi n'okugaruka okuhitsya obu kibaasika kusisimura okwetegyereza n'okwingurura obwongo obwomunda.

Kwonka, nk'oku okuteekateeka oku okutahikaana kutarikubaasa kutaana omu kugamba kw'omuringo, n'okubaasa kutaana omu kugamba okw'omurungi, okwakaaga okuruga omu bwongo oburikukora kutaikiriza.

Abantu abo abakundiire obwongo nibakwenda kuta omu nyanja omu kikopo ky'omuringo, bagamba ngu yunivasite erikubaasa kukora aha bwenge bw'ensi yoona kandi ngu amateeka goona g'ensi yoona nigo gari kukora omu mateeka gaabo agaragumire.

Hataroho okukwata, eby'obwenge, okukangura omu muringo ogu bari.

Emirembe emingi abantu abo nibatangarira obwire oburikwija omu nsi y'ebyahandiikwa, okukiraaho bagwire nk'emiro gy'obusheeshe, bagwire omu muringo gw'omutima, nibateebwa obwongo kandi nibagwire omu kifo ekyo obutoosha.

Obugufu bw'obwongo tiburikubaasa kuhika aha muhanda murungi gwa Ruhanga, kwonka okukora okurikubaasa kutwara abeegi omu miringo yoona egy'ebigambo kwonka bishema.

Amaani g'okugamba ebigambo by'omurungi omu muringo gwona tikirikumanyisa okukora ebintu ebyamazima.

Okuhereza okurikukora okw'okugamba kw'omurungi, okurikukoresa omuntu orikugamba okugamba omu muringo murungi n'okumureetera kwesiibya engwe n'embwa.

Okutangarira okurikurugamu omu biteekateeko kurikumweteera omuntu orikuteekateeka kandi kurikumureetera okwegamba nk'omuntu ow'omugasho kugira ngu yafe omu by'okubikira omu maktaba n'ebyahandiiko bya yunivasite.

"Ekibira ky'obwire" ky'abasinzi abarikunwa amaarwa kine ebyo kuronda ebitarikusiibya, kwonka abarikusinziira omu biteekateeko nibesiibya omu bwenge.

Obu turihikire aha kicweka egi ky'omutwe gwitu, turikugamba ngu buzima nikigumire kumanya ahu obwenge bw'ababira burikuhwera n'ahu obushema burikutandika.

Obu turikugumizamu kutaana omu mateeka agarikuhwaara n'agaragumire g'obwongo, nikirikukora kukira okutabaasa okukora aha kintu ekitarimu bwongo, aha kintu ekitarimu bwire, aha kintu ekiri amazima.
