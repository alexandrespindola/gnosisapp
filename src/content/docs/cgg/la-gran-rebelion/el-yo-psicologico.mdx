Ekibuuzo eki ekikwatiraine nange omweene, eki ndi, ekirikuteekateeka, ekirikuhurra, n’ekirikukora, n’ekintu eki twine kweshwijuma kubaasa kumanya omuhendo gwakyo.

Hariho enyegyesa nyamwingi ezirikurarikira n’ezirikushisha; kwonka tibirikugasa twaba tutarikumanya eitwe.

Nikishisha kwega ebiri aha iguru nari kweshemeza obundi orikusoma ebitabo by’omutaano, kwonka nikiswaza kuba omwegi omukuru otarikumanya kintu kyona aha iwe omweene, aha ndi, aha buntu bw’abantu abu twine.

Buli omwe aine obugabe bw’okuteekateeka eki arikwenda kandi okuteekateeka kw’ekisoro-maniina ekirikwetwa omuntu kukuru nikireetera byona, nikibaasa kukora ebiraayi n’ebibi; abamaniina baingi nibashambana n’okuteekateeka okurikukiriza obwenge.

Okuba omwegi omukuru tikirikumanyisa kuba omunyabwenge. Abatazikwega abamanya baine obwingi nk’omutandi kandi tibarikumanya kwonka, kandi nabo tibarikumanya ngu tibarikumanya.

Muteegyereze ngu abatazikwega abamanya nibo abamanya abarikwikiriza ngu nibamanya kandi nabo tibarikumanya abeene.

Twikibaasa kwegesa aha nze ow’ebirikukwata aha mitwe y’abantu, kwonka tikyo twine omutaano nagyo omu kicweka eki.

Twine kwemanya omuhanda ogugwiiremu tutarikukora enshwamu.

Ti kikubaasika twaba tutarikwekwata oburo n’oburo, omukati n’omukati.

Tikirikukwata aha kwereeba kuraba omu nyegyesa nari omu kuteekateeka kw’obwenge.

Kweraba nk’oku turi nikyo kirikushisha; nikwo twarikubaasa kuhika aha kumanya abeene kw’amazima.

N’obu kirikusaana nk’ekitatikurikwikirizibwa turi abeshuba aha bitukwataho.

Ebintu bingi ebi turikwikiriza ngu tituriho twine, kandi ebi turikwikiriza ngu twine tituriho.

Twikomile enshonga z’obushuba aha bitukwataho kandi twine kukora ebyetengo kugira ngu tumanye eki turikutera n’eki turikubura.

Turikwikiriza ngu twine obumanzi bwaingi obu tutaine kandi emitwarize mirungi mingi ei twine nitugitwariza omuhanda.

Turi abantu abasinzire, abatahamukira kandi nikyo kibi. Tibarikushemererwa nituteekateeka ebirungi aha bitukwataho kandi titurikukwatirira ngu tusinzire.

Ebyahandiikwa ebirikwera nibishisha aha mugisha gw’okuramuka, kwonka tibirikushoboorora omuringo gw’okuhikiirira okuramuka oku.

Ekisingire obubi n’okuba hariho abantu baingi abasoma ebyahandiikwa ebirikwera kandi nabo tibarikwetegyereza ngu basinzire.

Omuntu weena arikwikiriza ngu yeyamanya kandi nabo tibarikukwatirira ngu hariho “enyegyesa y’abantu baingi.”

Mubwamazima nze ow’ebirikukwata aha mitwe ya buli omwe aine obwingi, emazire kuba nk’abantu baingi.

Neki eki turikukunda kumanyisa ngu twine abanze baingi kandi omwe nk’oku abatazikwega abamanya barikwikiriza.

Kuhakana enyegyesa y’abantu baingi nikwesheshema iwe omweene, ahabw’okuba ekirikubaasa kuba ekisingireyo n’okurabyamu obuteetegyereza obuhikire obu buli omwe omuitu aine.

Ngiye kusoma ekibarua, nze ow’obwenge; omugisha kurya orukurata, nze ow’okuhingura; nyamara kugyenda orusikara omugisha. Omuringo gurikusaana ki, n’eki omuntu arikwiita atyo, okugwa omukoro omweshatu; nyamara kurya, ndimu ndeka.

Twakibasa kwereeba omu ndiro ey’omubiri gwona, nk’oku turi, tukazura abeene omuhanda ogugwiiremu enyegyesa y’abantu baingi.

Omutwarize gw’abantu kiri nk’ekishaija ekirikugambira ekirikwegamira aha migwa etarikureebwa.

Nze orikwegaana obwengye bw’ebiro byoona n’obw’omutima, omwenda n’obwo tikurikukwata aha ndagaano; obwo omuntu naagwa.

Nze orikwegaana obwengye bw’ebiro byoona n’obw’omutima, omwenda n’obwo tikurikukwata aha ndagaano, obwo omuntu naagwa omu kwenda omukazi ondiijo kandi ekigo ky’omukono kiyere. Ekisoro-maniina ekirikwetwa omuntu nikisaana nk’enju eijwiiremu abantu baingi.

Tihariho enteekaniza nari okwetegyereza kwona omu banzwe baingi, boona nibarwanisa abeene kandi nibashangana okuhigwaho. Omwe aha bo ku arikuhikiirira obugabe bw’omu bitoongo by’omutwe gw’ekyuma, nayehurra nk’omwe, omwami, kwonka omuheru nagwa.

Nitureeba ebintu okuruga omumutwe ogu, nituhika aha kushushanya okurikukiriza ngu ekisoro-ky’omubwenge tikirimu obugabe bw’okutwara omuntu.

Okuraburura ekyo eky’ekyuma kirikugamba nari kukora omu kati, kwegamira aha mutindo ogwa nze orikugyendera ahabwo obu arikugitwara.

Nibagamba ngu Yesu ogwa Nazareti akagya abantu musanju omu mubiri gwa Maria Magdalena, abanze musanju, okugira omubiri kw’ebyonoono musanju.

Nokuba buli omwe aha bantu musanju akakora ebi arikwenda, twine kwemerera nk’eky’okuteekateeka ngu Kristo akabaasa kugyenda omu mubiri gwa Magdalena okukora ebyo arikwenda.

Niturekuriza ebintu byona ebi twine kukora twine kumanya ngu eky’omutaano eki twine omu nda yitu n’OBUNTA, tiburikusaana obwo nibushangirwa omu banzwe baingi obwo omu by’emitwe y’abantu.

Nikirukuswaza ngu obunta nibugyenda nibukora enshonga z’okuteekateeka.

Kikuru, obunta nari okumanya eki kigwiire, nikusiinziira omuhendo.
