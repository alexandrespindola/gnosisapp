Tikurikushobora kureeba omubusho bwitu obuturikwetegyereza nari kwikiriza; twine kuleeta omushana gw'okwetegyereza omu busho obwo.

Ekigyendererwa ky'okushoma kwaitu okwa Gnostic n'okureetera okwetegyereza kwitu okukwata aha bwitu kureebeka gye.

Obu orikugira ebintu bingi omuriiwe ebi otarikwetegyereza nari kwikiriza, ebintu nk'ebyo nibikukomera omubwomeezi bwawe munonga kandi bikareeta embeera zoona ezi orikubaasa kwetantara kuraba omu kwetegyereza.

Ekirikukirayo obubi omu byoona ebi n'okugira ngu turikushisha obusho obwo obuturikwetegyereza omuri abantu abandi reero tukabureeba omuri bo.

Eky'okureeberaho: tubareeba nk'abashuba, abatazikiriza, ab'omutima omubi, n'ebindi, aha mukura gw'ebi turikwetwara omunda.

Gnosis negira aha kikwatiraine n'ekyo, ngu turikugumira omu kicweka kikye munonga ekya bwitu.

Nikimanyisa ngu okwetegyereza kwaitu nikukwata aha kicweka kikye munonga ekya bwitu.

Eky'obwengye obw'okukora okwa Gnostic n'okugaziya okwetegyereza kwaitu.

Tikurikushobora kureebeka ngu kukira kureebeka ngu obuturikuba twine omukago murungi n'abaitu, titurikuba twine omukago murungi n'abandi kandi ekirikurugamu n'enyana zoona ez'ekika kyona.

Nikyetagisa kugira okwetegyereza okwingi aha bwitu kuraba omu kureeba bwitu butoosha.

Eky'okutegyeka ekya Gnostic omukukora okwa Gnostic, n'okugira ngu obuturikuba tutarikwetegyerezana n'omuntu, noobaasa kugira obuhame ngu ogwo n'omushango gwonka ogurikwetagisa kukora ahari gwitu.

Eki turikucuma munonga omu bandi kintu ekirikwegamira aha busho bwitu obuturikwetegyereza kandi eki tutarikwenda kumanya nari kwikiriza.

Obu turikuba omunsi eyo obusho bwitu n'obwingi munonga, kwonka obu omushana gw'okureeba gwitu gurikushana obusho obwo, okwetegyereza nikukura kuraba omu kwetegyereza.

Egi niyo Ngyenda y'Ekitsyo ky'Akarima, erikukirayo obusungu okukira amahoha, baingi nibagitatikira, bake munonga nibashika aha kuheru.

Nk'oku Omwezi gukugira orubaju orwe orwehingire orutarikureebeka, orubaju orutarikumanywa, nikwo kirikubaasa kugira Omwezi gw'Omutima ogu turikwetwara omunda.

Nikirangwa gye ngu Omwezi gw'Omutima ogwo gukozirwe Ego, Ise, Meeze, Yeeze.

Omu mwezi ogu gw'omutima turikwetwara ebintu ebitarikukwata aha bantu ebirikutinisa, ebirikuhunga kandi ebi tutarikwenda kwikiriza kugira.

Ngyenda yoona ya OKWEMALIRIRA KW'OMUNDA AHA BY'OMUTIMA, ni mataba ki!, ni ngyenda ki enzibu!, ni misiri ki ey'obutiiji!.

Obumwe omuhanda ogw'omunda bwanyima y'okwezinga kwingi n'okutaagana, okwemerera okutinja n'okutsigama okunaku, gutwarira omu matungo g'omushesha, torikumanya egi gurikukurata kandi n'omushana gumwe gutarikukumurikira.

Ngyenda ejwire eby'akabi omunda n'aha rubaju; omuhanda gw'eby'ekyama ebitarikugambika, aharikuhuhira eihuura ry'okufa.

Omu muhanda ogu ogw'omunda obu omuntu arikwikiriza ngu arikugyenda gye munonga, omu mazima arikugyenda kubi munonga.

Omu muhanda ogu ogw'omunda obu omuntu arikwikiriza ngu arikugyenda kubi munonga, nikibaho ngu arikugyenda gye munonga.

Omu muhanda ogu ogw'ekyama hariho obwire obumwe ngu omuntu takimanya eki kirungi nari eki kibi.

Eki obumwe kirikuzibira, obumwe nikishangwa ngu kirungi; nikwo omuhanda gw'omunda guri.

Amateeka goona ag'obuhangwa omu muhanda ogw'omunda nigaruga; amagambo marungi nari eky'okutegyeka kirungi, omu bwire obumwe nikibaasa kuhinduka ekizibu kikuru aha Kwemalirira kw'omunda aha By'omutima.

Omu kwegamba Kristo w'Omunda kuruga aha ntandikiro y'omutima gwaitu arikukora n'amaani, arikubonabona, arikurira, arikucwekyereza ebintu ebirikwetaaga kutinda ebi turikwetwara omunda.

Kristo nazairwa nk'omwana omuto omu mutima gw'omuntu kwonka nk'oku arikwiha ebintu ebitarikukirizibwa ebi turikwetwara omunda, arikukura bukye bukye kuhitsya aha kuhinduka omushaija orikumara.
