Okwehikiriza omuntu owa sayikoloji kutureetera kumanya oburemeezi obuhikireho obw'omutindo gw'ahaiguru omuli buri omwe ahariitwe.

Omuntu ku arikubaasa kwemeza omumazima eky'okuba abantu babiri omumuntu omwe, owaansi omumutindo ogwa burijo, owahaiguru omumurongo gwaana, obwo byona nibihinduka kandi tukateeraho omukwo kworeka omubwomeezi omuhanda gw'enteerane ez'omusingi ezi atuura ahaiguru y'omwoyo gwe.

Nk'oku ekyo kiriyo obwomeezi bw'ahahenda, nikyo kiriyo obwomeezi bw'omunda.

Omuntu w'ahahenda ti byona, okwehikiriza omuntu owa sayikoloji kutureetera kumanya amazima g'omuntu w'omunda.

Omuntu w'ahahenda aine omutindo gwe, kintu kimwe n'emibaro mingi n'okugarukana omubwomeezi, ekihande ekirikugyemwa emiguha etarikureebwa.

Omuntu w'omunda noyo Ndi mazima, akora omu biragiro ebindi ebitari bimwe, tarikubaasa kuhindurwa ekyoma.

Omuntu w'ahahenda tarikora mugo gwona ataine kacuupu, nayehurra ngu bamushashwire bubi, nimusasiira, nayeteeraho muno, yaaba musirikare naayenda kuba jenerali, yaaba mukozi omu faakitale naayemereza yaaba bamuhwereza, naayenda ngu eby'okusiimwa bye bireebwe.

Tihariho orikubaasa kuhika aha kazaaro ka KABIRI, okuzooka nk'oku kyagambwa omu Makuru ga Ruhanga, okuhitsya akagyenda naaguma naatuura n'emiteekateeka y'omuntu owaansi owa burijo.

Omuntu ku araakumanya obutariho bwe hamwe n'ebitahikire bye eby'omunda, ku aine amaani g'okwejuura obwomeezi bwe, hatarikwetenga n'aza kumanya omumazima ngu tarimu eby'okusiimwa byona.

"Abagumisiriza omumwoyo bafeerwe ahabw'okuba nibo barikwija kuhabwa obukama bw'omu iguru".

Abagumisiriza omumwoyo nari abafeere omumwoyo, abo mazima nibo abarikumanya obutariho bwabo, obunaku n'ebitahikire byabo eby'omunda. Abantu boona abashoboirwe, nabo nibahabwa okumasamasa.

"Kyorobi endogoya kuhita omu bushobo bw'omugisha, okukira omugagga kutaaha omu bukama bw'omu iguru".

Kyorekwa ngu obwomeezi bukagagazibwe eby'okusiimwa bingi, ebiteeso n'emidaali, emikoro mirungi ey'obuntu n'obwengye bw'eshaaza obutagira muheru, tiburi bagumisiriza omumwoyo kandi ahabw'ekyo tiburikubaasa kutaaha omu bukama bw'omu iguru.

Okutaaha omu Bukama nikwo kwesigika eky'okusiima kw'okwikiriza. Okuhitsya okukoreka omu buri omwe ahaariitwe okwehikiriza omuntu owa sayikoloji, OKWIKIRIZA nikureebeka kuba kukuru okukira ekyo ekitabaasike.

OKWIKIRIZA nobumanya obwerere, amagezi g'okukora.

OKWIKIRIZA buzibuzaibwe n'obwikiriza obutari bwa mazima, Abagunositiki titushemereire kugwa omumuhiso ogu mubi.

OKWIKIRIZA nobumazima bw'ekirikubaho; okwegyemereza kw'omuntu w'omunda okungi; obumanya obw'obuhangwa obuhikire.

Omuntu w'omunda, okumanya omumazima ebirooto bye eby'omunda, kyorekwa ngu namanya ebirooto by'omunda by'abantu boona abarikutuura ahaiguru y'ensi.

Tihariho orikubaasa kumanya ebirooto by'omunda by'ensi egi, eky'omusingi gw'omushana hamwe n'ekyombeko eki turimu, yaaba atakamanire ebirooto bye eby'omunda. Eki kiringana n'omuntu orikweshaanga okuhunga obwomeezi aharugiisa rwaijwire.

Okureeba kw'omwongyereza aine omukara gw'amateeka g'omuringo gw'omubanga omu KUNDARTIGUADOR (omusota orikuleetera okugwa omu kigwo eky'omuhwa).

Obumanya obuteebembeze omubugufu bw'ebintu byingi ebirikugumya Ego nikikora ahabw'okuteebembeza kwayo.

Obumanya bwa ego nibuza kumala gukareebeka, omumutindo ogwa koma, n'ebirooto eby'okwetenga eby'okuringana n'obwomeezi obwoona obw'omuntu orikubaasa kuba noomara noba noona omukara ogwoona.

Nitubaasa kuteera enshonga egi omu muringo ogurikukurataho: ebirooto by'obumanya bwa ego nibiringana n'ebirooto ebirugire omukara.

Nikireebeka emiringo egi yoona eshatu aine enshoroojo zaayo ez'omusingi omu KUNDARTIGUADOR (Reeba eky'omutwe XVI omu kitabo eki).

Hatariho okukaguuza, emikara neyita omu miiniro eya alfa, obwo hatariho okukaguuza nibwija kwibwa omukago gw'omumazima hagati y'omutwe hamwe n'obwomeezi, eki omumazima nikireetera eky'obusa.

Omwongyereza nahindura obunyansi kuba eddiini n'okushisha akateekateeka kagyenda noomeza eky'omumazima ahansi y'omukara, n'akakiiko ngu okureeba okureetwa marihwana, L.S.D., morfine, obututu oburikworeka, cocaine, heroine, hashis, empapu ezirikuhumuuriza omu bungi, amfetamine, barbiturates, n'ebindi, nibyo kwetenga ebikoreirwe omuringo ogw'omubanga omu KUNDARTIGUADOR.

Abongyereza abarikugwa, barikushisha obwiire, nibetaaha omumazima omu birooto by'omunda ebi.
