Hariho abantu bangi ab’obushwereka abatahikire aha muhanda murungi kandi bashashire omubisi bw’okutaha emikangara.

Buzima oburo bw’okutaha emikangara bw’abantu bw’abantu bw’abantu bw’abantu omutwe omubisi bukizire omutwe bw’abantu bw’abantu bw’abantu omu kikumi ky’ekumi na munaana.

Eizooba eri rikuru eiri omu maisho ga Spain, ekaba neyoreka buri kasumi.

Tihariho okubanganisa ngu eizooba ezi ziri omu maisho ga Spain. Hariho eby’okushisha bingi ebiri omu maisho ga Spain.

Bwanyima y’omwaka gw’ekumi na munaana, eizooba eri rikabura omu bwire butahwa, tihariho omuntu orikumanya kyonka kukikurira.

Omu biro by’omugabe Aturo n’abarwanisi b’ekiti ekitungire, eby’obutonde bikaba nibyoreka buri kasumi, nibita omu kikaro kyaitu eky’omubiri.

Hariho enshagama nyingi ezirikukwata aha bishushani, abanyabushoboorozi, n’enkoni ezi zikugwa omu Erim y’akaaka, Irelendi; omu bwitaafu, ebintu ebi byona ebirungi, oburungi bw’omutima gw’ensi, tibukikyereka abantu ahabw’obushwereka bw’abantu oburi obugufu kandi n’okukura kw’eky’enyama.

Eizooba eri abashwereka nibaseka ebintu ebi byona, tibabikira n’obu batahikire aha kushemererwa.

Abantu bakwetegyeka ngu twine emitwe eshatu, omuriro gukugenda, ahakuba nibenda kwetegyeka ebintu ebi.

Omu bwitaafu, abashomereire abatazabire, abahungwire omu by’okushomereira byabo, tibarikugira obwire bw’okukuratira eby’okwetegyeka byaitu.

Abantu ab’emicwe mibi nibefuriza, nibefuriza omu bwengye obutari bwa mazima, nibateekateeka ngu bari omu muhanda murungi kandi tibarikuteekateeka ngu bari omu muhanda gw’okwebuurira.

Omu iziina ry’amazima tushemereire kugamba ngu omu bw’obuhupi, twine emitwe eshatu.

Omutwe ogw’okubanza tushemereire kugutuma omutwe gw’obwengye. Ogw’akabiri tugwongeze omu iziina ry’omutwe oguri hagati. Ogw’akashatu tugutume omutwe oguri omunda.

Hati ka tugyende tukwetegyeke buri mutwe omu mitwe egi eshatu omu muhanda murungi.

Buzima omutwe gw’obwengye guteekateeka ebyo gurikugenderera ahabw’ebintu ebi turikureeba.

Omu mbeera ezi omutwe gw’obwengye guri omubi munonga kandi ogw’omubiri, tigurikukira kyonka kukibazaire omu mubiri.

Ahabw’okuba ebyo omutwe gw’obwengye gurikukoma ahabw’ebintu ebi turikureeba, buzima tihariho ekirikubaasa kumanya aha mazima, aha mazima, aha by’okuhisha by’obwomeezi n’okufa, aha mutima n’omwoyo, n’ebindi.

Ahabw’abashwereka, abataahire omu byoona ahabw’ebintu ebi turikureeba kandi n’okwegwire omu byoona by’omutwe gw’obwengye, eby’okwetegyeka byaitu nibishisha.

Omu bwengye obutari bw’obwengye, omu nsi y’obushisha, baine obwengye ahabw’okuba nibaremirwe ensi ei turikureeba. Omugabe gwa bwengye gutya kusiima ekitarikureebwa?

Ku biri ebi turikureeba nibikozesa byoona eby’omutwe gw’obwengye, kirikubasa kuba kyoreka ngu ebi byona nibireeta ebyo turikureeba.

Omutwe oguri hagati nigukurugaana, kwonka tigurikukira kyonka kukikira kyonka.

Omu mutwe oguri hagati hariyo abakurikiraine, eby’obuhangwa ebitozirika, n’ebindi.

Omutwe oguri omunda n’omukuru aha kumanya amazima.

Buzima omutwe oguri omunda guteekateeka ebyo gurikukoma ahabw’ebintu ebi obwengye bukuru obw’okuhikaana kw’omuntu.

Buzima obwengye nibukira kushiisha kandi n’okumanya amazima. Tihariho okubanganisa ngu obwengye bumanya amazima.

Kwonka, ahabw’okworeka obwengye nibwenda omuriisa, ekikozeso ky’okukora, n’okuki omu omutwe oguri omunda.

Obwengye nibumanya mazima buri kintu omu butonde kandi kurabira omu mutwe oguri omunda nibubasa kukyoreka.

Kugura omutwe oguri omunda nikibaasa kworeka enshagama eziri omu nsi y’okubanganisa n’obugafu.

Eki nikibaasa kworeka ngu kugura omutwe oguri omunda nikizaara okwikiriza kw’omuntu.

Kureeba ekibuuzo eki kuruga aha ngondi endiijo, tugambe ngu okutaha emikangara n’ekintu kibi munonga ky’obugafu. Tihariho okubanganisa ngu abashomereire abatazabire nibaruga omu bugafu.

Okwikiriza nikwo kumanya amazima; obwengye obukuru; okwikiriza ekiri ahaiguru y’omubiri, okukunda n’omutwe.

Orekere aha okwikiriza n’okwikiriza. Okwikiriza kuri omu mutwe oguri hagati, okwikiriza nikwo omutwe oguri omunda.

Omu bwitaafu hariho eky’okuta okwikiriza n’okwikiriza. N’obu kikureetera obushisha, twine kugamba ngu: “OGWO ORIKWIKIRIZA AMAZIMA TARIKWENDA KUKIRIZA”.

Ahabw’okuba okwikiriza mazima n’obwengye, okumanya okw’amazima, okwikiriza okw’amazima.

Kikaba omu myaka mingi okwikiriza n’okwikiriza kikirikukora omurimo muhango kugira ngu abantu beetegyeke ngu okwikiriza n’obwengye obw’amazima kandi tipyo by’okwikiriza.

Eby’omutwe gw’omunda nibikozesa eby’obwengye oburikuhikire omu bwengye oburi omu bwengye.

Ogwo ogwire omutwe gw’omunda aijuka amagara ge agahingwire, amanya eby’okuhisha by’obwomeezi n’okufa, aha kyakushoma nari atashoma, aha kyakugambiire nari atagambiire, aha kyakwikiriza nari atakwikiriza, kureka aha kumanya okw’amazima, okw’amazima.

Eki turikugamba tikirikushisha omutwe gw’obwengye, tigurikukira kukikira ahabw’okuba nigiruka kuruga omu kikaro kyago, tihariho ekirikukwata aha byoona ebi turikureeba, nikintu ekirikukurugaana n’ebyo bigirikukoma, ekyo ekyomwegire omu isoomero, ekyo ekyeyegire omu bitabo ebirikukurugaana, n’ebindi.

Eki turikugamba tikirikukiriza omutwe oguri hagati ahabw’okuba n’okuhakanisa ebyo turikwikiriza, nikwohururaho ebyo abakurikiire be bamwegire okwega omu mutwe, n’ebindi.

Yesu Omukuru Kabiri nabuurira abegi be ngu: “Mwegendereze omubisi gw’abasadukayo n’omubisi gw’abafarisaayo”.

Nikireetera akamwenyu ngu Yesu Kristo n’okubuurira eki akareeta aha by’okwetegyeka by’abasadukayo n’abahugaifarisaayo.

Eby’okwetegyeka by’abasadukayo biri omu mutwe gw’obwengye, n’ebyo ebi turikureeba.

Eby’okwetegyeka by’abafarisaayo biri omu mutwe oguri hagati, eki tikirikubaasa kuhanangwa.

Nikireebeka ngu abafarisaayo nibaza aha mikoro yaabo kugira ngu abantu bagambe ngu bantu barungi, kweyoreka aha bandi, kwonka tibarikukora ahari bo.

Tikitubaasa kugura omutwe oguri omunda reero twega okuteekateeka omu mutima.

Buzima obwo omuntu atandika kwetegyeka nikibaasa kworeka ngu atandika okuteekateeka omu mutima.

Omuntu atakyikiriza mazima g’omutima gwe n’omugisha gw’okukihindura, buzima tarikureeba omukago gw’okwetegyeka omu mutima.

Obwo omuntu akikiriza eby’okwetegyeka by’abantu bangi kandi aketegyeka omukago gw’okugyaho ebyo byona ebi turikutwara omu mutima gw’okugyaho obwengye, ahakuba buzima kuruga aha kyakukora n’omu mazima atandika okwetegyeka omu mutima.

Obundi okugyaho ebyo turikuta omu mutima nibireeta okugura omutwe oguri omunda.

Eki kityo kyona nikikubaasa kworeka ngu okugura okwo nikikora omu muhanda oguri ahansi, obwo turikugyaho ebyo turikuta omu mutima.

Ogwo ogwire ebyo byona ebi turikuta omu mutima omu kikumi, obundi n’omutwe gwe oguri omunda aguire omu kikumi.

Omuntu nk’ogwo naija kugira okwikiriza okuhikire. Hati noija kwetegyeka ebigambo bya Kristo obwo yagambire ngu: “Ku mubaasa kugira okwikiriza nka akantu k’endima, mubaasa kuhindura enshagama”.
