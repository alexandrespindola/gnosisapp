Ekikopo kya Mukama kirabira omu mwizima gw'emyaka yoona. Abasirikale b'omu myaka gya wakati omu biro by'entalo za Katonda bakasera busera kukishanga ekikopo kya Mukama omu ttaka erikwere omu kwona tibakakishanga.

Obu Abrahamu nabi akomera kuruga omu rutalo rwe na bakama ba Sodoma na Gomora, nibagamba ngu akashanga Melquisedec omuntu w'amaani ogw'ensi. Buzima omuntu ogwo mukuru akaba atwiire omu kigo ekirahinduka ekifo ekirajangarirwa Yerusalemu, ekihanga ky'ababi ekigonzibwa.

Ekyefumiire kya myaka niko kiragira kandi eki abantu n'abairu bakimanyire, ngu Abrahamu akakora okusiigwa kwa Bagunositi kw'okugabana omugaati n'envinnyi omu maisho ga Melquisedec.

Tikiri bubi kwemeza ngu obwo Abrahamu akahereza Melquisedec eky'okumi n'ebibereberye nk'oku kiri omu Kitabo ky'amateeka.

Abrahamu akatunga ekikopo kya Mukama kuruga omu ngalo za Melquisedec; enyima omu muringo oguragire ekikopo eki kikaza omu hekalu ya Yerusalemu.

Tihari kubanganisa ngu omwamikazi wa Sheba akakora nk'omuhabwezibwa ahabw'ekikorwa eki. Akemerera omurai Salomoni na ekikopo kya Mukama kandi enyima y'okumuteera omu kugezesa okukomeire akamuhereza eky'omugisha eki kikuru.

Yesu mukuru akanywa omu kikopo eki omu mukoro ogurikwere ogw'okuriibwa okw'ahaheru nk'oku kyajangarirwe omu Njiri enya.

Yosefu ow'Arimatea akaijuza ekikopo n'esagama eyaizire kuruga omu bihwa bya Mukundwa omu Rushozi rw'ebikokoro.

Obu abaserikale ba Roma baatahize omuka gwa Senatori ogw'omurimo tibalishangire ekikopo eki kikuru.

Senatori ow'e Roma tatiinikire kyoka ekikopo eki kikuru baitu kandi nawe hamu n'ekikopo akaterekere omuseeto ogwa Longibus ogu centurioni w'e Roma akahweire orubaju rw'Omukama.

Yosefu ow'Arimatea akagwirwa omu kihome kibii ahabw'okukagaana kuhereza ekikopo kya Mukama.

Obu Senatori ogu yaraizire kuruga omu kihome akagenda e Roma ayeeteera ekikopo kya Mukama.

Obu yaahikire e Roma Yosefu ow'Arimatea akashanga okugyenzegyenzebwa kwa Neroni aha Bakristaayo kandi akagenda ahari eibanga ry'enyanja ya Mediterranean.

Omu kiro omu ndooti engeloi yakumemerera kandi ekagira eti: "Ekikopo eki kigire amaani manene ahabw'okuba omu kikopo eki harimu esagama y'Omucunguzi w'Ensi." Yosefu ow'Arimatea akakuratira ebiragiro by'engeloi akaziika ekikopo eki omu hekalu etwiire Montserrat, Catalonia, Spain.

Enteera ekikopo eki kikahinduka ekitalirabwa hamu n'ekihanga n'ekicweka ky'orusozi.

Ekikopo kya Mukama nikyo ekikopo kya Hermes, ekikopo kya Salomoni, ekikopo ekikuru eky'amarekere goona ag'obwiheru.

Omu sindooki y'endagaano tikihakabaaho kubura ekikopo kya Mukama omu muringo gw'ekikopo nari gomor, omunda harimu mana y'eihamba.

Ekikopo kya Mukama nikitaho obwengye aha YONI y'omukazi, omunda y'ekikopo eki ekirikwere harimu obwoki bw'oburafeerwa, Soma y'abemani, ekyokunywa ekikuru ky'abairu abarikwere.

Kristo omutukura anywa kuruga omu kikopo kya Mukama omu saaha ey'ahaiguru y'obukristo, nikwo kyajangarirwe omu Njiri y'Omukama.

Tiharibura ekikopo kya Mukama aha itabaza ly'ekihanga. Eky'obuhereza omukuru asemereire kunywa envinnyi y'omushana omu Kikopo Ekirikwere.

Kukaba okushemberera kukora ekihanga ky'obwiheru omunda eki ekyakubura ekikopo eky'omugisha eky'emyaka yoona.

Eki nikitwijutsya Ginevra Omwamikazi w'abajin, ogu Lanceloti akayokera envinnyi omu bikopo ebirungi ebya SUFRA na MANTI.

Abairu ab'oburafeerwa nibaijutsya n'ekinywebwa ekiri omu Kikopo Ekirikwere; abarikwenda ekikopo eky'omugisha, nibaramwa okurwisa omwoyo ogurikwere.

Omuntu omukuru asemereire kuliirwa n'obwoki bw'oburafeerwa oburi omu kikopo ky'omwiheru eky'ekihanga.

Okuhindura amaani ag'okuhanga nikikuru obu orikwenda kunywa omu Kikopo Ekirikwere.

Kristo omutukura omuturukiisha buri obwire, omukayambwa buri obwire, omugumisiriza buri obwire, omutsinga buri obwire, aguma n'agamba ahabw'abairu anywa omu kikopo kya zahabu.

Mumurinde mushemeezeze ekikopo kyanyu kandi mwerinde kutonera nari obutozi obw'envinnyi ey'omugisha.

Mwijukye ngu ekyemezo kyaitu nikyo THELEMA (obukundwa).

Abairu abatalingirika ab'enganda zoona z'enyunyu baguma nibanywa kuruga omu kinywebwa ky'oburafeerwa omu kikopo eky'oburafeerwa.

Obuhehere bw'omwezi nibuleeta obuhindukanisa omu bwire; nikiri etainazigwa kunywa kuruga omu nvinnyi erikwere y'omushana omu kikopo ekirikwere eky'obuganga.

Emiringo erangi y'ebijaruba y'abakama abarikwere, engamba erangi n'ezaahabu erikutangaaza n'eya Kristo omutukura yoka.

Omukama w'enkuba n'okututama ayeeteera omu mukono gwe ogw'obulyo ekikopo kya Mukama kandi anywa envinnyi ya zaahabu kukuriirwa.

Abatonera ekikopo kya Hermes omu bukurembe bw'obushwere bw'ekyemiko, buzima bahinduka ebirukwata bikye by'omu nda y'ensi.

Byoona ebyo twajangarire aha birooteire ebyetegyereza omu kitabo kyangye ekiyemere "Obushwere Oburikukirira kimwe."
