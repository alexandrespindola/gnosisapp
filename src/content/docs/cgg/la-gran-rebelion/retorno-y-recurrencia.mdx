Omushaija ni eki obwomeezi bwe buli: omushaija yaaba atarikukora obwomeezi bwe, aba atarikumara bwire bwe omu muringo murungi.

Tushemereire kwihaho ebintu ebi tutarikwenda ebi turikutwara omunda yaitu, twija kukora obwomeezi bwaitu obukuru.

Okufa ni okugaruka aha ntandikiro y'obwomeezi, n'omugisha gw'okubugyendamya bwona omu busaasi bw'obwomeezi obusya.

Amashuuri agari kumwe g'obushamari, nigashuuruza engiga y'obwomeezi bwona oburikugarukamu, ekintu eki kigwire.

Obwomeezi ni firimu; twamara kureeba, turikwinginga obutambi aha kareete kandi tukabutwara ebiro byoona.

Okugarukamu kuriyo, okugaruka kuriyo; twagaruka omu nsi egi turikuha firimu emwe, obwomeezi bumwe.

Nitubaasa kuteeka enjobe y'obwomeezi oburikugarukamu; kwonka hatariho obwomeezi oburikugarukamu ahabw'okuba firimu emwe.

Omuntu aine ebicweka bishatu aha kikumi by'obwomeezi obutakomiirwe na ebicweka makumi mwenda na musanju aha kikumi by'obwomeezi obutakomiirwe omubushaho omuri emye.

Twagaruka ebicweka bishatu aha kikumi by'obwomeezi obutakomiirwe nibishushana itungo eririkuzaarwa; hatariho kubanganisa nitugumizamu omu mbibo y'abaana baitu.

Emico erihenda; tihariho nyencweeka y'omuntu ofiire; ogu aheerizeho atandika kugwa omu nyombekyenda.

Omu muzaarwa omusya omuri yo hakagarukamu obucweka bukye bw'obwomeezi obutakomiirwe; eki nikira omuntu obwengye n'oburungi bw'omunda.

Emye etari emwe niyo erikugaruka ekuba ebandiire omwana omuzaarwa omusya, nibagenda nibaija omu bwesigye, nibakunda kuingira omunda y'omubiri kwonka eki tikirikubaasika kutosha okutaho omuntu omusya.

Nibishemeza kumanya ngu omuntu ayombekirweho amaani kandi ayombekirweho okumanya ebiro byingi.

Kyahandiikwa ngu omuntu ashemereire kutunga omu myaka mushanju ey'obuto kandi hahaho atandika kuguma.

Emye nitutandika kwetantara omunda y'omubiri omu muringo murungi aha kuhingura omuntu omusya.

Okufa ni okugyenda kwehindura, twamara kukora ekibaro ekikuru ekirikugumizamu kyonka amamurike (eki ni emye mirungi na mibi, egy'omugasho na egy'oburemeezi, enungi na embi).

Amamurike omu muzoora nigenda hamwe kandi nigamwena hagati yaago nikurugiirira aha mateeka g'okukwata kw'ensi yoona.

Itwe turi namba aha mwanya ogu turikuheereza hamwe amagomborora g'oburungi.

Omunda y'omuntu omwe buri omwe omuri itwe haroho amamurike aga gari kumwe agarikukora nk'omusingi gw'eiteeka ry'okugarukamu.

Buri kimwe nikigaruka omu muringo ogu kyabaireho kwonka ekyarugiremu nari ekiraande ky'ebikorwa byaitu.

Nka twena omuri itwe turimu emye ya hare ebandiire, nitubaasa kugamba omu muringo gw'amaani ngu buri omwe ahari ago ni muntu orikwenda.

Eki nikitushaba kwetegyereza ngu omunda ya buri omwe omuri itwe hariho abantu abingi abaine ebi baraikukora.

Omunda y'omuntu omwibi harimu obwiha obukuru bw'ababi; omunda y'omuntu aita hariho ekibiina ky'abiti; omunda y'omuntu omubeezi harimu enju y'abezi; omunda y'omuntu omwenda harimu ebandiire.

Buri omwe aha bantu abo abu turikutwara omunda y'emitima yaitu, aine ebizibu bye n'ebi araikukora.

Abantu abarikugara omunda y'abantu, abantu abarikugara omunda y'abantu; eki nikyo mazima, tikirikwenda kubanganisibwa.

Ekikuru aha byona ebi n'okuba buri omwe aha bantu abo nari emye egi turikuza omu nda yaitu, eruga omu bwomeezi bwa hare kandi aine ebi araikukora.

Omwe ogu omu bwomeezi obwa hare aba aine omukundwa aha myaka makumi ashatu, omu bwomeezi obusya arainde eyo myaka kworekwa kandi twahika aha bwire obwo araashake omuntu w'emirooto ye, araayeterane omu bwengye n'omuntu ogwo kandi aha muheru araagaruke na kugyendamya obusaasi.

Omwe ogu aha myaka makumi ana aba aine orugambo ahabw'ebintu, omu bwomeezi obusya arainde eyo myaka kwegyendamya orugambo rumwe.

Omwe ogu aha myaka makumi abiri na etaano aba arukaine n'omushaija omwe omu kabari, arainde omu bwomeezi obusya emyaka makumi abiri na etaano kweshera omumegyane we na kugyendamya obusaasi.

Nibashera hagati yaago emye y'omuntu omwe n'ondiijo kuraba omu matwara kandi hahaho nibagaruka kugyendamya omu bwengye.

Egi nikyo buzima eiteeka ry'okugarukamu, egi nikyo eky'obusaasi ky'obwomeezi.

Kuraba omu myaka nkumi na nkumi abantu abarikwenda nibagaruka kugyendamya okuseka n'eby'obusaasi.

Omuntu takiri masini erikuheereza emye egi eine ebi eraikukora.

Ekibi aha byona ebi n'okuba ebi byona ebi abantu abu turikutwara omunda yaitu nibamara hatariho obwengye bwaitu obutekateekire kumanya.

Omuntu waitu omu muringo ogu arikushusha ekaari erikukwatwa entama nyingi.

Hariho obwomeezi oburikugyendamya, oburikugarukamu obutari kwehindura.

Tuhindikira okuseka n'eby'obusaasi by'obwomeezi aha kyererezi ky'obwomeezi, hatariho batiita.

Abatiita b'emisaasi egi yoona niyo emye egi turikutwara omunda yaitu neruga omu bwomeezi obwa hare.

Twakwihaho emye y'obusungu, okuseka kw'obusungu nikuhwa.

Twatereera abagibita ebiheruura by'empeereza, ebizibu by'empeereza nibihwa.

Twatereera emye y'obubeezi, okuseka kw'enju y'abezi nikuhwa.

Twatereera abantu ebiheruura by'enzigu, ebigambo by'enzigu nibihwa.

Twaita emye y'obukuru, omulingo, obugwagwa, obukuru, okuseka kw'emitwarize egi nikuhwa ahabw'okubura abatiita.

Twakwihaho omunda yaitu oburemeezi bw'obunegye, okuseka kubi kw'ekika kya emitwarize egi tikurikubaasa kugyendamya ahabw'okubura abatiita.

Twagyenda emye y'obugufu, obuteeteeri, embaga, obuteeteeri nibihwa ahabw'okubura abatiita.

Nka emye egi nyingi erikukora omu maganya omu bitongole ebiri kumwe by'okuba, nikiba kirikwenda kumanya enshonga, entandikiro n'oburinganiza obu ha muheru burikututwara aha kufa kwange n'aha kucungurwa kw'aha muheru.

Kwega Kristo, kwega Kristo ni kikuru twaba nitushaba okwehindura okukuru; eki nikyo turaajega omu bicweka ebirikukurataho.
