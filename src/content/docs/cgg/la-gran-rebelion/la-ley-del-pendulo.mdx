Nikukwenda kuba nobutaama bw'oku kisenge omu maka gaitu, tikurora obwire kwonka kaitu n'okuteekateeka.

Otaburayo akati akateera, obutaama tibukora; okuteera kw'akati nikukirira ahansi.

Omu biro by'enyima, omugisha gw'okukyusaamu tikurikwija; hati, abanyabwenge bakaba nibamanya ngu ebikwatiraine n'eby'afaayo buri kiro nibiza omu muringo gw'Eteeko ry'Obutaama.

Buri kimwe nirikwija, nirigenda, nirikwima, nirigwa, nirikukura, niriceera, niriruga, nirigaruka omuringo ogwo gwe'Eteeko eririkukwenda.

Tikirikwetenga ngu buri kimwe ni kiteera, buri kimwe nikiheebwa obwire, buri kimwe nikihinduka.

Omu mpera y'obutaama hariho okushemererwa, omu yindi obusaasi; okwetenga kwaitu, okuteekateeka, okwetenga, okwenda, nibiteera omu muringo gw'Eteeko ry'Obutaama.

Ameizi n'obuswaire, obutari bwameizi n'okwetenga, eihumbo n'obusaasi, okutsinda n'okugwa, okuguzaho n'okufiirwa, byona nibijuna omuringo gw'okuteera kw'obutaama.

Egipita akakurayo n'amaani gaayo goona aha rubaju rw'omugyera omweru, baitu obutaama bwagendayo omu rubaju orundi, obwo oburugireho omu mpera egwirireho, eihanga ry'abafarao lyagwa hamu na Yerusalemu, orurembo orukundwa omu bagambi.

Isiraeli akagwa obwo obutaama buhindukireho omwanya na Roma, obukama bugakurayo omu rubaju orundi.

Okuteera kw'obutaama nikukura kwaramura Bukama, kwaramura okuhangwa okw'amaani reero nikubisa.

Nitubaasa kuteeka omu mpera y'oburyo y'obutaama amasomero agarikushusha gandi g'oburogo n'oburogo, ediini n'amakanisa.

Nitubaasa kuteeka omu mpera y'omushoberwa gw'okuteera kw'obutaama amasomero goona ag'omulingo ogw'eby'omubiri, Abamaaki, abatari b'ediini, abarikuteebereza, etc. Okuhindura kw'okuteera kw'obutaama, okuhindukire, okuriho okucweera buri kiro.

Omuntu orikwetenga ediini, ahabw'ekintu kyona ekirikutinda nari okugwa, nikubaasa kugenda omu mpera y'obutaama orundi, akahinduka omuntu atari w'ediini, omuntu w'omubiri, omuteebeereza.

Omuntu orikwetenga eby'omubiri, atari w'ediini, ahabw'ekintu kyona ekirikwetenga, taliba ekintu ekirikukirira okutaaho, obwire bw'okutiina obutari busigireho, nikimureetera kugenda omu mpera y'obutaama ehindukireho kandi akahinduka omuntu orikukirira ediini atarikusiimwa.

Eky'okureeberaho: Omuherezi atsindwa omu kubuuza okw'Omurogo, afuba kwehonga kandi ahinduka atarikwikiriza kandi ari w'omubiri.

Tukamanya ensonga y'omukazi atari w'ediini kandi atarikwikiriza ahabw'ekintu ky'oburogo ekirikumaliriza kandi ekirikushoberwa, akahinduka omugambirizi murungi gw'oburogo obukora.

Omu iziina ry'amazima, twine kwikiriza ngu omuntu atari w'ediini kandi w'omubiri ow'amazima kandi ow'oburaingana, n'ekishuba, tairiyo.

Omu maisho g'okufa okutari kwetengwa, omu kare ak'okutiina okutari busigireho, abazigu b'obwire boona, abantu b'omubiri n'abatari kwikiriza, nibaija obwire obwo omu mpera y'obutaama orundi kandi nibaruga nibashaba, nibarira kandi nibagarukira hamu n'okwikiriza okutari kwahukana n'okutwariza kw'ahaiguru.

Omu Carlos Marx wenka, omunyazi w'Omulingo gw'Eby'omubiri, akaba ari omuntu orikwetenga ediini y'Abayudaaya, kandi bwanyima y'okufa kwe, bakamuha ebiragiro by'omuhango bya Rabii omukuru.

Carlos Marx, akakuraho Omulingo gwe gw'Eby'omubiri omu kigendererwa kimwe kwonka: "OKUHANGIRA ECUMU AHAMU OKUBISA EDIINI ZOONA OMU NSI ENU OMU MURINGO GW'OKUTEEBEREZA".

Ni ensonga ey'omulingo ogurikukirira ey'okutunga ediini eretwa omu mpera; omu muringo gwoona ogurikubaasa, Marx tarabaasa kwikiriza obubaho bw'ediini endiijo kandi akasiima okubisa ahabw'Omulingo gwe.

Carlos Marx akakora omwe gw'Ebiragiro bya Sioni ogurikugamba omulingo gw'okuhereera: "Tikirikukora omulimo ngu twijuzaho ensi enu n'omubiri n'obutari kusima obutari bwediini, omu kiro eki turitsinda, turaijuzaho ediini ya Musa erikurora omulingo gurungi omu muringo gw'okubuuza kandi titurikwikiriza ediini endiijo omu nsi enu".

Nikikurora omulimo muno ngu omu Kurunga ya Sovieti ediini ziba zirikukwatwa kandi abantu nibegyesebwa omulingo gw'omubiri, obwo omu masinagogi nibashoma ebya Talmudi, Baibuli n'ediini kandi nibakora omuhanda gwona n'okutaaho oburemeezi.

Abanyazi b'obulemi bwa Russia nibantu abarikwetenga ediini y'Eteeko rya Musa, kandi nibakweera abantu n'ekishuba ekyo ky'Omulingo gw'Eby'omubiri.

Titurikwetenga kwerurungura kurwana n'abantu ba Isiraeli; tukirukwirukiriza kurwana n'abantu abarikukuruza abantu abarikukora omurimo ogurikwebembereza okwetenga okutari kwikirizibwa, nibakweera abantu n'Omulingo gw'Eby'omubiri, obwo omu kwihisha nibakora ediini ya Musa.

Eby'omubiri n'eby'omwoyo, n'eby'obutareka kwikiriza, okuteebereza n'eby'omutwe by'omulingo gwona, nibikorerwa omu mutwe omu muringo gw'Eteeko ry'Obutaama kandi nibahinduka omufasho omu burembo n'eby'okukora.

Omwoyo n'omubiri bigambo bibiri ebirikutindana kandi ebirikugumira ebi omuntu yena atarikwetenga.

Tihariho eki omutwe gurikumanya aha by'omwoyo, tihariho eki gurikumanya aha by'omubiri.

Eriiso tirikukirira, eriiso. Eby'amazima tibiri eriiso n'obu omutwe gubaasa kweshemeza amaiiso maingi aha by'amazima.

Omwoyo n'omwoyo (Omuntu), kandi kwerora kwonka nikibaasa kwerora.

Kyajambirweho: "OMUNTU N'OMUNTU KANDI ENSHONGA Y'OKUBA N'OKUBA N'OMUNTU KWENKA".

Abantu abarikwetenga Ruhanga w'omubiri, abakuru b'Omulingo gw'Eby'omubiri nibo abakora omulimo kandi abarikukora eby'obushara omu kikorwa kikuru. Nibagamba aha by'omubiri n'okwikiriza kwabo kw'ahaiguru n'obushema, obwo omu mazima batarikumanya aha gu.

Omubiri ki? Noha omu bakuru aba abashema abarikugumira? Omubiri ogurikuhumura gukira nago n'eriiso ery'amaani eririkugumira.

Omubiri ki?, Ecotooni?, Ekyoma?, Enyama?, Ekyangaara?, Eibaare?, Ekikomo?, Ekiire nari ki? Okugamba ngu buri kimwe n'omubiri nikirabaasa kuba ky'omulimo kandi ky'obushara nk'okuhakana ngu omubiri gw'omuntu goona n'omwijuzo, nari omutima nari omunsyo. Eki ky'obumanzi, ekintu kimwe n'ekintu kimwe kandi ekintu ekindi n'ekindi, buri kimwe kirahukana kandi buri kimwe kirayengana. Hati, ki kimwe omu kintu eki kyona n'omubiri ogurikuhumura gukira?

N'amaiso g'obutaama abantu baingi nibacweera, baitu omu mazima ameisyo tigago mazima.

Omutwe gwenka nikimanya emiringo ebirakire y'obutaama, baitu tihariho eki gurikumanya aha mazima agari omunsi y'emiringo egyo.

Okuteebereza nikuhwa omu muure hamu n'emyaka, kandi eki omuntu yayegereze omu somero nikiruga ahagati.

Okumaliriza: tihariho omuntu orikumanya.

Amanyiso g'eryoho erikuru nari ery'omushoberwa gw'obutaama nigahwaho nk'emifaashoni y'abakazi, byona ebyo nibikorwa by'omutwe, ebintu ebiriho aha rubaju rw'okwetenga, obushara, obweitungo bw'omutwe.

Aha kwegyesa kw'omutwe kwona nikiteekwa okwegyesa okundi, aha kukora kw'omutwe kwona okurikwenda omulingo, nikiteekwa ekindi ekirikushusha, kandi bwanyima y'ebyona, ki?

Eby'amazima, amazima, nibyo ebi turikwetenga; kukirira ahaiguru ti nshonga y'obutaama, tiharikusangwa omu kuteera kw'okuteebereza n'okwikiriza.

Amazima tigarikumanyika omu kare n'okuremwa, okuremwa.

Amazima gali omu mutwe gw'obutaama, ti omu mpera y'oburyo kandi tikali omu mpera y'omushoberwa.

Obwo Yesu yabuzibweho: Amazima ki?, akagumaho atazireetsa. Kandi obwo Buda yabuzibweho ekibuuzo ekyo, akamugira amaguru akagaruka.

Amazima ti nshonga y'okwerangira, nari okuteebereza, nari okwebembera okuhingurira omu ryoha nari okushoberwa.

Eriiso eri omutwe gubaasa kweraga aha mazima, tibiramaliza kuba amazima.

Eriiso eri okwetenga kurikutunga aha mazima, tiribaramaliza kuba amazima.

Omuze ogu twine aha mazima, ahabw'okuba n'omugisha ogwo gwoona gwona, omu muringo gwoona tigago mazima.

N'obuteera bw'omwoyo nari okurwana n'eby'omubiri, tibarabaasa kututwara aha mazima.

Amazima kintu ekihwera okukora omu muringo ogw'okutwara, nk'obwo omuntu aza omu muriro kandi akashisha, nari nk'obwo omuntu amize amaizi kandi akashaba.

Omutwe gw'obutaama guli omunsi yaitu enyenkahi, kandi omu kyo nitwetenga okwehurira kandi tukakora omu muringo ogw'okutwara eby'amazima, amazima.

Nitwetenga okwehurira enyenkahi okwehurira kandi tumanye enyenkahi.

Okukora kw'amazima nikwija kwonka obwo twahwireho ebintu ebirikutetenga ebi hamu bikuruza okwerora.

N'okuhwaho kw'okushisha amazima nikwija. N'okubisa kw'Omuntu, okushisha kwangye, okwebembera kwangye n'okutiina, ebihumbo byangye n'okwenda, okwikiriza n'obutari burooro, okwegamira omutwe n'okwikiriza byona by'omulingo gwona, kukwija aha izooba nitukora eki turi eby'amazima.

Amazima tihariho eki gurikutwara n'eki kyagambwa nari kyarekwa kugamba, n'ekyajambwa nari kyarekwa kujamba, rikwinama nikwija aha izooba kwonka obwo "okwerora" kwafire.

Omutwe tigurikubaasa kushanga amazima ahabw'okuba tigukigumire. Omutwe tigurikubaasa kumanya amazima ahabw'okuba tigwakigumire. Amazima nikwija aha izooba omuringo gw'okwija aha izooba obwo twahwireho ebintu byona ebitari kwetengwa ebi bikuruza "okwerora", "omuntu wenkahi".

Obwo okwetenga kwakagumaho kurikwetenga omuntu, tikirikubaasa kukora eki kyona eky'amazima, eki kiri ahaiguru y'omubiri, y'enganda n'omutwe, eki kuri amazima.

Obwo okwerora kwaceerwa okweziha omukungu, okwetenga nikujukira okuzinduka bwanyima yaakyo kandi kakora aha izooba amazima.

N'obukama kwagambira omugambi omukuru Yesu: "MUMANYE AMAZIMA KANDI IRAHABAMURA OKURUGIRIRAHO".

Nikikora ki omuntu okukunda okumanya okuteebereza makumi ashatu byona obu atakoze Amazima?

Omulingo gw'omutwe gw'omuntu goona nikushobozesa, kandi omu mulingo gwona kurikushoberwa ekindi kandi tini kimwe nari ekindi n'amazima.

Nikikurora kurungi okwehurira okwehurira kandi tukora aha izooba omu muringo ogw'okutwara, amazima, AMAZIMA.
