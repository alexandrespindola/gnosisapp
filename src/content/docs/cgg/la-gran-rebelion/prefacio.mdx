OKWESHONGA

Ebya: V.M. GARGHA KUICHINES

"OBWESIGURE OBUNENE" obw'Omwegyesi Omukuru Samael Aun Weor butwoleka omuhanda ogutali gwakuzibaana omu bwomeezi.

Kikahi kubbijjamu ebyo byoona ebitusiba aha bintu by'obushuhe omu bwomeezi bunu.

Hano twateraniize enyegyesa ya buri mutwe gw'ekicweka kworeka omushaija w'amaani okweyongyera omu Rugambaana hatali nandi, kundi.

Endimi zoona eziri omu kitabo eki zikukurra omu kuziba Yoze zaitu, kwijeemura Omugisha oguri omunda yaitu.

Yoze tahi kujja kufa kandi mwine anyumirwa kushumba omu byaha.

Omu nsi huzula abatayesimbiire kandi obutiinisa buzaanisa abantu yoona.

"TARIIHO KINTU KYESIGAAHI, EKIHIRRIHO NI ABASHAIJA ABATAYESIMBIRE".

# OMUTWE GWA 1

Abantu tibarina oburungi omu mutiima; eby'ahaiguru nibisibya byona. Ekisa tikiriho. Obusharabu buri n'abagondye. Emirembe tiriho habwokuba abantu nibaitwara n'obutaaga n'okwiiba.

Omugisha gw'abanyazigwa guri omu ngalo z'abakozibi b'emiringo yoona.

# OMUTWE GWA 2

Enjara n'okwitiinya nibyongyerra omukanya aha kandi eby'obushara nibishwaangaaza omwika gw'ensi, baitu harrho omuti okukora ahari akabi akatutwangaaziize: "Obutahirizi Obw'Obwengye" rundi okwesiga enanga y'omuntu okuhindura omu MAANI omu maabaraatori y'omuntu waitu kandi bwanyima omu Mwesyo n'Omuriro twegesa okugyemesereza ibiika bisatu by'okukanguka kw'obwengye: 1. Okuziita enengo zaitu. 2. Okutwanga ebituuba by'eizooba omunda yaitu. 3. Okukorera Omujaane Omukazi Omubi (Abantu).

Itaka, amaizi n'omwika, nibakozibwa ensi n'obukozibi bw'obusharo bura, etendeka terisobora kuletera oburungi; naitukorere n'ekyanga ky'amazi ogutu, endanga yaitu tona, tukikorese n'obwengye n'okumanya ekiturikukora, nukwo tusobora kurungirra ensi n'okukorera n'obwengye obukanguke.

Tukutwaangira Eitunga lyoona Ery'Okujuna Abantu aboona abari abaesimbiire abarikugyema aha Avatara ya Akwario, kuraba omu Nyegyesa y'Okutwanga Kristo erijuna abantu boona omu bubi boona.

Kuora kurungirra, orungirra ensi.

# OMUTWE GWA 3

Kubaingi obusangwa tiburiho, tibarumanya ngu tukitwanga, ngu turi abakukora, abazimbi; tukuzimba n'ekyanga kyaitu, Endanga yaitu.

Kuora twesimbiire nitwehurra obusangwa, baitu obwo buri obw'omukanya; kuora otarina obushobozi aha mutima gwawe, oriba omugondye, habwokuba tigujja kusiima n'ekintu kimwe. Kikahi kutuuraho omu Nsi hatali mugondye.

# OMUTWE GWA 4 NIGAMBA AHARI OBWESIGURE

Obwesigure butunyoma, tukwegomba kuba abesigure, baitu nibatugambira babi aha muntu kandi tugumaho twaziba, kandi naitwo tuhinduka abesigure kandi tusiinga omu babi.

Ogwo agyenda agamba amajuumu g'obusharabu, aba mubi okukira ogwo agataaho, habwokuba oyo nasobora kuba naloliremu obubi rundi obwisi. "Muserre amazima kandi garyabajuna". Baitu orihi nasobora kushiika aha Mazima Omushubaazi? Omubushwere omwo, ayoleka ekanya omu kugarukirra omuihi, Amazima.

Amazima mugisha gwa Ishe Waitu Omutakatifu, nka Ndiima. Orihi asobora kugira ndiima Omushubaazi, kuora eri ekicweka kya Ishe? Ebirabo bya Ishe tasobora kubyakiira ogwo ajwiire omubukoozi, endagiro, obwegombezi bw'obuheereza n'obugambire. Turi abagondye b'okweriika kwaitu; obanze omuntu orora n'amaisho agaziba ogwo agirra ekyo aroora omu nda; oyo natunda Egulu kandi byona birijwa kwija kuruga ahariwe.

"Orihi muesigure? Orihi akushiika aha buesigure obwesibwe? Abangahi bakwesigura? Ay!, Ay!, Ay!", (Samael). Ogwo agusha taryasobora kuba muesigure habwokuba ari omu maisho g'Omutakatifu ogwo ari Amazima gari.

# OMUTWE GWA 5 NIGAMBA AHARI ETEERA YA PENDULO

Byona bigyenda n'amaizi, bihingura, biruga n'okusiika, bigyenda nibigaruka; baitu abantu nibeesiga okugyenda kw'omuturanyi okukira okwabo kandi nibo bagyenda omu nyomyo y'obwomeezi bwabo, nibakozesa okwehurra kwabo okurimu obukoozi kukora aha ruganda rw'omuturanyi waabo; kandi ibwe? Omusaija kuora aziita yoze ziwe rundi obukoozi buwe namujuna, namujuna omu biragiro bingi, namenyeka ekireere kimwe okukira ebirala bitwingire kandi ayegomba obuesigure.

Ebiconco birija kuba by'akabi boona, kikahi tuserre omuhanda ogurikusiima.

Endago yeyekera aha kintu ekikozibwe kandi endago yeyekera aha mazima g'ekishusho. "Okuziita enshobe yentama Amazima" (Samael).

# OMUTWE GWA 6 ENDAGO N'AMAZIMA

Kikahi ngu omusomi yesige omutwe ogu kwirra kukanguka ngu orimo orengerwa enshobe ezitali zamazima; obwo tukiri n'obukoozi, endagiro n'empereza, endago zaitu zirija kuba nshobe; ekinu: "Ekyo nikwo kiri habwokuba ninyenda‚Äù, ekiri nka butama, byona birina amaisho, obutali bume, obutali bume, obutaaha n'oburuga, entuura, ebire, omuntu butama aroora ebintu omu muringo gwe, abireetera omu bushara, abatiinisa abamukwatanisa.

# OMUTWE GWA 7 DIALEKTIKA Y'OBWENGYE

Turarumanya kandi butwegesa, ngu tusobora kukanguka obwengye aha mukozi g'obwengye n'okubaisibwa okw'obwesigure.

Omwegondye w'Omuhanda naswanga ENERJI y'omutindo ogutali munene gw'obwengye kuora yeegatta n'ebyakuletera eby'obwomeezi bwe.

Omwegyesi orwesimbiire, nayetabaho omu Kizano ky'Obwomeezi, tagyenda n'ekizano ekyo, nayehurra nka murebi omu kizano ky'obwomeezi; hanu nka omu sinema, abarebi nibakwatanisa n'ogwo ashiishe rundi ogwo ashiishibwe. Omwegyesi w'Obwomeezi ni ogwo nyegesa ebintu birungi kandi eby'omugisha omu mwegondye w'omuhanda, nabakora kurunga kuruta ekyo bari, Nyina w'ensi natuurra era kandi abantu nibamugonda n'OKUGONDA.

"Obwengye Musyo ogwo obutali buroora" (Samael Aun Weor) aha gore omwesyo gw'Obwengye, ekyo kiba aha omuziba n'Omwesyo gw'Eizooba.

Kuora omuganda gw'obwengye bwaitu gukyenga, omuntu yomwene arikurora omu nda ekyo kiri kikuru.

# OMUTWE GWA 8 OLULIMI RW'OBWENGYE

Abantu omu bintu eby'okuruga omuri ensi nibatiina kandi nibarinda kubiita; obwengye nibabiranga kandi nibabita amaina magarama, kuleka abatangye kutaguma babagamba.

Harrho abantu obukaikuru abarumanya amaina g'obubi bwabo, baitu tibarumanya oku babuzibaho.

Omushaija nagyemesereza kurungi ebigyenda by'omuganda ogwo atakozere, baitu tarumanya okugyemesereza omuganda gwe: Omubiri ogwo agyenda omunda omukanya aha Kandhi; omushaija kugurumanya, ekyamuletera, omu maabaraatori n'enyanya ebihoore; baitu omushaija agambiibwe ngu akusiibure, azibe obukoozi bwe, empereza, endagiro n'ebindi, kandi tikirikwesimbiire, arikwikiriza ngu obwo kusiiba burungi.

# OMUTWE GWA 9 ANTICRISTO

Tumwitwazire omunda. Tatuleka kushiika aha Ishe Waitu Omwesimbiire. Baitu kuora twamushoborra orwesimbiire nyingi omu kushiisha kwakwe.

Anticristo nayanga emitwarre ya kristo ey'Ekyanga, Obugumiikiriza, Obucureezi, n'ebindi. "Omushaija" nayemba obwengye bwe kandi namugondera.

# OMUTWE GWA 10 OMUZIMBA GWA YOZE

Tukahi kwetorora omu mukozi omukanya aha kandhi, okurumanya kuora ekyo turimo kukurungaire, habwokuba okushwaangaaza by'abandi tikuritukorera. Kikahikuyikyo kitwetere omu kwikiriza ngu turi abashwaangaaza abesimbiire, baitu ekinu kirungi kuora tushwaangaaza obubi bwaitu omunda yaitu, okurungaire kurikutwara Kristo omulamu ogwo twitwazire omu maani okwesereza n'okurungirra omuringo gw'Abantu.

Okunyegesa okuyanga, ekyo bakirumanya boona, baitu okunyegesa OKUGONDA, ekyo nikiba kigorriire.

Soma n'amaani omusomi omwesiimwe omutwe ogu, kuora origomba okuziba obubi bwawe omuiizi.

# EMITWE 11 OKUSIINGA 20

Abantu nibakunda okuteekereza, okuboleka aha bandhi nka oku babaroora, baitu tiharriho ogwo asobora okwerumanya, ekyo kiri kikuru omu Muhanda gw'Okutwanga Kristo.

Ogwo agarukamu obushuba bwingi ari omu mulingo; Omwesyo ni obwengye kandi kuora gweyoleka omunda yaitu, gukora omukozi ogusobora. "Omu mikozi yabo murabarumanya", nikwo Yesu Kristo agarukiire.

Tagarukiire ngu omu byaro byoona. Gunositi... mukanguke!!!

Omushaija omuzima rundi orikwehurra nayetwarra omu mwazi gwe rundi okwehurra kundi. Ebyo nka abalamuzi nibatiinisa, baruura ekyo kibaleta n'omugisha kandi bacwera rundi baha nka mazima okuruga ahari Ruhanga, ekyo Omushubaazi omukuru okubakakakurra.

Omuri harrho omwesyo, harrho obwengye. Okurugira ni mukozi gw'omurima, ekyo tikirikuruga omu mweeso.

Omu mutwe gwa 12 nigugamba aha mizima isatu eyitwazire: Omuzima ogurikwehurra rundi egy'okwehurra, Omuzima Oguri hagati; ogu nikwikiriza byona ebyo aruurire kandi gacwera okuruga aha oshiishe rundi omujuna; kuora gurongoirwe obwengye, guriba omuhagazima omusobora, guhinduka muganda gw'omukozi; ebintu ebirindiriize omu muzima oguri hagati nitwo twikire.

Ogwo atungire ndiima yamazima, tahi kwija kwikiriza; omushubaazi taryasobora kutunga ndiima, omugisha gwa Ruhanga n'okwesiga obumwe, rundi omuzima oguri omunda, ogwo turorra kuora turimo turaziita ebyo abatayegomba ebyo twitwazire omu Muzimu gwaitu.

Omugisha gw'okurumanya obukoozi bwaitu, bwanyima tukukikora n'amaani kandi bwanyima okubuziba n'obuhwezi bwa nyina waitu RAM-IO, gutuleka okuhinduka hatali bagondye omu batemba abo abakuruga omu kwikiriza kwa boona.

Yoze, Ego, ni obutahiizire omunda yaitu; SERI yoona yitwazire amaani okutaho obutebe omu nda yaitu, omu Muzimu gwaitu.

Omu kwega omutwe gwa 13, niturorra ekyo kiba aha Muroori atayesimbiire, kuora yasangire Yoze abatarikwegombwa b'obuwerezibwe bwa buri owaitu omu Muhanda. Kuora turimo twetorora tukareka okugamba babi aha muntu.

SERI n'OBWENGYE, kikahi okwehwerangana omurundi gumu; nukwo obumanya buzaarwa. Okurumanya, hatari omanya Serri, nakuleta kweshobanya kw'omuzima kw'omulingo gyoona; omubi azaarwa.

Kuora SERI esinga OKURUMANYA, omuhweza muzindire azaarwa. Omutwe gwa 14 nigutuha endimi kwetorora; Turi Ruhanga Omukuru, n'ekitongole okwetora ekitarikukwata ahaigwa; okureka byona ebyo nibwo kujuna kandi bagambire...

"Ekibi kyija n'ekanisa y'Omucweri, n'ekanisa y'Omwegyesi, n'ekigambo ky'omwesengeezi, n'ekizuukizo kya Mukama kandi n'ekanisa ya Kristo" (Samael).

Nyina Waitu Omukuru Marah, Maria rundi RAM-IO nka oku tumweeta Abagunositi, ni omukwaniriza hagati ya Ishe Waitu Omwesimbiire naitwo, omukwaniriza hagati ya Ruhanga w'eby'obutama omu nsi n'omurozi; omu nda yawe n'omumwa gwe, eby'obutama bitugondera. Ni Omugisha gwaitu Omukuru, omukwaniriza hagati ya Ruhanga Omukazi Omuhurra ogw'ensi n'omuganda gwaitu gw'ebiro, okushiika aha by'ekitangaaza n'okukorera abo batutwangaaziize.

Omu kwehwerangana kw'Eby'omurango n'Omukazi Omuleevi, omushaija ahinduka omukazi kandi omukazi ahinduka omushaija; Nyina waitu RAM-IO yomwenka asobora kugarura omukungu g'obwomeezi omu yoze zaitu n'ebijunga byayo. N'ebiro birikwehurra titusobora okurumanya ebintu bya Serri, habwokuba okwehurra muganda gurimu obubi bwingi, nka mwine; kikahi okukurra, kuziita omunda obukoozi, endagiro, okwegomba, okwegomba, na byona ebyo bigonda omuzima, ekyo kituletela okugamburura.

Omu mutwe gwa 18 nituroora, okurugarira aha kiragiro ky'obwiire, ngu nka oku turi omuri ensi rundi omu mwanya gw'ensi, nukwo omuiizi omunda omwanya gw'obwengye ogwo nitusangirra. Soma omusomi omwesiimwe omutwe ogu murungi ngu orurumanye omunda omu rubaju oruhi ogwo origyeenda.

Kuora tukukozesa Nyina waitu Omukuru RAM-IO tuziita Yoze zaitu za shitani kandi titujuna omu biragiro 96 eby'obwengye, omu bubi bwingi. Okwanga tikutuleka kwongyerra omunda.

Omushubaazi akora ekibi aha Ishe yomwene kandi omufuumu okukora ekibi aha Mwoyo Orweera; okufuumu omu mutima, ekigambo n'omukozi.

Harho abatemba abagamba oburungi bwabo, nibahwamu abatangye bingi, baitu, kuora, babakora, nitusangirra okushwaangaaza n'obutahiizire; obwomeezi bunyemeerwa okubasenga nobutabareebwa.

Omu mutwe gwa 19, gutuha omwesyo okutatwa omu kujweka ngu turi abesobora. Turi abegyesi boona omu buwerezi bw'Avatara; omuleevi akumya kumushiisha kandi omu butama okumutendeka. Kuora turamanyire ngu obwomeezi bukahikuziba, kuora orimo akutuhwereza omu mukozi ogwo muremirre kirungi okwebaza.

Ndiima ni okurumanya okuri, obwengye obumwesigwa obwokuruga ahari Serri, "ebicweka by'obwengye egoica biraizire nka ebicweka ebyatwetwa ebitamiizi" (Samael).

Omu mutwe gwa 20, gutuha endimi okuziita obutiti bw'omwazi ogwo nitugyenda.

# EMITWE OKURUGA 21 OKUSIINGA 29

Omu 21 nigugamba nigutunyegesa okuteekereza n'okujukura, okurumanya okuhinduka. Orikutalumanya okuteekereza taryasobora kwesha Ego.

Omu 22 nigugamba aha "OKUGARUKA N'OKUGARUKA". Ni kyangu okugamba aha kugaruka; kuora taturikugomba kugaruka omu byalo by'oburumi, kikahi kushwaangaaza Yoze, ezo zitulagana; tutunyegesa okurungirra omutindo gw'abana baitu. Okugaruka kukwata aha byakuletera eby'obwomeezi bwaitu, kuora tukiri n'omubiri.

Kristo w'omunda ni omuriro gw'omuriro; ekyo turikuroora nitwehurra ni ekitundu ky'omubiri gw'omuriro gwa Kristo. Obujweka bw'omuriro gwa Kristo nikyo kintu ekikuru kyokutwa omu bwomeezi bwaitu, omuriro ogu gutwara ebintu byoona eby'emigongo yaitu rundi emigongo, ekyo butaahi kusiibura n'ebintu 5 eby'Obutama, okwetonderra obuwerezibwe bwa Nyina Waitu Omukuru RAMIO.

"Omutandisi kahikuyega kutuura n'akabi; nukwo kyahandiikibwe".

Omu mutwe gwa 25, Omwegyesi natugambirra aha rubaju rwaitu oruturumanyire, ogwo turagiza nka turimo tukokera ekizano, kandi nukwo, nituroora obukoozi bwaitu aha langi y'abandi.

Byona ebyo bitwoleka aha kwetonderra okwaboneebwa; nka oku okwehurra kwaitu kusubba nukwo turi abashubaazi; okwehurra kwahishwe kuletera obushwaanzi kuora tukukakanguka hatari kuziita obukoozi bwaitu.

Omu mutwe gwa 26 nigugamba aha bagonye basatu, abazigaine ba Hiram Abiff, Kristo w'Omunda, amadaimoni ga: 1.- Omurimo 2.- Obushara obubi 3.- Okwegomba

Buri omwe omuri itwo twitwazire omu Muzimu gwaitu abagonye basatu.

Nigutunyegesa ngu Kristo w'Omunda ari obwerra n'okwesimbiira, natuhwerreza okukuba obutaaga obukaikuru obwo twitwazire omunda. Omu mutwe ogwo gutunyegesa ngu Kristo ow'Ekihama Mukama w'OBWESIGURE OBUNENE, agaanwa abalevi, abakuru n'abandiizi b'eka.

Omu mutwe gwa 28, nigugamba aha Omushaija-Omukuru n'obutamanya bugwiire bwa bantu aha igwa.

Emiganda y'Omuntu okuhinduka omu Omushaija-Omukuru ni kurwana n'okurwana hatali nandi, hali n'ensi n'ebintu byona ebikurungirra ensi y'obutaaga.

Omu mutwe gwa 29, omutwe oguri ahansi, nigugamba aha EKIKOMBE EKIHURRI, ekihamu kya Hermes, omuganda gwa Salomoni; Ekikombe Ekihurri nikiteekereza omu muringo gume aha Yoni Omukazi, okuranga, soma y'abahwezi ogwo Ruhanga arikunywa.

Omuganda ogu ogw'oburungi gutaheerana omu Ka yomuringo gwona, rundi omu bwomeezi bw'Omuleevi Omugunositi.

Kuora Abagunositi baramanziize ekiyama ekyi, kiryahindurra obwomeezi bwabo obw'omuganda kandi ekyala ekiraro kirijakuba omuwerezi okukorera nka muleevi omu Ka erikugonda.

Omirembe ogurungi guhiirra omutima gwawe.

GARGHA KUICHINES
