ENYEFUNGUZA
Kuruga omu: V.M. GARGHA KUICHINES

"OKUGUMUKA KWA MAANI" okw'Omwegyesa Omukuru Samael Aun Weor kworekana gye omuringo ogwetura omu bwomeezi.

Kihikire kumenya ebyo byona ebitutaire aha bintu by'obushuhe omu bwomeezi obu.

Hano tukunganiize eby'okwegesa bya buri mutwe kworeka omugumiisa orikwijuka omu Rugamba okurwisa iwe mwenyewe.

Enshonga zona eziri omu kitabo eki zirikutwara aha kucwekyereza Ebinye byaitu, kubaasa kureka Omubazi ogwo nigwo gukuru omuriitwe.

Ekinye tikirikwenda kufa kandi nyini kyo naarowoora ngu ndi munaku kukira enshobi.

Omu nsi omuriho abantu abatakwasisa kandi obwoba nibureeta akabi ahonaaho.

"TIHARIIHO KINTU EKITARIKWIKUBAASA, EKIRIHO N'ABANTU ABATAKWASISA".

OMUTWE 1

Obuntu tiburine burungi bw'omunda; ebyaheza nibicwekyereza byona. Embabazi tizimanyirwe. Obukara buine abakuratsi. Obuheereza tiburiho ahabw'okuba abantu barikugumirwa kandi barikucwekyera.

Omugisa gw'abarikugumirwa guri omu mikono y'abacumuzi ab'emiringo yoona.

OMUTWE 2

Enjara n'okucwekyera nibikura obutoosha na butoosha kandi ebintu by'obushangwa nibicwekyereza omwoya gw'ensi, baitu hariho omuti gw'okukiza ekibi ekiturukworekereza: "Obutahikanwa obwa Sayansi" nari okukoresa embibo y'omuntu turiyo tugihindura AMANI omu kisibo kyaitu ky'omuntu bwanyima turiyo tugihindura Omushana n'Omuriro twiga okukwata aha bintu bisatu eby'okuziikura obwengye: 1. Okufa kw'enshobi zaitu. 2. Kukora emibiri y'izuba omuriitwe. 3. Kukuriza Omunaku Omufuzi (Obuntu).

Itaka, amaizi n'omwoya, nibihuma ahabw'obubi bw'okukura kw'abantu kw'omuriizo; ezaabu y'ensi yoona tikurikukwasa kworekereza obubi; tukukoresa ezaabu y'amaizi eyi twena turikukora, embibo yaitu yenyine, turiyo tugikoresa obwengye n'okumanya ensonga, obwo nitwongyera amaani okuhumuza ensi n'okukora n'obwengye obuziikwire.

Turi kukora Eggye ry'Okurokora Ensi yoona n'abantu boona abagumire abarikuhingura ebyanzi n'omuhabuzi wa Acuario, kuraba omu By'okwegesa by'Obukristo oburikuturokora kuruga omu bubi bwona.

Waagihumuza iwe, humuza ensi.

OMUTWE 3

Ahabw'abantu abingi obushemererwa tiburiho, tibarikumanya ngu kikorwa kyaitu, ngu nitwe tubukora, abombeki; turiyo tubwombeka n'ezaabu y'amaizi yaitu, Embibo yaitu.

Obuturikushemererwa nitwehurira twine obushemererwa, baitu obwo butoosha nibubura; otakwatire aha mente yaawe y'ensi, noija kuba omwijuka wayo, ahabw'okuba terikwerarikirira n'akantu koona. Kihikire kutuura omu Nsi hatari kuba Omwijuka wayo.

OMUTWE 4 GURIKUGAMBA AHA BWIKIRIZA

Obwikiriza nibutushusha, tukurarikira kuba abeikirize, baitu nibatugambira kubi aha muntu kandi tukakwatwa kandi obwo nituhinduka abaremesire kandi turayo twahinduka ababi.

Orikuddamu ebigambo ebibi, nayija kuba mubi kukira ogwo ogabihendera, ahabw'okuba ogu asobora kukora ahabw'obujunaanwa, eihari nari okushobya. Omuddumizi nakikora nk'omwegeswa orikwesiga ekibi, nomubi omu maani. "Muserre Obumanzi kandi noija kubara omweikirize". Baitu noija kubaasa kutwara ota omu Bumanzi omubeesi? Omu mbeera ezo naraye aheereza obutoosha kuruga omubugu bw'eihangiro, Obumanzi.

Obumanzi n'ekitiinisa kya Taata Orikutendekwa gye, nk'okwikiriza. Noija kubaasa kutunga ota okwikiriza omubeesi, yaaba eky'obusaasizi kuruga aha Taata? Ebirabo bya Taata tarikusobora kubitunga ogwo aijwire enshobi, emicwe, obwetaagi bw'amaani n'omuntu okuremesa. Turi abajuka b'ebirukumirirwa byaitu; yihura ahari orikureeba kurungi orikugamba ebyo areeba omu nda; ogwo arikutunda Eguru kandi byona noija kubigyibwaho. "Niwe ow'eikirize? Niwe atungire eby'amaani eby'eikirize? Bahaabw'obukama bangahe? Abee!, Abee!, Abee!", (Samael). Ogwo orikubeesa tarikubaasa kuba omweikirize ahabw'okuba ari ahagati y'ogwo Orikutendekwa ogwo ni Obumanzi obucweraine.

OMUTWE 5 GURIKUGAMBA AHA MATEEKA G'EKIRAGO

Byona nibigenda kandi bigaruka, bigamba kandi bigwa, bigenda kandi biza; baitu aha bantu nibenda kukira obugenda n'okugaruka bw'omuturani okukira obugenda n'okugaruka bwabo kandi obwo nibagendera omu nyanja y'obuzibu y'obwomeezi bwabo, barikukoresa obwengye bwabo obuhwereire kwerekereza okukubagana kw'omuturani wabo; kandi weena ki? Omuntu obwaaita ebikye bye nari enshobi nayijuka, naajukira kuruga omu mateeka amangi ag'engyenda, namenya ekihimba kimu kuruga omu birindi bingi ebitukora kandi nahurira obwetaagi bw'obwikiriza.

Enkomerero zona noija kuba mbi obutoosha, tuteekwa kusera omu ihangiro, ebipingano.

Ensonga neegonda omu by'okufeerwa omu maisho g'ekikorwa ekikozirwe kandi eky'okumanya nikibura omu bumanzi obutangaaine. "Kwonka okugyaho enshobi nibukwireeta Obumanzi" (Samael).

OMUTWE 6 EKY'OKUMANYA N'ENSONGA

Nikikwasa omushomi kwega gye omu mutwe ogu kukwirizayo kwezirika okugyemwa n'ebyo akuratiziise ebihwereire; twiine enshobi z'obwengye, emicwe, obusango, eby'okumanya byaitu nazo noija kuba ebihwereire; eki: "Ekyo nikyo kityo ahabw'okuba nimanyire", nikya busitooro, byona biine obusitooro, ebyanzi, okukubagana, eiguru n'omunsi, amamera, obutoosha, ahabw'okuba omusitooro omucwekeraine naareeba ebintu omu muringo gwe, nabiboha n'amaani, naatiinisa abamuhurikiza.

OMUTWE 7 ENSONGA Z'OBWENGYE

Tumanyire kandi ekyo nikitwegesa, ngu nitusobora kwongyera obwengye turiyo tukora ebikorwa eby'obwengye n'ebyo turikugumirwa kuronda.

Orikwesiga Engira n'acwekyereza AMANI z'obuhingo butooto bw'obwengye obwaayijuka n'ebyabaho by'obwomeezi bwe.

Omwegesa atungire, orikutwara omu Rugambo rw'Obwomeezi, tarikujuka n'orugambo orwo, naehurira nk'omureebi omu kibaare ky'obwomeezi; omu nda nk'omuri sinema, abareebi nibakwatanisa n'omuroki nari n'omurokirwe. Omwegesa w'Obwomeezi niwe orikwegesa ebintu birungi kandi eby'omugaso aha mwesiga w'engira, nabakora kuba abaranjire kukira ebyo bari, Mama Ntaho naamuha obugambirizi kandi abantu nibamukurata n'OKUKUNDA.

"Obwengye n'Omushana ogwo omuntu atarikubona" (Samael Aun Weor) ekitindire nikimubaho n'Omushana gw'Obwengye, ekyo omukugu n'Omushana gw'Izuba.

Obutwongyera oburemwa bw'obwengye bwaitu, iwe mwenyewe nobyetegyereza omu nda ekikuru, ekiriho.

OMUTWE 8 ORURIMI RWA SAYANSI

Abantu omumaisho g'eby'okwangaazya bya Ntaho nibatiina kandi nibarinda ngu bigende; sayansi nebiranga kandi nebiha amaziina agazibu, kubaasa kuleka abatooro batakuratiziise okubatondera.

Hariho obukaikuru bw'ebintu ebirikumanya eiziina ly'obubi bwabo, baitu tibarikumanya oku obukucwekyereza.

Omuntu nakwata kurungi aha motoka ezikuru ezi akora, baitu tarikumanya okukwata aha motoka yawe: Omubiri ogwo agenderaho obutoosha na butoosha; omuntu okugumanya, nikimumanyisa, ekyo okukora ekisibo n'ebyangaazi byayo; baitu omuntu nagambirwa ngu agihumye, naaita enshobi ze, emicwe, obubi, nibwo, kandi tarikubaasa, naikiriza ngu n'okwona buri izooba nikikwase.

OMUTWE 9 ANTICRISTO

Nitumutwara omu nda. Titurikutureka kutwaara Taata Orikutendekwa gye. Baitu obwatumucwerekyereza byona nayija kuba mungi omu kureeta kwabo.

Anticristo naayanga ebintu by'Obukristo eby'okwikiriza, Obuheereza, Obucureezi, nibwo. "Omuntu" naaramya sayansi ye kandi naagugondera.

OMUTWE 10 EKIRORO KY'OBWENGYE

Tuteekwa kureeba omu bikorwa obutoosha na butoosha, kumanya waaba ebyo turikukora nibituhumuza, ahabw'okuba okucwekyereza okw'ahali tikuritukwasa. Ekyo nikitureeta kwikiriza ngu turi abacwekyereza barungi, baitu eki nikirungi obuturiyo tucwekyereza obubi bwaitu omuriitwe, okutuhumuza kurikurondera Omukristo omwomeezi ogwo turiyo tutwaara omu maani okutangaaza n'okuhumuza omuringo gw'Abantu.

Kwegesa okwanga, ekyo bakimanyire, baitu okwegesa OKUKUNDA, ekyo kikorwa kigumu.

Soma gye omushomi murungi omutwe ogu, waaza kukora enshobi okuruga aha mizi.

EMITWE 11 KUKIRA 20

Abantu nibakunda kugamba, okworeka abandi nk'oku babareeba, baitu tihariho orikwenda kumenya we, ekyo nikyo kirikumara omu Ngira y'Obukristo.

Orikugamba amabeera amangi nayija kuba ari omu mutindo; Omushana n'obwengye kandi obu burikworekana omuriitwe, nukukora omulimo mukuru. "Ahabw'emilimo yaabo noijuka". Yagamba Yesu Omukristo.

Taragamba ngu ahabw'ebiroko ebi barakora. Abagnostici mukanguke!!!

Omuntu orikwega nari ow'obwengye nakora kurikurondera eby'okwega byabo nari obwengye. Aba nk'abacwezi baba abatiinisa, bahurira ebibarikukwenda kandi bacwezi nari baba nk'obumanzi bwa Ruhanga, ekyo Omubeesi omukuru kukira bo naabanyweza.

Ahonda hariho omushana, hariho obwengye. Okugamba kubi n'omulimo gw'omwirima, ekyo tikirikuruga omu mushana.

Omu mutwe 12 gurikugamba aha mente isatu ezitwiine: Mente yoona nari ey'ebintu, Mente y'Ahagati; Egi niyo erikwikiriza ebyona ebi ahurira kandi acwezi kurikurondera ogwo arookire nari ogwo arookirwe; obu burikutwaarwa obwengye, n'omuhereza orikutendekwa, nahinduka ekikoreso ky'ekikorwa; ebintu ebirikuterwa omu mente y'ahagati nibikora ebikurutendereza byaitu.

Otwine okwikiriza okw'obumanzi, tarikwetaaga kwikiriza; omubeesi tarikutunga okwikiriza, ekitiinisa kya Ruhanga n'okubaho obwomeezi, nari mente y'omunda, eyo turaibura obutwaha ebintu ebi tutarikwenda ebi tutwaara omu bwengye bwaitu.

Omugisa gw'okumanya enshobi zaitu, bwanyima tukazetegyereza kandi bwanyima tukazicwekyereza n'obuhereza bwa mama waitu RAM-IO, nibiturekereza okuhindura kandi hatari kuba abajuka b'abamanzi abarikugenda omu ndongo z'aha buri kimu ekirikutendereza.

Ekinye, Ego, n'akasumiirizo omuriitwe; Omuntu wenye nikwe arina amaani okusimbaho endago omuriitwe, omu bwengye bwaitu.

Kuruga omu kwega okw'amaani omutwe 13, nitumanya ebyorikubaho aha Mureebi Omucwekeraine, obu atungire ebikye ebi tutarikwenda by'omunyanyazi wena w'Engira. Obuturikureeba twena nituleka kugamba kubi aha muntu.

Omuntu n'Okumanya, tuteekwa kubahindura aha gundi; obwo nibuzaara okwetegyereza. Okumanya, hatari okumanya Omuntu, kureeta okusoberwa kw'obwengye okw'emiringo yoona; omubibi nazaara.

Yaaba Omuntu mukuru kukira Okumanya, omuntu omucureezi nazaara. Omutwe 14 nigutuha enshonga ezirikukwasa okwetegyereza; Turi Ruhanga omutengeire, n'abantu abamwetororeize abatamurikukwata; okuleka ebyo byona n'okwijuka kandi bagambe...

"Enshobi neyambara omukago gw'Omucwezi, n'omukago gw'Omwegesa, n'obugarukiro bw'omusabi, n'omukago gw'Omukama kandi kuhika n'omukago gw'Omukristo" (Samael).

Nyina itwe Ow'Omutima ogusangwa Marah, Maria nari RAM-IO nk'oku tumuhunga abagnostici, n'omubari aha Taata Orikutendekwa gye naitwe, omubari aha Ruhanga omutabani ab'emirembe ya Ntaho n'omuhinguriizi; kuraba omu nda n'omu nda weena, abemirembe ya Ntaho nibatugondera. N'omutengeere Deva waitu, omubari ahagati y'Omukurasi Omutendekwa Mama w'ensi n'emotoka yenyine, okutunga eby'ekitangaaza ebi turikukora n'okukorera abantu baitu.

Kuruga omu kweterana n'omukazi Omuhinguriizi, omusaija neehinduka omukazi kandi omukazi neehinduka omusaija; Mama waitu RAM-IO niwe wenka orikusobora kugarura obuhu bw'ebirembe omu Bikye byaitu n'ab'eggye yaabo. N'ebikoreso ebirikwetenga titusobora kumanya ebintu by'Omuntu, ahabw'okuba ebyo nibikoreso ebiringwa, ebijwire enshobi, nk'oku nyini kyo ari; nikwetenga okubyihuraho, okwita omuriitwe enshobi, emicwe, obubi, okukwata, obwetaagi, n'ebyona ebi mente y'ensi erikukunda, ebirikutuha oburoko bwingi.

Omu mutwe 18 tureebe, kurikurondera amateeka g'obubiri, ngu nk'oku turikutuura omu ihanga nari omumera gw'ensi, obwo omuriitwe okubaho ekihuro aha muringo ogwetura nitusangwa turiyo.

Soma omushomi murungi omutwe ogurikushusha ebi kukumanya omu nda aha disiturikiti, omunsi nari omumera ogwo otusangwa.

Obuturikukoresa Mama waitu omutengeere RAM-IO nitucwekyereza ebinye byaitu ebitaroori kandi nitwijuka omu mateeka 96 z'obwengye, kuruga omu kubora okwingi. Okwanga tikuritulekera kukura omu nda.

Omubeesi nacumuza omumaisho ga Taata we yenyine kandi omusambazi narokera Omwoyo Orikwera; naasamba omu sente, omu kigambo n'omulimo.

Hariho abamanzi abarikugamba eby'ekitangaaza byabo, bakundwa abatooro abingi, baitu yaaba, tuketegyereza omulimo gwabo, tusanga okucwekyereza n'okuremesa; obwomeezi wenye nari bwaheereza okubahukya n'okubeebwa.

Omu mutwe 19, nigutuha omushana okutagwa omu bwengye bw'okwehura okukira. Twena turi abesomi aha buheereza bw'omuhabuzi; omukara narumuura obumushobeza kandi omusitooro ngu batamutendeke.

Obutwetegyereza ngu okubaho tuteekwa kukucwekyereza, waaba hariho orikutukwasa omu mulimo ogwo oragumire n'okusiima.

Okwikiriza n'okumanya okucwereire, obwengye obwomeezi bwenyine Omuntu, "ebirooto by'obwengye bwi Ego nibyegarabize omu birooto ebirikureetera omu miti" (Samael).

Omu mutwe 20, nigutuha enshonga okucwekyereza obwire bwa Ruhanga omu byo turikukura n'okukura.

EMITWE KURUGA 21 KUHIKA 29

Omu 21 nigutugambira kandi nigutwegesa okuteekereza n'okucwa, okumanya okuhindura. Otarikumanya kuteekereza tarikubaasa kusebeza Ego.

Omu 22 nigutugambira aha "OKUGARUKA N'OKUDDUMIZA". Ni kyangu okugamba nk'oku gutugambira aha kugaruka; tutarikwenda kuddamu abaremi abamanyire, tuteekwa kusebeza Ebinye, ebyo bitutworeke; nitwegesebwa okuhumuza omutindo gw'abaana baitu. Okuddumiza nikugenderera aha byabaho by'obwomeezi bwaitu, obutwiine omubiri gwenyine.

Omukristo ahambire n'omuriro gw'omuriro; ebyo tureeba kandi twetegyereza n'omutwe gw'omubiri gw'omuriro gw'Obukristo.

Okwija kw'omuriro gw'Obukristo n'ekikorwa ekikuru omu bwomeezi bwaitu bwenyine, omuriro ogu nagukwatira aha bikorwa byona eby'ebihimba byaitu nari obwengye, ebyo tureekwa kubihumya n'ebintu 5 eby'Obutonde, turikukoresa obuhereza bwa Mama waitu Omutendekwa RAMIO.

"Omusangizi ateekwa kwega okutuura omu kabi; obwo nikyo kihaandikire".

Omu mutwe 25, Omwegesa nagutugambira aha bugu obutarikumanywa obwitu, obwo tubuterekereza nk'otukora mashini erikuterekereza sinema, kandi obwo, tureeba enshobi zaitu omu kibaare ky'ahali.

Ebyo byona nibyoreka aha bamanzi abashobire; nk'oku obwengye bwaitu butubeesa obwo nitubeesa; ebigendereerwa by'obuhisho nibireeta akabi obututaziikura tutaite enshobi zaitu.

Omu mutwe 26 nigutugambira aha beesi basatu, abanzi ba Hiram Abiff, Omukristo Omunda, emisambwa ya: 1.- Emente 2.- Okwenda kubi 3.- Obwetaagi

Buri omwiitwe titwaara omu bwengye bwaitu abeasi basatu.

Nitwegesa ngu Omukristo Omunda oburaire obucweraine n'obuhikire, natukwasa okusengura obukaikuru bw'ebintu ebi tutarikwenda ebi tutwaara omu nda. Omu mutwe ogwo nitwegesebwa ngu Omukristo ekihugo n'Omukama w'OKUGUMUKA KUKURU, akayangirwa Abahinguriizi, abakuru n'abaandiiki b'eyekalu.

Omu mutwe 28, nagutugambira aha Muntu-Mukuru n'okutooro okwingi ahari we.

Ebironda by'Omuntu kuba omuntu mukuru n'entaro n'entaro kurwisa iwe, kurwisa ensi n'okurwisa byona ebi turikureeta omu nsi.

Omu mutwe 29, omutwe gw'enkomerero, nagutugambira aha Grial Orikwera, endaho ya Hermes, endaho ya Salomón; Grial Orikwera neegamba omu muringo gw'ekihugo aha Yoni y'omukazi, okusamba, soma y'abarikwekwata aha Ruhanga ahu Abakristo Abatengeire bari kunywa.

Endaho egi y'okushemererwa tesobora kubura omu Yekalu yoona ey'ebihugo, nari omu bwomeezi bw'Omuhinguriizi Omunyegnostic.

Abagnostici obubaretgyereza eki kihugo, noija kuhindura obwomeezi bwabo bw'omugole kandi eky'okuramya ekiriho nibukora okumuheereza nk'omuhinguriizi omu Yekalu y'Obukama.

Ngu obusinge oburi omunda buhingure omu mutima gwaawe.

GARGHA KUICHINES.
