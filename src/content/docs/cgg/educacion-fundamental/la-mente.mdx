Kuraba omu byahitiiremu, nitukwetegyereza ngu tikirikubaasika kwetegyereza EKIRIKUGAMBWA ngu NI ORUKUNDO, okuhitsya obu turaabe twetegyereize omu muringo ogw'OKUJUMUURA ebizibu by'OBWENGE oburikukomire.

Abarikuteekateeka ngu OBWENGE nibwo BWONGO, nibakyerarikira munonga. OBWENGE ni AMANI, buzire, nibubaasa kwehakana omubiri, nibubaasa omu mbeera zimwe ez'okucwekyereza nari omu kuroota kwaburiijo, kweteengyereza ahantu ha hare munonga kureeba n'okuhurra ebirikugyenda nibibaho omuri ebyo bibanza.

Omu maraburatwari g'EKICONGO, eby'okucwekyereza birakolebwa aha bantu abari omu MBEERA Y'OKUCWEKYEREZA.

Abantu baingi omu MBEERA Y'OKUCWEKYEREZA, babaasize kutooreza obuhame bw'ebyahitiireho, abantu n'embeera ezibaireho omu butami bwabo bw'okucwekyereza hare.

Abashomesa babaasize kwehameeza bwanyima y'ebyo by'okucwekyereza, obuhame bw'EBYO BY'AMAKURU. Babaasize kwetegyereza obuhame bw'ebyahitiireho, obuhame bw'EBYAHITIIREHO.

N'ebyo by'okucwekyereza by'amaraburatwari g'EKICONGO, kihameeziibwe kimazire omu kureeba n'ebyahitiireho ngu BWONGO tibwo BWENGE.

Eky'amazima kandi okuruga omu mazima goona nitubaasa kugamba ngu obwenge nibubaasa kuteengyereza omu bwire n'omwanya, n'okwehakana obwongo, kureeba n'okuhurra ebintu ebirikugyenda nibibaho omuri ebyo bibanza ebiherize.

EKY'AMAZEERI ky'OKUREEBA KWA HAAHI ERIYO KWERAKURIRA KITIINISA kyahamiziibwe kimazire kandi ni omusheeki wenka nari omutsyozi, orikubaasa kwerarikira kuhakana amazima g'OKUREEBA KWA HAAHI.

Obwongo bukoleirweho kwombeka okuteekateeka kwonka tibwo buteekateeka. Obwongo bwo bwonyini n'ekirikukozesebwa ky'OBWENGE, tibwo bwenge.

Nitwetenga kwega obwenge omu muringo ogurikukomire, twaba nitwenda kumanya omu muringo ogw'OKUJUMUURA ekyo ekirikugambwa ngu NI ORUKUNDO.

Abaana n'abato, abashaija n'abakazi, baine obwenge obutereire, oburikukwatanisa, oburikwihuta, oburikweteengyeereza, n'ebindi.

Abaana na bato baingi nibashemererwa kubuuza abazaire baabo n'abegyesi, aha bintu nk'ebyo nari nk'ebyo, nibenda kumanya ekirikukiraho nibenda kumanya kandi ahabw'ekyo nibabuuza, nibareeba, nibareeba obuhame obumwe obu abakuru barikugaya nari batarikureeba.

Nk'oku emyaka erikugyenda neeyita, nk'oku turikugyenda nitukura, obwenge nibugyenda nibukwatana butunu butunu.

Obwenge bw'abakuru buhamiireho, bukubiire, tiburikuhinduka n'amashasi.

Abakuru bakuzire bataihi kandi nibafa bataihi, tibarikuhinduka, boona nibabihika kuruga aha kashemeire kamwe akahamiireho.

"OKUKURAARA" kw'abakuru, ebicweka byabo, ebyeteengo ebihamiireho, n'ebindi. byoona hamwe nibisaana nk'EBARE, EIBARE eritarikuhinduka omu muringo gwona. Ahabw'ekyo omugani gwa buliijo nigugamba "EMBAGO Y'OMUNTU EREKWA OMU KINIGA KWE".

Kikuru munonga ngu abegyesi abarikukoresa OKUTUNGA KW'ABANYASHOOMERO, beegye obwenge omu muringo ogurikukomire, ahabw'okubaasa kwegesa ebihe by'omumaisho omu buringaniza.

Kirikuguma omutima kwetegyereza omu muringo ogurikukomire, nk'oku obwire burikugyenda nibuhita nibukwatana OBWENGE butunu butunu.

OBWENGE niyo enyitsi y'EKY'AMAZIMA, eky'amazima. OBWENGE nibusiisika ORUKUNDO.

Orikukura naba atakibasa KUKUNDA ahabw'okuba obwenge bwe buijwiire ebyahitiiremu ebirikuguma omutima, ebicweka, ebyeteengo ebihamiireho nk'amacumu g'omutali, n'ebindi.

Hariho abakuru abataikiriza abarikuteekateeka ngu nibabasa KUKUNDA HATI, kwonka ebirikubaho n'okuba abakuru abo baijwiire okwetenga okw'ebishuba kw'obukuru kandi nibashobya OKWETENGA n'ORUKUNDO.

"OMUKURU ORIKUGAYA" goona n"OMUKURU-MUKAZI ORIKUGAYA" boona nibayita omu mbeera z'obushushu ez'amatsiko amaingi batakafa kandi nibateekateeka ngu ekyo nikyo ORUKUNDO.

ORUKUNDO rw'abakuru teririkubaasika ahabw'okuba obwenge bururisiisika n"OKUKURAARA EBYETEENGO EBIHAMIIREHO", "EBIKWEKA", "EBYAHITIIREMU", "OKWIJUKA", okwetenga okw'ebishuba, n'ebindi n'ebindi n'ebindi.

OBWENGE niyo mbeeyi erikukirayo ya RUKUNDO. Omu mahanga AGAHWERAIRE munonga ORUKUNDO tikirikubaho ahabw'okuba obwenge bw'abantu nibahumurira engyero, ebibaaza bya bbanka, petroli na celuloide.

Hariho ebicupa bingi by'obwenge kandi obwenge bw'omuntu weena burikugyenda nibubaho.

Abamwe baine OBWENGE oburikugenda omu BUKOMUNIISTI OBUTASHOOBEREKA, abamwe baine oburikugenda omu BUKAPITAALI OBUTASHUMURIRA.

Hariho abarikugira OBWENGE BURAABE omu buhahiye, omu kwanga, omu kwetenga okuba omugurusi, omu mwanya murungi ogw'obuntu, omu kwesiba omu bantu abamwe, omu kwesiba aha magezi gaabo, omu bizibu byabo by'eka, n'ebindi n'ebindi n'ebindi.

Abantu nibakunda kuzaaho OBWENGE, Abantu batari banyingi n'abarikucwamu okugarukaho okutegyereza okugarukamu ebiceebire.

Nitwetenga KUGWAHA, OBWENGE kwonka abantu nibakunda obuhuuku, kikuru munonga okushanga omuntu omu magara ataine OBWENGE buraabe.

Abegyesi nibasheeshereza abaana baabo byona ebyo. Nibasheeshereza ebihe by'omumaisho kweshera obwenge bwabo bwonyini, okubureeba, okubwetegyereza, nikwo twabaasa kwerinda obwenge kukwatana, kwesiiba, kwesiiba.

Ekintu kimwe kyoonka ekirikubaasa kuhindura ensi n'ekyo ekirikugambwa ngu NI ORUKUNDO, kwonka obwenge nibusiisika ORUKUNDO.

Nitwetenga KWEYEGA obwenge bwaitu bwonyini, okubwetegyereza, okubweshera omu muringo ogurikukomire, okubwetegyereza omu mazima. Nikwo twabaasa, nikwo turikwija kuba bagurusi baabo, obwenge bwaitu bwonyini, nitwija kwita enyitsi y'ORUKUNDO kandi nitwija kushemererwa omu mazima.

Abo abarikutuura nibaroota aha RUKUNDO, abo abarikutuura nibakora omuko gwa ORUKUNDO, abo abarikwenda ngu ORUKUNDO rukore nikirugiirira aha miringo yaabo n'ebitarikubashemeza, emiko n'emirabyo, emiringo n'ebicweka, ebijukuzo n'ebyayitiireho, n'ebindi tibarikubaasa kumanya ekyo orukundo, omu mazima batunga kuteerana n'abanywani ba RUKUNDO.

Nikwetengwa kwetegyereza omu muringo gw'OKUJUMUURA ekyo ebirikugyenda nibibaho by'obwenge omu mbeera y'okuteeranira hamwe ebyayitiireho.

Omwegyesi, omwegyesi-mukazi, obu bingi nibabajuna omu muringo oguhikire kwonka obumwe omu butaroho kandi hatariho nshonga z'amazima, hatariho kwetegyereza ngu buli kubajuna okutahikire nikusigara omu bwenge bw'abanyashoomero, obugingo bw'okukora nk'okwo okutahikire, nibugyenda nibuba obuta ORUKUNDO kw'OMEGYESI, kw'OMEGYESI-MUKAZI.

OBWENGE nibusiisika ORUKUNDO kandi ekyo nikintu ekiriho ekyo ABAHESHURI n'ABAHESHURI-ABAKAZI b'amashomero, ebika n'amashomero bagyenzi batarikubaasa kwirira.

Nikwetengwa kwetegyereza omu muringo ogurikukomire ebyo byoona ebirikugyenda nibibaho omu bwenge ebirikumaraho oburungi bwa RUKUNDO.

Tirikumara kuba taata nari maama, nikibaasika KUKUNDA. Abazaire n'obumwe nibateekateeka ngu nibakunda abaana baabo ahabw'okuba nibabeegaita, ahabw'okuba nibo baabo, ahabw'okuba nibabagyenda nk'omuntu orikugyenda ebaicycle, emootoka, enju.

Ekyo kuteekateeka kw'okugyenda ahari omuntu, nikisana n'okushobya ORUKUNDO kwonka tikirikubaasa kuba ORUKUNDO.

Abegyesi n'abegyesi-bakazi ab'eka yaitu eyakabiri eyo niyo eshuuri, nibateekateeka ngu nibakunda abegyesi baabo, ahabw'okuba babaireho nabo, ahabw'okuba baba gyenda, kwonka ekyo tikyo RUKUNDO. Okugyenda nari okwesiba TI RUKUNDO.

OBWENGE nibusiisika ORUKUNDO kandi nikwo kwetegyereza byoona ebirikukora omu muringo ogutahikire gw'obwenge, omu muringo gwaitu ogw'ekisheeki okuteekateeka, emicwe yaitu emibi, emicwe eshusha, emiringo y'ekisheeki y'okureeba ebintu, n'ebindi nitubaasa kuhika aha kureeba, okuhitiiramu ekyo ekitarikubaasa kuhikanaho obwire, ekyo ekirikugambwa ngu NI ORUKUNDO.

Abo abarikwenda ngu ORUKUNDO ruhinduke ekiceerezo ky'omushini yaabo eyaburiijo, abo abarikwenda ngu ORUKUNDO ruteengye omu marembo agatahikire g'ebicweka byabo, amatsiko, obwoba, ebyahitiireho omu magara, emiringo y'ekisheeki ey'okureeba ebintu, emiringo y'ekisheeki ey'okuteekateeka, n'ebindi nibamara ORUKUNDO ahabw'okuba tarurikwerinda kuteengyezibwa.

Abo abarikwenda ngu ORUKUNDO rukore nk'OKU NDAKWERA, nk'OKU NDINDA, nk'OKU NDINDA, nibarinda ORUKUNDO ahabw'okuba CUPIDO, RUHANGA rw'ORUKUNDO, tarurikwerinda kuteengyezibwa na YO.

Nikibaasika kutaaho aha YO, aha MI KWONKA, aha KWONKA kukira kureetera abaana b'ORUKUNDO.

EYE ni omuganda gw'ebijukuzo, amatsiko, obwoba, okwanga, ebyetengo, ebyahitiireho, okwerarikira, obushushu, okwetenga, n'ebindi n'ebindi n'ebindi.

Nikwo kwetegyereza buli kubi okwehakiire; nikwo okukwegyesa, okukwetegyereza omu muringo ogurahingire hatariho omu kicweka ky'obwengye, kwonka era omu mbeera zoona ezirikugyenda ahansi y'obwenge, buli kubi niriba nihwa, nitufa omu kasumi aha kasumi. Nk'oku n'okwo tukahikira aha kuzibaho kwa YO.

Abo abarikwenda okuzaaha ORUKUNDO omunda y'ekicupa ekibi kya yO, nibarinda ORUKUNDO, nibasigara batarikugira, ahabw'okuba ORUKUNDO tarurikubaasa kuzaahwa.

Nibagasaire abantu nibakwenda ngu ORUKUNDO rwetware nikirugiirira aha micwe yaabo bwonyini, ebyeteengo, emicwe, n'ebindi, abantu nibakwenda ngu ORUKUNDO rucweke aha YO kandi ekyo tikirikubaasika ahabw'okuba ORUKUNDO tarurikwerindza YO.

Abashomi b'abeeshereza, nari tubagambe ngu abetengwa obushuba, ekyo nikyo kirikukira okubaho omu Nsi egi, nibakwetegyereza ngu ORUKUNDO rurikuteengyezibwa kuteengyeza omu marembo g'obwenda bwabo bwonyini, okwetenga, enshobi, n'ebindi, kandi omuri eki nibakyerarikira munonga.

Ka tugambe eby'ombiri! nibagamba abakundwa nari abetengwa obushuba, nikyo kirikukira okubaho omu Nsi egi, kandi, bwanyima nija okugamba, omuko, okwetenga n'okusuuta. Buli muntu nagamba ekintu, nazaaho omuko gwe, ebyeteengo, omu muringo ogw'okureeba ebintu by'obwomeezi kandi nagwenda ngu ORUKUNDO ruteengye nk'omushini gw'enyanja y'obwongo omu marembo g'eby'okutwara ebikurwa by'obwenge.

Obu omuringo abarikutunga abo abakundwa nari abetengwa!, ekitari haihi n'amazima.

ORUKUNDO tarurikwerinda YO kandi obu abasomi barikwenda kuteeka amagyeega aha mutwe n'okukucweka, nairuka n'okureetera abashomi omu bushatu.

OBWENGE buine omuringo omubi gw'okuringanisa. Omushaija aringanisa omukazi n'omundi. Omukazi aringanisa omushaija n'omundi. Omwegyesi aringanisa omwegyesi n'omundi, n'omwegyesi-mukazi n'omundi nk'omwegyesi weena orikukunda okushiimwa okuhiirwe. Omu mazima okuringanisa kwona NI KURI KUBI.

Orikureeba okwejuura kw'eizoba kurungi kandi akaringanisa n'okundi, takimanya amazima g'okwetegyereza oburungi oburi omu maisho ge.

Orikureeba orusozi rurungi kandi akaringanisa n'orundi oru arareba omunaku, tarikumanya amazima g'okwetegyereza oburungi bw'orusozi oru aine omu maisho ge.

Aho OKURINGANISA kuriyo ORUKUNDO rw'AMAZIMA terurikubaho. Taata na Maama abarikukunda abaana baabo omu mazima, tibarikubaringanisa na omuntu weena, nibabakunda kandi nikyo kyona.

Omushaija orikukunda omukazi we omu mazima, takikora enshobi y'okumuringanisa na omuntu weena, namukunda kandi nikyo kyona.

OMEGYESI nari Omwegyesi-Mukazi abarikukunda abegyesi baabo, tibarikubagaya, tibarikubaringanisa, nibabakunda omu mazima kandi nikyo kyona.

OBWENGE oburikugabanisibwa ahabw'okuringanisa, obwenge oburi omwojo bw'OBUMWE, nibusiisika ORUKUNDO.

Obwenge oburikugabanisibwa ahabw'okurwanagana kw'okutateerana tiburikubaasa kwetegyereza ekisya, nibugangana, nibwesiiba.

OBWENGE BUINE OBWOOROBI bunyigire, Ebicweka, ebisha, ebikunkumuka, kwonka ekirikukirayo oburungi n'OBWOMEZI, OBWENGE kandi ruri aha KICWEKA.

Obu OBUMWE burikumaraho, obu obwenge burikuba BURAABE, OBUTAIHWA, OBUTARIKUTEREERA, OBWOMEZI, obu butarikuringanisa, nukwo OBWOMEZI burikuba nigwera, OBWENGE kandi nikyo kirikuba nikyo kirikuba kigenda omu maisho ky'OBWEGYESA obw'AMAISINGO.

Tushobye omu maisho n'ebirikukwaata ha muntu. Omu by'OMUMAISHO hariho obwenge oburikugwera. Omu by'OKU KUKWAATA HAMUNTU hariho obwenge oburikugenda aha nsi, OKUTAHIKIRA.

Nikyo OBWENGE BWONYINI burikubaasa kushiisha OBWOMEZI.

Amakuru g'obwengye agarikutunga abegyesi n'abegyesi-bakazi okuhingurukiriza Amashomero goona, Ebika n'Amashomero bagyenzi, n'OKUKWAATA HAMUNTU ekiceerezo ku ky'omukaga.

EBIRIKUKWAATAHO OMU MAISHO tikirikubaasa kutungwa hatariho OBWENGE OMU MAISHO.

Abegyesi n'abegyesi-bakazi bashemereire kubanza kuhika aha KWEYEGYESA KUKIRA KUTAHIKIRA.

Nikwo omu RUBURIRO RW'ORUKUNDO nitubaasa kuhika aha BWENGE OMU MAISHO n'OBWOMEZI OMU MAISHO.

Nikwetengwa kwetegyereza EKIZIBU KY'OBWENGE twaba nitwenda kuteengyeza omu RUBURIRO RW'ORUKUNDO.
