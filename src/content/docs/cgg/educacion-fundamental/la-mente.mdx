Kuraba omu bintu ebitubaireho, tusangire ngu tikirikubaasika kwetegyereza EKI EKYETWA OKUKUNDA, okuhitsya obu turaabe twetegyereize omu muringo OMWEBIJWIRE ekizibu kigumire eky'OBWENGE.

Abo abarikuteekateeka ngu OBWENGE n'OBWONGO, nibakozesa munonga. OBWENGE ni MAANI, bukye, nibubaasa kweyamba kuruga omu BINTU, nibubaasa omu mbeera zimwe ez'okwebaka nari omu birooto bya butoosha, kuza omu myanya ya hare kukureeba n'okuhurira ebirikubaho omumyanya egyo.

Omu marabiratwari g'EBY'OMUTIMA nibakora ebintu by'omutaano aha bantu abari omu MBEERA Y'OKWEBKA.

Abantu baingi abari omu MBEERA Y'OKWEBKA nibabaasise kubarira n'obwegendereza ebintu, abantu, n'embeera ezaaba zirikubaho omu bwire bwabo obw'okwebaka omu bihara bya hare.

Abanyakutegyereza nibabaasise kuhameza bwanyima y'okukora ebintu ebyo, amazima g'EBIHURIRE ebyo. Nibabaasise kuhameza amazima g'ebyaho, obuhikire bw'EBYABAHO.

N'ebintu ebyo ebirikukorwa omu marabiratwari g'EBY'OMUTIMA kikumirwe kimazire kurugiirira aha kwetegyereza n'obuhame ngu OMONGO tigwo BWENGE.

Amazima n'agokubanza twetegyereize ngu obwenge nibubaasa kuza aha bwiro n'omu bihara, buzire aha bwongo, kureeba n'okuhurira ebintu ebirikubaho omu myanya ya hare.

AMAZIMA g'EBIHURIRE BY'OMUTIMA byahamirwe kimazire kandi n'omushema omu bwire obwo nari omugwenga, n'omuntu orikubaasa kugaana amazima g'EBIHURIRE.

Obwongo bukoreirwe okwetenga okuteekateeka kwonka tibwo kuteekateeka. Obwongo nibwo kindiiso ky'OBWENGE, tibwo bwenge.

Turi abetengo kushooma obwenge munonga kureka ngu buzima nitwenda kumanya omu muringo OMWEBIJWIRE eki ekyetwa OKUKUNDA.

Abaana bato n'abakuru, abashaija n'abakazi, baine obwenge burikubaasa kucwecweera, burikwenda, obwangu, oburikumanya, n'ebindi.

Abaana bato na bakuru baingi nibashemererwa kubuuza abazaire baabo n'abegyesa, aha bintu nk'ebyo, nibenda kumanya ebintu bingi nibenda kumanya kandi nikyo ahabw'enki babuuza, bareeba, bareeba ebintu bimwe abakuru abatarikusiima nari abatarikwetegyereza.

Nkoku emyaka erikugyenda, nkoku turikukura omu myaka, obwenge nibugyenda nibuhinduka nk'eky'omutwaro omu bwiito.

Obwenge bw'abakuru bukomeireho, bwigwire nk'eibaare, tiburikukyusa n'obwomeezi.

Abakuru bamaze kuba batyo kandi nibo bafa batyo, tibarikukyusa, boona nibabegaita kuruga ahantu hamwe hakomeireho.

"OBURUNDU" bw'abakuru, okucwamu kwabo, ebiteekateeko bikomeireho, n'ebindi byoona birikusaana nk'EIBARA, EITAABA ry'omutwaro eritakikyusa omu muringo gwona. Nikyo ahabw'enki omugani ogurikukwata guti "OKUTWARA N'EKISHUSHANI OKUHITSYA OKUFA".

Kikuru munonga ngu abegyesa abarikukwata aha kukuza OMUNTU wa beegi, bashoomere obwenge munonga, ngu nibabaasa kworeka abomeezi abasheija omu bwengye.

Nikihumuriza okwetegyereza munonga, nkoku omu bwire burikugyenda, obwenge burikubaasa kuhinduka nk'eibaare omu bwiito.

OBWENGE nibwo muitsi w'AMAZIMA, eky'amazima. OBWENGE nibwononga OKUKUNDA.

Orikukura tamazire kubaasa KUKUNDA ahabw'okuba obwenge bwe bwijuwire ebintu bibaireho ebihumuriza, okucwamu, ebiteekateeko bikomeireho nk'amahanga g'okukwata, n'ebindi.

Hariho abakuru abazigu abaarikuteekateeka ngu nibabaasa KUKUNDA N'OBUTAREMEIRE, kwonka ekirikubaho n'okuba abakuru abazigu bajumire okwenda kw'omubiri okurikukura kandi nibahukaniza OKWENDA n'OKUKUNDA.

"OMUKURU WEENA AZIGU" na "OMUKAZI WEENA AZIGU" barikuhita omu mbeera ezigumire ez'okwenda omubiri enyima y'okufa kandi nibateekateeka ngu ekyo nikyo KUKUNDA.

OKUKUNDA kw'abakuru tikirikubaasika ahabw'okuba obwenge nibukwononga n'ebintu byabo eby'OBURUNDU "EBITEEKATEEKO BIKOMEIREHO", "OKUCWAMU", "OBUJUNA", "EBINTU EBIBAIREHO", "EBIJUKO", okwenda kw'omubiri, n'ebindi n'ebindi n'ebindi.

Hariho buteerwa bwingi bw'obwenge kandi obwenge bw'omuntu weena buteerwe gye.

Abantu nibakunda kuteera OBWENGE, tikurikukira kwanga abo abarikucwamu buzima kugarukamu ebutto.

Abegyesa baine kwegyesa beegi baabo ebintu ebi byoona. Baine kwegyesa abomeezi abasheija kushooma obwenge bwabo, okubwetegyereza, okubwetegyereza, nikyo kuraba omu KWETEGEREZA okurikukomooka turikubaasa kwanga obwenge kukoma, kweteera, kuteerwa.

Abo abarikutunga omu kirooto aha KUKUNDA, abo abarikukora purojekiti aha KUKUNDA, abo abarikwenda ngu OKUKUNDA kukore nk'oku barikukunda n'okutarikukunda, purojekiti n'ebirooto, amateeka n'okucwamu, obwijuko n'ebintu ebibaireho, n'ebindi tibarikubaasa kumanya buzima ekyo KUKUNDA, omu mazima bamaze kuba abazigu b'OKUKUNDA.

Nikyetengo okwetegyereza omu muringo OMWEBIJWIRE ebyo ebirikubaho eby'obwenge omu mbeera y'okukora ebintu.

Omwegyesa, omwegyesa atongye munonga omu muringo murungi kwonka obumwe omu bushema kandi ataine nshonga y'amazima, atarikwetegyereza ngu okutonga kwona okutari kw'amazima kurikusiigwa omu bwenge bw'abeeshoomi, ebyarugiremu nk'okukora ekyo okucwekyereireho, nibibaasa kuba okubura kw'OKUKUNDA kwaitu ahabw'Omwegyesa.

OBWENGE nibwononga OKUKUNDA kandi eki kintu eky'abegyesa b'essomero, amakomera, na yunivasiti batarikubaasa kweyerabira.

Nikyetengo okwetegyereza munonga ebyo ebirikugyenda omu bwenge ebirikuhwera aha burungi bw'OKUKUNDA.

Tikikumire kuba omushaija nari omukazi w'eka, orikubaasa KUKUNDA. Abazaire n'abanyina bagyenda nibateekateeka ngu nibakunda abaana baabo ahabw'okuba baine, ahabw'okuba ni baabo, ahabw'okuba baribeine, nk'omuntu orikugira egaari, emotoka, enju.

OBWENGE nibwononga OKUKUNDA kandi nikyo okwetegyereza ebihakanizibwe byoona eby'obwenge, omuringo gwaitu ogurikushisha ogw'okuteekateeka, emizaarwa mibi yaitw, emize emeesererezi, eby'emashini, muringo murungi gw'okureeba ebintu, n'ebindi turikubaasa kuhika aha kugumirira, kuhura buzima ekyo ekitarikwetenga obwire, ekyo ekyetwa OKUKUNDA.

Abo abarikwenda ngu OKUKUNDA kuhinduke ekicweekye ky'emashini yaabo, abo abarikwenda ngu OKUKUNDA kugyende omu myanya erikushisha y'okucwamu kwabo, okwenda, okutiina, ebintu ebibaireho omu magara, omuringo gw'obushaija gw'okureeba ebintu, muringo ogurikushisha gw'okuteekateeka, n'ebindi nibahwera aha KUKUNDA ahabw'okuba eki tikirikuleka kutooranizibwa.
