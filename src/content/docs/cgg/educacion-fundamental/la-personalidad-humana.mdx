Omushaija akazaalwa, akagumaho emyaka makumi mukaaga na etaano, naafa. Kwonka akaba ari waire obutooro bwa 1900, kandi ni akaha abeere ahali obwo obutooro bwa 1965? Obwengye bw'abantu tibumanyi aha byoona ebyo. Eki nikyo kiikirira ebibuuzo byoona aha bwomeezi n'okufa.

N'obuhame, nitubaasa kwemeza: "OMUSHAIJA AFA NKA'BANTU OBURO BWE BURIHO BIRUGWAHO, TITIIRIHO NYENDA NYE OKUBERAHO KW'OMUFU".

Buli izooba n'ekiziba ky'oburo, buli omwezi n'ekiziba kindi ky'oburo, buli omwaaka ogwo n'ekiziba kindi ky'oburo, kandi ebyo biziba byoona ebirikugaitwa hamwe nibikora EKIZIBA EKIKURU EKITORO KYE BWOMEEZI.

Oburo bwoona n'empanga kandi obwomeezi bw'OKUBERAHO KW'OMUSHAIJA n'akakura akugwa aha meeza.

Obwomeezi bw'OKUBERAHO KW'OMUSHAIJA nibukura omu buro bwe, nibuzaalwa omu buro bwe, kandi nibufa omu buro bwe, tiburibaasa kubaho okukira oburo bwe.

Eky'oburo eki k'ekizibu eki kyegamiirwe abanyabwengye baingi. Okukira aha kubuuza oburo n'EIKUMI NA IRIIYO ERAMBI.

Okushoboorora kwa EUCLIDES nikukora burungi omu nsi ya ERARAMBI, konka ensi erimu eikumi na mushanju, kandi eya IKUMI NA IRIIYO n'OBUTOORO.

Ebirooto by'omutwe gw'omuntu nibitererezanya OBUTAAHERWA nka'bureebureeza bw'oburo omu murongo ogusingwireho, titiiriho kintu eki kibaasa kwongyerwa omutwe gw'okukanganisa ahabw'okuba OBUTAAHERWA n'EIKUMI NA INA ERAAMBI.

Buli oburo bw'okubaho nibugwa omu butooro kandi nibuzooka ebiro byoona.

Okufa n'OBWOMEEZI n'empanga ezaamaize okugwaho. Obwomeezi niburigwa omushaija orikufa konka nibutandika okundi. Oburo niburigwa konka obundi nibutandika, okufa nikurugire aha KUZOOKA KWEBIRO BYOONA.

Eki nikigira ngu twine kugaruka, kugaruka omu nsi enu kugarukamu emikono egi y'okubaho, kyonka shi, OKUBERAHO kw'omuntu nikufa n'okufa, ni wuuha shi oba niki ekigaruka?

Kwesigwa kwetegyereza okuza kw'obwomeezi ebiro byoona, ngu ni MEINE ogumaho okurra okufa, ngu ni MEINE ogaruka, ngu ni MEINE ogaruka omu kiikaro kya maziga.

Kwesigwa ngu abasomi baitu tibakwanisa ETEERA eya KUGARUKA n'Enjigirizo ya KUZOOKA eyegambirwa obwengye obuhya.

Enjigirizo eyo y'okugaruka yakurire omu kugumaho kwa KRISHNA ori n'EDINI ya INDIA ey'emikooro ya VEDIC, ekuhindurwa kandi eronoonwa abahinduzi.

Omu kugumaho kw'amazima okuza kw'orindi kwa Krishna, Abakuru, Abahabuzi, abarikutunga OKUBERAHO OKURIKWEERA, nibo bonka abarizooka.

MEINE OMUNGI GARUKA, kugaruka konka eki tikukugwaho. Ebiziba, ebiingi BAZOOKA, konka eki tikukugwaho.

Endeebeka y'OKUGARUKA kw'ebintu n'ebindi, endeebeka y'okugarukamu etaara ti nkuru kandi nitubaasa okugisanira omu bwengye bwa PITAGORICA n'omu kugaruka kw'omutwe gwa India.

Okugaruka kw'ebiro n'ebiro bya BRAHAMA, okugarukamu okutaaherwa kwa KALPAS, n'ebindi, nibakwatirwa aha bwengye bwa Pitagórica n'Eteera y'OKUGARUKAMU okutaaruka nari OKUGARUKA kw'ebiro byoona.

Gautama BUDHA yegambire omurungi ENJIGIRIZA y'OKUGARUKA KW'EBIRO BYOONA n'empanga z'obwomeezi obuzookireho, konka ENJIGIRIZA ye yahindurwe abamukurira.

OKUGARUKA kw'ebiro byoona nikurugwa omu kukora OKUBERAHO okushya kw'omuntu, eki nikikurira emyaka mushanju ey'okubanza y'obutooto.

Oburo bwoona bw'eka, obwomeezi bw'omu barabara, n'eshomero, nibibiha OKUBERAHO kw'OMUNTU, omuhuro gw'ahabanza ogurikwetwa.

EKY'OKUTWANA ky'abakuru nikikwongyera ebyoona okukura kw'okuberaaho kw'omwaana.

Omwaana nayega bingi n'eky'okutwana okukira n'okugambirwa. Omuringo ogubii ogw'okugumaho, ky'okutwana ekitarikwesigwa, emitegyere egurugireho y'abakuru, nibiha okuberaaho kw'omwaana omuhuro ogurikwetwa omutetoona kandi ogurikwongekera oburo obutugumiireho.

Omu biro binu ebihya okuteeba kwakuzire okukira obutakari n'ebitunguru kandi nka'bintu ebikukora ebiro byoona eki nikikora ebintu by'oburaro omu maaka.

Baingi abaaana abatwine kwikika omu biro binu okujugira ekihande kw'enshiko n'ebihande bya taata wundi nari maama wundi. Kirikworeka ngu omu muringo ogwo OKUBERAHO kw'omwaana nibikura omu muti gw'okujugira, okukara, n'okwanga.

Tihiriho okugamba kw'abantu abakuru abagira bati: "Omwaana w'omundi aharurra omu biikaro byoona". Nitureeta omu mazima eki kiriho ebihindura konka ebi nibiha kubaasa okubara n'engaro zoona kandi zingaro zikura.

Okuhakanisa hagati ya taata na maama ahabw'omutetoona, okugambirra n'okujugira kwa maama orikutetebwa nari iba orikukumbatirwa, ahindurwa kandi aheerwa, nibareetera OKUBERAHO kw'omwaana akakurakazi k'okujugira kandi akatoojo eki butarigaruka kwerabirwa omu bwomeezi bwoona.

Omu maaka agarikukura abakyala abakuru barukumbatira abakazi baabo obu barikugyenda omu kihunguriro ky'oburungi nari bakasiiga amaisho. Okukura kw'abakyala kurugireyo nikwejwekyera.

Omwaana orikutunga ebyo byoona, ebintu eby'obushushani araba omutima gwe obii shi atare, omutima gwe nikuguruma ngu aza kwegendera omu maama we orikukura kandi orikwehumbira, nari orikwenda omukazi ori omubi kandi orikumutendera omwanya, ebyarugireyo nibiba babi aha KUKURA KW'OBWOMEEZI BW'ABATOOTO.

Okurugirra obu televizoni yaikirweho eka yaburaho. Omu biro ebindi omushaija naaza kuruga omu barabara kandi naakiirirwa omukazi we n'okushemererwa okwingi. Ebiro binu omukazi tariyo kugyenda kutaara iba we omunyu aha muryango ahabw'okuba aijwire okulola televizoni.

Omu maaka magyenzi taata, maama, abaana, abakazi, barikugambwa nk'abantu abatakwetegyereza omu maisho ga televizoni.

Hati iba tariyo kugamba nomukazi n'akatara kyona n'ebizibu by'omwaana, omurimo n'ebindi ahabw'okuba asaana nomurooti orikwenda firimu y'eizooba eriherereho, enshusha y'omugisha ya Al Capone, okucenka kw'ahaherereho kw'embahizi embi, n'ebindi.

Abaana abakurire omu muringo ogwo oguhya gw'eka obumara nibateekereza aha mataba, enganda, emisumaali y'ekikaato okutwaana n'okugumaho omu muringo gwabo ensusha zoona z'oburaro bw'obushushani nka'baziire aha televizoni.

N'ekihanda eki kyokukora ekirikwetwa televizoni nikikoresibwa aha migisha egy'obushushani. Shi abantu bakakoresa eki ky'okukora omu muringo ogurikugira ekitiinisa okwesomesa okushoboorora obwomeezi, okwegambira obwomeezi obw'amazima obw'omukama NATURE, okwegambira enjigiriza engonzire abantu, obwo eki ky'okukora kibaasa okuba omugisha aha bantu, bibaasa kukoresibwa omu bwengye okukura okuberaaho kw'omuntu.

Kiri aha maisho kuteeka OKUBERAHO KW'ABATOOTO n'emiziki etarikugwa, etarikukwatana, ey'obuzigu. N'ekisiinziro okuheereza OKUBERAHO kw'abaana, n'ebijanja by'abashuma n'abapolisi, ebyo by'okuta omutima aha biriisi n'obukoreezi, emizizo y'oburaro, pornografia, n'ebindi.

Ekyarugireyo by'omuringo ogwo nitubaasa okukireeba omu Barebelire abatarimara kwenda okutuga, okutsiga okw'abato.

N'ekitima ngu abamaama babaire abaana babo, babaihe ebiri, babagambire n'ebigambo ebyonooneireho kandi eby'ekikaro. Ekyarugireyo ky'obutoogo obwo n'okujuga, okwanga, okubura kw'okukunda n'ebindi.

Omu kukora twabaasa kureeba ngu abaana abakurire hagati y'ebiri, okujugira, n'ebigambo, bahinduka abantu ababuzigu abaijwire obutamanya n'obukutwe, omu kwanga kutoora, omu mazima.

Kwesigwa kwetegyereza enyenda y'okuteeka akataro k'amazima omu maaka.

Nikusangwa okumanya ngu obwomeezi obusemeire n'obutooma bitwine okusangwa n'engaro zaingaro ezimbiri omu mwanza gw'ebiboneebonezo.

TATA nikiikirira OKUKORA, MAAMA nikiikirira OKUSEMEERA. Taata nakiikirira OBWENGYE. Maama nikugamba Okukunda.

OBWENGYE n'OKUKUNDA, OKUKORA n'OKUSEMEERA nibikusangwa omu ngaro zaingaro ezimbiri omu mwanza gwa cosmic.

Abamaama na Data omu kaito okusangwa hamwe ebirungi by'omwomeezi.

Kwesigwa, n'enyenda, ngu abaamaama na Data boona omu maaka bakwetegyereza enyenda y'okusiga omu bitekerezo by'abaana OKUGAMBA KWA MAZIMA okutaaruka kw'omwoyo.

N'ekitima ngu abaana abagenzi tabagumaho omutima gw'OKUTAARA, eki kyabire omu bajanja b'emotoka, abashuma n'abapolisi, televizoni, sinema, n'ebindi. baryoone OKUBERAHO kw'abato.

PSICOLOGIA REVOLUCIONARIA y'ENDEEBEKA GNÓSTICO, omu muringo ogurikworeka nibakora engyenderwaho hagati y'EGO n'EMBAARA.

Omu myaka eshatu ey'okubanza nari ena y'obwomeezi, omwaana n'ayoleka omurungi gw'EMBAARA, obwo omwaana aba omusemeire, omurungi, omurungi omu migisha ye yoona Psicologicos.

Obu EGO etandika okugamba okuberaaho kw'omwaana okurungi kw'EMBAARA yoona kuhinduka n'omwanya gwayo nibayoleka ebintu ebibi Psicologicos bya muntu weena.

Nka'batuurimu kukora engyenderwaho hagati ya EGO na EMBAARA, n'enyenda okugwaho hagati ya OBUMAIZI n'EMBAARA.

Omuntu akazaalirwa EMBAARA konka akazaalirwa OBUMAIZI, n'enyererezo yokukora.

OBUMAIZI n'EMBAARA bitwine kukura omu muringo oguri hamwe kandi ogusikwa.

Omu kukora twabaasa kwemeza ngu obu OBUMAIZI bukura omu muringo ogurikwongyera amani aha EMBAARA, ebyarugireyo n'EKIYENDEERERO.

Okutwara omu maisho n'okubanza kw'emyaka mingi bikatwongyera okwetegyereza ngu obu EMBAARA ekura ebiro byoona okutata omutima aha kukura n'oburungi obw'OBUMAIZI, ekyarugireyo n'omuntu orikwetonda atarikutegyereza, atarikugira OBUMAIZI, omurungi omu mutima konka atarikwenda, atarikukora.

OKUKURA Okurikugwa kw'OBUMAIZI n'EMBAARA nibiha abashaija n'abakazi.

Omu EMBAARA twine ebyaitu, omu OBUMAIZI twine ebyatuheebwa.

Omu EMBAARA twine ebintu byaitu ebituterekire omu mutima, omu OBUMAIZI twine eky'okutwaana ky'abakuru baitu, ebyetusomesire omu ka, omu Shomero, omu Barabara.

N'ekyakakagurukiirwe okuha abaana ebyokulya by'EMBAARA n'ebyokulya by'OBUMAIZI.

EMBAARA nekwesibwa n'okusemeera, okufugama ebiro byoona, okukunda, emiziki, ebimuri, eby'oburungi, okusikwa, n'ebindi.

OBUMAIZI butwine kwesiibwa n'eky'okutwaana ekirungi ky'abakuru baitu, n'enjigiriza ya shomero y'obwengye, n'ebindi.

Nikusangwa ngu abaana barikuhinga ebitabo by'abato omyaka mushanju babanza okugyenda omumaisho omu kiikaro ky'abaana.

Abaana batwine kwega obugambo bw'okubanza barikucenceza, nikikora okwesomesa eki, okukuruza, okusemeera, okugumaho.

ENJIGIRIZA ENDAZAALI nibegambira ngu Kuruga omu muringo gumwe gwa KINDER nari eka y'abaana, nikwetwa aha muringo ogurikukuruza ekika ky'omwomeezi OMUSHAIJA, kirikumanywa nka kitekerezo, omugisha, n'ekikorwa, nikikora okuberaaho kw'omwaana omu muringo ogurikusiangwa kandi ogurikurikugumaho.

Eky'obukuru bw'okukora OBUMAIZI bw'omwaana n'okukura kwabo, kiri omu bujunanizibwa obukuru aha BAMAAMA NA DATA n'ABEGAMBIRA B'OMU SHOMERO.

Oburungi bw'OBUMAIZI bw'OMUNTU nigurugira omu muringo gw'ebintu Psicologico ebirakozire kandi biriheereze.

Okukwetera OBUMAIZI, EMBAARA, EGO nari MEINE, kiri hagati y'abegesi b'obwengye.

Abandi nibakwanisa OBUMAIZI n'EMBAARA kandi abandi nibakwanisa EGO nari MEINE n'EMBAARA.

Ningi eshomero lya Seudo-Esotéricas nari Seudo-Ocultistas aharibo balimu ekikorwa ky'ENDEEBEKA ey'obwomeezi obutaramara.

Kwesigwa okugwaho ngu teri OBUMAIZI eki twine okusiima.

N'endengo okumanya ngu twine okuteeka EGO, MINE, MEINE okurikusiima aha muntu.

OBUMAIZI n'okusingaho okugiraho okukora, omugisha ogwabire gwenyenda kukora.

Omu nsi hariho CALIGULAS, ATILAS, HITLERES, n'ebindi. Buli busingwireho bwoona obutajwekyereho bubaasa kuhindurwa obu EGO nar MEINE zikuzire ebiro byoona.

Ekyo ky'okusiima EGO nari MEINE nikihindura n'okuronoono Seudo-Esoteristas mingi. Ebyo nibakanganisa ngu EGO eri OKURA, nibakkiriza ngu EGO nari MEINE n'OMUNTU gumwe, OKUKUNDA OKURA, n'ebindi.

Nikusangwa, n'endengo, kutabaireka okwetegyereza ngu EGO nari MEINE netara kandi eri OKURA.

EGO nari MEINE n'OMUKAMA w'EBIGAMBO, okusangwa kw'ebijukuzo, ebyendengo, ebintu, ebyanga, okujugira, ebigwagwa, obugonjwa, obwomi, okukoresa, okuronoono, okuruga omu ka, ekika, orurembo n'ebindi.

Baingi abarikugamba omu muringo gw'ekisiinziro ngu omu buturimu hariho MEINE ERIRUKURU nari OKURA kandi MEINE ERITO.

ERIRIKURU n'ERITO zikura n'ebitundu bibiri eby'ekintu gumwe. MEINE ERIRUKURU, MEINE ERITO, n'ebitundu bibiri eby'EGO gumwe.

OMUNTU OKURA, okukunda, OKUKURA, tiharaaho ekikutwara omu muringo gwa MEINE gyona. OMUNTU n'OMUNTU kandi ekyo kyona. Omuhuro gw'okutwaana n'OMUNTU gumwe.

OBUMAIZI omu butunzi n'omugisha kandi teri kintu, omwaami w'okuberaaho bwaasa kworekwa EGO nari OMUNTU, ebintu byoona nitwetwarira.

N'endengo okulonda MEINE, EGO, ngu bakole omu muringo ogurikworeka okurabira omu OBUMAIZI bwaitu, EMBAARA PSICOLÓGICA y'OMUNTU WAITU WA MAZIMA.

Nikusangwa ngu ABESOMESA bakyetegyereza omu bujuni okukura omu muringo ogurugiremu emigisha eshatu y'OBUMAIZI BW'ABANTU.

Okusikwa okurikukwongyera hagati y'OBUMAIZI n'EMBAARA, okukura okurikugwa KW'OKUTEEREZA, OKUTWANA, n'OKUGYENDA OMU MAISHO, ETHICA REVOLUCIONARIA, nibakora EKIZINGIRANO ky'ENJIGIRIZA ENDAZAALI.
