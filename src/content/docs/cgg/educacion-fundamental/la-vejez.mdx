Emyaka makumi ana egy'obulamu bwaffe egyasooka gituwa ekitabo, amakumi asatu agaddirira ne gatuwa obubaka obukirimu.

Omuisaija ow'emyaka amakumi abiri aba nk'ei swanga; ow'amakumi asatu, aba nk'entale; ow'amakumi ana, aba nk'engamiya; ow'amakumi ataano, aba nk'omusota; ow'amakumi mukaaga, aba nk'embwa; ow'amakumi musanvu, aba nk'enkima, na ow'amakumi munaana, aba iraka n'ekifaananyi byonka.

Obwire bujumbura ebintu byona: ni kabonereza murungi ogwoleka omu mazima n'obu oba otamubuuza kintu kyona.

Tihariho kintu kikirweho omukono gw'enyama ey'obwenge omubi, ei etumwa omuntu, ekitakyekubisa ha bwire oburikwija.

"FUGIT IRRÉPARABILE TEMPUS", obwire oburikugenda tiburikubaasa kuzoorwa.

Obwire bujumurira omu lwatu ekirikubiikwa hati kandi bukubiika kandi busiga ekirikwaka n'amaani hati.

Obukuru nk'okugonza, tiburikubaasa kusiigwa n'obu twayambara eby'abato.

Obukuru buteesa eitima ry'abaisaija kandi nibubahindura abato, baitu ekintu kimwe kwetoora kandi ekindi kugawa oteeretwe.

Obwire bw'okufa bwiraire haihi, abakuru abatairekukiriza obulamu basanga ngu obukuru tikirikuba kiremera.

Abaisaija boona baini amatsiko g'okutura obwire buraiire kandi okukura baitu obukuru nibubatiina.

Obukuru butandiika ha myaka amakumi ataano na mukaaga kandi nibuza buzooka omu biire by'emyaka musanvu ebitutwara omu bujune na rufu.

Ekizibu ekikuru eky'abakuru kibeera, titikiri omu mazima g'okukura, baitu omu busiru bw'okutakiriza ngu bakuru na omu busito bw'okwetwara nk'abato nk'obukuru ni musango.

Ekintu ekirungi ekirikukirayo aha bukuru, ni ngu oraiire haihi n'ekigyendereerwa.

OBUBONE BW'OMUTIMA, NZE MWENE, EGO, tiburikwenda n'emyaka n'obumanyirizi; nibwongera kukataaza, nibuhinduka obuzibu, oburikwetagisa omurimo, nikyo okugamba okurikukwatwa: "ENDWAANO N'EKIFANANYI KUTANDIIRA OKUHIKA HA KIITIIBA".

OBUBONE BW'OMUTIMA obw'abakuru abazibu bweyemereza kubaha amagezi marungi ahabw'obuhikiriza bwabo obw'okutaha eby'okureeberaho bibi.

Abakuru nibamanyira kimwe ngu obukuru ni omuteesi omubi ow'atiina munonga abaziriza, ha mutwe gw'okufa, okusemererwa ebintu by'abato abasito kandi nibenda kweyemerereza kubaha amagezi marungi.

NZE nsiga NZE, NZE nsiga ekitundu kyange kandi byona nibiyoolwa n'ebigambo ebirungi n'amagezi marungi.

Ekitundu KIMWE kyange kiniba ekitundu ekindi kyange. NZE nsiga ekirikwanga kwikiriza.

Kikyolekerwe kimwe kuraba omu kureeba n'obumanyirizi ngu obwomeezi obubi buturekeire twenda kuteekereza ngu itwe nitwo twaburekereire.

Omutima gw'ENYAMA EY'OBWENGE tigurikwenda n'emyaka, baitu niburabya, buratwara enjazi kandi tubaire abagangaazima, ababaisi, abarikusungura omu buto, omu bukuru nituza kuba bingi munonga.

Abakuru batuura omu biire ebiraiire, abakuru nibo bigwiireho ebiraiire bingi, abakuru tibamanyira kimwe obwire obuturimu, abakuru ni mijweko eyongiirwe.

Omuhanda gwonka ogw'okuhika aha bukuru obuhikiriireho ni okucwekyereza OBUBONE BW'OMUTIMA. Obutwegera okufa omumutwaro ha mutwaro, nituhika aha bukuru oburungi.

Obukuru buini amana munonga, obuteekane n'obugabe ahabw'abamara kucwekyereza NZE.

Obuganzi bwafireho omu muhanda ogurikwenda, ogwonka na obutaahiireho, otuura otari mugurusi, baitu bagurusi bingi.

Kirazibu munonga kusanga omu bulamu abakuru abataahiireho abatarikukiriina n'obukuru bw'OBUNTU, ekika ky'abakuru ekyo kisezereire okw'enkanya kandi nibatuura omukatwaro ha katwaro.

Omuisaija ayijwiire omu MAGARA. Omukuru omu kumanya, omuisa ow'okugonza, ahinduka omu mwanya gw'omuruuru ogwoleka omu muhanda ogurikukwenda eby'omulembe ogutabarika.

Omu Muzaire w'omuka abeeraho akabonero k'amagezi. Omu Maaire w'eka abeera OKUGONZA, abaisaija nibakabonera ekigambo.

Omuzaire omukuru ahemererwa okwebembererwa abaisaija. Omuzaire omukuru takiri kubaasa kukora kandi kibaire kirungi ngu abaisaija bamulinde kandi bamutiinise.

Omaiire orikugonzesa amaze kukura kandi nikikwatwa okureeba abaisaija kandi abaija nibamureeba kandi nibamugonza kandi nibahindura okugonza okwo edini.
