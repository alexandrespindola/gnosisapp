Emyaka makumi ana egy'okubanza egy'obulamu gituwa ekitabo, amakumi asatu agaddirira gatuleetera obuvunzikiirizi.

Omuishengu omuhika omuhanda ayombeka nk'entaama; owamakumi ashatu nk'entale; owamakumi ana nk'entaama emunyamagaro; owamakumi ataano nk'enzoka; owamakumi mukaaga nk'embwa; owamakumi mushanju nk'enkima, kandi owamakumi munaana, iraka n'ekisio ky'ekisio.

Obwire buzibura ebintu byoona: ni muhandiisi orikufunzika kandi orikweshonga orikwegamba obwe, n'obu oraabe otamubuuza kintu kyona.

Tihariho kintu kyona ekikozirwe engaro y'ekitondekwa ekihabire ekiteekateeka, ekyetwa omuntu omuhambo, eky'obwire obukire butaahumuza.

"FUGIT IRRÉPARABILE TEMPUS," obwire obuhunga tiburi kubasirikwa.

Obwire buleta omu bwerere byoona ebi hano bihishirwe kandi bugira buzibire kandi buhise byoona ebi omunaku ogu birikutaahirira n'amaani.

Obukaire ni nka rukundo, tiburi kubisirirwa n'obu buraabe bwesiigisiriza n'eby'okwambara by'obuto.

Obukaire buhabya eitumbiirizo ry'abantu kandi bubahumiliza, baitu ekintu ekimu n'okuba omucureezi n'ekindi kugwa wahumilizibwe.

Obu okufa kurikusemberera, abakaire abahwamu amaani baitu bakwata obukaire butakiri muhimbo.

Abantu boona bagira amatsiko g'okutuuraho obwire bwingi n'okutuuka omu bukaire, na hati obukaire bubatiisa.

Obukaire butandika ha myaka makumi ataano na mukaaga kandi nibuba burikumanyika omubwire bw'emyaka musanju okutuhuura okutuusa omu bushoberwa n'okufa.

Eky'obusaasi ekirikusaaniza abakaire kikuru nikisangwa, hatariho omu muringo ogwo gutyo ogw'okuba abakaire, baitu omu busha gw'okukabaata kwikiriza ngu nibo, kandi omu bushoberwa bw'okwetondera abato nk'oku obukaire buraabe bukozirwe.

Ekilungi ekirikusaana obukaire, n'okushanga omuntu ari haihi n'ekigyenda.

IYE ETEEKATEEKA, MINE YOONA, EGO, tibirikukora kurungi n'emyaka n'obuhangwa; niribura, nirireetera obuzibu, nirireetera okukora, ahabw'ekyo nigibangwa ekigambo ekirikuhenda: "GENIO NA FIGURA OKUHIISA OMUSHOBOZI".

IYE ETEEKATEEKA y'abakaire abarikureetera obuzibu neetondereza omwoyo n'okubaha amagezi marungi ahabw'obuteezi bwabo kubaayo eby'okureeberaho ebibi.

Abakaire nibamanya gye ngu obukaire ni ntindi erikatiisa egi bari kutaikiriza, omu mahano g'okufa, kweshemererwa eby'okushemeza eby'obuto bwa mbanga kandi nibaguma nibesiimisa okubaha amagezi marungi.

IYE neehisa IYE, IYE neehisa eicweeka lyayo n'ebintu byoona nibibandikwa n'ebigambo ebirikumanyisa n'amagezi marungi.

EICWEEKA LIMU lya MINE YOONA neehisa icweeka erindi lya MINE YOONA. IYE neehisa ekitasaana.

Kirimanyirwe kirungi kuraba omu kweyoreka n'obuhangwa ngu obu obutarozi butureka twesiima okuteekereza ngu itwe twabwerekire.

Omutima gw'ENYAMAGARO YETEEKEZA tigukora kurungi n'emyaka, baitu gwabura, buri obwiire neguguma n'eibaare kandi obu omu buto twabaire turikuteekereza, ababeshi, abanyabururu, omu bukaire nituba bwingi.

Abakaire nibatuura omu bwanyima, abakaire nibo ebikirukureetwa ebireere ebiringi, abakuru tibarikumanya obwiire obu turikutuura, abakaire ni kwijuka okukundize.

Omuringo gumu gw'okutuuka omu bukuru obuhikire n'okushoborra IYE EETEEKEZA. Obu turikwegera okufa obwiire obwiire, nitutuuka omu bukuru oburikujuna.

Obukaire buine amanyiso makuru, omu ruhuuko n'obugabe ahaabo abaraashobora IYE.

Obu emihingo ehwire omu muringo ogwa nyamunonga, omwingi kandi oguhikireho, hasigaraho omuntu atari mukama, baitu ahabakama baingi.

Kiri kigumire kushanga omu buhambi abakuru abatariho musango abatarikuhamagara n'obu oburaabe obusiga bw'IYE, ekicweka eki ky'abakuru nikishemerera kihango kandi nibatuura obwiire obwiire.

Omushaija orugyezi omu BUSAHO. Omukuru omu kumanya, omukama w'okukunda, naguma agira omuti gw'omusana ogurikuguma n'obwengye oburiisa omugyenda gw'emyaka mwingi etaarikubara.

Omu nsi baho kandi nibabaho hati ABARAAMI ABAKURU abataine n'obu oburaabe obusiga obuhendereirwe obwa IYE. ABA ARHAT GNÓSTICOS nibakora nk'omutaahirizi kandi n'omulimbiti nk'eihwa lya loto.

OMUKURU ARAAMI OWAHABWIRE IYE ERIKUKUNDA OMU MURINGO GWA NYAMUNONGA kandi oguhikireho n'okweyoreka okuhikire kw'OBWENGYE OBUHIKIRE, obw'OKUKUNDA OBWA MULUNGI N'AMAANI AGARIKUJUNA.

OMUKURU ARAAMI atakyine IYE, n'omuringo oguhikire ogw'OKWEYOREKA KW'OKUBEHO KWA MULUNGI.

ABAKURU ABO ABARIKUJUNA, ABO ARHAT GNÓSTICOS babonekire ensi kuruga omu bwire obw'enyima, turekwe kwijuka BUDHA, MOISÉS, HERMES, RAMARKRISHNA, DANIEL, SANTO LAMA, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Abegyesa b'eshomero, amashomero agirikukura n'amauvanivarsiiti, abegyeseza, abazaire b'omu ka, baine kwegesa obutanduuti obuhyaka kusiima n'okusiima abakuru.

EKITARIKUINE IZAINA, EKYO ekiri MULUNGI, EKYO ekiri EKYENENE, kine ebikuru bishatu: OBWENGYE, OKUKUNDA, EIGAMBO.

EKI MULUNGI nk'OKUTAIZA n'OBWENGYE BW'OKUMUNYARARA, NK'OKUMANYA n'OKUKUNDA OKUTAASHWA, nk'omwana n'EIGAMBO.

Omu Kutaiiza w'eka hasangwa akabonero k'obwengye. Omu Nyina w'eka hasangwa OKUKUNDA, abaana nibabonereza ebigambo.

Omukuru Kutaiiza asaaniza okushagikwa abaana. Kutaiiza asya akakwata tikarikubaasa kukora kandi kiri kirungi ngu abaana bamuhame kandi bamusiime.

Nyina Ow'okusiima asya takarikubaasa kukora kandi ahabw'ekyo kiri kyamanyirwe ngu abaana b'abaishiki bareeba ahabwe kandi bamukunde kandi bakore okukunda okwo eddiini.

Orikukabaata kukunda Kutaiiza, orikukabaata KUGUMYA Nyina, nagyenda omuhanda gw'omukono gw'omumazere, omuhanda gw'okushaama.

Abaana tibaine sente z'okucwera Kutaiiza ebirango, tihariho orihikire omu nsi egi kandi abo abatarikwikiriza ebishoro omu burikukorra ekigyenda, tubine omu kindi, twakatirwe twona n'ebisiima ebimu.

Abandi nibagendabanya OKUKUNDA KWA KUTAIZA, abandi kushemererwa kuruga OKUKUNDA KWA KUTAIZA. Abo abarikwesiima batyo omu buhambi baine kwija kuruga ahabwiire obwo oburikuhata omuhanda ogurikuhata ahabwiire obwo obutarikuine izaina.

Omwana atarikusiima orikureetera obukazi Kutaiiza kandi orikwebwa Nyina n'amazima omuntu atarikukira okureeta obukazi orikweterera byoona ebi biri MULUNGI.

OKWEHINDURA KW'OBWENGYE tikirikukunyisa OKUTASIIMA, okwebwa ishe, okugendabanya Nyina orikukunda. OKWEHINDURA KW'OBWENGYE n'OBWENGYE BW'OKUKUNDA n'AMAANI AMAHIKIRE.

Omu Kutaiiza hasangwa akabonero k'obwengye kandi omu Nyina hasangwa ensuro eriyo erikukunda hatariho eyo esensire erikuguma nitukora eby'OBWENGE ebihango okukira eby'OKURUGAMU.
