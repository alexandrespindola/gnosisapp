Obwire bukahiire obu kworeka oburyarya n'emicwe emibi ey'okwerarikirira aha by'embesa.

Nikiba kyetaagisa kwetegyereza gye ekizibu ky'eby'embesa aha batiinagaroona abato b'omubiri gwabo.

Aha myaka ikumi na ina, amaani g'eby'embesa gabonekire omu mubiri gw'omutiinagaroona, gagaruka gataire embabazi omu nkora y'obwongo.

Obwomeezi obu bw'amaani buguura omubiri gw'omuntu, bukaguura eiraka ry'omushaija, kandi nibwo buhwereera omukazi kuzaara.

Omubiri gw'omuntu n'ekika ky'efakitore eguura ebintu bikuru bikaba ebintu birungi eby'obwomeezi.

Ebyokurya ebi turya nibigurwa omulundi gwaitaano, birengwa bureezi, kuhitsya ku kirungi kya byona, ekintu ky'obutaba kimwe na nkama, ekintu ekitaba kimwe na manzi, eki Paracelso yaabaire arikweta Ens-Seminis (Ekintu ky'Eiziho).

Endeebo egi y'amanzi, ehinduka, eyorobi, eiziho eryo, ririimu omunda embuto z'obwomeezi zona.

Ekitebyo kikiriza omu iziho engaro y'aharikuruga obwomeezi n'amaani.

Abakuru b'aba Alchemist eyoona nk'aba Paracelso, Sendivogius, Nicol√°s Flamel, Raymundo Lulio, n'abandi, bakashoma n'okukunda omu bwiino Ens-Seminis rundi ebishuba by'embesa.

Eki VITRIOLO, n'endabo mazima ehangirwe n'obwengye, eitweire omu byomubiri.

Omu bwiino egi y'obwengye bw'omutaano, omu iziho, nibwo orikusanga ebintu byona ebirikubaasa kubaho.

Nikiraariirwa ebiroora abato baingi ahabw'okubura obuhabuzi obuhikire bahayo omubiri aha kiihaara rundi bagwe omu muhanda mubi gw'obushaija-bukazi.

Abaana n'abato nibahaburwa aha bintu bingi, bakagirwa kugyenda omu muhanda gw'okwesherura, ekirikuhwereera amagara mabi, kwonka eky'obusaasi, amaani g'eby'embesa garugireho obutiinagaroona, abazaire n'abegyesi, ahabw'oburyarya n'emicwe mibi, bamara barahira kuguma barikucekura.

Haroho okucekura okuri kwita, haroho ebigambo ebiri ebigambo ebibi. Kucekura aha kizibu ky'eby'embesa n'omushango. Kugamba kubi aha kizibu ky'eby'embesa n'omushango ogundi.

Abazaire n'abegyesi ku barikucekura, abantu ababi nibagamba, abatiinagaroona abato nibeegwa.

Omutiinagaroona ku araabe atarikubaasa kubuuza abazaire be, rundi abegyesi be, abuuza banywani be ahabw'omushomo ogwo. Ebirugaamu tibirikutindaho bwire bwingi, omutiinagaroona omusya, arikukurata obuhabuzi bubi, agwera aha kiihaara rundi agwe omu muhanda gw'obushaija-bukazi.

Ekiihaara kihwereera amaani g'obwongo goona. Nikiba kyetaagisa kumanya ngu hariho omukago gw'amaani hagati y'eiziho n'obwongo. Nikiba kyetaagisa kuguura eiziho omu bwongo. Nikiba kyetaagisa kuta obwongo omu iziho.

Obwongo nibuta omu iziho kurabira omu kuguura amaani g'eby'embesa, kugakugura, kugafeerera omu maani g'obwongo.

Omu muringo ogu, eiziho ryatairwe omu bwongo, obwongo nibuta omu iziho.

Ekitebyo kishoma gye endokrinoloji, nikegyesa emiringo n'enkora z'okuguura amaani g'eby'embesa, kwonka egi n'ensonga etarikusangwa omu kitabo eki.

Omushomi ku araabe ayenda amakuru aha kitebyo, ashemereire kushoma ebitabo byaitu eby'ekitebyo, yaingira omu mashomo gaitu.

Abatiinagaroona bashemereire kukugura amaani g'eby'embesa nibakura ebihurira, nibega omuziki, okubumba, okusiiga amarangi, nibagyenda aha nshozi ezirikureenga, n'ebindi.

Enkumi z'enyinjo ezirikubaasa kuba nungi nizitwaara!

Enkumi z'obwongo nibweruura! Byona ahabw'okubura okugamba okw'okurabura aha bwire obuhikire.

Ekiihaara, omu bato n'abashiki, kyayambukire kukira okwombeka engaro.

Amaka g'abarwaire gajwiire abashaija n'abakazi abaahwereire obwongo bwabo omu kiihaara. Endazo y'abakora ekiihaara n'omaka g'abarwaire.

Omucwe gw'obushaija-bukazi gwahwereere obuzizi bw'ekika eki ekizaire n'ekirikumera.

Kirishemeza ngu omu nsi nk'ey'Obwingereza ebarebera amagara n'ebya buryo, hariyo amasinema agoreka filimu z'obushaija-bukazi.

Kirishemeza ngu omuri Obwingereza nibwo bashweruranira bushwere bw'obushaija-bukazi.

Omu minsi mikuru y'ensi, hati hariho ebiteiha n'amaka g'obushaija-bukazi.

Ekibiina ky'omwirima ky'abazigu b'omukazi, hati kigaireko ebikorwa ebirikutangaaza.

Abashomi baingi nibaija kutangaara aha "kukundana kw'okwemerize" kwonka titushemereire kwijuka ngu omu biro byona by'omutindo, hariho obutume bw'emishango.

Ekibiina eky'okwemerize ky'abazigu b'omukazi, tibarikubaasa kukurata butume bw'omushango.

Abazigu b'omukazi baguma bakwata emirimo eyorobi omu kiihaara.

Omushaija-bukazi ku agyenda omu kitoogo, ahweera ahinduka ahabw'omukago gw'abantu.

Omushaija-bukazi ku agwa omu buzibu, narukirwa omu by'emisaani.

Abaana b'omwirima b'obushaija-bukazi bareebanisa emigara yabo.

Nikishusha kureeba abashaija nibakoresa emigara, kwonka n'eka. Omugara gw'abashaija-bukazi guhwereera aha miringo. Abashaija-bukazi nibatandika emirongo. Muringo ku gumara kuba gyorobi, nibatandika ogundi. Omu muringo ogu, engaro y'ekibiina ky'omushango ebaire ensya.

Obureezi bw'ensi yona, hati burimu obumiriyooni bw'abashaija-bukazi.

Omucwe gw'obushaija-bukazi gutandika kuruga omu butiinagaroona.

Amashuuri maingi g'abatiinagaroona abashaija n'abashiki nibo ebiteiha by'obushaija-bukazi.

Obumiriyooni bw'abatiinagaroona abashiki nibagyenda omu muhanda gw'abazigu b'omushaija.

Obumiriyooni bw'abatiinagaroona abakazi nibo bashaija-bukazi. Ekibiina ky'omushango hagati y'obushaija-bukazi obw'abakazi kikagire nk'ekibiina ky'omushango hagati y'omushaija.

Nikihweera kugumaho kuruga omu burya, gworeka abatiinagaroona abashaija n'abakazi eby'embesa.

Obwo nibwo obwomeezi obusya nibwiija kworekwa omu muhanda gw'okutaho obusya.
