Kusooka omu mmeeza z'eshomero, abashomi bashemereire kumanya omu muringo oguhikire eki ekiitwa okukunda.

Obwoba n'okwesigama nibata omutwe hamwe n'okukunda kwonka tigwo kukunda.

Abashomi beesigama ha bazaire baabo n'abegyesi kandi nikirora haihi ngu nibabatiina kandi nibabatiinira omu kasumi kamwe.

Abaana bato n'abahara, abato n'abakazi beesigama ha bazaire baabo ahabw'ebyambaro, ebyokurya, esente, eihuro, n'ebindi, kandi nikirora haihi ngu nibeeshunga, nibamanya ngu nibeesigama ha bazaire baabo, ahabw'ekyo nibabatiina kandi n'okubatya, kwonka ogwo tigwo kukunda.

Ahabw'okworeka eki turikugamba, twine kukoresa obuhame ngu omuhara nari omwojo weena, omuto nari omukazi, naija kwesiga abanywani be omu shomero, okukira ha bazaire baabo.

Mubuzima, abahungu n'abahara, abato n'abakazi nibagamba n'abanywani baabo, ebintu by'omu mutima ebi bataagambe n'abazaire baabo.

Ekyo nikitworeka ngu tihariho kwesiga kw'amazima hagati y'abaana n'abazaire, ngu tihariho kukunda kw'amazima.

Okutiina n'okukunda bishemereire kuba hamwe, kwonka titushemereire kutahira omutwe ekimu n'ekindi.

Abazaire nibatiina abaana baabo, nibenda ebirungi byabo, omulimo murungi, obushwere burungi, oburinzi, n'ebindi, kandi nibatahira omutwe okutiina okwo n'okukunda kw'amazima.
