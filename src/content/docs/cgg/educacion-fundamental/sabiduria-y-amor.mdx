Amagezi n'okukunda nibyo bikuru omu by'obuteera omunsi yona ey'amazima.

Omu kibaa kya munzania y'oburingaaniza twine kuteekamu amagezi, omu kibaa ekindi twine kuteekamu okukunda.

Amagezi n'okukunda bishemereire kuringaaniza. Amagezi hatariho okukunda n'ekintu ekirikusaanya. Okukunda hatariho amagezi nikubaasa kututwara omu nshobe "OKUKUNDA NI MATEEKA KUGIRA NGU OKUKUNDA KUBE KWEESIMIRE".

Nikikuru muno kwega kandi tukeega ebintu bingi, kwonka nikyetengo kiingi okukura omuntu w'omwoyo omuriitwe.

Okumanya hatariho omuntu w'omwoyo akuzire gye omuriitwe, nikireeta ekintu ekirikwetwa obubonezi.

Omuntu w'omwoyo akuzire gye omuriitwe kwonka hatariho kumanya okw'omutwe kwona, nikireeta abahereza abashema.

Omuhereza shema aine omuntu w'omwoyo akuzire gye muno, kwonka ahabw'okuba tarimu kumanya okw'omutwe, tabaasa kukora kyona ahabw'okuba tazihire kukora.

OMUHEREZA shema aine amaani g'okukora kwonka tarikubaasa kukora ahabw'okuba tazihire kukora.

Okumanya okw'omutwe hatariho omuntu w'omwoyo akuzire gye nikireeta okwetantaza omu mutwe, obugumiisa, obweziriki, n'ebindi.

Omu bwire bw'olutalo rw'aha kabiri omu nsi yoona abasheeki bingi abatarimu kintu kyona eky'omwoyo omu iziina ry'esomo n'obuntu, bakakora ebyaha ebitiisya ahabw'okugyenda bakukora enyesherizo z'esomo.

Twine kwombeka obumanyi bw'omutwe obw'amaani kwonka oburingaanizibwe muno n'obwoyo bw'amazima obweesimire.

Twine obukuru bw'omutima obuhindura n'obwengye obuhindura kugira ngu twemazireho 'YO' kugira ngu tukure OMUNTU ow'Omwoyo omu mazima omuriitwe.

Kihwera amaani ngu ahabw'okubura okukunda abantu bakoresa OBWENGE omu muringo ogurikusaanya.

Abegi bashemereire kwega sayansi, ebyafaayo, eby'omubaro, n'ebindi.

Nikyetengo okwega ebyo omuntu akwetenga kukora, ahabw'okugira ngu tubashe kusangwaasa abantu bandi.

Kwega nikyetengo. Kweteraniriza hamwe eby'okutandika nikikuru, kwonka okutiina tikikuru.

Abantu baingi bateraniriza hamwe eby'okumanya ahabw'okutiina; Baine okutiina obwomeezi, okufa, enjara, obworo, ebi abantu barikwija kugamba, n'ekindi, kandi ahabw'enshonga egi nibega.

Omuntu ashemereire kwega ahabw'okukunda bagenzi baitu n'ekihika ky'okubaheereza gye, kwonka titushemereire kwega ahabw'okutiina.

Omu bwomeezi oburikukorwa twebasize kureeba ngu abegi boona abarikwega ahabw'okutiina, bwanyima bagaruka kuba ababonezi.

Twine kwejumba naitwe beene kugira ngu twebone kandi tubashe kureeba omu by'okutiina byona omu bwire obwo.

Titushemereire kwebwa omu bwomeezi ngu okutiina kwoine emiringo mingi. Obumwe okutiina nibwetantaza n'obugiraani. Abasirikare omu ruguuto rw'olutalo nibarora nk'abagiraani muno kwonka omu mazima nibahinduka kandi nibarwana ahabw'okutiina. Omuntu orikwita amagara ge namu araanga nk'omugiraani muno kwonka omu mazima n'omutiini orikutiina obwomeezi.

Buri mubonezi omu bwomeezi arikurangira nk'omugiraani muno kwonka omu mutima n'omutiini. Ababonezi nibakoresa omulimo n'obushobozi omu muringo ogurikusaanya obubatiina. Eky'okureeberaho; Castro Rúa; omuri Cuba.

Titarikugamba ebi twakubaire twareeba omu bwomeezi bw'okukora nari okukura obwenge, kwonka nitucwera omusango okubura kw'OKUKUNDA.

Okumanya n'ebi twareeba omu bwomeezi nibirora nk'ebirikusaanya obubura OKUKUNDA.

EGO eretaho ebi turikureeba n'okumanya okw'omutwe obura obwo ekirikwetwa OKUKUNDA.

EGO neshisha ebi turikureeba n'obwenge obu erikukoresa kukomezaho amaani gaayo.

Kusaanya EGO, YO, MI MISMO, ebi turikureeba n'obwenge bisigara omu mikono ya SER INTIMO kandi okweshisha kwona nikuba nk'ekitarikubaasika.

Buri omwegi ashemereire kurukurwa omu muhanda gw'eby'okukora kandi yeyegye muno teoli zonna ezirikukwata aha byo.

Okwega, obwenge, tibirikukora muntu mbi kwonka titushemereire kweshisha obwenge.

Twine kwega kugira ngu tuteshishe omutwe. Akoresa omutwe orikwenda kwega teoli ezirikukwata aha byo, orikwenda kukora abandi kubi n'obwenge, orikukora ekyondo aha mutwe gw'abandi, n'ebindi.

Nikyetengo kwega ebintu by'emilimo n'ebintu by'omwoyo kugira ngu tube n'omutwe oguringaanize.

Nikyetengo kiingi okuhika aha SÍNTESIS y'omutwe kandi n'aha síntesis y'omwoyo obutabaasa kuba turikwenda omutwe oguringaanize.

Abegyesa abakuru n'abato b'omunishomero, amakoreegi, Yunivasiti, n'ebindi, bashemereire kwega muno Sicologi yaitu ehindura obutabaasa kuba nibenda kutwara abegi baabo omu muhanda gw'OBUHINDUZI bw'omusingi.

Nikyetengo ngu abegi beegiire SER ESPIRITUAL, bakure omuri bo SER VERDADERO, kugira ngu baruge omu nishomero bagwire kuba abantu abesigwa hatariho babonezi bashema.

Tihaine mugasho gw'Amagezi hatariho Okukunda. Obwenge hatariho Okukunda nibureeta babonezi.

Amagezi omu byayo byo n'ekintu ky'Ahomu, omuhigo gw'Ahomu ogushemereire kukuzibwa abantu abaijwiire Okukunda kw'amazima.
