Amagezi n’okukunda nibyo mpagi nkuru z’obuteesi bwona obuhikire.

Omu kikopo ky’omuzaniro gw’oburingaaniza twine kuteekamu amagezi, omu kikopo ekindi twine kuteekamu okukunda.

Amagezi n’okukunda baine kuringaanizibwa. Amagezi hatariho okukunda n’ekintu ekirikushisha. Okukunda hatariho amagezi nikubaasa kututwara omu nshobi "OKUKUNDA N'EIRAGIIRO KWONA OKUKUNDA KURIHO OBWENGE".

Nikyetengyesa kwega munoona kukwata obwengye, konka nobukuru kutunguura omuriitwe EMBERA Y'OMWOYO.

Okumanya hatariho EMBERA Y'OMWOYO etunguriirwe gye omu buringaaniza omuriitwe, nikiza kuba enshonga y'ekyo ekirikwetwa OBUSHUMBO.

EMBERA etunguriirwe gye omuriitwe konka hatariho okumanya kw'omutwe kw'ekika kyona, neereza abaantu abarikwera abashazi.

Omuntu orikwera omushazi atungire EMBERA Y'OMWOYO etunguriirwe munoona, konka ahabw'okuba ataine okumanya kw'omutwe, tarikubaasa kukora kintu kyona ahabw'okuba atarikumanya oku akukora.

OMUNTU ORIKWERA omushazi aine amaani g'okukora konka tarikubaasa kukora ahabw'okuba atarikumanya oku akukora.

Okumanya kw'omutwe hatariho EMBERA Y'OMWOYO etunguriirwe gye kureeta obuteerana bw'omutwe, obushiishi, okwetunguura, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Omu Rwango Rw'Ensi Yoona orwa Kabiri aba sayansi obukaikuru abataireho eby'omwoyo byona omu iziina rya sayansi n'obuntu, bakakora enshisha ezitikushobororwa n'ekigyendererwa ky'okukora ebitebyo bya sayansi.

Nitwetenga kwombeka obumanyiso bw'omutwe obw'amaani konka oburingaaniziibwe munoona n'eby'omwoyo by'amazima ebiriyo obwenge.

Nitwetenga EMITWARIZE EY'OKUHINDUURA hamwe na PSICOLOGIA EY'OKUHINDUURA kwaba nitukwikiriza amazima kugyeza YEGO kwongyera EMBERA y'Eby'omwoyo omu buringaaniza omuriitwe.

Kihweiremu ahabw'okubura OKUKUNDA abantu bakoresa OBWENGE omu muringo ogurikushisha.

Abaana abeshomesa n'abashomesa abakazi nibetenga kwega sayansi, ebyafaayo, obubazi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Nikyetengyesa kukwata okumanya kw'emirimo, n'ekigyendererwa ky'okugasa abataahi.

Kwegyesa nikyetengyesa. Kubumbira okumanya kw'omusingi nikikuru, konka obwoba tibukikuru.

Abantu baingi nibabumbira okumanya ahabw'obwoba; baine Obwoba bw'amagara, orufu, enjara, obworo, eki abantu baraagambire, n'enshonga egi nibega.

Oshemereire kwega ahabw'okukunda abantu baitu abarikushusha naitwe n'ekyeteengo ky'okubakorera gye, konka toshemereire kwega ahabw'obwoba.

Omu magara g'okukora twabaasa kumanya ngu abeshomesa boona abarikwega ahabw'obwoba, kare nari bwangu nibahinduka abashumbo.

Nitwetenga kweshemeza naitwe bene kwetegyereza n'okushanga omuriitwe bene emigyenderezo yoona y'obwoba.

Tutushemereire kwirwa omumagara ngu obwoba buine ebicuuku bingi. Obumwe obwoba nibutwarwa nk'amaani. Abaserikale omu kibiinja ky'orutaro nibareebeka nka ab'amaani munoona konka omu mazima nibeeshisha n'okurwana ahabw'obwoba. Omuntu orikwiita naye areebeka aha maisho nk'omuntu w'amaani konka omu mazima ni omutiina orikutiina amagara.

Buri mushumbo omumagara areebeka nka omuntu w'amaani konka aha mutima ni omutiina. Abashumbo nibakoresa omurimo n'obushobozi omu muringo ogurikushisha obu baine obwoba. Eky'okureeberaho; Castro Rúa; omuri Cuba.

Tutarikwemerera kuhakanisa ekikumanyisa amagara g'okukora nari kwombeka obwenge, konka nitushisha okubura kw'OKUKUNDA.

Okumanya n'ebikumanyisa amagara nibireeta okushisha obu okukunda kubwireyo.

EGO etwara ebikumanyisa amagara n'okumanya kw'omutwe obu okubura kw'ekyo ekirikwetwa OKUKUNDA kuriyo.

EGO ekoresa kubi ebikumanyisa amagara n'obwenge obu ekukoresa kwombeka yenyine.

Ninkugyaho EGO, YEGO, MI MISMO, ebikumanyisa amagara n'Obwenge bisigaire omu mikono ya SER INTIMO kandi okukoresa kubi kwona nikuba kutahika.

Buri mweshomesa ashemereire kugyendera omu muhanda gw'omurimo kandi yegye ahansi teoriya yoona erikukwatanisa n'omurimo gwe.

Okwega, obwenge, tibukushisha omuntu weena okukira konka titushemereire kukoresa kubi obwenge.

Nitwetenga kwega obutatukoresa kubi omu mutwe. Akoresa kubi omu mutwe ogurikwenda kwega teoriya z'emirimo etari emwe, ogurikwenda kuhuura abandi n'obwenge, ogukora eby'obusharara aha mutwe gw'ondiijo, n'ebindi. n'ebindi. n'ebindi.

Nikyetengyesa kwega ebintu by'emirimo n'ebintu by'omwoyo kutunga omu mutwe oguringaaniziibwe.

Nikikuru kuhika aha SÍNTESIS y'omutwe n'aha SÍNTESIS y'Eby'omwoyo kwaba nitukwikiriza amazima omu mutwe oguringaaniziibwe.

Abeshomesa hamwe n'abashomesa abakazi b'amasomero, amakuru, yunivasite, n'ebindi, baine kwega ahansi obukuru Sicología yaitu ey'Okuhinduura kwaba nitukwikiriza amazima kuhiga abeshomesa baabo omu muhanda gw'OKUHINDUURA OMUKURU.

Nikyetengyesa ngu abeshomesa bakwate EMBERA Y'OMWOYO, batunguure omuri bo bene EMBERA EY'AMAZIMA, ngu baruge omu isomero bahindukire abaantu abeesigwa kandi hatariho ABASHUMBO abashazi.

Tihagasa amagezi hatariho Okukunda. Obwenge hatariho Okukunda nibureka abashumbo.

Amagezi omuri yo yenyine ni SUSTANCIA ATÓMICA, eikuru rya ATÓMICA eryokireeta erishemereire kwebemberwa abantu abaijwiire Okukunda kw'amazima.
