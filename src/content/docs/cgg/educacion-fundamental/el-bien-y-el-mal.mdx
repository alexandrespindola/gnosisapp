Ebirungi n’ebibi tibiriwo. Ekintu kiba kirungi bwekitugasa, ate kiba kibi bwekitatugasa. Ebirungi n’ebibi kyekikwatagana n’obuggya, ate n’ebirooto by’omutwe.

Omusajja eyayiiya ebigambo eby’akabi ebyo ebya BIRUNGI na BIBI yali Munnayatulante ayitibwa MAKARI KRONVERNKZYON mmemba omukulu owa ekibiina kya Sayansi ekya AKALDAN, ekisangibwa ku lukalu olwazikibwa olwa Atulantisi.

Omugezi omukulu owa edda teyateebereza bulabe bungi bwe yali agenda okuleeta eri abantu, ng’akozesa ebigambo bye ebibiri.

Abegeezi ba Atulantisi baasoma nnyo amaanyi gonna ag’OKUKULA, AG’OKUKENDEERA n’ATALI NDEWO mu BUTONDE, naye omukulu omugezi ono yajja n’ekirowoozo ky’okunnyonnyola ebiri ebisooka ng’akozesa ebigambo BIRUNGI na BIBI. Amaanyi ag’ekika kya KUKULA yagayitanga malungi, ate amaanyi ag’ekika kya KUKENDEERA yagabattiza erinnya libi. Amaanyi ag’obutali ndewo teyagatuuma mannya.

Amaanyi ago gakolebwa munda mu muntu ate ne munda mu butonde, nga amaanyi ag’obutali ndewo ge gakuumira ebintu nga biri mu kyenkanyi.

Oluvannyuma lw’emyaka mingi olukalu lwa ATULANTISI bwe lwazikibwa ne POISEDONIS yaalyo ennyimpala, Platón gyayogererako mu kitabo kye ekiyitibwa Republic, waaliwo mu byafaayo by’abantu ab’ebuvanjuba ekiyitibwa TIKLYAMISHAYANA omusumba omukulu ennyo eyakola ensobi ey’amaanyi ey’okukozesa ebigambo BIRUNGI na BIBI mu ngeri embi okulamula empisa z’abantu. Erinnya ly’omusajja oyo omusumba lyaali ARMANATOORA.

Nga ebiseera bigenda, abantu baayonoonebwa ebigambo ebibiri ebyo ne babifuula omusingi gw’amateeka gaabwe gonna ag’empisa. Ennaku zino ebigambo ebibiri ebyo obisanga buli wantu.

Mu kiseera kino waliwo ABALONGOOSA bangi abaagala OKUDDAABIRIZA EMPIISA naye eky’ennaku eri bo ate n’ensi eno ennaku, emitwe gyabwe gijjudde ebigambo BIRUNGI na BIBI.

Empisa zonna zeesigamiziddwa ku bigambo BIRUNGI na BIBI, yensonga lwaki OMULONGOOSA W’EMPIISA yonna mu butuufu MUDDA MABUDA.

Ebigambo BIRUNGI na BIBI bikozesebwa bulijjo OKWOGGERA Kintu oba OKUVUMIRIRA ensobi zaffe.

Ayoogera oba avumirira tategeera. Kiba ky’amagezi okutegeera okukula kw’amaanyi ag’OKUKULA, naye teky’amagezi okugaanukiriza n’ekigambo BIRUNGI. Kiba ky’amagezi okutegeera ebikolebwa mu maanyi ag’OKUKENDEERA naye kiba busirusiru okugavumirira n’ekigambo BIBI.

Amaanyi gonna agava mu muntu gayinza okufuuka amaanyi agadda mu muntu. Amaanyi gonna ag’OKUKENDEERA gayinza okukyuka ne gafuuka AG’OKUKULA.

Munda mu bikolwa by’amaanyi ebitagambika ebiri mu mbeera y’OKUKULA waliwo ebikolwa by’amaanyi ebitagambika ebiri mu mbeera y’OKUKENDEERA.

Munda mu buli muntu waliwo ebika by’amaanyi eby’enjawulo EBIKULA, EBIGENDEERA ate ne bikyuka butalibaako lwewayo.

Okwaanukiriza ekika ky’amaanyi ekimu n’okuvumirira ekirala, si kutegeera. Ekikulu kwe kutegeera.

Okumanya AMAZIMA kubeeraddewo busaawe mu bantu olw’ekintu ekyo eky’okuba nti emitwe gyabwe gijjudde ebigambo. Abantu bajjudde enjawulo eziri wakati wa BIRUNGI na BIBI.

OBUMANYI BW’OMUNTU OBUPYA obwa EKIBIINA kya GNÓSTICO bukwata ku kusoma ebika by’amaanyi eby’enjawulo ebikolera munda mu mubiri gw’omuntu ate ne munda mu butonde.

EKIBIINA kya GNÓSTICO kirina EMPIISA EMPYA etekwatagana n’empisa za abadda mabuda oba ebigambo eby’edda ebya BIRUNGI na BIBI.

Munda mu labulatore y’Omubiri gw’Omuntu waliwo amaanyi ag’okukula, ag’okukendeera n’agatali ndewo, agasaanidde okusomebwa ate ne gategeerekeka mu bujjuvu.

Ekigambo BIRUNGI kiremesa OKUTEGEERA amaanyi ag’OKUKULA olw’okugaanukiriza.

Ekigambo BIBI kiremesa okutegeera amaanyi ag’OKUKENDEERA olw’okugavumirira.

Okwaanukiriza oba okuvumirira te kitegeeza kutegeera. Oyo ayagala okuggyawo ensobi ze tasaanidde kugaanukiriza wadde okuzivumirira. Kyanguwa OKUTEGEERA ensobi zaffe.

Okutegeera OBUSUNGU ku mitendera gyonna egy’omutwe kye kikulu okusobola okutuyamba okufuna obuteefu n’obuwombeefu.

Okutegeera ebikosa obugayaavu kye kikulu okusobola okutuyamba okufuna okwagala abantu n’okuyamba abalala.

Okutegeera obwenzi ku mitendera gyonna egy’omutwe kye kikulu okusobola okutuyamba okufuna obuyonjo obw’amazima.

Okutegeera obuggya mu bitundu byonna eby’omutwe kimaze okutuyamba okufuna omukwano n’okwenyumiriza mu bulamu bw’abalala n’enkulaakulana.

Okutegeera amalala mu ngeri n’emitendera gyonna kye kimu ku by’okutandikirako okusobola okutuyamba okufuna okwetoowaza mu ngeri ennyangu.

Okutegeera ekintu ekyo ekiremesa okukola ekiyitibwa obugayaavu, si mu mbeera embi zokka naye ne mu mbeera ennyangu, kye kikulu okusobola okutuyamba okufuna okukola.

Okutegeera engeri ez’enjawulo ez’OBULULU n’emmere kye kimu n’okuzikiriza emize gy’oku mutendera ogw’omunda, gamba ng’embaga, okutamiira, okuyigga, okulya ennyama, okutya okufa, okwagala okubeerawo, okutya okuzikirizibwa, n’ebirala.

Abasomesa b’amasomero, colleggiyo n’amatendekero bagamba abayizi baabwe okukyusa nga singa YE ayinza okukyuka, okufuna engeri ezimu ennungi nga singa YE ayinza okufuna engeri ennungi, n’ebirala.

Kyanguyiriza okutegeera nti YE takyuka, nti tamalira ddala ate nti oyo alulunkanira engeri ennungi anyweza YE.

OKUMALIRIZA okujjuvu kuzalibwa munda mu ffe okusaanuuka kwa YE. Engeri ennungi zizalibwa munda mu ffe mu ngeri entonde n’ennyangu bwe tutegeera ensobi zaffe ez’omunda si ku mutendera gw’amagezi gwokka naye ne mu bitundu byonna eby’omutwe ebyekusifu n’ebitatangaaza.

Okwagala okukyusa busirusiru, okwagala obutukuvu buggya, okululunkanira engeri ennungi kitegeeza kunyweza YE n’obutwa bw’omululu.

Twetaaga okufa okujjuvu kwa YE si ku mutendera gw’amagezi gwokka naye ne mu bitundu byonna, ebyalo, ettaka n’enguudo z’omutwe. Bwe tuba tufudde ddala, kisigala mu ffe KIRI KYO KIMU ekituukirivu. EKIRYO KIMU ekijjudde engeri ennungi, EKIRYO KIMU bwe BUTONDE bwaFFE OBW’OMUNDA, EKIRYO KIMU ekitali kya biseera.

Bwe tutegeera ebikolwa byonna ebitagambika eby’amaanyi ag’okukula ebikula munda mu ffe wano era kati. Bwe tutegeera mu bujjuvu engeri ez’enjawulo ez’amaanyi ag’OKUKENDEERA agakolebwa munda mu ffe buli kiseera, tusobola okusaanuusa YE.

Ebigambo BIRUNGI na BIBI bikozesebwa OKWOGGERA Kintu n’OKUVUMIRIRA naye tekiberaawo kutegeera.

Buli nsobi erina emirembe mingi, emisingi, ennyingo n’obunzivu. Okutegeera ensobi ku mutendera gw’amagezi tekitegeeza nti ogitegeera mu bitundu byonna eby’omutwe ebyekusifu, ebitalangaaza n’ebisukkulumu.

Ensobi yonna eyinza okubula ku mutendera gw’amagezi ne yeeyongera mu bitundu ebirala eby’omutwe.

OBUSUNGU bwekwasa ekyambalo ky’Omulamuzi. Bangi balulunkanira obutaba bagayaavu, waliwo abatagayaaza ssente naye balulunkanira amaanyi g’omutwe, engeri ennungi, okwagala essanyu wano oba oluvannyuma lw’okufa, n’ebirala, n’ebirala.

Abasajja n’abakazi bangi basanyuka era ne basikirizibwa abantu b’ekika ekirala “NGAMBA” olw’okuba baagala obulungi, ebyama byabwe bibaleeta emabega, OBWENZI bwekwasa okutegeera obulungi.

Abantu bangi abalina obuggya balina obuggya eri abatukuvu era bakola emisango era beekuba olw’okuba nabo baagala okufuuka ABATUKUVU.

Abantu bangi abalina obuggya balina obuggya eri abo abeewaayo eri abantu era oluvannyuma nga baagala okufuuka abakulu nabo, banyooma abo be balina obuggya nabo era ne babalumba n’oludda lwaabwe oluvuma.

Waliwo abeenyumiriza mu kifo, ssente, ettutumu n’ekitiibwa ate waliwo abeenyumiriza mu mbeera yaabwe eya wansi.

Diyogene yeenyumirizanga mu Nteete eyasulanga era bwe yatuuka ew’amaka ga Socrates yamulamusa ng’agamba nti: “Nnyimiridde ku malala go Socrates, nnyimiridde ku malala go”. “Yee Diyogene, n’amalala go onyimiridde ku malala gange”. Gyali ennyanukula ya Socrates.

Abakazi abeegulumiza beesiba enviiri, bambala era beetikka ebintu byonna bye basobola okusobola okuleeta obuggya mu bakazi abalala, naye OKWEGULUMIZA nakwo kye kwasa ekyambalo eky’okwetoowaza.

Olugero lugamba nti Aristipo omugezigezi Omuyonaani nga yagala okulaga abantu bonna amagezi ge n’obwetoowaze yambala olugoye olukadde era olujjuddemu ebinnya, yakwata omuggo gw’Obugezigezi mu mukono gwe ogwa ddyo era n’agenda mu nguudo za Atenasi. Socrates bwe yamulaba ng’ajja, yayogera nti: “Obwegulumivu bwo bulabika okuyita mu binnya eby’engoye zo, ai Aristipo”.

Bangi bali mu bwavu olw’ekintu ekiremesa obugayaavu, naye waliwo abantu abakola nnyo okusobola okweyimirizaawo naye bawulira obugayaavu okusoma n’okweamanya okusobola okusaanuusa YE.

Bangi balekedde awo OBULULU n’emmere naye eky’ennaku batamiira era ne bagenda okuyigga.

Buli nsobi ya njawulo era ekula era ekolebwa mu ngeri entonde okuva ku sseminti asinga obuwufu ku kaliba ak’Omuntu okutuuka ku sseminti asinga okuba waggulu.

Munda mu lunyiriri oluwa essanyu olw’oluyimba, n’omusango gwekweka.

Omusango nagwo gwekwasa Omuntu Omutukuvu, Omunaku, Omuyonjo, Omutume, n’ebirala.

EBIRUNGI n’embi te biriwo, ebigambo ebyo bikozesebwa kwemulula ate n’okwewala okusoma okw’omunda n’okulambulukufu ku nsobi zaffe.
