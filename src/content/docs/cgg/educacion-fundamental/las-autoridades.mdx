Obutegyeki bwiine OBUSHOBOROZI, IHANGA lyo libiine OBUSHOBOROZI. Abaserikale, amateeka, omujaasi, abazaire, abegyesa, abarikwegyesa eby'obusinge, n'ebindi, baine OBUSHOBOROZI.

Hariho amataare abiri g'OBUSHOBOROZI. Ery'okubanza, OBUSHOBOROZI BW'OKUTARIKUMANYA. Ery'akabiri, OBUSHOBOROZI BW'OKUMANYA.

OBUSHOBOROZI BW'OKUTARIKUMANYA tihurikumarwa. Nitwetaga n'amaani OBUSHOBOROZI BW'ABARIKWEMANYA.

OBUSHOBOROZI BW'OKUTARIKUMANYA buzizire ensi amarira n'obusaasi.

Omu maka n'omu isomo OBUSHOBOROZI BW'OKUTARIKUMANYA nibukoresa kubi OBUSHOBOROZI ahabw'okuba tibarikumanya.

Abazaire n'abegyesa abatarikumanya, obwo tibari abegyese b'abahuzi kandi nk'oku Ebyahandiikwa birikugamba, boona baryagwera omu mwena.

Abazaire bari OBUSHOBOROZI BW'OKUTARIKUMANYA nk'oku kirikworekwa ahabw'okukora aha baana nk'enyanda, nk'okugira ngu nibarikukiraho abantu.

Abegyesa nibarumwa abanyeshuri bamwe, kandi nibatendela abandi. Obumwe nibahanga omunyeshuri orumwa n'obu araabe atari mubi kandi nibaha amanota g'ahaiguru abanyeshuri abamwe abarikuba batarikugasa.

Abazaire n'abegyesa omu masomero nibaha abaana ebiragiro bibi, abashangwa, abato, abashija, n'abandi.

OBUSHOBOROZI obutarikwiine OKWEMANYA nibukora ebintu ebitarikumarwa.

Nitwetaga OBUSHOBOROZI OBW'OKWEMANYA. Ni kigambwa ngu OKWEMANYA n'OKUMANYA OMUBIRI GWAWE GOONA, okumanya ebirungi byaitu byoona.

Owine okumanya ebirungi byawe byoona, naazinduka omu muringo oguhikire. Ekyo nikyo okugira OKWEMANYA.

Omuntu weena naikiriza ngu na kwemanya, kwonka kiragumira kushanga omu magara omuntu orikumanya omubiri gwawe. Abantu baine obuteekateeki burikukwata ahari bo buri buri.

Kwemanya nikwetaga amaani maingi n'ag'obutiinisa. Obu tukumanya omubiri gwaitu, nituhika aha KWEMANYA.

OKUKORESA KUBI OBUSHOBOROZI kuruga aha BUTARIKUMANYA. OBUSHOBOROZI BW'OKWEMANYA tiburikukoresa kubi OBUSHOBOROZI.

Abafilizoofa bamwe nibazinduka okurwana na OBUSHOBOROZI, nibaronda OBUSHOBOROZI. Omuringo ogwo gw'okuteekateeka niguba gw'obushuba ahabw'okuba omu bintu byoona ebyahangirwe, okuruga aha tunyama twato tw'ahaiguru, hariho ebiremera n'ebiremera, amaani g'ahaiguru agarikuragira kandi agarikwebembera n'amaani g'ahaansi agarikwebemberwa kandi agarikuragirwa.

Omu kiiziba ky'enyuki hariho obushobozi omu MUGABE. Omu rutara rw'ebisoro buriho obushobozi n'amateeka. Okutsiga obushobozi nikikuruza obutari buteeka.

OBUSHOBOROZI bw'obwire obu turimu bwa butiinisa, nibw'okutari kumanya kandi nikyoreka ngu ahabw'ekyo, nibagira abahuuku, nibaboha, nibakoresa kubi kandi nibareeta obusaasi.

Nitwetaga ABAGYESA, abarikwegyesa nari abebemberi ab'eby'omwoyo, ab'obushobozi bw'obuteegyeki, abazaire, n'abandi, abarikutenderezebwa ahabw'okwega. Obwo nitukora ensi erungi.

N'ekishema kugamba ngu titurikwetaga abegyesa n'abebemberi ab'eby'omwoyo. N'ekishema okugaana obushobozi omu bintu byoona ebyahangirwe.

Abo abarikukira abandi, abarikwejumbura, nibatekateeka ngu ABAGYESA n'ABEBERI AB'EBY'OMWOYO, TI BATWEETAAGA.

Twetengesa okumanya embabazi zaitu. Twetengesa okwetenga OBUSHOBOROZI, ABAGYESA, ABARIKWEGYESA EBY'OMWOYO, n'ebindi. KWONKA ABARIKWEMANYA tubasobole kutwebembera, kutuhwera, kutwebembera omu buryo burungi.

OBUSHOBOROZI BW'OKUTARIKUMANYA bw'ABAGYESA nibutsiga amaani g'okuhanga g'abanyeshuri. Omunyeshuri yayega, omwegyesa atarikumanya naamugambira eki arikwetenga kwega, ekiti nari ekiragiro eki arikwetenga kukopiinga kandi omunyeshuri naaraganwa okuruga omu biragiro by'omwegyesa.

Ekyo t'okuhanga. Kikuru omunyeshuri kuba omuhangi. Kusonsobora obushoboorozi bw'omwegyesa atarikumanya, asobole kworeka ekyo arikuhurra okukwata aha kiti, omugisha gw'amagara ogurikukwera omu matooki g'ekiti, obusinge bwaakyo bwona.

OMWEGYESA ORIKUMANYA tarikurwana n'okuhanga kw'omwoyo.

ABAGYESA abaine OBUSHOBOROZI BW'OKUMANYA, tibarikukata ebiteekateeki by'abanyeshuri.

Abegyesa ABATARIKUMANYA nibatsiga obushobozi obuteekateeki n'amagezi g'abanyeshuri.

ABAGYESA abaine OBUSHOBOROZI BW'OKUTARIKUMANYA, nibamanya kuhanga kandi nibaha ebiragiro ebishema ahabw'okugira ngu abanyeshuri bagire omutwaro murungi.

ABAGYESA ABARIKWEMANYA nibegyesa abanyeshuri omu muringo murungi, nibabahwera kwetegyereza obugumikiirizi bwaabo buri omwe, basobole kwetegyereza ebishuba byaabo byoona n'okweyongyera omu maisho omu buryo burungi.

OBUSHOBOROZI BW'OKUMANYA nari OBUSHOBOROZI BW'OKWEMANYA tiburikutsiga AMAGEZI.

OBUSHOBOROZI BW'OKUTARIKUMANYA nibutsiga AMAGEZI kandi nikireeta obusaasi bw'amaani aha banyeshuri.

Amagezi nigaza ahariitwe obu tujiine obugabe kandi ABAGYESA ABARIKUGIRA OBUSHOBOROZI BW'OKWEMANYA nibamanya okutwara ekitiinisa ahari OBEBERI BW'OKUHANGA.

Abegyesa ABATARIKUMANYA nibakikiriza ngu nibamanya byoona kandi nibanyaga obugabe bw'abanyeshuri nibatsiga amagezi gaabo n'ebiragiro byabo ebitari by'amagara.

Abegyesa ABARIKWEMANYA BAMANYA ngu tibarikumanya kandi nibamanya okwega nibareeba obushoboorozi bw'abegyi baabo.

Kikuru ngu abanyeshuri b'omu masomero, akoleeji na yunivasiti, baruge omu mbeera y'okurondera ebiragiro, baza omu mbeera y'abantu abarikugira amagezi n'obugabe basobole kurwana n'obugumikiirizi bw'obumagara.

Eki nikyetaga ABAGYESA ABARIKWEMANYA, abakomeezi abarikutwara omutima abegyi baabo, abegyesa abarikuhaibwa omusaara murungi kutasiima eby'entaabo.

Eky'obusaasi, buri MWEGYESA, buri mwezire, buri mwezi naakiriza ngu NA KWEMANYA. NAZINDUKA kandi ekyo nikyo KISHUBA KIKURU.

Kiragumira kushanga omu magara omuntu orikwemaanya kandi azindukire. Abantu barota obu omubiri gugwejere kandi nibarota obu omubiri guba guri omu mbeera y'okurinda.

Abantu nibasaha emootoka, nibarota; nibakora nibarota; nibagyeenda omu nguuto nibarota, nibatuura obwiire boona nibarota.

Nikyakabiita ngu omwegyesi yeerabire omucungwa nari ngu asigye ekitabo omu motoka. Ebyo byoona nibibaho ahabw'okugira ngu omwegyesi aine omutima ogwejere, na rota...

Kiragumira abantu kwikiriza ngu nibariroota, omuntu weena naakiriza ngu azindukire. Omuntu ku yaakiriza ngu aine omutima gwejere, nikyoreka ngu kuruga obwo naatandika kuzinduka.

Omunyeshuri yeerabira ekitabo omu ka, nari ekahuku akurikwetenga kutwara omu isomo, okwerabira oku nikureebeka nk'okwa butoosha kwonka nikworeka omutima gw'abantu omu mbeera y'okurota.

Abagyenzi omu bintu byoona ebirikutwara abantu, nibasigaza ebiragiro, baba bariroota kandi obu bazinduka nibabonekereza ngu basigaziire ebiragiro kandi hati nibagenda kugaruka aha maguru ebiragiro bimumwe.

Obumwe omu magara, omuntu naazinduka bwabaire, n'obu araabe abaireho obwiire bumwe, nk'omu mbeera z'okutiina, naareeba omubiri gwawe gwoona. Obwiire obwo tibwerabirwa.

Omushaija orikugaruka omu ka yaheza kucweeza ekicweka, kiragumira kwijuka ebiteekateeki bye byoona, ebintu, abantu, ebintu, obuteekateeki n'ebindi. Omu kugeza kwijuka, naashanga okwerabira okwa maani omu kwijuka kwe okurikukwata aha mbeera z'okuroota okw'amaani.

Abanyeshuri ba sayikolaji bamwe bakeejumbura kutuura beebemberire okuruga obwiire obu nibazinduka, talibaaha nibagwejera, talibaaha nibashanga omukundwa omu nguuto, omu magazaani kukozesa ekintu, n'obu haabe hariho esaaha nyingi nibaijuka okucweeza kubaho beebemberire okuruga obwiire obu nibazinduka, obwo nibamanyisa ngu babaire bagwejere obu baingire omu kifo ekyo, nari obu bashangire omuntu ogwo, n'ebindi.

Kugira OKWEMANYA nikigumira kwonka noosobora kuhika aha mbeera egi obegyeire kutuura beebemberire kandi nibarinda KURUGA OMWIREE OBU.

Twenda kuhika aha KWEMANYA nitwetenga kwemanya omubiri gwaitu woona.

Itwe toona twine MMI, OMUBIRI GWANGE, EGO ya kuronda twemanye tukure kwemanya kandi tukure ABARIKWEMANYA.

Kiriho n'amaani okwerunda, KUCWEERA n'OKWETEGEREZA buri kamwe aha butahi bwaitu.

Kikuru kwerunda omu kiteekateeki, okuhurra, emikora, enyeegyesa n'obutakuru.

Obuteekateeki buriine EBIREMA BINGI, enshonga nari ebicweka EBIRIKUTEEREZA EBIHUUHA ebi turikwetenga kumanya kurabira omu KWERUNDA, okucweera, okuteekateeka kandi n'OKWETEGEREZA OKW'OKUGYENDA OMU MAISHO.

Obutahi obu burikubasa kusaana omu kiteekateeki kandi nibagumizamu kubaho omu miringo y'okwerabira omu biteekateeki.

Ekintu kya bwangu eky'okwetenga n'OKUZINDUKA kwetegyereza enshonga zaitu, okuriisa n'obusaasi. Obwo nitutandika OMUBIRI KUFWA okuruga obwiire obwo. KIRIHO KWETENGA OKUFA KW'OMUBIRI GWA PSYCHOLOGICAL.

Ahabw'okufa niyo tuzaara omubiri ogurikwemaanya omuriitwe. Omubiri ogwo nigusobora kukoresa OBUSHOBOROZI BW'OKUMANYA.

KUZINDUKA, KUFA, KUZAARA. Ezi nizo mbeera za PSYCHOLOGICAL eziturikwetenga aha KUBAHO KW'AMANANU.

Kikuru kuzinduka tukafwa kandi kikuru kufa tukuzaare. Orikufa atazindukire afuuka OMURUNGI OBUSHEMA. Orikazaarwa atakafile afuuka OMUNTU OWEBUNTU ABIRI, ORUKURARIRE n'ORUBI.

Okukoresa OBUSHOBOROZI nikubasika kwesereswa abo abaine omubiri ogurikwemaanya.

Abo abatarikugira omubiri ogurikwemaanya, abo abatarikwemaanya, nibakoresa kubi OBUSHOBOROZI kandi nibareeta obusaasi bwingi.

ABAGYESA baine kwega kuragiira kandi abanyeshuri baine kwega kworobera.

Abasayikolajisti abo abararukurutwe omu kworobera nibashobya ahabw'okugira ngu tihariho orikubasa kuragiira omu buryo bw'okumanya ku yaheza kwega kworobera.

Kikuru kumanya kuragiira n'obwegendereza kandi nikikuru kumanya kworobera n'obwegendereza.
