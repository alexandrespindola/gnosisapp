Obutegyeki buine OBUTWEKYE, EKITIINISA ekihangwa kiine OBUTWEKYE. Abapolisi, amateeka, omuserikale, abazaire, abegesi, abakuratsi b'eddiini, n'ebindi, biine OBUTWEKYE.

Haliyo emigyerere ebiri y'OBUTWEKYE. Okubanza, OBUTWEKYE OBUTAMANYISA. Eyakabiri, OBUTWEKYE OBUMANYISA.

Tihagasa OBUTWEKYE OBUTARIKUMANYISA rundi OBUTWEKYE OBUTAMANYISA. Nitwetaga n'amaani OBUTWEKYE OBWEMANYISA.

OBUTWEKYE OBUTARIKUMANYISA rundi OBUTWEKYE OBUTAMANYISA bwijwiize ensi n'amaizi n'obusaasi.

Omu ka n'omu isomo, OBUTWEKYE OBUTAMANYISA bugwisa OBUTWEKYE kubanga tibukumanyise.

Abazaire n'abegesi abatukumanyise, obwahati nibo barongozi b'abazibe b'amaisho kandi nk'oku byahandiikirwe omu bihandiiko ebirikweera, boona bagwera omu bunya.

Abazaire n'abegesi abatukumanyise nibatugamba omu buto kukora ebintu ebitarikwetegereza, beitu nibatugamba ngu nibyo birungi. Nibagamba ngu eki nikikoleirwe ahabw'oburungi bwaitu.

Abazaire nibo OBUTWEKYE OBUTAMANYISA nk'oku kirikwoleka omu muringo gw'okutwaza abaana nk'ebisasi, nk'abantu abarikukira omuringo gw'omuntu.

Abegesi nabo nibemera barikwenda abeeshungu abamwe rundi abeeshungu abamwe, beitu nibakunda abeeshungu abamwe. Obumwe nibahabura abeeshungu abamwe nangwa n'obu bataba abantu babi, beitu nibaha abeeshungu abeeshungu abarikukunda ebirungi ebirungi nangwa n'obu bataba babirikwesimira.

Abazaire n'abegesi omu masomero nibaha abaana amateeka agahwerekire, abaana abato, abashaija abato, abakazi abato, n'ebindi.

OBUTWEKYE obutaine KWEMANYISA burakora ebintu ebitarikwetegereza.

Nitwetaga OBUTWEKYE OBWEMANYISA. Yetegeere KWEMANYISA n'OKUMANYA OKUJWIRE KW'OMUNTU, okumanya okujwire kw'EBY'OMUGASHA BYOONA EBY'OMU MUTIMA GWITU.

Oyo wenka aine okumanya kw'amaani okwajwire okw'OMUNTU, yaramukiirwe omu muringo ogujwire. Ekyo nikyo kwemanyisa.

Ensi yoona neeyenda kwemanya, beitu nikigumire kushanga omuntu omu magara ogwo akwikumanya. Abantu baine embeera eziihwire aha bwabo.

Kwikumanya nikikwetaaga amaani maingi n'ag'obutiinisa. Kureka okurikumarira omu KUMANYA KW'OMUNTU nikikureetera KUKUKWEMANYISA.

OKUGWISA OBUTWEKYE nikiruga omu BUTAMANYISA. Tiburakubaho OBUTWEKYE OBWEMANYISA bwagwisa OBUTWEKYE.

Abafilosofe abamwe bataine OBUTWEKYE, nibakenga OBUTWEKYE. Empamuro nk'eyi n'EY'OBUSHEGU kubanga omu byahangirwe byoona, kuruga aha kabobe okuhika aha izooba, haliho ebirango n'ebirango, amaani agari ahaiguru agata obwegendereza n'okuragira n'amaani agari ahansi agarikuteerwa obwegendereza n'okuragirwa.

Omu kicaafu ky'enyuki kikye haliho obutegyeki omu MUKAMA. Omu kirimbi ky'ebishishi kyona haliho obutegyeki n'amateeka. Okuhwereka engyezi y'OBUTWEKYE kurareeta ENTABURO.

OBUTWEKYE bw'obwire obu obugumire obuturimu nibwo BUTAMANYISA kandi nikikworeka ngu ahabw'ekintu eki EKITEREREZI, nibahwerera, nibasiba, nibagwisa, nibareeta obusaasi.

Nitwetaga abegesi, abahereza rundi abakuratsi b'omwoyo, ab'obuteegyeki, abazaire, n'ebindi, abemanyise buzima. Ekyo nikyo tukukora ensi erungi buzima.

N'obushegu kugamba ngu tibetaga abegesi n'abakuratsi b'omwoyo.

N'ekyahwekire kwereka engyezi y'OBUTWEKYE omu byahangirwe byoona.

Abo abajwire, ABAIKUNDA, nibagamba ngu ABEGESI n'ABAKURATSI B'OMWOYO, TITABETAAGWA.

Tushemereire kwereka OBUTWEKYE bwaitu n'ENSHONI. Tushemereire kwetegereza ngu nitwetaga OBUTWEKYE, ABEGESI, ABAHEREEZA B'OMWOYO, n'ebindi. BEITU ABEMANYISE ngu nibatukuruza, nibatuhwera kandi nibaturongoora obwengye.

OBUTWEKYE OBUTAMANYISA bw'ABEGESI nibuhwereka amaani g'okuhanga g'abaana. Omweeshungu yaazoba, omwegesi atarikumanyisa naamugambira eki ashemereire kuzoba, ekiti rundi ekyanga eki ashemereire kusaaho kandi omweeshungu atiintire tateera nkanda kwihaho amateeka g'omwegesi.

Ekyo tikwo kuhanga. Nikyetaga ngu omweeshungu abe muhangi. Abe asobora kwihaho amateeka g'omwegesi atarikumanyisa, ngu asobore kwihaho byoona ebi arikuhurira naakamu, oburungi bw'amagara oburikuzunguruka ebiragwe by'akamu, obugufu bwaago boona.

Omwegesi omumanyise tarikwiremesa obugabe bw'okuhanga bw'omwoyo.

ABEGESI abaine OBUTWEKYE OBUMANYISA, tibarakata omutwe gw'abaana.

Abegesi abatukumanyise nibahwereka emiteekateekire y'abaana n'obwengye bw'abaana baabo n'OBUTWEKYE bwabo.

ABEGESI abaine OBUTWEKYE OBUTAMANYISA, nibamanya okufeerera n'okugaba amateeka agataaine omugasha ngu abaana beeyendeze.

ABEGESI ABEMANYISE nibegesa n'obumanzi abeeshungu baabo, nibabahwera kwetegereza obugumire bwabo, ngu nibasobora kwetegereza okucwera ensobi zaabo zoona n'okugenda omu maisho n'okusingura.

OBUTWEKYE OBUMANYISA rundi OBWEMANYISA tiburakuhwereke OBWENGYE.

OBUTWEKYE OBUTAMANYISA nibuhwereka OBWENGYE kandi nibureeta ebizibu ebiija aha beeshungu.

Obwengye nibwija kwitu tukahurra obugabe obw'amazima kandi ABEGESI ABARINE OBUTWEKYE OBWEMANYISE nibamanya obw'amazima okuha obugabe bw'OKUHANGA ekitiinisa.

Abegesi abatukumanyise nibagamba ngu nibamanya byoona kandi nibagwisa obugabe bw'abaana nibabagamba ngu nibahwereka obwengye bwabo n'amateeka gaabo agarikufa.

Abegesi ABEMANYISE nibamanya ngu tibamanya kandi nibaronda n'okwegera aha bushoborozi bw'okuhanga bw'abeeshungu baabo.

Nikyetaga ngu abeeshungu omu masomero, amakuru n'abebembezi, baruge omu mbeera y'okuba abantu abagonda ab'obwegendereza, bahike aha kifo eky'amaani eky'abantu ab'obwengye n'ab'obugabe ngu basobore kusingura obugumire bw'amagara goona n'okusingura.

Eki nikikwetaaga ABEGESI ABEMANYISE, abakikora bagyenda omu maisho abaronda abeeshungu baabo, abegesi abarikuheebwa omusaara murungi ngu batenegwa n'enyekundi y'obugaga.

Ky'obusaasi, buri MWEGESI, buri muzaire, buri mweeshungu, neeyenda kuba KWEMANYISA. YARAMUKIIRE kandi ekyo n'ENSOBI YE ERIKUKIRA.

Nikisangwa kitari kyangu kushanga omuntu KWEMANYISA n'OKURAMUKIRA omu magara.

Abantu nibaroota omubiri gwabo gwoonire kandi nibaroota omubiri gwaabo gukikara.

Abantu nibagendesa emitooka, nibaroota; nibakora nibaroota; nibagyenda omu nguuto nibaroota, nibatuura nibaroota obutoosha.

N'ekyomutindo mwingi ngu omwegesi yaakwebwa akatama rundi yaareka ekitabo rundi ebipande bye omu mwitooka. Ebyo byoona nibibaho kubanga omwegesi aine okwetegereza kwoonire, naroota.

Nikigumire muno ngu abantu beetikirize ngu boonaire, buli omwe neeyenda ngu yaramukiire. Omuntu yaakwikiriza ngu aine okwetegereza kwe kumwoonire, kiri kweri ngu kuruga aha kikeesho kimwe arikwija kutandika kuramuka.

Omweeshungu orairirwe omwika, rundi omuhanda ogu ashemereire kutwara omu isomo, okwebwa okwo nikusaana kutwara nk'ekyomutindo kandi nikiba, beitu nikikwoleka embeera y'okwoona okuri omu okwetegereza kw'omuntu.

Abagendesi omu bihingo by'omu tauni, obumwe nibahita omu nguuto, niboonaire kandi nibaramuka nibamanya ngu bahitire omu nguuto kandi ngu obwahati nibo bagyenda n'amagaisha omu nguuto.

Ekiire kukubaho ngu omuntu yaramukiire buzima kandi obu aaramukiire obumwe, nk'omu mbeera y'obutiinisa butagira muheru, neereeba omuntu omu muringo ogujwire. Obwire obwo tiburairirwa.

Omushaija agaruka omu ka yaaba yaagendeseze tauni yoona, nikigumire muno kumuronderera kwoona ebi arikuteekereza, ebintu, abantu, ebintu, ebiteekateeko, n'ebindi. Obu arikuteekateeka, nasanga ebicuucu ebiija omu muringo gw'okuwoona kwingi.

Abegesi ba Psycology abamwe beihireyo okutuura nibaramuka kuruga omukika kuhika aha kika, beitu omu bwangu boona niboona, obworaaba nibashanga omunywani omu nguuto, nibataaha omu duka bagura ekintu, n'ebindi. Obu obwire bw'okucwa orubanja kuruga aha mirembe yabo okutuura nibaramuka n'okuramuka kuruga omukika kuhika aha kika, nibamanya ngu boonaire obu bataahire omu kifo, rundi obu bashangire omuntu, n'ebindi.

Kwemanyisa n'ekintu kigumire muno beitu nikisobora kuhika omu mbeera egi okwega okutuura nibaramuka n'okuteera eizooba kuruga OMUKIKA KUHIKA AHA KIKA.

Yaaba nitwenda kuhika aha KWEMANYISA nitwetaga kwikumanya omu muringo ogujwire.

Izoona twine EGO, EMINE, TUTANDIKIRE okwetaaga okugyenda okwetegyereza okwikumanya n'okugaruka KWEMANYISA.

N'EKIRINDI okwetegereza, okuronderera n'okwetegereza buli kimwe aha buleme bwaitu.

Nikyetaga okwetegyereza omu kicweka ky'obwengye, ebyenda, emizano, n'emikora.

Omutwe guine ebirango bingi, ebicweka rundi emigo g'okwetegyereza enungi ebi tushemereire kumanya okurabira omu KUTEERA EIZOBA, OKURONDERERA, OKUTEERERA EIZOBA, OKWETEGEREZA OMU MAISHO.

Obuleme bwona busobora kuherera omu kicweka ky'obwengye kandi bugumireyo omu ebirango bingi by'omutwe.

Ekintu ekibanza ekirikwetaga ni OKURAMUKA okwetegereza OBUTWEKYE BWAITU, OKWISHONGA, N'OUSAASI. Kandi EGO etandika KUFA kuruga aha kika kuhika aha kika. N'EKIRINDI OKUFA KW'EGO.

Okufa nikyo kizaara OMUNTU oine OKUMANYA kw'amazima omuritu. Oyo wenka nuwe aine OBUTWEKYE OBUMANYISA obw'amazima.

OKURAMUKA, OKUFA, OKUZAARA. Ezo nizo mpindo eshatu ezirikutureetera okubaho okw'amazima okurikukikara.

Oshemereire kuramuka okufa kandi oshemereire kufa okumuzaara. Oyufa ataramukire akugaruka omu KUHANGANA KUTARI KWAHANGA. Oyo azaarwa atafa akugaruka omuntu oine EMBEERA EBIIRI, omurungi muno n'omubi muno.

Okukora OBUTWEKYE obw'amazima nikusobora kukorwa abo abaine OMUNTU okumanya.

Abo abatakatunga OMUNTU OKUMANYA, abo abatakatunga KWEMANYISA, nibagwisa OBUTWEKYE kandi nibahwereka ebizibu bingi.

Abegesi bashemereire kwega okufeerera kandi abeeshungu bashemereire kwega okugonda.

Abo ba PSICOLOGOS abarugire ahabw'okugonda nibahwerire buzima kubanga tasobora kufeerera yaaba atakayegire kubanza kugonda.

Oshemereire kumanya okufeerera OMUMANYISA kandi oshemereire kumanya okugonda OMUMANYISA.
