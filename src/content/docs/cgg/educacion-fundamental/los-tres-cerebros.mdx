Okutendeka kw’obwengye obuhinduzi bw’obwire obusya nikukiriza ngu makina y’omubiri ey’ENYAMA ERIHO OBWENGYE erikwetwa omuntu, ehangirwe omu muringo gw’obutendeka bushatu nari obwongo bushatu.

Obwongo bw’okubanza nibukomire omunda y’ekyanga ky’omutwe. Obwongo bw’akabiri nibwo mugongo n’obwongo oburi omuhanda ogwo n’oburembo bwaago bwona. Obwongo bw’akashatu tiburi ahantu hamwe nari orugyendo orumwe. Buzima obwongo bw’akashatu nibwo butendeka bw’emigongo n’obuhwezi bw’emigongo y’omuntu.

Obwongo bw’okubanza nibwo bucentre bw’okuteekateeka. Obwongo bw’akabiri nibwo bucentre bw’okugyenda, obutungiirwe nk’obucentre bw’amaani. Obwongo bw’akashatu nibwo bucentre bw’okuhurra.

Kikakuratirwa omu kukora ngu okukoresa obwongo bw’okuteekateeka kukwata amaani maingi ag’obwengye. Nikiba nikyesiga okugamba hatariho okutiina ngu amalwaliro g’abantu abatakurora gari amariro g’abantu abafire omu bwengye.

Emizaano erikukwata hamwe n’ehurikire eri y’omugasho aha bwongo bw’okugyenda, kwonka okukoresa emizaano maingi nikimanyisa okukwata amaani maingi ag’okugyenda kandi ebirugamu nibiba bibi. Tikirikwesiga kugamba ngu hariho abantu abafire omu bwongo bw’okugyenda. Abantu abo nibamanya nk’abarwaire ba Hemiplejia, Paraplejia, Parálisis progresiva, n’ebindi.

Eky’okushisha, eky’okushisha, okukwata empindahinduka, enyimbo, nibihabura okukurra obucentre bw’okuhurra, kwonka okukoresa obwongo obwo nikureeta okucweecweeta okutari kw’omugasho n’okucweecweeta amaani g’okuhurra. Abakoresa obwongo bw’okuhurra nibo ab’okubaaho ab’ “omutindo omusya”, abarikugwera emyole y’Enganda, abahingi b’emikono abarikukurra, abarikutwara omu mikono, n’ebindi.

N’obu kirikweshusha kibe kitarikukirizibwa, okufa buzima nikukurata omu bitundu omu muntu weena. Ekyo kimaze kworeka ngu endwaire yoona eriyo omu bwongo obwo bushatu.

Eteeka rikuru rirekire omu bwongo obu bushatu bw’enyama eyo, omurwaaro gw’obusingye obw’AMAANI. Okutereera omurwaaro ogwo nikimanyisa kuhangura amagara, okucweecweeta omurwaaro ogwo nikireeta okufa.

Emigani y’enyakatura ezaahitireho okuza omu kiro kyoona, zikiriza ngu omuhendo gw’amagara g’omuntu omu nyika ya MU, eriyo omu Nyanja Pacific, ekagwera omu myaka ikumi na ebiri na ikumi na itaano.

N’okuhinduka kw’emyaka omu myaka yoona okukoresa obwongo obu bushatu okwabuzya amagara bukye bukye.

Omu ihanga rya KEM… omu Misiri ya Bakama omuhendo gw’amagara g’omuntu gwakomerere omuri myaka ikikumi na makumi ana.

Buzima omu bwire obu obwa benzina na celuloide, omu bwire bwa ab’okubaaho n’abayole b’enganda, omuhendo gw’amagara g’omuntu nk’oku ebiconco by’Obusingye birikugamba, n’emyaka makumi ataano.

Bakama ba Marxista-Leninista ba UNIÓN SOVIÉTICA, abarikubyatura ebihendo n’okushutama nk’oku bari, nibagamba ngu bakahanguura eddagala ry’omutaano ery’okuhangura amagara kwonka omukuru Kruschev tariho myaka makumi omuhendo kandi ateekwa kushaba omuguru gwe gw’okubanza okuyimuka ogundi.

Omu bucentre bwa ASIA hariho ekihunga ky’abadiini ekihaangirwe abakuru abatarijukire obuto bwabo. Omuhendo gw’amagara g’abakuru abo nikugwa omu myaka magana ana na magana ataano.

Ekyama ky’amagara maremare g’abakuru abo kigarukira omu kukoresa obwongo obu bushatu.

Okukora kw’oburinganiza okurikurora hamwe kw’obwongo obu bushatu nikimanyisa okutereera AMAANI kandi nk’entebekanisa, okuhangura amagara.

Hariho Eteeka ry’ebyanya erimanyirwe nk’ “OKUZAHAZA ENJURIRA ZOONA Z’ENSHURO NYINGI”. Abakuru b’eka eno nibamanya kukoresa eteeka eryo kurabira omu kukoresa obwongo obu bushatu.

Okubuurira okuri omumwanya kubuza abanyeshuri kukoresa obwongo bw’okuteekateeka enkomerero yabwo Psiquiatría yaamanya.

Okukuza obwongo obu bushatu n’OBWENGYE OBUSINGYE. Omu mashuuri g’obwihe omu Babilonia, Grecia, India, Persia, Egipto, n’ebindi, abanyeshuri bakatunga amakuru agatungiire obwongo bwabo bushatu kurabira omu kukoresa, okuzina, enyimbo, n’ebindi, ebirikukwatanisa.

Eby’emizano y’ebiro ebyahwire byaba biri ebitundu by’ishuuri. Drama, eby’okuseka, eby’obusaasi, nibikwatanisa n’emikono, enyimbo, okwegyesa ebigambo, n’ebindi. Byakozesezwe kuteekateeka obwongo bushatu bwa muntu weena.

Obwo abanyeshuri bakaba batarikukoresa obwongo bw’okuteekateeka kandi bakamanya kukoresa n’obwengye oburinganize obwongo bwabo bushatu.

Okuzina kw’ebwihe bya Eleusis omu Grecia, emizaano omu Babilonia, eby’okutunga omu Grecia byakozesezwe kukoresa obwengye aha bahingi.

Obwire obu omu bwire obu obw’okushisha obw’Enganda, abanyeshuri babuzirwe kandi nibakuratira omuhanda gw’omwirima gw’okukoresa obwongo.

Buzima tiharimu obutebeekanisa obuhanguzi bw’okukuza obwongo obu bushatu.

Abeekambi b’amashuuri, bacollege, na ba yunivasiti, nibagamba aha kyetengo ky’abanyeshuri abatayombeka abarikurinda n’obusaasi eizooba ry’okuruga omu rurembo.

Kiri eshumi, kiri ekikuru kumanya kukwatanisa obwengye, okugyenda, n’okuhurra omu bwihuriro bw’okutwara amakuru agatungiire obwongo bushatu bw’abanyeshuri.

Nikiba kitarikwesiga kuteekateeka obwongo bumwe. Obwongo bw’okubanza tibwo bwonyini bw’okumanya. Nikiba nikiteera amahe n’okukoresa obwongo bw’okuteekateeka bw’abanyeshuri.

OBWENGYE OBUSINGYE nibuza kwata abanyeshuri aha muhanda gw’okukurra.

Okutendeka kw’obwengye kworeka ngu obwongo obu bushatu buriyo emiringo ishatu y’omuhwezi erigyerereire eyaataana. Emihwezi egi ishatu neeta emitwarize etari emwe y’omubiri.

Ekyo kireetera obuzima emitwarize eshatu eyaataana etariho n’ekirikukwata hamwe omu buringaniza bwabyo nari omu kworekwa kwabyo.

Okutendeka kw’obwengye obuhinduzi bw’obwire obusya kwegyesa ngu omu muntu weena hariyo ebintu bishatu eby’obwengye ebyaataana. N’ekicweka kimwe ky’enshonga turikwenda ekintu, n’ekicweka ekindi turikwenda ekintu ekitaana buzima kandi ahabw’ekicweka ekya kashatu turikukora ekintu ekirikutaana.

Omu kasumi k’okusaasira okuri ahaiguru, obu turoobereize orukundo nari endwaire endiijo, oburinganiza bw’emitwarize bw’okuhurra nibuzaaha okucweecweeta obu oburinganiza bw’obwengye nibubuuza ahabw’enki ekyo kikaba kiri ekihangaanisa n’oburinganiza bw’okugyenda nibukwenda kukizibwaho.

Emitwarize egi eshatu eyaataana eshemereire kukuzibwa n’obwengye kandi etendekwe n’emiringo y’omutaano omu mashuuri, amacollege, na ba yunivasiti.

Kuruga omu buringaniza bw’obwengye nikiba kitarikwesiga kwegyesa buzima emitwarize ey’obwengye. Omuntu ayine emitwarize eshatu erikwetenga OBWENGYE OBUSINGYE.
