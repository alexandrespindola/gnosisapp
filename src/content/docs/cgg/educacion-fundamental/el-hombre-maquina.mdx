OMUSHAIJA EKIMEERA niyo ensoro erukukirayo obusaasi oburiyo omu kiikaro eki, kwonka aine, OKWESHAMURA na okuhitsya omu MAGARA okwetuma MWENE eihanga ly'OBUTONDE.

"NOCE TE IPSUN" "MUSHAIJA YEYORE". Eki nikyo ekihandiiko kya DHAHABI ekya ira ekihandikirwe aha bisika by'ekikoomi ebyatashemererwekywa eky'ekaalu ya Delfos omu BUGEREKA BWA IRA.

Omushaija, ensoro enyeeteete eyo y'OBWENGYE eyeyeta eky'obushobezi OMUSHAIJA, yazimbire ebimeera nkumi na nkumi eby'amakanga n'ebigumu kandi namanya gye ngu kubaasa kukora n'EKIMEERA, ebiro bimwe nibimwetagisya emyaka mingi y'okushoma n'okwega, kwonka kubaasa kukora na MWENE anyamaara kimwe aha mazima aga, nangwa n'obu yaaba yaaba EKIMEERA ekigumire kukira byona ebi yazimbire.

Tihariho mushaija ataine, ebicweka by'endimi ebishingire kimwe aha MWENE, ekigumire kukirayo n'okwetondera kwenda kukoma kumanya ngu buzima n'EKIMEERA.

Ekimeera ky'omuntu tikirikugira obugabe bw'okutambuka, nikikora kwonka habw'enganda nyingi n'enzigye ezirikwingana omu mutima n'omumaisho.

Okutambuka kwona, ebikorwa, ebigambo, endimi, ebirungi, okwetenga, okushiima, by'EKIMEERA ky'omuntu nibireetwa enganda ezirikwingana omu maisho n'obusaasi obwingi omu mutima obw'obusaasi n'obugumu.

ENSORO Y'OBWENGYE n'omugo gw'amajuta gw'aha bwereere ogw'okugamba n'okwijuka n'amaani, omuko gw'okwomeera, ogurimu oburofa bw'obushiisha, ngu n'obasa KUKORA, obwo buzima kuruga aha mazima atarikubasa KUKORA kantu.

Teekateeka akanya, omusomi omukundwa, omuko gw'ekimeera gw'enganda ezirikuragirwa endagiriro.

Teekateeka ngu omuko ogwo aine amagara, ayenda, nagamba, nagyenda, nayenda, nakora entaro, n'ebindi.

Teekateeka ngu omuko ogwo n'obasa kuhindura abanyine omumaiso goona. Oshemereire okuteekateeka ngu omwine omwe na buri omwe aine omutindo gwe, omuringo gwe gw'okweshemeza, okwetenga, okwomeera, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Omwine omwe kuronda kukwata esente nabaasa kukwata aha bitoezi ebimwe obwo omuko n'agukora ebikorwa, omwine ondiijo, eshaaha erikubaho bwangu nari eshaaha nyingi bwangu, aine omutwe ogurikwingana kandi n'ateeka omuko gwe okuziina na okuseka, ogw'obushatu naguteeka okurwana, ogw'okuna naguteeka okukunda omukazi, ogw'okutaano naguteeka okukunda ondiijo, ogw'omukaaga naguteeka okurwana n'omutuurani kandi nagwetondera ekizibu ky'abaserikale, n'ogw'omusanvu naguteeka okuhindura ahabw'omutwe.

Buzima omuko gw'eky'okureeberaho kaitu tigukozire kantu kwonka naikiriza ngu nikukozire, aine oburofa ngu NIKIKORA obwo buzima kuruga aha mazima atarikubasa KUKORA kantu habw'okuba taine OBUNTU BWE MWENE.

Aheeru y'okugyemesereza byona bigyezere nka okunyuka, okwera, okwozesa omushana, kwonka omuko omugara nikiriza ngu NIKIKORA; aine OBUTOOSA oburofa ngu byona yakozire obwo buzima takozire kantu, abanyine nibashemeza omuko gw'ekimeera.

Niko ensoro enyeeteete erikuba, omusomi omukundwa omuko gw'ekimeera nka ogw'eky'okureeberaho kaitu, akiriza ngu NIKIKORA obwo buzima atarikukora, n'omugo gw'enyama n'amagufa ogurikuragirwa LEGIÓN Y'ENDA Y'AMAANI ENTITI ezirikweteraniza nikyo kirukwetwa EGO, YO ERIKUBA NOBUNGI.

EBY'OMUKYALO EBIRIKUGAMBA nibita ENTITI ezo zona EBIGWI n'eiziina lyabo ery'amazima LEGIÓN.

Twagamba ngu YO n'LEGIÓN y'EBIGWI erikuragira ekimeera ky'omuntu, titurikukuza, nikwo kiri.

OMUSHAIJA-EKIMEERA taine BUNYE BWOMWE, tazenga kubeera n'OBUNTU, obwomeezi bw'amazima nibwo burimu AMAANI G'OKUKORA.

OBWOMEZI BWO BWENE nibwobasa kutuha OBUNTU BWE MWENE obw'amazima, obwomeezi nibutuha OMUSHAIJA OW'AMAZIMA.

Ogu buzima oyenda kureka kubeera omuko gw'ekimeera, ashemereire kusaanyaawo buri omwe aha nda ezo ezirukweteraaniza YO. Buri omwe aha ENTITI ezo ezirikuzana n'ekimeera ky'omuntu. Ogu buzima oyenda kureka kubeera omuko gw'ekimeera, ashemereire kutandika okwikiriza n'okwetondera embeera y'ekimeera kye mwene.

Ogu atenda kwetondera nari okwikiriza embeera y'ekimeera kye mwene, ogu atenda kwetondera amazima aga obuhangwa, tarikyasa kuhindura, n'omugara, omubi, akibaasa kukoma ahabw'omusingi gwe eibaare ry'okuhunisa kandi nayegwera omu nyanja.

ENSORO Y'OBWENGYE n'ekimeera, kwonka ekimeera ky'omutaano muno, ekimeera eki kikaraba kikamanya ngu EKIMEERA, kikaraba kikateeka muno kandi embeera zikaraba zikarikwikiriza, n'obasa kureka kubeera ekimeera kandi nayehindura OMUSHAIJA.

Enyima y'ebyona, kigwere okutandika okwetondera omu maisho n'omu mitwe yona, ngu titureeba obuntu bwo bwene, ngu tutaine OMUTIMA GW'OKUTUNURA OGW'OKUKOMERAHO, ngu omumaiso gwa nyamara turyaba turi omuntu kandi ondiijo, ondiijo; byona nibishinga aha ENTITI erikuragira embeera omu kashaanzi koona.

Ekyo ekirikweta OBUTOOSA bw'OBUNTU n'OBUNTU BWA ENTORO BENSORO Y'OBWENGYE nigashinga ekyo ekirimo ekihuriira OBUNTU BWAYO OBW'OMUBIRI, ahabw'okuhikiiriza eiziina lye n'amaiso ge kandi kumaliza okwijuka n'omubaro gw'oburofa bw'ekimeera obutaairwe omuri ye habw'EBWEZIMU, nari okweta ebintu by'ekirofa n'oburofa bw'okutoora.

ENSORO enyeeteete ey'OBWENGYE terikyasa kureka KUBEERA EKIMEERA, terikyasa kuhindura, terikyasa kureeba OBUNTU BWE MWENE OBW'AMAZIMA nayehindura omushaija omwesigwa, obwo HATARIHO omutindo gw'ESAALA OKUSAANYAAHO OMU KWETONDERA OMU MUTIMA n'omuringo ogurikubaho, buri omwe aha ENTITI ezo METAFIÍSICAS ezirikweteraniza nikyo kirukwetwa EGO, YO, MWENE.

Buri ENDIMI, buri KUSAASIRA, buri kitozi, buri KUKUNDA, buri KUROBA, buri okwetenga, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi. Aine ENTITI ey'omutaano kandi ekibuga ky'ENTAITI ezo zona niyo YO eri OKUBA NOBUNGI y'OBUTONDE bw'OKUHINDUKA.

ENTAITI ezo zona METAFIÍSICAS, ezo zona YOES ezirikweteraniza EGO, tibaine okwesiga kw'amazima hagati yabo, tibaine okukurataniza kw'omutindo gwona. Buri omwe aha ENTITI ezo nishinga kimwe aha mbeera, okuhindura enganda, ebyahikire, n'ebindi.

EPAREKO y'OMUTWE neehindura emibara n'ebishushani omu kasaanzi koona, byona nibishinga aha ENTITI erikuragira omutwe omu kashaanzi koona.

Ahabw'EPAREKO y'omutwe nibagenda omu kyanya ky'enteerane ky'ENTAITI ezirikwingana ezirikweteraniza EGO nari YO PSICOLÓGICO.

ENTAITI ezirikwingana ezirikweteraniza YO OKUBA NOBUNGI nibagaitwa, nibagaitwa, nibatoonya ekibuga ky'omutaano hakukurataniza kwabo, nibarwana hagati yabo, nibagaragura, nibayangaane, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Buri ENTITI y'OLEGIÓN erukwetwa YO, buri YO omuhango, nikiriza kubeera byona, EGO YONA, atashukumu byona ngu nikiyo kimu kimwe omwanya omuhango.

ENTAITI erikurahura amagara agataahuka omukazi, nesaaniirwe bwangu habw'ondiijo ENTITI etaine kantu kyona ekirikukura ahabw'okurahura okwo kandi obwo ekisitaagi kya naipes kigwe omu musi kandi omukazi omugara yareeba okugwa omu magara.

ENTAITI erikurahura obwesigwa omu nsonga, nesaaniirwe enyeshereereza habw'ondiijo ENTITI etaine kantu kyona ekirikukura omu nsonga eyo kandi obwo omuntu n'agwa.

ENTAITI erikurahura obwesigwa omu GNOSIS, nesaaniirwe enyeshereereza habw'ondiijo ENTITI erobire GNOSIS.

Abegesa n'Abegesa b'amasomero, obukoreji na Yunivaasiti, bashemereire kwega eki kitabu ky'EBWEZIMU ERAZIMA kandi omu buntu bagire omutindo gw'okurondereza abegesibwa n'abegesibwa ahabw'omuringo ogurikukoma OBUTONDE BW'OKUHINDUKA KW'OBWENGYE.

Kikuru ngu abegesibwa beetondere okwetenga okweyoreza boona omu byalo by'omutwe.

Nibetaga endondoozi y'endimi erikukomera, nibetaga kwetondera ekituri kandi eki kigwere kutandika kuruga omu kitozi ky'Eishomero.

Titurikuremesa ngu esente nibetaga okuriisa, okushuura enju n'okwebazya.

Titurikuremesa ngu nibetaga okutebeekanisibwa kw'endimi, ekikorwa, omutindo gw'okukwata esente, kwonka ekyo tikyo byona, nikyo kya kabiri.

Ekya mbere, okukomeraho n'okumanya abu turi, ekituri, eki turuga, egi tugyenda, ehiyo kirimu okubaaho kaitu.

N'ekisaasi okugumizamu nka emiko ey'ekimeera, abantu abataine omugisha, abashaija-ebimeera.

Kigwere kureka kubeera ebimeera bikuru, kigwere okwehindura ABASHAIJA B'AMAZIMA.

Nibetaga okuhindura okwa mufu kandi eki kigwere kutandika kuruga kuruga omu OKUSAANYAAHO buri omwe aha ENTITI ezo ezirikweteraniza YO OKUBA NOBUNGI.

ENSORO embi y'OBWENGYE ti OMUSHAIJA kwonka aine omuri ye omu mbeera erihisire, amagara goona okwehindura OMUSHAIJA.

TIYO okutegerera ngu amagara ago nagatunga, ekirikubaho byangu n'okugafuura.

KWONKA Habw'okureba byoona OKUSINGURA-OMUGISHA nibasa kukurana amagara goona g'omuntu.

Turi abingi okusaanyaawo kandi turi abingi okweta. Nikikorwa okukora okubara okumanya egiturahura kandi n'ekitureeba.

Nikyererezi ngu YO OKUBA NOBUNGI eruga kuruga, n'ekintu ekitaine mugasho kandi ekihata.

N'OBWENGYE okugamba ngu tushemereire kukurana amaani agandi, ebizibu, omutindo, obusaasi obwo OMUSHAIJA-EKIMEERA n'agugamba ngu aine kandi naikiriza okugira kwonka omu mazima TATAINE.

OMUSHAIJA-EKIMEERA akiriza ngu aine OBUNTU BWE MWENE obw'amazima, OKUZUKUKA OKWENGYE, okwenda okumanya, AMAANI G'OKUKORA, n'ebindi kandi tihaine ekirikukura aha byo.

Twayenda kureka kubeera ebimeera, twayenda okuzukura OKWENGYE, okugira okwenda okumanya okw'amazima, OBUNTU, obusaasi bw'OKUKORA, nikikuru okutandika okweyoreza boona kandi obwo nesaanyaawo YO PSICOLÓGICO.

Obwo YO OKUBA NOBUNGI yasaaniirwe hakiriho omunda yaitu OBWOMEZI BWO BWENE.
