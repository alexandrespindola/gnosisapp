Okwijukira nikutwara okugyezaho kushinga omu Bwongo byatwarebera na byatwahurra, ebyatwashoma, ebindi bantu byabatugambira, ebyatugwireho, n’ebindi n’ebindi.

Abegesa nibenda abegi baabo bashinge omu bwongo bwabo ebigambo byabo, emigongo yaabo, ekyahandiikirwe omu bitabo by’eshomero, eby’emitwe yoona, emirimo eremeire, n’ebintu byoona nk’oku byahandiikirwe, n’ebindi.

Okuhitsya ebigezo nikimanyisa okwijukira byatugambirwe, byatwashoma titurikwetegyereza, okugamba ebyo twashingire omu bwongo, okwiija nk’enyoni, okw’iija byoona ebitusheenziire omu bwongo.

Nikibaasa ab’emyaka emihyahire kutegyereza ngu okwiija nk’ecupa y’omuziki byoona ebyashingirwe omu bwongo, tikirikimanyisa ngu twayetegeereza omu muringo oguhikire. Okwijukira tikwetegyereza, tihariho mugasho okwijukira kutarikwetegyereza, okwijukira kwabaireho omu bwire obuhingwire, nikintu ekifire, ekitarimu magara.

Nikikuru, nikyihutirwa kandi kyangango n’abaegi boona omu mashomero, amasyomero agaahe, na yunivasite kutegyereza amakuru agarimu omulingo oguhikire.

OKWETEGYEREZA nikintu ky’omurangaanzi, omulingo oguhikire, nikintu ekitukuraho amagara, nikintu ekitwebembera omu muringo oguhikire kandi ekiine omugasho okukira byoona omu kukora ebintu by’obwegendereza.

Okwijukira, okuteebereza nikintu ekifire, kwabaireho omu bwire obuhingire kandi okutahirwa niko kwahinduka ekiteeso, ekigambo, ekiteekerezo, ebiteeso eby’okw’iija omu mulingo gw’amaani kandi okukuratira titurikwetegyereza.

Omu KWETEGYEREZA KW’AMAZIMA, omu kwetegyereza okurikwingana, omu kwetegyereza okurikwingana aha mutima hariho okuremererwa kw’obwegendereza kwonka, okuremererwa okutahwaho okuzairwe omu mazima agu twetwaire omunda kandi ekyo kyonka nico kiriho.

Okushisha omu muringo oguhikire nikuza nk’omuringo gw’okukora ebintu obwangango, obutonganire, obwanguhire, obutariimu kutwaara omusango ogurikuremesa; obwerere obutarimu kuteganisibwa kwona. OKWETEGYEREZA okuhindukire ENTEGYEREZA EY’EKISIISI ey’okukora n’ekintu ekirikukwasa, ekihamagaziise, ekirikuzimbaho kandi nikikuru omu kumanyagira.

Okukora okurikwesigamizibwa aha kwijukira ebyatwashoma, aha kiteeso eki turikusiima, aha biragiro, aha miringo y’okwetwaara byatwegeserezebwa, aha byakumirwe omu bwongo, n’ebindi, nikuteekanisibwa, nikyesigamizibwa aha muringo ogurikuremesa, nigwoolekera-miriingo ebiri, nikyesigamizibwa aha muringo ogurikuremesa kandi nikikutuura aha nshobe n’obusaasi.

Ekyo ky’okuhikaana okukora n’okwijukira, ekyo ky’okugyezaho kuhindura okukora kikonane n’ebyo twakumire omu bwongo, nikintu ekyatekereezibwe, ekitali ky’amazima ekitariimu kubwangangana kandi ekyo kyonka nikitutuura aha nshobe n’obusaasi.

Ekyo ky’okuhitsya ebigezo, ekyo ky’okuhitsya omwaka, nikikorerwa omuntu weena omugwezi aine ebimukwasa kumanya n’okwijukira.

Okutegyereza ensonga ezi twegeserezebwa kandi ezi turikwiija kugezeserezebwa, nikintu kindi munonga, tiriho kimu kikwataine n’okwijukira, kwabaara abamanyi b’amazima abatairekwiija kweshobekanisibwa n’obumanyi.

Abantu abarikwenda kwesigamizibwa omu bikorwa byoona by’amagara gaabo aha biteeso, enyegyesa n’okwijukira byoona ebyakumirwe omu materekero g’obwongo, bagyenda buri obwire nibaringanisibwa kandi aharikubaa okuringanisibwa harimu obusingisi. Abantu abo nibaringanisa abantu baabo, ab’eka yaabo, abaana baabo n’abaana b’omuturwaani, n’abantu b’omuturwaani. Nibaringanisa enju yaabo, ebintu byabo, engoye zaabo, ebintu byabo byoona, n’ebintu by’omuturwaani nari abaturwaani nari abayombekire. Nibaringanisa ebiteekerezo byabo, obwengye bw’abaana baabo n’ebiteekerezo by’abandi bantu, n’obwengye bw’abandi bantu kandi obusingisi bukaza nambere obuhinduka entegereza y’ekisisani y’okukora.

N’obusaasi bw’ensi omuringo gw’obusinge bwona gwesigamizibwa aha busingisi n’omwoyo gw’okutunga. Buli muntu asingisa buli muntu. Turasingisa ebiteekerezo, ebintu, abantu kandi tukwenda kutunga esente n’esente nyingi, enyegyesa emihyahire, ebiteekerezo emihyahire ezi tushanga omu bwongo, ebintu bisya okutangaaza abantu abatukundwa, n’ebindi.

Omu KWETEGYEREZA KW’AMAZIMA, okw’embaganiza, okw’amazima hariho okukunda okw’amazima hatariho kugamba okw’omu bwongo.

Ebintu ebijukirwa, ebyo etumirwe omu bwongo, mangu bigwa omu kwebwa ahabw’okuba obwongo tibwesiga. Abaegi bashanga omu materekero g’obwongo, ebiteeso, enyegyesa, ebitabo ebitooraine ebitarimu mugasho omu magara ahakuba aha muheru bigenda omu bwongo bitasheeize kantu.

Abantu abarikugumaho nibashoma n’okushoma omu mulingo gw’amaani, abantu abarikusiima okukuma enyegyesa omu materekero g’obwongo nibashisha obwongo, nibubukwasya omu muringo ogurikuremesa.

Titurikwegamba obwomeezi bw’okwegesa obw’amazima omu mulingo oguhikire obwesigamizibwa aha kwetegyereza okurikwingana. Itwe twonka nitucwera emiringo egahinguraine ey’ebyesomesa by’omubwire. Nitucwera obusinge bwona bw’amaani bw’okwegesa, okushinga byoona, n’ebindi. Okwijukira nikurugaho aharikubaa okwetegyereza okw’amazima.

Nitwetaga kwega, nitwetaga ebitabo by’omugasho, nitwetaga abegesa b’eshomero, amasyomero agaahe, yunivasite. Nitwetaga GURÚ, abetwebembezi b’eby’omwoyo, abakuru, n’ebindi beitu nikikuru kutegyereza omu muringo oguhikire enyegyesa kandi okurekyera aharikukuma omu materekero g’obwongo obutarikwesigwa.

Titurikwiija kuba abagyezire nk’oku twine omugwisa kubi okuringanisa itwe n’okwijukira okw’aha bwongo, n’ekiteeso, n’eki turikugomba kuhikaaho kandi twayehayo, n’ebindi n’ebindi.

Omu bwire obu turikutegyereza enyegyesa ezi twatungire, titwetaga kuzijukira omu bwongo, nari okuzihindura ebiteeso.

Aharikubaa okuringanisa eki tuli hano hati n’eki turikwenda kuhinduka hanyiima, aharikubaa okuringanisa amagara gaitu ag’omutindo n’ekiteeso nari omuringo ogu turikwenda kuhikaanisa, hariho okukunda okw’amazima.

Okuringanisa kwona nikinyoma, okuringanisa kwona kureeta obwoba, obusingisi, eitendo, n’ebindi. Obwoba bw’obutahikiiriza eki turikwenda, obusingisi ahabw’okweyongyera kw’omuturwaani, eitendo ahabw’okuba twetwara nk’abarikukira abandi. Ekikuru omu magara ag’omutindo agu turimu, nangwa nituba tuli ababi, abasingisi, abenyinafsi, abagaya, n’ebindi, tikirikugambira abantu okuba abasheeshe, okuruga ahansi munonga, n’okwetegyereza nk’oku turi omu mulingo oguhikire, nk’oku tuli hatali nk’oku turikwenda kuhinduka nari nk’oku turikwegamba okuba.

Tirikubaasa okugwera ME, OKU TWE NIYE, kutamanya okwetegyereza, okurora okutegyereza eki turi buzima hano hati omu muringo ogurikukora kandi omu muringo oguhikire.

Twaba nitukwenda kutegyereza twine kwora abegesa baitu, abegesa b’abakazi, abegesa, abaherezabwomeezi, abetwebembezi b’eby’omwoyo, n’ebindi n’ebindi.

Abashaija, n’abashaija b’abakazi b’omutsindo omubyahire bahitsire aha mazima g’okusiima, okuhereza abazaire baitu, abegesa, abegesa b’abakazi, abetwebembezi b’eby’omwoyo, abegesa, abakuru, n’ebindi.

Tirikubaasa kutegyereza enyegyesa obutamanya kuhereza n’okusiima abazaire baitu, abegesa, abaherezabwomeezi nari abetwebembezi b’eby’omwoyo.

Okushanga omu muringo gw’amaani ebyatwegeserezebwa twayega byonka tutarikwetegyereza, nituhindura obwongo n’omutima kandi nituzaara obusingisi, obwoba, eitendo, n’ebindi.

Omu bwire obu tumanyire kurora omu muringo ogw’obwegendereza okurikwingana niburuga omunda yaitu amaani ag’okutangaaza, okwetegyereza okuhamagaziise, okw’obwangango, obutonganire, okutariimu businge bwona obw’amaani, obutariimu kuteekereza kwona, okutariimu kwijukira kwona.

Obwongo bw’omwegi bwaba burugireho omulimo munonga gw’okwijukira ogu ashemereire kukora, nikibaasa okwegesa ebitekateeko by’omutima n’endagaano y’ebintu ebirimu aha baegi b’omu muringo ogw’omubiri okwetegyereza okugarukamu n’eby’omuhendo gw’omushomesa.

Nk’oku twagambire n’abashomesha bamwe n’abashomesha b’abakazi b’amashomero agaahe nitwetegyereza ngu nibatiina n’omutima gwona enyegyesa ya kare ehinguraine n’ey’omubwire. Nibenda abaegi b’abashaija n’abakazi beyegye byoona omu muringo gw’amaani nangwa batarikwetegyereza.

Obumwe nibakkiriza ngu nikirungi kutegyereza okukira okukuma omu bwongo beitu nibagumaho aha mulingo gwa fiziki, kemistri, okubara, n’ebindi gushemereire kushingwa omu bwongo.

Nikyetegyereza ngu ekyo nikikyerize ahabw’okuba obwomeezi bwa fiziki, kemistri, okubara, n’ebindi, nibwetegerezebwa obutonganire aha mutindo gw’obwengye, beitu n’aha mitindo endiindi y’obwongo nk’obwegendereza, obutwegendereza, oburiyo omunsi, n’ebindi n’ebindi, tibwetaga kushingwa omu bwongo, nikuza kuhinduka omutwe gw’obwomeezi bwaitu kandi nikuza kwolesa nk’obumanyi bw’omubiri obw’omurangaanzi obu embeera z’omutindo zigyendereza.

Obumanyi obu obuhikire nibutuha omulingo gw’okumanya byoona, omulingo gw’okwolesa obwomeezi oburikwetegyereza.

Okutegyereza okurikwingana n’aha mitindo yoona y’obwongo nikibaasa okurugaho, okugyezaho okuteekateeka omunda okurikwingana.
