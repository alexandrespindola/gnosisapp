Abantu nibakanganisa OBUMANYI n'AMAGEZI nari OBWENGE, kandi omuntu omunyakagezi muno nari ow'obwenge, bamuha ekitinisa ky'okuba omumanyi muno.

Twebaza ngu OBUMANYI omu muntu tiburi buzibu bwona kandi nitwesiga okubeera ab'amazima, orukura rwe'ENZIYO ey'AMAGEZI ey'OMUNDA erikwetenga okuba etarikwetenga kukora omutwe.

Obushoboorozi bw'OBUMANYI nibutuha okumanya EBITEERAHO.

OBUMANYI nibutuha okumanya buri kimwe ekiri KIKI, ahu kiri, ekimanyirwe, ekimanyirwe.

OBUTONZI BW'OKUHINDUURA nibwegyesa ngu omuntu wenka ashoobora kwetanga.

Ise twena twena twine obushoboorozi bw'okumanya ekyo tuba abamanyi omu kakeeti kakwa.

Omu wenka ashoobora kumanya obumanyi bwe kandi n'okuba nibubaho omu kakeeti kakwa.

Omuntu wenka kandi hatariho ondiijo, ashoobora kumanya omu mulingo gw'omuteera, omu kakeeti ngu embere y'omuteera ogwo, embere y'akakeeti ako, buzima tali omumanyi, obumanyi bwe bukaba buri obugendere, bwanyima alagirira ekyo ekiba kiri kyehozo nari okukijukira, nk'okujukira ekyahwireho.

Kyetenga okumanya ngu OBUMANYI omu NYAMU ERIYO BUSHOBOOROZI ti kintu ekirikugenderera, eky'oluberera.

N'obutale OBUMANYI omu NYAMU ERYO BWENGE erikwetwa omuntu, bugendere muno.

Tibarikukunda, aba buli buhoora obumanyi nibuhulukukire; enyamu eriyo obwenge ekora, egaragaza ebidukanya, eshwera, efwa, n'ebindi n'obumanyi obugendere kandi omu buhoora obutandiire buhulukuka:

Obwomeezi bw'omuntu ni bwomeezi bw'okuroota, baitu abanza ngu ahulukukire kandi talikiriza ngu ari kuroota, ngu obumanyi bwe bugendere.

Kuhwereireho omuntu kuhulukuka, alifuura omushoonyeire kuteera ha bwomeezi bwe, alitegera omu kakeeti okusheeka kwe, okubaire kubeine mugaso.

Obwomeezi obu bushoonyeize muno, bubaire bubi butarukurwa kandi obutandiire obw'ahaiguru.

Kuhwereireho omuboxi kuhulukuka omu kakeeti omu lutalo, alireeba omushoonyeize ha bantu abaine ekitinisa kandi atindaho okuhunga okureeberera okubi, omubembeezi gw'embaga ezendereire ezitaine bumanyi.

Omuntu ku alikiriza ngu aine OBUMANYI OBUGENDERE, mushoobora kwesiga ngu atandiika kuhulukuka.

Eshomero z'okubanza ez'OBUTONZI ezigendereire ezirikuregana obubaho bw'OBUMANYI kandi n'okuba obutaine mugaso bw'ekigambo eki, zibanza embeera y'okuroota ey'ahaiguru muno. Abakurata ba Eshomero ezitaliyo bagendere muno omu mbeera erikweba obumanyi n'obumanyi.

Aba abakanganisa obumanyi n'omulimo gw'OBUTONZI; okuteekateeka, okufooka, okwenda, n'okuhurra, buzima tibaine bumanyi, bagendere muno.

Aba abalikiriza obubaho bw'OBUMANYI baitu nibaregana obutandiire embeera z'okwetanga, batabaire na ekyahwireho, okuroota kw'obumanyi.

Omuntu wenka ayahulukuka omu kakeeti, amanyire gye n'okureeba kwe ngu haine obutandiire embeera z'obumanyi ezirikureebwa omu bwomeezi bwe.

Obwangu. Kakeeti ki okugumira aha okwetanga?

Obw'ekibiri. Mulingo ki. Mbeera ziha, twayahulukuka tuketanga?

Obw'okushatu. EBY'OKUHAMYA N'OKUHAIGURU. Eki kiki ekirikwetanga?

OBUTONZI BW'OKUHINDUURA kandi na PHILOKALIA ya ira zibanza ngu okukira okweeta enziyo ez'omulingo gw'omutaano mushoobora kuhulukuka obumanyi n'okukukora okugenderera n'okukukwasisa.

OBWEGYESA BW'ENZIYO buine ekigendererwa ky'OKUHULUKUKA OBUMANYI. Tibuha mugaso myaka ikumi nari ikumi na taano ey'obwegyesa omu Eshomero, Ekolegyo, nari Yunivasiti, ku turiyo turwa omu bibuga turi abesherekerere abagendere.

Tikiri kwebazibwa okubanza ngu okukira okweeta enziyo enyamu eriyo obwenge mushoobora kwetanga kwetegaliza omutwe ogwo gwena okumara obumara obuke.

Kili kyangu ngu omuli guli haine okubaaho obutaano obutandiire obutwine okushaka n'etabaaza ya Di√≥genes, embeera ezo zitandiire ziraba ABAJUNA ABAZIMA, BUDDHA, YESU, HERMES, QUETZACOATL, n'ebindi.

Aba abataizi b'EDINIINE bakagira OBUMANYI OBW'OKUGENDERERA, bakaba abahamire ab'ahaiguru.

N'obutale abantu tebaine bumanyi aha biteeraaho. Okukanganisa kw'okwetanga omu mulingo gw'okugenderera, kuruga omu kwijukira n'omulimo gwona ogw'okuteekateeka.

Omuntu akora ogw'okukurata okwomeezi kwe kwona, ashoobora buzima kukijukira, okwijukira mulingo ki ashewere, abaana bangaha, bazaire be bakaba bari baani, ABAJUNA be, n'ebindi, baitu eki tikimanyisa kuhulukuka obumanyi, eki kyonka n'okwijukira ebikorwa ebitaine bumanyi kandi ekyo kikyaro.

Kyetenga okukorra eki twamaze kukubanza omu mitwe erikukurata. Haine embeera ina ez'OBUMANYI. Ezi nizo: OKUROOTA, embeera y'OKUHULUKUKA, KWETANGA N'OBUMANYI OBWERIKUGENDERERA.

ENYAMU ERYO BWENGE enyakiiriire erikwetwa OMUNTU, ebaho yoka omu mbere zombi ezo. Omutaano gw'obwomeezi bwayo egera omu kuroota kandi ondiijo omu erikwetwa obubi embeera y'OKUHULUKUKA, engo niyo kuroota.

Omuntu aroota kandi ari kuroota, abanza kuhulukuka ahabw'okugaruka omu mbeera y'okuhulukuka, baitu omu buzima omu mbeera y'okuhulukuka agumizamu kuroota.

Eki kiri nk'okucheera, enyonyoozi zibisiibwa ahabw'omusana baitu ziguumizamu zibaho n'obu amaisho g'okukureeba gatabazire.

Omu bwomeezi obwa burijo omuntu taliho ekimanyire eky'OKWETANGA kandi okukira omuli guli eky'OBUMANYI OBWERIKUGENDERERA.

Kwonka abantu baine eitima kandi buli omwe abanza kwetanga; enyamu eriyo obwenge ekwesiiga muno ngu eine obumanyi aha biteeraaho kandi talyikiriza omu mulingo gwona okugambirwa ngu agendere kandi ngu ebaho ataine bumanyi aha biteeraaho.

Haine obuhoora obutandiire omu ekiro enyamu eriyo obwenge ehulukuka, baitu obuhoora obwo butandiire, bushoobora kugaragazibwa omu kakeeti k'akabi ahaiguru, omu kikeeti eky'omutima ogwingi, omu embeera enziyo, omu embeera enziyo eteri kuteekateekwa, n'ebindi.

Buzima kibi ngu enyamu eriyo obwenge enyakiiriire teine obushoboorozi aha mbeera ezo ez'okutera obumanyi, ngu tishoobora kuzigaita ngu tishoobora kuzikora okugenderera.

Kwonka OBWEGYESA BW'ENZIYO bukwesiiga ngu omuntu ashoobora KWIKIRIZA okukwasa OBUMANYI kandi atunge OKWETANGA.

OBUTONZI BW'OKUHINDUURA buine enkorera z'amulingo gw'omutaano ez'OKUHULUKUKA OBUMANYI.

Ku twebaza KWIKIRIZA OBUMANYI nitwetenga okutandiika okwega, okwega kandi hanyima okugyaho okulemesa kwona okurikutuza omu muhanda, omu kitabo eki twegyesize omuhanda gw'okuhulukuka OBUMANYI okutandiika kuruga omu bibuga by'Eshomero.
