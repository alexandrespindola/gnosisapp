Abantu batera CONCIENCIA n’OBWENGE oba INTELECTO kandi omuntu omuhingi munonga oba intellectual, bamuha erinnya ly’omuntu ow’amaani ag’okumanya.

Ise twemereza ngu CONCIENCIA omunthu eri hanzi ya kukangana yona kandi okutinya okuhabura, ekika eky’amaani munonga eky’OKUTUNGA OKUMANYA KW’OMUNDA okwihire yoona aha bikorwa by’omutwe.

Obushobozi bwa CONCIENCIA butuha omumanya ogw’OKWETUNGA.

CONCIENCIA etuha okumanya okw’omuzima ogw’EKIRIho, ahari, eki tushemerire kumanya, eki twabura kumanya.

PSICOLOGÍA REVOLUCIONARIA egisya ngu omunthu wenene niwe asobora okwetunga.

Ise ithwe nitusobora kumanya niba turi conscious omu obwire obuhwereireho.

Ise omunthu niwe asobora kumanya CONCIENCIA ye kandi niba eriho omu obwire obuhwereireho.

Omunthu wenene kandi atali omunzi, asobora okumanya obunaku, obwire obuhwereireho obwo, hataliho CONCIENCIA, CONCIENCIA ye ekarare munonga, bwanyima araihwaa ekiroto ekyo oba aragukwatirira nk’ekihumuro, nk’ekihumuro ky’ekiroto ekirikuguma.

Ni kigwa ky’amaani okumanya ngu CONCIENCIA omu NYAMASHA KANDI ENKOREZA teri kintu ekirikugenda omu maisho, ekirihira.

Buri obwire CONCIENCIA omu NYAMASHA ENKOREZA eyo omunthu erarira munonga.

Ntoni, ntoni munonga obwire obwo CONCIENCIA eba erazire, Enyamasha enkoreza ekora, ekora emmotoka, esora, efa, n’ebindi na CONCIENCIA eyarare munonga kandi obwire bw’akashisha munonga bukarara:

Obwomeezi bw’omuntu ni bwomeezi bw’ekiroto, baitu ataikiriza ngu arazire, ngu CONCIENCIA ye ekarare.

Habwaki omuntu okarazire, arabatiireho munonga yaigwa, arategera obubi bwe, obukongoro bwe.

Obwomeezi bunu ni obw’ekyejo munonga, obutahikire bw’amaani kandi obutakabaho.

Habwaki omutabani okarazire omu kukaza, atungaireho yaigwa abantu ab’ekitiinisa yona kandi yairuka obulema obwo oburihire, omu kwesema abantu abarare.

Obwo omuntu airiza ngu CONCIENCIA ye ERARARE, musobora kwesiga ngu yatandika okukararira.

Amashomero agateranaaga aga Sicología endaire agazindika obukabaho bwa CONCIENCIA kandi okukora ebitali by’omugaso by’erinnya eryo, bagamba embeera y’ekiroto ey’amaani munonga. Abakuratira amashomero ago bararira munonga omu mbeera enziire munonga kandi etarimu CONCIENCIA.

Abatera CONCIENCIA n’ebikorwa bya PSICOLÓGICAS; okuteekereza, okwetenga, okugwira, n’okuhurra, nibararira munonga.

Abairiza obukabaho bwa CONCIENCIA baitu nibazindika emigano mingi ey’okukwata omunthu omu mutwe, barata esonga y’obuhumure obukwata omunthu omu mutwe, okurara kwa CONCIENCIA.

Buri omunthu weena ogu akararire obwire bukye, amanya kurugiirira aha byarabireho ngu hariyo emigano etali emwe ey’okumanya, erorereka omunthu.

Obwire bw’okubanza. Obwire ki obwo tukamarayo turi conscious?

Omugano ogw’akabiri. Bingana ki, obwire ki tukararire CONCIENCIA?

Omugano ogw’akashaatu. Obwihuriire n’Okutahangaania. Niki ekirikwija okuba conscious?

PSICOLOGÍA REVOLUCIONARIA n’eya PHILOKALIA ya ira beemereza ngu habw’OKUKORA MUNONGA ebikora by’ekika ekirikwoleka busya omunthu asobora okukararira CONCIENCIA kandi yaigira ebirikugenda omu maisho kandi ebirikwegwata.

EBIRIKUKURATAHO BIRINA EKIGENDERERWA ky’OKUKARARIRA CONCIENCIA. Tihariho kintu ekirikukora emyaaka ikumi oba ikumi na etaano y’emisomo omu Ishomero, Ekoleegi n’Eyunivasiti, habwaki omu kuturuga omu byombeko turi ab’omukago abarenga.

Tikirikukura okwemereza ngu habw’AMANI aga munonga NYAMASHA ENKOREZA asobora okwetunga obukye obwire bwa minuta ibiri.

Ni kyangu ngu omu bunu harimu obwerinda obutakatabaho oburikukutunga habw’akatabaazi ka Diógenes, emisango egi etakatabaho emererwa ABANZI AB’ENYENENE, BUDDHA, JESÚS, HERMES, QUETZACOATL, n’ebindi.

Abatandikiizi b’OBUTONGOZI bunu bakaiyire CONCIENCIA eya buri obwire, bakaba ABAMURIKIRWE ab’amaani.

Buri obwire abantu tibarikwetunga. Obulema bw’okwetunga omu muringo ogwa buri obwire, buzairwa omu kwijuka n’omuzimu gw’obuteekereza.

Omuntu orikukora okwijuka okurikugarukira enyima kwijuka obwomeezi bwe yona, asobora kurora, kwijuka bingana ki obwire yasoroire, abaana bangana ki yazaire, abazaire be bali barha, abegisha be, n’ebindi baitu bunu tikirikumanyisa okukararira CONCIENCIA, bunu ni kwijuka ebikorwa ebitaikuratiirwe kandi nikyo kyonka.

Ni kigwa ky’amaani okugarukamu eki twaremerezire omu bitabo ebirindiire. Hariyo embeera ina za CONCIENCIA. Ezi ni: OKURARA, embeera y’OKURORA, Okwetunga-CONCIENCIA n’e CONCIENCIA OBJETIVA.

NYAMASHA ENKOREZA aihabiremu obukwata omunthu ariyo NAMUNTU, abeera omu emwe omu embeera ezo. Omugabo gw’obwomeezi bwe gugwa omu kurara n’omunzi omu mbeera ebiriiremu EBWEHA, okwihaho okurara.

Omuntu orarire kandi ari kuroota, aikiriza ngu arazire habw’esonga y’okugaruka omu mbeera y’okurora, baitu omu mazima omu mbeera y’okurora ayomeerera kuroota.

Eki nikisirana n’okwesibaho, enyonyozi zihisirwe habw’omuriro gw’eizooba baitu zikomeera kwesibaho nangwa n’amaisho tibarazirore.

Omu bwomeezi oburikukora buri omunthu tamanya kintu ky’OKWETUNGA-CONCIENCIA kandi nakutakazana CONCIENCIA OBJETIVA.

Baitu abantu bari ab’omutima omugumu kandi abantu b’ensi yona nibaikiriza OKWETUNGA-CONCIENTE; NYAMASHA ENKOREZA airiza omu mutima gwe yona ngu aina CONCIENCIA ye kandi tibazaikiriza okugambirwa ngu n’orarenga kandi abeera atarikwetunga.

Hariho obwire obutakatabaho obwo NYAMASHA ENKOREZA arakaarara, baitu obwire obwo ni butakabaho, busobora kumererwa omu buremwa obukuru munonga, omumasira, omu embeera empyaka, omu mbeera empyaka erikwija etarikuteekerezebwa, n’ebindi.

Ni kikuru eky’obubi ngu NYAMASHA ENKOREZA ataina kutegyeka emibaro egyo ey’obwire bw’obuto ey’okumanya, ngu tasobora kugihuumuza ngu tasobora kugigira egirikugenda omu maisho.

Baitu EBIGISHA EBIRIKUKURATAHO beemereza ngu omuntu asobora KUTUNGA okutegyeka kwa CONCIENCIA kandi atunge OKWETUNGA-CONCIENC1A.

PSICOLOGIA REVOLUCIONARIA irina engyeso z’okugyendera ebwerere okUKARARIRA CONCIENCIA.

Niba nitwetenga okUKARARIRA CONCIENCIA nitwetenga okutandika okwebeza, okusoma bwanyima tukakora ebirihoho okuza emihanda eturikugenda, omu kitabo bunu tukegisize emihanda y’okukararira CONCIENCIA okutandikira kurugiirira omu myanya y’ishomero.
