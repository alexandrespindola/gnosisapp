Okutandika obuto n'obuto, ekkubo ly'okubonaabona kw'obulamu bwaffe obw'olusaniya litandika n'obuzibu obw'omutwe, ennaku z'omu maka, obuzibu mu maka n'omu ssomero, n'ebirala.

Kyetangaavu nti mu buto n'obuvubuka, okujjako ebitono ddala, obuzibu buno tebututuukako mu ngeri ey'amaanyi, naye bwe tukula ne tuba abantu abakulu, ebibuuzo bitandika. Nze ani? Nva wa? Lwaki nteekwa okubonaabona? Obulamu buno bugenderedde ki? N'ebirala.

Ffenna mu lugendo lw'obulamu twebuuzizza ebibuuzo bino, ffenna olumu twagadde okunoonya, okubuuliriza, okumanya "lwaki" ebibeetoolo byonna, obuzibu, okulwana n'okubonaabona, naye ennaku zonna tukomawo nga twesibye mu tteori, mu ndowooza, mu kukkiriza mu ekyo omuliraanwa kye yayogera, mu ekyo omukadde omukozi kye yatuddamu, n'ebirala.

Tufiiriddwa obutaliiko musango obw'amazima n'emirembe gy'omutima omuteefu era kyetuvudde tubeera abataliiko busobozi bwa kutegeera butereevu mazima mu bwonoonefu bwago bwonna, tusiigaddeyo ku ekyo abalala kye boogera era kyeyoleka nti tuli ku kkubo eritali lyo.

Ekibiina kya bakapitali kiggyawo ddala abatakkiriza Katonda, abo abatakkiriza mu Katonda.

Ekibiina kya Marxist-Leninist kiggyawo abo ABALI kkiriza mu KATONDA, naye mu buziba ebintu byombi bifaanana, kibuzo kya ndowooza, emimwa gy'abantu, ebyo ebirowoozebwa omutwe. TEBIRI kukkiriza, wadde obutakkiriza, wadde okubuusabuusa, kitegeeza okubeera nga tutegeezezza mazima.

Omutwe gusobola okweweereza okukkiriza, okubuusabuusa, okuba n'endowooza, okukola ebikeerezi, naye ekyo si kutegeereza mazima.

Era tusobola okweweereza okukkiriza mu njuba oba obutakkiriza mu yo era n'okubuusabuusa ku yo, naye emmunyeenye enkulu egenda mu maaso n'okuwa ekitangaala n'obulamu ku buli kimu ekiriwo nga endowooza zaffe tezirina kye zigitutte.

Emabega w'okukkiriza okuzibe, emabega w'obutakkiriza n'okubuusabuusa, mukwekeddwa ebikolwa bingi ebibi eby'obulimba n'endowooza nnyingi enkyamu ez'ekitiibwa eky'obulimba omutwe gwe gukula okukirawo.

Ekitongole kya bakapitali n'ekitongole kya baakominisiti buli kimu mu ngeri yaakyo era okusinziira ku mimwa gyabyo, endowoza n'enjigiriza zaabyo, birina ekika kyabyo eky'enjawulo eky'empisa. Ekyo ekiri eky'empisa munda mu kibiina kya bakapitali si ky'empisa munda mu kibiina kya baakominisiti n'oluvannyuma.

Empisa zisinziira ku mpisa, ekifo, ebbanga. Ekyo ekiri eky'empisa mu nsi emu si ky'empisa mu nsi endala era ekyo mu kiseera ekimu kyali ky'empisa, mu kiseera ekirala si ky'empisa. Empisa teziriiko muwendo gwonna omukulu, nga tuzinoonyereza mu buziba, zisirusiru mu kikumi ku kikumi.

Obuyigirize obukulu tebuyigiriza mpisa, obuyigirize obukulu buyigiriza EMPISA EZ'OBUSUMULUZI era ekyo kye beetaaga emirembe emipya.

Okuva mu kiro eky'entiisa eky'emyaka, mu biseera byonna, wabaddewo abasajja abeeyawulako ku nsi okusobola okunoonya AMAZIMA.

Kyakuggya mu nsaano okweyawula ku nsi okusobola okunoonya AMAZIMA kubanga lisangiddwa munda mu nsi era munda mu muntu wano era kati.

AMAZIMA bye bitamanyiddwa okuva ku ssaawa emu okudda ku ndala era si kweyawula ku nsi wadde okuleka banno nga tusobola okubitegeera.

Kyakuggya mu nsaano okugamba nti amazima gonna mazima ag'ekitundu era nti amazima gonna nsobi yaamu.

AMAZIMA mazima ddala ERA GALIWO oba TEGALIWO, tegayinza kubeera ga kitundu, tegayinza kubeera nsobi yaamu.

Kyakuggya mu nsaano okugamba: AMAZIMA ga biseera era ekyo mu kiseera ekimu kyaliwo mu kiseera ekirala TEKIRIIWO.

AMAZIMA tegalina kakwate konna na biseera. AMAZIMA GA KATONDA. OMUTWE biseera era olwekyo tegusobola kumanya MAZIMA.

Kyakuggya mu nsaano okuteebereza amazima ag'ekiyivu, ag'ekiseera, ag'ekigero. Abantu babuzaabuza endowooza n'ebyo bye tulowooza ne ekyo kye kiyitibwa AMAZIMA.

AMAZIMA tegalina kakwate konna na ndowooza wadde n'ebyo ebyitibwa amazima ag'ekiyivu, kubanga byo byebirowoozebwa omutwe obutabaawo.

AMAZIMA bye bitamanyiddwa okuva ku ssaawa emu okudda ku ndala era bisobola okutegeerekeka mu butabaawo bw'omutwe.

Amazima tebukwata ku magezi, endowooza. Amazima gasobola okumanyibwa okuyita mu kutegeereza butereevu.

Omutwe gusobola okuba n'endowooza era endowooza teziriiko kakwate na mazima.

Omutwe tegusobola kutegeera MAZIMA.

Abasomesa, abasomesa b'amasomero, amakolledde, yunivasite, bateekwa okutegeereza amazima era okulaga ekkubo eri abayigirizwa baabwe.

AMAZIMA kibuzo kya kutegeereza butereevu, si kibuzo kya njigiriza, ndowooza oba ndowooza.

Tusobola era tuteekwa okusoma naye kikulu okutegeereza ffe kennyini era mu ngeri entuufu ekyo ekirina okuba amazima mu buli njigiriza, ndowooza, endowooza, n'ebirala.

Tuteekwa okusoma, okunoonyereza, okubuuliriza, naye era twetaaga n'AMANGU agatalekebwa okutegeereza AMAZIMA erimu mu buli kye tusoma.

Tekisoboka okutegeereza AMAZIMA nga omutwe gukyali mu bweraliikirivu, obuzibu, okutulugunyizibwa olw'endowooza eziddanyuma.

Kisoboka okutegeereza AMAZIMA ng'omutwe guli muteefu, ng'omutwe guli mu kasirise.

Abasomesa n'abasomesa b'amasomero, amakolledde ne yunivasite, bateekwa okulaga abayizi ekkubo ly'okufumiitiriza emunda.

Ekkubo ly'okufumiitiriza emunda litutuusa okutuuka ku buteefu n'okussirika kw'omutwe.

Omutwe bwe guli muteefu, omutwe bwe guli omweru okuva mu birowoozo, okwegomba, endowooza, n'ebirala, omutwe bwe guli mu kasirise amazima gatujjira.
