Tikyashoboirwe ngu abegyesa ba Hisitoria y'ensi yoona omu burengyerwa bw'izuba nibasheerura BUDDHA, Confucio, Mahoma, Hermes, Quetzacoal, Moisés, Krishna, n'ebindi.

Tihariho kubanganisa ngu twashoboirwe kumanya okusheerura, okuhakanira, n'okuseerereza oku abegyesa bashoora aha madini ag'obuhangwa, aha banyamahe, aha nteekaniza y'ebyahangwa, n'ebindi. Ekyo kyona nikyo kitarikwetegyereza.

Omu mashuuri, amakuruuge, na yunivasite, abantu bashemereire kugamba aha nshonga z'ediini, n'ekitiinisa kingi, n'okusiima kwingi, n'obwengye bw'amazima oburikuhanga.

Engeri z'ediini zirikukuumira omuhendo ogutahwaho kandi zitebeekanisiibwe kukurugana n'ebyetaago by'omutima na Hisitoria ya buli ihanga, buli rurengo.

Ediini zoona zineemitwarize emwe, omuhendo ogutahwaho gumwe kandi nibashoboraana omu muringo.

Tikirikwetegyereza omukristaayo kusheerura ediini ya Buddha nari ediini ya Heburayo nari ya Hindu ahabw'okuba ediini zoona zigamiire aha mataba agashushana.

Okucumita kw'abamanya-bwingi abarikurwana n'ediini n'abatandisi baazo nikurugirira aha busaati bw'Ekimarxist oburikurugire omutwe gw'abantu abatahaba.

Abegyesa b'omu mashuuri, amakuruuge, na yunivasite bashemereire kworeka abanyeshuri baabo omuhanda gw'ekitiinisa ky'amazima aha bataahi baabo.

N'ekibi kyakabi n'ekirikushisha omuntu otaine buzima omu iziina ry'enyegyesa ya ruriijo rwona, asheerure ebitembe by'ediini, ebiina, amashuuri nari ebigombe by'omwoyo.

Abanyeshuri ku baraabe nibaruga omu byombeko by'okwega bashemereire kumanya abantu b'ediini zoona, amashuuri, ebiina kandi tikirikwetegyereza omuntu kutamanya kukwata ekyombeko.

Abanyeshuri ku baraabe nibaruga omu byombeko by'okwega bwanyima y'emyaka ikumi nari ikumi na itaano y'okwega, abanyeshuri bato n'abashaija bashangwa bari babi munonga kandi bagwejegyere nk'abantu abandi, bazaire omuhendo muhango kandi tibaine bwengye nk'omunaku gwabo gw'okubanza ku baayingire ishuuri.

Nikyanguhire ngu abanyeshuri omu bindi bintu bikuze omutima ahabw'okuba byona tibiri bwengye. Nikyetagwa kwega okuhurra okurungi kwingi kw'amagara, oburungi bw'omuti gwa omwe, endirimbo y'akashooro omu ishanga, endirimbo y'ebicurangari n'amabara y'akasheeshe karungi.

Nikyetagwa kandi okuhurra n'okwetegyereza omuhendo ogutandikaine gw'amagara, nk'obuteekaniza bw'abantu obw'obuhungu obutarikumanya eby'okusaasira obw'obwire obu turimu, enganda eizwire abanyina abatarikushemererwa abarikushenda ekicweka ky'omugaati n'abaana baabo abaine enjara, ebyombeko ebibi ahu emiryango mingi erimu obunaku egaruka, ebigyezi ebirikurugu ahu emotoka mingi zirikwekwata n'amafuta agahendeka ebirungo, n'ebindi.

Omunyeshuri orikuruga omu byombeko by'okwega ashemereire kwerinda obunyanya bw'amaani bwe n'oburemeezi bwe, kwonka kandi n'obunyanya bw'amaani bw'abantu boona n'oburemeezi bwingi bw'abantu.

Ekirikukirayo obubi y'okuba munyeshuri orikuruga omu byombeko by'okwega, noona kuba aine obuteekaniza bw'omutwe, taine bwengye, obwengye bwe bwagwejegyera, atebeekanisiibwe kubi kurwana n'amagara.

Obwire buhikire bw'okushereera n'okumanya ekyo ekirikwetwa obwengye. Ekigambo, enciclopedia, tezirikushobora kumanyisa obwengye.

Bwatarimu bwengye tihariho okushisha kwamazima nari okushemererwa kwamazima kandi n'ekishemeza munonga omu magara kushanga abantu abaine obwengye bwamazima.

Ekikuru omu magara tikukuratira ekigambo obwengye kwonka, kureka okukyekombesa omu mazima omunda.

Baingi nibeeha obwengye, tihariho omutamiizi atarikweeha obwengye kandi Carlos Marx yeecwire kuba aine obwengye bwingi, akahandika ekishuba kye eky'ebintu ebirikukwata aha bintu ebyakosiize ensi okubura omuhendo ogutahwaho, okucumita kwa baingi b'abanyamadini b'ediini ezirikushoboraana, okuronda kw'abakazi b'abanyakuru, Ababuddha, Abakristaayo, n'ebindi, okuhwerekyereza ebitembe bingi, okubonabonesa kwa baingi n'abantu obumillion, n'ebindi.

Omuntu weena ashemereire kweeha obwengye, kiragwire kuba mazima.

Tirikukura amahururu g'ebitabo, okumanya kwingi, okukombesa kwingi, ebintu bingi by'okuta abantu omu kigusha, esente nyingi z'okugura abaramuzi na polisi, n'ebindi nk'okuronderera ekyo ekirikwetwa obwengye.

Tiri aha nyingi, nk'oku orikubaasa kuhika aha bwengye. Nibakora enshobi abarikugamba ngu obwengye nibubaasa kutaahwa n'omuringo gw'okugira bingi.

Nikyetagwa kwetegyereza omuhendo omuri byoona omunda n'omuri byoona, omu bwengye obutamanya n'obutamanya, eki omuringo ogwo ogw'akabi ogw'ebyabaingi, ahabw'okuba omu buto nibegyesa ekihise, n'endijo, n'eka, eyenda buri obwiire okugira bingi okugira ngu ekure kandi ekomire.

Ogu Mefistófeles ogu twine omunda, ogu Sitaani, ogu I, nigugamba: Ndyo ndikugira esente nyingi, oburungi bwingi, obwengye bwingi okukira ogwo, ekitiinisa kingi, obwengye bwingi, n'ebindi.

Orikwenda kumanya obwengye, ashemereire kwega okukihurra, ashemereire kukikombaasa n'okukyekombesa okuraba omu kuteekateeka okurikwiha munonga.

Byona ebi abantu barunda omu entaana ebihwerekyerwe y'okwibuka okutari kwesigwa, amahururu g'omutwe, okukombesa kw'amagara, nibishitura kubi omu mazima omu muringo gw'ebyabaingi. Nk'ekirikurugirira, tibarikumanya amakuru agarikurabaho g'ebintu byona ebi barunda.

Baingi basoma ekitabo bwanyima bakateeka omu kwibuka bagahendera okukurira amahururu gandi, kwonka ku barikwetwa kugarukamu n'enyegyesa ehambiriire omu kitabo eki basomere, nibashanga batarikumanya amakuru agarikurabaho g'enyegyesa, kwonka I ekwenda amahururu gandi, ebitabo bingi, noona noona kuba atakakombesize nyegyesa yoona ahari byo.

Obwengye tiburikushangwa n'amhururu g'ebitabo gandi, nari okukombesa gandi, nari esente nyingi, nari ekitiinisa kingi, obwengye nibubaasa kureeba omuriitwe ku twetegyereza omuringo gw'I, ku twetegyereza omuhendo gw'okwikora omu bwengye ogw'ebyabaingi.

Nikyetagwa kwetegyereza ngu obwengye nibwo mutwe gw'ebyabaingi. Amazima ebyabaingi ebyo nibyo I yoona erikwetaga kandi obwengye nibwo ndiba yaayo enkuru.

Orikwenda kuba omwengye mazima, ashemereire kureebire okufa tarikumanyisa omu mutwe gw'ahaiguru, kureka kandi omu buto obutamanya n'obutamanya bw'omutwe.

Ku I efa, ku I egwe yoona ekisigara omuriitwe n'obwomi obw'amazima, okuba kw'amazima, obwengye oburikwetagwa munonga kandi buragwire.

Abantu nibakuratira ngu obwengye nibwo buhanga, nibakora enshobi. I tibuhanga kandi obwengye nibwo ndiba enkuru y'I.

Obwengye nibwo buhanga ahabw'okuba buri obw'okuba, n'omutwarize gw'okuba. Titushemereire kwiringaaniza obwengye n'omutwe.

Nibakora enshobi ya PLANO kandi omu muringo murungi abarikugamba ngu obwengye nikintu ekirikubaasa kukuza nk'ekyomuriishabwa nari ekirikubaasa kugura nk'oku bagura emitwarize y'ekitiinisa nari okugira ekitabo kikuru munonga.

Nikyetagwa kwetegyereza omuhendo gw'emiringo yoona y'omutwe, okugarukamu, ebyabaingi by'omutwe ebirunda, n'ebindi. Nikyo obwiire buringwa omuriitwe omu muringo gw'obuhangwa n'obusangire obwa flame yaaka y'obwengye.

Nk'oku Mefistófeles ogu twine omunda arikugwa, omuriro gw'obwengye oburikuhanga nigwe kworeka butunuha omuriitwe, kugira ngu guteere munonga.

Okuba kwitu kwamazima n'okukunda kandi okuruga omu kukunda okwo obwengye obw'amazima nibuzaarwa obutari bw'obwire.
