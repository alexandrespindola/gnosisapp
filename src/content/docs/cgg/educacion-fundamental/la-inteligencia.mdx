Twabonere ngu abegesa ba History y'ensi yoona omu burengyerwa bw'izuba nibasheekyera BUDDHA, Confucio, Mahoma, Hermes, Quetzacoal, Moisés, Krishna, n'ebindi.

Tihariho okubanganisa, twabonere kandi obushaarizi, okusheeka, okujanjaara okurikukorwa abegesa ahari diini za ira, ahari banyasaija, ahari by'okubaraana, n'ebindi. Ekyo kyona kiri kubura kw'obwengye.

Omu mashomero, ebicweka by'amashomero, na za yunivasite, twine kwegesa aha nshonga z'ediini, n'ekitiinisa kingi, n'obuhereza burungi, n'obwengye bw'okuhanga obw'amazima.

Emitwarize y'ediini nekurinda emiringo etarikuhwaho kandi neyetegure kurugiirira aha bwetaazi bw'obwengye n'ebyafaayo by'amahanga goona, z'amagara goona.

Ediini zoona zineemitwarize emwe, emiringo emwe etarikuhwaho kandi zihindaine omu muringo.

Tikirikwenda ngu Omukristaayo asheekyere ediini ya Buddha nari ediini ya Heburayo nari ya Hindu ahabw'okuba ediini zoona zihamiire aha ntamu emwe.

Okujanjaara kw'abamanya bingi kurikukora aha diini n'abatandisi baazo kuruga omu butwa bwa MARX, oburikwenda kutoora ebiteekateeko by'abantu abanyanteera omu biro ebi.

Abegesa omu mashomero, ebicweka by'amashomero, na za yunivasite baine kwegesa abegi baabo omu muhanda gw'ekitiinisa ky'amazima aha banywani baitu.

Kiri kibi kandi kitarikwetenga omuntu arikutwara omu biteekateeko by'omutaano kwesheekyera tempu, ediini, ebiina, amashomero nari ebibiina by'eby'omwoyo.

Abegi ku baraabe nibaruga omu byombeko by'okwega baine kukora n'abantu ab'ediini zoona, amashomero, ebiina kandi tikirikwenda ngu batarikubaasa kurinda obuhereza omu tempu.

Abegi ku baraabe nibaruga omu byombeko by'okwega bwanyima y'emyaka ikumi nari ikumi na itaano, abato n'abashiki nibashangwa baine amagara maruhire nk'abantu abandi boona, baijuze obutaarikumara n'okubura obwengye nk'omunaku gwabo okutandika kw'ishomero.

Kiri kyetengo abegi omukindi kugira embeera y'okwetegyereza ahabw'okuba tikyona kiri obwengye. Kyetaagisa kwega okuhendaana n'obumwe oburikukwaata aha magara, oburungi bw'omuti oguri wenka, okwimba kw'enyonyi omukishaka, okujunga kw'ebitunguru n'emibala y'akasheeshe karungi.

Kyetenga kandi okuhendaana n'okwetegyereza munonga obutahoora bukuru bw'amagara, nk'oruteerane rw'abantu ababi n'abatarikwenda kumanya omu bwire obu turimu, emihanda ejwiire abamaama abatairemerirwe abaine abaana abanyanteera kandi abashwera bari kushesherereza akantu k'omugaati, ebyombeko ebibi ahu ab'eka enkumi na nkumi abanyanteera baraare, enguuto ezirimu ebyaha ahu emootoka enkumi na nkumi zirikugyenda n'amafuta agarikwanga emibiri, n'ebindi.

Omwegi arikuruga omu byombeko by'okwega aine kwemerera ti kureka butoosha n'obwegye bwe na nshonga ze, kureka n'obwegye bw'abantu boona n'enshonga nyingi z'abantu.

Ekirikukiraho oburemeezi kyona n'okuba omwegi arikuruga omu byombeko by'okwega, n'obushoboorozi bw'okwetegyereza, ataine bwengye, emitima ye egamiire, ateteegwire gye ahabw'okurwanisa amagara.

Obwire buhikire okukora okunoonyereza n'okushuuruura ekyo ekirikwetwa OBWENGYE. Edikishoonari, ensaikoropedia, niziba zitaine maani kugira ngu zishoboorore munonga OBWENGYE.

Tihariho okuhinduka kw'emizi nari okushemererwa kw'amazima kurikubaasa kubaho hatariho obwengye kandi kiri kikuru omu magara kushanga abantu abaine obwengye bw'amazima.

Ekirikukiraho obukuru omu magara ti kureka kumanya ekigambo OBWENGYE, kureka okukikoraho omu mitima yaitu okugira ngu tumanye amakuru gaakyo.

Baingi nibeeshamba ngu baine obwengye, tihariho omutamiizi atarikwesamba ngu aine obwengye kandi Carlos Marx ari kweteerereza kuba aine obwengye munonga, akahandiika obushaarizi bwe obw'eby'omugasho obutari bwa ruhanga obu buhingwire ensi okubura emiringo etarikuhwaho, okutoora abasaaho enkumi na nkumi ab'ediini ezirikushaanisa, okukora ebyaha aha bashiki b'abasaaho, AbaBudisito, Abakristaayo, n'ebindi, okushwekyereza tempu nyingi, okubonaabonesa abaingi abantu, n'ebindi n'ebindi n'ebindi.

Omuntu weena ashemereire kweesamba ngu aine obwengye, ekirikugumira ni kuba obw'amazima.

Ti kukwata amakuru maingi omu bitabo, okumanya bingi, okukora ebintu, okugira ebintu ebirikujanjaara abantu, esente nyingi kugura abacwezi na bapolisi; n'ebindi nk'oku twine kushiika ekyo ekirikwetwa OBWENGYE.

Ti n'okugira eki, nk'oku turikubaasa kushiika OBWENGYE. Nibashobeera munonga abo abarikuteekateeka ngu obwengye nibubaasa kushiikwa n'omuringo gw'okugira eki.

Kyetenga okwetegyereza munonga omu byona eby'enteekateeko ezigamiire n'ezirikwenda, eki ekiri omuringo mubi ogw'okugira eki, ahabw'okuba omu mazima, kiri kigufu munonga EGO, I, MI MISMO, egi erikwenda obutoosha eki n'eki kugira ngu egumeho.

Ogu Mefistófeles ogu turimu omunda, ogu SATÁN, egi YO, neegira eti: Ndiine esente nyingi, oburungi bungi, obwengye bungi kukira ogwo, ekitiinisa kingi, obwengye bungi, n'ebindi n'ebindi n'ebindi.

Orikwenda kwetegyereza obwengye, ashemereire kwega okuhendaana nabo, aine kubihurikana naabyo kandi abikoreho omu kweteenganisa kunuunuuka.

Byoona ebi abantu barikukwata omunyamara ogurikukozire omu kwijukira okutaine mazima, amakuru g'okwetegyereza, ebintu ebirikukorwa omu magara, bihinduka obutoosha omu mazima okuhikiiriza eki n'eki. Obwo nibo batashangwa nibamanya amakuru ga byoona ebi barikukwata.

Baingi nibashoma ekitabo bwanyima bakakitwara omu kwijukira n'okusiima ahabw'okukwata amakuru maingi, baitu ku baraabe nibetwa kugarukamu aha nteekateeko ehandiikirwe omu kitabo ekyo bashomire, bashangwa batarikumanya amakuru g'okwegesa kw'omunda, baitu YO neeyenda amakuru maingi n'amaingi, ebitabo bingi n'ebingi noona obwo batarikwetegyereza okwegesa kw'ekirikwoona.

Obwengye tiburikushangwa n'amakuru maingi g'ebitabo, nari okukora ebintu, nari esente nyingi, nari ekitiinisa kingi, obwengye nibubaasa kwemerera omuriitwe ku turaba twine kwetegyereza omuringo gwona ogwa YO, ku turaba twine kwetegyereza munonga ekyo kyonka kyonka okukora eby'amagara by'okugira eki.

Kiri kigumire kwetegyereza ngu oteekateeko niyo ntamu y'okugira eki. Omu mazima okugira eki nikwo YO omumwoyo erikugyenda kandi oteekateeko niyo ntamu yaayo.

Orikwenda kuba omwengye bw'amazima, aine kwicwa ti kureka aha mutwe gwayo gw'okwetegyereza, kureka n'omu byona eby'obwengye n'ebyokushisha.

YO ku erikwiicwa, ku YO erikuhwaho, ekirikusiigara omunda yaitu ni SER ey'amazima, SER ey'amazima, obwengye obw'amaani oburikwetererezibwa kandi oburikugumira.

Abantu nibateekateeka ngu oteekateeko niyo ehangaanisa, nibashobeera. YO tihangaanisa kandi oteekateeko niyo ntamu ya YO.

Obwengye nibuhangaanisa ahabw'okuba buruga omu SER, n'emikoro ya SER. Titushemereire kucwekyereza oteekateeko n'OBWENGYE.

Nibashobeera omu muringo ogurikukiraho kandi omu muringo ogurikukira abo abarikuteekateeka ngu OBWENGYE nikintu ekirikubaasa kurimwa nk'ekyebara omunju nari ekirikubaasa kugurwa nk'oku abantu barikugura emitwarize y'obukuru nari okugira ebitabo by'amaani.

Kyetenga kwetegyereza munonga emiringo yoona y'oteekateeko, okugarukamu kwona, okugira eki omu mwoyo eki turikukwata, n'ebindi. Nikwo okushangaanisa omuriitwe omu muringo gw'omutindo n'okutandika okwaka kw'obushoozi bw'OBWENGYE.

Nk'oku Mefistófeles ogu turimu omunda arikugyenda ahwaho, omuriro gw'obwengye bw'okuhanga nigugyenda gushangwaho buke bukye omuriitwe, kuhitsya gurikwaka gwaaka munonga.

SER yaitu ey'amazima ni OKUGONDA kandi omu KUGONDA okwo nikwo obwengye bw'amazima oburikushangwa obutari bw'omutwe.
