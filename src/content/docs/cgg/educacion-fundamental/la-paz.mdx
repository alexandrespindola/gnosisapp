Emirembe tikirikuba kwija kuraba omu Bwomeezi bw’Omuntu ahabw’okuba tiburimu. Emirembe nigo mwamusiyo ogurikukirira aha mutima ogutekaine.

Emirembe tikiri kintu ky’omugasho, aha mwiherero gw’abaserikale, ekibiina kya UN, OEA, endagaano z’ensi yoona nari abaserikale abarikuteera abantu abarikurwanira emirembe.

Twaba nitwenda emirembe gw’amazima tushemereire kwega okutuura nk’omuringiizi omu bwire bw’orutaro, obutoosha turi abeesimire kandi turindiriire, n’obwomeezi obwangu kandi oburikutwara, ahabw’okuba emirembe tiguri nshonga y’ebirooto by’abaganzi nari ebirooto birungi.
