Abeshomesa bingi aba buli nsi omu nsi yoona bagenda buli lunaku omu ssomero n'omu yunivasite omu muringo oguteeremushwe, ogwa burema, ogw'omuntu kwonka, batarikumanya habwaki, n'obu barikuzaayo habwaki.

Abeshomesa nibagambwa kwega emibaro, fiziki, kemistri, geogirafi, n'ebindi.

Omutwe gw'abeshomesa nigukwata ebintu buli lunaku baitu tihariho n'omwe ogurikwehurra kweteera akanya ak'okuteekateeka habwaki ebintu ebyo, ekigyendererwa ky'ebintu ebyo.

Habwaki nitwijuzya omutwe n'ebintu ebyo? Nitubijuzya habwaki?

Abeshomesa buzima baagumaho omu magara g'obu mashine kandi nibamanya kwonka ngu baine kukwata ebintu eby'obwenge kandi babiherekye omu bwongo obutarikwesigwa, kiki kyona.

Abeshomesa tibarikwija omutwe kweteekateeka habwekyo buzima obw'obuhomesa obu, bagenda omu ssomero, omu kigooba nari yunivasite habw'okuba abazaire baabo babatumayo kandi kiki kyona.

Tiharikwija habw'abeshomesa, abegyesa bakazi nari abashaija kweibuuza: Habwaki ndi hano? Nijeire hano habwaki? Buzima kiki ekigyendererwa ekikuru ekindeehire hano?

Abegyesa, abeshomesa abashaija n'abashiki, baagumaho n'obwenge obugwejire, bakora nk'obu baine oburema, bagenda omu ssomero, omu kigooba n'omu yunivasite omu muringo oguteeremushwe, ogw'omuntu kwonka, batarikumanya buzima habwaki, nari habwaki.

Nikihangwa kuba oburema, kuzuukura obwenge, kwetunga wenka ekiki ekirwanisa egi enkaikuru habw'okuhita ebigezo, habw'okwega, habw'okugumaho omu mwanya gurikuteerwa habw'okwega buli lunaku n'okuhita omwaka n'okugumirira obutiinya, okuba omu maganya, okuhurra obusaasi, okukora emizaano, okurwana n'abeshomesa abandi omu ssomero, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Abegyesa bashemereire kwetora abetegereza kurungi okukonyera kuruga omu ssomero, omu kigooba nari yunivasite okukonyera abeshomesa kuzuukura obwenge.

Nikihangwa kureeba abantu abaingi bagwejire omu burema omu ntebe z'essomero, ebigooba na yunivasite, nibakwata ebintu ebi baine kurengyesereza omu bwongo batarikumanya habwaki nari habwaki.

Abato nibahurra obusaasi kwonka habw'okuhita omwaka; bagambiirwe ngu baine kweteekaniza okwezirika, okutunga emirimo, n'ebindi. Kandi benga bateekaniza enziro zingaha omu mutwe gwabo okukiriza eby'omu maisho, batarikumanya buzima ekyo oburemeezi habw'ekyo baine kwega fiziki, kemistri, bioloji, aritimetiki, geogirafi, n'ebindi.

Abashiki ab'omulembe bagaiga okugira okweteekaniza okurikubabasa kutunga omushaija murungi, nari okwezirika n'okuteekanizibwa kurungi habw'omushana ogurikubareka, nari okusigara bafire abashaija baabo nari abakazi abataashwerwe.

Enziro buta omu mutwe habw'okuba buzima tibarikumanya ekyo obwo bwire bwabo bw'omu maisho burikuba, nari habw'emyaka eyi barikufa.

Amagara omu ssomero gari amaganya muno, agateeremushwe muno, g'omuntu kwonka muno, omwana akorwa kwega obumwe obw'ebintu ebi omu magara g'okukora tibirikukonyera habw'ekintu kyona.

Omu bwire obu ekikuru omu ssomero kuhita omwaka kandi kiki kyona.

Omu bindi biro hakaba hariho n'obumwe oburikukiraho aha ekyo ky'okuhita omwaka. Hati tihariho kutyo ETIKA. Abazaire nibabasa kurya abegyesa omu bwihisho kandi omuishaiki nari omwishiki n'obu aba ari OMUSHOMESA MBI, arihitanwa omwaka ATARIKWEPENA.

Abashiki b'omu ssomero nibatendeka kwenda omwegyesa n'ekigyendererwa ky'OKUHITA OMWAKA kandi ebijumiro nikiba kiikiriza muno, n'obu batakamanyire "J" y'ekyo omwegyesa arikwegyesa, obundi bashaagaala kurungi omu EBIGEZO kandi bahita omwaka.

Hariho abato abashaija n'abashiki abarikushobora kurungi kuhita omwaka. Ekino nikyo ekigyendererwa ky'obucwezi omu miringo emingi.

Omuishaiki ohitiire kurungi ekigezo ekimwe (ekigezo kyeera kimwe) tikirikumanyisa ngu ayine obwenge oburikuteerwa habwekyo ekintu ekyo eyahiirwe ekigezo.

Omweshomesa naruganda nk'oruhoora, ekihuha nari ekihaha kandi omu muringo ogwa mashine ekyo kintu eki yegaire kandi eki yahiirwe ekigezo.

Ekyo tikikweta kweteereza ekintu ekyo, ekyo kurengyesereza n'okuruganda nk'oruhoora nari ekihuha ekyo twegire kandi kiki kyona.

Kuhita ebigezo, kuhita omwaka, tikirikweta KUBA OMUNTU WA MANA. Omu magara g'okukora tumanyire abantu b'amana aba omu ssomero batakaire bakora kurungi omu bigezo. Twaitu tumanyire abahandiiki abarungi n'abemibaro abahango aba omu ssomero bakaba abeshomesa ababi kandi abataahitiire kurungi ebigezo omu grama n'omumibaro.

Turikumanya ekintu ky'omweshomesa omubi omu ANATOMI kandi nyima y'okugumirira bingi abasire kukora kurungi omu bigezo by'ANATOMI. Omuri obwo bwire omweshomesa ogwo n'omuhandiiki w'omulimo muhango habw'ANATOMI.

Kuhita omwaka tikirikweta kwetwa omuntu w'amana. Hariho abantu abatarahitire omwaka kandi abari ab'amana.

Hariho ebintu ebirikukiraho kuhita omwaka, hariho ebintu ebirikukiraho kwega ebintu ebyo kandi buzima n'okugira obwenge obuhikire OBURIKUTEERWA oburikumurikira kurungi aha byo ebintu ebi biri kwegwa.

Abegyesa bashemereire kwetantara okukonyera abeshomesa kuzuukura obwenge; okwetantara kwona kw'abegyesa kushemereire kutwarwa aha bwenge bw'abeshomesa. NIKYETAGISA muno ngu abeshomesa beeteere buzima ABETEEZEREZA aha byo ebintu ebi barikwega.

Kwega omu mutwe, kwega nk'oruhoora, kuteerwa enshoni omu makuru gona.

Abeshomesa nibahangwa kwega ebintu bigumire n'okubiherekya omu bwongo bwabo habw' "OKUHITA OMWAKA" kandi nyima omu magara g'okukora ebyo ebintu tibirikubakonyera kwonka kaitu nibyerabirwa habw'okuba obwongo butarikwesigwa.

Abato bagaiga n'ekigyendererwa ky'okutunga, omulimo n'okwezirika kandi nyima beegira omugisha gw'okutunga omulimo ogwo, bakora by'obuhangwa, abasaizi, ababuranizi, n'ebindi, ekintu kyona eki barikutunga kugarukamu eby'okurugaana ebi, bashaagaala, bagumirira, bagira abaana kandi bafa batakazire obwenge, bafa batagirwe obwenge bw'amagara gaabo. Kiki kyona.

Abashiki bashaagaala, bagumaho omu maka gaabo, bagira abaana, nibarwana n'abataahi, n'abashaija baabo, n'abaana baabo, nibasharurana kandi bagarukashweera, bafire abashaija baabo, bagyere, n'ebindi kandi nyima bafa nyima y'okugumaho bagwejire, batarikwetegereza, nibarugaana nk'ebiro byona DRAMA erikumereza ebiro byona.

TIKIRIKWENDA ABEGYESA n'ABEGYESA b'essomero kwetegereza oburemeezi ngu abantu bona baine obwenge obugwejire. Nikyetagisa ngu abegyesa b'essomero nabo bazookukure bashemereise kuzuukura abeshomesa.

Tiririkukonyera kwijuzya omutwe gwaitu n'ebintu n'ebintu bingi n'okuganda Dante, Homero; Virgilio, n'ebindi, twina obwenge obugwejire tutaine bwenge oburikuteerwa, oburikwera n'oburikuhika aha bitwetwa, aha byo ebintu ebi turikwega, aha magara g'okukora.

Eshomero niririkukonyera ki twetikora abahanga, abarikwetegereza, ab'amana buzima?

Eshomero ly'amazima tiririkugambira kumanya kushoma n'okuhandiika. Omuntu omugara, omutareeba nasobora kumanya kushoma n'okuhandiika.

Twetaga kuba AB'AMANA kandi OBWAMANA buzookukura omutwe gwaitu buringwa BUZOOKUKURA OBWENGE.

Abantu baine 97% y'OBWENGE OBUTARIKUTEGEREZA na 3% y'OBWENGE.

Twetaga kuzuukura OBWENGE, twetaga kuhindura OBWENGE OBUTARIKUTEGEREZA omu OBWENGE. Twetaga kugira 100% y'obwenge.

Omuntu tarota kwonka obumubiri bwe buba bugwejire, baitu narota obumubiri bwe butarikugweja, obuba buri ahagati.

Nikihangwa kuleka kurota, nikihangwa kuzuukura obwenge kandi ekino ekintu ky'okuzuukura kishemereire kutandikira kuruga omu maka n'omu ssomero.

Okwetantara kw'abegyesa kushemereire kutwarwa aha BWENGE bw'abeshomesa kandi kutari aha bwongo kwonka.

Abeshomesa bashemereire kwega okuteekateeka beenka kandi kutari kuruganda kwonka nk'oruhoora nari ekihuha eby'abandi.

Abegyesa bashemereire kurwanisa habw'okumalaho obutiinya aha beshomesa.

Abegyesa bashemereire kureka abeshomesa, obugabe bw'okutarikukirizana n'okucwa emanja kurungi n'okwombeka ebyo ebintu byona ebi barikwega.

N'obushaagizi kubahangata kugaaya omu muringo GWA DOGMATIKA ebintu byona ebi biri kwegwa omu ssomero, ekigooba nari yunivasite.

Nikihangwa ngu abeshomesa bareke obutiinya habw'okuba bareke kwega okuteekateeka beenka. Nikyetagisa ngu abeshomesa bareke obutiinya habw'okuba bareke kwetegyereza ebyo ebintu ebi bari kwega.

Obutiinya n'ekikondoozi kimwe habw'obwenge. Omweshomesa aine obutiinya TARIKUTWARA kwetegyereza kandi naikiriza nk'ekintu ky'OKUFEESHA OMU MAISHO, ekintu kyona eki abahandiiki barikugamba.

Tiririkukonyera ngu abegyesa bagambe habw'obutatiina beine obutiinya. Abegyesa bashemereire kuba abaireho obutiinya. Abegyesa abatiina ekihano, abarikugamba ki, n'ebindi, TIBARIKUBAASA kuba ab'amana buzima.

Ekigyendererwa ky'amazima eky'eshomero kishemereire kuba kumalaho obutiinya n'okuzuukura obwenge.

Nirikukonyera ki kuhita ebigezo nitugumaho tutili batinti n'abatatiina?

Abegyesa baine omugisha gw'okukonyera abeshomesa kuruga omu ntebe z'essomero habw'okuba ab'omugasho omu magara, baitu okurikugumaho obutiinya tihariho orikubaasa kuba ow'omugasho omu magara.

Omuntu oijuzire obutiinya tarikutwara kwetegyereza okwikiriza kw'omundi.

Omuntu oijuzire obutiinya tasobora kugira obushoborozi obw'okutandika.

N'omurimo gw'omwegyesa weena, n'omulingo, ogw'okukonyera buli na buli omu aha beshoma baabo ab'essomero lye kuba abaireho buzima obutiinya, habw'okuba bashemereise kukora omu muringo oguteeremushwe batarikutagisa kubagambira, kubatumayo.

Nikyetagisa ngu abeshomesa bareke obutiinya habw'okuba bareke kugira obushoborozi obw'okutandika ogurikuteeremushwa n'ogurikuhanga.

Obumweshomesa n'omukwongyera kwe, oguri ogwabugabe kandi oguteeremushwe basobora kwetegyereza n'okucwa emanja habugabe ebintu ebyo ebi bari kwega, nibalekereera okuba abagumire ab'okumashine, abantu kwonka n'abagara.

Nikyetagisa ngu hariho obushoborozi obw'okutandika habw'okuba obwenge bw'okuhanga burakurira aha beshoma abashaija n'abashiki.

Nikihangwa kumuha obugabe bw'OKUGAMBA KWERIKUHIKA okurikuteeremushwe kandi obutarikureterwa ekintu kyona, aha beshoma abashaija n'abashiki habw'okuba bashemereire kweteereza ekyo eki bari kwega.

Obushoborozi bw'okuhanga obwabugabe butoora kwolesa obutatina obutiinya bw'ekihano, ogwo orikugamba ki, aha magara g'omwegyesa, aha biragiro n'ebindi n'ebindi n'ebindi.

Omutwe gw'abantu guremirwe habw'obutiinya n'obutakwegereza kandi nikiba NIKYETAGISA kugarukamu omu muringo gw'obushoborozi oburikuteeremushwe kandi ogurekerwe obutiinya.

Twetaga kweteereza amagara gaitu, kandi ekino ekintu ky'okuzuukura kishemereire kutandikira kuruga omu ntebe y'essomero.

Titiirikukonyera ekitu eshomero nitwaruga omunyamara n'abaitu.

Okumalaho obutiinya n'obushoborozi oburikureeta okukora omu muringo oguteeremushwe kandi ogurikwera.

Habw'obushoborozi oburikureeta abeshoma abashaija n'abashiki bashemereire kugira omugisha omu mashomero goona okugana omu nterane ebintu byona ebi bari kwega.

Nico nk'oku oburikureeta kulekerera obutiinya n'obugabe bw'okugana, okwetegyereza, KUTEEKATEEKA, n'okuhana kurungi eki turikwega, turikubaasa kweteereza ebyo ebintu ebyo kandi kutari kwonka abagumiire abaheereza ebyo ebi bakusanza omu bwongo.
