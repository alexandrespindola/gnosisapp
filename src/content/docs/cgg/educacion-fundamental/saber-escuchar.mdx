Omu nsi harimu abagambirizi abangi abatarabisa ahabw'obuhangwa bwabo, baitu abantu bake nibo abamanyi oku hurikiza.

Okumanya oku hurikiza nikikuru munonga, abantu bake nibo abamanya oku hurikiza.

OMUGURUSI OMUKURU NAAGAMBA, omugurusi omukazi, omugambiizi, ekitebe kiba kigarukiireho, nk'okukurata obugambo bwona bw'omugambirizi, byona nibireeta eky'okuba nibahurikiza, ngu bari omumutima, baitu omunda y'omuntu ogwo na ogwo harimu omunyamabanga orikushobora obugambo bwona bw'omugambirizi.

OMUNYAMABANGA ONU NO, NZE, NINE, NINE MWENE. Omulimo gw'omunyamabanga ogu niko kuraba mu kubahurikiza batarikwetenga, okubahendera ebigambo by'omugambirizi.

NZE naraba omumiteekateeka yange, obutiini bwange, okutiina kwange, amalala gange, obwoba bwange, endowoza yange, ebijukirizo byange, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Abashomesibwa omushomero, abashomesibwa abakazi, abantu abajumwire hamwe nibareeta ekitebe ekirikuhurikiza, buzima tibarikuhurikiza omugambirizi, nibeehurikiza boona, nibahurikiza EGO yaabo, EGO yaabo ey'omutima MAQUIAVÃ‰LICO, etarikwetenga kwikiriza EKIRIKWOLEKA, EK'AMAZIMA, EK'OMUTIMA.

Buzima omumutima gwangye OMUSHABA OMUHWERA, N'OBWENGE OKURUGIRA OMUMUTIMA gwangye ogutarikwetenga ebintu by'omubihingwire, omumutima gw'OKWEMERERA, nitushobora kuhurikiza batarikwetenga omunyamabanga ogwo orikwenda obubi orikwetwa NZE, NINE MWENE, EGO.

Obwenge obuhurikize obujukiro, nibwongera kukora ebi orikutunga.

Obwenge obuhurikize ebintu ebikumalirira, nibushobora kureeba ensi egi kuraba omu myagaano y'omubihingwire.

TUYETENDE KUHAMANYA OKUHURIKIZA, twayetenga kwega oku hurikiza kwikiriza ebishasha, tushemeza kuba tukurikurata obwengye bw'OBWANYI.

Nikikuru kuba tukurikukora okumara obwanya bwonka okutariire omubihingwire, n'ebikorwa by'omumaisho.

AMAZIMA n'ebitarikumanyika okumara obwanya bwonka, obwenge bwaitu nibuteekwa kuba obwengamizi, omumutima gwaitu, obutariire obutiini, obwengamizi, kwikiriza.

Abagurusi abakuru n'abakazi omushomero nibateekwa kwegesa abashomesibwa baabo ebikuru ebirimu okuhamanya oku hurikiza.

Nikikuru kwega oku kora omu obwengye, okugumya obwengye bwaitu, okubenda obugambe bwaitu, endowoza yaito, obutumwa bwaitu.

Nitatugasa kugira obugombe obukuru obw'obwengye, twayetenda oku hurikiza, twayetenda okukika ebishasha okumara obwanya bwonka.

Nitweteenga kubenda omutima, kubenda ebi twaba nitukora, kubenda abantu baitu, eby'okukora, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Tikirikushobora kuba ab'omutima gubendire buzima twaba tutarikumanya oku hurikiza.

Obwenge obutariire obw'omutima, ebitendera ebyahangire n'ebitarikumanya oku hurikiza, tibarikushobora kukika ebishasha, obwenge obwo nibwo bugumire, nibwo buhurikize omumulingo ogutazire ebi omunyamabanga orikuraba omu buhwere abatugambira NZE, NINE MWENE, EGO.

Kuba omuntu w'omutima gugendire nikikuru munonga kandi nikyetenga omutima gwona. Omunu asobora kuba omuntu w'omutima gugendire omu mulingo, eby'okujwara, eby'okwerinda, amaka, eby'emotoka, abanywani, kwongera omunda yabo kuba ab'obutariire omutima, ab'ekikuba, ab'obuzito.

Nibeera nibaamanya oku kora omumwanya gwona, kugenda omuhanda gw'okubenda omutima.

Omuntu ogwo agira obwenge obw'obwengamizi, obw'omumutima, obuhweraine, omumutima, kugenda omuhanda gw'okubenda omutima.

Omuntu ogwo agyera omunsi yona arikureka obuzito bw'omubihingwire, ebi orikuraba omu mutima, obutiini, ebishuba, n'ebindi, kugenda omuhanda gw'okubenda omutima oguhwere.

Obwenge obwo butariire obw'omubihingwire, oburikuza omu butiini, amalala, okukunda omuntu, obutiini, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Obwenge obwo butariire tiburikumanya okureeba ebishasha, tiburikumanya oku hurikiza, nibwo bugumire omu KUKUNDA OMUNTU.

Abakunzire MARXISMO-LENINISMO tibarikukikiriza ebishasha; tibarikukikiriza ENKURU ya byona, DIMENSIYO, ahabw'okukunda omuntu, nibetenda munonga, nibakwata amagezi gaabo amarungi aga nibahwa okukora ebigambo byabo.

Obwenge obwo obutariire, obwenge obwo obuzire okwega oku hurikiza, tiburikukikiriza ekitengwa ahabw'okuba nibo bakundire. Obwenge obwo obutarikumanya engyezi z'abandi, obwenge obutarikwetenga, oburikuhurikiza EGO yaabo.

OBWEGESA BW'OMUTIMA kwegesa oku hurikiza, kwegesa oku kora omu bwengye.

Abagurusi abakuru n'abakazi omushomero, nibateekwa kwegesa abashomesibwa omuhanda gw'okubenda obwomeezi.

Tibirikutugasa kumara emyaka ikumi na ikumi n'etaano tukuzibwe omu mashomero, tuliba tufeereize omu ndowoza, endowoza, obutumwa n'enzarukirizo.

Nitumanyisa OBWEGESA BW'OMUTIMA omu mulingo oguteire ahabw'okuba obutahi obushasha nibumanyisa okutandika kw'omubaro ogushasha.

Obwanya bwatuura obw'ENKURU BUZIMA, obwanya bwatuura obw'ENKURU BW'OMUTIMA.

Obuhingire n'obuhingire kandi bukareeta ebijumwire. Nitweteenga kwetenga ekitengwa ky'obwanyima obu turi kukora.
