Omu nsi erimu abantu bangi abarikugamba ebigambo ebirungi, kwonka abantu bake barikumanya okuhora.

Okukuratira kigumire munonga, abantu bake nibo barikumanya okuhora buzima.

KU ARARE OMUHWEZI, omuhwezikazi, omugambirizi, ekitebeisa nikirebeka nk'ekikuratira munonga, nk'ekurikurata buri kigambo ky'omugambirizi, byona nibiha obwengye ngu nibarikukuratira, ngu bari omu mbeera y'obwengye, kwonka omunda omu bitekerezo bya buri muntu harimu omubazi arikushoborora buri kigambo ky'omugambirizi.

OMUBAZI OGU N'NYE, NYENA, NYENKA. Omurimo gw'omubazi ogu nigwo kushoborora bubi, kushoborora bubi ebigambo by'omugambirizi.

NYE nashoborora kurugiirira aha miteekateeko yaanye, enteekateeka, okutiina, obwerembe, obuhunga, ebiteekateeko, ebijukizo, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Abeeshuri omu ishomero, abeeshurikazi, abantu abari hamwe nibakora ekitebeisa ekirikukuratira, buzima tibarikukuratira omugambirizi, nibarikukuratira boona, nibarikukuratira NYE yaabo, NYE yaabo enungi MAQUIAVÉLICA, etarikwenda kwikiriza EKIRA, EKIZIMA, EKIKURU.

Omu mbeera y'obwengye EBIRIKUTANDIIKA, hamu n'OBWENGE OBURIHO obutariho oburemeezi bw'enyima, omu mbeera y'OKWIKIRIZA okuhitsya aha rugamba, nitubaasa buzima kukurata tutarikwetenga omubazi omubi ogwo owa NYE, NYENA, NYENKA, NYE.

Obwenge obu bukondire aha kwijukya, nibuzaamu byona ebi bukunganize.

Obwenge obu bukondire aha birukumanywa by'enyima nyingi, nibubaasa kureeba obwire obu buriho kuraba omu ndeeberwamu ezirikubiha ez'enyima.

TWENDA KUMANYA OKUKURATIRA, twenda kwega kukurata kushangwa ebihyaka, twine kubeho kurugiirira aha filosofia y'EBIRE EBI.

Nikihweera okubeho omuri obwire aha bure butariho okutwara aha bwengye obw'enyima, hamu n'enteekateeka z'omumaisho.

OBUTUUTU nibwo obutarikumanywa omuri obwire aha bure, obwenge bwitu bushemereire kuba burikwemerera, omu bwengye bwona, butariho miteekateeko, enteekateeko, kugira ngu tube abaikiriza buzima.

Abahwezi n'abahwezikazi b'omu ishomero bashemereire kwegyesa abeeshuri baabo n'abeeshurikazi amakuru agarimu omunda agari omuri oku kumanya kukurata.

Nikyetenga kwega okubeho n'obwengye, kugarukamu obwengye bwitu, kuhuura embeera yitu, ebiteekateeko byitu, enyehurira zitu.

Tikirikumarira okugira obumanyiso bukuru obw'eshomero, tutarikumanya kukurata, tutarikubaasa kushangwa ebire bya buri obwire.

Nitwetenga kuhuura obwengye, kuhuura emiteekateeko yitu, kuhuura abantu bitu, ebintu, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Tikirikubaasika kuba omunyakugara buzima tutarikumanya kukurata.

Obwenge obutari burungi, obukweteire, obuhungire tiburikumanya kukurata, tiburikumanya kushangwa ebire, obwenge obwo nibweteereza, nibwo teereza omu muringo mushobeire okushoborora okutari kwesigwa kw'omubazi ogwo owa NYE, NYENA, NYE.

Kugara nikintu kigumire munonga kandi nikyetenga obwengye bwona. Omuntu abaasa kuba omunyakugara munonga omu miteindo, engoye, ebijanja, emotoka, abanywani, kwonka akagumizamu omunda kuba mubi, ogweteire, oguremereire.

Orikumanya okubeho omuri obwire aha bure, nagyenda buzima omu muhanda gw'okugara okw'amazima.

Orikugira obwenge oburikwemerera, oburiho, obuhikire, oburikwemerera, nagyenda omu muhanda gw'okugara okw'amazima.

Orikwigura aha byona ebire arikureka oburemeezi bw'enyima, enteekateeka, miteekateeko, obutari bwesigwa, okugwera aha bintu, n'ebindi, nagyenda arikutsinda omu muhanda gw'okugara okuriho.

Obwenge obuhungire nibubeho buzibire omu nyima, omu nteekateeka, obwerembe, okukunda, miteekateeko, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Obwenge obuhungire tiburikumanya kureeba ebire, tiburikumanya kukurata, bukondire aha KUKUNDA.

Abagwererezi ba MARXISMO-LENINISMO tibarikwikiriza ebire; tibarikwikiriza EKINE kya buri kintu, EKINE kya DIMENSIÓN, ahabw'okukunda, nibakunda boona munonga, nibegata aha nyegyesa yaabo ey'ebintu ebiriho etarikwetengwa kandi obutuhitsire aha musiri gw'ebikorwa ebirikukwataho, obutubareebere ebitarikwetengwa by'ebigambo byabo, nibayimutsya omukono gwabo ogw'omushoberera, nibareeba amashaaha gaabo g'aha mukono, nibaha eky'okweshuuruza n'ebagyenda.

Ego nizo obwenge obuhungire, obwenge obukaire obutarikumanya kukurata, obutarikumanya kushangwa ebire, obutarikwikiriza amazima ahabw'okuba buzibire omu KUKUNDA. Obwenge oburikukunda boona munonga, obwenge obutarikumanya ebiriho, obwenge obutari burungi, obwenge obukweteire, oburikukuratira NYE yaabo yoona.

OBWENGE OBW'OMUSINGI nibwegyesa kukurata, nibwegyesa okubeho n'obwengye.

Abahwezi n'abahwezikazi b'omu mashomero, amakoro, yunivasiti bashemereire kwegyesa abeeshuri baabo n'abeeshurikazi omuhanda gw'okugara okw'obwomeezi okw'amazima.

Tikirikumarira kuguma emyaka ikumi na ikumi na itaano etaiire omu mashomero, amakoro na yunivasiti, twaruga niturikuruga omu munda turi engobe buzima omu biteekateeko byitu, ebiteekateeko, enyehurira n'emigyenzo.

Nikyetenga OBWENGE OBW'OMUSINGI omu muringo gw'obwangu ahabw'okuba emigarukiro emihyaka nikimanyisa okutandika kw'obwire obuhyaka.

Obwire bw'okukora empinduka buzire, obwire bw'okukora empinduka obw'omusingi buzire.

Enyima niyo nyima kandi emaire kukuza ebijuma byayo. Nitwetenga kweteereza amakuru agarimu omunda ag'obwire obu turimu.
