Nikukirira kumanya gye obuhangwa bw'okufa omu biteekateeko byaitu byoona, niki eki okufa kiri, obwo nibwo tusobora kumanya gye obuhangwa bw'okutafa.

Okureeba omubiri gw'omuntu ogwolekaire omu ssanduuku tikikumanyisa ngu otahurile omugisa gw'okufa.

Amazima nigo tugukenga buri kasumi. Amazima g'okufa tigasobora kuba gatarukukiraine.

Ndi ningonza obwire boona, nk'obuhangwa bwago, obucunguzi bw'okufa, obuhiganwa obundi, omuntu ow'obusobozi okutucungura omwanya omulungi n'obuhangwa bwona obw'okutafa orunyoro.

Ndi tinyenda kufa. Ndi ningonza kugyenda omu maiso. Ndi neetiina okufa.

Amazima tigali kucwera rundi kwikiriza. Amazima tigagira akakwate n'okucwera rundi okukanga. Amazima tigali bugingo, biteekateeko, ndagaano, nteekateeka, bujingira, bujingiriro, ndagaano, nteekateeka, n'ebindi. Amazima g'omugisa gw'okufa tigali gatarukukiraine.

Amazima g'omugisa gw'okufa gasobora kumanywa kuraba omu kundagaana ey'amaisho.

Nikikanga kusobora kumanyisa kundagaana y'amaisho ey'okufa ha muntu otagimanyire.

Omushaija wena asobora kuhandiika ebitabo birungi by'okukunda, baitu nikikanga kumanyisa amazima g'okukunda ha bantu abatakundaganeho, nk'oku tugamba ngu nikikanga kumanyisa amazima g'okufa ha bantu abatagikundageneho.

Oyo ayenda kumanya amazima g'okufa asemereire kwongera kumanya, okukundagana yoona, kuserra nk'oku kiriri, obwo nibwo tusobora kusanurira amakuru makuru g'okufa.

Okureeba n'okukundagana kw'emyaka mingi bituhaire obusobozi okwetegereza ngu abantu tibarikwenda kumanya gye amakuru makuru g'okufa; abantu ekirikubasikiriza ni kugyenda omu maiso omu runyoro kandi nikyo kyonka.

Abantu baingi nibenda kugyenda omu maiso kuraba omu mbeera, obukuru, enganda, okwikiriza, obugingo, abaana, n'ebindi, kandi obu baraamanya ngu obuhangwa bwona obw'obusobozi bukuru nibuba bwangwa, obw'omuhangamo, obw'akanya kakye, obutareebekire, obwo nibo beetiina, nibakanga, nibasaasira, nibaijuza okutiina kw'amaisho.

Tibarikutwara kukimanya abantu abanaku, tibarikwenda kwetegereza ngu kyoona ekirikugyenda omu maiso kikirira omu obwire.

Tibarikutwara kukimanya abantu abanaku ngu kyoona ekirikugyenda omu maiso kibaasa omu obwire.

Tibarikutwara kukimanya abantu abanaku ngu kyoona ekirikugyenda omu maiso kikora nk'ekizibo, kikora nk'ekiro, nikizikaana.

Nikikuru, nikyetagisa, nikigwanire, okumanya gye amakuru makuru g'okufa, obwo nibwo okutiina okureka kubaho kuraamara.

Turikureeba obuntu n'obwegendereza, tusobora kukora ngu obwomeezi buri obwire nibutweera omu kimanyirwe kandi nibwenda ngu eki kimanyirwe kigyende omu maiso orunyoro.

Obwomeezi obutweere omu kimanyirwe tiburasobora kukundagana n'ekitakimanyirwe, ekya mazima, ekihikire.

Kuraba omu kumena ecupa y'obwire kuraba omu kuteekateeka okuhiire, tusobora kukundagana n'obwire butahwa, obutagira obwire, ekihikire.

Abo abarikwenda kugyenda omu maiso nibetiina okufa kandi okwikiriza kwabo n'obugingo nibubabera obutaasi.

Okufa kwoona tikugira kukanga, niki ekirungi, eky'obuhangwa, ekikanga okugamba, baitu obwomeezi obutweere: omu kimanyirwe, nibugyenda omu nkoonyane ey'amaisho eya kugyenda omu maiso okwikiriza.

Obu turamanyire gye amakuru makuru g'okufa, tusanurira obwaitu kuraba omu kundagaana ey'amaisho, ngu Obwomeezi n'Okufa nibukora ekyona ekihikire, ekyona.

Okufa niko iterekero ly'Obwomeezi. Engira y'Obwomeezi eyombekire n'ebicweka by'amaguru g'okufa.

Obwomeezi n'Amaani agatetereire kandi agatetereera. Okuruga omu kubaha kuhitsya omu kufa amaani agarukukiraine nigatemba omu mubiri gw'omuntu.

Obuhangwa bw'amaani obwo omubiri gw'omuntu gutasobora kusiima, niko Omushana gw'Okufa. Omushana ogu gwiina obungi bw'amaisanyalaza bwingi munonga. Omubiri gw'omuntu tigusobora kusiima obungi obu.

Nk'oku omushana gusobora kusazura omuti, nk'okumara omushana gw'okufa ogutemba omu mubiri gw'omuntu, nigugusazura n'amaani.

Omushana gw'okufa nigukwata ha muheru gw'okufa, hamu n'omubaro gw'okubaha.

Omushana gw'okufa nigukora okwehingira kw'amaisanyalaza kwingi n'omunyegenyege ogurimu amaani, okugata ebihimba omu gi ebirekire.

Omushana gw'okufa nigucumura omubiri gw'omuntu ha butonde bwayo obuhango.

Ego, Ndi ow'amaani, ngyenda omu baana baitu obwomeezi.

Eki Amazima g'Okufa kiri, eki obwire buraire orukumu n'okugwisa niki, tigiriho obwire kandi kuraba omu sayansi y'okuteekateeka tusobora kukundagana.

Abegesa n'Abegesa b'Eshomero, Ekigaijo n'Orunaku, baseemereire kwegesa abegeswa baabo engira eitura omu kundagaana y'Ekihikire, eky'Amazima.
