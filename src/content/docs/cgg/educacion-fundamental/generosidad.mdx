Nikusana kwenda kandi okukundwa, baitu eky'obusaasi omu nsi abantu tibarikwenda nari okukundwa.

Ekyo ekirikwetwa okukunda ni kintu abantu batarikumanya kandi nibakihumuza n'okwetenga n'okutiina.

Abantu ku barikubaasa kwenda kandi bakakundwa, entaro zaba zitarikubaasa kubaho aha rurembo rw'ensi.

Eshwereero nyingi ezirikubaasa kuba ez'okushemererwa, eky'obusaasi tizirikubaasa ahabw'obusungu bwa ira oburikukwatanisibwa omu biteekateeko.

Abashweeraine ku barikugira obumanzi, baribagirwa ebiro by'obusaasi ebyahweireho kandi bakatuura omu bwijwire, beijwire okushemererwa kw'amazima.

Obwomeezi nibwita okukunda, nibukushiisha. Eby'obwomeezi, okushemererwa kwa ira, obugayaazi bwa ira, ebi byoona ebikwatanisibwa omu biteekateeko, nibishiisha okukunda.

Abakazi baingi abaine obusungu nibabaasa kuba ab'okushemererwa ku barikugira obumanzi oburikwenda kugirwa ebiro byahweireho kandi bakatuura omu biro ebi nibaramya ibaabo.

Abashaija baingi nibabaasa kuba ab'okushemererwa n'abakazi baabo ku barikugira obumanzi oburikwenda kugirwa enshobero za ira kandi bakagira amani g'okugirwa entaro n'obusaasi obukwatanisibwa omu biteekateeko.

Nikusana, ni ekihamire ngu abashweeraine bategyereze amakuru makuru g'obwire.

Abashaija n'abakazi bashemereire kuhurira nk'abaakashweera obuhya, bagirwe ebyahweireho kandi batuure nibashemererwa omu biro ebi.

Okukunda n'obusungu n'ebintu by'amaani ebitarikukwatanisa. Omu kukunda tiharikubaasa kubaho obusungu bwona. Okukunda ni okusonshomerwa okutahwaho.

Harimu okukunda omu abo abarikuhurira okucweera mazima ahabw'okubonabona kw'abanywani baabo n'abazigu. Harimu okukunda kw'amazima omu ou orikukora n'omutima gweena ahabw'obuhangwa bw'abacureezi, abagyenyi, abarikwetenga.

Harimu okukunda omu ou orikuhurira obusaasi n'omutima gweena ahabw'omurimi orikunywesa omushana gwe, ahabw'omutaahi orikubonabona, ahabw'omushoberi orikushaba sente n'embwa ecureezi erikucweera kandi erikufwa enjara aha muhanda.

Twahiriza omuntu n'omutima gweena, tukakora omu muringo gw'obuhangwa kandi tukakora omuti kandi tukanywesa eby'omubisi omu kibanja tutarikugirwa omuntu weena, harimu obumanzi bw'amazima, obusaasi bw'amazima, okukunda kw'amazima.

Eky'obusaasi omu nsi, abantu tibagira obumanzi bw'amazima. Abantu nibafaayo aha bi barikukora byabo by'obugomi, ebi barikwetenga, eby'okutsinda, okumanya, eby'obwomeezi, okubonabona, okushemererwa, n'ebindi.

Omu nsi harimu abantu baingi, abarikugira obumanzi bw'obushuba. Harimu obumanzi bw'obushuba omu mupolitikia orikukora eby'obushuba, omu nhama erikushisha sente ahabw'okugira amani, ekitiinisa, ekifo, obugyenyi, n'ebindi. Titushemereire kuhumuza embwa n'engwe.

Obumanzi bw'amazima tiburikukwata aha kyona, baitu nikikwata aha bumanzi bw'obushuba obw'abapolitiikia abarikukora eby'obushuba, abajubi b'emishahara, abagomi abarikwetenga omukazi, n'ebindi.

Tushemereire kuba ab'obumanzi omutima. Obumanzi bw'amazima tiburi bw'obwomeezi, obusaasi bw'amazima ni ekihuriira ky'omutima.

Abantu ku barikugira obumanzi, barigirwa obusungu bwona obukwatanisibwa omu biteekateeko, eby'obwomeezi byoona eby'obusaasi eby'enkiizo nyingi kandi bayegye okutuura omu bwire, burizooba bashemererwa, burizooba ab'obumanzi, beijwire amazima g'amazima.

Eky'obusaasi, nyowe ni ekiteekateeko kandi nikituura omu biro byahweireho, burizooba nikwenda kugaruka omu biro byahweireho. Ebyahweireho nibihwera abantu, nibishiisha okushemererwa, nibwita okukunda.

Obwomeezi oburi omu biro byahweireho tiburikubaasa kwetegyereza omu muringo gw'amaani amakuru makuru g'obwire obu turikutuura.

Ni abantu baingi abaturikuhandiika nibasherura okuhuumurwa, nibasherura akagira amazima ahabw'okukiza omutima gwabo ogurikubonabona, baitu ni bake abarikufaayo okuhuumura abarikucweera.

Ni abantu baingi abaturikuhandiika kugira ngu batugambe embeera mbi ei barikutuuramu, baitu ni bake abarikushebura omugaati gumwe ogurikubagaburira kugira ngu bagugabane n'abandi abarikwetenga.

Abantu tibarikwenda kwetegyereza ngu enyima y'ekintu kyona harimu ensonga kandi ngu tukyenda okuhindura ensonga tukahindura ekintu.

Nyowe, nyowe owaitu ogurikukundwa, ni amaani agatuurire omu bakuru baitu kandi agaretere ensonga za ira ezi ebi turikukora nibigumira obwomeezi bwaitu.

Nitwetenga OBUMANZI kugira ngu tuhindure ensonga kandi tuhindure ebi turikukora. Nitwetenga obumanzi kugira ngu twebembeze engarawa y'obwomeezi bwaitu.

Nitwetenga obumanzi kugira ngu tuhindure obwomeezi bwaitu.

Obumanzi oburikukora oburikumarwa tiburi bw'obwomeezi. Obusaasi bw'amazima n'okukunda kw'amazima tibirikubaasa kuba eky'okutiina.

Nikusana kwetegyereza ngu okutiina nikushiisha obusaasi, nikuhwera obumanzi bw'omutima kandi nikutwiita ekiyoyooto kirungi ekirikukunda.

Okutiina ni omuzi gw'obutahikiirira bwona, entandikiro y'entaro yoona engunu, oburoho oburikushiisha kandi bukaita.

Abegyesa n'abegyesya b'amasomero, amakanisa n'amashuuri agari haihi bashemereire kwetegyereza okwetenga kw'okwebembera abegi baabo aha muhanda gw'obumanzi bw'amazima, obumanzi n'amazima g'omutima.

Abantu abashunguzi n'abagomi b'omu mirembe eyahweireho, omu mwanya gw'okwetegyereza eki oburoho bw'okutiina buri, bakabukora nk'ekyoyooto ekibi ekiri omu nju. Ekyareetwa oburingo obwo bukareeta obutahikiirira, obutaikiriza n'akarengo.

Abegyesa n'abegyesya bashemereire kwetegyereza obwire obu turikutuuramu, embeera mbi ei turikugira n'okwetenga kw'okuhiga emirembe emihya aha ntandikiro y'obuhangwa bw'ebiro by'okucweera n'obusaasi ebi turikutandika omu bwire obu bw'okubonabona n'omutima.

OBWEGYESA BW'AMAANI busingirwe aha saikoloji ey'okuhindura n'obuhangwa bw'okuhindura, ebikwataine n'omuringo oguhya gw'ebiro by'obuhya.

Omutindo gw'okukoragana gutunga okukora omu mwanya gw'okurwanisa okubi okw'okurwanisa obugomi. Nikiba kitarikubaasa kumanya kukoragana twaba twegumire aha itungwa ry'obumanzi oburikukora n'okuhindura.

Ni ekihamire kwetegyereza omu muringo gw'amaani, hatari aha mutindo gw'obwomeezi, baitu n'omu miringo etarikumanywa ey'obwomeezi etarikumanywa n'ey'ahansi eki oburemeezi bw'okugira obumanzi buri n'okutiina kw'obugomi. Tukora obwomeezi bw'eki obugomi buri omuriitwe n'oburemeezi bw'okugira obumanzi, buhurira omutima gwaitu ekihuriira kirungi ekirikwenda kandi kikagira OBUMANZI oburikukora obutari bw'obwomeezi.
