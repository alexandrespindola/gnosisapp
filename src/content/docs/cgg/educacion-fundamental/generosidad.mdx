Nikusooka kwenda n'okwendwa, kwonka ekihombo ky'ensi, abantu tibarikwenda nari kubendwa.

Ekyo ekitumwa okukunda nikintu eki abantu batarikumanya kandi nibakitwara nk'obwesherure n'okutiina.

Abantu ku baraabe nibabaasa kwenda n'okwendwa, entaro zaba zitariho omu nsi.

Obushwere bwingi oburikubaasa kushemererwa, tiburikukora batyo ahabw'obukiizi obwaahaiganwa omu bwengye.

Abashaija n'abakazi baaba nibaine embabazi, bakaihurira ebyahise ebibi kandi bakatura omu kushushuruka, beijuure okushemererwa kw'amazima.

Obwengye nibwita okukunda, nibukuhwerekyereza. Ebintu ebyabaho, ebitarikushemeza eby'enyima, eihari ry'enyima, byona ebi byateraniirwe omu bwengye, nibihwerekyereza okukunda.

Abakazi baingi abarikukwatirwa ekiniga bakabaasa kushemererwa ku baraabe baine embabazi ezirikukwera kwihurira ebyahise n'okutura omu biro ebi nibaramya abaami baabo.

Abaami baingi bakabaasa kushemererwa n'abakazi baabo ku baraabe baine embabazi ezirikukwera, nk'okutwara enshobi z'enyima n'okwihurira ebitarikushemeza ebyateraniirwe omu bwengye.

Nikusooka, nikikuru abashwere kumanya amakuru g'omumaiso.

Abaami n'abakazi bashemereire kuhurira nk'abashwerirwe obuhya, nibaihurira ebyahise kandi nibatura omu kushemererwa omu biro ebi.

Okukunda n'obukiizi nibintu ebitarikukwatana. Omu kukunda tihaine obukiizi bwona oburikubaasa kubaaho. Okukunda n'okusaasira ebiro byoona.

Harimu okukunda omu abo abarikuhurira obusaasi bw'amazima ahabw'okubonabona kw'abanywani baabo n'abazigu baabo. Harimu okukunda kw'amazima omu ogwo orikukora n'omutima gwona ahabw'amagara marungi g'abacureezi, abarikweburwa, abarikwetenga.

Harimu okukunda omu ogwo orikuhurira embabazi omuntu orikuhira emirerere n'entumbo ze, omuntu w'ekyaro orikubonabona, omushabirizi orikushaba esente n'embwa ecureezi erikubonabona kandi erikukwata endwara erikufa enjara aha rubaju rw'omugisha.

Twahwera omuntu n'omutima gwona, twahweera omuti n'okuhira ebimera omu musiri omu muringo ogw'obuntunzi n'okwekwatanisa hatariho oturikugyenda, harimu embabazi ez'amazima, embabazi z'amazima, okukunda kw'amazima.

Eky'obusaasi ahabw'ensi, abantu tibaine embabazi z'amazima. Abantu nibakuratira ebintu byabo by'obukopi, ebirikwetengwa, ebirikukwata, ebimanyiso, ebintu ebyabaho, okubonabona, okushemererwa, n'ebindi.

Omu nsi harimu abantu baingi abarikukwata embabazi z'obushuba. Harimu embabazi z'obushuba omu munyamahanga omuhinguzi, omu rufuto rw'eby'obushubani orikuta esente ahabw'ekigendereerwa ky'okugira amaani, ekitiinisa, omwanya, obugaiga, n'ebindi. Titushemereire kweteera empisi n'enkwata.

Embabazi z'amazima nizituruka buri kimwe, kwonka n'oburaingwa zirikubaasa kweteera n'embabazi z'obushuba ez'obukopi z'orufuto rw'eby'obushubani, obupiira bwa capitalistic, abashaija abarikwenda omukazi, n'ebindi.

Tushemereire kugira embabazi omutima. Embaazi z'amazima tizirikuruga omu bwengye, embabazi z'amazima n'omuhimbo gw'omutima.

Abantu ku baraabe nibaine embabazi, bakaihurira obukiizi bwona obwahaiganwa omu bwengye, ebintu byona ebibi ebyabaho eby'obwire obwingi obwahise kandi bakega kutura omu kasumi aha kasumi, obutoosha nibashemererwa, obutoosha baine embabazi, beijuure amazima g'amazima.

Eky'obusaasi, EME eri omutwe kandi etura omu byahise, eyo etoosha kwagaruka aha byahise. Ebyahise nibihwera abantu, nibihwerekyereza okushemererwa, nibwita okukunda.

Obwengye oburi omu byahise tiburikubaasa kumanya omuringo gwaahika amakuru g'kasumi oku turikukwata.

Harimu abantu baingi abarikutuhandiika nibashaka eky'okubahuumuriza, nibashaba omuti gw'omugisha okucureereza omutima gwabo ogubonabona, kwonka harimu abake abaarikukuratanisa kukuumuriza abari omu maganya.

Harimu abantu baingi abarikutuhandiika kutugamba aha mbeera embi ebi barikuturamu, kwonka harimu abake abarikugabura omukate gumwe ogu baraakuriire okugurunga n'abandi abarikwetenga.

Abantu tibarikwenda kwetegyereza ngu enyima y'ebyihurikizi byoona harimu akagwate kandi ngu tukundize akagwate nituhindura ekyihurikizi.

EME, EME yitu eyo turikukunda, n'amaani agaturire omu bakuru baitu kandi agahindura akagwate k'enyima ekandi ebiriho ebi nibikoma amagara gaitu.

Nitwetenga EMBAZI okukundiza akagwate n'okuhindura ebyihurikizi. Nitwetenga embabazi okwebembera n'obwengye ekaato ry'amagagara gaitu.

Nitwetenga embabazi okuhindura amagara gaitu buri kimwe.

Embabazi z'amazima ezirikukora tizirikuruga omu bwengye. Embaaazi ez'amazima n'okukunda kw'amazima, tibrikuubaasa kuruga omu kutiina.

Nikusooka kwetegyereza ngu okutiina nikuhwerekyereza embabazi, nikuhwera embabazi z'omutima kandi nihwerekyereza omuhiimbo ogurikununuuka ogw'OKUKUNDA.

Okutiina n'omuzi gw'ebishuba byoona, entandiiro y'ekyama y'entaroo yonna, oburogo oburikujwera kandi oburikwita.

Abegyesa n'abegyesa abakuru ab'eshuuri, abakariiji n'amakanisa bashemereire kwetegyereza ekyetengo ky'okwebembera abegyi baabo aha muhanda gw'embabazi z'amazima, obujajure n'amazima g'omutima.

Abantu abatokoreire n'ababiine ab'omumaiso agaahise, omu mwanya gw'okwetegyereza eki oburogo bw'okutiina buleeza, bakabirima nk'ekimera eky'okufa ky'ekigo cy'amahuumiro. Ekyarugiremu nikyo bishuba, okuteeranira n'obutariho bw'amateeka.

Abegyesa n'abegyesa abakuru bashemereire kwetegyereza obwire obu turikukwata, embeera embi ebi turimu n'ekyetengo ky'okwijutsya obusinge obuhya aha mitwarize y'ebihinduka erikukwatana n'emyaka y'ekiro kya atomic ekyo omu kasumi kano ak'amaganya n'obusaasi kirikutaandika omu mwanya omurungi gw'okututuma kw'obwengye.

OBWEGYESO BWA FUNDAMENTAL bukwatana n'eby'obwengye ebirikuhinduka n'emitwarize y'ebihinduka, birikukwatana n'omugisha omusya ogurikuhugura ogw'emyaka emisya.

Amakuru g'okukora hamwe nigahindura omuringo gwona gw'okutera omu mutwe ogw'okugana obukopi. Nikiba kigumire kumanya okukora hamwe twihireho omusingi gw'embabazi ezirikukora n'okuhinduka.

Nikusooka kwetegyereza omuringo gwona, hatariho aha mutego gw'ebwengye, kureka omu mahuuro gatari gamanyirwe g'obwengye obutari gamanyirwe n'obwengye obucureezi eki okubura embabazi n'obujunaamu buleeza. Okukora obwengye bw'eki kiri omuriitwe obujunaamu n'okubura embabazi nikiruga omu mutima gwaitu omuhuimbo ogurikununuuka ogw'OKUKUNDA KW'AMAZIMA n'EMBAZI EZIRIKUKORA ezi zitari kuruga omu bwengye.
