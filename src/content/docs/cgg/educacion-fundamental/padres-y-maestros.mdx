Ekizibu ekikuru eky'ebyaara by'abantu bonka tikiri abeeshuri ab'eshomero ribanza nari erya haiguru, baitu babazyali n'abeeshesi.

Babazyali n'abeeshesi baaba batarikumanya eki bari, batarikubaasa kwetegyereza omwana omuto, baaba batarikwetegyereza obukorano bwabu n'ebiremwa ebitandika okutunga amagara, baaba nibareeberera okukuza amagezi g'abaana babu bonka, nitubaasa kureeta emishomesa misya tuta?

Omwana, omweshuuri, agenda aha ishuuri kutooreza emihanda, baitu abeeshesi baaba ab'omutima mugufu, ab'enyima, ab'ekihakanisa embiringito, nikwo omweshuuri araabe.

Abeeshesi bashemereire kwetegyereza obundi, okwetegyereza eki bari, okucweereza ebi baamanyi, okwetegyereza ngu turikwingira omwanya mushya.

Abeeshesi baakuhinduka, ebyaara by'abantu bonka nibyahinduka.

Kushomesa omushomesha nikyo kikuru ahabw'okuba omuntu weena ashomire bingi, omuntu weena atungire ebishashani, omuntu weena aine eky'okushomesa, akora nk'omweheshe w'eishomero, ahindukire nk'oku ari, obwomeezi bwe buzibwe omu nteekateeka enkumi na nkumi ezi ashomereire kandi tarikwija kuhinduka n'okwita.

Abeeshesi bashemereire kushomesa OKU TUTEKATEEKA, baitu bahigaana okubashomesa EKI BASHEMEREIRE KUTEEKATEEKA.

Babazyali n'abeeshesi nibatuura bazaaire eby'okutekateeka ebibi, eby'eby'obushoboorozi, eby'emitima, n'ebindi.

Babazyali n'abeeshesi nibatuura baheebwa obwire bwabo, okukonagana n'eby'obusungu bwabo, batarikukunda kwega hamu n'okumaraho ebizibu ebi abashaija n'abashiki b'OMURONGO MUSHYA barikureeta.

Habaho obushwere obubi bw'omu biteekateeko, okugamba n'eby'obushoboorozi, baitu babazyali n'abeeshesi bazaaire okutekateeka hamu n'eby'okutekateeka byabo kandi baine obwire bw'okutekateeka aha by'obushoboorozi bw'abaana baabo, okubaha omurimo batarikufa enjara kandi nikwo kwonka.

Okuhakana n'okwikiriza kw'abantu boona, abazaire baingi tibakukunda abaana baabo, baaba nibabakunda, bakurwanira eby'omugasho, bakutekateeka aha bizibu by'EBYAARA BY'ABANTU BONKA n'ekigyendererwa ky'okumaraho enkyukakukyu.

Babazyali baaba nibakunda abaana baabo omu mazima, tigu haraabaho entaro, tibakukunda orubaju orukuru hamu n'ihanga omu kukonagana n'ensi yoona, ahabw'okuba nikireeta ebizibu, entaro, okucweiganisa okubi, obusinge obubi bw'abaana baitu abashaija n'abashiki.

Abantu nibashoma, nibetebeekanisa kuba abaganga, abanyamyane, abashazi, n'ebindi kandi omu nteekateeka tibetebeekanisa omurimo ogurikukora n'ogurikugumaho ogwo kwora abazaire.

Obwegamo bw'eka, obutakurata abantu baitu, obusinge bw'obutoro bw'eka, tiburimu omugasho omu kikumi ahansi y'ekikumi, ahabw'okuba nihinduka enshonga y'okuhwerera n'okushwekyereza obutoosha.

Okuhinduka, omuguto omu mazima, nibibasaika okugwa aha mwanya ogurikucweiganisa abantu omu nsi yoona.

Itwe boona turi EKA EMWE kandi tihirimu omugasho kutuhurukya omwe aha ondi, okureeberera nka muka zaitu abantu aba turikugaita, n'ebindi.

Obwegamo OMWESHONGORE OMWEKA nikirora okuhinduka kw'abantu okweitungo, nikicweiganisa abantu, nikireeta entaro, abakuru, eby'obutungi bw'enyima, ebizibu by'eby'obushoboorozi, n'ebindi.

Babazyali baakukunda abaana baabo omu mazima, nibyaagwa aha butaka obunyakabi bw'obushatu hamu n'enyungu ezirikushwekyereza okutoro kandi eka neereka okuhinduka okwegamiire n'okutaramu mugasho.

Kugwa omu nyungu z'obwegamiire bw'eka, nikwo okugaita kw'obunywani hamu n'abazaire abandi boona, hamu n'abeeshesi, hamu n'abantu boona.

Ebiruga omu BUGANDA OMU MAZIMA, nikyo OKUHINDUKA KW'ABANTU OMU MAZIMA, OBUGUTO bw'obunywani bw'AHEISHOMERO okw'ensi enungi.

OMUHEESI ashemereire kuba mweteereze, ashemereire kugaita babazyali boona, aha kitebe ky'ababazyali abakuru hamu n'okubagambira omu mazima.

Nikikuru babazyali kwetegyereza ngu omurimo gw'ebyaara by'abantu bonka nigukorwa aha nyunguto ey'amaani ey'okukoragana omu butunu hagati y'abazaire n'abeeshesi.

Nikikuru kugambira babazyali ngu EMISHOMESA EY'AMAANI n'ekikuru okurinda ebyembi by'emirongo emisya.

Nikikuru kugambira babazyali ngu okukuza amagezi n'ekikuru baitu tikirikukora byoona, nikikuru kureka okurikukora, nikikuru kushomesa abashaija n'abashiki okwetegyereza eki bari, okwetegyereza enshobe zaabo hamu n'obusaasi bwabo bw'omutima.

Tushemereire kugambira babazyali ngu abaana bashemereire kwima ahabw'OKUKUNDA kandi hatariho okwenda kw'ebiremwa.

Nikusangwa nkubi hamu n'okukora ebintu ebibi okutora okwenda kwaitu kw'ebiremwa, ebiragiro byaitu eby'amaani eby'okwebwanisa, okubazaire omutima gwaitu obubi hamu n'ebiteekateeka byaitu by'obushwekyereza.

Abaana b'abashaija n'abashiki nibatorera omurima gwaitu kandi nshangwa mbi okweta ensi y'ebiremwa.

Abeeshesi b'eishomero, ebikwaate hamu na yunivasite bashemereire kugaita omu zaaro y'ebikorwa, babazyali baine eky'okugyendera ekirungi ky'okubashomesa omuhanda gw'obujunaanizibwa bw'eby'omutima aha bana baabo hamu n'abantu boona hamu n'ensi.

ABEESHESI baine omurimo gw'OKWETEREEREZA boona hamu n'okutooreza babazyali.

Tushemereire kukunda omu mazima okuhindura ensi. Tushemereire kugaita okurinda hagati yaitwe boona, EKA ey'obuhangwa ey'OMWANYA MUSHYA ogurikutandika omu bwomeezi omu kubanda kw'okuteekateeka.
