Ekizibu ekikuru ekya EDUCATION PUBLICA tikiri abeeshuli abato oba abakuru, baitu ni BAZAIRE na BAISHOMBYA.

Bazaire na Baishombya batarikumanya ebi bari, batarikukwasisa omwana omuto, omwishiki, batarikwetegyereza omukago gwabu n’ebiremwa ebi, bakuruha omu kwengyesa obwengye bw’abeeshuli babu, nitubaasa kutaaho omuringo musya ogw’obushomesa?

Omwana, omweeshuli, omwishiki, nazaaha Eshomero kukuza obwengye, baitu Baishombya, Banyinaishombya, nibaba ab’obwengye bukye, abakuru, abashubiza enyima, nikwo abeebiija kubaasa kuba.

Abengyesa bashemereire kwerengyesa, kwemanya, kushusha ebi barikumanya, kwetegyereza ngu nituringira omu bwire busya.

Abengyesa baakushushuka, obushomesa bwa publica nibushushuka.

KWENGYESA OMUSHOMBYA nikyo kigumire ahabw’okuba omuntu weena ashoomize bingi, omuntu weena aine ekitsibo, omuntu weena aine eki arikwegyesa, orikukora nk’omushomesa w’Eshomero, yamala kuba nk’oku ari, obwengye bwe bwajula omu biteekateeko enkumi itaano ebi ashomize kandi tarikubaasa kuhinduka.

Baishombya bashemereire kwegyesa OKUTEEKATEEKA, baitu eky’obusaasi nibakuruha omu kubegyesa EKI BASHEMEREIRE KUTEEKATEEKA.

Bazaire na Baishombya bazaara obuhuna obutatiina obw’eby’entaari, obw’embeera, obw’emitima, n’ebindi.

Bazaire na Baishombya nibakuruha n’obutaari bwabu bwona, tibarikukora n’amaani kushooma n’okumaraho obutaari oburikubuzibwa abashaija na bashiki b’omuringo musya.

Hariho okwonooneka kw’obwengye, okw’empisa, n’obw’embeera, baitu bazaire na Baishombya bajwire okutaataagura n’obutaari bwabu kandi baine obwire bukye obw’okuteekateeka aha by’eby’entaari by’abaana babu, omu kubeha omulimo batarikufa enjara kandi nikyo kyoona.

Okurugaana n’okwikiriza kw’abantu, abazaire baingi tibarikukunda abaana babu buzima, baaba nibabakunda, bakurwana ahabw’obugumiire bwabu, bakutaataagura aha butaari bw’obushomesa bwa publica n’ekigyendererwa ky’okuhindura buzima.

Bazaire baaba nibakunda abaana babu buzima, tihariho ntaro, tibarikugira omukago mwingi hagati y’eka n’ihanga omu kuraara eby’ensi yoona, ahabw’okuba eki kireeta obutaari, entaro, okugabura okw’akabi, embeera mbi ahabw’abaana baitu.

Abantu nibashooma, nibeteekaniza kuba abasaahazi, abajingi, abaramuzi, n’ebindi, baitu tibeteekaniza omulimo ogukuru ogurikuguma ogw’okuba abazaire.

Obwegundi bw’eka ekyo, okubura okukunda abantu abandi, eky’obweeru bw’eka ekyo, tibirikwetegyerezibwa omu myakaana ijana, ahabw’okuba nibihinduka ekintu ekirikwonoona kandi n’okutahuka kw’embeera.

Okukura, Okuhinduka buzima, nibibaasika buzima kukutsindika obugara obwo oburi nk’obwa China obutugabura, obututangaanisa n’ensi yoona.

Itwe twona tuli EKA EMWE kandi tikirikwetegyerezibwa kutubaaza, okutwara nk’eka abantu bakye abaturikugumaho, n’ebindi.

Ekyo ky’obwegundi ky’eka kishomesa okukura kw’embeera, kugabura abantu, kureeta entaro, eby’emikago, eby’entaari ezirikutegyekwa, obutaari bw’eby’entaari, n’ebindi.

Bazaire nibakunda abaana babu buzima, ebyombeko, obugara obubi obw’okwetangaanisa nibiitwaara, kandi eka nerekeraaho kuba ekintu eky’obwegundi ekitarikwetegyerezibwa.

Obugara bw’eka obw’obwegundi bwagwa, haho nikubaho obumwe n’abazaire abandi boona, Baishombya, n’embeera yoona.

Ekyarugamu ky’OBUMWE BW’AMAANI, n’OKUHINDURA EMMEERA OKW’AMAANI, OKUHINDURA kw’ebya EDUCATION ahabw’ensi enungi.

OMWENGYESA ashemereire kumanya bingi, ashemereire kukora ekyegombe ky’abazaire n’Abanyinaitwe, ekitebe ekikuru eky’abazaire n’okubagambira obuhame.

Nikyetagisa abazaire kwetegyereza ngu omulimo gw’obushomesa bwa publica nigukorwa aha musingi muhango ogw’obukwatanisa hagati y’abazaire na Baishombya.

Nikyetagisa kugambira abazaire ngu EDUCATION EY’OKUTANDIKA nikyetagisa okuhigura ebyembiija ebisya.

Nikyetagisa kugambira abazaire ngu okukuza obwengye nikyetagisa baitu tikiri kyoona, nikyetagisa ekintu ekindi, nikyetagisa kwegyesa abashaija na bashiki kwemanya, kumanya enshobi zabu, ebinyeenyo byabu ebya Psykologia.

Nitweshemerire kugambira abazaire ngu abaana bashemereire kwimwa ahabw’OKUKUNDANA hatariho KUKWAATA NK’ENYAMWA.

Nikikora obusaasi n’obunaku kutwara ebyetengo byaitu eby’enyama, okukwaata kwaitu kw’enyama okw’amaani, okutekateeka kwaitu okubi n’engyenzi zaitu omu bazaarwa baitu.

Abaana n’Abashiki nigo obutangwa bwaitu kandi nikikora ekyaha okwongya ensi n’obutangwa bw’enyama.

Baishombya b’Eshomero, Kolegyo na Yunivasiti bashemereire kukora omukoro omu nyumba y’ebikorwa, abazaire boona n’ekigyendererwa ky’okubegyesa omuhanda gw’obujunaanizibwa bw’empisa aha baana babu n’aha Beemera n’Ensi.

ABENGYESA baine obujunaanizibwa bw’OKWERENGYESA na kukuza abazaire.

Nitwetaga kukunda buzima okuhindura ensi. Nitwetaga kwegaita okuhigura omu itwe twona, Ekishaani eky’omutaano eky’Obwire Busya ekyerikushoberwa omu biro ebi omu ruteengo oruhango rw’okuteekateeka.
