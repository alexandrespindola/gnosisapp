Abantu abazimbireho okutambula amahanga goona okusoma ebika by'abantu byoona omu bwengye obuhwerereize, basoboire kwetegyereza ngu obutonde bw'ekiramu ekitegeera ekihabwe eiziina ly'omuntu, buri obumu, obwaba omu Europe enkuru nari Africa eyehiririireire omu bugwate, omu itaka eririkwera erya Vedas nari endiima z'oburengyerwa z'India, omu Austria nari omu China.

Ekigyendererwa eki, ekihango eki ekirikukangaiza omuntu weena omwegesi, nikisobora kwetegyerezebwa munonga omutambuzi aazindwire amasomero, Koleegi na Yunivasite.

Twahikire omu bwire bw'okukora ebintu byingi omu bwire bumwe. Hati byoona bikakorwa biri omu mukerre egwire kandi bingi. Engege z'enyangi, Emotoka, Ebintu by'amagunju, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

N'obu kyakuba kikirukangaiza, kiri mazima ngu amasomero g'eby'emikono, Yunivasite, n'ebindi nabyo bizindukire omu kufuura ebitegeerezo by'abantu bingi omu bwire bumwe.

Omu bwire obu obw'okukora ebintu byingi omu bwire bumwe, ekigyendererwa kyonyini omu bwomezi nikwo kutunga obuteekateeki bw'ebyefayo. Abantu batiina byoona kandi nibaserra obuteekateeki.

Okuteekereza ebyafeerwe omu bwire obu obw'okukora ebintu byingi omu bwire bumwe, nikirema ahakuba emitwarize y'eby'omunsomero ezembaine esembiire ha kureeba buzima.

"Eirengerezibwa eryahweraho" rirakuratira obunaku obu. Omuntu yaba nayenda kuba ow'omutaano, atarikushusha abandi, boona nibamucweecweeta, boona nibamugamba, nibamuhereza omusaara, nibamwebembera omulimo, n'ebindi.

Okukunda kw'okutunga esente z'okutunga n'okushemererwa, okuhuta kw'okuhika aha buhikirire omu bwomezi, okuserra obuteekateeki bw'ebyefayo, okukunda kw'okugura ebintu byingi okweranga abandi, n'ebindi, nibigira ngu okuteekereza okurungi, okwomutima n'okwebonekeza birekeho.

Kyetegyereizeire kimazima ngu okutiina nikuzimbya omu mutwe kandi nikagumya omutima.

Omu bwire obu obwingi obw'okutiina n'okuserra obuteekateeki, abantu nibeyama omu bujumo bwabo, omu bihuriro byabo, omu kantu kabo, omwanya omu bwara nibateekereza ngu nibasobora kutunga obuteekateeki obwingi, obutazara obuzibu kandi tibarikwenda kuruga omwo, nibatiina obwomezi, nibatiina emigonzi emya, ebyahukire, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Emisomo yoona enunuura eyembaine esembiire ha kuteekereza n'okuserra obuteekateeki, abantu bagira ubwoba, nibatiina n'ekisiiki kyabo.

Abantu, nibatiina byoona, nibatiina kuruga omu miteerane emikuru egitaireho, kuba abahukire aha bandi, kuteekereza omu muringo ogwa buhinduzi, kucweeka n'ebiteekerezo byoona eby'ekitongore ekikaziire, n'ebindi.

N'aha mazima habaho abantu bake omunsi abesimire kandi abetegereza, abarikukunda kwetegyereza obuzibu obwoona oburi omu mutwe, kwonka omuringo mwingi oburi omuri itwe tigurikukundwa kukora n'omutima ogukwatanisa.

Habaho emigwa ibiri ey'OKUTEERA akatima egi etaaireho. Okubanza: Okuteera akatima omu bwenge obutatiinire. Eyakabiri: Okuteera akatima omu bwenge obuhwereireize obw'OBWENGYE.

Omugwa gw'okubanza ogw'Okuteera akatima nikwo KUHINDURA kw'obuteekateeki. Omugwa gw'okabiri ogw'Okuteera akatima nikwo BUHINDUZI.

Omu mugwa gw'okubanza ogw'Okuteera akatima turasanga OMUHINDUZI ohemba engoye nkuru kandi arungiisa orugo orukuru rutarikugwa, omulingo ogurikugaruka enyima, Omuhinduzi w'obusa n'omwenge, omwebemberi w'embaga kandi akareeta obuhanguzi, omuntu w'emundu aha kibega, Omutegesereza orikukunda kutwara abantu aha kibuga abarikwenda kukuratira eby'okubura.

Omu mugwa gwakabiri ogw'Okuteera akatima turasanga BUDDHA, YESU, HERMES, omuhinduzi, OMUTEERI akatima OMUSHONGOYE, AKAMANYI, abapaladini ABAHAGO ba BUHINDUZI BWA MAANI, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Abari somera aha ndeebe y'obushabafu y'okwambuka emyanya mirungi omu kiterero ky'omwanya ogurikugumisiriza, kwambuka, kusyamba aha iguru ry'okuseera, kwebonekeza, n'ebindi, tibarimu bwengye buzima, bakuba abasirusiru omu butonde, abatekateeki, abajwiirire, abashuma ikumi omu kikumi.

Kyetegyereizeire kimazima ngu omuntu yaba atarimu KUHWERA hamwe kw'okuteekereza n'okukunda, n'obu twayeega emisomo mingi, obwomezi nibuhinduka obutahikire, obukanganisa, obw'embabazi n'obw'obutiinisa obwingi obw'amako goona.

Habwekyo kwonka n'obutiinisa okukanganisa, nitusobora kugamba ngu kutari kwega OBWEGI, obwomezi nibuhinduka oburikuhukya, obutagasiire kandi oburikworeka.

EKIRAMU EKITEGEERA kirimu EGO ERIRIMU ekikorwa eky'OBUTONDE obw'okubura oburikugumya emisomo EMIBI.

MMI MINGI eitu twetwara omunda, niyo ntandikwa nkuru y'obuzibu bwitu boona n'okukanganisa.

EMISOMO EKURU eratendeke ebiziikuru byitu emyenda DIDAKTIKA yitu ey'omutwe ey'OKUREKA MMI.

Kurugiirira aha miringo emigwa egirikukora Ego (MMI) nitusobora kwetwaza omunda ey'amagara agatazara omu mitwe yaitu, nukwo turiba ABAHIKIRE.

Ensi yona n'okubaho omu nda ya omwe omwe okw'emi MMI, titurikwija kwemera obwomezi baitu kwonka turikwija kubumera n'abandi.

Ekirikugasa ki twesomera amateeka kandi twayehindura abanyamateeka, twakomeza enka? Ekirikugasa ki kuterereza omu mutwe gwitu obwengye obwingi, twakomeza kukanganisibwa? Ebikorwa bita by'eby'emikono n'obwegesi twabikozesa okucweka abantu baitu?

Tikirikugasa kutendeka, kugyenda aha masomo, kwesoma, twaba nitucweka abantu baitu habw'okukora obubi obuzibire.

Ekigyendererwa ky'emisomo tikisemeiire kuba kukora buli mwaka abaserri b'emilimo basya, emigwa emyenda ey'abashuma, abagwere basya abaritarakubasa kusiima edini y'omunywani, n'ebindi.

Ekigyendererwa kirungi EKIKURU ky'EMISOMO nikyo kukora abasaija n'abakazi ABAHIKIRE kandi nukwo nibagumisiriza kandi nibengezi.

Kwonka abegesa b'omu masomero, Koleegi na Yunivasite, nibateekereza byoona, habw'okuzindura OBWENGYE OBW'OMUNDA bw'ABEGI.

Omuntu weena asobora kwegomba kandi atunge eby'omulingo, eby'omuhendo, dipulooma kandi akomere omunywani omu by'omubazi by'obwomezi, kwonka eki tikirikugamba ngu nobwengezi.

OBWENGYE tiburikuba obukora obumazi, OBWENGYE tiburikuba kimwe n'okumanya obwengye bw'ebitabo, OBWENGYE tikwo kubasa okugarukamu ebigambo aha kizibu kyona. OBWENGYE tikwo kuheereza amaani agatazara aha mutwe. OBWENGYE nikwo kubasa okutunga OBWEGI bw'eiziina, EKYA MAZIMA, ekyokugira ngu KIRI.

EMISOMO EKURU niyo sayaansi erikutureetera kukangusa obubasa obu omuri itwe n'omuri abandi.

EMISOMO EKURU neyehamba buli MUNTU okutunga ENTEEKATEEKA ZA MAZIMA ezirikugyenda omu mazima gw'okwetegyereza ekikuru n'OKUHWERERA hamwe KWEENEENDA.

Obutoora OBWENGYE BWEENEENDA omuri itwe, nukwo OKWEBONEKEZA kuhinduka OKWEGUMYA EKYEGOISTA kandi EKICWEEKYA.

EMISOMO EKURU neyetangira n'okukangusa omuri buli muntu OKUBASA okwetegyereza omu by'omutwe byoona kandi hatari kwereka aha kulonda okwebonekeza okw'EMI MINGI.
