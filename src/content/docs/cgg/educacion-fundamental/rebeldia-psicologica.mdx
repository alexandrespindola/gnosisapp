Abo abagendeye omu nsi yoona, nibeejenda kushoma engyenderwaza y'abantu boona, bashoboirwe kumanya ngu obutonde bw'enshorra enu, ekihangwe eky'amagezi ekyetwa omuntu, buzima nibumwe, kwaba omu Buraya omuhere, rundi Africa eyarukire okwera, omu itaka erikwera erya Vedas rundi omu Buhindi bw'oburengerazuba, omu Austria rundi omu China.

Eky'amazima eki, amazima makuru agu gashembeza omushomi weena, nikishobora kurora ahu omugendeye agenderere amasomero, za kolegi n'amashomero agali ahaiguru.

Tushikire omubiro by'okukora ebintu byingi omu bwingi. Obu byoona nibikorwa omu mulingo gw'aha rugyendo, omu bwingi. Misiri y'endegye, emotoka, ebintu by'omuhendo, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

N'obu kirahika, kirungi kukimanya ngu amasomero g'emikono, amashomero agali ahaiguru, n'ebindi, nabo bakolelwe nk'amakolero ag'amagezi ag'okukora ebintu omu bwingi.

Omu biro ebi eby'okukora ebintu omu bwingi, ekigendererwa kimwe omu magara ni kushanga obuteekaniza bw'eby'obushubuzi. Abantu batiina byoona kandi nibashaba obuteekaniza.

Okuteekateeka kw'obwegendereza omu biro ebi eby'okukora ebintu omu bwingi, kiba kirukumira habw'okuba engyenderwaza mpya eshimutsirwe aha migisha.

"Omucwe gw'Obuhya" bagumire omu bucucu bw'amagezi. Omuntu yaba ayenda kuba mwebaine, atali nk'abandi boona, buli omwe namucwekyereza, buli omwe namunega, natekwa omu kihumuro, naganirwa omurimo, n'ebindi.

Okushaba kwo kushanga esente okuba omugisha, okuhereza omu magara, okushaba obuteekaniza bw'eby'obushubuzi, okushaba okugura ebintu bingi okwesimisa abandi, n'ebindi, bihamya okuteekateeka okweru, okw'obutonde n'okw'obwegendereza.

Kishoboirwe kwooleka buli kimwe ngu okutiina kushwekyereza obwongo kandi kikalubya omutima.

Omu biro ebi eby'okutiina kw'amanyi n'okushaba obuteekaniza, abantu nibeyamira omu bihira byabo, omu buriri bwabo, omu kibanza kyabo, omu kibanza eky'abarikwikiriza ngu nibashobora kutunga obuteekaniza obwingi, obutali bwingi bw'ebizibu kandi tibarikwenda kuvaayo, batiina amagara, batiina endagiriro empya, okwetegereza empya, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Ekyetwa engyenderwaza mpya egi yoona eshimutsirwe aha kutiina n'okushaba obuteekaniza, abantu nibatiina, batiina n'ekishushani kyabo.

Abantu, batiina byoona, batiina kuva omu biragiro eby'omu nyima ebitekereziibweho, kuba batali nk'abandi bantu, kuteekateeka omu mulingo ogw'empinduka, kwera n'emyemerero yoona y'abantu abacwerere, n'ebindi.

N'omugisha, abantu abeesimire n'abamanyirizi nibatura omu nsi, abarikwenda kumanya obuzibu bw'obwongo, kwonka omu bwingi obutali bwaweitu tihaliho okuba n'omwoyo ogutagumire n'ogw'okutagaliza.

Haliho miringo ebiri ey'OKUTAGUMIRA eyamanyirwe. Okubanza: Okutagumira kw'obwongo okw'amaani. Okwakabiri: Okutagumira kw'obwongo okuhwere kw'ENSHONGO.

Omulingo gw'okubanza ogw'Okutagumira ni Reakshinari konservatifu kandi Retardatari. Omulingo gw'okwakabiri ogw'Okutagumira ni REVOLUSHONARI.

Omu mulingo gw'okubanza ogw'Okutagumira kw'Obwongo tushangamu OMUHINDURA arikutunga engoye enjeru kandi arikusanura enyanya z'amazimbe agarukire kugwa, omulingo ogw'aha nyima, Revolushonari w'eshagama n'amarwa, omwebembezi w'eby'obusilikaani n'okugwa kw'obulemi, omuntu aine emundu aha rubaju, Dikitaata arikushemererwa okugabula buli omwe atarikwikiriza embeera ze, enyoroto ze.

Omu mulingo gw'okwakabiri ogw'Okutagumira kw'Obwongo tushangamu BUDDHA, YESU, HERMES, omuhinduzi, OMUGAMBIRE AINE ENSHONGO, OMUSHUNGURI, abashomesi bahango aba REVOLUSHONI Y'OBWENGYE, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Abo abeshomesa n'ekigendererwa ky'omujuju eky'okwambukira aha migisha mirungi omu kirima ky'abakozi ba gavumenti, kwambuka, kwambukira aha ntango y'ekyambu, okwerekana, n'ebindi, tibali bahwere, nibo bafwiire okuba abakafu, abatali bahwere, abahwere, ciento por ciento bribones.

Kimanyirwe okuhereza kwooleka ngu, bwanya omu muntu tihaliho OBUGUMU bw'okuteekateeka n'obuhuli, n'obu twakaba twahangire engyenderwaza enungi, amagara gaba manafu, agatakwatanisa, agarukumya n'okukora entekerezo zingi ez'obwona.

Omuhanda gwona ogw'okukwehana kandi tutatina kwebuliza, tushobora kuhereza ngu hataliho engyenderwaza Y'OBWOMEZI, amagara gaba mabi, agatalimo mugasho n'agabisa.

ENSHORRA EY'ENSHONGO aine EGO Y'OMUNDA ekoleirwe mubi kuraba omu ENTIDADES ez'ombali ezirikuguma n'ENGYENDERWAZA EMBI.

EYO Y'OBWINGI bw'omuheru, n'entandikwa y'ebizibu byaitu byoona n'ebitakwatanisa.

ENGYENDERWAZA Y'ENTANDIKWA eshomerize emirembe empya ENYEGYESA y'OBWONGO okwa okugwa kwa EYO.

Omu kwebisazamu by'enyanya ez'obwingi ezirikukora Ego (EYO) tushobora kuteekateeka omuheru omuheru ogw'okwetegereza kw'omuntu, obwo nituba TUGUMIRE.

N'obukama haliho omunda yaitwe buri omwe EYO Y'OBWINGI, titurikwamisha amagara gaitu, kwonka nitukwamisha n'abandi.

Kiriho mugasho ki kusoma amateeka kandi tukora aba looya, twagumizamu obutali bw'embabazi? Kiriho mugasho ki kwegerereza omu bwongo bwaitwe okumanya kungi, tukagumira kutakwatanisa? Kiriho mugasho ki okumanya kw'emikono n'eby'obwomezi twabikozesa okucwekyereza abandi bantu?

Tikiliho mugasho kweshomesa, kutaaha amasomo, kusoma, twagumira kukora kwebisazamu omu mulingo omubi buli omwe oundi.

Ekigendererwa ky'engyenderwaza tikishemereire kuba ekindi kyonka kukora abashabi b'emirimo, omuce gw'oburiifu, abayivu abatamanyi kusiima edini y'omuturanyi, n'ebindi.

Ekigendererwa kikuru ekya ENGYENDERWAZA Y'ENTANDIKWA kishemereire kuba okukora abashaija abazima n'abakali ABAGUMIRE kandi habw'ekyo abantu bahwere kandi abeeshongo.

Mubi, abashomesa b'amasomero, za kolegi n'amashomero agali ahaiguru, boona nibateekateeka, kubuza ENYEGYESA Y'ENSHONGO entumwa.

Buli muntu nashobora kwenda kandi atunge emitwaro, okusiima, dipulooma kandi akakora omu kibanja ky'eby'omulingo gw'emikono, kwonka eki tikirikumanyisa kuba ENSHONGO.

ENSHONGO etashobora kuba mero funzionalismo mekhaniko, ENSHONGO etashobora kuba okugaruka kw'omugisha gw'okumanya omu bitabo, ENSHONGO tebukubasa okugaruka omu bigambo ebikwata ahari buli kizibu. ENSHONGO teburo mero verbalizacion y'omutima. ENSHONGO ni amani okutunga ENSHONGO, EKY'AMAZIMA, eki buzima KIRI.

ENGYENDERWAZA Y'ENTANDIKWA ni ekirungo ekirikuturetera kwebusibusa omu mani agaitu omu bagandi baitu.

ENGYENDERWAZA Y'ENTANDIKWA neyamba buli OMUNTU kushanga OBUHWEZI bw'amazima oburikuruga omu kweshomesa n'OKWETEGEREZA kw'omuheru kw'EYEENE.

Obu hataliho omunda yaitwe KWETEGEREZA, obwo OKWEREKA kwahinduka OKUHAMEZA okw'EGOISTA N'OKWECWEKYEREZA.

ENGYENDERWAZA Y'ENTANDIKWA negenderera kwebusibusa omu buli muntu AMANI okwetegereza omu kibanja ky'obwongo kandi kutali kwewa okushemererwa okwa OKWEREKA okubi okwa EYO Y'OBWINGI.
