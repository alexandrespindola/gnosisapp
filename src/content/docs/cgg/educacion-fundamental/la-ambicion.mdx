OKUHUKAANA kwiine enshonga eziraingi, ne emwe ahaazo n'ekyo ekiyitwa OKUTIIINA.

Omwojo omucureezi ogwo owaaza engaito z'abakungu ab'ekitiinisa omu paaka z'emishaaha mikuru, nabaasa kufuuka omushumaayi yaaba atandiise kuteeina obwooro, kuteeina ab'omunda ye, kuteeina ebiro bye eby'omu maisho.

Omushubaija omucureezi ogwo orikukora omu duka erikuru ery'omuguzi mukuru, nabaasa kufuuka omushumaayi nari omwendwa omu nyekiro obwire bukya, yaaba atandiise kuteeina ebiro by'omu maisho, kuteeina amagara, kuteeina obukuru, kuteeina ab'omunda ye, n'ebindi.

Omuheereza orikuzimburuka omu resitooraani erikuru nari omu wooteeri enkuru, nabaasa kufuuka GANSTER, omwoza omubanka, nari omushumaayi omukuru munonga, yaaba ateeina ab'omunda ye, obuheereza bwe obucureezi, ebiro bye eby'omu maisho, n'ebindi.

Akaazu kakye nikahukanira kuba ak'ekitiinisa. Omukozi omwooro ogwo orikutuura aha meza yaabaza abaguzi kandi n'obumanzi akatworeka akatooro, akajumu, engaito, n'okwetooroba kuraingi n'okuseeka n'obucureezi obw'obutari bwa buzima, nahukanira ekintu ekirikukiraaho ahabw'okuba ateeina, munonga, ateeina obwooro, ateeina ebiro bye eby'omu maisho ebirikuhisa, ateeina obukuru, n'ebindi.

OKUHUKAANA nikurugamu ebintu birikwera. OKUHUKAANA kwiine omuisho gw'omuntu omweru n'omushaiitani, omuisho gw'omushaija n'omukazi, omuisho gw'omunywani n'ogw'omutari munywani, omuisho gw'omuntu omurungi n'omwija.

Okuhukaana kwo kw'ori kukunda kushwerwa, kandi n'omukuru orikuzaarira bw'amaani ogwo orikwanga okushwera.

Okuhukaana kwo kw'ori kukunda munonga kuba "OMUNTU OMUKURU", "KWERANGIRIRA", "KWEMEREERA" kandi okuhukaana kwo kw'ori kuba ANACORETA, atarikwenda kintu kyona ekiri omu nsi, ahabw'okuba okuhukaana kwe kweenka ni kwegera EIGURU, KWERURURA, n'ebindi.

Hariho OKUHUKAANA KW'OMU NSI na OKUHUKAANA KW'OMUMUTIMA. Obumwe okuhukaana nikukozesa omuisho gw'OKUTAARI KUGIRA MUGASHO n'OKWEITWARIZA.

Orikutahukanira ensi enu embi n'enjara, nahukanira endiijo, kandi orikutahukanira sente, nahukanira AMAANI G'OMUMUTIMA.

IWE, AB'OMUNDA YANGYE, AB'OMUNDA YE, nibakunda kusiiba okuhukaana, okukuteera omu muringo ogw'ekyama ogurikukiraaho omu bunte, kandi obwo nigwo oguruga guti: "Tindi kuhukanira kintu kyona", "Ninkunda abantu b'ekika kyangye", "Ndikora ntagizire mugasho ahabw'oburungi bw'abantu boona".

OMUNTU W'EBY'OBUZIBWE OMUHANGUZI kandi orikumanya byona, naajuza abantu boona ebintu ebirikukora ebiri kworeka ngu tarikugira mugasho, baitu ku arikurekyeraaho omulimo, nikitahwire kureka ihanga rye arikutwaara amamiriyooni g'amadoola.

Okuhukaana okurikusibwe n'OMU-ISHOMBO GW'OKUTAARI KUGIRA MUGASHO, nikikunda kushobeza abantu abakumanya amagezi.

Hariho abantu abairaingi omu nsi abarikuhukanira kuteeba OKUHUKANA.

Hariho abantu abairaingi abarikurekyeraaho amarembo goona n'ebigambo by'ensi ahabw'okuba nibahukanira okukora OKWENDA KW'ABO KW'OKWENDA KW'ABO.

Omuntu orikuhinduka orikugyenda n'amajunga arikugyenda omu hekalu kandi arikuteera ekiniga aijuza okwikiriza, tarikuhukanira kintu kyona kandi naitwaara ebintu ahabw'okuba tarikugira kintu kyona ahabw'omuntu weena, baitu kirahwereire ngu nahukanira EKICUUKU, okukira, obwomeezi ahabw'omunda ye nari aha mweshija weena, nari oburokore obutahwaho.

Nitwetomboita abashaija n'abakazi abari ab'edini buzima, baitu nitwebembera ngu tibarikukunda edini yaabo n'OKUTAARI KUGIRA MUGASHO.

Edini enyera, ebika ebirungi, ebiragiro, ebitebeekaniso by'omumutima, n'ebindi. Nibasaanira OKUKUNDA KWITU OKUTAARI KUGIRA MUGASHO.

Nikibaire ekikuru munonga kusanga omu nsi muno omuntu weena orikukunda edini ye, esomero rye, ekika kye, n'ebindi atagizire mugasho. Ekyo nikitwebembera.

Ensi yoona eijuza okuhukana. Hitler akatandika orutaro ahabw'okuhukana.

Orutaro rwoona ruruga omu kutinya n'OKUHUKANA. Obuzibu oburikukiraaho omu magara buruga omu OKUHUKANA.

Ensi yoona etuura omu rutaro rw'ensi yoona ahabw'okuhukana, abamwe baine abandi kandi boona baine boona.

Omuntu weena omu magara NAHUKANIRA KUBA EKINTU kandi abantu abakuru, abeesomesa, abazaire, abahereza, n'ebindi. nibahiga abaana bato, abasheija, abashiki, abato, n'ebindi kugyenda omu muringo ogw'enyima ogw'OKUHUKANA.

Abakuru nibagamba abanywani n'abanywani, otine kuba ekintu omu magara, okugirira, kushwera abantu abagumire, okugira amaani, n'ebindi.

Abantu abakuru ab'enyima, ababi, ab'obuheru, nibakunda ngu abantu abashasha nabo babe ab'okuhukana, ababi, n'ab'enyima nkabo.

Ekirikukiraaho omu byona, ni ngu abantu abashasha nibarekeraho "KUHERERWA" kandi nibarekeraaho kutwaazibwa omu muringo ogwo ogubi ogw'OKUHUKANA.

Abeesomesa basaanire kwegesa ABANYWANI n'ABANYWANI ngu omulimo gwona ogw'ahaiguru tigusaanira kwebemberwa, nikiba kitahwereire kwebemberwa omushoferi w'etekisi, omukozi w'aha meza, omurimi, omwoza w'engaito, n'ebindi.

Omulimo gwona ogucureezi murungi. Omulimo gwona ogucureezi gusaana omu magara g'abantu.

Titwazaairwe twona kuba bamakanika, bagavana, bapulezidenti, badookita, baaloya, n'ebindi.

Omu kwetomboita kw'abantu ebintu byoona nibibaasa kuba, ebitebeekaniso byoona, omulimo gwona ogw'ahaiguru tigusaana kwebemberwa.

Omu magara g'okukora, omuntu weena akuza ahabw'ekintu kandi ekikuru ni okumanya eky'omuntu weena akuza.

Ni omurimo gw'ABESOMESA okucwamu OKWETWA kw'omwezi kandi okumuhereza omu muringo ogwo.

Ogwo orikukora omu magara okukurata OKWETWA kwe, arakora n'OKUKUNDA KWA BUZIMA atagizire OKUHUKANA.

OKUKUNDA kusaanire kuhandiika OKUHUKANA. OKWETWA n'ekyo ekitukunda, omulimo ogwo ogutukunda kuheereza ahabw'okuba nikyo kitushemeza, ekitUKUNDA.

Omu magara g'obwa miraabu, abantu nibakora n'okutaba bakugondeerera kandi n'okuhukanira ahabw'okuba nibakora emirimo etarikukwatana n'okwetwa kwaabo.

Omu nsi, omuntu ku arikukora ekyo arikukunda, omu kwetwa kwe okwa buzima, nakikora n'OKUKUNDA ahabw'okuba nakunda okwetwa kwe, ahabw'okuba EBYO AKORA aha magara nibyo OKWETWA kwe.

Ekyo nicyo mulimo gw'abeesomesa. Okumanya okuheereza abanywani baabo n'abanywani, okucwamu ebintu byabo, okubaheereza omu muringo gw'okwetwa kwaabo okwa buzima.
