Ahabw’omulyango omukuru gw’ekiggwa kya Delfus, waarimu ekiwandiko ekikulu ekyali kiwandikirwe mu mayinja amalamu nga kigamba nti: "OTEMANYE MWENKA". Bw’otegeera obwomeeme bwo, ojja kutegeera obutonde bwonna n’abakatonda.

Eby’obwengye ebiwanvu ennyo eby’okufumiitiriza birina omusingi omukulu ennyo ogw’ekigambo kino ekitukuvu okuva eri ABAKULU ABAKADDE ABA GEREKA.

Bwe tuba twagala okussaawo omusingi ogutuufu ogw’okufumiitiriza mu mazima n’obwesimbu, kyetaagisa okutegeera obwomeeme bwaffe ku mitendera gyonna egy’obwongo.

Okussaawo omusingi ogutuufu ogw’okufumiitiriza, mu butuufu, kuba kulekera awo omululu, obunafu, okutya, obukyayi, obugayaavu bw’amaanyi ag’ekyama, okwegomba ebiruvvamu, n’ebirala, n’ebirala, n’ebirala.

Kyeyoleka bulungi eri buli omu era nga tekirina kubuusabuusa nti oluvannyuma lw’okussaawo OMUSINGI OMUKULU ogw’okufumiitiriza, obwongo busigala buteerera era nga buli mu kasirise ak’amaanyi era aka kitalo.

Okusinziira ku ndowooza entuufu, tekitera kutegeerekeka kwagala kugezesa EKITUUKIRIVU nga tetwannategeera bwomeeme bwaffe.

Kyetaagisa nnyo okutegeera mu BUJJUVU era mu bitundu byonna eby’obwongo, buli buzibu nga bujja mu bwongo, buli kwegomba, buli kijjukizo, buli kamogo ak’obwongo, n’ebirala.

Kyeyoleka bulungi nti mu kiseera ky’okufumiitiriza, obulamu bwaffe bwonna obukyamu butambula ku lukoba lw’obwongo mu musono ogutiisa, essanyu lyonna n’ennaku zaffe, ebijjukizo bingi nnyo, ebikolwa bingi ebiva mu nsi ebweru, oba mu nsi emunda, okwegomba kwa buli kika, okwagala okwa buli ngeri, obukyayi obukadde, obukyayi, n’ebirala.

Oyo ayagala okussaawo omusingi omukulu ogw’okufumiitiriza mu bwongo bwe, ateekwa okussaayo omwoyo gwe gwonna ku bintu bino ebirungi n’ebibi eby’okutegeera kwaffe n’okubitegeera mu bujjuvu si ku mutendera gwokka ogw’okumanya, wabula ne mu bitundu byonna ebikwekeddwa, ebitannategeerekeka n’ebitaliiwo mu bwongo. Tetuteekwa kwerabira nti obwongo bulina emitendera mingi.

Okwekenneenya ebintu bino byonna mu bujjuvu kitegeeza mu butuufu okumanya obwomeeme bwo.

Filimu yonna ku lukoba lw’obwongo erina entandikwa n’enkomerero. Ekitundu kyonna bwe kimala okuggwaawo, okwegomba, okwagala, okwegomba, ebijjukizo, n’ebirala, obwongo busigala buteerera era nga buli mu kasirise akanene NTAJJUVU ku ndowooza zonna.

Abayizi ab’ennaku zino ab’eby’obwongo beetaaga okugezesa OBUJJUVU OBUMULISA. Okuyingira kw’OBUJJUVU mu bwongo bwaffe kutusobozesa okugezesa, okuwulira, okubeera mu kintu ekikyusa, EKINTU ekyo kye KITUUKIRIVU.

Yawula wakati w’obwongo obuteereera n’obwongo obukkakkanyiziddwa mu bukambwe.

Yawula wakati w’obwongo obuli mu kasirise n’obwongo obasirisiddwa mu bujjawa.

Okusinziira ku ndowooza yonna etuufu, tuteekwa okutegeera nti obwongo bwe buba bukkakkanyiziddwa mu bukambwe, mu nsi munda ne ku mitendera emirala tebuba buteerera era bulwanirira okweggyawo.

Okusinziira ku ndowooza eyeetegereza, tuteekwa okutegeera nti obwongo bwe buba busirisiddwa mu bujjawa, mu nsi munda tebuba mu kasirise, bwekakasa era beegayirira mu ngeri ey’entiisa.

Okuteerera n’okusiima obutonde obw’obwongo obwa bulijjo, bitujjira ng’ekisa, ng’essanyu, ng’ekitundu eky’amaanyi ennyo eky’obulamu bwaffe ku lukoba olw’ekitalo olw’amagezi bwe kimala okuggwaawo.

Bw’obwongo buba buteerera era nga bwe bulijjo, bw’obwongo buba mu kasirise akasanyusa, okuyingira kw’OBUJJUVU OBUMULISA kujja.

OBUJJUVU tebwangu kukunnyonnyola. Tebusobola kunnyonnyolwa oba okugereka, endowooza yonna gye tuwaayo ku bwo esobola okulemererwa mu nsonga enkulu.

OBUJJUVU tebusobola kugereekebwa oba okwogerwa mu bigambo. Kino kiva ku kuba nti olulimi lw’abantu lukolebwa nnyo okugera ebintu, endowooza n’ennyimirira eziriwo; tekituukana okwogera mu ngeri entegeerekeka era ennamu, ebinnyonnyozo, ebintu n’ennyimirira EZITALIIWO.

Okugezaako okukubaganya ebirowoozo ku BUJJUVU mu nsalo z’olulimi olutali lujjuvu olukomekkerezebwa engeri z’obulamu, mu butuufu, kiba kisirusiru era kikyamu ddala.

"OBUJJUVU BWE BUTEERIWO, ERA OBULAMU SI BUJJUVU".

"ENGERI TEYAWUKANA N'OBUJJUVU, ERA OBUJJUVU TEBWUKANA N'ENGERI".

"ENGERI BWE BUJJUVU ERA OBUJJUVU BWE NGERI, KIVA KU BUJJUVU EBINTU OKUBAAWO".

"OBUJJUVU N'OBULAMU BYEZIMBA ERA TEBYEKAAYANA". OBUJJUVU N'OBULAMU BYEGATTIRA ERA BYEBEBAATIRA.

"ABANTU ABA WABWE BWE BALABA EKINTU, BALABA ENGERI YAAKYO EY'OBULAMU YOKA, TEBALABA NGERI YAAKYO EY'OBUJJUVU".

"Buli MUNTU AJJULUMIDDWA asobola okulaba engeri y'obulamu era n'OBUJJUVU obw'ekintu kyonna mu kiseera kye kimu.

"OBUJJUVU kigambo kyokka ekigera endwadde ETAJJUVU era etali YA BWOMEEME ey'ebintu, era akabonero akalaga embeera ey'okweyambulula n'eddembe".

Abasomesa n'abakulembeze b'amasomero, amakolegi n'amatendekero bateekwa okusoma mu bujjuvu eby'obwongo byaffe eby'enkyukakyuka ate oluvannyuma basomesenga abayizi baabwe ekkubo eritwala ku kugezesa EKITUUKIRIVU

Kisoboka okutuuka ku KUGESESA EKITUUKIRIVU ng'endowooza emaze okuggwaawo.

Okuyingira kw'OBUJJUVU kutusobozesa okugezesa OMUSANA OMULONGOVU ogw'ENGERI ENTUUFU.

Okumanya okwo OKULIIWO mu butuufu OBUJJUVU, obutalina mbeera era obutalina langi, OBUJJUVU BW'OBUTONDE, bwe BULUNGI obw'ensi yonna.

AMAGEZI GO agali mu butuufu OBUJJUVU obutalina kutunuulirwa ng'OBUJJUVU obw'OBUTALIYO wabula ng'AMAGEZI KENNYINI awatali kutangirirwa, agalabika, ag'ensi yonna era agasanyusa bwe BWEMEEME, BUDDHA ow'amagezi ow'ensi yonna.

BWEMEEME BWO obwa OBUJJUVU n'AMAGEZI amalangalanga era agasanyusa tebyawukana. OKUGATTIBWA KWABWE kwe DHARMA-KAYA; EMBAALE EY'OKUJJULUMIRWA OKUTUUFU.

BWEMEEME BWO obulangalanga, obw'OBUJJUVU era obuteeyawula ku MUBIRI omunene OGW'OBUNENE, tebulina KUZAA LWA na KUFFA era bwe musana ogutakyuka AMITARA BUDDHA.

Okumanya kuno kumala. Okutegeera OBUJJUVU bw'AMAGEZI GO ng'EMBAALE ya BUDDHA era okukitwala ng'BWEMEEME BWO, kwe kukomekkereza mu MWOYO OW'EKITONDA owa BUDDHA.

Kuumira ENDOWOOZA yo nga tetaggudde mu KUFUNIITIRIZA, werabire nti oli mu Kufumiitiriza, tolowoozanga nti ofumiitiriza kubanga bwe olowooza nti ofumiitiriza, endowooza eyo emaala okutaataaganya okufumiitiriza. Obwongo BWO buteekwa okuba OBUJJUVU okugezesa EKITUUKIRIVU.
