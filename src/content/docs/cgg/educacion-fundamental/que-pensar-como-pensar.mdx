Omu maka gaitu n'ahaishomero, abazaire n'abegyesa butoosha nibatugambira eki tushemereire kuteekateeka, kwonka tiharikitwegyesa OMUNGAHI tuteekateeka.

Okumanya eki tuteekateeka nikyorobi munonga. Abazaire baitu, abegyesa, abari kutwegyesa, abahandiiki b'ebitabo, n'ebindi n'ebindi, buli omwe n'omutegeeki omu muringo gwe, buli omwe nayenda ngu tuteekateeke ebimwehire, ebikorwa bye, emisingi ye, okucweera abantu orubanja, n'ebindi.

Abategeeki b'obwengye nibabundabunda nk'omutango. Hariho oburyo bubi obw'okuhuura obwengye bw'abandi, okubusingira, okubuhata kukora omu biragiro ebimwe, okucweera abantu orubanja, amashomero, n'ebindi.

Abaheereza n'abaheereza ba BATEGEEKI b'obwengye tibarikukunda kusiima obugabe bw'obwengye bwa muntu weena. Omuntu yaaba atakuteekateeka nkabo, nateerwa aha rubanja nk'omuntu mubi, omuhingu, omutaamanya, n'ebindi.

Buli omwe nayenda kuhuura buli omwe, buli omwe nayenda kutooragana n'obugabe bw'obwengye bw'abandi. Tiho ogurikukunda kusiima obugabe bw'okuteekateeka kw'abandi. Buli omwe naihurira aine oburyo, omunyakibiina, omujule, kandi nayenda nk'omuntu ow'obutonde ngu abandi babe nk'e, ngu bamugire nk'ekiteengo kyabo, ngu bateekateeke nk'e.

Obwengye nibuseeburwa munonga. Muhurikize ABACUURUZI, n'ebicweka byabo omu mweshuro, rediyo, terevizoni, n'ebindi n'ebindi.

Ebicweka by'obucuuruzi nibikorwa omu muringo gw'obutegeeki! Guura esabuni egi! Engato ezi! Eby'amaani bingahi! Doola zingahi! Guura hati ahonaaho! Hatariho kuzaaririza! Otareeka kusheesha! Nitushemereire kuba hati ahonaaho! N'ebindi. Nibaguruka bagambe ngu waaba otarikukorera haihi na hati, nitukuta omu kigo, nari tukwite.

Taata nayenda kuta omwana we ebiteekateeko bye aha maani kandi omwegyesa w'ahaishomero nareebesa, ahabura kandi ataho obumanzi oburikureeba ku mwojo nari omwishiki atarikusiima EBITEEKATEEKO by'omwegyesa.

Obucweka bw'abantu nibwenda kuhuura obwengye bw'obucweka obundi bw'abantu. Okukora ekyo okw'okuhuura obwengye bw'abandi nikureebeka butungi twega orupapura orwiragire rw'ebyafaayo biragire.

Omu busingye byoona haroho kandi haroho EBITEGEEKI BIRIKUTEEKATEEKA okuhuura amahanga. Ebitegeeki birikutweekateeka eki abantu bashemereire kuteekateeka. Omugisha guhwere! ogwo orikugeza kuteekateeka omu bugabe: ogwo nagenda aha butare bw'obutegeeki, Siberia, aha kigo, aha mirimo y'okutegyera, aha mugeera, aha kwita, aha kubinga, n'ebindi.

N'ABEGYESA n'ABEGYESA, nari ABAZAIRE B'AMAKA, nari ebitabo, tibarikwenda kwegyesa OMUNGAHI OKUTEEKATEEKA.

Abantu nibakunda kuhata abandi kuteekateeka nk'oku barikwikiriza ngu nikishemereire kuba kiri kandi nikirikureeba ku buli omwe omu kyo n'OMUTEGEEKI omu muringo gwe, buli omwe nayekiriiza eizire ery'aha muheru, buli omwe nayikiriza eizire ngu abandi boona bashemereire kuteekateeka nk'e, ahabw'okuba e n'ekirungi kusinga.

Abazaire b'amaka, abegyesa, abanyamabara, n'ebindi n'ebindi, nibareebesa kandi nibagaruka kureebesa abari ahansi yaabo.

Nikiteeramuruka okukora okwo okubiiha okw'abantu okuhweraho abandi ekitinisa, okutooragana n'obwengye bw'abandi, okw'okuta aha kigo, okugara, okuhuura, okusiba okuteekateeka kw'abandi.

Omuhwezi nayenda kuta omukazi we ebiteekateeko bye omu mutwe kandi aha maani, enyegyesa ye, ebiteekateeko bye, n'ebindi kandi omukazi nayenda kukora ekimwehire.

Emirundi mingi abahwezi n'abakazi nibashaarana ahabw'okuba tibarikukwatanisa ebiteekateeko.

Abahwezi tibarikukunda kwetegyereza obuhangwa bw'okusiima obugabe bw'obwengye bw'abandi.

Tiho muhwezi orikugira obushoboorozi bw'okuhuura obwengye bw'omuhwezi ondiijo. Buli omwe eky'amazima ashemereire kutinisa. Buli omwe aine obushoboorozi bw'okuteekateeka nk'oku ayenda, okuhereza edini ye, okuba omu kibiina kya politiki eki ayenda.

Abaana abato n'abashiki b'ahaishomero nibahata kuteekateeka aha maani omu biteekateeko nk'ebyo kwonka tibegyesibwa kucweera obwengye.

Obwengye bw'abaana buba butoono, burakwata, burarungama kandi obw'abakuru bwona buraguma, burazirika, nk'endambi omu mwesherezo, tiburikuhinduka, tiburikubaasa kuhinduka.

Obwengye bw'abaana abato n'abato burakweemerera ebihinduka bingi, burakubaasa kuhinduka.

Abaana n'abato nibabaasa kwegyesibwa OMUNGAHI OKUTEEKATEEKA. Abaheereza nikigumire munonga kubegyesa OMUNGAHI OKUTEEKATEEKA ahabw'okuba boona bahindukire nk'oku bari kandi nikwo barikufeerwa.

Nikiteera okushanga omu magara omukuru weena orikwenda kuhindura munonga.

Obwengye bw'abantu nibwetoorongyeka kuruga obu bato. Ekyo nikyo abazaire n'abegyesa b'ahaishomero barikukunda kukora. Nibashemeerwa kuta obwengye bw'abaana abato n'abato.

Obwengye obutairwe omu mwesherezo nikyo mazima obwengye oburikugira obushoboorozi, obwengye obuhuurire.

Nikishemereire ngu ABEGYESA n'ABEGYESA b'ahaishomero bamanure engoogo y'obwengye.

Nikihweera ngu abegyesa bamanya kuragiira obwengye bw'abaana kuza omu bugabe bw'amazima ngu batareke kuhuurwa bwindi.

Nikihweera ngu abegyesa begyese abegyesibwa OMUNGAHI NIMUSHANGAIRE KUTEEKATEEKA.

Abegyesa bashemereire kwetegyereza obuhangwa bw'okwegyesa abegyesibwa omugisha gw'okucweera, okuteekateeka, okwetegyereza.

Tiho muntu weena orikwetegyereza orikugira ngu yakiriize aha muringo gw'okwetegyereza eki kyona. Nikihweera okubanza kucondooza. Kwetegyereza, kubuuza, atashemerire kukiriiza.

Omu magambo gondi nitugira ngu tiho buhangwa bw'okukiriiza, kwonka okukonda, kucweera, kuteekateeka n'okwetegyereza. Obwetegyerezi obu bwahendera, okukiriiza tikirikwetagisa.

Tiho murimo gw'okujuuza omutwe gwitu n'amahangaazi g'obwengye twaruga ahaishomero TUTARIKUMANYA KUTEEKATEEKA kandi nitugumizamu nk'ABANTU ABARIKUTAMBULA ABATARINGA, nk'emashini, kugarukamu omurimo gumwe gw'abazaire baitu, abakuru baitu n'abakuru b'abakuru, n'ebindi.

Butoosha kugarukamu ekimwehire, kutuura amagara g'emashini, kuruga omu maka okwija aha ofiisi n'okuruga aha ofiisi okwija omu maka, okushwerwa okuba emashini y'okukora abaana, ekyo tikintu kya kutuura kandi twega ahabw'ekyo, kandi twenda kuza ahaishomero n'aha koleegi n'aha yunivasite omu myaka ikumi nari ikumi na etaano, nikirungi tutarega.

MAHATMA GHANDI akaba ari omuntu orikushobora munonga. Emirundi mingi abashumba abarikweemeera bakagumire aha muryango gwe ebiro n'ebiro barikurwanirira kumuhindura omu Bukristayo omu muringo gwabo gw'okweemeera.

Ghandi takakiriize okwegyesa kw'abashumba, kandi tarakareebese, AKAMWETEGEREZA, AKAMUSIIMA, kandi nikwo kwagira.

Emirundi mingi MAHATMA akagira ati: "Ndi Muburahami, Omuyahudi, Omukristayo, Omuhamedi, n'ebindi n'ebindi.

MAHATMA aketegyereza ngu edini zoona nzikwetagisa ahabw'okuba zoona nizirikumariza OBUHANGWA OBUTARIKUHWAHO bumwe.

Ekyo ky'okukiriiza nari kureebesa enyegyesa nari ekitongore, nikireebeka kuhwereerwa obukuru bw'omutwe.

Obu tureebesa nari tukakiriiza ekintu, n'ahabw'okuba tutakietegyereize.

Aho haroho KWETEGEREZA okukiriiza nari okureebesa nirugwamu.

Obwengye oburikukiriza, obwengye obutarikukiriza, obwengye oburikukizibuura, obwengye ni butaamanya.

Omugisha gw'OBULEMI ti gw'OKUKIRIZA nari okutarikukiriza nari OKUKIZIBUURA.

Omugisha gw'OBULEMI nigw'OKUBUUZA, kucweera, kuteekateeka n'OKUHUKUMANA.

AMAZIMA nigo amarangaazi aha kasumi n'akasumi. Amazima tihariho ky'okukora n'eki omuntu arikwikiriza nari areke kukiriza, nari okusheesha amaisho.

AMAZIMA ti kibuuzo ky'okukiriiza ekintu nari okukireebesa. AMAZIMA n'ekibuuzo ky'OKUKORAKO, KUTUURAKO, KWETEGEREZA.

Omurimo gwona gw'ABEGYESA gushemereire omu muheru kwija aha abegyesibwa aha KUHUKUMANA kw'ekintu ky'amazima, eky'amazima.

Nikishemereire EKIKURU ngu ABEGYESA n'ABEGYESA bareke okukora okwo kw'enyima kandi okubiiha oburikuragirira obutoosha OKWEHANGYISA obwengye oburikugira obushoboorozi kandi oburikweshereza bw'abaana.

Nikihabisa ngu abantu ABARIKUKURA bajuzire okucweera abantu orubanja, obugingo, okuhimba ebintu by'enyima, n'ebindi bitooragane otyo n'obwengye bw'abaana n'abato, nibagerageza kwebembera obwengye kukurikira ebiteekateeko byabo ebishiisha, ebitagumiire, eby'enyima.

Nikirungi kusiima OBUTUUNGUZI BW'OBWENGYE bw'ABEGYESIBWA, kusiima oburahuku bwabo bw'omutwe, okukora kwabo okw'amagara.

Abegyesa n'abegyesa tibarikugira obushoboorozi bw'okuta aha kigo obwengye bw'abegyesibwa.

Eky'omutaano tikukweekateeka AHA BWENGYE bw'abegyesibwa eki bashemereire kuteekateeka, kwonka okubegyesa omu muringo ogujwire, OMUNGAHI OKUTEEKATEEKA.

OBWENGYE nigo murimo gw'OBUMANYI kandi nikyetagisa ngu ABEGYESA n'ABEGYESA begyese abegyesibwa baabo kucweera ekyo omurimo omu bwegendereza.
