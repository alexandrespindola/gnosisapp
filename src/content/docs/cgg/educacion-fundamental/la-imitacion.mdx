Tikurora kityo kimanyirwe kimwe ngu obwoba niburemesa omuntu kukora ebintu omu bwesigye. Embeera embi y’eby’obushubuzi erikukwata abantu miriyooni, teirikubaasa kuteeberezibwa, neeterwa obwoba.

Omwana orikuba orikutinya ashaba nyina omukundwa kandi amwegamaho arikusherura obusingye. Omushaija orikutinya ayegamira omukazi we kandi arikumuramya munonga. Omukazi orikutinya ashaba iba n’abaana be kandi arikubakunda munonga.

Kurugiirira aha biteekateeko by’omuntu, nikitangaaza munonga kumanya ngu obwoba obumwe na bumwe nibubisiikwa omu mwenda gw’OKUGONDA.

Abantu abarikuba batarikwetenga MIHEMBO EYA ROOHO, abantu abari abanafu munda, buri obwiire bashaba kuruga hanu kwijuzibwa.

Abantu abari abanafu munda, nibagumaho, buri obwiire nibashusha, buri obwiire nibakora obutaari bwa mugasho, okuteera enyeegyere, okushemererwa kw’ebihamba, n’ebindi.

Abantu abari abanafu munda nibaguma nibatiina kandi nk’oku kiriyo, nibegamira iba, omukazi, abazaire, abaana, ebiraganiso by’aha ira ebirikukoresibwa kandi ebirikutaagurwa, n’ebindi.

Omukuru weena orikurwaire kandi omworo omu BY’OKUTEEKATEEKA obumwe ajwaire obwoba kandi arikutiina n’omutima omwingi amagufa, ebiraganiso by’omu ka, abaana b’abaana, ebimwijutsya, n’ebindi. nk’okusherura obusingye. Eki kintu eki, twena nitubaasa kukihurikiza tweshomeserezeho abakuru.

Buri obwiire abantu obu batiina nibyekubiita enyima y’engabo erikubarinda eya KITIINISA. Kuronda ebiraganiso, obundi obw’eihanga, obw’eka, eihanga, n’ebindi.

Buzima, buri kiraganiso n’okwejjura kutaari kwa mugasho, eky’omushaija, ekitaari ky’omugasho buzima.

Abantu boona baine omutindo gw’OKWEREBERA ebintu by’abandi. Ekyo ky’OKWEREBERA kibaho ahabw’OBWOBA.

Abantu abaine obwoba niberebera boona abu begamira. Niberebera iba, omukazi, abaana, ab’eishe-emwe, abanywani abarikubarabira, n’ebindi.

OKWEREBERA nikuruga omu OBWOHO. OKWEREBERA nikwetaagurira kimwe OKWEYAMBIISA OMU BWESIGYE.

Omu mashomero, omu matendeka, omu yunivasiti, abeesomesa nibakora enshobe y’okwegyesa abanyeshuri abashaija n’abakazi, ekyo eki kyeetwa OKWEREBERA.

Omu masomo g’okushusha n’okubanza, abanyeshuri nibegyesebwa kukopiinga, kushusha ebishushani by’emiti, amaka, ensozi, enyamaishwa, n’ebindi. Ekyo tikikora. Ekyo nikwerekereza, KUFOTOGRAFA.

Okukora tikwerekereza. Okukora tikufotografa. Okukora n’okujuramu, okuhitsya n’obushaazi n’omuti ogurikutushemeza, okugwa kw’eizooba kurungi, okutandika kw’omusana n’ebizino byakyo ebitaarikubatwa, n’ebindi.

Harimu okukora kw’amazima omu bukuru bw’ABAINA KINA N’ABAINA JAAPAANI ABA ZEN, omu bukuru obutakaire n’Obukaire-Bukaire.

Omushushi weena orikuruga omu CHAANI n’omu ZEN tarikutwara OKWEREBERA n’okufotografa. Abashushi b’eihanga rya China n’erya Jaapaani: nibashemererwa okukora n’okugaruka okukora.

Abashushi ba ZEN n’aba CHAANI, tibarikwerekereza, nibAKORA kandi ogwo nugwo murimo gwabo.

Abashushi b’eihanga rya CHINA n’erya JAAPAANI tibarikwetenga kushusha nari okufotografa omukazi orikurungi, nibashemererwa okuhitsya oburungi bwabo obutakaire.

Abashushi b’eihanga rya CHINA n’erya JAAPAANI tibarikwija kwerekereza okugwa kw’eizooba kurungi, nibashemererwa okuhitsya omu burungi obutakaire oburungi bwona obw’okugwa kw’eizooba.

Ekikuru tikwerekereza, kukopiinga omu mweru nari omwiragura; ekikuru n’okuhurira amakuru marungi ag’oburungi n’okumanya okugahitsya, kwonka eki nikyetenga okutabaaho obwoba, okwegamira amateeka, aha biraganiso, nari okutiina eki abantu baraabe nibooreka nari okuragiirira kw’omwegyesa.

Nikyetagisa abeesomesa kwetegyereza obwetengo bw’abanyeshuri abashaija n’abakazi okukura amaani g’okukora.

Omu maisho goona, nikitaangaaza kwegyesa, abanyeshuri kwerekereza. Nikikuru kubegyesa okukora.

Ekintu ky’omuntu n’ekintu ekirikugyenda kyonka ekyebase kitarikumanya, eki kimanyire kwerekereza.

Nitwerekereza engoye z’abandi kandi kuruga omu kwerekereza nikuruga emiringo yoona y’empamuro.

Nitwerekereza emico y’abandi nangwa n’obu eba neyerabisiza.

Nitwerekereza emico emibi, nitwerekereza byona ebirikutaangaaza, eki buri obwiire kiriyo kyejjura omu bwiire, n’ebindi.

Nikyetagisa ABEEGYESA b’omu ishomero okwegyesa abanyeshuri okuteekateeka enyima omumaisho omu bwesigye.

Abeesomesa baine kureetera abanyeshuri obusobozi bwona okureka kuba EBINTU EBIRIKUGYENDA BYONKA EBIRIKWEREKEREZA.

Abeesomesa baine kwanguhya abanyeshuri oburyo burungi kubabasa kukura amaani g’okukora.

Nikyetagisa abanyeshuri kumanya obusingye bw’amazima, kuba batatiina kubasa kwega okuteekateeka enyima omumaisho, omu bwesigye.

Omutwe ogurikuguma omu buhuuku bw’EKI ABANTU BARAABE NIBOOReeka, omutwe ogurikwerekereza, ahabw’okutiina kutaagura ebiraganiso, amateeka, emico, n’ebindi. Tiguri mutwe ogurikukora, tiguri mutwe gw’obusingye.

Omutwe gw’abantu nigukuratira amaka agari agasibe kandi agasiirwe n’obusiisi mushanju, amaka ahu harimu ekintu kisya ekirikubaasa kubaho, amaka ahu omusana gutarikwingira, amaka ahu obufu n’obusaasi nibitegyeka.

Ekintu EKISYA nikikuru kubaho ahu hatariyo bwoba, ahu hatariyo OKWEREBERA, ahu hatariyo kwegamira ebintu, amafuta, abantu, ebiraganiso, emico, n’ebindi.

Abantu nibaguma omu buhuuku bw’okushusha, obugugu, emico y’eka, emikora, okwetenga kutahumura okw’okutunga ebifo, okushangara, okwemerera, okwiha aha mutwe gw’edalaari, okwehurira, n’ebindi.

Nikyetagisa ABEEGYESA okwegyesa abanyeshuri baabo abashaija n’abakazi, obwetengo bw’okutwerekereza omurongo ogu goona ogurikukoresibwa n’ogurikutaagurwa gw’ebintu by’okukura.

Nikyetagisa ABANYESHURI kwega omu ishomero okukora omu bwesigye okuteekateeka omu bwesigye, okuhurira omu bwesigye.

Abanyeshuri abashaija n’abakazi nibakoresa obwiire bwabo oburungi omu ishomero nibatunga AMAKURU kandi omu by’obuhangwa tibeine bwiire bw’okuteekateeka aha bintu ebi byoona.

Emyaka ikumi nari ikumi na etaano omu ishomero nibagumaho omu bwomi bw’ebintu ebirikugyenda bitarikumanya kandi nibaruga omu ishomero n’obwengye obwesiire, kwonka nibaruga omu ishomero nibarikwikiririza ngu nibazindwire munonga.

Omutwe gw’ekintu ky’omuntu niguguma niguteekateeka hagati y’ebiteekateeko ebirikukora n’ebirikugarukamu.

Ekintu ky’omuntu tikirikubaasa kuteekateeka n’obusingye bw’amazima ahabw’okuba ijuura OBWOBA.

Ekintu ky’omuntu kitiina obwomeezi, kitiina obufa, kitiina eki abantu baraabe nibooreka, eky’okugamba ngu nikigambwa, okuteera enyeegyere, okubuza omurimo, okutaagura amateeka, okugira omuntu akutwaare omweene nari akwibe omweene, n’ebindi., n’ebindi., n’ebindi.

Omu ishomero nitwegyesebwa OKWEREBERA kandi nituruga omu ishomero twahinduka ABEREBERA.

Tituriyo TURI KUKORA OMU BWESIGYE ahabw’okuba kuruga aha ntebe z’eshomero nitwegyesebwa OKWEREBERA.

Abantu niberebera ahabw’obwoba aha eki abantu abandi baraabe nibooreka, abanyeshuri abashaija n’abakazi niberebera ahabw’okuba ABEEGYESA buzima nibatiinisa abanyeshuri abagafu, nibatiinisa obwiire bwona, nibatiinisa okujumirira, nibatiinisa ebihano ebimwe, nibatiinisa okugoba, n’ebindi.

Buzima twetenga kugaruka kukora omu makuru g’obujumbu bwona obw’ekigambo, twine kwemanyisa omurongo ogwo gwona gw’OKWEREBERA ogurikuganyiriza kututeegyeka.

Obu turyaba nitubasa kumanya omurongo gwona gw’OKWEREBERA, obu turyaba tweshomesereza buli kimwe omu OKWEREBERA, nitwemanyisa na byo kandi nk’eky’okugarukamu eky’obwomeezi, obwo nibwo tubaaza omuriitwe omu mutindo oguri omushoboora, amaani g’okukora.

Nikyetagisa abanyeshuri abashaija n’abakazi ab’omu ishomero, ekoreegi nari yunivasiti, kwihaho OKWEREBERA kwona kuba bahinduka abakora buzima.

Abeesomesa nibashoberwa abarikuteebereza kubi ngu abanyeshuri abashaija n’abakazi nibetenga OKWEREBERA okwega. Ogwo orikwerekereza tareega, ogwo orikwerekereza ahinduka EKINTU EKIRIKUGYENDA kandi ekyo nikyo kyona.

Tikwerekereza eki abahandiiki b’eby’enshonga z’obuhangwa, eby’embirizo, okubara, ebyafaayo, n’ebindi. OKWEREBERA, OKUJUURA, okwejjura nk’embooko nari enkoko, nikishemeerera, nikikuru KUMANYA N’OBWENGYE eki turikwega.

ENJEGYESA YA MUHENDO n’ENSAAYENSI Y’OBWENGYE, ensaayensi erikuturikuramu okuburaana kwaitu n’ebintu by’omuntu, n’obutonde bw’ensi, n’ebintu byona.

Omutwe ogwo gumanyire kwerekereza n’OBWENGYE, mashini erikukora, TITIRI KUKORA, tikirikubaasa kukora, tikirikuteekateeka buzima, kureka kugarukamu kandi ekyo nikyo kyona.

Abeesomesa baine kwetegyereza ahabw’okuzinduka kw’OBWENGYE omu mwegi buri omwe.

Abanyeshuri abashaija n’abakazi nibetegyereza okumara omwaka kandi enyima... hahandi y’ishomero, omu bwomeezi oburiyo bukolebwa, bahinduka abakozi b’omu ofiisi nari obumashini bw’okukora abaana.

Emyaka ikumi nari ikumi na etaano ey’okwesomesa okuruga omu bukarani bw’okugamba n’okugyenda, ebintu ebyesomesebwa nibyebwa kakeekare, kureka aha muheru tikihikire omu kwijukya.

Abanyeshuri obu bakora OBWENGYE bw’ebintu ebyesomesebwa, obu okwesomesa kwabo kukuratira kimwe aha AMAKURU, OKWEREBERA n’OKUJUURA, akagwiro kandi kabataagura. Barugaruga omu ishomero n’obumanyi OBURIKUMANYA, EBITARIKUBOORA, EBIRIKUJUZIBWA, ebitarikubaasa kwemerera aha KWIJUKYA OKUTARIKWESIGA.

ENJEGYESA EY’AMAANI eja kwangusha abanyeshuri kubazindura OBWENGYE n’OBWENGYERERO.

ENJEGYESA EY’AMAANI etwara abato aha muhanda gw’ENDAARO Y’AMAZIMA.

Abanyeshuri abashaija n’abakazi baine kuteeragana ngu ABEEGYESA babaha ENJEGYESA Y’AMAZIMA, ENJEGYESA EY’AMAANI.

Tikikumara abanyeshuri abashaija n’abakazi kwemerera aha ntebe z’eshomero okutunga amakuru kuruga omu mugaabe nari aha rugamba, nikyetenga ekintu ekindi, nikyetenga ENJEGYESA EY’AMAANI kuzindura OBWENGYE.

N’OKWIHUKYA abanyeshuri kuruga omu ishomero abakuzire, ABAMANYI buzima, ABATEEKATEEKA, ngu batabaasa kuhinduka ebihingye by’obugyenda obw’abantu.
