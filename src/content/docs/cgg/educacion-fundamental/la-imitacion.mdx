Nikyo nikimanyisibwa kimwe ngu obwoba nibuzibira omuntu kutandika emirimo omu muringo ogurikwetenga. Obutungi obubi oburikukwata abantu obukakafu, nibutandikwa obwoba.

Omwana orikugira obwoba ashaba nyina kandi yamwegamaho arikusherura obuteeka. Omusaija orikugira obwoba yamwegamaho omukazi we kandi naagira ngu naamukunda munonga. Omukazi orikugira obwoba ashaba iba n’abaana be kandi naagira ngu nabaakunda munonga.

Kurugiirira omu biteekateeko by’omuntu nikikuru kandi kikusetura kumanya ngu obwoba obumwe nabweza kwegyerera omu ngoye z’OKUKUNDA.

Abantu abatairemu emigasho mingi omu by’OMWOYO, abantu abakeni omu mutima, buliijo nibasherura hanu aha rubaju kwejuzya.

Abantu abakeni omu mutima, batuura omu kwetomboita, omu by’obutaho, omu bigambo by’obushambani, eby’okweshemeza ebitaaine mazima, n’ebindi.

Abantu abakeni omu mutima nibatuura omu bwoba buriijo kandi nk’oku kirikwetengyesa, nibegamaho iba, omukazi, abazaire, abaana, emihango ya ira erikuhwaire n’ebitaaine mugasho, n’ebindi.

Omuntu wena orikukuraire omubembezi kandi omukeni omu biteekateeko by’OMUNTU buliijo aijwire obwoba kandi naatiina n’amatsiko amagumiire ensimbi, emihango y’eka, abaijukuru, ebijukizo bye, n’ebindi nk’okusherura obuteeka. Eki nikintu eki, twena turikubaasa kushiisha twetegyereza abakuru.

Buli omuntu ku agira obwoba abeihura omu ngabo erikukurira y’OBUTEEKATEEKA. Kurugira aha muhaango, gw’ekika, eka, ihanga, n’ebindi.

Buzima omuhaango gwona n’okwijuka butaine mugasho gwona, gutaine mugasho gwoona.

Abantu boona baine emikono erikukurira y’OKWEYEGEREZA eby’abandi. Ekyo ky’OKWEYEGEREZA nikiva omu BW’OBA.

Abantu abarikugira obwoba nibeyegereza abantu boona abarikubegamaho. Nibeyegereza iba, omukazi, abaana, ab’eishe-emwe, abanywani abarikubakurira, n’ebindi.

OKWEYEGEREZA n’omuguruko gw’OBWOBA. OKWEYEGEREZA nikuhwerekyereza kimwe OBUHWERA BW’OKUTANDIKA.

Omu mashomero, omu bitongore, omu yunivasiti, abegyesi nibakora enshobi y’okwegyesa abanyeshuri abashaija n’abakazi, ekyo kye bayeta OKWEYEGEREZA.

Omu kuraasi y’okushushana n’okubaza nibegyese abanyeshuri kucopa, kubaza ebishushani by’emiti, enju, ensozi, enyamaishwa, n’ebindi. Ekyo tikureeta. Ekyo n’OKWEYEGEREZA, OKUTOORA EFOTO.

Kureeta t’OKWEYEGEREZA. Kureeta t’OKUTOORA EFOTO. Kureeta nikushoboorora, kuhereza n’akajwaro n’omuti ogurikutushemeza, obusingye oburungi bw’eizooba, obusheeshe n’obuhame bwaabyo obutari bwa buzima, n’ebindi.

Harimu okureeta kw’amazima omu by’obuganga by’Abachiina na JAPON ab’ENZIKU, omu by’obuganga oburikujumira n’oburikukirayo.

Omubazi weena omu bachiina ab’ECHAN n’ab’ENZIKU tibarikwetenga KWEYEGEREZA, kutoora efoto. Ababazi ba China na JAPON: nibashemerererwa kureeta kandi nibagaruka kureeta obundi.

Ababazi b’ENZIKU na CHAN, tibeyegereza, BAREETA kandi ogwo nigwo omurimo gwabo.

Ababazi ba CHINA na JAPON tibarikwetenga kubaza nari kutoora efoto y’omukazi murungi, nibashemerererwa kuhereza oburungi bwabo oburikujumira.

Ababazi ba CHINA na JAPON tibari kwegereza buzima obusingye oburungi bw’eizooba, nibashemerererwa kuhereza omu burungi oburikujumira oburungi bwona obw’akasheeshe.

Ekikuru t’OKWEYEGEREZA, kucopa omu mwiru nari omu kweeru; ekikuru n’okuhurira amakuru g’amaani ag’oburungi kandi okumanya okuguhereza, baitu eki nikyetenga ngu hatabemu bwoba, kwegamaho amateeka, omuhaango, nari obwoba bw’eki abantu baraagambaho nari okukangwa kw’omwegyesi.

Nikikuru MUNONGA ngu abegyesi bategeere obwetengo bw’abanyeshuri okukuraaza amaani g’okureeta.

Aha maisho goona nikishusha obushema kwegyesa, abanyeshuri OKWEYEGEREZA. Nikirungi kubegyesa kureeta.

Omuntu obubiire omukazi, orikwenda kwezirika orikwejumba, orikumanya OKWEYEGEREZA.

Nitwegereza engoye z’abandi kandi kuruga omu kwegereza okwo kuruga enshenga ezitari zimwe z’omuringo.

Nitwegereza emihango y’abandi n’obu gira ngu ekanganisibwe munonga.

Nitwegereza emico mibi, nitwegereza byona ebirikushusha obushema, eki buliijo kirikutuura nikyegaanwa omu bwire, n’ebindi.

Nikyetengyesa ngu ABEGYESI b’eshomero begyese abanyeshuri kuteekateeka abeeka omu muringo ogutegyendetse.

Abegyesi bashemereire kuha abanyeshuri obusinge bwona kuleka kuba ABAAJA abarikwega.

Abegyesi bashemereire kwanguhya abanyeshuri omugisha murungi munonga ngu bakure amaani g’okureeta.

Nikikuru MUNONGA ngu abanyeshuri bamanya obusinge bw’amazima, ngu okutagira obwoba bwoona babanze kwega kuteekateeka abeeka, omu businge.

Omutwe ogutuuraho orikwenda eki abantu baraagambaho, omutwe ogurikwega, ahabw’obwoba bw’okuhabukya emihango, amateeka, emihingo, n’ebindi. Tigumutwe ogurikureeta, tigumutwe oguri omu businge.

Omutwe gw’abantu guri nk’enju enzigwaire yoona n’ekakibwa n’akagomera mushanju, enju ahatarikubaasa kubaho ekihyaka kyona, enju ahatari kugira eizooba eririkutaaha, enju ahu obusaasi n’oburumi bwinganiza.

EKISHYAKA nikibaasa kubaaho ahu hatarimu bwoba, ahu hatarimu OKWEYEGEREZA, ahu hatarimu kwegamaho ebintu, ensimbi, abantu, emihango, emihingo, n’ebindi.

Abantu nibatuura nk’abaaja b’okwetomboita, okufiira abandi, emihingo y’eka, emicwe, okwetenga okutagira ekitengwa okwejumbira ebifo, kukyega, kwembuka aha mutwe gw’ekirambi, kwejumbuza, n’ebindi.

Nikikuru MUNONGA ngu ABEGYESI begyese abanyeshuri abashaija n’abakazi, obwetengo obw’okutegereza obuteekateeka bwona oburi omu muringo ogurikuhwaire n’ebintu by’ekishwere eby’obukuru.

Nikikuru MUNONGA ngu ABANYESHURI begye omu shomero kureeta omu businge okuteekateeka omu businge, okuhurira omu businge.

Abanyeshuri nibahita omurungi gw’amagara gaabo omu shomero nibega AMAKURU kandi kwonka tibarikushumbura obwire kuteekateeka aha bintu byona ebi.

Emyaka ikumi nari ikumi na etaano omu shomero nibatuura amagara g’okwejumba abatamanyire kandi nibaruga omu shomero n’amagara gaabo nigebere, baitu nibaruga omu shomero nibegira ngu nibazinduka munonga.

Omutwe gw’omuntu gutuuraho guteerwe omu biteekateeko ebirikukurira n’ebirikutereeza.

Omuntu tarikubaasa kuteekateeka n’obusinge bw’amazima ahabw’okugira ngu aijwire OBWOBA.

Omuntu agira OBWOBA bw’amagara, OBWOBA bw’okufa, OBWOBA bw’eki abantu baraagambaho, aha kugamba ngu kugambirwa, aha bigambo by’obushambani, okucweera omulimo, okuhabukya ebiragiro, ngu omuntu amutwaare omugo gw’omushaija nari okwiba omugo gw’omukazi, n’ebindi.

Omu shomero nitwegyesebwa OKWEYEGEREZA kandi turuga omu shomero twahinduka ABEYEGEREZA.

Titugira OBUHWERA BW’OKUTANDIKA ahabw’okugira ngu kuruga omu ntebe z’eshomero nitwegyesebwa OKWEYEGEREZA.

Abantu BAREEREZA ahabw’obwoba bw’eki abantu abandi baraagambe, abanyeshuri ABEYEGEREZA ahabw’okugira ngu ABEGYESI bagira buzima barikweterera abanyeshuri abakenye, nibateerwa obuteeka omu bwire bwona, nibateerwa obuteeka n’okugura ebiroto bibi, nibateerwa obuteeka n’ebihano ebitari bimwe, nibateerwa obuteeka n’okuginga, n’ebindi.

Kwonka nitwetenga kugaruka turikureeta omu makuru gona ag’ekigambo, tushemereire kwebanga amakuru gona ag’OKWEYEGEREZA ago agatikurimisa.

Obu turikubaasa kumanya omurongo gona ogw’OKWEYEGEREZA, obu twareebire kimwe buri KAMWE k’OKWEYEGEREZA, nitwebanga amakuru gabyo kandi nk’omuguruko gw’obuhame, orizirwe kuruga omuriiyo omu muringo ogutari ogw’amaani, amaani g’okureeta.

Nikyetengyesa ngu abanyeshuri b’eshomero, ekitongore nari yunivasiti, beihure kuruga omu KWEYEGEREZA kwona kuleka kuba abakureeta ab’amazima.

Abegyesi abarikukanganisibwa nibashobya ngu abanyeshuri nibetenga OKWEYEGEREZA kwega. Ogurikwega taagyega, ogurikwega ahinduka AJA kandi nikwo kwona.

Togyezaho OKWEYEGEREZA eki abahandiki b’ebyahandikirwe eby’okujumira, eby’omubiri, okubara, ebyafaayo, n’ebindi. OKWEYEGEREZA, KWETEGEREZA, kugaana nk’ab’enyamishwa ezirikugaana nari ab’enjuga, nikishema, nikirungi KUTEGEREZA KIMWE eki turikwega.

OBWEGYESA BW’OMUSINGI n’ENZIKU Y’AMAGARA, enziku erikuturikuhereza kumanyisa obushemezi bwaitu n’abantu, n’obutonde, n’ebintu byona.

Omutwe ogurikubaasa kwega nikintu OMUKENDE, n’ekimaaki ekirikukora, TIKIRIKUREETA, tarikubaasa kureeta, tiguriteekateeka buzima, nikigaana kwonka nikwo kwona.

Abegyesi bashemereire kukwata aha kuzinduka kw’AMAGARA omu mwana wena.

Abanyeshuri nibakwata kwonka aha kugura omwaka kandi bwanyima... hanu aha rubaju rw’eshomero, omu magara g’obukama, bahinduka abakozi b’obwamaizi nari abamaaki abarikukora abaana.

Emyaka ikumi nari ikumi na etaano y’ebyegyeso kuguruka bahinduka ab’okwejumba abarikwegaana, ebitabo ebirikwegwa nibaguruka bahinduka ebiceera haiceera kandi ahagati hatasigara kintu omu kwijuka.

Kwonka abanyeshuri bakora AMAGARA g’ebitabo ebirikwegwa, kwonka ebyo ebirikwega tibirikubaasa kwemererwa kwonka aha MAKURU, OKWEYEGEREZA n’OKWIJUKA, ogundi MWENDA baguhimbire. Bari kuruga omu shomero n’okumanya kw’AMAGARA, okutareebwa, okurikuhikaana, okutarikuteerwa OBWENEENE OBUTARI BW’AMANU.

OBWEGYESA BW’OMUSINGI burikuhwera abanyeshuri nibabehurira AMAAGARA n’OBWENGYE.

OBWEGYESA BW’OMUSINGI buhereza abato omu muhanda gw’OKUKORA EMPINDUKA KW’AMAZIMA.

Abanyeshuri bashemereire kureetera ngu ABEGYESI babahe OBWEGYESA BW’AMAZIMA, OBWEGYESA BW’OMUSINGI.

Tikirikukika ngu abanyeshuri batuure omu ntebe z’eshomero kukuuma amakuru g’omugabe nari akarwa, nikyetenga ekindi, nikyetenga OBWEGYESA BW’OMUSINGI okuzindura AMAGARA.

NIKIKURU ngu abanyeshuri baruge omu shomero bagumire, ab’AMAGARA, ab’amazima, ABENGYEREREZI, ngu batacweera haiceera bahinduka ebitabo ebikenye eby’enganda.
