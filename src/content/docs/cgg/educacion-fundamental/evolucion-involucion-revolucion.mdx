Omu bwe tukora tukasanga ngu AMAISHOMERO G'EBY'OMUBIRI n'AMAISHOMERO G'EBY'OMWOYO goona gataahire kimu omu BIYOOTOOZI BY'ENKURAKURA.

Emiiro egirikukwata aha ntandikiro y'omuntu n'ENKURAKURA ye ey'enyima, omu bunyima n'obuhemu bw'amaani, tigurikubaasa kwemera okuteekateeka kwingi.

N'obu DARWIN yaabaire aine enyegyesa ze ezirikukirira aha kwikiriza nk'ekintu ky'okwikiriza, CARLOS MARX n'ebintu bye eby'AMAANI BY'EBY'OMUBIRI, abashomesa b'omuri obu bwire tibarikumanya aha ntandikiro y'omuntu, tibarikumanya, tibarikukora omu muringo murungi kandi tibarikugira obuhame bw'amazima, oburikukwata aha NKURAKURA Y'ABANTU.

Kureka, twatwara abantu ab'omu byafaayo, okugira ngu, ab'emyaka enkumi makumi abiri nari makumi ashatu enyima ya Yesu Kristo, tusanga obuhame oburikukirira, obubonero obutahwerekereza obw'omuringo gw'omuntu oguri haihi, ogutarikwetegyereza abantu b'omuri obu bwire, kandi okubaho kwago nikubaasa kworekwa ahabw'obujurizi bwingi, Ebihandiiko by'enyima, Enyumba ndungi ez'enyima, Ebijaanwa ebirikukirira haihi, Ebihandiiko ebirikuhururuka n'ebijaanwa by'enyima ebirikuruga omu bwire oburikwingana.

Kukira aha MUNTU OW'ENYIMA, ebihangirwe eby'amaani n'ebirikuhururuka ebirikusaana n'ENYAMA EW'AMAGEZI, kandi kwonka zirikutaana, zirikushusha, zirikuhururuka kandi amagufa gaazo ag'omugisha gaterekirwe munonga omu by'enyima by'omuri obwire bw'obutiti nari oburikukuratira obwo, abashomesa b'omuri obu bwire tibarikumanya omu muringo murungi ahabw'ebirikukuratira.

EKISHOMERO KY'OBWENGYE nikwegyesa ngu ENYAMA EKUTEEKATEEKA nk'oku tugimanyi, ti MURINGO MURUNGI, toori MUNTU omu muringo ogurikwingana gw'ekigambo; obutonde nibugikura okuhika aha bunyima oburikwingana bw'okugireka etembererwa kukura nari kubura obugabe bwago bwona n'okucwekyerezebwa.

AMATEEKA G'ENKURAKURA n'AG'OKUCWEKYEREZA niyo mpimbi y'eby'obutonde byoona kandi tigarikukwata aha KWIHANGAAZA KW'OMWOYO.

Omu NYAMA EW'AMAGEZI harimu obugabe oburikuhururuka oburikubaasa kukura nari kubura, ti iteeka ngu bukure. Eby'OKUBAASA KUKURA tibirikubaasa kubikurisa.

Okukura kw'obugabe obwo obutagarukireho, nikibaasa kuba omu mbeera ezirikushushana kandi egi nikyetenga OKWEYONGYERA KW'AMAANI kw'omuntu kandi okuhwereera kurungi okurikuruga omu BAKURU abakakora omurimo ogwo omu bwire obwahwaire.

Owaayenda kukura obugabe bwe bwona obutagarukireho kuhinduka omuntu, ashemereire kutaaha omu muhanda gw'OKUHINDUKA KW'OBWENGYE.

ENYAMA EW'AMAGEZI n'AKAGORO, EMBARE; okuruga omu mbare egyo nikubaasa kuzarwa OMUTI GW'AMAGARA, OMUNTU OW'AMAZIMA, OMUNTU ogwo ogu DIOGENES yaabaire arikusherura n'etaala eyakaire omu mabarabara g'e ATENAS na ha kati y'eizooba kandi atarikubaasa kumusanga.

TI ITEEKA ngu akagoro aka, ngu embare egi erikushusha erikubaasa kukura, ekirikwetenga, ekirikwemerera n'okubura.

OMUNTU OW'AMAZIMA ataine buzahura na NYAMA EW'AMAGEZI, nk'oku OMWANAAZA guri ekire.

Waaba akagoro kaafa embare etemerera, nikyetengwa, nikihuthu okufa OKWEYENDA, YEENA, MEENE, paca kuzaara omuntu.

Abashomesa abarikukirira n'abashomesa b'amaheesha n'univaasite, bashemereire kwegyesa abegi baabo OMUHANDA GW'OBWENGYE OBW'OKUHINDUKA, nikibaasa kuba ngu okufa kw'OKWEYENDA.

Kurugiirira twine okugira ngu OKUHINDUKA KW'OBWENGYE tikurikukirira omunaku omu nsi enu, kureka nikukirira kuba orufu n'orufu.

OKUHINDUKA KW'OBWENGYE kwiine ebintu bishatu ebirikushushana: Ekikuru, Okufa; Ekyakabiri, Okuzarwa; Ekyakashatu, Okwetanga ahabw'abantu. Omurongo gw'ebintu tigurikuhindura ekirikurugamu.

OKUFA n'ensonga y'OBWENGYE OBW'OKUHINDUKA n'OKUHWERERA kw'OBWEYENDA BW'OBWENGE.

OKUZARWA n'ensonga y'OKUHAMIRA KW'OMUBIRI, ensonga enu nekwata aha BY'OMUBIRI eby'AMAANI, owaayenda kwega ensonga enu, ashemereire kutuhandiikira n'okumanya ebitabo byaitu eby'Obwengye.

OKWETANGA ahabw'abantu n'OBUNTU OBW'ENSI YOONA OBW'OBWENGYE.

Twaba tutarikwenda OKUHINDUKA KW'OBWENGYE, twaba tutarikukora OKWEYONGEZA KW'AMAANI kukura obugabe obwo obutagarukireho oburikwija kututwara aha KWIHANGAAZA KW'OMWOYO, nikirungi ngu obugabe obwo tiburikukura.

N'orufu abarikwIHANGAAZA, abarikujuna kandi omu kyo tikuhabura kwona, habwaki ENYAMA EW'AMAGEZI yaba nekwenda ekyo ekitakirikwenda?

Nikyeteengwa okuhinduka kw'amazima kwona n'okuhweera kwona kureka abantu boona tibarikwenda okuhinduka okwo, tibarikukwenda, tibarikukimanya kandi nibabagambira kandi tibarikwetegyereza, tibarikukiguza, tibarikukikwenda. Habwaki baraheebwe aha maani ekyo ekitabarikwenda?

Amazima gari ngu omuntu atakatungire EBIRIKUMUSHOBOZESA EBIPYA nari AMAANI MAPYA, ebi atarikumanya nari kurora haihi kandi atakatungire, ashemereire kutunga obushoboorozi n'amaani ebi arikwikiriza kukuratira, kureka omu mazima tabirikugira.
