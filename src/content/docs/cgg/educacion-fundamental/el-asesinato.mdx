Okutta omuntu kwolesa bulungi, era tewali kubuusabuusa, kye kikolwa ekisinga okwonoona era n'okubba eky'amaanyi ekimanyiddwa mu nsi yonna.

Enkola embi ennyo ey'obutemu kwe kuzikiriza obulamu bw'abantu bannaffe.

Nga kitiisa nnyo omuyizzi oyo ng'amattayo agattika ebitonde ebitakka mu kibira kyokka omuntu atta bannaffe abasinga nnyo obubi, abasinga nnyo okukyayibwa.

Si na matta gokka ng'agammattayo, emmundu, emizinga, emmundu ento, oba bbomu za atomu, era oyinza okutta n'ekirabo ekiyita mu mutima, ekirabo ekitenda, ekirabo ekijjudde obukyayi, ekirabo ekijjudde obukyayi; oba oyinza okutta n'ekikolwa ekitasiima, n'ekikolwa ekirala, oba n'ekivumo, oba n'ekigambo ekiluma.

Ensi ejjudde abatta bazadde baabwe, abatta abakyala baabwe abatasiima abasse bazadde baabwe, mu kirabo kyabwe, mu bigambo byabwe, oba mu bikolwa byabwe ebikambwe.

Ensi ejjudde abasajja abatali bamanyi basse abakyala baabwe era n'abakyala abatali bamanyi basse babbaabwe.

Okusingawo obubi mu nsi eno enkyayibwa mwe tubeera, omuntu atta ekyo ky'asinga okwagala.

Omuntu tabaawo na mugaati gwokka wabula n'obukakafu obw'enjawulo.

Abaami bangi bandibadde bayinza okuwangaala singa abakyala baabwe babakkiriza.

Abakyala bangi bandibadde bayinza okuwangaala singa abaami baabwe babakkiriza.

Bazadde bangi bandibadde bayinza okuwangaala singa abaana baabwe babakkiriza.

Obulwadde obutwala omuntu waffe gwe twagala mu ntaana bulina ekireetawo, ebigambo ebitta, ebirabo ebiyita, ebikolwa ebitasiima, n'ebirala.

Omukago guno ogukadde era ogukyayibwa gujjudde abatta abatali bamanyi nga beesigama ku butaliiko musango.

Amakomera gajjudde abatta kyokka ekika ekibi ennyo eky'abamenyi b'amateeka kye beesigamya ku butaliiko musango era nga batambula bwerere.

Tewali ngeri yonna ey'obutemu esobola okuba n'obutuukirivu bwonna. Okutta omulala tekigonjoola buzibu bwonna mu bulamu.

Entalo tezinnagonjoola buzibu bwonna. Okubomba ebibuga ebitaliiko kikuumi era n'okutta obuwumbi bw'abantu tegonjoola kintu kyonna.

Entalo kintu kikambwe nnyo, kikakanyavu, kya bugulumbo, kikyayibwa. Obuwumbi bw'ebyuma by'abantu ebiri mu tulo, abataliiko bumanyi, abasirusiru, batandika olutalo n'ekigendererwa eky'okuzikiriza obuwumbi bw'ebyuma by'abantu abataliiko bumanyi.

Emirundi mingi kimaze okuba okuzikirira kw'enkulukuta mu cosmos, oba ekifo ekibi ennyo eky'emmunyeenye mu ggulu, okusobozesa abasajja obuwumbi okutandika olutalo.

Ebyuma by'abantu tebirina bumanyi bwonna, byetambuliza mu ngeri eyonoona ng'ekika ky'amayengo ga cosmos bwe gibayita mu kyama.

Singa abantu basensimuka obumanyi, singa okuva mu bbanka z'essomero abayizi n'abayizi bayigirizibwa mu magezi nga babatwala mu kutegeera obumanyi obw'ekyo obulabe n'entalo ze biri, endulu endala yandiyimbye gwe, tewali yanditandise lutalo era n'amayengo agazikiriza aga cosmos gandikozeseddwa mu ngeri ya njawulo.

Entalo ennuuna ng'obulya bw'abantu, obulamu bw'empuku, obusolo bw'ekika ekibi ennyo, akabbanga, omusaale, effumu, oluyi olw'omusaayi, tebiyinza kutambulira wamu n'enkulaakulana.

Abasajja bonna mu lutalo batidde, batiddwa era abazira abajjudde emidaali be batidde ennyo, abatiddwa ennyo.

Omuntu eyeetta alinga n'omuvumu nnyo kyokka ye mutiitiizi kubanga yatidde obulamu.

Omuzira mu makkati ye muntu eyeetta eyakola obusirusiru bw'omuntu eyeetta mu kiseera ky'entiisa esinga obukulu.

Obusirusiru bw'omuntu eyeetta butabukamu nnyangu n'obuvumu bw'omuzira.

Bwe tulabirira n'obwegendereza empisa z'omusirikale mu kiseera ky'olutalo, engeri ze, ekirabo kye, ebigambo bye, ebigere bye mu lutalo, tusobola okukakasa obutiitiizi bwe bwonna.

Abasomesa b'amassomero, amatendekero, Yunivasite bateekwa okuyigiriza abayizi baabwe amazima agali ku lutalo. Bateekwa okutwala abayizi baabwe okukiriza amazima ago mu bumanyi.

Singa abantu balina obumanyi obujjuvu obw'amazima gano agatiisa ag'olutalo, singa abasomesa bamanyi okuyigiriza abagoberezi baabwe mu magezi, tewali mutuuze yandirekereddwa okutwalibwa mu kattu.

Eby'enjigiriza ebikulu biteekwa okutandikwawo kati mu Massomero gonna, Amatendekero, ne Yunivasite, kubanga kiva mu bbanka z'essomero, omulimu we guteekwa okukolerwa okuyamba Emirembe.

Kyanguwa okuba emirembe emipya egimanyi bulungi obubi n'entalo ze biri.

Mu Massomero, Amatendekero, Yunivasite, obulabe n'entalo biteekwa okutegeerwa mu bujjuvu mu ngeri zaabyo zonna.

Emirembe emipya eteekwa okutegeera nti abakadde n'ebirowoozo byabwe ebikadde era ebikakanyavu, bulijjo bagamba abato era nga babatwala ng'ente mu kattu.

Abato tebateekwa kukkirizibwa okukubirizibwa okwenyigira mu ntalo, oba olw'ensonga z'abakadde, kubanga ensonga eyolekera ensonga endala era endowooza eyolekera endala, kyokka newankubadde okuteesa oba endowooza ze mazima ku lutalo.

Abakadde balina obuwumbi bw'ensonga z'okutwala olutalo mu butuukirivu era n'okutwala abato mu kattu.

Ekikulu si kuteesa ku lutalo wabula okukiriza amazima g'olutalo.

Tetwogera ku Lwazoni oba okunoonyereza, twagala kugamba nti tuteekwa okusooka okukiriza amazima agali ku lutalo era oluvannyuma tusobola okwewa omukisa gw'okuteesa n'okunoonyereza.

Tekisoboka okukiriza amazima agali ku BUTATTA, bwe tuba tusizzaamu okufumiitiriza okw'omunda.

Okufumiitiriza okw'omunda kwokka kwe kusobola okututwala okukiriza amazima agali ku Lutalo.

Abasomesa tebateekwa kuweereza bayizi baabwe bwanguzi bwa magezi bwokka. Abasomesa bateekwa okuyigiriza abayizi baabwe okukozesa ebirowoozo byabwe, okukiriza AMAZIMA.

Ekika kino ekikadde era ekyonoonefu tekikyalowooza ku kutta. Ekyo eky'okutta n'okutta, kisinga kuba kya buli kika ky'omuntu ekikyayibwa.

Okuyita mu ttivi ne firimu, abakulembeze b'emisango babunyisa ebirowoozo byabwe eby'obumenyi bw'amateeka.

Abaana abalenzi n'abawala ab'omulembe omupya bafuna buli lunaku nga bayita mu ttivi n'ennyiriri z'abaana n'okuva mu firimu, magazini n'ebirala, ekitundu ekirungi eky'obutwa obw'obutemu, emmundu, emisango egy'entiisa n'ebirala.

Tokyalina kusobola kussa ttivi mu nkola nga tofunye bigambo bijjudde obukyayi, emmundu, obukyayi.

Gavumenti z'ensi tezirina kye zikola ku kubunyisibwa kw'emisango.

Ebirowoozo by'abaana n'abavubuka bitwalibwa abakulembeze b'emisango, mu kkubo ly'obumenyi bw'amateeka.

Ekiteeso eky'okutta kyakunyisiddwa nnyo, kyakunyisiddwa nnyo okuyita mu firimu, ennyiriri n'ebirala, kifuluddwa okumanyika bulungi eri buli omu.

Abajeemu ab'omuyaga omupya bayigiriziddwa olw'obumenyi bw'amateeka era ne batta olw'okwagala okutta, banyiikirira okulaba abalala nga bafa. Ekyo bakiyize ku ttivi ey'ennyumba, mu firimu, mu nnyiriri, mu magazini.

Okumenya amateeka kufuga buli kifo era gavumenti teziriiko kye zikola okukola obulongoofu obw'okutta okuva mu mirandira gyabyo.

Kituuka ku Basomesa b'amassomero, Amatendekero, ne Yunivasite, okutema omulanga mu ggulu era n'okuzza ggulu n'ensi okukola obulongoofu ku ndwadde eno ey'ebirowoozo.

Kyanguwa abasomesa b'amassomero, Amatendekero, ne Yunivasite, okutema omulanga ogw'entiisa era n'okusaba gavumenti zonna ez'ensi okukendeeza ku firimu, ttivi n'ebirala.

Omusango gweyongera nnyo olw'ebyolesebwa by'omusaayi ebyo byonna era n'omugendo gwe tugenda gutuuka olunaku lwe tewaliba n'omu asobola okutambula mu nguudo mu ddembe nga tatidde okuttibwa.

Leediyo, Firimu, Ttivi, Magazini z'omusaayi, ziweeredde okunyisibwa ng'okwo okw'omusaayi gw'okutta, bazifuludde nga zinyumira ebirowoozo ebinafu era ebyonoonefu, nga tewali n'omu akutte omutima okuteeka omuntu omulala omusaale oba oluyi.

Olw'amaanyi g'okunyisa omusango gw'okutta, ebirowoozo ebinafu bimenyeddwa nnyo ku musango era kati bajiwa n'omukisa okutta olw'okukoppa ebyo bye baalaba mu firimu oba ku ttivi.

Abasomesa abasomesa abantu bawalirizibwa mu kutuukiriza omulimu gwabwe okulwanirira emirembe emipya nga basaba Gavumenti ez'ensi okugaana ebyolesebwa by'omusaayi, mu bufunze, okusazaamu buli kika kya firimu ku butemu, ababbi n'ebirala.

Olutalo lw'Abasomesa luteekwa okugaziwazibwa okutuuka ku kusaaga n'ebikonde.

Omutindo gw'omusaazi gwe mutindo ogutidde ennyo era omumenyi w'amateeka. Omusaazi ayagala obulungi bwonna bwe bwokka era atta okusanyusa abantu.

Omutindo gw'omukononi gwe gw'ekiserengeto ky'obutemu, mu ngeri ye ey'obukyayi eyiyita era n'okutta okusanyusa abantu.

Ekika kino eky'ebyolesebwa by'omusaayi kya busoso buli kimu era kikubiriza ebirowoozo nga bibitwala mu kkubo ly'omusaayi. Bwe twagala okukola olutalo olw'amazima olw'emirembe gy'ensi, tuteekwa okutandika kampeyini ennyingi ku byolesebwa by'omusaayi.

Nga munda mu birowoozo by'omuntu mwe muli ebintu ebiyinza okuzikiriza entalo ze zibeerawo.

Munda mu birowoozo by'omuntu mwe muli ebintu ebireetawo entalo, ebintu ebyo bwe bukyayi. ettemu mu ngeri zaabyo zonna, obubi, obusungu, okutya, ebintu by'obumenyi bw'amateeka, ebirowoozo ebikubiriza olutalo nga bibunyisibwa ttivi, leediyo, firimu n'ebirala.
