Abegyesi b'amasomero, koleji na yunivasite nibaha oburemeezi bwingi ahari disipiuliine kandi twine kugishoma omu mutwe ogu burikureeba.

Itoona twahitsire omu masomero, koleji, yunivasite, n'ebindi. Nitumanya gye eki disipiuliine, ebiragiro, empindo, okugamba, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Disipiuliine nikyo ekirikwetwa KUKURA OKUHAMBA. Abegyesi b'amasomero nibakunda kukura OKUHAMBA.

Nitwegyesibwa kuhamba, kuzimbira ekintu ekindi. Nitwegyesibwa kuhamba ebiteetiso by'enyama kandi nitwekwata omuko kandi tukakora okwebaza okuhamba.

Nitwegyesibwa KUHAMBA ebiteetiso ebireetwa obugaya, ebiteetiso by'obutashoma, obutagiira omu ishomero, okucweka, okuseka, okuseka abegyesi, kutaahirira ebiragiro, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Abegyesi n'abegyesikazi baine okwetegyereza okushobya ngu omu disipiuliine nitubaasa kwetegyereza omugasho gw'okutiina oburagiriro bw'ishomero, omugasho gw'okushoma, okutunga obusaasi omu maisho g'abegyesi, okwetwaza gye n'abegyeswa bagenzi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Hariho okwetegyereza okushobya omu bantu ngu okukira eki turikuhamba, okukira eki turikwanga, nituba abetegyereize, abataire, abajwire, abatsinzi.

Abantu tibarikwenda kwetegyereza ngu okukira eki turikurwanisa ekintu, okukira eki turikukihamba, okukira eki turikukwanga, nikyo kigufu OKWETEGEREZA.

Twaza kurwanisa omugisa gw'okunywa, ogu guraayangaanisa obwire bukye, kwonka ahabw'okuba tutakatetegyereize gye omu BITA GYOONA EBY'OBWENGE, ogu guraagaruka nyima twamara kwetegyereza oburinzi kandi tukunywe omurundi gumwe omwaka guyoona.

Twaanga omugisa gw'obushambani, obwire bukye turaabe abaine obutungwa obukuru (n'obu omu BITA BINDI EBY'OBWENGE turaabe nitugumizamu kuba ABAISI ABAHIMA nk'oku ebirooto EBIRIKUTUNGA EMISI nibibaasa kworeka), kandi nyima tugaruke n'amaani mungi ahari eby'enyima byaitu eby'ABASHAMBANI ABATARIKWENDWA, ahabw'okuba omuntu atetegyereize gye eki obushambani burikureeba.

Abingi nibo abarikwanga OBUTAMI, abarikukurwanisa, abarikwehireera kurwanisa bakuratira EBIRAGIRO by'okwetwaza, kwonka ahabw'okuba tibatetetegyereize mazima omulingo gw'OBUTAMI, nibareeta enshonga omu butami omu butami obutaba obutami.

Abingi nibo abarikwehireera kurwanisa OBUGWISA, abarikwega kukuhamba, kwonka ogu nigugumizamu kubaaho omu bita bindi eby'obwenge obutari obw'omuntu, n'obu omu maisho byaba byayangaanisize omu mulingo gwaitu kandi aha kutetegyereza oburinzi, obutari obw'omuntu nibutureeta ensonga kandi obwo tukaburara kandi tukamurika tukajwara obugwisa, obu tukaba tutarikwenda kandi obundi ahabw'enshonga etarikwetagisa OBUREMEEZI BWONNA.

Abingi nibo abarikwehireera kurwanisa obujunaanwa kandi aha muheru nibakikiriza omu mutima ngu nibabyangaanisa kwonka ahabw'okuba tibabutetegyereize nikireeba ngu aba nibareeta ensonga empya omu kibanza obwo twaba nitubikiriza kuba bafiire gye.

Nikiija n'obuhereza obujwire obutaine disipiuliine, omu buhairwe obw'amazima, nikigwa omu bwenge, okwaka okujumagira okw'OKWETEGEREZA.

OBUHAIRWE OBW'OKUREETA tiburikubaasa kubaho omu AMAANI. Nitwetaga obuhairwe KWETEGEREZA enshonga zaitu Z'OBWENGE omu mulingo ogujwire.

Nitwetaga n'OMUKO kumara empanga n'okwasa enkoondo z'ebyoma, kuba abataire.

Twine kugezesa itwe abatwe ebyo abegyesi baitu omu Ishomero n'abazaire baitu batugambire ngu n'ebirungi kandi ebikuru. Tikirikumaraho kwega omutwe n'okutooreza. Nitwetaga kwetegyereza.

Omulingo gw'okukora ogw'abegyesi n'abegyesikazi gushemereire kutungwa ahari omuntu omu abegyeswa. Bashemereire kweyongyera ahabw'okuba babaayingira omu muhanda gw'OKWETEGEREZA.

Tikirikumaraho kugamba abegyeswa ngu bashemereire kuba egi nari egi, nikwetagisa ngu abegyeswa beega kuba abataire kubaasa kubareba, kushoma, kushanaana ebintu byoona, ebintu byoona ebi abantu bagambire ngu nibigasa, ebikuru, ebirungi kandi batabikiriza nari okubitooreza.

Abantu tibarikwenda kushanga itwe abatwe, baine obwenge obutariho, obushoberwa, obwenge obutarikwenda kushanaana, obwenge obw'obukuru obutatashanaana kandi obutooreza.

Nikyetagisa, nikyanguhire, nikikuru ngu abegyeswa n'abegyesikazi okuruga omu myaka yaabo ey'obuto bwabo kukuhitsya obwire bw'okubazibwa omu BYUUNA bakunde obuhairwe bw'amazima kushanga itwe abatwe, kucweecweeta, kwetegyereza kandi ngu tibarikuremesa empanga z'amacaafu z'emirimo, okugamba na disipiuliine.

Abegyeswa bakagambirwa eki bashemereire kukora kandi eki batashemereire kukora kandi batarikwikirizibwa KWETEGEREZA n'okugezesa, OBWO eri haihi obwenge bwaabo? OKUKUKULA eri omugisha oguhairwe aha bwenge?

Kiri kigasha ki obwo kuhita ebirooto, kwambara gye, kutunga banywani baingi twaba tutari baine bwenge?

Obwenge nibwija itwe obwo twaba abataire mazima kushanaana itwe abatwe, kwetegyereza, kushanaana hatariho okutiina okugamba n'omupindo gw'Obuhendo.

Abegyeswa abatiinisa, abatwara, abataire ahansi ha disipiuliine etali yo tibarikubaasa KUMANYA. Tibarikubaasa kuba ab'obwenge.

Ebiro ebi abazaire n'abegyesi, ekintu kimwe abarikukunda nikyo ngu abegyeswa n'abegyesikazi bakole omulimo, babe abasaahozi, abamanzi, enyinja, abakozi b'ofisi, okukuleeta abakozesa obwenge abahuriire kandi nyima bashwakiire kandi babe n'omu MAKINA G'OKUKORA ABAANA kandi ogwo nigwo gwona.

Abasheija nari abashaijakazi nibakwenda kukora ekintu ekipya, ekintu ekindi, obwo bamanya omugasho gw'okuruga omu maani ogwo, okushora, emigyenzo ey'enyima, disipiuliine, emiringo y'oruganda nari orurembo, n'ebindi, obwo abazaire nibakwata gye enkoondo z'ekikomera kandi bagambe aha musheija nari aha mushaijakazi: Otakora ekyo! titurikwenda kukukwatanisa omu kyo, ebyo nibishemesha, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

BYONA omusheija nari omushaijakazi bamuta omu nkomo omu nkomo y'emiringo, emiringo y'obushwere emiringo y'enyima, ebirooto by'obukuru, n'ebindi.

OBWENGE OBW'OKUZINDURA nibwegyesa kukwatanisa OBURAGIRIRO n'OBUHAIRWE.

OBURAGIRIRO obutaine OBUHAIRWE n'OBUTABANISA. OBUHAIRWE obutaine OBURAGIRIRO n'OKUSEMEERERWA.

OBUHAIRWE N'OBURAGIRIRO ebikwatanisibwe obwenge nibikoraho OMUSINGI gw'OBWENGE OBW'OKUZINDURA.

ABEGYESWA bashemereire kukunda obuhairwe obuhikire okushobya itwe abatwe, KUCWEECWEETA kwetegyereza ekyo mazima, ekyo mazima bari BENE ITWE kandi eki babaasa kukora omu bwomeezi.

Abegyeswa n'abegyesikazi, abasirikare na poliisi kandi omu mulingo gw'okuzindura abantu boona abarikwetaga kutwarwa omu disipiuliine erikuguma, nibatunga enshonga, abetweire ahansi y'obusaasi bw'omuntu, abatari baine busaasi.

DISIPIULIINE neyonoonisa OBUHURIRE bw'omuntu kandi ekyo kyamara kworeka buzima aha KUREEBA n'OKUGESESA.

Ahabw'emirimo mingi na disipiuliine, abantu b'obwire obu bayangaanisize kimwe OBUHURIRE kandi batunga enshonga kandi abatari baine busaasi.

Kuba abataire mazima nikwetaga kuba abasaasi munonga kandi ab'obuntu.

Omu masomero, koleji na yunivasite, abegyeswa nibegyesibwa KUTA OMU KLASI kandi abegyeswa n'abegyesikazi nibata omu klasi kwanga okugamba, okujwara amatu, okukuba n'omupindo nari n'ekiragiro, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi. Kwonka nshonga nibatwegyesa KWETEGEREZA KIMWE eki KUTA KIRIHO.

Ahabw'okwehireera omwegyeswa nata omu maisho kandi akamara amaani g'okureeta emirimo myingi omu mulingo ogutagasa.

Amaani g'okureeta nikyo kimwe aha mulingo gw'amaani agatoreire agakoorwa aha MAKINA AGALIHO.

Itwe nituliira kandi tukanywa kandi emiringo yoona ey'okutoora nikyo kimwe emirimo y'okutoha omu yo eby'emikoro nibareetwa omu mulingo n'amaani ebikuru.

Amaani g'okureeta nigo: Omulingo gw'EKINTU n'amaani agatoreire agakoorwa oruganisimu.

Twamanya KUTA AMAANI TULIHO nitubaasa kugaana amaani g'okureeta.

Tukasharira n'omugisha abegyesi n'abegyesikazi tibarikwegyesa abegyeswa baabo eki KUTA KURIHO.

Doquiera turikurugiriza KUTA tukamara AMAANI G'OKUREETA. Nitubaasa kugaana amaani ago twagabura amaani, tutaitangiza ebintu, abantu, ebirooto.

Obwo itwe twaitangiza abantu, ebintu, ebirooto, twirwa itwe abatwe kandi obwo turaleka amaani g'okureeta omu mulingo ogurikusaasa.

NIKYANGUHIRE kumanya ngu nitwetaga kugaana AMAANI G'OKUREETA okuzindura OBWENGE kandi ngu AMAANI G'OKUREETA nigo MAANI AGALIHO, EKIYENJURO ky'OBWENGE, eky'omunda kya KUZINDURA OBWENGE.

Obwo twayega kutalirwa ITWE ABATWE, obwo twayega kugabura KUTA ahagati y'OMUNTU; EKINTU n'EKIBANJA, tugaana AMAANI G'OKUREETA okuzindura OBWENGE.

NIKWETAGA kwega kureebereza KUTA okuzindura obwenge kwonka abegyeswa n'abegyesikazi tibamanyire aha kyo ahabw'okuba ABAGYESI baabo na BAMAGYESIKAZI tibakibaegyesize.

OBU twayega kukozesa KUTA buzima, disipiuliine obwo nikija kuruga.

OMWEGYESWA nari omwegyesikazi ahuriire aha klaso, aha bigambo bye, aha buragiriro, TATWETEGA disipiuliine y'obuhendo bwona.

NIKYANGUHIRE ngu ABAGYESI betegyereze omugasho gw'okukwatanisa obwenge OBUHAIRWE n'OBURAGIRIRO kandi ekyo nikibaasa kukorwa KUTA TULIHO.

KUTA TULIHO nikumara ekyo ekirikwetwa KWETANGIZA. Obwo twetangiza abantu, ebintu, ebirooto, nibija okumanya kandi ogu nyima nireeta EBINOONI omu OBWENGE.

Twine kumanya KUTA hatariho KWETANGIZA. OBU twareeta omu kintu nari omu muntu kandi twirwa itwe abatwe, ebireetwa nibyo KUMANYA n'EBINOONI by'OBWENGE.

Reeba gye omuntu arikureeba sinema. Nasangwa agwetse, byoona nabigaya, akagaya itwe, aliho, nareeba nk'omuntu arikugenda omu kirooto, aroota aha firimu arikureeba, aha mutsindi w'aha firimu.

ABEGYESWA n'abegyesikazi bashemereire kuta omu klasi hatariho kwirwa ITWE ABATWE kutagwa omu EBINOONI EBIRIKUTIINA eby'OBWENGE.

Omwegyeswa ashemereire kureeba itwe omu kibanza obwo arikuleeta omweheru nari obwo arikuba omu tabura nari omu pisan omu buragiriro bw'omwegyesi, nari obwo ayetegyereza nari arukuhumura nari akacweka n'abegyeswa be.

KUTA okugaburwa omu BITA ISHAA: OMUNTU, EKINTU, EKIBANJA, buzima nikyo KUTA TULIHO.

Obwo tutakora ENSHOBA yo'KWETANGIZA abantu, ebintu, ebirooto, n'ebindi tugaana AMAANI G'OKUREETA kandi twareeta omu itwe okuzindura kw'OBWENGE.

Orikwenda kuzindura OBWENGE omu MAISHO AGARI JUUHA, ashemereire kutandika na KUZINDURA egi n'obu.

Obwo OMWEGYESWA akora enshoba yo'KWETANGIZA abantu, ebintu, ebirooto, obwo akora enshoba y'okwirwa itwe, obwo atwa omu kumanya n'ebinooni.

Disipiuliine teyegyesa abegyeswa KUTA TULIHO. Disipiuliine n'enkomo y'amazima y'obwenge.

Abegyeswa n'abegyesikazi bashemereire kwega kureebereza KUTA TULIHO okuruga omu mabaanka g'ishomero ngu nyima omu bwomeezi oburikutwarwa, hanze y'ishomero, batakora enshoba y'okwirwa itwe.

Omushaija orirwa itwe aheeru y'omuntu arikugamba emibaro, akaitangiza, akamanyi, agatwa omu binooni by'obutari bwa buzima kandi obwo agasaara nari aita kandi akagenda omu nkomo atarikuganya.

Oguturikuleka KUMANYA n'omuntu arikugamba emibaro, ogutarikwaitangiza, ogutarikwirwa itwe, ogurikumanya KUTA TULIHO, araabe atarikubaasa kugira omugasho aha bigambo by'omuntu arikugamba emibaro, nari okumusaara nari okumwita.

Enshoba yoona eki omuntu akora omu bwomeezi zibaho ahabw'okuba arikwirwa itwe, arikwaitangiza, arikumanya kandi agatwa omu binooni.

Kirungi aha bukyanga, aha begyeswa boona, ngu bakwegyesibwe okuzindura kw'OBWENGE omu kiikaro ky'okubata omu disipiuliine nyingi ezitarikugasa.
