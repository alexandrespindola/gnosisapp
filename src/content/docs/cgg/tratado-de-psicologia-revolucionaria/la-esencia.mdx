Eki ekireetera omwana omuhya kuba mulungi kandi ow'engaro nungi, bwekuba obuntu bwe; obwo nibwo buzimbe bw'obumunyarwanda bwe bwona... Okukurira kw'obuntu omu kiremwa kyona, buzire buzire, bukye bukye...

Omubiri gw'omuntu gukura kandi guteera ahaiguru n'amateeka g'obutonde bw'ekika ky'omuntu, kwonka emigisha egi nikigye habw'obuntu... Tikiriho kubuza obuntu nikibasa kukura enyenyekare bwoona hatariho omuntu, kukye kukye...

Nitugamba orutaro kandi hatariho okwikiririza ebigambo, okukurira kw'obuntu okw'obwomeezi n'obutonde, nikukwatira ahari emyaka eshatu, ina na itaano y'obukuru, okugira ngu, omu myaka ya ira y'obwomeezi... Abantu nibatekereza ngu okukurira n'okuteera ahaiguru kw'obuntu nikikora buri kasumi, nikirikukwatana n'enkora y'okuhinduka, kwonka obuhangwa bw'omunsi yoona nibwegyesa orutaro ngu eki tikirikubaasa kubaho...

N'ekigendererwa ky'okwongera obuntu kukura, ekintu kikuru kishemereire kubaho, ekintu kihyahya kishemereire kukorwa. Ninyenda kworekereza omu muringo ogurikukiraho omurimo ahaiguru yaawe. Okukurira kw'obuntu nikibasa kwemerera ahaiguru ya mirimo y'okumanya n'okuhereza...

Nikushemereire kumanya ngu emirimo egi terikukwatagana n'ebibuzo by'omurimo, amabanga, obabazi, abazimbi, okutebeekereza emigwa y'engara rundi ensonga z'omu ofiisi... Omurimo ogu ngu ogw'omuntu weena ayeteereireho obuntu bwe; nigw'ensonga z'omutwe...

Itwe twena nitumanya ngu twina omunda y'omunda ya itwe ekintu ekirikwetwa EGO, NYOWE, AKANGE, MWENE NKA... Obusaasi obungi, obuntu nibushangwa butamiire, buri omunda y'omunda, ahaagati ya EGO kandi eki nikishema. Okucwekyereza NYOWE w'omutwe, okuhwerekyereza ebintu byaawe ebirikwenda, nikihuta, tikirikwegondeza, tikirikusiibwe... nikwo okumanyisa kw'omurimo ahaiguru yaawe. Titurikubaasa kutorora obuntu hatariho okucwekyereza NYOWE w'omutwe...

Omu buntu muno, obweine Ruhanga, BUDDHA, amagezi, ebice by'obusaasi bya Tata itwe oriyo omu iguru hamu n'ebikumanyiso byona ebi turikwenda ahabw'OKWETEERAAHO okurikwegonza OMUNDA y'OBUNTU. Tihariho muntu orikubaasa kucwekyereza NYOWE w'omutwe hatariho okumara omubabaro ebintu by'obuhangwa ebi turikutwara omunda...

Nitwetaaga kukwata omuriro omutindo mubi gw'omutima ogurikukiraho omubiiro ogw'omubembe gw'obwire buno: obusangwa oburikusaasa, omubembe gw'obwire buno: obusangwa oburikukwatana n'emirimiro y'omutima ogurikubasaire kubaire ogw'ekisibo ky'ekikorwa; obuganzi oburikwimuka oburemereze obubaire bwona nibutitiire: okugamba obubi okurikusaasa; obwesigire oburikuruga omu ndwara nyingi: okwetamiira; obusambanyi oburikahuura oburiiro oburi bu...

Aha mugerengo ogw'ebintu ebyo byona eby'omubembe n'obunazi oburikukwata omuriro, obuntu hamu n'okubangwaka, bukurira kandi bukwatagana... Hatariho kubuza obwo NYOWE w'omutwe yaafa, bwezirika omuriiro omuriiro omuri itwe obuntu...

Obuntu oburikwetegereza nibutuha oburungi bw'omunda; ahabw'oburungi obwo buziruka obushemererwa obuhikiriire hamu n'okukunda okw'amazima... Obuntu butungire amagombe g'obuhikiriire hamu n'obushoborozi bw'obutonde oburikujuma... Obwo "Turikufa Omuriiro Omunda Ya Itwe", obwo turikumaraho NYOWE w'omutwe, nitushemerererwa amagombe g'omutindo hamu n'obushoborozi bw'obuntu...
