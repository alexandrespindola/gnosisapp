Okwehaba omu mbeera ezihendura amagara, omu bwire buno obw’omwirima, buzima kikuru munonga kwonka kiragire, nari okw’okulya amagara.

Omulimo gwona ogwo abantu bakora aha igulu lyabo n’ekigendererwa ky’okutuunga obukuru bw’omwoyo n’okutunga obwomeezi bw’omwoyo, buri obwire bugambirirana n’okwetantara kwamazima munonga, ahabwokuba omu maani g’amagara nk’oku buri obwire tugagira, tikirikubasika okutuunga ekintu ekindi kukira obuntu.

Titurikugyezaho kwanga okutuunga obuntu bw’omuntu, buzima kikuru omu kubaho, kwonka buzima kintu ky’omuringo, tikikuru, buzima omuriitwe.

Embeera ya ensi y’obushema, ebi abantu barikuteekateeka nibishushana n’ebirikutukoraho omu magara gaitu ga butoosha kandi tubiha amaani gaitu omu bwoba n’okushisha, n’okuteekateeka ahabwaitwe n’ebigambo by’obusa by’omushaija, tiharukubasika kuteeka ekintu kyona ekirikutuunga omuriitwe, okujwera ebyo ebiri omu nsi y’obubingwa.

Buzima ogwo orikwenda buzima okutuunga omu bugumi bw’obugumi, ashemereire kuhinduka ogufunzire munonga. Eki nikigambirirana n’ekintu ky’aha mutima ekigambirirana n’okucwa.

Ekigambo kija kuruga omu bwire bw’edha, obu baabaire nibegyesa omu bwihisho enyegyesa ahari okutuunga omuntu omu butima bw’omuntu ekirikugambirirana n’eiziina lya Hermes.

Ku omuntu arikwenda ekintu ky’amazima kukura omu butima bw’omuntu, nikitangaza kwetantara obuhungi bw’amaani ga omuntu.

Ku omuntu aine obuhungi bw’amaani kandi atarikw’ebaha omu mutima gw’omuntu, tikirikukwatagwako ngu tarikubaasa kutuunga ekintu ky’amazima kuteeka omu biteekateeko by’omuntu.

Amagara ga butoosha nigenda kutumira munonga; twine kureebaana n’amagara burizooba, twine kwega okw’emeesa oruzi…

Omulimo ogu nigenda kurwana n’amagara, kintu kikirukutaanisa n’ebya burizooba kwonka twine kukora omu kasumi n’akasumi; ndikwenda kugamba okukora empinduka y’okumanya.

Nikimanyirwe ngu enshonga yaitu aha magara ga butoosha neshuba obushobezi; ku turikwikiriza ngu byona nibigenda gye, ahabw’okuba nibigenda gye, nibija kwija obushaame…

Abantu nibenda ngu ebintu bibagendere gye, “ahabw’okuba nibigenda gye”, ahabw’okuba byona nibishemereire kugenda nk’enteekateeka yaabo, kwonka buzima orutaro nirutaana, omu bwire bwona obu omuntu atarikukyusa omu butima bw’omuntu, narikukunda nari tarikukunda burizooba ariba akatumbiira, obwo obumbeera.

Nibagamba kandi nibahandiika aha magara, obushobezi bwingi bw’emihanda y’abantu, kwonka ekibuuzo eki kya Psychologia Revolucionaria kirukutaanisa.

Enyegyesa egi neija aha mazima, aha byakozirwe ebiri gye, ebiri gye kandi eby’okumaraaho; nirihamya ngu “Enyamaishwa y’Obwengye” ekwikiriza abantu, kintu ekigambira kubiri, okutamanya, obutindire.

“Omwine w’Enju Omurungi” tarikwija kwikiriza Psychologia Revolucionaria; niribanganisa n’omugisha gwaabo nk’omuzaire, omushaija, n’ebindi, kandi ahabw’ekyo nibateekateeka ahabwabo obuntu oburikukira obundi, kwonka nibakora enshonga ya butonde kandi nikyo kyona.

Tukutaanise nibwo tugamba ngu naiwe hariho “Omwine w’Enju Omurungi” orikw’emeesa oruzi, orikwenda kurekwa amagara; embero, abantu aba nibasya omu nsi, tibarikwijura.

Ku omuntu arikuteekateeka n’enteekateeka z’ekibuuzo eki kya Psicologia Revolucionaria, nitutuunga okworeka okuri gye kw’amagara.
