Obusinge obu buri omuri buri omwe ahariitwe, niburuga ahaiguru, omu iguru, omu nyonyozi...

Tikiriho kubanganisa ngu obusinge oburikukangaaza niburuga omu mwanya gwa "LA" (Omuhanda gw'amata, omugyera ogu turimu)

Obusinge oburikukangaaza nibuhita omu mwanya gwa "SOL" (Eizooba) reero omu mwanya gwa "FA" (Ekicweka ky'ensi) nibwingira omu nsi egi kandi nibwingira omunda yaitu.

Abazaire baitu bakahanga omubiri ogurikwetagisa kwakira obusinge obu buruga omu nyonyozi...

Twakora n'amaani ahaiguru yaitu kandi tukyemereza ahabagenzi baitu, turyagaruka turimu obusinguzi omu kifuba ekirikuteera ahaiguru ya Urania...

Itwe turi omuri ensi egi ahabw'ensonga, ahabw'ekintu, ahabw'ekintu ekikuru...

Nikimanyisa ngu omuriitwe hariho bingi ebi twine kureeba, kwega, n'okwetegyereza, twaba nitwetenga kumanya ekintu ahariitwe, aha magara gaitu...

Kikuru n'eky'okufa kw'omuntu orikufa atarikumanya ensonga y'amagara ge...

Buri omwe ahariitwe ashemereire kushangwa ensonga y'amagara ge, ekirikumugumya omu ekomera ry'obusaasi...

Nikwo kuturikwega ngu omuri buri omwe ahariitwe hariho ekirikutureetera obusaasi omu magara kandi twine kurwanisa n'amaani...

Tikirikwetagisa ngu tugume turi omu buzibu, nikyetagisa kugyezaho kubeeza omukungu oguturikutureetera obutoosha n'obusaasi.

Tihariho mugasho kwebengesa omutwe n'ebitiinisa, dipulooma, esente, okwetenga okwesiga, emicwe mirungi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Titukwihiirwe ngu oburyarya n'ebikarabata by'obutoosha by'omuntu wa beene, bitureetera kuba abantu ababiihi, abakuru, abarikureebya, abarikurwanisa, abatari kubasa kureeba ebintu bisya...

Okufa kurimu amakuru mangi, amakuru marungi n'amabii.

Tuteekateeke aha kureeba okurikukangaaza kwa "Omukuru KABIR Yesu Kristo". "Abafu babe nibaziika abafu baabo". Abantu baingi n'obu barikugira amagara, bafu ahabw'omurimo gwona ogurikubasika ahaiguru yaabo n'eky'okukyusa kimwe omunda.

Ni bantu abakwatirwe omu by'okwikiriza byaabo; abantu abahindukire omu kwijuka kwingi; abantu abajwire oburyarya bw'abakuru; abantu abaziguza ekirikubagambira, abatata, abarikwetenga, obumwe "abamanyi" abarikwikiriza ngu bari omu mazima ahabw'okuba bakagambirwa, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Tibarikwetenga kwetegyereza ngu ensi egi n' "Ekifo ky'Okukorera Emiyeeto" ekirikubasika kwihaho obubi obwo twena turikwetwaza omunda...

Abantu abo abahaya baakwetegyereza embeera embi ebi barimu, bakateterera...

Kwonka, abantu nk'abo nibateekateeka ebirungi ahari boona; nibeejumba ahabuhanda bwabo, nibeejumba nibahikiirire, abasaasizi, abakurisa, ab'amaani, abagabirizi, abamanya, abarikukora emirimo yaabo, n'ebindi.

Amagara g'okukora nk'essomero n'ekirikukangaaza kureka kugata nk'aha maisho, nikimanyisa obusaasi.

Abo abarikugata amagara, nk'oku barikugagira burizooba, tibarikwetegyereza obwetengo bw'okukora ahaiguru yaabo kwija "Okukyusa Okurungi".

Ekisaasi ngu abantu nibagira amagara baine emikara, tibarikuhurra ekintu aha bikorwa by'omunda...

Kukyusa nikyetagisa kureka abantu tibarikumanya oku barikukyukamu; nibagira obusaasi bwingi kandi tibarikumanya n'ensonga yaabo...

Kugira esente tihariho. Amagara g'abantu baingi abarikuhingura gabe ge n'agasaasi...
