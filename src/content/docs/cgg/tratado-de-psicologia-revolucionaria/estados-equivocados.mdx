Tikurikugurukana omu kuhendera omuntu, nikitureetera obutooreka enjawulo ezirikugyendera n'obwengye aha bikorwa eby'obuhangwa n'embeera z'omutima ogw'omuntu.

Nitwenda kumanya ahu twemereziire omu kasumi akatuhwera, twaba turi omu mbeera y'omutima, nari aha bikorwa ebiri haihi naitwe.

Obwomeezi bwonyini bweine ebintu ebi birikugyenda omumaiso omu bwire n'omwanya...

Omuntu akagira ati: "Obwomeezi n'omugwa, ogw'okubonabona ogu omuntu arikutwara atairwe omu mutima..."

Buli omweine obugabe bw'okuteekateeka eki arikwenda; inshabire ngu enyegyesereza ez'omwanya omwe zirikukurataho obusaasi n'obugaahe...

Buli kintu kyoona kyeine akamuri akakiima, kandi embeera z'omunda nazo nizihukana; eki nikyo mazima, tekirikuhakanisibwa...

Amazima, omulimo gw'omunda aha muntu gurikwegamira aha mbeera z'obwengye ezirikuhukana...

Tihariho omuntu orikubaasa kuhakana ngu omu nda yaitu nitwetwara enshobe nyingi kandi ngu haraaho embeera ezitagizire...

Twaba nitwenda kuhinduka, nitwenda kuhindura eby'obusaasi, n'embeera ezitagizire omu bwengye...

Okuhindura omu buryo bwona embeera ezitagizire, nikireeta okuhinduka omu by'obwomeezi...

Omuntu yaakora aha mbeera ezitagizire, ebirikubaasa kukukwataho omu bwomeezi tibirikukukwataho omu bwangutsya...

Nitugamba ekintu eki orikubaasa kwetegyereza waakigyezaho, okukiikiriza omu mazima...

Orikutakorera aha mbeera ze, naamutwara nk'omugugu omu maizi g'enyanja...

Ebintu nibihinduka omu miringo nyingi; nibizaho kimwe aha kindi omu makuru, nizo ntumwa...

Amazima haraaho ebintu birungi n'ebiibi; ebimwe birikubaasa kuba birungi nari bibi kukira ebindi...

Kuhindura ebimwe birikubaasa; Kujahura ebirikukurataho, kuhindura embeera, n'ebindi, biri omu bukuru...

Kwonka haraaho embeera ezirikubaasa kuhindurwa; omu mbeera enu, zigira zigirwe, nobworaaba niziteera omunaku...

Amazima obusaasi nibusaanawo twaba tutarikwegamira aha kibazo ekiriho...

Tusemeera kureeba obwomeezi nk'oruuruuza rw'embeera z'omunda; ebyafaayo eby'amazima eby'obwomeezi bwaitu nibikwatira hamwe embeera zona...

Ku turikugyendera aha mazima g'obwomeezi bwaitu, nitubaasa kwemeza omu muringo murungi, ngu embeera mbi zizireho ahabw'embeera z'omunda ezitagizire...

Alejandro Magno, nobworaaba akaba atarikwetenga byingi, akagwera omu miteekateeko emibi eyamureeta omubiiro...

Francisco I akahwa ahabw'obushambani obutaagizire, ebyafaayo nibimwijuka...

Marat ku yaaitirwe omukazi orikurwanisa, akaba naamutwara nk'omugabe...

Abakazi b'omu Parque de los Siervos bakamaraama amaani g'omushambani orikwetwa LUIS XV.

Abantu baingi nibahwa ahabw'omutima gw'okwenda obukuru, ekihagaro nari obufiira, eki abakugu nibakimanya...

Obugabe bwaitu ku buraamara kwemeza aha muringo, nituba abeesimire okugenda omu mataba nari omu ruburi...

Otelo ahabw'obufiira akahinduka omwiti kandi ekaari eijwiire enshobe.
