Kwetegyereza ebirooto byaitu omunda, tikirikwegomba turinde, na munonga obutatsiiturana n'embeera zaitu z'omutwe ezitagwire.

Tikirikukwata kubaire, embeera zaitu ez'omunda ezitagwire nizibaasa kukyezibwa kuraba omu miringo migwire.

Nk'oku amagara gaitu g'omunda gali nk'emheeri erikureeta ebintu by'aha rubaju, nitwegomba munonga kwihaho omu bitekerezo byaitu embeera z'omutwe ezitagwire.

Kukyeza embeera z'omutwe ezitagwire nikikuru munonga obu turikwenda kuhindura eby'obuhangwa bw'ebintu ebimwe ebiturikwenda kwemera.

Kuhindura omukago gwaitu n'ebintu ebimwe, nikibaasa kureetera obuzibu obu twihireho omu nda yaitu embeera z'omutwe ezitaine mugasho.

Entebe z'aha rubaju ezirikushwekyereza, nizibaasa kuhinduka ezitatyoora nari n'ezirikugira akamaro kuraba omu kukyeza embeera z'omunda ezitagwire.

Omuntu naabaasa kuhindura obuhangwa bw'ebintu ebirikunyeha ebitutwara obu twareetire obutuku omu nda yaitu. Omuntu atarikukyeza embeera z'omutwe ezitaine mugasho, orikwegamba ngu aine amaani muno naaba omu mbeera embi.

Kuteeka omu bukuru aha nju yaitu ey'omunda erimu obuhereza nikikuru, obu turikwenda kuhindura eby'obuhangwa bw'amagara ag'obusaasi.

Abantu nibeemereza aha byoona, nibabonabona, nibarira, nibagaaya, nibenda kuhindura amagara gaabo, kwihaho obusaasi obu barimu, ekikyehererwa tibarikukora aha bwabo.

Abantu tibarikwenda kumanya ngu amagara g'omunda nigareeta embeera z'aha rubaju kandi ngu obwo obusaasi buteera embeera z'omunda ezitaine mugasho.

Eby'aha rubaju n'ekyoona ekirikworeka eby'omunda, omuntu orikuhinduka omunda naareeta entebe ensya.

Ebintu by'aha rubaju tibirikubaasa kuba bikuru nk'oku turikukora aha byo.

Ogyenderere kwetegyereza orikukutuka? Oyakire ebintu ebirikukutwara ezirikureetwa abantu abarikukwegendereza?

Oyakozere ota aha butaikiriza bw'omukundwa waawe? Okaire kwetwara omu busaasi bw'enkyanya? Okaite? Ori omu ekomera?

Ebyaro, ebyara by'abafu nari panteooni, amakomera, bijwire abakukiriza abagwire abakozere omu muringo gurikutsitaza aha bintu by'aha rubaju.

Ekintu kirungi munonga omushaija arikubaasa kukoresa omu magara, n'embeera y'omutwe egwire.

Omuntu naabaasa kwetangira enyamaishwa kandi akagunuura abahingura, kuraba omu mbeera z'omunda ezirikukwatana.

Embeera z'omunda ezitagwire nituhindura abantu abarikubonabona abatatireho abarikukora obubi.

Yegyeere kwemerera aha by'obusaasi byona omu magara ag'obutoosha n'empindahinduka y'omunda egwire...

Mutarikwetegyereza aha kintu kyona; mujjukire ngu byoona nibitiita; yegyeere kureeba amagara nk'omu firimu kandi muyakiire emigisha...

Muteerarikurwa ngu ebintu ebitaine mugasho nibibaasa kubatwara omu busaasi, muraareke kwihaho omu bitekerezo byanyu embeera z'omunda ezitagwire.

Buli kintu ky'aha rubaju nikyegomba, buzima ebarafu erikukwatana; nikwo, embeera y'omutwe etaireho.
