Omu itaka ry’obwomeezi, itwe turoora enyanyaasi eziitutangaza. Abantu abagagga abaine enju nungi n’emikago mingi, obumwe nibagumirirwa munonga... Abakolera ahansi ab’ebyoma nari abantu ab’omu bwengye obucureezi, nibaitura bagumeera omu kushemererwa okuhwereire.

Abagagga munonga baingi nibagumirirwa obutaabaasa kukora omukago gw’obutunuuzi kandi abakazi abagagga nibarira munonga obutasiima bw’abaami baabo... Abagagga b’ensi nibasaasa nk’ebinyonyi by’enjaza omukagaali y’ezaabu, omu biro ebi tibarikubaasa kutuura hatariho “abarikubarikinga”… Abantu b’ebihanga nibatakira enyeegyere, tibariikiriza, nibagenda omu byona barikwetoronkanizibwa abantu abaine ebyoma bingi…

Twegye ekyetengo eki munonga. Twinganisa kumanya obwomeezi niki. Buli omwe ayikirizibwe kuteekateeka nk’oku arikwenda… Mugambe eki murikwenda kugamba, buzima tihariho omuntu oinahaki, obwomeezi nibureeta obuzibu obu hatariho omuntu orikwetegyereza…

Obu abantu barikwenda kutugambira eby’obwomeezi bwabo kwonka, nibahandiika ebyabaireho, amaziina n’amaziina ag’omuhanda, ebiro by’omwaka, nibahurra nibashemererwa obubarikukora ebyabaireho… Abantu abo abacureezi tibarikumanya ngu ebyabaireho tibihwereire ahabw’okuba ebyabaireho, amaziina n’ebiro by’omwaka, nikyo kyorekyerero kyonka ekya firimu, okureebeka omunda nikubura…

N’ekyanguhi kumanya “embeera y’amagezi”, ebyabaireho byona nikirukuratira embeera y’amagezi egi nari egi. Embeera ziri omunda kandi ebyabaireho biri aha rugyendo, ebyabaireho aha rugyendo ti byona…

Okwetegyereza embeera eri omunda n’obusinge obu buraingi nari bubi, okuteera amaganya, okucwamu amagezi, okukanya, okutiina, okuteebereza, okusaasira, okwetenga, okwetenga omuntu, embeera y’okuhurra oshemererwa, embeera y’okusemererwa, n’ebindi, n’ebindi, n’ebindi.

Tihariho kubanganisa ngu embeera eri omunda ebaasa kwemerereza ebyabaireho ebiri aha rugyendo nari ebyabuzireho ahabw’ego, nari obutaabaasa kukwatagana n’ebyo… Omu byona, embeera n’ebyabaireho biri orugyendo. Tikirikuhitsya embeera ezirikukurataniza embeera.

Embeera eri omunda ey’ebyabaireho ebyoshemererwa terikubaasa kwemerereza n’ego. Embeera eri omunda ey’ebyabaireho eby’obutahemererwa terikubaasa kwemerereza n’ego. Ebyabaireho ebyarindirirwe obwiire buraira, obu byaije twahurra ngu hakabura ekintu…

Buzima hakaba hakabura embeera eri omunda erikurugana n’ebyabaireho ebiri aha rugyendo… Emirundi mingi ebyabaireho ebitarikurindiriirwa nibyo birikureeta obwiire oburikukirayo.
