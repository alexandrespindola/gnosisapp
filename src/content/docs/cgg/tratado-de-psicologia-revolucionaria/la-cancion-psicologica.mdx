Obwire obu buhingwire tushemerire kuteekateeka munonga aha kintu eki beeta "okwebemberera omunda".

Tihariho okubanganisa kwona aha mbeera mbi ey' "okwebemberera omunda"; eki ekindi nikiremesa obwengye, kandi nikituremesa amaani maingi.

Omuntu yaaba atarikukora enshobi y'okweyegamira munonga, okwebemberera omunda kwaba kutarikuba nikukonzeka.

Omuntu yaayegamira, naba ayakunda munonga, naasaasira, naayebemberera, naateekateeka ngu abaireho omu muringo murungi munonga na fulano, na zutano, n'omukazi, n'abaana, n'ebindi, kandi tihariho omuntu omuzireeteire ekitinisa, n'ebindi. Mukuru we, ni mutukuvu kandi abandi boona ni abantu ababi, abahambi.

Emwe aha miringo erikukira obwingi ey'okwebemberera omunda ni okwehabura aha eki abandi barikubaasa kuteekateeka ahaitwe; obundi nibateekateeka ngu tituri abeesigwa, abamazima, ab'amazima, abeesimire, n'ebindi.

Ekirikukirayo kutaangaaza aha byoona ebi ni ngu titurikwetegyereza obuzaare bw'amaani oburikuzira omu mbeera egi y'okwehabura.

Enyikiriza nyingi z'obuzigu aha bantu abamwe abataratukoreire kibi kyona, zirikuruga omu kwehabura kw'omunda.

Omu mbeera ezi, kwenda munoongaa, okwebemberera omu muringo ogu, nikyetegyerezebwa ngu "IYE" nari tugambe "IYE" omu bwingi omu mwanya gw'okuhwereera nibaguma nibanywaniira omu muringo gw'obutiinisa.

Omuntu yaayegamira, naasaasira munonga embeera ye bwanye kandi naahika n'okubara.

Nikwo arikuteekateeka ngu fulano, ngu zutano, ngu omuhinguzi, ngu omuhiinguzibwe, ngu omujwara w'eishengyero, ngu mukama we, ngu omukundane, n'ebindi, tibamushiiremu nk'oku kirikusaana n'obu arikuba ari omurungi omu muringo ogurikwetegerezebwa kandi yaajwarira omu ki eki, naaba omutagumiisibwa kandi omucweki aha bantu boona.

Omuntu nk'ogu, tikirikukonzeka kugamba nawe ahabw'okuba okugamba kwona nikuramaraaha kuza omu kaatabo ke k'okubara n'obusaasi bwe obwingi obw'okwebemberera.

Kyahandiikwa ngu omu murimo gw'eby'okwombeka omwoyo ogwa Gnostic, n'obwengye nibubaasa kukura kurabira omu kusaasira abandi.

Omuntu yaaba naatuura buri kasumi, buri obwire, naagumiisibwa ebi bamubanziire, ebi bamukoreire, obusaasi obu bamureeteire, obutoosha na rureengo rwe rumwe, tihariho ekirikubaasa kukura omunda ye.

Eshara y'Omukama yaagira eti: "Tutusaasire amabanja gaitu nk'oku turikusaasira abaturembiise".

Ekyeteengo ky'okuba bamubanziire, obusaasi bw'ebibi ebi abandi bamukoreire, n'ebindi, nibiremesa obutsigazi bwona omunda y'omwoyo.

Yesu Omukuru KABIR, akagira ati: "Teeraniriza n'omuzigu waawe bwangu, omu muringo ogu orikuba noyeenda, hatariho omuzirika kukuha omucwezi, kandi omucwezi omutuuzi, kandi otwarirwe omu kihome. Bwangu nibimugambira ngu torikuza kuruga omwo, kuhitsya obwo orikushurura eikumi ry'aha mwisho". (Matayo, V, 25, 26)

Yaaba nitubanza, twine kubanza. Yaaba nitushaba ngu tushuurwe okuhitsya aha denario yaaha mwisho, twine kubanza kushuurura okuhitsya aha cuadrante yaaha mwisho.

Egi n' "Eteeka ly'Okwihooza", "Eisho ry'eisho n'eriino ry'eriino". "Omusingi omubi", ogutaine magezi.

Okusaba obusaasizi, okuhindura obukuru n'okweyereera ebi turikushaba abandi ahabw'ebibi ebi batureeteire, naitwe nitubishabwa n'obu turikuba nitwetegyereza obuziga obutagumire.

Okwefeerereza aha mateeka agatasaana tihaine magezi, orikukiraho ni okwefeerereza aha nteekateeka empya.

Eteeka ry'Embabarira n'enteekateeka erikukiraho okwombeka kuruga aha Eteeka ry'omuntu w'enjara: "Eisho ry'eisho, eriino ry'eriino".

Ni kikuru, nikyetengyesa, tikirikwemerereza, kwetwara omu bwingi aha nteekateeka erikutaangaaza ey'omurimo gw'eby'okwombeka omwoyo ogwa Gnostic, kwebwa ngu nitubanzibwe kandi tukuturire omu mwoyo gwaitu omuringo gwona gw'okwebemberera.

Titushemerire kwemerereza omunda yaitu, enyikiriza z'okwihooza, okugamba, enyikiriza mbi, okwehabura ahabw'ebibi ebi batureeteire, enjara, okugira omutima, okwijuka amabanja butoosha, n'ebindi.

Gnosis etwaarirwe abantu abarikwesiga abarikwenda kukora n'okuhindura.

Twarora aha bantu nitubaasa kureetera omuringo omutaanu, ngu buri omuntu aine orureengo rwe bwanye.

Buri omwe naareenga orureengo rwe rwa psicológica; ndikwenda kureetera omu muringo omukuru aha kibaazo ky'okubara psicológica; kwenda ngu bamubanza, okwemerereza, okwebemberera, n'ebindi.

Obumwe abantu "bareenga orureengo rwabo, batyo ahabw'eka", hatariho eki kiharira, hatariho eki kireetera, kandi omu bwire obundi bwona abanywereire obw'eviini…

Nitugira ngu orureengo rwaitu orukutagumiisibwa rushemerire kukuturwa; eki nikituremesa omunda, nikituremesa amaani maingi.

Omu bibaazo bya Psicología Revolucionaria, omuntu areenga kurungi munonga, —titurikureetera aha iraka ririkutaangaaza, nari aha kureenga kw'omubiri—, buzima tarikubaasa kuza ahaiguru ye; naaguma omu bwira burengire…

Omuntu oreetsirwe enyima enkongoota eziriho obusaasi tarikubaasa kuhindura Ekicweka ky'okuba omu Ndagano; tarikubaasa kuza ahaiguru y'eki arikuba.

Kuhika aha Kicweka Ekikuru ky'okuba omu Ndagano, ni kikuru kureka okuba eki ori; twine kuremwa okuba eki turi.

Twaguma nituba eki turi, tituribaasa kuhika aha Kicweka Ekikuru ky'okuba omu Ndagano.

Omu kibanja ky'amagara g'okukora nibihikaho ebintu ebikutaangaaza. Obumwe omuntu ahikaho obukundane n'omundi, n'ahabw'okuba kikyererezi kureenga orureengo rwe.

Obusaasi bukuru mbeera nk'ezo nizimara obwo omureengi ashabwa kucweka, kuhindura edisko, kugamba ekindi, n'ebindi.

Obwo omureengi agamba, naagenda ashaka omukundane omusya, omuntu orikwenda kumuhurikiza obwiire bwona.

Okwetegyereza nikishaba omureengi, omuntu orikumwetegyereza, nk'okurikwererezi kwetegyereza omuntu omundi.

Kwetegyereza omuntu omundi ni kikuru kwetegyereza omuntu.

Obusaasi bukuru abareengi abangi nibareenga orureengo rw'obutayetegyerezebwa kandi nibaroota ensi erikutaangaaza omu mwanya gwoona ogu bari abantu abakuru.

Kwonka abareengi boona tibari abantu, ekyo kiriho okusiga; tibareenga orureengo rwabo omu muringo omutaanu, kwonka omu bwire nibareenga.

Ni bantu abakoire munonga, abagumiisibwa munonga, nibeesima, nibateekateeka ngu amagara gababanziire byoona ebyo batarikubaasa kureetera.

Nibakuratira omu muringo gw'omunda oguriho obusaasi, okugumiisibwa kw'endala n'obucweki bw'obutiinisa, obukoowa obw'omunda nari okuhakanisa okurikubura omu nteekateeka.

Okuroraaho enkongoota eziri omu bwire nibituremesa omu muhanda gw'okukora omunda omukuru gw'okuba omukuru omu Ndagano.

Ekyo kiriho obusaasi enkongoota eziri omunda omu bwire, zirikureetwa omu buteekateeki ahabw'eki turikufeerereza.

Obwomeezi kw'okurora, kureetera omushana omunda yaitu, omu nzibira zaitu z'omunda.

Tihariho okuhindura kwona omunda okurikubaasa kubaho omu mwoyo gwaitu haatali okutwaarira omu mushana gw'okwerora.

Ni kikuru kwerora nk'oku ori, omu muringo gwoona oguri omutaanu, omu muringo gwe ngu orikutwara omu bantu.

Obwo ori omutaanu, "YE" omu miringo emitaanu, okuteekateeka omu miringo emitaanu, enyikiriza mbi, n'ebindi, nibareetera.

Toori omutaanu, gye otwaara obwo ori omutaanu. Ni kyererezi, ni omulingo, kuba watoora munoonga omu bwire bwona obwo ori omutaano. "YE" erikukira obubi n'ery'obutiinisa nibaetera obwo ori omutaanu.

Twakwenda kuhindura omu muringo gwona, twine kuhangazya obusaasi bwaitu.

Obumwe nitushabura obusaasi bwaitu omu kongoota omu muringo omutaanu.
