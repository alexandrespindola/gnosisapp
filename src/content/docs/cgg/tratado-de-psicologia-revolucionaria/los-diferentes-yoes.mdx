Enyama yoona eyo erikuteekateeka etarikumanyisa omuntu, buzima tiine omuntu omwe omwebembezi. Tikiriho okukikanya, okubura kw’obumwe bw’omutima omu Muntu nikwo kureetera oburemeezi n’obusaasi bwingi.

Omubiri gw’omuntu gukora nk’ekintu kimwe ekihikire kandi gukora nk’ekintu kimwe, kucweraaho ahabw’oburwaire. Kwonka, amagara gyoona agari omunda y’Omuntu tigari bumwe omu mutima. Ekikuru munonga, n’obu amashomero aga Seudo-Esoteric na Seudo-Ocultist gagamba ki, okubura kw’entebeekanisa y’omutima omunda y’omuntu weena.

Buzima, omu mbeera ezi, tihariho omurimo gw’obuhweezi nk’ekintu kimwe omu magara g’abantu. Omuntu, aha mbeera ye y’omunda, ni bingi omu mutima, omuganda gw’“Iyes”.

Abataahi abamanyirwe omu bunaku obu nibaramya “IYE”, nibamuhimbisa, nibamuteeka aha itambiro, nibamweta “ALTER EGO”, “IYE ORUKURU”, “IYE ORUKUTUKU”, n’ebindi, n’ebindi, n’ebindi. Tibarikwenda kumanya abakugu b’emyaka enziru ei turimu, ngu “IYE ORUKURU” nari “IYE ORUTO”, n’ebicweka bibiri by’Ego emingi.

Omuntu buzima taine “IYE ORUHOORHOIREHO” kureka emingi y’“IYES” ezitabaine mugasho. Enyama enyoro erikuteekateeka etarikumanyisa omuntu erashushana n’eka eyahembererire ahu omuhanda gw’omwene gw’eka guri, hariho abaheereza baingi abarikwenda obutoosha kuragira kandi bakakora eki barikwenda...

Enshobi erikuru ya Seudo-Esoterism na Seudo-Ocultism enyoro ni okuteekateeka ngu abandi baine nari ngu oine “IYE ORUHOORHOIREHO ERITAKAKORA” etaine okutandika nari enkomerero... Abarikuteekateeka batyo baaza kuba nibaroota kandi nibamanya nobukiraabe obwire bukye, baraashobora kworeka boona ngu Omuntu erikuteekateeka tori omwe obutoosha...

Enyama erikuteekateeka, okuruga aha bw’ekirikwenda omutima, neehinduka obutoosha... Okuteekateeka ngu omuntu yaayetwa Luis, obutoosha ni Luis, ni nka ekyeshongooro ekibi munonga... Omuntu ogwo owaayetwa Luis aine “IYES” endiijo omunda ye, Egos endiijo, ezirikwegambisa omu buteeka bwe omu biro bitari bimwe, n’obu Luis atakukunda obugaya, “IYE” endiijo omuwe—tumwete Pepe—nakunda obugaya kandi nikwo kirikugyenda...

Tihariho omuntu ori omwe obutoosha; buzima tikirikwetenga kuba mugugu munonga kumanya okuhinduka okutabarika n’okurwanangana kw’omuntu weena... Okuteekateeka ngu omuntu aine “IYE ORUHOORHOIREHO ERITAKAKORA” nikishushanisa okugira omuntu okutekateeka kubi nari otaine mpamuro.

Omunda y’omuntu weena harimu abantu baingi, “IYES” nyingi, eki kirashobora kwemeeserezebwa boona kandi omu muringo murungi omuntu weena oraroote, orikumanya...
