Tikirikutwara kubahwekyereza abasomi baitu, ngu haroho akacumulo k’okubara omunda yaitu...

Omu mazima akacumulo aka, tikari omubwire obwahwereire, n’obu omubwire ogurija...

Oyo ayenda kushangura akacumulo aka, ashemereire kukishera hati n’ahati, omunda ye, omu kasumi aka, hatariho akasumi akari omuhanda, nari akari enyima...

Enkondo ibiri ez’omushinga, eya Vertical na Horizontal ey’Omusaraba Orikwera, zishangaanwa, aha acumulo aka...

Nitushangwa obutoosha aha mihanda ebiri: ogwa Horizontal na Vertical...

Nikireebeka ngu ogwa Horizontal guli “omushana”, ahariyo “Vicente n’abantu boona”, “Villegas n’oyo wena orikuhika”, “Don Raimundo n’abantu boona”...

Nikyetegyereza ngu ogwa Vertical guri orikutaaniire; guni omugisha gw’abantu abakugwire obwengye, ogw’abareeburuzi...

Omuntu yaibuka itwe, yaakora ahari itwe, yaatakutegereraana n’ebizibu byoona n’obusaasi bw’obwomeezi, omu mazima araaba ari omuhanda gwa Vertical...

Omu mazima tikirikwangukira kwihaho enyehurira embi; okubura okutegereraana n’obwomeezi bwaitu; ebizibu by’emiringo yoona, eby’ebucuruzi, amabanja, okusasuura ebbaruha, eby’amaka, telefoni, amaizi, omushana, n’ebindi, n’ebindi.

Abantu abataakora, abantu abo ababuriire emirimo, omu mazima nibabonabona ahabw’okubura esente kandi kwerabira ensonga yaabo, kutatwariza, nari kutategereraana n’ekizibu kyabo, omu mazima nikiguma kigwiire.

Ababonabona, abarira, abantu abo ababaire abakugwire eky’obusaasi, okusasuurwa kubi omu bwomeezi, Obuhakanyi, obushiisha nari obubaire, omu mazima nibeerabira itwe, Obuntu bwaabo obw’omunda, nibatwariza hamwe n’obusaasi bwabo...

Omulimo ahari itwe guni omukoro mukuru gw’Omuhanda gwa Vertical. Titwakubaasa kwongera aha Muhanda gw’Okugomera okukuru, yaaba tutarikukora ahari itwe...

Omulimo ogu turikugambaho guli ogw’Omutima; nigukwata aha kuhindura akasumi aka turimu. Tushemereire kwega kutoora obwomeezi obutoosha...

Nk’eky’okureeberaho, omuntu orikukukuzibwa ahabw’ekizibu ky’omutima, eky’ebyefayo nari eky’obusiima omu mazima aheeragiire itwe...

Omuntu ogwo yaaba ayemerera akasumi, yaareeba embeera kandi akeegyesa kwijuka itwe kandi bwanyima akeega kwetegyereza amakuru g’emitwarize ye...

Yaatekateeka omucweera, yaatekateeka ngu byoona birahwa; ngu obwomeezi bushiisha, burahwaho kandi ngu okufa nikuhindura omukuufu byoona eby’obushubani bw’ensi...

Yaetegyereza ngu ekizibu kye okuruga aha mutwe tikirikukira “okukwata omuriro”, omuriro ogurikuhwa obuhooro, araareeba obwangu n’okutangaara ngu byoona bihindukire.

Kuhindura okukora omu muringo gw’ekimashine nikibaasika omu kureeberera amakuru n’okureeberera omunda kw’Obuntu...

Nikyetegyereza ngu abantu nibakora omu muringo gw’ekimashine aha mbeera ezitaaniire z’obwomeezi...

Abantu abagwiire! Nibatera kuba ebiitwa. Omuntu yaabahimbisa nibaseeka; omuntu yaabahumilia, nibabonabona. Nibagamba kubi omuntu yaabagamba kubi; nibagonza omuntu yaabagonza; tibarikuba ab’obusingye; abantu baabo baine amaani g’okubatwara okuruga aha kuseeka okuhika aha busaasi, okuruga aha matsi okuhika aha kubura amatsiko.

Buri muntu omwe aha bantu abo abarikugyenda omuhanda gwa Horizontal, afaanana n’ekicweka ky’ebicurangi, omuntu weena omu bantu baabo arikucuranga ekyo aayenda...

Oyo oraegye kuhindura emiringo y’ekimashine, omu mazima araateera omu “Muhanda gwa Vertical”.

Eki nikiragira empindahinduka omukuru omu “Mutindo gw’Obuntu” eky’omugisha ogurikutaaniire oguruga omu “Kugomera Omwoyo”.
