Omuntu n'ekyo ekikora obwomezi bwe. Ekyo ekigumiisiriza okukira okufa, bwo bwomezi. Ensonga niyo ey'ekitabo ky'obwomezi ekigulukuka n'okufa.

Oreebe ekigambo eki kuruga omu mutima gwa muntu, obutoosha bwoona omu bwomezi bwitu, buba buzima nk'ekifaananyi ekito ekirukworeka obwomezi bwitu bwona.

Kuruga omuri byona ebi nitubaasa kwegyesa ekigwiire: Omuntu ku atarikukora aha bwomezi bwe obw'eraijo, tarigwera kukora.

Obu tubanza kugamba ngu nitwenda kukora aha bwomezi bwitu, kandi titukora obw'eraijo kureka tukuriza enshooha, ekigambo kityo nikiba kiteekateeka obutakiine, ahabw'okuba omuri bwo obw'eraijo nibwo burimu ekifaananyi ky'obwomezi bwitu bwona.

Hariho orugambo orubi orurikugira ngu: "Otarikurinda aha'nshooha ekyo orikubaasa kukora obw'eraijo."

Omuntu ku arikugira ngu: "Nindyakora aha bwomezi bwange, aha'nshooha," tarigwera kukora aha bwomezi bwe, ahabw'okuba obutoosha buriho enshooha.

Eki kyakuba nk'omugyemeso, endangiriro rundi eky'okweegyesa eki abaguzi abamwe barikuhandiika omu maduuka gaabo: "OBW'ERAIJO TIINDIIBAZA, AHA'NSHOHA NINDIIBAZA."

Obu omuntu arimu obutazi arata omukisa gw'okuhenda, naataana n'omugyemeso ogwo, kandi ku agaruka enshaaho egi, naashanga endangiriro egi.

Ekyo nikyo ekirikwetwa omu mutima gw'omuntu "oburwaire bw'enshooha." Obu omuntu arikugira "enshooha," tarigwera kuhinduka.

Nitwetaga kukora aha bwomezi bwaitu obw'eraijo, tutarikuroota omu bumareere omu biro by'omumaisho rundi omukisa ogutari gwa buliijo.

Abo abarikugira ngu: "Ndiya kuba mbabaziire kukora eki rundi kile, reero nkakora." Tibarigwera kukora aha bwomezi bwabo, abo nibo abantu abarikutuura omu nsi abarikugambwaho omu Byahandiikwa Ebirikwera.

Nkamanya omutungi omunene orikugira ngu: "Nyetegeera okukwata aha mpenda ze, reero nkakora aha bwange."

Obu yarwarire omurwaire gw'okufa nkamukoratira, reero nkamubuuza ekibuuzo eki: "N'obundi noriinda kukwata aha mpenda ze?"

"Ndi omurembe kubanga nkayereza obwire," akangarukamu. Enshooha zimwe zikabaho akacweka, ahire'nganiza okukora enshonga ye.

Omuntu ogwo akatungire eitaka linene, baitu akatungire kwejumba omu by'abantu ab'omuhanda, "okwejumba" reero yateerera ebintu byabo omuri abanyazi.

"Basa ekizibu kigwiire obutoosha obwo!", nikwo KABIR YESU akagira. Kweetegereza obw'eraijo, omu kuzaana aha ky'obutareebwa, ekifaananyi ky'obwomezi bwitu bwona.

Obu omuntu atandika kukora aha bwomezi bwe, obw'eraijo obu arikureeba enaku ze n'ebiremwa, arikugyenda omu muhanda gw'omugisha.

Tikurikubaasa kusoboka okukiraho eki twareebezire. Tuteekwa kureeba enshonga zaitu obutakiine.

Nitwetaga kutamanya omutoosha gwitu obutakiine, kureka n'omukago oguri hagati yaabyo. Hariho omutoosha gwa buliijo ogu omuntu weena araata omu bunyazi, okutaana n'ebintu ebirikubaasa kubaho, ebitari bya buliijo.

Kikuru kureeba ekirikugarukwamu obutoosha, okugarukwamu kw'ebigambo n'ebintu, kw'omuntu weena, n'ebindi.

Okugarukwamu kw'ebintu n'ebigambo, nikikwanisa kwegyezibwa, nikitutwara okwetegyereza.
