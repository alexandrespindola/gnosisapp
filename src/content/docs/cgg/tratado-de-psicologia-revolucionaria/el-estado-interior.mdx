Okugatta embeera y'omunda n'ebintu ebiri ebweru mu ngeri entuufu, kwe kumanya okutunga obulamu mu magezi...

Ekintu kyonna ekibeereddwamu mu magezi kyetaaga embeera y'omunda ekijjanika...

Naye, eky'ennaku abantu bwe beekenneenya obulamu bwabwe, balowooza nti bulamu bwabwe bwokka bwe bw'ebintu ebiri ebweru...

Abantu abanaku! balowooza nti singa ekintu kino oba ekyo tekyabatuukiriddeko, obulamu bwabwe bwandibadde bulungi...

Balowooza nti omukisa gwabayita era nti baafiirwa omukisa okuba abasanyufu...

Banakuwalira ebyo bye baafiirwa, bakaaba ebyo bye baanyooma, basinda nga bajjukira ensobi z'edda n'ebikyamu...

Abantu tebaagala kutegeera nti okuweerera si kwe kubeerawo era nti obusobozi okubeerawo mu bweteefu buteesigamye ku bulungi bw'embeera y'omunda ey'Omwoyo...

Tewali nsonga mazima nga obulungi bw'ebintu eby'ebweru bw'obulamu bwe bunene, singa tetuli mu mbeera y'omunda etuufu mu kiseera ekyo, ebintu ebisinga obulungi bisobola okutulabika ng'ebiggyawo essanyu, ebinafuya oba ebinafuusa...

Omuntu alindirira embaga n'essanyu, kye kintu, naye kiyinza okubaawo ng'ali mu bweraliikirivu bungi mu kiseera ekituufu eky'ekintu, nga tamanyi ssanyu lyonna mu kyo era nga byonna bifuuka nkalu era nnyogovu nga protokol...

Obumanyirivu butuyigirizza nti si bantu bonna ababeera ku mbaga oba okuzina, basanyuka mu mazima...

Tewabulawo atagenda kunyumirwa mu mikolo egisinga obulungi era n'ebintu ebirungi bisanyusa abamu ne bakaabya abalala...

Batono nnyo abantu abamanyi okugatta mu bweteefu ekintu ekiri ebweru n'embeera y'omunda entuufu...

Kinnaku nti abantu tebamanyi kutunga mu bweteefu: bakaaba bwe baba balina okuseka era baseka bwe baba balina okukaaba...

Okufuga kwawukana: Omuntu omumanyi alina okusanyuka naye si lujjuluko lwabalaazi; Munaku naye si mu kwesiba emizimu na kuwamma; mukkakkamu wakati mu bukambwe; muteefi wakati mu bwenzi; muyonjo wakati mu bugwagwa, n'ebirala.

Abantu abeeraliikirivu era abalootooboto balowooza ebibi ku bulamu era mu butuufu tebaagala kubeerawo...

Buli lunaku tulaba abantu abatali basanyufu bokka, naye okusinga byonna, bafuula obulamu bw'abalala okuba obukambwe...

Abantu ng'abo tebandikyuka ne bwe bandibeeredde mu mbuga buli lunaku; obulwadde bw'omutwe babutambuliza munda... abantu ng'abo balina embeera ez'omunda ezikyamu ddala...

Naye abantu abo beeyita abatuukirivu, abatukuvu, abalungi, abakungu, abawereza, abafiiriddwa, n'ebirala...

Be bantu abeeyogerera nnyo; abantu abeeyagala nnyo...

Abantu abeekolera ekisa era abanoonya obuwonvu okwewala obuvunaanyizibwa bwabwe...

Abantu ng'abo bamenyera ku mbeera ez'omunda ez'omunda era kirambika nti lwa nsonga eyo batonda buli lunaku ebintu eby'omwoyo ebitali bya bantu.

Ebintu ebibi, enkyukakyuka z'omukisa, obwavu, amabanja, obuzibu, n'ebirala, bya bantu abo abatamanyi kutunga...

Buli muntu ayinza okwekulakulanya mu magezi mangi, naye batono nnyo abantu abayize okutunga obulamu mu butuufu...

Omuntu bw'ayagala okwawula ebintu eby'ebweru ku mbeera ez'omunda ez'omanya, alaga mu butuufu obutabaawo bwe okubeerawo mu kitiibwa.

Abo abayiga okugatta mu bweteefu ebintu eby'ebweru n'embeera ez'omunda, batambula mu kkubo ery'obuwanguzi...
