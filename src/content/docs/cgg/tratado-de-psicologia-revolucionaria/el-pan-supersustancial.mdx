Twagyezaho kureeba buli lunaku rw'obwomeezi bwaitu, turyareeba ngu buzimazima titumanyi kutoora obwomeezi omu muringo gw'okwetegyereza.

Obwomeezi bwaitu nibworeeka nk'egaari y'omuriro erikugyenda, erikugyenda omu mihanda etaterwa y'emikora emikuru, emikara, y'obwomeezi obutariho mporo kandi obutakiriire.

Ekintu ekirikushusha omu nshonga egi n'okuba ngu titurikugira akateekateeka k'okuhindura emikora, ninkoreeka ngu titurikucwa omutima kwongyera kuhayo ebintu eby'omuringo gumwe obutoosha.

Emikora netuhinga, kureka nituteekateeka ngu tuli abasheegu; tuli ababi munonga kureka nitwezirika nka Apolos...

Tuli abantu ab'ekintu, ensonga erikukiraaho kwerarikiriza kw'obwomeezi.

Nitugyenda buli izooba omu muhanda ogwekare ogw'emikora yaitu etarikukora n'etarikugira makuru kandi eki nikireetera ngu tutagira obwomeezi buzimazima; omu mwanya gw'okutoora obwomeezi, nitubumbura kimishe obutoosha, kandi titurikwakiira obuhangwa busya.

Omuntu ku araatandikye eizooba rye omu muringo gw'okwetegyereza, nikworeka ngu eizooba eryo ririba ririkumanyisa munonga kuruga omu mizooba endiijo.

Omuntu ku araakwate obwingana bw'obwomeezi bwe, nka eizooba eryo rikutoora, ku ararekye eizooba ryenyomunsi ekyo ashemereire kukora obwe, naija kumanya buzimazima ekyo kirikumanyisa kukora aha bwomeezi bwe.

Tihariho eizooba eririkubura obukuru; buzimazima nitwenda kuhinduka kimazima, twine kureeba, kureeba kandi kutegyereza buli izooba.

Kureka, abantu tibarikwenda kureeba abantu abandi, abamwe bagira eki barikwenda kukora aha bwomeezi bwabo, nibajeemesereza obugwate bwabo n'ebigambo nk'ebi: "Omurimo omu ofiisi tigurikwemerera kukora aha bwomeezi bw'omuntu". Ebigambo ebi tibiriho, byabuusa, byeshamba, ebirikukora kwemerereza obugwate, obugayaavu, okubura okukunda Enshenga enkuru.

Abantu ab'emiringo egi, nangwa bagira ebibazo by'omwoyo bingi, nikworeeka ngu tibarikuhinduka.

Kureeba nka itwe tibwerarikiriza, tikirikujanjaaza, tikirikugirwa enshonga endiijo. Okwetegyereza kw'okunda nikukuru aha hinduka ezimazima.

Obwomeezi bwawe burikugamba ki okurahuka? Obwomeezi bwawe burikugamba ki omu kyakura? Wakaba otarikugumisiriza n'omukazi? Ahabwaki wakaba otarikugumisiriza? Kiki ekirikukutabagura obutoosha? n'ebindi.

Okutamiira n'okurya kutooro tabyo hinduka zoona, kureka nibiranga obukuru. Nitumanya ngu obugayaavu n'obugomi nibitabugana kandi nibishusha abanyamaishwa.

Tikirungi ngu omuntu orikwegyenda omu muhanda gw'ekyama, agira omubiri gw'enyama, ogwaibwa munonga kandi agira endaa endiito kandi erikuruga omu kuhingura kw'okuhikiriira. Ekyo kirikuranga obugomi, ebintu ebi abantu barikukunda okurya hamwe n'obugayaavu.

Obwomeezi obwa buli izooba, omurimo, akagugu, nangwa by'obwingana aha bwomeezi, nibigira ebirooto by'okwetegyereza.

Kumanya ngu obwomeezi n'ekirooto tikirikushusha kutegyereza. Okutegyereza nikwiija n'okwetegyereza kandi n'omurimo mwingi aha bwomeezi bw'omuntu.

Aha kukora aha bwomeezi bwawe, nikikuru kukora aha bwomeezi bwawe obwa buli izooba, obwe, kandi noija kutegyereza ekyo kirikumanyisa ebigambo by'Omusaija w'Omukama: "Tuhe emigaati yaitu eya buli izooba".

Ebigambo "Buli Izooba", nibikumanyisa "Omugaati ogurikukira ebindi byoona" omu Kiguriiki nari "Omugaati gw'aha Iguru".

Obwomeezi nibuha omugaati ogw'Obwomeezi omu muringo ogw'okubanza ogw'ebiteekateeka n'amaani ebirikutureetera kucwekyereza enshobi z'omwoyo.

Buli omwe twegyerize aha kitabu ky'abantu beine obwomeezi busya, nitutunga obwomeezi bw'omwoyo, turya "Omugaati gw'Obwengye", twakiira okumanya kusya.

Obwomeezi nibutuha "Omugaati ogurikukira ebindi byoona", "Omugaati gw'Obwengye", kandi nibuturanga munonga obwomeezi busya oburikutanira omuntu, omunda y'omuntu, aha nihati.

Obwe, hatariho orikubaasa kuhindura obwomeezi bwe nari kuhindura ekintu kyona ekirikukwata aha bikorwa by'ekintu eky'okugyenda, kwihaho ku araabe aine obuhanguzi bw'ebiteekateeka bisya kandi yakire obuyambi bw'Omukama.

Obwomeezi nibuha ebiteekateeka ebi kandi nibwegyesa "oburinganiza bw'okukora" oburikureetera omuntu kubyambwa Amaani agarikukira aha miteekateeka.

Nitwetenga kutebeekanisiza ebindi eby'omubiri gwaitu kukwakiira ebiteekateeka n'amaani ebirikuruga omu bitabo ebirikukira.

Omu murimo gw'okukora aha bwomeezi bw'omuntu tihariho ekintu ekirikugirwa obugayaavu. Ekiteekateeka kyona nangwa kiibe kikye, kishemereire kureebwa. Ekijujwa kyona ekibi, ekikorwa, n'ebindi, bishemereire kureebwa.
