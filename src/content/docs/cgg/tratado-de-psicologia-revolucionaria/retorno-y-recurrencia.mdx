Omushaija ni eki amagara ge gali, omushaija yaaba atarahindura kantu omu biteekateeko bye, yaaba atarahindura amagara ge omu muringo mukuru, yaaba atarikukora ahari we, aba ari kwonka obwire bwe omu muringo mubii.

Okufa nikugaruka aha kutandika kw'amagara ge n'omugisha gw'okugarukamu omurundi ogundi.

Ebingi bikagambwa omu bitabo by'obushwere bw'ebishuba n'obuteekateeko bw'ebishuba, aha nshonga y'amagara agarikukuratanisa, kirungi twijukire amagara agarikukuratanisa.

Amagara ga buri omwe ahariitwe n'obwire bwe bwona nigo garaamwe buri obwiire nigagarukamu kuruga omu magara kugyenda omu magara, okurabira omu kyasha kingi.

Tikirikugurutsya nitukugumizamu omu nshozi y'abazukuru baitwe; eki kintu ekimaze kworekwa.

Amagara ga buri omwe ahariitwe omu muringo gw'omutaano, ni firimu erikuhuriiriza, twatwaara ebyaro ebi omu butahwa.

Buri omwe ahariitwe atwaara firimu ye kandi agaruka nayo kureeta kugi yereka omurundi ogundi aha kikoomi ky'amagara mapya.

Okugarukamu kw'ebizibu, okusekura n'eby'obusaasi, n'ekigambo kigumire ky'Eitwaare ry'Okugarukamu.

Omu magara mapya goona embeera zimwe nigo zigarukamu obwiire bwona. Abakwata omu by'okureetera ebyo bigarukemu obwiire bwona, n'abantu abarikutuura omu nda yaitu, "Abee".

Twazaana abaakwata, "Abee" aba nibareeta ebintu ebigarukamu obwiire bwona omu magara gaitu, okugarukamu kw'ebintu ebyo kwaba kwaba kurikukiraho kukakika.

Omu buringaniza hatariho abaakwata hatirikubaasa kubaaho ebintu; eki kintu tikirikubaasa kuhakanwa.

Niyo muringo turikubaasa kwijuka omu Mateeka g'Okugaruka n'Okugarukamu; niyo muringo turikubaasa kwemerera mazima.

Omu buringaniza buri omwe omu bantu (Abee) aba turikwetwaara omunda yaitu, agarukamu omu magara kugyenda omu magara omurimo gwe gumwe; twazaana, abaakwata baafa omurimo gwahendera.

Kuteekateeka omu mazima aha Iteeka ry'Okugarukamu nari okugarukamu kw'ebintu omu Bugaruki bwa buri omwe, nitushanga okureeba aha munda yaitu, eby'omuhendo by'ekibuzo eki.

Obu twabaire turi omu magara agahingwireho aha myaka makumi abiri n'etaano (25), tukaba twine omukago gw'obushwere nikyeyorekwa ngu "Bee" y'omukago ogwo neeshanga omukazi w'endooti ze aha myaka makumi abiri n'etaano (25) y'amagara mapya.

Omukazi oyo obwo akaba aine myaka ikumi na etaano (15) gye, "Bee" y'omukago ogwo neeshanga ogwo arikukunda omu magara mapya aha myaka egyo gumwe.

Nikireebeka kyetengo kumanya ngu "Abee" bombi ye nawe, nibashanga omu buteekateeko kandi bagashanga omurundi ogundi kugarukamu omukago gumwe gw'omukago gw'obushwere gw'amagara agahingwireho...

Abalwani babiri aba barwaanire kumara omuntu omu magara agahingwireho, nibeeshanga omurundi ogundi omu magara mapya kugarukamu eby'obusaasi byabo aha myaka ehikire.

Abantu babiri baaba baine enkaika y'eby'omutungo aha myaka makumi ana (40) omu magara agahingwireho, aha myaka egyo gumwe nibeeshanga omu buteekateeko omu magara mapya kugarukamu ekintu kimwe.

Omunda ya buri omwe ahariitwe hatuura abantu baingi abaijwire obwemereza; ekyo tikirikubaasa kuhakanwa.

Omubi agaruka omunda y'empanga y'ababi n'obwemereza butari bumwe bw'ebibi. Omwiti ahendera omunda ye "ekibiina" ky'abiti n'omujurizi atwaara omu biteekateeko bye "Enju y'Okushangaanira".

Ekibi ekikuru omu byona ebyo n'okumanya kureetera okugaya okubaho kw'abantu abariyo nari "Abee" omunda ye gumwe n'obwemereza obwo ebi nibikora omu muringo gw'okufa.

Obwemereza bwona bw'Abee aba nibatuura omunda yaitu, nibukuratanisa ahansi y'okuteekateeka kwaitu.

N'ebintu ebi turikugaya, ebintu ebi tubaire nitukora, ebiro ebi birikukora omu bwengye n'obutamanya.

N'enshonga enungi, twagambirwa ngu byona nibituhikiiraho, nk'obu ekira nikitonye nari nk'obu okubaaramu kurikukuba.

Omu mazima twine okwetenga kukora, kwonka titurikukora kantu, nibituhikiiraho, eki nikifa, n'enkorera...

Obuntu bwaitu ni kintu ky'abantu (Abee) abataari bamwe, okuraba omu muringo ogwo buri omwe omu bantu (Abee), nikakora obwemereza bwaabo.

Ahansi y'obushoboorozi bwaitu bw'okumanya ebintu bingi nibikora, kukibabaza titurikukimanya eki kirikukora ahansi y'okuteekateeka kwaitu kutoondye.

Nitwetenga abagabe obu omu mazima titurikubaasa kumanya ngu titurikubaasa kumanya.

Turi ebito by'ebiti ebitoondye, ebirikwetaagisa enyanja y'amagara y'amahembe.

Kuruga omu kabi aka, omu butamanya obu, omu mbeera erikukazaaza obu twashangirwe, nikibaasika kwonka omu kufa omunda yaabo...

Turikwemerera tutarikufa ira? Kwonka okufa nikwija n'ekikuru! Enyanzi yaaba etarafeire ekimera tikiriibaara.

Oriikwemerera omu mazima amanyira ahabw'enshonga egyo okwetenga burikwija omu biteekateeko bye, okumurikira okw'amazima, okushemererwa...
