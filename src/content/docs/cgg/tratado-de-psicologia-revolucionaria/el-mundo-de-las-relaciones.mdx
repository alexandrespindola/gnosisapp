Ensi y'obuntu erimu emisingi eshatu egishemire kumanywa.

Ekisooka: Turi abagwebane n'omubiri gw'ensi. Nikyo kugamba n'omubiri ogw'omubiri.

Ekyakabiri: Turi ab'okugara omu nsi kandi omu bwengye tukagwebane n'ensi ehandi omulimo gw'amazooba aga buri izooba, amaka, sente, omulimo, eby'obushagiki, eby'obufuzi n'ebindi bingi.

Ekyakashatu: Obugwebane bw'omuntu nawe. Abantu abangi tibarikuteekateeka aha mugwebane ogu.

Eky'obusaasi, abantu niboolekyera eby'obugwebane ebibiri eby'enyima kandi tibarikufayo aha ky'obugwebane ekyakashatu.

Eby'okulya, obushemezi, sente n'obushuba nibyo bikuru eby'omuntu ogwo owerowooryo akwitwa omuntu.

Baitu: Kikuru kumanya ngu omubiri ogw'omubiri n'ebintu by'ensi biri ahagati yaaitu.

Omubiri gw'ensi (omubiri ogw'omubiri), obumwe n'egwara, obumwe guri murungi kandi nikwo kiri.

Nitwikiriza ngu twine obwengye bwingi aha mibiri yaitu, baitu mukuri, n'abantu ab'obwengye tibarikumanya bwingi aha mibiri yaitu.

Tihariho kubuusabuusa ngu omubiri gw'omubiri, ahabw'okuba guri mwingi kandi mugumu, guri nk'ahagati y'obwengye bwaitu.

Eky'akabiri ky'obugwebane, itwe turatsindwa emibeera; kigirwa kubi kuba tukataire kwega kureeta emibeera turikukimanya.

Haroho abantu abangi abatarikubaasa kwegaita nari omuntu weena nari kugira obusinguzi omu magara.

Okuteekateeka ahari boora okurugiirira aha mulimo gw'okwegamwa, nikikuru kumanya eky'obugwebane eki turikubura.

Nikibaasa kubaho eky'okubeera turikugwebana obubi n'omubiri kandi tukagwara.

Nikibaasa kubaho ngu turi kugwebana obubi n'ensi ehandi kandi tukagira obutahangane, oburemeezi bw'ebya'obushuba, n'ebya'obuhangwa, n'ebindi bingi.

Nikibaasa kubaho ngu turi kugwebana obubi n'aboora kandi tukabonabona ahabw'okubura kw'omusana omu mutima.

Ekiroraire, omutaara gw'eka yaitu gubaire guteekwate omu mashini ey'amasanyu, eka yaitu ebaire omu mwirima.

Abarikubonabona ahabw'okubura kw'omusana omu mutima, bashemire kuteeka emitwe yaabo aha Matendeka agari ahaiguru aga Omuntu waabo.

Nitushemire kureeta obugwebane oburungi, hatariho n'omubiri gwaitu (omubiri ogw'omubiri) n'ensi ehandi, baitu na buri kimwe aha bintu byaitu.

Abagwire n'ab'amagezi abahukire abasaija b'ekirokota n'emibazi, tibarikwenda kucura kandi abarwayi abakutendera nibarwanira kukora.

Omu Casino ya Montecarlo abagwiire abangi abaabwire eby'obushuba byabo, bakaitwa. Bamaama abangi abakeni nibakora kugumya abaana baabo.

Hariho abantu abangi abarikwenda baitu abaire bakurwa ahabw'okubura amaani g'omutima n'omusana omutima, beebemberire omulimo gw'okwegamwa ahari boora. Bakeene abarikumanya oku bareeta emibeera emirungi.

Omu bwiire bw'okutendwa okw'amaani, okukurwa n'oburugwa, omuntu ashemire kwikiriza okwibuka.

Omu mutima gwitu haho TONANZIN Azteca, STELLA MARIS, ISIS Egipcia, Nyina Ruhanga, aturindiriire kucura emitima yaitu.

Obu omuntu yaheereza okwikaraza kw'okwibuka, ekyo kireeta enkyukira y'amaani omu mulimo gw'omubiri, ekireetera ebicweka by'omubiri kwakiira eby'okulya ebindi.
