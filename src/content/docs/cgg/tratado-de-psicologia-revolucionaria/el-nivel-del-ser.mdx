Turi baani? Turuga nkahi? Turaagye nkahi? Ahabwaki turikugumaho? Ahabwaki turikutuura omu bwomeezi?...

Tikiri kukaanisa ngu "Enyama y'Obwengye" ehikire kweetwa omuntu omwoojo, tikirikumanya, kureka atarikumanya n'okumanya ngu tarikukimanya...

Ekirikukirayo kubi n'embeera egumire kandi ey'omutaano ei turimu, titurikwetegyereza ekyama ky'obusaasi bwaitu bwona kwonka tukaine obuhame ngu nitumanya byona...

Otwale "Enyama Y'Amaazi", omuntu omu ahari abo abarikwezirika omu bwomeezi ngu nibaine obushoboorozi, omu itenga ry'e Sahara, omureke ahareehi n'obwo oreebe kuruga omu ndege ebirikubaho byona...

Ebyakozirwe nibiragira; "Omuntu Wenka Ow'Obwengye" n'obu araagirire ngu ni mwingi kandi akabaasa kuba omushaija munungi, omu mutima agumire aba munafu muno...

"Enyama Y'Obwengye" n'omutaahi omu kikumi omu kikumi; Akatekateeka ebiri ahikire ahari we; n'ayikiriza ngu naabaasa kwegumira arikukora nungi ahabw'Ekibina Ky'Abaana, Ebitabo by'Obukuru, Amashomero g'Eby'okubanza, Aga Secondary, Aga Bachillerato, University, eiziina rirungi rya taata, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Eky'obusaasi, enyima y'ebaruha nyingi nk'ezo n'obukuru oburungi, ebikumbo n'emishaani, tumanyire kurungi ngu obusaasi bw'omunda butuhuriiza obusaasi kandi omu mutima nitugumizamu kuba abataahi n'abasaasi...

Kira kuhuura Ebyafaayo by'Ensi Yoona okumanya ngu tuli ababarika abamwe ab'omubusingye kandi omu kifo ky'okukora nungi twahinduka ababi kubi...

Ekyaagana eki ekya 20 n'oburungi bw'amaani bwona, entaro, obumaraya, obushambani bw'ensi yoona, okukora ebintu ebitali birungi omu by'obushambani, ebinwagwa, etima erikukirayo, okukora ebintu bibi ebirikukirayo, okuburira, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ekyo ekireebi eki twine kwereebamu; tihariho ensonga ehikire nk'okwezirika ngu turaagiire omu mbeera erikukiraho ey'okukura...

Okutekateeka ngu obwire nibumanyisa okukura n'ekihama, eky'obusaasi "abataahi abashomereire" nibagumizamu kuguma omu "By'okwikiriza by'Okukura"...

Omu mpapura z'omwirima zoona ez' "Ebyafaayo Eby'Omwirima" tusangamu obutombo bwenka obuburire, ekyetengo, entaro, n'ebindi.

Kwonka ab'omu bunaku bwaitu "Abaashemezibwe Muno" nibakiri kwikiriza ngu ekyo ky'Entaro n'ekintu ekikuru, akabenzi akarikwiita ekitaine kakwate n' "Okushemeza kw'Ensi y'Omurembe" okurikukundwa...

Amazima ekikuru n'omuringo buri muntu arikubaasa kuba; abantu abamwe nibabaasa kuba abatamiizi, abandi abarikwezirika, abo abarikukora kurungi naabaaba abandi abatari barungi; byona biri omu bwomeezi...

Ekibiina n'okugattira hamwe kw'abantu; ekyo omuntu arikubaasa kuba n'ekibiina, n'Obugabe, n'ebindi.

Ekibiina ekikumirwe ekirikwenda omuntu; tikirikubaasika okuhindura ebibiina, abantu, ku omuntu, ku buri muntu, atahindukire...

Tihariho orikubaasa kukaana ngu hariho emitendera y'obushoboorozi erikushaanana; hariho abantu b'ekiroko n'ab'eka y'obumaraya; ab'eby'obushuubuzi n'ab'omu ishanga, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

N'ahabw'ekyo hariho Emitendera y'Obuntu erikushaanana. Eki turi omunda, ab'oburungi rundi ab'obusaasi, abagabi rundi abanyami, abakali rundi abateefu, abaine obwerengo rundi ab'obushambani, nikireeta embeera ezirikushaanana z'obwomeezi...

Omuntu w'obushambani obuto nikireeta obuto, obusaasi naabaaba obutombo bw'obushambani omu buriyo araagye...

Omutamiizi nikireeta abatamiizi kandi araagye omu migoongo n'omu bbaa, ekyo kiri kumanywa...

Niki ekiraareeta omuntu arikutunga enyima y'abantu, omweziriki? Oburemeezi obuingi ki, amakoomera, eby'obusaasi?

Kwonka abantu abarugaine, abaine omugisha ogw'okubonabona, baine ekyetengo ky'okuhinduka, okuhindura empapura y'ebyafaayo byabo...

Abantu abaasi! Nibenda kuhinduka kandi tibarikumanya oku; tibarikumanya omuringo; bagwire omu muhanda ogutaine murungo...

Ekyabaho nyomushana nikibaho eriizooba kandi nikiraabaho nyenkya; nibazooka enshobe zimwe kandi tibarikwega eby'okwegaho by'obwomeezi n'obu oraasindikiize...

Ebintu byona nibyizooka omu bwomeezi bwabo; nibagamba ebintu bimwe, nibakora ebintu bimwe, nibarugaaza ebintu bimwe...

Okugyezaho oku kw'obusaasi okw'ebitombo, okusekesya n'obusaasi, nikugumizamu obwire bwoona nitureetaga omu mutima gwaitu ebintu ebitarikukundwa by'obusungu, okwetenga, obushambani, obujuna, okwezirika, obureezi, okutamira, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi.

Omutendera gwaitu murungi ki?, rundi tushemereire kugira: Omutendera Gwaitu gw'Obuntu ki?

Obwire boona Omutendera gw'Obuntu gutahindukire muno, okwizooka kw'obusaasi bwaitu bwona nikugumizamu, ebintu ebitarikushemeza, eby'obusaasi n'obugwagwa...

Ebintu byona, embeera zoona, ezirikubaho kuruga ahagati yaaitu, omu kibanja ky'ensi, nibyo byonka ebirikureebeka ebi turikureeta omunda.

N'enshonga ehikire nitubaasa kugamba aha mazima ngu "ebiri aheeru nibyo ebirikureebeka eby'omunda".

Omuntu ku arikuhinduka omunda kandi okuhinduka okwo kukaba kw'amaani, ebiri aheeru, embeera, obwomeezi, nibihinduka.

Ndimu ndikureeba obwire obu, (Omwaka gwa 1974), ekibiina ky'abantu abarikwiba itenga ry'abandi. Hano omu Mexico abantu abarikwenda batyo nibatungwa eiziina ririkurusha amaani erya "AB'EKIYOMBO".

Bari abaturanyi b'omu kikungu Campestre Churubusco, bari haihi muno n'eka yangye, ensonga enu enshonga ehi nabaasa kubega haihi...

Okuba omuhingyi tikirikubaasa kuba omusango, kureka ekirikukirayo kubi tikiri omuri ekyo, kureka omu Mutendera gwabo gw'Obuntu...

Buri izooba nibarwana hagati yaabo, nibatamira, nibajuma omwanya hamwe, nibahinduka abajoozi b'abanywani baabo benka ab'obugwagwa, mazima nibatuura omu by'obusaani ebirimu omu mwanya gw'okukunda obwigomi buriyo butegyeka...

Omurundi gwingi ndikutekateeka ngu ku omuntu weena ahari abo, araaze kuruga omu mutima gwe obwigomi, obusungu, obushambani, okutamira, okugamba abantu kubi, obutombo, okwezirika, okushukuma, obujuna, okukunda omuntu omwe, okwezirika, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, n'ebindi, arakunde abantu abandi, arakwatane ahabw'Ebiragiro by'Okukwatana kw'Obwomeezi n'abantu abashemezibwe muno, ab'omwoyo muno; okukwatana oku kwingye kwaba okw'amaani ahabw'okuhindura eby'emishaani n'eby'obushoboorozi...

Ekyo kyaba omuringo ogurikwija kumwikiriza omuntu, kureka "ekigo kya sitoowa", "obusaani" obuburire...

Ahakuba, ku twena turikwenda okuhinduka okw'amaani, ekintu ekya kare twine kwetegyereza n'okuba ngu buri omwe ahariitwe (yaaba omweru rundi omwirima, orutookye rundi orukurumaine, omutaahi rundi omushomereire, n'ebindi), ari omuri "Mutendera gw'Obuntu" ogurikushaanana.

Omutendera Gwaitu gw'Obuntu ki? Mwaatekateeka ahari ekyo omurundi gwona? Tikirikubaasika kugyenda omu mutendera ogundi ku turikutaamanya embeera ei turimu.
