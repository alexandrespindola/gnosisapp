Nitwesiga kwenda empinduka y’amazima, kuruga omu muringo ogutari murungi, omu magara g’obunaku obutari buteeka, ogutari murungi...

Ekintu ekya bwango eki twine kwetegyereza gye n’okuba buri omwe ahariitwe, n’obu yaaba omutungi nari omworo, omutungi nari owa wakati, omugwera nari omunaku, aharizo omu Rwego rw’Obuntu...

Eego y’omusinzi neyihire aha y’omutaine ekyenda okusinza, kandi eyo y’omwamara neyihire eya omushumba. Eki turikugamba tikirikuhakanisibwa...

Twahika aha kicweka ky’omutwe gwitu ogu, tihari eki turikusa, twine okuteekateeka ekinanu ekiri ruguru, omu bwima, kirengire omu bugazi...

N’ekikuru kuba twine ekicweka aha kinanu eki; ahansi aha kicweka eki hariho abantu ababi kutukira; ruguru hariho abantu abarikutushemeza...

Omu bwima oburikushusha, omu kinanu ekirikushusha, nikirora ngu nitubaasa kushanga amarango g’obuntu; buri muntu neyihire kandi tihariho orikubaasa kukihakana...

Tikiri kukanganisa ngu hatugarukamu eby’okurora, nari eby’emyaka. Hariho abato n’abakuru, abakuru abarikwenda kufa n’abaana abazaire obuto...

Ekibuuzo ky’obwire n’emyaka; eki ky’okuzaarwa, okukura, okukura, okushwerwa, okuzara, okukura n’okufa, nikyegendereza ahansi...

Omu “Kinanu ekirikushusha”, omu bwima ekiteeko ky’obwire tikirikwikirizibwa. Aha marango g’ekinanu eki nitubaasa kushanga “Amarango g’Obuntu”...

Amatsiko g’abantu ag’omutima tigarikukora; nibekanga ngu n’obwire ebintu biraba birungi; n’obwo baitu bakuru na ba seena bakatekereza; ebintu ebirikumanyisa bikarugamu okutaikirizana...

“Eego y’Obuntu” niyo etungana kandi egi niyo bwima; nitushangwa aha kicweka kwonka nitubaasa kutambira aha kicweka kindi...

“Ekinanu ekirikushusha” eki turikugamba ahaiguru kandi ekirikweta “Amarango g’Obuntu” ag’omutaano, mazima tihaine kakwate n’obwire oburikutambira...

“Eego y’Obuntu” ehagire erizo ahaiguru yaitu omumutindo oguriho...

Teri omu biro by’omumaisho ebiri ruguru, kureka hano hati; omu biteekateeko byaitu; omu bwima...

Nikikanganisa kandi buri omwe naabaasa kwetegyereza, ngu emisalaba ebiri—Eri ruguru n’ebwima—nimushangwa omumutindo omu biteekateeko byaitu by’omunda kandi zigatungaanisa Omusalaba...

Obuntu bukurara kandi bugyendera omu musalaba oguri ruguru gw’Amagara. Buzarwa kandi bufa omu bwire bwabwo oburikugyenda; bushoberwa; tihariho enyentsya y’obuntu bw’omufu; teri Buntu...

Amarango g’Obuntu; Obuntu bwonyini, tibw’obwire, tihaine kakwate n’Omusalaba oguri ruguru; nishangwa omu biteekateeko byaitu. Hati, omu bwima...

Kyarugamu obusiima oburikukanganisa okushaka Obuntu bwaitu obwonyini okuruga hanu...

Tirikubi okutaho nk’ekiruguza eki: Emitwaro, ebibaro, okukwera, nibwo, omu nsi yoona, tiharikurugamu okushushana kw’amazima, okwongera kugyema aha Bantu, okutambira aha kicweka ahaiguru omu “Marango g’Obuntu”...
