Kureeba, kucwerera, na kukora, nibyo bintu bishatu eby'omusingi ebiri omu kucwekyereza.

Okubanza: kureeba. Okwakabiri: kucwerera. Okw'akashatu: kukora.

Enkumi eziri omu ntaro, okubanza nibazireeba; okwakabiri nibazicwerera; okw'akashatu nibazitaaya.

Omu kukwatana harimu okweyezura n'okweyereka. Oguruga omu kubaho n'abantu ahabw'okuba bamushushana, naguruka n'okweyezura.

Ekintu kyona ekirikubaho omu magara noobwo kireebeka nk'ekirikuzana, tikirikutwara busya ngu kirimu omuntu owa hafihi omu ituura ryaitu, eky'okutaanisa omu biteekateeko, "Mbe".

Okweyezura nikibaasika twaba turi omumazima, turi amaara, turi abashanju.

"Mbe", yaagweirwe omu kabi, ashemereire kureebwa n'obwegendereza omu bwongo bwaitu, omu mutima gwaitu, n'omu bushe bwaitu.

Mbe yoona y'obumaramari, neebaasa kweyereka omu mutima nk'okukunda, omu bwongo nk'ekirikukirayo, kwonka twatekateeka aha bushe, twahurira ekirikukuzana ekirikutaikirizika.

Okucwerera Mbe yoona kushemereire kuba okw'amaisho. Nitwetaga kumutuurisa aha ntebe y'abareebwa n'emisango twamucwerere n'obusharara.

Okugira ngu twaba nitutengyeera, nitwegyerereza, nituteekateeka, kushemereire kucwekyerezebwa, twaba nitwenda kumanya "Mbe" ei turikwenda kucwekyereza omu biteekateeko byaitu.

Kukora nikukiraho; tikurikubaasika kukora aha "Mbe" yoona, tutakamureebire kandi tutakamucwereere.

Okushaba omu murimo gw'ebiteekateeko n'eky'omusingi ahabw'okucwekyereza. Nitwetaga amaani agari ahaiguru y'obwongo, twaba nitwenda kucwekyereza "Mbe" yoona.

Obwongo bwonyini tiburikubaasa kucwekyereza "Mbe" yoona, eki tikirikubaasa kureebwa, tikirikubaasa kuhakanisibwa.

Okushaba ni okuganiira na Ruhanga. Twine kwetegyereza Ruhanga Maama omu Mutima gwaitu, twaba nitwenda kucwekyereza "Mbe", ogwo atakukunda nyina, omwojo atarikusiima, nacwererwa omu murimo ogu arikukora.

Buri omwe ahariitwe aine Ruhanga Maama we ow'omutaano, eyaahi, we akaba arikuba ahabw'obwe omutwaro gw'Omunywani gwaitu bwonyini, kwonka kuruga ahariwe.

Hariho Baatata abarikwingana omu Iguru nk'abashaija abarikwingana omu nsi. Ruhanga Maama omu mwika gwaitu n'emiterere y'obukazi eya Tata waitu orikubaho omu bwihisho.

We na We buzima nibyo bitwaro bibiri eby'ahaiguru eby'Omunywani gwaitu. Buzima We na We nibyo Bitwaro byaitu by'Obusingye ahari "Mbe" y'ebiteekateeko.

We ayeyabura omu We yaategeka, yaahamya, yaayegyesa. We acwekyereza ebintu ebitarikwetagisa ebi turikwetwara omu ituura ryaitu, ahabw'okukora omurimo buri izooba.

Twamara kufa kwona, twamara kucwekyereza ebintu byona ebitarikwetagisa twamara kukora emirimo mingi etwireeba n'obusaasi obutukunda twajuna ekajuna n' "ISHEENYU - MAAMA", obwo turiba Ruhanga abarikutiinisa, abarikwera munonga, abarikukira ebirungi n'ebibi.

Maama waitu ow'omutaano, eyaahi, ahabw'amaani ge agarikufuka neebaasa kucwekyereza "Mbe" yoona, ei yareebirwe kandi yacwererwa.

Tihaine buryo bwingana oburikwetagisa kushaba Maama waitu omu ituura ryaitu. Tushemereire kuba ab'obunaku bwingana kandi aboroire twirikugamba n'owe. Omwana ogurikugamba na nyina, tarimu miringo ey'omutaano, nagamba ekyo ekirikuruga omu mutima gwe nikwo kwona.

"Mbe" yoona ticwekyerezebwa bwangu; Ruhanga Maama waitu ashemereire kukora kandi akahurira obusaasi bwingana atakaatumurura "Mbe" yoona.

Mugarukye omunda, mutwaze okushaba kwaitu omunda, murondere omu ituura ryanyu Mutsigazi Ruhanga waitu kandi n'okwegayirira okurikuruga omu mutima nimubaasa kugamba n'owe. Mumwegayirire acwekyereze "Mbe" ei mureebire kandi mwaacwerera.

Omurimo gw'okweebembera omunda, omu muringo ogurikukura, nigwija kubasobozesa kugyenda omu maisho g'omurimo gwanyu.

Kwetegyereza, kumanya obuhangwa, n'eby'omusingi, kwonka nitwetaga ekirikukiraho twaba nitwenda kucwekyereza "Nkaanye".

Obwongo nibubaasa kugira amaganya g'okushingurura ekibi kyona, kukihitsya omu kabiina eki, kukoorekya, kukihisa, n'ebindi, kwonka tiburikubaasa kukihindura.

Nitwetaga "amaani g'omutaano" agari ahaiguru y'obwongo, amaani agarikufuka agarikubaasa kucwekyereza ekibi kyona.

STELLA MARIS, Ruhanga Maama waitu, aine amaani ago, neebaasa kucwekyereza ekibi kyona ky'ebiteekateeko.

Ruhanga Maama waitu, atuuramu omu ituura ryaitu, ahari omubiri, ahabw'engyenzi n'obwongo. We ahabw'okuba aine amaani agarikufuka agari ahaiguru y'obwongo.

Maama waitu w'ensi yoona ow'omutaano, eyaahi, aine Obwengye, Okukunda n'Amaani. Omuri we harimu obusingye obukuru.

Ebirimu byona ebirungi n'okugarukamu obutareetera, tikirikukora.

Tikirikukora kugarukamu: "tinija kuba omuramaramari"; Mbe z'obushaija zikatungaireho omu mwika gwaitu.

Tikirikukora kugarukamu buri izooba: "tinija kugira ekiniga"; "Mbe" z'ekiniga zikatungaireho omu mwika gwaitu.

Tikirikukora kugamba buri izooba: "tinija kugira obutamiizi"; "Mbe" z'obutamiizi zikatungaireho omu myika yaate.

Tikirikukora kuruga omu nsi tukahisana omu kihongore nari kubaho omu bwina; "Mbe" omuriitwe zikatungaireho.

Abashaija abamwe abayimaire aha bwina ahabw'okugyenda omu maisho baraheire aha kwetenga kw'abarikwera kandi bakatwarwa omu iguru, ahu bareebire kandi bakahurra ebyo abantu batarikubaasa kwetegyereza; kwonka "Mbe" zikagumaho omunda.

Tikirikukiraho ngu Obusingye nibubaasa kuruga omu "Mbe" ahabw'okugyenda omu maisho kandi bakagyenda omu kwetenga, okukunda, okurikugaruka omunda y' "Nkaanye".

Abo abamazire kusiima, batakacwekyereize "Ego", nibateekateeka ngu bamara kwetunga okugurukana, nibeyenga nibatunga Abegyesi kandi nibayingira omu Kugaruka obugyenzi.

Titwija kucwerera omuntu arikuza omu mazima, okwetenga n'okusiima kw'Omwoyo omuri "EGO".

Nitwenda kwongyera aha ky'okwetenga kucwekyereza "Mbe" ahabw'okwetunga okugurukana.

Obusingye bw'omushaija yoona ogurikugyenda omu maisho, akaziriihiize kwiruka "Mbe", nagarukamu okukora obwo amaze kufa omubiri, nagyenda omu kwetenga kandi bwanyima agaruka nk'Omugenzi w'ekitaamu kya Aladino omu ndobo, aha Ego, aha Nkaanye.

Obwo atakaba akirimu buryo bwa kugura omubiri ogundi, n'ekigendererwa ky'okugarukamu amagara ge ahaiteete ya buriho.

Abantu abingi abarikugyenda omu mazima abarikufiira omu bubina bw'e Himalayas, omu Asia ya Hagati, hati nibantu ababi, abarikukora ebintu ebitarikwetagisa omu nsi, n'obu abakurasi baabo bakabatungireho kandi bakabaramya.

Okugyezaho okugurukana n'obu kirikuba kigufu, hatarikwetwara omumanya okwetenga kucwekyereza Ego, nikucwererwa.
