Okuteekateeka munonga hamu n’obumanzi bwingi tikirikukwatanisa n’obwire obu obw’okutaaba n’okuhwerekyereza.

Ebirooto by’omutwe nibiruga omu mutima gw’obwengye, tibirikuruga omu “Nze” ow’okutandika nk’oku abantu abatazikiriza abaine obwengye batarikumanya, kureka omu “Nze” orikushushana omu buri omwe ahariitwe.

Omushaija ku arikuteekateeka, naikiriza munonga ngu ye omwine wenka arikuteekateeka.

Tarikwenda kwemereza omunyama omwengye mubi ngu ebirooto ebingi ebirikuhita omu bwengye bwe, nibiruga omu “Nze” ezirikushushana ezitwazire omunda.

Eki nikimanyisa ngu tituri bantu abarikuteekateeka ab’amazima; buzima titurakagira mutwe gwa bwengye gw’omuntu omwe.

Kwonka, buri “Nze” orikushushana oritwazire omunda, nakoresa omutima gwitu gw’obwengye, nagukoresa buri obu araabe ashooroire kutekateeka.

Kiba nikyemereza, twemereza omu kuteekateeka kubi nokutakukwatanisa, twikiriza ngu kiri kyaitu ky’omuntu omwe.

Ekirikwerarikiriza ngu okuteekateeka kubi nikuruga omu “Nze” yoona aha kasumi akatongoirwe ekakoresa kubi omutima gwitu gw’obwengye.

Okuteekateeka kubi kuriyo kw’amako gashushana: Okuteebereza, obutakiriza, omutima mubi aha muntu ondiijo, obujunaanwa, obujunaanwa bw’ediini, obujunaanwa bw’obufuzi, obujunaanwa bw’omukago nari obw’eka, obugomi, obwenge, okwerunda, ekiniga, eitendo, obugaya, okuhiga, obufumfu, oburaya, obugaya, okujuta, n’ebindi, n’ebindi, n’ebindi.

Buzima obuseerereza oburikukora omu bwengye bwaitu bwingi, ngu n’obu twakugira ebira by’ebyoma hamu n’endirimi nkumi okugamba, titurakagira kukubara byona.

Nk’okurugamu nari ekirikukurata aha bintu ebimazire kugambwa, nikyemereza kwemereza okuteekateeka kubi.

Nk’oku kitarikubaasika ngu hagireho ekirikubaho hatariho nshonga, nitugamba n’obumanzi ngu tikurikubaasika ngu hagireho okuteekateeka kwonka, kuruga omu kintu ekitariho...

Omukago hagati y’omuntu orikuteekateeka hamu n’okuteekateeka nikirikwebembera; buri kuteekateeka kubi kuriyo kuruga omu muntu orikuteekateeka orikushushana.

Omu buri omwe ahariitwe kuriyo abantu abarikuteekateeka ababi bingi, nk’oku kuriyo okuteekateeka kw’amako g’omutaano.

Obu ekibuuzo eki kireebire kuruga omubanga bw’abantu abarikushushana “Abarikuteekateeka hamu n’Okuteekateeka”, nikibaho ngu buri “Nze” oritwazire omu Magezi gaitu, buzima n’omuntu orikuteekateeka orikushushana.

Kitazibuura ngu omunda ya buri omwe ahariitwe, kuriyo abantu abarikuteekateeka bingi; kwonka, buri omwe aha baaba, n’obu araabe ari omutaano bwooka, neeyikiriza ngu ye yoona, aha kasumi akatongoirwe...

Abantu abarikuhimba ebishuba, abeefura, abarikwefeerera, abarikuteebereza, tibarikukiriza okugamba kw’okuti “Obwingi bw’Abarikuteekateeka” ahabw’okuba nibekunda munonga, nibehurra “Tata Tarzan” nari “Maama w’enkoko”...

Abantu abarikukora ebirikutaarikukwatana batyo barakakire okwikiriza ebirooto by’okugira omutwe gw’omuntu omwe, ogw’obwengye, ogw’okutangaaza?...

Kwonka abantu ab’amagezi batyo nibatekateeka ahari boona ebirungi munonga kandi nibazamba n’omwenda gwa Aristipo okworeka obwengye n’obucureezi...

Eky’omugano ky’emyaka nikigamba ngu Aristipo, arikwenda kworeka obwengye n’obucureezi, akazamba omwenda omukuru omujwire ebitiba n’ebirukirizi; akayihiira n’omukono ogwa buryo Omugoha gw’Obweziriki kandi akagenda omu nguuto za Atenas...

Nibagamba ngu Socrates ku aramureebire, akagambira omu iraka riingi: “Aha Aristipo, ekyeetendo kyaawe kirikureebwa omu birukirizi by’omwenda gwaawe!”.

Orikutahoora obwire bwona omu mbeera y’Okureeba Obuhya, Okureeba, arikuteekateeka ngu arikuteekateeka, neyeemerereza omu kuteekateeka kwona kubi.

Kuruga omu ki, nahigaanisa obushoboorozi bw’okugwa omu mutego bw’omu “Nze Kubi”, omukuru w’okuteekateeka okurikukwatanisa.

Buri obu twemereza okuteekateeka kubi, buzima nituba bagonzibwa b’omu “Nze” orikukukora.

Ahabw’Obwengye, omuhanda gw’Ekaama, omurimo gw’okwezirika, okusikimwa kwitu kuriyo omu “Nze” ezirikwanka Obwengye, omurimo gw’ahaiguru, ahabw’okuba tibarikumanya ngu obuhanga bwaazo omunda y’amagezi gaitu buriyo burikurugwamu ahabw’Obwengye hamu n’omurimo.

Ezi “Nze Kubi” hamu n’ezirikurwana nibaasa kwihaho ebyazindukiirwe ebirikubekwa omu mutima gwitu gw’obwengye kandi nibitandika obunyoro bw’ebyazindukiirwe obutarikukwatanisa n’obubi.

Twikiriza okuteekateeka okwo, ezo “Nze Kubi” ezirikukora omutima gwitu gw’obwengye aha kasumi akatongoirwe, nituba batarikubaasa kweggyaho ebirikurugamu.

Titurikubaasa kwebwa ngu buri “Nze Kubi” “Neeyebaguuza” kandi “Neebaguuza”, ebirikurugamu: Neeshuba.

Buri obu turaabe twihurra ngu obushoboorozi bwaitu burikubura, omuhinguzi ku araabe ataine amatsiko, Obwengye, omurimo gw’ahaiguru, ku araabe yabuza obumanzi kandi aleke ekirungi munonga, nikirikwerarikiriza ngu yabaguzibweho Nze Kubi.

“Nze Kubi y’Oburaya”, neehwerekyereza amaka marungi kandi neekora abaana obubi.

“Nze Kubi y’Obujunaanwa”, neebaaguza abantu abarikukundana kandi neehwerekyereza okushemererwa kwabo.

“Nze Kubi y’Eitendo ry’Obuhinguzi” neebaaguza abarikwenda omuhanda kandi ebi, nibaihurra baine obwengye, nibanka Omwegyesa waabo nari nibamutengyera...

Nze Kubi neekoresa ebyahwireho byaitu, ebirikwijuka byaitu, ebyetengo byaitu ebirungi munonga, obutahakanisa bwaitu, kandi, omu kworoora obumanzi byona ebi, neeyoreka ekintu omu mushana gw’ebishuba, ekintu ekirikwegamira kandi okugwa omutego kurijo...

Kwonka, omuntu ku araabe areebire “Nze” omu kukora, ku araabe ayegiire kutahoora omu mbeera y’okureeba, obushuba butyo tibubaasika...
