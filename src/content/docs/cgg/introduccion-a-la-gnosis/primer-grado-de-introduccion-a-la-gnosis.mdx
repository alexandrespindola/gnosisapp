Egi nigwo omugamba gw'okutandika kw'enyegyesa y'Eby'amagezi bya Gnostic rundi emigamba ey'aha rubaju y'eby'amagezi. Nikirukirira buri omwegi kutandika n'omugamba ogu bwanyima akagenda omumaisho n'emigamba egindi nk'ogw'okubanza, ogw'akabiri, ogw'akashatu n'egindi. Kishera kwetegyereza ngu egi teri emigamba y'eby'amagezi bya Gnostic ekyama. Emigamba y'eby'amagezi bya Gnostic ekyama ehabwa omwegi obu abeire omweteefu. Emigamba ekyama, egwo niyo migamba ya Gnostic y'amazima, terukirizibwe kwolesibwa omuntu weena ogihaire; eki kyezibira. Ogwo orikugira ngu: "Ndi n'emigamba nyingi, okutandika kungi", omubeihi.

Omuntu ku araabe ayenda kuba injiniya, loya rundi omuganga, n'ebindi, ashemereire kweyeteekaniza. Omuntu ogwo araaze aha ishomero kandi ayegye munoonga. Hanyuma y'okugira obwengye burungi aha itabi eryo erikushooma, bwanyima araatandike kukora ekyo ayegire. Okukora nikureeta okuhikiirira. Abantu abamanyire, abakugu abahambire, abantu abamanya ebintu bya sayansi abahambire, n'ebindi, tibarikuba nibagira obwengye burungi bwankaaha aha matabi gaabo ag'okushoma, kureka nibata obwengye obu omu kukora. Okushoma kwonka tikurikubaasa kureeta kyona (okwihaaho okushemeza omu bwenge kwankaaha aha babwetegyereza). Nikyetagisa kukora.

Shooma ekuruusi y'amasomo aga kandi ogishoome n'obwegendereza bw'amazima, n'omutaano gw'okwega, n'omutaano gw'okumanya obwengye oburikukirayo. Kwonka ijukire ngu oshemereire kuta omu kukora ekyo oyegire, ku oraabe oyenda kuhikiirira omu Murimo.
