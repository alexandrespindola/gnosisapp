YO ogwo leero orama OKUGONZA KWAKAIMA omu mokaikara, bwabingwa obu YO endi eki hatariho kukora n'okurama okwo kandi nobwakaikara akatunga okugwa omu mutego.

YO etyo erukurata byara okukurata kwandi kandi endi nibashangwa okutwarwa kwandi, baitu hatariho hagati ya YOES zoona endagiriro nari endagarano.

Buri omu aha YOES ezi akikiriza omu muda ogu okuba byoona, baitu mazima tikiri bingi okukira ekiti ekyomunda eky'obukorwa bwaitu, obukiraabe aine ekyerezi kyokuba byoona, amazima, omushaija ajuwire.

Ekintu ekirikwetengesa nikyo tukugaba okukiriza aha YO omu muda ogu, obukiraabe obuda obwo YO kwibwa ekikaro YO endi. EGO LUNAR n'obwihuriizo bw'ABA YOES abarikutwanguha omu kiti ekirikukora.

Nikyetaga kumanya ngu buri omu omu cilindros etaano egy'omashini areeba ebimanyiso byabo ngu ntitushemereire kwehuuna.

Hagati y'ebiti etaano by'omashini harimu obutasantana bw'obutairira.

Abantu nibatendeka bingi enteekateeka, baitu omu mazima ebara ebara ekiti kya NTEEKATEEKA n'ekirikugwa. Hanyuma, obukiraabe bingi obwangu, nibaija ebiti INSTINTIVO n'EBY'OKUHIGA nari MOTOR, ebirimu obutairira obumu. Ekirikubanza aha byona n'ekiti kya SEX, kandi erikukurutira omu ndagiro y'okwanguha, ekiti EMOCIONAL.

Harimu obutasantana bwamaani bw'obutairira hagati ya buri kimwe omu BITI etaano by'omashini. Obwangu bw'ebiti nibushobanura ebyahukire by'ebyengeso zirikushangwa ngu saiensi etari ya bwengye erimu ebara teshobora kushobanura; nikyetaaga kwijuka obwangu oburikutingisa obw'obukorwa obumwe obwa psicológicos, fisiológicos na mentales.

Buri EKITI eibwiire ebihande bibiri: ekirungi n'ekibi; okugaba eki kiri kyangu kyara ahabw'EKITI kya NTEEKATEEKA na EKITI INSTINTIVO.

Omurimo gwona gw'EKITI kya NTEEKATEEKA nigugaba omu bihande bibiri: AMAKURU na OKUGANA; EEHE n'OYA, TESIS na ANTÍTESIS.

Omu EKITI INSTINTIVO harimu okurwaisana okumu hagati y'ekirikunshemerereza n'ekitarikunshemerereza; ebinunu byeyagaaze, ebinunu bitarikuyagaaza kandi ebinunu byona ebyo bikwatanisibwe n'ebihika etaano: kureeba, okuwulira, okununuuta, okubona, okuhikiirira.

Omu EKITI MOTOR nari ekya OKUHIGA, harimu okurwaisana hagati y'OKUHIGA n'OKUHEEMBERA.

Omu EKITI EMOCIONAL harimu okusiima okurikuyagaaza n'okutarikuyagaaza: okushemerererwa, okusiima, okugonzangana, obwesiga, n'ebindi, biri birungi.

Okusiima okutarikuyagaaza, nk'okutakusemera, obufindo, ebugugu, obushato, okuteemerwa, obutiino, biri bibi kwijwiire.

Omu EKITI kya SEX harimu okuruguuzana n'okutabasa kuruguuzana, obweremu n'obushambani omu kutairana kwakaima.

ENYAMU INTELECTUAL yakuhurwa okusiima kwayo obwo nikyetaga, baitu tikirikubaasa kukuhurra okusiima kw'oburumi bwayo.

Oryenda KUGANISA EGO PLURALIZADO, ashemereire kukuhurra okusiima kw'oburumi bwayo. OBUFUNDO nibureeta oburumi, obuturikuteera obufindo, oburumi burafe, okukuhurra oburumi, okukuhurra oburumi.

OBUGASHO nibuleeta OBURUMI; obuturikutegyereza na BUGSABO nitukuhurra OBURUMI, nitukugwisa.

Nikyetaga OKWEBABURURA omu Buda okubuda; EGO PLURALIZADO nikikora omu buri kimwe omu BITI ETAANO EBY'OMASHINI. Obumwe YO omu EKITI EMOCIONAL owaarukukurakutunga colérico nari okufindo nari obugugu, obumwe okubanga omu maisho n'obukorwa obubi eby'ekiti ky'obwengye n'obutebwamu bwaayo, abata balwaisana, endi ebikorwa ebibi okututwara aha kubanga abahaire, n'ebindi.

Buri EKITI kireebire AMAKUMI ANA NA MWENDA eby'obutuurwa SUBCONSCIENTES kandi omu buri kimwe omu by'obutuurwa ebyo nibahikiirira obukaikuru bwa YOES abarikwetaga KUZUURA kuraba omu KUTEEKATEEKA kw'omu mutima.

Obuturikutuzuura, obuturikutunga OBUBWENGYE bw'ebikorwa bya YO omu biti etaano eby'omashini n'omu makumi ana na mwenda eby'obutuurwa subconscientes, hati nitukukura OBUBWENGYE.
