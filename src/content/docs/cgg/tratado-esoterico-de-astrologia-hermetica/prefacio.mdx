## OKUTANDIKAHO

### OKUVA OMURI MUKAMA GARGHA KUICHINES

Hariho okwetegyereza enjuba okw'obwengye nari okw'omubaro okwetenga okushooma okwingi okwegyegyesa, n'okwo abakugu omu by'enjuba omu bwire bwona bakozireho obuhanuzi aha bintu by'omugasho. Kandi abantu abakuru nk'eky'okureeberaho Hitler omuri Germany bakozeseze okwetegyereza okwo, ou yakozeseze abakugu omu by'enjuba abahereza omu bitaro byabo.

Itoona Abagnositiki twetantuura n'emishomo egyo, ahabw'okuba omuntu n'ekijwaro kurugiirira aha buhanuzi, omuteebi omu maisho g'ebimanyiso ebimanyisa obutareengana obutari bumwe n'okuhita kw'enjuba, nitumanya okwetegyereza enjuba okutwegyesa kukwata enjuba kandi nitubaasa kwetantara ebintu ebi abakugu b'okwetegyereza enjuba okw'omubaro baraaba nibashobora kuhuura. Eki nikyetenga kuhindura emibiri y'omwezi eyi twazaalirwe nayo, n'emibiri y'enjuba nari y'omushana, twesigamire aha muzizi gw'obuntu bwitu nari embibo yaitu.

Omuzigu aturagiiriza kukwata okumanya kw'obwa Ruhanga n'omwoyo gwitu ogwa Sitaani nari Omukuru w'ebirikare orikutegeka omubiri gwitu. Omuringo ogurikukirayo obureezi okucwekyereza Omutwarizi w'Omulyango nk'oku ab'omukono gw'obumosho barikumweta, n'okuraba omu muringo gw'Okutandika ogurikwetagirwa itwe Abagnositiki n'okutaaha omu bigo nari Lumisiales, twesigamire aha byegyeso ebirikwebemberwa n'okukomezibwa omu Avatar ya Aquarius "Samael Aun Weor".

Omu bwire obu Omutaahi Yesu yahaire abahereza b'omu muhanda ebyegyeso bye, akagira ati: "Ndi omugisha, ndi amazima, ndi amagara, hakabaaho omujurizi omukuru orikwetwa Simooni Omujurizi, omujwiire amaani n'obugagga bwingi, ou yaayorekire abegyesibwa be: "Yesu yaahikire ishe ahabw'omurimo gwe, nanye Simooni, ninyija kuhika ishe ahabw'omurimo gwangye kandi eky'akakora kikaba kukurata omugisha gw'obumosho, arikwehaana omuhimbi gwe, akabi aka nikagaraama obutoosha abahereza b'omu muhanda obubarikwikiriza ngu bo bareebembera kwambuka omugisha.

Ahabw'abo abahikireho obwengye obu omurundi ogwabo ogw'okubanza, nitubamanyisa ngu nikyetenga emyezi mukaaga y'ebishomo by'okutandika, arikushoma ebitabo bya Samael Aun Weor n'okuta okumanya kwabo omu mikoora, bwanyima yaaba ahaire obuhame ngu nayenda kwetegyereza omutima, kandi nayenda amagara agarikukira, hati, n'aba ahiirwe omu Lumisiales kuraba omu muringo gw'okwegyesa ogurikukirayo.

Obu omuhereza w'omugisha aba ateekanisiibwe ahabw'ekishomo ky'okugyezibwa, okugyezibwa kw'okubanza oku arikugyenda kukwata n'Omutwarizi w'Omulyango nari okureebana na Sitaani we owenyine ogwaabaire omwebemberi waitu n'Omwegyesa omu myaka mingi. Nimijukira oku omwaka gwa 1949, omwegyesibwa wa Hierofante w'Emishanana Emisyo Aun Weor, kandi bwanyima y'okwebanga kw'emyezi ena kwaba kuhikiire aha kugyezibwa kw'Omutwarizi omu kiro kya 27 Kyakanga. Omutandikiire akaba ari ahantu ahairwe wenka, akaba ari omunda, kwonka akaba atakimanyire, obu yaayetsire Omutwarizi w'Omulyango, akaba atarindiriize, omutandikiire akagira ngu okubanza akahurra obutiti bw'okufa, obwire obwo bukaba burikumurika bukaba nibutaataana obutoosha, nibwongyera n'obutiiti obwo n'omuhimbo ogurikufundukira ogwaamuhikize okuteerera, akahurra ebirikwenda kuruga omu busingye, kwonka amaani gaa Kristo agaa akaba yaayongyeire omubiri gwe kuraba omu kuhindura amataare, Arcane A. Z. F., gakamuha omugisha okugumira aha hantu huraaba hotenda. Omujumo akareeba ngu nikwija ahaariwe ensoro erikushushanira ekaaya, erirweire byona, erina amashyoho aha maisho gaayo agarikumurika n'okukuuma obu barikwegarura, einyindo n'omunwa nk'eky'embunda, kandi akagira ati: Omu muringo ogwo noyenda kundeka? Noondiha otyo enshonga ezi nkukozere? Noyenda kunhindura omuntu ou otarikumanya? Kandi akamugarukamu ajuwire obutiiti ngu naamuleka, ensoro ekamweteera n'omuringo gw'okutaahira, omutandikiire akamubanda, kwonka tikiramugasa kubanda okwo okugweri kw'omuntu ogweri kandi ogutiitiire, kwonka akajukira ngu ari Chela wa Kristo kandi akamubanda omu izina rya Kristo kandi kikaaguma kigaruka enyima obutoosha, obutoosha obu arikuteera, akasaba oburinzi ahari Kristo n'Abegyesa be abarikukundwa kandi akamugira ati: torikubaasa kukuza ahaari nyowe, ninkwija kukusingura kandi ensoro ekagaruka enyima neehiga amateeka goona. Obwetaagira bw'omwegyesibwa ogwo bukaba bwingi munonga, ahabw'okuba Hierofante akaba araaze naagendera omu rurembo orundi kandi eki kikaba nikyetenga ebiro bishatu by'okubuura, kwonka okugaruka akamubuuza kandi akamugarukamu ati: Nimutendereza, mwarugiremu gye okugyezibwa kw'okubanza n'Omutwarizi w'Omulyango, omuntu ogu ni Sitaani wawe owenyine, omweziheereize kandi ogondire omu myaka mingi, kandi noondiha otyo? Omwegyesibwa ogutiitiire akabuuza kandi Omwegyesa akamugarukamu, nagondiira aha kukoona kwawe okucureire, nagaburira aha kwenda kwaitu okucureire, kukoona kubi, obwenzi, amajwaro n'amagara acureire, byona nibireeta abatunzi b'eka y'obwa Ruhanga ei Kristo yaatugambira, nibatunda eka yaitu, hati nikyetenga n'emigo gw'obugumiro kuruga omunda yaawe abatunzi abo boona abaabaire bakubaire abahuuku ahari Sitaani, hati nikyetenga kubasingura buli omwe obwaba nooyenda kurugamu obubi kandi okwata omugisha omweru.

Kuraba omu kitabo eki nitubaasa kutaayaaya abaingi ab'enjuba, nituhwereera eka y'omutima gw'enjuba, nitusaba kandi turikukora n'abaingi b'enjuba, tutarikuba abajwaro b'oburemeezi, kwonka okubanza twine kugingira abatunzi kuruga omu ka yaitu, twine kwega kukwata aha gataru yaitu. Eki nikireetaho omuhereza kugyenda obutoosha aha mikoro ya Lumisiales; aharakwo n'ayega kukunda n'okuhereza Ruhanga aha bintu byona n'omutaahi, naamanyira aha mikoro egyo kandi bwanyima akakwata ngu amahururu goona g'omukoro gw'okuramya nigagira omukago gw'omutima n'egataru eririho kandi n'akushanga eby'okutangaaza ebitarikubaasa kwikirizibwa. JACHIN na BOAZ z'egataru nikyetenga okukwata egataru yaawe kandi n'ohika omu bwire obu oregire kwega kuta omuhanda mushanju ogurikwetenga kukora twemanyiire aha gataru ya Ruhanga oriho kandi omu maisho g'omugisha omukazi Ruhanga.

N'enyegyesa y'okuzooka nitwega kwita embuzi ei turimu kandi nituyaaba n'omuringo gw'obwire obwo ekisibo ky'Omwana gw'Endama ow'Okuhimbarwa. Hati nitwikura omu Mukama w'Obwire okwomeera amagara agatahwaho ajuwire okushemererwa okutaataana.
