Obwomeizi bw'Abazungu obukaire, bwabaire nibukora EKIRAGIRO EKITARIKUHINDUKA EKY'OKUHINDUKA, bukaija kwebwa kimwe ebintu ebiri kukwata ahari okubonabonesa omu Butonde. Nikitangaaza ngu obwomeizi obwononekire tiburikubaasa kweteenga omulingo ogurikuhinduka enyima, OKULIISA, aha MUHINGO OMUNENE.

Obwomeizi obuli omu mbeera y'okucweera butebereza okugwa n'okushura, n'omulingo gw'okubonabonesa, okushiisha omu muhingo munene, okwonoonwa, n'ebindi, nibugutaataaza nk'okuhinduka, okugyenda omu maisho, OKUHINDUKA.

Byona BIHINDURUKA kandi BIKURA, byatura kandi bishura, bikura kandi bicweeka, bigyenda kandi bikoma, biruga kandi bitaaha; omu byona harimu okutendeka n'okwegamira, okukuratira EITSIYO ly'endiba.

OKUHINDUKA n'omunyaanya waakyo OMUHINDUKI, n'amategeko abiri agatura kandi agakora omu mulingo oguli kugyenda hamwe n'obulingaaniza omu byona ebyahangirwe.

OKUHINDUKA n'OKUHINDUKA nibitwara ekitabo ky'obuhinguzi bw'obutonde.

OKUHINDUKA n'OKUHINDUKA n'amategeko abiri agari buhinguzi bw'obutonde agarikugira ngu tibagira kyokukwata ahari OKWEHANGWA OKW'OMUNDU OWEITEKA.

OKWEHANGWA OKW'OMUNDU OWEITEKA, tirikubaasa kuba ekintu ekirugire omu itegeko lyona eriri buhinguzi, kureka obumwe oburugire omu mulimo oguli omanye, ogukozirwe ahari wenka kandi omunda YENKA, okucwa emisingye ya TREMENDOS SÚPER-ESFUERZOS, okwetegyereza kandi n'okubonabona kureetera abantu kwenda.

Byona BIRUGA ahantu hatandikiirwe kandi EGO LUNAR nirugaruka bwanyima y'okufa aha nda ensya.

Kyahandiikirwe ngu omuntu weena atoorwa obwomeezi CIENTO OCHO kugira ngu AYEHANGYE. Abantu baingi nibacweekaho obwire. Ogu atayehangire omu bwire bwe, areka okuzarwa kwigarukira omu MUNDOS INFIERNOS.

"Ai Kountreya!, abantu aba abarukwesiga baryaguma n'okugyenda omu matrices Demoníacas omu bwomeezi bwaingi kandi baryaguma nibagwa omu mibiri egyekugwa haiguru". (OKUHINDUKA).

"Ebikozirwe eby'enyanja nibyo muryango gw'okubonabona oku okurikubonabonesa; bikozirwe okuruga omu busharaazi, ekiniga n'obusheeguzi; ahabw'ekyo nikibaasika okureka".

Omu kicweka kya kabiri tukagamba ha VACA SAGRADA n'obuhangwa bwayo obw'amaani; nikitangaaza ngu buri BRAHMÁN omu INDIA, bwaba nasaba ROSARIO naronda ciento ocho ezaayo.

Hariho ABAINDIA abatarikumanya ngu ebikwatiraine n'emirimo yaabo eyeranga, bakora kureka kubanga barya omu VACA PRINCIPAL, ciento ocho okucweka n'eizaza omu ngaro zaabo, kandi, nibayijuza engoto y'amaizi kandi nibaijumya bwake ahakigwo ky'empumura ya VACA, tibarikunywa, nk'amaizi agashemerire kandi agagwere.

Nikyetengwa kwijuka ngu omugyezi gwa BUDHA guine ciento ocho EMIRUNDA. Ebi byona nibituhamagira kweteereza ahari CIENTO OCHO EWOMEZI egi etoorwa OMUNTU.

Nikyetegerezebwa ngu ogu atarikukoresa CIENTO OCHO EMIRUNDA, aigarukira omu KUZAARA kw'OMUNDOS-INFIERNOS.

OKULIISA OKW'OHAKA nibugwa hanyima, nibugyenda enyima, nibwija aha bwomeezi bwona bw'enyamaishwa, ebindimuka n'engiga, okurabira omu kubonabona okutatsigire.

Ekicweka ky'ahaheru ky'OKULIISA OKW'OHAKA n'embeera y'okuzika, hanyima nikija OKUHINDUKA kw'abazimbire.

Kintu kimwe kyekukira kuruga omu kibonaboneso ekyo kyona, kintu kimwe ekiturikugaya n'obwomeezi, BUDHATA, ekicweka ky'OMWOYO OMUNTU eki ENYAMAISHWA ENTEINE egi etwara omu MIBIRI YAABO EYA LUNA.

OKULIISA omu MUNDO-INFIERNOS ngu ngu kureka BUDHATA, OMWOYO OMUNTU, kuruga omu mbeera ya caos okutandika okutaaha OKUHINDUKA okurabira omu migo gy'ebitaro, ebindimuka, enyamaishwa, kukahitsya omu rugyengyo rw'ENYAMAISHWA ENTEINE egi etoorwa MUNTU.

Nikibabaza ngu abantu baingi nibatandika, bagarukayo omurundi gundi aha MUNDOS-INFIERNOS.

Obwire omu MUNDOS-INFIERNOS y'OBWAMI BW'ENSI OBW'OMUHAIGURU, nikishangwa nibucweera kandi nibwonabonesa; buri CIEN AÑOS biraira omu IBONABONEZI EZ'OMUHAIHURU ez'obutonde, nitureeba amagoba ga KARMA.

Ogu atahinduruka obumwe omu MUNDOS-INFIERNOS, aguma omu bugingo n'obuhwezi n'EITEGEKO RY'A KARMA.

OLUSINGE ni rugo rw'obwomeezi kandi buri bwomeezi rugarukanwa ruli aha mbigazi ezihaiguru, OKUHINDUKA nari ruli aha migo gy'omutaano, OKULIISA.

Buri bwomeezi n'okugarukamu okwabireho, okweyongyera aha magoba amarungi nari agabi, agashemeire nari agarikubonabonesa.

Abantu baingi omu mulingo murungi kandi ab'obumanzi, nibashura omu bwomeezi omu bwomeezi omu rugo rw'omugo ogurikugarukamu, kukahika okugarukira omu MUNDOS-INFIERNOS.

Ogu arikwenda KWEIHANGYA ahaiguru, ateekwa kwekwatamu okuruga omu mbeera y'amategeko OKUHINDUKA n'OKULIISA eya butonde.

Ogu OMU MAISHO akwenda kuruga omu MBEERA Y'ENYAMAISHWA-ENTEINE, ogu omu bugundi akwenda kuhinduka MUNTU OMU MAISHO, ateekwa kwekwatamu okuruga omu MATEGEKO G'OBUHINGUZI bw'obutonde.

Weena ogu arikwenda kuhinduka ABAIRI BAZAARE, weena ogu arikwenda OKWEHANGYA OKW'OMUNDU OWEITEKA, ateekwa kwigura omu rugo rw'OKUHINDURA KW'OBUMANYI; eno niyo nzira y'OKUHANGWA KW'OBUSHAAZI. Enzira eno eijuzire akabi omunda n'ahaiguru.

Yesu Cristo nigigira ngu: "Omukati kw'enkumi abanzomereera omwe naanzindura, omu nkumi abanzindura omwe... nandi omugyendera, omu nkumi abandi omugyendera, omwe n'owange".

BHAGAVAD GITA nigigira ngu: "Omukati kw'abantu enkumi omweteenga kuhika aha bujuni; omukati kw'abo abeteenga, nk'okukubaasika, omwe nahika aha bujuni, kandi omukati kw'abo abajwire, akaba omwe NANGUMA NIMMANYI gye".

RABÍ DIVINO DE GALILEA tatakagira ngu EITEGEKO ry'OKUHINDUKA niriruganiza abantu boona aha bujuni. YESÚS, omu ngyenderwaho ina nateekereza aha kutahukika kw'okugarukira aha BUZIMA.

EITEGEKO LY'OKUTOROORA KW'OBUTONDE, ribaho omu byona ebyahangirwe; abeeshomesa boona abagarukira aha ntebe tibarikureetwa omu bwesomesa.

ABASHABA-ESOTERISTAS na ABASHABA-OCULTISTAS nibagira ngu aha nzira nyingi nibahitsya RUHANGA. Enu niyo SOFISMA eyi barukwenda kurugiraho enshobe zaabo.

HIEROFANTE OMUHABWE YESÚS CRISTO obumwe atoorwa omuryango omwe n'enzira emwe: "Omuryango muruhanga kandi enyanya niyo nzira ehitsya aha Ngyenderwaho kandi obucaire nibo abagirunda".

OMURYANGO n'ENZIRA nibisibire ekigyenda kikuru, eikiragiro omwe abasa kwita akajwera EKIBANGA EKY'OKUSHOORA, baitu nikiba nikikwatiraine n'ecweka y'Escorpio, hati nitushoma ekigyenda ky'ensimbi y'OBUSHOBOZI, ekigyenda kya LIBRA.

Nitwetenga okuba abamanye KARMA yaaitu kandi ekikweta nikibaasika okurabira omu MBEERA Y'OKURORA OKUNSURWA.

Buri EKIRIKUMANYISA eky'obwomeezi ekihango, kigira ensonga yaakyo omu bwomeezi obwazire, baitu nitwetenga okuba abamanye ekyo.

Buri akanya k'OKUSHEMERA nari OKUBONABONA kiteekwa okugumizamu OKWETEEREZA n'obwomeezi BWEITEKA kandi n'OBWIRE OBWAMAANI. EKIRIKURUGAMWO nikiba n'okukora ekintu eki kine obwomeezi obwazire. Bwanyima tureka OBUMANYI bw'ENSONGA y'ekintu, kureka kyongyera nari kirikubonabonesa.

Ogu ayazooka OBUMANYI, arikubaasa kugyenda omu MIBIRI Y'OMUNDA kwiruga omu MUBIRI, aha KWENDA OKWAMANYA kandi ashoma ekitabo kye eky'okuhikiriza.

Omu TEMPLO DE ANUBIS n'ABAMALI BAINI KWENDA N'ABABIRI, omu ntandikiro arikubaasa kushoma ekitabo kye.

ANUBIS n'OMUTONGOLI MUKURU OMU KARMA. Itambiro lya ANUBIS rilinda omu MUNDO MOLECULAR, etoorwa abantu baingi MUNDO ASTRAL.

ABATANDIKIRE nibarikubaasa kuronda ANUBIS. Turikubaasa kuronda edeni Kármica n'EMIRIMO EMIRUNGI, baitu otwareka kuronda ANUBIS.

EITEGEKO RY'A KARMA, EITEGEKO ry'OMUSAALABA, rya CÓSMICA tiriri EITEGEKO erigwire; turikubaasa okushaba eibanja ABAMI BA KARMA, baitu eibanja lyona ryateekwa kuliha n'emirimo emirungi kandi kuba toriliha, hanyima EITEGEKO lyaricwa n'okubonabona.

LIBRA, ekigyenda ky'ensimbi y'OBUSHOBOZI, niritegeka amafunguro. LIBRA n'ekigyenda ky'amaani agarukukwata n'okujaguura amafunguro omu mulingo murungi.

Yimirira aha rubaraza, omu mbeera ey'okuwasa kandi hanyima n'emikono eyeyambukire omu mulingo gwa KRUZI, nari gwa OBUSHOBOZI, mwetendereze omu mulingo gw'OBUSHOBOZI nikimutereereza emirundi musanju aha rubaju orwa buriijo n'emirundi musanju aha rubaju orwa bwamosho n'okwekwata kw'okuba ngu amaani gaanyu goona gajaguziirwe omu mafunguro. Okutendereza kw'omukati gwa engo ey'omugongo kiteekwa kuba nk'obushobozi.

Amaani agarikutaaha omu nsi kurabira omu kutendeka kw'ebijanja byaitu okutandika n'omubiri gwona, gateekwa kujaguziirwa aha bwejumi kandi ekyo kiba omu bwesigwa okurabira omu KWETENDEREZA okwa LIBRA.

LIBRA ritegekwa VENUS na SATURNO. METAL, omuhaho. EIGA, OKULONDA.

Omu kukora tumanyire ngu abazirwe omu LIBRA batendeka, aha mwingi, okugira obushororo omu byona ebikwatiraine n'obwomeezi bw'obushwere, okukunda.

Abazirwe omu LIBRA nibatunga emigisha mingi ahabw'omulingo gwaabo ogw'okuba abeshemereza kandi abeesigwa.

Ab'e LIBRA bagyendereize gye, nibakunda ebintu ebigendereire omu mazima. Abantu tibarikwetegyereza gye ab'e LIBRA, aba barugira n'okurora ab'obushaarizi kandi abataine obusaasi, tibarikumanya kandi nibakwenda kumanya eby'obukama, eby'obunanuzi nibibatangaaza, ebigambo ebyomuhazi nibibaremesa omu busingi bw'okubacweekereza.

Ab'e LIBRA bagira enshobe y'okutamanya kulekera abantu abandi, omu byona nibakwenda kureeba EIZOBA kandi atali ekyo, nibeebwa obuhandiki.

ABAZIWE OMU LIBRA nibakunda kugyenda n'omutima gwabo ogwoona.

Abazirwe omu LIBRA nibo abari kandi atali ekyo, abeshemereza n'abeesigwa. Abantu nibaremesa abazirwe omu LIBRA, nibagamba omu busingi ahabw'obwo nibaba bayeteera kandi nikibe nk'obutooma nibaijuzibwa abazigu.

Aba LIBRA tibarikusaasira ABAHESA ABAIRI, ekyo tibarikukigaana LIBRA kandi tibarikulekera.

Aha bazirwe omu LIBRA otwa teekwa kuba n'okukunda kandi okujaguura nari ekitangaaza buri omu, baitu otalikora okutendereza kw'obusaasi n'obugumire, ahabw'okuba ekyo tibarikukigaana kandi tibarikulekera ab'e LIBRA.

Enganda ya SUPERIOR omu LIBRA omutima gwona agambira okuba MU BWAJUNA. Enganda embi ya LIBRA n'ey'abashingwa kandi abasaazi.

Enganda SUPERIOR eya LIBRA egira okwetegyereza okw'OMWOYO kw'OMWOYO oku aba SPIRITUALISTAS abatarikwetegyereza kandi nibacwamu omu busingi.

Enganda embi ya LIBRA, egira abantu abarikwetenga n'abatakyabanga, baguma tibarikugira kureeba kw'okuronderwa ahaiguru, okurabaire, aha bujuni.

Enganda SUPERIOR eya LIBRA egambira OMUTIMA n'okweteengesa n'okujaguura. Enganda embi eya LIBRA egira okushiisha n'obusheeguzi.

Omu nganda ya LIBRA abingi nibakugurana omulingo mwingi n'omubi ez'amaganda goona SUPERIOR n'egya LIBRA embi.

Abazirwe omu LIBRA nibajugunuzibwa okushwerwa abazirwe omu Piscis.

Abazirwe omu LIBRA nibakunda kukora emirimo ey'obusaasi tibarugire aha magoba nari okugaruka amagoba nari okutongoora omulimo ogukozirwe.

Enganda SUPERIOR eya LIBRA nekunda omuziki murungi, negushunguurwa kandi negugwera omu bugye obuhaiguru.

Ab'e LIBRA baletwa omu kushemeza omu maisho, enyimbo enungi, ebindi, ebintu bingi bingi.
