Kuri 21 Omwezi Gwamwenda kuhika 20 Omwezi Kw'ikumi na ibiri.

Hariho embeera ina ezirikubaasa kuba omuntu arikumanya: kurota, okumanya turi abazindutsi, okweyoreka, n'okumanya ebirikubaho.

Teekateeka, omusomi omukundwa, enju erimu amakaana ana. Enyamaishwa omunyabwenge eyorobire kwetwa omuntu etuura omu maakaana abiri ag'ahaansi, kwonka teerikukoresa maakaana abiri ag'ahaiguru.

Enyamaishwa omunyabwenge egabura amagara gaayo ag'obusaasi n'obugyenyi omu kurota kw'obutoosha n'embeera eyorobire kwetwa okuzinduka, ekiriyo ekindi ekirikufaana kurota.

Omubiri gw'omubiri nigwo gusiiba gukwire omu kitanda, EGO eyerirwe omu MIBIIRI YA LUNA nibwo egenda n'okumanya okwire nk'omuntu orikugyenda omu butongi orikugyeenda aharubaju rw'ebirimba. EGO aharubaju rw'ebirimba eroota n'etuura omuri byo, tihariho sente nyingi omu birooto byayo, obutoosha, embeera, ebirikukurata, ebikorwa, emirimo yoona ey'omutima nekorera hatariho obuhabuzi kandi ebyererezi byeyorekera hamwe n'ebintu ebirikubaho, ebintu ebitarikukwatanisa, ebitarikworeka, ebitahikirire, n'ebindi.

EGO eyeyirwe omu MIBIIRI YA LUNA ku eragaruka omu MUBIIRI GW'OMUBIRI, nibwo embeera ya kabiri ey'okumanya erijaho eroorobire kwetwa okuzinduka, ekitari kintu kindi kyonka ekirikufaana kurota.

EGO ku eragaruka omu MUBIIRI GW'OMUBIRI gwayo, ebirooto nibigumizamu omunda, embeera eyorobire kwetwa OKUZINDUKA nobwengye bwa mazima nikurota okuzindutse.

Enjuba ku eraazire, enyenye zirekera aho kweroka, kwonka tizirikuleka kubaho; nikwo ebirooto biri omu mbeera y'okuzinduka, nibigumizamu omu bwihisho, tizirikuleka kubaho.

Eki nikimanyisa ngu ENYAMAISHWA MUNYABWENGE eyorobire kwetwa OMUNTU etuura omu nsi y'ebirooto; n'obuhikirire omushiiri akagamba ngu amagara nirooto.

ENYAMAISHWA Y'OBWENGE negyenda emotooroka eroota, nekorera omu faakitari, omu ofiisi, omu musiri, n'ebindi, eroota, neyegomba omu birooto, neyombeka omu birooto; entaane, entaane munonga omu magara, neyerekana, etuura omu nsi y'ebirooto kandi nekikiriza munonga ngu yerekana.

Enjiri Ina zishaba OKUYEREKANA, kwonka buzima tizirikumanyisa oku OKUYEREKANA.

Okw'okubanza, nikyetaaga kwetegyereza ngu tukwire; obumwe wenka omuntu ku araabe amanyire kimwe ngu akwire, naataaha omuhanda gw'OKUYEREKANA.

Orikuhika aha KUYEREKANA OKUMANYA, nabo ni YEEYOREKA, ayebwa OKUMANYA KWE.

Ekihagaro ekirikukirayo obubi ky'ABARIKWEGIIRA AB'ENZIINA hamwe n'AB'ENZIINA AB'OBWIHISO abatarikumanya, n'okwezirika nk'ABEYOREKA kandi nibakikiriza n'okugira ngu buli omwe nayerekana, ngu abantu boona baine OKWEYOREKA.

Abantu boona ku baraabe baine OKUMANYA OKWEREKANA ensi yaraabe erikuhimbisa, tiharaabeho ntaro, tiharaabeho ekyange n'ekyawe, byoona yaraabe by'abantu boona, twaraabe nitutuuraho omu MWAKA GWA ZAABU.

Obumwe omwe KU AREKANA OKUMANYA, ku araabe YEEYOREKA, ku araabe aine OKUMANYA KWE, nibwo buzima araabe aije kumanya AMAZIMA ahariwe.

Butakaihire aha mbeera ya kashatu ey'OKUMANYA (OKWEYOREKA), omwe buzima tarikwezirika, nangwa n'obu araabe akikiriza ngu arezirika.

Nikyetagisisa okutunga embeera ya kashatu ey'okumanya, okwambuka aha kaana ka kashatu ak'enju, butakaihire okugira obushoboorozi bw'okuhika aha kaana ka kana.

EMBEERA YA KANA EY'OKUMANYA, EKAAKA KA KANA ak'enju, buzima nikikukundwa munonga. Orikuhika aha OKUMANYA EBIRIKUBAHO wenka, aha MBEERA YA KANA, nabaasa kwega ebintu byenyini, ensi nk'oku eri.

Orikuhika aha kaana ka kana ak'enju, taliyo kutaanisa kwona akamwenurizi, namanya kuraba omu kumanyisa obwomeezi n'okufa, ayebwa obwengye, amanya ebiri aheeru.

Omu KWIIRA MUNONGA nitubaasa kugira okwereka kw'EMBEERA Y'OKUZINDUKA. Omu MBEERA Y'OKUZINDUKA nitubaasa kugira okwereka kw'OKWEYOREKA. Omu MBEERA Y'OKWEYOREKA nitubaasa kugira okwereka kw'OKUMANYA EBIRIKUBAHO.

Twaba nitwenda kuhika aha KUYEREKANA KW'OKUMANYA, aha KWEYOREKA, twine kukora n'OKUMANYA aha hi n'obunaku obu. Nikwo buzima hano omu nsi ey'omubiri ahatwine kukorera OKUYEREKANA OKUMANYA, orikwerekana hano nayerekana omuri byoona, omu miringo yoona ey'Eisaza.

EKIBUMBE KY'OMUNTU ni ZODIAKA ERIYO N'ETUURA kandi omu kirikujumira kyoona omuri ikumi na ibiri, okumanya nikukwire munonga.

Nikihuta OKUYEREKANA okumanya omu bicweka ikumi na bibiri eby'ekibumbe ky'omuntu kandi ekyo n'okukora ebikorwa bya zodiaka.

Aries, nefeera omutwe; Taurus, ekyerero; Gemini, amaboko, amaguru n'ebihaha; Kansa, ekireezi kya timo; Leo omutima; Virgo, orubaju, enduru; Libra, ensya; Scorpio, ebicweka by'omubiri by'ebishuba; Sagitario, emiringa y'omukono; Kapurikoni, amaguru; Akwariyo, emikono; Piscis, ebigyere.

Nikibi munonga ngu zodiaka eriho n'etuura eya MIKIRO-KOOSIMO omuntu, ekwire munonga. Nikyetetaagisa okuhikiiriza aha kwemererwa okwa SÚPER-ESFUERZOS, OKUYEREKANA KW'OKUMANYA omu kimwe kimwe eky'EBIRIKO BYA ZODIAKA EBIRI IKUMI NA BIBIRI.

Omushana n'Okumanya biri ebintu bibiri eby'ekintu kimwe; aha mutindo gw'ahaansi gw'Okumanya, omutindo gw'ahaansi gw'omushana; aha mutindo ogurikukirayo gw'Okumanya, omutindo ogurikukirayo gw'omushana.

Nitwetaaga OKUYEREKANA OKUMANYA kworeka aha mushana kandi kwetekateeka buri kimwe eky'ebicweka ikumi na bibiri ebya zodiaka yaitu ya MICRO-CÓSMICO. Zodiaka yaitu yoona eine kuba omushana n'obununuuzi.

Omulimo na Zodiaka yaitu gwatandika na ARIES. Yiikara OMWEGI omu ntebe y'okwikara neruhuka n'omutima omuriiraine kandi omwire, omwire omuri bwona bw'obwengye. Gara omwegyereza amaisho gaawe kugira ngu tihariho kintu kyona omu nsi ekirikumugira omunaku, teekateeka ngu omushana ogurikwera ogwa ARIES gujuuza obwengye bwaawe, gumizamu omu mbeera y'okuteekateeka obwire bwona obu orikwenda reero oririmbe orukundo orurikukirayo orwa Mantram AUM orikuguraho omunwa na A, orikugurikagura na U kandi orikugara na M orikwera.

Orukundo orurikukirayo orwa Mantram AUM omumwekumi n'ekyenyini ekihangwa EKIRIKUTIINA OMU BWENURUZI, ahabw'okugira ngu nirwehirira amaani ga TATA, orikukundwa munonga, OW'OMURIISA orikuramizibwa munonga n'OMWOYO ORIKWERA orikukirayo obwengye. Vocal A nehirira amaani ga TATA, vocal U nehirira amaani g'OW'OMURIISA, vocal M nehirira amaani g'OMWOYO ORUKWERA. AUM ni MANIRAMU y'OMUNYABWENGE.

Omwegyereza ashemereire kurimba orukundo oru orurikukirayo okukora oku murundi ina omu kikora eki kya ARIES reero yemerere n'obuhabuzi omu bureezi arikurambura omukono gwa buryo omu maisho arikuhindura omutwe emirundi mushanju omu maisho, mushanju enyima, mushanju arikugyenda omu rubaju rwa buryo, mushanju arikugyenda omu rubaju rw'obumoso n'okugyenda kw'okugira ngu omushana gwa ARIES gukore omu bwengye orikuyerekana ebizina pineal n'ebizina pituitaria ebiturikurekyera okumanya EBIRIKUBAMBIRA HAIGURU AHABW'ENSI.

Nikihuta ngu OMUSHANA GWA ARIES guhinguke omu bwengye bwaitu arikwerekana OKUMANYA, arikuhindura amaani ag'obwihisho agari omu EBIZINA PITUITARIA na PINEAL.

ARIES n'akabonero ka RA, RAMA, omwana gw'entaama. MANIRAMU RA orikukirayo, noririmba nk'oku kirikubemereire, nekanga emiriro ey'omugongo hamwe n'ebicweka mushanju by'aha mugongo.

ARIES n'akabonero ka zodiaka ak'omuriro, ayebwa amaani g'amaani kandi MICRO-CÓSMOS OMUNTU nagata ekyo okurikukurata n'engyerreka zaawe z'okuteekateeka, okumanya n'okukora.

HITLER, owaazairwe omu ARIES, akakoresa omutindo ogu gw'amaani omu muringo ogurikubanza, kwonka tushemereire kwikiriza ngu omu kutandika, butakaihire kukora eky'obushazi ky'okwatsya abantu omu ntaro ya kabiri y'ensi yoona, akakoresa amaani ga ARIES omu muringo ogurikwombeka, arikwambura omutindo gw'obwomeezi bw'ABANTU BA GERMANY.

Tubasaize kwebanga okukuraba omu kumanyisa obwomeezi ngu abazairwe omu ARIES barwana munonga n'omugole.

Abazairwe omu ARIES baine okwenda okurwana nibarikurwaana munonga omu butonde.

Abazairwe omu ARIES nibeezira nibasaize kwemereza omu makampani agari agahango kandi okubihikiiriza omu kuteera.

Hariho omu bazairwe omu ARIES ekihagaro eky'amaani eky'okwenda kukoresa amaani g'okwenda omu muringo gw'obwegyendereza, omutindo gwa HITLER, ANTI-ABANTU kandi erikubanza.

Abazairwe omu ARIES nibakunda munonga amagara agari abekererezi, kwonka ARIES nyingi nibakunda obusiraamu kandi omu busiraamu ekyo tikiriho.

Omu mbeera ARIES ni eryanyi, okwesiga, obwegombo n'obugumiizi bwa mazima.

Ekikombo kya ARIES n'EKYOMA, ejanja, RUBÍ, ibara, ROKO, ekintu, OMURIRO.

Abazairwe omu ARIES nibakundisa obushweere n'abantu ba LIBRA, ahabw'okugira ngu omuriro n'omuyaga nibetegyereza gye.

Abazairwe omu ARIES ku baraabe nibenda kushemererwa omu bushweere, bashemereire kuhweraana n'ekihagaro ky'obushaza.
