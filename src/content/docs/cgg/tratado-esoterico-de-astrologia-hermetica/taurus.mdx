KUTANDIKA 21 OMWEZI GWA KASESE KUSHA KUSHA OMWEZI GWA KAMWENA

Ahabw'okuba Taurus nigo MURINGO GW'OMUSAIZIMU ogurikutegeka EKIRUMBA KY'OKUGAMBA, nda egi erikutanisa EIZOBA, EKIGAMBO, nikishemereire ngu twetegyereze omu isomo eri amajambo ga YOOHANE, obu yaagizire ati: "Omu kutandika hakaba hariho EIZOBA kandi EIZOBA rikabaho na Ruhanga kandi EIZOBA rikaba Ruhanga, kurigye byona bikakorwa kandi hatari kurigye tihakakorwa kantu konakamo".

Harimu oburagane musanju bw'ensi, obusiisani musanju obwakozirwe n'amaani g'Eizooba, n'enyimba, n'omuriro.

Esiisani ya mbere eri ahagati y'OMURYO GUTAKOZIIRWE aha Ruhanga.

Oburagane bwa kabiri bw'ensi nibwo nsi zihuriire omu mwanya ogutahwaho.

Oburagane bwa kashatu bw'ensi nigo mugato gw'enyangye zoona eziri omu mwanya ogutahwaho.

Oburagane bwa kana bw'ensi n'EIZOBA eriturikira n'amateeka n'emiringo yaaryo.

Oburagane bwa kataano bw'ensi nigwo mirongozo yoona y'omuringo gw'eizooba.

Oburagane bwa mukaaga bw'ensi nigo mushaija gwena, n'emiringo musanju n'obushwere oburikukirwa abantu.

Oburagane bwa mushanju bw'ensi nibwo obusiisani musanju obw'enyika y'OMURUNGU oguri ahansi y'obushaija bw'Ensi.

Enyimba, EIZOBA, eryashirirweho LOGOS omu bwangu musanju bw'enyimba, ribugire obusiisani bwena.

Oburagane bwa mbere bw'ensi, omuriro DO. Oburagane bwa kabiri bw'ensi, omuriro SI. Oburagane bwa kashatu bw'ensi, omuriro LA. Oburagane bwa kana bw'ensi, omuriro SOL. Oburagane bwa kataano bw'ensi, omuriro FA. Oburagane bwa mukaaga bw'ensi, omuriro MI. Oburagane bwa mushanju bw'ensi, omuriro RE. Hanyuma byona bigaruka aha Ruhanga n'omuriro DO.

Hatariho enyimba, hatariho EIZOBA, hatariho EIZOBA ERIHAIRE, kikaba kitarikubaasika kubaho kw'obusiisani MUSANJU.

DO-RE-MI-FA-SOL-LA-SI. SI-LA-SOL-FA-MI-RE-DO. Entegyereza musanju z'EIZOBA ERIHAIRE, zigamba omu byona ebyakozirwe, ahabw'okuba omu kutandika hakaba hariho EIZOBA.

Oburagane bwa mbere bw'ensi nibutegekwa n'ENTEGYEREZA ENUNU, n'ENTEGYEREZA ERAIKURU. Oburagane bwa kabiri bw'ensi nibutegekwa n'ENTEGYEREZA SHATU. Oburagane bwa kashatu bw'ensi nibutegekwa n'entegeereza mukaaga. Oburagane bwa kana bw'ensi nibutegekwa n'entegeereza ikumi na ibiri. Oburagane bwa kataano bw'ensi nibutegekwa n'entegeereza makumi abiri na ina. Oburagane bwa mukaaga bw'ensi nibutegekwa n'entegeereza makumi ana na munaana. Oburagane bwa mushanju bw'ensi nibutegekwa n'entegeereza makumi kenda na mukaaga.

Obu tugamba aha EIZOBA, nitugamba n'omuriro gw'enyimba, n'ENTEGYEREZA, n'OMURUNGU n'obusiisani bushatu bwa MAHAVAN na CHOTAVAN ebirikubugira obusiisani buhame.

ABARIKUSHERURA n'ABARIKUSHERURA OBUHUUHA, nibajura obusiisani bukye, n'OBUSIISANI OBURAIKURU, nibajura oburagane bubiri bw'ensi, obu omu mazima burimu OBUSIISANI MUSANJU, oburagane musanju bw'ensi ebirikubugirwa EIZOBA, n'enyimba, n'OMURYO GURIKURIKIRA n'OBUTAAHI bw'obwiire bwa mbere.

Buri omwe aha OBUSIISANI MUSANJU n'ekintu ekiramu ekihuumura, kikahurra kandi kigumireho.

Kuruga omu muringo gw'OKUSHERURA, nitubaasa kwemeza ngu buri kugyenda ahaiguru, n'ebyevayo by'okugyenda ahansi. Torikubaasa kutaaha hatariho okutahayo. Okubanza oshemereire kutaaha hanyuma otashe.

Twenda kumanya OBUSIISANI, tushemereire okubanza kumanya ebiri ahaiguru na ebiriyo ahansi, ahabw'okuba byombi nibishoborora embeera zyoona n'ebyabuzaale bya OBUSIISANI ebi turikwenda kwega, kumanya.

Eky'okureeberaho: Omu bwire obu abashomesa barikurwanira okutaaha omu mwanya, nibakora byevayo birungi bingi omu nsi y'ebitini.

Okukora OBUSIISANI MUSANJU kwabaasikire kurugirira omu izooba, kurugirira omu ijambo, kurugirira omu nyimba.

Abeegi baitu ABA GNÓSTICOS tibarikushemereire kwehurira ebi biri amaani gashatu agaayetwa ISHE-OMWANA-OMWOYO ORIKWERA. Amaani gashatu aga nigago gashiraho ENTANDIKWA ENUNU.

Egi niyo kwemeza okurikwera, okuzirika okurikwera, okugaitwa okurikwera; RUHANGA orikwera, ORWEMERERO orikwera, ORUTAFWA orikwera.

Omu mashanyarazi, ebi nibyo bisika bushatu EBIRUNGI-EBIBI-EBIRIKUGYENDA. Hatariho ekikora bw'ebisika bushatu ebi, obutaho bwona tiburikubaasika.

Omu Byeshomesa ESOTÉRICA GNÓSTICA, amaani GASHATU agarikwekuriza gataire amaranzi aga: SURP-OTHEOS; SURP-SKIROS; SURP-ATHANATOS. AMAANI GARIKUKYENDA, GARIKWEMEZA, GARIKURUNGI. AMAANI MABI, AMAANI G'OKUZIRIKA, AMAANI G'OKURWANISA. AMAANI GARIKUGAITA, AMAANI GARIKUREKURA, AMAANI GARIKWEKUURURA.

Amaani gashatu aga omu MURINGO GW'OKUTANDIKA nigashemereire kuba nka bwende bushatu, obwengye bushatu, obumwe bushatu. Buri omwe aha maani gashatu agaa arimu ebyokubaasa byona eby'ebishatu. Embeera yoona yaali eri, aha ihururizana ryabo, buri omwe aha bo naragira entandika yaabo: oburungi, obubi nari okugyenda.

Nikishemeza okureeba amaani gashatu gaayija omu kukora: nibyeyahaana, nibagendera hare hanyuma bagaruka kugaaitana kuhanga endagaano mpya ezirikuza nsi, obutaho obupya.

Aha RUHANGA, amaani gashatu nigo LOGOS MUNU, egye ly'OMURYO omu bwomeezi oburi omu kwegurura.

ENTEGYEREZA Y'OKUTANDIKA EY'ENTANDIKWA ENUNU EZIRIKUKIRWA OMU KWIKARA yatandika n'okwihaho okw'omushaija n'omukazi kw'eizooba ahabw'okuba omu kutandika hakaba hariho eizooba, kandi eizooba rikaba hamwe na RUHANGA kandi eizooba rikaba Ruhanga. Kurigye ebintu byona bikakorwa kandi hatari kurigye tihakabaasa kukorwa kintu.

Kurugirira aha ENTEGYEREZA ERAIKURU YA HEPTAPARAPARSHINOKH (ENTEGYEREZA Y'OMUSANJU), byatahirweho ekikaikuru musanju omu BWIGO habw'okwombeka omuringo gw'eizooba ogu.

Kurugirira aha ENTEGYEREZA ERAIKURU YA TRIAMAZIKAMNO (ENTEGYEREZA Y'EBISATU), ABA ELOHIM bakegyenda omu bibinja bishatu omu buli kaikuru okuririmba kurugirira aha LITURGIA y'OMURUNGU.

Omurimo gw'okuhindura PRAKRITI, okugira ngu BWIGO, okugira ngu NYINA OBUSIISANI, ERIJU eryaikuru, nigo murimo buri obwiire ogw'omurikwera bingi TEOMERSMALOGOS, AMAANI GA KASHATU.

Omu buli kaikuru bibinja bishatu byatekatekaniizibwe bw'eti; Okubanza, OMUGAMBI. Okwa kabiri, OMUKAZI OMUGAMBI. Okwa kashatu: ekibinja ky'ABA ELOHIM ekiriyo.

Twabaasa kureeba ngu ABA ELOHIM nibeegonda, hati nikiirukire ngu bakatahano batyo omu muringo gw'OMUSHAIJA, OMUKAZI na OGW'EGIRE, kurugirira aha ENTANDIKWA ERAIKURU YA TRIAMAZIKAMNO OBUSIISANI ENUNU.

OMUGAMBI n'OMUKAZI OMUGAMBI omu maisho g'eitaara y'okushabira ahaitaara na aha itaaka ry'eikaikuru, ekitebe ky'ABA ELOHIM ekirikwegonda.

ENTEGYEREZA Z'OMURUNGU zikaririmbwa kandi okwihaho okw'omushaija n'omukazi kw'eizooba kukahindura ERIJU eryaikuru ery'obwigo kandi ensi yazaairwa.

Abamalaika nibahanga n'amaani g'eizooba. Ekirumba nikyo nda erikutanisa eizooba.

Tushemereire KUZUKUURA OBWENGYE omu EIZOBA, omu EKIRUMBA KY'OKUTANDIKA, okugira ngu obwiire bumwe tubaase kujura OMURYO GURIKURIKIRA N'ENSI NUNU ey'obwiire bwa mbere.

OBWENGYE nibwotsire omu kirumba kyaitu, turi abatari kumanya n'eizooba, twine kukora OBWENGYE bwona obw'EIZOBA.

Nibagira ngu okutiima n'ezahabu. Nitugira ngu hariho okutiima kw'omusheesho. Kibi kugamba obu oshemereire kutiima, nk'okutiima obu oshemereire kugamba.

Hariho obwiire obu kugamba n'omusheesho, hariho obwiire obu okutiima nabwo n'omusheesho.

Nibahindukire nk'eihwa erirungi, erijwiire ebyererezi, kwonka rihabire omukooto, nigwo majambo marungi, kwonka agataire aha ari kukora kurugirira ebi ari kugamba.

Kwonka nibahindukire nk'eihwa erirungi, erijwiire ebyererezi kandi erijwiire omukooto, nigwo majambo marungi kandi agataire aha ari kukora kurugirira ebi ari kugamba.

Nikikuru kumara n'okwegomba kw'eizooba, nikyetaagisa kugamba n'obwegendereza, omu muringo gw'OBWENGYE kandi oguhikireho. Turi kwetaaga kukora OBWENGYE bw'eizooba.

Harimu obujunaanizibwa omu majambo kandi okucwera n'eizooba n'okushwera. Tihariho orikugira obushoboorozi bw'okucwera omuntu; n'obushangwa kubandiriza omuntu; n'obusheegye kwegaana obwomeezi bw'abandi.

Amazooba g'omusheesho nigagwa aha itwe bwangu nari bwangu, nk'omuringo gw'okuhana. Amazooba g'okubandiriza, agabi, bagaruka buri obwiire aha ayagajwire bahindukire amabaare agarikugwisa.

Omu bwire obundi, obu abantu bakaba batakatunga okwegomba bingi n'obunyakage bw'obushangwa ebi, abashaija bakaba nibatwara ente aha kibaaro barikuririmba omu muringo gurungi kandi ogurikwetenga.

ENTE, ENTE NSHEKAZI, endamaana, nibashemereire n'enyimba, bahindukire omuringo gw'omusaiizimu gwa TAURUS, endagaano y'eizooba, y'enyimba.

Omu BIGAMBO BIKURU, enshaija erikugobererwa PRITHU neehunga ehindukire ENTE kandi neeyamiire BRAHAMA. Kwonka omu BRAHAMA ogu n'omuntu wa mbere owa TRIMURTI Indostán. VACH, ENTE nigo wa kabiri kandi VIRAH omushaija murungi, endamaana, KABIR, LOGOS, nigo muntu wa kashatu.

BRAHAMA nigo ISHE. ENTE niyo NYINA OMURUNGI, BWIGO; ENDAMAANA niyo KABIR, LOGOS.

ISHE, NYINA, OMWANA, egi niyo TRIMURTI PURANICA. ISHE nigo BWENGYE. NYINA nigo OKUKUNDA, OMWANA nigo LOGOS, EIZOBA.

ENTE ASTRAL ey'amaguru gataano egi Coronel Olcott arikurora, omu maisho g'eihanga ly'amabaare ery'aha Karli; ente eshoborora kandi ey'ekyama omu shaija omuto arikureeba omu Andes, nk'omurinzi oguriyo ogw'obutungi ebi abaiyi bazireebire, erashobora NYINA OMURUNGI, REA, CIBELES, erikukura byoona omu MUNTU OGW'AMAZIMA, omu MWEGYESA EYEHINDUIRIRE.

GAUTAMA omwezi aha nyangye nari GOTAMA, nikimanyisa kwenda akairu k'ENTE. Buri MUHENGERE, buri mwebembera w'ENTE, narikubaasa kukoresa omuriro JAINO ogw'ENTE kutaaha omu itaaka, ebyombeko, ebikaikuru n'obunyage bwa JINAS.

N'amaani ga NYINA OMURUNGI nitubaasa kushasha AGHARTI, obunyage bwa JINAS obw'ensi eriyo ahansi.

TAURUS netutuma KUTEEKATEEKA. Tukijukye ngu MERCURIO yaijwiire ENTE ZA EIZOBA.

TAURUS nategeka EKIRUMBA KY'OKUTANDIKA Nikikuru ngu KUNDALINI agabye omu birenge byaitu by'obutaahe byahindukire EIZOBA, nikwo turikubaasa kukoresa omuriro JAINO kutaaha omu BUNYAGE bwa JINAS.

Omu bwire obu obwa TAURUS tushemereire kutwara OMURYO aha kirumba kyaitu ekirikuhanga n'ekigyendererwa ky'okukyeteekanisa okwija kw'OMURUNGU.

Omwegi yikare omu ntebe erikukama; azibire amaisho gyehabw'okuba kantu kahoona omu nsi enu kamushoshoore, ajwaze omutwe, agye omu mutwe gwe ebiteekateeka, ebyetengo, ebyeteko byona. Yeyeendereze hati ngu OMURYO ogurikwongyezibwa omu bwire bwa ARIES omu kabungo kaagwo, omu mutwe gwaagwo, nigugyenda na TAURUS aha kirumba eky'okutandika.

Aririmbire omwegi MANTRAM AUM. Aguruke akanwa ke n'A, yeyeendereze ngu OMURYO nigushura kuruga omu mutwe kugyenda aha kirumba; ayegambire U, yeyeendereze omu bwoomezi ngu omuriro nigwijaaza akanwa; oshemereire kuziba akanwa kurungi okuririmba U.

Ebaruha y'ahaheru niyo M, aziba ebibero, arora obuhuumo n'amaani nk'okumaraho ebyabire aha kanwa. Omurimo ogu nigukorwa kuririmba emirundi ina MANTRAM AUM erikugumira.

Omu GLÁNDULA TIROIDES erikushutama omutaka ogw'amazzi, harimu ekicweka eky'amaani eky'OKUTWI KW'AMAGARA. N'obwegeso bwa TAURUS, OKUTWI KW'AMAGARA nikukura, amaani g'okuhurra enyimba za'busiisani, enyimba z'ensi, ENTEGYEREZA Z'OMURUNGU ezirikuza obusiisani musanju kurugirira aha ntegyereza z'enyimba.

GLÁNDULA TIROIDES eri omu nsingo, omu kirumba ekirikuhanga.

GLÁNDULA TIROIDES neetegwekwa VENUS kandi PARATIROIDES neetegwekwa MARTE.

TAURUS n'ejuura ya VENUS. Eibaare ya TAURUS niyo ÁGATA, ekikomo ky'omuringo ogu nigo COBRE.

Omu kukora twareeba ngu ABA TAURINOS tibarikushemereire kushwerwa abantu ABA ACUARIO, ahabw'okuba nibagwamu kukirwa, ahabw'okubura kugaita.

Omuringo gwa TAURUS niguhama, ogw'itaaka, nigwegomba okuhama kandi nk'omuringo gwa ACUARIO n'ogw'omwika, ogurikwegurura okuhinduka, nikiirukire ngu tibarikubaasa kugaaitana.

ABA TAURINOS nibashemereire kuba nka nte, abagumisiriza kandi abakorera, kwonka obu bagyema nibatiina nka ENTE NSHAIJA.

ABA TAURINOS nibagenda omu bwomeezi bwabo n'OKUGWA OKWAIKURU OMU KUNDA, nibotiima, nibagumya, nibakuratira ekirenge aha kirenge, nk'ente, omu muhanda oguhendire.

ABA TAURINOS nibotiina, ekiniga omu ABA TAURINOS nikikura obunaku kandi kigwa omu kurasa kungi nk'omuriro.

Omuringo ogutaine mugasho ogwa TAURUS nibeegonda, barire, barwane, abegombe, abanyiniga, abeegamba.

Omuringo ogurikukirayo ogwa TAURUS gwijwiire OKUKUNDA, bakunda enyimba zoona, Obwengye, bakora bashemerirwe habw'obuntu, bagira obwengye, abetegyereza, abeesigwa, abeesimbi omu bugyenzi, Ishe murungi, Nyina murungi, mugyenzi murungi, muma murungi, omuntu murungi, n'ebindi.

OBURUNGI BW'EKYAMA bw'ENTE MITRAICO eritaburire abantu abataetiima ab'obwiire obubi bw'ekyasha makumi abiri, yakekyemera bwangu omu kusiisani ky'ENDAMAANA YA ZAABU.

ENTE ERIKWERA neerekana ISIS, NYINA OMURUNGI kandi ENDAMAANA yaayo neerekana MERCURIO omweherereza w'ABA RUHANGA, KABIR, LOGOS.

Omu muringo gw'ENTE nibashemeriremu esotericamente PLÉYADES, CABRILLAS nari ENTE ZA CELESTES, ezi zirikuba musanju, kwonka omu mazima zirikukiraho abari muka ikumi, n'obusiisani bwazo Mayas, enyangye yaayo ALCYONE, n'abarikumuzoora ATLAS, TAIGETE, n'ebindi.

Enzira ya nyangye y'OMURUNGU nari ALDEBARÁN, mununi arikubaasa kumurikira omu bwerere na MARTE, nibakugana omu muringo gurungi HYADAS, ENTE ENDI CELESTE.

Aha nyuma y'OMURUNGU n'owa HAIKURU ORIÓN. Ahaiguru hamwe na North of the CONSTELACIÓN DEL TORO, hariho ekibinja eky'omu busiisani, ekirikushoborora OBUSINGE CEFEO, CÉFIRO NARI ZÉFIRO, owa Kweeni Casiopea; Omurekura Perseo n'omutwe gwa Medusa aha mukono gwe na Andrómeda, omurekuriirwe; obwe ahamaisho haaza omuhano ogurikutungwa na PISCIS na ACUARIO.

Ebyerekano bya TAURUS n'omwalo ogurikukirana omu mwalo gwa hafi nikiikirira okutangaaza.
