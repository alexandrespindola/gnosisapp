22 KUKO GWA KABIRI N'ENA OKUHIKA 23 KUKO GWA MWENDA

PRAKRITI NIIWE NYINA MUKURU, EKINTU EKIRIKUBANZA KY'OBUTONZI.

Omu bwengye oburikukiraho haroho ebintu bingi, ebintu bitarikushushana hamu n'ebice byabyo, kwonka byona ebyo n'emyerere y'EKINTU KIMWE.

EKINTU EKIRIKUBANZA ky'obutonzi n'AKASA ENZIRU eyiri omubwengye bwona, NYINA MUKURU, PRAKRITI.

MAHANVANTARA na PRALAYA n'ebigambo bibiri EBY'ORULIMI ORUKURU by'omugasho muno ebi abegi ba GNÓSTICOS bashemereire kumanyira.

MAHANVANTARA N'OMUSANA MUKURU OW'OMU BWENGYE. PRALAYA N'EKIRO MUKURU OW'OMU BWENGYE. Omu MUSANA ogwo MUKURU haroho obwengye. Obu EKIRO kiyo MUKURU kirikutwara, obwengye buleka kuhaho, bukwatanisa omunda ya PRAKRITI.

Eihanga erikukiraho ririijuure EMYONDO Y'ENJUBA egi erimu MAHANVANTARAS zabu na PRALAYAS zabu.

Obu ezindi ziri omuri MAHANVANTARA, endiijo ziri omuri PRALAYA.

Obukaikuru na butwarane bw'OBWENGYE buzaarwa kandi buraara omunda ya PRAKRITI.

COSMOS yoona ezaarwa omu PRAKRITI kandi erwa omu PRAKRITI. Ensi yoona n'omupiira gw'omuriro ogwaka kandi gurugwa omunda ya PRAKRITI.

Byoona bizaarwa omu PRAKRITI, byoona bizayo omu PRAKRITI. Niiwe NYINA MUKURU.

BHAGAVAD GITA egamba: "PRAKRITI ENENE niire ndiba z'ange, omunsi niingira embuto kandi omuri yo, ai Bharata!, nibazaara abantu boona".

"Ai Kountreya!, PRAKRITI niire ndiba y'amazima ey'ekintu kyona ekirikuzara omu ndiba ezirikushushana, kandi niine mpandiiki PATERNO".

"SATTVA, RAJO na TAMO, ezi GUNAS eshatu (oburemeezi nari emiringo), ezaarirwe omu PRAKRITI, ai iwe ow'amaboko ag'amaani!, zikoma omubiri gwa butunu aha kintu ekizaarirwe".

"Omu byo, SATTVA ekuruguru, ekweru kandi enungi, ekoma aha kintu ekizaarirwe!, ai iwe otarikukora kibi!, kuraba omuri ebiriiriza aha kushemererwa hamu n'okumanya".

"Ai KOUNTREYA!, manya ngu RAYAS n'emiringo y'omutaano kandi n'enzigu y'OKUTORIZA hamu n'ebiriiriza; ei GUNA ekoma aha kintu ekizaarirwe kukora".

Ai Bharata!, manya ngu TAMO ezaarwa omu butamanya kandi ekugyema abantu boona; akoma aha kintu ekizaarirwe kuraba omuri ekyererezi, obunyamurwa hamu n'otulo». (OKUGIRA OBWAMANYI OKUTARIKUGONDA, OTULO TWA BWAMANYI.)

Omu bwiire bwa PRALAYA ENENE ezi GUNAS ESATU ziri omu kwihirira obulingaaniza omubunikiro bw'oburingaaniza obw'OBURINGAANIZA; obu obutarikwihirira bw'ezi GUNAS eshatu burikutandika, obwihirira bwa MAHANVANTARA nibuzaarwa kandi obwengye bwezaarwa omunda ya PRAKRITI.

Omu bwiire bwa PRALAYA ENENE, PRAKRITI NI YIMWE, EHWEREREZI. Omu KWOREKA, omuri MAHANVANTARA, PRAKRITI erikushushana omu MIRONGO eshatu ey'OMU BWENGYE.

Emirogo eshatu ya PRAKRITI omu KWOREKA, n'egi: Okubanza, ey'EIHANGA ERIKUKIRIRHO; Okwa kabiri, ey'OBUTONZI; Okwa kashatu, ey'OMUNTU.

NYINA OWEZAAHURA, omu ihanga erikukiraho; NYINA OWEZAAHURA omu OBUTONZI; NYINA OWEZAAHURA omu muntu. Ezi nizo BAMAIKAIKURU bashatu; BAMARIA bashatu B'OBUSHUBANI.

Abegi ba GNÓSTICOS bashemereire kwetegyereza muno emirogo eshatu ei ya PRAKRITI, habwokuba eki n'ekikuru omu murimo gw'OKUSHIISHA. Kandi, ki RYAGAMBA KUMANYA ngu PRAKRITI egi erimu obuhangwa omuri buri muntu.

Abegi ba GNÓSTICOS tibashemereire kwetangaize obu tubagambira ngu PRAKRITI ya buri muntu egi erimu eiziina lyayo ly'omutaano. Eki nikimanyisa ngu twena nitugira NYINA OWEZAAHURA. Kwetegyereza eki, N'EKIKURU kukuraba omu MURIMO GW'OKUSHIISHA.

OKUZARA OKWA KABIRI n'ekindi kintu. LOGOS EYA KASHATU, OMURiro OWEZAHAURA, ashemereire okubanza kureeta embuto ndiba ya NYINA OWEZAHAURA, bwanyima nikireeta OKUZARA OKWA KABIRI.

Iwe, PRAKRITI, buzima ahabutenazara, omu bwiire bw'okuzara kandi bwanyima y'okuzara.

Omu mutwe ogwa munaana gw'ekitabo eki turyayegyesa omurimo ogurikukora ogurikutwara okuzara okwa kabiri. Hati nituha obwenganizo oburikworeka.

Buri MWEGYESA owa LOGIA ENZIRU agira nyina owezahura, PRAKRITI ye.

Buri MWEGYESA ni mwana w'omwishiki atarikwekwataho. Twegaamiza EBIGAMBO by'amadiini, nitugufuurwa obutenazara oburiho buli hamwe; YESU azaarwa omurimo na amagaba g'OMWOYO OWEZAHAURA, NYINA wa YESU akaba MWISHIKI ATARIKWEKWATAHO.

Ebyahandikirwe by'amadiini nibigamba ngu BUDHA, JÚPITER, ZEUS, APOLO, QUETZALCOATL, FUJI, LAOTSE, n'ebindi, bakaba abaana b'ABAISHIKI ABATARIKWEKWATAHO, abaishiki ahabutenazara, omu bwiire bw'okuzara kandi bwanyima y'okuzara.

Omu nsi ezirukwera omu VEDAS, DEVAKI, OMUISHIKI OW'EHINDU azaara KRISHNA kandi omu BELEM OMUISHIKI MARIA azaara YESU.

Omu CHINA AMAJANDARA, aha mugezi gwa ruzi FUJI, OMUISHIKI HO-AE, ataaha omuti gw'OMUNTU ENENE, agwejereze ekiharo kirungi kandi omu nda ye azaara omurimo na amagaba g'OMWOYO OWEZAHAURA aha CRISTO CHINA FUJI.

Ni emirembe erikukuruza omu OKUZARA OKWA KABIRI ngu okubanza omu LOGOS EYA KASHATU etwarire, OMWOYO OWEZAHAURA, gwiirenda omunda y'omwishiki eya NYINA OWEZAHURA.

OMURiro GW'OMUKAZI n'OMUSHAIJA owa LOGOS EYA KASHATU omu HINDU gumanyirwe n'eiziina KUNDALINI kandi gumanyisirwe n'omugongo gw'omuriro ogwaka.

NYINA OWEZAAHURA ni ISIS, TONANTZÍN, KALI nari PARVATI, omukazi wa SHIVA, LOGOS EYA KASHATU kandi eky'okubonabona ekirikugira amani n'ENOMI ESAAGIRE.

Omugongo gushemereire kwimuka kuraba omu mukwongo gw'enomi esaagire, omugongo gushemereire kureeta embuto endiba ya NYINA OWEZAAHURA, obwo nikiza okwitwa okutazara omurungi na OKUZARA OKWA KABIRI.

KUNDALINI, omumwanya gw'amaani, ni omuriro gw'EIZUBA oguri omunda y'ekicweka ekirikuhendera kiri aha iwaju ry'omugongo.

Obu omuriro ogurikwera gwimukire, gwiimuka omuri mukwongo kugienda aha mugongo gwona, guzibura ebicweka mushanju by'omugongo hamu n'okuzeera PRAKRITI.

OMURiro gwa KUNDALINI guri gumuheru mushanju gw'amani kandi kigambirweyo kwimuka aha kaguru eka mushanju egi ya OMURiro kugira ngu ogere OKUZARA OKWA KABIRI.

Obu PRAKRITI ereete embuto n'omuriro ogukorozire negira amani marungi okutuhweera.

OKUZARA bwanyima ya ekyo n'okUGIINGIRA omu BUKAMA. Ni kikuru muno kufuurwa omuntu okazaarwa emirundi ebiri. Kikuru ooyo aZAARIRWE omurundi GWA KABIRI.

Ooyo orikutoorana KUZARA bwanyima, ooyo orikutoorana KUTOROKWA KWA HAMAHEREZI, ashemereire kurugaho omu butonde bwe GUNAS ESATU eza PRAKRITI.

Ooyo otarikurugaho GUNA SATTVA, yeebwa omu kabale ka TEORÍAS kandi areka OMURIMO GW'OKUSHIISHA.

Ooyo otarikurugaho RAYAS, ayombeka EGO LUNAR kuraba omu BUSHA, OKUTORIZA, OBUSHUUZI.

Titugashemereire kwebwa ngu RAYAS n'ENZIGU yoona ey'okutoriza kw'ebisoro n'omutaano gurikukiraho amani.

RAYAS n'ENZIGU y'okukwatwa. Eki eky'enyima, omumwanya gw'amaani, n'okutandika kw'okutoriza kwona.

Ooyo orikutoorana kurugaho OKUTORIZA, ashemereire okubanza kurugaho GUNA RAYAS.

Ooyo otarikurugaho TAMO, burijo aija kugira OBWAMANYI oburikugonda, aija kuba omunyamurwa, aija kureka OMURIMO GW'OKUSHIISHA, ahabw'obusiisi, okuhwera, obunyamurwa, obutagira kwenda, okukara, obutagira kusononga, aija kuba ekintu ky'embabazi z'ensito z'ensi enu kandi aija kurugwa omu butamanya.

Kigambirwe ngu bwanyima y'okufa, abantu abarikugira omutima SATTVICO bagyenda omu bihariro by'omu maraizo nari AMABUKAMA g'obunyerezi n'obusirikale ahu bashemerererwa ebirungi byona batakeera KUGYENDA omu ndiba enyakahire.

ABAIIHI bayoleka gye muno, omuri abandi boona, ngu abantu abarikugira omutima RAYASICO bakugyenda nabo nari BAKUZA omu nsi enu omu muringo GUMWE nari bagutegyereza omukoro ogw'okugira omu ndiba enyakahire, kwonka batagira omugisha ogw'ebihiiro omu AMABUKAMA agirikushushana ag'obuhwezi.

Buri MUNTU AHWEREREZI amanyire butunu ngu bwanyima y'okufa abantu abarikugira omutima TAMOSICO bakingira omu NSI EZIRIKWITA oburikushangwa aha DANTE omu BWENGYE BWE, omunsi eyirimu obucweka bw'ensi okuri omunda y'oburaaro bw'ensi oburi ahansi.

Ni RYAGAMBA KURUGAAHO omu butonde bwitu omunda GUNAS eshatu, obu twendire kukora eby'amazima OMURIMO GW'OKUSHIISHA n'omugisha.

BHAGAVAD GITA egamba: "Obu omuhombo arikureeba ngu GUNAS nizo ezirikukora, kandi amanyire OOYO orikukiraho GUNAS, obwo atuura aha MANSI y'ange".

Bingi abarikutoorana omutindo gw'okukuraho GUNAS eshatu nituleeta ngu omurimo ogurikukora gurugaho EGO LUNAR nigugira GUNAS eshatu.

Ooyo orikugumaho atari murungi kandi atarikulemesa GUNAS, akakora ngu GUNAS nizo ezirikukora, kandi agumaho atarikwekwata, habwokuba yamara kurugaho EGO LUNAR.

Ooyo orikwehurira kuba bamwe omu kushemererwa nari omu kubabazibwa, orikutuura omu MANSI ye yawe; orikureka omugisha gw'omutwaro gw'entale aha kaito nari aha gongo ly'ezaabu; orikugumaho atarikwegyerereza aha kirungi hamu n'ekitarikurungi, omu kutegeereza nari omu kusiima, omu kusiimwa nari omu kutasiimwa, aha murukuratane nari aha mulwanisa kandi akareka aha murimo gwona ogwa EGOÍSTA n'ow'ensi, habwokuba yamara kurugaho GUNAS ESATU kandi ARUGAHO EGO LUNAR.

Ooyo atagira okukwatwa, orugwaho omuriro gw'OBUSHUUZI omu bicweka makumi ana na mwenda BY'OBUGAMBIRIZA bw'omutima, kurugaho GUNAS ESATU kandi aragaho EGO LUNAR.

"Ensi, amazzi, omuriro, omuyaga, eihanga, omutima, okutegeereza hamu na EGO, nizo bitongore munaana ahu PRAKRITI yange egabirwe". Nikihandikirwe, ebi nibigambo ebirungi.

"Obu burikusaasa omusana ogu MUKURU, abantu boona nibooreka nibarugaho omu PRAKRITI ETEKOREKWE; kandi omu bwire bw'ekiro, bibaheerera omu ETETUMERE EYO".

Aha nyima ya PRAKRITI ETETUMERE haroho ABUTAHUMWIRE OKUHINGURRA. Ni kigambirwe okubanza kwingiira omu BUTAGAMBA obutoorane okwenyima omunda ya ABUTAHUMWIRE OKUHINGURRA.

OMUKAZI MUKURU OWEZAABWE OW'ENSI n'ekyo ekirikwetwa OKUGONDA. Niiwe ISIS, ooyo otagira omuntu akaguza ekitiindo; omu rurimi rw'OMUGONGO twamusiima.

AMADIINI GOONA MAKURI gakora omukoro gw'OMUKAZI OMU BWENGYE; niiwe ADONÍA, INSOBERTA, REA, CIBELES, TONANTZÍN, n'ebindi.

OMUHEEREZI owa MWISHIKI NYINA nagira kutoorana; ebyahandikirwe ebirukwera nibigamba: Musabe kandi muliheebwa, mukonkone kandi muli zigurirwa.

Omu NDA ENENE eya NYINA OWEZAAHURA eyo nsi yombekirwe. VIRGO atereka ENDA.

Virgo ekwataniise muno n'enda enyere hamu n'omutindo ogwa Páncreas hamu n'enyanja ya LARGEHANS egirikuhisha INSULINA ey'omugasho muno omu kufuurwa kw'omushororo.

Amani egi erikuimuka omu nsi, aha kukwatagana aha nda, nigiizibwa n'ebihambo bya ndozi ebirikubitebeza kandi bishemeye kuhika aha mutima.

Omu ky'omugongo kya VIRGO (OMWISHIKI OW'OMWEYA), itwe, turagondesa ensuro n'omubiri gutari mwangu, tushemereire kuha endozi entindo ento, n'ekigendereerwa ngu amani egi erikuimuka omu nsi, yiizire aha nda n'ebihambo bya ndozi.

Omu BEGI ba GNÓSTICOS bashemereire kwetegyereza obw'amaani bw'endima ekutwa orunda hamu n'okumalirra ebyara ebi bikwatire aha kujanjaara.

Abegi ba mukama BUDHA bagumaho n'endima enungi emwe omu izooba.

Ensuuri n'ebijuma nibihwereza okurima kw'abantu abatuura aha runyerezi VENUS.

Omu ngano n'ebindi by'omuringo gwona, haroho okutandika kw'okugira amani okurungi.

Kugamba GANAADO, ente, emishaija, n'ekibi kibi eky'abantu bano kandi n'ekika LUNAR.

Omu nsi haroho kika kyamira kubiri omu ndwano egi erikuguma EKIZUBA n'EKYAMWEZI.

ABRAHAM, IA-SAC, IA-CAB, IO-SEP, bakaba basima ENTE ESAAGIRE, IO, nari, owa DIOSA EGIPCIA IS-IS; obu ya MARIMUZEEZI, nari shi twagambira OMUHINDURA ESDRAS ogwahindura ebyegyeso za MARIMUZEEZI, agamba okutunga EKICONCO ky'ENTE n'ensana kandi omushaazi gwazo gwegwire aha mitwe ya boona, okukira abaana baabo.

ENTE ESAAGIRE ni eky'okubonabona kya NYINA OWEZAAHURA, ISIS, ooyo otagira omuntu akaguza ekitiindo.

ABEZAIRWE EMIRUNDI EBIRI nibakora EKIKA EKIZUBA, ABANTU B'EKIZUBA. ABANTU b'EKEKA EKIZUBA tibariija kwita ENTE ESAAGIRE. Babezaire EMIRUNDI EBIRI nibo baana b'ENTE ESAAGIRE.

ÉXODO, mutwe XXIX, nobutunu kandi ni MAGIA ENYIRA. Omu mutwe ogwo okugambwa aha MARIMUZEEZI, gusobora butunu omukoro gw'omulembe gw'okugamba ebigyendereerwa eby'okuhungurwa.

EKIKA EKYAMWEZI kibenga ebira ENTE ESAAGIRE. EKIKA EKIZUBA kisima ENTE ESAAGIRE.

H.P.B., akareeba butunu ENTE y'AMAGURU TAANO. Ekaguru eya kashanju yagenda eruga omu jubwe, nayo yakiyeeruza, kukuraho esheeri, n'ebindi.

ENTE eyo yagondekerezibwa owa muheesi omu SADHU, omu nsi za HINDU.

ENTE ESAAGIRE EY'AMAGURU TAANO niyo MURINDA y'ensi n'amakanisa gya JINAS; PRAKRITI, NYINA OWEZAAHURA, okukura omu MUNTU EKIZUBA, amani egirikutureetera kwingiira omu NSI ZA JINAS, omu macumbi gye, omu makanisa gye, omu MIHAMA GYA BAKAMA.

Kintu kimwe ekirikutwitana n'ensi ey'emitwara n'obusaasi bwa JINAS, n'EJANJA enene erikutushemerera kumanya okuronda.

KÁBALA niyo SIENCIA y'ENTE; kusema ebigambo bishatu bya KÁBALA aha nyima, nitugira LA-VA-CA.

EJANJA eya KABA omu MECA okusemwa aha nyima VACA nari EJANJA y'ENTE.

EHEMA ENENE EYA KABA buzima nobutunu EHEMA Y'ENTE. PRAKRITI omu muntu ezeire n'omuriro ogurikwera kandi ehinduka omu ENTE ESAAGIRE EY'AMAGURU ATAANO.

SURA 68 eya KORAN niirungi; omuri yo egamba aha nyima y'amaguru ya ENTE, nk'ekintu esheija, nikugonzeseza okuzora abaafu, shi abantu ba LUNAR (ebisoro EBY'OMUNDA), kureetera aha MWERO ogw'OBUKULU obw'EDIINI EKIZUBA.

Itwe, ABA GNÓSTICOS, tusima ENTE ESAAGIRE turiire OMUKAZI OWEZAAHURA.

N'obuhwezi bwa ENTE ESAAGIRE EY'AMAGURU TAANO, nitubaasa kwingiira n'omubiri omu byaro bya JINAS omunda y'AMAKANISA gya BAKAMA.

Obu omwegi areebire omu bwiire bungi ENTE Y'AMAGURU ATAANO, omu NYINA OWEZAAHURA kandi amusabe ngu ayombeeke omubiri gwe omu byaro bya JINAS, nagira kusingura.

Ekikuru ni kwimuka bwanyima y'obutanda atarikubeera muntu otyo, nk'omuntu oroota.

Kuta omubiri omu BWENGYE BWA KANA n'ekintu esheija, ekintu kitari kimwe, kandi eki kibahereke n'obuhwezi bwa ENTE ESAAGIRE ey'amaguru ataano.

Nitwenda kukura ENTE ESAAGIRE buzima omunsi yaitu, kukora abantu hamu n'obwenganizo obw'ensaasi bwa ciencia JINAS.

NYINA OWEZAAHURA ari haihi aha mwana we, ari omunda y'OMUNTU ogwo kandi iwe, buri bwangu iwe, nitushemereire kumuhendera obuhwezi omu bwiire obuzibu.

Haroho obutindo bushatu bw'ebyama: SATTVICOS, RAYASICOS na TAMASICOS. Ebyama SATTVICOS biba byombekirwe n'ebimuli, ngano, ebimuli kandi n'ekyo ekirikwetwa OKUGONDA.

Ebyama RAYASICOS ni amani, obutaano, okuluma, okwengeze, okutamiira, n'ebindi.

Ebyama TAMASICOS buzima nibyombekirwe n'omushaazi kandi n'enyama emyikara, tibagira OKUGONDA, bigulwa kandi biriguzibwa nari byaheebwa n'obusiima, ekitiinisa n'oburemeezi.

Murime ekirikwetaagisa okutuura, nari kirubaire ahansi, munywe amazzi agazirire, mugumye ebyama.

VIRGO niky'omugongo kya MWISHIKI NYINA ow'ENSI, ni enju ya MERCURIO, emyazi gye n'eJASPE n'eESMERALDA.

Omu kukora tumanyire ngu abazaairwe omu VIRGO nibonaazibwa n'okugaba okutaayiruka, kukuraho aha bigendereerwa hamu n'okugwamu amaani omu butonde.

Okugaba, omutima, nibikwetaagisa muno, kwonka obu birikushoberwa omumwanya gwabyo, nikireeta obubi.

Abazaairwe omu VIRGO nibahwereza omu ciencia, Psiquiatría, omutabazi, obutonde, elaburatori, obwegyesi, n'ebindi.

Abazaairwe omu VIRGO tibabaasa kwetegyereza n'abantu ba PISCIS habw'ekyo nitubatebeza kureka abafeerwibwe abarira n'abantu ba Piscianas.

Ekirikukiraho obusaasi n'abantu ba VIRGO n'okuhwera hamu n'okugwamu amaani ebiribamanya. Kwonka, nikireeta amatsiko okumanya ngu obugumiizi obu burikugumaho kubiingura aha mwebembezi, obuhika oku omu bwengye.

Obukugu bwa CRÍTICO-ANALÍTICO bwa VIRGO n'amatsiko kandi omu ABAKUURU GENIOS b'eky'omugongo eki, hari GOETHE, ogwaireete okwiha omu mwebembezi, okuhwera n'okwingiira omu bwengye oburikukiraho obw'obwengye oburikukiraho.

Emero, abazaairwe boona omu VIRGO, TINIIWE GOETHE. Okumanyisa kwembaho hagwa omu butiti bw'eky'omugongo eki, ABAKURATIZE ATEOS, abarwanisa kintu kyona ekirikuhumura aha BWENGYE.

EGOÍSMO y'abantu abarikugamba omu VIRGO, n'ekintu kirikushuta kandi kirikusiisa, kwonka GOETHE ba VIRGO ni basheija, abaruguri muno kandi abatagira kyebagambe.

Abazaairwe omu VIRGO bababazibwa omu kugonda kandi bagyenda omu kushoberwa kwenene, ahabwokuba VENUS, ekyaara ky'okugonda, omu VIRGO eri aha buhungiro.
