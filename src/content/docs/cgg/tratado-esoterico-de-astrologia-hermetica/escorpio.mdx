Abo abarikugumisiriza KUZAIRWA omurindi gwa kabiri okw'obwomeezi oburikwiija, baherereza ekinazi kandi bashindwe N'OBWOMEEZI BUKUMI NA MUNAANA, bataaha omu NSI Z'OMURIIRO, omu handa n'okurira n'okusigwa kw'eminyo.

DIÓGENES akashaka n'entaala ye OMUNTU omu Atenas yoona kandi atamushangire. ABAZAIRWE MIRINDI EBIRI, ABUSAHWA B'OKUSHOBOORA bajetenga kubashaka n'entaala ya DIÓGENES, ab'okushanga baine.

Bariyo abegi ba SEUDO-OCULTISTAS na SEUDO-ESOTERISTAS abarikwenda DIZQUE KWIHARA, baitu nka ABA MUTAKA, obu bamanya esayansi egi eya KIKUMI NA MWENDA, nibafooka abakugu, nibatutyora, nibatukorra obubi n'amaazi gaabo aga butyoori kandi nobwora turikuba omu biro bya ESDRAS, baayehambire ENTE ENKURU nibagamba: "ERAGU LYABYO LITURE OMU NYUMA ITWE NA HA BAAANA BAITU".

Omuhanda ogurikukuriza ha bwiira guteireho ebigyezi birungi. Tarikutaaha kwihaho ha bwiira ababi; twijuke enshomi y'omuti gw'omutiini ogutarirengire. Omuti ogutarizure kibala, guteera no gutairwa omu muriro.

Omu NSI-Z'OMURIIRO nibaguma abegi ba MAANI aba SEUDO-OCULTISMO na SEUDO-ESOTERISMO.

ESCORPIO n'akabonero akarikuteekesa, obutaha bw'ESCORPIÓN ni kyo buta abazigu ba MAITHUNA, abatyoro abari omu butangwa abarikukoraza OMUBIRI, abarikuseebereza LOGOS YA KASHATU, abashura ababi, ab'okuhindurwaho ab'OKWERINDA OMUBIRI, abarikweteera obwagu, etc.

ESCORPIO neehindura EBY'OMUBIRI. ESCORPIO n'enju ya MARTE, omushaija gw'orugamba kandi omu mubiri n'enjuni y'orugamba oruringi hali ABA MAGOS ABARAGURA n'ABAIRAGURA, aha maani g'ORWAAZA n'OMUTAKA.

ORUZAARO RUTAKA nirukoraza muno ekintu kyona ekigumire ha MAITHUNA (EKIHAMA ky'OMUBIRI) TANTRISMO OBURAGURA, ENTE ENKURU, etc.

Abahungire owa ESCORPIO nibabaasa kusiira omu miringo etarikushiisha nari KUZIRUKIRWA omu byona.

Omu kukora twabaasa kuhumuza ngu abahungire owa ESCORPIO nibajuma omu cweka ky'okubanza eky'obwomeezi bwabo kandi bakugize omukundwa ogurikubareetera okugwa omu muringo, baitu omu cweka kya kabiri eky'obwomeezi ebintu byona bihinduka, obwangu bwabo bukuzibwa kimwe.

Abahungire owa ESCORPIO baine okukwenda kukuzibwa kw'OBUSHURE n'okwehunga, tibarikusonyiha omuntu.

Abakazi ba ESCORPIO obwiire bwona bari omu buzibu bw'okushindwa kandi okushinda okwetenga by'empyira bingi omu kitundu ky'okubanza eky'obwomeezi bwabo.

Abashaija ba ESCORPIO nibajuma obutabuha bwingi omu kitundu ky'okubanza eky'obwomeezi bwabo, baitu ahabw'obumanyiso bwabo, bakuzibwa omu cweka kya kabiri eky'obwomeezi bwabo.

Abahungire owa ESCORPIO n'abantu b'amaani, ab'okugwa, abekandiire, ab'amaani.

Abahungire owa ESCORPIO, nka BAANAMUNSAMI, n'abanaamunsami B'OKUSHOBOORA, ab'amazima, ab'okushikiriza, abarikubaasa kwehambira ahabw'obwihire, baitu nka bazigu, nibatiisa muno, ab'okwehunga, abarikutiisa.

Ekintu kya ESCORPIO n'OMUTIMA, eibumbe ni TOPACIO.

Okukora kwa ESCORPIO ni MAITHUNA kandi ekyo tikirikukorwa kwihaho omu ESCORPIO baitu omu bwiire bwona, omu mulingo ogurikugyerera, okutuuka ha KUZAIRWA KW'OMURINDI GWA KABIRI.

Naahoona, tweetenge kugamba ngu tibirikukuzibwa kukora emirindi ebiri kuraba omu kiro kimwe. N'abarikukikirizibwa kukora omurindi gumwe gw'akazora.

Kandi n'ekyangwa kumanya ngu atakeekanisibwa okucwama kugira okwa MAITHUNA obu erimu endwaire nari obu eri n'okwema kw'omutaka, nari omu mbeera y'obwahuka, ahabw'okuba n'omusango.

Omukazi aziremu ekintu, no kwetenga kwikora MAITHUNA orwaaza 40 enyima y'okuzaira.
