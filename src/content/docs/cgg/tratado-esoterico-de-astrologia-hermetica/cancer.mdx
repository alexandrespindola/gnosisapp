## 22 OKWEKUMI NA EBIRI OKUZA OKWEKUMI NA ISHATU MUKASANVU

"Omuntu kuva omu mubiri, natwara omuhanda gw'omuriro, omusana, okwekumina orw'omwezi okwakaire n'obusesani bw'omurazima, abamanyi BRAHAMA, bagenda ahari BRAHAMA". (Ekicurisho 24, Eshuura ya 8-Bhagavad-Gita).

"Omuntu owa YOGUI, ow'arikufa natwara omuhanda gw'omwotsi, okwekumina orw'omwezi orwiire n'obusesani bw'omurazima nagyenda aha kibanja ky'omwezi, hanyuma azooka". (Ekicurisho 25, Eshuura ya 8-Bhagavad-Gita).

"Emihanda egi ibiri, ogwakaire n'ogwiire, niturikuteekateeka ngu n'eya butoosha. Omurundi ogw'okubanza, nagwejutsya, kandi, omurundi ogw'akabiri, nazooka". (Ekicurisho 26, Eshuura ya 8-Bhagavad-Gita).

"OMUNTU tazooka, nari kufa, nari kugaruka; tarimu kutandika; n'obutoosha, atakyuka, ow'okubanza omu byoona, kandi tarikufa obw'orikwita omubiri". (Ekicurisho 20, Eshuura ya 8-Bhagavad-Gita).

EGO ezoooka, EGO eyo kufa. Shemekanisa hagati ya EGO n'OMUNTU. OMUNTU tazooka nari kufa nari kugaruka.

"Ebyamahwa by'emikora biri eby'emitwaro eshatu: ebitarikusiima, ebisiima n'omushagaro gwabyo byombi. Ebyo byamahwa nibikwatira, bwanyima y'okufa, ow'atarabirekire, baitu, hatari omushaija w'omutwe". (Ekicurisho 12, Eshuura ya XVIII-Bhagavad-Gita).

"Oyegye kuruga ahariiwe, iwe, orimu mikono y'amaani!, ahari ebi bitwaro bitaanu, ebirikukwatanisa n'okuhikiiriza emikora, omu buringaniza n'obwengye oburikugwaahaire, ogwo n'omuhindiro gw'omukora yoona". (Ekicurisho 13, Eshuura ya XVIII-Bhagavad-Gita).

"Omubiri, EGO, endimi, emikora n'Enjura (EMPORO) ezirikukurira endimi, ezo nizo bitwaro bitaanu". (Ekicurisho 14, Eshuura ya 18-Bhagavad-Gita).

"Omukora gwona ogurikusiima nari ogutarikusiima, yaba ogw'omubiri, ogw'ekigambo nari ogw'obwomeezi, guri ebyo bitwaro bitaanu". (Ekicurisho 15, Eshuura ya 18, Bhagavad-Gita).

"Obwo buri bwekityo, ogwo arikuraba omu kwetegyereza okurikubundamuka nateekateeka ATMAN (OMUNTU), aha BURIHOONA, nk'omukora, omwo muntu murimi talora EKIKUBAHOONA". (Ekicurisho 16-Eshuura ya 81-Bhagavad Gita).

BHAGAVAD GITA yakoora, n'ahabweki, okushemekaanisa hagati ya EGO (NYOWE), n'OMUNTU (ATMAN).

ENYAMAAHWA ENTEEKATEEKIRE buzahabu erikwetwa OMUNTU, n'ekyombeko ky'OMUBIRI, EGO (NYOWE), ENDIMI n'emikora. Ekimaakiina eyerembywe ENJURA nari tukugambe turungi, EMPORO.

Emirundi mingi kimara kamaramu omuyaga gwona, ngu omuyaga ogurikugyenda aha itaka, gubugye ebyo bimaakiina by'abantu abariire, omu bibanja by'entaro. Obukaikuru bw'amaakiina agariire, kurwana n'obukaikuru bw'amaakiina agariire.

OMWEZI nigureeta EGO aha kiziba kandi Omwezi nigubihemba. Max Heindel nagamba ngu OKUTWAARA kibaho obutoosha Omwezi guri omu CÁNCER. Hatariho Omwezi okutwaara tikirikubaasa.

Emyaka musaanvu ey'okubanza y'amagara nirigyendesywa OMWEZI. Emyaka musaanvu ey'akabiri y'amagara n'eya MERCURIAN omu kikumi, obwo omwana nagyenda omu Ishuuru, ariyehwahwire, ari omu kukenga okutari kurangwa.

Ekitwambara eky'akashatu ky'amagara, obuto obuto oburikushusha hagati y'emyaaka ikumi na enya na makumi abiri na emwe y'amagara, n'erya Venus, Enjura y'okukunda; ogwo n'emyaka y'okwetungura, emyaka y'okukunda, emyaaka ei turoora amagara omu ibara ry'amakunde.

Kuruga aha 21 (MAKUMI ABIRI NA EMWE) okukuza aha 42 (MAKUMI ANA NA IBIRI) y'amagara twine kujumbura ekibanja kyaitu ekito aha musana hamu n'okujujuna amagara gaitu. Obwire obu nirigyendesywa Omusana.

Ekitwambara ekirikushusha hagati y'emyaaka makumi ana na ibiri na makumi ana na itaanu y'emyaka, n'erya MARCIAN omu kikumi kandi amagara nigagaruka obwo ekibanja ky'entaro ekikuubaireho, habwokuba MARTE n'entaro.

Obwire oburikushusha hagati y'emyaaka makumi ana na itaanu na makumi ataano na mukaaga y'amagara, n'erya JUPITER; abarikugira JÚPITER ahikire omu horóscopo yabo, nikirora ngu omu bwire obu bw'amagara gaabo nibagumisiriza abantu boona kandi bakaba bataine EBIHAMBA BY'ENSHOZI EBIRIKWEREKWA, bagira omurundi ogw'omukoro kubasa kugumaho.

Eky'obugabe ekindi n'ekyabo abarikugira JÚPITER atakihikire omu horóscopo yaabo; abantu abo nibabonabona obutoosha, nibagira oburemeezi bw'omugaati, ekyokwambara, obwekugamba, nibahakanisibwa abandi, ebyo n'ebindi, ebyo n'ebindi, ebyo n'ebindi.

Obwire bw'amagara oburikushusha hagati y'emyaaka makumi ataano na mukaaga na makumi ataano na ishatu, nirigyendesywa omuntu omukuru w'eiguru, Saturno orikukuraire.

Buzima obukuru bukatandika aha myaaka makumi ataano na mukaaga. Okuhingura obwire bwa Saturno, Omwezi nagagaruka, nagureeta EGO, aha KUZOOKA kandi nagubireeta.

Twaba nitumegyereza enyima amagara g'abakuru abakuzire muno, turikubaasa kwemeza ngu buzima nibagaruka aha myaaka y'abaana, abakuru abandi na bakuru nibagaruka okuzana n'engari n'ebibeerwa. Abakuru abarikuhingura emyaaka makumi ataano na ishatu n'abaana abato ahansi y'emyaaka musaanvu nibagyendesywa OMWEZI.

"Hagati y'obukaikuru bw'abashaija, talibaaho omwe arikugyenda okukora EKIKUHIIRIZE; hagati y'abo abarikugyenda talibaaho omwe arikukora ekikuhiirize, kandi hagati y'abakozire ekikuhiirize talibaaho omwe andimanya EKIKUHIIRIZE". (Ekicurisho 3, Eshuura ya VII-Bhagavad-Gita.)

EGO n'eya LUNAR kandi arikuleka omubiri gw'obuntu nagyenda omu muhanda gw'omwotsi, okwekumina orwiire orw'OMWEZI n'obusesani bw'okumaisho kandi ahonaaho agaruka aha kiziba ekishaaisha. OMWEZI nagubireeta kandi OMWEZI nagugarukayo, ogwo n'EKIRAGIRO.

EGO yambaire EMIBIRI EYA LUNAR. Ebyombeko eby'omunda ebiri kwegyerezebwa TEOSOFÍA, biri ebwebihiko bya LUNAR.

Ebyahandiikwa ebirikukwera by'ABA JAINOS nibigamba: "ENSYO YIJUZIRE EBINTU EBIRIKUSHEMEERA EBIRIKUBA OMU SAMSARA, EBIZOOKIRE KURUGA OMU MIBARU N'AMAKONDO G'OMUGASHO KURABA OMU MIKORA, kandi N'OBURINGANIZA GURYO EGYO ZIRYAGYENDA EMIRUNDI OMU ENSYO Y'ENJURA, EMIRUNDI EBINDI OMU KUZIMIRE HAMU N'EMIRUNDI EBINDI NIRIGARUKA OMU BASURA (ABANTU AB'EBIROGO). N'AHABWENKI OMU SAMSARA ABANTU ABARIKUGUMIRIZA KUZOOKA N'OKUGARUKA KURABA OMU MIKORA YAABO EMIBI".

OMWEZI nigutwara EGO YOONA, baitu hatari yoona nigugireeta enyima. Ebi bwire ebyingi nibingira omu NYIKA-EZIRIKUZIMIRA, omu bibanja SUBLUNARES, omu BWEGA KY'OBUTARE OBURIKUGAWA, omu mazima agari omuhanda gumu owa buri kimwe kurira n'okugyenda kw'obw'emiino.

Bari abingi abarikugaruka omu bwangu nari omurundi ogundi bagyendesywa kandi baretsibwe OMWEZI, batarajumbura okusiima kw'ensi enshemeire.

ABAKOZIRE EKIKUHIIRIZE, abatoreire, abo ABATAIREHO EGO; BAKAKORA EMIBIRI YAABO EYA SOLARES kandi BAKAHANGANIRA abantu, nibasiima, arikuleka omubiri gw'obuntu n'okufa, nibatwara omuhanda gw'omuriro, omusana, omusana, okwekumina okwakaire orw'OMWEZI n'obusesani bw'omurazima, babaire baagumize OMUNTU, nibamanya BRAHAMA (ISE ATARIHO OMURIKUGYENDA) kandi kirora ngu nibagyenda ahari BRAHAMA (ISE).

JAINISMO nagamba ngu omu BWAIKARA obu BUKURU bwa BRAHAMA nibashisha omu nsi egi aba PROFETA abashaanzi abataireho EKIKUHIIRIZE KYOONA.

Ebyahandiikwa GNÓSTICAS nibigamba ngu habaho ABAROKOZI KUMI NA IBIRI, okugamba: KUMI NA IBIRI AVATARAS; ekindi twatekateeka omuntu omwe JUAN BAUTISTA nk'omutandika n'omuntu omwe JESÚS nk'omwo AVATARA, ahabwa PISCIS obwire oburikwija kuhingura, obwo turi kubaasa kwetegyereza ngu ahabwa buri omu omu bwire ikumi na ibiri bw'amashaka haraara butoosha omutandika n'omu AVATARA, kwoona PROFETA mburungi makumi abiri na enya.

RASKOARNO orikukwera (OKUFA), yijuzire oburungi bw'omunda oburikubundaamukamu. Omwe akamanya AMAZIMA aha KUFA, owa KWEYEGYESA omu muringo oguhikire, ekibanza kyago kikonyeire.

OMWEZI nigutwara kandi nigugarukayo abafwiire. Ebirukurangirira nibikushoraho. Okufa n'okutwaara nibisangwa nikibikwatanisa. Omuhanda gw'AMAGARA gukozire n'ebinyarizi by'empita z'EMPAARE Y'OKUFA.

Okutaaha kw'ebintu byoona ebirikukora omubiri gw'obuntu, nibitandika okukenga kw'omutaano ogurikubundaamukamu ogurikuhingura obutari kuroreka kuraba omu rugo n'obwire.

Nibashushana n'omuyaga gw'omutambi gw'ebikungu orikuraba omu mashusho, niyo muyaga erikukenga ey'abafwiire. Eki kiri orushozi aha MUYAGA gw'emishenyi erikuguruma, n'omwana ogw'akazi aha muyaga gw'okufa.

EMUYAGA ERY'OKUTAMBI EKIRIKUKENGA ERIKURABA OMU SHUSHO ry'owafwiire. Elishusho eri nirishigaraho omu ggi erikugaba.

Ahansi y'OMUGASHO GWA LUNAR endiba y'amaizi nibayingira kuraba omu kibabi ky'eggi, ekyerikugaruka okugwiira okutagyezere. Omu aharizo ebanga erikushemeereza muno ery'okureeta, rikureeta kandi rirayire aha kirere ky'omukazi ekirikugumiriza omu rubaju rw'eggi.

Obwo endiba ezi zombi EZIKURU zikushanganira omu BUMWE, CROMOSOMAS nibatandika obwo okuzinira kwazo okurikushaanza, nibakutwariza hamu nibagaruka okutwariza hamu omu kabanga. Nikwo omutaano gw'omuntu ogurikugambwa nikugaruka gukakatanisa omu mwana ogw'akazi.
