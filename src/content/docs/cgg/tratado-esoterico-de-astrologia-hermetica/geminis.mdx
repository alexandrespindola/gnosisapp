OKWESHUURUZA hamwe n'OKUTANGAARA nibyo bitwara omu KUROOTA kw'AMAGEZI. Eky'okureeberaho: Nimugyenda mwihangaanire omu muhanda; nimushanga embaraza y'abantu omu ruhanga; nibataire ebiruruuta, nibagamba Abarwaniirizi b'eihanga, emitwe eremeerwa omu mwanya, abantu nibareeba nk'abashazi, boona nibagamba, boona nibataira.

Eiyo MBARAZA y'ABANTU neegumisiriza munoonga; imwe mwebwaire ebintu byona eimwaba mwiine kukora, nimweshuuruza n'abantu, ebigambo by'abagyenda bagamba nibimukwasisa.

MBARAZA y'ABANTU neegumisiriza munoonga mwebwaire MWE BAMWENE, mweshuuruzire munoonga n'Eiyo MBARAZA Y'OMU MUHANDA, hatariho ekindi eki murikwenda kucwamu, nimutangaara, hati nimwegwa omu kuroota kw'amagezi; mwegattire hamwe n'abantu abarikutaira, imwe nanye nimutaaira kandi nimuramiza amabaare n'ebisumaalyo; nimuroota omuhanda omurungi, timurikubaasa kumanya eimuri, ebintu byona bimwebwaire.

Ka tugende hatali aha kureeberaho ekiri kyorooro: Nimutuurire omu zaala yaanyu mutuurire ha mutwe gw'ekishushani kya Televiziini, nibaboneka ebishushani by'aba Cowboy, harimu okuteera, ebikomo by'abakundwa, n'ebindi bingi.

Filimu neegumisiriza munoonga, yareetera kimwe amaisho gaanyu, mwebwaire munoonga MWE BAMWENE, kuhika n'okutaira mutaaniriirwe, nimwESUURUZA n'aba Cowboy, n'okuteera, n'omukundwa hamwe n'omukundwa.

Okutangaara hati kwahukana, hatariho ekintu kyona eki murikwenda kwijuka, mwinjire omu kuroota kureengyeire, omu bwire obwo nimwenda kworeka obuhinguzi bw'omugabe wa filimu, omu bwire obwo nimwenda omugaisha ogu araaze.

Nimpingwa n'obukaikuru n'obumiriyo ebireetera OKWESHUURUZA, OKUTANGAARA, OKUROOTA. Abantu nibESUURUZA n'ABANTU, EMBARAZA hamwe n'ekika kyona ky'OKWESHUURUZA kikirirweho OKUTANGAARA n'OKUROOTA.

Abantu nibatuura n'AMAGEZI GAAGWIIRIRE, nibakora nibaroota, nibagendesa ebigaari nibaroota kandi nibaita n'abagyezi abarikugyenda nibaroota omu mihanda, nibatekateeka enjura zaabo bwene.

Omu biro by'okuhinga kw'omubiri ogw'omubiri, EGO(IWE) neeruuga omu MUBIRI OGWA MUBIRI kandi neetwara ebirooto byayo omu kifo kyona eki egarukurire. Okuigaruka omu mubiri ogw'omubiri, okwinjira busya omu mbeera y'Obusharazi, neegumizamu n'ebirooto byayo bimwe kandi nikwo etwara obwomeezi bwayo bwona neeroota.

Abantu abarikufa barekeraaho kubaaho, kwonka EGO, IWE, neegumizamu omu bitundu BY'AHAAIGURU by'okufa. Omu kasumi k'okufa EGO neetwara ebirooto byayo, obwomeezi bwayo bw'ensi kandi etuura omu nsi y'abafu n'ebirooto byayo, neegumizamu neeroota, n'AMAGEZI gaagwiire, negyenda nk'omuntu araazi, araazi, atarikumanya.

Omuntu weena orikwenda KUSHARAZA AMAGEZI ashemereire kugakorra aha hamwe n'obwo. Twiine AMAGEZI AGATWAZIRWE kandi n'ahabw'ekyo tushemereire KUGAKORRA aha hamwe n'obwo. Omuntu weena orikwenda KUSHARAZA AMAGEZI aha omu nsi enu asharaza omu Nsi zona.

Omuntu weena orikwenda KUSHARAZA AMAGEZI omu NSI ENU YA BIJUMBI ISHATU, neesharaza omu BIJUMBI byana, bitaano, mukaaga na mushanju.

Omuntu weena orikwenda kutuura ARIKUMANYA omu NSI Z'AHAAIGURU, ashemereire KUSHARAZA aha hamwe n'obwo.

Enjiri ina nizijutsya aha bw'omutaarizo gw'OKUSHARAZA, kwonka abantu tibarikwetegyereza.

Abantu nibarara munoonga, kwonka nibakuratiiriza ngu nibasharazi, obu omuntu akiriza ngu narara, nikimanyisa kimwe ngu atandiise kusharaza.

Kiragyeire kimwe kureetera abantu abandi kumanya ngu baine AMAGEZI gagiire, abantu tibarikukiriza amazima makuru ngu nibarara.

Omuntu weena orikwenda KUSHARAZA AMAGEZI ashemereire kwegyesa kuruga omu KASUMI omu KASUMI ERIJUKYO ERIHWEEREEREZA erya IWE MWENE.

Eki ky'OKUIJUKYA IWE MWENE kuruga omu KASUMI omu KASUMI, omuringo gw'okukora kuremeire.

Kihikiire akasumi kamwe, akanya k'okwebwa kutarikusharaza kutaanda omu kuroota omuhanda omurungi.

Nitwetaga n'OBWIIRA kutaariza okuteeka kwitu kwona, okutumwa, okwenda, okuteesa, emikora, okuteekateeka, okwenda okwegonza, n'ebindi.

Okuteeka kwona, okutumwa kwona, okugyenda kwona, omukora gwona, okuteekateeka kwona okw'okwegonza, bishemereire kweyoreka hamwe n'obwire obu birikuruga omu PSIQUIS yaitu; okubuurira kwona omu maisho, kirahikiire nkokukora amagezi.

Emirundi mingi nimugyenda omu muhanda nimutekateeka enjura zaanyu bwene, mwashuuruzire n'okuteeka okwo, nimutangaara, nimuroota omuhanda omurungi; omu bunaku arikuhita omunywani haihi naiwe, naaramutisa, timurikugarukamu okuramutisa ahabw'okugira ngu timurikumureeba, nimurarara; omunywani naaganya, naamanya ngu imwe nimuri abantu abatarikukuzaho kwegyesibwa nari okuba mugarukire, munywani nanye naarya, ahabwenki okuba aharikusharaza atarikwija kwemanyiisa omwegyeso gwona ogwo, araakuratiiriza hamwe n'obwire obu imwe nimurara.

N'emirundi mingi emwingi emiribaasa kwesanga n'okukoonga aha rugi otarikushemereire kukoonga, ahabw'okuba nimurarara.

Nimugyenda omu motoka y'ekibuga, mwiine kushuuruza omu muhanda ogurikwetwa, kwonka nimugyenda nimwashuuruzire, nimutangaara, nimuroota omuhanda omurungi n'ebyafaayo omu bwongo bwanyu, nari n'okwijuka, nari n'okukunda, omu bunaku mwemanyiisize ngu mumaze kushuuruza omuhanda, murahaaza motoka hamwe n'okugaruka hanyuma omu maguru aha mihanda mingi.

Kiragyeire kimwe kugumizamu omu busharazi kuruga omu kasumi omu kasumi kwonka KIKURU.

Obu turaakwegyera kutuura omu busharazi kuruga omu kasumi omu kasumi, nikwo tureka kuroota aha hamwe n'ahahendo y'omubiri ogw'omubiri.

Nikyetagisisa kumanya ngu abantu obu baraara nibaruuga omu mibiri yaabo, kwonka nibatwara ebirooto byabo, nibatuura omu nsi y'omunda nibaroota kandi okuigaruka omu mubiri ogw'omubiri, nibagumizamu n'ebirooto byabo, nibagumizamu nibaroota.

Obu omuntu araakwegyera kutuura OMU BUSHARAZI kuruga omu kasumi omu kasumi, areka kuroota aha hamwe n'omu nsi y'omunda.

Nikyetagisisa kumanya ngu EGO (IWE) egatiirwe omu MIBIRI YA LUNA, neeruuga omu MUBIRI OGWA MUBIRI obu omubiri guraara, kurugirira aha kubi EGO etuura egiire omu NSI Y'OMUNDA.

Omu MIBIRI YA LUNA harimu hamwe na EGO, eki kirikwetwa ESENCIA, ALMA, ECUMBI RYA ALMA, BUDHATA, CONCIENCIA. Nikwo CONCIENCIA eki tushemereire KUSHARAZA aha hamwe n'obwo.

Aha omu nsi enu twiine CONCIENCIA, aha tushemereire KUSHARAZA, ngu ahabwenki okuba nitwenda kureka kuroota n'okutuura turikumanya omu nsi y'ahaiguru.

OMUNTU n'amagezi agasharaziire obwire obu omubiri gwombekire omu buriri bwayo, natuura, nakora, nakora ari kumanya omu NSI Z'AHAAIGURU.

Omuntu ARIKUMANYA tariine buzibu bw'OKWEBABURA, obuzibu bw'okwegyera OKWEBABURA n'okwenda n'omuntu ARAAZI.

Omuntu OMUHARARUKE n'atarikwetsigamaho okwegyera okwebabura, natuura arikumanya omu NSI Z'AHAAIGURU, obu omubiri gw'omubiri guraara omu buriri.

Omuntu omuhararuke hati TARIKUROOTA, omu kuhinga kw'omubiri natuura omu bitundu ebi abantu barikugyenda nibaroota, kwonka n'AMAGEZI AGAHARARUKIRE.

OMUNTU OMUHARARUKE ari omu kukwatana na LOGIA BRANCA, naakyendera EBITEMPURA by'OBUNYWANISA OBUNENE OBW'ENSI YOONA obw'OMUHERU, neefundikira hamwe na GURÚ-DEVA yaayo, obu omubiri guraara.

OKUIJUKYA OMUHWEEREEREZA kw'IWE MWENE kuruga omu kasumi omu kasumi, nikukuraasa okwetegyereza OMWEHWA kandi nikwo turaabaasa kureeba ebirooto by'abantu abarikugyenda omu mihanda.

Okwetegyereza OMUHWA nikukwatanisa omu IWE MWENE, okureeba, okuhurra, okuhuumurirwa, okuhumuza, okukora aha, n'ebindi. Okwetegyereza OMUHWA n'OKUKORA kw'AMAGEZI AGAHARARUKIRE.

EMISHO, ebi ebiri kwogera omu bitabo, n'enkwatanisa y'omwehwa, nibyo ebi omuriro gurikweta, n'enkwatanisa n'EIZUBA.

Obu OKUIJUKYA OMUHWEEREEREZA kw'IWE MWENE kuruga omu kasumi omu kasumi, kikuuru ahabw'OKUSHARAZA AMAGEZI, tikiri ahahendo kwegyera okukoresa AMAISHO.

Abanyeshuri ba GNÓSTICOS bashemereire kwegyera okugabura AMAISHO omu bicweka bishatu: OMUNTU, EKINTU, EKIFO.

OMUNTU. Kutaragwera omu kwebwa kw'IWE MWENE omu maisho g'eky'okureeberaho kyona.

EKINTU. Kutaariza omu bushwerekyere ekintu kyona, eky'okureeberaho kyona, omukora gwona, ekigyezo kyona ahabw'okugira ngu nikigaruka ekirikureebeka, hatariho KWEYEBWA kw'IWE MWENE.

EKIFO. Okutaariza enjura y'ekifo eki turikushangwa, tukakanganisa IWE MWENE: Ni kifo ki eki? Ahabwenki ndi aha?

Omu mutwe gw'ekifo EKI, tushemereire kukwatanisa ekigyezo KY'OKWENDA, ahabw'okuba kibasa kutureetera kwesanga nk'abaharirwe omu kyana nari omu njumbi ey'okutaano omu butahangaanisi omu kasumi k'OKUTAARIZA; turaijukye ngu obutahangaanisi buriine BIJUMBI MUSHANJU.

Omu MUNDO YA BIJUMBI ISHATU harimu etteeka ly'okutwaara omu maisho. Omu BIJUMBI Z'AHAAIGURU z'obutahangaanisi, harimu ETTEEKA RY'OKUHUUHA.

Okutaariza ekifo, titushemereire kwebwa ekigyezo ky'amaani ebya BIJUMBI MUSHANJU eby'obutahangaanisi; nikirungi nkokukanganisa IWE MWENE: Ndi omu BIJUMBI ki?, kandi hanyuma nikyetagisisa, enkoorezi, kuhuha kimwe n'obwira oburikubasa n'okugira ekigyendererwa ky'okuguruka omu maisho. Nikikwatagana ngu nituguruka ahabw'okugira ngu nitwesanga ahahendo y'OMUBIRI OGWA MUBIRI. Titushemereire kwebwa ngu obu omubiri ogw'omubiri guraara, EGO n'EMIBIRI YA LUNA hamwe na ESENCIA omu nda, negyenda atarikumanya nk'omuntu araazi omu NSI Y'EBIBUMBE.

Okugabura AMAISHO omu OMUNTU, EKINTU, EKIFO, nikikora aha KUSHARAZA kw'AMAGEZI.

Abanyeshuri baingi ba GNÓSTICOS hanyuma y'okwegyesa okukora okwegyesa, okugabura AMAISHO omu bicweka bishatu, aha bibuuzo, omu kahuha, n'ebindi, omu bwire bw'obusharazi, omu kasumi omu kasumi, bagumizamu nibakora okwegyesa kumwe omu kuroota kw'omubiri ogw'omubiri, obu bari bari omu nsi y'ahaiguru kandi n'okuhuha okw'amagyezi, baguruka omu maisho; nikwo basharaziire AMAGEZI, nikwo baijukire ngu omubiri ogw'omubiri gwasigarire gwiire ahagati y'omubiri kandi bagwiire omu kushemererwa bagasobora okugonza okwegyera EBIGAMBO by'obwomeezi n'OKUFA, omu BIJUMBI Z'AHAAIGURU.

Nikukwatagana kwogera ngu okwegyesa oku kurikukora kuruga omu kasumi omu kasumi buri izooba, eki kitungura omu mukora, omu kizino, kyahemberwa munoonga omu bitundu by'obutekateeki, eki hanyuma kiragaruka kimwe omu kuroota, obuturaaba nituri ahahendo y'omubiri ogw'omubiri kandi ebirikurugamu n'OKUSHARAZA kw'AMAGEZI.

GÉMINIS n'eky'okubonabona eky'omwanya, kirikugambwa PLANETA MERCURIO. GÉMINIS netegyeka emihuri, amaboko n'amagaaju.

OKUKORA. Omu KY'OKUBONABONA EKIRIKUTUNGUURA GÉMINIS, abanyeshuri ba Gnósticos bashemereire kwebaka n'emigongo yabo hamwe n'okuhinga omubiri. Hanyuma baine kwetegereza omwanya emirundi itaano n'okuguhinga emirundi itaano; okwetegereza baine kweteera omu mutwe ngu omushana ogu gwaitiine omumira gwa laringe, nigukora hati omu bronquios n'emihuri. Okwetegereza baraaguruke amagaaju n'amaboko aha buryo bw'oburya n'amasho, okuhika baraagarure amagaaju n'amaboko.

Eky'okugamba kya GÉMINIS n'omutwe ogu MERCURIO, eibaare BERILO ORO, ibara AMARILLO.

Aba GÉMINIS nibakunda munoonga obwomeezi, nibakora enshobi y'okwenda amagyezi omu mutima, nibenda kucwamu ebintu byona n'obutekateeki, nibaganya bwangu, bakuguma, bakuguruka, bakukaraaza, abanyabwenge, amagyezi gaabo gwijwiire hamwe n'obunaku, baine omutima muhango.

Aba GÉMINIS n'ab'obuzibu ahabw'OKUBIRI kwabo, ahabw'ABANTU ABAIRI abarikuba nibabatunga kandi ekireebeka aha bagiriki ABANYAMUTAARIRIZA abarikwetwa CASTOR na PÓLUX.

Omu ntwazisa erikutwarwa GÉMINIS n'ekikyeere munoonga kandi ahabw'ekyo tikyeteera kubana nabo.
