KUTANDIKA 22 OMWEZI GWA KABIRI KUSHEREZA 23 OMWEZI GWA MUNANA

ANNIE BESANT naagamba aha ky’okurorraho kya MUKAMA WAITU NANAK ekirikukira kimwe kukikopiinga.

"Gwakaba gwa Kyakabiri omu izooba eryo, kandi obu obwire bw’okushaba bwakahika, mukama na omurimo bakatandika omu musigiti. Obu KARI (OMUKURU W’ABAISIRAAMU) yaatandika okushaba, NABAB na abarikumukurata bakafukama, nk’oku RITO MAHOMETANO eragira, NANAK akagumaho ayemerire, atarikwehuzya kandi ahunire. Okushaba ku kwahwereire, NABAB akagamba na omutsigazi kandi yaamubuuza na ekiniga: Ahabw’enki otahikiirize emikoro y’Eteeka?. Oli murimbi kandi orikwehurra. Tikirikutwara kuba iija ahaaha kugumaho nk’omuti".

NANAK YAAGAMBIRE:

"Mukafukama ebirungi omu itaka obu obwomeezi bwanyu bwaaba nibuza omu bire, ahabw’okuba mwaaba nimuteekateeka kureeta embaraasi kuruga CANDAR okukira okugamba okushaba. Aha muntu ogwo Omutabani, yaakora emikoro y’okufukama omu bwomeezi, omu mutwe gwe gwaaba niguteekateeka kuhonerra endogoyi eyaazaara eizooba ryaherize. Mbwenu ninkora ki n’abantu abarikufukama ahabw’omugyenzo kandi bagarukamu ebigambo nk’ekinyoni?"

"NABAB akagambira kimwe ngu akaba arikuteekateeka omu bwire bw’omukoro gwona aha kugura embaraasi ekaba neyenda. Ekirikukwata aha KARI, yaaloreka obugumire bwe aha bwomeezi kandi yaabuuzisa omutsigazi ebibuuzo bingi".

Kikuru kwega kushaba omu bwire; ogwo ogwaiga kukwatanisa okushaba n’okuteekateeka, azaakuratirra ebiva omu bwire ebirungi.

Kwonka nikiraganira okwetegyereza ngu haroho okushaba okurikushushana kandi ebiva omu byo nibirikushushana.

Haroho okushaba kurikumanyirra omu biteengo, kwonka okushaba kwona tikurikukuratirra omu biteengo.

Haroho okushaba kwingi okwa ira okurikuba kugarukamu kw’ebintu by’ensi yona kandi nitubaasa kumanya ebiri omuriiyo byona twateekateeka omu kigambo kyona, omu muringo gwona, n’okwemereza kw’obwomeezi.

ISHE WAITU ogu muringo gw’amaani g’obwa LEVITE, kwonka nikiraganira okwetegyereza omu bwomeezi bwona amakuru g’obwomeezi bwa buri kigambo, buri muringo, buri kuteenga.

ISHE WAITU okushaba kw’okuteenga, okushaba kw’okugamba na ISHE arikubaa omu kihirima. ISHE WAITU ku kwatanisiibwe n’okuteekateeka kw’ahaiguru, nikireeta ebiva omu bwire ebirungi.

EMIKORO GNÓSTICOS, EMIRIMO Y’EDIINI, obusaho oburikumara obw’obwengye obukihirima, ahabw’oyo arikumanya okuteekateeka, ahabw’abo abarikubyetegyereza n’omutima.

Oyo orikwenda kucweera omugisha gw’OMUTIMA OGUTAHURIRA, ashemereire kukwata PRANA, OBWOMEZI, AMAANI G’OBUSHWERA omu bwomeezi bw’omutwe n’obwomeezi omu MUTIMA.

Nikikuru kwega okuteekateeka n’omutima, kuta obwomeezi omu KIBIRA KY’OMUTIMA. OMUSARABA gw’OKUTANDIKA guheebwa omu KIBIRA ekiikirungi ky’omutima.

NANAK, MUKAMA OWAATANDIIRE EDIINI SIKH omu itaka erikwera erya VEDAS, yaayegeseza omugisha gw’OMUTIMA.

NANAK yaayegeseza obunywani bw’ediini zona, amasomero, obutabanzi, n’ebindi.

Obu turikurwanisa ediini zona nari ediini emwe, tukukora omusango gw’okurwisa ETEEKA ry’OMUTIMA.

Omu KIBIRA-MUTIMA haroho ekifo ky’ediini zona, obutabanzi, obuteeka, n’ebindi, n’ebindi.

Ediini zona n’amaisho g’omuhendo agatungirwe omu muzinga ogwa zahabu gw’OBWAKAAMA.

OKWEHULURA GNÓSTICO kwaitu kugizweho abantu abarikuruga omu ediini zona, amasomero, obutabanzi, ebiteeka by’omwoyo, n’ebindi, n’ebindi.

Omu KIBIRA-MUTIMA haroho ekifo ky’ediini zona, ahabw’okukuza kwona. Yesu akagamba: "Omu kunywaniisa omukama omu kiina, muragarra ngu muli abeegi bange".

Ebyahandiikwa bya SIKIAS, nk’eby’ediini zona, buzima tizirikugambwa.

Omu SIKIOS OMKARA MUKAMA WENYINE owatwire omugongo, itaka, amaizi, byona ebiriho.

OMKARA MWOYO, ATARIKWOLEKA, ATARIKUHWA, atarikutandika omu mazima, atarikuhwa omu mazima, omushana ogurikusiimiriza EBIBIINA EBIRI, omuhiiganizi w’obwomeezi; omutegyeki w’omunda gw’omutima gwona».

"Ekibanga n’amaani gawe. EIZOBA n’OMWEZI mashasha gawe. Obusereeti bw’enyonyo nyonyo n’obujinja bwawe bw’omuhendo. Mukama!. Omuyaga gurikunuha ogwa Himalayas n’ekihurru kyawe. Omuyaga gurikukugumya. Obwami bw’ebimera nibukuheereza ebimuli, omushana!. Aaahe ebinyamwa by’okusiima, omucwekyerezi w’okutiina!. ANATAL SHABDHA (EKYOMA EKIRIKWERA) nikiruga nk’engoma zawe. Torikutunga amaisho kandi oine amaisho nkumi. Torikutunga amaguru kandi oine amaguru nkumi. Torikutunga engo kandi oine engo nkumi. Omurimo gwawe ogurikusiimisa nigutuhisha amaani. Omushana gwawe, ekitiinisa! guli omu bintu byona. Kuruga aha by’obwomeezi byona nikuza omu Mushana gwawe. Kuruga aha kwegesa kw’omukama nikuza omushana ogu. Ni ARATI".

MUKAMA OMUKURU NANAK, okurugiirra aha UPANISHADAS, nikwetegyereza ngu BRAHAMA (ISHE), ni UMWE kandi ngu EBIGAMBI BY’OMUGONGO nibyo biroorekanwa bye bingi, ebibonesereza eby’OBURUNGI OBURIKUKIRA.

GURÚ-DEVA n’ogwo ogwamazire kuba hamwe na ISHE (BRAHAMA). Ow’omugisha n’ogwo orikutunga GURÚ-DEVA arikumuheereza nk’omwegi n’omuriisa. Ow’omugisha ogwo orikushanga MUKAMA OW’OKUHIKIIRIRA.

Omugisha n’omugufu, mugufu kandi gurikutiinisa bingi. Nikyetenga GURÚ-DEVA, omwegi, omuriisa.

Omu KIBIRA-MUTIMA turaashanga HARI OMUNYAMURWA. Omu KIBIRA-MUTIMA turaashanga GURÚ-DEVA.

Hati turakopiinga obusereeti bwa CIKIES aha kwekundiira GURÚ-DEVA.

"Oh NANAK! Mwegye ahabw’omugisha ogwa GURÚ, ogwo ogurikukwanisa ku byona..."

"Enshuro kikumi omu izooba nindikwenda kwihayo ahabwa GURÚ yangye eyangucwire kuba MUKAMA omu bwire bukye".

"N’obu amaisho nkumi n’amaisho nkumi gabaire nikusiimiriza, gabaire nigaaba nikuteekateeka omugongo".

"Agumeere GURÚ yangye erikusiimwa erikumanya HARI (OBUNYAMURWA) kandi etuyegeseze okureeba abanywani n’abazigu kimwe".

"!Mukama!. Tushiimira n’obunywani bwa GURÚ-DEVA, kuba hamwe nawe tumbeasa, abakozibi abaabireho, kukora omugisha okurabamu".

"GURÚ-DEVA, GURÚ w’amazima, ni PARABRAHMAN Mukama omukuru. NANAK afukama ha GURÚ DEVA HARI".

Omu INDOSTÁN SAMYASIN w’okuteekateeka n’ogwo orikuheereza GURÚ-DEVA, ogwo ogwamazire kumushanga omu mutima, ogwo orikukora omu KUCWERERA KW’EGO LUNAR.

Oyo orikwenda kuhwera hamwe n’EGO, hamwe nawe, ashemereire kuziika EKINIGA, EKIKOMBA, OBUSHWERA, EITIMA, EKITIIISA, OBUSIKARIRE, EBIRIIYO. Kukoma hamwe n’obulema obu bwona omu bibiina byona eby’OMUTWE, afa nawe omu muringo ogwa RADICAL, oburikuhwera kimwe kandi obuhweereire kimwe.

OKUTEEKATEEKA omu zina lya HARI (OBUNYAMURWA), nikutureetera kumanya EKIRYO, amazima.

Nikyetenga kwega KUSHABA ISHE WAITU, kwega kugamba na BRAHAMA (ISHE) orikuba omu kihirima.

ISHE WAITU wenka orikushabwa oburungi kandi omu bwengye akwatanisiibwe n’OKUTEEKATEEKA, n’omurimo gwona gw’oburozi bwahaiguru.

ISHE WAITU wenka orikushabwa oburungi akorwa omu saa ya burema nari omuriisa gye y’okukira saa.

Enima y’okushaba eshemereire kumanya kuhinduka okugarukamu kwa ISHE kandi eki nikimanyisa kumanya okuteekateeka, okutunga obwomeezi oburikuhunira kandi omucecerrezi, orikutwara obwomeezi bwona, orikuhinduka okugarukamu kwa ISHE.

Obu OBWOMEZI burikuhunira omunda kandi hanyuma, obu OBWOMEZI burikuba omu CECEEREZI omunda kandi hanyuma, obu obwomeezi buzirekure kuruga omu DUALISMO, bwonyima nikiruga kw’itwe EKISHAAHYA.

Nikyetenga KUJUUZA obwomeezi bw’ebiteekateeko byona, okwenda, okushema, ebiryo, okutiina, n’ebindi, kugira ngu kiriize tw’itwe okumanya EKIRYO.

Okuteera omuri, OKUMANYA omu MURI OGURIKUMURIKIZA, nikibaasa byonka obu ESENCIA, OMWOYO, BUDHATA, kurekura ekitara.

ESENCIA erikubuzibwa aha mbeera y’okurwanisa okw’omugongo obwomeezi n’oburungi, okushemererra n’obucureezi, eego n’obuto, oburungi n’obubi, ekishemererra n’oburungi.

Obu OBWOMEZI burikuhunira, obu OBWOMEZI burikuba omu ceceerezi, bwoinyima ESENCIA erarekura kandi nikiriiza okumanya EKIRYO omu MURI OGURIKUMURIKIZA.

SHABA, abeegi abarikusiima kandi bwonyima n’obwomeezi oburikuhunira bingi kandi omu ceceerezi, JUJURRA ebiteekateeko byona, tindika okugarukamu kwa ISHE: "Sabaho kandi kigahabwe iwe, teera kandi kiraahurwa iwe".

KUSHABA nikwegerana na MUKAMA kandi buzima nikiraganira kwega okwegerana n’ISHE, na BRAHAMA.

KIBIRA MUTIMA n’enju y’OKUSHABA. Omu kibira omutima nibashanga amaani agarikuruga haiguru n’amaani agarikuruga ahansi, nibakora ekikumbya kya SALOMÓN.

Nikyetenga kushaba kandi OKUTEEKATEEKA munonga. Nikikuru kumanya kurekura omubiri ahabw’okugira ngu OKUTEEKATEEKA kwaba kurungi.

Enyima y’okutandika ebitegekano by’OKUSHABA n’OKUTEEKATEEKA okukwatanisiibwe, rekura oburungi omubiri.

Akwate omwegi GNÓSTICO omu mbeera ya DECÚBITO DORSAL, nikimanyisa, kugwa omu maguru omu itaka nari omu kitanda, amaguru n’emikono byahurwa omu mukono ogwa burya na ogwa bumoso, omu muringo gw’ENYONYO ey’ebijanjaano bitaano.

Ombeera egi ey’ENYONYO PENTAGONAL n’erikuganyira ahabw’okumanyisa kw’ahaiguru, kwonka abantu abarikutabaasa kuteekateeka omu mbeera egi, bwonyima bateekateeke barikuta omubiri gwabo omu MBEERA Y’OMUNTU AFIIRE: ebigyere hamwe, amaisho g’ebigyere nigahurwa omu muringo gw’ekipapura emikono n’obugaro hatarikwesiba, nibatungirwe omu muringo gw’omubiri.

Amaisho gashemereire kwebiswa ahabw’okugira ngu ebintu by’omubiri tigurikuretera kwihirira. Okuroota ku kwatanisiibwe n’OKUTEEKATEEKA nikivaamu kukira ahabw’ebiva bya OKUTEEKATEEKA.

Nikyetenga kwega kurekura amagagara goona g’omubiri kandi bwonyima kukwata AMAANI ha maisho g’enyindo okuhitsya omu kumurra omutima omu rupa ogwo rukuza, bwonyima turaakurata n’okutu kw’amaiso okwa burya okuhitsya omu kumurra omutima omuri, bwonyima turaahikayo hamwe n’omukono gw’amaiso ogwa burya, ekigyere ky’amaiso ekya burya, ekigyere ky’amaiso ekya bumoso, omukono gw’amaiso ogwa bumoso, okutu kw’amaiso okwa bumoso kandi obuuri, turikumurra kimwe okumurra omutima omuri buri kiina aha obwomeezi obu tutwire n’AMAANI.

Okutegyeka aha mubiri nikutandika n’okutegyeka aha kumurra. Okumurra omutima ogurikuhunira nikumurra n’omurra gwona omu bwomeezi bwayo bwona omu bwomeezi, kwonka abantu ba GNÓSTICOS nibabaasa kumurra n’okwenda kwabo omu kiina kyona ky’omubiri, kyaba amaisho g’inyindo, okutu, omukono, ekigyere, n’ebindi.

Kirarekwa kuruga omu kukora ngu tugira okubaasa okutegeka, kuhuta nari kuremesa okumurra, nibabaasa kuhuta nari kuremesa okubetera kw’omutima.

Okutegyeka aha kubetera kw’omutima tikirikubaasa kuruga aha magagara g’omutima, kwonka kwesigira aha kutegyeka omurra. Eki nibwo bwire obutariho kubuza, OKURARAMA KWA KABIRI nari OMUTIMA OMUKURU.

Okutegyeka omurra nari okutegyeka omutima ogwa kabiri, nikuhikiirizibwa bingi okurugiirra aha KUREKURA ekitwaaza amagagara goona.

Okurugiirra aha AMAANI nitubaasa kuhuta nari kuremesa OKUMURRA omutima ogwa kabiri n’okubetera kw’omutima ogwa ira.

SHAMADHÍ, ÉXTASIS, SATORI, nikukurata buri izooba n’okumurra okurikuremesa bingi, kandi omu MAHA-SHAMADHÍ okumurra guhweere.

Omu bwire bwa SHAMADHÍ ESENCIA, BUDHATA, erarugamu okuruga omu BUNYAMUNTU, bwonyima erakwatanisa hamwe na BUNYAMURWA kandi kiriize kumanya EKIRYO omu MURI OGURIKUMURIKIZA.

Byonka omu kubura kwawe nitubaasa kugamba na ISHE, BRAHAMA.

SHABA kandi MUTEKATEEKE, kugira ngu mwije kuhurra IRU LY’OKUHUMIRA.

LEO n’entebe y’EIZOBA, omutima gw’ENSI. LEO nigurebera omutima gw’abantu.

EIZOBA ry’obwomeezi n’OMUTIMA. Omu mutima amaani agaiso nigakwatanisa hamwe n’aga ahansi, kugira ngu aga ahansi garerekura.

Ekyoma kya LEO n’ezaabu enyeeru. Eibaare rya LEO n’eibaare ly’omuhendo; erangi ya LEO n’EZAABU.

Omu kukora tumaabiise kumanya ngu abazairwe abaija LEO nibashushana nk’ENTAHAZI, ab’amaani, abanyazi, abagugu, abarikusiima, ab’enyima.

Kwonka abantu nibingi kandi kirakworeka ngu omu bazairwe ba LEO nibashanga eby’ahaiguru, ab’eitima, abatooraine, abarikutegeka.

Abazairwe ba LEO baine obugabe bw’okwegera, nibakuremesa omutima n’obuzira bw’ENTAHAZI. Abantu abakuzire b’ekimanyiso eki, bahika kuba ABAZIRA ABAKUKURU.

Omuhendo gw’ahaiguru gw’EIZOBA gwa LEO guri munonga omutima kandi ogurikunaziibwa. Omuhendo gw’ahaiguru gw’EIZOBA gwa LEO nikugirra bingi aha bugabe bwabo.

Omu omwezirwe gwona ogwa LEO nahoho okwekundiira okurikwera omuri embeera erikwetenga; byona nibesiimira omu muringo gw’omuntu.

Abazairwe ba LEO buri izooba nibakoresa kurwanisa oburwaire bw’emikono n’emikono.
