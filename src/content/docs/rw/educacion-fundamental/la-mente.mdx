Binyuze mu byo twanyuzemo, twasanze bidashoboka gusobanukirwa IGIHE CYITWA URUKUNDO, keretse tumaze gusobanukirwa mu buryo BWUZUYE ikibazo kitoroshye cy'UBWONKO.

Abatekereza ko UBWONKO ari UBUBONKO, baribeshya rwose. UBWONKO ni IMARASHA, burihariye, bushobora kwigenga kuri MATERIEL, bushobora mu bihe bimwe na bimwe byo gusinzira cyangwa mu gihe cyo gusinzira bisanzwe, kwiyimura ahantu ha kure cyane kugira ngo burebe kandi bwumve ibirimo kubera ahantu nk'aho.

Mu ma laboratwari ya PARAPSICOLOGI, hakorwa ubushakashatsi bukomeye ku bantu bari mu BIHE BYO GUSINZIRA.

Abantu benshi bari mu BIHE BYO GUSINZIRA bashoboye gutanga amakuru arambuye ku byabaye, abantu n'imimerere mu gihe bari mu bihe byo gusinzira byabereye ahantu ha kure.

Abahanga bashoboye kwemeza nyuma y'ubwo bushakashatsi, ukuri kw'AYO MAKURU. Bashoboye kwemeza ukuri kw'ibyabaye, ubwo butungane bw'IBIBAZO.

Binyuze muri ubwo bushakashatsi bwo mu ma laboratwari ya PARAPSICOLOGI, byagaragajwe neza n'ubushishozi n'ubunararibonye ko UBUBONKO atari UBWONKO.

Mu kuri no mu kuri kose dushobora kuvuga ko ubwonko bushobora gutembera mu bihe no mu kirere, bitagendeye ku bwonko, kugira ngo turebe kandi twumve ibintu birimo kubera ahantu ha kure.

UKURI kw'IBYUMVIRO BIKOMEYE byaragaragajwe RWANDI kandi umusazi gusa cyangwa ikigoryi, ashobora gutekereza guhakana ukuri kw'IBYUMVIRO BIKOMEYE.

Ubwonko bukorerwa gukora ibitekerezo ariko si ibitekerezo. Ubwonko ni igikoresho cy'UBWONKO gusa, si ubwonko.

Dukeneye kwiga ubwonko mu buryo bwimbitse niba dushaka kumenya mu buryo BWUZUYE icyitwa URUKUNDO.

Abana n'urubyiruko, abahungu n'abakobwa, bafite ubwonko bworoshye, bwihuta, bwiteguye, bukeye, n'ibindi.

Abana n'urubyiruko benshi bishimira kubaza ababyeyi babo n'abarimu, ku bintu nk'ibi n'ibi, bifuza kumenya byinshi bashaka kumenya kandi ni yo mpamvu babaza, bareba, babona ibintu bimwe na bimwe abantu bakuru basuzugura cyangwa batabona.

Uko imyaka igenda ihita, uko tugenda dukura mu myaka, ubwonko bugenda bukomeza buhoro buhoro.

Ubwonko bw'abakambwe burahamye, bwarakomeye, ntibugihinduka na gato.

Abasaza barameze gutyo kandi ni ko bapfa, ntibahinduka, bagera ku bintu byose baturutse ahantu hagaragara.

"UBUKECURU" bw'abasaza, imyumvire yabo, ibitekerezo bihamye, n'ibindi. bisa nk'ibyateraniye hamwe IKARE, IBUYE ridahinduka na gato. Ni yo mpamvu umugani uzwi cyane uvuga ngo "UBWANGA N'ISHUSHO KUGEZA KU GITURO".

Ni ngombwa KO ABARIMU n'ABARIMUKAZI bashinzwe guhugura UBUNTU bw'abanyeshuri, biga ubwonko mu buryo bwimbitse, kugira ngo bashobore kuyobora abazaza mu buryo bw'ubwenge.

Birababaje gusobanukirwa neza, uko uko igihe kigenda gihita UBWONKO bugenda bukomeza buhoro buhoro.

UBWONKO ni bwo bwica UKURI, ukuri. UBWONKO bwangiza URUKUNDO.

Umuntu ugeze mu zabukuru ntashobora gukunda kuko ubwonko bwe bwuzuye ibintu bibabaje, imyumvire, ibitekerezo bihamye nk'icyuma cy'ubwugarizi, n'ibindi.

Hariho abasaza bahumura bahangara ko bashoboye gukunda NONAHA, ariko ibibaho ni uko abo basaza buzuye irari ry'ibitsina rijyanye n'imyaka kandi bakitiranya IRARI n'URUKUNDO.

"UMUSAZA WESE UHUMURA" na "UMUKECURU WESE UHUMURA" banyura mu bihe bikomeye by'ubusambanyi mbere yo gupfa kandi bibwira ko ari URUKUNDO.

URUKUNDO rw'abasaza ntirushoboka kuko ubwonko burwangiza rufite "IBITEKEREZO BIHAMYE", "IMYUMVIRE", "IFUHARARU", "IBYO BANYUZEMO", "IBYO BIBUKA", irari ry'ibitsina, n'ibindi.

UBWONKO ni bwo mwanzi mubi w'URUKUNDO. Mu bihugu BIRENZE UBUTEGETSI, URUKUNDO ntirukibaho kuko ubwonko bw'abantu bunukirwa n'inganda, konti za banki, lisansi na seleru.

Hariho amacupa menshi y'ubwonko kandi ubwonko bwa buri muntu bupfunyitse neza.

Bamwe bafite UBWONKO bupfunyitse mu BUCOMUYI BUBI, abandi babufite bupfunyitse muri CAPITALISME itagira imbabazi.

Hariho abafite UBWONKO BUPFUNYITSE mu ifuha, mu rwango, mu kwifuza kuba umukire, mu mwanya mwiza mu muryango, mu bwoba bwo kubona ibintu mu buryo bubi mu kwishingikiriza ku bantu bamwe na bamwe, mu kwishingikiriza ku mibabaro yabo bwite, mu bibazo byabo byo mu muryango, n'ibindi.

Abantu bakunda gufunga UBWONKO, Abantu bake nibo bafata umwanzuro wo kumenagura icupa.

Dukeneye KUBOHORA, UBWONKO ariko abantu bakunda ubucakara, biragoye cyane kubona umuntu mu buzima udafite UBWONKO bupfunyitse neza.

Abarimu n'abarimu bakwiye kwigisha abanyeshuri babo ibyo bintu byose. Bakwiye kwigisha abazaza gukora ubushakashatsi ku bwonko bwabo bwite, kubwitegereza, kubusobanukirwa, muri ubwo buryo gusa binyuze mu GUSOBANUKIRWA neza dushobora kwirinda ko ubwonko bukomeza, bukagwa mu gihe, bukangirika.

Ikintu kimwe gishobora guhindura isi ni icyitwa URUKUNDO, ariko ubwonko bwangiza URUKUNDO.

Dukeneye KWIGA ubwonko bwacu bwite, kubwitegereza, kubukorera ubushakashatsi bwimbitse, kubusobanukirwa by'ukuri. Muri ubwo buryo gusa, muri ubwo buryo gusa twiyemeza kuba ba nyirubwite, ubwonko bwacu bwite, tuzica umwicanyi w'URUKUNDO kandi tuzishimira by'ukuri.

Ababeshya ku URUKUNDO, abakora imishinga ku URUKUNDO, abashaka ko URUKUNDO rukora hakurikijwe ibyo bakunda n'ibyo badakunda, imishinga n'ibeshya, amategeko n'imyumvire, ibyo bibuka n'ibyo banyuzemo, n'ibindi. ntibazigera bamenya by'ukuri icyo URUKUNDO ari cyo, mu by'ukuri bahindutse abanzi b'URUKUNDO.

Ni ngombwa gusobanukirwa mu buryo BWUZUYE ibyo ari byo buryo bwo gukora ubwonko mu bihe byo kwegeranya ibyo banyuzemo.

Umwarimu, umwarimukazi akenshi barakara mu buryo bwiza ariko rimwe na rimwe mu buryo bwa gicucu kandi nta mpamvu nyayo, batumva ko umujinya wose utari uwo ukwiye usigara mu bwenge bw'abanyeshuri, ingaruka z'imikorere nk'iyo itari yo, akenshi ni ugutakaza URUKUNDO ku MWARIMU, ku MWARIMUKAZI.

UBWONKO bwangiza URUKUNDO kandi ibi ni ikintu ABARIMU n'ABARIMUKAZI bo mu mashuri, Amakoleji na za kaminuza batagomba kwibagirwa na gato.

Ni ngombwa gusobanukirwa neza ubwo buryo bwose bwo mu mutwe burangiza ubwiza bw'URUKUNDO.

Ntibihagije kuba umubyeyi w'umuryango, ugomba kumenya GUKUNDA. Ababyeyi b'umuryango bizera ko bakunda abahungu babo n'abakobwa babo kuko babafite, kuko ari ababo, kuko babatunze, nk'uko umuntu afite igare, imodoka, inzu.

Icyo gitekerezo cyo gutunga ubwite, gikunze kwitiranwa n'URUKUNDO ariko ntigishobora kuba URUKUNDO.

Abarimu n'abarimukazi bo mu rugo rwacu rwa kabiri ari rwo ishuri, bizera ko bakunda abigishwa babo, abigishwakazi babo, kuko ari ababo, kuko babatunze, ariko ibyo si URUKUNDO. Igitekerezo cyo gutunga cyangwa kwishingikiriza SI URUKUNDO.

UBWONKO bwangiza URUKUNDO kandi gusobanukirwa gusa n'imikorere mibi yose y'ubwonko, uburyo bwacu butagira ishingiro bwo gutekereza, imigenzo yacu mibi, ingeso zikora byikora, uburyo butari bwo bwo kubona ibintu, n'ibindi. dushobora kugera ku kubaho, kugerageza UKURI icyo kitari icyigihe, icyitwa URUKUNDO.

Abashaka ko URUKUNDO ruhinduka igice cy'imashini yabo bwite, abashaka ko URUKUNDO rugendera mu nzira zikocamye z'imyumvire yabo bwite, irari, ubwoba, ibyo banyuzemo mu buzima, uburyo bwo kwikunda bwo kubona ibintu, uburyo butari bwo bwo gutekereza, n'ibindi. birangiza URUKUNDO kuko rutigera rwemera gucishwa bugufi.

Abashaka ko URUKUNDO rukora nk'UKO MBA NSHAKA, nk'UKO MBYIFUZA, nk'UKO MBITEKEREZA, baratakaza URUKUNDO kuko CUPIDO, IMANA y'URUKUNDO, ititeguye na gato kwemera gucishwa bugufi na JYE.

Ugomba kurangiza JYE, hamwe na JYE UBWANJYE, hamwe na WE UBWANJYE kugira ngo utatakaza umwana w'URUKUNDO.

JYE ni ikimasa cy'ibyo bibuka, irari, ubwoba, urwango, amarangamutima, ibyo banyuzemo, ubwikunde, ishyari, umwina, ubusambanyi, n'ibindi.

Gusobanukirwa gusa buri kosa ukwaryo; gusa kwiga, kubyitegereza mu buryo butaziguye atari mu gace k'ubwenge gusa, ahubwo no ku nzego zose z'ubwenge bucye bw'ubwonko, buri kosa rigenda ribura, turagenda dupfa umwanya ku wundi. Muri ubwo buryo gusa, muri ubwo buryo gusa, tugera ku kureka JYE.

Abashaka gufunga URUKUNDO mu icupa riteye ubwoba rya jye, baratakaza URUKUNDO, bagasigara batarufite, kuko URUKUNDO rutigera rushobora gufungwa.

Ku bw'amahirwe make abantu bashaka ko URUKUNDO rwitwara hakurikijwe ingeso zabo bwite, ibyifuzo, imigenzo, n'ibindi., abantu bashaka ko URUKUNDO rwiyoroshya kuri JYE kandi ibyo ntibishoboka rwose kuko URUKUNDO rutumvira JYE.

Abashakanye bakundana, cyangwa byaba byiza tuvuze ko bafite amarangamutima, ari byo byiganje cyane muri iyi SI, batekereza ko URUKUNDO rugomba kugenda mu budahemuka mu nzira z'ibyifuzo byabo bwite, ibyifuzo bibi, amakosa, n'ibindi., kandi muri ibyo baribeshya rwose.

Nimuvuge ku byerekeye bombi!, abakundana cyangwa abafite amarangamutima yo gukora imibonano mpuzabitsina baravuga, ari byo byiganje cyane muri iyi SI, kandi, hanyuma hakaza ibiganiro, imishinga, ibyifuzo n'imihemo. Buri wese aravuga ikintu, agatanga imishinga ye, ibyifuzo bye, uburyo bwe bwo kubona ibintu by'ubuzima kandi ashaka ko URUKUNDO rugenda nk'imashini ya gari ya moshi mu nzira z'icyuma zashyizweho n'ubwonko.

Abo Bakundana cyangwa abafite amarangamutima baribeshya cyane!, bari kure y'ukuri.

URUKUNDO ntirumvira JYE kandi iyo abashakanye bashaka gushyira ingoyi ku ijosi no kuruyobora, ruhunga rugasiga umugabo n'umugore mu kaga.

UBWONKO bufite umuco mubi wo kugereranya. Umugabo agereranya umukobwa n'undi. Umugore agereranya umugabo n'undi. Umwarimu agereranya umunyeshuri n'undi, umunyeshuri w'umukobwa n'undi nk'aho abanyeshuri be bose badakwiye agaciro kamwe. Mu by'ukuri kugereranya kose ni BIBI.

Umuntu witegereza irembo ry'izuba ryiza akagereranya n'irindi, ntabwo abasha gusobanukirwa ubwiza afite imbere y'amaso.

Umuntu witegereza umusozi mwiza akagereranya n'undi yabonye ejo, ntabwo asobanukirwa ubwiza bw'umusozi afite imbere y'amaso.

Aho KUGERERANYA kubaho nta URUKUNDO RW'UKURI rubaho. Umubyeyi n'Umubyeyikazi bakunda abahungu babo by'ukuri, ntibabagereranya na gato na buri wese, barabakunda kandi ibyo ni byo byose.

Umugabo ukunda umugore we by'ukuri, ntakora ikosa ryo kumugereranya na buri wese, aramukunda kandi ibyo ni byo byose.

UMWARIMU cyangwa Umwarimukazi bakunda abanyeshuri babo, ntibatonesha na gato, ntibabagereranya hagati yabo, barabakunda by'ukuri kandi ibyo ni byo byose.

Ubwonko Bwagabanijwe no kugereranya, ubwonko bwabaye imbata ya DUALISME, bwangiza URUKUNDO.

Ubwonko bwagabanijwe no kurwanira abatari bahuje ntibushobora gusobanukirwa ibishya, burakomera, buragwa mu gihe.

UBWONKO BUFITE IBINTU BYINSHI BIJIMYE, Uturere, ubutaka bw'ubwenge bucye, imihanda itaziguye, ariko icyiza kuruta ni ESENCE, UBWA Buntu kandi buri hagati.

Iyo DUALISME irangiye, iyo ubwonko bufata BWUZUYE, BUTUJE, GACECE, BWIMBITSE, iyo butakigereranya, noneho ESENCE irakanguka, UBWA Buntu kandi ugomba kuba intego nyayo y'UBUREZI BW'INGENZI.

Reka dutandukanye hagati ya INTEGO na IGITEKEREZO. Mu BIREBA, hariho ubwenge bukeye. Mu BIREBA, hariho Ubwenge businziriye, UBWENGE BUCYE.

UBWENGE BWUZUYE gusa bushobora kwishimira UBUMENYI BWUZUYE.

Amakuru y'ubwenge abanyeshuri bahabwa muri iki gihe mu Mashuri yose, Amakoleji na za Kaminuza, ni IGITEKEREZO ijana ku ijana.

UBUMENYI BWUZUYE ntibushobora kuboneka nta UBWENGE BWUZUYE.

Abanyeshuri bagomba kubanza kugera ku KWIMENYA ubwabo hanyuma bakagera ku BWENGE BWUZUYE.

Binyuze mu NZIRA Y'URUKUNDO gusa dushobora kugera ku BWENGE BWUZUYE no ku BUMENYI BWUZUYE.

Ni ngombwa gusobanukirwa IKIBAZO KIGOYE CY'UBWONKO niba dushaka by'ukuri guca mu NZIRA Y'URUKUNDO.
