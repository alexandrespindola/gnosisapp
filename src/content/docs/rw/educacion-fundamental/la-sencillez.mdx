Birakenewe cyane, birakwiye guteza imbere ubwenge bw’ubuhanzi kuko buzana umuntu umudendezo nyakuri wo kubaho. Hatariho ubwenge, ntibishoboka kugera ku bushobozi nyakuri bwo gusesengura byimbitse.

Abarimu n’abarezi bo mu mashuri, amakoleji na za kaminuza bagomba kuyobora abanyeshuri babo mu nzira y’ubwenge bwo kwisuzuma.

Mu gice cyacu cyashize twize byinshi ku bijyanye n’inzira z’ishyari kandi niba dushaka gukuraho ibintu byose bigize ishyari, yaba ari ibyo mu idini, ibyo mu rukundo n’ibindi, tugomba kumenya neza icyo ishyari ari cyo, kuko ari uko gusa dusobanukiwe neza kandi twimbitse inzira zitagira ingano z’ishyari, tubasha gukuraho ishyari iryo ari ryo ryose.

Ishyari risenya ingo, ishyari risenya ubucuti, ishyari ritera intambara z’amadini, urwango rw’abavandimwe, ubwicanyi n’imibabaro y’uburyo bwose.

Ishyari hamwe n’ibintu byose byinshi rigizwe naryo ryihisha inyuma y’intego nziza cyane. Ishyari ribaho muri wa muntu wamenyeshejwe ku kubaho kw’abera b’abahanga cyane. Mahatma cyangwa Gurús, na we ashaka kuba umweranda. Ishyari ribaho muri wa mukunzi w’abantu uhatanira kurenza abandi bakunzi b’abantu. Ishyari ribaho muri buri muntu wifuza imico myiza kuko yari afite amakuru, kuko mu mutwe we harimo amakuru ku bijyanye n’abantu bera buzuye imico myiza.

Icyifuzo cyo kuba umweranda, icyifuzo cyo kugira imico myiza, icyifuzo cyo kuba mukuru gifite ishingiro ku ishyari.

Abera n’imico yabo myiza bateje ibyago byinshi. Turibuka inkuru y’umugabo na we ubwe yifataga nk’umweranda cyane.

Rimwe na rimwe, umusizi ushonje kandi ukennye yakomanze ku miryango ye kugira ngo amushyikirize igisigo cyiza cyane cyahariwe umweranda wo mu nkuru yacu. Umusizi yari ategereje igiceri gusa kugira ngo agure ibiryo byo kugaburira umubiri we urushye kandi ushaje.

Umusizi yari yiyumvira byose usibye gutukwa. Igitangaje cyari kinini ubwo umweranda amureba impuhwe kandi ahinnye ibirenge afunze umuryango abwira umusizi utagira isoni ati: "va hano nshuti, genda, genda... sinakunda ibintu nk'ibi, nanga ikinamico... sinkunda ubusa bw'isi, ubu buzima ni ibinyoma... nkurikira inzira yo kwicisha bugufi no kwiyoroshya. Umusizi utagira isoni, washakaga igiceri gusa, yakiriye igitutsi cy’umweranda, ijambo ribabaza, urushyi, kandi umutima we uremereye n’inanga ye yagizwe ibice yagiye mu mihanda y’umujyi buhoro... buhoro... buhoro.

Igisirikare gishya kigomba kuzamuka hashingiwe ku bwenge nyakuri kuko ubu ari ubuhanzi rwose.

Kwibuka no kwibuka ntibikora ubuhanzi. Kwibuka ni imva y’ahashize. Kwibuka no kwibuka ni urupfu.

Ubwenge nyakuri ni ikintu cyo mu mutwe cyo kubohoka rwose.

Kwibuka ntibishobora kuzana umudendezo nyakuri kuko ari iby’ahashize kandi bityo bikaba byarapfuye.

Ubwenge si ibintu by’ahashize cyangwa ejo hazaza. Ubwenge ni ubw’igihe turimo kubamo hano ubu. Kwibuka buri gihe kuzana igitekerezo cy’ejo hazaza.

Ni ngombwa kwiga siyansi, filozofiya, ubuhanzi n’idini, ariko ntugomba kwizera kwiga ukuri kw’ibyo wibuka kuko bitari byo.

Ni ubupfu gushyira ubumenyi mu mva y’ibyo wibuka. Ni ubupfu guhamba mu rwobo rw’ahashize ubumenyi dukwiye gusobanukirwa.

Ntabwo twigeze twamagana kwiga, ubwenge, siyansi, ariko biragaragara ko bitemewe gushyira imibavu myiza y’ubumenyi mu mva yononekaye y’ibyo wibuka.

Birakenewe kwiga, birakenewe gukora ubushakashatsi, birakenewe gusesengura, ariko tugomba kuzirikana cyane kugira ngo dusobanukirwe ku rwego rwose rw’ubwenge.

Umuntu woroshye by’ukuri arasobanukirwa cyane kandi afite ubwenge busanzwe.

Icy’ingenzi mu buzima si ibyo twakusanyirije mu mva y’ibyo twibuka, ahubwo ni ibyo twasobanukiwe bitari ku rwego rw’ubwenge gusa ahubwo no mu turere dutandukanye tw’ubwenge butagaragara.

Siyansi, ubumenyi, bigomba guhinduka ubwenge bwihuse. Iyo ubumenyi, iyo kwiga byahindutse ubwenge nyakuri bw’ubuhanzi dushobora gusobanukirwa ibintu byose ako kanya kuko ubwenge buhita buba ubwihuse.

Mu muntu woroshye nta bibazo biri mu bwenge kuko ikibazo cyose cy’ubwenge giterwa n’ibyo wibuka. YO ya Machiavelian dufite imbere ni kwibuka kwakusanyijwe.

Ibyabaye mu buzima bigomba guhinduka ubwenge nyakuri.

Iyo ibyabaye bitahindutse ubwenge, iyo ibyabaye bikomeje kwibukwa bigize ibintu bibora by’imva hejuru yabyo hakaba hacanye umuriro usukuye kandi wa luciferica.

Birakenewe kumenya ko ubwenge bw’inyamaswa bwambuwe rwose ubwenge bwose bwo mu buryo bw’umwuka ari ukugaragaza amagambo y’ibyo wibuka gusa, isukari y’imva yacanye ku rubaho rwo gushyingura.

Umuntu woroshye afite ubwenge butagira ibyabaye kuko byahindutse ubwenge, byahindutse ubwenge bw’ubuhanzi.

Urupfu n’ubuzima bifitanye isano rya bugufi. Ari uko imbuto ipfa nibwo igihingwa kivuka, ari uko ibyabaye bipfa nibwo ubwenge buvuka. Iyi ni inzira yo guhinduka nyakuri.

Umuntu ugoye afite ibyo yibuka byuzuye ibyabaye.

Ibi birerekana ko atari afite ubwenge bw’ubuhanzi kuko iyo ibyabaye byasobanukiwe rwose ku rwego rwose rw’ubwenge biba bitagihari nk’ibyabaye ahubwo bikavuka nk’ubwenge.

Birakenewe kubanza kubigerageza, ariko ntitugomba kuguma mu kibanza cy’ibyabaye kuko ubwenge buba bugoye kandi bugatandukana. Birakenewe kubaho ubuzima mu buryo bukomeye no guhindura ibyabaye byose mu bwenge nyakuri bw’ubuhanzi.

Abibeshya bibwira ko kugira ngo dusobanukirwe ibintu byoroshye kandi bisanzwe tugomba kuva mu isi, tukaba abasabirizi, tukaba mu nzu nto z’ibyatsi kandi tukambara umwenda aho kwambara isutiye y’agaciro, baribeshya rwose.

Abamonaki benshi, abimukira benshi bonyine, abasabirizi benshi, bafite ubwenge bugoye cyane kandi butandukanye.

Nta cyo bimaze kuva mu isi no kubaho nk’abamonaki niba ibyo wibuka byuzuye ibyabaye bigenga imitekerereze itagira ingano.

Nta cyo bimaze kubaho nk’abimukira bashaka kubaho nk’abera niba ibyo wibuka byuzuye amakuru atarasobanurwa neza, atarahindutse ubwenge mu mfuruka zitandukanye, inzira n’uturere tw’ubwenge butagaragara.

Abahindura amakuru yo mu mutwe mu bwenge nyakuri bw’ubuhanzi, bahindura ibyabaye mu buzima mu bwenge nyakuri bwimbitse nta kintu na kimwe bafite mu byo bibuka, babaho umwanya ku wundi buzuye ubuziranenge nyakuri, bahindutse aboroheje kandi basanzwe nubwo baba mu mazu meza kandi mu rubibi rw’ubuzima bw’umujyi.

Abana bato batarageza imyaka irindwi buzuye ubworoherane n’ubwiza nyakuri bw’imbere kubera ko byerekana gusa binyuze muri bo UBUTUMWA buzima bw’ubuzima mu gihe YO PSYCHOLOGIQUE itaboneka.

Tugomba kongera kwigarurira ubuto bwatakaye, mu mitima yacu no mu bwenge bwacu. Tugomba kongera kwigarurira ubwere niba dushaka by’ukuri kugira ibyishimo.

Ibyabaye no kwiga byahinduwe mu bwenge bwimbitse ntibisiga imyanda mu mva y’ibyo wibuka maze, tugahinduka aboroheje, basanzwe, abere, abishimye.

Kuzirikana byimbitse ku byabaye n’ubumenyi bwabonetse, kwisuzuma byimbitse, psychoanalysis yimbitse guhindura, guhindura byose mu bwenge bw’ubuhanzi bwimbitse. Iyi ni inzira y’ibyishimo nyakuri bivuka mu bwenge n’urukundo.
