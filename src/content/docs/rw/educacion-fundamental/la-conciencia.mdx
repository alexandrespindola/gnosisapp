Abantu benshi bitiranya UBWENGE n'UBUHANGA cyangwa UBUTEKAMUTWE, maze umuntu uhanga cyane cyangwa ufite ubwenge bwinshi bakamwita umuntu uzi ubwenge cyane.

Twebwe tuvuga ko UBWENGE mu muntu ntakabuza kandi tudatinya kubeshya, ari ubwoko bwihariye cyane bwo KUMENYA IBIRI IMBERE byigenga rwose ku bikorwa byose byo mu mutwe.

Ubushobozi bw'UBWENGE butuma tumenya ubwacu.

UBWENGE butanga ubumenyi bwuzuye bw'IYO ARIYO, aho iherereye, ibyo azi by'ukuri, ibyo itazi by'ukuri.

INYIGISHO Z'IMPINDUKA MU BITEKEREZO zerekana ko umuntu ubwe ari we ushobora kwimenya.

Twebwe ubwacu ni twe dushobora kumenya niba tuzi ubwenge mu gihe runaka cyangwa tutabuzi.

Umuntu ubwe ni we ushobora kumenya ubwenge bwe bwite n'ubwaho niba bubaho mu gihe runaka cyangwa butabaho.

Umuntu ubwe nta wundi usibye we, ashobora kubona umwanya muto, akanya gato ko mbere y'ako kanya, mbere y'ako kanya, atari azi ubwenge by'ukuri, yari afite ubwenge businziriye cyane, hanyuma azibagirwa ubwo bunararibonye cyangwa abubike nk'urwibutso, nk'urwibutso rw'ubunararibonye bukomeye.

Ni ngombwa kumenya ko UBWENGE mu NYAMASWA Y'UBWENGE atari ikintu gihoraho, gihoraho.

Ubusanzwe UBWENGE mu NYAMASWA Y'UBWENGE yitwa umuntu, businzira cyane.

Ni gake, ni gake cyane ibihe UBWENGE bubyuka; inyamaswa y'ubwenge irakora, iratwara imodoka, irashyingiranwa, irapfa, n'ibindi ifite ubwenge businziriye rwose kandi mu bihe bidasanzwe cyane ni bwo ibyuka:

Ubuzima bw'umuntu ni ubuzima bwo kurota, ariko we yizera ko abyutse kandi ntabwo azigera yemera ko arota, ko afite ubwenge businziriye.

Niba hari umuntu ubyutse, yakumva isoni cyane n'ubwe, yahita asobanukirwa n'ubupfapfa bwe, agasekeje.

Ubu buzima burasekeje cyane, buteye agahinda cyane kandi ni gake cyane buba bwiza.

Niba umukinnyi w'iteramakofe abyutse ako kanya mu gihe cyo kurwana, yareba afite isoni abantu bose bubashywe maze agahunga icyo gikorwa kibi, bitangaje imbaga nyamwinshi isinziriye kandi itazi ubwenge.

Iyo umuntu yemeye ko afite UBWENGE BUSINZIRIYE, mushobora kwizera ko yamaze gutangira kubyuka.

Amashuri y'inyigisho zinyuranye za Sicologiya zishaje ahakana kubaho k'UBWENGE ndetse n'akamaro k'iryo jambo, arega ikindi gihugu cyo gusinzira cyane. Abayoboke b'ayo Mashuri basinzira cyane mu buryo butaboneka kandi butazi ubwenge.

Abantu bitiranya ubwenge n'imikorere ya Psychologiya; ibitekerezo, amarangamutima, ibitekerezo byo gutwara ibinyabiziga n'ibyumviro, mu by'ukuri ntibazi ubwenge cyane, barasinzira cyane.

Abemera ko UBWENGE bubaho ariko bagahakana ku buryo busobanutse amarembo atandukanye y'ubwenge, barega kubura ubunararibonye bw'ubwenge, gusinzira k'ubwenge.

Umuntu wese wigeze kubyuka by'akanya gato, azi neza ku bwe ko hariho amarembo atandukanye y'ubwenge abasha kugaragara muri we.

Igihe cya mbere. Ni igihe kingana iki tumara tuzi ubwenge?

Icya kabiri Inshuro. Ni inshuro zingahe twabyutse dufite ubwenge?

Icya gatatu. UBUNINI N'IMBARAGA. Ni iki azazira ubwenge?

INYIGISHO Z'IMPINDUKA MU BITEKEREZO na PHILOKALIA ya kera bemeza ko binyuze mu MIHIGO IKOMEYE cyane y'ubwoko bwihariye cyane bishobora kubyutsa ubwenge maze bugahoraho kandi bugakoreshwa.

INTEGO y'UBUVUGIZI BW'INGENZI ni ukubyutsa UBWENGE. Nta kamaro ko kwiga imyaka icumi cyangwa cumi n'itanu mu Ishuri, Koleji na Kaminuza, niba tuvuye mu byumba twiga turi ibikoresho byo mu ruganda bisinziriye.

Ntabwo ari ugukabya kwemeza ko binyuze mu MIHIGO mikuru ANIMAL Y'UBWENGE ishobora kumenya ubwayo by'iminota mike.

Biragaragara ko muri ibi hakunze kubaho ubu ibintu bidasanzwe tugomba gushaka itara rya Di√≥genes, ibyo bintu bidasanzwe bihagarariwe n'ABAGABO B'UKURI, BUDDHA, YESU, HERMES, QUETZACOATL, n'ibindi.

Aba bashinze AMADINI bari bafite UBWENGE BUHORAHO, bari ABAHUNGUYE.

Ubusanzwe abantu NTABWO bazi ubwenge. Kwiyitiranya ko tuzi ubwenge burundu, biva mu kwibuka no mu nzira zose zo gutekereza.

Umuntu ukora imyitozo yo gusubiza amaso inyuma kugira ngo yibuke ubuzima bwe bwose, ashobora kwibuka by'ukuri, kwibuka inshuro yashatse, abana yabyaye, ababyeyi be bari bo, Abigisha be, n'ibindi, ariko ibi ntibivuze kubyutsa ubwenge, ibi ni ugutekereza ku bikorwa bidafite ubwenge kandi ibyo birarangiye.

Ni ngombwa gusubiramo ibyo twamaze kuvuga mu bice byabanje. Hariho ibyiciro bine by'UBWENGE. Ibyo ni: ICYIZU, igihugu cyo GUTUMIRA, Ubwenge bw'Umuntu ubwe na UBWENGE BWOSE.

NYAMASWA Y'UBWENGE ikennye yitwa UMUNTU, ibaho muri ibyo bihugu bibiri gusa. Igice kimwe cy'ubuzima bwayo kigenda mu nzozi naho ikindi mu byitwa nabi IGIHUGU CYO GUTUMIRA, na cyo ni inzozi.

Umuntu usinzira kandi akrota, yizera ko abyuka kubera ko asubiye mu gihugu cyo gutumira, ariko mu by'ukuri muri iki gihugu cyo gutumira akomeza kurota.

Ibi bisa n'umuseke, inyenyeri zirihisha kubera umucyo w'izuba ariko zirakomeza kubaho nubwo amaso y'umubiri atazibona.

Mu buzima busanzwe bwa buri munsi umuntu ntazi na kimwe ku bw'UBWENGE BW'UMUNTU ubwe kandi cyane cyane ku BWENGE BWOSE.

Ariko abantu baribeshya kandi buri wese yizera ko AZI UBWENGE; NYAMASWA Y'UBWENGE yizera cyane ko ifite ubwenge bwayo kandi ntizigera yemera ko yabwirwa ko isinziriye kandi ko ibaho itazi ubwenge bwayo.

Hariho ibihe bidasanzwe NYAMASWA Y'UBWENGE ibyuka, ariko ibyo bihe ni bike cyane, bishobora guhagararirwa mu mwanya muto w'akaga gakomeye, mu gihe cy'amarangamutima akomeye, mu bihe bishya, mu bihe bishya bitunguranye, n'ibindi.

Ni ibyago by'ukuri ko NYAMASWA Y'UBWENGE ikennye itagira ubuyobozi na bumwe kuri ibyo bihugu bihoraho by'ubwenge, ko itabasha kubibutsa ko itabasha kubikomeza.

Ariko UBUVUGIZI BW'INGENZI bwo buvuga ko umuntu ashobora KUGERA ku kugenzura UBWENGE no kubona UBWENGE BW'UMUNTU ubwe.

INYIGISHO Z'IMPINDUKA MU BITEKEREZO zifite uburyo bwa siyansi bwo KUBYUTSA UBWENGE.

Niba dushaka KUBYUTSA UBWENGE dukeneye gutangira gusuzuma, kwiga hanyuma tugakuraho inzitizi zose zigaragara mu nzira yacu, muri iki gitabo twigishije inzira yo kubyutsa UBWENGE duhereye mu bigo by'Amashuri.
