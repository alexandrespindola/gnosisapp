Ubuzima bw'umuntu butangirana n'akagingo kamwe koroshye kandi kagengwa, nk'uko bisanzwe, n'igihe cyihuta cyane cy'utugingo tubaho.

Gusama inda, gutwita, kuvuka, ni iteka itsinda ryiza cyane kandi rihambaye umuntu wese atangirana n'ubuzima.

Biratangaje rwose kumenya ko iminsi yacu ya mbere y'ubuzima tugomba kuyibera mu bintu bito cyane, duhindutse, buri wese muri twe akagingo kamwe gato cyane.

Dutangira kubaho mu buryo bw'akagingo gato cyane maze tukarangiza ubuzima dukuze, dushaje kandi twuzuye ibitekerezo.

JYE ni urwibutso. Abasheshe akanguhe benshi ntibanibuka ko bariho, abasaza benshi bibuka ibyahise gusa. Umusaza wese nta kindi arenze ijwi n'igicucu. Umusaza wese ni umuzimu w'ibyahise, urwibutso rwakusanyijwe kandi ni rwo rukomeza mu Miterere ya shushingi y'abazadukomokaho.

Gusama umuntu bitangirana n'ibihe byihuta cyane, ariko binyuze mu nzira zitandukanye z'ubuzima bigenda biba bigufi kandi bigufi.

Abasomyi benshi bakwiriye kwibuka isano y'igihe. Agakoko gato katabaho amasaha make ku mugoroba wo mu mpeshyi, bisa n'aho katabaho, ahubwo kabaho byose umuntu abaho mu myaka mirongo inani, igitangaje ni uko kaba cyihuta, umuntu abaho mu myaka mirongo inani ibyo umubumbe ubaho mu myaka miliyoni.

Iyo Intanga ngabo ihuye n'igi, gutwita biratangira. Akagingo umuntu atangirana n'ubuzima, gakubiyemo uturemangingo mirongo ine n'umunani.

Uturemangingo tugabanyijemo imiterere ya shushingi, ijana cyangwa se arenze ho gato muri yo ni byo bigize neza Uruhu rw'inyama.

Imiterere ya shushingi iragoye cyane kuyiga kuko buri imwe igizwe n'imimolekile mike ihinda umushyitsi mu buryo butumvikana.

Isi itangaje y'imiterere ya shushingi igizwe n'akarere ko hagati y'isi y'uburebure butatu n'isi y'uburebure bwa kane.

Mu miterere ya shushingi dusangamo atome z'umurage. JYE PSYCHOLOGICAL y'abakurambere bacu, iza kwinjira mu gi cyasamye.

Muri iki gihe cya Electro-technique na siyansi ya atome, nta buryo na bumwe bwo gukabya byemejwe ko ikiganza cya electro-magnetique cyasizweho n'umukurambere wahumekeye umwuka we wa nyuma waje kwiyandika mu miterere ya shushingi n'uturemangingo tw'igi ryasamye n'umuntu wazadukomokaho.

Inzira y'ubuzima igizwe n'ibirenge by'inkweto z'ifarasi y'urupfu.

Mu gihe cy'ubuzima, amoko atandukanye y'ingufu atemba mu mubiri w'umuntu; ubwoko bwose bw'ingufu bufite uburyo bwabwo bw'ibikorwa, ubwoko bwose bw'ingufu bwigaragaza mu gihe cyabwo n'isaha yabwo.

Mu mezi abiri tumaze gusama inda dufite imikorere yo kugogora ibyo kurya kandi mu mezi ane tumaze gusama inda imbaraga zitangira gukora zifitanye isano rya bugufi n'imikorere y'ubuhumekero n'imitsi.

Igitaramo cya gihanga cyo kuvuka no gupfa by'ibintu byose kiratangaje.

Abahanga benshi bemeza ko hari isano rya bugufi hagati yo kuvuka k'umuntu n'ivuka ry'isi mu kirere.

Nyuma y'amezi icyenda umwana aravuka, nyuma y'amezi icumi gutembera mu maraso kuratangira hamwe na metebolizimi zayo zose zitangaje n'iterambere ry'inyama zihuje kandi zitunganye.

Iyo Fontanela yo mu ruhanga rw'abana bavutse imaze gufunga nyuma y'imyaka ibiri cyangwa itatu, ni ikimenyetso cy'uko imikorere y'ubwonko-umugongo yarangiye neza.

Abahanga benshi bavuze ko kamere ifite ubushishozi kandi ko ubwo bushishozi butanga imiterere ihoraho kuri byose biriho, byose byabayeho na byose bizabaho.

Abantu benshi baseka ubushishozi kandi bamwe banabwita "UMUSAZI WO MU Nzu".

Hirya no hino y'ijambo UBUSHISHOZI hari urujijo rwinshi kandi hari benshi bitiranya UBUSHISHOZI na FANTASY.

Abahanga bamwe bavuga ko hari ubushishozi bubiri. Ubwa mbere babwita UBUSHISHOZI BWA GIMOTERI naho ubwa kabiri bakabwita UBUSHISHOZI BWATEGANWE: Ubwa mbere bugizwe n'imyanda y'ubwenge naho ubwa kabiri bujyanye n'ikintu cyiyubashye kandi cyiza dufite imbere.

Binyuze mu kwitegereza no kumenya twashoboye kumenya ko hariho kandi ubwoko bwa SUB-UBUSHISHOZI BWA GIMOTERI BUZAHARA BWO HASI Y'UMUTIMA NO KWISHYIRA HEJURU.

Amashusho ateye isoni, filimi ziteye isoni, inkuru zirimo akantu, urwenya ruteye isoni, n'ibindi, bikunze gukoresha SUB-UBUSHISHOZI BWA GIMOTERI mu buryo butaboneka.

Isuzuma ryo hasi ryatugejeje ku mwanzuro ushyize mu gaciro w'uko inzozi ziteye isoni n'imyuka yo mu ijoro iterwa na SUB-UBUSHISHOZI BWA GIMOTERI.

UBUNYANGAMUGAYO BWUZUYE ntibishoboka mu gihe SUB-UBUSHISHOZI BWA GIMOTERI bugihari.

Ni ibisanzwe rwose ko UBUSHISHOZI BUZIRIKIJE butandukanye cyane n'ibyo byitwa UBUSHISHOZI BWA GIMOTERI, BWO KWISHYIRA HEJURU, BWO Hasi Y'UMUTIMA, BUHISHWE.

Icyo ari cyo cyose kigereranya gishobora kugaragara mu buryo BWO KWIYUBAHA no gukomeza, ariko SUB-UBUSHISHOZI bw'ubwoko bwa g moteri, bwo hasi y'umutima, bwihishe, butaboneka bushobora kutugambanira bukora ku buryo butaziguye bwo gushushanya n'amashusho atari meza, akaze, yatembereye.

Niba dushaka UBUNYANGAMUGAYO BWUZUYE, bwuzuye, bwo hasi, dukeneye kureba ntabwo ari UBUSHISHOZI BUZIRIKIJE gusa, ahubwo tunareba UBUSHISHOZI BWA GIMOTERI na SUB-UBUSHISHOZI BUTABONEKA, BWIHUTIRA, BUHISHWE, BWIHISHE.

Ntitugomba kwigera twibagirwa isano rya bugufi iri hagati y'IBITSINA no KWIYUMVIRA.

Binyuze mu gutekereza hasi dukeneye guhindura ubwoko bwose bw'ubushishozi bwa g moteri n'uburyo bwose bwa SUB-UBUSHISHOZI na INFRA-UBUSHISHOZI BWIHUTIRA, mu UBUSHISHOZI BUZIRIKIJE, bugamije.

UBUSHISHOZI BUGAMIJE muri yo ubwayo ni ingenzi cyane mu guhanga, hatariho umuhanga ntiyari gushobora gutekereza terefone, radiyo, indege, n'ibindi.

UBUSHISHOZI BW'UMUGORE utwite ni ingenzi cyane ku iterambere ry'umwana. Byagaragaye ko buri mama wese ashobora guhindura imitekerereze y'umwana akoresheje UBUSHISHOZI bwe.

Ni ngombwa ko umugore utwite areba amashusho meza, ahantu heza, akanumva umuziki wa kera n'amagambo y'ubwumvikane, bityo ashobora gukora ku mitekerereze y'umwana atwite mu buryo buhuje.

Umugore utwite ntakwiriye kunywa inzoga, cyangwa itabi, cyangwa kureba ibintu bibi, bitari byiza kuko ibyo byose byangiza iterambere rihuje ry'umwana.

Umuntu agomba kumenya kubabarira impuhwe zose n'amakosa y'umugore utwite.

Abagabo benshi batari boroshye kandi batabifite uko byari bikwiriye, bararakara bakanatuka umugore utwite. Agahinda kawe, amarorerwa yatewe n'umugabo utuzuye, agira ingaruka ku mwana utwite, ntabwo ari ku mubiri gusa ahubwo no mu mutwe.

Turebye imbaraga z'ubushishozi bwo guhanga, ni ibisanzwe kwemeza ko umugore utwite, atagomba kureba ibintu bibi, bitari byiza, bidahuje, bisekeje, n'ibindi.

Igihe kirageze ko za guverinoma zigomba guhangayikishwa no gukemura ibibazo bikomeye bijyanye n'ububyeyi.

Biratangaje ko mu muryango wiyita uw'abakristo na demokarasi, batamenya kubaha no gusenga ibisobanuro by'idini by'ububyeyi. Biteye ubwoba kubona abagore ibihumbi batwite badafite uburinzi na bumwe, batereranywe n'umugabo n'umuryango, basabiriza agace k'umugati cyangwa akazi kandi akenshi bakora imirimo ikomeye, kugira ngo bashobore kubaho n'umwana batwite.

Ibi bintu bitari byiza by'umuryango muri iki gihe, ubugome no kutagira inshingano by'abayobozi n'abaturage biratwereka neza ko demokarasi itarahari.

Amavuriro afite ibyumba by'ababyeyi ntabwo aracyemura ikibazo, kuko abo bagore bashobora kugera muri ayo mavuriro igihe ibise byo kubyara byegereje gusa.

Hakenewe mu maguru mashya amazu rusange, imijyi myiza y'ubusitani ifite ibyumba n'amacumbi y'abagore batwite bakennye cyane, amavuriro na quindes y'abana babo.

Aya mazu rusange ni amacumbi y'abagore bakennye cyane batwite, yuzuye ubwoko bwose bw'ubworoherane, indabyo, umuziki, ubwumvikane, ubwiza, n'ibindi, byakemura rwose ikibazo gikomeye cy'ububyeyi.

Dukwiriye gusobanukirwa ko umuryango w'abantu ari umuryango mugari kandi ko nta kibazo cy'umuntu utari uwo kuko ikibazo cyose mu buryo bumwe cyangwa ubundi kigira ingaruka mu rugero rwagwo ku bagize umuryango bose. Biratandukanye kurobanura abagore batwite kubera ko ari abakene cyane. Ni icyaha kubagaya, kubasuzugura cyangwa kubafungirana mu buhungiro bw'abakene.

Muri uyu muryango tubamo ntihashobora kubaho abana n'abatereranywe, kuko twese turi abantu kandi dufite uburenganzira bumwe.

Dukeneye guhanga demokarasi nyayo, niba mu by'ukuri tutashaka kumirwa na Communisme.
