Kwibuka ni ukugerageza kubika mu bwenge ibyo twabonye n’ibyo twumvise, ibyo twasomye, ibyo abandi batubwiye, ibyatubayeho, n’ibindi.

Abarezi barashaka ko abanyeshuri babo babika mu mutwe amagambo yabo, interuro zabo, ibyanditse mu bitabo by’ishuri, ibice byose, imirimo iremereye, n’utudomo twose n’utunyuguti, n’ibindi.

Gukora ibizamini bisobanura kwibuka ibyo batubwiye, ibyo twasomye mu buryo bwa kimashini, kuvuga ibyo twibuka, gusubiramo nk’inyoni, ibyo twabitswe mu mutwe byose.

Ni ngombwa ko ab’igihe gishya basobanukirwa ko gusubiramo nk’indirimbo za Radioconsola ibyo byose byafashwe mu mutwe, ntibivuze ko twabyumvise neza. Kwibuka si ugusobanukirwa, ntacyo bimaze kwibuka utabyumva, kwibuka ni ibyahise, ni ikintu cyapfuye, ikintu kitagifite ubuzima.

Birakenewe cyane, ni ngombwa kandi ni iby’ingenzi muri iki gihe ko abanyeshuri bose bo mu mashuri abanza, ayisumbuye na za kaminuza basobanukirwa neza icyo gusobanukirwa byimbitse bisobanura.

Kwibuka, gusubiramo ni ibintu byapfuye, ni ibyahise kandi bibabaje biba intego, inyigisho, igitekerezo, ikintu dushaka kwigana mu buryo bwa kimashini no gukurikira mu buryo butaboneye.

Igikorwa gishingiye ku kwibuka ibyo twasomye, intego twifuza, amahame, imyitwarire batwigishije, ubunararibonye twakusanyije mu mutwe, n’ibindi, ni uguharura, bishingiye ku guhitamo guhangayikishije, ni ubwibone, bishingiye ku guhitamo kw’ibitekerezo kandi biganisha ku makosa n’ububabare.

Ibyo guhuza igikorwa n’ibyo twibuka, ibyo kugerageza guhindura igikorwa kugira ngo gihuze n’ibyo twakusanyije mu mutwe, ni ikintu gikoranye amayeri, kidafite ishingiro, kidatunganye kandi ntibishobora kutugeza ku makosa no ku bubabare.

Ibyo gukora ibizamini, ibyo kwimura umwaka, bikorwa n’umuntu uwo ari we wese ufite ubwenge buke ufite ubwenge n’ubwenge bwinshi.

Gusobanukirwa ibintu byize kandi tuzakoreraho ibizamini, ni ikintu gitandukanye cyane, ntaho bihuriye no kwibuka, ibyo ni ubwenge nyakuri butagomba kwitiranywa n’ubwenge bwo mu mutwe.
