Kwibuka ni ukugerageza kubika mu bwenge ibyo twabonye n'ibyo twumvise, ibyo twasomye, ibyo abandi batubwiye, ibyabaye ku buzima bwacu, n'ibindi n'ibindi.

Abarezi barashaka ko abanyeshuri babo babika mu mutwe amagambo yabo, interuro zabo, ibyanditswe mu bitabo by'ishuri, ibice byose, imirimo iremereye, n'utudomo n'utwugarizo twabyo, n'ibindi.

Gukora ibizamini bisobanura kwibuka ibyo batubwiye, ibyo twasomye mu buryo bwa kimachine, kuvuga ibyo twibuka, gusubiramo nk'inyoni z'umuhondo, inkware cyangwa impyisi, ibyo dufite byose bibitse mu mutwe.

Birakenewe ko ibisekuru bishya byumva ko gusubiramo nk'umuzingo wa Radioconsola ibyafashwe byose mu mutwe, ntibisobanura ko wabyumvise neza. Kwibuka si ugusobanukirwa, ntacyo bimaze kwibuka utabyumva, kwibuka ni ibyahise, ni ikintu cyapfuye, ni ikintu kitagifite ubuzima.

Birakenewe cyane, ni ngombwa kandi birashyushye ko abanyeshuri bose bo mu mashuri abanza, ayisumbuye na za kaminuza bumva neza ibisobanuro byimbitse byo gusobanukirwa neza.

GUSOBANUKIRWA ni ikintu kiba ako kanya, ako kanya, ikintu tubamo cyane, ikintu tubona cyimbitse kandi kidashidikanywaho kiza guhinduka isoko nyakuri y'imbere y'igikorwa cy'ubwenge.

Kwibuka, gusubira mu bintu byahise ni ikintu cyapfuye, ni ibyahise kandi bibabaje bihinduka intego, ihame, igitekerezo, idealisme dushaka kwigana mu buryo bwa kimachine no gukurikira tutabizi.

Mu GUSOBANUKIRWA NYAKURI, mu gusobanukirwa neza, mu gusobanukirwa imbere cyane, habamo gusa igitutu cy'imbere cy'ubwenge, igitutu gihoraho kivutse mu ngingo iri imbere muri twe kandi nibyo gusa.

Gusobanukirwa nyakuri kugaragara nk'igikorwa kizamuka ubwacyo, gisanzwe, cyoroshye, kibohowe ku gikorwa giteye agahinda cyo guhitamo; kitanduye nta guhitamo kw'ubwoko ubwo aribwo bwose. GUSOBANUKIRWA guhindutse ISOKO RY'IBANGA ry'igikorwa ni igitangaza, gitangaje, cyubaka kandi cyubahiriza agaciro by'ingenzi.

Igikorwa gishingiye ku kwibuka ibyo twasomye, intego twifuza kugeraho, ihame, imyitwarire batwigishije, uburambe twagiye tubona mu mutwe, n'ibindi, ni igikorwa cyo kubara, gishingiye ku guhitamo giteye agahinda, ni dualiste, gishingiye ku guhitamo kw'igitekerezo kandi kiyobora gusa ku makosa n'ububabare.

Ibyo guhuza igikorwa no kwibuka, ibyo kugerageza guhindura igikorwa kugirango gihure n'ibyo twibuka byagiye tubika mu mutwe, ni ikintu cy'ubwenge, kidafite ishingiro kidafite ubworoherane kandi kidashobora kutuyobora ku makosa no ku bubabare.

Ibyo gukora ibizamini, ibyo gutsinda umwaka, bikorwa n'umuntu wese ufite ubwenge buke ufite ubwenge n'ubwibuke bihagije.

Gusobanukirwa amasomo yizwe kandi azatugerageza, ni ikintu gitandukanye cyane, ntaho bihuriye no kwibuka, ni ubwenge nyabwo budakwiye kwitiranywa n'ubwenge bwa gihanga.

Abantu bashaka gushingira ibikorwa byabo byose by'ubuzima ku ntego, inyigisho n'ibyo bibuka by'ubwoko bwose byagiye babika mu bubiko bw'ubwibuke, bahora bihereranya kandi aho kwigereranya kubaho, ishyari naryo rirahaba. Abo bantu bagereranya abantu babo, abo mu miryango yabo, abana babo n'abana b'abaturanyi, n'abaturanyi. Bagereranya amazu yabo, ibikoresho byabo, imyenda yabo, ibintu byabo byose, n'ibintu by'umuturanyi cyangwa abaturanyi cyangwa abaturanyi. Bagereranya ibitekerezo byabo, ubwenge bw'abana babo n'ibitekerezo by'abandi bantu, n'ubwenge bw'abandi bantu kandi ishyari riza rihinduka isoko ry'ibanga ry'igikorwa.

Ku bw'ibyago by'isi yose uburyo bwose bw'imibereho bushingiye ku ishyari no ku mutima wo kugura. Buri wese agirira ishyari buri wese. Tugirira ishyari ibitekerezo, ibintu, abantu kandi dushaka kugura amafaranga menshi n'andi menshi, inyigisho nshya, ibitekerezo bishya tubika mu mutwe, ibintu bishya byo gushimisha abantu bagenzi bacu, n'ibindi.

Mu GUSOBANUKIRWA NYAKURI, kwemewe, nyakuri, habamo urukundo nyakuri kandi ntirube kuvuga gusa ibyo twibuka.

Ibyo twibuka, ibyo twizeye mu mutwe, bidatinze byibagirana kuko kwibuka ari ukudashikamye. Abanyeshuri bashyira mu bubiko bw'ubwibuke, intego, inyigisho, imyandiko yuzuye itagira akamaro mu buzima busanzwe kuko amaherezo izimira mu mutwe ntihasige ikimenyetso na kimwe.

Abantu babeshwaho no gusoma no gusoma mu buryo bwa kimachine gusa, abantu bishimira kubika inyigisho mu bubiko bw'ubwibuke bangiza ubwenge, barabwangiza bikabije.

Ntabwo twemeza kwiga byimbitse kandi tuzi gushingiye ku gusobanukirwa neza. Turamagana gusa uburyo bwa kera bwa pédagogie y'igihe cyashize. Turamagana uburyo bwose bwo kwiga bwa kimachine, kwibuka byose, n'ibindi. Kwibuka biruta aho gusobanukirwa nyakuri kubaho.

Dukeneye kwiga, hakenewe ibitabo by'ingirakamaro, hakenewe abarimu n'abarezi bo mu mashuri, amakoleji, za kaminuza. Hakenewe GURU, abayobozi b'umwuka, mahatmas, n'ibindi ariko ni ngombwa gusobanukirwa mu buryo bwuzuye inyigisho kandi ntibizibike gusa mu bubiko bw'ubwibuke budashikamye.

Ntituzigera tubohoka byukuri igihe cyose tuzaba dufite umuco mubi wo kwigereranya n'ibyo twibuka twagiye tubika mu mutwe, n'intego, n'ibyo twifuza kuba kandi tutari byo, n'ibindi n'ibindi.

Igihe tuzasobanukirwa byukuri inyigisho twahawe, ntituzazikeneye kwibuka mu mutwe, cyangwa kuzihindura intego.

Aho kwigereranya ibyo turi byo hano n'ubu n'ibyo dushaka kuba nyuma, aho kwigereranya ubuzima bwacu busanzwe n'intego cyangwa icyitegererezo dushaka guhuza, ntihashobora kubaho urukundo nyakuri.

Kwigereranya kose ni ikizira, kwigereranya kose kuzana ubwoba, ishyari, ubwibone, n'ibindi. Gutinya kutagera ku byo dushaka, ishyari ry'iterambere ry'abandi, ubwibone kuko twiyumva ko turuta abandi. Icy'ingenzi mu buzima busanzwe tubamo, twaba turi babi, abanyeshyari, abanyamwaga, abanyamururumba, n'ibindi, ni ukutiyita abera, guhera kuri zeru rwose, no kwisobanukirwa ubwacu cyane, uko turi hano n'ubu kandi ntabwo ari uko dushaka kuba cyangwa uko twiyita.

Ntibishoboka gusesa JYE, NJYE UBWANJYE, niba tutize kubyitegereza, kubona kugirango dusobanukirwe ibyo turi byo byukuri hano n'ubu mu buryo bufatika kandi bwuzuye.

Niba dushaka byukuri gusobanukirwa, tugomba kumva abarimu bacu, abarezi, abaganga, abapadiri, abigisha, abayobozi b'umwuka, n'ibindi n'ibindi.

Abahungu n'abakobwa b'igihe gishya batakaje icyubahiro, icyubahiro ku babyeyi bacu, abarimu, abarezi, abayobozi b'umwuka, abaganga, mahatmas, n'ibindi.

Ntibishoboka gusobanukirwa inyigisho iyo tutazi kubaha no kubaha ababyeyi bacu, abarimu, abigisha cyangwa abayobozi b'umwuka.

Kwibuka gusa kwa kimachine ibyo twize mu mutwe gusa tutagombye gusobanukirwa neza, guhindura ubwenge n'umutima no kubyara ishyari, ubwoba, ubwibone, n'ibindi.

Iyo tuzi byukuri kumva mu buryo bw'ubwenge kandi bwimbitse, imbere muri twe havuka imbaraga itangaje, gusobanukirwa gutangaje, bisanzwe, byoroshye, bibohowe ku gikorwa cyose cya kimachine, bibohowe ku gutekereza kose, bibohowe ku kwibuka kose.

Niba ubwonko bw'umunyeshuri buvanyweho imbaraga nyinshi zo kwibuka agomba gukora, bizashoboka rwose kwigisha imiterere ya noyau n'urutonde rw'ibintu ku banyeshuri bo mu mashuri yisumbuye no gutuma umunyeshuri w'icyiciro cya kabiri asobanukirwa relativité na Quanta.

Nkuko twaganiriye n'abarimu bamwe n'abarezi bo mu mashuri yisumbuye, turasobanukirwa ko batinya cyane fanatisme pédagogie ya kera y'igihe cyashize. Barashaka ko abanyeshuri biga ibintu byose mu mutwe nubwo batabisobanukirwa.

Rimwe na rimwe baremera ko ari byiza gusobanukirwa kuruta kwibuka ariko noneho bagashimangira ko formules za physique, chimie, imibare, n'ibindi zigomba kubikwa mu mutwe.

Biragaragara ko igitekerezo kivuzwe ari ikinyoma kuko iyo formule ya physique, chimie, imibare, n'ibindi, isobanukirwa neza si ku rwego rw'ubwenge gusa, ahubwo no ku zindi nzego z'ubwenge nk'ubwenge butazwi, ubwenge buke, ubwenge buke n'ibindi n'ibindi. Ntagomba kubikwa mu mutwe, iza kuba igice cya psyché yacu kandi ishobora kugaragara nk'ubumenyi bw'umubiri bwihuse iyo ibintu bibaho mu buzima bibisaba.

Ubu bumenyi BWUZUYE butuzanira uburyo bwa OMNICIENCE, uburyo bwo kugaragaza ubwenge bugamije.

Gusobanukirwa neza kandi ku nzego zose z'ubwenge bishoboka gusa binyuze mu kwitekerezaho cyane.
