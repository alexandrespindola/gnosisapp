Mu bikorwa, twashoboye kumenya ko haba AMASHURI Y’IBINTU gusa ndetse n’AMASHURI Y’UMWUGA ari mu BIBAZO byo kwemera INYIGISHO za EVOLUTION.

Ibitecyerezo bigezweho ku nkomoko y’umuntu no kuri EVOLUTION ye yo ha mbere, mu by’ukuri ni UBUSHOBORA BUKEYE, ntibyihanganira ikibazo gikomeye.

Nubwo hari theories zose za DARWIN zemewe nk’ingingo yo KWIZERA impumyi na CARLOS MARX n’IMYUMVIRE YE Y’IBINTU IKORESHEJE, nta kintu abahanga ba none bazi ku nkomoko y’umuntu, nta kintu bazi, nta kintu bigeze babona mu buryo butaziguye kandi ntibafite ibimenyetso bifatika, nyakuri, bijyanye na EVOLUTION Y’ABANTU.

Ku rundi ruhande, niba dufata inyokomuntu y’amateka, ni ukuvuga imyaka ibihumbi makumyabiri cyangwa mirongo itatu ishize mbere ya Yesu Kristo, tubona ibimenyetso nyakuri, ibimenyetso bidashidikanywaho by’ubwoko bw’umuntu burenze, butumvikana ku bantu bo muri iki gihe, kandi ukaba uhari bushobora kwerekwa n’ubuhamya butabarika, Inyandiko za kera, Pyramide za kera, ibikoresho by’amabuye byo mu mahanga, impapuro z’imizingo zidasanzwe n’ibindi byibutso bya kera.

Ku bijyanye n’UMUNTU WO MU BIHE BYA KARANDE, kuri ibyo biremwa bidasanzwe kandi bidasobanutse byo mu isura bisa n’INYAMASWA Z’UBUHANGA ariko zitandukanye cyane, zitandukanye cyane, zidasobanutse kandi amagufwa yazo azwi cyane ahishwa cyane rimwe na rimwe mu masoko ya kera yo mu gihe cya Glacial cyangwa Preglaciar, nta kintu abahanga b’iki gihe bazi mu buryo butaziguye kandi mu buryo butaziguye.

SIYANSI YA GNOSTIC yigisha ko INYAMASWA YUMVISE nk’uko tubizi, atari UMUGEZI UTUNGANIRA, ntaraba UMUNTU mu buryo bwuzuye bw’ijambo; kamere iramukuza kugeza igihe runaka hanyuma ikamureka mu mudendezo wuzuye wo gukomeza iterambere rye cyangwa akatakaza amahirwe ye yose akangirika.

AMATEGEKO ya EVOLUTION na INVOLUTION ni yo shingiro rya mécanique ya kamere yose kandi nta ho bihuriye na AUTOREALIZACION INTIMA y’UMUGEZI.

Mu NYAMASWA ZUMVISE harimo amahirwe akomeye ashobora gutezwa imbere cyangwa agatakarizwa agaciro, ntabwo ari itegeko ko atera imbere. Mechanique EVOLUTIVE ntishobora kuyiteza imbere.

Iterambere ry’ayo mahirwe atagaragara, rishoboka gusa mu bihe bisobanutse neza kandi ibi bisaba imbaraga zidasanzwe z’umuntu ku giti cye no gufashwa neza n’ABAGISHA bamaze gukora uwo murimo mu gihe cyashize.

Ushaka guteza imbere amahirwe ye yose atagaragara kugira ngo abe umuntu, agomba kwinjira mu nzira ya REVOLUTION Y’UBUZIMA.

INYAMASWA ZUMVISE ni INYAMASWA, IMPEWU; kuri iyo mbuto hashobora kuvuka IGITI CY’UBUZIMA, UMUNTU W’UKURI, wa MUNTU washakishwaga na DIOGENES afite itara ryaka mu mihanda ya ATHENA no ku Manywa y’ihangu kandi bibabaje ntiyashoboye kubona.

SI ITEGEKO ko iyi nyamuneka, ko iyi mbuto idasanzwe ishobora gutera imbere, ibisanzwe, kamere ni uko ibura.

UMUNTU W’UKURI atandukanye cyane n’INYAMASWA ZUMVISE, nk’uko IMIRABYO iri ku gicu.

Niba imbuto idapfuye imbuto ntizimera, birakenewe, byihutirwa ko EGO ipfa, JE, MI ubwayo, paca kugira ngo UMUNTU avuke.

Abagisha n’ABAGISHA bo mu Mashuri, Amakoleji na za Kaminuza, bagomba kwigisha abanyeshuri babo INZIRA y’IMYITWARIRE YA REVOLUTION, ni bwo buryo bwonyine bushoboka bwo kugera ku rupfu rwa EGO.

Dushimangira ko REVOLUTION Y’UBUZIMA atari iyoroheye muri iyi si gusa, ahubwo ko irushaho gukendera.

REVOLUTION Y’UBUZIMA ifite ibintu bitatu bisobanutse neza: Icya mbere, Gupfa; Icya kabiri, Kuvuka; Icya gatatu, Kwitangira inyokomuntu. Urukurikirane rw’ibintu ntiruhindura umusaruro.

GUPFA ni ikibazo cy’IMYITWARIRE YA REVOLUTION n’IBYA KUVANWAHO n’UMUNTU WA PSYCHOLOGIQUE.

KUVUKA ni ikibazo cyo GUHINDURA IMIBONANO MPUZABITSINA, iki kibazo kijyanye na SEXOLOGIE TRANSCENDENTAL, ushaka kwiga iyi ngingo, agomba kutwandikira akamenya ibitabo byacu bya Gnostique.

KWITANGIRA inyokomuntu ni URUKUNDO RUSANZWE RUMENYETSE.

Niba tudashaka REVOLUTION Y’UBUZIMA, niba tutagira IMYITOZO IKOMEYE cyane yo guteza imbere ayo mahirwe atagaragara azadushyira AUTO-REALI2ACION INTIMA, biragaragara ko ayo mahirwe atazigera atera imbere.

Abantu bake cyane ni bo AUTO-BAZAHARA, abakizwa kandi nta karengane na gato kabibamo, kuki INYAMASWA YO MU BWENGE YOROSHE yagira ibyo itifuza?

Hakenewe impinduka zikaze rwose kandi zidasubirwaho ariko si abantu bose bashaka iyo mpinduka, ntibayifuza, ntibabizi kandi barabibabwira ntibabyumva, ntibabishishikajwe. Kuki bagomba guhabwa ku gahato ibyo badashaka?

Ukuri ni uko mbere yo kugira umuntu UBUSHAKE BUSHYA cyangwa UBUSHAKE BUSHYA, ibyo atazi cyangwa atunze, agomba kugira ubushobozi n’imbaraga yizera ko afite mu buryo butari bwo, ariko mu by’ukuri atabifite.
