Kwicya ubuzima, biragaragara kandi nta gushidikanya ko ari igikorwa cyangiza cyane kandi cyuzuye ruswa kurusha ibindi byose bizwi ku isi.

Ubwicanyi bubi kurusha ubundi ni uguhitana ubuzima bw'abantu bagenzi bacu.

Umuhigi ukoresha imbunda ye yica ibiremwa by'inyanja bitagira ingano ni ikintu giteye ubwoba, ariko umuntu wica abantu bagenzi be ni mubi cyane inshuro igihumbi, ni ikizira cyane inshuro igihumbi.

Ntitwicwa gusa n'imbunda, imbunda nto, imbunda nini, imbunda cyangwa ibisasu bya kirimbuzi, dushobora no kwicwa n'ijisho rikomeretsa umutima, ijisho ridahwitse, ijisho ryuzuye agasuzuguro, ijisho ryuzuye urwango; cyangwa dushobora kwicwa n'igikorwa kibi, n'igikorwa cy'icuraburindi, cyangwa n'igitutsi, cyangwa ijambo ribabaza.

Isi yuzuye abicana, abicana babi bishe ababyeyi babo, cyangwa amaso yabo, cyangwa amagambo yabo, cyangwa ibikorwa byabo by'ubugome.

Isi yuzuye abagabo bishe abagore babo batabizi, n'abagore bishe abagabo babo batabizi.

Ikibabaje kurushaho muri iyi si y'inkoramutima tubamo, umuntu yica ibyo akunda cyane.

Umuntu ntatungwa n'umutsima gusa, ahubwo atungwa n'ibintu bitandukanye bya psychologiya.

Hari abagabo benshi bari gushobora kubaho igihe kirekire iyo abagore babo bari kubyemera.

Hari abagore benshi bari gushobora kubaho igihe kirekire iyo abagabo babo bari kubyemera.

Hari ababyeyi benshi bari gushobora kubaho igihe kirekire iyo abana babo bari kubyemera.

Indwara yohereza umuntu wacu dukunda ku mva ifite nk'impamvu nyamukuru, amagambo yica, amaso akomeretsa, ibikorwa bibi, n'ibindi.

Iyi sosiyete ishaje kandi yasamaye yuzuye abicanyi batabizi bibwira ko ari abere.

Amagereza yuzuye abicanyi ariko ubwoko bubi kurusha ubundi bw'abagizi ba nabi bibwira ko ari abere kandi bagendera ku buntu.

Nta bwoko na bumwe bw'ubwicanyi bushobora kugira impamvu iyo ari yo yose. Kwica undi ntibicyemura ikibazo icyo ari cyo cyose mu buzima.

Intambara ntizigeze zicyemura ikibazo icyo ari cyo cyose. Gutera ibisasu imigi itagira ingabo no kwica abantu miliyoni ntabwo bikemura ikintu icyo ari cyo cyose.

Intambara ni ikintu kigoye cyane, kidahwitse, kibi, kigayitse. Imashini z'abantu miliyoni nyinshi ziraryamye, zidashyira mu gaciro, zipfuye ubwenge, ziriyemeza kujya mu ntambara zigamije kurimbura izindi mashini z'abantu miliyoni nyinshi zidashyira mu gaciro.

Akenshi ibiza bya planetary mu isanzure birahagije, cyangwa imyanya mibi y'inyenyeri mu kirere, kugira ngo abantu miliyoni nyinshi biyemeze kujya mu ntambara.

Imashini z'abantu ntizishobora kugira icyo zimenya, zigenda mu buryo bwangiza iyo ubwoko bwihariye bw'umuraba w'ikirere zikomeretsa rwihishwa.

Iyo abantu bazanzamuka, iyo uhereye ku ntebe z'ishuri abanyeshuri bigishwa mu buryo bw'ubwenge babaganisha ku gusobanukirwa neza icyo ubugome n'intambara ari byo, inkoko yabo yabyiniraga, nta muntu wajya mu ntambara kandi imiraba yangiza y'isanzure yari gukoreshwa mu buryo butandukanye.

Intambara inuka nk'ubwicanamuntu, ubuzima bw'ubuvumo, ubugome bw'ubwoko bubi kurusha ubundi, umuheto, umwambi, icumu, ibirori by'amaraso, ntibihuje uburezi.

Abagabo bose mu ntambara ni ibigwari, batinya kandi intwari zikurikiranwa n'imidari ni zo zigwari cyane, zitinya cyane.

Uwishyira mu byago bisa nk'aho ari intwari ariko ni ikigwari kuko yatinye ubuzima.

Intwari ahishuye ni umwiyahuzi mu kanya gato k'ubwoba bukomeye yakoze ubusazi bw'umwiyahuzi.

Ubusazi bw'umwiyahuzi bwitiranywa byoroshye n'ubutwari bw'intwari.

Iyo twitegereje neza imyifatire y'umusirikare mu ntambara, imyifatire ye, amaso ye, amagambo ye, intambwe ze mu rugamba, dushobora kwerekana ubwoba bwe bwose.

Abarimu b'amashuri, amakolere, za kaminuza, bagomba kwigisha abanyeshuri babo ukuri ku ntambara. Bagomba gutuma abanyeshuri babo bagira uburambe bwiza kuri iryo kuri.

Iyo abantu bamenya neza icyo kuri gukomeye ku ntambara ari cyo, iyo abalimu babimenya kwigisha abigishwa babo mu buryo bw'ubwenge, nta muturage wakwemererwa kujyanwa ku itongo.

Uburezi bw'ibanze bugomba gutangwa ako kanya mu mashuri yose, amakolere na za kaminuza, kuko ariho ku ntebe z'ishuri, aho tugomba gukorera AMAHORO.

Birihutirwa ko ibisekuru bishya bimenya neza icyo ubugome n'icyo intambara ari byo.

Mu mashuri, amakolere, za kaminuza, ubugome n'intambara bigomba gusobanukirwa neza mu buryo bwose.

Ibihe bishya bigomba gusobanukirwa ko abasaza bafite ibitekerezo byabo bishaje kandi bidahwitse, bahora batamba abasore kandi bakabajyana nk'inka ku itongo.

Abasore ntibagomba kwemerwa n'ubutumwa bw'intambara, cyangwa n'impamvu z'abasaza, kuko impamvu imwe irwanya indi mpamvu n'igitekerezo kimwe kirwanya ikindi, ariko ibitekerezo cyangwa ibitekerezo ntabwo ari Ukuri ku ntambara.

Abasaza bafite impamvu ibihumbi byinshi zo kwemeza intambara no kujyana abasore ku itongo.

Icy'ingenzi si ibitekerezo ku ntambara ahubwo ni ukugira uburambe bw'ukuri icyo intambara ari cyo.

Ntitwamagana impamvu cyangwa isesengura, turashaka kuvuga gusa ko tugomba kubanza kugira uburambe bw'ukuri ku ntambara hanyuma dushobore kwiha uburenganzira bwo gutekereza no gusesengura.

Ntibishoboka kugira uburambe bw'ukuri bwo KUTICA, niba dukuyemo kuzirikana umutima wimbitse.

Kuzirikana gukomeye gusa gushobora kutugeza ku burambe bw'Ukuri ku ntambara.

Abalimu ntibagomba guha abanyeshuri babo amakuru y'ubwenge gusa. Abalimu bagomba kwigisha abanyeshuri babo gucunga ubwenge, kugira uburambe bw'UKURI.

Iri ryari rishaje kandi ryasamaye ntiritekereza gusa kwica. Iki kintu cyo kwica no kwica, ni cyo cyihariye ubwoko bw'abantu basamaye.

Binyuze muri televiziyo na sinema, abagizi ba nabi bakwirakwiza ibitekerezo byabo by'ubugizi bwa nabi.

Abana b'ibyiciro bishya bakira buri munsi binyuze kuri ecran ya televiziyo n'imigani y'abana na sinema, ikinyamakuru n'ibindi, umuti mwiza w'uburozi bw'ubwicanyi, amasasu, ibyaha biteye ubwoba, n'ibindi.

Ntushobora gushyira televiziyo mu bikorwa utabonye amagambo yuzuye urwango, amasasu, ubugome.

Nta kintu na kimwe guverinoma z'isi zikora mu kurwanya ikwirakwizwa ry'icyaha.

Ubwenge bw'abana n'urubyiruko burimo kuyoborwa n'abagizi ba nabi, mu nzira y'icyaha.

Igitekerezo cyo kwica cyakwirakwijwe cyane, cyakwirakwijwe cyane binyuze muri filime, inkuru, n'ibindi byabaye byoroshye kuri buri wese.

Inyeshyamba z'urubyiruko zarezwe gukora icyaha kandi zica kugira ngo zinezezwe no kwica, zishimishwa no kubona abandi bapfa. Uko ni ko babyigiye kuri televiziyo yo mu rugo, muri sinema, mu nkuru, mu binyamakuru.

Icyaha kiraganza hose kandi nta kintu na kimwe guverinoma zikora kugira ngo zikemure imyumvire yo kwica kuva ku mizi yayo.

Ni umwanya w'Abalimu b'amashuri, amakolere na za kaminuza, gutera hejuru mu ijuru no kuzenguruka ijuru n'isi kugira ngo bakemure iki cyorezo cyo mu mutwe.

Birihutirwa ko Abalimu b'amashuri, amakolere na za kaminuza, batanga umuburo kandi basaba guverinoma zose z'isi guhagarika sinema, televiziyo, n'ibindi.

Icyaha kigenda cyikuba incuro nyinshi kubera ibyo bikorwa byose by'amaraso kandi intambwe turimo izagera umunsi nta muntu uzabasha kugendera mu mihanda ku buntu adatinya kwicwa.

Radio, Sinema, Televiziyo, Ibinyamakuru by'amaraso, byatanze ikwirakwizwa ry'icyaha cyo kwica, byabigize bishimisha ubwenge budakomeye kandi bwangiritse, ku buryo nta muntu ukigira isoni zo gutera isasu cyangwa gucumita umuntu undi.

Kubera gukwirakwiza icyaha cyo kwica cyane, ubwenge budakomeye bumenyereye cyane icyaha kandi ubu butanga umwanya wo kwica kugira ngo bigane ibyo babonye muri sinema cyangwa kuri televiziyo.

Abalimu ari bo barezi b'abaturage bagomba mu gusohoza inshingano zabo guharanira ibihe bishya basaba Guverinoma z'isi guhagarika ibikorwa by'amaraso, muri make, guhagarika ubwoko bwose bwa filime zerekeye ubwicanyi, abajura, n'ibindi.

Urugero rw'Abalimu rugomba no kwaguka kugeza ku gutera ingabo mu bitugu no gukirana.

Ubwoko bw'uwatera ingabo mu bitugu ni ubwoko bw'ikigwari cyane kandi cy'abagizi ba nabi. Uwatera ingabo mu bitugu ashaka ibyiza byose kuri we kandi akica kugira ngo ashimishe abantu.

Ubwoko bw'uwakina umukino wo gukirana ni ubwa monster w'ubwicanyi, mu buryo bwe bubabaza kandi bwica kugira ngo ashimishe abantu.

Ubu bwoko bw'ibikorwa by'amaraso ni ubugome mu ijana ku ijana kandi bushishikariza ubwenge bubayobora mu nzira y'icyaha. Niba dushaka by'ukuri guharanira Amahoro y'Isi, tugomba gutangiza ibikorwa by'ubutabazi ku bijyanye n'ibikorwa by'amaraso.

Mugihe ibintu byangiza byahariwe ubwenge bw'abantu, hazabaho intambara idashoboka.

Mu mutwe w'umuntu hari ibintu biteza intambara, ibyo bintu ni urwango. ihohoterwa mu buryo bwose, ubwikunde, umujinya, ubwoba, imyumvire mibi, ibitekerezo by'intambara bikwirakwizwa na televiziyo, radio, sinema, n'ibindi.

Ubutumwa bw'AMAHORO, ibihembo bya NOBEL Y'AMAHORO biragaragara ko bidafite ishingiro mugihe hakiri imyumvire myiza mu muntu itera intambara.

Kuri ubu abicanyi benshi bafite igihembo cya NOBEL Y'AMAHORO.
