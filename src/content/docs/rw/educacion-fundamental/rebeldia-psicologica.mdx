Abashinzwe ingendo mu bihugu byose by’isi bagamije kwiga birambuye ubwoko bwose bw’abantu, babashije kwiyumvira ubwabo ko kamere y’iki NYAMASWA IKOMEZA ibeshya yitwa umuntu, ihora ari imwe, haba i Burayi ya kera cyangwa muri Afurika irambiwe ubucakara bwinshi, mu butaka butagatifu bwa Vedas cyangwa mu Buhinde bw’iburengerazuba, muri Otirishiya cyangwa mu Bushinwa.

Iki gikorwa gifatika, iyi kuri ikomeye itangaza umuntu wese wiga, irashobora kwemezwa by’umwihariko niba umugenzi asuye Amashuri, Amakoleji na za Kaminuza.

Twageze mu gihe cyo gukora ibintu byinshi. Ubu byose bikorerwa kuri tapi itondekanyije kandi ku rugero runini. Uruhererekane rw'Indege, Imodoka, Ibicuruzwa by'agaciro, n'ibindi, n'ibindi, n'ibindi.

N’ubwo byagaragara nk’ibisekeje gato, ni ukuri ko Amashuri y’inganda, za Kaminuza, n’ibindi, na byo byabaye inganda z’ubwenge zikora ibintu byinshi.

Muri ibi bihe byo gukora ibintu byinshi intego imwe rukumbi mu buzima ni ukubona umutekano w'ubukungu. Abantu batinya byose kandi bagashaka umutekano.

Gutekereza wigenga muri ibi bihe byo gukora ibintu byinshi, bigoye cyane kuko ubwoko bwa none bw'Uburezi bushingiye ku nyungu gusa.

"Imbaraga Nshya" ibaho yishimye cyane muri ubu bucucu bw'ubwenge. Niba hari umuntu ushaka kuba atandukanye, atandukanye n'abandi, buri wese aramusebya, buri wese aramunenga, akorerwa ubusa, akenywa akazi, n'ibindi.

Icyifuzo cyo kubona amafaranga yo kubaho no kwishimisha, ubwangu bwo kugera ku ntsinzi mu buzima, gushaka umutekano w'ubukungu, icyifuzo cyo kugura ibintu byinshi kugira ngo wirate abandi, n'ibindi, bimubuza gutekereza kwiza, kwa kamere kandi kutikoresha.

Byaragaragaye rwose ko ubwoba buhumbya ubwenge kandi bugakaza umutima.

Muri ibi bihe by'ubwoba bwinshi no gushaka umutekano, abantu biyubakira mu buvumo bwabo, mu byobo byabo, mu nguni zabo, ahantu bizera ko hashobora kugira umutekano mwinshi, ibibazo bike kandi ntibashaka kuhava, batinya ubuzima, batinya ibintu bishya, ibintu bishya, n'ibindi, n'ibindi, n'ibindi.

Ubu burezi bwose bwa none bushingiye ku bwoba no gushaka umutekano, abantu baratangaye, batinya n'igicucu cyabo bwite.

Abantu batinya byose, batinya kuva ku mategeko ya kera yashyizweho, kuba batandukanye n'abandi bantu, gutekereza mu buryo buhindura ibintu, guca ukubiri n'imyitwarire yose y'Umuryango uriho.

Ku bw'amahirwe, ku isi hariho bake b'inyangamugayo kandi bumva, bashaka by'ukuri gusesengura byimbitse ibibazo byose by'ubwenge, ariko mu bwinshi bwacu nta n'umwuka wo kutanyurwa n'ubwigomeke uhari.

Hariho ubwoko bubiri bw'UBWIGOMEKE bwamaze gushyirwa mu byiciro. Iya mbere: Ubwigomeke bwo mu mutwe bukaze. Iya kabiri: Ubwigomeke bwo mu mutwe bwimbitse bw'UBWENGE.

Ubwo bwoko bwa mbere bw'Ubwigomeke ni ukugarukira inyuma kandi bugatinda. Ubwoko bwa kabiri bw'Ubwigomeke ni IHINDURO.

Mu bwoko bwa mbere bw'Ubwigomeke bwo mu mutwe dusanga UMUHINDUZI uduga imyenda ishaje akanasana inkuta z'inyubako zishaje kugira ngo zitagwa, ubwoko busubira inyuma, Impinduramatwara y'amaraso n'inzoga, umuyobozi w'ibirindiro n'ihirikwa ry'ubutegetsi, umugabo ufite imbunda ku bitugu, Umudikita wishimira kujyana ku rukuta abantu bose batemera ibyifuzo bye, inyigisho ze.

Mu bwoko bwa kabiri bw'Ubwigomeke bwo mu mutwe dusanga BUDDHA, YESU, HERMES, uhindura, UMWIGOMEKE W'UBWENGE, UMUHANGA, intwari ZIKOMEYE z'IHINDURO RY'UBUMENYI, n'ibindi, n'ibindi, n'ibindi.

Abo biga gusa bagamije kugera ku myanya ikomeye muri colmena bureaucrática, kuzamuka, kuzamuka hejuru y'urwego, kwigaragaza, n'ibindi, ntibafite ubujyakuzimu nyakuri, ni Ibicucu ku buryo busanzwe, ibintu byo hejuru, ubusa, ijana ku ijana abagome.

Byaragaragaye kugeza magingo aya ko iyo mu muntu nta HUZABUZIMA nyakuri ihari mu bitekerezo n'ibyiyumvo, n'ubwo twaba twarahawe uburezi bukomeye, ubuzima buba budatunganye, bunyuranye, butandukanye kandi butotezwa n'ubwoba butabarika bw'ubwoko bwose.

Utitaye ku cyo tutinya kwibeshya, dushobora kwemeza duhamye ko nta burezi BUZIRANGE, ubuzima buteza akaga, ntacyo bumaze kandi bugatera ibibazo.

INyamaswa IKOMEZA ifite EGO MPEMU igizwe n’IBINDI BYITWA ko byunga ubumwe bikomezwa n’UBUREZI BUTARI BWO.

NJYE WINSHI buri wese muri twe atwaye imbere, ni yo ntandaro y'ingenzi y'ibibazo byacu byose n'inyuraburyo.

UBUREZI BW'INGENZI bugomba kwigisha ibisekuru bishya Imyigishirizo yacu yo mu Mutwe yo GUKURAHO NJYE.

Gusa gukuraho ibintu byinshi bigize Ego (NJYE) dushobora gushinga muri twe ikigo gihoraho cy'ubwenge bwite, hanyuma tukaba Tuzima.

Mugihe imbere muri buri umwe muri twe NJYE WINSHI ahari, ntituzicira gusa ubuzima ahubwo tuzanicira abandi.

Byamaze iki ko twiga amategeko tukaba abanyamategeko, niba dukomeza impaka? Byamaze iki gukusanya mu bwenge bwacu ubumenyi bwinshi, niba dukomeza kwitiranya? Bimaze iki ubuhanga bwa tekinike n'inganda niba tubukoresha mu kurimbura abandi bantu?

Ntacyo bimaze kwiyungura ubumenyi, kwitabira amasomo, kwiga, niba mu nzira y'ubuzima bwa buri munsi turimburana nabi.

Intego y'uburezi ntigomba kuba gusa gutanga buri mwaka abashakisha akazi bashya, ubwoko bushya bw'abagome, abagabo bashya batamenya no kubaha Idini ry'umuturanyi, n'ibindi.

Intego nyayo y'UBUREZI BW'INGENZI igomba kuba gushyiraho abagabo n'abagore nyabo BUZIMA kandi bityo bazi ubwenge.

Ku bw'amahirwe, Abigisha n'Abigishakazi b'Amashuri, Amakoleji na za Kaminuza, bose baratekereza, keretse kubyutsa UBWENGE BWOSE BW'ABO BIGA.

Uwo ari we wese ashobora kwifuza no kubona impamyabumenyi, imidari, impamyabushobozi ndetse no kuba umuhanga cyane mu butaka bwa gimekanike bw'ubuzima, ariko ibi ntibisobanura kuba UMUHANGA.

UBWENGE ntibushobora kuba gusa imikorere ya gimekanike, UBWENGE ntibushobora kuba ingaruka z'amakuru y'ibitabo gusa, UBWENGE si ubushobozi bwo kwitwara byikora ku magambo aciye umuhondo ku kintu icyo ari cyo cyose. UBWENGE si ugukoresha amagambo gusa y'ubwenge. UBWENGE ni ubushobozi bwo kwakira ESENSI, UKURI, ibyo ARI BYO by'ukuri.

UBUREZI BW'INGENZI ni siyansi itwemerera kubyutsa ubu bushobozi muri twe ubwacu no mu bandi.

UBUREZI BW'INGENZI bufasha buri MUNYABUBAHA kuvumbura INDANGACIRO nyakuri ziva ku bushakashatsi bwimbitse no ku GUSOBANUKIRWA BYUZUYE.

Iyo tutagira UBWIMENYI, noneho UMWIYEREKANO uhinduka KWEMEZA UBWINE BUBI BWANGIZA.

UBUREZI BW'INGENZI buhangayikishijwe gusa no kubyutsa muri buri muntu UBUSHOBOZI bwo kwiyumvisha mu butaka bwose bw'ubwenge kandi si ukwiha kwishimisha kwo KWIVUGA ko NJYE WINSHI ari bibi.
