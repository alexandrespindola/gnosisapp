Igihe kirageze cyo kureka burundu isoni z’uburyarya n’imyumvire itari yo ku bijyanye n’ibibazo by’imibonano mpuzabitsina.

Ni ngombwa gusobanukirwa neza kandi mu buryo butaziguye ikibazo cy’imibonano mpuzabitsina ku bangavu b’ibitsina byombi.

Ku myaka cumi n’ine, imbaraga z’imibonano mpuzabitsina zigaragara mu mubiri w’umwangavu, zikayoboka cyane imitsi y’umubiri.

Ubu bwoko bwihariye bw’imbaraga buhindura umubiri w’umuntu, bugahindura ijwi ku bahungu, bukaganisha ku mikorere y’intanganga ngore ku bakobwa.

Umubiri w’umuntu ni uruganda nyarwo ruhindura ibintu bibi mo ibintu byiza by’ingenzi.

Ibiryo tujyana mu nda binyura mu mpinduka nyinshi n’isukura kugeza igihe bigaragarira burundu muri icyo kintu kimeze nk’igice gikomeye, igice cy’amazi cyavuzwe na Paracelse akoresheje ijambo Ens.-Seminis (Ubuzima bw’Intanga)

Iryo reme ry’amazi, rikunze guhinduka, ryoroheye gukorwa, iryo ntanga, ririmo ubwaryo, mu buryo bwo gushobora mikorobe yose y’ubuzima.

Ubuhanga bwa Gnostic bwemera mu ntanga Chaos aho ubuzima buturuka ku buryo bukomeye.

Abahanga ba kera mu bijyanye n’ubuhanga nka Paracelse, Sendivogius, Nicolás Flamel, Raymundo Lulio, n’abandi, biganye cyane Ens-Seminis cyangwa mercury y’ubuhanga bw’ibanga.

Iyi VITRIOL, ni umuti nyawo ukorwa mu buryo bw’ubuhanga, n’ubwenge kamere imbere y’imitsi y’intanga.

Muri iyi mercury y’ubwenge bwa kera, muri iyi ntanga, haboneka koko ibishoboka byose by’ubuzima.

Birababaje kuba urubyiruko rwinshi kubera kubura ubuyobozi nyabwo bwa psychologiya rwiyeguriye ingeso yo kwikinisha cyangwa rukayoba ku buryo bubabaje mu nzira yo munsi y’imibonano mpuzabitsina y’ubutinganyi.

Abana n’urubyiruko bahabwa amakuru y’ubwenge ku ngingo nyinshi kandi bagashyirwa mu nzira yo gukora siporo ikabije yica ubuzima mu buryo bubabaje, ariko ikibabaje ni uko imbaraga z’imibonano mpuzabitsina zigaragara ubwo ingimbi zitangira, ababyeyi bombi n’abarimu b’ishuri, bashingiye ku bwitange bw’uburyarya no ku mahame mbwirazina y’ubupfapfa, bafata icyemezo cyo guceceka ku buryo bw’ubugome.

Hariho guceceka kw’ibyaha kandi hariho amagambo mabi. Guceceka ku kibazo cy’imibonano mpuzabitsina ni icyaha. Kuvuga mu buryo butari bwo ku kibazo cy’imibonano mpuzabitsina nabyo ni ikindi cyaha.

Niba ababyeyi n’abarimu bacecetse, abagizi ba nabi bo mu mibonano mpuzabitsina baravuga kandi abahohotewe baba abangavu badafite ubunararibonye.

Niba umwangavu adashobora kubaza ababyeyi cyangwa abarimu, azabaza rero bagenzi be bigana, bashobora kuba baramaze kuyobywa n’inzira itari yo. Igisubizo ntigitinda kandi umwangavu mushya akurikiza inama z’ibinyoma azayoboka ingeso yo kwikinisha cyangwa akayoba mu nzira y’ubutinganyi.

Ingeso yo kwikinisha yangiza rwose ubushobozi bw’ubwonko. Ni ngombwa kumenya ko hariho isano ya bugufi hagati y’intanga n’ubwonko. Ni ngombwa gushyira ubwonko mu ntanga. Ni ngombwa gushyira intanga mu bwonko.

Ubwonko bushyingurwa mu ntanga buhindura imbaraga z’imibonano mpuzabitsina, bukazizamura, bugahindura imbaraga z’ubwonko.

Muri ubu buryo, intanga igumaho ubwonko bushinguye kandi ubwonko bukashyingurwa mu ntanga.

Siyansi ya Gnostic yiga cyane endocrinologiya kandi yigisha uburyo na sisitemu zo guhindura imbaraga z’imibonano mpuzabitsina, ariko iyi ni ingingo itajyanye muri iki gitabo.

Niba umusomyi ashaka amakuru ku bijyanye na Gnostic, agomba kwiga ibitabo byacu bya Gnostic kandi akitabira amasomo yacu.

Abangavu bagomba kuzamura imbaraga z’imibonano mpuzabitsina bahinga uburyohe, biga umuziki, ububaji, gushushanya, bajya mu ngendo mu misozi miremire, nibindi.

Ni amaso angahe yashoboraga kuba meza arakama!

Ni ubwonko bwingahe bwangirika! Ibyo byose kubera kubura itangazo ry’akaga ku gihe.

Ingeso yo kwikinisha, haba ku rubyiruko rw’abahungu n’abakobwa, yabaye isanzwe kuruta gukaraba intoki.

Ibitaro by’abasazi byuzuye abagabo n’abagore bangije ubwonko bwabo mu ngeso iteye ishozi yo kwikinisha. Iherezo ry’abakinisha ni ibitaro by’abasazi.

Ingeso y’ubutinganyi yaboze imizi y’ubu bwoko bushaje kandi bubi.

Birasa nk’aho bitangaje ko mu bihugu nka Angletere bitekereza ko byateye imbere kandi byarateye imbere cyane, hari za sinema aho filime z’ubutinganyi zerekanwa ku buntu.

Birasa nk’aho bitangaje ko ari muri Angletere aho imihati yamaze gukorwa kugira ngo yemere ku mugaragaro gushyingiranwa kw’ubutinganyi.

Mu mijyi minini y’isi, ubu hari indaya n’amatsinda y’ubutinganyi.

Ubunyamaswa bw’umwijima bw’abanzi b’umugore, muri iki gihe bufite imiryango y’ubusambo butangaje kubera ubwoba bwabo.

Abasomyi benshi bashobora gutangazwa cyane n’iki kintu cyo "ubwoba bwangiritse" ariko ntitwagomba kwibagirwa ko mu bihe byose by’amateka, hahora hari ubwoko butandukanye bw’ubuvandimwe bw’ibyaha.

Ubunyamaswa bubi bw’abanzi b’umugore, ntibushidikanywaho n’ubuvandimwe bw’ibyaha.

Abanzi b’umugore bahora cyangwa hafi ya yose bafata imyanya y’ingenzi muri colmena ya bureaucratie.

Iyo umutinganyi agiye muri gereza, vuba cyane arekurwa bitewe n’influenza ikwiye y’abagabo b’ingenzi bo mu bunyamaswa bw’ibyaha.

Niba umugore aguye mu bushotanyi, vuba cyane ahabwa ubufasha bw’amafaranga n’abantu bose babi bo mu Bunyamaswa bw’ibyaha.

Abanyamuryango babi b’ubutinganyi baramenyana hagati yabo kubera umwambaro bambara.

Biratangaje kumenya ko abatinganyi bambara umwenda w’akazi, ariko ni uko bimeze. Umwenda w’akazi w’abatinganyi uhwanye n’imideri yose itangira. Abatinganyi nibo batangira imideri mishya yose. Iyo imideri ibaye isanzwe, noneho batangira indi. Muri ubu buryo, umwambaro w’akazi w’ubunyamaswa bw’ibyaha burahora ari mushya.

Imigi minini yose y’isi, muri iki gihe ifite abatinganyi babarirwa muri za miliyoni.

Ingeso y’ubutinganyi itangira urugendo rwayo ruteye isoni mu gihe cy’ubwangavu.

Amashuri menshi y’abakobwa n’abahungu ari indaya nyazo z’ubutinganyi.

Abakobwa bangavu babarirwa muri za miliyoni bagenda bashize amanga mu nzira y’umwijima y’abanzi b’umugabo.

Abangavu babarirwa muri za miliyoni b’igitsina gore ni abatinganyi. Ubunyamaswa bw’ibyaha mu batinganyi b’abagore burakomeye, nk’ubunyamaswa bw’ibyaha mu gitsina gabo.

Birihutirwa kureka burundu kandi mu buryo butaziguye isoni z’uburyarya no kugaragariza abangavu b’ibitsina byombi, ukuri kwose kw’ibanga ry’imibonano mpuzabitsina.

Ni muri ubwo buryo gusa ibisekuru bishya bishobora kuyoborwa mu nzira yo KUVUGURURA.
