Igihe kirageze cyo kureka burundu isoni z’uburiganya n’imyumvire ibogamye ku kibazo cy’imibonano mpuzabitsina.

Ni ngombwa gusobanukirwa neza kandi mu buryo butaziguye ikibazo cy’imibonano mpuzabitsina ku bangavu b’ibitsina byombi.

Ku myaka cumi n’ine, imbaraga z’imibonano mpuzabitsina zirabukereye mu mibiri y’umwangavu, zikamanuka zidasiba mu buryo bw’imyakura.

Ubu bwoko bwihariye bw’imbaraga buhindura umubiri w’umuntu, bugahindura ijwi ku bagabo no gutangiza imikorere y’intanga ngore ku bagore.

Umubiri w’umuntu ni uruganda nyarwo ruhindura ibintu bibisi mo ibintu byiza by’ubuzima.

Ibiryo dushyira mu nda binyura mu bihe byinshi byo guhindurwa no kunozwa kugeza ubwo bigera ku ndunduro mu kintu kimeze nk’igice gikomeye, igice cy’amazi cyavuzwe na Paracelse akoresheje ijambo Ens.-Seminis (Ikintu cy’Intanga).

Iryo sukari ry’amazi, riyoroshya, ryoroshye gucurwa, iryo tanga, ririmo ubwaryo, mu buryo bushoboka ingirabuzima zose.

Gnosticisme yemera ko intanga ari CAOS aho ubuzima buturuka n’imbaraga.

Abahanga mu by’ubugenge bo mu gihe cya kera nka Paracelse, Sendivogius, Nicolás Flamel, Raymundo Lulio, n’ibindi, biganye bubaha cyane ENS-SEMINIS cyangwa mercure y’ubuhanga bw’ibanga.

Iyi VITRIOLO, ni umuti nyakuri ukoranywe ubwenge, n’umubyeyi mu mitsi y’intanga.

Muri iyi mercure y’ubwenge bwa kera, muri iyi ntanga, niho uburyo bwose bwo kubaho buboneka.

Birababaje ko abakiri bato benshi kubera kubura ubuyobozi nyabwo bwa psychologique biyanduza ingeso yo kwikinisha cyangwa bakayoba mu buryo bubabaje mu nzira yo hasi y’imibonano mpuzabitsina y’abahuje ibitsina.

Abana n’urubyiruko bahabwa amakuru y’ubwenge ku ngingo nyinshi kandi bakayoborwa mu buryo bw’imikino, aho gukabya bigabanya ubuzima mu buryo bubabaje, ariko bibabaje ko imbaraga z’imibonano mpuzabitsina zigaragara ubwo ingimbi zitangira, ababyeyi bombi ndetse n’abarezi, bashingiye ku bwirasi bw’uburiganya n’imyifatire ya gipfayongo, bahitamo guceceka mu buryo bw’ubugizi bwa nabi.

Hariho guceceka gukabije kandi hariho amagambo ateye isoni. Guceceka ku kibazo cy’imibonano mpuzabitsina ni icyaha. Kuvuga nabi ikibazo cy’imibonano mpuzabitsina nabyo ni ikindi cyaha.

Niba ababyeyi n’abarezi bacecetse, abangizi b’imibonano mpuzabitsina baravuga kandi abahohotewe baba abangavu badafite ubunararibonye.

Niba umwangavu atabasha kubaza ababyeyi cyangwa abarezi, azabaza abanyeshuri bigana bashobora kuba baramaze kuyobywa mu nzira itari yo. Ibisubizo ntibitinda kugaragara kandi umwangavu mushya akurikiza inama z’ibinyoma azishora mu ngeso yo kwikinisha cyangwa akayoba mu nzira y’abahuje ibitsina.

Ingeso yo kwikinisha yangiza ubushobozi bw’ubwonko burundu. Ni ngombwa kumenya ko hariho isano ya bugufi hagati y’intanga n’ubwonko. Ni ngombwa guhindura intanga mo ubwonko. Ni ngombwa guhindura ubwonko mo intanga.

Ubwonko buhindurwa mo intanga binyuze mu guhindura imbaraga z’imibonano mpuzabitsina, kuzamura, kuzihindura imbaraga z’ubwonko.

Muri ubu buryo intanga iguma ihinduwe mo ubwonko n’ubwonko bugahindurwa mo intanga.

Ubumenyi bwa Gnostic bwiga mu buryo bwimbitse endocrinologie kandi bwerekana uburyo na sisitemu zo guhindura imbaraga z’imibonano mpuzabitsina, ariko iyi ni ingingo itabasha kwinjira muri iki gitabo.

Niba umusomyi ashaka amakuru kuri Gnosticisme, agomba kwiga ibitabo byacu bya Gnostic no kwinjira mu masomo yacu.

Abangavu bagomba kuzamura imbaraga z’imibonano mpuzabitsina bahinga uburyohe bw’ubwiza, biga umuziki, ububaji, gushushanya, bakora ingendo mu misozi miremire, n’ibindi.

Mbega amaso angana yashoboraga kuba meza arayitse!

Mbega ubwonko bwingana buranduka! Byose biterwa no kubura itabaza ry’akabarore ku gihe gikwiriye.

Ingeso yo kwikinisha, haba mu bakobwa bato ndetse no mu bakobwa bakuru yabaye isanzwe kuruta gukaraba intoki.

Ibitaro byo mu mutwe byuzuye abagabo n’abagore bononekaye ubwonko bwabo mu ngeso iteye ishozi yo kwikinisha. Iherezo ry’abikinisha ni ikigo cy’abashazi.

Ingeso y’ubutinganyi ifite imizi ibora y’ubu bwoko bw’abantu bubora kandi bubi.

Birasa n’ibidasanzwe ko mu bihugu nka Angleterre byiyita ibihangange kandi byateye imbere, hariho za sinema zigaragaza filime z’ubutinganyi.

Birasa n’ibidasanzwe ko ari muri Angleterre aho imbaraga zimaze gukorwa kugira ngo bemererwe mu buryo bwemewe n’amategeko gushyingiranwa kw’ubutinganyi.

Mu mijyi minini y’isi, ubu hariho amazu y’uburaya n’amakipe y’ubutinganyi.

Umuryango w’ibanga w’abanzi b’umugore, muri iki gihe ufite imiryango yangiritse itangaza kubera ubwiyandarike bwabo.

Abasomyi benshi bashobora gutungurwa cyane n’iki kintu cyo "ubwiyandarike", ariko ntitwagomba kwibagirwa ko mu bihe byose by’amateka buri gihe habagaho ubwoko butandukanye bw’ubuvandimwe bw’ibyaha.

Umuryango w’uburwayi w’abanzi b’umugore, nta gushidikanya ni ubwoko bw’ubuvandimwe bw’ibyaha.

Abanzi b’umugore buri gihe bakora cyangwa hafi ya buri gihe imyanya y’ingenzi mu nzu y’ubuyobozi.

Iyo umutinganyi agiye muri gereza, ahita arekurwa bitewe n’uruhare rukwiye rw’abagabo b’ingenzi mu muryango w’ibyaha.

Niba ikiremba kigiye mu kaga, gihita kibona ubufasha bw’amafaranga buturutse ku bantu babi bose bo mu muryango w’ibyaha.

Abagize umuryango w’umwijima w’ubutinganyi baramenyana ubwabo ku mwambaro bambara.

Bitangaje kumenya ko abatinganyi bambara umwambaro, ariko ni uko biri. Umwambaro w’abatinganyi uhwanye na buri buryo bushya butangira. Abatinganyi batangiza uburyo bwose bushya. Iyo uburyo bwabaye busanzwe, noneho batangira ubundi. Muri ubu buryo, umwambaro w’umuryango w’ibyaha buri gihe uba mushya.

Imijyi minini yose yo ku isi, ubu ifite miliyoni z’abatinganyi.

Ingeso y’ubutinganyi itangira urugendo rwayo ruteye isoni mu gihe cy’ubwangavu.

Amashuri menshi y’abangavu b’abahungu n’abakobwa ni amazu y’uburaya nyayo y’ubutinganyi.

Miliyoni z’abakobwa b’abangavu bagenda bashikamye mu nzira y’umwijima y’abanzi b’umugabo.

Miliyoni z’abangavu b’igitsina gore ni abatinganyi. Umuryango w’ibyaha mu butinganyi bw’igitsina gore urakomeye, nk’umuryango w’ibyaha mu gitsina gabo.

Birihutirwa kureka burundu isoni z’uburiganya no kwerekana abangavu b’ibitsina byombi, mu kuri amayobera yose y’imibonano mpuzabitsina.

Ni yo nzira yonyine ibisekuru bishya bishobora gucamo mu nzira ya REGENERACIÓN.
