Amahoro ntashobora kuza binyuze mu BWENGE kuko atari ay’Ubwenge. Amahoro ni umwiberuro uryohera UMUTIMA UTUZA.

Amahoro si ibintu byo gutegura imishinga, polisi mpuzamahanga, ONU. OEA, amasezerano mpuzamahanga cyangwa ingabo ziteye zigwira mu izina ry’AMAHORO.

Niba dushaka by’ukuri AMAHORO nyakuri, tugomba kwiga kubaho nk’umurinzi mu gihe cy’intambara, duhora twiteguye kandi turi maso, dufite UBwENGE bwihuta kandi bworoshye, kuko AMAHORO atari ibintu byo GUHIMBA ibintu biryoheye cyangwa ibintu byo kurota inzozi nziza.

Nitudatozwa kubaho mu buryo bwo guhora twiteguye umwanya ku wundi, noneho inzira igana ku MAHORO iragoye, irafunganye, hanyuma nyuma yo gukomera cyane, izarangirira mu nzira ifunze.

Birakenewe kumva, ni ngombwa kumenya ko Amahoro nyakuri y’UMUTIMA UTUZA atari inzu dushobora kugeramo kandi aho umukobwa mwiza atutegereje yishimye. Amahoro si intego, ahantu, n'ibindi.

Gukurikirana AMAHORO, kuyashaka, gukora imishinga kuri yo, kurwanira mu izina ryayo, kuyamamaza, gushinga imiryango yo kuyakorera, n'ibindi, ni ubupfu rwose kuko AMAHORO atari ay’UBwENGE, AMAHORO ni umwiberuro mwiza w’umutima utuje.

Amahoro ntigurwa, ntigurishwa kandi ntashobora kugerwaho na sisitemu yo KWICISHA BUGUFI, igenzura ryihariye, polisi, n'ibindi.

Mu bihugu bimwe na bimwe, ingabo z’igihugu zigendera mu mirima zisenya imidugudu, zica abantu kandi zirasa abakekwaho kuba abajura, ibyo byose ngo ni mu izina ry’AMAHORO. Ingaruka z'imyitwarire nk'iyi ni ukwiyongera k'UBUNYAMASWA.

Urugerero rutera urugerero, urwango rutera urwango. Amahoro ntashobora kwigarurirwa, amahoro ntashobora kuba ingaruka z’urugomo. Amahoro azaza gusa kuri twe iyo dusesaguye JYE, iyo turimbuye imbere muri twe ibintu byose bya PSYCHOLOGICAL biteza intambara.

Niba dushaka AMAHORO, tugomba kwitegereza, tugomba kwiga, tugomba kubona ishusho yose kandi ntiturebe gusa igice kimwe cyayo.

Amahoro avutse muri twe iyo twahindutse rwose mu buryo bwimbitse.

Ikibazo cy’igenzura, imiryango ya PRO AMAHORO, kwicisha bugufi, n’ibindi, ni ibisobanuro bitandukanye, ingingo, mu nyanja y’ubuzima, ibice bitandukanye by’ishusho yose y’UBUZIMA, bidashobora gukemura ikibazo cy’AMAHORO mu buryo bwayo bukomeye, bwuzuye kandi burundu.

Tugomba kureba ishusho yose mu buryo bwuzuye, ikibazo cy’isi ni ikibazo cy’umuntu ku giti cye; niba UMUNTU ku giti cye adafite AMAHORO imbere muri we, umuryango, isi izabaho mu ntambara bidashoboka.

Abagisha n'abigisha bo mu mashuri, amakoleji, amayunivesite bagomba gukorera AMAHORO, keretse niba bakunda UBUNYAMASWA n'URUGOMO.

Ni ngombwa, ni ngombwa kwerekana abanyeshuri n’abanyeshurikazi b’urubyiruko rw’ejo hazaza inzira bagomba gukurikira, inzira yimbitse ishobora kutugeza neza ku MAHORO nyakuri y’umutima utuje.

Abantu ntibazi gusobanukirwa neza icyo AMAHORO nyakuri yo mu mutima aricyo kandi bashaka gusa ko ntihagira umuntu ubaca mu nzira, ko batabangamirwa, ko batavuga rumwe, nubwo bifata ku giti cyabo uburenganzira bwo kubangamira no kubangamira no gusharirira ubuzima bwa bagenzi babo.

Abantu ntibigeze bagira AMAHORO nyakuri kandi bafite gusa ibitekerezo bidafite ishingiro kuri yo, ibitekerezo biryoheye, ibitekerezo bibi.

Ku bajura, AMAHORO yaba ari umunezero wo kuba bashobora kwiba badahanwa nta polisi ibaca mu nzira. Ku bacuruzi b’abacanshuro, AMAHORO yaba ari ugushobora gushyira ibicuruzwa byabo bya magendu ahantu hose nta bategetsi babibuza. Ku bashonjesha abaturage, AMAHORO yaba ari ukugurisha cyane, bakabyaza umusaruro iburyo n’ibumoso nta bagenzuzi b’amategeko ba leta babibujije. Ku ndaya AMAHORO, yaba ari ukwishimira mu buriri bwabo bwo kwishimisha no kubyaza umusaruro abagabo bose ku buntu nta bategetsi b’ubuzima cyangwa polisi bagira uruhare na gato mu buzima bwabo.

Buri wese yiyubakira mu bwenge ibitekerezo bidafite ishingiro ibihumbi mirongo itanu ku MAHOHO. Buri wese agerageza kuzamura hakurya y’urukuta rwo kwikunda rw’ibitekerezo by’ibinyoma, imyizerere, ibitekerezo n’ibitekerezo bidafite ishingiro ku byo AMAHORO aricyo.

Buri wese ashaka AMAHORO mu buryo bwe, ukurikije ibyo yifuza, ibyo akunda, imico ye, imigenzo mibi, n'ibindi. Buri wese ashaka kwifungirana mu rukuta rurengera, rw’agatangaza, agamije kubaho AMAHORO ye, yatekerejwe nabi.

Abantu baharanira AMAHORO, barayifuza, barayashaka, ariko ntibazi icyo AMAHORO aricyo. Abantu bashaka gusa ko batabangamirwa, kugira ngo buri wese abe ashobora gukora ibikorwa byabo bibi batuje cyane kandi uko bashaka. Ibyo nibyo bita AMAHORO.

Ntacyo bitwaye ibikorwa bibi abantu bakora, buri wese yizera ko ibyo akora ari byiza. Abantu babona impamvu n’ibyaha bibi kurusha ibindi. Niba umusinzi ababaye aranywa kuko ababaye. Niba umusinzi yishimye aranywa kuko yishimye. Umusinzi ahora yemeza ko akoresha inzoga. Uko niko abantu bose bameze, ku cyaha cyose babona impamvu, nta n’umwe ufata ko ari mubi, bose bavuga ko ari abakiranutsi kandi b’inyangamugayo.

Hariho abazerera benshi bakeka mu buryo butari bwo ko AMAHORO ari ukubaho utakorera, utuje cyane kandi nta mbaraga zikoreshejwe mu isi yuzuye ibitekerezo biryoheye by’igitangaza.

Ku bijyanye n’AMAHORO hariho ibitekerezo n’ibitekerezo bibi miliyoni. Muri iyi si ibabaje tubamo: buri wese ashaka AMAHORO ye y’igitangaza, amahoro y’ibitekerezo bye. Abantu bashaka kubona mu isi amahoro y’inzozi zabo, ubwoko bwabo bwihariye bw’amahoro, nubwo imbere muri bo buri wese afite imbere muri we ibintu bya psychologique biteza intambara, ubwumvikane buke, ibibazo by’ubwoko bwose.

Muri ibi bihe by’ikibazo gikomeye ku isi, umuntu wese ushaka kwamamara ashinga imiryango ya PRO-AMAHORO, aramamaza kandi ahinduka intwari y’AMAHORO. Ntitugomba kwibagirwa ko abanyapolitiki benshi b’amayeri begukanye igihembo cya NOBEL cy’AMAHORO nubwo bafite ikimoteri cyose ku giti cyabo kandi ko mu buryo bumwe cyangwa ubundi bategetse kwica abantu benshi mu buryo bw’ibanga, igihe babonaga ko hari akaga ko kwigaragara.

Hariho kandi abagisha nyakuri b’inyokomuntu bitanga bigisha ahantu hose ku isi Inyigisho zo Gusesagura JYE. Abo bagisha bazi ku giti cyabo ko ari ukuzimya Mefistoferi dufite imbere, amahoro y’umutima azaza kuri twe.

Igihe cyose urwango, umururumba, ishyari, ifuhe, umwuka wo kwigarurira, ubwibone, umujinya, ubwibone, n'ibindi bizaba biri mu muntu ku giti cye, hazabaho intambara bidashoboka.

Tuzi abantu benshi ku isi bavuga ko babonye AMAHORO. Iyo twize neza abo bantu, twashoboye kugaragaza ko batigeze na gato bamenya AMAHORO kandi ko bashyize gusa mu ngeso runaka y’ubwiganze kandi ihoza, cyangwa mu myizerere runaka yihariye, n’ibindi, ariko mu by’ukuri abo bantu ntibigeze na gato bagira ibyo AMAHORO nyakuri y’umutima utuje aricyo. Mu by’ukuri abo bantu bakennye biyubakiye gusa amahoro yo gukora bakayobya n’AMAHORO AUTENTIQUE Y’UMUTIMA mu bujiji bwabo.

Ni ubupfu gushakira AMAHORO mu nkuta zibeshya z’urwikekwe rwacu, imyizerere, ibitekerezo, ibyifuzo, imigenzo, n’ibindi.

Igihe cyose mu BWENGE hazabaho ibintu bya psychologique biteza ubwumvikane buke, amacakubiri, ibibazo, intambara, ntihazabaho AMAHORO nyakuri.

AMAHORO nyakuri ava mu bwiza bwemewe bwumvikanyweho n’ubwenge.

Ubwiza bw’umutima utuje buhumeka umwiberuro uryoheye w’AMAHORO nyakuri yo mu mutima.

Ni ngombwa gusobanukirwa ubwiza bw’ubucuti n’umwiberuro w’ubwubahane.

Ni ngombwa gusobanukirwa ubwiza bw’ururimi. Ni ngombwa ko amagambo yacu yibitsemo ubwibone. Ntitugomba na rimwe gukoresha amagambo adakurikije gahunda, adahuza, ateye isoni, adafite ishingiro.

Ijambo ryose rigomba kuba indirimbo y’ukuri, interuro yose igomba kuba yuzuye ubwiza bw’umwuka. Ni bibi cyane kuvuga igihe ugomba guceceka, no guceceka igihe ugomba kuvuga. Hariho guceceka kw’ubugizi bwa nabi kandi hariho amagambo ateye isoni.

Hariho igihe kuvuga ari icyaha, hariho igihe guceceka nabyo ari ikindi cyaha. Umuntu agomba kuvuga igihe agomba kuvuga no guceceka igihe agomba guceceka.

Ntitureke gukina n’ijambo kuko rifite inshingano zikomeye.

Ijambo ryose rigomba gupimwa mbere yo kuvugwa kuko ijambo rimwe na rimwe rishobora guteza byinshi byo gufasha n’iby’ubusa ku isi, inyungu nyinshi cyangwa igihombo kinini.

Tugomba kwita ku myifatire yacu, imyitwarire, imyambarire n’ibikorwa by’ubwoko bwose. Ko imyifatire yacu, imyambarire yacu, uburyo twicara ku meza, uburyo twitwara iyo turya, uburyo twita ku bantu mu cyumba, mu biro, mu muhanda, n'ibindi bihora byuzuye ubwiza n'ubwumvikane.

Ni ngombwa gusobanukirwa ubwiza bw’ineza, kumva ubwiza bw’umuziki mwiza, gukunda ubwiza bw’ubuhanzi bwo guhanga, kunoza uburyo bwacu bwo gutekereza, kumva no gukora.

Ubwiza bukuru bushobora kuvuka muri twe gusa iyo JYE yapfuye mu buryo bukomeye, bwuzuye kandi burundu.

Turi babi, duteye ubwoba, duteye ishozi igihe tugifite imbere kandi turoboka JYE PSYCHOLOGIQUE. Ubwiza mu buryo bwuzuye ntibushoboka muri twe igihe cyose JYE PLURALIZADO ibaho.
