Ikibazo gikomeye cyane cy’UBUREZI BWA LETA si abanyeshuri bo mu mashuri abanza, ayisumbuye cyangwa ayo hejuru, ahubwo ni ABABYEYI n’ABAREZI.

Niba Ababyeyi n’Abarimu batamenye ubwabo, niba badashoboye gusobanukirwa umwana w’umuhungu, umwana w’umukobwa, niba batazi neza imibanire yabo n’ibyo biremwa bitangiye kubaho, niba bahangayikishijwe gusa no guhinga ubwenge bw’abigishwa babo, twazashobora gute gushyiraho ubwoko bushya bw’uburezi?

Umwana w’umuhungu, umunyeshuri, umwana w’umukobwa, ajya ku Ishuri kwakira ubuyobozi buboneye, ariko niba Abarimu, Abarezi, bafite imyumvire igufi, bigumira ku mategeko, bakagaranzura, bagasubira inyuma, abe ari ko umunyeshuri, umunyeshuri w’umukobwa, azaba.

Abarezi bagomba kwiyongerera ubumenyi, kwimenya ubwabo, gusuzuma ubumenyi bwabo bwose, kumva ko twinjiye mu gihe gishya.

Abarezi bahindutse, uburezi bwa leta burahinduka.

KWIGISHA UMWIGISHA ni cyo kigoye cyane kuko buri wese wasomye byinshi, buri wese ufite impamyabumenyi, buri wese ugomba kwigisha, ukora nk’umwarimu w’Ishuri, ameze uko ameze, ubwenge bwe bupfunyitse mu bitekerezo ibihumbi mirongo itanu yize kandi ntazigera ahinduka.

Abarezi bagombye kwigisha UKO BATEKEREZA, ariko ikibabaje ni uko bahangayikishijwe no kubigisha IBYO BAGOMBA KUTEKEREZA.

Ababyeyi n’Abarimu buzuye impungenge zikomeye z’ubukungu, imibereho, amarangamutima, n’ibindi.

Ababyeyi n’Abarimu ahanini bahugiye mu makimbirane yabo bwite n’agahinda kabo, ntibashishikajwe by’ukuri no kwiga no gukemura ibibazo byagaragajwe n’abahungu n’abakobwa bo mu “GIHE GISHYA”.

Hariho ihinduka rikomeye mu mutwe, mu myifatire no mu mibereho, ariko ababyeyi n’Abarimu buzuye impungenge n’imihangayiko yabo bwite kandi bafite umwanya wo gutekereza ku bijyanye n’ubukungu bw’abana, kubaha umwuga kugira ngo batazicwa n’inzara kandi ibyo ni byo byose.

Bitandukanye n’uko abantu benshi babyemera, ababyeyi benshi ntibakunda abana babo by’ukuri, iyo babakunda, baharanira ineza rusange, bahangayikishwa n’ibibazo by’UBUREZI BWA LETA hagamijwe kugera ku mpinduka nyayo.

Iyo ababyeyi bakunda abana babo by’ukuri, ntihabaho intambara, ntibashimangira cyane umuryango n’igihugu mu kurwanya isi yose, kuko ibyo biteza ibibazo, intambara, amacakubiri yangiza, ikirere kibi ku bahungu n’abakobwa bacu.

Abantu bariga, bitegura kuba abaganga, abinjiniyeri, abanyamategeko, n’ibindi, ariko ntibitegura umurimo ukomeye cyane kandi ukomeye kurusha iyindi ari wo wo kuba Ababyeyi.

Ubwo bwikunde bw’umuryango, ubwo kutagira urukundo ku bantu bacu, iyo politiki yo kwigunga kw’imiryango, nta shingiro na rimwe ijana ku ijana, kuko ihinduka ikintu kigira uruhare mu kwangirika no gusenyuka k’imibereho y’abantu.

Amajyambere, Impinduramatwara nyayo, birashoboka gusa gusenya izo nkike zizwi cyane z’Abashinwa zidutandukanya, zidushyira mu bwigunge n’isi yose.

Twese turi UMURYANGO UMWE KANDI ntibifite ishingiro kubabazanya, gufata gusa nk’umuryango abantu bake babana natwe, n’ibindi.

Ubwikunde BW’UMURYANGO budindiza amajyambere y’imibereho y’abantu, bugatanya abantu, bugateza intambara, amoko, abatoneshejwe, ibibazo by’ubukungu, n’ibindi.

Iyo ababyeyi bakunda abana babo by’ukuri, inkuta, inkike zizira z’ubwihisho ziragwa ivumbi maze umuryango ugahagarara kuba uruziga rw’ubwikunde n’ubupfapfa.

Inkuta z’ubwikunde bw’umuryango ziguye, habaho ubumwe bw’abavandimwe n’abandi babyeyi bose, n’Abarimu, n’umuryango wose.

Ingaruka z’UBUVANDIMWE BW’UKURI, ni IMPINDUKA NYAYO MU MIBEREHO Y’ABANTU, IMPINDUKAMATWARA nyayo y’ishami RY’UBUREZI kugira ngo isi ibe nziza.

UMUREZI agomba kuba ashishoza cyane, agomba guhuriza hamwe Ababyeyi, akanama gashinzwe ubuyobozi bw’Ababyeyi kandi akababwira neza.

Ni ngombwa ko ababyeyi bumva ko umurimo w’uburezi bwa leta ushingiye ku bufatanye busesuye hagati y’Ababyeyi n’Abarimu.

Ni ngombwa kubwira ababyeyi ko UBUREZI BW’ISHINGIRO bukenewe kugira ngo hazamurwe ibisekuru bishya.

Ni ngombwa kubwira ababyeyi ko amahugurwa y’ubwenge akenewe ariko ko atari yo yose, hakenewe ikindi kintu, hakenewe kwigisha abahungu n’abakobwa kwimenya ubwabo, kumenya amakosa yabo bwite, amakemwa yabo bwite mu mitekerereze.

Tugomba kubwira ababyeyi ko abana bagomba gutwara inda kubera URUKUNDO kandi ntibaterwe n’IBYIFUZO BY’INYAMASWA.

Biteye isoni kandi biteye ubwoba guteganya ibyifuzo byacu by’inyamaswa, irari ryacu ry’imibonano mpuzabitsina y’urukozasoni, amarangamutima yacu arwaye n’ibyiyumvo by’inyamaswa ku bakomoka kuri twe.

Abahungu n’Abakobwa ni ibitekerezo byacu bwite kandi ni icyaha kwanduza Isi ibitekerezo by’inyamaswa.

Abarezi b’Amashuri, Amakoleji na za Kaminuza bagomba guhuriza Ababyeyi mu cyumba cy’inama, bafite intego nziza yo kubigisha inzira y’inshingano z’imyifatire ku bana babo no ku Muryango n’Isi.

ABAREZI bafite inshingano yo KWONGERA KWIHUGURA no kuyobora Ababyeyi.

Dukeneye gukunda by’ukuri kugira ngo duhindure isi. Dukeneye guhurira hamwe kugira ngo twubake twese, Ingoro nziza y’Igihe Gishya itangiye muri iki gihe hagati y’inkuba ikomeye y’ibitekerezo.
