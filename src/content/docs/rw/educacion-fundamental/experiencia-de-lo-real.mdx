Ku muryango w'urusengero rukomeye rwa Delfus hari handitse inyandiko yera ibajwe mu ibuye rizima igira iti: "UMENYE WEMWE". Menya wewe ubwawe maze uzamenya isanzure n'Imana.

Ubumenyi bwa gihanga bw'Umwimerere wo Kuzirikana bufite nk'ishingiro ry'ibanze iryo hame ryera ry'abahanga ba kera b'Abagereki.

Niba dushaka by'ukuri kandi mu buryo bwimbitse gushyiraho ishingiro ryo kuzirikana nyakuri, birakenewe kwiyumvisha ubwacu mu nzego zose z'ubwenge.

Gushyiraho ishingiro nyakuri ryo kuzirikana ni byo koko, kubohoka ubwibone, ubwikunde, ubwoba, urwango, umururumba w'imbaraga zidasanzwe, inyota yo kugera ku ntego, n'ibindi, n'ibindi, n'ibindi.

Biragaragarira buri wese kandi nta gushidikanya ko nyuma yo gushyiraho IBANGA RYO MU NGERO RY'IBANZE ryo kuzirikana ubwenge buguma butuje kandi mu guceceka kwimbitse kandi gutangaje.

Ku ruhande rw'ubwenge bwuzuye, birasa n'aho bidafite ishingiro gushaka kugerageza UKURI tutabanje kumenya ubwacu.

Ni ngombwa gusobanukirwa mu buryo BWUZUYE kandi mu turere twose tw'ubwenge, buri kibazo uko kigaragara mu bwenge, buri cyifuzo, buri kwibuka, buri kamenyero kabi mu mitekerereze, n'ibindi.

Biragaragarira buri wese ko mu gihe cyo gukora kuzirikana, ibintu bibi byose bigaragara mu bwenge byerekana kamere yacu, ibyishimo byacu byose n'agahinda kacu, kwibuka byinshi, ibitekerezo byinshi bituruka mu isi yo hanze, cyangwa mu isi yo imbere, ibyifuzo by'ubwoko bwose, amarangamutima y'ubwoko bwose, inzika za kera, urwango, n'ibindi.

Ushaka by'ukuri gushyira mu bwenge bwe ibuye ry'ibanze ryo kuzirikana, agomba kwita cyane kuri izo ndangagaciro nziza n'imbi z'ubwenge bwacu maze akazisobanukirwa mu buryo bwuzuye atari ku rwego rw'ubwenge gusa, ahubwo no mu turere twose tudasobanutse, tutarebera kure n'udashobora kumenyekana bw'ubwenge. Ntitwagomba kwigera twibagirwa ko ubwenge bufite ibipimo byinshi.

Kwiga byimbitse indangagaciro zose bivuze kumenya ubwawe.

Filimi yose iri kuri ecran y'ubwenge ifite intangiriro n'iherezo. Iyo imyiyerekano y'ibishushanyo, ibyifuzo, amarangamutima, ubwibone, kwibuka, n'ibindi, birangiye, ubwenge buguma butuje kandi mu guceceka kwimbitse NTABWO bwatekereza.

Abanyeshuri bo muri iki gihe ba Psychologie bakeneye kwihererana IKINTU CY'UMUCYO. Ukwinjira kw'UBUSA mu bwenge bwacu bwite biduha ubushobozi bwo kwihererana, kumva, kwihererana ikintu gihindura, ICYO KINTU ni UBUTUMWA.

Gutandukanya hagati y'ubwenge butuje n'ubwenge bucecekeshejwe ku ngufu.

Gutandukanya hagati y'ubwenge buri mu guceceka n'ubwenge bwahagaritswe ku ngufu.

Nk'uko bigaragara ku bwenge ubwo aribwo bwose, tugomba gusobanukirwa ko iyo ubwenge bucecekeshejwe ku ngufu, mu mutima no mu zindi nzego ntibutuje kandi bugaharanira kwibohora.

Ku ruhande rw'isesengura, tugomba gusobanukirwa ko iyo ubwenge bucecekeshejwe ku ngufu, mu mutima ntibucecetse, burarira kandi bugatakamba cyane.

Umutuzo nyakuri n'uguhora uhari kandi kwikora kw'ubwenge, bitugeraho nk'ubuntu, nk'umunezero, iyo filimi y'ibanga cyane y'ubuzima bwacu bwite irangiye kuri ecran itangaje y'ubwenge.

Iyo ubwenge butuje mu buryo busanzwe kandi bwihariye gusa, iyo ubwenge buri mu guceceka gushimishije gusa, ni bwo ukwinjira kw'IKINTU CY'UMUCYO kuzaza.

UBUSA ntibworoshye gusobanura. Ntibusobanurwa cyangwa ngo busobanurwe, igitekerezo cyose twatanga kuri bwo gishobora gutsindwa ku ngingo y'ingenzi.

UBUSA ntibushobora gusobanurwa cyangwa ngo bugaragazwe mu magambo. Ibi biterwa n'uko ururimi rw'abantu rwaremewe ahanini kugena ibintu, ibitekerezo n'ibyiyumvo biriho; ntirukwiriye kugaragaza mu buryo busobanutse kandi bwihariye, ibintu, ibintu n'ibyiyumvo BIDAHARI.

Kugerageza kuganira ku BUSA mu mbibi z'ururimi rugufi rushingiye ku buryo bubaho, rwose nta gushidikanya, birasa n'aho ari ubupfapfa kandi byibeshye rwose.
