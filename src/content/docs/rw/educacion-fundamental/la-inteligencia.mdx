Twabonye ko abagabo n'abagore benshi bigisha Amateka y'Isi mu Burengerazuba bw'isi bakunze gusuzugura BUDDHA, Confucius, Muhammad, Hermes, Quetzacoal, Mose, Krishna, n'abandi.

Nta gushidikanya kandi twabonye bihagije, ubunyamaswa, isesereza, urwenya rwatanzwe n'abagabo n'abagore bigisha amadini ya kera, imana, imigani, n'ibindi. Ibyo byose ni ukubura ubwenge.

Mu mashuri, amakoleji na za kaminuza, ibijyanye n'amadini bikwiye kuvugwa, bubahana cyane, babigaragaza ko babiyubaha cyane, babikoresha ubwenge nyabwo bwo guhanga.

Uburyo bw'amadini bubungabunga indangagaciro zihoraho kandi butegurwa hakurikijwe ibyo buri bwoko, buri bwoko bugomba kuba bufite ubwenge mu by'amateka.

Amadini yose afite amahame amwe, indangagaciro zihoraho kandi zitandukanira gusa mu buryo.

Iyo abasore n'inkumi bavuye mu ishuri nyuma y'imyaka icumi cyangwa cumi n'itanu, basanga bararaye kandi basinziriye nk'abandi bantu, buzuye ubusa kandi babura ubwenge nk'umunsi wa mbere binjiye mu ishuri.

Ni ngombwa ko abanyeshuri mu bindi bintu bateza imbere ikigo cy'amarangamutima kuko byose atari ubwenge. Birakenewe kwiga kumva ubwumvikane bwa hafi bw'ubuzima, ubwiza bw'igiti cyonyine, indirimbo y'inyoni nto mu ishyamba, ihuriro ry'umuziki n'amabara y'izuba rirenga ryiza.

Birakenewe kandi kumva no gusobanukirwa neza itandukaniro ryose riteye ubwoba ry'ubuzima, nk'uko umutekano mboneragihugu ari mubi kandi utagira impuhwe muri iki gihe tubayemo, imihanda yuzuye ababyeyi batishimye n'abana babo bagwingiye kandi bashonje basabiriza agace k'umutsima, amazu mabi abamo imiryango ibihumbi n'ibihumbi ikennye, imihanda iteye ishozi inyuraho ibihumbi by'imodoka zikoresha ibyo bicanwa byangiza ingingo, n'ibindi.
