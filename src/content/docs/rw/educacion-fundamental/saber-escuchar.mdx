Ku isi hariho abavuga benshi batangaza kubera ubuhanga bwabo mu kuvuga, ariko abantu bake nibo bazi gutega amatwi.

Kumenya gutega amatwi biragoye cyane, bake nibo bantu bazi gutega amatwi byukuri.

IYO MWARIMU, umwarimukazi, umuhanga avuga, abateraniye hamwe basa nk'abakurikirana cyane, nk'aho bakurikiranira hafi buri jambo ry'umuvuzi, byose bigatanga igitekerezo cy'uko batega amatwi, ko bari maso, ariko mu miterere ya psychologiya ya buri muntu hari umunyamabanga uhindura buri jambo ry'umuvuzi.

UYU MUNYAMABANGA NI NJYE, NI JYENEWE, NI WE UBWE. Umurimo w'uwo munyamabanga ni ukudasesengura neza, guhindura nabi amagambo y'umuvuzi.

NJYE asobanura ukurikije ibitekerezo bye bibi, ibitekerezo yifitiye mbere, ubwoba, ishema, impungenge, ibitekerezo, ibitekerezo, n'ibindi, n'ibindi, n'ibindi.

Abanyeshuri ku ishuri, abanyeshurikazi, abantu bafatanyiriza hamwe bagakora abateraniye hamwe batega amatwi, mu by'ukuri ntibatega amatwi umuvuzi, barimo kwiyumvira, barimo kumva EGO yabo bwite, EGO yabo bakunda cyane ya MAQUIAVÃ‰LICA, idashaka kwemera UKURI, IBINTU NYABYO, IBINTU BY'INGENZI.

Ariko mu gihe cyo kuba maso UBUHANGA, hamwe n'UBWONKO BWIZA butaremererwa n'ibyahise, mu gihe cyo KWAKIRA neza, dushobora gutega amatwi byukuri tudafite aho uwo munyamabanga mubi witwa NJYE, NJYENEWE, WE UBWE, EGO ahuriye.

Iyo ubwenge bushingiye ku kwibuka, busubiramo ibyo bwabitswe.

Ubwenge bushingiye ku byabaye kera kenshi, bushobora kubona ibiriho binyuze mu ndorerwamo zanduye z'ibyashize.

NIBA DUSHYAKA KUMENYA GUTEGA AMATWI, niba dushaka kwiga gutega amatwi kugirango tuvumbure ibishya, tugomba kubaho dukurikije filozofiya yo MU MWANYA.

Ni ngombwa kubaho umwanya ku wundi tudahangayikishijwe n'ibyashize, kandi nta mishinga y'ejo hazaza.

UKURI ni ibitazwi umwanya ku wundi, ubwenge bwacu bugomba guhora bukeye, twitonze cyane, twirinda ibitekerezo bibi, ibitekerezo yifitiye mbere, kugirango tube abakira byukuri.

Abarimu n'abarimukazi b'ishuri bagomba kwigisha abanyeshuri babo ibisobanuro byimbitse biri mu kumenya gutega amatwi.

Ni ngombwa kwiga kubaho mu bwenge, kongera gushimangira ibitekerezo byacu, kunoza imyitwarire yacu, ibitekerezo byacu, ibyiyumvo byacu.

Nta cyo bimaze kugira umuco ukomeye w'amasomo, niba tutazi gutega amatwi, niba tutabasha kuvumbura ibishya umwanya ku wundi.

Dukeneye kunoza ibyo twitaho, kunoza imyitwarire yacu, kunoza abantu bacu, ibintu, n'ibindi, n'ibindi, n'ibindi.

Ntibishoboka kuba abanoze byukuri iyo tutazi gutega amatwi.

Ubwenge bukabije, bubi, bwangiritse, bwazahaye ntibwigeze bumenya gutega amatwi, ntibwigeze bumenya kuvumbura ibishya, ubwo bwenge busobanukiwe gusa, busobanukiwe gusa mu buryo bukocamye ibisobanuro bidafite ishingiro by'uwo munyamabanga wa shitani witwa NJYE, NJYENEWE, EGO.

Kuba umunyamuco ni ikintu kigoye cyane kandi bisaba kwitonda cyane. Umuntu ashobora kuba umuntu w'umunyamuco cyane mu buryo, imyenda, imyambaro, ubusitani, imodoka, ubucuti, nyamara agakomeza mu mutima we kuba umuntu utaranozwa, ukabije, uremereye.

Umuntu uzi kubaho umwanya ku wundi, agenda byukuri mu nzira yo kunoza byukuri.

Umuntu ufite ubwenge bwakira, bwiza, bwuzuye, bwiteguye, agenda mu nzira yo kunoza nyakuri.

Umuntu wugurura ibintu byose bishya akareka umutwaro w'ibyahise, ibitekerezo yifitiye mbere, ibitekerezo bibi, gushidikanya, uburosobyi, n'ibindi, agenda neza mu nzira yo kunoza kwemewe.

Ubwenge bwazahaye bubaho bufungiwe mu byashize, mu bitekerezo yifitiye mbere, ishema, kwikunda, ibitekerezo bibi, n'ibindi, n'ibindi.

Ubwenge bwazahaye ntibuzi kubona ibishya, ntibuzi gutega amatwi, bushingiye ku KWIKUNDA.

Abafana ba MARXISME-LENINISME ntibemera ibishya; ntibemera ICYA KANE KIRANGA ibintu byose, ICYA KANE CY'INGERO, kubera kwikunda, barakunda cyane, bakurikiza inyigisho zabo bwite zidakomeye kandi IYO tubashyize mu kibuga cy'ibikorwa bifatika, iyo tubereka ubuswa bw'amagambo yabo y'ubushukanyi, bazamura ukuboko kw'ibumoso, bakareba amasakoshi yo ku ntoki zabo, bagatanga urwitwazo rutumvikana maze bakagenda.

Ubwo ni ubwenge bwazahaye, ubwenge bushaje butazi gutega amatwi, butazi kuvumbura ibishya, butemera ukuri kubera ko bufungiwe mu KWIKUNDA. Ubwenge bukunda cyane, ubwenge butazi IBIRANGA UMUCO, ubwenge bukabije, ubwenge bubi, bumva gusa EGO yabo bakunda.

UBUREZI BW'IBANZE bwigisha gutega amatwi, bwigisha kubaho mu bwenge.

Abarimu n'abarimukazi b'amashuri, amakoleji, za kaminuza bagomba kwigisha abanyeshuri babo inzira nyakuri yo kunoza ubuzima byukuri.

Nta cyo bimaze kumara imyaka icumi na cumi n'itanu winjiye mu mashuri, amakoleji na za kaminuza, iyo dusohotse imbere turi ingurube nyakuri mu bitekerezo byacu, ibitekerezo, ibyiyumvo n'imico.

Hakenewe UBUREZI BW'IBANZE byihutirwa kuko ibisekuru bishya bisobanura intangiriro y'igihe gishya.

Igihe kigeze cyo GUKORA IMPINDUKA NYAKURI, igihe kigeze cyo GUKORA IMPINDUKA Z'IBANZE.

Ibyashize ni ibyashize kandi byatanze imbuto zabyo. Dukeneye gusobanukirwa n'igisobanuro cyimbitse cy'igihe turimo.
