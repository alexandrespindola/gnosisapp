Ku isi hari abavuga benshi batangaza kubera ubuhanga bwabo mu kuvuga, ariko bake ni bo bazi gutega amatwi.

Kumenya gutega amatwi biragoye cyane, bake ni bo bantu bazi gutega amatwi by’ukuri.

IYO UMWARIMU AVUGA, umwarimukazi, umuhanga mu byo avuga, abamuteze amatwi basa nk’abakurikirana cyane, nk’aho bakurikiranira hafi ijambo ryose ry’umuvuzi, byose bitanga igitekerezo cy’uko bari gutega amatwi, ko bari maso cyane, ariko mu mutima wa buri muntu hari umunyamabanga uhindura ijambo ryose ry’umuvuzi.

UYU MUNYAMABANGA NI NJYE, NDI NJYE, NDI NJYE UBWANJYE. Akazi k’uwo munyamabanga ni ugusobanura nabi, guhindura nabi amagambo y’umuvuzi.

NJYE mpindura ukurikije ibitekerezo byanjye, ibitekerezo byanjye, ubwoba bwanjye, ubwibone bwanjye, impungenge zanjye, ibitekerezo byanjye, ibyo nibuka, n’ibindi, n’ibindi.

Abanyeshuri ku ishuri, abanyeshurikazi, abantu bose hamwe bagize abumviriza, mu by’ukuri ntibari kumva umuvuzi, bari kwiyumva bo ubwabo, bumva EGO yabo bwite, EGO yabo bakunda cyane ya MAQUIAVÉLICO, ititeguye kwemera UKURI, UKURI, ICY'INGENZI.

Iyo turi maso gusa, dufite UBWONKO BWITEBUYE budafite umutwaro w’ibihe byashize, mu gihe cyo kwakira byuzuye, dushobora koko kumva tutagize uruhare rw’uwo munyamabanga mubi witwa NJYE, NDI NJYE, NDI NJYE UBWANJYE, EGO.

Iyo ubwenge bwangijwe n’ibyo bwibuka, busubiramo ibyo bwagiye bwegeranya.

Ubwenge bwangijwe n’ibintu byabaye kera, bushobora kureba ibiriho gusa binyuze mu ndorerwamo zanduye z’ibihe byashize.

NIBA DUSHYAKA KUMENYA GUTWEGA AMATWI, niba dushaka kwiga gutega amatwi kugira ngo tuvumbure ibishya, tugomba kubaho dukurikije filozofiya y’IGIHE GITO.

Ni ngombwa kubaho umwanya ku wundi tudahangayikishijwe n’ibihe byashize, kandi nta mishinga y’igihe kizaza.

UKURI ni ibitazwi umwanya ku wundi, ubwenge bwacu bugomba guhora bwiteguye, dufite amaso yose, twibohoye ibitekerezo, ibitekerezo, kugira ngo tubashe kwakira by’ukuri.

Abigisha n’Abigishakazi b’ishuri bagomba kwigisha abanyeshuri babo icyo bisobanuye byimbitse kumenya gutega amatwi.

Ni ngombwa kwiga kubaho mu bwenge, gushimangira ibyiyumvo byacu, gutunganya imyitwarire yacu, ibitekerezo byacu, ibyiyumvo byacu.

Nta cyo bimaze kugira umuco mwiza wo mu ishuri, niba tutazi gutega amatwi, niba tudashoboye kuvumbura ibishya umwanya ku wundi.

Dukeneye gutunganya amaso yacu, gutunganya imyitwarire yacu, gutunganya abantu bacu, ibintu, n’ibindi, n’ibindi.

Ntibishoboka kuba intyoza by’ukuri iyo tutazi gutega amatwi.

Ubwenge bubi, bubi, bwangiritse, bwateshejwe agaciro ntibuzi gutega amatwi, ntibuzi kuvumbura ibishya, ubwo bwenge busobanukirwa gusa, busobanukirwa gusa mu buryo butari bwo ubuhinduzi budafite ishingiro bw’uwo munyamabanga wa shitani witwa NJYE, NDI NJYE, EGO.

Kuba intyoza ni ikintu kigoye cyane kandi gisaba amaso yose. Umuntu ashobora kuba umuntu w’intyoza cyane mu buryo, imyenda, imyenda, ubusitani, imodoka, ubucuti, ariko akomeza kuba mubi, umunyamaswa, uremereye mu mutima.

Uzi kubaho umwanya ku wundi, mu by’ukuri agenda mu nzira yo gutyoza by’ukuri.

Ufite ubwenge bwakira, bwiteguye, bwuzuye, buteguye, agenda inzira yo gutyoza nyakuri.

Ukingurira ibishya byose areka umutwaro w’ibihe byashize, ibitekerezo, ibitekerezo, inzika, ubunyamaswa, n’ibindi, agenda yishimye mu nzira yo gutyoza byemewe.

Ubwenge bwateshejwe agaciro bubaho bupfunywe mu bihe byashize, mu bitekerezo, ubwibone, kwikunda, ibitekerezo, n’ibindi, n’ibindi.

Ubwenge bwateshejwe agaciro ntibuzi kubona ibishya, ntibuzi gutega amatwi, bwangijwe no KWIKUNDA.

Abayoboke ba MARXISME-LENINISME ntibemera ibishya; ntibemera ICYICIRO cya kane cy’ibintu byose, IGICE cya kane, kubera kwikunda, bakunda cyane bo ubwabo, bifatanye n’inyigisho zabo z’ubupfumu zidafite ishingiro kandi iyo tubashyize mu by’ukuri, iyo tubagaragarije ubusazi bw’ubuhanga bwabo, bazamura ukuboko kw’ibumoso, bareba amasakoshi y’isaha yabo, batanga urwitwazo rwo kwihunza maze baragenda.

Ubwo ni ubwenge bwateshejwe agaciro, ubwenge bwacitse intege butazi gutega amatwi, butazi kuvumbura ibishya, butemera ukuri kuko bupfunywe mu KWIKUNDA. Ubwenge bukunda cyane ubwabo, ubwenge butazi GUTYOZWA UMUCYO, ubwenge bubi, ubwenge bubi, bwumva gusa EGO yabo bakunda.

UBUREZI BW'INGENZI bwiga gutega amatwi, bwiga kubaho mu bwenge.

Abigisha n’Abigishakazi b’amashuri, amakoleji, kaminuza bagomba kwigisha abanyeshuri babo inzira nyakuri yo gutyoza ubuzima by’ukuri.

Nta cyo bimaze kumara imyaka icumi na cumi n’itanu twinjiye mu mashuri, amakoleji na kaminuza, niba dusohotse imbere turi ingurube nyakuri mu bitekerezo byacu, ibitekerezo byacu, ibyiyumvo byacu n’imigenzo yacu.

Hakenewe UBUREZI BW'INGENZI mu buryo bwihutirwa kuko ibisekuru bishya bisobanura intangiriro y’igihe gishya.

Igihe kirageze cyo GUHINDUKA K'UKURI, igihe kirageze cyo GUHINDUKA KW'INGENZI.

Ibihe byashize ni ibihe byashize kandi byatanze imbuto zabyo. Dukeneye gusobanukirwa neza icyo umwanya tubayemo usobanura.
