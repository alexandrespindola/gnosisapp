Uburezi BW'INGENZI mu buryo bwabwo nyabwo ni ukwisobanukirwa ubwawe byimbitse; muri buri muntu harimo amategeko yose y'umuremyi.

Ushaka kumenya ibitangaza byose by'umuremyi, agomba kubyiga muri we ubwe.

Uburezi bw'ibinyoma bushyira imbere gusa gutunga ubwenge kandi buri wese yabikora. Biragaragara ko amafaranga atuma buri wese yishimira kugura ibitabo.

Ntiturwanya umuco w'ubwenge, turwanya gusa umururumba wo kwegeranya ibitekerezo.

Uburezi bw'ibinyoma bwo mu bwenge butanga uburyo bwo kwihunga.

Umugabo wese wize, umunyabwenge wese w'umunyabinyoma, ahora afite uburyo bwiza bwo kwihunga.

UBUBWENGE BWA KIMUNTU BUDATANYE N'UMWUKA BUJYANA KU BURIGANYI KANDI ABA BO BATUMYE ABANTU BAGWA MU MUVURUNGANO NO MU IRIMBUKA.

Ikoranabuhanga ntiryatuma twimenya ubwacu mu buryo BWUZUYE.

Ababyeyi bohereza abana babo ku ishuri, muri koleji, muri kaminuza, muri politekinike, n'ibindi, kugira ngo bige ikoranabuhanga, kugira ngo bagire umwuga, kugira ngo bashobore kubona imibereho.

Biragaragara ko dukeneye kumenya ikoranabuhanga, kugira umwuga, ariko ibyo ni ibintu bya kabiri, ikintu cya mbere, ikintu cy'ingenzi, ni ukwimenya, kumenya abo turi bo, aho tuvuye, aho tujya, intego y'ubuzima bwacu.

Mu buzima harimo byose, ibyishimo, agahinda, urukundo, umubabaro, ibinezeza, ububabare, ubwiza, isura mbi, n'ibindi. Iyo tuzi kubaho byimbitse, iyo tubyumva mu NGERO zose z'ubwenge, tubona umwanya wacu mu muryango, turema ikoranabuhanga ryacu bwite, uburyo bwacu bwihariye bwo kubaho, kumva no gutekereza, ariko ibinyuranye ni ibinyoma ijana ku ijana, ikoranabuhanga ubwaryo, ntirishobora gutuma habaho kumva byimbitse, kumva nyakuri.

Uburezi bwa none bwagaragaye ko ari ugutsindwa gukomeye kubera ko buha agaciro gakabije ikoranabuhanga, umwuga kandi biragaragara ko gushimangira ikoranabuhanga bihindura umuntu kuba ikintu kigendanwa, bikangiza amahirwe ye meza.

Guhinga ubushobozi n'ubushobozi budafite kumva ubuzima, hatariho kwimenya, hatariho kumva neza uko NDIMO, hatariho kwiga neza uburyo bwo gutekereza, kumva, kwifuza no gukora, bizatuma gusa twongera ubugome bwacu bwite, ubwikunde bwacu bwite, ibyo bintu bya Psikoroji biteza intambara, inzara, ubukene, ububabare.

Iterambere ryonyine ry'ikoranabuhanga ryatumye habaho abakanishi, abahanga, abahanga mu by'ikoranabuhanga, abahanga mu bya fiziki atomike, abahanga mu by'ikoranabuhanga bakorera ku nyamaswa z'abakene, abahanga mu by'intwaro zisenya, n'ibindi, n'ibindi, n'ibindi.

Abo banyamwuga bose, abo bahanga bose mu by'ibisasu bya Atome n'ibisasu bya Hydrogène, abo bahanga bose mu by'ikoranabuhanga bakorera ku binyabuzima byo mu muryango, abo bariganya bose, ikintu cyonyine bakorera, ni intambara n'irimbuka.

Abo bariganya bose ntacyo bazi, ntacyo bumva ku buzima bwose mu buryo bwabwo bwose butagira iherezo.

Iterambere rusange ry'ikoranabuhanga, uburyo bwo gutwara abantu, imashini zibara, amashanyarazi, imashini zizamura abantu mu nyubako, ubwonko bw'ikoranabuhanga bw'ubwoko bwose, n'ibindi, bikemura ibibazo ibihumbi bikorerwa ku rwego rwo hejuru rw'ubuzima, ariko byinjiza mu muntu no mu muryango, ibibazo byinshi byagutse kandi byimbitse.

Kubaho gusa ku RWANDYO RWO HEJURU tutita ku butaka butandukanye n'uturere twimbitse kurushaho mu bwenge, bivuze ko duhamagarira kuri twe no ku bana bacu, ubukene, amarira no kwiheba.

Ikenewe cyane, ikibazo cyihutirwa cya buri MWENE GIHUGU, cya buri muntu, ni ukumva ubuzima mu buryo bwayo BWUZUYE, kuko ari bwo dushobora gukemura neza ibibazo byacu byose byihariye.

Ubumenyi bwo mu by'ikoranabuhanga ubwaryo ntibushobora gukemura ibibazo byacu byose bya Psikoroji, ibibazo byacu byimbitse.

Niba dushaka kuba ABANTU nyakuri, DUSHOBORA KWIYITEGEKA BYA PSIKOROJIA, tukimenya ubwacu byimbitse mu turere twose tw'ibitekerezo, kuko IKORANABUHANGA riri hanze y'ugushidikanya kose, rihinduka igikoresho cyangiza, iyo TUTUMVA neza inzira yose y'ubuzima, iyo tutimenya ubwacu mu buryo BWUZUYE.

Iyo INYAMASWA Y'UBWENGE ikunda neza, iyo yimenya ubwayo, iyo imenye inzira yose y'ubuzima ntiyari gukora icyaha cyo GUSENGA ATOME.

Iterambere ryacu mu by'ikoranabuhanga ni ryiza ariko ryatumye gusa twongera imbaraga zacu zo gutera abandi no kwirimbura kandi hirya no hino hateye ubwoba, inzara, ubujiji n'indwara.

Nta mwuga, nta ikoranabuhanga rishobora kuduha ibyo bita UBUZIMA BWUZUYE, UMUNEZERO NYAKURI.

Buri wese mu buzima arababazwa cyane mu kazi ke, mu mwuga we, mu buzima bwe busanzwe kandi ibintu n'imirimo bihinduka ibikoresho by'ishyari, kunegura, urwango, umujinya.

Isi y'abaganga, isi y'abanyabugeni, abahinzi, abavoka, n'ibindi, buri imwe muri izo si, yuzuye ububabare, kunegura, guhangana, ishyari, n'ibindi.

Hatariho kwisobanukirwa ubwacu gusa, umurimo, akazi cyangwa umwuga, bitujyana ku bubabare no gushaka uko twihunga. Bamwe bashaka uburyo bwo guhunga binyuze mu nzoga, akabari, inzu y'imbyino, abandi bashaka guhunga binyuze mu biyobyabwenge, morfine, cocaïne, marijuana n'abandi binyuze mu busambanyi no kwangirika, ibitsina, n'ibindi, n'ibindi.

Iyo dushaka kugabanya UBUZIMA bwose mu ikoranabuhanga, mu mwuga, mu buryo bwo gukorera amafaranga menshi, ibisubizo ni ukuruhuka, umunaniro no gushaka uko twihunga.

Tugomba guhinduka ABANTU BUZUYEMO, byuzuye kandi ibyo bishoboka gusa twimenya ubwacu no gukuraho UBWONKO BWA PSIKOROJIA.

UBUZIMA BW'INGENZI mu gihe bukangurira kwiga ikoranabuhanga ryo kubona imibereho, bugomba gukora ikintu cy'ingenzi kurushaho, bugomba gufasha umuntu, kugerageza, kumva mu buryo bwose no mu turere twose tw'ubwenge, inzira y'ubuzima.

Niba hari umuntu ufite icyo avuga avuge kandi ibyo byo kubivuga birashimishije cyane kuko buri wese aremera ubwe uburyo bwe bwite, ariko yiga uburyo bw'abandi atabanje kubona ubuzima mu buryo bwayo BWUZUYE; biganisha gusa ku kwisanzura.
