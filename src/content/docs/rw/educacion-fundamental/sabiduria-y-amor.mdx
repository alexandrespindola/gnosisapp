UBUHANGA n'URUKUNDO ni inkingi zikomeye z'umuryango nyakuri wose.

Ku isahani imwe y'umunzani w'ubutabera, tugomba gushyiraho UBUHANGA, ku yindi sahani tugomba gushyiraho URUKUNDO.

Ubuhanga n'Urukundo bigomba gushyirwa mu rwego rumwe. Ubuhanga budafite Urukundo ni ikintu cyangiza. Urukundo rudafite Ubuhanga rushobora kutuyobora mu makosa "URUKUNDO NI ITEGEKO ARIKO URUKUNDO RUZI ICYO RUKORA".

Birakenewe kwiga cyane no kugira ubumenyi, ariko kandi ni IHUTIRWA guteza imbere muri twe UBUGINGO BW'UMWUKA.

Ubumenyi budafite UBUGINGO BW'UMWUKA butezwe imbere neza mu buryo buhuje muri twe, biza kuba intandaro y'ibyo bita UBUTIRANYI.

UBUGINGO butezwe imbere neza muri twe ariko budafite ubumenyi bw'ubwenge bw'ubwoko ubwo aribwo bwose, butera Intungane z'injiji.

Intungane y'injiji ifite UBUGINGO BW'UMWUKA bwateye imbere cyane, ariko kubera ko idafite ubumenyi bw'ubwenge, ntishobora kugira icyo ikora kuko itazi uko yabikora.

INTUNGANE y'injiji ifite ubushobozi bwo Gukora ariko ntishobora gukora kuko itazi uko yabikora.

Ubumenyi bw'ubwenge budafite UBUGINGO BW'UMWUKA butezwe imbere neza butera urujijo mu bwenge, ubugome, ubwibone, n'ibindi, n'ibindi.

Mu gihe cy'Intambara ya Kabiri y'Isi Yose, abahanga ibihumbi n'ibihumbi batari bafite ikintu cyose cy'Umwuka mu izina rya siyansi n'ubumuntu, bakoze ibyaha biteye ubwoba bagamije gukora ubushakashatsi bwa siyansi.

Dukeneye kwihangira umuco ukomeye w'ubwenge ariko ushyizwe mu rwego rumwe n'Ubumana nyakuri buzwi.

Dukeneye AMAHAME SHINGIRO Y'IMPINDUKA n'INDWARA ZO MU MUTWE Z'IMPINDUKA niba dushaka by'ukuri gusesa JYE kugira ngo duteze imbere UBUGINGO bwemewe byemewe muri twe.

Birababaje kubera kubura URUKUNDO abantu bakoresha UBWA BWO mu buryo bubi.

Abanyeshuri b'abahungu n'abakobwa bakeneye kwiga siyansi, amateka, imibare, n'ibindi, n'ibindi.

Birakenewe kugira ubumenyi bw'umwuga, hagamijwe kugirira abaturanyi akamaro.

Kwiga birakenewe. Gukusanya ubumenyi bw'ibanze birakomeye, ariko ubwoba ntibukomeye.

Abantu benshi bakusanya ubumenyi kubera ubwoba; batinya ubuzima, urupfu, inzara, ubukene, icyo abantu bazavuga, n'ibindi, kandi ni yo mpamvu biga.

Umuntu agomba kwiga kubera Urukundo akunda abo bagenzi be afite icyifuzo cyo kubakorera neza, ariko ntazigere na rimwe yiga kubera ubwoba.

Mu buzima busanzwe twashoboye kugaragaza ko abanyeshuri bose biga kubera ubwoba, vuba cyangwa se kera bahinduka abanyamabanga.

Dukeneye gushyira mu gaciro n'ubwacu kugira ngo twitegereze kandi tuvumbure muri twe inzira zose z'ubwoba.

Ntitwagomba kwigera twibagirwa mu buzima ko ubwoba bufite ibyiciro byinshi. Rimwe na rimwe ubwoba bwitiranywa n'ubutwari. Abasirikare ku rugamba basa n'intwari cyane ariko mu by'ukuri baragenda kandi bakarwana bitewe n'ubwoba. Umwiyahuzi na we ku isura ya mbere asa n'intwari cyane ariko mu by'ukuri ni umunyabwoba utinya ubuzima.

Umunyamabanga wese mu buzima asa n'intwari cyane ariko mu nsi ye ni umunyabwoba. Abanyamabanga bakunze gukoresha umwuga n'ubutegetsi mu buryo bubi iyo bafite ubwoba. Urugero; Castro RÃºa; muri Cuba.

Ntituzigera twivuguruza ku byerekeye ubunararibonye bw'ubuzima busanzwe cyangwa se guhinga ubwenge, ariko turamagana kubura URUKUNDO.

Ubumenyi n'ubunararibonye bw'ubuzima bigira ingaruka mbi iyo habuze URUKUNDO.

EGO ikunze gufata ubunararibonye n'ubumenyi bw'ubwenge iyo habayeho kutabaho kw'ibyo bita URUKUNDO.

EGO ikoresha nabi ubunararibonye n'ubwenge iyo ibikoresha kugira ngo yiyubake.

Tuvana EGO, JYE, NJYE UBWANJYE, ubunararibonye n'Ubwenge bisigara mu maboko ya SER INTIMO kandi iyicarubozo ryose rihita ridashoboka.

Umweshuri wese agomba kuyoborwa n'inzira y'umwuga we kandi akiga cyane inyigisho zose zijyanye n'umwuga we.

Kwiga, ubwenge, ntibigirira nabi umuntu uwo ari we wese mas ntugomba gukoresha nabi ubwenge.

Dukeneye kwiga kugira ngo tutagira ibyo dukoresha nabi ubwenge. Ukoresha nabi ubwenge ni ushaka kwiga inyigisho z'imirimo itandukanye, ushaka kugirira abandi nabi abifashishije ubwenge, ukoresha ingufu ku bwenge bw'abandi, n'ibindi.

Birakenewe kwiga amasomo y'umwuga n'amasomo y'umwuka kugira ngo ugire ubwenge bushyize mu gaciro.

NI IHUTIRWA kugera kuri SINTESI y'ubwenge no kuri sintesi y'Umwuka niba dushaka by'ukuri ubwenge bushyize mu gaciro.

Abarimu n'abigisha bo mu Mashuri, amakoleji, za Kaminuza, n'ibindi, bagomba kwiga neza Indwara zo mu Mutwe z'Impinduka zacu niba dushaka by'ukuri kuyobora abanyeshuri babo mu nzira y'IMPINDUKA Z'UMUSINGI.

Birakenewe ko abanyeshuri bagira UBUGINGO BW'UMWUKA, bakazamura muri bo UBUGINGO BW'UKURI, kugira ngo bave mu Ishuri bahindutse abantu barangwa n'inshingano kandi batari ABAVUMBUZI b'injiji.

Nta cyo bimaze Ubuhanga budafite Urukundo. Ubwenge budafite Urukundo butera gusa Abambuzi.

Ubuhanga ubwabwo ni Ikintu kigize Atome, umurwa mukuru wa Atome ugomba gucungwa n'abantu buzuye Urukundo nyakuri gusa.
