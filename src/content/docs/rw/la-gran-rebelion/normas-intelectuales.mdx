Mu buzima busanzwe, buri muntu wese afite uko abona ibintu, uburyo bwe bwo gutekereza bwacitse intege, kandi ntiyigeze yihugura ku bintu bishya; ibi ntibishidikanywaho, ntibihakana, ntibihinyuzwa.

Ubwenge bw’umuntu ufite ubwenge bwarangiritse, bwarangiritse, buri mu gihe cyo gusubira inyuma.

Mu by’ukuri, imyumvire y’ikiremwamuntu muri iki gihe isa n’inyubako ishaje idafite akamaro kandi idashobora kugira ibintu by’ukuri by’umwimerere.

Ubwenge burabuze, bwashyizwe mu mategeko menshi akomeye kandi adakurikizwa.

Buri wese afite uko abona ibintu n'amategeko akomeye agenderaho akora ibikorwa kandi akabyitwaramo ubudahwema.

Ikibabaje kurusha ibindi muri iki kibazo ni uko abantu babarirwa muri za miriyoni bafite ibitekerezo bingana n’amategeko aborera kandi adafite ishingiro.

Uko biri kose, abantu ntibigera bumva ko bibeshye, buri mutwe ni isi kandi nta gushidikanya ko mu mfuruka nyinshi z’ubwenge harimo amayeri menshi yo gukwirakwiza no gupfobya bidashoboka.

Ariko ibitekerezo bigufi by’abantu benshi ntibikeka na gato igihombo cyo mu bwenge barimo.

Aba bantu bo muri iki gihe bafite ubwonko bw’inyenzi bibatekerezaho ibyiza, bibwira ko ari abanyaliberali, ibihangange, bibwira ko bafite ibitekerezo byagutse cyane.

Abajiji bigize abahanga biragoye cyane, kuko mu by’ukuri, tuvugiye muri iki gihe mu buryo bwa Gikora, twavuga tuti: "ntabwo bazi gusa, ahubwo, banirengagiza ko batazi."

Ibigoryi byo mu bwenge byifatanije n’ayo mategeko ya kera yo mu gihe cyashize bikora ibikorwa by’urugomo biturutse ku gihombo cyabo kandi bakanga mu buryo bukomeye kwemera ikintu icyo ari cyo cyose kidashobora kwinjira mu mategeko yabo y’icyuma.

Abahanga bigize abahanga batekereza ko ikintu cyose cyasohoka mu nzira ikomeye y’imikorere yabo y’umuhinzi kubera impamvu imwe cyangwa iyindi ari ubuswa ijana ku ijana. Muri ubwo buryo, abo bantu bakennye bafite ibitekerezo bigoye cyane barashukana mu buryo bubabaje.

Abahanga bigize abahanga bo muri iki gihe bibwira ko ari ibihangange, basuzugura abantu bafite agaciro ko kwitandukanya n’amategeko yabo yashaje, ikibi kurusha ibindi ni uko batakeka na gato ukuri kw’ubugoryi bwabo bwite.

Ubugome bwo mu bwenge bw’ubwenge bwa kera burangana ku buryo bujya kwifuza ko hagaragazwa ibintu nyabyo, ibintu bitari iby’ubwenge.

Abantu bafite imyumvire mike kandi idashobora kwihanganira ntibashaka kumva ko ubunararibonye bw’ukuri bwiza gusa iyo EGO itariho.

Nta gushidikanya ko bidashoboka rwose kumenya amayobera y’ubuzima n’urupfu mu gihe umutwe wacu w’imbere utarafunguka.

Ntibitwara byinshi gusubiramo muri iki gice ko ubwenge bw’ikirenga gusa bwa SER bushobora kumenya ukuri.

Ubwenge bw’imbere bushobora gukora gusa hamwe n’amakuru yatanzwe n’ubwenge bwa Cosmic bwa SER.

Ubwenge bwigenga, hamwe na dialectique yayo ishingiye ku bimenyetso, ntacyo bushobora kumenya kuri ibyo bintu birenga ububasha bwayo.

Turabizi ko ibitekerezo by’ibirimo dialectique ishingiye ku bimenyetso bikorwa hamwe n’amakuru yatanzwe n’ibyumviro byo hanze.

Abantu bafungiwe mu mikorere yabo yo mu bwenge n’amategeko ahamye, bahora barwanya ibyo bitekerezo bishya.

Gusa gusesa EGO mu buryo bukomeye kandi ntakuka birashoboka gukangura ubwenge no gufungura umutwe w’imbere.

Ariko, kubera ko aya magambo mashya atajyanye n’uburyo bwemewe, cyangwa n’uburyo bwa dialectique, imyitwarire y’ubwenge isubira inyuma irarwanya cyane.

Abo bantu bakennye bo mu bwenge bashaka kwinjiza inyanja mu kirahuri, bibwira ko kaminuza ishobora kugenzura ubwenge bwose bw’isanzure kandi ko amategeko yose ya Cosmos ategetswe kugandukira amategeko yabo ya kera ya akademiki.

Ntibakeka na gato abo bakorera ubushake, ibyitegererezo by’ubwenge, uko bangiritse.

Rimwe na rimwe abo bantu barazamuka akanya gato iyo baje mu isi ya Esoteriste, ariko vuba bakazimira nk’umuriro, bakabura mu buryo bw’impungenge zo mu buryo bw’umwuka, bagamishwa n’ubwenge kandi bakabura burundu.

Ubusanzwe bw’ubwenge ntibushobora kwinjira mu ndiba yemewe ya SER, ariko imikorere yo mu buryo bwo gushyira mu gaciro ishobora gutuma abapfapfa bagera ku myanzuro yose y’indashyikirwa ariko idafite ishingiro.

Imbaraga zo gutanga ibitekerezo byumvikana ntibisobanura uburambe bwukuri.

Umukino uteye ishema wa dialectique ishingiye ku bimenyetso, wiyemeza gukurura uwiyumvira, agahora yitiranya injangwe n’urukwavu.

Uruhererekane rw’ibitekerezo birabagirana rutangaza igihebe cyo mu bwenge kandi rukamuha kwigenga ku buryo bufite ishingiro ku buryo yanga ibintu byose byumva impumuro y’umukungugu w’amasomero n’umuti w’ikaramu wa kaminuza.

"Delirium tremens" y’abasinzi b’inzoga ifite ibimenyetso bitazibagirana, ariko iby’abasinzi b’inyigisho biroroshye kwitiranya n’ubuhanga.

Tugeze kuri iki gice cy’igice cyacu, twavuga ko mu by’ukuri bigoye kumenya aho ubwenge bw’ibihebe burangirira naho gusara bitangirira.

Mu gihe dukomeje gufungirwa mu mategeko abora kandi ashaje y’ubwenge, bizaba birenga ubunararibonye bw’ibyo bitari iby’ubwenge, iby’igihe, ibyo aribyo.
