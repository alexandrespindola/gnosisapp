Umuntu ni uko ubuzima bwe bumeze: niba umuntu adakora ku buzima bwe bwite, aba atakaza igihe mu buryo bubabaje.

Tuvanyeho gusa ibintu bitifuzwa twikoreye imbere muri twe, dushobora guhindura ubuzima bwacu igihangano.

Urupfu ni ukugaruka ku itangiriro ry'ubuzima, hamwe n'ubushobozi bwo kubisubiramo bundi bushya ku rubyiniro rw'ubuzima bushya.

Amashuri atandukanye y'ubwoko bwa pseudo-esoteriste na pseudo-occultiste ashyigikira inyigisho zihoraho z'ubuzima bukurikirana, igitekerezo nk'iki kiribeshya.

Ubuzima ni filime; iyo kwerekana birangiye, dufunga kasete mu mwobo wayo maze tukayitwara iteka.

Kugaruka birahari, kugaruka birahari; mu kugaruka muri iyi si tugaragaza kuri tapi ya noneho filime imwe, ubuzima bumwe.

Dushobora gushingira thesis ku buzima bukurikirana; ariko ntabwo ari ubuzima bukurikirana kuko filime ari imwe.

Ikiremwa muntu gifite esanse y'ubuntu ingana na 3% na 97% by'esanse ifunzwe hagati y'ubundi bwawe.

Mu kugaruka 3% by'ubutware bw'ubuntu bwuzuzwa burundu mu kintu cyarumbye; ntibishidikanywaho ko dukomeza mu mbuto z'abazadukomokaho.

Ubumuntu buratandukanye; nta ejo hazaza kubera ubumuntu bw'umuntu wapfuye; ubu bwa nyuma bukomeza gushonga buhoro buhoro mu irimbi.

Mu mwana ucyivuka niho hagarukira gusa ku gipimo gito cy'ubutware bw'ubuntu; ibi biha ikiremwa ubwenge bwite n'ubwiza bw'imbere.

Ubwundi bwawe butandukanye bugenda buzenguruka umwana ucyivuka, buragenda buraza mu bwisanzure ahantu hose, bwifuza kwinjira imbere mu mashini y'umubiri ariko ntibishoboka mu gihe ubumuntu bushya butararemwa.

Bikwiye kumenya ko ubumuntu ari ingufu kandi ko bushyirwaho n'uburambe mu gihe.

Byanditswe ko ubumuntu bugomba kuremwa mu myaka irindwi ya mbere y'ubwana kandi ko nyuma bukomeza kandi bugakomezwa n'imyitozo.

Ubwundi bwawe butangiye kwinjira imbere mu mashini y'umubiri buhoro buhoro uko ubumuntu bushya bugenda buremwa.

Urupfu ni ikurwaho ry'imibare, iyo igikorwa cy'imibare kirangiye ikintu gikomeza gusa ni agaciro (ibi ni ubwundi bwawe bwiza n'ububi, bw'ingirakamaro n'ubw'ubusa, bwiza n'ububi).

Agaciro mu rumuri rwa astral rikuranwa kandi rikabyanga hagati yaryo ukurikije amategeko y'iherezo ry'isi yose.

Turi ingingo z'imibare mu kirere dukoresha nk'imodoka ku ngero zimwe na zimwe z'agaciro.

Imbere mu bumuntu bw'umuntu wese muri twe hahora hari aka gaciro gakora nk'ishingiro ry'itegeko ryo Kugaruka.

Buri kintu cyose kigaruka kuba nk'uko cyabaye ariko umusaruro cyangwa ingaruka z'ibikorwa byacu byabanje.

Nk'uko imbere muri buri wese muri twe hari ubwundi bwawe bwinshi bw'ubuzima bwabanje, dushobora kwemeza mu buryo bukomeye ko buri wese muri abo ari umuntu utandukanye.

Ibi biratwemeza gusobanukirwa ko imbere muri buri wese muri twe hatuye abantu benshi bafite inshingano zitandukanye.

Imbere mu bumuntu bw'umujura hari isenga nyayo y'abajura; imbere mu bumuntu bw'umwicanyi hariho club yose y'abicanyi; imbere mu bumuntu bw'umuntu urarikira hari inzu yo guhuriramo; imbere mu bumuntu bw'indaya iyo ari yo yose hari indaro yose.

Buri umwe muri abo bantu twikoreye imbere mu bumuntu bwacu, afite ibibazo bye n'inshingano ze.

Abantu batuye imbere mu bantu, abantu batuye imbere mu bantu; ibi ntibyashidikanywaho, ntibihakana.

Ikintu gikomeye kuri ibi byose ni uko buri umwe muri abo bantu cyangwa ubwundi bwawe butuye imbere muri twe, bava mu buzima bwa kera kandi bafite inshingano zimwe na zimwe.

Ubwundi bwawe mu buzima bwashize bwagize urugendo rw'urukundo ku myaka mirongo itatu, mu buzima bushya buzategereza iyo myaka kugira ngo bugaragare kandi igihe kigeze buzashaka umuntu w'inzozi zabo, buze guhura nawe kuri terefoni maze amaherezo haboneke guhura no gusubiramo ishusho.

Ubwundi bwawe ku myaka mirongo ine bwagize impaka ku mutungo, mu buzima bushya buzategereza iyo myaka kugira ngo busubiremo amagambo amwe.

Ubwundi bwawe ku myaka makumyabiri n'itanu bwarwanye n'undi mugabo mu kabari, buzategereza mu buzima bushya imyaka makumyabiri n'itanu bundi kugira ngo bushake umubisha wabwo maze busubiremo ishyano.

Ubwundi bwawe bwa buri muntu bushakishwa hagati yabwo binyuze mu majwi ya terefoni maze nyuma bagahura kugira ngo basubiremo mu buryo bwa mekanike ibintu bimwe.

Iyi ni yo nyama y'itegeko ryo Kugaruka, iri ni ryo shyano ry'ubuzima.

Mu myaka ibihumbi n'ibihumbi abantu batandukanye bongera guhura kugira ngo basubiremo amakinamico amwe, ibiseko n'amashyano.

Umuntu ntabwo arenze imashini ikorera ubwundi bwawe bafite inshingano nyinshi.

Ikintu kibi kurusha ibi byose ni uko izo nshingano zose z'abantu twikoreye imbere muri twe zisohozwa nta bwenge bwacu bwabanje kugira amakuru ayo ari yo yose.

Ubumuntu bwacu bw'umuntu muri iyi ntera busa n'igare rikuruwe n'amafarashi menshi.

Hari ubuzima bwo gusubiramo neza, ubuzima bugenda busubiramo butagira icyo buhindura.

Mu buryo ubwo ari bwo bwose amakinamico, amashyano n'ibiseko by'ubuzima ntibyashobora gusubirwamo ku ishusho y'ubuzima, iyo abakinnyi batabaho.

Abakinnyi b'izo ntera zose ni ubwundi bwawe twikoreye imbere muri twe kandi buva mu buzima bwa kera.

Niba turimbuye ubwundi bwawe bw'uburakari, intera z'ishyano z'urugomo zirangira bitazuyaje.

Niba tugabanyije ikinyabiziga cyo mu isanzure abakozi b'ibanga ry'umururumba, ibibazo byabyo bizashira burundu.

Niba twarimbuye ubwundi bwawe bw'irari, intera z'indaro n'urwikekwe zirangira.

Niba duhinduye umuyonga abantu b'ibanga ry'ishari, ibikorwa byaryo bizashira burundu.

Niba twishe ubwundi bwawe bw'ubwibone, ubwibone, ubwiyemezi, kwiyemera, intera zisekeje z'aya makosa zizarangira kubera kubura abakinnyi.

Niba tuvanye mu bwonko bwacu ibintu by'ubunebwe, ubunebwe no kunanirwa, intera ziteye ubwoba z'ubu bwoko bw'amacosha ntizishobora gusubirwamo kubera kubura abakinnyi.

Niba duhinduye umuyonga ubwundi bwawe buca intege bw'ubugugu, ubukunzi, bizashira ibirori, ubusinzi, n'ibindi kubera kubura abakinnyi.

Nk'uko ubwundi bwawe bwinshi bw'agahinda bukorwa mu ntera zitandukanye z'ikiremwa, birakenewe kumenya impamvu zabyo, inkomoko yabyo n'inzira za Kristo zizadushuka amaherezo ku rupfu rw'umuntu wanjye ubwanjye no kubohoka kwanyuma.

Kwigana Kristo wa hafi, kwigana esoterismo ya Kristo ni ishingiro iyo bireba gutera muri twe ihinduka rikomeye kandi ritazibagirana; ibi ni byo tuziga mu bice bizaza.
