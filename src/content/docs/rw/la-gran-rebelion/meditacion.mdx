Mu buzima, ikintu cy'ingenzi ni impinduka ikomeye, yuzuye kandi ihoraho; ibindi byose ntabwo bifite akamaro na gato.

Kuzirikana ni ngombwa cyane iyo dushaka by'ukuri iyo mpinduka.

Ntabwo dushaka kuzirikana kudafite akamaro, gikeye kandi kw'ubusa.

Dukeneye kuba abantu bakomeye kandi tukareka ubupfapfa bwinshi bwiganje mu bumenyi bwa gihimbano no mu bwihisho buhendutse.

Ugomba kumenya uburyo bwo gukomera, ugomba kumenya guhinduka niba ushaka by'ukuri kudatsindwa mu murimo wa esoteric.

Utazi kuzirikana, umunyagitugu, injiji, ntazigera ashobora gukuraho Ego; azahora ari igiti kidafite imbaraga mu nyanja y'ubuzima irakaye.

Ikembe ryavumbuwe mu buzima busanzwe, rigomba gusobanukirwa neza binyuze mu ikoranabuhanga ryo kuzirikana.

Ibyigisho byo kuzirikana biboneka neza mu birori bitandukanye cyangwa ibihe bya buri munsi by'ubuzima busanzwe, ibi ntibishidikanywaho.

Abantu bahora bitotomba ku bintu bibabaje, ntibigera bamenya akamaro k'ibyo bintu.

Twebwe aho kwitotombera ibintu bibabaje, tugomba kubikuramo, binyuze mu kuzirikana, ibintu bifite akamaro ku mikurire y'ubugingo bwacu.

Kuzirikana ku buryo bwimbitse kuri iki cyangwa kiriya kintu gishimishije cyangwa kidashimishije, bituma twumva ubwacu uburyohe, umusaruro.

Ni ngombwa gukora itandukaniro ryuzuye ry'uburyohe mu mitekerereze hagati y'ibyo ari byo uburyohe bw'akazi n'uburyohe bw'ubuzima.

Muri byose, kugirango wumve wowe ubwawe uburyohe bw'akazi, bisaba guhindura burundu imyifatire ukurikiza bisanzwe ku mimerere y'ubuzima.

Ntawe washobora kuryoherwa n'uburyohe bw'akazi igihe cyose yakora ikosa ryo kwishyira mu myanya y'ibintu bitandukanye.

Mu by'ukuri kwishyira mu myanya y'abandi bibuza isuzuma rikwiye ryo mu mutwe ry'ibintu.

Iyo umuntu yishyize mu myanya y'iki cyangwa kiriya kintu cyabaye, ntabwo abasha kubikuramo ibintu bifite akamaro mu kwivumbura no gukuza imbere imitekerereze.

Umukozi wa Esoteriste usubiye mu kwishyira mu myanya y'abandi nyuma yo gutakaza ubwirinzi, yongera kumva uburyohe bw'ubuzima aho kumva uburyohe bw'akazi.

Ibi byerekana ko imyifatire yo mu mutwe yahinduwe mbere, yasubiye mu kwishyira mu myanya y'abandi.

Ikintu cyose kidashimishije kigomba kongera kubakwa hifashishijwe ibitekerezo bizima binyuze mu ikoranabuhanga ryo kuzirikana.

Kugarura ishusho iyo ari yo yose bituma twisuzuma ubwacu kandi mu buryo butaziguye uburyo abantu batandukanye bitabira muri yo.

Urugero: Ishusho y'ishyari ry'urukundo; muri yo harimo abantu bo mu burakari, ishyari ndetse n'urwango.

Gusobanukirwa buri muntu muri bo, buri kintu muri ibyo, bishimangira mu by'ukuri gutekereza cyane, kwibanda, kuzirikana.

Ingeso ikomeye yo gushinja abandi ni inzitizi, imbogamizi zo gusobanukirwa amakosa yacu bwite.

Kubwamahirwe, biragoye cyane gukuraho muri twe ingeso yo gushinja abandi.

Mu izina ry'ukuri tugomba kuvuga ko turi twe bonyine dufite icyaha ku mimerere itandukanye idashimishije y'ubuzima.

Ibirori bitandukanye bishimishije cyangwa bibabaje bibaho hamwe natwe cyangwa tutariho kandi bikisubiramo mu buryo bwa gimekanike.

Dushingiye kuri iri hame, nta kibazo gishobora kugira umuti wa burundu.

Ibibazo ni iby'ubuzima kandi iyo habaho umuti wa burundu, ubuzima ntibwaba ari ubuzima ahubwo bwari urupfu.

Noneho hashobora kubaho guhindura imimerere n'ibibazo, ariko ntibizigera bireka kwisubiramo kandi ntibizigera bigira umuti wa burundu.

Ubuzima ni uruziga ruzenguruka mu buryo bwa gimekanike hamwe n'imimerere yose ishimishije n'idashimishije, ihora isubiramo.

Ntidushobora guhagarika uruziga, imimerere myiza cyangwa mibi ihora itunganywa mu buryo bwa gimekanike, dushobora gusa guhindura imyifatire yacu ku bintu by'ubuzima.

Uko tugenda tumenya gukuramo ibikoresho byo kuzirikana mu mimerere y'ubuzima ubwaho, tuzagenda twivumbura.

Mu mimerere iyo ari yo yose ishimishije cyangwa idashimishije hariho abantu batandukanye bagomba gusobanukirwa byuzuye hakoreshejwe ikoranabuhanga ryo kuzirikana.

Ibi bivuze ko itsinda iryo ari ryo ryose ry'abantu bitabira iyi cyangwa iriya kinamico, ibitwenge cyangwa ibyago by'ubuzima busanzwe, nyuma yo gusobanukirwa byuzuye bagomba gukurwaho binyuze mu mbaraga za Nyina w'Imana Kundalini.

Uko tugenda dukoresha ubwenge bwo kwitegereza imitekerereze, ubu bwa nyuma nabwo buzagenda butera imbere mu buryo butangaje. Noneho tuzashobora kumenya imbere atari abantu mbere yo gukorwa, ahubwo no mugihe cy'akazi kose.

Iyo aba bantu bacibwe imitwe bakanatandukanywa, twumva turuhutse cyane, twishimye cyane.
