Ni byihutirwa, bidatinze, bitasubikwa, kwitegereza ibiganiro by'imbere n'aho bituruka neza.

Nta gushidikanya ko ibiganiro by'imbere bibi ari "Intandaro y'Intandaro" y'imibereho myinshi yo mu mutwe itari myiza kandi itanezeza mu gihe cya none no mu gihe kizaza.

Biragaragara ko ayo magambo y'ubusa adafite ishingiro y'ibiganiro biteye urujijo kandi muri rusange ibiganiro byose biteye akaga, byangiza, bidafite ishingiro, bigaragara mu isi yo hanze, bifite inkomoko mu biganiro by'imbere bibi.

Birazwi ko muri Gnosis habaho umuco wa esoteric wo guceceka imbere; ibi bizwi n'abigishwa bacu ba "Chambre ya Gatatu".

Ntibitangaje kuvuga neza ko guceceka imbere bigomba kwerekezwa ku kintu cyihariye kandi kigaragara.

Iyo inzira yo gutekereza irangiye nkana mugihe cyo kuzirikana imbere mu buryo bwimbitse, guceceka imbere kuragerwaho; ariko ntabwo aribyo dushaka gusobanura muri iki gice.

"Kweguza ubwenge" cyangwa "kubushyira mu cyobo" kugira ngo ugerweho koko guceceka imbere, ntabwo aribyo tugerageza gusobanura ubu muri iyi mirongo.

Gukora guceceka imbere tuvuga, ntibisobanura kandi kubuza ikintu kwinjira mu bwenge.

Mubyukuri turimo kuvuga ubwoko bwoguceceka imbere butandukanye cyane. Ntabwo ari ikintu kidasobanutse muri rusange ...

Turashaka gukora guceceka imbere bijyanye nikintu cyamaze kuba mubwenge, umuntu, ibyabaye, ikibazo cyawe cyangwa icyabandi, ibyo batubwiye, ibyo kanaka yakoze, nibindi, ariko tutagikozeho nururimi rwo imbere, nta biganiro bya hafi ...

Kwiga guceceka ntabwo ari gusa nururimi rwo hanze, ahubwo kandi, byongeye kandi, nururimi rwibanga, imbere, biratangaje, biratangaje.

Benshi baraceceka hanze, ariko nururimi rwabo rwo imbere baratera abaturanyi babo ari bazima. Ibiganiro by'imbere by'uburozi kandi bibi, bitera urujijo rwo imbere.

Niba witegereje ibiganiro by'imbere bibi, uzabona ko bigizwe n'ukuri gucyeya, cyangwa ukuri guhurirana mu buryo bukwiye cyangwa butari bwo, cyangwa ikintu cyongeweho cyangwa cyaretswe.

Ikibabaje ni uko ubuzima bwacu bwiyumvo bushingiye gusa ku "kwikunda".

Byongeyeho kwihemukira gukabije, twishimira gusa ubwacu, "Ego dukunda" cyane, kandi tukagira urwikekwe ndetse tukanga abatari kumwe natwe.

Turakundana cyane, turi abanebwe ijana ku ijana, ibi ntibishidikanywaho, ntibishobora guhakana

Mugihe dukomeje gucucuma mu "kwikunda", iterambere ryose ry'Ikiremwa, riba ikintu kirenze ubushobozi.

Dukeneye kwiga kubona ibitekerezo by'abandi. Ni ngombwa kumenya kwishyira mu mwanya w'abandi.

"Bityo rero, ibintu byose mushaka ko abantu babagirira, abe ari ko namwe mubagirira." (Matayo: VII, 12)

Icy'ingenzi koko muri izi nyigisho ni uburyo abantu bitwara imbere mu buryo butagaragara.

Ikibabaje kandi nubwo twaba twita kubandi cyane, ndetse tukaba inyangamugayo rimwe na rimwe, ntakabuza ko mu buryo butagaragara kandi bwimbere tubana nabi cyane.

Abantu basa nabagira neza cyane, buri munsi bakurura abantu babo babi mu buvumo bwabo bw'ibanga, kugira ngo babakorere ibyo bashaka byose. (Gukubita, gusuzugura, gukwena, n'ibindi)
