Mu buzima busanzwe, duhora twisanga mu bitandukanye bidutangaza. Abantu bakize bafite amazu meza n'inshuti nyinshi, rimwe na rimwe barababara cyane... Abakozi baciriritse bakora imirimo ivunanye cyangwa abantu bo mu rwego rwo hagati, bakunze kubaho bishimye rwose.

Abantu benshi bafite amamiliyoni menshi barwaye indwara yo kutagira ubushake bwo gukora imibonano mpuzabitsina naho abagore bakize bararira amarira menshi bitewe n’ubuhemu bw’abagabo babo... Abakire bo ku isi basa n’ibisiga biri mu nkuta za zahabu, muri iki gihe ntibabasha kubaho badatunzwe na "ba gardes"... Abagabo bo mu nzego za leta bakurura iminyururu, ntibabasha kwigenga, bagendana n’abantu bitwaje intwaro kugeza ku menyo...

Reka twige iyi mimerere mu buryo burambuye. Tugomba kumenya icyo ubuzima aricyo. Buri wese afite uburenganzira bwo gutanga ibitekerezo bye uko ashaka... Uko byagenda kose, nta muntu numwe uzi ikintu, ubuzima ni ikibazo nta numwe usobanukiwe...

Iyo abantu bashaka kutubwira ku buntu amateka y’ubuzima bwabo, bavuga ibyabaye, amazina y’abantu, amatariki n’ibindi, kandi bakumva bishimye iyo babivuze... Abo bantu bakennye ntibazi ko inkuru zabo zidatunganye kuko ibyabaye, amazina n’amatariki, ari wo muco w’inyuma w’iyi filimi gusa, hakenewe umuco w’imbere...

Ni ngombwa kumenya "imimerere y’ubwenge", buri kintu cyose gihuye n’iyo mimerere. Imimerere iri imbere naho ibyabaye biri hanze, ibintu bibaho hanze si byose...

Ibyitwa imimerere y’imbere ni imyifatire myiza cyangwa mibi, impungenge, kwiheba, kwemera imiziririzo, ubwoba, gukeka, impuhwe, kwiyemera, kwiyemera cyane; imimerere yo kwishima, imimerere yo kunezerwa, n’ibindi, n’ibindi, n’ibindi.

Nta gushidikanya ko imimerere y’imbere ishobora guhura neza n’ibyabaye hanze cyangwa bikaba byaratewe na byo, cyangwa ntibigire aho bihuriye na byo... Uko byagenda kose, imimerere n’ibyabaye biratandukanye. Ntabwo buri gihe ibintu bibaho bihuye neza n’imimerere bifitanye isano.

Imimerere y’imbere y’ikintu gishimishije ishobora kuba itari yo. Imimerere y’imbere y’ikintu kidashimishije ishobora kuba itari yo. Ibyabaye byarategerejwe igihe kirekire, iyo byabaye twumva hari ikiburaga...

Mu by’ukuri hari haburaga imimerere y’imbere ihuye nayo yagombaga guhuzwa n’ikintu cyo hanze... Akenshi ikintu umuntu atari yiteze gihinduka icyatumye tugira ibihe byiza.
