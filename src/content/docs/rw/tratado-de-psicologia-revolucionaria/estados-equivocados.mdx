Nta gushidikanya ko mu kwitegereza witonze Njye ubwanjye, buri gihe birasaba kandi ntibitindiganye gukora itandukaniro ryuzuye rishingiye ku bwenge ku bijyanye n'ibyabaye byo hanze y'ubuzima busanzwe ndetse n'imimerere yimbitse y'ubwenge.

Dukeneye byihutirwa kumenya aho duherereye mu gihe runaka, haba ku bijyanye n'imiterere yimbitse y'ubwenge, ndetse n'imiterere yihariye y'ikintu cyo hanze kitubaho.

Ubuzima ubwabwo ni urukurikirane rw'ibintu binyura mu gihe n'ikirere...

Hari uwagize ati: "Ubuzima ni umunyururu, w'ibyamamare umuntu yizingiraho mu Bugingo..."

Buri wese afite uburenganzira bwo gutekereza uko ashaka; ndizera ko ibyishimo by'akanya gato birangirana no kwiheba n'uburakari...

Buri kintu cyose kigira uburyohe bwacyo bwihariye kandi imiterere yo mu mutima nayo ni iy'ubwoko butandukanye; ibi ntibishidikanywaho, ntibihakana...

Nta gushidikanya ko umurimo wo mu mutima wo kwiyubaka werekana mu buryo bukomeye imiterere itandukanye ya psychologiya y'ubwenge...

Nta wakwirengagiza ko mu mutima wacu twikoreye amakosa menshi kandi ko hari imiterere itari yo...

Niba dushaka koko guhinduka by'ukuri, dukeneye byihutirwa kandi bidatinze, guhindura burundu iyo miterere itari yo y'ubwenge...

Guhindura burundu imiterere itari yo, bitera impinduka zuzuye mu buzima busanzwe...

Iyo umuntu akora cyane ku miterere itari yo, birumvikana ko ibintu bibabaje byo mu buzima bitagishobora kumubabaza byoroshye...

Turi kuvuga ikintu gishobora gusobanukirwa gusa mu kukibaho, tukumva koko mu miterere nyayo y'ibintu...

Udakora kuri we ubwe ahora ari umukobanyi w'imimerere; ameze nk'igiti kibabaje mu mazi y'inyanja iteye ubwoba...

Ibyabaye bihinduka ubudahwema mu buryo butandukanye; biza bikurikirana mu nyanja, ni ingaruka...

Birumvikana ko hari ibintu byiza n'ibibi; ibintu bimwe bizaba byiza cyangwa bibi kuruta ibindi...

Guhindura ibintu bimwe birashoboka; Guhindura ibisubizo, guhindura imiterere, n'ibindi, biri mu mibare y'ibishoboka.

Ariko hari imiterere imwe n'imwe y'ukuri idashobora guhindurwa; muri ibyo bibazo bya nyuma bigomba kwemerwa mu buryo bwuzuye, nubwo bimwe bishobora kuba biteje akaga cyane ndetse biteye ububabare...

Nta gushidikanya ko ububabare buva ho iyo tutiyumva mu kibazo cyagaragaye...

Tugomba gufata ubuzima nk'urukurikirane rw'imimerere yo mu mutima; amateka nyayo y'ubuzima bwacu bwite agizwe n'iyo mimerere yose...

Mu gusuzuma ubuzima bwacu bwose, dushobora kwemeza ubwacu mu buryo butaziguye, ko imimerere myinshi itari myiza yashoboye kubaho kubera imiterere itari yo yo mu mutima...

Alexandre le Grand n'ubwo yari azi gufata ku kigero cyisumbuye ku buryo bw'umwimerere, yaretse akarangwa n'ubwibone butera gukabya byamuteye gupfa...

Igihe Marat yicwaga n'umubikira mubi, yapfaga yikunda kandi agira ishyari, yiyemeraga ko ari umukiranutsi rwose...

Abagore bo muri Parike y'Abakozi nta gushidikanya ko barangije burundu imbaraga z'umugabo usambanyi uteye ubwoba witwa LOUIS XV.

Abantu benshi bapfa bazize irari, umujinya cyangwa ishyari, ibi abahanga mu by'imitekerereze barabizi neza...

Igihe ubushake bwacu bwemejwe mu buryo butavuguruzwa mu buryo budasanzwe, duhinduka abakandida ba panteon cyangwa irimbi...

Otelo kubera ishyari yahindutse umwicanyi kandi gereza yuzuye abantu bibeshya bafite ukuri...
