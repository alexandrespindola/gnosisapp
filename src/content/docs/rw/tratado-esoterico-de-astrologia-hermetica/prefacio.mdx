## IMBAHARO

### BY MAESTRO GARGHA KUICHINES

Hariho Ubushakashatsi bwa Astrologiya bw'Ubumenyi cyangwa Imibare busaba ubushakashatsi bwimbitse kugira ngo bwigwe, hamwe nabwo Abanyenyesi bo mu bihe byose bagiye bategura ibizaba ku bintu by'ingenzi. Nanone ibyamamare bikomeye byagiye bikoresha ubu bumenyi nko mu gihe cya Hitler mu Budage wakoresheje Abanyenyesi b'inzobere kugira ngo babayobore mu bitero byabo by'intambara.

Twebwe Abanyaginosi twitandukanyije n'ubwo buryo bwo kwiga, kuko umuntu abaye igikinisho ukurikije ibiteganijwe, atagira kirengera imbere y'ibimenyetso biranga imiterere itandukanye n'inzira y'inyenyeri, twebwe tuzi astrologiya itwigisha gukoresha inyenyeri kandi bityo dushobora kwirinda ibintu bishobora gutegurwa n'inzobere za Astrologiya y'Imibare. Kubw'ibyo bisaba guhindura imibiri y'ukwezi tuvukana, tukayisimbuza imibiri y'izuba cyangwa iy'urumuri dukoresheje umuzi nyirizina w'ikiremwa cyacu cyangwa imbuto yacu bwite.

Umwanzi utubuza kuzamuka ubumenyi bw'ijuru ni Jye wacu wa Gisatani cyangwa Umukuru w'ingabo utegeka umubiri wacu. Uburyo bwiza kuruta ubundi bwo kwikuraho Umurinzi w'Umuryango nk'uko ab'ukuboko kw'ibumoso babivuga ni ukunyura mu nzira y'Itangizwa ry'ibikorwa, kuri twebwe Abanyaginosi ritangirana no kwinjira mu ngoro cyangwa Lumisiales, dukoresheje inyigisho ziyoborwa kandi zigatezwa imbere na Avatar ya Aquarius "Samael Aun Weor".

Mu gihe Intumwa Yesu yatanze inyigisho ze ku bakunzi b'inzira yaravuze ati: "Ndi inzira, ndi ukuri, ndi ubuzima, muri ibyo bihe hariho umupfumu ukomeye witwa Simoni Umupfumu, wuzuye ububasha n'ubutunzi bwinshi, wabwiraga abigishwa be ati: "Niba Yesu yageze kuri se kubera ibikorwa bye bwite, nanjye Simoni, nzagera kuri se kubera ibikorwa byanjye bwite" kandi icyo yakoze ni ugukurikira inzira y'ibumoso, yitandukanya n'umukunzi we, aka kaga karahoraho iyo abakunzi b'inzira bizera ko bo ubwabo bashobora kurenga inzira.

Ku bwa mbere abagezweho n'ubu bwenge, tubamenyesha ko bisaba amezi atandatu y'imyiteguro, biga ibihangano bya Samael Aun Weor kandi bagashyira mu bikorwa ubumenyi bwabo, hanyuma niba batanga ibimenyetso by'uko bashaka kwishyira mu bikorwa byimazeyo, kandi bagashaka ubuzima busumba ubundi, noneho bakemererwa kwinjira muri Lumisiales binyuze mu nzira yo kwimenyereza umwihariko.

Iyo umukunzi w'inzira yiteguye amasomo y'igerageza, ikizamini cya mbere agomba gutsinda ni icya Murinzi w'Umuryango cyangwa guhangana na Satani we bwite wabaye umuyobozi n'Umwigisha wacu mu binyejana byinshi. Ndibuka ko ahagana mu mwaka wa 1949, umwigishwa w'uwari Hierofante w'Amayobera Mato Aun Weor, kandi nyuma y'amezi arenga ane y'ubumanzi busesuye yashyizwe mu kizamini cy'Umurinzi ku itariki ya 27 Nyakanga. Uwo wari ukimara gutangira yari ahantu hiherereye, yari imbere, ariko ntiyabimenya, ubwo yahamagaraga Umurinzi w'Umuryango, ntiyatinze kuza, uwo wari ukimara gutangira yavugaga ko yabanje kumva ubukonje bw'urupfu, igihe cyari gityaye cyagiye gicura umwijima, ndetse ubwo bukonje buriyongera n'impumuro mbi yatuma ahinda umushyitsi, yumvaga ashaka guhunga, ariko imbaraga za Kristo yari amaze kwegeranya mu mubiri we binyuze mu guhindura ibintu, Arcano A. Z. F., yaramuhaye ubutwari bwo kuguma muri ako gace kadashimishije. Ako kanya abona inyamaswa ifite isura y'inguge, ifite ubwoya bwinshi, ifite amahembe ku gahanga yaka kandi agatura iyo agenze, ifite amazuru n'umunwa nk'iy'inyumbu, aramubwira ati: Ubwo urashaka kundeka? Unishyura gutyo ibyo nagukoreye byose? Urandekera uwo muntu utazi? Aramusubiza yuzuye ubwoba ko amureka, inyamaswa iramusagarira mu buryo bwo gutera, uwari ukimara gutangira arayihana, ariko ntacyo byamumariye guhana guciriritse kw'umuntu unaniwe kandi utewe ubwoba, ariko yibuka ko yari Chela wa Kristo arayihana mu izina rya Kristo maze isubira inyuma gato, uko yamusagariraga, yasabaga Kristo n'Abamwigisha be bakundwa kumurinda, akamubwira ati: ntushobora kunesha, nzagutsinda maze inyamaswa iragenda itanga ubwoko bwose bw'amaterabwoba. Umuhungabana w'uwo mwigishwa wari munini cyane, bitewe n'uko Hierofante yari yagiye yerekeza mu wundi mujyi kandi ibi byasabaga byibuze iminsi itatu adahari, ariko agarutse aramubaza maze aramusubiza ati: Ndagushimiye, watsinze neza ikizamini cya mbere n'Umurinzi w'Umuryango, iki kiremwa ni Satani wawe bwite, wagiye ukorera kandi ukagaburira mu binyejana byinshi, kandi se nzamugaburira nte?, Umunyeshuri utewe ubwoba arabaza maze Umwigisha aramusubiza, agaburirwa n'irari ryawe ribi, yitagatifuza ibyifuzo byacu bibi, irari ry'indwara, ubusambanyi, indaya n'ubuzima bubi, ibyo byose bigize abacuruzi bo mu rusengero Kristo yatubwiraga, bacuruza urusengero rwacu bwite, ubu biragusaba gukoresha inkoni y'ubushake ukirukana muri wowe abo bacuruzi bose bakugize imbata ya Satani, ubu biragusaba kubatsinda buri muntu niba ushaka kwikiza ikibi kandi ugafata inzira yera.

Binyuze muri iki gikorwa dushobora gusura abahanga bose bo mu nyenyeri, tujya mu rusengero rw'umutima w'inyenyeri, dusaba kandi tugakorana n'abamalayika bo mu nyenyeri, kugira ngo tutabe ibikinisho by'ibihe, ariko tubanje gukura abacuruzi mu rusengero rwacu bwite, tugomba kwiga gukorera ku Gicaniro cyacu bwite. Kubw'ibyo, umukunzi ajya mu mihango ya Lumisiales kenshi; aho yiga gukunda no gukorera Imana kuruta ibintu byose n'abaturanyi be, amenyera iyo mihango kandi nyuma atekereza ko amakuru yose y'umuhango wo gusenga afitanye isano rya bugufi n'igicaniro kizima kandi avumbura ibitangaza bitavugwa. JACHIN na BOAZ by'ibicaniro birakenewe kugirango ukoreshe igicaniro cye kandi ndetse hariho umwanya yiga gutera intambwe zirindwi zikenewe kugirango akore abizi ku gicaniro cy'Imana nzima kandi imbere y'imana ihebuje.

Hamwe n'inyigisho z'umuzuko, twigira kwica isekurume dutwaye imbere maze uko igihe kigenda kizana tuzaremamo umukumbi w'Umwana w'Intama wa Pasika. Bityo twikiza Nyagasani w'Igihe kugira ngo tubaho ubuzima bw'iteka buzuye ibyishimo by'iteka.
