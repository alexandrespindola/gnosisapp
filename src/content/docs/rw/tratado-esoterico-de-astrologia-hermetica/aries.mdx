Kuva ku ya 21 Werurwe kugeza ku ya 20 Mata

Umuntu ashobora kugira ibyiciro bine byo Kumenya: Igitotsi, Kumenya ibiriho, Kwimenya no Kumenya ibintu uko biri.

Erega mureke dutekereze umwanya muto, musomyi mukundwa, inzu ifite amagorofa ane. Inyamaswa itagira ubwenge yitwa UMUNTU ikunze kuba mu magorofa abiri yo hasi, ariko ntabwo ikoresha amagorofa abiri yo hejuru.

INyamaswa itagira ubwenge igabanya ubuzima bwayo bubabaje kandi buteye agahinda hagati y’ibitotsi bisanzwe n’icyo bita KWIYUMVIRA ibiriho, ariko bibabaje ko ari ubundi buryo bw’ibitotsi.

Mu gihe umubiri uruhukiye ku buriri, EGO yazingiye mu MIYOBORO yayo y’UKWEZI igendana no kumenya gusinziriye nk’umuntu ugendera mu nzozi yigendera mu karere ka molekuleri. EGO mu karere ka molekuleri itanga INZOZI kandi ikazibamo, nta bwenge na buke buri mu NZOZI zayo, gukomeza, impamvu, ingaruka, imikorere yose ya PSYCHIC ikora nta buyobozi kandi amashusho akagaragara akazima, ibice bidafatika, bidahwitse, n'ibindi.

Iyo EGO yazingiye mu MIYOBORO yayo y’UKWEZI igarutse mu MUTWE W’UMUBIRI, habaho icyiciro cya kabiri cyo kumenya cyitwa KWIYUMVIRA ibiriho, ariko mu by’ukuri ni ubundi buryo bw’ibitotsi.

Iyo EGO igarutse mu MUTWE W’UMUBIRI, inzozi zirakomeza imbere, icyo bita IGIHE CYO KWIYUMVIRA ibiriho ni ukurota uri maso.

Iyo izuba rirashe, inyenyeri zirihisha, ariko ntizireka kubaho; uko ni ko inzozi zimeze mu gihe cyo kwiyumvira ibiriho, zikomeza rwihishwa, ntizireka kubaho.

Ibi bivuze ko INyamaswa itagira ubwenge yitwa UMUNTU iba mu nzozi gusa; umusizi yaravuze afite impamvu ko ubuzima ari inzozi.

INyamaswa ifite ubwenge itwara imodoka irota, ikora mu ruganda, mu biro, mu murima, n’ibindi, irota, ikundana mu nzozi, irashyingiranwa mu nzozi; gake cyane mu buzima, irakangutse, iba mu nzozi kandi ikizera cyane ko ikangutse.

Amavanjili Ane arasaba GUKANGUKA, ariko bibabaje ko atavuga uko GUKANGUKA.

Mbere ya byose, ni ngombwa kumva ko usinziriye; ari uko umuntu amenye neza ko asinziriye ni bwo atangira inzira yo GUKANGUKA.

Ugeze ku GUKANGURA UBWENGE, ahita YIMENYA, akagira UBWENGE BWO KWIYUMVIRA ubwe.

Ikosa rikomeye cyane ry’ABAHUMBYI benshi ba PSEUDO ESOTERISTS na PSEUDO OCCULTISTS, ni ukwiyita ABAZI n’ukwizera kandi ko buri wese yakangutse, ko abantu bose bafite UBWENGE BWO KWIYUMVIRA ubwabo.

Iyo abantu bose bagira UBWENGE BUKANGUTSE Isi yaba paradizo, ntihaba intambara, ntihabaho ibyanjye n’ibyawe, byose byaba ari ibya bose, twabaho mu BIHE BYA ZAHABU.

Iyo umuntu AKANGUYE UBWENGE, iyo YIMENYE, iyo agize UBWENGE BWO KWIYUMVIRA ubwe, ni bwo amenya UKURI kuri we.

Mbere yo kugera ku cyiciro cya gatatu cyo Kumenya (KWIMENYA), umuntu ntabwo yimenya, niyo yaba yizera ko yiyizi.

Ni ngombwa kugira icyiciro cya gatatu cyo kumenya, kuzamuka mu igorofa rya gatatu ry’inzu, mbere yo kugira uburenganzira bwo kujya mu igorofa rya kane.

ICYICIRO CYA KANE CYO KUMENYA, IGOROFA RYA KANE ry’inzu, ni RIKOMEYE rwose. Umuntu ugera ku BWENGE BUTAGIRA ICYO BUBOGAMIRAHO, ku CYICIRO CYA KANE gusa, ashobora kwiga ibintu ubwabyo, isi uko iri.

Umuntu ugera mu igorofa rya kane ry’inzu, nta gushidikanya ko YAMURITSWE, amenya binyuze mu bushishozi bwe amayobera y’UBUZIMA N’URUPFU, afite UBGENGE, ubumenyi bwe bwo mu mwanya burateye imbere rwose.

Mu GITOTSI CYIMBITSE dushobora kugira ibice byo KWIYUMVIRA ibiriho. Mu GIHE CYO KWIYUMVIRA ibiriho dushobora kugira ibice byo KWIMENYA. Mu GIHE CYO KWIMENYA dushobora kugira ibice byo KUMENYA IBINTU UKO BIRI.

Niba dushaka kugera ku GUKANGURA UBWENGE, ku KWIMENYA, tugomba gukora n’UBWENGE hano no ubu. Ni hano rwose muri iyi si isanzwe aho tugomba gukora kugira ngo TUKANGURE UBWENGE, ukangukira hano arakangukira hose, mu bice byose by’Isi.

UMUBIRI W’UMUNTU ni ZODIAC IZIMA kandi muri buri tsinda ry’inyenyeri ryayo, ubumenya burasinziriye cyane.

Ni ngombwa gukangura ubumenya muri buri gice cy’umubiri w’umuntu kandi ibyo bikorwa bya zodiac birabigenzurwa.

Aries, ategeka umutwe; Taurus, umuhogo; Gemini, amaboko, amaguru n’ibihaha; Cancer, glande ya timo; Leo umutima; Virgo, inda, amara; Libra, impyiko; Scorpio, ibice by’imyororokere; Sagittarius, imitsi ya femoral; Capricorn, amavi; Acuario, ibirenge; Piscis, ibirenge.

Birababaje cyane ko iyi zodiac izima ya MICRO-COSMOS umuntu, isinziriye cyane. Ni ngombwa kugerageza gukora INGARUKA ZIDASANZWE, GUKANGURA UBWENGE muri buri kimenyetso cya ZODIACAL cya twe cumi na bibiri.

Urumuri n’Ubumenya ni ibintu bibiri by’ikintu kimwe; uko urwego rw’Ubumenya ruri hasi, ni ko urwego rw’urumuri ruri hasi; uko urwego rw’Ubumenya ruri hejuru, ni ko urwego rw’urumuri ruri hejuru.

Dukeneye GUKANGURA UBWENGE kugira ngo tucane kandi ducanye buri gice cya zodiac yacu bwite ya MICRO-COSMIC. Zodiac yacu yose igomba guhinduka urumuri n’ubwiza.

Gukorana na Zodiyake yacu bwite bitangirana na ARIES rwose. UMUNYESHURI yicara ku ntebe yoroheje afite ubwenge butuje kandi bwo guceceka, butarimo ibitekerezo byose. Umuntu wiyeguriye Imana arafunga amaso ye kugira ngo ntihagire ikintu cyo ku isi kimurangaza, yiyumvisha ko urumuri rutagira ikizinga rwa ARIES rwuzuye ubwonko bwe, aguma muri icyo gihe cyo kuzirikana igihe cyose ashaka hanyuma akaririmba Mantram ikomeye AUM afunguye neza umunwa we na A, akawuzengurutsa na U maze awufunge na M yera.

Mantram ikomeye AUM ubwayo ni iremwa RYATEYE UBWOBA BIRENZE, kuko ikurura imbaraga za PAI, ikundwa cyane, umuhungu akaramirwa cyane n’UMWUKA WERA w’ubwenge cyane. Ijwi A rikurura imbaraga za PAI, ijwi U rikurura imbaraga z’Umuhungu, ijwi M rikurura imbaraga z’UMWUKA WERA. AUM ni MANTRAM LOGIQUE ikomeye.

Umuntu wiyeguriye Imana agomba kuririmba iyi MANTRAM ikomeye inshuro enye muri iyi myitozo ya ARIES hanyuma agahagarara yerekeye iburasirazuba akagereza ukuboko kwe kw’iburyo imbere yimyoza umutwe we inshuro zirindwi imbere, zirindwi inyuma, zirindwi azenguruka ku ruhande rw’iburyo, zirindwi azenguruka ku ruhande rw’ibumoso afite intego ko urumuri rwa ARIES rukorera imbere mu bwonko rukangura glande pineal na pituitaria zitwemerera kumenya INGINGO ZO HEJURU Z’UMWANZURO.

Ni ngombwa ko URUMURI RWA ARIES rukura imbere mu bwonko bwacu rukangura UBWENGE, rutera imbere imbaraga zihishe muri GLANDE PITUITARIA na PINEAL.

ARIES ni ikimenyetso cya RA, RAMA, umwana w’intama. MANTRAM RA ikomeye, tuyiririmbye uko bikwiye, ituma imiriro ya spinal ihinda umushyitsi n’ibigo ndangamikorere birindwi byo mu mugongo.

ARIES ni ikimenyetso cya zodiac cy’umuriro, ifite imbaraga zikomeye kandi MICRO-COSMOS UMUNTU arazifata ukurikije uko we ubwe atekereza, yumva kandi akora.

HITLER, wavukiye muri ARIES, yakoresheje ubu bwoko bw’imbaraga mu buryo bwo gusenya, ariko, tugomba kwemera ko mu ntangiriro, mbere yo gukora ubujiji bwo kujugunya abantu mu ntambara ya kabiri y’isi yose, yakoresheje imbaraga za ARIES mu buryo bwubaka, azamura urwego rw’ubuzima bw’ABANTU B’ABADAGE.

Twashoboye kugenzura binyuze mu bushishozi butaziguye ko abavukiye muri ARIES bashwana cyane n’uwo bashakanye.

Abavukiye muri ARIES bakunze gushwana cyane ni abanyamahirwe ku miterere yabo.

Abavukiye muri ARIES bumva bashoboye gutangiza ibikorwa bikomeye no kubishyira mu bikorwa neza.

Hariho inenge ikomeye mu bantu bavukiye muri ARIES yo gushaka gukoresha imbaraga z’ubushake bwabo mu buryo bwa bwikunde, mu buryo bwa HITLER, bw’IMYITWARIRE MIBI no kubangamira.

Abavukiye muri ARIES bakunda cyane ubuzima bwigenga, ariko ARIAL benshi bakunda igisirikare kandi muri bwo nta bwisanzure buriho.

Mu mico ya ARIAL, ishema, kwigirira icyizere, icyifuzo no kugira agaciro gakabije ni byo biganje.

Icyuma cya ARIES ni UBUYE, ibuye, RUBÍ, ibara, UMUTUKU, ikintu, UMURIRO.

Abavukiye muri ARIES bakwiriye gushakana n’abantu ba LIBRA, kuko umuriro n’umwuka byumvikana neza.

Niba abavukiye muri ARIES bashaka kugira ibyishimo mu gushyingiranwa, bagomba guca inenge y’umujinya.
