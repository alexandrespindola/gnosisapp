Abo kavukire ba CAPRICORNIO bafite ubushishozi bwo kwigisha, bababara cyane, bafite IBITEKEREZO BIKOMEYE KU MIRIMO, ni ingirakamaro muri kamere kandi bagenda mu buzima bwabo buri gihe bababara cyane, umuntu arabagambanira.

Abagore ba CAPRICORNIO ni abagore bashimishije cyane, indahemuka kugeza ku rupfu, bakora imirimo yo mu rugo, bakora, bababara kugeza ku kintu kidavugwa, ariko nubwo bafite imico yose, umugabo arabagambanira, arabareka kandi akenshi ndetse no ku bwende bwabo, ni karma yabo kubwamahirwe.

Abagore bamwe ba CAPRICORNIO bagirana umubano n'abandi bagabo, ariko ibi ni ukuba bamaze kurekwako n'umugabo no nyuma yo kubabara cyane.

Abagabo n'abagore ba CAPRICORNIO ni abanyabwira cyane, nubwo atari bose; turimo turavuga ubwoko bwo hasi bwa CAPRICORNIO. Bitewe n'ibi, kuri ubwo BWANGA, bagirana amasezerano menshi ndetse bakuzura abanzi.

Abo kavukire ba CAPRICORNIO bakunda cyane ibintu, amafaranga kandi bamwe ndetse bahinduka abanyagashwa cyane.

CAPRICORNIO ni ikimenyetso cy'isi, gihamye, gishikamye. Ariko kandi, abo kavukire ba CAPRICORNIO bakora ingendo nyinshi nubwo zabaye ngufi.

Ububabare bwo kwishyira hejuru bwa CAPRICORNIANOS buteye ubwoba, bababara cyane, kubwamahirwe ibitekerezo byabo byo gukora ubuzima birabakiza kandi vuba бихиsha kuri amarwa mabi y'ubuzima.
