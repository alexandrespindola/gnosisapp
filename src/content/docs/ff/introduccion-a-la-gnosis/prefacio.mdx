Koɗdiɗi ɗiɗi ngoodi, koɗdiɗal gite e koɗdiɗal ɓernde, ganndal daañangal e ganndal nder-ɗer walla nderɗe, ganndal tinɗungal walla janngirgal e ganndal miijital walla ngeendam. Ganndal janngirgal walla tinɗungal ina nafii nguurndam e heɓde nguurndam amen. Ganndal nderɗe e ganndal miijital walla miijo amen ina ngondan min e ganndal Alla ngal ko bonngol, sabu ganndo moƴƴo ina haani anndude hoore mum.

Geeƴe njoweeɗiɗi daañagol ina ndenndina min e ganndal ngal woɓɓe nanii huunde, e jeeɗiɗi nder-ɗer ina ndenndina min e anndude ko wi'etee esoterique walla cuuɗi, geeƴe ɗe ɗoo: yewtere, cellal yewtere, polividencia, nango cuuɗi, tinndinol, telepático e remembarol nguurndam arande. Wondirol mum en: pineal, hipófisis (gelde e nder ɓalndu), tiroides (pomme du cou), ɓernde e plexo solar walla epigastrio (dow wuddere); e nder maaje minin ndenndina jeeɗiɗi (7) ɓalli gorko: ɓandu, ngeendam, astral, miijo, ɗi ngoni ɓalli nayi jambi ɗi ngoni lunaires protoplasmatiques e tati ɗi ngoni ɓalli doole, e ɓernde e ruuhu, ɗi ngoni ko ina ngomɗina ganndal miijo, ngal ganndal ko nguura sabu minin ngomɗina, ko waɗi ko njamiɗingol e waɗingol ɗum nguurndam, ina waɗi ko renndooɓe e joomiraawo ɗum walla philosophie nanii ɓernde.

Si minin ngomɗina geeƴe minin ngomɗina ganndal amen. Geeƴe ina ndenndina nde minin ngommba bonngol, si minin ngoni fenaañeeɓe geeƴe amen ko fenaañeeɓe, si minin ngoni jaamɓe geeƴe amen noon.

E nder pinal ngal ina ndenndina min ngommbugol bonngol amen ngam ngomɗinirde informateur walla geeƴe amen. Annda barke debbo Pinal Gnóstica ngal ina janngina min Formation Fondamentale ngal ina waɗata goɗɗo heɓugol haa maayo.

JULIO MEDINA V.
