Men ɗenndaandi wiide wonde jannginooɓe keewɗi winndere ɗon ngonaa ko heewi fof yoŋa BUDDA, Konfusiyu, Muhammadu, Hermes, Quetzacoal, Muusaa, Krishna, ekn.

Ndaa e dow ɗum kadi, men ɗenndaandi e nder nguurndam men fof, njamu e yeeñitaare, e njamu e yeeñitaare jannginooɓe ngam diine mum en mawɓe, ngam reeduuji, ngam mitolosi, ekn. Ɗum fof ko heewi e waawataa hakkilo.

E nder janngirɗe, e nder jami'ooji, e nder kolleeji, e nder jannginaaɗe ngam maudooji diineeji, ngam nanndeede, ngam waɗde darnde, ngam hakkilo taggoowo moƴƴo.

Sifaa diineeji ɗon njogii e gollalaji dawriiɗi, njogeede e haalaaji hakkillo e taariika ummatoore fof, yimɓe fof.

Diineeji fof ɗon njogii waɗɗiiji gootel, gollalaji dawriiɗi gootel, e njoggitaa e sifaa.

Hakkilo ngoodi e kiristaŋjo yoŋa diine Budda walaa diine Hebree walaa Hindu ngam diineeji fof njogi e nder waɗɗiiji gootel.

Njoguuji yimɓe keewɗi e nder diineeji e nder fundinaaɓe mum en ngam sumuugu MARXISTE ngam nde haalataa mbeɗeeji fof.

Jannginooɓe nder janngirɗe, e nder jami'ooji, e nder kolleeji, e nder jannginaaɗe waɗde jannginooɓe mum en e laawol hakkile ko tottan e nder njogoram men.

Ndaari keewɗi e nder jikke yoŋa e ndaa e wi'eede goɗɗol e nder kuugal, yoŋa e nder reeduuji diineeji, darnde, janngirɗe walaa jameeji.

Ndaarii e jannginaaɗe mum en e janngirɗe, ɗon njogii e goɗɗol e diineeji fof, darnde, janngirɗe, e hakkilo ngoodi e goɗɗo ngaraa waawde tottande darnde e nder reedu.

Ndaarii e jannginaaɗe mum en caggal juuɗe e jannginaaɗe, ɗon njogii e nguurndam maaje mum en, ɗon njogii e goɗɗol e diineeji fof, darnde, janngirɗe, e hakkilo ngoodi e goɗɗo ngaraa waawde tottande darnde e nder reedu.

Haa hannde e ɓaawo ɗum, njogii e jaŋgejo'en e goɗɗol ngaraa hakkile ngam, ɗon njogii e njamɗe hakkillo e haalaaji leydi e koli e nder waɗde hakkilo e nguurndam.

Haa hannde e ɓaawo ɗum, njogii e jaŋgejo'en e goɗɗol ngaraa hakkile ngam, ɗon njogii e njamɗe hakkillo e haalaaji leydi e koli e nder waɗde hakkilo e nguurndam.
