Gooto e ɓurɗo mawnude e hakkille ko yottude e FELAARE FUU.

Si miin noon ko gooto, ñakku e FELAARE HAKKILLE saɗataa feeñde, kono ko bone e aduna oo miin noon ko nder neɗɗo kala e noone keewɗe.

Miin keewɗo ko sabu moƴƴuɗo e haalaaji men nder men.

Si men waawnoo yeesde hoore men e ƴeeɓre ɓandu fuu no men woniri HAKKILLE haa e haalaaji men nder men, men yottoto e fannu bone wonde men alaa ko men woni neɗɗo gooto.

Gimɗo neɗɗo ko mashin moƴƴo mo miin keewɗo laamii mo HAKKILLE WURNINOONDI jaŋta ko moƴƴi.

Mi jannginta jaaynde miin HAKKILLE WIIGOTOONDI; mi yidi yaade feestuwal miin MBOBTEEWO YELLITA; aljanna e feestuwal oo miin MBADIJO GEEYTOONDO, ɓuri mi yaade haalirde, miin mi yidaa haalirde miin mbadiijo nguurndam, miɗo nannga, mi nyaamata, ekn.

Goɗɗo e miin ɗiɗi ɗi ngoni EGO oo, yidi yamiirde, wonde jaagorɗo, joomiraawo.

E leeso hakkille wurninoonde men waawi faamude miin ko legioon, e gimɗo ko mashin.

Miin ɗiɗi ɗi ɗowtana nder hakkunde mun, ɗi tuugnitoo sabu jaagorɗe, goɗɗo kala yidi wonde hooreejo, joomiraawo.

Ɗumɗoo firti haala bone noonde heebinde hakkille ɗe neɗɗo gillaare wiyete ko AADAMA.

Ko foti faamude ko firti konngol HEEBINDE e HAKKILLE. Heebinde ko jaɓɓaade, seeraade, seerseeraade, haɓtitaade, ekn.

Sabu moƴƴuɗo e HEEBINDE HAKKILLE ko hasɗe ɗe feeñata wakkati goɗɗo e noone ɗuuɗe e ɗe ɗumɗo yiɗi.

Hasɗe ko ɗemɗe keewɗe e alaa e ko ɓuri hasɗe aduna jaɓɓita. Halifuɓe ɗumɗo yiɗi jaɓɓitaade hasɗe.

Galɗo hasɗi galɗo e yidi wonde galɗo ɓuri. Miskinɓe hasɗi galɗoɓe e ɗumɗo yidi wonde galɗoɓe kadi. Bindoowo hasɗi bindoowo e yidi binda ɓuri. Mo alaa e faham hasɗi mo alaa e faham e ɗumɗo yidi alaa ɓuri heen.

Yimɓe ɓe ɗon ngonaana e mburu, surka e keeri. Sabu hasɗe boyte e karoosaji goɗɗo, suudu goɗɗo, e ubbe waɗi e ɗemngal, e ceede keewɗe e baawɗo e coɗɗo ekn. feññina yidde ɓeydude, heɓde huunde e huunde, subakaaji, ubbe, gile, ngam wota ngonaa ɓeɗɗi.

Ko ɓuri bone nder ɗum fuu ko gollal heɓde faham, gile, huunde, ceede ekn. ɗumɗoo ɓeydaaki miin keewɗo to ɗumɗoo waɗi hakkunde men haalaaji men nder men, seerseeraade bone, konuuji bone nder men.

Ɗum fuu ko naawndi. Alaa e ɗumɗoo waawi addanaade ɓernde bone. Ɗum fuu feññina ɓeydude bone e hakkille men, ɓeydude naawndi, bone feewi no ɓuri.

MIIN KEEWƊO naniima jaɓɓitaade haa e bone ɓuri bone e ngoolaa gollal hasɗe, heɓde, ɓeydude, naatude haa e galdi golle goɗɗo wiyete nguurndam, tago, jeegotoonde.

Yimɓe ngalaa e hakkiilo e ɓe ngoni hasɗoɓe, boneeɓe, hanɗuɓe, fiyo, e nde sabu goɗɗo yottoto e faamude ɗum fuu, to ɗumɗoo jaɓɓitaama, ɗumɗoo boneeɓe, ɗumɗoo wulli, kono ɗumɗoo faamtaa.

Hasɗe saɗi newnude sabu miijo neɗɗo ko hasɗo. Giɗɗo miijo ko e hasɗe e heɓde.

Hasɗe fuɗɗoto e bankiiji janngirde. Men hasɗi faham moƴƴo e jannge, kalifisiyooji moƴƴi, ubbe moƴƴi, subakaaji moƴƴi, paade moƴƴi, boyte moƴƴi, balli moƴƴi ekn.

Jannginɓe e jannginooɓe e noddinaama feññinaade neɗɗo e jannge, ko foti faamude ko gollal hasɗe e tabitinaade nder HAKKILLE janngoɓe mun aslu moƴƴo ngam faamude.

Miijo, hasɗo e tabia, miijo e gollal MAS. "MI WAWI newnude moƴƴi, MI ALAA E FHAM ɓuri, MI NGONI ko faham, MI ALAA E GILE ɓuri, santifikasiyooji ɓuri, perfeksiyooji ɓuri, taggo ɓuri, ekn."

Gollal miijo fuu ko e MAS. EL MAS ko resorte nder men hasɗe.

EL MAS ko gollal feenya. Gollal feenya fuu ko ABOMINABLE. Misal: Mi ngoni ko faham ko maaɗa. Fulanooɗo e baawaaɗo ɓuri maaɗa. Fulanaaɗo e moƴƴi ɓuri maaɗa, faham, moƴƴo, bone, ekn.

EL MAS feññina wakkati. MIIN KEEWƊO nafa wakkati sabu wonde moƴƴo ɓuri coɗɗo, ngam haɓtitaade e suudu ɓe ngoni ko mawɓe e ko ɓe waawi, ngam yottude wonde goɗɗo e nguurndam, ngam haɓtitaade e coɗɗo, e ɓe ɗon hasɗi, ɓe ngoni faham, semmbe, ekn.

Miijo feenya naatata e hasɗe e feññina ɗumɗoo ɗe wiyete bone, heewde, suurde.

Boneeje yimɓe yaada e leete goɗɗo leete goɗɗo, gitaare goɗɗe gitaare goɗɗe, ɓe ngalaa nguurndam e nder. Ɗuuɗuɓe njahara e bone, hasɗe, hanɗuɓe, fiyo, kono jahro e bone addataa kotoore ɓernde moƴƴe.

Ko urgent faamude ko kotoore ɓernde deeyɗo, ɗumɗoo soosaata, ɗumɗoo njaha, e feeñata e men e tabia e noone feeñe e no men faamii sabu bone; fiyo, hasɗe, hanɗuɓe ekn.

Ɓe ɗon ngidi heɓde ceede, noonde jaɓɓitaande, gile, nangaade fuu, e yidde yottude kotoore moƴƴe, ɗumɗoo ɓe fewjinaa sabu ɗumɗoo naatata e hasɗe e laawol hasɗe waawaa newnude men e warnde ɓernde deeyɗo e kotoore.

Miijo waɗi e nder MIIN KEEWƊO waɗi hasɗe gile haa waɗi leeso waɗde inɗe bone. Tago, taggooɗam, yidde ɓeydude, jahro sabu jaɓɓitagol ekn.

Ɗum fuu feññina heebinde, haalaaji nder, konuuji sirri, ñakku de newnude ekn.

Saɗi heɓde e nguurndam neɗɗo ɗe ngoni FELAARE e fannu timmunde e konngol.

Ko alaa e ɗumɗoo naatude yottude FELAARE FUU gilla nder men miin keewɗo.

Ko urgent faamude ko e nder neɗɗo goɗɗo alaa e baaje tati, Arande: Neɗɗo. Ɗiɗaɓo: MIIN KEEWƊO. Tataɓo: Gimɗo hakkille, wani, ASUUL NEƊƊO.

MIIN KEEWƊO waɗnata gimɗo hakkille e teewuuji atomiki e hasɗe, fiyo, hanɗuɓe ekn. Ko foti seertaade miin keewɗo, ngam ɓeydude nder, gimɗo hakkille ngam tabitinaade nder men e nder haala hakkille.

Ɓe ɗon ngalaa e nder haala hakkille, ɓe ngalaa e wonde felare.

Nder haala hakkille newnina men neɗɗo gooto.

Nder haala hakkille waɗi men ngoni felare.
