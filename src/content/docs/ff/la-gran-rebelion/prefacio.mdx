Nde : V.M. GARGHA KUICHINES

"JAAJIRDE MAWNDE" ndee, ka jannginoowo teddugu Samael Aun Weor holliti men hande nokku men e nguurndam.

Ko teeɗaani ceertinaade kala ko jokkata men e huunde fuuɗunde e nguurndam ɗam.

Ko ɗo men suɓti jannginol fannu kala ngam jaɓɓinde cuusal mo daranii hare e hoore mum.

Kala keye e golle ɗee njahiirta e bonnugol miinɗe men, ngam yuurnde tawa ko heewi e men.

Miinɗe nani alaa e faande maayde, neeneende na feela e dow tampere.

Yimɓe ɓe nani tampuɓe e nder aduna e jikkere na halaka nokku kala.

"ALAA HUUNDE TEEƊAANINDE, KO WONI KO WORɓE TAMPUNOO".

# FANNU 1

Aadamu alaa e jamal nderɗam; ko laaɓiiru na bonna kala. Newre haaɗiima. Boneeji ngoodi jaɓɓitooɓe. Sallama alaa sabu yimɓe ɓe na nguurdi e ɗerɗe e nguurndi.

Ngeendam tampuɓe ina e juuɗe yimɓe hakkeeji e kala jaŋde.

# FANNU 2

Weelo e nguurndi ina ɓeyda e saa'i kala e kemikaal na bonna leydi, kono ngoodi ndiyam jaɓɓirɗam e boneeji e men : "Huurndam ganndal" maa nafa huunde aadamu ngam waylude e NERSE e laboratuwaar aadamu men e ɓaawo e Lellan e Yiite, nde men janngi jokkude koɗume 3 e ummanugol ganndal : 1. Maayde tampere men. 2. Formude nderɗe oorɓe e men. 3. Golleɗe ngam Ɓiyum debbo jaafnuɗo (Aadamu).

Ledi, ndiyam e henndu, na njahra e boneeji ngam nguurndam hande; alaa jawdi aduna ngam waylude boneeji; ko men nafa jawdi ndiyam men ko men fuu men fuddii, huunde men, ndah men nawa e ganndal, ndah men joomi ngam waylude aduna e golleɗe e ganndal ummaade.

Men e forma sojaji warɓe aduna ngam hisnugol kala cuusal joopini e Avatara Aquarius, bee Jannginooɗe Cristifikasyon mo yurti men e kala boneeji.

Si a waylita hooremaa, aduna waylita.

# FANNU 3

Ngam yimɓe heewɓe jam weli alaa, ɓe anndaa ko golle men, ko men waɗannde, men e waɗannde; men e waɗa bee jawdi ndiyam men, huunde men.

Nde men ngoodi e nder ɗernde men na feela e jam weli, kono ko jawtiiɗe saa'i ɗee; si a alaa e nder ɗernde aduna maa, maa wonaa jeyaaɗo e nder ma, sabu ta wela e huunde fuu. Ko teeɗaani nguurnde e nder aduna e alaa woni jeyaaɗo e nder ma.

# FANNU 4 YIMƁE NDOOWAN E NDEERDE

Ndeerde na nela men, men faandi wonude yuurɓe, kono ɓe e ngooloo boneeji e wootere e men ngoodi hedditooɗo e ndah men wayla e jeyaaɓe e ndah men yahi e boneeji.

Mo juula juulde boneeji, ko boneere ɓurndi mo fuddii, sabu mo ɗon no yaaki ngam cellal, ngenndiyankaagal maa tampere sinceree; mo juuliiɗo na waɗa wa jeyaaɗo goonɗinɗo e boneeji, ko boneere e mbaadi. "Ɓaaɓe Goonga e nder ma maa wuurnde". Kono nde ko no a yahi e Goonga, ko pewɗo? E ndaw huunde ma o yahi e saa'i kala e dow goonga.

Goonga ko mbaadi Baaba Goonga yiɗaa, ko wayndi e Goonga. Ko no a jaɓɓa Goonga mo pewɗo, sabu ta ko huunde Baaba? Huunde Baaba nani a jaɓɓa mo ina heewi e tampere, nguurndi, nderde ngam power e laamorde. Men e jaɓɓitaɓe e goonɗinnde men; juuta e clarividente mo ngooloo ko o yi'a nderɗam; mo ɗon ina yonda kaaw e kala huunde maa haɗe e dow maa.

"Mo woni uurɓe? Mo ngolii hournde? Ko jeyaaɓe e no?" Aa! Aa! Aa! (Samael). Mo pewi haaɗi maa wona uurɓe sabu ina e hakkunde e Goonga yiɗaa ko Goonga laaɓiiru.

# FANNU 5 YIMƁE NDOOWAN E LEEDI PENNDUL

Kala na yuula e yuulii, e dow e les, yaa e wara; kono e yimɓe ɓe e waɗi ngenndiyankaagal e nokkuujo ɓee ɓe ɓuri jaɓɓinde yuulal nokkuujo ɓee, ngam ndah ina yahii e bahru ɗee. Nde waɗani boneeji maa yuurndi, ina uurti e leede heewde, ina bonni geene ɗee no formi, ina feela e nderde.

Dow ɗee fuu maa bonni, ko teeɗaani ɓaaɓe laaɓiiru.

Ndaaru maa joopa e dow huunde waɗi e ndaaru maa njahra e nder Goonga laaɓiiru. "Ko haaɗi bonnugol tampere, a yahi e Goonga" (Samael).

# FANNU 6 FAAMUDE E GONDU

Ko teeɗaani a jangaa kaakide e nder fannu ɗon ngam juutugo e ndahaaru bonnugol; nde men ngoodi e tampere, nguurndi, njahraagu, ndaaru men ina bonni; ko ɗon nde : "Ko ɗon sabu men e ndaarii", ko boneere, kala ngoodi baadi, teeri, ɓeydii, dow e les, durude, saa'i, to boneere nde a yi'a huunde dow njahiirde, ina ndowa ɗum bee power, ina huula mo ina hanndoo.

# FANNU 7 DILLAAL GAANNDAL

Men e anndi e ndah men njanginni, nde men umminii gaanndal ɓe ngam golleeji gaanndel e nguurndi njertiniri.

Mo e yahii e Senda ina bonni NERSE koɗume e gaanndal nde o joopa e nguurndi.

Jaanginoowo e njertiniri, joopa e nguurndi, alaa jaɓɓita e nguurndi, feeli no mo ndaaraniiɗo e nokku ɗoon; ta to no e nokku ndaaraniiɓe ina njopa e dow to ɗon bonni maa ina juuma. Jaanginoowo Nguurndam ko mo janngina huunde ɓuri moyya e jaɓɓirde e nder Senda, ina waɗi ɓe ɓuraade huunde e ndon, Neeneende na njopa e dow ɗoon e ndaaru ina ndowa e nder yiide.

"Gaanndal ko Lellan mo inngaantal a feela" (Samael Aun Weor) e jimoowo ina jooppi e nder Lellan Gaanndal, ko wayndi e balluɓe e Lellan Naange.

Nde ɓeydii radio gaanndal maa men, men men ndaara nderɗam ko gonga.

# FANNU 8 LEESDI GAANNDAL

Yimɓe e njawti e kaakidi naange e nder mbirndi e ɓe ngoolii ndah njahi, gaanda ina ngooloo huunde e ina ngoloo inɗe tampuɗe, ngam wano gaanda ɓe ɓeyda.

Ngoɗi miliyoon ngooloo inɗe boneeji maa, kono ɓe anndaa to ɓe bonna ɓe.

Worɓe ina njokkina waatil mawde mo o formi, kono o anndaa to o jokkina watiire maa : nder nder o waɗii saa'i kala; ko worɓe nde o anndaa, ina jooppi ko wayndi e laboratuwaar e tampere; kono e worɓe ina waɗi ndah laaɓiira, bonnugol tampere, nguurndi, njahraagu, e.e., o teeɗi ngam, goonɗinno e ballol ko to ɓuri.

# FANNU 9 ANTICRIST

Men e ɓaaɓe nderɗam. O a jooppa men nde a yaha e Baaba Yiɗaa. Kono nde men laamiri ɗum ko ɓuri e mbaadi.

Anticristo ina njoraade ɓeydaɗe Cristiana Goonga, Hoolol, Hooreles, e.e. "Worɓe" ina ndowa gaanndal maa e ina njopa e dow ɗuum.

# FANNU 10 MIINƊE SIKOLOJI

Ko teeɗaani men nde ndaara e golleeji saa'i kala, anndu si to men ina waylita men, sabu bonnugol nokkuujo a joopa men. Ina ndowa e men nde men goonɗiniri ko men njertiniri boneeji, kono ko ɗon ko moyyi nde men bonnii e nder men boneeji ngam waylitude e dewgal Crist e nguurndi nder men ngam nelaade e waylitude Aadamu.

Jannginugo njoraade, ko fuu na anndi, kono jannginugo YIIDE, ko teeɗi.

Jangaa kaakide Goonga janginoowo ɗon, si a yiɗi bonnude tampere maa nderɗam.
