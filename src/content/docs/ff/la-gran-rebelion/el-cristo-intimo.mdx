Almasihu ko Yiyo Yiyo, Lewru Lewru, Signatura Astral Yiyo.

Dow Lekki Mawɗo Kalvari ɗon felte Mistere Almasihu bee bolle gootel ɗe jogii leyɗe nayi: INRI. Ignis Natura Renovatur Integram - Yiyo ɗon sokka Nature hakkunde ngolɗo.

Goolɗe Almasihu nder ɓernde neɗɗo, ɗon waylita ɓurane.

Almasihu ko LOGOS SOLAR, gootel keewɗum haa timmi. Almasihu ko nguurndam ɗum wiirta nder duniyaaru fuu, ko ngolɗo woni, ko ngolɗo woni hannde e ko ngolɗo waɗan hannde.

Keewɗum haalaama dow Drama Kosmik; ko gooŋa Drama ɗon haɓii bee leyɗe nayi.

Haalaama wonde Drama Kosmik ɗum ardi bee Elohim haa leydi; Jawmiraawo mawɗo Atlantida ɗon felte drama ɗon nder Ɓandu e Ɓandu.

Mawɗo KABIR JESÚS kadi jogii felte drama gooto nder Leydi Senoowo.

Hay Almasihu jibinaa teemeeje waɗi nder Belem, huune fuu nafaatake si o jibinaaki nder ɓernde meeɗen kadi.

Hay o maayii e o ummii nder ɗiɗiɗal nyalaade, huune fuu nafaatake si o maayaaki e o ummiita nder meeɗen kadi.

Ceede ɓarnaare e hakkilantaare yiyo ko ceede ɓarnaare Alla, ko ɓernde nde ɗon fellite hannde nder jaɓaare yiyo.

Caangol ɗum wiirta (Éxodo, III, 2) e ngolɗam Sinaí hakkunde ngolɗa ɗum okkiti Decálogo (Éxodo, XIX, 18): ko fellite ɗiɗi ɗum Alla felliti haa Moisés.

Dow seere ɗum woni Jaspe e Sardónico nder kalaamu yiyo, jooɗiiɗo dow Gada ɗum waari e ɗum wiirta, San Juan ɗon felte jom duniyaaru. (Apocalipsis, IV, 3,5). "Alla meeɗen ko Yiyo ɗum ɗon nannana", San Pablo ɗon winnda nder Epístola haa Hebreos.

Almasihu jogiiɗo, Yiyo Kammu, ɗon jibinaa nder meeɗen e ɗon jibinaa ko gooŋa si meeɗen ɗon yaaji nder golle Psikológico.

Almasihu jogiiɗo ɗon haɓiita nder Nature meeɗen Psikológica, ko sabu ngolɗo jaɓaare; YOES CAUSAS.

Woonataa waawaa haltaare sabu Ego si Almasihu jogiiɗo jibinaaki nder meeɗen.

Yiyo nguurndam e Filosofal, Almasihu jogiiɗo, ko Yiyo Yiyo, ko laaɓal nder laaɓal.

Yiyo ɗon wiira meeɗen e ɗon wela meeɗen nder banndi fuu, ɗon ara haa meeɗen bee henndu, bee ndiyam e bee leydi gootel ɗum ko konservadores e karamajeeji fuu.

Yiyo Kammu ɗon felte nder meeɗen, ko Almasihu jogiiɗo, jom Hisnaa meeɗen jooɗiiɗo nder.

Jawmiraawo Jogiiɗo ɗon haɓii e Psikís meeɗen fuu nder Cilindros jooɗeeji nder makina Orgánica; nder processes Mental meeɗen fuu, Emocionales, Motores, Instintivos Sexuales.
