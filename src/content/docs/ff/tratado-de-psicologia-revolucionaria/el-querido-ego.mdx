Ko woni ɓurngal e lesɗal ko fedde ɗiɗi nder huunde gootel, haa tawaani ko haani settinde ɓanndu gootel: "MI ƁURNGAL, MI LESƊAL" ko fannuuji ɗiɗi nder ngootiri Ego mawɗo e jaɓɓaaɗo.

Ngoonɗo "MI REWBE" walla "MI ƁURNGAL", "ALTER EGO" walla huunde noone, ko tawaani ko lepte nder "MI MISME", laawol AUTO-ENGAÑO.

Nde MI naati yeeso ɗo e ko ɓuri ɗoon, o Auto-Engaña e miijo jaamuɗo nder MI Rewbe Baññeeri...

Goɗɗo e men alaa "Mi" goonga, ñalnde fof, jiiɓngal, sahaa fof, dewgal, ekn, ekn, ekn.

Goɗɗo e men alaa e goonga ngootiri goonga nder Mbaadi; ko ɓuri ñiŋde, men ngalaa kadi ina woni kawtal goonga.

Ego, hay so o tawtoraama ko ɓuri haa leydi, ina woodi taawal e battindi.

Ego, MI, alaa e sahaa fof ko fedde gootel, ngootiri, unitotal. Ko haani, MI ko "MIIJE".

E nder Tibet Horeejo, "MIIJE" ɗe teddinaa ko "AGREGADOS PSIQUICOS" walla tan "Hoolaaɗe", hay so ɗi ɓurti ko ɓurngal walla ko lesɗal.

So men miijii e dow "Mi" fof no neɗɗo woɗɗo, men mbaawaa settinde e ɓanndu mawndu gootel: "Nder neɗɗo fof mo yoori e aduna, ina woodi yimɓe ɗuuɗɗe".

Ko haani, nder goɗɗo e men ina yoori yimɓe ɗuuɗɗe woɗɗe, ƴeewteeje ɓurti, ƴeewteeje lesɗe...

Goɗɗo e MIIJE ɗee fof, goɗɗo e yimɓe ɗee fof ina hayre ngam ɓurna, ina yidi wonde tan, ina jaggira koppi nguurndam walla fannuuji nguurndam e motor nde o mbaawi fof, nde woɗɗo naati...

Doktiina MIIJE ɗuuɗɗe ɗe jannginaama nder Tibet Horeejo e Clarividentes goonga, e Illuminaates goonga...

Goɗɗo e lepte men psikolosiiji ina teddinaama e dow Mi ɗum walla ɗum. Ko woni men ngoodi ujunnaaji e haa milioniiji nder lepte, ko haani ina yoori yimɓe ɗuuɗɗe nder men.

E nder haalaaji psikolosiiji, men mbaawaa seedaade e ɓanndu goonga seɓɓe paranoicos, ególatras e mitómanos ngam alaa e nguurndam ndaɗaaɗe nguurndam ngam teddine Ego heewi.

Ko haani, yimɓe noone ɗee ina haanii e nguurndam doktiina MIIJE ɗuuɗɗe.

Nde goɗɗo ina yidi e goonga annda hoore mum, o haani auto-observarse e hayre ngam annda MIIJE woɗɗe ɗe naati nder nguurndam.

So goɗɗo e karallo men ngalaa haala doktiina MIIJE ɗuuɗɗe, ɗum woni tan ko men ndenndaaka e dow haala Auto-Observación.

Ko men mbaɗata Auto-Observación Nder, ina yaha feewde hoore mum haala yimɓe ɗuuɗɗe, haala MIIJE ɗuuɗɗe, ɗe yoori nder nguurndam men.

Ɓeen ɓe ndenndata doktiina MIIJE ɗuuɗɗe, ɓeen ɓe teddinaa MI Rewbe, ko men ndenndaaka ko Auto-Observado e ɓanndu goonga. Ko mbiida e ɓanndu Socrático ɗo ɓee yimɓe ngalaa tan, seeɗa ngalaa ko ngalaa.

Ko haani, men ndaɗaaɗe hoore mum, seeɗa auto-observación seɓɓe e jaamuɗo.

So neɗɗo fof ina yaha e miijoore no gootel, ko haani goɗɗo nder a woni huunde ɓuri haala.
