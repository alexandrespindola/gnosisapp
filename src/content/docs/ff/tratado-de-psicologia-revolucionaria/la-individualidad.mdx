Gollal "Gootel", ko weltere moƴƴaani; nde sikkitii ni hilaare laaɓndi ndii no feewi e men gooto kala.

Aawo, firoore men no feewi e men ko ɓuri moƴƴude, men miijitaa faamude wonde men alaa kadi ɗemngal gootel goonga.

Ko ɓuri bonde kadi ko haa men nantaa men heen ndeke men no njogii miijo gootel e tinnginoore men.

Miskin men! Men no wulii! Goonga ko jaahiliya ɓuri bonde e boneeji.

Nder men gooto kala no woodi ujunaaje e ujunaaje yimɓe keeriiɗe, koode keeriiɗe, Miijooji tooñiiɗe, koode mbaɗiɗe nguurndam koode ndiiɗe nguurndam ɓural e laamu e koode ngalaa laabi e jaɓɓere woo.

Si men miijittaa, si men ummaa e yoore e yeeɗe, nguurndam wonataa noo naati. ..

Nde nii, ko ɓuri bonde e boneeji men, ko miijooji bonɗi e ndeenka e nelaaɗum e yidde hoore men, no nandi e men, no tuggira e men, men miijitaa weltaade e hoore men, yiitaade men hono no men woni...

Men gootii men no njogii miijo gootel nde men no njogii miijooji keeriiɗi. (Gooto kala no jogii ɗum mum)

Kalaaje ndiiɗe e miijooji men nder no feeñi; miijooji men nder no njoggii ɓural, no nguurdi nder ndeenka nder miijooji keeriiɗi.

Si men no ngoodaa e ndeenka gootel, si men no ngoodaa gootel nder gootel e koode, men no ngoodaa jaɓɓere nde njogii, miijitore ummotoodo, tinnginoore gootel, gootel.

Ceede wonii ko kaɲum woni ko gootel gootel gootel, ɗum noon men ngada e laaɓnde e hoore men.

Men no ngoodaa waɗude jaɓɓere hakkillaare hoore men men ngoodaa men no njoggii e koode men njogaaani.

No gasirii ngoodi ndeenka gootel, nde nii si men no njogii ndeenka, ɗum haalaa.

E feeñi men ndeenkaa e ko men gootii men yidde ko men no njogii. Yoore no waɗaa men gootii men ko men no njogii ndeenka kadi no woodi nder aduna janngirde gootii men non.

Ko ɓuri haajorde ko haltaade e yoore ndii, ɗum no waɗaa men feeñaa men ko men woni, nde men no ngoodaa men no ngoonda ngoonga, ndeeska e koode.

Men no gootii men ko men worɓe, nde men no ngoodaa ko men njanaaɓe hakkillaare ɓe ngalaa ndeenka.

Ɓe mballoowo ɓe no ngootii ko men Joomiraawo, Mahatmas, e koode, ɓe miijitaa koode ɓe ngalaa hakkillaare gootel e Tinnginoore Miijitinoore.

Ɓe njiddaaɗo hoore ɓe no njidda nguurndam Egoo, ɓe jaɓɓataa haala ndee ndee e koode Egoo e nder hoore mum.

Ɓe ngartiraaɗo e nguurndam ndee e koode, ɓe jaŋgataa deftere ndee...

Ko haajorde hallude yaake e yoore e hoore men, si men ngidaa men wona yimɓe e miijooji men njogii e nde nii, men njoggii hakke waɗude haalaaje boneeji e ndeeska e ɓe men.

Njooɗi hakkillaare ndee no nde tuggirtaa e yoore, ndum no yooraa ko men leydi.

Mballoowo ndee jaɓɓataa haalaaje ndee ɓe ngoolɗaa; o no gootii ko archihierofante koode no ɗum woni; o no miijitaa yoore ko koode woni koode, "koode noon ɗum ko yoore".

Yoore ko koode nder aduna koode e koode no haajaa nguurndam e koode no njooɗinii yimɓe hakkillaare e yoore, naataade ɓe no gootii worɓe ɓe njogii ndeenka, tinnginoore, hakkillaare, miijooji gootel, e koode.

Nde men no gootii men gootel, men ɗuumkaa ko men ngoodaa nder hoore men, men haajaa koode.

Gooto kala no woodi nder nguurndam hakkillaare gootel e men ngalaa ummade e laabi nde men ngada koode no nguurdi nder hoore men.

E feeñi ko miijooji men hakkillaare ngada yiitaade koode nguurdi nder hoore men e men no ngoodaa ceede koode.

Yiitaade ndee, auto-yiitaade ndee, ceede ɗum ko miijooji men e hoore men e koode, men no feeñiraa ko men ngalaa ndeenka gootel.

E no men auto-yiitaa, men nguurndam nder yoore e men no gootii men gootel e nder nguurndam men wonii koode.

Men ngalaa ngoodude e ɓe men ngoodaa e laabi nde men waɗi ceede nder hoore men.

Ceede nder nder ngoodaa ceede koode ɓe njoggii nder.

Men ngalaa ko men waɗi koode ndee si men yiitaa ɗum nder hoore men.

ɓe gootii koode ɓe no gootii ko men no njoggii ɓuri moƴƴude, ɓe jaɓɓataa koode waɗude e koode, men mbaɗata koode e waɗude koode.

Men ngalaa ceede si men ngalaa waɗude, nde nii men no gootii njogii ndeenka si men ngada ngoodaa ceede, nde men no ngoonga waɗude ɗum.

Empero, men mbaɗiita ko men no gootii haalaa hoore ɓe ngada waɗude, nde nii ɓe ngalaa gootii.

Men no ngoodaa "desegoistizarnos" ngada "individualizarnos", nde nii men no gootii njogii ndeenka ɗuum haalaa waɗude desegoistizarse.

Ndeenka ndee ko koode e nder ceede e koode, nde nii men no njoggii.

Men waɗi men waɗude "Yoes", si men gootii men no njogii "Yo" gootel?

Goonga ko ɓe men ngalaa auto-yiitaade men no gootii njogii Yo gootel.

Empero, men ngoodaa waɗude ceede nder janngude nder hakkiilo men mbadata koode e ndeenka gootel ngoonga e koode e koode e koode.

Ndeenka Sagrada ko koode nder haalaaje "Yo", koode woni ko koode.

Ndeenka legitima woni Ser e nguurndam woni Ser, woni Ser.

Distíngase e nder Ser e Yo. Quiénes confunden al Yo con el Ser, ciertamente nunca se han auto-observado seriamente.

Nde ko njoggoo Esencia, nde hakkiilo, embotellada nder haalaaje e nder Yoes ɓe njoggii nder, ceede koode wonataa haaloo.
