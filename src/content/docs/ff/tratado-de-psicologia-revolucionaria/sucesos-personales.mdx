Miijo e hoore nder hakkille ko foti, haa laaɓi so a faalaano anndude no yiyeele hakkille ko woowaani.

Sikke alaa, no yiyeele nder hakkille ko woowaani no waawi feewnude nder laabi feewundi.

Sababu nguurndam nder hakkille ko ko ɗaɓɓita ko woni yaasi, menen no haani menen, e jonde nde no haani feewnude, menen no haani ittude nder hakkille amen no yiyeele hakkille ko woowaani.

Feewnude no yiyeele hakkille ko woowaani ko foti so a faalaano waylude goonga nguurndam ko woowaani.

Waylude alakaamen e goonga nguurndam, ko waawi so a ittaaki nder hakkille amen no yiyeele hakkille ko fayaani.

Ndaardi goonga nguurndam ko boni, no waawi wartaade ko welaani haa ko feewi, sabu feewnude hakkille nder hakkille ko woowaani.

Neɗɗo no waawi waylude goonga nguurndam ko boni, so o laɓɓinaaki nder hakkille. Mo feewnataaki no yiyeele hakkille ko fayaani, oɗon tiiɗiɗo o warti njamndi goonga nguurndam.

Wallude nder suudu amen nder hakkille ko boni ko foti, so a faalaano waylude nguurndam ko boni.

Yimɓe no wi'a dow kala, no suyo, no woyaa, no wulaa, no faalaa waylude nguurndam, yaltaade nder bonde nde ɓe woodi, ko boni ɓe gollaaki dow hoore maɓɓe.

Yimɓe faalaani anndude nguurndam nder hakkille no ɗaɓɓita goonga nguurndam yaasi, so ɗum ko metti ko sabu no yiyeele nder hakkille ko fayaani.

Ko yaasi ko tan ko misal nguurndam nder hakkille, mo waylitoo nder hakkille no fuɗɗi ndaardi goonga nguurndam keso.

Goonga nguurndam yaasi alaa ko ɓuri mawnude no neɗɗo welta e ɗum.

Aɗon jooɗii e ɗow? Aɗon nange nde ɓe bonni?

No a weltee e nde ko boni ɗun? Aɗon acci e nde ko boni ɗun? Aɗon waree? Aɗon e kasoo?

Asibitiiji, gineeeji, kasooji no heewi e ɓe bonni ɓe weltaa e no bonni ɗun.

Ngalaaji ɓuri moƴƴi neɗɗo no waawi huutoraade nder nguurndam, ko no hakkille nder hakkille ko feewundi.

Neɗɗo no waawi ittaade kulle e ittaade naane, sabu no hakkille nder hakkille ko feewundi.

No hakkille nder hakkille ko woowaani no waylitoo e njamndi bonde.

Ndaa no welte e ndaardi nguurndam ko boni e no hakkille nder hakkille ko feewundi...

Taa teeɗo e kala ndaardi, haalanen ko kala no yahdi; ndaa nguurndam no film e nangen.

Taa yejjito ndaardi alaa ko yaara no waawi yahaade e bonde, so a ittaaki nder hakkille no hakkille nder hakkille ko woowaani.

Kala ndaardi yaasi no haani, sikke alaa e tikete nde ko haani; ko haani no hakkille nder hakkille ko haani.
