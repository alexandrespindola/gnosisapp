Nawndu goɗɗo hakkilantaagal mo innetee neɗɗo, alaa e sifa gooto. Pelɗal, nde wonaa gootum hakkiilo nder neɗɗo ina addani ɗum tampere e mette keewɗe.

Nawndu fannu ina gootum tee ina golla ko wayi no fannu fulu, si nawndu naawni alaa. Kono, nguurndam nder neɗɗo alaa ko wayi no gootum hakkiilo. Ko ɓuri bonde e nder ɗum, hay si janngirɗe seedo-ganndal e seedo-suuyeeɓe ina mbi'a, ko alaa peeje hakkiilo nder nderteende neɗɗo kala.

Goonga, e ɗuum noone, golle paayodiral alaa ko wayi no fulu nder nguurndam nder yimɓe. Neɗɗo, e sifa mum nderteende, ko keewal hakkiilo, fotti "Maa".

Jahiluɓe waylude nder zaman buudu nduu ina ngumɗa "Maa", ina ngeejo, ina ngaddana baabal, ina nji'a "ALTER EGO", "MAA ƁURNDO ƁAWGAL", "MAA REWƁE", ekn, ekn, ekn. Ɓe nanii faamu jahiluɓe-ɗuuɗu nder zaman buudu nde nguurɗen, "Maa Ɓurndo Ɓawgal" maa "Maa Ɓural" ko goɗɗe fedde goɗɗe nden Egoo keewɗe...

Neɗɗo alaa "MAA TABBITAANDO" kono keewal "Maa" njamndi e majjuɓe. Nyamndu hakkiilo mo innetee neɗɗo ina wayi no galle boɗɗaaɗo nde e nder galoo alaa jom galle gooto, walaa maccuɓe keewɗe ina faala jaggina e gollude ko ɓe faalaa...

Error gooto ɓurndo ɓawgal nder Seedo-Ganndal e Seedo-Suuyeeɓe leefɗe ko miijaade woɓɓe ina keɓi maa taw ina keɓaa "MAA TABBITAANDO E ALAA WAYLODE" alaa fuɗɗoore e alaa banndu... Si ɓee ina miija noone innamaaki e hakkiilo hay si e saanga gooto, ɓe waawi hollirde goonga neɗɗo hakkilantaagal faya waylude...

Nawndu hakkilantaagal, e fannu hakkiilo, ina waylude tuma... Miijaade neɗɗo gooto no innetee Luwis ko Luwis tuma, ina wayi no lolle bonnde... Nde neɗɗo mo innetee Luwis ina keɓi e hoore mum goɗɗe "Maa", goɗɗe egoo, nde ngala e hakkillo mum e waqti goɗɗo hay si Luwis nanngataa cikka, goɗɗo "Maa" nder makko-ina innetee Pepe-ina nannga cikka e noone...

Alaa neɗɗo no noone tuma; goonga haaje alaa wonde ganndo jaɓɓude waylude e jalɓe nder neɗɗo kala... Jaɓɓude wonde neɗɗo ina keɓi "MAA TABBITAANDO E ALAA WAYLODE" ina wayi no ɓaawo neɗɗo e hoore mum...

E nder neɗɗo kala ina nguurdi yimɓe keewɗe, "Maa" keewɗe, ɗuum ina jaɓɓa hoore mum neɗɗo innamaaki, hakkiilo...
