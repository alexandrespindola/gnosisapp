Se tinndindirde, nder deenɗeende e nder teskinde hoore mum, ko feewi e ko waawi tan ko waɗude seernditingol laaɓtingol hakkunde ko foti e ko gandi e nguurndam e nder ngonkaaji ɓernde.

Eɗen ngandi to ngonɗen e nder saa'iire, ɗum foti e ngonkaaji ɓernde e ngenndi ngonndi e ko waɗi. Nguurndam kanko e hoore mum ko kala ko waɗi e nder jamano e nokku...

Neɗɗo gooto wi'i: "Nguurndam ko careeli ñawleeli nde neɗɗo na jaggitoo nder Pulaar mum..." Kala neɗɗo ko hooreejo sikka e ko o welaa; miin miɗo sikki e nder welde baasiire nde saa'iire ndee yettii, ɓaleefi e suurɗude. Kala ko waɗi woodi tomnu mum keewɗum e ngonkaaji nder e hoore mum noon ɗum ko nooneeji goɗɗi; ko ɗum fotaani reetaaninde.

Goonga ko golle nder e hoore mum no feewi e nooneeji pusuuji nder hakkillaaji mum... Hay gooto waawataa haɗiɗe e nder men men njaggitii kulle e ngonkaaji tiiɗɗi... Si men njooti hiirde e nder goonga, men nangi hiirde e no feewi e laabiire ngam waylitinde nooneeji tiiɗɗi nder hakkillaaji men...

Waylititingol nooneeji tiiɗɗi iwri e waylitinde timmunde e nder nguurndam... Nde neɗɗo golli feewi e nooneeji tiiɗɗi, ko waawi ko yimɓe bonnaaɓe nder nguurndam, ɗum waawaa ɓaɗtinde ɗum law.

Men mbi'aama ko waawi ko faamaade ɗum e nder nguurnam, e hakkillaaji men e nder goonga... Mo gollaaki e hoore mum ko ganyanke e ko waawi; ko no leño nder ndiyam laamɗam nder baharu.

Ko waawi waɗata waylaade e nder nooneeji keewɗi; iwri e nder welleeje, ɗum ko tiɗɗindire... Goonga woodi ko waawi moƴƴi e ko waawi boni; ko waawi goɗɗi ɓuri moƴƴude e ko ɓuri bonde... Waylitinde ko waawi goɗɗi ko waawi; waylitinde njoolla, waylitinde ngonkaaji, ekn, ko e nder limgal ko waawi.

Ammaa woodi ngonkaaji goonga nde waawaa waylitaade; e nder ɗum'en ɓaawo eɗen njaɓa e hakkillaaji men, haa ɗum'en goɗɗi ko kulɗi e saɗɗi... Se tinndindirde, suurɗude iwri nde men ngiɗaa e nder kulle nde iwri.

Eɗen njogii nguurndam no careeli ngonkaaji nder; taariika goonga e nguurndam men keewɗum e ngonkaaji mum'en... Nde men ngaccitii timmunde e nguurndam men, men njogiima men e hoore mum'en e laabiire men, ko kulle keewɗe ko men waawi ngam ngonkaaji nder tiiɗɗi.

Alhazana Magno, haa kanko e hoore mum na heɓti e ngonkaaji nde iwri e maayde... Francisco I maayi ngam naane e bonde nder jime, nde taariika na hiirtini haa hannde... Nde Marat waɗi e monjaa gaajo, ɗum ɓaadi e maayde e tiiɗi e hasidi, ɗum hooreejo na laaɓi.

Rewɓe Parco de los Ciervos se tinndindirde timminii ngoonga e nguurndam fornicaajo kulɗo nde wi'etee Luis XV. Yimɓe keewɗe maayde ngam yidde, tikkere e gilaaji, ɗum ko sikke ngam Psikologiyaajo...

Si jiɗnde men goonga e nooneeji fooli, men ngadiima ngam Panteon e cemeterio... Otelo ngam gilaaji waɗi e warooje e kaso na heewi e fooliiɓe goongaaɓe.
